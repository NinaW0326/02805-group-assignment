<p>This <b>violet-cloaked wizard</b> was an old man <a href="/wiki/Vernon_Dursley" title="Vernon Dursley">Vernon Dursley</a> ran into in <a href="/wiki/November" title="November">November</a> <a href="/wiki/1981" title="1981">1981</a>. Upon being knocked over by the latter, he stood back up and, told Vernon no apology was necessary as nothing could upset him that day and walked off, but not before giving Dursley a hug around the middle. He, among many other wizards, was in gleeful spirits that day as <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a> had been nearly vanquished (although everyone believed he was completely gone) thanks to the baby <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-PS1_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PS1-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Violet-cloaked_wizard?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Violet-cloaked_wizard?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>Given the similarity in their descriptions, it is possible that this individual is <a href="/wiki/Filius_Flitwick" title="Filius Flitwick">Filius Flitwick</a>. Even though this scene takes place during the <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts</a> school year, <a href="/wiki/Albus_Dumbledore" title="Albus Dumbledore">two other</a> <a href="/wiki/Minerva_McGonagall" title="Minerva McGonagall">Hogwarts professors</a> and <a href="/wiki/Rubeus_Hagrid" title="Rubeus Hagrid">one other staff member</a> were known to be outside the school that day as well.</li>
<li>It is also possible that this man is <a href="/wiki/Dedalus_Diggle" title="Dedalus Diggle">Dedalus Diggle</a>, who is described in some similar ways as well.</li></ul>
