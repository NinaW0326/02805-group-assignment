<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Durmstrang_girl?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Durmstrang_girl?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>On <a href="/wiki/31_October" title="31 October">31 October</a>, <a href="/wiki/1994" title="1994">1994</a>, she was seen standing next to <a href="/wiki/Poliakoff" title="Poliakoff">Poliakoff</a> when he first saw <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Great_Hall" title="Great Hall">Great Hall</a> at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a>, during the selection of the candidates for <a href="/wiki/Triwizard_Tournament" title="Triwizard Tournament">Triwizard Tournament</a>. Surprised to see him, Poliakoff pointed at Harry and told her that he was the famous <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-GOF16_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF16-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Durmstrang_girl?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Durmstrang_girl?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>She is the only female student mentioned in the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_(book_series)" title="Harry Potter (book series)">book series</a> to study at the <a href="/wiki/Durmstrang_Institute" title="Durmstrang Institute">Durmstrang Institute</a>.</li>
<li>She does not appear in the film because Durmstrang was portrayed as an all-boys school, the counterpart to the all-girls <a href="/wiki/Beauxbatons_Academy_of_Magic" title="Beauxbatons Academy of Magic">Beauxbatons</a>.</li>
<li>During <a href="/wiki/Bartemius_Crouch_Snr" class="mw-redirect" title="Bartemius Crouch Snr">Barty Crouch's</a> madness, he claimed that <a href="/wiki/Igor_Karkaroff" title="Igor Karkaroff">Igor Karkaroff</a> was bringing twelve students to the Triwizard Tournament.<sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> This might or might not have been true, considering Crouch's mental state at the time, but if it is true, then this girl was probably one of the top twelve students at Durmstrang.</li></ul>
