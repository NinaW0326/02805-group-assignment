<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Sixth-Year_Inquisitorial_Squad_Member?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Sixth-Year_Inquisitorial_Squad_Member?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span id="1995–1996_school_year"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="1995.E2.80.931996_school_year">1995–1996 school year</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Sixth-Year_Inquisitorial_Squad_Member?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: 1995–1996 school year">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Sixth-Year_Inquisitorial_Squad_Member?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: 1995–1996 school year"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<figure class="thumb tleft show-info-icon" style="width: 182px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/0/0a/Inquisitorial_Squad.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20161216055102" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/0/0a/Inquisitorial_Squad.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/180?cb=20161216055102" decoding="async" width="180" height="108" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="Inquisitorial Squad.jpg" data-image-key="Inquisitorial_Squad.jpg" /></a> 	 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:Inquisitorial_Squad.jpg" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption">Dolores Umbridge inducting her, Draco Malfoy, Vincent Crabbe and several other students into the Inquisitorial Squad</p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure>
<p>She was in her <a href="/wiki/Sixth_year" title="Sixth year">sixth year</a> in the <a href="/wiki/1995%E2%80%931996_school_year" title="1995–1996 school year">1995–1996 school year</a>. During that year, she became a member of the short-lived <a href="/wiki/Inquisitorial_Squad" title="Inquisitorial Squad">Inquisitorial Squad</a>, an organisation created that year by the newly appointed <a href="/wiki/Defence_Against_the_Dark_Arts" title="Defence Against the Dark Arts">Defence Against the Dark Arts</a> <a href="/wiki/Professor" title="Professor">Professor</a> and <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_High_Inquisitor" title="Hogwarts High Inquisitor">High Inquisitor</a> <a href="/wiki/Dolores_Umbridge" title="Dolores Umbridge">Dolores Umbridge</a>. Her involvement in the discovery of <a href="/wiki/Dumbledore%27s_Army" title="Dumbledore&#39;s Army">Dumbledore's Army</a> in <a href="/wiki/1996" title="1996">1996</a> is unknown, although along with all the other squad members, she would have used her power abusively to discriminate against other non-Slytherin students.
</p><p>In <a href="/wiki/June" title="June">June</a> 1996, when <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>, <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ronald Weasley</a>, <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione Granger</a>, <a href="/wiki/Ginevra_Weasley" title="Ginevra Weasley">Ginevra Weasley</a>, <a href="/wiki/Neville_Longbottom" title="Neville Longbottom">Neville Longbottom</a> and <a href="/wiki/Luna_Lovegood" title="Luna Lovegood">Luna Lovegood</a> tried to use the fireplace in <a href="/wiki/Defence_Against_the_Dark_Arts_teacher%27s_office" title="Defence Against the Dark Arts teacher&#39;s office">Umbridge's office</a> to communicate with <a href="/wiki/Sirius_Black" title="Sirius Black">Sirius Black</a> via the <a href="/wiki/Floo_Network" title="Floo Network">Floo Network</a>, Umbridge and several squad members, including herself, caught them. She managed to restrain Ginny by grabbing hold of her and holding both her arms in a tight-grip.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP32_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP32-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> However, she and the squad failed to keep the Dumbledore's Army members restrained for very long, as once Umbridge had left with Harry and Hermione to the <a href="/wiki/Forbidden_Forest" title="Forbidden Forest">Forbidden Forest</a>, all the present squad members were attacked with various nasty <a href="/wiki/Jinx" title="Jinx">jinxes</a>, <a href="/wiki/Hex" title="Hex">hexes</a> and <a href="/wiki/Curse" title="Curse">curses</a> by Ginny, Neville and Luna in revenge, such as with Ginny's particularly powerful <a href="/wiki/Bat-Bogey_Hex" title="Bat-Bogey Hex">Bat-Bogey Hex</a>. She was presumably incapacitated during the incident.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP33_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP33-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Post-Inquisitorial_Squad">Post-Inquisitorial Squad</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Sixth-Year_Inquisitorial_Squad_Member?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Post-Inquisitorial Squad">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Sixth-Year_Inquisitorial_Squad_Member?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Post-Inquisitorial Squad"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>The <a href="/wiki/Inquisitorial_Squad" title="Inquisitorial Squad">Inquisitorial Squad</a> was disbanded following the suspension and departure of Umbridge from Hogwarts, following the <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">British Ministry of Magic</a>'s long awaited (but still reluctant) acceptance that <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a> had indeed <a href="/wiki/Return_of_Lord_Voldemort" title="Return of Lord Voldemort">returned</a>. Her activities after the disbandment are unknown.
</p>
