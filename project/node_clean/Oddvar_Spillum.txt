<b>Oddvar Spillum</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Norway" title="Norway">Norwegian</a> <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> who, in <a href="/wiki/2014" title="2014">2014</a>, coached the <a href="/wiki/Norwegian_National_Quidditch_team" title="Norwegian National Quidditch team">Norwegian National Quidditch team</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-PM_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PM-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Oddvar_Spillum?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Oddvar_Spillum?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>When his team, one of the joint favourites of the <a href="/wiki/2014_Quidditch_World_Cup" title="2014 Quidditch World Cup">2014 Quidditch World Cup</a>, suffered a heavy defeat against <a href="/wiki/Bulgarian_National_Quidditch_team" title="Bulgarian National Quidditch team">Bulgaria</a> (170 to 20), Oddvar Spillum had no post-match comment for reporters, apart from broken sobs.<sup id="cite_ref-PM_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PM-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
