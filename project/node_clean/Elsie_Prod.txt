<b>Elsie Prod</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch</a> who lived in <a href="/wiki/Didsbury" title="Didsbury">Didsbury</a>, <a href="/wiki/England" title="England">England</a> in late <a href="/wiki/1992" title="1992">1992</a><sup id="cite_ref-COS8_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COS8-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> and was the wife of <a href="/wiki/Demetrius_Prod" title="Demetrius Prod">Demetrius Prod</a>.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Elsie_Prod?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Elsie_Prod?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Sometime prior to <a href="/wiki/January" title="January">January</a> <a href="/wiki/1992" title="1992">1992</a>, her husband wrote to <a href="/wiki/Kwikspell_Company" title="Kwikspell Company">Kwikspell Company</a>, and a month thereafter managed to <a href="/wiki/Human_to_yak_spell" title="Human to yak spell">turn Elsie into a yak</a>; he wrote an advertisement for the Kwikspell Company, proud of this feat. <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> and <a href="/wiki/Argus_Filch" title="Argus Filch">Argus Filch</a> would later read about this.<sup id="cite_ref-COS8_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COS8-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>On the eve of <a href="/wiki/27_November" title="27 November">27 November</a>, <a href="/wiki/1998" title="1998">1998</a><sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> after supper, Demetrius and Elsie got into an argument over the washing-up. He died that night, described by the <i><a href="/wiki/Daily_Prophet" title="Daily Prophet">Daily Prophet</a></i> to have been "noisily". His obituary claimed Elise "sadly missed"<sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> her husband, despite seemingly not getting along well with him.
</p>
