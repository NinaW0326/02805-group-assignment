<p><b>Professor Brian Gagwilde</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> and a <a href="/wiki/Headmaster" title="Headmaster">Headmaster</a> of <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-HPLE_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HPLE-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> Professor Gagwilde also wrote <i><a href="/wiki/Jiggery_Pokery_and_Hocus_Pocus" title="Jiggery Pokery and Hocus Pocus">Jiggery Pokery and Hocus Pocus</a></i>.<sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-PM_2-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PM-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>His portrait, in which he is depicted as playing a game of chess with <a href="/wiki/Walter_Aragon" title="Walter Aragon">Walter Aragon</a>, is still at the <a href="/wiki/Headmaster%27s_office" class="mw-redirect" title="Headmaster&#39;s office">Headmaster's office</a>, to give advice to the current Head.
</p>
