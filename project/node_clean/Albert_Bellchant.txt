<b>Albert Belchant</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> and student at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> and member of the up and coming <a href="/wiki/Bellchant_family" title="Bellchant family">Bellchant family</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-FBC5_3-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-FBC5-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Albert_Bellchant?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Albert_Bellchant?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>His father Astor received <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_letter_to_Astor_Bellchant" title="Hogwarts letter to Astor Bellchant">a letter</a> from Hogwarts detailing how Albert was failing <a href="/wiki/Transfiguration_(class)" title="Transfiguration (class)">Transfiguration</a>, <a href="/wiki/Charms_(class)" title="Charms (class)">Charms</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Herbology" title="Herbology">Herbology</a>. This motivated Astor to blackmail <a href="/wiki/Bilius_Finbok" title="Bilius Finbok">Bilius Finbok</a>, with whom he had attended Hogwarts, into brewing an illegal mental acuity <a href="/wiki/Potion" title="Potion">potion</a>. <a href="/wiki/Mathilda_Grimblehawk" title="Mathilda Grimblehawk">Mathilda Grimblehawk</a> and <a href="/wiki/Mathilda_Grimblehawk%27s_partner" title="Mathilda Grimblehawk&#39;s partner">her partner</a> investigated the situation after Finbok was injured and sent to <a href="/wiki/St_David%27s_Hospital" class="mw-redirect" title="St David&#39;s Hospital">a Muggle hospital</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-FBC5_3-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-FBC5-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
