<p>The real-life <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Dodie_Smith" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Dodie Smith">Dodie Smith</a> was the author of <i>I Capture the Castle</i>, but is perhaps most well-known for her title <i><a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/The_Hundred_and_One_Dalmatians" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:The Hundred and One Dalmatians">The Hundred and One Dalmatians</a></i>, which was also adapted as a popular Disney animated film in 1961, and again as a live-action film in 1998.
</p>
