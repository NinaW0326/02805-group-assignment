<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Alberic_Grunnion?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Alberic_Grunnion?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_life">Early life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Alberic_Grunnion?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Early life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Alberic_Grunnion?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Early life"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Alberic Grunnion was born in <a href="/wiki/1803" class="mw-redirect" title="1803">1803</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-COSG_1-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COSG-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> somewhere in the <a href="/wiki/Great_Britain" title="Great Britain">British Isles</a>. If he enrolled at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a>, he attended the school from around <a href="/wiki/1814" class="mw-redirect" title="1814">1814</a> to <a href="/wiki/1821" class="mw-redirect" title="1821">1821</a>.
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Adulthood">Adulthood</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Alberic_Grunnion?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Adulthood">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Alberic_Grunnion?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Adulthood"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>At some point in the <a href="/wiki/19th_century" title="19th century">nineteenth century</a>, Alberic Grunnion invented the <a href="/wiki/Dungbomb" title="Dungbomb">Dungbomb</a>, a prank item that was still very popular among young British <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizards</a> by the <a href="/wiki/1990s" title="1990s">1990s</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-COSG_1-10" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COSG-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Death_and_post-mortem">Death and post-mortem</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Alberic_Grunnion?veaction=edit&amp;section=4" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Death and post-mortem">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Alberic_Grunnion?action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Death and post-mortem"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>He died in <a href="/wiki/1882" class="mw-redirect" title="1882">1882</a>, at the age of 79. By the early <a href="/wiki/1990s" title="1990s">1990s</a>, he was featured on a <a href="/wiki/Chocolate_Frog_Card" title="Chocolate Frog Card">Chocolate Frog Card</a>. His card was one of the first in <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>'s collection, being among the ones he obtained on his <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Express" title="Hogwarts Express">trip</a> to <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> in <a href="/wiki/1991" title="1991">1991</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-PS6_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PS6-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Alberic_Grunnion?veaction=edit&amp;section=5" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Alberic_Grunnion?action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Grunnion's name could possibly be in recognition of <i>Alberich,</i> a powerful wizard from "Song of the Nibelungen." The poem is mythical, but was the basis for Wagner's Ring Cycle. In Wagner's version, Alberich makes a ring from gold that he has found and places a curse on it against its thief. Interestingly, one of the prizes given to a 'hero' of the Ring Cycle is an <a href="/wiki/Invisibility_cloak" title="Invisibility cloak">Invisibility cloak</a>.
</p>
