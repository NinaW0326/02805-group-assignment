<b>Raul Almeida</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Brazil" title="Brazil">Brazilian</a> <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> and the <a href="/wiki/Keeper" title="Keeper">Keeper</a> for the <a href="/wiki/2014" title="2014">2014</a> <a href="/wiki/Brazilian_National_Quidditch_team" title="Brazilian National Quidditch team">Brazilian National Quidditch team</a>. On the first match of the quarter-finals, Brazil versus <a href="/wiki/Welsh_National_Quidditch_team" title="Welsh National Quidditch team">Wales</a>, on <a href="/wiki/4_June" title="4 June">4 June</a>, <a href="/wiki/2014" title="2014">2014</a>, Almeida was struck to the head by a vicious <a href="/wiki/Bludger" title="Bludger">Bludger</a> by Welsh <a href="/wiki/Beater" title="Beater">Beater</a> <a href="/wiki/Iefan_Rice" title="Iefan Rice">Iefan Rice</a>, while the <a href="/wiki/Quaffle" title="Quaffle">Quaffle</a> was at the other end of the pitch. This earned Brazil a penalty.<sup id="cite_ref-PM_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PM-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
