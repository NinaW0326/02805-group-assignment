<p><b>Squibbert</b> (b. <a href="/wiki/1976" title="1976">1976</a> or earlier)<sup id="cite_ref-birth_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-birth-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch</a> and student at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-COSG_3-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COSG-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Squibbert?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Squibbert?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Sometime throughout her education, she <a href="/wiki/Teapot_to_ferret_spell" title="Teapot to ferret spell">changed a teapot into a ferret</a>, albeit poorly, as its nose remained being a spout. A "rather large" <a href="/wiki/Slytherin" title="Slytherin">Slytherin</a> boy then chased after it with a <a href="/wiki/Butterfly" title="Butterfly">butterfly</a> net. A <a href="/wiki/Second_year" title="Second year">second year</a> student witnessed this incident, and eventually wrote about it in a book after becoming an author. A copy of this book eventually found its way into the <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Library" title="Hogwarts Library">Hogwarts Library</a> by the <a href="/wiki/1992-1993_school_year" class="mw-redirect" title="1992-1993 school year">1992-1993 school year</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-COSG_3-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COSG-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
