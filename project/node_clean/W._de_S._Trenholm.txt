<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/W._de_S._Trenholm?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/W._de_S._Trenholm?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>He served as the foreman of a <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/grand_jury" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:grand jury">grand jury</a> tasked with investigating <a href="/wiki/Prohibition" title="Prohibition">Prohibition</a> violations.<sup id="cite_ref-fb-chronicle_1-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-fb-chronicle-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-eveningworld_2-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-eveningworld-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> On <a href="/wiki/6_December" title="6 December">6 December</a> <a href="/wiki/1926" title="1926">1926</a>, this jury was censured by the presiding judge, <a href="/wiki/Rufus_Foster" title="Rufus Foster">Rufus Foster</a>, for leaking a sealed letter to the press, which was written by de S. Trenholm and intended to be read only by Foster.<sup id="cite_ref-fb-chronicle_1-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-fb-chronicle-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-eveningworld_2-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-eveningworld-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> The jury was ordered by Foster to deliver any indictments they had found within three days, at which point they would be dismissed.<sup id="cite_ref-fb-chronicle_1-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-fb-chronicle-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-eveningworld_2-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-eveningworld-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> The incident was reported in the 6 December 1926 issue of the <i><a href="/wiki/New_York_Chronicle" title="New York Chronicle">New York Chronicle</a></i>.<sup id="cite_ref-fb-chronicle_1-10" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-fb-chronicle-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-eveningworld_2-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-eveningworld-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
