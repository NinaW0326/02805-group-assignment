<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Daniel_Page%27s_sister?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Daniel_Page%27s_sister?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>She was born to a <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggle</a> <a href="/wiki/Page_siblings%27_father" title="Page siblings&#39; father">father</a> and <a href="/wiki/Page_siblings%27_mother" title="Page siblings&#39; mother">a mother</a> who was a witch. She had two brothers and at least one, <a href="/wiki/Daniel_Page" title="Daniel Page">Daniel Page</a> was younger than her. Her other brother, <a href="/wiki/Reggie_Page" title="Reggie Page">Reggie Page</a> was a <a href="/wiki/Squib" title="Squib">Squib</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-MA_3-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-MA-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<figure class="thumb tright show-info-icon" style="width: 222px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/d/de/The_Bloody_Pages.png/revision/latest?cb=20200604125037" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/d/de/The_Bloody_Pages.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/220?cb=20200604125037" decoding="async" width="220" height="101" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="The Bloody Pages.png" data-image-key="The_Bloody_Pages.png" /></a> 	 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:The_Bloody_Pages.png" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption">A Muggle newspaper reporting the arrest of her family</p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure>
<p>Her father was involved in illicit activities, and he involved Reggie in it. They were eventually caught by Muggle police, and her mother tried to use her magic to help the two escape. She frightened the police and violated the <a href="/wiki/International_Statute_of_Wizarding_Secrecy" title="International Statute of Wizarding Secrecy">International Statute of Secrecy</a> in doing so.<sup id="cite_ref-MA_3-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-MA-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Her father and brother were taken to a Muggle prison, and her mother was sentenced to <a href="/wiki/Azkaban" title="Azkaban">Azkaban</a> for being found guilty of being a member of the <a href="/wiki/Nocturnal_Order_of_Tricks_and_Exhibitions" title="Nocturnal Order of Tricks and Exhibitions">Nocturnal Order of Tricks and Exhibitions</a>. The incident was put in a Muggle newspaper under the heading, "The Bloody Pages". Her younger brother, Daniel was put under her guardianship. He later began attending <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-MA_3-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-MA-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
