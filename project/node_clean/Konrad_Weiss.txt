<b>Konrad Weiss</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Germany" title="Germany">German</a> <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> who played as <a href="/wiki/Seeker" title="Seeker">Seeker</a> on the <a href="/wiki/German_National_Quidditch_team" title="German National Quidditch team">German National Quidditch team</a> during the <a href="/wiki/1994_Quidditch_World_Cup" title="1994 Quidditch World Cup">422nd Quidditch World Cup</a>, in <a href="/wiki/1994" title="1994">1994</a>, and wore <a href="/wiki/Quidditch_Goggles" title="Quidditch Goggles">goggles</a> when playing.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>During the <a href="/wiki/1996%E2%80%931997_school_year" title="1996–1997 school year">1996–1997 school year</a>, Weiss' <a href="/wiki/Chocolate_Frog_Card" title="Chocolate Frog Card">Chocolate Frog Card</a> could be won by beating <a href="/wiki/Sean_Ogbourne" title="Sean Ogbourne">Sean Ogbourne</a> at an <a href="/wiki/Exploding_Snap" title="Exploding Snap">Exploding Snap</a> challenge.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
