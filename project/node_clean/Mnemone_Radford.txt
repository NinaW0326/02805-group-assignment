<b>Mnemone Radford</b> (<a href="/wiki/1562" class="mw-redirect" title="1562">1562</a> - <a href="/wiki/1649" class="mw-redirect" title="1649">1649</a>) was a <a href="/wiki/Great_Britain" title="Great Britain">British</a> <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch</a>, who first developed <a href="/wiki/Memory_Charm" title="Memory Charm">Memory Modifying Charms</a>. She became the first <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">British Ministry of Magic</a> <a href="/wiki/Obliviator" title="Obliviator">Obliviator</a>, due to her skill and expertise with casting the <a href="/wiki/Charm" title="Charm">charm</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-WotM_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-WotM-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Magical_abilities_and_skills">Magical abilities and skills</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Mnemone_Radford?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Mnemone_Radford?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li><b><a href="/wiki/Spell_creation" title="Spell creation">Spell creation</a></b>: Radford had succeeded in the incredibly difficult and complex <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magical</a> art of inventing new <a href="/wiki/Spell" title="Spell">spells</a>, as she successfully invented the <a href="/wiki/Memory_Charm" title="Memory Charm">Memory Charm</a>, a particularly useful and practical charm.</li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Charm" title="Charm">Charms</a></b>: Radford was highly accomplished and talented with charm-work, given how she successfully invented the <a href="/wiki/Memory_Charm" title="Memory Charm">Memory Charm</a> and was highly skilled with the casting of it, given how she was the first <a href="/wiki/Obliviator" title="Obliviator">Obliviator</a> who worked for the <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">British Ministry of Magic</a>.</li></ul>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Mnemone_Radford?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Mnemone_Radford?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>There are a few issues with this character's chronological timeframe. For one,  <i><a href="/wiki/Pottermore" title="Pottermore">Pottermore</a></i> reveals that the <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">Ministry of Magic</a> was only formally established in <a href="/wiki/1707" class="mw-redirect" title="1707">1707</a>, 58 years after Mnemone Radford died, so it is uncertain exactly how she could have worked for the Ministry. Even if one considers that the phrase "Ministry of Magic" used on <i><a href="/wiki/Wizard_of_the_Month" title="Wizard of the Month">Wizard of the Month</a></i> is an over-simplification of "<a href="/wiki/Wizards%27_Council" title="Wizards&#39; Council">Wizards' Council</a>", it is still left unexplained what use would an <a href="/wiki/Obliviator" title="Obliviator">Obliviator</a> have in the days before the establishment of the <a href="/wiki/International_Statute_of_Wizarding_Secrecy" title="International Statute of Wizarding Secrecy">International Statute of Wizarding Secrecy</a> in <a href="/wiki/1692" class="mw-redirect" title="1692">1692</a>.</li></ul>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Mnemone_Radford?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Mnemone_Radford?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Mnemone Radford is described as inventing the <a href="/wiki/Memory_Charm" title="Memory Charm">Memory Modifying Charm</a>. <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mnemosyne" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Mnemosyne">Mnemosyne</a> was an ancient Greek goddess of memory. Her daughter, <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mneme" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Mneme">Mneme</a> was the <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Boeotian_muses" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Boeotian muses">muse</a> of memory.
</p>
