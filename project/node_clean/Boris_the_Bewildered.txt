<b>Boris the Bewildered</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> from an unknown time period. A statue of him stands on the fifth floor of <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Castle" title="Hogwarts Castle">Hogwarts Castle</a>, near the <a href="/wiki/Prefects%27_bathroom" class="mw-redirect" title="Prefects&#39; bathroom">Prefects' bathroom</a>. His statue featured a lost-looking wizard with his gloves on the wrong hands.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> A portrait of Boris also hangs on <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts'</a> walls, guarding a secret passageway between the second floor corridor and the <a href="/wiki/Third-floor_corridor" title="Third-floor corridor">third floor corridor</a>. The password was <i>Forget-Me-Never</i>.<sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Boris_the_Bewildered?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Boris_the_Bewildered?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
