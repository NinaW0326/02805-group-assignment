During the fifth match of the <a href="/wiki/2014_Quidditch_World_Cup" title="2014 Quidditch World Cup">427th Quidditch World Cup</a>, Liechtenstein versus <a href="/wiki/Chadian_National_Quidditch_team" title="Chadian National Quidditch team">Chad</a>, was believed to literally have fallen asleep shortly before play was stopped for the second evening.<sup id="cite_ref-lie-v-tcd_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-lie-v-tcd-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
