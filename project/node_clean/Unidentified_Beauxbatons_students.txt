<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Beauxbatons_boy">Beauxbatons boy</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_Beauxbatons_students?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Beauxbatons boy">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_Beauxbatons_students?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Beauxbatons boy"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>This <b>boy</b> attended <a href="/wiki/Beauxbatons_Academy_of_Magic" title="Beauxbatons Academy of Magic">Beauxbatons Academy of Magic</a> during the <a href="/wiki/Triwizard_Tournament" title="Triwizard Tournament">Triwizard Tournament</a>.
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_Beauxbatons_students?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_Beauxbatons_students?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>This wizard was born in either <a href="/wiki/1977" title="1977">1977</a> or <a href="/wiki/1978" title="1978">1978</a>. He was one of the students representing Beauxbatons to potentially compete in the <a href="/wiki/1994" title="1994">1994</a> Triwizard Tournament.
</p><p>After failing to be chosen as champion, the boy remained at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts</a> to watch his champion, <a href="/wiki/Fleur_Delacour" title="Fleur Delacour">Fleur Delacour</a>. He attended the <a href="/wiki/Yule_Ball" title="Yule Ball">Yule Ball</a>, during which he noticed <a href="/wiki/Parvati_Patil" title="Parvati Patil">Parvati Patil</a> and asked her for a dance. Parvati eagerly accepted, and the two spent the rest of the night together.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> Parvati and the Beauxbatons boy spent large amounts of time together, meeting in <a href="/wiki/Hogsmeade" title="Hogsmeade">Hogsmeade</a> at least once.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>It was unknown what happened to the Beauxbatons boy after the Triwizard Tournament, or if he and Parvati remained in contact.
</p>

<ul><li>The Beauxbatons boy was replaced by <a href="/wiki/Igor_Karkaroff%27s_aide" class="mw-redirect" title="Igor Karkaroff&#39;s aide">Igor Karkaroff's aide</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Goblet_of_Fire_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire (film)">film adaptation</a> of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Goblet_of_Fire" title="Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire">Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire</a></i>, probably because, in the movie, Beauxbatons is portrayed as an all-girls' school.</li></ul>

<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography_2">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_Beauxbatons_students?veaction=edit&amp;section=6" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_Beauxbatons_students?action=edit&amp;section=6" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>When a gang of <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a> rampaged through <a href="/wiki/Mr_Roberts%27s_campsite" class="mw-redirect" title="Mr Roberts&#39;s campsite">Mr Roberts's campsite</a>, this witch, along with her classmates, hid in the nearby forest, where she looked for <a href="/wiki/Olympe_Maxime" title="Olympe Maxime">Madame Maxime</a>, whom they had lost. She then met <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ronald Weasley</a>, and she asked him if he had saw her. He did not understand what she told him, since he did not speak French, letting the girl understand he was from Hogwarts.<sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Physical_appearance">Physical appearance</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_Beauxbatons_students?veaction=edit&amp;section=7" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Physical appearance">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_Beauxbatons_students?action=edit&amp;section=7" title="Edit section: Physical appearance"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>This girl was described to be little, with thick and curly hair.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Crying_Beauxbatons_girls">Crying Beauxbatons girls</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_Beauxbatons_students?veaction=edit&amp;section=9" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Crying Beauxbatons girls">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_Beauxbatons_students?action=edit&amp;section=9" title="Edit section: Crying Beauxbatons girls"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<div class="quote">
<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire">"<i>Oh look, they're all disappointed.</i>"</span></dd>
<dd>&#8212;<a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione Granger</a> expresses her <i>"sympathy"</i><sup class="noprint"><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Goblet_of_Fire" title="Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire">[src]</a></sup></dd>
<dd></dd></dl></div>
<p>These <b>two girls</b> attended <a href="/wiki/Beauxbatons_Academy_of_Magic" title="Beauxbatons Academy of Magic">Beauxbatons Academy of Magic</a> and were part of their school's delegation to <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> in <a href="/wiki/1994" title="1994">1994</a> for the <a href="/wiki/Triwizard_Tournament" title="Triwizard Tournament">Triwizard Tournament</a>. They entered their names in the <a href="/wiki/Goblet_of_Fire" title="Goblet of Fire">Goblet of Fire</a>, and when <a href="/wiki/Fleur_Delacour" title="Fleur Delacour">Fleur Delacour</a> was selected as the Beauxbatons Champion, they broke down crying in disappointment. They presumably remained at Hogwarts for the remainder of the school year to watch the Tournament.
</p>
