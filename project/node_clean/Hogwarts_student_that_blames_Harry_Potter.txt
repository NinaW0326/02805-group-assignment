<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire (film)">"<i>He's not even seventeen yet.</i>"</span></dd>
<dd>&#8212;This student's anger at Harry Potter<sup class="noprint"><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Goblet_of_Fire_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire (film)">[src]</a></sup></dd>
<dd></dd></dl></div>
<p>This <b>boy</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> and student at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> during the <a href="/wiki/1990s" title="1990s">1990s</a>. He and a <a href="/wiki/Unidentified_Hufflepuff_boy_(II)" title="Unidentified Hufflepuff boy (II)">boy</a> from <a href="/wiki/Hufflepuff" title="Hufflepuff">Hufflepuff</a> blamed <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> for being chosen as champion in the <a href="/wiki/Triwizard_Tournament" title="Triwizard Tournament">Triwizard Tournament</a> in the <a href="/wiki/1994%E2%80%931995_school_year" title="1994–1995 school year">1994–1995 school year</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-GOFF_2-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOFF-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>He noted that Harry was not yet even seventeen, which had been designated as the minimum allowed age for any student who wished to enter the tournament.<sup id="cite_ref-GOFF_2-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOFF-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
