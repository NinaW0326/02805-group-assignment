<b>Vaisey</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> and student at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> and who <a href="/wiki/Sorting_ceremony" title="Sorting ceremony">sorted</a> into the <a href="/wiki/Slytherin" title="Slytherin">Slytherin</a> house. He played <a href="/wiki/Chaser" title="Chaser">Chaser</a> for the <a href="/wiki/Slytherin_Quidditch_team" title="Slytherin Quidditch team">Slytherin Quidditch team</a> during the <a href="/wiki/1996%E2%80%931997_school_year" title="1996–1997 school year">1996–1997 school year</a>. The day before Slytherin's match against Gryffindor, Vaisey was hit in the head by a <a href="/wiki/Bludger" title="Bludger">bludger</a> and was unable to play.<sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
