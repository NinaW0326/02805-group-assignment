<p><b>Stanley "Stan" Shunpike</b> (born between <a href="/wiki/15_September" title="15 September">15 September</a> <a href="/wiki/1974" title="1974">1974</a> and <a href="/wiki/13_September" title="13 September">13 September</a> <a href="/wiki/1975" title="1975">1975</a><sup id="cite_ref-DOB_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DOB-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>) was a <a href="/wiki/Great_Britain" title="Great Britain">British</a> <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> who was the conductor and helper of the <a href="/wiki/Knight_Bus" title="Knight Bus">Knight Bus</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-POA3_3-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-POA3-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> On <a href="/wiki/14_September" title="14 September">14 September</a> <a href="/wiki/1996" title="1996">1996</a>, he was arrested and sent to <a href="/wiki/Azkaban" title="Azkaban">Azkaban</a> for being a <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eater</a> when he was overheard claiming to have inside information about the organisation, although in all likelihood, he was only fooling around.<sup id="cite_ref-DOB_1-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DOB-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> In <a href="/wiki/1997" title="1997">1997</a>, he broke free from prison and joined the Death Eaters under the <a href="/wiki/Imperius_Curse" title="Imperius Curse">Imperius Curse</a>. His fate after the war is unknown; he most likely awoke from the Curse following <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a>'s ultimate defeat, like all the other Imperiused victims, and returned to his job.
</p>

<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_life">Early life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Stanley_Shunpike?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Early life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Stanley_Shunpike?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Early life"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Stanley was born between <a href="/wiki/15_September" title="15 September">15 September</a> <a href="/wiki/1974" title="1974">1974</a> and <a href="/wiki/13_September" title="13 September">13 September</a> <a href="/wiki/1975" title="1975">1975</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-DOB_1-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DOB-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> and possibly grew up in inner <a href="/wiki/London" title="London">London</a>, as he had a strong <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Cockney" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Cockney">Cockney</a> accent.<sup id="cite_ref-POA3_3-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-POA3-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> It is unknown if he ever attended <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a>, but if so, he would have attended from <a href="/wiki/1986" title="1986">1986</a> to <a href="/wiki/1993" title="1993">1993</a> unless he was born in early September.
</p>

<p>Stan was a skilled motorist, but not a man of high intelligence. As such, Stan failed to recognise <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> who had just fled from <a href="/wiki/4_Privet_Drive" title="4 Privet Drive">4 Privet Drive</a> and instead believed his alias of <a href="/wiki/Neville_Longbottom" title="Neville Longbottom">Neville Longbottom</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-POA3_3-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-POA3-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>When talking to Harry and others on the Knight Bus he showed his love of gossip and of having inside information. He was shocked when Harry nonchalantly spoke the name "<a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Voldemort</a>", and even when later meeting up with <a href="/wiki/Cornelius_Fudge" title="Cornelius Fudge">Cornelius Fudge</a>, who confirmed Harry's true identity, Stan continued to refer Harry as "Neville" for the rest of that trip.<sup id="cite_ref-POA3_3-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-POA3-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Stan attended the <a href="/wiki/1994" title="1994">1994</a> <a href="/wiki/Quidditch_World_Cup" title="Quidditch World Cup">Quidditch World Cup</a>, and after the match, Stan and several others were witnessed by Harry and <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ron Weasley</a> trying to chat up a group of <a href="/wiki/Veela" title="Veela">Veela</a>, with Stan making the outrageous and false comment of him being "<i>about to be made the youngest <a href="/wiki/Minister_for_Magic" title="Minister for Magic">Minister for Magic</a> ever</i>", in an effort to impress them.<sup id="cite_ref-GOF9_5-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF9-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

Indeed, the interrogation with Stan has revealed that he has absolutely no link with the Death Eaters, but the Ministry kept him in order to put up a faÃ§ade of progress.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP16_8-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP16-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup>

<div class="quote">
<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows">"<i>I saw Stan Shunpike. . . . You know, the bloke who was the conductor on the Knight Bus? And I tried to Disarm him instead of â well, he doesnât know what heâs doing, does he? He must be Imperiused!</i>"</span></dd>
<dd>&#8212;Harry Potter discussing Stan to <a href="/wiki/Remus_Lupin" title="Remus Lupin">Remus Lupin</a> after the Battle of the Seven Potters<sup class="noprint"><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Deathly_Hallows" title="Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows">[src]</a></sup></dd>
<dd></dd></dl></div>
<p>In <a href="/wiki/1997" title="1997">1997</a>, Azkaban experienced a <a href="/wiki/Azkaban_escapes#1997" title="Azkaban escapes">breakout</a> at the hands of Death Eaters. Although Stan was no Death Eater, he was still freed from his cell (perhaps because he was close to the others, or the Death Eaters might have simply wanted to show the Ministry they had no power in Azkaban). Either way, Stan joined the Death Eaters' ranks, albeit under the <a href="/wiki/Imperius_Curse" title="Imperius Curse">Imperius Curse</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-DH4_9-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH4-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Stan took part in the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_the_Seven_Potters" title="Battle of the Seven Potters">Battle of the Seven Potters</a>, during which he and nearly thirty other Death Eaters flew into battle against members of the <a href="/wiki/Order_of_the_Phoenix" title="Order of the Phoenix">Order of the Phoenix</a> who were trying to transport Harry Potter to safety. Stan and several others, unknowingly, tracked down the real Harry (who had been given decoys to lure pursuers away). In combat, Harry fired a spell that knocked Stan's Death Eater mask off, exposing his "blank eyes" (a tell-tale sign of Imperius control), and, because a <a href="/wiki/Stunning_Spell" title="Stunning Spell">Stunning Spell</a> while over a hundred feet in the air would result in Stan falling to his <a href="/wiki/Death" title="Death">death</a>, Harry elected to shoot a <a href="/wiki/Disarming_Charm" title="Disarming Charm">Disarming Charm</a>, blowing Stan's wand out of his hand, though Stan's comrades recognised this as Harry's "signature move" and broke off their attack, instead summoning <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Voldemort</a> himself into the fray.<sup id="cite_ref-DH4_9-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH4-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Later that year, <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ronald Weasley</a> used his identity to trick a gang <a href="/wiki/Snatchers" title="Snatchers">Snatchers</a> he encountered somewhere in <a href="/wiki/England" title="England">England</a> to avoid them arresting him as a <a href="/wiki/Muggle-born" title="Muggle-born">Muggle-born</a>, which succeeded. Ron later used his identity again to fool <a href="/wiki/Scabior" title="Scabior">Scabior</a>'s gang of Snatchers in <a href="/wiki/March" title="March">March</a> <a href="/wiki/1998" title="1998">1998</a>, which unlike previously, was unsuccessful, as Scabior was not fooled as he knew Shunpike personally.<sup id="cite_ref-DH23_10-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH23-10">&#91;10&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>It is unknown what he proceeded to do, but he did know a <a href="/wiki/Snatchers" title="Snatchers">Snatcher</a> named <a href="/wiki/Scabior" title="Scabior">Scabior</a> and likely his master, <a href="/wiki/Fenrir_Greyback" title="Fenrir Greyback">Fenrir Greyback</a>. Shortly before the <a href="/wiki/Skirmish_at_Malfoy_Manor" title="Skirmish at Malfoy Manor">Skirmish at Malfoy Manor</a>, Scabior mentioned that Shunpike "put a bit of work our way".<sup id="cite_ref-DH23_10-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH23-10">&#91;10&#93;</a></sup> It is also unknown if Shunpike participated in the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a>.
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Later_life">Later life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Stanley_Shunpike?veaction=edit&amp;section=8" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Later life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Stanley_Shunpike?action=edit&amp;section=8" title="Edit section: Later life"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Following the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a>, it was reported that hundreds of people under the control of the Imperius curse were freed. If Stan survived to this point,<sup id="cite_ref-DH36_11-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH36-11">&#91;11&#93;</a></sup> he most likely returned to his job on the Knight Bus, though his fate is unknown.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Physical_description">Physical description</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Stanley_Shunpike?veaction=edit&amp;section=9" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Physical description">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Stanley_Shunpike?action=edit&amp;section=9" title="Edit section: Physical description"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>He was described as being "eighteen or nineteen at most" in <a href="/wiki/1993" title="1993">1993,</a> and as having "<i>large, protruding ears and quite a few pimples</i>". He wore a purple uniform as to go with the colour of the <a href="/wiki/Knight_Bus" title="Knight Bus">Knight Bus</a>. He had a fairly slovenly appearance and was somewhat more unkempt appearance than most wizards<sup id="cite_ref-POA3_3-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-POA3-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> (although not to the extent of the likes of <a href="/wiki/Morfin_Gaunt" title="Morfin Gaunt">Morfin Gaunt</a>).
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Personality_and_traits">Personality and traits</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Stanley_Shunpike?veaction=edit&amp;section=10" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Personality and traits">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Stanley_Shunpike?action=edit&amp;section=10" title="Edit section: Personality and traits"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<div class="quote">
<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire">"<i>I'm about to become the youngest ever Minister of Magic, I am.</i>"</span></dd>
<dd>&#8212;Shunpike trying to impress a group of <a href="/wiki/Veela" title="Veela">Veela</a><sup class="noprint"><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Goblet_of_Fire" title="Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire">[src]</a></sup></dd>
<dd></dd></dl></div><p>In spite of being a friendly and well-meaning wizard, Stan Shunpike was not very bright, as he failed to recognise the child celebrity <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>. But then again it might have simply been that he did not get time to properly examine Harry's scar, as Harry hid it immediately after Stan showed interest, which would have likely confirmed Harry's true identity.<sup id="cite_ref-POA3_3-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-POA3-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> He loved gossip and having inside information, a trait that later landed him in <a href="/wiki/Azkaban" title="Azkaban">Azkaban</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP11_7-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP11-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup> Like most wizards, he was openly disturbed when wizards spoke the name "<a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Voldemort</a>".<sup id="cite_ref-POA3_3-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-POA3-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Stan was very vain, a trait akin to <a href="/wiki/Gilderoy_Lockhart" title="Gilderoy Lockhart">Gilderoy Lockhart</a>, although less pronounced. Lockhart easily made women and girls swoon over him, whereas Stan had often resorted to boasting when in the presence of attractive young women in order to try and impress them. His slovenly appearance suggested that he resorted to toughness to bolster his perceived credibility with girls. The two most infamous examples of his vanity is his trying to chat up <a href="/wiki/Veela" title="Veela">Veela</a> that he would become the <a href="/wiki/Minister_for_Magic" title="Minister for Magic">Minister for Magic</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-GOF9_5-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF9-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> and falsely boasting in a pub that he knew of the <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a>' secret plans.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP11_7-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP11-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<div class="quote">
<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban">"<i>Them! Donâ listen properly, do they? Donâ look properly either. Never notice nuffink, they donâ.</i>"</span></dd>
<dd>&#8212;Stan's mockery of Muggles<sup class="noprint"><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Prisoner_of_Azkaban" title="Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban">[src]</a></sup></dd>
<dd></dd></dl></div>
<p>He seemed to disdain <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggles</a> to some degree, as when Harry asked him whether the Muggles are able to see and hear the <a href="/wiki/Knight_Bus" title="Knight Bus">Knight Bus</a>, Stan contemptuously remarked that Muggles do not listen and see properly, not to mention they never notice anything <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magical</a>, in his eyes.<sup id="cite_ref-POA3_3-10" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-POA3-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Stanley_Shunpike?veaction=edit&amp;section=11" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Stanley_Shunpike?action=edit&amp;section=11" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The name Stanley also belonged to one of <a href="/wiki/J._K._Rowling" title="J. K. Rowling">J. K. Rowling</a>'s grandfathers, with the other one being named Ernie, which was the name given to the driver of the <a href="/wiki/Knight_Bus" title="Knight Bus">Knight Bus</a>, <a href="/wiki/Ernest_Prang" title="Ernest Prang">Ernie Prang</a>. The name <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Shunpike" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Shunpike">Shunpike</a> may refer to "shunning a pike", i.e. swerving onto to a highway exit in order to avoid a tollbooth on the turnpike.
</p>
