<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Stairwell_Gargoyle?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Stairwell_Gargoyle?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Origins">Origins</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Stairwell_Gargoyle?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Origins">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Stairwell_Gargoyle?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Origins"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>The Stairwell Gargoyle was a stone gargoyle, described as large and ugly. Like all of the Hogwarts Gargoyles, it is likely that it was carved at some point in the past (before <a href="/wiki/1992" title="1992">1992</a>, at any rate) and was <a href="/wiki/Charm" title="Charm">enchanted</a> into sentience. The Gargoyle was tasked with guarding the <a href="/wiki/Griffin_Stairwell" title="Griffin Stairwell">stairway</a> to the <a href="/wiki/Headmaster%27s_Office" title="Headmaster&#39;s Office">Headmaster's Office</a>, only stepping aside if it heard the right <a href="/wiki/Password" title="Password">password</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-COS12_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COS12-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> The Gargoyle rarely spoke during its duties.
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="1996"><a href="/wiki/1996" title="1996">1996</a></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Stairwell_Gargoyle?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: 1996">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Stairwell_Gargoyle?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: 1996"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Late in the year, <a href="/wiki/Dolores_Umbridge" title="Dolores Umbridge">Dolores Umbridge</a> proclaimed herself the <a href="/wiki/Headmaster" title="Headmaster">Headmistress</a> of Hogwarts after <a href="/wiki/Albus_Dumbledore" title="Albus Dumbledore">Albus Dumbledore</a> was forced to into hiding. However, she was unable to penetrate the <a href="/wiki/Headmaster%27s_Office" title="Headmaster&#39;s Office">Headmaster's Office</a>, shut out of it by the Gargoyle (presumably backed by the <a href="/wiki/Sorting_Hat" title="Sorting Hat">Sorting Hat</a> and <a href="/wiki/Headmaster_portraits" title="Headmaster portraits">Headmaster portraits</a>).<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP28_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP28-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> While whether she made a serious attempt to force entry following her initial rejection is doubtful, the Stairwell Gargoyle apparently managed to hold his ground against Umbridge for the remainder of the year.
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="1998"><a href="/wiki/1998" title="1998">1998</a></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Stairwell_Gargoyle?veaction=edit&amp;section=4" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: 1998">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Stairwell_Gargoyle?action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: 1998"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>At the end of the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a>, <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>, <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ron Weasley</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione Granger</a> went to the Headmaster's office, and the Gargoyle (who had been a rather battered by the battle) let them in without a password, telling them to move along without worrying about itself.<sup id="cite_ref-DH33_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH33-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> It is unknown, though probable, whether the Gargoyle was then repaired and reinstated in its duties after the Battle ended for good.
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="2010s"><a href="/wiki/2010s" title="2010s">2010s</a></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Stairwell_Gargoyle?veaction=edit&amp;section=5" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: 2010s">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Stairwell_Gargoyle?action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: 2010s"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>During the 2010s, the Gargoyle appeared as a <a href="/wiki/Foundable" title="Foundable">Foundable</a> during the <a href="/wiki/Calamity" title="Calamity">Calamity</a>. Volunteer members of the <a href="/wiki/Statute_of_Secrecy_Task_Force" title="Statute of Secrecy Task Force">Statute of Secrecy Task Force</a> were able to return it to its original time.<sup id="cite_ref-WU_4-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-WU-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Stairwell_Gargoyle?veaction=edit&amp;section=6" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Stairwell_Gargoyle?action=edit&amp;section=6" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>From the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Chamber_of_Secrets_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets (film)">film adaptation</a> of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Chamber_of_Secrets" title="Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets">Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets</a></i> onwards, the Stairwell Gargoyle in the films was depicted as golden and resembling a <a href="/wiki/Griffin" title="Griffin">griffin</a> (possibly in allusion to the name <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor" title="Gryffindor">Gryffindor</a>, etymologically derived from the French for "golden griffin", or a simpler pun between the name Gryffindor and the Gargoyle being a "griffin door" to the Office).
<ul><li>This is all in apparent contradiction with the books' description of the Gargoyle as being made of stone, though it is possible that the gargoyle may be made of stone with a golden varnish.</li></ul></li>
<li>The Stairwell Gargoyle does not talk in the films, and, indeed, shows no signs of being sentient at all, as it is magically moved aside (instead of stepping away on its own) when the <a href="/wiki/Password" title="Password">password</a> is spoken, standing absolutely still.</li>
<li>The Stairwell Gargoyle is comparable to the <a href="/wiki/Fat_Lady" title="Fat Lady">Fat Lady</a> and <a href="/wiki/Eagle_door_knocker" title="Eagle door knocker">Eagle door knocker</a>, all three being sentient, <a href="/wiki/Charm" title="Charm">enchanted</a> objects of dubious origin who were tasked with restricting some areas of <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts</a>, unless their questions were answered correctly.</li></ul>
