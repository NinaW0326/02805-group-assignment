<b>Norvel Twonk</b>, <span class="template-c"><a href="/wiki/Order_of_Merlin" title="Order of Merlin">O.M. (First Class)</a></span> (<a href="/wiki/1888" class="mw-redirect" title="1888">1888</a> - <a href="/wiki/1957" title="1957">1957</a>) was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a>, who died saving a <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggle</a> child from a <a href="/wiki/Manticore" title="Manticore">Manticore</a>. 
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Norvel_Twonk?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Norvel_Twonk?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Twonk was posthumously awarded the <a href="/wiki/Order_of_Merlin" title="Order of Merlin">Order of Merlin</a>, First Class.<sup id="cite_ref-FWC_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-FWC-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> There was a portrait of Twonk hung at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Castle" title="Hogwarts Castle">Hogwarts Castle</a>, guarding a secret shortcut between the fifth-floor landing of the <a href="/wiki/Grand_Staircase" title="Grand Staircase">Grand Staircase</a> and the <a href="/wiki/Entrance_Hall" title="Entrance Hall">Entrance Hall</a> side room. The <a href="/wiki/Password" title="Password">password</a> required to gain access to said shortcut is "<i>Manticore</i>".<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
