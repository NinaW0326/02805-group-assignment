<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="History">History</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Giant_colony?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: History">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Giant_colony?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: History"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>In the past, the <a href="/wiki/Giant" title="Giant">giants</a> had around a hundred different tribes located around the world. However, by the end of the twentieth century, only one colony of giants remained, comprised of all the surviving giants and located in a remote mountainous region in Northern <a href="/wiki/Europe" title="Europe">Europe</a> (possibly somewhere in the Ural Mountains).<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP20_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP20-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>By <a href="/wiki/1995" title="1995">1995</a>, the tribe's population numbered around eighty, although that number was dwindling due to continued infighting, usually for food, mates, or old grudges remaining between members of previous tribes, but sometimes for even more trivial matters such as better sleeping places near the fireplace or simply for no reason other than to satisfy their lust for violence.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP20_1-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP20-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>The <a href="/wiki/Wizarding_world" title="Wizarding world">magical community</a>, the majority of whom detest and fear giants for their atrocities committed in the past, decided to leave the colony isolated and unsupervised, seeing no reason to attack them as long as they do not cause any trouble for them. <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggle</a> mountain-climbers who approach this colony would lose their lives, but such incidents have been written off as accidents.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP20_1-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP20-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>The <a href="/wiki/Gurg" title="Gurg">Gurg</a> of the giants at the time of the <a href="/wiki/Second_Wizarding_War" title="Second Wizarding War">Second Wizarding War</a> was <a href="/wiki/Karkus" title="Karkus">Karkus</a>, who was approached by <a href="/wiki/Rubeus_Hagrid" title="Rubeus Hagrid">Rubeus Hagrid</a> and <a href="/wiki/Olympe_Maxime" title="Olympe Maxime">Olympe Maxime</a>, acting as representatives of <a href="/wiki/Albus_Dumbledore" title="Albus Dumbledore">Albus Dumbledore</a> in an attempt to form an alliance. However, that same year Karkus was killed and replaced as Gurg by <a href="/wiki/Golgomath" title="Golgomath">Golgomath</a>, who allied the remaining giants with <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a> and the <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a> instead.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP20_1-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP20-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> On the <a href="/wiki/2_May" title="2 May">2 May</a> <a href="/wiki/1988" title="1988">1988</a>, the colony participated in the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a> along with Lord Voldemort and his Death Eaters.<sup id="cite_ref-DH31_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH31-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> It is unknown what happened to the colony after Voldemort was defeated and killed after the battle, although it is possible that all the participating giants were killed during the battle, thus rendering the colony extinct.
</p>
