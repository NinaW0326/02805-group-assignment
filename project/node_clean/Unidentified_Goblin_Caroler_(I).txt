This <b>caroler</b>&#160;was a <a href="/wiki/Goblin" title="Goblin">goblin</a> and a member of the <a href="/wiki/Hogsmeade_choir" title="Hogsmeade choir">choir</a> in <a href="/wiki/Hogsmeade" title="Hogsmeade">Hogsmeade</a> in <a href="/wiki/1993" title="1993">1993</a> during the <a href="/wiki/Christmas" title="Christmas">Christmas</a> holidays. During the singing he was pushed rudely by an angry <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> under his <a href="/wiki/Invisibility_cloak" title="Invisibility cloak">Invisibility cloak</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-POAF_1-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-POAF-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
