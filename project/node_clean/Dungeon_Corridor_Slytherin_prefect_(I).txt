<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Dungeon_Corridor_Slytherin_prefect_(I)?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Dungeon_Corridor_Slytherin_prefect_(I)?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>In his <a href="/wiki/Fifth_year" title="Fifth year">fifth year</a>, he was made a <a href="/wiki/Prefect" title="Prefect">prefect</a>, and, during the <a href="/wiki/1993%E2%80%931994_school_year" title="1993–1994 school year">1993–1994 school year</a>, he often patrolled the <a href="/wiki/Dungeon_Corridor" title="Dungeon Corridor">Dungeon Corridor</a> outside the <a href="/wiki/Potions_Classroom" title="Potions Classroom">Potions Dungeon</a> with <a href="/wiki/Dungeon_Corridor_Slytherin_prefect_(II)" title="Dungeon Corridor Slytherin prefect (II)">a fellow Slytherin prefect</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-POAG_3-11" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-POAG-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>He was in on <a href="/wiki/Draco_Malfoy" title="Draco Malfoy">Draco Malfoy</a>'s plan of stealing the <a href="/wiki/Quidditch" title="Quidditch">Quidditch</a> crate from <a href="/wiki/Rolanda_Hooch" title="Rolanda Hooch">Rolanda Hooch</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Rolanda_Hooch%27s_office" title="Rolanda Hooch&#39;s office">office</a> and switching it with one with <a href="/wiki/Jinx" title="Jinx">jinxed</a> balls. He informed Malfoy after a <a href="/wiki/Potions_(class)" title="Potions (class)">Potions</a> lesson that <a href="/wiki/Marcus_Flint" title="Marcus Flint">Marcus Flint</a> and <a href="/wiki/Peregrine_Derrick" title="Peregrine Derrick">Peregrine Derrick</a> that they had acted on his plan and hidden the real crate in the <a href="/wiki/Clock_Tower" title="Clock Tower">Clock Tower</a> <a href="/wiki/Clock_Tower_Courtyard" title="Clock Tower Courtyard">Courtyard</a>. They then proceeded to the <a href="/wiki/Quidditch_pitch" title="Quidditch pitch">Quidditch stadium</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-POAG_3-12" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-POAG-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Dungeon_Corridor_Slytherin_prefect_(I)?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Dungeon_Corridor_Slytherin_prefect_(I)?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>As Draco implied that the prefect was part of the <a href="/wiki/Slytherin_Quidditch_team" title="Slytherin Quidditch team">Slytherin Quidditch team</a>, as well as the fact that he knew about Draco's plan and that Flint and Derrick had acted upon it, it is possible that he was <a href="/wiki/Cassius_Warrington" title="Cassius Warrington">C. Warrington</a>, <a href="/wiki/Graham_Montague" title="Graham Montague">Graham Montague</a> or <a href="/wiki/Lucian_Bole" title="Lucian Bole">Lucian Bole</a>, whom he was similar in appearance to.</li>
<li>The prefect was voiced by <a href="/wiki/Lewis_MacLeod" title="Lewis MacLeod">Lewis MacLeod</a> in the video game adaptation of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Prisoner_of_Azkaban" title="Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban">Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban</a></i>.</li></ul>
