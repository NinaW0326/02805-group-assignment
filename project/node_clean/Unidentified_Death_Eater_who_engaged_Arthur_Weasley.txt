<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_Death_Eater_who_engaged_Arthur_Weasley?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_Death_Eater_who_engaged_Arthur_Weasley?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<dl><dd><div class="noprint"><i>Main article&#58; <a href="/wiki/Skirmish_at_the_Quad_battlements" title="Skirmish at the Quad battlements">Skirmish at the Quad battlements</a></i></div></dd></dl>
<figure class="thumb tleft show-info-icon" style="width: 182px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/6/65/Death_Eater.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20160312051045" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/6/65/Death_Eater.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/180?cb=20160312051045" decoding="async" width="180" height="77" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="Death Eater.jpg" data-image-key="Death_Eater.jpg" /></a> 	 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:Death_Eater.jpg" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption">The Death Eater killing another wizard by casting the Killing Curse on him, moments before his duel with Arthur Weasley</p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure>
<p>In <a href="/wiki/1998" title="1998">1998</a>, he was present during the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a>. After the <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a> managed to <a href="/wiki/Shield_penetration_spell" title="Shield penetration spell">break</a> the protective enchantments protecting <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Castle" title="Hogwarts Castle">the castle</a>, he blew up part of <a href="/wiki/The_Quad_battlements" title="The Quad battlements">the battlements</a> around <a href="/wiki/The_Quad" title="The Quad">The Quad</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Apparition" title="Apparition">Apparated</a> into the <a href="/wiki/Catwalks_by_the_Quad_battlements" title="Catwalks by the Quad battlements">catwalks by the Quad battlements</a>. There, he killed <a href="/wiki/Unidentified_wizard_killed_at_the_Quad_battlements" title="Unidentified wizard killed at the Quad battlements">a wizard</a> and <a href="/wiki/Duelling" title="Duelling">duelled</a> with <a href="/wiki/Arthur_Weasley" title="Arthur Weasley">Arthur Weasley</a>; he with a <a href="/wiki/Killing_Curse" title="Killing Curse">Killing Curse</a> and Arthur with either a <a href="/wiki/Stunning_Spell" title="Stunning Spell">Stunning Spell</a> or <a href="/wiki/Disarming_Charm" title="Disarming Charm">Disarming Charm</a>. The outcome of the duel was unknown, same as the fate of this individual; though given that Arthur survived the Battle of Hogwarts, it was likely that the individual was either killed, disarmed, defeated or had abandoned the Battle and Voldemort along with many other Death Eaters.<sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Magical_abilities_and_skills">Magical abilities and skills</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_Death_Eater_who_engaged_Arthur_Weasley?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_Death_Eater_who_engaged_Arthur_Weasley?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li><b><a href="/wiki/Dark_Arts" title="Dark Arts">Dark Arts</a></b>: Being a Death Eater, this <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> would have been very proficient in the dark arts, and was able to kill another wizard effortlessly with the <a href="/wiki/Killing_Curse" title="Killing Curse">Killing Curse</a>. He was highly likely to have been skilled in casting the other <a href="/wiki/Unforgivable_Curses" title="Unforgivable Curses">Unforgivable Curses</a> along with other <a href="/wiki/Dark_charm" title="Dark charm">dark charms</a> and spells.</li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Duelling" title="Duelling">Duelling</a></b>: This wizard was clearly a very skilled and accomplished duellist, as he fought against numerous other wizards and even Arthur Weasley in the Battle of Hogwarts.</li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Nonverbal_spell" title="Nonverbal spell">Nonverbal magic</a></b>: This wizard could cast a nonverbal killing curse, a mark of superior <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magical</a> skill given the difficulty of casting non-verbal spells and the Killing Curse itself.</li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Apparition" title="Apparition">Apparition</a></b>: This wizard was capable of apparating.</li></ul>
