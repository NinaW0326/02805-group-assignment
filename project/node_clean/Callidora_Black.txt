<b>Callidora Longbottom</b> (née <b>Black</b>) (b. <a href="/wiki/1915" title="1915">1915</a>) was a <a href="/wiki/Pure-blood" title="Pure-blood">pure-blood</a> witch, the eldest daughter of <a href="/wiki/Arcturus_Black_II" title="Arcturus Black II">Arcturus Black II</a> and <a href="/wiki/Lysandra_Yaxley" title="Lysandra Yaxley">Lysandra Yaxley</a> and sister of <a href="/wiki/Charis_Black" title="Charis Black">Charis</a> and <a href="/wiki/Cedrella_Black" title="Cedrella Black">Cedrella</a>. She married <a href="/wiki/Harfang_Longbottom" title="Harfang Longbottom">Harfang Longbottom</a>, and the couple had two children, a <a href="/wiki/Harfang_Longbottom%27s_son" title="Harfang Longbottom&#39;s son">son</a> and <a href="/wiki/Harfang_Longbottom%27s_daughter" title="Harfang Longbottom&#39;s daughter">a daughter</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-BFT_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-BFT-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> She is related to the <a href="/wiki/Crouch_family" title="Crouch family">Crouch</a> and <a href="/wiki/Weasley_family" title="Weasley family">Weasley</a> families through her sisters, and may be related to <a href="/wiki/Alice_Longbottom" title="Alice Longbottom">Alice</a> and <a href="/wiki/Frank_Longbottom" title="Frank Longbottom">Frank Longbottom</a> and their son <a href="/wiki/Neville_Longbottom" title="Neville Longbottom">Neville</a>.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Callidora_Black?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Callidora_Black?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li><i>Callidora</i> means "gift of beauty" in Greek.<sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> It is also the name of a <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Setiostoma_callidora" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Setiostoma callidora">species of sedge moths</a>.</li></ul>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Callidora_Black?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Callidora_Black?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>Callidora’s sister <a href="/wiki/Cedrella_Black" title="Cedrella Black">Cedrella</a> was disowned by their <a href="/wiki/House_of_Black" title="House of Black">family</a> for marrying <a href="/wiki/Septimus_Weasley" title="Septimus Weasley">Septimus Weasley</a>, a "<a href="/wiki/Blood_traitor" title="Blood traitor">blood traitor</a>". Given that Callidora was not burned off the <a href="/wiki/House_of_Black" title="House of Black">Black family tree</a> tapestry for her marriage, this suggests that the <a href="/wiki/Longbottom_family" title="Longbottom family">Longbottoms</a> are considered to be a more "respectable" pure-blood family than <a href="/wiki/Weasley_family" title="Weasley family">Weasleys</a>.</li>
<li>Since Callidora and <a href="/wiki/Cassiopeia_Black" title="Cassiopeia Black">Cassiopeia</a> were born in the same year, it is most likely that they were classmates at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a>.</li>
<li>Callidora's and Elladora’s pictures on the family tree in the film are the same.</li>
<li>Callidora was not featured on the Longbottom family tree shown on Pottermore.</li></ul>
