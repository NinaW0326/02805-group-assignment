<b>Martha Steward </b>was a <a href="/wiki/Great_Britain" title="Great Britain">British</a> <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggle</a>. She was the mother of <a href="/wiki/James_Steward" title="James Steward">James Steward</a>, who co-founded <a href="/wiki/Ilvermorny_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Ilvermorny School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Ilvermorny School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> with his wife, <a href="/wiki/Isolt_Sayre" title="Isolt Sayre">Isolt Sayre</a>.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Martha_Steward?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Martha_Steward?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Martha and her husband had at least one child, <a href="/wiki/James_Steward" title="James Steward">James Steward</a>. They came from <a href="/wiki/Great_Britain" title="Great Britain">Britain</a>. James left and went with the Plymouth settlement to the New World where he married a witch, <a href="/wiki/Isolt_Sayre" title="Isolt Sayre">Isolt Sayre</a> and had two daughters, <a href="/wiki/Martha_Steward_II" title="Martha Steward II">Martha Steward</a> and <a href="/wiki/Rionach_Steward" title="Rionach Steward">Rionach Steward</a>. Martha died sometime before 1632, since her granddaughter was said to have been named after her late grandmother.<sup id="cite_ref-Pottermore_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Pottermore-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
