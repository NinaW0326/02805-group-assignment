<b>Abraham Potter</b> was an <a href="/wiki/United_States_of_America" title="United States of America">American</a> <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> and one of the <a href="/wiki/Auror#Twelve_originals" title="Auror">original twelve</a> <a href="/wiki/Auror" title="Auror">Aurors</a> in the <a href="/wiki/United_States_of_America" title="United States of America">United States of America</a>.
</p><p>Abraham was also a distant relative of the famous&#160;<a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>,&#160;a fact which would be uncovered by eager genealogists centuries later.<sup id="cite_ref-PM_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PM-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>He died before reaching "old age."<sup id="cite_ref-PM_2-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PM-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Since the family name Potter originated from Linfred of Stinchcombe's nickname 'The Potterer', Abraham must be a descendant of his.
</p>
