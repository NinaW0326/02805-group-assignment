</p><p>The <b>giant rat</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Rat" title="Rat">rat</a> that lived in the cellar of the <a href="/wiki/Leaky_Cauldron" title="Leaky Cauldron">Leaky Cauldron</a>. Once a normal rat, during the summer of <a href="/wiki/1993" title="1993">1993</a>, it consumed a large quantity of <a href="/wiki/Rat_tonic" title="Rat tonic">rat tonic</a> from <a href="/wiki/Tom" title="Tom">Tom</a>'s stores. This over-consumption caused it to grow to an immense size.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
