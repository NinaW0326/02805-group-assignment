<b>Philbert Chivers</b> was an eccentric <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> from <a href="/wiki/London" title="London">London</a> who believed that <a href="/wiki/Nargle" title="Nargle">Nargles</a> were stealing from the shops on <a href="/wiki/Diagon_Alley" title="Diagon Alley">Diagon Alley</a> and hiding in <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggle</a> sewers.<sup id="cite_ref-FBC_1-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-FBC-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Philbert_Chivers?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Philbert_Chivers?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>In a beast-related incident investigated by <a href="/wiki/Mathilda_Grimblehawk" title="Mathilda Grimblehawk">Mathilda Grimblehawk</a> and her <a href="/wiki/Mathilda_Grimblehawk%27s_partner" title="Mathilda Grimblehawk&#39;s partner">partner</a> from the <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">Ministry of Magic</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Beast_Division" title="Beast Division">Beast Division</a>, Chivers was reported to be missing. He was later found unconscious in the London sewers having been knocked out by falling bricks. He was then transported to <a href="/wiki/St_Mungo%27s_Hospital_for_Magical_Maladies_and_Injuries" title="St Mungo&#39;s Hospital for Magical Maladies and Injuries">St Mungo's Hospital for Magical Maladies and Injuries</a> and treated by <a href="/wiki/Healer" title="Healer">Healer</a> <a href="/wiki/Omar_Abasi" title="Omar Abasi">Omar Abasi</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-FBC_1-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-FBC-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Chivers believed that the <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">British Ministry of Magic</a> was involved with covering up the existence of Nargles. He went to <a href="/wiki/Slug_%26_Jiggers_Apothecary" title="Slug &amp; Jiggers Apothecary">Slug &amp; Jiggers Apothecary</a> hoping to purchase "<a href="/wiki/Poison" title="Poison">Narglecide</a>", so <a href="/wiki/Humphrey_Balsamo" title="Humphrey Balsamo">Humphrey Balsamo</a> relabelled some dud <a href="/wiki/Potion" title="Potion">potions</a>, and gave them to him free. These potions included an unstable <a href="/wiki/Swelling_Solution" title="Swelling Solution">Swelling Solution</a> which was consumed by the <a href="/wiki/Beast" title="Beast">beast</a> dwelling in the sewers.<sup id="cite_ref-FBC_1-10" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-FBC-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
