<b>Derek</b><sup id="cite_ref-CRF_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-CRF-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> who attended <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> and was a member of <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor" title="Gryffindor">Gryffindor</a> house, who ran the <a href="/wiki/Wizard_Card_Collectors%27_Club" title="Wizard Card Collectors&#39; Club">Wizard Card Collectors' Club</a> during the <a href="/wiki/1990s" title="1990s">1990s</a>.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Derek_(Wizard_Card_Collectors%27_Club)?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Derek_(Wizard_Card_Collectors%27_Club)?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="1991-1992_school_year"><a href="/wiki/1991-1992_school_year" class="mw-redirect" title="1991-1992 school year">1991-1992 school year</a></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Derek_(Wizard_Card_Collectors%27_Club)?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: 1991-1992 school year">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Derek_(Wizard_Card_Collectors%27_Club)?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: 1991-1992 school year"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>This individual first met <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> at <a href="/wiki/Diagon_Alley" title="Diagon Alley">Diagon Alley</a> during the latter's first visit to the alley, in <a href="/wiki/1991" title="1991">1991</a>. There, he offered Harry a <a href="/wiki/Folio_Universitas" title="Folio Universitas">Folio Magi</a> and some card combinations. He was very proud to help Harry. When the <a href="/wiki/1991%E2%80%931992_school_year" title="1991–1992 school year">school year</a> started, he could be seen at the <a href="/wiki/Classroom_5B" title="Classroom 5B">Classroom 5B</a> where he was in charge of a game of pairs with <a href="/wiki/Chocolate_Frog_Card" title="Chocolate Frog Card">Chocolate Frog Cards</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span id="1992–1993_school_year"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="1992.E2.80.931993_school_year"><a href="/wiki/1992%E2%80%931993_school_year" title="1992–1993 school year">1992–1993 school year</a></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Derek_(Wizard_Card_Collectors%27_Club)?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: 1992–1993 school year">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Derek_(Wizard_Card_Collectors%27_Club)?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: 1992–1993 school year"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>In <a href="/wiki/1992" title="1992">1992</a> he met <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> again at <a href="/wiki/Diagon_Alley" title="Diagon Alley">Diagon Alley</a>, and talked to him about the new card combinations. In the <a href="/wiki/1992%E2%80%931993_school_year" title="1992–1993 school year">school year</a>, he maintained his post at the Club. This year, he was in charge of giving prizes to students who possessed a certain set of cards.<sup id="cite_ref-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span id="1993–1994_school_year"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="1993.E2.80.931994_school_year"><a href="/wiki/1993%E2%80%931994_school_year" title="1993–1994 school year">1993–1994 school year</a></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Derek_(Wizard_Card_Collectors%27_Club)?veaction=edit&amp;section=4" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: 1993–1994 school year">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Derek_(Wizard_Card_Collectors%27_Club)?action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: 1993–1994 school year"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>In the <a href="/wiki/1993%E2%80%931994_school_year" title="1993–1994 school year">1993–1994 school year</a>, this student could be seen by the Club yet again. He maintained the award-giving game he had ran the previous year.<sup id="cite_ref-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Derek_(Wizard_Card_Collectors%27_Club)?veaction=edit&amp;section=5" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Derek_(Wizard_Card_Collectors%27_Club)?action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>

<ul><li>Derek is never referred to by name in the normal gameplay of all the video games he makes an appearance. The line of dialogue that mentions his name was intended to be used in the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Chamber_of_Secrets_(video_game)" title="Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets (video game)">Game Boy Color version</a> of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Chamber_of_Secrets" title="Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets">Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets</a></i>. However, this piece of dialogue was cut and went unused, though it still appears in the game's ROM.<sup id="cite_ref-CRF_3-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-CRF-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup></li></ul>
