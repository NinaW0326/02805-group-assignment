<p><b>Graham Montague</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> and <a href="/wiki/Slytherin" title="Slytherin">Slytherin</a> student who attended <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> in the <a href="/wiki/1990s" title="1990s">1990s</a>. He played <a href="/wiki/Chaser" title="Chaser">Chaser</a> on the Slytherin <a href="/wiki/Slytherin_Quidditch_team" title="Slytherin Quidditch team">Quidditch team</a> from <a href="/wiki/1993" title="1993">1993</a> to <a href="/wiki/1996" title="1996">1996</a> and was a member of the <a href="/wiki/Inquisitorial_Squad" title="Inquisitorial Squad">Inquisitorial Squad</a>. Montague was heavily built and had large, hairy forearms.<sup id="cite_ref-ootp19_4-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-ootp19-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Graham_Montague?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Graham_Montague?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="1993-1994_Quidditch_season">1993-1994 Quidditch season</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Graham_Montague?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: 1993-1994 Quidditch season">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Graham_Montague?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: 1993-1994 Quidditch season"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>While it is uncertain whether Montague was on the Slytherin Quidditch Team prior, Montague played in the final match between Slytherin and <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor" title="Gryffindor">Gryffindor</a> in <a href="/wiki/1994" title="1994">1994</a>. When Gryffindor Chaser <a href="/wiki/Katie_Bell" title="Katie Bell">Katie Bell</a> got possession of the <a href="/wiki/Quaffle" title="Quaffle">Quaffle</a>, Montague flew in front of her, but, instead of taking the ball, he grabbed her head. Although Katie was able to keep from falling off her <a href="/wiki/Broomstick" title="Broomstick">broom</a>, she dropped the Quaffle. <a href="/wiki/Rolanda_Hooch" title="Rolanda Hooch">Madam Hooch</a> scolded Montague and awarded a penalty to Katie.<sup id="cite_ref-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="1995-1996_Quidditch_season">1995-1996 Quidditch season</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Graham_Montague?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: 1995-1996 Quidditch season">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Graham_Montague?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: 1995-1996 Quidditch season"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>At the beginning of the <a href="/wiki/1995%E2%80%931996_school_year" title="1995–1996 school year">1995-1996</a> <a href="/wiki/Quidditch" title="Quidditch">Quidditch</a> season, Montague was made <a href="/wiki/Quidditch_Captain#Hogwarts_Quidditch_Captains" title="Quidditch Captain">Captain</a> of the Slytherin Quidditch team,<sup id="cite_ref-ootp19_4-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-ootp19-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> replacing <a href="/wiki/Marcus_Flint" title="Marcus Flint">Marcus Flint</a>. He chose <a href="/wiki/Vincent_Crabbe" title="Vincent Crabbe">Vincent Crabbe</a> and <a href="/wiki/Gregory_Goyle" title="Gregory Goyle">Gregory Goyle</a> to be the team's new <a href="/wiki/Beater" title="Beater">Beaters</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-ootp19_4-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-ootp19-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Vanishing_Cabinet_incident">Vanishing Cabinet incident</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Graham_Montague?veaction=edit&amp;section=4" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Vanishing Cabinet incident">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Graham_Montague?action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Vanishing Cabinet incident"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Montague joined the <a href="/wiki/Inquisitorial_Squad" title="Inquisitorial Squad">Inquisitorial Squad</a> in April of 1996. During morning break on his first day as a member, he tried to take <a href="/wiki/House_points" title="House points">House points</a> from <a href="/wiki/Fred_Weasley" title="Fred Weasley">Fred</a> and <a href="/wiki/George_Weasley" title="George Weasley">George Weasley</a>, but the twins shoved him into the <a href="/wiki/Vanishing_Cabinet" title="Vanishing Cabinet">Vanishing Cabinet</a> before he could finish speaking.<sup id="cite_ref-ootp28_6-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-ootp28-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup> While trapped inside, Montague could occasionally hear goings-on at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts</a>, as well as at <a href="/wiki/Borgin_and_Burkes" title="Borgin and Burkes">Borgin and Burkes</a>, where the Cabinet's counterpart was located.<sup id="cite_ref-hbp27_7-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-hbp27-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup> He managed to <a href="/wiki/Apparition" title="Apparition">Apparate</a> out the next day, despite never having obtained an <a href="/wiki/Apparition_(class)" title="Apparition (class)">Apparition licence</a>, but ended up lodged inside a toilet on the fourth floor.<sup id="cite_ref-ootp28_6-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-ootp28-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-hbp27_7-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-hbp27-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<figure class="thumb tleft show-info-icon" style="width: 106px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/4/49/MontagueVanishingCabinet.png/revision/latest?cb=20160503153057" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/4/49/MontagueVanishingCabinet.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/104?cb=20160503153057" decoding="async" width="104" height="236" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="MontagueVanishingCabinet.png" data-image-key="MontagueVanishingCabinet.png" /></a> 	 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:MontagueVanishingCabinet.png" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption"><a href="/wiki/Fred_Weasley" title="Fred Weasley">Fred</a> and <a href="/wiki/George_Weasley" title="George Weasley">George Weasley</a> push Montague head-first into the Vanishing Cabinet</p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure>
<p>The Apparition nearly proved fatal to Montague.<sup id="cite_ref-hbp27_7-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-hbp27-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup> He was left in a disoriented state for weeks afterwards.<sup id="cite_ref-ootp30_8-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-ootp30-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup> In May, his parents, <a href="/wiki/Graham_Montague%27s_parents" title="Graham Montague&#39;s parents">Mr and Mrs Montague</a>, came to the school, looking furious.<sup id="cite_ref-ootp30_8-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-ootp30-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>, <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ron Weasley</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione Granger</a> watched the couple arrive, and Hermione suggested that they should tell <a href="/wiki/Poppy_Pomfrey" title="Poppy Pomfrey">Madam Pomfrey</a> what had happened to Montague, in case it would help her treat him.<sup id="cite_ref-ootp30_8-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-ootp30-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup> However, both Ron and Harry dismissed this idea,<sup id="cite_ref-ootp30_8-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-ootp30-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup> so Hermione apparently let the matter drop. Several weeks later, during the <a href="/wiki/Ordinary_Wizarding_Level" title="Ordinary Wizarding Level">O.W.L.</a> exams in June, Harry went to the <a href="/wiki/Hospital_Wing" title="Hospital Wing">Hospital Wing</a>, where he saw Madam Pomfrey spoon-feeding a vivid blue <a href="/wiki/Potion" title="Potion">potion</a> to Montague.<sup id="cite_ref-ootp30_8-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-ootp30-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Montague eventually told his story to others, including <a href="/wiki/Draco_Malfoy" title="Draco Malfoy">Draco Malfoy</a>, who was the only one to reach the conclusion that there was a connection between the Cabinet at Hogwarts and the Cabinet at <a href="/wiki/Borgin_and_Burkes" title="Borgin and Burkes">Borgin and Burkes</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-hbp27_7-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-hbp27-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup> This knowledge gave Draco the means to allow <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a> to enter the school the following year.<sup id="cite_ref-hbp27_7-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-hbp27-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Physical_appearance">Physical appearance</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Graham_Montague?veaction=edit&amp;section=5" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Physical appearance">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Graham_Montague?action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Physical appearance"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Montague was described as being large and muscular, even more so than Crabbe and Goyle. He was also described as having hairy arms.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Personality_and_traits">Personality and traits</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Graham_Montague?veaction=edit&amp;section=6" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Personality and traits">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Graham_Montague?action=edit&amp;section=6" title="Edit section: Personality and traits"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Being the <a href="/wiki/Captain" title="Captain">Captain</a> and <a href="/wiki/Chaser" title="Chaser">Chaser</a> for the <a href="/wiki/Slytherin_Quidditch_team" title="Slytherin Quidditch team">Slytherin Quidditch team</a>, he was presumably rather ambitious and determined, being a member of <a href="/wiki/Slytherin" title="Slytherin">Slytherin</a>. He was most likely rather mean-spirited, having became a member of the short-lived <a href="/wiki/Inquisitorial_Squad" title="Inquisitorial Squad">Inquisitorial Squad</a>, which persecuted <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor" title="Gryffindor">Gryffindor</a>, <a href="/wiki/Hufflepuff" title="Hufflepuff">Hufflepuff</a> and <a href="/wiki/Ravenclaw" title="Ravenclaw">Ravenclaw</a> students for petty and spiteful reasons. He once tried to deduct house points from <a href="/wiki/Fred_and_George_Weasley" title="Fred and George Weasley">Fred and George Weasley</a> with ill-intent, which proved to be his downfall considering what they did to him as a result. He also showed poor sportsmanship whilst playing <a href="/wiki/Quidditch" title="Quidditch">Quidditch</a>, being inclined to commit fouls when he grabbed <a href="/wiki/Katie_Bell" title="Katie Bell">Katie Bell</a>'s head during the <a href="/wiki/1993%E2%80%931994_school_year" title="1993–1994 school year">1993–1994 school year</a> Quidditch season and being penalised by <a href="/wiki/Rolanda_Hooch" title="Rolanda Hooch">Madam Hooch</a> as punishment. 
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Magical_abilities_and_skills">Magical abilities and skills</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Graham_Montague?veaction=edit&amp;section=7" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Graham_Montague?action=edit&amp;section=7" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li><b><a href="/wiki/Apparition" title="Apparition">Apparition</a></b>: Montague was capable of apparating, despite not having not received his formal <a href="/wiki/Apparition_(class)" title="Apparition (class)">Apparition licence</a>, as he somehow managed to apparate out the <a href="/wiki/Vanishing_Cabinet" title="Vanishing Cabinet">Vanishing Cabinet</a> that <a href="/wiki/Fred_and_George_Weasley" title="Fred and George Weasley">Fred and George Weasley</a> shoved and trapped him inside one, quite an impressive <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magical</a> feat.</li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Flight" title="Flight">Flying</a></b>: Having been both the <a href="/wiki/Captain" title="Captain">Captain</a> and <a href="/wiki/Chaser" title="Chaser">Chaser</a> for the <a href="/wiki/Slytherin_Quidditch_team" title="Slytherin Quidditch team">Slytherin Quidditch team</a> whilst as a Hogwarts student, Montague was obviously a very accomplished and talented <a href="/wiki/Broomstick" title="Broomstick">broomstick</a> flyer and <a href="/wiki/Quidditch" title="Quidditch">Quidditch</a> player, despite his unsporting behaviour displayed on the Quidditch pitch.</li></ul>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Graham_Montague?veaction=edit&amp;section=8" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Graham_Montague?action=edit&amp;section=8" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The name "Graham" is from a Scottish surname which was originally derived from an English place name which probably meant "gravelly homestead" in Old English. The surname was first taken to Scotland in the 12th century by the Norman baron William de Graham. Montague is also the surname of Romeo in Shakespeare's famous play <i>Romeo and Juliet.</i>
</p>
