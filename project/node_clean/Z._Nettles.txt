<p><b>Madam Z. Nettles</b> was a <a href="/wiki/England" title="England">English</a> <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch</a> who lived in <a href="/wiki/Topsham" title="Topsham">Topsham</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-COS8_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COS8-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Z._Nettles?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Z._Nettles?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>She was apparently a mediocre witch, and could not remember <a href="/wiki/Incantation" title="Incantation">incantations</a> or mix <a href="/wiki/Potion" title="Potion">potions</a>. She took a <a href="/wiki/Kwikspell" title="Kwikspell">Kwikspell</a> course to improve her <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magical</a> skills. According to a testimonial she wrote for the company, the course helped her to improve her skills, as she became the centre of attention at parties and her friends begged her for the recipe of her <a href="/wiki/Scintillation_Solution" title="Scintillation Solution">Scintillation Solution</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-COS8_1-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COS8-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
