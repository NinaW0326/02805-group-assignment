<b>Orion Amari</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> who attended <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> in the <a href="/wiki/1980s" title="1980s">1980s</a>, during which he became a <a href="/wiki/Chaser" title="Chaser">Chaser</a> and eventually also the <a href="/wiki/Quidditch_Captain" title="Quidditch Captain">Captain</a> of the <a href="/wiki/Quidditch_team" title="Quidditch team">Quidditch team</a> of his house; playing alongside <a href="/wiki/Skye_Parkin" title="Skye Parkin">Skye Parkin</a> and <a href="/wiki/Jacob%27s_sibling" title="Jacob&#39;s sibling">Jacob's sibling</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-HM_3-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HM-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<figure class="thumb tleft show-info-icon" style="width: 182px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/5/58/Orion_Amari_HM_001.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20200612190445" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/5/58/Orion_Amari_HM_001.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/180?cb=20200612190445" decoding="async" width="180" height="180" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="Orion Amari HM 001.jpg" data-image-key="Orion_Amari_HM_001.jpg" /></a> 	 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:Orion_Amari_HM_001.jpg" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption">Orion in the <a href="/wiki/Quidditch_Changing_Rooms" title="Quidditch Changing Rooms">Quidditch Changing Rooms</a></p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure>
<p>Orion was an orphan, but he considered his <a href="/wiki/Quidditch" title="Quidditch">Quidditch</a> team to be his family.
</p><p>During his time at Hogwarts, Orion was <a href="/wiki/Sorting_ceremony" title="Sorting ceremony">Sorted</a> into an unknown house. At some point during his time at school, he became a&#160;<a href="/wiki/Chaser" title="Chaser">Chaser</a> and the <a href="/wiki/Captain" title="Captain">Captain</a>&#160;of an unknown&#160;Hogwarts <a href="/wiki/Quidditch_team" title="Quidditch team">Quidditch team</a>.
</p><p><br />
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Orion_Amari?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Orion_Amari?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>He is in whichever house the player chose for <a href="/wiki/Jacob%27s_sibling" title="Jacob&#39;s sibling">Jacob's sibling</a>.</li>
<li>His year is not shown in the friends section, similar to <a href="/wiki/Skye_Parkin" title="Skye Parkin">Skye Parkin</a> and <a href="/wiki/Murphy_McNully" title="Murphy McNully">Murphy McNully</a>.</li>
<li>His name could be based on Orion's Belt, which may explain his personality being spiritually aligned.</li>
<li>He labels his pep talks as ‘vivications’ &#8211; a play on Rastafarianism’s livication terminology, which means dedication without connotation of death.</li></ul>
