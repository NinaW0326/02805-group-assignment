He is a selected <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">student</a> from the Slytherin house to represent the house in the <a href="/wiki/Founder_Duels" title="Founder Duels">Founder Duels</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-COSG_3-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COSG-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>

<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_life">Early life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Slytherin_Wizard_Duelling_Champion?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Early life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Slytherin_Wizard_Duelling_Champion?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Early life"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>This boy attended <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> in the <a href="/wiki/1990s" title="1990s">1990s</a> and was <a href="/wiki/Sorting_ceremony" title="Sorting ceremony">sorted</a> into <a href="/wiki/Slytherin" title="Slytherin">Slytherin House</a>. His <a href="/wiki/Head_of_House" title="Head of House">Head of House</a> was <a href="/wiki/Severus_Snape" title="Severus Snape">Severus Snape</a>, the <a href="/wiki/Potions_Master" title="Potions Master">Potions Master</a>, and he shared a dormitory with four boys in his year in the <a href="/wiki/Slytherin_Dungeon" title="Slytherin Dungeon">Slytherin Dungeon</a>, which is located below ground level.<sup id="cite_ref-COSG_3-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COSG-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span id="1992–1993_school_year"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="1992.E2.80.931993_school_year"><a href="/wiki/1992%E2%80%931993_school_year" title="1992–1993 school year">1992–1993 school year</a></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Slytherin_Wizard_Duelling_Champion?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: 1992–1993 school year">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Slytherin_Wizard_Duelling_Champion?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: 1992–1993 school year"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>He apparently excelled at <a href="/wiki/Defence_Against_the_Dark_Arts" title="Defence Against the Dark Arts">Defence Against the Dark Arts</a> and became the <a href="/wiki/Slytherin" title="Slytherin">Slytherin</a> Duelling Champion sometime in or prior to <a href="/wiki/1992" title="1992">1992</a>. When <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> defeated him, he gave him <a href="/wiki/Salazar_Slytherin" title="Salazar Slytherin">Salazar Slytherin</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Chocolate_Frog_Card" title="Chocolate Frog Card">card</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-COSG_3-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COSG-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
