This <b><a href="/wiki/Ghoul" title="Ghoul">ghoul</a></b> was, at one point, living in one of <a href="/wiki/12_Grimmauld_Place" title="12 Grimmauld Place">12 Grimmauld Place</a>'s bathrooms. Though most ghouls are simple-minded and peaceful, this one was described as "murderous" and was considerably more of a threat than normal. During their cleansing of the house in the summer of <a href="/wiki/1995" title="1995">1995</a>, <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>, <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione Granger</a>, <a href="/wiki/Sirius_Black" title="Sirius Black">Sirius Black</a> and <a href="/wiki/Weasley_family" title="Weasley family">the Weasleys</a> came across this ghoul. They managed to get rid of it with the assistance of <a href="/wiki/Nymphadora_Tonks" title="Nymphadora Tonks">Nymphadora Tonks</a>, who was visiting that day.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP6_1-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP6-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
