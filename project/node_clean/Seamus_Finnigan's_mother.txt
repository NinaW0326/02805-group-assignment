<p><b>Mrs Finnigan</b> (fl. <a href="/wiki/1980" title="1980">1980</a>-<a href="/wiki/1997" title="1997">1997</a>) was an <a href="/wiki/Ireland" title="Ireland">Irish</a><sup id="cite_ref-Irish_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Irish-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch</a>.
</p><p>She married <a href="/wiki/Seamus_Finnigan%27s_father" title="Seamus Finnigan&#39;s father">Mr Finnigan</a>, a <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggle</a>, before she told him she was a witch, and it was a "bit of a nasty shock for him" when she told him after the wedding.<sup id="cite_ref-Half_4-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Half-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> The two had a son named <a href="/wiki/Seamus_Finnigan" title="Seamus Finnigan">Seamus</a>.
</p>

<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_life">Early life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Seamus_Finnigan%27s_mother?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Early life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Seamus_Finnigan%27s_mother?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Early life"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<div class="quote">
<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;Harry Potter and the Philosopher&#39;s Stone">"<i>I'm half-and-half. Me dad's a Muggle. Mam didn't tell him she was a witch 'til after they were married. Bit of a nasty shock for him.</i>"</span></dd>
<dd>&#8212;Seamus<sup class="noprint"><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Philosopher%27s_Stone" title="Harry Potter and the Philosopher&#39;s Stone">[src]</a></sup></dd>
<dd></dd></dl></div><p>Being <a href="/wiki/Ireland" title="Ireland">Irish</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-Irish_3-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Irish-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> she presumably attended <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a>, likely being <a href="/wiki/Sorting_ceremony" title="Sorting ceremony">sorted</a> into <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor" title="Gryffindor">Gryffindor</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-House_2-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-House-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> She was a fan of <a href="/wiki/Quidditch" title="Quidditch">Quidditch</a>, supporting the <a href="/wiki/Irish_National_Quidditch_team" title="Irish National Quidditch team">Irish National Quidditch team</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-GoF7_5-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GoF7-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>She met and fell in love with <a href="/wiki/Seamus_Finnigan%27s_father" title="Seamus Finnigan&#39;s father">Mr Finnigan</a>, a <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggle</a>, marrying him without telling him that she was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch</a>. At some point after the Finnigans' wedding, she told him that she was a witch, which proved a "nasty shock" to him.<sup id="cite_ref-Half_4-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Half-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> They had a <a href="/wiki/Half-blood" title="Half-blood">half-blood</a> son named <a href="/wiki/Seamus_Finnigan" title="Seamus Finnigan">Seamus</a>, who went on to study at Hogwarts.
</p><p>Mrs Finnigan told Seamus that <a href="/wiki/Ghoul" title="Ghoul">ghouls</a> were scarier than they were dangerous.<sup id="cite_ref-Ghoul_6-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Ghoul-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="1994">1994</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Seamus_Finnigan%27s_mother?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: 1994">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Seamus_Finnigan%27s_mother?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: 1994"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<div class="quote">
<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix"><b>Ron Weasley</b>: "<i>Harry wouldn't do that — we met your mother, we liked her...</i>"</span></dd>
<dd><b>Harry Potter</b>: "<i>That's before she started believing every word the stinking Daily Prophet writes about me!</i>"</dd>
<dd>&#8212; <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ron Weasley</a> and <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> on Mrs Finnigan<sup class="noprint"><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Order_of_the_Phoenix" title="Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix">[src]</a></sup></dd></dl>
</div><p>Mrs Finnigan took Seamus and his friend, <a href="/wiki/Dean_Thomas" title="Dean Thomas">Dean Thomas</a>, to <a href="/wiki/Roberts" title="Roberts">Roberts</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Quidditch_World_Cup_Campsite" title="Quidditch World Cup Campsite">campsite</a> to watch the <a href="/wiki/1994_Quidditch_World_Cup" title="1994 Quidditch World Cup">1994 Quidditch World Cup</a> final between <a href="/wiki/Irish_National_Quidditch_team" title="Irish National Quidditch team">Ireland</a> and <a href="/wiki/Bulgarian_National_Quidditch_team" title="Bulgarian National Quidditch team">Bulgaria</a>. The Finnigans' tent was covered in shamrocks, and located near several similarly-decorated ones.<sup id="cite_ref-Tents_7-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Tents-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup> The <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">Ministry of Magic</a> was displeased with the decoration,<sup id="cite_ref-Ministry_8-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Ministry-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup> but Mrs Finnigan believed that they had the right to show their colours, pointing out the <a href="/wiki/Viktor_Krum" title="Viktor Krum">Viktor Krum</a> posters being hung by the supporters of <a href="/wiki/Bulgaria" title="Bulgaria">Bulgaria</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-Irish_3-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Irish-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>At the campsite, Mrs Finnigan met <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>, <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ron Weasley</a> and <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione Granger</a>, though it is unknown if she knew who Harry was at the time. She asked the trio if they were supporting Ireland, eyeing them beadily before their confirmation.
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="1995">1995</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Seamus_Finnigan%27s_mother?veaction=edit&amp;section=4" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: 1995">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Seamus_Finnigan%27s_mother?action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: 1995"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<div class="quote">
<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix"><b>Harry Potter</b>: "<i>Just read the Daily Prophet like your mother, why don’t you? That’ll tell you all you need to know</i>"</span></dd>
<dd><b>Seamus Finnigan</b>: "<i>Don’t you have a go at my mother.</i>"</dd>
<dd><b>Harry Potter</b>: "<i>I’ll have a go at anyone who calls me a liar,</i>"</dd>
<dd>&#8212; Seamus and <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> arguing about Mrs Finnigan<sup class="noprint"><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Order_of_the_Phoenix" title="Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix">[src]</a></sup></dd></dl>
</div><p>A reader of <i><a href="/wiki/Daily_Prophet" title="Daily Prophet">The Daily Prophet</a></i>, Mrs Finnigan believed the <a href="/wiki/Campaign_to_discredit_Albus_Dumbledore_and_Harry_Potter" title="Campaign to discredit Albus Dumbledore and Harry Potter">series of slander and lies</a> they printed against <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> and <a href="/wiki/Albus_Dumbledore" title="Albus Dumbledore">Albus Dumbledore</a> in the summer of <a href="/wiki/1995" title="1995">1995</a>. She did not want Seamus to return to the school that year as a result, believing that Dumbledore was incapable of acting as <a href="/wiki/Headmaster" title="Headmaster">headmaster</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-OotP11_9-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OotP11-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup> However, she relented, and allowed him to.
</p><h3><span class="mw-headline" id="1996">1996</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Seamus_Finnigan%27s_mother?veaction=edit&amp;section=5" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: 1996">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Seamus_Finnigan%27s_mother?action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: 1996"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<div class="quote">
<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix">"<i>I just wanted to say, I believe you. And I've sent a copy of that magazine to me mam.</i>"</span></dd>
<dd>&#8212;Seamus to Harry<sup class="noprint"><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Order_of_the_Phoenix" title="Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix">[src]</a></sup></dd>
<dd></dd></dl></div><p>Seamus sent a copy of <i><a href="/wiki/The_Quibbler" title="The Quibbler">The Quibbler</a></i> to his mother in <a href="/wiki/1996" title="1996">1996</a> to sway her into believing Harry over <i>The Daily Prophet</i>.
</p><h3><span class="mw-headline" id="1997">1997</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Seamus_Finnigan%27s_mother?veaction=edit&amp;section=6" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: 1997">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Seamus_Finnigan%27s_mother?action=edit&amp;section=6" title="Edit section: 1997"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<div class="quote">
<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince">"<i>Seamus Finnigan, on the other hand, refused point-blank to accompany his mother home; they had a shouting match in the Entrance Hall which was resolved when she agreed that he could remain behind for the funeral. She had difficulty in finding a bed in Hogsmeade, Seamus told Harry and Ron, for wizards and witches were pouring into the village, preparing to pay their last respects to Dumbledore.</i>"</span></dd>
<dd>&#8212;Description<sup class="noprint"><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Half-Blood_Prince" title="Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince">[src]</a></sup></dd>
<dd></dd></dl></div><p>After Dumbledore's death and the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_the_Astronomy_Tower" title="Battle of the Astronomy Tower">Battle of the Astronomy Tower</a> in <a href="/wiki/June" title="June">June</a> <a href="/wiki/1997" title="1997">1997</a>, Mrs Finnigan attempted to take Seamus home. However, he refused, leading to a loud argument between them in the <a href="/wiki/Entrance_Hall" title="Entrance Hall">Entrance Hall</a> before she agreed to allow him to stay for the <a href="/wiki/Funeral_of_Albus_Dumbledore" title="Funeral of Albus Dumbledore">funeral</a>. Mrs Finnigan encountered considerable difficulty finding somewhere to stay in <a href="/wiki/Hogsmeade" title="Hogsmeade">Hogsmeade</a>, due to the large amount of witches and wizards wishing to attend the funeral.<sup id="cite_ref-Funeral_10-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Funeral-10">&#91;10&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>When <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Voldemort</a> made Hogwarts attendance compulsory after <a href="/wiki/Fall_of_the_Ministry_of_Magic" title="Fall of the Ministry of Magic">he took over the Ministry</a>, Mrs Finnigan was forced to send Seamus to school for the <a href="/wiki/1997-1998_school_year" class="mw-redirect" title="1997-1998 school year">1997-1998 school year</a>.
</p><p>It is unknown whether Voldemort's control of the Ministry affected Irish wizards as strongly as it affected English, Scottish, and Welsh wizards, but if it did, Mrs Finnigan might have been under suspicion for her marriage to a muggle and her son's participation in <a href="/wiki/Dumbledore%27s_Army" title="Dumbledore&#39;s Army">Dumbledore's Army</a>.
</p>

<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix">"<i>You don't know my mother, she'd weasel anything out of anyone!</i>"</span></dd>
<dd>&#8212;Seamus on his mother<sup class="noprint"><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Order_of_the_Phoenix" title="Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix">[src]</a></sup></dd>
<dd></dd></dl></div><p>Mrs Finnigan was said to be able to shiftily manipulate others into giving out information.<sup id="cite_ref-Weasel_11-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Weasel-11">&#91;11&#93;</a></sup> She was likeable and worried for her son, not wanting him to stay at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts</a> while under the impression that <a href="/wiki/Albus_Dumbledore" title="Albus Dumbledore">Dumbledore</a> was unstable or after the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_the_Astronomy_Tower" title="Battle of the Astronomy Tower">Battle of the Astronomy Tower</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-Funeral_10-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Funeral-10">&#91;10&#93;</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ron Weasley</a> noted that telling Mrs Finnigan that he was anything but an <a href="/wiki/Irish_National_Quidditch_team" title="Irish National Quidditch team">Ireland</a> fan would not have ended well for him.<sup id="cite_ref-GoF7_5-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GoF7-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>
