<b>Teffington</b><sup id="cite_ref-script_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-script-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> (d. <a href="/wiki/1_May" title="1 May">1 May</a>, <a href="/wiki/1998" title="1998">1998</a>)<sup id="cite_ref-script_2-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-script-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> who served as <a href="/wiki/Gringotts_Guards" title="Gringotts Guards">a wizard guard</a> of <a href="/wiki/Gringotts_Wizarding_Bank" title="Gringotts Wizarding Bank">Gringotts Wizarding Bank</a> in <a href="/wiki/1998" title="1998">1998</a> during the <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a>' reign over the <a href="/wiki/Wizarding_world" title="Wizarding world">wizarding world</a>. He distrusted <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione Granger</a> (<a href="/wiki/Polyjuice_Potion" title="Polyjuice Potion">disguised</a> as <a href="/wiki/Bellatrix_Lestrange" title="Bellatrix Lestrange">Bellatrix Lestrange</a>) and <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ronald Weasley</a> (disguised as Dragomir Despard), as <a href="/wiki/Griphook" title="Griphook">Griphook</a> said, they knew Hermione was an imposter. He was standing in the entrance and stepped forward when <i>Bellatrix</i> denied to give her wand to <a href="/wiki/Bogrod" title="Bogrod">Bogrod</a>.
</p><p>After the break-in, Teffington was summoned to <a href="/wiki/Malfoy_Manor" title="Malfoy Manor">Malfoy Manor</a>, where he was killed by <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a>, in rage at having lost <a href="/wiki/Hufflepuff%27s_Cup" title="Hufflepuff&#39;s Cup">one of his Horcruxes</a>, along with other guards and goblins.<sup id="cite_ref-script_2-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-script-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
