<p>The <b>Snargaluff</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magical</a> plant with the appearance of a gnarled stump, but had dangerous hidden thorn-covered vines that attacked when provoked, and was usually best handled by more than one person.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP14_2-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP14-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Description">Description</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Snargaluff?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Description">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Snargaluff?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Description"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<figure class="thumb tright show-info-icon" style="width: 182px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/1/14/HM_y3_Snargaluff.png/revision/latest?cb=20180609041847" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/1/14/HM_y3_Snargaluff.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/180?cb=20180609041847" decoding="async" width="180" height="156" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="HM y3 Snargaluff.png" data-image-key="HM_y3_Snargaluff.png" /></a> 	 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:HM_y3_Snargaluff.png" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption">A Snargaluff pod</p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure>
<p>The plant contained green pulsating pods, about the size of a grapefruit, which could be extracted. These were then broken open and the contents, which resembled pale green tubers, collected in bowls. <a href="/wiki/Sixth_year" title="Sixth year">Sixth year</a> <a href="/wiki/Herbology" title="Herbology">Herbology</a> students at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> learned to extract these pods, which were best when fresh.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP14_2-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP14-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="History">History</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Snargaluff?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: History">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Snargaluff?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: History"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>On <a href="/wiki/1_December" title="1 December">1 December</a> <a href="/wiki/1926" title="1926">1926</a>, <a href="/wiki/The_New_York_Ghost" title="The New York Ghost">The New York Ghost</a> featured a game "Spot the Snargaluff" on page 16 of their Sunset Final edition.<sup id="cite_ref-FBF_4-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-FBF-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>At the end of the <a href="/wiki/1986%E2%80%931987_school_year" title="1986–1987 school year">1986–1987 school year</a>, this plant was taught to <a href="/wiki/Jacob%27s_sibling" title="Jacob&#39;s sibling">Jacob's sibling</a> by their <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Houses" title="Hogwarts Houses">House</a> <a href="/wiki/Prefect" title="Prefect">Prefect</a> as part of a farewell gift before the prefect graduated.<sup id="cite_ref-HM3_5-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HM3-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p><a href="/wiki/Xenophilius_Lovegood" title="Xenophilius Lovegood">Xenophilius Lovegood</a> had some of these plants (or, at least, stumps resembling Snargaluffs) in the garden in front of his <a href="/wiki/Lovegood_House" title="Lovegood House">house</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-DH21_1-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH21-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>During the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a>, <a href="/wiki/Pomona_Sprout" title="Pomona Sprout">Professor Sprout</a>, <a href="/wiki/Peeves" title="Peeves">Peeves</a> and other students of <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts</a> used Snargaluff pods as weapons against the attacking <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-DH31_3-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH31-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
