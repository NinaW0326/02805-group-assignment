<b>Henry</b> was an <a href="/wiki/United_States_of_America" title="United States of America">American</a>&#160;<a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">No-Maj</a> who worked as an&#160;assistant at <a href="/wiki/Kowalski_Quality_Baked_Goods" title="Kowalski Quality Baked Goods">Kowalski Quality Baked Goods</a>. In <a href="/wiki/March" title="March">March</a> <a href="/wiki/1927" title="1927">1927</a>, just before <a href="/wiki/Queenie_Goldstein" title="Queenie Goldstein">Queenie Goldstein</a> came into the shop for the first time, <a href="/wiki/Jacob_Kowalski" title="Jacob Kowalski">Jacob Kowalski</a> handed Henry a set of keys and asked him to go to the storage room.<sup id="cite_ref-FB123_1-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-FB123-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-FB124_2-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-FB124-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
