<b>Malcolm McGonagall</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Scotland" title="Scotland">Scottish</a> <a href="/wiki/Half-blood" title="Half-blood">half-blood</a> <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a>, who was the second child and first son of the <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggle</a> <a href="/wiki/Robert_McGonagall_Senior" title="Robert McGonagall Senior">Robert McGonagall</a> and witch <a href="/wiki/Isobel_Ross" title="Isobel Ross">Isobel Ross</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-pottermore_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-pottermore-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Malcolm_McGonagall?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Malcolm_McGonagall?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Malcolm revealed his <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magical</a> ability at an early age. His mother, and his older sister <a href="/wiki/Minerva_McGonagall" title="Minerva McGonagall">Minerva</a>, worked together to teach him and his younger brother, <a href="/wiki/Robert_McGonagall_Jr" class="mw-redirect" title="Robert McGonagall Jr">Robert Jr</a>, that they could not go about showing off their magic. They also worked to conceal the "accidents and embarrassments" that the two boys' magic sometimes caused from their father.<sup id="cite_ref-pottermore_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-pottermore-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Malcolm eventually fathered at least one child. His offspring would often visit his sister's home in <a href="/wiki/Hogsmeade" title="Hogsmeade">Hogsmeade</a> during her brief marriage between <a href="/wiki/1981" title="1981">1981</a> and <a href="/wiki/1984" title="1984">1984</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-pottermore_1-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-pottermore-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
