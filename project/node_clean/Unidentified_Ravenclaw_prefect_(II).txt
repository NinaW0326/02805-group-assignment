This <b>individual</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> who attended <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> and was <a href="/wiki/Sorting_ceremony" title="Sorting ceremony">sorted</a> into <a href="/wiki/Ravenclaw" title="Ravenclaw">Ravenclaw</a> at some point between <a href="/wiki/1981" title="1981">1981</a> and <a href="/wiki/1985" title="1985">1985</a><sup id="cite_ref-age_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-age-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>. In his <a href="/wiki/Fifth_year" title="Fifth year">fifth year</a> he was made a <a href="/wiki/Prefect" title="Prefect">Prefect</a> and between this year and his <a href="/wiki/Seventh_year" title="Seventh year">seventh year</a>, he duelled <a href="/wiki/Gabriel_Truman" title="Gabriel Truman">Gabriel Truman</a>, a <a href="/wiki/Third_year" title="Third year">third year</a> at that time, when he claimed that <a href="/wiki/Bridget_Wenlock" title="Bridget Wenlock">Bridget Wenlock</a> was <a href="/wiki/Hufflepuff" title="Hufflepuff">not a Ravenclaw</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
