<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Terence_Higgs?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Terence_Higgs?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>He was the <a href="/wiki/Slytherin_Quidditch_team" title="Slytherin Quidditch team">Slytherin Quidditch team</a> <a href="/wiki/Seeker" title="Seeker">Seeker</a> during the <a href="/wiki/1991%E2%80%931992_school_year" title="1991–1992 school year">1991–1992</a> season and in the first game of the year, raced <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> for the <a href="/wiki/Golden_Snitch" title="Golden Snitch">Golden Snitch</a>. Like <a href="/wiki/Adrian_Pucey" title="Adrian Pucey">Adrian Pucey</a>, he appeared to be one of the more decent Slytherin players, as he seemed to play by the rules of Quidditch and did not resort to cheating.<sup id="cite_ref-PS11_2-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PS11-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> 
</p><p>But in the <a href="/wiki/1992%E2%80%931993_school_year" title="1992–1993 school year">1992–1993 school year</a>, he was replaced by <a href="/wiki/Draco_Malfoy" title="Draco Malfoy">Draco Malfoy</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-COS7_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COS7-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>  However, it is unknown if he was still attending <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts</a> at that time, or if he had already graduated.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Magical_abilities_and_skills">Magical abilities and skills</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Terence_Higgs?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Terence_Higgs?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li><b><a href="/wiki/Flight" title="Flight">Flying</a></b>: As the <a href="/wiki/Seeker" title="Seeker">Seeker</a> for the <a href="/wiki/Slytherin_Quidditch_team" title="Slytherin Quidditch team">Slytherin Quidditch team</a>, Higgs was evidently a talented <a href="/wiki/Broomstick" title="Broomstick">broomstick</a> flyer and <a href="/wiki/Quidditch" title="Quidditch">Quidditch</a> player, as he was deemend good enough to be Seeker for the team.<sup id="cite_ref-PS11_2-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PS11-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup></li></ul>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Terence_Higgs?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Terence_Higgs?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>Terence Higgs was portrayed by <a href="/wiki/Great_Britain" title="Great Britain">British</a> actor <a href="/wiki/Will_Theakston" title="Will Theakston">Will Theakston</a>, with the credits incorrectly stating he was played by <a href="/wiki/Scot_Fearn" title="Scot Fearn">Scot Fearn</a>. Fearn's name was also misspelled as Fern.</li>
<li>In the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Philosopher%27s_Stone_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Philosopher&#39;s Stone (film)">film adaptation</a> of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Philosopher%27s_Stone" title="Harry Potter and the Philosopher&#39;s Stone">Harry Potter and the Philosopher's Stone</a></i> Terence Higgs used the <a href="/wiki/Cobbing" title="Cobbing">cobbing</a> foul against <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>, although he could have done it simply in retaliation, since Harry cobbed him first. It is unclear if this was the case, as Terence ended up cobbing Harry four times as opposed to twice.</li></ul>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Terence_Higgs?veaction=edit&amp;section=4" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Terence_Higgs?action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Terentius was a Roman family name. It may mean "tender" or "gracious". Higgs is variation of Hicks, from old English Hicke, a nickname for the Norman French name Richard. The early English substituted an "H" because they had trouble saying the letter "R".
</p>
