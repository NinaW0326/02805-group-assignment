<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Alistair_Fidgen?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Alistair_Fidgen?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Alistair got to know <a href="/wiki/Jacob_(Hogwarts_student)" title="Jacob (Hogwarts student)">Jacob</a> when the latter visited <a href="/wiki/Knockturn_Alley" title="Knockturn Alley">Knockturn Alley</a>. They had a deal where Jacob would give Alistair items from <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts</a> to sell and Alistair helped Jacob search for a painting. The last time Alistair saw Jacob was at <a href="/wiki/The_White_Wyvern" title="The White Wyvern">The White Wyvern</a>, where he had found the painting. Alistair claimed Jacob "was excited to finally solve some mystery, and said he wanted to thank me before 'meeting up with the others.'"<sup id="cite_ref-Y5SQ_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Y5SQ-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> He also knew an individual from the <a href="/wiki/MacFusty_clan" title="MacFusty clan">MacFusty clan</a>, who, according to him, owed him a favour.<sup id="cite_ref-Y5SQ_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Y5SQ-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>In <a href="/wiki/1988" title="1988">1988</a>, he met <a href="/wiki/Charles_Weasley" title="Charles Weasley">Charlie Weasley</a> and <a href="/wiki/Jacob%27s_sibling" title="Jacob&#39;s sibling">Jacob's sibling</a>, and offered to sell them an <a href="/wiki/Acromantula_egg" title="Acromantula egg">Acromantula egg</a>, which he claimed to be a <a href="/wiki/Dragon_egg" title="Dragon egg">dragon egg</a>, for 500 <a href="/wiki/Galleon" title="Galleon">galleons</a>. Seeing it as a scam, Jacob's sibling refused, but after offering information about Jacob in addition to the egg for 495 galleons, Jacob's sibling reluctantly agreed.<sup id="cite_ref-Y5SQ_1-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Y5SQ-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>After Jacob's sibling and Charlie managed to get the money needed to buy the egg, the three met up in the <a href="/wiki/Forbidden_Forest" title="Forbidden Forest">Forbidden Forest</a>. Alistair gave the egg to the two before they paid, something he did not normally do. After he was paid, he allowed Jacob's sibling to ask one question about their brother. After answering, Alistair left, saying he did not want to be there when the egg hatched.<sup id="cite_ref-Y5SQ_1-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Y5SQ-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>At some point, Alistair made a deal with <a href="/wiki/Jae_Kim" title="Jae Kim">Jae Kim</a> in which the latter would give him a <a href="/wiki/Love_Potion" class="mw-redirect" title="Love Potion">Love Potion</a>. However, the Love Potion that Jae gave him turned out to be <a href="/wiki/Butterbeer" title="Butterbeer">Butterbeer</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-Y6CH10_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Y6CH10-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>In around <a href="/wiki/1989" title="1989">1989</a>, Jae, Jacob's sibling and <a href="/wiki/Andre_Egwu" title="Andre Egwu">Andre Egwu</a> attempted to get an <a href="/wiki/Invisibility_cloak" title="Invisibility cloak">invisibility cloak</a> from him in exchange for a <a href="/wiki/Remembrall" title="Remembrall">Remembrall</a> and <a href="/wiki/Fireproof_sock" title="Fireproof sock">fireproof socks</a>. Alistair refused the offer due to the earlier bad experience with Jae, and instead attacked Jae. Jacob's sibling fought back by casting the <a href="/wiki/Bird-Conjuring_Charm" title="Bird-Conjuring Charm">Bird-Conjuring Charm</a> and defeating Alistair in a duel. After losing, he still refused to offer, but agreed to give his invisibility cloak if the students have him a real Love Potion.<sup id="cite_ref-Y6CH10_2-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Y6CH10-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
