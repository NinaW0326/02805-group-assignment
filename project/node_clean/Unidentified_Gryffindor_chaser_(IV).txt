<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_Gryffindor_chaser_(IV)?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_Gryffindor_chaser_(IV)?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>During her time at Hogwarts, she was <a href="/wiki/Sorting_ceremony" title="Sorting ceremony">sorted</a> into <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor" title="Gryffindor">Gryffindor</a> <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Houses" title="Hogwarts Houses">House</a>. At some point by the <a href="/wiki/2019%E2%80%932020_school_year" title="2019–2020 school year">2019–2020 school year</a>, she became the <a href="/wiki/Chaser" title="Chaser">Chaser</a> of <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor_Quidditch_team" title="Gryffindor Quidditch team">Gryffindor Quidditch team</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-WU_2-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-WU-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>On the eve of the final <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor_Quidditch_team" title="Gryffindor Quidditch team">Gryffindor</a>-<a href="/wiki/Slytherin_Quidditch_team" title="Slytherin Quidditch team">Slytherin</a> matches of the <a href="/wiki/Inter-House_Quidditch_Cup" title="Inter-House Quidditch Cup">Hogwarts Inter-House Quidditch Cup</a> she feared that her broom would vanish or weaken as a result of becoming a <a href="/wiki/Foundable" title="Foundable">Foundable</a>. <a href="/wiki/Headmaster" title="Headmaster">Headmistress</a> <a href="/wiki/Minerva_McGonagall" title="Minerva McGonagall">Minerva McGonagall</a> assured her that the Foundable in question was almost certainly the broom of a former famous <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">student</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-WU_2-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-WU-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
