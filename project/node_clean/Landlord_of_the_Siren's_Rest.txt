The <b>Siren's Rest's landlord</b> was the&#160;<a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggle</a>&#160;owner of the <a href="/wiki/Siren%27s_Rest" title="Siren&#39;s Rest">Siren's Rest</a> pub.

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Landlord_of_the_Siren%27s_Rest?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Landlord_of_the_Siren%27s_Rest?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p><a href="/wiki/Mathilda_Grimblehawk" title="Mathilda Grimblehawk">Mathilda Grimblehawk</a> and <a href="/wiki/Mathilda_Grimblehawk" title="Mathilda Grimblehawk">her partner</a> investigated his story of bring attacked by a 50-foot-long <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magical</a> <a href="/wiki/Beast" title="Beast">beast</a> while fishing in the <a href="/wiki/Lake_in_Wales" title="Lake in Wales">nearby lake</a>. <a href="/wiki/Landlord_of_the_Siren%27s_Rest%27s_fishing_pole" title="Landlord of the Siren&#39;s Rest&#39;s fishing pole">His fishing pole</a> bore the marks of the magical beast which he hoped would help draw patrons to his pub. <a href="/wiki/Landlord_of_the_Siren%27s_Rest%27s_spectacles" title="Landlord of the Siren&#39;s Rest&#39;s spectacles">His spectacles</a>, which he lost during the encounter, were later retrieved by the <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">Ministry</a> investigators at the bottom of the lake, with marks of suckers on them.
</p><p>When visiting monster hunter <a href="/wiki/Laura_Thorn" title="Laura Thorn">Laura Thorn</a> went missing he drew a map and rounded up some local lads to go find her.<sup id="cite_ref-FBC4.3_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-FBC4.3-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> After <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">Ministry</a> investigators <a href="/wiki/Mathilda_Grimblehawk" title="Mathilda Grimblehawk">Mathilda Grimblehawk</a> and <a href="/wiki/Mathilda_Grimblehawk" title="Mathilda Grimblehawk">her partner</a> (in the guise of people from "the lake safety commission") retrieved Thorn and sent her to <a href="/wiki/St_Mungo%27s_Hospital_for_Magical_Maladies_and_Injuries" title="St Mungo&#39;s Hospital for Magical Maladies and Injuries">St Mungo's Hospital for Magical Maladies and Injuries</a>, the Landlord agreed to call off the Muggle mob, but expressed his intention to contact the newspapers with information about <a href="/wiki/Grindylow" title="Grindylow">the monster</a> from the lake (his ultimate goal to "<i>make a fortune off monster-seeking tourists</i>"). Ultimately, he had to be <a href="/wiki/Memory_Charm" title="Memory Charm">Obliviated</a> to prevent a breach of the <a href="/wiki/International_Statute_of_Wizarding_Secrecy" title="International Statute of Wizarding Secrecy">International Statute of Wizarding Secrecy</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-FBC4.4_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-FBC4.4-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
