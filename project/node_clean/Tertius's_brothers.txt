The <b>two older brothers</b> of <a href="/wiki/Tertius" title="Tertius">Tertius</a> (fl. <a href="/wiki/16th_century" title="16th century">16th century</a>) were <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggle</a> brothers living somewhere in <a href="/wiki/Scotland" title="Scotland">Scotland</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-WBP_1-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-WBP-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Tertius%27s_brothers?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Tertius%27s_brothers?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>When their parents died, these two brothers chased Tertius out of their house and broke his <a href="/wiki/Wand" title="Wand">wand</a>, afraid of him because he was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a>. Sometime afterwards, <a href="/wiki/Zygmunt_Budge" title="Zygmunt Budge">Zygmunt Budge</a>, who had heard this story from Tertius, sent these two Muggles a large bottle of <a href="/wiki/Shrinking_Solution" title="Shrinking Solution">Shrinking Solution</a> by <a href="/wiki/Owl_Postal_Service" title="Owl Postal Service">owl post</a>, claiming it to be <a href="/wiki/Butterbeer" title="Butterbeer">Butterbeer</a>. This shrank them to the size of <a href="/wiki/Beetle" title="Beetle">beetles</a> and, according to the story Budge heard, spend the next few days hiding under their cupboard from their <a href="/wiki/Cat" title="Cat">cat</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-WBP_1-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-WBP-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
