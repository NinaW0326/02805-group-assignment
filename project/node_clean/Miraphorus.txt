<b>Miraphorus</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch</a> after whom the <a href="/wiki/Miraphorus_Magic_Set" title="Miraphorus Magic Set">Miraphorus Magic Set</a>, a <a href="/wiki/Weasleys%27_Wizard_Wheezes" title="Weasleys&#39; Wizard Wheezes">Weasleys' Wizard Wheezes</a> product, was named. Every set included a replica pair of the distinctive red gloves she wore.<sup id="cite_ref-hpfilmwiz_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-hpfilmwiz-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
