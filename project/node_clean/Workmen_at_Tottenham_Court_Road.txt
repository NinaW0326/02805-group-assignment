<p>A group of <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggle</a> <b>workmen</b> were out drinking at pubs on <a href="/wiki/Tottenham_Court_Road" title="Tottenham Court Road">Tottenham Court Road</a> on the night of <a href="/wiki/1_August" title="1 August">1 August</a>, <a href="/wiki/1997" title="1997">1997</a>. They spotted <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>, <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ron Weasley</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione Granger</a> that night, just after the trio <a href="/wiki/Apparition" title="Apparition">Disapparated</a> from <a href="/wiki/The_Burrow" title="The Burrow">the Burrow</a> after the <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a> <a href="/wiki/Fall_of_the_Ministry_of_Magic" title="Fall of the Ministry of Magic">took over</a> the <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">Ministry of Magic</a>. The men wolf-whistled at Hermione, and one drunkenly asked her to go get a drink with him. She ignored him, and entered a <a href="/wiki/Luchino_Caffe" title="Luchino Caffe">café</a> with Harry and Ron.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Workmen_at_Tottenham_Court_Road?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Workmen_at_Tottenham_Court_Road?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>After the trio unwittingly triggered the <a href="/wiki/Taboo" title="Taboo">Taboo curse</a> on <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a>'s name, they were tracked by Death Eaters <a href="/wiki/Antonin_Dolohov" title="Antonin Dolohov">Antonin Dolohov</a> and <a href="/wiki/Thorfinn_Rowle" title="Thorfinn Rowle">Thorfinn Rowle</a>, who disguised themselves as workmen and followed Harry, Ron, and Hermione into the café, where a brief <a href="/wiki/Luchino_Caffe_Duel" title="Luchino Caffe Duel">skirmish</a> ensued. It is possible that they incapacitated or killed the drunk workers in order to obtain the disguises, a theory which is supported by the detail of their existence being added at all.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup></li></ul>
