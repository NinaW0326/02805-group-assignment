This <b>individual</b> (fl. <a href="/wiki/1996" title="1996">1996</a>) was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a>. He may have been a former member of the <a href="/wiki/Slug_Club" title="Slug Club">Slug Club</a>, as in <a href="/wiki/20_December" title="20 December">20 December</a>, <a href="/wiki/1996" title="1996">1996</a>, he attended <a href="/wiki/Horace_Slughorn" title="Horace Slughorn">Professor Slughorn</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Slug_Club_Christmas_party" title="Slug Club Christmas party">Christmas party</a>. He was most likely acquainted with a <a href="/wiki/Unidentified_female_Hogwarts_teacher" title="Unidentified female Hogwarts teacher">female Hogwarts teacher</a>, as he stood with her at the party.<sup id="cite_ref-HBPf_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBPf-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<ul><li>This individual was played by an unknown actor in <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Half-Blood_Prince_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince (film)">film adaptation</a> of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Half-Blood_Prince" title="Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince">Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince</a></i>.</li>
<li>Given that the <a href="/wiki/Unidentified_female_Hogwarts_teacher" title="Unidentified female Hogwarts teacher">female Hogwarts teacher</a>, whom he stayed with during the <a href="/wiki/Slug_Club_Christmas_party" title="Slug Club Christmas party">Slug Club Christmas party</a>, is depicted as wearing a wedding ring, this individual may be her husband. This is, of course, backed by no canon information, and should not be treated as fact.</li></ul>
