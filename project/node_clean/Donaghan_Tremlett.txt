<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_life">Early life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Donaghan_Tremlett?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Early life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Donaghan_Tremlett?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Early life"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Donaghan Tremlett was born in <a href="/wiki/1972" title="1972">1972</a> to <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggle</a> <a href="/wiki/Tremlett_family" title="Tremlett family">parents</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-fwc_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-fwc-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> He started playing the bass guitar at some point in his childhood, and was noted to be talented at it during his Hogwarts years.<sup id="cite_ref-HM_4-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HM-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Donaghan began attending <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> in <a href="/wiki/1983" title="1983">1983</a> or <a href="/wiki/1984" title="1984">1984</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> During his Hogwarts years, he saw the <a href="/wiki/Weird_Sisters" title="Weird Sisters">The Weird Sisters</a> perform their song <i><a href="/wiki/Do_the_Hippogriff" title="Do the Hippogriff">Do the Hippogriff</a></i> and auditioned to become the new bass player of the band. Donaghan became a member of the band, replacing <a href="/wiki/Jacob%27s_sibling" title="Jacob&#39;s sibling">Jacob's sibling</a>, as he could actually play the bass without the use of <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magic</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-HM_4-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HM-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Later_life">Later life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Donaghan_Tremlett?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Later life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Donaghan_Tremlett?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Later life"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>On <a href="/wiki/25_December" title="25 December">Christmas Day</a> in <a href="/wiki/1994" title="1994">1994</a>, the band attended the <a href="/wiki/Yule_Ball" title="Yule Ball">Yule Ball</a> at Hogwarts,<sup id="cite_ref-gof23_6-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-gof23-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup> where they played classic hits like "<a href="/wiki/Magic_Works" title="Magic Works">Magic Works</a>" and "<a href="/wiki/This_Is_the_Night" title="This Is the Night">This Is the Night</a>," as well as a new song titled "<a href="/wiki/Do_the_Hippogriff" title="Do the Hippogriff">Do the Hippogriff</a>."<sup id="cite_ref-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Donaghan married <a href="/wiki/Donaghan_Tremlett%27s_wife" title="Donaghan Tremlett&#39;s wife">a woman</a> in September <a href="/wiki/1995" title="1995">1995</a>. The wedding was mentioned in the <i><a href="/wiki/Daily_Prophet" title="Daily Prophet">Daily Prophet</a></i> on <a href="/wiki/7_September" title="7 September">7 September</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<figure class="thumb tright show-info-icon" style="width: 252px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/0/06/B6C30M1_funeral_of_Albus_Dumbledore.png/revision/latest?cb=20161209004341" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/0/06/B6C30M1_funeral_of_Albus_Dumbledore.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/250?cb=20161209004341" decoding="async" width="250" height="98" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="B6C30M1 funeral of Albus Dumbledore.png" data-image-key="B6C30M1_funeral_of_Albus_Dumbledore.png" /></a> 	 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:B6C30M1_funeral_of_Albus_Dumbledore.png" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption">The <a href="/wiki/Funeral_of_Albus_Dumbledore" title="Funeral of Albus Dumbledore">Funeral of Albus Dumbledore</a> that Donaghan attended</p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure>
<p>In June <a href="/wiki/1997" title="1997">1997</a>, Donaghan attended the <a href="/wiki/Funeral_of_Albus_Dumbledore" title="Funeral of Albus Dumbledore">funeral</a> of <a href="/wiki/Albus_Dumbledore" title="Albus Dumbledore">Albus Dumbledore</a>, and was one of several familiar people <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> noticed among the crowd.<sup id="cite_ref-hbp30_9-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-hbp30-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<p>After the <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">Ministry of Magic</a> was <a href="/wiki/Fall_of_the_Ministry_of_Magic" title="Fall of the Ministry of Magic">taken over</a> by <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a> in September 1997, Donaghan, as a <a href="/wiki/Muggle-born" title="Muggle-born">Muggle-born</a>, presumably faced charges before the <a href="/wiki/Muggle-Born_Registration_Commission" title="Muggle-Born Registration Commission">Muggle-Born Registration Commission</a>. He may have been sent to <a href="/wiki/Azkaban" title="Azkaban">Azkaban</a>, or else gone into hiding, like many others of his heritage.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Physical_appearance">Physical appearance</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Donaghan_Tremlett?veaction=edit&amp;section=4" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Physical appearance">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Donaghan_Tremlett?action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Physical appearance"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Donaghan was described as "hairy"<sup id="cite_ref-hbp30_9-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-hbp30-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup> and, like all members of the Weird Sisters, wore "artfully torn" black robes when onstage.<sup id="cite_ref-gof23_6-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-gof23-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Donaghan_Tremlett?veaction=edit&amp;section=5" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Donaghan_Tremlett?action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>Donaghan Tremlett was portrayed by <a href="/wiki/Steve_Mackey" title="Steve Mackey">Steve Mackey</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Goblet_of_Fire_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire (film)">film adaptation</a> of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Goblet_of_Fire" title="Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire">Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire</a></i>.</li>
<li>Donaghan's favourite <a href="/wiki/Quidditch" title="Quidditch">Quidditch</a> <a href="/wiki/Quidditch_team" title="Quidditch team">team</a> was the <a href="/wiki/Kenmare_Kestrels" title="Kenmare Kestrels">Kenmare Kestrels</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-fwc_1-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-fwc-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li>Depending on his month of birth, he may have been in the same year as <a href="/wiki/Charles_Weasley" title="Charles Weasley">Charles Weasley</a>, <a href="/wiki/Nymphadora_Tonks" title="Nymphadora Tonks">Nymphadora Tonks</a> and <a href="/wiki/Jacob%27s_sibling" title="Jacob&#39;s sibling">Jacob's sibling</a>, among others.</li></ul>
