<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_bearded_centaur?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_bearded_centaur?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>During the <a href="/wiki/Skirmish_in_the_Forbidden_Forest" title="Skirmish in the Forbidden Forest">Skirmish in the Forbidden Forest</a> in <a href="/wiki/1996" title="1996">1996</a>, this centaur stood at the back of the <a href="/wiki/Forbidden_Forest_Centaur_colony" title="Forbidden Forest Centaur colony">herd of centaurs</a> that tried to execute <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> and <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione Granger</a>, when he suggested that the two teenagers "pay the consequences". Fortunately, all the <a href="/wiki/Centaur" title="Centaur">centaurs</a> were chased away by <a href="/wiki/Grawp" title="Grawp">Grawp</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP33_1-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP33-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
