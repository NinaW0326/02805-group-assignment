<p><b>Ug the Unreliable</b> (fl. <a href="/wiki/17th_century" title="17th century">17th century</a>) was an infamous <a href="/wiki/Goblin" title="Goblin">goblin</a> con artist, who became notable for organising the <a href="/wiki/Demiguise_Derby" title="Demiguise Derby">Demiguise Derby</a> and absconded with the profits. For orchestrating this famous fraud, he was featured on a <a href="/wiki/Chocolate_Frog_Card" title="Chocolate Frog Card">Chocolate Frog Card</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>At some point in his life he peddled <a href="/wiki/Leprechaun_gold" title="Leprechaun gold">Leprechaun gold</a>, which led to him being tracked down, and then imprisoned by <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizards</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-WOMBAT_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-WOMBAT-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> His imprisonment provoked one of the <a href="/wiki/Goblin_Rebellions" title="Goblin Rebellions">Goblin Rebellions</a> of the <a href="/wiki/17th_century" title="17th century">seventeenth</a> or <a href="/wiki/18th_century" title="18th century">eighteenth centuries</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
