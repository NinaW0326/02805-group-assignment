<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Wagga_Wagga_Werewolf?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Wagga_Wagga_Werewolf?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>He was believed to have been vanquished by <a href="/wiki/Gilderoy_Lockhart" title="Gilderoy Lockhart">Gilderoy Lockhart</a> by means of the <a href="/wiki/Homorphus_Charm" title="Homorphus Charm">Homorphus Charm</a>, returning him to <a href="/wiki/Human" title="Human">human</a> form after letting out a piteous moan.<sup id="cite_ref-COS10_1-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COS10-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> It was actually an <a href="/wiki/Armenian_warlock" title="Armenian warlock">Armenian warlock</a> who performed this feat, though Lockhart used a <a href="/wiki/Memory_Charm" title="Memory Charm">Memory Charm</a> on him and took all the credit for the werewolfâ€™s defeat himself.<sup id="cite_ref-COS16_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COS16-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>This werewolf was likely the subject of Lockhart's book <i><a href="/wiki/Wanderings_with_Werewolves" title="Wanderings with Werewolves">Wanderings with Werewolves</a></i> and was discussed by Lockhart during a second-year class during his time as <a href="/wiki/Defence_Against_the_Dark_Arts" title="Defence Against the Dark Arts">Defence Against the Dark Arts</a> teacher at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a>. Lockhart asked <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> to reenact the "defeat" of the werewolf as part of a lecture, in which Harry only reluctantly agreed to participate in order to have Lockhart sign a permission form for him to get a <a href="/wiki/Moste_Potente_Potions" title="Moste Potente Potions">library book</a> from the <a href="/wiki/Restricted_Section" title="Restricted Section">Restricted Section</a>. For a homework assignment, he asked students to compose a poem about the werewolf's defeat, and would give signed copies of <i><a href="/wiki/Magical_Me" title="Magical Me">Magical Me</a></i> to the best writers.<sup id="cite_ref-COS10_1-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COS10-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
