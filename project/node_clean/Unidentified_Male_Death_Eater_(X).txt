This <b><a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eater</a></b> and <a href="/wiki/Walden_Macnair" title="Walden Macnair">Walden Macnair</a> were sent by <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a> to convince the <a href="/wiki/Giant" title="Giant">Giants</a> to join him. They arrived and tried to convince the new <a href="/wiki/Gurg" title="Gurg">Gurg</a> <a href="/wiki/Golgomath" title="Golgomath">Golgomath</a> to join them. He eventuatly agreed and then told this Death Eater and Macnair how <a href="/wiki/Rubeus_Hagrid" title="Rubeus Hagrid">Rubeus Hagrid</a> and <a href="/wiki/Olympe_Maxime" title="Olympe Maxime">Olympe Maxime</a> had tried to convince the old Gurg <a href="/wiki/Karkus" title="Karkus">Karkus</a> to join the <a href="/wiki/Order_of_the_Phoenix" title="Order of the Phoenix">Order of the Phoenix</a> led by <a href="/wiki/Albus_Dumbledore" title="Albus Dumbledore">Albus Dumbledore</a>. Then this Death Eater and Macnair started searching the caves but soon after Hagrid and Maxime left.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP20_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP20-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
