<p><b>Mrs Norris</b> was the pet <a href="/wiki/Cat" title="Cat">cat</a> of <a href="/wiki/Argus_Filch" title="Argus Filch">Argus Filch</a>, the caretaker of <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a>. Mrs Norris was described as having an unusually strong connection with her master, alerting him to any students misbehaving inside the school castle. She was described as appearing almost the same way as Filch, with bulging yellow, lamp-like eyes, a scrawny, skeletal body and dust-coloured fur.<sup id="cite_ref-PS8_1-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PS8-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Mrs_Norris?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Mrs_Norris?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span id="Under_Argus_Filch's_ownership"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="Under_Argus_Filch.27s_ownership">Under Argus Filch's ownership</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Mrs_Norris?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Under Argus Filch&#039;s ownership">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Mrs_Norris?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Under Argus Filch&#039;s ownership"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<figure class="thumb tleft show-info-icon" style="width: 252px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/8/86/MrsNorris_PM.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20170911015555" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/8/86/MrsNorris_PM.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/250?cb=20170911015555" decoding="async" width="250" height="100" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="MrsNorris PM.jpg" data-image-key="MrsNorris_PM.jpg" /></a> 	 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:MrsNorris_PM.jpg" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption">Mrs Norris on the look out for mischief makers</p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure>
<p>Mrs Norris was born sometime prior to <a href="/wiki/1984" title="1984">1984</a>, and become the pet cat of the Hogwarts school caretaker, <a href="/wiki/Argus_Filch" title="Argus Filch">Argus Filch</a>. She formed an unusually strong bond with her owner, and often alerted him to any misbehaving students almost instantly. Because of this, she along with Filch were widely loathed by most of the Hogwarts student body, and many students had the urge to "<i>give her a good kick</i>".<sup id="cite_ref-PS8_1-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PS8-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="1991-1992_school_year">1991-1992 school year</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Mrs_Norris?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: 1991-1992 school year">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Mrs_Norris?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: 1991-1992 school year"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<p>During the <a href="/wiki/1991%E2%80%931992_school_year" title="1991–1992 school year">1991–1992 school year</a>, <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>, <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ron Weasley</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione Granger</a> often narrowly escaped the cat on their nightly trips through the school throughout their <a href="/wiki/First_year" title="First year">first year</a>. Harry once got caught by her one day for tracking mud on the floor.<sup id="cite_ref-PS8_1-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PS8-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> She and Filch nearly caught Harry, Ron, Hermione and <a href="/wiki/Neville_Longbottom" title="Neville Longbottom">Neville Longbottom</a> on the night Harry had gone to duel <a href="/wiki/Draco_Malfoy" title="Draco Malfoy">Draco Malfoy</a>. To avoid them, they hid in a room on the forbidden third floor corridor, and unknowingly encountering <a href="/wiki/Fluffy" title="Fluffy">Fluffy</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-PS9_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PS9-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>During the 1991 Christmas holidays when Harry and Ron first encountered the <a href="/wiki/Mirror_of_Erised" title="Mirror of Erised">Mirror of Erised</a> on a late night stroll, she noticed them being out of bed and wandering around the castle at night. Harry had a suspicion that she was able to see him even when he was under <a href="/wiki/Cloak_of_Invisibility" title="Cloak of Invisibility">his Invisibility Cloak</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-PS12_4-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PS12-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> although she may have just sensed someone there but cannot see, as cats are known to possess highly advanced senses that <a href="/wiki/Human" title="Human">humans</a> do not have.
</p><p>In <a href="/wiki/1992" title="1992">1992</a>, The <a href="/wiki/Trio" title="Trio">trio</a> encountered Mrs Norris at the foot of the stairs as they were wearing Harry's cloak at night, as they were going to the third floor corridor. She merely looked at them, and Ron recommended they kick her; however they just left her and continued to the third floor.<sup id="cite_ref-PS17_5-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PS17-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<p>Mrs Norris was the first victim of the <a href="/wiki/Serpent_of_Slytherin" title="Serpent of Slytherin">Serpent of Slytherin</a> when the <a href="/wiki/Chamber_of_Secrets" title="Chamber of Secrets">Chamber of Secrets</a> was re-opened in <a href="/wiki/1992" title="1992">1992</a>. On <a href="/wiki/Hallowe%27en" title="Hallowe&#39;en">Hallowe'en</a> night, she was <a href="/wiki/Petrification" title="Petrification">petrified</a> by the <a href="/wiki/Salazar_Slytherin%27s_Basilisk" class="mw-redirect" title="Salazar Slytherin&#39;s Basilisk">basilisk</a> when she saw its reflection in a puddle of <a href="/wiki/Water" title="Water">water</a> in front of the unused bathroom, in the second floor. <a href="/wiki/Ginevra_Weasley" title="Ginevra Weasley">Ginny Weasley</a>, possessed by <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Tom Marvolo Riddle</a>, hung her from a torch bracket by her tail, where she was found by <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>, <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ron Weasley</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione Granger</a>, next to the <a href="/wiki/Writing_on_the_Wall" title="Writing on the Wall">threatening message</a> written on the wall.<sup id="cite_ref-COS9_6-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COS9-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup> Headmaster <a href="/wiki/Albus_Dumbledore" title="Albus Dumbledore">Albus Dumbledore</a> examined the cat but was unable to revive her. <a href="/wiki/Argus_Filch" title="Argus Filch">Argus Filch</a> was devastated, and even accused Harry Potter of having attacked her.<sup id="cite_ref-COS10_7-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COS10-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup> Thankfully, Mrs Norris was revived by Professor <a href="/wiki/Pomona_Sprout" title="Pomona Sprout">Pomona Sprout</a> with the help of a <a href="/wiki/Mandrake_Restorative_Draught" title="Mandrake Restorative Draught">Mandrake Restorative Draught</a> at the end of the <a href="/wiki/1992%E2%80%931993_school_year" title="1992–1993 school year">1992–1993 school year</a>.
</p>

</p>

Harry dropped his <a href="/wiki/Golden_egg" title="Golden egg">Golden egg</a> behind a tapestry, and Filch and Mrs Norris found it. However, they were disturbed by Peeves who was causing trouble for them again, with Filch believing that Peeves had stolen the egg. When <a href="/wiki/Severus_Snape" title="Severus Snape">Severus Snape</a> entered the scene, she left with Filch and Snape to find an intruder who Snape knew was about. </p>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="1995-1996_school_year">1995-1996 school year</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Mrs_Norris?veaction=edit&amp;section=7" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: 1995-1996 school year">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Mrs_Norris?action=edit&amp;section=7" title="Edit section: 1995-1996 school year"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<p>At the <a href="/wiki/1995" title="1995">1995</a> <a href="/wiki/Welcoming_Feast" title="Welcoming Feast">Welcoming Feast</a>, Filch held Mrs Norris as he listened to <a href="/wiki/Dolores_Umbridge" title="Dolores Umbridge">Dolores Umbridge</a>'s speech in the <a href="/wiki/Great_Hall" title="Great Hall">Great Hall</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTPF_12-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTPF-12">&#91;12&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>During the <a href="/wiki/1995%E2%80%931996_school_year" title="1995–1996 school year">1995–1996 school year</a>, Harry came across Mrs Norris as he was going to the <a href="/wiki/Owlery" title="Owlery">Owlery</a> one evening. She came with Filch when he interrogated Harry in the Owlery, where Filch was dismissed by <a href="/wiki/Cho_Chang" title="Cho Chang">Cho Chang</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP14_13-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP14-13">&#91;13&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>In <a href="/wiki/December" title="December">December</a> <a href="/wiki/1995" title="1995">1995</a>, Mrs Norris encountered Harry, Ron and <a href="/wiki/Minerva_McGonagall" title="Minerva McGonagall">Minerva McGonagall</a> going to the <a href="/wiki/Headmaster%27s_Office" title="Headmaster&#39;s Office">Headmaster's Office</a> at night. She hissed faintly at them, but McGonagall shooed her away, with her retreating back into the shadows.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP22_14-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP22-14">&#91;14&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>In <a href="/wiki/1996" title="1996">1996</a>, <a href="/wiki/Peeves" title="Peeves">Peeves</a> locked Mrs Norris inside a suit of armour twice after <a href="/wiki/Fred_and_George_Weasley" title="Fred and George Weasley">Fred and George Weasley</a> had instructed him to wreak as much havoc as possible on their <a href="/wiki/Fred_and_George_Weasley%27s_departure_from_Hogwarts" title="Fred and George Weasley&#39;s departure from Hogwarts">departure</a> from the school. Filch personally came to her rescue.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP30_15-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP30-15">&#91;15&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="1996-1997_school_year">1996-1997 school year</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Mrs_Norris?veaction=edit&amp;section=8" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: 1996-1997 school year">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Mrs_Norris?action=edit&amp;section=8" title="Edit section: 1996-1997 school year"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<p>During the <a href="/wiki/1996%E2%80%931997_school_year" title="1996–1997 school year">1996–1997 school year</a>, Mrs Norris encountered Harry, Ron and <a href="/wiki/Ginevra_Weasley" title="Ginevra Weasley">Ginny Weasley</a> who were having a dispute in a school corridor, in which Ron fired an <a href="/wiki/Orange_spell" title="Orange spell">Orange spell</a> at Ginny, his sister, which Mrs Norris alerted Filch of.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP14_16-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP14-16">&#91;16&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Mrs Norris later encountered <a href="/wiki/Rubeus_Hagrid" title="Rubeus Hagrid">Rubeus Hagrid</a>, Harry and Hermione at the school at night with Filch, who got into a tense argument with Hagrid over the students being out of bed.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP19_17-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP19-17">&#91;17&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<p>Before the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a> started, Mrs Norris was seen hissing, screeching and batting at some <a href="/wiki/Owl" title="Owl">owls</a> who had escaped from the <a href="/wiki/Owlery" title="Owlery">Owlery</a> to return them to their proper place.<sup id="cite_ref-DH31_18-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH31-18">&#91;18&#93;</a></sup>

She survived the battle, and was with Filch as he swept up debris in the <a href="/wiki/Great_Hall" title="Great Hall">Great Hall</a>, sitting next to a pile of rubble.<sup id="cite_ref-DHF2_19-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DHF2-19">&#91;19&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Physical_appearance">Physical appearance</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Mrs_Norris?veaction=edit&amp;section=11" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Physical appearance">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Mrs_Norris?action=edit&amp;section=11" title="Edit section: Physical appearance"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Mrs Norris was a dust-coloured cat with bulging yellow, lamp-like eyes,<sup id="cite_ref-eyes_2-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-eyes-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> and a scrawny skeletal body.<sup id="cite_ref-PS8_1-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PS8-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Personality_and_traits">Personality and traits</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Mrs_Norris?veaction=edit&amp;section=12" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Personality and traits">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Mrs_Norris?action=edit&amp;section=12" title="Edit section: Personality and traits"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Mrs Norris appeared to be a very intelligent and perspective cat, who often looked out for misbehaving students and seemed to be very good at spotting them. She also had an excellent knowledge of <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Castle" title="Hogwarts Castle">Hogwarts Castle</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-PS8_1-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PS8-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> She also appeared to have a somewhat hostile temperament, often hissing at people,<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP22_14-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP22-14">&#91;14&#93;</a></sup> but she was very affectionate towards her owner Filch. 
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Relationships">Relationships</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Mrs_Norris?veaction=edit&amp;section=13" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Relationships">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Mrs_Norris?action=edit&amp;section=13" title="Edit section: Relationships"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Argus_Filch">Argus Filch</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Mrs_Norris?veaction=edit&amp;section=14" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Argus Filch">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Mrs_Norris?action=edit&amp;section=14" title="Edit section: Argus Filch"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<figure class="thumb tright show-info-icon" style="width: 182px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/6/60/Filch-Norris.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20170102071427" class="image"><img alt="" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw%3D%3D" decoding="async" width="180" height="248" class="thumbimage lazyload" data-image-name="Filch-Norris.jpg" data-image-key="Filch-Norris.jpg" data-src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/6/60/Filch-Norris.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/180?cb=20170102071427" /></a> 	<noscript><a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/6/60/Filch-Norris.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20170102071427" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/6/60/Filch-Norris.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/180?cb=20170102071427" decoding="async" width="180" height="248" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="Filch-Norris.jpg" data-image-key="Filch-Norris.jpg" data-src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/6/60/Filch-Norris.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/180?cb=20170102071427" /></a></noscript> 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:Filch-Norris.jpg" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption">Mrs Norris with her owner, Argus Filch</p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure>
<p>Filch and Mrs Norris had an unusually close bond. Filch was extremely fond and protective of her. Even when patrolling separate corridors, the two seem to be able to communicate and Filch came directly to her when she spotted a student misbehaving.<sup id="cite_ref-PS8_1-10" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PS8-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-PS9_3-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PS9-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> When she was attacked by the basilisk in 1992, Filch's first thought was that she was dead, and he was understandably devastated.<sup id="cite_ref-COS9_6-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COS9-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup> It is unknown what happened to Mrs Norris after the war.
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Rubeus_Hagrid">Rubeus Hagrid</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Mrs_Norris?veaction=edit&amp;section=15" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Rubeus Hagrid">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Mrs_Norris?action=edit&amp;section=15" title="Edit section: Rubeus Hagrid"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<div class="quote">
<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;Harry Potter and the Philosopher&#39;s Stone">"<i>An' as fer that cat, Mrs Norris, I'd like ter introduce her to <a href="/wiki/Fang" title="Fang">Fang</a> some time.</i>"</span></dd>
<dd>&#8212;Hagrid's intense dislike of Mrs Norris<sup class="noprint"><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Philosopher%27s_Stone" title="Harry Potter and the Philosopher&#39;s Stone">[src]</a></sup></dd>
<dd></dd></dl></div>
<p><a href="/wiki/Rubeus_Hagrid" title="Rubeus Hagrid">Rubeus Hagrid</a> could not stand her; as he was allergic to cats. He suspected that Filch set her up to follow him around whenever he came up to the school. Hagrid once remarked that he would like to introduce her to <a href="/wiki/Fang" title="Fang">Fang</a>, his pet <a href="/wiki/Dog" title="Dog">dog</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-PS8_1-11" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PS8-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> However, she seemed to respect the other Hogwarts teachers.
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Hogwarts_students">Hogwarts students</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Mrs_Norris?veaction=edit&amp;section=16" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Hogwarts students">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Mrs_Norris?action=edit&amp;section=16" title="Edit section: Hogwarts students"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Mrs Norris was hated by a majority of the students. Much like her master, she had an excellent knowledge of the <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Castle" title="Hogwarts Castle">castle</a>. Being caught misbehaving by her was just as bad as being caught by <a href="/wiki/Argus_Filch" title="Argus Filch">Filch</a>, because she would fetch him straight away. It was the "dearest ambition of many to give Mrs Norris a good kick."<sup id="cite_ref-PS8_1-12" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PS8-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Mrs_Norris?veaction=edit&amp;section=17" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Mrs_Norris?action=edit&amp;section=17" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Rowling said she obtained the name Mrs Norris from a character in <a href="/wiki/Jane_Austen" title="Jane Austen">Jane Austen</a>'s <i>Mansfield Park</i>, who is similarly odious and is hanging around in the background frequently.<sup id="cite_ref-connection_20-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-connection-20">&#91;20&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<ul><li>Mrs Norris seems exceptionally smart for a cat, to the point that many have suspected she is a cat/<a href="/wiki/Kneazle" title="Kneazle">Kneazle</a> cross like <a href="/wiki/Crookshanks" title="Crookshanks">Crookshanks</a> or an <a href="/wiki/Animagus" title="Animagus">Animagus</a>. However, <a href="/wiki/J._K._Rowling" title="J. K. Rowling">J. K. Rowling</a> has stated on her website that she is just a highly unpleasant cat. This was confirmed again when Rowling stated on Twitter in response to a fan query that Mrs Norris would lose in a very brutal, close fight with Crookshanks, as Crookshanks's kneazle ancestry would eventually give him the edge.<sup id="cite_ref-fight_21-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-fight-21">&#91;21&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li>Mrs Norris's role in the first two films is much the same as her role in the books. She also appears briefly in the third film and in the fourth and fifth films she, along with Filch, are used more as comic relief. In the fourth film, <a href="/wiki/Argus_Filch" title="Argus Filch">Argus Filch</a> is seen dancing with her at the <a href="/wiki/Yule_Ball" title="Yule Ball">Yule Ball</a> showing a brief sensitive side to Filch and showing his love for his cat, and on the fifth film Mrs Norris is seen climbing on Filch during the Welcoming Feast, while he is clapping enthusiastically at <a href="/wiki/Dolores_Umbridge" title="Dolores Umbridge">Dolores Umbridge</a>'s speech. She has significantly more screen time in the final film; Filch carries her as he runs into the <a href="/wiki/Great_Hall" title="Great Hall">Great Hall</a> screaming that the students are out of bed, as well at the end of the film, where she is comically sitting next to a pile of rubble as Filch attempts to sweep.</li>
<li>In the earlier films, Mrs Norris has red eyes. In later films, however, she has blue eyes. Also, in the films, Mrs Norris has brown and grey fur.</li>
<li>The cat that played Mrs Norris is a Maine Coon. In sharp contrast to Mrs Norris's description in the books as being scrawny, Maine Coons are a large, stocky breed.</li>
<li>Several animal actors portray Mrs Norris throughout the films. The most notable include Maine Coons named <a href="/wiki/Maximus" title="Maximus">Maximus</a>, <a href="/wiki/Alanis" title="Alanis">Alanis</a> and <a href="/wiki/Cornilus" title="Cornilus">Cornilus</a>, and animatronics were also used. Specific animal actors were used for specific purposes. Maximus was trained to run alongside Filch and jump onto his shoulders, while Alanis was trained to be held in Filch's arms without squirming. She was so good at her job that often she fell asleep during shoots.</li>
<li>It is unknown if she joined the fight with or without <a href="/wiki/Argus_Filch" title="Argus Filch">Filch</a>. It is unknown what happened to her after the war, although given her age of around 14 or possibly older during the Battle of Hogwarts, it is plausible that Mrs Norris had since passed away.</li></ul>
