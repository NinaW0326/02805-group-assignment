<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_first_year_boy?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_first_year_boy?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<div class="quote">
<dl><dd><span>"<i>An autograph from Wigtown Wanderer <a href="/wiki/Ethan_Parkin" title="Ethan Parkin">Ethan Parkin</a>? Brilliant!</i>"</span></dd>
<dd>&#8212;This boy to <a href="/wiki/Skye_Parkin" title="Skye Parkin">Skye Parkin</a></dd>
<dd></dd></dl></div><p>He was born somewhere in <a href="/wiki/Great_Britain" title="Great Britain">Great Britain</a> or <a href="/wiki/Ireland" title="Ireland">Ireland</a> during the early <a href="/wiki/1970s" title="1970s">1970s</a>. He began attending <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> in the <a href="/wiki/1985%E2%80%931986_school_year" title="1985–1986 school year">1985–1986 school year</a>.
</p><p>In his first year, he spotted <a href="/wiki/Skye_Parkin" title="Skye Parkin">Skye Parkin</a> and <a href="/wiki/Jacob%27s_sibling" title="Jacob&#39;s sibling">Jacob's sibling</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Common_room" title="Common room">common room</a> of his house. Skye told the boy to leave and tell no one, which he agreed to do when Skye promised him an autograph from <a href="/wiki/Ethan_Parkin" title="Ethan Parkin">her father</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-HM2_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HM2-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_first_year_boy?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_first_year_boy?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>Which house he is in depends on that of <a href="/wiki/Jacob%27s_sibling" title="Jacob&#39;s sibling">Jacob's sibling</a> (chosen by the player). If Jacob's sibling is in <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor" title="Gryffindor">Gryffindor</a>, <a href="/wiki/Hufflepuff" title="Hufflepuff">Hufflepuff</a> or <a href="/wiki/Slytherin" title="Slytherin">Slytherin</a>, this boy is in <a href="/wiki/Ravenclaw" title="Ravenclaw">Ravenclaw</a>. If <a href="/wiki/Jacob%27s_sibling" title="Jacob&#39;s sibling">Jacob's sibling</a> is in Ravenclaw, he is in Slytherin.</li>
<li>His a fan of the <a href="/wiki/Quidditch_team" title="Quidditch team">Quidditch team</a> <a href="/wiki/Wigtown_Wanderers" title="Wigtown Wanderers">Wigtown Wanderers</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-HM2_2-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HM2-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup></li></ul>
