<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_Gryffindor_girl_(1993-1994)_(I)?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_Gryffindor_girl_(1993-1994)_(I)?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_life">Early life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_Gryffindor_girl_(1993-1994)_(I)?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Early life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_Gryffindor_girl_(1993-1994)_(I)?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Early life"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>This girl was born in either <a href="/wiki/1979" title="1979">1979</a> or <a href="/wiki/1980" title="1980">1980</a>. She started her <a href="/wiki/First_year" title="First year">first year</a> of <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magical</a> education at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> in <a href="/wiki/1991" title="1991">1991</a>, and was <a href="/wiki/Sorting_ceremony" title="Sorting ceremony">Sorted</a> into <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor" title="Gryffindor">Gryffindor</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-POAF_2-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-POAF-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> She shared a dormitory in <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor_Tower" title="Gryffindor Tower">Gryffindor Tower</a> with <a href="/wiki/Lavender_Brown" title="Lavender Brown">Lavender Brown</a>, <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione Granger</a>, <a href="/wiki/Parvati_Patil" title="Parvati Patil">Parvati Patil</a> and <a href="/wiki/Unidentified_Gryffindor_girl_(1993-1994)_(II)" title="Unidentified Gryffindor girl (1993-1994) (II)">another girl</a>. She and the ginger girl befriended, while Parvati and Lavender grew close and Hermione gradually became friends with <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> and <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ronald Weasley</a>.
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Third_year">Third year</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_Gryffindor_girl_(1993-1994)_(I)?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Third year">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_Gryffindor_girl_(1993-1994)_(I)?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Third year"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>In her <a href="/wiki/Third_year" title="Third year">third year</a> in <a href="/wiki/1993" title="1993">1993</a>, the girl chose <a href="/wiki/Care_of_Magical_Creatures" title="Care of Magical Creatures">Care of Magical Creatures</a> and <a href="/wiki/Divination_(class)" title="Divination (class)">Divination</a> as her elective subjects.<sup id="cite_ref-POAF_2-10" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-POAF-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>In her first <a href="/wiki/Defence_Against_the_Dark_Arts" title="Defence Against the Dark Arts">Defence Against the Dark Arts</a> lesson, she and her <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor" title="Gryffindor">Gryffindor</a> classmates were taught about <a href="/wiki/Boggart" title="Boggart">Boggarts</a> by <a href="/wiki/Remus_Lupin" title="Remus Lupin">Remus Lupin</a>, the new teacher as of <a href="/wiki/Gilderoy_Lockhart" title="Gilderoy Lockhart">Professor Lockhart</a>'s departure, and had a practical lesson on how to <a href="/wiki/Boggart-Banishing_Spell" title="Boggart-Banishing Spell">banish</a> them. She faced the Boggart, and it frightened her by becoming either a <a href="/wiki/Rat" title="Rat">rat</a>, <a href="/wiki/Rattlesnake" title="Rattlesnake">rattlesnake</a> or eyeball, the other two belonging to her friend and to Lavender.<sup id="cite_ref-POA7_3-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-POA7-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<figure class="thumb tright show-info-icon" style="width: 253px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/3/36/Care_of_magical_creatures_class_1993.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20170729173305" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/3/36/Care_of_magical_creatures_class_1993.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/251?cb=20170729173305" decoding="async" width="251" height="105" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="Care of magical creatures class 1993.jpg" data-image-key="Care_of_magical_creatures_class_1993.jpg" /></a> 	 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:Care_of_magical_creatures_class_1993.jpg" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption">The girl in a Care of Magical Creatures lesson</p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure>
<p>During the first <a href="/wiki/Care_of_Magical_Creatures" title="Care of Magical Creatures">Care of Magical Creatures</a>, which she went with her friend, they were told that their copies of <i><a href="/wiki/The_Monster_Book_of_Monsters" title="The Monster Book of Monsters">The Monster Book of Monsters</a></i>, which had bitten them, had to be stroked in order to open. When <a href="/wiki/Rubeus_Hagrid" title="Rubeus Hagrid">Professor Hagrid</a>, who took Kettleburn's place after he resigned, introduced the <a href="/wiki/Hippogriff" title="Hippogriff">Hippogriff</a> <a href="/wiki/Buckbeak" title="Buckbeak">Buckbeak</a>, the girl, along with the rest of the class, did not seem to be able to believe her eyes. After Hagrid asked for a volunteer, she was afraid and stepped back, leaving <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> to interact with Buckbeak instead. She watched apprehensively behind Hermione and <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ronald Weasley</a>, and applauded happily after Harry flew the Hippogriff. Unfortunately for him, Malfoy insulted Buckbeak, who proceeded to attack him. The girl ran and hid behind a rock with her best-friend and Parvati. The class was dismissed while Hagrid took Malfoy to <a href="/wiki/Poppy_Pomfrey" title="Poppy Pomfrey">Madam Pomfrey</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Hospital_Wing" title="Hospital Wing">Hospital Wing</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-POAF_2-11" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-POAF-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>The girl went to <a href="/wiki/Hogsmeade" title="Hogsmeade">Hogsmeade Village</a>, on at least the first weekend they were allowed, and went with a girl other than her friend, as she may not have been able to go.<sup id="cite_ref-POAF_2-12" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-POAF-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>The girl was later present during the Divination lesson that Hermione, after being insulted by <a href="/wiki/Sybill_Trelawney" title="Sybill Trelawney">Professor Trelawney</a>, stormed out of the class. At the time, she was crystal-gazing with her friend, and looked up in surprise after Hermione left. She returned to her work straight away, and was later one of the last out of the classroom. On the <a href="/wiki/Divination_Stairwell" title="Divination Stairwell">Divination staircase</a>, she walked with another girl, rather than her friend, who walked into <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-POAF_2-13" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-POAF-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Physical_description">Physical description</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_Gryffindor_girl_(1993-1994)_(I)?veaction=edit&amp;section=4" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Physical description">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_Gryffindor_girl_(1993-1994)_(I)?action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Physical description"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>She was considerably taller than her friend, with brown hair which reached her shoulder blades. She had blue eyes and often wore her uniform tidily, though she took off her jumper and robe during one <a href="/wiki/Care_of_Magical_Creatures" title="Care of Magical Creatures">Care of Magical Creatures</a> lesson and carried them around on her arm. Occasionally, she wore her hair in pigtails, like her friend.<sup id="cite_ref-POAF_2-14" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-POAF-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_Gryffindor_girl_(1993-1994)_(I)?veaction=edit&amp;section=5" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_Gryffindor_girl_(1993-1994)_(I)?action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li><a href="/wiki/J._K._Rowling" title="J. K. Rowling">J. K. Rowling</a> has been asked about the two unidentified girls in in the third film, in three interviews. She said their names were in her notebook, and promised twice to post them on her <a href="/wiki/J._K._Rowling%27s_official_site" title="J. K. Rowling&#39;s official site">website</a>, but has not as of yet.<sup id="cite_ref-wbd_4-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-wbd-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-leaky_5-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-leaky-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-pottercast_6-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-pottercast-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup> Even if she does, it will be impossible to say which name belongs to which girl.</li>
<li>The girls' names may have already appeared partially on the class list shown on <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_Me" title="Harry Potter and Me">Harry Potter and Me</a></i>.</li>
<li>It is highly likely that the girl was either <a href="/wiki/Alice_Tolipan" title="Alice Tolipan">Alice Tolipan</a> or <a href="/wiki/Luca_Caruso_(wizard)" title="Luca Caruso (wizard)">Luca Caruso</a>, the two unknown individuals who joined <a href="/wiki/Dumbledore%27s_Army" title="Dumbledore&#39;s Army">Dumbledore's Army</a>. If not, then she and her friend were the only Gryffindors in their year not to join the group.</li>
<li>Other possible identities include <a href="/wiki/Sally-Anne_Perks" title="Sally-Anne Perks">Sally-Anne Perks</a>, <a href="/wiki/Lily_Moon" title="Lily Moon">Lily Moon</a>, <a href="/wiki/Sophie_Roper" title="Sophie Roper">Sophie Roper</a>, <a href="/wiki/Sally_Smith" title="Sally Smith">Sally Smith</a>, or <a href="/wiki/Runcorn" title="Runcorn">Runcorn</a>.</li>
<li>She is portrayed by an unidentified actress in the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Prisoner_of_Azkaban_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban (film)">film adaptation</a> of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Prisoner_of_Azkaban" title="Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban">Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban</a></i>.</li></ul>
