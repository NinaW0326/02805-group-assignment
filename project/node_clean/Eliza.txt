<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Eliza?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Eliza?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Eliza was born somewhere in <a href="/wiki/Great_Britain" title="Great Britain">Great Britain</a> or <a href="/wiki/Ireland" title="Ireland">Ireland</a> during the early <a href="/wiki/1970s" title="1970s">1970s</a>. She began attending <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> in the <a href="/wiki/1985%E2%80%931986_school_year" title="1985–1986 school year">1985–1986 school year</a>, where she was <a href="/wiki/Sorting_ceremony" title="Sorting ceremony">sorted</a> into <a href="/wiki/Ravenclaw" title="Ravenclaw">Ravenclaw</a>. 
</p><p>Eliza was revising for her <a href="/wiki/Potions_(class)" title="Potions (class)">Potions</a> exam late one night in the <a href="/wiki/Ravenclaw_Common_Room" title="Ravenclaw Common Room">Ravenclaw Common Room</a>, and the next day she could not find her notes. She thought another pupil might have used the <a href="/wiki/Severing_Charm" title="Severing Charm">Severing Charm</a> on her bag, which made them fall out. Eliza then searched the common room for the notes, which she eventually found.
</p>

<ul><li>The search for Eliza's notes was a specific assignment for Ravenclaw only players in <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter:_Hogwarts_Mystery" title="Harry Potter: Hogwarts Mystery">Harry Potter: Hogwarts Mystery</a></i>. In the game, <a href="/wiki/Rowan_Khanna" title="Rowan Khanna">Rowan Khanna</a> tells <a href="/wiki/Jacob%27s_sibling" title="Jacob&#39;s sibling">Jacob's sibling</a> why Eliza needs their help and they agree to search the room with her. Eliza was very grateful for their help and went to tell <a href="/wiki/Filius_Flitwick" title="Filius Flitwick">Professor Flitwick</a> about their kindness towards <a href="/wiki/First_year" title="First year">first year</a> Ravenclaws.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup></li></ul>
