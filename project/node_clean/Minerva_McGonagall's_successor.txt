This <b>individual</b> succeeded <a href="/wiki/Minerva_McGonagall" title="Minerva McGonagall">Minerva McGonagall</a> in the post of <a href="/wiki/Headmaster" title="Headmaster">Head</a> of <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a>. This individual became Headmaster or Headmistress some time between <a href="/wiki/2008" title="2008">2008</a><sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> and&#160;<a href="/wiki/2017" title="2017">2017</a> after Minerva McGonagall went into retirement as she was "getting on a bit."<sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Minerva_McGonagall%27s_successor?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Minerva_McGonagall%27s_successor?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>Although <a href="/wiki/J._K._Rowling" title="J. K. Rowling">J. K. Rowling</a> initially said in interviews that McGonagall was "getting on a bit" and that Hogwarts had a new Headmaster by the time of the Epilogue in <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Deathly_Hallows" title="Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows">Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows</a></i> (set in <a href="/wiki/2017" title="2017">2017</a>), this was later retconned in <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Cursed_Child" title="Harry Potter and the Cursed Child">Harry Potter and the Cursed Child</a></i>, which has McGonagall in office as late as <a href="/wiki/2020" title="2020">2020</a>.</li></ul>
