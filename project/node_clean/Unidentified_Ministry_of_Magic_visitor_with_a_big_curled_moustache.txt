This <b>individual</b> (fl. <a href="/wiki/1997" title="1997">1997</a>) was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> who was visiting the <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">Ministry of Magic</a> during the <a href="/wiki/Infiltration_of_the_Ministry_of_Magic" title="Infiltration of the Ministry of Magic">Infiltration of the Ministry of Magic</a>, on <a href="/wiki/2_September" title="2 September">2 September</a>, <a href="/wiki/1997" title="1997">1997</a>. He wore a black cloak and hat, and had a big white curled moustache. He was rudely pushed aside by <a href="/wiki/Corban_Yaxley" title="Corban Yaxley">Yaxley</a> who was preventing <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>, <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ronald Weasley</a> and <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione Granger</a> to leave the Ministry.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
