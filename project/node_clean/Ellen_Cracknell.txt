<b>Ellen Cracknell</b> was the <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggle</a> mother of <a href="/wiki/Dolores_Umbridge" title="Dolores Umbridge">Dolores Umbridge</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-PM_1-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PM-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Ellen_Cracknell?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Ellen_Cracknell?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Ellen Cracknell married the <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> <a href="/wiki/Orford_Umbridge" title="Orford Umbridge">Orford Umbridge</a>, and together they had two children: a daughter, <a href="/wiki/Dolores_Umbridge" title="Dolores Umbridge">Dolores</a>, and a son who was born a <a href="/wiki/Squib" title="Squib">Squib</a>.
</p><p>The marriage was not a happy one. Orford blamed Ellen for the birth of their Squib son, and Dolores came to loathe her mother not only for her Muggle heritage but also her flightiness and untidiness.<sup id="cite_ref-PM_1-10" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PM-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>When Dolores was fifteen, the tension between Ellen and Orford came to a head and the pair separated. While her ex-husband and daughter continued to live in the <a href="/wiki/Wizarding_world" title="Wizarding world">wizarding world</a>, Ellen and her son returned to the <a href="/wiki/Muggle_world" title="Muggle world">Muggle world</a>. Ellen would never see her daughter again.<sup id="cite_ref-PM_1-11" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PM-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
