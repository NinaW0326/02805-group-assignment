<b>Elsie Valentine Schroeder</b><sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch</a> who was reported missing on the <i><a href="/wiki/Potterwatch" title="Potterwatch">Potterwatch</a></i> wireless programme in <a href="/wiki/1997" title="1997">1997</a>. Her disappearance was most likely linked to some sort of <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eater</a> attack.
</p>
