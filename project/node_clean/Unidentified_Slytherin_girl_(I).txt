This <b>girl</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch</a> who attended <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> and was <a href="/wiki/Sorting_ceremony" title="Sorting ceremony">sorted</a> into <a href="/wiki/Slytherin" title="Slytherin">Slytherin House</a>. She was a member of the short-lived <a href="/wiki/Inquisitorial_Squad" title="Inquisitorial Squad">Inquisitorial Squad</a> in early <a href="/wiki/1996" title="1996">1996</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTPF_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTPF-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>

<h2><span id="1995–1996_school_year"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="1995.E2.80.931996_school_year"><a href="/wiki/1995%E2%80%931996_school_year" title="1995–1996 school year">1995–1996 school year</a></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_Slytherin_girl_(I)?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: 1995–1996 school year">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_Slytherin_girl_(I)?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: 1995–1996 school year"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<figure class="thumb tleft show-info-icon" style="width: 202px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/0/0a/Inquisitorial_Squad.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20161216055102" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/0/0a/Inquisitorial_Squad.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/200?cb=20161216055102" decoding="async" width="200" height="121" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="Inquisitorial Squad.jpg" data-image-key="Inquisitorial_Squad.jpg" /></a> 	 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:Inquisitorial_Squad.jpg" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption">Dolores Umbridge inducting her, Draco Malfoy, Vincent Crabbe and several other students into the Inquisitorial Squad</p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure>
<p>In the spring of <a href="/wiki/1996" title="1996">1996</a>, she was one of the members of the <a href="/wiki/Inquisitorial_Squad" title="Inquisitorial Squad">Inquisitorial Squad</a>, formed by then-Headmistress <a href="/wiki/Dolores_Umbridge" title="Dolores Umbridge">Dolores Umbridge</a>. She was one of the students that saw <a href="/wiki/Fred_Weasley" title="Fred Weasley">Fred</a> and <a href="/wiki/George_Weasley" title="George Weasley">George Weasley</a> flying away from school. At the end of the school year, the Inquisitorial Squad was disbanded after <a href="/wiki/Albus_Dumbledore" title="Albus Dumbledore">Albus Dumbledore</a> returned.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTPF_2-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTPF-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_Slytherin_girl_(I)?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_Slytherin_girl_(I)?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>She is portrayed in the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Order_of_the_Phoenix_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix (film)">film adaptation</a> of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Order_of_the_Phoenix" title="Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix">Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix</a></i> by <a href="/wiki/Gemma_Boyle" title="Gemma Boyle">Gemma Boyle</a>.</li>
<li>She is a student in the same year as <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>, since she was taking the <a href="/wiki/Ordinary_Wizarding_Level" title="Ordinary Wizarding Level">O.W.L.</a> examinations at the same time.</li>
<li>She may possibly be <a href="/wiki/Daphne_Greengrass" title="Daphne Greengrass">Daphne Greengrass</a> because she is in the same year as Harry Potter and since she is a member of the <a href="/wiki/Inquisitorial_Squad" title="Inquisitorial Squad">Inquisitorial Squad</a>.</li>
<li>She may also be <a href="/wiki/Tracey_Davis" title="Tracey Davis">Tracey Davis</a>, <a href="/wiki/Sally-Anne_Perks" title="Sally-Anne Perks">Sally-Anne Perks</a>, <a href="/wiki/Sophie_Roper" title="Sophie Roper">Sophie Roper</a>, Runcorn, Sally Smith, or <a href="/wiki/Lily_Moon" title="Lily Moon">Lily Moon</a>.</li>
<li>She may even be <a href="/wiki/Millicent_Bulstrode" title="Millicent Bulstrode">Millicent Bulstrode</a> as Millicent was also in Harry's year and a member of the Inquisitorial Squad.</li></ul>
