<b>Corvus Lestrange</b> was a <a href="/wiki/French" title="French">French</a> <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> and a member of the <a href="/wiki/Pure-blood" title="Pure-blood">pure-blood</a> <a href="/wiki/Lestrange_family" title="Lestrange family">Lestrange family</a>. He was the son of <a href="/wiki/Corvus_Lestrange_(II)" title="Corvus Lestrange (II)">Corvus Lestrange</a> and <a href="/wiki/Minette_Lestrange" title="Minette Lestrange">Minette Lestrange</a>. He had two sisters, <a href="/wiki/Manon_Perrot" title="Manon Perrot">Manon</a> and <a href="/wiki/Josette_Gage" title="Josette Gage">Josette</a>. He married his cousin <a href="/wiki/Eglantine_Lestrange" title="Eglantine Lestrange">Eglantine Lestrange</a> and had a son with her, <a href="/wiki/Corvus_Lestrange_(IV)" title="Corvus Lestrange (IV)">Corvus</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
