This <b><a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a></b> fought during the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a> against the <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a>. During the one-hour armistice he cared for the injured students, along with the <a href="/wiki/Healer" title="Healer">Healers</a>. He survived the battle.<sup id="cite_ref-DHF2_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DHF2-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
