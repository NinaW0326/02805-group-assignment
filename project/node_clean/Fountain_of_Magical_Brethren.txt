<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Description">Description</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Fountain_of_Magical_Brethren?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Description">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Fountain_of_Magical_Brethren?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Description"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<figure class="thumb tright show-info-icon" style="width: 182px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/c/c2/Ministry_of_Magic_%28concept_artwork_for_HP5_movie%29.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20091121022847" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/c/c2/Ministry_of_Magic_%28concept_artwork_for_HP5_movie%29.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/180?cb=20091121022847" decoding="async" width="180" height="129" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="Ministry of Magic (concept artwork for HP5 movie).jpg" data-image-key="Ministry_of_Magic_(concept_artwork_for_HP5_movie).jpg" /></a> 	 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:Ministry_of_Magic_(concept_artwork_for_HP5_movie).jpg" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption">The fountain in the Ministry Altrium</p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure>
<p>The fountain was located halfway down the hall of the <a href="/wiki/Atrium" title="Atrium">Atrium</a>, on level 8 of the Ministry. The large golden statues are located in the middle of the pool, and the tallest of them all was a wizard with a wand pointing straight into the air. Around this statue were statues of a witch, a centaur, a goblin, and a house-elf. Jets of water came from the ends of the two wands, the centaurs arrow, the tip of the goblin's hat, and each of the house-elf's ears. All proceeds from the fountain were given to <a href="/wiki/St_Mungo%27s_Hospital_for_Magical_Maladies_and_Injuries" title="St Mungo&#39;s Hospital for Magical Maladies and Injuries">St Mungo's Hospital for Magical Maladies and Injuries</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-OP7_1-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OP7-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Messages_of_the_Fountain">Messages of the Fountain</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Fountain_of_Magical_Brethren?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Messages of the Fountain">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Fountain_of_Magical_Brethren?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Messages of the Fountain"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The witch and wizard are the two focal points of the fountain, with the other, "lower" beings looking up in "awe and adoration."<sup id="cite_ref-OP7_1-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OP7-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> This is very unrealistic, as centaurs and goblins consider themselves superior to wizards and witches. House-elves, on the other hand, have no other joy in life other than to be in the service of wizards. The fountain supposedly represented harmony in the wizarding world, although <a href="/wiki/Albus_Dumbledore" title="Albus Dumbledore">Albus Dumbledore</a> thought otherwise, saying that wizards and witches have mistreated and abused their fellows for a long time and they are now reaping what they've sown.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="History">History</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Fountain_of_Magical_Brethren?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: History">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Fountain_of_Magical_Brethren?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: History"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>As <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> entered the Ministry in <a href="/wiki/1995" title="1995">1995</a> to attend his hearing for <a href="/wiki/Attack_in_Little_Whinging" title="Attack in Little Whinging">inadvertently breaching</a> the <a href="/wiki/Decree_for_the_Reasonable_Restriction_of_Underage_Sorcery" title="Decree for the Reasonable Restriction of Underage Sorcery">Decree for the Reasonable Restriction of Underage Sorcery</a> and <a href="/wiki/International_Statute_of_Wizarding_Secrecy" title="International Statute of Wizarding Secrecy">International Statute of Wizarding Secrecy</a>, he saw the fountain and the sign for all the proceedings: he mentally noted that if he was not expelled, he would donate ten galleons.<sup id="cite_ref-OP7_1-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OP7-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> Upon being acquitted, Harry placed not just ten galleons, but the entire contents of his wallet into the fountain in triumph.<sup id="cite_ref-OP9_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OP9-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>During the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_the_Department_of_Mysteries" title="Battle of the Department of Mysteries">Battle of the Department of Mysteries</a> in <a href="/wiki/1996" title="1996">1996</a>, much of the Atrium was heavily damaged. The floor was gouged and pocketed, the security booth was burned and the fountain largely destroyed. It also participated in the battle when Dumbledore brought the statues to life for various purposes in his duel with Voldemort. The wizard statue (which had previously had its head blown off by <a href="/wiki/Bellatrix_Lestrange" title="Bellatrix Lestrange">Bellatrix Lestrange</a>) acted as a bodyguard for <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>, the witch pinned Bellatrix to the floor to prevent her from intervening on her master's behalf, the centaur (minus one arm) took a <a href="/wiki/Killing_Curse" title="Killing Curse">Killing Curse</a> meant for Dumbledore, and the goblin and house-elf figures departed to alert Ministry personnel as to the disturbance, as well as being the only ones to survive largely intact. The head of the wizard was turned into a <a href="/wiki/Portkey" title="Portkey">Portkey</a> by Albus Dumbledore, which sent <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> to <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-OP36_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OP36-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>After the <a href="/wiki/Fall_of_the_Ministry_of_Magic" title="Fall of the Ministry of Magic">fall</a> of the Ministry in <a href="/wiki/1997" title="1997">1997</a>, the fountain was temporarily replaced by the <a href="/wiki/Magic_is_Might_statue" title="Magic is Might statue">Magic is Might monument</a>, a dark statue of a wizard and a witch sitting atop enormous thrones made of&#160;powerless <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggless</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-DH12_4-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH12-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> It is unknown whether it was restored to its original form after the death of <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a> in <a href="/wiki/1998" title="1998">1998</a>, or if it was replaced by a new statue correctly promoting equality amongst magical beings once the Ministry was reformed by <a href="/wiki/Kingsley_Shacklebolt" title="Kingsley Shacklebolt">Kingsley Shacklebolt</a>.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Fountain_of_Magical_Brethren?veaction=edit&amp;section=4" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Fountain_of_Magical_Brethren?action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<figure class="thumb tright show-info-icon" style="width: 182px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/8/8e/FountainofMagicalBrethenWU.png/revision/latest?cb=20190725165623" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/8/8e/FountainofMagicalBrethenWU.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/180?cb=20190725165623" decoding="async" width="180" height="158" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="FountainofMagicalBrethenWU.png" data-image-key="FountainofMagicalBrethenWU.png" /></a> 	 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:FountainofMagicalBrethenWU.png" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption">The Fountain as depicted in <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter:_Wizards_Unite" title="Harry Potter: Wizards Unite">Harry Potter: Wizards Unite</a></i></p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure>
<ul><li>Unlike the books, in the films the fountain contains the <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch</a>, the <a href="/wiki/Centaur" title="Centaur">centaur</a>, the <a href="/wiki/House-elf" title="House-elf">house-elf</a> and the <a href="/wiki/Goblin" title="Goblin">goblin</a> while a gigantic statue of the wizard stands on a pedestal further into the <a href="/wiki/Atrium" title="Atrium">Atrium</a>.</li>
<li>In <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Deathly_Hallows:_Part_1_(video_game)" title="Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 1 (video game)">Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 1 (video game)</a></i> it is revealed what was done to this fountain. It was repaired and placed in the depths of the Ministry (among a detention area below the <a href="/wiki/Wizengamot" title="Wizengamot">Wizengamot</a> Courtrooms), directly below the larger golden statue of the wizard that remained on its plinth in the Atrium.</li></ul>
