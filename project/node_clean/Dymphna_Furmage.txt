<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Dymphna_Furmage?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Dymphna_Furmage?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>

<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_life">Early life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Dymphna_Furmage?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Early life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Dymphna_Furmage?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Early life"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Ms. Furmage was born in <a href="/wiki/1612" class="mw-redirect" title="1612">1612</a>, somewhere in the <a href="/wiki/Great_Britain" title="Great Britain">British Isles</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-PSG_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PSG-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-COSG_2-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COSG-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-POAG_3-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-POAG-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> If she enrolled at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a>, she attended the school from around <a href="/wiki/1623" class="mw-redirect" title="1623">1623</a> to <a href="/wiki/1630" class="mw-redirect" title="1630">1630</a>.
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Adulthood">Adulthood</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Dymphna_Furmage?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Adulthood">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Dymphna_Furmage?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Adulthood"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Sometime in the mid to late <a href="/wiki/17th_century" title="17th century">seventeenth century</a>, Furmage went on holiday to <a href="/wiki/Cornwall" title="Cornwall">Cornwall</a>, in southwest <a href="/wiki/England" title="England">England</a>. There, she was the victim of an abduction by <a href="/wiki/Pixie" title="Pixie">Pixies</a>, a species of pesky <a href="/wiki/Beast" title="Beast">beast</a> endemic to the region. As a result Ms. Furmage developed a mortal dread of them for the rest of her life.<sup id="cite_ref-PSG_1-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PSG-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-COSG_2-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COSG-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-POAG_3-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-POAG-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<figure class="thumb tright show-info-icon" style="width: 152px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/3/3c/DymphnaFurmagePOA.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20120107015749" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/3/3c/DymphnaFurmagePOA.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/150?cb=20120107015749" decoding="async" width="150" height="244" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="DymphnaFurmagePOA.jpg" data-image-key="DymphnaFurmagePOA.jpg" /></a> 	 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:DymphnaFurmagePOA.jpg" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption">Ms Furmage is abducted by <a href="/wiki/Pixie" title="Pixie">Pixies</a></p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure>
<p>Later, and most assuredly fuelled by her fear of the <a href="/wiki/Pixie" title="Pixie">Pixies</a>, she strongly campaigned for the <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">British Ministry of Magic</a> to "humanely [destroy]" all of the species. She failed, however, and ended up dying in <a href="/wiki/1698" class="mw-redirect" title="1698">1698</a>, still in mortal dread of the mischievous <a href="/wiki/Creature" title="Creature">creatures</a>. For her efforts, she was included on a <a href="/wiki/Famous_Witches_and_Wizards_Cards" title="Famous Witches and Wizards Cards">Famous Witch Card</a>, as part of the <a href="/wiki/Chocolate_Frog_Card" title="Chocolate Frog Card">Chocolate Frog Card</a> series.<sup id="cite_ref-PSG_1-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PSG-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-COSG_2-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COSG-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-POAG_3-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-POAG-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Dymphna_Furmage?veaction=edit&amp;section=4" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Dymphna_Furmage?action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>The name "Dymphna" is the anglicised form of <i>Damhnait</i>. <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Dymphna" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Dymphna">Saint Dymphna</a> was a seventh-century <a href="/wiki/Ireland" title="Ireland">Irish</a> saint who was martyred by her father. She is the patron saint of the mentally ill.</li>
<li>"Furmage" is an alternate form of the Old French word "fromage," meaning cheese. It is descended from the Latin "formare," meaning "to shape" or "to fashion."</li></ul>

<ul><li>The <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">British Ministry of Magic</a> was founded in <a href="/wiki/1707" class="mw-redirect" title="1707">1707</a>, nearly ten years after her <a href="/wiki/Death" title="Death">death</a>. it is possible that she tried to convince the <a href="/wiki/Wizard%27s_Council" class="mw-redirect" title="Wizard&#39;s Council">Wizard's Council</a>, or that she returned as a <a href="/wiki/Ghost" title="Ghost">ghost</a>.</li></ul>
