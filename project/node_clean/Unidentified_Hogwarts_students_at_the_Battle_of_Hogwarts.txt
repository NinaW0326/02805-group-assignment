<p>This <b>individual</b> was a student at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> at least during the <a href="/wiki/1997%E2%80%931998_school_year" title="1997–1998 school year">1997–1998 school year</a>. She was seen tending to injured people next to <a href="/wiki/Molly_Weasley" title="Molly Weasley">Molly Weasley</a> during the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a>. It is unknown if she survived.
</p>
<dl><dt>Behind the scenes</dt></dl>
<ul><li>The girl only appeared as an extra in the film, and was never identified.</li>
<li>She may be <a href="/wiki/Luca_Caruso" title="Luca Caruso">Luca Caruso</a>, <a href="/wiki/Alice_Tolipan" title="Alice Tolipan">Alice Tolipan</a>, or another as-of-yet unidentified person. However, it is equally likely that she is a recognised character, given that only her hair was seen.</li></ul>

This <b>individual</b> was a student at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> and was <a href="/wiki/Sorting_ceremony" title="Sorting ceremony">sorted</a> into <a href="/wiki/Ravenclaw" title="Ravenclaw">Ravenclaw</a>.
