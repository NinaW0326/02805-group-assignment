<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Foul-smelling_Snatcher?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Foul-smelling_Snatcher?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_life">Early life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Foul-smelling_Snatcher?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Early life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Foul-smelling_Snatcher?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Early life"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>This was born sometime in or before <a href="/wiki/1980" title="1980">1980</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-birth_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-birth-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> most likely to a <a href="/wiki/Pure-blood" title="Pure-blood">Pure</a> or <a href="/wiki/Half-blood" title="Half-blood">Half-blood</a> family,<sup id="cite_ref-blood_2-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-blood-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> somewhere in the <a href="/wiki/Great_Britain" title="Great Britain">British Isles</a>. He might have <a href="/wiki/Part-troll" title="Part-troll">troll ancestry</a>, as noted by <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ronald Weasley</a> in <a href="/wiki/1997" title="1997">1997</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-DH19_3-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH19-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> He most likely acquired <a href="/wiki/Blackthorn_wand" title="Blackthorn wand">his wand</a> prior to his magical education. After the <a href="/wiki/Fall_of_the_Ministry_of_Magic" title="Fall of the Ministry of Magic">Death Eater coup</a> over the <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">Ministry of Magic</a>, he joined a <a href="/wiki/Snatchers" title="Snatchers">Snatcher</a> chapter.<sup id="cite_ref-DH19_3-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH19-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="As_a_Snatcher">As a Snatcher</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Foul-smelling_Snatcher?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: As a Snatcher">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Foul-smelling_Snatcher?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: As a Snatcher"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<div class="quote">
<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows">"<i>Anyway, they had a row about whether I was Stan or not. It was a bit pathetic to be honest, but there were still five of them and only one of me, and they'd taken my wand. Then two of them got into a fight and while the others were distracted I managed to hit the one holding me in the stomach, grabbed his wand, Disarmed the bloke holding mine, and Disapparated. I didn't do it so well. Splinched myself again and I came out miles from where you were. By the time I got back to that bit of riverbank where we'd been… you were gone.</i>"</span></dd>
<dd>&#8212;<a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ronald Weasley</a> tells <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> and <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione Granger</a> of his encounter with <a href="/wiki/Snatchers" title="Snatchers">Snatchers</a>.<sup class="noprint"><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Deathly_Hallows" title="Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows">[src]</a></sup></dd>
<dd></dd></dl></div><p>In <a href="/wiki/December" title="December">December</a>, <a href="/wiki/1997" title="1997">1997</a>, this Snatcher's gang caught <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ronald Weasley</a>, who had abandoned <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> and <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione Granger</a> during their <a href="/wiki/Horcrux_hunt" class="mw-redirect" title="Horcrux hunt">quest to destroy Lord Voldemort's Horcruxes</a>. After telling them he was <a href="/wiki/Stanley_Shunpike" title="Stanley Shunpike">Stan Shunpike</a>, Ron managed to distract them enough so that he took back <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley%27s_wand" class="mw-redirect" title="Ronald Weasley&#39;s wand">his wand</a>, as well as this <a href="/wiki/Blackthorn_wand" title="Blackthorn wand">individual's</a> and <a href="/wiki/Apparition" title="Apparition">Disapparated</a> from the place.<sup id="cite_ref-DH19_3-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH19-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>After the <a href="/wiki/Second_Wizarding_War" title="Second Wizarding War">Second Wizarding War</a>, this individual was likely captured and arrested for his crimes against <a href="/wiki/Muggle-born" title="Muggle-born">Muggle-borns</a> under <a href="/wiki/Kingsley_Shacklebolt" title="Kingsley Shacklebolt">Kingsley Shacklebolt</a>'s de-corrupted <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">Ministry of Magic</a>.
</p>
