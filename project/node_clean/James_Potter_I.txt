<p><b>James Potter</b> (<a href="/wiki/27_March" title="27 March">27 March</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-DH16_1-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH16-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/1960" title="1960">1960</a> – <a href="/wiki/31_October" title="31 October">31 October</a>, <a href="/wiki/1981" title="1981">1981</a>),<sup id="cite_ref-DH16_1-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH16-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> also known as <b>Prongs</b>, was an <a href="/wiki/England" title="England">English</a> <a href="/wiki/Pure-blood" title="Pure-blood">pure-blood</a><sup id="cite_ref-blood_3-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-blood-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-blood2_4-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-blood2-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> and the only son of <a href="/wiki/Fleamont_Potter" title="Fleamont Potter">Fleamont</a> and <a href="/wiki/Euphemia_Potter" title="Euphemia Potter">Euphemia Potter</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-Pottermore_11-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Pottermore-11">&#91;11&#93;</a></sup> He attended <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> from <a href="/wiki/1971" title="1971">1971</a>-<a href="/wiki/1978" title="1978">1978</a> and was sorted into <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor" title="Gryffindor">Gryffindor</a>. When James started at Hogwarts, he met and became best friends with three fellow Gryffindor students: <a href="/wiki/Remus_Lupin" title="Remus Lupin">Remus Lupin</a>, <a href="/wiki/Sirius_Black" title="Sirius Black">Sirius Black</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Peter_Pettigrew" title="Peter Pettigrew">Peter Pettigrew</a>. He also met <a href="/wiki/Severus_Snape" title="Severus Snape">Severus Snape</a>, a <a href="/wiki/Slytherin" title="Slytherin">Slytherin</a> student with whom he became bitter rivals. During his seventh year, James was appointed <a href="/wiki/Head_Boy_or_Girl" title="Head Boy or Girl">Head Boy</a> and began dating <a href="/wiki/Lily_J._Potter" title="Lily J. Potter">Lily Evans</a>. 
</p><p>After graduating from Hogwarts, he <a href="/wiki/Wedding_of_James_Potter_I_and_Lily_Evans" title="Wedding of James Potter I and Lily Evans">married</a> Lily and together they had a son, <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry James Potter</a>, of whom he made Sirius Black the godfather. James, Lily, and their friends all fought in the <a href="/wiki/First_Wizarding_War" title="First Wizarding War">First Wizarding War</a> as members of the <a href="/wiki/Order_of_the_Phoenix" title="Order of the Phoenix">Order of the Phoenix</a>; he and his wife defied <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a> three times. However, James and Lily were forced to go into hiding after a <a href="/wiki/Sybill_Trelawney%27s_first_prophecy" title="Sybill Trelawney&#39;s first prophecy">prophecy</a> was made concerning Voldemort and their infant son. James and Lily were ultimately betrayed to Voldemort by one of their close friends (Peter Pettigrew).  
</p><p>As a result of this, James was tragically murdered by Voldemort on <a href="/wiki/Hallowe%27en" title="Hallowe&#39;en">Hallowe'en</a> in 1981, along with his wife while they were trying to protect Harry. 
</p><p>James briefly appeared again in May <a href="/wiki/1998" title="1998">1998</a> through the <a href="/wiki/Resurrection_Stone" title="Resurrection Stone">Resurrection Stone</a>. He appeared through it along with Lily (his wife), Sirius Black and Remus Lupin (his friends). After the <a href="/wiki/Second_Wizarding_War" title="Second Wizarding War">Second Wizarding War</a>, James posthumously had three grandchildren through his son: <a href="/wiki/James_Potter_II" title="James Potter II">James Sirius</a>, <a href="/wiki/Albus_Potter" title="Albus Potter">Albus Severus</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Lily_L._Potter" title="Lily L. Potter">Lily Luna Potter</a>, the first of whom was named after his grandfather.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/James_Potter_I?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/James_Potter_I?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span id="Early_life_(1960-1971)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_life_.281960-1971.29">Early life <span class="template-c">(1960-1971)</span></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/James_Potter_I?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Early life (1960-1971)">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/James_Potter_I?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Early life (1960-1971)"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<div class="quote">
<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;Pottermore">"<i>They had quite given up hope of a son or daughter when, to their shock and surprise, Euphemia found that she was pregnant and their beloved boy, James, was born.</i>"</span></dd>
<dd>&#8212;James is born to Fleamont and Euphemia Potter<sup class="noprint"><a href="/wiki/Pottermore" title="Pottermore">[src]</a></sup></dd>
<dd></dd></dl></div><p>James Potter was born on <a href="/wiki/27_March" title="27 March">27 March</a>, <a href="/wiki/1960" title="1960">1960</a> to <a href="/wiki/Fleamont_Potter" title="Fleamont Potter">Fleamont Potter</a> and his wife <a href="/wiki/Euphemia_Potter" title="Euphemia Potter">Euphemia</a>. Fleamont was an accomplished, yet retired cosmetic <a href="/wiki/Potioneer" title="Potioneer">potioneer</a> and entrepreneur best known for inventing <a href="/wiki/Sleekeazy%27s_Hair_Potion" title="Sleekeazy&#39;s Hair Potion">Sleekeazy's Hair Potion</a> in <a href="/wiki/1926" title="1926">1926</a>. Both Fleamont and Euphemia were elderly even by <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizarding</a> standards.<sup id="cite_ref-Pottermore_11-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Pottermore-11">&#91;11&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Having been conceived so late in his parents' lives, he was raised in a doting household with little material limits, and pampered a great deal. Hence, it was no surprise that he grew to be proud, arrogant, and boastful, but deep down still a good person.<sup id="cite_ref-JKR_16-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-JKR-16">&#91;16&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<p>James started his first year at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> in <a href="/wiki/1971" title="1971">1971</a> and was Sorted into <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor" title="Gryffindor">Gryffindor</a> house. While there, he befriended fellow Gryffindors <a href="/wiki/Sirius_Black" title="Sirius Black">Sirius Black</a>, <a href="/wiki/Remus_Lupin" title="Remus Lupin">Remus Lupin</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Peter_Pettigrew" title="Peter Pettigrew">Peter Pettigrew</a>, and together they formed a small gang known only to themselves as the <a href="/wiki/Marauders" title="Marauders">Marauders</a>. Though more mischievous than diligent, James proved to be a clever student. At some point during his education he became <a href="/wiki/Chaser" title="Chaser">Chaser</a> for <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor_Quidditch_team" title="Gryffindor Quidditch team">his house's Quidditch team</a>, in which he was completely aware of his talent. It is possible that he also collected <a href="/wiki/Chocolate_Frog_Card" title="Chocolate Frog Card">Chocolate Frog Cards</a>, as at an unknown time, the <a href="/wiki/History_of_Magic" title="History of Magic">History of Magic</a> <a href="/wiki/Professor" title="Professor">teacher</a>, <a href="/wiki/Cuthbert_Binns" title="Cuthbert Binns">Professor Cuthbert Binns</a>, gave him the <a href="/wiki/Circe" title="Circe">Circe</a> card, which later came into the possession of <a href="/wiki/Quirinus_Quirrell" title="Quirinus Quirrell">Quirinus Quirrell</a>, who kept it in <a href="/wiki/Gringotts_Wizarding_Bank" title="Gringotts Wizarding Bank">Gringotts Wizarding Bank</a> in <a href="/wiki/Vault_998" title="Vault 998">Vault 998</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-PSG_17-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PSG-17">&#91;17&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<p>As second years, James, Sirius, and Peter figured out that Remus was a <a href="/wiki/Werewolf" title="Werewolf">werewolf</a>, but rather than abandoning him (James, in particular, insisted this monthly hazard did not mean Remus was truly abnormal), they spent three years learning to become <a href="/wiki/Animagus" title="Animagus">Animagi</a> in order to accompany Remus in his transformations. In his fifth year, James managed to perfect the transformation and become a <a href="/wiki/Deer" title="Deer">stag</a> Animagus, which earned him the nickname "Prongs". Using their new nicknames, the four friends created the <a href="/wiki/Marauder%27s_Map" title="Marauder&#39;s Map">Marauder's Map</a>, which displayed the <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Castle" title="Hogwarts Castle">Hogwarts Castle</a> area and everyone within it —including all staff, ghosts and students — in their current location at a given time, and thus made sneaking out of the castle and avoiding teachers much easier.<sup id="cite_ref-POA18_5-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-POA18-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>For the vast majority of his time at school, James was somewhat an obnoxious youth with a love of showing off and exceptional self-confidence that bordered on arrogance. <a href="/wiki/Severus_Snape" title="Severus Snape">Severus Snape</a> once said that he was so arrogant that "criticism simply bounced off him".<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP24_18-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP24-18">&#91;18&#93;</a></sup> This rebellious attitude was notably shaped by the negative qualities he developed, such as the habit of ruffling his already messy hair to make it even untidier to look as though he had just alighted from his <a href="/wiki/Broomstick" title="Broomstick">broomstick</a>. Also <a href="/wiki/Hex" title="Hex">hexing</a> innocent students for no other reason than because he could (especially Snape, a <a href="/wiki/Slytherin" title="Slytherin">Slytherin</a> in the same year as him). These qualities came about as a result of his pampered and spoiled upbringing. 
</p>

<p>James and Snape had a strong rivalry throughout their time at Hogwarts. To Remus and Sirius, the reason this rivalry began was due to Snape's jealousy over James' talent at <a href="/wiki/Quidditch" title="Quidditch">Quidditch</a> and his popularity, as well as Snape's interest in the <a href="/wiki/Dark_Arts" title="Dark Arts">Dark Arts</a>, which James despised. However, it was later revealed that James and Sirius had got off on the wrong foot with Snape from the beginning: when they first met Snape during their first year on the <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Express" title="Hogwarts Express">Hogwarts Express</a>, they insulted him (setting a solid foundation for rivalry), and their further interactions only served to make their rivalry intense and permanent <i>— </i>Snape followed the <a href="/wiki/Marauders" title="Marauders">Marauders</a> around, looking for reasons to get them expelled. James, in turn, used the hexes Snape himself had made up against him.<sup id="cite_ref-DH33_19-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH33-19">&#91;19&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>At some point in their school years, Snape grew very interested in where Remus went every month, and saw <a href="/wiki/Poppy_Pomfrey" title="Poppy Pomfrey">Madam Pomfrey</a> and Remus going to the <a href="/wiki/Shrieking_Shack" title="Shrieking Shack">Shrieking Shack</a> using a secret passage under the <a href="/wiki/Whomping_Willow" title="Whomping Willow">Whomping Willow</a>. Sirius had, as a joke, told him that the trick to getting past the Whomping Willow was to poke the knot at the bottom. Snape followed Sirius's directions, and was nearly at the Shrieking Shack when James pulled him back. At great risk to his own life, James saved Severus from certain death at the claws of the Remus in werewolf form, but Snape adamantly refused to believe that his old rival would have done him a favour, believing that James had only saved him in order to avoid expulsion, and despised being in his debt.<sup id="cite_ref-POA14_20-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-POA14-20">&#91;20&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-POA18_5-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-POA18-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<p>James spent much of his academic career chasing after <a href="/wiki/Lily_J._Potter" title="Lily J. Potter">Lily Evans</a>, an attractive <a href="/wiki/Muggle-born" title="Muggle-born">Muggle-born</a> fellow Gryffindor from Snape's hometown of <a href="/wiki/Cokeworth" title="Cokeworth">Cokeworth</a> who was secretly Snape's best friend and childhood crush since before they both started at Hogwarts. Lily was among those who were not so impressed of James, considering him arrogant and obnoxious. She once demanded James to quit jinxing Snape for fun. The former and his friends were in the middle of bullying, with a spell of Snape's own invention. Lily told him to stop and James responded with, "<i>I will if you'll go out with me, Evans</i>." When James took the <a href="/wiki/Levicorpus" title="Levicorpus">jinx</a> off, Snape in his humiliation and anger, called Lily a "<i>filthy little <a href="/wiki/Mudblood" title="Mudblood">Mudblood</a>.</i>" James demanded him to apologise, but Lily responded that James was as bad as Snape and she would not date him "<i>even if it were a choice between him and the <a href="/wiki/Giant_Squid" title="Giant Squid">Giant Squid</a></i>".<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP28_15-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP28-15">&#91;15&#93;</a></sup> Snape's friendship with the vivacious and pretty Lily may have sparked envy in James and further fuelled the hateful enmity between the two.<sup id="cite_ref-JKR_16-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-JKR-16">&#91;16&#93;</a></sup>

<p>At the age of sixteen, James's parents allowed <a href="/wiki/Sirius_Black" title="Sirius Black">Sirius</a> to move in with them so he could escape his own horrible <a href="/wiki/House_of_Black" title="House of Black">family</a>, and Sirius soon became an unofficial member of their family. By the time he was at his seventh year, James had matured to the point that he lost the more mischievous aspects of his personality, and was even appointed <a href="/wiki/Head_Boy_or_Girl" title="Head Boy or Girl">Head Boy</a> alongside Lily, despite the fact that he had not been a <a href="/wiki/Prefect" title="Prefect">prefect</a>. He stopped hexing bystanders for fun, though he still secretly hexed Snape, and in turn, Snape never lost an opportunity to hex him back, although it's implied that James was willing to stop for Lily's sake.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP29_21-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP29-21">&#91;21&#93;</a></sup> He also finally managed to impress Lily, and began dating her at this time.
</p><p>In late <a href="/wiki/1977" title="1977">1977</a>, Lily and James had a disastrous first meeting with Lily's sister <a href="/wiki/Petunia_Dursley" title="Petunia Dursley">Petunia Evans</a> and her then-fiancé, <a href="/wiki/Vernon_Dursley" title="Vernon Dursley">Vernon Dursley</a>, which presumably took place over <a href="/wiki/Christmas" title="Christmas">Christmas</a> break, as they were both still at Hogwarts. When Vernon tried to impress James with his car, the wizard in turn boasted of his own material wealth, and a row ensued. The evening ended with both Vernon and Petunia storming out of the restaurant and Lily bursting into tears, with James promising he'd apologise and make it up (which, due to his and Lily's untimely deaths, never happened). Petunia married by the end of that year. Though Lily and James were invited to the <a href="/wiki/Wedding_of_Vernon_Dursley_and_Petunia_Evans" title="Wedding of Vernon Dursley and Petunia Evans">wedding</a>, Petunia refused to have Lily as a <a href="/wiki/Bridesmaid" title="Bridesmaid">bridesmaid</a>, upsetting her deeply. At the reception, Vernon would not talk to James, but described him as "<i>some magician</i>" within his earshot.<sup id="cite_ref-dursleystory_22-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-dursleystory-22">&#91;22&#93;</a></sup>

Sirius and James used their wands to raise the <a href="/wiki/Police_car" title="Police car">police car</a> that had been chasing them, and their attackers crashed into it.<sup id="cite_ref-24" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-24">&#91;24&#93;</a></sup> During the course of the next three years, James and Lily defied the Dark Lord three times as the war continued. The first was when they refused to join Voldemort's side when he attempted to recruit them.<sup id="cite_ref-pottercast130_25-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-pottercast130-25">&#91;25&#93;</a></sup>

James also used his wealth to support Remus, who was unemployable due to his status as a <a href="/wiki/Werewolf" title="Werewolf">werewolf</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-26" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-26">&#91;26&#93;</a></sup>

<p>James was a tall, thin man with hazel eyes and untidy black hair that stuck up at the back.<sup id="cite_ref-eyes_10-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-eyes-10">&#91;10&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-hair2_9-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-hair2-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup> During his <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts</a> years, he had an indefinable air of having been well cared for and even adored. As late as his fifth year, he started wearing glasses, though he had not in his first year.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP28_15-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP28-15">&#91;15&#93;</a></sup>

However, as observed by Harry in Snape's worst memory, James' nose was slightly longer than his son's.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP28_15-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP28-15">&#91;15&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>In his Animagus form, James was a tall and powerful red stag, large enough to subdue an adolescent werewolf alongside Sirius' bear-like black dog form.<sup id="cite_ref-POA18_5-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-POA18-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Personality_and_traits">Personality and traits</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/James_Potter_I?veaction=edit&amp;section=13" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Personality and traits">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/James_Potter_I?action=edit&amp;section=13" title="Edit section: Personality and traits"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<div class="quote">
<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows"><b>Severus Snape</b>: "<i>He fancies you, James Potter fancies you! And he’s not... everyone thinks... big Quidditch hero —</i>"</span></dd>
<dd><b>Lily Evans</b>: "<i>I know James Potter’s an arrogant toerag. I don’t need you to tell me that.</i>"</dd>
<dd>&#8212; Snape and <a href="/wiki/Lily" title="Lily">Lily</a> discussing James in their fifth year<sup class="noprint"><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Deathly_Hallows" title="Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows">[src]</a></sup></dd></dl>
</div>
<p>James was a clever and talented wizard, but very mischievous in his youth. He was arrogant and boastful, and occasionally bullied and jinxed other students just for fun, particularly his long-time rival, <a href="/wiki/Severus_Snape" title="Severus Snape">Severus Snape</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP28_15-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP28-15">&#91;15&#93;</a></sup> However, even in this stage of arrogance, he still displayed some positive qualities of character. Despite being a <a href="/wiki/Pure-blood" title="Pure-blood">pure-blood</a>, he strongly disagreed with the concept of <a href="/wiki/Blood_status" title="Blood status">blood purity</a> and was disgusted with any prejudice towards <a href="/wiki/Muggle-borns" class="mw-redirect" title="Muggle-borns">Muggle-borns</a>, and did not hold prejudice towards <a href="/wiki/Werewolf" title="Werewolf">werewolves</a> (such as <a href="/wiki/Remus_Lupin" title="Remus Lupin">Remus Lupin</a>, who was one of his dearest friends). This loyalty would extend to his adulthood when he used his wealth to support the unemployable Remus. Additionally, even despite his bad relationship with Snape, James never wanted him dead and even risked his life to prevent Snape from being killed by Lupin in his wolf form, showing he may humiliate but even at his worst he would never stoop to the level of murder by inaction.<sup id="cite_ref-POA18_5-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-POA18-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> Snape would later point out that James was lazy.<sup id="cite_ref-OTPF_36-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OTPF-36">&#91;36&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>James ultimately matured and changed his ways; he lost some of his arrogance and stopped using his magic irresponsibly on others. He became enough of a responsible student and leader to be <a href="/wiki/Head_Boy_or_Girl" title="Head Boy or Girl">Head Boy</a> in his final year, and later a member of the original <a href="/wiki/Order_of_the_Phoenix" title="Order of the Phoenix">Order of the Phoenix</a>. Though he never completely lost his rebellious side, it was clear James had left Hogwarts a far better person than he had been upon entering it.
</p><p>His best quality was his willingness to sacrifice himself for his wife and son, as demonstrated when he told Lily to take Harry and run while he held off <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Voldemort</a>, proving himself to be a true <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor" title="Gryffindor">Gryffindor</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-DH17_30-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH17-30">&#91;30&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<p>Since James was once asked by Voldemort to join his <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a>, this was clear proof that James was a highly powerful and accomplished wizard. Remus once stated that James had been one of the cleverest Hogwarts students of his time,<sup id="cite_ref-POA18_5-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-POA18-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> and he was able to conjure a corporeal stag <a href="/wiki/Patronus_Charm" title="Patronus Charm">Patronus</a> (a mark of superior magical ability).<sup id="cite_ref-Patronus_37-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Patronus-37">&#91;37&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<ul><li><b><a href="/wiki/Animagus" title="Animagus">Animagus</a></b>: James was able to become an Animagus at the early age of fifteen, an incredibly difficult, complex and even dangerous transfiguration process that only the most proficient can accomplish. His Animagus form allowed him to transform into a <a href="/wiki/Deer" title="Deer">Stag</a> at will.<sup id="cite_ref-POA18_5-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-POA18-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Transfiguration" title="Transfiguration">Transfiguration</a></b>: James' wand was said to be <i>"excellent for Transfiguration"</i>,<sup id="cite_ref-PS5_12-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PS5-12">&#91;12&#93;</a></sup> with his own skills showing his exceptional proficiency in this notably very difficult, <a href="/wiki/Science" title="Science">scientific</a> and even dangerous branch of <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magic</a>. He was so skilled in <a href="/wiki/Human_Transfiguration" title="Human Transfiguration">Human Transfiguration</a> , that he learned how to become an Animagus.<sup id="cite_ref-POA18_5-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-POA18-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> He also very likely succeeded in this <a href="/wiki/Transfiguration_(class)" title="Transfiguration (class)">subject</a> whilst as a Hogwarts student.</li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Potion" title="Potion">Potions</a></b>: James was exceptionally proficient in potion-making - at the age of fifteen, he successfully brewed an effective <a href="/wiki/Animagus_potion" title="Animagus potion">Animagus potion</a>, which testified to his remarkable talent in the subject, as the potion was known to be highly complex, difficult to brew, and had great consequences if done incorrectly.<sup id="cite_ref-PP_38-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PP-38">&#91;38&#93;</a></sup> Remus also described the Animagus transformation was a very difficult branch of magic that <i>"could go horribly wrong"</i>, and the fact that James not only succeeded at it,<sup id="cite_ref-POA18_5-10" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-POA18-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> but also helped Peter to manage it as well was concrete evidence of his being an expert potioneer.</li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Charm" title="Charm">Charms</a></b>: James was highly talented in charm-work from a young age - he and his fellow Marauders managed to create the magically-complex <a href="/wiki/Marauder%27s_Map" title="Marauder&#39;s Map">Marauder's Map</a> during their teenage years, with the <a href="/wiki/Homonculous_Charm" title="Homonculous Charm">Homonculous Charm</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-POA18_5-11" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-POA18-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> along with a <a href="/wiki/Charm_to_insult_Severus_Snape" title="Charm to insult Severus Snape">Charm to insult Severus Snape</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-POA14_20-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-POA14-20">&#91;20&#93;</a></sup> He was also able to cast the highly advanced <a href="/wiki/Patronus_Charm" title="Patronus Charm">Patronus Charm</a>, conjuring a corporeal Patronus whose form was identical with his Animagus form, a stag.<sup id="cite_ref-accio-quote.org_13-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-accio-quote.org-13">&#91;13&#93;</a></sup> He could also cast simpler charms proficiently, such as the <a href="/wiki/Disarming_Charm" title="Disarming Charm">Disarming Charm</a> and <a href="/wiki/Scouring_Charm" title="Scouring Charm">Scouring Charm</a>, which he once used to bully Snape in his fifth year.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP28_15-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP28-15">&#91;15&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Duelling" title="Duelling">Duelling</a></b>: James was a highly skilful duellist. During his student years he was able to humiliate Severus Snape, who was already a skilled wizard in his youth, albeit with help from Sirius Black. He could cast several offensive spells competently, such as the <a href="/wiki/Disarming_Charm" title="Disarming Charm">Disarming Charm</a>, <a href="/wiki/Impediment_Jinx" title="Impediment Jinx">Impediment Jinx</a>, <i><a href="/wiki/Levicorpus" title="Levicorpus">Levicorpus</a></i> (along with its <a href="/wiki/Counter-jinx" title="Counter-jinx">counter-jinx</a> <i><a href="/wiki/Liberacorpus" title="Liberacorpus">Liberacorpus</a></i>) and other <a href="/wiki/Hex" title="Hex">hexes</a>, which he often used to bully Snape.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP28_15-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP28-15">&#91;15&#93;</a></sup> During the First Wizarding War he presumably took part in battles against the Death Eaters and other dark forces, surviving until the end of the war. He and his wife, Lily, also defied Voldemort himself three times, which showcased his exceptional talent in <a href="/wiki/Martial_magic" title="Martial magic">martial magic</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-DH33_19-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH33-19">&#91;19&#93;</a></sup></li></ul>

<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Parents">Parents</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/James_Potter_I?veaction=edit&amp;section=18" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Parents">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/James_Potter_I?action=edit&amp;section=18" title="Edit section: Parents"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<p>James was born into a very wealthy pure-blood family. He had an excellent relationship with his parents, who loved him very much. His father was Sorted in <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor" title="Gryffindor">Gryffindor</a> and used to tell him about how the house prized bravery and boldness above all other qualities. James' parents were on the older side when he was born and died before he was murdered in <a href="/wiki/1981" title="1981">1981</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-JKR_16-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-JKR-16">&#91;16&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<p>James's relationship with Lily Evans was initially a rocky one. While James was a popular student and talented <a href="/wiki/Quidditch" title="Quidditch">Quidditch</a> player, well-liked by most, Lily was unimpressed with him. This had to do with James' hostile relationship with her best friend, <a href="/wiki/Severus_Snape" title="Severus Snape">Severus Snape</a> and because of James' bad habit of <a href="/wiki/Hex" title="Hex">hexing</a> innocent students just for the fun of it.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP28_15-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP28-15">&#91;15&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>James had developed romantic feelings for Lily by his fifth year. He was always going out of his way to try to impress her, only to end up making a fool out of himself most of the time. She still regarded him as an arrogant bully, treating him with absolute disdain.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP28_15-10" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP28-15">&#91;15&#93;</a></sup> In their seventh year, however, Lily was finally willing to go out with James, after he smoothed out and stopped hexing people for the fun of it (though she was unaware he still hexed Snape).
</p><p>The two were <a href="/wiki/Head_Boy_or_Girl" title="Head Boy or Girl">Head Boy</a> and <a href="/wiki/Head_Boy_or_Girl" title="Head Boy or Girl">Head Girl</a> at Hogwarts and married soon after graduation. James and Lily were both members of the <a href="/wiki/Order_of_the_Phoenix" title="Order of the Phoenix">Order of the Phoenix</a>, and together defied <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Voldemort</a> himself three times. Their son, Harry James Potter, was born on <a href="/wiki/31_July" title="31 July">31 July</a>, <a href="/wiki/1980" title="1980">1980</a>.
</p>

On <a href="/wiki/Hallowe%27en" title="Hallowe&#39;en">Hallowe'en</a> night in 1981 they sacrificed themselves at 21 years old, in order to save him from <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a>, when he was just an infant.<sup id="cite_ref-DH17_30-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH17-30">&#91;30&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-DH33_19-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH33-19">&#91;19&#93;</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/Albus_Dumbledore" title="Albus Dumbledore">Albus Dumbledore</a> always maintained that Harry's love for his parents and the love they had in return for him, was his greatest power.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP23_43-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP23-43">&#91;43&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Harry regarded his parents with great reverence, and fiercely defended their memories. In 1995, he <a href="/wiki/Muggle_duelling" title="Muggle duelling">punched</a> <a href="/wiki/Draco_Malfoy" title="Draco Malfoy">Draco Malfoy</a> for insulting his mother,<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP19_44-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP19-44">&#91;44&#93;</a></sup> and he frequently became furious with <a href="/wiki/Severus_Snape" title="Severus Snape">Professor Snape</a>, who taunted Harry that James was not the great man others made him out to be.<sup id="cite_ref-POA14_20-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-POA14-20">&#91;20&#93;</a></sup> Much to Harry's horror, Snape was proven right in the spring of 1996, when Harry accidentally witnessed James bullying Snape in one of his memories during Harry's <a href="/wiki/Occlumency" title="Occlumency">Occlumency</a> lessons (via <a href="/wiki/Albus_Dumbledore%27s_Pensieve" class="mw-redirect" title="Albus Dumbledore&#39;s Pensieve">Albus Dumbledore's Pensieve</a>).<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP28_15-11" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP28-15">&#91;15&#93;</a></sup> However, <a href="/wiki/Remus_Lupin" title="Remus Lupin">Remus Lupin</a> and <a href="/wiki/Sirius_Black" title="Sirius Black">Sirius Black</a> assured him that his father was no longer the bully that he once was. <h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Vernon_Dursley">Vernon Dursley</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/James_Potter_I?veaction=edit&amp;section=21" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Vernon Dursley">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/James_Potter_I?action=edit&amp;section=21" title="Edit section: Vernon Dursley"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<p>James's first meeting with his brother-in-law <a href="/wiki/Vernon_Dursley" title="Vernon Dursley">Vernon Dursley</a> went badly. Vernon asked James what car he drove, but James described his racing <a href="/wiki/Broomstick" title="Broomstick">broom</a>, thus Vernon started patronising him and finally offended James by supposing out loud that <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizards</a> had to live on unemployment benefit. Having heard from the wizard that he had a fortune in solid gold, he grew angry and stormed out of the restaurant, where they had met with their wives. After that Vernon refused to talk with James, but described him, within James’ earshot, as "<i>some kind of amateur <a href="/wiki/Magician" title="Magician">magician</a></i>".<sup id="cite_ref-dursleystory_22-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-dursleystory-22">&#91;22&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>After Lily and James' deaths, Vernon and Petunia reluctantly adopted Harry due to the <a href="/wiki/Sacrificial_protection" title="Sacrificial protection">sacrificial protection</a> that shielded Harry from Voldemort. However, the Dursleys spent the following ten years doing everything in their power to prevent Harry from learning about his magical heritage and abilities, such as lying about how Lily and James died by attributing their deaths to a car crash.<sup id="cite_ref-PS4_6-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PS4-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<p>While at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts</a>, James became the very best of friends with <a href="/wiki/Sirius_Black" title="Sirius Black">Sirius Black</a>, <a href="/wiki/Remus_Lupin" title="Remus Lupin">Remus Lupin</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Peter_Pettigrew" title="Peter Pettigrew">Peter Pettigrew</a>, and the group called themselves "the Marauders". He met Sirius on the <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Express" title="Hogwarts Express">Hogwarts Express</a> in their first year, and they seemed to bond quickly. In their second year, when the group discovered that Remus was a <a href="/wiki/Werewolf" title="Werewolf">werewolf</a>, they refused to ostracise him, as most would, instead learning to become <a href="/wiki/Animagus" title="Animagus">Animagi</a> in order to keep him company during his transformations.<sup id="cite_ref-POA18_5-13" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-POA18-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> James insisted that Remus was not abnormal because of his condition, and later used his wealth to support Remus when <a href="/wiki/Anti-werewolf_legislation" title="Anti-werewolf legislation">anti-werewolf legislation</a> made it difficult for him to get a job.
</p><p>The four friends enjoyed untold popularity while at school and particularly liked playing pranks together; they also invented the <a href="/wiki/Marauder%27s_Map" title="Marauder&#39;s Map">Marauder's Map</a> and all joined the <a href="/wiki/Order_of_the_Phoenix" title="Order of the Phoenix">Order of the Phoenix</a>, after graduating from Hogwarts. Although he was very close to all of his friends, James was probably closest to Sirius, whom he loved as a brother and who moved in with James and his parents after running away from <a href="/wiki/12_Grimmauld_Place" title="12 Grimmauld Place">home</a> at age sixteen. James also chose Sirius as his best man at <a href="/wiki/Wedding_of_James_Potter_I_and_Lily_Evans" title="Wedding of James Potter I and Lily Evans">his wedding to Lily</a>, as well as his son's godfather. When the <a href="/wiki/Potter_family" title="Potter family">Potters</a> became <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a>'s targets in <a href="/wiki/1981" title="1981">1981</a>, James was adamant about Sirius being their <a href="/wiki/Secret-Keeper" class="mw-redirect" title="Secret-Keeper">Secret-Keeper</a>, though he agreed to Sirius's plan to make Peter the Secret-Keeper instead, to throw off the enemy. 
</p><p>James's unshakeable trust in his friends would be his undoing, as Pettigrew betrayed him, his wife, and his son to Voldemort. This betrayal was something Sirius and Remus later attempted to kill him for. The only thing that stopped them was Harry, who thought that his father would not want his best friends to become killers just for a traitor. Dumbledore later assured Harry that his belief had been right, saying that he knew James very well, both at Hogwarts and later and he was perfectly sure that James would have saved Pettigrew as well.
</p><p>The ending to the three Marauders who had stayed true to their friendship to the very end — James, Sirius, and Remus — was bittersweet: though they all lost their lives to the two wizarding wars, they died as heroes of the Order of the Phoenix, and in the case of James and Lupin, devoted fathers who had tried to give their sons a better world to live in.  
</p>

<p>James met Severus Snape on the <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Express" title="Hogwarts Express">Hogwarts Express</a> in their first year at Hogwarts, and the two immediately got off on the wrong foot. James scoffed at Snape's desire that he and <a href="/wiki/Lily_J._Potter" title="Lily J. Potter">Lily Evans</a> would be <a href="/wiki/Sorting_ceremony" title="Sorting ceremony">Sorted</a> into <a href="/wiki/Slytherin" title="Slytherin">Slytherin</a> house, while Snape sneered at James's hope that he would follow his father's footsteps and end up in <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor" title="Gryffindor">Gryffindor</a>. He and Sirius bestowed the mocking nickname of "<i>Snivellus</i>" on Snape at this first meeting.<sup id="cite_ref-DH33_19-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH33-19">&#91;19&#93;</a></sup> This enmity would continue for the rest of their school years and (on Snape's part) well after James's death. According to Sirius, Snape envied James's popularity and <a href="/wiki/Quidditch" title="Quidditch">Quidditch</a> talents, and always tried to get him and his friends in trouble, while James despised Snape's fascination with the <a href="/wiki/Dark_Arts" title="Dark Arts">Dark Arts</a>. James also frequently bullied Severus Snape during his fifth year, the memories of which could be viewed in Snape's <a href="/wiki/Pensieve" title="Pensieve">Pensieve</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP28_15-12" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP28-15">&#91;15&#93;</a></sup>

<p>Later, it is revealed that James and Snape would regularly pass snide remarks and taunts towards each other. Although James disliked and bullied Snape, in the end, James never wanted Snape dead despite his distaste of him and risked his life to save Snape's when Sirius played a potentially lethal prank on him by sending him to the <a href="/wiki/Shrieking_Shack" title="Shrieking Shack">Shrieking Shack</a> when <a href="/wiki/Remus_Lupin" title="Remus Lupin">Remus Lupin</a> was there transforming. However, Snape despised being in James's debt and felt, even twenty years later, that James only saved him to avoid expulsion.<sup id="cite_ref-POA18_5-14" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-POA18-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>

Snape was also involved in James's death: Snape was the <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eater</a> who relayed <a href="/wiki/Sybill_Trelawney%27s_first_prophecy" title="Sybill Trelawney&#39;s first prophecy">the Prophecy</a> that foretold the birth of the one who would defeat Snape's then-master <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP37_27-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP37-27">&#91;27&#93;</a></sup> When Voldemort was informed of this, he chose to get rid of James, his wife, and their son. Snape intervened by pleading for Lily's life, while ignoring the danger into which his actions put James and Harry.<sup id="cite_ref-DH33_19-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH33-19">&#91;19&#93;</a></sup>

<p>After Lily and James were killed, Snape reluctantly agreed to help Dumbledore protect Harry from Voldemort. However, Snape never forgot James' bullying or earlier arrogance, and constantly projected many of James' flaws unto Harry during their interactions as teacher and student. This was one of many factors that led to Harry hating Snape during this period, until Harry witnessed many of Snape's repressed memories and gained a better understanding of Snape's loyalties.<sup id="cite_ref-DH33_19-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH33-19">&#91;19&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Ironically, despite their mutual antipathy, James' <a href="/wiki/Albus_Potter" title="Albus Potter">second grandson</a> was named after Snape years after both of their deaths.<sup id="cite_ref-DHE_35-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DHE-35">&#91;35&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Albus_Dumbledore">Albus Dumbledore</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/James_Potter_I?veaction=edit&amp;section=24" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Albus Dumbledore">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/James_Potter_I?action=edit&amp;section=24" title="Edit section: Albus Dumbledore"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<figure class="thumb tright show-info-icon" style="width: 167px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/0/09/600full-Albus-Dumbledore-the-prisoner-of-azkaban-photo.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20141122220412" class="image"><img alt="" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw%3D%3D" decoding="async" width="165" height="223" class="thumbimage lazyload" data-image-name="600full-Albus-Dumbledore-the-prisoner-of-azkaban-photo.jpg" data-image-key="600full-Albus-Dumbledore-the-prisoner-of-azkaban-photo.jpg" data-src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/0/09/600full-Albus-Dumbledore-the-prisoner-of-azkaban-photo.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/165?cb=20141122220412" /></a> 	<noscript><a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/0/09/600full-Albus-Dumbledore-the-prisoner-of-azkaban-photo.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20141122220412" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/0/09/600full-Albus-Dumbledore-the-prisoner-of-azkaban-photo.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/165?cb=20141122220412" decoding="async" width="165" height="223" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="600full-Albus-Dumbledore-the-prisoner-of-azkaban-photo.jpg" data-image-key="600full-Albus-Dumbledore-the-prisoner-of-azkaban-photo.jpg" data-src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/0/09/600full-Albus-Dumbledore-the-prisoner-of-azkaban-photo.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/165?cb=20141122220412" /></a></noscript> 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:600full-Albus-Dumbledore-the-prisoner-of-azkaban-photo.jpg" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption"><a href="/wiki/Albus_Dumbledore" title="Albus Dumbledore">Albus Dumbledore</a>, his former headmaster</p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure>
<p>James always had a good relationship with Albus Dumbledore. Dumbledore was the Headmaster of <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts</a> at the time James attended the school. He became fond of James knowing that, despite his being an arrogant, highly mischievous trouble-maker, he was also a kind-hearted, good-natured person. James became a member of the <a href="/wiki/Order_of_the_Phoenix" title="Order of the Phoenix">Order of the Phoenix</a>, an organisation founded by Dumbledore, during the <a href="/wiki/First_Wizarding_War" title="First Wizarding War">First Wizarding War</a> and fought in several battles with him. Dumbledore also attempted to protect James, Lily, and Harry from Voldemort, who was out to kill their son by offering to be their Secret Keeper. However, the role of Secret Keeper went instead to <a href="/wiki/Peter_Pettigrew" title="Peter Pettigrew">Peter Pettigrew</a>, which turned out to be a fatal mistake, as Peter was, unbeknownst to them, a spy for Voldemort. When James and Lily were murdered by Voldemort, Dumbledore was devastated. After death, Dumbledore was amused and impressed that James, Sirius and Peter became Animagi without his knowledge. 
</p><p>Albus later became a mentor to James's <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">son</a>, and they shared an intimate relationship, more like a grandfather and his grandson, rather than a headmaster and his pupil. Dumbledore often sympathised with Harry about the grief and loneliness caused by his parents' death, and would comfort him at times when he really needed it. He also firmly believed and emphasised many times that Harry's love for his parents, James and Lily, was the reason why he had a power stronger than Voldemort's.
</p><p>Years after James and Dumbledore's deaths, the former's <a href="/wiki/Albus_Potter" title="Albus Potter">second grandson</a> was named after the latter.<sup id="cite_ref-DHE_35-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DHE-35">&#91;35&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<p>Minerva McGonagall was the <a href="/wiki/Transfiguration_(class)" title="Transfiguration (class)">Transfiguration</a> Professor and also the <a href="/wiki/Head_of_Gryffindor_House" title="Head of Gryffindor House">Head of Gryffindor House</a>, at the time James was a student. McGonagall was simultaneously fond of, and exasperated with James: she knew that he was a mischievous trouble-maker and often played cruel pranks on some of his school rivals (such as <a href="/wiki/Severus_Snape" title="Severus Snape">Severus Snape</a>), but she also knew that he was a clever and talented wizard, and essentially a good person; overall, they had a good relationship, which presumably improved significantly after James cleaned up his act, and became worthy of being made <a href="/wiki/Head_Boy_or_Girl" title="Head Boy or Girl">Head Boy</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-POA10_28-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-POA10-28">&#91;28&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Upon learning of James and Lily's deaths a few hours after they were murdered, McGonagall was devastated, and barely managed to keep her composure.<sup id="cite_ref-PS1_7-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PS1-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup> In her later years, she shared quite a close relationship with James's son, <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry</a>.
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Order_of_the_Phoenix">Order of the Phoenix</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/James_Potter_I?veaction=edit&amp;section=26" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Order of the Phoenix">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/James_Potter_I?action=edit&amp;section=26" title="Edit section: Order of the Phoenix"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>

<p>James did have a good relationship with most of the members of the <a href="/wiki/Order_of_the_Phoenix" title="Order of the Phoenix">Order of the Phoenix</a>, during the First Wizarding War. He might have been friendly with some members, such as <a href="/wiki/Elphias_Doge" title="Elphias Doge">Elphias Doge</a> and <a href="/wiki/Alastor_Moody" title="Alastor Moody">Alastor Moody</a>, as they were all members during the first war and most likely fought in several battles together. It is possible that James had many other friendships during his time in the Order, but they were unknown; his most famous friendship being the one he shared with the Marauders. Most of the original members of the Order were killed during the <a href="/wiki/Second_Wizarding_War" title="Second Wizarding War">Second Wizarding War</a>; only a few of them managed to survive.
</p>

<ul><li>James is played by <a href="/wiki/Adrian_Rawlins" title="Adrian Rawlins">Adrian Rawlins</a> in the film adaptations of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Philosopher%27s_Stone_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Philosopher&#39;s Stone (film)">Harry Potter and the Philosopher's Stone</a></i>, <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Goblet_of_Fire_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire (film)">Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire</a></i> and reprised the role for <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Deathly_Hallows_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows (film)">Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows</a></i>. In the film adaptations of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Chamber_of_Secrets_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets (film)">Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets</a></i>, <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Prisoner_of_Azkaban_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban (film)">Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban</a>,</i> and <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Order_of_the_Phoenix_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix (film)">Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix</a></i>, footage from earlier films was used. In <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Order_of_the_Phoenix_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix (film)">Order of the Phoenix</a></i>, James was played as a teenager by <a href="/wiki/Robbie_Jarvis" title="Robbie Jarvis">Robbie Jarvis</a> and as a child by <a href="/wiki/Alfie_McIlwain" title="Alfie McIlwain">Alfie McIlwain</a> in <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Deathly_Hallows:_Part_2" title="Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 2">Deathly Hallows: Part 2</a></i> in flashback scenes.<sup id="cite_ref-45" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-45">&#91;45&#93;</a></sup> This means the only film adaption which James doesn't appear in is <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Half-Blood_Prince_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince (film)">Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince</a></i>. By an interesting coincidence, Rawlins was also born on <a href="/wiki/27_March" title="27 March">27 March</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Tom_Milligan" title="Tom Milligan">Tom Milligan</a> portrays James in the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Cursed_Child_(play)" title="Harry Potter and the Cursed Child (play)">original West End production</a> of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Cursed_Child" title="Harry Potter and the Cursed Child">Harry Potter and the Cursed Child</a></i>.</li></ul>
<ul><li>In the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Philosopher%27s_Stone_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Philosopher&#39;s Stone (film)">film adaptation</a> of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Philosopher%27s_Stone" title="Harry Potter and the Philosopher&#39;s Stone">Harry Potter and the Philosopher's Stone</a></i>, James was said to have been a <a href="/wiki/Seeker" title="Seeker">Seeker</a>. However, <a href="/wiki/J._K._Rowling" title="J. K. Rowling">J. K. Rowling</a> has stated that James was a Chaser.<sup id="cite_ref-accio_39-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-accio-39">&#91;39&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li>To date, the <i><a href="/wiki/Untitled_charity_auction_story" title="Untitled charity auction story">Harry Potter Prequel</a></i> is the only story to feature James while he was alive. All other appearances have either been as an echo or as a flashback.</li>
<li>In the last film, in Snape's memory, it shows that James was killed at the top of the staircase instead of in the living room.</li>
<li>When <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> speaks with <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Voldemort</a> in the room of the Mirror of Erised in <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Philosopher%27s_Stone" title="Harry Potter and the Philosopher&#39;s Stone">Harry Potter and the Philosopher's Stone</a></i>, Voldemort says that James "put up a good fight" before he killed him; this is inconsistent with James later being revealed not to have had his wand at the time. It's possible, however, that Voldemort was exaggerating, as James <i>did</i> try to hold him off, but was killed quickly, being that his wand was not on him at the time.</li>
<li>In the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Order_of_the_Phoenix_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix (film)">film adaptation</a> of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Order_of_the_Phoenix" title="Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix">Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix</a></i>, James is depicted as having brown instead of black hair. His son's hair colour was also changed.</li>
<li>In the movie adaptations, James appears to be much older than he was intended to be, being only twenty-one-years old when he died.</li>
<li>There has been some speculation that James's parents were <a href="/wiki/Charlus_Potter" title="Charlus Potter">Charlus Potter</a> and <a href="/wiki/Dorea_Black" title="Dorea Black">Dorea Black</a>, who appear on the <a href="/wiki/House_of_Black" title="House of Black">Black family tree</a> and are said to have had one <a href="/wiki/Charlus_Potter%27s_son" title="Charlus Potter&#39;s son">son</a>. However, with the new information revealed on  <i><a href="/wiki/Pottermore" title="Pottermore">Pottermore</a></i>, this was stated to not be the case, with James's parents named as Fleamont and Euphemia Potter.<sup id="cite_ref-46" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-46">&#91;46&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li>In <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Deathly_Hallows:_Part_2" title="Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 2">Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 2</a></i>, James was shown to be without glasses during his earliest Hogwarts years. However, a possible explanation would that <a href="/wiki/Fleamont_Potter" title="Fleamont Potter">his father</a> did not wear glasses, and his eyesight gradually required them.</li>
<li>Rawlins reprised James's role for the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Deathly_Hallows:_Part_2_(video_game)" title="Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 2 (video game)">video game adaption of <i>Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 2</i></a>.</li>
<li>James was the only member of the Marauders not to live to the 1990s.</li>
<li>If James had lived, he would have been in his thirties during the Harry Potter story-line.</li>
<li>Ironically, James' actor Adrian Rawlins shares the same first and last initials as <a href="/wiki/Alan_Rickman" title="Alan Rickman">Alan Rickman</a> - the actor who played James' hated rival Severus Snape.</li></ul>
