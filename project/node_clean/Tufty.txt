<b>Tufty</b> was one of <a href="/wiki/Arabella_Figg" title="Arabella Figg">Mrs Figg's</a> part-<a href="/wiki/Kneazle" title="Kneazle">Kneazle</a> cats. The other three were <a href="/wiki/Mr_Tibbles" title="Mr Tibbles">Mr Tibbles</a>, <a href="/wiki/Snowy" title="Snowy">Snowy</a> and <a href="/wiki/Mr_Paws" title="Mr Paws">Mr Paws</a>. <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> saw it (or possibly its picture) when he went to stay with Mrs Figg when <a href="/wiki/Dudley_Dursley" title="Dudley Dursley">Dudley</a> got to go to places on his birthday. Harry found a hard time being remorseful when Mrs Figg broke her leg after reminding himself that it would be a while before he had to see Mr Tibbles, Snowy, Tufty and Mr Paws again.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
