<b>Phineas Black</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Pure-blood" title="Pure-blood">pure-blood</a> <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a>, the second eldest son of <a href="/wiki/Phineas_Nigellus_Black" title="Phineas Nigellus Black">Phineas Nigellus Black</a> and his wife <a href="/wiki/Ursula_Flint" title="Ursula Flint">Ursula Flint</a> and brother of <a href="/wiki/Cygnus_Black_II" title="Cygnus Black II">Cygnus</a>, <a href="/wiki/Sirius_Black_II" title="Sirius Black II">Sirius</a>, <a href="/wiki/Belvina_Black" title="Belvina Black">Belvina</a> and <a href="/wiki/Arcturus_Black_II" title="Arcturus Black II">Arcturus</a>. He was disowned for supporting <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggle</a> rights and removed from the <a href="/wiki/House_of_Black" title="House of Black">Black family tree</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>It is unknown whether Phineas ever sired children, but if he did, his patrilineal descendants must have died out by <a href="/wiki/1996" title="1996">1996</a> because his great-great-nephew <a href="/wiki/Sirius_Black" title="Sirius Black">Sirius</a> was the last of the Blacks.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Phineas_Black?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Phineas_Black?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>Based on a statement by <a href="/wiki/Horace_Slughorn" title="Horace Slughorn">Horace Slughorn</a>, Phineas may have been a member of the <a href="/wiki/Slytherin" title="Slytherin">Slytherin House</a>. However, Phineas would have attended in the <a href="/wiki/1880s" title="1880s">1880s</a> or the <a href="/wiki/1890s" title="1890s">1890s</a>, when Slughorn was not <a href="/wiki/Potions_(class)" title="Potions (class)">Potions</a> teacher.</li>
<li>Phineas was named after his <a href="/wiki/Phineas_Nigellus_Black" title="Phineas Nigellus Black">father</a>, but ironically, this son became Phineas Nigellus's greatest shame.</li>
<li>On the <a href="/wiki/Black_Family_Tapestry" title="Black Family Tapestry">Black family tree tapestry</a> from the film Phineas is depicted as a red hat with a black hole. His name is misspelled <b>Phineus</b> on the tapestry, and so is the name of <a href="/wiki/Phineas_Nigellus_Black" title="Phineas Nigellus Black">his father</a>. This may be because the names are also incorrectly spelled on a tree drawn by <a href="/wiki/J._K._Rowling" title="J. K. Rowling">J. K. Rowling</a>. The hand drawn family tree was given to Book Aid International.</li>
<li>Based on his estimated birth year(s), Phineas would have attended Hogwarts around the same time as <a href="/wiki/Albus_Dumbledore" title="Albus Dumbledore">Albus Dumbledore</a>, the latter of whom was born in 1881. While Dumbledore was Sorted into <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor" title="Gryffindor">Gryffindor</a>, Phineas was likely Sorted into <a href="/wiki/Slytherin" title="Slytherin">Slytherin</a>. Both Phineas and Dumbledore also would have attended Hogwarts while Phineas's father, <a href="/wiki/Phineas_Nigellus_Black" title="Phineas Nigellus Black">Phineas Nigellus Black</a>, was likely the Headmaster. Given Phineas's support of Muggle rights, a belief he shared with Dumbledore, it is possible that Phineas and Dumbledore may have been acquaintances or friends. This could also explain Dumbledore's seeming fondness for the portrait of <a href="/wiki/Phineas_Nigellus_Black" title="Phineas Nigellus Black">Phineas Nigellus Black</a> in the books, as Dumbledore may have had a friendly relationship with his son.</li></ul>

<p><a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Phinehas" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Phinehas">Phinehas</a> was the grandson of Aaron in the Bible. He stopped a plague by killing two of the sinners who caused it. His name is sometimes spelled as <i>Phineas</i>.
