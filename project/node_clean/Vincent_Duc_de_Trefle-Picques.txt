<b>Vincent Duc de Trefle-Picques</b> (fl. <a href="/wiki/1794" class="mw-redirect" title="1794">1794</a>) was a <a href="/wiki/France" title="France">French</a> <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> and nobleman, active around the time of the <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/French_Revolution" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:French Revolution">French Revolution</a>; he managed to escape the <a href="/wiki/Terror" title="Terror">Reign of Terror</a> of <a href="/wiki/1793" class="mw-redirect" title="1793">1793</a>-<a href="/wiki/1794" class="mw-redirect" title="1794">1794</a> by casting a <a href="/wiki/Concealment_Charm" title="Concealment Charm">Concealment Charm</a> on his neck and pretending that his head had already been cut off. In his youth, he attended <a href="/wiki/Beauxbatons_Academy_of_Magic" title="Beauxbatons Academy of Magic">Beauxbatons Academy of Magic</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-Pottermore_1-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Pottermore-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
