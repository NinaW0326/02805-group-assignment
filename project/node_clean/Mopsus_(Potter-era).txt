<b>Mopsus</b> was a blind <a href="/wiki/Seer" title="Seer">Seer</a> whose power was great enough for him to accurately predict future events.<sup id="cite_ref-itv_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-itv-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Mopsus_(Potter-era)?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Mopsus_(Potter-era)?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li><a href="/wiki/J._K._Rowling" title="J. K. Rowling">J. K. Rowling</a> stated at a press conference in <a href="/wiki/2005" title="2005">2005</a> that Mopsus was <i>"a very, very early character"</i>, conceived of while she was still in the process of planning out <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Philosopher%27s_Stone" title="Harry Potter and the Philosopher&#39;s Stone">Harry Potter and the Philosopher's Stone</a></i>.<sup id="cite_ref-itv_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-itv-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> She said she chose to cut him because his skill at <a href="/wiki/Divination" title="Divination">Divination</a> was "too good", and that having such a talented <a href="/wiki/Seer" title="Seer">Seer</a> around during <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>'s time would be detrimental to the story's drama, because it meant there would be someone who knew how things were going to turn out.<sup id="cite_ref-itv_1-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-itv-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> However, it seems Mopsus may not have been discarded until after the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Chamber_of_Secrets" title="Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets">second book</a>, as he is given as the <a href="/wiki/Professor" title="Professor">Professor</a> of Divination on a list of <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts</a> teachers which Rowling created at the time she was working on <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Prisoner_of_Azkaban" title="Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban">Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban</a></i>.<sup id="cite_ref-lex_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-lex-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup></li></ul>
