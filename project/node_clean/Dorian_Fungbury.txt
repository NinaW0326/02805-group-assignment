<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Dorian_Fungbury?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Dorian_Fungbury?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>In the <a href="/wiki/2010s" title="2010s">2010s</a>, Dorian Fungbury offered an informative stay over for an <a href="/wiki/Auror" title="Auror">Auror</a> member of the <a href="/wiki/Statute_of_Secrecy_Task_Force" title="Statute of Secrecy Task Force">Statute of Secrecy Task Force</a>; learning from their host, they benefited from it when out in the field.<sup id="cite_ref-WU_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-WU-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>In <a href="/wiki/July" title="July">July</a> <a href="/wiki/2020" title="2020">2020</a>, <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione Granger</a> asked Dorian to do some digging into illegal beast trading. Since the <a href="/wiki/Calamity" title="Calamity">Calamity</a>, the Ministry pulled recource away from combating the black market. He reached out to some underworld contacts and found that the black market - particularly the illegal Magical Creature market in <a href="/wiki/Indonesia" title="Indonesia">Indonesia</a> - has utterly exploded.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
