<b>Andrew Owly</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> and a <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor" title="Gryffindor">Gryffindor</a> <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">student</a> at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> in the <a href="/wiki/1990s" title="1990s">1990s</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-list_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-list-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>His name appeared on a list of students with overdue <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Library" title="Hogwarts Library">library</a> books that was posted on the noticeboard in the <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor_Tower" title="Gryffindor Tower">Gryffindor common room</a> by <a href="/wiki/Irma_Pince" title="Irma Pince">Madam Pince</a> sometime in the 1990s.<sup id="cite_ref-list_2-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-list-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
