<b>Helen Dawlish</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch</a>, member of the <a href="/wiki/Dawlish_family" title="Dawlish family">Dawlish family</a> who was a <a href="/wiki/Ravenclaw" title="Ravenclaw">Ravenclaw</a> student at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> in the <a href="/wiki/1990s" title="1990s">1990s</a>. She was also a member of the <a href="/wiki/Duelling_Club" title="Duelling Club">Duelling Club</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-HBPG_4-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBPG-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Helen_Dawlish?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Helen_Dawlish?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>In <a href="/wiki/1993" title="1993">1993</a>, she could be found in the <a href="/wiki/Fat_Lady%27s_Corridor" title="Fat Lady&#39;s Corridor">Fat Lady's Corridor</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-POAG_3-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-POAG-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> She was a fan of <a href="/wiki/Gilderoy_Lockhart" title="Gilderoy Lockhart">Gilderoy Lockhart</a>, and spent the summer of <a href="/wiki/1996" title="1996">1996</a> reading his books, of which <i><a href="/wiki/Magical_Me" title="Magical Me">Magical Me</a></i> was her favourite. After returning to school for the <a href="/wiki/1996%E2%80%931997_school_year" title="1996–1997 school year">1996–1997 school year</a>, she ran a <a href="/wiki/Duelling_Club" title="Duelling Club">Duelling Club</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Middle_Courtyard" title="Middle Courtyard">Transfiguration Courtyard</a>, which also included <a href="/wiki/Shoma_Ichikawa" title="Shoma Ichikawa">Shoma Ichikawa</a> and <a href="/wiki/Rhonda_Fladbury" title="Rhonda Fladbury">Rhonda Fladbury</a>. She offered <a href="/wiki/Sloth_brain" title="Sloth brain">sloth brains</a> as a reward to everyone who beat her in a <a href="/wiki/Duelling" title="Duelling">duel</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-HBPG_4-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBPG-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Helen_Dawlish?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Helen_Dawlish?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The name "Helen" is the English form of the Greek ‘Ελενη (<i>Helene</i>), probably from Greek ‘ελενη (<i>helene</i>) "torch" or "corposant", or possibly related to σεληνη (<i>selene</i>) "moon". In Greek mythology Helen was the daughter of Zeus and Leda, whose kidnapping by Paris was the cause of the Trojan War.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Helen_Dawlish?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Helen_Dawlish?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>This particular student was only featured in the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Prisoner_of_Azkaban_(PlayStation_2,_Xbox,_GameCube)" title="Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban (PlayStation 2, Xbox, GameCube)">console versions</a> of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Prisoner_of_Azkaban" title="Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban">Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban</a></i> and the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Half-Blood_Prince_(video_game)" title="Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince (video game)">Nintendo DS version</a> of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Half-Blood_Prince" title="Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince">Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince</a></i>.</li>
<li>If Helen is indeed related to John Dawlish, her mandatory attendance at Hogwarts, where she was at the mercy of the Carrows, presumably played a part in his cooperation with the Death Eater-controlled Ministry.</li></ul>
