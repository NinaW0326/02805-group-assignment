<p>A <b>knight</b>,  was someone who is granted an honorary title of knighthood for service to the <a href="/wiki/King" title="King">monarch</a> or country, especially in a military capacity.<sup id="cite_ref-Wikipedia_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Wikipedia-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>There were many knight <a href="/wiki/Suit_of_armour" title="Suit of armour">suits of armour</a> at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Castle" title="Hogwarts Castle">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> under <a href="/wiki/Spirit" title="Spirit">spectral</a> possession. They could be aggressive to passers-by, but were particularly vulnerable to the <i><a href="/wiki/Knockback_Jinx" title="Knockback Jinx">Flipendo</a></i>, <i><a href="/wiki/Fire-Making_Spell" title="Fire-Making Spell">Incendio</a></i> and <i><a href="/wiki/Freezing_Spell" title="Freezing Spell">Glacius</a></i> spells.<sup id="cite_ref-PAgba_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PAgba-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> In contrast, they were resistant to <i><a href="/wiki/Red_Sparks" title="Red Sparks">Vermillious</a></i>.
</p>
