<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_fourth_year_Chaser?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_fourth_year_Chaser?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>This individual started attending <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> in <a href="/wiki/1986" title="1986">1986</a> and was <a href="/wiki/Sorting_ceremony" title="Sorting ceremony">Sorted</a> into the same house as <a href="/wiki/Jacob%27s_sibling" title="Jacob&#39;s sibling">Jacob's sibling</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-HM614_1-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HM614-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>By their <a href="/wiki/Fourth_year" title="Fourth year">fourth year</a>, the individual had become a <a href="/wiki/Chaser" title="Chaser">Chaser</a> on their Quidditch team. During their fourth year, they fell three storeys off their <a href="/wiki/Broomstick" title="Broomstick">broom</a> and broke their nose. Because of this, they ended up in the <a href="/wiki/Hospital_Wing" title="Hospital Wing">Hospital Wing</a>. Shortly after this incident, <a href="/wiki/Poppy_Pomfrey" title="Poppy Pomfrey">Poppy Pomfrey</a> was petrified, and the Chaser left the Hospital Wing as it was evacuated.<sup id="cite_ref-HM614_1-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HM614-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_fourth_year_Chaser?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_fourth_year_Chaser?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>This individual's house is the same as that of <a href="/wiki/Jacob%27s_sibling" title="Jacob&#39;s sibling">Jacob's sibling</a>, which is chosen by the player.
<ul><li>This is similar to how characters in the Quidditch storyline (such as <a href="/wiki/Skye_Parkin" title="Skye Parkin">Skye Parkin</a>) are in specific houses depending on the player's house. As such, it is possible this character will eventually appear as a teammate in a yet to be released Quidditch Season.</li></ul></li></ul>
