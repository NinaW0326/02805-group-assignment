<b>Daisy Ecklington</b> (n√©e <b>Twemlove</b>) was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch</a> who, in her youth, was engaged to be married to <a href="/wiki/Perseus_Ecklington" title="Perseus Ecklington">Perseus Ecklington</a>, even though she had another admirer, <a href="/wiki/Norville_Bezerko" title="Norville Bezerko">Norville Bezerko</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-BPsleepingpotion_1-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-BPsleepingpotion-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>On the eve of her wedding to Ecklington, <a href="/wiki/Norville_Bezerko" title="Norville Bezerko">Norville Bezerko</a>, his love rival, slipped a powerful <a href="/wiki/Sleeping_Potion" title="Sleeping Potion">Sleeping Potion</a> into his <a href="/wiki/Butterbeer" title="Butterbeer">butterbeer</a> mug, sending him into a deep death-like sleep. Bezerko prompty presented himself at Daisy's house, telling her that Ecklington had died of panic at the prospect of marrying her. An unsuspecting Daisy accepted to marry Bezerko the moment Ecklington was buried.<sup id="cite_ref-BPsleepingpotion_1-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-BPsleepingpotion-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Ecklington was put in a coffin, and, just as <a href="/wiki/Funeral_of_Perseus_Ecklington" title="Funeral of Perseus Ecklington">he was being lowered into the ground</a>, he woke up, the effects of the <a href="/wiki/Sleeping_Potion" title="Sleeping Potion">Sleeping Potion</a> gone. Ecklington soon realised Bezerko's scheme, and a brawl ensued. Bezerko was left with an <a href="/wiki/Elephant" title="Elephant">elephant</a>'s trunk and six ears, thus hampering his chances of finding another bride, and Daisy Twemlove was reunited with Perseus Ecklington.<sup id="cite_ref-BPsleepingpotion_1-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-BPsleepingpotion-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
