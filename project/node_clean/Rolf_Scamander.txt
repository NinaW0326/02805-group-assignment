<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Rolf_Scamander?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Rolf_Scamander?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_life">Early life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Rolf_Scamander?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Early life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Rolf_Scamander?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Early life"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Rolf was born into the wizarding <a href="/wiki/Scamander_family" title="Scamander family">Scamander family</a> somewhere in the <a href="/wiki/Great_Britain" title="Great Britain">British Isles</a>. Not much is known about Rolf's early life other than the fact that he was the grandson of <a href="/wiki/Newton_Scamander" title="Newton Scamander">Newt Scamander</a> and his wife <a href="/wiki/Porpentina_Goldstein" title="Porpentina Goldstein">Porpentina</a>. He may have identified as <a href="/wiki/Judaism" title="Judaism">Jewish</a>, given that his grandmother was possibly a Jewish witch. 
</p><p>At some point after he left school, he followed in the footsteps of his grandfather and went on to become a well-respected magizoologist. 
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Marriage">Marriage</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Rolf_Scamander?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Marriage">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Rolf_Scamander?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Marriage"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Rolf eventually married <a href="/wiki/Luna_Lovegood" title="Luna Lovegood">Luna Lovegood</a>, a fellow naturalist.<sup id="cite_ref-mugglenet_3-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-mugglenet-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> According to <a href="/wiki/Rita_Skeeter" title="Rita Skeeter">Rita Skeeter</a> (a dubious source), he had an unforgettably shocked expression when he saw Luna's <a href="/wiki/Luna_Lovegood%27s_wedding_dress" title="Luna Lovegood&#39;s wedding dress">wedding dress</a>. Her dress was decorated with rainbows and spangles, and she wore a tiara of silver unicorn horns. This dress was voted â€˜<a href="/wiki/Most_Hideous_Outfit_of_the_Year" title="Most Hideous Outfit of the Year">Most Hideous Outfit of the Year</a>' by readers of Skeeter's regular <i><a href="/wiki/Daily_Prophet" title="Daily Prophet">Daily Prophet</a></i> column.<sup id="cite_ref-reunion_2-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-reunion-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> The couple had twin sons named <a href="/wiki/Lorcan_Scamander" title="Lorcan Scamander">Lorcan</a> and <a href="/wiki/Lysander_Scamander" title="Lysander Scamander">Lysander</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-unwritten_4-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-unwritten-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Chief_Consulting_Magizoologist">Chief Consulting Magizoologist</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Rolf_Scamander?veaction=edit&amp;section=4" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Chief Consulting Magizoologist">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Rolf_Scamander?action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Chief Consulting Magizoologist"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>By the <a href="/wiki/2010s" title="2010s">2010s</a>, Rolf Scamander was the <a href="/wiki/Chief_Consulting_Magizoologist" title="Chief Consulting Magizoologist">Chief Consulting Magizoologist</a> to the <i><a href="/wiki/Daily_Prophet" title="Daily Prophet">Daily Prophet</a></i>. It was in this capacity that he remarked about the <a href="/wiki/Argentinian_Council_of_Magic" title="Argentinian Council of Magic">Argentinian Council of Magic</a>'s lack of foresight over the organisation of the opening ceremony of the <a href="/wiki/2014_Quidditch_World_Cup" title="2014 Quidditch World Cup">2014 Quidditch World Cup</a>, which escalated into a fight between various team mascots prompted by an attempt to make the <a href="/wiki/Fiji" title="Fiji">Fijian</a> <a href="/wiki/Dukuwaqa" title="Dukuwaqa">Dukuwaqa</a> and <a href="/wiki/Norway" title="Norway">Norwegian</a> <a href="/wiki/Selma" title="Selma">Selma</a> share a lake.<sup id="cite_ref-PM_5-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PM-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>He also attended the final of the <a href="/wiki/2014_Quidditch_World_Cup" title="2014 Quidditch World Cup">2014 Quidditch World Cup</a> and sat in the VIP boxes with his wife, where he held an interesting conversation with <a href="/wiki/Charles_Weasley" title="Charles Weasley">Charlie Weasley</a> and held <a href="/wiki/Luna_Lovegood" title="Luna Lovegood">Luna</a>'s hands, which might have been because he was trying to prevent his wife from putting on one of her famous <a href="/wiki/Special_Event_Hat" title="Special Event Hat">Special Event Hats</a>, according to <a href="/wiki/Rita_Skeeter" title="Rita Skeeter">Rita Skeeter</a>.
</p><p>In <a href="/wiki/2019" title="2019">2019</a>, he assisted prospective members of the then recently established <a href="/wiki/Statute_of_Secrecy_Task_Force" title="Statute of Secrecy Task Force">Statute of Secrecy Task Force</a> seeking a career as Magizoologists, to whom he shared the expertise passed down to him from his celebrated grandfather on how to understand the mind of the <a href="/wiki/Erumpent" title="Erumpent">Erumpent</a>, as well as his research about <a href="/wiki/Occamy" title="Occamy">Occamies</a>, hoping it would help them in the field.<sup id="cite_ref-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Magical_abilities_and_skills">Magical abilities and skills</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Rolf_Scamander?veaction=edit&amp;section=5" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Rolf_Scamander?action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li><b><a href="/wiki/Magizoology" title="Magizoology">Magizoology</a></b>: Being a professional <a href="/wiki/Magizoologist" title="Magizoologist">Magizoologist</a> who worked as the <a href="/wiki/Chief_Consulting_Magizoologist" title="Chief Consulting Magizoologist">Chief Consulting Magizoologist</a> for the <a href="/wiki/Daily_Prophet" title="Daily Prophet">Daily Prophet</a>, Rolf was an expert in the study of <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magical</a> <a href="/wiki/Creature" title="Creature">creatures</a>, who covered various different creatures in his newspaper columns and would have had much knowledge of the creatures he covered. He also enjoyed exploring for strange new creatures with his wife and fellow Magizoologist <a href="/wiki/Luna_Lovegood" title="Luna Lovegood">Luna Lovegood</a> across the globe. He was able to use his expertise knowledge and skill in this field to educate members of the <a href="/wiki/Statute_of_Secrecy_Task_Force" title="Statute of Secrecy Task Force">Statute of Secrecy Task Force</a> in <a href="/wiki/2019" title="2019">2019</a> about how to understand and handle various <a href="/wiki/Beast" title="Beast">beasts</a> in their adventures.</li>
<li><b>Journalism</b>: Being the <a href="/wiki/Chief_Consulting_Magizoologist" title="Chief Consulting Magizoologist">Chief Consulting Magizoologist</a> for the <a href="/wiki/Daily_Prophet" title="Daily Prophet">Daily Prophet</a>, Rolf was a highly talented and professional Journalist and a skilled newspaper reporter on various articles and events in his field. His pieces in the paper were both insightful and informative, showing he had a high degree of Journalistic integrity and that he took his career seriously.</li></ul>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Relationships">Relationships</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Rolf_Scamander?veaction=edit&amp;section=6" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Relationships">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Rolf_Scamander?action=edit&amp;section=6" title="Edit section: Relationships"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Luna_Lovegood">Luna Lovegood</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Rolf_Scamander?veaction=edit&amp;section=7" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Luna Lovegood">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Rolf_Scamander?action=edit&amp;section=7" title="Edit section: Luna Lovegood"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p><a href="/wiki/Luna_Lovegood" title="Luna Lovegood">Luna</a> was Rolf's wife, whom he had a very close and <a href="/wiki/Love" title="Love">loving</a> relationship with. They were both professional <a href="/wiki/Magizoologist" title="Magizoologist">Magizoologists</a> and both spent their time "globetrotting and looking for weird creatures". Rolf had two sons with Luna; both were born "considerably later" than the children of <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> and their other friends.<sup id="cite_ref-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Rolf_Scamander?veaction=edit&amp;section=8" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Rolf_Scamander?action=edit&amp;section=8" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Rolf is a modern variant of the Norse name Hrolfr, which came from the German name Hrodulf (famous wolf). Rolf is formed from the words <i>hrodr</i> and <i>ulf</i>, meaning "honour" and "wolf", respectively. His name therefore means "Honourable wolf". Rolf, being a form of Rudolph, would be related etymologically to the Potter name <a href="/wiki/Rodolphus_Lestrange" title="Rodolphus Lestrange">Rodolphus Lestrange</a>.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Rolf_Scamander?veaction=edit&amp;section=9" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Rolf_Scamander?action=edit&amp;section=9" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>It's unknown if he attended <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a>.</li>
<li>According to an interview with <a href="/wiki/Evanna_Lynch" title="Evanna Lynch">Evanna Lynch</a> at the Oz Comic Con in Sydney in 2015, J. K. Rowling initially planned to introduce Rolf in <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Deathly_Hallows" title="Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows">Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows</a></i>, where he would have told <a href="/wiki/The_Tale_of_the_Three_Brothers" title="The Tale of the Three Brothers">The Tale of the Three Brothers</a> to the Golden Trio. Ultimately, however, he was removed from the novel so that <a href="/wiki/Xenophilius_Lovegood" title="Xenophilius Lovegood">Xenophilius Lovegood</a> could tell the story instead.<sup id="cite_ref-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup></li></ul>
