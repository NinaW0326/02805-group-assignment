<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Eustace_Fawley%27s_father?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Eustace_Fawley%27s_father?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>He had at least one child, a son, <a href="/wiki/Eustace_Fawley" title="Eustace Fawley">Eustuce</a>, to whom he gave his best set of <a href="/wiki/Gobstones" title="Gobstones">gobstones</a> to take to <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a>. Eustace lost the gobstones in a <a href="/wiki/Fire" title="Fire">fire</a> at the <a href="/wiki/Boathouse" title="Boathouse">boathouse</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>He was part of the <a href="/wiki/Fawley_family" title="Fawley family">Fawley family</a>, who appeared in the <i><a href="/wiki/Pure-Blood_Directory" title="Pure-Blood Directory">Pure-Blood Directory</a></i> as belonging to the <a href="/wiki/Sacred_Twenty-Eight" title="Sacred Twenty-Eight">Sacred Twenty-Eight</a>.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Eustace_Fawley%27s_father?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Eustace_Fawley%27s_father?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Fawley is a name derived from the Old English word fugelere which means "hunter of wild birds, fowler".
</p>
