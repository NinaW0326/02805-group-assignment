<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Rowena_Rowle?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Rowena_Rowle?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>At some point in the <a href="/wiki/Calamity" title="Calamity">Calamity</a>, she lets a <a href="/wiki/Calamity_Investigator" title="Calamity Investigator">Calamity Investigator</a> into the <a href="/wiki/Department_of_Mysteries" title="Department of Mysteries">Department of Mysteries</a> to share theories about <a href="/wiki/Grim_Fawley" title="Grim Fawley">Grim Fawley</a>. As she monologues, the Investigator stares into the Tank of Brains - accidentally inspiring one to leap onto them. Rowena saves the Investigator.<sup id="cite_ref-WU_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-WU-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
