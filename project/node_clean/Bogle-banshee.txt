The <b>bogle-banshee</b> was allegedly a <a href="/wiki/Banshee" title="Banshee">Banshee</a>, a <a href="/wiki/Dark_Arts" title="Dark Arts">dark</a> <a href="/wiki/Spirit" title="Spirit">spirit</a> which dwelt in the city of <a href="/wiki/Kathmandu" title="Kathmandu">Kathmandu</a>, <a href="/wiki/Nepal" title="Nepal">Nepal</a>. On the "<a href="/wiki/Defence_Against_the_Dark_Arts_Second_Year_Essential_Knowledge_Test" title="Defence Against the Dark Arts Second Year Essential Knowledge Test">Second Year Essential Knowledge Test</a>" that <a href="/wiki/Gilderoy_Lockhart" title="Gilderoy Lockhart">Gilderoy Lockhart</a> gave to his <a href="/wiki/Second_year" title="Second year">second-year</a> <a href="/wiki/Defence_Against_the_Dark_Arts" title="Defence Against the Dark Arts">Defence Against the Dark Arts</a> class in <a href="/wiki/1992" title="1992">1992</a>, there was a question that asked "What, in your opinion, is Gilderoy Lockhart's greatest achievement to date?", to which one student answered "to banish the bogle-banshee in Katmandu [sic]."<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> The defeat of this creature was possibly covered in <i><a href="/wiki/Break_with_a_Banshee" title="Break with a Banshee">Break with a Banshee</a></i>.
