<b>Jackie Jernigan</b> was a Welsh witch and a <a href="/wiki/Chaser" title="Chaser">Chaser</a> for the <a href="/wiki/2014" title="2014">2014</a> <a href="/wiki/Welsh_National_Quidditch_team" title="Welsh National Quidditch team">Welsh National Quidditch team</a>. On the first match of the quarter-finals of the <a href="/wiki/2014_Quidditch_World_Cup" title="2014 Quidditch World Cup">427th Quidditch World Cup</a>, <a href="/wiki/Brazilian_National_Quidditch_team" title="Brazilian National Quidditch team">Brazil</a> versus Wales, on <a href="/wiki/4_June" title="4 June">4 June</a>, <a href="/wiki/2014" title="2014">2014</a>, Jernigan scored one of the tournament's most stunning goals from a distance of fifty yards from the <a href="/wiki/Goalpost" title="Goalpost">goalposts</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-PM_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PM-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
