<b>Eduardus Lima</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> who worked as a reporter for the <i><a href="/wiki/Daily_Prophet" title="Daily Prophet">Daily Prophet</a></i>. He wrote <a href="/wiki/BLACK_STILL_AT_LARGE" title="BLACK STILL AT LARGE">the front-page article</a> for the <a href="/wiki/6_August" title="6 August">6 August</a>, <a href="/wiki/1993" title="1993">1993</a> issue, about the escape of <a href="/wiki/Sirius_Black" title="Sirius Black">Sirius Black</a> from <a href="/wiki/Azkaban" title="Azkaban">Azkaban</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-HPLE_1-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HPLE-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
