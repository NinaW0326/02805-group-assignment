<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_life">Early life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Megan_Jones?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Early life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Megan_Jones?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Early life"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Megan was born in either 1979 or 1980. She may be related to <a href="/wiki/Hestia_Jones" title="Hestia Jones">Hestia</a>, <a href="/wiki/Peter_Jones" title="Peter Jones">Peter</a> and <a href="/wiki/Gwenog_Jones" title="Gwenog Jones">Gwenog Jones</a>.
</p><p>Megan began attending <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> in September 1991.
</p><p>She would have boarded the Hogwarts Express and arrived at <a href="/wiki/Hogsmeade_station" title="Hogsmeade station">Hogsmeade Station</a> to the school. Along with the rest of the first years, she would have boarded an enchanted boat and be welcomed by <a href="/wiki/Minerva_McGonagall" title="Minerva McGonagall">Minerva McGonagall</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Chamber_of_Reception" title="Chamber of Reception">Entrance Hall Antechamber</a>.
</p><p>They proceeded to the <a href="/wiki/Great_Hall" title="Great Hall">Great Hall</a> where the <a href="/wiki/Sorting_ceremony" title="Sorting ceremony">Sorting ceremony</a> was held. The <a href="/wiki/Sorting_Hat" title="Sorting Hat">Sorting Hat</a> placed Megan in <a href="/wiki/Hufflepuff" title="Hufflepuff">Hufflepuff House</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-PM_4-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PM-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> She shared a dormitory in the <a href="/wiki/Hufflepuff_Basement" title="Hufflepuff Basement">Hufflepuff Basement</a> with <a href="/wiki/Hannah_Abbott" title="Hannah Abbott">Hannah Abbott</a> and <a href="/wiki/Susan_Bones" title="Susan Bones">Susan Bones</a>.
</p>
