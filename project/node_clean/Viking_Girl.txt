<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Viking_Girl?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Viking_Girl?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<figure class="thumb tleft show-info-icon" style="width: 202px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/9/9c/VikingGirlHermetray.png/revision/latest?cb=20141125185500" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/9/9c/VikingGirlHermetray.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/200?cb=20141125185500" decoding="async" width="200" height="101" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="VikingGirlHermetray.png" data-image-key="VikingGirlHermetray.png" /></a> 	 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:VikingGirlHermetray.png" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption">The Viking Girl and her companions flirting with the Beautified Budge</p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure>
<p>This girl noticed Budge's handsome appearance during his testing of the <a href="/wiki/Beautification_Potion" title="Beautification Potion">Beautification Potion</a> and became entranced with him, along with several other Viking women. They travelled to <a href="/wiki/Hermetray" title="Hermetray">Hermetray</a> in boats and began to circle the island, calling such things as "cooee" and "give us a kiss, handsome" to him. Though this indicated to Budge that his potion was successful, he became annoyed by the attention and, once he returned to his normal appearance, frightened the Viking Girl and her companions away by appearing outside his house dressed only in his favourite loincloth.<sup id="cite_ref-WBP_2-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-WBP-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Viking_Girl?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Viking_Girl?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>The depiction of this girl and her companions as Vikings is anachronistic. Zygmunt Budge lived during the 16th century, but the Viking Age is generally considered to have ended in the <a href="/wiki/11th_century" title="11th century">11th century</a>. In addition, Peter Dobbin, a concept artist for <i><a href="/wiki/Wonderbook:_Book_of_Potions" title="Wonderbook: Book of Potions">Wonderbook: Book of Potions</a></i>, refers to the Viking Girl as "a medieval Muggle" on his official site, but the Medieval period ended in the <a href="/wiki/15th_century" title="15th century">15th century</a>.</li></ul>
