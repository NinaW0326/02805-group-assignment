<p><b>Glover Hipworth</b> (<a href="/wiki/1742" class="mw-redirect" title="1742">1742</a> – <a href="/wiki/1805" class="mw-redirect" title="1805">1805</a>) was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> who specialised in <a href="/wiki/Potion" title="Potion">Potion</a> development. He became famous for the invention of the <a href="/wiki/Pepperup_Potion" title="Pepperup Potion">Pepperup Potion</a>, the cure for the <a href="/wiki/Common_cold" title="Common cold">common cold</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-COSG_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COSG-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Glover_Hipworth?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Glover_Hipworth?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_life">Early life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Glover_Hipworth?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Early life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Glover_Hipworth?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Early life"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Glover Hipworth was born in <a href="/wiki/1742" class="mw-redirect" title="1742">1742</a>. If he enrolled at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a>, he attended the school from around <a href="/wiki/1753" class="mw-redirect" title="1753">1753</a>-<a href="/wiki/1760" class="mw-redirect" title="1760">1760</a>, under <a href="/wiki/Headmaster" title="Headmaster">Headmistress</a> <a href="/wiki/Dilys_Derwent" title="Dilys Derwent">Dilys Derwent</a>.
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Adulthood">Adulthood</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Glover_Hipworth?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Adulthood">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Glover_Hipworth?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Adulthood"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<div class="quote">
<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets">"<i>Madam Pomfrey, the nurse, was kept busy by a sudden spate of colds among the staff and students. Her Pepperup potion worked instantly, though it left the drinker smoking at the ears for several hours afterward.</i>"</span></dd>
<dd>&#8212;Description<sup class="noprint"><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Chamber_of_Secrets" title="Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets">[src]</a></sup></dd>
<dd></dd></dl></div><p>It is likely Hipworth studied further into <a href="/wiki/Potion" title="Potion">Potion-making</a>, becoming a fully-fledged <a href="/wiki/Potioneer" title="Potioneer">potioneer</a> in his adult years.
</p><p>Sometime in the late <a href="/wiki/18th_century" title="18th century">eighteenth</a> or early <a href="/wiki/19th_century" title="19th century">nineteenth</a> centuries, Glover Hipworth developed the <a href="/wiki/Pepperup_Potion" title="Pepperup Potion">Pepperup Potion</a>, based on an earlier remedy by <a href="/wiki/12th_century" title="12th century">12th century</a> <a href="/wiki/Potioneer" title="Potioneer">potioneer</a> <a href="/wiki/Linfred_of_Stinchcombe" title="Linfred of Stinchcombe">Linfred of Stinchcombe</a>, used today as an efficacious <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magical</a> remedy for the common cold.<sup id="cite_ref-COSG_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COSG-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>By the <a href="/wiki/1990s" title="1990s">1990s</a>, Hipworth was featured on a <a href="/wiki/Chocolate_Frog_Card" title="Chocolate Frog Card">Chocolate Frog Card</a>, which pictured him symbolically sneezing, in a reference to his achievement.<sup id="cite_ref-COSG_1-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COSG-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> In the <a href="/wiki/1993%E2%80%931994_school_year" title="1993–1994 school year">1993–1994 school year</a> a <a href="/wiki/Portrait" title="Portrait">portrait</a> of him guarded a secret room in the <a href="/wiki/Dungeons" title="Dungeons">Dungeons</a>, at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Castle" title="Hogwarts Castle">Hogwarts Castle</a>; the password was <i>Gesundheit</i> (the German word for "health", also used for "Bless you").<sup id="cite_ref-POAG_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-POAG-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Magical_abilities_and_skills">Magical abilities and skills</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Glover_Hipworth?veaction=edit&amp;section=4" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Glover_Hipworth?action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li><b><a href="/wiki/Potion" title="Potion">Potions</a></b>: Hipworth was a professional <a href="/wiki/Potioneer" title="Potioneer">Potioneer</a> and was highly proficient in the art of brewing potions, having invented the <a href="/wiki/Pepperup_Potion" title="Pepperup Potion">Pepperup Potion</a>, a <a href="/wiki/Healing_magic" title="Healing magic">Healing</a> potion which remedies the effects of the <a href="/wiki/Common_cold" title="Common cold">Common cold</a>. For his achievement, he was included on a <a href="/wiki/Chocolate_Frog_Card" title="Chocolate Frog Card">Chocolate Frog Card</a>, showing the great level of talent he possessed in this branch of <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magic</a> and <a href="/wiki/Science" title="Science">science</a>.</li></ul>
