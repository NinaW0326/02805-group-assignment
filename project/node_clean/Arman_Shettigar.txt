<b>Arman Shettigar</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> and <a href="/wiki/Ravenclaw" title="Ravenclaw">Ravenclaw</a><sup id="cite_ref-house_4-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-house-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> student at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> in the <a href="/wiki/1990s" title="1990s">1990s</a>. He competed in the school's <a href="/wiki/Gobstones" title="Gobstones">Gobstones</a> tournament during the <a href="/wiki/1996-1997_school_year" class="mw-redirect" title="1996-1997 school year">1996-1997 school year</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-HBPG_3-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBPG-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
