This <b>individual</b> was a male <a href="/wiki/Giant" title="Giant">giant</a> who fought in the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a> for <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a> and his <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a>. He was seen in a courtyard close to <a href="/wiki/Greenhouse_One" title="Greenhouse One">Greenhouse One</a> in which he slept. He held a <a href="/wiki/Ice_pop" title="Ice pop">blue ice cream</a>, which <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ronald Weasley</a> and <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione Granger</a> took in order to make an <a href="/wiki/Exploding_Potion" title="Exploding Potion">Exploding Potion</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
