This <b>individual</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> in his lifetime, having died of hanging. After his death he returned as a <a href="/wiki/Ghost" title="Ghost">ghost</a> and resided at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a>. In <a href="/wiki/1993" title="1993">1993</a>, he was seen in the <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor_Tower" title="Gryffindor Tower">Gryffindor Tower</a>, floating through the arriving students. When he walked through <a href="/wiki/Dean_Thomas" title="Dean Thomas">Dean Thomas</a>, he reacted as if he had just walked through <a href="/wiki/Cobweb" title="Cobweb">cobwebs</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-POAF_1-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-POAF-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
