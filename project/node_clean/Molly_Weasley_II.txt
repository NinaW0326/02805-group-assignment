<b>Molly Weasley</b> (b. <a href="/wiki/2000s" title="2000s">2000s</a>) was the elder daughter of <a href="/wiki/Percy_Weasley" title="Percy Weasley">Percy</a> and <a href="/wiki/Audrey_Weasley" title="Audrey Weasley">Audrey Weasley</a>. She has a younger sister, <a href="/wiki/Lucy_Weasley" title="Lucy Weasley">Lucy</a>. She is named after her <a href="/wiki/Molly_Weasley" title="Molly Weasley">paternal grandmother</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-WFT_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-WFT-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>

<ul><li>Her cousin, <a href="/wiki/Lily_Potter" title="Lily Potter">Lily Potter</a> is also named after her paternal grandmother, <a href="/wiki/Lily_J._Potter" title="Lily J. Potter">Lily Evans</a>.</li>
<li>She is named after her grandmother, but Molly is also the middle name of her aunt (Percy's sister) <a href="/wiki/Ginevra_Weasley" title="Ginevra Weasley">Ginevra Molly Potter (née Weasley)</a>.</li></ul>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Molly_Weasley_II?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Molly_Weasley_II?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Molly is an English name of Irish origin, meaning "sea of bitterness," and is a diminutive form of Mary, with the Latin meaning "star of the sea".
</p>
