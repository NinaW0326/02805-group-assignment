<b>Barrett Fay</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> and a strong advocate of the <a href="/wiki/Pure-blood_supremacy" title="Pure-blood supremacy">supremacy</a> of <a href="/wiki/Pure-blood" title="Pure-blood">pure-blood</a> wizards, having authored at least two books on the subject: <i><a href="/wiki/Mudbloods_and_How_to_Spot_Them" title="Mudbloods and How to Spot Them">Mudbloods and How to Spot Them</a></i>.<sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> and <i><a href="/wiki/When_Muggles_Attack" title="When Muggles Attack">When Muggles Attack</a></i><sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>. <a href="/wiki/Dolores_Umbridge" title="Dolores Umbridge">Dolores Umbridge</a> had copies of his works in her desk at the <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">Ministry of Magic</a>, while on the post of <a href="/wiki/Senior_Undersecretary_to_the_Minister_for_Magic" title="Senior Undersecretary to the Minister for Magic">Senior Undersecretary to the Minister for Magic</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-DHF1_5-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DHF1-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>
