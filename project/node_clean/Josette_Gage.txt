She married <a href="/wiki/Reynard_Gage" title="Reynard Gage">Reynard Gage</a> and had two sons with him, <a href="/wiki/Louis_Gage" title="Louis Gage">Louis</a> and <a href="/wiki/Mathis_Gage" title="Mathis Gage">Mathis</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
