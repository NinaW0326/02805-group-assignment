<p><b>Cadwallader</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> who was <a href="/wiki/Sorting_ceremony" title="Sorting ceremony">Sorted</a> into <a href="/wiki/Hufflepuff" title="Hufflepuff">Hufflepuff</a> <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Houses" title="Hogwarts Houses">house</a> at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a>. He played the position of <a href="/wiki/Chaser" title="Chaser">Chaser</a> for his house <a href="/wiki/Quidditch" title="Quidditch">Quidditch</a> <a href="/wiki/Hufflepuff_Quidditch_team" title="Hufflepuff Quidditch team">team</a>.
</p><p>In <a href="/wiki/1996" title="1996">1996</a>, during a match against the <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor" title="Gryffindor">Gryffindor</a> <a href="/wiki/Quidditch" title="Quidditch">Quidditch</a> <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor_Quidditch_team" title="Gryffindor Quidditch team">team</a>, commentator <a href="/wiki/Luna_Lovegood" title="Luna Lovegood">Luna Lovegood</a> could not remember Cadwallader's name, calling him "Bibble" and "Buggins" before <a href="/wiki/Minerva_McGonagall" title="Minerva McGonagall">Professor McGonagall</a> corrected her.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Cadwallader?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Cadwallader?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>The <a href="/wiki/Password" title="Password">password</a> to the <a href="/wiki/Portrait" title="Portrait">portrait</a> of <a href="/wiki/Bertie_Bott" title="Bertie Bott">Bertie Bott</a>, guarding a secret room, was <i>Bibble</i>.</li>
<li>The name of the seeker in the Welsh Quidditch team during the <a href="/wiki/2014_Quidditch_World_Cup" title="2014 Quidditch World Cup">2014 Quidditch World Cup</a> is said to be <a href="/wiki/Eurig_Cadwallader" title="Eurig Cadwallader">Eurig Cadwallader</a> in <a href="/wiki/Ginevra_Weasley" title="Ginevra Weasley">Ginny Potter</a>'s daily prophet commentary of the game <a href="/wiki/Wales" title="Wales">Wales</a> against <a href="/wiki/Germany" title="Germany">Germany</a>. It is possible Cadwallader is this very same Eurig Cadwallader, given their similarities in age and surname, and the fact that Hogwarts students come only from Britain, with Wales being in Britain. It is also possible that Eurig Cadwallader and this Cadwallader are relatives.</li></ul>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Cadwallader?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Cadwallader?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>Cadwallader is the name of the heraldic red Welsh dragon, as well as that of a Welsh king who declared it his mission to exterminate the English.</li>
<li>Cadwallader is also used as the alias for the devil in disguise on Escape Clause, the sixth episode of American TV Series The Twilight Zone.</li></ul>
