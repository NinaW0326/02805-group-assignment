<b>Yumboes</b> are a kind of <a href="/wiki/Africa" title="Africa">African</a> <a href="/wiki/House-elf" title="House-elf">house-elf</a> native to <a href="/wiki/Senegal" title="Senegal">Senegal</a>. They are spiteful creatures, and tend to steal food from <a href="/wiki/Human" title="Human">humans</a> as revenge for crossing them. They also have intrinsic <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magical</a> power, and can <a href="/wiki/Apparition" title="Apparition">vanish at will</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-PM_1-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PM-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="History">History</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Yumbo?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: History">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Yumbo?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: History"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>During the <a href="/wiki/1998_Quidditch_World_Cup" title="1998 Quidditch World Cup">1998 Quidditch World Cup</a>, more stringent security measures were put in place (as a response to the <a href="/wiki/Reappearance_of_the_Dark_Mark" title="Reappearance of the Dark Mark">riots that broke out</a> on the night of the final of the <a href="/wiki/1994_Quidditch_World_Cup" title="1994 Quidditch World Cup">previous World Cup</a>). One notable incident was the arrest of the <a href="/wiki/Senegalese_National_Quidditch_team" title="Senegalese National Quidditch team">Senegalese team mascots</a>, Yumboes, outside the stadium. The Yumboes responded to the arrest by stealing every bit of food within a ten-mile radius in revenge and vanishing into the night.<sup id="cite_ref-PM_1-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PM-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Yumbo?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Yumbo?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li><a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Yumboes" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Yumboes">Yumboes</a> are a type of <a href="/wiki/Fairy" title="Fairy">fairy</a> from the mythology of the <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Lebou_people" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Lebou people">Lebou people</a> who lived in what is today Senegal. One of the traits attributed to them is the theft of corn from <a href="/wiki/Human" title="Human">humans</a>.</li>
<li>In <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Goblet_of_Fire_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire (film)">film adaptation</a> of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Goblet_of_Fire" title="Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire">Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire</a></i>, two <a href="/wiki/House-elves_at_the_1994_Quidditch_World_Cup" title="House-elves at the 1994 Quidditch World Cup">house-elves</a> are seen riding camels at the <a href="/wiki/1994_Quidditch_World_Cup" title="1994 Quidditch World Cup">1994 Quidditch World Cup</a>. Camels are native to Africa, making it possible that these so-called house-elves are African Yumboes.</li>
<li>Yumboes' status is never stated, though it is likely they are considered <a href="/wiki/Being" title="Being">Beings</a>.</li></ul>
