<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Cecily?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Cecily?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>In <a href="/wiki/1926" title="1926">1926</a>, Cecily was in a relationship with <a href="/wiki/Magical_Congress_of_the_United_States_of_America" title="Magical Congress of the United States of America">MACUSA</a> employee <a href="/wiki/Sam_(Obliviator)" title="Sam (Obliviator)">Sam</a>, who worked as an <a href="/wiki/Obliviator" title="Obliviator">Obliviator</a>. Unbeknownst to her, however, Sam was also romantically involved with his co-worker&#160;<a href="/wiki/Ruby_(witch)" title="Ruby (witch)">Ruby</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-fb68_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-fb68-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/Queenie_Goldstein" title="Queenie Goldstein">Queenie Goldstein</a>, a <a href="/wiki/Legilimency" title="Legilimency">Legilimens</a>, uncovered Sam's infidelity by <a href="/wiki/Legilimency" title="Legilimency">reading his mind</a>, and bargained with him that she would not reveal his secret if he did not <a href="/wiki/Memory_Charm" title="Memory Charm">wipe the memory</a> of the <a href="/wiki/Muggle" class="mw-redirect" title="Muggle">No-Maj</a> <a href="/wiki/Jacob_Kowalski" title="Jacob Kowalski">Jacob Kowalski</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-fb68_1-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-fb68-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
