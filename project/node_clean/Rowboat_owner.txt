</p><p>The <b>rowboat owner</b> was a toothless old <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggle</a>. In <a href="/wiki/1991" title="1991">1991</a>, he lent his <a href="/wiki/Toothless_old_man%27s_rowboat" title="Toothless old man&#39;s rowboat">rowboat</a> to <a href="/wiki/Vernon_Dursley" title="Vernon Dursley">Vernon Dursley</a>, so that the <a href="/wiki/Dursley_family" title="Dursley family">Dursleys</a> and <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> could get to the <a href="/wiki/Hut-on-the-Rock" title="Hut-on-the-Rock">hut placed on a rock off the sea coast</a>, and subsequently spend the night there.<sup id="cite_ref-PS3_1-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PS3-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> They needed to do so, because Vernon and <a href="/wiki/Petunia_Dursley" title="Petunia Dursley">Petunia Dursley</a> wanted to escape the grasp of <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts</a>. But they were unsuccessful, as <a href="/wiki/Rubeus_Hagrid" title="Rubeus Hagrid">Rubeus Hagrid</a> appeared and personally delivered Harry's letter.<sup id="cite_ref-PS4_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PS4-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> It is uncertain whether he returned for the <a href="/wiki/Dursley_family" title="Dursley family">Dursleys</a>, or they returned to the mainland on some other transportation.
</p>
