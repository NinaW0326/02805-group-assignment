<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_Life">Early Life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Erica_Stainwright?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Early Life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Erica_Stainwright?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Early Life"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Erica Stainwright was born in <a href="/wiki/1932" title="1932">1932</a> into parents of unknown <a href="/wiki/Blood_status" title="Blood status">blood status</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-JKRS_1-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-JKRS-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>She became rich selling supposed <i>"cleaning"</i> <a href="/wiki/Potion" title="Potion">potions</a>, such as <a href="/wiki/Everklena" title="Everklena">Everklena</a>, but fell into disgrace when it was discovered that these products actually spawned more mould and grime, which presumably forced people to buy more of them to try to fix the problem.<sup id="cite_ref-JKRS_1-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-JKRS-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Death_and_Post-Mortem">Death and Post-Mortem</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Erica_Stainwright?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Death and Post-Mortem">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Erica_Stainwright?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Death and Post-Mortem"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Stainwright <a href="/wiki/Death" title="Death">died</a> in <a href="/wiki/2001" title="2001">2001</a> at the age of 68 or 69.<sup id="cite_ref-JKRS_1-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-JKRS-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Magical_abilities_and_skills">Magical abilities and skills</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Erica_Stainwright?veaction=edit&amp;section=4" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Erica_Stainwright?action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li><b><a href="/wiki/Potion" title="Potion">Potions</a></b>: Stainwright was a talented professional <a href="/wiki/Potioneer" title="Potioneer">Potioneer</a>, given how she successfully created her own product range of cleaning potions such as <a href="/wiki/Everklena" title="Everklena">Everklena</a>, on the market and managed to make a good living out of selling them. However, these potions were fraudulent solutions which actually spawned mould and grime as opposed to eradicating it, meaning she was far from well-regarded in this branch of <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magic</a> and <a href="/wiki/Science" title="Science">science</a>.</li></ul>
