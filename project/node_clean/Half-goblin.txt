A <b>half-goblin</b>&#160;or <b>part-goblin&#160;</b>is a cross-breed between a <a href="/wiki/Goblin" title="Goblin">goblin</a>, whose ancestry accounts for their diminutive size, and a <a href="/wiki/Human" title="Human">human</a>.

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Description">Description</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Half-goblin?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Description">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Half-goblin?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Description"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="High_Intelligence">High Intelligence</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Half-goblin?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: High Intelligence">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Half-goblin?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: High Intelligence"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p><a href="/wiki/Filius_Flitwick" title="Filius Flitwick">Filius Flitwick</a> who was sorted into <a href="/wiki/Ravenclaw" title="Ravenclaw">Ravenclaw</a> house at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a>, was a "model student" during his time there and eventually returned to the school to teach. It is possible that this was inherited from his goblin ancestor, as goblins are known for their intelligence (though whether this is a matter of cultural encouragement of cunning, or an actual genetic disposition, is unclear).
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Magic_Resistance">Magic Resistance</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Half-goblin?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Magic Resistance">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Half-goblin?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Magic Resistance"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>It is possible that certain spells and potions do not work on Half-goblins. For instance, it is unknown if Half-goblins can use <a href="/wiki/Polyjuice_Potion" title="Polyjuice Potion">Polyjuice Potion</a> as this particular potion is designed for human transformations only, and therefore does not work on other beings. For instance, <a href="/wiki/Polyjuice_Potion" title="Polyjuice Potion">Polyjuice Potion</a> is useless to any <a href="/wiki/Half-Giant" class="mw-redirect" title="Half-Giant">Half-Giant</a>. However, because the potion is known to be effective on <a href="/wiki/Half-Veela" title="Half-Veela">Part-Veelas</a>, if their Veela ancestry goes back at least two generations, it is possible that Part-Goblins (and Part-Giants) whose ancestry goes further back than their direct parentage could use this potion.
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Place_in_society">Place in society</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Half-goblin?veaction=edit&amp;section=4" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Place in society">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Half-goblin?action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Place in society"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>As <a href="/wiki/Half-breed" title="Half-breed">half-breeds</a>, half-goblins fall into the same prejudices that other half-breeds suffer. <a href="/wiki/Dolores_Umbridge" title="Dolores Umbridge">Dolores Umbridge</a> was renowned for and later punished for crimes against half-breeds. On the other hand, the only known part-goblin, <a href="/wiki/Filius_Flitwick" title="Filius Flitwick">Filius Flitwick</a>, is trained in the human magical arts, as shown by his carrying a wand, having attended Hogwarts and teaching there. This proves that part-goblins are allowed to carry wands and perform wand magic, and are not covered by the Wand Ban that forbids full goblins from using wands, though they would be capable of it.&#160;
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Known_Part-Goblins">Known Part-Goblins</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Half-goblin?veaction=edit&amp;section=5" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Known Part-Goblins">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Half-goblin?action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Known Part-Goblins"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li><a href="/wiki/Filius_Flitwick" title="Filius Flitwick">Filius Flitwick</a> has a <a href="/wiki/Goblin" title="Goblin">Goblin</a> <a href="/wiki/Filius_Flitwick%27s_goblin_ancestor" title="Filius Flitwick&#39;s goblin ancestor">ancestor</a>, though it is unknown how far back. Despite this, there were a number of Ravenclaw students who incorrectly believed that he was part <a href="/wiki/House-elf" title="House-elf">elf</a>, though they were never "rude enough to ask".<sup id="cite_ref-pottermore_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-pottermore-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li>Circus Arcanus half-goblins<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup></li></ul>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Half-goblin?veaction=edit&amp;section=6" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Half-goblin?action=edit&amp;section=6" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>It is unknown if goblins can breed with <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggles</a>, but since (after the&#160;<a href="/wiki/International_Statute_of_Wizarding_Secrecy" title="International Statute of Wizarding Secrecy">Statute of Secrecy</a> was installed) very few Muggles ever became aware that goblins even exist, this is a remote possibility.</li></ul>
