<figure class="thumb tright show-info-icon" style="width: 182px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/e/ed/Gwf.PNG/revision/latest?cb=20100515152119" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/e/ed/Gwf.PNG/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/180?cb=20100515152119" decoding="async" width="180" height="233" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="Gwf.PNG" data-image-key="Gwf.PNG" /></a> 	 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:Gwf.PNG" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption">The girl in <i><a href="/wiki/LEGO_Harry_Potter_(disambiguation)" title="LEGO Harry Potter (disambiguation)">LEGO Harry Potter</a></i></p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure>
<ul><li>This individual depicted may have died sometime prior to <a href="/wiki/1991" title="1991">1991</a>, as many portraits hung at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Castle" title="Hogwarts Castle">Hogwarts Castle</a> depict deceased individuals.</li></ul>
<ul><li>In the film adaptations of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Philosopher%27s_Stone_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Philosopher&#39;s Stone (film)">Philosopher's Stone</a></i>, <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Chamber_of_Secrets_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets (film)">Chamber of Secrets</a></i> and <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Prisoner_of_Azkaban_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban (film)">Prisoner of Azkaban</a></i>, the girl with flowers was portrayed by <a href="/wiki/Violet_Columbus" title="Violet Columbus">Violet Columbus</a>. She is the daughter of <a href="/wiki/Chris_Columbus" title="Chris Columbus">Chris Columbus</a>, the director of the first two films, the younger sister of <a href="/wiki/Eleanor_Columbus" title="Eleanor Columbus">Eleanor Columbus</a> and <a href="/wiki/Brendan_Columbus" title="Brendan Columbus">Brendan Columbus</a>, who portrayed <a href="/wiki/Susan_Bones" title="Susan Bones">Susan Bones</a> and a boy in study hall in <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Chamber_of_Secrets_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets (film)">Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets</a>, respectively, and the older sister of <a href="/wiki/Isabella_Columbus" title="Isabella Columbus">Isabella Columbus</a>, who played a girl in <a href="/wiki/Flourish_and_Blotts" title="Flourish and Blotts">Flourish and Blotts</a> in <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Chamber_of_Secrets_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets (film)">Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets</a></i>.</li>
<li>A similar portrait (<i><a href="/wiki/Shepherdess" title="Shepherdess">Shepherdess</a></i>) is seen in the video game adaptations of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Order_of_the_Phoenix_(video_game)" title="Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix (video game)">Order of the Phoenix</a></i> and <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Half-Blood_Prince_(video_game)" title="Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince (video game)">Half-Blood Prince</a></i>.</li>
<li>In <i><a href="/wiki/LEGO_Harry_Potter:_Years_1-4" title="LEGO Harry Potter: Years 1-4">LEGO Harry Potter: Years 1-4</a></i> the portrait also appears in <a href="/wiki/Madam_Malkin%27s" class="mw-redirect" title="Madam Malkin&#39;s">Madam Malkin's</a>.</li>
<li>In <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter:_Hogwarts_Mystery" title="Harry Potter: Hogwarts Mystery">Harry Potter: Hogwarts Mystery</a></i>, a portrait appears in the <a href="/wiki/East_Towers" title="East Towers">East Towers</a>. If the portrait is empty, the player can click it to free one free <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter:_Hogwarts_Mystery#Energy" title="Harry Potter: Hogwarts Mystery">energy</a>.</li>
<li>In <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter:_Magic_Awakened" title="Harry Potter: Magic Awakened">Harry Potter: Magic Awakened</a></i>, the portrait appears in the Grand Staircase. She's asked by the player and two of their friends if she has saw <a href="/wiki/Ivy_Warrington" title="Ivy Warrington">Ivy Warrington</a>, but she was worrying their <a href="/wiki/Wand" title="Wand">Wand</a>-light was going to peel her oils.</li></ul>
