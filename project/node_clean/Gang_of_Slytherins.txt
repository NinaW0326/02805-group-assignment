<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="History">History</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Gang_of_Slytherins?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: History">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Gang_of_Slytherins?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: History"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>According to <a href="/wiki/Sirius_Black" title="Sirius Black">Sirius Black</a>, they were all in <a href="/wiki/Slytherin" title="Slytherin">Slytherin House</a> and most, if not all, became <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a> during the <a href="/wiki/First_Wizarding_War" title="First Wizarding War">First Wizarding War</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-GOF27_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF27-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> This group appeared to occasionally bully other students such as <a href="/wiki/Mary_Macdonald" title="Mary Macdonald">Mary Macdonald</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-DH33_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH33-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> They likely targeted <a href="/wiki/Muggle-born" title="Muggle-born">Muggle-born</a> students and <a href="/wiki/Blood_traitor" title="Blood traitor">blood traitors</a>.
</p><p>It was partially <a href="/wiki/Severus_Snape" title="Severus Snape">Severus Snape</a>'s relationship with this group that lead his friendship with <a href="/wiki/Lily_J._Potter" title="Lily J. Potter">Lily Evans</a> to end unpleasantly.<sup id="cite_ref-DH33_2-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH33-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> It is likely that they played a role in influencing his <a href="/wiki/Pure-blood_supremacy" title="Pure-blood supremacy">prejudice</a> towards <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggles</a> and his inclusion into the Death Eater ranks.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Gang_of_Slytherins?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Gang_of_Slytherins?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li><a href="/wiki/Sirius_Black" title="Sirius Black">Sirius Black</a> claims that <a href="/wiki/Bellatrix_Lestrange" title="Bellatrix Lestrange">Bellatrix Lestrange</a> was associated with this group,<sup id="cite_ref-GOF27_1-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF27-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> but according to the Black family tree, she was born in <a href="/wiki/1951" title="1951">1951</a> and her time at Hogwarts would not have overlapped with <a href="/wiki/Severus_Snape" title="Severus Snape">Severus Snape</a>'s at all. This might be a mistake on <a href="/wiki/J._K._Rowling" title="J. K. Rowling">J. K. Rowling</a>'s part, but it is possible that Sirius remembered his cousin Bellatrix (who presumably lived in <a href="/wiki/12_Grimmauld_Place" title="12 Grimmauld Place">12 Grimmauld Place</a> before her marriage) spending time with her fellow future Death Eaters <i>outside </i>of school and simplified the connection when he was speaking to <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>, <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ron Weasley</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione Granger</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Lucius_Malfoy" title="Lucius Malfoy">Lucius Malfoy</a> might have been associated with this group as well. He is familiar with Snape and many of the other members of the gang, and he attended Hogwarts around the same time.</li>
<li>There are members from the <a href="/wiki/Avery_family" title="Avery family">Avery</a>, <a href="/wiki/Lestrange_family" title="Lestrange family">Lestrange</a>, <a href="/wiki/Mulciber_family" title="Mulciber family">Mulciber</a>, and the <a href="/wiki/Rosier_family" title="Rosier family">Rosier families</a> in both this gang and in <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle%27s_gang" title="Tom Riddle&#39;s gang">Tom Riddle's gang</a>. Both gangs are noted for their membership with the Death Eaters, and considering the timeline, it is possible that they are sets of parents and children. The parents would have joined Tom Riddle's gang in the <a href="/wiki/1940s" title="1940s">1940s</a>, and the children joined the Gang of Slytherins in the <a href="/wiki/1970s" title="1970s">1970s</a>.</li>
<li>None of these individuals attempted to help Snape during <a href="/wiki/Snape%27s_Worst_Memory" class="mw-redirect" title="Snape&#39;s Worst Memory">Snape's Worst Memory</a>, even though Avery and Mulciber (whom <a href="/wiki/Lily_J._Potter" title="Lily J. Potter">Lily Evans</a> singled out as his close friends among the gang) were probably in his year and would have joined the exodus outside after their Defence Against the Dark Arts OWL. It is unknown why, but considering how adamantly Snape defended them during the Prince's Tale, there might have been a hierarchy to their friendship in which the impoverished half-blood ranked lower than the two (presumed) pure-bloods. Alternatively, they had simply not lingered on the grounds and already gone elsewhere, and hence only learned about the incident after the fact.</li></ul>
