<p><b>Professor Aurora Sinistra</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch</a> who taught in the <a href="/wiki/Astronomy_(class)" title="Astronomy (class)">Astronomy</a> <a href="/wiki/Astronomy_department" title="Astronomy department">department</a> at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a>. <a href="/wiki/Professor" title="Professor">Professor</a> Sinistra was teaching by <a href="/wiki/September" title="September">September</a> <a href="/wiki/1985" title="1985">1985</a>. The subject included studying the night sky with <a href="/wiki/Telescope" title="Telescope">telescopes</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-PS_7-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PS-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>The professor was present at many important events throughout <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>'s time at Hogwarts. In the <a href="/wiki/1992-1993_school_year" class="mw-redirect" title="1992-1993 school year">1992-1993 school year</a>, the professor was present at the discovery of a <a href="/wiki/Petrification" title="Petrification">petrified</a> student, <a href="/wiki/Justin_Finch-Fletchley" title="Justin Finch-Fletchley">Justin Finch-Fletchley</a>, and helped transport him with <a href="/wiki/Filius_Flitwick" title="Filius Flitwick">Filius Flitwick</a>, the <a href="/wiki/Charms_Master" title="Charms Master">Charms Master</a>, to <a href="/wiki/Poppy_Pomfrey" title="Poppy Pomfrey">Madam Pomfrey</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Hospital_Wing" title="Hospital Wing">Hospital Wing</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-COS_3-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COS-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>In <a href="/wiki/1994" title="1994">1994</a>, Sinistra attended the <a href="/wiki/Yule_Ball" title="Yule Ball">Yule Ball</a> with "<a href="/wiki/Bartemius_Crouch_Junior" title="Bartemius Crouch Junior">Alastor Moody</a>", and danced a nervous two-step, careful to avoid his wooden leg.<sup id="cite_ref-GOF_4-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> Sinistra prepared <a href="/wiki/Fifth_year" title="Fifth year">fifth year</a>'s for their <a href="/wiki/Ordinary_Wizarding_Level" title="Ordinary Wizarding Level">Ordinary Wizarding Levels</a> by demanding large amounts of homework. She was probably good friends with <a href="/wiki/Wilhelmina_Grubbly-Plank" title="Wilhelmina Grubbly-Plank">Wilhelmina Grubbly-Plank</a>, who she was found chatting with numerous times throughout the year.<sup id="cite_ref-OP_8-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OP-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>It is unknown if Sinistra participated in the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a> alongside the students and fellow staff. If she did, then she witnessed the final defeat of <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a> at the hands of Harry Potter. It is also unknown what she did after the <a href="/wiki/Second_Wizarding_War" title="Second Wizarding War">Second Wizarding War</a>.
</p>

<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_life">Early life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Aurora_Sinistra?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Early life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Aurora_Sinistra?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Early life"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Little is known about Professor Sinistra's early life. She presumably excelled at <a href="/wiki/Astronomy_(class)" title="Astronomy (class)">Astronomy</a> during her student years, being either homeschooled by her parents or educated at a school.
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Career_at_Hogwarts">Career at Hogwarts</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Aurora_Sinistra?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Career at Hogwarts">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Aurora_Sinistra?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Career at Hogwarts"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<figure class="thumb tright show-info-icon" style="width: 246px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/c/c2/Sinistraminervaalbus.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20170904133706" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/c/c2/Sinistraminervaalbus.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/244?cb=20170904133706" decoding="async" width="244" height="107" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="Sinistraminervaalbus.jpg" data-image-key="Sinistraminervaalbus.jpg" /></a> 	 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:Sinistraminervaalbus.jpg" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption">Sinistra, <a href="/wiki/Albus_Dumbledore" title="Albus Dumbledore">Albus Dumbledore</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Minerva_McGonagall" title="Minerva McGonagall">Minerva McGonagall</a> singing the <a href="/wiki/Hoggy_Warty_Hogwarts" title="Hoggy Warty Hogwarts">Hogwarts school song</a> in 1991</p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure>
<p>Before September 1985,<sup id="cite_ref-HMy2_6-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HMy2-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup> Sinistra applied for a teaching position at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> and was accepted, becoming a <a href="/wiki/Professor" title="Professor">Hogwarts Professor</a>. She taught many of her lessons at midnight at the top of the <a href="/wiki/Astronomy_Tower" title="Astronomy Tower">Astronomy Tower</a>, the highest point of the castle.<sup id="cite_ref-PS_7-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PS-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup> Her students each required a telescope for her lessons, and she taught all about the different stars and constellations, as well as moons and planets.
</p><p>She regularly attended the <a href="/wiki/Welcoming_Feast" title="Welcoming Feast">Start-of-Term Feast</a>, where she appeared to enjoy socialising with other teachers, such as <a href="/wiki/Pomona_Sprout" title="Pomona Sprout">Pomona Sprout</a>, the <a href="/wiki/Herbology" title="Herbology">Herbology</a> professor,<sup id="cite_ref-GOF_4-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> and <a href="/wiki/Wilhelmina_Grubbly-Plank" title="Wilhelmina Grubbly-Plank">Wilhelmina Grubbly-Plank</a>, the <a href="/wiki/Care_of_Magical_Creatures" title="Care of Magical Creatures">Care of Magical Creatures</a> professor and later substitute for <a href="/wiki/Rubeus_Hagrid" title="Rubeus Hagrid">Professor Rubeus Hagrid</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-OP_8-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OP-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup> She was known to give large amounts of homework to students in their <a href="/wiki/Fifth_year" title="Fifth year">fifth year</a> and those who continued <a href="/wiki/Astronomy_(class)" title="Astronomy (class)">Astronomy</a> for <a href="/wiki/Sixth_year" title="Sixth year">sixth</a> and <a href="/wiki/Seventh_year" title="Seventh year">seventh</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-OP_8-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OP-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h4><span id="1985–1986_school_year"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="1985.E2.80.931986_school_year">1985–1986 school year</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Aurora_Sinistra?veaction=edit&amp;section=4" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: 1985–1986 school year">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Aurora_Sinistra?action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: 1985–1986 school year"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<p>Professor Sinistra was teaching in <a href="/wiki/Astronomy_Tower" title="Astronomy Tower">Astronomy Tower</a> when she became trapped in <a href="/wiki/Cursed_ice" title="Cursed ice">cursed ice</a> along with the children in her class. She was later broken out of the ice by her fellow teachers.<sup id="cite_ref-HMy2_6-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HMy2-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h4><span id="1989–1990_school_year"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="1989.E2.80.931990_school_year">1989–1990 school year</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Aurora_Sinistra?veaction=edit&amp;section=5" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: 1989–1990 school year">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Aurora_Sinistra?action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: 1989–1990 school year"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<p>Professor Sinistra attended the <a href="/wiki/Start-of-Term_Feast" title="Start-of-Term Feast">Start-of-Term Feast</a>, where she informed students that she taught at midnight.<sup id="cite_ref-HM61_9-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HM61-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<p>That year, Hogwarts was the reluctant host of Ministry of Magic bureaucrat <a href="/wiki/Dolores_Umbridge" title="Dolores Umbridge">Dolores Umbridge</a>, who had been appointed to the position of <a href="/wiki/Defence_Against_the_Dark_Arts" title="Defence Against the Dark Arts">Defence Against the Dark Arts</a> teacher but was in reality there to undermine Dumbledore and ensure Ministry control of the school. At the Welcoming Feast, Professor Umbridge interrupted Headmaster Dumbledore's usual announcement and proceeded to make an incredibly dull speech about the Ministry and Hogwarts, at one point making a little bow to the other teachers. Professor Sinistra, like her colleagues, understood the real meaning of the speech (the <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">Ministry</a> was interfering at Hogwarts) and neither returned the bow nor applauded when the speech was over.<sup id="cite_ref-OP_8-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OP-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Umbridge was later appointed High Inquisitor by order of Minister <a href="/wiki/Cornelius_Fudge" title="Cornelius Fudge">Cornelius Fudge</a>, which gave her the power to inspect and dismiss teachers who ostensibly were not up to scratch (in reality it was a means of eliminating teachers who were either too close to Dumbledore or who taught things she felt were inappropriate). Professor Sinistra presumably endured this intrusive inspection of her classes, but evidently proved a competent teacher as she was neither sacked or placed on probation. She was also presumably among the teachers who requested Umbridge's help in getting rid of rogue fireworks in their classrooms and those who cheered her eventual departure at the end of the year.<sup id="cite_ref-OP_8-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OP-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Personality_and_traits">Personality and traits</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Aurora_Sinistra?veaction=edit&amp;section=13" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Personality and traits">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Aurora_Sinistra?action=edit&amp;section=13" title="Edit section: Personality and traits"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Sinistra was a serious and strict woman.<sup id="cite_ref-HM6_13-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HM6-13">&#91;13&#93;</a></sup> She feared the <a href="/wiki/Dark_Wizard" title="Dark Wizard">Dark</a> <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a> and her <a href="/wiki/Boggart" title="Boggart">Boggart</a> assumed his form.<sup id="cite_ref-LEGO_5-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-LEGO-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> She was also proven to have been a highly competent teacher, as her lessons inspected by <a href="/wiki/Dolores_Umbridge" title="Dolores Umbridge">Dolores Umbridge</a> were deemed satisfactory enough that she was not subject to disciplinary action by her.<sup id="cite_ref-OP_8-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OP-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup> Her competency as a teacher was also proven by how she taught her subject for at least ten years at Hogwarts, showing her to have been highly experienced and skilled in her field. 
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Magical_abilities_and_skills">Magical abilities and skills</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Aurora_Sinistra?veaction=edit&amp;section=14" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Aurora_Sinistra?action=edit&amp;section=14" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li><b><a href="/wiki/Astronomy_(class)" title="Astronomy (class)">Astronomy</a></b>: Aurora was a highly accomplished professional <a href="/wiki/Astronomer" title="Astronomer">Astronomer</a> who was very knowledgeable and experienced in her <a href="/wiki/Science" title="Science">scientific</a> field,  and taught the subject for at least ten years at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a>.</li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Charm" title="Charm">Charms</a></b>: Aurora was able to conjure a non-corporeal <a href="/wiki/Patronus_Charm" title="Patronus Charm">Patronus</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-LEGO_5-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-LEGO-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> a very advanced piece of <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magic</a> and a mark of superior magical ability, given the immense difficulty of casting such a powerful protective charm.</li></ul>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Aurora_Sinistra?veaction=edit&amp;section=15" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Aurora_Sinistra?action=edit&amp;section=15" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Professor Sinistra's surname originates from the Latin "<i>sinister</i>", meaning "<i>left</i>" or "<i>with a direction to the left</i>".<sup id="cite_ref-Sinester_14-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Sinester-14">&#91;14&#93;</a></sup> The most probable explanation for this is that most planets of the solar system rotate anti-clockwise. Sinistra is also the name of a star of the constellation of Ophiuchus. The contemporary meaning of the word "<i>sinister</i>" is "evil" or "menacing"<sup id="cite_ref-Sinester_14-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Sinester-14">&#91;14&#93;</a></sup> and can be a synonym of "dark" (like in "dark events" for example). Here, the name "Sinistra" probably refers to the fact that Astronomy is mostly practised at night, when the sky is dark.
</p><p>The first name 'Aurora' indicates a relationship with the dawn, however it is probably derived from 'Aurora', a phenomena which occurs when solar winds interact with the earth's magnetic field.<sup id="cite_ref-15" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-15">&#91;15&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
