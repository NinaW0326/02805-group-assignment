<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_male_Muggle_Studies_teacher?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_male_Muggle_Studies_teacher?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>When <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> was given a torch by a <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggle</a> student, he brought it to the professor's classroom, thinking it might be the right place for it. Instead of the teacher, he met his assistant and the torch was included in the artefact collection.<sup id="cite_ref-COSG_2-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COSG-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> He left at some point before the <a href="/wiki/1996%E2%80%931997_school_year" title="1996–1997 school year">1996–1997 school year</a>, and was replaced by <a href="/wiki/Professor" title="Professor">Professor</a> <a href="/wiki/Charity_Burbage" title="Charity Burbage">Charity Burbage</a>.
</p>
