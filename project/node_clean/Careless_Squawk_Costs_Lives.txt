<p><b>Careless Squawk Costs Lives</b> was an official notice from the <a href="/wiki/Department_for_the_Regulation_and_Control_of_Magical_Creatures" title="Department for the Regulation and Control of Magical Creatures">Department for the Regulation and Control of Magical Creatures</a> reminding all owners of pet <a href="/wiki/Fwooper" title="Fwooper">Fwoopers</a> to maintain the <a href="/wiki/Silencing_Charm" title="Silencing Charm">Silencing Charms</a> on them at all times. 
</p><p>As extended exposure to Fwooper song can cause madness or death, caution and protection is advised and individual should report to <a href="/wiki/St_Mungo%27s_Hospital_for_Magical_Maladies_and_Injuries" title="St Mungo&#39;s Hospital for Magical Maladies and Injuries">St Mungo's Hospital for Magical Maladies and Injuries</a> if they exhibit signs of Fwooper-induced mania.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> 
</p>
