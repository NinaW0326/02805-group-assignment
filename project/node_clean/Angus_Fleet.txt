<b>Angus Fleet</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggle</a> who lived in <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Peebles" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Peebles">Peebles</a>, <a href="/wiki/Scotland" title="Scotland">Scotland</a>. On <a href="/wiki/1_September" title="1 September">1 September</a>, <a href="/wiki/1992" title="1992">1992</a>, the <i><a href="/wiki/Daily_Prophet" title="Daily Prophet">Daily Prophet</a></i> reported that Fleet spotted a <a href="/wiki/Flying_Ford_Anglia" title="Flying Ford Anglia">Ford Anglia</a> in flight, and informed the police of the sighting.
</p>

<ul><li>Angus Fleet was presumably one of the <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggles</a> that <a href="/wiki/Molly_Weasley" title="Molly Weasley">Molly Weasley</a> mentioned to her son <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ron</a>, saying that he caused <a href="/wiki/Arthur_Weasley" title="Arthur Weasley">Arthur Weasley</a> to face an inquiry at <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">work</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup></li>
