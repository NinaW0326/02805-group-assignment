<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_Male_Death_Eater_(VIII)?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_Male_Death_Eater_(VIII)?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_life">Early life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_Male_Death_Eater_(VIII)?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Early life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_Male_Death_Eater_(VIII)?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Early life"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>This Death Eater was presumably born prior to <a href="/wiki/1981" title="1981">1981</a>. He would have been a <a href="/wiki/Half-blood" title="Half-blood">half-blood</a> or <a href="/wiki/Pure-blood" title="Pure-blood">pure-blood</a> <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a>, as his master <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a> was an extreme <a href="/wiki/Pure-blood_supremacy" title="Pure-blood supremacy">pure-blood supremacist</a> who despised and sought to eradicate <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggles</a> and <a href="/wiki/Muggle-born" title="Muggle-born">Muggle-borns</a>, along with <a href="/wiki/Blood_traitor" title="Blood traitor">blood traitors</a>. It is also highly possible that he fought for Voldemort during the <a href="/wiki/First_Wizarding_War" title="First Wizarding War">First Wizarding War</a>. 
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Second_Wizarding_War">Second Wizarding War</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_Male_Death_Eater_(VIII)?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Second Wizarding War">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_Male_Death_Eater_(VIII)?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Second Wizarding War"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>This Death Eater <a href="/wiki/Raid_on_the_Hogwarts_Express" title="Raid on the Hogwarts Express">raided</a> the <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Express" title="Hogwarts Express">Hogwarts Express</a> on <a href="/wiki/1_September" title="1 September">1 September</a>, <a href="/wiki/1997" title="1997">1997</a>, in order to search the train for <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> with <a href="/wiki/Unidentified_male_bald_Death_Eater" title="Unidentified male bald Death Eater">another Death Eater</a>. At the time, both the <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">Ministry of Magic</a> and <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts</a> were under the control of <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a>. He was, however, unsuccessful in his search for Potter, as he, along with <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione Granger</a> and <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ron Weasley</a>, were on a mission to find and destroy Voldemort's <a href="/wiki/Horcrux" title="Horcrux">Horcruxes</a> at the time, and did not return to Hogwarts for their <a href="/wiki/Seventh_year" title="Seventh year">final year</a> of education at the school.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<p>This Death Eater was at <a href="/wiki/Gringotts_Wizarding_Bank" title="Gringotts Wizarding Bank">Gringotts Wizarding Bank</a> as <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>, <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ronald Weasley</a>, <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione Granger</a> and <a href="/wiki/Griphook" title="Griphook">Griphook</a> staged a <a href="/wiki/Break-in_of_Gringotts_Wizarding_Bank_(1998)" title="Break-in of Gringotts Wizarding Bank (1998)">break-in</a> to retrieve <a href="/wiki/Helga_Hufflepuff%27s_Cup" class="mw-redirect" title="Helga Hufflepuff&#39;s Cup">Helga Hufflepuff's Cup</a> from the <a href="/wiki/Lestrange_Vault" title="Lestrange Vault">Lestrange Vault</a>.
</p>

<p>Later that day, he was <a href="/wiki/Killing_Curse" title="Killing Curse">killed</a>&#160;by <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a> in a fit of rage at <a href="/wiki/Malfoy_Manor" title="Malfoy Manor">Malfoy Manor</a>, after the Dark Lord&#160;was informed by the <a href="/wiki/Goblin" title="Goblin">goblins</a> working at Gringotts that <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> had managed to steal <a href="/wiki/Helga_Hufflepuff%27s_Cup" class="mw-redirect" title="Helga Hufflepuff&#39;s Cup">Helga Hufflepuff's Cup</a> from the bank.<sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Magical_Abilities_and_Skills">Magical Abilities and Skills</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_Male_Death_Eater_(VIII)?veaction=edit&amp;section=4" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Magical Abilities and Skills">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_Male_Death_Eater_(VIII)?action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Magical Abilities and Skills"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li><b><a href="/wiki/Wandless_Magic" class="mw-redirect" title="Wandless Magic">Wandless Magic</a></b>: This Death Eater was highly skilled at using <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magic</a> without a <a href="/wiki/Wand" title="Wand">wand</a>. When searching for <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> he was able to halt the <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Express" title="Hogwarts Express">Hogwarts Express</a> simply with a hand gesture.</li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Apparition" title="Apparition">Apparition</a></b>: He was able to apparate in front of the <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Express" title="Hogwarts Express">Hogwarts Express</a>.</li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Dark_Arts" title="Dark Arts">Dark Arts</a></b>: Being a Death Eater, he would have been highly proficient in the dark arts and the casting of <a href="/wiki/Dark_charm" title="Dark charm">dark spells</a>&#160;and most likely the <a href="/wiki/Unforgivable_Curses" title="Unforgivable Curses">Unforgivable Curses</a>.</li></ul>
