<ul><li>Elfric the Eager's species is not established, but since he was responsible for a revolt, and is covered in a <a href="/wiki/History_of_Magic" title="History of Magic">History of Magic</a> class, it is likely he was a <a href="/wiki/Goblin" title="Goblin">Goblin</a>.
