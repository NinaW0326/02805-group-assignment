<b>Chang</b><sup id="cite_ref-role_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-role-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> and <a href="/wiki/Auror" title="Auror">Auror</a> who lived in the early <a href="/wiki/20th_century" title="20th century">20th century</a>. In <a href="/wiki/1927" title="1927">1927</a>, he was one of the <a href="/wiki/Auror" title="Auror">Aurors</a> led by <a href="/wiki/Theseus_Scamander" title="Theseus Scamander">Theseus Scamander</a> who were summoned to break-up <a href="/wiki/Gellert_Grindelwald" title="Gellert Grindelwald">Gellert Grindelwald</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Gellert_Grindelwald%27s_Paris_rally" title="Gellert Grindelwald&#39;s Paris rally">rally</a> at the <a href="/wiki/Lestrange_Mausoleum" title="Lestrange Mausoleum">Lestrange Mausoleum</a> in <a href="/wiki/Paris" title="Paris">Paris</a>, <a href="/wiki/France" title="France">France</a>.
</p><p>Chang was <a href="/wiki/Cho_Chang" title="Cho Chang">Cho Chang</a>'s paternal grandfather.<sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
