<p>This <b><a href="/wiki/Goblin" title="Goblin">goblin</a></b> was a guard at <a href="/wiki/Gringotts_Wizarding_Bank" title="Gringotts Wizarding Bank">Gringotts Wizarding Bank</a> in <a href="/wiki/1991" title="1991">1991</a>. He wore a uniform of scarlet and gold, and stood by the bank's outer doors to greet visitors. He was described as having a pointed beard, a swarthy, clever face, and was about a head shorter than <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> was when he was eleven.<sup id="cite_ref-PS5_1-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PS5-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
