<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/White-Haired_Healer?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/White-Haired_Healer?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Born somewhere in <a href="/wiki/Europe" title="Europe">Europe</a>, this <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch</a> was informed of the <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">existence of magic</a> and was trained as a witch.
On <a href="/wiki/2_May" title="2 May">2 May</a>, <a href="/wiki/1998" title="1998">1998</a>, she was called to <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Castle" title="Hogwarts Castle">Hogwarts Castle</a> for the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a>, and helped as a <a href="/wiki/Healer" title="Healer">healer</a> there. 
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Physical_appearance">Physical appearance</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/White-Haired_Healer?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Physical appearance">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/White-Haired_Healer?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Physical appearance"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>This witch had greyish white hair and fair skin; she wore the uniform of a <a href="/wiki/Healer" title="Healer">healer</a>. It can be noted that even when bending over a corpse, she managed to retain rather good posture.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Magical_abilities_and_skills">Magical abilities and skills</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/White-Haired_Healer?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/White-Haired_Healer?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The witch, whoever she was, has a few skills:
</p>
<ul><li><b><a href="/wiki/Healing_magic" title="Healing magic">Healing magic</a></b>: She was called upon to act as a <a href="/wiki/Healer" title="Healer">healer</a> in the battle, and hence it is reasonable to assume that she was well-versed in this field of magic.</li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Defence_Against_the_Dark_Arts" title="Defence Against the Dark Arts">Defence Against the Dark Arts</a></b>: She managed to survive at least half of the battle, and given that <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">her opponents</a> sought <a href="/wiki/Killing_Curse" title="Killing Curse">to kill</a>, it is fair to say she was good in this field of magic as well.</li></ul>
