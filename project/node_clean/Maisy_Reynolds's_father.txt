<p><b>Mr Reynolds</b> was the father of <a href="/wiki/Maisy_Reynolds" title="Maisy Reynolds">Maisy Reynolds</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-script_1-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-script-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>At some point during the <a href="/wiki/1997-1998_school_year" class="mw-redirect" title="1997-1998 school year">1997-1998 school year</a>, her daughter Maisy disobeyed <a href="/wiki/Alecto_Carrow" title="Alecto Carrow">Alecto Carrow</a>, then a teacher at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School</a>, which made the <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eater</a> "pay a visit" to her father. Alecto Carrow would, afterwards, threaten to do it again if Maisy challenged her.<sup id="cite_ref-script_1-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-script-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
