<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Ismelda_Murk?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Ismelda_Murk?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span id="Early_life_(1970s-1983)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_life_.281970s-1983.29">Early life <span class="template-c">(1970s-1983)</span></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Ismelda_Murk?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Early life (1970s-1983)">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Ismelda_Murk?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Early life (1970s-1983)"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Born in the early <a href="/wiki/1970s" title="1970s">1970s</a>, Ismelda Murk had a <a href="/wiki/Ismelda_Murk%27s_sister" title="Ismelda Murk&#39;s sister">sister</a>, who achieved great success as a student and so was showered with <a href="/wiki/Love" title="Love">love</a> and attention from their parents at Ismelda's expense. This parental neglect prompted Ismelda to begin acting out in the hopes of getting more attention. Her futile tactics involved <a href="/wiki/Transfiguration" title="Transfiguration">Transfiguration</a>, <a href="/wiki/Hex" title="Hex">hexes</a>, and animal abuse. Her desire for attention became increasingly dormant until it was a pivotal part of her personality.<sup id="cite_ref-crushed_2-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-crushed-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span id="Hogwarts_years_(1984â€“91)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="Hogwarts_years_.281984.E2.80.9391.29">Hogwarts years <span class="template-c">(1984â€“91)</span></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Ismelda_Murk?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Hogwarts years (1984â€“91)">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Ismelda_Murk?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Hogwarts years (1984â€“91)"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Third_year">Third year</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Ismelda_Murk?veaction=edit&amp;section=4" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Third year">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Ismelda_Murk?action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Third year"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<p>Ismelda took at least <a href="/wiki/Care_of_Magical_Creatures" title="Care of Magical Creatures">Care of Magical Creatures</a> and <a href="/wiki/Divination_(class)" title="Divination (class)">Divination</a> as an elective.<sup id="cite_ref-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>During the <a href="/wiki/1986%E2%80%931987_school_year" title="1986â€“1987 school year">1986â€“1987 school year</a>, she, along with fellow <a href="/wiki/Slytherin" title="Slytherin">Slytherin</a> <a href="/wiki/Barnaby_Lee" title="Barnaby Lee">Barnaby Lee</a>, joined <a href="/wiki/Merula_Snyde" title="Merula Snyde">Merula Snyde</a> on her search for the <a href="/wiki/Cursed_Vaults" title="Cursed Vaults">Cursed Vaults</a>. When Barnaby asked his associates' opinion on what <a href="/wiki/Vault_of_Fear" title="Vault of Fear">the next Cursed Vault</a> could contain, Ismelda hoped it to be "something that could bring <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">the Dark Lord</a> back".<sup id="cite_ref-y3ch2_4-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-y3ch2-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Ismelda accompanied Merula to confront <a href="/wiki/Jacob%27s_sibling" title="Jacob&#39;s sibling">Jacob's sibling</a>, <a href="/wiki/Tulip_Karasu" title="Tulip Karasu">Tulip Karasu</a>, Barnaby, and another of their friends at the entrance of the <a href="/wiki/Restricted_Section" title="Restricted Section">Restricted Section</a>, hoping to beat them to get into the next Cursed Vault first. When Jacob's sibling beat Merula over a fair duel, Ismelda fired <i><a href="/wiki/Everte_Statum" title="Everte Statum">Everte Statum</a></i> out of the blue, which would have hit Jacob's sibling had Barnaby not jumped out to take the hit. Ismelda showed no remorse about harming her former ally, and when Barnaby refused to back down, she stated he could suffer with his new friends. Both Ismelda and Merula were then held back by Barnaby while the others entered the Restricted Section to figure out how to gain entry to the next vault.<sup id="cite_ref-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Fourth_year">Fourth year</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Ismelda_Murk?veaction=edit&amp;section=5" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Fourth year">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Ismelda_Murk?action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Fourth year"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<p>In her <a href="/wiki/Fourth_year" title="Fourth year">fourth year</a>, Ismelda attended the <a href="/wiki/Celestial_Ball" title="Celestial Ball">Celestial Ball</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Fifth_year">Fifth year</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Ismelda_Murk?veaction=edit&amp;section=6" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Fifth year">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Ismelda_Murk?action=edit&amp;section=6" title="Edit section: Fifth year"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<p>Sometime during her <a href="/wiki/Fifth_year" title="Fifth year">fifth year</a>, Ismelda accidentally bumped into the popular <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor" title="Gryffindor">Gryffindor</a> <a href="/wiki/Emily_Tyler" title="Emily Tyler">Emily Tyler</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Courtyard" title="Courtyard">Courtyard</a>. When Ismelda didn't apologise, however, Emily thought it a 'fitting punishment' to snatch the former's diary and read it aloud. This soon escalated into a commotion, which drew a large crowd of onlookers. <a href="/wiki/Penny_Haywood" title="Penny Haywood">Penny Haywood</a> noticed the disturbance and requested for <a href="/wiki/Jacob%27s_sibling" title="Jacob&#39;s sibling">Jacob's sibling</a> to come to the Courtyard, after which the two students asked around the crowd of onlookers so as to know what was happening, with Jacob's sibling noting that although some of the onlookers were laughing, most of them looked uncomfortable. Jacob's sibling and Penny then watched as Emily cruelly taunted Ismelda by reading aloud her diary, publicly humiliating her and revealing from it Ismelda's love confession to <a href="/wiki/Barnaby_Lee" title="Barnaby Lee">Barnaby Lee</a>, which highly surprised Jacob's sibling. Emily then continued to relentlessly insult Ismelda upon this discovery, asserting that the latter was unlovable and repulsive, upon which Penny expressed that she couldn't watch this anymore.
</p><p>Once the commotion was over, Ismelda requested that Jacob's sibling meet her in the <a href="/wiki/Great_Hall" title="Great Hall">Great Hall</a>. Upon their arrival, Ismelda revealed that she was desperate and needed someone to confide in, to which Jacob's sibling inquired of her reasoning behind asking them, noting that that the two of them weren't exactly friends, prompting Ismelda to state that <a href="/wiki/Merula_Snyde" title="Merula Snyde">Merula Snyde</a>, one of her few friends, would only mock her if she told her about her problems while the <a href="/wiki/Bloody_Baron" title="Bloody Baron">Bloody Baron</a>, her <a href="/wiki/Slytherin" title="Slytherin">house</a> <a href="/wiki/Ghost" title="Ghost">ghost</a>, refused to give comment, leading Ismelda to the conclusion that she could only confide in to Jacob's sibling as she didn't have many friends. When Jacob's sibling inquired as to if this was related to the commotion earlier, Ismelda disclosed that she thought Emily might be right in calling Ismelda unlovable, declaring that Barnaby could only ever like her if he was forced to. This prompted Jacob's sibling to note that they had never seen Ismelda talk like this before, to which she arrogantly warned them not to get used to it, as she still detested them. Upon this, Jacob's sibling flushed angrily, inducing Ismelda to express how she didn't mean that and beseeched them to talk with her, to which they supposed they could at least hear her out.
</p><p>After successfully addressing Ismelda's concerns over confessing her feelings to Barnaby, Jacob's sibling contacted a friend who could help her in preparing to talk to Barnaby. Soon after, Ismelda decided to show her appreciation for Jacob's sibling's help by teaching them the <a href="/wiki/Curse_of_the_Bogies" title="Curse of the Bogies">Curse of the Bogies</a>, even allowing them to try it out on her. Jacob's sibling then accompanied Ismelda and watched her confess to Barnaby. Barnaby, however, completely misunderstood Ismelda's love confession, causing her to run away, upset. Concerned that she might try something drastic, as she insinuated that she would use a <a href="/wiki/Love_potion" title="Love potion">love potion</a> on Barnaby, Jacob's sibling and Penny soon found her consorting with <a href="/wiki/Jae_Kim" title="Jae Kim">Jae Kim</a>, who gave her a sandwich infused with love potion, which she could give to Barnaby. Jacob's sibling, upon intervening, could prompt Ismelda to give Barnaby the love potion-infused sandwich or dissuade her from it.
</p><p>Regardless, Ismelda will resolve to remain friends with Barnaby after openly talking with him, to which Jacob's sibling lauded her for her suprising maturity and level-headedness. Upon revealing her appreciation for their assistance towards her, Jacob's sibling asked Ismelda if this meant that she no longer despised them, to which she disclosed that she never actually did, but rather despised who they reminded her of. Upon further inquiry, Ismelda revealed that it was her sister, who had achieved great success as a student and so was showered with love and attention from their parents at Ismelda's expense. This parental neglect prompted Ismelda to begin acting out in the hopes of getting more attention, thus explaining somewhat her behaviour in general. While Ismelda acknowledged that she wasn't friends with Jacob's sibling, she proposed to threaten them with bodily harm less often, which they somewhat appreciated. Before they could leave her, however, Ismelda finally thanked them properly for all their help, but instantly threatened to cast the <a href="/wiki/Killing_Curse" title="Killing Curse">Killing Curse</a> on them, should they tell anyone about this. She then sped off, leaving them completely bewildered.<sup id="cite_ref-crushed_2-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-crushed-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<p>In her <a href="/wiki/Sixth_year" title="Sixth year">sixth year</a>, Ismelda started hanging out with <a href="/wiki/Beatrice_Haywood" title="Beatrice Haywood">Beatrice Haywood</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup> The two spent time tossing <a href="/wiki/Puffskein" title="Puffskein">Puffskeins</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-10" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-10">&#91;10&#93;</a></sup> They could also be found at <a href="/wiki/Flourish_and_Blotts" title="Flourish and Blotts">Flourish and Blotts</a> where Ismelda mentioned working on a hex that spoils novel endings.<sup id="cite_ref-11" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-11">&#91;11&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Sometime that same year, <a href="/wiki/Rowan_Khanna" title="Rowan Khanna">Rowan Khanna</a> was tragically murdered by the <a href="/wiki/Dark_Wizard" title="Dark Wizard">dark witch</a> <a href="/wiki/Patricia_Rakepick" title="Patricia Rakepick">Patricia Rakepick</a>, who was working for the organisation "<a href="/wiki/R" title="R">R</a>". This motivated <a href="/wiki/Jacob%27s_sibling" title="Jacob&#39;s sibling">Jacob's sibling</a>, <a href="/wiki/Ben_Copper" title="Ben Copper">Ben Copper</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Merula_Snyde" title="Merula Snyde">Merula Snyde</a>, to create a group devoted to avenging Rowan, which they called "<a href="/wiki/Circle_of_Khanna" title="Circle of Khanna">Circle of Khanna</a>". The groups motives were to Fight "R" and locate the final <a href="/wiki/Cursed_Vaults" title="Cursed Vaults">Cursed Vault</a> of <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Castle" title="Hogwarts Castle">Hogwarts</a>. She joined a team which consisted of Merula Snyde and <a href="/wiki/Beatrice_Haywood" title="Beatrice Haywood">Beatrice Haywood</a>, who found about the Circle of Khanna from the two and showed interest in joining. The three went to the <a href="/wiki/Classroom_3C" title="Classroom 3C">Defence Against the Dark Arts classroom</a>, where they studied <a href="/wiki/Curse" title="Curse">curses</a> and dark artefacts, as this knowledge could be useful for entering a Cursed Vault. Some time later, Jacob's sibling came to the classroom to see how the group was doing and was surprised to find Beatrice, as the group was meant to be secret. Not long after, Penny came to the classroom, as she heard from <a href="/wiki/Cedric_Diggory" title="Cedric Diggory">Cedric Diggory</a> that Beatrice had gone there.<sup id="cite_ref-hm620_12-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-hm620-12">&#91;12&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Personality_and_traits">Personality and traits</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Ismelda_Murk?veaction=edit&amp;section=8" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Personality and traits">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Ismelda_Murk?action=edit&amp;section=8" title="Edit section: Personality and traits"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<div class="quote">
<dl><dd><span>"<i>I would murder everyone here for a <a href="/wiki/Pumpkin_Fizz" class="mw-redirect" title="Pumpkin Fizz">Pumpkin Fizz</a>.</i>"</span></dd>
<dd>&#8212;Ismelda Murk<sup id="cite_ref-13" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-13">&#91;13&#93;</a></sup></dd>
<dd></dd></dl></div>
<p>Ismelda was generally a mean-spirited and even cruel young witch, saying that she would "cast the <a href="/wiki/Killing_curse" class="mw-redirect" title="Killing curse">killing curse</a> on the first <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor" title="Gryffindor">Gryffindor</a>" she would see,<sup id="cite_ref-y3ch6_14-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-y3ch6-14">&#91;14&#93;</a></sup> or coming up with the idea to torture <a href="/wiki/Jacob%27s_sibling" title="Jacob&#39;s sibling">Jacob's sibling</a> with the <a href="/wiki/Cruciatus_Curse" title="Cruciatus Curse">Cruciatus Curse</a> in order to obtain information about the <a href="/wiki/Cursed_Vaults" title="Cursed Vaults">Cursed Vaults</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-15" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-15">&#91;15&#93;</a></sup> She was overheard telling <a href="/wiki/Merula_Snyde" title="Merula Snyde">Merula Snyde</a> that she once had a jar of <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggle</a> <a href="/wiki/Blood" title="Blood">blood</a> that dried out,<sup id="cite_ref-y3ch6_14-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-y3ch6-14">&#91;14&#93;</a></sup> and gleefully voiced her hope that she would see Professor <a href="/wiki/Silvanus_Kettleburn" title="Silvanus Kettleburn">Silvanus Kettleburn</a> "lose his other eye this year."<sup id="cite_ref-16" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-16">&#91;16&#93;</a></sup> She also appeared to sympathise with the ideals of <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a>, as she sometimes voiced support for his extreme beliefs in the importance of <a href="/wiki/Pure-blood_supremacy" title="Pure-blood supremacy">pure-blood supremacy</a> and her apparent support for the usage of the <a href="/wiki/Dark_Arts" title="Dark Arts">Dark Arts</a>. It is unknown, however, how many of these sentiments are her actual opinions or feelings, or if she said them merely for shock value.
</p><p>That being said, Ismelda wasn't entirely unsympathetic; she had a soft spot for dogs (in contrast to her general predilection towards cruelty), and seemed to value her friendship with Merula Snyde. She also befriended and seemed protective of <a href="/wiki/Beatrice_Haywood" title="Beatrice Haywood">Beatrice Haywood</a>. She also had a romantic attraction to the third member of their trio, Barnaby Lee, and though she reacted poorly to his wishing to remain friends with her, could be persuaded to eventually accept his decision. Finally, although often antagonistic towards Jacob's sibling, Ismelda was nonetheless willing to be their date for the Celestial Ball if asked (and claimed she didn't "want to be unpleasant" towards them despite rarely being kind to them). She was also willing to help them humiliate Rita Skeeter, albeit more for the chance to work mischief than sympathy for their plight.
</p><p>Finally, Ismelda had a sense of inferiority as a result of living in the shadow of her older sister, who had achieved great success as a student and so was showered with love and attention from their parents at Ismelda's expense. This parental neglect prompted Ismelda to begin acting out in the hopes of getting more attention, thus explaining somewhat her behaviour in general. This was also reflected in her attitude towards Jacob's sibling and <a href="/wiki/Penny_Haywood" title="Penny Haywood">Penny Haywood</a>, saying that she hated the former because they reminded her of her sister, and contrasting the "popular and pretty" Penny with herself, who she was convinced would never be <a href="/wiki/Love" title="Love">loved</a> and thus resorted to a <a href="/wiki/Love_potion" title="Love potion">love potion</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-crushed_2-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-crushed-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Magical_abilities_and_skills">Magical abilities and skills</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Ismelda_Murk?veaction=edit&amp;section=9" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Ismelda_Murk?action=edit&amp;section=9" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li><b><a href="/wiki/History_of_Magic" title="History of Magic">History of Magic</a></b>: Before the <a href="/wiki/Ordinary_Wizarding_Level" title="Ordinary Wizarding Level">O.W.L.</a> for the subject, Ismelda had shown her expertise in the subject during a study session with <a href="/wiki/Jacob%27s_sibling" title="Jacob&#39;s sibling">Jacob's sibling</a> and <a href="/wiki/Badeea_Ali" title="Badeea Ali">Badeea Ali</a>. Despite falling asleep in lessons like many others, she fell asleep due to her already knowing the subject so well, which matched her love for bloodshed and gore.<sup id="cite_ref-17" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-17">&#91;17&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Potion" title="Potion">Potions</a></b>: While her skills were not quite as proficient as <a href="/wiki/Merula_Snyde" title="Merula Snyde">Merula Snyde</a> in the art of potion-making, Ismelda was capable of successfully producing <a href="/wiki/Weedosoros" title="Weedosoros">Weedosoros</a>, a potent <a href="/wiki/Poison" title="Poison">poison</a> which she learned how to concoct in <a href="/wiki/Potions_(class)" title="Potions (class)">Potions class</a>.</li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Care_of_Magical_Creatures" title="Care of Magical Creatures">Care of Magical Creatures</a></b>: Ismelda earned an O.W.L. in this subject, and studied it further at N.E.W.T. level, showing aptitude in the field of <a href="/wiki/Magizoology" title="Magizoology">Magizoology</a>. However initially, she merely took the class to find out the weaknesses of <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magical</a> <a href="/wiki/Creature" title="Creature">creatures</a>, so that she could either kill them, or capture them to kill others.</li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Duelling" title="Duelling">Duelling</a></b>: Whilst she was not as skilled a duellist as her friend <a href="/wiki/Merula_Snyde" title="Merula Snyde">Merula</a>, she was still fairly competent in <a href="/wiki/Martial_magic" title="Martial magic">martial magic</a>, being able to cast a variety of offensive <a href="/wiki/Spell" title="Spell">spells</a> which would cause significant damage to her opponent in duels. She was known to have been able cast a particularly strong <i><a href="/wiki/Everte_Statum" title="Everte Statum">Everte Statum</a></i>, an offensive spell very useful in duelling.</li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Dark_Arts" title="Dark Arts">Dark Arts</a></b>: Ismelda was rather skilled with dark magic, as she could successfully cast a number of <a href="/wiki/Dark_charm" title="Dark charm">dark charms</a>, such as the <a href="/wiki/Hex" title="Hex">hex</a> <i><a href="/wiki/Densaugeo" title="Densaugeo">Densaugeo</a></i>, <a href="/wiki/Sea_Urchin_Jinx" title="Sea Urchin Jinx">Sea Urchin Jinx</a>, <a href="/wiki/Stretching_Jinx" title="Stretching Jinx">Stretching Jinx</a>, <a href="/wiki/Ear-Shrivelling_Curse" title="Ear-Shrivelling Curse">Ear-Shrivelling Curse</a> and the <a href="/wiki/Curse_of_the_Bogies" title="Curse of the Bogies">Curse of the Bogies</a>, the latter of which she even taught to Jacob's sibling.</li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Divination" title="Divination">Divination</a></b>: Ismelda was proficient in Divination, as she achieved an <a href="/wiki/Ordinary_Wizarding_Level" title="Ordinary Wizarding Level">O.W.L.</a> in the subject and studied it further at <a href="/wiki/Nastily_Exhausting_Wizarding_Test" title="Nastily Exhausting Wizarding Test">N.E.W.T.</a>-level.</li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Defence_Against_the_Dark_Arts" title="Defence Against the Dark Arts">Defence Against the Dark Arts</a></b>: Ismelda was skilled in Defence Against the Dark Arts, as she was to achieve an O.W.L. in the subject and study it further at N.E.W.T.-level, thus showing aptitude in the subject.</li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Astronomy_(class)" title="Astronomy (class)">Astronomy</a></b>: Ismelda was skilled in Astronomy, as she was able to achieve an O.W.L. in the subject and study it further at N.E.W.T.-level, thus showing aptitude in the subject.</li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Healing_magic" title="Healing magic">Healing magic</a></b>; Ismelda had some skill in the field of healing, as she was able to successfully cast the <a href="/wiki/Healing_spell" title="Healing spell">healing spell</a> <i><a href="/wiki/Episkey" title="Episkey">Episkey</a></i>.</li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Charm" title="Charm">Charms</a></b>: Ismelda was rather skilled in charm-work, as she was capable of casting some duelling charms such as the <a href="/wiki/Disarming_Charm" title="Disarming Charm">Disarming Charm</a>, <a href="/wiki/Tickling_Charm" title="Tickling Charm">Tickling Charm</a>, <a href="/wiki/Freezing_Charm" title="Freezing Charm">Freezing Charm</a> and <i><a href="/wiki/Everte_Statum" title="Everte Statum">Everte Statum</a></i> successfully.</li></ul>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Ismelda_Murk?veaction=edit&amp;section=11" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Ismelda_Murk?action=edit&amp;section=11" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Ismelda is a name of German or Germanic<sup id="cite_ref-18" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-18">&#91;18&#93;</a></sup> origin, meaning "she who battles with sword."<sup id="cite_ref-19" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-19">&#91;19&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Murk is an Americanised or Germanic<sup id="cite_ref-20" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-20">&#91;20&#93;</a></sup> spelling of the following, with the majority of them meaning "dark:" Old English <i>mirce</i>, Proto-Germanic <i>*merkuz</i>, and Proto-Indo-European <i>*merg-</i>. It also cognate with various languages with the meaning of "dark," including Danish and Norwegian <i>mÃ¸rk</i>, Swedish <i>mÃ¶rk</i>, and Icelandic <i>myrkur</i>.<sup id="cite_ref-21" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-21">&#91;21&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
