<p><b>Hector Bolobolo</b> was a Fijian wizard and the manager and trainer of the <a href="/wiki/Fijian_National_Quidditch_team" title="Fijian National Quidditch team">Fijian National Quidditch team</a> in <a href="/wiki/2014" title="2014">2014</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-PM_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PM-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Hector_Bolobolo?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Hector_Bolobolo?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>On the second match of the <a href="/wiki/2014_Quidditch_World_Cup" title="2014 Quidditch World Cup">427th Quidditch World Cup</a>, Fiji against <a href="/wiki/Nigerian_National_Quidditch_team" title="Nigerian National Quidditch team">Nigeria</a>, his team suffered a blistering defeat due to <a href="/wiki/Seeker" title="Seeker">Seeker</a> <a href="/wiki/Joseph_Snuka" title="Joseph Snuka">Joseph Snuka</a>'s choice to catch the <a href="/wiki/Golden_Snitch" title="Golden Snitch">Snitch</a> when his <a href="/wiki/Fijian_National_Quidditch_team" title="Fijian National Quidditch team">team</a> was losing 400 to 10. Amid the bewilderment of the commentators, the fury of the Fijian supporters and the jeers of the Nigerians, Bolobolo's only comment was "<i>I am going to kill him</i>".<sup id="cite_ref-PM_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PM-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
