<b>Yann Fredericks</b> (born <a href="/wiki/2006" title="2006">2005</a> or <a href="/wiki/2007" title="2007">2006</a>) was a <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor" title="Gryffindor">Gryffindor</a> student in <a href="/wiki/Albus_Potter" title="Albus Potter">Albus Potter</a>'s year at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a>.

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Yann_Fredericks?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Yann_Fredericks?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_life">Early life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Yann_Fredericks?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Early life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Yann_Fredericks?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Early life"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Yann Fredericks was born somewhere in the <a href="/wiki/Great_Britain" title="Great Britain">United Kingdom</a> between <a href="/wiki/1_September" title="1 September">1 September</a> <a href="/wiki/2006" title="2006">2005</a> and <a href="/wiki/31_August" title="31 August">31 August</a> <a href="/wiki/2007" title="2007">2006</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-act1scene4_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-act1scene4-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="First_year">First year</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Yann_Fredericks?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: First year">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Yann_Fredericks?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: First year"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Yann began attending <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> on 1 September <a href="/wiki/2017" title="2017">2017</a> and was sorted into <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor" title="Gryffindor">Gryffindor</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-act1scene4_2-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-act1scene4-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-act2scene11_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-act2scene11-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> During the <a href="/wiki/Sorting_ceremony" title="Sorting ceremony">sorting ceremony</a>, he remarked that <a href="/wiki/Albus_Potter" title="Albus Potter">Albus Potter</a> had hair just like his father, <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-act1scene4_2-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-act1scene4-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> He sardonically retracted this comment when Albus was placed in <a href="/wiki/Slytherin" title="Slytherin">Slytherin</a> a short time later.<sup id="cite_ref-act1scene4_2-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-act1scene4-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Yann was able to raise his <a href="/wiki/Broomstick" title="Broomstick">broomstick</a> on the first attempt during the <a href="/wiki/First_year" title="First year">first years</a>' initial <a href="/wiki/Flying_(class)" title="Flying (class)">flying</a> lesson with <a href="/wiki/Rolanda_Hooch" title="Rolanda Hooch">Madam Hooch</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-act1scene4_2-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-act1scene4-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> The only other student in the class who accomplished this feat was <a href="/wiki/Rose_Granger-Weasley" title="Rose Granger-Weasley">Rose Granger-Weasley</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-act1scene4_2-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-act1scene4-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Fourth_year">Fourth year</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Yann_Fredericks?veaction=edit&amp;section=4" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Fourth year">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Yann_Fredericks?action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Fourth year"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Upon returning to <a href="/wiki/2020" title="2020">2020</a> after <a href="/wiki/Time-Turner" title="Time-Turner">altering the past</a>, Albus Potter and <a href="/wiki/Scorpius_Malfoy" title="Scorpius Malfoy">Scorpius Malfoy</a> found themselves in an <a href="/wiki/Alternate_reality" title="Alternate reality">alternate reality</a>, in which Albus was a Gryffindor and <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione Granger</a> taught <a href="/wiki/Defence_Against_the_Dark_Arts" title="Defence Against the Dark Arts">Defence Against the Dark Arts</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-act2scene11_3-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-act2scene11-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> During a lesson, Albus questioned her about the changed circumstances, prompting her to deduct points from Gryffindor.<sup id="cite_ref-act2scene11_3-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-act2scene11-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> Albus persisted, however, prompting Yann to threateningly insinuate that he would do something to Albus if he didn't stop.<sup id="cite_ref-act2scene11_3-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-act2scene11-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>After finding himself in another alternate reality, in which <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a> won and Albus was never born, Scorpius was greeted by Yann and <a href="/wiki/Karl_Jenkins" title="Karl Jenkins">Karl Jenkins</a> in the Hogwarts grounds.<sup id="cite_ref-act3scene2_4-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-act3scene2-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> Yann asked Scorpius if he was still on for an event the following night and Karl enthused they were both ready to "spill some proper <a href="/wiki/Muggle-born" title="Muggle-born">Mudblood</a> guts."<sup id="cite_ref-act3scene2_4-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-act3scene2-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
