<b>Belvina Burke</b> (née <b>Black</b>) (<a href="/wiki/1886" class="mw-redirect" title="1886">1886</a>–<a href="/wiki/1962" title="1962">1962</a>) was a <a href="/wiki/Pure-blood" title="Pure-blood">pure-blood</a> witch and member of the noble <a href="/wiki/House_of_Black" title="House of Black">Black family</a>.

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Belvina_Black?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Belvina_Black?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Belvina was born in <a href="/wiki/1886" class="mw-redirect" title="1886">1886</a>. She was the only daughter of <a href="/wiki/Phineas_Nigellus_Black" title="Phineas Nigellus Black">Phineas Nigellus Black</a> (Headmaster of <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a>) and <a href="/wiki/Ursula_Flint" title="Ursula Flint">Ursula Flint</a>. She was also the sister of <a href="/wiki/Sirius_Black_II" title="Sirius Black II">Sirius II</a>, <a href="/wiki/Cygnus_Black_II" title="Cygnus Black II">Cygnus II</a>, <a href="/wiki/Phineas_Black" title="Phineas Black">Phineas</a> and <a href="/wiki/Arcturus_Black_II" title="Arcturus Black II">Arcturus</a>. She married <a href="/wiki/Herbert_Burke" title="Herbert Burke">Herbert Burke</a>, and they had three children: <a href="/wiki/Belvina_Burke%27s_sons" title="Belvina Burke&#39;s sons">two sons</a> and <a href="/wiki/Belvina_Burke%27s_daughter" title="Belvina Burke&#39;s daughter">a daughter</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-BFT_1-15" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-BFT-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Belvina_Black?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Belvina_Black?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The name <i>Belvina</i> does not fit in with the <a href="/wiki/House_of_Black" title="House of Black">Black family</a> tradition of naming its members after stars or constellations. It may derived from Latin, in which it would mean something like "lovely wine" or "fair one". Alternatively, it could be derived from Beltane, an ancient Celtic holiday related to <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Walpurgis_Night" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Walpurgis Night">Walpurgis Night</a>, "Witches' Night".<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Belvina_Black?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Belvina_Black?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>Based on a statement by <a href="/wiki/Horace_Slughorn" title="Horace Slughorn">Horace Slughorn</a>, Belvina may have been a member of the <a href="/wiki/Slytherin" title="Slytherin">Slytherin House</a>. However, she attended Hogwarts c. 1897 - 1904, when Slughorn was not Potions teacher.</li>
<li>It is possible that she was related to <a href="/wiki/Elizabeth_Burke" title="Elizabeth Burke">Elizabeth Burke</a> by marriage.</li>
<li>It is also possible that <a href="/wiki/Araminta_Meliflua_Black" title="Araminta Meliflua Black">Araminta Meliflua</a> was her granddaughter, since she was described as a cousin of <a href="/wiki/Walburga_Black" title="Walburga Black">Walburga Black</a> (Belvina's great-niece) and then it can be assumed that they were in the same generation.</li></ul>
