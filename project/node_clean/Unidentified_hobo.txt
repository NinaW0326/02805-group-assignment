<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="History">History</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_hobo?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: History">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_hobo?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: History"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>He was one of the witnesses when some of <a href="/wiki/Newton_Scamander" title="Newton Scamander">Newt Scamander</a>'s creatures escaped from his <a href="/wiki/Newton_Scamander%27s_suitcase" title="Newton Scamander&#39;s suitcase">briefcase</a> that year.<sup id="cite_ref-FBS_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-FBS-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>When a <a href="/wiki/NYPD_Police_Officer_No._743" title="NYPD Police Officer No. 743">policeman</a> asked questions about the situation, the drunken hobo was about to tell about what he really saw while <a href="/wiki/Unidentified_housewife" title="Unidentified housewife">another witness</a> thought it was gas explosion, his memories were <a href="/wiki/Memory_Charm" title="Memory Charm">obliviated</a> by Newt leaving him and the crowd to agree that it was actually gas.<sup id="cite_ref-FBS_1-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-FBS-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
