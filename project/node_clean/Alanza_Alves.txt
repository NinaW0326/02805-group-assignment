<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Alanza_Alves?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Alanza_Alves?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_life">Early life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Alanza_Alves?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Early life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Alanza_Alves?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Early life"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Not much is known about Alanza's early life, but she was born into a prominent Brazilian <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizarding</a> family. She attended <a href="/wiki/Castelobruxo" title="Castelobruxo">Castelobruxo</a>, the <a href="/wiki/Brazil" title="Brazil">Brazilian</a> school of <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magic</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-HM622_3-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HM622-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> During her first year, she got lost in the jungle surrounding Castelobruxo castle and survived off nothing but <a href="/wiki/Water" title="Water">rain</a> and <a href="/wiki/Ant" title="Ant">ants</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-HM622_3-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HM622-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Visiting_Hogwarts">Visiting Hogwarts</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Alanza_Alves?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Visiting Hogwarts">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Alanza_Alves?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Visiting Hogwarts"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<p>At one point during the <a href="/wiki/1989%E2%80%931990_school_year" title="1989–1990 school year">1989–1990 school year</a>, Alanza visited <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> to be an exchange student for her <a href="/wiki/Sixth_year" title="Sixth year">sixth year</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-HM622_3-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HM622-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> In the <a href="/wiki/Headmaster%27s_Office" title="Headmaster&#39;s Office">Headmaster's Office</a>, <a href="/wiki/Professor" title="Professor">Professor</a> <a href="/wiki/Albus_Dumbledore" title="Albus Dumbledore">Albus Dumbledore</a> introduced her to <a href="/wiki/Jacob%27s_sibling" title="Jacob&#39;s sibling">Jacob's sibling</a> and requested that they be Alanza's tour guide, showing her around Hogwarts and introducing her to her classmates and professors in order to help her get settled and adapt to life at Hogwarts. Jacob's sibling accepted the task and suggested that Alanza visit the <a href="/wiki/Training_Grounds" title="Training Grounds">Training Grounds</a> first, as the former was to meet their friends <a href="/wiki/Talbott_Winger" title="Talbott Winger">Talbott Winger</a> and <a href="/wiki/Badeea_Ali" title="Badeea Ali">Badeea Ali</a> there.<sup id="cite_ref-HM622_3-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HM622-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Upon arrival, Jacob's sibling introduced Alanza to Talbott and Badeea, with Talbott somewhat taken aback by Alanza's energetic enthusiasm but Badeea appreciating it. Alanza then noticed Badeea's <a href="/wiki/Map_of_the_Great_Lake" title="Map of the Great Lake">Map of the Great Lake</a> and asked if it was made for her as part of her tour, prompting Jacob's sibling to ask her to give them a moment with Talbott and Badeea, during which the three agreed to keep the <a href="/wiki/Circle_of_Khanna" title="Circle of Khanna">Circle of Khanna</a> a secret from her and instead use the map to teach Alanza about the castle and its grounds. Afterwards, Alanza expressed how useful she found the map, to which Badeea inadvertently mentioned the Circle of Khanna aloud but quickly recognised her mistake by trying to pass it off as something else. Alanza, however, proceeded to inquire further and asked if she could join the group, prompting Jacob's sibling to suggest taking her to <a href="/wiki/Potions_(class)" title="Potions (class)">Potions class</a> and introduce her to Professor <a href="/wiki/Severus_Snape" title="Severus Snape">Severus Snape</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-HM622_3-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HM622-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Alanza then accompanied Jacob's sibling to the <a href="/wiki/Potions_Classroom" title="Potions Classroom">Potions Classroom</a>, where the pair found Professor Snape, <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Librarian" title="Hogwarts Librarian">Hogwarts Librarian</a> <a href="/wiki/Madam" title="Madam">Madam</a> <a href="/wiki/Irma_Pince" title="Irma Pince">Irma Pince</a> and <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Caretaker" title="Hogwarts Caretaker">Hogwarts Caretaker</a> <a href="/wiki/Argus_Filch" title="Argus Filch">Argus Filch</a> converged there and discussing the disappearance of various Potions-related items, such as ingredients and textbooks. Jacob's sibling then proceeded to make introductions between Alanza and the three members of faculty, whose grouchy demeanours became quickly apparent and were pointed out by Alanza, who wondered if the rest of the Hogwarts faculty behaved as unpleasantly as the aforementioned three. Filch then noted his suspicions of Alanza to his <a href="/wiki/Cat" title="Cat">cat</a> <a href="/wiki/Mrs_Norris" title="Mrs Norris">Mrs Norris</a>, although the feline, much to the surprise of everybody present, took an instant liking to Alanza and affectionately rubbed herself against the student's legs. Upon the arrival of other students, however, Snape proceeded to terminate this discourse and ordered Alanza and Jacob's sibling to take their seats for class was beginning.<sup id="cite_ref-HM622_3-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HM622-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Alanza then proceeded to unknowingly take the seat that belonged to the late <a href="/wiki/Rowan_Khanna" title="Rowan Khanna">Rowan Khanna</a>. This prompted the class to stare at her, confusing Alanza and leading her to ask Jacob's sibling if the seat was taken, to which they responded that it used to be occupied by their best friend Rowan but not anymore. Alanza then witnessed as <a href="/wiki/Merula_Snyde" title="Merula Snyde">Merula Snyde</a> angrily confronted Jacob's sibling and demanded that they talk to her privately, regarding something important, although Snape ordered her to take her sit as she could talk all she wanted after class.<sup id="cite_ref-HM622_3-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HM622-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Once all the students took their seats, Snape announced Alanza's arrival to the class and commenced the lesson, after which Alanza became occupied answering questions from her classmates. Using this opening, Merula proceeded to berate Jacob's sibling for their supposed lack of dedication to the Circle of Khanna, although they reassured her that that was not the case. Alanza then joined their conversation and inquired of the aforementioned group again, upon which Merula incredulously asked Jacob's sibling if they had told Alanza about the group. Jacob's sibling promised to reveal everything to Merula in due time, as <a href="/wiki/Rubeus_Hagrid" title="Rubeus Hagrid">Rubeus Hagrid</a> had invited them and Alanza to his garden.<sup id="cite_ref-HM622_3-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HM622-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Possessions">Possessions</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Alanza_Alves?veaction=edit&amp;section=5" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Possessions">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Alanza_Alves?action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Possessions"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li><b>Wand</b>: It can be presumed that Alanza owned a <a href="/wiki/Wand" title="Wand">wand</a> (as she attended a <a href="/wiki/Wizarding_school" title="Wizarding school">wizarding school</a>), which was of unknown length, <a href="/wiki/Wand_wood" title="Wand wood">wand wood</a> and <a href="/wiki/Wand_core" title="Wand core">wand core</a>. She most likely purchased her <a href="/wiki/Wand" title="Wand">wand</a> from a Brazilian <a href="/wiki/Wandmaker" title="Wandmaker">wandmaker</a> before starting her education at Castelobruxo.</li></ul>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Alanza_Alves?veaction=edit&amp;section=6" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Alanza_Alves?action=edit&amp;section=6" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>Alanza's last name has been changed as "Álvez" in the Spanish version of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter:_Hogwarts_Mystery" title="Harry Potter: Hogwarts Mystery">Harry Potter: Hogwarts Mystery</a></i>.</li>
<li>When Alanza thanks <a href="/wiki/Jacob%27s_sibling" title="Jacob&#39;s sibling">Jacob's sibling</a> after they accept being her guide to Hogwarts, she says "<i>obrigado</i>" ("thanks" in Portuguese). However, as an adjective (even though used interjectionally), it ought to suffer <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Inflexion" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Inflexion">inflection</a> for gender: "<i>obrigada</i>" would be the correct form.</li></ul>
