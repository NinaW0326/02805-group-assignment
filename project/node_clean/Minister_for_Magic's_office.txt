</p><p>The <b>Minister for Magic's office</b> was the section of the <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic_Headquarters" title="British Ministry of Magic Headquarters">British Ministry of Magic Headquarters</a> where the <a href="/wiki/Minister_for_Magic" title="Minister for Magic">Minister for Magic</a> conducted business. It was located on Level 1, <a href="/wiki/Minister_for_Magic_and_Support_Staff" title="Minister for Magic and Support Staff">Minister for Magic and Support Staff</a>.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="History">History</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Minister_for_Magic%27s_office?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: History">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Minister_for_Magic%27s_office?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: History"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<div class="quote">
<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix"><b>Harry Potter</b>: "<i>Why wasn't <a href="/wiki/Lucius_Malfoy" title="Lucius Malfoy">he</a> waiting outside Fudge's office if they've got business to do together? What was he doing down here?</i>"</span></dd>
<dd><b>Arthur Weasley</b>: "<i>Trying to sneak down to the <a href="/wiki/Courtroom_Ten" title="Courtroom Ten">courtroom</a> if you ask me. Trying to find out whether you'd been expelled or not.</i>"</dd>
<dd>&#8212; Harry Potter and Arthur Weasley<sup class="noprint"><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Order_of_the_Phoenix" title="Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix">[src]</a></sup></dd></dl>
</div><p>On <a href="/wiki/12_August" title="12 August">12 August</a>, <a href="/wiki/1995" title="1995">1995</a>, <a href="/wiki/Lucius_Malfoy" title="Lucius Malfoy">Lucius Malfoy</a> had a meeting with Minister <a href="/wiki/Cornelius_Fudge" title="Cornelius Fudge">Cornelius Fudge</a> in his office. He waited for Fudge just outside the <a href="/wiki/Department_of_Mysteries" title="Department of Mysteries">Department of Mysteries</a>, as Fudge was, at the time, in the nearby <a href="/wiki/Courtroom_Ten" title="Courtroom Ten">Courtroom Ten</a> presiding over the <a href="/wiki/Disciplinary_hearing_of_Harry_Potter" title="Disciplinary hearing of Harry Potter">disciplinary hearing of Harry Potter</a>. <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> privately wondered why Malfoy did not simply wait outside the Minister's office for him. Though <a href="/wiki/Arthur_Weasley" title="Arthur Weasley">Arthur Weasley</a> told Harry he figured Malfoy was trying to sneak into the trial, it is most likely he believed Malfoy was scouting the Department of Mysteries for <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Voldemort</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP9_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP9-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>In <a href="/wiki/January" title="January">January</a> of <a href="/wiki/1996" title="1996">1996</a>, Fudge gave an interview to the <i><a href="/wiki/Daily_Prophet" title="Daily Prophet">Daily Prophet</a></i> in his office regarding the <a href="/wiki/Azkaban_escapes#1996" title="Azkaban escapes">recent mass breakout from Azkaban</a>. In this interview, he discussed his belief that the breakout was masterminded by <a href="/wiki/Sirius_Black" title="Sirius Black">Sirius Black</a>. Said interview was later published as part of the article <i><a href="/wiki/MASS_BREAKOUT_FROM_AZKABAN" title="MASS BREAKOUT FROM AZKABAN">MASS BREAKOUT FROM AZKABAN</a></i>.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP25_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP25-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>In <a href="/wiki/2022" class="mw-redirect" title="2022">2022</a>, Hermione Granger had <a href="/wiki/Charm" title="Charm">bewitched</a> her collective library in the Minister for Magic office to suck an individual into it when picking up a book. The individual had to defuse the riddle to allow the library to open the book which hid the illegal Time-Turner.<sup id="cite_ref-CC_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-CC-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
