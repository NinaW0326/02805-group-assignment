<b>S. Fawcett</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch</a> and <a href="/wiki/Ravenclaw" title="Ravenclaw">Ravenclaw</a><sup id="cite_ref-gof16_2-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-gof16-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> student at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> during the <a href="/wiki/1990s" title="1990s">1990s</a>. She lived with her <a href="/wiki/Fawcett_family" title="Fawcett family">family</a> near <a href="/wiki/Ottery_St_Catchpole" title="Ottery St Catchpole">Ottery St Catchpole</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-gof6_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-gof6-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Fawcett?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Fawcett?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span id="1992–1993_school_year"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="1992.E2.80.931993_school_year"><a href="/wiki/1992%E2%80%931993_school_year" title="1992–1993 school year">1992–1993 school year</a></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Fawcett?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: 1992–1993 school year">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Fawcett?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: 1992–1993 school year"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>In <a href="/wiki/1992" title="1992">1992</a>, she attended the <a href="/wiki/Duelling_Club" title="Duelling Club">Duelling Club</a> set up by <a href="/wiki/Gilderoy_Lockhart" title="Gilderoy Lockhart">Gilderoy Lockhart</a> and received a minor injury. She was probably paired up with another student and started <a href="/wiki/Duelling" title="Duelling">duelling</a> like everyone else.<sup id="cite_ref-COS11_4-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COS11-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span id="1994–1995_school_year"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="1994.E2.80.931995_school_year"><a href="/wiki/1994%E2%80%931995_school_year" title="1994–1995 school year">1994–1995 school year</a></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Fawcett?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: 1994–1995 school year">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Fawcett?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: 1994–1995 school year"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>In <a href="/wiki/1994" title="1994">1994</a>, she did not attend the <a href="/wiki/Quidditch_World_Cup" title="Quidditch World Cup">Quidditch World Cup</a>, because her family couldn't get tickets.<sup id="cite_ref-gof6_3-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-gof6-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> On <a href="/wiki/Hallowe%27en" title="Hallowe&#39;en">Hallowe'en</a>, she attempted to place her name in the <a href="/wiki/Goblet_of_Fire" title="Goblet of Fire">Goblet of Fire</a> to participate in the <a href="/wiki/Triwizard_Tournament" title="Triwizard Tournament">Triwizard Tournament</a> by using an <a href="/wiki/Ageing_Potion" title="Ageing Potion">Ageing Potion</a>. Because of <a href="/wiki/Albus_Dumbledore" title="Albus Dumbledore">Albus Dumbledore</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Age_Line" title="Age Line">Age Line</a>, however, she grew a long white beard instead.<sup id="cite_ref-gof16_2-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-gof16-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> Later, on Christmas Eve, Fawcett attended the <a href="/wiki/Yule_Ball" title="Yule Ball">Yule Ball</a> with <a href="/wiki/Stebbins_(Potter-era)" title="Stebbins (Potter-era)">Stebbins</a>, and both were docked ten <a href="/wiki/House_points" title="House points">points</a> by <a href="/wiki/Severus_Snape" title="Severus Snape">Professor Snape</a> when he caught them together in the rose garden.<sup id="cite_ref-gof23_5-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-gof23-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> This suggests that she was a <a href="/wiki/Fourth_year" title="Fourth year">fourth year</a> or above, as she was able to attend the Yule Ball, unless she was invited by Stebbins (just like <a href="/wiki/Ginevra_Weasley" title="Ginevra Weasley">Ginny Weasley</a> was invited by <a href="/wiki/Neville_Longbottom" title="Neville Longbottom">Neville Longbottom</a> as she didn't have the proper age to attend the Yule Ball at that time); but given that Fawcett and Stebbins were caught together in the rose garden, they might have met each other for a time; they might have been the same age and started attending Hogwarts the same year.
</p><p>Sometime during her time at the school, she borrowed the <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Library" title="Hogwarts Library">library</a> copy of <i><a href="/wiki/Quidditch_Through_the_Ages" title="Quidditch Through the Ages">Quidditch Through the Ages</a></i>, and was due to return it on <a href="/wiki/16_September" title="16 September">16 September</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-QTAR_6-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-QTAR-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Fawcett?veaction=edit&amp;section=4" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Fawcett?action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>Fawcett was portrayed by <a href="/wiki/Alex_Argenti" title="Alex Argenti">Alex Argenti</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Goblet_of_Fire_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire (film)">film adaptation</a> of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Goblet_of_Fire" title="Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire">Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire</a></i>, but her scenes were cut from the theatrical version, but appeared in a deleted scene on the DVD of the film. In the scene, she and <a href="/wiki/Stebbins_(Potter-era)" title="Stebbins (Potter-era)">Stebbins</a> are not caught by <a href="/wiki/Severus_Snape" title="Severus Snape">Professor Snape</a> in the rose bushes, but in a <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Carriages" title="Hogwarts Carriages">Hogwarts carriage</a> parked in the <a href="/wiki/Viaduct_Courtyard" title="Viaduct Courtyard">Entrance Courtyard</a>.
<ul><li>However, in some television showings, this scene is in the actual film.</li></ul></li>
<li>In the <a href="/wiki/Great_Britain" title="Great Britain">British</a>, <a href="/wiki/Canada" title="Canada">Canadian</a>, <a href="/wiki/Ukraine" title="Ukraine">Ukrainian</a>, Russian, Polish, and Danish editions of <i>Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire</i>, when Snape catches Fawcett with Stebbins in chapter 23, he says, "Ten points from <a href="/wiki/Hufflepuff" title="Hufflepuff">Hufflepuff</a>, Fawcett! And ten points from <a href="/wiki/Ravenclaw" title="Ravenclaw">Ravenclaw</a>, too, Stebbins!", although Fawcett is referred to as a Ravenclaw by <a href="/wiki/Albus_Dumbledore" title="Albus Dumbledore">Albus Dumbledore</a> in chapter 16.<sup id="cite_ref-lexicon_7-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-lexicon-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup> The American edition of the novel amends this error, with Snape taking Fawcett's points from Ravenclaw, and Stebbins' from Hufflepuff.<sup id="cite_ref-lexicon_7-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-lexicon-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup> In the <i>Goblet of Fire</i> film, however, Snape makes a similar error where he refers to Fawcett as a Hufflepuff and Stebbins as a Ravenclaw. The only way it couldn't be a mistake is that Snape mentions the consequences of the partner's rule-breakings.</li></ul>
