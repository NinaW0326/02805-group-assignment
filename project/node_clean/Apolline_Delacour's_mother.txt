<p>The <b>mother</b> of <a href="/wiki/Apolline_Delacour" title="Apolline Delacour">Apolline Delacour</a> was a <a href="/wiki/Veela" title="Veela">Veela</a>. Her daughter, a <a href="/wiki/Half-Veela" title="Half-Veela">half-Veela</a>, married <a href="/wiki/Delacour_sisters%27_father" title="Delacour sisters&#39; father">Monsieur Delacour</a>, a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a>, and had two daughters; <a href="/wiki/Fleur_Delacour" title="Fleur Delacour">Fleur</a> and <a href="/wiki/Gabrielle_Delacour" title="Gabrielle Delacour">Gabrielle</a>. When it came time for Fleur to have her <a href="/wiki/Fleur_Delacour%27s_wand" title="Fleur Delacour&#39;s wand">first wand</a>, she donated one of her <a href="/wiki/Veela_hair" title="Veela hair">hairs</a> to serve as its <a href="/wiki/Wand_core" title="Wand core">core</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-Wand_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Wand-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
