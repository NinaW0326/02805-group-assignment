<p><b>Theresa Stadt</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Great_Britain" title="Great Britain">British</a> <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch</a> who, somehow, managed to raise <a href="/wiki/Mr_Whiskers" title="Mr Whiskers">a pet Manticore</a> from infancy. When the <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">Ministry of Magic</a> was notified of this, they sent a <a href="/wiki/Magizoologist" title="Magizoologist">Magizoologist</a> member of the <a href="/wiki/Statute_of_Secrecy_Task_Force" title="Statute of Secrecy Task Force">Statute of Secrecy Task Force</a> to retrieve the <a href="/wiki/Manticore" title="Manticore">Manticore</a>, which proved a "remarkably ghastly affair".<sup id="cite_ref-WU_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-WU-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
