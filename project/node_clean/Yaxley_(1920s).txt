This <b>individual</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Great_Britain" title="Great Britain">British</a> <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> born into the prominent <a href="/wiki/Yaxley_family" title="Yaxley family">Yaxley family</a> and a well-known proponent of <a href="/wiki/Pure-blood_supremacy" title="Pure-blood supremacy">pure-blood values</a>. At some point after the completion of his magical education, he joined the <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">Ministry of Magic</a> in <a href="/wiki/London" title="London">London</a>, landing a job in the <a href="/wiki/Department_of_Magical_Law_Enforcement" title="Department of Magical Law Enforcement">Department of Magical Law Enforcement</a>, where he met and enjoyed a cordial relationship with the well-respected war hero and eventual Head of the <a href="/wiki/Auror_Office" title="Auror Office">Auror Office</a>, <a href="/wiki/Theseus_Scamander" title="Theseus Scamander">Theseus Scamander</a>. He seemed to have been an either effective or otherwise influential official, as he was promoted in <a href="/wiki/1927" title="1927">1927</a> and was due to become part of then <a href="/wiki/Hector_Fawley" title="Hector Fawley">Hector Fawley</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Minister_for_Magic_and_Support_Staff" title="Minister for Magic and Support Staff">Support Staff</a>. &#160;
</p><p>Shortly thereafter, he attended a wizarding ball and was busily socialising with fellow Ministry officials when he was greeted by the aforementioned Dark Wizard-catcher, who congratulated him on "moving upstairs." <a href="/wiki/Leta_Lestrange" title="Leta Lestrange">Leta Lestrange</a>&#160;was shown to dislike his behaviour of bee-lining towards&#160;her due to her <a href="/wiki/Blood_status" title="Blood status">blood status</a>&#160;as a&#160;<a href="/wiki/Pure-blood" title="Pure-blood">pure-blood</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-COG_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COG-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Around a decade later, he and his kinsmen became proud members of the <a href="/wiki/Sacred_Twenty-Eight" title="Sacred Twenty-Eight">Sacred Twenty-Eight</a> in the <i><a href="/wiki/Pure-Blood_Directory" title="Pure-Blood Directory">Pure-Blood Directory</a></i>.&#160;
</p>
