<p>This<b> <a href="/wiki/Puffskein" title="Puffskein">Puffskein</a></b> belonged to the <a href="/wiki/Weasley_family" title="Weasley family">Weasley family</a><sup id="cite_ref-FB.7CR.7CAn_A-Z_of_Fantastic_Beasts.7CPuffskein_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-FB.7CR.7CAn_A-Z_of_Fantastic_Beasts.7CPuffskein-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> in the early <a href="/wiki/1990s" title="1990s">1990s</a>. He was kept in the family's <a href="/wiki/Weasleys%27_Puffskein_patch" title="Weasleys&#39; Puffskein patch">Puffskein patch</a>, where his favourite delicacies included wild bushes.<sup id="cite_ref-CSps1_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-CSps1-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> Sometime before <a href="/wiki/1992" title="1992">1992</a>, <a href="/wiki/Fred_Weasley" title="Fred Weasley">Fred Weasley</a>, along with his twin brother <a href="/wiki/George_Weasley" title="George Weasley">George</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-CSps1_2-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-CSps1-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> used the family's Puffskein as a <a href="/wiki/Bludger" title="Bludger">Bludger</a> during <a href="/wiki/Quidditch" title="Quidditch">Quidditch</a> practice.<sup id="cite_ref-FB.7CR.7CAn_A-Z_of_Fantastic_Beasts.7CPuffskein_1-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-FB.7CR.7CAn_A-Z_of_Fantastic_Beasts.7CPuffskein-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/Ginevra_Weasley" title="Ginevra Weasley">Ginevra Weasley</a> would in her youth look after the creature in its <a href="/wiki/Weasleys%27_Puffskein_patch" title="Weasleys&#39; Puffskein patch">patch</a> to make sure the twins did not do it again.<sup id="cite_ref-CSps1_2-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-CSps1-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
In <a href="/wiki/August" title="August">August</a>, <a href="/wiki/1992" title="1992">1992</a>, the Puffskein swallowed Ginny's <a href="/wiki/Remembrall" title="Remembrall">Remembrall</a>, and escaped to a <a href="/wiki/Ottery_St_Catchpole_forest" title="Ottery St Catchpole forest">small forest area</a>. Ginny followed it and got lost, only to be found along with the Puffskein by <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-CSps1_2-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-CSps1-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
