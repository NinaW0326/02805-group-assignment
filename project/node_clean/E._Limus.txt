<b>E. Limus</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch or wizard</a> who worked as a reporter and columnist for the <i><a href="/wiki/Daily_Prophet" title="Daily Prophet">Daily Prophet</a></i>. In <a href="/wiki/1993" title="1993">1993</a>, Limus wrote an article on how <a href="/wiki/Sirius_Black" title="Sirius Black">Sirius Black</a>, then believed to be a fugitive mass-murderer, had been sighted near <a href="/wiki/Dufftown" title="Dufftown">Dufftown</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-PA.2Ff_11_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PA.2Ff_11-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> By <a href="/wiki/1995" title="1995">1995</a>, Limus had a column on page five of the paper, presumably dedicated to advice or opinion.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> In mid-<a href="/wiki/1997" title="1997">1997</a>, his column moved to page six.<sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Limus also wrote the book <i><a href="/wiki/Olde_and_Forgotten_Bewitchments_and_Charmes" title="Olde and Forgotten Bewitchments and Charmes">Olde and Forgotten Bewitchments and Charmes</a></i>.<sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
