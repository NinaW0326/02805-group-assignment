<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Glanmore_Peakes?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Glanmore_Peakes?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Peakes was born in 1677, in Scotland, <a href="/wiki/Great_Britain" title="Great Britain">Great Britain</a>. It is likely<sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> that he attended <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> from <a href="/wiki/1688" class="mw-redirect" title="1688">1688</a> to <a href="/wiki/1695" class="mw-redirect" title="1695">1695</a>.
</p><p>Sometime after graduating, Peakes killed the <a href="/wiki/Sea_Serpent_of_Cromer" title="Sea Serpent of Cromer">Sea Serpent of Cromer</a>, a <a href="/wiki/Sea_serpent" title="Sea serpent">sea serpent</a> that lived off the shores of <a href="/wiki/Cromer" title="Cromer">Cromer</a>, <a href="/wiki/Norfolk" title="Norfolk">Norfolk</a>. This led to his having a <a href="/wiki/Chocolate_Frog_Card" title="Chocolate Frog Card">Famous Wizard Card</a> fashioned depicting him.
</p><p>There was a <a href="/wiki/Glanmore_Peakes%27_Corridor" title="Glanmore Peakes&#39; Corridor">corridor</a> named after him on the sixth floor of <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Castle" title="Hogwarts Castle">Hogwarts Castle</a>, in which a portrait of him hangs. There was also a bust of Glanmore in the <a href="/wiki/Sixth-floor_corridor" title="Sixth-floor corridor">sixth-floor corridor</a>. There was also a set of portraits of Peakes hiding a secret passage between the sixth-floor landing of the <a href="/wiki/Grand_Staircase" title="Grand Staircase">Grand Staircase</a> and the <a href="/wiki/Entrance_Hall" title="Entrance Hall">Entrance Hall</a> side room. The password to said passage was "<i>Sea Serpent</i>".<sup id="cite_ref-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Glanmore_Peakes?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Glanmore_Peakes?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>In the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Prisoner_of_Azkaban_(video_game)" title="Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban (video game)">Console version</a> of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Prisoner_of_Azkaban" title="Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban">Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban</a></i>, Glanmore Peakes's portrait speaks in a pronounced Scottish accent.</li>
<li>Glanmore may be related to <a href="/wiki/Jimmy_Peakes" title="Jimmy Peakes">Jimmy Peakes</a>, a student who attended <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts</a> from <a href="/wiki/1994" title="1994">1994</a> to <a href="/wiki/2001" title="2001">2001</a>.</li>
<li>It's unknown why Glanmore was featured on a famous wizard card for killing a Sea Serpent, as Sea Serpents are harmless creatures and are not dangerous to humans, according to <a href="/wiki/Newton_Scamander" title="Newton Scamander">Newt Scamander</a>. It is possible, however, that wizards were unaware of the fact at the time, or that the sea-monster was unwilling to move on elsewhere and had to be dispatched to prevent breaches of wizarding secrecy.</li></ul>
