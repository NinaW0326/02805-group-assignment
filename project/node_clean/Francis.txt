<p><b>Francis</b> was the name of a pet fish owned by <a href="/wiki/Horace_Slughorn" title="Horace Slughorn">Horace Slughorn</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-HBPF_2-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBPF-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Francis?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Francis?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<figure class="thumb tleft show-info-icon" style="width: 122px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/e/e0/Horace_Slughorn.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20121226172917" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/e/e0/Horace_Slughorn.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/120?cb=20121226172917" decoding="async" width="120" height="188" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="Horace Slughorn.jpg" data-image-key="Horace_Slughorn.jpg" /></a> 	 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:Horace_Slughorn.jpg" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption">Horace Slughorn, Francis' owner</p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure>
<p>It was originally a <a href="/wiki/Lily" title="Lily">lily</a> petal which <a href="/wiki/Lily_J._Potter" title="Lily J. Potter">Lily Evans</a> had <a href="/wiki/Piscifors" title="Piscifors">transfigured</a>&#160;into piscine form during her studying years at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-HBPF_2-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBPF-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>The fish came into Slughorn's possession when Lily set the petal to float inside a bowl filled with <a href="/wiki/Water" title="Water">water</a>. When Slughorn found his gift, the petal sank down in the bowl and <a href="/wiki/Transformation" title="Transformation">turned</a> into Francis, much to the <a href="/wiki/Professor" title="Professor">Professor</a>'s delight. Slughorn kept Francis for many years, until the morning of <a href="/wiki/1_November" title="1 November">1 November</a>, <a href="/wiki/1981" title="1981">1981</a>, when he found Francis had disappeared. This was due to Lily's <a href="/wiki/Attack_at_Godric%27s_Hollow_(1981)" title="Attack at Godric&#39;s Hollow (1981)">death</a> at the hands of <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-HBPF_2-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBPF-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
