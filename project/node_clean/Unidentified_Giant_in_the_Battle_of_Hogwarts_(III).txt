</p><p>This <b>individual</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Giant" title="Giant">giant</a> and fought in the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a> fighting for <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a>. He fought during the <a href="/wiki/Onslaught_at_the_Viaduct" title="Onslaught at the Viaduct">Onslaught at the Viaduct</a> and was killed during that battle, halfway across the <a href="/wiki/Viaduct" title="Viaduct">Viaduct</a>. His dead body is later moved away into the <a href="/wiki/Ravine" title="Ravine">Ravine</a> below, when Voldemort walks towards <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts</a> to announce <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>'s death.
</p>
