The <b>grandmother</b> of <a href="/wiki/Jacob_Kowalski" title="Jacob Kowalski">Jacob Kowalski</a> lived in the early <a href="/wiki/20th_century" title="20th century">20th century</a>. She was an experienced baker, as her grandson mentioned her recipe for <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Paczki" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Paczki">paczki</a> when he applied for a loan to open <a href="/wiki/Kowalski_Quality_Baked_Goods" title="Kowalski Quality Baked Goods">his own bakery</a><sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>. She was also responsible to instilling a love of baking into him.
</p><p>A picture of her hung above Jacob Kowalski's bed at his home in <a href="/wiki/New_York" title="New York">New York</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
