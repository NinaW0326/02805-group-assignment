<b>Kevin Entwhistle</b> (c. <a href="/wiki/1979" title="1979">1979</a>-<a href="/wiki/1980" title="1980">1980</a>) was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> who began attending <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> in <a href="/wiki/1991" title="1991">1991</a>. He was in the same year as <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> and was Sorted into either <a href="/wiki/Hufflepuff" title="Hufflepuff">Hufflepuff</a> or <a href="/wiki/Ravenclaw" title="Ravenclaw">Ravenclaw</a> house.<sup id="cite_ref-PM_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PM-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Kevin_Entwhistle?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Kevin_Entwhistle?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>Anglicised form of the Irish name Caoimhín, derived from the older Irish Cóemgein, composed of the Old Irish elements cóem "kind, gentle, handsome" and gein "birth". Saint Caoimhín established a monastery in Glendalough, Ireland in the 6th century and is the patron saint of Dublin.</li>
<li><i>Entwhistle</i> could be a form of <i>Entwistle</i> which is an English habitual name from the village of Entwisle in Lancashire, named from Old English henna ‘(water) hen’ or ened ‘duck’ + twisla ‘tongue of land in a river fork’<a target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener" class="external autonumber" href="https://www.ancestry.com/name-origin?surname=entwistle">[1]</a>.</li></ul>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Kevin_Entwhistle?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Kevin_Entwhistle?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>Kevin Entwhistle is listed as a <a href="/wiki/Ravenclaw" title="Ravenclaw">Ravenclaw</a> on the list of students in a page of <a href="/wiki/J._K._Rowling" title="J. K. Rowling">J. K. Rowling</a>'s notes shown on <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_Me" title="Harry Potter and Me">Harry Potter and Me</a></i>. However, as two listed Hufflepuffs (<a href="/wiki/Anthony_Goldstein" title="Anthony Goldstein">Anthony Goldstein</a> and <a href="/wiki/Michael_Corner" title="Michael Corner">Michael Corner</a>) had their houses changed to Ravenclaw in the novels as published, two of those listed as Ravenclaws must have been changed to Hufflepuff. Entwhistle may be one of those whose house was changed.</li></ul>
