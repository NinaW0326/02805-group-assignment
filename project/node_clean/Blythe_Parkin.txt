<b>Blythe Parkin</b> was an <a href="/wiki/England" title="England">English</a> <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch</a> who played as <a href="/wiki/Seeker" title="Seeker">Seeker</a> on the <a href="/wiki/English_National_Quidditch_team" title="English National Quidditch team">English National Quidditch team</a> in <a href="/wiki/1994" title="1994">1994</a>. Whilst playing <a href="/wiki/Quidditch" title="Quidditch">Quidditch</a>, she <a href="/wiki/Flight" title="Flight">flew</a> on a Silver White <a href="/wiki/Nimbus_2000" title="Nimbus 2000">Nimbus 2000</a> <a href="/wiki/Broomstick" title="Broomstick">broomstick</a>. She may be a relative to <a href="/wiki/Walter_Parkin" title="Walter Parkin">Walter Parkin</a> and his <a href="/wiki/Walter_Parkin%27s_children" title="Walter Parkin&#39;s children">children</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-QWC_1-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-QWC-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>By <a href="/wiki/1996" title="1996">1996</a>, she was included on a <a href="/wiki/Chocolate_Frog_Card" title="Chocolate Frog Card">Chocolate Frog Card</a> for her achievements in the sport.<sup id="cite_ref-HBPG_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBPG-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
