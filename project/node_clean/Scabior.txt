</p>

<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_life">Early life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Scabior?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Early life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Scabior?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Early life"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<div class="quote">
<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows">"<i>Funny 'ow they all thinks we wants to 'ear that </i>[they were in Slytherin]<i>. But none of 'em can tell us where the common room is.</i>"</span></dd>
<dd>&#8212;Scabior commenting on the people he's snatched <sup class="noprint"><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Deathly_Hallows" title="Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows">[src]</a></sup></dd>
<dd></dd></dl></div>
<p>Little is known of Scabior's early life. However, given his intimate knowledge of the interior of the <a href="/wiki/Slytherin" title="Slytherin">Slytherin</a> <a href="/wiki/Slytherin_Common_Room" title="Slytherin Common Room">common room</a> and its location, he would have attended <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> in his youth and was a member of that house, as only members of that house would possess such detailed knowledge of the common room.<sup id="cite_ref-DH23_4-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH23-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Imprisonment_in_Azkaban">Imprisonment in Azkaban</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Scabior?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Imprisonment in Azkaban">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Scabior?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Imprisonment in Azkaban"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>At some point in his life Scabior was imprisoned in <a href="/wiki/Azkaban" title="Azkaban">Azkaban</a> for undisclosed crimes, but was released sometime before or after <a href="/wiki/Fall_of_the_Ministry_of_Magic" title="Fall of the Ministry of Magic">Lord Voldemort's infiltration</a> of the <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">Ministry of Magic</a> on <a href="/wiki/1_August" title="1 August">August</a> <a href="/wiki/Infiltration_of_the_Ministry_of_Magic" title="Infiltration of the Ministry of Magic">1,</a> <a href="/wiki/1997" title="1997">1997</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-DHvillainsfeature_5-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DHvillainsfeature-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Second_Wizarding_War">Second Wizarding War</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Scabior?veaction=edit&amp;section=4" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Second Wizarding War">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Scabior?action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Second Wizarding War"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<div class="quote">
<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows"><b>Fenrir Greyback</b>: "<i>We've caught Harry Potter!</i>"</span></dd>
<dd><b>Scabior</b>: "<i>I know 'es swollen, ma'am, but it's 'im! If you look a bit closer, you'll see 'is scar. And this 'ere, see the girl? The Mudblood who's been travelling around with 'im, ma'am. There's no doubt it's 'im, and we've got 'is wand as well! 'Ere, ma'am —</i>"</dd>
<dd><b>Narcissa Malfoy</b>: "<i>Bring them in.</i>"</dd>
<dd>&#8212; Scabior's gang of Snatchers arrives at Malfoy Manor with a captured trio<sup class="noprint"><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Deathly_Hallows" title="Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows">[src]</a></sup></dd></dl>
</div><figure class="thumb tleft show-info-icon" style="width: 242px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/c/c9/SnatchersMinistry.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20100922192726" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/c/c9/SnatchersMinistry.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/240?cb=20100922192726" decoding="async" width="240" height="143" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="SnatchersMinistry.jpg" data-image-key="SnatchersMinistry.jpg" /></a> 	 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:SnatchersMinistry.jpg" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption">Scabior at the <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">Ministry of Magic</a>, during the course of his Snatcher activities</p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure>
<p>Following his release from <a href="/wiki/Azkaban" title="Azkaban">Azkaban</a>, Scabior became a <a href="/wiki/Snatchers" title="Snatchers">Snatcher</a> during the height of the <a href="/wiki/Second_Wizarding_War" title="Second Wizarding War">Second Wizarding War</a>, catching <a href="/wiki/Muggle-born" title="Muggle-born">Muggle-born</a> <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizards</a> and anyone who opposed Voldemort, in return for gold. Several of his victims lied that they were in <a href="/wiki/Slytherin" title="Slytherin">Slytherin House</a>, but when Scabior asked them where their <a href="/wiki/Slytherin_Common_Room" title="Slytherin Common Room">common room</a> was, none of them could answer. He also knew <a href="/wiki/Stanley_Shunpike" title="Stanley Shunpike">Stanley Shunpike</a> and would later recall that the bus driver had given him grief.<sup id="cite_ref-DH23_4-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH23-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>He was among the group that captured <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>, Hermione Granger and <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ron Weasley</a> in the spring of <a href="/wiki/1998" title="1998">1998</a>. On Greyback's orders, Scabior checked the list of truants, searched the prisoners' tent for Harry's glasses, read a cutting from the <i><a href="/wiki/Daily_Prophet" title="Daily Prophet">Daily Prophet</a></i> and expressed terrified awe at the thought of summoning <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Voldemort</a>. He was also the only member of the group not to jeer when Greyback expressed a desire to bite Hermione, instead encouraging his leader to restrain himself.<sup id="cite_ref-DH23_4-10" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH23-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<figure class="thumb tright show-info-icon" style="width: 237px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/6/61/Bellatrix_attacking_Snatchers_GIF.gif/revision/latest?cb=20140622055455" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/6/61/Bellatrix_attacking_Snatchers_GIF.gif/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/235?cb=20140622055455" decoding="async" width="235" height="136" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="Bellatrix attacking Snatchers GIF.gif" data-image-key="Bellatrix_attacking_Snatchers_GIF.gif" /></a> 	 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:Bellatrix_attacking_Snatchers_GIF.gif" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption">Bellatrix and Scabior before she <a href="/wiki/Stunning_Spell" title="Stunning Spell">stuns</a> him and the other Snatchers</p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure>
<p>Having taken the trio to <a href="/wiki/Malfoy_Manor" title="Malfoy Manor">Malfoy Manor</a>, where Voldemort had set up base, Scabior assured <a href="/wiki/Narcissa_Malfoy" title="Narcissa Malfoy">Narcissa Malfoy</a> the prisoner they had caught was Harry Potter. Later, when <a href="/wiki/Bellatrix_Lestrange" title="Bellatrix Lestrange">Bellatrix Lestrange</a> took <a href="/wiki/Godric_Gryffindor%27s_Sword" class="mw-redirect" title="Godric Gryffindor&#39;s Sword">Godric Gryffindor's Sword</a> from one of the Snatchers, Scabior drew his wand on her but was quickly Stunned. <a href="/wiki/Draco_Malfoy" title="Draco Malfoy">Draco Malfoy</a> then moved Scabior's unconscious form to the courtyard with those of the other Snatchers.<sup id="cite_ref-DH23_4-11" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH23-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Personality_and_traits">Personality and traits</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Scabior?veaction=edit&amp;section=6" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Personality and traits">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Scabior?action=edit&amp;section=6" title="Edit section: Personality and traits"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>In his youth, Scabior was <a href="/wiki/Sorting_ceremony" title="Sorting ceremony">sorted</a> into <a href="/wiki/Slytherin" title="Slytherin">Slytherin</a> at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts</a>, so it can be assumed he was, in adulthood, a very cunning, resourceful and ambitious individual who was committed to pursuing his goals as a Snatcher. Scabior appeared to be primarily motivated as a Snatcher for personal gain, since Snatchers were generally motivated by the promise of a monetary reward from the Ministry, which involved taking part in the hunting, kidnapping and murder of Muggle-borns. He also referred to Muggle-borns as "<a href="/wiki/Mudblood" title="Mudblood">Mudbloods</a>", showing he was prejudiced against them to an extent. He seemed to both fear and respect Lord Voldemort, and seemed eager and scared at the idea of delivering Harry Potter to the <a href="/wiki/Dark_Lord" title="Dark Lord">Dark Lord</a> directly.<sup id="cite_ref-DH23_4-12" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH23-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>He was also the only member of the group not to jeer when Greyback expressed a desire to bite Hermione, instead encouraging his leader to restrain himself.<sup id="cite_ref-DH23_4-13" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH23-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> This restraint was a quality rarely seen amongst Voldemort's allies and may have been Scabior's only redeeming quality.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Magical_abilities_and_skills">Magical abilities and skills</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Scabior?veaction=edit&amp;section=7" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Scabior?action=edit&amp;section=7" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li><b><a href="/wiki/Duelling" title="Duelling">Duelling</a></b>: Scabior was a moderately skilled duellist, as he was able to capture several rebellious <a href="/wiki/Muggle-borns" class="mw-redirect" title="Muggle-borns">Muggle-borns</a> and other runaways, even killing some in battle, showing he had some skill in <a href="/wiki/Martial_magic" title="Martial magic">martial magic</a>. However he was neither fast nor skilled enough to defend himself against <a href="/wiki/Bellatrix_Lestrange" title="Bellatrix Lestrange">Bellatrix Lestrange</a> when she attacked him and the other Snatchers.<sup id="cite_ref-DH23_4-14" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH23-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Apparition" title="Apparition">Apparition</a></b>: Like most adult wizards, Scabior was capable of apparating, as he transported the trio to Malfoy Manor via apparition with his band of Snatchers in 1998.<sup id="cite_ref-DH23_4-15" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH23-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Dark_Arts" title="Dark Arts">Dark Arts</a></b>: Scabior was skilled with dark magic, as he was able to successfully cast the <a href="/wiki/Killing_Curse" title="Killing Curse">Killing Curse</a> and <a href="/wiki/Imperius_Curse" title="Imperius Curse">Imperius Curse</a>, handle <a href="/wiki/Curse" title="Curse">cursed</a> objects,<sup id="cite_ref-LEGOY2_6-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-LEGOY2-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup> and was also capable of casting the <a href="/wiki/Impediment_Jinx" title="Impediment Jinx">Impediment Jinx</a> proficiently.<sup id="cite_ref-DHF1_7-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DHF1-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup></li></ul>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Scabior?veaction=edit&amp;section=9" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Scabior?action=edit&amp;section=9" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>Scabior is portrayed by <a href="/wiki/Nick_Moran" title="Nick Moran">Nick Moran</a> in the two-part <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Deathly_Hallows_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows (film)">film adaptations</a> of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Deathly_Hallows" title="Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows">Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows</a></i>.<sup id="cite_ref-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li>In the films, Scabior's role was expanded as he, instead of Greyback, was depicted as the leader of the Snatchers. He also teases Hermione, again instead of Greyback. In fact, the first of the two-part film, Greyback seemed to be a subordinate of Scabior's group, while the second part Scabior led a very large army of Snatchers to invade Hogwarts.</li>
<li>Scenes that were cut from the film involved Scabior being violent and perverse. Nick Moran said: "It's all very reminiscent of <a href="/wiki/Germany" title="Germany">Germany</a> in the <a href="/wiki/1930s" title="1930s">1930s</a>. I give <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ron Weasley</a> a proper kicking and call him Ginger. Then I sniff <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione</a> and say: "You're going to be my favourite". If you take the wands out, it's something out of an <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Eli_Roth" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Eli Roth">Eli Roth</a> movie."<sup id="cite_ref-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li>Scabior is a boss in <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Deathly_Hallows:_Part_2_(video_game)" title="Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 2 (video game)">Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 2 (video game)</a></i>.</li>
<li>In <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Deathly_Hallows:_Part_1" title="Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 1">Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 1</a></i>, Scabior is briefly strangled by a <a href="/wiki/Whip_Spell" title="Whip Spell">whip</a> <a href="/wiki/Conjuration" title="Conjuration">conjured</a> by Bellatrix, then thrown to the ground and ordered to leave along with Fenrir. This differs from the book, in which he was <a href="/wiki/Stunning_Spell" title="Stunning Spell">stunned</a> by Bellatrix and his fate left unknown after the said incident.
<ul><li>In the book Bellatrix mentions that she plans on killing him and the rest of the Snatchers for defying her. It's never shown whether or not she ends up doing so, or if they were killed in a rage by Voldemort for letting Harry and his friends escape. It's possible that he was spared and lived on to fight in the Battle of Hogwarts like in the films.</li></ul></li>
<li>When the trio were captured by the snatchers in <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Deathly_Hallows:_Part_1" title="Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 1">Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 1</a></i> to be brought to Malfoy Manor, Scabior appears to be wearing Hermione's scarf, which was tied to a tree by Hermione earlier on in the film. This was noticed by an Alohomora listener during their live film watch.</li></ul>
