<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Hector_Podmore?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Hector_Podmore?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>On <a href="/wiki/6_December" title="6 December">6 December</a> <a href="/wiki/1926" title="1926">1926</a>, he attended an emergency meeting of the <a href="/wiki/International_Confederation_of_Wizards" title="International Confederation of Wizards">International Confederation of Wizards</a> to discuss the <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magical</a> disturbances happening in <a href="/wiki/New_York" title="New York">New York</a>. He questioned <a href="/wiki/Newton_Scamander" title="Newton Scamander">Newt Scamander</a> about his reasons for being in New York and was sceptical of the reply that he was there to buy an <a href="/wiki/Appaloosa_Puffskein" title="Appaloosa Puffskein">Appaloosa Puffskein</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-FBS_1-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-FBS-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Hector_Podmore?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Hector_Podmore?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>Hector Podmore was portrayed by <a href="/wiki/Richard_Clothier" title="Richard Clothier">Richard Clothier</a> in <i><a href="/wiki/Fantastic_Beasts_and_Where_to_Find_Them_(film)" title="Fantastic Beasts and Where to Find Them (film)">Fantastic Beasts and Where to Find Them</a></i>, but was credited as British Envoy.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li>Given how the British Ministry of Magic has a <a href="/wiki/Unidentified_Minister_of_Magical_Transportation" title="Unidentified Minister of Magical Transportation">Minister of Magical Transportation</a>, it is not unthinkable that Podmore is a Minister of International Magical Cooperation and, as such, presumably head of that department and representing the Ministry and its interests at the ICW. He could also have possibly been a junior minister for the <a href="/wiki/Department_of_Magical_Accidents_and_Catastrophes" title="Department of Magical Accidents and Catastrophes">Department of Magical Accidents and Catastrophes</a>, given that there was a magical catastrophe going on in New York and he was summoned to New York for discussion.</li>
<li>Alternatively, he could have been intended to be <a href="/wiki/Hector_Fawley" title="Hector Fawley">Hector Fawley</a>, then Minister for Magic but there being a mix up in the names.</li></ul>
