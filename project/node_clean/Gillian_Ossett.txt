<p><b>Gillian Ossett</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Hufflepuff" title="Hufflepuff">Hufflepuff</a> <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">student</a> at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> in the <a href="/wiki/1990s" title="1990s">1990s</a>. As a lover of <a href="/wiki/Wizard%27s_Chess" title="Wizard&#39;s Chess">wizard chess</a>, she collected chess pieces, and traded her extra ones with other students for various objects. She could usually be found in the <a href="/wiki/Defence_Against_the_Dark_Arts" title="Defence Against the Dark Arts">Defence Against the Dark Arts</a> <a href="/wiki/Classroom_3C" title="Classroom 3C">classroom</a> during the <a href="/wiki/1996-1997_school_year" class="mw-redirect" title="1996-1997 school year">1996-1997 school year</a>.
</p>
