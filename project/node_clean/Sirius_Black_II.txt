<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Sirius_Black_II?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Sirius_Black_II?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>Based on a statement by <a href="/wiki/Horace_Slughorn" title="Horace Slughorn">Horace Slughorn</a>, Sirius may have been a member of the <a href="/wiki/Slytherin" title="Slytherin">Slytherin House</a>. That would make him the only Sirius Black who was sorted into Slytherin house; his uncle <a href="/wiki/Sirius_Black_I" title="Sirius Black I">Sirius Black I</a> died before going to Hogwarts and <a href="/wiki/Sirius_Black_III" class="mw-redirect" title="Sirius Black III">Sirius Black III</a> was sorted into <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor" title="Gryffindor">Gryffindor</a> house. However, he attended Hogwarts c. <a href="/wiki/1888" class="mw-redirect" title="1888">1888</a> - <a href="/wiki/1895" class="mw-redirect" title="1895">1895</a>, when Slughorn was not the Potions teacher.</li>
<li>Sirius II had the same name as his <a href="/wiki/Sirius_Black_I" title="Sirius Black I">paternal uncle</a> who died very young.</li>
<li>Sirius II named his son Arcturus, the same as <a href="/wiki/Arcturus_Black_II" title="Arcturus Black II">his brother</a> and <a href="/wiki/Arcturus_Black_I" title="Arcturus Black I">his great uncle</a>.</li></ul>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Sirius_Black_II?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Sirius_Black_II?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p><i>Sirius</i> is the name of the <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sirius" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Sirius">Dog Star</a> (<i>Alpha Canis Majoris</i>), which with an apparent magnitude of -1.46 is the brightest star in the night sky.
</p>
