<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Dugald_McPhail?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Dugald_McPhail?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_life">Early life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Dugald_McPhail?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Early life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Dugald_McPhail?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Early life"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Dugald McPhail was born on an unknown date, no later than <a href="/wiki/1841" class="mw-redirect" title="1841">1841</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> to at least one magical parent. His surname, coupled with the fact that he spoke in a heavy Scottish brogue, implies he was from <a href="/wiki/Scotland" title="Scotland">Scotland</a>, or had Scottish ancestry.
</p><p>In his youth, he attended <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a>, where he was <a href="/wiki/Sorting_ceremony" title="Sorting ceremony">Sorted</a> into <a href="/wiki/Hufflepuff" title="Hufflepuff">Hufflepuff</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-pottermore_2-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-pottermore-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="As_Minister_for_Magic">As Minister for Magic</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Dugald_McPhail?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: As Minister for Magic">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Dugald_McPhail?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: As Minister for Magic"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<figure class="thumb tleft show-info-icon" style="width: 252px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/4/4a/Knight_Bus.png/revision/latest?cb=20141109164450" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/4/4a/Knight_Bus.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/250?cb=20141109164450" decoding="async" width="250" height="212" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="Knight Bus.png" data-image-key="Knight_Bus.png" /></a> 	 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:Knight_Bus.png" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption">The <a href="/wiki/Knight_Bus" title="Knight Bus">Knight Bus</a>, Minister Dugald McPhail's brainchild</p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure>
<p>McPhail was appointed <a href="/wiki/Minister_for_Magic" title="Minister for Magic">Minister for Magic</a>, sometime after leaving Hogwarts, from <a href="/wiki/1858" class="mw-redirect" title="1858">1858</a> to <a href="/wiki/1865" class="mw-redirect" title="1865">1865</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-pottermore_2-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-pottermore-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>.  He was elected Minister at least once, perhaps twice.
</p><p>During his time as Minister, McPhail came up with the idea of mimicking the then-new <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggle</a> bus service to provide underage and infirm <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witches and wizards</a> with a discreet method of transportation, leading to the introduction of the <a href="/wiki/Knight_Bus" title="Knight Bus">Knight Bus</a> in <a href="/wiki/1865" class="mw-redirect" title="1865">1865</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-pottermore_2-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-pottermore-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>His overall term in office was viewed as "successful" by <a href="/wiki/Gabriel_Truman" title="Gabriel Truman">Gabriel Truman</a> in the <a href="/wiki/1990s" title="1990s">1990s</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-pottermore_2-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-pottermore-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Legacy">Legacy</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Dugald_McPhail?veaction=edit&amp;section=4" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Legacy">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Dugald_McPhail?action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Legacy"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>On <a href="/wiki/1_September" title="1 September">1 September</a>, <a href="/wiki/1991" title="1991">1991</a>, <a href="/wiki/Prefect" title="Prefect">Prefect</a> <a href="/wiki/Gabriel_Truman" title="Gabriel Truman">Gabriel Truman</a> welcomed the <a href="/wiki/Hufflepuff" title="Hufflepuff">Hufflepuff</a> <a href="/wiki/First_year" title="First year">first years</a> to <a href="/wiki/Hufflepuff_Basement" title="Hufflepuff Basement">Hufflepuff Basement</a> with a short speech in which he mentioned former Minister Dugald McPhail as one of the many notable Hogwarts students who were <a href="/wiki/Sorting_ceremony" title="Sorting ceremony">Sorted</a>, like them, into Hufflepuff.<sup id="cite_ref-pottermore_2-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-pottermore-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p><br />
</p>
