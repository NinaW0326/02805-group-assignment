He was a direct descendant of the <a href="/wiki/Auror" title="Auror">Auror</a> <a href="/wiki/Theodard_Fontaine" title="Theodard Fontaine">Theodard Fontaine</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-WW_1-11" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-WW-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
