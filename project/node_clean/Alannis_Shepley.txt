She played casual <a href="/wiki/Quidditch" title="Quidditch">Quidditch</a> matches with other students during the <a href="/wiki/1996%E2%80%931997_school_year" title="1996–1997 school year">1996–1997 school year</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
