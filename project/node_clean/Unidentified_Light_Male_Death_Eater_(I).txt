This <b><a href="/wiki/Dark_Wizard" title="Dark Wizard">Dark Wizard</a> </b>was a <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eater</a> and servant of <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a> who participated and fought in the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a>. During the early stages of the battle he was near the front of the group of Death Eaters who observed the protective enchantments placed around <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts</a>. He presumably fought in the Battle of Hogwarts where he was either killed or arrested and imprisoned in <a href="/wiki/Azkaban" title="Azkaban">Azkaban</a>, though he was possibly one of the Death Eaters who opted to flee once <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> revealed himself to be alive.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
