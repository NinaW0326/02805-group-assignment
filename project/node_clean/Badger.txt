<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Badgers_in_the_wizarding_world">Badgers in the wizarding world</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Badger?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Badgers in the wizarding world">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Badger?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Badgers in the wizarding world"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>It is the mascot of <a href="/wiki/Hufflepuff" title="Hufflepuff">Hufflepuff</a> <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Houses" title="Hogwarts Houses">House</a> at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a>. Like the house it represents, the badger is often underestimated and deemed non-threatening because it is not generally aggressive unless provoked, at which point it can and will viciously fight off threats even larger than itself.<sup id="cite_ref-PM_1-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PM-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> This symbolism was perhaps showcased best in the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a>, where many Hufflepuffs stayed to fight and defend their school, with only <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor" title="Gryffindor">Gryffindor</a> outnumbering them.<sup id="cite_ref-DH31_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH31-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p><a href="/wiki/Uric_the_Oddball" title="Uric the Oddball">Uric the Oddball</a> once turned up for a presentation before the <a href="/wiki/Wizards%27_Council" title="Wizards&#39; Council">Wizards' Council</a> wearing nothing but a toupee that turned out to be a <a href="/wiki/Death" title="Death">dead</a> badger, after listening to a <a href="/wiki/Fwooper" title="Fwooper">Fwooper</a>'s song continuously for three months.<sup id="cite_ref-FBR_4-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-FBR-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>There is a <a href="/wiki/Spell" title="Spell">spell</a> that can <a href="/wiki/Transfiguration" title="Transfiguration">transfigure</a> <a href="/wiki/Cauldron_to_badger_spell" title="Cauldron to badger spell">a cauldron into a badger</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTPG_5-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTPG-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> One could turn a <a href="/wiki/Badgering" title="Badgering">human into a bager</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-COS11_6-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COS11-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-TCG_7-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-TCG-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>It is a known corporeal form of the <a href="/wiki/Patronus_Charm" title="Patronus Charm">Patronus Charm</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-patronus_8-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-patronus-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
