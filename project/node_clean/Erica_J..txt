<b>Erica J.</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch</a> and <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor" title="Gryffindor">Gryffindor</a> student at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a>. She once signed up for <a href="/wiki/Quidditch" title="Quidditch">Quidditch</a> training at seven, Tuesday evening, on a sign-up sheet that was posted on the <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor_Notice_Board" title="Gryffindor Notice Board">bulletin board</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor_Common_Room" title="Gryffindor Common Room">Gryffindor Common Room</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Erica_J.?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Erica_J.?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>Her name; <i>Erica</i> means several things. In Latin it means Heather. In the ancient language of Norse it means Eternal Ruler. In old German it means Honourable Ruler. The Latin, <i>Erica</i>, is used in gardening terms to mean Heather even in the modern day.</li>
<li>Her possible surname, <i>Jerankordr</i>, may have come from Indian or Moroccan roots where Jerank is used to describe a type of cuisine.</li></ul>
