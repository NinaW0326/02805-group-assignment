<p><b>Madam Rolanda Hooch</b> (b. before <a href="/wiki/1918" title="1918">1918</a>)<sup id="cite_ref-GreatWar_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GreatWar-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch</a> who worked as the <a href="/wiki/Flying_(class)" title="Flying (class)">Flying</a> <a href="/wiki/Flying_Instructor" title="Flying Instructor">instructor</a>, <a href="/wiki/Quidditch" title="Quidditch">Quidditch</a> <a href="/wiki/Quidditch_referee" title="Quidditch referee">referee</a>, and coach at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a>. She learnt to fly on <a href="/wiki/Rolanda_Hooch%27s_Silver_Arrow_broomstick" title="Rolanda Hooch&#39;s Silver Arrow broomstick">her own Silver Arrow broomstick</a> sometime before or during the <a href="/wiki/First_World_War" title="First World War">Great War</a> and returned to Hogwarts for a job. While on staff at Hogwarts, Hooch worked under <a href="/wiki/Headmaster" title="Headmaster">Headmasters</a> <a href="/wiki/Albus_Dumbledore" title="Albus Dumbledore">Albus Dumbledore</a>, <a href="/wiki/Dolores_Umbridge" title="Dolores Umbridge">Dolores Umbridge</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Minerva_McGonagall" title="Minerva McGonagall">Minerva McGonagall</a>.
</p><p>Madam Hooch taught <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> and his classmates how to ride a <a href="/wiki/Broomstick" title="Broomstick">broomstick</a> in <a href="/wiki/1991" title="1991">1991</a>, in addition to acting as the referee the <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor_Quidditch_team" title="Gryffindor Quidditch team">Gryffindor</a> versus <a href="/wiki/Slytherin_Quidditch_team" title="Slytherin Quidditch team">Slytherin</a> match that same year. One of her games was cancelled just after starting, due to the <a href="/wiki/Petrification" title="Petrification">Petrification</a> of <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione Granger</a> and <a href="/wiki/Penelope_Clearwater" title="Penelope Clearwater">Penelope Clearwater</a> in <a href="/wiki/1993" title="1993">1993</a>. During the next school year, Hooch (along with <a href="/wiki/Filius_Flitwick" title="Filius Flitwick">Professor Flitwick</a>) was in charge of examining a <a href="/wiki/Firebolt" title="Firebolt">Firebolt</a> sent to <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> in case it was jinxed, hexed or cursed.
</p><p>Hooch survived the Second Wizarding War and returned to her post as Flying Instructor and Quidditch referee. She taught Harry Potter's second son (Albus Potter) how to ride a broomstick.<sup id="cite_ref-11" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-11">&#91;11&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_life">Early life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Rolanda_Hooch?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Early life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Rolanda_Hooch?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Early life"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<div class="quote">
<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;Harry Potter and the Philosopher&#39;s Stone (video game)">"<i>A spell! I haven't been this annoyed since my broom was singed by overzealous anti-aircraft during the Great War!</i>"</span></dd>
<dd>&#8212;Madam Hooch relaying some information on her past<sup class="noprint"><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Philosopher%27s_Stone_(video_game)" title="Harry Potter and the Philosopher&#39;s Stone (video game)">[src]</a></sup></dd>

<p>Rolanda Hooch was born prior to <a href="/wiki/1918" title="1918">1918</a><sup id="cite_ref-GreatWar_1-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GreatWar-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> She learnt to fly on a <a href="/wiki/Silver_Arrow" title="Silver Arrow">Silver Arrow</a> <a href="/wiki/Broomstick" title="Broomstick">broomstick</a> at some point.<sup id="cite_ref-PA13_12-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PA13-12">&#91;12&#93;</a></sup> One night, during the <a href="/wiki/First_World_War" title="First World War">Great War</a> (<a href="/wiki/1914" title="1914">1914</a>-<a href="/wiki/1918" title="1918">1918</a>), <a href="/wiki/Rolanda_Hooch%27s_Silver_Arrow_broomstick" title="Rolanda Hooch&#39;s Silver Arrow broomstick">her broom</a> was singed by a <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggle</a> anti-aircraft device, which she found personally insulting.<sup id="cite_ref-PSgba_13-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PSgba-13">&#91;13&#93;</a></sup> She declared that the only thing she found more humiliating than that incident was when <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">a student</a> <a href="/wiki/Jinx" title="Jinx">jinxed</a> her many decades later, in <a href="/wiki/1991" title="1991">1991</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-PSgba_13-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PSgba-13">&#91;13&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>She attended <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> in her youth, and was a member of her House Quidditch team.<sup id="cite_ref-HM2_14-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HM2-14">&#91;14&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<figure class="thumb tleft show-info-icon" style="width: 252px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/b/b2/Quidditchpitch.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20160723002056" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/b/b2/Quidditchpitch.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/250?cb=20160723002056" decoding="async" width="250" height="98" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="Quidditchpitch.jpg" data-image-key="Quidditchpitch.jpg" /></a> 	 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:Quidditchpitch.jpg" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption">The Hogwarts Quidditch pitch where Hooch was selected to referee</p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure> 
<p>Sometime before her tenure at Hogwarts, Rolanda was selected by the <a href="/wiki/Department_of_Magical_Games_and_Sports" title="Department of Magical Games and Sports">Department of Magical Games and Sports</a> as the Hogwarts Quidditch referee. After being selected she underwent a series of complicated and rigorous tests, both written and practical.<sup id="cite_ref-QTA6_15-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-QTA6-15">&#91;15&#93;</a></sup> She more then likely passed the tests as she eventually joined Hogwarts as the flying instructor for first year students.
</p>

<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;Harry Potter: Hogwarts Mystery">"<i>Flying is not unlike walking. It's just much more difficult, and far more dangerous.</i>"</span></dd>
<dd>&#8212;Madam Hooch teaching a <a href="/wiki/Flying_(class)" title="Flying (class)">Flying Class</a><sup class="noprint"><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter:_Hogwarts_Mystery" title="Harry Potter: Hogwarts Mystery">[src]</a></sup></dd>

<p>Rolanda's duties at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts</a> included <a href="/wiki/Flying_Instructor" title="Flying Instructor">First Year Flight Instructor</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-PS9_5-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PS9-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/Quidditch" title="Quidditch">Quidditch</a> <a href="/wiki/Quidditch_referee" title="Quidditch referee">referee</a><sup id="cite_ref-PS11_10-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PS11-10">&#91;10&#93;</a></sup> and coach in charge of all broom-related activities.<sup id="cite_ref-PS7_4-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PS7-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> She was now referred to as "Madam Hooch",<sup id="cite_ref-PS7_4-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PS7-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> and was respected by her students because of her liking for fair-play and clean games. She was given <a href="/wiki/Rolanda_Hooch%27s_office" title="Rolanda Hooch&#39;s office">an office</a> located somewhere in <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Castle" title="Hogwarts Castle">the Castle</a>, in which the crate which held the <a href="/wiki/Golden_Snitch" title="Golden Snitch">Golden Snitch</a>, <a href="/wiki/Quaffle" title="Quaffle">Quaffle</a> and <a href="/wiki/Bludger" title="Bludger">Bludgers</a> was kept safe under lock-and-key.<sup id="cite_ref-CS10_16-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-CS10-16">&#91;16&#93;</a></sup> Her lessons teaching the <a href="/wiki/First_year" title="First year">first years</a> how to fly were held in the <a href="/wiki/Training_Grounds" title="Training Grounds">Training Grounds</a><sup id="cite_ref-HBPg_17-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBPg-17">&#91;17&#93;</a></sup> at the back of the castle, near the <a href="/wiki/Quidditch_pitch" title="Quidditch pitch">Quidditch stadium</a> and <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_greenhouses" title="Hogwarts greenhouses">Herbology Greenhouses</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-HBPg_17-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBPg-17">&#91;17&#93;</a></sup> year <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor" title="Gryffindor">Gryffindors</a> and <a href="/wiki/Slytherin" title="Slytherin">Slytherins</a> in the Training Grounds on Thursday, <a href="/wiki/12_September" title="12 September">12 September</a>, during the second week of term.<sup id="cite_ref-PS9_5-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PS9-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> She had twenty of the school broomsticks lined up ready on the grass before she arrived, despite the fact that the school brooms vibrated if the rider flew too high and always flew slightly to the left.<sup id="cite_ref-PS9_5-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PS9-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> She probably had a personal acquaintance with a student named Amanda, whom Hooch greeted personally.<sup id="cite_ref-PSf15_6-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PSf15-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup> To begin the class, Hooch ordered the students to stand on the left side of their brooms with their right hand over it. They were told to say, "up!" to summon the brooms to their hands and were then to hover slightly in the air. Unfortunately, a <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor" title="Gryffindor">Gryffindor</a> named <a href="/wiki/Neville_Longbottom" title="Neville Longbottom">Neville Longbottom</a> kicked off too early and lost control, resulting in an accident and breaking his wrist. After examining the wound, Madam Hooch took Neville to the infirmary and warned that anyone caught flying without supervision would be expelled "faster than you can say Quidditch."<sup id="cite_ref-PS9_5-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PS9-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Despite this, when <a href="/wiki/Draco_Malfoy" title="Draco Malfoy">Draco Malfoy</a> threw Neville's dropped <a href="/wiki/Remembrall" title="Remembrall">Remembrall</a> high in the air <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> used his broom to retrieve it, showing great natural skill. Fortunately for Potter, due to  <a href="/wiki/Minerva_McGonagall" title="Minerva McGonagall">Professor McGonagall's</a> intervention, he was spared expulsion and was offered a position as <a href="/wiki/Seeker" title="Seeker">seeker</a> on the <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor_Quidditch_team" title="Gryffindor Quidditch team">Gryffindor Quidditch team</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-PS9_5-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PS9-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<p>Not long after <a href="/wiki/Hallowe%27en" title="Hallowe&#39;en">Hallowe'en</a>, the first match of the <a href="/wiki/Quidditch" title="Quidditch">Quidditch</a> season took place: Gryffindor versus <a href="/wiki/Slytherin_Quidditch_team" title="Slytherin Quidditch team">Slytherin</a>. Madam Hooch was to referee the game and told both teams to play fairly, though she was obviously speaking more to the Slytherin team.<sup id="cite_ref-PS11_10-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PS11-10">&#91;10&#93;</a></sup> She released the <a href="/wiki/Bludger" title="Bludger">Bludgers</a> first, then the <a href="/wiki/Golden_Snitch" title="Golden Snitch">Golden Snitch</a>, and the game began when she threw the <a href="/wiki/Quaffle" title="Quaffle">Quaffle</a> into the air.<sup id="cite_ref-PSf19_18-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PSf19-18">&#91;18&#93;</a></sup> During that game, many fouls were committed by the Slytherins, and she awarded several penalties to the Gryffindors, who won when the new <a href="/wiki/Seeker" title="Seeker">Seeker</a>, <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>, caught the Golden Snitch.<sup id="cite_ref-PS11_10-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PS11-10">&#91;10&#93;</a></sup> Hooch, riding her broom, blew the whistle and announced the Gryffindors' victory to the Hogwarts crowd.<sup id="cite_ref-PSf20_19-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PSf20-19">&#91;19&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Later on in the year, Madam Hooch was relieved of the responsibility of refereeing the Gryffindor versus <a href="/wiki/Hufflepuff_Quidditch_team" title="Hufflepuff Quidditch team">Hufflepuff</a> match, as <a href="/wiki/Severus_Snape" title="Severus Snape">Professor Snape</a> insisted on refereeing the game<sup id="cite_ref-PS13_20-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PS13-20">&#91;20&#93;</a></sup> to make sure Harry Potter's <a href="/wiki/Nimbus_2000" title="Nimbus 2000">Nimbus 2000</a> was not <a href="/wiki/Broom_jinx" title="Broom jinx">jinxed</a> again.<sup id="cite_ref-PS17_21-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PS17-21">&#91;21&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<p>In August, <a href="/wiki/1992" title="1992">1992</a>, Hooch went to <a href="/wiki/Diagon_Alley" title="Diagon Alley">Diagon Alley</a>, and rushed to <a href="/wiki/Flourish_and_Blotts" title="Flourish and Blotts">Flourish and Blotts</a> to see <a href="/wiki/Gilderoy_Lockhart" title="Gilderoy Lockhart">Gilderoy Lockhart</a>, who had been hired by Dumbledore to teach <a href="/wiki/Defence_Against_the_Dark_Arts" title="Defence Against the Dark Arts">Defence Against the Dark Arts</a> that year. She was clearly a fan of him and of his written works.<sup id="cite_ref-LEGOY1_7-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-LEGOY1-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>The next school year, Madam Hooch refereed the <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor_Quidditch_team" title="Gryffindor Quidditch team">Gryffindor</a> versus <a href="/wiki/Slytherin_Quidditch_team" title="Slytherin Quidditch team">Slytherin</a> Quidditch match, during which <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>, the Gryffindor <a href="/wiki/Seeker" title="Seeker">Seeker</a>, was attacked by a <a href="/wiki/Jinx" title="Jinx">jinxed</a> Bludger. The mystery was how the Bludger was jinxed, since it had been kept safe and locked in Madam Hooch's office. She was forced to stop the game momentarily for the Gryffindors once they deliberated over the rogue <a href="/wiki/Bludger" title="Bludger">Bludger</a>, but they ultimately decided to continue playing, as otherwise they would have had to forfeit the match.<sup id="cite_ref-CS10_16-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-CS10-16">&#91;16&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>The Gryffindor versus <a href="/wiki/Hufflepuff_Quidditch_team" title="Hufflepuff Quidditch team">Hufflepuff</a> match followed later on in the year, but was cancelled by <a href="/wiki/Minerva_McGonagall" title="Minerva McGonagall">Professor McGonagall</a> shortly after Madam Hooch had released the balls and began the game. The reason for the cancellation was that <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione Granger</a> and <a href="/wiki/Penelope_Clearwater" title="Penelope Clearwater">Penelope Clearwater</a> had been found <a href="/wiki/Petrification" title="Petrification">petrified</a> near the library.<sup id="cite_ref-CS14_22-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-CS14-22">&#91;22&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<p>Near the end of the year, Madam Hooch and the rest of the staff were called to the staffroom by Professor McGonagall, who had become temporary Headmistress of Hogwarts in <a href="/wiki/Albus_Dumbledore" title="Albus Dumbledore">Professor Dumbledore</a>'s absence. McGonagall told the professors that a student had been kidnapped by Salazar Slytherin's monster and taken into the <a href="/wiki/Chamber_of_Secrets" title="Chamber of Secrets">Chamber of Secrets</a>. After revealing this, Madam Hooch became too weak at the knees to stand, collapsed into a chair and asked who had been taken, when she was told that it was <a href="/wiki/Ginevra_Weasley" title="Ginevra Weasley">Ginevra Weasley</a>, whom Hooch had been giving <a href="/wiki/Flying_(class)" title="Flying (class)">Flying Lessons</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-CS16_23-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-CS16-23">&#91;23&#93;</a></sup> However, she was saved by the end of the year by Harry Potter and <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ron Weasley</a> and then the school returned to its normal routines.<sup id="cite_ref-CS18_24-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-CS18-24">&#91;24&#93;</a></sup> She attended the Feast held at the end of the year.<sup id="cite_ref-CSf36_25-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-CSf36-25">&#91;25&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<p>Not long after the beginning of the 1993–1994 school year, Madam Hooch refereed the <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor_Quidditch_team" title="Gryffindor Quidditch team">Gryffindor</a> versus <a href="/wiki/Hufflepuff_Quidditch_team" title="Hufflepuff Quidditch team">Hufflepuff</a> Quidditch match, which took place in severe weather conditions and poor visibility. But during that match, <a href="/wiki/Dementor" title="Dementor">Dementors</a>, who were supposed to be guarding the school, interfered and made Harry Potter fall off of his broomstick, enabling the Hufflepuff Seeker, <a href="/wiki/Cedric_Diggory" title="Cedric Diggory">Cedric Diggory</a>, to catch the Golden Snitch.<sup id="cite_ref-PA9_26-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PA9-26">&#91;26&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>After the attack on the <a href="/wiki/Fat_Lady" title="Fat Lady">Fat Lady</a> by Sirius Black, Professor McGonagall asked Madam Hooch to oversee his Quidditch practices on the pitch for his safety, which she did for McGonagall.<sup id="cite_ref-PA8_27-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PA8-27">&#91;27&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Furthermore, Harry mysteriously received a <a href="/wiki/Firebolt" title="Firebolt">Firebolt</a> broomstick for Christmas, which McGonagall thought could have come from <a href="/wiki/Sirius_Black" title="Sirius Black">Sirius Black</a>. In order to ensure that the broom was safe to ride, McGonagall handed it over to Madam Hooch and <a href="/wiki/Filius_Flitwick" title="Filius Flitwick">Professor Flitwick</a>, the Charms Master, who would "strip it down" to look for curses, hexes, or jinxes.<sup id="cite_ref-PA11_28-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PA11-28">&#91;28&#93;</a></sup> A few weeks later, once Hooch and Flitwick had ensured the broom's safety, the Firebolt was returned to Harry Potter to use in his Quidditch practices and games.<sup id="cite_ref-PA12_29-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PA12-29">&#91;29&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<p>She was quite taken with the broom and rhapsodised over it for some time before <a href="/wiki/Oliver_Wood" title="Oliver Wood">Oliver Wood</a>, the Gryffindor Quidditch Captain and <a href="/wiki/Keeper" title="Keeper">Keeper</a>, reminded her that they were there to practise and not listen to her talking about her old Silver Arrow. Though she was supposed to keep an eye on Harry, she then proceeded to fall asleep in the stands while the <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor_Quidditch_team" title="Gryffindor Quidditch team">Gryffindor Quidditch team</a> practised.<sup id="cite_ref-PA13_12-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PA13-12">&#91;12&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>The game between the Gryffindor and <a href="/wiki/Ravenclaw_Quidditch_team" title="Ravenclaw Quidditch team">Ravenclaw</a> was refereed by Madam Hooch. The game was concluded when <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> caught the Golden Snitch, awarding one-hundred and fifty points to his already-winning Gryffindor team.
</p><p>The final match, for the <a href="/wiki/Inter-House_Quidditch_Cup" title="Inter-House Quidditch Cup">Quidditch Cup</a>, was between Gryffindor and <a href="/wiki/Slytherin" title="Slytherin">Slytherin</a>, and Madam Hooch was tasked with overseeing the match as a referee. The final was a violent game, and Madam Hooch commented several times that she has never seen such flagrant fouls committed. Indeed, she was rendered almost apoplectic with fury at the level of cheating demonstrated by the Slytherin team, awarding a grand total of five penalties to the Gryffindors as the match grew progressively dirtier. The match concluded with Harry Potter's capturing of the <a href="/wiki/Golden_Snitch" title="Golden Snitch">Golden Snitch</a>, earning the Gryffindor Quidditch team the Quidditch Cup for the first time in roughly seven years.<sup id="cite_ref-PA15_30-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PA15-30">&#91;30&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Later_life">Later life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Rolanda_Hooch?veaction=edit&amp;section=11" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Later life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Rolanda_Hooch?action=edit&amp;section=11" title="Edit section: Later life"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Madam Hooch survived the war and continued to serve as the <a href="/wiki/Flying_Instructor" title="Flying Instructor">Flying Instructor</a> and <a href="/wiki/Quidditch_referee" title="Quidditch referee">Quidditch referee</a> at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a>. During the <a href="/wiki/Calamity" title="Calamity">Calamity</a>, she would help members of the <a href="/wiki/Statute_of_Secrecy_Task_Force" title="Statute of Secrecy Task Force">Statute of Secrecy Task Force</a> improve their ability to target elusive foes by having them cast spells while she dodges on her broom.
</p><p>Madam Hooch was still teaching as of 2020,<sup id="cite_ref-act2scene12_40-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-act2scene12-40">&#91;40&#93;</a></sup> at the age of at least one-hundred and two. In September <a href="/wiki/2017" title="2017">2017</a> she taught <a href="/wiki/Albus_Potter" title="Albus Potter">Albus Potter</a> and was not impressed with him, as he struggled to make his broom rise.<sup id="cite_ref-act1scene4_41-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-act1scene4-41">&#91;41&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Physical_description">Physical description</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Rolanda_Hooch?veaction=edit&amp;section=12" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Physical description">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Rolanda_Hooch?action=edit&amp;section=12" title="Edit section: Physical description"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<div class="quote">
<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;Harry Potter and the Philosopher&#39;s Stone">"<i>Their teacher, Madam Hooch, arrived. She had short, grey hair and yellow eyes like a hawk.</i>"</span></dd>
<dd>&#8212;A description of Madam Hooch in <a href="/wiki/1991" title="1991">1991</a><sup class="noprint"><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Philosopher%27s_Stone" title="Harry Potter and the Philosopher&#39;s Stone">[src]</a></sup></dd>
<dd></dd></dl></div><p>In her old age, during the 1990s, Madam Hooch had spiky grey hair which was cut short and had piercing, yellow, hawk-like eyes,<sup id="cite_ref-PS9_5-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PS9-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> usually hidden behind goggles.<sup id="cite_ref-PSf19_18-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PSf19-18">&#91;18&#93;</a></sup> She usually wore a white button-down collared shirt and black necktie with the Hogwarts crest, under a cloak,<sup id="cite_ref-PSf15_6-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PSf15-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup> but wore more formal black robes with a hat while sitting in the <a href="/wiki/Great_Hall" title="Great Hall">Great Hall</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-PSf12_42-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PSf12-42">&#91;42&#93;</a></sup>
</p><h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Personality_and_traits">Personality and traits</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Rolanda_Hooch?veaction=edit&amp;section=13" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Personality and traits">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Rolanda_Hooch?action=edit&amp;section=13" title="Edit section: Personality and traits"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<div class="quote">
<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;Harry Potter and the Philosopher&#39;s Stone">"<i>Well, what are you waiting for? Everyone stand by a broomstick. Come on, hurry up.</i>"</span></dd>
<dd>&#8212;Madam Hooch being impatient with a class of <a href="/wiki/First_year" title="First year">first years</a><sup class="noprint"><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Philosopher%27s_Stone" title="Harry Potter and the Philosopher&#39;s Stone">[src]</a></sup></dd>
<dd></dd></dl></div>
<p>Madam Hooch was known and respected by her students because of her belief in good sportsmanship, fairness, and clean games of <a href="/wiki/Quidditch" title="Quidditch">Quidditch</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-PS11_10-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PS11-10">&#91;10&#93;</a></sup> She sometimes seemed impatient whilst teaching her students flying, as while teaching a group of <a href="/wiki/First_year" title="First year">first years</a>, during the <a href="/wiki/1991-1992_school_year" class="mw-redirect" title="1991-1992 school year">1991-1992 school year</a> she was continually telling them to "hurry up" and asking "well, what're you waiting for?".<sup id="cite_ref-PS9_5-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PS9-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Regardless she was still kind and caring, as evident when <a href="/wiki/Neville_Longbottom" title="Neville Longbottom">Neville Longbottom</a> broke his wrist after injuring himself during a <a href="/wiki/Flying_(class)" title="Flying (class)">Flying Class</a>, she tended to him after his accident and took him immediately to the <a href="/wiki/Hospital_Wing" title="Hospital Wing">Hospital Wing</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-PS9_5-10" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PS9-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> Another example was when she learnt that <a href="/wiki/Ginevra_Weasley" title="Ginevra Weasley">Ginevra Weasley</a> had been taken into the <a href="/wiki/Chamber_of_Secrets" title="Chamber of Secrets">Chamber of Secrets</a>. After learning this she had sunk (weak-kneed) into a chair in the <a href="/wiki/Staffroom" title="Staffroom">staffroom</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-CS16_23-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-CS16-23">&#91;23&#93;</a></sup> Hooch was also very strict and was unafraid to use an <a href="/wiki/Impediment_Jinx" title="Impediment Jinx">Impediment Jinx</a> on <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> and <a href="/wiki/George_Weasley" title="George Weasley">George Weasley</a> after the two attacked <a href="/wiki/Draco_Malfoy" title="Draco Malfoy">Draco Malfoy</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-OP19_33-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OP19-33">&#91;33&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Magical_abilities_and_skills">Magical abilities and skills</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Rolanda_Hooch?veaction=edit&amp;section=14" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Rolanda_Hooch?action=edit&amp;section=14" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li><b><a href="/wiki/Flight" title="Flight">Flying</a></b>: Madam Hooch had considerable skill and talent in <a href="/wiki/Broomstick" title="Broomstick">broomstick</a> flight. She learnt how to fly on a <a href="/wiki/Silver_Arrow" title="Silver Arrow">Silver Arrow</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-PA13_12-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PA13-12">&#91;12&#93;</a></sup> and flew over the <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Quidditch_pitch" title="Hogwarts Quidditch pitch">Quidditch stadium</a> at the end of the <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor" title="Gryffindor">Gryffindor</a> versus <a href="/wiki/Slytherin" title="Slytherin">Slytherin</a> match in <a href="/wiki/1991" title="1991">1991</a>, announcing the Gryffindors' victory.<sup id="cite_ref-PSf20_19-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PSf20-19">&#91;19&#93;</a></sup> Her accomplishment and talent in flying allowed to teach <a href="/wiki/Flying_(class)" title="Flying (class)">Flying class</a> to Hogwarts students as the school's <a href="/wiki/Flying_Instructor" title="Flying Instructor">Flying Instructor</a>, which she did highly competently and professionally, teaching students how to safely and successfully fly broomsticks.<sup id="cite_ref-PS9_5-11" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PS9-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Broomology" title="Broomology">Broomology</a></b>: Hooch was also known to be highly adept in the lore of brooms. This was evident when <a href="/wiki/Minerva_McGonagall" title="Minerva McGonagall">Minerva McGonagall</a> handed over a <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter%27s_Firebolt" title="Harry Potter&#39;s Firebolt">Firebolt</a> to her and <a href="/wiki/Filius_Flitwick" title="Filius Flitwick">Filius Flitwick</a> so that they could test it for <a href="/wiki/Jinx" title="Jinx">jinxes</a>, <a href="/wiki/Hex" title="Hex">hexes</a> and <a href="/wiki/Curse" title="Curse">curses</a>; Flitwick using his charms mastery, and Hooch using her knowledge.<sup id="cite_ref-PA11_28-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PA11-28">&#91;28&#93;</a></sup> Also, while examining Harry's <a href="/wiki/Firebolt" title="Firebolt">Firebolt</a> before his first flight on it, she commented how the Nimbus series develop a drag after a few years of flying.<sup id="cite_ref-PA13_12-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PA13-12">&#91;12&#93;</a></sup> This implied that she possessed very considerable knowledge of <a href="/wiki/Broomstick" title="Broomstick">broomsticks</a>.</li></ul>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Rolanda_Hooch?veaction=edit&amp;section=15" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Rolanda_Hooch?action=edit&amp;section=15" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Rolanda is a feminine form of Roland, a name of Germanic origin that means "fame-land", "fame of the land", "old and wise", or "judicious, wise". Roland was a paladin of Charlemagne and a principal figure in the Matter of France. The "Roland" was a Franco-German missile system, and the WWI aircraft manufacturer Luft-Fahrzeug-Gesellschaft was known for their various "Roland" designs.
</p><p>Hooch is a Germanic word that means "high" or "tall."
</p>
