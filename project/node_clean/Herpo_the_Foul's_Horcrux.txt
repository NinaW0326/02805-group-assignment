<b>Herpo the Foul's Horcrux</b> was the first known <a href="/wiki/Horcrux" title="Horcrux">Horcrux</a> to be created.<sup id="cite_ref-PC_1-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PC-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> It was used to make its <a href="/wiki/Herpo_the_Foul" title="Herpo the Foul">owner</a> <a href="/wiki/Immortality" title="Immortality">immortal</a>. It is unknown what kind of object it was, nor what defences were placed on it, if any. It is also unknown when, or if, it was destroyed.
