The <b>Sea Serpent of Cromer</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Sea_serpent" title="Sea serpent">sea serpent</a> which lived off the coast of <a href="/wiki/Cromer" title="Cromer">Cromer</a>, <a href="/wiki/Norfolk" title="Norfolk">Norfolk</a>. It was slain by <a href="/wiki/Glanmore_Peakes" title="Glanmore Peakes">Glanmore Peakes</a> during the early <a href="/wiki/18th_century" title="18th century">eighteenth century</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Sea_Serpent_of_Cromer?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Sea_Serpent_of_Cromer?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>It is never revealed why Peakes slayed the Sea Serpent of Cromer, as <a href="/wiki/Sea_serpent" title="Sea serpent">Sea Serpents</a> are regarded as docile creatures that pose no threat to <a href="/wiki/Human" title="Human">humans</a> (despite hysterical <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggle</a> accounts of their ferocious behaviour). It could be that this particular serpent had an unusually aggressive demeanour, or that it wasn't strictly a Sea Serpent, but rather an aggressive relative, like the <a href="/wiki/Selma" title="Selma">Selma</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup></li></ul>
