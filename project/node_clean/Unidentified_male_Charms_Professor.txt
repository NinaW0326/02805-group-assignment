<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="History">History</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_male_Charms_Professor?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: History">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_male_Charms_Professor?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: History"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_life">Early life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_male_Charms_Professor?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Early life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_male_Charms_Professor?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Early life"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>This individual was presumably born somewhere in <a href="/wiki/Great_Britain" title="Great Britain">Great Britain</a>. He may have attended <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a>, where he would most likely have gotten high marks in the subject of Charms. Sometime in his adult life, he took on the position of Charms Professor at Hogwarts.
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Teaching_with_the_Book_of_Spells">Teaching with the <i>Book of Spells</i></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_male_Charms_Professor?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Teaching with the Book of Spells">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_male_Charms_Professor?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Teaching with the Book of Spells"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>One night, he was in the <a href="/wiki/Restricted_Section" title="Restricted Section">Restricted Section</a> of the <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Library" title="Hogwarts Library">Hogwarts Library</a> when he happened upon a young Hogwarts student. After admonishing them for being there, he promised not to take any <a href="/wiki/House_points" title="House points">house points</a> and inquired as to what they were doing there. The student produced a note indicating that they were permitted to be in that section to check out a copy of <i>Book of Spells</i>. The Professor was impressed that this student got permission for such a task, and offered to guide them through the book's contents.<sup id="cite_ref-WBS_2-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-WBS-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Together, the two explored the old book, the Professor giving occasional advice on how to complete the book's assigned tasks and offering his own opinions on Goshawk's words, as well as awarding house points when the student achieved a particularly remarkable task. In time, the student had learned all the spells the book had to offer, and the Professor expressed his pride in his student.<sup id="cite_ref-WBS_2-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-WBS-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>His favourite <a href="/wiki/Spell" title="Spell">spell</a> was the <a href="/wiki/Bird-Conjuring_Charm" title="Bird-Conjuring Charm">Bird-Conjuring Charm</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Magical_abilities_and_skills">Magical abilities and skills</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_male_Charms_Professor?veaction=edit&amp;section=4" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_male_Charms_Professor?action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li><b><a href="/wiki/Charm" title="Charm">Charms</a></b>: As a <a href="/wiki/Charms_Master" title="Charms Master">Charms Master</a>, he was naturally learned in the subject. He could easily recognise and advise on all the spells printed in <i>Book of Spells</i>, including those that had merely been written in the margins by former students. He was also highly proficient in the casting of numerous charms, such as being capable of casting the <a href="/wiki/Muffliato_Charm" title="Muffliato Charm">Muffliato Charm</a> with proper reflexes to prevent a student from harm when the <i>Book of Spells</i> conjured a greenhouse full of <a href="/wiki/Mandrake" title="Mandrake">Mandrakes</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-WBS_2-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-WBS-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Magizoology" title="Magizoology">Magizoology</a></b>: He could identify a number of <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magical</a> <a href="/wiki/Creature" title="Creature">creatures</a> conjured forth by <i>Book of Spells</i> on sight. He was also knowledgeable in dealing with them; for instance, he knew that a <a href="/wiki/Kelpie" title="Kelpie">Kelpie</a> can only be subdued by bridling it.<sup id="cite_ref-WBS_2-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-WBS-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Herbology" title="Herbology">Herbology</a></b>: He could also identify magical plants that <i>Book of Spells</i> spawned, such as <a href="/wiki/Mandrake" title="Mandrake">Mandrakes</a> and <a href="/wiki/Devil%27s_Snare" title="Devil&#39;s Snare">Devil's Snare</a>, as well as advise on how to deal with them.<sup id="cite_ref-WBS_2-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-WBS-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li><b>Logical thinking</b>: This wizard possessed a great deal of logical thinking skills, a trait rare in a wizard. He was able to quickly adapt to the challenges <i>Book of Spells</i> provided and give sufficient advice to guide the student reading the book through them.<sup id="cite_ref-WBS_2-10" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-WBS-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup></li></ul>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_male_Charms_Professor?veaction=edit&amp;section=5" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_male_Charms_Professor?action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>The Professor was voiced by <a href="/wiki/Rupert_Degas" title="Rupert Degas">Rupert Degas</a> in the English edition of <i><a href="/wiki/Wonderbook:_Book_of_Spells" title="Wonderbook: Book of Spells">Wonderbook: Book of Spells</a></i>.</li>
<li>His reaction to the player's permission note depends on their <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Houses" title="Hogwarts Houses">House</a>. For example, if the player's character a <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor" title="Gryffindor">Gryffindor</a>, he will say that hopes they know what they are doing, and if they are a&#160;<a href="/wiki/Slytherin" title="Slytherin">Slytherin</a>, he will assume their permission note is falsified, and that they forged the signature.</li></ul>
