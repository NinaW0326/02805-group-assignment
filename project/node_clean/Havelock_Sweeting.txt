<b>Havelock Sweeting</b> (<a href="/wiki/1634" class="mw-redirect" title="1634">1634</a>â€“<a href="/wiki/1710" class="mw-redirect" title="1710">1710</a>) was a <a href="/wiki/Great_Britain" title="Great Britain">British</a> <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> and <a href="/wiki/Magizoologist" title="Magizoologist">Magizoologist</a> well-known for being an expert on <a href="/wiki/Unicorn" title="Unicorn">Unicorns</a>. He started many <a href="/wiki/Unicorn_reservation" title="Unicorn reservation">Unicorn reservations</a> in <a href="/wiki/Great_Britain" title="Great Britain">Great Britain</a>. For his dedication to Unicorns, he was immortalised on a <a href="/wiki/Chocolate_Frog_Card" title="Chocolate Frog Card">Chocolate Frog Card</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-PSG_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PSG-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-COSG_1-10" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COSG-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
