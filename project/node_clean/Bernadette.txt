<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Bernadette?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Bernadette?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>After <a href="/wiki/Gellert_Grindelwald" title="Gellert Grindelwald">Gellert Grindelwald</a> (disguised as <a href="/wiki/Percival_Graves" title="Percival Graves">Percival Graves</a>) sentenced <a href="/wiki/Newton_Scamander" title="Newton Scamander">Newt Scamander</a> and <a href="/wiki/Porpentina_Goldstein" title="Porpentina Goldstein">Tina Goldstein</a> to death, Bernadette helped lead them to the <a href="/wiki/Death_Cell" title="Death Cell">Death cell</a>. She <a href="/wiki/Memory_extraction_spell" title="Memory extraction spell">extracted</a> Tina's memories and cast them in the <a href="/wiki/Death_potion" title="Death potion">death potion</a> and encouraged her to get in.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> Just as her fellow executioner was about to do the same to Newt, he broke free (his pet <a href="/wiki/Bowtruckle" title="Bowtruckle">Bowtruckle</a> having <a href="/wiki/Lock_picking" title="Lock picking">picked the lock</a> on his handcuffs) and unleashed his <a href="/wiki/Swooping_Evil" title="Swooping Evil">Swooping Evil</a> which knocked out the third executioner. The second executioner tried to use her wand on Newt, but he redirected her aim so that she hit Bernadette with a <a href="/wiki/Stunning_Spell" title="Stunning Spell">Stunning Spell</a> instead, rendering Bernadette unconcious. With no backup, the second executioner was then knocked out and Newt managed to save Tina from her execution, leaving Bernadette and her fellow executioners alone at the Death Cell.<sup id="cite_ref-FBS_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-FBS-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>In the <a href="/wiki/2010" title="2010">2010s</a>, Bernadette appeared during the <a href="/wiki/Calamity" title="Calamity">Calamity</a> in the <a href="/wiki/2010s" title="2010s">2010s</a> as a <a href="/wiki/Confoundable" title="Confoundable">Confoundable</a>, attempting to extract the memories of both Percival Graves and Newt Scamander. Volunteer members of the <a href="/wiki/Statute_of_Secrecy_Task_Force" title="Statute of Secrecy Task Force">Statute of Secrecy Task Force</a> could repel her by casting the <a href="/wiki/Ebublio_Jinx" title="Ebublio Jinx">Ebublio Jinx</a> on her, <a href="/wiki/Foundable" title="Foundable">returning</a> both wizards to their original timelines.<sup id="cite_ref-WU_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-WU-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Possessions">Possessions</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Bernadette?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Possessions">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Bernadette?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Possessions"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<div class="floatright"><a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/2/25/Bernadette%27s_wand.png/revision/latest?cb=20170124215549" class="image"><img alt="Bernadette&#39;s wand.png" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/2/25/Bernadette%27s_wand.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/250?cb=20170124215549" decoding="async" width="250" height="93" data-image-name="Bernadette&#39;s wand.png" data-image-key="Bernadette&#39;s_wand.png" /></a></div>
<ul><li><b><a href="/wiki/Bernadette%27s_wand" title="Bernadette&#39;s wand">Wand</a></b>: Bernadette's wand was of unknown length, wood, and core materials. It featured a carving of a head on the handle. The style and carvings of the wand may have indicated that it was a <a href="/wiki/Shikoba_Wolfe" title="Shikoba Wolfe">Shikoba Wolfe</a> wand, and therefore had a <a href="/wiki/Thunderbird" title="Thunderbird">Thunderbird</a> <a href="/wiki/Thunderbird_tail_feather" title="Thunderbird tail feather">feather</a> core. The wand was destroyed in the <a href="/wiki/Death_Cell" title="Death Cell">Death Cell</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Death_potion" title="Death potion">Death potion</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-FBS_1-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-FBS-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup></li></ul>
