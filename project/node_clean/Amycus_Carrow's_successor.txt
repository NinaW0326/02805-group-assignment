This <b>individual</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard or witch</a> who taught <a href="/wiki/Defence_Against_the_Dark_Arts" title="Defence Against the Dark Arts">Defence Against the Dark Arts</a> sometime after the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a> and the removal of the <a href="/wiki/Jinx_on_the_post_of_Defence_Against_the_Dark_Arts_teacher" title="Jinx on the post of Defence Against the Dark Arts teacher">jinx</a> that was placed on the post by <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Voldemort</a>. As such, Hogwarts was able to keep this good teacher in the post going forward. From time to time, the professor allowed <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> to give lectures on the subject, after Potter became the head of the <a href="/wiki/Auror_Office" title="Auror Office">Auror Department</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-AQ_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-AQ-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
