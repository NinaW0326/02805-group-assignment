<b>Primrose Jorkins</b> was the wife of <a href="/wiki/Albert_Jorkins" title="Albert Jorkins">Albert Jorkins</a>, and the mother of <a href="/wiki/Grimwold_Jorkins" title="Grimwold Jorkins">Grimwold</a>, <a href="/wiki/Granville_Jorkins" title="Granville Jorkins">Granville</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Griselda_Jorkins" title="Griselda Jorkins">Griselda Harmonia</a>. Along with her husband, she announced the birth of her youngest daughter, Griselda, in the <i><a href="/wiki/Daily_Prophet" title="Daily Prophet">Daily Prophet</a></i>.<sup id="cite_ref-DP_2-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DP-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
