<b>Mrs Leesk</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch</a> living in <a href="/wiki/New_York" title="New York">New York</a> city in <a href="/wiki/1926" title="1926">1926</a>. She placed a flyer in <a href="/wiki/The_Blind_Pig" title="The Blind Pig">the Blind Pig</a> advertising <a href="/wiki/Cat" title="Cat">cats</a> for sale, which directed those interested to send an <a href="/wiki/Owl" title="Owl">owl</a> to her home at <a href="/wiki/34_Mercer_Street" title="34 Mercer Street">34</a> <a href="/wiki/Mercer_Street" title="Mercer Street">Mercer Street</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-cb_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-cb-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
