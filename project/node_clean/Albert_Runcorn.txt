<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Albert_Runcorn?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Albert_Runcorn?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_life">Early life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Albert_Runcorn?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Early life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Albert_Runcorn?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Early life"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>It is unknown where Runcorn was born, but it is expected that he joined the Ministry of Magic and eventually attained a position of power.
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Death_Eater_control_of_the_Ministry">Death Eater control of the Ministry</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Albert_Runcorn?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Death Eater control of the Ministry">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Albert_Runcorn?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Death Eater control of the Ministry"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<figure class="thumb tleft show-info-icon" style="width: 252px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/e/eb/Runcorn23.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20150816121911" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/e/eb/Runcorn23.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/250?cb=20150816121911" decoding="async" width="250" height="104" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="Runcorn23.jpg" data-image-key="Runcorn23.jpg" /></a> 	 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:Runcorn23.jpg" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption">Albert Runcorn and Dolores Umbridge were two high-ranking members of the Ministry after it was taken over by Voldemort</p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure>
<p>After <a href="/wiki/Rufus_Scrimgeour" title="Rufus Scrimgeour">Rufus Scrimgeour</a> was murdered by Voldemort on <a href="/wiki/1_August" title="1 August">1 August</a>, <a href="/wiki/1997" title="1997">1997</a>, the Ministry of Magic <a href="/wiki/Fall_of_the_Ministry_of_Magic" title="Fall of the Ministry of Magic">fell</a> and become controlled by <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a> and his <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-DH8_4-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH8-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> During <a href="/wiki/Pius_Thicknesse" title="Pius Thicknesse">Pius Thicknesse</a>'s administration of the Ministry of Magic, Runcorn informed on Muggle-borns to the Ministry of Magic, subsequently leading to their arrest and <a href="/wiki/Azkaban" title="Azkaban">imprisonment</a>. It is uncertain whether he was actively in Voldemort's camp, or was simply making use of the upheaval to advance his own aims within the Ministry as <a href="/wiki/Dolores_Umbridge" title="Dolores Umbridge">Dolores Umbridge</a> was doing. He did not necessarily have to be a <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eater</a> in order to pose as one, nor to do their bidding. 
</p>
<h3><span id="Harry_Potter's_infiltration_of_the_Ministry"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="Harry_Potter.27s_infiltration_of_the_Ministry">Harry Potter's infiltration of the Ministry</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Albert_Runcorn?veaction=edit&amp;section=4" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Harry Potter&#039;s infiltration of the Ministry">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Albert_Runcorn?action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Harry Potter&#039;s infiltration of the Ministry"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<dl><dd><div class="noprint"><i>Main article&#58; <a href="/wiki/Infiltration_of_the_Ministry_of_Magic" title="Infiltration of the Ministry of Magic">Infiltration of the Ministry of Magic</a></i></div></dd></dl>
<figure class="thumb tright show-info-icon" style="width: 251px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/5/54/Infiltration.png/revision/latest?cb=20160812020055" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/5/54/Infiltration.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/249?cb=20160812020055" decoding="async" width="249" height="115" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="Infiltration.png" data-image-key="Infiltration.png" /></a> 	 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:Infiltration.png" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption">Harry (centre) disguised as Runcorn, with Hermione (left) as <a href="/wiki/Mafalda_Hopkirk" title="Mafalda Hopkirk">Mafalda Hopkirk</a> and Ron (right) as <a href="/wiki/Reginald_Cattermole" title="Reginald Cattermole">Reginald Cattermole</a></p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure>
<p>In <a href="/wiki/1997" title="1997">1997</a>, <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> impersonated Runcorn (who he learned was apparently quite senior in the Ministry) when he, <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione Granger</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ron Weasley</a> <a href="/wiki/Infiltration_of_the_Ministry_of_Magic" title="Infiltration of the Ministry of Magic">infiltrated the Ministry</a> in order to recover <a href="/wiki/Salazar_Slytherin%27s_Locket" class="mw-redirect" title="Salazar Slytherin&#39;s Locket">Salazar Slytherin's Locket</a>, one of Voldemort's <a href="/wiki/Horcrux" title="Horcrux">Horcruxes</a>. The <a href="/wiki/Polyjuice_Potion" title="Polyjuice Potion">Polyjuice Potion</a> used to achieve this impersonation was made with Runcorn's <a href="/wiki/Human_hair" title="Human hair">hair</a>. Harry became a fake Runcorn and used the disguise to gain access to the Ministry.<sup id="cite_ref-DH12_2-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH12-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>
Once in the ministry, a wizard who bumped into him in the <a href="/wiki/Atrium" title="Atrium">Atrium</a> apologised and rushed away, and the Minister for Magic, Pius Thicknesse, addressed him familiarly as "Albert." At one point, Harry, as Runcorn, was sharing an elevator with an unnamed wizard who apparently was applauding Runcorn's revelation of the Muggle ancestry of Dirk Cresswell, and trying to grease his way into the position that Dirk had been ousted from. Later, in another elevator, <a href="/wiki/Arthur_Weasley" title="Arthur Weasley">Arthur Weasley</a> told "Runcorn" that this revelation of Cresswell's background was the sort of thing that would get him (Runcorn) into trouble.<sup id="cite_ref-DH13_5-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH13-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup></p><figure class="thumb tleft show-info-icon" style="width: 252px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/9/9f/Potter_as_Runcorn_Patronus_Pottermore.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20150624175739" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/9/9f/Potter_as_Runcorn_Patronus_Pottermore.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/250?cb=20150624175739" decoding="async" width="250" height="112" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="Potter as Runcorn Patronus Pottermore.jpg" data-image-key="Potter_as_Runcorn_Patronus_Pottermore.jpg" /></a> 	 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:Potter_as_Runcorn_Patronus_Pottermore.jpg" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption">Harry Potter as Albert Runcorn at Muggle-Born Registration Commission hearing</p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure>
<p>Harry, disguised as Runcorn, with the assistance of Hermione Granger (disguised as <a href="/wiki/Mafalda_Hopkirk" title="Mafalda Hopkirk">Mafalda Hopkirk</a>) and Ron Weasley (disguised as <a href="/wiki/Reginald_Cattermole" title="Reginald Cattermole">Reginald Cattermole</a>), helped the Muggle-born witch <a href="/wiki/Mary_Cattermole" title="Mary Cattermole">Mary Cattermole</a> escape her trial before the Muggle-Born Registration Commission headed by Dolores Umbridge, as well as obtaining the locket and helping Mary and the real Reginald Cattermole escape with their children.<sup id="cite_ref-DH13_5-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH13-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>It is possible that he took part in the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a>, though it is unconfirmed.
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Later_Life">Later Life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Albert_Runcorn?veaction=edit&amp;section=5" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Later Life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Albert_Runcorn?action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Later Life"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>After <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a>'s rule of the Ministry fell and Dolores Umbridge was imprisoned, Runcorn may also have been sent to <a href="/wiki/Azkaban" title="Azkaban">Azkaban</a> for his crimes, though his fate is unknown.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Physical_appearance">Physical appearance</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Albert_Runcorn?veaction=edit&amp;section=6" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Physical appearance">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Albert_Runcorn?action=edit&amp;section=6" title="Edit section: Physical appearance"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Runcorn was described as more than six feet tall, and powerfully built, with a black beard and a gravelly voice. It was said that he was very intimidating to the likes of Reginald Cattermole.<sup id="cite_ref-DH12_2-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH12-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Personality_and_traits">Personality and traits</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Albert_Runcorn?veaction=edit&amp;section=7" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Personality and traits">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Albert_Runcorn?action=edit&amp;section=7" title="Edit section: Personality and traits"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Runcorn was very high in the Ministry and had apparently done things that were underhanded in order to achieve his rank. There were wizards who were frightened of him,<sup id="cite_ref-DH12_2-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH12-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> wizards who were toadying up to him to try and catch a ride on his coattails, wizards secure in their ancestry who were coldly furious at his tactics, and the upper echelons who treated him, apparently, as a near equal.<sup id="cite_ref-DH13_5-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH13-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Relationships">Relationships</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Albert_Runcorn?veaction=edit&amp;section=8" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Relationships">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Albert_Runcorn?action=edit&amp;section=8" title="Edit section: Relationships"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Arthur_Weasley">Arthur Weasley</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Albert_Runcorn?veaction=edit&amp;section=9" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Arthur Weasley">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Albert_Runcorn?action=edit&amp;section=9" title="Edit section: Arthur Weasley"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Runcorn and Arthur Weasley worked at the Ministry of Magic together, and did not get along very well. This was due to the fact that Mr Weasley did not approve of Runcorn's persecution of Muggle-borns.<sup id="cite_ref-DH13_5-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH13-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Dolores_Umbridge">Dolores Umbridge</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Albert_Runcorn?veaction=edit&amp;section=10" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Dolores Umbridge">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Albert_Runcorn?action=edit&amp;section=10" title="Edit section: Dolores Umbridge"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Dolores Umbridge and Runcorn were colleagues, working in close proximity with each other during the <a href="/wiki/Second_Wizarding_War" title="Second Wizarding War">Second Wizarding War</a>. Although Umbridge was Runcorn's superior, they got along quite well as they had similar opinions concerning Muggle-born witches and wizards. Despite this Harry doubted (while disguised as Runcorn) that he would be able to get away with interrupting a trial, suggesting that his power was somewhat limited.<sup id="cite_ref-DH13_5-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH13-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Corban_Yaxley">Corban Yaxley</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Albert_Runcorn?veaction=edit&amp;section=11" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Corban Yaxley">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Albert_Runcorn?action=edit&amp;section=11" title="Edit section: Corban Yaxley"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p><a href="/wiki/Corban_Yaxley" title="Corban Yaxley">Corban Yaxley</a>, a Death Eater, seemed to like or at least respect Runcorn for his role is persecuting muggle-borns in the Death Eater ruled Ministry.<sup id="cite_ref-DH13_5-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH13-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> This is especially notable as Yaxley was quite rude or cruel to most other employees, and even fellow Death Eaters on occasion.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Albert_Runcorn?veaction=edit&amp;section=12" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Albert_Runcorn?action=edit&amp;section=12" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>Albert is derived from the Germanic words <i>adal</i>, meaning "noble", and <i>beraht</i>, meaning "bright".</li>
<li><a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Runcorn" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Runcorn">Runcorn</a> is a town and cargo port within the borough of Halton in Cheshire, <a href="/wiki/England" title="England">England</a>.</li></ul>

<ul><li>The real Runcorn only appears twice in the film adaptation, once when <a href="/wiki/Pius_Thicknesse" title="Pius Thicknesse">Pius Thicknesse</a> gives a speech in the Atrium, and again before Harry transforms into him using the Polyjuice Potion. He has no speaking lines in the film.</li>
<li>Albert Runcorn is portrayed by <a href="/wiki/Scotland" title="Scotland">Scottish</a> actor <a href="/wiki/David_O%27Hara" title="David O&#39;Hara">David O'Hara</a> in <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Deathly_Hallows:_Part_1" title="Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 1">Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 1</a></i>.<sup id="cite_ref-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li>Runcorn sports a very light stubble in the film, but in the book, he has a full beard.</li>
<li>Albert's hair is described as being somewhat curly in the book, but in the film his hair is straight. It may have been in the films that the hair was so short it was unable to curl, as Runcorn was seen with a military-style "buzz cut".</li>
<li>The book says that Albert is more than 6 feet tall, yet he doesn't appear to be quite that tall in the <i>Deathly Hallows Part 1</i> film. Also Albert is clearly shorter than Reg Cattermole who is much shorter than him in the book.</li>
<li>The outfit which Runcorn wears in the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Deathly_Hallows:_Part_1" title="Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 1">film adaptation</a> of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Deathly_Hallows" title="Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows">Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows</a></i>, is in line with the totalitarian imagery of the film. The leather trench coat when combined with Runcorn's duties and demeanour give him the appearance of a Cheka or Gestapo operative.</li></ul>
