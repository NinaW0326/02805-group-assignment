<p>In <a href="/wiki/1927" title="1927">1927</a>, <a href="/wiki/Gunnar_Grimmson" title="Gunnar Grimmson">Gunnar Grimmson</a> was a bounty hunter, pursuing <a href="/wiki/Credence_Barebone" title="Credence Barebone">Credence Barebone</a> for a financial reward (but was actually working for <a href="/wiki/Gellert_Grindelwald" title="Gellert Grindelwald">Gellert Grindelwald</a> to help him bring Credence under his control).<sup id="cite_ref-pmn_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-pmn-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>The <a href="/wiki/Snatchers" title="Snatchers">Snatchers</a> that operated during <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a>'s control of the <a href="/wiki/Ministry_of_Magic" title="Ministry of Magic">Ministry of Magic</a> were a bounty hunter organisation, capturing those for which the <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a> were prejudiced against for financial gain.<sup id="cite_ref-DH19_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH19-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-DH23_4-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH23-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
