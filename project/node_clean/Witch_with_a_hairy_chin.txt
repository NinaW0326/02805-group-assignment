<p>The <b>witch with a hairy chin</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch</a>, who was the individual who banished the <a href="/wiki/Bandon_Banshee" title="Bandon Banshee">Bandon Banshee</a>. However, shortly after her deed, <a href="/wiki/Gilderoy_Lockhart" title="Gilderoy Lockhart">Gilderoy Lockhart</a> tracked her down, and tricked her into revealing in detail of how she defeated the banshee. After Lockhart got all the information he needed, he cast a <a href="/wiki/Memory_Charm" title="Memory Charm">Memory Charm</a> on the witch to erase her <a href="/wiki/Memory" title="Memory">memory</a> of the meeting and of her actions, and published the book <i><a href="/wiki/Break_with_a_Banshee" title="Break with a Banshee">Break with a Banshee</a></i> with all of the information under his own credentials.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
