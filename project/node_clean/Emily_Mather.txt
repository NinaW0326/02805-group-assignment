</p><p><b>Emily Mather</b><sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch</a> reported missing along with her relative, <a href="/wiki/Gabriella_Mather" title="Gabriella Mather">Gabriella</a>, on the <i><a href="/wiki/Potterwatch" title="Potterwatch">Potterwatch</a></i> radio programme in <a href="/wiki/1997" title="1997">1997</a>. Her disappearance was most likely linked to a <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eater</a> attack.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Emily_Mather?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Emily_Mather?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>This character is presumably a tribute to a relative of <a href="/wiki/James_Mather" title="James Mather">James Mather</a>, who worked as a supervising sound editor on every <i>Harry Potter</i> film from <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Order_of_the_Phoenix_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix (film)">Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix</a></i> onward.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li>She may be related to New England Mather family, of whom, Increase, who, along with his son was responsible for several writings and involvement in the Salem Witch Trials.<sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> Increase's father, Richard, was born in Lancashire, England.<sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup></li></ul>
