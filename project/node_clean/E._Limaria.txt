<b>E. Limaria</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch or wizard</a> who worked as a reporter for the <i><a href="/wiki/Daily_Prophet" title="Daily Prophet">Daily Prophet</a></i>. Limaria wrote an article titled "Underage Wizards Found With High Voltage Exploding Bon-Bons" for the paper in <a href="/wiki/1993" title="1993">1993</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-HPLE_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HPLE-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
