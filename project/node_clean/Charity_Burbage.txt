<p><b>Professor Charity Burbage</b> (d. <a href="/wiki/July" title="July">July</a>, <a href="/wiki/1997" title="1997">1997</a>) was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch</a> and the <a href="/wiki/Professor" title="Professor">Professor</a> of <a href="/wiki/Muggle_Studies" title="Muggle Studies">Muggle Studies</a> at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a>, after the departure of <a href="/wiki/Unidentified_male_Muggle_Studies_teacher" title="Unidentified male Muggle Studies teacher">the wizard</a> who formerly held the post. Her career lasted from <a href="/wiki/1993" title="1993">1993</a> to <a href="/wiki/1997" title="1997">1997</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-DH1_2-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH1-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>She believed that <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggles</a> were not so different from <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizards and witches</a>, and used this viewpoint in her teaching. It would eventually lead to her <a href="/wiki/Death" title="Death">death</a> as <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a> <a href="/wiki/Killing_Curse" title="Killing Curse">murdered</a> her personally for writing a <a href="/wiki/Daily_Prophet" title="Daily Prophet">Daily Prophet</a> article about her views during the <a href="/wiki/Second_Wizarding_War" title="Second Wizarding War">Second Wizarding War</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-DH1_2-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH1-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Charity_Burbage?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Charity_Burbage?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_life">Early life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Charity_Burbage?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Early life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Charity_Burbage?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Early life"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Charity was born to Mr and Mrs Burbage<sup id="cite_ref-marital_status_4-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-marital_status-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> somewhere in <a href="/wiki/Great_Britain" title="Great Britain">Great Britain</a>. Her parentage is unknown, but it is possible that she was <a href="/wiki/Half-blood" title="Half-blood">half-blood</a> or <a href="/wiki/Pure-blood" title="Pure-blood">pure-blood</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-Blood_3-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Blood-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Burbage probably studied magic at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a>. It is likely that she achieved high <a href="/wiki/Ordinary_Wizarding_Level" title="Ordinary Wizarding Level">O.W.L.</a> and <a href="/wiki/Nastily_Exhausting_Wizarding_Test" title="Nastily Exhausting Wizarding Test">N.E.W.T.</a> marks in <a href="/wiki/Muggle_Studies" title="Muggle Studies">Muggle Studies</a>, as she secured a job teaching the subject later.<sup id="cite_ref-DH1_2-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH1-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> She may have also studied <a href="/wiki/Muggle_Art" title="Muggle Art">Muggle Art</a> and <a href="/wiki/Muggle_Music" title="Muggle Music">Muggle Music</a>.
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Career_as_a_professor">Career as a professor</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Charity_Burbage?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Career as a professor">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Charity_Burbage?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Career as a professor"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<div class="quote">

<p>Burbage took up the post of <a href="/wiki/Muggle_Studies" title="Muggle Studies">Muggle Studies</a> <a href="/wiki/Professor" title="Professor">professor</a> after a male teacher left. Her office was located on the first floor (indicated as where Hermione took her Muggle Studies exam). It was full of Muggle toys and instruments, suggesting that it was possibly used for <a href="/wiki/Muggle_Music" title="Muggle Music">Muggle Music</a> classes as well. She also kept a <a href="/wiki/Lumos_Duo_spellbook" title="Lumos Duo spellbook">Lumos Duo spellbook</a> there.<sup id="cite_ref-POAG_7-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-POAG-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Her class was described as a "soft" option, as opposed to <a href="/wiki/Minerva_McGonagall" title="Minerva McGonagall">Minerva McGonagall</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Transfiguration_(class)" title="Transfiguration (class)">Transfiguration</a> classes. However, <a href="/wiki/Percy_Weasley" title="Percy Weasley">Percy Weasley</a> proposed that her classes were very useful for students who were planning careers in Muggle relations. She apparently gave quite a bit of homework, such as essays on why Muggles need <a href="/wiki/Electricity" title="Electricity">electricity</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-POA_8-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-POA-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Third-years <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione Granger</a> and <a href="/wiki/Ernest_Macmillan" title="Ernest Macmillan">Ernie Macmillan</a> both took her class and found her teaching fascinating. However, Hermione dropped the class at the end of the year, feeling a need to have an easier schedule.<sup id="cite_ref-POA_8-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-POA-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Death">Death</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Charity_Burbage?veaction=edit&amp;section=4" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Death">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Charity_Burbage?action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Death"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>

<p>During the summer holidays of <a href="/wiki/1997" title="1997">1997</a>, Burbage was abducted by <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a> and his <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a>. An official statement claimed that she had 'resigned,' although many who knew her were suspicious about her disappearance. She was suspended over a table at <a href="/wiki/Malfoy_Manor" title="Malfoy Manor">Malfoy Manor</a> while the Death Eaters were <a href="/wiki/Meeting_at_Malfoy_Manor" title="Meeting at Malfoy Manor">having a meeting there</a>. Voldemort accused her of polluting the minds of young witches and wizards with pro-muggle ideas. He vilified her Muggle-friendly beliefs, claiming that she wanted to encourage more magical people to "mate" with Muggles.<sup id="cite_ref-DH1_2-10" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH1-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="The_Calamity">The Calamity</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Charity_Burbage?veaction=edit&amp;section=6" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: The Calamity">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Charity_Burbage?action=edit&amp;section=6" title="Edit section: The Calamity"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<p>During the 2010s, Charity Burbage appeared as a <a href="/wiki/Foundable" title="Foundable">Foundable</a> during the <a href="/wiki/Calamity" title="Calamity">Calamity</a>. Volunteer members of the <a href="/wiki/Statute_of_Secrecy_Task_Force" title="Statute of Secrecy Task Force">Statute of Secrecy Task Force</a> were able to free her, allowing Charity to return to her original time.<sup id="cite_ref-WU_13-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-WU-13">&#91;13&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Personality_and_traits">Personality and traits</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Charity_Burbage?veaction=edit&amp;section=7" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Personality and traits">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Charity_Burbage?action=edit&amp;section=7" title="Edit section: Personality and traits"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<div class="quote">
<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows">"<i>Yes ... Professor Burbage taught the children of witches and wizards all about Muggles ... how they are not so different from us...</i>"</span></dd>
<dd>&#8212;Voldemort condemning Burbage's pro-Muggle views<sup class="noprint"><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Deathly_Hallows" title="Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows">[src]</a></sup></dd>
<dd></dd></dl></div>
<p>Charity Burbage held very strong and passionate views in favour of the rights of <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggles</a> and Muggle-borns. She held very tolerant attitudes towards Muggles, believing them to be fully equal to <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">Wizardkind</a> and that the decline of <a href="/wiki/Pure-blood" title="Pure-blood">pure-bloods</a> was beneficial to <a href="/wiki/Wizarding_world" title="Wizarding world">wizarding society</a>, most likely based on the belief that it would lead to greater acceptance of Muggles among wizards and the decline of <a href="/wiki/Pure-blood_supremacy" title="Pure-blood supremacy">pure-blood supremacy</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-DH1_2-12" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH1-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> She also had a highly deep and thorough knowledge of the <a href="/wiki/Muggle_world" title="Muggle world">Muggle world</a> in general, including Muggle ways of living, <a href="/wiki/Science" title="Science">science</a> and technology. She also appeared to be a highly competent and talented teacher of her subject <a href="/wiki/Muggle_Studies" title="Muggle Studies">Muggle Studies</a> whilst she was a <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts</a> <a href="/wiki/Professor" title="Professor">Professor</a>, as several students described her lessons as being fascinating, showing her to have been very devoted to her career and an expert in her field.<sup id="cite_ref-POA_8-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-POA-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Magical_abilities_and_skills">Magical abilities and skills</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Charity_Burbage?veaction=edit&amp;section=8" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Charity_Burbage?action=edit&amp;section=8" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li><b><a href="/wiki/Muggle_Studies" title="Muggle Studies">Muggle Studies</a></b>: Charity had immense knowledge of <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggles</a> and their ways of living considering that she was the professor of Muggle Studies at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a>. She incorporated her highly advanced and sophisticated knowledge of Muggles and their ways into her teaching philosophy of promoting tolerance and understanding between Muggles and <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">Wizardkind</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-DH1_2-13" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH1-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Charm" title="Charm">Charms</a></b>: Charity could produce a non-corporeal <a href="/wiki/Patronus_Charm" title="Patronus Charm">Patronus</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-LEGOY2_6-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-LEGOY2-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup> a mark of superior <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magical</a> ability given the immense difficulty of performing the highly advanced protective charm.</li></ul>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Charity_Burbage?veaction=edit&amp;section=11" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Charity_Burbage?action=edit&amp;section=11" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>'Charity' is from the English word <i>charity</i>, ultimately derived from Late Latin <i>caritas</i> meaning "generous love", from Latin <i>carus</i> 'dear, beloved.' Caritas was in use as a Roman Christian name. The English name Charity came into use among the Puritans after the Protestant Reformation.
</p>

<ul><li>Burbage is portrayed by <a href="/wiki/Great_Britain" title="Great Britain">British</a> actress <a href="/wiki/Carolyn_Pickles" title="Carolyn Pickles">Carolyn Pickles</a> in <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Deathly_Hallows:_Part_1" title="Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 1">Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 1</a></i>.<sup id="cite_ref-15" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-15">&#91;15&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li>Charity says the same last words that Albus Dumbledore did, except she adds another 'please'.</li>
<li>One of Charity Burbage's predecessors and her immediate successor were professors who served <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a>, though <a href="/wiki/Quirinus_Quirrell" title="Quirinus Quirrell">Professor Quirrell</a> only fell to Voldemort during his year off after leaving the job; she herself was murdered by Lord Voldemort.</li>
<li>In <i><a href="/wiki/LEGO_Harry_Potter:_Years_5-7" title="LEGO Harry Potter: Years 5-7">LEGO Harry Potter: Years 5-7</a></i>, she has grey hair and she appears to be older.</li></ul>
