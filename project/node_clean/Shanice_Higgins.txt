<p><b>Shanice Higgins</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Jamaica" title="Jamaica">Jamaican</a> <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch</a> who played as the <a href="/wiki/Seeker" title="Seeker">Seeker</a> for the <a href="/wiki/2014" title="2014">2014</a> <a href="/wiki/Jamaican_National_Quidditch_team" title="Jamaican National Quidditch team">Jamaican National Quidditch team</a>.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Shanice_Higgins?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Shanice_Higgins?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>During the fourth match of the <a href="/wiki/2014_Quidditch_World_Cup" title="2014 Quidditch World Cup">427th Quidditch World Cup</a>, <a href="/wiki/American_National_Quidditch_team" title="American National Quidditch team">USA</a> versus Jamaica, she was beaten to the <a href="/wiki/Golden_Snitch" title="Golden Snitch">Snitch</a> by US Seeker <a href="/wiki/Darius_Smackhammer" title="Darius Smackhammer">Darius Smackhammer</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-usa-v-jam_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-usa-v-jam-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> resulting in a narrow victory for the United States.<sup id="cite_ref-usa-v-jam2_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-usa-v-jam2-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
