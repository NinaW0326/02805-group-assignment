This <b>individual</b> was a witch or wizard who began attending <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> in the late <a href="/wiki/1970s" title="1970s">1970s</a> or early-mid-<a href="/wiki/1980s" title="1980s">1980s</a> and was Sorted into <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor" title="Gryffindor">Gryffindor</a>. By the <a href="/wiki/1985%E2%80%931986_school_year" title="1985–1986 school year">1985–1986 school year</a>, he or she had been made <a href="/wiki/Keeper" title="Keeper">Keeper</a> of the <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor_Quidditch_team" title="Gryffindor Quidditch team">Gryffindor Quidditch team</a>, and was known for misplacing the <a href="/wiki/Broom_Wax" title="Broom Wax">Broom Wax</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
