<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Poppy_Pomfrey%27s_Death_Eater_Opponent?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Poppy_Pomfrey%27s_Death_Eater_Opponent?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>At some point in her life, this <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch</a> became learned in the <a href="/wiki/Dark_Arts" title="Dark Arts">Dark Arts</a> and joined the Death Eaters, becoming a loyal follower and servant of <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a>. It is likely that she fought during the <a href="/wiki/First_Wizarding_War" title="First Wizarding War">First Wizarding War</a>, as most of her fellow Death Eaters did so.
</p><p>She was seen attending the <a href="/wiki/Meeting_at_Malfoy_Manor" title="Meeting at Malfoy Manor">meeting</a> at <a href="/wiki/Malfoy_Manor" title="Malfoy Manor">Malfoy Manor</a> in <a href="/wiki/1997" title="1997">1997</a>, in which Voldemort and the Death Eaters discussed the most efficient way of killing <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-DHF1_2-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DHF1-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<figure class="thumb tright show-info-icon" style="width: 242px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/b/ba/Female_Death_Eater_disarming_George.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20111116231504" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/b/ba/Female_Death_Eater_disarming_George.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/240?cb=20111116231504" decoding="async" width="240" height="102" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="Female Death Eater disarming George.jpg" data-image-key="Female_Death_Eater_disarming_George.jpg" /></a> 	 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:Female_Death_Eater_disarming_George.jpg" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption">The female <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eater</a> listening to Voldemort's speech after disarming <a href="/wiki/George_Weasley" title="George Weasley">George Weasley</a></p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure>
<p>Much later on <a href="/wiki/2_May" title="2 May">2 May</a> <a href="/wiki/1998" title="1998">1998</a>, she fought in the final battle of the Second War, the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a> alongside her fellows and Voldemort himself. During the battle she duelled <a href="/wiki/George_Weasley" title="George Weasley">George Weasley</a> and possibly others, before Voldemort ordered a temporary ceasefire. She was seen with the other Death Eaters in the <a href="/wiki/Forbidden_Forest" title="Forbidden Forest">Forbidden Forest</a> where she witnessed Voldemort attempting to kill Harry Potter with a <a href="/wiki/Killing_Curse" title="Killing Curse">Killing Curse</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-DHF2_4-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DHF2-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Instead of abandoning the battle like the <a href="/wiki/Malfoy_family" title="Malfoy family">Malfoys</a> or some other <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a>, she was fighting loyal to Voldemort alongside several members of the <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a>, showing her to have been one of the more loyal Death Eaters and possibly one of the more sadistic ones. She was was last seen fighting against <a href="/wiki/Poppy_Pomfrey" title="Poppy Pomfrey">Poppy Pomfrey</a> during the <a href="/wiki/Skirmish_at_the_Great_Hall" title="Skirmish at the Great Hall">Skirmish at the Great Hall</a>, moments before <a href="/wiki/Molly_Weasley" title="Molly Weasley">Molly Weasley</a> and <a href="/wiki/Bellatrix_Lestrange" title="Bellatrix Lestrange">Bellatrix Lestrange</a>'s duel started. Given how she fell down after Pomfrey lunged forward at her, she lost the duel to her opponent.<sup id="cite_ref-DHF2_4-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DHF2-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>After <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Voldemort</a>'s defeat and <a href="/wiki/Death" title="Death">death</a>, she might have been killed in battle, or arrested, tried and imprisoned in <a href="/wiki/Azkaban" title="Azkaban">Azkaban</a> for her crimes as a Death Eater.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Magical_abilities_and_skills">Magical abilities and skills</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Poppy_Pomfrey%27s_Death_Eater_Opponent?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Poppy_Pomfrey%27s_Death_Eater_Opponent?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li><b><a href="/wiki/Dark_Arts" title="Dark Arts">Dark Arts</a></b>: Being a Death Eater, this witch would have been accomplished with dark magic, likely including the casting of the <a href="/wiki/Unforgivable_Curses" title="Unforgivable Curses">Unforgivable Curses</a>.</li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Duelling" title="Duelling">Duelling</a></b>: This witch fought in the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a>, being one of the last Death Eaters fighting before Voldemort's defeat. During the battle she was able to defeat <a href="/wiki/George_Weasley" title="George Weasley">George Weasley</a> and fought against <a href="/wiki/Poppy_Pomfrey" title="Poppy Pomfrey">Poppy Pomfrey</a>, though she was unable to defeat the matron.<sup id="cite_ref-DHF2_4-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DHF2-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Charm" title="Charm">Charms</a></b>: She has some skill in the field of charms, as seen by her successful use of the <a href="/wiki/Disarming_Charm" title="Disarming Charm">Disarming Charm</a> against <a href="/wiki/George_Weasley" title="George Weasley">George Weasley</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-DHF2_4-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DHF2-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup></li></ul>

<ul><li>This individual was played by <a href="/wiki/Great_Britain" title="Great Britain">British</a> actress <a href="/wiki/Penelope_McGhie" title="Penelope McGhie">Penelope McGhie</a>.</li>
<li>This Death Eater is only seen in the first and second <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Deathly_Hallows_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows (film)">film adaptations</a> of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Deathly_Hallows" title="Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows">Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows</a></i>, during which she is shown at the meeting at Malfoy Manor and battle of Hogwarts during which she fights against the <a href="/wiki/Order_of_the_Phoenix" title="Order of the Phoenix">Order of the Phoenix</a> and the staff of <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">battle</a> for the school.</li>
<li>The <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eater</a> is one of only five known female Death Eaters.</li></ul>
