<b>Mrs Pettigrew</b> (fl. <a href="/wiki/1981" title="1981">1981</a>) was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch</a> and the mother of <a href="/wiki/Peter_Pettigrew" title="Peter Pettigrew">Peter Pettigrew</a>. Following her son's alleged death at the hands of <a href="/wiki/Sirius_Black" title="Sirius Black">Sirius Black</a> in <a href="/wiki/1981" title="1981">1981</a>, she was given the <a href="/wiki/Order_of_Merlin" title="Order of Merlin">Order of Merlin</a> that was awarded to him posthumously. It is known that the largest piece of <a href="/wiki/Peter_Pettigrew" title="Peter Pettigrew">Peter Pettigrew</a> she received was his index finger.<sup id="cite_ref-POA19_1-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-POA19-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Peter_Pettigrew">Peter Pettigrew</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Peter_Pettigrew%27s_mother?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Peter Pettigrew">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Peter_Pettigrew%27s_mother?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Peter Pettigrew"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Little is known about the relationship between mother and son. The only thing Mrs Pettigrew received after her son faked his <a href="/wiki/Death" title="Death">death</a> the first time was his index finger. She also received his <a href="/wiki/Order_of_Merlin" title="Order of Merlin">Order of Merlin</a>, First Class, for standing up to <a href="/wiki/Sirius_Black" title="Sirius Black">Sirius Black</a> (who was actually framed).<sup id="cite_ref-POA19_1-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-POA19-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> It's unknown if she lived long enough to find the truth when it was exposed.
</p>
