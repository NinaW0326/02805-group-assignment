<b>Clarisse Lestrange</b> (née <b>Tremblay</b>) was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch</a> and a member of the <a href="/wiki/Pure-blood" title="Pure-blood">pure-blood</a> <a href="/wiki/Lestrange_family" title="Lestrange family">Lestrange family</a>. She was the daughter of <a href="/wiki/Bernard_Tremblay" title="Bernard Tremblay">Bernard Tremblay</a> and <a href="/wiki/Leonie_Tremblay" title="Leonie Tremblay">Leonie Lestrange</a>, and the sister of <a href="/wiki/Falco_Tremblay" title="Falco Tremblay">Falco</a>. She married <a href="/wiki/Corvus_Lestrange_(IV)" title="Corvus Lestrange (IV)">Corvus Lestrange</a> and had a son with him, <a href="/wiki/Corvus_Lestrange_(V)" title="Corvus Lestrange (V)">Corvus</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-FB88_4-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-FB88-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-FB96_5-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-FB96-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-COGteaser_6-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COGteaser-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Clarisse_Lestrange?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Clarisse_Lestrange?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Clarisse Tremblay was the second wife of <a href="/wiki/Corvus_Lestrange_(IV)" title="Corvus Lestrange (IV)">Corvus Lestrange</a>, her first cousin.<sup id="cite_ref-COGteaser_6-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COGteaser-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup> She married him after his first wife, Laurena, had died in childbirth. In <a href="/wiki/1901" title="1901">1901</a>, at the age of at least twenty one, she gave birth to a son, named Corvus after his father.<sup id="cite_ref-COGteaser_6-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COGteaser-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup> At some point thereafter, her son was separated from his family.<sup id="cite_ref-FB88_4-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-FB88-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-FB96_5-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-FB96-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> Her later fate is unknown.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Clarisse_Lestrange?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Clarisse_Lestrange?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>The name Clarisse stems from the Latin word "clarus", meaning "bright or clear."</li>
<li>Tremblay is a topographical name that stems from the Old French word "tremblaie", meaning "aspen" or "a place planted with aspens."</li></ul>
