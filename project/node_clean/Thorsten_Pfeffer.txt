&#160;
<b>Thorsten Pfeffer</b> was a German wizard who played as the <a href="/wiki/Seeker" title="Seeker">Seeker</a> for the <a href="/wiki/2014" title="2014">2014</a>&#160;<a href="/wiki/German_National_Quidditch_team" title="German National Quidditch team">German National Quidditch team</a>.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Thorsten_Pfeffer?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Thorsten_Pfeffer?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>During their match against <a href="/wiki/Welsh_National_Quidditch_team" title="Welsh National Quidditch team">Wales</a>, he attempted the life-threatening <a href="/wiki/Wronski_Feint" title="Wronski Feint">Wronski Feint</a>. However, he failed to pull out in time and collided with the ground at what onlookers estimated to be sixty miles an hour. <a href="/wiki/Healer" title="Healer">Healers</a>&#160;immediately flooded the <a href="/wiki/Quidditch_pitch" title="Quidditch pitch">Pitch</a> and administered <a href="/wiki/Skele-Gro" title="Skele-Gro">Skele-Gro</a>. According to the team manager <a href="/wiki/Franziska_Faust" title="Franziska Faust">Franziska Faust</a>, Pfeffer was likely to make a complete recovery, though he has broken most of his bones and currently believes himself to be a <a href="/wiki/Budgerigar" title="Budgerigar">budgerigar</a> called Klaus.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
