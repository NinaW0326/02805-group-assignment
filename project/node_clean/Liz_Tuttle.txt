<b>Liz "Lizard" Tuttle</b><sup id="cite_ref-Polyjuice_5-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Polyjuice-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch</a> who began attending <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> in <a href="/wiki/1984" title="1984">1984</a> and was Sorted into <a href="/wiki/Slytherin" title="Slytherin">Slytherin</a>. She chose <a href="/wiki/Care_of_Magical_Creatures" title="Care of Magical Creatures">Care of Magical Creatures</a> as one of her electives.<sup id="cite_ref-comcFireCrab_6-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-comcFireCrab-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup>

<h3><span id="Hogwarts_years_(1984-1991)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="Hogwarts_years_.281984-1991.29">Hogwarts years <span class="template-c">(1984-1991)</span></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Liz_Tuttle?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Hogwarts years (1984-1991)">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Liz_Tuttle?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Hogwarts years (1984-1991)"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Liz began attending <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> on <a href="/wiki/1_September" title="1 September">1st of September</a> <a href="/wiki/1984" title="1984">1984</a> and was sorted into <a href="/wiki/Slytherin" title="Slytherin">Slytherin</a>. Liz owned a male <a href="/wiki/Frog" title="Frog">frog</a> named <a href="/wiki/Sir_Ribbithe" title="Sir Ribbithe">Sir Ribbithe</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-Y6Ch5_7-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Y6Ch5-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Third_year">Third year</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Liz_Tuttle?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Third year">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Liz_Tuttle?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Third year"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>

<p>During her third year, Liz took <a href="/wiki/Care_of_Magical_Creatures" title="Care of Magical Creatures">Care of Magical Creatures</a> and <a href="/wiki/Divination_(class)" title="Divination (class)">Divination</a> as electives.<sup id="cite_ref-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup> She befriended <a href="/wiki/Hufflepuff" title="Hufflepuff">Hufflepuff</a> <a href="/wiki/Penny_Haywood" title="Penny Haywood">Penny Haywood</a> at some point during her year, before meeting <a href="/wiki/Jacob%27s_sibling" title="Jacob&#39;s sibling">a friend of Penny's</a>, who was looking for ingredients for <a href="/wiki/Polyjuice_Potion" title="Polyjuice Potion">Polyjuice Potion</a>. Liz pointed her wand at the person and demanded them to choose between the <a href="/wiki/Billywig" title="Billywig">Billywig</a>, <a href="/wiki/Bowtruckle" title="Bowtruckle">Bowtruckle</a> and <a href="/wiki/Bundimun" title="Bundimun">Bundimun</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-Polyjuice_5-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Polyjuice-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Fourth_year">Fourth year</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Liz_Tuttle?veaction=edit&amp;section=4" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Fourth year">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Liz_Tuttle?action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Fourth year"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<p>In the first <a href="/wiki/Care_of_Magical_Creatures" title="Care of Magical Creatures">Care of Magical Creatures</a> class during the <a href="/wiki/1987%E2%80%931988_school_year" title="1987–1988 school year">1987–1988 school year</a>, aside from <a href="/wiki/Barnaby_Lee" title="Barnaby Lee">Barnaby Lee</a>, Liz appeared to be one of the friendlier Slytherin students, who showed genuine interest in the course. She exclaimed to not having seen anything as beautiful as a <a href="/wiki/Fire_crab" class="mw-redirect" title="Fire crab">Fire Crab</a> shooting <a href="/wiki/Fire" title="Fire">flame</a> out its rear.<sup id="cite_ref-comcFireCrab_6-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-comcFireCrab-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>She later mentioned during another Care of Magical Creatures class that she had attended a luau in <a href="/wiki/Fiji" title="Fiji">Fiji</a> where Fire Crabs were used.<sup id="cite_ref-comc411_9-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-comc411-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Fifth_year">Fifth year</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Liz_Tuttle?veaction=edit&amp;section=5" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Fifth year">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Liz_Tuttle?action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Fifth year"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<p>When <a href="/wiki/Jacob%27s_sibling" title="Jacob&#39;s sibling">Jacob's sibling</a> went to talk to the <a href="/wiki/Bloody_Baron" title="Bloody Baron">Bloody Baron</a> to ask about <a href="/wiki/Duncan_Ashe" title="Duncan Ashe">Duncan Ashe</a>, Liz joined them along with fellow Slytherins <a href="/wiki/Barnaby_Lee" title="Barnaby Lee">Barnaby Lee</a>, <a href="/wiki/Merula_Snyde" title="Merula Snyde">Merula Snyde</a> and <a href="/wiki/Ismelda_Murk" title="Ismelda Murk">Ismelda Murk</a>. Liz thought Barnaby's jokes involving the word "bloody" were funny.<sup id="cite_ref-Y5Ch6_10-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Y5Ch6-10">&#91;10&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Sixth_year">Sixth year</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Liz_Tuttle?veaction=edit&amp;section=6" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Sixth year">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Liz_Tuttle?action=edit&amp;section=6" title="Edit section: Sixth year"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<p>During the <a href="/wiki/1989%E2%80%931990_school_year" title="1989–1990 school year">1989–1990 school year</a>, her <a href="/wiki/Sixth_year" title="Sixth year">sixth year</a>, Liz proceeded to take <a href="/wiki/Nastily_Exhausting_Wizarding_Test" title="Nastily Exhausting Wizarding Test">N.E.W.T.</a> level <a href="/wiki/Potion" title="Potion">Potions</a>, <a href="/wiki/History_of_Magic" title="History of Magic">History of Magic</a>, <a href="/wiki/Care_of_Magical_Creatures" title="Care of Magical Creatures">Care of Magical Creatures</a> and <a href="/wiki/Divination" title="Divination">Divination</a>.
</p><p>Sometime during the year, she became a founding member of the <a href="/wiki/Circle_of_Khanna" title="Circle of Khanna">Circle of Khanna</a>, a group devoted to avenging <a href="/wiki/Rowan_Khanna" title="Rowan Khanna">Rowan Khanna</a> and locating the final <a href="/wiki/Cursed_Vaults" title="Cursed Vaults">cursed vault</a>. It was at the groups first meeting in the <a href="/wiki/Hog%27s_Head_Inn" title="Hog&#39;s Head Inn">Hog's Head Inn</a> that she learned of the malevolent organisation "<a href="/wiki/R" title="R">R</a>" and itss <a href="/wiki/Plot_to_find_the_Cursed_Vaults" title="Plot to find the Cursed Vaults">motives</a> to locate the final Cursed Vault before them.<sup id="cite_ref-hm20_4-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-hm20-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Personality_and_traits">Personality and traits</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Liz_Tuttle?veaction=edit&amp;section=7" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Personality and traits">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Liz_Tuttle?action=edit&amp;section=7" title="Edit section: Personality and traits"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>She was socially awkward and had difficulty with befriending others, and most other students viewed her as weird. She loved <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magical</a> <a href="/wiki/Creature" title="Creature">creatures</a> and had the desire to become a <a href="/wiki/Magizoologist" title="Magizoologist">Magizoologist</a> like <a href="/wiki/Newton_Scamander" title="Newton Scamander">Newt Scamander</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-Polyjuice_5-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Polyjuice-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> She seemed to care more about the well-being of creatures than humans, as she feared the <a href="/wiki/Blood" title="Blood">blood</a> on the <a href="/wiki/Bloody_Baron" title="Bloody Baron">Bloody Baron</a> was the blood of an animal.<sup id="cite_ref-Y5Ch6_10-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Y5Ch6-10">&#91;10&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Magical_abilities_and_skills">Magical abilities and skills</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Liz_Tuttle?veaction=edit&amp;section=8" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Liz_Tuttle?action=edit&amp;section=8" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li><b><a href="/wiki/Potion" title="Potion">Potions</a></b>: Liz achieved an '<a href="/wiki/Outstanding" title="Outstanding">Outstanding</a>' in her O.W.L. exam, as she was able to advance to the N.E.W.T.-level Potions class taught by <a href="/wiki/Severus_Snape" title="Severus Snape">Severus Snape</a>, showing considerable talent in this field.</li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Care_of_Magical_Creatures" title="Care of Magical Creatures">Care of Magical Creatures</a></b>: From an early age, Liz was highly passionate about <a href="/wiki/Magizoology" title="Magizoology">Magizoology</a> and started studying Care of Magical Creatures from her <a href="/wiki/Third_year" title="Third year">third year</a> onward. She later earned either an "<a href="/wiki/Exceeds_Expectations" title="Exceeds Expectations">Exceeds Expectations</a>" or "<a href="/wiki/Outstanding" title="Outstanding">Outstanding</a>" grade in her <a href="/wiki/Ordinary_Wizarding_Level" title="Ordinary Wizarding Level">O.W.L.</a>, as she studied the subject at <a href="/wiki/Nastily_Exhausting_Wizarding_Test" title="Nastily Exhausting Wizarding Test">N.E.W.T.</a>, showing considerable proficiency in this field.</li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Divination" title="Divination">Divination</a></b>: Whilst not being a natural born <a href="/wiki/Seer" title="Seer">Seer</a> like her <a href="/wiki/Liz_Tuttle%27s_mother" title="Liz Tuttle&#39;s mother">mother</a>, Liz was still talented in the art of Divination in her own right, as she earned a high enough grade at O.W.L. in the <a href="/wiki/Divination_(class)" title="Divination (class)">subject</a> which allowed her to study it at N.E.W.T. level.</li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/History_of_Magic" title="History of Magic">History of Magic</a></b>: Liz was knowledgeable and proficient in the study of magical history, as she was able to study the subject at N.E.W.T. level.</li></ul>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Liz_Tuttle?veaction=edit&amp;section=9" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Liz_Tuttle?action=edit&amp;section=9" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>In <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter:_Hogwarts_Mystery" title="Harry Potter: Hogwarts Mystery">Harry Potter: Hogwarts Mystery</a></i>, players who advanced into Year 4 prior to <a href="/wiki/12_July" title="12 July">12 July</a>, <a href="/wiki/2018" title="2018">2018‎</a>, would first meet Liz in Year 4; this is due to the belated released Side Quest "Polyjuice Potion," which actually chronologically happened in Year 3.<sup id="cite_ref-11" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-11">&#91;11&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li>In <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter:_Hogwarts_Mystery" title="Harry Potter: Hogwarts Mystery">Harry Potter: Hogwarts Mystery</a></i>, during the "Search for Odour" part of the Care of Magical Creatures class, when clicking "Sniff Liz," the speech bubble "Lizard smells like lake water and hay" would appear; indicating either her full first name or nickname is "Lizard," or it is a typo.<sup id="cite_ref-12" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-12">&#91;12&#93;</a></sup> It was later revealed that Lizard was in fact her nickname.<sup id="cite_ref-Polyjuice_5-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Polyjuice-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li>Liz Tuttle was used in the Rotate Puzzle game on Novel Games on 13 July 2019.</li>
<li>During the Talk to Liz section of Thestral Care of Magical Creatures classes, Liz can mention wanting to request meat-free sandwiches for meals, which suggests she might be a vegetarian or vegan.</li></ul>
