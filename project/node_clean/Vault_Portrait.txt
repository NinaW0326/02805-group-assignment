<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="History">History</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Vault_Portrait?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: History">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Vault_Portrait?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: History"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>In the <a href="/wiki/1988%E2%80%931989_school_year" title="1988–1989 school year">1988–1989 school year</a>, it was stolen by <a href="/wiki/Peeves" title="Peeves">Peeves</a>, who gave it to <a href="/wiki/Jacob%27s_sibling" title="Jacob&#39;s sibling">Jacob's sibling</a> after they caused chaos at Hogwarts.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p><a href="/wiki/Patricia_Rakepick" title="Patricia Rakepick">Patricia Rakepick</a> later inspected it, and told her student <a href="/wiki/Curse_Breaker" class="mw-redirect" title="Curse Breaker">Curse Breaker</a> team to prepare to face a <a href="/wiki/Dragon" title="Dragon">dragon</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Later that year, the Vault Portrait was used by Jacob's sibling, Patricia Rakepick, <a href="/wiki/William_Weasley" title="William Weasley">William Weasley</a>, <a href="/wiki/Ben_Copper" title="Ben Copper">Ben Copper</a>, <a href="/wiki/Merula_Snyde" title="Merula Snyde">Merula Snyde</a>, <a href="/wiki/Charles_Weasley" title="Charles Weasley">Charles Weasley</a> or <a href="/wiki/Penny_Haywood" title="Penny Haywood">Penny Haywood</a> in order to get into the <a href="/wiki/Buried_Vault" title="Buried Vault">Portrait Vault</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
