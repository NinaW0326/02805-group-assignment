<p>This <b>Professor</b> (d. <a href="/wiki/2_May" title="2 May">2 May</a>, <a href="/wiki/1998" title="1998">1998</a><sup id="cite_ref-DHP2_2-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DHP2-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>) was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch</a> who taught at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a>, having possibly once been a student there as a member of <a href="/wiki/Horace_Slughorn" title="Horace Slughorn">Horace Slughorn</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Slug_Club" title="Slug Club">Slug Club</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP_8-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>In <a href="/wiki/1994" title="1994">1994</a>, she attended the <a href="/wiki/Welcoming_Feast" title="Welcoming Feast">Welcoming Feast</a> held for the arrival of <a href="/wiki/Beauxbatons_Academy_of_Magic" title="Beauxbatons Academy of Magic">Beauxbatons</a> and <a href="/wiki/Durmstrang_Institute" title="Durmstrang Institute">Durmstrang</a>, as well as the <a href="/wiki/Yule_Ball" title="Yule Ball">Yule Ball</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-GOF_6-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup> Her lessons were presumably evaluated by <a href="/wiki/Dolores_Umbridge" title="Dolores Umbridge">Dolores Umbridge</a> the following year, during which she sat by <a href="/wiki/Sybill_Trelawney" title="Sybill Trelawney">Sybill Trelawney</a> at the <a href="/wiki/Start-of-Term_Feast" title="Start-of-Term Feast">Start-of-Term Feast</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-OTP_7-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OTP-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup> She later attended the <a href="/wiki/Slug_Club_Christmas_party" title="Slug Club Christmas party">Slug Club Christmas party</a> in <a href="/wiki/1996" title="1996">1996</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP_8-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>After fighting in the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a>, the Professor was killed by an unknown <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eater</a>, <a href="/wiki/Snatchers" title="Snatchers">Snatcher</a>, or other servant of <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Voldemort</a>. Trelawney and <a href="/wiki/Padma_Patil" title="Padma Patil">Padma Patil</a> apparently brought her body in.<sup id="cite_ref-DHP2_2-10" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DHP2-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_life">Early life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_female_Hogwarts_teacher?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Early life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_female_Hogwarts_teacher?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Early life"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>This witch was born no later than <a href="/wiki/1977" title="1977">1977</a><sup id="cite_ref-Birth_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Birth-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> somewhere in <a href="/wiki/Great_Britain" title="Great Britain">Great Britain</a> or <a href="/wiki/Ireland" title="Ireland">Ireland</a> to at least one parent of <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizardkind</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-Blood_3-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Blood-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>It is possible that she attended <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> as a member of <a href="/wiki/Potions_Master" title="Potions Master">Potions Master</a> <a href="/wiki/Horace_Slughorn" title="Horace Slughorn">Horace Slughorn</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Slug_Club" title="Slug Club">Slug Club</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP_8-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup> She presumably attained high marks in one particular <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_subjects" title="Hogwarts subjects">subject</a>.
</p><p>After leaving school, she got married.<sup id="cite_ref-Married_4-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Married-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> She was married by <a href="/wiki/September" title="September">September</a>, <a href="/wiki/1994" title="1994">1994</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-GOF_6-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup>.
</p>

<p>The Professor was teaching at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> by <a href="/wiki/1994" title="1994">1994</a>. She attended the <a href="/wiki/Start-of-Term_Feast" title="Start-of-Term Feast">Start-of-Term Feast</a> at the beginning of the <a href="/wiki/1994%E2%80%931995_school_year" title="1994–1995 school year">1994–1995 school year</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-GOF_6-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Later, the Professor watched the delegations from <a href="/wiki/Beauxbatons_Academy_of_Magic" title="Beauxbatons Academy of Magic">Beauxbatons Academy of Magic</a> and <a href="/wiki/Durmstrang_Institute" title="Durmstrang Institute">Durmstrang Institute</a> arrive at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Castle" title="Hogwarts Castle">Hogwarts Castle</a> for the <a href="/wiki/Triwizard_Tournament" title="Triwizard Tournament">Triwizard Tournament</a>. After they arrived, she and the rest of the staff made their way to the <a href="/wiki/Great_Hall" title="Great Hall">Great Hall</a> for the <a href="/wiki/Welcoming_Feast" title="Welcoming Feast">Welcoming Feast</a>, during which she spent some of her time writing in <a href="/wiki/Unidentified_female_Hogwarts_teacher%27s_book" title="Unidentified female Hogwarts teacher&#39;s book">a book</a>. She watched the foreign students with interest, and smiled warmly when <a href="/wiki/Albus_Dumbledore" title="Albus Dumbledore">Albus Dumbledore</a> greeted <a href="/wiki/Igor_Karkaroff" title="Igor Karkaroff">Igor Karkaroff</a> as a friend.<sup id="cite_ref-GOF_6-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>On <a href="/wiki/31_October" title="31 October">31 October</a>, she watched the <a href="/wiki/Goblet_of_Fire" title="Goblet of Fire">Goblet of Fire</a> select <a href="/wiki/Fleur_Delacour" title="Fleur Delacour">Fleur Delacour</a> and <a href="/wiki/Viktor_Krum" title="Viktor Krum">Viktor Krum</a> become champions of Beauxbatons and Durmstrang respectively, and then <a href="/wiki/Cedric_Diggory" title="Cedric Diggory">Cedric Diggory</a> and <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> both become champions of Hogwarts. She did not seem quite as confused as her colleagues following the fourth champion's selection.<sup id="cite_ref-GOF_6-10" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Later on in the year, shortly before <a href="/wiki/Christmas" title="Christmas">Christmas</a>, the Professor oversaw a study session in the <a href="/wiki/Great_Hall" title="Great Hall">Great Hall</a>, as did several other colleagues, including <a href="/wiki/Severus_Snape" title="Severus Snape">Severus Snape</a>, who she apparently disliked.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP_8-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup> It is possible that she did this in addition to her teaching duties.<sup id="cite_ref-GOF_6-11" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

</p><p>Later in the year, she watched the Second Task of the Tournament, which <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> and <a href="/wiki/Cedric_Diggory" title="Cedric Diggory">Cedric Diggory</a> came in first and second respectively.<sup id="cite_ref-GOF_6-13" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup>

<p>During the <a href="/wiki/1995%E2%80%931996_school_year" title="1995–1996 school year">1995–1996 school year</a>, the Professor once again attended the <a href="/wiki/Start-of-Term_Feast" title="Start-of-Term Feast">Start-of-Term Feast</a>, sitting by <a href="/wiki/Black_1995_Start-of-Term_Feast_attendant" class="mw-redirect" title="Black 1995 Start-of-Term Feast attendant">a black wizard</a> and <a href="/wiki/Sybill_Trelawney" title="Sybill Trelawney">Sybill Trelawney</a>. She listened with interest to <a href="/wiki/Dolores_Umbridge" title="Dolores Umbridge">Dolores Umbridge</a>'s speech, despite being distracted by Trelawney's appalling table manners.<sup id="cite_ref-Manners_10-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Manners-10">&#91;10&#93;</a></sup> Though some of the staff "<i>brought their hands together only once or twice before stopping</i>", the Professor did not clap at all.<sup id="cite_ref-Manners_10-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Manners-10">&#91;10&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>That year, the Professor's classes were presumably inspected by Umbridge following her employment as <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_High_Inquisitor" title="Hogwarts High Inquisitor">Hogwarts High Inquisitor</a>. If so, Umbridge must have believed that she was a competent teacher, as she was not suspended.<sup id="cite_ref-OTP_7-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OTP-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<p>Once again, the Professor watched the <a href="/wiki/Sorting_ceremony" title="Sorting ceremony">sorting</a> of the <a href="/wiki/First_year" title="First year">first years</a> in <a href="/wiki/1996" title="1996">1996</a>. She sat on Professor Dumbledore's left-hand side, next to <a href="/wiki/Rubeus_Hagrid" title="Rubeus Hagrid">Rubeus Hagrid</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP_8-10" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>When Dumbledore announced <a href="/wiki/Horace_Slughorn" title="Horace Slughorn">Horace Slughorn</a>'s reinstatement as <a href="/wiki/Potions_Master" title="Potions Master">Potions Master</a>, she applauded merrily and smiled at him. However, she did not applaud when <a href="/wiki/Severus_Snape" title="Severus Snape">Severus Snape</a> was declared <a href="/wiki/Professor" title="Professor">Professor</a> of <a href="/wiki/Defence_Against_the_Dark_Arts" title="Defence Against the Dark Arts">Defence Against the Dark Arts</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP_8-11" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Shortly before <a href="/wiki/Christmas" title="Christmas">Christmas</a>, the Professor went to the <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Library" title="Hogwarts Library">Hogwarts Library</a>, looking for books, scanning the shelves while walking down the aisle. After only a fraction of a minute, she had found the books she needed and left the library with the books <a href="/wiki/Levitation" title="Levitation">levitating</a> after her.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP_8-12" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<p>Weeks later, the Professor attended the <a href="/wiki/Slug_Club_Christmas_party" title="Slug Club Christmas party">Slug Club Christmas party</a> held by her apparent friend and colleague, <a href="/wiki/Horace_Slughorn" title="Horace Slughorn">Horace Slughorn</a>. She was arm-in-arm with a white-haired wizard when <a href="/wiki/Argus_Filch" title="Argus Filch">Argus Filch</a> interrupted the party, dragging <a href="/wiki/Draco_Malfoy" title="Draco Malfoy">Draco Malfoy</a> with him. After Snape led him out, the Professor carried on with the party and smiled to <a href="/wiki/Flora_Carrow" title="Flora Carrow">Flora</a> and <a href="/wiki/Hestia_Carrow" title="Hestia Carrow">Hestia Carrow</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP_8-13" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>After the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_the_Astronomy_Tower" title="Battle of the Astronomy Tower">Battle of the Astronomy Tower</a>, the Professor raised her wand along with the other Professors and students to show respect for Albus Dumbledore and to vanquish the Dark Mark.

<h4><span id="1997–1998_school_year"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="1997.E2.80.931998_school_year">1997–1998 school year</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_female_Hogwarts_teacher?veaction=edit&amp;section=7" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: 1997–1998 school year">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_female_Hogwarts_teacher?action=edit&amp;section=7" title="Edit section: 1997–1998 school year"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<p>The Professor apparently continued to teach at Hogwarts during the <a href="/wiki/1997%E2%80%931998_school_year" title="1997–1998 school year">1997–1998 school year</a>, during which <a href="/wiki/Severus_Snape" title="Severus Snape">Severus Snape</a>, whom the Professor appeared not to like, was made <a href="/wiki/Headmaster" title="Headmaster">Headmaster</a> following the <a href="/wiki/Fall_of_the_Ministry_of_Magic" title="Fall of the Ministry of Magic">Death Eater coup</a> over the <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">Ministry of Magic</a>.
</p><p>On <a href="/wiki/1_May" title="1 May">1 May</a>, <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> arrived in the Castle to destroy one of Voldemort's <a href="/wiki/Horcrux" title="Horcrux">Horcruxes</a>, but the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a> began soon after. The female teacher appeared behind Harry looking frightened. The Professor helped defend <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Castle" title="Hogwarts Castle">Hogwarts Castle</a>, partaking in the fighting.<sup id="cite_ref-DHP2_2-11" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DHP2-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<p>She did not live to see the end of the battle at dawn of <a href="/wiki/2_May" title="2 May">2 May</a>. At some point before <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Voldemort</a> temporarily called back his forces, the Professor was killed, presumably by means of the <a href="/wiki/Killing_Curse" title="Killing Curse">Killing Curse</a>, as she showed no signs of external injury. Her body was brought to the <a href="/wiki/Great_Hall" title="Great Hall">Great Hall</a>, where <a href="/wiki/Sybill_Trelawney" title="Sybill Trelawney">Sybill Trelawney</a>, assisted by <a href="/wiki/Padma_Patil" title="Padma Patil">Padma Patil</a>, covered her corpse with a sheet.<sup id="cite_ref-DHP2_2-12" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DHP2-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Physical_description">Physical description</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_female_Hogwarts_teacher?veaction=edit&amp;section=8" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Physical description">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_female_Hogwarts_teacher?action=edit&amp;section=8" title="Edit section: Physical description"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The Professor was a tall witch with white skin, brown eyes, and brown hair. She appeared to have a fondness for the colour red during social occasions, such as the <a href="/wiki/Yule_Ball" title="Yule Ball">Yule Ball</a><sup id="cite_ref-GOF_6-14" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup> and the <a href="/wiki/Slug_Club_Christmas_party" title="Slug Club Christmas party">Slug Club Christmas party</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-HBP_8-14" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup> and black for everyday school-wear.<sup id="cite_ref-OTP_7-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OTP-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup> She normally had her hair out with a plait but sometimes had it in other hairstyles.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Personality_and_traits">Personality and traits</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_female_Hogwarts_teacher?veaction=edit&amp;section=9" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Personality and traits">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_female_Hogwarts_teacher?action=edit&amp;section=9" title="Edit section: Personality and traits"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The Professor was an intelligent person, as shown when she appeared to decode <a href="/wiki/Dolores_Umbridge" title="Dolores Umbridge">Dolores Umbridge</a>'s cryptic speech and refused to clap for her.<sup id="cite_ref-OTP_7-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OTP-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup> She also may have had a dislike, or a neutral opinion, for <a href="/wiki/Severus_Snape" title="Severus Snape">Severus Snape</a>, not applauding his employment as <a href="/wiki/Defence_Against_the_Dark_Arts" title="Defence Against the Dark Arts">Defence Against the Dark Arts</a> teacher, while happily clapping and smiling for <a href="/wiki/Horace_Slughorn" title="Horace Slughorn">Horace Slughorn</a>'s return.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP_8-15" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Her soft side showed when Dumbledore embraced <a href="/wiki/Igor_Karkaroff" title="Igor Karkaroff">Igor Karkaroff</a>, smiling warmly,<sup id="cite_ref-GOF_6-15" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup> and when she went arm-in-arm with a white-haired wizard during the Christmas party.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP_8-16" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup> She may have also been bookish, as she wrote in <a href="/wiki/Unidentified_female_Hogwarts_teacher%27s_book" title="Unidentified female Hogwarts teacher&#39;s book">a book</a> instead of watching some of <a href="/wiki/Beauxbatons_Academy_of_Magic" title="Beauxbatons Academy of Magic">Beauxbatons</a> and <a href="/wiki/Durmstrang_Institute" title="Durmstrang Institute">Durmstrang</a>'s students rehearsed entrances,<sup id="cite_ref-GOF_6-16" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup> and when she visited the library during <a href="/wiki/1996" title="1996">1996</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP_8-17" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Relationships">Relationships</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_female_Hogwarts_teacher?veaction=edit&amp;section=10" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Relationships">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_female_Hogwarts_teacher?action=edit&amp;section=10" title="Edit section: Relationships"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Hogwarts_staff">Hogwarts staff</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_female_Hogwarts_teacher?veaction=edit&amp;section=11" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Hogwarts staff">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_female_Hogwarts_teacher?action=edit&amp;section=11" title="Edit section: Hogwarts staff"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<figure class="thumb tleft show-info-icon" style="width: 172px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/6/6f/Female_teacher2.PNG/revision/latest?cb=20110728064451" class="image"><img alt="" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw%3D%3D" decoding="async" width="170" height="209" class="thumbimage lazyload" data-image-name="Female teacher2.PNG" data-image-key="Female_teacher2.PNG" data-src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/6/6f/Female_teacher2.PNG/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/170?cb=20110728064451" /></a> 	<noscript><a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/6/6f/Female_teacher2.PNG/revision/latest?cb=20110728064451" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/6/6f/Female_teacher2.PNG/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/170?cb=20110728064451" decoding="async" width="170" height="209" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="Female teacher2.PNG" data-image-key="Female_teacher2.PNG" data-src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/6/6f/Female_teacher2.PNG/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/170?cb=20110728064451" /></a></noscript> 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:Female_teacher2.PNG" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption">The Professor with Dumbledore at the <a href="/wiki/Yule_Ball" title="Yule Ball">Yule Ball</a></p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure>
<p>The Professor appeared to have a good relationship with the <a href="/wiki/Headmaster" title="Headmaster">Headmaster</a>, <a href="/wiki/Albus_Dumbledore" title="Albus Dumbledore">Albus Dumbledore</a>. She stood beside him during the <a href="/wiki/Yule_Ball" title="Yule Ball">Yule Ball</a>, and grinned to herself when he took to the floor with <a href="/wiki/Minerva_McGonagall" title="Minerva McGonagall">Minerva McGonagall</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-GOF_6-17" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup> She also sat by him during the <a href="/wiki/1996%E2%80%931997_school_year" title="1996–1997 school year">1996–1997 school year</a> <a href="/wiki/Start-of-Term_Feast" title="Start-of-Term Feast">Start-of-Term Feast</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP_8-18" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>She also seemed to be friendly with <a href="/wiki/Horace_Slughorn" title="Horace Slughorn">Horace Slughorn</a>, <a href="/wiki/Potions_Master" title="Potions Master">Potions Master</a> and <a href="/wiki/Head_of_Slytherin_House" title="Head of Slytherin House">Head of Slytherin House</a>. She applauded happily (which was a rarity in itself) for him after he took his old job back, and attended his <a href="/wiki/Slug_Club_Christmas_party" title="Slug Club Christmas party">Christmas party</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP_8-19" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>The Professor may also have had a relationship of sorts with <a href="/wiki/Sybill_Trelawney" title="Sybill Trelawney">Sybill Trelawney</a>, a <a href="/wiki/Seer" title="Seer">Seer</a> and <a href="/wiki/Divination_(class)" title="Divination (class)">Divination</a> <a href="/wiki/Professor" title="Professor">teacher</a>. She sat by her during the <a href="/wiki/Start-of-Term_Feast" title="Start-of-Term Feast">Start-of-Term Feast</a> of <a href="/wiki/1995" title="1995">1995</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-OTP_7-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OTP-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup> and Trelawney was the one that tended to her dead body during the hour-long break during the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-DHP2_2-13" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DHP2-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>However,she seemed to be unfriendly with and <a href="/wiki/Dolores_Umbridge" title="Dolores Umbridge">Dolores Umbridge</a>, whom she did not clap for during her announcement of becoming <a href="/wiki/Defence_Against_the_Dark_Arts" title="Defence Against the Dark Arts">Defence Against the Dark Arts</a> teacher,<sup id="cite_ref-OTP_7-10" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OTP-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-HBP_8-20" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup> despite applauding for Professor Slughorn. She might have had a neutral opinion of <a href="/wiki/Severus_Snape" title="Severus Snape">Severus Snape</a>. She did not clap for him when he became Defence Against the Dark Arts teacher, however she was shortly seen deliberately moving towards him during the <a href="/wiki/Yule_Ball" title="Yule Ball">Yule Ball</a>, and then stood next to him without any visible discomfort, although she most likely disliked him after the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_the_Astronomy_Tower" title="Battle of the Astronomy Tower">death of Albus Dumbledore</a>.
</p><p>Slytherin members also had some sort of relationship with her, as she smiled at <a href="/wiki/Flora_and_Hestia_Carrow" title="Flora and Hestia Carrow">Flora and Hestia Carrow</a> at the Slug Club Christmas party. The professor also seemed to like Horace Slughorn because once she clapped for him but refused to clap for anyone else.
</p>
