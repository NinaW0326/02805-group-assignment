<p>This <b>individual</b> worked as a groundskeeper of the <a href="/wiki/Chudley_Cannons_training_grounds" title="Chudley Cannons training grounds">training grounds</a> of the <a href="/wiki/Chudley_Cannons" title="Chudley Cannons">Chudley Cannons</a> <a href="/wiki/Quidditch" title="Quidditch">Quidditch</a> <a href="/wiki/Quidditch_team" title="Quidditch team">team</a>.
</p><p>When <a href="/wiki/Chudley_Cannons" title="Chudley Cannons">Chudley Cannons</a> <a href="/wiki/Keeper" title="Keeper">Keeper</a> <a href="/wiki/Gordon_Horton" title="Gordon Horton">Gordon Horton</a> was training alone and was attacked by a <a href="/wiki/Hippogriff" title="Hippogriff">Hippogriff</a> that had been nursing an egg in a nearby <a href="/wiki/Old_Bell_Tower" title="Old Bell Tower">bell tower</a>, this groundskeeper heard terrible screeching coming from the pitch and fled for help, ending up calling the <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">Ministry of Magic</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
