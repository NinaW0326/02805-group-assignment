<p><b>Michael Corner</b> (b. <a href="/wiki/1979" title="1979">1979</a>/<a href="/wiki/1980" title="1980">1980</a>) was a <a href="/wiki/Half-blood" title="Half-blood">half-blood</a><sup id="cite_ref-HPME_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HPME-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> and <a href="/wiki/Ravenclaw" title="Ravenclaw">Ravenclaw</a> student at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> in <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>'s year. He was friends with house mates <a href="/wiki/Terry_Boot" title="Terry Boot">Terry Boot</a> and <a href="/wiki/Anthony_Goldstein" title="Anthony Goldstein">Anthony Goldstein</a>. In his fifth year he joined <a href="/wiki/Dumbledore%27s_Army" title="Dumbledore&#39;s Army">Dumbledore's Army</a>, an organisation taught and led by Harry. That year, Michael also dated <a href="/wiki/Ginevra_Weasley" title="Ginevra Weasley">Ginny Weasley</a>, though she dumped him by the end of the school year, after which Michael dated <a href="/wiki/Cho_Chang" title="Cho Chang">Cho Chang</a>. In his <a href="/wiki/1997%E2%80%931998_school_year" title="1997–1998 school year">seventh year</a>, he rejoined the D.A. and fought courageously in the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a>, and presumably survived, as his name was not given among the <a href="/wiki/Fallen_Fifty" title="Fallen Fifty">fallen deceased</a>. 
</p>

<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_life">Early life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Michael_Corner?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Early life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Michael_Corner?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Early life"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Michael was born in either <a href="/wiki/1979" title="1979">1979</a> or <a href="/wiki/1980" title="1980">1980</a>. His family had both presumably <a href="/wiki/Magic_genes" title="Magic genes">magical</a> and <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">muggle</a> heritage.<sup id="cite_ref-HPME_1-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HPME-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span id="Hogwarts_years_(1991–1998)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="Hogwarts_years_.281991.E2.80.931998.29">Hogwarts years <span class="template-c">(1991–1998)</span></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Michael_Corner?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Hogwarts years (1991–1998)">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Michael_Corner?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Hogwarts years (1991–1998)"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_years">Early years</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Michael_Corner?veaction=edit&amp;section=4" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Early years">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Michael_Corner?action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Early years"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<p>Michael Corner started his <a href="/wiki/First_year" title="First year">first year</a> <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magical</a> education at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> at the same time as <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>, in <a href="/wiki/1991" title="1991">1991</a>. Michael was <a href="/wiki/Sorting_ceremony" title="Sorting ceremony">sorted</a> into the house <a href="/wiki/Ravenclaw" title="Ravenclaw">Ravenclaw</a> upon his arrival at Hogwarts. Michael would have shared a few classes with Harry Potter but as he was in a different house the two did not really get to know each other too well.
</p>

<p>During <a href="/wiki/1994%E2%80%931995_school_year" title="1994–1995 school year">1994–1995 school year</a>, his <a href="/wiki/Fourth_year" title="Fourth year">fourth year</a>, Hogwarts was host to the <a href="/wiki/Triwizard_Tournament" title="Triwizard Tournament">Triwizard Tournament</a>. As part of the traditions the <a href="/wiki/Yule_Ball" title="Yule Ball">Yule Ball</a> was held and it was here that Michael first met <a href="/wiki/Ginevra_Weasley" title="Ginevra Weasley">Ginny Weasley</a>, a <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor" title="Gryffindor">Gryffindor</a> in the year below himself. Ginny had recently gotten over her crush on <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> and eventually towards the end of July <a href="/wiki/1995" title="1995">1995</a>, the two began dating after becoming more friendly with one another at the ball.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP16_3-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP16-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> 
</p><p>Ginny's brother, <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ron Weasley</a>, didn't find out about their relationship until a few months after it began and it was likely the two kept it quiet as he would disapprove. However, <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione Granger</a> apparently had known about the relationship for some time before Ron's discovery. After finding out the two were dating Ron would frequently question Michael's intelligence, something Ravenclaws pride themselves on. At the end of the school year <a href="/wiki/Cedric_Diggory" title="Cedric Diggory">Cedric Diggory</a>, boyfriend of fellow Ravenclaw student <a href="/wiki/Cho_Chang" title="Cho Chang">Cho Chang</a>, was tragically murdered and <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a> returned to power. These claims were not widely believed and Headmaster <a href="/wiki/Albus_Dumbledore" title="Albus Dumbledore">Albus Dumbledore</a> was <a href="/wiki/Campaign_to_discredit_Albus_Dumbledore_and_Harry_Potter" title="Campaign to discredit Albus Dumbledore and Harry Potter">slated</a> as mad and seditious by the <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">British Ministry of Magic</a>, who were deeply paranoid and ignorant about Voldemort returning to power.
</p>

<p>The <a href="/wiki/1995%E2%80%931996_school_year" title="1995–1996 school year">1995–1996 school year</a> was Michael's <a href="/wiki/Fifth_year" title="Fifth year">Fifth year</a> of education, an important year for students as he would be taking his <a href="/wiki/Ordinary_Wizarding_Level" title="Ordinary Wizarding Level">O.W.L.</a>'s. This year <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts</a> had been put under the watchful eye of Ministry employee, <a href="/wiki/Dolores_Umbridge" title="Dolores Umbridge">Dolores Umbridge</a> following the previous years events. After Umbridge's refusal to teach students any form of practical <a href="/wiki/Defence_Against_the_Dark_Arts" title="Defence Against the Dark Arts">Defence Against the Dark Arts</a> and with an <a href="/wiki/Second_Wizarding_War" title="Second Wizarding War">imposing war</a> ahead, <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione Granger</a> founded a secret alliance called <a href="/wiki/Dumbledore%27s_Army" title="Dumbledore&#39;s Army">Dumbledore's Army</a> where students could be taught how to defend themselves by <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>.

Michael joined the reconstituted <a href="/wiki/Dumbledore%27s_Army" title="Dumbledore&#39;s Army">Dumbledore's Army</a> in defying the newly appointed and tyrannical <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eater</a> <a href="/wiki/Professor" title="Professor">professors</a> <a href="/wiki/Alecto_Carrow" title="Alecto Carrow">Alecto</a> and <a href="/wiki/Amycus_Carrow" title="Amycus Carrow">Amycus Carrow</a>. The reformed student alliance took pride in creating havoc for the Death Eater's and would often paint messages such as <i>Dumbledore's Army: Still Recruiting</i> around the school.<sup id="cite_ref-DH29_10-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH29-10">&#91;10&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>On one occasion Michael sneaked out and released a <a href="/wiki/First_year" title="First year">first year</a> that had been chained up by the Carrows. As punishment for releasing the first-year from imprisonment Michael was <a href="/wiki/Cruciatus_Curse" title="Cruciatus Curse">tortured</a> greatly for it. The Carrows made an example of Michael and soon the rebellion began to quiet down for fear of similar consequences.<sup id="cite_ref-DH29_10-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH29-10">&#91;10&#93;</a></sup>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Magical_abilities_and_skills">Magical abilities and skills</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Michael_Corner?veaction=edit&amp;section=11" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Michael_Corner?action=edit&amp;section=11" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li><b><a href="/wiki/Potion" title="Potion">Potions</a></b>: Michael was very proficient in the <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magic</a> of potion-making, as he was one of four <a href="/wiki/Ravenclaw" title="Ravenclaw">Ravenclaws</a> in <a href="/wiki/1996" title="1996">1996</a> to progress to <a href="/wiki/Nastily_Exhausting_Wizarding_Test" title="Nastily Exhausting Wizarding Test">N.E.W.T.</a>-level.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP9_9-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP9-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup> He must have earned either an '<a href="/wiki/Outstanding" title="Outstanding">Outstanding</a>' or an '<a href="/wiki/Exceeds_Expectations" title="Exceeds Expectations">Exceeds Expectations</a>' on his <a href="/wiki/Ordinary_Wizarding_Level" title="Ordinary Wizarding Level">O.W.L.</a> exam in order to progress to such an advanced <a href="/wiki/Potions_(class)" title="Potions (class)">class</a>.</li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Defence_Against_the_Dark_Arts" title="Defence Against the Dark Arts">Defence Against the Dark Arts</a></b>: Michael was a loyal member of the <a href="/wiki/Dumbledore%27s_Army" title="Dumbledore&#39;s Army">D.A.</a> and being so he must have mastered numerous defensive and offensive spells under <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>'s exemplary teaching.</li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Charm" title="Charm">Charms</a></b>: Michael was capable of producing a non-corporeal <a href="/wiki/Patronus_Charm" title="Patronus Charm">Patronus</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP20_4-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP20-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-LEGOY2_5-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-LEGOY2-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> a piece of highly advanced <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magic</a> and a mark of superior magical ability.</li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Duelling" title="Duelling">Duelling</a></b>: Michael fought bravely in the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a> against the <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a> and presumably survived, showing he had a certain level of skill as a duellist.</li></ul>

<p>Micheal and Ginny Weasley met at the <a href="/wiki/Yule_Ball" title="Yule Ball">Yule Ball</a> in <a href="/wiki/1994" title="1994">1994</a>, and began dating at the end of the school year, she used the relationship as an attempt to "move on" from Harry Potter. In <a href="/wiki/1995" title="1995">1995</a> she brought him and some of his friends along to the first meeting of what would become <a href="/wiki/Dumbledore%27s_Army" title="Dumbledore&#39;s Army">Dumbledore's Army</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP16_3-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP16-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> When the two duelled during meetings Michael did not hit Ginny with anything, possibly because she was his girlfriend. When Michael drew unnecessary attention to the D.A. at one point, Ginny called him a "<i>fool</i>", though not to his face.
</p><p>Ginny ended the relationship on the basis of Michael being a "<i>bad loser</i>," following Michael's poor reaction to Gryffindor beating Ravenclaw in a <a href="/wiki/Quidditch" title="Quidditch">Quidditch</a> match.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP6_8-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP6-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup> Michael went on to date <a href="/wiki/Cho_Chang" title="Cho Chang">Cho Chang</a>, who (like Ginny) also had a romance with <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Michael_Corner?veaction=edit&amp;section=14" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Michael_Corner?action=edit&amp;section=14" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The name "Michael" is from the Hebrew name מִיכָאֵל (<i>Mikha'el</i>) meaning "who is like God?". This is a rhetorical question, implying no person is like God. Saint Michael was one of the seven archangels in Hebrew tradition and the only one identified as an archangel in the Bible. In the Book of Revelation in the New Testament he is portrayed as the leader of heaven's armies, and thus is considered the patron saint of soldiers.
</p>
