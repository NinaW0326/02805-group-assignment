<p><b>Porpentina Esther</b><sup id="cite_ref-macusaid_1-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-macusaid-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> <b>"Tina" Scamander</b> (née <b>Goldstein</b><sup id="cite_ref-JKR-twitter-goldstein_12-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-JKR-twitter-goldstein-12">&#91;12&#93;</a></sup>) (b. <a href="/wiki/19_August" title="19 August">19 August</a>, <a href="/wiki/1901" title="1901">1901</a><sup id="cite_ref-macusaid_1-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-macusaid-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>) was an <a href="/wiki/United_States_of_America" title="United States of America">American</a> <a href="/wiki/Half-blood" title="Half-blood">half-blood</a> <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch</a>. She attended <a href="/wiki/Ilvermorny_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Ilvermorny School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Ilvermorny School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a>, where she was sorted into <a href="/wiki/Thunderbird_(House)" title="Thunderbird (House)">Thunderbird house</a>. After graduating from Ilvermorny, she became an <a href="/wiki/Auror" title="Auror">Auror</a> for the <a href="/wiki/Magical_Congress_of_the_United_States_of_America" title="Magical Congress of the United States of America">Magical Congress of the United States of America</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-wb_13-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-wb-13">&#91;13&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Tina was dismissed from her position as an Auror for <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magically</a> assaulting the <a href="/wiki/Muggle" class="mw-redirect" title="Muggle">No-maj</a> <a href="/wiki/Mary_Lou_Barebone" title="Mary Lou Barebone">Mary Lou Barebone</a>. She assaulted Mary Lou in front of witnesses as a response to her beating her adopted son, <a href="/wiki/Credence_Barebone" title="Credence Barebone">Credence Barebone</a>. Goldstein was investigating the <a href="/wiki/New_Salem_Philanthropic_Society" title="New Salem Philanthropic Society">New Salem Philanthropic Society</a>, against the orders of her superiors. She was reinstated in <a href="/wiki/1926" title="1926">1926</a> after helping to arrest <a href="/wiki/Gellert_Grindelwald" title="Gellert Grindelwald">Gellert Grindelwald</a>.
</p><p>Tina later became the wife of famed <a href="/wiki/Magizoologist" title="Magizoologist">Magizoologist</a> and author <a href="/wiki/Newton_Scamander" title="Newton Scamander">Newt Scamander</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-fbb_14-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-fbb-14">&#91;14&#93;</a></sup> Sometime after retiring from the Department of Aurors she moved to the <a href="/wiki/Great_Britain" title="Great Britain">United Kingdom</a> with her husband. The couple had at least one child, through who they had a grandson, <a href="/wiki/Rolf_Scamander" title="Rolf Scamander">Rolf Scamander</a> and two known great grandsons, <a href="/wiki/Lorcan_Scamander" title="Lorcan Scamander">Lorcan</a> and <a href="/wiki/Lysander_Scamander" title="Lysander Scamander">Lysander</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-grandparent_5-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-grandparent-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> 
</p>

<p>Porpentina Esther Goldstein was born on <a href="/wiki/19_August" title="19 August">19 August</a> <a href="/wiki/1901" title="1901">1901</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-macusaid_1-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-macusaid-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-wb_13-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-wb-13">&#91;13&#93;</a></sup> She had at least one sibling, a younger sister named <a href="/wiki/Queenie_Goldstein" title="Queenie Goldstein">Queenie</a><sup id="cite_ref-Twitter2_15-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Twitter2-15">&#91;15&#93;</a></sup> and was a half-blood witch.<sup id="cite_ref-macusaid_1-10" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-macusaid-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>  The sisters lost their <a href="/wiki/Goldstein_sisters%27_mother" title="Goldstein sisters&#39; mother">mother</a> and <a href="/wiki/Goldstein_sisters%27_father" title="Goldstein sisters&#39; father">father</a> at a very young age due to <a href="/wiki/Dragon_Pox" title="Dragon Pox">Dragon Pox</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-fb44_16-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-fb44-16">&#91;16&#93;</a></sup> Afterwards, they "raised each other" and consequently maintained a very strong bond into adulthood.<sup id="cite_ref-collider_6-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-collider-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-pottermorevideo_17-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-pottermorevideo-17">&#91;17&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Porpentina's friends and family called her by the nickname "Tina,"<sup id="cite_ref-Twitter1_2-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Twitter1-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> although Queenie occasionally used the affectionate variation of "Teenie." She and her sister Queenie may have been <a href="/wiki/Judaism" title="Judaism">Jewish</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-jewish_18-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-jewish-18">&#91;18&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>During her youth, Tina attended <a href="/wiki/Ilvermorny_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Ilvermorny School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Ilvermorny School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-ilvermorny_19-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-ilvermorny-19">&#91;19&#93;</a></sup> where she was <a href="/wiki/Sorting_ceremony" title="Sorting ceremony">sorted</a> into <a href="/wiki/Thunderbird_(House)" title="Thunderbird (House)">Thunderbird</a> house.<sup id="cite_ref-house_11-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-house-11">&#91;11&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<p>Tina worked for the <a href="/wiki/Magical_Congress_of_the_United_States_of_America" title="Magical Congress of the United States of America">Magical Congress of the United States of America</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-entertainmentweekly12_20-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-entertainmentweekly12-20">&#91;20&#93;</a></sup> Originally, she was an <a href="/wiki/Auror" title="Auror">Auror</a>;<sup id="cite_ref-entertainmentweekly12_20-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-entertainmentweekly12-20">&#91;20&#93;</a></sup> however, she stood up for <a href="/wiki/Credence_Barebone" title="Credence Barebone">Credence Barebone</a> by using <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magic</a> to assault his adoptive mother <a href="/wiki/Mary_Lou_Barebone" title="Mary Lou Barebone">Mary Lou Barebone</a>, the leader of the <a href="/wiki/New_Salem_Philanthropic_Society" title="New Salem Philanthropic Society">New Salem Philanthropic Society</a>, for mistreating Credence. As a result, No-Majs that witnessed the event needed to be <a href="/wiki/Memory_Charm" title="Memory Charm">Obliviated</a>, and Tina was demoted to the position of <a href="/wiki/Federal_Wand_Permit_Officer" title="Federal Wand Permit Officer">Federal Wand Permit Officer</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-FBAWTFT_21-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-FBAWTFT-21">&#91;21&#93;</a></sup> This job was well below her abilities and personal ambitions.<sup id="cite_ref-entertainmentweekly12_20-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-entertainmentweekly12-20">&#91;20&#93;</a></sup> Her sister Queenie also had a menial desk job in the wand permit office.<sup id="cite_ref-NewMagicalPower2_22-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-NewMagicalPower2-22">&#91;22&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<p>In <a href="/wiki/1926" title="1926">1926</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-pottermore-everything_10-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-pottermore-everything-10">&#91;10&#93;</a></sup> Tina was living in a <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/brownstone" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:brownstone">brownstone</a> with her sister at <a href="/wiki/679_West_24th_Street" title="679 West 24th Street">679 West 24th Street</a> in <a href="/wiki/New_York" title="New York">New York</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-macusaid_1-11" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-macusaid-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-collider_6-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-collider-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-entertainmentweekly1-queenie_23-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-entertainmentweekly1-queenie-23">&#91;23&#93;</a></sup> On <a href="/wiki/6_December" title="6 December">6 December</a> <a href="/wiki/1926" title="1926">1926</a>, Tina left from work and observed Mary Lou preaching in an anti-witchcraft rally at the steps of <a href="/wiki/Steen_National_Bank" title="Steen National Bank">Steen National Bank</a>. She noticed a <a href="/wiki/Billywig" title="Billywig">billywig</a> flying above them before she met <a href="/wiki/Great_Britain" title="Great Britain">British</a> <a href="/wiki/Magizoologist" title="Magizoologist">magizoologist</a> <a href="/wiki/Newton_Scamander" title="Newton Scamander">Newt Scamander</a>, who was running through the crowd in search of a <a href="/wiki/Niffler" title="Niffler">Niffler</a> that had escaped <a href="/wiki/Newton_Scamander%27s_suitcase" title="Newton Scamander&#39;s suitcase">his suitcase</a>. Tina thought he seemed strange and decided to follow him.<sup id="cite_ref-wb_13-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-wb-13">&#91;13&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

She asked him why he caused a disruption inside the bank and whether he <a href="/wiki/Memory_Charm" title="Memory Charm">Obliviated</a> Jacob. He revealed that he had not, and Tina discovered that he knew very little about how things worked in America's wizarding community. Tina took him in to MACUSA headquarters on a <a href="/wiki/Section_3A" title="Section 3A">Section 3A</a>: failing to Obliviate a No-Maj.<sup id="cite_ref-24" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-24">&#91;24&#93;</a></sup>

<p>Once they arrived at the headquarters, Tina interrupted a meeting being held by <a href="/wiki/President_of_the_Magical_Congress_of_the_United_States_of_America" title="President of the Magical Congress of the United States of America">President</a> <a href="/wiki/Seraphina_Picquery" title="Seraphina Picquery">Seraphina Picquery</a>, who interrupted her attempts to explain herself and then sent her away. Returning to her office, she and Newt were eventually joined by the Head of the Department of Magical Law Enforcement, <a href="/wiki/Percival_Graves" title="Percival Graves">Percival Graves</a>, who wanted to know about Newt's suitcase. However, when she opened it, she found it contained pastries belonging to Jacob. She and Newt then realised that he and Jacob had accidentally taken each other's cases instead of their own. The two left to retrieve Newt's suitcase from the No-Maj.

<p>Arriving at Jacob's apartment, they found it in partial ruin, which Newt quickly repaired. They found Jacob unwell, having been bitten by a <a href="/wiki/Murtlap" title="Murtlap">murtlap</a>, and discovered creatures had escaped. Tina offered them a place to stay at her home, believing it best to keep the No-Maj with them until he recovered. She introduced them to her <a href="/wiki/Queenie_Goldstein" title="Queenie Goldstein">sister</a>, and they shared a meal together. She later showed them to their room and left them alone. However, when she checked up on them later that evening, she found both men and the suitcase missing.
</p>

<p>She tracked them down to an ice rink under a bridge in <a href="/wiki/Central_Park" title="Central Park">Central Park</a>. When they climbed into the suitcase, Tina locked it and took it to MACUSA where she interrupted a delegation of the <a href="/wiki/International_Confederation_of_Wizards" title="International Confederation of Wizards">International Confederation of Wizards</a> to inform them about the creatures and the wizard who smuggled them into the country. Newt was immediately blamed for allowing one of his creatures to murder Senator <a href="/wiki/Henry_Shaw_Junior" title="Henry Shaw Junior">Henry Shaw Jnr</a>. However, upon observing the scars and markings on Shaw's body projection, Newt stated that only an <a href="/wiki/Obscurus" title="Obscurus">Obscurus</a> could have left the distinct markings upon his body, which caused unrest in the room, during which President Picquery denied the existence of any Obscurus in America. Tina, Newt and Jacob were<span> soon taken under arrest and Newt's case was impounded. In the jail cell, Tina and Newt explained the meaning of an Obscurus to Jacob and how no record existed at the time of an Obscurial having existed past the age of ten. </span>

</p><p>After pulling Kama out from the sewer they headed to the <a href="/wiki/Alexander_III_Bridge" title="Alexander III Bridge">Alexander III Bridge</a>, where Newt found the <a href="/wiki/Zouwu" title="Zouwu">Zouwu</a> and put it back into his suitcase. Newt removed with tweezers a <a href="/wiki/Water_dragon_parasite" title="Water dragon parasite">water dragon parasite</a> from Yusuf's eye which had been weakening him. Both Tina and Newt deduced that traces on Kama's hands showed that he made an <a href="/wiki/Unbreakable_Vow" title="Unbreakable Vow">Unbreakable Vow</a>.
</p>

<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Later_life">Later life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Porpentina_Goldstein?veaction=edit&amp;section=12" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Later life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Porpentina_Goldstein?action=edit&amp;section=12" title="Edit section: Later life"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Tina eventually married Newt and moved to the <a href="/wiki/Great_Britain" title="Great Britain">United Kingdom</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-fbb_14-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-fbb-14">&#91;14&#93;</a></sup> The couple had at least one child, who in turn gave them a grandson, <a href="/wiki/Rolf_Scamander" title="Rolf Scamander">Rolf</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-BWC_26-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-BWC-26">&#91;26&#93;</a></sup>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Physical_appearance">Physical appearance</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Porpentina_Goldstein?veaction=edit&amp;section=13" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Physical appearance">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Porpentina_Goldstein?action=edit&amp;section=13" title="Edit section: Physical appearance"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Tina Goldstein was a beautiful, slender woman with brown eyes and hair. She usually wore a dark blue jacket over a plain white shirt with long sleeves. Jacob and Newt have both acknowledged her beauty, with the latter citing that "she has the eyes of a <a href="/wiki/Fire_Dwelling_Salamander" title="Fire Dwelling Salamander">salamander</a>", which was a compliment coming from the <a href="/wiki/Magizoologist" title="Magizoologist">Magizoologist</a>, despite Jacob acknowledging it as a rather awkward and strange comparison.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Personality_and_traits">Personality and traits</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Porpentina_Goldstein?veaction=edit&amp;section=14" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Personality and traits">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Porpentina_Goldstein?action=edit&amp;section=14" title="Edit section: Personality and traits"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Tina had a practical, level-headed personality. She was described as being "grounded."<sup id="cite_ref-29" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-29">&#91;29&#93;</a></sup> However, in true <a href="/wiki/Thunderbird_(House)" title="Thunderbird (House)">Thunderbird</a> fashion, Tina was also described as being "very soulful" and possessed <i>"a lot of heart, strengths, and courage.</i>"<sup id="cite_ref-pottermorevideo_17-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-pottermorevideo-17">&#91;17&#93;</a></sup> She was naturally ambitious, and had a desire to stand up for what was right<sup id="cite_ref-entertainmentweekly1_9-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-entertainmentweekly1-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup>, which was evident in Tina attacking the <a href="/wiki/New_Salem_Philanthropic_Society" title="New Salem Philanthropic Society">Second Salemers</a> leader <a href="/wiki/Mary_Lou_Barebone" title="Mary Lou Barebone">Mary Lou Barebone</a> after the latter brutally punished her adopted son <a href="/wiki/Credence_Barebone" title="Credence Barebone">Credence</a>, with Tina later consoling the young man. That incident got Tina a temporary demotion from <a href="/wiki/Auror" title="Auror">Auror</a> to <a href="/wiki/Federal_Wand_Permit_Officer" title="Federal Wand Permit Officer">Federal Wand Permit Officer</a>, but she would still keep an eye on the Second Salemers, due to her desire for justice. Another example of her sense of justice was when she turned in Newt and Jacob for unleashing the animals in the former's suitcase, considering it the right thing to do. However, Tina valued her own life, crying softly when faced with execution and fighting the culprit, <a href="/wiki/Gellert_Grindelwald" title="Gellert Grindelwald">Gellert Grindelwald</a>/<a href="/wiki/Percival_Graves" title="Percival Graves">Percival Graves</a>, with a clear look of apprehension on her face.
</p><p>Tina was also very persistent, demonstrated during the <a href="/wiki/Obscurial_attack_on_New_York" title="Obscurial attack on New York">Obscurial attack on New York</a> and again during her search for Credence, whom she was determined to protect. Her sister Queenie playfully labelled her as a "career girl". She was also shown to be vastly empathetic, as shown by her interactions with Queenie, Newt, Jacob, and Credence. However, she also had a tendency to become bossy and unnecessarily forceful when things go out of her control. 
</p><p>Initially, Tina was a valiant and ambitious <a href="/wiki/Auror" title="Auror">Auror</a>, but following her demotion, she became discontented and lost some of her nerve. However, her strong will allowed her to not only push through this but also persist with the <a href="/wiki/Federal_Wand_Permit_Officer" title="Federal Wand Permit Officer">job</a> she was given, but also to oppose the <a href="/wiki/New_Salem_Philanthropic_Society" title="New Salem Philanthropic Society">Second Salemer</a> No-Majes. Tina initially had a rather neutral opinion about <a href="/wiki/Newton_Scamander" title="Newton Scamander">Newt Scamander</a>, considering him a threat to the Statute of Secrecy. However, due to his handsome appearance, tender heart, and endearing personality, and most of all his recognised innocence and the utter sense of right and wrong they shared, she began developing tender feelings for him, which he certainly reciprocated. For Tina, their exploits together have been a journey of renewal. Her experiences before and during the <a href="/wiki/Obscurial_attack_on_New_York" title="Obscurial attack on New York">Obscurial attack on New York</a> have given her more serenity and allowed her to come to terms with her hardships and attempted to find peace and calm within herself. Having grown bolder and happier, she set her sights on finding and redeeming <a href="/wiki/Credence_Barebone" title="Credence Barebone">Credence Barebone</a>, hoping to save him from both the <a href="/wiki/Alliance" title="Alliance">Alliance</a> and himself. Unlike her sister <a href="/wiki/Queenie_Goldstein" title="Queenie Goldstein">Queenie</a>, Tina demonstrated an incorruptible moral code when pushed to the forefront, as she resisted <a href="/wiki/Gellert_Grindelwald" title="Gellert Grindelwald">Gellert Grindelwald</a>'s persuasive speech, and joined the <a href="/wiki/Global_wizarding_war" title="Global wizarding war">war efforts</a> to oppose the Dark Lord.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Magical_abilities_and_skills">Magical abilities and skills</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Porpentina_Goldstein?veaction=edit&amp;section=15" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Porpentina_Goldstein?action=edit&amp;section=15" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li><b><a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">Magical</a> mastery</b>: Tina Goldstein was an accomplished and rather powerful witch, since, as an <a href="/wiki/Ilvermorny_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Ilvermorny School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Ilvermorny</a> student, she excelled in her studies enough to meet the qualifications for <a href="/wiki/Auror_training" title="Auror training">Auror training</a> at the <a href="/wiki/Magical_Congress_of_the_United_States_of_America" title="Magical Congress of the United States of America">MACUSA</a>, becoming an Auror trusted with important assignments, such as breaking up <a href="/wiki/New_Salem_Philanthropic_Society" title="New Salem Philanthropic Society">Second Salemer</a> Muggle rallies in <a href="/wiki/New_York" title="New York">New York City</a>, and apprehending <a href="/wiki/Credence_Barebone" title="Credence Barebone">Credence Barebone</a> in <a href="/wiki/Paris" title="Paris">Paris</a>. Tina was also able to successfully aid her future husband, future brother-in-law, <a href="/wiki/Yusuf_Kama" title="Yusuf Kama">Yusuf</a> and <a href="/wiki/Nicolas_Flamel" title="Nicolas Flamel">Nicolas Flamel</a> in saving Paris from the incredibly destructive <a href="/wiki/Protego_Diabolica" title="Protego Diabolica">black fire</a> conjured by <a href="/wiki/Gellert_Grindelwald" title="Gellert Grindelwald">Gellert Grindelwald</a>.</li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Auror" title="Auror">Auror</a> skills</b>: Tina was a highly skilful Auror prior to her demotion, with her effectively using <a href="/wiki/Gnarlak" title="Gnarlak">Gnarlak</a> of <a href="/wiki/The_Blind_Pig" title="The Blind Pig">The Blind Pig</a> speakeasy as an informant. Even while temporarily demoted to a <a href="/wiki/Federal_Wand_Permit_Officer" title="Federal Wand Permit Officer">Federal Wand Permit Officer</a>, Tina still maintained her Auror aptitude, as she quickly deduced that <a href="/wiki/Newton_Scamander" title="Newton Scamander">Newt Scamander</a> was a foreign wizard with illegal magical beasts, and promptly apprehended Newt before he could react during their first meeting, and was able to later apprehend Newt and <a href="/wiki/Jacob_Kowalski" title="Jacob Kowalski">Jacob Kowalski</a> (while they had climbed into the <a href="/wiki/Newton_Scamander%27s_suitcase" title="Newton Scamander&#39;s suitcase">case with magical beasts</a>), and bring them before <a href="/wiki/Seraphina_Picquery" title="Seraphina Picquery">President Picquery</a>. After getting reinstated as an Auror, Tina was trusted by the <a href="/wiki/Magical_Congress_of_the_United_States_of_America" title="Magical Congress of the United States of America">MACUSA</a> with the international assignment of helping the <a href="/wiki/French_Ministry_of_Magic" title="French Ministry of Magic">French Ministry of Magic</a> to track down the <a href="/wiki/Obscurial" title="Obscurial">Obscurial</a> <a href="/wiki/Credence_Barebone" title="Credence Barebone">Credence Barebone</a>. Indeed, Tina tracked Credence down to the <a href="/wiki/Circus_Arcanus" title="Circus Arcanus">Circus Arcanus</a> before any of the <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">British Ministry of Magic</a> Aurors under <a href="/wiki/Theseus_Scamander" title="Theseus Scamander">Theseus Scamander</a> did, and then went on to successfully question the gruff Circus owner <a href="/wiki/Skender" title="Skender">Skender</a>, and to swiftly deduce that <a href="/wiki/Yusuf_Kama" title="Yusuf Kama">Yusuf Kama</a> was also interested in Credence. Tina later promptly discovered the location of the <a href="/wiki/Lestrange_family" title="Lestrange family">Lestrange family</a> section in the <a href="/wiki/French_Ministry_of_Magic_Records_Room" title="French Ministry of Magic Records Room">French Ministry of Magic Archives</a>.</li>

Later on, when Grindelwald fought at full capability, Tina, even alongside Theseus and Newt, both very proficient duellists, was still considerably outclassed, and would have been killed easily had <a href="/wiki/Leta_Lestrange" title="Leta Lestrange">Leta Lestrange</a> not interfered.</li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Defence_Against_the_Dark_Arts" title="Defence Against the Dark Arts">Defence Against the Dark Arts</a></b>: As an Auror, Tina was highly accomplished and skilled in this field, with her skills evident in her assistance in breaking up a rally of <a href="/wiki/Gellert_Grindelwald%27s_army" class="mw-redirect" title="Gellert Grindelwald&#39;s army">Gellert Grindelwald's army</a>, in her ability to hold off the extremely powerful <a href="/wiki/Protego_Diabolica" title="Protego Diabolica">black fire</a> cast by <a href="/wiki/Gellert_Grindelwald" title="Gellert Grindelwald">Grindelwald</a> long enough to be saved by <a href="/wiki/Leta_Lestrange" title="Leta Lestrange">Leta Lestrange</a>, and later in her aiding <a href="/wiki/Nicolas_Flamel" title="Nicolas Flamel">Nicolas Flamel</a> in casting the <a href="/wiki/General_Counter-Spell" title="General Counter-Spell">General Counter-Spell</a> to contain and extinguish the violent black flames.</li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Charm" title="Charm">Charms</a></b>: Tina was shown to be highly capable in charms, and had a keen interest in this field, given that she owned the book <i><a href="/wiki/Spell_Casting_in_the_Age_of_Rappaport%27s_Law" title="Spell Casting in the Age of Rappaport&#39;s Law">Spell Casting in the Age of Rappaport's Law</a></i>. Tina's talent in charms was shown when she set the table for a dinner using a <a href="/wiki/Locomotion_Charm" title="Locomotion Charm">Locomotion Charm</a>, found the <a href="/wiki/Lestrange_family" title="Lestrange family">Lestrange family</a> section in the <a href="/wiki/French_Ministry_of_Magic_Records_Room" title="French Ministry of Magic Records Room">French Ministry of Magic Archives</a> with a single spell, was able to protect herself for a prolonged period of time against Grindelwald's black fire with the <a href="/wiki/Shield_Charm" title="Shield Charm">Shield Charm</a> while on her own before <a href="/wiki/Leta_Lestrange" title="Leta Lestrange">Leta Lestrange</a> saved her, and when Tina was later able to quickly follow <a href="/wiki/Nicolas_Flamel" title="Nicolas Flamel">Nicolas Flamel</a>'s instructions on how to cast the <a href="/wiki/General_Counter-Spell" title="General Counter-Spell">General Counter-Spell</a> necessary to contain and extinguish the expanding black fire.</li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Transfiguration" title="Transfiguration">Transfiguration</a></b>: As an Auror, Tina was skilled in Transfiguration, notably being able to transform her clothing into a dress before entering the <a href="/wiki/The_Blind_Pig" title="The Blind Pig">Blind Pig</a> speakeasy, and later <a href="/wiki/Incarcerous_Spell" title="Incarcerous Spell">conjuring ropes</a> to bind <a href="/wiki/Theseus_Scamander" title="Theseus Scamander">Theseus Scamander</a>.</li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Divination" title="Divination">Divination</a></b>: Tina had some knowledge of the subject, as she demonstrated a familiarity with <a href="/wiki/The_Predictions_of_Tycho_Dodonus" title="The Predictions of Tycho Dodonus">The Predictions of Tycho Dodonus</a> in a conversation with <a href="/wiki/Yusuf_Kama" title="Yusuf Kama">Yusuf Kama</a>.</li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Apparition" title="Apparition">Apparition</a></b>: Tina, like most accomplished adult wizards and witches, could Apparate, with her doing so across <a href="/wiki/New_York" title="New York">New York City</a> and <a href="/wiki/Paris" title="Paris">Paris</a> with relative ease. Indeed, Tina was more skilled at Apparating than most, since she swiftly apprehended <a href="/wiki/Newton_Scamander" title="Newton Scamander">Newt Scamander</a> before he could react during their first meeting and Apparated away from the <a href="/wiki/Protego_Diabolica" title="Protego Diabolica">black fire</a> left by <a href="/wiki/Gellert_Grindelwald" title="Gellert Grindelwald">Grindelwald</a>, while also saving <a href="/wiki/Jacob_Kowalski" title="Jacob Kowalski">Jacob Kowalski</a> in the process.</li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Nonverbal_spell" title="Nonverbal spell">Nonverbal magic</a></b>: Tina was quite skilled at casting nonverbal spells, as she set the table for dinner using a <a href="/wiki/Locomotion_Charm" title="Locomotion Charm">Locomotion Charm</a>, <a href="/wiki/Transfiguration" title="Transfiguration">transfigured</a> her clothing into a dress, restrained <a href="/wiki/Theseus_Scamander" title="Theseus Scamander">Theseus Scamander</a>, duelled against <a href="/wiki/Gellert_Grindelwald" title="Gellert Grindelwald">Gellert Grindelwald</a> (disguised as <a href="/wiki/Percival_Graves" title="Percival Graves">Percival Graves</a>), found the <a href="/wiki/Lestrange_family" title="Lestrange family">Lestrange family</a> section in the <a href="/wiki/French_Ministry_of_Magic_Records_Room" title="French Ministry of Magic Records Room">French Ministry of Magic Archives</a>, and later assisted <a href="/wiki/Newton_Scamander" title="Newton Scamander">Newt Scamander</a>, <a href="/wiki/Theseus_Scamander" title="Theseus Scamander">Theseus Scamander</a>, <a href="/wiki/Nicolas_Flamel" title="Nicolas Flamel">Nicolas Flamel</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Yusuf_Kama" title="Yusuf Kama">Yusuf Kama</a> in containing and dissipating the lethally dangerous <a href="/wiki/Protego_Diabolica" title="Protego Diabolica">black fire</a> left behind by <a href="/wiki/Gellert_Grindelwald" title="Gellert Grindelwald">Grindelwald</a>, all without uttering a single <a href="/wiki/Incantation" title="Incantation">incantation</a>.</li>
<li><b>Indomitable willpower</b>: Tina possessed immense willpower and bravery, since she persisted with her job at the <a href="/wiki/Magical_Congress_of_the_United_States_of_America" title="Magical Congress of the United States of America">MACUSA</a> after being demoted to a <a href="/wiki/Federal_Wand_Permit_Officer" title="Federal Wand Permit Officer">Federal Wand Permit Officer</a>, she did her best to reach out to <a href="/wiki/Credence_Barebone" title="Credence Barebone">Credence Barebone</a> despite <a href="/wiki/Gellert_Grindelwald" title="Gellert Grindelwald">Gellert Grindelwald</a> getting in her way, and she consistently found excuses to oppose the <a href="/wiki/New_Salem_Philanthropic_Society" title="New Salem Philanthropic Society">Second Salemer</a> No-Majes. Tina's willpower also allowed her to fairly quickly snap out of the mesmerising effect of the <a href="/wiki/Death_potion" title="Death potion">Death potion</a>. Moreover, Tina, unlike her sister <a href="/wiki/Queenie_Goldstein" title="Queenie Goldstein">Queenie</a>, demonstrated an incorruptible moral code, as she resisted <a href="/wiki/Gellert_Grindelwald" title="Gellert Grindelwald">Gellert Grindelwald</a>'s persuasive speech, and joined the <a href="/wiki/Global_wizarding_war" title="Global wizarding war">Global wizarding war</a> efforts against him.</li></ul>

<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Newt_Scamander">Newt Scamander</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Porpentina_Goldstein?veaction=edit&amp;section=18" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Newt Scamander">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Porpentina_Goldstein?action=edit&amp;section=18" title="Edit section: Newt Scamander"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>

<p>Newt, to say the least, was the <a href="/wiki/Love" title="Love">love</a> of Tina's life. The two initially got off to a rough start after Tina followed him around while he caused trouble in a bank, and she kept having to demand he take action since he clearly did not want to. Eventually, she turned him in to MACUSA for the threat he was unintentionally posing to New York. However, after he saved her life they began to bond while they were on the run from MACUSA as "Percival Graves" wanted to use her as a scapegoat. They also teamed up when Credence attacked the city. They temporarily parted in good spirits after the fight, with Tina promising to buy his book. Newt said she had "eyes just like a salamander". The two worked together once again when Grindelwald was summoning his followers. Later in her life, she married Newt, with the two happily living together in <a href="/wiki/Dorset" title="Dorset">Dorset</a>, <a href="/wiki/England" title="England">England</a>. 
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Gellert_Grindelwald">Gellert Grindelwald</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Porpentina_Goldstein?veaction=edit&amp;section=19" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Gellert Grindelwald">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Porpentina_Goldstein?action=edit&amp;section=19" title="Edit section: Gellert Grindelwald"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>

<p>Tina had interacted with Grindelwald while he was disguised as Percival Graves. Her prim personality put them at odds. During Newt's interrogation, Grindelwald sentenced her and Newt to death, for her participation, without hesitation. Tina attempted to incapacitate him during the hunt for Credence in New York, infuriating him, as he angrily told her she always turned up when he least wanted her, implying she may have intervened with his attempts to find the Obscurus previously. She lost to him when he attempted to crack her skull by throwing a car at her without hesitation, but she survived. A year later, while convincing Queenie to join his ranks, Grindelwald painted Tina in a negative light by telling Queenie that it wasn't the latter's fault for having an Auror as a sister. He then ruthlessly tried to burn her alive with his dark flames and would have succeeded had Leta not interfered. 
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Credence_Barebone">Credence Barebone</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Porpentina_Goldstein?veaction=edit&amp;section=20" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Credence Barebone">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Porpentina_Goldstein?action=edit&amp;section=20" title="Edit section: Credence Barebone"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Tina first encountered Credence when he was being physically abused by his mother. Outraged, she rescued him and consoled him afterwards. As a result, Tina lost her job and Credence began having dreams about her. She was surprised when he was revealed to be the Obscurus that had been running rampant across New York on a few occasions and had now revealed himself openly. True to her immense compassion, however, she attempted to "save" him rather than share everyone else's objective to destroy him. In her attempts, she battled Grindelwald (Graves) and tried to soothe his intense energy, with Newt noting she was the only person capable of coming anywhere close to reasoning with the Obscurial. She cried out when he was destroyed. When Credence was revealed to be very much alive, she determinedly sought him out, fearing for the child's well-being, though sadly the enemy found him first. 
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Porpentina_Goldstein?veaction=edit&amp;section=21" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Porpentina_Goldstein?action=edit&amp;section=21" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p><i>Porpentina</i> is derived from the archaic word "Porpentine". Porpentine is a form of the term "porcupine", used by <a href="/wiki/William_Shakespeare" title="William Shakespeare">William Shakespeare</a> in the play <a href="/wiki/Hamlet" title="Hamlet">Hamlet</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-30" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-30">&#91;30&#93;</a></sup>  
</p><p><i>Tina</i> originates from the Old English "Tyne" meaning river.  
</p><p><i>Esther</i> may come from the eponymous heroine of the Book of Esther, a Jewish queen whose story of thwarting the genocide of her people is the basis of the celebration of Purim in Jewish tradition. The name has several proposed origins, including the Median word <i>astra,</i>meaning "myrtle" (which would match Esther's Hebrew name, Hadassah, which shares this meaning); the Persian word <i>setareh</i>, meaning "star" (as Esther was said to be "as beautiful as the Morning Star", aka the planet Venus, named after the Roman goddess of love); or a derivation of Ishtar, an ancient Mesopotamian goddess associated with love, beauty, sex, desire, fertility, war, justice, and political power. The name's root letters in Hebrew mean "hide" or "conceal", with their passive form meaning "to be hidden".  
</p><p><i>Goldstein</i> is an occupational German name and an Ashkenazi Jewish surname, meaning "gold" with Stein being translated to "stone". 
</p>
