<b>James Tuckett</b> (born c. <a href="/wiki/1984" title="1984">1984</a>-<a href="/wiki/1985" title="1985">1985</a><sup id="cite_ref-age_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-age-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>) was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> who began attending <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> in <a href="/wiki/1996" title="1996">1996</a><sup id="cite_ref-age_1-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-age-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> and was <a href="/wiki/Sorting_ceremony" title="Sorting ceremony">sorted</a> into <a href="/wiki/Hufflepuff" title="Hufflepuff">Hufflepuff House</a>. In his <a href="/wiki/1996-1997_school_year" class="mw-redirect" title="1996-1997 school year">first year</a>, he caught <a href="/wiki/Draco_Malfoy" title="Draco Malfoy">Draco Malfoy</a> trying to steal the <a href="/wiki/Birds_on_the_Seventh_Floor" title="Birds on the Seventh Floor">birds</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Seventh-floor_corridor" title="Seventh-floor corridor">Seventh-floor corridor</a>, but was too frightened to tell anyone about it due to being intimidated by Draco and his <a href="/wiki/Draco_Malfoy%27s_gang" title="Draco Malfoy&#39;s gang">friends</a>. <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> gave him some <a href="/wiki/Cauldron_Cake" title="Cauldron Cake">Cauldron Cakes</a> and afterwards he felt good enough to tell Harry what he knew. James also played informal <a href="/wiki/Quidditch" title="Quidditch">Quidditch</a> matches with other students during his <a href="/wiki/First_year" title="First year">first year</a>.
</p>
