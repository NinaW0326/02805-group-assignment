<b>Lydia Fry</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggle</a> media personality. She gave an interview in the <a href="/wiki/28_February" title="28 February">28th February</a>-<a href="/wiki/26_March" title="26 March">26th March</a> <a href="/wiki/1997" title="1997">1997</a> issue of <i><a href="/wiki/My_Teeth" title="My Teeth">My Teeth</a></i>, in which she shared her latest personal tips on life and maintaining dental health.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> In the summer of that same year, she gave an interview in <i><a href="/wiki/Radio_Times" title="Radio Times">Radio Times</a></i>, in which she discussed what motivated her to take up <a href="/wiki/Fishing" title="Fishing">fishing</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
