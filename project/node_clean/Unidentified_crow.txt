This <b><a href="/wiki/Crow" title="Crow">crow</a></b> sat on the top of a wooden marker, holding <a href="/wiki/Buckbeak" title="Buckbeak">Buckbeak</a> the <a href="/wiki/Hippogriff" title="Hippogriff">Hippogriff</a>. The crow appeared to be a kind of guard, biting <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> when he tried to free Buckbeak. The crow then flew away when the <a href="/wiki/Walden_Macnair" title="Walden Macnair">Executioner</a> used his <a href="/wiki/Axe" title="Axe">axe</a> to cut a <a href="/wiki/Pumpkin" title="Pumpkin">pumpkin</a> from <a href="/wiki/Rubeus_Hagrid" title="Rubeus Hagrid">Rubeus Hagrid</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Rubeus_Hagrid%27s_pumpkin_patch" title="Rubeus Hagrid&#39;s pumpkin patch">patch</a> in two.<sup id="cite_ref-POAF_1-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-POAF-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
