<b>L. Wakefield</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch or wizard</a>,&#160;<a href="/wiki/Numerology" title="Numerology">numerologist</a>, and the author of at least one book on the subject, <i><a href="/wiki/Numerology_(book)" title="Numerology (book)">Numerology</a></i>.<sup id="cite_ref-MinaLima_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-MinaLima-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Wakefield also gave their name to <a href="/wiki/L._Wakefield%27s_Broomcare_Wax" title="L. Wakefield&#39;s Broomcare Wax">L. Wakefield's Broomcare Wax</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> It is unknown whether Wakefield was this product's inventor or simply its manufacturer.
</p>
