<b>Hengist of Woodcroft</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Middle_Ages" title="Middle Ages">Medieval</a> <a href="/wiki/England" title="England">English</a> <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a>, best known as the founder of the village of <a href="/wiki/Hogsmeade" title="Hogsmeade">Hogsmeade</a> in <a href="/wiki/Scotland" title="Scotland">Scotland</a>, the only all-wizarding village in <a href="/wiki/Great_Britain" title="Great Britain">Great Britain</a>.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Hengist_of_Woodcroft?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Hengist_of_Woodcroft?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Hengist was born in the <a href="/wiki/Middle_Ages" title="Middle Ages">Middle Ages</a>, no earlier than <a href="/wiki/First_millennium_A.D." title="First millennium A.D.">982</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-birthdate_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-birthdate-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> in <a href="/wiki/Woodcroft" title="Woodcroft">Woodcroft</a>, <a href="/wiki/England" title="England">Gloucestershire</a>. In his youth, Hengist attended <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a>, where he was <a href="/wiki/Sorting_ceremony" title="Sorting ceremony">sorted</a> into <a href="/wiki/Hufflepuff" title="Hufflepuff">Hufflepuff</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-pottermore_2-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-pottermore-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> He might have been taught by Helga Hufflepuff herself.
</p>
<figure class="thumb tleft show-info-icon" style="width: 182px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/8/86/Hengist_of_Woodcroft_USAcard.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20091112000229" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/8/86/Hengist_of_Woodcroft_USAcard.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/180?cb=20091112000229" decoding="async" width="180" height="233" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="Hengist of Woodcroft USAcard.jpg" data-image-key="Hengist_of_Woodcroft_USAcard.jpg" /></a> 	 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:Hengist_of_Woodcroft_USAcard.jpg" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption">Hengist founding Hogsmeade</p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure>
<p>Hengist is traditionally credited with the founding of the village of <a href="/wiki/Hogsmeade" title="Hogsmeade">Hogsmeade</a> after being driven from his home by <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggle</a> persecutors. According to popular lore, Hengist used the <a href="/wiki/Three_Broomsticks_Inn" title="Three Broomsticks Inn">Three Broomsticks Inn</a> as a home.<sup id="cite_ref-PSvg_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PSvg-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Hengist died at some point during the <a href="/wiki/Middle_Ages" title="Middle Ages">Middle Ages</a>. Remembered for his founding of <a href="/wiki/Hogsmeade" title="Hogsmeade">Hogsmeade</a>, he was immortalised in a <a href="/wiki/Chocolate_Frog_Card" title="Chocolate Frog Card">Chocolate Frog Card</a><sup id="cite_ref-PSvg_3-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PSvg-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> Hengist of Woodcroft's Chocolate Frog Card was one of the very first collected by <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-ps6_4-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-ps6-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> There was also a bust of Hengist on the <a href="/wiki/Fourth-floor_corridor" title="Fourth-floor corridor">Fourth Floor Corridor</a> of <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Castle" title="Hogwarts Castle">Hogwarts Castle</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-POAG_5-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-POAG-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>On <a href="/wiki/1_September" title="1 September">1 September</a>, <a href="/wiki/1991" title="1991">1991</a>, <a href="/wiki/Prefect" title="Prefect">Prefect</a> <a href="/wiki/Gabriel_Truman" title="Gabriel Truman">Gabriel Truman</a> welcomed the <a href="/wiki/Hufflepuff" title="Hufflepuff">Hufflepuff</a> <a href="/wiki/First_year" title="First year">first years</a> to <a href="/wiki/Hufflepuff_Basement" title="Hufflepuff Basement">Hufflepuff Basement</a> with a short speech in which he mentioned Hengist of Woodcroft as one of the many notable Hogwarts students who were <a href="/wiki/Sorting_ceremony" title="Sorting ceremony">sorted</a>, like them, into Hufflepuff.<sup id="cite_ref-pottermore_2-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-pottermore-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Hengist_of_Woodcroft?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Hengist_of_Woodcroft?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The name "Hengist" means "stallion" in Germanic. Hengist and his brother Horsa were the leaders of the first Germanic settlers in Britain. Hengist established a kingdom in <a href="/wiki/Kent" title="Kent">Kent</a> in the <a href="/wiki/First_millennium_A.D." title="First millennium A.D.">5th century</a>.
</p>
