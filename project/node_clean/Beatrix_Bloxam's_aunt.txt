The <b>aunt</b> of <a href="/wiki/Beatrix_Bloxam" title="Beatrix Bloxam">Beatrix Bloxam</a> had <a href="/wiki/Beatrix_Bloxam%27s_cousins" title="Beatrix Bloxam&#39;s cousins">children</a> who were older than her niece. 
When she was young, Beatrix listened at a keyhole and was disturbed to hear this aunt tell her children <a href="/wiki/The_Warlock%27s_Hairy_Heart" title="The Warlock&#39;s Hairy Heart">The Warlock's Hairy Heart</a>, as well as parts of a shocking tale involving Beatrix's uncle <a href="/wiki/Nobby" title="Nobby">Nobby</a>, a <a href="/wiki/Hag" title="Hag">hag</a> who lived in the area, and a sack of <a href="/wiki/Bouncing_Bulb" title="Bouncing Bulb">Bouncing Bulbs</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>It is unlikely that Beatrix's aunt was married to her uncle Nobby, as she probably wouldn't have chosen to tell her children a scandalous story about their father, particularly not one of the nature implied.
</p>
