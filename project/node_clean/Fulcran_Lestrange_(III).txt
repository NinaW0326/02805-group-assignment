<b>Fulcran Lestrange</b> was a French wizard and a member of the <a href="/wiki/Pure-blood" title="Pure-blood">pure-blood</a> <a href="/wiki/Lestrange_family" title="Lestrange family">Lestrange family</a>. He was the son of <a href="/wiki/Fulcran_Lestrange_(II)" title="Fulcran Lestrange (II)">Fulcran Lestrange</a> and <a href="/wiki/Sot%C3%A8re_Lestrange" title="Sotère Lestrange">Sotère Petit</a>. He married his cousin <a href="/wiki/Quintilla_Lestrange" title="Quintilla Lestrange">Quintilla Lestrange</a> and had a daughter with her, <a href="/wiki/Noz%C3%A9a_Lestrange" title="Nozéa Lestrange">Nozéa Lestrange</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
