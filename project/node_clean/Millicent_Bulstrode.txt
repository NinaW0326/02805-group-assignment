<p><b>Millicent Bulstrode</b> (b. <a href="/wiki/1979" title="1979">1979</a>/<a href="/wiki/1980" title="1980">1980</a>) was a <a href="/wiki/Half-blood" title="Half-blood">half-blood</a><sup id="cite_ref-blood_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-blood-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch</a>. She began attending <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> and was sorted into <a href="/wiki/Slytherin" title="Slytherin">Slytherin House</a> in <a href="/wiki/1991" title="1991">1991</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-PS7_3-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PS7-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> In her <a href="/wiki/Fifth_year" title="Fifth year">fifth year</a>, Millicent became a member of the short-lived <a href="/wiki/Inquisitorial_Squad" title="Inquisitorial Squad">Inquisitorial Squad</a> led by <a href="/wiki/Dolores_Umbridge" title="Dolores Umbridge">Dolores Umbridge</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP32_8-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP32-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_life">Early life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Millicent_Bulstrode?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Early life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Millicent_Bulstrode?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Early life"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Millicent Bulstrode was born sometime between <a href="/wiki/1_September" title="1 September">1 September</a> <a href="/wiki/1979" title="1979">1979</a> and <a href="/wiki/31_August" title="31 August">31 August</a> <a href="/wiki/1980" title="1980">1980</a> into the <a href="/wiki/Pure-blood" title="Pure-blood">pure-blooded</a>, <a href="/wiki/Sacred_Twenty-Eight" title="Sacred Twenty-Eight">Sacred Twenty-Eight</a> <a href="/wiki/Bulstrode_family" title="Bulstrode family">Bulstrode family</a>, though was herself a half-blood.<sup id="cite_ref-PM_7-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PM-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-blood_1-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-blood-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> She may have been related to <a href="/wiki/Violetta_Bulstrode" title="Violetta Bulstrode">Violetta Black</a> <span class="template-c">(née <a href="/wiki/Bulstrode_family" title="Bulstrode family">Bulstrode</a>)</span> wife of <a href="/wiki/Cygnus_Black_II" title="Cygnus Black II">Cygnus Black II</a>, and therefore related to the <a href="/wiki/Lestrange_family" title="Lestrange family">Lestrange</a>, <a href="/wiki/Malfoy_family" title="Malfoy family">Malfoy</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Tonks_family" title="Tonks family">Tonks</a> families.
</p>

<p>Millicent started her education at Hogwarts in <a href="/wiki/1991" title="1991">1991</a>. Prior to the school year, she likely purchased her equipment from <a href="/wiki/Diagon_Alley" title="Diagon Alley">Diagon Alley</a>, and brought her cat with her on the <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Express" title="Hogwarts Express">Hogwarts Express</a>. During the <a href="/wiki/Welcoming_Feast" title="Welcoming Feast">Welcoming Feast</a>, Millicent was <a href="/wiki/Sorting_ceremony" title="Sorting ceremony">sorted</a> into the house of <a href="/wiki/Slytherin" title="Slytherin">Slytherin</a> whose head at the time was <a href="/wiki/Severus_Snape" title="Severus Snape">Severus Snape</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-PS7_3-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PS7-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> She would share a dormitory with the other Slytherin girls, <a href="/wiki/Pansy_Parkinson" title="Pansy Parkinson">Pansy Parkinson</a>, <a href="/wiki/Tracey_Davis" title="Tracey Davis">Tracey Davis</a>, <a href="/wiki/Daphne_Greengrass" title="Daphne Greengrass">Daphne Greengrass</a>, and another girl.
</p>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Second_year">Second year</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Millicent_Bulstrode?veaction=edit&amp;section=5" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Second year">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Millicent_Bulstrode?action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Second year"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<div class="quote">
<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets">"<i>Remember Millicent Bulstrode wrestling with me at the Duelling Club? She left this on my robes when she was trying to strangle me! And she’s gone home for Christmas — so I’ll just have to tell the Slytherins I’ve decided to come back..</i>"</span></dd>
<dd>&#8212;Hermione Granger on whose form she will take under Polyjuice Potion<sup class="noprint"><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Chamber_of_Secrets" title="Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets">[src]</a></sup></dd>
<dd></dd></dl></div>
<p>In <a href="/wiki/1992" title="1992">1992</a> Hogwarts became under threat from the re-opened <a href="/wiki/Chamber_of_Secrets" title="Chamber of Secrets">Chamber of Secrets</a> and the <a href="/wiki/Salazar_Slytherin%27s_basilisk" class="mw-redirect" title="Salazar Slytherin&#39;s basilisk">basilisk</a> it contained inside. As a precaution, students were taught how to duel. Millicent and <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione Granger</a> were paired together during the short-lived <a href="/wiki/Duelling_Club" title="Duelling Club">Duelling Club</a>. Their duel spiralled into a <a href="/wiki/Muggle_duelling" title="Muggle duelling">physical brawl</a>, with Millicent holding Hermione in a headlock, in spite of the standard "no contact" rule in <a href="/wiki/Duelling" title="Duelling">duelling</a>. Hermione's friend, <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>, came to her rescue, breaking the two girls up.<sup id="cite_ref-COS11_6-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COS11-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup>

<p>In <a href="/wiki/1996" title="1996">1996</a>, Millicent was one of the students of the Slytherin-dominated <a href="/wiki/Inquisitorial_Squad" title="Inquisitorial Squad">Inquisitorial Squad</a> an organisation started by then-headmistress <a href="/wiki/Dolores_Umbridge" title="Dolores Umbridge">Dolores Umbridge</a> to silence all opposition to <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">Ministry of Magic</a> activities at Hogwarts. She was one of the students who caught <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>, <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione Granger</a>, <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ron Weasley</a>, <a href="/wiki/Neville_Longbottom" title="Neville Longbottom">Neville Longbottom</a>, <a href="/wiki/Ginevra_Weasley" title="Ginevra Weasley">Ginny Weasley</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Luna_Lovegood" title="Luna Lovegood">Luna Lovegood</a>, who were attempting to use the Floo Network in Umbridge's office to find out if <a href="/wiki/Sirius_Black" title="Sirius Black">Sirius Black</a> had been attacked in the <a href="/wiki/Department_of_Mysteries" title="Department of Mysteries">Department of Mysteries</a>. Particularly, she helped in holding down Hermione.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP32_8-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP32-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup> However, given how the four remaining DA members managed to overpower the Inquisitorial Squad and incapacitated them with various <a href="/wiki/Stunning_Spell" title="Stunning Spell">Stunning Spells</a>, <a href="/wiki/Disarming_Charm" title="Disarming Charm">Disarming Charms</a> and a powerful <a href="/wiki/Impediment_Jinx" title="Impediment Jinx">Impediment Jinx</a> cast by <a href="/wiki/Neville_Longbottom" title="Neville Longbottom">Neville Longbottom</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP33_10-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP33-10">&#91;10&#93;</a></sup> Millicent was most likely herself assaulted and injured with their offensive spells, as the other Squad members were quite badly.
</p>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Sixth_year">Sixth year</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Millicent_Bulstrode?veaction=edit&amp;section=8" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Sixth year">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Millicent_Bulstrode?action=edit&amp;section=8" title="Edit section: Sixth year"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<p>In Millicent's sixth year she had lost her former power of being in the <a href="/wiki/Inquisitorial_Squad" title="Inquisitorial Squad">Inquisitorial Squad</a> and could no longer use it to her advantage. Her fellow Slytherin <a href="/wiki/Draco_Malfoy" title="Draco Malfoy">Draco Malfoy</a> had joined the Death Eaters, which put a strain on his friendships with other Slytherins, possibly including Millicent. Severus Snape, her head of house, had now taken on the position of <a href="/wiki/Defence_Against_the_Dark_Arts" title="Defence Against the Dark Arts">Defence Against the Dark Arts</a> teacher and favoured Slytherins during his lessons. Later on in this year, headmaster <a href="/wiki/Albus_Dumbledore" title="Albus Dumbledore">Albus Dumbledore</a> was killed.
</p>

<p>Due to her half blood status and <a href="/wiki/Slytherin" title="Slytherin">Slytherin</a> relations, Millicent likely was allowed to return to Hogwarts in 1997 when it was under the control of <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a>, with her former head of house <a href="/wiki/Severus_Snape" title="Severus Snape">Severus Snape</a> acting as headmaster. If she did, Millicent would have been present for the evacuation of Hogwarts preceding the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a>, and it would be assumed that Millicent left the castle along with the other members of the House of Slytherin, rather than choosing to fight Voldemort's army. It could also be a possibility that Millicent would return to Hogwarts, fighting against the school alongside Death Eaters, as did some other students of Slytherin.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Physical_appearance">Physical appearance</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Millicent_Bulstrode?veaction=edit&amp;section=10" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Physical appearance">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Millicent_Bulstrode?action=edit&amp;section=10" title="Edit section: Physical appearance"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Millicent was black-haired and had a heavy, jutting jaw. She had a large, square build that allowed her to physically intimidate smaller people, and was bigger than <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> in their second year. <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ron Weasley</a> once remarked that Millicent was "ugly," and Harry thought that she looked like a <a href="/wiki/Hag" title="Hag">hag</a> he had seen pictured in <i><a href="/wiki/Holidays_with_Hags" title="Holidays with Hags">Holidays with Hags</a></i>.<sup id="cite_ref-COS11_6-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COS11-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup> She was also described by Harry as being "no <a href="/wiki/Pixie" title="Pixie">pixie</a>."<sup id="cite_ref-COS12_9-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COS12-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Personality_and_traits">Personality and traits</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Millicent_Bulstrode?veaction=edit&amp;section=11" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Personality and traits">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Millicent_Bulstrode?action=edit&amp;section=11" title="Edit section: Personality and traits"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<div class="quote">
<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix">"<i>And Hermione began to cry weakly into the back of Millicent Bulstrode’s robes. Millicent stopped trying to squash her against the wall immediately and dodged out of her way looking disgusted.</i>"</span></dd>
<dd>&#8212;Millicent's hostile personality<sup class="noprint"><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Order_of_the_Phoenix" title="Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix">[src]</a></sup></dd>
<dd></dd></dl></div>
<p>Given her interactions with others and her membership of the <a href="/wiki/Inquisitorial_Squad" title="Inquisitorial Squad">Inquisitorial Squad</a> in her fifth year, Millicent appeared to be a rather mean-spirited and aggressive witch who enjoyed bullying other Hogwarts students, mainly by using her large figure to intimidate and even in some cases assault them, as shown when she physically restrained the smaller Hermione Granger in both 1992 and 1996.<sup id="cite_ref-COS11_6-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COS11-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-OOTP32_8-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP32-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup> She also would have used her authority in <a href="/wiki/Dolores_Umbridge" title="Dolores Umbridge">Umbridge's</a> short-lived <a href="/wiki/Inquisitorial_Squad" title="Inquisitorial Squad">Inquisitorial Squad</a> to bully and harass other students, and was therefore probably supportive of Umbridge's reign of cruelty and authoritarianism over Hogwarts.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP32_8-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP32-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup> Having been sorted into Slytherin house, she was most likely a rather cunning, resourceful and ambitious girl, as most members of her house were.
</p>

<ul><li><b><a href="/wiki/Millicent_Bulstrode%27s_cat" title="Millicent Bulstrode&#39;s cat">Cat</a></b>: Millicent owned a pet <a href="/wiki/Cat" title="Cat">cat</a>, which was of <a href="/wiki/Black_cat" title="Black cat">black hair</a>, an acceptable animal to keep as a pet at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts</a>. <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione Granger</a> unknowingly extracted some of her cat's <a href="/wiki/Cat_hair" title="Cat hair">hair</a> in <a href="/wiki/1992" title="1992">1992</a> during the <a href="/wiki/Duelling_Club" title="Duelling Club">Duelling Club</a>, which she mixed in her glass of <a href="/wiki/Polyjuice_Potion" title="Polyjuice Potion">Polyjuice Potion</a>, believing the hair to be <a href="/wiki/Human_hair" title="Human hair">Human hair</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-COS12_9-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COS12-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup></li>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Millicent_Bulstrode?veaction=edit&amp;section=13" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Millicent_Bulstrode?action=edit&amp;section=13" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>The name <i>Millicent</i> is Germanic, meaning "she with the gentle gait". This is ironic, considering that Millicent Bulstrode is not portrayed as gentle or graceful in the least; <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> once remarked that she's "no <a href="/wiki/Pixie" title="Pixie">pixie</a>" while they were taking <a href="/wiki/Polyjuice_Potion" title="Polyjuice Potion">Polyjuice Potion</a>, in relation to the fact that Millicent, Crabbe and Goyle would not fit into a single toilet cubicle.</li>
<li>Millicent may also come from the Latin words <i>mille</i>, meaning "thousand", and <i>centum</i>, meaning "hundred". It also sounds similar to the word "militant", which matches her aggressive personality.</li></ul>

She is also mentioned by other female students as being "a real witch", and she leaves Harry many a snarky remark.</li></ul>
