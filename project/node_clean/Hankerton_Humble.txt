<b>Hankerton Humble</b> was the first ever <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Caretaker" title="Hogwarts Caretaker">caretaker</a> of <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a>. He was hired by the <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_founders" title="Hogwarts founders">founders of Hogwarts</a> themselves, and began the never-ending caretaker tradition of hating <a href="/wiki/Peeves" title="Peeves">Peeves</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-WW_4-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-WW-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Hankerton_Humble?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Hankerton_Humble?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Hankerton was born sometime in the <a href="/wiki/Middle_Ages" title="Middle Ages">Middle Ages</a>, presumably in <a href="/wiki/Great_Britain" title="Great Britain">Great Britain</a> or <a href="/wiki/Ireland" title="Ireland">Ireland</a> given that Hogwarts only accepts students from there.<sup id="cite_ref-WW_4-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-WW-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Soon after the founding of Hogwarts, he was accepted as the caretaker there. During his time, he started the everlasting tradition of caretakers' hating <a href="/wiki/Peeves" title="Peeves">Peeves</a>; it is unknown how he got along with the students, though judging by his name it may have been fairly well. Sometime before <a href="/wiki/1876" class="mw-redirect" title="1876">1876</a>, however, he had retired and was succeeded (though by how many generations is unknown) by <a href="/wiki/Rancorous_Carpe" title="Rancorous Carpe">Rancorous Carpe</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-WW_4-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-WW-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Hankerton_Humble?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Hankerton_Humble?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p><i>Hankerton</i> is a town in <a href="/wiki/Wiltshire" title="Wiltshire">Wiltshire</a>, known for its ancient church. <i>Humble</i> means "meek", or "polite", possibly reflecting on his personality. <i>Humble</i> could also be short for "humblebee" another term for "bumblebee."
</p>
