<p>This <b>individual</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> and actor who played the part of <a href="/wiki/Crapaud" title="Crapaud">Crapaud</a> in a disastrous performance of <a href="/wiki/Malecrit" title="Malecrit">Malecrit</a>'s classic wizarding play <i><a href="/wiki/H%C3%A9las,_Je_me_suis_Transfigur%C3%A9_Les_Pieds" title="Hélas, Je me suis Transfiguré Les Pieds">Hélas, Je me suis Transfiguré Les Pieds</a></i>.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Actor_who_played_Crapaud?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Actor_who_played_Crapaud?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The names of the actors of this particular performance were not recorded and, as such, are unknown to history.<sup id="cite_ref-Wonderbook_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Wonderbook-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>This individual's performance, alongside the performance of the <a href="/wiki/Actor_who_played_Grenouille" title="Actor who played Grenouille">wizard</a> who played <a href="/wiki/Grenouille" title="Grenouille">Grenouille</a>, was not particularly appealing and, as such, the bored audience spent much of act one throwing pumpkins at them.<sup id="cite_ref-Wonderbook_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Wonderbook-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>During the eponymous foot-transfiguration scene, the <a href="/wiki/Special_effects_wizard" title="Special effects wizard">special effects wizard</a> tried to liven up the play by conjuring a <a href="/wiki/Fire-Making_Spell" title="Fire-Making Spell">stream of flames</a> instead of the puff of yellow smoke he was supposed to conjure. The curtains caught alight and soon enough the whole theatre was ablaze. The actor playing Crapaud took this as his moment to shine and, protected by a <a href="/wiki/Flame-Freezing_Charm" title="Flame-Freezing Charm">Flame-Freezing Charm</a>, delivered the play's final rousing speech in the flame-engulfed stage, as the rest of the cast and the audience fled in panic, and the <a href="/wiki/Special_effects_wizard" title="Special effects wizard">special effects wizard</a> vainly trying to put out the fire while dodging <a href="/wiki/Hex" title="Hex">hexes</a> from the play's director.<sup id="cite_ref-Wonderbook_1-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Wonderbook-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
