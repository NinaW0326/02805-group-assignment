<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_female_Hungarian_Horntail?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_female_Hungarian_Horntail?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<div class="quote">
<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire">"<i>Harry put his hand into the silk bag and pulled out the Hungarian Horntail, and the number four.</i>"</span></dd>
<dd>&#8212;The miniature <a href="/wiki/Dragon" title="Dragon">dragon</a><sup class="noprint"><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Goblet_of_Fire" title="Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire">[src]</a></sup></dd>
<dd></dd></dl></div><p> Due to his earlier espionage, <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> was, in fact, aware of the sole remaining choice before he drew it from the bag of selection. According to <a href="/wiki/Charles_Weasley" title="Charles Weasley">Charles Weasley</a>, she could shoot <a href="/wiki/Fire" title="Fire">fire</a> forty feet. He felt sorry for whomever got the Horntail, as its "back end's as dangerous as its front", and felt unable to tell his mother of the terrors awaiting Harry.<sup id="cite_ref-GOF19_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF19-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> He proceeded the inevitable and drew the tiny Horntail <a href="/wiki/Model_dragon" title="Model dragon">model dragon</a> depicting the from the bag, revealing his opponent to the rest of the occupants in the <a href="/wiki/Waiting_Tent" title="Waiting Tent">Waiting Tent</a>.
</p><figure class="thumb tleft show-info-icon" style="width: 222px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/b/ba/Accio_Firebolt.gif/revision/latest?cb=20190817235111" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/b/ba/Accio_Firebolt.gif/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/220?cb=20190817235111" decoding="async" width="220" height="92" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="Accio Firebolt.gif" data-image-key="Accio_Firebolt.gif" /></a> 	 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:Accio_Firebolt.gif" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption">Harry during the First Task</p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure>
<p>When it was Harry's turn, he <a href="/wiki/Summoning_Charm" title="Summoning Charm">Summoned</a> <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter%27s_Firebolt" title="Harry Potter&#39;s Firebolt">his Firebolt</a> <a href="/wiki/Broomstick" title="Broomstick">broomstick</a> to him and used it to manoeuvre around the dragon to retrieve the <a href="/wiki/Golden_egg" title="Golden egg">Golden egg</a>. Charlie helped transport the dragon from <a href="/wiki/Romania" title="Romania">Romania</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-GOF20_1-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF20-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>After the task, Harry kept the <a href="/wiki/Model_dragon" title="Model dragon">miniature version</a> of the Horntail he received when he drew the Horntail for the Task.<sup id="cite_ref-GOF21_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF21-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>When faced with the agony of trying to ask someone to the <a href="/wiki/Yule_Ball" title="Yule Ball">Yule Ball</a>, Harry said that he would have preferred facing the Horntail again.<sup id="cite_ref-GOF22_4-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF22-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<ul><li>The neck part of <a href="/wiki/Serpent_of_Slytherin" title="Serpent of Slytherin">the Basilisk</a> prop from the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Chamber_of_Secrets_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets (film)">film adaptation</a> of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Chamber_of_Secrets" title="Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets">Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets</a></i> was reused to create the neck of this Hungarian Horntail.<sup id="cite_ref-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li>In the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Goblet_of_Fire_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire (film)">film adaptation</a> of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Goblet_of_Fire" title="Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire">Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire</a></i>, the Horntail broke free of its chain and attacked Harry. The two of them fought in an exciting chase that spanned all around the castle grounds, almost causing Harry to fall to his death at one point when he was trying to reach his <a href="/wiki/Firebolt" title="Firebolt">Firebolt</a>, but when the chase continued, Harry flew through the <a href="/wiki/Viaduct" title="Viaduct">Viaduct</a>, which the Horntail crashed into, breaking its wing and causing it to fall into the chasm below, presumably to its <a href="/wiki/Death" title="Death">death</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-GOFF_6-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOFF-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li>In the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Half-Blood_Prince_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince (film)">film adaptation</a> of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Half-Blood_Prince" title="Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince">Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince</a></i>, the <a href="/wiki/Model_dragon" title="Model dragon">Hungarian Horntail model</a> that was given to Harry from Barty Crouch Snr is seen in the <a href="/wiki/Weasleys%27_Dragon_Roasted_Nuts" title="Weasleys&#39; Dragon Roasted Nuts">Roast chestnuts sale</a>, located in <a href="/wiki/Diagon_Alley" title="Diagon Alley">Diagon Alley</a>. It is possible that Harry gave his dragon to Fred and George who put it there.</li>
<li>In <i><a href="/wiki/LEGO_Harry_Potter:_Years_1-4" title="LEGO Harry Potter: Years 1-4">LEGO Harry Potter: Years 1-4</a></i>, Hermione falls into the arena with Harry and they both have to battle it. It not only breaks free like in the film, but it also chases them into the castle.<sup id="cite_ref-LEGOY1_7-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-LEGOY1-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li>In the film, Harry is shown to have a sling around his arm after he gets slashed by the tail.</li></ul>
