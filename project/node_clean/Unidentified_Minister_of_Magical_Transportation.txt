<p>This <b>Minister of Magical Transportation</b> (born c. <a href="/wiki/1949" title="1949">1949</a>) was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> who headed the <a href="/wiki/Department_of_Magical_Transportation" title="Department of Magical Transportation">Department of Magical Transportation</a> by <a href="/wiki/1996" title="1996">1996</a>. During his school years at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts</a>, he was a member of the <a href="/wiki/Slug_Club" title="Slug Club">Slug Club</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-script_3-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-script-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_life">Early life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_Minister_of_Magical_Transportation?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Early life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_Minister_of_Magical_Transportation?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Early life"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>This individual was born in around <a href="/wiki/1949" title="1949">1949</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-graduation_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-graduation-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> He attended <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a>, where he was made a member of the <a href="/wiki/Slug_Club" title="Slug Club">Slug Club</a> by his <a href="/wiki/Potions_Master" title="Potions Master">Potions Master</a>, <a href="/wiki/Horace_Slughorn" title="Horace Slughorn">Horace Slughorn</a>. </p>

<p>On <a href="/wiki/20_December" title="20 December">20 December</a>, <a href="/wiki/1996" title="1996">1996</a>, this individual attended a <a href="/wiki/Slug_Club_Christmas_party" title="Slug Club Christmas party">Christmas party</a> at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Castle" title="Hogwarts Castle">Hogwarts Castle</a>, held by his former professor, <a href="/wiki/Horace_Slughorn" title="Horace Slughorn">Horace Slughorn</a>. Slughorn was delighted to see this individual and quickly dismissed <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> upon seeing him.<sup id="cite_ref-script_3-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-script-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
