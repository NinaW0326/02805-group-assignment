<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Lorcan_McLaird?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Lorcan_McLaird?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_life">Early life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Lorcan_McLaird?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Early life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Lorcan_McLaird?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Early life"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Lorcan McLaird was born no later than <a href="/wiki/1906" title="1906">1906</a>, somewhere in the <a href="/wiki/Great_Britain" title="Great Britain">British Isles</a>. His surname suggests he was Scottish, or had Scottish ancestry.
</p><p>In his youth, he attended <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a>, where he was <a href="/wiki/Sorting_ceremony" title="Sorting ceremony">Sorted</a> into <a href="/wiki/Ravenclaw" title="Ravenclaw">Ravenclaw</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-pottermore_3-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-pottermore-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="As_Minister_for_Magic">As Minister for Magic</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Lorcan_McLaird?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: As Minister for Magic">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Lorcan_McLaird?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: As Minister for Magic"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>McLaird rose to the office of <a href="/wiki/Minister_for_Magic" title="Minister for Magic">Minister for Magic</a> of <a href="/wiki/Great_Britain" title="Great Britain">Great Britain</a> and <a href="/wiki/Ireland" title="Ireland">Ireland</a> in <a href="/wiki/1923" title="1923">1923</a>. He was quite a talented <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a>, but was considered something of an eccentric, as he liked to communicate by puffing smoke from the tip of his <a href="/wiki/Wand" title="Wand">wand</a>. Eventually, he was forced out of office in <a href="/wiki/1925" title="1925">1925</a> due to irritation at his eccentricities.<sup id="cite_ref-pottermore2_4-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-pottermore2-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>The <i><a href="/wiki/Daily_Prophet" title="Daily Prophet">Daily Prophet</a></i> reported in its <a href="/wiki/29_November" title="29 November">29 November</a> <a href="/wiki/1926" title="1926">1926</a> issue that <a href="/wiki/Ravenclaw" title="Ravenclaw">Ravenclaw</a> students were planning to honour McLaird.<sup id="cite_ref-FBF_5-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-FBF-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Lorcan_McLaird?veaction=edit&amp;section=4" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Lorcan_McLaird?action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li><a href="/wiki/Luna_Lovegood" title="Luna Lovegood">Luna Lovegood</a> may have named her son — <a href="/wiki/Lorcan_Scamander" title="Lorcan Scamander">Lorcan</a> — after this man, probably in admiration for his eccentricity.</li>
<li>Calvin Coolidge, the thirtieth President of the United States, was also known for being incredibly taciturn. He, too, came into office in 1923.</li></ul>
