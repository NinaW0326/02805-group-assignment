This <b><a href="/wiki/Slytherin" title="Slytherin">Slytherin</a> girl</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch</a> who started attending <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> in <a href="/wiki/1991" title="1991">1991</a>, where she was <a href="/wiki/Sorting_ceremony" title="Sorting ceremony">sorted</a> into&#160;<a href="/wiki/Slytherin" title="Slytherin">Slytherin</a>. In <a href="/wiki/1994" title="1994">1994</a>, she sat behind <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ron Weasley</a> in <a href="/wiki/Bartemius_Crouch_Junior" title="Bartemius Crouch Junior">Barty Crouch Junior</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Defence_Against_the_Dark_Arts" title="Defence Against the Dark Arts">Defence Against the Dark Arts</a> class, disguised as <a href="/wiki/Alastor_Moody" title="Alastor Moody">Alastor Moody</a> at that time using <a href="/wiki/Polyjuice_Potion" title="Polyjuice Potion">Polyjuice Potion</a>. In that lesson, she would have witnessed "Moody" practise all three of the <a href="/wiki/Unforgivable_Curses" title="Unforgivable Curses">Unforgivable Curses</a> on <a href="/wiki/Alastor_Moody%27s_spiders" title="Alastor Moody&#39;s spiders">spiders</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-GOFF_3-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOFF-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
