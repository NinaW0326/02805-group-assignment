<div class="mw-parser-output"><p>The <b>Carneiro Players</b> were a dramatic troupe. In the summer of <a href="/wiki/1997" title="1997">1997</a>, they staged an adapted production of <i><a href="/wiki/Romeo_and_Juliet" title="Romeo and Juliet">Romeo and Juliet</a></i>, <a href="/wiki/William_Shakespeare" title="William Shakespeare">William Shakespeare</a>'s classic tragedy, at the <a href="/wiki/Regency_Playhouse" title="Regency Playhouse">Regency Playhouse</a> in <a href="/wiki/London" title="London">London</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Carneiro_Players?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Carneiro_Players?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>This troupe was named after Mauricio Carneiro, who worked as a costume illustrator for every <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_films" class="mw-redirect" title="Harry Potter films"><i>Harry Potter</i> film</a> from <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Goblet_of_Fire_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire (film)">Goblet of Fire</a></i> onward, and as a costumer for <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Order_of_the_Phoenix_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix (film)">Order of the Phoenix</a></i> and <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Half-Blood_Prince_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince (film)">Half-Blood Prince</a></i>.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup></li></ul>
