This <b>wizard</b> (d. <a href="/wiki/2_May" title="2 May">2 May</a> <a href="/wiki/1998" title="1998">1998</a>) was a <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eater</a> that fought during the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a> in <a href="/wiki/1998" title="1998">1998</a> and was killed during the battle. Before that, he was seen as the Death Eaters and <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a> first attacked the castle and broke down the defences. He was also seen towards the end of the battle. He was one of the Death Eaters who had tied up <a href="/wiki/Rubeus_Hagrid" title="Rubeus Hagrid">Hagrid</a> while he held the "dead" body of <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>. He was killed later during the second part of the battle.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
