<b>Sheila Brooks</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Ravenclaw" title="Ravenclaw">Ravenclaw</a> student at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> in the <a href="/wiki/1990s" title="1990s">1990s</a>. During the <a href="/wiki/1996%E2%80%931997_school_year" title="1996–1997 school year">1996–1997 school year</a>, she agreed to give <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> a <a href="/wiki/Skiving_Snackbox" title="Skiving Snackbox">Skiving Snackbox</a> in exchange for six <a href="/wiki/Stink_Pellets" title="Stink Pellets">Stink Pellets</a>, telling Harry, "<i>I never had you, of all people, down as a skiver.</i>" Her least favourite subject was <a href="/wiki/Herbology" title="Herbology">Herbology</a> as she did not like to get her hands dirty.
