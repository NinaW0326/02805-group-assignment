<p>A <b>Healer</b> is a qualified <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch or wizard</a> who tends to the sick and injured using <a href="/wiki/Healing_magic" title="Healing magic">healing magic</a>. They are the <a href="/wiki/Wizarding_world" title="Wizarding world">wizarding world</a> equivalent of a <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggle</a> <a href="/wiki/Doctor" title="Doctor">doctor</a>. <a href="/wiki/St_Mungo%27s_Hospital_for_Magical_Maladies_and_Injuries" title="St Mungo&#39;s Hospital for Magical Maladies and Injuries">St Mungo's Hospital for Magical Maladies and Injuries</a> (which is England's wizarding community's primary hospital) is one such place where a Healer may work.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP22_1-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP22-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-OOTP23_2-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP23-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Nature">Nature</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Healer?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Nature">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Healer?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Nature"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<div class="quote">
<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix">"<i>Witches and wizards in lime-green robes were walking up and down the rows, asking questions and making notes on clipboards like Umbridgeâ€™s. Harry noticed the emblem embroidered on their chests: a wand and bone, crossed.</i>"</span></dd>
<dd>&#8212;Description of the Healers that work at St Mungo's<sup class="noprint"><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Order_of_the_Phoenix" title="Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix">[src]</a></sup></dd>
<dd></dd></dl></div><p>Healers and Mediwizards are the <a href="/wiki/Wizarding_world" title="Wizarding world">wizarding world</a> equivalent of Muggle doctors and nurses.
</p><p><a href="/wiki/St_Mungo%27s_Hospital_for_Magical_Maladies_and_Injuries" title="St Mungo&#39;s Hospital for Magical Maladies and Injuries">St Mungo's Hospital for Magical Maladies and Injuries</a> is one of the most well known places where Healers work. It was founded by Healer <a href="/wiki/Mungo_Bonham" title="Mungo Bonham">Mungo Bonham</a> in the <a href="/wiki/17th_century" title="17th century">17th century</a>. They treat magical diseases such as <a href="/wiki/Dragon_Pox" title="Dragon Pox">Dragon Pox</a>, <a href="/wiki/Spattergroit" title="Spattergroit">spattergroit</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Vanishing_sickness" title="Vanishing sickness">vanishing sickness</a> and injuries. Healers at St Mungo's wear lime green robes with the sign of a <a href="/wiki/Bone" title="Bone">bone</a> and a <a href="/wiki/Wand" title="Wand">wand</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP22_1-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP22-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Healers can work in a variety of places. At <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a>, there is a <a href="/wiki/Hospital_Wing" title="Hospital Wing">Hospital Wing</a> run by the matron.<sup id="cite_ref-PS17_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PS17-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-COS10_4-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COS10-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> Madam <a href="/wiki/Poppy_Pomfrey" title="Poppy Pomfrey">Poppy Pomfrey</a> was the matron from <a href="/wiki/1971" title="1971">1971</a> until at least <a href="/wiki/2014" title="2014">2014</a>. Healers at Hogwarts tend to the sick and wounded from the school but transfer anyone they do not have the resources to treat, to St Mungo's.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP12_5-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP12-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Healing_magic">Healing magic</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Healer?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Healing magic">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Healer?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Healing magic"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<dl><dd><div class="noprint"><i>Main article&#58; <a href="/wiki/Healing_magic" title="Healing magic">Healing magic</a></i></div>Healing magic is a branch of magic used by Healers and <a href="/wiki/Mediwizard" title="Mediwizard">Mediwizards</a> to treat the sick or wounded.<sup id="cite_ref-PM_6-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PM-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup></dd></dl>
<p>There are many different types of spells in this branch of magic, that have a variety of effects. There are also a vast group of potions that are dedicated to healing as well. Magical medication is also implemented and is normally related to healing potions, but things such as chocolate are also a type of medication. There are some described below:

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Requirements">Requirements</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Healer?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Requirements">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Healer?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Requirements"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>To become a Healer, one must have excellent academic credentials. In Britain they need at least five N.E.W.T.s, with a high mark of either '<a href="/wiki/Outstanding" title="Outstanding">Outstanding</a>' or '<a href="/wiki/Exceeds_Expectations" title="Exceeds Expectations">Exceeds Expectations</a>' at: <a href="/wiki/Potions_(class)" title="Potions (class)">Potions</a>, <a href="/wiki/Transfiguration_(class)" title="Transfiguration (class)">Transfiguration</a>, <a href="/wiki/Herbology" title="Herbology">Herbology</a>, <a href="/wiki/Charms_(class)" title="Charms (class)">Charms</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Defence_Against_the_Dark_Arts" title="Defence Against the Dark Arts">Defence Against the Dark Arts</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP29_9-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP29-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
