The <b>Head of Gryffindor House</b> is a <a href="/wiki/Professor" title="Professor">Professor</a> at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> with the specific role of looking after the students in their <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Houses" title="Hogwarts Houses">House</a>. It is unknown how a professor would be elected for the post, but it's most likely that the headmaster chooses the professor whom embodies all the qualities <a href="/wiki/Godric_Gryffindor" title="Godric Gryffindor">Godric Gryffindor</a> himself had (bravery, daring, conscientiousness, etc.) and was in Gryffindor House when he/she was at Hogwarts.<sup id="cite_ref-PS7_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PS7-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-OOTP11_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP11-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Every portrait in <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor_Tower" title="Gryffindor Tower">Gryffindor Tower</a> depicts a current or former Head of Gryffindor.<sup id="cite_ref-MHP_4-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-MHP-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<p>The head of house before and during Harry Potter's time was <a href="/wiki/Minerva_McGonagall" title="Minerva McGonagall">Minerva McGonagall</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-PS8_5-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PS8-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> Minerva became the head of house possibly in 1956, when she was appointed <a href="/wiki/Transfiguration_(class)" title="Transfiguration (class)">Transfiguration</a> professor at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a>, taking over Albus Dumbledore's previous teaching position when he became Headmaster. When she was made <a href="/wiki/Headmaster" title="Headmaster">Headmistress</a> in 1998 following the end of the <a href="/wiki/Second_Wizarding_War" title="Second Wizarding War">Second Wizarding War</a>, former student and eventual <a href="/wiki/Herbology" title="Herbology">Herbology</a> teacher <a href="/wiki/Neville_Longbottom" title="Neville Longbottom">Neville Longbottom</a> became the new Head of Gryffindor House.<sup id="cite_ref-MA_6-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-MA-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Special_duties">Special duties</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Head_of_Gryffindor_House?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Special duties">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Head_of_Gryffindor_House?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Special duties"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>The following are special duties that were performed by the Head of Gryffindor in the past.
</p>
<ul><li><a href="/wiki/1992%E2%80%931993_school_year" title="1992–1993 school year">1992–1993 school year</a>
<ul><li>To inform the <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor" title="Gryffindor">Gryffindor</a> students of new rules regarding the opening of the <a href="/wiki/Chamber_of_Secrets" title="Chamber of Secrets">Chamber of Secrets</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-COS14_7-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COS14-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup></li></ul></li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1993%E2%80%931994_school_year" title="1993–1994 school year">1993–1994 school year</a>
<ul><li>To notify their students of the rules in regards to protecting them from <a href="/wiki/Azkaban" title="Azkaban">Azkaban</a> escapee, <a href="/wiki/Sirius_Black" title="Sirius Black">Sirius Black</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-POA_8-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-POA-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li>To keep watch over the Gryffindors, along with the <a href="/wiki/Prefect" title="Prefect">Prefects</a> whilst <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Castle" title="Hogwarts Castle">Hogwarts Castle</a> was being searched for <a href="/wiki/Sirius_Black" title="Sirius Black">Sirius Black</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-POA_8-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-POA-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup></li></ul></li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1997%E2%80%931998_school_year" title="1997–1998 school year">1997–1998 school year</a>
<ul><li>To round up their students prior to the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-DH30_9-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH30-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup></li></ul></li></ul>
