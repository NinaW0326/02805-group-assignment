<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_Keeper?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_Keeper?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>He started his education in <a href="/wiki/1980" title="1980">1980</a> and was <a href="/wiki/Sorting_ceremony" title="Sorting ceremony">Sorted</a> into an unknown house. At some point by the <a href="/wiki/1985%E2%80%931986_school_year" title="1985–1986 school year">1985–1986 school year</a>, he became the <a href="/wiki/Keeper" title="Keeper">Keeper</a> of an unknown Quidditch team.<sup id="cite_ref-HM_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HM-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>He continued playing on the team the following year.<sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>He later went on to become a professionally <a href="/wiki/Quidditch" title="Quidditch">Quidditch</a> player after graduating from Hogwarts. He became the <a href="/wiki/Keeper" title="Keeper">Keeper</a> for <a href="/wiki/Wigtown_Wanderers" title="Wigtown Wanderers">Wigtown Wanderers</a>' reserve team.<sup id="cite_ref-HMQch2_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HMQch2-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
