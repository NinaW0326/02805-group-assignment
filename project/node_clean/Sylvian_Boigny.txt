<b>Sylvian Boigny</b> was an <a href="/wiki/Ivory_Coast" title="Ivory Coast">Ivorian</a> <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch</a> who played as the <a href="/wiki/Seeker" title="Seeker">Seeker</a> for the <a href="/wiki/2014" title="2014">2014</a> <a href="/wiki/Ivorian_National_Quidditch_team" title="Ivorian National Quidditch team">Ivorian National Quidditch team</a>. During the first match of the <a href="/wiki/2014_Quidditch_World_Cup" title="2014 Quidditch World Cup">2014 Quidditch World Cup</a> against <a href="/wiki/Norwegian_National_Quidditch_team" title="Norwegian National Quidditch team">Norway</a>, Boigny was beaten by her opposite <a href="/wiki/Sigrid_Kristoffersen" title="Sigrid Kristoffersen">Sigrid Kristoffersen</a> to the <a href="/wiki/Golden_Snitch" title="Golden Snitch">Golden Snitch</a> after 128 minutes of play, securing a win for Norway.<sup id="cite_ref-PM_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PM-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
