<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Daisy_Pennifold?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Daisy_Pennifold?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>In <a href="/wiki/1700s" title="1700s">1711</a>, shortly after the change in colour of the <a href="/wiki/Quaffle" title="Quaffle">Quaffle</a>, Daisy <a href="/wiki/Spell_creation" title="Spell creation">invented</a> a <a href="/wiki/Arresto_Momentum" class="mw-redirect" title="Arresto Momentum">charm to slow the target's descent</a> and used it on the Quaffle, allowing <a href="/wiki/Chaser" title="Chaser">Chasers</a> to retrieve the ball while it was still in the air rather than having to dive to the ground. These <a href="/wiki/Charm" title="Charm">enchanted</a> Quaffles became known as "Pennifold Quaffles" and are still used in modern times.<sup id="cite_ref-QTA_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-QTA-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Magical_abilities_and_skills">Magical abilities and skills</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Daisy_Pennifold?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Daisy_Pennifold?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li><b><a href="/wiki/Spell_creation" title="Spell creation">Spell creation</a></b>: Pennifold succeeded in the very advanced and difficult <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magical</a> art of inventing new <a href="/wiki/Spell" title="Spell">spells</a>, having successfully created the highly useful and practical <a href="/wiki/Slowing_Charm" title="Slowing Charm">Slowing Charm</a>.</li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Charm" title="Charm">Charms</a></b>: Pennifold was highly accomplished and talented with charm-work, given how she successfully invented the <a href="/wiki/Slowing_Charm" title="Slowing Charm">Slowing Charm</a>.</li></ul>
