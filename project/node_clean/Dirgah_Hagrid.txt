<p><b>Dirgah Hagrid</b> was supposedly <a href="/wiki/Rubeus_Hagrid" title="Rubeus Hagrid">Rubeus Hagrid</a>'s evil twin brother. In <a href="/wiki/1991" title="1991">1991</a> there was rumour among the <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts</a> student body that the <a href="/wiki/Third-floor_corridor" title="Third-floor corridor">Forbidden Corridor</a> housed Dirgah because <a href="/wiki/Albus_Dumbledore" title="Albus Dumbledore">Headmaster Dumbledore</a> did not know how to get rid of him.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> This proved false, as the contents of the Forbidden Corridor were a <a href="/wiki/Philosopher%27s_Stone_Chambers" class="mw-redirect" title="Philosopher&#39;s Stone Chambers">series of obstacles</a> to protect the <a href="/wiki/Philosopher%27s_Stone" title="Philosopher&#39;s Stone">Philosopher's Stone</a>.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Dirgah_Hagrid?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Dirgah_Hagrid?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>"Dirgah" is "Hagrid" spelt backwards. Therefore, "Dirgah Hagrid" spelt backwards is "Dirgah Hagrid" as well. Thus his full name is a palindrome.</li></ul>
