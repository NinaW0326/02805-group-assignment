<b>Devlin Whitehorn</b> (b. <a href="/wiki/1945" title="1945">1945</a>) was a <a href="/wiki/Great_Britain" title="Great Britain">British</a> <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a>, who in <a href="/wiki/1967" title="1967">1967</a> founded the <a href="/wiki/Nimbus_Racing_Broom_Company" title="Nimbus Racing Broom Company">Nimbus Racing Broom Company</a>, considered to be "<i>the top of the field.</i>" For this achievement, he was included on a bronze <a href="/wiki/Famous_Witches_and_Wizards_Cards" title="Famous Witches and Wizards Cards">Famous Wizard Card</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-PSG_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PSG-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-COSG_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COSG-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-POAG_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-POAG-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-QTAR_4-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-QTAR-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-JKRS_5-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-JKRS-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>
