<b>Charlie Baverstock</b> was a wizard from New Zealand who worked as manager of the <a href="/wiki/New_Zealand_National_Quidditch_team" title="New Zealand National Quidditch team">New Zealand National Quidditch team</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-PM_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PM-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>He proclaimed himself 'madder then a bloke who's been locked in a box of Fwoopers' after his <a href="/wiki/Chaser" title="Chaser">Chaser</a> <a href="/wiki/Dennis_Moon" title="Dennis Moon">Dennis Moon</a> was sent off in the 106th minute of New Zealand's first round match against <a href="/wiki/Bulgarian_National_Quidditch_Stadium" title="Bulgarian National Quidditch Stadium">Bulgaria</a> at the <a href="/wiki/2014_Quidditch_World_Cup" title="2014 Quidditch World Cup">2014 Quidditch World Cup</a>. New Zealand lost the match 410 - 170.<sup id="cite_ref-PM_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PM-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
