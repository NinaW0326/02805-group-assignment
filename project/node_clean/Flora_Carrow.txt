<b>Flora Carrow</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch</a> and <a href="/wiki/Slytherin" title="Slytherin">Slytherin</a> student who attended <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> in the <a href="/wiki/1990s" title="1990s">1990s</a>. She and her twin sister <a href="/wiki/Hestia_Carrow" title="Hestia Carrow">Hestia</a> were both members of the <a href="/wiki/Slug_Club" title="Slug Club">Slug Club</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-HBPF_4-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBPF-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Flora_Carrow?veaction=edit&amp;section=4" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Flora_Carrow?action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>"Flora" comes from the Latin <i>flōs</i> (meaning "flower"). Flora was also the name of a Roman fertility goddess whose domain was flowers, Spring, and youth. This goddess was not of Greek origin, unlike many Roman gods, instead coming from the lore of the Italic Sabine folk.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Flora_Carrow?veaction=edit&amp;section=5" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Flora_Carrow?action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>This character is only introduced in the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Half-Blood_Prince_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince (film)">film adaptation</a> of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Half-Blood_Prince" title="Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince">Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince</a></i> and <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Deathly_Hallows:_Part_1" title="Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 1">Part 1</a></i> and <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Deathly_Hallows:_Part_2" title="Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 2">Part 2</a></i> of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Deathly_Hallows" title="Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows">Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows</a></i>.</li>
<li>Flora Carrow is portrayed by actress <a href="/wiki/Amber_and_Ruby_Evans" title="Amber and Ruby Evans">Amber Evans</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-SnitchSeekerCarrowTwins_6-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-SnitchSeekerCarrowTwins-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li>Both she and her sister <a href="/wiki/Hestia_Carrow" title="Hestia Carrow">Hestia</a> were likely invited to join the <a href="/wiki/Slug_Club" title="Slug Club">Slug Club</a> due to their pure-blood heritage (and perhaps a devotion to Slytherin House, possibly indicated by their fondness for green). The Carrow family are one of the <a href="/wiki/Sacred_Twenty-Eight" title="Sacred Twenty-Eight">Sacred Twenty-Eight</a>, meaning they are one of the twenty-eight pure-blooded families left in Great Britain.</li>
<li>It is likely that the Carrow twins are related to <a href="/wiki/Alecto_and_Amycus_Carrow" title="Alecto and Amycus Carrow">Alecto and Amycus Carrow</a>, as all four of them have Greco-Roman forenames and the same surname.</li></ul>
