<b>Ministry Official No. 39423</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard or witch</a> who worked at the <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">Ministry of Magic</a>. A Ministry Official, this individual was in charge of validating Ministry of Magic Identity Cards, having validated <a href="/wiki/Mafalda_Hopkirk" title="Mafalda Hopkirk">Mafalda Hopkirk</a>'s.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<ul><li>This character's signature seems to say "Wakefield", but that is not entirely legible. If that is correct, this character was presumably named after <a href="/wiki/Lauren_Wakefield" title="Lauren Wakefield">Lauren Wakefield</a>, a graphic artist who worked on <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Half-Blood_Prince_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince (film)">Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince</a></i>, and both parts of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Deathly_Hallows_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows (film)">Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows</a></i>.</li></ul>
