<b>Rebecca Ramsea</b> (c. <a href="/wiki/1994" title="1994">1994</a>) was a <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggle</a> woman who was buried in the <a href="/wiki/Little_Hangleton" title="Little Hangleton">Little Hangleton</a> <a href="/wiki/Little_Hangleton_graveyard" title="Little Hangleton graveyard">graveyard</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-GOFF_1-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOFF-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
