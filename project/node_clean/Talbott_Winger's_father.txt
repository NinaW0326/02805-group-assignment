<b>Talbott Winger's father</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> who opposed <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a> during the <a href="/wiki/First_Wizarding_War" title="First Wizarding War">First Wizarding War</a>, though neither he nor <a href="/wiki/Talbott_Winger%27s_mother" title="Talbott Winger&#39;s mother">Talbott's mother</a> joined any defence organisation.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Talbott_Winger%27s_father?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Talbott_Winger%27s_father?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>He wrote for the <i><a href="/wiki/Daily_Prophet" title="Daily Prophet">Daily Prophet</a></i>, and had at one point, snuck in his son <a href="/wiki/Talbott_Winger" title="Talbott Winger">Talbott Winger</a>'s poems to be published within.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>He and Talbott's mother were killed by <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a>, though their son managed to escape by transforming into his <a href="/wiki/Animagus" title="Animagus">animagus</a> eagle form.<sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
