<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="1993">1993</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Marsh?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: 1993">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Marsh?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: 1993"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>In <a href="/wiki/1993" title="1993">1993</a>, she travelled on the <a href="/wiki/Knight_Bus" title="Knight Bus">Knight Bus</a> at the same time as <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>. She got off the bus at <a href="/wiki/Abergavenny" title="Abergavenny">Abergavenny</a> looking queasy.<sup id="cite_ref-POA3_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-POA3-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="1996">1996</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Marsh?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: 1996">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Marsh?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: 1996"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>In <a href="/wiki/1996" title="1996">1996</a>, Madam Marsh was seen travelling again on the <a href="/wiki/Knight_Bus" title="Knight Bus">Knight Bus</a>. This time she became sick and vomited. She was let off before Harry, <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione</a>, and the <a href="/wiki/Weasley_family" title="Weasley family">Weasleys</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP24_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP24-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
