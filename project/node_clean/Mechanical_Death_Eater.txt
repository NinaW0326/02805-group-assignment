<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="History">History</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Mechanical_Death_Eater?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: History">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Mechanical_Death_Eater?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: History"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<figure class="thumb tright show-info-icon" style="width: 182px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/5/55/Mechanical_Death_Eater_2.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20171202140435" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/5/55/Mechanical_Death_Eater_2.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/180?cb=20171202140435" decoding="async" width="180" height="182" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="Mechanical Death Eater 2.jpg" data-image-key="Mechanical_Death_Eater_2.jpg" /></a> 	 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:Mechanical_Death_Eater_2.jpg" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption">A Mechanical Death Eater for defensive spell practice</p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure>
<p>By the mid-<a href="/wiki/1980s" title="1980s">1980s</a>, several of these dummies had been placed at the <a href="/wiki/Training_Grounds" title="Training Grounds">Training Grounds</a> of <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a>, on which several students, including as <a href="/wiki/William_Weasley" title="William Weasley">Bill Weasley</a>, practised their defensive spellwork. These dummies had a <a href="/wiki/Wand" title="Wand">wand</a> so it was able to act as a real <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eater</a> would, and they could also be <a href="/wiki/Disarming_Charm" title="Disarming Charm">disarmed</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-HM_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HM-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>By the mid-<a href="/wiki/1990s" title="1990s">1990s</a>, one such contraption had been placed inside the <a href="/wiki/Room_of_Requirement" title="Room of Requirement">Room of Requirement</a>, and was provided the group calling themselves for the <a href="/wiki/Dumbledore%27s_Army" title="Dumbledore&#39;s Army">Dumbledore's Army</a> by the room's enchantments among an assortment of stored books and objects that would, during the <a href="/wiki/1995%E2%80%931996_school_year" title="1995–1996 school year">1995–1996 school year</a>, provide them with tools necessary to train themselves in Defence Against the Dark Arts in preparation for the forthcoming <a href="/wiki/Second_Wizarding_War" title="Second Wizarding War">Second Wizarding War</a> in a time when the Ministry of Magic were still denying the return of Lord Voldemort. The dummy in question was subsequently used by the aforementioned student organisation to practise their spells inside . The dummy was used as a target while the group trained with such spells as <i><a href="/wiki/Diminuendo" title="Diminuendo">Diminuendo</a></i>, <i><a href="/wiki/Disarming_Charm" title="Disarming Charm">Expelliarmus</a></i>, <i><a href="/wiki/Stunning_Spell" title="Stunning Spell">Stupefy</a></i>, and <i><a href="/wiki/Reductor_Curse" title="Reductor Curse">Reducto</a></i>.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTPF_2-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTPF-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>During the <a href="/wiki/Calamity" title="Calamity">Calamity</a> which affected the <a href="/wiki/Wizarding_world" title="Wizarding world">wizarding world</a> in the <a href="/wiki/2010s" title="2010s">2010s</a>, <a href="/wiki/Troll" title="Troll">Troll</a> <a href="/wiki/Confoundable" title="Confoundable">Confoundables</a> guarded these dummies as <a href="/wiki/Foundable" title="Foundable">Foundables</a>, for which members of the <a href="/wiki/Statute_of_Secrecy_Task_Force" title="Statute of Secrecy Task Force">Statute of Secrecy Task Force</a> could overpower with the <a href="/wiki/Exploding_Charm" title="Exploding Charm">Exploding Charm</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-WU_4-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-WU-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
