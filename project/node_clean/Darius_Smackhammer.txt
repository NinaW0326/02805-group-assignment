<p><b>Darius Smackhammer</b> was an <a href="/wiki/United_States_of_America" title="United States of America">American</a> <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> who played as the <a href="/wiki/Seeker" title="Seeker">Seeker</a> for the <a href="/wiki/2014" title="2014">2014</a> <a href="/wiki/American_National_Quidditch_team" title="American National Quidditch team">American National Quidditch team</a>. During the fourth match of the <a href="/wiki/2014_Quidditch_World_Cup" title="2014 Quidditch World Cup">427th Quidditch World Cup</a>, US versus <a href="/wiki/Jamaican_National_Quidditch_team" title="Jamaican National Quidditch team">Jamaica</a>, Smackhammer beat Jamaican Seeker <a href="/wiki/Shanice_Higgins" title="Shanice Higgins">Shanice Higgins</a> to the <a href="/wiki/Golden_Snitch" title="Golden Snitch">Snitch</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-usa-v-jam_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-usa-v-jam-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> resulting in a narrow victory for the United States.<sup id="cite_ref-usa-v-jam2_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-usa-v-jam2-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Darius_Smackhammer?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Darius_Smackhammer?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>During the USA-<a href="/wiki/Brazil" title="Brazil">Brazil</a> match running on <a href="/wiki/5_July" title="5 July">5 July</a> (continued from <a href="/wiki/4_July" title="4 July">the previous day</a>), Smackhammer was assaulted by dual <a href="/wiki/Bludger" title="Bludger">Bludgers</a> sent by <a href="/wiki/Carlos_Clodoaldo" title="Carlos Clodoaldo">Clodoaldo</a> and <a href="/wiki/Rafael_Santos" title="Rafael Santos">Santos</a>, preventing him from immediately reaching the <a href="/wiki/Golden_Snitch" title="Golden Snitch">Snitch</a>. He and <a href="/wiki/Tony_Silva" title="Tony Silva">Tony Silva</a> subsequently dove simultaneously for the Snitch, both sliding to the handles of their brooms diving at breakneck speed towards the earth and thus to the Snitch. At first it was almost impossible to determine who had really caught the Snitch, but after less than a minute it was apparent that Silva had narrowly beaten Smackhammer to it, resulting in USA being dropped out of the finals.
</p>
