This <b>individual</b> (fl. <a href="/wiki/1998" title="1998">1998</a>) was a <a href="/wiki/Dark_Wizard" title="Dark Wizard">Dark Wizard</a> and <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eater</a> who fought during the <a href="/wiki/Second_Wizarding_War" title="Second Wizarding War">Second Wizarding War</a>.

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_Black_Death_Eater_at_the_Battle_of_Hogwarts?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_Black_Death_Eater_at_the_Battle_of_Hogwarts?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>At some point in his life, he became learned in the <a href="/wiki/Dark_Arts" title="Dark Arts">Dark Arts</a> and joined the <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a>, as a loyal follower and servant of <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a>. On the <a href="/wiki/2_May" title="2 May">2 May</a> <a href="/wiki/1998" title="1998">1998</a>, he was called upon by his master to fight in <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a>, which he did alongside his fellow Death Eaters. He fought in the <a href="/wiki/Onslaught_at_the_Viaduct" title="Onslaught at the Viaduct">onslaught</a> at the <a href="/wiki/Viaduct" title="Viaduct">Viaduct</a> against the <a href="/wiki/Suit_of_armour" title="Suit of armour">suits of armour</a>. He later witnessed the believed death of <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Forbidden_Forest" title="Forbidden Forest">Forbidden Forest</a> at the hands of <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a>. Eventually, after Harry Potter revealed he was alive, he would have either abandoned the battle and his master, or continued to fight. He was either killed in battle, defeated, arrested and sent to <a href="/wiki/Azkaban" title="Azkaban">Azkaban</a> for his crimes as a Death Eater, or became an outlaw.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
