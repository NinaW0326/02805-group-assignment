<b>M. Carneirus</b> was a reporter for the <i><a href="/wiki/Daily_Prophet" title="Daily Prophet">Daily Prophet</a></i> who wrote a story about the <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eater</a> attack at the <a href="/wiki/1994_Quidditch_World_Cup" title="1994 Quidditch World Cup">Quidditch World Cup</a> in <a href="/wiki/1994" title="1994">1994</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
