<p>A <b>Head of Department</b> was, in the <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">British Ministry of Magic</a> hierarchical structure, the most senior official within each of the Ministry's seven departments.
</p><p>The Heads of Department answered to the <a href="/wiki/Minister_for_Magic" title="Minister for Magic">Minister for Magic</a>, and were all in some degree answerable to the Head of the <a href="/wiki/Department_of_Magical_Law_Enforcement" title="Department of Magical Law Enforcement">Department of Magical Law Enforcement</a>, the largest department in the Ministry.<sup id="cite_ref-FBR_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-FBR-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> As such, all these top Ministry officials had regular contact.<sup id="cite_ref-DH1_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH1-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>The heads of the several offices and divisions within each department answered to their respective Head of Department.
</p>
