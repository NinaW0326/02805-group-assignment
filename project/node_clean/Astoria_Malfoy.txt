<b>Astoria Malfoy</b><sup id="cite_ref-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup> (née <b>Greengrass</b>) (c. <a href="/wiki/1982" title="1982">1982</a> – <a href="/wiki/2019" title="2019">2019</a>) was a <a href="/wiki/Great_Britain" title="Great Britain">British</a> <a href="/wiki/Pure-blood" title="Pure-blood">pure-blood</a> <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch</a>. She also was a member of the <a href="/wiki/Greengrass_family" title="Greengrass family">Greengrass family</a>, one of the <a href="/wiki/Sacred_Twenty-Eight" title="Sacred Twenty-Eight">Sacred Twenty-Eight</a>. Astoria began attending <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> in <a href="/wiki/1993" title="1993">1993</a>, two years below her husband, <a href="/wiki/Draco_Malfoy" title="Draco Malfoy">Draco Malfoy</a>, and her elder sister, <a href="/wiki/Daphne_Greengrass" title="Daphne Greengrass">Daphne Greengrass</a>. After her education at Hogwarts, she met and married Draco and the two had a son, <a href="/wiki/Scorpius_Malfoy" title="Scorpius Malfoy">Scorpius Hyperion Malfoy</a>. Astoria died during the summer of <a href="/wiki/2019" title="2019">2019</a>, due to her <a href="/wiki/Blood_malediction" title="Blood malediction">blood curse</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-died_3-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-died-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Astoria_Malfoy?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Astoria_Malfoy?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span id="Early_life_(1982-1993)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_life_.281982-1993.29">Early life <span class="template-c">(1982-1993)</span></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Astoria_Malfoy?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Early life (1982-1993)">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Astoria_Malfoy?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Early life (1982-1993)"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Born into the <a href="/wiki/Greengrass_family" title="Greengrass family">Greengrass family</a>, among <a href="/wiki/Sacred_Twenty-Eight" title="Sacred Twenty-Eight">the oldest, truly pure-blooded families in Britain</a>, Astoria and her older sister, <a href="/wiki/Daphne_Greengrass" title="Daphne Greengrass">Daphne</a>, were brought up in the ideals of <a href="/wiki/Pure-blood_supremacy" title="Pure-blood supremacy">Pure-blood supremacy</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-PMdraco_5-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PMdraco-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> Astoria's life was cut short by a curse placed upon <a href="/wiki/Astoria_Malfoy%27s_ancestor" title="Astoria Malfoy&#39;s ancestor">her ancestor</a> long before her time, resulting in her body becoming extremely frail.<sup id="cite_ref-died_3-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-died-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span id="Hogwarts_years_(1993-2000)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="Hogwarts_years_.281993-2000.29">Hogwarts years <span class="template-c">(1993-2000)</span></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Astoria_Malfoy?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Hogwarts years (1993-2000)">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Astoria_Malfoy?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Hogwarts years (1993-2000)"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Astoria attended <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a>. During the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a>, she was evacuated for being too young. It is also unknown what <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Houses" title="Hogwarts Houses">house</a> she was <a href="/wiki/Sorting_ceremony" title="Sorting ceremony">sorted</a> into at Hogwarts.
</p><p>The events of the <a href="/wiki/Second_Wizarding_War" title="Second Wizarding War">Second Wizarding War</a> during her teenage years made Astoria espouse a more tolerant life view, turning back on her family's blood purity ideals, although her experience through the war was less than that of what Draco Malfoy suffered.<sup id="cite_ref-PMdraco_5-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PMdraco-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span id="Adulthood_(2000-2019)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="Adulthood_.282000-2019.29">Adulthood <span class="template-c">(2000-2019)</span></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Astoria_Malfoy?veaction=edit&amp;section=4" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Adulthood (2000-2019)">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Astoria_Malfoy?action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Adulthood (2000-2019)"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Marriage">Marriage</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Astoria_Malfoy?veaction=edit&amp;section=5" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Marriage">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Astoria_Malfoy?action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Marriage"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<p>At some point between 2000 and 2004/5, she eventually married <a href="/wiki/Draco_Malfoy" title="Draco Malfoy">Draco Malfoy</a>, who was two years ahead of her and was a classmate of her older sister, <a href="/wiki/Daphne_Greengrass" title="Daphne Greengrass">Daphne Greengrass</a>. <a href="/wiki/Lucius_Malfoy" title="Lucius Malfoy">Lucius</a> and <a href="/wiki/Narcissa_Malfoy" title="Narcissa Malfoy">Narcissa Malfoy</a> had "disapproved" of Draco's choice to take her as a wife, but he chose her anyway and stood up to both of them. This was something which Astoria once told her son "<i>was one of the most courageous things she'd ever seen</i>".
</p>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Raising_Scorpius_Malfoy">Raising Scorpius Malfoy</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Astoria_Malfoy?veaction=edit&amp;section=6" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Raising Scorpius Malfoy">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Astoria_Malfoy?action=edit&amp;section=6" title="Edit section: Raising Scorpius Malfoy"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<p>Frail, and knowing she was not destined for old age due to her ancestor's blood borne curse, Astoria longed for a child so that Draco, after her inevitable death, would not be alone.
</p>
<figure class="thumb tleft show-info-icon" style="width: 237px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/c/c1/Malfoys_on_Platform_Nine_and_Three-Quarters.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20200621194235" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/c/c1/Malfoys_on_Platform_Nine_and_Three-Quarters.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/235?cb=20200621194235" decoding="async" width="235" height="119" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="Malfoys on Platform Nine and Three-Quarters.jpg" data-image-key="Malfoys_on_Platform_Nine_and_Three-Quarters.jpg" /></a> 	 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:Malfoys_on_Platform_Nine_and_Three-Quarters.jpg" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption">Astoria with her family on the platform</p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure>
<p>She was successful in producing a child, despite Draco telling her that he didn't care if the Malfoy family ended with him, but the pregnancy greatly weakened her and the family vanished into seclusion when <a href="/wiki/Scorpius_Malfoy" title="Scorpius Malfoy">Scorpius Hyperion Malfoy</a><sup id="cite_ref-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup> was born. This, despite the intentions of Draco and Astoria, led to false rumours that Astoria had been unable to conceive and had used a <a href="/wiki/Time-Turner" title="Time-Turner">Time-Turner</a> to go back in time and conceive a child with Lord Voldemort - which, if she actually <i>was</i> unable to conceive would have been a failure anyway. The stigma of these rumours were borne by her son who, at Hogwarts, was accused of being Voldemort's child.
</p><p>After Scorpius's birth, Astoria refused to teach her son the pure-blooded belief that <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggles</a> were scum. <a href="/wiki/Lucius_Malfoy" title="Lucius Malfoy">Lucius</a> and <a href="/wiki/Narcissa_Malfoy" title="Narcissa Malfoy">Narcissa Malfoy</a> therefore found her a somewhat disappointing daughter-in-law; family gatherings were, as a result, often fraught with tension.<sup id="cite_ref-PMdraco_5-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PMdraco-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>In 2017, Astoria saw her son off at <a href="/wiki/King%27s_Cross_Station" title="King&#39;s Cross Station">King's Cross Station</a> for his <a href="/wiki/First_year" title="First year">first year</a> at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> in <a href="/wiki/2017" title="2017">2017</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup> loading him up with sweets, having told him on at least one occasion, "Sweets, they always help you make friends." As Albus and Scorpius bonded over sweets, her advice was apparently correct.
</p>
<h3><span id="Death_(2019)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="Death_.282019.29">Death <span class="template-c">(2019)</span></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Astoria_Malfoy?veaction=edit&amp;section=7" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Death (2019)">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Astoria_Malfoy?action=edit&amp;section=7" title="Edit section: Death (2019)"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>In the summer before Scorpius's <a href="/wiki/Third_year" title="Third year">third year</a> at Hogwarts, Astoria died due to the toll of her family curse, leaving her son and husband heartbroken. Scorpius and Albus would later on attend her funeral.
</p><p>Draco, having inherited a "true" <a href="/wiki/Time-Turner" title="Time-Turner">Time-Turner</a> from his father, was later urged to use it to go back in time to see Astoria, but he resisted, knowing the potential damage he could cause to reality.<sup id="cite_ref-died_3-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-died-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Astoria_Malfoy?veaction=edit&amp;section=8" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Astoria_Malfoy?action=edit&amp;section=8" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The name <i>Astoria</i> may be derived from the London Astoria, a music venue in London, England, or the Waldorf-Astoria Hotel, a famously luxurious hotel in New York City. This hotel was named after the Astors, a very wealthy and influential American family.<sup id="cite_ref-10" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-10">&#91;10&#93;</a></sup> The name may also be related to <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Astraea_(mythology)" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Astraea (mythology)">Astraea</a>, a daughter of Zeus in Greek mythology who personified justice. <i>Astraea</i> is derived from Greek meaning "star-maiden"; this may relate to her husband's <a href="/wiki/House_of_Black" title="House of Black">maternal family</a> often naming its members after stars and constellations. The name may also be spelled <i>Asteria</i>, as shown on the <a href="/wiki/Weasley_family#Family_Tree" title="Weasley family">Weasley Family Tree</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-11" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-11">&#91;11&#93;</a></sup> Like Hyperion — her son <a href="/wiki/Scorpius_Malfoy" title="Scorpius Malfoy">Scorpius</a>' middle name —<a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Asteria" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Asteria"> Asteria</a> is the name of one of the Titans of Greek mythology.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Astoria_Malfoy?veaction=edit&amp;section=9" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Astoria_Malfoy?action=edit&amp;section=9" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<figure class="thumb tright show-info-icon" style="width: 182px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/0/0e/Astoria_Malfoy.png/revision/latest?cb=20190505165024" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/0/0e/Astoria_Malfoy.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/180?cb=20190505165024" decoding="async" width="180" height="266" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="Astoria Malfoy.png" data-image-key="Astoria_Malfoy.png" /></a> 	 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:Astoria_Malfoy.png" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption">Astoria Malfoy in <i><a href="/wiki/LEGO_Harry_Potter:_Years_5-7" title="LEGO Harry Potter: Years 5-7">LEGO Harry Potter: Years 5-7</a></i></p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure>
<ul><li>Astoria is played by <a href="/wiki/Jade_Olivia" title="Jade Olivia">Jade Olivia</a>, in the epilogue scene of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Deathly_Hallows:_Part_2" title="Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 2">Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 2</a></i>.<sup id="cite_ref-12" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-12">&#91;12&#93;</a></sup> She was the real-life girlfriend of Draco's actor <a href="/wiki/Tom_Felton" title="Tom Felton">Tom Felton</a> at the time.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/J._K._Rowling" title="J. K. Rowling">J. K. Rowling</a> has said that she chose to have Draco marry someone other than <a href="/wiki/Pansy_Parkinson" title="Pansy Parkinson">Pansy Parkinson</a>, who seems to have been his girlfriend during his adolescence, because she always hated Pansy, who represented "the girls who teased me when I was at school". She has referred to Pansy as "the anti-<a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione</a>".<sup id="cite_ref-13" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-13">&#91;13&#93;</a></sup> Indeed, keeping in line with this, J.K. Rowling draws a much more sympathetic portrait of Astoria Greengrass on <i><a href="/wiki/Pottermore" title="Pottermore">Pottermore</a></i>.<sup id="cite_ref-PMdraco_5-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PMdraco-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li>Astoria is the only character in the epilogue who <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>, <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ron Weasley</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione Granger</a> do not know, though they are aware of Scorpius's identity and probably also Astoria's, even if they do only refer to her as "Draco's wife." In a school context, a two-year gap is considerable, and since even her sister <a href="/wiki/Daphne_Greengrass" title="Daphne Greengrass">Daphne</a> was barely peripheral to their school days, they may not have been aware of her until she married Draco.</li>
<li>Daphne was <a href="/wiki/Sorting_ceremony" title="Sorting ceremony">Sorted</a> into <a href="/wiki/Slytherin" title="Slytherin">Slytherin</a>, but it is unknown if Astoria was Sorted into Slytherin as well.</li>
<li>Astoria's maiden name, Greengrass, could refer to the primary colour of Slytherin House (green) and the common phrase, "a snake in the grass," as snakes are Slytherin's emblem. This is ironic, as the phrase refers to someone who is treacherous and venal, traits that Astoria does not seemingly possess.</li></ul>
