<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows">"<i>Alecto, Amycus's sister, teaches Muggle Studies, which is compulsory for everyone. We've all got to listen to her explain how Muggles are like animals, stupid and dirty, and how they drive wizards into hiding by being vicious toward them, and how the natural order is being re-established.</i>"</span></dd>
<dd>&#8212;Alecto's prejudiced and hateful teachings<sup class="noprint"><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Deathly_Hallows" title="Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows">[src]</a></sup></dd>
<dd></dd></dl></div>
<p><b>Professor Alecto Carrow</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Dark_Wizard" title="Dark Wizard">Dark witch</a>, <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eater</a> and the sister of <a href="/wiki/Amycus_Carrow" title="Amycus Carrow">Amycus Carrow</a>. Alecto fought in the <a href="/wiki/First_Wizarding_War" title="First Wizarding War">First Wizarding War</a> along with her brother. They did not search for <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a> after his first downfall, but were forgiven after Voldemort's <a href="/wiki/Return_of_Lord_Voldemort" title="Return of Lord Voldemort">return</a> in <a href="/wiki/1995" title="1995">1995,</a> and joined the Death Eater ranks once more in the <a href="/wiki/Second_Wizarding_War" title="Second Wizarding War">Second Wizarding War</a>. 
</p><p>After the <a href="/wiki/Fall_of_the_Ministry_of_Magic" title="Fall of the Ministry of Magic">fall of the Ministry of Magic</a> in <a href="/wiki/1997" title="1997">1997</a>, Alecto was made Professor of <a href="/wiki/Muggle_Studies" title="Muggle Studies">Muggle Studies</a>; she taught the thinking that <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggles</a> are lesser than <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">Wizardkind</a>, and likely taught the <a href="/wiki/Pure-blood" title="Pure-blood">pureblood</a>-philosophy as well. When <a href="/wiki/Severus_Snape" title="Severus Snape">Severus Snape</a> became <a href="/wiki/Headmaster" title="Headmaster">Headmaster</a> of Hogwarts, she was appointed as <a href="/wiki/Headmaster" title="Headmaster">Deputy Headmistress</a>, alongside her brother (who was appointed as <a href="/wiki/Headmaster" title="Headmaster">Deputy Headmaster</a>). She loved to punish and torture the students who opposed them. 
</p><p>On the eve of <a href="/wiki/1_May" title="1 May">1 May</a>, <a href="/wiki/1998" title="1998">1998</a>, shortly before the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a>, she was <a href="/wiki/Stunning_Spell" title="Stunning Spell">stunned</a> by <a href="/wiki/Luna_Lovegood" title="Luna Lovegood">Luna Lovegood</a> when she summoned <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a>. She was then magically tied up in <a href="/wiki/Ravenclaw_Tower" title="Ravenclaw Tower">Ravenclaw Tower</a> by <a href="/wiki/Minerva_McGonagall" title="Minerva McGonagall">Professor McGonagall</a>. After the battle, she was likely sent to <a href="/wiki/Azkaban" title="Azkaban">Azkaban</a> along with her brother for their crimes. 
</p>

<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_life">Early life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Alecto_Carrow?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Early life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Alecto_Carrow?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Early life"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Alecto Carrow was born sometime before <a href="/wiki/1981" title="1981">1981</a><sup id="cite_ref-born_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-born-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> into the <a href="/wiki/Pure-blood" title="Pure-blood">pure-blood</a> <a href="/wiki/Carrow_family" title="Carrow family">Carrow family</a> somewhere in the <a href="/wiki/Great_Britain" title="Great Britain">British Isles</a>. She likely acquired her <a href="/wiki/Alecto_Carrow%27s_wand" title="Alecto Carrow&#39;s wand">wand</a> at either <a href="/wiki/Ollivander%27s_Wand_Shop" class="mw-redirect" title="Ollivander&#39;s Wand Shop">Ollivander's Wand Shop</a> in <a href="/wiki/Diagon_Alley" title="Diagon Alley">Diagon Alley</a> or <a href="/wiki/Wands_by_Gregorovitch" title="Wands by Gregorovitch">Wands by Gregorovitch</a> at the <a href="/wiki/Carkitt_Market" title="Carkitt Market">Carkitt Market</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-DH30_7-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH30-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Alecto attended <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> in her youth along with her brother, and was a member of <a href="/wiki/Slytherin" title="Slytherin">Slytherin</a> house.<sup id="cite_ref-LEGOY2_10-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-LEGOY2-10">&#91;10&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<p>Alecto and her brother, <a href="/wiki/Amycus_Carrow" title="Amycus Carrow">Amycus</a>, supported <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a> and became <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a> during the <a href="/wiki/First_Wizarding_War" title="First Wizarding War">First Wizarding War</a>. After <a href="/wiki/Attack_at_Godric%27s_Hollow_(1981)" title="Attack at Godric&#39;s Hollow (1981)">Voldemort's downfall</a> in <a href="/wiki/1981" title="1981">1981</a>, they did not search for their master, believing that Voldemort was gone forever, and thus avoided imprisonment in <a href="/wiki/Azkaban" title="Azkaban">Azkaban</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP2_11-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP2-11">&#91;11&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-carrowsjkr_12-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-carrowsjkr-12">&#91;12&#93;</a></sup>

<p>Alecto and her brother were, most likely, among the <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a> that were summoned by <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a> to the <a href="/wiki/Little_Hangleton_graveyard" title="Little Hangleton graveyard">churchyard</a> at <a href="/wiki/Little_Hangleton" title="Little Hangleton">Little Hangleton</a> on <a href="/wiki/24_June" title="24 June">24 June</a>, <a href="/wiki/1995" title="1995">1995</a>, as their absence wasn't noted by Voldemort himself.<sup id="cite_ref-GF33_13-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GF33-13">&#91;13&#93;</a></sup>

<p>On the evening of <a href="/wiki/30_June" title="30 June">30 June</a>, <a href="/wiki/1997" title="1997">1997</a>, Alecto and her brother participated in the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_the_Astronomy_Tower" title="Battle of the Astronomy Tower">assault</a> on <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Castle" title="Hogwarts Castle">Hogwarts Castle</a>, orchestrated by <a href="/wiki/Draco_Malfoy" title="Draco Malfoy">Draco Malfoy</a>, that resulted in the death of <a href="/wiki/Albus_Dumbledore" title="Albus Dumbledore">Albus Dumbledore</a>. When the <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a> arrived at the top of the <a href="/wiki/Astronomy_Tower" title="Astronomy Tower">Astronomy Tower</a>, where Dumbledore was trapped, Alecto gave "wheezing giggles", as her brother made fun of the <a href="/wiki/Headmaster" title="Headmaster">Headmaster</a>. When <a href="/wiki/Severus_Snape" title="Severus Snape">Severus Snape</a> arrived on the scene, he cast <a href="/wiki/Killing_Curse" title="Killing Curse">the curse</a> that ended the <a href="/wiki/Headmaster" title="Headmaster">Headmaster</a>'s life.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP27_5-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP27-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>On Snape's orders, she and her brother swiftly left the Tower, panting excitedly. In the corridors below, Alecto and <a href="/wiki/Amycus_Carrow" title="Amycus Carrow">Amycus</a> retreated after Snape with their arms over their heads, under attack from blasts by <a href="/wiki/Minerva_McGonagall" title="Minerva McGonagall">Minerva McGonagall</a>. As the brother and sister descended the <a href="/wiki/Marble_Staircase" title="Marble Staircase">Marble Staircase</a>, <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry</a> took a shortcut and arrived on the grounds before them.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP28_14-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP28-14">&#91;14&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>As the two <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a> closed in after him, one of them cast a spell that sent Harry to the ground. Harry hit one of them, who stumbled and fell, tripping up the other, with another <a href="/wiki/Impediment_Jinx" title="Impediment Jinx">Impediment Jinx</a>. The two siblings then ran to the gates, and <a href="/wiki/Apparition" title="Apparition">Disapparated</a> beyond the school boundaries.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP28_14-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP28-14">&#91;14&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<p>That summer the Carrows attended a Death Eater <a href="/wiki/Meeting_at_Malfoy_Manor" title="Meeting at Malfoy Manor">meeting at Malfoy Manor</a>. This meeting concerned <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_the_Seven_Potters" title="Battle of the Seven Potters">plans to capture or kill Harry Potter</a> and take over the <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">Ministry of Magic</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-DHpt1_15-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DHpt1-15">&#91;15&#93;</a></sup> During this meeting it was revealed that the Death Eaters had successfully infiltrated the Ministry and that they were aware of the date in which Harry would be removed from Privet Drive. This meeting took place during the height of the Second Wizarding War.

<p>On <a href="/wiki/1_August" title="1 August">1 August</a>, <a href="/wiki/1997" title="1997">1997</a>, the <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a> managed to stage a <a href="/wiki/Fall_of_the_Ministry_of_Magic" title="Fall of the Ministry of Magic">coup</a> within the <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">Ministry of Magic</a>, thus gaining influence over all of <a href="/wiki/Wizarding_world" title="Wizarding world">wizarding Britain</a>. One month later, as of <a href="/wiki/1_September" title="1 September">1 September</a>, Alecto was appointed as <a href="/wiki/Muggle_Studies" title="Muggle Studies">Muggle Studies</a> teacher as well as <a href="/wiki/Minerva_McGonagall" title="Minerva McGonagall">Deputy Headmistress</a> under the new <a href="/wiki/Headmaster" title="Headmaster">Headmaster</a> <a href="/wiki/Severus_Snape" title="Severus Snape">Severus Snape</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-DH12_4-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH12-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-DH28_9-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH28-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-DH11_16-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH11-16">&#91;16&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p><a href="/wiki/Muggle_Studies" title="Muggle Studies">Muggle Studies</a> was made a compulsory core class. The curriculum was changed to include the <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">Ministry of Magic</a>'s anti-<a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggle</a> propaganda exclusively (instead of teaching aspects of <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggle</a> culture and science).<sup id="cite_ref-PA12_17-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PA12-17">&#91;17&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>As one of the <a href="/wiki/Headmaster" title="Headmaster">Deputy Heads</a>, Alecto was in charge of all disciplinary action (along with her brother). All Hogwarts staff were obliged to report any rule-breaking student to her or her brother (although the staff did not turn students in if they could avoid it). Alecto's disciplinary measures included torture by way of the <a href="/wiki/Cruciatus_Curse" title="Cruciatus Curse">Cruciatus Curse</a>, as well as binding students with chains, and corporal punishment in the form of beatings.<sup id="cite_ref-DH29_18-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH29-18">&#91;18&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p><a href="/wiki/Dumbledore%27s_Army" title="Dumbledore&#39;s Army">Dumbledore's Army</a>, led by <a href="/wiki/Neville_Longbottom" title="Neville Longbottom">Neville Longbottom</a>, spearheaded the resistance against the Carrows. Alecto made a slash on his face after he asked her how much <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggle</a> blood she and her brother had. The group, which was banned after the reenactment of <a href="/wiki/Educational_Decree_Number_Twenty-Four" title="Educational Decree Number Twenty-Four">Educational Decree Number Twenty-Four</a>, would sneak into the corridors at night and write on the walls with messages like "<i>Dumbledore's Army, Still Recruiting</i>". <a href="/wiki/Michael_Corner" title="Michael Corner">Michael Corner</a> was caught releasing a <a href="/wiki/First_year" title="First year">first year</a> that Alecto and/or Amycus had chained up, and he was thus brutally tortured.<sup id="cite_ref-DH29_18-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH29-18">&#91;18&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>When Alecto and Amycus understood they had no hold over Neville, they planned to kill him or send him to <a href="/wiki/Azkaban" title="Azkaban">Azkaban</a>. As they chased him, Neville vanished into the <a href="/wiki/Room_of_Requirement" title="Room of Requirement">Room of Requirement</a>, and the rest of <a href="/wiki/Dumbledore%27s_Army" title="Dumbledore&#39;s Army">Dumbledore's Army</a> soon followed in his footsteps, moving into the room as both Alecto and Amycus tried to hunt them all down.<sup id="cite_ref-DH29_18-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH29-18">&#91;18&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<p>By the evening of <a href="/wiki/1_May" title="1 May">1 May</a>, <a href="/wiki/1998" title="1998">1998</a>, news of Harry Potter, <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ronald Weasley</a> and <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione Granger</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Break-in_of_Gringotts_Wizarding_Bank_(1998)" title="Break-in of Gringotts Wizarding Bank (1998)">break-in</a> of <a href="/wiki/Gringotts_Wizarding_Bank" title="Gringotts Wizarding Bank">Gringotts Wizarding Bank</a> reached the student body; <a href="/wiki/Terry_Boot" title="Terry Boot">Terry Boot</a> was beaten up by Alecto and Amycus for yelling about it in the <a href="/wiki/Great_Hall" title="Great Hall">Great Hall</a> over dinner.<sup id="cite_ref-DH29_18-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH29-18">&#91;18&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>That same day, as <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a> became aware that Harry Potter, Ronald Weasley and Hermione Granger were <a href="/wiki/Albus_Dumbledore_and_Harry_Potter%27s_Horcrux_Hunt" title="Albus Dumbledore and Harry Potter&#39;s Horcrux Hunt">hunting down and destroying</a> his <a href="/wiki/Horcrux" title="Horcrux">Horcruxes</a>, he told Alecto and Amycus that Harry might try to get into <a href="/wiki/Ravenclaw_Tower" title="Ravenclaw Tower">Ravenclaw Tower</a>. At her urgent request <a href="/wiki/Filius_Flitwick" title="Filius Flitwick">Professor Flitwick</a>, <a href="/wiki/Head_of_Ravenclaw_House" title="Head of Ravenclaw House">Head of Ravenclaw House</a>, let Alecto into the common room, where she stood guard.<sup id="cite_ref-DH30_7-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH30-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Alecto was keeping watch when Harry arrived with <a href="/wiki/Luna_Lovegood" title="Luna Lovegood">Luna Lovegood</a>. She immediately touched her <a href="/wiki/Dark_Mark" title="Dark Mark">Dark Mark</a>, letting <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Voldemort</a> and all the <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a> know that they had got him. Alecto was immediately <a href="/wiki/Stunning_Spell" title="Stunning Spell">stunned</a> by Luna, and Amycus demanded that Minerva McGonagall let him into the Tower. Once inside, he saw his unconscious sister but no sign of Harry, as both Harry and Luna were hidden beneath Harry's <a href="/wiki/Cloak_of_Invisibility" title="Cloak of Invisibility">Invisibility Cloak</a>. Aware that they were going to feel Voldemort's wrath if they did not hand him Harry, Amycus planned to pass it off on the students, and when Professor McGonagall objected calling Amycus and Alecto cowards in the process, he spat in her face. This caused Harry to use the <a href="/wiki/Cruciatus_Curse" title="Cruciatus Curse">Cruciatus Curse</a> on him, which knocked him out. <a href="/wiki/Minerva_McGonagall" title="Minerva McGonagall">Minerva McGonagall</a> used a spell to conjure a net, and the two siblings were tied up and hoisted into the air, where they were left dangling beneath the ceiling.<sup id="cite_ref-DH30_7-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH30-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Post-war">Post-war</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Alecto_Carrow?veaction=edit&amp;section=9" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Post-war">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Alecto_Carrow?action=edit&amp;section=9" title="Edit section: Post-war"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Neither Alecto nor her brother fought in the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a> as both of them were kept tied-up at <a href="/wiki/Ravenclaw_Tower" title="Ravenclaw Tower">Ravenclaw Tower</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-DH30_7-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH30-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup> It is likely that after the battle and the end of the <a href="/wiki/Second_Wizarding_War" title="Second Wizarding War">Second Wizarding War</a> they both were imprisoned in <a href="/wiki/Azkaban" title="Azkaban">Azkaban</a> for their crimes under <a href="/wiki/Kingsley_Shacklebolt" title="Kingsley Shacklebolt">Kingsley Shacklebolt</a>'s de-corrupted <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">Ministry of Magic</a>.

<p>Alecto Carrow was a stocky little woman like the <a href="/wiki/Fred_and_George_Weasley" title="Fred and George Weasley">Weasley twins</a>, with a wheezy giggle, much like her brother.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP27_5-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP27-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> Sloping-shouldered, she had stubby fingers and, as a <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eater</a>, she was branded with the <a href="/wiki/Dark_Mark" title="Dark Mark">Dark Mark</a> on her forearm.<sup id="cite_ref-DH30_7-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH30-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Personality_and_traits">Personality and traits</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Alecto_Carrow?veaction=edit&amp;section=11" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Personality and traits">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Alecto_Carrow?action=edit&amp;section=11" title="Edit section: Personality and traits"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<div class="quote">
<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows">"<i>They're in charge of all discipline. They like punishment, the Carrows.</i>"</span></dd>
<dd>&#8212;Neville Longbottom, on the Carrows' love for punishing students<sup class="noprint"><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Deathly_Hallows" title="Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows">[src]</a></sup></dd>
<dd></dd></dl></div>
<p>Alecto was an extreme sadist who enjoyed torturing students or just people in general. Indeed, <a href="/wiki/Neville_Longbottom" title="Neville Longbottom">Neville</a> even claimed that Alecto "made <a href="/wiki/Umbridge_family" title="Umbridge family">Umbridge</a> look tame".<sup id="cite_ref-DH29_18-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH29-18">&#91;18&#93;</a></sup> Alecto had strong <a href="/wiki/Pure-blood_supremacy" title="Pure-blood supremacy">pure-blood supremacist</a> beliefs (specifically the belief in <a href="/wiki/Blood_status" title="Blood status">blood purity</a>), as she taught that <a href="/wiki/Blood_traitor" title="Blood traitor">blood traitors</a>, <a href="/wiki/Mudblood" title="Mudblood">mudbloods</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggles</a> were lesser than <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizardkind</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-DH29_18-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH29-18">&#91;18&#93;</a></sup> She was not highly intelligent, however, as she needed <a href="/wiki/Filius_Flitwick" title="Filius Flitwick">Filius Flitwick</a> to let her into <a href="/wiki/Ravenclaw_Tower" title="Ravenclaw Tower">Ravenclaw Tower</a>. However, she was seemingly slightly brighter than her brother Amycus.<sup id="cite_ref-DH30_7-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH30-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup> Like Bellatrix Lestrange, she admired Voldemort and detested "<a href="/wiki/Blood_traitor" title="Blood traitor">blood traitors</a>", <a href="/wiki/Muggle-borns" class="mw-redirect" title="Muggle-borns">Muggle-borns</a> and <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggles</a> alike; though unlike Bellatrix, Alecto was not loyal to Voldemort enough to seek him out after his first downfall, nor brave Azkaban in his name.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Magical_abilities_and_skills">Magical abilities and skills</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Alecto_Carrow?veaction=edit&amp;section=12" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Alecto_Carrow?action=edit&amp;section=12" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Alecto Carrow was a <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eater</a>, which meant she was adept in the Dark Arts. She along with her brother Amycus, gained an infamous reputation within the wizarding world, particularly during their stint as joint <a href="/wiki/Deputy_Headmaster/Headmistress" class="mw-redirect" title="Deputy Headmaster/Headmistress">Deputy Heads</a> at Hogwarts, as they severely punished students for any rule-breaking within the school.<sup id="cite_ref-DH29_18-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH29-18">&#91;18&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<ul><li><b><a href="/wiki/Dark_Arts" title="Dark Arts">Dark Arts</a></b>: Being a Death Eater, Alecto was a natural when it came to the Dark Arts. She showed particular skill in utilising the <a href="/wiki/Cruciatus_Curse" title="Cruciatus Curse">Cruciatus Curse</a>, as she used it as a common form of punishment during her time as a teacher at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry.<sup id="cite_ref-DH29_18-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH29-18">&#91;18&#93;</a></sup> It can also be assumed that Alecto was able to summon the <a href="/wiki/Dark_Mark" title="Dark Mark">Dark Mark</a>, having it branded on her forearm like most Death Eaters. She used this same mark to summon Lord Voldemort when she found Harry Potter in <a href="/wiki/Ravenclaw_Tower" title="Ravenclaw Tower">Ravenclaw Tower</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-DH30_7-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH30-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Duelling" title="Duelling">Duelling</a></b>: Alecto must have been a somewhat skilled duellist as she was an active participant in both the first and second wizarding wars and survived them both. She participated in the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_the_Astronomy_Tower" title="Battle of the Astronomy Tower">Battle of the Astronomy Tower</a>;<sup id="cite_ref-HBP27_5-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP27-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> however, she was no match for the extremely powerful Minerva McGonagall, as she was forced to retreat with her arms over her head as McGonagall sent spells and insults after her in equal measure.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP28_14-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP28-14">&#91;14&#93;</a></sup> She also wasn't quick enough to block Luna Lovegood's <a href="/wiki/Stunning_Spell" title="Stunning Spell">Stunning Spell</a> upon discovering her and Harry Potter in the Ravenclaw Common Room (although it should be noted she was attempting to summon Voldemort at the time and her focus seemed to be solely on Harry Potter).<sup id="cite_ref-DH30_7-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH30-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Apparition" title="Apparition">Apparition</a></b>: Like most adult wizards and witches, Alecto was capable of apparating at will. Alecto disapparated away from beyond Hogwarts grounds following the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_the_Astronomy_Tower" title="Battle of the Astronomy Tower">Battle of the Astronomy Tower</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP28_14-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP28-14">&#91;14&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li><b>Teaching Skills</b>: Alecto was a Professor at Hogwarts, where she taught <a href="/wiki/Muggle_Studies" title="Muggle Studies">Muggle Studies</a>. Her teachings conformed to the Ministry propaganda that Muggles are filthy, stupid and like animals that have violently forced wizards and witches into hiding and that the natural order of things was being restored with wizards having supreme dominance over all muggles. Most of the students likely ignored her anti-Muggle teachings and some such as Neville Longbottom verbally opposed them; however, these students would find themselves being severely punished if they did.<sup id="cite_ref-DH29_18-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH29-18">&#91;18&#93;</a></sup></li></ul>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Alecto_Carrow?veaction=edit&amp;section=13" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Alecto_Carrow?action=edit&amp;section=13" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Alecto">Alecto</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Alecto_Carrow?veaction=edit&amp;section=14" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Alecto">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Alecto_Carrow?action=edit&amp;section=14" title="Edit section: Alecto"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<ul><li>The name "Alecto" is Greek, and means "unceasing in anger". "Alecto" is known to have been derived from the earlier male name; "Alektos".</li>
<li>In Greek mythology, <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Alecto" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Alecto">Alecto</a> was one of the <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Erinyes" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Erinyes">Erinyes</a>, also known as the Furies; they are the Greek personifications of vengeance and pangs of conscience, and are known for being cruel and bloodthirsty towards wrongdoers.</li></ul>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Carrow">Carrow</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Alecto_Carrow?veaction=edit&amp;section=15" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Carrow">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Alecto_Carrow?action=edit&amp;section=15" title="Edit section: Carrow"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<ul><li>In English, the word, "Carrow," can refer to two places. "Carrow" in Norfolk, or "Carraw" in Northumberland. The first is assumed to have its name derived from the Old English word "carr", meaning "rock", and a word of Celtic origin; "hoh", meaning "spur of a hill". While the second appears to have been named either after the plural of the Old English word "carr", or after a mixture of a form of "carr", and another Old English word; "raw", meaning "row".</li>
<li>The surname may also have derived from the Welsh surname <i>Caeriw</i> (meaning "dweller at the foot of a hill).</li></ul>

<ul><li>Alecto is portrayed by <a href="/wiki/Suzie_Toase" title="Suzie Toase">Suzie Toase</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Half-Blood_Prince_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince (film)">film adaptation</a> of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Half-Blood_Prince" title="Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince">Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince</a></i> and the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Deathly_Hallows:_Part_1" title="Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 1">first</a> and <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Deathly_Hallows:_Part_2" title="Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 2">second</a> part of the film adaptations of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Deathly_Hallows" title="Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows">Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows</a></i>.<sup id="cite_ref-19" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-19">&#91;19&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li>Actress <a href="/wiki/Vanessa_Stevenson" title="Vanessa Stevenson">Vanessa Stevenson</a> doubled for Suzanne Toase in the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_(film_series)" title="Harry Potter (film series)">film adaptations</a> of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Deathly_Hallows" title="Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows">Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows</a></i>.</li>
<li>In the films Alecto is diminished to a background character with no speaking lines. In the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Half-Blood_Prince_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince (film)">film adaptation</a> of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Half-Blood_Prince" title="Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince">Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince</a></i> she takes no active part in the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_the_Astronomy_Tower" title="Battle of the Astronomy Tower">Battle of the Astronomy Tower</a>, witnessing Dumbledore's death without a word and then fleeing with her fellow <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a>. In <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Deathly_Hallows:_Part_1" title="Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 1">Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 1</a></i>, she only appears during the <a href="/wiki/Meeting_at_Malfoy_Manor" title="Meeting at Malfoy Manor">Meeting at Malfoy Manor</a>. In <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Deathly_Hallows:_Part_2" title="Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 2">Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 2</a></i>, Alecto and Amycus are stunned by Headmaster <a href="/wiki/Severus_Snape" title="Severus Snape">Severus Snape</a>'s deflects of two of <a href="/wiki/Minerva_McGonagall" title="Minerva McGonagall">Minerva McGonagall</a>'s curses directed at himself, which either knock them unconscious or kill them (it is not made clear and the pair are not seen again, though it is unlikely that McGonagall would cast a curse that would kill). It is highly likely that Snape intended for this to happen whilst making it look like an accident.</li>
<li>In <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Deathly_Hallows:_Part_2_(video_game)" title="Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 2 (video game)">Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 2 (video game)</a></i>, she appears as one of the foes that <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> must fight. <a href="/wiki/Suzie_Toase" title="Suzie Toase">Suzie Toase</a> also did the voice of the character for this game.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/David_Yates" title="David Yates">David Yates</a> mistakenly called Alecto and Amycus "the Carrow twins" even though they were not twins.<sup id="cite_ref-20" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-20">&#91;20&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li>Ironically, when comparing Alecto and her brother to <a href="/wiki/Dolores_Umbridge" title="Dolores Umbridge">Dolores Umbridge</a>, the most hated character in the franchise, <a href="/wiki/Neville_Longbottom" title="Neville Longbottom">Neville Longbottom</a> claims that she makes her look tame.</li></ul>
