<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Cecil_Lee?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Cecil_Lee?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_life_and_schooling">Early life and schooling</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Cecil_Lee?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Early life and schooling">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Cecil_Lee?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Early life and schooling"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Cecil Lee was born into the wizarding <a href="/wiki/Lee_family" title="Lee family">Lee family</a>, and had at least one sibling.
</p><p>During his formative years, he attended <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a>, where he did not seem to be a particularly gifted student: he remembered little of <a href="/wiki/Charms_(class)" title="Charms (class)">Charms class</a> apart from what his seat was. According to himself, however, he "[did] well" on his <a href="/wiki/Nastily_Exhausting_Wizarding_Test" title="Nastily Exhausting Wizarding Test">N.E.W.T.s</a>.
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Career_in_the_Werewolf_Capture_Unit">Career in the Werewolf Capture Unit</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Cecil_Lee?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Career in the Werewolf Capture Unit">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Cecil_Lee?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Career in the Werewolf Capture Unit"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Sometime after school, he joined the <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">Ministry of Magic</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Werewolf_Capture_Unit" title="Werewolf Capture Unit">Werewolf Capture Unit</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-31oct_2-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-31oct-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> Throughout his career, he hunted werewolves with extreme prejudice, and reportedly fought and captured several werewolves: he claimed to have had several run-ins with <a href="/wiki/Fenrir_Greyback" title="Fenrir Greyback">Fenrir Greyback</a>, a who gained notoriety as the most savage werewolf of them all — though, by his own admission, he never managed to catch him.
</p><p>Lee was an admirer of <a href="/wiki/Gilderoy_Lockhart" title="Gilderoy Lockhart">Gilderoy Lockhart</a>, who he was convinced was the most gifted wizard in the world. He believed Lockhart's (later proven false) account of his victory over the <a href="/wiki/Wagga_Wagga_Werewolf" title="Wagga Wagga Werewolf">Wagga Wagga Werewolf</a> using the complex <a href="/wiki/Homorphus_Charm" title="Homorphus Charm">Homorphus Charm</a>; Lee would himself supposedly use this charm to "stop werewolves in their tracks" during the course of his duties.
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Greyback_attack_on_Hogwarts">Greyback attack on Hogwarts</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Cecil_Lee?veaction=edit&amp;section=4" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Greyback attack on Hogwarts">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Cecil_Lee?action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Greyback attack on Hogwarts"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>After a pack of <a href="/wiki/Werewolf" title="Werewolf">werewolves</a> led by <a href="/wiki/Fenrir_Greyback" title="Fenrir Greyback">Fenrir Greyback</a> attacked Hogwarts on <a href="/wiki/Hallowe%27en" title="Hallowe&#39;en">Hallowe'en</a> during the <a href="/wiki/1980s" title="1980s">1980s</a>, then <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Headmaster" title="Hogwarts Headmaster">Headmaster</a> <a href="/wiki/Albus_Dumbledore" title="Albus Dumbledore">Albus Dumbledore</a> sent an owl to the Ministry of Magic soliciting their assistance, who in response, sent Cecil to investigate. With the help of <a href="/wiki/Jacob%27s_sibling" title="Jacob&#39;s sibling">Jacob's sibling</a>, he went to look for clues about the attack. They started by interviewing <a href="/wiki/Chiara_Lobosca" title="Chiara Lobosca">Chiara Lobosca</a> and staff members <a href="/wiki/Rolanda_Hooch" title="Rolanda Hooch">Rolanda Hooch</a>, <a href="/wiki/Argus_Filch" title="Argus Filch">Argus Filch</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Filius_Flitwick" title="Filius Flitwick">Filius Flitwick</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-31oct_2-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-31oct-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>The following full moon, when Greyback arrived for a second attack, Cecil Lee and <a href="/wiki/Jacob%27s_sibling" title="Jacob&#39;s sibling">Jacob's sibling</a> successfully captured Greyback using an <a href="/wiki/Incarcerous_Spell" title="Incarcerous Spell">Incarcerous Spell</a>, only for him to escape again not long after. Nevertheless, Lee thanked the student for their help, and presented them with honorary membership of the <a href="/wiki/Werewolf_Capture_Unit" title="Werewolf Capture Unit">Werewolf Capture Unit</a>, complete with their own uniform.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Physical_appearance">Physical appearance</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Cecil_Lee?veaction=edit&amp;section=5" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Physical appearance">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Cecil_Lee?action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Physical appearance"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Cecil Lee had chocolate brown hair and emerald green eyes. He was tall and lean, and had a thick brown moustache. Cecil reportedly had a strong masculine scent.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Personality_and_traits">Personality and traits</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Cecil_Lee?veaction=edit&amp;section=6" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Personality and traits">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Cecil_Lee?action=edit&amp;section=6" title="Edit section: Personality and traits"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>A rather bumptious and opinionated man, Cecil Lee was an immensely inflexible and intolerant individual who apparently subscribed strongly to the <a href="/wiki/Werewolf#Prejudice_and_discrimination" title="Werewolf">common view on werewolves</a> in the magical community, as he frequently voiced the opinion that all werewolves should be eradicated before they did "the same to us". Even when faced with a werewolf-offspring in the form of a <a href="/wiki/Borf" title="Borf">pup</a> from the <a href="/wiki/Forbidden_Forest_wolf_pack" title="Forbidden Forest wolf pack">Forbidden Forest wolf pack</a>, he refused to believe that it had werewolf blood in its veins, insisting that if it did, it would have gone for their throats. It would appear that his stance softened somewhat, however, when he saw a werewolf come to the defence of <a href="/wiki/Jacob%27s_sibling" title="Jacob&#39;s sibling">a student</a> that was about to be attacked by <a href="/wiki/Fenrir_Greyback" title="Fenrir Greyback">Fenrir Greyback</a>, after which he brought the unconscious body of <a href="/wiki/Chiara_Lobosca" title="Chiara Lobosca">Chiara Lobosca</a> to the office of <a href="/wiki/Albus_Dumbledore" title="Albus Dumbledore">Albus Dumbledore</a> rather than taking her back to the Ministry.
</p><p>While unafraid to admit that he feared Fenrir Greyback for his savage villainy, he was nevertheless a man who was decisive in both thought and deed, and ultimately unflappable when it mattered and confronted him and his pack, and even placed himself in front of <a href="/wiki/Jacob%27s_sibling" title="Jacob&#39;s sibling">Jacob's sibling</a> to protect them from the attackers. He did not think very highly about his own intelligence, however, commenting that he did not know very much, but was still considered an expert on werewolves, although this might have been a joke.
</p><p>Another trait he shared with his nephew, <a href="/wiki/Barnaby_Lee" title="Barnaby Lee">Barnaby Lee</a>, is a large appetite, having apparently raided the <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_kitchens" title="Hogwarts kitchens">Hogwarts kitchens</a> when he was supposed to be hunting werewolves.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Magical_abilities_and_skills">Magical abilities and skills</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Cecil_Lee?veaction=edit&amp;section=7" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Cecil_Lee?action=edit&amp;section=7" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Cecil Lee appeared to be a very capable <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a>, having reportedly done very well in his <a href="/wiki/Nastily_Exhausting_Wizarding_Test" title="Nastily Exhausting Wizarding Test">N.E.W.T.s</a> and was, according to himself, "highly trained".
</p>
<ul><li><b><a href="/wiki/Charm" title="Charm">Charms</a></b>: He claimed to be able to perform the "immensely complex" <a href="/wiki/Homorphus_Charm" title="Homorphus Charm">Homorphus Charm</a> to return werewolves to their human form more than once, although he failed to demonstrate this and this charm may only have been another fabrication of <a href="/wiki/Gilderoy_Lockhart" title="Gilderoy Lockhart">Gilderoy Lockhart</a>.</li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Transfiguration" title="Transfiguration">Transfiguration</a></b>: He was able to effectively use the <a href="/wiki/Incarcerous_Spell" title="Incarcerous Spell">Incarcerous Spell</a> to restrain <a href="/wiki/Fenrir_Greyback" title="Fenrir Greyback">Fenrir Greyback</a> with conjured ropes. Given how <a href="/wiki/Conjuration" title="Conjuration">Conjuration</a> is a highly difficult branch of transfiguration, this demonstrates that he was skilled in this field.</li></ul>
