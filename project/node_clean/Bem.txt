<p><b>Bem</b><sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> (born c. <a href="/wiki/1979" title="1979">1979</a>-<a href="/wiki/1980" title="1980">1980</a><sup id="cite_ref-age_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-age-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>) was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> of <a href="/wiki/Nigeria" title="Nigeria">Nigerian</a> descent<sup id="cite_ref-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> who attended <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> from <a href="/wiki/1991" title="1991">1991</a> to <a href="/wiki/1998" title="1998">1998</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-poafilm_6-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-poafilm-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup> He was Sorted into <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor" title="Gryffindor">Gryffindor</a> and was in <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>'s year, however he did not share a dormitory with Harry and his friends.
</p>

<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Hogwarts_education">Hogwarts education</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Bem?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Hogwarts education">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Bem?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Hogwarts education"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_years">Early years</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Bem?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Early years">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Bem?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Early years"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<p>Being <a href="/wiki/Nigeria" title="Nigeria">Nigerian</a> in descent, Bem began his education at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> in <a href="/wiki/1991" title="1991">1991</a>, along with students <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>, <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione Granger</a> and <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ron Weasley</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-age_1-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-age-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> He was <a href="/wiki/Sorting_ceremony" title="Sorting ceremony">Sorted</a> into the house of <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor" title="Gryffindor">Gryffindor</a>, but would not share a dormitory with the other known Gryffindor boys in his year.<sup id="cite_ref-poafilm_6-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-poafilm-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

</p><h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Physical_appearance">Physical appearance</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Bem?veaction=edit&amp;section=5" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Physical appearance">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Bem?action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Physical appearance"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>He was a black boy with dark eyes and a round face. He was also slightly overweight with a lisp.<sup id="cite_ref-poafilm_6-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-poafilm-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Bem?veaction=edit&amp;section=6" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Bem?action=edit&amp;section=6" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The name Bem means "peace" in the language of the <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Tiv" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Tiv">Tiv</a>, of <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Nigeria" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Nigeria">Nigeria</a> and <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Cameroon" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Cameroon">Cameroon</a> and also means "good" or "well" in Portuguese.<sup id="cite_ref-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup> "BEM" is also an abbreviation for <i>British Empire Medal</i>, a medal received from British royalty for meritorious civil or military service, which reflects Bem being <a href="/wiki/Sorting_ceremony" title="Sorting ceremony">Sorted</a> into Gryffindor.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Bem?veaction=edit&amp;section=7" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Bem?action=edit&amp;section=7" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>Like the character <a href="/wiki/Nigel_Wolpert" title="Nigel Wolpert">Nigel Wolpert</a>, Bem is not mentioned in the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_(book_series)" title="Harry Potter (book series)">books</a>, but is credited in the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_(film_series)" title="Harry Potter (film series)">films</a>. Played by <a href="/wiki/Ekow_Quartey" title="Ekow Quartey">Ekow Quartey</a>, the character is listed in the credits for <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Prisoner_of_Azkaban_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban (film)">Prisoner of Azkaban</a></i> as simply "Boy 1". However, on the <i>Prisoner of Azkaban</i> DVD, the <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Closed_captioning" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Closed captioning">closed captions</a> identify the character as "Bem" during the Divination class scene.</li>
<li>Bem cannot be considered <a href="/wiki/Canon" title="Canon">canon</a>, as the books identify in several places that there are only five <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor" title="Gryffindor">Gryffindor</a> boys in Harry's year: <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>, <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ronald Weasley</a>, <a href="/wiki/Neville_Longbottom" title="Neville Longbottom">Neville Longbottom</a>, <a href="/wiki/Seamus_Finnigan" title="Seamus Finnigan">Seamus Finnigan</a> and <a href="/wiki/Dean_Thomas" title="Dean Thomas">Dean Thomas</a>.</li>
<li>Bem also makes an appearance as a member of <a href="/wiki/Dumbledore%27s_Army" title="Dumbledore&#39;s Army">Dumbledore's Army</a> in <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Order_of_the_Phoenix_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix (film)">Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix</a></i>. His house was changed to <a href="/wiki/Ravenclaw" title="Ravenclaw">Ravenclaw</a>, probably to correct their mistake of putting him in Gryffindor last time. He appears in the credits as Ravenclaw boy.</li></ul>
