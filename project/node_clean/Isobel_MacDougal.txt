<b>Isobel MacDougal</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Pure-blood" title="Pure-blood">pure-blood</a> <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch</a> who began attending <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> in <a href="/wiki/1991" title="1991">1991</a>. She was sorted into the <a href="/wiki/Ravenclaw" title="Ravenclaw">Ravenclaw</a> house.

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Isobel_MacDougal?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Isobel_MacDougal?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>The existence of this character cannot be considered <a href="/wiki/Canon" title="Canon">canon</a>. In <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Philosopher%27s_Stone" title="Harry Potter and the Philosopher&#39;s Stone">Harry Potter and the Philosopher's Stone</a></i>, the <a href="/wiki/Sorting_ceremony" title="Sorting ceremony">Sorting ceremony</a> proceeds alphabetically, and <a href="/wiki/Neville_Longbottom" title="Neville Longbottom">Neville Longbottom</a> is immediately followed <a href="/wiki/Morag_MacDougal" title="Morag MacDougal">Morag MacDougal</a>.</li>
<li>As revealed by <a href="/wiki/J._K._Rowling" title="J. K. Rowling">J. K. Rowling</a> on  <i><a href="/wiki/Pottermore" title="Pottermore">Pottermore</a></i>, this character was originally named "Katrina", but this was crossed out on her hand-written list of the forty students in <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry's</a> year and replaced with "Isobel".<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li>As an Isobel McDougal was never featured in a published book in the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_(book_series)" title="Harry Potter (book series)">series</a>, but a <a href="/wiki/Morag_MacDougal" title="Morag MacDougal">Morag MacDougal</a> was, it is possible that the two are siblings (given the shared last name), though it is debated if the two are independent characters or if Isobel was simply renamed Morag.</li></ul>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Isobel_MacDougal?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Isobel_MacDougal?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>"Dougall" is from the Gaelic Dubhgall, or "dark stranger," and "Mac" is "son of."
</p>
