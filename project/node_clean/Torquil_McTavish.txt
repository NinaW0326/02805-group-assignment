<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Torquil_McTavish?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Torquil_McTavish?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>In <a href="/wiki/1754" class="mw-redirect" title="1754">1754</a>, McTavish agreed to participate in a <a href="/wiki/Broomstick" title="Broomstick">broom</a> <a href="/wiki/Broom_racing" title="Broom racing">race</a> from <a href="/wiki/Aberdeen" title="Aberdeen">Aberdeen</a> to <a href="/wiki/Rome" title="Rome">Rome</a> with the <a href="/wiki/Italy" title="Italy">Italian</a> <a href="/wiki/Silvio_Astolfi" title="Silvio Astolfi">Silvio Astolfi</a>, his bitter rival.<sup id="cite_ref-WBoS_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-WBoS-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>The race was held at night in order to avoid attracting <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggle</a> attention. Arriving in Rome at dawn, McTavish and Astolfi were seen racing toward the finish line at the <a href="/wiki/Coliseum" title="Coliseum">Coliseum</a>, but at that point, a violent confrontation between supporters of the two racers who were gathered there broke out. During the course of the fight, Astolfi was <a href="/wiki/Pullus_Jinx" title="Pullus Jinx">transformed</a> into a <a href="/wiki/Chicken_(animal)" title="Chicken (animal)">chicken</a>, and McTavish was hit with a <a href="/wiki/Knee-reversal_hex" title="Knee-reversal hex">knee-reversal hex</a>. The conflict culminated in an explosion that left the Colosseum in ruins.<sup id="cite_ref-WBoS_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-WBoS-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Thanks to <a href="/wiki/Orabella_Nuttley" title="Orabella Nuttley">Orabella Nuttley</a>, who stepped forward and fixed a few columns with her <a href="/wiki/Mending_Charm" title="Mending Charm">Mending Charm</a>, the damage to the Colosseum was undone by the time that the Muggles woken by the explosion finally showed up at the scene. All they found was a few strangely-dressed men attempting to calm the agitated Astolfi, who was still a chicken. What happened to McTavish is unknown, but it can be presumed that his knees were returned to facing the proper way.<sup id="cite_ref-WBoS_1-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-WBoS-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Magical_abilities_and_skills">Magical abilities and skills</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Torquil_McTavish?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Torquil_McTavish?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li><b><a href="/wiki/Flight" title="Flight">Flying</a>: </b>Torcquil was an incredibly talented and accomplished broomstick flyer, to the extent of being world renowned for his skill. Torcquil was so skilled he was able to participate in a long-distance broom race from Aberdeen to Rome.</li></ul>
