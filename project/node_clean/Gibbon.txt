<p><b>Gibbon</b> (d. <a href="/wiki/30_June" title="30 June">30 June</a>, <a href="/wiki/1997" title="1997">1997</a>) was a <a href="/wiki/Dark_wizard" class="mw-redirect" title="Dark wizard">Dark wizard</a> and one of <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a>'s loyal <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a>. He fought in the <a href="/wiki/Second_Wizarding_War" title="Second Wizarding War">Second Wizarding War</a>, including the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_the_Astronomy_Tower" title="Battle of the Astronomy Tower">Battle of the Astronomy Tower</a> in 1997. During the Battle of the Astronomy Tower, Gibbon <a href="/wiki/Morsmordre" title="Morsmordre">set</a> the <a href="/wiki/Dark_Mark" title="Dark Mark">Dark Mark</a> above the <a href="/wiki/Astronomy_Tower" title="Astronomy Tower">Astronomy Tower</a>. He was accidentally killed by fellow Death Eater <a href="/wiki/Thorfinn_Rowle" title="Thorfinn Rowle">Thorfinn Rowle</a>, when he was hit by a stray <a href="/wiki/Killing_Curse" title="Killing Curse">Killing Curse</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP29_1-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP29-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Gibbon?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Gibbon?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<div class="quote">
<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince">"<i>We were in trouble, we were losing. Gibbon was down, but the rest of the Death Eaters seemed ready to fight to the death. Neville had been hurt, Bill had been savaged by Greyback… It was all dark… curses flying everywhere…</i>"</span></dd>
<dd>&#8212;<a href="/wiki/Nymphadora_Tonks" title="Nymphadora Tonks">Nymphadora Tonks</a> conveys details of the battle to <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a><sup class="noprint"><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Half-Blood_Prince" title="Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince">[src]</a></sup></dd>
<dd></dd></dl></div>
<p>Gibbon was an active <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eater</a> during the <a href="/wiki/Second_Wizarding_War" title="Second Wizarding War">Second Wizarding War</a>. It is unknown if he also took part in the <a href="/wiki/First_Wizarding_War" title="First Wizarding War">First Wizarding War</a> or whether or not he was sent to <a href="/wiki/Azkaban" title="Azkaban">Azkaban</a> following <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a>'s original downfall.
</p>
<figure class="thumb tright show-info-icon" style="width: 125px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/0/00/Gibbon_2.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20110211142359" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/0/00/Gibbon_2.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/123?cb=20110211142359" decoding="async" width="123" height="221" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="Gibbon 2.jpg" data-image-key="Gibbon_2.jpg" /></a> 	 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:Gibbon_2.jpg" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption">Gibbon during the Battle of the Astronomy Tower</p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure>
<p>On <a href="/wiki/30_June" title="30 June">30 June</a> <a href="/wiki/1997" title="1997">1997</a>, Gibbon fought in the Battle of the Astronomy Tower, along with fellow Death Eaters <a href="/wiki/Alecto_Carrow" title="Alecto Carrow">Alecto</a> and <a href="/wiki/Amycus_Carrow" title="Amycus Carrow">Amycus Carrow</a>, <a href="/wiki/Corban_Yaxley" title="Corban Yaxley">Corban Yaxley</a>, <a href="/wiki/Thorfinn_Rowle" title="Thorfinn Rowle">Thorfinn Rowle</a>, <a href="/wiki/Draco_Malfoy" title="Draco Malfoy">Draco Malfoy</a>, <a href="/wiki/Severus_Snape" title="Severus Snape">Severus Snape</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Fenrir_Greyback" title="Fenrir Greyback">Fenrir Greyback</a>. Gibbon was the Death Eater who set the Dark Mark above the Astronomy Tower. It is supposed that he did not like the idea of staying at the tower and waiting for <a href="/wiki/Albus_Dumbledore" title="Albus Dumbledore">Albus Dumbledore</a> alone, so he ran downstairs. He died shortly afterwards, when a stray <a href="/wiki/Killing_Curse" title="Killing Curse">Killing Curse</a> cast by <a href="/wiki/Thorfinn_Rowle" title="Thorfinn Rowle">Thorfinn Rowle</a> that missed <a href="/wiki/Remus_Lupin" title="Remus Lupin">Remus Lupin</a> struck Gibbon instead.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP29_1-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP29-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> It is unknown what happened to his body after the battle.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Personality_and_traits">Personality and traits</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Gibbon?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Personality and traits">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Gibbon?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Personality and traits"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Almost nothing is known about Gibbon's exact personality, except the fact that he feared Albus Dumbledore and that he did not want to stay at the Astronomy Tower and wait for him alone.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP29_1-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP29-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Magical_abilities_and_skills">Magical abilities and skills</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Gibbon?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Gibbon?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li><b><a href="/wiki/Dark_Arts" title="Dark Arts">Dark Arts</a></b>: Being a Death Eater, Gibbon would have been proficient in dark magic, including the casting of <a href="/wiki/Dark_charm" title="Dark charm">Dark charms</a> and likely the <a href="/wiki/Unforgivable_Curses" title="Unforgivable Curses">Unforgivable Curses</a>. Gibbon was capable of <a href="/wiki/Morsmordre" title="Morsmordre">creating</a> the <a href="/wiki/Dark_Mark" title="Dark Mark">Dark Mark</a> above Hogwarts, during a battle, something that only a skilled and experienced dark wizard could create, according to <a href="/wiki/Arthur_Weasley" title="Arthur Weasley">Arthur Weasley</a>.</li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Duelling" title="Duelling">Duelling</a></b>: As a Death Eater, he was a competent and experienced duellist, as he fought against the Order of the Phoenix and escaping in order to go to the <a href="/wiki/Astronomy_Tower" title="Astronomy Tower">Astronomy Tower</a>, meaning he had some skill in <a href="/wiki/Martial_magic" title="Martial magic">martial magic</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP29_1-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP29-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup></li></ul>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Gibbon?veaction=edit&amp;section=4" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Gibbon?action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>A "gibbon" is a type of primate. Gibbon is also a 15th century diminuative form of Gilbert. <a target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener" class="external autonumber" href="http://www.surnamedb.com/Surname/Gibbons">[1]</a> <a target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener" class="external autonumber" href="http://www.babynamespedia.com/meaning/Gibbon">[2]</a> <a target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener" class="external autonumber" href="http://gibbinshatleykelpie.blogspot.ca/2009/03/gibbons-surname.html">[3]</a> <a target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener" class="external autonumber" href="http://www.etymonline.com/index.php?term=gibbon">[4]</a>. See <a href="/wiki/Gilbert_Wimple" title="Gilbert Wimple">Gilbert Wimple</a> and <a href="/wiki/Eleanor_Sarah_Gibbs" title="Eleanor Sarah Gibbs">Eleanor Sarah Gibbs</a> for further details.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Gibbon?veaction=edit&amp;section=5" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Gibbon?action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>During the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_the_Astronomy_Tower" title="Battle of the Astronomy Tower">Battle of the Astronomy Tower</a> he was recognised by <a href="/wiki/Remus_Lupin" title="Remus Lupin">Remus Lupin</a>, suggesting that the two have encountered each other in the past, or at least that he was a known Death Eater to the Order prior to the battle.</li>
<li>In the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Half-Blood_Prince_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince (film)">film adaptation</a> of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Half-Blood_Prince" title="Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince">Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince</a></i>, the Battle of the Astronomy Tower did not take place, meaning Gibbon was not killed by a stray Killing Curse.</li></ul>
