<b>Dan</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Great_Britain" title="Great Britain">British</a> <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> who had a <a href="/wiki/Portrait" title="Portrait">portrait</a> of himself hung somewhere in <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Castle" title="Hogwarts Castle">Hogwarts Castle</a> by the <a href="/wiki/1991-1992_school_year" class="mw-redirect" title="1991-1992 school year">1991-1992 school year</a>. Dan attended <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> in his youth, and was <a href="/wiki/Sorting_ceremony" title="Sorting ceremony">sorted</a> into <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor" title="Gryffindor">Gryffindor</a> <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Houses" title="Hogwarts Houses">House</a>, and wore the Gryffindor <a href="/wiki/Lion" title="Lion">Lion</a> emblem on his long brown cloak which we wore as an adult, as shown in his portrait.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
