<p>A <b>Metamorphmagus</b> (pl. <b>Metamorphmagi</b>) is a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch or wizard</a> with the rare ability to change their physical appearance through sheer will alone, without the need of <a href="/wiki/Polyjuice_Potion" title="Polyjuice Potion">Polyjuice Potion</a> or a <a href="/wiki/Spell" title="Spell">spell</a> like most of the <a href="/wiki/Wizarding_world" title="Wizarding world">wizarding population</a>.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Difference_from_Animagi">Difference from Animagi</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Metamorphmagus?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Difference from Animagi">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Metamorphmagus?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Difference from Animagi"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<div class="quote">
<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows">"<i>Metamorphmagi are </i>born<i>, not made.</i>"</span></dd>
<dd>&#8212;Tonks explaining to Harry about Metamorphmagi<sup class="noprint"><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Deathly_Hallows" title="Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows">[src]</a></sup></dd>
<dd></dd></dl></div><p>Unlike <a href="/wiki/Animagus" title="Animagus">Animagi</a>, Metamorphmagi can take a wide variety of forms, changing sex and age, potentially looking like anyone at all, or even just changing a part of their appearance, such as the colour of their hair or shape of their noses.<sup id="cite_ref-OTP_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OTP-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> Animagi also transform at will, but only into one animal form which is determined by their inner traits. It is never revealed if Metamorphmagi can take the full form of an animal, and Tonks simply describes the ability as being able to change her "appearance".
</p><p>Also, a Metamorphmagus is born, not made; it is not a skill that can be learned.<sup id="cite_ref-OTP_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OTP-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> There also seems to be a degree of heritability, as both <a href="/wiki/Nymphadora_Tonks" title="Nymphadora Tonks">Nymphadora Tonks</a> and her son, <a href="/wiki/Edward_Lupin" title="Edward Lupin">Teddy Lupin</a>, were Metamorphmagi, although both of Tonks' parents, <a href="/wiki/Andromeda_Tonks" title="Andromeda Tonks">Andromeda</a> and <a href="/wiki/Edward_Tonks" title="Edward Tonks">Ted</a>, were not. The ability presents very early in life, as Teddy Lupin's hair changed colour when he was a newborn infant, as did his mother's.<sup id="cite_ref-DH_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Metamorphic_usage">Metamorphic usage</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Metamorphmagus?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Metamorphic usage">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Metamorphmagus?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Metamorphic usage"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<div class="quote">
<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix">"<i>I got top marks in Concealment and Disguise during <a href="/wiki/Auror" title="Auror">Auror</a> training without any study at all, it was great.</i>"</span></dd>
<dd>&#8212;Tonks regarding her Metamorphmagus abilities<sup class="noprint"><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Order_of_the_Phoenix" title="Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix">[src]</a></sup></dd>
<dd></dd></dl></div><figure class="thumb tleft show-info-icon" style="width: 238px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/2/23/Metamorphmagi.gif/revision/latest?cb=20160523202936" class="image"><img alt="" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw%3D%3D" decoding="async" width="236" height="125" class="thumbimage lazyload" data-image-name="Metamorphmagi.gif" data-image-key="Metamorphmagi.gif" data-src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/2/23/Metamorphmagi.gif/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/236?cb=20160523202936" /></a> 	<noscript><a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/2/23/Metamorphmagi.gif/revision/latest?cb=20160523202936" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/2/23/Metamorphmagi.gif/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/236?cb=20160523202936" decoding="async" width="236" height="125" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="Metamorphmagi.gif" data-image-key="Metamorphmagi.gif" data-src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/2/23/Metamorphmagi.gif/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/236?cb=20160523202936" /></a></noscript> 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:Metamorphmagi.gif" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption"><a href="/wiki/Nymphadora_Tonks" title="Nymphadora Tonks">Nymphadora Tonks</a> changing her facial features at will and with ease</p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure>
<p>It is unknown whether Metamorphmagi receive any training in developing their innate skill, or if they learn through trial and error. Given their rarity, it is unlikely that much training is available. However, Tonks has shown that she only needs to concentrate a little to change her appearance, so it may be simply a matter of the Metamorphmagi wanting to change. While it has been debated whether Animagi transformations and <a href="/wiki/Apparition" title="Apparition">Disapparition</a> require a wand, it seems Metamorphmagi do not need theirs to utilise their abilities. Tonks has claimed that other wizards need a wand or potions to transform, meaning Metamorphmagi such as herself are not restricted as such.
</p><p>It is unknown if Metamorphmagi must register with the <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">Ministry of Magic</a> like <a href="/wiki/Animagus" title="Animagus">Animagi</a> must, as Metamorphmagi did not choose to become what they are, while Animagi did. Although unlike Animagi, Metamorphmagi do not possess any distinct markings that come with their transformation, so it would be far more difficult to keep track of them for any signs of ability abuse.
</p><p>Like many other types of magic, a Metamorphmagus' abilities are affected by their emotional state. The person's appearance at any moment will often reflect the emotion the person is feeling at the time. Strong emotions, such as shock or sadness, can affect their abilities to the point of having trouble using them at all. For example, Nymphadora Tonks had difficulty using her abilities&#160;following&#160;<a href="/wiki/Sirius_Black" title="Sirius Black">Sirius Black</a>'s death,&#160;when she was depressed over her feelings for <a href="/wiki/Remus_Lupin" title="Remus Lupin">Remus Lupin</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Metamorphic abilities can be very helpful when it comes to disguises. For example, Nymphadora Tonks was able to put her abilities to use in performing espionage for the <a href="/wiki/Order_of_the_Phoenix" title="Order of the Phoenix">Order of the Phoenix</a>. She also got full marks in <a href="/wiki/Concealment_and_Disguise" title="Concealment and Disguise">Concealment and Disguise</a> of her <a href="/wiki/Auror_training" title="Auror training">Auror training</a>, implying that this ability can be used for official examinations and competitions.<sup id="cite_ref-OTP_1-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OTP-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p><a href="/wiki/Albus_Dumbledore" title="Albus Dumbledore">Albus Dumbledore</a> wrote an article examining whether it was possible for Metamorphmagi to use their transformational abilities to disappear without a trace in a <a href="/wiki/1926" title="1926">1926</a> issue of <i><a href="/wiki/Transfiguration_Today" title="Transfiguration Today">Transfiguration Today</a></i>.<sup id="cite_ref-fb-mag_4-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-fb-mag-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Metamorphmagi can use their abilities to make themselves look exactly like another person. During her school years at Hogwarts School, Tonks often got detentions for turning into teachers.<sup id="cite_ref-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> This also included <a href="/wiki/Ghost" title="Ghost">ghosts</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Metamorphmagus?veaction=edit&amp;section=4" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Metamorphmagus?action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>In Latin, the female form of Magus is Maga. However, female Metamorphmagi are never referred to as "Metamorphmaga" or "Metamorphmagae", which would be the female plural, in canon.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Jacob%27s_sibling" title="Jacob&#39;s sibling">Jacob's sibling</a> will jokingly suggest that they may be a Metamorphmagus to distract <a href="/wiki/Nymphadora_Tonks" title="Nymphadora Tonks">Nymphadora Tonks</a> when playing Gobstones with her, an apparent tongue-in-cheek reference to the player being able to adjust their appearance at any time. However there is no canon reference to this. During the game the character may use both <a href="/wiki/Polyjuice_potion" class="mw-redirect" title="Polyjuice potion">polyjuice potion</a> and become an <a href="/wiki/Animagus" title="Animagus">animagus</a>, suggesting that it may not be the case.</li></ul>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Metamorphmagus?veaction=edit&amp;section=5" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Metamorphmagus?action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>In Greek, "meta" means "change," "morph" means "shape," and "magus" (in Latin) means "<a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magic</a> or <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a>." Hence, a wizard that can change shape, and the term "Metamorphosis" is the term to describe an animal changing into another animal such as how a <a href="/wiki/Caterpillar" title="Caterpillar">caterpillar</a> changes into a <a href="/wiki/Butterfly" title="Butterfly">butterfly</a>.
</p>
