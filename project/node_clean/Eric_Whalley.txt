<b>Eric Whalley</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggle</a> orphan, living in <a href="/wiki/Wool%27s_Orphanage" title="Wool&#39;s Orphanage">Wool's Orphanage</a>, in <a href="/wiki/London" title="London">London</a>, as well as <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Tom Marvolo Riddle</a>. At the time Tom was eleven, Eric and <a href="/wiki/Billy_Stubbs" title="Billy Stubbs">Billy Stubbs</a> had the <a href="/wiki/Chicken_pox" title="Chicken pox">chicken pox</a> and were being cared for by <a href="/wiki/Martha_(disambiguation)" title="Martha (disambiguation)">Martha</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP13_1-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP13-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Eric_Whalley?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Eric_Whalley?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The name "Eric" is from the Old Norse name Eiríkr, derived from the elements ei, meaning "ever" and ríkr, meaning "ruler".
