<b>Ragnok the Pigeon-Toed</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Goblin" title="Goblin">Goblin</a>. He was an activist for goblin rights and the author of <i><a href="/wiki/Little_People,_Big_Plans" title="Little People, Big Plans">Little People, Big Plans</a></i>. He was a member of the <a href="/wiki/Brotherhood_of_Goblins" title="Brotherhood of Goblins">Brotherhood of Goblins</a>, and gave statements to the Daily Prophet after the <a href="/wiki/Chipping_Clodbury_riot" title="Chipping Clodbury riot">Chipping Clodbury riot</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-DP_1-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DP-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
