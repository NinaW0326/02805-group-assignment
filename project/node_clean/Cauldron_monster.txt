A <b>cauldron monster</b> is an <a href="/wiki/Charm" title="Charm">enchanted</a> object resembling a man, made up of a <a href="/wiki/Cauldron" title="Cauldron">cauldron</a> and burning torches. It has a <a href="/wiki/Human" title="Human">human</a>-shaped torso and head made of a charmed cauldron and its arms and legs are made up of burning torches. This charmed object spits <a href="/wiki/Fire" title="Fire">fire</a> and can throw its burning arms to cause damage.<sup id="cite_ref-POAG_1-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-POAG-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
