<b>Robert Bliss</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggle</a> reporter who co-wrote a front-page article with <a href="/wiki/Amanda_Pettet" title="Amanda Pettet">Amanda Pettet</a> about the heat wave that swept over <a href="/wiki/Great_Britain" title="Great Britain">Great Britain</a> for the <i><a href="/wiki/Daily_Express" title="Daily Express">Daily Express</a></i> in the summer of <a href="/wiki/1995" title="1995">1995</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-HPLE_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HPLE-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
