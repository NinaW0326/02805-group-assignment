<p><b>Professor Phineas Nigellus Black</b> (<a href="/wiki/1847" class="mw-redirect" title="1847">1847</a><sup id="cite_ref-family_1-35" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-family-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> – <a href="/wiki/1925" title="1925">1925</a><sup id="cite_ref-family_1-36" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-family-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>) was a <a href="/wiki/Pure-blood" title="Pure-blood">pure-blood</a> <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a>, possibly the son of <a href="/wiki/Cygnus_Black_I" title="Cygnus Black I">Cygnus</a> and <a href="/wiki/Ella_Max" title="Ella Max">Ella Black</a>, and brother of <a href="/wiki/Sirius_Black_I" title="Sirius Black I">Sirius</a>, <a href="/wiki/Elladora_Black" title="Elladora Black">Elladora</a> and <a href="/wiki/Iola_Black" title="Iola Black">Iola Black</a>. He attended <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts</a> as a member of <a href="/wiki/Slytherin" title="Slytherin">Slytherin House</a>, later becoming the school's least popular <a href="/wiki/Headmaster" title="Headmaster">Headmaster</a>, loathing his profession. Following his death in <a href="/wiki/1925" title="1925">1925</a>, his <a href="/wiki/Portrait" title="Portrait">portrait</a> was hung in the <a href="/wiki/Headmaster%27s_office" class="mw-redirect" title="Headmaster&#39;s office">Headmaster's office</a> to offer advice to the current headmaster.
</p>

<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_life">Early life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Phineas_Nigellus_Black?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Early life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Phineas_Nigellus_Black?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Early life"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<figure class="thumb tleft show-info-icon" style="width: 152px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/f/f0/Phineas_Nigellus.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20140727134843" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/f/f0/Phineas_Nigellus.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/150?cb=20140727134843" decoding="async" width="150" height="117" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="Phineas Nigellus.jpg" data-image-key="Phineas_Nigellus.jpg" /></a> 	 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:Phineas_Nigellus.jpg" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption">Phineas Nigellus, as depicted in the <a href="/wiki/Black_Family_Tapestry" title="Black Family Tapestry">Black family tree tapestry</a></p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure>
<p>Phineas Nigellus was born to the <a href="/wiki/House_of_Black" title="House of Black">House of Black</a> in <a href="/wiki/1847" class="mw-redirect" title="1847">1847</a>, possibly to <a href="/wiki/Cygnus_Black_I" title="Cygnus Black I">Cygnus Black I</a> and <a href="/wiki/Ella_Max" title="Ella Max">Ella Max</a>. He had three siblings; <a href="/wiki/Sirius_Black_I" title="Sirius Black I">Sirius</a>, <a href="/wiki/Elladora_Black" title="Elladora Black">Elladora</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Iola_Black" title="Iola Black">Iola Black</a>.
</p><p>The siblings were raised in a household that strongly believed in the importance of <a href="/wiki/Blood_status" title="Blood status">blood purity</a>, and as such Phineas carried a grudge against <a href="/wiki/Muggle-born" title="Muggle-born">Muggle-borns</a>, whom he called <a href="/wiki/Mudblood" title="Mudblood">Mudbloods</a>, that lasted throughout his life. His brother Sirius died in <a href="/wiki/1853" class="mw-redirect" title="1853">1853</a>, at the age of eight, which likely had an effect on young Phineas Nigellus.
</p><p>At the age of eleven, circa <a href="/wiki/1858" class="mw-redirect" title="1858">1858</a>, Phineas Nigellus began his schooling at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a>, where he was <a href="/wiki/Sorting_ceremony" title="Sorting ceremony">sorted</a> into <a href="/wiki/Slytherin" title="Slytherin">Slytherin House</a>.
</p><p>Sometime after completing his schooling in c. <a href="/wiki/1865" class="mw-redirect" title="1865">1865</a>, Phineas Nigellus married <a href="/wiki/Ursula_Flint" title="Ursula Flint">Ursula Flint</a>, and, at the age of 30, the two had a son, <a href="/wiki/Sirius_Black_II" title="Sirius Black II">Sirius Black</a>, named after his deceased brother. Four more children followed the first; <a href="/wiki/Phineas_Black" title="Phineas Black">Phineas</a>, who did not take his father and namesake's view on the topic of <a href="/wiki/Pure-blood" title="Pure-blood">pure-blood</a> supremacy and was disowned by the family for supporting <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggle</a> rights; <a href="/wiki/Cygnus_Black_II" title="Cygnus Black II">Cygnus</a>, <a href="/wiki/Belvina_Black" title="Belvina Black">Belvina</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Arcturus_Black_II" title="Arcturus Black II">Arcturus</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-family_1-37" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-family-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="As_Headmaster_of_Hogwarts">As Headmaster of Hogwarts</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Phineas_Nigellus_Black?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: As Headmaster of Hogwarts">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Phineas_Nigellus_Black?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: As Headmaster of Hogwarts"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<div class="quote">
<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix">"<i>There's Phineas Nigellus. . . my great-great-grandfather, see? . . . least popular Headmaster Hogwarts ever had.</i>"</span></dd>
<dd>&#8212;<a href="/wiki/Sirius_Black" title="Sirius Black">Sirius Black</a> on Phineas's legacy as Hogwarts Headmaster.<sup class="noprint"><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Order_of_the_Phoenix" title="Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix">[src]</a></sup></dd>
<dd></dd></dl></div>
<p>At some point, and in spite of his dislike of young people, whom he found self-obsessed and tedious, Black returned to <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts</a> as a teacher. He sometime was made <a href="/wiki/Headmaster" title="Headmaster">Headmaster</a> of Hogwarts. His great-great-grandson <a href="/wiki/Sirius_Black_III" class="mw-redirect" title="Sirius Black III">Sirius Black III</a> would later describe him as the least popular head the school had ever known. It is unknown what approach Black took towards Muggle-borns in his capacity as <a href="/wiki/Headmaster" title="Headmaster">Headmaster</a>; whatever it might have been, the <a href="/wiki/House_of_Black" title="House of Black">Black family</a> did not disown him for it; this may, however, have been because he was the head of the family.
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Death_and_post-mortem">Death and post-mortem</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Phineas_Nigellus_Black?veaction=edit&amp;section=4" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Death and post-mortem">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Phineas_Nigellus_Black?action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Death and post-mortem"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<div class="quote">
<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix">"<i>We are honour-bound to give service to the present Headmaster of Hogwarts! Shame on you, Phineas!</i>"</span></dd>
<dd>&#8212;<a href="/wiki/Armando_Dippet" title="Armando Dippet">Armando Dippet</a>'s portrait to a sleepy Phineas<sup class="noprint"><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Order_of_the_Phoenix" title="Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix">[src]</a></sup></dd>
<dd></dd></dl></div>
<p>Professor Black died in <a href="/wiki/1925" title="1925">1925</a> at age 78. At the time of his death, Phineas Nigellus had five children, at least thirteen grandchildren, and two great-grandchildren,<sup id="cite_ref-family_1-38" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-family-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> although more would be born after he died.
</p><p>Phineas Nigellus's portrait in the <a href="/wiki/Headmaster%27s_office" class="mw-redirect" title="Headmaster&#39;s office">headmaster's office</a> did not seem to enjoy assisting <a href="/wiki/Albus_Dumbledore" title="Albus Dumbledore">Albus Dumbledore</a>, believing that he was eccentric and disagreeing with his pro-<a href="/wiki/Muggle-born" title="Muggle-born">Muggle-born</a> beliefs. The second portrait of Black was hung at <a href="/wiki/12_Grimmauld_Place" title="12 Grimmauld Place">12 Grimmauld Place</a>, and like other portrait subjects in the wizarding world, Black could travel between his portraits, which he did often.
</p><p>In <a href="/wiki/1995" title="1995">1995</a>, <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> stayed in the bedroom where Black's portrait hangs, and Black took him messages from Albus Dumbledore. His voice could sometimes be heard coming from the frame when he was not in it, making sarcastic comments. He also seems to be against people with <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggle</a> parentage. One of the main messages Phineas passed was when <a href="/wiki/Arthur_Weasley" title="Arthur Weasley">Arthur Weasley</a> was attacked by <a href="/wiki/Nagini" title="Nagini">Nagini</a> whilst on duty for the order, letting <a href="/wiki/Sirius_Black" title="Sirius Black">Sirius</a> know he was going to have company.
</p>

<p>During <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>, <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ron Weasley</a> and <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione Granger</a>'s quest to destroy <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Horcrux" title="Horcrux">Horcruxes</a>, Phineas Nigellus's Grimmauld Place portrait was hidden in Hermione's <a href="/wiki/Extension_Charm" title="Extension Charm">magically enlarged</a> <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger%27s_handbag" class="mw-redirect" title="Hermione Granger&#39;s handbag">beaded bag</a>, to stop him from being able to report their location to <a href="/wiki/Severus_Snape" title="Severus Snape">Severus Snape</a>, then headmaster of Hogwarts. His portrait was then taken with the trio when they left the house. 
</p><p>During this time, Phineas reluctantly dropped certain snippets of information on what was going on in Hogwarts to them, and aided them in their quest; he managed to find out where they were camping and informed Snape, who was able to give them <a href="/wiki/Godric_Gryffindor%27s_Sword" class="mw-redirect" title="Godric Gryffindor&#39;s Sword">Godric Gryffindor's Sword</a>. He appeared less reluctant to help Snape than he was when helping Dumbledore, probably proud to help another Slytherin headteacher. 
</p><p>Phineas Nigellus possibly watched the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a> in <a href="/wiki/1998" title="1998">1998</a>. After Lord Voldemort's defeat, he saw Harry Potter, Ron Weasley and Hermione Granger at the Headmaster's office; like all the headmaster portraits, he looked very happy and proudly brought up the fact that Slytherin house had contributed to the victory, referring to his own contribution, as well as those of Snape, Slughorn, and the Malfoys. He probably would have continued to help future headteachers when the <a href="/wiki/Second_Wizarding_War" title="Second Wizarding War">Second Wizarding War</a> was over.
</p><p>In <a href="/wiki/2019" title="2019">2019</a>, the portrait Phineas Nigellus stand was captured by the <a href="/wiki/Calamity" title="Calamity">Calamity</a> and began to appear around the world as a <a href="/wiki/Foundable" title="Foundable">Foundable</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-WU_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-WU-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<p>Professor Black was a clever looking wizard, with black hair, dark eyes, a pointed beard and thin eyebrows. </p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Personality_and_traits">Personality and traits</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Phineas_Nigellus_Black?veaction=edit&amp;section=6" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Personality and traits">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Phineas_Nigellus_Black?action=edit&amp;section=6" title="Edit section: Personality and traits"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<div class="quote">
<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix">"<i>Never try to understand the students. They hate it. They would much rather be tragically misunderstood, wallow in self-pity, stew in their own —</i>"</span></dd>
<dd>&#8212;Phineas Nigellus' portrait<sup class="noprint"><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Order_of_the_Phoenix" title="Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix">[src]</a></sup></dd>
<dd></dd></dl></div>
<p>Phineas was a snide, sarcastic fellow. He has little patience for the feelings and the problems of others, particularly young people, whom he found tedious and self-absorbed, and had no qualms whatsoever in using demeaning phrases when referring to others. He also seemed to support <a href="/wiki/Blood_status" title="Blood status">blood purity</a> since he looked down on <a href="/wiki/Muggle-born" title="Muggle-born">Muggle-borns</a> and even used the offensive term <a href="/wiki/Mudblood" title="Mudblood">Mudblood</a>, though his views didn't seem as aggressive as some of his descendants. He seemed sentimental of the Black family possessions, as he was quite dismayed upon learning that <a href="/wiki/Mundungus_Fletcher" title="Mundungus Fletcher">Mundungus Fletcher</a> was stealing and selling them.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Magical_abilities_and_skills">Magical abilities and skills</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Phineas_Nigellus_Black?veaction=edit&amp;section=7" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Phineas_Nigellus_Black?action=edit&amp;section=7" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li><b><a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">Magical</a> Knowledge:</b>  Black took great pride in his knowledge, looking down upon those who did not appear to appreciate the value of learning, having taught his portrait to mock whoever appeared to be less knowledgeable than it and, by extension, himself, by having it refer to them as "ignorant". Having taught his portrait to talk and act like himself, he also educated it on things he believed later generations of Headmasters and Headmistresses would find useful, among them specifics of the metalwork of goblins.<sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup></li></ul>

<div class="quote">

<p>Phineas Nigellus Black's older brother, <a href="/wiki/Sirius_Black_I" title="Sirius Black I">Sirius</a>, died in childhood. He had two sisters, <a href="/wiki/Elladora_Black" title="Elladora Black">Elladora</a> who began the family tradition of beheading aged <a href="/wiki/House-elf" title="House-elf">house-elves</a> and <a href="/wiki/Iola_Black" title="Iola Black">Iola</a> who was disowned for marrying <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggle</a> <a href="/wiki/Bob_Hitchens" title="Bob Hitchens">Bob Hitchens</a>. 
</p>

<p>Phineas Nigellus Black was married to <a href="/wiki/Ursula_Flint" title="Ursula Flint">Ursula Flint</a> and they are among the oldest known ancestors of the "Noble and Most Ancient House of Black." They had five children: <a href="/wiki/Sirius_Black_II" title="Sirius Black II">Sirius</a>, <a href="/wiki/Phineas_Black" title="Phineas Black">Phineas</a>, <a href="/wiki/Cygnus_Black_II" title="Cygnus Black II">Cygnus</a>, <a href="/wiki/Belvina_Black" title="Belvina Black">Belvina</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Arcturus_Black_II" title="Arcturus Black II">Arcturus</a>. Phineas was disowned for supporting Muggle rights.
</p><p>As the House of Black was numerous and very powerful, many other wizards have ancestral ties to Phineas Nigellus Black, including the <a href="/wiki/Malfoy_family" title="Malfoy family">Malfoy</a>, <a href="/wiki/Lestrange_family" title="Lestrange family">Lestrange</a>, <a href="/wiki/Tonks_family" title="Tonks family">Tonks</a> and <a href="/wiki/Weasley_family" title="Weasley family">Weasley</a> families. Additionally, Phineas may have descendants or distant relatives in the <a href="/wiki/Potter_family" title="Potter family">Potter</a> and <a href="/wiki/Longbottom_family" title="Longbottom family">Longbottom</a> clans, as well as in the <a href="/wiki/Crouch_family" title="Crouch family">Crouch</a>, <a href="/wiki/Prewett_family" title="Prewett family">Prewett</a> and <a href="/wiki/Burke_family" title="Burke family">Burke</a> families. Furthermore, many other familiar surnames appear among those who married into the Black family tree, which may tie Phineas with other wizarding families. For example, <a href="/wiki/Ursula_Flint" title="Ursula Flint">Ursula Flint</a>, <a href="/wiki/Violetta_Bulstrode" title="Violetta Bulstrode">Violetta Bulstrode</a>, <a href="/wiki/Melania_Macmillan" title="Melania Macmillan">Melania Macmillan</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Irma_Crabbe" title="Irma Crabbe">Irma Crabbe</a> may be related to individuals in <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>'s generation.
</p><p>Though Black did not get along well with most people, his portrait was upset to learn his great-great-grandson <a href="/wiki/Sirius_Black" title="Sirius Black">Sirius Black</a>, the last of the surname Black, was dead in <a href="/wiki/1996" title="1996">1996</a>, and was visibly upset when he learned that <a href="/wiki/Mundungus_Fletcher" title="Mundungus Fletcher">Mundungus Fletcher</a> was stealing Black family heirlooms the following year. This could have been from dynastic or emotional reasons.
</p>

<p>Black did not seem to hold much with one of his more distant successors as Headmaster, <a href="/wiki/Albus_Dumbledore" title="Albus Dumbledore">Albus Dumbledore</a>. While others, including <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> and <a href="/wiki/Rubeus_Hagrid" title="Rubeus Hagrid">Rubeus Hagrid</a>, considered Dumbledore the greatest sorcerer in the world, Phineas considered him to be too eccentric and to be wasting his time by trying to understand the students.
</p><p>Phineas Nigellus was, along with the other <a href="/wiki/Portrait" title="Portrait">portraits</a> of the dead headmasters of <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts</a>, bound to serve the head of the day, although, with Dumbledore at the very least, he despised doing this.
</p><p>Black's opinion of the man went on even after Dumbledore's self-planned death at the hands of <a href="/wiki/Severus_Snape" title="Severus Snape">Severus Snape</a> in <a href="/wiki/1997" title="1997">1997</a>, and he proceeded to admire Snape, who later became Headmaster himself, considerably more.
</p><p>However, after Dumbledore escaped from being arrested by the <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">Ministry of Magic</a> in <a href="/wiki/1996" title="1996">1996</a>, Phineas Nigellus commented to <a href="/wiki/Cornelius_Fudge" title="Cornelius Fudge">Cornelius Fudge</a> that although he disagreed with Dumbledore on many accounts, one couldn't deny that he had style. This may indicate that Phineas Nigellus had a little bit of admiration for Dumbledore.
</p><p>Given the timeline it's possible that Phineas Nigellus was the headmaster when Dumbledore was a student in the <a href="/wiki/1890s" title="1890s">1890s</a>. This has never been confirmed however.
</p>

<p>Any relationship with Phineas before <a href="/wiki/Severus_Snape" title="Severus Snape">Severus Snape</a> became Headmaster of Hogwarts School is unknown, but by the time that the Snape was appointed to the post in <a href="/wiki/1997" title="1997">1997</a>, Black came to greatly admire Snape, who was the first Head of the school to have once been in <a href="/wiki/Slytherin" title="Slytherin">Slytherin</a> since he himself had presided over the castle. Whenever others would insult Snape, Phineas Nigellus would immediately end the conversation, although this disliking of anyone who insulted the subject of his respect did not carry so far as to stop other factors, such as curiosity, from refusing to speak to them indefinitely. Snape himself, however, resented Phineas' tendency towards slurs directed at Muggle-borns, immediately demanding that Phineas never used the term "Mudblood" after Black let the phrase slip in Snape's presence.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Phineas_Nigellus_Black?veaction=edit&amp;section=13" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Phineas_Nigellus_Black?action=edit&amp;section=13" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li><a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Phineas" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Phineas">Phineas</a> was the Biblical grandson of Aaron and was a controversial high priest who murdered two lovers after they had intercourse on the steps of the Tabernacle. Another reason for the murder was that one was Hebrew and the other was not. His example has been used to justify attacks on interracial couples over the centuries. Some believe the Hebrew name <i>Pinhas</i> means "snake's mouth," or else it could be derived from  the Egyptian name <i>Pa-Nehasy</i>, which means "Nubian" or "black man." It may also reference the Greek legend of the Phineus,who angered Zeus by telling mortals the secrets of the deities. He was made blind and sentenced to an island where there would be a lot of food, but when he tried to eat some, the harpies would come and snatch it out of his hands.</li>
<li>In the film adaptation of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Order_of_the_Phoenix" title="Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix">Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix</a></i>, Phineas is played by <a href="/wiki/John_Atterbury" title="John Atterbury">John Atterbury</a>. In the film, Dumbledore is seen asking the portrait of Phineas to check its opposite number at the Order headquarters during efforts to locate <a href="/wiki/Arthur_Weasley" title="Arthur Weasley">Arthur Weasley</a>. This is the only "active" appearance of Black in the film series, seeing as he does not appear in the film adaptations of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Half-Blood_Prince" title="Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince">Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince</a></i> and <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Deathly_Hallows" title="Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows">Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows</a></i>. However, this makes it unclear how Snape located Harry and Hermione in the <a href="/wiki/Forest_of_Dean" title="Forest of Dean">Forest of Dean</a>.</li>
<li>Phineas Nigellus's portrait, as featured in the film adaptation of <i>Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix</i>, seems to be a reference to <a href="http://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File:Portrait_of_Baldassare_Castiglione_by_Raphael.jpg" class="extiw" title="commons:File:Portrait of Baldassare Castiglione by Raphael.jpg">a 1515 portrait of Balthazar Castiglione</a> by <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Raphael" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Raphael">Raphael</a>.<sup class="noprint">&#91;<a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Wikipedia:Citing_sources" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Wikipedia:Citing sources"><i><span title="The material in the vicinity of this tag needs references to reliable sources." style="white-space: nowrap;">citation needed</span></i></a>&#93;</sup></li>
<li>It can be briefly seen that the name Phineas is misspelled Phineus on the tapestry in the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Order_of_the_Phoenix_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix (film)">film</a>.</li>
<li>Phineas Nigellus was possibly the son of <a href="/wiki/Cygnus_Black_I" title="Cygnus Black I">Cygnus Black I</a> and nephew of <a href="/wiki/Arcturus_Black_I" title="Arcturus Black I">Arcturus Black I</a>, two wizards who appeared only in the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Order_of_the_Phoenix_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix (film)">film adaptation</a> of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Order_of_the_Phoenix" title="Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix">Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix</a></i>.</li>
<li>It is quite possible that Phineas Nigellus's portrait was moved from another location to its current one when the <a href="/wiki/Order_of_the_Phoenix" title="Order of the Phoenix">Order</a> moved into <a href="/wiki/12_Grimmauld_Place" title="12 Grimmauld Place">Grimmauld Place</a> so that he could keep an eye on Harry for <a href="/wiki/Albus_Dumbledore" title="Albus Dumbledore">Dumbledore</a>.</li>
<li>Phineas' appearance in the movies was quite different, with grey hair and a large beard, wearing, instead of his Slytherin-coloured robes, eccentric headdresses quite common to the wizarding world, such as a pointed red cap with a fur brim and a turban.<sup id="cite_ref-film_5-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-film-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup></li></ul>
