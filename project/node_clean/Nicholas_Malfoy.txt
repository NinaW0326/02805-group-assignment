<b>Nicholas Malfoy</b> (fl. c. <a href="/wiki/1340s" title="1340s">1340s</a>) was a <a href="/wiki/Pure-blood" title="Pure-blood">pure-blood</a> <a href="/wiki/England" title="England">English</a> <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> and a member of the <a href="/wiki/Malfoy_family" title="Malfoy family">Malfoy family</a>. He is believed to have dispatched many <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggle</a> tenants under the guise of the <a href="/wiki/Black_Death" title="Black Death">Black Death</a>. If this is true, he escaped censure by the <a href="/wiki/Wizards%27_Council" title="Wizards&#39; Council">Wizards' Council</a>, and thus was never punished for it.<sup id="cite_ref-PM_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PM-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Nicholas_Malfoy?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Nicholas_Malfoy?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>The name <i>Nicholas </i>is a Greek name meaning "victory of the people." This could refer to the fact that after Malfoy killed many a Muggle, he was never punished.</li>
<li>The name <i>Malfoy </i>comes from the French <i>mal foi</i>, meaning "bad faith." If Malfoy is anything like his descendants, then this name is fitting.</li></ul>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Nicholas_Malfoy?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Nicholas_Malfoy?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>In <a href="/wiki/1992" title="1992">1992</a>, <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>, speaking about <a href="/wiki/Draco_Malfoy" title="Draco Malfoy">Draco Malfoy</a>, said, <i>"Look at his family... The whole lot of them have been in Slytherin, he's always boasting about it"</i>.<sup id="cite_ref-COS9_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COS9-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> This indicates that Nicholas was most likely a <a href="/wiki/Slytherin" title="Slytherin">Slytherin</a> if he attended <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a>, though it cannot be confirmed.</li></ul>
