These <b>five people</b> were <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizards or witches</a> (or both) who were known to be registered <a href="/wiki/Animagus" title="Animagus">Animagi</a>; however their animal forms are unknown. <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione Granger</a> referred to them by saying that there were only seven registered Animagi in the <a href="/wiki/20th_century" title="20th century">20th century</a>. Since the <a href="/wiki/Marauders" title="Marauders">Marauders</a> trio (<a href="/wiki/James_Potter_I" title="James Potter I">Prongs</a>, <a href="/wiki/Sirius_Black" title="Sirius Black">Padfoot</a> and <a href="/wiki/Peter_Pettigrew" title="Peter Pettigrew">Wormtail</a>) were unregistered, it is at least conceivable that others may have effected this transformation without registering.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<ul><li><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_registered_Animagus" title="Unidentified registered Animagus">An unidentified Animagus</a> appears in <i><a href="/wiki/LEGO_Harry_Potter:_Years_1-4" title="LEGO Harry Potter: Years 1-4">LEGO Harry Potter: Years 1-4</a></i>. She took the form of a black cat. As she changed shape in a public location, it is likely she was registered.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li>It is possible that <a href="/wiki/Jacob%27s_sibling" title="Jacob&#39;s sibling">Jacob's sibling</a> is one of them, as in the Animagus achievement quest in <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter:_Hogwarts_Mystery" title="Harry Potter: Hogwarts Mystery">Harry Potter: Hogwarts Mystery</a></i>, the player gets to choose whether or not they want to register.</li></ul>
