<b>Edmund Bollifant</b> was a publisher based in <a href="/wiki/London" title="London">London</a> in the late <a href="/wiki/16th_century" title="16th century">16th century</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-hple_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-hple-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> His imprint published <i><a href="/wiki/A_Perambulation_of_Kent" title="A Perambulation of Kent">A Perambulation of Kent</a></i> in <a href="/wiki/1596" class="mw-redirect" title="1596">1596</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-hple_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-hple-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
