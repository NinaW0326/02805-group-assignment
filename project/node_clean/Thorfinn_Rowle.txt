<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows">"<i>I think the big one's Thorfinn Rowle.</i>"</span></dd>

<p><b>Thorfinn Rowle</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Great_Britain" title="Great Britain">British</a> <a href="/wiki/Dark_Wizard" title="Dark Wizard">Dark Wizard</a> and a <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eater</a> who fought in the <a href="/wiki/Second_Wizarding_War" title="Second Wizarding War">Second Wizarding War</a>. He fought at the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_the_Astronomy_Tower" title="Battle of the Astronomy Tower">Battle of the Astronomy Tower</a>, where he accidentally <a href="/wiki/Killing_Curse" title="Killing Curse">killed</a> fellow Death Eater <a href="/wiki/Gibbon" title="Gibbon">Gibbon</a>, burned <a href="/wiki/Rubeus_Hagrid%27s_cabin" class="mw-redirect" title="Rubeus Hagrid&#39;s cabin">Rubeus Hagrid's cabin</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Cruciatus_Curse" title="Cruciatus Curse">tortured</a> <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP28_2-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP28-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> On <a href="/wiki/1_August" title="1 August">1 August</a>, <a href="/wiki/1997" title="1997">1997</a>, he tracked Harry Potter, <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ron Weasley</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione Granger</a> to <a href="/wiki/Tottenham_Court_Road" title="Tottenham Court Road">Tottenham Court Road</a> along with <a href="/wiki/Antonin_Dolohov" title="Antonin Dolohov">Antonin Dolohov</a> disguised as construction workers, but the two Death Eaters were defeated and their memories were <a href="/wiki/Memory_Charm" title="Memory Charm">modified</a> by Hermione to cover their escape. After this failure, Rowle was punished severely by Lord Voldemort. He also fought at the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a> on <a href="/wiki/2_May" title="2 May">2 May</a>, <a href="/wiki/1998" title="1998">1998</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-DH9_4-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH9-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Rowle's fate after the Second Wizarding War and Voldemort's final defeat is uncertain, although it is highly likely he was either killed or defeated during the Battle of Hogwarts, or that he was imprisoned in <a href="/wiki/Azkaban" title="Azkaban">Azkaban</a> with a life sentence for his crimes.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Thorfinn_Rowle?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Thorfinn_Rowle?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Second_Wizarding_War">Second Wizarding War</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Thorfinn_Rowle?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Second Wizarding War">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Thorfinn_Rowle?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Second Wizarding War"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Rowle was one of the <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a> of <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a> who fought in the <a href="/wiki/Second_Wizarding_War" title="Second Wizarding War">Second Wizarding War</a>. It is unknown if he had also fought in the <a href="/wiki/First_Wizarding_War" title="First Wizarding War">First Wizarding War</a>, though the fact that <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ronald Weasley</a> recognised him as well as <a href="/wiki/Antonin_Dolohov" title="Antonin Dolohov">Antonin Dolohov</a> from old wanted posters suggests that he was. It's unknown if he was imprisoned in <a href="/wiki/Azkaban" title="Azkaban">Azkaban</a>, though if not it's most likely that he was one of the <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a> who did not search for Voldemort after his downfall.
</p>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Battle_of_the_Astronomy_Tower">Battle of the Astronomy Tower</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Thorfinn_Rowle?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Battle of the Astronomy Tower">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Thorfinn_Rowle?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Battle of the Astronomy Tower"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<figure class="thumb tleft " style="width: 89px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/3/31/Battle2.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20090118224312" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/3/31/Battle2.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/87?cb=20090118224312" decoding="async" width="87" height="166" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="Battle2.jpg" data-image-key="Battle2.jpg" /></a> 	 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption">Thorfinn Rowle at the Astronomy Tower in 1997</p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure>
<p>Rowle participated in the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_the_Astronomy_Tower" title="Battle of the Astronomy Tower">attack</a> on <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> in <a href="/wiki/1997" title="1997">1997</a>; he stayed below the <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Castle" title="Hogwarts Castle">Astronomy Tower</a> in the main part of the Castle, and fought defenders by firing curses in all directions. He hit and killed fellow Death Eater <a href="/wiki/Gibbon" title="Gibbon">Gibbon</a> with a <a href="/wiki/Killing_Curse" title="Killing Curse">Killing Curse</a> meant for <a href="/wiki/Remus_Lupin" title="Remus Lupin">Remus Lupin</a> as Gibbon re-entered the battle. Later, Rowle hit a section of roof with another curse during a duel with <a href="/wiki/Nymphadora_Tonks" title="Nymphadora Tonks">Tonks</a> causing rubble to rain down and the barrier to be lifted.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP28_2-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP28-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>As battle recommenced, Rowle again fired <a href="/wiki/Curse" title="Curse">curses</a> everywhere, and after being hit in the face by one of Harry Potter's <a href="/wiki/Hex" title="Hex">hexes</a> he began to retreat from the school. During his retreat he somehow managed to make it to the grounds before Harry. He attempted to <a href="/wiki/Curse" title="Curse">curse</a> <a href="/wiki/Rubeus_Hagrid" title="Rubeus Hagrid">Rubeus Hagrid</a>, but his curses <a href="/wiki/Spell_resistance" title="Spell resistance">had no effect</a> on Hagrid because of his <a href="/wiki/Half-giant" title="Half-giant">Half-giant</a> blood. Instead, Rowle <a href="/wiki/Fire-making_spell" class="mw-redirect" title="Fire-making spell">set</a> <a href="/wiki/Hagrid%27s_hut" class="mw-redirect" title="Hagrid&#39;s hut">Hagrid's hut</a> on <a href="/wiki/Fire" title="Fire">fire</a> forcing Hagrid to rescue his dog <a href="/wiki/Fang" title="Fang">Fang</a>, who was inside. He was then ordered by <a href="/wiki/Severus_Snape" title="Severus Snape">Severus Snape</a>, who at the time was operating in his capacity as a double-agent in open support of Voldemort, to leave the grounds and <a href="/wiki/Apparition" title="Apparition">Disapparate</a> away with <a href="/wiki/Alecto_and_Amycus_Carrow" title="Alecto and Amycus Carrow">Alecto and Amycus Carrow</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP28_2-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP28-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

He was not identified by name to <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> until they met face to face in the chaos following the attack on the <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">Ministry of Magic</a> in <a href="/wiki/1997" title="1997">1997</a>. He and <a href="/wiki/Antonin_Dolohov" title="Antonin Dolohov">Antonin Dolohov</a> tracked Harry, <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ron Weasley</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione Granger</a> to a <a href="/wiki/Luchino_Caffe" title="Luchino Caffe">café</a> on <a href="/wiki/Tottenham_Court_Road" title="Tottenham Court Road">Tottenham Court Road</a> via the <a href="/wiki/Taboo" title="Taboo">Taboo curse</a> on Voldemort's name after their escape from <a href="/wiki/The_Burrow" title="The Burrow">the Burrow</a>. A <a href="/wiki/Luchino_Caffe_Duel" title="Luchino Caffe Duel">fight</a> broke out, and Rowle was <a href="/wiki/Stunning_Spell" title="Stunning Spell">stunned</a> by Harry, who was under his <a href="/wiki/Cloak_of_Invisibility" title="Cloak of Invisibility">Invisibility Cloak</a>. Afterward, his and Dolohov's memories were <a href="/wiki/Memory_Charm" title="Memory Charm">modified</a> by Hermione, even though she had not previously performed the enchantment. Ron recognised him and his partner, <a href="/wiki/Antonin_Dolohov" title="Antonin Dolohov">Antonin Dolohov</a>, from an old <i><a href="/wiki/Daily_Prophet" title="Daily Prophet">Daily Prophet</a></i> wanted poster.<sup id="cite_ref-DH9_4-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH9-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup></p><figure class="thumb tleft show-info-icon" style="width: 237px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/0/00/Attackrowle.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20100607134100" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/0/00/Attackrowle.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/235?cb=20100607134100" decoding="async" width="235" height="156" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="Attackrowle.jpg" data-image-key="Attackrowle.jpg" /></a> 	 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:Attackrowle.jpg" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption">Rowle, being stunned by Harry Potter</p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure>
<p>Through his connection to Voldemort, Harry witnessed Voldemort ordering <a href="/wiki/Draco_Malfoy" title="Draco Malfoy">Draco Malfoy</a> to <a href="/wiki/Cruciatus_Curse" title="Cruciatus Curse">torture</a> Rowle soon after this incident and threatened to feed him to <a href="/wiki/Nagini" title="Nagini">Nagini</a>, as punishment for failing to capture Harry and his friends. His partner, Antonin Dolohov was also similarly punished. Despite this, both Rowle and Dolohov were spared though other <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a> were more careful about signalling Voldemort after that.<sup id="cite_ref-DH9_4-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH9-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Physical_appearance">Physical appearance</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Thorfinn_Rowle?veaction=edit&amp;section=6" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Physical appearance">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Thorfinn_Rowle?action=edit&amp;section=6" title="Edit section: Physical appearance"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<div class="quote">
<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince">"<i>...the huge, blond <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eater</a>...</i>"</span></dd>
<dd>&#8212;Description of Thorfinn Rowle<sup class="noprint"><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Half-Blood_Prince" title="Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince">[src]</a></sup></dd>
<dd></dd></dl></div><p>Thorfinn Rowle was an enormous, muscled Death Eater with blond hair.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP28_2-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP28-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> As a Death Eater, he bore the <a href="/wiki/Dark_Mark" title="Dark Mark">Dark Mark</a> on his left inner forearm, which faded to a mere scar upon Voldemort's death.
</p><h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Personality_and_traits">Personality and traits</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Thorfinn_Rowle?veaction=edit&amp;section=7" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Personality and traits">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Thorfinn_Rowle?action=edit&amp;section=7" title="Edit section: Personality and traits"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Rowle was very cruel and extremely reckless, as he cast several <a href="/wiki/Killing_Curse" title="Killing Curse">Killing Curses</a> indiscriminately during the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_the_Astronomy_Tower" title="Battle of the Astronomy Tower">Battle of the Astronomy Tower</a> which resulted in the death of fellow Death Eater <a href="/wiki/Gibbon" title="Gibbon">Gibbon</a>. He didn't hesitate to <a href="/wiki/Fire-making_spell" class="mw-redirect" title="Fire-making spell">burn</a> down <a href="/wiki/Rubeus_Hagrid%27s_cabin" class="mw-redirect" title="Rubeus Hagrid&#39;s cabin">Rubeus Hagrid's cabin</a> while <a href="/wiki/Fang" title="Fang">Fang</a> was still inside, whether to distract Hagrid or simply out of sheer cruelty. He also seemed to be highly disrespectful and impatient as shown when he waved away the <a href="/wiki/Waitress_at_Luchino_Caffe" title="Waitress at Luchino Caffe">waitress</a> when she arrived to take his and Dolohov's order, though this may be more due to the fact that she was a <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggle</a> and that they were undercover.<sup id="cite_ref-DH9_4-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH9-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>He apparently had little regard for his comrades as he did not seem to care about accidentally killing Gibbon, though he may have gotten along with other Death Eaters such as <a href="/wiki/Antonin_Dolohov" title="Antonin Dolohov">Antonin Dolohov</a>. He showed no reluctance or resentment towards <a href="/wiki/Severus_Snape" title="Severus Snape">Severus Snape</a> when ordered to flee rather to continue <a href="/wiki/Cruciatus_Curse" title="Cruciatus Curse">torturing</a> <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>, showing a willingness to take orders from higher ranking comrades.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP28_2-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP28-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>As a Death Eater and a member of the pure-blooded <a href="/wiki/Rowle_family" title="Rowle family">Rowle family</a>, he likely held Muggles, Muggle borns, and blood traitors with intense scorn and hatred.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Magical_abilities_and_skills">Magical abilities and skills</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Thorfinn_Rowle?veaction=edit&amp;section=8" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Thorfinn_Rowle?action=edit&amp;section=8" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<figure class="thumb tright show-info-icon" style="width: 182px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/c/c3/Rowleee.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20100924152013" class="image"><img alt="" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw%3D%3D" decoding="async" width="180" height="272" class="thumbimage lazyload" data-image-name="Rowleee.jpg" data-image-key="Rowleee.jpg" data-src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/c/c3/Rowleee.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/180?cb=20100924152013" /></a> 	<noscript><a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/c/c3/Rowleee.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20100924152013" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/c/c3/Rowleee.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/180?cb=20100924152013" decoding="async" width="180" height="272" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="Rowleee.jpg" data-image-key="Rowleee.jpg" data-src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/c/c3/Rowleee.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/180?cb=20100924152013" /></a></noscript> 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:Rowleee.jpg" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption">Rowle battling at the Wedding of Bill and Fleur</p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure>
<ul><li><b><a href="/wiki/Duelling" title="Duelling">Duelling</a></b>: Rowle was a highly proficient and aggressive duellist, as he was able to hold his own against <a href="/wiki/Nymphadora_Tonks" title="Nymphadora Tonks">Nymphadora Tonks</a> during the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_the_Astronomy_Tower" title="Battle of the Astronomy Tower">Battle of the Astronomy Tower</a> until <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> hit him in the face with a hex. He seemed to generally cause the most damage during the battle and held several <a href="/wiki/Order_of_the_Phoenix" title="Order of the Phoenix">Order of the Phoenix</a> and <a href="/wiki/Dumbledore%27s_Army" title="Dumbledore&#39;s Army">Dumbledore's Army</a> members at bay while his fellows cornered a weakened Albus Dumbledore and managed to survive the battle mostly unscathed.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP28_2-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP28-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> He also fought against <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>, <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ronald Weasley</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione Granger</a>, but was quickly defeated by Harry, who was under his Invisibility Cloak. He later fought with his fellow Death Eaters in the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-DH34_5-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH34-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> though his actions during the battle are mostly unknown.</li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Dark_Arts" title="Dark Arts">Dark Arts</a></b>: Rowle was able to cast the <a href="/wiki/Unforgivable_Curses" title="Unforgivable Curses">Unforgivable Curses</a> (which require ability and genuine malice to perform); he used the <a href="/wiki/Killing_Curse" title="Killing Curse">Killing</a> and <a href="/wiki/Cruciatus_Curse" title="Cruciatus Curse">Cruciatus Curses</a> during the Battle of the Astronomy Tower in 1997. He also fired a destructive <a href="/wiki/Curse" title="Curse">curse</a> that was strong enough to cause heavy damage to the Hogwarts roof, and destroy the <a href="/wiki/Cursed_barrier" title="Cursed barrier">cursed barrier</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP28_2-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP28-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Charm" title="Charm">Charms</a></b>: Rowle set <a href="/wiki/Rubeus_Hagrid" title="Rubeus Hagrid">Hagrid's</a> hut on <a href="/wiki/Fire" title="Fire">fire</a> during the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_the_Astronomy_Tower" title="Battle of the Astronomy Tower">Battle of the Astronomy Tower</a> by use of the <a href="/wiki/Fire-Making_Spell" title="Fire-Making Spell">Fire-Making Spell</a>. The spell was powerful, as it was enough to set the whole hut ablaze and caused an "explosive bang".<sup id="cite_ref-HBP28_2-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP28-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> He was also able to cast the <a href="/wiki/Silencing_Charm" title="Silencing Charm">Silencing Charm</a> in <a href="/wiki/1998" title="1998">1998</a> during the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a> on Hagrid with success despite the latter being a half-giant.<sup id="cite_ref-DH34_5-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH34-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Nonverbal_spell" title="Nonverbal spell">Nonverbal magic</a></b>: Rowle was able to cast the <a href="/wiki/Silencing_Charm" title="Silencing Charm">Silencing Charm</a> on Hagrid without speaking the <a href="/wiki/Incantation" title="Incantation">incantation</a> during the Battle of Hogwarts,<sup id="cite_ref-DH34_5-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH34-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> and was able to fire multiple non-verbal spells at Tonks during their duel in the Battle of the Astronomy Tower, and later at Hagrid during his escape.</li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Apparition" title="Apparition">Apparition</a></b>: Being an accomplished adult wizard, Rowle was capable of Apparition. Rowle was able to disapparate from beyond Hogwarts grounds following the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_the_Astronomy_Tower" title="Battle of the Astronomy Tower">Battle of the Astronomy Tower</a>.</li></ul>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Thorfinn_Rowle?veaction=edit&amp;section=9" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Thorfinn_Rowle?action=edit&amp;section=9" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p><i>Thorfinn</i> is a Norse name, and may refer to Thorfinn the Mighty, an eleventh-century earl of Orkney. In the <i>Orkneyinga Saga</i>, he is described as "unusually tall and strong...a forceful man, greedy for fame and fortune. He did well in battle, for he was both a good tactician and full of courage."<sup id="cite_ref-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup> It may also link with Thor, the Viking god of thunder, and a mighty warrior of massive physical stature.

<ul><li>Thorfinn Rowle is portrayed by <a href="/wiki/Great_Britain" title="Great Britain">British</a> actor <a href="/wiki/Rod_Hunt" title="Rod Hunt">Rod Hunt</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Half-Blood_Prince_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince (film)">film adaptation</a> of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Half-Blood_Prince" title="Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince">Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince</a></i> and in the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Deathly_Hallows_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows (film)">film adaptations</a> of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Deathly_Hallows" title="Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows">Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows</a></i>.</li>
<li>In the book <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Half-Blood_Prince" title="Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince">Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince</a></i> he is never named, being only referred to as "The Big Blond Death Eater" or some variation. He is not properly named until the next book, <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Deathly_Hallows" title="Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows">Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows</a></i>​​, when <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ron Weasley</a> recognises him from old wanted posters.</li>
<li>In the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Half-Blood_Prince_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince (film)">film adaptation</a> of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Half-Blood_Prince" title="Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince">Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince</a></i>, Rowle is present for <a href="/wiki/Albus_Dumbledore" title="Albus Dumbledore">Albus Dumbledore</a>'s death at the hands of <a href="/wiki/Severus_Snape" title="Severus Snape">Severus Snape</a>. This differs from the novel where he remained behind to fight the Order of the Phoenix and Dumbledore's army members in order to prevent them from interfering.</li>
<li>Also in the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Half-Blood_Prince_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince (film)">film adaptation</a> of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Half-Blood_Prince" title="Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince">Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince</a></i>, Rowle's role in the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_the_Astronomy_Tower" title="Battle of the Astronomy Tower">Battle of the Astronomy Tower</a> is handed over to <a href="/wiki/Bellatrix_Lestrange" title="Bellatrix Lestrange">Bellatrix Lestrange</a>, during which she burns down <a href="/wiki/Rubeus_Hagrid" title="Rubeus Hagrid">Hagrid</a>'s hut instead of Rowle, as he does in the book.</li>
<li>In the book of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Deathly_Hallows" title="Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows">Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows</a></i>, Rowle is the <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eater</a> to shout at <a href="/wiki/Rubeus_Hagrid" title="Rubeus Hagrid">Hagrid</a>, however, in <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Deathly_Hallows:_Part_2" title="Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 2">Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 2</a></i>, an <a href="/wiki/Unidentified_male_Death_Eater_during_the_Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Unidentified male Death Eater during the Battle of Hogwarts">unidentified male Death Eater</a> does, possibly because Rowle's character is not as big as in the books—which is strange, considering Rowle has been in the films before and this character randomly showed up for this scene.</li>
<li>Since he was featured on old wanted posters, it's likely that he was imprisoned in <a href="/wiki/Azkaban" title="Azkaban">Azkaban</a> following Voldemort's first defeat. If so this is ironic as his possible ancestor <a href="/wiki/Damocles_Rowle" title="Damocles Rowle">Damocles Rowle</a> was the one to establish Azkaban as a prison.</li></ul>
