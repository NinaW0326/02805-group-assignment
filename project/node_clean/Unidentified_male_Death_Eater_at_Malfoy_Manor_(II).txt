<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_male_Death_Eater_at_Malfoy_Manor_(II)?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_male_Death_Eater_at_Malfoy_Manor_(II)?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>He was one of the many Death Eaters who were present at the <a href="/wiki/Meeting_at_Malfoy_Manor" title="Meeting at Malfoy Manor">meeting</a> in <a href="/wiki/Malfoy_Manor" title="Malfoy Manor">Malfoy Manor</a>. He was sitting next to <a href="/wiki/Corban_Yaxley" title="Corban Yaxley">Corban Yaxley</a> and appeared to be the youngest of the Death Eaters present with the exception of <a href="/wiki/Draco_Malfoy" title="Draco Malfoy">Draco Malfoy</a>. When <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a> asked one of his followers to hand over their <a href="/wiki/Wand" title="Wand">wand</a>, he remained silent with his head down, in fear of his master's deeply malign and terrifying presence.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>He later fought during the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a> on <a href="/wiki/2_May" title="2 May">2 May</a>, <a href="/wiki/1998" title="1998">1998</a>. He survived the first round, and after <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> <a href="/wiki/Horcrux" title="Horcrux">sacrificed himself</a> to <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Forbidden_Forest" title="Forbidden Forest">Forbidden Forest</a>, he guarded the captured <a href="/wiki/Rubeus_Hagrid" title="Rubeus Hagrid">Rubeus Hagrid</a> back to the castle along with other Death Eaters, while Hagrid carried Harry's believed dead body.
</p><p>After Harry revealed that he was actually alive, is possible that he fled the battle, along with other <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a>. It is also highly possible that he was killed during the final stages of the battle, or arrested and sent to <a href="/wiki/Azkaban" title="Azkaban">Azkaban</a> for his crimes as a Death Eater.<sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
