<p>This <b>dummy</b> is a <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magical</a> female doll in a dress that sits at the window of <a href="/wiki/Purge_and_Dowse,_Ltd" title="Purge and Dowse, Ltd">Purge and Dowse, Ltd</a>, which leads to <a href="/wiki/St_Mungo%27s_Hospital_for_Magical_Maladies_and_Injuries" title="St Mungo&#39;s Hospital for Magical Maladies and Injuries">St Mungo's Hospital for Magical Maladies and Injuries</a>. To enter the hospital, a witch or wizard must declare to the dummy their reason for visitation. <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> thought it looked particularly ugly.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP22_1-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP22-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
