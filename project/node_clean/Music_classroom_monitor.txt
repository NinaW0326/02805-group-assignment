<p><b>Music classroom monitor</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Ravenclaw" title="Ravenclaw">Ravenclaw</a> student at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School</a> during the <a href="/wiki/1990s" title="1990s">1990s</a>.
</p><p>He attended <a href="/wiki/Music" title="Music">Music</a> classes and worked in the capacity of classroom monitor, in order to assist the <a href="/wiki/Unidentified_1992_-_1993_Music_teacher" title="Unidentified 1992 - 1993 Music teacher">professor</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-CS_GBC_4-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-CS_GBC-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="1992">1992</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Music_classroom_monitor?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: 1992">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Music_classroom_monitor?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: 1992"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>When <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> gained possession of a harmonica during his second year, he thought he should give it to the <a href="/wiki/Music" title="Music">Music</a> <a href="/wiki/Unidentified_1992_-_1993_Music_teacher" title="Unidentified 1992 - 1993 Music teacher">Professor</a>. Finding out he was absent from his <a href="/wiki/Music_Classroom" title="Music Classroom">classroom</a>, he gave it to his assistant. In exchange for the harmonica, in return, the classroom monitor gave Harry a magic whistle.<sup id="cite_ref-CS_GBC_4-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-CS_GBC-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
