<p>On <a href="/wiki/31_July" title="31 July">31 July</a>, <a href="/wiki/1991" title="1991">1991</a>, <a href="/wiki/Rubeus_Hagrid" title="Rubeus Hagrid">Rubeus Hagrid</a> took <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> to Diagon Alley for the first time to purchase school supplies for his <a href="/wiki/First_year" title="First year">first year</a> at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a>. Harry looked around the Apothecary while Hagrid dealt with the man behind the counter.<sup id="cite_ref-PS5_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PS5-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
