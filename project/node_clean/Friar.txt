<p>The <b>Friar</b> (fl. Middle Ages) was from a medieval <a href="/wiki/Worcestershire" title="Worcestershire">Worcestershire</a> Monastery during the <a href="/wiki/Middle_Ages" title="Middle Ages">Middle Ages</a>. When <a href="/wiki/Benedict" title="Benedict">Brother Benedict</a> was bitten in the nose by a "talking <a href="/wiki/Ferret" title="Ferret">Ferret</a>" (actually a <a href="/wiki/Jarvey" title="Jarvey">Jarvey</a>), the Friar did not believe him, and asked him if he had been drinking <a href="/wiki/Boniface" title="Boniface">Brother Boniface</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Turnip_Wine" title="Turnip Wine">Turnip Wine</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
