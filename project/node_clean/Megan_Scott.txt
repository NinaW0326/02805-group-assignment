<b>Megan Scott</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Great_Britain" title="Great Britain">British</a> <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggle</a> that lived in <a href="/wiki/Yorkshire" title="Yorkshire">Yorkshire</a>.

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Megan_Scott?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Megan_Scott?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>She was found unconscious in a <a href="/wiki/Yorkshire" title="Yorkshire">Yorkshire</a> graveyard by <a href="/wiki/Mathilda_Grimblehawk" title="Mathilda Grimblehawk">Mathilda Grimblehawk</a> and <a href="/wiki/Mathilda_Grimblehawk%27s_partner" title="Mathilda Grimblehawk&#39;s partner">her partner</a> after she was attacked.<sup id="cite_ref-FBC9-1_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-FBC9-1-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/Healer" title="Healer">Healer</a> <a href="/wiki/Omar_Abasi" title="Omar Abasi">Omar Abasi</a> was able to mend her wounds and she explained that <a class="new" title="Catherine Tyler (page does not exist)" rel="nofollow" data-uncrawlable-url="L3dpa2kvQ2F0aGVyaW5lX1R5bGVyP2FjdGlvbj1lZGl0JnJlZGxpbms9MQ==">her grandmother</a>, who was recently deceased, had given her a map of the area and told to 'seek her past' and that '<a href="/wiki/Horatio_Cloke" title="Horatio Cloke">Cloke</a> knows the truth'.<sup id="cite_ref-FBC9-1_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-FBC9-1-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p><a href="/wiki/Jacturus_Cloke_III" title="Jacturus Cloke III">Jacturus Cloke III</a> later attacked her and cast the <a href="/wiki/Incarcerous_Spell" title="Incarcerous Spell">Incarcerous Spell</a>, tying her up.<sup id="cite_ref-FBC9-3_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-FBC9-3-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> Mathilda Grimblehawk and her partner were able to set her free, but because of everything she had seen, they ultimately had to <a href="/wiki/Memory_Charm" title="Memory Charm">Obliviate</a> her. They delivered to her a chest full of gold and jewels that <a href="/wiki/Horatio_Cloke" title="Horatio Cloke">Horatio Cloke</a> had stashed away when planning to elope with her grandmother.<sup id="cite_ref-FBC9-4_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-FBC9-4-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
