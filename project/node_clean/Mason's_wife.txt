<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Mason%27s_wife?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Mason%27s_wife?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>On <a href="/wiki/31_July" title="31 July">31 July</a>, <a href="/wiki/1992" title="1992">1992</a>, the couple were dinner guests at the <a href="/wiki/4_Privet_Drive" title="4 Privet Drive">Dursley home</a>. Their evening was nearly ruined when the dessert <a href="/wiki/Petunia_Dursley" title="Petunia Dursley">Petunia Dursley</a> had made crashed to the floor (an incident blamed on <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> though it was <a href="/wiki/Dobby" title="Dobby">Dobby</a>'s fault), which they were extremely shocked at. The evening could have been rescued were it not for the barn <a href="/wiki/Owl" title="Owl">owl</a>, that flew through the open window to deliver a letter of warning of a breach of the <a href="/wiki/Decree_for_the_Reasonable_Restriction_of_Underage_Sorcery" title="Decree for the Reasonable Restriction of Underage Sorcery">Decree for the Reasonable Restriction of Underage Sorcery</a> for Harry. The owl dropped the letter on Mrs Mason's head, and due to her extreme fear of <a href="/wiki/Bird" title="Bird">birds</a>, she “screamed like a <a href="/wiki/Banshee" title="Banshee">banshee</a>” and ran from the house shouting something about lunatics, followed quickly by her husband.<sup id="cite_ref-COS2_1-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COS2-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Mason%27s_wife?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Mason%27s_wife?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<figure class="thumb tright show-info-icon" style="width: 182px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/b/b0/Mrs_Mason.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20180613190627" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/b/b0/Mrs_Mason.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/180?cb=20180613190627" decoding="async" width="180" height="75" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="Mrs Mason.jpg" data-image-key="Mrs_Mason.jpg" /></a> 	 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:Mrs_Mason.jpg" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption">Mrs Mason after Dobby dropped the pudding on her head</p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure>
<ul><li>Mrs Mason was portrayed by <a href="/wiki/Veronica_Clifford" title="Veronica Clifford">Veronica Clifford</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Chamber_of_Secrets_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets (film)">film adaptation</a> of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Chamber_of_Secrets" title="Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets">Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets</a></i>.</li>
<li>In the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Chamber_of_Secrets_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets (film)">film adaptation</a> of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Chamber_of_Secrets" title="Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets">Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets</a></i>, the dessert, levitated by <a href="/wiki/Dobby" title="Dobby">Dobby</a>, crashed down on Mrs Mason's head, ruining the dinner party and causing <a href="/wiki/Vernon_Dursley" title="Vernon Dursley">Vernon Dursley</a> to lock Harry in his bedroom.</li>
<li>In <i><a href="/wiki/LEGO_Harry_Potter:_Years_1-4" title="LEGO Harry Potter: Years 1-4">LEGO Harry Potter: Years 1-4</a></i>, <a href="/wiki/Dudley_Dursley" title="Dudley Dursley">Dudley</a> eats the <a href="/wiki/Pudding" title="Pudding">pudding</a> off Mrs Mason's head.</li>
<li>In a deleted scene, when <a href="/wiki/Petunia_Dursley" title="Petunia Dursley">Petunia Dursley</a> says to Mrs Mason that she had a nice dress, and she responds that all her dresses were tailor-made. This was her only line in the film, but was cut from the theatre version.</li></ul>
