<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Carrow_(1920s)?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Carrow_(1920s)?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Carrow became an ardent follower of <a href="/wiki/Gellert_Grindelwald" title="Gellert Grindelwald">Gellert Grindelwald</a> at some point prior to <a href="/wiki/1927" title="1927">1927</a> and became one of his <a href="/wiki/Gellert_Grindelwald%27s_army" class="mw-redirect" title="Gellert Grindelwald&#39;s army">acolytes</a>. She accompanied Grindelwald to <a href="/wiki/Paris" title="Paris">Paris</a> and she took part along with the other acolytes in murdering a <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Non-magique</a> <a href="/wiki/Unidentified_Non-magique_Father" title="Unidentified Non-magique Father">man</a> and <a href="/wiki/Unidentified_Non-magique_Mother" title="Unidentified Non-magique Mother">woman</a>, along with their <a href="/wiki/Unidentified_Non-magique_toddler" title="Unidentified Non-magique toddler">infant son</a>, whom she murdered personally on Grindelwald's orders with the <a href="/wiki/Killing_Curse" title="Killing Curse">Killing Curse</a>. After murdering the family, they used their home as a temporary headquarters and safe house as they searched for <a href="/wiki/Credence_Barebone" title="Credence Barebone">Credence Barebone</a>. She attended Grindelwald's rally at the <a href="/wiki/Cimeti%C3%A8re_du_P%C3%A8re-Lachaise" title="Cimetière du Père-Lachaise">Cimetière du Père-Lachaise</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Lestrange_Mausoleum" title="Lestrange Mausoleum">Lestrange Mausoleum</a> and <a href="/wiki/Apparition" title="Apparition">disapparated</a> away after crossing the <a href="/wiki/Protego_Diabolica" title="Protego Diabolica">enchanted fire</a> that tested one's loyalty following the entrance of <a href="/wiki/Auror" title="Auror">Aurors</a> and did not participate in the subsequent <a href="/wiki/Gellert_Grindelwald%27s_Paris_rally" title="Gellert Grindelwald&#39;s Paris rally">battle</a> after that.<sup id="cite_ref-COGS_4-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COGS-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Personality_and_traits">Personality and traits</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Carrow_(1920s)?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Personality and traits">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Carrow_(1920s)?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Personality and traits"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<figure class="thumb tleft show-info-icon" style="width: 252px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/d/d4/Carrow_kills_child.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20190325020729" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/d/d4/Carrow_kills_child.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/250?cb=20190325020729" decoding="async" width="250" height="104" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="Carrow kills child.jpg" data-image-key="Carrow_kills_child.jpg" /></a> 	 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:Carrow_kills_child.jpg" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption">Carrow seconds before killing a child</p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure>
<p>Carrow was a dedicated follower of the Dark wizard Gellert Grindelwald and his beliefs in <a href="/wiki/Wizarding_supremacy" title="Wizarding supremacy">Wizarding supremacy</a>. She was obedient to her master, and was even willing to commit the murder of an innocent child when instructed to do so. When she and several other acolytes seized control of a Parisian home belonging to a non-magical family the task of murdering the infant boy fell to Carrow. No verbal command was spoken, but she deduced Grindelwald's desire, and did not flinch from the task; executing the child swiftly with little to no display of emotion.<sup id="cite_ref-COGS_4-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COGS-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Magical_abilities_and_skills">Magical abilities and skills</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Carrow_(1920s)?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Carrow_(1920s)?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li><b><a href="/wiki/Dark_Arts" title="Dark Arts">Dark Arts</a></b>: Carrow was proficient with dark magic, as she was able to cast the <a href="/wiki/Killing_Curse" title="Killing Curse">Killing Curse</a> on a <a href="/wiki/Unidentified_Non-magique_toddler" title="Unidentified Non-magique toddler">male infant</a>, an <a href="/wiki/Unforgivable_Curses" title="Unforgivable Curses">Unforgivable Curse</a> which requires significant <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magical</a> ability to perform as well as genuine malice.<sup id="cite_ref-COGS_4-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COGS-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Apparition" title="Apparition">Apparition</a></b>: Carrow as accomplished in apparition, as she was able to apparate successfully away from the <a href="/wiki/Lestrange_Mausoleum" title="Lestrange Mausoleum">Lestrange Mausoleum</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-COGS_4-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COGS-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup></li></ul>
