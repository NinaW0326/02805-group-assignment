<p><b>Luna Scamander</b><sup id="cite_ref-lastname_16-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-lastname-16">&#91;16&#93;</a></sup><b> </b>(n√©e <b>Lovegood</b>)<b> </b>(b. <a href="/wiki/13_February" title="13 February">13 February</a>, <a href="/wiki/1981" title="1981">1981</a><sup id="cite_ref-JKR-twitter-birthday_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-JKR-twitter-birthday-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>) was a <a href="/wiki/Great_Britain" title="Great Britain">British</a> <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch</a>, the only child and daughter of <a href="/wiki/Xenophilius_Lovegood" title="Xenophilius Lovegood">Xenophilius</a> and <a href="/wiki/Pandora_Lovegood" title="Pandora Lovegood">Pandora Lovegood</a>. Her mother accidentally <a href="/wiki/Death" title="Death">died</a> while experimenting with <a href="/wiki/Spell" title="Spell">spells</a> when Luna was nine and Luna was raised by her father, editor of the magazine <i><a href="/wiki/The_Quibbler" title="The Quibbler">The Quibbler</a></i>, in a <a href="/wiki/Lovegood_House" title="Lovegood House">rook-like house</a> near the village of <a href="/wiki/Ottery_St_Catchpole" title="Ottery St Catchpole">Ottery St Catchpole</a> in <a href="/wiki/Devon" title="Devon">Devon</a>. 
</p><p>Luna attended <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> from <a href="/wiki/1992" title="1992">1992</a>-<a href="/wiki/1999" title="1999">1999</a> and was sorted into <a href="/wiki/Ravenclaw" title="Ravenclaw">Ravenclaw House</a>. In her fourth year, Luna joined <a href="/wiki/Dumbledore%27s_Army" title="Dumbledore&#39;s Army">Dumbledore's Army</a>, an organisation taught and led by <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>, of which she became an important member. She participated in the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_the_Department_of_Mysteries" title="Battle of the Department of Mysteries">Battle of the Department of Mysteries</a> (<a href="/wiki/1996" title="1996">1996</a>) and the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_the_Astronomy_Tower" title="Battle of the Astronomy Tower">Battle of the Astronomy Tower</a> (<a href="/wiki/1997" title="1997">1997</a>), and co-led the reconstituted <a href="/wiki/Dumbledore%27s_Army" title="Dumbledore&#39;s Army">Dumbledore's Army</a> when <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts</a> fell under the control of <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a> with Severus Snape as headmaster. 
</p><p>Because of her father's political dissidence at the time, Luna was abducted by <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a> to be held ransom, and imprisoned in the dungeons of <a href="/wiki/Malfoy_Manor" title="Malfoy Manor">Malfoy Manor</a> for months. She was <a href="/wiki/Skirmish_at_Malfoy_Manor" title="Skirmish at Malfoy Manor">freed</a> by <a href="/wiki/Dobby" title="Dobby">Dobby</a> along with several other prisoners in the spring of <a href="/wiki/1998" title="1998">1998</a>, and stayed at <a href="/wiki/Shell_Cottage" title="Shell Cottage">Shell Cottage</a> until she returned to Hogwarts to participate in the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">final battle</a> of the <a href="/wiki/Second_Wizarding_War" title="Second Wizarding War">Second Wizarding War</a>.
</p><p>After the war, Luna became a <a href="/wiki/Magizoologist" title="Magizoologist">Magizoologist</a> (Studies magical creatures) discovering and classifying many magical species that had never been encountered before. She eventually married <a href="/wiki/Rolf_Scamander" title="Rolf Scamander">Rolf Scamander</a>, the grandson of <a href="/wiki/Newton_Scamander" title="Newton Scamander">Newt Scamander</a>, a famed Magizoologist, with whom she had twin sons, <a href="/wiki/Lorcan_and_Lysander" title="Lorcan and Lysander">Lorcan and Lysander</a>. Luna's good friends <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry</a> and <a href="/wiki/Ginevra_Weasley" title="Ginevra Weasley">Ginny Potter</a> also named their daughter and third child, <a href="/wiki/Lily_L._Potter" title="Lily L. Potter">Lily Luna Potter</a>, in honour of her.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Luna_Lovegood?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Luna_Lovegood?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span id="Early_life_(1981-1992)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_life_.281981-1992.29">Early life <span class="template-c">(1981-1992)</span></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Luna_Lovegood?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Early life (1981-1992)">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Luna_Lovegood?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Early life (1981-1992)"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Luna was born on <a href="/wiki/13_February" title="13 February">13 February</a>, <a href="/wiki/1981" title="1981">1981</a>, the only child of <a href="/wiki/Xenophilius_Lovegood" title="Xenophilius Lovegood">Xenophilius</a> and <a href="/wiki/Pandora_Lovegood" title="Pandora Lovegood">Pandora Lovegood</a>. Luna's mother was accidentally killed while experimenting with a spell that she created when Luna was nine years old. Thus, Luna was subsequently raised by her father. As she witnessed her mother's death, she was able to see <a href="/wiki/Thestral" title="Thestral">Thestrals</a>, the magical skeletal black horses that led the Hogwarts carriages, from that point onward.<sup id="cite_ref-OTP_5-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OTP-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span id="Hogwarts_years_(1992-1998)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="Hogwarts_years_.281992-1998.29">Hogwarts years <span class="template-c">(1992-1998)</span></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Luna_Lovegood?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Hogwarts years (1992-1998)">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Luna_Lovegood?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Hogwarts years (1992-1998)"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_years">Early years</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Luna_Lovegood?veaction=edit&amp;section=4" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Early years">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Luna_Lovegood?action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Early years"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<div class="quote">
<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix">"<i>Some people call me 'Loony' Lovegood, actually.</i>"</span></dd>
<dd>&#8212;Hogwarts' students view of Luna<sup class="noprint"><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Order_of_the_Phoenix" title="Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix">[src]</a></sup></dd>
<dd></dd></dl></div><p>Luna attended <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> in <a href="/wiki/1992" title="1992">1992</a> and was sorted into <a href="/wiki/Ravenclaw" title="Ravenclaw">Ravenclaw House</a>. One year behind the famous Harry Potter, she noticed <a href="/wiki/Thestral" title="Thestral">Thestrals</a> on her very first day of school, <a href="/wiki/1_September" title="1 September">1 September</a> <a href="/wiki/1992" title="1992">1992</a>.
</p><p>During her years at Hogwarts, she was often teased by many of the other students due to her strange behaviour and odd beliefs. Some time during her early years at Hogwarts students had also taken to calling her "Loony Lovegood". Luna also met and became a friend of <a href="/wiki/Ginevra_Weasley" title="Ginevra Weasley">Ginny Weasley</a>, who was in the same year as her. In the summer of <a href="/wiki/1994" title="1994">1994</a>, she went to the <a href="/wiki/Quidditch_World_Cup" title="Quidditch World Cup">Quidditch World Cup</a> with her father.<sup id="cite_ref-GOF8_17-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF8-17">&#91;17&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>In her <a href="/wiki/Third_year" title="Third year">third year</a> at Hogwarts, she chose <a href="/wiki/Divination_(class)" title="Divination (class)">Divination</a> as an elective, as in the <a href="/wiki/1996%E2%80%931997_school_year" title="1996‚Äì1997 school year">1996‚Äì1997 school year</a> <a href="/wiki/Sybill_Trelawney" title="Sybill Trelawney">Professor Trelawney</a> claimed to miss her in class; Luna explained she had <a href="/wiki/Firenze" title="Firenze">Firenze</a> that year.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP15_18-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP15-18">&#91;18&#93;</a></sup> She may also have attended <a href="/wiki/Study_of_Ancient_Runes" title="Study of Ancient Runes">Study of Ancient Runes</a>, as before the <a href="/wiki/1995%E2%80%931996_school_year" title="1995‚Äì1996 school year">1995‚Äì1996 school year</a>, she was seen reading an article written about runes in <i><a href="/wiki/The_Quibbler" title="The Quibbler">The Quibbler</a>.</i><sup id="cite_ref-OOTP10_7-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP10-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<p>In Luna's fourth year, she met and befriended <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>, <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ron Weasley</a>, <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione Granger</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Neville_Longbottom" title="Neville Longbottom">Neville Longbottom</a>. She believed Harry's declaration that <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a> was back. As the year progressed, she was invited to join an underground <a href="/wiki/Defence_Against_the_Dark_Arts" title="Defence Against the Dark Arts">Defence Against the Dark Arts</a> group called <a href="/wiki/Dumbledore%27s_Army" title="Dumbledore&#39;s Army">Dumbledore's Army</a>, which was taught and led by Harry Potter. This organisation was to teach practical defence. She eagerly accepted, working hard along with the others and enjoying their company. In an effort to make people believe Harry's story, Hermione arranged an interview for him with <a href="/wiki/Rita_Skeeter" title="Rita Skeeter">Rita Skeeter</a> at the <a href="/wiki/Three_Broomsticks_Inn" title="Three Broomsticks Inn">Three Broomsticks</a> ‚Äî Luna accompanied them so as to persuade her father to publish the interview in <i><a href="/wiki/The_Quibbler" title="The Quibbler">The Quibbler</a></i>. The copy sold out, every Hogwarts student and possibly every professor as well, read the copy because of the Education Decree that stated that it was banned on school grounds.<sup id="cite_ref-OTP_5-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OTP-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<p>Near the end of the year, Harry had a vision that his godfather, <a href="/wiki/Sirius_Black" title="Sirius Black">Sirius Black</a> was being tortured by Lord Voldemort in the Department of Mysteries. Luna and Ginny stood guard while Harry and Hermione snuck into <a href="/wiki/Dolores_Umbridge" title="Dolores Umbridge">Professor Umbridge</a>'s office, to use the fireplace to try to contact Sirius, while Ron attempted to distract Umbridge. However, they were caught, along with Neville Longbottom, by Umbridge and members of the <a href="/wiki/Inquisitorial_Squad" title="Inquisitorial Squad">Inquisitorial Squad</a>. They were restrained and gagged, but Luna hardly seemed bothered by this as she was staring, dreamily, through the window as if bored.<sup id="cite_ref-OTP_5-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OTP-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> 
</p><p>Harry and Hermione drew Umbridge away into the <a href="/wiki/Forbidden_Forest" title="Forbidden Forest">Forbidden Forest</a>, and Luna, Ginny, Ron, and Neville managed to escape from the Inquisitorial Squad using a variety of hexes and jinxes. When they all met up again in the forest, Harry planned to go to the <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">Ministry of Magic</a>. Luna reminded Harry about the <a href="/wiki/Thestral" title="Thestral">Thestrals</a> and suggested using them to get to the Ministry. She then mounted her Thestral side-saddle, "as though she did this every day." She also helped Ron, Hermione and Ginny mount their Thestrals, as the animals were invisible to them. The six D.A. members flew to the Ministry and entered the <a href="/wiki/Department_of_Mysteries" title="Department of Mysteries">Department of Mysteries</a>, where Luna heard voices coming from behind a <a href="/wiki/Veil" title="Veil">Veil</a>, just as Harry did. The group was soon ambushed by twelve <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-OTP_5-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OTP-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<p>Luna participated in the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_the_Department_of_Mysteries" title="Battle of the Department of Mysteries">battle</a> that ensued. She protected Ron, who had been hit with a <a href="/wiki/Confundus_Charm" title="Confundus Charm">spell</a> that made him highly confused. Luna also attacked a Death Eater who had seized Ginny's ankle with the <a href="/wiki/Reductor_Curse" title="Reductor Curse">Reductor Curse</a>, which destroyed a model of the planet Pluto. The model blew up in the Death Eater's face; however, the impact broke Ginny's ankle. Luna guided both the injured Ginny and the <a href="/wiki/Confundus_Charm" title="Confundus Charm">Confunded</a> Ron until they met up with Harry and Neville. Luna was one of the last D.A. members to fall, eventually being <a href="/wiki/Stunning_Spell" title="Stunning Spell">stunned</a> by a Death Eater and thrown across the room. She regained focus just before the end of the battle and survived relatively unscathed. At the end of the school year, Luna spoke to Harry, who was mourning the murder of his godfather and had found no comfort in talking to <a href="/wiki/Ghost" title="Ghost">ghosts</a>. Luna told him about <a href="/wiki/Pandora_Lovegood" title="Pandora Lovegood">her mother</a>'s death and her belief that she would see her again someday, which was a comforting thought to Harry.<sup id="cite_ref-OTP_5-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OTP-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>That summer, Luna and <a href="/wiki/Xenophilius_Lovegood" title="Xenophilius Lovegood">her father</a> went to <a href="/wiki/Sweden" title="Sweden">Sweden</a> using the money the <i><a href="/wiki/Daily_Prophet" title="Daily Prophet">Daily Prophet</a></i> paid them to reprint <i>The Quibbler'</i>s interview with Harry to see if they could catch a <a href="/wiki/Crumple-Horned_Snorkack" title="Crumple-Horned Snorkack">Crumple-Horned Snorkack</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-OTP_5-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OTP-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<p>This year was Luna's <a href="/wiki/Ordinary_Wizarding_Level" title="Ordinary Wizarding Level">O.W.L.</a> year. On her way to Hogwarts in the <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Express" title="Hogwarts Express">Hogwarts Express</a>, Luna shared a compartment with <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> and she was accompanied by <a href="/wiki/Neville_Longbottom" title="Neville Longbottom">Neville Longbottom</a>. Luna was holding another edition of the Quibbler, which she told Harry was going strong because of his interview. She then put on a brightly coloured pair of free "Spectrespecs" that were from inside the Quibbler. She was very disappointed to find out that the <a href="/wiki/Dumbledore%27s_Army" title="Dumbledore&#39;s Army">D.A.</a> would not continue that year. When <a href="/wiki/Romilda_Vane" title="Romilda Vane">Romilda Vane</a> asked Harry if he wanted to join her and her friends in another compartment, while treating Luna and Neville with disdain, Harry coldly rebuffed her, stating that they were his friends. Luna correctly observed that people expected Harry to associate with "cooler" people; he replied that she and Neville were cool, and among his closest and most trustworthy friends.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP_6-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Towards Christmas, Luna comforted <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione Granger</a> after <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ron Weasley</a> mocked her in <a href="/wiki/Transfiguration_(class)" title="Transfiguration (class)">Transfiguration</a> and Hermione ran into the girl's bathroom in tears. Luna had found Hermione crying and Harry had been looking for Hermione to return the books she left in Transfiguration class. After Hermione left, Harry asked Luna if she would like to go to Professor Slughorn's Christmas party with him, just as friends, and Luna excitedly accepted his invitation.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP_6-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup>

<p>Luna wore a set of spangled silver robes that was attracting a certain amount of giggling from the onlookers, but she otherwise looked quite nice. While there, she conversed with multiple people, and took an interest in <a href="/wiki/Sanguini" title="Sanguini">Sanguini</a>, a <a href="/wiki/Vampire" title="Vampire">vampire</a> guest. She also made Harry laugh so hard that mead came out of his nose when she discussed the <a href="/wiki/Rotfang_Conspiracy" title="Rotfang Conspiracy">Rotfang Conspiracy</a> after <a href="/wiki/Severus_Snape" title="Severus Snape">Professor Snape</a> sneered at Harry's ambition to become an <a href="/wiki/Auror" title="Auror">Auror</a>. She continued the discussion of the Rotfang Conspiracy with <a href="/wiki/Sybill_Trelawney" title="Sybill Trelawney">Professor Trelawney</a>, who seemed completely interested. Luna was also present when Draco Malfoy was caught out of bed after hours by Filch and dragged into the party to be punished.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP_6-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Also that year, Luna would temporarily replace <a href="/wiki/Zacharias_Smith" title="Zacharias Smith">Zacharias Smith</a> as <a href="/wiki/Quidditch" title="Quidditch">Quidditch</a> commentator for the school. Many found Luna's commentary amusing, as Luna forgot players' names and went off on tangents such as speculating that Smith, who was unable to maintain procession of the Quaffle for longer than a minute, was suffering from "<a href="/wiki/Loser%27s_Lurgy" title="Loser&#39;s Lurgy">Loser's Lurgy</a>", or saying how a cloud was interestingly shaped. She never actually attempted reported the score, and <a href="/wiki/Minerva_McGonagall" title="Minerva McGonagall">Professor McGonagall</a> had to report it. However, after Ron heard this commentary, which he found highly amusing, he said "<i>you know, Luna's grown on me,</i>" when previously he found her very strange and slightly off-putting.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP_6-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup>

<p>Luna once gave Harry a note from <a href="/wiki/Albus_Dumbledore" title="Albus Dumbledore">Albus Dumbledore</a> telling him to come to his office for a private lesson. At the end of the school year, the castle came under attack by <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a>, and Luna was one of the few D.A. members to participate in the ensuing <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_the_Astronomy_Tower" title="Battle of the Astronomy Tower">Battle of the Astronomy Tower</a>, along with Neville, <a href="/wiki/Ginevra_Weasley" title="Ginevra Weasley">Ginny</a>, Ron, and Hermione. <p>It was believed that Luna was held captive at <a href="/wiki/Azkaban" title="Azkaban">Azkaban</a>, though she was actually imprisoned in the basement of <a href="/wiki/Malfoy_Manor" title="Malfoy Manor">Malfoy Manor</a>, along with the famed <a href="/wiki/Wandmaker" title="Wandmaker">wandmaker</a> <a href="/wiki/Garrick_Ollivander" title="Garrick Ollivander">Garrick Ollivander</a>, to whom she proved a great comfort. Her father was so desperate to get her back that he tried to turn Harry, Hermione, and Ron over to the Ministry in exchange for her freedom; the trio escaped, but Hermione ensured that the Death Eaters <a href="/wiki/Xenophilius_Lovegood" title="Xenophilius Lovegood">Xenophilius</a> called glimpsed Harry as they left, so that neither he nor Luna would be punished. The <a href="/wiki/Lovegood_House" title="Lovegood House">Lovegoods' home</a> was largely destroyed with an erumpent horn, which Xenophilius had believed to be a <a href="/wiki/Crumple-Horned_Snorkack" title="Crumple-Horned Snorkack">Crumple-Horned Snorkack</a>'s, blew up inside. After the failed ambush Xeno was taken to Azkaban prison for the remainder of the war.<sup id="cite_ref-DH_12-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH-12">&#91;12&#93;</a></sup>

<p>The prisoners in Malfoy Manor were soon joined by Harry, Ron, <a href="/wiki/Dean_Thomas" title="Dean Thomas">Dean Thomas</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Griphook" title="Griphook">Griphook</a> the goblin, while Hermione was <a href="/wiki/Cruciatus_Curse" title="Cruciatus Curse">tortured</a> for information upstairs by <a href="/wiki/Bellatrix_Lestrange" title="Bellatrix Lestrange">Bellatrix Lestrange</a>. After Luna helped cut the ropes off their new cell-mates, <a href="/wiki/Dobby" title="Dobby">Dobby</a> the <a href="/wiki/House-elf" title="House-elf">house-elf</a> came to free them. He took Luna, Dean and Ollivander to <a href="/wiki/Shell_Cottage" title="Shell Cottage">Shell Cottage</a>, the home of <a href="/wiki/William_Weasley" title="William Weasley">Bill</a> and <a href="/wiki/Fleur_Delacour" title="Fleur Delacour">Fleur Weasley</a>, and then returned to assist Harry, Hermione, Ron and Griphook in escaping the Manor. He died in rescuing them.<sup id="cite_ref-DH_12-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH-12">&#91;12&#93;</a></sup> 
</p><p>Up until the Battle of Hogwarts, Luna stayed at the cottage for the remainder of the war, and when Dobby was buried, she closed the house-elf's eyes and provided the eulogy, thanking him for rescuing her. During her stay, she practised using a new wand that Ollivander had made for her. She also seemed to have started a friendship with Dean. It is unknown what Luna did after Harry, Ron and Hermione left Shell Cottage in order to <a href="/wiki/Break-in_of_Gringotts_Wizarding_Bank_(1998)" title="Break-in of Gringotts Wizarding Bank (1998)">break into</a> <a href="/wiki/Gringotts_Wizarding_Bank" title="Gringotts Wizarding Bank">Gringotts Wizarding Bank</a>, but she returned to <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts</a> in May when Neville signalled her as to the trio's return. <p>After leaving school, Luna became a famous <a href="/wiki/Magizoologist" title="Magizoologist">wizarding naturalist</a>, travelling the world in search of strange creatures.<sup id="cite_ref-blc_14-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-blc-14">&#91;14&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-msnbc_15-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-msnbc-15">&#91;15&#93;</a></sup> She discovered and classified many species that had never been encountered before, but was never able to find the <a href="/wiki/Crumple-Horned_Snorkack" title="Crumple-Horned Snorkack">Crumple-Horned Snorkack</a>. Thus, she had to come to terms with the fact that there are some things that simply do not exist.<sup id="cite_ref-blc_14-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-blc-14">&#91;14&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-msnbc_15-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-msnbc-15">&#91;15&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Luna married fellow naturalist <a href="/wiki/Rolf_Scamander" title="Rolf Scamander">Rolf Scamander</a>, grandson of <i><a href="/wiki/Fantastic_Beasts_and_Where_to_Find_Them" title="Fantastic Beasts and Where to Find Them">Fantastic Beasts and Where to Find Them</a></i> author <a href="/wiki/Newton_Scamander" title="Newton Scamander">Newt Scamander</a>, considerably later in life than Harry, Ron, Hermione, and Ginny, who all married and started families in their early-to-mid twenties.<sup id="cite_ref-blc_14-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-blc-14">&#91;14&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-WFT_19-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-WFT-19">&#91;19&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-yitl_20-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-yitl-20">&#91;20&#93;</a></sup> According to <a href="/wiki/Rita_Skeeter" title="Rita Skeeter">Rita Skeeter</a>, Luna wore a <a href="/wiki/Luna_Lovegood%27s_wedding_dress" title="Luna Lovegood&#39;s wedding dress">wedding dress</a> decorated with rainbows and spangles, and a tiara of silver unicorn horns, which her husband supposedly looked at with a shocked expression. This dress was voted ‚Äò<a href="/wiki/Most_Hideous_Outfit_of_the_Year" title="Most Hideous Outfit of the Year">Most Hideous Outfit of the Year</a>' by readers of <a href="/wiki/Rita_Skeeter" title="Rita Skeeter">Rita Skeeter</a>'s regular <i><a href="/wiki/Daily_Prophet" title="Daily Prophet">Daily Prophet</a></i> column. The couple also had twin sons named <a href="/wiki/Lorcan_Scamander" title="Lorcan Scamander">Lorcan</a> and <a href="/wiki/Lysander_Scamander" title="Lysander Scamander">Lysander</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-WFT_19-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-WFT-19">&#91;19&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-yitl_20-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-yitl-20">&#91;20&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Luna and Rolf attended the <a href="/wiki/2014_Quidditch_World_Cup" title="2014 Quidditch World Cup">2014 Quidditch World Cup</a> along with the rest of her friends in Dumbledore's Army. She wore robes made of the flags of all sixteen qualifying countries, but her twins were at home with their grandpa and did not come. At the <a href="/wiki/2014_Quidditch_World_Cup_final" title="2014 Quidditch World Cup final">2014 Quidditch World Cup final</a>, she sat in the VIP boxes with <a href="/wiki/Dumbledore%27s_Army" title="Dumbledore&#39;s Army">Dumbledore's Army</a> and her husband, where he held her hands, which might have been because he was trying to prevent his wife from putting on one of her famous <a href="/wiki/Special_Event_Hat" title="Special Event Hat">Special Event Hats</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-PMQuidditch_21-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PMQuidditch-21">&#91;21&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>In <a href="/wiki/2019" title="2019">2019</a>, she met <a href="/wiki/Magizoologist" title="Magizoologist">at least one</a> of the members of the recently created <a href="/wiki/Statute_of_Secrecy_Task_Force" title="Statute of Secrecy Task Force">Statute of Secrecy Task Force</a>, whom she dazzled with her knowledge about the many ways in which magical creatures protect themselves.<sup id="cite_ref-WU_22-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-WU-22">&#91;22&#93;</a></sup> Luna also instructed them on how she performed the <a href="/wiki/Stamina_Charm" title="Stamina Charm">Stamina Charm</a>, which instantly improved their casting both when used on themselves and teammates and allowed them to accompany her in her search for <a href="/wiki/Bowtruckle" title="Bowtruckle">Bowtruckles</a> and <a href="/wiki/Thunderbird" title="Thunderbird">Thunderbirds</a> to mentor them how to detect and look after them, improving their focus.
</p>

<p>In <a href="/wiki/1997" title="1997">1997</a>, sixteen-year-old Luna had waist-length, straggly, dirty blonde hair,<sup id="cite_ref-hair_8-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-hair-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup> protuberant silvery eyes,<sup id="cite_ref-eyes_9-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-eyes-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup> which often seemed to be dreamily distracted, and faint eyebrows. She had an aura of "distinct dottiness" about her,<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP10_7-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP10-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup> and was known to put <a href="/wiki/Luna_Lovegood%27s_wand" class="mw-redirect" title="Luna Lovegood&#39;s wand">her wand</a> behind her left ear for safekeeping.
</p><p>Luna had a unique fashion sense that other people usually found bizarre. She often wore odd pieces of jewellery such as a <a href="/wiki/Butterbeer" title="Butterbeer">Butterbeer</a> cork necklace and <a href="/wiki/Dirigible_plum_earrings" title="Dirigible plum earrings">Dirigible plum earrings</a>, and in 1996, a pair of <a href="/wiki/Spectrespecs" title="Spectrespecs">Spectrespecs</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP_6-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup> Luna also did not wear shoes because students in her house would take her shoes and hide them.<sup id="cite_ref-OTP_5-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OTP-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>At Hogwarts <a href="/wiki/Quidditch" title="Quidditch">Quidditch</a> matches, Luna supported <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor" title="Gryffindor">Gryffindor</a> against every House except <a href="/wiki/Ravenclaw" title="Ravenclaw">Ravenclaw</a>; to show her support for Gryffindor, she would wear a hat in the shape of a lion head that roars, and to show support for Ravenclaw she would wear an eagle hat with flapping wings.<sup id="cite_ref-OTP_5-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OTP-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> At <a href="/wiki/Horace_Slughorn" title="Horace Slughorn">Professor Slughorn</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Slug_Club_Christmas_party" title="Slug Club Christmas party">Christmas party</a> in 1996, she wore spangled silver robes.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP_6-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup> At <a href="/wiki/William_Weasley" title="William Weasley">Bill Weasley</a> and <a href="/wiki/Fleur_Delacour" title="Fleur Delacour">Fleur Delacour</a>'s wedding, she wore bright yellow robes because she was taught it brought luck to a wedding to wear sun colours and had a sunflower in her hair, as well.<sup id="cite_ref-DH_12-10" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH-12">&#91;12&#93;</a></sup> She was described as "pretty" on some occasions, despite her odd clothes.
</p>

<p>Luna was an extremely quirky girl, with a serene disposition and many eccentric beliefs and qualities. She was a complete nonconformist; she lacked self-consciousness and was not afraid to show who she truly was. Luna was also intelligent and unusually perceptive, having been sorted into <a href="/wiki/Ravenclaw" title="Ravenclaw">Ravenclaw</a><sup id="cite_ref-OTP_5-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OTP-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> <i>"where those of wit and learning will always find their kind.</i>" A prime example of Luna‚Äôs perceptiveness was when she pointed out the ironically drastic measures taken against <a href="/wiki/Sirius_Black" title="Sirius Black">Sirius Black</a> when he escaped Azkaban, but when ten Death Eaters (including <a href="/wiki/Bellatrix_Lestrange" title="Bellatrix Lestrange">Bellatrix Lestrange</a>) escaped, barely any measures were taken to recapture them.
</p>
<figure class="thumb tright show-info-icon" style="width: 144px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/b/ba/Luna_in_class.JPG/revision/latest?cb=20070516180455" class="image"><img alt="" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw%3D%3D" decoding="async" width="142" height="197" class="thumbimage lazyload" data-image-name="Luna in class.JPG" data-image-key="Luna_in_class.JPG" data-src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/b/ba/Luna_in_class.JPG/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/142?cb=20070516180455" /></a> 	<noscript><a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/b/ba/Luna_in_class.JPG/revision/latest?cb=20070516180455" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/b/ba/Luna_in_class.JPG/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/142?cb=20070516180455" decoding="async" width="142" height="197" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="Luna in class.JPG" data-image-key="Luna_in_class.JPG" data-src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/b/ba/Luna_in_class.JPG/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/142?cb=20070516180455" /></a></noscript> 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:Luna_in_class.JPG" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption">Luna introducing Harry to the Thestrals</p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure>
<p>Although many thought Luna was bizarre, Harry Potter, Hermione Granger, Ron Weasley, Neville Longbottom, Ginny Weasley, Dean Thomas and Ollivander eventually became very fond of her.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP_6-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Luna's demeanour and voice were often distant and dreamy; she would drift away from a conversation she did not find interesting. However, whenever someone made fun of her father or his magazine <i><a href="/wiki/The_Quibbler" title="The Quibbler">The Quibbler</a></i>, the dreamy quality in her voice would vanish and be replaced by one of steel, and she would immediately become very angry. She had an uncanny way of making others uncomfortable by being bluntly honest ‚Äî <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> described her as having a "<i>knack for embarrassing honesty</i>". She was also completely unflappable and rarely seemed anxious or stressed, even under difficult circumstances such as being a prisoner of <a href="/wiki/Malfoy_Manor" title="Malfoy Manor">Malfoy Manor</a>. Luna was very good at comforting others. She comforted Harry after <a href="/wiki/Sirius_Black" title="Sirius Black">his godfather</a>'s death<sup id="cite_ref-OTP_5-10" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OTP-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> and <a href="/wiki/Garrick_Ollivander" title="Garrick Ollivander">Mr Ollivander</a> while they were imprisoned together.<sup id="cite_ref-DH_12-11" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH-12">&#91;12&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<figure class="thumb tleft show-info-icon" style="width: 237px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/b/bf/Luna_and_Harry.gif/revision/latest?cb=20140307034001" class="image"><img alt="" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw%3D%3D" decoding="async" width="235" height="153" class="thumbimage lazyload" data-image-name="Luna and Harry.gif" data-image-key="Luna_and_Harry.gif" data-src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/b/bf/Luna_and_Harry.gif/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/235?cb=20140307034001" /></a> 	<noscript><a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/b/bf/Luna_and_Harry.gif/revision/latest?cb=20140307034001" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/b/bf/Luna_and_Harry.gif/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/235?cb=20140307034001" decoding="async" width="235" height="153" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="Luna and Harry.gif" data-image-key="Luna_and_Harry.gif" data-src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/b/bf/Luna_and_Harry.gif/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/235?cb=20140307034001" /></a></noscript> 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:Luna_and_Harry.gif" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption">Luna comforting Harry after the death of his godfather</p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure>
<p>Luna was often the subject of ridicule and had difficulty making friends. Other students gave her the nickname of "Loony Lovegood" and even stole and hid her things. She was unfazed by this, and instead chose to be nonjudgmental and accepting of those around her.<sup id="cite_ref-OTP_5-11" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OTP-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> She once said that she did not like dancing much, though she did dance some at the <a href="/wiki/Wedding_of_William_Weasley_and_Fleur_Delacour" title="Wedding of William Weasley and Fleur Delacour">wedding of Bill and Fleur</a>, along with her father. 
</p><p>Luna seemed to have enjoyed artistic pursuits in her free time; her house was filled with various things that she painted.<sup id="cite_ref-DH_12-12" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH-12">&#91;12&#93;</a></sup> She even painted very life-like portraits of her friends that seemed to breathe. Luna was very loyal to the few friends she had ‚Äî she published Rita Skeeter's interview of Harry Potter on <i>The Quibbler</i> (March issue, 1996)<sup id="cite_ref-23" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-23">&#91;23&#93;</a></sup> during the latter's fifth year and supported Harry even when he was outlawed by the Ministry of Magic. Ron stated that she certainly has far more courage than her father, who was at the time stressed when under duress. She also displayed admirable bravery during both the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_the_Department_of_Mysteries" title="Battle of the Department of Mysteries">Battle of the Department of Mysteries</a> and the Battle of Hogwarts.
</p>

<p>Luna believed in many things that few others do; given her inclination towards superstition, she would have probably liked <a href="/wiki/Divination" title="Divination">Divination</a>. However, as she grew older, she came to accept that some things simply do not exist.<sup id="cite_ref-blc_14-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-blc-14">&#91;14&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-msnbc_15-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-msnbc-15">&#91;15&#93;</a></sup>

<p>She seemed to believe everything she read in <i><a href="/wiki/The_Quibbler" title="The Quibbler">The Quibbler</a></i>, mainly because her father wrote it. Some theories she believed in included: <a href="/wiki/Sirius_Black" title="Sirius Black">Sirius Black</a> being <a href="/wiki/Stubby_Boardman" title="Stubby Boardman">Stubby Boardman</a>, that <a href="/wiki/Rufus_Scrimgeour" title="Rufus Scrimgeour">Rufus Scrimgeour</a> was a <a href="/wiki/Vampire" title="Vampire">vampire</a>, that the <a href="/wiki/Auror" title="Auror">Aurors</a> were part of the <a href="/wiki/Rotfang_Conspiracy" title="Rotfang Conspiracy">Rotfang Conspiracy</a> to bring down the Ministry of Magic from within using <a href="/wiki/Dark_Arts" title="Dark Arts">Dark magic</a> and gum disease, that the <a href="/wiki/Tutshill_Tornados" title="Tutshill Tornados">Tutshill Tornados</a> were top of the league by a combination of bewitched brooms and torture, that <a href="/wiki/Cornelius_Fudge" title="Cornelius Fudge">Cornelius Fudge</a> ordered <a href="/wiki/Goblin" title="Goblin">goblins</a> to be made into pies, drowned, or assassinated in other various ways, had an army of <a href="/wiki/Heliopath" title="Heliopath">Heliopaths</a>, and used the <a href="/wiki/Department_of_Mysteries" title="Department of Mysteries">Department of Mysteries</a> to develop terrible poisons to feed to anyone who disagreed with him.
</p><p>Luna also believed in things that were proven true: Harry's story about <a href="/wiki/Rebirth_of_Tom_Marvolo_Riddle" class="mw-redirect" title="Rebirth of Tom Marvolo Riddle">Voldemort's return</a>, the voices beyond the <a href="/wiki/Veil" title="Veil">veil</a> and the belief that there were people behind it, and that <a href="/wiki/Hufflepuff" title="Hufflepuff">Hufflepuff</a> <a href="/wiki/Chaser" title="Chaser">Chaser</a> <a href="/wiki/Zacharias_Smith" title="Zacharias Smith">Zacharias Smith</a> was suffering from <a href="/wiki/Loser%27s_Lurgy" title="Loser&#39;s Lurgy">Loser's Lurgy</a>. Loser's Lurgy is not proven to exist, but it was true that when Smith lost in Quidditch he thus acted accordingly.
</p><p>Some of the animals she believed in were: <a href="/wiki/Moon_Frog" title="Moon Frog">Moon Frogs</a>, <a href="/wiki/Blibbering_Humdinger" title="Blibbering Humdinger">Blibbering Humdingers</a>, <a href="/wiki/Crumple-Horned_Snorkack" title="Crumple-Horned Snorkack">Crumple-Horned Snorkacks</a>, <a href="/wiki/Heliopath" title="Heliopath">Heliopaths</a>, <a href="/wiki/Umgubular_Slashkilter" title="Umgubular Slashkilter">Umgubular Slashkilters</a>, <a href="/wiki/Nargle" title="Nargle">Nargles</a>, <a href="/wiki/Aquavirius_Maggot" title="Aquavirius Maggot">Aquavirius Maggots</a>, <a href="/wiki/Wrackspurt" title="Wrackspurt">Wrackspurts</a>, <a href="/wiki/Gulping_Plimpy" title="Gulping Plimpy">Gulping Plimpies</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Dabberblimp" title="Dabberblimp">Dabberblimps</a>.
</p><p>She also believed in the fable of <a href="/wiki/Ravenclaw%27s_Diadem" title="Ravenclaw&#39;s Diadem">Lost Diadem</a>, a crucial Ravenclaw item and a <a href="/wiki/Horcrux" title="Horcrux">horcrux</a>, which turned out to be a turning point in Harry's battle against Voldemort.<sup id="cite_ref-DH_12-13" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH-12">&#91;12&#93;</a></sup>

<ul><li><b><a href="/wiki/Defence_Against_the_Dark_Arts" title="Defence Against the Dark Arts">Defence Against the Dark Arts</a></b>: As a committed member of <a href="/wiki/Dumbledore%27s_Army" title="Dumbledore&#39;s Army">Dumbledore's Army</a>, Luna learned how to cast numerous defensive and offensive spells under the instruction of <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-OTP_5-12" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OTP-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> Luna demonstrated her excellent defensive abilities by fighting in both the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_the_Department_of_Mysteries" title="Battle of the Department of Mysteries">Battle of the Department of Mysteries</a>, <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_the_Astronomy_Tower" title="Battle of the Astronomy Tower">Battle of the Astronomy Tower</a> and the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a> against numerous <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a>, and surviving all of them.</li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Duelling" title="Duelling">Duelling</a></b>: Despite her young age, Luna was a very competent duellist. At the age of fifteen, she participated in and survived the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_the_Department_of_Mysteries" title="Battle of the Department of Mysteries">Battle of the Department of Mysteries</a> with no more than a few minor injuries. She also emerged from the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_the_Astronomy_Tower" title="Battle of the Astronomy Tower">Battle of the Astronomy Tower</a> with no major injuries. During the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a> she, <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Ginevra_Weasley" title="Ginevra Weasley">Ginny</a> duelled against the notorious <a href="/wiki/Bellatrix_Lestrange" title="Bellatrix Lestrange">Bellatrix Lestrange</a> and emerged as one of the survivors.</li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Charm" title="Charm">Charms</a></b>: In her fourth year, Luna learned how to conjure a corporeal <a href="/wiki/Patronus_Charm" title="Patronus Charm">Patronus</a> in the form of a <a href="/wiki/Hare" title="Hare">hare</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-DH_12-14" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH-12">&#91;12&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-25" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-25">&#91;25&#93;</a></sup> which is an incredibly advanced, powerful and protective charm, as well as a mark of superior <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magical</a> ability and talent, showing her proficiency for charm-work. She could also cast simple charms such as the <a href="/wiki/Locking_Spell" title="Locking Spell">Locking Spell</a>, <a href="/wiki/Reductor_Curse" title="Reductor Curse">Reductor Curse</a>, <a href="/wiki/Stunning_Spell" title="Stunning Spell">Stunning Spell</a>, <a href="/wiki/Levitation_Charm" title="Levitation Charm">Levitation Charm</a>, <a href="/wiki/Revelio_Charm" title="Revelio Charm">Revelio Charm</a>, <a href="/wiki/General_Counter-Spell" title="General Counter-Spell">General Counter-Spell</a> and <a href="/wiki/Wand-Lighting_Charm" title="Wand-Lighting Charm">Wand-Lighting Charm</a> successfully without difficulty.</li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Nonverbal_spell" title="Nonverbal spell">Nonverbal magic</a></b>: In 1998, Luna wordlessly stunned the <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eater</a> <a href="/wiki/Alecto_Carrow" title="Alecto Carrow">Alecto Carrow</a> with the <a href="/wiki/Stunning_Spell" title="Stunning Spell">Stunning Spell</a>, while inside Ravenclaw Tower.<sup id="cite_ref-DH_12-15" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH-12">&#91;12&#93;</a></sup> The fact that she could effectively cast it nonverbally is a testament of sorts to her skill, since non-verbal magic is highly difficult, <a href="/wiki/Nastily_Exhausting_Wizarding_Test" title="Nastily Exhausting Wizarding Test">N.E.W.T.</a>-level magic.</li></ul>

<ul><li><b><a href="/wiki/Care_of_Magical_Creatures" title="Care of Magical Creatures">Care of Magical Creatures</a></b>: She may have studied this subject at Hogwarts. As an adult Luna became a famous <a href="/wiki/Magizoologist" title="Magizoologist">Magizoologist</a>, a wizarding naturalist. She discovered and classified many species that had never been encountered before.<sup id="cite_ref-blc_14-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-blc-14">&#91;14&#93;</a></sup> She was also shown to be very skilled at handling various <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magical</a> <a href="/wiki/Creature" title="Creature">creatures</a>, such as <a href="/wiki/Thestral" title="Thestral">Thestrals</a>, showing her proficiency in <a href="/wiki/Magizoology" title="Magizoology">Magizoology</a>.</li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Study_of_Ancient_Runes" title="Study of Ancient Runes">Study of Ancient Runes</a></b>: In the <a href="/wiki/July" title="July">July</a>/<a href="/wiki/August" title="August">August</a> edition of <i><a href="/wiki/The_Quibbler" title="The Quibbler">The Quibbler</a></i> in <a href="/wiki/1995" title="1995">1995</a>, Luna was able to read that issue's segment written in Ancient <a href="/wiki/Rune" title="Rune">runes</a> upside down, heavily implying she was able to understand and translate such runes. This also implies that she may have studied the subject at Hogwarts.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP10_7-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP10-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li><b>Creativity</b>: Luna was a very creative and artistic witch. She invented a <a href="/wiki/Luna_Lovegood%27s_lion_hat" class="mw-redirect" title="Luna Lovegood&#39;s lion hat">lion-shaped hat</a> that, with a tap of a wand, made a realistic roar, and she made a <a href="/wiki/Luna_Lovegood%27s_eagle_hat" title="Luna Lovegood&#39;s eagle hat">flapping eagle hat</a> of the same kind. She also painted on her bedroom's ceiling the portraits of Harry Potter, Ron Weasley, Hermione Granger, Ginny Weasley, and Neville Longbottom, woving them with a "chain" consisting of a single word, "<i>friends</i>", "<i>repeated a thousand of times in golden ink</i>",<sup id="cite_ref-26" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-26">&#91;26&#93;</a></sup> and it is suggested that she painted birds, flowers and insects on the walls of the kitchen of her family house.<sup id="cite_ref-27" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-27">&#91;27&#93;</a></sup> All of this show her impressive skills as an artist.</li>
<li><b>Leadership skills</b>: At the age of sixteen, Luna co-led Dumbledore's Army along with her friends, Ginny and Neville. Their leadership was so efficient that she was abducted in an attempt to force the resistance to surrender, though she was ultimately rescued by Harry and Ron.</li></ul>

<ul><li><b><a href="/wiki/Luna_Lovegood%27s_wands" title="Luna Lovegood&#39;s wands">Wand</a></b>: Luna Lovegood's first wand was of unknown length, <a href="/wiki/Wand_wood" title="Wand wood">wood</a> and <a href="/wiki/Wand_core" title="Wand core">core</a> material. This wand she used through out her life as a Hogwarts student, until she was captured and her wand confiscated and broken by Death Eaters in the middle of her sixth year. Her second wand was of unknown length, wood, and core material as well. It was made by <a href="/wiki/Garrick_Ollivander" title="Garrick Ollivander">Garrick Ollivander</a> in the spring of <a href="/wiki/1998" title="1998">1998</a> after his rescue from <a href="/wiki/Malfoy_Manor" title="Malfoy Manor">Malfoy Manor</a>.</li></ul>

<ul><li><b><a href="/wiki/Luna_Lovegood%27s_lion_hat" class="mw-redirect" title="Luna Lovegood&#39;s lion hat">Lion hat</a></b>: Luna created this hat that had the appearance of an ordinary pointed hat topped with a fake stuffed lion. When she tapped the hat with her wand, it let out an extremely realistic roar. She wore it to show her support of <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor" title="Gryffindor">Gryffindor</a> whenever they played against the <a href="/wiki/Slytherin_Quidditch_team" title="Slytherin Quidditch team">Slytherin Quidditch team</a>.</li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Luna_Lovegood%27s_eagle_hat" title="Luna Lovegood&#39;s eagle hat">Eagle hat</a></b>: Luna wore this hat whenever the <a href="/wiki/Ravenclaw_Quidditch_team" title="Ravenclaw Quidditch team">Ravenclaw Quidditch team</a> played against the <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor_Quidditch_team" title="Gryffindor Quidditch team">Gryffindor Quidditch team</a>. On top of the hat is what appears to be a living eagle, which flapped its wings.</li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Luna_Lovegood%27s_dress_robes" title="Luna Lovegood&#39;s dress robes">Dress robes</a></b>: Luna wore silver dress robes to the <a href="/wiki/Slug_Club_Christmas_party" title="Slug Club Christmas party">Slug Club Christmas party</a> in 1996 when she was taken as a guest by Harry Potter. Luna also owned a pair of <a href="/wiki/Luna_Lovegood%27s_yellow_dress_robes" title="Luna Lovegood&#39;s yellow dress robes">yellow dress robes</a> that she wore to the <a href="/wiki/Wedding_of_William_Weasley_and_Fleur_Delacour" title="Wedding of William Weasley and Fleur Delacour">wedding of Bill and Fleur</a> in 1997.</li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Luna_Lovegood%27s_beetle_wing_earrings" title="Luna Lovegood&#39;s beetle wing earrings">Beetle wing earrings</a></b>: This was one of the items on her lost possessions list. She, at first, had believed they were stolen and taken away by <a href="/wiki/Nargle" title="Nargle">Nargles</a>, but then later realised it was just her classmates.</li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Luna_Lovegood%27s_silver_star_earrings" title="Luna Lovegood&#39;s silver star earrings">Silver star earrings</a></b>: Luna wore these to the Slug Club Christmas Party in 1996, along with her silver dress robes.</li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Dirigible_plum_earrings" title="Dirigible plum earrings">Dirigible plum-shaped earrings</a></b>: Luna wore these earrings when she expressed her belief in Harry, during 1995. In 1998 Harry noticed a bush covered in exceptionally strange and orange, radish-like fruit called <a href="/wiki/Dirigible_plum" title="Dirigible plum">Dirigible plum</a> and notes their striking resemblance to Luna's earrings.</li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Luna_Lovegood%27s_hare_bracelet" title="Luna Lovegood&#39;s hare bracelet">Hare bracelet</a></b>: This bracelet was owned by Luna. It was made of a golden material and adorned with a small hare penchant, similar to her <a href="/wiki/Patronus_Charm" title="Patronus Charm">Patronus</a>.</li>
<li><b>Patterned shoes</b>: Luna has a pair of patterned Converse sneakers, with what looks likes strawberries on them. They were stolen by some other pupils and hidden, with the rest of her shoes. Luna had to walk around barefoot for most of the <a href="/wiki/Order_of_the_Phoenix" title="Order of the Phoenix">Order of the Phoenix</a> because of this.</li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Purple_Quill" title="Purple Quill">Purple Quill</a></b>: Luna owned this quill during the <a href="/wiki/1995-1996_school_year" class="mw-redirect" title="1995-1996 school year">1995-1996 school year</a> before it was stolen by her classmates. It was produced from a feather of a woodpecker.</li></ul>

<li><b><a href="/wiki/Beetle_ring" title="Beetle ring">Beetle ring</a></b>: Luna wore this ring in 1995 and possibly earlier.</li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Bubble_Bow_Booster" title="Bubble Bow Booster">Bubble Bow Booster</a>: </b>Luna owned a kit that was still in its original packaging, but someone took it from her and hid it during the 1995-1996 school year.</li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Sum-solving_ink" title="Sum-solving ink">Sum-solving ink</a></b>: This was a type of ink that presumably helped solve math problems. Luna owned a bottle of turquoise sum-solving ink, but someone took it from her and hid it during her fourth year.</li>

<p>With the death of <a href="/wiki/Pandora_Lovegood" title="Pandora Lovegood">her mother</a> when she was nine years old, Luna was raised solely by <a href="/wiki/Xenophilius_Lovegood" title="Xenophilius Lovegood">her father</a>. Because of this, she was an only child and the two were very close. Xenophilius raised his daughter to share many of his unusual beliefs, and the two enjoyed going on holidays to look for creatures such as <a href="/wiki/Crumple-Horned_Snorkack" title="Crumple-Horned Snorkack">Crumple-Horned Snorkacks</a> in Sweden.<sup id="cite_ref-OTP_5-13" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OTP-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> Luna believed anything her father wrote in <a href="/wiki/The_Quibbler" title="The Quibbler">The Quibbler</a> magazine, and came to his defence when <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione Granger</a> claimed the magazine was "rubbish." 
</p><p>When Luna was abducted by <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a> in <a href="/wiki/1997" title="1997">1997</a> to force Xenophilius to stop opposing the Voldemort-controlled <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">Ministry of Magic</a> in <i><a href="/wiki/The_Quibbler" title="The Quibbler">The Quibbler</a></i>, her father was beside himself. He was desperate to the point of ceasing his public support of <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> and <a href="/wiki/Ambush_at_the_Lovegood_House" title="Ambush at the Lovegood House">attempting</a> to turn him, Hermione Granger and <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ron Weasley</a> over to the Death Eaters in exchange for Luna's safety. When he failed to do so, Xenophilius was arrested by <a href="/wiki/Selwyn" title="Selwyn">Selwyn</a> and <a href="/wiki/Travers" title="Travers">Travers</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-DH_12-16" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH-12">&#91;12&#93;</a></sup> Luna would also eventually meet <a href="/wiki/Rolf_Scamander" title="Rolf Scamander">Rolf Scamander</a>, grandson of the famous <a href="/wiki/Newton_Scamander" title="Newton Scamander">Newt Scamander</a> and his wife <a href="/wiki/Porpentina_Goldstein" title="Porpentina Goldstein">Porpentina</a>. Rolf would become her husband and the union would produce twins, <a href="/wiki/Lorcan_Scamander" title="Lorcan Scamander">Lorcan</a> and <a href="/wiki/Lysander_Scamander" title="Lysander Scamander">Lysander</a>.
</p>

<p>When Harry Potter first met Luna in 1995, he found her rather odd, but was grateful that she was one of the few people to believe his story that <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a> had returned, and sympathised with the loss of her mother and her status as an outcast. After the Battle of the Department of Mysteries, they discussed the death of <a href="/wiki/Sirius_Black" title="Sirius Black">Sirius Black</a> and the possibility of an afterlife. Harry "<i>found that the terrible weight in his stomach seemed to have lessened slightly.</i>"<sup id="cite_ref-OTP_5-14" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OTP-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>At the beginning of the next school year, he reacted coldly when <a href="/wiki/Romilda_Vane" title="Romilda Vane">Romilda Vane</a> implied that Luna and <a href="/wiki/Neville_Longbottom" title="Neville Longbottom">Neville Longbottom</a> were not worth spending time with; a contrast to the previous year, when he felt slightly embarrassed that his crush <a href="/wiki/Cho_Chang" title="Cho Chang">Cho Chang</a> saw him in Luna's company. In <a href="/wiki/1996" title="1996">1996</a>, Harry asked Luna to attend <a href="/wiki/Horace_Slughorn" title="Horace Slughorn">Professor Slughorn</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Slug_Club_Christmas_party" title="Slug Club Christmas party">Christmas party</a> with him as friends after reflecting on how "<i>he had never met anyone quite like her.</i>"<sup id="cite_ref-HBP_6-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup> Harry was one of the portraits of her friends that Luna painted and hung on her bedroom ceiling, to which, upon Harry's discovery in the last book during his Horcrux-seeking journey, made him feel "<i>a great rush of affection"</i> towards her. When Harry took <a href="/wiki/Polyjuice_Potion" title="Polyjuice Potion">Polyjuice Potion</a> to look like Barny at the <a href="/wiki/Wedding_of_William_Weasley_and_Fleur_Delacour" title="Wedding of William Weasley and Fleur Delacour">Wedding of William Weasley and Fleur Delacour</a>, she recognised him from the expression on his face, hinting again at the closeness of their friendship, recognising him even under the disguising effects of the potion. Shortly before going to sacrifice himself during the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a>, Harry noted that Luna was among his closest friends, whom he had hoped to see one last time before his death. After Voldemort's defeat at Harry's hands, Luna sensed that Harry wanted to get away from the victory celebrations, and created a distraction so that he could slip away with Ron and Hermione.<sup id="cite_ref-DH_12-17" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH-12">&#91;12&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Later in life, Harry and Luna would remain close friends. He and Ginny even gave their <a href="/wiki/Lily_L._Potter" title="Lily L. Potter">daughter</a> the middle name "Luna" in honour of her. It's also quite possible that Harry became friends with her husband (<a href="/wiki/Rolf_Scamander" title="Rolf Scamander">Rolf Scamander</a>) and close to her twins, <a href="/wiki/Lorcan_Scamander" title="Lorcan Scamander">Lorcan</a> and <a href="/wiki/Lysander_Scamander" title="Lysander Scamander">Lysander</a>.
</p>

<p>Luna and Hermione Granger did not have an ideal first meeting, as Hermione called <i><a href="/wiki/The_Quibbler" title="The Quibbler">The Quibbler</a> </i>"rubbish" without knowing that Luna's father was the editor. She was initially frustrated with Luna's belief in all manner of things without logical grounds or proof. However, she invited Luna to <a href="/wiki/Dumbledore%27s_Army" title="Dumbledore&#39;s Army">Dumbledore's Army</a> and the two became friends. Hermione's opinion of Luna particularly seemed to improve after Luna proved her loyalty and bravery by participating in the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_the_Department_of_Mysteries" title="Battle of the Department of Mysteries">Battle of the Department of Mysteries</a>, as she simply accepted that she and Luna had very different world views.<sup id="cite_ref-OTP_5-15" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OTP-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> The following school year, Luna comforted Hermione over her fight with <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ron Weasley</a> when she ended up crying in the bathroom at one point.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP_6-10" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>At <a href="/wiki/William_Weasley" title="William Weasley">Bill Weasley</a> and <a href="/wiki/Fleur_Delacour" title="Fleur Delacour">Fleur Delacour</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Wedding_of_William_Weasley_and_Fleur_Delacour" title="Wedding of William Weasley and Fleur Delacour">wedding</a>, Hermione tried to kick Ron for teasing Luna, and later defended her again from the insults of the portrait of <a href="/wiki/Phineas_Nigellus_Black" title="Phineas Nigellus Black">Phineas Nigellus Black</a>. Hermione was among the portraits Luna painted on her bedroom ceiling, circled by the words "friends." When <a href="/wiki/Xenophilius_Lovegood" title="Xenophilius Lovegood">Luna's father</a> attempted to turn Harry, Ron and Hermione over to <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a> in exchange for his daughter's safety in <a href="/wiki/1998" title="1998">1998</a>, Hermione engineered their escape so that the Death Eaters saw Harry briefly, thus ensuring that both Luna and her father were not harmed.<sup id="cite_ref-DH_12-18" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH-12">&#91;12&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Luna and Hermione also fought at the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_the_Astronomy_Tower" title="Battle of the Astronomy Tower">Battle of the Astronomy Tower</a> and the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a>, where she, Seamus Finnigan, and <a href="/wiki/Ernest_Macmillan" title="Ernest Macmillan">Ernie Macmillan</a> saved Hermione, Harry and Ron from Dementors. During the final stage of the battle, Luna, Hermione, and <a href="/wiki/Ginevra_Weasley" title="Ginevra Weasley">Ginny Weasley</a> fought together against <a href="/wiki/Bellatrix_Lestrange" title="Bellatrix Lestrange">Bellatrix Lestrange</a>, <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Voldemort</a>'s most powerful <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eater</a>. Hermione and Luna both survived the war, and it can be presumed that the pair remained very good friends during adulthood as well. Luna may be one of the best female friends of Hermione (along with Ginny), as she was only seen with these girls during her school-time at Hogwarts.
</p>

<p>At the beginning of their acquaintance, Ron Weasley thought Luna was weird and was not particularly interested in knowing her, even occasionally referring to her as "Loony." Luna, on the other hand, seemed to find Ron very funny but rather insensitive in his relationships at times. Ron eventually grew fonder of Luna, marking her as his good friend, after getting to know her better through <a href="/wiki/Dumbledore%27s_Army" title="Dumbledore&#39;s Army">Dumbledore's Army</a> and her being one of the only D.A. members to come to the trio's assistance when they tried to save <a href="/wiki/Sirius_Black" title="Sirius Black">Sirius Black</a>, during the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_the_Department_of_Mysteries" title="Battle of the Department of Mysteries">Battle of the Department of Mysteries</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-OTP_5-16" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OTP-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>He was particularly amused by her <a href="/wiki/Quidditch" title="Quidditch">Quidditch</a> commentary in <a href="/wiki/1996" title="1996">1996</a><sup id="cite_ref-HBP_6-11" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup> and thought highly of her bravery. When she attended his brother Bill's wedding, Ron was further amused by her behaviour and remarked that she gave "good value." However, Ron was still prone to tease Luna and scoff at her unusual beliefs at times.<sup id="cite_ref-DH_12-19" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH-12">&#91;12&#93;</a></sup> Ron and Luna also fought at the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_the_Astronomy_Tower" title="Battle of the Astronomy Tower">Battle of the Astronomy Tower</a> and the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a>, where she, <a href="/wiki/Seamus_Finnigan" title="Seamus Finnigan">Seamus Finnigan</a> and Ernie Macmillan saved Ron, Harry and Hermione from Dementors. Ron and Luna both survived the war, and the good friends remained in contact in adulthood.
</p>

<p>Luna and Ginny Weasley were in the same year at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts</a>, though in different houses; Ginny was in Gryffindor whereas Luna was in Ravenclaw. Ginny seemed to find Luna rather peculiar, as she first referred to Luna as "Loony Lovegood" (though in an affectionate way). The two were very friendly, as Ginny led <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> and <a href="/wiki/Neville_Longbottom" title="Neville Longbottom">Neville Longbottom</a> into her train compartment in <a href="/wiki/1995" title="1995">1995</a>, conversing politely with her.<sup id="cite_ref-OTP_5-17" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OTP-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Luna became one of Ginny's best friends through <a href="/wiki/Dumbledore%27s_Army" title="Dumbledore&#39;s Army">Dumbledore's Army</a>, and Ginny began telling others not to call Luna "Loony," prompting Luna to randomly state during her <a href="/wiki/Quidditch" title="Quidditch">Quidditch</a> commentary that Ginny was nice and she liked her.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP_6-12" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup> Along with Neville, Luna and Ginny led the re-formed Dumbledore's Army while Hogwarts was under Voldemort's control.<sup id="cite_ref-DH_12-20" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH-12">&#91;12&#93;</a></sup> Ginny was also one of the five portraits ‚Äî along with Harry, <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ron</a>, <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione</a>, and Neville ‚Äî that Luna painted and hung from her bedroom ceiling, with the word "friends" joining them together in gold writing.
</p><p>Ginny and Harry Potter named <a href="/wiki/Lily_Potter" title="Lily Potter">their daughter</a> after Luna, considering Luna is a "dear friend." Luna may be one of the best female friends of Ginny (along with Hermione), as she was only seen with these girls during her school-time at Hogwarts. Indeed, Ginny insisted Luna to guide Harry into <a href="/wiki/Ravenclaw_Tower" title="Ravenclaw Tower">Ravenclaw Tower</a> over <a href="/wiki/Cho_Chang" title="Cho Chang">Cho Chang</a>, confidently trusting her to not incite any clash of romance as Cho might have.
</p>

<p>Luna and <a href="/wiki/Neville_Longbottom" title="Neville Longbottom">Neville Longbottom</a>, a Gryffindor student, first met on the Hogwarts Express, in 1995. Neville found Luna a bit "odd," as she believed in the existence of strange, mythical creatures, had a tendency to read magazines upside down, and wore earrings that appeared to be made of radishes. Luna, like Harry and Neville, had the ability to see Thestrals, as they had all seen someone die. Neville fought alongside Luna at the Battle of the Department of Mysteries, the Battle of the Astronomy Tower and the Battle of Hogwarts. 
</p><p>At some point, they became very close friends. During Luna's sixth year at Hogwarts, she, Neville and Ginny, re-formed and co-led Dumbledore's Army in order to oppose the Carrows.
</p>

</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Luna_Lovegood?veaction=edit&amp;section=25" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Luna_Lovegood?action=edit&amp;section=25" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>Luna means "Moon" in Latin (as well as in Italian, Russian, Spanish, Bulgarian and Romanian). People believed that the moon caused craziness, which may be why she's the way she is. Her nickname, "Loony," also references the moon and its ties with insanity, as it is short for "lunatic."</li>
<li><i>Lovegood</i> is a real surname and a compound word (Love and Good). Speaking in accordance of her having good feelings for every person she knew, hence Lovegood.</li>
<li>Luna Lovegood is also alliterative, for effect.</li></ul>

<ul><li>Luna is the only one of Harry's close school friends who is not a fellow Gryffindor.</li>
<li>Harry names his daughter, Lily Luna, after her. Luna is the only person still living to give their name to one of Harry's children.</li>
<li>The <a href="/wiki/Dirigible_plum_earrings" title="Dirigible plum earrings">Dirigible plum earrings</a> Luna wears in <i>Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix</i> were made by <a href="/wiki/Evanna_Lynch" title="Evanna Lynch">Evanna Lynch</a> herself, as was a bracelet she wore (in the shape of Luna's patronus) in <i>Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince</i>.</li>
<li>In the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Order_of_the_Phoenix_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix (film)">film adaptation</a> of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Order_of_the_Phoenix" title="Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix">Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix</a></i>, it is <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione Granger</a> who introduces Luna to her friends, instead of <a href="/wiki/Ginevra_Weasley" title="Ginevra Weasley">Ginny Weasley</a>, though the books imply that Hermione had never met Luna before.</li>
<li>Luna was the last person in the books known to commentate a <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts</a> <a href="/wiki/Quidditch" title="Quidditch">Quidditch</a> match. It is unknown if she commented during the final match during <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>'s sixth year.</li>
<li>In <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Half-Blood_Prince" title="Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince">Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince</a></i>, <a href="/wiki/Nymphadora_Tonks" title="Nymphadora Tonks">Nymphadora Tonks</a> rescues Harry from the <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Express" title="Hogwarts Express">Hogwarts Express</a> after <a href="/wiki/Draco_Malfoy" title="Draco Malfoy">Draco Malfoy</a> uses the <a href="/wiki/Full_Body-Bind_Curse" title="Full Body-Bind Curse">Full Body-Bind Curse</a> on him. However, in the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Half-Blood_Prince_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince (film)">film version</a>, Luna is the one who rescues Harry, by using her <a href="/wiki/Spectrespecs" title="Spectrespecs">spectrespecs</a> to see the <a href="/wiki/Wrackspurt" title="Wrackspurt">Wrackspurt</a> buzzing around his head, alerting her to his presence under the <a href="/wiki/Cloak_of_Invisibility" title="Cloak of Invisibility">cloak</a>.</li>
<li>In <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Half-Blood_Prince" title="Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince">Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince</a></i>, Hermione points out that Harry put something in Ron's drink before the Quidditch match, but in the film version, it is Luna who notices, thinking it is a tonic.</li>
<li>According to the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Half-Blood_Prince_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince (film)">film adaptation</a> of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Half-Blood_Prince" title="Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince">Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince</a></i> Luna sleepwalks at night and says because of that she wears shoes to bed.</li>
<li>It was rumoured that Severus Snape was Luna's father and that Luna and Neville Longbottom were going to end up together at the end of <i>Deathly Hallows</i>. Both these rumours were false. However, in the film adaptation of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Deathly_Hallows:_Part_2" title="Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 2">Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 2</a></i>, <a href="/wiki/Neville_Longbottom" title="Neville Longbottom">Neville</a> confesses to <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry</a> and <a href="/wiki/Ginevra_Weasley" title="Ginevra Weasley">Ginny</a> that he is crazy for Luna. They are later seen sitting beside each other in the infirmary. However, <a href="/wiki/Matthew_Lewis" title="Matthew Lewis">Matthew Lewis</a> has said that this is merely a "summer fling" before Neville marries <a href="/wiki/Hannah_Abbott" title="Hannah Abbott">Hannah Abbott</a> and Luna marries <a href="/wiki/Rolf_Scamander" title="Rolf Scamander">Rolf Scamander</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-28" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-28">&#91;28&#93;</a></sup>.</li>
<li>Luna and fellow <a href="/wiki/Dumbledore%27s_Army" title="Dumbledore&#39;s Army">Dumbledore's Army</a> member, <a href="/wiki/Neville_Longbottom" title="Neville Longbottom">Neville Longbottom</a>, both have compound last names. Love and good for Luna, and long and bottom for Neville.</li>
<li>In <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Deathly_Hallows:_Part_2" title="Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 2">Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 2</a></i>, Luna seems to have an intimate friendship with <a href="/wiki/Helena_Ravenclaw" title="Helena Ravenclaw">Helena Ravenclaw</a>, who knew the location of the diadem of Ravenclaw.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Evanna_Lynch" title="Evanna Lynch">Evanna Lynch</a>, who plays Luna Lovegood in the film series, named her cats after Harry Potter characters: she has a cat named Luna and another named <a href="/wiki/Albus_Dumbledore" title="Albus Dumbledore">Dumbledore</a>. She also had a cat named <a href="/wiki/Crookshanks" title="Crookshanks">Crookshanks</a>, although it is now deceased.</li>
<li>In a deleted scene from <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Deathly_Hallows:_Part_2" title="Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 2">Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 2</a></i>, Luna leaves <a href="/wiki/Shell_Cottage" title="Shell Cottage">Shell Cottage</a> to return to <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a>. Harry tells her that it is not the same, to which Luna replies: "Neither am I."<sup id="cite_ref-29" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-29">&#91;29&#93;</a></sup> This is a reference to Luna's more mature and realistic worldview following the war.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Scarlett_Byrne" title="Scarlett Byrne">Scarlett Byrne</a> auditioned to play Luna,<sup id="cite_ref-30" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-30">&#91;30&#93;</a></sup> as did the famous Irish actress <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Saoirse_Ronan" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Saoirse Ronan">Saoirse Ronan</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-31" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-31">&#91;31&#93;</a></sup> and <i><a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/The_Dark_Knight_Rises" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:The Dark Knight Rises">The Dark Knight Rises</a></i> star <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Juno_Temple" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Juno Temple">Juno Temple</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-32" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-32">&#91;32&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li>Evanna recently portrayed Luna again for one of <a href="/wiki/Jessie_Cave" title="Jessie Cave">Jessie Cave</a>'s Pindippy videos.</li>
<li>In the film adaptation of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Deathly_Hallows:_Part_2" title="Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 2">Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 2</a></i>, Luna did not show the <a href="/wiki/Ravenclaw_Tower#Ravenclaw_Common_Room" title="Ravenclaw Tower">Ravenclaw Common Room</a> but instead shouted at <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> because of his reluctance of listening to her attempts of her approach about finding <a href="/wiki/Rowena_Ravenclaw%27s_Diadem" class="mw-redirect" title="Rowena Ravenclaw&#39;s Diadem">Rowena Ravenclaw's Diadem</a>.</li>
<li>A variant of Luna is available in the "Downloadable Character Pack" <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Downloadable_content" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Downloadable content">DLC</a> for <i><a href="/wiki/LEGO_Harry_Potter:_Years_5-7" title="LEGO Harry Potter: Years 5-7">LEGO Harry Potter: Years 5-7</a></i>. The variant is called "Luna (Lion Head)" because it portrays Luna wearing her <a href="/wiki/Luna_Lovegood%27s_lion_hat" class="mw-redirect" title="Luna Lovegood&#39;s lion hat">lion hat</a>.</li>
<li>In Part 2 of the final film, Luna is not seen duelling <a href="/wiki/Bellatrix_Lestrange" title="Bellatrix Lestrange">Bellatrix Lestrange</a> alongside <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione Granger</a> and <a href="/wiki/Ginevra_Weasley" title="Ginevra Weasley">Ginny Weasley</a> but can be seen duelling two Death Eaters alongside an unidentified student behind Bellatrix during her duel with <a href="/wiki/Molly_Weasley" title="Molly Weasley">Molly Weasley</a>.</li>
<li>IGN listed Luna Lovegood as their 12th top <i>Harry Potter</i> character, saying that loony-ness made her a "delight".<sup id="cite_ref-33" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-33">&#91;33&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li>Empire Magazine listed Luna as their 10th favourite Harry Potter character in "The 25 Greatest Harry Potter Characters".<sup id="cite_ref-34" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-34">&#91;34&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li>In the early drafts, Luna's name was "Lily Moon," until <a href="/wiki/J._K._Rowling" title="J. K. Rowling">Rowling</a> changed it.</li>
<li>Luna is the only known member of the Lovegood family whose first name is not of Greek origin, rather it is of Latin origin.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/J._K._Rowling" title="J. K. Rowling">Rowling</a> originally intended Luna and Neville to end up together. In an interview with USA Today, she stated that she "started to feel a bit of a pull between them" but added that if they were together by the end it would have "felt too neat". Additionally, she stated that "Neville would always find Luna‚Äôs wilder flights of fancy alarming, despite their close friendship."<sup id="cite_ref-35" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-35">&#91;35&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li>Evanna Lynch has stated that she considers Luna to be a <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Vegetarianism" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Vegetarianism">vegetarian</a>, as it is her belief that Luna would not <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Vegetarianism#Treatment_of_animals" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Vegetarianism">feel morally right eating animals who have suffered</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-36" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-36">&#91;36&#93;</a></sup> However, Luna has eaten meat dishes in the books, such as <a href="/wiki/Gulping_Plimpy" title="Gulping Plimpy">Gulping Plimpy</a> soup.</li></ul>
<h3><span id="Author's_comments"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="Author.27s_comments">Author's comments</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Luna_Lovegood?veaction=edit&amp;section=27" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Author&#039;s comments">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Luna_Lovegood?action=edit&amp;section=27" title="Edit section: Author&#039;s comments"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p><a href="/wiki/J._K._Rowling" title="J. K. Rowling">J. K. Rowling</a> has said that the character of Luna took her by surprise, but that she was very fun to write, and contrasted her with <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione Granger</a>: "<i>She's slightly out of step in many ways but she's the anti-Hermione. Hermione's so logical and inflexible in so many ways and Luna is likely to believe ten impossible things before breakfast.</i>"<sup id="cite_ref-37" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-37">&#91;37&#93;</a></sup> Rowling has named Luna as one of her favourite characters.<sup id="cite_ref-38" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-38">&#91;38&#93;</a></sup> Together with Harry, Ron, Hermione, Ginny, Neville, and Draco, Luna is part of the "big seven" characters that Rowling considers to be the most major characters in the series.<sup id="cite_ref-rowlingtalksdeathly2_39-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-rowlingtalksdeathly2-39">&#91;39&#93;</a></sup> She was also noted to be very pleased with the casting of <a href="/wiki/Evanna_Lynch" title="Evanna Lynch">Evanna Lynch</a> as Luna as she had had lunch with the actress and said she was "perfect".

<p><a href="/wiki/Evanna_Lynch" title="Evanna Lynch">Evanna Lynch</a> plays Luna in the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_(film_series)" title="Harry Potter (film series)">films</a>, and since Evanna is <a href="/wiki/Ireland" title="Ireland">Irish</a>, Luna has a slight touch of an Irish accent. In the films, Harry meets her at the school carriages where she is introduced by Hermione instead of Ginny. Luna's <a href="/wiki/Patronus_Charm" title="Patronus Charm">Patronus</a> is revealed to be a Hare, this is later confirmed to be canon in <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Deathly_Hallows" title="Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows">Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows</a></i>. Luna is seen barefoot throughout the film because students had stolen her shoes. In addition, Luna seems to have a taste for pudding and introduces Harry to the <a href="/wiki/Thestral" title="Thestral">Thestrals</a>; she offers a young Thestral an apple, which was turned down, before offering it a bit of raw meat with an accepting smile.
She also skipped around the school. Her <a href="/wiki/Butterbeer" title="Butterbeer">butterbeer</a> cork necklace has only one cork, which she says is to repel <a href="/wiki/Nargle" title="Nargle">Nargles</a>. She also wears a beetle ring. During the battle at the Ministry, instead of being knocked unconscious, she was hit in the mouth by a <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eater</a> before using an unusual verbally-incanted version of <i><a href="/wiki/Levicorpus" title="Levicorpus">Levicorpus</a></i> to hurl her attacker away. When Ginny disintegrates the prophecy shelves with a <a href="/wiki/Reductor_Curse" title="Reductor Curse">Reductor Curse</a>, Luna is so distracted by the light that she does not run away, but Neville saves her. She is then taken hostage along with all of Harry's companions but is rescued by the appearance of the Order of the Phoenix members (Sirius, Remus, Tonks, Kingsley, and Mad Eye).
In the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Half-Blood_Prince_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince (film)">film adaptation</a> of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Half-Blood_Prince" title="Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince">Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince</a></i>, she states that she often sleep-walks at night and wears her shoes to bed just in case.
Lynch was a huge fan of the series before she was cast. After J.K. Rowling had met with the actress, she told the director one thing: "Perfect."
</p>

<p>Luna's role in the game is much the same as her role in the film, though we do not meet her until Harry goes to find the people who signed up for the D.A. to tell them about the <a href="/wiki/Room_of_Requirement" title="Room of Requirement">Room of Requirement</a>. The Player has to help Luna feed the Thestrals, and then she will appear in the Room of Requirement. Later in the game, <a href="/wiki/Fred_and_George_Weasley" title="Fred and George Weasley">Fred and George Weasley</a> send Luna to tell Harry how to swamp the clock tower courtyard, with one of their <a href="/wiki/Portable_Swamp" title="Portable Swamp">Portable Swamps</a>. At the end of the game, the player must help Luna find her missing objects, which are: a jumper, a newspaper, a lion mask, a pair of pink shoes, and her purse. Luna is also a playable character in the HBP game. She is a two-player <a href="/wiki/Duelling" title="Duelling">duelling</a> character and is unlocked (with Draco Malfoy) by retrieving two crests.
</p>
