<b>Reggie Page</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Squib" title="Squib">Squib</a> born to a <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggle</a> <a href="/wiki/Page_siblings%27_father" title="Page siblings&#39; father">father</a> and <a href="/wiki/Page_siblings%27_mother" title="Page siblings&#39; mother">a witch</a>. He had a <a href="/wiki/Daniel_Page%27s_sister" title="Daniel Page&#39;s sister">sister</a> and one brother who was a wizard, <a href="/wiki/Daniel_Page" title="Daniel Page">Daniel Page</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-MA_1-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-MA-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Reggie_Page?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Reggie_Page?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<figure class="thumb tright show-info-icon" style="width: 222px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/d/de/The_Bloody_Pages.png/revision/latest?cb=20200604125037" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/d/de/The_Bloody_Pages.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/220?cb=20200604125037" decoding="async" width="220" height="101" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="The Bloody Pages.png" data-image-key="The_Bloody_Pages.png" /></a> 	 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:The_Bloody_Pages.png" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption">A Muggle newspaper reporting the arrests in the Page family</p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure>
<p>Born and raised somewhere in the <a href="/wiki/Great_Britain" title="Great Britain">British Isles</a>, Reggie was the elder son of a <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggle</a> father and a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch</a> mother. He had a <a href="/wiki/Daniel_Page%27s_sister" title="Daniel Page&#39;s sister">sister</a> and a younger brother, <a href="/wiki/Daniel_Page" title="Daniel Page">Daniel Page</a>. Unlike Daniel, he wasn't born with any magical abilities. He attended a Muggle primary school.<sup id="cite_ref-MA_1-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-MA-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Their father was involved in illicit activities and in due time, he joined in. At some point, they were eventually caught by the Muggle <a href="/wiki/Police" title="Police">police</a> and arrested. The mother tried to intervene using magic so they could escape, scaring the police and violating the <a href="/wiki/International_Statute_of_Wizarding_Secrecy" title="International Statute of Wizarding Secrecy">International Statute of Wizarding Secrecy</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-MA_1-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-MA-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>While they were tried and sentenced to a Muggle prison, her violation of the Statute of Secrecy led to her being sentenced to <a href="/wiki/Azkaban" title="Azkaban">Azkaban</a>. The family's arrest was covered by a Muggle newspaper, dubbing them "The Bloody Pages".<sup id="cite_ref-MA_1-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-MA-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
