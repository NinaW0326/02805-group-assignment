<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Stebbins_(Potter-era)?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Stebbins_(Potter-era)?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>A member of <a href="/wiki/Hufflepuff" title="Hufflepuff">Hufflepuff</a> <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Houses" title="Hogwarts Houses">House</a>, he attended the <a href="/wiki/Yule_Ball" title="Yule Ball">Yule Ball</a> with <a href="/wiki/Fawcett" title="Fawcett">Fawcett</a> of <a href="/wiki/Ravenclaw" title="Ravenclaw">Ravenclaw</a> house. They were caught, presumably kissing, in the <a href="/wiki/Rose" title="Rose">rose</a> bushes during the Ball by <a href="/wiki/Severus_Snape" title="Severus Snape">Professor Snape</a>, and were each docked ten <a href="/wiki/House_points" title="House points">House points</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-GOF23_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF23-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Stebbins_(Potter-era)?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Stebbins_(Potter-era)?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>In a deleted scene from the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Goblet_of_Fire_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire (film)">film adaptation</a> of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Goblet_of_Fire" title="Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire">Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire</a></i>, Stebbins and <a href="/wiki/Fawcett" title="Fawcett">Fawcett</a> are caught kissing in one of the <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Carriages" title="Hogwarts Carriages">Hogwarts Carriages</a>, which were parked in the <a href="/wiki/Viaduct_Courtyard" title="Viaduct Courtyard">Entrance Courtyard</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li>In the <a href="/wiki/Great_Britain" title="Great Britain">British</a>, <a href="/wiki/Canada" title="Canada">Canadian</a>, <a href="/wiki/Ukraine" title="Ukraine">Ukrainian</a>, Russian, Polish and Danish editions of <i>Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire</i>, when Snape catches Fawcett with Stebbins in chapter 23, he says, "Ten points from <a href="/wiki/Hufflepuff" title="Hufflepuff">Hufflepuff</a>, Fawcett! And ten points from <a href="/wiki/Ravenclaw" title="Ravenclaw">Ravenclaw</a>, too, Stebbins!", although Fawcett is referred to as a Ravenclaw by <a href="/wiki/Albus_Dumbledore" title="Albus Dumbledore">Albus Dumbledore</a> in chapter 16.<sup id="cite_ref-lexicon_5-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-lexicon-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> The American edition of the novel amends this error, with Snape taking Fawcett's points from Ravenclaw, and Stebbins' from Hufflepuff.<sup id="cite_ref-lexicon_5-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-lexicon-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> In the <i>Goblet of Fire</i> film, however, Snape makes a similar error where he refers to Fawcett as a Hufflepuff and Stebbins as a Ravenclaw. The only way it couldn't be a mistake is that Snape mentions the consequences of the partner's rule-breakings.</li></ul>
