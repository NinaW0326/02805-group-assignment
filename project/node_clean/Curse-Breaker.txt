<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Description">Description</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Curse-Breaker?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Description">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Curse-Breaker?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Description"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p><a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">Ministry</a> <a href="/wiki/Office_for_the_Removal_of_Curses,_Jinxes,_and_Hexes" title="Office for the Removal of Curses, Jinxes, and Hexes">officials</a> tasked with the removal of unfriendly spells from illegally bewitched objects notwithstanding, the term 'Curse-Breaker' was commonly used in reference to a group of adventurous bankers in the employment of <a href="/wiki/Gringotts_Wizarding_Bank" title="Gringotts Wizarding Bank">Gringotts Wizarding Bank</a> at <a href="/wiki/Diagon_Alley" title="Diagon Alley">Diagon Alley</a> in <a href="/wiki/London" title="London">London</a>, <a href="/wiki/England" title="England">England</a>. Operating as something akin to treasure-hunting archaeologists, Gringotts Curse-Breakers was tasked with venturing through tombs, caves, mountains, deserts and forests and the like in search of old treasure troves from which the bank might make a profit, and specialised in the removal of magical security measures placed upon such areas. Not a very formal affair, Curse-Breakers did not have any form of formal uniform, seen as how the <a href="/wiki/Goblin" title="Goblin">Goblins</a> running generally cared little about what they look like in terms of appearance or dress, as long as they bring much treasure back to the bank. it was, however, nevertheless widely considered to be a very serious profession due to the amount of danger involved, as in addition to the risk they all ran of being <a href="/wiki/Death" title="Death">killed</a> as a result of an old curse left to protect their objectives, they would also encounter and have to deal with a great many dark or otherwise dangerous creatures, artefacts. and even rivals on their travels; <a href="/wiki/Dark_Wizard" title="Dark Wizard">Dark Wizards</a> among them.<sup id="cite_ref-HM_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HM-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Qualifications">Qualifications</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Curse-Breaker?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Qualifications">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Curse-Breaker?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Qualifications"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>In order to qualify, an <a href="/wiki/Ordinary_Wizarding_Level" title="Ordinary Wizarding Level">O.W.L.</a> in <a href="/wiki/Arithmancy_(class)" title="Arithmancy (class)">Arithmancy</a> was required, as some banking and transaction would be done.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP29_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP29-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> Presumably, <a href="/wiki/Nastily_Exhausting_Wizarding_Test" title="Nastily Exhausting Wizarding Test">N.E.W.T.s</a> in <a href="/wiki/Defence_Against_the_Dark_Arts" title="Defence Against the Dark Arts">Defence Against the Dark Arts</a>, <a href="/wiki/Potions_(class)" title="Potions (class)">Potions</a>, <a href="/wiki/Transfiguration_(class)" title="Transfiguration (class)">Transfiguration</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Charms_(class)" title="Charms (class)">Charms</a> would be necessary, given the dangerous aspects of the job, as well as one in <a href="/wiki/Study_of_Ancient_Runes" title="Study of Ancient Runes">Ancient Runes</a>, however, this is unknown for certain. Based on the Gringotts' recruitment pamphlet, the career is meant for witches and wizards so adventurous they think that the perks of the job outweigh the risks involved. There was no official training programme proscribed for prospective applicants once they were hired by the bank; with the only training they got being on the job.<sup id="cite_ref-SQ_4-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-SQ-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> Consequently, professional curse-breakers have their own methods and ways of doing things, based on their experiences. He also noted that curse-breakers often had both physical and emotional challenges to contend with during their assignments.<sup id="cite_ref-HM623_5-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HM623-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
