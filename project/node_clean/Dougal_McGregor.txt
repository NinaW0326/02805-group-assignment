<b>Dougal McGregor</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggle</a>, son of <a href="/wiki/Dougal_McGregor%27s_father" title="Dougal McGregor&#39;s father">a farmer</a>.

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Dougal_McGregor?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Dougal_McGregor?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>He was handsome and intelligent. He and <a href="/wiki/Minerva_McGonagall" title="Minerva McGonagall">Minerva McGonagall</a> were briefly engaged in the summer of <a href="/wiki/1954" title="1954">1954</a>; he asked her to marry him and at first she accepted, but she later decided not to do it as she had not told him she was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch</a> and she thought it would mean the end of her career at the <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">Ministry of Magic</a>. She also remembered how it was hard for her mother when she married her <a href="/wiki/Minerva_McGonagall%27s_father" class="mw-redirect" title="Minerva McGonagall&#39;s father">muggle father,</a> whom she was close to. Dougal sent her several <a href="/wiki/Dougal_McGregor%27s_letters_to_Minerva_McGonagall" title="Dougal McGregor&#39;s letters to Minerva McGonagall">letters</a> trying to win her back, but with no success. He would later marry a <a href="/wiki/Dougal_McGregor%27s_wife" title="Dougal McGregor&#39;s wife">Muggle woman</a> who was the daughter of <a href="/wiki/Dougal_McGregor%27s_wife%27s_father" class="mw-redirect" title="Dougal McGregor&#39;s wife&#39;s father">another local farmer</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-pottermore_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-pottermore-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Dougal McGregor was murdered in a random anti-<a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggle</a> attack by the <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a> during the height of the <a href="/wiki/First_Wizarding_War" title="First Wizarding War">First Wizarding War</a> in the <a href="/wiki/1970s" title="1970s">1970s</a>, along with <a href="/wiki/Dougal_McGregor%27s_wife" title="Dougal McGregor&#39;s wife">his wife</a> and <a href="/wiki/Dougal_McGregor%27s_children" title="Dougal McGregor&#39;s children">children</a>. News of these events were a terrible blow to Minerva, who wondered if she could have been able to save him if she had married him.<sup id="cite_ref-shortstories_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-shortstories-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
