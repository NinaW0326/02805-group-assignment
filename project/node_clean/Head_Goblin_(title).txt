The&#160;<b>Head Goblin</b>&#160;was an occupational title denoting the <a href="/wiki/Goblin" title="Goblin">Goblin</a> that was in charge of all goblins at <a href="/wiki/Gringotts_Wizarding_Bank" title="Gringotts Wizarding Bank">Gringotts Wizarding Bank</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> presumably overseeing all of the bank's activities and affairs.&#160;
</p><p>One of the duties that comes with the position appeared to be the assignment of <a href="/wiki/Niffler" title="Niffler">Nifflers</a> to <a href="/wiki/Curse-Breaker" title="Curse-Breaker">Curse-Breakers</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
