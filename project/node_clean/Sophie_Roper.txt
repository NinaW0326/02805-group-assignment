<b>Sophie</b><sup id="cite_ref-pottermore_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-pottermore-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> <b>Roper</b> (c. <a href="/wiki/1979" title="1979">1979</a>-1980) was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch</a> who began attending <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> in <a href="/wiki/1991" title="1991">1991</a>. She was in the same year as <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> and was <a href="/wiki/Sorting_ceremony" title="Sorting ceremony">sorted</a> into either <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor" title="Gryffindor">Gryffindor</a>, <a href="/wiki/Hufflepuff" title="Hufflepuff">Hufflepuff</a> or <a href="/wiki/Slytherin" title="Slytherin">Slytherin</a> house.
</p>
