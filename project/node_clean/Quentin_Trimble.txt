<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Quentin_Trimble?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Quentin_Trimble?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Lifetime">Lifetime</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Quentin_Trimble?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Lifetime">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Quentin_Trimble?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Lifetime"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Quentin attended <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> in his youth, and was later appointed to the position of <a href="/wiki/Headmaster" title="Headmaster">Headmaster</a> of the school.<sup id="cite_ref-WWHP_3-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-WWHP-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> Sometime throughout his adulthood, Quentin (or, as he was known after his appointment as Headmaster, Professor Trimble) authored the book <i><a href="/wiki/The_Dark_Forces:_A_Guide_to_Self-Protection" title="The Dark Forces: A Guide to Self-Protection">The Dark Forces: A Guide to Self-Protection</a></i>, a required reading for any <a href="/wiki/Defence_Against_the_Dark_Arts" title="Defence Against the Dark Arts">Defence Against the Dark Arts</a> student.<sup id="cite_ref-PS_4-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PS-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-HPLE_5-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HPLE-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Post-mortem">Post-mortem</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Quentin_Trimble?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Post-mortem">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Quentin_Trimble?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Post-mortem"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<figure class="thumb tleft show-info-icon" style="width: 177px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/c/ca/Professor_Quentin_Trimble.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20100627211242" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/c/ca/Professor_Quentin_Trimble.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/175?cb=20100627211242" decoding="async" width="175" height="215" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="Professor Quentin Trimble.jpg" data-image-key="Professor_Quentin_Trimble.jpg" /></a> 	 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:Professor_Quentin_Trimble.jpg" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption">Professor Trimble's portrait in the <a href="/wiki/Headmaster%27s_office" class="mw-redirect" title="Headmaster&#39;s office">Headmaster's office</a></p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure>
<p>Trimble was no longer the Headteacher when <a href="/wiki/Armando_Dippet" title="Armando Dippet">Armando Dippet</a> took over in the early <a href="/wiki/20th_century" title="20th century">20th century</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup> In or prior to the end of the school term in <a href="/wiki/1986" title="1986">1986</a>, Professor Trimble passed away, and a <a href="/wiki/Portrait" title="Portrait">portrait</a> was hung of him at the <a href="/wiki/Headmaster%27s_office" class="mw-redirect" title="Headmaster&#39;s office">Headmaster's office</a> in <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Castle" title="Hogwarts Castle">Hogwarts Castle</a> so that he could offer advice and solace to future <a href="/wiki/Headmaster" title="Headmaster">Headmasters</a> of the school.<sup id="cite_ref-WWHP_3-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-WWHP-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> Unfortunately, whoever painted his name misspelled it, a mistake that was never fixed.<sup id="cite_ref-HPLE_5-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HPLE-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Magical_abilities_and_skills">Magical abilities and skills</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Quentin_Trimble?veaction=edit&amp;section=4" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Quentin_Trimble?action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li><b><a href="/wiki/Defence_Against_the_Dark_Arts" title="Defence Against the Dark Arts">Defence Against the Dark Arts</a></b>: Trimble was very knowledgeable in how to defend oneself from the <a href="/wiki/Dark_Arts" title="Dark Arts">Dark Arts</a>, as he authored <i><a href="/wiki/The_Dark_Forces:_A_Guide_to_Self-Protection" title="The Dark Forces: A Guide to Self-Protection">The Dark Forces: A Guide to Self-Protection</a></i>, an authoritative text in the subject. He had very good knowledge of defensive spells and <a href="/wiki/Dark_charm" title="Dark charm">Dark charms</a> and how to perform them, and was highly knowledgeable of various dark <a href="/wiki/Creature" title="Creature">creatures</a>, meaning he had likely experienced them himself.</li></ul>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Quentin_Trimble?veaction=edit&amp;section=5" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Quentin_Trimble?action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>"Trimble" is most likely a derivation of "tremble", meaning "to shake" - fitting considering his career as an author of books against the <a href="/wiki/Dark_Arts" title="Dark Arts">dark forces</a>. As the shaky <a href="/wiki/Quirinus_Quirrell" title="Quirinus Quirrell">Professor Quirrell</a> was the <a href="/wiki/Defence_Against_the_Dark_Arts" title="Defence Against the Dark Arts">Defence Against the Dark Arts</a> teacher in the year that <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> purchased Trimble’s book, it is likely to be a reference to that.</li></ul>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Quentin_Trimble?veaction=edit&amp;section=6" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Quentin_Trimble?action=edit&amp;section=6" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>Professor Trimble's name is considered to be misspelled "Quintin Trimble" on his <a href="/wiki/Portrait" title="Portrait">portrait</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_(film_series)" title="Harry Potter (film series)">films</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-HPLE_5-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HPLE-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> But the truth is that Quintin is a variant of spelling. It may be a hint to the fact that he also was the 15th Hogwarts headmaster (15th is 'quindecim' in Latin).</li></ul>
