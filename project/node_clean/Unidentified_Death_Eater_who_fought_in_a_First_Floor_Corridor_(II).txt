<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 2">"<i>Let's go!</i>"</span></dd>
<dd>&#8212;The Death Eater<sup class="noprint"><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Deathly_Hallows:_Part_2" title="Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 2">[src]</a></sup></dd>

<p>This <b>individual</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eater</a> who fought at the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a>. He was first shown with others, firing curses at the protective enchantments around <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Castle" title="Hogwarts Castle">Hogwarts Castle</a>. He later fought near the <a href="/wiki/Marble_Staircase" title="Marble Staircase">Marble Staircase</a>. Later, he was cornered while fighting in a <a href="/wiki/First-floor_corridor" title="First-floor corridor">first-floor corridor</a> while being hit with <a href="/wiki/Incarcerous_Spell" title="Incarcerous Spell">Incarcerous</a> cast by Harry Potter at the <a href="/wiki/Marble_Staircase" title="Marble Staircase">Marble Staircase</a>.
</p><p>He was later seems laying down next to the staircase, implying he had died from his injuries, or was <a href="/wiki/Stunning_Spell" title="Stunning Spell">unconscious</a>.
</p>
