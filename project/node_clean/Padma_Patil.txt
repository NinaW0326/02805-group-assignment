<p><b>Padma Patil</b> (b. <a href="/wiki/1979" title="1979">1979</a>/<a href="/wiki/1980" title="1980">1980</a>) was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch</a> of <a href="/wiki/India" title="India">Indian</a> heritage who was in the same year as the famous <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-PS7_13-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PS7-13">&#91;13&#93;</a></sup> She was the identical twin sister of <a href="/wiki/Parvati_Patil" title="Parvati Patil">Parvati Patil</a>. Padma was <a href="/wiki/Sorting_Hat" title="Sorting Hat">sorted</a> into <a href="/wiki/Ravenclaw" title="Ravenclaw">Ravenclaw</a> <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Houses" title="Hogwarts Houses">house</a> and became a <a href="/wiki/Prefect" title="Prefect">prefect</a> in her <a href="/wiki/1995%E2%80%931996_school_year" title="1995–1996 school year">fifth year</a>. She was also a member of <a href="/wiki/Dumbledore%27s_Army" title="Dumbledore&#39;s Army">Dumbledore's Army</a>, an organisation taught and led by <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-OTP_4-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OTP-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> She later bravely fought in the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a> in <a href="/wiki/1998" title="1998">1998</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-DH_14-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH-14">&#91;14&#93;</a></sup> Padma survived the final battle of the <a href="/wiki/Second_Wizarding_War" title="Second Wizarding War">Second Wizarding War</a> and graduated from school.<sup id="cite_ref-DHF2_15-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DHF2-15">&#91;15&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Padma_Patil?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Padma_Patil?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_life">Early life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Padma_Patil?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Early life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Padma_Patil?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Early life"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Parvati and Padma were both born somewhere between <a href="/wiki/1979" title="1979">1979</a> and <a href="/wiki/1980" title="1980">1980</a>. They were probably raised in a wizarding household.<sup id="cite_ref-upbringing_16-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-upbringing-16">&#91;16&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span id="Hogwarts_years_(1991-1998)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="Hogwarts_years_.281991-1998.29">Hogwarts years <span class="template-c">(1991-1998)</span></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Padma_Patil?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Hogwarts years (1991-1998)">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Padma_Patil?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Hogwarts years (1991-1998)"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>In <a href="/wiki/1991" title="1991">1991</a>, Padma began attending <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> and was sorted into <a href="/wiki/Ravenclaw" title="Ravenclaw">Ravenclaw</a> house and <a href="/wiki/Parvati_Patil" title="Parvati Patil">Parvati</a> was sorted into <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor" title="Gryffindor">Gryffindor</a>, separating the two.<sup id="cite_ref-PS7_13-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PS7-13">&#91;13&#93;</a></sup> Padma shared a dormitory with <a href="/wiki/Mandy_Brocklehurst" title="Mandy Brocklehurst">Mandy Brocklehurst</a>, <a href="/wiki/Lisa_Turpin" title="Lisa Turpin">Lisa Turpin</a>, <a href="/wiki/Morag_MacDougal" title="Morag MacDougal">Morag MacDougal</a> and <a href="/wiki/Sue_Li" title="Sue Li">Sue Li</a>. It is likely that Padma and Parvati both knew <a href="/wiki/Pansy_Parkinson" title="Pansy Parkinson">Pansy Parkinson</a> before starting their education due to the way Pansy addressed her sister.<sup id="cite_ref-PS9_17-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PS9-17">&#91;17&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Fourth_year">Fourth year</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Padma_Patil?veaction=edit&amp;section=4" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Fourth year">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Padma_Patil?action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Fourth year"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<div class="quote">
<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire"><b>Padma</b>: "<i>Are you going to ask me to dance at all?</i>"</span></dd>
<dd><b>Ron Weasley</b>: "<i>No.</i>"</dd>
<dd><b>Padma</b>: "<i>Fine.</i>"</dd>
<dd>&#8212; Padma's unsuccessful date with Ron at the Yule Ball<sup class="noprint"><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Goblet_of_Fire" title="Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire">[src]</a></sup></dd></dl>
</div>
<p>Padma's fourth year at Hogwarts school would play host to the <a href="/wiki/Triwizard_Tournament" title="Triwizard Tournament">Triwizard Tournament</a>, where students from three schools competed against one another. Normally three finalists are selected from the Goblet of Fire, but this year four students were selected, two from Hogwarts: <a href="/wiki/Cedric_Diggory" title="Cedric Diggory">Cedric Diggory</a> and <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-GOF_5-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<figure class="thumb tleft show-info-icon" style="width: 243px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/5/52/YuleBallPatils.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20180319051424" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/5/52/YuleBallPatils.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/241?cb=20180319051424" decoding="async" width="241" height="109" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="YuleBallPatils.jpg" data-image-key="YuleBallPatils.jpg" /></a> 	 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:YuleBallPatils.jpg" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption">Parvati, Harry, Ron, and Padma at the Yule Ball</p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure>
<p>As part of the tournament, a traditional Yule Ball is held by the hosting school, Padma attended the Yule Ball with <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ron Weasley</a>, at the urging of Parvati (Harry's date) when Harry begged her to find a date for Ron. Padma, however, was less than enthusiastic at having Ron as a partner, since he refused to dance with her and instead spent the evening stewing in jealousy over <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione</a>'s date with <a href="/wiki/Viktor_Krum" title="Viktor Krum">Viktor Krum</a>. Padma wore turquoise dress robes for the occasion. Due to both Harry and Ron showing a lack of interest in the twins, Padma and Parvati both abandoned their dates and danced with some boys from <a href="/wiki/Beauxbatons_Academy_of_Magic" title="Beauxbatons Academy of Magic">Beauxbatons</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-GOF_5-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Personality_and_traits">Personality and traits</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Padma_Patil?veaction=edit&amp;section=10" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Personality and traits">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Padma_Patil?action=edit&amp;section=10" title="Edit section: Personality and traits"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<div class="quote">
<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire">"<i>She didn’t look too enthusiastic about having Ron as a partner, though; her dark eyes lingered on the frayed neck and sleeves of his dress robes as she looked him up and down.</i>"</span></dd>
<dd>&#8212;Padma Patil's lack of enthusiasm for having Ron as her Yule Ball date<sup class="noprint"><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Goblet_of_Fire" title="Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire">[src]</a></sup></dd>
<dd></dd></dl></div>
<p>Padma seemed to be the less outgoing and more studious of the twins. She was sorted into <a href="/wiki/Ravenclaw" title="Ravenclaw">Ravenclaw</a>, a mark of her intelligence. Padma was not yet asked to the Yule Ball by the time she was fixed up with Ron Weasley. Despite her physical beauty the boys in her year did not seem to be interested in her, not even Dean Thomas who seemed to have attended the ball alone. Padma was also made a <a href="/wiki/Prefect" title="Prefect">prefect</a>, something her sister never achieved, making her appear better trusted for the position by her teachers than her sister. She was also much quieter than her sister Parvati.<sup id="cite_ref-OTP_4-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OTP-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> She was very annoyed when Parvati began dancing with another male Hogwartian, but when Ron didn't want to dance with her, she deserted him as well, something <a href="/wiki/Luna_Lovegood" title="Luna Lovegood">Luna Lovegood</a> later found hard to understand her reasoning behind. Her desertion of Ron after seeming angry that her sister deserted Harry made her slightly hypocritical.<sup id="cite_ref-GOF_5-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>In spite of her flaws, Padma, much like her Gryffindor sister, was a courageous and determined individual, being unafraid to disobey and stand up to Dolores Umbridge's tyranny,<sup id="cite_ref-OTP_4-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OTP-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-OOTPG_18-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTPG-18">&#91;18&#93;</a></sup> and later bravely fought in the Battle of Hogwarts against the <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a> and survived.<sup id="cite_ref-DH_14-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH-14">&#91;14&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Magical_abilities_and_skills">Magical abilities and skills</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Padma_Patil?veaction=edit&amp;section=11" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Padma_Patil?action=edit&amp;section=11" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li><b><a href="/wiki/Defence_Against_the_Dark_Arts" title="Defence Against the Dark Arts">Defence Against the Dark Arts</a></b>: Padma along with her sister were loyal and devoted members of <a href="/wiki/Dumbledore%27s_Army" title="Dumbledore&#39;s Army">Dumbledore's Army</a>, where she learned how to cast numerous defensive and offensive <a href="/wiki/Spell" title="Spell">spells</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-OTP_4-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OTP-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Potion" title="Potion">Potions</a></b>: In <a href="/wiki/1996" title="1996">1996</a>, Padma and <a href="/wiki/Parvati_Patil" title="Parvati Patil">Parvati</a> successfully brewed <a href="/wiki/Babbling_Beverage" title="Babbling Beverage">Babbling Beverage</a> to disturb <a href="/wiki/Dolores_Umbridge" title="Dolores Umbridge">Dolores Umbridge</a>'s loudspeakers.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTPG_18-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTPG-18">&#91;18&#93;</a></sup> Unlike her sister, she took N.E.W.T. classes in this <a href="/wiki/Potions_(class)" title="Potions (class)">subject</a> in her sixth year. This indicated she earned either an '<a href="/wiki/Outstanding" title="Outstanding">Outstanding</a>' or '<a href="/wiki/Exceeds_Expectations" title="Exceeds Expectations">Exceeds Expectations</a>' <a href="/wiki/Ordinary_Wizarding_Level" title="Ordinary Wizarding Level">O.W.L.</a> in the subject, showing her proficiency in this field.<sup id="cite_ref-HBPF_19-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBPF-19">&#91;19&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Duelling" title="Duelling">Duelling</a></b>: Padma would have been a very skilled and accomplished duellist, as she was taught how to duel as a member of Dumbledore's Army<sup id="cite_ref-OTP_4-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OTP-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> and later survived the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a> in <a href="/wiki/1997" title="1997">1997</a>, showing her excellent skills in <a href="/wiki/Martial_magic" title="Martial magic">martial magic</a>, given her ability to fight successfully against the <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a> and survive.<sup id="cite_ref-DH_14-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH-14">&#91;14&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Charm" title="Charm">Charms</a></b>: Padma was capable of conjuring a non-corporeal <a href="/wiki/Patronus_Charm" title="Patronus Charm">Patronus</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-LEGOY2_12-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-LEGOY2-12">&#91;12&#93;</a></sup> which is a mark of superior <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magical</a> ability and talent, given the immense difficulty of casting such an advanced and powerful protective charm. She presumably learnt how to perform this charm with Dumbledore's Army.</li></ul>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Possessions">Possessions</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Padma_Patil?veaction=edit&amp;section=12" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Possessions">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Padma_Patil?action=edit&amp;section=12" title="Edit section: Possessions"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li><b><a href="/wiki/Wand" title="Wand">Wand</a></b>: Padma owned a wand, that was of an unknown length, <a href="/wiki/Wand_wood" title="Wand wood">wood</a> and <a href="/wiki/Wand_core" title="Wand core">core</a> material. She presumably purchased it from <a href="/wiki/Ollivanders" title="Ollivanders">Ollivanders</a>, <a href="/wiki/Diagon_Alley" title="Diagon Alley">Diagon Alley</a>, before starting Hogwarts in <a href="/wiki/1991" title="1991">1991</a>, and wielded her wand throughout her school life.</li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Padma_Patil%27s_dress_robes" title="Padma Patil&#39;s dress robes">Dress robes</a></b>: Along with her sister, Padma owned dress robes for which she wore to formal occasions and celebrations, such as for the <a href="/wiki/Yule_Ball" title="Yule Ball">Yule Ball</a> in <a href="/wiki/1994" title="1994">1994</a>. They were turquoise in colour.<sup id="cite_ref-GOF_5-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Prefect_badge" title="Prefect badge">Prefect badge</a></b>: Padma owned a Ravenclaw Prefect Badge, due to her becoming a <a href="/wiki/Prefect" title="Prefect">Prefect</a> in her <a href="/wiki/Fifth_year" title="Fifth year">fifth year</a> in <a href="/wiki/1995" title="1995">1995</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-OTP_4-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OTP-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup></li></ul>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Padma_Patil?veaction=edit&amp;section=13" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Padma_Patil?action=edit&amp;section=13" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The Patils' names and appearance in the films imply that the characters are of Indian descent. <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Patil_(surname)" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Patil (surname)">Patil</a> is a common Marathi surname in the state of Maharashtra, India. It signifies that at least one of Padma's ancestors was a village head in western India. In Sanskrit, Padma means "lotus," which symbolises divinity and purity, because of its nature and birth in dirty mud water. Also, Padma is a by-name for the Hindu goddess of prosperity, Lakshmi, the consort of the Hindu god of protection, Lord Vishnu. Rowling initially intended her and her sister to bear forenames beginning with Ma. Their surname on Rowling's class list is also spelled as "<a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Patel" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Patel">Patel</a>", which is another version of the name Patil but from a different region of India (Gujarat); this surname represents a community of agriculturalists and merchants.
</p>

<li>The Patils are not portrayed as identical twins in the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_(film_series)" title="Harry Potter (film series)">films</a>, but rather fraternal twins who look alike in some ways, as the actresses who play the girls are not sisters in real life. Afshan Azad (Padma) speaks in a Mancunian accent from Manchester, while Shefali Chowdhury (Patil) normally speak in a Brummie accent as she grew up in Birmingham. To combine the two, they speak in a general Northern English accent, but still distinguishable from a Yorkshire accent <a href="/wiki/Neville_Longbottom" title="Neville Longbottom">(Neville</a>).</li>
