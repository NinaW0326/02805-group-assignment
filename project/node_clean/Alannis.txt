<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Alannis?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Alannis?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Alannis was sorted into <a href="/wiki/Ravenclaw" title="Ravenclaw">Ravenclaw</a> when she arrived at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a>. In <a href="/wiki/1996" title="1996">1996</a> Alannis became captain for <a href="/wiki/Ravenclaw" title="Ravenclaw">Ravenclaw</a> when the <a href="/wiki/Duelling_Club" title="Duelling Club">Duelling Club</a> was revived by <a href="/wiki/Albus_Dumbledore" title="Albus Dumbledore">Albus Dumbledore</a> as a precaution to help students defend themselves if the event of an attack from <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a> or <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a> was ever to happen. <a href="/wiki/Minerva_McGonagall" title="Minerva McGonagall">Minerva McGonagall</a> convinced <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> to join the Ravenclaw Duelling Club, introducing him to Alannis, who requested that he showed a group of watching Ravenclaws how to cast <i><a href="/wiki/Levicorpus" title="Levicorpus">Levicorpus</a></i>. After her duel with Harry she jokingly told him to watch his back as other Ravenclaws might hit him with their newly learnt <a href="/wiki/Jinx" title="Jinx">jinx</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-HBPG_3-11" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBPG-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Magical_abilities_and_skills">Magical abilities and skills</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Alannis?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Alannis?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li><b><a href="/wiki/Duelling" title="Duelling">Duelling</a></b>: As head of the <a href="/wiki/Ravenclaw" title="Ravenclaw">Ravenclaw</a> <a href="/wiki/Duelling_Club" title="Duelling Club">Duelling Club</a>, Alannis was a highly skilled duellist, who was proficient with the casting of various offensive and defensive spells, such as the <a href="/wiki/Stunning_Spell" title="Stunning Spell">Stunning Spell</a>, <a href="/wiki/Shield_Charm" title="Shield Charm">Shield Charm</a>, <a href="/wiki/Disarming_Charm" title="Disarming Charm">Disarming Charm</a> and <i><a href="/wiki/Levicorpus" title="Levicorpus">Levicorpus</a></i>, showing she was talented with <a href="/wiki/Martial_magic" title="Martial magic">Martial magic</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-HBPG_3-12" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBPG-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup></li></ul>
