<b>Mrs McGregor</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggle</a> woman who resided in <a href="/wiki/Caithness" title="Caithness">Caithness</a>, <a href="/wiki/Scotland" title="Scotland">Scotland</a>. She was the daughter of a local farmer, and married the son of <a href="/wiki/Dougal_McGregor%27s_father" title="Dougal McGregor&#39;s father">another farmer</a>, <a href="/wiki/Dougal_McGregor" title="Dougal McGregor">Dougal McGregor</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-pottermore_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-pottermore-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p><a href="/wiki/Dougal_McGregor" title="Dougal McGregor">Dougal McGregor</a> and his wife were murdered in a random anti-<a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggle</a> attack by the Death Eaters during the height of the <a href="/wiki/First_Wizarding_War" title="First Wizarding War">First Wizarding War</a> in the <a href="/wiki/1970s" title="1970s">1970s</a>, along with their <a href="/wiki/Dougal_McGregor%27s_children" title="Dougal McGregor&#39;s children">children</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-shortstories_4-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-shortstories-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
