<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Piers_Polkiss%27s_mother?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Piers_Polkiss%27s_mother?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>She brought her son to <a href="/wiki/4_Privet_Drive" title="4 Privet Drive">4 Privet Drive</a> for a birthday outing with <a href="/wiki/Dudley_Dursley" title="Dudley Dursley">Dudley Dursley</a> in <a href="/wiki/1991" title="1991">1991</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-PS2_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PS2-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> In <a href="/wiki/1995" title="1995">1995</a>, Dudley told his mother, <a href="/wiki/Petunia_Dursley" title="Petunia Dursley">Petunia</a>, that he was going out for <a href="/wiki/Tea" title="Tea">tea</a> at the Polkisses', when in fact he was planning to cause trouble with his <a href="/wiki/Dudley_Dursley%27s_gang" title="Dudley Dursley&#39;s gang">gang of friends</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP1_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP1-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>As <a href="/wiki/Vernon_Dursley" title="Vernon Dursley">Vernon Dursley</a>'s initial reaction to the aftermath of Dudley's Dementor attack was that Mrs Polkiss had served something foreign for tea,<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP2_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP2-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> it is a possibility that Mrs Polkiss has a fondness for international dishes. However, it is more likely that this comment was merely an example of Vernon's ignorance, and completely unrelated to anything that Vernon may have known about Mrs Polkiss.
</p>
