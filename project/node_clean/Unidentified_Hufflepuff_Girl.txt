This <b>girl</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch</a> who attended <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> in the <a href="/wiki/1990s" title="1990s">1990s</a>. She was sorted into <a href="/wiki/Hufflepuff" title="Hufflepuff">Hufflepuff</a> in a unknown year. In <a href="/wiki/1995" title="1995">1995</a>, she, along with <a href="/wiki/Cho_Chang" title="Cho Chang">Cho Chang</a> and <a href="/wiki/Fred_Weasley" title="Fred Weasley">Fred</a> and <a href="/wiki/George_Weasley" title="George Weasley">George Weasley</a>, watched <a href="/wiki/Argus_Filch" title="Argus Filch">Argus Filch</a> hammer notices into a wall, displaying <a href="/wiki/Dolores_Umbridge" title="Dolores Umbridge">Dolores Umbridge</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Educational_Decree" title="Educational Decree">Educational Decrees</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTPF_2-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTPF-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
