<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_third_year_boy?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_third_year_boy?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<div class="quote">
<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;Harry Potter: Hogwarts Mystery">"<i>I can't believe they even let you in here after your mental brother got expelled, and ruined [house]'s reputation.</i>"</span></dd>
<dd>&#8212;This boy to <a href="/wiki/Jacob%27s_sibling" title="Jacob&#39;s sibling">Jacob's sibling</a> on the latter's first day of school.<sup class="noprint"><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter:_Hogwarts_Mystery" title="Harry Potter: Hogwarts Mystery">[src]</a></sup></dd>
<dd></dd></dl></div><p>This wizard started attending Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry in <a href="/wiki/1982" title="1982">1982</a> and was <a href="/wiki/Sorting_ceremony" title="Sorting ceremony">sorted</a> in the same house as <a href="/wiki/Jacob_(Hogwarts_student)" title="Jacob (Hogwarts student)">Jacob</a>, a student who was expelled while looking for the <a href="/wiki/Cursed_Vaults" title="Cursed Vaults">Cursed Vaults</a>. The boy disliked Jacob to the extent that he considered him to have ruined their house's reputation.<sup id="cite_ref-HM1_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HM1-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>In his <a href="/wiki/Third_year" title="Third year">third year</a>, the <a href="/wiki/Jacob%27s_sibling" title="Jacob&#39;s sibling">younger sibling</a> of Jacob got sorted into the same house as him. Shortly after the Sorting ceremony, he confronted Jacob's sibling in their <a href="/wiki/Common_room" title="Common room">common room</a> and expressed his dislike towards the sibling being in the same house as him. <a href="/wiki/Rowan_Khanna" title="Rowan Khanna">Rowan Khanna</a> defended them. The boy then left, telling the two he would keep an eye on them.<sup id="cite_ref-HM1_2-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HM1-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
