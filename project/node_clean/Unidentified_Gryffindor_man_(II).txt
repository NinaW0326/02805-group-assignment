<p>This <b>individual</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> who, during his lifetime, was <a href="/wiki/Head_of_Gryffindor_House" title="Head of Gryffindor House">Head of Gryffindor House</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-MHP_3-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-MHP-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>A portrait of this wizard hung in <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor_Tower" title="Gryffindor Tower">Gryffindor Tower</a>. He seemed to spend most of his time sleeping, although he was willing to speak to students if they woke him up. In <a href="/wiki/1994" title="1994">1994</a> when <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> opened his <a href="/wiki/Golden_egg" title="Golden egg">golden egg</a> he was terrified and was seen holding his hands over his ears. In <a href="/wiki/1995" title="1995">1995</a>, the <a href="/wiki/Golden_Snitch" title="Golden Snitch">Golden Snitch</a> from the <a href="/wiki/Unidentified_Gryffindor_seeker" title="Unidentified Gryffindor seeker">Quidditch Player</a>'s portrait on the opposite wall made its way into the background of his picture, much to the <a href="/wiki/Seeker" title="Seeker">Seeker</a>'s annoyance. He seemed to have known <a href="/wiki/James_and_Lily_Potter" title="James and Lily Potter">James and Lily Potter</a> when they were at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts</a>, as he spoke of their personalities and behaviour, and remarked on their son Harry's similarities in appearance.
</p>
