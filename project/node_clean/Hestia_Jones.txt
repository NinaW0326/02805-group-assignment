</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Hestia_Jones?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Hestia_Jones?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_life">Early life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Hestia_Jones?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Early life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Hestia_Jones?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Early life"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Hestia Jones was born somewhere in the <a href="/wiki/Great_Britain" title="Great Britain">British Isles</a> no later than the year of <a href="/wiki/1978" title="1978">1978</a>, into a wizarding family of unknown degree. She presumably purchased a <a href="/wiki/Wand" title="Wand">wand</a> from <a href="/wiki/Ollivander%27s_Wand_Shop" class="mw-redirect" title="Ollivander&#39;s Wand Shop">Ollivander's Wand Shop</a> at the age of eleven and attended <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> during her formative years like most magical children in the wizarding community. Since she did not appear in the photography or is otherwise indicated of having been a member of the original Order, she might have been too young at the time, and possibly attended school during or after the <a href="/wiki/First_Wizarding_War" title="First Wizarding War">First Wizarding War</a>.
</p>
<h3><span id="Second_Wizarding_War_(1995-1998)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="Second_Wizarding_War_.281995-1998.29">Second Wizarding War <span class="template-c">(1995-1998)</span></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Hestia_Jones?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Second Wizarding War (1995-1998)">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Hestia_Jones?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Second Wizarding War (1995-1998)"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Hestia joined the reconstituted <a href="/wiki/Order_of_the_Phoenix" title="Order of the Phoenix">Order of the Phoenix</a> after the return of <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a>. She presumably fought in several battles during the <a href="/wiki/Second_Wizarding_War" title="Second Wizarding War">Second Wizarding War</a>, including the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a>.
</p>
<figure class="thumb tleft show-info-icon" style="width: 232px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/2/22/B5C3M1_Advance_Guard_at_Privet_Drive.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20180424050733" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/2/22/B5C3M1_Advance_Guard_at_Privet_Drive.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/230?cb=20180424050733" decoding="async" width="230" height="90" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="B5C3M1 Advance Guard at Privet Drive.jpg" data-image-key="B5C3M1_Advance_Guard_at_Privet_Drive.jpg" /></a> 	 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:B5C3M1_Advance_Guard_at_Privet_Drive.jpg" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption">The <a href="/wiki/Advance_Guard" title="Advance Guard">Advance Guard</a> assigned to protect Harry Potter</p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure>
<p>She was among the members of the <a href="/wiki/Advance_Guard" title="Advance Guard">Advance Guard</a>, a group of members of the Order who had volunteered to escort <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> safely from the house of his aunt and uncle at <a href="/wiki/4_Privet_Drive" title="4 Privet Drive">4 Privet Drive</a> in <a href="/wiki/Surrey" title="Surrey">Surrey</a> to their headquarters at <a href="/wiki/12_Grimmauld_Place" title="12 Grimmauld Place">12 Grimmauld Place</a> in <a href="/wiki/London" title="London">London</a>. Whilst examining the <a href="/wiki/Dursley_family" title="Dursley family">Dursley</a>'s kitchen, she came across a <a href="/wiki/Potato" title="Potato">potato</a> peeler in a drawer and found it was immensely funny. A few days later, Hestia borrowed Alastor Moody's invisibility cloak after relieving <a href="/wiki/Kingsley_Shacklebolt" title="Kingsley Shacklebolt">Kingsley Shacklebolt</a> of guard duty and stood watch over the entrance of the <a href="/wiki/Department_of_Mysteries" title="Department of Mysteries">Department of Mysteries</a> in order to prevent <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a> from entering the area to steal a <a href="/wiki/Sybill_Trelawney%27s_first_prophecy" title="Sybill Trelawney&#39;s first prophecy">prophecy</a> <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a> wished to get a hold of.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP3_6-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP3-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>In <a href="/wiki/1997" title="1997">1997</a>, she escorted the Dursleys from their home to a safe location. Hestia was surprised when she saw that the <a href="/wiki/Dursley_family" title="Dursley family">Dursleys</a> did not care about Harry at all and also got very angry when they said that they did not know where Harry was about to go. It can be assumed she played a role in ensuring the continued safety at the safe house for them.<sup id="cite_ref-DH3_7-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH3-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>When <a href="/wiki/Calamity" title="Calamity">The Calamity</a> broke out in the late <a href="/wiki/2010s" title="2010s">2010s</a>, Hestia Jones, along with former members of the Order, played a part in helping members of the <a href="/wiki/Statute_of_Secrecy_Task_Force" title="Statute of Secrecy Task Force">Statute of Secrecy Task Force</a> to prepare for the challenges they would be facing in containing the situation. In addition to holding a workshop that members of the aforementioned task force attended, to whom she demonstrated a 'rigorous methodology' for how to cast <a href="/wiki/Hex" title="Hex">hexes</a> and <a href="/wiki/Charm" title="Charm">charms</a> with 'perfect form.'<sup id="cite_ref-WU_8-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-WU-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup> She was also at some point invited by <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> to the <a href="/wiki/Auror_Office" title="Auror Office">Auror Office</a> to hold a special lesson for those members of the S.O.S. pursuing a future career as an Auror where she taught them how to maintain one's <a href="/wiki/Shield_Charm" title="Shield Charm">Shield Charm</a> against an onslaught, such as a barrage of successive spells or one, big, immensely destructive one.<sup id="cite_ref-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Physical_appearance">Physical appearance</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Hestia_Jones?veaction=edit&amp;section=4" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Physical appearance">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Hestia_Jones?action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Physical appearance"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Hestia was described as a young, black-haired woman with pink cheeks.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP3_6-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP3-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Personality_and_traits">Personality and traits</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Hestia_Jones?veaction=edit&amp;section=5" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Personality and traits">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Hestia_Jones?action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Personality and traits"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Hestia was a good, kind person who liked Harry Potter and was shocked when she learned that his uncle, aunt, and cousin did not know where he was going in 1997. She was also quite a tactful person, suggesting that Dedalus and herself should wait outside whilst Harry and the Dursleys said goodbye to each other. This shows that she did no know much about Harry's relationship with the Dursleys as well as the fact that she was quite a caring person.<sup id="cite_ref-DH3_7-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH3-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup> She also appeared to be rather intelligent, cleverly seeing past the <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">Ministry of Magic</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Campaign_to_discredit_Albus_Dumbledore_and_Harry_Potter" title="Campaign to discredit Albus Dumbledore and Harry Potter">denial of the Dark Lord's return</a>, as well as very brave in the face of adversity, siding with <a href="/wiki/Albus_Dumbledore" title="Albus Dumbledore">Albus Dumbledore</a> and the rest of those who had rallied to his side to oppose <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a> at a great personal risk, and despite the very real possibility that the Ministry of Magic would go out of its way to tarnish her reputation if her allegiance to Dumbledore became known outside of the Order. She was also one of the few who was unafraid to call Voldemort by his true name. In her work for the Order of the Phoenix, Hestia proved herself to be no less brave, dependable and selfless than anyone else in the Order, displaying great bravery and resilience through her participation in the war effort and an admirable selfless willingness to put her own life on the line for the sake of following through with the Order's resistance movements against the Dark Forces.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Magical_abilities_and_skills">Magical abilities and skills</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Hestia_Jones?veaction=edit&amp;section=6" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Hestia_Jones?action=edit&amp;section=6" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>A member of the reconstituted <a href="/wiki/Order_of_the_Phoenix" title="Order of the Phoenix">Order of the Phoenix</a>, Hestia was an accomplished witch, with sufficient ability to fulfil her duties satisfactorily and do solid work for the Order.
</p>
<ul><li><b><a href="/wiki/Duelling" title="Duelling">Duelling</a></b>: Hestia was an accomplished duellist, being one of the members of the Order on rotation that were entrusted the task of guarding the entrance of the <a href="/wiki/Department_of_Mysteries" title="Department of Mysteries">Department of Mysteries</a>, in which capacity she was expected to prevent <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a> from breaking in to steal the prophecy, which would have required her to engage them in a duel if they had done so on her watch. She was also one of the volunteers that joined the  participate in the escort of Harry Potter on broomsticks, hinting her ability to handle herself in combat to be good enough to do so even during mid-flight, which is noted to be no small feat. Excelling in both defensive and offensive combative magic, in a duel, Hestia would employ a rigorous methodology to her spell-casting, increasing the amount of damage she could inflict against her adversaries in combat. On the flip side, she was also rather good at defensive spell work, as she was also able to maintain a <a href="/wiki/Shield_Charm" title="Shield Charm">Shield Charm</a>, even under an onslaught. These were skills that she would eventually pass on to those of the members of the then recently established <a href="/wiki/Statute_of_Secrecy_Task_Force" title="Statute of Secrecy Task Force">Statute of Secrecy Task Force</a> who pursued a career as an <a href="/wiki/Auror" title="Auror">Auror</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-WU_8-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-WU-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Charm" title="Charm">Charms</a></b>: Hestia was competent and experienced with the casting of protective charms, as she was able to successfully teach trainee <a href="/wiki/Auror" title="Auror">Aurors</a> how to perform numerous defensive charms, most notably the <a href="/wiki/Shield_Charm" title="Shield Charm">Shield Charm</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-WU_8-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-WU-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup> In addition, Hestia was capable of casting a non-corporeal <a href="/wiki/Patronus_Charm" title="Patronus Charm">Patronus Charm</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-LEGOY2_5-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-LEGOY2-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> a mark of superior <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magical</a> ability and talent given the immense difficulty of casting such a powerful protective charm.</li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Flight" title="Flight">Flying</a></b>: Hestia was an accomplished <a href="/wiki/Broomstick" title="Broomstick">broomstick</a> flyer; she was a member of the <a href="/wiki/Advance_Guard" title="Advance Guard">Advance Guard</a> who escorted Harry Potter to 12 Grimmauld Place via broomstick flight.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP3_6-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP3-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup> Also as seen during the instance where she escorted Harry alongside her allies, Hestia was a sufficiently skilled flyer to have confidence in her ability to handle potential attacks while flying, which even fellow Order member <a href="/wiki/Nymphadora_Tonks" title="Nymphadora Tonks">Nymphadora Tonks</a>, who was a trained <a href="/wiki/Auror" title="Auror">Auror</a>, remarked to be very difficult.<sup id="cite_ref-DH5_10-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH5-10">&#91;10&#93;</a></sup></li></ul>

<p>Hestia did not have a good start to her relationship with the Dursleys, and she rather disliked them for their ignorance and selfishness. She was surprised and very angry, when she saw that they did not love Harry at all and that they did not care about where he would go after he would leave their house.<sup id="cite_ref-DH3_7-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH3-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup> However, the relationship between Hestia and the Dursleys may have improved as they were hidden from <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a> together, but this is highly unlikely because the Dursleys were <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggles</a> who despised and feared <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magic</a> and <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">Wizardkind</a>. The Dursleys only maintained a stable relationship with Harry at the very end and it was <a href="/wiki/Dudley_Dursley" title="Dudley Dursley">Dudley</a> who became kinder, expressing his thanks to Harry for saving his life.

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Hestia_Jones?veaction=edit&amp;section=11" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Hestia_Jones?action=edit&amp;section=11" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Hestia is named after the Greek goddess Hestia, who is the goddess of the hearth and of the right ordering of domesticity and the family. The link between Hestia Jones and the Greek goddess Hestia is cleverly underlined by <a href="/wiki/J._K._Rowling" title="J. K. Rowling">Rowling</a> in <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Order_of_the_Phoenix" title="Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix">Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix</a></i>, as during Harry's introduction to Hestia, she waves from next to the toaster.
</p>
