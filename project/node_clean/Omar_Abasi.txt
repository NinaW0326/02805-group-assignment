<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Omar_Abasi?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Omar_Abasi?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_career">Early career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Omar_Abasi?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Early career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Omar_Abasi?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Early career"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>At some point in his life, Omar successfully underwent the required tutelage to become a fully-fledged <a href="/wiki/Healer" title="Healer">Healer</a> and were eventually successful at obtaining a position at <a href="/wiki/St_Mungo%27s_Hospital_for_Magical_Maladies_and_Injuries" title="St Mungo&#39;s Hospital for Magical Maladies and Injuries">St Mungo's Hospital for Magical Maladies and Injuries</a> in <a href="/wiki/London" title="London">London</a>, where he worked in the <a href="/wiki/%22Dangerous%22_Dai_Llewellyn_Ward_for_Serious_Bites" title="&quot;Dangerous&quot; Dai Llewellyn Ward for Serious Bites">"Dangerous" Dai Llewellyn Ward for Serious Bites</a> on the first floor, specialising in creature-induced injuries.<sup id="cite_ref-FBC1_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-FBC1-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Working_with_the_Ministry">Working with the Ministry</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Omar_Abasi?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Working with the Ministry">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Omar_Abasi?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Working with the Ministry"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>At some point in his career, Omar was drafted in for conjecture by the <a href="/wiki/Beast_Division" title="Beast Division">Beast Division</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Department_for_the_Regulation_and_Control_of_Magical_Creatures" title="Department for the Regulation and Control of Magical Creatures">Department for the Regulation and Control of Magical Creatures</a> at the <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">Ministry of Magic</a>, wherein he would lend its investigators his expertise and give his opinion on cases they were working on. At one point, he attended to <a href="/wiki/Chudley_Cannons" title="Chudley Cannons">Chudley Cannons</a>' <a href="/wiki/Keeper" title="Keeper">keeper</a> <a href="/wiki/Gordon_Horton" title="Gordon Horton">Gordon Horton</a> after he was attacked at the <a href="/wiki/Chudley_Cannons%27s_training_ground" class="mw-redirect" title="Chudley Cannons&#39;s training ground">Chudley Cannons's training ground</a> and diagnosed him with a mild concussion. He also noted that Horton's kit had been badly torn, likely by sharp claws.<sup id="cite_ref-FBC1_2-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-FBC1-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>His observation was indeed correct, as the investigators from the <a href="/wiki/Department_for_the_Regulation_and_Control_of_Magical_Creatures" title="Department for the Regulation and Control of Magical Creatures">Department for the Regulation and Control of Magical Creatures</a> discovered Mr Horton had been attacked by a <a href="/wiki/Hippogriff" title="Hippogriff">Hippogriff</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-FBC1_2-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-FBC1-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>He later used the <a href="/wiki/Memory_Charm" title="Memory Charm">Memory Charm</a> to erase the memories of the <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggle</a> <a href="/wiki/Laura_Thorn" title="Laura Thorn">Laura Thorn</a> after she had found about a <a href="/wiki/Mountain_troll" title="Mountain troll">Mountain troll</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-FBC2_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-FBC2-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Magical_abilities_and_skills">Magical abilities and skills</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Omar_Abasi?veaction=edit&amp;section=4" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Omar_Abasi?action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li><b><a href="/wiki/Healing_magic" title="Healing magic">Healing magic</a></b>: As a professional <a href="/wiki/Healer" title="Healer">Healer</a>, he was highly adept and knowledgeable in the field of healing magic. He was an expert at dealing with <a href="/wiki/Creature" title="Creature">creature</a>-induced injuries.<sup id="cite_ref-FBC1_2-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-FBC1-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> In order to have become a Healer, he would have had at least five <a href="/wiki/Nastily_Exhausting_Wizarding_Test" title="Nastily Exhausting Wizarding Test">N.E.W.T.s</a> in the subjects of <a href="/wiki/Charms_(class)" title="Charms (class)">Charms</a>, <a href="/wiki/Transfiguration_(class)" title="Transfiguration (class)">Transfiguration</a>, <a href="/wiki/Herbology" title="Herbology">Herbology</a>, <a href="/wiki/Potions_(class)" title="Potions (class)">Potions</a> and <a href="/wiki/Defence_Against_the_Dark_Arts" title="Defence Against the Dark Arts">Defence Against the Dark Arts</a>, all at either "<a href="/wiki/Exceeds_Expectations" title="Exceeds Expectations">Exceeds Expectations</a>" or "<a href="/wiki/Outstanding" title="Outstanding">Outstanding</a>" level.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP29_4-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP29-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Charm" title="Charm">Charms</a></b>: Being a Healer, Abasi was accomplished with charms. In addition for needing both an O.W.L. and N.E.W.T. in the subject at high grades for his career,<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP29_4-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP29-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> he was also capable of using the <a href="/wiki/Memory_Charm" title="Memory Charm">Memory Charm</a> to erase the memories of the Muggle <a href="/wiki/Laura_Thorn" title="Laura Thorn">Laura Thorn</a> after she had discovered a <a href="/wiki/Mountain_troll" title="Mountain troll">Mountain troll</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-FBC2_3-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-FBC2-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> showing his proficiency in the field.</li></ul>
