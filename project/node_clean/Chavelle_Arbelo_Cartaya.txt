<b>Chavelle Arbelo Cartaya</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Spain" title="Spain">Spanish</a> <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch</a> who played as a <a href="/wiki/Chaser" title="Chaser">Chaser</a> on the <a href="/wiki/Spanish_National_Quidditch_team" title="Spanish National Quidditch team">Spanish National Quidditch team</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>During the <a href="/wiki/1996%E2%80%931997_school_year" title="1996–1997 school year">1996–1997 school year</a>, Cartaya's <a href="/wiki/Chocolate_Frog_Card" title="Chocolate Frog Card">Chocolate Frog Card</a> could be won by beating <a href="/wiki/Scott_Ancrum" title="Scott Ancrum">Scott Ancrum</a> at a <a href="/wiki/Quidditch" title="Quidditch">Quidditch</a> challenge.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
