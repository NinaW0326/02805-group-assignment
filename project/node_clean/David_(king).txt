<b>David</b> was a <a href="/wiki/King" title="King">king</a> who ruled over <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/United_Kingdom_of_Israel_and_Judah" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:United Kingdom of Israel and Judah">Israel and Judah</a> during ancient times.<sup id="cite_ref-wikipedia1_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-wikipedia1-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> He was a prominent figure in the <a href="/wiki/Bible" title="Bible">Bible</a>, and was credited with writing many of the psalms contained in one of its books, the <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Book_of_Psalms" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Book of Psalms">Book of Psalms</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-wikipedia1_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-wikipedia1-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> 
</p><p>The <a href="/wiki/Psalter" title="Psalter">Psalter</a>, a volume incorporating the Book of Psalms and other devotional writings, was included in the <i><a href="/wiki/Book_of_Common_Prayer" title="Book of Common Prayer">Book of Common Prayer</a></i>.<sup id="cite_ref-hple_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-hple-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-wikipedia2_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-wikipedia2-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> A copy of the <i>Book of Common Prayer</i> was kept in the <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Library" title="Hogwarts Library">library</a> at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-hple_2-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-hple-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/David_(king)?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/David_(king)?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>In the <a href="/wiki/Bible" title="Bible">Bible</a>, David slays the <a href="/wiki/Giant" title="Giant">giant</a> <a href="/wiki/Goliath" title="Goliath">Goliath</a> while still a boy, casting a stone at him with a sling. However, the <a href="/wiki/Chocolate_Frog_Card" title="Chocolate Frog Card">Chocolate Frog Card</a> for Goliath only refers to the giant's slayer as a "young boy," and does not explicitly name David. It is thus possible that Goliath was not slain by David in the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_universe" title="Harry Potter universe"><i>Harry Potter</i> universe</a>.</li></ul>
