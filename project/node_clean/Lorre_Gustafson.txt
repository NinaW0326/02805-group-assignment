<b>Lore Gustaffson</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Sweden" title="Sweden">Swedish</a> <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch</a> who played as a <a href="/wiki/Chaser" title="Chaser">Chaser</a> on the <a href="/wiki/Nordic_National_Quidditch_team" title="Nordic National Quidditch team">Nordic National Quidditch team</a> during the four-hundred and twenty-second <a href="/wiki/Quidditch_World_Cup" title="Quidditch World Cup">Quidditch World Cup</a>, in <a href="/wiki/1994" title="1994">1994</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-QWC_1-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-QWC-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>During the <a href="/wiki/1996%E2%80%931997_school_year" title="1996–1997 school year">1996–1997 school year</a>, her <a href="/wiki/Chocolate_Frog_Card" title="Chocolate Frog Card">Chocolate Frog Card</a> could be won by beating <a href="/wiki/Manoj_Pandit" title="Manoj Pandit">Manoj Pandit</a> at an <a href="/wiki/Exploding_Snap" title="Exploding Snap">Exploding Snap</a> challenge.<sup id="cite_ref-HBPG_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBPG-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
