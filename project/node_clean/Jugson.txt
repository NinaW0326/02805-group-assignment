<p><b>Jugson</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Dark_Wizard" title="Dark Wizard">Dark Wizard</a> and a <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eater</a> of <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a>. It is possible that he had been an <a href="/wiki/Azkaban_escapes#1996" title="Azkaban escapes">Azkaban escapee</a>. He fought in several battles of the <a href="/wiki/Second_Wizarding_War" title="Second Wizarding War">Second Wizarding War</a>, including the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_the_Department_of_Mysteries" title="Battle of the Department of Mysteries">Battle of the Department of Mysteries</a>, after which he was defeated and imprisoned in <a href="/wiki/Azkaban" title="Azkaban">Azkaban</a> along with fellow Death Eaters. It is possible that he escaped in <a href="/wiki/1997" title="1997">1997</a>, along with other <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eater</a> convicts, and that he fought in several other battles, including the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a>. After the battle and <a href="/wiki/Duel_in_the_Great_Hall" class="mw-redirect" title="Duel in the Great Hall">Voldemort's final defeat</a>, Jugson was presumably killed or imprisoned in <a href="/wiki/Azkaban" title="Azkaban">Azkaban</a> again.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP35_2-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP35-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Jugson?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Jugson?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<div class="quote">
<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix">"<i>Jugson, <a href="/wiki/Antonin_Dolohov" title="Antonin Dolohov">Dolohov</a>, to that door...</i>"</span></dd>
<dd>&#8212;<a href="/wiki/Lucius_Malfoy" title="Lucius Malfoy">Lucius Malfoy</a> gives orders to the group of Death Eaters<sup class="noprint"><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Order_of_the_Phoenix" title="Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix">[src]</a></sup></dd>
<dd></dd></dl></div>
<p>Jugson was one of the many Death Eaters who fought in the <a href="/wiki/Second_Wizarding_War" title="Second Wizarding War">Second Wizarding War</a>. It is unknown if he also fought in the <a href="/wiki/First_Wizarding_War" title="First Wizarding War">First War</a>, and if he was imprisoned or he managed to avoid <a href="/wiki/Azkaban" title="Azkaban">Azkaban</a>. It is most likely that he was one of the Death Eaters who didn't search for Voldemort after his downfall.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP35_2-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP35-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Jugson participated in the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_the_Department_of_Mysteries" title="Battle of the Department of Mysteries">Battle of the Department of Mysteries</a> in <a href="/wiki/1996" title="1996">1996</a>. During the battle, Jugson temporarily separated from the other <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a>, possibly because he wanted to capture <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> or help <a href="/wiki/Nott" title="Nott">Nott</a> who was stunned, as <a href="/wiki/Lucius_Malfoy" title="Lucius Malfoy">Lucius Malfoy</a>, the commander, told him to "come here". He was paired with <a href="/wiki/Antonin_Dolohov" title="Antonin Dolohov">Antonin Dolohov</a>, and they pursued <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>, <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione Granger</a> and <a href="/wiki/Neville_Longbottom" title="Neville Longbottom">Neville Longbottom</a>, hitting all three with <a href="/wiki/Impediment_Jinx" title="Impediment Jinx">Impediment Jinxes</a>. Afterwards, Harry struck him with a <a href="/wiki/Full_Body-Bind_Curse" title="Full Body-Bind Curse">Full Body-Bind Curse</a>, leaving him incapacitated, though he likely came around on time to confront Harry in the Death Chamber with the other Death Eaters. He also likely took part in the resulting battle against the Order of the Phoenix members. He was presumably recaptured and sent to <a href="/wiki/Azkaban" title="Azkaban">Azkaban</a> following the battle.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP35_2-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP35-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>He likely broke out in <a href="/wiki/1997" title="1997">1997</a> and joined in <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eater</a> activities. It is possible that he fought in several other battles, including the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a>.
</p><p>After Voldemort's defeat, Jugson was presumably killed or imprisoned in <a href="/wiki/Azkaban" title="Azkaban">Azkaban</a> again, along with all the other surviving Death Eaters.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Personality_and_traits">Personality and traits</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Jugson?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Personality and traits">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Jugson?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Personality and traits"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Jugson seemed to be a somewhat impatient man, as shown when he shoved Dolohov aside after the latter had been hit with a <a href="/wiki/Silencing_Charm" title="Silencing Charm">Silencing Charm</a>. Despite this he may have possibly cared about his comrades to an extent, as he may have been attempting to help <a href="/wiki/Nott" title="Nott">Nott</a> after he was injured when Lucius told him to "come here", though it is equally possible that he was merely trying to catch up to Harry Potter.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP35_2-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP35-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Magical_abilities_and_skills">Magical abilities and skills</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Jugson?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Jugson?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li><b><a href="/wiki/Duelling" title="Duelling">Duelling</a></b>: Jugson was able to cast an <a href="/wiki/Impediment_Jinx" title="Impediment Jinx">Impediment Jinx</a>, along with <a href="/wiki/Antonin_Dolohov" title="Antonin Dolohov">Antonin Dolohov</a>, during the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_the_Department_of_Mysteries" title="Battle of the Department of Mysteries">Battle of the Department of Mysteries</a>. They hit <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>, <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione Granger</a> and <a href="/wiki/Neville_Longbottom" title="Neville Longbottom">Neville Longbottom</a>, but Jugson was later hit by a <a href="/wiki/Full_Body-Bind_Curse" title="Full Body-Bind Curse">Full Body-Bind Curse</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP35_2-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP35-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> He also likely took part in the battle against the Order of the Phoenix later on. It is possible that he also took part in the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a>.</li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Dark_Arts" title="Dark Arts">Dark Arts</a></b>: Being a Death Eater, Jugson would have been proficient in the dark arts and the casting of various <a href="/wiki/Dark_charm" title="Dark charm">dark charms</a>, including the <a href="/wiki/Unforgivable_Curses" title="Unforgivable Curses">Unforgivable Curses</a>. He was proficient with the casting of the <a href="/wiki/Impediment_Jinx" title="Impediment Jinx">Impediment Jinx</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP35_2-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP35-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup></li></ul>
