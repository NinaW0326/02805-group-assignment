<p><b>Julie Parkes</b> (late <a href="/wiki/1970s" title="1970s">1970s</a> or early <a href="/wiki/1980s" title="1980s">1980s</a>â€”<a href="/wiki/1997" title="1997">1997</a>) was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch</a> and a <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts</a> student in the <a href="/wiki/1990s" title="1990s">1990s</a>, Sorted into <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor" title="Gryffindor">Gryffindor House</a>. <h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_life">Early life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Julie_Parkes?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Early life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Julie_Parkes?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Early life"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Julie Parkes was born in or before <a href="/wiki/1982" title="1982">1982</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-age_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-age-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> presumably sometime in the late <a href="/wiki/1970s" title="1970s">1970s</a> or early <a href="/wiki/1980s" title="1980s">1980s</a>. She might have been born, lived, or had family in <a href="/wiki/Godric%27s_Hollow" title="Godric&#39;s Hollow">Godric's Hollow</a>, as the village's cemetery was where she was buried after her untimely death.<sup id="cite_ref-DHpart1videogame_2-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DHpart1videogame-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<dd></dd></dl></div><p>By <a href="/wiki/1993" title="1993">1993</a>, Julie was a Hogwarts student, <a href="/wiki/Sorting_ceremony" title="Sorting ceremony">Sorted</a> into <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor" title="Gryffindor">Gryffindor</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-HBPvideo_game_4-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBPvideo_game-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> In the <a href="/wiki/1996-1997_school_year" class="mw-redirect" title="1996-1997 school year">1996-1997 school year</a> she asked <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> to use a <a href="/wiki/Summoning_Charm" title="Summoning Charm">Summoning Charm</a> to bring a jar of <a href="/wiki/Mandrake" title="Mandrake">mandrakes</a> down from a shelf she could not reach in the <a href="/wiki/Herbology_Store" title="Herbology Store">Herbology Store</a>. Later that same term, she traded eight <a href="/wiki/Wizard%27s_Chess" title="Wizard&#39;s Chess">chess pieces</a> for sprigs of peppermint, another <a href="/wiki/Potion" title="Potion">potion</a> ingredient.<sup id="cite_ref-HBPvideo_game_4-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBPvideo_game-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
