</p><p><b>Fulcran Lestrange</b> was a wizard and a member of the <a href="/wiki/Pure-blood" title="Pure-blood">pure-blood</a> <a href="/wiki/Lestrange_family" title="Lestrange family">Lestrange family</a>. He was the son of <a href="/wiki/Cyrille_Lestrange_(I)" title="Cyrille Lestrange (I)">Cyrille Lestrange</a> and <a href="/wiki/Darenne_Lestrange" title="Darenne Lestrange">Darenne Moreau</a>. He had a brother <a href="/wiki/Cyrille_Lestrange_(II)" title="Cyrille Lestrange (II)">Cyrille</a>. He married <a href="/wiki/Pucine_Lestrange_(I)" title="Pucine Lestrange (I)">Pucine Leroy</a> and had a son with her, <a href="/wiki/Fulcran_Lestrange_(II)" title="Fulcran Lestrange (II)">Fulcran</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
