<p><b>Gabriel Truman</b> (born c. <a href="/wiki/1975" title="1975">1975</a>) was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> and a <a href="/wiki/Hufflepuff" title="Hufflepuff">Hufflepuff</a> student at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School</a> in his youth. In the early <a href="/wiki/1990s" title="1990s">1990s</a>, he was one of the school&#39;s <a href="/wiki/Prefect" title="Prefect">prefects</a>, and dutifully welcomed Hufflepuff <a href="/wiki/First_year" title="First year">first years</a> to <a href="/wiki/Hufflepuff_Basement" title="Hufflepuff Basement">Hufflepuff Basement</a> with a short speech. 
<sup id="cite_ref-pottermore_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-pottermore-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Gabriel_Truman?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Gabriel_Truman?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_life">Early life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Gabriel_Truman?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Early life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Gabriel_Truman?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Early life"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Gabriel Truman was born sometime between <a href="/wiki/1973" title="1973">1973</a> and <a href="/wiki/1976" title="1976">1976</a>, somewhere in the <a href="/wiki/Great_Britain" title="Great Britain">British Isles</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-pottermore_2-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-pottermore-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Hogwarts_years">Hogwarts years</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Gabriel_Truman?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Hogwarts years">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Gabriel_Truman?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Hogwarts years"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<div class="quote">
<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;Pottermore">"<i>I got into big trouble during my third year for duelling a Ravenclaw prefect who insisted that Bridget Wenlock had come from his house, not mine. I should have got a week of detentions, but Professor Sprout let me off with a warning and a box of coconut ice.</i>"</span></dd>
<dd>&#8212;Gabriel Truman.<sup class="noprint"><a href="/wiki/Pottermore" title="Pottermore">[src]</a></sup></dd>
<dd></dd></dl></div><p>He began attending <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> sometime between <a href="/wiki/1985" title="1985">1985</a> and <a href="/wiki/1987" title="1987">1987</a>, where he was <a href="/wiki/Sorting_ceremony" title="Sorting ceremony">Sorted</a> into <a href="/wiki/Hufflepuff" title="Hufflepuff">Hufflepuff House</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-pottermore_2-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-pottermore-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>In his <a href="/wiki/Third_year" title="Third year">third year</a>, he landed in trouble by getting into a <a href="/wiki/Duelling" title="Duelling">duel</a> with a <a href="/wiki/Ravenclaw" title="Ravenclaw">Ravenclaw</a> <a href="/wiki/Prefect" title="Prefect">prefect</a> who argued that the famous <a href="/wiki/Arithmancer" title="Arithmancer">Arithmancer</a> <a href="/wiki/Bridget_Wenlock" title="Bridget Wenlock">Bridget Wenlock</a> must have been in his own house, not Hufflepuff, as she was in fact. Gabriel felt he deserved a week&#39;s worth of detentions for his own actions, but <a href="/wiki/Pomona_Sprout" title="Pomona Sprout">Professor Sprout</a> went easy on him, sending him off with a warning and a box of <a href="/wiki/Pink_Coconut_Ice" title="Pink Coconut Ice">coconut ice</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-pottermore_2-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-pottermore-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>By the <a href="/wiki/1991-1992_school_year" class="mw-redirect" title="1991-1992 school year">1991-1992 school year</a>, Gabriel had been made a <a href="/wiki/Prefect" title="Prefect">prefect</a> himself and, after the <a href="/wiki/Start-of-Term_Feast" title="Start-of-Term Feast">Start-of-Term Feast</a> on <a href="/wiki/1_September" title="1 September">1 September</a>, <a href="/wiki/1991" title="1991">1991</a>, welcomed the <a href="/wiki/Hufflepuff" title="Hufflepuff">Hufflepuff</a> <a href="/wiki/First_year" title="First year">first years</a> as they arrived at <a href="/wiki/Hufflepuff_Basement" title="Hufflepuff Basement">Hufflepuff Basement</a> with a short speech about the House traditions, symbols, characteristics, and famous past students.<sup id="cite_ref-pottermore_2-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-pottermore-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
