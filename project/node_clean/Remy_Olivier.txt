<b>Remy Olivier</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor" title="Gryffindor">Gryffindor</a> student at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a>. He once signed up for <a href="/wiki/Quidditch" title="Quidditch">Quidditch</a> training at 7pm on Thursday on a sign-up sheet that was posted on the <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor_Notice_Board" title="Gryffindor Notice Board">bulletin board</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor_Common_Room" title="Gryffindor Common Room">Gryffindor Common Room</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
