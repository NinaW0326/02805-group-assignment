<p><b>Anji Oliver</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggle</a> from <a href="/wiki/Ilford" title="Ilford">Ilford</a> in <a href="/wiki/Essex" title="Essex">Essex</a>, who witnessed the <a href="/wiki/Millennium_Bridge" title="Millennium Bridge">Millennium Bridge</a> disaster in <a href="/wiki/1996" title="1996">1996</a> and managed to film it on her <a href="/wiki/Mobile_phone" title="Mobile phone">mobile phone</a>. Her footage showed blasts being fired onto the bridge, from multiple directions, with no identifiable source.<sup id="cite_ref-HPLE_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HPLE-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
