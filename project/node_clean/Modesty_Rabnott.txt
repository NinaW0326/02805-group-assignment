<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Modesty_Rabnott?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Modesty_Rabnott?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Rabnott attended the game of <i>Cuaditch</i> (<i><a href="/wiki/Quidditch" title="Quidditch">Quidditch</a></i>) in <a href="/wiki/1269" class="mw-redirect" title="1269">1269</a> where Chief of the <a href="/wiki/Wizards%27_Council" title="Wizards&#39; Council">Wizards' Council</a> <a href="/wiki/Barberus_Bragge" title="Barberus Bragge">Barberus Bragge</a> released a <a href="/wiki/Golden_Snidget" title="Golden Snidget">Golden Snidget</a> onto the pitch. Appalled by the treatment of the <a href="/wiki/Bird" title="Bird">bird</a>, Rabnott protested to Chief Bragge, but he laughed at her. Using the <a href="/wiki/Summoning_Charm" title="Summoning Charm">Summoning Charm</a> to call the bird to her, Madam Rabnott stuffed the Snidget down the front of her robes and fled. Although she was caught, she managed to release the Snidget into the wild. While she feared that Chief Bragge would <a href="/wiki/Human_Transfiguration" title="Human Transfiguration">turn</a> her into a <a href="/wiki/Horned_toad" title="Horned toad">horned toad</a>, she was fined only ten <a href="/wiki/Galleon" title="Galleon">Galleons</a> instead. Not able to afford the fine, she lost her house. Fortunately, however, they did not take <a href="/wiki/Modesty_Rabnott%27s_hippogriff" title="Modesty Rabnott&#39;s hippogriff">her hippogriff</a>, allowing her to fly to <a href="/wiki/Aberdeen" title="Aberdeen">Aberdeen</a> to stay with her sister, <a href="/wiki/Prudence" title="Prudence">Prudence</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-QTA_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-QTA-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>The <a href="/wiki/Modesty_Rabnott_Snidget_Reservation" title="Modesty Rabnott Snidget Reservation">Modesty Rabnott Snidget Reservation</a> was named in her honour by <a href="/wiki/Elfrida_Clagg" title="Elfrida Clagg">Elfrida Clagg</a> when she banned the use of the Golden Snidget in Quidditch in the following century.<sup id="cite_ref-QTA_1-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-QTA-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Magical_abilities_and_skills">Magical abilities and skills</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Modesty_Rabnott?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Modesty_Rabnott?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li><b><a href="/wiki/Charm" title="Charm">Charms</a></b>: According to herself, Madam Rabnott was very good at aiming at targets with the <a href="/wiki/Summoning_Charm" title="Summoning Charm">Summoning Charm</a>, and during the <a href="/wiki/1269" class="mw-redirect" title="1269">1269</a> Quidditch match, she was able to successfully summon the <a href="/wiki/Golden_Snidget" title="Golden Snidget">Golden Snidget</a> used in the match, showing her proficiency in this field of <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magic</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-QTA_1-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-QTA-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Care_of_Magical_Creatures" title="Care of Magical Creatures">Care of Magical Creatures</a></b>: Madam Rabnott owned a pet <a href="/wiki/Modesty_Rabnott%27s_hippogriff" title="Modesty Rabnott&#39;s hippogriff">Hippogriff</a>, which she used to fly to <a href="/wiki/Aberdeen" title="Aberdeen">Aberdeen</a> with to live with her sister. This means she was skilled with the handling of <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magical</a> <a href="/wiki/Creature" title="Creature">creatures</a>, given how the <a href="/wiki/Hippogriff" title="Hippogriff">Hippogriff</a> is a rather difficult <a href="/wiki/Beast" title="Beast">beast</a> to tame.<sup id="cite_ref-QTA_1-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-QTA-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup></li></ul>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Modesty_Rabnott?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Modesty_Rabnott?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
