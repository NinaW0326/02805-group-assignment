<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Ghost_of_a_ragged_man_wearing_chains?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Ghost_of_a_ragged_man_wearing_chains?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>It is unknown if this ghost resided at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Castle" title="Hogwarts Castle">Hogwarts Castle</a> or if he only came there to celebrate Sir Nicholas's Deathday.</li>
<li>The Ghost's description is reminiscent of the famous ghost created by <a href="/wiki/Charles_Dickens" title="Charles Dickens">Charles Dickens</a>, Jacob Marley from his novel <i>A Christmas Carol</i>. It is possible that he&#160;<i>is</i>, in fact, Marley; an interpretation of the Deathday Party scene holds that all the unnamed ghosts are actually sneaky cameos, with the&#160;<a href="/wiki/Knight_with_an_arrow_sticking_out_of_his_forehead" title="Knight with an arrow sticking out of his forehead">Knight with an arrow sticking out of his forehead</a>&#160;being Harold II Godwinson, and the <a href="/wiki/Portly_ghost" title="Portly ghost">Portly ghost</a> being Daniel Lambert, among other things.</li></ul>
