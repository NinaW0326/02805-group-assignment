<b>P. Oxley</b> was an American <a href="/wiki/Muggle" class="mw-redirect" title="Muggle">No-Maj</a> who worked as a clerk for the <a href="/wiki/Division_of_Vital_Statistics" title="Division of Vital Statistics">Division of Vital Statistics</a> in the <a href="/wiki/New_York_State_Department_of_Health" title="New York State Department of Health">New York State Department of Health</a>. On the <a href="/wiki/25_October" title="25 October">25 October</a>, <a href="/wiki/1905" title="1905">1905</a>, Oxley attested to and signed off on the certificate for the adoption of <a href="/wiki/Credence_Barebone" title="Credence Barebone">Credence Barebone</a> by <a href="/wiki/Mary_Lou_Barebone" title="Mary Lou Barebone">Mary Lou Barebone</a> of the <a href="/wiki/New_Salem_Philanthropic_Society" title="New Salem Philanthropic Society">New Salem Philanthropic Society</a>, which had taken place <a href="/wiki/21_October" title="21 October">four days earlier</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-Archive_1-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Archive-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
