This <b><a href="/wiki/Giant" title="Giant">giant</a></b> was a member of the <a href="/wiki/Giant_colony" title="Giant colony">Giant colony</a> who fought in the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a>, fighting for <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a> and his <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a>. It is unknown if he survived the battle, but he possibly died.<sup id="cite_ref-DHF2_1-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DHF2-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_Giant_in_the_Battle_of_Hogwarts_(IV)?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_Giant_in_the_Battle_of_Hogwarts_(IV)?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>This giant is played by <a href="/wiki/Matthew_Bancroft" title="Matthew Bancroft">Matthew Bancroft</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Deathly_Hallows:_Part_2" title="Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 2">film adaptation</a> of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Deathly_Hallows" title="Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows">Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows</a></i>, but he was not credited along with the other giants so only three of them are credited.</li>
<li>Bancroft's giant, unlike I, II and III, did not appear in the video game.</li>
<li>In <i><a href="/wiki/LEGO_Harry_Potter:_Years_5-7" title="LEGO Harry Potter: Years 5-7">LEGO Harry Potter: Years 5-7</a></i> this giant stands on the top of an area in a courtyard. He has to be shot with a catapult three times before he falls backwards into the <a href="/wiki/Ravine" title="Ravine">Ravine</a> below.<sup id="cite_ref-LEGOY2_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-LEGOY2-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup></li></ul>
