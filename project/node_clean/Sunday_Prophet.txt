The <b>Sunday Prophet</b> is the name of the Sunday edition of the <a href="/wiki/Daily_Prophet" title="Daily Prophet">Daily Prophet</a>. Presumably, this edition includes different features, compared to the <a href="/wiki/Daily_Prophet" title="Daily Prophet">regular edition</a>.
</p><p>On <a href="/wiki/13_April" title="13 April">13 April</a>, <a href="/wiki/2014" title="2014">2014</a>, the <i>Sunday Prophet</i> had an article, entitled <a href="/wiki/NORWAY_VERSUS_IVORY_COAST" title="NORWAY VERSUS IVORY COAST">NORWAY VERSUS IVORY COAST</a>, on the opening match of the <a href="/wiki/2014_Quidditch_World_Cup" title="2014 Quidditch World Cup">427th Quidditch World Cup</a>, in which <a href="/wiki/Norwegian_National_Quidditch_team" title="Norwegian National Quidditch team">Norway</a> defeated <a href="/wiki/Ivorian_National_Quidditch_team" title="Ivorian National Quidditch team">Ivory Coast</a> 340 to 100.<sup id="cite_ref-PM_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PM-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
