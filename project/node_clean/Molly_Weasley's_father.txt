<b>Mr Prewett</b> was an <a href="/wiki/England" title="England">English</a> <a href="/wiki/Pure-blood" title="Pure-blood">pure-blood</a> <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> and a member of the <a href="/wiki/Prewett_family" title="Prewett family">Prewett family</a>.

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Molly_Weasley%27s_father?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Molly_Weasley%27s_father?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>He was the brother of <a href="/wiki/Ignatius_Prewett" title="Ignatius Prewett">Ignatius Prewett</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> the husband of <a href="/wiki/Molly_Weasley%27s_mother" title="Molly Weasley&#39;s mother">Mrs Prewett</a>, and the father of <a href="/wiki/Molly_Weasley" title="Molly Weasley">Molly Weasley née Prewett</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Fabian_Prewett" title="Fabian Prewett">Fabian</a> and <a href="/wiki/Gideon_Prewett" title="Gideon Prewett">Gideon Prewett</a>. He was the maternal grandfather of the <a href="/wiki/Weasley_family" title="Weasley family">Weasley</a> children. He was also the father-in-law of <a href="/wiki/Arthur_Weasley" title="Arthur Weasley">Arthur Weasley</a>. He could also possibly be the nephew of <a href="/wiki/Muriel" title="Muriel">Muriel</a>.
</p><p>He may have owned a <a href="/wiki/Wizard%27s_Chess" title="Wizard&#39;s Chess">Wizard's Chess</a> set<sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> and may also have had a big nose.<sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
