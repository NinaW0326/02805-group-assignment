<b>Achilles Tolliver</b> was an <a href="/wiki/United_States_of_America" title="United States of America">American</a> <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> who worked as an <a href="/wiki/Auror" title="Auror">Auror</a> for the <a href="/wiki/Magical_Congress_of_the_United_States_of_America" title="Magical Congress of the United States of America">Magical Congress of the United States of America</a> in the <a href="/wiki/1920s" title="1920s">1920s</a>.
</p><p>While <a href="/wiki/Jacob_Kowalski" title="Jacob Kowalski">Jacob Kowalski</a> was under the influence of an <a href="/wiki/Entrancing_Enchantments" title="Entrancing Enchantments">Entrancing Enchantment</a> cast by <a href="/wiki/Queenie_Goldstein" title="Queenie Goldstein">Queenie Goldstein</a>, he thought the name 'Tolliver' was amusing.
</p>
