<b>Mr Warbeck</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Wales" title="Wales">Welsh</a> <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> who worked as a minor functionary in the <a href="/wiki/Muggle_Liaison_Office" title="Muggle Liaison Office">Muggle Liaison Office</a> in the early <a href="/wiki/20th_century" title="20th century">20th century</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-Pottermore_1-14" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Pottermore-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>He met his <a href="/wiki/Celestina_Warbeck%27s_mother" title="Celestina Warbeck&#39;s mother">wife</a>, a <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggle</a> actress, when she was attacked by a <a href="/wiki/Lethifold" title="Lethifold">Lethifold</a> which had disguised itself as a stage curtain.<sup id="cite_ref-Pottermore_1-15" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Pottermore-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> The couple had at least one child, <a href="/wiki/Celestina_Warbeck" title="Celestina Warbeck">Celestina</a>, who was born <a href="/wiki/18_August" title="18 August">18 August</a><sup id="cite_ref-Pottermore_1-16" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Pottermore-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/1917" title="1917">1917</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-chocolatefrog_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-chocolatefrog-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Celestina proved to have an exceptional voice at a young age. She grew up to become an internationally famous wizarding singer.<sup id="cite_ref-Pottermore_1-17" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Pottermore-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
