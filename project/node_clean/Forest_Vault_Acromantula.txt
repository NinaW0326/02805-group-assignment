This <b><a href="/wiki/Acromantula" title="Acromantula">Acromantula</a></b> was a member of the <a href="/wiki/Forbidden_Forest_Acromantula_colony" title="Forbidden Forest Acromantula colony">Forbidden Forest Acromantula colony</a>, which guarded the <a href="/wiki/Forest_Vault" title="Forest Vault">Forest Vault</a>, one of the <a href="/wiki/Cursed_Vaults" title="Cursed Vaults">Cursed Vaults</a>, in the <a href="/wiki/Forbidden_Forest" title="Forbidden Forest">Forbidden Forest</a>.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Forest_Vault_Acromantula?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Forest_Vault_Acromantula?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>In the <a href="/wiki/1987%E2%80%931988_school_year" title="1987–1988 school year">1987–1988 school year</a>, it stayed near the entrance of the <a href="/wiki/Forest_Vault" title="Forest Vault">Forest Vault</a>, as it could easily prey on the <a href="/wiki/Sleepwalking_curse" title="Sleepwalking curse">sleepwalking</a> students approaching it and devour their <a href="/wiki/Flesh" title="Flesh">flesh</a>. Thankfully, the <a href="/wiki/Centaur" title="Centaur">Centaur</a> <a href="/wiki/Torvus" title="Torvus">Torvus</a> sent the students back to the castle before the foul <a href="/wiki/Beast" title="Beast">beast</a> could get a chance to devour a student. <a href="/wiki/Jacob%27s_sibling" title="Jacob&#39;s sibling">Jacob's sibling</a>, when they ventured into the forest to find out the truth surrounding the vault, came across the dangerous giant <a href="/wiki/Spider" title="Spider">spider</a>, and attacked it with <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magic</a>, before finally defeating it with the <a href="/wiki/Spider_repelling_spell" title="Spider repelling spell">Spider repelling spell</a>, stunning it and sending it fleeing back to its colony.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
