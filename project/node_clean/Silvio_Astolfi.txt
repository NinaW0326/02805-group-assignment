<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Silvio_Astolfi?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Silvio_Astolfi?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>In <a href="/wiki/1754" class="mw-redirect" title="1754">1754</a>, Astolfi agreed to participate in a <a href="/wiki/Broomstick" title="Broomstick">broom</a> <a href="/wiki/Broom_racing" title="Broom racing">race</a> from <a href="/wiki/Aberdeen" title="Aberdeen">Aberdeen</a> to <a href="/wiki/Rome" title="Rome">Rome</a> with the <a href="/wiki/Scotland" title="Scotland">Scottish</a> <a href="/wiki/Torquil_McTavish" title="Torquil McTavish">Torquil McTavish</a>, his bitter rival.<sup id="cite_ref-WBoS_1-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-WBoS-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>The race took place at night so as to avoid attracting the attention of <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggles</a>. Reaching Rome at daybreak, Astolfi and McTavish were seen racing toward the finish line at the <a href="/wiki/Coliseum" title="Coliseum">Colosseum</a>, but at that point, a violent clash broke out between rival fans that had gathered there. During the course of the fight, Astolfi was <a href="/wiki/Pullus_Jinx" title="Pullus Jinx">turned into a chicken</a>, and McTavish was hit with a <a href="/wiki/Knee-reversal_hex" title="Knee-reversal hex">hex that left his knees back-to-front</a>. The conflict culminated in an explosion that reduced the Colosseum to rubble.<sup id="cite_ref-WBoS_1-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-WBoS-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Thanks to <a href="/wiki/Orabella_Nuttley" title="Orabella Nuttley">Orabella Nuttley</a>, who came forward and proceeded to fix a few columns with a <a href="/wiki/Spell_creation" title="Spell creation">spell of her own invention</a>, the <a href="/wiki/Mending_Charm" title="Mending Charm">Mending Charm</a>, the damage to the Colosseum had been undone by the time that Muggles roused from their beds by the sound of the explosion finally arrived up at the scene. All they found was a few strangely-dressed men attempting to quell Astolfi, who was obviously enraged, and still stuck in the form of a chicken.<sup id="cite_ref-WBoS_1-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-WBoS-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> It can be presumed afterwards, that after the Muggle observers had left, Silvio was <a href="/wiki/Untransfiguration" title="Untransfiguration">untransfigured</a> back into a human.
</p>
