<b>Bob Hitchens</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggle</a><sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>&#160;who married the <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">pure-blood witch</a> <a href="/wiki/Iola_Black" title="Iola Black">Iola Black</a>, the youngest sister of famous <a href="/wiki/Phineas_Nigellus_Black" title="Phineas Nigellus Black">Phineas Nigellus Black</a>. Their <a href="/wiki/Marriage" title="Marriage">marriage</a> caused her to be struck off the <a href="/wiki/Black_Family_Tapestry" title="Black Family Tapestry">Black family tree tapestry</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Bob_Hitchens?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Bob_Hitchens?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>It was not clear at first whether Hitchens was a <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggle</a> or a <a href="/wiki/Muggle-born" title="Muggle-born">Muggle-born</a> <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a>. Although the partial family tree on the Harry Potter Lexicon lists him as a <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggle</a>, it does the same for <a href="/wiki/Muggle-born" title="Muggle-born">Muggle-born</a> wizard <a href="/wiki/Edward_Tonks" title="Edward Tonks">Ted Tonks</a>, possibly indicating that the <a href="/wiki/House_of_Black" title="House of Black">Blacks</a> did not draw a distinction between Muggles and Muggle-borns. However, in the partial tree&#160;written by <a href="/wiki/J._K._Rowling" title="J. K. Rowling">J. K. Rowling</a>&#160;given&#160;in the <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_Film_Wizardry" title="Harry Potter Film Wizardry">Harry Potter Film Wizardry</a></i>, Ted Tonks is listed&#160;as a Muggle-born and Hitchens as a Muggle.</li></ul>
