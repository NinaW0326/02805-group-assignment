She married <a href="/wiki/Corvus_Lestrange_(I)" title="Corvus Lestrange (I)">Corvus Lestrange</a> and had two sons with him,&#160;<a href="/wiki/Corvus_Lestrange_(II)" title="Corvus Lestrange (II)">Corvus</a>&#160;and <a href="/wiki/Falco_Lestrange" title="Falco Lestrange">Falco</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
