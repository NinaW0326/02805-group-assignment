<p><b>Ted</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggle</a> television newsreader in <a href="/wiki/1981" title="1981">1981</a>, working alongside <a href="/wiki/Jim_McGuffin" title="Jim McGuffin">Jim McGuffin</a>. Following the downfall of <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a>, he reported on the unusual amount of <a href="/wiki/Owl" title="Owl">owl</a> activity during the day as messages were passed throughout the <a href="/wiki/Wizarding_world" title="Wizarding world">Wizarding world</a>, while being unaware that the activity was a result of the <a href="/wiki/Fall_of_Lord_Voldemort_celebrations" title="Fall of Lord Voldemort celebrations">celebrations of Lord Voldemort's apparent demise</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-PS1_1-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PS1-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
