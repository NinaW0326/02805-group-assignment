<b>Andrew Snowy Owl</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> and <a href="/wiki/Slytherin" title="Slytherin">Slytherin</a> student at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> in the <a href="/wiki/1940s" title="1940s">1940s</a> and <a href="/wiki/1950s" title="1950s">1950s</a>. He was <a href="/wiki/Quidditch_Captain#Hogwarts_Quidditch_Captains" title="Quidditch Captain">Captain</a> of the <a href="/wiki/Slytherin_Quidditch_team" title="Slytherin Quidditch team">Slytherin Quidditch team</a> from <a href="/wiki/1948" title="1948">1948</a> to <a href="/wiki/1952" title="1952">1952</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Andrew_Snowy_Owl_(Slytherin)?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Andrew_Snowy_Owl_(Slytherin)?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The name "Andrew" comes from the Greek name Ανδρεας (Andreas), which was derived from ανηρ (aner) "man" (genitive ανδρος (andros) "of a man"). "Snowy Owl" refers to the <a href="/wiki/Snowy_owl" title="Snowy owl">species of owl</a>.
</p>
