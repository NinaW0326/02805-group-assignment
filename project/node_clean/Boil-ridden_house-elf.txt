<p>This male&#160;<b><a href="/wiki/House-elf" title="House-elf">house-elf</a></b> worked for <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> as one of their <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_house-elves" title="Hogwarts house-elves">staff of house-elves</a>. He&#160;apparently had skin problems, as the house-elf had developed a terrible case of boils. <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_potions_champion" title="Hogwarts potions champion">A Hogwarts student</a>, using the advice of the spirit of <a href="/wiki/Zygmunt_Budge" title="Zygmunt Budge">Zygmunt Budge</a> possessing <i><a href="/wiki/Book_of_Potions" title="Book of Potions">Book of Potions</a></i>, brewed a <a href="/wiki/Cure_for_Boils" title="Cure for Boils">Cure for Boils</a> <a href="/wiki/Potion" title="Potion">potion</a> that remedied this elf's skin affliction.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
