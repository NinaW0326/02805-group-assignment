<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_reporter?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_reporter?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>He interviewed a <a href="/wiki/Unidentified_witness" title="Unidentified witness">witness</a> who saw a building get destroyed by an <a href="/wiki/Obscurus" title="Obscurus">Obscurus</a> in <a href="/wiki/New_York" title="New York">New York</a> in <a href="/wiki/1926" title="1926">1926</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-FBS_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-FBS-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>After interviewing, he asked a <a href="/wiki/Unidentified_photographer_(II)" title="Unidentified photographer (II)">photographer colleague</a> if he felt like getting a drink, to which the photographer replied that he had promised <a href="/wiki/Martha_(disambiguation)" title="Martha (disambiguation)">Martha</a> (presumably his wife or girlfriend) that he would abstain from consuming <a href="/wiki/Alcohol" title="Alcohol">alcohol</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-FBS_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-FBS-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
