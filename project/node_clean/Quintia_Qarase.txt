<p><b>Quintia Qarase</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Fiji" title="Fiji">Fijian</a> <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch</a> who played as a <a href="/wiki/Beater" title="Beater">Beater</a> for the <a href="/wiki/2014" title="2014">2014</a> <a href="/wiki/Fijian_National_Quidditch_team" title="Fijian National Quidditch team">Fijian National Quidditch team</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-PM_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PM-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Quintia_Qarase?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Quintia_Qarase?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>During the second match of the <a href="/wiki/2014_Quidditch_World_Cup" title="2014 Quidditch World Cup">427th Quidditch World Cup</a>, against <a href="/wiki/Nigerian_National_Quidditch_team" title="Nigerian National Quidditch team">Nigeria</a>, Qarase and her fellow Beater <a href="/wiki/Narinder_Singh" title="Narinder Singh">Narinder Singh</a> "lacked the ferocity" of the Nigerian Beaters, <a href="/wiki/Aliko_Okoye" title="Aliko Okoye">Aliko Okoye</a> and <a href="/wiki/Mercy_Ojukwu" title="Mercy Ojukwu">Mercy Ojukwu</a>. This resulted in the <a href="/wiki/Bludger" title="Bludger">Bludgers</a> doing much harm to the Fijian <a href="/wiki/Chaser" title="Chaser">Chasers</a>, which contributed to the team's bruising defeat.<sup id="cite_ref-PM_2-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PM-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
