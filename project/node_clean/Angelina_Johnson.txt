<p><b>Angelina Weasley</b><sup id="cite_ref-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup> (née<b> Johnson</b>) (b. <a href="/wiki/24_October" title="24 October">24</a> – <a href="/wiki/30_October" title="30 October">30 October</a>, <a href="/wiki/1977" title="1977">1977</a>) was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch</a> and a <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor" title="Gryffindor">Gryffindor</a> student at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a>. She was also a member of <a href="/wiki/Dumbledore%27s_Army" title="Dumbledore&#39;s Army">Dumbledore's Army</a>, an organisation taught and led by <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>. She was also the captain of the <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor_Quidditch_team" title="Gryffindor Quidditch team">Gryffindor Quidditch team</a> in her seventh year, which was also <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry's</a> fifth year.
</p><p>In <a href="/wiki/1998" title="1998">1998</a>, two years after she had left school, she returned and fought in the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a> against the <a href="/wiki/Dark_Wizard" title="Dark Wizard">Dark Wizard</a> <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a> and his <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">followers</a>. Sometime after the end of the <a href="/wiki/Second_Wizarding_War" title="Second Wizarding War">Second Wizarding War</a>, she <a href="/wiki/Marriage" title="Marriage">married</a> <a href="/wiki/George_Weasley" title="George Weasley">George Weasley</a>, and the couple had two children, <a href="/wiki/Fred_Weasley_II" title="Fred Weasley II">Fred II</a> and <a href="/wiki/Roxanne_Weasley" title="Roxanne Weasley">Roxanne Weasley</a>.
</p>

<h3><span id="Early_life_(1977-1989)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_life_.281977-1989.29">Early life <span class="template-c">(1977-1989)</span></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Angelina_Johnson?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Early life (1977-1989)">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Angelina_Johnson?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Early life (1977-1989)"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Angelina was born in late <a href="/wiki/October" title="October">October</a>, <a href="/wiki/1977" title="1977">1977</a> on or in between the <a href="/wiki/19_October" title="19 October">24th</a> and <a href="/wiki/25_October" title="25 October">30th.</a> When she was eleven, Angelina was accepted into <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a>, and visited <a href="/wiki/Diagon_Alley" title="Diagon Alley">Diagon Alley</a> to purchase the required equipment for school, including her wand.
</p>

<p>Angelina began attending Hogwarts in 1989, when she was nearly twelve years old, and was sorted into <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor" title="Gryffindor">Gryffindor House</a>. She became a <a href="/wiki/Chaser" title="Chaser">Chaser</a> on the <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor" title="Gryffindor">Gryffindor</a> <a href="/wiki/Quidditch" title="Quidditch">Quidditch</a> <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor_Quidditch_team" title="Gryffindor Quidditch team">team</a> by or before her third year. Angelina was a popular student, and friends with fellow Gryffindors Alicia Spinnet, <a href="/wiki/Fred_Weasley" title="Fred Weasley">Fred</a> and <a href="/wiki/George_Weasley" title="George Weasley">George Weasley</a>, Katie Bell, and <a href="/wiki/Lee_Jordan" title="Lee Jordan">Lee Jordan</a>; the latter appeared to be attracted to Angelina, making various admiring comments about her when he commentated on <a href="/wiki/Quidditch" title="Quidditch">Quidditch</a> games.<sup id="cite_ref-OTP_8-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OTP-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Angelina did not like the way that <a href="/wiki/Oliver_Wood" title="Oliver Wood">Oliver Wood</a> referred to his players as "men" or "lads", as she, along with Katie Bell and Alicia Spinnet were female players and she would often have to correct him whilst he gave motivational speeches before games in the locker rooms. In 1991, the famous <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> would arrive at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts</a>, and after Professor McGonagall witnessed his flying skills, she appointed him <a href="/wiki/Seeker" title="Seeker">Seeker</a> for the team and Angelina would play alongside him in his first game. During an early Quidditch match, <a href="/wiki/Severus_Snape" title="Severus Snape">Severus Snape</a> officiated instead of <a href="/wiki/Rolanda_Hooch" title="Rolanda Hooch">Madam Hooch</a> and he called a penalty against Angelina during a <a href="/wiki/Hufflepuff_Quidditch_team" title="Hufflepuff Quidditch team">Hufflepuff</a>-Gryffindor game.<sup id="cite_ref-PS_9-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PS-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup>

<p>When playing against <a href="/wiki/Slytherin" title="Slytherin">Slytherin</a>, Angelina seemed to be an open target for a group take-out: she was boxed in by two players and knocked off her broom during one game. In 1992, Hogwarts was under threat of closure as the <a href="/wiki/Chamber_of_Secrets" title="Chamber of Secrets">Chamber of Secrets</a> had been declared open once again and <a href="/wiki/Muggle-born" title="Muggle-born">Muggle-born</a> students were being attacked.<sup id="cite_ref-COS_10-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COS-10">&#91;10&#93;</a></sup> As a precaution against this, a Duelling Club was organised and Angelina attended the first gathering with her friends Katie Bell and <a href="/wiki/Alicia_Spinnet" title="Alicia Spinnet">Alicia Spinnet</a>, and the three fought over Professor <a href="/wiki/Gilderoy_Lockhart" title="Gilderoy Lockhart">Gilderoy Lockhart</a>'s cloak when he slung it into the audience.<sup id="cite_ref-COSF_11-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COSF-11">&#91;11&#93;</a></sup>

<p>In <a href="/wiki/1994" title="1994">1994</a>, Hogwarts was the holding grounds to the <a href="/wiki/Triwizard_Tournament" title="Triwizard Tournament">Triwizard Tournament</a>, a tournament required to test the bravery and skills of a wizard that was thought to be too dangerous for wizards under a certain age. Angelina was among the many students who entered their names to the <a href="/wiki/Goblet_of_Fire" title="Goblet of Fire">Goblet of Fire</a>, as she was just of age the previous week, but she was not selected as a champion. Her friends, Fred and George also entered, but under the influence of an <a href="/wiki/Ageing_Potion" title="Ageing Potion">Ageing Potion</a> because they were still underage, which backfired on them. Due to the Tournament taking place, all <a href="/wiki/Quidditch" title="Quidditch">Quidditch</a> games and practise had been cancelled, and despite <a href="/wiki/Oliver_Wood" title="Oliver Wood">Oliver Wood</a> leaving the previous year, Angelina would not yet take on the position of Captain of the <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor" title="Gryffindor">Gryffindor</a> <a href="/wiki/Quidditch" title="Quidditch">Quidditch</a> <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor_Quidditch_team" title="Gryffindor Quidditch team">team</a> as it was not required to be filled.<sup id="cite_ref-GOF_13-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF-13">&#91;13&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<p>Throughout the Tournament Angelina was a supporter of champion <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>. She was seen cheering loudly with Fred and George during the first task, in which Harry used his flying skills to avoid the Dragon and get the egg. Also in that same year as a requirement of the Triwizard Tournament traditions Hogwarts would hold a <a href="/wiki/Yule_Ball" title="Yule Ball">Yule Ball</a>. Angelina was asked to the Yule Ball by Fred Weasley, which she accepted.<sup id="cite_ref-GOF_13-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF-13">&#91;13&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>In the final task, champions were required to enter a maze and fight their way to the <a href="/wiki/Triwizard_Cup" title="Triwizard Cup">Triwizard Cup</a> to win the tournament. Harry Potter and <a href="/wiki/Cedric_Diggory" title="Cedric Diggory">Cedric Diggory</a> both reached this cup, but it had been turned into a <a href="/wiki/Portkey" title="Portkey">Portkey</a> which transported both of them to the graveyard at <a href="/wiki/Little_Hangleton" title="Little Hangleton">Little Hangleton</a>. Cedric was murdered by Peter Pettigrew on Voldemort's orders, while Harry's blood was used to revive the Dark Lord. Harry later returned Cedric's body back to the school grounds and Angelina was amongst the first to notice that all was not well and her short-lived cheers turned to loud screams. At the end of the school year Angelina attended the <a href="/wiki/Memorial_feast_to_Cedric_Diggory" title="Memorial feast to Cedric Diggory">Memorial feast to Cedric Diggory</a> that <a href="/wiki/Albus_Dumbledore" title="Albus Dumbledore">Albus Dumbledore</a> held and she appeared quite emotional, after all Cedric was in her year, he was popular and a fan of Quidditch like herself. During this memorial service, Albus Dumbledore told the students, against the wishes of the <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">Ministry of Magic</a>, exactly how Diggory had died and confirmed that <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a> had returned to power.<sup id="cite_ref-GOF_13-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF-13">&#91;13&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<p>After Oliver Wood left Hogwarts in <a href="/wiki/1994" title="1994">1994</a>, Angelina became <a href="/wiki/Quidditch_Captain#Hogwarts_Quidditch_Captains" title="Quidditch Captain">Quidditch captain</a> once Quidditch resumed following the <a href="/wiki/Triwizard_Tournament" title="Triwizard Tournament">Triwizard Tournament</a> and proved to be at least as demanding of her team as <a href="/wiki/Oliver_Wood" title="Oliver Wood">Oliver Wood</a> had been. She attributed this hard-driving attitude to the stress of the job, saying that she regretted having been so hard on Wood when he was Quidditch captain. Angelina was a hard-working captain who didn't take losing well.<sup id="cite_ref-OTP_8-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OTP-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>When Fred, George, and Harry were banned from <a href="/wiki/Quidditch" title="Quidditch">Quidditch</a> for fighting (although their bans were lifted immediately after <a href="/wiki/Dolores_Umbridge" title="Dolores Umbridge">Dolores Umbridge</a> finally was removed from Hogwarts), she found it even more challenging not to get angry with the new teammates, one in particular <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ron Weasley</a> who was not the best on pitch. Angelina was furious at Harry, deciding that it was his fault that he got detention, and ordered him to do whatever he could to get out of detention and be there for practise, something he could not do. After she had gone, Harry commented that they should check with <a href="/wiki/Puddlemere_United" title="Puddlemere United">Puddlemere United</a> and see if Oliver Wood was still alive, as Angelina seemed to be channelling his spirit.<sup id="cite_ref-OTP_8-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OTP-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Finding that Harry was not able to convince Umbridge to change his Friday detention, Angelina angrily suggested that people who wanted to stay on the team would find a way to make it to practises. Friday evening, after the try-outs, Angelina apologised to Harry for her earlier curtness, and said that she may have been too hard on Wood; she found managing the team to be a bit stressful. Ron had not been the best <a href="/wiki/Keeper" title="Keeper">Keeper</a> she tried out; there were two better players, one was not willing to commit the time to practise and the other was a bit of a whiner, and Angelina hoped that Ron, being from a family with excellent <a href="/wiki/Quidditch" title="Quidditch">Quidditch</a> players, would develop over time.<sup id="cite_ref-OTP_8-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OTP-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup>

<p>Also during her seventh year, Angelina, along with friends <a href="/wiki/Katie_Bell" title="Katie Bell">Katie Bell</a>, <a href="/wiki/Alicia_Spinnet" title="Alicia Spinnet">Alicia Spinnet</a>, <a href="/wiki/Lee_Jordan" title="Lee Jordan">Lee Jordan</a>, <a href="/wiki/George_Weasley" title="George Weasley">George Weasley</a>, and Fred Weasley joined the alliance formed to fight back against <a href="/wiki/Dolores_Umbridge" title="Dolores Umbridge">Dolores Umbridge</a>, <a href="/wiki/Dumbledore%27s_Army" title="Dumbledore&#39;s Army">Dumbledore's Army</a>. Angelina joined the alliance as she hated Umbridge for favouring <a href="/wiki/Slytherin" title="Slytherin">Slytherin</a>, and later for taking her best players off the Gryffindor team.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP16_14-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP16-14">&#91;14&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>When Umbridge cancelled all group meetings, including Quidditch, Angelina had to ask for her team to be re-instated, but was told that time was needed to consider the request. She ultimately went to Professor McGonagall, who may have appealed to Dumbledore, and forced Umbridge to allow the team to re-form.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP18_15-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP18-15">&#91;15&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>At the last meeting of Dumbledore's Army before the Christmas holidays, Angelina told Harry that she had finally managed to find new <a href="/wiki/Beater" title="Beater">Beaters</a> and a <a href="/wiki/Seeker" title="Seeker">Seeker</a> for the Quidditch team. The Seeker was <a href="/wiki/Ginevra_Weasley" title="Ginevra Weasley">Ginny Weasley</a>, who was surprisingly good, though not a match for Harry. It was Alicia who told Harry the names of the new Beaters: <a href="/wiki/Andrew_Kirke" title="Andrew Kirke">Andrew Kirke</a> and <a href="/wiki/Jack_Sloper" title="Jack Sloper">Jack Sloper</a>. At the end of her final year at Hogwarts, Angelina's friends <a href="/wiki/Fred_Weasley" title="Fred Weasley">Fred Weasley</a> and his twin brother George made a dramatic exit, tormenting Dolores Umbridge as they left. Umbridge was later sacked and <a href="/wiki/Albus_Dumbledore" title="Albus Dumbledore">Albus Dumbledore</a> was placed as headmaster once again.<sup id="cite_ref-OTP_8-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OTP-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup>

<h3><span id="Later_life_(1998-2014)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="Later_life_.281998-2014.29">Later life <span class="template-c">(1998-2014)</span></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Angelina_Johnson?veaction=edit&amp;section=9" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Later life (1998-2014)">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Angelina_Johnson?action=edit&amp;section=9" title="Edit section: Later life (1998-2014)"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Sometime after the end of the <a href="/wiki/Second_Wizarding_War" title="Second Wizarding War">Second Wizarding War</a>, Angelina grew closer to her friend <a href="/wiki/George_Weasley" title="George Weasley">George Weasley</a>. The two eventually married and went on to have two children together: <a href="/wiki/Fred_Weasley_II" title="Fred Weasley II">Fred Weasley II</a>, named after his late uncle who also was Angelina's teenage romance, and <a href="/wiki/Roxanne_Weasley" title="Roxanne Weasley">Roxanne Weasley</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-17" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-17">&#91;17&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>In the 2010s, Angelina's younger self appeared as a <a href="/wiki/Foundable" title="Foundable">Foundable</a> in two different instances during the <a href="/wiki/Calamity" title="Calamity">Calamity</a>. In the first, she was hounded by a pair of rogue <a href="/wiki/Bludger" title="Bludger">Bludger</a> <a href="/wiki/Confoundable" title="Confoundable">Confoundables</a>, requiring volunteer members of the <a href="/wiki/Statute_of_Secrecy_Task_Force" title="Statute of Secrecy Task Force">Statute of Secrecy Task Force</a> to cast the <a href="/wiki/Slowing_Charm" title="Slowing Charm">Slowing Charm</a> in order to aid her. She also appeared alongside Fred Weasley as they appeared at the Yule Ball, trapped by a snowstorm Confoundable. With the usage of <a href="/wiki/Meteolojinx_Recanto" title="Meteolojinx Recanto">Meteolojinx Recanto</a>, the storm was able to be repelled and the pair could be returned back to 1994.<sup id="cite_ref-WU_18-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-WU-18">&#91;18&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Angelina left her marital home to care for her ailing father shortly before the <a href="/wiki/2014_Quidditch_World_Cup" title="2014 Quidditch World Cup">Quidditch World Cup</a> in July 2014.<sup id="cite_ref-the-qwc2014-final_19-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-the-qwc2014-final-19">&#91;19&#93;</a></sup> Nonetheless, Angelina travelled with her husband, son, and daughter to the <a href="/wiki/Patagonian_Desert" title="Patagonian Desert">Patagonian Desert</a> to attend the final of the Quidditch World Cup on 11 July, where the four put on "<i>a show of togetherness up in the box</i>".<sup id="cite_ref-the-qwc2014-final_19-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-the-qwc2014-final-19">&#91;19&#93;</a></sup> During the match, commentator <a href="/wiki/Rita_Skeeter" title="Rita Skeeter">Rita Skeeter</a> mentioned rumours that Angelina had "grown restless" in her marriage to George, and thus chosen to leave her marital home to live elsewhere (presumably with her lover).<sup id="cite_ref-the-qwc2014-final_19-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-the-qwc2014-final-19">&#91;19&#93;</a></sup> Her fellow commentator, Angelina's sister-in-law <a href="/wiki/Ginevra_Weasley" title="Ginevra Weasley">Ginny Potter</a>, quickly refuted these rumours, explaining that Angelina had recently been caring for her father.<sup id="cite_ref-the-qwc2014-final_19-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-the-qwc2014-final-19">&#91;19&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Physical_appearance">Physical appearance</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Angelina_Johnson?veaction=edit&amp;section=10" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Physical appearance">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Angelina_Johnson?action=edit&amp;section=10" title="Edit section: Physical appearance"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<div class="quote">
<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;Harry Potter and the Philosopher&#39;s Stone">"<i>What an amazing Chaser that girl is, and rather attractive too!"</i>"</span></dd>
<dd>&#8212;<a href="/wiki/Lee_Jordan" title="Lee Jordan">Lee Jordan</a> commenting on Angelina's appearance<sup class="noprint"><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Philosopher%27s_Stone" title="Harry Potter and the Philosopher&#39;s Stone">[src]</a></sup></dd>
<dd></dd></dl></div>
<p>Angelina was described to be a tall, pretty witch with dark skin, brown eyes and long black hair that was often braided. Angelina was apparently attractive, receiving romantic advances and affection from multiple classmates, such as Lee Jordan and Fred Weasley. Pansy Parkinson mocked Angelina's appearance (among many other Gryffindors), claiming that her braided hairstyle resembled that of <a href="/wiki/Worm" title="Worm">worms</a>, but given Pansy's nastiness and bias, her evaluation was most certainly untruthful.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Personality_and_traits">Personality and traits</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Angelina_Johnson?veaction=edit&amp;section=11" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Personality and traits">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Angelina_Johnson?action=edit&amp;section=11" title="Edit section: Personality and traits"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<div class="quote">
<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix">"<i>I think we'd better check with <a href="/wiki/Puddlemere_United" title="Puddlemere United">Puddlemere United</a> whether <a href="/wiki/Oliver_Wood" title="Oliver Wood">Oliver Wood</a>'s been killed during a training session, because Angelina seems to be channelling his spirit.</i>"</span></dd>
<dd>&#8212;Angelina's seriousness about being Quidditch captain<sup class="noprint"><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Order_of_the_Phoenix" title="Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix">[src]</a></sup></dd>
<dd></dd></dl></div>
<p>Angelina Johnson was very driven to succeed, whether it be at <a href="/wiki/Quidditch" title="Quidditch">Quidditch</a><sup id="cite_ref-OTP_8-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OTP-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup> or in trying out for the <a href="/wiki/Triwizard_Tournament" title="Triwizard Tournament">Triwizard Tournament</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-GOF_13-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF-13">&#91;13&#93;</a></sup> Angelina showed diligence and leadership ability as <a href="/wiki/Quidditch_Captain#Hogwarts_Quidditch_Captains" title="Quidditch Captain">Quidditch Captain</a> of her team, as well as competitiveness.<sup id="cite_ref-OTP_8-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OTP-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup> She is also brave, as shown by her sorting into <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor" title="Gryffindor">Gryffindor</a> and her decision to return to <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts</a> to fight in the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a> alongside old classmates and friends.<sup id="cite_ref-DH_16-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH-16">&#91;16&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Given her friendship and eventual romantic involvement with both Weasley twins, it is likely that Angelina also enjoyed pranks and had a good sense of humour. However, her sense of humour did not compromise her seriousness about Quidditch. Angelina wouldn't hesitate to give her blunt opinion, telling Harry Potter that <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ron Weasley</a> was not a fabulous Quidditch player. She also became enraged at Harry when he kept getting detentions from Dolores Umbridge, but apologised later.
</p>

<p>Angelina and <a href="/wiki/George_Weasley" title="George Weasley">George Weasley</a> were good friends throughout their Hogwarts years. Fred's death devastated them both, and it may have been their shared grief that led to the two becoming closer. Angelina and George eventually married and had two children, <a href="/wiki/Roxanne_Weasley" title="Roxanne Weasley">Roxanne Weasley</a> and <a href="/wiki/Fred_Weasley_II" title="Fred Weasley II">Fred Weasley II</a>
</p>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Children">Children</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Angelina_Johnson?veaction=edit&amp;section=18" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Children">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Angelina_Johnson?action=edit&amp;section=18" title="Edit section: Children"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<p>After the Second Wizarding War, Angelina married <a href="/wiki/George_Weasley" title="George Weasley">George Weasley</a> and they had two children; a son, named <a href="/wiki/Fred_Weasley_II" title="Fred Weasley II">Fred Weasley II</a>, in honour of George's brother, and a daughter, <a href="/wiki/Roxanne_Weasley" title="Roxanne Weasley">Roxanne</a>.
</p>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Nieces_and_nephews">Nieces and nephews</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Angelina_Johnson?veaction=edit&amp;section=19" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Nieces and nephews">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Angelina_Johnson?action=edit&amp;section=19" title="Edit section: Nieces and nephews"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<p>Angelina had also several nieces and nephews through George's siblings: <a href="/wiki/Victoire_Weasley" title="Victoire Weasley">Victoire</a>, <a href="/wiki/Dominique_Weasley" title="Dominique Weasley">Dominique</a> and <a href="/wiki/Louis_Weasley" title="Louis Weasley">Louis</a>, <a href="/wiki/William_Weasley" title="William Weasley">Bill</a> and <a href="/wiki/Fleur_Delacour" title="Fleur Delacour">Fleur</a>'s children; <a href="/wiki/Molly_Weasley_II" title="Molly Weasley II">Molly</a> and <a href="/wiki/Lucy_Weasley" title="Lucy Weasley">Lucy</a>, <a href="/wiki/Percy_Weasley" title="Percy Weasley">Percy</a> and <a href="/wiki/Audrey_Weasley" title="Audrey Weasley">Audrey</a>'s children; <a href="/wiki/Rose_Granger-Weasley" title="Rose Granger-Weasley">Rose</a> and <a href="/wiki/Hugo_Granger-Weasley" title="Hugo Granger-Weasley">Hugo</a>, Ron and Hermione's children; and <a href="/wiki/James_Potter_II" title="James Potter II">James</a>, <a href="/wiki/Albus_Potter" title="Albus Potter">Albus</a> and <a href="/wiki/Lily_Potter" title="Lily Potter">Lily</a>, Ginny and Harry's children. It is supposed that Angelina and George had a good relationship with his nieces and nephews, and that they liked them. James also appeared to take after George, because they both enjoyed doing pranks and teasing their siblings.
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Lee_Jordan">Lee Jordan</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Angelina_Johnson?veaction=edit&amp;section=20" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Lee Jordan">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Angelina_Johnson?action=edit&amp;section=20" title="Edit section: Lee Jordan"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<div class="quote">
<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;Harry Potter and the Philosopher&#39;s Stone">"<i>What an amazing Chaser that girl is, and rather attractive too!</i>"</span></dd>
<dd>&#8212;Lee Jordan displaying his feelings for Angelina during a Quidditch match<sup class="noprint"><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Philosopher%27s_Stone" title="Harry Potter and the Philosopher&#39;s Stone">[src]</a></sup></dd>
<dd></dd></dl></div>
<p><a href="/wiki/Lee_Jordan" title="Lee Jordan">Lee Jordan</a> had a crush on Angelina and didn't hesitate to let her know it, often showing his attraction to her in his Quidditch commentaries. He also stated that Angelina had been refusing to go out with him, indicating that she didn't feel the same way. However, Angelina and Lee were both close to the Weasley twins, which probably hints they were friends themselves, although this was never made clear.
</p>
<h3><span id="Dumbledore's_Army"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="Dumbledore.27s_Army">Dumbledore's Army</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Angelina_Johnson?veaction=edit&amp;section=21" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Dumbledore&#039;s Army">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Angelina_Johnson?action=edit&amp;section=21" title="Edit section: Dumbledore&#039;s Army"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<figure class="thumb tleft show-info-icon" style="width: 237px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/9/96/Dumbledore%27s_Army.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20161123060553" class="image"><img alt="" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw%3D%3D" decoding="async" width="235" height="156" class="thumbimage lazyload" data-image-name="Dumbledore&#39;s Army.jpg" data-image-key="Dumbledore&#39;s_Army.jpg" data-src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/9/96/Dumbledore%27s_Army.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/235?cb=20161123060553" /></a> 	<noscript><a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/9/96/Dumbledore%27s_Army.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20161123060553" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/9/96/Dumbledore%27s_Army.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/235?cb=20161123060553" decoding="async" width="235" height="156" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="Dumbledore&#39;s Army.jpg" data-image-key="Dumbledore&#39;s_Army.jpg" data-src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/9/96/Dumbledore%27s_Army.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/235?cb=20161123060553" /></a></noscript> 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:Dumbledore%27s_Army.jpg" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption"><a href="/wiki/Dumbledore%27s_Army" title="Dumbledore&#39;s Army">Dumbledore's Army</a>, her allies</p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure><p>Angelina became a member of Dumbledore's Army in her seventh year. It was an organisation founded and led by <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> in order to teach <a href="/wiki/Defence_Against_the_Dark_Arts" title="Defence Against the Dark Arts">Defence Against the Dark Arts</a> to the members since professor <a href="/wiki/Dolores_Umbridge" title="Dolores Umbridge">Dolores Umbridge</a> did not. Angelina knew most members of the army, and was friendly with them.
</p><p>Angelina was also friends with fellow Gryffindors <a href="/wiki/Alicia_Spinnet" title="Alicia Spinnet">Alicia Spinnet</a> and <a href="/wiki/Katie_Bell" title="Katie Bell">Katie Bell</a>. The three girls were all <a href="/wiki/Chaser" title="Chaser">Chasers</a> on the <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor" title="Gryffindor">Gryffindor</a> <a href="/wiki/Quidditch" title="Quidditch">Quidditch</a> <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor_Quidditch_team" title="Gryffindor Quidditch team">team</a>, and joined <a href="/wiki/Dumbledore%27s_Army" title="Dumbledore&#39;s Army">Dumbledore's Army</a> together in <a href="/wiki/1995" title="1995">1995</a>. Angelina was also friendly with, though not very close to, <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>, her team's <a href="/wiki/Seeker" title="Seeker">Seeker</a> (although she, along with Alicia Spinnet and Katie Bell, kissed Harry when he won them a match against <a href="/wiki/Ravenclaw_Quidditch_team" title="Ravenclaw Quidditch team">Ravenclaw</a>) and <a href="/wiki/Lee_Jordan" title="Lee Jordan">Lee Jordan</a>, the Quidditch commentator who repeatedly asked her out, without success. She was friends with mostly everyone on the <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor_Quidditch_team" title="Gryffindor Quidditch team">Gryffindor Quidditch team</a>. She also appeared to be friendly with <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ron Weasley</a>, but she was annoyed by his lack of confidence when he was a member of the <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor" title="Gryffindor">Gryffindor</a> <a href="/wiki/Quidditch" title="Quidditch">Quidditch</a> <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor_Quidditch_team" title="Gryffindor Quidditch team">team</a> in <a href="/wiki/1996" title="1996">1996</a>. She was also a friend of <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione Granger</a>, another <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor" title="Gryffindor">Gryffindor</a> girl.
</p><p>She was also good friends with <a href="/wiki/Oliver_Wood" title="Oliver Wood">Oliver Wood</a>, the former Captain of the <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor" title="Gryffindor">Gryffindor</a> <a href="/wiki/Quidditch" title="Quidditch">Quidditch</a> <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor_Quidditch_team" title="Gryffindor Quidditch team">team</a> before her. Although Oliver was not a member of Dumbledore's Army, as he had left Hogwarts when it was founded, he supported both Dumbledore's Army and the <a href="/wiki/Order_of_the_Phoenix" title="Order of the Phoenix">Order of the Phoenix</a>, fighting with them at the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a>.
</p><p>Other members of Dumbledore's Army were <a href="/wiki/Dean_Thomas" title="Dean Thomas">Dean Thomas</a>, <a href="/wiki/Seamus_Finnigan" title="Seamus Finnigan">Seamus Finnigan</a>, <a href="/wiki/Padma_and_Parvati_Patil" title="Padma and Parvati Patil">Parvati and Padma Patil</a>, <a href="/wiki/Lavender_Brown" title="Lavender Brown">Lavender Brown</a>, <a href="/wiki/Neville_Longbottom" title="Neville Longbottom">Neville Longbottom</a>, <a href="/wiki/Luna_Lovegood" title="Luna Lovegood">Luna Lovegood</a>, <a href="/wiki/Ginevra_Weasley" title="Ginevra Weasley">Ginny Weasley</a>, <a href="/wiki/Colin_and_Dennis_Creevey" title="Colin and Dennis Creevey">Colin and Dennis Creevey</a>, <a href="/wiki/Hannah_Abbott" title="Hannah Abbott">Hannah Abbott</a>, <a href="/wiki/Ernest_Macmillan" title="Ernest Macmillan">Ernie Macmillan</a>, <a href="/wiki/Susan_Bones" title="Susan Bones">Susan Bones</a>, <a href="/wiki/Terry_Boot" title="Terry Boot">Terry Boot</a>, <a href="/wiki/Justin_Finch-Fletchley" title="Justin Finch-Fletchley">Justin Finch-Fletchley</a>, <a href="/wiki/Anthony_Goldstein" title="Anthony Goldstein">Anthony Goldstein</a>, <a href="/wiki/Cho_Chang" title="Cho Chang">Cho Chang</a>, <a href="/wiki/Michael_Corner" title="Michael Corner">Michael Corner</a>, <a href="/wiki/Zacharias_Smith" title="Zacharias Smith">Zacharias Smith</a> and <a href="/wiki/Marietta_Edgecombe" title="Marietta Edgecombe">Marietta Edgecombe</a>. Their relationship with Angelina is unknown, though it is possible that most of them were friends of hers.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Angelina_Johnson?veaction=edit&amp;section=22" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Angelina_Johnson?action=edit&amp;section=22" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p><i><a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Angelina" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Angelina">Angelina</a></i> is a Latin diminutive of the name <i>Angela</i>, which is derived from the Greek άγγελος, <i>angelos</i>, meaning "messenger" or "angel".<sup id="cite_ref-21" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-21">&#91;21&#93;</a></sup> Johnson means "son of John."
</p>

<ul><li>Angelina is played by <a href="/wiki/Danielle_Tabor" title="Danielle Tabor">Danielle Tabor</a> in the films <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Philosopher%27s_Stone_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Philosopher&#39;s Stone (film)">Harry Potter and the Philosopher's Stone</a></i>, <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Chamber_of_Secrets_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets (film)">Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets</a></i> and <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Prisoner_of_Azkaban_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban (film)">Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban</a></i>. In the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Goblet_of_Fire_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire (film)">film adaptation</a> of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Goblet_of_Fire" title="Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire">Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire</a></i>, she is replaced by actress <a href="/wiki/Tiana_Benjamin" title="Tiana Benjamin">Tiana Benjamin</a>, as she looked physically the same age as the Weasley twins.</li>
<li>Tiana Benjamin was unable to reprise the role in <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Order_of_the_Phoenix_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix (film)">Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix</a></i> because her role in <i><a target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener" class="external text" href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Eastenders">EastEnders</a></i> was at a point of character development and she could not get time out of this role to film for <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a></i>. However, Benjamin did voice the character for the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Order_of_the_Phoenix_(video_game)" title="Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix (video game)">video game adaptation</a>. This led to confusion among fans as Tiana had said she would return as Angelina (referring to the video game), but people thought she was returning to the movies.</li>
<li>Angelina Johnson once borrowed <i><a href="/wiki/Quidditch_Through_the_Ages" title="Quidditch Through the Ages">Quidditch Through the Ages</a></i> from the <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Library" title="Hogwarts Library">Hogwarts Library</a>, and was due to return the book on <a href="/wiki/19_July" title="19 July">19 July</a>.</li>
<li>In the film adaptation of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Goblet_of_Fire" title="Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire">Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire</a></i> Angelina and Fred could be seen among the few people in the Yule Ball who remained the longest, still dancing together.</li>
<li>Danielle Tabor returned to set to play the character again in the <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Forbidden_Journey" title="Harry Potter and the Forbidden Journey">Harry Potter and the Forbidden Journey</a></i> ride at <a href="/wiki/The_Wizarding_World_of_Harry_Potter" title="The Wizarding World of Harry Potter">The Wizarding World of Harry Potter</a> theme park.</li></ul>
