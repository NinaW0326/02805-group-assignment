<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Description">Description</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Eagle?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Description">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Eagle?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Description"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Eagles are differentiated from other birds of prey mainly by their larger size, more powerful build, and heavier head and bill. Like all birds of prey, eagles have very large powerful hooked beaks for tearing flesh from their prey, strong legs, and powerful curved talons for snatching and grasping prey.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>The eagle is the mascot of <a href="/wiki/Ravenclaw" title="Ravenclaw">Ravenclaw House</a> at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a>.
</p><p>It is a possible corporeal form of the <a href="/wiki/Patronus_Charm" title="Patronus Charm">Patronus Charm</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Eagle feathers are sometimes used for <a href="/wiki/Quill" title="Quill">quills</a>.
</p><p>The body of a <a href="/wiki/Hippogriff" title="Hippogriff">Hippogriff</a> is half-eagle and half-<a href="/wiki/Horse" title="Horse">horse</a> while the body of a <a href="/wiki/Griffin" title="Griffin">Griffin</a> is half-eagle and half-<a href="/wiki/Lion" title="Lion">lion</a>.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="History">History</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Eagle?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: History">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Eagle?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: History"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p><a href="/wiki/Talbott_Winger" title="Talbott Winger">Talbott Winger</a> could transform into an eagle as an <a href="/wiki/Animagus" title="Animagus">Animagus</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-hm_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-hm-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>On <a href="/wiki/Christmas" title="Christmas">Christmas</a> in <a href="/wiki/1992" title="1992">1992</a>, <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione Granger</a> presented <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> with a luxury eagle-feather quill as a Christmas present.<sup id="cite_ref-CS12_4-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-CS12-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> Harry later used that quill do his homework during the summer holidays in <a href="/wiki/1993" title="1993">1993</a><sup id="cite_ref-PA1_5-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PA1-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> and also when writing a letter to <a href="/wiki/Sirius_Black" title="Sirius Black">Sirius Black</a> during the summer holidays in <a href="/wiki/1994" title="1994">1994</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-GF2_6-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GF2-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>In <a href="/wiki/May" title="May">May</a> <a href="/wiki/1996" title="1996">1996</a>, <a href="/wiki/Luna_Lovegood" title="Luna Lovegood">Luna Lovegood</a> wore a <a href="/wiki/Luna_Lovegood%27s_eagle_hat" title="Luna Lovegood&#39;s eagle hat">hat</a> that took the form of a lifelike eagle to the <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor" title="Gryffindor">Gryffindor</a> vs. Ravenclaw Quidditch match.<sup id="cite_ref-OP30_7-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OP30-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
