This <b>man</b> was a member of the <a href="/wiki/Pocomtuc" title="Pocomtuc">Pocomtuc</a> people during the <a href="/wiki/17th_century" title="17th century">17th century</a>. He had at least <a href="/wiki/Martha_Steward_II%27s_friend" title="Martha Steward II&#39;s friend">one sibling</a>, who was friends with <a href="/wiki/Martha_Steward_II" title="Martha Steward II">Martha Steward</a>, the <a href="/wiki/Squib" title="Squib">Squib</a> daughter of <a href="/wiki/Isolt_Sayre" title="Isolt Sayre">Isolt Sayre</a> and <a href="/wiki/James_Steward" title="James Steward">James Steward</a>. He was known to be non-<a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magical</a>, but it is unknown whether he was a <a href="/wiki/Muggle" class="mw-redirect" title="Muggle">No-Maj</a> with a <a href="/wiki/Muggle-born" title="Muggle-born">No-Maj-born</a> sibling, or a <a href="/wiki/Squib" title="Squib">Squib</a> born to <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> parents. He eventually married Martha, and she subsequently integrated into No-Maj society.<sup id="cite_ref-Pottermore_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Pottermore-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
