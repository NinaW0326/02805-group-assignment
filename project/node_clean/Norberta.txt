<p>During the <a href="/wiki/1991%E2%80%931992_school_year" title="1991–1992 school year">1991–1992 school year</a>, <a href="/wiki/Professor" title="Professor">Professor</a> <a href="/wiki/Quirinus_Quirrell" title="Quirinus Quirrell">Quirinus Quirrell</a>, trying to figure out how to get past <a href="/wiki/Fluffy" title="Fluffy">Fluffy</a> and retrieve the <a href="/wiki/Philosopher%27s_Stone" title="Philosopher&#39;s Stone">Philosopher's Stone</a>, disguised himself and enticed <a href="/wiki/Rubeus_Hagrid" title="Rubeus Hagrid">Rubeus Hagrid</a> to play a game of cards by wagering Norberta's <a href="/wiki/Dragon_egg" title="Dragon egg">egg</a>. They had a few drinks, and Quirrell asked him if he was sure he could handle raising a dragon. Hagrid told him about how he managed Fluffy, inadvertently giving Quirrell the information he needed. Hagrid later assumed that 'the stranger' was a <a href="/wiki/Dragon_dealer" title="Dragon dealer">Dragon dealer</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-PS16_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PS16-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<figure class="thumb tright show-info-icon" style="width: 252px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/a/a1/Norbert_hagrid.gif/revision/latest?cb=20170719034800" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/a/a1/Norbert_hagrid.gif/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/250?cb=20170719034800" decoding="async" width="250" height="104" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="Norbert hagrid.gif" data-image-key="Norbert_hagrid.gif" /></a> 	 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:Norbert_hagrid.gif" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption">Norberta shortly after hatching</p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure>
<p>Rubeus Hagrid kept the egg in his <a href="/wiki/Rubeus_Hagrid%27s_cabin" class="mw-redirect" title="Rubeus Hagrid&#39;s cabin">hut</a>, warming it over a <a href="/wiki/Fire" title="Fire">fire</a> until it hatched. <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ron</a>, <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione</a> were present when Norberta hatched. Also present (though at first unknown) was <a href="/wiki/Draco_Malfoy" title="Draco Malfoy">Draco Malfoy</a>, who peered through a window of Hagrid's hut. The trio feared that Hagrid would get in trouble, and so convinced him to get rid of the dragon. The baby dragon looked "like a crumpled black umbrella" with large spiny wings, a skinny body and a long snout with wide nostrils, horn stubs and bulging,orange eyes. Hagrid immediately adored her, although she tried to bite him. He named the newborn dragon "Norbert", and fed it with a bucket of <a href="/wiki/Mixture_of_brandy_and_chicken_blood" title="Mixture of brandy and chicken blood">brandy mixed with chicken blood</a> every half hour, as recommended in <i><a href="/wiki/Dragon_Breeding_for_Pleasure_and_Profit" title="Dragon Breeding for Pleasure and Profit">Dragon Breeding for Pleasure and Profit</a></i>.<sup id="cite_ref-PS14_1-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PS14-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<p>However, as the young dragon grew, it became too violent and dangerous to keep. After Norbert bit <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ron Weasley</a>'s hand, for which he had to go to the <a href="/wiki/Hospital_Wing" title="Hospital Wing">Hospital Wing</a> due to the venom in its fangs, Hagrid had no choice but to release Norbert to <a href="/wiki/Charles_Weasley" title="Charles Weasley">Charles Weasley</a>, who worked with dragons in <a href="/wiki/Romania" title="Romania">Romania</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-PS14_1-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PS14-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>When Hagrid met Charlie at <a href="/wiki/William_Weasley" title="William Weasley">Bill Weasley</a> and <a href="/wiki/Fleur_Delacour" title="Fleur Delacour">Fleur Delacour</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Wedding_of_William_Weasley_and_Fleur_Delacour" title="Wedding of William Weasley and Fleur Delacour">wedding</a>, he was informed that the dragon, originally thought to be a male named Norbert, was now called Norberta, as Charlie had discovered that she was in fact a female. He said that he was able to tell by the females being much more violent and ferocious.<sup id="cite_ref-DH7_2-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH7-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<ul><li>Norberta has a different departure in the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Philosopher%27s_Stone_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Philosopher&#39;s Stone (film)">film adaptation</a> of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Philosopher%27s_Stone" title="Harry Potter and the Philosopher&#39;s Stone">Harry Potter and the Philosopher's Stone</a></i>. Instead of her presence remaining a secret and Harry and Hermione handing her over to Charlie's friends, the Hogwarts staff becomes aware of her, and Dumbledore had her sent off to Romania. Also, the dragon is shown to be affectionate with the groundskeeper, rather than violent. <h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Norberta?veaction=edit&amp;section=6" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Norberta?action=edit&amp;section=6" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p><i>Norbert</i> is a Germanic first name, derived from <i>nord</i> ("north") and <i>berht</i> ("bright"). <i>Norberta</i> is simply a feminine form of this name.
</p>
