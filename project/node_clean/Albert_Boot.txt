</p><p><b>Minister Albert Boot</b> was <a href="/wiki/Minister_for_Magic" title="Minister for Magic">Minister for Magic</a> from <a href="/wiki/1747" class="mw-redirect" title="1747">1747</a> to <a href="/wiki/1752" class="mw-redirect" title="1752">1752</a>. He was considered a likeable Minister, but also an inept one. After mismanaging a <a href="/wiki/18th_century_goblin_rebellion" title="18th century goblin rebellion">goblin rebellion</a>, he resigned from the post.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>  He was first elected Minister by the Wizarding population of <a href="/wiki/Great_Britain" title="Great Britain">Great Britain</a> and <a href="/wiki/Ireland" title="Ireland">Ireland</a> after the death of <a href="/wiki/Eldritch_Diggory" title="Eldritch Diggory">Eldritch Diggory</a>.
</p>
