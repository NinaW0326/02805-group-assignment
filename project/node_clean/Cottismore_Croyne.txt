<b>Cottismore Croyne</b>, <span class="template-c"><a href="/wiki/Order_of_Merlin" title="Order of Merlin">O.M. (unknown class)</a></span>, was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> who was appointed <a href="/wiki/Supreme_Mugwump" title="Supreme Mugwump">Supreme Mugwump</a> of the <a href="/wiki/International_Confederation_of_Wizards" title="International Confederation of Wizards">International Confederation of Wizards</a> sometime after <a href="/wiki/Pierre_Bonaccord" title="Pierre Bonaccord">Pierre Bonaccord</a>. This was likely the reason he was awarded an <a href="/wiki/Order_of_Merlin" title="Order of Merlin">Order of Merlin</a>.
</p><p>Cottismore Croyne had a <a href="/wiki/Portrait" title="Portrait">portrait</a> of himself hung at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Castle" title="Hogwarts Castle">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> by the time <a href="/wiki/Albus_Dumbledore" title="Albus Dumbledore">Albus Dumbledore</a>, Supreme Mungwump himself in the <a href="/wiki/1990s" title="1990s">1990s</a>, was <a href="/wiki/Headmaster" title="Headmaster">Headmaster</a> of the school. The portrait depicted him in one of the hallways in the <a href="/wiki/Middle_Courtyard" title="Middle Courtyard">Middle Courtyard</a>, which suggests that he may have visited <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Castle" title="Hogwarts Castle">Hogwarts Castle</a> once.
</p>
