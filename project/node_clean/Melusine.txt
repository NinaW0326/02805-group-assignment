<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Melusine?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Melusine?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>In <a href="/wiki/1927" title="1927">1927</a>, <a href="/wiki/Newton_Scamander" title="Newton Scamander">Newton Scamander</a> and <a href="/wiki/Porpentina_Goldstein" title="Porpentina Goldstein">Porpentina Goldstein</a> encountered Melusine when they looked to access the <a href="/wiki/French_Ministry_of_Magic_Records_Room" title="French Ministry of Magic Records Room">French Ministry of Magic Records Room</a>, and were allowed entrance. <a href="/wiki/Leta_Lestrange" title="Leta Lestrange">Leta Lestrange</a> later met her for the same reason. This caused Melusine obvious suspicion, given how Goldstein had in fact claimed to have been Lestrange beforehand.<sup id="cite_ref-COGS_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COGS-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<figure class="thumb tleft show-info-icon" style="width: 182px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/0/0e/LetaStupefy.gif/revision/latest?cb=20190224172918" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/0/0e/LetaStupefy.gif/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/180?cb=20190224172918" decoding="async" width="180" height="75" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="LetaStupefy.gif" data-image-key="LetaStupefy.gif" /></a> 	 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:LetaStupefy.gif" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption">Leta attempting to stun Melusine's Matagots</p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure>
<p>By the time the three were to leave, they were faced with Melusine and a handful of <a href="/wiki/Matagot" title="Matagot">Matagots</a>, beastly black felines who guarded the Ministry. When Leta attempted to <a href="/wiki/Stunning_Spell" title="Stunning Spell">stun</a> one, it multiplied and began to attack them in larger numbers. Thankfully, Newt managed to have them all escape with the help of his <a href="/wiki/Zouwu" title="Zouwu">Zouwu</a> in his <a href="/wiki/Newton_Scamander%27s_suitcase" title="Newton Scamander&#39;s suitcase">suitcase</a><sup id="cite_ref-COGS_1-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COGS-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
