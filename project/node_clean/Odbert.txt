<b>Odbert</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Goblin" title="Goblin">Goblin</a> who worked at <a href="/wiki/Gringotts_Wizarding_Bank" title="Gringotts Wizarding Bank">Gringotts Wizarding Bank</a> at some point during <a href="/wiki/1990" title="1990">1990</a>. Like most goblins, he was rather suspicious of most <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizards</a>, including the <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts</a> students that visited the bank looking for <a href="/wiki/William_Weasley" title="William Weasley">Bill Weasley</a>. He did, however, appear quite friendly with his colleague and fellow goblin, <a href="/wiki/Burgock" title="Burgock">Burgock</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-HM6_1-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HM6-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
