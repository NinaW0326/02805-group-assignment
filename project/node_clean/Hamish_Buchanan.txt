<b>Hamish Buchanan</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Scotland" title="Scotland">Scottish</a> <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> who lived in the <a href="/wiki/19th_century" title="19th century">19th century</a>. He was the son of <a href="/wiki/Angus_Buchanan%27s_father" class="mw-redirect" title="Angus Buchanan&#39;s father">Mr</a> and <a href="/wiki/Angus_Buchanan%27s_mother" class="mw-redirect" title="Angus Buchanan&#39;s mother">Mrs Buchanan</a> and the older brother of <a href="/wiki/Angus_Buchanan" title="Angus Buchanan">Angus Buchanan</a>.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Hamish_Buchanan?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Hamish_Buchanan?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Hamish was born into the <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizarding</a> <a href="/wiki/Buchanan_family" title="Buchanan family">Buchanan family</a> sometime during the mid-<a href="/wiki/19th_century" title="19th century">19th century</a> and grew up in a village in the <a href="/wiki/Scottish_Borders" title="Scottish Borders">Scottish Borders</a>. He was his parents' first- or second-born son, and had a total of ten siblings, including his sister <a href="/wiki/Flora_Buchanan" title="Flora Buchanan">Flora</a> and his younger brother <a href="/wiki/Angus_Buchanan" title="Angus Buchanan">Angus</a>. All of the Buchanan children had a reputation for being of above-average size and behaving wildly.
</p><p>When Hamish turned eleven, he received a <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_acceptance_letter" title="Hogwarts acceptance letter">Hogwarts acceptance letter</a>, and began attending <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a>. The local <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggle</a> villagers, not knowing that the Buchanan family were <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magical</a>, assumed that he had been sent to a corrective facility or mental institution. It is unknown what <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Houses" title="Hogwarts Houses">house</a> Hamish was <a href="/wiki/Sorting_ceremony" title="Sorting ceremony">sorted</a> into at Hogwarts.
</p><p>Hamish and his other siblings were especially fond of their brother Angus. Since <a href="/wiki/Angus_Buchanan%27s_father" class="mw-redirect" title="Angus Buchanan&#39;s father">their father</a> was highly prejudiced against <a href="/wiki/Squib" title="Squib">Squibs</a>, Hamish and his siblings covered for Angus when, unlike them, he displayed no signs of magical ability. On <a href="/wiki/1_September" title="1 September">1 September</a>, <a href="/wiki/1858" class="mw-redirect" title="1858">1858</a>, the day that eleven-year-old Angus was due to begin his <a href="/wiki/First_year" title="First year">first year</a> at Hogwarts, Hamish flew him to <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Castle" title="Hogwarts Castle">Hogwarts Castle</a> on the back of <a href="/wiki/Hamish_Buchanan%27s_broomstick" title="Hamish Buchanan&#39;s broomstick">his broomstick</a>, desperately hoping that the faculty would allow him to stay once he arrived, or that the school might be able to coax some magic out of him.<sup id="cite_ref-Pottermore_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Pottermore-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
