<b>Patricia Stimpson</b> (born c. <a href="/wiki/1977" title="1977">1977</a>-<a href="/wiki/1978" title="1978">1978</a>) was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch</a> who began attending <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> in <a href="/wiki/1989" title="1989">1989</a>. <a href="/wiki/George_Weasley" title="George Weasley">George Weasley</a> stated that she repeatedly fainted out of stress in the weeks preceding her <a href="/wiki/Ordinary_Wizarding_Level" title="Ordinary Wizarding Level">O.W.L.s</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP12_2-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP12-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
