<b>Alejandra Alonso</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Brazil" title="Brazil">Brazilian</a> <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch</a> and a <a href="/wiki/Chaser" title="Chaser">Chaser</a> for the <a href="/wiki/2014" title="2014">2014</a> <a href="/wiki/Brazilian_National_Quidditch_team" title="Brazilian National Quidditch team">Brazilian National Quidditch team</a>. In her article for the <a href="/wiki/Daily_Prophet" title="Daily Prophet">Daily Prophet</a> on the <a href="/wiki/American_National_Quidditch_team" title="American National Quidditch team">USA</a>-Brazil game, <a href="/wiki/Ginevra_Weasley" title="Ginevra Weasley">Ginny Potter</a> described her as one of Brazil's finest. Ludo Bagman also put her down as the Outstanding Player on the Brazil team during the first two rounds of the <a href="/wiki/2014_Quidditch_World_Cup" title="2014 Quidditch World Cup">2014 Quidditch World Cup</a> in his guide which rates the teams who made it to the semi-finals.<sup id="cite_ref-Insider_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Insider-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Alejandra certainly had a solid game during the tournament. During the Brazil-<a href="/wiki/Haitian_National_Quidditch_team" title="Haitian National Quidditch team">Haiti</a> match, her and her fellow Brazil Chasers, <a href="/wiki/Fernando_Diaz" title="Fernando Diaz">Diaz</a> and <a href="/wiki/Gon%C3%A7alo_Flores" title="GonÃ§alo Flores">Flores</a>, made as many as thirty attempts to score during the beginning of the game, although they only made ten goals. Ginny Potter also commended all three Brazilian Chasers for keeping their heads during the Brazil-<a href="/wiki/Welsh_National_Quidditch_team" title="Welsh National Quidditch team">Wales</a> match that was deemed the most contentious matches of the tournament. 
</p><p>During the USA-Brazil semifinal game, she fumbled the <a href="/wiki/Quaffle" title="Quaffle">Quaffle</a> twice, as did her teammate, Fernando Diaz. She compensated for this error, however, by scoring when <a href="/wiki/Susan_Blancheflower" title="Susan Blancheflower">Susan Blancheflower</a> was hit in the face by a <a href="/wiki/Bludger" title="Bludger">Bludger</a>, and put Brazil ten points ahead.
</p>
