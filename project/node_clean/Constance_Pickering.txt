<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Constance_Pickering?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Constance_Pickering?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_life">Early life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Constance_Pickering?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Early life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Constance_Pickering?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Early life"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Born somewhere in the <a href="/wiki/Great_Britain" title="Great Britain">British Isles</a> to parents (one of whom was at least magical) around the dawn of the <a href="/wiki/20th_century" title="20th century">20th century</a>.
</p><p>As a child, she lived next door to a <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggle</a> girl, <a href="/wiki/Silvia" title="Silvia">Silvia</a>. Constance convinced her that "<a href="/wiki/Knarl" title="Knarl">hedgehogs</a>" loved it when you left them snacks overnight. Silvia left out a plate and and the next day her garden was destroyed, leaving Constance feeling guilty.<sup id="cite_ref-BabyBeasts_7-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-BabyBeasts-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>She once had a <a href="/wiki/Crup" title="Crup">Crup</a> named <a href="/wiki/Ross" title="Ross">Ross</a>, but her family had to give him away when Constance's brother turned out to be a <a href="/wiki/Squib" title="Squib">squib</a>, and Ross just wouldn't stop nipping his bum, which scarred him for life.<sup id="cite_ref-BabyBeasts_7-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-BabyBeasts-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>When Constance was a girl, her family took her to a <a href="/wiki/Norwegian_Dragon_Sanctuary" title="Norwegian Dragon Sanctuary">Dragon sanctuary</a> in <a href="/wiki/Norway" title="Norway">Norway</a>. They got to see the baby <a href="/wiki/Dragon" title="Dragon">dragons</a> up close and one of them bit Constance's finger, which hurt for a month.<sup id="cite_ref-BabyBeasts_7-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-BabyBeasts-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Constance always thought <a href="/wiki/Christmas_cracker" title="Christmas cracker">Christmas crackers</a> looked like loads of fun when her <a href="/wiki/Constance_Pickering%27s_mother" title="Constance Pickering&#39;s mother">mother</a> and <a href="/wiki/Constance_Pickering%27s_sister" title="Constance Pickering&#39;s sister">sister</a> pulled them together. The pop always made her squeal with delight. But they were never able to have a tree at they house. Constance's mum said she was “allergic” to decorating. Therefore Constance loved looking in their neighbours' windows and seeing their trees, all bright and shiny.<sup id="cite_ref-ChristmasCalamity_4-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-ChristmasCalamity-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>When Constance was ten years old, her <a href="/wiki/Constance_Pickering%27s_grandmother" title="Constance Pickering&#39;s grandmother">grandmother</a> came over for Christmas. She wore a <a href="/wiki/Badger" title="Badger">badger</a> brooch that Constance pilfered to play with. As punishment, Constance spent the rest of Christmas locked in the attic.<sup id="cite_ref-ChristmasCalamity_4-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-ChristmasCalamity-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>As a little girl, Constance used to adore vintage circus posters. However, during a wave of the <a href="/wiki/Calamity" title="Calamity">Calamity</a> focusing on the <a href="/wiki/Circus_Arcanus" title="Circus Arcanus">Circus Arcanus</a>, she finally saw the old-time circus up close and lost the desire to go to one. She also hated clowns.<sup id="cite_ref-CircusCalamitous_8-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-CircusCalamitous-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Hogwarts_years">Hogwarts years</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Constance_Pickering?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Hogwarts years">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Constance_Pickering?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Hogwarts years"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Constance displayed signs of <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magical</a> ability at some point in her early childhood and was subsequently admitted to <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a>, which issued her a <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Acceptance_letter" class="mw-redirect" title="Hogwarts Acceptance letter">letter of acceptance</a> on her eleventh birthday. Her <a href="/wiki/Constance_Pickering%27s_mother" title="Constance Pickering&#39;s mother">mother</a> wouldn't buy her a new cauldron. She gave Constance <a href="/wiki/Constance_Pickering%27s_brother_(II)" title="Constance Pickering&#39;s brother (II)">her brother</a>'s old, used one and bought him a new one.<sup id="cite_ref-FirstYearAtHogwarts_5-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-FirstYearAtHogwarts-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> She later boarded the <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Express" title="Hogwarts Express">Hogwarts Express</a> from <a href="/wiki/Platform_Nine_and_Three-Quarters" title="Platform Nine and Three-Quarters">Platform 9¾</a> at <a href="/wiki/King%27s_Cross_Station" title="King&#39;s Cross Station">King's Cross Station</a> in order to embark on her first year of magical education.
</p><p>At the time of her attendance, which began no earlier than <a href="/wiki/September" title="September">September</a> <a href="/wiki/1998" title="1998">1998</a>, the school was under the firm stewardship of <a href="/wiki/Professor" title="Professor">Professor</a> <a href="/wiki/Minerva_McGonagall" title="Minerva McGonagall">Minerva McGonagall</a>. Also she never met <a href="/wiki/Horace_Slughorn" title="Horace Slughorn">Professor Slughorn</a>, but always thought she would have been a perfect candidate for the "<a href="/wiki/Slug_Club" title="Slug Club">Slug Club</a>".<sup id="cite_ref-FirstYearAtHogwarts_5-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-FirstYearAtHogwarts-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> Throughout her childhood, she heard stories about the <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a> that the school's inspirational <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Headmaster" title="Hogwarts Headmaster">Headmistress</a> and her allies had fought against at the legendary <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a>, and found the group to be terrifying.<sup id="cite_ref-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>As a student, one of her favourite things about school life was the <a href="/wiki/Christmas" title="Christmas">Christmas</a> <a href="/wiki/Christmas_Feast" title="Christmas Feast">celebration</a> at the <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Castle" title="Hogwarts Castle">Hogwarts Castle</a>, and would later go on to leave the school with many fond memories about spending the <a href="/wiki/Christmas_holidays" title="Christmas holidays">holidays</a> at the school; marvelling at the pudding, the baubles, the trees and the fairy lights. At some point, she also learned that there had been a <a href="/wiki/Yule_Ball" title="Yule Ball">Yule Ball</a> that took place during the <a href="/wiki/Triwizard_Tournament" title="Triwizard Tournament">Triwizard Tournament</a> in <a href="/wiki/1994" title="1994">1994</a> and later admitted to having harboured high hopes that the competition would be held once more just so her generation got one of their own.<sup id="cite_ref-ChristmasCalamity_4-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-ChristmasCalamity-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Constance loathe the <a href="/wiki/The_Monster_Book_of_Monsters" title="The Monster Book of Monsters">Monster Book of Monsters</a>, because her copy bit her once.<sup id="cite_ref-FirstYearAtHogwarts_5-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-FirstYearAtHogwarts-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>She was never quite as adept at <a href="/wiki/Charms_(class)" title="Charms (class)">Charms</a> as she would have liked. She failed in Charms, and never really got over it. They had a parent's meeting which included <a href="/wiki/Headmaster" title="Headmaster">Headmistress</a> <a href="/wiki/Minerva_McGonagall" title="Minerva McGonagall">McGonagall</a>. It was awful, and Constance spent the time between classes practising various <a href="/wiki/Charm" title="Charm">charms</a> on her pet <a href="/wiki/Newt" title="Newt">newt</a>, <a href="/wiki/Nevin" title="Nevin">Nevin</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-FirstYearAtHogwarts_5-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-FirstYearAtHogwarts-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Career">Career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Constance_Pickering?veaction=edit&amp;section=4" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Constance_Pickering?action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Career"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Constance almost became a <a href="/wiki/Magizoologist" title="Magizoologist">Magizoologist</a> when she graduated Hogwarts. But she didn't have the stomach for it. One cold night hiding in a bramble bush by a <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggle</a> field, waiting for a <a href="/wiki/Mooncalf" title="Mooncalf">Mooncalf</a> to do its dance was all it took. Constance was out looking for a new post by morning.<sup id="cite_ref-WU_10-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-WU-10">&#91;10&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Working_for_the_Ministry">Working for the Ministry</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Constance_Pickering?veaction=edit&amp;section=5" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Working for the Ministry">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Constance_Pickering?action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Working for the Ministry"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<figure class="thumb tright show-info-icon" style="width: 127px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/9/96/HPWU_BE_Stronger_United_-_Constance_Pickering.png/revision/latest?cb=20200704054650" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/9/96/HPWU_BE_Stronger_United_-_Constance_Pickering.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/125?cb=20200704054650" decoding="async" width="125" height="441" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="HPWU BE Stronger United - Constance Pickering.png" data-image-key="HPWU_BE_Stronger_United_-_Constance_Pickering.png" /></a> 	 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:HPWU_BE_Stronger_United_-_Constance_Pickering.png" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption">Constance as a Ministry employee</p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure>
<p>Seeing her as an icon, there was nothing Constance wanted more upon graduating from Hogwarts than the chance to work with <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione Granger</a>, a high-ranking official in the <a href="/wiki/Department_of_Magical_Law_Enforcement" title="Department of Magical Law Enforcement">Department of Magical Law Enforcement</a> and famously progressive voice that had played no small part in the eradication of oppressive, <a href="/wiki/Pure-blood_supremacy" title="Pure-blood supremacy">pro-pureblood</a> laws following her death-defying participation in the legendary <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a>. As such, when she applied for the position as a <a href="/wiki/Junior_Undersecretary" title="Junior Undersecretary">Junior Undersecretary</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Department_of_Magical_Law_Enforcement" title="Department of Magical Law Enforcement">Department of Magical Law Enforcement</a>, where her hero worked, Constance discreetly slipped her application into the pile that she knew Mrs Granger would draw her future assistant from, and to her delight found herself picked for the job.<sup id="cite_ref-11" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-11">&#91;11&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="The_Calamity">The Calamity</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Constance_Pickering?veaction=edit&amp;section=6" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: The Calamity">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Constance_Pickering?action=edit&amp;section=6" title="Edit section: The Calamity"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<p>In the <a href="/wiki/2010s" title="2010s">2010s</a>, during the series of large-scale breaches of the <a href="/wiki/International_Statute_of_Wizarding_Secrecy" title="International Statute of Wizarding Secrecy">Statute of Wizarding Secrecy</a> that became known as <a href="/wiki/Calamity" title="Calamity">The Calamity</a>, the <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">British Ministry of Magic</a> and the <a href="/wiki/International_Confederation_of_Wizards" title="International Confederation of Wizards">International Confederation of Wizards</a> worked together to set up an <a href="/wiki/International_Task_Force" title="International Task Force">International Task Force</a> whose job it would be to track down and contain the chaotic outbreaks of <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magic</a> from being noticed by <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggles</a>. To this end, Ms Pickering was asked by <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione Granger</a> upon its sanction to set up what would become known as the <a href="/wiki/Statute_of_Secrecy_Task_Force" title="Statute of Secrecy Task Force">Statute of Secrecy Task Force</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-12" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-12">&#91;12&#93;</a></sup> At some point, presumably in her capacity as the leader of the <a href="/wiki/Statute_of_Secrecy_Task_Force" title="Statute of Secrecy Task Force">Statute of Secrecy Task Force</a>, she verified the casting of a <a href="/wiki/Muggle-Repelling_Charm" title="Muggle-Repelling Charm">Muggle-Repelling Charm</a> over an undisclosed object or location.<sup id="cite_ref-13" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-13">&#91;13&#93;</a></sup> She was also the one who suggested using banners to identify areas of high concentration of <a href="/wiki/Foundable" title="Foundable">Foundables</a> that seemed to be somehow related to one another, at which point her boyfriend, <a href="/wiki/Lucas_Sparrowvale" title="Lucas Sparrowvale">Lucas Sparrowvale</a>, proceeded to spend no less than six months travelling the world and placing them.<sup id="cite_ref-14" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-14">&#91;14&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>In <a href="/wiki/2019" title="2019">2019</a>, the <a href="/wiki/Pickering_family" title="Pickering family">Pickering family</a> had Christmas at the new house Constance's sister, but they did not send Constance any details or address. As a result, Constance was invited to a Christmas dinner at the <a href="/wiki/Potter_family" title="Potter family">Potter family</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-ChristmasCalamity_4-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-ChristmasCalamity-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Magical_abilities_and_skills">Magical abilities and skills</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Constance_Pickering?veaction=edit&amp;section=7" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Constance_Pickering?action=edit&amp;section=7" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>An accomplished witch, Constance Pickering became an important member of the <a href="/wiki/Calamity_Management_Team" title="Calamity Management Team">Calamity Management Team</a> and was eventually tasked with heading up the <a href="/wiki/Statute_of_Secrecy_Task_Force" title="Statute of Secrecy Task Force">Statute of Secrecy Task Force</a>, whose job it was to investigate and contain the outbreaks of chaotic magic unleashed by the <a href="/wiki/Calamity" title="Calamity">Calamity</a>, showing the trust that the Ministry had in her abilities, both as a leader and where her magical prowess was concerned.
</p>
<ul><li><b><a href="/wiki/Charm" title="Charm">Charms</a></b>: Constance appeared to be highly accomplished with charms, as she was able to conjure a corporeal <a href="/wiki/Patronus_Charm" title="Patronus Charm">Patronus</a> in the form of a <a href="/wiki/Unicorn" title="Unicorn">Unicorn</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-BabyBeasts_7-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-BabyBeasts-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">Magical</a> <a href="/wiki/Creature" title="Creature">creatures</a> were among the rarer Patronus forms.<sup id="cite_ref-WW_15-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-WW-15">&#91;15&#93;</a></sup> As leader of the <a href="/wiki/Statute_of_Secrecy_Task_Force" title="Statute of Secrecy Task Force">Statute of Secrecy Task Force</a>, she was also involved in returning <a href="/wiki/Foundable" title="Foundable">Foundables</a>.</li></ul>
