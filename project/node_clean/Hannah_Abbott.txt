<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix">"<i>All the fifth years were suffering from stress to some degree, but Hannah Abbott became the first to receive a <a href="/wiki/Calming_Draught" title="Calming Draught">Calming Draught</a> from <a href="/wiki/Poppy_Pomfrey" title="Poppy Pomfrey">Madam Pomfrey</a> after she burst into tears during <a href="/wiki/Herbology" title="Herbology">Herbology</a> and sobbed that she was too stupid to take exams and wanted to leave school now.</i>"</span></dd>
<dd>&#8212;Hannah's easily flustered personality<sup class="noprint"><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Order_of_the_Phoenix" title="Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix">[src]</a></sup></dd>

<p><b>Hannah Abbott</b> (b. <a href="/wiki/1979" title="1979">1979</a>/<a href="/wiki/1980" title="1980">1980</a>)<sup id="cite_ref-birth_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-birth-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> was a <a href="/wiki/Half-blood" title="Half-blood">half-blood</a><sup id="cite_ref-pottercast_2-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-pottercast-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch</a> and a member of the <a href="/wiki/Abbott_family" title="Abbott family">Abbott family</a>. In <a href="/wiki/1991" title="1991">1991</a> she started attending <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> and was <a href="/wiki/Sorting_ceremony" title="Sorting ceremony">sorted</a> into <a href="/wiki/Hufflepuff" title="Hufflepuff">Hufflepuff House</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-PS7_6-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PS7-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup> Hannah was in the same year as the famous <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>.
</p><p>In her <a href="/wiki/Fifth_year" title="Fifth year">fifth year</a>, she became both a <a href="/wiki/Prefect" title="Prefect">prefect</a> and a member of <a href="/wiki/Dumbledore%27s_Army" title="Dumbledore&#39;s Army">Dumbledore's Army</a>, an organisation taught and led by Harry Potter. In <a href="/wiki/1996" title="1996">1996</a>, Hannah was pulled out of school after <a href="/wiki/Hannah_Abbott%27s_mother" title="Hannah Abbott&#39;s mother">her mother</a> was murdered by <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a>, but she returned the following year and was once again a member of the D.A. She fought in the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a> on <a href="/wiki/2_May" title="2 May">2 May</a>, <a href="/wiki/1998" title="1998">1998</a>.
</p><p>Later in life, Hannah married fellow classmate <a href="/wiki/Neville_Longbottom" title="Neville Longbottom">Neville Longbottom</a> and became landlady of the <a href="/wiki/Leaky_Cauldron" title="Leaky Cauldron">Leaky Cauldron</a>, which the couple lived above. She eventually trained as a <a href="/wiki/Healer" title="Healer">healer</a> and applied for the job of <a href="/wiki/Matron" title="Matron">Matron</a> at Hogwarts.<sup id="cite_ref-QWCfinal_3-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-QWCfinal-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_life">Early life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Hannah_Abbott?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Early life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Hannah_Abbott?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Early life"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Hannah was born in <a href="/wiki/Great_Britain" title="Great Britain">Great Britain</a> or <a href="/wiki/Ireland" title="Ireland">Ireland</a> in <a href="/wiki/1979" title="1979">1979</a> or <a href="/wiki/1980" title="1980">1980</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-birth_1-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-birth-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> She was a member of the <a href="/wiki/Pure-blood" title="Pure-blood">pure-blood</a> wizarding <a href="/wiki/Abbott_family" title="Abbott family">Abbott family</a> that was one of the so-called <a href="/wiki/Sacred_Twenty-Eight" title="Sacred Twenty-Eight">Sacred Twenty-Eight</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-Pure_blood_11-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Pure_blood-11">&#91;11&#93;</a></sup> though she herself was a half-blood,<sup id="cite_ref-pottercast_2-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-pottercast-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> indicating she also had some Muggle heritage.
</p>

<p>Hannah began attending <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> on <a href="/wiki/1_September" title="1 September">1 September</a>, <a href="/wiki/1991" title="1991">1991</a>, along with <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-PS7_6-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PS7-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Since Hannah's surname, Abbott, was the first alphabetically, she was the first new student in her year to be sorted into her house during the <a href="/wiki/Welcoming_Feast" title="Welcoming Feast">Welcoming Feast</a>. She was Sorted into <a href="/wiki/Hufflepuff" title="Hufflepuff">Hufflepuff House</a>, along with other new students <a href="/wiki/Ernest_Macmillan" title="Ernest Macmillan">Ernie Macmillan</a>, <a href="/wiki/Susan_Bones" title="Susan Bones">Susan Bones</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Justin_Finch-Fletchley" title="Justin Finch-Fletchley">Justin Finch-Fletchley</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-PS7_6-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PS7-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup> She shared a dormitory with her fellow Hufflepuff classmates; <a href="/wiki/Megan_Jones" title="Megan Jones">Megan Jones</a>, <a href="/wiki/Susan_Bones" title="Susan Bones">Susan Bones</a>, and others.
</p>

<dd></dd></dl></div>
<p>In <a href="/wiki/1993" title="1993">1993</a>, her <a href="/wiki/Third_year" title="Third year">third year</a>, when news got out that <a href="/wiki/Sirius_Black" title="Sirius Black">Sirius Black</a> had escaped from <a href="/wiki/Azkaban" title="Azkaban">Azkaban</a>, Hannah held the belief that <a href="/wiki/Sirius_Black" title="Sirius Black">Black</a> could transform himself into a flowering shrub to avoid detection, and would tell this theory to anyone who would listen.<sup id="cite_ref-POA9_15-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-POA9-15">&#91;15&#93;</a></sup> Even though her theory of transforming into a shrub was incorrect, Sirius Black did in fact use a <a href="/wiki/Animagus" title="Animagus">transformation</a> to avoid detection and escape <a href="/wiki/Azkaban" title="Azkaban">Azkaban</a>; however, his transformation was into the form of a dog. Hannah's belief that Sirius could transform into a flowering shrub was likely to have originated from her liking of <a href="/wiki/Herbology" title="Herbology">Herbology</a> and the <a href="/wiki/Professor" title="Professor">Professor</a> of <a href="/wiki/Herbology" title="Herbology">Herbology</a>, <a href="/wiki/Pomona_Sprout" title="Pomona Sprout">Pomona Sprout</a>, who was her <a href="/wiki/Head_of_House" title="Head of House">Head of House</a>.
</p>

Hannah fully supported him during the tournament's tasks, as he was also in Hufflepuff. Hannah was among the many Hufflepuffs who were displeased when Harry appeared to be usurping Cedric's rightful place, by becoming the second <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts</a> champion during the Triwizard Tournament, and she showed support only for her fellow Hufflepuff.<sup id="cite_ref-GOF19_17-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF19-17">&#91;17&#93;</a></sup>

Hannah was then presumably amongst the students that believed Harry Potter's claims that Lord Voldemort had indeed returned, and that the <a href="/wiki/Second_Wizarding_War" title="Second Wizarding War">Second Wizarding War</a> had really broken out.

<h3><span id="Later_life_(1998-2014)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="Later_life_.281998-2014.29">Later life <span class="template-c">(1998-2014)</span></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Hannah_Abbott?veaction=edit&amp;section=11" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Later life (1998-2014)">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Hannah_Abbott?action=edit&amp;section=11" title="Edit section: Later life (1998-2014)"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Having missed a year in education it is likely that, like <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione Granger</a>, Hannah would resit the same year and they would graduate together in 1999.
</p><p>Some time before <a href="/wiki/2014" title="2014">2014</a>, Hannah married her former classmate and leader of the student rebellion, <a href="/wiki/Neville_Longbottom" title="Neville Longbottom">Neville Longbottom</a>. She became the landlady at the <a href="/wiki/Leaky_Cauldron" title="Leaky Cauldron">Leaky Cauldron</a>, replacing <a href="/wiki/Tom" title="Tom">Tom</a>, and the couple lived together above the pub. Neville's students considered the fact they lived above the pub "cool".<sup id="cite_ref-CH_28-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-CH-28">&#91;28&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Hannah eventually gave up being landlady of the Leaky Cauldron and decided to train as a <a href="/wiki/Healer" title="Healer">healer</a>. She applied for the job of Matron at Hogwarts. On <a href="/wiki/8_July" title="8 July">8 July</a> <a href="/wiki/2014" title="2014">2014</a>, <a href="/wiki/Rita_Skeeter" title="Rita Skeeter">Rita Skeeter</a> scathingly reported that she and Neville enjoyed "<i>a little more Ogden's Firewhisky than most would expect from custodians of our children</i>".<sup id="cite_ref-QWCfinal_3-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-QWCfinal-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> Since Rita enjoyed fabricating information to slander people, it is likely that her statement is untrue.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Physical_description">Physical description</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Hannah_Abbott?veaction=edit&amp;section=12" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Physical description">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Hannah_Abbott?action=edit&amp;section=12" title="Edit section: Physical description"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Hannah was described as a pink-faced girl with blonde hair. In her younger years, she usually wore pigtails.<sup id="cite_ref-hair.26skin_7-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-hair.26skin-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Personality_and_traits">Personality and traits</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Hannah_Abbott?veaction=edit&amp;section=13" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Personality and traits">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Hannah_Abbott?action=edit&amp;section=13" title="Edit section: Personality and traits"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Hannah appeared to have a naive streak since she was easily convinced by Ernie that Harry might be the <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Heir of Slytherin</a>, despite having always considered him to be nice.<sup id="cite_ref-COS11_12-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COS11-12">&#91;12&#93;</a></sup> She also believed that <a href="/wiki/Sirius_Black" title="Sirius Black">Sirius Black</a> could turn into a flowering shrub (although, given that he was, in fact, an <a href="/wiki/Animagus" title="Animagus">Animagus</a>, the belief wasn't too far from the truth).<sup id="cite_ref-POA9_15-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-POA9-15">&#91;15&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Hannah was easily flustered and appeared to lack self-confidence, as was evidenced by her breakdown in <a href="/wiki/Herbology" title="Herbology">Herbology</a> and her claims of being stupid.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP27_19-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP27-19">&#91;19&#93;</a></sup> It seemed that, when under pressure, she was prone to making mistakes.<sup id="cite_ref-ootp31_20-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-ootp31-20">&#91;20&#93;</a></sup> Despite this, Hannah was a loyal friend and showed her bravery firstly by joining <a href="/wiki/Dumbledore%27s_Army" title="Dumbledore&#39;s Army">Dumbledore's Army</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP16_18-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP16-18">&#91;18&#93;</a></sup> and then fighting in the defence of Hogwarts.<sup id="cite_ref-DH31_26-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH31-26">&#91;26&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Magical_abilities_and_skills">Magical abilities and skills</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Hannah_Abbott?veaction=edit&amp;section=14" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Hannah_Abbott?action=edit&amp;section=14" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li><b><a href="/wiki/Defence_Against_the_Dark_Arts" title="Defence Against the Dark Arts">Defence Against the Dark Arts</a></b>: Hannah joined <a href="/wiki/Dumbledore%27s_Army" title="Dumbledore&#39;s Army">Dumbledore's Army</a> in her <a href="/wiki/Fifth_year" title="Fifth year">fifth year</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP16_18-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP16-18">&#91;18&#93;</a></sup> and learned how to cast various defensive and offensive <a href="/wiki/Spell" title="Spell">spells</a>, under the masterful teaching of <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP38_21-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP38-21">&#91;21&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Duelling" title="Duelling">Duelling</a></b>: Hannah helped retaliate against <a href="/wiki/Draco_Malfoy" title="Draco Malfoy">Draco Malfoy</a> and his <a href="/wiki/Draco_Malfoy%27s_gang" title="Draco Malfoy&#39;s gang">friends</a> when they tried to gang up on and attack Harry Potter, where she and her friends managed to <a href="/wiki/Hex" title="Hex">hex</a> them into having the appearances of <a href="/wiki/Slug" title="Slug">slugs</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP38_21-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP38-21">&#91;21&#93;</a></sup> She also impressively fought in and survived the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a>, showing her to have been an excellent duellist with considerable proficiency in <a href="/wiki/Martial_magic" title="Martial magic">martial magic</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-DH31_26-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH31-26">&#91;26&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-DH36_27-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH36-27">&#91;27&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Herbology" title="Herbology">Herbology</a></b>: Hannah achieved a high mark on her <a href="/wiki/Ordinary_Wizarding_Level" title="Ordinary Wizarding Level">O.W.L.</a> exam, and in her <a href="/wiki/Sixth_year" title="Sixth year">sixth year</a> she attended N.E.W.T. Herbology classes, showing her proficiency in the subject.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP11_8-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP11-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Healing_magic" title="Healing magic">Healing magic</a></b>: In her adulthood, Hannah trained to become a <a href="/wiki/Healer" title="Healer">Healer</a> and applied for the position of the Hogwarts <a href="/wiki/Matron" title="Matron">Matron</a>, meaning she would have been very accomplished and learned in healing magic, including with <a href="/wiki/Healing_spell" title="Healing spell">healing spells</a>, <a href="/wiki/Potion" title="Potion">potions</a> and other magical remedies.<sup id="cite_ref-QWCfinal_3-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-QWCfinal-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Charm" title="Charm">Charms</a></b>: Hannah was capable of conjuring a non-corporeal <a href="/wiki/Patronus_Charm" title="Patronus Charm">Patronus</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-LEGOY2_10-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-LEGOY2-10">&#91;10&#93;</a></sup> a mark of superior <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magical</a> ability given how difficult the said charm is to perform. She likely learned how to cast the spell whilst attending Dumbledore's Army meetings.</li></ul>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Possessions">Possessions</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Hannah_Abbott?veaction=edit&amp;section=15" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Possessions">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Hannah_Abbott?action=edit&amp;section=15" title="Edit section: Possessions"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li><b><a href="/wiki/Wand" title="Wand">Wand</a></b>: Hannah owned a wand, which was of an unknown length, <a href="/wiki/Wand_wood" title="Wand wood">wood</a> and <a href="/wiki/Wand_core" title="Wand core">core</a> material. Like most witches and wizards in <a href="/wiki/Great_Britain" title="Great Britain">Great Britain</a>, she presumably purchased it from <a href="/wiki/Ollivanders" title="Ollivanders">Ollivanders</a> in <a href="/wiki/Diagon_Alley" title="Diagon Alley">Diagon Alley</a>, <a href="/wiki/London" title="London">London</a>, prior to starting her <a href="/wiki/First_year" title="First year">first year</a> at Hogwarts in <a href="/wiki/1991" title="1991">1991</a>. She would have wielded her wand throughout her school life, and during the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a> in <a href="/wiki/1998" title="1998">1998</a>.</li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Leaky_Cauldron" title="Leaky Cauldron">Leaky Cauldron</a></b>: After graduating from Hogwarts, Hannah eventually become the Landlady of the Leaky Cauldron, the popular wizarding pub in Diagon Alley, thus becoming its owner.</li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Prefect_badge" title="Prefect badge">Prefect badge</a></b>: Hannah owned a Hufflepuff Prefect badge, prior to becoming a <a href="/wiki/Prefect" title="Prefect">Prefect</a> at the start of the <a href="/wiki/1995%E2%80%931996_school_year" title="1995–1996 school year">1995–1996 school year</a>, which she would have worn during her Prefect duties.</li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Potter_Stinks_badge" title="Potter Stinks badge">Potter Stinks badge</a></b>: At the start of the <a href="/wiki/Triwizard_Tournament" title="Triwizard Tournament">Triwizard Tournament</a> in <a href="/wiki/1994" title="1994">1994</a>, Hannah owned and wore one of these enchanted badges to show her contempt for Harry Potter becoming the second Hogwarts champion, believing he was stealing <a href="/wiki/Cedric_Diggory" title="Cedric Diggory">Cedric Diggory</a>'s limelight. She stopped wearing hers after becoming a supporter of Harry during the Tournament.</li></ul>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Relationships">Relationships</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Hannah_Abbott?veaction=edit&amp;section=16" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Relationships">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Hannah_Abbott?action=edit&amp;section=16" title="Edit section: Relationships"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Neville_Longbottom">Neville Longbottom</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Hannah_Abbott?veaction=edit&amp;section=17" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Neville Longbottom">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Hannah_Abbott?action=edit&amp;section=17" title="Edit section: Neville Longbottom"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>

<p>Although it appeared that Hannah and Neville Longbottom were rarely ever together during their school days, and given that Hannah supported <a href="/wiki/Cedric_Diggory" title="Cedric Diggory">Cedric Diggory</a> in their fourth year, their friendship probably evolved from their time in <a href="/wiki/Dumbledore%27s_Army" title="Dumbledore&#39;s Army">Dumbledore's Army</a> together, and during the student rebellion during their seventh year, as well as just being in the same year and sharing classes for so long — particularly <a href="/wiki/Herbology" title="Herbology">Herbology</a>, Neville's best subject, which the <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor" title="Gryffindor">Gryffindors</a> shared with the <a href="/wiki/Hufflepuff" title="Hufflepuff">Hufflepuffs</a>.
</p><p>It is possible that Hannah and Neville both found comfort in each other during their seventh year, since in the previous year Hannah's mother had been killed by <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a>, and Neville would understand how she would be feeling as Death Eaters tortured his parents into <a href="/wiki/Insanity" title="Insanity">insanity</a>. Hannah joined Dumbledore's Army under the leadership of Neville, and in the absence of Harry, and proved her loyalty to him throughout the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a>.
</p><p>Following the <a href="/wiki/Second_Wizarding_War" title="Second Wizarding War">Second Wizarding War</a>, Hannah and Neville became closer and eventually married. Neville became the new Professor of Herbology at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts</a>, taking over from <a href="/wiki/Pomona_Sprout" title="Pomona Sprout">Professor Sprout</a>, whilst Hannah became the new landlady of the <a href="/wiki/Leaky_Cauldron" title="Leaky Cauldron">Leaky Cauldron</a>, taking over from <a href="/wiki/Tom" title="Tom">Tom</a>. The couple lived together above the pub, a fact that Neville's students thought was "cool".<sup id="cite_ref-CH_28-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-CH-28">&#91;28&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Hannah eventually applied for the job of Matron at Hogwarts, which would have brought her closer to Neville.
</p>

<p>Hannah and Ernie Macmillan were close friends during their time at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a>, both having been sorted into the house of <a href="/wiki/Hufflepuff" title="Hufflepuff">Hufflepuff</a> during their first year. During their second year they both discussed the possibility of Harry being the Heir of <a href="/wiki/Slytherin" title="Slytherin">Slytherin</a> after the Chamber of Secrets had been declared open. Hannah had her doubts, as she thought Harry was nice, but Ernie was easily able to sway her opinion.<sup id="cite_ref-COS11_12-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COS11-12">&#91;12&#93;</a></sup>

</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Hannah_Abbott?veaction=edit&amp;section=21" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Hannah_Abbott?action=edit&amp;section=21" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>"Hannah" is from the Hebrew name חַנָּה (Channah) which meant "favour" or "grace". Hannah was the mother of the prophet Samuel in the Old Testament. As an English name, Hannah was not regularly used until after the Protestant Reformation.<sup id="cite_ref-29" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-29">&#91;29&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li>"Abbott" is a Middle English surname.<sup id="cite_ref-30" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-30">&#91;30&#93;</a></sup> An abbot was a church position below the pope, archbishops and bishops in the Church hierarchy in the Middle Ages. The ancestors of people with the surname Abbott probably were abbots or abbesses in medieval times (900-1300).</li></ul>

<ul><li>During the <a href="/wiki/Ordinary_Wizarding_Level" title="Ordinary Wizarding Level">O.W.L.s</a>, Hannah Abbott was doing her <a href="/wiki/Transfiguration_(class)" title="Transfiguration (class)">Transfiguration</a> practical at the same time as <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>. This is odd as the examinations are done by surname, meaning that Hannah should have finished a while earlier. One possible explanation is that the lists for that examination were mistakenly compiled by first name. Another is that Hannah's exam took an unusually long time, possibly due to her nerves.<sup id="cite_ref-ootp31_20-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-ootp31-20">&#91;20&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> and <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione Granger</a> saw the grave of someone named "<a href="/wiki/Abbott_(Godric%27s_Hollow)" class="mw-redirect" title="Abbott (Godric&#39;s Hollow)">Abbott</a>" when they visited <a href="/wiki/Godric%27s_Hollow" title="Godric&#39;s Hollow">Godric's Hollow</a> in <a href="/wiki/1997" title="1997">1997</a>. This may have been the grave of a relative of Hannah's, perhaps indicating that her <a href="/wiki/Abbott_family" title="Abbott family">family</a>, like the <a href="/wiki/Potter_family" title="Potter family">Potters</a>, come from Godric's Hollow.</li>
<li>While Hannah's mother was revealed to be killed by the Death Eaters, her father's fate was left unknown, indicating that he was either alive or deceased by 1996. So, Hannah may have possibly been left an orphan after her mother's death. Alternatively, her father could have been the one who pulled her out of Hogwarts.</li>
<li>Hannah Abbott was portrayed by <a href="/wiki/Charlotte_Skeoch" title="Charlotte Skeoch">Charlotte Skeoch</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_(film_series)" title="Harry Potter (film series)">film adaptations</a> of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Chamber_of_Secrets_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets (film)">Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets</a></i> and <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Goblet_of_Fire_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire (film)">Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire</a></i>, as well as voicing the character for the game <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Order_of_the_Phoenix_(video_game)" title="Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix (video game)">Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix</a></i>. She was portrayed by <a href="/wiki/Louisa_Warren" title="Louisa Warren">Louisa Warren</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Deathly_Hallows_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows (film)">film adaptations</a> of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Deathly_Hallows" title="Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows">Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows</a></i> in a background role.</li>
<li>She is one of the Original Forty students listed by <a href="/wiki/J._K._Rowling" title="J. K. Rowling">J. K. Rowling</a> along with <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>, <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione Granger</a> and <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ronald Weasley</a>.</li>
<li>From early production notes viewed in the <a href="/wiki/2001" title="2001">2001</a> television special, <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_Me" title="Harry Potter and Me">Harry Potter and Me</a></i>, Hannah was supposed to be a <a href="/wiki/Muggle-born" title="Muggle-born">Muggle-born</a> witch.<sup id="cite_ref-31" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-31">&#91;31&#93;</a></sup> However, J. K. Rowling stated in an <a href="/wiki/J.K._Rowling_PotterCast_Interview" title="J.K. Rowling PotterCast Interview">interview</a> that she always considered Hannah to be a <a href="/wiki/Pure-blood" title="Pure-blood">pure-blood</a>. To compromise, she was officially made a <a href="/wiki/Half-blood" title="Half-blood">half-blood</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-pottercast_2-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-pottercast-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li>In the credits of the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Chamber_of_Secrets_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets (film)">film adaptation</a> of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Chamber_of_Secrets" title="Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets">Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets</a></i>, she is listed as "Hannah Hufflepuff".<sup id="cite_ref-32" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-32">&#91;32&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li>In the <a href="/wiki/Sorting_ceremony" title="Sorting ceremony">sorting</a> scene in the the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Philosopher%27s_Stone_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Philosopher&#39;s Stone (film)">film adaptation</a> of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Philosopher%27s_Stone" title="Harry Potter and the Philosopher&#39;s Stone">Harry Potter and the Philosopher's Stone</a></i>, <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione Granger</a> gets called up first instead of Hannah Abbott.</li>
<li>An Alice Abbott was accused of witchcraft during the Northhamptonshire witch trials of 1612.</li></ul>
