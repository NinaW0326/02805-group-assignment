<b>Merula Snyde's gang</b> was founded by <a href="/wiki/Merula_Snyde" title="Merula Snyde">Merula Snyde</a> in <a href="/wiki/1984" title="1984">1984</a> to assist her in seeking out clues to the location of the <a href="/wiki/Cursed_Vaults" title="Cursed Vaults">Cursed Vaults</a>, as well as to possibly bully <a href="/wiki/Muggle-born" title="Muggle-born">Muggle-borns</a> and <a href="/wiki/Blood_traitor" title="Blood traitor">Blood traitors</a>. This gang was overall very inefficient in carrying out its intended purpose.

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="History">History</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Merula_Snyde%27s_gang?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: History">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Merula_Snyde%27s_gang?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: History"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p><a href="/wiki/Jacob%27s_sibling" title="Jacob&#39;s sibling">Jacob's sibling</a> first encountered Merula Snyde's gang during the <a href="/wiki/Wideye_Potion" title="Wideye Potion">Wideye Potion</a> lesson in the <a href="/wiki/1986%E2%80%931987_school_year" title="1986–1987 school year">1986–1987 school year</a>. Merula sarcastically welcomed back Jacob's sibling, while stating her new associates, <a href="/wiki/Ismelda_Murk" title="Ismelda Murk">Ismelda Murk</a> and <a href="/wiki/Barnaby_Lee" title="Barnaby Lee">Barnaby Lee</a>, would assist her in opening the remaining <a href="/wiki/Cursed_Vaults" title="Cursed Vaults">Cursed Vaults</a>.
</p><p>After the class, they discussed what could possibly be in the next vaults, to which Merula rudely stated that it could be something that can bring back <a href="/wiki/Jacob_(Hogwarts_student)" title="Jacob (Hogwarts student)">Jacob</a>. Regardless of Jacob's sibling's reponse, Merula's gang left, with the exception of Barnaby, staying back to threaten Jacob's sibling with "vanishing every bone" in their body should they continue to interfere with Merula's plans. Jacob's sibling attempted to reason with Barnaby by pointing out that Merula was terrible to everyone around her and she was most likely lying to Barnaby in order to manipulate him. Barnaby pondered on Jacob's sibling statement for a moment, before rebuffing them and leaving the class.<sup id="cite_ref-y3ch2_1-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-y3ch2-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
