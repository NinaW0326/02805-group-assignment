This <b>individual</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Great_Britain" title="Great Britain">British</a> <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">Wizard</a> who attended <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> in <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor" title="Gryffindor">Gryffindor</a> house in the <a href="/wiki/1920s" title="1920s">1920s</a>. At that time, he became friends with another student named <a href="/wiki/McClaggan" title="McClaggan">McClaggan</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-COGS_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COGS-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
