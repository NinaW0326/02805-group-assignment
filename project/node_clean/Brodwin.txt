<figure class="thumb tleft show-info-icon" style="width: 182px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/e/ec/Queen2.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20091229080038" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/e/ec/Queen2.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/180?cb=20091229080038" decoding="async" width="180" height="91" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="Queen2.jpg" data-image-key="Queen2.jpg" /></a> 	 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:Queen2.jpg" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption">Brodwin, when flying</p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure>
<ul><li>In the short skit <i><a href="/wiki/The_Queen%27s_Handbag" title="The Queen&#39;s Handbag">The Queen's Handbag</a></i>, Brodwin was sent to <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>, <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ronald Weasley</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione Granger</a> at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts</a> when the Queen's Handbag went missing. The <a href="/wiki/Owl" title="Owl">owl</a> landed on a chair in the <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor_Tower" title="Gryffindor Tower">Gryffindor common room</a> with the letter about the disappearance. Ron initially believed the letter came from the <a href="/wiki/Minister_for_Magic" title="Minister for Magic">Minister for Magic</a>, however Hermione realised it actually came from the <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggle</a> <a href="/wiki/Prime_Minister" title="Prime Minister">Prime Minister</a>. After watching Harry and Ron attempt to use a <a href="/wiki/Summoning_Charm" title="Summoning Charm">Summoning Charm</a> to retrieve the handbag, the owl was given a letter from Hermione granting <a href="/wiki/Buckingham_Palace" title="Buckingham Palace">Buckingham Palace</a> a temporary allowance of <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magic</a> so they could <a href="/wiki/Summoning_Charm" title="Summoning Charm">summon</a> the handbag themselves.</li>
<li>Although this owl looks similar to Harry's owl <a href="/wiki/Hedwig" title="Hedwig">Hedwig</a>, since Harry did not recognise it, it would seem to be a different owl. In the beginning of the skit, however, the owl is referred to as "Brodwin".</li></ul>
