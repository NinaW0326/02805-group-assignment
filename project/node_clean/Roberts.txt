<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Roberts?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Roberts?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>He lived with his <a href="/wiki/Roberts%27s_wife" title="Roberts&#39;s wife">wife</a> and <a href="/wiki/Roberts_children" title="Roberts children">two children</a> in a stone cottage at the front of the campground. His youngest child was a boy.<sup id="cite_ref-GOF7_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF7-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Mr Roberts believed that his <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizarding</a> guests were confused with <a href="/wiki/Muggle_money" title="Muggle money">Muggle money</a> because they were all from out of the country; one even tried to pay him with a <a href="/wiki/Galleon" title="Galleon">galleon</a>. <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">Ministry of Magic</a> employees were required to perform <a href="/wiki/Memory_Charm" title="Memory Charm">Memory Charms</a> on him several times a day.<sup id="cite_ref-GOF7_1-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF7-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<figure class="thumb tright show-info-icon" style="width: 182px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/c/cf/Robertses_GOF_trailer.gif/revision/latest?cb=20181104170947" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/c/cf/Robertses_GOF_trailer.gif/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/180?cb=20181104170947" decoding="async" width="180" height="77" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="Robertses GOF trailer.gif" data-image-key="Robertses_GOF_trailer.gif" /></a> 	 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:Robertses_GOF_trailer.gif" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption">Roberts and his family being levitated into the air by Death Eaters during the <a href="/wiki/Reappearance_of_the_Dark_Mark" title="Reappearance of the Dark Mark">riot</a></p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure>
<p>The night after the <a href="/wiki/Quidditch" title="Quidditch">Quidditch</a> match, Mr Roberts, his wife, and their two children were all abducted by <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a>, who proceeded to <a href="/wiki/Levicorpus" title="Levicorpus">levitate</a> them through the air, and, like puppeteers controlling four marionettes with invisible strings, contorted the defenceless family into grotesque shapes and span them around mid-air as means to humiliate and frighten them.<sup id="cite_ref-GOF9_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF9-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> When <a href="/wiki/Arthur_Weasley" title="Arthur Weasley">Arthur Weasley</a> and his party were leaving the campsite the following morning, Mr Roberts wished them a Merry <a href="/wiki/Christmas" title="Christmas">Christmas</a>, although it was the middle of summer. Mr Weasley explained that Mr Roberts had been left temporarily disoriented by the <a href="/wiki/Memory_Charm" title="Memory Charm">Memory Charm</a> that had been used to erase his memories of the Death Eater attack.<sup id="cite_ref-GOF10_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF10-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Roberts?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Roberts?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>The Robertses did not make it to the final cut of the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Goblet_of_Fire_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire (film)">film adaptation</a> of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Goblet_of_Fire" title="Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire">Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire</a></i>. A brief shot of them being tortured by Death Eaters during the <a href="/wiki/Reappearance_of_the_Dark_Mark" title="Reappearance of the Dark Mark">Quidditch World Cup riot</a> can be seen, however, in the film's official trailer.</li></ul>
