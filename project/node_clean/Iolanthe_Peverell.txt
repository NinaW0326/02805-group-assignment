<b>Iolanthe Peverell</b><sup id="cite_ref-Pottermore_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Pottermore-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> was an <a href="/wiki/England" title="England">English</a> <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch</a> and the granddaughter of <a href="/wiki/Ignotus_Peverell" title="Ignotus Peverell">Ignotus Peverell</a>. 
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Iolanthe_Peverell?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Iolanthe_Peverell?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Described as "beautiful," she lived in <a href="/wiki/Godric%27s_Hollow" title="Godric&#39;s Hollow">Godric's Hollow</a>. Due to the lack of male heirs, she, the eldest of her generation, inherited her <a href="/wiki/Ignotus_Peverell" title="Ignotus Peverell">grandfather's</a> <a href="/wiki/Cloak_of_Invisibility" title="Cloak of Invisibility">Cloak of Invisibility</a>. Upon her marriage to <a href="/wiki/Hardwin_Potter" title="Hardwin Potter">Hardwin Potter</a>, she explained that, in the tradition of the Peverell family, possession of the cloak was kept secret. Hardwin honoured her family's tradition, and, from then on, the cloak was passed to the eldest child of each new generation in the <a href="/wiki/Potter_family" title="Potter family">Potter family</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-Pottermore_2-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Pottermore-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p><a href="/wiki/Henry_Potter" title="Henry Potter">Henry Potter</a>, a member of the <a href="/wiki/Wizengamot" title="Wizengamot">Wizengamot</a> from <a href="/wiki/1913" title="1913">1913</a> to <a href="/wiki/1921" title="1921">1921</a> and the great-grandfather of <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>, was a direct descendant of Hardwin and Iolanthe.<sup id="cite_ref-Pottermore_2-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Pottermore-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
