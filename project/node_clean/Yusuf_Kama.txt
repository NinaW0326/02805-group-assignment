<p><b>Yusuf Kama</b> (born c. <a href="/wiki/1884" class="mw-redirect" title="1884">1884</a>) was a <a href="/wiki/France" title="France">French</a>-<a href="/wiki/Africa" title="Africa">African</a> <a href="/wiki/Pure-blood" title="Pure-blood">pure-blood</a> <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> of <a href="/wiki/Senegal" title="Senegal">Senegalese</a> ancestry, son of <a href="/wiki/Laurena_Kama" title="Laurena Kama">Laurena</a> and <a href="/wiki/Mustafa_Kama" title="Mustafa Kama">Mustafa Kama</a>, and half-brother of <a href="/wiki/Leta_Lestrange" title="Leta Lestrange">Leta Lestrange</a>. He claimed to be the last male in his <a href="/wiki/Pure-blood" title="Pure-blood">pure-blood</a> family line.<sup id="cite_ref-ss_3-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-ss-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Yusuf_Kama?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Yusuf_Kama?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span id="Early_life_(1884-1896)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_life_.281884-1896.29">Early life (1884-1896)</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Yusuf_Kama?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Early life (1884-1896)">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Yusuf_Kama?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Early life (1884-1896)"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Yusuf was born around 1884, the son of Mustafa and Laurena Kama, a pair of <a href="/wiki/Pure-blood" title="Pure-blood">pure-blooded</a> wizards. They all lived in <a href="/wiki/Kama_Mansion" title="Kama Mansion">Kama Mansion</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-COGS_2-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COGS-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span id="Seeking_vengeance_(1896-1926)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="Seeking_vengeance_.281896-1926.29">Seeking vengeance (1896-1926)</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Yusuf_Kama?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Seeking vengeance (1896-1926)">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Yusuf_Kama?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Seeking vengeance (1896-1926)"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<figure class="thumb tleft show-info-icon" style="width: 222px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/4/4c/Young_Yusuf_with_Laurena_and_Mustafa.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20190822183249" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/4/4c/Young_Yusuf_with_Laurena_and_Mustafa.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/220?cb=20190822183249" decoding="async" width="220" height="91" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="Young Yusuf with Laurena and Mustafa.jpg" data-image-key="Young_Yusuf_with_Laurena_and_Mustafa.jpg" /></a> 	 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:Young_Yusuf_with_Laurena_and_Mustafa.jpg" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption">12 years old Yusuf with his mother <a href="/wiki/Laurena_Kama" title="Laurena Kama">Laurena</a> and father <a href="/wiki/Mustafa_Kama" title="Mustafa Kama">Mustafa</a></p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure>
<p>Yusuf's parents were acquainted with <a href="/wiki/Corvus_Lestrange_(IV)" title="Corvus Lestrange (IV)">Corvus Lestrange</a>, who desired Laurena. One night in <a href="/wiki/1896" class="mw-redirect" title="1896">1896</a>, he used the <a href="/wiki/Imperius_Curse" title="Imperius Curse">Imperius Curse</a> to lure Laurena out of her home and abduct her. Yusuf tried to stop her from leaving, but Lestrange <a href="/wiki/Curse" title="Curse">cursed</a> him. That was the last time Yusuf saw his mother.<sup id="cite_ref-COGS_2-10" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COGS-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Later he and his father learned that Laurena died giving birth to Lestrange's daughter <a href="/wiki/Leta_Lestrange" title="Leta Lestrange">Leta</a>. Mustafa became insane of grief and with his dying breath, he made Yusuf take an <a href="/wiki/Unbreakable_Vow" title="Unbreakable Vow">Unbreakable Vow</a> to seek revenge by killing the person, who Lestrange loved most in the world.<sup id="cite_ref-COGS_2-11" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COGS-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Yusuf first thought that his intended victim was Leta, since she was at the time Lestrange's only close relative, but he learned that Lestrange didn't love his daughter. But Yusuf learned that Lestrange married <a href="/wiki/Clarisse_Lestrange" title="Clarisse Lestrange">Clarisse Tremblay</a> soon after Laurena's death, and while Lestrange didn't love his wife either, he later, in <a href="/wiki/1901" title="1901">1901</a>, had a son with her, named after himself, whom he loved more than anything. So <a href="/wiki/Corvus_Lestrange_(V)" title="Corvus Lestrange (V)">Corvus</a> became Yusuf's target for revenge.<sup id="cite_ref-COGS_2-12" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COGS-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Lestrange learned of Yusuf's vow of revenge and sent both his children with the servant <a href="/wiki/Irma_Dugard" title="Irma Dugard">Irma Dugard</a> to America. Because of Irma's weak magic, Yusuf couldn't trace her and Corvus seemed to have escaped him.<sup id="cite_ref-COGS_2-13" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COGS-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span id="Global_wizarding_war_(1927)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="Global_wizarding_war_.281927.29">Global wizarding war (<a href="/wiki/1927" title="1927">1927</a>)</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Yusuf_Kama?veaction=edit&amp;section=4" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Global wizarding war (1927)">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Yusuf_Kama?action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Global wizarding war (1927)"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Search_for_Credence_Barebone">Search for Credence Barebone</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Yusuf_Kama?veaction=edit&amp;section=5" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Search for Credence Barebone">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Yusuf_Kama?action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Search for Credence Barebone"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<figure class="thumb tright show-info-icon" style="width: 222px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/1/11/Yusuf_in_Circus_Arcanus.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20190822180146" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/1/11/Yusuf_in_Circus_Arcanus.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/220?cb=20190822180146" decoding="async" width="220" height="91" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="Yusuf in Circus Arcanus.jpg" data-image-key="Yusuf_in_Circus_Arcanus.jpg" /></a> 	 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:Yusuf_in_Circus_Arcanus.jpg" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption">Yusuf watching Credence's and Nagini's escape</p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure>
<p>In <a href="/wiki/1927" title="1927">1927</a>, Yusuf Kama was on a mission and tracked <a href="/wiki/Credence_Barebone" title="Credence Barebone">Credence Barebone</a> to Paris. Yusuf was determent to track Credence down and kill him because he believed him to be <a href="/wiki/Credence_Barebone" title="Credence Barebone">Corvus Lestrange</a>, the son of <a href="/wiki/Corvus_Lestrange_(IV)" title="Corvus Lestrange (IV)">Corvus Lestrange IV</a> who had <a href="/wiki/Imperius_Curse" title="Imperius Curse">Imperiused</a> Yusuf's mother into marriage.<sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> He had heard rumours about Credence's identity and managed to locate Credence at the <a href="/wiki/Circus_Arcanus" title="Circus Arcanus">Circus Arcanus</a>, where <a href="/wiki/Nagini" title="Nagini">Nagini</a>, the <a href="/wiki/Maledictus" title="Maledictus">Maledictus</a>, had a show. During Nagini's performance, Kama carefully observed the behaviour of <a href="/wiki/Skender" title="Skender">Skender</a>, the circus owner provoking the woman. When Nagini turned into a snake, she attacked Skender. A moment later, as a result of Credence releasing animals from the cage, panic broke out in the circus and the crowd began to run away. Credence and Nagini <a href="/wiki/Escape_from_Circus_Arcanus" title="Escape from Circus Arcanus">escaped</a> from the circus, which prevented Yusuf from capturing the Obscurial. Hearing the conversation between the Auror, <a href="/wiki/Porpentina_Goldstein" title="Porpentina Goldstein">Porpentina Goldstein</a> and Skender, he quickly realised that the topic of the conversation was Barebone. Therefore, when the woman finished talking, he invited her for <a href="/wiki/Tea" title="Tea">tea</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-COGS_2-14" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COGS-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Personality_and_traits">Personality and traits</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Yusuf_Kama?veaction=edit&amp;section=12" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Personality and traits">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Yusuf_Kama?action=edit&amp;section=12" title="Edit section: Personality and traits"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<figure class="thumb tright show-info-icon" style="width: 222px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/4/4f/Yusuf_in_sewers.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20200818184501" class="image"><img alt="" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw%3D%3D" decoding="async" width="220" height="87" class="thumbimage lazyload" data-image-name="Yusuf in sewers.jpg" data-image-key="Yusuf_in_sewers.jpg" data-src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/4/4f/Yusuf_in_sewers.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/220?cb=20200818184501" /></a> 	<noscript><a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/4/4f/Yusuf_in_sewers.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20200818184501" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/4/4f/Yusuf_in_sewers.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/220?cb=20200818184501" decoding="async" width="220" height="87" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="Yusuf in sewers.jpg" data-image-key="Yusuf_in_sewers.jpg" data-src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/4/4f/Yusuf_in_sewers.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/220?cb=20200818184501" /></a></noscript> 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:Yusuf_in_sewers.jpg" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption">Yusuf in his hideout</p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure>
<p>Yusuf Kama was extremely ambitious, cunning, and resourceful, shown in his determination to track down and kill <a href="/wiki/Corvus_Lestrange_(V)" title="Corvus Lestrange (V)">Corvus Lestrange</a> to fulfil the <a href="/wiki/Unbreakable_Vow" title="Unbreakable Vow">Unbreakable Vow</a> which his father, <a href="/wiki/Mustafa_Kama" title="Mustafa Kama">Mustafa Kama</a>, had forced him to make. Indeed, Yusuf was so desperate to complete this mission, that he tricked and trapped <a href="/wiki/Newton_Scamander" title="Newton Scamander">Newt Scamander</a>, <a href="/wiki/Porpentina_Goldstein" title="Porpentina Goldstein">Tina Goldstein</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Jacob_Kowalski" title="Jacob Kowalski">Jacob Kowalski</a>, and later even ruthlessly threatened to kill Jacob if the <a href="/wiki/Muggle" class="mw-redirect" title="Muggle">No-Maj</a> was to get in his way. Moreover, Yusuf was notably cunning enough to trick even the highly skilled <a href="/wiki/Auror" title="Auror">Auror</a> Tina.<sup id="cite_ref-COGS_2-23" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COGS-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Despite Yusuf's occasional ruthlessness, however, he had a softer side and was capable of genuine love and had a decent degree of morals and empathy. Yusuf deeply loved his mother <a href="/wiki/Laurena_Kama" title="Laurena Kama">Laurena Kama</a> and his father and was devastated by the tragedy that separated them. Although he was determined to kill Corvus, Yusuf seemed to pity the young man who he thought was Corvus V for his fated doom caused by his apparent father's actions. He didn't want to kill anyone else and demanded them to move. Moreover, despite his pride in being a pure-blood wizard, Yusuf was not a <a href="/wiki/Pure-blood_supremacy" title="Pure-blood supremacy">Pure-blood supremacist</a>. While willing to trap Newt, Jacob, and Tina after completing his mission, Yusuf wanted to release them after he killed Corvus V. Yusuf also seemed to harbour no ill will towards his half-sister <a href="/wiki/Leta_Lestrange" title="Leta Lestrange">Leta Lestrange</a>, and even seemed to love her in a way, even hesitating before stating that her father <a href="/wiki/Corvus_Lestrange_(IV)" title="Corvus Lestrange (IV)">Corvus Lestrange (IV)</a> had never loved her (apparently not wishing to hurt Leta's feelings).<sup id="cite_ref-COGS_2-24" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COGS-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>After learning that his Vow was void (due to Corvus already being dead), Yusuf started to display a more moral and selfless side - not being tempted by Grindelwald's persuasive speech, risking his life to save <a href="/wiki/Nagini" title="Nagini">Nagini</a> from the black fire, and then helping <a href="/wiki/Nicolas_Flamel" title="Nicolas Flamel">Nicolas Flamel</a>, as well as his former situational enemies, by conjuring purifying orange flames. Inspired by the selfless sacrifice his half-sister Leta, Yusuf officially joined the <a href="/wiki/Global_wizarding_war" title="Global wizarding war">Global wizarding war</a> on the side opposing Grindelwald, and journeyed to <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> to meet with <a href="/wiki/Albus_Dumbledore" title="Albus Dumbledore">Albus Dumbledore</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-COGS_2-25" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COGS-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Magical_abilities_and_skills">Magical abilities and skills</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Yusuf_Kama?veaction=edit&amp;section=13" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Yusuf_Kama?action=edit&amp;section=13" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li><b><a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">Magical</a> mastery</b>: Yusuf Kama was shown to be an accomplished and rather powerful <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a>, as he was confident in his ability to <a href="/wiki/Killing_Curse" title="Killing Curse">kill</a> the immensely dangerous <a href="/wiki/Credence_Barebone" title="Credence Barebone">Credence Barebone</a>, in order to fulfil the <a href="/wiki/Unbreakable_Vow" title="Unbreakable Vow">Unbreakable Vow</a> his father had forced him to give. Yusuf also successfully aided <a href="/wiki/Nicolas_Flamel" title="Nicolas Flamel">Nicolas Flamel</a>, <a href="/wiki/Newton_Scamander" title="Newton Scamander">Newt Scamander</a>, <a href="/wiki/Theseus_Scamander" title="Theseus Scamander">Theseus Scamander</a> and <a href="/wiki/Porpentina_Goldstein" title="Porpentina Goldstein">Tina Goldstein</a> in saving Paris from the incredibly destructive <a href="/wiki/Protego_Diabolica" title="Protego Diabolica">black fire</a> conjured by <a href="/wiki/Gellert_Grindelwald" title="Gellert Grindelwald">Gellert Grindelwald</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-COGS_2-26" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COGS-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Defence_Against_the_Dark_Arts" title="Defence Against the Dark Arts">Defence Against the Dark Arts</a></b>: Yusuf was highly skilled at fending off <a href="/wiki/Dark_Arts" title="Dark Arts">Dark Magic</a>, as seen when he was able to quickly follow <a href="/wiki/Nicolas_Flamel" title="Nicolas Flamel">Nicolas Flamel</a>'s instructions on how to cast the <a href="/wiki/General_Counter-Spell" title="General Counter-Spell">General Counter-Spell</a> necessary to contain and extinguish the violent black fire left by Gellert Grindelwald, and thus helped to prevent the destruction of Paris.<sup id="cite_ref-COGS_2-27" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COGS-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Charm" title="Charm">Charms</a></b>: Yusuf was apparently highly capable in Charms, since he swiftly and effortlessly cast the <a href="/wiki/Disarming_Charm" title="Disarming Charm">Disarming Charm</a> to disarm Newt Scamander of his <a href="/wiki/Newton_Scamander%27s_wand" title="Newton Scamander&#39;s wand">wand</a>. Moreover, Yusuf only failed to track down <a href="/wiki/Corvus_Lestrange_(V)" title="Corvus Lestrange (V)">Corvus Lestrange (V)</a> prior to the shipwreck due to <a href="/wiki/Irma_Dugard" title="Irma Dugard">Irma Dugard</a>'s barely detectable half-elf magic, showing he was proficient with tracking charms.<sup id="cite_ref-COGS_2-28" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COGS-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Transfiguration" title="Transfiguration">Transfiguration</a></b>: Yusuf was able to swiftly <a href="/wiki/Conjuration" title="Conjuration">conjure</a> <a href="/wiki/Bar_conjuring_spell" title="Bar conjuring spell">bars</a> through a doorway, trapping <a href="/wiki/Newton_Scamander" title="Newton Scamander">Newt Scamander</a> and <a href="/wiki/Jacob_Kowalski" title="Jacob Kowalski">Jacob Kowalski</a>, along with <a href="/wiki/Porpentina_Goldstein" title="Porpentina Goldstein">Tina Goldstein</a>, in a chamber.<sup id="cite_ref-COGS_2-29" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COGS-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Divination" title="Divination">Divination</a></b>&#160;: Yusuf had some knowledge of this very subjective and esoteric branch of magic, given his great familiarity with <a href="/wiki/The_Predictions_of_Tycho_Dodonus" title="The Predictions of Tycho Dodonus">The Predictions of Tycho Dodonus</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-COGS_2-30" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COGS-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Healing_magic" title="Healing magic">Healing magic</a></b>: Yusuf had at least some skill in this field, given his ability to effectively keep the <a href="/wiki/Water_dragon_parasite" title="Water dragon parasite">Water dragon parasite</a> in his eye at bay with <a href="/wiki/Bright_green_liquid" title="Bright green liquid">magical eye-drops</a>. However, Yusuf's treatments did not prevent the parasite from incapacitating him suddenly, while Newt was able to extract it from Yusuf's eye in mere seconds.<sup id="cite_ref-COGS_2-31" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COGS-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Love" title="Love">Love</a></b>: Yusuf deeply loved his parents, being devastated at the loss of his beloved mother <a href="/wiki/Laurena_Kama" title="Laurena Kama">Laurena Kama</a> to <a href="/wiki/Corvus_Lestrange_(IV)" title="Corvus Lestrange (IV)">Corvus Lestrange (IV)</a>, and agreeing with his father <a href="/wiki/Mustafa_Kama" title="Mustafa Kama">Mustafa Kama</a>'s order to give an <a href="/wiki/Unbreakable_Vow" title="Unbreakable Vow">Unbreakable Vow</a> to avenge his family's ruin by killing the one Corvus IV most loved. While not getting much time to know his half-sister <a href="/wiki/Leta_Lestrange" title="Leta Lestrange">Leta Lestrange</a>, Yusuf loved her too in a way, sympathising with that fact that she wasn't loved by her own father. He is fair enough to not blame Leta for their mother's death. Indeed, Yusuf seemingly resolved to honour Leta's memory by joining the side opposing <a href="/wiki/Gellert_Grindelwald" title="Gellert Grindelwald">Gellert Grindelwald</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Global_wizarding_war" title="Global wizarding war">Global wizarding war</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-COGS_2-32" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COGS-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Apparition" title="Apparition">Apparition</a></b>: Yusuf, like most adult wizards, could Apparate at will, though he seemed more skilled than most, as he Apparated away from the black fire left by Grindelwald, while also saving <a href="/wiki/Nagini" title="Nagini">Nagini</a> in the process.<sup id="cite_ref-COGS_2-33" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COGS-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Nonverbal_spell" title="Nonverbal spell">Nonverbal magic</a></b>: Yusuf was very skilled at nonverbal magic, with him conjuring bars through a doorway without uttering any <a href="/wiki/Incantation" title="Incantation">incantation</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-COGS_2-34" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COGS-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li><b>Detective skills</b>: Yusuf had immense skills in tracking and deduction, since he tracked down <a href="/wiki/Credence_Barebone" title="Credence Barebone">Credence Barebone</a> to the <a href="/wiki/Circus_Arcanus" title="Circus Arcanus">Circus Arcanus</a> just as quickly as the highly skilled MACUSA Auror <a href="/wiki/Porpentina_Goldstein" title="Porpentina Goldstein">Tina Goldstein</a> did. Yusuf was later able to track down Credence and <a href="/wiki/Nagini" title="Nagini">Nagini</a> again, this time to the <a href="/wiki/Lestrange_Mausoleum" title="Lestrange Mausoleum">Lestrange Mausoleum</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-COGS_2-35" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COGS-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li><b>Indomitable willpower</b>: Yusuf possessed immense willpower and bravery, with him resisting <a href="/wiki/Gellert_Grindelwald" title="Gellert Grindelwald">Grindelwald</a>'s persuasive speech, instead valiantly fighting against the Dark Wizard's <a href="/wiki/Protego_Diabolica" title="Protego Diabolica">black fire</a>, and, inspired by his half-sister <a href="/wiki/Leta_Lestrange" title="Leta Lestrange">Leta</a>'s sacrifice, joining the <a href="/wiki/Global_wizarding_war" title="Global wizarding war">Global wizarding war</a> against Grindelwald.<sup id="cite_ref-COGS_2-36" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COGS-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li><b>Bilingualism:</b> Yusuf, due to his Senegalese heritage, fluently spoke both his native French and English, though he had a slight accent in the latter.<sup id="cite_ref-COGS_2-37" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COGS-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup></li></ul>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Yusuf_Kama?veaction=edit&amp;section=14" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Yusuf_Kama?action=edit&amp;section=14" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>Yusuf is the Arabic equivalent to Joseph, meaning "God increases in piety, power, and influence" in Hebrew.
<ul><li>It is also the name of a prophet in the Qu'ran, the only one of Jacob's/Yaacob's children gifted with prophecy. He was Jacob's eleventh child, and was considered his favourite.</li></ul></li>
<li>Kama is a Sanskrit name meaning "love."</li></ul>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Yusuf_Kama?veaction=edit&amp;section=15" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Yusuf_Kama?action=edit&amp;section=15" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>Yusuf Kama was played by <a href="/wiki/William_Nadylam" title="William Nadylam">William Nadylam</a> in <i><a href="/wiki/Fantastic_Beasts:_The_Crimes_of_Grindelwald" title="Fantastic Beasts: The Crimes of Grindelwald">Fantastic Beasts: The Crimes of Grindelwald</a></i>.</li>
<li>It is possible that Yusuf Kama, if <a href="/wiki/Newton_Scamander" title="Newton Scamander">Newt Scamander</a> had obtained and maintained the Elder Wand's allegiance by that time, may have temporarily become Master of the <a href="/wiki/Elder_Wand" title="Elder Wand">Elder Wand</a> in the <i><a href="/wiki/Fantastic_Beasts:_The_Crimes_of_Grindelwald" title="Fantastic Beasts: The Crimes of Grindelwald">Fantastic Beasts: The Crimes of Grindelwald</a></i> movie, as the Elder Wand switches allegiance when its Master is overpowered. Since Yusuf Kama, by disarming and imprisoning Newt, overpowered him beyond resistance, it's possible that the Elder Wand perceived in Kama a superior level of (real) power. If Kama did in fact become the Elder Wand's Master, then, following the events of the saga, Grindelwald would have to directly (or indirectly) gain the Elder Wand's allegiance back, before his final duel with <a href="/wiki/Albus_Dumbledore" title="Albus Dumbledore">Albus Dumbledore</a>.</li></ul>
