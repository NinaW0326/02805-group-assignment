<p>This <b>individual</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Great_Britain" title="Great Britain">British</a> <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> who, in the early <a href="/wiki/1990s" title="1990s">1990s</a>, ran the <a href="/wiki/Ye_Olde_Curiosity_Shop" title="Ye Olde Curiosity Shop">Ye Olde Curiosity Shop</a> in <a href="/wiki/Knockturn_Alley" title="Knockturn Alley">Knockturn Alley</a>. He refused to sell any of his <a href="/wiki/Dark_Arts" title="Dark Arts">Dark</a> curios to a twelve-year-old <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> in the summer of <a href="/wiki/1992" title="1992">1992</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-CSgbc_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-CSgbc-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
