<p><b>Buckbeak</b>, later renamed <b>Witherwings</b>, was a male <a href="/wiki/Hippogriff" title="Hippogriff">hippogriff</a>. He lived with <a href="/wiki/Rubeus_Hagrid" title="Rubeus Hagrid">Rubeus Hagrid</a> during <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>'s third year at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts</a>, along with a few other hippogriffs. He was later unfairly sentenced to death, when he attacked <a href="/wiki/Draco_Malfoy" title="Draco Malfoy">Draco Malfoy</a> after being taunted and provoked.<sup id="cite_ref-POA6_4-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-POA6-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> Buckbeak's death sentence was delivered from the <a href="/wiki/Committee_for_the_Disposal_of_Dangerous_Creatures" title="Committee for the Disposal of Dangerous Creatures">Committee for the Disposal of Dangerous Creatures</a>. Many of the members of this committee had been threatened by Draco's father, <a href="/wiki/Lucius_Malfoy" title="Lucius Malfoy">Lucius Malfoy</a>, into voting for that verdict.<sup id="cite_ref-POA11_9-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-POA11-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>With the help of <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>, <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione Granger</a>, and her <a href="/wiki/Time-Turner" title="Time-Turner">Time-Turner</a>, Buckbeak escaped execution in 1994 and he came under the care of <a href="/wiki/Sirius_Black" title="Sirius Black">Sirius Black</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-POA21_8-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-POA21-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup> Sirius kept Buckbeak with him while on the run during the 1994–1995 school year, and later kept him in his <a href="/wiki/Walburga_Black" title="Walburga Black">mother</a>'s bedroom while in hiding at <a href="/wiki/12_Grimmauld_Place" title="12 Grimmauld Place">12 Grimmauld Place</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP23_10-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP23-10">&#91;10&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>After Sirius's death, Buckbeak came to be owned by <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> through Sirius's will, though Harry allowed him to live with Hagrid. He participated in two battles of the <a href="/wiki/Second_Wizarding_War" title="Second Wizarding War">Second Wizarding War</a>, most notably the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-DH36_6-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH36-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup> He also showed great affection and loyalty toward Harry,<sup id="cite_ref-POA6_4-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-POA6-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> defending him whenever he was in danger.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP28_7-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP28-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<p>In 1993, <a href="/wiki/Rubeus_Hagrid" title="Rubeus Hagrid">Rubeus Hagrid</a> brought his first <a href="/wiki/Care_of_Magical_Creatures" title="Care of Magical Creatures">Care of Magical Creatures</a> class to see and study Buckbeak, as well as other hippogriffs. Under Hagrid's direction, Harry was able to successfully approach Buckbeak and was even allowed to ride him for a short flight around the paddock.<sup id="cite_ref-POA6_4-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-POA6-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>As hippogriffs are immensely proud creatures, Buckbeak was offended when <a href="/wiki/Draco_Malfoy" title="Draco Malfoy">Draco Malfoy</a> taunted and provoked him. This resulted in him injuring the boy's arm.<sup id="cite_ref-POA6_4-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-POA6-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> Draco's father, <a href="/wiki/Lucius_Malfoy" title="Lucius Malfoy">Lucius Malfoy</a> insisted that the <a href="/wiki/Hippogriff" title="Hippogriff">Hippogriff</a> be put to death.<sup id="cite_ref-POA11_9-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-POA11-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup> Malfoy's influence in the <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">Ministry of Magic</a> led to his views being the majority vote, and the execution was to be carried out. <a href="/wiki/Walden_Macnair" title="Walden Macnair">Walden Macnair</a> came to the <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts</a> as the employed executioner, and Buckbeak was tied in Hagrid's pumpkin patch to await his death.<sup id="cite_ref-POA17_11-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-POA17-11">&#91;11&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<figure class="thumb tright show-info-icon" style="width: 253px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/4/4b/B3C21M4_Sirius_flying_off_on_Buckbeak.png/revision/latest?cb=20180424014356" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/4/4b/B3C21M4_Sirius_flying_off_on_Buckbeak.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/251?cb=20180424014356" decoding="async" width="251" height="91" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="B3C21M4 Sirius flying off on Buckbeak.png" data-image-key="B3C21M4_Sirius_flying_off_on_Buckbeak.png" /></a> 	 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:B3C21M4_Sirius_flying_off_on_Buckbeak.png" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption">Harry and Hermione rescue Buckbeak and <a href="/wiki/Sirius_Black" title="Sirius Black">Sirius Black</a></p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure>
<p>However, using a <a href="/wiki/Time-Turner" title="Time-Turner">Time-Turner</a>, Harry Potter and <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione Granger</a> <a href="/wiki/Rescue_of_Sirius_Black_and_Buckbeak" title="Rescue of Sirius Black and Buckbeak">rescued</a> Buckbeak just prior to the execution.<sup id="cite_ref-POA21_8-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-POA21-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup> Originally, they had heard the swish of an axe and assumed Buckbeak had been killed.<sup id="cite_ref-POA17_11-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-POA17-11">&#91;11&#93;</a></sup> Later, as it became evident that Buckbeak had escaped, Macnair was frustrated upon realising Buckbeak had gone, and was happily dismissed by <a href="/wiki/Albus_Dumbledore" title="Albus Dumbledore">Dumbledore</a>. Hermione and Harry took Buckbeak into the <a href="/wiki/Forbidden_Forest" title="Forbidden Forest">Forbidden Forest</a> with them to remain hidden from sight, and Hermione tied him to a tree to stop him escaping. As Hermione and Harry realised <a href="/wiki/Remus_Lupin" title="Remus Lupin">Remus Lupin</a> in his <a href="/wiki/Werewolf" title="Werewolf">werewolf</a> was entering the forest, they went inside Hagrid's hut to hide. Hermione stayed in the hut with Buckbeak, who was very happy to back home again. Harry then went off to the <a href="/wiki/Great_Lake" title="Great Lake">Black Lake</a> to <a href="/wiki/Attack_at_the_Great_Lake" title="Attack at the Great Lake">fight</a> the <a href="/wiki/Dementor" title="Dementor">Dementors</a> with the <a href="/wiki/Patronus_Charm" title="Patronus Charm">Patronus Charm</a>, in order to save his past self and <a href="/wiki/Sirius_Black" title="Sirius Black">Sirius Black</a>. After this, Hermione took Buckbeak with her to see what was going on in the forest. Buckbeak was then used to fly to <a href="/wiki/Filius_Flitwick" title="Filius Flitwick">Filius Flitwick</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Filius_Flitwick%27s_office" title="Filius Flitwick&#39;s office">office</a>, in which Sirius was locked in, enabling Black to escape the Dementors and leave Hogwarts.<sup id="cite_ref-POA21_8-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-POA21-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Living_with_Sirius_Black">Living with Sirius Black</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Buckbeak?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Living with Sirius Black">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Buckbeak?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Living with Sirius Black"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>

<p><a href="/wiki/Sirius_Black" title="Sirius Black">Sirius Black</a> then took possession of Buckbeak while both were on the run from the <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">Ministry of Magic</a>. <h3><span id="Back_in_Hagrid's_care"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="Back_in_Hagrid.27s_care">Back in Hagrid's care</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Buckbeak?veaction=edit&amp;section=4" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Back in Hagrid&#039;s care">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Buckbeak?action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Back in Hagrid&#039;s care"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<div class="quote">

<p>In <a href="/wiki/July" title="July">July</a> 1996, month after Sirius' death, <a href="/wiki/Albus_Dumbledore" title="Albus Dumbledore">Albus Dumbledore</a> told Harry that according to <a href="/wiki/Sirius_Black%27s_will" title="Sirius Black&#39;s will">Sirius' will</a>, he was now the legal owner of Buckbeak, though Harry told Dumbledore that Buckbeak could continue living with <a href="/wiki/Rubeus_Hagrid" title="Rubeus Hagrid">Rubeus Hagrid</a>. In order to protect the hippogriff's identity, Buckbeak was renamed "Witherwings" at this time.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP3_2-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP3-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-HBP6_3-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP6-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> He was greeted by Harry Potter in front of Hagrid's hut when the trio dropped in for a visit.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP11_13-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP11-13">&#91;13&#93;</a></sup> He was also present near the hut during <a href="/wiki/Aragog%27s_funeral" class="mw-redirect" title="Aragog&#39;s funeral">Aragog's funeral</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP22_14-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP22-14">&#91;14&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p><a href="/wiki/30_June" title="30 June">30 June</a> <a href="/wiki/1997" title="1997">1997</a>, Buckbeak joined Harry's duel with <a href="/wiki/Severus_Snape" title="Severus Snape">Severus Snape</a> across the grounds, attacking Snape and driving him off, thus protecting Harry. Buckbeak survived the battle and was still in very good health.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP28_7-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP28-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Later_life">Later life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Buckbeak?veaction=edit&amp;section=6" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Later life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Buckbeak?action=edit&amp;section=6" title="Edit section: Later life"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>It is unknown what became of Buckbeak after the war, but it is possible that he still lives at Hagrid's hut, or else in the forest with the other hippogriffs. It is also likely that the death sentence on him was overturned as part of the wider reforms within the Ministry of Magic.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Personality_and_traits">Personality and traits</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Buckbeak?veaction=edit&amp;section=7" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Personality and traits">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Buckbeak?action=edit&amp;section=7" title="Edit section: Personality and traits"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Buckbeak was a proud creature and at first he seemed to be fairly aggressive, attacking <a href="/wiki/Draco_Malfoy" title="Draco Malfoy">Draco Malfoy</a> when the latter insulted him.<sup id="cite_ref-POA6_4-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-POA6-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> Despite his earlier signs of aggression, Buckbeak has been shown to be very loyal and protective of those who treat him with kindness and respect.<sup id="cite_ref-POA6_4-10" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-POA6-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-POA21_8-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-POA21-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-HBP28_7-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP28-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Buckbeak was intelligent in his ability to recognise his past acquaintances, as he defended a cornered Harry from <a href="/wiki/Severus_Snape" title="Severus Snape">Severus Snape</a> in <a href="/wiki/1997" title="1997">1997</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP28_7-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP28-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup> His loyalty towards Harry was undying, and further proven when he joined the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a> in <a href="/wiki/1998" title="1998">1998</a> to fight.<sup id="cite_ref-DH36_6-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH36-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

The film portrayal also depicts a much longer ride, with Buckbeak achieving a sort of smooth, idyllic glide not described in the novel.</li>

If the player attempts to ride Buckbeak as Ron, he will knock him back and caw at him.</li></ul>
