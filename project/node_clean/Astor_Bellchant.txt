<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Astor_Bellchant?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Astor_Bellchant?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Astor was born at some point into the respectable wizarding <a href="/wiki/Bellchant_family" title="Bellchant family">Bellchant family</a>. In his adult life, he had at least one son, <a href="/wiki/Albert_Bellchant" title="Albert Bellchant">Albert</a>, who attended <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-FBC5_1-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-FBC5-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>In an investigation concerning the disappearance of a wizard named <a href="/wiki/Bilius_Finbok" title="Bilius Finbok">Bilius Finbok</a>, Astor Bellchant was suspected to have blackmailed Finbok into brewing an illegal <a href="/wiki/Potion" title="Potion">potion</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-FBC5_1-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-FBC5-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Possessions">Possessions</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Astor_Bellchant?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Possessions">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Astor_Bellchant?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Possessions"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li><b><a href="/wiki/Astor_Bellchant%27s_owl" title="Astor Bellchant&#39;s owl">Owl</a></b>: Astor owned an owl to deliver his mail. Astor Bellchant used this owl to send a <a href="/wiki/Threatening_note" title="Threatening note">blackmail note</a> to <a href="/wiki/Bilius_Finbok" title="Bilius Finbok">Bilius Finbok</a>, who was hospitalised at the <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggle</a> <a href="/wiki/St_David%27s_Hospital" class="mw-redirect" title="St David&#39;s Hospital">St David's Hospital</a>. <a href="/wiki/Michael_Babatola" title="Michael Babatola">Michael Babatola</a>, who was a nurse there, noted that Finbok had run away from the hospital shortly after an owl landed on his window in the middle of the day.<sup id="cite_ref-FBC5.2_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-FBC5.2-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup></li></ul>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Astor_Bellchant?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Astor_Bellchant?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>Astor, rarely used as a given name, derives from a surname derived from Occitan <i>astur</i>, meaning "hawk". The <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Astor_family" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Astor family">Astor family</a> was a very prominent family in business and politics in the United States and the United Kingdom during the 19th and 20th centuries; famous members include <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/John_Jacob_Astor" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:John Jacob Astor">John Jacob Astor I</a>, the first multi-millionaire in the United States; <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/William_Waldorf_Astor,_1st_Viscount_Astor" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:William Waldorf Astor, 1st Viscount Astor">William Waldorf Astor</a>, the first Viscount Astor; and <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/John_Jacob_Astor_IV" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:John Jacob Astor IV">John Jacob Astor IV</a>, the richest passenger aboard the <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/RMS_Titanic" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:RMS Titanic">RMS <i>Titanic</i></a>.</li></ul>
