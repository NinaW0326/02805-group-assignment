<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Nigelle_le_Narcissique?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Nigelle_le_Narcissique?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>She was part of the <a href="/wiki/Circus_Arcanus" title="Circus Arcanus">Circus Arcanus</a>, a travelling freak show, which billed her as a "bon-fide" member of the species <a href="/wiki/Homo_amphibia" title="Homo amphibia">Homo Amphibia</a>, a <a href="/wiki/Human" title="Human">humanoid</a> reptilian who "freakishly devour[ed]" her own tail while singing popular <a href="/wiki/Gaelic" title="Gaelic">Gaelic</a> folk songs.<sup id="cite_ref-poster_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-poster-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Nigelle_le_Narcissique?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Nigelle_le_Narcissique?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>While being named "Nigelle", a seemingly female name, the french article used to describe it, "le", is masculine. If Nigelle was a female, its name should be Nigelle "la" Narcissique.</li>
<li>"Narcissique" is the french for Narcissistic.</li></ul>
