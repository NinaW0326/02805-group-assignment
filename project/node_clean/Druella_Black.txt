<b>Druella Black</b><sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup><b> </b>(née<b> Rosier</b>) (fl. 1951-1955) was a <a href="/wiki/Pure-blood" title="Pure-blood">pure-blood</a> <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch</a> who married <a href="/wiki/Cygnus_Black_III" title="Cygnus Black III">Cygnus Black III</a>. <h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Druella_Black?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Druella_Black?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<figure class="thumb tleft show-info-icon" style="width: 202px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/5/50/Narcissa%2C_Andromeda%2C_and_Bellatrix.jpeg/revision/latest?cb=20180502172712" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/5/50/Narcissa%2C_Andromeda%2C_and_Bellatrix.jpeg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/200?cb=20180502172712" decoding="async" width="200" height="269" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="Narcissa, Andromeda, and Bellatrix.jpeg" data-image-key="Narcissa,_Andromeda,_and_Bellatrix.jpeg" /></a> 	 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:Narcissa,_Andromeda,_and_Bellatrix.jpeg" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption">Druella's three children from left to right, <a href="/wiki/Narcissa_Malfoy" title="Narcissa Malfoy">Narcissa</a>, <a href="/wiki/Bellatrix_Lestrange" title="Bellatrix Lestrange">Bellatrix</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Andromeda_Tonks" title="Andromeda Tonks">Andromeda</a></p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure>
<p>Druella was a member of the <a href="/wiki/Rosier_family" title="Rosier family">Rosier family</a>, a family that strongly believed in blood purity.
</p><p>Druella and her husband had three daughters: <a href="/wiki/Bellatrix_Lestrange" title="Bellatrix Lestrange">Bellatrix</a>, <a href="/wiki/Andromeda_Tonks" title="Andromeda Tonks">Andromeda</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Narcissa_Malfoy" title="Narcissa Malfoy">Narcissa</a>. Bellatrix was the oldest and Narcissa the youngest. Her oldest and youngest shared her belief in the importance of blood purity. This belief led them to make acceptable pure-blood marriages, Andromeda however did not.
</p><p>Through Bellatrix, Druella is the mother-in-law of <a href="/wiki/Rodolphus_Lestrange" title="Rodolphus Lestrange">Rodolphus Lestrange</a>. She is also the grandmother of Bellatrix's only daughter, Delphini. <a href="/wiki/Delphini" title="Delphini">Delphini</a> was fathered by Voldemort through an affair he and Bellatrix shared. Through Andromeda (who was disowned for marrying <a href="/wiki/Muggle-born" title="Muggle-born">Muggle-born</a> wizard <a href="/wiki/Edward_Tonks" title="Edward Tonks">Ted Tonks</a>) she is the grandmother of <a href="/wiki/Nymphadora_Tonks" title="Nymphadora Tonks">Nymphadora Tonks</a> and great-grandmother of <a href="/wiki/Edward_Lupin" title="Edward Lupin">Teddy Lupin</a>. Through Narcissa, she is the mother-in-law of <a href="/wiki/Lucius_Malfoy" title="Lucius Malfoy">Lucius Malfoy</a>, the grandmother of <a href="/wiki/Draco_Malfoy" title="Draco Malfoy">Draco Malfoy</a>, and great-grandmother of <a href="/wiki/Scorpius_Malfoy" title="Scorpius Malfoy">Scorpius Malfoy</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-BFT_2-18" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-BFT-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Druella may be related to <a href="/wiki/Gellert_Grindelwald" title="Gellert Grindelwald">Gellert Grindelwald</a>'s lieutenant <a href="/wiki/Vinda_Rosier" title="Vinda Rosier">Vinda Rosier</a>, the Death Eater <a href="/wiki/Evan_Rosier" title="Evan Rosier">Evan Rosier</a>, and another Death Eater named <a href="/wiki/Rosier" title="Rosier">Rosier</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-GOF30_5-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF30-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Druella_Black?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Druella_Black?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>Based on a statement by <a href="/wiki/Horace_Slughorn" title="Horace Slughorn">Horace Slughorn</a>, Druella may have been a member of the <a href="/wiki/Slytherin" title="Slytherin">Slytherin House</a>, like her husband Cygnus. However, he may have not meant Druella, as she was a Rosier at the time.</li>
<li>In the Black family, the name Ella also appears before Druella married Cygnus. There were <a href="/wiki/Ella_Max" title="Ella Max">Ella Max</a> and her daughter <a href="/wiki/Elladora_Black" title="Elladora Black">Elladora</a>.</li>
<li>One of Druella's daughters, <a href="/wiki/Bellatrix_Lestrange" title="Bellatrix Lestrange">Bellatrix</a>, has thick eyelids (she is described by <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> as having heavily-lidded eyes). It is likely that this feature of her appearance was inherited from her mother as no mention is ever made of this characteristic in any members of the <a href="/wiki/House_of_Black" title="House of Black">Black family</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Sirius_Black" title="Sirius Black">Sirius Black</a>'s statement that "I'm the last Black left" may indicate that Druella is dead, as her married name is Black. However, more likely Sirius only meant Blacks by birth, who would be eligible to inherit <a href="/wiki/12_Grimmauld_Place" title="12 Grimmauld Place">12 Grimmauld Place</a>. Neither the books nor the Black family tree give any indication of her death, and since her daughters are only in their mid-40s at the end of <i>Deathly Hallows</i>, she may well still be alive.</li>
<li>Druella broke the Black family tradition of naming members of their family after stars when she and her husband, Cygnus, named their youngest daughter <a href="/wiki/Narcissa_Malfoy" title="Narcissa Malfoy">Narcissa</a>, derived from the flower of the same name, the daffodil. Narcissa however restarted the tradition, naming her only son <a href="/wiki/Draco_Malfoy" title="Draco Malfoy">Draco</a>, who in turn named his son <a href="/wiki/Scorpius_Malfoy" title="Scorpius Malfoy">Scorpius</a>. However, Narcissa may be meant as a reference to Narcissus from Greek mythology, and many constellations are based on Greek mythology, This includes the constellation Narcissa's sister Andromeda was named after.</li></ul>
<p><br />
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Druella_Black?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Druella_Black?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Druella may be a combination of the names <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Drusilla" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Drusilla">Drusilla</a>, often shorted to <i>Dru</i> and meaning "dewy-eyed" or "fruitful", and <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ella" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Ella">Ella</a>, a Norman name derived from the Germanic word <i>ali</i>, meaning "other".<sup id="cite_ref-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup> Drusilla was the name of infamous Roman Emperor <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Caligula" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Caligula">Caligula</a>'s favourite sister, as well as the daughter named after her.
</p>
