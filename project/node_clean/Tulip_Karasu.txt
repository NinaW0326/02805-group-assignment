<p><b>Tulip Karasu</b> (born c. <a href="/wiki/1973" title="1973">1973</a>) was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch</a> who began attending <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> in <a href="/wiki/1984" title="1984">1984</a> and was <a href="/wiki/Sorting_ceremony" title="Sorting ceremony">sorted</a> into <a href="/wiki/Ravenclaw" title="Ravenclaw">Ravenclaw</a>. She was eccentric, had a disregard for rules, and valued intelligence in others.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Tulip_Karasu?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Tulip_Karasu?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_life">Early life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Tulip_Karasu?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Early life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Tulip_Karasu?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Early life"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>When Tulip was young, she lived a very strict life due to her parent's employment in the <a href="/wiki/Department_of_Magical_Law_Enforcement" title="Department of Magical Law Enforcement">Department of Magical Law Enforcement</a> in the <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">British Ministry of Magic</a>. This resulted in her growing a rebellious spirit, which she hid from her parents or anyone else.<sup id="cite_ref-y3ch7_2-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-y3ch7-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span id="Hogwarts_years_(1984-1991)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="Hogwarts_years_.281984-1991.29">Hogwarts years <span class="template-c">(1984-1991)</span></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Tulip_Karasu?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Hogwarts years (1984-1991)">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Tulip_Karasu?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Hogwarts years (1984-1991)"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_years">Early years</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Tulip_Karasu?veaction=edit&amp;section=4" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Early years">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Tulip_Karasu?action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Early years"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<p>When she first attended Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry, she met <a href="/wiki/Merula_Snyde" title="Merula Snyde">Merula Snyde</a> and was attracted to her independence (with her parents in <a href="/wiki/Azkaban" title="Azkaban">Azkaban</a>, Merula was free to do what she wanted,) so she became friends with her.
</p><p>As the <a href="/wiki/Cursed_Vaults" title="Cursed Vaults">Cursed Vaults</a> became important in their first school year, they made their destiny to open the Vaults and show the world that they were as good as everyone else. Tulip and Merula soon stumbled upon Jacob's old research room, but then started to gather information behind the others back, thus causing a falling out. They locked the room with two keys, one for each of them, so that neither could do any research nor could anyone else get in.<sup id="cite_ref-y3ch7_2-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-y3ch7-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Third_year">Third year</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Tulip_Karasu?veaction=edit&amp;section=5" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Third year">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Tulip_Karasu?action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Third year"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<figure class="thumb tleft show-info-icon" style="width: 252px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/7/7f/HM_y3ch5_Tulip_and_Dennis.png/revision/latest?cb=20180603051118" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/7/7f/HM_y3ch5_Tulip_and_Dennis.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/250?cb=20180603051118" decoding="async" width="250" height="141" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="HM y3ch5 Tulip and Dennis.png" data-image-key="HM_y3ch5_Tulip_and_Dennis.png" /></a> 	 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:HM_y3ch5_Tulip_and_Dennis.png" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption">Tulip paying no attention to <a href="/wiki/Jacob%27s_sibling" title="Jacob&#39;s sibling">Jacob's sibling</a> and their friend, asking them to talk to <a href="/wiki/Dennis_(toad)" title="Dennis (toad)">Dennis</a></p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure>
<p>In the <a href="/wiki/1986%E2%80%931987_school_year" title="1986–1987 school year">1986–1987 school year</a>, upon noticing Tulip's name on the lock outside Jacob's room, <a href="/wiki/Jacob%27s_sibling" title="Jacob&#39;s sibling">Jacob's sibling</a>, who was in the same year as Tulip, brought a friend to meet her in the <a href="/wiki/Classroom_1B" title="Classroom 1B">Transfiguration Classroom</a> for the first time, attempting to gain information from her. Tulip initially responded to being busy and asked them to meanwhile converse with her pet <a href="/wiki/Toad" title="Toad">toad</a>, <a href="/wiki/Dennis_(toad)" title="Dennis (toad)">Dennis</a>, who was strapped with a <a href="/wiki/Dungbomb" title="Dungbomb">Dungbomb</a> that had to be defused. Tulip admitted she was somewhat testing Jacob's sibling after the bomb was successfully defused. She told them there were two keys for the lock, one of which was possessed by <a href="/wiki/Merula_Snyde" title="Merula Snyde">Merula Snyde</a>. They then proceeded to make plans on getting the Merula's key.<sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Around this time, Tulip also offered Dennis to Jacob's sibling for their companion when they were about to go to a <a href="/wiki/Frog_Choir" title="Frog Choir">Frog Choir</a> audition. Tulip helped Jacob's sibling prepare for the audition.<sup id="cite_ref-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>After getting the key back, they both entered Jacob's room, only to find <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Voldemort</a> there, therefore made a hasty retreat. Tulip correctly deduced that they had encountered a <a href="/wiki/Boggart" title="Boggart">boggart</a> after calming down, though the conversation was interrupted by Professor <a href="/wiki/Severus_Snape" title="Severus Snape">Severus Snape</a>, who told them to stay away from the corridor they were at, and to give up the search for the remaining vaults. They, however, continued the discussion after Professor Snape left, and concluded they needed to learn the <a href="/wiki/Boggart-Banishing_Spell" title="Boggart-Banishing Spell">Boggart-Banishing Spell</a>. They went to the <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Library" title="Hogwarts Library">Library</a> to find a book on the spell, afterward, Tulip left with it to learn it first and then tracked down a supposedly less terrifying Boggart in the <a href="/wiki/Artefact_Room" title="Artefact Room">Artefact Room</a> to teach Jacob's sibling and for practising purpose, before facing the one residing in Jacob's room.<sup id="cite_ref-y3ch7_2-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-y3ch7-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Tulip was one of the four students that ventured into the <a href="/wiki/Vault_of_Fear" title="Vault of Fear">Vault of Fear</a> that year.<sup id="cite_ref-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Fourth_year">Fourth year</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Tulip_Karasu?veaction=edit&amp;section=6" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Fourth year">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Tulip_Karasu?action=edit&amp;section=6" title="Edit section: Fourth year"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<p>Tulip was one of the first victims of Hogwarts' <a href="/wiki/Sleepwalking_curse" title="Sleepwalking curse">sleepwalking</a> epidemic of that year, which was caused by the opening of a third <a href="/wiki/Cursed_Vaults" title="Cursed Vaults">Cursed Vault</a>. When she met with Jacob's sibling at the Courtyard, they cast <i><a href="/wiki/Episkey" title="Episkey">Episkey</a></i> to heal a sleepwalking-related headache she had. Tulip was then taken to the <a href="/wiki/Hospital_Wing" title="Hospital Wing">Hospital Wing</a>, where she talked about the incident with Jacob's sibling, with the two agreeing that the third vault was probably in the forest, despite being unsure about the reason sleepwalking students went there. Shortly after her recovery, Tulip unexpectedly met Madam <a href="/wiki/Patricia_Rakepick" title="Patricia Rakepick">Patricia Rakepick</a> in a corridor and Rakepick asked her about the Cursed Vaults and the recent epidemic. Tulip later confessed to Jacob's sibling that she followed Rakepick after that encounter.<sup id="cite_ref-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>For the rest of the year, Tulip continued to gather with her friends to devise plans to find the next vault. With the group learning that a <a href="/wiki/Broomstick" title="Broomstick">broom</a> was the only (or better) way to get to the to <a href="/wiki/Forbidden_Forest" title="Forbidden Forest">Forbidden Forest</a> and investigate the possible next vault, they discussed how to get one. After Jacob's sibling failed to borrow a broom from Madam <a href="/wiki/Rolanda_Hooch" title="Rolanda Hooch">Rolanda Hooch</a>, Tulip explained to them what the <a href="/wiki/Black_Lake_incident" title="Black Lake incident">Black Lake incident</a> was. The two later sneaked into <a href="/wiki/Argus_Filch" title="Argus Filch">Argus Filch</a>'s office to try to steal a broom from there but were unable to find any usable brooms. They found, however, another transfigured black quill. They took it to Jacob's room and untransfigure it, revealing a message from <a href="/wiki/R" title="R">R</a> ordering Rakepick to leave the school, with a threat of punishment in case she disobeyed.<sup id="cite_ref-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Fifth_year">Fifth year</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Tulip_Karasu?veaction=edit&amp;section=7" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Fifth year">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Tulip_Karasu?action=edit&amp;section=7" title="Edit section: Fifth year"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<p>Early in the <a href="/wiki/1988%E2%80%931989_school_year" title="1988–1989 school year">1988–1989 school year</a>, Tulip pulled a prank releasing all the <a href="/wiki/Niffler" title="Niffler">nifflers</a>, which Jacob's sibling had to stop with the <a href="/wiki/Freezing_Charm" title="Freezing Charm">Freezing Charm</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-10" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-10">&#91;10&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Sixth_year">Sixth year</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Tulip_Karasu?veaction=edit&amp;section=8" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Sixth year">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Tulip_Karasu?action=edit&amp;section=8" title="Edit section: Sixth year"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<p>Sometime during her <a href="/wiki/Sixth_year" title="Sixth year">sixth year</a>, Tulip became a founding member of the <a href="/wiki/Circle_of_Khanna" title="Circle of Khanna">Circle of Khanna</a>, a group devoted to avenging <a href="/wiki/Rowan_Khanna" title="Rowan Khanna">Rowan Khanna</a> and locating the final <a href="/wiki/Cursed_Vaults" title="Cursed Vaults">Cursed Vault</a>. It was at the groups first meeting in the <a href="/wiki/Hog%27s_Head_Inn" title="Hog&#39;s Head Inn">Hog's Head Inn</a> that she learned of the malevolent organisation "<a href="/wiki/R" title="R">R</a>" and its <a href="/wiki/Plot_to_find_the_Cursed_Vaults" title="Plot to find the Cursed Vaults">motives</a> to locate the final Cursed Vault before them.<sup id="cite_ref-hm20_11-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-hm20-11">&#91;11&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Physical_appearance">Physical appearance</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Tulip_Karasu?veaction=edit&amp;section=9" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Physical appearance">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Tulip_Karasu?action=edit&amp;section=9" title="Edit section: Physical appearance"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<figure class="thumb tright show-info-icon" style="width: 182px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/1/13/Tulip_Karasu.jpeg/revision/latest?cb=20180603220813" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/1/13/Tulip_Karasu.jpeg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/180?cb=20180603220813" decoding="async" width="180" height="359" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="Tulip Karasu.jpeg" data-image-key="Tulip_Karasu.jpeg" /></a> 	 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:Tulip_Karasu.jpeg" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption">Tulip's appearance</p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure>
<p>Tulip had brown eyes, fair skin, red hair, and black eyebrows. While donning school attire, she wore a <a href="/wiki/Necklace" title="Necklace">necklace</a> that resembled a <a href="/wiki/Dungbomb" title="Dungbomb">Dungbomb</a> and carried a booklet that had <a href="/wiki/Zonko%27s_Joke_Shop" title="Zonko&#39;s Joke Shop">Zonko's Joke Shop</a>'s Z logo as the cover in the pocket of her skirt.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Personality_and_traits">Personality and traits</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Tulip_Karasu?veaction=edit&amp;section=10" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Personality and traits">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Tulip_Karasu?action=edit&amp;section=10" title="Edit section: Personality and traits"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<div class="quote">
<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;Harry Potter: Hogwarts Mystery"><b>Tulip Karasu</b>: "<i>I was quite jealous to be honest. I've always wanted to be told off in front of the entire school at a Welcoming Feast.</i>"</span></dd>
<dd><b>Jacob's sibling</b>: "<i>Why?</i>"</dd>
<dd><b>Tulip Karasu</b>: "<i>Because rules are made to be broken, and Hogwarts has <b>far too many rules</b>.</i>"</dd>
<dd>&#8212; Tulip's opinion on rules<sup class="noprint"><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter:_Hogwarts_Mystery" title="Harry Potter: Hogwarts Mystery">[src]</a></sup></dd></dl>
</div><p>When Jacob's sibling first met her in the <a href="/wiki/Transfiguration" title="Transfiguration">Transfiguration</a> classroom, it surprised Jacob's sibling that she was eccentric. As a <a href="/wiki/Ravenclaw" title="Ravenclaw">Ravenclaw</a>, she was witty and had a knack for getting to the bottom of things.<sup id="cite_ref-y3ch8_12-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-y3ch8-12">&#91;12&#93;</a></sup> She showed interest in clever and quick-thinking people, such as <a href="/wiki/Merula_Snyde" title="Merula Snyde">Merula Snyde</a>. Tulip was able to recognise and appreciate people for their intelligence alone, despite potentially having other problematic qualities.
</p><p>Tulip was rebellious, believing that rules were made to be broken. She admitted enjoying chaos as an aesthetic.<sup id="cite_ref-y3ch7_2-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-y3ch7-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> Those streaks led Tulip into performing some daring acts, such as sneaking a book of pranks out of the <a href="/wiki/Restricted_Section" title="Restricted Section">Restricted Section</a>, resulting in nearly blowing up the <a href="/wiki/Owlery" title="Owlery">Owlery</a>, an incident she viewed as one of her finer moments.<sup id="cite_ref-y3ch8_12-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-y3ch8-12">&#91;12&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>During her early years at Hogwarts, Tulip was left incredibly guilt-ridden over the fact that she selfishly betrayed her then best and only friend, Merula, in their shared pursuit of the Cursed Vaults. This soon developed intense feelings of inadequacy and self-loathing within her, with Tulip deeming herself unworthy of ever forming friendships with anyone (to the point that her boggart was Merula telling her that she will never have any friends), due to Tulip fearing that she will inevitably betray those she befriended. As a result, she chose to operate independently during her early years, keeping close to her pet toad Dennis and avoiding social encounters with Hogwarts students.
</p><p>This all changed one day, however, when Jacob's sibling sought her out for help in their quest for the Cursed Vaults and began to befriend her, with the former reassuring Tulip that she indeed <i>can</i> change and have friends in her life. After spending some time with said student, Tulip gradually began to open up to them and ultimately came to their point of view regarding her adequacy for friendship when they told her that they considered her their friend. Their encouragement inspired and gave Tulip a more optimistic and hopeful outlook, since she enthusiastically agreed to help them out in their search for the vaults after accepting their offer of friendship.
</p><p>This renewed hope in herself induced Tulip to turn away from her former independent and self-relying methods, accepting that she can have friends and indeed, she made numerous once accepting Jacob's sibling offer of friendship, with Tulip ultimately finding herself a best friend in fellow prankster and rule-breaker <a href="/wiki/Nymphadora_Tonks" title="Nymphadora Tonks">Nymphadora Tonks</a>. Compared to her fellow pranksters though, Tulip tended to be cleaner and more methodological.
</p><p>She was known to be a descent espionage, a skill she picked up from her parents who worked in the Ministry. She used her skills to scout <a href="/wiki/Jacob%27s_sibling" title="Jacob&#39;s sibling">Jacob's sibling</a> as a replacement singer to pair with Dennis to try out for <a href="/wiki/Frog_Choir" title="Frog Choir">Frog Choir</a>, as Tulip was tone-deaf.
</p><p>Tulip also enjoyed swimming in the lake, which was considered very cold by most, possibly indicating she was physically tougher than she appeared.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Tulip_Karasu?veaction=edit&amp;section=12" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Tulip_Karasu?action=edit&amp;section=12" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Tulip, the flower of the same name, originated from <a href="/wiki/Persia" title="Persia">Persia</a> and <a href="/wiki/Turkey" title="Turkey">Turkey</a>;<sup id="cite_ref-13" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-13">&#91;13&#93;</a></sup> Tulip, as a name, is also of Turkish origin as a result of it.<sup id="cite_ref-14" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-14">&#91;14&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-15" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-15">&#91;15&#93;</a></sup> In Turkish, <i>tülbent</i> means turban – the shape some thought the flowers resembled.
</p><p>Karasu is most common as a surname in Turkey.<sup id="cite_ref-16" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-16">&#91;16&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-17" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-17">&#91;17&#93;</a></sup> It means "black water" derived from Turkish <i>kara</i> meaning "black, dark" combined with <i>su</i> "water".<sup id="cite_ref-18" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-18">&#91;18&#93;</a></sup> Karasu is also the romanization of the Japanese for "crow" or" raven," a possible reflection of the house she was sorted into; while Karasu is not a surname in Japan, many names in the <i>Harry Potter</i> universe do not exist in real life and are used for symbolism. 
</p>
