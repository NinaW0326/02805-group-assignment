<p>The <b>paladins</b> were the foremost warriors of <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Charlemagne" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Charlemagne">Emperor Charlemagne</a>'s court in around the <a href="/wiki/First_millennium_A.D." title="First millennium A.D.">8th century</a>. By extention, the term has been used to refer to any heroic champion or knightly defender of a noble cause.<sup id="cite_ref-Wikipedia_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Wikipedia-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>There were many paladin <a href="/wiki/Suit_of_armour" title="Suit of armour">suits of armour</a> at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Castle" title="Hogwarts Castle">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> under <a href="/wiki/Spirit" title="Spirit">spectral</a> possession. They could be aggressive to passers-by, but were particularly vulnerable to the <i><a href="/wiki/Fire-Making_Spell" title="Fire-Making Spell">Incendio</a></i> and <i><a href="/wiki/Freezing_Spell" title="Freezing Spell">Glacius</a></i> spells.<sup id="cite_ref-PAgba_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PAgba-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
