<p><b>Fleamont Potter</b> (pre-<a href="/wiki/1909" title="1909">1909</a><sup id="cite_ref-DOB_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DOB-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> – c.<a href="/wiki/1979" title="1979">1979</a><sup id="cite_ref-death_2-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-death-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>) was a <a href="/wiki/Pure-blood" title="Pure-blood">pure-blood</a><sup id="cite_ref-blood_3-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-blood-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> and the son of <a href="/wiki/Henry_Potter" title="Henry Potter">Henry Potter</a>. He attended <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> and was sorted into <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor" title="Gryffindor">Gryffindor House</a>. At some point after graduating Hogwarts he married <a href="/wiki/Euphemia_Potter" title="Euphemia Potter">Euphemia.</a> 
</p><p>Fleamont had only one son later in life, <a href="/wiki/James_Potter_I" title="James Potter I">James Potter</a>. He is the paternal grandfather of the famous <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>, though he did not live long enough to meet him. He is also the great-grandfather of <a href="/wiki/James_Potter_II" title="James Potter II">James Sirius</a>, <a href="/wiki/Albus_Potter" title="Albus Potter">Albus Severus</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Lily_L._Potter" title="Lily L. Potter">Lily Luna Potter</a>.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Fleamont_Potter?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Fleamont_Potter?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_life">Early life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Fleamont_Potter?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Early life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Fleamont_Potter?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Early life"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Fleamont was born into the wealthy <a href="/wiki/Pure-blood" title="Pure-blood">pure-blooded</a> <a href="/wiki/Potter_family" title="Potter family">family</a>, to <a href="/wiki/Henry_Potter" title="Henry Potter">Henry Potter</a>, a prominent <a href="/wiki/Wizengamot" title="Wizengamot">Wizengamot</a> member. He was named after his paternal grandmother's maiden name, as it was her dying wish that he perpetuate her maiden name, which would otherwise die out.<sup id="cite_ref-Pottermore_4-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Pottermore-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>He started at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> at the age of eleven and was <a href="/wiki/Sorting_ceremony" title="Sorting ceremony">Sorted</a> into <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor" title="Gryffindor">Gryffindor House</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-DH33_5-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH33-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> Due to the teasing he got for his first name, he often got into fights over it, to which he would later attribute his dexterity at <a href="/wiki/Duelling" title="Duelling">duelling</a>. At some point and per the family tradition, he inherited the <a href="/wiki/Cloak_of_Invisibility" title="Cloak of Invisibility">Cloak of Invisibility</a> from his father Henry Potter, who was a distant descendant of <a href="/wiki/Ignotus_Peverell" title="Ignotus Peverell">Ignotus Peverell</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-Pottermore_4-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Pottermore-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Career_and_family">Career and family</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Fleamont_Potter?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Career and family">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Fleamont_Potter?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Career and family"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>After his graduation, Fleamont developed <a href="/wiki/Sleekeazy%27s_Hair_Potion" title="Sleekeazy&#39;s Hair Potion">Sleekeazy's Hair Potion</a>, sales of which quadrupled the family gold.<sup id="cite_ref-Pottermore_4-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Pottermore-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> On <a href="/wiki/29_November" title="29 November">29 November</a> <a href="/wiki/1926" title="1926">1926</a>, the <i><a href="/wiki/Daily_Prophet" title="Daily Prophet">Daily Prophet</a></i> ran a front page story on how his hair potion had wooed <a href="/wiki/United_States_of_America" title="United States of America">American</a> witches.<sup id="cite_ref-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup> Upon retirement, he sold the company that made the potion at a vast profit.<sup id="cite_ref-Pottermore_4-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Pottermore-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Fleamont married <a href="/wiki/Euphemia_Potter" title="Euphemia Potter">Euphemia Potter</a>, and although the family prospered, they had difficulty in having children. They eventually gave up the hope of having a son or daughter of their own. However, in <a href="/wiki/1959" title="1959">1959</a>, to the couple's shock and surprise, Euphemia became pregnant.<sup id="cite_ref-Pottermore_4-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Pottermore-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> Their son, <a href="/wiki/James_Potter_I" title="James Potter I">James</a>, was born on <a href="/wiki/27_March" title="27 March">27 March</a>, <a href="/wiki/1960" title="1960">1960</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-DH16_7-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH16-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup> Since the Potters had James later in their lives, he was their only child, and was pampered and cherished accordingly.<sup id="cite_ref-TLC_8-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-TLC-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Around <a href="/wiki/1976" title="1976">1976</a>, Fleamont and Euphemia took in their son's best friend, <a href="/wiki/Sirius_Black" title="Sirius Black">Sirius Black</a>, who had run away from <a href="/wiki/12_Grimmauld_Place" title="12 Grimmauld Place">home</a> at age 16, because his family hated him. They treated Sirius like a second son. Though Sirius stayed in the Potter home for only a year, eventually using money inherited from his uncle, <a href="/wiki/Alphard_Black" title="Alphard Black">Alphard Black</a>, to buy his own flat, Mr and Mrs Potter always welcomed him over for Sunday dinner thereafter.<sup id="cite_ref-op6_9-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-op6-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Death_and_postmortem">Death and postmortem</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Fleamont_Potter?veaction=edit&amp;section=4" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Death and postmortem">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Fleamont_Potter?action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Death and postmortem"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>James later married the <a href="/wiki/Muggle-born" title="Muggle-born">Muggle-born</a> witch, <a href="/wiki/Lily_J._Potter" title="Lily J. Potter">Lily Evans</a>, in around <a href="/wiki/1978" title="1978">1978</a>-<a href="/wiki/1979" title="1979">1979</a>. Shortly after their son's wedding, both Fleamont and Euphemia (who were advanced in age) succumbed to <a href="/wiki/Dragon_Pox" title="Dragon Pox">Dragon Pox</a>, dying within days of each other.<sup id="cite_ref-Pottermore_4-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Pottermore-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-TLC_8-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-TLC-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup> However, they had presumably died happy, for they have had the satisfaction of living long enough to see their son settled in life and married to a fine young woman.
</p><p>With their deaths, James inherited an <a href="/wiki/Cloak_of_Invisibility" title="Cloak of Invisibility">invisibility cloak</a> that had been passed down through the Potter family for many generations,<sup id="cite_ref-aol_10-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-aol-10">&#91;10&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-dh35_11-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-dh35-11">&#91;11&#93;</a></sup> and the considerable Potter fortune (which enabled him and his wife to lead a comfortable but all too brief life).<sup id="cite_ref-aol_10-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-aol-10">&#91;10&#93;</a></sup> 
</p><p>Fleamont's and Euphemia's deaths also meant that they did not meet their grandson, <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry James Potter</a>, who would be born on <a href="/wiki/31_July" title="31 July">31 July</a>, <a href="/wiki/1980" title="1980">1980</a>, nor did they realise tragedy would befall the Potter family soon thereafter.
</p><p>Since Harry was, himself, orphaned at a very young age, it soon became his heart's desire to meet his family. When, in <a href="/wiki/1991" title="1991">1991</a>, he glimpsed into the <a href="/wiki/Mirror_of_Erised" title="Mirror of Erised">Mirror of Erised</a>, Harry saw his grandparents, on both his father's and his mother's side, among a number of other relatives he had never met.<sup id="cite_ref-PS12_12-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PS12-12">&#91;12&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Physical_appearance">Physical appearance</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Fleamont_Potter?veaction=edit&amp;section=5" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Physical appearance">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Fleamont_Potter?action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Physical appearance"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>When Fleamont's grandson <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry</a> looked into the <a href="/wiki/Mirror_of_Erised" title="Mirror of Erised">Mirror of Erised</a> and saw his <a href="/wiki/Potter_family" title="Potter family">family</a>, he noted several individuals who had "<i>green eyes like his, other noses like his, </i>[and]<i> even a little old man who looked as though he had Harry's knobbly knees.</i>" While it is almost certain that Fleamont Potter could not have had green eyes, which Harry inherited from his mother, <a href="/wiki/Lily_J._Potter" title="Lily J. Potter">Lily Evans</a>, he may have been one of the other individuals who shared traits with his descendants. Fleamont Potter did not wear glasses.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Magical_abilities_and_skills">Magical abilities and skills</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Fleamont_Potter?veaction=edit&amp;section=6" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Fleamont_Potter?action=edit&amp;section=6" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li><b><a href="/wiki/Martial_magic" title="Martial magic">Martial magic</a></b>: Due to the numerous times he had to magically fight people at Hogwarts after they had mocked his name, Fleamont was a skilful <a href="/wiki/Duelling" title="Duelling">duellist</a>.</li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Potion" title="Potion">Potions</a></b>: Fleamont was an accomplished <a href="/wiki/Potioneer" title="Potioneer">potioneer</a>. After his graduation from Hogwarts he invented the <a href="/wiki/Sleekeazy%27s_Hair_Potion" title="Sleekeazy&#39;s Hair Potion">Sleekeazy's Hair Potion</a>, in doing so founded a potion-making company.</li>
<li><b>Entrepreneurship and business acumen</b>: Fleamont was known to have quadrupled his family's fortune with his potion-making company, and had sold his company upon his retirement for a vast profit. This showcased his skills as an accomplished businessman.</li></ul>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Fleamont_Potter?veaction=edit&amp;section=7" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Fleamont_Potter?action=edit&amp;section=7" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>It was once presumed that <a href="/wiki/Charlus_Potter" title="Charlus Potter">Charlus Potter</a> and <a href="/wiki/Dorea_Black" title="Dorea Black">Dorea Black</a>, who appeared on the <a href="/wiki/House_of_Black" title="House of Black">Black family tree</a>, might have been James's parents (since it was known that Charlus and Dorea also had <a href="/wiki/Charlus_Potter%27s_son" title="Charlus Potter&#39;s son">one son</a>). However, <a href="/wiki/J._K._Rowling" title="J. K. Rowling">J. K. Rowling</a> revealed via  <i><a href="/wiki/Pottermore" title="Pottermore">Pottermore</a></i> that that is not the case. It is still more than likely, however, that Charlus Potter is some sort of relative of Fleamont's.</li>
<li>Harry's view of his paternal grandparents was the polar opposite of Voldemort's. Whereas Tom Riddle had hatred for both sides of his family, particularly his patrilineal descent, Harry, who lost his father at a young age, was also saddened by never getting the chance to meet his father's parents. The Mirror of Erised showing Harry having a family reunion reflected his love for the <i>idea</i> of grandparents, and of a caring family in general. As Harry knew nothing about them, whether this feeling of love and respect for Fleamont and Euphemia Potter would have born out had he known them is likely, but debatable.</li></ul>
