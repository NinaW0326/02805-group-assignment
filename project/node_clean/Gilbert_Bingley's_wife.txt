<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Gilbert_Bingley%27s_wife?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Gilbert_Bingley%27s_wife?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>In <a href="/wiki/December" title="December">December</a> of <a href="/wiki/1926" title="1926">1926</a>, <a href="/wiki/Newton_Scamander" title="Newton Scamander">Newt Scamander</a> had <a href="/wiki/Frank" title="Frank">Frank</a> the <a href="/wiki/Thunderbird" title="Thunderbird">thunderbird</a> disperse <a href="/wiki/Swooping_Evil" title="Swooping Evil">Swooping Evil</a> <a href="/wiki/Swooping_Evil_venom" title="Swooping Evil venom">venom</a> over <a href="/wiki/New_York" title="New York">New York</a> by creating a <a href="/wiki/Water" title="Water">rainstorm</a>, in order to wipe the memories of a <a href="/wiki/Obscurial" title="Obscurial">recent magical catastrophe</a> from the city's No-Maj population. Mrs Bingley was brushing her teeth in the bathroom while her husband showered when this occurred. Her expression was described as becoming "vacant" and "carefree" as she lost the memories.<sup id="cite_ref-FB118_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-FB118-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
