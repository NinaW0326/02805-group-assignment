The <b>two brothers</b> of <a href="/wiki/Arthur_Weasley" title="Arthur Weasley">Arthur Weasley</a><sup id="cite_ref-JKR_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-JKR-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> were <a href="/wiki/England" title="England">English</a> <a href="/wiki/Pure-blood" title="Pure-blood">pure-blood</a> <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizards</a>. Their parents were <a href="/wiki/Septimus_Weasley" title="Septimus Weasley">Septimus</a> and <a href="/wiki/Cedrella_Black" title="Cedrella Black">Cedrella Weasley</a> (née <a href="/wiki/House_of_Black" title="House of Black">Black</a>).<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> At least one of them appears to have had children, as there were mentions of various <a href="/wiki/Weasley_family" title="Weasley family">Weasley</a> cousins at the <a href="/wiki/Wedding_of_William_Weasley_and_Fleur_Delacour" title="Wedding of William Weasley and Fleur Delacour">wedding</a> of <a href="/wiki/William_Weasley" title="William Weasley">Bill Weasley</a> and <a href="/wiki/Fleur_Delacour" title="Fleur Delacour">Fleur Delacour</a> in August of <a href="/wiki/1997" title="1997">1997</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-DH_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Arthur_Weasley%27s_brothers?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Arthur_Weasley%27s_brothers?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li><a href="/wiki/J._K._Rowling" title="J. K. Rowling">J. K. Rowling</a> stated that Arthur had two brothers.<sup id="cite_ref-JKR_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-JKR-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> They have not been named thus far.</li>
<li>In addition, an <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley%27s_uncle" class="mw-redirect" title="Ronald Weasley&#39;s uncle">uncle</a> of the Weasley children who attended <a href="/wiki/William_Weasley" title="William Weasley">Bill</a> and <a href="/wiki/Fleur_Delacour" title="Fleur Delacour">Fleur</a>'s wedding,<sup id="cite_ref-DH_3-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> may have been one of Arthur's brothers.</li>
<li>When <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> attended <a href="/wiki/Wedding_of_William_Weasley_and_Fleur_Delacour" title="Wedding of William Weasley and Fleur Delacour">Bill and Fleur's wedding</a> disguised as a <a href="/wiki/Red-haired_Muggle_boy" title="Red-haired Muggle boy">red-haired Muggle boy</a>, one of the Weasley uncles, who was intoxicated at the time, wondered if Harry was his son. This may mean that one of Arthur's brothers has at least one son, or he may have simply been <i>extremely</i> drunk.</li>
<li>Two men who may be Arthur's brothers are seen at the wedding in <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Deathly_Hallows:_Part_1" title="Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 1">Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 1</a></i>.</li>
<li>Ron has an uncle Bilius who could be one of Arthur's brothers, or Molly might have another brother that we don't know about.<sup id="cite_ref-DH_3-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li>These individuals were not Featured on the Weasley family tree shown on  <i><a href="/wiki/Pottermore" title="Pottermore">Pottermore</a></i>.</li></ul>
