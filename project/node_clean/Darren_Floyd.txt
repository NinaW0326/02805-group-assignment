<b>Darren Floyd</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Wales" title="Wales">Welsh</a> <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> and a <a href="/wiki/Beater" title="Beater">Beater</a> for the <a href="/wiki/Welsh_National_Quidditch_team" title="Welsh National Quidditch team">Welsh National Quidditch team</a> in the <a href="/wiki/2014_Quidditch_World_Cup" title="2014 Quidditch World Cup">2014 Quidditch World Cup</a><sup id="cite_ref-PM_2-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PM-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>. During their match against <a href="/wiki/Brazil" title="Brazil">Brazil</a>, he single-handedly prevented at least seventeen Brazilian goals. 
</p><p>After Brazil's victory, he had to help fellow Beater <a href="/wiki/Iefan_Rice" title="Iefan Rice">Iefan Rice</a> drag their manager <a href="/wiki/Gwenog_Jones" title="Gwenog Jones">Gwenog Jones</a> off the pitch after she attacked Brazilian manager <a href="/wiki/Jos%C3%A9_Barboza" title="José Barboza">José Barboza</a> in retaliation for calling the Welsh Chasers "talentless hags".<sup id="cite_ref-PM_2-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PM-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
