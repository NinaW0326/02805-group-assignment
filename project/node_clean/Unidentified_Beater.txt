<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_Beater?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_Beater?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>During her time at Hogwarts, she was <a href="/wiki/Sorting_ceremony" title="Sorting ceremony">Sorted</a> into an unknown <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Houses" title="Hogwarts Houses">house</a>. At some point by the <a href="/wiki/1985%E2%80%931986_school_year" title="1985–1986 school year">1985–1986 school year</a>, she became a <a href="/wiki/Beater" title="Beater">Beater</a> of an unknown Quidditch team.<sup id="cite_ref-q1_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-q1-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>She stood on her head every night before a match as a way to inspire good fortune. At some point before the final match in the 1985–1986 school year, she also received a rock with a hole in it from her mother for good luck.<sup id="cite_ref-qc_4-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-qc-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>She continued playing on the team the following year. As her fellow Beater, <a href="/wiki/Bean" title="Bean">Bean</a>, was suspended from the team, she played in the position alongside <a href="/wiki/Jacob%27s_sibling" title="Jacob&#39;s sibling">Jacob's sibling</a>, who had been a <a href="/wiki/Chaser" title="Chaser">Chaser</a> in the previous year.<sup id="cite_ref-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
