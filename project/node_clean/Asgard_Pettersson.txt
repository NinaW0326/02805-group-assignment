<b>Asgard Pettersson</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Scandinavia" title="Scandinavia">Scandinavian</a> <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> who played as a <a href="/wiki/Beater" title="Beater">Beater</a> on the <a href="/wiki/Nordic_National_Quidditch_team" title="Nordic National Quidditch team">Nordic National Quidditch team</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>During the <a href="/wiki/1996%E2%80%931997_school_year" title="1996–1997 school year">1996–1997 school year</a>, Pettersson's <a href="/wiki/Chocolate_Frog_Card" title="Chocolate Frog Card">Chocolate Frog Card</a> could be won by beating <a href="/wiki/Ross_Gibberd_(II)" title="Ross Gibberd (II)">Ross Gibberd</a> at a <a href="/wiki/Quidditch" title="Quidditch">Quidditch</a> challenge.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Asgard_Pettersson?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Asgard_Pettersson?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>In Norse religion, "Asgard" (Old Norse: Ásgarðr; meaning "Enclosure of the Æsir"[1]) is one of the Nine Worlds and is the country or capital city of the Norse Gods surrounded by an incomplete wall attributed to a Hrimthurs riding the stallion Svadilfari, according to Gylfaginning. Valhalla is located within Asgard. Odin and his wife, Frigg are the rulers of Asgard.
</p>
