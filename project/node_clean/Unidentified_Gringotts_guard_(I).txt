This <b>individual</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> who was assigned to guard the entrance of <a href="/wiki/Gringotts_Wizarding_Bank" title="Gringotts Wizarding Bank">Gringotts Wizarding Bank</a> in <a href="/wiki/1998" title="1998">1998</a> during the <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a>' reign over the <a href="/wiki/Wizarding_world" title="Wizarding world">wizarding world</a>. He and <a href="/wiki/Marius" title="Marius">Marius</a> used <a href="/wiki/Probity_Probe" title="Probity Probe">Probity Probes</a> to search anyone attempting to enter. Both guards were <a href="/wiki/Confundus_Charm" title="Confundus Charm">Confuded</a> by <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> on <a href="/wiki/1_May" title="1 May">1 May</a>, <a href="/wiki/1998" title="1998">1998</a> when he and his friends <a href="/wiki/Break-in_of_Gringotts_Wizarding_Bank_(1998)" title="Break-in of Gringotts Wizarding Bank (1998)">broke into the bank</a> to steal one of <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Voldemort</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Horcrux" title="Horcrux">Horcruxes</a> from the <a href="/wiki/Lestrange_Vault" title="Lestrange Vault">Lestrange Vault</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-DH26_1-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH26-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
