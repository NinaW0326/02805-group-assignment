<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Benjy_Fenwick?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Benjy_Fenwick?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Though nothing is known about his early life, it is likely that as a <a href="/wiki/Great_Britain" title="Great Britain">British</a> <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a>, he attended <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> when he turned eleven and that he had graduated prior to <a href="/wiki/1981" title="1981">1981</a> as the First Wizarding War ended that year.
</p><p>During the <a href="/wiki/First_Wizarding_War" title="First Wizarding War">First Wizarding War</a>, Fenwick became a member of the <a href="/wiki/Order_of_the_Phoenix" title="Order of the Phoenix">Order of the Phoenix</a>, an organisation founded and led by <a href="/wiki/Albus_Dumbledore" title="Albus Dumbledore">Albus Dumbledore</a>, in order to oppose the evil <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a> and his <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a>. Fenwick most likely fought in several battles of the <a href="/wiki/First_Wizarding_War" title="First Wizarding War">First Wizarding War</a>.
</p><p>Fenwick was brutally murdered by Death Eaters during the war. On the day of his <a href="/wiki/Death" title="Death">death</a>, a Death Eater fired a deadly <a href="/wiki/Curse" title="Curse">curse</a> that blasted Fenwick into pieces (possibly a <a href="/wiki/Blasting_Curse" title="Blasting Curse">Blasting Curse</a>), and then disposed of his remains. According to fellow Order member <a href="/wiki/Alastor_Moody" title="Alastor Moody">Alastor Moody</a> in their search for Fenwick's body they only ever found <i>"bits of him."</i><sup id="cite_ref-OOTP9_2-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP9-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
