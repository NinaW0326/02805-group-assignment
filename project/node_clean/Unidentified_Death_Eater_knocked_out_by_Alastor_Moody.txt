This <b>individual</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Dark_Wizard" title="Dark Wizard">Dark Wizard</a> and <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eater</a> who fought during the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_the_Department_of_Mysteries" title="Battle of the Department of Mysteries">Battle of the Department of Mysteries</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Second_Wizarding_War" title="Second Wizarding War">Second Wizarding War</a>. He was about to cast a <a href="/wiki/Curse" title="Curse">curse</a> when <a href="/wiki/Alastor_Moody" title="Alastor Moody">Alastor Moody</a> used his <a href="/wiki/Alastor_Moody%27s_walking_stick" title="Alastor Moody&#39;s walking stick">staff</a> to <a href="/wiki/Knockback_Jinx" title="Knockback Jinx">knock</a> him out.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
