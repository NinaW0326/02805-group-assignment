This <b><a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a></b> was seen in the <a href="/wiki/Leaky_Cauldron" title="Leaky Cauldron">Leaky Cauldron</a> in <a href="/wiki/1993" title="1993">1993</a>, reading the <a href="/wiki/Muggle_world" title="Muggle world">Muggle</a> physics book <i><a href="/wiki/A_Brief_History_of_Time" title="A Brief History of Time">A Brief History of Time</a></i> by <a href="/wiki/Stephen_Hawking" title="Stephen Hawking">Stephen W. Hawking</a> while possibly performing <a href="/wiki/Wandless_magic" title="Wandless magic">wandless magic</a> on his cup of <a href="/wiki/Coffee" title="Coffee">coffee</a>, causing it to stir&#160;itself.<sup id="cite_ref-POAF_1-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-POAF-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<ul><li>This wizard was portrayed by <a href="/wiki/Ian_Brown" title="Ian Brown">Ian Brown</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Prisoner_of_Azkaban_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban (film)">film adaptation</a> of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Prisoner_of_Azkaban" title="Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban">Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban</a></i>&#160;in a celebrity cameo.</li>
<li>His reading of a book titled&#160;<i>A Brief History of Time</i> is likely foreshadowing for the use of <a href="/wiki/Time" title="Time">time</a> travel later in <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Prisoner_of_Azkaban" title="Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban">the film</a>.&#160;
<ul><li><a target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener" class="external text" href="https://www.amazon.com/gp/product/0553346148/ref=dbs_a_def_rwt_bibl_vppi_i14">Said book</a> was published in <a href="/wiki/1990" title="1990">1990</a> in real life. Therefore, it can't be suggested as a proof that the wizard might actually be a time traveller himself since the scene is placed in 1993.</li>
<li>He does not appear in the <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Prisoner_of_Azkaban" title="Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban">Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban</a></i> book.</li></ul></li></ul>
