<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Merton_Graves?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Merton_Graves?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>If he attended <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a>, he would have been in school from around <a href="/wiki/1989" title="1989">1989</a> to <a href="/wiki/1996" title="1996">1996</a>, making him a <a href="/wiki/Third_year" title="Third year">third year</a> during <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>'s first year there, and a <a href="/wiki/Sixth_year" title="Sixth year">sixth year</a> during the <a href="/wiki/Triwizard_Tournament" title="Triwizard Tournament">Triwizard Tournament</a>, when the <a href="/wiki/Weird_Sisters" title="Weird Sisters">Weird Sisters</a> performed at Hogwarts for the <a href="/wiki/Yule_Ball" title="Yule Ball">Yule Ball</a>.</li>
<li>Merton Graves and <a href="/wiki/Herman_Wintringham" title="Herman Wintringham">Herman Wintringham</a> were absent from the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Goblet_of_Fire_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire (film)">film adaptation</a> of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Goblet_of_Fire" title="Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire">Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire</a></i>, and did not play with the <a href="/wiki/Weird_Sisters" title="Weird Sisters">Weird Sisters</a>.</li>
<li>It's possible he is a relative of the <i><a href="/wiki/Fantastic_Beasts_(film_series)" title="Fantastic Beasts (film series)">Fantastic Beasts and Where to Find Them</a></i> character <a href="/wiki/Percival_Graves" title="Percival Graves">Percival Graves</a>.</li></ul>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Merton_Graves?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Merton_Graves?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The name "Merton" is from a surname which was derived from a place name meaning "town on a lake" in Old English.
