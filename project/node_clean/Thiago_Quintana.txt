<b>Thiago Quintana</b> was an <a href="/wiki/United_States_of_America" title="United States of America">American</a> <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-pottermore-1920s_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-pottermore-1920s-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> He, along with <a href="/wiki/Shikoba_Wolfe" title="Shikoba Wolfe">Shikoba Wolfe</a>, <a href="/wiki/Johannes_Jonker" title="Johannes Jonker">Johannes Jonker</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Violetta_Beauvais" title="Violetta Beauvais">Violetta Beauvais</a>, was one of four <a href="/wiki/Wandmaker" title="Wandmaker">wandmakers</a> who served <a href="/wiki/North_America" title="North America">North America</a> in the early <a href="/wiki/20th_century" title="20th century">20th century</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-pottermore-1920s_3-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-pottermore-1920s-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Thiago_Quintana?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Thiago_Quintana?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Quintana's <a href="/wiki/Wand" title="Wand">wands</a> caused a sensation when they were first introduced.<sup id="cite_ref-pottermore-1920s_3-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-pottermore-1920s-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> Sleek and unusually long in design, they produced <a href="/wiki/Spell" title="Spell">spells</a> of "force and elegance". They contained a single <a href="/wiki/White_River_Monster_spine" title="White River Monster spine">spine</a> taken from the back of one of the <a href="/wiki/White_River_Monster" title="White River Monster">White River Monsters</a> native to <a href="/wiki/Arkansas" title="Arkansas">Arkansas</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-pottermore-1920s_3-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-pottermore-1920s-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> Initially, there were fears that the species was being over-fished, but the ecological impact was lessened by the fact that only Quintana knew how to lure the creatures.<sup id="cite_ref-pottermore-1920s_3-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-pottermore-1920s-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> He fiercely guarded this secret up until his death, after which wands containing White River Monster spine <a href="/wiki/Wand_core" title="Wand core">cores</a> were no longer made.<sup id="cite_ref-pottermore-1920s_3-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-pottermore-1920s-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
