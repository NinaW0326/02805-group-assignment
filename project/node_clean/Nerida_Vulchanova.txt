<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Nerida_Vulchanova?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Nerida_Vulchanova?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>At some point, Vulchanova suffered a mysterious <a href="/wiki/Death" title="Death">death</a>, and shortly afterwards <a href="/wiki/Harfang_Munter" title="Harfang Munter">Harfang Munter</a> took over the school, in what was later to be considered a dark period in the history of the school: Munter substantially altered the curriculum to focus on <a href="/wiki/Duelling" title="Duelling">duelling</a> and <a href="/wiki/Martial_magic" title="Martial magic">martial magic</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-Pottermore_3-10" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Pottermore-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Nerida_Vulchanova?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Nerida_Vulchanova?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>In the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Goblet_of_Fire_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire (film)">film adaptation</a> of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Goblet_of_Fire" title="Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire">Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire</a></i>, the Durmstrang Institute is portrayed as an all male school. However according to  <i><a href="/wiki/Pottermore" title="Pottermore">Pottermore</a></i>, the school was founded by Nerida Vulchanova; a witch. In <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Goblet_of_Fire" title="Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire">Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire</a></i>, at least one <a href="/wiki/Durmstrang_girl" title="Durmstrang girl">female</a> is known to have attend Durmstrang during the <a href="/wiki/1994-1995_school_year" class="mw-redirect" title="1994-1995 school year">1994-1995 school year</a>.</li></ul>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Nerida_Vulchanova?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Nerida_Vulchanova?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li><i>Nerida</i> is an uncommon variant of the female Nereida (Spanish, Greek) or Nerine (Greek), which can be translated as <i>sea nymph</i>.</li></ul>
