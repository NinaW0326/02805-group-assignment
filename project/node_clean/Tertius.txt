<b>Tertius</b> (fl. <a href="/wiki/16th_century" title="16th century">16th century</a>) was a <a href="/wiki/Scotland" title="Scotland">Scottish</a> <a href="/wiki/Muggle-born" title="Muggle-born">Muggle-born</a> <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a>, the youngest of <a href="/wiki/Tertius%27s_brothers" title="Tertius&#39;s brothers">three brothers</a>. Chased from his home, Tertius found his way to the island of <a href="/wiki/Hermetray" title="Hermetray">Hermetray</a> and met <a href="/wiki/Zygmunt_Budge" title="Zygmunt Budge">Zygmunt Budge</a>, who used him as an unwilling guinea pig for his <a href="/wiki/Felix_Felicis" title="Felix Felicis">Felix Felicis</a> and thus changed his life forever.<sup id="cite_ref-BOP_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-BOP-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Tertius?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Tertius?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Tertius was born to a <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggle</a> family somewhere in <a href="/wiki/Scotland" title="Scotland">Scotland</a>. He was born the youngest of three brothers, the other two being non-magical. Sometime in his youth, his parents died, leaving Tertius and his brothers as orphans. Frightened of their brother's magical abilities, Tertius's brothers broke his <a href="/wiki/Wand" title="Wand">wand</a> and chased him out the house, where Tertius accidentally fell into the sea.<sup id="cite_ref-BOP_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-BOP-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Unconscious, Tertius floated to the island of Hermetray, where he was found by the hermit potioneer Zygmunt Budge. Though normally disinclined to visitors, Budge took pity on Tertius, brought him into his house, and revived him. Once there, Tertius informed Budge of his history. Despite having taken Tertius in, Budge was unwilling to keep Tertius, as Budge felt he disrupted his potion-making, and Budge was wary of anyone stealing his recently perfected Felix Felicis. Budge gave Tertius a few <a href="/wiki/Wizarding_currency" title="Wizarding currency">coins</a>, a new <a href="/wiki/Cloak" title="Cloak">cloak</a> and boat, and a sample of Felix Felicis, only telling Tertius the latter was a "farewell drink".<sup id="cite_ref-BOP_1-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-BOP-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>As Tertius sailed back to the mainland, a wind blew his boat off-course. He landed ashore at the home of the only wizarding family for fifty miles. Concerned that Tertius had no wand, they offered to accompany him to <a href="/wiki/Diagon_Alley" title="Diagon Alley">Diagon Alley</a>. Upon his arrival, he found an abandoned bag of <a href="/wiki/Gold" title="Gold">gold</a> in the gutter, and thus paid for his new wand and a new set of robes. Walking back to <a href="/wiki/Gringotts_Wizarding_Bank" title="Gringotts Wizarding Bank">Gringotts Wizarding Bank</a>, he overheard a pair of wizards discussing openings for <a href="/wiki/Curse-Breaker" title="Curse-Breaker">Curse-Breakers</a>. Tertius applied and was accepted.<sup id="cite_ref-BOP_1-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-BOP-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Though the effects of Budge's Felix Felicis (which had caused this string of good luck) wore off after twelve hours, Tertius never looked back. He went on to become one of Gringotts's most famous Curse-Breakers.<sup id="cite_ref-BOP_1-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-BOP-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
