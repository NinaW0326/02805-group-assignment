<p><b>Marshall Fawley</b> (died c. <a href="/wiki/1997" title="1997">1997</a>) was a <a href="/wiki/Great_Britain" title="Great Britain">British</a> <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a>, the husband of <a href="/wiki/Lucretia_Fawley" title="Lucretia Fawley">Lucretia Fawley</a>, and the father of <a href="/wiki/Grim_Fawley" title="Grim Fawley">Grim Fawley</a>.
</p><p>Mr Fawley and his wife were both killed during the height of the <a href="/wiki/Second_Wizarding_War" title="Second Wizarding War">Second Wizarding War</a>, while their son Grim was a student at Hogwarts.<sup id="cite_ref-WU_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-WU-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
