<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_life">Early life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_Hufflepuff_girl_(III)?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Early life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_Hufflepuff_girl_(III)?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Early life"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>This girl was born in approximately <a href="/wiki/1981" title="1981">1981</a>, to unknown parents. Whether she was born a <a href="/wiki/Muggle-born" title="Muggle-born">Muggle-born</a>, <a href="/wiki/Half-blood" title="Half-blood">half-blood</a>, or <a href="/wiki/Pure-blood" title="Pure-blood">pure-blood</a> is unknown.
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Hogwarts_years">Hogwarts years</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_Hufflepuff_girl_(III)?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Hogwarts years">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_Hufflepuff_girl_(III)?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Hogwarts years"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_years">Early years</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_Hufflepuff_girl_(III)?veaction=edit&amp;section=4" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Early years">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_Hufflepuff_girl_(III)?action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Early years"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<p>This unidentified girl started <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts</a> in <a href="/wiki/1992" title="1992">1992</a>. She was <a href="/wiki/Sorting_ceremony" title="Sorting ceremony">sorted</a> into <a href="/wiki/Hufflepuff" title="Hufflepuff">Hufflepuff</a>, in the same year as <a href="/wiki/Ginevra_Weasley" title="Ginevra Weasley">Ginny Weasley</a> and <a href="/wiki/Luna_Lovegood" title="Luna Lovegood">Luna Lovegood</a>.
</p>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Third_year"><a href="/wiki/Third_year" title="Third year">Third year</a></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_Hufflepuff_girl_(III)?veaction=edit&amp;section=5" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Third year">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_Hufflepuff_girl_(III)?action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Third year"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<p>In this girl's third year, the <a href="/wiki/Triwizard_Tournament" title="Triwizard Tournament">Triwizard Tournament</a> was held, as well as, the <a href="/wiki/Yule_Ball" title="Yule Ball">Yule Ball</a>. In <a href="/wiki/December" title="December">December</a>, she asked <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> to the ball. Taken aback, Harry said no so abruptly that he hurt her feelings. He then had to endure the taunts of <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ron Weasley</a>, <a href="/wiki/Seamus_Finnigan" title="Seamus Finnigan">Seamus Finnigan</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Dean_Thomas" title="Dean Thomas">Dean Thomas</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
