<p><b>Bilius</b> (surname <b>Weasley</b> or <b>Prewett</b>) was a <a href="/wiki/Pure-blood" title="Pure-blood">pure-blood</a> <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> and a brother of either <a href="/wiki/Arthur_Weasley" title="Arthur Weasley">Arthur Weasley</a> or <a href="/wiki/Molly_Weasley" title="Molly Weasley">Molly Weasley née Prewett</a>. A lifelong bachelor, he was fun-loving and lively, but was prone to eccentric behaviour.<sup id="cite_ref-dh8_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-dh8-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> He reportedly died twenty-four hours after seeing a <a href="/wiki/Grim" title="Grim">Grim</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-poa6_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-poa6-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Bilius?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Bilius?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Bilius was a brother of either <a href="/wiki/Arthur_Weasley" title="Arthur Weasley">Arthur Weasley</a> or <a href="/wiki/Molly_Weasley" title="Molly Weasley">Molly Weasley née Prewett</a> and an uncle to the couple's <a href="/wiki/Weasley_family" title="Weasley family">seven children</a>, as well as the namesake for the youngest son, <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ronald Bilius Weasley</a> .
</p><p>Bilius never married and presumably had no children.<sup id="cite_ref-dh8_2-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-dh8-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> According to his nephews, <a href="/wiki/Fred_Weasley" title="Fred Weasley">Fred</a> and <a href="/wiki/George_Weasley" title="George Weasley">George Weasley</a>, he was always a joker, and was fun to have at weddings and parties. On more than one occasion, he was said to have consumed an entire bottle of <a href="/wiki/Firewhisky" title="Firewhisky">firewhisky</a> and then run to the dance floor, where he lifted his robes and began to pull bouquets of flowers out of an alarming part of his body.<sup id="cite_ref-dh8_2-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-dh8-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Bilius was said to have gone "loopy" towards the end of his life.<sup id="cite_ref-dh8_2-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-dh8-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> According to his nephew Ron, he died twenty-four hours after having seen a <a href="/wiki/Grim" title="Grim">Grim</a> sometime in or prior to September <a href="/wiki/1993" title="1993">1993</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-poa6_3-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-poa6-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Bilius?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Bilius?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>"Bilious" is the adjective for 'bile,' a yellowish-green secretion of the liver. The word "bilious" goes back to the old belief that there were four bodily humours (black bile, yellow bile, phlegm, and blood) and these four humours determined a person's temperament. "Bilious" was the personality type associated with an excess of yellow bile, and indicates a peevish, ill-natured disposition.<sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Bilius?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Bilius?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>It is often presumed that Bilius was <a href="/wiki/Arthur_Weasley" title="Arthur Weasley">Arthur Weasley</a>'s brother, as <a href="/wiki/J._K._Rowling" title="J. K. Rowling">J. K. Rowling</a> revealed that Arthur was "one of three brothers,"<sup id="cite_ref-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> but did not give the names of the other <a href="/wiki/Arthur_Weasley%27s_brothers" title="Arthur Weasley&#39;s brothers">two brothers</a>. However, it is still possible that Bilius was a brother of <a href="/wiki/Molly_Weasley" title="Molly Weasley">Molly Weasley née Prewett</a>, as although Rowling has confirmed that <a href="/wiki/Fabian_Prewett" title="Fabian Prewett">Fabian</a> and <a href="/wiki/Gideon_Prewett" title="Gideon Prewett">Gideon Prewett</a> were Molly's brothers,<sup id="cite_ref-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup> she has not stated that they were Molly's only brothers.</li>
<li>Bilius seemed prone to outrageous behaviour, and is hinted to have been an <a href="/wiki/Alcohol" title="Alcohol">alcoholic</a>.</li>
<li>If Bilius was the brother of Molly, then he would distantly link to the <a href="/wiki/House_of_Black" title="House of Black">House of Black</a>, for Molly's aunt by marriage is <a href="/wiki/Lucretia_Black" title="Lucretia Black">Lucretia Prewett née Black</a>. If he was the brother of Arthur, he would be closer, as his possible (and Arthur's) mother&#160;would be <a href="/wiki/Cedrella_Black" title="Cedrella Black">Cedrella Weasley née Black</a>.</li></ul>
