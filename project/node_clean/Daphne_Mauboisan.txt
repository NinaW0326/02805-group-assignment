<p><b>Daphne Mauboisan</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch</a> who lived nearby <a href="/wiki/Armando_Dippet" title="Armando Dippet">Armando Dippet</a> in the early <a href="/wiki/1990s" title="1990s">1990s</a>. On <a href="/wiki/1_September" title="1 September">1 September</a>, <a href="/wiki/1992" title="1992">1992</a>, when <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> and <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ronald Weasley</a> flew a <a href="/wiki/Flying_Ford_Anglia" title="Flying Ford Anglia">Ford Anglia</a> to <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Castle" title="Hogwarts Castle">Hogwarts Castle</a> (a breach of the <a href="/wiki/International_Statute_of_Wizarding_Secrecy" title="International Statute of Wizarding Secrecy">International Statute of Wizarding Secrecy</a>), Mauboisan, along with <a href="/wiki/Leonora_Gore" title="Leonora Gore">Leonora Gore</a>, blamed elderly <a href="/wiki/Armando_Dippet" title="Armando Dippet">Armando Dippet</a>, who was known for his erratic flying.<sup id="cite_ref-COSF_1-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COSF-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
