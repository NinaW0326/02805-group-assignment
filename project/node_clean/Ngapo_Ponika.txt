<b>Ngapo Ponika</b> (born c. <a href="/wiki/1993" title="1993">1993</a>) was a wizard from New Zealand who was the <a href="/wiki/Seeker" title="Seeker">Seeker</a> for the <a href="/wiki/2014" title="2014">2014</a> <a href="/wiki/New_Zealand_National_Quidditch_team" title="New Zealand National Quidditch team">New Zealand National Quidditch team</a>. During the first round match of the <a href="/wiki/2014_Quidditch_World_Cup" title="2014 Quidditch World Cup">427th Quidditch World Cup</a>, against <a href="/wiki/Bulgarian_National_Quidditch_team" title="Bulgarian National Quidditch team">Bulgaria</a>, Ponika was beaten to the <a href="/wiki/Golden_Snitch" title="Golden Snitch">Snitch</a> by <a href="/wiki/Viktor_Krum" title="Viktor Krum">Viktor Krum</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-PM_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PM-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
