<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_life">Early life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Elizabeth_Burke?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Early life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Elizabeth_Burke?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Early life"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>

<p>Elizabeth attended <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> in her youth and was <a href="/wiki/Sorting_ceremony" title="Sorting ceremony">sorted</a> into <a href="/wiki/Slytherin" title="Slytherin">Slytherin</a>. She was appointed <a href="/wiki/Headmaster" title="Headmaster">Headmistress</a> of Hogwarts School at an unknown point.<sup id="cite_ref-Kinect_4-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Kinect-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> Burke had a portrait painted of herself when she was thirty-five years old.<sup id="cite_ref-LE_8-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-LE-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span id="1991–1992_and_1992–1993_school_years"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="1991.E2.80.931992_and_1992.E2.80.931993_school_years"><a href="/wiki/1991%E2%80%931992_school_year" title="1991–1992 school year">1991–1992</a> and <a href="/wiki/1992%E2%80%931993_school_year" title="1992–1993 school year">1992–1993 school years</a></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Elizabeth_Burke?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: 1991–1992 and 1992–1993 school years">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Elizabeth_Burke?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: 1991–1992 and 1992–1993 school years"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>By <a href="/wiki/1991" title="1991">1991</a>, Elizabeth's portrait was hung at the <a href="/wiki/Grand_Staircase" title="Grand Staircase">Grand Staircase</a>, above the stairs between the archway to the <a href="/wiki/Entrance_Hall" title="Entrance Hall">Entrance Hall</a> and the doorway to the first-floor.<sup id="cite_ref-PSF_9-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PSF-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>During the next school year, Elizabeth's portrait was maintained at the same place at the Grand Staircase.<sup id="cite_ref-COSF_10-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COSF-10">&#91;10&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span id="1995–1996_school_year"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="1995.E2.80.931996_school_year"><a href="/wiki/1995%E2%80%931996_school_year" title="1995–1996 school year">1995–1996 school year</a></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Elizabeth_Burke?veaction=edit&amp;section=4" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: 1995–1996 school year">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Elizabeth_Burke?action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: 1995–1996 school year"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>By <a href="/wiki/1995" title="1995">1995</a> her portrait was no longer hung by the first-floor landing of the <a href="/wiki/Grand_Staircase" title="Grand Staircase">Grand Staircase</a>, but two floors below, at the <a href="/wiki/Entrance_Dungeon" title="Entrance Dungeon">Entrance Dungeon</a>. During this school year, she hid a secret passage to the <a href="/wiki/Dungeons" title="Dungeons">Main Dungeons</a>. In order to gain passage, one had to give her the password "<i>Slytherins are Supreme</i>", which only she and the <a href="/wiki/Slytherin" title="Slytherin">Slytherin</a> students knew about.<sup id="cite_ref-OP.2Fg_7-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OP.2Fg-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup> There was another portrait of her, hung near the entrance to the <a href="/wiki/Potions_Classroom" title="Potions Classroom">Potions Classroom</a>, hiding a passage to the seventh-floor corridor. Its password was "<i>Facta, non verba</i>".<sup id="cite_ref-OP_GBA_6-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OP_GBA-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span id="1996–1997_school_year"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="1996.E2.80.931997_school_year"><a href="/wiki/1996%E2%80%931997_school_year" title="1996–1997 school year">1996–1997 school year</a></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Elizabeth_Burke?veaction=edit&amp;section=5" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: 1996–1997 school year">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Elizabeth_Burke?action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: 1996–1997 school year"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>By the next school year, her portraits no longer hid any secret passageway, nor did they stay on the same walls.<sup id="cite_ref-HBPG_11-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBPG-11">&#91;11&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="1997">1997</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Elizabeth_Burke?veaction=edit&amp;section=6" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: 1997">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Elizabeth_Burke?action=edit&amp;section=6" title="Edit section: 1997"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>One of her portraits was hung in <a href="/wiki/Malfoy_Manor" title="Malfoy Manor">Malfoy Manor</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-DHG1_12-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DHG1-12">&#91;12&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Elizabeth_Burke?veaction=edit&amp;section=7" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Elizabeth_Burke?action=edit&amp;section=7" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>Elizabeth died sometime prior to <a href="/wiki/1991" title="1991">1991</a>, as the portraits hung at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Castle" title="Hogwarts Castle">Hogwarts</a> depict deceased individuals. Her clothing seems to indicate that she lived during the sixteenth or seventeenth century.</li>
<li>Elizabeth Burke's portrait bears a resemblance to a <a target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener" class="external text" href="http://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/0/06/Elizabeth_Talbot%2C_Countess_of_Shrewsbury_from_NPG.jpg">1592 portrait of Bess of Hardwick, Countess of Shrewsbury</a> by <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Rowland_Lockey" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Rowland Lockey">Rowland Lockey</a>.</li>
<li>It is possible she may be related to <a href="/wiki/Caractacus_Burke" title="Caractacus Burke">Caractacus Burke</a>, co-founder of <a href="/wiki/Borgin_and_Burkes" title="Borgin and Burkes">Borgin and Burkes</a>.</li>
<li>The start screen for <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_for_Kinect" title="Harry Potter for Kinect">Harry Potter for Kinect</a></i> is the only time Burke's portrait is seen in the Headmaster's Office.</li></ul>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Elizabeth_Burke?veaction=edit&amp;section=8" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Elizabeth_Burke?action=edit&amp;section=8" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>"Elizabeth" is from <i>Ελισαβετ</i> (<i>Elisabet</i>), the Greek form of the Hebrew name אֱלִישֶׁבַע (<i>Elisheva</i>) meaning "my <a href="/wiki/God" title="God">God</a> is an oath" or perhaps "my God is abundance". It has been very popular in England since the reign of Queen <a href="/wiki/Elizabeth_I" title="Elizabeth I">Elizabeth I</a> in the 16th century. Famous modern bearers include the British queen <a href="/wiki/Elizabeth_II" title="Elizabeth II">Elizabeth II</a> (1926-).</li></ul>
