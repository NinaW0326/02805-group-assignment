<p><b>Professor Firenze</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Centaur" title="Centaur">centaur</a>, part of the <a href="/wiki/Forbidden_Forest_Centaur_colony" title="Forbidden Forest Centaur colony">Forbidden Forest Centaur colony</a>, and a <a href="/wiki/Divination" title="Divination">Divination</a> teacher at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> from <a href="/wiki/1996" title="1996">1996</a>-<a href="/wiki/1998" title="1998">1998</a>.
</p><p>Before sometime in March 1996, he lived with the colony in the <a href="/wiki/Forbidden_Forest" title="Forbidden Forest">Forbidden Forest</a>, on the borders of Hogwarts in <a href="/wiki/Scotland" title="Scotland">Scotland</a>. In 1992, Firenze saved <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> in the forest from <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a>, frightening Voldemort away and carrying Harry on his back to safety. Despite his heroics, his herd saw this as a dishonourable act, as they considered themselves too great to be ridden by humans.
</p><p>About four years later, in March 1996, <a href="/wiki/Albus_Dumbledore" title="Albus Dumbledore">Albus Dumbledore</a>, <a href="/wiki/Headmaster" title="Headmaster">Headmaster</a> of <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts</a>, hired him to teach Divination in <a href="/wiki/Sybill_Trelawney" title="Sybill Trelawney">Sybill Trelawney</a>'s stead, after she was sacked by <a href="/wiki/Dolores_Umbridge" title="Dolores Umbridge">Dolores Umbridge</a>. But once again, the other centaurs saw this as servitude, and his colony attacked and banished him. They would have killed him were it not for the intervention of <a href="/wiki/Rubeus_Hagrid" title="Rubeus Hagrid">Rubeus Hagrid</a>.
</p><p>After Dumbledore was reinstated as headmaster, Trelawney was returned to her classroom. However, since Firenze was still estranged with his colony, Dumbledore allowed them both to continue teaching, though Trelawney was uncomfortable with sharing her classes with a centaur.
</p><p>When Firenze was injured fighting in the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a>, he returned to his colony later when the other centaurs had come to the opinion that his pro-human leanings were not so shameful.
</p>

<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Life_with_his_herd">Life with his herd</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Firenze?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Life with his herd">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Firenze?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Life with his herd"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<div class="quote">
<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;Harry Potter and the Philosopher&#39;s Stone">"<i>This is where I leave you. You are safe now. Good luck, Harry Potter. The planets have been read wrongly before now, even by centaurs. I hope this is one of those times.</i>"</span></dd>
<dd>&#8212;Firenze bids Harry Potter goodbye after seeing him to safety<sup class="noprint"><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Philosopher%27s_Stone" title="Harry Potter and the Philosopher&#39;s Stone">[src]</a></sup></dd>

<p>Firenze was, presumably, born to the centaur colony in the Forbidden Forest, and lived part of his life there.<sup id="cite_ref-PS15_4-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PS15-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> He was gifted in <a href="/wiki/Divination" title="Divination">Divination</a> and taught fellow centaur <a href="/wiki/Torvus" title="Torvus">Torvus</a> about it.<sup id="cite_ref-HM6ch4_10-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HM6ch4-10">&#91;10&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>In <a href="/wiki/1989" title="1989">1989</a>, Firenze was chosen as the centaur who decided whether <a href="/wiki/Jacob%27s_sibling" title="Jacob&#39;s sibling">Jacob's sibling</a> was allowed to visit the <a href="/wiki/Centaur_Camp" title="Centaur Camp">Centaur Camp</a> based on the three gifts offered by them. Firenze decided the student could stay, and helped them figure out what <a href="/wiki/Sybill_Trelawney" title="Sybill Trelawney">Professor Trelawney</a>'s prophecy meant.<sup id="cite_ref-HM6ch4_10-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HM6ch4-10">&#91;10&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>On the night of 26 May 1992, Hagrid took Harry, <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione Granger</a>, <a href="/wiki/Neville_Longbottom" title="Neville Longbottom">Neville Longbottom</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Draco_Malfoy" title="Draco Malfoy">Draco Malfoy</a> to their detention in the forest. Their task was to find out why <a href="/wiki/Unicorn" title="Unicorn">unicorns</a> were turning up mauled and dead.<sup id="cite_ref-PS15_4-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PS15-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> 
</p>
<figure class="thumb tleft show-info-icon" style="width: 247px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/d/db/Harry_Potter_and_Firenze_in_the_Forbidden_Forest.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20170721182311" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/d/db/Harry_Potter_and_Firenze_in_the_Forbidden_Forest.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/245?cb=20170721182311" decoding="async" width="245" height="107" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="Harry Potter and Firenze in the Forbidden Forest.jpg" data-image-key="Harry_Potter_and_Firenze_in_the_Forbidden_Forest.jpg" /></a> 	 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:Harry_Potter_and_Firenze_in_the_Forbidden_Forest.jpg" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption">Firenze meets Harry Potter after saving him from Voldemort</p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure>
<p>Harry and Draco later discovered a dead unicorn and a hooded figure (<a href="/wiki/Quirinus_Quirrell" title="Quirinus Quirrell">Quirinus Quirrell</a> in disguise) drinking its <a href="/wiki/Unicorn_blood" title="Unicorn blood">blood</a>. As Draco ran away, horrified, the hooded Quirrell turned to face Harry. The pain on his <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter%27s_scars#The_lightning-bolt_scar" title="Harry Potter&#39;s scars">scar</a> made him fall to his knees, but Firenze, coming across the scene, galloped forwards and charged at Quirrell, scaring him away.<sup id="cite_ref-PS15_4-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PS15-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> Harry found out afterwards, it was <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a> who, drinking the unicorns' blood while sharing Quirrell's body, tried to remain alive long enough so that he could drink the <a href="/wiki/Elixir_of_Life" title="Elixir of Life">Elixir of Life</a> and return to both his body and full strength.<sup id="cite_ref-PS17_11-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PS17-11">&#91;11&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Realising how dangerous it would be to let Harry stay in the Forest, he let him on his back so that he could take him to safety. <a href="/wiki/Bane" title="Bane">Bane</a> and <a href="/wiki/Ronan" title="Ronan">Ronan</a>, two other centaurs from the colony, arrived there and, while Ronan said he was sure Firenze thought he had been acting for the best, Bane started yelling at him because he regarded it as a despicable action, because a centaur is not for humans to ride. Firenze was angered and, leaving both centaurs behind, carried Harry on his back to safety where he was reunited with Hermione, Neville, Draco and Hagrid.<sup id="cite_ref-PS15_4-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PS15-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<p>In March 1996, Dumbledore hired Firenze as the <a href="/wiki/Divination_(class)" title="Divination (class)">Divination</a> teacher when Dolores Umbridge (then-<a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_High_Inquisitor" title="Hogwarts High Inquisitor">High Inquisitor of Hogwarts</a>) sacked Sybill Trelawney.<sup id="cite_ref-OP26_3-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OP26-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>This resulted in Firenze being cast out of his colony, since agreeing to work for a human was considered a betrayal of their kind. His colony had even attacked him, and might have killed him if not for the intervention of Hagrid.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP30_12-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP30-12">&#91;12&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Dumbledore transformed the classroom into a forest-type state for Firenze, and students were requested to lie back and look at the stars on the small enchanted ceiling. <a href="/wiki/Lavender_Brown" title="Lavender Brown">Lavender Brown</a> and Parvati Patil found him very appealing and looked forward to his lessons, even though they liked Professor Trelawney as well.<sup id="cite_ref-OP27_13-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OP27-13">&#91;13&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Later_life">Later life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Firenze?veaction=edit&amp;section=7" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Later life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Firenze?action=edit&amp;section=7" title="Edit section: Later life"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>After the Battle, Firenze was welcomed back into his colony, when they were forced to acknowledge that Firenze's pro-human leanings were not shameful, but honourable.<sup id="cite_ref-JKLC_8-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-JKLC-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>During the <a href="/wiki/Calamity" title="Calamity">Calamity</a>, Firenze helped members of the <a href="/wiki/Statute_of_Secrecy_Task_Force" title="Statute of Secrecy Task Force">Statute of Secrecy Task Force</a> who sought a Magizoologist profession by leading a week-long camp to enhance their physical attributes,<sup id="cite_ref-18" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-18">&#91;18&#93;</a></sup>
while he offered a mind-over-matter seminar to those who sought a Professorship profession to increase their focus.<sup id="cite_ref-19" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-19">&#91;19&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Physical_appearance">Physical appearance</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Firenze?veaction=edit&amp;section=8" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Physical appearance">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Firenze?action=edit&amp;section=8" title="Edit section: Physical appearance"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Firenze was a centaur with a palomino <a href="/wiki/Horse" title="Horse">horse</a> hindbody and blond <a href="/wiki/Human" title="Human">human</a> hair. He had blue eyes and looked younger than both Bane and Ronan.<sup id="cite_ref-PS15_4-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PS15-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<p>Firenze was a courageous <a href="/wiki/Centaur" title="Centaur">centaur</a> who was not as traditional or proud as most of his kind.<sup id="cite_ref-PS15_4-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PS15-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> Whereas most centaurs believed it was shameful to work alongside humans, Firenze was happy to allow Harry to ride him.<sup id="cite_ref-PS15_4-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PS15-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> and became a teacher at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-OP27_13-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OP27-13">&#91;13&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Firenze was willing to stand up for what he believed in even if it meant defying his colony;<sup id="cite_ref-PS15_4-10" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PS15-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> unfortunately this resulted in him nearly being stomped to death by the other centaurs.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP30_12-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP30-12">&#91;12&#93;</a></sup> However, after the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a>, his colony acknowledged his ways as being honourable and welcomed him back.
</p><p>He was friendly with <a href="/wiki/Rubeus_Hagrid" title="Rubeus Hagrid">Hagrid</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-PS15_4-11" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PS15-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> and seemed to get on quite well with <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-PS15_4-12" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PS15-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> who was on speaking terms with him unlike most students, who initially found him intimidating.<sup id="cite_ref-OP27_13-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OP27-13">&#91;13&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Like all centaurs, Firenze commonly tried to read the future in the stars, and <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ron Weasley</a> considered him to be a slightly better teacher than <a href="/wiki/Sybill_Trelawney" title="Sybill Trelawney">Sybill Trelawney</a>, though he was still frustrated that Firenze seemed to be able to provide very little definite information.<sup id="cite_ref-OP27_13-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OP27-13">&#91;13&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Magical_abilities_and_skills">Magical abilities and skills</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Firenze?veaction=edit&amp;section=10" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Firenze?action=edit&amp;section=10" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<div class="quote">
<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix">"<i>Centaurs have unraveled the mysteries of these movements over centuries. Our findings teach us that the future may be glimpsed in the sky above us...</i>"</span></dd>

<ul><li><b><a href="/wiki/Divination" title="Divination">Divination</a></b>: Being a <a href="/wiki/Centaur" title="Centaur">Centaur</a> (and therefore a natural-born <a href="/wiki/Seer" title="Seer">Seer</a>), Firenze was naturally gifted in the <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magical</a> art of divination, and even taught the <a href="/wiki/Divination_(class)" title="Divination (class)">subject</a> at Hogwarts from 1996-1998. Being a centaur, Firenze performed divination through observing planets, <a href="/wiki/Moon" title="Moon">moons</a> and stars, and had a low opinion of human divination, viewing it as nonsensical.<sup id="cite_ref-OP27_13-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OP27-13">&#91;13&#93;</a></sup></li>

This was especially notable as Quirrell was possessed by <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Voldemort</a> at the time, which enhanced his magical powers, although Voldemort was also in a weakened form at the time.<sup id="cite_ref-PS15_4-13" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PS15-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> Despite getting injured in the first half of the battle,<sup id="cite_ref-DH33_16-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH33-16">&#91;16&#93;</a></sup> he also survived the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a> with no fatal injury.<sup id="cite_ref-DH36_17-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH36-17">&#91;17&#93;</a></sup></li></ul>

<ul><li>He is portrayed by <a href="/wiki/Ray_Fearon" title="Ray Fearon">Ray Fearon</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Philosopher%27s_Stone_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Philosopher&#39;s Stone (film)">film adaptation</a> of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Philosopher%27s_Stone" title="Harry Potter and the Philosopher&#39;s Stone">Harry Potter and the Philosopher's Stone</a></i>, but does not appear in any of the later films.</li>
<li>In the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_(book_series)" title="Harry Potter (book series)">books</a>, Firenze is said to have silver-blond hair and is even considered handsome by some students. In the <i>Philosopher's Stone</i> film, however, he has dark hair. He doesn't stay with Harry or let him ride on his back either, because <a href="/wiki/Rubeus_Hagrid" title="Rubeus Hagrid">Hagrid</a> and the others appear straight away.</li>
<li>It is possible that Firenze is based on the legend of <a target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener" class="external text" href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Chiron">Chiron</a>, a centaur from Greek mythology who left his "wild and lusty" brethren to instead tutor heroes.</li>
<li>In the film of <i>Harry Potter and the Philosopher's Stone,</i> Firenze is shown to have strikingly, almost glowing, amber eyes instead of blue eyes.</li>
<li>Firenze is the fourth known professor to not be completely human, after <a href="/wiki/Filius_Flitwick" title="Filius Flitwick">Filius Flitwick</a> (part-<a href="/wiki/Goblin" title="Goblin">goblin</a>), <a href="/wiki/Remus_Lupin" title="Remus Lupin">Remus Lupin</a> (a <a href="/wiki/Werewolf" title="Werewolf">werewolf</a>), and Rubeus Hagrid (a half-<a href="/wiki/Giant" title="Giant">giant</a>).</li>
<li>In addition, out of all the known professors at Hogwarts (including <a href="/wiki/Cuthbert_Binns" title="Cuthbert Binns">Cuthbert Binns</a>, who was a wizard during his lifetime and is now a ghost), Firenze is the only one who is not a wizard.</li></ul>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Firenze?veaction=edit&amp;section=12" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Firenze?action=edit&amp;section=12" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>"Firenze" is the Italian name for the city of <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Florence" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Florence">Florence</a>. It is interesting to note that Galileo Galilei, one of history's greatest astronomers, lived out his final years there. Firenze's teaching of Divination deals with Astrology and Astronomy, as opposed to Trelawney's fortune-telling, hence this connection.</li>
<li>The meaning of the name Firenze is Flower, Blossom.</li></ul>
