This <b>individual</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch</a> at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a>, who was a member of <a href="/wiki/Slytherin" title="Slytherin">Slytherin</a> house. During the <a href="/wiki/1996%E2%80%931997_school_year" title="1996–1997 school year">1996–1997 school year</a>, <a href="/wiki/Draco_Malfoy" title="Draco Malfoy">Malfoy</a> had <a href="/wiki/Gregory_Goyle" title="Gregory Goyle">Goyle</a> turn into this girl via <a href="/wiki/Polyjuice_Potion" title="Polyjuice Potion">Polyjuice Potion</a> to be able to guard the <a href="/wiki/Room_of_Requirement" title="Room of Requirement">Room of Requirement</a> while Malfoy was repairing the <a href="/wiki/Vanishing_Cabinet" title="Vanishing Cabinet">Vanishing Cabinet</a> inside.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP21_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP21-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
