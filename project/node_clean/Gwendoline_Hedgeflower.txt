<b>Gwendoline Hedgeflower</b><sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch</a> who attended <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> in the <a href="/wiki/1990s" title="1990s">1990s</a> and was Sorted in <a href="/wiki/Hufflepuff" title="Hufflepuff">Hufflepuff</a> house.
</p><p>She participated and fought alongside most of her fellow Hogwarts students in the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a> against <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a> and his army.
</p>

During the <a href="/wiki/1995%E2%80%931996_school_year" title="1995–1996 school year">1995–1996 school year</a>, she appeared at the <a href="/wiki/King%27s_Cross_station" class="mw-redirect" title="King&#39;s Cross station">King's Cross station</a> when Harry had a hallucination of <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Voldemort</a>.<sup class="noprint">&#91;<a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Wikipedia:Citing_sources" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Wikipedia:Citing sources"><i><span title="The material in the vicinity of this tag needs references to reliable sources." style="white-space: nowrap;">citation needed</span></i></a>&#93;</sup> She later appeared celebrating in the <a href="/wiki/Viaduct_Courtyard" title="Viaduct Courtyard">Entrance Courtyard</a> just as the <a href="/wiki/Weasley_family" title="Weasley family">Weasley</a> <a href="/wiki/Fred_and_George_Weasley" title="Fred and George Weasley">twins</a> were <a href="/wiki/Fred_and_George_Weasley%27s_departure_from_Hogwarts" title="Fred and George Weasley&#39;s departure from Hogwarts">departing</a> from <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts</a>.

<p>During the <a href="/wiki/1997%E2%80%931998_school_year" title="1997–1998 school year">1997–1998 school year</a>, Gwendoline continued to attend Hogwarts under <a href="/wiki/Severus_Snape" title="Severus Snape">Severus Snape</a>'s regime, implying that she was <a href="/wiki/Half-blood" title="Half-blood">half-blood</a> or <a href="/wiki/Pure-blood" title="Pure-blood">pure-blood</a>.
</p><p>On <a href="/wiki/2_May" title="2 May">2 May</a>, <a href="/wiki/1998" title="1998">1998</a>, she participated in the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a>. After the <a href="/wiki/Ousting_of_Severus_Snape" title="Ousting of Severus Snape">Ousting of Severus Snape</a>, Gwendoline was seen running up the <a href="/wiki/Grand_Staircase" title="Grand Staircase">Grand Staircase</a> alongside <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>. She stood next to <a href="/wiki/Luna_Lovegood" title="Luna Lovegood">Luna Lovegood</a> and <a href="/wiki/Draco_Malfoy" title="Draco Malfoy">Draco Malfoy</a> in the Entrance Courtyard during Voldemort's speech.
</p>
