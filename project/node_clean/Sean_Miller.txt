<p><b>Sean Miller</b> (born c. <a href="/wiki/1974" title="1974">1974</a>-<a href="/wiki/1980" title="1980">1980</a>) was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> who attended <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> in the <a href="/wiki/1990s" title="1990s">1990s</a>. His quill was accidentally eaten by a female <a href="/wiki/Ravenclaw" title="Ravenclaw">Ravenclaw</a> student during the <a href="/wiki/1991%E2%80%931992_school_year" title="1991–1992 school year">1991–1992 school year</a>.<sup><sup id="cite_ref-PSgba_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PSgba-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup></sup>
</p>
