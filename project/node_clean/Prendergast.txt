<p><b>Prendergast </b>was a <a href="/wiki/Great_Britain" title="Great Britain">British</a>&#160;<a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a>&#160;who&#160;worked at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> in around the <a href="/wiki/1910s" title="1910s">1910s</a>. He once gave <a href="/wiki/Newton_Scamander" title="Newton Scamander">Newton Scamander</a> a month's worth of detention for arguing that there are no strange <a href="/wiki/Creature" title="Creature">creatures</a>, just blinkered people. <a href="/wiki/Leta_Lestrange" title="Leta Lestrange">Leta Lestrange</a> later set off a <a href="/wiki/Dungbomb" title="Dungbomb">dungbomb</a> under Prendergast's desk so she could join Newt in detention.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
