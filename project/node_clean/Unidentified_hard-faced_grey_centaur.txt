<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_hard-faced_grey_centaur?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_hard-faced_grey_centaur?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>by the <a href="/wiki/1995-1996_school_year" class="mw-redirect" title="1995-1996 school year">1995-1996 school year</a>. He resented the <a href="/wiki/Centaur" title="Centaur">centaur</a> <a href="/wiki/Firenze" title="Firenze">Firenze</a> going to work for <a href="/wiki/Albus_Dumbledore" title="Albus Dumbledore">Albus Dumbledore</a>, and considered it going "into servitude to <a href="/wiki/Human" title="Human">humans</a>." After <a href="/wiki/Rubeus_Hagrid" title="Rubeus Hagrid">Rubeus Hagrid</a> saved Firenze, this centaur with the other centaurs kicked both Firenze and Hagrid out, and resented Hagrid's <a href="/wiki/Giant" title="Giant">giant</a> half-brother <a href="/wiki/Grawp" title="Grawp">Grawp</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP30_1-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP30-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<div class="quote">
<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix"><b>Centaur</b>: "<i>What are you doing in our forest? Why are you here?</i>"</span></dd>
<dd><b>Umbridge</b>: "'<i>Your' forest? I would remind you that you live here only because the <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">Ministry of Magic</a> permits you certain areas of land-</i>"</dd>
<dd>&#8212; hard-faced centaur and <a href="/wiki/Dolores_Umbridge" title="Dolores Umbridge">Dolores Umbridge</a><sup class="noprint"><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Order_of_the_Phoenix" title="Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix">[src]</a></sup></dd></dl>
</div>
<p>Later that year, the centaur with his allies attacked <a href="/wiki/Dolores_Umbridge" title="Dolores Umbridge">Dolores Umbridge</a>, when she entered their area of the forest, and took Umbridge when she called centaurs "of near-human intelligence." After ridding themselves of Umbridge, this centaur and <a href="/wiki/Unidentified_centaur" title="Unidentified centaur">another</a> tried to decide what to do with <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> and <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione Granger</a> who had insulted them, despite the latter's protests. Fortunately, this centaur and the others were scared off by <a href="/wiki/Grawp" title="Grawp">Grawp</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP33_2-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP33-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
