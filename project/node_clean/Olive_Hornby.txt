</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Olive_Hornby?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Olive_Hornby?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_life">Early life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Olive_Hornby?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Early life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Olive_Hornby?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Early life"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Olive was born between <a href="/wiki/1926" title="1926">1926</a> and <a href="/wiki/1932" title="1932">1932</a>, around the time <a href="/wiki/Myrtle_Warren" title="Myrtle Warren">Myrtle</a> and born. She also had a <a href="/wiki/Olive_Hornby%27s_brother" title="Olive Hornby&#39;s brother">brother</a>.
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Hogwarts_School">Hogwarts School</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Olive_Hornby?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Hogwarts School">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Olive_Hornby?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Hogwarts School"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>When she was eleven years-old, Olive received a letter, accepting her into <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a>. It's unknown if she was older than or was the same age as <a href="/wiki/Myrtle_Warren" title="Myrtle Warren">Myrtle</a>, so their <a href="/wiki/Sorting_ceremony" title="Sorting ceremony">Sorting</a> might have been at the same time or in different years.
</p>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Bullying_Myrtle">Bullying Myrtle</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Olive_Hornby?veaction=edit&amp;section=4" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Bullying Myrtle">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Olive_Hornby?action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Bullying Myrtle"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<p><a href="/wiki/Myrtle_Warren" title="Myrtle Warren">Myrtle</a> was <a href="/wiki/Sorting_ceremony" title="Sorting ceremony">Sorted</a> into <a href="/wiki/Ravenclaw" title="Ravenclaw">Ravenclaw</a> in <a href="/wiki/1940" title="1940">1940</a>. She was constantly teased and bullied by her fellow students. On one such occasion, Olive cruelly taunted her about her glasses, which was the reason Myrtle was in the bathroom that concealed the <a href="/wiki/Chamber_of_Secrets" title="Chamber of Secrets">Chamber of Secrets</a> on the <a href="/wiki/13_June" title="13 June">night of her murder</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-COS16_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COS16-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> After realising that no one had seen <a href="/wiki/Myrtle_Warren" title="Myrtle Warren">Myrtle</a> for a couple of hours, and knowing that Olive Hornby had previously bullied Myrtle, Professor <a href="/wiki/Armando_Dippet" title="Armando Dippet">Armando Dippet</a>, then-<a href="/wiki/Headmaster" title="Headmaster">Headmaster</a> of Hogwarts, requested her to go and look for Myrtle. When Olive got to the bathroom, the first thing she saw was Myrtle's <a href="/wiki/Death" title="Death">dead</a> body lying on the floor. This left Olive in a state of shock and horror, according to Myrtle's <a href="/wiki/Ghost" title="Ghost">ghost</a>, who watched the scene with delight.<sup id="cite_ref-GOF25_4-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF25-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Haunted_by_Moaning_Myrtle">Haunted by Moaning Myrtle</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Olive_Hornby?veaction=edit&amp;section=5" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Haunted by Moaning Myrtle">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Olive_Hornby?action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Haunted by Moaning Myrtle"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p><a href="/wiki/Myrtle_Warren" title="Myrtle Warren">Moaning Myrtle</a>, as Myrtle's ghost was now known, haunted Olive Hornby for a long period of time, even after she left Hogwarts.<sup id="cite_ref-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> After Moaning Myrtle interrupted her <a href="/wiki/Olive_Hornby%27s_brother" title="Olive Hornby&#39;s brother">brother</a>'s wedding, Olive went to the <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">Ministry of Magic</a> to get rid of the troublesome ghost. <a href="/wiki/Myrtle_Warren" title="Myrtle Warren">Myrtle Warren</a> was then sent back to <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Castle" title="Hogwarts Castle">Hogwarts Castle</a>, where she haunted the bathroom in which she died.<sup id="cite_ref-GOF25_4-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF25-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Death">Death</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Olive_Hornby?veaction=edit&amp;section=6" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Death">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Olive_Hornby?action=edit&amp;section=6" title="Edit section: Death"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Olive Hornby was already <a href="/wiki/Death" title="Death">dead</a> before the events surrounding the <a href="/wiki/1994%E2%80%931995_school_year" title="1994–1995 school year">1994-1995</a> <a href="/wiki/Triwizard_Tournament" title="Triwizard Tournament">Triwizard Tournament</a> at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a>, although it is unknown what year she actually died.<sup id="cite_ref-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Olive_Hornby?veaction=edit&amp;section=7" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Olive_Hornby?action=edit&amp;section=7" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>The olive branch to the ancient Greeks, was sacred to Athena and was a sign of peace.</li>
<li>Her last name, <i>Hornby</i>, is an Old Norse personal name composed of the elements <i>horn</i> ‘horn' + <i>boði</i> ‘messenger'. This could possibly point to the fact that Olive was most likely the first to send the message about Myrtle's death. Horns were also a symbol of power in many cultures, which could mean that Olive had power and authority over Myrtle. It might also be a "Last Trump" (in the sense of trumpet) reference.</li></ul>
