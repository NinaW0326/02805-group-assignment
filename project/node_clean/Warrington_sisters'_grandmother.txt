This <b>woman</b> was the grandmother of <a href="/wiki/Ivy_Warrington" title="Ivy Warrington">Ivy</a> and <a href="/wiki/Winifred_Warrington" title="Winifred Warrington">Winifred Warrington</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-MA_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-MA-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>When her granddaughters were growing up, Ivy accidentally vanished Winifred. In an effort to help Ivy forget what she did, she put a <a href="/wiki/Memory_Charm" title="Memory Charm">Memory Charm</a> on her. Unfortunately, it caused Ivy to wholly forget about Winifred.<sup id="cite_ref-MA_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-MA-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>  
</p>
