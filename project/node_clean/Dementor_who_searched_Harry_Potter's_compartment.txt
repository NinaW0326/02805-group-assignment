<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Origins">Origins</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Dementor_who_searched_Harry_Potter%27s_compartment?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Origins">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Dementor_who_searched_Harry_Potter%27s_compartment?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Origins"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>It is possible that the Dementor was one of the original Dementors found by the <a href="/wiki/Auror" title="Auror">Aurors</a> when they investigated <a href="/wiki/Azkaban" title="Azkaban">Azkaban</a> following the death of <a href="/wiki/Ekrizdis" title="Ekrizdis">Ekrizdis</a>, as Dementors are immortal. It is also possible that this particular Dementor was "younger" and had grown from the despair inherent to <a href="/wiki/Azkaban" title="Azkaban">Azkaban</a> at some point before 1993.<sup id="cite_ref-PM_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PM-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="1993-1994">1993-1994</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Dementor_who_searched_Harry_Potter%27s_compartment?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: 1993-1994">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Dementor_who_searched_Harry_Potter%27s_compartment?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: 1993-1994"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>

<p>The Dementor then surveyed the compartment, creating an unhappy and freezing environment, feeding on human happiness and creating feelings of depression and despair in any person in close proximity to it. As a result, <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>, noted for his particularly bad reaction to a Dementor's presence, began to relive the night he heard his parents murdered by Voldemort. The Dementor then started to focus on him (which <a href="/wiki/Remus_Lupin" title="Remus Lupin">Professor Lupin</a> attributes to his being easier prey because of all the terrible things he had gone through). Harry fainted and fell out of his seat, prompting Lupin to step forward and tell the Dementor that <a href="/wiki/Sirius_Black" title="Sirius Black">Sirius Black</a> was obviously not in their compartment, and that it must leave. The Dementor refused to comply, apparently eager to keep feeding on Harry Potter; realising it would get nowhere by bargaining with it, Lupin cast a non-coroporeal <a href="/wiki/Patronus_Charm" title="Patronus Charm">Patronus Charm</a> to drive the Dementor away, and the Dementor withdrew. It soon joined the rest of its fellows and left the train.<sup id="cite_ref-POA5_2-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-POA5-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>

<p>During the <a href="/wiki/Second_Wizarding_War" title="Second Wizarding War">Second Wizarding War</a>, the Dementor probably joined its kin in switching allegiance back to <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a> and deserted its duties at <a href="/wiki/Azkaban" title="Azkaban">Azkaban</a> to fight alongside him. It is likely that it was present at the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a>, where Voldemort had pooled his entire army (Dementors included) in an attempt to finally destroy his opponents and take <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Castle" title="Hogwarts Castle">Hogwarts Castle</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Magical_abilities_and_skills">Magical abilities and skills</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Dementor_who_searched_Harry_Potter%27s_compartment?veaction=edit&amp;section=5" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Dementor_who_searched_Harry_Potter%27s_compartment?action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li><b><a href="/wiki/Wandless_magic" title="Wandless magic">Wandless magic</a>:</b> The Dementor appeared able to slide a compartment door on the <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Express" title="Hogwarts Express">Hogwarts Express</a> through a mere wave of its hand.</li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Dementor%27s_Kiss" title="Dementor&#39;s Kiss">Dementor's Kiss</a>:</b> Like all <a href="/wiki/Dementor" title="Dementor">Dementors</a>, the Dementor could presumably perform the dreaded Kiss on a <a href="/wiki/Human" title="Human">human</a> being, sucking out their <a href="/wiki/Soul" title="Soul">soul</a> and leaving their body an empty shell.</li></ul>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Dementor_who_searched_Harry_Potter%27s_compartment?veaction=edit&amp;section=6" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Dementor_who_searched_Harry_Potter%27s_compartment?action=edit&amp;section=6" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>In the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Prisoner_of_Azkaban_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban (film)">film adaptation</a> of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Prisoner_of_Azkaban" title="Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban">Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban</a></i>, the Dementor displays an aptitude for <a href="/wiki/Wandless_magic" title="Wandless magic">telekinesis</a>, sliding the compartment door open without touching it. In the books, it is mentioned that "the compartment door slid open", leaving it ambiguous whether the Dementor simply pulled it aside silently or used magic.</li>
<li>Due to how hard it is to tell Dementors apart, it is unknown if this particular Dementor was present in any subsequent incidents involving limited groups of Dementors — such as the <a href="/wiki/Attack_in_Little_Whinging" title="Attack in Little Whinging">attack on Harry Potter and Dudley Dursley</a> in <a href="/wiki/1995" title="1995">1995</a> or <a href="/wiki/Dolores_Umbridge" title="Dolores Umbridge">Dolores Umbridge</a>'s guard in her <a href="/wiki/Muggle-Born_Registration_Commission_courtroom" title="Muggle-Born Registration Commission courtroom">courtroom</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/J._K._Rowling" title="J. K. Rowling">J. K. Rowling</a> has said that under <a href="/wiki/Kingsley_Shacklebolt" title="Kingsley Shacklebolt">Minister Shacklebolt</a>, the Dementors were no longer employed in <a href="/wiki/Azkaban" title="Azkaban">Azkaban</a>, and also stated that while they cannot actually be destroyed, Dementors can <a href="/wiki/Death" title="Death">dissipate</a> should they be unable to feed for long periods of time. It is thus possible this Dementor, like the others guarding <a href="/wiki/Azkaban" title="Azkaban">Azkaban</a>, was starved to “death” after 1998.</li></ul>
