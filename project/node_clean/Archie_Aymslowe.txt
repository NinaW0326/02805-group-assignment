<dd>&#8212;Archie to a <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">Ministry of Magic</a> official at the <a href="/wiki/1994_Quidditch_World_Cup" title="1994 Quidditch World Cup">1994 Quidditch World Cup</a><sup class="noprint"><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Goblet_of_Fire" title="Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire">[src]</a></sup></dd>

<p><b>Archie Aymslowe</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> and president of the <a href="/wiki/Fresh_Air_Refreshes_Totally" title="Fresh Air Refreshes Totally">Fresh Air Refreshes Totally</a> (F.A.R.T.) movement, a fringe group whose members, in direct violation of the <a href="/wiki/International_Statute_of_Wizarding_Secrecy" title="International Statute of Wizarding Secrecy">International Statute of Wizarding Secrecy</a>, refuse to wear <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggle</a> trousers in public, insisting that they "stem the <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magical</a> flow at source."<sup id="cite_ref-WW_2-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-WW-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Archie attended the <a href="/wiki/1994_Quidditch_World_Cup" title="1994 Quidditch World Cup">1994 Quidditch World Cup</a>. Since the event was a large gathering of wizards, the <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">Ministry of Magic</a> wanted them to dress as Muggles. Archie himself wore a flowery female nightdress without realising that what he had chosen was inappropriate. However, he resisted all efforts by a <a href="/wiki/Unidentified_Ministry_of_Magic_Wizard_at_the_1994_Quidditch_World_Cup" title="Unidentified Ministry of Magic Wizard at the 1994 Quidditch World Cup">Ministry official</a> to get him to change into a pair of pin-striped trousers, insisting that he preferred a "healthy breeze" around his "privates." <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione Granger</a> found this amusing, having a fit of giggles and having to duck out of the area, waiting until Archie had left to return to the others.<sup id="cite_ref-GOF7_1-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF7-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
