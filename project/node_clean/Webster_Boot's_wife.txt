<b>Mrs Boot </b>was a <a href="/wiki/Scotland" title="Scotland">Scottish</a> <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch</a> who worked for the <a href="/wiki/Ministry_of_Magic" title="Ministry of Magic">Ministry of Magic</a> during the <a href="/wiki/17th_century" title="17th century">17th century</a>. She was the wife of <a href="/wiki/Webster_Boot" title="Webster Boot">Webster Boot</a>, one of the founders of <a href="/wiki/Ilvermorny_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Ilvermorny School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Ilvermorny School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a>.

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Webster_Boot%27s_wife?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Webster_Boot%27s_wife?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Mrs Boot was born in <a href="/wiki/Scotland" title="Scotland">Scotland</a> sometime in the <a href="/wiki/17th_century" title="17th century">17th century</a>. She was likely educated at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-Pottermore_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Pottermore-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>She eventually landed an unspecified job with the <a href="/wiki/Ministry_of_Magic" title="Ministry of Magic">Ministry of Magic</a>. When <a href="/wiki/Webster_Boot" title="Webster Boot">Webster Boot</a>, as an <a href="/wiki/Auror" title="Auror">Auror</a> for hire, travelled to <a href="/wiki/London" title="London">London</a> to repatriate a fugitive British <a href="/wiki/Dark_Wizard" title="Dark Wizard">dark wizard</a>, they both fell in love. Webster then relocated to <a href="/wiki/Great_Britain" title="Great Britain">Britain</a> to be with her. Their descendants, likely including <a href="/wiki/Terry_Boot" title="Terry Boot">Terry Boot</a>, would go on to be educated at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-Pottermore_1-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Pottermore-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
