<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_Hufflepuff_lady?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_Hufflepuff_lady?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span id="1991–1992_school_year"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="1991.E2.80.931992_school_year"><a href="/wiki/1991%E2%80%931992_school_year" title="1991–1992 school year">1991–1992 school year</a></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_Hufflepuff_lady?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: 1991–1992 school year">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_Hufflepuff_lady?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: 1991–1992 school year"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>In <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>'s first year at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a>, she was guarding a secret room in the Castle's Grounds, concealing a <a href="/wiki/Chocolate_Frog_Card" title="Chocolate Frog Card">Chocolate Frog Card</a>. The password required to gain entrance was "<i>Catweazle</i>".<sup id="cite_ref-PS_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PS-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span id="1992–1993_school_year"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="1992.E2.80.931993_school_year"><a href="/wiki/1992%E2%80%931993_school_year" title="1992–1993 school year">1992–1993 school year</a></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_Hufflepuff_lady?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: 1992–1993 school year">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_Hufflepuff_lady?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: 1992–1993 school year"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>A year later, her portrait had been moved, and now guarded the way to the Hufflepuff Duelling Turrets.<sup id="cite_ref-CS_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-CS-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> Another portrait was also created which guarded a lever room inside a secret area behind a bookcase in the <a href="/wiki/Knights%27_Room" title="Knights&#39; Room">Knights' Room</a>. She was kept with company by the <a href="/wiki/Unidentified_Slytherin_knight" title="Unidentified Slytherin knight">Slytherin knight</a>.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_Hufflepuff_lady?veaction=edit&amp;section=4" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_Hufflepuff_lady?action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>This witch may have passed away sometime prior to <a href="/wiki/1991" title="1991">1991</a> as many portraits hung at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Castle" title="Hogwarts Castle">Hogwarts Castle</a> depict deceased individuals.</li>
<li><a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Catweazle" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Catweazle"><i>Catweazle</i></a> was a British children's television series in the <a href="/wiki/1970s" title="1970s">1970s</a> that featured an eccentric, time-travelling wizard.</li>
<li>In the Danish version of <i>Philosopher's Stone</i> there is a mistake when Harry uses the password: <a href="/wiki/Fred_Weasley" title="Fred Weasley">Fred</a> and <a href="/wiki/George_Weasley" title="George Weasley">George Weasley</a> tell Harry to say "<i>Katvæsel</i>" (<i>Catweasle</i>), but Harry says <i>"Katvæsen"</i> (<i>Catcreature</i>) to the portrait. This appears to be a typo.</li></ul>
