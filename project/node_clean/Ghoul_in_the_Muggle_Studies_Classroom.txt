<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="History">History</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Ghoul_in_the_Muggle_Studies_Classroom?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: History">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Ghoul_in_the_Muggle_Studies_Classroom?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: History"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>In <a href="/wiki/1993" title="1993">1993</a>, it attacked <a href="/wiki/Neville_Longbottom" title="Neville Longbottom">Neville Longbottom</a>, after being set free by <a href="/wiki/Draco_Malfoy" title="Draco Malfoy">Draco Malfoy</a>, <a href="/wiki/Gregory_Goyle" title="Gregory Goyle">Gregory Goyle</a> and <a href="/wiki/Vincent_Crabbe" title="Vincent Crabbe">Vincent Crabbe</a>. The ghoul threw objects at Neville and wailed at him. However, when <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> and <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ron Weasley</a> arrived at the classroom, Ron prevented it from continuing to attack by using the <a href="/wiki/Wand-Lighting_Charm_Duo" title="Wand-Lighting Charm Duo"><i>Lumos Duo</i></a> spell, which allowed him to trap it in a small box room.<sup id="cite_ref-POAG_1-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-POAG-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> What <a href="/wiki/Charity_Burbage" title="Charity Burbage">Charity Burbage</a>, the <a href="/wiki/Muggle_Studies" title="Muggle Studies">Muggle Studies</a> <a href="/wiki/Professor" title="Professor">professor</a>, did with the ghoul is unknown.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Ghoul_in_the_Muggle_Studies_Classroom?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Ghoul_in_the_Muggle_Studies_Classroom?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>The ghoul appears only in <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Prisoner_of_Azkaban_(PlayStation_2,_Xbox,_GameCube)" title="Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban (PlayStation 2, Xbox, GameCube)">the PS2, Xbox, and GameCube version</a> of the <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Prisoner_of_Azkaban" title="Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban">Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban</a></i> <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Prisoner_of_Azkaban_(video_game)" title="Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban (video game)">video game adaptation</a>.</li>
<li>The ghoul can be hit with spells such as  the <a href="/wiki/Knockback_Jinx" title="Knockback Jinx">Knockback Jinx</a>, though if done so, it will ignore Neville and charge at the caster with rage at a speed which makes it difficult to outrun. After damaging the caster with its dash, the ghoul will resume targeting Neville, unless provoked again.
<ul><li>It will also only charge at the caster if they happen to be within a certain range of it, as too far from the distance, the A.I. will not notice the player if they happen to be hiding behind something too far from it. It is possible however, to quickly climb onto something in order to make the ghoul cancel all its actions and return to its normal pattern if the player finds the jack-in-the-box Ron is meant to examine without opening it.</li></ul></li></ul>
