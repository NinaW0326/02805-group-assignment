</p><p><b>Enid Longbottom</b> (fl. <a href="/wiki/1988" title="1988">1988</a>) was the wife of <a href="/wiki/Algie_Longbottom" title="Algie Longbottom">Algie Longbottom</a>, and thus the aunt-by-marriage of <a href="/wiki/Frank_Longbottom" title="Frank Longbottom">Frank Longbottom</a>, and the great-aunt of <a href="/wiki/Neville_Longbottom" title="Neville Longbottom">Neville Longbottom</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-longbottomfamilytree_1-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-longbottomfamilytree-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Enid_Longbottom?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Enid_Longbottom?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>One time when Neville was eight, his great-uncle <a href="/wiki/Algie_Longbottom" title="Algie Longbottom">Algie</a> came over for tea, and ended up hanging the boy out of an upstairs window in yet another one of his many attempts to "force some <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magic</a>" out of him. When Enid offered Algie a <a href="/wiki/Meringue" title="Meringue">meringue</a>, he accidentally dropped Neville, but the boy bounced safely down the garden and out into the road. The family was overjoyed to learn that Neville was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-PS7_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PS7-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Enid_Longbottom?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Enid_Longbottom?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The name "Enid" was derived from Welsh <i>enaid</i> meaning "soul" or "life". She was the wife of Geraint in Arthurian legend.}
