<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Gryffindor_boy_who_fetched_Harry_Potter?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Gryffindor_boy_who_fetched_Harry_Potter?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>In <a href="/wiki/1992" title="1992">1992</a>, he fetched <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>, who had just finished a <a href="/wiki/Charms_(class)" title="Charms (class)">Charms</a> class with <a href="/wiki/Filius_Flitwick" title="Filius Flitwick">Filius Flitwick</a>, and told him to go up to <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor_Tower" title="Gryffindor Tower">Gryffindor Tower</a>, as <a href="/wiki/Draco_Malfoy" title="Draco Malfoy">Draco Malfoy</a> had placed a <a href="/wiki/Leg-Locker_Curse" title="Leg-Locker Curse">Leg-Locker Curse</a> on <a href="/wiki/Neville_Longbottom" title="Neville Longbottom">Neville Longbottom</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-PSG_2-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PSG-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
