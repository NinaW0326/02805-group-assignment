<p><b>Godric Gryffindor</b> (fl. c. <a href="/wiki/First_millennium_A.D." title="First millennium A.D.">993</a>) was an <a href="/wiki/England" title="England">English</a> <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> and one of the <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_founders" title="Hogwarts founders">four founders</a> of <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a>. He was great friends with <a href="/wiki/Salazar_Slytherin" title="Salazar Slytherin">Salazar Slytherin</a>, but opposed him when he later thought that they should not accept <a href="/wiki/Muggle-born" title="Muggle-born">Muggle-born</a> students at Hogwarts. It is unknown when he died. In his honour Gryffindor's birthplace became known as <a href="/wiki/Godric%27s_Hollow" title="Godric&#39;s Hollow">Godric's Hollow</a>. Godric Gryffindor's <a href="/wiki/Portrait" title="Portrait">portrait</a> still remains at Hogwarts.
</p>

<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_life">Early life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Godric_Gryffindor?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Early life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Godric_Gryffindor?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Early life"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<div class="quote">
<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire">"<i>Bold Gryffindor, from wild moor...</i>"</span></dd>
<dd>&#8212;The <a href="/wiki/Sorting_Hat" title="Sorting Hat">Sorting Hat</a> singing about the founders' birthplaces<sup class="noprint"><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Goblet_of_Fire" title="Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire">[src]</a></sup></dd>

<p>Gryffindor hailed from a moor which is now known as <a href="/wiki/Godric%27s_Hollow" title="Godric&#39;s Hollow">Godric's Hollow</a><sup id="cite_ref-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup>, a small West Country village in <a href="/wiki/England" title="England">England</a>. He was described as being "the best <a href="/wiki/Duelling" title="Duelling">duellist</a> of his time".<sup id="cite_ref-WOTM_7-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-WOTM-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Hogwarts_founder">Hogwarts founder</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Godric_Gryffindor?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Hogwarts founder">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Godric_Gryffindor?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Hogwarts founder"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<div class="quote">
<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire">"<i>By Gryffindor, the bravest were prized far beyond the rest.</i>"</span></dd>
<dd>&#8212;<a href="/wiki/Sorting_Hat" title="Sorting Hat">Sorting Hat</a> regarding Gryffindor<sup class="noprint"><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Goblet_of_Fire" title="Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire">[src]</a></sup></dd>
<dd></dd></dl></div><p>Said to be one of "<i>the four most brilliant witches and wizards of the time</i>"<sup id="cite_ref-CS9_3-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-CS9-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>, Godric Gryffindor founded <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> in his adulthood, with friends <a href="/wiki/Salazar_Slytherin" title="Salazar Slytherin">Salazar Slytherin</a>, <a href="/wiki/Helga_Hufflepuff" title="Helga Hufflepuff">Helga Hufflepuff</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Rowena_Ravenclaw" title="Rowena Ravenclaw">Rowena Ravenclaw</a>, though he later severed ties with Slytherin. He even appears to have carefully selected members of <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor" title="Gryffindor">his own House</a>.
</p><p>Gryffindor is said to have praised courage, determination and strength of heart above all other qualities. Indeed, he selected students for his house based upon their daring and bravery, according to the <a href="/wiki/Sorting_Hat" title="Sorting Hat">Sorting Hat</a>. He also was the most in favour of allowing <a href="/wiki/Muggle-borns" class="mw-redirect" title="Muggle-borns">Muggle-borns</a> into the school.
</p><p>The founders, however, were unsure of how they would Sort the students when they were dead. To solve this, Gryffindor took off his hat and all four of them enchanted it so that it could sort the students long after their deaths. It was, from then on, known as the <a href="/wiki/Sorting_Hat" title="Sorting Hat">Sorting Hat</a>.
</p><p>Gryffindor was initially a close friend of <a href="/wiki/Salazar_Slytherin" title="Salazar Slytherin">Salazar Slytherin</a>, but over time their friendship deteriorated. After a number of years of working peacefully together, Slytherin eventually grew apart from his fellow founders due to his distrust of Muggle-borns, and his belief that they should not be accepted into <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts</a>. When Slytherin failed to convince Gryffindor and the others to accept his ideas, he chose to leave the school. Unbeknownst to Gryffindor, his former friend built a <a href="/wiki/Chamber_of_Secrets" title="Chamber of Secrets">hidden chamber</a> within the castle, in which he hid a <a href="/wiki/Salazar_Slytherin%27s_Basilisk" class="mw-redirect" title="Salazar Slytherin&#39;s Basilisk">basilisk</a> that his heir would later use to try and purge the school of Muggle-borns.
</p><p>It is unknown how or when Gryffindor died, though he left an enduring legacy.
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Post-mortem">Post-mortem</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Godric_Gryffindor?veaction=edit&amp;section=4" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Post-mortem">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Godric_Gryffindor?action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Post-mortem"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>A portrait of Gryffindor hung at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Castle" title="Hogwarts Castle">Hogwarts Castle</a> guarded a secret short cut between the seventh-floor landing of the <a href="/wiki/Grand_Staircase" title="Grand Staircase">Grand Staircase</a> and the <a href="/wiki/Entrance_Hall" title="Entrance Hall">Entrance Hall</a> side room. The password required to gain access to said shortcut was <i>Starshine</i>.<sup id="cite_ref-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>
Gryffindor left two known relics: a <a href="/wiki/Goblin" title="Goblin">goblin</a>-made <a href="/wiki/Sword_of_Gryffindor" title="Sword of Gryffindor">sword</a>, adorned with rubies, and the <a href="/wiki/Sorting_Hat" title="Sorting Hat">Sorting Hat</a>. The two items share a particular bond; whenever a "true <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor" title="Gryffindor">Gryffindor</a>" needs it, the Sword will let itself be pulled out of the hat. Godric's sword was capable of acquiring powers from those it had slain and thus was imbued with venom from <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>'s defeat of the <a href="/wiki/Salazar_Slytherin%27s_Basilisk" class="mw-redirect" title="Salazar Slytherin&#39;s Basilisk">Basilisk</a>, making it suitable as a tool for destroying <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Voldemort</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Horcrux" title="Horcrux">Horcruxes</a>. <a href="/wiki/Albus_Dumbledore" title="Albus Dumbledore">Albus Dumbledore</a> used it to destroy <a href="/wiki/Marvolo_Gaunt" title="Marvolo Gaunt">Marvolo Gaunt</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Marvolo_Gaunt%27s_Ring" title="Marvolo Gaunt&#39;s Ring">Ring</a>, <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ron Weasley</a> used it to eliminate <a href="/wiki/Salazar_Slytherin" title="Salazar Slytherin">Salazar Slytherin</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Salazar_Slytherin%27s_Locket" class="mw-redirect" title="Salazar Slytherin&#39;s Locket">Locket</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Neville_Longbottom" title="Neville Longbottom">Neville Longbottom</a> used it to kill <a href="/wiki/Nagini" title="Nagini">Nagini</a>, the final <a href="/wiki/Horcrux" title="Horcrux">Horcrux</a>.</p><figure class="thumb tright show-info-icon" style="width: 142px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/8/8f/Godricg.gif/revision/latest?cb=20070806020645" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/8/8f/Godricg.gif/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/140?cb=20070806020645" decoding="async" width="140" height="266" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="Godricg.gif" data-image-key="Godricg.gif" /></a> 	 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:Godricg.gif" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption">Godric Gryffindor with his sword</p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Physical_appearance">Physical appearance</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Godric_Gryffindor?veaction=edit&amp;section=5" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Physical appearance">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Godric_Gryffindor?action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Physical appearance"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Godric Gryffindor's portrait depicts him as a tall, muscular man with a lion-like mane of wavy red hair and a beard to match. He had green eyes, peach-coloured skin and is often shown with his famous sword, with which he had great skill in wielding. 
</p><p>He has also been shown as wearing segmented red sword gauntlets adorned with gold fittings that are often depicted as being rather worn and burned-looking. He is also shown as wearing magnificent red robes with gold highlights and rather intricate designs on the sleeves. He is also frequently depicted with a rather powerful and stern gaze.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Personality_and_traits">Personality and traits</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Godric_Gryffindor?veaction=edit&amp;section=6" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Personality and traits">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Godric_Gryffindor?action=edit&amp;section=6" title="Edit section: Personality and traits"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Godric Gryffindor valued courage, determination, chivalry, and strength of heart, and was described as one of the four most brilliant witches and wizards of his time. He was also the most tolerant and accepting of <a href="/wiki/Muggle-born" title="Muggle-born">Muggle-borns</a> out of the Founders, a point which brought about the end of his friendship with <a href="/wiki/Salazar_Slytherin" title="Salazar Slytherin">Salazar Slytherin</a>. He was very forward thinking due to his creating the Sorting Hat to preserve the selection of the students after his time. This showcases his cleverness and creativity.
</p><p>He also had a versatile mindset as he chose to wield a sword as well as a wand, making him a rather dynamic man who deferred to the duelling style of his opponent, muggle and wizard alike. Going along with this, it shows that he was respectful and fair, choosing to wield a sword instead of magic against muggle combatants out of a sense of fair play as magic would be a most decidedly unbalanced weapon in a duel against a simple sword. In addition to showing his sense of fairness, his deference to the duelling style of opponent also shows a need to challenge himself, proving his worth and courage, which became one of the signature traits of his House at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts</a>. 
</p><p>His threat to unsheathe and use his famous blade against <a href="/wiki/Ragnuk" title="Ragnuk">Ragnuk the First</a> and his followers shows that despite his noble heart and good intentions, that Godric was very much capable of taking the lives of sapient beings such as <a href="/wiki/Goblin" title="Goblin">Goblins</a>. This would not have been an uncommon trait for a man of his era or personality due to the constant violence that pervaded medieval Europe and the acceptability of duelling to the death among both wizard and muggles alike.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Magical_abilities_and_skills">Magical abilities and skills</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Godric_Gryffindor?veaction=edit&amp;section=7" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Godric_Gryffindor?action=edit&amp;section=7" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li><b><a href="/wiki/Duelling" title="Duelling">Duelling</a></b>: Godric Gryffindor was said to be the greatest duellist of his time with considerable talent in <a href="/wiki/Martial_magic" title="Martial magic">martial magic</a>, and was also highly skilled in <a href="/wiki/Muggle_duelling" title="Muggle duelling">Muggle duelling</a>, using his sword in combat. His skill as a swordsman was such that he preferred to duel muggles with his sword instead of his magic, despite magic being the more decisive and powerful weapon.<sup id="cite_ref-PM-sword_9-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PM-sword-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Charm" title="Charm"><b>Charms</b></a>: Godric was shown to be highly proficient in charm-work, as he helped enchant the <a href="/wiki/Sorting_Hat" title="Sorting Hat">Sorting Hat</a> into a sentient <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magical</a> artefact with personality, to <a href="/wiki/Sorting_ceremony" title="Sorting ceremony">sort</a> students after his lifetime, with the help of the other founders. He was also capable of producing a non-corporeal <a href="/wiki/Patronus_Charm" title="Patronus Charm">Patronus</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-LEGOHP_5-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-LEGOHP-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> a testament to his superior magical abilities as a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a>, given the immense difficulty of conjuring a Patronus.</li></ul>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Possessions">Possessions</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Godric_Gryffindor?veaction=edit&amp;section=8" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Possessions">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Godric_Gryffindor?action=edit&amp;section=8" title="Edit section: Possessions"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li><b>Wand</b>: Gryffindor owned a wand made of an unknown wood, length, core, and flexibility.</li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Sword_of_Gryffindor" title="Sword of Gryffindor">Sword</a></b>: Gryffindor owned a <a href="/wiki/Goblin" title="Goblin">Goblin</a> made sword made from <a href="/wiki/Goblin-wrought_silver" title="Goblin-wrought silver">Goblin-wrought silver</a>. Gryffindor used this sword in Muggle duelling.</li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Sorting_Hat" title="Sorting Hat">Sorting Hat</a></b>: Gryffindor was said to have owned the Sorting Hat. He, alongside the other four founders, <a href="/wiki/Transfiguration" title="Transfiguration">transfigured</a> and <a href="/wiki/Charm" title="Charm">enchanted</a> the Sorting Hat into a sentient artefact to sort students into appropriate <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts</a> Houses after his lifetime.</li></ul>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Godric_Gryffindor?veaction=edit&amp;section=10" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Godric_Gryffindor?action=edit&amp;section=10" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>The name Godric is an Anglo-Saxon name in form, and can have several meanings, including, "he who rules with God" or "he who rules well".</li>
<li>His surname is a reference to the half-lion magical beast, the Griffin, a fierce, legendary beast with the body of a lion, and the head and wings of an eagle. The Hogwarts house named after him uses a lion as its symbol. Furthermore, the last part of his surname, "dor", is similar to both the French <i>d'or</i>, "made from gold", "golden", which is one of the colours of his Hogwarts House, or the suffix <i>dore</i>, which means "gift of," in which case the meaning of "Gryffindor" would be "gift of the Gryffin."</li>
<li>Gryffindor is also a pun on the golden griffin, which is a lion that only has eagle wings, so it has the head and front paws of a lion, and has golden fur and feathers. Even the French for Griffindor, Griffondor is a French homophone for the actual beast, Griffon d'Or.</li></ul>

<ul><li>Godric Gryffindor was named the <a href="/wiki/July" title="July">July</a> 2007 "Wizard of the Month" on Rowling's website. <li>J.K. Rowling once jokingly stated that if Godric Gryffindor was still alive: he would be the <a href="/wiki/Giant_Squid" title="Giant Squid">Giant Squid</a> that lives in Hogwarts lake, that would make him the biggest <a href="/wiki/Animagus" title="Animagus">Animagus</a> (and, quite possibly, the oldest) in the <a href="/wiki/Wizarding_world" title="Wizarding world">Wizarding world</a>. <li>On the official website of <a href="/wiki/The_Wizarding_World_of_Harry_Potter" title="The Wizarding World of Harry Potter">The Wizarding World of Harry Potter</a>, Godric mentions that he is proud to have <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> in his house and calls him "A remarkable young man".</li>
<li>Gryffindor's name and birthplace may allude to the Hardy Boys story "The Witchmaster's Key", which references the fictional village of Gruffinmoor.</li>
<li>The real-world Gryffindor family name is apparently of <a href="/wiki/Wales" title="Wales">Welsh</a> origin, although it spread to <a href="/wiki/Ireland" title="Ireland">Ireland</a> in the 12th century.<sup id="cite_ref-11" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-11">&#91;11&#93;</a></sup></li>
