<p><b>Williamson</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Great_Britain" title="Great Britain">British</a> <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> who worked as an <a href="/wiki/Auror" title="Auror">Auror</a> for the <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">British Ministry of Magic</a>. He was described as wearing scarlet red <a href="/wiki/Robes" title="Robes">robes</a> and a long pony tail when <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> first met him.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP36_1-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP36-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Williamson?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Williamson?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>He was the first Auror to see <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a> just before he fled the <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">Ministry of Magic</a> in <a href="/wiki/1996" title="1996">1996</a> after the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_the_Department_of_Mysteries" title="Battle of the Department of Mysteries">Battle of the Department of Mysteries</a>. Fudge then sent him and <a href="/wiki/John_Dawlish" title="John Dawlish">John Dawlish</a> to deal with the captured <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP36_1-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP36-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
