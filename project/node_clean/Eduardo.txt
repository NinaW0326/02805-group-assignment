<b>Eduardo</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a>. According to the <i><a href="/wiki/Daily_Prophet" title="Daily Prophet">Daily Prophet</a></i>, he was not a <a href="/wiki/Pure-blood" title="Pure-blood">pure-blood</a> wizard as he had always pretended; he was not even a pure <a href="/wiki/Human" title="Human">human</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> However, as this article was written by <a href="/wiki/Rita_Skeeter" title="Rita Skeeter">Rita Skeeter</a>, this information can be taken with a grain of salt.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Eduardo?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Eduardo?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>This individual was only mentioned on a <i><a href="/wiki/Daily_Prophet" title="Daily Prophet">Daily Prophet</a></i> prop in the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Goblet_of_Fire_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire (film)">film adaptation</a> of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Goblet_of_Fire" title="Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire">Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire</a></i>. The text on the prop appears to be a copy of the "DUMBLEDORE'S GIANT MISTAKE" article featured in the novel (chapter twenty-four), however, in the prop Rubeus Hagrid's name is replaced by "Eduardo", "he" and "him", and <a href="/wiki/Draco_Malfoy" title="Draco Malfoy">Draco Malfoy</a>'s is replaced by "<a href="/wiki/Mauricius" title="Mauricius">Mauricius</a>".</li>
<li>"Eduardo" is most likely a reference to <a href="/wiki/Eduardo_Lima" title="Eduardo Lima">Eduardo Lima</a>, graphic artist for the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_films" class="mw-redirect" title="Harry Potter films"><i>Harry Potter</i> films</a> since <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Prisoner_of_Azkaban_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban (film)">Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban</a></i>.</li></ul>
