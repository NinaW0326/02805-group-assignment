This <b>unidentified individual</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> who attended <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> in the <a href="/wiki/1990s" title="1990s">1990s</a>. He was also a member of the <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_orchestra" title="Hogwarts orchestra">Hogwarts orchestra</a> as a violist but was said to be clumsy. In <a href="/wiki/1994" title="1994">1994</a>, he was part of the orchestra during the <a href="/wiki/Yule_Ball" title="Yule Ball">Yule Ball</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-GOFF_2-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOFF-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
