Maxime's preferred mode of transportation was to travel with her students in a powder-blue <a href="/wiki/Beauxbatons_Carriage" title="Beauxbatons Carriage">carriage</a> drawn by twelve winged <a href="/wiki/Abraxan" title="Abraxan">Abraxan</a> horses, which were bred at Beauxbatons Academy.<sup id="cite_ref-GOF15_3-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF15-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-HBP30_7-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP30-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup> Madame Maxime was a half-giantess, although she claimed merely to have "big bones."<sup id="cite_ref-GOF23_1-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF23-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> This was a necessity, however, as the approach to <a href="/wiki/Giant" title="Giant">giants</a> (or part-giants) was generally hostile in the <a href="/wiki/Wizarding_world" title="Wizarding world">wizarding world</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-GOF28_8-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF28-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Olympe_Maxime?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Olympe_Maxime?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_life">Early life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Olympe_Maxime?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Early life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Olympe_Maxime?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Early life"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Olympe Maxime was born to one <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">magical parent</a> and one <a href="/wiki/Giant" title="Giant">giant</a>, making her a <a href="/wiki/Half-giant" title="Half-giant">half-giantess</a>. She kept this heritage hidden, out of fear of the prejudice that would strike her were it to be revealed.<sup id="cite_ref-GOF23_1-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF23-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> She was likely educated at Beauxbatons in <a href="/wiki/France" title="France">France</a>, where she later became <a href="/wiki/Headmaster" title="Headmaster">Headmistress</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-GOF15_3-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF15-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> It is unknown exactly when she became Headmistress of Beauxbatons.
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="1980s"><a href="/wiki/1980s" title="1980s">1980s</a></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Olympe_Maxime?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: 1980s">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Olympe_Maxime?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: 1980s"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>In the <a href="/wiki/1985%E2%80%931986_school_year" title="1985–1986 school year">1985–1986 school year</a>, she visited <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> along with Beauxbatons student <a href="/wiki/Aur%C3%A9lie_Dumont" title="Aurélie Dumont">Aurélie Dumont</a>. At Hogwarts, she met <a href="/wiki/Penny_Haywood" title="Penny Haywood">Penny Haywood</a> and <a href="/wiki/Jacob%27s_sibling" title="Jacob&#39;s sibling">Jacob's sibling</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-HM2B_9-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HM2B-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="1994-1995"><a href="/wiki/1994" title="1994">1994</a>-<a href="/wiki/1995" title="1995">1995</a></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Olympe_Maxime?veaction=edit&amp;section=4" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: 1994-1995">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Olympe_Maxime?action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: 1994-1995"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<div class="quote">
<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire"><b>Maxime</b>: "<i>My steeds require — er — forceful ’andling. Zey are very strong....</i>"</span></dd>
<dd><b>Dumbledore</b>: "<i>I assure you that Hagrid will be well up to the job.</i>"</dd>
<dd><b>Maxime</b>: "<i>Very well. Will you please inform zis ’Agrid zat ze ’orses drink only <a href="/wiki/Single_malt_whiskey" title="Single malt whiskey">single-malt whiskey</a>?</i>"</dd>
<dd>&#8212; Dumbledore and Maxime discussing the caring of her <a href="/wiki/Abraxan" title="Abraxan">Abraxan</a> horses at Hogwarts<sup class="noprint"><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Goblet_of_Fire" title="Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire">[src]</a></sup></dd></dl>
</div><p>
In August 1994, Madame Maxime attended the final of the <a href="/wiki/1994_Quidditch_World_Cup" title="1994 Quidditch World Cup">422nd Quidditch World Cup</a> in <a href="/wiki/England" title="England">England</a> with some of her students.<sup id="cite_ref-GOF8_10-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF8-10">&#91;10&#93;</a></sup> When the <a href="/wiki/Dark_Mark" title="Dark Mark">Dark Mark</a> was <a href="/wiki/Morsmordre" title="Morsmordre">conjured</a> by <a href="/wiki/Bartemius_Crouch_Junior" title="Bartemius Crouch Junior">Barty Crouch Jnr</a>, they got separated. While trying to find her, her students asked <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ronald Weasley</a> if he had seen her. Ron, who did not speak any French, did not understand the question and was therefore unable to help them.<sup id="cite_ref-GOF9_11-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF9-11">&#91;11&#93;</a></sup></p><figure class="thumb tleft show-info-icon" style="width: 252px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/9/9b/Yull_ball_dancing.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20160516021242" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/9/9b/Yull_ball_dancing.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/250?cb=20160516021242" decoding="async" width="250" height="104" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="Yull ball dancing.jpg" data-image-key="Yull_ball_dancing.jpg" /></a> 	 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:Yull_ball_dancing.jpg" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption">Maxime at the Yule Ball dancing with Hagrid</p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure>
<p>Maxime and some of her students went to <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> in October of <a href="/wiki/1994" title="1994">1994</a> for the <a href="/wiki/Triwizard_Tournament" title="Triwizard Tournament">Triwizard Tournament</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-GOF15_3-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF15-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> She made such an impact on <a href="/wiki/Professor" title="Professor">Professor</a> <a href="/wiki/Rubeus_Hagrid" title="Rubeus Hagrid">Rubeus Hagrid</a> that he immediately started trying to impress her. Since he was another half-giant like her, he thought she would understand his feelings. He tried to tame his hair, wore his best ill-fitting clothes, and even showed her the <a href="/wiki/Triwizard_Tournament" title="Triwizard Tournament">Triwizard Tournament</a> <a href="/wiki/Dragon" title="Dragon">dragons</a> while they were still meant to be a secret.<sup id="cite_ref-GOF19_12-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF19-12">&#91;12&#93;</a></sup> Given his lack of social graces, however, this was not so successful. It is unclear whether she was impressed by this, but either way, <a href="/wiki/Rubeus_Hagrid" title="Rubeus Hagrid">Hagrid</a> scuppered any chance he might have had at the <a href="/wiki/Yule_Ball" title="Yule Ball">Yule Ball</a>. It was here that he put it to Olympe that she was a <a href="/wiki/Half-giant" title="Half-giant">half-giant</a> just like him <i>—</i> he was right, of course, but she was not inclined to concede such a point.<sup id="cite_ref-GOF23_1-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF23-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> Because of this line of questioning she avoided his company. However, <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry</a> noticed that she did attempt to re-initiate contact with <a href="/wiki/Rubeus_Hagrid" title="Rubeus Hagrid">Hagrid</a>, while he dug in his garden. Apparently Hagrid did not warmly greet her and she left soon after.<sup id="cite_ref-GOF28_8-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF28-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup> However the two reconciled eventually.<sup id="cite_ref-GOF37_13-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF37-13">&#91;13&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<p>When Harry returned clutching the body of <a href="/wiki/Cedric_Diggory" title="Cedric Diggory">Cedric Diggory</a>, Olympe was one of the first to realise that he was dead and clutched her heart in shock,<sup id="cite_ref-GOF35_14-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF35-14">&#91;14&#93;</a></sup> after he had been <a href="/wiki/Killing_Curse" title="Killing Curse">murdered</a> by <a href="/wiki/Peter_Pettigrew" title="Peter Pettigrew">Peter Pettigrew</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Little_Hangleton_graveyard" title="Little Hangleton graveyard">Little Hangleton graveyard</a> on <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Voldemort's</a> orders, after him and Harry were transported there via the <a href="/wiki/Triwizard_Cup" title="Triwizard Cup">Triwizard Cup</a> (which had been <a href="/wiki/Portus" title="Portus">turned</a> into a <a href="/wiki/Portkey" title="Portkey">Portkey</a>). She separated from <a href="/wiki/Rubeus_Hagrid" title="Rubeus Hagrid">Hagrid</a> during the return journey, however, because <a href="/wiki/Rubeus_Hagrid" title="Rubeus Hagrid">he</a> would not abandon his half-brother <a href="/wiki/Grawp" title="Grawp">Grawp</a>, who proved to be a highly taxing travelling companion; she returned to Beauxbatons alone.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP20_2-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP20-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>

<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Later_life">Later life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Olympe_Maxime?veaction=edit&amp;section=6" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Later life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Olympe_Maxime?action=edit&amp;section=6" title="Edit section: Later life"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>It is unknown if she and Hagrid ever rekindled their relationship, although ultimately they never married.<sup id="cite_ref-Carnegie_16-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Carnegie-16">&#91;16&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>During the Calamity, Madame Maxime was known for helping members of the <a href="/wiki/Statute_of_Secrecy_Task_Force" title="Statute of Secrecy Task Force">Statute of Secrecy Task Force</a> who took a short sabbatical to Beauxbatons to study her transformative duelling style to increase the amount of damage they can inflict. She also appeared as a <a href="/wiki/Foundable" title="Foundable">Foundable</a> alongside Hagrid as they appeared together at the Yule Ball, being pelted by snowballs from a <a href="/wiki/Pixie" title="Pixie">Pixie</a> <a href="/wiki/Confoundable" title="Confoundable">Confoundable</a>. The Foundable of her and Hagrid could be overpowered and released with the <a href="/wiki/Knockback_Jinx" title="Knockback Jinx">Knockback Jinx</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-WU_17-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-WU-17">&#91;17&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<p>Madam Maxime was described to be a beautiful woman: an olive-skinned face with a beak-like nose and large, black, liquid-looking eyes. Her hair was drawn back in a shining knob at the base of her neck. She dressed from head to foot in black satin, and also wore <a href="/wiki/Opal" title="Opal">opal</a> jewellery.<sup id="cite_ref-GOF15_3-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF15-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> Despite her size, she was elegant and graceful, and Dumbledore once stated that she was a wonderful dancer.<sup id="cite_ref-GOF28_8-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF28-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Maxime's huge size was due to her <a href="/wiki/Half-giant" title="Half-giant">half-giant</a> background. Initially, she fiercely denied this, claiming that she merely had "big bones".<sup id="cite_ref-GOF23_1-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF23-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> Harry noted he "had only ever seen one person as large as this woman in his life, and that was Hagrid; he doubted whether there was an inch difference in their heights".<sup id="cite_ref-GOF15_3-10" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF15-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Magical_abilities_and_skills">Magical abilities and skills</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Olympe_Maxime?veaction=edit&amp;section=9" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Olympe_Maxime?action=edit&amp;section=9" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<div class="quote">
<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire"><b>Cornelius Fudge</b>: "<i>But did you say it was just past the Beauxbatons Carriage? Dumbledore, do you know what that woman </i>is<i>?</i>"</span></dd>

<p>As the Headmistress of a prominent European <a href="/wiki/Wizarding_school" title="Wizarding school">Wizarding school</a>, Madame Olympe Maxime was a highly competent and accomplished <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch</a> deserving of the title bestowed upon her.
</p>
<div class="quote">
<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix">"<i>Wouldn’ta done if Olympe hadn’ bin there. She pulled out her wand an’ did some o’ the fastes’ spellwork I’ve ever seen. Ruddy marvelous. Hit the two holdin’ me right in the eyes with Conjunctivitus Curses an’ they dropped me straightaway —</i>"</span></dd>
<dd>&#8212;Madame Maxime's talent for combative spell-casting<sup class="noprint"><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Order_of_the_Phoenix" title="Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix">[src]</a></sup></dd>
<dd></dd></dl></div>
<ul><li><b><a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">Magical</a> mastery</b>: Rising to the level of <a href="/wiki/Headmaster" title="Headmaster">Headmistress</a> at Beauxbatons Academy, despite the prejudice surrounding <a href="/wiki/Giant" title="Giant">Giants</a>, it is suffice to say that Madame Maxime was a very powerful and sophisticated <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch</a>, with her <a href="/wiki/Spell" title="Spell">spell</a>-work and prowess in <a href="/wiki/Martial_magic" title="Martial magic">martial magic</a> greatly impressing <a href="/wiki/Rubeus_Hagrid" title="Rubeus Hagrid">Rubeus Hagrid</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP20_2-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP20-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Martial_magic" title="Martial magic">Martial magic</a></b>: Though her skills as a <a href="/wiki/Duelling" title="Duelling">duellist</a> remains unknown, Maxime had proven herself to be highly proficient with combative magic, since <a href="/wiki/Rubeus_Hagrid" title="Rubeus Hagrid">Rubeus Hagrid</a> claimed that Madam Maxime performed an excellent <a href="/wiki/Conjunctivitis_Curse" title="Conjunctivitis Curse">Conjunctivitis Curse</a> during their mission to rescue him when he was ambushed by the giants under <a href="/wiki/Golgomath" title="Golgomath">Golgomath</a>'s command. He also said that she was able to do very fast spell-work.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP20_2-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP20-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Care_of_Magical_Creatures" title="Care of Magical Creatures">Care of Magical Creatures</a></b>: At Beauxbatons, Maxime bred <a href="/wiki/Abraxan" title="Abraxan">Abraxans</a> (<a href="/wiki/Winged_horse" title="Winged horse">Winged horses</a>), showing she was talented and experienced in the field of <a href="/wiki/Magizoology" title="Magizoology">Magizoology</a> and with the handling of <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magical</a> <a href="/wiki/Beast" title="Beast">beasts</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-GOF15_3-12" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF15-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Spell_resistance" title="Spell resistance">Spell resistance</a></b>: As a half-giant, Maxime had inherited the passive ability to resist many <a href="/wiki/Spell" title="Spell">spells</a>.</li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Nonverbal_spell" title="Nonverbal spell">Nonverbal magic</a></b>: Maxime was also proficient in nonverbal magic and <a href="/wiki/Conjuration" title="Conjuration">conjured</a> <a href="/wiki/Ribbon_conjuring_spell" title="Ribbon conjuring spell">ribbon</a> from the end of her <a href="/wiki/Olympe_Maxime%27s_wand" title="Olympe Maxime&#39;s wand">wand</a> without uttering an <a href="/wiki/Incantation" title="Incantation">incantation</a>. Her talent in nonverbal magic was testament to her superior <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magical</a> abilities as a witch, given the high level of difficulty required in the casting of nonverbal spells.<sup id="cite_ref-GOF20_6-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF20-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Transfiguration" title="Transfiguration">Transfiguration</a></b>: Maxime was capaple of performing <a href="/wiki/Conjuration" title="Conjuration">Conjuration</a>, a highly advanced and difficult branch of transfiguration, <a href="/wiki/Ribbon_conjuring_spell" title="Ribbon conjuring spell">conjuring a ribbon</a> to give <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> a score of eight out of ten after the first task of the Triwizard Tournament.<sup id="cite_ref-GOF20_6-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF20-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup></li></ul>

<p>During the <a href="/wiki/Triwizard_Tournament" title="Triwizard Tournament">Triwizard Tournament</a>, Hagrid began a <a href="/wiki/Love" title="Love">romantic</a> relationship with Madame Olympe Maxime, Headmistress of Beauxbatons Academy. They were often seen together, talking and walking around the grounds, and they danced together at the <a href="/wiki/Yule_Ball" title="Yule Ball">Yule Ball</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-GOF23_1-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF23-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> Though the two had a minor falling-out when Hagrid implied (correctly) that Maxime, like him, was part <a href="/wiki/Giant" title="Giant">giant</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-GOF23_1-10" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF23-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> they eventually reconciled and travelled together to appeal to the giants of the Northern <a href="/wiki/Giant_colony" title="Giant colony">colonies</a> and dissuade them from joining forces with <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Voldemort</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP20_2-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP20-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> Maxime was also seen comforting Hagrid during <a href="/wiki/Albus_Dumbledore" title="Albus Dumbledore">Albus Dumbledore</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Funeral_of_Albus_Dumbledore" title="Funeral of Albus Dumbledore">funeral</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP30_7-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP30-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup> The current status of their relationship is unknown, but as Hagrid never got married, it can be assumed that their relationship did not work out.<sup id="cite_ref-Carnegie_16-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Carnegie-16">&#91;16&#93;</a></sup> However, they likely remained good friends.
</p>

Referring to him as "Dumbly-dorr",<sup id="cite_ref-GOF15_3-13" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF15-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>  Maxime and Dumbledore later danced at the Yule Ball.<sup id="cite_ref-GOF23_1-11" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF23-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> After meeting with Dumbledore, she agreed to help with his request to journey to the giant colonies<sup id="cite_ref-GOF37_13-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF37-13">&#91;13&#93;</a></sup> and ultimately attended his funeral.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP30_7-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP30-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Olympe_Maxime?veaction=edit&amp;section=13" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Olympe_Maxime?action=edit&amp;section=13" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Olympe is the <a href="/wiki/French" title="French">French</a> word for <i>Olympus</i>, the mountain home of the Greek gods. <i>Maxime</i> is French for "principle" (or "maxim"), a pun on "principal". It is derived from the Latin <i>maxima</i>, the feminine form of "maximus", meaning "greatest" or "biggest".
</p>

<ul><li>"Madame" is French for "Mrs.", which may imply that at some point, Maxime has been married. However, "madame" is also used in French for someone older than you, above you, or who commands respect, such as a teacher, so her marital status remains unclear.</li>
<li>Olympe Maxime is portrayed by English actress <a href="/wiki/Frances_de_la_Tour" title="Frances de la Tour">Frances de la Tour</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_(film_series)" title="Harry Potter (film series)">film adaptations</a> of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Goblet_of_Fire_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire (film)">Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire</a></i> and <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Deathly_Hallows:_Part_1" title="Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 1">Deathly Hallows: Part 1</a></i>. British basketball player <a href="/wiki/Ian_Whyte" title="Ian Whyte">Ian Whyte</a> portrayed her for the full-body shots.
<ul><li>In <i>Deathly Hallows: Part 1</i>, she appears at the <a href="/wiki/Wedding_of_William_Weasley_and_Fleur_Delacour" title="Wedding of William Weasley and Fleur Delacour">wedding of William Weasley and Fleur Delacour</a>, making her the only major character to appear in a film despite not appearing in the corresponding book.</li></ul></li>
<li>In <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Goblet_of_Fire" title="Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire">Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire</a></i>, Maxime is described as roughly the same height as Hagrid, whereas in the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Goblet_of_Fire_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire (film)">film adaptation</a> she is considerably taller than him. <a href="/wiki/Robbie_Coltrane" title="Robbie Coltrane">Robbie Coltrane</a> explained in an interview that it was due to her high heels. Also, in the books she is described as having olive skin and black hair while in the films, she has pale skin and brown hair.</li>
<li>In <i><a href="/wiki/LEGO_Harry_Potter:_Years_1-4" title="LEGO Harry Potter: Years 1-4">LEGO Harry Potter: Years 1-4</a></i>, Olympe and Hagrid's relationship was testy, much as it was in the books and films.</li></ul>
