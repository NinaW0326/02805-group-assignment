<p><b>Jay</b> (b. <a href="/wiki/2000s" title="2000s">2000s</a>) was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> who attended <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> in the early <a href="/wiki/21st_century" title="21st century">21st century</a>.
</p><p>On <a href="/wiki/1_September" title="1 September">1 September</a>, <a href="/wiki/2017" title="2017">2017</a>, Jay boarded the <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Express" title="Hogwarts Express">Hogwarts Express</a> with a <a href="/wiki/Jay%27s_friend" title="Jay&#39;s friend">friend of his</a>. He was apparently late, and found it hard to take a seat.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
