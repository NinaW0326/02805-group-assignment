He released at least one album, <i><a href="/wiki/No_One_Looks_at_the_Ceiling" title="No One Looks at the Ceiling">No One Looks at the Ceiling</a></i>, which was available for sale at <a href="/wiki/Record_Shop" title="Record Shop">Record Shop</a> on <a href="/wiki/Charing_Cross_Road" title="Charing Cross Road">Charing Cross Road</a> in <a href="/wiki/London" title="London">London</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
