Her file was signed by Chief Registration Authority <a href="/wiki/F._L._Wakefield" title="F. L. Wakefield">F. L. Wakefield</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
