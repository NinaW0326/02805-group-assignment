<b>Ellerby</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard or witch</a> who worked along with <a href="/wiki/Able_Spudmore" title="Able Spudmore">Able Spudmore</a> and founded the <a href="/wiki/Black_Forest" title="Black Forest">Black Forest</a> Broomstick Company <a href="/wiki/Ellerby_and_Spudmore" title="Ellerby and Spudmore">Ellerby and Spudmore</a>. They released the <a href="/wiki/Tinderblast" title="Tinderblast">Tinderblast</a> in <a href="/wiki/1940" title="1940">1940</a> and then the <a href="/wiki/Swiftstick" title="Swiftstick">Swiftstick</a> in <a href="/wiki/1952" title="1952">1952</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
