<p>The <b>Horned Serpent</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magical</a> <a href="/wiki/Snake" title="Snake">snake</a> found in several regions of the world<sup id="cite_ref-FB2017_1-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-FB2017-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>, but was most common in <a href="/wiki/North_America" title="North America">North America</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-PM-Ilvermorny_2-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PM-Ilvermorny-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>

</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Description">Description</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Horned_Serpent?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Description">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Horned_Serpent?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Description"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<figure class="thumb tright show-info-icon" style="width: 102px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/c/cd/Horned_Serpent_Egg_WU.png/revision/latest?cb=20200722154514" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/c/cd/Horned_Serpent_Egg_WU.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/100?cb=20200722154514" decoding="async" width="100" height="123" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="Horned Serpent Egg WU.png" data-image-key="Horned_Serpent_Egg_WU.png" /></a> 	 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:Horned_Serpent_Egg_WU.png" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption">Horned Serpent Egg</p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure>
<figure class="thumb tright show-info-icon" style="width: 182px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/2/24/Horned_Serpent_WU.png/revision/latest?cb=20190701233131" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/2/24/Horned_Serpent_WU.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/180?cb=20190701233131" decoding="async" width="180" height="219" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="Horned Serpent WU.png" data-image-key="Horned_Serpent_WU.png" /></a> 	 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:Horned_Serpent_WU.png" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption">The Horned Serpent</p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure>
<p>There were several different species of Horned Serpents found world-wide; large specimens were found in the Far East, and the most diverse group still in existence was native to <a href="/wiki/North_America" title="North America">North America</a>. Horned Serpents inhabited bodies of <a href="/wiki/Water" title="Water">water</a> as their natural habitat.<sup id="cite_ref-FB2017_1-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-FB2017-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>&#160;
</p><p>Horned Serpents had a horned head, thus why they are named. Certain American species also sported a jewel in the forehead.<sup id="cite_ref-FB2017_1-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-FB2017-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>They were regarded as intelligent, with fierce eyes.<sup id="cite_ref-FB2017_1-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-FB2017-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>These serpents emitted a low, musical note to sound danger. Shavings from its <a href="/wiki/Horned_Serpent_horn" title="Horned Serpent horn">horn</a> can be used as <a href="/wiki/Wand_core" title="Wand core">wand cores</a>. Its jewel was said to grant powers of <a href="/wiki/Invisibility" title="Invisibility">invisibility</a> and <a href="/wiki/Flying" class="mw-redirect" title="Flying">flight</a>, thus making it the most sought after element of the species.<sup id="cite_ref-FB2017_1-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-FB2017-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="History">History</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Horned_Serpent?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: History">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Horned_Serpent?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: History"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Ancient bestiaries suggested Horned Serpents were once native to Western <a href="/wiki/Europe" title="Europe">Europe</a>, but were hunted to extinction by <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizards</a> in search of <a href="/wiki/Potion" title="Potion">potion</a> ingredients.<sup id="cite_ref-FB2017_1-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-FB2017-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Around <a href="/wiki/1620" class="mw-redirect" title="1620">1620</a>, the <a href="/wiki/Ireland" title="Ireland">Irish</a>-born <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch</a>&#160;<a href="/wiki/Isolt_Sayre" title="Isolt Sayre">Isolt Sayre</a> befriended a Horned Serpent near <a href="/wiki/Mount_Greylock" title="Mount Greylock">Mount Greylock</a> in present-day Massachusetts. She was able to understand the serpent, and when founding <a href="/wiki/Ilvermorny_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Ilvermorny School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Ilvermorny School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> she chose the <a href="/wiki/Horned_Serpent_(House)" title="Horned Serpent (House)">Horned Serpent</a> as the symbol of one of the school's four <a href="/wiki/Horned_Serpent_(House)" title="Horned Serpent (House)">houses</a>. Later, a long shard of horn from the same serpent was used by Isolt and her husband <a href="/wiki/James_Steward" title="James Steward">James Steward</a> to craft the first American-made wands, for their adopted sons <a href="/wiki/Chadwick_Boot" title="Chadwick Boot">Chadwick</a> and <a href="/wiki/Webster_Boot" title="Webster Boot">Webster Boot</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-PM-Ilvermorny_2-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PM-Ilvermorny-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-FB2017_1-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-FB2017-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Known_Horned_Serpents">Known Horned Serpents</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Horned_Serpent?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Known Horned Serpents">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Horned_Serpent?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Known Horned Serpents"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Isolt Sayre befriended a Horned Serpent when she went to a creek by <a href="/wiki/Mount_Greylock" title="Mount Greylock">Mount Greylock</a> in the <a href="/wiki/17th_century" title="17th century">17th century</a>. Isolt and the Horned Serpent could understand each other, which scared Isolt's companion, <a href="/wiki/William_(Pukwudgie)" title="William (Pukwudgie)">William the Pukwudgie</a>. The serpent kept telling her the same thing - "<i>Until</i> <i>I am part of your family, your family is doomed." </i> The cryptic message concerned Isolt because she had no family at the moment and she began to think she was imagining hearing the serpent say things.
</p><p>However, Isolt still visited the serpent over the years because of their strange, shared kinship, and when she and her eventual family were thinking of names for houses for <a href="/wiki/Ilvermorny_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Ilvermorny School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Ilvermorny School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> she named her house "<a href="/wiki/Horned_Serpent_(House)" title="Horned Serpent (House)">Horned Serpent</a>" in honour of it. Shortly after, Isolt received a dream the evening before <a href="/wiki/Chadwick_Boot" title="Chadwick Boot">Chadwick Boot</a>'s eleventh birthday. She had promised him a wand but she did not know what to use as a <a href="/wiki/Wand_core" title="Wand core">core</a>. She went to the creek in the dream where the Horned Serpent rose up out the water and let her take a shard of its <a href="/wiki/Horned_Serpent_horn" title="Horned Serpent horn">horn</a>. When she woke and went to the creek, she received a horn the same way as she had in her dream. She then made a wand with the core as a core set in <a href="/wiki/Prickly_ash" title="Prickly ash">prickly ash</a> <a href="/wiki/Wand_wood" title="Wand wood">wood</a>, subsequently repeating the process with Chadwick's brother <a href="/wiki/Webster_Boot" title="Webster Boot">Webster</a>'s wand core. This would prove the fulfilment of the serpent's prophecy, as these wands later warned Chadwick and Webster of the approach of <a href="/wiki/Gormlaith_Gaunt" title="Gormlaith Gaunt">Gormlaith Gaunt</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-PM-Ilvermorny_2-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PM-Ilvermorny-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Horned_Serpent?veaction=edit&amp;section=5" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Horned_Serpent?action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>The <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Horned_Serpent" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Horned Serpent">Horned Serpent</a> is a concept found in many Native American mythologies. Horned Serpents also being found in East Asia may be in reference to <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Chinese_dragon" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Chinese dragon">Chinese dragons</a> which are depicted differently than the European <a href="/wiki/Dragon" title="Dragon">dragons</a> that form the basis of the <a href="/wiki/Dragon" title="Dragon">dragons</a> of <i>Harry Potter</i>.</li>
<li>The Sioux Tribal Nation refer to the Horned Serpent as Unktehi. This creature was seen as the mortal enemy of the Wakinyan, the Sioux name for the Thunderbird, another house of Ilvermorny.</li></ul>
