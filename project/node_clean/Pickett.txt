<b>Pickett</b> was a male <a href="/wiki/Bowtruckle" title="Bowtruckle">Bowtruckle</a> that was kept by <a href="/wiki/Newton_Scamander" title="Newton Scamander">Newt Scamander</a> as a pet. Although he had at least six Bowtruckles<sup id="cite_ref-EW2016-08-12_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-EW2016-08-12-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> (Pickett, <a href="/wiki/Titus" title="Titus">Titus</a>, <a href="/wiki/Finn" title="Finn">Finn</a>, <a href="/wiki/Poppy" title="Poppy">Poppy</a>, <a href="/wiki/Marlow" title="Marlow">Marlow</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Tom_(Bowtruckle)" title="Tom (Bowtruckle)">Tom</a><sup id="cite_ref-bowtruckles_2-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-bowtruckles-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>), Newt was especially fond of Pickett, and even carried him around in his top pocket,<sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> first because Pickett had a cold and needed body warmth, and also because Pickett had attachment issues.<sup id="cite_ref-EW2016-08-12_3-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-EW2016-08-12-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Pickett?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Pickett?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>In <a href="/wiki/1926" title="1926">1926</a>, Pickett accompanied Newt on his journey to <a href="/wiki/New_York" title="New York">New York</a>, sitting inside his top pocket. He was present when the <a href="/wiki/Niffler" title="Niffler">Niffler</a> escaped at the <a href="/wiki/Steen_National_Bank" title="Steen National Bank">Steen National Bank</a> and when Newt was taken to the <a href="/wiki/Magical_Congress_of_the_United_States_of_America" title="Magical Congress of the United States of America">Magical Congress of the United States of America</a> by <a href="/wiki/Porpentina_Goldstein" title="Porpentina Goldstein">Tina Goldstein</a>.
</p><p>Pickett was still hidden in Newt's pocket when <a href="/wiki/Gellert_Grindelwald" title="Gellert Grindelwald">Gellert Grindelwald</a>, in the guise of <a href="/wiki/Percival_Graves" title="Percival Graves">Percival Graves</a>, wrongfully sentenced them to death.<sup id="cite_ref-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> As bowtruckles are able to <a href="/wiki/Lock_picking" title="Lock picking">pick locks</a>, Pickett was able to release Newt from his shackles and help Newt and Tina escape from the <a href="/wiki/Death_Cell" title="Death Cell">Death Cell</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-FB69_6-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-FB69-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Later, when Newt met the goblin gangster <a href="/wiki/Gnarlak" title="Gnarlak">Gnarlak</a>, Newt agreed to give Pickett to him as part of a deal to obtain information about the last missing beasts. Gnarlak betrayed them and turned them in to <a href="/wiki/Magical_Congress_of_the_United_States_of_America" title="Magical Congress of the United States of America">MACUSA</a>. In the resulting fray, Jacob punched Gnarlak out so Newt was able to rescue Pickett and escape.<sup id="cite_ref-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup> Pickett was not pleased with Newt for giving him away and expressed it by sulking and blowing raspberries.<sup id="cite_ref-FB92_8-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-FB92-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>In <a href="/wiki/1927" title="1927">1927</a> Pickett was present while Newt was waiting for an interrogation at the <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">British</a> <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic_Headquarters" title="British Ministry of Magic Headquarters">Ministry of Magic</a> regarding his international travel ban.<sup id="cite_ref-COGS_9-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COGS-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Pickett also accompanied Newt on his journey to <a href="/wiki/Paris" title="Paris">Paris</a>. When Newt and <a href="/wiki/Jacob_Kowalski" title="Jacob Kowalski">Jacob Kowalski</a> found Tina imprisoned by <a href="/wiki/Yusuf_Kama" title="Yusuf Kama">Yusuf Kama</a> in his hideout in the Paris' sewers, they were also imprisoned by him. When Yusuf fainted, due <a href="/wiki/Water_dragon" title="Water dragon">water dragon</a> <a href="/wiki/Water_dragon_parasite" title="Water dragon parasite">parasite</a> in his eye, Pickett opened the lock in the gate and helped get them out of the room.<sup id="cite_ref-COGS_9-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COGS-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Skills">Skills</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Pickett?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Skills">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Pickett?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Skills"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li><b><a href="/wiki/Lock_picking" title="Lock picking">Lock picking</a></b>: Pickett was named for his uncanny skill for picking locks, unlocking Newt's shackles in the Death Cell,<sup id="cite_ref-FB69_6-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-FB69-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup> and unlocking the gate in Yusuf Kama's hideout in the sewers.<sup id="cite_ref-COGS_9-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COGS-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup></li></ul>
