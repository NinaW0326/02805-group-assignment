<b>Syna Hyslop</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch</a> who co-invented the <a href="/wiki/Doubling_Charm" title="Doubling Charm">Doubling Charm</a> with her her twin sister <a href="/wiki/Helixa_Hyslop" title="Helixa Hyslop">Helixa</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-WBoS_1-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-WBoS-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Syna_Hyslop?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Syna_Hyslop?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Noted for their eccentricity, the twins preferred to keep to themselves, living alone together in a mansion their whole lives. After they died, their relatives found that their home contained duplicates of every item inside, down to the set of hand-written instructions for the Doubling Charm that each twin left on one of the matching tables in the kitchen.<sup id="cite_ref-WBoS_1-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-WBoS-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
