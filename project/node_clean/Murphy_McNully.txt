<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Murphy_McNully?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Murphy_McNully?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<figure class="thumb tright show-info-icon" style="width: 182px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/5/59/Murphy_McNully_promo.png/revision/latest?cb=20190911232805" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/5/59/Murphy_McNully_promo.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/180?cb=20190911232805" decoding="async" width="180" height="180" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="Murphy McNully promo.png" data-image-key="Murphy_McNully_promo.png" /></a> 	 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:Murphy_McNully_promo.png" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption">Murphy commentating during a game of Quidditch</p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure>
<p>When Murphy was seven years old, his mother taught him the <a href="/wiki/Blackboard_Writing_Spell" title="Blackboard Writing Spell">Blackboard Writing Spell</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-HMQ4_5-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HMQ4-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> Murphy had been subscribed to <i><a href="/wiki/Which_Broomstick%3F" title="Which Broomstick?">Which Broomstick?</a></i> ever since he could read.<sup id="cite_ref-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Murphy McNully was a student at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> in the <a href="/wiki/1980" title="1980">1980s</a>. He was physically disabled and immobile, and thus required the use of <a href="/wiki/Wheelchair" title="Wheelchair">Wheelchair</a> for locomotion. He was a <a href="/wiki/Quidditch" title="Quidditch">Quidditch</a> fanatic who would go so far as to commentate on Hogwarts' Quidditch friendlies, practise matches that required no commentator.<sup id="cite_ref-HM_4-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HM-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Personality">Personality</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Murphy_McNully?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Personality">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Murphy_McNully?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Personality"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Murphy loved Quidditch, and had always wanted to be an announcer. He also immensely enjoyed talking. Due to his using a wheelchair, when meeting new people, he tried to display every aspect of his personality so to make them remember him not as "the wizard in the wheelchair". Murphy had a very charming personality, and was a skilled strategist.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Magical_abilities_and_skills">Magical abilities and skills</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Murphy_McNully?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Murphy_McNully?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li><b><a href="/wiki/Charm" title="Charm">Charms</a></b>: Murphy was taught the <a href="/wiki/Blackboard_writing_spell" class="mw-redirect" title="Blackboard writing spell">Blackboard writing spell</a> by his <a href="/wiki/Murphy_McNully%27s_mother" title="Murphy McNully&#39;s mother">Murphy McNully's mother</a>, and was proficient with the casting of this enchantment himself.</li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Quidditch" title="Quidditch">Quidditch</a> Knowledge</b>: Murphy was knowledgeable about <a href="/wiki/Quidditch" title="Quidditch">Quidditch</a> and Quidditch strategies. He was an entertaining Quidditch commentator, and was also an avid user of statistics, at the time when the sports only had the basics.</li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Wizard%27s_Chess" title="Wizard&#39;s Chess">Wizard's Chess</a></b>: Murphy was quite skilled at wizard's chess, frequently beating Jacob's sibling at it.</li></ul>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Possessions">Possessions</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Murphy_McNully?veaction=edit&amp;section=4" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Possessions">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Murphy_McNully?action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Possessions"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li><b><a href="/wiki/Wand" title="Wand">Wand</a></b>: Murphy owned a wand, which was of an unknown length, <a href="/wiki/Wand_wood" title="Wand wood">wood</a> and <a href="/wiki/Wand_core" title="Wand core">core</a>, which he presumably purchased before his <a href="/wiki/First_year" title="First year">first year</a> at Hogwarts.</li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Wheelchair" title="Wheelchair">Wheelchair</a></b>: Being physically disabled and unable to walk, Murphy owned and operated a wheelchair for movement.</li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Kneazle" title="Kneazle">Kneazle</a></b>: Murphy owned a pet Kneazle named <a href="/wiki/Kneil" title="Kneil">Kneil</a>.</li></ul>
