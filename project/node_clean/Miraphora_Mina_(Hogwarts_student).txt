<b>Miraphora Mina</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch</a> and <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor" title="Gryffindor">Gryffindor</a> student who attended <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> in the <a href="/wiki/1990s" title="1990s">1990s</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-gobstones_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-gobstones-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> She signed up to join the <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Gobstone_Club" title="Hogwarts Gobstone Club">Hogwarts Gobstone Club</a> on a sign-up sheet that was posted on the <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor_Notice_Board" title="Gryffindor Notice Board">bulletin board</a> in <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor_Tower" title="Gryffindor Tower">Gryffindor Tower</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-gobstones_2-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-gobstones-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
