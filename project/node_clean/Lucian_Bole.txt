<b>Lucian Bole</b> (born c. <a href="/wiki/1977" title="1977">1977</a>) was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> and <a href="/wiki/Slytherin" title="Slytherin">Slytherin</a> student who attended <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts</a> from <a href="/wiki/1988" title="1988">1988</a> to <a href="/wiki/1995" title="1995">1995</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-ootp19_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-ootp19-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> He was a <a href="/wiki/Beater" title="Beater">Beater</a> on the <a href="/wiki/Slytherin_Quidditch_team" title="Slytherin Quidditch team">Slytherin Quidditch team</a> in the <a href="/wiki/1993%E2%80%931994_school_year" title="1993–1994 school year">1993–1994</a> and <a href="/wiki/1994%E2%80%931995_school_year" title="1994–1995 school year">1994–1995 school years</a>.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Lucian_Bole?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Lucian_Bole?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Lucian Bole started his education at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts</a> in <a href="/wiki/1988" title="1988">1988</a>, he was sorted into the house of <a href="/wiki/Slytherin" title="Slytherin">Slytherin</a> and became a member of the <a href="/wiki/Slytherin_Quidditch_team" title="Slytherin Quidditch team">Slytherin Quidditch team</a>, playing the role of beater.
</p><p>During the final match between <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor" title="Gryffindor">Gryffindor</a> and Slytherin in his sixth year, Bole clubbed Gryffindor <a href="/wiki/Chaser" title="Chaser">Chaser</a> <a href="/wiki/Alicia_Spinnet" title="Alicia Spinnet">Alicia Spinnet</a>, claiming he had mistaken her for a <a href="/wiki/Bludger" title="Bludger">Bludger</a>. In revenge, <a href="/wiki/George_Weasley" title="George Weasley">George Weasley</a> elbowed Bole in the face, leading <a href="/wiki/Rolanda_Hooch" title="Rolanda Hooch">Madam Hooch</a> to give both teams penalties.
</p><p>In Bole's seventh year, the <a href="/wiki/Triwizard_Tournament" title="Triwizard Tournament">Triwizard Tournament</a> lead all <a href="/wiki/Quidditch" title="Quidditch">Quidditch</a> games at Hogwarts to be cancelled, but he nonetheless nominally retained his position on the Slytherin team until his graduation in June 1995.<sup id="cite_ref-ootp19_2-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-ootp19-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> He and fellow Slytherin Beater, <a href="/wiki/Peregrine_Derrick" title="Peregrine Derrick">Peregrine Derrick</a>, were replaced by <a href="/wiki/Vincent_Crabbe" title="Vincent Crabbe">Vincent Crabbe</a> and <a href="/wiki/Gregory_Goyle" title="Gregory Goyle">Gregory Goyle</a> at the beginning of the next Quidditch season. <a href="/wiki/Angelina_Johnson" title="Angelina Johnson">Angelina Johnson</a>, the new captain of the <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor_Quidditch_team" title="Gryffindor Quidditch team">Gryffindor Quidditch team</a>, was glad to see Bole and Derrick gone, stating mockingly that she was always stunned the pair were able to find their way to Quidditch pitch without directions.
</p>
