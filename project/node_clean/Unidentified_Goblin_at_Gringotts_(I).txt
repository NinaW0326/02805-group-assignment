<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_Goblin_at_Gringotts_(I)?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_Goblin_at_Gringotts_(I)?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>On <a href="/wiki/31_July" title="31 July">31 July</a>, <a href="/wiki/1991" title="1991">1991</a>, this <a href="/wiki/Goblin" title="Goblin">goblin</a> was present at <a href="/wiki/Gringotts_Wizarding_Bank" title="Gringotts Wizarding Bank">Gringotts Wizarding Bank</a> when <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> came to pick up his money in <a href="/wiki/Vault_687" title="Vault 687">Vault 687</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-PSF_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PSF-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>The goblin continued working in <a href="/wiki/Gringotts_Wizarding_Bank" title="Gringotts Wizarding Bank">Gringotts</a> when <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a> took over the <a href="/wiki/Wizarding_world" title="Wizarding world">Wizarding world</a> and was present when <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>, <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ron Weasley</a> and <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione Granger</a> went to Gringotts to <a href="/wiki/Break-in_of_Gringotts_Wizarding_Bank_(1998)" title="Break-in of Gringotts Wizarding Bank (1998)">steal</a> <a href="/wiki/Helga_Hufflepuff%27s_Cup" class="mw-redirect" title="Helga Hufflepuff&#39;s Cup">Helga Hufflepuff's Cup</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Lestrange_Vault" title="Lestrange Vault">Lestrange Vault</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-DHF2_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DHF2-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
