<b>Stuart Hogg</b> (b. <a href="/wiki/24_June" title="24 June">24 June</a>, <a href="/wiki/1992" title="1992">1992</a>), a professional rugby union player who plays as a <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Rugby_union_positions#Full-back" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Rugby union positions">full-back</a> for the <a href="/wiki/Scottish_rugby_team" title="Scottish rugby team">Scottish rugby team</a> since <a href="/wiki/2012" title="2012">2012</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-Wikipedia_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Wikipedia-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> was rumoured to be a very talented <a href="/wiki/Squib" title="Squib">Squib</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-Pottermore_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Pottermore-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> He was, however, a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-wizard_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-wizard-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>In <a href="/wiki/2014" title="2014">2014</a>, it came under suspect among <a href="/wiki/Wizarding_world" title="Wizarding world">wizarding circles</a>, that Stuart Hogg, along with <a href="/wiki/Kelly_Brown" title="Kelly Brown">Kelly Brown</a> and <a href="/wiki/Jim_Hamilton" title="Jim Hamilton">Jim Hamilton</a>, were smuggled into the team by the <a href="/wiki/Wizarding_Supporters_of_Scottish_Rugby_Union" title="Wizarding Supporters of Scottish Rugby Union">WSSRU</a>, in violation of the <a href="/wiki/International_Statute_of_Wizarding_Secrecy" title="International Statute of Wizarding Secrecy">International Statute of Wizarding Secrecy</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-Pottermore_2-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Pottermore-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
