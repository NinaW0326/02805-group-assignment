<b>Yardley Platt</b> (<a href="/wiki/1446" class="mw-redirect" title="1446">1446</a> - <a href="/wiki/1557" class="mw-redirect" title="1557">1557</a>) was an infamous <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> who was known for being a serial <a href="/wiki/Goblin" title="Goblin">goblin</a> killer.<sup id="cite_ref-JKRS_1-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-JKRS-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> He was featured on a <a href="/wiki/Chocolate_Frog_Card" title="Chocolate Frog Card">Chocolate Frog Card</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-COSG_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COSG-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Yardley_Platt?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Yardley_Platt?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>On the <a href="/wiki/Chocolate_Frog_Card" title="Chocolate Frog Card">Chocolate Frog Cards</a> featured in the video games, Platt is portrayed as a blond wizard. At the <i><a href="/wiki/Wizard_of_the_Month" title="Wizard of the Month">Wizard of the Month</a></i> section at <a href="/wiki/J._K._Rowling%27s_official_site" title="J. K. Rowling&#39;s official site">J. K. Rowling's official site</a>, however, he is featured as a black-haired wizard.</li>
<li>The <a href="/wiki/File:Yardley_Platt-95-chocFrogCard.png" title="File:Yardley Platt-95-chocFrogCard.png">Chocolate Frog Card portrait of Platt</a> appears to be a direct homage to <a href="/wiki/File:Frank_gorshin_riddler1.jpg" title="File:Frank gorshin riddler1.jpg">a famous promotional photo</a> of Frank Gorshin as the <i>Batman</i> villain Edward Nygma ("the Riddler").</li></ul>
