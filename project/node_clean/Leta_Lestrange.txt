<p><b>Leta Lestrange</b><sup id="cite_ref-FB92_8-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-FB92-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup> (c. <a href="/wiki/1896" class="mw-redirect" title="1896">1896</a> – early September <a href="/wiki/1927" title="1927">1927</a>) was a <a href="/wiki/Great_Britain" title="Great Britain">British</a> <a href="/wiki/Pure-blood" title="Pure-blood">pure-blood</a> <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch</a><sup id="cite_ref-blood_3-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-blood-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> born into the <a href="/wiki/Lestrange_family" title="Lestrange family">Lestrange family</a>. From an early age, she was distraught over her unstable family lineage and unintentionally bringing about the <a href="/wiki/Death" title="Death">death</a> of an infant, <a href="/wiki/Corvus_Lestrange_(V)" title="Corvus Lestrange (V)">Corvus Lestrange</a>.
</p><p>She attended <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> between <a href="/wiki/1908" title="1908">1908</a> to <a href="/wiki/1915" title="1915">1915</a>, where she was sorted into <a href="/wiki/Slytherin" title="Slytherin">Slytherin</a>, and was often the target of bullying.<sup id="cite_ref-COG_6-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COG-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup> While at school, she developed a close relationship with <a href="/wiki/Newton_Scamander" title="Newton Scamander">Newton Scamander</a>, but the two parted ways when she caused trouble using a <a href="/wiki/Jarvey" title="Jarvey">Jarvey</a> and Newt took the blame, leading to his <a href="/wiki/Discipline_at_Hogwarts" title="Discipline at Hogwarts">expulsion</a>.
</p><p>In her adult life, Leta became determined to turn over a fresh leaf and live as a productive and appreciated member of the <a href="/wiki/Department_of_Magical_Law_Enforcement" title="Department of Magical Law Enforcement">Department of Magical Law Enforcement</a>. Leta had become the fiancée of Newt's older brother <a href="/wiki/Theseus_Scamander" title="Theseus Scamander">Theseus</a> by <a href="/wiki/1927" title="1927">1927</a>. In this year, she sacrificed herself for them during a <a href="/wiki/Gellert_Grindelwald%27s_Paris_rally" title="Gellert Grindelwald&#39;s Paris rally">rally</a> held in <a href="/wiki/Paris" title="Paris">Paris</a> by <a href="/wiki/Gellert_Grindelwald" title="Gellert Grindelwald">Gellert Grindelwald</a> by distracting him, incapacitating <a href="/wiki/Vinda_Rosier" title="Vinda Rosier">Vinda Rosier</a>, and destroying their <a href="/wiki/Gellert_Grindelwald%27s_skull-hookah" title="Gellert Grindelwald&#39;s skull-hookah">skull-hookah</a>. Grindelwald directed his previously conjured <a href="/wiki/Fire" title="Fire">fire</a> into her flesh, disintegrating her body. Despite this, Leta's demise inspired Newt to openly go to <a href="/wiki/Global_wizarding_war" title="Global wizarding war">war</a> against the terrorist that had ended her life.<sup id="cite_ref-COG_6-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COG-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Leta_Lestrange?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Leta_Lestrange?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_life">Early life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Leta_Lestrange?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Early life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Leta_Lestrange?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Early life"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Leta Lestrange was born at <a href="/wiki/Lestrange_Manor" title="Lestrange Manor">Lestrange Manor</a> in <a href="/wiki/1896" class="mw-redirect" title="1896">1896</a>, the only child of <a href="/wiki/Corvus_Lestrange_(IV)" title="Corvus Lestrange (IV)">Corvus Lestrange</a> IV from his rape of <a href="/wiki/Laurena_Kama" title="Laurena Kama">Laurena Kama</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-parents_4-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-parents-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> The <a href="/wiki/Lestrange_family" title="Lestrange family">Lestranges</a> were an old <a href="/wiki/Pure-blood" title="Pure-blood">pure-blood</a> family, deemed one of the "<a href="/wiki/Sacred_Twenty-Eight" title="Sacred Twenty-Eight">Sacred Twenty-Eight</a>" by <a href="/wiki/Cantankerus_Nott" title="Cantankerus Nott">Cantankerus Nott</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-pm-pureblood_9-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-pm-pureblood-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup> On her mother's side, Leta is descended from a powerful and wealthy pure-blood family of Senegalese origin.
</p><p>Corvus had coveted the already happily married Laurena and placed her under the <a href="/wiki/Imperius_Curse" title="Imperius Curse">Imperius Curse</a> to draw her away from her husband and son.
</p>
<figure class="thumb tleft show-info-icon" style="width: 222px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/e/ea/Leta_with_Corvus.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20190325141943" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/e/ea/Leta_with_Corvus.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/220?cb=20190325141943" decoding="async" width="220" height="91" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="Leta with Corvus.jpg" data-image-key="Leta_with_Corvus.jpg" /></a> 	 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:Leta_with_Corvus.jpg" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption">Leta with her brother Corvus on the ship</p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure>
<p>Soon after Leta's birth, her mother passed away, and Corvus <a href="/wiki/Marriage" title="Marriage">married</a> <a href="/wiki/Clarisse_Lestrange" title="Clarisse Lestrange">Clarisse Tremblay</a>. This union produced one child, a son, <a href="/wiki/Corvus_Lestrange_(V)" title="Corvus Lestrange (V)">Corvus</a> V.<sup id="cite_ref-parents_4-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-parents-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> Clarisse also died soon after the birth. On her mother's side Leta had an older half-brother named Yusuf, but it's unlikely that they had any contact with each other, due to the family feud.
</p><p>Upon learning that the <a href="/wiki/Kama_family" title="Kama family">Kama family</a> had sworn an <a href="/wiki/Unbreakable_Vow" title="Unbreakable Vow">Unbreakable Vow</a> of vengeance upon the one he loved most because of his abduction and rape of their matriarch, Corvus IV sent <a href="/wiki/Irma_Dugard" title="Irma Dugard">Irma Dugard</a>, his half-elf family servant, to take Leta and her infant half-brother to America. Bothered by his incessant crying, Leta switched him with <a href="/wiki/Credence_Barebone" title="Credence Barebone">another baby</a> in a nearby cabin. When the ship sank, one of the escape boats capsized and her half-brother ostensibly drowned.<sup id="cite_ref-10" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-10">&#91;10&#93;</a></sup> The baby taken by Leta ended up being raised by an <a href="/wiki/Mary_Lou_Barebone" title="Mary Lou Barebone">unrelated woman</a> in <a href="/wiki/New_York" title="New York">New York</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-FB88_11-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-FB88-11">&#91;11&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-FB96_12-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-FB96-12">&#91;12&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Hogwarts_years">Hogwarts years</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Leta_Lestrange?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Hogwarts years">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Leta_Lestrange?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Hogwarts years"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<div class="quote">
<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;Fantastic Beasts and Where to Find Them: The Original Screenplay"><b>Queenie Goldstein</b>: "<i>That was a real close friendship you had at school.</i>"</span></dd>
<dd><b>Newt Scamander</b>: "<i>Yes, well, neither of us really fitted in at school, so we —</i>"</dd>
<dd>&#8212; Newt and Leta's unusually close relationship<sup class="noprint"><a href="/wiki/Fantastic_Beasts_and_Where_to_Find_Them:_The_Original_Screenplay" title="Fantastic Beasts and Where to Find Them: The Original Screenplay">[src]</a></sup></dd></dl>
</div><p>Before beginning education at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> in <a href="/wiki/1908" title="1908">1908</a>, Leta purchased her <a href="/wiki/Leta_Lestrange%E2%80%99s_wand" title="Leta Lestrange’s wand">first wand</a>, likely from <a href="/wiki/Ollivanders" title="Ollivanders">Ollivanders</a>, and was sorted into <a href="/wiki/Slytherin" title="Slytherin">Slytherin</a> on <a href="/wiki/1_September" title="1 September">1 September</a>.
</p><p>Leta was bullied at Hogwarts due to the gossip about her being an unwanted child and the tragic death of her half-brother.
</p>

</p><p>Newt was once given a month's detention for being cheeky to <a href="/wiki/Prendergast" title="Prendergast">Prendergast</a>, so Leta set off a <a href="/wiki/Dungbomb" title="Dungbomb">Dungbomb</a> under the professor's desk to get the same punishment too.

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Personality_and_traits">Personality and traits</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Leta_Lestrange?veaction=edit&amp;section=8" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Personality and traits">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Leta_Lestrange?action=edit&amp;section=8" title="Edit section: Personality and traits"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<div class="quote">
<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;Fantastic Beasts and Where to Find Them: The Original Screenplay">"<i>She was a taker.</i>"</span></dd>
<dd>&#8212;Leta's self-serving nature<sup class="noprint"><a href="/wiki/Fantastic_Beasts_and_Where_to_Find_Them:_The_Original_Screenplay" title="Fantastic Beasts and Where to Find Them: The Original Screenplay">[src]</a></sup></dd>
<dd></dd></dl></div><figure class="thumb tleft show-info-icon" style="width: 302px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/0/05/Leta%27s_Boggart.gif/revision/latest?cb=20190325143448" class="image"><img alt="" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw%3D%3D" decoding="async" width="300" height="125" class="thumbimage lazyload" data-image-name="Leta&#39;s Boggart.gif" data-image-key="Leta&#39;s_Boggart.gif" data-src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/0/05/Leta%27s_Boggart.gif/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/300?cb=20190325143448" /></a> 	<noscript><a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/0/05/Leta%27s_Boggart.gif/revision/latest?cb=20190325143448" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/0/05/Leta%27s_Boggart.gif/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/300?cb=20190325143448" decoding="async" width="300" height="125" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="Leta&#39;s Boggart.gif" data-image-key="Leta&#39;s_Boggart.gif" data-src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/0/05/Leta%27s_Boggart.gif/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/300?cb=20190325143448" /></a></noscript> 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:Leta%27s_Boggart.gif" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption">Leta's greatest fear: reliving her brother's death</p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure>
<p>According to Queenie, Leta was a "taker" rather than a "giver",<sup id="cite_ref-FB92_8-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-FB92-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup> and she did display a selfish streak throughout her youth: Leta swapped her baby half-brother <a href="/wiki/Corvus_Lestrange_(V)" title="Corvus Lestrange (V)">Corvus</a> for another infant simply because she couldn't tolerate his crying; at Hogwarts, she allowed Newt to take the blame and be expelled for her own childish mistake. On the other hand, Leta suffered a great deal of tragedy throughout her life, and this had a marked effect on her personality: The death of her mother soon after Leta's birth and loveless upbringing at the hands of her father left Leta damaged and confused; the unintentional role she had played in her infant brother's death left her with feelings of incredible guilt and self-loathing (to the point that her <a href="/wiki/Boggart" title="Boggart">Boggart</a> was her infant brother's drowning). Leta regarded herself as not only guilty of killing her brother (rather than the unforeseen accident it was), but as evil and a monster. She also felt additional guilt over ruining her best friend's education. Despite all this, Leta maintained a polite and considerate personality. She remained friendly towards Newt, for whom she continued to have feelings, and later got engaged to Newt's older brother, <a href="/wiki/Theseus_Scamander" title="Theseus Scamander">Theseus Scamander</a>. Newt also continued to care for Leta, and even kept mementos of their time together.<sup id="cite_ref-bustle_18-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-bustle-18">&#91;18&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>In several ways, Leta's personality contradicted the "evil Slytherin" stereotype held by many students of the other Houses (<a href="/wiki/Gryffindor" title="Gryffindor">Gryffindors</a> in particular), and was in stark contrast to her father <a href="/wiki/Corvus_Lestrange_(IV)" title="Corvus Lestrange (IV)">Corvus</a>, as well as later Lestranges such as <a href="/wiki/Rodolphus_Lestrange" title="Rodolphus Lestrange">Rodolphus</a> and <a href="/wiki/Rabastan_Lestrange" title="Rabastan Lestrange">Rabastan</a>. For instance, Leta seemed to put no stock in <a href="/wiki/Pure-blood_supremacy" title="Pure-blood supremacy">pure-blood supremacy</a> beliefs, despite her family being one the the <a href="/wiki/Sacred_Twenty-Eight" title="Sacred Twenty-Eight">Sacred Twenty-Eight</a>. Much like <a href="/wiki/Horace_Slughorn" title="Horace Slughorn">Horace Slughorn</a> and <a href="/wiki/Andromeda_Tonks" title="Andromeda Tonks">Andromeda Tonks</a>, Leta could be said to represent the more "positive" aspects of <a href="/wiki/Slytherin" title="Slytherin">Slytherin</a>, in her case ambition and resourcefulness without any of the usual ruthlessness, elitism, or arrogance associated with members of the house. She also did not consider her Slytherin status as a marker of "superiority", as she quickly befriended and grew very close to the <a href="/wiki/Hufflepuff" title="Hufflepuff">Hufflepuff</a> <a href="/wiki/Newton_Scamander" title="Newton Scamander">Newt Scamander</a>, the two bonding over their shared status as outsiders. While she would sometimes hex other students, she did so out of anger in the face of bullying, rather than any unprovoked malice. She was quite liked by her <a href="/wiki/Defence_Against_the_Dark_Arts" title="Defence Against the Dark Arts">Defence Against the Dark Arts</a> professor <a href="/wiki/Albus_Dumbledore" title="Albus Dumbledore">Albus Dumbledore</a>, but struggled to get along with anyone at Hogwarts, except Newt.
</p><p>Leta ultimately showed courage and selflessness when she sacrificed her life to save the Scamander brothers from certain death, much like how her fellow Slytherin <a href="/wiki/Severus_Snape" title="Severus Snape">Severus Snape</a> would likewise sacrifice his life for a good cause many years later. After hearing her former teacher <a href="/wiki/Albus_Dumbledore" title="Albus Dumbledore">Albus Dumbledore</a> confessed his own guilt over not loving his late sister <a href="/wiki/Ariana_Dumbledore" title="Ariana Dumbledore">Ariana</a> enough, Leta was able to take his advice and confess to what she had done to Corvus, thereby saving <a href="/wiki/Credence_Barebone" title="Credence Barebone">Credence Barebone</a> from <a href="/wiki/Yusuf_Kama" title="Yusuf Kama">Yusuf Kama</a>'s revenge.
</p><p>Leta's selfless and heroic death would have a profound impact on Newt, giving him the resolve he needed to at last choose a side in the <a href="/wiki/Global_wizarding_war" title="Global wizarding war">Global wizarding war</a>, as well as inducing Dumbledore to honour Leta's sacrifice by more actively supporting the resistance against Grindelwald.
</p>

<ul><li><b><a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">Magical</a> mastery</b>: Leta Lestrange was a very powerful and talented witch, being recognised by <a href="/wiki/Albus_Dumbledore" title="Albus Dumbledore">Albus Dumbledore</a> himself as one of his best students at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts</a>, proving herself a very accomplished witch.</li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Defence_Against_the_Dark_Arts" title="Defence Against the Dark Arts">Defence Against the Dark Arts</a></b>: Leta was highly proficient in this field, given her ability to fight Grindelwald alongside <a href="/wiki/Newton_Scamander" title="Newton Scamander">Newt</a> and <a href="/wiki/Theseus_Scamander" title="Theseus Scamander">Theseus</a> for a time and saving the Scamander brothers from death at the hands of the fearsome Dark Wizard's black fire even if at the cost of her own life. <a href="/wiki/Albus_Dumbledore" title="Albus Dumbledore">Albus Dumbledore</a>, perhaps the greatest master of the field to ever live, considered Leta to be one of his best students in the subject. Indeed, Leta only had trouble repelling a <a href="/wiki/Boggart" title="Boggart">Boggart</a> due to her incredible guilt and self-loathing over the role she played in the death of her brother <a href="/wiki/Corvus_Lestrange_(V)" title="Corvus Lestrange (V)">Corvus</a>.</li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Martial_magic" title="Martial magic">Martial magic</a></b>: Leta was shown to have a natural proclivity with the casting of offensive spells since her early years at Hogwarts. In her <a href="/wiki/Third_year" title="Third year">third year</a> at Hogwarts, she successfully cast the <a href="/wiki/Dark_charm" title="Dark charm">Dark charm</a> <i><a href="/wiki/Oscausi" title="Oscausi">Oscausi</a></i> on a <a href="/wiki/Unidentified_Gryffindor_girl_2_(1910%27s)" class="mw-redirect" title="Unidentified Gryffindor girl 2 (1910&#39;s)">Gryffindor girl</a> who was speaking spitefully about her, vengefully sealing her mouth shut. In her adult life, she was highly capable in casting the <a href="/wiki/Stunning_Spell" title="Stunning Spell">Stunning Spell</a>; in <a href="/wiki/September" title="September">September</a>, <a href="/wiki/1927" title="1927">1927</a>, she used the spell in an attempt to stun some vicious <a href="/wiki/Matagot" title="Matagot">Matagots</a> in the <a href="/wiki/French_Ministry_of_Magic" title="French Ministry of Magic">French Ministry of Magic</a>, and then again to successfully stun <a href="/wiki/Vinda_Rosier" title="Vinda Rosier">Vinda Rosier</a> during <a href="/wiki/Gellert_Grindelwald%27s_Paris_rally" title="Gellert Grindelwald&#39;s Paris rally">Gellert Grindelwald's Paris rally</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Lestrange_Mausoleum" title="Lestrange Mausoleum">Lestrange Mausoleum</a>, sending her flying and smashing <a href="/wiki/Gellert_Grindelwald%27s_skull-hookah" title="Gellert Grindelwald&#39;s skull-hookah">Gellert Grindelwald's skull-hookah</a>. She was even able to <a href="/wiki/Duelling" title="Duelling">duel</a> against <a href="/wiki/Gellert_Grindelwald" title="Gellert Grindelwald">Gellert Grindelwald</a> himself, an extremely dangerous <a href="/wiki/Dark_Wizard" title="Dark Wizard">Dark Wizard</a> (before he killed her).</li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Charm" title="Charm">Charms</a></b>: Leta was shown to be accomplished with charm-work. She effortlessly located the <a href="/wiki/Lestrange_family" title="Lestrange family">Lestrange family</a> records in the <a href="/wiki/French_Ministry_of_Magic_Records_Room" title="French Ministry of Magic Records Room">French Ministry of Magic Records Room</a> using <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magic</a>, along with revolving the cabinets with the <i><a href="/wiki/Circumrota" title="Circumrota">Circumrota</a></i> charm to reveal <a href="/wiki/Newton_Scamander" title="Newton Scamander">Newt Scamander</a> and <a href="/wiki/Porpentina_Goldstein" title="Porpentina Goldstein">Tina Goldstein</a> followed with the <i><a href="/wiki/Reverte" title="Reverte">Reverte</a></i> charm to return them to their original position. She also successfully used the <a href="/wiki/Summoning_Charm" title="Summoning Charm">Summoning Charm</a> to summon the <a href="/wiki/Lestrange_Family_Tree_(Box)" title="Lestrange Family Tree (Box)">Lestrange family tree box</a> to her in the <a href="/wiki/Lestrange_Vault" title="Lestrange Vault">Lestrange Vault</a>. In addition, she was proficient with the casting of the <a href="/wiki/Stunning_Spell" title="Stunning Spell">Stunning Spell</a>.</li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Apparition" title="Apparition">Apparition</a></b>: Being an accomplished adult witch, Leta was able to Apparate at will, as shown when she was able to Apparate to <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a>, along with <a href="/wiki/Torquil_Travers" title="Torquil Travers">Torquil Travers</a>, <a href="/wiki/Theseus_Scamander" title="Theseus Scamander">Theseus Scamander</a>, and some other <a href="/wiki/Auror" title="Auror">Aurors</a>.</li>
<li><b>Indomitable willpower</b>: Leta's <a href="/wiki/Slytherin" title="Slytherin">Slytherin</a> ambition granted her an immense willpower, with her thus managing to rise up high through the <a href="/wiki/Department_of_Magical_Law_Enforcement" title="Department of Magical Law Enforcement">Department of Magical Law Enforcement</a> despite once failing to fit in at Hogwarts as a student, and with her determination to not be the freak that she was perceived to be by other students, and her own father. At the end of her life, Leta demonstrated her incorruptible spirit and morals by resisting <a href="/wiki/Gellert_Grindelwald" title="Gellert Grindelwald">Grindelwald</a>'s persuasive speech, and willingly sacrificing her own life to save her beloved friend <a href="/wiki/Newton_Scamander" title="Newton Scamander">Newt</a> and fiance <a href="/wiki/Theseus_Scamander" title="Theseus Scamander">Theseus</a> from the lethal <a href="/wiki/Protego_Diabolica" title="Protego Diabolica">black fire</a>. Indeed, it was this incorruptible willpower demonstrated in Leta's final moments that induced Newt, as well as her half-brother <a href="/wiki/Yusuf_Kama" title="Yusuf Kama">Yusuf Kama</a>, to finally join the <a href="/wiki/Global_wizarding_war" title="Global wizarding war">Global wizarding war</a> against Grindelwald.</li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Nonverbal_spell" title="Nonverbal spell">Non-verbal magic</a></b>: Leta was able to cast a non-verbal <a href="/wiki/Stunning_Spell" title="Stunning Spell">Stunning Spell</a> at <a href="/wiki/Vinda_Rosier" title="Vinda Rosier">Vinda Rosier</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Lestrange_Mausoleum" title="Lestrange Mausoleum">Lestrange Mausoleum</a>, which is highly advanced, <a href="/wiki/Nastily_Exhausting_Wizarding_Test" title="Nastily Exhausting Wizarding Test">N.E.W.T.</a>-level <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magic</a>.</li></ul>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Leta_Lestrange?veaction=edit&amp;section=10" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Leta_Lestrange?action=edit&amp;section=10" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>Leta most likely originates from the name Leda, daughter of Aetolian king Thestius, an Aetolian princess who became a Spartan queen in Greek mythology, of which the story of Leda and the Swan is the focus.
<ul><li>In the story, Zeus takes the form of a swan and rapes her. Their consummation, on the same night as Leda lay with her husband Tyndareus, resulted in two eggs from which hatched Helen (aka "Helen of Troy", said to be the most beautiful woman in the world and an instigator of the Trojan War), Clytemnestra, and Castor and Pollux (who became the constellation Gemini).</li></ul></li></ul>
<ul><li>The name Lita is of Latin origin and means "gladly."</li>
<li>The word <i>leta</i> is a variation of the Latin word <i>leto</i>, which means "to kill" or "to slay".</li></ul>
