<p><b>Trevor</b> was <a href="/wiki/Neville_Longbottom" title="Neville Longbottom">Neville Longbottom</a>’s pet <a href="/wiki/Toad" title="Toad">toad</a>, whom he frequently lost during his first year at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts</a>. Trevor was a gift from Neville’s Great Uncle <a href="/wiki/Algie_Longbottom" title="Algie Longbottom">Algie</a> in recognition of the first time Neville showed <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magical</a> ability and thus gaining admission to Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry.<sup id="cite_ref-ps7_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-ps7-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> Eventually, Trevor wandered off and joined the other amphibians in and around the <a href="/wiki/Great_Lake" title="Great Lake">Black Lake</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-PM_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PM-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_life">Early life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Trevor?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Early life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Trevor?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Early life"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<figure class="thumb tleft show-info-icon" style="width: 182px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/f/f8/Family_Longbottom.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20180904190911" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/f/f8/Family_Longbottom.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/180?cb=20180904190911" decoding="async" width="180" height="236" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="Family Longbottom.jpg" data-image-key="Family_Longbottom.jpg" /></a> 	 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:Family_Longbottom.jpg" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption">Neville with his grandmother and Trevor</p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure>
<p><a href="/wiki/Neville_Longbottom" title="Neville Longbottom">Neville's</a> great uncle <a href="/wiki/Algie_Longbottom" title="Algie Longbottom">Algie</a> used to own Trevor until he gave him to Neville as a gift from him for receiving permission to go to <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-ps7_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-ps7-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="1990s">1990s</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Trevor?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: 1990s">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Trevor?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: 1990s"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p><a href="/wiki/Neville_Longbottom" title="Neville Longbottom">Neville</a> lost Trevor many times. For example, the first time he came to Hogwarts on the <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Express" title="Hogwarts Express">Hogwarts Express</a>, Neville lost Trevor but was soon helped by <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione Granger</a> who met <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> and <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ron Weasley</a> during her search.<sup id="cite_ref-ps6_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-ps6-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Neville eventually found Trevor once reaching Hogwarts in one of the boats after Hagrid asked Neville about Trevor.
</p><p><a href="/wiki/Filius_Flitwick" title="Filius Flitwick">Professor Flitwick</a> once made Trevor fly around his <a href="/wiki/Charms_(class)" title="Charms (class)">Charms</a> classroom to skilfully demonstrate the <a href="/wiki/Levitation_Charm" title="Levitation Charm">Levitation Charm</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-ps10_4-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-ps10-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>During the night Harry, Ron and Hermione went to penetrate the <a href="/wiki/Underground_Chambers" title="Underground Chambers">Underground Chambers</a>, the trio left the common room but was confronted by Neville and a croaking Trevor until Hermione froze Neville using the <a href="/wiki/Full_Body-Bind_Curse" title="Full Body-Bind Curse">Full Body-Bind Curse</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-PS16_5-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PS16-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<figure class="thumb tright show-info-icon" style="width: 182px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/1/1d/Neville_poa.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20050817015249" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/1/1d/Neville_poa.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/180?cb=20050817015249" decoding="async" width="180" height="245" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="Neville poa.jpg" data-image-key="Neville_poa.jpg" /></a> 	 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:Neville_poa.jpg" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption">Neville Longbottom and Trevor in 1993</p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure>
<p>In Neville's <a href="/wiki/1993%E2%80%931994_school_year" title="1993–1994 school year">third year</a>, Trevor was part of the <a href="/wiki/Frog_Choir" title="Frog Choir">Frog Choir</a> when they performed <i><a href="/wiki/Double_Trouble" title="Double Trouble">Double Trouble</a></i> on <a href="/wiki/1_September" title="1 September">1 September</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-POAF_6-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-POAF-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Later on the school year, <a href="/wiki/Severus_Snape" title="Severus Snape">Professor Snape</a> made Neville test his <a href="/wiki/Shrinking_Solution" title="Shrinking Solution">Shrinking Solution</a> on the toad with the warning that if made incorrectly, it would likely be <a href="/wiki/Poison" title="Poison">poisonous</a>. After Trevor drank the <a href="/wiki/Potion" title="Potion">potion</a>, he successfully transformed into a tadpole, much to Neville's delight. However, Professor Snape was displeased and spitefully deducted five points from <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor" title="Gryffindor">Gryffindor</a> because Hermione had helped Neville create the <a href="/wiki/Potion" title="Potion">potion</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-pa7_7-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-pa7-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Harry used Trevor to practise the <a href="/wiki/Summoning_Charm" title="Summoning Charm">Summoning Charm</a> in his and Neville's <a href="/wiki/Fourth_year" title="Fourth year">fourth year</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-gof20_8-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-gof20-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<figure class="thumb tright show-info-icon" style="width: 182px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/5/5e/Stinksap.png/revision/latest?cb=20170210033114" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/5/5e/Stinksap.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/180?cb=20170210033114" decoding="async" width="180" height="126" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="Stinksap.png" data-image-key="Stinksap.png" /></a> 	 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:Stinksap.png" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption">Trevor, Harry, Neville, Ginny and Luna covered in Stinksap</p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure> 
<p>When Harry encountered Neville in the last carriage of the Hogwarts Express in their <a href="/wiki/Fifth_year" title="Fifth year">fifth year</a>, Neville had a one-handed grip on a struggling Trevor. Later, Neville dumped the toad into Harry's lap so that he could demonstrate the <a href="/wiki/Stinksap" title="Stinksap">defensive mechanism</a> of his <a href="/wiki/Mimbulus_mimbletonia" title="Mimbulus mimbletonia">Mimbulus Mimbletonia</a>. This was to Harry's regret when <a href="/wiki/Cho_Chang" title="Cho Chang">Cho Chang</a> then visited the compartment, as "he would not have chosen to be sitting with Neville and <a href="/wiki/Luna_Lovegood" title="Luna Lovegood">Luna Lovegood</a>, clutching a toad and dripping in Stinksap."<sup id="cite_ref-op10_9-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-op10-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>In <a href="/wiki/1996" title="1996">1996</a>, Trevor made a bid for freedom on the Hogwarts Express, but Neville caught him again.<sup id="cite_ref-hbp7_10-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-hbp7-10">&#91;10&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<ul><li>The species of toad used for Trevor in <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Philosopher%27s_Stone_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Philosopher&#39;s Stone (film)">Harry Potter and the Philosopher's Stone (film)</a></i> and <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Prisoner_of_Azkaban_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban (film)">Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban (film)</a></i> is a smooth sided toad.</li>
<li>Trevor also appears briefly in <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Chamber_of_Secrets_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets (film)">Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets (film)</a></i> in front of Neville after <a href="/wiki/Professor" title="Professor">Professor</a> <a href="/wiki/Minerva_McGonagall" title="Minerva McGonagall">McGonnagall</a> demonstrates a transfiguration spell on her pet hornbill.</li>

<li>A fan-proposed theory stated Trevor was the Animagus form of Neville's Uncle Algie, but this was said to be false by <a href="/wiki/J._K._Rowling" title="J. K. Rowling">J. K. Rowling</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-11" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-11">&#91;11&#93;</a></sup></li>

<li>In the scene when the trio leaves the common room to save the Philosopher's Stone, Trevor is mistakenly referred to as a female in the German version. All other times he is mentioned, including all other books, he is male.
<ul><li>In the Polish version, Trevor was referred to as a female too.</li></ul></li>
<li>In the initial PC release of <i>Harry Potter and the Philosopher's Stone</i>, when Harry has a broomstick battle with Malfoy to retrieve Neville's <a href="/wiki/Remembrall" title="Remembrall">Remembrall</a>, Neville, upon Harry's retrieval of the Remembrall, learns that Trevor is in the <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_greenhouses" title="Hogwarts greenhouses">Herbology greenhouse</a> and runs to find him, due to the greenhouses containing carnivorous plants.</li>
<li>In Harry Potter and the Philosopher's Stone, Neville finds Trevor in one of the boats (with a little help from <a href="/wiki/Rubeus_Hagrid" title="Rubeus Hagrid">Hagrid</a>). In <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Philosopher%27s_Stone_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Philosopher&#39;s Stone (film)">Harry Potter and the Philosopher's Stone (film)</a></i>, Neville finds Trevor on the top stair leading to one of the hallways to the Great Hall while <a href="/wiki/Minerva_McGonagall" title="Minerva McGonagall">Minerva McGonagall</a> is discussing the Sorting Ceremony with the first years.</li></ul>
