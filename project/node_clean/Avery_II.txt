<p><b>Avery II</b><sup id="cite_ref-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> was a <a href="/wiki/Dark_wizard" class="mw-redirect" title="Dark wizard">Dark wizard</a>, possibly the son of <a href="/wiki/Avery_(Riddle-era)" class="mw-redirect" title="Avery (Riddle-era)">Avery</a>, and a <a href="/wiki/Slytherin" title="Slytherin">Slytherin</a> student at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a>. After graduating from Hogwarts, he became a <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eater</a>.
</p><p>Avery participated in the <a href="/wiki/First_Wizarding_War" title="First Wizarding War">First Wizarding War</a>, but like most Death Eaters, didn't search for <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a> after his downfall, although he was forgiven after his master's return. During the <a href="/wiki/Second_Wizarding_War" title="Second Wizarding War">Second Wizarding War</a>, Avery fought at the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_the_Department_of_Mysteries" title="Battle of the Department of Mysteries">Battle of the Department of Mysteries</a> with a group of Death Eaters, attempting to take a <a href="/wiki/Sybill_Trelawney%27s_first_prophecy" title="Sybill Trelawney&#39;s first prophecy">prophecy</a> from <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP35_6-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP35-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Avery_II?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Avery_II?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Hogwarts_years">Hogwarts years</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Avery_II?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Hogwarts years">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Avery_II?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Hogwarts years"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Avery attended <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> in the <a href="/wiki/1970s" title="1970s">1970s</a> and was sorted into <a href="/wiki/Slytherin" title="Slytherin">Slytherin</a> <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Houses" title="Hogwarts Houses">House</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-GOF27_7-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF27-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup> He and his friend <a href="/wiki/Mulciber_(Marauder-era)" class="mw-redirect" title="Mulciber (Marauder-era)">Mulciber</a> had a dark sense of humour, such as when Mulciber tried to use <a href="/wiki/Dark_Arts" title="Dark Arts">Dark Magic</a> on <a href="/wiki/Mary_Macdonald" title="Mary Macdonald">Mary Macdonald</a> during the <a href="/wiki/1975" title="1975">1975</a>-<a href="/wiki/1976" title="1976">1976</a> school year.<sup id="cite_ref-DH33_4-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH33-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Avery was also a friend of <a href="/wiki/Severus_Snape" title="Severus Snape">Severus Snape</a>, something that bothered <a href="/wiki/Lily_J._Potter" title="Lily J. Potter">Lily Evans</a>, who was Snape's best friend at the time. She found Avery's sense of humour to be very sadistic and could not understand how Snape could associate with him.<sup id="cite_ref-DH33_4-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH33-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> According to <a href="/wiki/Sirius_Black" title="Sirius Black">Sirius Black</a>, Avery was part of a <a href="/wiki/Gang_of_Slytherins" title="Gang of Slytherins">"gang of Slytherins"</a> who later become <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a> and also included <a href="/wiki/Bellatrix_Lestrange" title="Bellatrix Lestrange">Bellatrix Black</a>, her future husband <a href="/wiki/Rodolphus_Lestrange" title="Rodolphus Lestrange">Rodolphus Lestrange</a>, <a href="/wiki/Evan_Rosier" title="Evan Rosier">Evan Rosier</a>, Snape, and <a href="/wiki/Wilkes" title="Wilkes">Wilkes</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-GOF27_7-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF27-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="First_Wizarding_War">First Wizarding War</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Avery_II?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: First Wizarding War">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Avery_II?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: First Wizarding War"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Sometime during the <a href="/wiki/First_Wizarding_War" title="First Wizarding War">First Wizarding War</a>, Avery was apprehended by <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">Ministry of Magic</a> officials and stood trial for being a <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eater</a>. Avery was one of few who "wormed his way out of trouble", according to <a href="/wiki/Sirius_Black" title="Sirius Black">Sirius Black</a>, since he pleaded the <a href="/wiki/Imperius_Curse" title="Imperius Curse">Imperius Curse</a>. He was not sent to <a href="/wiki/Azkaban" title="Azkaban">Azkaban</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-GOF27_7-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF27-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup> He was also one of the Death Eaters who didn't search for his master after his downfall.<sup id="cite_ref-GOF33_8-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF33-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Second_Wizarding_War">Second Wizarding War</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Avery_II?veaction=edit&amp;section=4" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Second Wizarding War">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Avery_II?action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Second Wizarding War"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Return_of_the_Dark_Lord">Return of the Dark Lord</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Avery_II?veaction=edit&amp;section=5" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Return of the Dark Lord">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Avery_II?action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Return of the Dark Lord"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<div class="quote">
<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire"><b>Avery</b>: "<i>Master! Master, forgive me! Forgive us all!</i>"</span></dd>
<dd><b>Voldemort</b>: "<i>Crucio!</i>"</dd>
<dd>&#8212; Voldemort responding to Avery's pleas for forgiveness<sup class="noprint"><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Goblet_of_Fire" title="Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire">[src]</a></sup></dd></dl>
</div>
<p>Avery was the first <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eater</a> to crack and grovel at <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a>'s feet during his rebirth at the cemetery, saying that he did not look for Voldemort after his downfall because he believed him to be "finished". He feebly begged Voldemort to forgive all the Death Eaters present for their past disloyalty. Voldemort responded by using the <a href="/wiki/Cruciatus_Curse" title="Cruciatus Curse">Cruciatus Curse</a> to sadistically torture him and saying he wanted thirteen years repayment before forgiving the Death Eaters for the lack of belief they had in him.<sup id="cite_ref-GOF33_8-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF33-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="The_prophecy">The prophecy</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Avery_II?veaction=edit&amp;section=6" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: The prophecy">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Avery_II?action=edit&amp;section=6" title="Edit section: The prophecy"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<div class="quote">
<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix">"<i>Well . . . Avery isn’t. . . . He’s in trouble.. . . He gave him the wrong information. . . . He’s really angry. . . .</i>"</span></dd>
<dd>&#8212;Harry Potter receiving a vision of Voldemort's activities in his sleep<sup class="noprint"><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Order_of_the_Phoenix" title="Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix">[src]</a></sup></dd>
<dd></dd></dl></div><p>In <a href="/wiki/1995" title="1995">1995</a>, Voldemort was seeking a <a href="/wiki/Sybill_Trelawney%27s_first_prophecy" title="Sybill Trelawney&#39;s first prophecy">prophecy</a> that had been made about himself and <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>, a record of which was kept in the <a href="/wiki/Department_of_Mysteries" title="Department of Mysteries">Department of Mysteries</a> in the <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">Ministry of Magic</a>. Avery told his master that it would be possible to obtain the prophecy by placing <a href="/wiki/Unspeakable" title="Unspeakable">Unspeakable</a> <a href="/wiki/Broderick_Bode" title="Broderick Bode">Broderick Bode</a> under the <a href="/wiki/Imperius_Curse" title="Imperius Curse">Imperius Curse</a> and having him get it for them. This attempt failed, as prophecies are protected by spells that only allow them to be retrieved by the person about whom they are made. Voldemort punished Avery for this mistake.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP26_9-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP26-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup>
</p><h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Battle_of_the_Department_of_Mysteries">Battle of the Department of Mysteries</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Avery_II?veaction=edit&amp;section=7" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Battle of the Department of Mysteries">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Avery_II?action=edit&amp;section=7" title="Edit section: Battle of the Department of Mysteries"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<div class="quote">
<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix">"<i>We'll split into pairs and search, and don't forget, be gentle with Potter until we've got the prophecy, you can kill the others if necessary...</i>"</span></dd>
<dd>&#8212;<a href="/wiki/Lucius_Malfoy" title="Lucius Malfoy">Lucius Malfoy</a> gives orders to the group of Death Eaters<sup class="noprint"><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Order_of_the_Phoenix" title="Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix">[src]</a></sup></dd>
<dd></dd></dl></div><p>Avery later fought at the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_the_Department_of_Mysteries" title="Battle of the Department of Mysteries">Battle of the Department of Mysteries</a> against members of <a href="/wiki/Dumbledore%27s_Army" title="Dumbledore&#39;s Army">Dumbledore's Army</a>, when he was paired with fellow Death Eater <a href="/wiki/Walden_Macnair" title="Walden Macnair">Macnair</a>, in an attempt to retrieve the <a href="/wiki/Sybill_Trelawney%27s_first_prophecy" title="Sybill Trelawney&#39;s first prophecy">prophecy</a> from the <a href="/wiki/Department_of_Mysteries" title="Department of Mysteries">Department of Mysteries</a> for Voldemort.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP35_6-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP35-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup> His fate during the battle is unknown, although it is likely he was incapacitated, arrested, and sent to <a href="/wiki/Azkaban" title="Azkaban">Azkaban</a>, like many of his comrades.
</p><p>Whenever Avery survived the <a href="/wiki/Second_Wizarding_War" title="Second Wizarding War">Second Wizarding War</a> is also unknown.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Personality_and_traits">Personality and traits</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Avery_II?veaction=edit&amp;section=8" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Personality and traits">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Avery_II?action=edit&amp;section=8" title="Edit section: Personality and traits"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Avery, when he was a student at Hogwarts, was described by Lily Evans as having an "evil sense of humour", indicating that he was a sadist. She also called Avery and Mulciber "Death Eaters" before they became two of them, meaning that they had a passion with <a href="/wiki/Dark_Arts" title="Dark Arts">Dark Arts</a> and <a href="/wiki/Blood_status" title="Blood status">blood purity</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-DH33_4-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH33-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> Avery was also disloyal, as he did not search for his master after his downfall. Much like <a href="/wiki/Peter_Pettigrew" title="Peter Pettigrew">Peter Pettigrew</a> he was characterised as a weak individual, shown when he cowardly begged Voldemort to forgive him and the other Death Eaters who did not search for him.<sup id="cite_ref-GOF33_8-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF33-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup> He also seemed to be incompetent, as his suggestion for helping Voldemort obtain the prophecy about himself and Harry Potter failed miserably<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP26_9-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP26-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup> and he was among the Death Eaters defeated in battle with the members of Dumbledore's Army.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP35_6-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP35-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup> It is unknown if his weak and cowardly characteristics were present during his time at Hogwarts or if they developed later in life, though <a href="/wiki/Lily_J._Potter" title="Lily J. Potter">Lily Evans</a> and <a href="/wiki/Sirius_Black" title="Sirius Black">Sirius Black</a> did not seem to regard him with as much disdain as they did towards his <a href="/wiki/Gang_of_Slytherins" title="Gang of Slytherins">friends</a>.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Magical_abilities_and_skills">Magical abilities and skills</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Avery_II?veaction=edit&amp;section=9" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Avery_II?action=edit&amp;section=9" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li><b><a href="/wiki/Dark_Arts" title="Dark Arts">Dark Arts</a></b>: As a Death Eater, Avery would have been accomplished with dark magic and <a href="/wiki/Dark_charm" title="Dark charm">dark charms</a>, most likely including the <a href="/wiki/Unforgivable_Curses" title="Unforgivable Curses">Unforgivable Curses</a>.</li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Martial_magic" title="Martial magic">Martial magic</a></b>: Avery was an active Death Eater during the <a href="/wiki/First_Wizarding_War" title="First Wizarding War">First Wizarding War</a> and survived to the end, meaning he was a reasonably competent <a href="/wiki/Duelling" title="Duelling">duellist</a> at the least. He also took part in the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_the_Department_of_Mysteries" title="Battle of the Department of Mysteries">Battle of the Department of Mysteries</a> where he fought against members of <a href="/wiki/Dumbledore%27s_Army" title="Dumbledore&#39;s Army">Dumbledore's Army</a> and the <a href="/wiki/Order_of_the_Phoenix" title="Order of the Phoenix">Order of the Phoenix</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP35_6-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP35-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Apparition" title="Apparition">Apparition</a></b>: Avery was capable of apparition, as he was able to apparate to the <a href="/wiki/Little_Hangleton" title="Little Hangleton">Little Hangleton</a> <a href="/wiki/Little_Hangleton_graveyard" title="Little Hangleton graveyard">graveyard</a> following <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Return_of_Lord_Voldemort" title="Return of Lord Voldemort">rebirth</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-GOF33_8-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF33-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup></li></ul>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Avery_II?veaction=edit&amp;section=10" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Avery_II?action=edit&amp;section=10" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p><i>Avery</i> is an English surname derived from an Old French version of the name Alfred. The meaning of the name is derived from the Old English words <i>aelf</i>, meaning elf, and <i>raed</i>, meaning counsel.
</p>
