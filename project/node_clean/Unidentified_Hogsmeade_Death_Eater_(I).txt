<div class="quote">
<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 2">"<i>POTTER!</i>"</span></dd>
<dd>&#8212;the <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eater</a> screaming at <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>.<sup class="noprint"><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Deathly_Hallows:_Part_2" title="Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 2">[src]</a></sup></dd>
<dd></dd></dl></div>
<p>This <b>individual</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Dark_Wizard" title="Dark Wizard">Dark Wizard</a> and <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eater</a> who guarded <a href="/wiki/Hogsmeade" title="Hogsmeade">Hogsmeade Village</a> in <a href="/wiki/1998" title="1998">1998</a> when <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Voldemort</a> took control over the <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">Ministry</a>. He and another Death Eater were searching for <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>, <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ronald Weasley</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione Granger</a> when the <a href="/wiki/Trio" title="Trio">trio</a> tried to find a safe place in the village.
</p><p>He later fought in the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a>, where he was seen participating in the <a href="/wiki/Onslaught_at_the_Viaduct" title="Onslaught at the Viaduct">Onslaught at the Viaduct</a>, where he casts the <a href="/wiki/Cruciatus_Curse" title="Cruciatus Curse">Cruciatus Curse</a>. He was later called back to the <a href="/wiki/Forbidden_Forest" title="Forbidden Forest">Forbidden Forest</a>. He was also present at Voldemort's speech of <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>'s "dead" body. After Harry revealed himself to be alive, he was seen <a href="/wiki/Apparition" title="Apparition">apparating</a> away, although he was later seen inside the <a href="/wiki/Great_Hall" title="Great Hall">Great Hall</a>, implying he was only transporting to the inside of the castle.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>After Voldemort's defeat and death, he was possibly killed or imprisoned in <a href="/wiki/Azkaban" title="Azkaban">Azkaban</a> for his crimes as a Death Eater.
</p>
