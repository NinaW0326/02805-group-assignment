<b>Theseus Scamander</b> (born c. <a href="/wiki/1888" class="mw-redirect" title="1888">1888</a><sup id="cite_ref-age_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-age-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>) was a <a href="/wiki/Great_Britain" title="Great Britain">British</a> <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> and <a href="/wiki/Auror" title="Auror">Auror</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-FB60_7-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-FB60-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-collider_8-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-collider-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-snitchseeker_9-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-snitchseeker-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup> He was the son of <a href="/wiki/Newton_Scamander%27s_mother" title="Newton Scamander&#39;s mother">Mrs Scamander</a> and the older brother of <a href="/wiki/Newton_Scamander" title="Newton Scamander">Newt Scamander</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-FB60_7-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-FB60-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-FB-real_10-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-FB-real-10">&#91;10&#93;</a></sup> Theseus was regarded as a war hero after his involvement in the <a href="/wiki/First_World_War" title="First World War">First World War</a>, and was promoted to Head Auror during the early days of the <a href="/wiki/Global_Wizarding_War" class="mw-redirect" title="Global Wizarding War">Global Wizarding War</a>, with Theseus greatly contributing to the effort against <a href="/wiki/Gellert_Grindelwald" title="Gellert Grindelwald">Gellert Grindelwald</a>.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Theseus_Scamander?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Theseus_Scamander?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_life_and_schooling">Early life and schooling</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Theseus_Scamander?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Early life and schooling">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Theseus_Scamander?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Early life and schooling"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Theseus Scamander was born into the <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizarding</a> <a href="/wiki/Scamander_family" title="Scamander family">Scamander family</a> sometime around <a href="/wiki/1888" class="mw-redirect" title="1888">1888</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-age_1-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-age-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> and had at least one sibling, a younger brother, <a href="/wiki/Newton_Scamander" title="Newton Scamander">Newton</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-FB60_7-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-FB60-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup> better known as "Newt". Despite his <a href="/wiki/Newton_Scamander%27s_mother" title="Newton Scamander&#39;s mother">mother</a>'s hobby of breeding fancy hippogriffs, Theseus evidently never shared his brother's interest in the field of <a href="/wiki/Magizoology" title="Magizoology">Magizoology</a>, and regarded caring of magical creatures as a fine way of passing the time, but did not take it seriously as a professional pursuit. This was an opinion he apparently had in common with his mother, as neither of them seemed to be very impressed with his younger brother's career.
</p><p>At some point, he bought or inherited a wand.
</p><p>During his formative years, he attended <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a>, having been <a href="/wiki/Sorting_ceremony" title="Sorting ceremony">Sorted</a> into <a href="/wiki/Hufflepuff" title="Hufflepuff">Hufflepuff</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-SnitchSeeker_6-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-SnitchSeeker-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup> where he excelled in his studies and proved himself an academically gifted student. Proficient in both the theoretical aspects of his lessons and savvy in regard to their practical application, he would finish his education earning at least five <a href="/wiki/Nastily_Exhausting_Wizarding_Test" title="Nastily Exhausting Wizarding Test">N.E.W.T.s</a>, with top grades of either 'Outstanding' or 'Exceeds Expectations'.<sup id="cite_ref-ootp29_11-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-ootp29-11">&#91;11&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_career">Early career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Theseus_Scamander?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Early career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Theseus_Scamander?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Early career"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>At some point after he finished his magical education, Theseus would join the <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">British Ministry of Magic</a>, and complete a stringent series of character and aptitude tests to become a fully-fledged applicant for individuals wishing to work at the <a href="/wiki/Auror_Office" title="Auror Office">Auror Office</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Department_of_Magical_Law_Enforcement" title="Department of Magical Law Enforcement">Department of Magical Law Enforcement</a>, which he would eventually do after he successfully satisfied the requirements necessary to join their ranks by passing their notoriously demanding <a href="/wiki/Auror_training" title="Auror training">training programme</a>.
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="First_World_War">First World War</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Theseus_Scamander?veaction=edit&amp;section=4" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: First World War">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Theseus_Scamander?action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: First World War"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>As an official <a href="/wiki/Auror" title="Auror">Dark Wizard catcher</a>, he proved himself a gifted employee and was recognised as a powerful wizard, yet proved himself to be a man of principle who was not content with sitting idly by when catastrophe strikes, as shown by how he was counted among the "<i>thousands</i>" who defied the then Minister for Magic <a href="/wiki/Archer_Evermonde" title="Archer Evermonde">Archer Evermonde</a>'s emergency legislation that forbade the British wizarding community to get involved when the Muggle world entered a period marked by a massive, military conflict that became known as the <a href="/wiki/First_World_War" title="First World War">First World War</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-12" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-12">&#91;12&#93;</a></sup> Throughout the war, his participation in the <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizarding</a> effort of the war was, while not decisive, nevertheless a distinguished one, during which he presumably fought and defeated numerous magical enemies and won many victories in preventing additional loss of life, seen as he would return home a tried and tested veteran of war, and would be donned by many as a war hero.
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Global_wizarding_war">Global wizarding war</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Theseus_Scamander?veaction=edit&amp;section=5" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Global wizarding war">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Theseus_Scamander?action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Global wizarding war"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>A considerably hard-working and conscientious man, Theseus would continue to work for the Ministry following his return to Britain, and rose to become a very high-ranking and well-respected Auror indeed, to the point where, when a <a href="/wiki/Dark_Wizard" title="Dark Wizard">Dark Wizard</a> by the name of <a href="/wiki/Gellert_Grindelwald" title="Gellert Grindelwald">Gellert Grindelwald</a> became active in the <a href="/wiki/Wizarding_World" class="mw-redirect" title="Wizarding World">Wizarding World</a> and whose charismatic idealism had begun to muster support for the cause of bringing about the annulment of the <a href="/wiki/International_Statute_of_Wizarding_Secrecy" title="International Statute of Wizarding Secrecy">International Statute of Wizarding Secrecy</a> and subsequently earned him the discontent of both Theseus's own superiors as well as the <a href="/wiki/International_Confederation_of_Wizards" title="International Confederation of Wizards">International Confederation of Wizards</a>, the celebrated "war hero" was assigned by the <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">Ministry</a> to leave for <a href="/wiki/Europe" title="Europe">Europe</a> to help track him down. A notable accomplishment, considering the whole department wanted the case, and excitedly wrote an <a href="/wiki/Theseus_Scamander%27s_letter_to_Newton_Scamander" title="Theseus Scamander&#39;s letter to Newton Scamander">owl</a> to his brother and told him of his mission. At the time, he had no knowledge of his brother's whereabouts, but simply wished him well "<i>wherever </i>[he was]" on "<i>whatever beastly quests </i>[he was]<i> undertaking!</i>".<sup id="cite_ref-13" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-13">&#91;13&#93;</a></sup> While his efforts to "ferret out" the aspiring revolutionary ultimately proved unsuccessful,<sup id="cite_ref-14" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-14">&#91;14&#93;</a></sup> he was eventually promoted to the Head of the Auror Office.
</p>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Hunt_for_Gellert_Grindelwald">Hunt for Gellert Grindelwald</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Theseus_Scamander?veaction=edit&amp;section=6" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Hunt for Gellert Grindelwald">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Theseus_Scamander?action=edit&amp;section=6" title="Edit section: Hunt for Gellert Grindelwald"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<p>As the <i><a href="/wiki/For_the_Greater_Good" title="For the Greater Good">For the Greater Good</a></i> revolutionary endeavours of Grindelwald grew ever fiercer and the number of individuals who joined his cause began to increase steadily, with both Grindelwald and his supporters having began launching several devastating attacks across Europe, aimed at exposing wizardkind, and provoke war with the Muggle world, any help Theseus could have provided in opposing him were most likely halted by people in authority such as <a href="/wiki/Minister" title="Minister">Minister</a> <a href="/wiki/Hector_Fawley" title="Hector Fawley">Hector Fawley</a>, who did not take the threat Grindelwald and his legion of fanatics seriously, seen as he was still working nationally by the mid-<a href="/wiki/1920s" title="1920s">1920s</a>.
</p><p>In <a href="/wiki/1926" title="1926">1926</a>, Theseus corresponded with the man whom he assumed to be <a href="/wiki/Percival_Graves" title="Percival Graves">Percival Graves</a>, the <a href="/wiki/Director_of_Magical_Security" title="Director of Magical Security">Director of Magical Security</a> of <a href="/wiki/Magical_Congress_of_the_United_States_of_America" title="Magical Congress of the United States of America">MACUSA</a>, regarding recent events in <a href="/wiki/New_York" title="New York">New York</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-collider_8-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-collider-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-snitchseeker_9-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-snitchseeker-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup> unaware that the man with whom he communicated was actually <a href="/wiki/Gellert_Grindelwald" title="Gellert Grindelwald">Gellert Grindelwald</a> himself, in disguise.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Personality_and_traits">Personality and traits</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Theseus_Scamander?veaction=edit&amp;section=13" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Personality and traits">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Theseus_Scamander?action=edit&amp;section=13" title="Edit section: Personality and traits"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Theseus was frequently described as being "very powerful" and was noted to be "commanding".<sup id="cite_ref-collider_8-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-collider-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-snitchseeker_9-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-snitchseeker-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup> Given his participation in the <a href="/wiki/First_World_War" title="First World War">First World War</a>, and his description as a hero, he appeared also to be extremely brave, and was therefore quite respected among <a href="/wiki/International_Confederation_of_Wizards" title="International Confederation of Wizards">International Confederation of Wizards</a> officials. Theseus is highly intelligent, as he was academically gifted in both the theoretical aspects of his lessons, as well as their practical applications. In stark contrast to his anti-social younger brother <a href="/wiki/Newton_Scamander" title="Newton Scamander">Newt Scamander</a>, Theseus is rather confident and outgoing.
</p><p>Despite this bossy and prim reputation, Theseus is a well-meaning, hard-working, noble, and responsible man, in true <a href="/wiki/Hufflepuff" title="Hufflepuff">Hufflepuff</a> fashion not allowing his status as a war hero to make him arrogant or conceited, and seemingly attempting to act as a good role model to his younger brother Newt Scamander. Theseus loved Newt, and attempted to give him useful advice to keep Newt out of political trouble, and patiently put up with the bitterness with which Newt treated him, simply wanting the best for his brother. Theseus is also quite affectionate, with him notably hugging Newt (though the latter was initially uncomfortable with this), and kissing his beloved fiancee <a href="/wiki/Leta_Lestrange" title="Leta Lestrange">Leta Lestrange</a>. Moreover, Theseus seems to despise violence, since he admitted to Newt that he hated the idea of <a href="/wiki/Gunnar_Grimmson" title="Gunnar Grimmson">Gunnar Grimmson</a> going after <a href="/wiki/Credence_Barebone" title="Credence Barebone">Credence</a> for the <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">British Ministry</a>. Theseus's nobleness is also shown when he acted calm and quiet while visiting <a href="/wiki/Albus_Dumbledore" title="Albus Dumbledore">Albus Dumbledore</a>, as opposed to the arrogance and disdain with which with his boss <a href="/wiki/Torquil_Travers" title="Torquil Travers">Torquil Travers</a> carried himself, with Theseus also taking to heart Dumbledore's advice about not showing counter-productive violence against <a href="/wiki/Gellert_Grindelwald%27s_army" class="mw-redirect" title="Gellert Grindelwald&#39;s army">Gellert Grindelwald's army</a> during the <a href="/wiki/Paris" title="Paris">Paris</a> rally. However, Theseus was tragically unable to stop one of his own Auror subordinates from making that mistake at the rally.
</p><p>Also, after fellow Auror <a href="/wiki/Porpentina_Goldstein" title="Porpentina Goldstein">Tina Goldstein</a> tied Theseus up, demanding that he "control his temper", Theseus was noble again by not resenting her for that later, understanding Tina's loyalty to Newt. Overall, Theseus lived up to his brave reputation, and had an incorruptible moral code, as he was able to resist <a href="/wiki/Gellert_Grindelwald" title="Gellert Grindelwald">Gellert Grindelwald</a>'s persuasive speech, and even had the bravery to subsequently duel Grindelwald, despite knowing that he was no match for the fearsome <a href="/wiki/Dark_Wizard" title="Dark Wizard">Dark Wizard</a>. Theseus also dearly loved his late fiancée <a href="/wiki/Leta_Lestrange" title="Leta Lestrange">Leta Lestrange</a>, not caring about her negative reputation, and her selfless death devastated Theseus, but left him more determined than ever to help stop Grindelwald and end the <a href="/wiki/Global_wizarding_war" title="Global wizarding war">Global wizarding war</a>, so he readily frees Dumbledore of the latter's <a href="/wiki/Admonitor" title="Admonitor">Admonitors</a>.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Magical_abilities_and_skills">Magical abilities and skills</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Theseus_Scamander?veaction=edit&amp;section=14" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Theseus_Scamander?action=edit&amp;section=14" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li><b><a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">Magical</a> mastery</b>: Theseus Scamander was described as "very powerful", and, as a <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts</a> student, he excelled in his studies enough to meet the qualifications for <a href="/wiki/Auror_training" title="Auror training">Auror training</a>, becoming an Auror, and eventually getting promoted to the position of Head Auror. While Theseus alone was admittedly no match for the extraordinarily powerful <a href="/wiki/Gellert_Grindelwald" title="Gellert Grindelwald">Gellert Grindelwald</a>, who was possibly the most powerful Dark Wizard to ever live, <a href="/wiki/Department_of_Magical_Law_Enforcement" title="Department of Magical Law Enforcement">Department of Magical Law Enforcement</a> Head <a href="/wiki/Torquil_Travers" title="Torquil Travers">Torquil Travers</a> trusted the combined might of Theseus and the latter's subordinate Aurors with the task of bringing Grindelwald in after <a href="/wiki/Albus_Dumbledore" title="Albus Dumbledore">Albus Dumbledore</a> refused to, with even Dumbledore seemingly believing that Theseus could survive a fight with Grindelwald if aided by his allies. Indeed, Theseus proved powerful and skilled enough to be able to survive such a fight, alongside Newt and Leta, and was moreover able to aid his brother, future sister-in-law, Yusuf, and <a href="/wiki/Nicolas_Flamel" title="Nicolas Flamel">Nicolas Flamel</a> in saving Paris from Grindelwald's black fire.</li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Auror" title="Auror">Auror</a> skills</b>: Theseus Scamander, as the Head Auror, was an exceptionally skilled Auror, more than likely completing the <a href="/wiki/Concealment_and_Disguise" title="Concealment and Disguise">Concealment and Disguise</a>, as well as the <a href="/wiki/Stealth_and_Tracking" title="Stealth and Tracking">Stealth and Tracking</a> portions of <a href="/wiki/Auror_training" title="Auror training">Auror training</a> with excellence. These skills greatly contributed to Theseus's outstanding performance during the <a href="/wiki/First_World_War" title="First World War">First World War</a>, and to him gaining the respected status of "war hero", to the point that <a href="/wiki/Hector_Podmore" title="Hector Podmore">Hector Podmore</a>, <a href="/wiki/Momolu_Wotorson" title="Momolu Wotorson">Momolu Wotorson</a>, <a href="/wiki/Rudolph_Spielman" title="Rudolph Spielman">Rudolph Spielman</a>, <a href="/wiki/Arnold_Guzman" title="Arnold Guzman">Arnold Guzman</a> and even the abrasive <a href="/wiki/Torquil_Travers" title="Torquil Travers">Torquil Travers</a> respected him, while <a href="/wiki/Gellert_Grindelwald" title="Gellert Grindelwald">Gellert Grindelwald</a> kept up a correspondence with Theseus while posing as <a href="/wiki/Percival_Graves" title="Percival Graves">Percival Graves</a>. During the <a href="/wiki/Global_wizarding_war" title="Global wizarding war">Global wizarding war</a>, Theseus was trusted by the <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">British Ministry of Magic</a> with the mission to participate in the international wizardhunt for <a href="/wiki/Gellert_Grindelwald" title="Gellert Grindelwald">Gellert Grindelwald</a> (which proved unsuccessful), and later with the international mission to help the <a href="/wiki/French_Ministry_of_Magic" title="French Ministry of Magic">French Ministry of Magic</a> to track down the <a href="/wiki/Obscurial" title="Obscurial">Obscurial</a> <a href="/wiki/Credence_Barebone" title="Credence Barebone">Credence Barebone</a>, with Theseus's Auror team ultimately succeeding at finding Credence in the <a href="/wiki/Lestrange_Mausoleum" title="Lestrange Mausoleum">Lestrange family Mausoleum</a>. Theseus was also observant enough to quickly notice his brother <a href="/wiki/Newton_Scamander" title="Newton Scamander">Newt</a> and American Auror <a href="/wiki/Porpentina_Goldstein" title="Porpentina Goldstein">Tina Goldstein</a> infiltrating the French Ministry, despite their efforts to be discreet.</li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Duelling" title="Duelling">Duelling</a></b>: As the Head Auror, Theseus was exceptionally skilled in <a href="/wiki/Martial_magic" title="Martial magic">martial magic</a>, surviving magical involvement in the <a href="/wiki/First_World_War" title="First World War">First World War</a> and being considered a "war hero" for his efforts, and later actively participated in the <a href="/wiki/Global_wizarding_war" title="Global wizarding war">Global wizarding war</a> against <a href="/wiki/Gellert_Grindelwald" title="Gellert Grindelwald">Gellert Grindelwald</a>. Theseus was notably only overpowered by fellow Auror <a href="/wiki/Porpentina_Goldstein" title="Porpentina Goldstein">Tina Goldstein</a> due to being caught off-guard by her, so he later greatly contributed to stopping Grindelwald's Paris rally, defeating many of Grindelwald's allies. Although Theseus was no match for the incredibly powerful <a href="/wiki/Dark_Wizard" title="Dark Wizard">Dark Wizard</a> alone, while aided by the combined efforts of his brother <a href="/wiki/Newton_Scamander" title="Newton Scamander">Newt</a> and girlfriend <a href="/wiki/Leta_Lestrange" title="Leta Lestrange">Leta</a> as well as future sister-in-law <a href="/wiki/Porpentina_Goldstein" title="Porpentina Goldstein">Tina</a>, he could still stand his ground against Grindelwald for a time.</li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Defence_Against_the_Dark_Arts" title="Defence Against the Dark Arts">Defence Against the Dark Arts</a></b>: As the Head Auror, Theseus would have received either an "<a href="/wiki/Outstanding" title="Outstanding">Outstanding</a>" or "<a href="/wiki/Exceeds_Expectations" title="Exceeds Expectations">Exceeds Expectations</a>" in his <a href="/wiki/Ordinary_Wizarding_Level" title="Ordinary Wizarding Level">O.W.L.</a> and <a href="/wiki/Nastily_Exhausting_Wizarding_Test" title="Nastily Exhausting Wizarding Test">N.E.W.T.</a> for this subject,<sup id="cite_ref-ootp29_11-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-ootp29-11">&#91;11&#93;</a></sup> as he was possibly taught this subject by <a href="/wiki/Albus_Dumbledore" title="Albus Dumbledore">Albus Dumbledore</a> himself, making him highly accomplished in defending against the <a href="/wiki/Dark_Arts" title="Dark Arts">Dark Arts</a>. Theseus's skills are evident in his victory in many duels against formidable dark wizards, such as <a href="/wiki/Gellert_Grindelwald%27s_acolytes" title="Gellert Grindelwald&#39;s acolytes">Grindelwald's allies</a>, and even surviving a brief duel against the incredibly powerful <a href="/wiki/Gellert_Grindelwald" title="Gellert Grindelwald">Grindelwald</a> himself, alongside Newt and Leta, being able to hold off Grindelwald's extremely powerful black flames long enough to be saved by Leta, and even pushing the flames back when Grindelwald was not focusing it on him (due to being distracted by Leta). Theseus also was able to easily follow <a href="/wiki/Nicolas_Flamel" title="Nicolas Flamel">Nicolas Flamel</a>'s instructions in casting the <a href="/wiki/General_Counter-Spell" title="General Counter-Spell">General Counter-Spell</a> to contain and extinguish the violent black flames.</li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Charm" title="Charm">Charms</a></b>: Theseus was highly capable in Charms, since as an Auror, he would have received at least an <a href="/wiki/Exceeds_Expectations" title="Exceeds Expectations">Exceeds Expectations</a> in his O.W.L. and N.E.W.T. for this <a href="/wiki/Charms_(class)" title="Charms (class)">subject</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-ootp29_11-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-ootp29-11">&#91;11&#93;</a></sup> He was particularly apt in the use of defensive charms and <a href="/wiki/Counter-spell" title="Counter-spell">Counter-spells</a>, as shown by his ability cast a <a href="/wiki/Shield_Charm" title="Shield Charm">Shield Charm</a> strong enough to protect himself for a prolonged period of time against Grindelwald's lethal <a href="/wiki/Protego_Diabolica" title="Protego Diabolica">black fire</a> while aided by Newt, by himself fending off a wild, but unfocused burst of said black flames after Grindelwald was distracted by Leta, and also later proving proficient enough in the <a href="/wiki/General_Counter-Spell" title="General Counter-Spell">General Counter-Spell</a> to use it to an enough scale alongside Nicolas Flamel, Newt, Tina and Yusuf in containing and extinguishing said black fire.</li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Transfiguration" title="Transfiguration">Transfiguration</a></b>: As an Auror, Theseus was skilled in Transfiguration, as he had to have received at least an Exceeds Expectations in his O.W.L. and N.E.W.T. for this <a href="/wiki/Transfiguration_(class)" title="Transfiguration (class)">subject</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-ootp29_11-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-ootp29-11">&#91;11&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Potion" title="Potion">Potions</a></b>: As an Auror, Theseus was skilled in potions, as he had to have received at least an Exceeds Expectations in his O.W.L. and N.E.W.T. for this <a href="/wiki/Potions_(class)" title="Potions (class)">subject</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-ootp29_11-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-ootp29-11">&#91;11&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Herbology" title="Herbology">Herbology</a></b>: As an Auror, Theseus was skilled in Herbology, as he had to have received at least an Exceeds Expectations in his O.W.L. and N.E.W.T. for this subject.<sup id="cite_ref-ootp29_11-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-ootp29-11">&#91;11&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Apparition" title="Apparition">Apparition</a></b>: Theseus, like most accomplished adult wizards, could Apparate at will.</li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Nonverbal_spell" title="Nonverbal spell">Nonverbal magic</a></b>: Theseus was very skilled at nonverbal magic, with him breaking up the <a href="/wiki/Paris" title="Paris">Paris</a> rally of <a href="/wiki/Gellert_Grindelwald%27s_acolytes" title="Gellert Grindelwald&#39;s acolytes">Gellert Grindelwald's acolytes</a>, and while assisting his brother <a href="/wiki/Newton_Scamander" title="Newton Scamander">Newt</a>, <a href="/wiki/Nicolas_Flamel" title="Nicolas Flamel">Nicolas Flamel</a>, <a href="/wiki/Porpentina_Goldstein" title="Porpentina Goldstein">Tina Goldstein</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Yusuf_Kama" title="Yusuf Kama">Yusuf Kama</a> in containing and dissipating the lethally dangerous <a href="/wiki/Protego_Diabolica" title="Protego Diabolica">black fire</a> left behind by Grindelwald, all without uttering a word</li>
<li><b>Leadership skills</b>: Theseus, as the <a href="/wiki/Auror_Office" title="Auror Office">Head Auror</a> in the <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">British Ministry of Magic</a>, had exceptional leadership skills, with him very effectively commanding a team of no less than 50 Aurors at Grindelwald's rally, and even Albus Dumbledore seemed to respect the Auror's leadership, since he took the time to give the younger man some useful advice. Tragically, however, Theseus was unable to stop one of his Auror subordinates from using the <a href="/wiki/Killing_Curse" title="Killing Curse">Killing Curse</a> at the rally.</li>
<li><b>Indomitable willpower</b>: Theseus possessed immense willpower and bravery, being a respected hero of the <a href="/wiki/First_World_War" title="First World War">First World War</a>, and joining in on the subsequent <a href="/wiki/Global_wizarding_war" title="Global wizarding war">Global wizarding war</a> as the <a href="/wiki/Auror" title="Auror">Head Auror</a> without hesitation. As such, despite recognising <a href="/wiki/Gellert_Grindelwald" title="Gellert Grindelwald">Gellert Grindelwald</a> as a "charismatic blighter", and despite seeing horrifying visions of a future <a href="/wiki/Second_World_War" title="Second World War">Second World War</a>, Theseus's moral code was incorruptible, and therefore, he resisted Grindelwald's persuasive speech, and continued valiantly fighting to break up the rally, whilst at the same time rejecting <a href="/wiki/Torquil_Travers" title="Torquil Travers">Travers</a>' oppressive orders to arrest anyone simply present.</li></ul>

His <a href="/wiki/Wand_wood" title="Wand wood">wand wood</a>, <a href="/wiki/Wand_core" title="Wand core">core</a> and length were all unknown.</li></ul>

<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Newt_Scamander">Newt Scamander</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Theseus_Scamander?veaction=edit&amp;section=17" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Newt Scamander">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Theseus_Scamander?action=edit&amp;section=17" title="Edit section: Newt Scamander"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<figure class="thumb tright show-info-icon" style="width: 252px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/b/b6/Ministry_family.jpeg/revision/latest?cb=20181108044305" class="image"><img alt="" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw%3D%3D" decoding="async" width="250" height="167" class="thumbimage lazyload" data-image-name="Ministry family.jpeg" data-image-key="Ministry_family.jpeg" data-src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/b/b6/Ministry_family.jpeg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/250?cb=20181108044305" /></a> 	<noscript><a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/b/b6/Ministry_family.jpeg/revision/latest?cb=20181108044305" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/b/b6/Ministry_family.jpeg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/250?cb=20181108044305" decoding="async" width="250" height="167" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="Ministry family.jpeg" data-image-key="Ministry_family.jpeg" data-src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/b/b6/Ministry_family.jpeg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/250?cb=20181108044305" /></a></noscript> 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:Ministry_family.jpeg" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption">Theseus Scamander with his brother Newt Scamander and Fiancee Leta Lestrange</p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure>
<p>While not very impressed with his brother's career, it would still appear that they got along reasonably well, given the light-hearted tone of the letter he sent his brother. Even so, he still had certain high expectations of his brother, demanding that Newt pick a side in the war against Grindelwald when the news reached them that the dark wizard had escaped MACUSA custody, and resumed his rise to dominance over the Wizarding World. Even though Newt is distant and bitter towards Theseus, due to his older brother's engagement to his ex-girlfriend <a href="/wiki/Leta_Lestrange" title="Leta Lestrange">Leta Lestrange</a>, Theseus harbours no ill will towards his brother, and has a tendency to hug Newt, much to Newt's discomfort. However, after Leta sacrifices her life to save the both of them, a devastated Newt is the one to initiate a hug with Theseus, mending their relationship, and assuring his brother that he has finally chosen a side in the <a href="/wiki/Global_wizarding_war" title="Global wizarding war">Global wizarding war</a>.
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Leta_Lestrange">Leta Lestrange</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Theseus_Scamander?veaction=edit&amp;section=18" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Leta Lestrange">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Theseus_Scamander?action=edit&amp;section=18" title="Edit section: Leta Lestrange"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>

<p>While it is unknown what kind of relationship they might have had during their younger years, if any, Theseus and Leta would eventually get better acquainted at the <a href="/wiki/Ministry_of_Magic" title="Ministry of Magic">Ministry of Magic</a>, where he worked as the Head Auror, and she as the assistant to the Head of <a href="/wiki/Department_of_Magical_Law_Enforcement" title="Department of Magical Law Enforcement">Magical Law Enforcement</a>, <a href="/wiki/Torquil_Travers" title="Torquil Travers">Torquil Travers</a>, resulting in the gradual burgeoning of a mutual love between them, and an engagement. Leta's death left Theseus absolutely devastated, and he readily agreed to free <a href="/wiki/Albus_Dumbledore" title="Albus Dumbledore">Albus Dumbledore</a> of the <a href="/wiki/Admonitor" title="Admonitor">Admonitors</a>, so that the professor could more actively help in the war against Grindelwald.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Theseus_Scamander?veaction=edit&amp;section=21" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Theseus_Scamander?action=edit&amp;section=21" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li><i>Theseus</i> (Θησεύς) was the mythical king of Athens who is most known as having undergone the Labyrinth where he slew the Minotaur. Like his mythological namesake, he laboured for the common good and won wide renown (as both an Auror and a war hero), battling against moral evil (comparable to the monsters of mythological renown).</li>
<li>Though it may seem Newt's brother Theseus has no connection with beasts on the outside, according to <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hippolytus_(play)" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Hippolytus (play)">Euripides</a>, Hippolytus (mythological Theseus's son with Hippolyta, Queen of the Amazons) was a loyal follower of the goddess <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Artemis" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Artemis">Artemis</a>, who besides being more known as the goddess of the hunt, also functions (among other things) as "mistress of the animals" (in her capacity of her title as "<i>Potnia Theron</i>") &amp; "nurse of youths" (epithet "<i>Kourotrophos</i>"); "Artemis" being one of Newt's middle names. Alternatively, Theseus's connection to beasts may be more direct. While Newt, nicknamed for a creature, seeks to protect animals, Theseus, with the name of a man who killed such a creature, the Minotaur, may have an opposing perspective.</li></ul>
