<p><b>Sturgis Podmore</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> and member of the <a href="/wiki/Order_of_the_Phoenix" title="Order of the Phoenix">Order of the Phoenix</a> who fought in both <a href="/wiki/First_Wizarding_War" title="First Wizarding War">First</a> and <a href="/wiki/Second_Wizarding_War" title="Second Wizarding War">Second Wizarding Wars</a>. He was also a member of the <a href="/wiki/Advance_Guard" title="Advance Guard">Advance Guard</a>. In <a href="/wiki/1995" title="1995">1995</a>, Podmore was put under the <a href="/wiki/Imperius_Curse" title="Imperius Curse">Imperius Curse</a> by <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a>, who made him attempt to break into the <a href="/wiki/Department_of_Mysteries" title="Department of Mysteries">Department of Mysteries</a>. He was sentenced to six months in <a href="/wiki/Azkaban" title="Azkaban">Azkaban</a> for attempting to break in.<sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h3><span id="Early_life_(1950s-1960s)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_life_.281950s-1960s.29">Early life <span class="template-c">(1950s-1960s)</span></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Sturgis_Podmore?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Early life (1950s-1960s)">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Sturgis_Podmore?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Early life (1950s-1960s)"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Sturgis Podmore was born at some point between <a href="/wiki/8_September" title="8 September">8 September</a> <a href="/wiki/1956" title="1956">1956</a> and <a href="/wiki/7_September" title="7 September">7 September</a> <a href="/wiki/1957" title="1957">1957</a>, eventually acquiring a <a href="/wiki/Wand" title="Wand">wand</a> and being trained in the <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magical</a> arts.
</p>

<p>Sturgis Podmore was a member of the original <a href="/wiki/Order_of_the_Phoenix" title="Order of the Phoenix">Order of the Phoenix</a>. The order was an organisation founded and led by <a href="/wiki/Albus_Dumbledore" title="Albus Dumbledore">Albus Dumbledore</a>, in order to oppose <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a> and his <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a> during the <a href="/wiki/First_Wizarding_War" title="First Wizarding War">First Wizarding War</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-OP9_5-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OP9-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> Podmore managed to survive the war since Lord Voldemort's first downfall.<sup id="cite_ref-OP3_3-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OP3-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span id="Second_Wizarding_War_(1995-1998)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="Second_Wizarding_War_.281995-1998.29">Second Wizarding War <span class="template-c">(1995-1998)</span></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Sturgis_Podmore?veaction=edit&amp;section=4" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Second Wizarding War (1995-1998)">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Sturgis_Podmore?action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Second Wizarding War (1995-1998)"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<div class="quote">
<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix">"<i>Sturgis Podmore, 38, of number two, Laburnum Gardens, Clapham, has appeared in front of the Wizengamot charged with trespass and attempted robbery at the Ministry of Magic on 31st August. Podmore, who refused to speak in his own defence, was convicted on both charges and sentenced to six months in Azkaban.</i>"</span></dd>
<dd>&#8212;<i><a href="/wiki/Daily_Prophet" title="Daily Prophet">Daily Prophet</a></i>, <a href="/wiki/1_September" title="1 September">1 September</a> <a href="/wiki/1995" title="1995">1995</a><sup class="noprint"><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Order_of_the_Phoenix" title="Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix">[src]</a></sup></dd>
<dd></dd></dl></div><p>After <a href="/wiki/Rebirth_of_Tom_Riddle" class="mw-redirect" title="Rebirth of Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort's return</a> to full power in <a href="/wiki/1995" title="1995">1995</a>, Sturgis Podmore returned to the regrouped Order of the Phoenix, contributing to their efforts during the <a href="/wiki/Second_Wizarding_War" title="Second Wizarding War">Second Wizarding War</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-OP3_3-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OP3-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> By this time, Sturgis lived in <a href="/wiki/2_Laburnum_Gardens" title="2 Laburnum Gardens">2 Laburnum Gardens</a>, in <a href="/wiki/Clapham" title="Clapham">Clapham</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-OP14_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OP14-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Escorting_Harry_Potter">Escorting Harry Potter</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Sturgis_Podmore?veaction=edit&amp;section=5" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Escorting Harry Potter">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Sturgis_Podmore?action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Escorting Harry Potter"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>

<p>Sturgis was a part of the <a href="/wiki/Advance_Guard" title="Advance Guard">Advance Guard</a> — together with <a href="/wiki/Alastor_Moody" title="Alastor Moody">Alastor Moody</a>, <a href="/wiki/Remus_Lupin" title="Remus Lupin">Remus Lupin</a>, <a href="/wiki/Nymphadora_Tonks" title="Nymphadora Tonks">Nymphadora Tonks</a>, <a href="/wiki/Kingsley_Shacklebolt" title="Kingsley Shacklebolt">Kingsley Shacklebolt</a>, <a href="/wiki/Elphias_Doge" title="Elphias Doge">Elphias Doge</a>, <a href="/wiki/Dedalus_Diggle" title="Dedalus Diggle">Dedalus Diggle</a>, <a href="/wiki/Emmeline_Vance" title="Emmeline Vance">Emmeline Vance</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Hestia_Jones" title="Hestia Jones">Hestia Jones</a> — that safely brought <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> from <a href="/wiki/4_Privet_Drive" title="4 Privet Drive">4 Privet Drive</a> to <a href="/wiki/12_Grimmauld_Place" title="12 Grimmauld Place">12 Grimmauld Place</a>, headquarters of the Order. Upon meeting Harry, Podmore winked at him and, while he went upstairs to get his trunk, examined the Dursleys' <a href="/wiki/Microwave" title="Microwave">microwave</a> with <a href="/wiki/Kingsley_Shacklebolt" title="Kingsley Shacklebolt">Kingsley Shacklebolt</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-OP3_3-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OP3-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Imprisonment_in_Azkaban">Imprisonment in Azkaban</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Sturgis_Podmore?veaction=edit&amp;section=6" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Imprisonment in Azkaban">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Sturgis_Podmore?action=edit&amp;section=6" title="Edit section: Imprisonment in Azkaban"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<p>On <a href="/wiki/12_August" title="12 August">12 August</a>, <a href="/wiki/1995" title="1995">1995</a>, which was, incidentally, the day of <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Disciplinary_hearing_of_Harry_Potter" title="Disciplinary hearing of Harry Potter">disciplinary hearing on the charge of magic in presence of a Muggle</a>, Sturgis Podmore was on Order of the Phoenix duty standing guard beside the entrance to the <a href="/wiki/Department_of_Mysteries" title="Department of Mysteries">Department of Mysteries</a>, to prevent <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a> from stealing <a href="/wiki/Sybill_Trelawney%27s_first_prophecy" title="Sybill Trelawney&#39;s first prophecy">The Prophecy</a>. <a href="/wiki/Lucius_Malfoy" title="Lucius Malfoy">Lucius Malfoy</a> was at the Ministry that day, and he put Podmore under the <a href="/wiki/Imperius_Curse" title="Imperius Curse">Imperius Curse</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-OP26_6-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OP26-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Still under the influence of Malfoy's <a href="/wiki/Imperius_Curse" title="Imperius Curse">Imperius Curse</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-OP26_6-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OP26-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup> Podmore was back on guard duty at the Ministry on <a href="/wiki/31_August" title="31 August">31 August</a>, <a href="/wiki/1995" title="1995">1995</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-OP14_1-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OP14-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> Under the control of the <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a>, Podmore broke into the <a href="/wiki/Department_of_Mysteries" title="Department of Mysteries">Department of Mysteries</a>, a highly classified area, and attempted to steal the Prophecy. However, he was discovered during the attempt and was arrested by <a href="/wiki/Eric_Munch" title="Eric Munch">Eric Munch</a>, a <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">Ministry</a> <a href="/wiki/Watchwizard" title="Watchwizard">watchwizard</a>, and was sentenced to six months in <a href="/wiki/Azkaban" title="Azkaban">Azkaban</a>, after refusing to speak in his defence.<sup id="cite_ref-OP14_1-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OP14-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p><a href="/wiki/Alastor_Moody" title="Alastor Moody">Mad-Eye Moody</a> was irritated when Podmore did not return his <a href="/wiki/Invisibility_cloak" title="Invisibility cloak">Invisibility cloak</a> and that he did not turn up to help transport <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> safely to <a href="/wiki/King%27s_Cross_Station" title="King&#39;s Cross Station">King's Cross Station</a>, not knowing Sturgis had been Imperiused and, by that time, arrested.<sup id="cite_ref-OP10_7-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OP10-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Physical_appearance">Physical appearance</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Sturgis_Podmore?veaction=edit&amp;section=8" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Physical appearance">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Sturgis_Podmore?action=edit&amp;section=8" title="Edit section: Physical appearance"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Sturgis was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> with a square jaw and thick straw-coloured hair.<sup id="cite_ref-OP3_3-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OP3-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> These features give the impression that his head was thatched.<sup id="cite_ref-OP14_1-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OP14-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Magical_abilities_and_skills">Magical abilities and skills</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Sturgis_Podmore?veaction=edit&amp;section=9" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Sturgis_Podmore?action=edit&amp;section=9" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li><b><a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">Magical</a> skills</b>: Being a long-standing member of the <a href="/wiki/Order_of_the_Phoenix" title="Order of the Phoenix">Order of the Phoenix</a> and surviving both the <a href="/wiki/First_Wizarding_War" title="First Wizarding War">First Wizarding War</a> and the <a href="/wiki/Second_Wizarding_War" title="Second Wizarding War">Second Wizarding War,</a> Sturgis was a talented <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-OP9_5-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OP9-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> However, Sturgis was not strong enough to throw off the <a href="/wiki/Imperius_Curse" title="Imperius Curse">Imperius Curse</a>, a feat which few wizards could pull off.<sup id="cite_ref-OP26_6-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OP26-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Flight" title="Flight">Flying</a></b>: Sturgis was a skilled and accomplished <a href="/wiki/Broomstick" title="Broomstick">broomstick</a> flyer, being a member of the <a href="/wiki/Advance_Guard" title="Advance Guard">Advance Guard</a> who successfully escorted <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> to <a href="/wiki/12_Grimmauld_Place" title="12 Grimmauld Place">12 Grimmauld Place</a> via broomstick flight.<sup id="cite_ref-OP3_3-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OP3-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup></li></ul>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Relationships">Relationships</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Sturgis_Podmore?veaction=edit&amp;section=10" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Relationships">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Sturgis_Podmore?action=edit&amp;section=10" title="Edit section: Relationships"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Order_of_the_Phoenix">Order of the Phoenix</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Sturgis_Podmore?veaction=edit&amp;section=11" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Order of the Phoenix">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Sturgis_Podmore?action=edit&amp;section=11" title="Edit section: Order of the Phoenix"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Sturgis' relationship with the other members of the order is unknown; it is possible that they had a good relationship. Sturgis seemed to like <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>, when he first met him. Harry was very upset learning that Sturgis attempted to break into the <a href="/wiki/Department_of_Mysteries" title="Department of Mysteries">Department of Mysteries</a>, and thought it was unfair that Sturgis was imprisoned. <a href="/wiki/Alastor_Moody" title="Alastor Moody">Alastor Moody</a> was firstly angry with Sturgis for taking his Invisibility Cloak. However, they were all upset, learning about Sturgis's imprisonment. Sturgis might have been also friendly with <a href="/wiki/Remus_Lupin" title="Remus Lupin">Remus Lupin</a>, <a href="/wiki/Kingsley_Shacklebolt" title="Kingsley Shacklebolt">Kingsley Shacklebolt</a>, <a href="/wiki/Nymphadora_Tonks" title="Nymphadora Tonks">Nymphadora Tonks</a>, <a href="/wiki/Hestia_Jones" title="Hestia Jones">Hestia Jones</a>, <a href="/wiki/Emmeline_Vance" title="Emmeline Vance">Emmeline Vance</a>, <a href="/wiki/Elphias_Doge" title="Elphias Doge">Elphias Doge</a> and <a href="/wiki/Dedalus_Diggle" title="Dedalus Diggle">Dedalus Diggle</a>, as they were all members of the <a href="/wiki/Advance_Guard" title="Advance Guard">Advance Guard</a> in <a href="/wiki/1995" title="1995">1995</a>. He was described as a "great friend" of <a href="/wiki/Albus_Dumbledore" title="Albus Dumbledore">Albus Dumbledore</a>, the founder of the Order.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Sturgis_Podmore?veaction=edit&amp;section=12" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Sturgis_Podmore?action=edit&amp;section=12" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>Sturgis Podmore was omitted from the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Order_of_the_Phoenix_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix (film)">film</a> and <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Order_of_the_Phoenix_(video_game)" title="Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix (video game)">video game</a> adaptations of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Order_of_the_Phoenix" title="Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix">Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix</a></i>.</li>
<li>Sturgis Podmore may be related to Sir <a href="/wiki/Patrick_Delaney-Podmore" title="Patrick Delaney-Podmore">Patrick Delaney-Podmore</a> of the <a href="/wiki/Headless_Hunt" title="Headless Hunt">Headless Hunt</a>.</li>
<li>Sturgis Podmore was the inspiration for the satirical Harry Potter podcast of the same name, <a target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener" class="external text" href="http://tbnlpodcast.com/series/sturgis-podmore">Sturgis Podmore</a>.</li>
<li>Although not confirmed, it is very likely that Sturgis fought in the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a> like most other Order of the Phoenix members. If so, he likely survived as The Order members who died were all singled out in the narrative.</li>
<li>If he attended <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts</a>, Sturgis would have been a student there from <a href="/wiki/1968" title="1968">1968</a> to <a href="/wiki/1975" title="1975">1975</a>. As such, his education would have overlapped with that of the <a href="/wiki/Marauders" title="Marauders">Marauders</a> and <a href="/wiki/Severus_Snape" title="Severus Snape">Severus Snape</a> by a few years.</li></ul>
