This <b>Princess</b> was the daughter of a <a href="/wiki/Unidentified_King" title="Unidentified King">monarch</a> of a land during the <a href="/wiki/Middle_Ages" title="Middle Ages">Middle Ages</a>. <a href="/wiki/Leticia_Somnolens" title="Leticia Somnolens">Leticia Somnolens</a>, the local <a href="/wiki/Hag" title="Hag">Hag</a>, was jealous of this princess and tricked the Princess into pricking her finger on a spindle tainted with a <a href="/wiki/Draught_of_Living_Death" title="Draught of Living Death">Draught of Living Death</a>, sending her into a deep sleep. A <a href="/wiki/Unidentified_young_wizard" title="Unidentified young wizard">young wizard</a> smeared his lips with <a href="/wiki/Wiggenweld_Potion" title="Wiggenweld Potion">Wiggenweld Potion</a>, and then kissed the princess, curing her and bringing her out of the sleep.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> Her story appears to be the source of inspiration for the <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggle</a> fairy tale, <a href="/wiki/Sleeping_Beauty" title="Sleeping Beauty">Sleeping Beauty</a>.
