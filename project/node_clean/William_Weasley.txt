<p><b>William Arthur</b><sup id="cite_ref-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup> <b>"Bill" Weasley</b> (b. <a href="/wiki/29_November" title="29 November">29 November</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-BD_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-BD-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-PM-DOB_2-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PM-DOB-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/1970" title="1970">1970</a>)<sup id="cite_ref-YOB_3-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-YOB-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> was an <a href="/wiki/England" title="England">English</a> <a href="/wiki/Pure-blood" title="Pure-blood">pure-blood</a> <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a>, the first child of <a href="/wiki/Arthur_Weasley" title="Arthur Weasley">Arthur</a> and <a href="/wiki/Molly_Weasley" title="Molly Weasley">Molly Weasley</a> <span class="template-c">(née Prewett)</span> and the eldest brother of <a href="/wiki/Charles_Weasley" title="Charles Weasley">Charlie</a>, <a href="/wiki/Percy_Weasley" title="Percy Weasley">Percy</a>, <a href="/wiki/Fred_Weasley" title="Fred Weasley">Fred</a>, <a href="/wiki/George_Weasley" title="George Weasley">George</a>, <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ron</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Ginevra_Weasley" title="Ginevra Weasley">Ginny</a>. He attended <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> from <a href="/wiki/1982" title="1982">1982</a>-<a href="/wiki/1989" title="1989">1989</a> and was both a <a href="/wiki/Prefect" title="Prefect">prefect</a> and <a href="/wiki/Head_Boy_or_Girl" title="Head Boy or Girl">Head Boy</a>. 
</p><p>After graduating from Hogwarts he went to work for <a href="/wiki/Gringotts_Wizarding_Bank" title="Gringotts Wizarding Bank">Gringotts Wizarding Bank</a> as a <a href="/wiki/Curse-Breaker" title="Curse-Breaker">Curse-Breaker</a>, working in <a href="/wiki/Egypt" title="Egypt">Egypt</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-cos12_10-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-cos12-10">&#91;10&#93;</a></sup> In 1995, he returned to England and joined the reconstituted <a href="/wiki/Order_of_the_Phoenix" title="Order of the Phoenix">Order of the Phoenix</a> to face the reborn <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP4_11-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP4-11">&#91;11&#93;</a></sup> During this time, he started courting <a href="/wiki/Fleur_Delacour" title="Fleur Delacour">Fleur Delacour</a> and eventually asked her to marry him after a year of dating.
</p><p>During the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_the_Astronomy_Tower" title="Battle of the Astronomy Tower">Battle of the Astronomy Tower</a>, Bill was severely injured by the <a href="/wiki/Werewolf" title="Werewolf">werewolf</a> <a href="/wiki/Fenrir_Greyback" title="Fenrir Greyback">Fenrir Greyback</a>. Although he wasn't turned into a werewolf, he did develop some wolfish tendencies such as a liking for very rare steaks. He <a href="/wiki/Wedding_of_William_Weasley_and_Fleur_Delacour" title="Wedding of William Weasley and Fleur Delacour">married</a> Fleur on 1 August 1997, although their wedding was disrupted by the arrival of <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a> following the fall of the <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">Ministry of Magic</a>. Bill and Fleur then moved to <a href="/wiki/Shell_Cottage" title="Shell Cottage">Shell Cottage</a>, where they assisted <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry</a>, Ron, and <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione</a> after their escape from <a href="/wiki/Malfoy_Manor" title="Malfoy Manor">Malfoy Manor</a>. Bill later fought in the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a> alongside the rest of the Order and survived.
</p><p>Following the war, Bill and Fleur had three children: <a href="/wiki/Victoire_Weasley" title="Victoire Weasley">Victoire</a>, <a href="/wiki/Dominique_Weasley" title="Dominique Weasley">Dominique</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Louis_Weasley" title="Louis Weasley">Louis Weasley</a>.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/William_Weasley?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/William_Weasley?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>

Burrow</a>, where Bill grew up with his family</p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure><p>William Weasley was the first of seven children born to <a href="/wiki/Arthur_Weasley" title="Arthur Weasley">Arthur</a> and <a href="/wiki/Molly_Weasley" title="Molly Weasley">Molly Weasley</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-COS4_12-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COS4-12">&#91;12&#93;</a></sup> He was a child during the height of the <a href="/wiki/First_Wizarding_War" title="First Wizarding War">First Wizarding War</a>, in which, his maternal uncles, <a href="/wiki/Fabian_Prewett" title="Fabian Prewett">Fabian</a> and <a href="/wiki/Gideon_Prewett" title="Gideon Prewett">Gideon Prewett</a>, were murdered fighting <a href="/wiki/Antonin_Dolohov" title="Antonin Dolohov">Antonin Dolohov</a> and four other <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-13" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-13">&#91;13&#93;</a></sup> While he was still a child, the <a href="/wiki/Wizarding_world" title="Wizarding world">Wizarding world</a> became peaceful again (for a time) after <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a>'s first defeat on <a href="/wiki/31_October" title="31 October">31 October</a> <a href="/wiki/1981" title="1981">1981</a>.
</p><p>Bill and his five brothers — Charlie, Percy, Fred, George, and Ron — as well as his younger sister, Ginny,<sup id="cite_ref-PS6_14-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PS6-14">&#91;14&#93;</a></sup> grew up in the Burrow on the outskirts of <a href="/wiki/Ottery_St_Catchpole" title="Ottery St Catchpole">Ottery St Catchpole</a> in <a href="/wiki/Devon" title="Devon">Devon</a>, <a href="/wiki/England" title="England">England</a>. Like all his siblings, he was home educated in reading, writing, and simple maths by his mother. The Weasley family was not wealthy compared to other wizarding families. Many other <a href="/wiki/Pure-blood" title="Pure-blood">pure-blood</a> families, particularly the <a href="/wiki/Malfoy_family" title="Malfoy family">Malfoys</a>, disdained them for their "<i>blood traitor</i>", pro-muggle beliefs and their lack of wealth.<sup id="cite_ref-15" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-15">&#91;15&#93;</a></sup> They tried their best to make up for this with their <a href="/wiki/Love" title="Love">love</a>. It can be assumed that Bill had a fairly happy childhood, as all the Weasley siblings did.
</p>
<h3><span id="Hogwarts_years_(1982-1989)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="Hogwarts_years_.281982-1989.29">Hogwarts years <span class="template-c">(1982-1989)</span></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/William_Weasley?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Hogwarts years (1982-1989)">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/William_Weasley?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Hogwarts years (1982-1989)"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_years">Early years</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/William_Weasley?veaction=edit&amp;section=4" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Early years">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/William_Weasley?action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Early years"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<p>Bill arrived at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> on September 1, 1982. Among his fellow first years were, presumably, the future <a href="/wiki/Weird_Sisters" title="Weird Sisters">Weird Sisters</a> members <a href="/wiki/Kirley_Duke" title="Kirley Duke">Kirley Duke</a> and <a href="/wiki/Myron_Wagtail" title="Myron Wagtail">Myron Wagtail</a>. Sorted into <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor" title="Gryffindor">Gryffindor House</a>, Bill quickly proved himself an academically strong and popular student. Apparently his best subject was <a href="/wiki/Defence_Against_the_Dark_Arts" title="Defence Against the Dark Arts">Defence Against the Dark Arts</a>.
</p><p>At some point, either shortly before or just after he began attending school, an older student by the name of <a href="/wiki/Jacob_(Hogwarts_student)" title="Jacob (Hogwarts student)">Jacob</a> was <a href="/wiki/Discipline_at_Hogwarts#Expulsion" title="Discipline at Hogwarts">expelled</a> for endangering the school in his search of the fabled <a href="/wiki/Cursed_Vaults" title="Cursed Vaults">Cursed Vaults</a>, which was announced in the <a href="/wiki/Daily_Prophet" title="Daily Prophet">Daily Prophet</a> and swiftly became the talk of the <a href="/wiki/The_Burrow" title="The Burrow">Weasley household</a>, which made Bill sympathise with the hardships of their younger sibling, who began attending school in his third year and were met with much whispering of Jacob's plight.<sup id="cite_ref-y2ch6_16-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-y2ch6-16">&#91;16&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Third_year">Third year</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/William_Weasley?veaction=edit&amp;section=5" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Third year">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/William_Weasley?action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Third year"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<p>In the <a href="/wiki/1984%E2%80%931985_school_year" title="1984–1985 school year">1984–1985 school year</a>, Bill took the following as electives: <a href="/wiki/Muggle_Studies" title="Muggle Studies">Muggle Studies</a>, <a href="/wiki/Care_of_Magical_Creatures" title="Care of Magical Creatures">Care of Magical Creatures</a>, <a href="/wiki/Divination" title="Divination">Divination</a>, <a href="/wiki/Arithmancy" title="Arithmancy">Arithmancy</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Study_of_Ancient_Runes" title="Study of Ancient Runes">Study of Ancient Runes</a>. It can be assumed that he achieved this through the use of a <a href="/wiki/Time-turner" class="mw-redirect" title="Time-turner">time-turner</a>, as some of the classes are in attendance at the same time. During the same school year, Bill learned that <a href="/wiki/Jacob%27s_sibling" title="Jacob&#39;s sibling">Jacob's sibling</a> had been involved in their own search for the <a href="/wiki/Cursed_Vaults" title="Cursed Vaults">Cursed Vaults</a>, leading to them entering a locked room on the fifth floor along with some fellow first-years containing <a href="/wiki/Cursed_ice" title="Cursed ice">cursed ice</a>, which, as word spread quickly at Hogwarts, soon became common knowledge among the students.
</p>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Fourth_year">Fourth year</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/William_Weasley?veaction=edit&amp;section=6" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Fourth year">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/William_Weasley?action=edit&amp;section=6" title="Edit section: Fourth year"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>

Usually found practising defensive spells in the <a href="/wiki/Training_Grounds" title="Training Grounds">Training Grounds</a>, he was approached by Jacob's sibling, who asked him for help investigating the Cursed Vaults.<sup id="cite_ref-y2ch5_17-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-y2ch5-17">&#91;17&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>To this, Bill heartily agreed, voicing the opinion that the cursed ice that had begun to spread throughout the school because someone had been meddling with one or more of said vaults, after all, were posing a threat to the entire school and he would gladly do whatever he could to help resolve the dire situation, expressing excitement at the prospect of his first "curse-breaking adventure". In preparation for entering the vault, Bill taught Jacob's sibling the <a href="/wiki/Fire-Making_Spell" title="Fire-Making Spell">Fire-Making Spell</a>, practised duelling and studied all known information about the vaults, including reviewing the old articles concerning Jacob and his expulsion. After these things were taken care of, Bill met up with the younger student to enter the vault, insisted that he was excited to finally have an opportunity to act as a Curse-Breaker as opposed to merely talking about becoming one.<sup id="cite_ref-y2ch6_16-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-y2ch6-16">&#91;16&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Once they entered the ice-covered outer hall leading into the vault itself, Bill marvelled at the complexity of magic required to build such a place. Finding their path blocked by massive blocks of cursed ice reaching all the way up to the high, arching ceiling. The pair had to get past these obstacles, which Jacob's sibling speculated might be additional defences put in place to prevent people from reaching the vault, by working together to melt them down with flames from their wands. Once they reached the door, however, Bill, mindful of the details he had received of what happened to Rowan, insisted they would have to "<i>attack the door before it had a chance to attack them</i>".<sup id="cite_ref-18" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-18">&#91;18&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Trying to force their way through the door the same way they had done to break through the cursed ice, Bill was caught off guard when it countered the flames and shot a jet of icy light at him without warning, encasing him in a block of cursed ice and restraining his arms and feet. Jacob's sibling was concerned that they might accidentally hurt Bill if they tried to melt the ice surrounding him. First meaning to run for help, the aforementioned individual finally made their best effort to carefully cast the Fire-Making Spell on the ice, freeing him. Bill informed them of how, while he had suffered no physical injury, it felt as if the cursed ice had somehow drained him of energy, and Jacob's sibling agreed it would be best to leave.
</p><p>Although he spent some time recovering from his injuries at the <a href="/wiki/Hospital_Wing" title="Hospital Wing">Hospital Wing</a>, Bill was quick to dismiss the concerns of Jacob's sibling, telling them how he had never felt as alive before as when the two had tried to break into the vault, eager to give it another try, though wisely suggesting they brought some more people into their second attempt, to which Jacob's sibling voiced their intention of enlisting the aid of Rowan, <a href="/wiki/Ben_Copper" title="Ben Copper">Ben Copper</a> and <a href="/wiki/Penny_Haywood" title="Penny Haywood">Penny Haywood</a>, all of whom Bill urged his fellow pupil to arm with the knowledge and skills necessary to use the Fire-Making Spell as well, and Jacob's sibling also got the idea of helping Ben make some <a href="/wiki/Burn-Healing_Paste" title="Burn-Healing Paste">Burn-Healing Paste</a>, prompting Jacob's sibling to take the nervously inclined <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor" title="Gryffindor">Gryffindor</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Potions_Classroom" title="Potions Classroom">Potions Classroom</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-19" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-19">&#91;19&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p><a href="/wiki/Minerva_McGonagall" title="Minerva McGonagall">Professor McGonagall</a> got furious at Bill for teaching the Charm, and he thought he would not be appointed Prefect later.<sup id="cite_ref-20" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-20">&#91;20&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Final_years">Final years</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/William_Weasley?veaction=edit&amp;section=7" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Final years">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/William_Weasley?action=edit&amp;section=7" title="Edit section: Final years"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<p>In the <a href="/wiki/1986%E2%80%931987_school_year" title="1986–1987 school year">1986–1987 school year</a>, Bill became a <a href="/wiki/Prefect" title="Prefect">prefect</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-21" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-21">&#91;21&#93;</a></sup> and helped Jacob's sibling, who was helping <a href="/wiki/Rubeus_Hagrid" title="Rubeus Hagrid">Rubeus Hagrid</a>, find <a href="/wiki/Fang" title="Fang">Fang</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Library" title="Hogwarts Library">Hogwarts Library</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-22" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-22">&#91;22&#93;</a></sup> He had a crush on <a href="/wiki/Emily_Tyler" title="Emily Tyler">Emily Tyler</a> and sought advice on romance from Jacob's sibling.<sup id="cite_ref-23" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-23">&#91;23&#93;</a></sup> He obtained twelve <a href="/wiki/Ordinary_Wizarding_Level" title="Ordinary Wizarding Level">O.W.Ls</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-COS4_12-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COS4-12">&#91;12&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>In his seventh year, Bill was made <a href="/wiki/Head_Boy_or_Girl" title="Head Boy or Girl">Head Boy</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-PS6_14-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PS6-14">&#91;14&#93;</a></sup> At some point, Bill had a pen-friend at <a href="/wiki/Castelobruxo" title="Castelobruxo">Castelobruxo</a>, the wizarding school in Brazil. When his pen-friend wanted him to go on exchange trip, Bill had to decline because his parents could not afford it. His pen-friend, though, was offended and sent him a cursed hat that made his ears shrivel up.<sup id="cite_ref-24" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-24">&#91;24&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>At some time prior to his graduation, Bill was given a "Medal of Magical Merit"<sup id="cite_ref-25" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-25">&#91;25&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span id="Working_for_Gringotts_(1989-1995)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="Working_for_Gringotts_.281989-1995.29">Working for Gringotts <span class="template-c">(1989-1995)</span></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/William_Weasley?veaction=edit&amp;section=8" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Working for Gringotts (1989-1995)">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/William_Weasley?action=edit&amp;section=8" title="Edit section: Working for Gringotts (1989-1995)"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>

<p>After graduating from Hogwarts, Bill was ultimately managed to live up to his life-long aspirations of joining <a href="/wiki/Gringotts_Wizarding_Bank" title="Gringotts Wizarding Bank">Gringotts Wizarding Bank</a>. Having successfully found an opening for Curse-Breakers, applied and been accepted, Bill threw himself into his work, pouring all of his energies into his curse-breaking missions not to think too much about the betrayal of <a href="/wiki/Patricia_Rakepick" title="Patricia Rakepick">Patricia Rakepick</a> at the end of his final term at school. Frequently concerned about the well-being of his friends back at school, not to mention that of his younger brothers <a href="/wiki/Charles_Weasley" title="Charles Weasley">Charlie</a>, <a href="/wiki/Percy_Weasley" title="Percy Weasley">Percy</a>, <a href="/wiki/Fred_Weasley" title="Fred Weasley">Fred</a> and <a href="/wiki/George_Weasley" title="George Weasley">George</a>, who were all also attending Hogwarts at the time, he eventually contacted <a href="/wiki/Albus_Dumbledore" title="Albus Dumbledore">Albus Dumbledore</a> expressing his concern about the news he had received telling him about the apparent ineffectiveness of Rakepick's successor as Defence Against the Dark Arts Professor, and asked if he might be granted permission to visit the school whenever he had down time in between his missions in order to offer supplementary evening classes on the subject in order to help pupils learn how to defend themselves should Rakepick or other members of <a href="/wiki/R" title="R">R</a> to breach the security of the castle once more. Wholeheartedly agreeing, not only did the <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Headmaster" title="Hogwarts Headmaster">Hogwarts Headmaster</a> permit him to visit the school on a regular basis throughout the <a href="/wiki/1989%E2%80%931990_school_year" title="1989–1990 school year">1989–1990 school year</a>, he even gave him free access to <a href="/wiki/Classroom_3C" title="Classroom 3C">Classroom 3C</a> after regular classes were done for the day.<sup id="cite_ref-:0_26-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:0-26">&#91;26&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Around <a href="/wiki/December" title="December">December</a> of <a href="/wiki/1989" title="1989">1989</a>, Bill was sent to an assignment on some tombs in <a href="/wiki/Egypt" title="Egypt">Egypt</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-:1_27-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:1-27">&#91;27&#93;</a></sup> during which he encountered a <a href="/wiki/Mummy" title="Mummy">Mummy</a> that he successfully defeated by blasting it to dust; a story he later told his younger brother <a href="/wiki/Charles_Weasley" title="Charles Weasley">Charlie</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-28" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-28">&#91;28&#93;</a></sup> On his way to the celebrate <a href="/wiki/Christmas" title="Christmas">Christmas</a> with his family at <a href="/wiki/The_Burrow" title="The Burrow">The Burrow</a>, he stopped by Hogwarts, having received a letter from Dumbledore asking him to stay at the school for "a few days" to help him the magical defences surrounding the school. Afterwards, he <a href="/wiki/Charles_Weasley" title="Charles Weasley">Charlie</a>, <a href="/wiki/Merula_Snyde" title="Merula Snyde">Merula Snyde</a> and <a href="/wiki/Jacob%27s_sibling" title="Jacob&#39;s sibling">another student</a> left for England with their father's <a href="/wiki/Flying_Ford_Anglia" title="Flying Ford Anglia">Flying Ford Anglia</a>, and arrived just in time to celebrate Christmas Eve at the Weasley household.<sup id="cite_ref-:1_27-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:1-27">&#91;27&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>By <a href="/wiki/1_September" title="1 September">September 1</a>, <a href="/wiki/1991" title="1991">1991</a>, however, Bill had gone from went from travelling back and forth between <a href="/wiki/Great_Britain" title="Great Britain">Great Britain</a> and foreign parts for his various missions to being stationed permanently in <a href="/wiki/Egypt" title="Egypt">Egypt</a>, in north-eastern <a href="/wiki/Africa" title="Africa">Africa</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-cos12_10-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-cos12-10">&#91;10&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<p>During the summer of 1993, the whole Weasley family went to Egypt on holiday after his <a href="/wiki/Arthur_Weasley" title="Arthur Weasley">father</a> won seven-hundred galleons in the annual <i><a href="/wiki/Daily_Prophet" title="Daily Prophet">Daily Prophet</a></i> Grand Prize Galleon Draw. Bill took his family on a tour of the Egyptian tombs while they were there.<sup id="cite_ref-29" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-29">&#91;29&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>In the summer of 1994, Bill returned to the Burrow to attend the <a href="/wiki/Quidditch_World_Cup" title="Quidditch World Cup">Quidditch World Cup</a> with his family. During his stay, he met his youngest brother Ron's best friend, <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>, for the first time.<sup id="cite_ref-30" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-30">&#91;30&#93;</a></sup> When the <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a> decided to stir up trouble in the aftermath of the Irish victory, Bill assisted the <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">Ministry of Magic</a>. During the skirmish, Bill was injured with a deep cut on his arm.<sup id="cite_ref-31" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-31">&#91;31&#93;</a></sup> Bill later escorted his siblings, Harry, and <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione</a> to King's Cross station to see them off to Hogwarts.<sup id="cite_ref-32" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-32">&#91;32&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>In 1995, Bill took more time off work to travel to Hogwarts with his mother to support Harry during the Third Task of the <a href="/wiki/Triwizard_Tournament" title="Triwizard Tournament">Triwizard Tournament</a>. It was during this time that he met <a href="/wiki/Fleur_Delacour" title="Fleur Delacour">Fleur Delacour</a>, who was attracted to him from the start.<sup id="cite_ref-33" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-33">&#91;33&#93;</a></sup> Following the death of <a href="/wiki/Cedric_Diggory" title="Cedric Diggory">Cedric Diggory</a> and the revelation that <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a> had returned, Bill immediately set out to inform his father of the news, and that <a href="/wiki/Albus_Dumbledore" title="Albus Dumbledore">Albus Dumbledore</a> would be contacting him.<sup id="cite_ref-34" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-34">&#91;34&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<p>Bill joined the <a href="/wiki/Order_of_the_Phoenix" title="Order of the Phoenix">Order of the Phoenix</a> and applied for a desk job in <a href="/wiki/England" title="England">England</a>, so he could help the Order more effectively. Although he missed the tombs, he did have one compensation — Fleur Delacour had taken a part-time job at <a href="/wiki/Gringotts_Wizarding_Bank" title="Gringotts Wizarding Bank">Gringotts</a> to improve her English, and the two began dating.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP4_11-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP4-11">&#91;11&#93;</a></sup> He was often involved in planning sessions and was used to gauge where the goblins' loyalties lay. During Christmas of 1995, Bill visited his father at <a href="/wiki/St_Mungo%27s_Hospital_for_Magical_Maladies_and_Injuries" title="St Mungo&#39;s Hospital for Magical Maladies and Injuries">St Mungo's Hospital for Magical Maladies and Injuries</a> several times after he had been <a href="/wiki/Attack_on_Arthur_Weasley" title="Attack on Arthur Weasley">attacked</a> by <a href="/wiki/Nagini" title="Nagini">Nagini</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP22_35-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP22-35">&#91;35&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>With the wizarding world at large aware of Lord Voldemort's return in the wake of the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_the_Department_of_Mysteries" title="Battle of the Department of Mysteries">Battle of the Department of Mysteries</a>, Bill asked Fleur to marry him after they had been dating for a year. In the summer of 1996, she stayed at the Burrow for several weeks to get to know Bill's family. However, her blunt nature rubbed <a href="/wiki/Molly_Weasley" title="Molly Weasley">Mrs Weasley</a> and Ginny the wrong way, and they thought Bill could do better than her.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP5_36-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP5-36">&#91;36&#93;</a></sup>

<p>In June 1997, Bill was one of the <a href="/wiki/Order_of_the_Phoenix" title="Order of the Phoenix">Order</a> members tasked by Dumbledore to patrol <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts</a> during his absence when he and Harry went to search for one of Lord Voldemort's <a href="/wiki/Horcrux" title="Horcrux">Horcruxes</a>. That night, <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a> broke into the school with the help of <a href="/wiki/Draco_Malfoy" title="Draco Malfoy">Draco Malfoy</a>, and during the battle that ensued, Bill was badly injured by the werewolf <a href="/wiki/Fenrir_Greyback" title="Fenrir Greyback">Fenrir Greyback</a>. His face was slashed and ripped, and because of the nature of his attacker, the wounds were cursed.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP29_37-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP29-37">&#91;37&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Although Greyback was not transformed at the time, the wounds were contaminated. Bill did not become a <a href="/wiki/Werewolf" title="Werewolf">werewolf</a> but did develop a liking for very rare steaks. It was his injuries that finally convinced his mother that Fleur was the right choice for him. <a href="/wiki/Molly_Weasley" title="Molly Weasley">Mrs Weasley</a> thought their union was based on physical attraction, but Fleur showed that she <a href="/wiki/Love" title="Love">loved</a> him no matter what he looked like, and the two women reconciled over his hospital bed.<sup id="cite_ref-38" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-38">&#91;38&#93;</a></sup> A few days later he attended Dumbledore's funeral, along with his family. <h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Later_life">Later life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/William_Weasley?veaction=edit&amp;section=14" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Later life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/William_Weasley?action=edit&amp;section=14" title="Edit section: Later life"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Following the war, Bill and Fleur had three children, <a href="/wiki/Victoire_Weasley" title="Victoire Weasley">Victoire</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-46" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-46">&#91;46&#93;</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/Dominique_Weasley" title="Dominique Weasley">Dominique</a> and <a href="/wiki/Louis_Weasley" title="Louis Weasley">Louis Weasley</a><sup id="cite_ref-WFT_47-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-WFT-47">&#91;47&#93;</a></sup> Victoire was born on the anniversary of the Battle of Hogwarts, thus her name, which means 'victory' in French. She also seemed to have inherited her mother's looks, as she was noted to be good looking. Victoire also seemed to have a relationship with <a href="/wiki/Edward_Lupin" title="Edward Lupin">Teddy Lupin</a>.
</p><p>As of <a href="/wiki/2013" title="2013">2013</a>, Bill was still employed as a Curse-Breaker for Gringotts and had returned to his work in Egypt.<sup id="cite_ref-48" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-48">&#91;48&#93;</a></sup> In <a href="/wiki/2014" title="2014">2014</a>, Bill attended the <a href="/wiki/2014_Quidditch_World_Cup_final" title="2014 Quidditch World Cup final">2014 Quidditch World Cup final</a> along with the rest of <a href="/wiki/Weasley_family" title="Weasley family">his family</a>. Later that same year, Bill spent his 44th birthday with his wife and the rest of his family.<sup id="cite_ref-49" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-49">&#91;49&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>At some point, Bill had wrote a handbook featuring his experiences with Werewolves, which would be used by members of the <a href="/wiki/Statute_of_Secrecy_Task_Force" title="Statute of Secrecy Task Force">Statute of Secrecy Task Force</a> pursuing an <a href="/wiki/Auror" title="Auror">Auror</a> Profession to increase their methods of overpowering a Werewolf's defences.<sup id="cite_ref-WU_50-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-WU-50">&#91;50&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

He wore clothes that, in <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>'s opinion, would not be out of place at a rock concert, and he favoured boots of <a href="/wiki/Dragon" title="Dragon">dragon</a> hide. He was once called a "<i>long-haired pillock</i>" by <a href="/wiki/Rita_Skeeter" title="Rita Skeeter">Rita Skeeter</a> in an article about the <a href="/wiki/Gringotts_Wizarding_Bank" title="Gringotts Wizarding Bank">Gringotts</a> <a href="/wiki/Curse-Breaker" title="Curse-Breaker">Curse Breakers</a>. Although untransformed at the time, Greyback's <a href="/wiki/Lycanthropy" title="Lycanthropy">lycanthropy</a> caused the wounds to be cursed, and they could not be treated totally, leaving him heavily scarred. At his wedding, however, he looked so happy one could "<i>hardly tell he had ever met Fenrir Greyback</i>."
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Personality_and_traits">Personality and traits</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/William_Weasley?veaction=edit&amp;section=16" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Personality and traits">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/William_Weasley?action=edit&amp;section=16" title="Edit section: Personality and traits"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<div class="quote">
<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince">"<i>Bill's not that down-to-earth. He's a <a href="/wiki/Curse-Breaker" title="Curse-Breaker">Curse-Breaker</a>, isn't he, he likes a bit of adventure, a bit of glamour...</i>"</span></dd>
<dd>&#8212;Description of Bill's personality<sup class="noprint"><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Half-Blood_Prince" title="Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince">[src]</a></sup></dd>
<dd></dd></dl></div>
<figure class="thumb tleft show-info-icon" style="width: 252px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/e/ee/B4C5M1_playing_in_the_garden_at_the_Burrow.png/revision/latest?cb=20161210160707" class="image"><img alt="" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw%3D%3D" decoding="async" width="250" height="91" class="thumbimage lazyload" data-image-name="B4C5M1 playing in the garden at the Burrow.png" data-image-key="B4C5M1_playing_in_the_garden_at_the_Burrow.png" data-src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/e/ee/B4C5M1_playing_in_the_garden_at_the_Burrow.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/250?cb=20161210160707" /></a> 	<noscript><a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/e/ee/B4C5M1_playing_in_the_garden_at_the_Burrow.png/revision/latest?cb=20161210160707" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/e/ee/B4C5M1_playing_in_the_garden_at_the_Burrow.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/250?cb=20161210160707" decoding="async" width="250" height="91" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="B4C5M1 playing in the garden at the Burrow.png" data-image-key="B4C5M1_playing_in_the_garden_at_the_Burrow.png" data-src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/e/ee/B4C5M1_playing_in_the_garden_at_the_Burrow.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/250?cb=20161210160707" /></a></noscript> 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:B4C5M1_playing_in_the_garden_at_the_Burrow.png" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption">Bill and <a href="/wiki/Charles_Weasley" title="Charles Weasley">Charlie</a> jousting with tables at <a href="/wiki/The_Burrow" title="The Burrow">the Burrow</a></p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure>
<p>Bill was laid back and easy going, and described as "<i>cool</i>". However, he had a serious side, being a high academic achiever as a student and being Hogwarts <a href="/wiki/Head_Boy_or_Girl" title="Head Boy or Girl">Head Boy</a> his seventh year. He took his studies seriously, as he referenced <a href="/wiki/History_of_Magic" title="History of Magic">History of Magic</a>, which normally many students would fall asleep due to <a href="/wiki/Cuthbert_Binns" title="Cuthbert Binns">the teacher's</a> dull style of teaching, during his discussion with Harry regarding goblin's ideals of ownership. He was knowledgeable about wizard-goblin relations for his work. Despite being the eldest of seven children, he had a lively sense of humour and knew how to have fun.
</p><p>He was very brave, working as a <a href="/wiki/Curse-Breaker" title="Curse-Breaker">Curse-Breaker</a> and joining the <a href="/wiki/Order_of_the_Phoenix" title="Order of the Phoenix">Order of the Phoenix</a> to fight against <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a>. He is also of great compassion and loyalty, as he was displeased with how Ron would abandon Harry and Hermione over a little argument, but did not pursue the issue seeing Ron's clear regret, and allowed him to stay for the time being. After his mauling at the hands of <a href="/wiki/Fenrir_Greyback" title="Fenrir Greyback">Fenrir Greyback</a>, it was feared that his personality would change and that he would take on some wolfish characteristics. However, Bill's personality remained intact, although he did develop a liking for very rare steaks.
</p><p>When Bill met <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> for the first time in 1994, Harry was taken by surprise at Bill's cool, laid-back demeanour; from everything Harry had heard from Ron, he had always pictured Bill as an older version of <a href="/wiki/Percy_Weasley" title="Percy Weasley">Percy</a>, who was more serious and stuffy than all his siblings.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Magical_abilities_and_skills">Magical abilities and skills</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/William_Weasley?veaction=edit&amp;section=17" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/William_Weasley?action=edit&amp;section=17" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Given how Bill earned twelve <a href="/wiki/Ordinary_Wizarding_Level" title="Ordinary Wizarding Level">O.W.L.s</a> in his <a href="/wiki/Fifth_year" title="Fifth year">fifth year</a>, and subsequently participated in and survived the <a href="/wiki/Second_Wizarding_War" title="Second Wizarding War">Second Wizarding War</a>, he was an accomplished and skilful <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> with many <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magical</a> gifts and talents.
</p>
<ul><li><b><a href="/wiki/Defence_Against_the_Dark_Arts" title="Defence Against the Dark Arts">Defence Against the Dark Arts</a></b>: Bill showed a natural flair for Defence Against the Dark Arts. During his time as a student, he would often be found on the school's <a href="/wiki/Training_Grounds" title="Training Grounds">Training Grounds</a>, practising defensive and offensive <a href="/wiki/Spell" title="Spell">spells</a> on mechanical Death Eater dummies. As a <a href="/wiki/Curse-Breaker" title="Curse-Breaker">Curse Breaker</a>, Bill was naturally skilled in <a href="/wiki/Counter-curse" title="Counter-curse">counter-curses</a>, and his skill was such that both he and <a href="/wiki/Albus_Dumbledore" title="Albus Dumbledore">Albus Dumbledore</a> felt that he had much to teach his former schoolmates after his graduation from school, whom he was permitted to visit in between assignments from Gringotts Wizarding Bank in order to tutor them in powerful defensive charms in order to prepare them for any potential encounters they might have with <a href="/wiki/Patricia_Rakepick" title="Patricia Rakepick">Patricia Rakepick</a> or for other reasons needed to defend themselves. During the <a href="/wiki/1989%E2%80%931990_school_year" title="1989–1990 school year">1989–1990 school year</a>, Bill gave extracurricular evening classes in this subject to Hogwarts students, due to the ineffectiveness of the teaching that year, to great success, showing he was also a very skilled teacher of this field.</li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Arithmancy" title="Arithmancy">Arithmancy</a></b>: Since becoming a Curse Breaker required at least an O.W.L. in Arithmancy, it is reasonable and logical to deduce that Bill was skilled in this subject and that he achieved a high grade in his O.W.L. in order to qualify for his career training.</li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Duelling" title="Duelling">Duelling</a></b>: Bill was shown to be a highly skilful duellist, as evidenced by how, by his fourth year of study at Hogwarts, a fellow fourth year advised <a href="/wiki/Jacob%27s_sibling" title="Jacob&#39;s sibling">Jacob's sibling</a> to seek him out if they sought to learn how to duel, claiming that the prefect-to-be already then was one of the most accomplished duellists at Hogwarts. As an adult, he participated in and survived numerous battles of the <a href="/wiki/Second_Wizarding_War" title="Second Wizarding War">Second Wizarding War</a> — the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_the_Astronomy_Tower" title="Battle of the Astronomy Tower">Battle of the Astronomy Tower</a>, the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_the_Seven_Potters" title="Battle of the Seven Potters">Battle of the Seven Potters</a>, and the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a>, showing his considerable talent and skill in <a href="/wiki/Martial_magic" title="Martial magic">martial magic</a>.</li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Transfiguration" title="Transfiguration">Transfiguration</a></b>: Bill was shown to be proficient in this branch of magic, as he was able to swiftly <a href="/wiki/Tablecloth_conjuring_spell" title="Tablecloth conjuring spell">conjure table cloths</a> out of thin air in <a href="/wiki/1994" title="1994">1994</a> at <a href="/wiki/The_Burrow" title="The Burrow">The Burrow</a>, as well as successfully using the <a href="/wiki/Vanishing_Spell" title="Vanishing Spell">Vanishing Spell</a> to vanish a bundle of scrolls whilst cleaning <a href="/wiki/12_Grimmauld_Place" title="12 Grimmauld Place">12 Grimmauld Place</a> in <a href="/wiki/1995" title="1995">1995</a>. <a href="/wiki/Conjuration" title="Conjuration">Conjuration</a> and <a href="/wiki/Vanishment" title="Vanishment">Vanishment</a> are both very advanced forms of transfiguration, showing his competency in this field.</li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Charm" title="Charm">Charms</a></b>: Bill was shown to be proficient with the casting of charms. He could successfully perform everyday charms such as the <a href="/wiki/Levitation_Charm" title="Levitation Charm">Levitation Charm</a>, <a href="/wiki/Mending_Charm" title="Mending Charm">Mending Charm</a>, <a href="/wiki/Fire-Making_Spell" title="Fire-Making Spell">Fire-Making Spell</a> and <a href="/wiki/Stunning_Spell" title="Stunning Spell">Stunning Spell</a> without difficulty. He could also cast <a href="/wiki/Defensive_Charm" title="Defensive Charm">Defensive Charms</a> like the <a href="/wiki/Imperturbable_Charm" title="Imperturbable Charm">Imperturbable Charm</a> and <i><a href="/wiki/Cave_inimicum" title="Cave inimicum">Cave inimicum</a></i>, and teach the casting of them to others. He was even capable of producing a non-corporeal <a href="/wiki/Patronus_Charm" title="Patronus Charm">Patronus</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-LEGOY2_7-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-LEGOY2-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup> a mark of superior <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magical</a> ability and talent, given the immense difficulty of casting such an advanced and powerful protective charm.</li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Care_of_Magical_Creatures" title="Care of Magical Creatures">Care of Magical Creatures</a></b>: As he worked for Gringotts as a Curse-Breaker, Bill was very skilful in interacting with <a href="/wiki/Goblin" title="Goblin">Goblins</a> and other <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magical</a> <a href="/wiki/Creature" title="Creature">creatures</a>, such as <a href="/wiki/Niffler" title="Niffler">Nifflers</a>. He was knowledgeable about wizard-goblin relations for his work, during which he used to gauge where the goblins' loyalties lay, and when he realised that Harry was planning something with Griphook during his hunt for <a href="/wiki/Helga_Hufflepuff%27s_Cup" class="mw-redirect" title="Helga Hufflepuff&#39;s Cup">Helga Hufflepuff's Cup</a>, he counselled Harry on the way goblins perceived things and warned him not to betray Griphook. He stated that by goblin standards, the maker of an item such as <a href="/wiki/Godric_Gryffindor%27s_Sword" class="mw-redirect" title="Godric Gryffindor&#39;s Sword">Godric Gryffindor's Sword</a>, not the purchaser, was the rightful owner and that the item is required to be returned to its maker after the death of the purchaser.</li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/History_of_Magic" title="History of Magic">History of Magic</a></b>: Bill was very knowledgeable about magical history, which normally was considered a boring subject by many students, as he referenced it during his discussion with Harry regarding Goblin's ideals of ownership, showing he had a strong understanding of Goblin history.</li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Spell_creation" title="Spell creation">Spell creation</a></b>: Bill accomplished the advanced <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magical</a> art of inventing new <a href="/wiki/Spell" title="Spell">spells</a>, having created a <a href="/wiki/William_Weasley%27s_modified_cursed_barrier" title="William Weasley&#39;s modified cursed barrier">modified cursed barrier</a> to prevent <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a> from entering a vicinity.</li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Dark_Arts" title="Dark Arts">Dark Arts</a></b>: Bill was surprisingly skilled with dark magic, as he successfully invented his own <a href="/wiki/Curse" title="Curse">curse</a>, a potent <a href="/wiki/Dark_charm" title="Dark charm">dark charm</a> of the highest severity. His <a href="/wiki/William_Weasley%27s_modified_cursed_barrier" title="William Weasley&#39;s modified cursed barrier">modified cursed barrier</a> was powerful enough to prevent <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a> from penetrating it, which is very impressive dark magic. He was also capable enough with <a href="/wiki/Dark_charm" title="Dark charm">dark charms</a> that he was able to teach students in his extra-curricular Defence Against the Dark Arts classes how to perform the <a href="/wiki/Ear-shrivelling_Curse" class="mw-redirect" title="Ear-shrivelling Curse">Ear-shrivelling Curse</a>, <i><a href="/wiki/Langlock" title="Langlock">Langlock</a></i> <a href="/wiki/Jinx" title="Jinx">jinx</a> and the <a href="/wiki/Sea_Urchin_Jinx" title="Sea Urchin Jinx">Sea Urchin Jinx</a> successfully.</li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Divination" title="Divination">Divination</a></b>: Bill was skilled and knowledgeable in this field, as he gained a high grade O.W.L. in the <a href="/wiki/Divination_(class)" title="Divination (class)">subject</a> at Hogwarts.</li></ul>

<ul><li><b><a href="/wiki/William_Weasley%27s_wand" title="William Weasley&#39;s wand">Wand</a></b>: The wand of Bill Weasley was of unknown length, <a href="/wiki/Wand_wood" title="Wand wood">wood</a> and <a href="/wiki/Wand_core" title="Wand core">core</a> materials. Like most wizards and witches in Great Britain, he likely purchased it at the age of eleven at <a href="/wiki/Ollivanders" title="Ollivanders">Ollivanders</a> before beginning his education at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a>.</li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Prefect_badge" title="Prefect badge">Prefect badge</a></b>: Having been made prefect in his <a href="/wiki/Fifth_year" title="Fifth year">fifth year</a> at Hogwarts, Bill owned a Gryffindor prefect badge, which he wore during his formal prefect duties.</li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Head_Boy_badge" title="Head Boy badge">Head Boy badge</a></b>: Bill was awarded one of these special badges when he was made <a href="/wiki/Head_Boy_or_Girl" title="Head Boy or Girl">Head Boy</a> at Hogwarts, for which he would have worn during his formal duties.</li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Shell_Cottage" title="Shell Cottage">Shell Cottage</a></b>: Bill purchased and became the owner of this beachfront cottage in <a href="/wiki/Tinworth" title="Tinworth">Tinworth</a>, <a href="/wiki/Cornwall" title="Cornwall">Cornwall</a>, where he lived with his wife <a href="/wiki/Fleur_Delacour" title="Fleur Delacour">Fleur Delacour</a>, and also used his house as a safe house for the <a href="/wiki/Order_of_the_Phoenix" title="Order of the Phoenix">Order of the Phoenix</a>.</li></ul>

<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Weasley_family">Weasley family</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/William_Weasley?veaction=edit&amp;section=21" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Weasley family">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/William_Weasley?action=edit&amp;section=21" title="Edit section: Weasley family"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>

<p>Bill had a good relationship with all his family members but seemed to be particularly close to <a href="/wiki/Charles_Weasley" title="Charles Weasley">Charlie</a>, the sibling closest to him in age and the one he chose as best man at his wedding.
</p><p>Bill's younger siblings tended to look up to him, especially his only sister, <a href="/wiki/Ginevra_Weasley" title="Ginevra Weasley">Ginny</a>. She supported Bill whenever their mother criticised his choice of hair and clothing, and a large part of her initial disdain for <a href="/wiki/Fleur_Delacour" title="Fleur Delacour">Fleur Delacour</a> was her belief that the witch was not good enough for her brother. It is unclear whether Ginny ever made her dislike for Fleur known to Bill, but she eventually accepted their relationship. Ginny also tended to take Bill's opinion very seriously; when she, Harry, Ron, and Hermione were discussing their hatred of Snape, she mentioned "<i>Bill doesn't like him either</i>," as if her older brother's opinion should settle it. 
</p><p><a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ron</a> also admired Bill, partly because he was the only one of his siblings who did not tease him. Towards the end of <a href="/wiki/1997" title="1997">1997</a>, when Ron temporarily abandoned <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry</a> and <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione</a> during their search for Voldemort's <a href="/wiki/Horcrux" title="Horcrux">Horcruxes</a>, he stayed with Bill and Fleur at <a href="/wiki/Shell_Cottage" title="Shell Cottage">Shell Cottage</a>. Although Bill was not impressed by his brother's actions, he realised how sorry Ron was about it and never mentioned it. 
</p><p>Normally, <a href="/wiki/Percy_Weasley" title="Percy Weasley">Percy</a> did look up to Bill, but when Percy had a falling out with his family due to conflict of belief over the return of Lord Voldemort, Percy cut all ties with his family, Bill included, and was the only sibling not to attend Bill's wedding despite being issued an invitation.
</p><p>Bill also got along well with his parents, though <a href="/wiki/Molly_Weasley" title="Molly Weasley">his mother</a> constantly tried to pressure him into cutting his hair and not dressing as flashily as he liked to. She also disapproved of his choice of wife initially, though she came to accept Fleur once she realised how in love the couple was and their liking of one another was not based on looks as she had first believed.
</p><p>Bill fought in the Battle of Hogwarts in <a href="/wiki/1998" title="1998">1998</a> with the rest of his family. He was devastated by his younger brother Fred's death and was seen comforting his wife Fleur in the Great Hall.
</p><p>Bill also became the uncle to many Weasleys, through his brothers. He also had two Potter nephews and one Potter niece, through Ginny's marriage to <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>, Bill's brother-in-law.
</p>

<p>Bill met <a href="/wiki/Fleur_Delacour" title="Fleur Delacour">Fleur Delacour</a> in June <a href="/wiki/1995" title="1995">1995</a>, when he accompanied his mother to Hogwarts to support Harry Potter during the Third Task of the <a href="/wiki/Triwizard_Tournament" title="Triwizard Tournament">Triwizard Tournament</a>, in which she was also a Champion. Fleur was attracted to him immediately, eyeing him from a distance.
</p><p>Shortly after, Bill moved back to England to work for the <a href="/wiki/Order_of_the_Phoenix" title="Order of the Phoenix">Order of the Phoenix</a>, taking a desk job at <a href="/wiki/Gringotts_Wizarding_Bank" title="Gringotts Wizarding Bank">Gringotts</a>. Fleur had taken a part-time job at the bank to improve her English, and the two began dating shortly after. After a year together Bill proposed and Fleur accepted. His <a href="/wiki/Molly_Weasley" title="Molly Weasley">mother</a> felt it was too soon for him to get married and worried that he was only doing it in response to the return of <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a>. In addition, Fleur's blunt attitude and tendency to speak her mind irritated both Molly and Ginny. Mrs Weasley became convinced that their feelings for each other were based on physical attraction and the adventurous side of each other — him as a Curse Breaker, and her as a <a href="/wiki/Triwizard_Tournament" title="Triwizard Tournament">Triwizard Champion</a>.  
</p><p>Following Bill's scarring during the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_the_Astronomy_Tower" title="Battle of the Astronomy Tower">Battle of the Astronomy Tower</a>, Fleur showed her feelings for Bill by declaring that she still <a href="/wiki/Love" title="Love">loved</a> him no matter what he looked like, and her insistence that she would take care of his injuries. Her declaration convinced Mrs Weasley that her son had made the right choice, and the two women finally put aside their differences. Bill and Fleur married on <a href="/wiki/1_August" title="1 August">1 August</a> <a href="/wiki/1997" title="1997">1997</a> and set up a home of their own, <a href="/wiki/Shell_Cottage" title="Shell Cottage">Shell Cottage</a>.  
</p><p>Their union produced three children: <a href="/wiki/Victoire_Weasley" title="Victoire Weasley">Victoire</a>, <a href="/wiki/Dominique_Weasley" title="Dominique Weasley">Dominique</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Louis_Weasley" title="Louis Weasley">Louis</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-WFT_47-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-WFT-47">&#91;47&#93;</a></sup> Due to Fleur's declaration that she didn't care about his looks due to his scarring by Greyback, it served to indicate that she was attracted to Bill's courage, intelligence, and adventurous personality, as well as his interest in other cultures, which likely served to bridge the cultural gap between them. 
</p>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Children">Children</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/William_Weasley?veaction=edit&amp;section=23" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Children">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/William_Weasley?action=edit&amp;section=23" title="Edit section: Children"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<p>After the Second Wizarding War, Bill and Fleur had three children; <a href="/wiki/Victoire_Weasley" title="Victoire Weasley">Victoire</a>, <a href="/wiki/Dominique_Weasley" title="Dominique Weasley">Dominique</a> and <a href="/wiki/Louis_Weasley" title="Louis Weasley">Louis</a>. It is likely he had a good relationship with all of them. In 2017, Victoire was seen snogging <a href="/wiki/Edward_Lupin" title="Edward Lupin">Teddy Lupin</a>. Bill did develop a rather protective streak in regards to his eldest daughter, as he tactically interrupted her and Teddy's conversation during the Quidditch World Cup of 2014, much to the dismay of the teenagers' budding romance. 
</p>

<p>Bill first met Harry Potter in 1994, around the time of the <a href="/wiki/Quidditch_World_Cup" title="Quidditch World Cup">Quidditch World Cup</a>. Harry found Bill cool, but he expected Bill to be like an older version of <a href="/wiki/Percy_Weasley" title="Percy Weasley">Percy Weasley</a> and was surprised and grateful to learn that he was not. Harry was Ron's best friend and Bill was sometimes like an older brother for Harry, as well.
</p><p>Bill and his mother visited Hogwarts in 1995, during the <a href="/wiki/Triwizard_Tournament" title="Triwizard Tournament">Triwizard Tournament</a>, at the time Harry was taking part in his third task. Harry was very glad to see them because he did not expect anyone to come to watch him. Bill also stuck by Harry's side in the <a href="/wiki/Hospital_Wing" title="Hospital Wing">Hospital Wing</a> after the Third Task and "<i>Harry saw Mrs Weasley, Bill, Ron, and Hermione grouped around a harassed-looking Madam Pomfrey. They appeared to be demanding to know where Harry was and what had happened to him</i>", showing that he cared for Harry's well being.
</p><p>Bill and Harry fought together in the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_the_Astronomy_Tower" title="Battle of the Astronomy Tower">Battle of the Astronomy Tower</a>. After the battle, Harry was shocked when he learned about Bill's injuries from <a href="/wiki/Fenrir_Greyback" title="Fenrir Greyback">Fenrir Greyback</a>. He participated in Bill and Fleur's wedding and later stayed for a short time at their house, <a href="/wiki/Shell_Cottage" title="Shell Cottage">Shell Cottage</a>. Bill also advised Harry not to attempt to deceive goblins. They also fought together in the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a> and survived the war.
</p><p>After the <a href="/wiki/Second_Wizarding_War" title="Second Wizarding War">Second Wizarding War</a>, they became brothers-in-law. Harry married Bill's younger sister (Ginny Weasley). Bill also had two nephews (<a href="/wiki/James_Potter_II" title="James Potter II">James Sirius</a> and <a href="/wiki/Albus_Potter" title="Albus Potter">Albus Severus</a>) and one niece (<a href="/wiki/Lily_L._Potter" title="Lily L. Potter">Lily Luna</a>) through Harry's marriage to Ginny.
</p>

<p>Bill met Hermione Granger in the summer of 1994, prior to Hermione's fourth year at Hogwarts. Although they were not very close, Hermione seemed to like Bill and the fact that he was a top student, which she heard from Ron and Percy. Hermione fought alongside Bill at the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_the_Astronomy_Tower" title="Battle of the Astronomy Tower">Battle of the Astronomy Tower</a>; after the battle, Hermione visited Bill at Hogwarts' hospital wing, while she, members of the <a href="/wiki/Order_of_the_Phoenix" title="Order of the Phoenix">Order of the Phoenix</a> and others discussed Dumbledore's death at the hands of <a href="/wiki/Severus_Snape" title="Severus Snape">Severus Snape</a>. Hermione participated at the wedding of Bill and Fleur, and she later stayed for a short time at their house. Bill was upset when he heard that Hermione had been tortured by <a href="/wiki/Bellatrix_Lestrange" title="Bellatrix Lestrange">Bellatrix Lestrange</a>. 
</p><p>After the Second Wizarding War, Hermione became Bill's sister-in-law, as she married his youngest brother (Ron Weasley). Bill also had a niece (<a href="/wiki/Rose_Weasley" class="mw-redirect" title="Rose Weasley">Rose Granger-Weasley</a>) and a nephew (<a href="/wiki/Hugo_Weasley" class="mw-redirect" title="Hugo Weasley">Hugo Granger-Weasley</a>) through Hermione's marriage to Ron.
</p>

He possibly had a good relationship with them, too.

<p>Bill became a member of the Order of the Phoenix in <a href="/wiki/1995" title="1995">1995</a>, after Voldemort's return. He had a good relationship with the most fellow members. He seemed to like <a href="/wiki/Albus_Dumbledore" title="Albus Dumbledore">Albus Dumbledore</a>, who was his headmaster at the time Bill attended Hogwarts. Bill was loyal to Dumbledore, and fought for him at the <a href="/wiki/Second_Wizarding_War" title="Second Wizarding War">Second Wizarding War</a>, after Dumbledore's death.
</p><p>He seemed to be a friend of <a href="/wiki/Remus_Lupin" title="Remus Lupin">Remus Lupin</a>, as they fought together in several battles of the Second War. After the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_the_Seven_Potters" title="Battle of the Seven Potters">Battle of the Seven Potters</a>, they both left the Burrow in order to search for <a href="/wiki/Alastor_Moody" title="Alastor Moody">Mad-Eye Moody</a>'s dead body. When Lupin was killed during the Battle of Hogwarts, his body was put near Fred's. It is possible that Bill cried for Lupin, too.
</p><p>He was also friendly with <a href="/wiki/Alastor_Moody" title="Alastor Moody">Alastor Moody</a>. In <a href="/wiki/1997" title="1997">1997</a>, Moody was the leader of a group that escorted Harry Potter to a safe place. However, Moody was killed. Bill, along with Fleur, was the one to witness his friend's death by <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a> and tell about it to the others, as well.
</p><p>Bill also appeared to be on good terms with <a href="/wiki/Nymphadora_Tonks" title="Nymphadora Tonks">Nymphadora Tonks</a>. Bill and Tonks fought together in many battles and were close in age; Bill was also one of the first to learn about her childbirth.
</p><p>He was also friendly with <a href="/wiki/Kingsley_Shacklebolt" title="Kingsley Shacklebolt">Kingsley Shacklebolt</a>. They fought together at many battles, such as the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_the_Seven_Potters" title="Battle of the Seven Potters">Battle of the Seven Potters</a> and the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a>. They both survived the war; Kingsley became <a href="/wiki/Minister_for_Magic" title="Minister for Magic">Minister for Magic</a>.
</p><p><a href="/wiki/Minerva_McGonagall" title="Minerva McGonagall">Minerva McGonagall</a> appeared to be on good terms with Bill's family. She was Bill's head of house and Transfiguration professor. They both participated at the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_the_Astronomy_Tower" title="Battle of the Astronomy Tower">Battle of the Astronomy Tower</a> and, after the battle, she visited Bill at the hospital, and they discussed Dumbledore and Snape.
</p><p>It was stated by <a href="/wiki/Ginevra_Weasley" title="Ginevra Weasley">Ginny Weasley</a> that Bill did not like <a href="/wiki/Severus_Snape" title="Severus Snape">Severus Snape</a>. After Snape killed Albus Dumbledore, Bill was angry with Snape; however, he learned that Snape was on their side and that Dumbledore had asked Snape to kill him. It is unknown how he reacted.
</p><p>Though most members of the <a href="/wiki/Order_of_the_Phoenix" title="Order of the Phoenix">Order of the Phoenix</a> were killed by <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a>. Bill and most of his family were among the few others who managed to survive.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/William_Weasley?veaction=edit&amp;section=29" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/William_Weasley?action=edit&amp;section=29" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The name "William" comes from the Germanic name Wilhelm, which was composed of the elements wil "will, desire" and helm "helmet, protection".
</p>

<li>Bill was omitted from the film adaptations of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Goblet_of_Fire_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire (film)">Goblet of Fire</a></i>, <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Order_of_the_Phoenix_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix (film)">Order of the Phoenix</a></i> and <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Half-Blood_Prince_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince (film)">Half-Blood Prince</a></i>, but appeared in the adaptations of <i>The Deathly Hallows</i>.</li>
<li>The first time Bill meets Harry Potter in the books is in the <i><a href="/wiki/Goblet_of_Fire" title="Goblet of Fire">Goblet of Fire</a></i>, but in the movies he meets him in the film adaptation of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Deathly_Hallows:_Part_1" title="Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 1">Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 1</a></i>.</li>
<li>Despite Bill not being attacked or even appearing in the film adaptation of <i>Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince</i>, it is simply explained in <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Deathly_Hallows:_Part_1" title="Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 1">Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 1</a></i> that he was attacked by a <a href="/wiki/Werewolf" title="Werewolf">werewolf</a> named <a href="/wiki/Fenrir_Greyback" title="Fenrir Greyback">Fenrir Greyback</a>.</li>
<li>Domhnall Gleeson's favourite line as Bill was "Mad-Eye's dead," as his father, Brendan Gleeson, portrays Mad-Eye Moody.<sup id="cite_ref-51" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-51">&#91;51&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li>Coincidentally, <a href="/wiki/Domhnall_Gleeson" title="Domhnall Gleeson">Domhnall Gleeson</a> portrays Bill in the Harry Potter series, as he is described as having a close resemblance to Mad-Eye Moody (due to the deep scars inflicted to his face by <a href="/wiki/Fenrir_Greyback" title="Fenrir Greyback">Fenrir Greyback</a>), who is played by Domhnall's father <a href="/wiki/Brendan_Gleeson" title="Brendan Gleeson">Brendan</a> in the film adaptations. Following Fenrir's attack, Bill is described as follows: "His scars were as bad as ever; in truth, he now bore a distinct resemblance to Mad-Eye Moody, though thankfully with both eyes and legs still intact."</li></ul>
