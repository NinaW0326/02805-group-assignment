<b>Prudence </b>(fl. <a href="/wiki/1269" class="mw-redirect" title="1269">1269</a>), nicknamed <b>Pru</b> by her sister, was a <a href="/wiki/Scotland" title="Scotland">Scot</a> who lived in <a href="/wiki/Aberdeen" title="Aberdeen">Aberdeen</a>, <a href="/wiki/Scotland" title="Scotland">Scotland</a> during the <a href="/wiki/13th_century" title="13th century">13th century</a>. She was the sister of <a href="/wiki/Modesty_Rabnott" title="Modesty Rabnott">Modesty Rabnott</a>, who she had received a <a href="/wiki/Modesty_Rabnott%27s_letter_to_Prudence" title="Modesty Rabnott&#39;s letter to Prudence">letter from</a> after she lost her home.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<ul><li>It is possible her surname is "Rabnott" since this was her sister's surname and there was no mention of Modesty having a husband come with her. It is possible, however, that Prudence was married.</li>
<li>In 1269, the <a href="/wiki/International_Statute_of_Wizarding_Secrecy" title="International Statute of Wizarding Secrecy">Statute of Secrecy</a> did not exist, meaning that it is possible for Prudence to be a Muggle who knows about magic.</li></ul>
