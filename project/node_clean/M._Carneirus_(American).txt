</p><p><b>M. Carneirus</b> was a business and finance reporter for <i><a href="/wiki/The_New_York_Ghost" title="The New York Ghost">The New York Ghost</a></i> in the <a href="/wiki/1920s" title="1920s">1920s</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-dec1_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-dec1-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-nyghost-dec6_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-nyghost-dec6-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-dec6sunrise_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-dec6sunrise-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-nyghost-dec7_4-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-nyghost-dec7-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> They also sometimes contributed to general news stories.<sup id="cite_ref-nyghost-dec7_4-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-nyghost-dec7-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="History">History</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/M._Carneirus_(American)?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: History">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/M._Carneirus_(American)?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: History"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Business_reporting">Business reporting</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/M._Carneirus_(American)?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Business reporting">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/M._Carneirus_(American)?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Business reporting"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Carneirus wrote an article about the "medical modes" of famous, wealthy people, which was published in the <a href="/wiki/1_December" title="1 December">1 December</a> <a href="/wiki/1926" title="1926">1926</a> issue.<sup id="cite_ref-dec1_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-dec1-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> Five days later, in the <a href="/wiki/6_December" title="6 December">6 December</a> sunrise edition, they wrote an article about whether record sales of non-<a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magic</a> <a href="/wiki/Broomstick" title="Broomstick">broomsticks</a> over <a href="/wiki/Hallowe%27en" title="Hallowe&#39;en">Hallowe'en</a> represented an investment opportunity.<sup id="cite_ref-dec6sunrise_3-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-dec6sunrise-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> An article by them about how low <a href="/wiki/Potion" title="Potion">potions</a> imports were impacting the value of the <a href="/wiki/Dragot" title="Dragot">Dragot</a> was featured in the 6 December sunset edition.<sup id="cite_ref-nyghost-dec6_2-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-nyghost-dec6-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> This was followed by a report by them in the <a href="/wiki/7_December" title="7 December">7 December</a> sunset edition about how fear generated by the recent magical attacks in <a href="/wiki/New_York" title="New York">New York</a> was negatively impacting <a href="/wiki/WizTour" title="WizTour">WizTour</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-nyghost-dec7_4-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-nyghost-dec7-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="General_reporting">General reporting</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/M._Carneirus_(American)?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: General reporting">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/M._Carneirus_(American)?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: General reporting"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Carneirus co-wrote a story with <a href="/wiki/C._Williamsum" title="C. Williamsum">C. Williamsum</a> and <a href="/wiki/R._Amorim_(American)" title="R. Amorim (American)">R. Amorim</a> about top <a href="/wiki/Auror" title="Auror">Aurors</a> being called to <a href="/wiki/New_York_City_Hall" title="New York City Hall">New York City Hall</a> to investigate the death of <a href="/wiki/Henry_Shaw_Junior" title="Henry Shaw Junior">Henry Shaw Junior</a> that was featured on the front page of the 7 December sunset edition of the <i>Ghost</i>.<sup id="cite_ref-nyghost-dec7_4-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-nyghost-dec7-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/M._Carneirus_(American)?veaction=edit&amp;section=4" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/M._Carneirus_(American)?action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>This character was presumably named after <a href="/wiki/Mauricio_Carneiro_(real-world)" title="Mauricio Carneiro (real-world)">Mauricio Carneiro</a>, who worked as a costume illustrator for every <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_films" class="mw-redirect" title="Harry Potter films"><i>Harry Potter</i> film</a> from <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Goblet_of_Fire_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire (film)">Goblet of Fire</a></i> onward, and as a costumer for <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Order_of_the_Phoenix_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix (film)">Order of the Phoenix</a></i> and <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Half-Blood_Prince_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince (film)">Half-Blood Prince</a></i>.<sup id="cite_ref-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup></li></ul>
