<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Gwenog_Jones?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Gwenog_Jones?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Gwenog Jones was born into the presumably wizarding <a href="/wiki/Jones_family" title="Jones family">Jones family</a> in 1968. Her family possibly came from <a href="/wiki/Wales" title="Wales">Wales</a> as Jones is a Welsh surname and she later become the manager of the <a href="/wiki/Welsh_National_Quidditch_team" title="Welsh National Quidditch team">Welsh National Quidditch team</a>.
</p><p>She began attending <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> in <a href="/wiki/1979" title="1979">1979</a> or <a href="/wiki/1980" title="1980">1980</a>. It's unknown what house she was sorted into. During her time at the school she was became a favourite student of <a href="/wiki/Horace_Slughorn" title="Horace Slughorn">Horace Slughorn</a>, and was consequently inducted into his <a href="/wiki/Slug_Club" title="Slug Club">Slug Club</a> throughout her time at the school, one of the last before his retirement.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP4_4-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP4-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> 
</p>
<figure class="thumb tright show-info-icon" style="width: 252px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/9/90/Halfblood-00282.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20141227203829" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/9/90/Halfblood-00282.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/250?cb=20141227203829" decoding="async" width="250" height="104" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="Halfblood-00282.jpg" data-image-key="Halfblood-00282.jpg" /></a> 	 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:Halfblood-00282.jpg" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption"><a href="/wiki/Horace_Slughorn" title="Horace Slughorn">Horace Slughorn</a>'s picture of Gwenog on his shelf of favourites</p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure> 
<p>Sometime after graduating likely in <a href="/wiki/1986" title="1986">1986</a> or <a href="/wiki/1987" title="1987">1987</a>, Jones became a <a href="/wiki/Beater" title="Beater">Beater</a> for the all-female <a href="/wiki/Holyhead_Harpies" title="Holyhead Harpies">Holyhead Harpies</a> <a href="/wiki/Quidditch_team" title="Quidditch team">Quidditch team</a>, and eventually became the Captain of said team. Remembering her former <a href="/wiki/Potions_(class)" title="Potions (class)">Potions</a> teacher, she gave Slughorn free tickets to her Quidditch matches whenever he so desired.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP4_4-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP4-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> Amongst Jones's biggest fans was <a href="/wiki/Ginevra_Weasley" title="Ginevra Weasley">Ginevra Weasley</a>, who had a poster of the prestigious Quidditch player on the wall of her small <a href="/wiki/Ginevra_Weasley%27s_bedroom" title="Ginevra Weasley&#39;s bedroom">bedroom</a> at <a href="/wiki/The_Burrow" title="The Burrow">the Burrow</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-DH7_5-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH7-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> In later years Ginny would go on to play for the Harpies, though it is unknown whether Jones was still on the team at that point.
</p>
<figure class="thumb tright show-info-icon" style="width: 252px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/c/ce/It_Should_Have_Been_Haiti.png/revision/latest?cb=20180318015502" class="image"><img alt="" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw%3D%3D" decoding="async" width="250" height="331" class="thumbimage lazyload" data-image-name="It Should Have Been Haiti.png" data-image-key="It_Should_Have_Been_Haiti.png" data-src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/c/ce/It_Should_Have_Been_Haiti.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/250?cb=20180318015502" /></a> 	<noscript><a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/c/ce/It_Should_Have_Been_Haiti.png/revision/latest?cb=20180318015502" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/c/ce/It_Should_Have_Been_Haiti.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/250?cb=20180318015502" decoding="async" width="250" height="331" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="It Should Have Been Haiti.png" data-image-key="It_Should_Have_Been_Haiti.png" data-src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/c/ce/It_Should_Have_Been_Haiti.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/250?cb=20180318015502" /></a></noscript> 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:It_Should_Have_Been_Haiti.png" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption">Gwenog Jones's "It Should Have Been Haiti" t-shirt</p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure>
<p>By <a href="/wiki/2014" title="2014">2014</a>, Gwenog Jones had retired from the <a href="/wiki/Holyhead_Harpies" title="Holyhead Harpies">Holyhead Harpies</a>, and had become the manager of the Welsh National Quidditch Team. Shortly before the <a href="/wiki/2014_Quidditch_World_Cup" title="2014 Quidditch World Cup">2014 Quidditch World Cup</a>, Gwenog Jones threatened to "<i><a href="/wiki/Curse" title="Curse">curse</a> the face off</i>" rival <a href="/wiki/Brazilian_National_Quidditch_team" title="Brazilian National Quidditch team">Brazilian</a> manager <a href="/wiki/Jos%C3%A9_Barboza" title="José Barboza">José Barboza</a>, when he called her Chasers "<i>talentless <a href="/wiki/Hag" title="Hag">hags</a></i>" (a comment that he later insisted had been taken out of context). During the <a href="/wiki/Brazil" title="Brazil">Brazil</a>-<a href="/wiki/Wales" title="Wales">Wales</a> quarter-final match, she showed up to the game in an 'It Should Have Been <a href="/wiki/Haiti" title="Haiti">Haiti</a>' T-shirt and was prevented from entering the stadium, therefore missing the first ten minutes of the game. She later ended up in custody when, after Brazilian Seeker <a href="/wiki/Tony_Silva" title="Tony Silva">Tony Silva</a> caught the <a href="/wiki/Golden_Snitch" title="Golden Snitch">Snitch</a> and won the game, she attempted to curse off Barboza's face in the packed stadium and was dragged away by her own Beaters.<sup id="cite_ref-PM_3-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PM-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Physical_appearance">Physical appearance</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Gwenog_Jones?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Physical appearance">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Gwenog_Jones?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Physical appearance"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Gwenog was described as being a tall, dark-skinned witch. Due to the fact she was a beater it can be presumed that she was fairly burly or at least had a 'beater's build'. She had dark brown hair and brown eyes.<sup id="cite_ref-JKRS_2-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-JKRS-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Personality_and_traits">Personality and traits</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Gwenog_Jones?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Personality and traits">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Gwenog_Jones?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Personality and traits"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Jones had said on a number of occasions that <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witches</a> made better <a href="/wiki/Quidditch" title="Quidditch">Quidditch</a> players than <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizards</a>, and no one was likely to disagree with her. This was because "<i>people who disagreed with [Jones] had a nasty habit of turning into woodlice.</i>" After a game she enjoyed relaxing with friends, drinking <a href="/wiki/Butterbeer" title="Butterbeer">butterbeer</a>, and listening to the <a href="/wiki/Weird_Sisters" title="Weird Sisters">Weird Sisters</a>. <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione Granger</a> considered Gwenog to be "a bit full of herself" after meeting her at one of Slughorn's <a href="/wiki/Slug_Club" title="Slug Club">Slug Club</a> meetings in 1996.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP14_6-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP14-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup> It is thought that the <a href="/wiki/International_Confederation_of_Wizards_Quidditch_Committee" title="International Confederation of Wizards Quidditch Committee">ICWQC</a>'s ban on 'managerial trash talk' was made with her in mind.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Magical_abilities_and_skills">Magical abilities and skills</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Gwenog_Jones?veaction=edit&amp;section=4" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Gwenog_Jones?action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li><b><a href="/wiki/Flight" title="Flight">Flying</a></b>: Jones was evidently a highly accomplished and talented <a href="/wiki/Broomstick" title="Broomstick">broomstick</a> flyer and <a href="/wiki/Quidditch" title="Quidditch">Quidditch</a> player, given how she became both the <a href="/wiki/Captain" title="Captain">Captain</a> and <a href="/wiki/Beater" title="Beater">Beater</a> for the <a href="/wiki/Holyhead_Harpies" title="Holyhead Harpies">Holyhead Harpies</a> Quidditch team, a very impressive achievement in the <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magical</a> sport which gained much fame and recognition in the world of Quidditch. She was so experienced in playing the sport that she eventually became the Manager of the <a href="/wiki/Welsh_National_Quidditch_team" title="Welsh National Quidditch team">Welsh National Quidditch team</a>.</li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Transfiguration" title="Transfiguration">Transfiguration</a></b>: It was said that "<i>people who disagreed with [Jones] had a nasty habit of turning into <a href="/wiki/Wood_louse" title="Wood louse">woodlice</a></i>", this meant that Jones was capable of casting a transfiguration <a href="/wiki/Spell" title="Spell">spell</a> which turned <a href="/wiki/Human" title="Human">humans</a> into <a href="/wiki/Wood_louse" title="Wood louse">woodlice</a>.</li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Dark_Arts" title="Dark Arts">Dark Arts</a></b>: Jones appeared to a level of skill with the casting of <a href="/wiki/Dark_charm" title="Dark charm">dark charms</a>, given how she threatened to <a href="/wiki/Curse" title="Curse">curse</a> <a href="/wiki/Jos%C3%A9_Barboza" title="José Barboza">José Barboza</a> during a Quidditch match and had to be pulled away by her own players to prevent her from cursing him.<sup id="cite_ref-PM_3-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PM-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup></li></ul>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Gwenog_Jones?veaction=edit&amp;section=5" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Gwenog_Jones?action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p><i>Gwenog </i>can trace back to the Welsh name <i>Gwen</i>, which means "fair, blessed". This can relate to her being the captain of the only all-witch quidditch team. Being the only one can be considered as "blessed".
</p><p><i>Jones </i>can refer to <i>John, </i>which means in Hebrew "God is gracious". This can relate to her being "blessed".
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Gwenog_Jones?veaction=edit&amp;section=6" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Gwenog_Jones?action=edit&amp;section=6" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>Gwenog may be related to <a href="/wiki/Hestia_Jones" title="Hestia Jones">Hestia Jones</a>, a member of the <a href="/wiki/Order_of_the_Phoenix" title="Order of the Phoenix">Order of the Phoenix</a>, <a href="/wiki/Peter_Jones" title="Peter Jones">Peter Jones</a>, a <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor" title="Gryffindor">Gryffindor</a> student at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a>, or <a href="/wiki/Megan_Jones" title="Megan Jones">Megan Jones</a>, a <a href="/wiki/Hufflepuff" title="Hufflepuff">Hufflepuff</a> student at Hogwarts in <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>'s year.</li>
<li>The illustration on Gwenog's <a href="/wiki/Wizard_of_the_Month" title="Wizard of the Month">Wizard of the Month</a> profile shows her to have dark skin and purple Quidditch robes. However, in the signed photo of Gwenog owned by <a href="/wiki/Horace_Slughorn" title="Horace Slughorn">Horace Slughorn</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Half-Blood_Prince_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince (film)">film adaptation</a> of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Half-Blood_Prince" title="Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince">Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince</a></i>, she has light skin and is wearing yellow Quidditch robes.</li>
<li>Although she definitely attended a party earlier in the year, it is unknown, being a former member of the Slug Club, whether or not she attended the <a href="/wiki/Slug_Club_Christmas_party" title="Slug Club Christmas party">Slug Club Christmas party</a> in <a href="/wiki/1996" title="1996">1996</a>.</li></ul>
