<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Morgan_le_Fay?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Morgan_le_Fay?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Lifetime">Lifetime</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Morgan_le_Fay?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Lifetime">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Morgan_le_Fay?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Lifetime"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Morgan le Fay was born and lived during the <a href="/wiki/Middle_Ages" title="Middle Ages">Middle Ages</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-fwc_2-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-fwc-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> She was known to have had a half-brother, <a href="/wiki/Arthur" title="Arthur">Arthur</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-fwc_2-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-fwc-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>During her lifetime, Morgan le Fay played a role in numerous events through her powerful magic and <a href="/wiki/Healer" title="Healer">healing</a> skills. She was an <a href="/wiki/Animagus" title="Animagus">Animagus</a> (taking the form of a <a href="/wiki/Bird" title="Bird">bird</a>).<sup id="cite_ref-wotc_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-wotc-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Morgan ruled as Queen of the Island of <a href="/wiki/Avalon" title="Avalon">Avalon</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-wotc_3-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-wotc-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> However, she became a skilful practitioner of the <a href="/wiki/Dark_Arts" title="Dark Arts">Dark Arts</a> and an enemy of the great wizard <a href="/wiki/Merlin" title="Merlin">Merlin</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-fwc_2-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-fwc-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Legacy">Legacy</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Morgan_le_Fay?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Legacy">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Morgan_le_Fay?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Legacy"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Morgan le Fay was featured on a <a href="/wiki/Chocolate_Frog_Card" title="Chocolate Frog Card">Chocolate Frog Card</a> centuries after her death.<sup id="cite_ref-ps6_4-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-ps6-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ron Weasley</a> claimed he owned "about six" of this card in <a href="/wiki/1991" title="1991">1991</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-ps6_4-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-ps6-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Magical_abilities_and_skills">Magical abilities and skills</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Morgan_le_Fay?veaction=edit&amp;section=4" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Morgan_le_Fay?action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li><b><a href="/wiki/Dark_Arts" title="Dark Arts">Dark Arts</a></b>: Morgana's famous Wizard Card described her as a powerful <a href="/wiki/Dark_Wizard" title="Dark Wizard">Dark witch</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-fwc_2-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-fwc-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> meaning she would have highly proficient in many areas of dark magic and with the casting of <a href="/wiki/Dark_charm" title="Dark charm">dark charms</a>.</li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Transfiguration" title="Transfiguration">Transfiguration</a></b>: Morgana was revealed to have mastered the art of <a href="/wiki/Human_Transfiguration" title="Human Transfiguration">Human Transfiguration</a>, having become an <a href="/wiki/Animagus" title="Animagus">Animagus</a>; being able to turn herself into a <a href="/wiki/Bird" title="Bird">bird</a> at will,<sup id="cite_ref-wotc_3-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-wotc-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> showing remarkable proficiency in this highly difficult branch of <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magic</a>.</li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Potion" title="Potion">Potions</a></b>: Being an Animagus, Morgan would have needed to have successfully brewed an <a href="/wiki/Animagus_potion" title="Animagus potion">Animagus potion</a>, in order to gain the ability to transform. This implies that she had great skill in potions, as brewing the Animagus potion is immensely difficult, complex and time-consuming.</li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Healing_magic" title="Healing magic">Healing magic</a></b>: Morgana was noted to be particularly gifted in the art of healing magic, as she was a proficient <a href="/wiki/Healer" title="Healer">Healer</a>, meaning she would have talented with <a href="/wiki/Healing_spell" title="Healing spell">healing spells</a>, potions and other magical remedies.<sup id="cite_ref-wotc_3-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-wotc-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup></li></ul>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Morgan_le_Fay?veaction=edit&amp;section=5" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Morgan_le_Fay?action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<figure class="thumb tright show-info-icon" style="width: 182px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/1/18/Morgan_Le_Fay-17-chocFrogCard.png/revision/latest?cb=20160606202350" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/1/18/Morgan_Le_Fay-17-chocFrogCard.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/180?cb=20160606202350" decoding="async" width="180" height="180" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="Morgan Le Fay-17-chocFrogCard.png" data-image-key="Morgan_Le_Fay-17-chocFrogCard.png" /></a> 	 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:Morgan_Le_Fay-17-chocFrogCard.png" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption">Morgan le Fay on a Famous Wizard Card</p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure>
<ul><li>In the 1998 television film <i><a target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener" class="external text" href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Merlin_(miniseries)">Merlin</a></i>, with <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sam_Neill" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Sam Neill">Sam Neill</a> as the title character, Morgan le Fay was portrayed by <a href="/wiki/Helena_Bonham_Carter" title="Helena Bonham Carter">Helena Bonham Carter</a>, who later went on to play Dark witch and <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eater</a> <a href="/wiki/Bellatrix_Lestrange" title="Bellatrix Lestrange">Bellatrix Lestrange</a>.</li>
<li>Before <a href="/wiki/Emma_Watson" title="Emma Watson">Emma Watson</a> got the part of <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione Granger</a>, she had acted only in school plays; one was "Arthur's Early Years", in which she played the role of Morgan le Fay.</li>
<li>Morgan le Fay is based on the legendary figure <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Morgan_le_Fay" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Morgan le Fay">of the same name</a>, the daughter of King Arthur's mother the Lady Igraine and her first husband Gorlois, Duke of Cornwall (making her Arthur's half-sister in legend too).</li>
<li>Morgan le Fay's time of existence in the Harry Potter Universe (like Merlin, King Arthur etc) is paradoxical as the Morgan le Fay of the Arthurian legends is said to have lived in the 5th or 6th century. As Merlin attended Hogwarts and she was an enemy of Merlin, then she, like the <i>legendary </i>version of Merlin predates Hogwarts by around 400/500 years.</li></ul>
