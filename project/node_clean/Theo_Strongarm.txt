<b>Theo Strongarm</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Great_Britain" title="Great Britain">British</a> wizard and a member of the <a href="/wiki/Statute_of_Secrecy_Task_Force" title="Statute of Secrecy Task Force">Statute of Secrecy Task Force</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-WU_1-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-WU-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Theo_Strongarm?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Theo_Strongarm?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>At some point during the <a href="/wiki/2010s" title="2010s">2010s</a>, Strongarm joined the Statue of Secrecy Task Force. As part of the task force, he developed a vigorous stirring method that increased the effectiveness of the <a href="/wiki/Tonic_for_Trace_Detection" title="Tonic for Trace Detection">Tonic for Trace Detection</a>. This tactic was soon adopted by other members of the task force in order to improve their own potions.<sup id="cite_ref-WU_1-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-WU-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
