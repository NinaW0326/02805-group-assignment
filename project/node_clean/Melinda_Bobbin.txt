<b>Melinda Bobbin</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch</a> who attended <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> during the <a href="/wiki/1990s" title="1990s">1990s</a>. <a href="/wiki/Horace_Slughorn" title="Horace Slughorn">Horace Slughorn</a> described her as "charming" and made her a member of the <a href="/wiki/Slug_Club" title="Slug Club">Slug Club</a> in <a href="/wiki/1996" title="1996">1996</a>. Her family owned a large <a href="/wiki/Bobbin_family%27s_chain_of_apothecaries" title="Bobbin family&#39;s chain of apothecaries">chain of apothecaries</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP11_4-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP11-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
