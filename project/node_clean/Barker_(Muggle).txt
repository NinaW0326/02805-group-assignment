<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Barker_(Muggle)?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Barker_(Muggle)?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>When <a href="/wiki/Langdon_Shaw" title="Langdon Shaw">Langdon Shaw</a> arrived with the <a href="/wiki/Barebone_family" title="Barebone family">Barebone family</a> on <a href="/wiki/6_December" title="6 December">6 December</a> <a href="/wiki/1926" title="1926">1926</a> hoping to see his father, Barker nervously told him that Shaw Snr was in a meeting with <a href="/wiki/Henry_Shaw_Junior" title="Henry Shaw Junior">his brother</a>, but Langdon pushed past him.<sup id="cite_ref-FB37_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-FB37-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> Barker then profusely apologised to his boss for Langdon's interruption.<sup id="cite_ref-FB38_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-FB38-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
