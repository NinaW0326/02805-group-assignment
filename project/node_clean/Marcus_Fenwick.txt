<b>Marcus Fenwick</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Ravenclaw" title="Ravenclaw">Ravenclaw</a> student at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> in the <a href="/wiki/1990s" title="1990s">1990s</a>. During his <a href="/wiki/Fifth_year" title="Fifth year">fifth year</a>, he was apparently head of the <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Gobstone_Club" title="Hogwarts Gobstone Club">Hogwarts Gobstone Club</a>, as students interested in joining the club were instructed to see him on a sign-up sheet posted on the <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor_Notice_Board" title="Gryffindor Notice Board">bulletin board</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor" title="Gryffindor">Gryffindor</a> <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor_Tower" title="Gryffindor Tower">common room</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-HPE_2-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HPE-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
