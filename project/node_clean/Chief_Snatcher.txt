The <b>Chief Snatcher</b> was the second in command of the <a href="/wiki/Snatchers" title="Snatchers">Snatchers</a> during the <a href="/wiki/Second_Wizarding_War" title="Second Wizarding War">Second Wizarding War</a>. He was second to <a href="/wiki/Fenrir_Greyback" title="Fenrir Greyback">Fenrir Greyback</a> who was the leader of the group.<sup id="cite_ref-DHF2_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DHF2-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>During the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a>, the Chief Snatcher participated the <a href="/wiki/Skirmish_at_the_Wooden_Bridge" title="Skirmish at the Wooden Bridge">Skirmish at the Covered Bridge</a> along with <a href="/wiki/Scabior" title="Scabior">Scabior</a> and many others and possibly fell to his death when the bridge collapsed.<sup id="cite_ref-DHF2_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DHF2-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
