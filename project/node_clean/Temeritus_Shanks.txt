<p><b>Temeritus Shanks</b><sup id="cite_ref-LE_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-LE-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a>. He seemed to like reading newspapers, and was interested in knowing the headlines of the day. He believed every word he read in the <i><a href="/wiki/Daily_Prophet" title="Daily Prophet">Daily Prophet</a></i>, including the stories about <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> and <a href="/wiki/Albus_Dumbledore" title="Albus Dumbledore">Albus Dumbledore</a> published during the <a href="/wiki/1995%E2%80%931996_school_year" title="1995–1996 school year">1995–1996 school year</a>. There are <a href="/wiki/Portrait" title="Portrait">portraits</a> of him hung on the walls of <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Castle" title="Hogwarts Castle">Hogwarts Castle</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-OP_1-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OP-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Temeritus_Shanks?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Temeritus_Shanks?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span id="1992–1993_school_year"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="1992.E2.80.931993_school_year"><a href="/wiki/1992%E2%80%931993_school_year" title="1992–1993 school year">1992–1993 school year</a></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Temeritus_Shanks?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: 1992–1993 school year">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Temeritus_Shanks?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: 1992–1993 school year"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>In early <a href="/wiki/1993" title="1993">1993</a>, Shanks had a portrait at the <a href="/wiki/Grand_Staircase" title="Grand Staircase">Grand Staircase</a>, hung immediately below the ground-floor landing.<sup id="cite_ref-COSF_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COSF-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="1993-1994_school_year"><a href="/wiki/1993-1994_school_year" class="mw-redirect" title="1993-1994 school year">1993-1994 school year</a></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Temeritus_Shanks?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: 1993-1994 school year">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Temeritus_Shanks?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: 1993-1994 school year"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>In <a href="/wiki/September" title="September">September</a> of <a href="/wiki/1993" title="1993">1993</a>, Shanks had a portrait on the seventh floor.<sup id="cite_ref-POAG_4-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-POAG-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span id="1995–1996_school_year"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="1995.E2.80.931996_school_year"><a href="/wiki/1995%E2%80%931996_school_year" title="1995–1996 school year">1995–1996 school year</a></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Temeritus_Shanks?veaction=edit&amp;section=4" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: 1995–1996 school year">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Temeritus_Shanks?action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: 1995–1996 school year"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>By <a href="/wiki/1995" title="1995">1995</a>, Shanks had a portrait hung at the <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Library" title="Hogwarts Library">Hogwarts Library</a>, guarding a shortcut to the fourth-floor corridor, under the password "<i>No news is good news</i>". Said shortcut was reserved to the staff only. When <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>, who was being highly discredited by the <i><a href="/wiki/Daily_Prophet" title="Daily Prophet">Daily Prophet</a></i> at the time, asked if he could use this secret passageway, Shanks made an exception for the student was constantly featured on the paper. The portrait agreed to give the student the password in exchange for the headline of the day ("<i>TRAGIC DEMISE OF MINISTRY OF MAGIC WORKER</i>", concerning <a href="/wiki/Broderick_Bode" title="Broderick Bode">Broderick Bode</a>'s assassination).<sup id="cite_ref-OP_1-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OP-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Shanks had another portrait at the <a href="/wiki/Grand_Staircase" title="Grand Staircase">Grand Staircase</a>, concealing a secret passage to the <a href="/wiki/Clock_Tower_Courtyard" title="Clock Tower Courtyard">Clock Tower Courtyard</a>. Its password was "<i>Audaces fortuna iuvat</i>".<sup id="cite_ref-OP_GBA_5-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OP_GBA-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span id="1996–1997_school_year"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="1996.E2.80.931997_school_year"><a href="/wiki/1996%E2%80%931997_school_year" title="1996–1997 school year">1996–1997 school year</a></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Temeritus_Shanks?veaction=edit&amp;section=5" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: 1996–1997 school year">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Temeritus_Shanks?action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: 1996–1997 school year"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Shanks's portrait was kept at the same place at the <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Library" title="Hogwarts Library">Library</a>, but hiding a different shortcut, leading to the <a href="/wiki/Grand_Staircase" title="Grand Staircase">Grand Staircase</a>. The new password was "<i>Happy Headlines</i>".<sup id="cite_ref-HBP_6-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup> The same shortcut could also lead to the second-floor corridor.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP_NDS_7-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP_NDS-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup> When Shanks heard <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> talking to <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione Granger</a> about <a href="/wiki/Draco_Malfoy" title="Draco Malfoy">Draco Malfoy</a>'s mysterious endeavours at the seventh floor, he gladly told him the new password in order to help him on what he deemed a "big scoop".<sup id="cite_ref-HBP_6-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Personality_and_traits">Personality and traits</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Temeritus_Shanks?veaction=edit&amp;section=6" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Personality and traits">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Temeritus_Shanks?action=edit&amp;section=6" title="Edit section: Personality and traits"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Temeritus Shanks was quite a naive and gullible wizard, believing everything he read on the papers.<sup id="cite_ref-OP_1-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OP-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> Despite this he was brave, speaking <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a>'s name out loud when he was at large.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP_6-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup> He would gladly help students in matters he deemed to be sensational, to the point of giving his password to them despite the passageway being reserved only to the staff.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP_6-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Temeritus_Shanks?veaction=edit&amp;section=7" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Temeritus_Shanks?action=edit&amp;section=7" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>Shanks may have passed away sometime prior to <a href="/wiki/1991" title="1991">1991</a> as many portraits hung at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Castle" title="Hogwarts Castle">Hogwarts Castle</a> depict deceased individuals.</li>
<li>Temeritus was voiced in both the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Order_of_the_Phoenix_(video_game)" title="Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix (video game)">video game version</a> of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Order_of_the_Phoenix" title="Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix">Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix</a></i> and the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Half-Blood_Prince_(video_game)" title="Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince (video game)">video game adaptation</a> of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Half-Blood_Prince" title="Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince">Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince</a></i> video games by <a href="/wiki/Jonathan_Kydd" title="Jonathan Kydd">Jonathan Kydd</a>.</li>
<li>Shanks's portrait was based on a real-world <a target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener" class="external text" href="http://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/9/9d/King_James_I_of_England_and_VI_of_Scotland_by_Arnold_van_Brounckhorst.jpg">portrait of a young King James I</a> of England.<sup id="cite_ref-LE_2-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-LE-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup></li></ul>
