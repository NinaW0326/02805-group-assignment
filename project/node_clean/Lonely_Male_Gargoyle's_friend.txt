<dd><b>Gargoyle</b>: "<i>Haven't seen another gargoyle since the one on that plinth disappeared.</i>"</dd>
<dd>&#8212; The <a href="/wiki/Lonely_Male_Gargoyle" title="Lonely Male Gargoyle">Lonely Male Gargoyle</a> talks about his friend<sup class="noprint"><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Order_of_the_Phoenix_(video_game)" title="Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix (video game)">[src]</a></sup></dd></dl>
</div>
<p>This <b>gargoyle</b> used to sit on a plinth opposite the <a href="/wiki/Lonely_Male_Gargoyle" title="Lonely Male Gargoyle">Lonely Male Gargoyle</a> by the <a href="/wiki/Viaduct" title="Viaduct">Viaduct</a> at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a>. Sometime in <a href="/wiki/1994" title="1994">1994</a>, it was knocked off of its plinth and fell off the side of the castle, leaving the Lonely Male Gargoyle without a companion.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTPG_1-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTPG-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Presumably, this gargoyle also had the ability to talk, although this is not certain.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTPG_1-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTPG-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<div class="quote">
<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix (video game)"><b>Hermione</b>: "<i>What happened to it?</i>"</span></dd>
<dd><b>Gargoyle</b>: "<i>About a year ago some boy flew through here being chased by a dragon - it knocked my friend right over the edge.</i>"</dd>
<dd><b>Harry</b>: "<i>Er...</i>"</dd>
<dd>&#8212; The <a href="/wiki/Lonely_Male_Gargoyle" title="Lonely Male Gargoyle">Lonely Male Gargoyle</a> reveals his friend's fate.<sup class="noprint"><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Order_of_the_Phoenix_(video_game)" title="Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix (video game)">[src]</a></sup></dd></dl>

<ul><li>In the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Order_of_the_Phoenix_(video_game)" title="Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix (video game)">video game adaptation</a> of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Order_of_the_Phoenix" title="Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix">Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix</a></i>, the Lonely Male Gargoyle implies that <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> was the one who inadvertently knocked this gargoyle over the edge while attempting to escape from the <a href="/wiki/Unidentified_female_Hungarian_Horntail" title="Unidentified female Hungarian Horntail">dragon</a> during the First Task of the <a href="/wiki/Triwizard_Tournament" title="Triwizard Tournament">Triwizard Tournament</a> the previous year. If this is the case, then it would be referential of an event that took place only in the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Goblet_of_Fire_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire (film)">film</a> / <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Goblet_of_Fire_(video_game)" title="Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire (video game)">video game</a> version of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Goblet_of_Fire" title="Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire">Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire</a></i>, as in the book version, the dragon never escaped its restraints.</li></ul>
