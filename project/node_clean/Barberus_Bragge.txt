Chief <b>Barberus Bragge</b> (fl. <a href="/wiki/1269" class="mw-redirect" title="1269">1269</a>) was a <a href="/wiki/Great_Britain" title="Great Britain">British</a> <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> and chief of the <a href="/wiki/Wizards%27_Council" title="Wizards&#39; Council">Wizards' Council</a> in the <a href="/wiki/1260s" title="1260s">1260s</a>. He was responsible for the introduction of the <a href="/wiki/Golden_Snidget" title="Golden Snidget">Golden Snidget</a> into <a href="/wiki/Quidditch" title="Quidditch">Cuaditch</a>, thus changing the face of <a href="/wiki/Quidditch" title="Quidditch">Quidditch</a> forever.<sup id="cite_ref-QA4_1-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-QA4-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> For this, he was featured on a <a href="/wiki/Chocolate_Frog_Card" title="Chocolate Frog Card">Chocolate Frog Card</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-PAg_2-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PAg-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Barberus_Bragge?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Barberus_Bragge?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_life">Early life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Barberus_Bragge?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Early life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Barberus_Bragge?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Early life"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Bragge was most likely born between the mid to late-<a href="/wiki/12th_century" title="12th century">twelfth century</a> and the early-<a href="/wiki/13th_century" title="13th century">thirteenth century</a>. He presumably attended <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> in his youth, like so many other wizards before him.
</p>
<h3><span id="As_Chief_of_the_Wizards'_Council"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="As_Chief_of_the_Wizards.27_Council">As Chief of the Wizards' Council</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Barberus_Bragge?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: As Chief of the Wizards&#039; Council">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Barberus_Bragge?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: As Chief of the Wizards&#039; Council"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>During a Cuaditch (period-Quidditch) game in <a href="/wiki/1269" class="mw-redirect" title="1269">1269</a>, Chief Bragge released a <a href="/wiki/Golden_Snidget" title="Golden Snidget">Golden Snidget</a> onto the field, and offered the player who captured it one-hundred and fifty <a href="/wiki/Wizarding_currency" title="Wizarding currency">Galleons</a>. In protest, the <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch</a> <a href="/wiki/Modesty_Rabnott" title="Modesty Rabnott">Modesty Rabnott</a> <a href="/wiki/Summoning_Charm" title="Summoning Charm">summoned</a> the Snidget to her and fled the game. Bragge was furious and was almost going to hex her, when his <a href="/wiki/Barberus_Bragge%27s_advisors" title="Barberus Bragge&#39;s advisors">advisors</a> calmed him down and she was fined ten <a href="/wiki/Wizarding_currency" title="Wizarding currency">Galleons</a> for disrupting the game. However, the idea caught on, and soon the Snidget was a part of the game, with a new player, the <a href="/wiki/Seeker" title="Seeker">Hunter</a> (Seeker), being added to the team.<sup id="cite_ref-QA4_1-10" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-QA4-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
