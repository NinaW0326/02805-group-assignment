<b>Irving Warble</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizarding</a> composer. He married <a href="/wiki/Celestina_Warbeck" title="Celestina Warbeck">Celestina Warbeck</a> after she left her <a href="/wiki/Celestina_Warbeck%27s_second_husband" title="Celestina Warbeck&#39;s second husband">second husband</a> to be with him.<sup id="cite_ref-Pottermore_1-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Pottermore-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Irving_Warble?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Irving_Warble?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>Irving is a <a href="/wiki/Scotland" title="Scotland">Scottish</a> surname, though it is sometimes used as a first name as well. The name is derived from the <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/River_Irvine" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:River Irvine">River Irvine</a>. <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Irving_Berlin" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Irving Berlin">Irving Berlin</a> is considered one of the greatest American songwriters; his most popular popular songs include such musical standards as <i><a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Alexander%27s_Ragtime_Band" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Alexander&#39;s Ragtime Band">Alexander's Ragtime Band</a></i>, <i><a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/White_Christmas_(song)" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:White Christmas (song)">White Christmas</a></i>, <i><a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Happy_Holiday_(song)" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Happy Holiday (song)">Happy Holidays</a></i>, <i><a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Cheek_to_Cheek" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Cheek to Cheek">Cheek to Cheek</a></i>, <i><a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Let%27s_Face_the_Music_and_Dance" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Let&#39;s Face the Music and Dance">Let's Face the Music and Dance</a></i> and <i><a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Oh!_How_I_Hate_to_Get_Up_in_the_Morning" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Oh! How I Hate to Get Up in the Morning">Oh! How I Hate to Get Up in the Morning</a></i>.</li>
<li>Warble is an English verb meaning "to sing in a high or shaky voice" and is a word often associated with the singing of birds.</li></ul>
