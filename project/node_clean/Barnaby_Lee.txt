<b>Barnaby Lee</b> (born c. <a href="/wiki/1973" title="1973">1973</a>) was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> born to <a href="/wiki/Barnaby_Lee%27s_father" title="Barnaby Lee&#39;s father">Mr</a> and <a href="/wiki/Barnaby_Lee%27s_mother" title="Barnaby Lee&#39;s mother">Mrs Lee</a>. At the age of roughly eleven he learned he had been accepted into <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a>. He began attending Hogwarts on <a href="/wiki/1_September" title="1 September">1 September</a> <a href="/wiki/1984" title="1984">1984</a> and was <a href="/wiki/Sorting_ceremony" title="Sorting ceremony">sorted</a> into <a href="/wiki/Slytherin" title="Slytherin">Slytherin House</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-house_6-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-house-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup> Barnaby possessed a less than exceptional academic mind, but he still excelled in his <a href="/wiki/Care_of_Magical_Creatures" title="Care of Magical Creatures">Care of Magical Creatures</a> class.
</p>

<h3><span id="Early_life_(Pre-1984)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_life_.28Pre-1984.29">Early life <span class="template-c">(Pre-1984)</span></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Barnaby_Lee?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Early life (Pre-1984)">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Barnaby_Lee?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Early life (Pre-1984)"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<div class="quote">
<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;Harry Potter: Hogwarts Mystery">"<i>STOP FIGHTING! Please, Rowan. No more fighting. It's like I'm back home, and I don't want to think about being back home.</i>"</span></dd>
<dd>&#8212;Barnaby breaking up a fight between Rowan and Jacob's Sibling<sup class="noprint"><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter:_Hogwarts_Mystery" title="Harry Potter: Hogwarts Mystery">[src]</a></sup></dd>
<dd></dd></dl></div><p>Barnaby Lee was born between <a href="/wiki/1_September" title="1 September">1 September</a> <a href="/wiki/1972" title="1972">1972</a> and <a href="/wiki/31_August" title="31 August">31 August</a> <a href="/wiki/1973" title="1973">1973</a>. Both his <a href="/wiki/Barnaby_Lee%27s_father" title="Barnaby Lee&#39;s father">father</a> and <a href="/wiki/Barnaby_Lee%27s_mother" title="Barnaby Lee&#39;s mother">mother</a> were <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a> that supported <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a>.
</p><p>Barnaby was raised in a highly toxic and abusive atmosphere, with his father scolding him for showing emotion. His father was often cruel and unloving towards him, and even made degrading insults about needing to buy Barnaby brains for his birthday. Barnaby was a very sensitive boy, when he was helping his father exterminate <a href="/wiki/Pixie" title="Pixie">Pixies</a>, he began to cry, for which Mr Lee proceeded to yell at his son for being weak. Strength was a trait drilled into Barnaby by his father, which became very important for him as he grew older.<sup id="cite_ref-y3ch8_7-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-y3ch8-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>As a young child, Barnaby was often looked after by <a href="/wiki/Barnaby_Lee%27s_grandmother" title="Barnaby Lee&#39;s grandmother">his grandmother</a>, who was just as abusive as his parents. While he stayed with her the only toys he had to play with were dark artefacts.
</p><p>When he was a child, before the end of the <a href="/wiki/First_Wizarding_War" title="First Wizarding War">First Wizarding War</a>, he remembered seeing the <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Dark Lord</a> when he came to meet with his parents. After <a href="/wiki/Attack_at_Godric%27s_Hollow_(1981)#Aftermath" title="Attack at Godric&#39;s Hollow (1981)">Voldemort's demise</a> on <a href="/wiki/31_October" title="31 October">31 October</a> <a href="/wiki/1981" title="1981">1981</a>, his parents were captured and sent to <a href="/wiki/Azkaban" title="Azkaban">Azkaban</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-y3ch8_7-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-y3ch8-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span id="Hogwarts_years_(1984-1991)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="Hogwarts_years_.281984-1991.29">Hogwarts years <span class="template-c">(1984-1991)</span></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Barnaby_Lee?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Hogwarts years (1984-1991)">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Barnaby_Lee?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Hogwarts years (1984-1991)"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="First_Year">First Year</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Barnaby_Lee?veaction=edit&amp;section=4" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: First Year">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Barnaby_Lee?action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: First Year"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<p>In <a href="/wiki/September" title="September">September</a> <a href="/wiki/1984" title="1984">1984</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup> he began attending <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> and was <a href="/wiki/Sorting_ceremony" title="Sorting ceremony">sorted</a> into <a href="/wiki/Slytherin" title="Slytherin">Slytherin</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-house_6-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-house-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup> In the <a href="/wiki/1984%E2%80%931985_school_year" title="1984–1985 school year">1984–1985 school year</a> while speaking with an unknown Slytherin girl, Barnaby mentioned that he liked to think of <a href="/wiki/Kneazle" title="Kneazle">Kneazle</a>'s as the him of cats.<sup id="cite_ref-Y1SQ_9-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Y1SQ-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>During the <a href="/wiki/Hallowe%27en_Feast" title="Hallowe&#39;en Feast">Hallowe'en Feast</a>, there was a <a href="/wiki/Werewolf" title="Werewolf">Werewolf</a> attack on the Hogwarts <a href="/wiki/Training_Grounds" title="Training Grounds">Training Grounds</a> by <a href="/wiki/Fenrir_Greyback" title="Fenrir Greyback">Fenrir Greyback</a>. This sparked Barnaby's uncle <a href="/wiki/Cecil_Lee" title="Cecil Lee">Cecil</a>, who was part of the <a href="/wiki/Werewolf_Capture_Unit" title="Werewolf Capture Unit">Werewolf Capture Unit</a> for the <a href="/wiki/Ministry_of_Magic" title="Ministry of Magic">Ministry of Magic</a> to investigate the incident. The events led Barnaby to practice his Werewolf howls during his Potions class with Professor Snape.<sup id="cite_ref-10" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-10">&#91;10&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>For <a href="/wiki/Christmas" title="Christmas">Christmas</a>, Barnaby returned home.
</p>

<p>During Barnaby's second year at Hogwarts, there were rumours circulating that a third year <a href="/wiki/Pippa_Macmillan" title="Pippa Macmillan">Pippa Macmillan</a>, had been attacked by a white werewolf in the <a href="/wiki/Forbidden_Forest" title="Forbidden Forest">Forbidden Forest</a>. While everyone in the courtyard discussing the rumours, Barnaby was no different, he, Merula and Ismelda were talking in the corner of the courtyard. It is possible this is when he was first approached about joining <a href="/wiki/Merula_Snyde%27s_gang" title="Merula Snyde&#39;s gang">Merula Snyde's gang</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-11" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-11">&#91;11&#93;</a></sup> After everyone had learned the rumours were false and Pippa had be scratched by a tree root, and had been startled by a <a href="/wiki/Hippogriff" title="Hippogriff">Hippogriff</a>, Hogwarts calmed down.
</p><p>For his second year, Barnaby once again returned home for Christmas.
</p><p>That was until there was news that a student and headmistress from <a href="/wiki/Beauxbatons_Academy_of_Magic" title="Beauxbatons Academy of Magic">Beauxbatons Academy of Magic</a> was coming to visit from France. Barnaby was sitting in Potions when <a href="/wiki/Aur%C3%A9lie_Dumont" title="Aurélie Dumont">Aurélie Dumont</a>, was walked into the classroom escorted by Jacob's sibling.<sup id="cite_ref-12" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-12">&#91;12&#93;</a></sup> Barnaby saw her again later, when she came in at the end of a potions class and asked Professor Snape to teach them basic <a href="/wiki/Alchemy" title="Alchemy">Alchemy</a>.
</p>

<h5><span class="mw-headline" id="The_Vault_of_Fear">The Vault of Fear</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Barnaby_Lee?veaction=edit&amp;section=8" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: The Vault of Fear">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Barnaby_Lee?action=edit&amp;section=8" title="Edit section: The Vault of Fear"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h5>
<p>With their friendship solidified, Barnaby joined Tulip, Jacob's sibling (and either Bill, Tonks, or Rowan), to break into the <a href="/wiki/Restricted_Section" title="Restricted Section">Restricted Section</a> of the <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Library" title="Hogwarts Library">Hogwarts Library</a>. Once they managed to sneak inside, the group was confronted by Merula and Ismelda, whom Barnaby volunteered to duel, allowing the rest of the group to proceed into the <a href="/wiki/Cursed_Vaults" title="Cursed Vaults">Cursed Vault</a>.
</p><p>After defeating his former friends in a duel, Barnaby revealed after rejoining the group that it wouldn't be long before Merula and Ismelda had alerted professors to their presence in the vault.<sup id="cite_ref-y3ch9_3-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-y3ch9-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> The group proceeded further into the Vault of Fear, when they were confronted by three <a href="/wiki/Boggart" title="Boggart">Boggarts</a> who took all but Jacob's sibling captive. Thinking quickly, Jacob's sibling rescued everyone with a <a href="/wiki/Boggart-Banishing_Spell" title="Boggart-Banishing Spell">Boggart-Banishing Spell</a>. But all was not well, another Boggart appeared as <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a>, whom Jacob's sibling had to duel. After the Boggarts defeat, the friends had to make the ultimate sacrifice by giving up magic to reap the rewards and answers the Vault held. Luckily Jacob's sibling had <a href="/wiki/Jacob%27s_wand" title="Jacob&#39;s wand">Jacob's broken wand</a> to present to the vault allowing the group to hold on to their magic without having to sacrifice anything. The vault revealed a map of the <a href="/wiki/Forbidden_Forest" title="Forbidden Forest">Forbidden Forest</a> and a broken arrow. The group took the peculiar items and escaped the vault prior to professors arriving.
</p><p>Everyone met up at the Three Broomsticks to discuss the items found in the vault. Jacob's sibling and Rowan admitted to <a href="/wiki/Ben_Copper" title="Ben Copper">Ben Copper</a> they found letters addressed to him from the mysterious <a href="/wiki/R" title="R">R</a>. Ben claimed to have no memory of it, and <a href="/wiki/William_Weasley" title="William Weasley">Bill Weasley</a> said it could wait for another day.
</p><p>Later at the fair well feast Barnaby said his good-byes to his friends before returning home on the <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Express" title="Hogwarts Express">Hogwarts Express</a>.
</p>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Fourth_Year">Fourth Year</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Barnaby_Lee?veaction=edit&amp;section=9" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Fourth Year">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Barnaby_Lee?action=edit&amp;section=9" title="Edit section: Fourth Year"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<p>When returning to Hogwarts for the <a href="/wiki/1987%E2%80%931988_school_year" title="1987–1988 school year">1987–1988 school year</a>, Barnaby was met with the announcement from <a href="/wiki/Albus_Dumbledore" title="Albus Dumbledore">Albus Dumbledore</a> advising the students that world famous <a href="/wiki/Curse-Breaker" title="Curse-Breaker">Curse-Breaker</a> Madam <a href="/wiki/Patricia_Rakepick" title="Patricia Rakepick">Patricia Rakepick</a> would stationed at the school.<sup id="cite_ref-17" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-17">&#91;17&#93;</a></sup> A <a href="/wiki/Sleepwalking_curse" title="Sleepwalking curse">sleepwalking curse</a> quickly took over the castle, causing students to unknowingly walk into the <a href="/wiki/Forbidden_Forest" title="Forbidden Forest">Forbidden Forest</a>.
</p><p>In Professor <a href="/wiki/Silvanus_Kettleburn" title="Silvanus Kettleburn">Silvanus Kettleburn</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Care_of_Magical_Creatures" title="Care of Magical Creatures">Care of Magical Creatures</a>, Barnaby along with <a href="/wiki/Charles_Weasley" title="Charles Weasley">Charlie Weasley</a>, and Jacob's Sibling, began studying <a href="/wiki/Fire_Crab" title="Fire Crab">Fire Crabs</a>, <a href="/wiki/Bowtruckle" title="Bowtruckle">Bowtruckles</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Niffler" title="Niffler">Nifflers</a> among other magical animals. In the first class of the year, a Fire Crab scorched Barnaby's ankles. He went on to meet with Rowan, Ben, Bill, Tulip, and Jacob's sibling in the Hogwarts Library to discuss Madam Rakepick.
</p><p>When everyone was preparing for their Potions class, Barnaby inquired, "How many times do you think Snape will call me stupid today? The record is nineteen." Later in Care of Magical Creatures, Jacob's sibling asked the professor the best way to enter the Forbidden Forest. Kettleburn only agreed to tell them if they studied Bowtruckles for him.
</p><p>Bill, Charlie, Tulip, Rowan, and Barnaby, all joined Jacob's sibling in studying Bowtruckles in hopes to get information to reach the next cursed vault quickest.
</p>
<h5><span class="mw-headline" id="Celestial_Ball">Celestial Ball</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Barnaby_Lee?veaction=edit&amp;section=10" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Celestial Ball">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Barnaby_Lee?action=edit&amp;section=10" title="Edit section: Celestial Ball"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h5>
<p>During a special announcement in the <a href="/wiki/Great_Hall" title="Great Hall">Great Hall</a>, Professor Dumbledore revealed that Hogwarts would be holding special dance. The <a href="/wiki/Celestial_Ball" title="Celestial Ball">Celestial Ball</a>, which would only be for fourth years and older was of great excitement. Barnaby after the announcement asked whether the students were allowed to bring dates to the ball. While Penny was curious about if students could help plan it. Dumbledore said yes to both and the festivities began.
</p><p>There was a vote to see who would lead the Student Committee, <a href="/wiki/Penny_Haywood" title="Penny Haywood">Penny Haywood</a> or <a href="/wiki/Emily_Tyler" title="Emily Tyler">Emily Tyler</a>. While they were trying to get people to vote for them Emily continued to trash talk Penny, but in the end Penny won. Barnaby along with Tonks were both invited to help make decorations for the Ball. In the end Barnaby wore a maroon pair of <a href="/wiki/Barnaby_Lee%27s_dress_robes" title="Barnaby Lee&#39;s dress robes">Dress robes</a> with light red floral detailing around the cuffs, and enjoyed the evening with his friends.
</p>

After he agreed, Barnaby, along with Charlie and Jacob's sibling flew to the Forbidden Forest at night in search of the Cursed Vault located within which has been causing the Sleep Walking epidemic. The flight to the forest wasn't easy, and they quickly lost Charlie. After a brief search, he located Barnaby and Jacob's sibling, but were unable to locate the vault.
</p><p>After Jacob's sibling returned from the Forest a second time, this time with the need for information on <a href="/wiki/Centaur" title="Centaur">Centaur</a>'s. Barnaby, Tulip and Rowan helped search through the library for information that could aid in the search of the <a href="/wiki/Forest_Vault" title="Forest Vault">Forest Vault</a><sup id="cite_ref-18" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-18">&#91;18&#93;</a></sup>

<p>While everyone in Potions was supposed to be brewing <a href="/wiki/Doxycide" title="Doxycide">Doxycide</a> from memory, Jacob's sibling was caught with a note which Professor Snape read out loud, "Do you like me? Yes or no. Circle your answer...?" Everyone felt their secondhand embarrassment while Merula and Ismelda laughed. Later one Barnaby went to meet Tulip, Andre, and Jacob's sibling at the library to study. While Barnaby would have rather changed the topic, instead asking Jacob's sibling how many horns a <a href="/wiki/Romanian_Longhorn" title="Romanian Longhorn">Romanian Longhorn</a> has. But instead he instructed them to wait for <a href="/wiki/Rubeus_Hagrid" title="Rubeus Hagrid">Rubeus Hagrid</a>, because only he could explain the challenges the vault presented.<sup id="cite_ref-20" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-20">&#91;20&#93;</a></sup>

<h5><span id="The_Spider's_Lair"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="The_Spider.27s_Lair">The Spider's Lair</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Barnaby_Lee?veaction=edit&amp;section=13" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: The Spider&#039;s Lair">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Barnaby_Lee?action=edit&amp;section=13" title="Edit section: The Spider&#039;s Lair"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h5>
<p>After safely returning from the Forbidden Forest, everyone met at the Three Broomsticks to devise a plan which would allow them to get around whatever was guarding the Forest Vault and to get inside of it. <h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Fifth_Year">Fifth Year</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Barnaby_Lee?veaction=edit&amp;section=14" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Fifth Year">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Barnaby_Lee?action=edit&amp;section=14" title="Edit section: Fifth Year"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<p>Upon returning to Hogwarts for the <a href="/wiki/1988%E2%80%931989_school_year" title="1988–1989 school year">1988–1989 school year</a>, Barnaby immediately met up with <a href="/wiki/Charles_Weasley" title="Charles Weasley">Charlie Weasley</a>, <a href="/wiki/Liz_Tuttle" title="Liz Tuttle">Liz Tuttle</a>, <a href="/wiki/Rowan_Khanna" title="Rowan Khanna">Rowan Khanna</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Jacob%27s_sibling" title="Jacob&#39;s sibling">Jacob's sibling</a> at the start of term feast, where they discussed how they spent their summer holidays. Their discussions were broken up by Professor <a href="/wiki/Minerva_McGonagall" title="Minerva McGonagall">Minerva McGonagall</a> calling for attention, she announced that Professor <a href="/wiki/Albus_Dumbledore" title="Albus Dumbledore">Albus Dumbledore</a> was away on business, in addition to introducing <a href="/wiki/Patricia_Rakepick" title="Patricia Rakepick">Patricia Rakepick</a> as the new <a href="/wiki/Defence_Against_the_Dark_Arts" title="Defence Against the Dark Arts">Defence Against the Dark Arts</a> professor. For everyone's first class under Madame Rakepick, they were taught how to properly defend against <a href="/wiki/Ghoul" title="Ghoul">Ghouls</a>. Throughout the Defence Against the Dark Arts classes, Barnaby was forced to continuously reflect on his life when confronted by all the Dark Artefacts in class. He confided in Jacob's sibling that, "I remember seeing one of these in my parents' house," when referring to the dark items. He noted, "[his] family did like to collect things."

The Baron revealed that the person Jacob's sibling was looking for <a href="/wiki/Duncan_Ashe" title="Duncan Ashe">Duncan Ashe</a>, is dead and became a ghost which haunts the areas of Hogwarts where nobody often goes like the <a href="/wiki/Prefects%27_Bathroom" title="Prefects&#39; Bathroom">Prefects' Bathroom</a>, and the <a href="/wiki/Artefact_Room" title="Artefact Room">Artefact Room</a>.
</p><p>Everyone ended up meeting in the Library to discuss the situation of the Portrait curse, and Bill, Merula, and Jacob's sibling working with Rakepick. The verbal fight was broken up by Barnaby, citing that all the fighting reminded him of home, and that he didn't want to think about being home. Once the situation calmed down, everyone put their heads together to research the <a href="/wiki/Vault_Portrait" title="Vault Portrait">Vault Portrait</a>, which supposedly the cause of the Portrait curse.
</p><p>Even though the <a href="/wiki/Marauder%27s_Map" title="Marauder&#39;s Map">Marauder's Map</a> was taken away from Jacob's sibling, they got a good enough look at it to know they needed to search the <a href="/wiki/Classroom_3C" title="Classroom 3C">Defence Against the Dark Arts Classroom</a>, so they enlisted the help of Barnaby. Together they find a <a href="/wiki/Black_Quill" title="Black Quill">Black Quill</a> in Rakepick's desk, which after casting <a href="/wiki/Reparifarge" title="Reparifarge">Reparifarge</a> on it, there is no affect undermining the idea that it was a transfigured secret message like the quills found before. Exhausted, Barnaby then returned to his dormitory to sleep.

In preparation for the end of the year O.W.L.'s Barnaby began devoting lots of time in the library revising to make sure he got a good enough score on the exam.<sup id="cite_ref-OWL_22-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OWL-22">&#91;22&#93;</a></sup>

<h5><span id="Voyages,_Vampires,_and_Valentines"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="Voyages.2C_Vampires.2C_and_Valentines">Voyages, Vampires, and Valentines</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Barnaby_Lee?veaction=edit&amp;section=16" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Voyages, Vampires, and Valentines">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Barnaby_Lee?action=edit&amp;section=16" title="Edit section: Voyages, Vampires, and Valentines"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h5>
<p>During the week of <a href="/wiki/Valentine%27s_Day" title="Valentine&#39;s Day">Valentine's Day</a>, <a href="/wiki/Gilderoy_Lockhart" title="Gilderoy Lockhart">Gilderoy Lockhart</a> did a book signing at <a href="/wiki/Flourish_and_Blotts" title="Flourish and Blotts">Flourish and Blotts</a>, and invited many students from Hogwarts. At the event Lockhart revealed he was going to choose a handful of students for a special <a href="/wiki/Voyages,_Vampires,_and_Valentines!" title="Voyages, Vampires, and Valentines!">event</a> to be held at <a href="/wiki/Madam_Puddifoot%27s_Tea_Shop" title="Madam Puddifoot&#39;s Tea Shop">Madam Puddifoot's Tea Shop</a> for his newest book <a href="/wiki/Voyages_with_Vampires" title="Voyages with Vampires">Voyages with Vampires</a>. Barnaby, along with <a href="/wiki/Andre_Egwu" title="Andre Egwu">Andre Egwu</a>, <a href="/wiki/Talbott_Winger" title="Talbott Winger">Talbott Winger</a>, <a href="/wiki/Jacob%27s_sibling" title="Jacob&#39;s sibling">Jacob's sibling</a>, and a few others were chosen to participate in the event including in it's planning.<sup id="cite_ref-HM5VD2_26-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HM5VD2-26">&#91;26&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Barnaby was tasked with preparing roses for the event using the <a href="/wiki/Rose_Growth" title="Rose Growth">Rose Growth</a> charm, while everyone else prepared their own parts for the event. During the preparations for the party, Barnaby asked Jacob's sibling to be his Valentine for the event.<sup id="cite_ref-HM5VD2_26-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HM5VD2-26">&#91;26&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>On <a href="/wiki/14_February" title="14 February">14 February</a>, at the event, Lockhart tried to gain as much information as he could on the <a href="/wiki/Cursed_Vaults" title="Cursed Vaults">Cursed Vaults</a> for a new book, he wanted to write. He attempted to <a href="/wiki/Memory_Charm" title="Memory Charm">Obliviate</a> everyone's memory, before disappearing. but the charm failed only making them forget bits and pieces.<sup id="cite_ref-27" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-27">&#91;27&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h5><span class="mw-headline" id="Ordinary_Wizarding_Levels">Ordinary Wizarding Levels</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Barnaby_Lee?veaction=edit&amp;section=17" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Ordinary Wizarding Levels">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Barnaby_Lee?action=edit&amp;section=17" title="Edit section: Ordinary Wizarding Levels"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h5>
<p>Towards the end of the year, while gathered in the <a href="/wiki/Great_Hall" title="Great Hall">Great Hall</a> Professor Dumbledore announced that <a href="/wiki/Ordinary_Wizarding_Level" title="Ordinary Wizarding Level">Ordinary Wizarding Level</a> testing, or O.W.L.'s would be held soon. After the announcement was over Barnaby immediately found Liz to discuss studying. When taking his potions OWL which was administered in the classroom and not in the Great Hall, Barnaby sat at the same table with Liz, Tonks, Tulip, <a href="/wiki/Chiara_Lobosca" title="Chiara Lobosca">Chiara</a>, Badeea, Jae and Diego.
</p><p>Later that evening Barnaby, Ismelda, Liz, and Merula revised their notes in preparation for their next O.W.L.'s in the <a href="/wiki/Slytherin_Common_Room" title="Slytherin Common Room">Slytherin Common Room</a>. Everyone revised for their Charms O.W.L. the next day. The following day, Barnaby along with everyone else took his <a href="/wiki/Transfiguration_(class)" title="Transfiguration (class)">Transfiguration</a> and <a href="/wiki/Herbology" title="Herbology">Herbology</a> O.W.L.s. That evening Barnaby, Charlie, Liz, and Jacob's sibling all met in the forbidden forest to prepare for their Care of Magical Creatures O.W.L.
</p><p>While in the forest Barnaby revealed he knew he wasn't the smartest wizard at Hogwarts, he's not great at exams and was worried about not getting a high enough score on his Care of Magical Creatures O.W.L. to become a <a href="/wiki/Magizoologist" title="Magizoologist">Magizoologist</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-OWL_22-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OWL-22">&#91;22&#93;</a></sup> Jacob's sibling began quizzing Barnaby certain he'd get the answer's right, but he didn't, only making him feel worse.
</p><p>Taking a break, the group assisted Kettleburn look for his missing <a href="/wiki/Bowtruckle" title="Bowtruckle">Bowtruckles</a> which had run off. While searching Barnaby knew Bowtruckles preferred, "...trees that are made of wand quality-wood." Thanks to him the Bowtruckles were found, giving him the confidence he'd do well on the next O.W.L. Barnaby took his Care of Magical Creature O.W.L. and did well on it. Then he took his History of Magic, and lastly his Defence Against the Dark Arts O.W.L.
</p><p><a href="/wiki/Care_of_Magical_Creatures" title="Care of Magical Creatures">Care of Magical Creatures</a> class began by Professor Kettleburn asking Barnaby, "can you tell me what kind of diet you'd feed to a sick unicorn?" With an inquisitive look Barnaby responded, "Not buttered peas, that's for sure!"<sup id="cite_ref-28" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-28">&#91;28&#93;</a></sup> Shortly after, Jacob's sibling revealed they needed help brainstorming creature ideas that would cause enough chaos to convince <a href="/wiki/Peeves" title="Peeves">Peeves</a> to hand over the information about the cursed vault they need. Barnaby proposed "an <a href="/wiki/Acromantula" title="Acromantula">Acromantula</a> at Hogwarts would freak out a lot of people." Liz proposed luring a <a href="/wiki/Troll" title="Troll">Troll</a> out of the <a href="/wiki/Forbidden_Forest" title="Forbidden Forest">Forbidden Forest</a>.
</p><p>After class <a href="/wiki/Diego_Caplan" title="Diego Caplan">Diego Caplan</a> and Jacob's sibling went to duel at the Training Grounds, Barnaby and Andre excitedly watched to see who would prove the stronger dueler. After Diego lost, Jacob's sibling revealed to him the plan to lure a Troll to the castle.
</p><p>While everyone was preparing for dinner in the great hall, prior to the arrival of professors, <a href="/wiki/Nymphadora_Tonks" title="Nymphadora Tonks">Tonks</a>, Tulip, Jae, and Jacob's sibling started unleashing chaos upon the student body in order to please Peeves. The four of them started throwing jinxed <a href="/wiki/Ever-Bashing_Boomerang" title="Ever-Bashing Boomerang">Ever-Bashing Boomerang</a> at the other students. Panic ensued and Barnaby quickly leapt to defend his friend Ismelda from being hit with the boomerangs, even though he didn't return her feelings he still cared about her safety.
</p><p>To evade the boomerangs all the students ran out to the training grounds, where they were all quickly confronted by a massive mountain troll. Jacob's sibling with some help was able to apprehend the dangerous creature before it injured any students. After the experience with the troll, Barnaby and other students congregated in the Clock Tower Court yard where <a href="/wiki/Badeea_Ali" title="Badeea Ali">Badeea Ali</a> cast a new invented spell <a href="/wiki/Star_Shower_Spell" title="Star Shower Spell">Stella Cascadia</a>. The spell resulted in falling stars crashing into the ground causing explosions and disorientation.
</p><p>Much later, Barnaby was with Ismelda in the Great Hall chatting when, Ben, Merula, Bill, and Jacob's sibling all <a href="/wiki/Apparition" title="Apparition">Apparited</a> into the room. Merula was still in tense pain from the <a href="/wiki/Cruciatus_Curse" title="Cruciatus Curse">Cruciatus Curse</a> which Rakepick cast upon her. Barnaby waited anxiously with Tulip, Andre, and Bill while the Dumbledore, <a href="/wiki/Poppy_Pomfrey" title="Poppy Pomfrey">Madam Pomfrey</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Severus_Snape" title="Severus Snape">Professor Snape</a> took Merula to the <a href="/wiki/Hospital_Wing" title="Hospital Wing">Hospital Wing</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-29" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-29">&#91;29&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>At the end of the year, Barnaby was invited to Bill's surprise graduation party being held at the Three Broomsticks.<sup id="cite_ref-30" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-30">&#91;30&#93;</a></sup> At the end of the year feast, Barnaby enjoyed himself before having to return home for the summer.<sup id="cite_ref-31" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-31">&#91;31&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Sixth_Year">Sixth Year</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Barnaby_Lee?veaction=edit&amp;section=18" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Sixth Year">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Barnaby_Lee?action=edit&amp;section=18" title="Edit section: Sixth Year"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<p>Returning to Hogwarts for the <a href="/wiki/1989%E2%80%931990_school_year" title="1989–1990 school year">1989–1990 school year</a>, at the <a href="/wiki/Start-of-Term_Feast" title="Start-of-Term Feast">Start-of-Term Feast</a>, Barnaby resigned himself to sit contently to listen to what his friends did over their <a href="/wiki/Summer_holidays" title="Summer holidays">Summer holidays</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-32" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-32">&#91;32&#93;</a></sup> Barnaby had started painting lessons with Badeea in an attempt to make himself a "Renaissance Wizard." Afterwards Barnaby gives his persective about <a href="/wiki/Beatrice_Haywood" title="Beatrice Haywood">Beatrice</a> and <a href="/wiki/Ismelda_Murk" title="Ismelda Murk">Ismelda</a> becoming friends.<sup id="cite_ref-33" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-33">&#91;33&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>With the new Defence Against the Dark Arts professor "particularly useless," according to Charlie. Bill, taking time away from being a <a href="/wiki/Curse-Breaker" title="Curse-Breaker">Curse-Breaker</a> with <a href="/wiki/Gringott" title="Gringott">Gringott</a>'s began to teach Defence Against the Dark Arts after hours to Charlie, Jae, Merula, Ismelda, Badeea, Liz, Andrea, Tulip, Tonks, Ben, Diego, Rowan, Penny, Barnaby, and Jacob's sibling.<sup id="cite_ref-34" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-34">&#91;34&#93;</a></sup>

<figure class="thumb tright show-info-icon" style="width: 252px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/4/44/Barnaby_Lee.jpeg/revision/latest?cb=20180603221435" class="image"><img alt="" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw%3D%3D" decoding="async" width="250" height="441" class="thumbimage lazyload" data-image-name="Barnaby Lee.jpeg" data-image-key="Barnaby_Lee.jpeg" data-src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/4/44/Barnaby_Lee.jpeg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/250?cb=20180603221435" /></a> 	<noscript><a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/4/44/Barnaby_Lee.jpeg/revision/latest?cb=20180603221435" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/4/44/Barnaby_Lee.jpeg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/250?cb=20180603221435" decoding="async" width="250" height="441" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="Barnaby Lee.jpeg" data-image-key="Barnaby_Lee.jpeg" data-src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/4/44/Barnaby_Lee.jpeg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/250?cb=20180603221435" /></a></noscript> 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:Barnaby_Lee.jpeg" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption">Barnaby Lee</p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure>
<p>Barnaby had spiky, dark brown hair and bright green eyes. He was quite burly and tall, reaching the same height as <a href="/wiki/William_Weasley" title="William Weasley">William Weasley</a>, and wore three silver rings on his right hand, and a silver bracelet on his left.
</p><p>Barnaby generally wore his school uniform well put together under his robes when necessary. Otherwise he dressed with the school uniform in a rather dishevelled relaxed manner. Although considered attractive by his peers, Barnaby was very self conscious of his bushy eyebrows.<sup id="cite_ref-39" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-39">&#91;39&#93;</a></sup> Though he did note that his sculpted cheekbones went with almost any outfit.
</p><p><a href="/wiki/Ismelda_Murk" title="Ismelda Murk">Ismelda Murk</a> described him as having sparkling eyes and chiselled cheekbones. Her flattering description of him was most likely due to her romantic crush on him.<sup id="cite_ref-40" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-40">&#91;40&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Personality_and_traits">Personality and traits</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Barnaby_Lee?veaction=edit&amp;section=20" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Personality and traits">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Barnaby_Lee?action=edit&amp;section=20" title="Edit section: Personality and traits"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<div class="quote">
<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;Harry Potter: Hogwarts Mystery">"<i>If what's inside the Cursed Vaults will make me stronger then I want it.</i>"</span></dd>
<dd>&#8212;Barnaby on the contents of the Vaults.<sup class="noprint"><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter:_Hogwarts_Mystery" title="Harry Potter: Hogwarts Mystery">[src]</a></sup></dd>
<dd></dd></dl></div><p>Barnaby was shown not to be the brightest student, nor particularly cunning, however, Barnaby did possess the <a href="/wiki/Slytherin" title="Slytherin">Slytherin</a> trait of ambition, in his case the ambition to become stronger.<sup id="cite_ref-y3ch8_7-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-y3ch8-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup> He was not particularly brave or strong-willed in his early Hogwarts years, as he was often easily manipulated and pushed around by <a href="/wiki/Merula_Snyde" title="Merula Snyde">Merula Snyde</a> and <a href="/wiki/Ismelda_Murk" title="Ismelda Murk">Ismelda Murk</a>. He also suffered from Coulrophobia, the mortal and irrational fear of clowns. In the <a href="/wiki/Vault_of_Fear" title="Vault of Fear">Vault of Fear</a>, <a href="/wiki/Jacob%27s_sibling" title="Jacob&#39;s sibling">Jacob's sibling</a> used the <a href="/wiki/Boggart-Banishing_Spell" title="Boggart-Banishing Spell">Boggart-Banishing Spell</a> to turn <a href="/wiki/Boggart" title="Boggart">boggarts</a> of <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a> into clowns to produce laughter, though Barnaby later admitted that he found them even more intimidating after.<sup id="cite_ref-y3ch9_3-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-y3ch9-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Despite his seeming cowardice, Barnaby was surprisingly adventurous, curious and daring, as he often participated in forbidden and dangerous missions with <a href="/wiki/Jacob%27s_sibling" title="Jacob&#39;s sibling">Jacob's sibling</a> and their friends, showing the <a href="/wiki/Slytherin" title="Slytherin">Slytherin</a> traits of determination and fraternity. Contrary to the usual members of the house however, Barnaby is very openhearted and compassionate.
</p><p>Unlike most wizards who depended solely on their <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magical</a> ability, Barnaby readily relied on his brute physical strength, which often helped him to succeed over others, such as when duelling Merula. Although he was a strong duellist, Barnaby also tended to act impulsively, often when attempting to protect his friends, and also showed at times to be self-sacrificing. He demonstrated his bravery, for example when he prevented <a href="/wiki/Rowan_Khanna" title="Rowan Khanna">Rowan Khanna</a>, who was bewitched at the time by "<a href="/wiki/R" title="R">R</a>", from <a href="/wiki/Curse" title="Curse">cursing</a> Jacob's sibling, saving them from harm.<sup id="cite_ref-Broken_Bonds_23-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Broken_Bonds-23">&#91;23&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>His uncle, <a href="/wiki/Cecil_Lee" title="Cecil Lee">Cecil Lee</a>, proudly told Jacob's sibling that Barnaby was the smartest person in the <a href="/wiki/Lee_family" title="Lee family">Lee family</a>, meaning he was significantly more intelligent and logical than any other member of family, despite his often poor self-confidence. Barnaby primarily displayed his intelligence through his encyclopaedic knowledge of <a href="/wiki/Magizoology" title="Magizoology">Magizoology</a>, and his excellent academic performance in <a href="/wiki/Care_of_Magical_Creatures" title="Care of Magical Creatures">Care of Magical Creatures</a> class. The fact that Barnaby can be seen attending many classes in his sixth year and that it is even mentioned that he surprisingly passed all his O.W.L. exams, proves that Barnaby might it fact be a very intelligent wizard.
</p><p><a href="/wiki/Pitts" title="Pitts">Pitts</a> noted that Barnaby has an appetite "like a <a href="/wiki/Mountain_troll" title="Mountain troll">Mountain troll</a>" and <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_kitchens" title="Hogwarts kitchens">Hogwarts kitchens</a> needed to increase their sandwich production.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Magical_abilities_and_skills">Magical abilities and skills</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Barnaby_Lee?veaction=edit&amp;section=21" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Barnaby_Lee?action=edit&amp;section=21" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li><b><a href="/wiki/Duelling" title="Duelling">Duelling</a></b>: Barnaby was a proficient duellist and noted to be the strongest duellist in his year by many of his peers, with an impressive talent for <a href="/wiki/Martial_magic" title="Martial magic">martial magic</a>. Barnaby best demonstrated his duelling talents when he successfully managed to hold up against both <a href="/wiki/Merula_Snyde" title="Merula Snyde">Merula Snyde</a> and <a href="/wiki/Ismelda_Murk" title="Ismelda Murk">Ismelda Murk</a> in his <a href="/wiki/Third_year" title="Third year">third year</a>.</li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Care_of_Magical_Creatures" title="Care of Magical Creatures">Care of Magical Creatures</a></b>: Barnaby was highly knowledgeable of various <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magical</a> <a href="/wiki/Creature" title="Creature">creatures</a> and was capable of discussing them in conversations with others. Barnaby ardently studied this subject whilst at Hogwarts, showing his passion, interest and talent in <a href="/wiki/Magizoology" title="Magizoology">Magizoology</a>. This is also proven by the fact that he was able to study the subject further at N.E.W.T.- level.</li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Flight" title="Flight">Flying</a></b>: Barnaby proved himself to be an accomplished <a href="/wiki/Broomstick" title="Broomstick">broomstick</a> flyer, as he successfully managed to fly to the <a href="/wiki/Forbidden_Forest" title="Forbidden Forest">Forbidden Forest</a> via his broomstick with <a href="/wiki/Charles_Weasley" title="Charles Weasley">Charlie Weasley</a> and <a href="/wiki/Jacob%27s_sibling" title="Jacob&#39;s sibling">Jacob's sibling</a> in his <a href="/wiki/Fourth_year" title="Fourth year">fourth year</a>, without being noticed by any Hogwarts staff member.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Defence_Against_the_Dark_Arts" title="Defence Against the Dark Arts"><b>Defence Against the Dark Arts</b></a>: Barnaby was skilled in Defence Against the Dark Arts, as he was to achieve an O.W.L. in the subject and study it further at N.E.W.T.-level, thus showing aptitude in the subject.</li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/History_of_Magic" title="History of Magic">History of Magic</a></b>: Barnaby was skilled in the field of History of Magic, as he was able to achieve an O.W.L. in the subject and study it further at N.E.W.T.-level, thus showing aptitude in the subject.</li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Divination" title="Divination">Divination</a></b>: Barnaby was skilled in the field of Divination, as he was able to achieve an O.W.L. in the subject and study it further at N.E.W.T.-level, even though he stated that the subject gave him headaches.</li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Astronomy_(class)" title="Astronomy (class)">Astronomy</a></b>: Barnaby was skilled in Astronomy, as he was able to achieve an O.W.L. in the subject and study it further at N.E.W.T.-level, thus showing aptitude in the subject.</li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Healing_magic" title="Healing magic">Healing magic</a></b>; Barnaby had some skill in the field of healing, as he was able to successfully cast the <i><a href="/wiki/Episkey" title="Episkey">Episkey</a></i> <a href="/wiki/Healing_Spell" class="mw-redirect" title="Healing Spell">healing Spell</a>.</li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Charm" title="Charm">Charms</a></b>: Barnaby was noted to be the strongest duellist in his year, thus meaning he was exceptionally skilled in charm-work. He had some mastery over some duelling spells like the <a href="/wiki/Disarming_Charm" title="Disarming Charm">Disarming Charm</a>, the <a href="/wiki/Tickling_Charm" title="Tickling Charm">Tickling Charm</a>, the <a href="/wiki/Freezing_Charm" title="Freezing Charm">Freezing Charm</a>. He was also able to successfully use the <a href="/wiki/Herbivicus_Charm" title="Herbivicus Charm">Herbivicus Charm</a>. He also mentioned that he achieved an O.W.L in this subject.</li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Dark_Arts" title="Dark Arts">Dark Arts</a></b>: Barnaby was shown to be possess a degree of skill in the Dark Arts, being able to successfully cast <a href="/wiki/Dark_charm" title="Dark charm">dark charms</a> such as the <a href="/wiki/Knockback_Jinx" title="Knockback Jinx">Knockback Jinx</a>, the <a href="/wiki/Full_Body-Bind_Curse" title="Full Body-Bind Curse">Full Body-Bind Curse</a> and the <a href="/wiki/Ear-Shrivelling_Curse" title="Ear-Shrivelling Curse">Ear-Shrivelling Curse</a>.</li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Transfiguration" title="Transfiguration">Transfiguration</a></b>: Although he struggled at first with this branch of magic, he eventually learned how to ably cast the <a href="/wiki/Lovebird_to_Love_Note" title="Lovebird to Love Note">Lovebird to Love Note</a> spell in his fifth year, and is shown to be able to cast <a href="/wiki/Vera_Verto" title="Vera Verto">Vera Verto</a> and the Feather Duster to Ferret switching spell in his O.W.L exam. It is stated that he passed this subject.</li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Herbology" title="Herbology">Herbology</a></b>: Barnaby mentioned during Bill's farewell party, which happened chronologically after the O.W.L. exams, that he surprisingly did not fail any subjects, which means that he achieved an O.W.L. in the subject.</li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Potions_(class)" title="Potions (class)">Potions</a></b>: Barnaby must have achieved an 'Outstanding' or 'Exceeds Expectations' in his O.W.L. exam to be able to advance to the N.E.W.T.-level class in his sixth year, much to the annoyance of Professor <a href="/wiki/Severus_Snape" title="Severus Snape">Severus Snape</a>.</li></ul>

<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Parents">Parents</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Barnaby_Lee?veaction=edit&amp;section=24" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Parents">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Barnaby_Lee?action=edit&amp;section=24" title="Edit section: Parents"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>

</p><h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Merula_Snyde">Merula Snyde</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Barnaby_Lee?veaction=edit&amp;section=26" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Merula Snyde">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Barnaby_Lee?action=edit&amp;section=26" title="Edit section: Merula Snyde"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Merula Snyde was one of the first people to befriend Barnaby at Hogwarts. She quickly started to manipulate him, and convinced him to join her <a href="/wiki/Merula_Snyde%27s_gang" title="Merula Snyde&#39;s gang">gang</a>. He did her dirty work until they were in their Third Year, when Jacob's sibling befriended him and made Barnaby realise he was being used. Despite Barnaby leaving her gang, he still continued to worry about her, going so far as to tell Jacob's sibling to go easy on her and that she's going through a rough time.
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Liz_Tuttle">Liz Tuttle</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Barnaby_Lee?veaction=edit&amp;section=27" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Liz Tuttle">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Barnaby_Lee?action=edit&amp;section=27" title="Edit section: Liz Tuttle"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Barnaby and Liz were good friends, they started bonding over their love of <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magical</a> <a href="/wiki/Creature" title="Creature">creatures</a>, even studying together for their <a href="/wiki/Ordinary_Wizarding_Level" title="Ordinary Wizarding Level">Ordinary Wizarding Levels</a> in <a href="/wiki/Care_of_Magical_Creatures" title="Care of Magical Creatures">Care of Magical Creatures</a>.
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Ismelda_Murk">Ismelda Murk</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Barnaby_Lee?veaction=edit&amp;section=28" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Ismelda Murk">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Barnaby_Lee?action=edit&amp;section=28" title="Edit section: Ismelda Murk"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Ismelda Murk was the final member of <a href="/wiki/Merula_Snyde%27s_gang" title="Merula Snyde&#39;s gang">Merula Snyde's gang</a>, completing the trio. She, like Merula, ridiculed Barnaby for his less than spectacular intelligence. A <a href="/wiki/Slytherin" title="Slytherin">Slytherin</a> in Barnaby's year, she developed a crush on him at some point in their schooling, and in their fifth year admitted it to him. Initially he misunderstood her confession of her feelings, but when he realised his error and went to clear it up with her, he overheard her plot to trick him into drinking a love potion. But she was talked out of it by Jacob's sibling. Despite not returning her feelings, like with Merula, Barnaby still cared for Ismelda, they would socialise in the Great Hall. He even went so far as to protect her from the <a href="/wiki/Ever-Bashing_Boomerang" title="Ever-Bashing Boomerang">Ever-Bashing Boomerang</a>'s that were let loose on the student body.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Barnaby_Lee?veaction=edit&amp;section=29" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Barnaby_Lee?action=edit&amp;section=29" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p><i><a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Barnaby" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Barnaby">Barnaby</a></i> is the English form of the Greek name <i>Barnabas,</i><sup id="cite_ref-41" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-41">&#91;41&#93;</a></sup><i> </i>meaning "son of consolation."<sup id="cite_ref-42" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-42">&#91;42&#93;</a></sup> In Acts in the New Testament the byname Barnabas was given to a man named Joseph, a Jew from Cyprus who was a companion of Paul on his missionary journeys. The original Aramaic form is unattested, but it may be from <i>בּר נביא (bar naviya')</i> meaning "son of the prophet",<sup id="cite_ref-43" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-43">&#91;43&#93;</a></sup> though in Acts 4:36 it is claimed that the name means "son of encouragement." All these names carry the meaning of a person who acts as the trusted companion to a person in power, much as Barnaby acted as a friend and confidant to Jacob's sibling.
</p><p><i>Lee</i> comes from the old english word <i><a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Leah_(given_name)" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Leah (given name)">Leah</a></i><sup id="cite_ref-44" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-44">&#91;44&#93;</a></sup> meaning "woodland, clearing."<sup id="cite_ref-45" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-45">&#91;45&#93;</a></sup> But the name <i>Leah</i> like most names carried different meanings in different cultures. The Celtic-Gaelic meaning of the name is, "the light of the sun."
</p>

<ul><li>In <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter:_Hogwarts_Mystery" title="Harry Potter: Hogwarts Mystery">Harry Potter: Hogwarts Mystery</a></i>, Barnaby is unlocked as a friend upon the completion of Chapter 8 of Year 3.
<ul><li>Upon reaching maximum friendship at level 10 with Barnaby, a unique clothing item is gifted to the player: a grey sweater with the house crest of the player on it.</li>
<li>He is one of the four characters (along with <a href="/wiki/Merula_Snyde" title="Merula Snyde">Merula Snyde</a>, <a href="/wiki/Penny_Haywood" title="Penny Haywood">Penny Haywood</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Andre_Egwu" title="Andre Egwu">Andre Egwu</a>) available as romance options for all four dating side quests: Celestial Ball, First Date, Valentine's Day, and Festival Fun. As with all other options, this can be done regardless of Jacob's sibling's gender.</li></ul></li>
<li>Barnaby's scent is fresh laundry and a hint of sandalwood.</li>
<li>In <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter:_Hogwarts_Mystery" title="Harry Potter: Hogwarts Mystery">Harry Potter: Hogwarts Mystery</a></i>, Barnaby stated that he loves <a href="/wiki/Niffler" title="Niffler">Nifflers</a>, Swimming and <a href="/wiki/Shepherd%27s_pie" title="Shepherd&#39;s pie">Shepherd's pie</a>.</li>
<li>Although it is not directly stated, it can be assumed that Barnaby lives with his grandmother when he is not at Hogwarts.</li>
<li>Barnaby, along with most of the other male characters in <i>Harry Potter: Hogwarts Mystery</i>, is voiced by American voice actor <a href="/wiki/Chris_Okawa" title="Chris Okawa">Chris Okawa</a>.</li>
<li>Barnaby might be bisexual as (regardless of selected gender for Jacob's sibling) he confessed that he liked them a lot after Jacob's sibling prevented Ismelda from using Love Potion on Barnaby.</li>
<li>Barnaby being confused by Jacob's sibling's niceness while being misled into thinking they are evil by <a href="/wiki/Merula_Snyde" title="Merula Snyde">Merula Snyde</a> echoes <a href="/wiki/Hannah_Abbott" title="Hannah Abbott">Hannah Abbott</a>'s conflicted attitude towards <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> due to <a href="/wiki/Ernest_Macmillan" title="Ernest Macmillan">Ernest Macmillan</a> regarding suspicions of Harry being the Heir of Slytherin in their second year.</li></ul>
