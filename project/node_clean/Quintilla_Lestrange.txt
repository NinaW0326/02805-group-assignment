She married her second cousin <a href="/wiki/Fulcran_Lestrange_(III)" title="Fulcran Lestrange (III)">Fulcran Lestrange</a> and had a daughter with him, <a href="/wiki/Noz%C3%A9a_Lestrange" title="Nozéa Lestrange">Nozéa</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
