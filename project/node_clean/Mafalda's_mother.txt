The <b>mother</b> of <a href="/wiki/Mafalda" title="Mafalda">Mafalda</a> was a <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggle</a> woman. She was married to an estranged <a href="/wiki/Molly_Weasley%27s_second_cousin" title="Molly Weasley&#39;s second cousin">second cousin</a> of <a href="/wiki/Molly_Weasley" title="Molly Weasley">Molly Weasley</a>.

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Mafalda%27s_mother?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Mafalda%27s_mother?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li><a href="/wiki/J._K._Rowling" title="J. K. Rowling">J. K. Rowling</a> has revealed that <a href="/wiki/Mafalda" title="Mafalda">Mafalda</a> and her parents were characters she once planned to include in <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Goblet_of_Fire" title="Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire">Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire</a></i>. After discovering their daughter was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch</a>, Mafalda's parents were supposed to visit the <a href="/wiki/Weasley_family" title="Weasley family">Weasley family</a>, hoping that they would help introduce her to the wizarding world. However, Rowling ultimately decided to cut Mafalda out of the plot, thus calling into question the canonicity of her and her parents.<sup id="cite_ref-mafalda_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-mafalda-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup></li></ul>
