<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Raymond?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Raymond?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_life">Early life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Raymond?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Early life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Raymond?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Early life"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Raymond was born between <a href="/wiki/1974" title="1974">1974</a> and <a href="/wiki/1980" title="1980">1980</a>, if not earlier. He was accepted into <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> and was <a href="/wiki/Sorting_ceremony" title="Sorting ceremony">sorted</a> into <a href="/wiki/Ravenclaw" title="Ravenclaw">Ravenclaw House</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-PS1_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PS1-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Cat_in_the_bottom_of_the_well">Cat in the bottom of the well</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Raymond?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Cat in the bottom of the well">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Raymond?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Cat in the bottom of the well"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> first met Raymond in the Hogwarts Grounds, in <a href="/wiki/1991" title="1991">1991</a>. The boy was sitting next to a well and when Harry went closer, he told him that <a href="/wiki/Raymond%27s_kitten" title="Raymond&#39;s kitten">his kitten</a> fell into the well. Later, Harry was in a <a href="/wiki/Potions_(class)" title="Potions (class)">potions</a> side-room and a cloaked <a href="/wiki/Quirinus_Quirrell" title="Quirinus Quirrell">Quirinus Quirrell</a> blasted him in a potions storeroom with the <i><a href="/wiki/Deprimo" title="Deprimo">Deprimo</a></i> <a href="/wiki/Charm" title="Charm">charm</a>. After defeating some <a href="/wiki/Troll" title="Troll">Trolls</a> Harry went into the bottom of the well and saw Raymond on top. That was the first time he mentioned his name to Harry. After giving back <a href="/wiki/Raymond%27s_kitten" title="Raymond&#39;s kitten">his kitten</a>, Raymond threw a <a href="/wiki/Chocolate_Frog_Card" title="Chocolate Frog Card">Chocolate Frog Card</a> to Harry.<sup id="cite_ref-PS1_2-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PS1-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Raymond?veaction=edit&amp;section=4" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Raymond?action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The name "Raymond" comes From the Germanic name Raginmund, composed of the elements ragin "advice" and mund "protector".
