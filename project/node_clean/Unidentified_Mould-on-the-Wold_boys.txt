<p><b>Three Muggle boys</b> who lived in the town of <a href="/wiki/Mould-on-the-Wold" title="Mould-on-the-Wold">Mould-on-the-Wold</a> during the <a href="/wiki/19th_century" title="19th century">19th century</a> bore witness to <a href="/wiki/Ariana_Dumbledore" title="Ariana Dumbledore">Ariana Dumbledore</a>, a young <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch</a> who lived with her <a href="/wiki/Dumbledore_family" title="Dumbledore family">family</a> nearby, performing <a href="/wiki/Underage_magic" title="Underage magic">Underage magic</a>, which scared them into "trying to stop the little freak from doing it" by abusing her, after which point she was mentally traumatised, resulting in a lack of ability to control her magic. Ariana's father, <a href="/wiki/Percival_Dumbledore" title="Percival Dumbledore">Percival</a>, went after the boys and attacked them, possibly through use of the <a href="/wiki/Cruciatus_Curse" title="Cruciatus Curse">Cruciatus Curse</a>, which resulted in the <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">Ministry of Magic</a> sending him to <a href="/wiki/Azkaban" title="Azkaban">Azkaban</a>. Percival would not satisfactorily explain why he had done it, knowing that his daughter would be taken to <a href="/wiki/St_Mungo%27s_Hospital_for_Magical_Maladies_and_Injuries" title="St Mungo&#39;s Hospital for Magical Maladies and Injuries">St Mungo's Hospital for Magical Maladies and Injuries</a> for the rest of her life as a hazard to the <a href="/wiki/International_Statute_of_Wizarding_Secrecy" title="International Statute of Wizarding Secrecy">International Statute of Wizarding Secrecy</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-DH28_1-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH28-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
