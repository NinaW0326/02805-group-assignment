<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Female_Death_Eater_at_the_Graveyard?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Female_Death_Eater_at_the_Graveyard?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="First_Wizarding_War">First Wizarding War</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Female_Death_Eater_at_the_Graveyard?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: First Wizarding War">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Female_Death_Eater_at_the_Graveyard?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: First Wizarding War"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>In order to have answered Voldemort's call on the night of his return, this witch&#160;must have been a Death Eater during the <a href="/wiki/First_Wizarding_War" title="First Wizarding War">First Wizarding War</a>, and had to have somehow avoided <a href="/wiki/Azkaban" title="Azkaban">imprisonment</a>.
</p>
<h3><span id="Voldemort's_return"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="Voldemort.27s_return">Voldemort's return</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Female_Death_Eater_at_the_Graveyard?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Voldemort&#039;s return">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Female_Death_Eater_at_the_Graveyard?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Voldemort&#039;s return"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<figure class="thumb tleft show-info-icon" style="width: 242px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/f/fa/Malfoysvoldemort.PNG/revision/latest?cb=20090810152535" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/f/fa/Malfoysvoldemort.PNG/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/240?cb=20090810152535" decoding="async" width="240" height="151" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="Malfoysvoldemort.PNG" data-image-key="Malfoysvoldemort.PNG" /></a> 	 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:Malfoysvoldemort.PNG" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption"><a href="/wiki/Lucius_Malfoy" title="Lucius Malfoy">Lucius Malfoy</a> standing with her whilst watching <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Voldemort</a> torture <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a></p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure>
<p>This witch&#160;arrived at the <a href="/wiki/Little_Hangleton_graveyard" title="Little Hangleton graveyard">graveyard</a> in <a href="/wiki/Little_Hangleton" title="Little Hangleton">Little Hangleton</a> after <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a> summoned his <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a> there on the night of <a href="/wiki/24_June" title="24 June">24 June</a>, <a href="/wiki/1995" title="1995">1995</a>. She <a href="/wiki/Apparition" title="Apparition">Apparated</a> into the area, but, unlike the other Death Eaters, was not wearing a Death Eater mask and hood.
</p><p>Walking around his circled followers, Voldemort viciously greeted <a href="/wiki/Crabbe" title="Crabbe">Crabbe Snr</a>, <a href="/wiki/Walden_Macnair" title="Walden Macnair">Walden Macnair</a> and <a href="/wiki/Goyle" title="Goyle">Goyle Snr</a>. When he came to the woman, instead of attacking her like the others, he simply looked at her as she bowed down to him, then moved on to greet <a href="/wiki/Lucius_Malfoy" title="Lucius Malfoy">Lucius Malfoy</a>.
</p><p>She witnessed <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> being put under the <a href="/wiki/Cruciatus_Curse" title="Cruciatus Curse">Cruciatus Curse</a> by Voldemort. When Harry made to escape, she stood by, as Voldemort had ordered the Death Eaters not to do anything.<sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Female_Death_Eater_at_the_Graveyard?veaction=edit&amp;section=4" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Female_Death_Eater_at_the_Graveyard?action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>This woman was portrayed by <a href="/wiki/Olivia_Higginbottom" title="Olivia Higginbottom">Olivia Higginbottom</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Goblet_of_Fire_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire (film)">film adaptation</a> of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Goblet_of_Fire" title="Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire">Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire</a></i>.</li>
<li>This woman may be <a target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener" class="external text" href="https://harrypotter.fandom.com/wiki/Narcissa_Malfoy">Narcissa&#160;Malfoy</a> though Narcissa was not an official Death Eater. It's possible that the filmmakers were unaware of this at the time. It should be noted that this woman did not wear a mask and her hood wasn't on.</li></ul>
