<b>Josiah Plunkett</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Quidditch" title="Quidditch">Quidditch</a> referee.<sup id="cite_ref-wizardsp_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-wizardsp-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> He oversaw the match between the <a href="/wiki/Appleby_Arrows" title="Appleby Arrows">Appleby Arrows</a> and the <a href="/wiki/Pride_of_Portree" title="Pride of Portree">Pride of Portree</a> during which <a href="/wiki/Dougal_McBride" title="Dougal McBride">Dougal McBride</a>, the <a href="/wiki/Seeker" title="Seeker">Seeker</a> for the Pride of Portree, <a href="/wiki/Transfiguration" title="Transfiguration">turned</a> the head of the Arrows Seeker, <a href="/wiki/Gregory_Cotton" title="Gregory Cotton">Gregory Cotton</a>, into a <a href="/wiki/Cabbage" title="Cabbage">cabbage</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-wizardsp_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-wizardsp-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-wizardsm_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-wizardsm-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-wizardsc_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-wizardsc-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> McBride defended his actions to Plunkett by claiming that Cotton had hit him with a <a href="/wiki/Jelly-Fingers_Curse" title="Jelly-Fingers Curse">Jelly-Fingers Curse</a> while they were both racing towards the <a href="/wiki/Golden_Snitch" title="Golden Snitch">Snitch</a> and thus deserved to be "cabbaged."<sup id="cite_ref-wizardsp_1-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-wizardsp-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-wizardsm_2-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-wizardsm-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
