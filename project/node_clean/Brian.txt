<b>Brian</b> was a boy who lived at <a href="/wiki/Godric%27s_Hollow" title="Godric&#39;s Hollow">Godric's Hollow</a>. On the night of <a href="/wiki/31_October" title="31 October">31 October</a> <a href="/wiki/1981" title="1981">1981</a>, he dressed up as a <a href="/wiki/Vampire" title="Vampire">vampire</a> for a <a href="/wiki/Hallowe%27en" title="Hallowe&#39;en">Hallowe'en</a> party in his house: mistaking a hooded <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a> for a friend who was late for the party, he approached him, only to be stricken with fear as he peered into the man's hood and quickly stumbled back into his yard.<sup id="cite_ref-:0_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:0-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
