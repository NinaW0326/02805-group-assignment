<b>Timothy the Timid</b><sup id="cite_ref-HBP_NDS_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP_NDS-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> was a very shy and nervous&#160;<a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> who had a moustache and very sparse brown hair. He was afraid of many things and disliked people (and other portraits)&#160;who stared at him, becoming incredibly paranoid with time. He had&#160;a portrait hung at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Castle" title="Hogwarts Castle">Hogwarts Castle</a>.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Timothy_the_Timid?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Timothy_the_Timid?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span id="1995–1996_school_year"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="1995.E2.80.931996_school_year"><a href="/wiki/1995%E2%80%931996_school_year" title="1995–1996 school year">1995–1996 school year</a></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Timothy_the_Timid?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: 1995–1996 school year">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Timothy_the_Timid?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: 1995–1996 school year"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>By 1995, Timothy had two sets of portraits, hiding different shortcuts. One set hid a secret passageway between the fifth-floor corridor and the Herbology Corridor. The password for this shortcut was "<i>Flaming <a href="/wiki/Earwig" title="Earwig">earwigs</a></i>".<sup id="cite_ref-OotP_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OotP-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> The other pair of portraits hid a shortcut between the <a href="/wiki/Middle_Courtyard" title="Middle Courtyard">Transfiguration Courtyard</a> and the fifth floor, and its password was "<i>Carpe Diem</i>".<sup id="cite_ref-OotP_GBA_4-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OotP_GBA-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> Both sets of portraits were placed next to portraits of a <a href="/wiki/Staring_Portrait" title="Staring Portrait">staring eye</a> and Timothy's portrait was very annoyed by it, asking students to "<i>cover </i>him<i> up</i>" and claiming that "<i>he is one of </i>them<i> </i>" (whatever that means).<sup id="cite_ref-OotP_3-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OotP-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span id="1996–1997_school_year"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="1996.E2.80.931997_school_year"><a href="/wiki/1996%E2%80%931997_school_year" title="1996–1997 school year">1996–1997 school year</a></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Timothy_the_Timid?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: 1996–1997 school year">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Timothy_the_Timid?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: 1996–1997 school year"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>In the next school year, both sets of portraits were hung on other places. Due to leading close to the Quidditch pitch, the portrait said its password to the <a href="/wiki/Quidditch_Captain#Hogwarts_Quidditch_Captains" title="Quidditch Captain">Quidditch Captains</a>, asking them to have their teams to keep it down, when they passed next to him. One of the two sets hid a passageway from the <a href="/wiki/Grand_Staircase" title="Grand Staircase">Grand Staircase</a> to a stairway, leading towards the Quidditch Gate, and its password was "<i>Boiled Beetles</i>".<sup id="cite_ref-HBP_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> The other pair hid a secret passageway from the Quidditch Gate to the Portrait Room.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP_NDS_2-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP_NDS-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Timothy_the_Timid?veaction=edit&amp;section=4" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Timothy_the_Timid?action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>Timothy the Timid may have passed away sometime prior to <a href="/wiki/1995" title="1995">1995</a>, as many portraits hung at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Castle" title="Hogwarts Castle">Hogwarts Castle</a> depict deceased individuals.</li>
<li>The portrait of him that can be seen on the fifth floor corridor close to the <a href="/wiki/Prefects%27_bathroom" class="mw-redirect" title="Prefects&#39; bathroom">Prefects' bathroom</a> was removed in <a href="/wiki/1998" title="1998">1998</a> and instead another part of the fifth floor corridor has been added, as it can be seen that <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Voldemort</a> chased <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> through this hallway.<sup id="cite_ref-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup></li></ul>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Timothy_the_Timid?veaction=edit&amp;section=5" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Timothy_the_Timid?action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The name "Timothy" comes from the Greek name "Τιμοθεος" ("<i>Timotheos</i>"), meaning "honouring God", derived from τιμαω ("<i>timao</i>"), "to honour" and θεος ("<i>theos</i>"), "god".
</p>
