<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_Ministry_Wizard_(I)?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_Ministry_Wizard_(I)?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>In <a href="/wiki/1981" title="1981">1981</a> after <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a>'s downfall he attended the trial of <a href="/wiki/Igor_Karkaroff" title="Igor Karkaroff">Igor Karkaroff</a> in which Karkaroff named <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-GOFF_1-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOFF-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>In <a href="/wiki/1994" title="1994">1994</a> he attended the <a href="/wiki/Quidditch_World_Cup" title="Quidditch World Cup">Quidditch World Cup</a> between <a href="/wiki/Ireland" title="Ireland">Ireland</a> and <a href="/wiki/Bulgaria" title="Bulgaria">Bulgaria</a>. When the <a href="/wiki/Dark_Mark" title="Dark Mark">Dark Mark</a> appeared in the sky he helped <a href="/wiki/Bartemius_Crouch_Senior" title="Bartemius Crouch Senior">Bartemius Crouch Snr</a> and some other wizards and witches to find the "<a href="/wiki/Bartemius_Crouch_Junior" title="Bartemius Crouch Junior">man</a>" in the ruins of the <a href="/wiki/Roberts%27s_campsite" class="mw-redirect" title="Roberts&#39;s campsite">campsite</a> owned by <a href="/wiki/Roberts" title="Roberts">Roberts</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-GOFF_1-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOFF-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
