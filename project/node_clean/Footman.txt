<p>A <b>footman</b> was a medieval equivalent of an infantryman; a soldier who is trained to engage, fight, and defeat the enemy in face-to-face combat.<sup id="cite_ref-Wikipedia_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Wikipedia-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>There were many footman <a href="/wiki/Suit_of_armour" title="Suit of armour">suits of armour</a> at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Castle" title="Hogwarts Castle">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> under possession by <a href="/wiki/Ghoul" title="Ghoul">ghouls</a>, <a href="/wiki/Ghost" title="Ghost">ghosts</a>, or <a href="/wiki/Spectre" title="Spectre">spectres</a>. They could be aggressive to passers-by, but were particularly vulnerable to the <i><a href="/wiki/Freezing_Spell" title="Freezing Spell">Glacius</a></i> spell.<sup id="cite_ref-PAgba_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PAgba-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> However, they were resistant to <i><a href="/wiki/Red_Sparks" title="Red Sparks">Vermillious</a></i>.
</p>
