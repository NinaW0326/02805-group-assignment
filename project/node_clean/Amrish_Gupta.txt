<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Amrish_Gupta?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Amrish_Gupta?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Amrish is a male given name, meaning "Lord Indra". Gupta is a North India surname, most likely derived from the Sanskrit "goptri" meaning "military governor". Another more direct translation from Sanskrit would be "secret" or "hidden".
</p>

<ul><li>Based on his name, he is most likely of <a href="/wiki/India" title="India">Indian</a> descent.</li></ul>
