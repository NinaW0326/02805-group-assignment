<p><b>Martha Steward</b> (b. <a href="/wiki/1600s" title="1600s">1600s</a>) was a <a href="/wiki/Squib" title="Squib">Squib</a> and the elder twin daughter of <a href="/wiki/Isolt_Sayre" title="Isolt Sayre">Isolt Sayre</a> and <a href="/wiki/James_Steward" title="James Steward">James Steward</a>. Isolt and James were the founders of <a href="/wiki/Ilvermorny_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Ilvermorny School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Ilvermorny School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a>.
</p><p>It was painful for Martha to grow up at Ilvermorny when she herself was unable to perform <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magic</a>. She eventually married the non-magical <a href="/wiki/Martha_Steward_II%27s_husband" title="Martha Steward II&#39;s husband">brother</a>&#160;of a friend from the <a href="/wiki/Pocomtuc" title="Pocomtuc">Pocomtuc</a> tribe and lived henceforth as a <a href="/wiki/No-Maj" class="mw-redirect" title="No-Maj">No-Maj</a>.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Martha_Steward_II?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Martha_Steward_II?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Martha Steward was born in or around <a href="/wiki/1634" class="mw-redirect" title="1634">1634</a> at <a href="/wiki/Ilvermorny_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Ilvermorny School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Ilvermorny</a> in Massachusetts, arriving before her fraternal twin sister, <a href="/wiki/Rionach_Steward" title="Rionach Steward">Rionach</a>. Her parents, <a href="/wiki/Isolt_Sayre" title="Isolt Sayre">Isolt Sayre</a>, a <a href="/wiki/Pure-blood" title="Pure-blood">pure-blood</a> <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch</a>, and <a href="/wiki/James_Steward" title="James Steward">James Steward</a>, a <a href="/wiki/Muggle" class="mw-redirect" title="Muggle">No-Maj</a>, were the founders of the school. She was named after her <a href="/wiki/Martha_Steward" title="Martha Steward">paternal grandmother</a>. She also had two adopted brothers: <a href="/wiki/Chadwick_Boot" title="Chadwick Boot">Chadwick</a> and <a href="/wiki/Webster_Boot" title="Webster Boot">Webster Boot</a>.
</p><p>Martha was the only one in the family to be born a <a href="/wiki/Squib" title="Squib">Squib</a>, and with no magic powers herself, she considered her upbringing at Ilvermorny painful. She was loved by her family regardless.
</p><p>When she was a baby, her great-aunt, <a href="/wiki/Gormlaith_Gaunt" title="Gormlaith Gaunt">Gormlaith Gaunt</a>, attacked the family. Her parents had been put in to a deep sleep by Gormlaith, but the cries of Martha and her sister woke them. The family were saved by <a href="/wiki/William_(Pukwudgie)" title="William (Pukwudgie)">William</a>, the <a href="/wiki/Pukwudgie" title="Pukwudgie">Pukwudgie</a> her mother had befriended several years earlier.
</p><p>Martha went on to marry the <a href="/wiki/Martha_Steward_II%27s_husband" title="Martha Steward II&#39;s husband">non-magical brother</a> of a friend from the <a href="/wiki/Pocomtuc" title="Pocomtuc">Pocomtuc</a> tribe. She assimilated into her husband's community, living as a <a href="/wiki/No-Maj" class="mw-redirect" title="No-Maj">No-Maj</a>. However, it is unknown whether Martha and her fraternal twin sister are the last of the Irish branch of the Gaunt family. Because Martha is the only one in the family who is married, she may have had children with her husband.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Martha_Steward_II?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Martha_Steward_II?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>Martha comes from the Aramic <i>marta </i>meaning "the lady, mistress".</li></ul>
<ul><li>The surname steward comes from&#160;Middle English&#160;<i>steward</i>, from&#160;Old English&#160;<i>stīweard</i>,&#160;<i>stīġweard</i>&#160;‎meaning “steward, housekeeper, one who has the superintendence of household affairs, guardian”, from&#160;<i>stīġ</i>&#160;in the sense&#160;<i>house</i>,&#160;<i>hall</i>&#160;+&#160;<i>weard</i>&#160; ‎“ward, guard, guardian, keeper”.</li></ul>
