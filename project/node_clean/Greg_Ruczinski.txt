<b>Greg "Crash" Ruczinski</b> was an <a href="/wiki/United_States_of_America" title="United States of America">American</a> <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> who played as a <a href="/wiki/Beater" title="Beater">Beater</a> on the <a href="/wiki/American_National_Quidditch_team" title="American National Quidditch team">American National Quidditch team</a>. He wore a helmet with a <a href="/wiki/Brown" title="Brown">brown</a> <a href="/wiki/Pumpkin" title="Pumpkin">pumpkin</a> design on it.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> By <a href="/wiki/1996" title="1996">1996</a>, he was featured on a <a href="/wiki/Chocolate_Frog_Card" title="Chocolate Frog Card">Chocolate Frog Card</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
