<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Marina_Sanchez?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Marina_Sanchez?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Marina Sanchez started attending Hogwarts on <a href="/wiki/1_September" title="1 September">1 September</a> <a href="/wiki/1984" title="1984">1984</a> and was <a href="/wiki/Sorting_ceremony" title="Sorting ceremony">Sorted</a> into an unknown <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Houses" title="Hogwarts Houses">House</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-HM_1-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HM-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>During her third year, Marina took <a href="/wiki/Care_of_Magical_Creatures" title="Care of Magical Creatures">Care of Magical Creatures</a> as electives.<sup id="cite_ref-HM_1-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HM-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>During the <a href="/wiki/1989%E2%80%931990_school_year" title="1989–1990 school year">1989–1990 school year</a>, her <a href="/wiki/Sixth_year" title="Sixth year">sixth year</a>, Marina proceeded to take <a href="/wiki/Nastily_Exhausting_Wizarding_Test" title="Nastily Exhausting Wizarding Test">N.E.W.T.</a> level <a href="/wiki/Charms_(class)" title="Charms (class)">Charms</a>, <a href="/wiki/Herbology_(class)" class="mw-redirect" title="Herbology (class)">Herbology</a>, <a href="/wiki/Transfiguration_(class)" title="Transfiguration (class)">Transfiguration</a>, <a href="/wiki/History_of_Magic" title="History of Magic">History of Magic</a> and <a href="/wiki/Care_of_Magical_Creatures" title="Care of Magical Creatures">Care of Magical Creatures</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-HM_1-10" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HM-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
