<b>Corvus Lestrange</b> (1729&#160;–&#160;1768) was a <a href="/wiki/French" title="French">French</a> <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> and a member of the <a href="/wiki/Pure-blood" title="Pure-blood">pure-blood</a> <a href="/wiki/Lestrange_family" title="Lestrange family">Lestrange family</a>. He married <a href="/wiki/Heloise_Lestrange" title="Heloise Lestrange">Heloise Beaufort</a> and had two sons with her, <a href="/wiki/Corvus_Lestrange_(II)" title="Corvus Lestrange (II)">Corvus</a> and <a href="/wiki/Falco_Lestrange" title="Falco Lestrange">Falco</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p><br />
</p>
