<p><b>Part-trolls</b> were, apparently, <a href="/wiki/Being" title="Being">beings</a> with some, but not a total, amount of <a href="/wiki/Troll" title="Troll">troll</a> heritage or blood, as well as part <a href="/wiki/Human" title="Human">human</a> blood.<sup id="cite_ref-PS11_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PS11-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-DH19_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH19-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Part-trolls were also, apparently, aggressive individuals<sup id="cite_ref-PS11_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PS11-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> with a <a href="/wiki/Troll" title="Troll">troll</a>'s characteristic foul smell.<sup id="cite_ref-DH19_2-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH19-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> They were also noted for their <a href="/wiki/Superhuman_strength" title="Superhuman strength">strength</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-COG41_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COG41-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
