<b>Glynnis Griffiths</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Great_Britain" title="Great Britain">British</a> <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch</a> who was the <a href="/wiki/Seeker" title="Seeker">Seeker</a> for the <a href="/wiki/Holyhead_Harpies" title="Holyhead Harpies">Holyhead Harpies</a>. She caught the <a href="/wiki/Golden_Snitch" title="Golden Snitch">Snitch</a> after a seven-day match against the <a href="/wiki/Heidelberg_Harriers" title="Heidelberg Harriers">Heidelberg Harriers</a> in <a href="/wiki/1953" title="1953">1953</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
