<b>Paloma Proudfoot</b> (b. <a href="/wiki/8_October" title="8 October">8 October</a>, <a href="/wiki/1900" title="1900">1900</a>) was an American <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch</a> who worked as a <a href="/wiki/Surveillor_of_Activity" title="Surveillor of Activity">Surveillor of Activity</a> for the <a href="/wiki/Magical_Congress_of_the_United_States_of_America" title="Magical Congress of the United States of America">Magical Congress of the United States of America</a> in <a href="/wiki/1926" title="1926">1926</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-ID_1-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-ID-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> She lived at <a href="/wiki/76_Monroe_Street" title="76 Monroe Street">76</a> <a href="/wiki/Monroe_Street" title="Monroe Street">Monroe Street</a> in <a href="/wiki/New_York" title="New York">New York</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-ID_1-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-ID-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>She may have been related to the <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">British</a> <a href="/wiki/Auror" title="Auror">Auror</a> <a href="/wiki/Proudfoot" title="Proudfoot">Proudfoot</a>.
</p>
