This <b>girl</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">student</a> at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> in the <a href="/wiki/1990s" title="1990s">1990s</a> and was sorted into <a href="/wiki/Hufflepuff" title="Hufflepuff">Hufflepuff</a> in <a href="/wiki/1991" title="1991">1991</a>. She participated in the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a> and survived.
