<p><b>Minister Grogan Stump</b> (<a href="/wiki/1770" class="mw-redirect" title="1770">1770</a>-<a href="/wiki/1884" class="mw-redirect" title="1884">1884</a>) was a <a href="/wiki/Great_Britain" title="Great Britain">British</a> <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a>, who was the <a href="/wiki/Minister_for_Magic" title="Minister for Magic">Minister for Magic</a> from <a href="/wiki/1811" class="mw-redirect" title="1811">1811</a> to <a href="/wiki/1819" class="mw-redirect" title="1819">1819</a> and was very popular in the job. His legacy includes the creation of the <a href="/wiki/Being_Division" title="Being Division">Being</a>, <a href="/wiki/Beast_Division" title="Beast Division">Beast</a> and <a href="/wiki/Spirit_Division" title="Spirit Division">Spirit Divisions</a> of the <a href="/wiki/Department_for_the_Regulation_and_Control_of_Magical_Creatures" title="Department for the Regulation and Control of Magical Creatures">Department for the Regulation and Control of Magical Creatures</a>, and the establishment of the <a href="/wiki/Department_of_Magical_Games_and_Sports" title="Department of Magical Games and Sports">Department of Magical Games and Sports</a>. He was elected Minister by the wizarding population in <a href="/wiki/Great_Britain" title="Great Britain">Great Britain</a> and <a href="/wiki/Ireland" title="Ireland">Ireland</a> at least twice.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Grogan_Stump?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Grogan_Stump?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>

<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_life">Early life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Grogan_Stump?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Early life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Grogan_Stump?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Early life"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Stump was born in <a href="/wiki/1770" class="mw-redirect" title="1770">1770</a>, somewhere in <a href="/wiki/Great_Britain" title="Great Britain">Great Britain</a><sup id="cite_ref-FWC_4-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-FWC-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> to at least one magical parent.<sup id="cite_ref-blood_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-blood-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> He attended <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> from c. <a href="/wiki/1781" class="mw-redirect" title="1781">1781</a> to <a href="/wiki/1788" class="mw-redirect" title="1788">1788</a>, where he was <a href="/wiki/Sorting_ceremony" title="Sorting ceremony">Sorted</a> into <a href="/wiki/Hufflepuff" title="Hufflepuff">Hufflepuff</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-pottermore_5-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-pottermore-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="As_Minister_for_Magic">As Minister for Magic</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Grogan_Stump?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: As Minister for Magic">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Grogan_Stump?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: As Minister for Magic"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>In <a href="/wiki/1811" class="mw-redirect" title="1811">1811</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-WOMBAT_6-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-WOMBAT-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-PMministers_7-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PMministers-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup> at age 41, Stump began his duties as a <a href="/wiki/Minister_for_Magic" title="Minister for Magic">Minister for Magic</a>. Stump particularly addressed the nomenclature of <a href="/wiki/Being" title="Being">beings</a> and <a href="/wiki/Beast" title="Beast">beasts</a>. He decreed that a being was "<i>any creature that has sufficient intelligence to understand the laws of the magical community and to bear part of the responsibility in shaping those laws</i>", and by doing so, he settled a debate that had been going on in the wizarding world since the fourteenth century. Stump created the three divisions of the <a href="/wiki/Department_for_the_Regulation_and_Control_of_Magical_Creatures" title="Department for the Regulation and Control of Magical Creatures">Department for the Regulation and Control of Magical Creatures</a>: <a href="/wiki/Being_Division" title="Being Division">Being</a>, <a href="/wiki/Beast_Division" title="Beast Division">Beast</a>, as well as later <a href="/wiki/Spirit_Division" title="Spirit Division">Spirit Divisions</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-FB_8-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-FB-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>It is likely<sup id="cite_ref-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup> that during Stump's tenure in office there were ghost demonstrations at the <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">Ministry of Magic</a> (or "<a href="/wiki/Protest_floats" title="Protest floats">protest floats</a>"), which caused Stump to create the aforementioned <a href="/wiki/Spirit_Division" title="Spirit Division">Spirit Division</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-WOMBAT_6-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-WOMBAT-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>A passionate <a href="/wiki/Quidditch" title="Quidditch">Quidditch</a> fan, supporting the <a href="/wiki/Tutshill_Tornados" title="Tutshill Tornados">Tutshill Tornados</a>, Minister Stump also was responsible for establishing the <a href="/wiki/Department_of_Magical_Games_and_Sports" title="Department of Magical Games and Sports">Department of Magical Games and Sports</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-PMministers_7-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PMministers-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Stump's term eventually ended in <a href="/wiki/1819" class="mw-redirect" title="1819">1819</a>, and he was succeeded by <a href="/wiki/Josephina_Flint" title="Josephina Flint">Josephina Flint</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-PMministers_7-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PMministers-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Grogan_Stump?veaction=edit&amp;section=4" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Grogan_Stump?action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>Grogan Stump's likeness, as featured on his <i><a href="/wiki/Wizard_of_the_Month" title="Wizard of the Month">Wizard of the Month</a></i> entry, bears some (presumably coincidental) resemblance to former President of Portugal, <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/An%C3%ADbal_Cavaco_Silva" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Aníbal Cavaco Silva">Aníbal Cavaco Silva</a>. Interestingly, <a href="/wiki/J._K._Rowling" title="J. K. Rowling">J. K. Rowling</a> did live there in the early 1990s, at a time when Cavaco Silva was the Prime Minister. Rowling has previously stated that <a href="/wiki/Salazar_Slytherin" title="Salazar Slytherin">Salazar Slytherin</a>'s first name was derived from <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ant%C3%B3nio_de_Oliveira_Salazar" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:António de Oliveira Salazar">António de Oliveira Salazar</a>, who served as Prime Minister of Portugal from 1932 to 1968, ruling a fascist dictatorship.</li></ul>
