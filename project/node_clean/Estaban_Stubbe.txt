<b>Estaban Stubbe</b> was an <a href="/wiki/United_States_of_America" title="United States of America">American</a> <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizarding</a> criminal who operated in the <a href="/wiki/1920s" title="1920s">1920s</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-FB-F_special_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-FB-F_special-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>

<p>In or around <a href="/wiki/1926" title="1926">1926</a>, the <a href="/wiki/Magical_Congress_of_the_United_States_of_America" title="Magical Congress of the United States of America">Magical Congress of the United States of America</a> issued a wanted poster for Stubbe, stating that he was wanted for <a href="/wiki/Muggle" class="mw-redirect" title="Muggle">No-Maj</a> smuggling &amp; <a href="/wiki/Death" title="Death">murder</a> and "bearing <a href="/wiki/Wand" title="Wand">wand</a>, most menacing and extremely dangerous" with a reward of 4850 <a href="/wiki/Dragot" title="Dragot">Dragots</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-FB-F_special_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-FB-F_special-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
