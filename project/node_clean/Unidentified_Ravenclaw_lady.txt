<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_Ravenclaw_lady?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_Ravenclaw_lady?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span id="1991–1992_school_year"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="1991.E2.80.931992_school_year"><a href="/wiki/1991%E2%80%931992_school_year" title="1991–1992 school year">1991–1992 school year</a></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_Ravenclaw_lady?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: 1991–1992 school year">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_Ravenclaw_lady?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: 1991–1992 school year"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>In <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>'s first year at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a>, she was guarding a secret room in the <a href="/wiki/Dungeons" title="Dungeons">Dungeons</a>, concealing a Quidditch Armour. The password required to gain entrance was "<i>Wanglewort</i>".<sup id="cite_ref-PS_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PS-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span id="1992–1993_school_year"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="1992.E2.80.931993_school_year"><a href="/wiki/1992%E2%80%931993_school_year" title="1992–1993 school year">1992–1993 school year</a></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_Ravenclaw_lady?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: 1992–1993 school year">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_Ravenclaw_lady?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: 1992–1993 school year"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>A year later, her portrait had been moved, and now guarded the way to the Ravenclaw Duelling Arena.<sup id="cite_ref-CS_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-CS-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Personality_and_traits">Personality and traits</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_Ravenclaw_lady?veaction=edit&amp;section=4" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Personality and traits">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_Ravenclaw_lady?action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Personality and traits"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>This witch was proud of her <a href="/wiki/Ravenclaw" title="Ravenclaw">House</a> and considered others who dare duel the Ravenclaw Duelling Champion to be "foolish".<sup id="cite_ref-CS_2-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-CS-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> However, in <a href="/wiki/1991" title="1991">1991</a> she became very nervous as she sensed there was <a href="/wiki/Quirinus_Quirrell" title="Quirinus Quirrell">someone</a> in the castle doing something <a href="/wiki/Dark_Arts" title="Dark Arts">evil</a>, showing she was very observant and intuitive.<sup id="cite_ref-PS_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PS-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
