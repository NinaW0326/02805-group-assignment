The <b>Inn Charm</b> (<i>Tribuomnus</i>) was a <a href="/wiki/Charm" title="Charm">charm</a> that improved the quality of food served at <a href="/wiki/Wizarding_world" title="Wizarding world">wizarding</a> Inns.<sup id="cite_ref-WU_1-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-WU-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

</p>
