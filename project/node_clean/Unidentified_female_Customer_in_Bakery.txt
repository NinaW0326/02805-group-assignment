<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="History">History</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_female_Customer_in_Bakery?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: History">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_female_Customer_in_Bakery?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: History"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>She visited <a href="/wiki/Kowalski_Quality_Baked_Goods" title="Kowalski Quality Baked Goods">Kowalski Quality Baked Goods</a> in <a href="/wiki/1926" title="1926">1926</a>. She became so impressed with the designs of the bakery's <a href="/wiki/Bread" title="Bread">bread</a> that she asked the owner <a href="/wiki/Jacob_Kowalski" title="Jacob Kowalski">Jacob Kowalski</a> where he came up with his creativity, to which he replied that they just came to him.<sup id="cite_ref-FBS_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-FBS-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
