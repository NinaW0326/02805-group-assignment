<b>Elphinstone Urquart</b> (d. <a href="/wiki/1985" title="1985">1985</a>) was a <a href="/wiki/Pure-blood" title="Pure-blood">pure-blood</a> <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> and a <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">Ministry of Magic</a> official for the <a href="/wiki/Department_of_Magical_Law_Enforcement" title="Department of Magical Law Enforcement">Department of Magical Law Enforcement</a>. He married <a href="/wiki/Minerva_McGonagall" title="Minerva McGonagall">Minerva McGonagall</a> in 1982, but their happy marriage was cut short after Elphinstone's accidental death in 1985.<sup id="cite_ref-pottermore_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-pottermore-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Elphinstone_Urquart?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Elphinstone_Urquart?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_life">Early life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Elphinstone_Urquart?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Early life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Elphinstone_Urquart?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Early life"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Elphinstone Urquart was born&#160;somewhere in the <a href="/wiki/Great_Britain" title="Great Britain">British Isles</a>, into the <a href="/wiki/Pure-blood" title="Pure-blood">pure-blood</a> <a href="/wiki/Urquart_family" title="Urquart family">Urquart family</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-pottermore_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-pottermore-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Employment_at_the_Ministry">Employment at the Ministry</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Elphinstone_Urquart?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Employment at the Ministry">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Elphinstone_Urquart?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Employment at the Ministry"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Sometime after his seventeenth birthday, Elphinstone joined the <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">Ministry of Magic</a> and eventually rose to a senior office in the <a href="/wiki/Department_of_Magical_Law_Enforcement" title="Department of Magical Law Enforcement">Department of Magical Law Enforcement</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-pottermore_1-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-pottermore-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p><a href="/wiki/Minerva_McGonagall" title="Minerva McGonagall">Minerva McGonagall</a> joined the <a href="/wiki/Department_of_Magical_Law_Enforcement" title="Department of Magical Law Enforcement">Department of Magical Law Enforcement</a> after her graduation. Under Elphinstone, McGonagall was a most efficient and gifted employee, and the two were very fond of each other. After two years at the Ministry, Minerva was offered a prestigious promotion which she turned down. Minerva missed <a href="/wiki/Scotland" title="Scotland">Scotland</a> and had a continuing love for <a href="/wiki/Dougal_McGregor" title="Dougal McGregor">Dougal McGregor</a>, a <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggle</a> Scottish farmer, whom she felt could not marry because she thought that would be the end of her life ambitions.<sup id="cite_ref-pottermore_1-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-pottermore-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Retirement_and_marriage">Retirement and marriage</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Elphinstone_Urquart?veaction=edit&amp;section=4" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Retirement and marriage">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Elphinstone_Urquart?action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Retirement and marriage"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>When McGonagall joined the <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> faculty, Elphinstone remained on terms of friendship with her. On occasion, while holidaying in <a href="/wiki/Scotland" title="Scotland">Scotland</a>, he encountered his old friend and proposed to her in <a href="/wiki/Madam_Puddifoot%27s_Tea_Shop" title="Madam Puddifoot&#39;s Tea Shop">Madam Puddifoot's Tea Shop</a>. McGonagall turned him down this and several other times, as she still had feelings for <a href="/wiki/Dougal_McGregor" title="Dougal McGregor">Dougal McGregor</a>. However, in 1982, after McGregor's death, Urquart proposed again during a summertime stroll around the lake in the Hogwarts grounds. This time Minerva accepted. Elphinstone, now retired, purchased a small cottage in <a href="/wiki/Hogsmeade" title="Hogsmeade">Hogsmeade</a> for the pair of them, whence Minerva could travel easily to <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Castle" title="Hogwarts Castle">Hogwarts Castle</a> every day. Minerva kept her  surname as she had always been something of a <a href="/wiki/Feminism" title="Feminism">feminist</a>. The marriage was a very happy one, and a period of great fulfilment for Minerva. The couple did not have any children of their own, but they were close with Minerva's nephews and nieces (children of her brothers) and they were frequent visitors at their cottage.<sup id="cite_ref-pottermore_1-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-pottermore-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Death">Death</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Elphinstone_Urquart?veaction=edit&amp;section=5" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Death">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Elphinstone_Urquart?action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Death"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>The marriage was cut short in <a href="/wiki/1985" title="1985">1985</a> after Elphinstone's accidental death from a <a href="/wiki/Venomous_Tentacula" title="Venomous Tentacula">Venomous Tentacula</a> bite. After <a href="/wiki/Funeral_of_Elphinstone_Urquart" title="Funeral of Elphinstone Urquart">her husband's funeral</a>, Minerva left the Hogsmeade cottage and returned to her living quarters at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Castle" title="Hogwarts Castle">Hogwarts Castle</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-pottermore_1-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-pottermore-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Elphinstone_Urquart?veaction=edit&amp;section=6" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Elphinstone_Urquart?action=edit&amp;section=6" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>Elphinstone is a village in the County of Sterling, Scotland and is the name of a Scottish Lowland clan - <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Clan_Elphinstone" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Clan Elphinstone">Clan Elphinstone</a>. The head of this clan also holds the title <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Lord_Elphinstone" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Lord Elphinstone">Lord Elphinstone</a> in the Peerage of Scotland. This title is historically significant since it was held by the maternal uncle (and later cousins) of <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Queen_Elizabeth_II" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Queen Elizabeth II">Queen Elizabeth II</a>.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Although not spelled the same, it is possible his last name is a reference to another Scottish clan - <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Clan_Urquhart" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Clan Urquhart">Clan Urquhart</a>. This is a Highland clan, and if there is in fact a link, his name would include a reference to both the <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Scottish_Highlands" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Scottish Highlands">Scottish Highlands</a> and the <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Scottish_Lowlands" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Scottish Lowlands">Scottish Lowlands</a>. It is also of interest that Urquhart Castle is on the banks of <a href="/wiki/Loch_Ness" title="Loch Ness">Loch Ness</a>, home of a <a href="/wiki/Kelpie" title="Kelpie">kelpie</a> known as the <a href="/wiki/Loch_Ness_Monster" title="Loch Ness Monster">Loch Ness Monster</a>.</li></ul>
