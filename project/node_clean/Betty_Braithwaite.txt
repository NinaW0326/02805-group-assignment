<b>Betty Braithwaite</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch</a> who worked as a reporter for the <i><a href="/wiki/Daily_Prophet" title="Daily Prophet">Daily Prophet</a></i>.<sup id="cite_ref-DH2_1-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH2-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>In <a href="/wiki/1997" title="1997">1997</a>, she got an exclusive interview with <a href="/wiki/Rita_Skeeter" title="Rita Skeeter">Rita Skeeter</a> about her then-upcoming book, <i><a href="/wiki/The_Life_and_Lies_of_Albus_Dumbledore" title="The Life and Lies of Albus Dumbledore">The Life and Lies of Albus Dumbledore</a></i>. She was invited into <a href="/wiki/Rita_Skeeter%27s_home" title="Rita Skeeter&#39;s home">Rita's home</a>, where Rita served her <a href="/wiki/Tea" title="Tea">tea</a> and a slice of <a href="/wiki/Pound_cake" title="Pound cake">pound cake</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-DH2_1-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH2-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Betty_Braithwaite?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Betty_Braithwaite?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>Braithwaite apparently took a liking to Rita Skeeter, or at least pretended to, as her article gave the impression of Skeeter being a sweet, kind woman, despite her cruel and libellous writings. However, this could have been due to an entirely understandable desire not to get on the wrong side of someone with such a poison pen, and a clear capacity to carry a grudge.</li></ul>
