<p><b>Ritchie Coote</b> (fl. <a href="/wiki/1985" title="1985">1985</a>-<a href="/wiki/1997" title="1997">1997</a>) was a student at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> during the 1990s. He was chosen by <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> to be one of the new <a href="/wiki/Beater" title="Beater">Beaters</a> for the <a href="/wiki/1996%E2%80%931997_school_year" title="1996–1997 school year">1996-1997</a> <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor_Quidditch_team" title="Gryffindor Quidditch team">Gryffindor Quidditch team</a>. Together with <a href="/wiki/Jimmy_Peakes" title="Jimmy Peakes">Jimmy Peakes</a>, he replaced <a href="/wiki/Jack_Sloper" title="Jack Sloper">Jack Sloper</a> and <a href="/wiki/Andrew_Kirke" title="Andrew Kirke">Andrew Kirke</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP11_3-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP11-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Hogwarts_years">Hogwarts years</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Ritchie_Coote?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Hogwarts years">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Ritchie_Coote?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Hogwarts years"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_years">Early years</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Ritchie_Coote?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Early years">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Ritchie_Coote?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Early years"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<p>Ritchie was educated at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> and apon his arrival was sorted into the <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor" title="Gryffindor">Gryffindor House</a>. Ritchie was a fair player at <a href="/wiki/Quidditch" title="Quidditch">Quidditch</a> and played best in the position of Beater.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP11_3-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP11-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> Ritchie had not tried out for the <a href="/wiki/Quidditch_team" title="Quidditch team">Quidditch team</a> as there was few open positions.
</p>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="1996-1997_school_year">1996-1997 school year</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Ritchie_Coote?veaction=edit&amp;section=4" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: 1996-1997 school year">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Ritchie_Coote?action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: 1996-1997 school year"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<div class="quote">

<dd>&#8212;Harry Potter regarding the Gryffindor team's improving skill<sup class="noprint"><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Half-Blood_Prince" title="Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince">[src]</a></sup></dd>

<p>In 1996, after the departure of Fred and George Weasley, Ritchie Coote along with <a href="/wiki/Jimmy_Peakes" title="Jimmy Peakes">Jimmy Peakes</a> replaced them as the new beaters for the <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor_Quidditch_team" title="Gryffindor Quidditch team">Gryffindor Quidditch team</a>. According to <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>, neither of the two lived up to the shadow that the Weasley twins had left them, but they played well enough and managed to stay on the team throughout the 1996-1997 <a href="/wiki/Quidditch" title="Quidditch">Quidditch</a> season.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP11_3-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP11-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>It was mentioned by <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione Granger</a> that he and <a href="/wiki/Jimmy_Peakes" title="Jimmy Peakes">Peakes</a> apparently caught Harry after he was knocked out by a Bludger to the head that <a href="/wiki/Cormac_McLaggen" title="Cormac McLaggen">Cormac McLaggen</a> accidently hit him with.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP11_3-10" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP11-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> At the end of this year, Headmaster <a href="/wiki/Albus_Dumbledore" title="Albus Dumbledore">Albus Dumbledore</a> was murdered by <a href="/wiki/Severus_Snape" title="Severus Snape">Severus Snape</a>.
</p>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="1997-1998_school_year">1997-1998 school year</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Ritchie_Coote?veaction=edit&amp;section=5" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: 1997-1998 school year">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Ritchie_Coote?action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: 1997-1998 school year"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<p>Not being in <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>'s year and the 1997–1998 school year being Harry's final year of education meant that Ritchie would also be returning to continue his education at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts</a> in 1997 after the death of former Headmaster <a href="/wiki/Albus_Dumbledore" title="Albus Dumbledore">Albus Dumbledore</a>. With Dumbledore dead the school was to be taken over by <a href="/wiki/Minerva_McGonagall" title="Minerva McGonagall">Minerva McGonagall</a> but with <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a>'s rise to power <a href="/wiki/Severus_Snape" title="Severus Snape">Severus Snape</a> took to the position.
</p>
<h5><span class="mw-headline" id="Battle_of_Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Ritchie_Coote?veaction=edit&amp;section=6" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Battle of Hogwarts">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Ritchie_Coote?action=edit&amp;section=6" title="Edit section: Battle of Hogwarts"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h5>
<p>In last few months of the school year, the <a href="/wiki/Second_Wizarding_War" title="Second Wizarding War">Second Wizarding War</a> came to a close with the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a>. Lord Voldemort and his <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eater</a> army invaded the castle and a battle broke out to defend it and protect <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>. The castle was almost completely destroyed and there were many casualties. It is unknown if Ritchie was present for the battle or if he was too young to stay in combat.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Physical_appearance">Physical appearance</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Ritchie_Coote?veaction=edit&amp;section=7" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Physical appearance">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Ritchie_Coote?action=edit&amp;section=7" title="Edit section: Physical appearance"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>It was said that Coote did not have the "old brilliance" of Fred and George (whom he and fellow new Beater Jimmy Peakes replaced) and he "looked weedy", but he did aim well.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP11_3-11" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP11-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Magical_abilities_and_skills">Magical abilities and skills</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Ritchie_Coote?veaction=edit&amp;section=8" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Ritchie_Coote?action=edit&amp;section=8" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li><b><a href="/wiki/Flight" title="Flight">Flying</a></b>: Coote was shown to be a skilled <a href="/wiki/Quidditch" title="Quidditch">Quidditch</a> player, as he was chosen to play as <a href="/wiki/Beater" title="Beater">Beater</a> on the <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor_Quidditch_team" title="Gryffindor Quidditch team">Gryffindor Quidditch team</a> in <a href="/wiki/1996" title="1996">1996</a>. He was said to aim well whilst playing.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP11_3-12" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP11-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup></li></ul>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Ritchie_Coote?veaction=edit&amp;section=9" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Ritchie_Coote?action=edit&amp;section=9" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Taken from pety coote, "a small coat," from petty + coat. Originally a padded coat worn by men under armour. May reference to the character's place originally being filled by Fred/George Weasley and now filled by a new man in armour (Quidditch Uniform).
</p>
