<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_Gryffindor_seeker?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_Gryffindor_seeker?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>A <a href="/wiki/Portrait" title="Portrait">portrait</a> of this wizard was hung in <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor_Tower" title="Gryffindor Tower">Gryffindor Tower</a>. In <a href="/wiki/1995" title="1995">1995</a>, he lost his <a href="/wiki/Golden_Snitch" title="Golden Snitch">Golden Snitch</a>. <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> found the Snitch in the <a href="/wiki/Unidentified_Gryffindor_man_(II)" title="Unidentified Gryffindor man (II)">portrait of an old man</a>. As a reward, the <a href="/wiki/Seeker" title="Seeker">Seeker</a> gave Harry one of his Snitches back, which he had been keeping behind his frame.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTPG_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTPG-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_Gryffindor_seeker?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_Gryffindor_seeker?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>In the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Order_of_the_Phoenix_(video_game)" title="Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix (video game)">video game adaptation</a> of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Order_of_the_Phoenix" title="Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix">Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix</a></i>, he was credited as <b>Quidditch Player</b> and was voiced by <a href="/wiki/Adrian_Fergus-Fuller" title="Adrian Fergus-Fuller">Adrian Fergus-Fuller</a>. Unlike the other speaking portraits in the Gryffindor Common Room in the game, he does not reappear in the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Half-Blood_Prince_(video_game)" title="Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince (video game)">video game adaptation</a> of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Half-Blood_Prince" title="Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince">Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince</a></i>.</li></ul>
