<p><b>Leanne</b> (c. <a href="/wiki/1979" title="1979">1979</a>-<a href="/wiki/1980" title="1980">1980</a>) was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch</a> who attended <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a>. She was a friend of <a href="/wiki/Katie_Bell" title="Katie Bell">Katie Bell</a> during her time at Hogwarts. She was sorted into <a href="/wiki/Hufflepuff" title="Hufflepuff">Hufflepuff</a><sup id="cite_ref-HBPF_5-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBPF-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> and attended Hogwarts around the same time as the famous <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP12_4-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP12-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> Leanne was not a member of <a href="/wiki/Dumbledore%27s_Army" title="Dumbledore&#39;s Army">Dumbledore's Army</a> and later fought in the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a> against <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a> and his <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a>. She survived the <a href="/wiki/Second_Wizarding_War" title="Second Wizarding War">Second Wizarding War</a> and her later life is currently unknown.
</p>

<p>Leanne was born in or around <a href="/wiki/1980" title="1980">1980</a> in <a href="/wiki/Great_Britain" title="Great Britain">Great Britain</a> or <a href="/wiki/Ireland" title="Ireland">Ireland</a>.

<p>Leanne started her <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magical</a> education at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> in <a href="/wiki/1991" title="1991">1991</a> and was <a href="/wiki/Sorting_ceremony" title="Sorting ceremony">sorted</a> into <a href="/wiki/Hufflepuff" title="Hufflepuff">Hufflepuff</a> <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Houses" title="Hogwarts Houses">House</a>. Her <a href="/wiki/Head_of_House" title="Head of House">Head of House</a> was the <a href="/wiki/Herbology" title="Herbology">Herbology</a> <a href="/wiki/Professor" title="Professor">Professor</a>, <a href="/wiki/Pomona_Sprout" title="Pomona Sprout">Pomona Sprout</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-HBPF_5-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBPF-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> Despite being in separate Houses, she became good friends with <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor" title="Gryffindor">Gryffindor</a> student <a href="/wiki/Katie_Bell" title="Katie Bell">Katie Bell</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP12_4-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP12-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> It is unknown if Leanne joined <a href="/wiki/Dumbledore%27s_Army" title="Dumbledore&#39;s Army">Dumbledore's Army</a> in <a href="/wiki/1995" title="1995">1995</a>, as Katie did, but she joined the group in late <a href="/wiki/1997" title="1997">1997</a>, when <a href="/wiki/Neville_Longbottom" title="Neville Longbottom">Neville Longbottom</a> assumed the group.<sup id="cite_ref-DHF2_6-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DHF2-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<p>In her <a href="/wiki/1996-1997_school_year" class="mw-redirect" title="1996-1997 school year">sixth year</a>, she and Katie went on a <a href="/wiki/Hogsmeade_weekend_trips" title="Hogsmeade weekend trips">weekend trip</a> to <a href="/wiki/Hogsmeade" title="Hogsmeade">Hogsmeade</a>, and they stopped off for a drink in the <a href="/wiki/Three_Broomsticks_Inn" title="Three Broomsticks Inn">Three Broomsticks Inn</a>. Katie excused herself and went to the lavatory whilst Leanne sat alone in the pub. After some time, she was spotted by Harry Potter sitting tapping her glass anxiously awaiting Katie's return. Finally, when she and Katie were returning to <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> after their visit to <a href="/wiki/Hogsmeade" title="Hogsmeade">Hogsmeade</a>, she and Katie got in an argument over a package that Katie had been given in the pub to take to the castle. Leanne did not want Katie to take it, as its origins were unknown and thereby suspicious. The argument caused the girls to fight over the package and Katie to accidentally touch the cursed <a href="/wiki/Opal_Necklace" title="Opal Necklace">Opal Necklace</a> that was given to her by <a href="/wiki/Rosmerta" title="Rosmerta">Madam Rosmerta</a>, who was under the <a href="/wiki/Imperius_Curse" title="Imperius Curse">Imperius Curse</a> at the time, when the package's wrapping tore.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP12_4-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP12-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>

<p>Leanne was very distraught when Katie was put under the <a href="/wiki/Curse" title="Curse">curse</a>, trying to pull her down from the sky in which she floated above from and was seen crying. Harry, <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ron</a> and <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione</a> got to the scene and, with the help of <a href="/wiki/Rubeus_Hagrid" title="Rubeus Hagrid">Rubeus Hagrid</a>, they got back to Hogwarts with Katie. When they got back inside the castle, Leanne broke down crying when telling <a href="/wiki/Minerva_McGonagall" title="Minerva McGonagall">Professor McGonagall</a> what happened and was sent to <a href="/wiki/Poppy_Pomfrey" title="Poppy Pomfrey">Madam Pomfrey</a> to get something for the shock.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP12_4-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP12-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> After Katie was healed and returned to Hogwarts, Leanne was present with her when Katie told Harry she did not know who gave her the package.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP24_7-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP24-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup>

<p>In Leanne's <a href="/wiki/1997%E2%80%931998_school_year" title="1997–1998 school year">seventh and final year</a> at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a>, her education would take a turn for the worst. With the former headmaster Albus Dumbledore dead, <a href="/wiki/Severus_Snape" title="Severus Snape">Severus Snape</a> took to the position of head and employed <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a>, <a href="/wiki/Amycus_Carrow" title="Amycus Carrow">Amycus Carrow</a> and <a href="/wiki/Alecto_Carrow" title="Alecto Carrow">Alecto Carrow</a> as new members of his staff. Any resistance against the war and defiance against their teaching methods had the student brutally punished, as demonstrated numerous times with pupil <a href="/wiki/Neville_Longbottom" title="Neville Longbottom">Longbottom</a>, the leader of the reformed <a href="/wiki/Dumbledore%27s_Army" title="Dumbledore&#39;s Army">Dumbledore's Army</a>, which she eventually joined in that year.<sup id="cite_ref-DHF2_6-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DHF2-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>On the <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Express" title="Hogwarts Express">Hogwarts Express</a> journey to the school that year, she sat amongst <a href="/wiki/Romilda_Vane" title="Romilda Vane">Romilda Vane</a>, <a href="/wiki/Lavender_Brown" title="Lavender Brown">Lavender Brown</a> and <a href="/wiki/Cormac_McLaggen" title="Cormac McLaggen">Cormac McLaggen</a>, as was present when the <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a> <a href="/wiki/Raid_on_the_Hogwarts_Express" title="Raid on the Hogwarts Express">raided and searched</a> the train for Harry Potter.<sup id="cite_ref-DHF1_8-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DHF1-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<p>Late into the academic year, the climax of the <a href="/wiki/Second_Wizarding_War" title="Second Wizarding War">Second Wizarding War</a> broke out and the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a> took place. Leanne courageously joined her fellow students and DA comrades in fighting against the <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a> and <a href="/wiki/Dark_Arts" title="Dark Arts">Dark Forces</a> which threatened their school. During the first part of the battle she was also seen fighting along side: Aberforth Dumbledore, Ginny Weasley, Padma Patil, Romilda Vane, Seamus Finnigan, and Katie Bell. She survived the battle and was later seen sitting in the Great Hall with Katie, Padma, and <a href="/wiki/Cho_Chang" title="Cho Chang">Cho Chang</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-DHF2_6-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DHF2-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup> However, what Leanne did after surviving the <a href="/wiki/Second_Wizarding_War" title="Second Wizarding War">Second Wizarding War</a>, is currently unknown.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Personality_and_traits">Personality and traits</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Leanne?veaction=edit&amp;section=7" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Personality and traits">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Leanne?action=edit&amp;section=7" title="Edit section: Personality and traits"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Leanne was evidently an intelligent and observant girl, as she warned Katie against touching Draco's necklace and later correctly deduced that she was acting under the influence of the <a href="/wiki/Imperius_Curse" title="Imperius Curse">Imperius Curse</a>. She was also very concerned for her friends' well-being and safety, showing her loyalty and kindness as a <a href="/wiki/Hufflepuff" title="Hufflepuff">Hufflepuff</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP12_4-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP12-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> In addition, Leanne was also highly courageous, as she bravely participated and fought in the Battle of Hogwarts against the Death Eaters, and even survived.<sup id="cite_ref-DHF2_6-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DHF2-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Magical_abilities_and_skills">Magical abilities and skills</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Leanne?veaction=edit&amp;section=8" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Leanne?action=edit&amp;section=8" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li><b><a href="/wiki/Duelling" title="Duelling">Duelling</a></b>: Leanne was clearly an excellent duellist, as she courageously fought in the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a> in <a href="/wiki/1998" title="1998">1998</a> as a member of <a href="/wiki/Dumbledore%27s_Army" title="Dumbledore&#39;s Army">Dumbledore's Army</a>, fighting against many highly dangerous <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a>, and even survived the battle, showing her to have been highly proficient in <a href="/wiki/Martial_magic" title="Martial magic">martial magic</a>, to have survived such a violent and bloody conflict.<sup id="cite_ref-DHF2_6-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DHF2-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup></li></ul>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Relationships">Relationships</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Leanne?veaction=edit&amp;section=9" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Relationships">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Leanne?action=edit&amp;section=9" title="Edit section: Relationships"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Katie_Bell">Katie Bell</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Leanne?veaction=edit&amp;section=10" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Katie Bell">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Leanne?action=edit&amp;section=10" title="Edit section: Katie Bell"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>

<p>Leanne was good friends with Katie Bell, chatting her to on the Hogwarts Express and attending <a href="/wiki/Hogsmeade" title="Hogsmeade">Hogsmeade</a> together. When Katie was bewitched with the <a href="/wiki/Imperius_Curse" title="Imperius Curse">Imperius Curse</a> and came into possession of the <a href="/wiki/Curse" title="Curse">cursed</a> <a href="/wiki/Opal_Necklace" title="Opal Necklace">Opal Necklace</a>, she tried to stop her friend from carrying the necklace to Hogwarts, with great effort. Leanne had a horrible reaction after Katie was harmed and nearly killed by the cursed necklace, showing her concern and worry over her friend's life, showing her to have been a very loyal and genuine friend to Katie.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP12_4-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP12-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Romilda_Vane">Romilda Vane</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Leanne?veaction=edit&amp;section=11" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Romilda Vane">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Leanne?action=edit&amp;section=11" title="Edit section: Romilda Vane"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Leanne seemed to get along well with <a href="/wiki/Romilda_Vane" title="Romilda Vane">Romilda Vane</a>, a bold and vivacious student two years younger than she was. The fought bravely together in the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a> against the <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a> to protect their school. Romilda sat with Leanne on the <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Express" title="Hogwarts Express">Hogwarts Express</a> on the way to school,<sup id="cite_ref-DHF1_8-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DHF1-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup> and much later they kept each other company and rested either during or after the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-DHF2_6-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DHF2-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup> Romilda also likely got along with Katie, given her personality, most likely as a result.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Leanne?veaction=edit&amp;section=12" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Leanne?action=edit&amp;section=12" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>Leanne is portrayed by <a href="/wiki/Isabella_Laughland" title="Isabella Laughland">Isabella Laughland</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Half-Blood_Prince_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince (film)">film adaptation</a> of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Half-Blood_Prince" title="Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince">Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince</a></i>.<sup id="cite_ref-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup> Based on screenshots from the film, she is a <a href="/wiki/Hufflepuff" title="Hufflepuff">Hufflepuff</a> who is potentially in the same year as Harry, although neither her house nor age is revealed in the book. She reprised her role in both parts of the film adaptation of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Deathly_Hallows" title="Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows">Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows</a></i>.<sup id="cite_ref-10" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-10">&#91;10&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li>In the book, it appears that Leanne was a <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor" title="Gryffindor">Gryffindor</a> in <a href="/wiki/Katie_Bell" title="Katie Bell">Katie Bell</a>'s year since Katie said <i>"Leanne was just telling me about McLaggen and the last match"</i> right after Harry saw her speaking with her fellow seventh years in the <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor_Tower" title="Gryffindor Tower">Gryffindor common room</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP24_7-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP24-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li>In the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Half-Blood_Prince_(video_game)" title="Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince (video game)">video game adaptation</a> of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Half-Blood_Prince" title="Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince">Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince</a></i>, Leanne has blonde hair during play mode, but during a cut scene mode she has brown, like in the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Half-Blood_Prince_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince (film)">film</a>.</li>
<li>In <i><a href="/wiki/LEGO_Harry_Potter:_Years_5-7" title="LEGO Harry Potter: Years 5-7">LEGO Harry Potter: Years 5-7</a></i>, Katie Bell's cursing is omitted and due to this, Leanne is absent from all the console versions of the game. However, Leanne is playable in the portable versions, including the ones for Nintendo DS and 3DS.</li></ul>
