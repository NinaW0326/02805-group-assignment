<p><b>Oliver Wiseman</b> (b. <a href="/wiki/1954" title="1954">1954</a>) was a <a href="/wiki/Detective_Inspector" title="Detective Inspector">Detective Inspector</a> for the <a href="/wiki/Westminster" title="Westminster">Westminster</a> <a href="/wiki/Anti-Terrorism_Unit" title="Anti-Terrorism Unit">Anti-Terrorism Unit</a>. He investigated the <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">collapse</a> of the <a href="/wiki/Millennium_Bridge" title="Millennium Bridge">Millennium Bridge</a> in <a href="/wiki/1996" title="1996">1996</a>, but refused to give <i><a href="/wiki/The_Guardian" title="The Guardian">The Guardian</a></i> any statement about it.<sup id="cite_ref-HPLE_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HPLE-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
