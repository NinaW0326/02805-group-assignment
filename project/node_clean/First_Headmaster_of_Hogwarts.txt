<div class="quote">
<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;The Wizarding World of Harry Potter">"<i>One statue is of Hogwarts' first headmaster: the founders were professors, none was ever a headmaster...</i>"</span></dd>
<dd>&#8212;Description<sup class="noprint"><a href="/wiki/The_Wizarding_World_of_Harry_Potter" title="The Wizarding World of Harry Potter">[src]</a></sup></dd>
<dd></dd></dl></div>
<p>This <b>individual</b> (fl. <i>circa</i> <a href="/wiki/11th_century" title="11th century">11th century</a>) was the first <a href="/wiki/Headmaster" title="Headmaster">Head</a> of <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a>, as none of the <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_founders" title="Hogwarts founders">Founders</a> ever held that title.<sup id="cite_ref-LTC_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-LTC-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> It is unknown how he came about to be Headmaster, but a statue of him was kept inside the <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Castle" title="Hogwarts Castle">castle</a> in his memory.<sup id="cite_ref-LTC_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-LTC-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<figure class="thumb tleft show-info-icon" style="width: 102px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/6/65/Studying.png/revision/latest?cb=20110108151317" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/6/65/Studying.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/100?cb=20110108151317" decoding="async" width="100" height="293" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="Studying.png" data-image-key="Studying.png" /></a> 	 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:Studying.png" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption">Some <a href="/wiki/Unidentified_studying_girls" title="Unidentified studying girls">girls</a> by his statue</p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure>
<p>During the <a href="/wiki/1992%E2%80%931993_school_year" title="1992–1993 school year">1992–1993 school year</a>, a <a href="/wiki/Unidentified_studying_girls" title="Unidentified studying girls">group of girls</a> were seen studying and reading books by his statue, before <a href="/wiki/Nicholas_de_Mimsy-Porpington" title="Nicholas de Mimsy-Porpington">Nicholas de Mimsy-Porpington</a> passed and they looked up.
</p><p>In <a href="/wiki/1998" title="1998">1998</a>, his statue replaced by the one of the <a href="/wiki/Architect_of_Hogwarts" title="Architect of Hogwarts">Architect of Hogwarts</a> at the <a href="/wiki/Entrance_Hall" title="Entrance Hall">Entrance Hall</a>. It is presumed the statue was destroyed in the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a>, as the ceiling above it came to collapse and, later on in the battle, all that was in the spot was a pile of rubble.<sup id="cite_ref-DHpt2vg_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DHpt2vg-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> For unknown reasons, a statue of him was stored in the <a href="/wiki/Room_of_Requirement" title="Room of Requirement">Room of Hidden Things</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-DHpt2_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DHpt2-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
