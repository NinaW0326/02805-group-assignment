<p><b>Mrs Abbott</b> (d. <a href="/wiki/1996" title="1996">1996</a>) was the mother of <a href="/wiki/Hufflepuff" title="Hufflepuff">Hufflepuff</a> student <a href="/wiki/Hannah_Abbott" title="Hannah Abbott">Hannah Abbott</a>. During the <a href="/wiki/Second_Wizarding_War" title="Second Wizarding War">Second Wizarding War</a>, Mrs Abbott was found dead in her home. She was murdered by <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a>, followers of <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a>. When Hannah was informed of this during a <a href="/wiki/Sixth_year" title="Sixth year">sixth year</a> <a href="/wiki/Herbology" title="Herbology">Herbology</a> class, she was removed from <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP11_1-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP11-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Hannah_Abbott%27s_mother?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Hannah_Abbott%27s_mother?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>Mrs Abbott may have been a <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggle</a> or <a href="/wiki/Muggle-born" title="Muggle-born">Muggle-born</a>, as the Abbott family is a known <a href="/wiki/Pure-blood" title="Pure-blood">pure-blood</a> family yet Hannah was a <a href="/wiki/Half-blood" title="Half-blood">half-blood</a>, which would explain why she was killed by Death Eaters. However, it is possible that it was instead one of Hannah's grandparents that had the Muggle ancestry.
<ul><li>Alternatively, she may have been a supporter of Muggles or refused to assist the Death Eaters.</li></ul></li></ul>
