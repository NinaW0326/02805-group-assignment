<b>Mrs Potter</b> (née <b>Fleamont</b>) was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch</a>. On her deathbed, she asked her son, <a href="/wiki/Henry_Potter" title="Henry Potter">Henry</a>, to preserve her maiden name, which would otherwise die out with her, as she was apparently the last of <a href="/wiki/Fleamont_family" title="Fleamont family">her line</a>. He honoured her request by naming his son <a href="/wiki/Fleamont_Potter" title="Fleamont Potter">Fleamont</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-Pottermore_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Pottermore-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
