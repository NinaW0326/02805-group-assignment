</p><p><a href="/wiki/Merchieftainess" title="Merchieftainess">Merchieftainess</a> <b>Murcus</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Selkie" title="Selkie">Selkie</a> and the leader of the <a href="/wiki/Merpeople" title="Merpeople">Merpeople</a> who lived in the <a href="/wiki/Great_Lake" title="Great Lake">lake</a> in the grounds of <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-GF26_1-10" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GF26-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Murcus?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Murcus?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Murcus was, presumably, born and raised in the <a href="/wiki/Great_Lake" title="Great Lake">Black Lake</a> in <a href="/wiki/Scotland" title="Scotland">Scotland</a>, and at some point in time was pronounced <a href="/wiki/Merchieftainess" title="Merchieftainess">Merchieftainess</a> of the <a href="/wiki/Great_Lake_Merpeople_colony" title="Great Lake Merpeople colony">Black Lake Selkie Colony</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-GF26_1-11" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GF26-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>She witnessed the <a href="/wiki/Triwizard_Tournament#1994_Triwizard_Tournament" title="Triwizard Tournament">Second Task</a> of the <a href="/wiki/Triwizard_Tournament" title="Triwizard Tournament">Triwizard Tournament</a> at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a>, watching carefully as each of the four champions struggled to achieve their task.<sup id="cite_ref-GF26_1-12" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GF26-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> 
</p><p>Thereafter, she relayed the events at the bottom of the lake to <a href="/wiki/Headmaster" title="Headmaster">Headmaster</a> <a href="/wiki/Albus_Dumbledore" title="Albus Dumbledore">Albus Dumbledore</a>, who could speak and understand <a href="/wiki/Mermish" title="Mermish">Mermish</a> and who was responsible for overseeing the task.<sup id="cite_ref-GF26_1-13" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GF26-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> 
</p><p>In <a href="/wiki/June" title="June">June</a> of <a href="/wiki/1997" title="1997">1997</a>, she and her tribe attended the <a href="/wiki/Funeral_of_Albus_Dumbledore" title="Funeral of Albus Dumbledore">Funeral of Albus Dumbledore</a>, where they sung for him, a tune that despite being very chilling was clearly a lament of sorrow.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP30_2-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP30-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> It was evident to the onlookers present that she and her colony, at least, were sad to see him go.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP30_2-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP30-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Physical_appearance">Physical appearance</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Murcus?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Physical appearance">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Murcus?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Physical appearance"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Murcus, like the rest of her tribe, had long, wild, dark-green hair and brilliant yellow eyes that matched her teeth.<sup id="cite_ref-GF26_1-14" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GF26-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> She was wilder and more ferocious than any of the others.<sup id="cite_ref-GF26_1-15" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GF26-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Personality_and_traits">Personality and traits</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Murcus?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Personality and traits">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Murcus?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Personality and traits"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Despite her lethal and ferocious looks, she appeared to be quite caring, advising the judges to increase all of the marks of the participants in the Triwizard Tournament<sup id="cite_ref-GF26_1-16" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GF26-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> and later singing gently as Albus Dumbledore was put to rest.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP30_2-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP30-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
