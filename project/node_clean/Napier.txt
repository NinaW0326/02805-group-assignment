<b>Napier</b> was an unidentified individual whose <a href="/wiki/Bone" title="Bone">bones</a> are noted on the <a href="/wiki/Marauder%27s_Map" title="Marauder&#39;s Map">Marauder's Map</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> Their bones were located near a tree stump and the entrance to a <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_secret_passages" title="Hogwarts secret passages">secret passageway</a><sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> that leads from the <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Castle#Fourth_Floor" title="Hogwarts Castle">Fourth Floor</a> in <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts</a> to <a href="/wiki/North_Street" title="North Street">North Street</a>, <a href="/wiki/Hogsmeade" title="Hogsmeade">Hogsmead</a>.
