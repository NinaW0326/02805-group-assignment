<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Gnarlak?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Gnarlak?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>During her time as an <a href="/wiki/Auror" title="Auror">Auror</a>, <a href="/wiki/Porpentina_Goldstein" title="Porpentina Goldstein">Porpentina Goldstein</a> used Gnarlak as an informant and was familiar with his establishment, <a href="/wiki/The_Blind_Pig" title="The Blind Pig">The Blind Pig</a>, and the business which occurred therein.<sup id="cite_ref-FBS_6-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-FBS-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Gnarlak carried&#160;two <a href="/wiki/Wand" title="Wand">wands</a>, although his skill in wand-based <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magic</a> is not known. It is also unknown how he gained ownership of such wands.<sup id="cite_ref-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<figure class="thumb tleft show-info-icon" style="width: 252px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/8/88/FB-_inside_the_blind_pig.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20180306181031" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/8/88/FB-_inside_the_blind_pig.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/250?cb=20180306181031" decoding="async" width="250" height="109" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="FB- inside the blind pig.jpg" data-image-key="FB-_inside_the_blind_pig.jpg" /></a> 	 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:FB-_inside_the_blind_pig.jpg" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption">Gnarlak inside The Blind Pig in 1926</p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure>
<p>While looking for <a href="/wiki/Newton_Scamander" title="Newton Scamander">Newton Scamander</a>'s missing animals, Tina suggested that Gnarlak could be useful in their hunt. Tina, Newt, <a href="/wiki/Jacob_Kowalski" title="Jacob Kowalski">Jacob Kowalski</a> and <a href="/wiki/Queenie_Goldstein" title="Queenie Goldstein">Queenie Goldstein</a> met with Gnarlak, whereupon they attempted to bargain for information. Gnarlak indicated that he wasn't interested in gold or trinkets, but when Newt offered an <a href="/wiki/Ashwinder_egg" title="Ashwinder egg">ashwinder egg</a>, his interest was piqued.<sup id="cite_ref-FBS_6-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-FBS-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>However, Gnarlak then spotted <a href="/wiki/Pickett" title="Pickett">Pickett</a>, one of Newt's <a href="/wiki/Bowtruckle" title="Bowtruckle">Bowtruckles</a>, and was greatly interested, as they were reportedly able to <a href="/wiki/Lock_picking" title="Lock picking">pick locks</a>. Newt reluctantly accepted this exchange.<sup id="cite_ref-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup>&#160;However, Gnarlak had already informed <a href="/wiki/Magical_Congress_of_the_United_States_of_America" title="Magical Congress of the United States of America">MACUSA</a> of Newt's location in an attempt to reap the bounty on his head. MACUSA agents entered the Pig and began arresting everyone in sight.<sup id="cite_ref-FBS_6-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-FBS-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>As he laughed with perceived victory, Jacob <a href="/wiki/Muggle_duelling" title="Muggle duelling">punched</a> Gnarlak in the face, noting that he reminded him of Jacob's <a href="/wiki/Jacob_Kowlaski%27s_foreman" title="Jacob Kowlaski&#39;s foreman">foreman</a>. As Gnarlak lay unconscious on the floor, Newt retrieved Pickett and <a href="/wiki/Apparition" title="Apparition">apparated</a> away with Jacob, Tina, and Queenie, leaving Gnarlak to face the <a href="/wiki/Auror" title="Auror">Aurors</a> once regaining consciousness. They have possibly arrested him for allowing "criminals" into his establishment, along with his general illegal dealings.<sup id="cite_ref-FBS_6-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-FBS-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Personality_and_traits">Personality and traits</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Gnarlak?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Personality and traits">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Gnarlak?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Personality and traits"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Gnarlak was a highly cunning, duplicitous and unpleasant Goblin, having engaged in many criminal dealings throughout his life as an black market gangster, showing no respect for American wizarding law. He was incredibly avaricious and money-minded, with his sole interest being earning a profit and gaining valuable assets, no matter what the cost. He was also very ruthless in achieving his aims, often being malicious to others, even to those who he dealt with, as when he slyly reported both <a href="/wiki/Newton_Scamander" title="Newton Scamander">Newton Scamander</a> and <a href="/wiki/Porpentina_Goldstein" title="Porpentina Goldstein">Porpentina Goldstein</a> to <a href="/wiki/Magical_Congress_of_the_United_States_of_America" title="Magical Congress of the United States of America">MACUSA</a>, even laughing spitefully afterwards, showing he clearly distrusted and disliked <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">Wizardkind</a> in general, like many of his species.
</p>

<ul><li><b><a href="/wiki/Wandless_magic" title="Wandless magic">Wandless magic</a></b>: Being a Goblin with his own <a href="/wiki/Goblin_magic" title="Goblin magic">kind of magic</a>, Gnarlak was capable of performing <a href="/wiki/Wandless_magic" title="Wandless magic">wandless magic</a>, being able to conjure a <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magical</a> protective barrier around himself and <a href="/wiki/Levitation" title="Levitation">levitate</a> objects around him.<sup id="cite_ref-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup></li></ul>
