The <b>Head of Ravenclaw</b> is a <a href="/wiki/Professor" title="Professor">Professor</a> at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> who is in charge of looking after and disciplining the students of their <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Houses" title="Hogwarts Houses">House</a>, Ravenclaw. The Head of House also serves as a Professor of a Subject, presumably a more academic subject for a Head of Ravenclaw. Most, if not all, of the heads of Ravenclaw house attended Hogwarts and were sorted into Ravenclaw during their attendance at the school, such as <a href="/wiki/Filius_Flitwick" title="Filius Flitwick">Filius Flitwick</a>. It is unknown how a professor would be elected to the post, but it's possible that the headmaster chooses a professor who embodies the qualities of house founder <a href="/wiki/Rowena_Ravenclaw" title="Rowena Ravenclaw">Rowena Ravenclaw</a> such as intelligence, wisdom, and wit.<sup id="cite_ref-PS7_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PS7-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-OOTP11_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP11-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>The first Head of Ravenclaw House was the famous co-founder of Hogwarts, Rowena Ravenclaw;<sup id="cite_ref-COS9_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COS9-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> the only other known Head of House was <a href="/wiki/Filius_Flitwick" title="Filius Flitwick">Filius Flitwick</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP29_4-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP29-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
