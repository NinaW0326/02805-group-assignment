<b>Joyce Haddock</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggle</a>. A swimming examiner at <a href="/wiki/Smeltings_Academy" title="Smeltings Academy">Smeltings Academy</a>, she presented <a href="/wiki/Distance_Award" title="Distance Award">an award</a> to <a href="/wiki/Dudley_Dursley" title="Dudley Dursley">Dudley Dursley</a> for swimming five metres, or one-quarter the full length of the pool in the <a href="/wiki/1990s" title="1990s">1990s</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
