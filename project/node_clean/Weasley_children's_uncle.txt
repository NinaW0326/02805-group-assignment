<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Weasley_children%27s_uncle?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Weasley_children%27s_uncle?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>As it is known that <a href="/wiki/Arthur_Weasley" title="Arthur Weasley">Arthur Weasley</a> had <a href="/wiki/Arthur_Weasley%27s_brothers" title="Arthur Weasley&#39;s brothers">two brothers</a> and no sisters,<sup id="cite_ref-JKR_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-JKR-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> it is widely assumed that the unnamed "uncle of Ron's" mentioned in <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Deathly_Hallows" title="Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows">Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows</a></i> is one of <a href="/wiki/Arthur_Weasley" title="Arthur Weasley">Arthur</a>'s brothers, and thus a Weasley. However, since it is unknown whether <a href="/wiki/Gideon_Prewett" title="Gideon Prewett">Gideon</a> and <a href="/wiki/Fabian_Prewett" title="Fabian Prewett">Fabian Prewett</a> were <a href="/wiki/Molly_Weasley" title="Molly Weasley">Molly Weasley</a>'s only siblings, it is possible the unnamed uncle was another brother of <a href="/wiki/Molly_Weasley" title="Molly Weasley">Molly</a> or the husband of her sister.</li></ul>
