<p><b>Lucius Malfoy I</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Great_Britain" title="Great Britain">British</a> <a href="/wiki/Pure-blood" title="Pure-blood">pure-blood</a> <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> in the <a href="/wiki/16th_century" title="16th century">16th century</a>. The <a href="/wiki/Malfoy_family" title="Malfoy family">Malfoy family</a> was correctly rumoured to come in contact with <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggles</a> and make a good living off of Muggle ways.
</p><p>At some point during his life, he aspired for the hand of <a href="/wiki/Elizabeth_I" title="Elizabeth I">Queen Elizabeth I</a> of <a href="/wiki/England" title="England">England</a>. He was unsuccessful in his attempt to woo her. Although never proven, many <a href="/wiki/Wizarding_world" title="Wizarding world">wizarding</a> historians believe this upset Lucius enough to make him <a href="/wiki/Jinx_against_marriage" title="Jinx against marriage">jinx</a> the Queen, resulting in her well-known reluctant behaviour towards marriage. This was denied by subsequent generations of the <a href="/wiki/Malfoy_family" title="Malfoy family">Malfoy family</a>, but there has never been solid evidence for either side.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>His name would eventually be reused for <a href="/wiki/Lucius_Malfoy" title="Lucius Malfoy">his descendant</a> about four centuries later.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Lucius_Malfoy_I?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Lucius_Malfoy_I?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>The name Lucius may come from the Roman dictator <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Lucius_Cornelius_Sulla" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Lucius Cornelius Sulla">Lucius Cornelius Sulla</a>, who extra judiciously executed his rivals. His name could also be a reference to the Roman Emperor Lucius Domitius Ahenobarbus, also known as <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Nero" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Nero">Nero</a>. Lucius is a name that derives from Latin meaning "bright" or "intelligent" and is related to the name Lucifer, the first name of Satan, which may reference Lucius Malfoy being bright but evil, or just evil.</li>
<li><i>Malfoy</i> is derived from the French <i>mal foi</i> or <i>mal foy</i>, meaning <i>bad faith</i> or <i>unfaithful</i>.</li>
<li>A Latin male first name. A character in Shakespeare's play Julius Caesar, Lucius is the servant of Brutus, the leader of the conspirators who plot against and assassinate Caesar. This is ironic, as Brutus is the name of one of Malfoy's descendants.</li></ul>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Lucius_Malfoy_I?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Lucius_Malfoy_I?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>In <a href="/wiki/1992" title="1992">1992</a>, <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>, speaking about <a href="/wiki/Draco_Malfoy" title="Draco Malfoy">Draco Malfoy</a>, said, <i>"Look at his family... The whole lot of them have been in <a href="/wiki/Slytherin" title="Slytherin">Slytherin</a>, he's always boasting about it"</i>. This indicates that Lucius was most likely a Slytherin if he attended <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a>, though it cannot be confirmed.</li>
<li>In <a href="/wiki/1997" title="1997">1997</a>, a <a href="/wiki/Unidentified_portrait_in_Malfoy_Manor_(II)" title="Unidentified portrait in Malfoy Manor (II)">portrait of a wizard in Elizabethan garb</a> was hung in the drawing room at <a href="/wiki/Malfoy_Manor" title="Malfoy Manor">Malfoy Manor</a>; Lucius may be that wizard.</li></ul>
