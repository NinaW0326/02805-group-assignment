<b>Angelica Cole</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch</a> who began attending <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> in <a href="/wiki/1980" title="1980">1980</a><sup id="cite_ref-age_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-age-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-graduation_4-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-graduation-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> and was <a href="/wiki/Sorting_ceremony" title="Sorting ceremony">Sorted</a> into <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor" title="Gryffindor">Gryffindor</a>. In <a href="/wiki/1984" title="1984">1984</a>, she was made a <a href="/wiki/Prefect" title="Prefect">Prefect</a>, and was responsible for watching over the <a href="/wiki/First_year" title="First year">first years</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Angelica was still at Hogwarts the following two years and graduated in <a href="/wiki/1987" title="1987">1987</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-graduation_4-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-graduation-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h3><span id="Hogwarts_years_(1980–1987)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="Hogwarts_years_.281980.E2.80.931987.29">Hogwarts years <span class="template-c">(1980–1987)</span></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Angelica_Cole?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Hogwarts years (1980–1987)">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Angelica_Cole?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Hogwarts years (1980–1987)"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Angelica became a Prefect in <a href="/wiki/1984" title="1984">1984</a>. She also became the <a href="/wiki/Quidditch_Captain#Hogwarts_Quidditch_Captains" title="Quidditch Captain">captain</a> of the <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor_Quidditch_team" title="Gryffindor Quidditch team">Gryffindor Quidditch team</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-Y3SQ_3-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Y3SQ-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>In the <a href="/wiki/1986%E2%80%931987_school_year" title="1986–1987 school year">1986–1987 school year</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-Y3SQTwitter_6-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Y3SQTwitter-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup> she met up with <a href="/wiki/Jacob%27s_sibling" title="Jacob&#39;s sibling">Jacob's sibling</a> and <a href="/wiki/Nicholas_de_Mimsy-Porpington" title="Nicholas de Mimsy-Porpington">Nicholas de Mimsy-Porpington</a>. She revealed that she had always liked <a href="/wiki/Jacob_(Hogwarts_student)" title="Jacob (Hogwarts student)">Jacob</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-Y3SQ_3-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Y3SQ-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>She graduated from Hogwarts in <a href="/wiki/1987" title="1987">1987</a>, and went on to join the <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">Ministry</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-graduation_4-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-graduation-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Angelica_Cole?veaction=edit&amp;section=4" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Angelica_Cole?action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>In <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter:_Hogwarts_Mystery" title="Harry Potter: Hogwarts Mystery">Harry Potter: Hogwarts Mystery</a></i>, given the fact that she left the <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor_vs._Slytherin_Quidditch_match_(1985)" class="mw-redirect" title="Gryffindor vs. Slytherin Quidditch match (1985)">Gryffindor vs. Slytherin</a> match early in her sixth year (for <a href="/wiki/Ravenclaw" title="Ravenclaw">Ravenclaw</a> and <a href="/wiki/Slytherin" title="Slytherin">Slytherin</a> players),<sup id="cite_ref-10" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-10">&#91;10&#93;</a></sup> it is possible she only became a regular on the Gryffindor Quidditch team in her <a href="/wiki/Seventh_year" title="Seventh year">seventh year</a>, during which she was the team's Captain. This would not be the only case of a new player becoming Quidditch Captain, as it was the case with <a href="/wiki/Urquhart" title="Urquhart">Urquhart</a> and the <a href="/wiki/Slytherin_Quidditch_team" title="Slytherin Quidditch team">Slytherin Quidditch team</a> in the <a href="/wiki/1996%E2%80%931997_school_year" title="1996–1997 school year">1996–1997 school year</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-11" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-11">&#91;11&#93;</a></sup></li></ul>
