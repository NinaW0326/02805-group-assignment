<b>Nurse Wainscott</b><sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch</a> who worked as a nurse at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> under <a href="/wiki/Poppy_Pomfrey" title="Poppy Pomfrey">Poppy Pomfrey</a> during the <a href="/wiki/1990s" title="1990s">1990s</a>. She attended to the fighters' injuries in the <a href="/wiki/Great_Hall" title="Great Hall">Great Hall</a> during the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-DH_3-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Wainscott?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Wainscott?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_life">Early life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Wainscott?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Early life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Wainscott?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Early life"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Little is known of Nurse Wainscott's early life; however, she was born presumably in <a href="/wiki/Great_Britain" title="Great Britain">Great Britain</a>, sometime before <a href="/wiki/1981" title="1981">1981</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-SENSE_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-SENSE-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="School_and_later_career">School and later career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Wainscott?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: School and later career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Wainscott?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: School and later career"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>She somehow learned magic, presumably at Hogwarts, though it is unknown exactly where or when. After having done so, she began her profession as a nurse at Hogwarts, working under <a href="/wiki/Poppy_Pomfrey" title="Poppy Pomfrey">Madam Poppy Pomfrey</a>
</p>

<p>In <a href="/wiki/1998" title="1998">1998</a>, Wainscott attended the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a>, attending to the various injuries of the fighters; it is possible that she fought as well, though this is considered unlikely.
</p>
