<p><b>Quidditch commentators</b> provide live commentary during <a href="/wiki/Quidditch" title="Quidditch">Quidditch</a> matches. As it can be difficult for spectators to visually follow the extremely fast played sport, commentary is audible throughout the <a href="/wiki/Quidditch_pitch" title="Quidditch pitch">stadium</a>. There are often two commentators working together as a team.<sup id="cite_ref-QWC_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-QWC-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
