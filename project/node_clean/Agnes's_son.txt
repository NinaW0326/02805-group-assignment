<p>An <b>unknown individual</b> was the son of <a href="/wiki/Agnes" title="Agnes">Agnes</a>, a patient in the long-term <a href="/wiki/Janus_Thickey_Ward" title="Janus Thickey Ward">Janus Thickey Ward</a> at <a href="/wiki/St_Mungo%27s_Hospital_for_Magical_Maladies_and_Injuries" title="St Mungo&#39;s Hospital for Magical Maladies and Injuries">St Mungo's Hospital for Magical Maladies and Injuries</a>. He intended to visit her over <a href="/wiki/Christmas" title="Christmas">Christmas</a> in <a href="/wiki/1995" title="1995">1995</a>. When the <a href="/wiki/Healer" title="Healer">Healer</a> told Agnes of the owl he had sent informing them that he would be coming, her only reply was to give several loud barks.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
