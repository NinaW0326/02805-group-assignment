<b>Old Mother Hubbard</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Hag" title="Hag">Hag</a> who lived in the <a href="/wiki/Middle_Ages" title="Middle Ages">Middle Ages</a>. She used to lure stray animals into her home and then starve them to <a href="/wiki/Death" title="Death">death</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-POAG_1-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-POAG-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
