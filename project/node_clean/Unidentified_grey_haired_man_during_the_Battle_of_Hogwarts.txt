This <b>individual</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> who fought during the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a> at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> in <a href="/wiki/1998" title="1998">1998</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-DHF2_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DHF2-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> 
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_grey_haired_man_during_the_Battle_of_Hogwarts?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_grey_haired_man_during_the_Battle_of_Hogwarts?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>On the <a href="/wiki/2_May" title="2 May">2 May</a>, <a href="/wiki/1998" title="1998">1998</a>, he was one of the many <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizards and witches</a> who fought bravely in the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a>, to defeat <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a> and <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaterss</a>. During the final stage of the battle, he was seen fighting a bald <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eater</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Great_Hall" title="Great Hall">Great Hall</a>, who he managed to kill while <a href="/wiki/Duelling" title="Duelling">duelling</a> ferociously against the Death Eaters. It is unknown if he survived the battle.<sup id="cite_ref-DHF2_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DHF2-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
