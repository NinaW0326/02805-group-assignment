This <b>witch</b> attended <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> and was <a href="/wiki/Sorting_ceremony" title="Sorting ceremony">sorted</a> into <a href="/wiki/Hufflepuff" title="Hufflepuff">Hufflepuff House</a>. She attended the school at some point between <a href="/wiki/1908" title="1908">1908</a> and <a href="/wiki/1914" title="1914">1914</a>, as she can be seen in a class with <a href="/wiki/Newton_Scamander" title="Newton Scamander">Newt Scamander</a>. This would mean that she is in the same age group as Newt, so it is likely that she was either born in <a href="/wiki/1896" class="mw-redirect" title="1896">1896</a> or <a href="/wiki/1897" class="mw-redirect" title="1897">1897</a>.
</p>

<h3><span id="Hogwarts_years_(1908-unknown)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="Hogwarts_years_.281908-unknown.29">Hogwarts years (1908-unknown)</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_Hufflepuff_girl_(V)?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Hogwarts years (1908-unknown)">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_Hufflepuff_girl_(V)?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Hogwarts years (1908-unknown)"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<figure class="thumb tleft show-info-icon" style="width: 182px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/1/15/Hufflepuff_girl_riddikulus.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20180722171248" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/1/15/Hufflepuff_girl_riddikulus.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/180?cb=20180722171248" decoding="async" width="180" height="179" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="Hufflepuff girl riddikulus.jpg" data-image-key="Hufflepuff_girl_riddikulus.jpg" /></a> 	 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:Hufflepuff_girl_riddikulus.jpg" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption">The girl casts Riddikulus on a Boggart during class</p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure>
<p>She likely started attending <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> in <a href="/wiki/1908" title="1908">1908</a>, being sorted into <a href="/wiki/Hufflepuff" title="Hufflepuff">Hufflepuff</a>. At some point during this time, she attended a class taught by <a href="/wiki/Albus_Dumbledore" title="Albus Dumbledore">Albus Dumbledore</a>. She cast <i><a href="/wiki/Boggart-Banishing_Spell" title="Boggart-Banishing Spell">Riddikulus</a></i> on a <a href="/wiki/Boggart_in_the_Wardrobe" title="Boggart in the Wardrobe">Boggart</a>, immediately before fellow student and Hufflepuff <a href="/wiki/Newton_Scamander" title="Newton Scamander">Newt Scamander</a> did so.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_Hufflepuff_girl_(V)?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_Hufflepuff_girl_(V)?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>Despite appearing in the <a target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener" class="external text" href="https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=vvFybpmyB9E">official Comic Con trailer</a>, casting <a href="/wiki/Boggart-Banishing_Spell" title="Boggart-Banishing Spell">Riddikulus</a> just before <a href="/wiki/Newton_Scamander" title="Newton Scamander">Newt Scamander</a>, she ultimately did not appear in the film. She was presumably cut for having no significance to the overall plot.</li></ul>
