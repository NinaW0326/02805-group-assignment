This <b>unidentified Death Eater</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Dark_Wizard" title="Dark Wizard">Dark Wizard</a>, <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eater</a> and servant of <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a>, who fought for his <a href="/wiki/Pure-blood_supremacy" title="Pure-blood supremacy">cause</a>&#160;in the <a href="/wiki/Second_Wizarding_War" title="Second Wizarding War">Second Wizarding War</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-LEGOHP5-7_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-LEGOHP5-7-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_Death_Eater_who_tortured_a_wizard?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_Death_Eater_who_tortured_a_wizard?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>He was one of the Death Eaters who participated and fought in the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a> with his fellow Death Eaters and Voldemort's army, on the <a href="/wiki/2_May" title="2 May">2 May</a> <a href="/wiki/1998" title="1998">1998</a>. During the battle of Hogwarts, he was seen <a href="/wiki/Cruciatus_Curse" title="Cruciatus Curse">torturing</a> an unknown <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a>, before Voldemort called of the battle and commanded all his Death Eaters to return to him in the <a href="/wiki/Forbidden_Forest" title="Forbidden Forest">Forbidden Forest</a>, which he did accordingly.<sup id="cite_ref-LEGOHP5-7_3-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-LEGOHP5-7-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
