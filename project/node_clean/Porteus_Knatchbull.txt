</p><p><b>Minister Porteus Knatchbull</b> was <a href="/wiki/Minister_for_Magic" title="Minister for Magic">Minister for Magic</a> from <a href="/wiki/1781" class="mw-redirect" title="1781">1781</a> to <a href="/wiki/1789" class="mw-redirect" title="1789">1789</a>. He was called in confidentially in <a href="/wiki/1782" class="mw-redirect" title="1782">1782</a> by the Muggle <a href="/wiki/Prime_Minister_(title)" title="Prime Minister (title)">Prime Minister</a> of the day, <a href="/wiki/Lord_North" title="Lord North">Lord North</a>, to see whether he could help with <a href="/wiki/George_III" title="George III">King George III</a>'s emerging mental instability. Word leaked out that Lord North believed in wizards, and he was forced to resign after a motion of no confidence.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>First elected in <a href="/wiki/1781" class="mw-redirect" title="1781">1781</a>, he was reelected at least once, and possibly was defeated for reelection in <a href="/wiki/1789" class="mw-redirect" title="1789">1789</a>.
</p>
