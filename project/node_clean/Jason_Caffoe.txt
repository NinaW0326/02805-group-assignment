</p><p><b>Jason Caffoe</b> is an artist and illustrator who worked alongside <a href="/wiki/Kazu_Kibuishi" title="Kazu Kibuishi">Kazu Kibuishi</a> to design the <a href="/wiki/United_States_of_America" title="United States of America">American</a> covers for the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_series" title="Harry Potter series">Harry Potter series</a>, which were released on <a href="/wiki/August" title="August">August</a> <a href="/wiki/27_August" title="27 August">27</a> <a href="/wiki/2013" title="2013">2013</a>. Jason works in Seattle, <a href="/wiki/Washington" title="Washington">Washington</a>, in the United States.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
