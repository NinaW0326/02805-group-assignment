<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Agnes?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Agnes?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>She had fur all over her head and barked instead of speaking. Her <a href="/wiki/Healer" title="Healer">Healer</a> <a href="/wiki/Miriam_Strout" title="Miriam Strout">Miriam Strout</a> informed that her <a href="/wiki/Agnes%27s_son" title="Agnes&#39;s son">son</a> would visit her <a href="/wiki/Christmas" title="Christmas">Christmas</a> night. It is possible that she remained at St Mungo's for the rest of her life. Her exact ailment was unknown, but it was noted to be a similar effect to consuming <a href="/wiki/Polyjuice_Potion" title="Polyjuice Potion">Polyjuice Potion</a> prepared with animal DNA.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP23_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP23-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Agnes?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Agnes?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>Given the description on  <i><a href="/wiki/Pottermore" title="Pottermore">Pottermore</a></i> presents what happens if an <a href="/wiki/Animagus" title="Animagus">Animagus</a> <a href="/wiki/Human_Transfiguration" title="Human Transfiguration">transformation</a> fails, it seems likely that this is what happened to Agnes, with her attempted Animagus form most likely being a <a href="/wiki/Dog" title="Dog">dog</a>.</li></ul>
