He played as the <a href="/wiki/Keeper" title="Keeper">Keeper</a> for the <a href="/wiki/Slytherin_Quidditch_team" title="Slytherin Quidditch team">Slytherin Quidditch team</a> in <a href="/wiki/1996" title="1996">1996</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-HBPF_2-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBPF-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
