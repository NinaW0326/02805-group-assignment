<b>Vicky Frobisher</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch</a> who attended <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> in the <a href="/wiki/1990s" title="1990s">1990s</a> and was <a href="/wiki/Sorting_ceremony" title="Sorting ceremony">sorted</a> into <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor" title="Gryffindor">Gryffindor</a> house.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP13_3-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP13-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Vicky_Frobisher?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Vicky_Frobisher?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>In <a href="/wiki/1995" title="1995">1995</a>, she tried out for the <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor_Quidditch_team" title="Gryffindor Quidditch team">Gryffindor Quidditch team</a>, but was denied the position of <a href="/wiki/Keeper" title="Keeper">Keeper</a> because she admitted she would put the <a href="/wiki/Charms_Club" title="Charms Club">Charms Club</a>, to which she belonged, before <a href="/wiki/Quidditch" title="Quidditch">Quidditch</a>. Being unable to attend some practises was the reason <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ron Weasley</a> was picked, despite the fact that Vicky played better. According to Angelina, Vicky was also involved in other Hogwarts clubs and societies, besides Charms.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP13_3-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP13-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
