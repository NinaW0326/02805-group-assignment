<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Hugo_Granger-Weasley?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Hugo_Granger-Weasley?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<figure class="thumb tleft show-info-icon" style="width: 181px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/5/5c/Aaf.png/revision/latest?cb=20120128193108" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/5/5c/Aaf.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/179?cb=20120128193108" decoding="async" width="179" height="185" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="Aaf.png" data-image-key="Aaf.png" /></a> 	 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:Aaf.png" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption">Hugo with his parents</p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure>
<p>Hugo Granger-Weasley was born around 2008 to a <a href="/wiki/Pure-blood" title="Pure-blood">pure-blood</a> father and a <a href="/wiki/Muggle-born" title="Muggle-born">Muggle-born</a> mother, making him half-blood. He is about two years younger than his sister <a href="/wiki/Rose_Granger-Weasley" title="Rose Granger-Weasley">Rose</a>, and around the same age as his cousin <a href="/wiki/Lily_L._Potter" title="Lily L. Potter">Lily Potter</a>, daughter of <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry</a> and <a href="/wiki/Ginevra_Weasley" title="Ginevra Weasley">Ginny Potter</a>, and the two seem to be quite close.<sup id="cite_ref-DH-Ep_4-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH-Ep-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>He, along with the rest of his family, attended the <a href="/wiki/2014_Quidditch_World_Cup" title="2014 Quidditch World Cup">2014 Quidditch World Cup</a>&#160;and sat in VIP Box Two. He was supporting <a href="/wiki/Brazil" title="Brazil">Brazil</a> in the final Brazil-<a href="/wiki/Bulgaria" title="Bulgaria">Bulgaria</a> match, as were his sister and father, but his mother remained neutral.
</p>
<figure class="thumb tleft show-info-icon" style="width: 253px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/9/9e/Hermione_%26_Ron_with_their_children_at_platform_nine_and_three_quarters.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20140813031055" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/9/9e/Hermione_%26_Ron_with_their_children_at_platform_nine_and_three_quarters.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/251?cb=20140813031055" decoding="async" width="251" height="128" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="Hermione &amp; Ron with their children at platform nine and three quarters.jpg" data-image-key="Hermione_&amp;_Ron_with_their_children_at_platform_nine_and_three_quarters.jpg" /></a> 	 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:Hermione_%26_Ron_with_their_children_at_platform_nine_and_three_quarters.jpg" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption">Hugo with his family on <a href="/wiki/Platform_9%C2%BE" class="mw-redirect" title="Platform 9¾">Platform <b>9¾</b></a><b> </b>in <a href="/wiki/2017" title="2017">2017</a></p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure>
<p>In 2017, he accompanied his parents to see off his sister and his <a href="/wiki/Potter_family" title="Potter family">Potter</a> cousins to <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a>. Hugo and Lily shared an eagerness to go to Hogwarts, despite both of them being too young. At <a href="/wiki/King%27s_Cross_Station" title="King&#39;s Cross Station">King's Cross Station,</a> the two had "<i>an animated discussion about which House they would be sorted into when they finally went to Hogwarts</i>".<sup id="cite_ref-DH-Ep_4-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH-Ep-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Hugo_Granger-Weasley?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Hugo_Granger-Weasley?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p><i><a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hugo" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Hugo">Hugo</a></i> is a Latinised form of the Germanic or Teutonic name <i>Hugh</i>, meaning "bright in spirit and mind". French novelist and poet <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Victor_Hugo" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Victor Hugo">Victor Hugo</a> was a famous bearer of this name. J. K. Rowling loved his works. As a muggle-born and a book lover, it is possible that Hermione enjoyed Victor Hugo's work as well, and named her son after him.
</p><p>It is also possible that Ron and Hermione wanted their children to have the same initials as their parents, <i>R</i> and <i>H</i>. Interestingly, both children also have names consisting of four letters.
</p><p><i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Goblet_of_Fire" title="Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire">Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire</a></i> was also awarded the <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hugo_Award" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Hugo Award">Hugo Award</a>, so it is possible that this was <a href="/wiki/J._K._Rowling" title="J. K. Rowling">J. K. Rowling</a>'s way of thanking them for that honour.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Hugo_Granger-Weasley?veaction=edit&amp;section=4" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Hugo_Granger-Weasley?action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>Hugo is portrayed in <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Deathly_Hallows:_Part_2" title="Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 2">Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 2</a></i> by <a href="/wiki/Ryan_Turner" title="Ryan Turner">Ryan Turner</a>.</li>
<li>In <i><a href="/wiki/LEGO_Harry_Potter" title="LEGO Harry Potter">LEGO Harry Potter</a></i>, Hugo appears licking a lollipop. His father, Ron, tries to swipe it from him but misses, as Hugo moves his arm.</li>
<li>In the film, Hugo has red hair like his father instead of brown hair like his mother. This is likely because the information about his hair colour was released after the film.</li>
<li>Hugo does not make an appearance in the Cursed Child, even though he was present in the same event (first year of Albus Potter) in the Epilogue of Deathly Hallows.</li></ul>
