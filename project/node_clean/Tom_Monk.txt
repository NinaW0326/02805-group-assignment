<b>Tom Monk</b> (b. <a href="/wiki/20_May" title="20 May">20 May</a>, <a href="/wiki/1995" title="1995">1995</a><sup id="cite_ref-20May_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-20May-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>) was a <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggle</a> boy, the son of <a href="/wiki/Dave_Monk" title="Dave Monk">Dave</a> and <a href="/wiki/Tilly_Monk" title="Tilly Monk">Tilly Monk</a>, and the twin brother of <a href="/wiki/Jack_Monk" title="Jack Monk">Jack</a>.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Tom_Monk?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Tom_Monk?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>In <a href="/wiki/1995" title="1995">1995</a>, Tom Monk and his brother were born prematurely, delivered simultaneously as what their father described as a "bundle," and received immediate medical care from "an amazing neonatal team." The twins were quickly taken to the <a href="/wiki/Neonatal_Intensive_Care_Unit" title="Neonatal Intensive Care Unit">Neonatal Intensive Care Unit</a>, where they were kept in incubators, receiving vital fluids through intravenous lines and breathing with the help of mechanical ventilators. Five days after his birth, Tom's condition was stable enough for his mother to hold him in her arms for the first time, which gave her a "huge boost." The twins' parents were finally able to take them home after twelve weeks. By this time, Tom weighed three-and-a-half pounds, having weighed only one pound, one ounce at birth.
</p><p>The twins' father wrote an article about their miraculous birth, which was featured on the front page of the <a href="/wiki/12_August" title="12 August">12 August</a>, <a href="/wiki/1995" title="1995">1995</a> issue of <i><a href="/wiki/Metro" title="Metro">Metro</a></i>, under the title "<i>Miracle babies make it</i>".<sup id="cite_ref-HPLE_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HPLE-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
