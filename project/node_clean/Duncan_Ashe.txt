<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Duncan_Ashe?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Duncan_Ashe?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>He was friends with <a href="/wiki/Jacob_(Hogwarts_student)" title="Jacob (Hogwarts student)">Jacob</a> from his first day at school until his dying day. The two were noted to be mischief makers by others, and frequently visited <a href="/wiki/Zonko%27s_Joke_Shop" title="Zonko&#39;s Joke Shop">Zonko's Joke Shop</a>. At some point, he and Jacob also broke their <a href="/wiki/Broomstick" title="Broomstick">brooms</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> He was also friends with the school <a href="/wiki/Poltergeist" title="Poltergeist">poltergeist</a>, <a href="/wiki/Peeves" title="Peeves">Peeves</a>. Sometime during the <a href="/wiki/1970s" title="1970s">1970s</a> or <a href="/wiki/1980s" title="1980s">1980s</a>, he died when compelled by <a href="/wiki/R" title="R">R</a> to concoct an Erumpent Potion, which exploded while he was preparing it. After his death, Duncan returned as a <a href="/wiki/Ghost" title="Ghost">ghost</a> and stayed at Hogwarts, though he rarely showed himself to the other residents of the Castle.<sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Personality">Personality</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Duncan_Ashe?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Personality">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Duncan_Ashe?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Personality"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>When he was alive, Duncan loved making mischief and was noted to be a mischief maker by others along with his friend, <a href="/wiki/Jacob_(Hogwarts_student)" title="Jacob (Hogwarts student)">Jacob</a>. After he died, he isolated himself and rarely showed himself to the other residents of the Castle. He was angry and resentful at Jacob for taking the blame for <a href="/wiki/R" title="R">R</a> and joining them.
</p>
