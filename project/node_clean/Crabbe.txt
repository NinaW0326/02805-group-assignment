<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Crabbe?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Crabbe?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_life">Early life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Crabbe?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Early life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Crabbe?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Early life"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Mr Crabbe was born into the <a href="/wiki/Crabbe_family" title="Crabbe family">Crabbe family</a>, a <a href="/wiki/Pure-blood" title="Pure-blood">Pure-blood</a> Wizarding family. He was possibly related to <a href="/wiki/Irma_Crabbe" title="Irma Crabbe">Irma Crabbe</a>, wife of <a href="/wiki/Pollux_Black" title="Pollux Black">Pollux Black</a>.
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="First_Wizarding_War">First Wizarding War</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Crabbe?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: First Wizarding War">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Crabbe?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: First Wizarding War"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Crabbe was a <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eater</a> during the <a href="/wiki/First_Wizarding_War" title="First Wizarding War">First Wizarding War</a> and was loyal to his master up until his downfall. In the aftermath, Crabbe was inaccurately acquitted of being a Death Eater by the <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">British Ministry of Magic</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> Around the downfall of Voldemort, Crabbe and his wife had a baby boy, <a href="/wiki/Vincent_Crabbe" title="Vincent Crabbe">Vincent Crabbe</a>, who would be educated at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts</a> from <a href="/wiki/1991" title="1991">1991</a>-<a href="/wiki/1998" title="1998">1998</a>, and he became friends with <a href="/wiki/Gregory_Goyle" title="Gregory Goyle">Gregory Goyle</a> and <a href="/wiki/Draco_Malfoy" title="Draco Malfoy">Draco Malfoy</a>.
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Second_Wizarding_War">Second Wizarding War</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Crabbe?veaction=edit&amp;section=4" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Second Wizarding War">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Crabbe?action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Second Wizarding War"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<div class="quote">
<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix">"<i>We'll split into pairs and search, and don't forget, be gentle with Potter until we've got the prophecy, you can kill the others if necessary...</i>"</span></dd>
<dd>&#8212;<a href="/wiki/Lucius_Malfoy" title="Lucius Malfoy">Lucius Malfoy</a> gives orders to the group of Death Eaters<sup class="noprint"><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Order_of_the_Phoenix" title="Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix">[src]</a></sup></dd>
<dd></dd></dl></div><p>Crabbe was one of the few Death Eaters who returned to <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a> when he rose to power again in <a href="/wiki/1995" title="1995">1995</a>. Voldemort was displeased when he learned that Crabbe didn't attempt to find him, but he forgave him.<sup id="cite_ref-GOF33_5-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF33-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>During the <a href="/wiki/Second_Wizarding_War" title="Second Wizarding War">Second Wizarding War</a>, Crabbe participated in the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_the_Department_of_Mysteries" title="Battle of the Department of Mysteries">Battle of the Department of Mysteries</a> in <a href="/wiki/1996" title="1996">1996</a>, and was paired with <a href="/wiki/Rabastan_Lestrange" title="Rabastan Lestrange">Rabastan Lestrange</a> during the pursuit of the six <a href="/wiki/Dumbledore%27s_Army" title="Dumbledore&#39;s Army">D.A.</a> members. He went with Lestrange to try and capture Harry, Hermione, and Neville, who were hiding in the <a href="/wiki/Time_Room" title="Time Room">Time Room</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP35_6-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP35-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup> He was likely imprisoned in Azkaban, but re-escaped in <a href="/wiki/1997" title="1997">1997</a>.
</p><p>During the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a> the following year, Crabbe's son Vincent (who had also become a <a href="/wiki/Dark_Wizard" title="Dark Wizard">Dark Wizard</a>) died by the effects of his own <a href="/wiki/Fiendfyre" title="Fiendfyre">Fiendfyre</a> <a href="/wiki/Curse" title="Curse">curse</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-DH31_7-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH31-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup> Crabbe's reaction over his son's death is unknown if he was even aware of it at all. Crabbe likely fought at the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a> as well, although his presence in this battle is unconfirmed.
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Post-war">Post-war</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Crabbe?veaction=edit&amp;section=5" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Post-war">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Crabbe?action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Post-war"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Crabbe was presumably killed or imprisoned in <a href="/wiki/Azkaban" title="Azkaban">Azkaban</a> after Voldemort's final death and defeat.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Physical_appearance">Physical appearance</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Crabbe?veaction=edit&amp;section=6" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Physical appearance">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Crabbe?action=edit&amp;section=6" title="Edit section: Physical appearance"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Much like his son, Crabbe was a large muscular man, and spoke with a dull voice.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Personality_and_traits">Personality and traits</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Crabbe?veaction=edit&amp;section=7" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Personality and traits">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Crabbe?action=edit&amp;section=7" title="Edit section: Personality and traits"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>He appeared to be rather unintelligent and slow-witted, but he was cunning enough to avoid <a href="/wiki/Azkaban" title="Azkaban">Azkaban</a> after his master's first downfall. Though he bowed sycophantically before Voldemort, his loyalty was highly questionable as he did not attempt to find his master when he disappeared.<sup id="cite_ref-GOF33_5-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF33-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> Like most Death Eaters, he was very sadistic and bloodthirsty, as he was willing to use the <a href="/wiki/Killing_Curse" title="Killing Curse">Killing Curse</a> on <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione Granger</a> who was a teenager, during the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_the_Department_of_Mysteries" title="Battle of the Department of Mysteries">Battle of the Department of Mysteries</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP35_6-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP35-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup> showing he had no qualms about harming innocents.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Magical_abilities_and_skills">Magical abilities and skills</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Crabbe?veaction=edit&amp;section=8" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Crabbe?action=edit&amp;section=8" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li><b><a href="/wiki/Dark_Arts" title="Dark Arts">Dark Arts</a></b>: Being a Death Eater, Crabbe was highly proficient in dark magic, including with the casting of the <a href="/wiki/Unforgivable_Curses" title="Unforgivable Curses">Unforgivable Curses</a> and other <a href="/wiki/Dark_charm" title="Dark charm">dark charms</a>. He was confident of his ability to murder <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione Granger</a> with the <a href="/wiki/Killing_Curse" title="Killing Curse">Killing Curse</a> in <a href="/wiki/1996" title="1996">1996</a>, a <a href="/wiki/Curse" title="Curse">curse</a> which requires much skill and malice to cast successfully.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP35_6-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP35-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Duelling" title="Duelling">Duelling</a></b>: Being a Death Eater, Crabbe would have been highly trained in <a href="/wiki/Martial_magic" title="Martial magic">Martial magic</a> and therefore a reasonably competent duellist, and likely participated in many battles during the <a href="/wiki/First_Wizarding_War" title="First Wizarding War">First Wizarding War</a>. He later participated in the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_the_Department_of_Mysteries" title="Battle of the Department of Mysteries">Battle of the Department of Mysteries</a>, where he fought against <a href="/wiki/Dumbledore%27s_Army" title="Dumbledore&#39;s Army">Dumbledore's Army</a> and the <a href="/wiki/Order_of_the_Phoenix" title="Order of the Phoenix">Order of the Phoenix</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP35_6-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP35-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Apparition" title="Apparition">Apparition</a></b>: Crabbe was accomplished in apparition, as he was able to apparate to the <a href="/wiki/Little_Hangleton_graveyard" title="Little Hangleton graveyard">Little Hangleton graveyard</a> to witness the <a href="/wiki/Return_of_Lord_Voldemort" title="Return of Lord Voldemort">return</a> of his master in <a href="/wiki/1995" title="1995">1995</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-GOF33_5-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF33-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup></li></ul>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Crabbe?veaction=edit&amp;section=9" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Crabbe?action=edit&amp;section=9" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>It is not specified if Crabbe attended <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a>.</li>
<li>In the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Goblet_of_Fire_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire (film)">film adaptation</a> of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Goblet_of_Fire" title="Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire">Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire</a></i>, Crabbe was played by either <a href="/wiki/Paschal_Friel" title="Paschal Friel">Paschal Friel</a> or <a href="/wiki/Philip_Rham" title="Philip Rham">Philip Rham</a>.</li>
<li>It's likely that he was the <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eater</a> whose head turned into that of a baby during the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_the_Department_of_Mysteries" title="Battle of the Department of Mysteries">Battle of the Department of Mysteries</a>. This essay<sup id="cite_ref-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup> states that either he or Rabastan Lestrange were the most likely Death Eaters in the Time Room. Though not identified, this Death Eater was large and had black hair (descriptions that apply to his son, <a href="/wiki/Vincent_Crabbe" title="Vincent Crabbe">Vincent Crabbe</a>) and was not recognised by Harry Potter. As his partner <a href="/wiki/Rabastan_Lestrange" title="Rabastan Lestrange">Rabastan Lestrange</a>'s appearance was known by Harry, he likely would've been identified.</li></ul>
