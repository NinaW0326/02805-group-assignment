<p><b>Wendelin the Weird</b> (fl. c. <a href="/wiki/14th_century" title="14th century">14th century</a>)<sup id="cite_ref-POA1_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-POA1-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> was an eccentric <a href="/wiki/Great_Britain" title="Great Britain">British</a> <a href="/wiki/Middle_Ages" title="Middle Ages">medieval</a> <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch</a> who was famous for being burnt at the stake no less than forty-seven times in various disguises.<sup id="cite_ref-POA1_1-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-POA1-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Wendelin_the_Weird?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Wendelin_the_Weird?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<figure class="thumb tright show-info-icon" style="width: 182px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/d/da/WendelintheWeird.png/revision/latest?cb=20190705064652" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/d/da/WendelintheWeird.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/180?cb=20190705064652" decoding="async" width="180" height="240" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="WendelintheWeird.png" data-image-key="WendelintheWeird.png" /></a> 	 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:WendelintheWeird.png" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption">Wendelin taking great pleasure in being burned at the stake</p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure>
<p>During the <a href="/wiki/Middle_Ages" title="Middle Ages">Middle Ages</a>, <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggle</a> <a href="/wiki/Witch-hunts" class="mw-redirect" title="Witch-hunts">witch-hunts</a> were at their peak. Stemming from the desire to experience a pleasant tickling sensation from the <a href="/wiki/Flame-Freezing_Charm" title="Flame-Freezing Charm">Flame-Freezing Charm</a>, Wendelin took upon herself to be captured no less than forty-seven times in various disguises to be repeatedly burned at the stake. With the use of the Flame-Freezing Charm, the <a href="/wiki/Fire" title="Fire">pyres</a> were rendered harmless, creating only a gentle tickling sensation which Wendelin enjoyed immensely.<sup id="cite_ref-PSG_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PSG-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-COSG_2-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COSG-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-POAG_4-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-POAG-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-POA1_1-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-POA1-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>During the <a href="/wiki/1992-1993_school_year" class="mw-redirect" title="1992-1993 school year">1992-1993 school year</a>, a <a href="/wiki/Portrait" title="Portrait">Portrait</a> of Wendelin was hung in the <a href="/wiki/Secret_Hallway" title="Secret Hallway">Secret Hallway</a>. <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> once traded a <a href="/wiki/Misprinted_Wendolyn_card" title="Misprinted Wendolyn card">rare misprint</a> of a Wendelin the Weird <a href="/wiki/Chocolate_Frog_Card" title="Chocolate Frog Card">Chocolate Frog Card</a> to this portrait in exchange for a baby <a href="/wiki/Spider" title="Spider">spider</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-COSG_2-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COSG-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Wendelin the Weird was mentioned when <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> had to write an essay for <a href="/wiki/History_of_Magic" title="History of Magic">History of Magic</a> in his <a href="/wiki/Third_year" title="Third year">third year</a>, the premise for which was that "<i><a href="/wiki/Witch_Burning_in_the_Fourteenth_Century_Was_Completely_Pointless_%E2%80%94_discuss" title="Witch Burning in the Fourteenth Century Was Completely Pointless — discuss">Witch Burning in the Fourteenth Century Was Completely Pointless — discuss</a></i>"<sup id="cite_ref-POA1_1-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-POA1-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Magical_abilities_and_skills">Magical abilities and skills</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Wendelin_the_Weird?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Wendelin_the_Weird?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li><b><a href="/wiki/Charm" title="Charm">Charms</a></b>: Wendelin was shown to be talented with charm-work, as she was highly capable of casting the <a href="/wiki/Flame-Freezing_Charm" title="Flame-Freezing Charm">Flame-Freezing Charm</a> to render <a href="/wiki/Fire" title="Fire">fire</a> harmless, which she cast frequently whenever she was burned at the stake, for a warm and tickling sensation every time, no less than forty seven times.<sup id="cite_ref-POA1_1-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-POA1-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup></li></ul>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Wendelin_the_Weird?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Wendelin_the_Weird?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>According to Harry's copy of <i><a href="/wiki/A_History_of_Magic" title="A History of Magic">A History of Magic</a></i> in <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Prisoner_of_Azkaban" title="Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban">Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban</a></i>, Wendelin was burned at least forty-seven times. However, according to her <a href="/wiki/Chocolate_Frog_Card" title="Chocolate Frog Card">card</a> in the video games, it states she was only burned fourteen times. It could be possible that all the other times, she was disguised and not credited with these "accomplishments". The Timeline of the Wizarding World given on  <i><a href="/wiki/Pottermore" title="Pottermore">Pottermore</a></i> reinforces that Wendelin was burned exactly forty-seven times.</li></ul>
