<b>Borf</b> was a male&#160;<a href="/wiki/Werewolf" title="Werewolf">werewolf</a> offspring of the <a href="/wiki/Forbidden_Forest_wolf_pack" title="Forbidden Forest wolf pack">Forbidden Forest wolf pack</a>.

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Borf?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Borf?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>As a member of the forbidden forest pack, Borf was descended from two werewolves who mated while in wolf form during a full moon, an extremely rare occurrence. Unlike humans affected with <a href="/wiki/Lycanthropy" title="Lycanthropy">Lycanthropy</a>, such wolves do not transform and are not any more savage than standard wolves while being extremely intelligent and beautiful.
</p><p>One day, Borf followed <a href="/wiki/Chiara_Lobosca" title="Chiara Lobosca">Chiara Lobosca</a> out of the <a href="/wiki/Forbidden_Forest" title="Forbidden Forest">Forbidden Forest</a>. He continued to live in the forest, but frequently visited Chiara, whom he had befriended. The cub was named Borf as that was what the sound he made sounded like.<sup id="cite_ref-Hallowe.27en_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Hallowe.27en-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>When a worker of the <a href="/wiki/Werewolf_Capture_Unit" title="Werewolf Capture Unit">Werewolf Capture Unit</a>, <a href="/wiki/Cecil_Lee" title="Cecil Lee">Cecil Lee</a> came to investigate Hogwarts following a werewolf attack, Chiara and <a href="/wiki/Rubeus_Hagrid" title="Rubeus Hagrid">Rubeus Hagrid</a> showed Borf as an example of a non-violent werewolf. <a href="/wiki/Jacob%27s_sibling" title="Jacob&#39;s sibling">Jacob's sibling</a>, who was also there, was confused.<sup id="cite_ref-Hallowe.27en_2-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Hallowe.27en-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Later, in the <a href="/wiki/1988%E2%80%931989_school_year" title="1988–1989 school year">1988–1989 school year</a>, Jacob's sibling and <a href="/wiki/Rowan_Khanna" title="Rowan Khanna">Rowan Khanna</a> came to to the Forbidden Forest to play with Borf in order to help Rowan calm down before their <a href="/wiki/Ordinary_Wizarding_Level" title="Ordinary Wizarding Level">O.W.L.s</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
