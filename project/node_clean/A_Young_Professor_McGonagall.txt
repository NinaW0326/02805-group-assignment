<p><i><b>A Young Professor McGonagall</b></i> was the name of a <a href="/wiki/Portrait" title="Portrait">portrait</a> of <a href="/wiki/Minerva_McGonagall" title="Minerva McGonagall">Minerva McGonagall</a> which hung on a wall within the <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor_Common_Room" title="Gryffindor Common Room">Gryffindor Common Room</a>, located inside <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor_Tower" title="Gryffindor Tower">Gryffindor Tower</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-MHP_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-MHP-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> The portrait depicted McGonagall when she was younger, while possibly still head of <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor" title="Gryffindor">Gryffindor House</a>.
</p>
