<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Fifth_year">Fifth year</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_blonde_Slytherin_girl?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Fifth year">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_blonde_Slytherin_girl?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Fifth year"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>During the <a href="/wiki/1995%E2%80%931996_school_year" title="1995–1996 school year">1995–1996 school year</a>, when <a href="/wiki/Fred_Weasley" title="Fred Weasley">Fred</a> and <a href="/wiki/George_Weasley" title="George Weasley">George Weasley</a> set off fireworks in the castle, she was seen looking at a <a href="/wiki/Weasleys%27_Wildfire_Whiz-bangs" title="Weasleys&#39; Wildfire Whiz-bangs">Whiz-Bang</a> that followed and hit <a href="/wiki/Vincent_Crabbe" title="Vincent Crabbe">Vincent Crabbe</a> in the rear end. She later attended her <a href="/wiki/Charms_(class)" title="Charms (class)">Charms</a> <a href="/wiki/Ordinary_Wizarding_Level" title="Ordinary Wizarding Level">O.W.L.</a> exam.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTPF_3-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTPF-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
