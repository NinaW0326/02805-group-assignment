<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_Death_Eater_killed_by_Kingsley_Shacklebolt?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_Death_Eater_killed_by_Kingsley_Shacklebolt?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Upon procuring a <a href="/wiki/Wand" title="Wand">wand</a> and learning the <a href="/wiki/Dark_Arts" title="Dark Arts">Dark Arts</a>, this wizard joined the <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a> and became a loyal follower and servant of <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a>. On <a href="/wiki/2_May" title="2 May">2 May</a> <a href="/wiki/1998" title="1998">1998</a>, he was called upon to fight in the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a>, along with all his fellow Death Eaters. After the protective enchantments around the castle were broken, he came smashing in through a window in <a href="/wiki/The_Quad" title="The Quad">The Quad</a> <a href="/wiki/The_Quad_battlements" title="The Quad battlements">battlements</a> to attack the defenders within. Before he could harm anybody, he was hit by <a href="/wiki/Momentum-reversing_spell" title="Momentum-reversing spell">Momentum-reversing spell</a> used by <a href="/wiki/Kingsley_Shacklebolt" title="Kingsley Shacklebolt">Kingsley Shacklebolt</a>, which halted him right in his tracks then sent him flying back out the window, <a href="/wiki/Death" title="Death">killing</a> him in the subsequent fall.<sup id="cite_ref-DHF2_1-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DHF2-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
