<p><b>Distractible Douglas</b> was a character in <a href="/wiki/Miranda_Goshawk" title="Miranda Goshawk">Miranda Goshawk</a>'s conundrum "<a href="/wiki/The_Sorry_Story_of_Distractible_Douglas" title="The Sorry Story of Distractible Douglas">The Sorry Story of Distractible Douglas</a>", published in the <i><a href="/wiki/Book_of_Spells" title="Book of Spells">Book of Spells</a></i>. Like all characters in these conundrums, he was based on a real <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a>, but had his name altered to protect his identity. Douglas was easily distracted, and liked being so.<sup id="cite_ref-WBoS_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-WBoS-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
