<div class="quote">
<dl><dd><span>"<i>I once knew a Muggle girl from Liverpool with hair like spun silk. No light could resist it...</i>"</span></dd>
<dd>&#8212;<a href="/wiki/Albus_Dumbledore" title="Albus Dumbledore">Albus Dumbledore</a> upon seeing a billboard advertising <a href="/wiki/Divine_Magic" title="Divine Magic">Divine Magic</a>, in <a href="/wiki/Surbiton_Station" title="Surbiton Station">Surbiton Station</a><sup id="cite_ref-script_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-script-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup></dd>
<dd></dd></dl></div>
<p>This <b><a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggle</a> girl from <a href="/wiki/Liverpool" title="Liverpool">Liverpool</a></b> was someone that the late <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts</a> <a href="/wiki/Headmaster" title="Headmaster">Headmaster</a> <a href="/wiki/Albus_Dumbledore" title="Albus Dumbledore">Albus Dumbledore</a> took a fancy to in his youth. A woman featured on a <a href="/wiki/Divine_Magic" title="Divine Magic">Divine Magic</a> billboard made <a href="/wiki/Albus_Dumbledore" title="Albus Dumbledore">Albus</a> remember about this girl.<sup id="cite_ref-script_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-script-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_Muggle_girl_from_Liverpool?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_Muggle_girl_from_Liverpool?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<div class="quote">
<dl><dd><span>"<i>In fact, recently I was in a script read through for the sixth film, and they had <a href="/wiki/Albus_Dumbledore" title="Albus Dumbledore">Dumbledore</a> saying a line to <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry</a> early in the script saying I knew a girl once, whose hair... [laughter]. I had to write a little note in the margin and slide it along to the scriptwriter, "<a href="/wiki/Albus_Dumbledore" title="Albus Dumbledore">Dumbledore</a>'s gay!" </i>"</span></dd>
<dd>&#8212;<a href="/wiki/J._K._Rowling" title="J. K. Rowling">J. K. Rowling</a></dd>
<dd></dd></dl></div>
<ul><li>This character was created to be mentioned in the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Half-Blood_Prince_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince (film)">film adaptation</a> of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Half-Blood_Prince" title="Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince">Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince</a></i> by her former boyfriend, <a href="/wiki/Professor" title="Professor">Professor</a> Dumbledore. However, the character was cut from the film when <a href="/wiki/J._K._Rowling" title="J. K. Rowling">J. K. Rowling</a> scribbled out her mention in the script and revealed that Dumbledore was gay. As such, this character is not considered to be <a href="/wiki/Canon" title="Canon">canon</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li>Rowling's note about this character marked the first time that Dumbledore was "outed", although Rowling does not directly indicate his orientation in any of the books beyond her subtle hints in <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Deathly_Hallows" title="Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows">Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows</a></i>.</li></ul>
