<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Pippa_Macmillan?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Pippa_Macmillan?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>During her second year at Hogwarts, Pippa could be seen sitting with <a href="/wiki/Ismelda_Murk" title="Ismelda Murk">Ismelda Murk</a> by the fountain in the <a href="/wiki/Courtyard" title="Courtyard">Courtyard</a>.
</p><p>In her <a href="/wiki/Third_year" title="Third year">third year</a>, during a full <a href="/wiki/Moon" title="Moon">moon</a> in late <a href="/wiki/September" title="September">September</a> or <a href="/wiki/October" title="October">October</a>, she was attacked by a white <a href="/wiki/Creature" title="Creature">creature</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Forbidden_Forest" title="Forbidden Forest">Forbidden Forest</a>, thinking that it was a <a href="/wiki/Werewolf" title="Werewolf">Werewolf</a>. She reported to <a href="/wiki/Poppy_Pomfrey" title="Poppy Pomfrey">Madam Pomfrey</a>, who treated her in the <a href="/wiki/Hospital_Wing" title="Hospital Wing">Hospital Wing</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-HM2_3-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HM2-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> Using a <a href="/wiki/Memory_Potion" title="Memory Potion">Memory Potion</a> with the help of <a href="/wiki/Chiara_Lobosca" title="Chiara Lobosca">Chiara Lobosca</a> and <a href="/wiki/Jacob%27s_sibling" title="Jacob&#39;s sibling">Jacob's sibling</a>, she found out that the creature was actually a <a href="/wiki/Hippogriff" title="Hippogriff">Hippogriff</a> and that she had walked into a tree.<sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
