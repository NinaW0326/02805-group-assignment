<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Dot?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Dot?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_life">Early life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Dot?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Early life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Dot?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Early life"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Dot was born to a <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggle</a> family sometime before <a href="/wiki/Frank_Bryce" title="Frank Bryce">Frank Bryce</a> was born. She was probably born in <a href="/wiki/Little_Hangleton" title="Little Hangleton">Little Hangleton</a>, since she remembers seeing him when he was a child, when he showed he had a "horrible temper."<sup id="cite_ref-GOF1_1-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF1-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="The_Riddle_Murders">The Riddle Murders</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Dot?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: The Riddle Murders">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Dot?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: The Riddle Murders"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>She was one of the many town residents who gathered at <a href="/wiki/The_Hanged_Man" title="The Hanged Man">The Hanged Man</a> to gossip on the night that the <a href="/wiki/Riddle_family" title="Riddle family">Riddle family</a> was found murdered in their home in <a href="/wiki/1943" title="1943">1943</a>. When it was announced that <a href="/wiki/Frank_Bryce" title="Frank Bryce">Frank Bryce</a> had been arrested, Dot said she thought Frank had always had a fierce temper, and that she had seen evidence of it when he was a child. The police later released Frank for lack of evidence, but Dot remained convinced of his guilt, remarking that he should leave, as all the villagers of <a href="/wiki/Little_Hangleton" title="Little Hangleton">Little Hangleton</a> "knew" he had done it.<sup id="cite_ref-GOF1_1-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF1-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> In all actuality <a href="/wiki/Frank_Bryce" title="Frank Bryce">Frank Bryce</a> was innocent, as the one who slain the <a href="/wiki/Riddle_family" title="Riddle family">Riddles</a> that night was <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Tom Marvolo Riddle</a>.
</p>
