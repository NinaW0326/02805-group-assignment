<b>Ignatius Prewett</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Pure-blood" title="Pure-blood">pure-blood</a> <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> who married <a href="/wiki/Lucretia_Black" title="Lucretia Black">Lucretia Black</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> He was an uncle by blood of <a href="/wiki/Fabian_Prewett" title="Fabian Prewett">Fabian</a> and <a href="/wiki/Gideon_Prewett" title="Gideon Prewett">Gideon Prewett</a>, as well as their sister <a href="/wiki/Molly_Weasley" title="Molly Weasley">Molly</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> and an uncle by <a href="/wiki/Marriage" title="Marriage">marriage</a> of <a href="/wiki/Sirius_Black" title="Sirius Black">Sirius</a> and <a href="/wiki/Regulus_Black" title="Regulus Black">Regulus Black</a>. Ignatius and his wife Lucretia did not have any children of their own.&#160;
</p><p>Given that Molly gave her third son <a href="/wiki/Percy_Weasley" title="Percy Weasley">Percy</a> the middle name "Ignatius", presumably in honour of her uncle, it is likely that she and Ignatius were close.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Ignatius_Prewett?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Ignatius_Prewett?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li><i>Ignatius</i> is a Roman name altered from its original Etruscan to resemble the Latin <i>ignis</i>, "<a href="/wiki/Fire" title="Fire">fire</a>". It was the name of several saints, including the founder of the Jesuits.<sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li>Given that <a href="/wiki/Lucretia_Black" title="Lucretia Black">Lucretia Black</a> from the <a href="/wiki/House_of_Black" title="House of Black">Black Family</a> was not disowned and burnt off the Black Family Tree, implies that her marriage to&#160;Ignatius Prewett was a "respectable" pureblood marriage.</li></ul>
