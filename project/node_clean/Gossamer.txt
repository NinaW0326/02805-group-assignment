<b>Gossamer </b> was an individual whose <a href="/wiki/Portrait" title="Portrait">portrait</a> was hung in <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Castle" title="Hogwarts Castle">Hogwarts Castle</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-MA_1-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-MA-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>He was unpopular among the other portraits for his chaotic personality. As a punishment, his portrait was removed from the <a href="/wiki/Grand_Staircase" title="Grand Staircase">Grand Staircase</a>, put into chains and hidden in one of the <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Houses" title="Hogwarts Houses">House</a> <a href="/wiki/Common_Room" class="mw-redirect" title="Common Room">Common Rooms</a>. Decades later, it was found by a group of students who freed him.<sup id="cite_ref-MA_1-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-MA-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
