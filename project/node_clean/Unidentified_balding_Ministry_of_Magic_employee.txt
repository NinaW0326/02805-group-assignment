<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_balding_Ministry_of_Magic_employee?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_balding_Ministry_of_Magic_employee?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>On the morning of <a href="/wiki/2_September" title="2 September">2 September</a>, <a href="/wiki/1997" title="1997">1997</a>, this wizard ran into "<a href="/wiki/Albert_Runcorn" title="Albert Runcorn">Albert Runcorn</a>" (actually <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> in disguise) while entering the Ministry headquarters through the fireplaces in the <a href="/wiki/Atrium" title="Atrium">Atrium</a>. At first he seemed annoyed, but he excused himself and hurried away nervously when he noticed who he had bumped into.<sup id="cite_ref-DH12_2-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH12-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Later, when there were <a href="/wiki/Infiltration_of_the_Ministry_of_Magic" title="Infiltration of the Ministry of Magic">intruders detected</a> in the Ministry, this wizard was sent to the <a href="/wiki/Atrium" title="Atrium">Atrium</a> in order to seal all exits and stop everyone from leaving. When "<a href="/wiki/Albert_Runcorn" title="Albert Runcorn">Albert Runcorn</a>" (actually <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>), "<a href="/wiki/Mafalda_Hopkirk" title="Mafalda Hopkirk">Mafalda Hopkirk</a>" (<a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione Granger</a>) and "<a href="/wiki/Reginald_Cattermole" title="Reginald Cattermole">Reginald Cattermole</a>" (<a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ronald Weasley</a>) were leading <a href="/wiki/Muggle-born" title="Muggle-born">Muggle-borns</a> in for <a href="/wiki/Muggle-Born_Registration_Commission" title="Muggle-Born Registration Commission">questioning</a> out to freedom, the wizard seemed aprehensive as to let them pass, but was intimidated by "Runcorn"'s threats and let them pass as <a href="/wiki/Corban_Yaxley" title="Corban Yaxley">Yaxley</a> emerged from one of the lifts. Harry, impersonating Runcorn, then told Yaxley that the balding wizard was trying to get the <a href="/wiki/Muggle-born" title="Muggle-born">Muggle-borns</a> out of the Ministry and punched him sending him flying through the air just before he jumped into one of the fireplaces and left the Ministry.<sup id="cite_ref-DH13_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH13-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
