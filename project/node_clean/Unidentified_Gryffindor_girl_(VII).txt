This <b>female Gryffindor student</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch</a> who attended <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> and was <a href="/wiki/Sorting_ceremony" title="Sorting ceremony">sorted</a> into <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor" title="Gryffindor">Gryffindor House</a>. She and other students were watching the first game of <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor" title="Gryffindor">Gryffindor</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Quidditch" title="Quidditch">Quidditch</a> Team with <a href="/wiki/Rubeus_Hagrid" title="Rubeus Hagrid">Rubeus Hagrid</a> in <a href="/wiki/1991" title="1991">1991</a> and celebrated with <a href="/wiki/Dean_Thomas" title="Dean Thomas">Dean</a> and <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ron</a> after the match was over.<sup id="cite_ref-PSF_2-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PSF-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_Gryffindor_girl_(VII)?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_Gryffindor_girl_(VII)?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>This girl started her <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magical</a> education at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> in <a href="/wiki/1991" title="1991">1991</a> and was <a href="/wiki/Sorting_ceremony" title="Sorting ceremony">Sorted</a> into <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor" title="Gryffindor">Gryffindor House</a>. This girl, like the other Gryffindor students, was very happy to go see the first game, and followed <a href="/wiki/Rubeus_Hagrid" title="Rubeus Hagrid">Rubeus Hagrid</a> to the <a href="/wiki/Quidditch" title="Quidditch">Quidditch</a> field.<sup id="cite_ref-PSF_2-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PSF-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<figure class="thumb tleft show-info-icon" style="width: 252px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/b/b2/Girl_-7_with_Rony_and_Dean.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20180720140346" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/b/b2/Girl_-7_with_Rony_and_Dean.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/250?cb=20180720140346" decoding="async" width="250" height="104" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="Girl -7 with Rony and Dean.jpg" data-image-key="Girl_-7_with_Rony_and_Dean.jpg" /></a> 	 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:Girl_-7_with_Rony_and_Dean.jpg" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption">The girl with <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ron</a> and <a href="/wiki/Dean_Thomas" title="Dean Thomas">Dean</a> going to the <a href="/wiki/Quidditch" title="Quidditch">Quidditch</a> field</p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure>
<p>Also in <a href="/wiki/1991" title="1991">1991</a>, she celebrated when the Gryffindor team was victorious in the first game. She and other <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor" title="Gryffindor">Gryffindor</a> students greeted <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry</a> in the middle of the <a href="/wiki/Quidditch" title="Quidditch">Quidditch</a> field.<sup id="cite_ref-PSF_2-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PSF-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
