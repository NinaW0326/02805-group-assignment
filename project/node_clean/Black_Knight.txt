<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Black_Knight?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Black_Knight?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>The Black Knight was one of the original ghosts created by <a href="/wiki/J._K._Rowling" title="J. K. Rowling">J. K. Rowling</a> but did not survive final edits.<sup id="cite_ref-pottermoreghosts_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-pottermoreghosts-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li>Though the Black Knight is never mentioned by name in any of the novels, it is possible that the <a href="/wiki/Knight_with_an_arrow_sticking_out_of_his_forehead" title="Knight with an arrow sticking out of his forehead">knight with an arrow sticking out of his forehead</a> that attended <a href="/wiki/Nicholas_de_Mimsy-Porpington%27s_five-hundredth_Deathday_Party" title="Nicholas de Mimsy-Porpington&#39;s five-hundredth Deathday Party">Nicholas de Mimsy-Porpington's five-hundredth Deathday Party</a> was intended to be this character.</li></ul>
