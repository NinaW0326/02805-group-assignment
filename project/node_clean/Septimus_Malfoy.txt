<b>Septimus Malfoy</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Pure-blood" title="Pure-blood">pure-blood</a> <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> who lived during the late <a href="/wiki/18th_century" title="18th century">18th century</a>. He was influential in the <a href="/wiki/Ministry_of_Magic" title="Ministry of Magic">Ministry of Magic</a>, and many claimed that when he served as an <a href="/wiki/Advisor_to_the_Minister_for_Magic" title="Advisor to the Minister for Magic">advisor</a> the <a href="/wiki/Minister_for_Magic" title="Minister for Magic">Minister for Magic</a> <a href="/wiki/Unctuous_Osbert" title="Unctuous Osbert">Unctuous Osbert</a> was little more than his puppet.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Septimus_Malfoy?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Septimus_Malfoy?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Septimus is a <a href="/wiki/Latin" title="Latin">Latin</a>&#160;word for "seventh." This naming may be an attempt to draw reference to the powerful <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magical</a> properties of the number <a href="/wiki/Seven" title="Seven">seven</a>.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Septimus_Malfoy?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Septimus_Malfoy?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>In <a href="/wiki/1992" title="1992">1992</a>, <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>, speaking about <a href="/wiki/Draco_Malfoy" title="Draco Malfoy">Draco Malfoy</a>, said, <i>"Look at his family... The whole lot of them have been in Slytherin, he's always boasting about it"</i>.<sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> This indicates that Septimus was most likely a <a href="/wiki/Slytherin" title="Slytherin">Slytherin</a> if he attended <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a>, though it cannot be confirmed.</li>
<li>In <a href="/wiki/1997" title="1997">1997</a> a <a href="/wiki/Unidentified_portrait_in_Malfoy_Manor_(II)" title="Unidentified portrait in Malfoy Manor (II)">portrait</a> was hung in <a href="/wiki/Malfoy_Manor" title="Malfoy Manor">Malfoy Manor</a> showing a wizard; Septimus may be that wizard.</li>
<li>Oddly, Septimus is a <a href="/wiki/Septimus_Weasley" title="Septimus Weasley">name</a> that also appears on the <a href="/wiki/Weasley_family" title="Weasley family">Weasley family</a> tree, an almost rival family of the <a href="/wiki/Malfoy_family" title="Malfoy family">Malfoys</a> in later years.</li>
<li>The relationship between Septimus and Unctuous Osbert is similar to that of his descendent <a href="/wiki/Lucius_Malfoy" title="Lucius Malfoy">Lucius Malfoy</a> and <a href="/wiki/Cornelius_Fudge" title="Cornelius Fudge">Cornelius Fudge</a>.</li></ul>
