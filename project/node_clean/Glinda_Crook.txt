<b>Glinda Crook</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch</a> who worked for the <a href="/wiki/Campaign_for_Greater_Freedom_for_Wizards" title="Campaign for Greater Freedom for Wizards">Campaign for Greater Freedom for Wizards</a><sup id="cite_ref-:0_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:0-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>. She spoke on behalf of the organisation in the <i><a href="/wiki/Daily_Prophet" title="Daily Prophet">Daily Prophet</a></i>, stating its opposition to new <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">Ministry of Magic</a> regulations<sup id="cite_ref-:0_2-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:0-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>, which were intended to limit magical activity on <a href="/wiki/Hallowe%27en" title="Hallowe&#39;en">Hallowe'en</a>.
