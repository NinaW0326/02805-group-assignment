<b>Jo King</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Slytherin" title="Slytherin">Slytherin</a> student at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> in the <a href="/wiki/1950s" title="1950s">1950s</a>. She was <a href="/wiki/Quidditch_Captain#Hogwarts_Quidditch_Captains" title="Quidditch Captain">Captain</a> of the <a href="/wiki/Slytherin" title="Slytherin">Slytherin</a> <a href="/wiki/Quidditch" title="Quidditch">Quidditch</a> <a href="/wiki/Slytherin_Quidditch_team" title="Slytherin Quidditch team">team</a> from <a href="/wiki/1952" title="1952">1952</a> to <a href="/wiki/1956" title="1956">1956</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
