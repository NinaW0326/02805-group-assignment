</td></tr></tbody></table><p>The <b>Head Curse-Breaker</b> is a high-ranking position at <a href="/wiki/Gringotts_Wizarding_Bank" title="Gringotts Wizarding Bank">Gringotts Wizarding Bank</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-y4ch1_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-y4ch1-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> denoting the <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch or wizard</a> of the highest degree of seniority over the other <a href="/wiki/Curse-Breakers" class="mw-redirect" title="Curse-Breakers">Curse-Breakers</a>, who presumably is involved in the employment and supervision of the other Curse-Breakers and oversees the operations carried out by other Curse-Breakers. Not merely an administrative position, however, the Head Curse-Breaker also appears to do some curse-breaking of their own.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
