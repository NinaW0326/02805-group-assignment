<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Rionach_O%27Neal?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Rionach_O%27Neal?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_years">Early years</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Rionach_O%27Neal?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Early years">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Rionach_O%27Neal?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Early years"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Rionach O'Neal started attending <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> and was <a href="/wiki/Sorting_ceremony" title="Sorting ceremony">Sorted</a> into <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor" title="Gryffindor">Gryffindor</a> <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Houses" title="Hogwarts Houses">House</a> at unknown date. Rionach was attending the school from at least the <a href="/wiki/1993%E2%80%931994_school_year" title="1993–1994 school year">1993–1994 school year</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-appearances_4-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-appearances-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span id="1994–1995_school_year"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="1994.E2.80.931995_school_year"><a href="/wiki/1994%E2%80%931995_school_year" title="1994–1995 school year">1994–1995 school year</a></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Rionach_O%27Neal?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: 1994–1995 school year">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Rionach_O%27Neal?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: 1994–1995 school year"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Rionach was at least in her second year when delegations from <a href="/wiki/Beauxbatons_Academy_of_Magic" title="Beauxbatons Academy of Magic">Beauxbatons Academy of Magic</a> and <a href="/wiki/Durmstrang_Institute" title="Durmstrang Institute">Durmstrang Institute</a> spent the year at the school for the <a href="/wiki/Triwizard_Tournament" title="Triwizard Tournament">Triwizard Tournament</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-appearances_4-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-appearances-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span id="1995–1996_school_year"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="1995.E2.80.931996_school_year"><a href="/wiki/1995%E2%80%931996_school_year" title="1995–1996 school year">1995–1996 school year</a></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Rionach_O%27Neal?veaction=edit&amp;section=4" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: 1995–1996 school year">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Rionach_O%27Neal?action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: 1995–1996 school year"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>

<p>Rionach was seen passing by <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> and <a href="/wiki/Cho_Chang" title="Cho Chang">Cho Chang</a>, when a commotion broke out as <a href="/wiki/Dolores_Umbridge" title="Dolores Umbridge">Dolores Umbridge</a> attempted to <a href="/wiki/Order_of_Dismissal" title="Order of Dismissal">dismiss</a> <a href="/wiki/Sybill_Trelawney" title="Sybill Trelawney">Sybill Trelawney</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>By this school year, she had entered into a romantic relationship and had kissed the boy in question in the halls of <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Castle" title="Hogwarts Castle">Hogwarts</a> on at least one occasion; however, <a href="/wiki/Professor" title="Professor">Professor</a> Umbridge had <a href="/wiki/Revulsion_Jinx" title="Revulsion Jinx">forced them apart</a> when she saw them.<sup id="cite_ref-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Later_years">Later years</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Rionach_O%27Neal?veaction=edit&amp;section=5" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Later years">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Rionach_O%27Neal?action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Later years"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Rionach attended the school in the <a href="/wiki/1996%E2%80%931997_school_year" title="1996–1997 school year">1996–1997 school year</a> when <a href="/wiki/Albus_Dumbledore" title="Albus Dumbledore">Professor Dumbledore</a> was killed at the hands of <a href="/wiki/Severus_Snape" title="Severus Snape">another teacher</a>. Then <a href="/wiki/1997%E2%80%931998_school_year" title="1997–1998 school year">the next year</a>, she continued to attend the school, despite it having fallen under <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a>' control. It's unknown whether she fought in the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a> or evacuated before the fight began.<sup id="cite_ref-appearances_4-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-appearances-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Physical_appearance">Physical appearance</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Rionach_O%27Neal?veaction=edit&amp;section=6" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Physical appearance">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Rionach_O%27Neal?action=edit&amp;section=6" title="Edit section: Physical appearance"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Rionach had fair skin and a good complexion. She had long dark brown hair, which she kept in two <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/French_braid" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:French braid">French braids</a> during the <a href="/wiki/1995%E2%80%931996_school_year" title="1995–1996 school year">1995–1996 school year</a>. She was tall and willowy.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Rionach_O%27Neal?veaction=edit&amp;section=7" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Rionach_O%27Neal?action=edit&amp;section=7" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Rionach is a variant of the Irish <i>Ríoghnach</i>, which means "queen". O'Neal is a form of the Irish <a href="/wiki/Gaelic" title="Gaelic">Gaelic</a> <i>Ua Néill</i>, meaning "grandson/descendant of Niall".
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Rionach_O%27Neal?veaction=edit&amp;section=8" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Rionach_O%27Neal?action=edit&amp;section=8" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>Rionach O'Neal was portrayed by <a href="/wiki/Marianne_Chase" title="Marianne Chase">Marianne Chase</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_(film_series)" title="Harry Potter (film series)"><i>Harry Potter</i> films</a>. Chase was initially only credited as O'Neal for <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Prisoner_of_Azkaban_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban (film)">Prisoner of Azkaban</a>,</i> with most of her other appearances credited as "Gryffindor Student". However, her Mandy profile was later updated to state she played the same character in her subsequent appearances.<sup id="cite_ref-appearances_4-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-appearances-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li>What Rionach did in every film is unknown, apart from her confirmed appearance in them. Exceptions being <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Prisoner_of_Azkaban_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban (film)">Prisoner of Azkaban</a></i> and <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Order_of_the_Phoenix_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix (film)">Order of the Phoenix</a></i>.
<ul><li>In the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Prisoner_of_Azkaban_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban (film)">film adaptation</a> of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Prisoner_of_Azkaban" title="Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban">Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban</a></i>, Rionach was a <a href="/wiki/Chaser" title="Chaser">Chaser</a> for the <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor_Quidditch_team" title="Gryffindor Quidditch team">Gryffindor Quidditch team</a> during <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor_vs_Hufflepuff_Quidditch_match_(1993)" title="Gryffindor vs Hufflepuff Quidditch match (1993)">the match</a> against <a href="/wiki/Hufflepuff_Quidditch_team" title="Hufflepuff Quidditch team">Hufflepuff</a>. She visited the <a href="/wiki/Hospital_Wing" title="Hospital Wing">Hospital Wing</a> after the match, as <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> and another team member were injured, and was seen at the bedside of the unknown team member.<sup id="cite_ref-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup>
<ul><li>This particular appearance of Rionach's is not <a href="/wiki/Canon" title="Canon">canon</a>, since every player in the team, including the three Chasers (<a href="/wiki/Katie_Bell" title="Katie Bell">Katie Bell</a>, <a href="/wiki/Angelina_Johnson" title="Angelina Johnson">Angelina Johnson</a> and <a href="/wiki/Alicia_Spinnet" title="Alicia Spinnet">Alicia Spinnet</a>,) are known in the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Prisoner_of_Azkaban" title="Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban">book</a>, with Rionach not being one of them.</li></ul></li>
<li>In the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Order_of_the_Phoenix_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix (film)">film adaptation</a> of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Order_of_the_Phoenix" title="Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix">Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix</a></i>, Rionach is recognised in three scenes, including her kissing scene.</li>
<li>Rionach had an unknown but confirmed appearance as a student in <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Deathly_Hallows:_Part_1" title="Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 1">Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 1</a></i> and <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Deathly_Hallows:_Part_2" title="Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 2">Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 2</a></i>, but it's unknown whether she fought in the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a> or evacuated.</li></ul></li></ul>
