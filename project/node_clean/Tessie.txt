<p><b>Tessie</b> was a great-aunt of the <a href="/wiki/Weasley_family" title="Weasley family">children</a> of <a href="/wiki/Arthur_Weasley" title="Arthur Weasley">Arthur</a> and <a href="/wiki/Molly_Weasley" title="Molly Weasley">Molly Weasley</a>. In <a href="/wiki/December" title="December">December</a> of <a href="/wiki/1994" title="1994">1994</a>, <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ron Weasley</a> commented that his <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley%27s_dress_robes" title="Ronald Weasley&#39;s dress robes">dress robes</a> for the <a href="/wiki/Yule_Ball" title="Yule Ball">Yule Ball</a> made him look and smell like her.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> She may have owned <a href="/wiki/Shell_Cottage" title="Shell Cottage">Shell Cottage</a> before <a href="/wiki/William_Weasley" title="William Weasley">Bill Weasley</a> and <a href="/wiki/Fleur_Delacour" title="Fleur Delacour">Fleur Delacour</a>.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Tessie?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Tessie?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<div class="quote">
<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire">"<i>Look at this! Weasley, you weren't thinking of wearing these, were you? I mean — they were very fashionable in about eighteen ninety…</i>"</span></dd>
<dd>&#8212;Draco Malfoy<sup class="noprint"><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Goblet_of_Fire" title="Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire">[src]</a></sup></dd>
<dd></dd></dl></div>
<ul><li>In <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Goblet_of_Fire" title="Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire">Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire</a></i>, <a href="/wiki/Draco_Malfoy" title="Draco Malfoy">Draco Malfoy</a> remarked on the fashionability of&#160;<a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley%27s_dress_robes" title="Ronald Weasley&#39;s dress robes">Ronald Weasley</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley%27s_dress_robes" title="Ronald Weasley&#39;s dress robes">dress robes</a>. If the remark is to be trusted, Tessie could presumably be born in the late <a href="/wiki/19th_century" title="19th century">19th century</a>.</li>
<li>Interestingly, another Weasley relation named <a href="/wiki/Muriel" title="Muriel">Muriel</a> was born in <a href="/wiki/1890" class="mw-redirect" title="1890">1890</a>. It was thought that she was a great-aunt of the Weasley children too and based on the approximate ages of Muriel and Tessie and their similar relation to the Weasley family, they might have been sisters. However,  <i><a href="/wiki/Pottermore" title="Pottermore">Pottermore</a></i> determined that Muriel was actually the great-great aunt of the Weasley children which makes Muriel more likely to be her aunt.</li></ul>
