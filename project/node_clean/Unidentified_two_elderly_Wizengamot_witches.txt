<div class="quote">
<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix">"<i>The members of the Wizengamot were muttering. All eyes were now on Dumbledore. Some looked annoyed, others slightly frightened; two elderly witches in the back row, however, raised their hands and waved in welcome.</i>"</span></dd>
<dd>&#8212;Description<sup class="noprint"><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Order_of_the_Phoenix" title="Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix">[src]</a></sup></dd>
<dd></dd></dl></div>
<p>These <b>two elderly women</b> were <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witches</a> and members of the <a href="/wiki/Wizengamot" title="Wizengamot">Wizengamot</a> in the mid-<a href="/wiki/1990s" title="1990s">1990s</a>. They seemed to be friendly with <a href="/wiki/Albus_Dumbledore" title="Albus Dumbledore">Albus Dumbledore</a>, and were not influenced by the <a href="/wiki/Campaign_to_discredit_Albus_Dumbledore_and_Harry_Potter" title="Campaign to discredit Albus Dumbledore and Harry Potter">Ministry's campaign to discredit Dumbledore and Harry Potter</a>. On <a href="/wiki/12_August" title="12 August">12 August</a>, they were present at <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Disciplinary_hearing_of_Harry_Potter" title="Disciplinary hearing of Harry Potter">disciplinary hearing</a>. When <a href="/wiki/Albus_Dumbledore" title="Albus Dumbledore">Dumbledore</a> arrived at <a href="/wiki/Courtroom_Ten" title="Courtroom Ten">Courtroom Ten</a> as witness for the defence, these two witches waved to him as a welcome.<sup id="cite_ref-OP8_1-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OP8-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
