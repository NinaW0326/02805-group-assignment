<b>Annis Black</b> (fl. <a href="/wiki/1999" title="1999">1999</a>) was a <a href="/wiki/Great_Britain" title="Great Britain">British</a>&#160;<a href="/wiki/Hag" title="Hag">Hag</a> who lived in <a href="/wiki/Annis_Black%27s_cave" title="Annis Black&#39;s cave">a cave</a> in <a href="/wiki/Deadmarsh" title="Deadmarsh">Deadmarsh</a>, <a href="/wiki/England" title="England">England</a> during the late twentieth century. She wrote a letter to the <i><a href="/wiki/Daily_Prophet" title="Daily Prophet">Daily Prophet</a></i> complaining about the paper's portrayal of Hags as <a href="/wiki/Flesh" title="Flesh">flesh</a>-eating monsters.<sup id="cite_ref-DP_1-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DP-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Annis_Black?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Annis_Black?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>The name Annis Black is presumably based on the legendary Hag <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Black_Annis" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Black Annis">Black Annis</a>, who is said to have eaten <a href="/wiki/Human" title="Human">human</a> children, and then hung their skins on the walls of her cave.</li>
<li>Annis Black was not related to the <a href="/wiki/Pure-blood" title="Pure-blood">pure-blood</a> <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizarding</a>&#160;<a href="/wiki/House_of_Black" title="House of Black">House of Black</a>.</li></ul>
