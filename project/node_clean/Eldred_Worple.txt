<p><b>Eldred Worple</b> (fl. <a href="/wiki/1970" title="1970">1970</a>-<a href="/wiki/1997" title="1997">1997</a>) was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> and writer who was once a favourite student of <a href="/wiki/Horace_Slughorn" title="Horace Slughorn">Horace Slughorn</a>. He studied <a href="/wiki/Vampire" title="Vampire">vampires</a>, even becoming friends with one named <a href="/wiki/Sanguini" title="Sanguini">Sanguini</a>, and wrote <i><a href="/wiki/Blood_Brothers:_My_Life_Amongst_the_Vampires" title="Blood Brothers: My Life Amongst the Vampires">Blood Brothers: My Life Amongst the Vampires</a></i>. Worple was described as "small, stout, and bespectacled."<sup id="cite_ref-HBP15_3-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP15-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Eldred_Worple?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Eldred_Worple?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>In <a href="/wiki/1996" title="1996">1996</a>, Worple attended a <a href="/wiki/Slug_Club" title="Slug Club">Slug Club</a> <a href="/wiki/Slug_Club_Christmas_party" title="Slug Club Christmas party">Christmas party</a> at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> and brought Sanguini as a guest. There, Professor Slughorn introduced them to <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>. Worple tried to convince Harry to let him write his biography citing the money to be made, but Harry who was not fond of fame and already had money, declined, and quickly went to speak to his friend <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione Granger</a>. Worple also tried to distract Sanguini from some young women's necks by shoving a pasty in his hand.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP15_3-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP15-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Eldred_Worple?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Eldred_Worple?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>Eldred Worple was portrayed by English actor <a href="/wiki/Paul_Ritter" title="Paul Ritter">Paul Ritter</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Half-Blood_Prince_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince (film)">film adaptation</a> of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Half-Blood_Prince" title="Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince">Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince</a></i>. He looks quite different from his book counterpart, as he is rather tall (rather than stout) and does not wear glasses.<sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup></li></ul>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Eldred_Worple?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Eldred_Worple?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The name "Eldred" is from an English surname which was derived from EALDRÆD. The name itself means "Old Advisor".
</p>
