<b>Hassan Mostafa</b> was an <a href="/wiki/Egypt" title="Egypt">Egyptian</a> <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> and a <a href="/wiki/Quidditch_referee" title="Quidditch referee">Quidditch referee</a> from <a href="/wiki/Egypt" title="Egypt">Egypt</a>. Physically, his appearance was that of a small, skinny <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a>, with a large moustache and a bald head. Mostafa was an acclaimed Chairwizard of the <a href="/wiki/International_Association_of_Quidditch" title="International Association of Quidditch">International Association of Quidditch</a>.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Hassan_Mostafa?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Hassan_Mostafa?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>In <a href="/wiki/1994" title="1994">1994</a>, he served as the referee for the <a href="/wiki/1994_Quidditch_World_Cup" title="1994 Quidditch World Cup">Quidditch World Cup of that year</a> after travelling all the way from <a href="/wiki/Egypt" title="Egypt">Egypt</a>. Halfway through the finals, however, he was entranced by the <a href="/wiki/Veela" title="Veela">Veela</a>, the Bulgarian mascots. After recovering from the shock, he wanted them sent off the pitch.<sup id="cite_ref-GOF8_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF8-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<ul><li>In <a href="/wiki/2000" title="2000">2000</a>, an Egyptian, <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hassan_Moustafa" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Hassan Moustafa">Hassan Moustafa</a>, was elected head of the <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/International_Handball_Federation" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:International Handball Federation">International Handball Federation</a>.</li></ul>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Hassan_Mostafa?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Hassan_Mostafa?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The name "Hassan" means "beautifier" in Arabic. "Mostafa" means "chosen" in Arabic, and it is a common name.
</p>
