The <b>First-Floor Fighter</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Dark_Wizard" title="Dark Wizard">Dark Wizard</a> who fought for the <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a> during the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a> in the <a href="/wiki/First-floor_corridor" title="First-floor corridor">First-floor corridor</a>, and later in the <a href="/wiki/Onslaught_at_the_Viaduct" title="Onslaught at the Viaduct">Onslaught at the Viaduct</a> where he charged the <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Castle" title="Hogwarts Castle">castle</a> with the <a href="/wiki/Giant" title="Giant">giants</a>.

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/First-Floor_Fighter?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/First-Floor_Fighter?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Born sometime in or prior to <a href="/wiki/1981" title="1981">1981</a>, to a <a href="/wiki/Pure-blood" title="Pure-blood">pure-blood</a> or <a href="/wiki/Half-blood" title="Half-blood">half-blood</a> family, the individual was trained somehow in the <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magical</a> arts. Eventually, he decided to join <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a> and the <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a> as one of his loyal servants.
</p><p>On <a href="/wiki/2_May" title="2 May">2 May</a>, <a href="/wiki/1998" title="1998">1998</a>, he was called to arms by the <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a> to participate in <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Voldemort's attempt to capture Hogwarts School</a>. He was seen fighting in the <a href="/wiki/First-floor_corridor" title="First-floor corridor">First-floor corridor</a> and later at the <a href="/wiki/Viaduct" title="Viaduct">Viaduct</a>.
</p><p>During the battle, he was hit with an offensive <a href="/wiki/Spell" title="Spell">spell</a> by <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ronald Weasley</a>, that either killed or incapacitated him.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Physical_appearance">Physical appearance</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/First-Floor_Fighter?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Physical appearance">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/First-Floor_Fighter?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Physical appearance"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>He was a thickset man with wavy black hair, a pale face with a pointy nose, and wore black robes. He wore a silvery glove.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Magical_abilities_and_skills">Magical abilities and skills</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/First-Floor_Fighter?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/First-Floor_Fighter?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li><b><a href="/wiki/Dark_Arts" title="Dark Arts">Dark Arts</a>:</b> To have been accepted into the ranks of the <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a>, he must have been a skilled and accomplished <a href="/wiki/Dark_Wizard" title="Dark Wizard">Dark Wizard</a> and with dark magic. He was most likely capable of casting the <a href="/wiki/Unforgivable_Curses" title="Unforgivable Curses">Unforgivable Curses</a>, along with other <a href="/wiki/Dark_charm" title="Dark charm">dark charms</a> and <a href="/wiki/Spell" title="Spell">spells</a>.&#160;</li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Duelling" title="Duelling">Duelling</a>:</b> In the Battle of Hogwarts, he fought in the <a href="/wiki/First-floor_corridor" title="First-floor corridor">First-floor corridor</a>, and survived to go onto the <a href="/wiki/Onslaught_at_the_Viaduct" title="Onslaught at the Viaduct">Onslaught at the Viaduct</a>, showing him to be a competent duellist with a considerable degree of skill in <a href="/wiki/Martial_magic" title="Martial magic">martial magic</a>.</li></ul>
