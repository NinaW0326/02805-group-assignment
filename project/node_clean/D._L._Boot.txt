<b>D. L. Boot</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> and a student at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> in the late <a href="/wiki/1970s" title="1970s">1970s</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-HPLE_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HPLE-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Boot played <a href="/wiki/Seeker" title="Seeker">Seeker</a> for his or her House <a href="/wiki/Quidditch" title="Quidditch">Quidditch</a> <a href="/wiki/Quidditch_team" title="Quidditch team">team</a>, and won an award for it in <a href="/wiki/1979" title="1979">1979</a>. A fellow Seeker's name, <a href="/wiki/J._E._Prewitt" title="J. E. Prewitt">J. E. Prewitt</a> was placed alongside his or hers.<sup id="cite_ref-HPLE_3-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HPLE-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
