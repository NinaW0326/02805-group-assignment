<b>Lucretia Prewett</b> (née<b> Black</b>) (<a href="/wiki/1925" title="1925">1925</a> – <a href="/wiki/1992" title="1992">1992</a>) was a <a href="/wiki/Pure-blood" title="Pure-blood">pure-blood</a> witch from the pureblooded <a href="/wiki/House_of_Black" title="House of Black">Black family</a> who was married into the <a href="/wiki/Prewett_family" title="Prewett family">Prewett family</a>.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Lucretia_Black?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Lucretia_Black?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>She was born in 1925 as the eldest daughter of <a href="/wiki/Arcturus_Black_III" title="Arcturus Black III">Arcturus Black III</a> and <a href="/wiki/Melania_Macmillan" title="Melania Macmillan">Melania Macmillan</a> and was the sister of <a href="/wiki/Orion_Black" title="Orion Black">Orion</a>. She went to <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a>, where she was in <a href="/wiki/Slytherin" title="Slytherin">Slytherin</a> house.<sup id="cite_ref-house_2-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-house-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>She married <a href="/wiki/Ignatius_Prewett" title="Ignatius Prewett">Ignatius Prewett</a><sup id="cite_ref-BFT_1-18" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-BFT-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>, making her the aunt of <a href="/wiki/Gideon_Prewett" title="Gideon Prewett">Gideon</a>, <a href="/wiki/Fabian_Prewett" title="Fabian Prewett">Fabian</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Molly_Weasley" title="Molly Weasley">Molly Prewett</a> (later <a href="/wiki/Weasley_family" title="Weasley family">Weasley</a>)<sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>. It is unknown if she was close to her relatives by marriage.
</p><p>She was also <a href="/wiki/Sirius_Black" title="Sirius Black">Sirius</a> and <a href="/wiki/Regulus_Black" title="Regulus Black">Regulus Black</a>'s biological aunt and a second cousin of <a href="/wiki/Arthur_Weasley" title="Arthur Weasley">Arthur Weasley</a>, Molly's husband.
</p><p>She died in <a href="/wiki/1992" title="1992">1992</a> and is depicted on the <a href="/wiki/Black_Family_Tapestry" title="Black Family Tapestry">Black family tree tapestry</a>.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Lucretia_Black?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Lucretia_Black?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p><a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Lucretia" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Lucretia">Lucretia</a> was a legendary Roman noblewoman who was raped by the son of the King of Rome. She told her husband and father what had happened to her and demanded they exact vengeance, then committed suicide. This led to the downfall of the Roman monarchy and the establishment of the Roman republic. The name "Lucretia" comes from the Roman family name <i>Lucretius</i>, possibly derived from the Latin <i>lucrum</i>, "profit, wealth."<sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> 
</p><p><a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Lucrezia_Borgia" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Lucrezia Borgia">Lucrezia Borgia</a> was a Renaissance noblewoman born into a family notorious for its ruthlessness and corruption. Personally, Lucrezia was accused of incest, poisoning, and murder, but she was most likely innocent of these crimes.
</p><p><a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/281_Lucretia" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:281 Lucretia">281 Lucretia</a> is an asteroid belonging to the Flora family in the Main Belt. It was discovered by Johann Palisa on <a href="/wiki/31_October" title="31 October">31 October</a> <a href="/wiki/1888" class="mw-redirect" title="1888">1888</a> in Vienna and named after Caroline Herschel, one of the first female astronomers, whose middle name was Lucretia. This fits with the Black family's tradition of astronomical names.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Lucretia_Black?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Lucretia_Black?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li><a href="/wiki/Horace_Slughorn" title="Horace Slughorn">Horace Slughorn</a> said that all the Blacks were in <a href="/wiki/Slytherin" title="Slytherin">Slytherin</a> except Sirius, so it is almost certain that Lucretia, who had Slughorn as her Potions professor from c. <a href="/wiki/1936" title="1936">1936</a>-<a href="/wiki/1943" title="1943">1943</a>, was likewise in Slytherin.</li>
<li>Since Lucretia and her cousin/sister-in-law <a href="/wiki/Walburga_Black" title="Walburga Black">Walburga Black</a> were born on the same year, it is most likely that they were classmates at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts</a>.</li>
<li>Lucretia is depicted on the family tree tapestry very much like <a href="/wiki/Misapinoa_Black" title="Misapinoa Black">Misapinoa Blishwick (née Black)</a>.</li></ul>
