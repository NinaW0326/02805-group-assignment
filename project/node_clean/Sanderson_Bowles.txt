<b>Sanderson Bowles</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> and a member of the <a href="/wiki/St._Troglodous_Order_of_Labours" title="St. Troglodous Order of Labours">St. Troglodous Order of Labours</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-WU_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-WU-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Sanderson_Bowles?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Sanderson_Bowles?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>By at least the <a href="/wiki/2010s" title="2010s">2010s</a>, Bowles became a member of the St. Troglodous Order of Labours. As part of this organisation, he proposed that blended <a href="/wiki/Dragon_dung_Fertiliser" title="Dragon dung Fertiliser">Dragon dung Fertiliser</a> increased the potency of <a href="/wiki/Leaping_Toadstool" title="Leaping Toadstool">Leaping Toadstools</a>. He shared this advice with a member of the <a href="/wiki/Statute_of_Secrecy_Task_Force" title="Statute of Secrecy Task Force">Statute of Secrecy Task Force</a>, who brought it back to the rest of the organisation and used it to great effect when using Leaping Toadstools to brew <a href="/wiki/Baruffio%27s_Brain_Elixir" title="Baruffio&#39;s Brain Elixir">Baruffio's Brain Elixir</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-WU_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-WU-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
