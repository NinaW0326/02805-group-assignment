<p><br />
<b>Dempster Wiggleswade</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> who worked for the <a href="/wiki/Department_of_Magical_Law_Enforcement" title="Department of Magical Law Enforcement">Department of Magical Law Enforcement</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-newsletters_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-newsletters-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>He also wrote for the <i><a href="/wiki/Daily_Prophet" title="Daily Prophet">Daily Prophet</a></i>, using his legal expertise to answer some of the questions submitted to the problem page, which also featured responses from <a href="/wiki/Grizel_Hurtz" title="Grizel Hurtz">Grizel Hurtz</a>, <a href="/wiki/Zamira_Gulch" title="Zamira Gulch">Zamira Gulch</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Helbert_Spleen" title="Helbert Spleen">Helbert Spleen</a>. He strongly advised <a href="/wiki/Buckley_Cooper" title="Buckley Cooper">Buckley Cooper</a> not to take revenge on his brother by turning his children into <a href="/wiki/Vampire" title="Vampire">vampire</a> <a href="/wiki/Ferret" title="Ferret">ferrets</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-newsletters_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-newsletters-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Following the return of <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a>, he answered security questions from <i>Daily Prophet</i> readers.<sup id="cite_ref-hple_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-hple-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
