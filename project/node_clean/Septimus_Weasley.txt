<b>Septimus Weasley</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Pure-blood" title="Pure-blood">pure-blood</a> <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> who married <a href="/wiki/Cedrella_Black" title="Cedrella Black">Cedrella Black</a>. Because he was considered to be a <a href="/wiki/Blood_traitor" title="Blood traitor">blood traitor</a> by the <a href="/wiki/House_of_Black" title="House of Black">Black</a> family, the marriage caused Cedrella's removal from the <a href="/wiki/Black_Family_Tapestry" title="Black Family Tapestry">Black Family tree</a><sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>. The couple had three sons, including <a href="/wiki/Arthur_Weasley" title="Arthur Weasley">Arthur Weasley</a><sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>. He was the father-in-law of <a href="/wiki/Molly_Weasley" title="Molly Weasley">Molly Weasley</a>, paternal grandfather of <a href="/wiki/William_Weasley" title="William Weasley">Bill</a>, <a href="/wiki/Charles_Weasley" title="Charles Weasley">Charlie</a>, <a href="/wiki/Percy_Weasley" title="Percy Weasley">Percy</a>, <a href="/wiki/Fred_Weasley" title="Fred Weasley">Fred</a>, <a href="/wiki/George_Weasley" title="George Weasley">George</a>, <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ron</a> and <a href="/wiki/Ginevra_Weasley" title="Ginevra Weasley">Ginny Weasley</a>, great-grandfather to <a href="/wiki/James_Potter_II" title="James Potter II">James</a>, <a href="/wiki/Albus_Potter" title="Albus Potter">Albus</a> and <a href="/wiki/Lily_L._Potter" title="Lily L. Potter">Lily Potter</a>, <a href="/wiki/Victoire_Weasley" title="Victoire Weasley">Victoire</a>, <a href="/wiki/Dominique_Weasley" title="Dominique Weasley">Dominique</a>, <a href="/wiki/Louis_Weasley" title="Louis Weasley">Louis</a>, <a href="/wiki/Molly_Weasley_II" title="Molly Weasley II">Molly II</a>, <a href="/wiki/Lucy_Weasley" title="Lucy Weasley">Lucy</a>, <a href="/wiki/Fred_Weasley_II" title="Fred Weasley II">Fred</a> and <a href="/wiki/Roxanne_Weasley" title="Roxanne Weasley">Roxanne Weasley</a> and <a href="/wiki/Rose_Granger-Weasley" title="Rose Granger-Weasley">Rose</a> and <a href="/wiki/Hugo_Granger-Weasley" title="Hugo Granger-Weasley">Hugo Granger-Weasley</a>.

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Septimus_Weasley?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Septimus_Weasley?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Septimus is a name of Latin origin. It means seventh.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Septimus_Weasley?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Septimus_Weasley?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>It is possible that Septimus was sorted in <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor" title="Gryffindor">Gryffindor</a> house, as his son and seven of his grandchildren were sorted into Gryffindor.</li>
<li>Septimus is a Roman name meaning "seventh" in Latin.<sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> This is interesting, considering that <a href="/wiki/Seven" title="Seven">seven</a> is the most magically powerful number, and that Septimus Weasley's son Arthur went on to have seven children. The name "Septimus" is usually given to a seventh-born son, so it can be assumed that Septimus Weasley had six older brothers<sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Bilius" title="Bilius">Bilius</a> may be another of Septimus and Cedrella's sons; however this cannot be confirmed, as it is only known that he was the uncle of Ron and his siblings, not whether he was born a <a href="/wiki/Weasley_family" title="Weasley family">Weasley</a> or a <a href="/wiki/Prewett_family" title="Prewett family">Prewett</a>.</li>
<li>Septimus was probably the previous owner of <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ron Weasley</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Wizard%27s_Chess" title="Wizard&#39;s Chess">Wizard Chess</a> set, as Ron's set was described to be a hand-me-down from one of his grandfathers, though it might have probably been from his <a href="/wiki/Molly_Weasley%27s_father" title="Molly Weasley&#39;s father">maternal grandfather</a>.</li>
<li>Ironically there was a <a href="/wiki/Septimus_Malfoy" title="Septimus Malfoy">member</a> of the <a href="/wiki/Malfoy_family" title="Malfoy family">Malfoy family</a> with the name Septimus. Both families would become enemies during the twentieth century.</li></ul>
