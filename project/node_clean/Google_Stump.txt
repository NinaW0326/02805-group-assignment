<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="History">History</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Google_Stump?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: History">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Google_Stump?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: History"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<figure class="thumb tleft show-info-icon" style="width: 102px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/e/ed/Google_Stump.JPG/revision/latest?cb=20101019064123" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/e/ed/Google_Stump.JPG/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/100?cb=20101019064123" decoding="async" width="100" height="110" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="Google Stump.JPG" data-image-key="Google_Stump.JPG" /></a> 	 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:Google_Stump.JPG" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption">His portrait</p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure>
<p>He had some portraits of himself hung in <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Castle" title="Hogwarts Castle">Hogwarts Castle</a>. In <a href="/wiki/1994" title="1994">1994</a> one of them hung in the <a href="/wiki/Tapestry_Corridor" title="Tapestry Corridor">Tapestry Corridor</a> and by <a href="/wiki/1995" title="1995">1995</a>, a <a href="/wiki/Portrait" title="Portrait">portrait</a> was in the <a href="/wiki/Viaduct_Entrance" title="Viaduct Entrance">Viaduct Entrance</a>. Stump's portrait guarded a secret passage from there to the first floor corridor. He never told anyone the password. When confronted by <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>, who asked if he was really doing his job if no one used his shortcut, the portrait told the student the password. The password, <i>Volo futurus unus</i>, meaning in Latin "<i>I want to be alone</i>", brings out the shyness of the <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a>. Later in the school year, he directed <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>, <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ron Weasley</a> and <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione Granger</a> to the portrait of <a href="/wiki/Giffard_Abbott" title="Giffard Abbott">Giffard Abbott</a> to obtain the <a href="/wiki/Password" title="Password">password</a> to access the shortcut behind <a href="/wiki/Percival_Pratt" title="Percival Pratt">Percival Pratt</a>'s portrait.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTPG_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTPG-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Google_Stump?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Google_Stump?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>He may have been related to former <a href="/wiki/Minister_for_Magic" title="Minister for Magic">Minister for Magic</a> <a href="/wiki/Grogan_Stump" title="Grogan Stump">Grogan Stump</a>.</li>
<li>This individual died sometime prior to <a href="/wiki/1991" title="1991">1991</a>, as the portraits hung at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Castle" title="Hogwarts Castle">Hogwarts</a> depict deceased individuals.<sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li>His first name is Google, possibly derived from the popular search engine Google. This is further implied when one of the other portraits describes him as a "man with answers."</li></ul>
