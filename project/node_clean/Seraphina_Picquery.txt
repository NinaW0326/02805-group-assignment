<p><b>Madam President Seraphina Picquery</b> (fl. <a href="/wiki/1903" title="1903">1903</a><sup id="cite_ref-birth_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-birth-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> â€“ <a href="/wiki/1928" title="1928">1928</a><sup id="cite_ref-PMIlvermorny_5-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PMIlvermorny-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>) was an <a href="/wiki/United_States_of_America" title="United States of America">American</a> <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch</a> born in <a href="/wiki/Savannah" title="Savannah">Savannah</a>, <a href="/wiki/Georgia" title="Georgia">Georgia</a>. She served as the <a href="/wiki/President_of_the_Magical_Congress_of_the_United_States_of_America" title="President of the Magical Congress of the United States of America">President</a> of the <a href="/wiki/Magical_Congress_of_the_United_States_of_America" title="Magical Congress of the United States of America">Magical Congress of the United States of America</a><sup id="cite_ref-mugglenet_4-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-mugglenet-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> from <a href="/wiki/1920" title="1920">1920</a>-<a href="/wiki/1928" title="1928">1928</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-PMIlvermorny_5-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PMIlvermorny-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> proving herself to be a strong and capable leader.
</p><p>During her tenure as President, <a href="/wiki/Gellert_Grindelwald" title="Gellert Grindelwald">Gellert Grindelwald</a> tried to incite a war between the magical and non-magical communities. He attempted this by attempting to expose the <a href="/wiki/Wizarding_world" title="Wizarding world">Wizarding world</a>. Also in 1927, Madam Picquery made the <a href="/wiki/Thunderbird" title="Thunderbird">Thunderbird</a> a protected species and later extended the law to include all North American <a href="/wiki/Beast" title="Beast">beasts</a>. She ended her term as President in 1928.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Seraphina_Picquery?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Seraphina_Picquery?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_Life">Early Life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Seraphina_Picquery?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Early Life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Seraphina_Picquery?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Early Life"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Seraphina was born in the city of <a href="/wiki/Savannah" title="Savannah">Savannah</a>, no later than 1903.<sup id="cite_ref-birth_1-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-birth-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> At the age of eleven she started her education at <a href="/wiki/Ilvermorny_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Ilvermorny School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Ilvermorny School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a>. During her sorting ceremony she was offered a place in all four Ilvermorny houses, becoming one of very few students to be so honoured. She chose <a href="/wiki/Horned_Serpent_(House)" title="Horned Serpent (House)">Horned Serpent</a> as her house. During that same evening, she was lead into the school's <a href="/wiki/Wand_selection_hall" title="Wand selection hall">wand selection hall</a> by members of the faculty, wherein she was chosen by a controversial <a href="/wiki/Violetta_Beauvais" title="Violetta Beauvais">Violetta Beauvais</a> <a href="/wiki/Seraphina_Picquery%27s_Wand" class="mw-redirect" title="Seraphina Picquery&#39;s Wand">wand</a>, a kind said to "<i>take to <a href="/wiki/Dark_Arts" title="Dark Arts">Dark magic</a> like a <a href="/wiki/Vampire" title="Vampire">vampire</a> to <a href="/wiki/Blood" title="Blood">blood</a></i>".<sup id="cite_ref-PMIlvermorny_5-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PMIlvermorny-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>A gifted student, she excelled in her schoolwork.<sup id="cite_ref-PMIlvermorny_5-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PMIlvermorny-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_career">Early career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Seraphina_Picquery?veaction=edit&amp;section=4" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Early career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Seraphina_Picquery?action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Early career"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<p>In 1920, Seraphina was elected <a href="/wiki/President_of_the_Magical_Congress_of_the_United_States_of_America" title="President of the Magical Congress of the United States of America">President</a> of the <a href="/wiki/Magical_Congress_of_the_United_States_of_America" title="Magical Congress of the United States of America">Magical Congress of the United States of America</a>.
</p><p>She allowed wizarding consumption of alcohol during her mandate, saying that life as a wizard of the time was hard enough without banning booze. She famously stated to her <a href="/wiki/Chief_of_Staff" title="Chief of Staff">Chief of Staff</a> that "The <a href="/wiki/Gigglewater" title="Gigglewater">Gigglewater</a> is non-negotiable".
</p>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Moving_against_Grindelwald">Moving against Grindelwald</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Seraphina_Picquery?veaction=edit&amp;section=5" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Moving against Grindelwald">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Seraphina_Picquery?action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Moving against Grindelwald"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<figure class="thumb tleft show-info-icon" style="width: 252px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/f/f1/Fantastic-beasts-seraphina_meetings.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20180125165736" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/f/f1/Fantastic-beasts-seraphina_meetings.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/250?cb=20180125165736" decoding="async" width="250" height="104" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="Fantastic-beasts-seraphina meetings.jpg" data-image-key="Fantastic-beasts-seraphina_meetings.jpg" /></a> 	 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:Fantastic-beasts-seraphina_meetings.jpg" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption">Seraphina working to maintain magical order with MACUSA <a href="/wiki/Auror" title="Auror">Aurors</a></p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure>
<p>In December <a href="/wiki/1926" title="1926">1926</a>, Seraphina was working to contain a series of magical disturbances in <a href="/wiki/New_York" title="New York">New York</a>, and concerned over the incidents in Europe tied to <a href="/wiki/Gellert_Grindelwald" title="Gellert Grindelwald">Gellert Grindelwald</a>. When <a href="/wiki/Magizoologist" title="Magizoologist">Magizoologist</a> <a href="/wiki/Newton_Scamander" title="Newton Scamander">Newton Scamander</a> arrived in <a href="/wiki/New_York" title="New York">New York</a>, his creatures escaped from his case causing a massive threat to the <a href="/wiki/International_Statute_of_Wizarding_Secrecy" title="International Statute of Wizarding Secrecy">Statute of Secrecy</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-FBS_2-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-FBS-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>On <a href="/wiki/6_December" title="6 December">6 December</a> <a href="/wiki/1926" title="1926">1926</a>, Picquery held a meeting with <a href="/wiki/Percival_Graves" title="Percival Graves">Percival Graves</a> and a few other workers in the <a href="/wiki/Major_Investigation_Department" title="Major Investigation Department">Major Investigation Department</a> to discuss the disturbances, she stated that she believed Grindelwald to be responsible. <a href="/wiki/Porpentina_Goldstein" title="Porpentina Goldstein">Porpentina Goldstein</a> interrupted the meeting, with Newt and his suitcase. However, when she did not explain herself, Seraphina considered her presence an unnecessary disruption and asked her to leave.<sup id="cite_ref-FBS_2-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-FBS-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>That evening, when the <a href="/wiki/Muggle" class="mw-redirect" title="Muggle">No-Maj</a> <a href="/wiki/Henry_Shaw_Junior" title="Henry Shaw Junior">Henry Shaw Jnr</a> was murdered by an unknown magical force at a <a href="/wiki/Henry_Shaw_Junior%27s_fundraising_dinner" class="mw-redirect" title="Henry Shaw Junior&#39;s fundraising dinner">fundraising dinner</a>, the <a href="/wiki/International_Statute_of_Wizarding_Secrecy" title="International Statute of Wizarding Secrecy">Statute of Secrecy</a> was placed in danger. The <a href="/wiki/International_Confederation_of_Wizards" title="International Confederation of Wizards">International Confederation of Wizards</a> delegation and held a large meeting at MACUSA's <a href="/wiki/Pentagram_Office" title="Pentagram Office">Pentagram Office</a> to discuss the attack, over which Picquery presided. Tina intruded again and explained that Newt had brought a suitcase with creatures and that some of them had escaped.<sup id="cite_ref-FBS_2-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-FBS-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<p>Seraphina was outraged by the revelation and asked to see Newt. Tina knocked on the suitcase and Newt appeared from it along with <a href="/wiki/Jacob_Kowalski" title="Jacob Kowalski">Jacob Kowalski</a>. Seraphina and the witches and wizards attending the meeting were upset to hear that Jacob was a No-Maj. Seraphina then showed them an image of <a href="/wiki/Henry_Shaw_Junior" title="Henry Shaw Junior">Henry Shaw Jnr's</a> body to inquire about what creature could have killed him. Newt then pointed out the unusual injuries of Henry could not be done by any creature, but rather by an <a href="/wiki/Obscurus" title="Obscurus">Obscurus</a>. Picquery had Graves confiscate the suitcase and Newt, Tina, and Jacob were placed in restraints and taken to the interrogation cells.<sup id="cite_ref-FBS_2-10" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-FBS-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>They later escaped with the suitcase, and Seraphina was later reunited with them as they were all seeking the Obscurus of <a href="/wiki/Credence_Barebone" title="Credence Barebone">Credence Barebone</a>, who had wreaked havoc on New York, creating a severe breach of the Statute of Secrecy. She and her Aurors had arrived in the <a href="/wiki/New_York_City_Hall" title="New York City Hall">New York City Hall</a> <a href="/wiki/New_York_City_Subway" title="New York City Subway">subway station</a> in time to find Newt, Tina and Graves trying to talk Credence down. However, her arrival scared Credence who returned to his Obscurus form. Seraphina then commanded the Aurors to destroy Credence.<sup id="cite_ref-FBS_2-11" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-FBS-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>When they had appeared to have destroyed him, Graves grew angry with her and openly questioned the value of the Statute of Secrecy. Seraphina told him that she believed the law protected them from the No-Maj community, but Percival argued that the law protected No-Majs more than it protected them. Graves responded that he refused to bow down any more, and began to attack the Aurors. Newt used his <a href="/wiki/Swooping_Evil" title="Swooping Evil">Swooping Evil</a> to distract him, and unleashed a binding spell. He then <a href="/wiki/Revealing_Charm" title="Revealing Charm">revealed</a> that Percival was actually the <a href="/wiki/Human_Transfiguration" title="Human Transfiguration">disguise</a> of <a href="/wiki/Gellert_Grindelwald" title="Gellert Grindelwald">Gellert Grindelwald</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-FBS_2-12" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-FBS-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>

She lamented that there was no way they could erase the memories of every No-Maj in New York and that the wizarding community was exposed for good. Newt then told her they could use the <a href="/wiki/Swooping_Evil_venom" title="Swooping Evil venom">venom</a> of the Swooping Evil by asking <a href="/wiki/Frank" title="Frank">Frank</a> the <a href="/wiki/Thunderbird" title="Thunderbird">Thunderbird</a> he had brought with him to fly across the city and create a storm with it. The rain <a href="/wiki/Water" title="Water">water</a> became infused with the venom, and successfully erased all the bad memories of every No-Maj New Yorker of having seen <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magic</a>. Seraphina agreed and thanked Newt, noting they were in his debt. However, before she left, she told them that she could not make an exception for Jacob and that he must have his memory erased. They agreed and she gave them a moment to say goodbye.<sup id="cite_ref-FBS_2-13" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-FBS-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>

<p>On the <a href="/wiki/30_May" title="30 May">30 May</a> <a href="/wiki/1927" title="1927">1927</a>, Madam Picquery allowed the <a href="/wiki/International_Confederation_of_Wizards" title="International Confederation of Wizards">International Confederation of Wizards</a> to extradite Grindelwald to <a href="/wiki/Europe" title="Europe">Europe</a> to answer for his crimes committed there, from imprisonment in the MACUSA headquarters in New York. Before his removal from his cell, Picquery informed and warned the <a href="/wiki/Germany" title="Germany">German</a> representative <a href="/wiki/Rudolph_Spielman" title="Rudolph Spielman">Rudolph Spielman</a> of Grindelwald's incredibly manipulative and persuasive ways, and how they had to remove his tongue to prevent him from brainwashing his guards. She also told him that MACUSA would have been more than willing to keep Grindelwald imprisoned in their headquarters. Before Grindelwald was flown off via a <a href="/wiki/Thestral" title="Thestral">Thestral</a> drawn <a href="/wiki/Incarceration_Carriage" title="Incarceration Carriage">Incarceration Carriage</a> into the night sky, she thanked MACUSA employee <a href="/wiki/Abernathy" title="Abernathy">Abernathy</a> after he gave Spielman a box seemingly containing Grindelwald's <a href="/wiki/Elder_Wand" title="Elder Wand">wand</a> (which actually contained the <a href="/wiki/Chupacabra" title="Chupacabra">Chupacabra</a> <a href="/wiki/Antonio" title="Antonio">Antonio</a>), before the carriage set flight. However, unbeknownst to her, Abernathy was a <a href="/wiki/Gellert_Grindelwald%27s_acolytes" title="Gellert Grindelwald&#39;s acolytes">follower</a> of Grindelwald, and that he had switched appearances with Grindelwald using <a href="/wiki/Human_Transfiguration" title="Human Transfiguration">Human Transfiguration</a>, which subsequently allowed Grindelwald to kill all the Aurors in the carriage (with only Spielman only just surviving) and <a href="/wiki/Gellert_Grindelwald%27s_escape" title="Gellert Grindelwald&#39;s escape">escape</a>, where he could continue his sinister campaign across the <a href="/wiki/Wizarding_world" title="Wizarding world">Wizarding world</a>. She was likely enraged when she learned of his successful escape from her headquarters.<sup id="cite_ref-COGS_6-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COGS-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>In late 1927, a year after Mr Scamander's visit to New York, Madam Picquery made the Thunderbird a protected species. She later extended the law to include all <a href="/wiki/North_America" title="North America">North American</a> <a href="/wiki/Beast" title="Beast">beasts</a>. At her request, Newt Scamander agreed not to include these beasts in the first edition of <a href="/wiki/Fantastic_Beasts_and_Where_to_Find_Them" title="Fantastic Beasts and Where to Find Them">his book</a>, which otherwise might encourage wizarding tourists to seek out and disturb them.<sup id="cite_ref-FBR_7-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-FBR-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Seraphina ended her term as President of MACUSA in <a href="/wiki/1928" title="1928">1928</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-PMIlvermorny_5-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PMIlvermorny-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

The TRUTH about Newt Scamander</a>" that Newt left Madam Picquery with a broken heart in 1926, something he later clarified was false.<sup id="cite_ref-FB_8-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-FB-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Physical_appearance">Physical appearance</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Seraphina_Picquery?veaction=edit&amp;section=7" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Physical appearance">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Seraphina_Picquery?action=edit&amp;section=7" title="Edit section: Physical appearance"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Seraphina Picquery had blonde hair with an ornate headdress, who usually wore a dark blue <a href="/wiki/Seraphina_Picquery%27s_dress_robes" title="Seraphina Picquery&#39;s dress robes">dress robe</a> with the <a href="/wiki/Magical_Congress_of_the_United_States_of_America" title="Magical Congress of the United States of America">MACUSA</a> symbol down the front, rings on every finger, and purple nail polish.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Personality_and_traits">Personality and traits</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Seraphina_Picquery?veaction=edit&amp;section=8" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Personality and traits">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Seraphina_Picquery?action=edit&amp;section=8" title="Edit section: Personality and traits"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Haughty, blunt, and domineering, Seraphina Picquery was implacable in nature, and not someone to let anyone put her in her place. This was evidenced by how she refused to be lectured by <a href="/wiki/Heinrich_Eberstadt" title="Heinrich Eberstadt">Heinrich Eberstadt</a>, who had allowed <a href="/wiki/Gellert_Grindelwald" title="Gellert Grindelwald">Gellert Grindelwald</a> to escape him.<sup id="cite_ref-FBS_2-14" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-FBS-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> In addition, Picquery was one of the incredible few <a href="/wiki/Ilvermorny_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Ilvermorny School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Ilvermorny</a> students to ever be offered a place in all four houses by all four carved statue mascots, however Picquery would ultimately select <a href="/wiki/Horned_Serpent" title="Horned Serpent">Horned Serpent</a>, indicating a high-level of scholarly intelligence on her part.<sup id="cite_ref-PM1920s_3-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PM1920s-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> 
</p><p>While not an intimately friendly person, Picquery was very politically astute and possessed a sort of authoritative charisma. Most people were not happy to cross her and she was rarely light-hearted. While she was grateful for <a href="/wiki/Newton_Scamander" title="Newton Scamander">Newt Scamander</a>'s assistance in protecting their secrecy, she did not make any exceptions for him in the case of <a href="/wiki/Memory_Charm" title="Memory Charm">obliviating</a> the <a href="/wiki/Muggle" class="mw-redirect" title="Muggle">No-Maj</a> <a href="/wiki/Jacob_Kowalski" title="Jacob Kowalski">Jacob Kowalski</a>. Despite her often stubbornness, she could also be highly pragmatic and liberal in her political nature, as she decided to declare the <a href="/wiki/Thunderbird" title="Thunderbird">Thunderbird</a> a protected species in <a href="/wiki/1927" title="1927">1927</a> (a law which was eventually extended to all <a href="/wiki/North_America" title="North America">North American</a> <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magical</a> <a href="/wiki/Creature" title="Creature">creatures</a>), showing her ability to change with the times and to pursue progressive political reform when needed, and immense respect for animal life forms.<sup id="cite_ref-PM1920s_3-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PM1920s-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Magical_abilities_and_skills">Magical abilities and skills</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Seraphina_Picquery?veaction=edit&amp;section=9" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Seraphina_Picquery?action=edit&amp;section=9" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li><b><a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">Magical</a> Mastery</b>: Having been elected to the highest office of the <a href="/wiki/Magical_Congress_of_the_United_States_of_America" title="Magical Congress of the United States of America">Magical Congress of the United States of America</a> as <a href="/wiki/President_of_the_Magical_Congress_of_the_United_States_of_America" title="President of the Magical Congress of the United States of America">President</a>, it can be widely assumed that Picquery was a highly accomplished and talented <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch</a>, who was proficient and knowledgeable in most branches of magic.</li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Apparition" title="Apparition">Apparition</a></b>: Like most accomplished adult witches, Madam Picquery was very capable of apparating successfully at will.<sup id="cite_ref-LEGOD_10-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-LEGOD-10">&#91;10&#93;</a></sup></li></ul>
