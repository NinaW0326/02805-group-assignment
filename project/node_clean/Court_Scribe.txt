<p>The <b>Court Scribe</b> was, in <a href="/wiki/Wizengamot" title="Wizengamot">Wizengamot</a> court proceedings, the <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> in charge of producing a verbatim transcription of hearings for record purposes. The Court Scribe seemed also to be the one in charge of courtroom logistics during the course of hearings, such as fetching extra chairs, or letting witnesses enter the courtroom.<sup id="cite_ref-OP8_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OP8-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="History">History</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Court_Scribe?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: History">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Court_Scribe?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: History"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p><a href="/wiki/Percy_Weasley" title="Percy Weasley">Percy Weasley</a> was a Court Scribe for the <a href="/wiki/Wizengamot" title="Wizengamot">Wizengamot</a> on <a href="/wiki/12_August" title="12 August">12 August</a> <a href="/wiki/1995" title="1995">1995</a>. He took notes at <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>'s disciplinary hearing for offences committed under the <a href="/wiki/Decree_for_the_Reasonable_Restriction_of_Underage_Sorcery" title="Decree for the Reasonable Restriction of Underage Sorcery">Decree for the Reasonable Restriction of Underage Sorcery</a> and the <a href="/wiki/International_Statute_of_Wizarding_Secrecy" title="International Statute of Wizarding Secrecy">International Statute of Secrecy</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-OP8_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OP8-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>   
</p>
