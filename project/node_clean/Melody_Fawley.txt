<b>Melody Fawley</b> was the daughter of <a href="/wiki/Grim_Fawley" title="Grim Fawley">Grim Fawley</a> and <a href="/wiki/Penelope_Fawley" title="Penelope Fawley">Penelope Fawley</a>, who were <a href="/wiki/Auror" title="Auror">Aurors</a>. She had a brother, Addison Fawley.
</p><p>In 2015,<sup id="cite_ref-date_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-date-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> her mother Penelope went missing and a subsequent investigation overseen by the council of which <a href="/wiki/Gareth_Greengrass" title="Gareth Greengrass">Gareth Greengrass</a> was a member, was called off. Her father began searching for her, leading <a href="/wiki/Constance_Pickering" title="Constance Pickering">Constance Pickering</a> to suspect he had unleashed the <a href="/wiki/Calamity" title="Calamity">Calamity</a> through the use of the <a href="/wiki/Foundables_Spell" title="Foundables Spell">Foundables Spell</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-WU_4-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-WU-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Melody was said to have inherited her mother Penelope's nose.<sup id="cite_ref-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
