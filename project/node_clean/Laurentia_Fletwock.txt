<b>Laurentia Fletwock</b> (b. <a href="/wiki/1947" title="1947">1947</a>) was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch</a> who was a celebrated breeder and <a href="/wiki/Winged_horse_racing" title="Winged horse racing">racer</a> of <a href="/wiki/Winged_horse" title="Winged horse">winged horses</a>. Presumably because of her involvement in the world of winged horses, she has subsequently campaigned for <a href="/wiki/Broomstick" title="Broomstick">broomstick</a> usage to come under stricter regulations.<sup id="cite_ref-TCG_1-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-TCG-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
