<p>This <b><a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a></b> was a <a href="/wiki/Snatchers" title="Snatchers">Snatcher</a> who served under <a href="/wiki/Fenrir_Greyback" title="Fenrir Greyback">Fenrir Greyback</a> during the <a href="/wiki/Second_Wizarding_War" title="Second Wizarding War">Second Wizarding War</a>. He was part of the group that captured <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>, <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ron Weasley</a> and <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione Granger</a> and took them to <a href="/wiki/Malfoy_Manor" title="Malfoy Manor">Malfoy Manor</a>. <a href="/wiki/Bellatrix_Lestrange" title="Bellatrix Lestrange">Bellatrix Lestrange</a> was shocked when she saw him holding the <a href="/wiki/Godric_Gryffindor%27s_Sword" class="mw-redirect" title="Godric Gryffindor&#39;s Sword">sword</a> of <a href="/wiki/Godric_Gryffindor" title="Godric Gryffindor">Gryffindor</a>, and demanded to know where he had gotten it (believing it to have been in her <a href="/wiki/Lestrange_Vault" title="Lestrange Vault">vault</a> at <a href="/wiki/Gringotts_Wizarding_Bank" title="Gringotts Wizarding Bank">Gringotts</a>). He most unwisely refused her demand to hand it over and was <a href="/wiki/Stunning_Spell" title="Stunning Spell">stunned</a> by the enraged witch.<sup id="cite_ref-DH23_2-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH23-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_Snatcher?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_Snatcher?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>This Snatcher was portrayed by <a href="/wiki/Samuel_Roukin" title="Samuel Roukin">Samuel Roukin</a> in <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Deathly_Hallows:_Part_1" title="Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 1">Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 1</a></i>.</li>
<li>In the film, the Snatcher tells Bellatrix that the sword was in Hermione's bag when they searched her and reckons that it belongs to him now. In response, Bellatrix hits him with a spell which blasts him backwards onto the stairs, killing him. He is also shown to have the Snitch in his possession, but it flutters out of his pocket and returns to Harry when he passes his corpse on the stairs.</li>
<li>In <i><a href="/wiki/LEGO_Harry_Potter:_Years_5-7" title="LEGO Harry Potter: Years 5-7">LEGO Harry Potter: Years 5-7</a></i>, the Snatcher is omitted. Instead, <a href="/wiki/Bellatrix_Lestrange" title="Bellatrix Lestrange">Bellatrix Lestrange</a> finds the <a href="/wiki/Sword_of_Gryffindor" title="Sword of Gryffindor">Sword of Gryffindor</a> in <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger%27s_beaded_handbag" title="Hermione Granger&#39;s beaded handbag">Hermione's handbag</a>.</li></ul>
