<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Description">Description</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Dukuwaqa?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Description">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Dukuwaqa?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Description"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>A shapeshifter, the Dukuwaqa possesses the ability to <a href="/wiki/Transformation" title="Transformation">transform</a> itself from a man into a <a href="/wiki/Shark" title="Shark">shark</a> and back (something impossible for non-<a href="/wiki/Animagus" title="Animagus">Animagus</a> <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizards</a>, as transformation into an animal renders the transformed individual with the mind of that animal, and are thus unable to perform the spell needed to transform back into a human). A Dukuwaqa was the mascot of the <a href="/wiki/Fijian_National_Quidditch_team" title="Fijian National Quidditch team">Fijian National Quidditch Team</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-PM_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PM-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="History">History</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Dukuwaqa?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: History">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Dukuwaqa?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: History"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>For the opening ceremony of the <a href="/wiki/2014_Quidditch_World_Cup" title="2014 Quidditch World Cup">2014 Quidditch World Cup</a>, the Fijian team transported their Dukuwaqa to a magical lake created by the <a href="/wiki/Argentinian_Council_of_Magic" title="Argentinian Council of Magic">Argentinian Council of Magic</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Patagonian_Desert" title="Patagonian Desert">Patagonian Desert</a>. During the ceremony, the Dukuwaqa, upset by being relocated from the warm open ocean to a small tepid lake, attacked the <a href="/wiki/Norway" title="Norway">Norwegian's</a> <a href="/wiki/Selma" title="Selma">Selma</a> it was meant to share the lake with. Attempts by both nations to stop the fighting were hampered by the <a href="/wiki/Brazil" title="Brazil">Brazilian</a> <a href="/wiki/Curupira" title="Curupira">Curupiras</a>, who believed the handlers to be attempting to cause the creatures harm.<sup id="cite_ref-PM_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PM-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Dukuwaqa?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Dukuwaqa?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>The <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Dakuwaqa" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Dakuwaqa">Dakuwaqa</a> is a god from Fijian mythology, a protector spirit to fisherman. Though he is reputed to be capable of taking on any shape, his preferred form is a Fijian male with the head of a shark.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Newton_Scamander" title="Newton Scamander">Newt Scamander</a> did not cover the Dukuwaqa in the fifty-second edition of <a href="/wiki/Fantastic_Beasts_and_Where_to_Find_Them" title="Fantastic Beasts and Where to Find Them">Fantastic Beasts and Where To Find Them</a>.</li></ul>
