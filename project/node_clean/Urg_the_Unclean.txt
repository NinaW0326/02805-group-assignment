<p><b>Urg the Unclean </b>(fl. <a href="/wiki/18th_century" title="18th century">18th century</a>) was a <a href="/wiki/Goblin" title="Goblin">goblin</a> rebel who became notorious for having led the <a href="/wiki/18th_century" title="18th century">18th century</a> <a href="/wiki/Goblin_Rebellions" title="Goblin Rebellions">Goblin Rebellions</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-PAvg_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PAvg-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>At some point in his life he was publicly dunked in a village pond by a group of young <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizards</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-WOMBAT_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-WOMBAT-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> This event precipitated one of the bloody rebellions in which Urg himself was the dissident leader.<sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> For his role in the <a href="/wiki/Goblin_Rebellions" title="Goblin Rebellions">Goblin Rebellions</a>, he was featured on one <a href="/wiki/Chocolate_Frog_Card" title="Chocolate Frog Card">Chocolate Frog Card</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-PAvg_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PAvg-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>When <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ron Weasley</a> sat his <a href="/wiki/Fourth_year" title="Fourth year">fourth year</a> <a href="/wiki/History_of_Magic" title="History of Magic">History of Magic</a> final, he forgot some of the names of the goblin rebels, and thus invented a few as cover, stating that this was easy because "they're all called stuff like <a href="/wiki/Bodrod_the_Bearded" title="Bodrod the Bearded">Bodrod the Bearded</a> and Urg the Unclean."<sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
