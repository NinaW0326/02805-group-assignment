<p><b>Golpalott</b> was (possibly) the <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard or witch</a> who formulated <a href="/wiki/Golpalott%27s_Third_Law" title="Golpalott&#39;s Third Law">Golpalott's Third Law</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP18_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP18-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> Alternatively, it may have been named after him or her by someone else.
</p>
