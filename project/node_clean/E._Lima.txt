<b>E. Lima</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard or witch</a> who competed in <a href="/wiki/Duelling" title="Duelling">duels</a>. The <i><a href="/wiki/Daily_Prophet" title="Daily Prophet">Daily Prophet</a></i> published a special report in <a href="/wiki/1996" title="1996">1996</a> about a duel between E. Lima and <a href="/wiki/M._Mina" title="M. Mina">M. Mina</a> that was the final match of a duelling competition.<sup id="cite_ref-HPLE_1-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HPLE-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
