<p>An <b>Assassin</b> is an individual that is hired to commit a targeted murder, often for political reasons, though they could also serve as hired killers, making them something akin to death-dealing <a href="/wiki/Bounty_hunter" title="Bounty hunter">bounty hunters</a>.
</p>
