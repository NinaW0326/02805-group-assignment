<p>This <b>individual</b> was the <a href="/wiki/Headmaster" title="Headmaster">Headteacher</a> of <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> during the <a href="/wiki/1790s" title="1790s">1790s</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-GF15_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GF15-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> In 1792, the <a href="/wiki/Triwizard_Tournament" title="Triwizard Tournament">Triwizard Tournament</a> was held at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts Castle</a>, and this Headteacher welcomed the <a href="/wiki/Headteacher_of_Beauxbatons_Academy_of_Magic_during_1792" title="Headteacher of Beauxbatons Academy of Magic during 1792">Head of Beauxbatons</a> and <a href="/wiki/Headmaster_of_Durmstrang_Institute_during_1792" title="Headmaster of Durmstrang Institute during 1792">Durmstrang</a> to <a href="/wiki/Scotland" title="Scotland">Scotland</a>. One of the tasks involved catching a <a href="/wiki/Cockatrice" title="Cockatrice">Cockatrice</a>, which went on a rampage and injured the judges, including the Hogwarts Head.<sup id="cite_ref-GF15_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GF15-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> This incident, along with the rising number of deaths, was a contributing factor to the eventual disbandment of the Tournament, though there were a few unsuccessful attempts to revive it.
</p>
