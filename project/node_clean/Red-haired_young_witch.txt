<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Red-haired_young_witch?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Red-haired_young_witch?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Following_Gellert_Grindelwald">Following Gellert Grindelwald</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Red-haired_young_witch?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Following Gellert Grindelwald">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Red-haired_young_witch?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Following Gellert Grindelwald"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<figure class="thumb tleft show-info-icon" style="width: 202px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/7/74/Red_Hair_Witch.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20190326040149" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/7/74/Red_Hair_Witch.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/200?cb=20190326040149" decoding="async" width="200" height="83" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="Red Hair Witch.jpg" data-image-key="Red_Hair_Witch.jpg" /></a> 	 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:Red_Hair_Witch.jpg" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption">Red haired witch standing next <a href="/wiki/Newton_Scamander" title="Newton Scamander">Newt Scamander</a> and <a href="/wiki/Porpentina_Goldstein" title="Porpentina Goldstein">Tina Goldstein</a> on one of Paris streets</p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure>
<p>This witch was born sometime before <a href="/wiki/1927" title="1927">1927</a>&#160;into a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizarding</a> family. In early <a href="/wiki/September" title="September">September</a> of 1927, she witnessed Grindelwald calling upon his followers in <a href="/wiki/Paris" title="Paris">Paris</a>, and was receptive to his call. That same day, she attended <a href="/wiki/Gellert_Grindelwald%27s_Paris_rally" title="Gellert Grindelwald&#39;s Paris rally">Grindelwald's rally</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Lestrange_Mausoleum" title="Lestrange Mausoleum">Lestrange Mausoleum</a> on the <a href="/wiki/Cimeti%C3%A8re_du_P%C3%A8re-Lachaise" title="Cimetière du Père-Lachaise">Père-Lachaise Cemetery</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-COGS_1-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COGS-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>When <a href="/wiki/Auror" title="Auror">Aurors</a> infiltrated the rally, she turned to attack <a href="/wiki/Cassius_Bell" title="Cassius Bell">an Auror</a> that stood behind her after becoming enraged at the Aurors' presence. This outburst of anger resulted in not only the Auror in question raising his <a href="/wiki/Wand" title="Wand">wand</a> to defend himself, but also her own demise with the <a href="/wiki/Killing_Curse" title="Killing Curse">Killing Curse</a>&#160;which the same Auror cast; which in turn caused Grindelwald to shout out in fury of her slaying.<sup id="cite_ref-COGS_1-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COGS-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Legacy">Legacy</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Red-haired_young_witch?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Legacy">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Red-haired_young_witch?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Legacy"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<figure class="thumb tleft show-info-icon" style="width: 202px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/2/23/Red_Hair_Witch_2.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20190326040615" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/2/23/Red_Hair_Witch_2.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/200?cb=20190326040615" decoding="async" width="200" height="83" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="Red Hair Witch 2.jpg" data-image-key="Red_Hair_Witch_2.jpg" /></a> 	 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:Red_Hair_Witch_2.jpg" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption">Gellert Grindelwald kneeling beside a dead body of red haired witch</p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure>
<p>Following her death, Gellert Grindelwald requested his other followers to take her corpse to her family, commending her as a "brave warrior". It can be presumed that this was done in accordance with Grindelwald's request.<sup id="cite_ref-COGS_1-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COGS-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Personality_and_traits">Personality and traits</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Red-haired_young_witch?veaction=edit&amp;section=4" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Personality and traits">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Red-haired_young_witch?action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Personality and traits"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Nothing is known about this witch's personality, apart from the fact that she became enamoured of Grindelwald's ideals enough to give her the courage to attack his enemies, though this bravery turned out to be foolishness leading to her abrupt demise.
</p>
