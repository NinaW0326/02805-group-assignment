<p><b>Amorette Deneuve</b>&#160;was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch</a> and the foremost expert on <a href="/wiki/Love_Potion" class="mw-redirect" title="Love Potion">love potions</a> and <a href="/wiki/Love#Love_and_magic" title="Love">spells</a>; she had many published works about <a href="/wiki/Love" title="Love">Love</a>, among them, <i><a href="/wiki/First_Love,_First_Loss" title="First Love, First Loss">First Love, First Loss</a></i>.<sup id="cite_ref-WU_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-WU-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Even though he was originally of the opinion that Deneuve's work was "<i>sentimental blather</i>", <a href="/wiki/Grim_Fawley" title="Grim Fawley">Grim Fawley</a> contacted Amorette Deneuve following the disappearance of his wife, <a href="/wiki/Penelope_Fawley" title="Penelope Fawley">Penelope</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-WU_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-WU-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
