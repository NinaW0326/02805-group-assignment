<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Sacharissa_Tugwood?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Sacharissa_Tugwood?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_life">Early life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Sacharissa_Tugwood?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Early life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Sacharissa_Tugwood?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Early life"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Tugwood was born in <a href="/wiki/1874" class="mw-redirect" title="1874">1874</a>. She presumably attended <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> (c. 1885- 1893) where she would have excelled in <a href="/wiki/Potions_(class)" title="Potions (class)">Potions</a>.
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Career_as_Potioneer">Career as Potioneer</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Sacharissa_Tugwood?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Career as Potioneer">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Sacharissa_Tugwood?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Career as Potioneer"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>In her adulthood, Tugwood would develop many <a href="/wiki/Beautification_Potion" title="Beautification Potion">cosmetic potions</a>. She became famous for being the first <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch</a> to use such a potion. Sacharissa also discovered the pimple-curing properties of <a href="/wiki/Bubotuber_pus" title="Bubotuber pus">Bubotuber pus</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-PSG_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PSG-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-COSG_2-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COSG-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-POAG_3-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-POAG-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Death_and_legacy">Death and legacy</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Sacharissa_Tugwood?veaction=edit&amp;section=4" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Death and legacy">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Sacharissa_Tugwood?action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Death and legacy"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Tugwood would eventually die in <a href="/wiki/1966" title="1966">1966</a>, aged ninety-two. Her gravestone credited her to have made of the world a "<i>more beautiful place</i>". She was also featured on a <a href="/wiki/Chocolate_Frog_Card" title="Chocolate Frog Card">Chocolate Frog Card</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-PSG_1-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PSG-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-COSG_2-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COSG-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-POAG_3-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-POAG-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Magical_abilities_and_skills">Magical abilities and skills</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Sacharissa_Tugwood?veaction=edit&amp;section=5" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Sacharissa_Tugwood?action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li><b><a href="/wiki/Potion" title="Potion">Potions</a></b>: Tugwood was a highly accomplished and talented professional <a href="/wiki/Potioneer" title="Potioneer">Potioneer</a>, having successfully invented numerous <a href="/wiki/Beautification_Potion" title="Beautification Potion">Beautification Potions</a>, cosmetic concoctions which enhance the beauty of the drinker, an achievement she was immortalised on a <a href="/wiki/Chocolate_Frog_Card" title="Chocolate Frog Card">Chocolate Frog Card</a> for, showing her proficiency in this branch of both <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magic</a> and <a href="/wiki/Science" title="Science">science</a>.</li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Herbology" title="Herbology">Herbology</a></b>: Tugwood also discovered the pimple curing properties of <a href="/wiki/Bubotuber_pus" title="Bubotuber pus">Bubotuber pus</a>, a natural substance extracted from the magical <a href="/wiki/Bubotuber" title="Bubotuber">Bubotuber</a> plant, an achievement in the field of Herbology, meaning she was knowledgeable of magical plants and their usages.</li></ul>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Sacharissa_Tugwood?veaction=edit&amp;section=6" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Sacharissa_Tugwood?action=edit&amp;section=6" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The name Sacharissa may be related to the verb <a target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener" class="external text" href="http://dictionary.reference.com/browse/saccharise">saccharize</a> which means to convert into sugar.
</p>
