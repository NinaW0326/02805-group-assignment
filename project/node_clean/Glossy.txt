<b>Madame Glossy</b> was presumably the <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch</a> who gave her name to <a href="/wiki/Madame_Glossy%27s_Silver_Polish" title="Madame Glossy&#39;s Silver Polish">Madame Glossy's Silver Polish</a> and <a href="/wiki/Madame_Glossy%27s_Self-Polishing_Shoe_Polish" title="Madame Glossy&#39;s Self-Polishing Shoe Polish">Madame Glossy's Self-Polishing Shoe Polish</a>, presumably being the owner or operator of <a href="/wiki/Madame_Glossy%27s_Finest_Cleaning_Products_Ltd." title="Madame Glossy&#39;s Finest Cleaning Products Ltd.">Madame Glossy's Finest Cleaning Products Ltd.</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-POAF_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-POAF-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-GOFF_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOFF-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> It is unknown whether she was the inventor or simply the manufacturer of these <a href="/wiki/Potion" title="Potion">products</a>.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Glossy?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Glossy?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The name Glossy comes from the adjective glossy, meaning "having a smooth, silklike, reflective surface".<sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> This suggests a rather effective silver polish.
</p>
