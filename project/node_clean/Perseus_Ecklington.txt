<b>Perseus Ecklington</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> who, in his youth, courted <a href="/wiki/Daisy_Twemlove" title="Daisy Twemlove">Daisy Twemlove</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-BPsleepingpotion_1-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-BPsleepingpotion-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>On the eve of Ecklington's wedding to Daisy Twemlove, <a href="/wiki/Norville_Bezerko" title="Norville Bezerko">Norville Bezerko</a>, his love rival, slipped a powerful <a href="/wiki/Sleeping_Potion" title="Sleeping Potion">Sleeping Potion</a> into his <a href="/wiki/Butterbeer" title="Butterbeer">butterbeer</a> mug, sending him into a deep death-like sleep. Bezerko prompty presented himself at Daisy's house, telling her that Ecklington had died of panic at the prospect of marrying her. An unsuspecting Daisy accepted a proposal of marriage from Bezerko the moment Ecklington was buried.<sup id="cite_ref-BPsleepingpotion_1-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-BPsleepingpotion-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Ecklington was put in a coffin, and, just as <a href="/wiki/Funeral_of_Perseus_Ecklington" title="Funeral of Perseus Ecklington">he was being lowered into the ground</a>, he woke up, the effects of the <a href="/wiki/Sleeping_Potion" title="Sleeping Potion">Sleeping Potion</a> gone. Ecklington soon realised Bezerko's scheme, and a brawl ensued. Bezerko was left with an <a href="/wiki/Elephant" title="Elephant">elephant</a>'s trunk and six ears, thus hampering his chances of finding another bride, and Daisy Twemlove was reunited with Perseus Ecklington.<sup id="cite_ref-BPsleepingpotion_1-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-BPsleepingpotion-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
