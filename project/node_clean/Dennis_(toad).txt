<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Dennis_(toad)?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Dennis_(toad)?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>During the <a href="/wiki/1986%E2%80%931987_school_year" title="1986–1987 school year">1986–1987 school year</a>, Dennis was present when <a href="/wiki/Jacob%27s_sibling" title="Jacob&#39;s sibling">Jacob's sibling</a> arrived to the <a href="/wiki/Classroom_1B" title="Classroom 1B">Transfiguration Classroom</a> with a friend, trying to talk to <a href="/wiki/Tulip_Karasu" title="Tulip Karasu">Tulip Karasu</a> for the first time. Tulip claimed to be busy, and told the newcomers to instead talk to Dennis, who had a <a href="/wiki/Dungbomb" title="Dungbomb">Dungbomb</a> strapped on its back. This prompted them to work on defusing the Dungbomb on Dennis before Tulip was ready to talk.<sup id="cite_ref-HM35_1-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HM35-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>After an announcement of a <a href="/wiki/Frog_Choir" title="Frog Choir">Frog Choir</a> audition made by <a href="/wiki/Filius_Flitwick" title="Filius Flitwick">Professor Flitwick</a>, Tulip Karasu met with Jacob's sibling and expressed Dennis' interest in joining the Frog Choir. Due to Tulip herself being a terrible singer, she wanted Jacob's sibling to take Dennis with him to the Frog Choir audition. Jacob's sibling agreed to do so, and bonded with Dennis over several events, one of which was playing tricks on <a href="/wiki/Irma_Pince" title="Irma Pince">Madam Pince</a>. Jacob's sibling then took Dennis to practise with the <a href="/wiki/Weird_Sisters" title="Weird Sisters">Weird Sisters</a> and sing in front of <a href="/wiki/Severus_Snape" title="Severus Snape">Professor Snape</a> to test their nerves.<sup id="cite_ref-HM3F_2-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HM3F-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
