<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_Muggle_woman_about_the_train_to_Kettering?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_Muggle_woman_about_the_train_to_Kettering?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>On <a href="/wiki/September" title="September">1 September</a>, <a href="/wiki/1992" title="1992">1992</a>, this woman stood near <a href="/wiki/Platform_Nine" title="Platform Nine">Platform Nine</a> in <a href="/wiki/King%27s_Cross_Station" title="King&#39;s Cross Station">King's Cross Station</a> in <a href="/wiki/London" title="London">London</a>, looking for the train going to <a href="/wiki/Kettering" title="Kettering">Kettering</a>, but found a guide-board that showed it. She also glimpsed <a href="/wiki/Dobby" title="Dobby">Dobby</a> hiding in the shadows after he <a href="/wiki/Blocked_Barrier" title="Blocked Barrier">blocked the barrier</a> to <a href="/wiki/Platform_Nine_and_Three-Quarters" title="Platform Nine and Three-Quarters">Platform Nine and Three-Quarters</a>, unknowingly exposing herself to <a href="/wiki/Wizarding_world" title="Wizarding world">wizarding world</a>, describing him as a creepy "part baby and part <a href="/wiki/Bat" title="Bat">bat</a>" <a href="/wiki/House-elf" title="House-elf">creature</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-COSG_1-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COSG-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_Muggle_woman_about_the_train_to_Kettering?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_Muggle_woman_about_the_train_to_Kettering?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The woman's description of Dobby may be a reference to "Bat Boy" a fictional character created by the American tabloid magazine The <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Weekly_World_News" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Weekly World News">Weekly World News</a>. Bat Boy also first appeared in 1992.  
</p>
