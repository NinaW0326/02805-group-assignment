<b>Irving Cram</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Slytherin" title="Slytherin">Slytherin</a> student at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> in the <a href="/wiki/1990s" title="1990s">1990s</a>. He stood guard over places that were off limits during the <a href="/wiki/1996-1997_school_year" class="mw-redirect" title="1996-1997 school year">1996-1997 school year</a>.
</p>
