The <b>2-Headed Baby</b> was a name given to a set of ischiopagous&#160;conjoined twins. They were part of the <a href="/wiki/Circus_Arcanus" title="Circus Arcanus">Circus Arcanus</a>, a travelling <a href="/wiki/Wizarding_world" title="Wizarding world">wizards'</a> freak show, and visited the <a href="/wiki/United_States_of_America" title="United States of America">United States</a> with it for the first in <a href="/wiki/November" title="November">November</a>-<a href="/wiki/December" title="December">December</a> <a href="/wiki/1926" title="1926">1926</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-poster_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-poster-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<ul><li>Their performance in a wizarding circus may suggest that 2-headed wizards had special magical abilities that one-headed wizards did not have, or their inclusion may be based on just their unusual physique.</li>
<li>The 2 headed Baby is an example of conjoined twins.</li></ul>
