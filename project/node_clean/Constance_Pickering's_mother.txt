This <b><a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch</a></b> was the mother of <a href="/wiki/Constance_Pickering" title="Constance Pickering">Constance Pickering</a>. Until the <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">Ministry of Magic</a> sent her a notice about permits and licencing, she used to make and sell batches of “special” <a href="/wiki/Pumpkin_juice" class="mw-redirect" title="Pumpkin juice">pumpkin juice</a> for grown-ups only.<sup id="cite_ref-WU_1-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-WU-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>During <a href="/wiki/Christmas" title="Christmas">Christmas</a>, she and her <a href="/wiki/Constance_Pickering%27s_sister" title="Constance Pickering&#39;s sister">daughter</a> pulled <a href="/wiki/Christmas_cracker" title="Christmas cracker">crackers</a> together. The pop always made little Constance squeal with delight. The family were never able to have a tree at they house, because Constance's mum said she was “allergic” to decorating. Constance loved looking in their neighbours windows and seeing their trees, all bright and shiny.<sup id="cite_ref-WU_1-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-WU-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Before the start of Constance's <a href="/wiki/First_year" title="First year">first year</a> at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts</a>, her mother wouldn't buy her a new cauldron. She gave Constance <a href="/wiki/Constance_Pickering%27s_brother_(II)" title="Constance Pickering&#39;s brother (II)">her brother</a>'s old, used one and bought him a new one.<sup id="cite_ref-FirstYearAtHogwarts_2-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-FirstYearAtHogwarts-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>In <a href="/wiki/2019" title="2019">2019</a>, the <a href="/wiki/Pickering_family" title="Pickering family">Pickering family</a> had Christmas at the new house of Constance's sister, but they did not send Constance any details or address. As a result, Constance was invited to a Christmas dinner at the <a href="/wiki/Potter_family" title="Potter family">Potter family</a>'s house instead.<sup id="cite_ref-WU_1-10" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-WU-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
