<b>Calchas</b> was an Ancient <a href="/wiki/Greece" title="Greece">Greek</a> <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> and a <a href="/wiki/Seer" title="Seer">Seer</a> who was bested by <a href="/wiki/Mopsus_(antiquity)" title="Mopsus (antiquity)">Mopsus</a> in a contest over who was the more powerful.<sup id="cite_ref-COSG_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COSG-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Calchas?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Calchas?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>Calchas was a seer featured in Homer's <i>Iliad</i>, famous for correctly predicting many events of the Trojan War. Upon being bested in a contest of soothsaying by Mopsus, he killed himself in shame.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup></li></ul>
