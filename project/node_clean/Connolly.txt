<b>Connolly</b> was an <a href="/wiki/Ireland" title="Ireland">Irish</a> <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> who played as a <a href="/wiki/Beater" title="Beater">Beater</a> for the <a href="/wiki/Irish_National_Quidditch_team" title="Irish National Quidditch team">Irish National Quidditch team</a> during the <a href="/wiki/1994" title="1994">1994</a> <a href="/wiki/Quidditch_World_Cup" title="Quidditch World Cup">Quidditch World Cup</a>. The <a href="/wiki/Broomstick" title="Broomstick">Broomstick</a> he rode was a <a href="/wiki/Firebolt" title="Firebolt">Firebolt</a> which had been ordered from <a href="/wiki/Quality_Quidditch_Supplies" title="Quality Quidditch Supplies">Quality Quidditch Supplies</a>. He was helping support <a href="/wiki/Aidan_Lynch" title="Aidan Lynch">Aidan Lynch</a> when being presented with the Cup in the top box.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
