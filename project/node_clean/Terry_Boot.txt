<p><b>Terry Boot</b> (b. <a href="/wiki/1979" title="1979">1979</a> or <a href="/wiki/1980" title="1980">1980</a>) was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> and <a href="/wiki/Ravenclaw" title="Ravenclaw">Ravenclaw</a> student at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> in <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>'s year.<sup id="cite_ref-PS7_5-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PS7-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> He became friends with housemates <a href="/wiki/Michael_Corner" title="Michael Corner">Michael Corner</a> and <a href="/wiki/Anthony_Goldstein" title="Anthony Goldstein">Anthony Goldstein</a>. In his <a href="/wiki/Fifth_year" title="Fifth year">fifth year</a>, Terry joined <a href="/wiki/Dumbledore%27s_Army" title="Dumbledore&#39;s Army">Dumbledore's Army</a>, an organisation taught and led by Harry Potter.
</p><p>On <a href="/wiki/2_May" title="2 May">2 May</a>, <a href="/wiki/1998" title="1998">1998</a> he fought in the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a>. It can be assumed that Terry survived the final battle, as he was not mentioned among the <a href="/wiki/Fallen_Fifty" title="Fallen Fifty">Fallen Fifty</a>.
</p>

<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_life">Early life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Terry_Boot?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Early life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Terry_Boot?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Early life"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Terry was born in c. 1979-1980 in <a href="/wiki/Great_Britain" title="Great Britain">Great Britain</a> or <a href="/wiki/Ireland" title="Ireland">Ireland</a>, to at least one <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">magical</a> parent. Terry could likely be a descendant of <a href="/wiki/Webster_Boot" title="Webster Boot">Webster Boot</a>, the adopted son of Ilvermorny founders <a href="/wiki/James_Steward" title="James Steward">James Steward</a> (a No-Maj, or Muggle) and <a href="/wiki/Isolt_Sayre" title="Isolt Sayre">Isolt Sayre</a> (a <a href="/wiki/Pure-blood" title="Pure-blood">Pure-blood</a> and descendant of <a href="/wiki/Salazar_Slytherin" title="Salazar Slytherin">Salazar Slytherin</a>).<sup id="cite_ref-Ilvermorny_8-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Ilvermorny-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<p>In <a href="/wiki/1991" title="1991">1991</a>, Terry started his education at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a>. Upon his arrival during the Sorting Ceremony, the <a href="/wiki/Sorting_Hat" title="Sorting Hat">Sorting Hat</a> placed Terry in <a href="/wiki/Ravenclaw" title="Ravenclaw">Ravenclaw House</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-PS7_5-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PS7-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> Terry was sorted into Ravenclaw with students <a href="/wiki/Padma_Patil" title="Padma Patil">Padma Patil</a>, <a href="/wiki/Michael_Corner" title="Michael Corner">Michael Corner</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Anthony_Goldstein" title="Anthony Goldstein">Anthony Goldstein</a>, with whom he became friends.
</p>

<p>In his <a href="/wiki/Second_year" title="Second year">second year</a> at Hogwarts, the school was under threat of closure after the <a href="/wiki/Chamber_of_Secrets" title="Chamber of Secrets">Chamber of Secrets</a> had been opened. As a precaution to protect students from any possible danger, a <a href="/wiki/Duelling_Club" title="Duelling Club">Duelling Club</a> started up, instructed by Professor <a href="/wiki/Gilderoy_Lockhart" title="Gilderoy Lockhart">Gilderoy Lockhart</a>. During one practise, chaos broke out and Lockhart had to tend to several students. Terry had received an injury and Lockhart told him to pinch his wound hard to stop the bleeding.<sup id="cite_ref-COS11_6-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COS11-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup> In his fourth year, Terry visited Professor Dumbledore's office for unknown reasons and took interest in the <a href="/wiki/Sword_of_Gryffindor" title="Sword of Gryffindor">Sword of Gryffindor</a>, with one of the <a href="/wiki/Portrait" title="Portrait">portraits</a> informing him that Harry Potter had used it to kill a <a href="/wiki/Basilisk" title="Basilisk">Basilisk</a> in the Chamber of Secrets.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP16_7-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP16-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<p>In Terry's fifth year of education, <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> was put under the eyes of the <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">Ministry of Magic</a>. <a href="/wiki/Dolores_Umbridge" title="Dolores Umbridge">Dolores Jane Umbridge</a> was stationed at Hogwarts as a spy due to events that took place the previous year, in addition to being Professor of <a href="/wiki/Defence_Against_the_Dark_Arts" title="Defence Against the Dark Arts">Defence Against the Dark Arts</a>. Umbridge caused an uproar amongst the students since she refused to teach defensive spells, despite it being said that Lord Voldemort was at large once again.
</p><p><a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione Granger</a> felt she ought to do something about the lack of a proper defence education. Hermione then founded an organisation called Dumbledore's Army, where students learned how to defend themselves and fight in combat. This organisation was taught and led by <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>, who has had numerous experience fighting off <a href="/wiki/Dark_Wizard" title="Dark Wizard">Dark Wizards</a>. Terry attended the first meeting of the D.A., which took place in the <a href="/wiki/Hog%27s_Head" class="mw-redirect" title="Hog&#39;s Head">Hog's Head</a>. When people began to mention Harry's spectacular feats, Terry mentioned that Harry had slayed a Basilisk with the Sword of Gryffindor in Dumbledore's office. This accomplishment amazed most of the students present, with only a few exceptions.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP16_7-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP16-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Magical_abilities_and_skills">Magical abilities and skills</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Terry_Boot?veaction=edit&amp;section=9" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Terry_Boot?action=edit&amp;section=9" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li><b><a href="/wiki/Potion" title="Potion">Potions</a></b>: Terry was very skilled at potion-making since he was one of four <a href="/wiki/Ravenclaw" title="Ravenclaw">Ravenclaws</a> in 1996 to advance to N.E.W.T.-level.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP9_11-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP9-11">&#91;11&#93;</a></sup> He must have earned either an '<a href="/wiki/Outstanding" title="Outstanding">Outstanding</a>' or an '<a href="/wiki/Exceeds_Expectations" title="Exceeds Expectations">Exceeds Expectations</a>' on his <a href="/wiki/Ordinary_Wizarding_Level" title="Ordinary Wizarding Level">O.W.L.</a> exam in order for him to progress to such an advanced <a href="/wiki/Potions_(class)" title="Potions (class)">class</a>.</li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Defence_Against_the_Dark_Arts" title="Defence Against the Dark Arts">Defence Against the Dark Arts</a></b>: Terry was a devoted member of the <a href="/wiki/Dumbledore%27s_Army" title="Dumbledore&#39;s Army">D.A.</a>, so he must have mastered numerous offensive and defensive spells under the masterful instruction of <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP18_13-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP18-13">&#91;13&#93;</a></sup></li>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Terry_Boot?veaction=edit&amp;section=10" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Terry_Boot?action=edit&amp;section=10" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>"Terry" is a form of <i>Terence</i>, which means "<i>tender, or gracious</i>".</li>
<li>"Boot" is a surname referring to a place called Boot, near <i>Raven</i>glass in Cumbria.</li></ul>
