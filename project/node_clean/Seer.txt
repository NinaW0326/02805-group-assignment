<p>A <b>Seer</b> is a gifted <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard or witch</a> who has the ability to see into the future with their <a href="/wiki/Inner_Eye" title="Inner Eye">Inner Eye</a>. Seers predict <a href="/wiki/Prophecy" title="Prophecy">prophecies</a>, which are then recorded and stored in the <a href="/wiki/Hall_of_Prophecy" title="Hall of Prophecy">Hall of Prophecy</a> in the <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">Ministry of Magic</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Department_of_Mysteries" title="Department of Mysteries">Department of Mysteries</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP34_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP34-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-OOTP35_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP35-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> According to <a href="/wiki/Minerva_McGonagall" title="Minerva McGonagall">Minerva McGonagall</a>, true Seers are extremely rare, and she had doubt that <a href="/wiki/Sybill_Trelawney" title="Sybill Trelawney">Sybill Trelawney</a> can be considered as one despite her sporadically accurate predictions.<sup id="cite_ref-POA6_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-POA6-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-POA16_4-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-POA16-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/Albus_Dumbledore" title="Albus Dumbledore">Albus Dumbledore</a> once told <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> that seeing into the future is incredibly difficult because of the complexity of every single action and their consequences.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP37_5-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP37-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p><a href="/wiki/Centaur" title="Centaur">Centaurs</a> are Seers with their own brand of <a href="/wiki/Divination" title="Divination">Divination</a>, which differs from that of humans.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP27_6-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP27-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
