<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Blenheim_Stalk?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Blenheim_Stalk?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_life">Early life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Blenheim_Stalk?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Early life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Blenheim_Stalk?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Early life"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Blenheim was born in <a href="/wiki/1920" title="1920">1920</a>. He became an expert on <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggles</a> and authored many books on the subject of them, including <i><a href="/wiki/Muggles_Who_Notice" title="Muggles Who Notice">Muggles Who Notice</a></i>, a book published in <a href="/wiki/1972" title="1972">1972</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-COSG_1-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COSG-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> The book references the <a href="/wiki/Ilfracombe_Incident" title="Ilfracombe Incident">Ilfracombe Incident</a> and the story of <a href="/wiki/Dirk" title="Dirk">"Dodgy" Dirk</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-FB_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-FB-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>By the 1990s, he was featured on a famous Wizard Card and was featured wearing a <a href="/wiki/Blenheim_Stalk%27s_pocket_watch" title="Blenheim Stalk&#39;s pocket watch">golden pocket watch</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-COSG_1-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COSG-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>He was awarded recognition for his noteworthy and valuable research into <a href="/wiki/Muggle_Studies" title="Muggle Studies">Muggle Studies</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Famous_Witches_and_Wizards_Cards" title="Famous Witches and Wizards Cards">Famous Witches and Wizards Cards</a> set, on a silver <a href="/wiki/Chocolate_Frog_Card" title="Chocolate Frog Card">Chocolate Frog Card</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-COSG_1-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COSG-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Magical_abilities_and_skills">Magical abilities and skills</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Blenheim_Stalk?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Blenheim_Stalk?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li><b><a href="/wiki/Muggle_Studies" title="Muggle Studies">Muggle Studies</a></b>: Stalk was an expert on <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggles</a> and their ways of living, such as how Muggles survive without <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magic</a> and their use of <a href="/wiki/Science" title="Science">science</a> and technology, as well as Muggle society and history. He authored many books on the subject of Muggles, including the book <a href="/wiki/Muggles_Who_Notice" title="Muggles Who Notice">Muggles Who Notice</a>, which discusses the rare cases of Muggles who witness <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magic</a> and the <a href="/wiki/Wizarding_world" title="Wizarding world">wizarding world</a>, showing he was highly knowledgeable and accomplished in this academic field of <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizarding</a> social science.</li></ul>
