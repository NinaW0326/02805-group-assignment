<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="History">History</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Second_wand_of_Jacob%27s_sibling?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: History">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Second_wand_of_Jacob%27s_sibling?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: History"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>In the <a href="/wiki/1988%E2%80%931989_school_year" title="1988–1989 school year">1988–1989 school year</a>, <a href="/wiki/Patricia_Rakepick" title="Patricia Rakepick">Patricia Rakepick</a> destroyed Jacob's sibling's first wand.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> Following this, she and Jacob's sibling went to Ollivanders to buy a new wand. <a href="/wiki/Garrick_Ollivander" title="Garrick Ollivander">Garrick Ollivander</a> presented Jacob's sibling with three wands, and explained the qualities of each one. Jacob's sibling took a look at the three wands, after which they picked one of the three wands based on the qualities they felt most fit them. This wand chose Jacob's sibling.<sup id="cite_ref-ChapterSeventeen_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-ChapterSeventeen-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Second_wand_of_Jacob%27s_sibling?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Second_wand_of_Jacob%27s_sibling?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The properties of the wand is player-determined, and which wand chooses you depends on what quality they are most drawn to.
</p>
<ul><li>If the player replies they are most drawn to individuality and <a href="/wiki/Martial_magic" title="Martial magic">combative magic</a>, Ollivander will give them a wand made of <a href="/wiki/Ebony" title="Ebony">Ebony</a> with a <a href="/wiki/Dragon_heartstring" title="Dragon heartstring">Dragon heartstring</a> core. It is eleven-and-a-quarter inch long.<sup id="cite_ref-ChapterSeventeen_2-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-ChapterSeventeen-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li>If the player replies they are most drawn to good fortune, Ollivander will give them a wand made of <a href="/wiki/Redwood" title="Redwood">Redwood</a> with a <a href="/wiki/Unicorn_hair" title="Unicorn hair">Unicorn hair</a> core. It is eleven inches long.<sup id="cite_ref-ChapterSeventeen_2-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-ChapterSeventeen-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li>If the player replies they are most drawn to loyalty and questing for glory. Ollivander will give them a wand made of <a href="/wiki/Laurel" title="Laurel">Laurel</a> with a <a href="/wiki/Phoenix_feather" title="Phoenix feather">Phoenix feather</a> core. It is twelve inches long.<sup id="cite_ref-ChapterSeventeen_2-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-ChapterSeventeen-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup></li></ul>
<ul><li>An error exists where <a href="/wiki/Garrick_Ollivander" title="Garrick Ollivander">Ollivander</a> will say that they received the <a href="/wiki/Hornbeam" title="Hornbeam">Hornbeam</a> wand for their  <a href="/wiki/First_wand_of_Jacob%27s_sibling" title="First wand of Jacob&#39;s sibling">first wand</a>, regardless of the player's choice.</li></ul>
