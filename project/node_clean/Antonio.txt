<p><b>Antonio</b> was a baby <a href="/wiki/Chupacabra" title="Chupacabra">Chupacabra</a> chained to <a href="/wiki/Gellert_Grindelwald" title="Gellert Grindelwald">Gellert Grindelwald</a>'s chair in his cell during his brief imprisonment at the <a href="/wiki/Magical_Congress_of_the_United_States_of_America" title="Magical Congress of the United States of America">Magical Congress of the United States of America</a> in <a href="/wiki/1926" title="1926">1926</a>-<a href="/wiki/1927" title="1927">1927</a>. Most notably, it participated in <a href="/wiki/Gellert_Grindelwald%27s_escape" title="Gellert Grindelwald&#39;s escape">Gellert Grindelwald's escape</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-COG2_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COG2-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Antonio?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Antonio?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Shortly before Grindelwald's attempted <a href="/wiki/Gellert_Grindelwald%27s_escape" title="Gellert Grindelwald&#39;s escape">extradition to Europe</a>, under escort by several <a href="/wiki/Auror" title="Auror">Aurors</a> and <a href="/wiki/Rudolph_Spielman" title="Rudolph Spielman">Rudolph Spielman</a> (the <a href="/wiki/International_Confederation_of_Wizards" title="International Confederation of Wizards">International Confederation of Wizards</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Head_of_Incarceration" title="Head of Incarceration">Head of Incarceration</a>) Grindelwald managed to switch places with MACUSA official <a href="/wiki/Abernathy" title="Abernathy">Abernathy</a> using the <a href="/wiki/Polyjuice_Potion" title="Polyjuice Potion">Polyjuice Potion</a>. After the <a href="/wiki/Incarceration_Carriage" title="Incarceration Carriage">high-security flying carriage</a> set off from the <a href="/wiki/Woolworth_Building" title="Woolworth Building">Woolworth Building</a>, the real Grindelwald <a href="/wiki/Apparition" title="Apparition">Apparated</a> onto the carriage, eliminated all the <a href="/wiki/Auror" title="Auror">Aurors</a>, and destroyed <a href="/wiki/Rudolph_Spielman" title="Rudolph Spielman">Rudolph Spielman</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Rudolph_Spielman%27s_wand" title="Rudolph Spielman&#39;s wand">wand</a>, who - panicking - opened the box that had been handed to him supposedly containing <a href="/wiki/Elder_Wand" title="Elder Wand">Grindelwald's wand</a>, only to have Antonio jump up from inside the opened box and sink his fangs deep into Spielmanâ€™s neck.<sup id="cite_ref-COG11_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COG11-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Once Grindelwald clambered inside the carriage, he grabbed Antonio from Spielman and rubbed him affectionately; but suddenly, Grindelwald looked at Antonio with distaste and judged the Chupacabra to be "<i>so needy</i>", throwing him out the door of the flying carriage.<sup id="cite_ref-COG15_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COG15-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> It is unknown if Antonio managed to survive the fall or not.
</p>
