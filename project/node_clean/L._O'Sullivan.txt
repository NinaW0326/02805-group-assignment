<b>L. O'Sullivan</b> was an <a href="/wiki/United_States_of_America" title="United States of America">American</a> <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard or witch</a> who worked for the <a href="/wiki/Magical_Congress_of_the_United_States_of_America" title="Magical Congress of the United States of America">Magical Congress of the United States of America</a> in the <a href="/wiki/1920s" title="1920s">1920s</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-cob_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-cob-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/L._O%27Sullivan?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/L._O%27Sullivan?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>On <a href="/wiki/7_December" title="7 December">7 December</a> <a href="/wiki/1926" title="1926">1926</a>, <a href="/wiki/Y._Blishen" title="Y. Blishen">Y. Blishen</a> sent a <a href="/wiki/Memorandum_Rodentium" title="Memorandum Rodentium">rat memo</a> to "all 2nd Floor Sorcery Staff," including L. O'Sullivan. The memo notified recipients that, following a vote approving the <a href="/wiki/Calufrax_Edict" title="Calufrax Edict">Calufrax Edict</a>, wand permit requests from aliens (non-U.S. citizens) would have to be filed with a variety of new codes.<sup id="cite_ref-cob_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-cob-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
