<p>This <b>individual</b> was the older brother of a <a href="/wiki/Ravenclaw" title="Ravenclaw">Ravenclaw</a> <a href="/wiki/First_year" title="First year">first year</a> at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School</a> in the <a href="/wiki/1991-1992_school_year" class="mw-redirect" title="1991-1992 school year">1991-1992 school year</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-PSgba_1-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PSgba-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>In <a href="/wiki/1991" title="1991">1991</a>, before the <a href="/wiki/First_year" title="First year">first-years'</a> first <a href="/wiki/Flying_(class)" title="Flying (class)">Flying</a> lesson, his brother spent some time chatting about it in the <a href="/wiki/Dungeons" title="Dungeons">Dungeons</a>. According to him, this individual had told him that it was possible for one to take off with such speed that one could look back and see the top of one's head.<sup id="cite_ref-PSgba_1-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PSgba-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
