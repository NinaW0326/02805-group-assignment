<dd>&#8212;<a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a><sup class="noprint"><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter:_Wizards_Unite" title="Harry Potter: Wizards Unite">[src]</a></sup></dd>

<p>The term <b>Foundable</b> was used by the <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">Ministry of Magic</a> to refer to people, things, and even memories displaced all over the <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggle</a> world during the <a href="/wiki/Calamity" title="Calamity">Calamity</a>. Foundables were stuck in place by a piece of chaotic magic, called a <a href="/wiki/Confoundable" title="Confoundable">Confoundable</a>, thus threatening to expose the <a href="/wiki/Wizarding_world" title="Wizarding world">wizarding world</a> and in potential serious breach of the <a href="/wiki/International_Statute_of_Wizarding_Secrecy" title="International Statute of Wizarding Secrecy">International Statute of Wizarding Secrecy</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-WU_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-WU-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>The <a href="/wiki/Statute_of_Secrecy_Task_Force" title="Statute of Secrecy Task Force">Statute of Secrecy Task Force</a>, created as a response to the Calamity, was tasked to investigate and contain this threat and prevent outbreaks of <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magic</a> from being noticed by <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggles</a> by retrieving all Foundables on the <a href="/wiki/Registry" title="Registry">Registry</a>: a listing of all the missing Foundables known to the Ministry.<sup id="cite_ref-WU_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-WU-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
