This <b>individual</b> (fl. <a href="/wiki/1975" title="1975">1975</a>) was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> and <a href="/wiki/Werewolf" title="Werewolf">werewolf</a>
who preferred to remain anonymous. He wrote a heartrending account of his struggle against <a href="/wiki/Lycanthropy" title="Lycanthropy">lycanthropy</a> in the <a href="/wiki/1970s" title="1970s">1970s</a>. <i><a href="/wiki/Hairy_Snout,_Human_Heart" title="Hairy Snout, Human Heart">Hairy Snout, Human Heart</a></i>, the title of his book, was a classic by the <a href="/wiki/1990s" title="1990s">1990s</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-FB_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-FB-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
