<b>Hieronymus</b> was a male <a href="/wiki/Fire_crab" class="mw-redirect" title="Fire crab">Fire crab</a> that was used by <a href="/wiki/Silvanus_Kettleburn" title="Silvanus Kettleburn">Silvanus Kettleburn</a> during his <a href="/wiki/Care_of_Magical_Creatures" title="Care of Magical Creatures">Care of Magical Creatures</a> class for <a href="/wiki/Fourth_year" title="Fourth year">fourth years</a> in the <a href="/wiki/1987%E2%80%931988_school_year" title="1987–1988 school year">1987–1988 school year</a>.

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Hieronymus?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Hieronymus?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>His name was given by one of Kettleburn's <a href="/wiki/Slytherin" title="Slytherin">Slytherin</a> students, <a href="/wiki/Liz_Tuttle" title="Liz Tuttle">Liz Tuttle</a>.
</p><p>During one of the classes, Liz mentioned to <a href="/wiki/Jacob%27s_sibling" title="Jacob&#39;s sibling">Jacob's sibling</a> that Hieronymus said Kettleburn was reckless because he put creatures first.<sup id="cite_ref-HM_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HM-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
