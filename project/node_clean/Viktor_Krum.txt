<p><b>Viktor Krum</b> (<a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bulgarian_language" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Bulgarian language">Bulgarian</a>: Виктор Крум; born <a href="/wiki/April" title="April">April</a>-<a href="/wiki/August" title="August">August</a>, <a href="/wiki/1976" title="1976">1976</a>) was a <a href="/wiki/Bulgaria" title="Bulgaria">Bulgarian</a> <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a>. He attended the <a href="/wiki/Durmstrang_Institute" title="Durmstrang Institute">Durmstrang Institute</a>, and was also the <a href="/wiki/Seeker" title="Seeker">Seeker</a> for the <a href="/wiki/Bulgarian_National_Quidditch_team" title="Bulgarian National Quidditch team">Bulgarian National Quidditch team</a> at the age of eighteen while still at school.
</p><p>In <a href="/wiki/1994" title="1994">1994</a>, he played in the final of the <a href="/wiki/Quidditch_World_Cup" title="Quidditch World Cup">Quidditch World Cup</a>. The <a href="/wiki/Irish_National_Quidditch_team" title="Irish National Quidditch team">Irish</a> won the match, but Viktor caught the <a href="/wiki/Golden_Snitch" title="Golden Snitch">Golden Snitch</a> to end the match on his terms. Later in the year, he was part of the Durmstrang delegation sent to <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> for the <a href="/wiki/Triwizard_Tournament" title="Triwizard Tournament">Triwizard Tournament</a>. Subsequently, he was selected by the <a href="/wiki/Goblet_of_Fire" title="Goblet of Fire">Goblet of Fire</a> as the Durmstrang <a href="/wiki/Triwizard_Tournament" title="Triwizard Tournament">Champion</a>, and competed bravely. However, the Tournament ended in tragedy with the death of <a href="/wiki/Cedric_Diggory" title="Cedric Diggory">Cedric Diggory</a>, and Viktor returned home. In <a href="/wiki/1997" title="1997">1997</a>, he attended the <a href="/wiki/Wedding_of_William_Weasley_and_Fleur_Delacour" title="Wedding of William Weasley and Fleur Delacour">wedding</a> of <a href="/wiki/Fleur_Delacour" title="Fleur Delacour">Fleur Delacour</a> and <a href="/wiki/William_Weasley" title="William Weasley">Bill Weasley</a>.
</p><p>He retired from Quidditch after the Bulgarian defeat in the final of the <a href="/wiki/2002_Quidditch_World_Cup" title="2002 Quidditch World Cup">2002 World Cup</a>, but came out of retirement to compete in the <a href="/wiki/2014_Quidditch_World_Cup" title="2014 Quidditch World Cup">2014 World Cup</a>, out of a desire "<i>to win the World Cup before I die</i>". The Bulgarian side famously won the World Cup that year, after beating <a href="/wiki/Brazilian_National_Quidditch_team" title="Brazilian National Quidditch team">Brazil</a> in the finals.
</p>

<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows">"<i>...this is <a href="/wiki/Sign_of_the_Deathly_Hallows" title="Sign of the Deathly Hallows">his symbol</a>, I recognised it at vunce: <a href="/wiki/Gellert_Grindelwald" title="Gellert Grindelwald">Grindelvald</a> carved it into a vall at Durmstrang ven he vos a pupil there. <dd></dd></dl></div><p>Viktor was born to <a href="/wiki/Viktor_Krum%27s_father" title="Viktor Krum&#39;s father">Krum</a> and <a href="/wiki/Viktor_Krum%27s_mother" title="Viktor Krum&#39;s mother">his wife</a> between <a href="/wiki/May" title="May">May</a> and <a href="/wiki/August" title="August">August</a> <a href="/wiki/1976" title="1976">1976</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-BD_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-BD-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> He grew up in Bulgaria, and attended the <a href="/wiki/Durmstrang_Institute" title="Durmstrang Institute">Durmstrang Institute</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-GF_9-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GF-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup> He was one of the last people to purchase a <a href="/wiki/Viktor_Krum%27s_wand" title="Viktor Krum&#39;s wand">wand</a> from <a href="/wiki/Gregorovitch_Zauberst%C3%A4be" title="Gregorovitch Zauberstäbe">Gregorovitch Zauberstäbe</a> before <a href="/wiki/Mykew_Gregorovitch" title="Mykew Gregorovitch">Mykew Gregorovitch</a>'s retirement.
</p><p>During his time at the school, Viktor was confronted by what he believed to be the <a href="/wiki/Gellert_Grindelwald%27s_mark" class="mw-redirect" title="Gellert Grindelwald&#39;s mark">symbol</a> of <a href="/wiki/Gellert_Grindelwald" title="Gellert Grindelwald">Gellert Grindelwald</a>, which was indeed the symbol of the <a href="/wiki/Deathly_Hallows" title="Deathly Hallows">Deathly Hallows</a>, that had been carved into the walls by the wizard himself. Other students at the school began copying the symbol onto their books and clothing as a way of trying to shock and impress. Highly insulted, since his grandfather had been murdered by Grindelwald, Viktor and several other students who had also lost family at the hands of the <a href="/wiki/Dark_Wizard" title="Dark Wizard">Dark Wizard</a> taught them the error of their ways.<sup id="cite_ref-DH_10-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH-10">&#91;10&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="The_1994_Quidditch_World_Cup">The 1994 Quidditch World Cup</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Viktor_Krum?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: The 1994 Quidditch World Cup">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Viktor_Krum?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: The 1994 Quidditch World Cup"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<div class="quote">

<p>A skilled <a href="/wiki/Quidditch" title="Quidditch">Quidditch</a> <a href="/wiki/Seeker" title="Seeker">Seeker</a>, Viktor was selected to play for the <a href="/wiki/Bulgarian_National_Quidditch_team" title="Bulgarian National Quidditch team">Bulgarian National Quidditch team</a> despite still being at school, and was widely regarded as one of the best players in the world. In 1994, he played in the <a href="/wiki/Quidditch_World_Cup" title="Quidditch World Cup">Quidditch World Cup</a>, and helped Bulgaria reach the final against the <a href="/wiki/Irish_National_Quidditch_team" title="Irish National Quidditch team">Irish National Quidditch team</a>. He was considered a fantastically exciting player to watch, so much so that even some of those who were supporting Ireland couldn't help but cheer for him.
</p><p>During the final match, Viktor fooled the Irish Seeker, <a href="/wiki/Aidan_Lynch" title="Aidan Lynch">Aidan Lynch</a>, by performing a <i><a href="/wiki/Wronski_Feint" title="Wronski Feint">Wronski Feint</a></i> that resulted in Lynch ploughing into the ground. Despite his skills, the Irish side were too good for the Bulgarians, and Viktor decided to end the match by catching the <a href="/wiki/Golden_Snitch" title="Golden Snitch">Golden Snitch</a>. During his pursuit of the Snitch, he was hit in the face by a <a href="/wiki/Bludger" title="Bludger">Bludger</a> which broke his nose and gave him two black eyes. However, he caught the Snitch and ended the match on his own terms. He received a runners-up medal along with the rest of the Bulgarian team following the conclusion of the match.<sup id="cite_ref-GF_9-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GF-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<div class="quote">

<p>Viktor was part of the Durmstrang delegation that travelled with <a href="/wiki/Igor_Karkaroff" title="Igor Karkaroff">Igor Karkaroff</a> to <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> to compete in the <a href="/wiki/Triwizard_Tournament" title="Triwizard Tournament">Triwizard Tournament</a> on 30 October. His presence at the school caused a stir amongst the student body since he was famous, and he was constantly followed by groups of giggling girls. One of the few exceptions to this was Hermione Granger.
</p><p>Viktor entered his name into the <a href="/wiki/Goblet_of_Fire" title="Goblet of Fire">Goblet of Fire</a> to participate in the Tournament; he most likely did this in order to gain eternal glory and to make his school proud. He was selected as the Durmstrang <a href="/wiki/Triwizard_Tournament" title="Triwizard Tournament">Champion</a> during the Hallowe'en Feast on 31 October. He expressed no opinion on the entry of a fourth Champion — <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> — into the Tournament when he found out. It seemed as though he viewed Harry as just another champion of the tournament.
</p>

<p>Prior to the start of the Tournament, Viktor took part in the <a href="/wiki/Wand_weighing_ceremony" title="Wand weighing ceremony">Wand weighing ceremony</a> overseen by <a href="/wiki/Garrick_Ollivander" title="Garrick Ollivander">Garrick Ollivander</a>, and warranted only a small mention in <a href="/wiki/Rita_Skeeter" title="Rita Skeeter">Rita Skeeter</a>'s article on the Tournament. Skeeter's article focused on Harry Potter's entrance into the Tournament, and Viktor was only mentioned in the last line of the article; even then, his name was misspelled.
</p><p>Viktor spent a lot of time at Hogwarts in the library, as he had become attracted to <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione Granger</a>. Each day, he went to the library with the intention of talking to her, but could not work up the courage. The groups of infatuated girls continued to follow him, lingering behind bookcases and watching him.<sup id="cite_ref-GF_9-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GF-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup>

<p>On 24 November, Viktor took part in the <a href="/wiki/Triwizard_Tournament" title="Triwizard Tournament">First Task</a> of the Triwizard Tournament. He had been forewarned of the <a href="/wiki/Dragon" title="Dragon">dragons</a> by Karkaroff (who had sneaked into the <a href="/wiki/Forbidden_Forest" title="Forbidden Forest">Forbidden Forest</a>), and showed no surprise when the <a href="/wiki/Dragon" title="Dragon">dragon</a> task was announced. He drew the <a href="/wiki/Chinese_Fireball" title="Chinese Fireball">Chinese Fireball</a> and the number three slot.
</p><p>Entering the arena after <a href="/wiki/Fleur_Delacour" title="Fleur Delacour">Fleur Delacour</a>, Viktor didn't even think of flying. He used the <a href="/wiki/Conjunctivitis_Curse" title="Conjunctivitis Curse">Conjunctivitis Curse</a> to blind the dragon and retrieve the <a href="/wiki/Golden_egg" title="Golden egg">Golden egg</a>; but the dragon, confused and blinded, stumbled around and crushed half the real eggs it was guarding. Viktor lost points for that, and tied with Harry Potter for first place.<sup id="cite_ref-GF_9-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GF-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<p>With the approach of the <a href="/wiki/Yule_Ball" title="Yule Ball">Yule Ball</a>, Viktor finally found the courage to ask Hermione to the ball as his date, even though he could barely pronounce her name. They, with the other Champions, opened the dancing at the Ball. Hermione spent the evening trying to teach Viktor how to pronounce her name correctly, but did not succeed. They also spend much of the evening dancing with one another, much to Ron's increasing displeasure. Ron even accused Hermione of cozening up to the enemy. An unexpected side-effect of their apparent relationship was that Hermione was chosen by the judges of the Tournament to be the hostage that Viktor would have to rescue from the Black Lake.

<p>Having worked out the clue from the <a href="/wiki/Golden_egg" title="Golden egg">golden egg</a>, Viktor knew he had to breathe underwater for at least an hour during the <a href="/wiki/Triwizard_Tournament" title="Triwizard Tournament">Second Task</a>. It is unknown if he worked the clue out on his own or if he had help from the <a href="/wiki/Durmstrang_Institute" title="Durmstrang Institute">Durmstrang</a> headmaster. He chose to attempt to <a href="/wiki/Human_to_shark_spell" title="Human to shark spell">transfigure</a> himself into a <a href="/wiki/Shark" title="Shark">shark</a>, but managed only to transfigure his head. Still, it worked well enough for him to reach the <a href="/wiki/Great_Lake_Merpeople_colony" title="Great Lake Merpeople colony">merpeople village</a>.
</p><p>However, the teeth on his shark head were not placed well enough to bite through the ropes holding Hermione. Harry, who had reached the village before Viktor, saw the trouble he was having, and feared for his friend. He grabbed a jagged rock, then thumped Viktor on the shoulder and gave it to him. Taking the rock, Viktor cut through the ropes holding Hermione and headed for the surface. He arrived outside the time-limit in second place, but was awarded third after the judges took into account Harry's determination to rescue all the hostages.
</p><p>Back on dry land, Viktor confessed to Hermione that he had never felt about a girl the way he felt about her; and invited her to stay with him in Bulgaria over the summer.<sup id="cite_ref-GF_9-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GF-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Third_Task">Third Task</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Viktor_Krum?veaction=edit&amp;section=7" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Third Task">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Viktor_Krum?action=edit&amp;section=7" title="Edit section: Third Task"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<p>Prior to the <a href="/wiki/Triwizard_Tournament" title="Triwizard Tournament">Third Task</a>, Viktor viewed the beginnings of the maze that they would have to navigate. On their way back to the castle, Viktor took Harry to one side to ask him about his relationship with Hermione. Following the Second Task, Rita Skeeter had written an article concerning Hermione toying with Harry and Viktor's affections. Viktor sought out Harry and questioned him about the article. Harry assured Viktor that he and Hermione were only friends, but their conversation was interrupted by the arrival of a confused <a href="/wiki/Bartemius_Crouch_Senior" title="Bartemius Crouch Senior">Barty Crouch Snr</a>. While Harry rushed to get help, Viktor remained with Crouch. However, he was stunned by <a href="/wiki/Bartemius_Crouch_Junior" title="Bartemius Crouch Junior">Barty Crouch Jnr</a>. <a href="/wiki/Albus_Dumbledore" title="Albus Dumbledore">Albus Dumbledore</a> revived him, and Viktor believed that Crouch Snr had Stunned him when his back was turned and had vanished into the woods after.
</p>

<p>Krum continued his Quidditch career, representing <a href="/wiki/Bulgaria" title="Bulgaria">Bulgaria</a> in international <a href="/wiki/Quidditch" title="Quidditch">Quidditch</a> matches, until <a href="/wiki/2002" title="2002">2002</a>. On the final of the <a href="/wiki/2002_Quidditch_World_Cup" title="2002 Quidditch World Cup">424th Quidditch World Cup</a> (held that year), Bulgaria suffered a crushing disappointment when outstanding <a href="/wiki/Egyptian_National_Quidditch_team" title="Egyptian National Quidditch team">Egyptian</a> <a href="/wiki/Seeker" title="Seeker">Seeker</a> <a href="/wiki/Rawya_Zaghloul" title="Rawya Zaghloul">Rawya Zaghloul</a> narrowly beat Krum to the Snitch, making them lose 450 to 300. After the match, a tearful Krum announced his retirement.<sup id="cite_ref-PM_13-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PM-13">&#91;13&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>However, Krum's ambition to help Bulgaria win the <a href="/wiki/Quidditch_World_Cup" title="Quidditch World Cup">Quidditch World Cup</a> made him come out of retirement to compete in the 2014 Quidditch World Cup. At 38 years old, Krum was old for a Seeker, but his dramatic return caused Bulgaria to attract support from those whose own countries had not qualified.<sup id="cite_ref-PM_13-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PM-13">&#91;13&#93;</a></sup> He ended up catching the Snitch and secured Bulgaria's victory over Brazil in the <a href="/wiki/2014_Quidditch_World_Cup_final" title="2014 Quidditch World Cup final">World Cup Final</a>. Bulgaria's success was described by <a href="/wiki/Ginevra_Weasley" title="Ginevra Weasley">Ginny Potter</a> as being mostly due to Viktor Krum's excellent performance.
</p><p>During the <a href="/wiki/Calamity" title="Calamity">Calamity</a>, Viktor appeared as a <a href="/wiki/Foundable" title="Foundable">Foundable</a> alongside Hermione as the pair appeared at the Yule Ball, trapped in a giant Christmas ornament <a href="/wiki/Confoundable" title="Confoundable">Confoundable</a>. Volunteer members of the <a href="/wiki/Statute_of_Secrecy_Task_Force" title="Statute of Secrecy Task Force">Statute of Secrecy Task Force</a> had to cast the <a href="/wiki/Exploding_Charm" title="Exploding Charm">Exploding Charm</a> in order to free them and return the two to their proper time.<sup id="cite_ref-14" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-14">&#91;14&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Viktor eventually "found <a href="/wiki/Love" title="Love">love</a>" with someone from his native <a href="/wiki/Bulgaria" title="Bulgaria">Bulgaria</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-JKR_7-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-JKR-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup> He also kept in contact with Hermione, although the relationship was strictly platonic, contrary to Rita Skeeter's commentary at the 2014 World Cup.
</p>

<p>Viktor was a tall, thin, sallow young man with dark hair and eyes. He had a large, curved nose that he inherited from his father, a sharp profile, thick, black eyebrows. Despite appearing graceful while flying on a <a href="/wiki/Broomstick" title="Broomstick">broomstick</a>, he was round-shouldered and duck-footed while on the ground.
</p><p><a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione Granger</a> described him as "<i>grumpy-looking</i>" and not particularly handsome, though on the latter occasion, she was annoyed because his "fan club" would follow him into the library and make noise while she was trying to read.<sup id="cite_ref-GF_9-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GF-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup> By the time of Fleur and Bill's <a href="/wiki/Wedding_of_William_Weasley_and_Fleur_Delacour" title="Wedding of William Weasley and Fleur Delacour">wedding</a>, Viktor had grown a small beard, which <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ron Weasley</a> criticised.<sup id="cite_ref-DH_10-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH-10">&#91;10&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p><br />
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Personality_and_traits">Personality and traits</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Viktor_Krum?veaction=edit&amp;section=12" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Personality and traits">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Viktor_Krum?action=edit&amp;section=12" title="Edit section: Personality and traits"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<div class="quote">
<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire">"<i>He's really nice, you know. He's not at all like you'd think, coming from Durmstrang.</i>"</span></dd>
<dd>&#8212;Viktor's pleasant personality<sup class="noprint"><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Goblet_of_Fire" title="Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire">[src]</a></sup></dd>
<dd></dd></dl></div><p>Surly and moody, Viktor usually kept his own company and rarely spoke unless he needed to. Despite his fame, or perhaps because of it, Viktor was reserved and not fond of the attention he was paid because of his celebrity. However, he was known to be good company to those he allowed close to him, such as <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione Granger</a>, to whom he spoke "<i>very enthusiastically</i>" once they became friendly.
</p><p>Hermione described Viktor as very kind and not at all what one would expect from a student of <a href="/wiki/Durmstrang_Institute" title="Durmstrang Institute">Durmstrang</a>. Viktor also responded well to those that treated him fairly or impressed him. <a href="/wiki/Cedric_Diggory" title="Cedric Diggory">Cedric Diggory</a> was always polite to him, and Viktor was saddened by Cedric's death. Viktor was also impressed by <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>'s flying skills during the First Task. Harry also considered Viktor to be "<i>a lot tougher than he looked</i>" after seeing him dive into <a href="/wiki/Great_Lake" title="Great Lake">Black Lake</a> in January.<sup id="cite_ref-GF_9-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GF-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup> He also has very little patience with people who insulted the deaths of his beloved ones, as he felt highly insulted by Durmstrang students who copied the mark of Grindelwald, the dark wizard who killed Viktor's grandfather, and was furious when he saw Xenophilius Lovegood wearing the symbol.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Magical_abilities_and_skills">Magical abilities and skills</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Viktor_Krum?veaction=edit&amp;section=13" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Viktor_Krum?action=edit&amp;section=13" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li><b><a href="/wiki/Flight" title="Flight">Flying</a></b>: Viktor was an incredibly skilled flyer and played <a href="/wiki/Seeker" title="Seeker">Seeker</a> for the <a href="/wiki/Bulgarian_National_Quidditch_team" title="Bulgarian National Quidditch team">Bulgarian National Quidditch team</a> by the age of seventeen. He was said to be one of the best seekers in the world, despite his young age.</li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Transfiguration" title="Transfiguration">Transfiguration</a></b>: Viktor was shown to be skilled in the field of transfiguration. He partially <a href="/wiki/Human_to_shark_spell" title="Human to shark spell">transfigured himself into a shark</a> during the Second Task of the <a href="/wiki/Triwizard_Tournament" title="Triwizard Tournament">Triwizard Tournament</a>, which is <a href="/wiki/Human_Transfiguration" title="Human Transfiguration">human Transfiguration</a> — a very difficult form of transfiguration.</li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Dark_Arts" title="Dark Arts">Dark Arts</a></b>: At Durmstrang, he learned how to use dark magic (as it was an <a href="/wiki/Dark_Arts_(class)" title="Dark Arts (class)">academic class</a> there), although he was not a <a href="/wiki/Dark_Wizard" title="Dark Wizard">Dark Wizard</a>. He was forced to use the <a href="/wiki/Cruciatus_Curse" title="Cruciatus Curse">Cruciatus Curse</a> on Cedric Diggory under the influence of the <a href="/wiki/Imperius_Curse" title="Imperius Curse">Imperius Curse</a>, though it is unknown if he was able to use the curse under normal circumstances. He was also able to cast an effective <a href="/wiki/Conjunctivitis_Curse" title="Conjunctivitis Curse">Conjunctivitis Curse</a> against a dragon during the First Task of the Triwizard Tournament.</li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Duelling" title="Duelling">Duelling</a></b>: As the practical application of <a href="/wiki/Martial_magic" title="Martial magic">martial magic</a> is a mandatory field of study at Durmstrang, it is reasonable to assume that Viktor Krum was an accomplished duellist. He claimed that, if it would not disturb <a href="/wiki/William_Weasley" title="William Weasley">Bill Weasley</a> and <a href="/wiki/Fleur_Delacour" title="Fleur Delacour">Fleur Delacour</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Wedding_of_William_Weasley_and_Fleur_Delacour" title="Wedding of William Weasley and Fleur Delacour">wedding</a>, he would <a href="/wiki/Duelling" title="Duelling">duel</a> <a href="/wiki/Xenophilius_Lovegood" title="Xenophilius Lovegood">Xenophilius Lovegood</a> for wearing <a href="/wiki/Gellert_Grindelwald" title="Gellert Grindelwald">Gellert Grindelwald</a>'s symbol, showing a certain confidence in his abilities despite the fact that Lovegood was much older and for all he knew, a superior duellist.</li>
<li><b>Bilingualism:</b> Victor was able to speak fluent English alongside his native Bulgarian, although with a strong accent in the former.</li></ul>

<p>Viktor became attracted to Hermione Granger shortly after his arrival at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a>, and spent a lot of time in the library, trying to find the courage to talk to her. Eventually, following the announcement of the <a href="/wiki/Yule_Ball" title="Yule Ball">Yule Ball</a>, he finally worked up the nerve and asked her to the Ball as his date. She accepted, but told no one except <a href="/wiki/Ginevra_Weasley" title="Ginevra Weasley">Ginny Weasley</a>. Thus, their arrival at the Ball together caused a minor stir, as Viktor was famous and Hermione made herself look exceptionally pretty.
</p><p>Following the Ball, Hermione and Viktor continued to see each other. Viktor, who was usually taciturn, spoke enthusiastically to Hermione, who became so engrossed in their conversations that at one point, she seemed to forget she was eating. Hermione later became the hostage for Viktor to retrieve for the Second Task of the Tournament. Following the Task, Viktor told Hermione that he had never felt about a girl like the way he felt about her, and invited her to Bulgaria to visit him during the summer. However, <a href="/wiki/Rita_Skeeter" title="Rita Skeeter">Rita Skeeter</a>, an unregistered <a href="/wiki/Animagus" title="Animagus">Animagus</a>, eavesdropped on their conversation and wrote an article that painted Hermione as toying with both Viktor's affections and <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>'s. This, as well as Hermione's tendency to talk about her friend, led Viktor to seek out Harry, who confirmed that he and Hermione were just friends<sup id="cite_ref-GF_9-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GF-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup>. During a confrontation with Ron, Ginny claimed that Viktor and Hermione kissed at some point during this period, whether that is true or if Ginny was just trying to get under Ron's skin is unknown. She never did visit him in Bulgaria, although she did write to him often.<sup id="cite_ref-OP_11-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OP-11">&#91;11&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>At <a href="/wiki/Fleur_Delacour" title="Fleur Delacour">Fleur Delacour</a> and <a href="/wiki/William_Weasley" title="William Weasley">Bill Weasley</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Wedding_of_William_Weasley_and_Fleur_Delacour" title="Wedding of William Weasley and Fleur Delacour">wedding</a> in <a href="/wiki/1997" title="1997">1997</a>, Viktor complimented Hermione upon seeing her, but was disappointed to learn that she and <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ron Weasley</a> were "sort of" a couple.<sup id="cite_ref-DH_10-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH-10">&#91;10&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<div class="quote">

<p>Krum first met Harry Potter when the latter was mysteriously chosen to be a fourth <a href="/wiki/Triwizard_Tournament" title="Triwizard Tournament">Triwizard Tournament</a> champion. While it is uncertain if Krum believed Harry put his name into the <a href="/wiki/Goblet_of_Fire" title="Goblet of Fire">Goblet of Fire</a>, he seemed to view Harry as just one more competitor. Having gone before him in the first task, Krum watched from the sidelines as Harry performed the task. He was greatly impressed by Harry's flying skills. When Krum began seeing <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione Granger</a>, he began to have some misplaced jealousy for Harry. He did not know what to believe with the rumours swirling around.
</p><p>In May, after the champions had been shown the maze, Krum took Harry aside to ask him personally about his relationship with Hermione, which caused Harry disbelief that the internationally famous seeker seemed to consider him "<i>...an equal -a real rival</i>". When Harry assured Krum that Hermione was not his girlfriend and that they were just friends, Krum complimented his flying. The two remained friendly afterwards, though for a brief period Harry thought Krum was mad for using the <a href="/wiki/Cruciatus_Curse" title="Cruciatus Curse">Cruciatus Curse</a> on <a href="/wiki/Cedric_Diggory" title="Cedric Diggory">Cedric Diggory</a> in the maze, but then he learned that Krum had been under the <a href="/wiki/Imperius_Curse" title="Imperius Curse">Imperius Curse</a>.
</p><p>The two saw each other again at the <a href="/wiki/Wedding_of_William_Weasley_and_Fleur_Delacour" title="Wedding of William Weasley and Fleur Delacour">wedding of Bill Weasley and Fleur Delacour</a>, where a disguised Harry jealously told Krum that the pretty girl, <a href="/wiki/Ginevra_Weasley" title="Ginevra Weasley">Ginny Weasley</a>, had a big, jealous boyfriend. Before the final match of the 2014 Quidditch World Cup, Harry embraced Viktor and introduced his two sons, <a href="/wiki/James_Potter_II" title="James Potter II">James</a> and <a href="/wiki/Albus_Potter" title="Albus Potter">Albus</a>, to him, indicating that the two are likely in contact after the war.
</p>

<p><a href="/wiki/Igor_Karkaroff" title="Igor Karkaroff">Igor Karkaroff</a> was the headmaster of <a href="/wiki/Durmstrang_Institute" title="Durmstrang Institute">Durmstrang Institute</a> while Viktor Krum was at school. It is unknown how his relationship with him was, though when Karkaroff fled Hogwarts after the <a href="/wiki/Return_of_Lord_Voldemort" title="Return of Lord Voldemort">return of Lord Voldemort</a>, he spoke about him only with contempt.

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Viktor_Krum?veaction=edit&amp;section=22" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Viktor_Krum?action=edit&amp;section=22" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>Viktor means the "victorious one," appropriate for a forceful Quidditch player. The name may have been intended by J. K. Rowling to be ironic, as Viktor competes in two major tournaments in <i>Goblet of Fire</i> (the Quidditch World Cup and the Triwizard Tournament) and loses both.</li>
<li><a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Krum" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Krum">Krum</a> is the name of one of the most famous Bulgarian rulers, who ruled in the Ninth century. Krum is, however, not a typical name in modern Bulgaria, being of pagan origin, and is in fact not a surname, but a given name.<sup id="cite_ref-15" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-15">&#91;15&#93;</a></sup> At the time Bulgarian rulers were known by their first name, as they all rose from the same house/family (Dulu).</li>
<li>It could be possible that his real surname is Krumov (Крумов) which would follow the formation of surnames in Bulgarian language, and that Krum was just an Anglicization of it.</li>
<li>It could also be possible that Krum is a wizarding surname, as wizarding culture often favours very old names. This is plausible since medieval Bulgarian surnames were not necessarily possessive forms.</li></ul>

<ul><li>Viktor Krum was portrayed by <a href="/wiki/Bulgaria" title="Bulgaria">Bulgarian</a> actor <a href="/wiki/Stanislav_Ianevski" title="Stanislav Ianevski">Stanislav Ianevski</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Goblet_of_Fire_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire (film)">film adaptation</a> of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Goblet_of_Fire" title="Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire">Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire</a></i>. Ianevski returned as Krum for <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Deathly_Hallows:_Part_1" title="Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 1">Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 1</a></i> and was suppose to have a scene in the <a href="/wiki/Wedding_of_William_Weasley_and_Fleur_Delacour" title="Wedding of William Weasley and Fleur Delacour">Wedding of Fleur Delacour and Bill Weasley</a> dancing with <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione</a> but his appearance was cut out of the final film.</li>
<li>Viktor is the Slavic version of the name <i>Victor</i>; it is derived from the Latin <i>victor</i>, meaning "victorious". Krum was the name of a Bulgarian monarch who ruled between 803 and 814, and established a dynasty that ruled the country until 997.</li>
<li>In the books, Viktor is described as being tall, thin, with a curved nose and thick black eyebrows. The actor who portrays him in the fourth film, Stanislav Ianevski, however, is stockier, with broad shoulders, a straight nose and thin sized eyebrows. He also has stubble and very short hair, neither of which are described in the novel. However, in the last book, Viktor is mentioned to have grown a beard, so the stubble may have grown into one.</li>
<li>In <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter:_Quidditch_World_Cup" title="Harry Potter: Quidditch World Cup">Harry Potter: Quidditch World Cup</a></i>, Krum's appearance is much closer to the book's description since the game had been made before the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Goblet_of_Fire_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire (film)">Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire film</a>. Krum has long, dark brown hair, a curved nose, and a fairly slim build. Since Cedric Diggory also appears in the game, the game probably takes place in the 1993 season, meaning Krum was 17 at the time.</li>
<li>In <i><a href="/wiki/LEGO_Harry_Potter:_Years_1-4" title="LEGO Harry Potter: Years 1-4">LEGO Harry Potter: Years 1-4</a></i>, Viktor Krum has a flat top hair style.
<ul><li>Additionally, Krum kisses Hermione in full view of Ron in the <i>LEGO</i> game, while in the books Ron does not find out that the two kissed until almost two years later.</li></ul></li>
<li>In the book, Krum attacks Cedric Diggory in the maze with the <a href="/wiki/Cruciatus_Curse" title="Cruciatus Curse">Cruciatus Curse</a>. In the film, he tries to <a href="/wiki/Killing_Curse" title="Killing Curse">kill</a> Diggory. Additionally, Harry recognises him in the film as having been bewitched by the <a href="/wiki/Imperius_Curse" title="Imperius Curse">Imperius Curse</a>, whereas in the book, he is completely puzzled by his actions.
<ul><li>On a similar note, in the film, shortly after Krum disabled Fleur, Krum has a brief run-in with Harry and briefly points his wand threateningly at Harry, only to lower it upon seeing him (presumably due to Barty Crouch Jnr after bewitching him leaving explicit orders not to kill or harm Harry). During this time, Harry witnessed his eyes being clouded, which was implied to be the reason why he deduced Krum's bewitched status in the film.</li></ul></li>
<li>In the films Harry and Krum never talk to each other, so their relationships in the films remain unknown.</li></ul>
