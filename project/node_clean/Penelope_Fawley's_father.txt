<b>Mr Padgett</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Great_Britain" title="Great Britain">British</a> <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a>, the husband of <a href="/wiki/Penelope_Fawley%27s_mother" title="Penelope Fawley&#39;s mother">Mrs Padgett</a> and the father of <a href="/wiki/Penelope_Fawley" title="Penelope Fawley">Penelope Fawley</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-WU_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-WU-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Penelope_Fawley%27s_father?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Penelope_Fawley%27s_father?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>During the <a href="/wiki/Second_Wizarding_War" title="Second Wizarding War">Second Wizarding War</a>, Mr and Mrs Padgett were outspoken supporters of <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a> and his <a href="/wiki/Magic_is_Might" title="Magic is Might">Magic is Might</a> campaign. Following the end of the war and the fall of Voldemort, Mrs Padgett was imprisoned in Azkaban for her role in the conflict, although it is unknown if Mr Padgett was also sent to Azkaban or not.<sup id="cite_ref-WU_2-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-WU-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
