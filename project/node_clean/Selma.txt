The <b>Selma</b> is a gigantic <a href="/wiki/Snake" title="Snake">serpent</a> native to the icy <a href="/wiki/Water" title="Water">freshwater</a> lakes of <a href="/wiki/Norway" title="Norway">Norway</a>. Unlike the peaceful <a href="/wiki/Sea_serpent" title="Sea serpent">sea serpent</a>, the Selma is a ferocious and carnivorous <a href="/wiki/Beast" title="Beast">beast</a>, consisting on a diet of fish and <a href="/wiki/Human" title="Human">human</a> <a href="/wiki/Flesh" title="Flesh">flesh</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-PM_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PM-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="History">History</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Selma?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: History">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Selma?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: History"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Rather than their usual group of preforming <a href="/wiki/Troll" title="Troll">trolls</a>, the <a href="/wiki/Norwegian_National_Quidditch_team" title="Norwegian National Quidditch team">Norwegian National Quidditch team</a> elected to bring a Selma as their mascot for the <a href="/wiki/2014_Quidditch_World_Cup" title="2014 Quidditch World Cup">2014 Quidditch World Cup</a>, a decision defended by team manager <a href="/wiki/Arnulf_Moe" title="Arnulf Moe">Arnulf Moe</a> as reflecting the <i>"steely determination and ferocity"</i> of his team. The Selma was placed in a magical lake in the <a href="/wiki/Patagonian_Desert" title="Patagonian Desert">Patagonian Desert</a> where it engaged in a fight with the <a href="/wiki/Fiji" title="Fiji">Fijian</a> <a href="/wiki/Dukuwaqa" title="Dukuwaqa">Dukuwaqa</a>, which eventually escalated into a conflict between many of the team mascots.<sup id="cite_ref-PM_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PM-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Selma?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Selma?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>The <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Selma_(lake_monster)" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Selma (lake monster)">Selma</a> is a cryptid said to live in the Norwegian lake <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Seljordsvatnet" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Seljordsvatnet">Seljordsvatnet</a>, and could be considered Norway's <a href="/wiki/Loch_Ness_Monster" title="Loch Ness Monster">Loch Ness Monster</a>.</li>
<li>Curiously, <a href="/wiki/Newton_Scamander" title="Newton Scamander">Newt Scamander</a> did not mention Selmas in his sea serpent entry in the fifty-second edition of <a href="/wiki/Fantastic_Beasts_and_Where_to_Find_Them" title="Fantastic Beasts and Where to Find Them"><i>Fantastic Beasts and Where To Find Them</i></a>. It is possible that the species had not yet been discovered by <a href="/wiki/1991" title="1991">1991</a>.</li></ul>
