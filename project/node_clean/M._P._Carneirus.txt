<b>M. P. Carneirus</b> was an <a href="/wiki/United_States_of_America" title="United States of America">American</a> <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard or witch</a> and the <a href="/wiki/Captain_of_Aurors" title="Captain of Aurors">Captain of Aurors</a> for the <a href="/wiki/Magical_Congress_of_the_United_States_of_America" title="Magical Congress of the United States of America">Magical Congress of the United States of America</a> in the <a href="/wiki/1920s" title="1920s">1920s</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-poster_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-poster-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> Their name appeared as official certification on some wanted posters issued by MACUSA.<sup id="cite_ref-poster_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-poster-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
