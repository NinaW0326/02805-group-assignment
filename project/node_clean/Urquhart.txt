<b>Urquhart</b> was a student at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a>, <a href="/wiki/Sorting_ceremony" title="Sorting ceremony">sorted</a> into <a href="/wiki/Slytherin" title="Slytherin">Slytherin</a> and <a href="/wiki/Quidditch_Captain#Hogwarts_Quidditch_Captains" title="Quidditch Captain">Captain</a> of the <a href="/wiki/Slytherin_Quidditch_team" title="Slytherin Quidditch team">Slytherin Quidditch team</a> during the <a href="/wiki/1996%E2%80%931997_school_year" title="1996â€“1997 school year">1996-1997</a> <a href="/wiki/Quidditch" title="Quidditch">Quidditch</a> season at Hogwarts.<sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> Urquhart was new to the squad and he had not held any position on the team before so it seems odd that he was made the Captain, playing as a <a href="/wiki/Chaser" title="Chaser">Chaser</a>. During the match against Gryffindor, Urquhart shook hands with Harry, 'crushing it'.
