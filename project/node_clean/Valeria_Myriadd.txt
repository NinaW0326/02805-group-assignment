</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Valeria_Myriadd?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Valeria_Myriadd?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Life">Life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Valeria_Myriadd?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Valeria_Myriadd?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Life"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Valeria was born someplace in either <a href="/wiki/Great_Britain" title="Great Britain">Great Britain</a> or <a href="/wiki/Ireland" title="Ireland">Ireland</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-PLACE_5-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PLACE-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> and when she was eleven years old began attending <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a>, where she was sorted into <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor" title="Gryffindor">Gryffindor</a> <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Houses" title="Hogwarts Houses">House</a>.
She graduated at the end of her <a href="/wiki/Seventh_year" title="Seventh year">seventh year</a> with good enough grades that she could return as a <a href="/wiki/Professor" title="Professor">professor</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-TMOHP_4-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-TMOHP-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> and eventually rose to the position of <a href="/wiki/Head_of_Gryffindor_House" title="Head of Gryffindor House">Head of Gryffindor House</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-TMOHP_4-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-TMOHP-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> She died sometime prior to <a href="/wiki/September" title="September">September</a> <a href="/wiki/1991" title="1991">1991</a><sup id="cite_ref-DEATH_2-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DEATH-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span id="1991–1992_school_year"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="1991.E2.80.931992_school_year"><a href="/wiki/1991%E2%80%931992_school_year" title="1991–1992 school year">1991–1992 school year</a></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Valeria_Myriadd?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: 1991–1992 school year">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Valeria_Myriadd?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: 1991–1992 school year"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>By <a href="/wiki/1991" title="1991">1991</a>, several portraits of Valeria hung at the main halls of <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Castle" title="Hogwarts Castle">Hogwarts Castle</a>, such as the <a href="/wiki/Grand_Staircase" title="Grand Staircase">Grand Staircase</a>. In <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor_Tower" title="Gryffindor Tower">Gryffindor Tower</a>, one of her portraits guarded a secret passage to a <a href="/wiki/Disused_seventh_floor_bathroom" title="Disused seventh floor bathroom">disused bathroom</a>. The password for said passage was "<i>Pig Snout</i>".<sup id="cite_ref-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="1992-1993_school_year"><a href="/wiki/1992-1993_school_year" class="mw-redirect" title="1992-1993 school year">1992-1993 school year</a></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Valeria_Myriadd?veaction=edit&amp;section=4" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: 1992-1993 school year">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Valeria_Myriadd?action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: 1992-1993 school year"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>By the <a href="/wiki/1992-1993_school_year" class="mw-redirect" title="1992-1993 school year">next school year</a>, her place in the Gryffindor Tower had been taken by <a href="/wiki/Oraclitus_Spheer" title="Oraclitus Spheer">Oraclitus Spheer</a>. Her portrait was moved to the <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Library" title="Hogwarts Library">Hogwarts Library</a> Annexe, hiding a shortcut to a section containing the original hand-written manuscript of <i><a href="/wiki/Hogwarts:_A_History" title="Hogwarts: A History">Hogwarts: A History</a></i>, by <a href="/wiki/Chroniculus_Punnet" title="Chroniculus Punnet">Chroniculus Punnet</a>. The password was "<i>Bibliophile</i>".<sup id="cite_ref-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Valeria_Myriadd?veaction=edit&amp;section=5" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Valeria_Myriadd?action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>"Valeria" is the feminine form of the name "<i>Valerius</i>", who was a Roman saint in the <a href="/wiki/1100s" title="1100s">second century</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-ETYMOLOGY_8-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-ETYMOLOGY-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup> "<i>Myriad</i>" means "countless" or "extremely great in number".<sup id="cite_ref-ETYMOLOGY2_9-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-ETYMOLOGY2-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
