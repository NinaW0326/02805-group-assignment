<b>Professor</b><sup id="cite_ref-HPLE_2-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HPLE-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> <b>Arsenius Jigger</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> and an author specialising in <a href="/wiki/Potions_(class)" title="Potions (class)">Potions</a> and <a href="/wiki/Defence_Against_the_Dark_Arts" title="Defence Against the Dark Arts">Defence Against the Dark Arts</a>.

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Arsenius_Jigger?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Arsenius_Jigger?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>In his lifetime, he wrote several books in the fields of <a href="/wiki/Potion" title="Potion">Potions</a> and <a href="/wiki/Defence_Against_the_Dark_Arts" title="Defence Against the Dark Arts">Defence Against the Dark Arts</a>, having written: <i><a href="/wiki/Magical_Drafts_and_Potions" title="Magical Drafts and Potions">Magical Drafts and Potions</a></i>,<sup id="cite_ref-PS5_3-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PS5-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> <i><a href="/wiki/Potion_Opuscule" title="Potion Opuscule">Potion Opuscule</a></i>,<sup id="cite_ref-hppts_4-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-hppts-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> and <i><a href="/wiki/The_Essential_Defence_Against_the_Dark_Arts" title="The Essential Defence Against the Dark Arts">The Essential Defence Against the Dark Arts</a>.</i><sup id="cite_ref-POAF_5-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-POAF-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Arsenius Jigger also contributed to <i><a href="/wiki/Transfiguration_Today" title="Transfiguration Today">Transfiguration Today</a></i> in the <a href="/wiki/1920s" title="1920s">1920s</a> such as the article <i><a href="/wiki/Lycanthropy" title="Lycanthropy">Lycanthropy</a> v. <a href="/wiki/Werewolf" title="Werewolf">Werewolfry</a></i>.<sup id="cite_ref-FBF_6-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-FBF-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup> He was also, at an unknown point, a professor of <a href="/wiki/Defence_Against_the_Dark_Arts" title="Defence Against the Dark Arts">Defence Against the Dark Arts</a> at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-HPLE_2-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HPLE-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> A shop in <a href="/wiki/Diagon_Alley" title="Diagon Alley">Diagon Alley</a>, <a href="/wiki/London" title="London">London</a>  bears the name <a href="/wiki/Slug_%26_Jiggers_Apothecary" title="Slug &amp; Jiggers Apothecary">Slug and Jiggers</a>, and may have been founded or owned by him.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Magical_abilities_and_skills">Magical abilities and skills</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Arsenius_Jigger?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Arsenius_Jigger?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li><b><a href="/wiki/Defence_Against_the_Dark_Arts" title="Defence Against the Dark Arts">Defence Against the Dark Arts</a></b>: Jigger was an expert in defensive <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magic</a>, having written the well-regarded DADA textbook <i><a href="/wiki/The_Essential_Defence_Against_the_Dark_Arts" title="The Essential Defence Against the Dark Arts">The Essential Defence Against the Dark Arts</a></i>, and was even the <a href="/wiki/Professor" title="Professor">Professor</a> of this subject at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> at an unknown point, showing his highly proficient knowledge in defending oneself against the <a href="/wiki/Dark_Arts" title="Dark Arts">Dark Arts</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-HPLE_2-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HPLE-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Potion" title="Potion">Potions</a></b>: Jigger was highly accomplished in the <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magical</a> art and <a href="/wiki/Science" title="Science">science</a> of potion-making, having written two Potions books in his lifetime, <i><a href="/wiki/Magical_Drafts_and_Potions" title="Magical Drafts and Potions">Magical Drafts and Potions</a></i><sup id="cite_ref-PS5_3-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PS5-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> and <i><a href="/wiki/Potion_Opuscule" title="Potion Opuscule">Potion Opuscule</a></i>,<sup id="cite_ref-hppts_4-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-hppts-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> the former of which was used as Hogwarts textbook in the <a href="/wiki/Potions_(class)" title="Potions (class)">subject</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-PS5_3-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PS5-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Transfiguration" title="Transfiguration">Transfiguration</a></b>: Jigger was also knowledgeable of this notably very advanced, difficult and scientific branch of <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magic</a>, having contributed to the Transfiguration academic journal <i><a href="/wiki/Transfiguration_Today" title="Transfiguration Today">Transfiguration Today</a></i>, with an article regarding <a href="/wiki/Werewolf" title="Werewolf">Werewolves</a> and <a href="/wiki/Lycanthropy" title="Lycanthropy">Lycanthropy</a>, showing he was well informed of the nature of <a href="/wiki/Human_Transfiguration" title="Human Transfiguration">Human Transfiguration</a>, a particularly hard form of transfiguration.<sup id="cite_ref-FBF_6-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-FBF-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup></li></ul>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Arsenius_Jigger?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Arsenius_Jigger?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>Arsenius may be derived from the word "arsenious" meaning "containing arsenic", a very poisonous metallic element. Arsenius may also be be a reference to the famous physicist and chemist <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Svante_Arrhenius" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Svante Arrhenius">Svante Arrhenius</a>, most widely known for formulating the first modern definition of acids and bases in molecular terms.</li>
<li>A jigger can refer to a liquid measurement that is used often for liquor, or a measuring cup specifically for measuring that amount of liquid. In the <a href="/wiki/United_States_of_America" title="United States of America">United States of America</a>, a jigger usually holds 1.5 fluid ounces (44 ml).<sup id="cite_ref-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup> In modern bartending, a jigger is 50 ml.</li></ul>
