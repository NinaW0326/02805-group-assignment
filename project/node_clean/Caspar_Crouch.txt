<b>Caspar Crouch</b>, (fl. 20th century) was a <a href="/wiki/Pure-blood" title="Pure-blood">pure-blood</a><sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup><a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> who married pure-blood <a href="/wiki/Charis_Black" title="Charis Black">Charis Black</a> from the noble <a href="/wiki/House_of_Black" title="House of Black">House of Black</a>. The couple had three children: a <a href="/wiki/Caspar_Crouch%27s_son" title="Caspar Crouch&#39;s son">son</a> and <a href="/wiki/Caspar_Crouch%27s_daughters" title="Caspar Crouch&#39;s daughters">two daughters</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> He might be the father of <a href="/wiki/Bartemius_Crouch_Senior" title="Bartemius Crouch Senior">Bartemius Crouch Snr</a>, though this is unconfirmed.
</p><p>His name appears on the <a href="/wiki/Black_Family_Tapestry" title="Black Family Tapestry">Black family tree tapestry</a>.
</p>
