<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets">"<i>Fascinating, Ingenious, really how many ways <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggles</a> have found ways of getting along without <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magic</a></i>"</span></dd>
<dd>&#8212;Arthur Weasley's fascination with Muggles and their technology<sup class="noprint"><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Chamber_of_Secrets" title="Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets">[src]</a></sup></dd>
<dd></dd></dl></div>
<p><b>Arthur Weasley</b> (b. <a href="/wiki/6_February" title="6 February">6 February</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-BD_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-BD-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/1950" title="1950">1950</a>)<sup id="cite_ref-PM-DOB_2-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PM-DOB-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> was an <a href="/wiki/England" title="England">English</a> <a href="/wiki/Pure-blood" title="Pure-blood">pure-blood</a><sup id="cite_ref-blood_4-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-blood-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> in the employ of the <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">Ministry of Magic</a>, as well as a member of the second <a href="/wiki/Order_of_the_Phoenix" title="Order of the Phoenix">Order of the Phoenix</a>. He was a staunch believer in the equality of all <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magical</a> and <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggle</a> folk and was the head of the <a href="/wiki/Weasley_family" title="Weasley family">Weasley family</a>.
</p><p>Arthur was born to <a href="/wiki/Septimus_Weasley" title="Septimus Weasley">Septimus</a> and <a href="/wiki/Cedrella_Black" title="Cedrella Black">Cedrella Weasley</a> (née <a href="/wiki/House_of_Black" title="House of Black">Black</a>) and had <a href="/wiki/Arthur_Weasley%27s_brothers" title="Arthur Weasley&#39;s brothers">two brothers</a>. He attended <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> from <a href="/wiki/1961" title="1961">1961</a>-<a href="/wiki/1968" title="1968">1968</a> and was sorted into <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor" title="Gryffindor">Gryffindor</a> house. Soon after graduating, Arthur married <a href="/wiki/Molly_Weasley" title="Molly Weasley">Molly Prewett</a>. During the <a href="/wiki/First_Wizarding_War" title="First Wizarding War">First Wizarding War</a>, he and Molly had seven children: sons <a href="/wiki/William_Weasley" title="William Weasley">Bill</a>, <a href="/wiki/Charles_Weasley" title="Charles Weasley">Charlie</a>, <a href="/wiki/Percy_Weasley" title="Percy Weasley">Percy</a>, twins <a href="/wiki/Fred_Weasley" title="Fred Weasley">Fred</a> and <a href="/wiki/George_Weasley" title="George Weasley">George</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ron</a>, as well as their daughter <a href="/wiki/Ginevra_Weasley" title="Ginevra Weasley">Ginny</a>. He was not a member of the Order of the Phoenix during this period, and it is unknown if he participated in any way in the First Wizarding War. At some point after graduating from Hogwarts, Arthur began working for the Ministry, in the <a href="/wiki/Misuse_of_Muggle_Artefacts_Office" title="Misuse of Muggle Artefacts Office">Misuse of Muggle Artefacts Office</a>.
</p><p>In <a href="/wiki/1996" title="1996">1996</a>, he was promoted to head of the <a href="/wiki/Office_for_the_Detection_and_Confiscation_of_Counterfeit_Defensive_Spells_and_Protective_Objects" title="Office for the Detection and Confiscation of Counterfeit Defensive Spells and Protective Objects">Office for the Detection and Confiscation of Counterfeit Defensive Spells and Protective Objects</a> by the new <a href="/wiki/Minister_for_Magic" title="Minister for Magic">Minister for Magic</a>, <a href="/wiki/Rufus_Scrimgeour" title="Rufus Scrimgeour">Rufus Scrimgeour</a>. When the <a href="/wiki/Second_Wizarding_War" title="Second Wizarding War">Second Wizarding War</a> began, Arthur joined the Order. While guarding the <a href="/wiki/Department_of_Mysteries" title="Department of Mysteries">Department of Mysteries</a>, he was <a href="/wiki/Attack_on_Arthur_Weasley" title="Attack on Arthur Weasley">attacked</a> by <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a>'s pet snake <a href="/wiki/Nagini" title="Nagini">Nagini</a>, but he made a full recovery. Arthur participated in the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_the_Seven_Potters" title="Battle of the Seven Potters">Battle of the Seven Potters</a> (<a href="/wiki/1997" title="1997">1997</a>) and the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a> (<a href="/wiki/1998" title="1998">1998</a>), in which he suffered the devastating loss of his <a href="/wiki/Fred_Weasley" title="Fred Weasley">son</a>; however, he survived the war.
</p><p>After the war, he saw his family expand even further with the addition of twelve grandchildren, through the marriage of many of his surviving children and returned as the Head of the Misuse of Muggle Artefacts Office.<sup id="cite_ref-PMI_9-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PMI-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h3><span id="Early_life_(1950-1968)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_life_.281950-1968.29">Early life <span class="template-c">(1950-1968)</span></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Arthur_Weasley?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Early life (1950-1968)">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Arthur_Weasley?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Early life (1950-1968)"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<div class="quote">
<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire"><b>Molly Weasley</b>: "<i><a href="/wiki/Fat_Lady" title="Fat Lady">She</a> gave me such a telling-off one night when I got back to the dormitory at four in the morning —</i>"</span></dd>

<dd>&#8212; Molly discussing her and Arthur's time at Hogwarts<sup class="noprint"><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Goblet_of_Fire" title="Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire">[src]</a></sup></dd></dl>

<p>Arthur Weasley was born on <a href="/wiki/6_February" title="6 February">6 February</a><sup id="cite_ref-BD_1-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-BD-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>, <a href="/wiki/1950" title="1950">1950</a><sup id="cite_ref-PM-DOB_2-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PM-DOB-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> to <a href="/wiki/Septimus_Weasley" title="Septimus Weasley">Septimus</a> and <a href="/wiki/Cedrella_Black" title="Cedrella Black">Cedrella Weasley</a> (née <a href="/wiki/House_of_Black" title="House of Black">Black</a>).<sup id="cite_ref-11" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-11">&#91;11&#93;</a></sup> His mother was disowned for marrying a "<a href="/wiki/Blood_traitor" title="Blood traitor">blood traitor</a>," as her family believed strongly in the notion of <a href="/wiki/Pure-blood" title="Pure-blood">pure-blood</a> supremacy. Arthur had <a href="/wiki/Arthur_Weasley%27s_brothers" title="Arthur Weasley&#39;s brothers">two brothers</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-12" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-12">&#91;12&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>He attended <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> from <a href="/wiki/1961" title="1961">1961</a>-<a href="/wiki/1968" title="1968">1968</a> and was sorted into <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor" title="Gryffindor">Gryffindor House</a>. In his third year in <a href="/wiki/1963" title="1963">1963</a>, he may have chosen to take <a href="/wiki/Muggle_Studies" title="Muggle Studies">Muggle Studies</a>, due to his interest in <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggles</a>. While at school, he met and began dating fellow Gryffindor <a href="/wiki/Molly_Weasley" title="Molly Weasley">Molly Prewett</a>. Arthur got caught out of bed by the school caretaker <a href="/wiki/Apollyon_Pringle" title="Apollyon Pringle">Apollyon Pringle</a> after he and Molly went for a night-time stroll one evening and was severely punished.<sup id="cite_ref-GOF_13-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF-13">&#91;13&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span id="First_Wizarding_War_(1968-1981)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="First_Wizarding_War_.281968-1981.29">First Wizarding War <span class="template-c">(1968-1981)</span></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Arthur_Weasley?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: First Wizarding War (1968-1981)">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Arthur_Weasley?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: First Wizarding War (1968-1981)"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<div class="quote">
<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince"><b>Molly Weasley</b>: "<i>It's all this uncertainty with You-Know-Who coming back, people think they might be dead tomorrow, so they're rushing all sorts of decisions they'd normally take time over. <dd>&#8212; <a href="/wiki/Molly_Weasley" title="Molly Weasley">Molly Weasley</a> reflecting on her marriage to Arthur<sup class="noprint"><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Half-Blood_Prince" title="Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince">[src]</a></sup></dd></dl>

<figure class="thumb tleft show-info-icon" style="width: 237px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/5/59/The_Burrow.png/revision/latest?cb=20150828154152" class="image"><img alt="" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw%3D%3D" decoding="async" width="235" height="158" class="thumbimage lazyload" data-image-name="The Burrow.png" data-image-key="The_Burrow.png" data-src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/5/59/The_Burrow.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/235?cb=20150828154152" /></a> 	<noscript><a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/5/59/The_Burrow.png/revision/latest?cb=20150828154152" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/5/59/The_Burrow.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/235?cb=20150828154152" decoding="async" width="235" height="158" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="The Burrow.png" data-image-key="The_Burrow.png" data-src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/5/59/The_Burrow.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/235?cb=20150828154152" /></a></noscript> 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:The_Burrow.png" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption">The Burrow, located on the outskirts of <a href="/wiki/Ottery_St_Catchpole" title="Ottery St Catchpole">Ottery St Catchpole</a></p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure>
<p>Shortly after graduating from Hogwarts, Arthur and Molly married. Their marriage was rather rushed, as <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a> was gaining power during that time and the <a href="/wiki/First_Wizarding_War" title="First Wizarding War">First Wizarding War</a> began.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP5_14-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP5-14">&#91;14&#93;</a></sup> Arthur was not a member of the first <a href="/wiki/Order_of_the_Phoenix" title="Order of the Phoenix">Order of the Phoenix</a>. It is unknown if he was otherwise involved in the war, although he was familiar with the <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a>' use of the <a href="/wiki/Imperius_Curse" title="Imperius Curse">Imperius Curse</a> and the trouble it caused the <a href="/wiki/Ministry_of_Magic" title="Ministry of Magic">Ministry of Magic</a> at the time.<sup id="cite_ref-15" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-15">&#91;15&#93;</a></sup> During the course of the war, he and Molly had seven children: <a href="/wiki/William_Weasley" title="William Weasley">Bill</a> in late <a href="/wiki/1970" title="1970">1970</a>, <a href="/wiki/Charles_Weasley" title="Charles Weasley">Charlie</a> in <a href="/wiki/1972" title="1972">1972</a>, <a href="/wiki/Percy_Weasley" title="Percy Weasley">Percy</a> in <a href="/wiki/1976" title="1976">1976</a>, twins <a href="/wiki/Fred_Weasley" title="Fred Weasley">Fred</a> and <a href="/wiki/George_Weasley" title="George Weasley">George</a> in <a href="/wiki/1978" title="1978">1978</a>, <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ron</a> in <a href="/wiki/1980" title="1980">1980</a>, and the first daughter to be born into the Weasley family in generations, <a href="/wiki/Ginevra_Weasley" title="Ginevra Weasley">Ginny</a>, in <a href="/wiki/1981" title="1981">1981</a>.
</p><p>The war ended soon after Ginny's birth, but not before Arthur's brothers-in-law <a href="/wiki/Fabian_Prewett" title="Fabian Prewett">Fabian</a> and <a href="/wiki/Gideon_Prewett" title="Gideon Prewett">Gideon Prewett</a>, both Order members, were killed by <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-OTP_16-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OTP-16">&#91;16&#93;</a></sup> Arthur was upset when some Death Eaters, such as <a href="/wiki/Lucius_Malfoy" title="Lucius Malfoy">Lucius Malfoy</a>, were able to escape punishment by claiming that they had been under the <a href="/wiki/Imperius_Curse" title="Imperius Curse">Imperius Curse</a>. Arthur believed they were lying, and he and Lucius were hostile towards one another whenever they crossed paths. 
</p>
<h3><span id="Between_the_wars_(1981-1995)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="Between_the_wars_.281981-1995.29">Between the wars <span class="template-c">(1981-1995)</span></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Arthur_Weasley?veaction=edit&amp;section=4" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Between the wars (1981-1995)">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Arthur_Weasley?action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Between the wars (1981-1995)"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>At some point between his graduation from Hogwarts and <a href="/wiki/1992" title="1992">1992</a>, Arthur gained employment in the <a href="/wiki/Misuse_of_Muggle_Artefacts_Office" title="Misuse of Muggle Artefacts Office">Misuse of Muggle Artefacts Office</a> at the <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">Ministry of Magic</a>. He also purchased a <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggle</a> <a href="/wiki/Car" title="Car">car</a>, a <a href="/wiki/Flying_Ford_Anglia" title="Flying Ford Anglia">Ford Anglia</a>, and bewitched it to make it <a href="/wiki/Flight" title="Flight">fly</a>; in order to do so, he made sure to include a loophole in a law he wrote for the Ministry: as long as he was not <i>intending</i> to fly the car, it would be legal. The car was part of Arthur's substantial personal collection of Muggle artefacts, which he kept in a <a href="/wiki/Arthur_Weasley%27s_shed" title="Arthur Weasley&#39;s shed">shed</a> and included <a href="/wiki/Plug" title="Plug">plugs</a> and batteries.<sup id="cite_ref-COS_17-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COS-17">&#91;17&#93;</a></sup> 
</p>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="The_Chamber_of_Secrets_and_Sirius_Black">The Chamber of Secrets and Sirius Black</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Arthur_Weasley?veaction=edit&amp;section=5" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: The Chamber of Secrets and Sirius Black">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Arthur_Weasley?action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: The Chamber of Secrets and Sirius Black"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>

<p>In <a href="/wiki/1992" title="1992">1992</a>, Ron, Fred, and George borrowed their father's car to rescue <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> from <a href="/wiki/4_Privet_Drive" title="4 Privet Drive">Privet Drive</a>, after not receiving letters from him all summer. Arthur and Molly later arrived at Hogwarts shortly after the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_the_Astronomy_Tower" title="Battle of the Astronomy Tower">Battle of the Astronomy Tower</a> to see their son Bill, who was gravely injured by <a href="/wiki/Fenrir_Greyback" title="Fenrir Greyback">Fenrir Greyback</a> during the conflict. The Weasley family sadly attended the funeral of Dumbledore, a man they respected greatly and who had led them as the head of the <a href="/wiki/Order_of_the_Phoenix" title="Order of the Phoenix">Order of the Phoenix</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP_19-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP-19">&#91;19&#93;</a></sup>

<p>Following the outbreak of the <a href="/wiki/Calamity" title="Calamity">Calamity</a>, Arthur Weasley assisted the newly created <a href="/wiki/Statute_of_Secrecy_Task_Force" title="Statute of Secrecy Task Force">Statute of Secrecy Task Force</a> in returning <a href="/wiki/Foundable" title="Foundable">Foundables</a> that had been displaced in the <a href="/wiki/Muggle_world" title="Muggle world">Muggle world</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-20" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-20">&#91;20&#93;</a></sup> Around the same time, a younger version of himself appeared as a <a href="/wiki/Foundable" title="Foundable">Foundable</a>, forcing volunteer <a href="/wiki/Calamity_Investigator" title="Calamity Investigator">members</a> of the <a href="/wiki/Statute_of_Secrecy_Task_Force" title="Statute of Secrecy Task Force">SOS Task Force</a> to track him down to free him, allowing him to return to his original time.<sup id="cite_ref-WU_21-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-WU-21">&#91;21&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Physical_appearance">Physical appearance</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Arthur_Weasley?veaction=edit&amp;section=18" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Physical appearance">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Arthur_Weasley?action=edit&amp;section=18" title="Edit section: Physical appearance"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Like many other members of the <a href="/wiki/Weasley_family" title="Weasley family">Weasley family</a>, Arthur had bright red hair, though he was balding by the early 1990s. He wore glasses and had blue eyes, and also possessed the tall, thin build inherited by his sons Ron and <a href="/wiki/William_Weasley" title="William Weasley">Bill</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-COS_17-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COS-17">&#91;17&#93;</a></sup> He also had visible marks somewhere on his body from a decent caning whilst at Hogwarts, for being late. The punishment was set by the caretaker at the time, <a href="/wiki/Apollyon_Pringle" title="Apollyon Pringle">Apollyon Pringle</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-GOF_13-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF-13">&#91;13&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Personality_and_traits">Personality and traits</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Arthur_Weasley?veaction=edit&amp;section=19" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Personality and traits">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Arthur_Weasley?action=edit&amp;section=19" title="Edit section: Personality and traits"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<div class="quote">
<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 1 (video game)">"<i>That's what's held Arthur back at the Ministry, honest as the day is long, reliable, trustworthy and able... all qualities guaranteed to stop you moving forward at the Ministry these days.</i>"</span></dd>
<dd>&#8212;An attendee of the Wedding of William Weasley and Fleur Delacour<sup class="noprint"><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Deathly_Hallows:_Part_1_(video_game)" title="Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 1 (video game)">[src]</a></sup></dd>
<dd></dd></dl></div><p>In the <a href="/wiki/Wizarding_world" title="Wizarding world">wizarding world</a>, Arthur Weasley was known for being a well-meaning, reasonable and competent every-man, trying to take care of his family and do the right thing. A man of principle, he was strongly opposed to the discrimination of <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggles</a>, <a href="/wiki/Muggle-borns" class="mw-redirect" title="Muggle-borns">Muggle-borns</a>, <a href="/wiki/Half-blood" title="Half-blood">half-bloods</a>, <a href="/wiki/Half-breed" title="Half-breed">half-breeds</a>, and cursed beings such as <a href="/wiki/Werewolf" title="Werewolf">werewolves</a>, and did not think highly of those who subscribed to such intolerant beliefs, despite it being the prevailing views of so many of his fellow <a href="/wiki/Pure-blood" title="Pure-blood">pure-bloods</a>. While this earned him respect of his friends and family, others, such as <a href="/wiki/Lucius_Malfoy" title="Lucius Malfoy">Lucius Malfoy</a>, considered him to be a "<a href="/wiki/Blood_traitor" title="Blood traitor">blood traitor</a>."<sup id="cite_ref-COS_17-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COS-17">&#91;17&#93;</a></sup> and a "disgrace to the name of wizard". The latter, however, he never truly took to heart, as his opinion about what disgraced the name of wizard differed greatly from theirs. While Arthur Weasley was often seen as "fun" in the earlier years, with the return of <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a> and the gathering crisis in the wizarding world he shouldered a role of increasing challenges, assuming duties within the <a href="/wiki/Order_of_the_Phoenix" title="Order of the Phoenix">Order of the Phoenix</a> in addition to new responsibilities at the Ministry. He was protective of Harry, and at times assumed a father like role, a position previously occupied by Harry's godfather <a href="/wiki/Sirius_Black" title="Sirius Black">Sirius Black</a>, and by <a href="/wiki/Albus_Dumbledore" title="Albus Dumbledore">Albus Dumbledore</a>. He was a man of principle and remained loyal to Dumbledore, even though this impeded his advancement in the <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">Ministry of Magic</a> and cost him the respect of Percy, who also worked at the Ministry but agreed more strongly with the policies of the <a href="/wiki/Minister_for_Magic" title="Minister for Magic">Minister for Magic</a>. A devoted father, Arthur was, while more laid-back parent than his wife, was nevertheless very protective of them, and loved his own flesh and blood so much that the only times he was seen to lose control of himself was when any of his children were hurt.

<p>Arthur was fascinated at how Muggles were able to go about their lives without magic. Whenever he found himself in close proximity to them, he had a hard time containing his enthusiasm. During Harry's stays at the Burrow, he liked to sit next to Harry, in order to ask him questions about living with them and had at various times attempted to engage the <a href="/wiki/Dursley_family" title="Dursley family">Dursleys</a> in conversation about electricity. In 1992, when he learned Harry had never travelled by the Floo Network, he joyfully began to ask about the "escapators" in the London Underground. When the Weasleys and Harry met <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione Granger</a> in <a href="/wiki/Diagon_Alley" title="Diagon Alley">Diagon Alley</a> that same year, Arthur was ecstatic to meet Hermione's parents, both Muggles, who were exchanging money at <a href="/wiki/Gringotts_Wizarding_Bank" title="Gringotts Wizarding Bank">Gringotts</a>. While the other family members went shopping for school supplies, Arthur insisted on taking <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger%27s_father" title="Hermione Granger&#39;s father">Mr</a> and <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger%27s_mother" title="Hermione Granger&#39;s mother">Mrs Granger</a> for a drink in the Leaky Cauldron.<sup id="cite_ref-COS_17-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COS-17">&#91;17&#93;</a></sup> In addition to his Ford Anglia, Arthur also collected a number of other Muggle items. He had a large collection of plugs and batteries, as well as other undisclosed items in his shed. After successfully transporting Harry to <a href="/wiki/The_Burrow" title="The Burrow">The Burrow</a> in the aftermath of the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_the_Seven_Potters" title="Battle of the Seven Potters">Battle of the Seven Potters</a>, Arthur showed Harry the remains of Sirius' motorcycle and told him he was eager to learn how the brakes worked and that he also wanted to put it back together when <a href="/wiki/Molly_Weasley" title="Molly Weasley">Molly</a> was not around. In time, Arthur did put it back together and returned it to Harry.<sup id="cite_ref-DH_8-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<p>Because of his fondness for Muggles, in 1992, he drafted a proposition for the Ministry called the <a href="/wiki/Muggle_Protection_Act" title="Muggle Protection Act">Muggle Protection Act</a>. Many pure-blood wizards took offence to this, and <a href="/wiki/Lucius_Malfoy" title="Lucius Malfoy">Lucius Malfoy</a> attempted to sabotage Arthur's plans; by planting Riddle's diary on Arthur's daughter, he hoped to discredit Arthur and bury the legislation.<sup id="cite_ref-COS_17-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COS-17">&#91;17&#93;</a></sup> So adamant in protecting Muggles, he was outraged at Fred and George's prank on Dudley Dursley, even though the twins did not target Dudley because he was a Muggle, but rather he was a horrible boy. When he took his family, Harry and Hermione to the <a href="/wiki/Quidditch_World_Cup" title="Quidditch World Cup">Quidditch World Cup</a> in 1994, he was adamant about remaining incognito. He insisted on putting up their tents by hand and cooking their food outside with a fire. While somewhat familiar with the concept, he had trouble with a Muggle matchbox; he would light a match and subsequently drop it in surprise. In the end, he managed to get the fire lit with Hermione's help and seemed to have a great time. When <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a> started a riot after the cup, Arthur was one of the people who acted to protect Muggle victims.<sup id="cite_ref-GOF_13-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF-13">&#91;13&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>His wall in the <a href="/wiki/Misuse_of_Muggle_Artefacts_Office" title="Misuse of Muggle Artefacts Office">Misuse of Muggle Artefacts Office</a> was covered with Muggle pictures, including several of Muggle cars (one of which was a disassembled motor) Muggle post boxes, which he appeared to have cut out of a Muggle children's book and a diagram of how to wire a plug. His greatest ambition was to find out how Muggles were able to keep aeroplanes airborne. Arthur's love of engineering and Muggle technology was inherited by his sons Fred and George in that they too possessed Arthur's ingenuity and almost compulsive need to invent or modify things from their intended purposes, resulting in many of the twins' joke items.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Magical_abilities_and_skills">Magical abilities and skills</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Arthur_Weasley?veaction=edit&amp;section=20" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Arthur_Weasley?action=edit&amp;section=20" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li><b><a href="/wiki/Charm" title="Charm">Charms</a></b>: Arthur was shown to be particularly gifted with charm-work, having placed various charms, spells, and enchantments on Muggle objects. For example, he once charmed a <a href="/wiki/Flying_Ford_Anglia" title="Flying Ford Anglia">Ford Anglia car</a> to be able to <a href="/wiki/Levitation" title="Levitation">fly</a> and become <a href="/wiki/Invisibility" title="Invisibility">invisible</a>. With help from Hagrid, Arthur also enchanted Sirius' flying motorcycle, enabling it to produce <a href="/wiki/Fire" title="Fire">fire</a> like that of a <a href="/wiki/Dragon" title="Dragon">dragon</a>'s as a weapon, and a brick wall as a defence from the tail-pipe. Arthur was also able to conjure a corporeal <a href="/wiki/Patronus_Charm" title="Patronus Charm">Patronus</a> in the form of a <a href="/wiki/Weasel" title="Weasel">Weasel</a>, which is an extremely advanced and powerful piece of magic as well as proof of superior <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magical</a> ability. As a member of the Order of the Phoenix, he was able to send messages using his Patronus.<sup id="cite_ref-DH_8-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup> Arthur was also highly proficient with the <a href="/wiki/Memory_Charm" title="Memory Charm">Memory Charm</a>, having used it to erase the memories of <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggles</a> who had unwittingly witnessed magic, as part of his career duties with the Ministry.<sup id="cite_ref-COS_17-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COS-17">&#91;17&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-GOF_13-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF-13">&#91;13&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Duelling" title="Duelling">Duelling</a></b>: Arthur was shown to be a very proficient duellist. He was fully confident that he could match his rival <a href="/wiki/Lucius_Malfoy" title="Lucius Malfoy">Lucius Malfoy</a> (a notably skilled duellist himself) in skill if not defeat him. When the Death Eaters attacked the Quiditch World Cup, he helped the Ministry chase off the Death Eaters and did not suffer any injuries from the battle. He was chosen as one of the protectors during the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_the_Seven_Potters" title="Battle of the Seven Potters">Battle of the Seven Potters</a> where he survived unscathed, most likely defeating several Death Eaters in the process or at least holding them at bay. Later during <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a>, he was chosen to lead one of the teams of combatants onto the grounds, and at one point engages in a fierce duel with <a href="/wiki/Unidentified_Death_Eater_who_engaged_Arthur_Weasley" title="Unidentified Death Eater who engaged Arthur Weasley">a Death Eater</a> where he likely emerged victorious. Later on, along with his son, <a href="/wiki/Percy_Weasley" title="Percy Weasley">Percy</a>, he took down the <a href="/wiki/Imperius_Curse" title="Imperius Curse">Imperiused</a> <a href="/wiki/Pius_Thicknesse" title="Pius Thicknesse">Pius Thicknesse</a> in the latter half of the battle, and emerged as one of the survivors of the Second Wizarding War.</li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Nonverbal_spell" title="Nonverbal spell">Non-verbal magic</a></b>: Arthur was capable of casting <a href="/wiki/Spell" title="Spell">spells</a> without speaking their <a href="/wiki/Incantation" title="Incantation">incantations</a> aloud, having been able to effectively perform a non-verbal <i><a href="/wiki/Oculus_Reparo" title="Oculus Reparo">Oculus Reparo</a></i> on <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>'s broken <a href="/wiki/Glasses" title="Glasses">Glasses</a> in <a href="/wiki/1992" title="1992">1992</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-COS_17-10" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COS-17">&#91;17&#93;</a></sup> which testifies to him being a powerful wizard as non-verbal magic is very difficult.</li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Transfiguration" title="Transfiguration">Transfiguration</a></b>: Arthur was shown to be skilled in this department of magic to a high level, given how he was able to effectively perform <a href="/wiki/Conjuration" title="Conjuration">conjuration</a>, which is a highly advanced and difficult branch of transfiguration. He <a href="/wiki/Candle_conjuring_spell" title="Candle conjuring spell">conjured candles</a> to light up the Burrow garden one summer evening, and <a href="/wiki/Chair-conjuring_spell" title="Chair-conjuring spell">conjured several chairs</a> for his family when they came to visit him at St Mungo's, both with seemingly little effort. Additionally, he was able to cast what seemed to be a conjuring enchantment on Sirius Black's Motorbike to allow it to conjure fire and a brick wall.</li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Apparition" title="Apparition">Apparition</a></b>: Arthur was shown to be adept in the art of apparition, as he was described as making only a faint "popping" noise, which contrasts to the loud "crack" that inexperienced apparition users would make, showing he was skilful with this method of magical transportation.</li>
<li><b>Mechanical skills</b>: Due to Arthur's interest in Muggle technology, particularly automobiles, Arthur developed quite a bit of skill in repairing muggle machinery in his spare time. Along with his flying car, Arthur made several mechanical and magical modifications to Sirius Black's motorcycle. Though the bike was severely damaged during the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_the_Seven_Potters" title="Battle of the Seven Potters">Battle of the Seven Potters</a>, he managed to repair the vehicle to perfect working order, which he then later returned to Harry, who as Sirius's named heir, was the bike's proper owner. He likely improved his skills significantly during this little sojourn into automotive engineering and learned a great deal from it.</li>
<li><b>Knowledge of Muggle world</b>: As stated above, unlike the most part of wizard community, Arthur Weasley was fascinated by the Muggles and the Muggle world; he spent his whole life studying Muggles' way of life and acquired an acceptable, although almost purely theoretical, knowledge of their world, even if he still had some gaps (for instance, he had troubles with the Muggle monetary system, particularly with banknotes). But, for instance, he learned how to drive a car.</li>
<li><b>Legal skills</b>: Working at the Ministry of Magic, supposedly Arthur developed a good knowledge of <a href="/wiki/Magical_Law" title="Magical Law">Magical Law</a>. Specifically referring to his area of specialisation, being the Muggles, he wrote the <a class="new" title="Law regarding ownership of enchanted objects (page does not exist)" rel="nofollow" data-uncrawlable-url="L3dpa2kvTGF3X3JlZ2FyZGluZ19vd25lcnNoaXBfb2ZfZW5jaGFudGVkX29iamVjdHM/YWN0aW9uPWVkaXQmcmVkbGluaz0x">Law regarding ownership of enchanted objects</a>.</li></ul>

<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Family">Family</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Arthur_Weasley?veaction=edit&amp;section=23" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Family">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Arthur_Weasley?action=edit&amp;section=23" title="Edit section: Family"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Parents_and_siblings">Parents and siblings</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Arthur_Weasley?veaction=edit&amp;section=24" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Parents and siblings">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Arthur_Weasley?action=edit&amp;section=24" title="Edit section: Parents and siblings"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<p>Arthur was the son of <a href="/wiki/Septimus_Weasley" title="Septimus Weasley">Septimus</a> and <a href="/wiki/Cedrella_Black" title="Cedrella Black">Cedrella Weasley</a>. They were both <a href="/wiki/Pure-blood" title="Pure-blood">pure-bloods</a>, although Cedrella was disowned by her family, because she married a blood-traitor. The relationship between them is unknown; it is possible that his father's point of view made Arthur realise that there was no difference between <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizards</a> and <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggles</a>. Arthur also had at least <a href="/wiki/Arthur_Weasley%27s_brothers" title="Arthur Weasley&#39;s brothers">two brothers</a>.
</p>
<h4><span id="Wife,_children_and_grandchildren"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="Wife.2C_children_and_grandchildren">Wife, children and grandchildren</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Arthur_Weasley?veaction=edit&amp;section=25" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Wife, children and grandchildren">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Arthur_Weasley?action=edit&amp;section=25" title="Edit section: Wife, children and grandchildren"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>

<p>Arthur eventually married <a href="/wiki/Molly_Weasley" title="Molly Weasley">Molly Prewett</a>, whom he had been dating since <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts</a>, and they had seven children; <a href="/wiki/William_Weasley" title="William Weasley">Bill</a>, <a href="/wiki/Charles_Weasley" title="Charles Weasley">Charlie</a>, <a href="/wiki/Percy_Weasley" title="Percy Weasley">Percy</a>, the twins <a href="/wiki/Fred_Weasley" title="Fred Weasley">Fred</a> and <a href="/wiki/George_Weasley" title="George Weasley">George Weasley</a>, <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ron</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Ginevra_Weasley" title="Ginevra Weasley">Ginny</a>. Arthur was less strict than his wife, and his children thought he was funny. He had a good relationship with all of them, before his son Percy became angry with him. However, during the <a href="/wiki/Second_Wizarding_War" title="Second Wizarding War">Second Wizarding War</a>, Arthur appeared to have been more protective, because he was afraid of what happened to his children.
</p><p>His son Bill was a very good student; he had become <a href="/wiki/Prefect" title="Prefect">prefect</a> and <a href="/wiki/Head_Boy_or_Girl" title="Head Boy or Girl">Head Boy</a> during his years at Hogwarts. He later worked for <a href="/wiki/Gringotts_Wizarding_Bank" title="Gringotts Wizarding Bank">Gringotts</a> in <a href="/wiki/Egypt" title="Egypt">Egypt</a> and married <a href="/wiki/Fleur_Delacour" title="Fleur Delacour">Fleur Delacour</a>.
</p><p>Charlie was also a good student; he was <a href="/wiki/Prefect" title="Prefect">prefect</a> and <a href="/wiki/Quidditch_Captain#Hogwarts_Quidditch_Captains" title="Quidditch Captain">Captain</a> for the <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor_Quidditch_team" title="Gryffindor Quidditch team">Gryffindor Quidditch team</a>. He studied <a href="/wiki/Dragon" title="Dragon">dragons</a> in <a href="/wiki/Romania" title="Romania">Romania</a>, but he never married.
</p><p>He also had a good relationship with Percy, but their relationship was broken up in 1995. Percy was more serious than his father, and they both worked for the <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">Ministry of Magic</a>, although Percy loved to obey the rules and was also very ambitious. Arthur forgave his son in 1998, during the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a> when Percy was ready to apologise for his previous actions.
</p><p>His sense of humour was similar to Fred and George's. He loved them very much, and, although they were not model students, Arthur knew they were very clever wizards. Though not as strict towards their mischievous behaviour, a few rare exceptions are when they attempted an <a href="/wiki/Unbreakable_Vow" title="Unbreakable Vow">Unbreakable Vow</a> with Ron in their earlier years, in 1994 when they left a <a href="/wiki/Ton-Tongue_Toffee" title="Ton-Tongue Toffee">Ton-Tongue Toffee</a> for <a href="/wiki/Dudley_Dursley" title="Dudley Dursley">Dudley Dursley</a> to have, and in 1995, when he agreed with his wife on their irresponsible use of magic ever since they had <a href="/wiki/Trace" title="Trace">come of age</a>. Fred was killed during the Battle of Hogwarts; Arthur was grief-stricken over his death.
</p>

<p>Arthur was very protective of Ron and Ginny, who were his youngest children. Ginny was the youngest and the only daughter. When she was possessed by a memory of <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Tom Marvolo Riddle</a>, in a diary, Arthur was very upset; he shouted at Ginny, saying that he had told her not to trust magical objects that had a mind of their own. He also helped Ron to his mission; he hexed the <a href="/wiki/Weasley_family_ghoul" title="Weasley family ghoul">family ghoul</a>, along with Fred and George, in order to make it look like Ron, who had gone to help Harry and Hermione find and destroy Voldemort's <a href="/wiki/Horcrux" title="Horcrux">Horcruxes</a>.
</p><p>After the Second Wizarding War, Arthur's family extended even more, through his children; he appeared to have at least twelve grandchildren: <a href="/wiki/Victoire_Weasley" title="Victoire Weasley">Victoire</a>, <a href="/wiki/Dominique_Weasley" title="Dominique Weasley">Dominique</a> and <a href="/wiki/Louis_Weasley" title="Louis Weasley">Louis</a>, who were the children of Bill and Fleur; <a href="/wiki/Molly_Weasley_II" title="Molly Weasley II">Molly</a> and <a href="/wiki/Lucy_Weasley" title="Lucy Weasley">Lucy</a>, Percy and <a href="/wiki/Audrey_Weasley" title="Audrey Weasley">Audrey</a>'s children; <a href="/wiki/Fred_Weasley_II" title="Fred Weasley II">Fred</a> and <a href="/wiki/Roxanne_Weasley" title="Roxanne Weasley">Roxanne</a> who were the children of George and <a href="/wiki/Angelina_Johnson" title="Angelina Johnson">Angelina Johnson</a>; <a href="/wiki/Rose_Granger-Weasley" title="Rose Granger-Weasley">Rose</a> and <a href="/wiki/Hugo_Granger-Weasley" title="Hugo Granger-Weasley">Hugo</a>, who were Ron and Hermione's children; and <a href="/wiki/James_Potter_II" title="James Potter II">James</a>, <a href="/wiki/Albus_Potter" title="Albus Potter">Albus</a> and <a href="/wiki/Lily_Potter" title="Lily Potter">Lily</a>, who were Ginny and Harry's children. He had a good relationship with his grandchildren, but, according to Ron (who may or may not have been joking), he would not let them marry any pure-bloods.
</p>

<p>Harry Potter was Ron's best friend. He met Arthur in <a href="/wiki/1992" title="1992">1992</a>, at <a href="/wiki/The_Burrow" title="The Burrow">the Burrow</a>. Arthur liked to discuss <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggle</a> things with Harry Potter and had entrusted Harry with certain information that many felt him too young to understand, such as in <a href="/wiki/1993" title="1993">1993</a> when the <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">Ministry</a> would not tell Harry that they thought <a href="/wiki/Sirius_Black" title="Sirius Black">Sirius Black</a> was going to seek him out. Harry had also saved Arthur's life once when Harry saw him being attacked by <a href="/wiki/Nagini" title="Nagini">Nagini</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Department_of_Mysteries" title="Department of Mysteries">Department of Mysteries</a> before Christmas of <a href="/wiki/1995" title="1995">1995</a>. They fought together at the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_the_Seven_Potters" title="Battle of the Seven Potters">Battle of the Seven Potters</a> and the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a>, and both survived the <a href="/wiki/Second_Wizarding_War" title="Second Wizarding War">Second Wizarding War</a>.
</p><p>As Molly once said that Harry was like a son to her, it is possible that Arthur felt the same way. Arthur became like a father to Harry. Harry married Arthur's daughter Ginny and became Arthur's only son-in-law. He also had three children with Ginny, who are some of Arthur's many grandchildren: James, Albus, and Lily.
</p>

<p>Because of her friendships with <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ron</a> and <a href="/wiki/Ginevra_Weasley" title="Ginevra Weasley">Ginny</a>, Hermione Granger became friendly with the entire <a href="/wiki/Weasley_family" title="Weasley family">Weasley family</a>. She spent a portion of many of her summer and <a href="/wiki/Christmas_holidays" title="Christmas holidays">Christmas holidays</a> at their home of <a href="/wiki/The_Burrow" title="The Burrow">the Burrow</a> and attended some family outings with them, such as shopping for school supplies in <a href="/wiki/Diagon_Alley" title="Diagon Alley">Diagon Alley</a> and attending the <a href="/wiki/1994" title="1994">1994</a> <a href="/wiki/Quidditch_World_Cup" title="Quidditch World Cup">Quidditch World Cup</a> with them. 
</p><p>Arthur was fascinated by the <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggle</a> world and thus Hermione's background and even invited Hermione's <a href="/wiki/Granger_family" title="Granger family">parents</a> for a drink upon first meeting them in Diagon alley before the start of her second year. He seemed to like Hermione, as if she were his daughter. Hermione married Ron, and became one of Arthur's many daughters-in-law. She also had two children with Ron, who were Arthur's grandchildren; Rose and Hugo.
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Albus_Dumbledore">Albus Dumbledore</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Arthur_Weasley?veaction=edit&amp;section=28" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Albus Dumbledore">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Arthur_Weasley?action=edit&amp;section=28" title="Edit section: Albus Dumbledore"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>

<p>Arthur considered Albus Dumbledore as being the greatest wizard of all time. Dumbledore was the <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts</a> headmaster at the time Arthur attended Hogwarts. They were both sorted into <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor" title="Gryffindor">Gryffindor</a> house and appeared to be on friendly terms. They both believed in the equality of all wizards and Muggles. Dumbledore seemed to like most people, especially the Weasleys, because they were all fair and good people. Arthur also served in the <a href="/wiki/Order_of_the_Phoenix" title="Order of the Phoenix">Order of the Phoenix</a> during the <a href="/wiki/Second_Wizarding_War" title="Second Wizarding War">Second Wizarding War</a> with Dumbledore, who was the leader. When Arthur was bitten by <a href="/wiki/Nagini" title="Nagini">Nagini</a>, Dumbledore was upset; he helped find him, using the portraits that were hung at the office of Dumbledore. 
</p><p>In <a href="/wiki/1997" title="1997">1997</a>, Arthur learned about Albus Dumbledore's <a href="/wiki/Death" title="Death">death</a> and that <a href="/wiki/Severus_Snape" title="Severus Snape">Severus Snape</a> was the murderer. Arthur was very sad and sat with his family, the professors, and other students, discussing how Snape killed Dumbledore. In fact, Dumbledore, who was about to die as a result of wearing a <a href="/wiki/Marvolo_Gaunt%27s_Ring" title="Marvolo Gaunt&#39;s Ring">cursed ring</a>, had asked Snape to kill him, because Dumbledore did not want the other <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a> to guess Snape's true loyalties, did not want <a href="/wiki/Draco_Malfoy" title="Draco Malfoy">Draco Malfoy</a> to become a murderer, and did not want to die a painful, drawn-out death.
</p>

<figure class="thumb tright show-info-icon" style="width: 152px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/d/d7/Cornelius_Fudge.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20101227025410" class="image"><img alt="" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw%3D%3D" decoding="async" width="150" height="239" class="thumbimage lazyload" data-image-name="Cornelius Fudge.jpg" data-image-key="Cornelius_Fudge.jpg" data-src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/d/d7/Cornelius_Fudge.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/150?cb=20101227025410" /></a> 	<noscript><a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/d/d7/Cornelius_Fudge.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20101227025410" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/d/d7/Cornelius_Fudge.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/150?cb=20101227025410" decoding="async" width="150" height="239" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="Cornelius Fudge.jpg" data-image-key="Cornelius_Fudge.jpg" data-src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/d/d7/Cornelius_Fudge.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/150?cb=20101227025410" /></a></noscript> 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:Cornelius_Fudge.jpg" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption"><a href="/wiki/Cornelius_Fudge" title="Cornelius Fudge">Cornelius Fudge</a>, former British Minister of Magic</p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure>
<p>As Arthur was a lower-class person and having a poor family, Cornelius Fudge always neglected Arthur while having a manipulated friendship with his wealthy rival, <a href="/wiki/Lucius_Malfoy" title="Lucius Malfoy">Lucius Malfoy</a>, due to Fudge's swaying of money and power. Fudge also happened to consider Arthur a weirdo because of his obsession with <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggles</a>, causing Fudge to think he lacks proper wizarding pride, thus holding Arthur back within the <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">Ministry</a>. 
</p><p>When <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a> had returned, Fudge suspected Arthur being in league with Dumbledore since he was aware that the two of them were friendly. He even invited Arthur's son, <a href="/wiki/Percy_Weasley" title="Percy Weasley">Percy</a> to become his Junior Assistant in an attempt to spy on the Weasley family and Dumbledore, which led to Percy defecting from his family after a row with Arthur. Fudge ignored Arthur and Harry's existences when he and the <a href="/wiki/Wizengamot" title="Wizengamot">Wizengamot</a> exited <a href="/wiki/Courtroom_Ten" title="Courtroom Ten">Courtroom Ten</a>. After Fudge was disgraced and forced to resign, Arthur was instantly promoted by Fudge's successor.
</p>

<p>Arthur and Lucius Malfoy despised each other, often exchanging insults when they met and even getting into a fistfight on at least one occasion. Lucius disdained the <a href="/wiki/Weasley_family" title="Weasley family">Weasley family</a> for their poverty and so-called <a href="/wiki/Blood_traitor" title="Blood traitor">blood traitor</a> status; while the <a href="/wiki/Malfoy_family" title="Malfoy family">Malfoys</a> prized their <a href="/wiki/Pure-blood" title="Pure-blood">pure-blood</a> heritage, the Weasleys accepted <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggles</a> and <a href="/wiki/Muggle-born" title="Muggle-born">Muggle-borns</a>. He and Arthur apparently clashed multiple times over this at the <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">Ministry of Magic</a>, where Arthur worked and Lucius visited regularly to see Minister for Magic, Cornelius Fudge.
</p><p>When Arthur proposed the <a href="/wiki/Muggle_Protection_Act" title="Muggle Protection Act">Muggle Protection Act</a> in <a href="/wiki/1992" title="1992">1992</a>, Lucius was furious to the point of trying to sabotage Arthur by placing an <a href="/wiki/T._M._Riddle%27s_Diary" title="T. M. Riddle&#39;s Diary">old diary</a> of <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Tom Marvolo Riddle</a>’s on Arthur’s eleven-year-old daughter, <a href="/wiki/Ginevra_Weasley" title="Ginevra Weasley">Ginny</a>, endangering her life as well as those of Muggle-born <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts</a> students, as this led to the opening of the <a href="/wiki/Chamber_of_Secrets" title="Chamber of Secrets">Chamber of Secrets</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-COS_17-11" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COS-17">&#91;17&#93;</a></sup> Also, Arthur never believed Lucius' claim that he had been under the <a href="/wiki/Imperius_Curse" title="Imperius Curse">Imperius Curse</a> during the <a href="/wiki/First_Wizarding_War" title="First Wizarding War">First Wizarding War</a>, indicating that their enmity likely dates back to their younger years. In 2017, Ron stated that Arthur would be very angry if <a href="/wiki/Rose_Granger-Weasley" title="Rose Granger-Weasley">Rose</a> married Lucius's grandson, mainly because the latter was a pure-blood.
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Order_of_the_Phoenix">Order of the Phoenix</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Arthur_Weasley?veaction=edit&amp;section=31" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Order of the Phoenix">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Arthur_Weasley?action=edit&amp;section=31" title="Edit section: Order of the Phoenix"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<figure class="thumb tright show-info-icon" style="width: 253px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/5/58/Order_of_the_Phoenix.png/revision/latest?cb=20140511035621" class="image"><img alt="" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw%3D%3D" decoding="async" width="251" height="105" class="thumbimage lazyload" data-image-name="Order of the Phoenix.png" data-image-key="Order_of_the_Phoenix.png" data-src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/5/58/Order_of_the_Phoenix.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/251?cb=20140511035621" /></a> 	<noscript><a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/5/58/Order_of_the_Phoenix.png/revision/latest?cb=20140511035621" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/5/58/Order_of_the_Phoenix.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/251?cb=20140511035621" decoding="async" width="251" height="105" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="Order of the Phoenix.png" data-image-key="Order_of_the_Phoenix.png" data-src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/5/58/Order_of_the_Phoenix.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/251?cb=20140511035621" /></a></noscript> 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:Order_of_the_Phoenix.png" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption"><a href="/wiki/Order_of_the_Phoenix" title="Order of the Phoenix">Order of the Phoenix</a>, his allies</p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure>
<p>Arthur had a good relationship with most members of the Order of the Phoenix. Arthur was not an official member of the first order, but he became a member of the second order during the <a href="/wiki/Second_Wizarding_War" title="Second Wizarding War">Second Wizarding War</a>.
</p><p>He seemed to know <a href="/wiki/Sirius_Black" title="Sirius Black">Sirius Black</a> since the <a href="/wiki/First_Wizarding_War" title="First Wizarding War">First Wizarding War</a>. After the war, Sirius was imprisoned in <a href="/wiki/Azkaban" title="Azkaban">Azkaban</a> for betraying the Potters to <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Voldemort</a> and killing <a href="/wiki/Peter_Pettigrew%27s_victims" class="mw-redirect" title="Peter Pettigrew&#39;s victims">twelve Muggles</a>. In fact, <a href="/wiki/Peter_Pettigrew" title="Peter Pettigrew">Peter Pettigrew</a> had committed the crimes and blamed Sirius. Arthur believed that when Sirius escaped from the prison, he wanted to kill <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>. He told Harry not to try to find Sirius, as he knew that Harry would learn about Sirius's crimes. Harry, Ron, and Hermione learned the truth in <a href="/wiki/1994" title="1994">1994</a>, and Pettigrew escaped again. Arthur and Sirius became friendly in the second Order. They lived in the same house for a short time; Sirius also tried to comfort Arthur's children when Arthur was bitten by <a href="/wiki/Nagini" title="Nagini">Nagini</a>. Sirius was killed by his cousin <a href="/wiki/Bellatrix_Lestrange" title="Bellatrix Lestrange">Bellatrix Lestrange</a>, during the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_the_Department_of_Mysteries" title="Battle of the Department of Mysteries">Battle of the Department of Mysteries</a>. Arthur's first reaction is unknown; he only tried not to talk about Sirius in front of Harry, knowing how that would make the boy feel. Arthur and Sirius were also distant relatives, through Arthur's mother.
</p><p>Arthur appeared to be on good terms with <a href="/wiki/Remus_Lupin" title="Remus Lupin">Remus Lupin</a>, too. Remus was a <a href="/wiki/Werewolf" title="Werewolf">werewolf</a>, but Arthur did not care about that; he thought Lupin was a good person. Furthermore, they had the same beliefs about blood purity, and they were both poor. Lupin was the <a href="/wiki/Defence_Against_the_Dark_Arts" title="Defence Against the Dark Arts">Defence Against the Dark Arts</a> professor at Hogwarts for a short time when Ron, Ginny, Fred, George and Percy were attending the school. They all thought Remus was a good teacher. Arthur and Remus became members of the Order during the <a href="/wiki/Second_Wizarding_War" title="Second Wizarding War">Second Wizarding War</a>; they fought together at the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_the_Seven_Potters" title="Battle of the Seven Potters">Battle of the Seven Potters</a> and the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a>; during the final battle, Remus was killed. His body lied near Fred's, and the Weasley family looked very sad by both deaths.
</p><p>Arthur seemed to like his daughter-in-law, <a href="/wiki/Fleur_Delacour" title="Fleur Delacour">Fleur Delacour</a>. Unlike his wife and daughter, Arthur did not dislike her; after the war, Arthur had three grandchildren through Fleur.
</p><p><a href="/wiki/Rubeus_Hagrid" title="Rubeus Hagrid">Rubeus Hagrid</a> was a member of the Order and very loyal to Dumbledore. He seemed to know the Weasleys since their earlier years, as they appeared to be friends in <a href="/wiki/1992" title="1992">1992</a>. Hagrid told Arthur not to argue with <a href="/wiki/Lucius_Malfoy" title="Lucius Malfoy">Lucius Malfoy</a>, believing that he was not worth it. Hagrid was also the <a href="/wiki/Care_of_Magical_Creatures" title="Care of Magical Creatures">Care of Magical Creatures</a> professor of Ron and Ginny. The whole Weasley family appeared to be friendly with Hagrid. They were members of the Order who fought together at the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_the_Seven_Potters" title="Battle of the Seven Potters">Battle of the Seven Potters</a> and the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a>. They both survived the war.
</p><p>Arthur had a high opinion of <a href="/wiki/Alastor_Moody" title="Alastor Moody">Alastor Moody</a>, whom he had met at the <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">Ministry of Magic</a>. He thought Moody was a little mad but a very powerful and brave Auror, who had captured dozens of <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a>. They were friends, as well as members of the second Order. Arthur's children thought Moody was mad, too. Moody led the group of order members that fought at the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_the_Seven_Potters" title="Battle of the Seven Potters">Battle of the Seven Potters</a>. Moody was killed by <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a> during the battle. Arthur was sad about his death.
</p><p>Arthur also seemed to be on good terms with <a href="/wiki/Severus_Snape" title="Severus Snape">Severus Snape</a>, despite the fact that Snape disliked his children. However, when he learned that Snape killed Dumbledore, he and his family obviously turned against him but that was most likely retracted when he learned of Snape's true loyalties from Harry. In addition, one of Arthur's grandchildren, <a href="/wiki/Albus_Potter" title="Albus Potter">Albus Potter</a>, was named after Snape. 
</p><p>Arthur was also friendly with <a href="/wiki/Nymphadora_Tonks" title="Nymphadora Tonks">Nymphadora Tonks</a>. Tonks was a young woman, as well as an Auror. Arthur and Tonks also knew each other through the Ministry of Magic. During the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_the_Seven_Potters" title="Battle of the Seven Potters">Battle of the Seven Potters</a>, Tonks was responsible for Ron, they were paired together. Tonks managed to save herself and Ron from <a href="/wiki/Bellatrix_Lestrange" title="Bellatrix Lestrange">Bellatrix Lestrange</a>, and then Arthur thanked Tonks. She was eventually murdered by Bellatrix during the Battle of Hogwarts. Her dead body was put near Fred and Lupin's, and the Weasleys looked very sad by the deaths of all of them. It is unknown if he had met her son <a href="/wiki/Edward_Lupin" title="Edward Lupin">Teddy Lupin</a>, but considering how Teddy was the godson of Arthur's son-in-law Harry, coupled with the fact that by 2017 Teddy was dating Arthur's granddaughter Victoire, it is possible that Teddy and Arthur were on good terms.
</p><p>He appeared to be a friend of <a href="/wiki/Kingsley_Shacklebolt" title="Kingsley Shacklebolt">Kingsley Shacklebolt</a>, as well. They were only members of the second Order. They fought together in many battles and survived the <a href="/wiki/Second_Wizarding_War" title="Second Wizarding War">Second Wizarding War</a>.
</p><p>Many members of the Order were killed during battles in both wars; it is likely Arthur got on well with them.

<ul><li>Arthur is portrayed by <a href="/wiki/Great_Britain" title="Great Britain">British</a> actor <a href="/wiki/Mark_Williams" title="Mark Williams">Mark Williams</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_(film_series)" title="Harry Potter (film series)">film adaptations</a> of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Chamber_of_Secrets_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets (film)">Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets</a></i>, <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Prisoner_of_Azkaban_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban (film)">Prisoner of Azkaban</a></i>, <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Goblet_of_Fire_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire (film)">Goblet of Fire</a></i>, <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Order_of_the_Phoenix_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix (film)">Order of the Phoenix</a></i>, <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Half-Blood_Prince_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince (film)">Half-Blood Prince</a></i> and <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Deathly_Hallows:_Part_1" title="Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 1">Deathly Hallows: Part 1</a></i> and <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Deathly_Hallows:_Part_2" title="Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 2">Part 2</a></i>.</li>
<li>Arthur was originally intended to die in either the fifth or seventh book (particularly when <a href="/wiki/Nagini" title="Nagini">Nagini</a> attacked him), but J. K. Rowling decided against it, because he was "one of the few good fathers in the series" and because she didn't want to turn Ron into "half a Harry" i.e. losing one of his parents. Doing so would remove Ron's light hearted comic relief, as most of his humour stems from his immaturity and insensitivity. <a href="/wiki/Remus_Lupin" title="Remus Lupin">Remus Lupin</a> and <a href="/wiki/Nymphadora_Tonks" title="Nymphadora Tonks">Nymphadora Tonks</a>, two other good parents, were killed off to compensate for this.<sup id="cite_ref-22" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-22">&#91;22&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li>There may be a pattern in the <a href="/wiki/Weasley_family" title="Weasley family">Weasley family</a> of naming after characters from Arthurian Legend. King Arthur was a legendary British ruler; the names "Percy" and "Ginny" may also be derived from the legendary figures of Percival and Guinevere, respectively. Ron could have originated from the name of King Arthur's spear.</li>
<li>In the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Chamber_of_Secrets_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets (film)">film adaptation</a> of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Chamber_of_Secrets" title="Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets">Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets</a></i>, he comments to Mr and Mrs Granger "I understand other Muggles are quite afraid of you." This refers to a common Muggle phobia of visiting the <a href="/wiki/Dentist" title="Dentist">dentist</a>, as Mr and Mrs Granger are both dentists.</li>
<li>In the films, Arthur is shown without glasses, and he is not balding, as he is described in the books. In fact, Arthur's hair appears to lengthen as the film series progresses.</li>
<li>He also wears bow-ties in the films, something which was not acknowledged in the novels.</li>
<li>In the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Prisoner_of_Azkaban_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban (film)">film adaptation</a> of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Prisoner_of_Azkaban" title="Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban">Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban</a></i>, when Arthur is telling Harry about <a href="/wiki/Sirius_Black" title="Sirius Black">Sirius Black</a>, Harry uses the name "Voldemort" and Arthur tells him not to say the name; but in the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Half-Blood_Prince_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince (film)">film adaptation</a> of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Half-Blood_Prince" title="Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince">Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince</a></i>, when Arthur is talking to Harry about Draco Malfoy, Arthur <i>actually says</i> the name "Voldemort."</li>
<li>In the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Goblet_of_Fire_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire (film)">film adaptation</a> of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Goblet_of_Fire" title="Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire">Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire</a></i>, Arthur Weasley attended the third task for the <a href="/wiki/Triwizard_Tournament" title="Triwizard Tournament">Triwizard Tournament</a>. In the book, Arthur was notably the only one of the older Weasley family members that did not attend any of the tasks, with Charlie managing the dragons in the first task, Percy filling in for <a href="/wiki/Bartemius_Crouch_Senior" title="Bartemius Crouch Senior">Crouch Snr</a> in the second task, and Molly and Bill attending as part of the audience during the third task. These four were cut from the film.</li>
<li>In the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Deathly_Hallows:_Part_1_(video_game)" title="Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 1 (video game)">video game adaptation</a> of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Deathly_Hallows:_Part_1" title="Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 1">Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 1</a></i>, it is Arthur who reads <a href="/wiki/Albus_Dumbledore" title="Albus Dumbledore">Dumbledore</a>'s <a href="/wiki/The_Last_Will_and_Testament_of_Albus_Percival_Wulfric_Brian_Dumbledore" title="The Last Will and Testament of Albus Percival Wulfric Brian Dumbledore">will</a> and gives the trio what was left for them, rather than <a href="/wiki/Rufus_Scrimgeour" title="Rufus Scrimgeour">Rufus Scrimgeour</a>.</li>
<li>It is revealed in the sixth book that his greatest ambition is learning how aeroplanes stay up.</li>
<li>Based on how Arthur developed his skills in automotive engineering and other skills in muggle machinery, it can be assumed that he has a kinesthetic learning style - in that he learns best by doing rather than simply analysing. This is supported by his very hands-on way of going about things, somewhat overly enthusiastic personality, and borderline need to challenge himself.</li></ul>
