<b>Sickleworth</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Niffler" title="Niffler">Niffler</a> owned by <a href="/wiki/Gringotts_Wizarding_Bank" title="Gringotts Wizarding Bank">Gringotts Wizarding Bank</a>, assigned to <a href="/wiki/Patricia_Rakepick" title="Patricia Rakepick">Patricia Rakepick</a> to aid in her assignments by burrowing underground in search of treasure at <a href="/wiki/Curse" title="Curse">cursed</a> sites. Rakepick chose his name because he is <i>"incredibly sweet, and utterly destructive."</i><sup id="cite_ref-HMy4ch6_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HMy4ch6-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Sickleworth?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Sickleworth?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>In the <a href="/wiki/1987%E2%80%931988_school_year" title="1987–1988 school year">1987–1988 school year</a>, Rakepick allowed Professor <a href="/wiki/Silvanus_Kettleburn" title="Silvanus Kettleburn">Silvanus Kettleburn</a> to borrow Sickleworth for a <a href="/wiki/Care_of_Magical_Creatures" title="Care of Magical Creatures">Care of Magical Creatures</a> class. Rakepick also allowed <a href="/wiki/Jacob%27s_sibling" title="Jacob&#39;s sibling">Jacob's sibling</a> to temporarily use the Niffler.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>The <a href="/wiki/1988%E2%80%931989_school_year" title="1988–1989 school year">following year</a>, Sickleworth was present in <a href="/wiki/Defence_Against_the_Dark_Arts" title="Defence Against the Dark Arts">Defence Against the Dark Arts</a> classes taught by Rakepick. During these classes, he occasionally let the <a href="/wiki/Pixie" title="Pixie">Pixies</a> escape.<sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> After Rakepick left the school, she left Sickleworth behind.<sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>The following year, Jacob's sibling spotted Sickleworth in the <a href="/wiki/Clock_Tower_Courtyard" title="Clock Tower Courtyard">Clock Tower Courtyard</a>, carrying a white quill with him. Jacob's sibling followed the Niffler to Jacob's room, where they and <a href="/wiki/Jacob_(Hogwarts_student)" title="Jacob (Hogwarts student)">Jacob</a> found the Niffler and took the quill, which was a message for Jacob's sibling.<sup id="cite_ref-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
