<p><b>Dennis Creevey</b> (b. <a href="/wiki/1982" title="1982">1982</a>/<a href="/wiki/1983" title="1983">1983</a>) was a <a href="/wiki/Muggle-born" title="Muggle-born">Muggle-born</a> <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> and the younger brother of <a href="/wiki/Colin_Creevey" title="Colin Creevey">Colin Creevey</a>. He was a student at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> three years below the famous <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>. Like his brother, he was <a href="/wiki/Sorting_ceremony" title="Sorting ceremony">Sorted</a> into <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor" title="Gryffindor">Gryffindor</a>. His <a href="/wiki/Colin_Creevey%27s_father" class="mw-redirect" title="Colin Creevey&#39;s father">father</a> was a milkman. In his <a href="/wiki/Second_year" title="Second year">second year</a>, he joined <a href="/wiki/Dumbledore%27s_Army" title="Dumbledore&#39;s Army">Dumbledore's Army</a>, an organisation led and taught by Harry Potter.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Dennis_Creevey?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Dennis_Creevey?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span id="Early_life_(1982-1994)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_life_.281982-1994.29">Early life <span class="template-c">(1982-1994)</span></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Dennis_Creevey?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Early life (1982-1994)">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Dennis_Creevey?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Early life (1982-1994)"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Dennis was born into the <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggle</a> <a href="/wiki/Creevey_family" title="Creevey family">Creevey family</a>. Dennis' father was a milkman, and his mother was also a Muggle. In <a href="/wiki/1992" title="1992">1992</a> Dennis' older brother, <a href="/wiki/Colin_Creevey" title="Colin Creevey">Colin Creevey</a>, received a <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_acceptance_letter" title="Hogwarts acceptance letter">letter</a> from <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> informing his family that Colin was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a>, something which came of a shock to the family as Colin was a <a href="/wiki/Muggle-born" title="Muggle-born">Muggle-born</a>. Colin and his family were fascinated with this discovery, and it struck Colin's love of taking photos, most likely so he could show his Muggle family all about his life in the <a href="/wiki/Wizarding_world" title="Wizarding world">wizarding world</a>. Dennis and his family would have gone through some stress over their son Colin's first year at Hogwarts as he was <a href="/wiki/Petrification" title="Petrification">petrified</a> by a <a href="/wiki/Salazar_Slytherin%27s_Basilisk" class="mw-redirect" title="Salazar Slytherin&#39;s Basilisk">Basilisk</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-COS_5-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COS-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Two years later in <a href="/wiki/1994" title="1994">1994</a>, Dennis also received a letter from Hogwarts, something which was very rare for a Muggle family: to bear two magically-gifted children.
</p>
<h3><span id="Education_at_Hogwarts_(1994-1997)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="Education_at_Hogwarts_.281994-1997.29">Education at Hogwarts <span class="template-c">(1994-1997)</span></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Dennis_Creevey?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Education at Hogwarts (1994-1997)">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Dennis_Creevey?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Education at Hogwarts (1994-1997)"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="First_year">First year</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Dennis_Creevey?veaction=edit&amp;section=4" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: First year">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Dennis_Creevey?action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: First year"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<div class="quote">
<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire">"<i>Guess what, Harry? My brother's starting! My brother Dennis!</i>"</span></dd>
<dd>&#8212;The start of Dennis' education at Hogwarts<sup class="noprint"><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Goblet_of_Fire" title="Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire">[src]</a></sup></dd>
<dd></dd></dl></div><p>After receiving his surprise letter of acceptance into <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a>, Dennis was excited to finally attend Hogwarts in the <a href="/wiki/1994-1995_school_year" class="mw-redirect" title="1994-1995 school year">1994-1995 school year</a> and his brother <a href="/wiki/Colin_Creevey" title="Colin Creevey">Colin Creevey</a> was very anxious for his brother to arrive and be <a href="/wiki/Sorting_Hat" title="Sorting Hat">Sorted</a>. Whilst crossing the <a href="/wiki/Great_Lake" title="Great Lake">school lake</a> as <a href="/wiki/First_year" title="First year">First year</a>'s traditionally do with <a href="/wiki/Rubeus_Hagrid" title="Rubeus Hagrid">Rubeus Hagrid</a>, Dennis fell into the lake, but he was rescued by a creature that Colin guessed was the <a href="/wiki/Giant_Squid" title="Giant Squid">Giant Squid</a>. After finally getting to the castle, dripping wet, Dennis was sorted into <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor" title="Gryffindor">Gryffindor</a>, the same house as his older brother Colin and Colin's hero, <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-GOF_2-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Dennis' first year at Hogwarts was somewhat different to that of a regular first year, since Hogwarts was the hosting grounds for the <a href="/wiki/Triwizard_Tournament" title="Triwizard Tournament">Triwizard Tournament</a>. Dennis witnessed the selection of both <a href="/wiki/Cedric_Diggory" title="Cedric Diggory">Cedric Diggory</a> and Harry Potter as school champions. Throughout the year Dennis would have been learning first-year magic and watching the tasks of the tournament. During the final task, <a href="/wiki/Hufflepuff" title="Hufflepuff">Hufflepuff</a> champion Cedric was murdered by <a href="/wiki/Peter_Pettigrew" title="Peter Pettigrew">Peter Pettigrew</a> under the direct orders of the newly restored to power <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a>. The claims of Voldemort's return meant worrying times for Dennis and his brother as they were <a href="/wiki/Muggle-born" title="Muggle-born">Muggle-born</a>, and Muggle-borns had been hunted during the <a href="/wiki/First_Wizarding_War" title="First Wizarding War">First Wizarding War</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-GOF_2-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Second_year">Second year</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Dennis_Creevey?veaction=edit&amp;section=5" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Second year">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Dennis_Creevey?action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Second year"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>

<p>In his <a href="/wiki/1995-1996_school_year" class="mw-redirect" title="1995-1996 school year">second year</a> at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts</a>, the school was put under the eyes of the <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">Ministry of Magic</a>, through <a href="/wiki/Dolores_Umbridge" title="Dolores Umbridge">Dolores Umbridge</a> being appointed <a href="/wiki/Defence_Against_the_Dark_Arts" title="Defence Against the Dark Arts">Defence Against the Dark Arts</a> <a href="/wiki/Professor" title="Professor">professor</a> and <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_High_Inquisitor" title="Hogwarts High Inquisitor">High Inquisitor</a>. During her stay Umbridge made student's education unpleasant, she enforced "medieval" forms of punishment and insisted on teaching only defensive magical theory. This provoked <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione Granger</a> to create an organisation known as <a href="/wiki/Dumbledore%27s_Army" title="Dumbledore&#39;s Army">Dumbledore's Army</a> with the help of her friends <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ron Weasley</a> and <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>, who would act as instructor to the group.<sup id="cite_ref-OOP_6-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOP-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Both Dennis and his brother Colin joined this group. Dennis broke school rules when he sneaked out and followed his brother Colin on one of the <a href="/wiki/Hogsmeade_weekend_trips" title="Hogsmeade weekend trips">Hogsmeade weekend trips</a> to go to the <a href="/wiki/Hog%27s_Head" class="mw-redirect" title="Hog&#39;s Head">Hog's Head</a> for the first D.A. meeting. His roommate <a href="/wiki/Nigel_Wolpert" title="Nigel Wolpert">Nigel Wolpert</a> would later join the D.A. as well. By the end of the year, it was confirmed that <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a> had indeed returned by the Ministry of Magic, following the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_the_Department_of_Mysteries" title="Battle of the Department of Mysteries">Battle of the Department of Mysteries</a> and therefore the <a href="/wiki/Second_Wizarding_War" title="Second Wizarding War">Second Wizarding War</a> had now officially started.<sup id="cite_ref-OOP_6-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOP-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

So much so that several students were being pulled out of education for their safety. With Dennis and his brother <a href="/wiki/Colin_Creevey" title="Colin Creevey">Colin</a> being Muggle-born, they would especially be in living fear.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP_7-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span id="Pulled_out_of_education_(1997-1998)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="Pulled_out_of_education_.281997-1998.29">Pulled out of education <span class="template-c">(1997-1998)</span></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Dennis_Creevey?veaction=edit&amp;section=7" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Pulled out of education (1997-1998)">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Dennis_Creevey?action=edit&amp;section=7" title="Edit section: Pulled out of education (1997-1998)"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>After his finishing the 1996–1997 school year Dennis and his brother Colin were both pulled out of education given the serious events that took place the following school year, their parents did not feel safe having them return for the next <a href="/wiki/1997%E2%80%931998_school_year" title="1997–1998 school year">school year</a> at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts</a> which was indirectly being run by <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a> through his followers, <a href="/wiki/Severus_Snape" title="Severus Snape">Severus Snape</a> and <a href="/wiki/Alecto_Carrow" title="Alecto Carrow">Alecto</a> and <a href="/wiki/Amycus_Carrow" title="Amycus Carrow">Amycus Carrow</a> now that Albus Dumbledore had been murdered. With control of Hogwarts, Voldemort also took control indirectly of the <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">Ministry of Magic</a> by appointing a puppet-minister after <a href="/wiki/Rufus_Scrimgeour" title="Rufus Scrimgeour">Rufus Scrimgeour</a> was killed. Now that the Ministry was in the hands of Voldemort a <a href="/wiki/Muggle-Born_Registration_Commission" title="Muggle-Born Registration Commission">Muggle-Born Registration Commission</a> was opened up, run by <a href="/wiki/Dolores_Umbridge" title="Dolores Umbridge">Dolores Umbridge</a>, against whom Dennis had rebelled in his second year. This meant that <a href="/wiki/Muggle-born" title="Muggle-born">Muggle-borns</a> and their families went on the run.<sup id="cite_ref-DH_8-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Seeking_refuge">Seeking refuge</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Dennis_Creevey?veaction=edit&amp;section=8" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Seeking refuge">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Dennis_Creevey?action=edit&amp;section=8" title="Edit section: Seeking refuge"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<div class="quote">
<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows">"<i>Harry glanced down, and felt another dull blow to his stomach: Colin Creevey, though under-age must have sneaked back just as Malfoy, Crabbe and Goyle had done. He was tiny in death.</i>"</span></dd>
<dd>&#8212;Harry witnesses Oliver Wood carry Colin's dead body inside<sup class="noprint"><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Deathly_Hallows" title="Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows">[src]</a></sup></dd>
<dd></dd></dl></div><p>After being on the run for several months in <a href="/wiki/May" title="May">May</a> of <a href="/wiki/1998" title="1998">1998</a> the final conflict of the <a href="/wiki/Second_Wizarding_War" title="Second Wizarding War">Second Wizarding War</a> was to take place at Hogwarts, known as the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a>. Dennis and his family had been in hiding, but when <a href="/wiki/Neville_Longbottom" title="Neville Longbottom">Neville Longbottom</a> summoned all members of <a href="/wiki/Dumbledore%27s_Army" title="Dumbledore&#39;s Army">Dumbledore's Army</a> into battle, Colin came out of hiding and went to fight. It is unknown if Dennis also returned to fight at Hogwarts as he was not only on the run, but also under-age to even participate. During the battle his brother Colin was killed out on the school grounds, his body was later recovered by <a href="/wiki/Oliver_Wood" title="Oliver Wood">Oliver Wood</a> and Neville. Dennis' final status following the war is unknown.<sup id="cite_ref-DH_8-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup>
</p><h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Physical_appearance">Physical appearance</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Dennis_Creevey?veaction=edit&amp;section=9" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Physical appearance">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Dennis_Creevey?action=edit&amp;section=9" title="Edit section: Physical appearance"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Like his elder brother, Dennis had mousy brown hair. At the start of his education, he was the smallest boy in his year.<sup id="cite_ref-appearance_4-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-appearance-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Personality_and_traits">Personality and traits</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Dennis_Creevey?veaction=edit&amp;section=10" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Personality and traits">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Dennis_Creevey?action=edit&amp;section=10" title="Edit section: Personality and traits"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>

</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Dennis_Creevey?veaction=edit&amp;section=12" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Dennis_Creevey?action=edit&amp;section=12" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li><a target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener" class="external text" href="http://www.behindthename.com/name/dennis">Dennis</a> comes from the name <a target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener" class="external text" href="http://www.behindthename.com/name/dionysos">Dionysos</a>; In Greek mythology Dionysos was the god of wine, revelry, fertility and dance.</li>
<li>Creevey is an actual English surname. It's an Anglicised form of the Gaelic "O'Craoibhe" meaning "descendant of Craobhach", a byname meaning "curly (-headed)" or "prolific" (from "craobh" meaning "branch, bough")</li></ul>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Dennis_Creevey?veaction=edit&amp;section=13" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Dennis_Creevey?action=edit&amp;section=13" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>In a deleted scene from the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Chamber_of_Secrets_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets (film)">film adaptation</a> of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Chamber_of_Secrets" title="Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets">Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets</a></i> Colin says that no one in his family is magical except him. At that time Dennis would already be 8 or 9 years old, and magical signs typically manifest in young witches and wizards around the age of 7. However, Dennis may be an exception to this, as was the case with <a href="/wiki/Neville_Longbottom" title="Neville Longbottom">Neville Longbottom</a>. It may also be a reason why the scene was cut.</li>
<li>Although Dennis doesn't physically appear in the films, his signature is seen on the list of members in <a href="/wiki/Dumbledore%27s_Army" title="Dumbledore&#39;s Army">Dumbledore's Army</a> shown in the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Order_of_the_Phoenix_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix (film)">film adaptation</a> of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Order_of_the_Phoenix" title="Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix">Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix</a></i>, meaning that his character is present offscreen.</li></ul>
