<p><b>Heathcote Barbary</b> (b. <a href="/wiki/1974" title="1974">1974</a>) was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> and the <a href="/wiki/Rhythm_guitar" title="Rhythm guitar">rhythm guitarist</a> for the popular <a href="/wiki/Wizarding_world" title="Wizarding world">wizarding</a> band the <a href="/wiki/Weird_Sisters" title="Weird Sisters">Weird Sisters</a>. He performed at the <a href="/wiki/1994" title="1994">1994</a> <a href="/wiki/Yule_Ball" title="Yule Ball">Yule Ball</a> along with the rest of his band.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Heathcote_Barbary?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Heathcote_Barbary?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>Barbary was portrayed by <a href="/wiki/Jason_Buckle" title="Jason Buckle">Jason Buckle</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Goblet_of_Fire_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire (film)">film adaptation</a> of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Goblet_of_Fire" title="Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire">Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire</a></i>.</li>
<li>If he attended Hogwarts, he would have been in school between <a href="/wiki/1985" title="1985">1985</a> and <a href="/wiki/1992" title="1992">1992</a>, which means he would have been in his <a href="/wiki/Seventh_year" title="Seventh year">seventh year</a> when <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> was in his <a href="/wiki/First_year" title="First year">first year</a>.</li></ul>
