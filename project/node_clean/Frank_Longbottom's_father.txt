<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Frank_Longbottom%27s_father?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Frank_Longbottom%27s_father?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Mr Longbottom was born sometime before <a href="/wiki/1947" title="1947">1947</a><sup id="cite_ref-date_1-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-date-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> into the <a href="/wiki/Longbottom_family" title="Longbottom family">Longbottom family</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-longbottomfamilytree_3-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-longbottomfamilytree-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> an old <a href="/wiki/Great_Britain" title="Great Britain">British</a> <a href="/wiki/Pure-blood" title="Pure-blood">pure-blood</a> family, considered to be one of the <a href="/wiki/Sacred_Twenty-Eight" title="Sacred Twenty-Eight">Sacred Twenty-Eight</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-pm-pureblood_4-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-pm-pureblood-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> He had one known sibling, a brother, <a href="/wiki/Algie_Longbottom" title="Algie Longbottom">Algie</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-longbottomfamilytree_3-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-longbottomfamilytree-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> and may have had others.<sup id="cite_ref-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>He married a witch, <a href="/wiki/Augusta_Longbottom" title="Augusta Longbottom">Augusta</a>, with whom he had one son, <a href="/wiki/Frank_Longbottom" title="Frank Longbottom">Frank</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-longbottomfamilytree_3-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-longbottomfamilytree-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>He died sometime between <a href="/wiki/1980" title="1980">1980</a> and <a href="/wiki/1995" title="1995">1995</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-ootp21_2-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-ootp21-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-longbottomfamilytree_3-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-longbottomfamilytree-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> His grandson, <a href="/wiki/Neville_Longbottom" title="Neville Longbottom">Neville</a>, witnessed his death, thus enabling the boy to see <a href="/wiki/Thestral" title="Thestral">Thestrals</a> at a young age.<sup id="cite_ref-ootp21_2-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-ootp21-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-longbottomfamilytree_3-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-longbottomfamilytree-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> Neville revealed as much to <a href="/wiki/Rubeus_Hagrid" title="Rubeus Hagrid">Hagrid</a> and <a href="/wiki/Dolores_Umbridge" title="Dolores Umbridge">Dolores Umbridge</a> during her time evaluating a <a href="/wiki/Care_of_Magical_Creatures" title="Care of Magical Creatures">Care of Magical Creatures</a> lesson.<sup id="cite_ref-ootp21_2-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-ootp21-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Frank_Longbottom%27s_father?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Frank_Longbottom%27s_father?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>Neville states in <i><a href="/wiki/The_Queen%27s_Handbag" title="The Queen&#39;s Handbag">The Queen's Handbag</a></i> that, in <a href="/wiki/1947" title="1947">1947</a>, his "granddad" put a&#160;<a href="/wiki/Fanged_gerbil" title="Fanged gerbil">fanged gerbil</a>&#160;into Augusta's handbag as a joke, prompting her to keep a mousetrap in her bag at all times thereafter. It is likely that this refers to Mr Longbottom, rather than to Neville's maternal grandfather, i.e. <a href="/wiki/Alice_Longbottom" title="Alice Longbottom">Alice Longbottom</a>'s father.</li></ul>
