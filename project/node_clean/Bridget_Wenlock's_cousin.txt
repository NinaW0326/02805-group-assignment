<p>This <b>individual</b> (fl. <a href="/wiki/13th_century" title="13th century">13th century</a>) was a <a href="/wiki/Scotland" title="Scotland">Scottish</a> <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch</a> and the cousin of <a href="/wiki/Bridget_Wenlock" title="Bridget Wenlock">Bridget Wenlock</a>, a celebrated <a href="/wiki/Arithmancer" title="Arithmancer">Arithmancer</a>. She lived in <a href="/wiki/John_O%27Groats" title="John O&#39;Groats">John O'Groats</a>, <a href="/wiki/Caithness" title="Caithness">Caithness</a>, <a href="/wiki/Scotland" title="Scotland">Scotland</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-WBoS_1-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-WBoS-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Bridget_Wenlock%27s_cousin?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Bridget_Wenlock%27s_cousin?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p><a href="/wiki/Bridget_Wenlock" title="Bridget Wenlock">Bridget Wenlock</a> once throught she had mistakenly sent an envelope (on which she thought she had scribbled the theorem that led to her discovery of the magical properties of the number <a href="/wiki/Seven" title="Seven">seven</a> in <a href="/wiki/Invisible_Ink" title="Invisible Ink">Invisible Ink</a>) to her cousin. After chasing the owl that brought the letter and after casting a <a href="/wiki/Revealing_Charm" title="Revealing Charm">Revealing Charm</a>, Wenlock found out that the envelope contained no more than a <a href="/wiki/Cake" title="Cake">cake</a> recipe, and that she had, in fact, scrawled her calculation on a sugar packet that she had left at home.<sup id="cite_ref-WBoS_1-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-WBoS-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
