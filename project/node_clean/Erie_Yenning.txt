<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Erie_Yenning?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Erie_Yenning?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_life">Early life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Erie_Yenning?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Early life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Erie_Yenning?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Early life"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Born somewhere in the <a href="/wiki/Great_Britain" title="Great Britain">British Isles</a>, Erie Yenning was the scion of a family of unknown wizarding descent. Like most other magical children born in the wizarding community of Great Britain, they presumably attended <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> during their formative years,<sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> and may have chosen to take <a href="/wiki/Muggle_Studies" title="Muggle Studies">Muggle Studies</a>, given their future occupation.
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Working_for_the_Ministry">Working for the Ministry</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Erie_Yenning?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Working for the Ministry">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Erie_Yenning?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Working for the Ministry"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Sometime after they turned seventeen, Erie joined the&#160;<a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">Ministry of Magic</a>&#160;in&#160;<a href="/wiki/London" title="London">London</a> and became an <a href="/wiki/Officer" class="mw-redirect" title="Officer">Officer</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Department_of_Magical_Law_Enforcement" title="Department of Magical Law Enforcement">Department of Magical Law Enforcement</a>, in which capacity they worked for the&#160;<a href="/wiki/Misuse_of_Muggle_Artefacts_Office" title="Misuse of Muggle Artefacts Office">Misuse of Muggle Artefacts Office</a> along with others such as <a href="/wiki/Myra_Curio" title="Myra Curio">Myra Curio</a>. While not as involved with the <a href="/wiki/Statute_of_Secrecy_Task_Force" title="Statute of Secrecy Task Force">Statute of Secrecy Task Force</a> as said colleague, Erie did become friendly with at least one of its members, whom they encountered at the Ministry during working hours. At some point, Yenning gifted this <a href="/wiki/Calamity_Investigator" title="Calamity Investigator">Calamity Investigator</a> with a set of <a href="/wiki/Dragon" title="Dragon">Dragon</a>-fire tampered, <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggle</a>-made cauldrons that allowed for super-heated potion-making, which, while they normally would destroy most potions during the brewing process, proved to increase the effectiveness of the <a href="/wiki/Potent_Exstimulo_Potion" title="Potent Exstimulo Potion">Potent Exstimulo Potion</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-OTP7_2-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OTP7-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
