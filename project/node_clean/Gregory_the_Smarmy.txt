<p><b>Gregory the Smarmy</b> (fl. <a href="/wiki/1189" class="mw-redirect" title="1189">1189</a>) was a <a href="/wiki/Middle_Ages" title="Middle Ages">medieval</a> <a href="/wiki/Great_Britain" title="Great Britain">British</a> <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> and <a href="/wiki/Potioneer" title="Potioneer">Potioneer</a>, who invented <a href="/wiki/Gregory%27s_Unctuous_Unction" title="Gregory&#39;s Unctuous Unction">Gregory's Unctuous Unction</a>, a <a href="/wiki/Potion" title="Potion">potion</a> that makes the drinker believe whomever gave the potion is his or her best friend.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Gregory_the_Smarmy?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Gregory_the_Smarmy?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>After inventing Gregory's Unctuous Unction, Gregory allegedly used the potion to worm his way into <a href="/wiki/Richard_I" title="Richard I">King Richard's</a> confidence, earning him a fortune.<sup id="cite_ref-COSg_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COSg-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> He appears on a <a href="/wiki/Chocolate_Frog_Card" title="Chocolate Frog Card">Chocolate Frog Card</a>.
</p><p>A secret passage out of <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a>, found by <a href="/wiki/Fred_and_George_Weasley" title="Fred and George Weasley">Fred and George Weasley</a> on their first week of school, is located behind a statue of Gregory the Smarmy. Early in the <a href="/wiki/1991-1992_school_year" class="mw-redirect" title="1991-1992 school year">1991-1992 school year</a>, <a href="/wiki/Lee_Jordan" title="Lee Jordan">Lee Jordan</a> discovered a secret passage that Fred and George suspected was probably this one.<sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>A statue of Gregory the Smarmy is located on the <a href="/wiki/First-floor_corridor" title="First-floor corridor">first floor corridor</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-POAG_4-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-POAG-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>A corridor on the fifth floor of the east wing was referred as "Gregory the Smarmyâ€™s corridor". Whether this is because it held a statue of him, or some other reason, was unclear.<sup id="cite_ref-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> In early <a href="/wiki/1996" title="1996">1996</a>, Fred and George set off a <a href="/wiki/Portable_Swamp" title="Portable Swamp">Portable Swamp</a> there to serve as a distraction so <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> could use <a href="/wiki/Dolores_Umbridge" title="Dolores Umbridge">Dolores Umbridge</a>'s fireplace to communicate with his godfather, <a href="/wiki/Sirius_Black" title="Sirius Black">Sirius Black</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Magical_abilities_and_skills">Magical abilities and skills</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Gregory_the_Smarmy?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Gregory_the_Smarmy?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li><b><a href="/wiki/Potion" title="Potion">Potions</a></b>: Gregory the Smarmy was a highly accomplished and successful <a href="/wiki/Potioneer" title="Potioneer">Potioneer</a>, having created <a href="/wiki/Gregory%27s_Unctuous_Unction" title="Gregory&#39;s Unctuous Unction">Gregory's Unctuous Unction</a> in his lifetime, a potion which fools the drinker into thinking the giver of the potion is their best friend. For this impressive achievement, he was included on a <a href="/wiki/Chocolate_Frog_Card" title="Chocolate Frog Card">Chocolate Frog Card</a>, showing his great talent and proficiency in the <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magical</a> art and <a href="/wiki/Science" title="Science">science</a> of potion-making.</li></ul>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Gregory_the_Smarmy?veaction=edit&amp;section=4" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Gregory_the_Smarmy?action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>The name <i><a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gregory" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Gregory">Gregory</a></i> is derived from the Latin <i>Gregorius</i>, meaning "watchful, alert".</li>
<li><i>Smarmy</i> means "excessively or unctuously flattering, ingratiating, servile"<sup id="cite_ref-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup>.</li></ul>
