<b>Chiara Lobosca</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch</a> and the only child of an <a href="/wiki/Chiara_Lobosca%27s_father" title="Chiara Lobosca&#39;s father">Arithmancer</a> and an <a href="/wiki/Chiara_Lobosca%27s_mother" title="Chiara Lobosca&#39;s mother">Obliviator</a>. She was afflicted with <a href="/wiki/Lycanthropy" title="Lycanthropy">lycanthropy</a> during her childhood, as a result of <a href="/wiki/Fenrir_Greyback" title="Fenrir Greyback">Fenrir Greyback</a> attacking her. She started attending <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> in <a href="/wiki/1984" title="1984">1984</a>, and was <a href="/wiki/Sorting_ceremony" title="Sorting ceremony">sorted</a> into the <a href="/wiki/Hufflepuff" title="Hufflepuff">Hufflepuff</a> house.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Chiara_Lobosca?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Chiara_Lobosca?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_life">Early life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Chiara_Lobosca?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Early life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Chiara_Lobosca?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Early life"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Chiara was the only child of an <a href="/wiki/Chiara_Lobosca%27s_father" title="Chiara Lobosca&#39;s father">Arithmancer</a> and an <a href="/wiki/Chiara_Lobosca%27s_mother" title="Chiara Lobosca&#39;s mother">Obliviator</a>. When Chiara was seven, <a href="/wiki/Werewolf" title="Werewolf">werewolf</a> <a href="/wiki/Fenrir_Greyback" title="Fenrir Greyback">Fenrir Greyback</a> bit her, causing her to become a werewolf as well. Greyback tried to recruit her for his army, but was driven away by Chiara's parents.<sup id="cite_ref-HM2_5-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HM2-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>At one point, Chiara befriended her neighbour <a href="/wiki/Selina" title="Selina">Selina</a>. When Chiara told Selina that she was a werewolf, Selina was not afraid at first. However, after Selina witnessed one of Chiara's transformations, she was terrified, and did not come near Chiara anymore due to her fear. Eventually, Chiara asked her mother to <a href="/wiki/Memory_Charm" title="Memory Charm">Obliviate</a> Selina so Selina would no longer live in fear. Following this, Chiara avoided contact with other people.<sup id="cite_ref-HM2_5-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HM2-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Hogwarts_years">Hogwarts years</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Chiara_Lobosca?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Hogwarts years">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Chiara_Lobosca?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Hogwarts years"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="First_year">First year</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Chiara_Lobosca?veaction=edit&amp;section=4" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: First year">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Chiara_Lobosca?action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: First year"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<p>In <a href="/wiki/1984" title="1984">1984</a>, Chiara began attending <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> and was <a href="/wiki/Sorting_ceremony" title="Sorting ceremony">sorted</a> into <a href="/wiki/Hufflepuff" title="Hufflepuff">Hufflepuff</a> where she may have shared a dorm with <a href="/wiki/Penny_Haywood" title="Penny Haywood">Penny Haywood</a> and <a href="/wiki/Nymphadora_Tonks" title="Nymphadora Tonks">Nymphadora Tonks</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-OWL_1-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OWL-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> Headmaster <a href="/wiki/Albus_Dumbledore" title="Albus Dumbledore">Albus Dumbledore</a> was aware of her condition and arranged for Professor <a href="/wiki/Severus_Snape" title="Severus Snape">Snape</a> to brew the newly discovered <a href="/wiki/Wolfsbane_potion" class="mw-redirect" title="Wolfsbane potion">wolfsbane potion</a> to control her transformations, though he was only able to brew one dose at a time due to an <a href="/wiki/Aconite" title="Aconite">Aconite</a> shortage.
</p><p>Chiara was very interested in <a href="/wiki/Healing_magic" title="Healing magic">healing magic</a>, and hoped to one day become a <a href="/wiki/Healer" title="Healer">healer</a> at <a href="/wiki/St_Mungo%27s_Hospital_for_Magical_Maladies_and_Injuries" title="St Mungo&#39;s Hospital for Magical Maladies and Injuries">St Mungo's Hospital for Magical Maladies and Injuries</a>. In her spare time, she volunteered at the <a href="/wiki/Hospital_wing" class="mw-redirect" title="Hospital wing">Hospital wing</a>, which <a href="/wiki/Poppy_Pomfrey" title="Poppy Pomfrey">Madam Pomfrey</a> greatly appreciated.
</p><p>At some point during her time at Hogwarts, as she was leaving the <a href="/wiki/Forbidden_Forest" title="Forbidden Forest">Forbidden Forest</a>, <a href="/wiki/Borf" title="Borf">Borf</a>, a cub from the <a href="/wiki/Forbidden_Forest_wolf_pack" title="Forbidden Forest wolf pack">Forbidden Forest wolf pack</a>, followed her. The cub continued living in the Forbidden Forest but occasionally visited her.<sup id="cite_ref-secret_7-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-secret-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>During a <a href="/wiki/Hallowe%27en_Feast" title="Hallowe&#39;en Feast">Hallowe'en Feast</a>, she was attacked by <a href="/wiki/Fenrir_Greyback" title="Fenrir Greyback">Fenrir Greyback</a> and two <a href="/wiki/Werewolf" title="Werewolf">werewolves</a>. The following day, she met up with <a href="/wiki/Jacob%27s_sibling" title="Jacob&#39;s sibling">Jacob's sibling</a> and <a href="/wiki/Cecil_Lee" title="Cecil Lee">Cecil Lee</a> to look for werewolves. Later, at nighttime, Chiara and <a href="/wiki/Rubeus_Hagrid" title="Rubeus Hagrid">Rubeus Hagrid</a> showed Borf to Jacob's sibling and Cecil. Shortly afterwards, Chiara told Jacob's sibling that she was a werewolf, a secret she had not told anyone at Hogwarts before.<sup id="cite_ref-secret_7-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-secret-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Second_year">Second year</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Chiara_Lobosca?veaction=edit&amp;section=5" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Second year">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Chiara_Lobosca?action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Second year"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<p>At some point<sup id="cite_ref-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup>, Chiara met <a href="/wiki/Remus_Lupin" title="Remus Lupin">Remus Lupin</a> while he was foraging for potion ingredients in the <a href="/wiki/Forbidden_Forest" title="Forbidden Forest">Forbidden Forest</a>, hoping to create his own <a href="/wiki/Wolfsbane_Potion" title="Wolfsbane Potion">Wolfsbane Potion</a>, despite his relative lack of skill in potions. As they got to talking she felt kinship with him and, realising that he had never experienced a peaceful transformation, began giving him her potion dose, claiming that it was a spare.
</p><p>Later, rumours began spreading around <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts</a> that a student had been attacked by a white <a href="/wiki/Werewolf" title="Werewolf">werewolf</a> during the last full moon and she began to fear that she may have been the perpetrator, though she didn't remember doing so. She began to quietly investigate the rumours, though she was afraid of the answers. <a href="/wiki/Jacob%27s_sibling" title="Jacob&#39;s sibling">Jacob's sibling</a> also began investigating the rumours and sought her help. She was initially reluctant to do so, out of fear of getting too close, but eventually agreed.
</p><p>Though she didn't know the name of the student, she discovered it by searching the infirmary files in the <a href="/wiki/Hospital_Wing" title="Hospital Wing">Hospital Wing</a> while Jacob's sibling distracted <a href="/wiki/Poppy_Pomfrey" title="Poppy Pomfrey">Madam Pomfrey</a> with a feigned illness. Afterwards the two questioned the student, <a href="/wiki/Pippa_Macmillan" title="Pippa Macmillan">Pippa Macmillan</a>, and discovered that Pippa wasn't actually certain that it was a werewolf. She had been surprised by a white creature while exploring the Forbidden Forest and had later been discovered with claw like scratches on her arm, leading her to believe it had been a werewolf, but had been too terrified at the time to properly remember. After the two brewed a <a href="/wiki/Memory_Potion" title="Memory Potion">Memory Potion</a> for her, Pippa recalled the truth: it hadn't been a werewolf that attacked her, but a white <a href="/wiki/Hippogriff" title="Hippogriff">hippogriff</a>. The claw-like scratches had been from a tree that she ran into while fleeing.
</p><p>Despite her name being cleared, Jacob's sibling continued to investigate, curious as to why Chiara had thought she was the attacker in the first place. They eventually discovered the truth and met Lupin. When Chiara let slip that she had been giving away her own potion, Lupin thanked her for her sacrifice but refused to take any more from her. That night happened to be the full moon and Chiara transformed without the benefit of the potion. She attacked Jacob's sibling, but her friend managed to use their skills at <a href="/wiki/Duelling" title="Duelling">duelling</a> to drive her off into the forbidden forest where she completed that month's transformation safely.<sup id="cite_ref-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Third_year">Third year</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Chiara_Lobosca?veaction=edit&amp;section=6" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Third year">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Chiara_Lobosca?action=edit&amp;section=6" title="Edit section: Third year"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>

<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Fifth_year">Fifth year</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Chiara_Lobosca?veaction=edit&amp;section=8" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Fifth year">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Chiara_Lobosca?action=edit&amp;section=8" title="Edit section: Fifth year"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<p>In the <a href="/wiki/1988%E2%80%931989_school_year" title="1988–1989 school year">1988–1989 school year</a>, she was talking to Hagrid in the <a href="/wiki/Clock_Tower_Courtyard" title="Clock Tower Courtyard">Clock Tower Courtyard</a> while Jacob's sibling was <a href="/wiki/Duelling" title="Duelling">duelling</a> <a href="/wiki/Ben_Copper" title="Ben Copper">Ben Copper</a>. She was also talking to Jae in the <a href="/wiki/Great_Hall" title="Great Hall">Great Hall</a> around the same time.<sup id="cite_ref-12" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-12">&#91;12&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Later that same school year, shortly before the <a href="/wiki/Ordinary_Wizarding_Level" title="Ordinary Wizarding Level">O.W.L.s</a>, she was introduced to <a href="/wiki/Rowan_Khanna" title="Rowan Khanna">Rowan Khanna</a>, a friend of Jacob's sibling. They met up in the Forbidden Forest with Hagrid in order to help Rowan calm down by letting them play with Borf. Chiara also told Jacob's sibling that she wanted to become a <a href="/wiki/Healer" title="Healer">Healer</a> and suggested this as a career option that Jacob's sibling could consider.<sup id="cite_ref-OWL_1-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OWL-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>

<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Sixth_year">Sixth year</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Chiara_Lobosca?veaction=edit&amp;section=9" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Sixth year">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Chiara_Lobosca?action=edit&amp;section=9" title="Edit section: Sixth year"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<p>Chiara continued taking Divination in her sixth year.<sup id="cite_ref-Divination_10-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Divination-10">&#91;10&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Chiara and <a href="/wiki/Liz_Tuttle" title="Liz Tuttle">Liz Tuttle</a> spent time in the Forbidden Forest, where they interacted with Borf and a mysterious white <a href="/wiki/Eagle_owl" title="Eagle owl">eagle owl</a>, named <a href="/wiki/Artemis_(owl)" title="Artemis (owl)">Artemis</a> by Liz.<sup id="cite_ref-13" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-13">&#91;13&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>After Jacob's sibling was attacked by a <a href="/wiki/Wizard_in_white_robes" title="Wizard in white robes">wizard in white robes</a> near the <a href="/wiki/Great_Lake" title="Great Lake">Black Lake</a>, Chiara healed the student.<sup id="cite_ref-14" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-14">&#91;14&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>After the death of <a href="/wiki/Rowan_Khanna" title="Rowan Khanna">Rowan Khanna</a> at the hands of <a href="/wiki/Patricia_Rakepick" title="Patricia Rakepick">Patricia Rakepick</a> during the <a href="/wiki/Duel_in_the_Forest_Grove" title="Duel in the Forest Grove">Duel in the Forest Grove</a>, Chiara was invited to the meeting that established the <a href="/wiki/Circle_of_Khanna" title="Circle of Khanna">Circle of Khanna</a>, but did not attend as "something came up".<sup id="cite_ref-HM620_6-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HM620-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Chiara was swamped with work at the Hospital Wing after <a href="/wiki/Poppy_Pomfrey" title="Poppy Pomfrey">Poppy Pomfrey</a> was petrified. She also healed <a href="/wiki/Cedric_Diggory" title="Cedric Diggory">Cedric Diggory</a>'s broken arm after a training accident. When <a href="/wiki/Jacob%27s_sibling" title="Jacob&#39;s sibling">Jacob's sibling</a> asked, she requested to talk to them privately - revealing that she was reluctant of risking letting others know she was a werewolf or harming them if she lost control, and took some convincing before agreeing to join.<sup id="cite_ref-15" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-15">&#91;15&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Chiara was present at the second meeting of the Circle of Khanna, but she was uncertain what she had got herself into. She partnered with Jacob's sibling in Divination class and learned their plan to investigate Rowan's murder scene, informing them that it was a full moon. They reaffirmed their commitment to keeping each other's secrets. They later ran into Chiara in the Hospital Wing, where she asked them to be careful. When a savage werewolf attacked the group in the forest, it was chased off by Chiara in her in-control werewolf form.<sup id="cite_ref-16" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-16">&#91;16&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Jacob's sibling and Chiara discussed their latest trip into the forest in the Hospital Wing, although Chiara was nervous as neither noticed Beatrice coming in and she hoped she did not overhear. Chiara was worried about Beatrice as she had been coming in and staring wordlessly at petrified students for days.<sup id="cite_ref-17" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-17">&#91;17&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>When Jacob and Jacob's sibling rescued Beatrice who nearly drowned in the lake, Chiara took care of her in the Hospital Wing.<sup id="cite_ref-18" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-18">&#91;18&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Penny and Jacob's sibling met with Chiara and <a href="/wiki/Diego_Caplan" title="Diego Caplan">Diego Caplan</a> in the greenhouse to discuss the plan, and both Chiara and Diego had their reservations. Chiara also mentioned <a href="/wiki/Fluxweed" title="Fluxweed">fluxweed</a> needed to be picked under a full moon. They gathered <a href="/wiki/Knotgrass" title="Knotgrass">knotgrass</a> in the greenhouse together.
</p><p>While Jacob's sibling and <a href="/wiki/Talbott_Winger" title="Talbott Winger">Talbott Winger</a> gathered the fluxweed in the forest under the full moon, Chiara watched over them in the shadows. When they finished gathering, Chiara appeared and howled at them; instantly on guard, Talbott pointed his wand at the werewolf, not knowing who it was, but Jacob's sibling interpreted that Chiara was warning them danger was coming and they needed to leave.<sup id="cite_ref-19" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-19">&#91;19&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>During a lecture on <a href="/wiki/Dream_interpretation" title="Dream interpretation">Dream interpretation</a>, Chiara commented that she already knew what her nightmares meant and did not want to share them.<sup id="cite_ref-20" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-20">&#91;20&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Chiara aided <a href="/wiki/Jacob%27s_sibling" title="Jacob&#39;s sibling">Jacob's sibling</a> in researching <a href="/wiki/Merpeople" title="Merpeople">Merpeople</a> at <a href="/wiki/Flourish_and_Blotts" title="Flourish and Blotts">Flourish and Blotts</a> for safer entry into the lake. Chiara found that Merpeople had a love for music and thus they planned to utilise that as a peace offering.<sup id="cite_ref-21" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-21">&#91;21&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Physical_appearance">Physical appearance</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Chiara_Lobosca?veaction=edit&amp;section=10" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Physical appearance">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Chiara_Lobosca?action=edit&amp;section=10" title="Edit section: Physical appearance"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>In <a href="/wiki/Human" title="Human">human</a> form, she was a young girl with light skin and blue eyes. She has pale lips, grey eyebrows, and short silver hair. Outside of class, she almost always wore a grey top, blue jeans, dark grey boots, and a moonstone pendant around her neck, as well as carried a small, dark grey satchel on her right shoulder. In her <a href="/wiki/Werewolf" title="Werewolf">werewolf</a> form, her silver fur colour matched her human hair colour.<sup id="cite_ref-31oct_4-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-31oct-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Chiara had a soft, mellow voice, and she was quieter than most of her peers.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Personality_and_traits">Personality and traits</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Chiara_Lobosca?veaction=edit&amp;section=11" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Personality and traits">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Chiara_Lobosca?action=edit&amp;section=11" title="Edit section: Personality and traits"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>In many ways, Chiara Lobosca was an embodiment of contradiction to werewolf stereotypes, as well as an ideal Hufflepuff. She was very calm and collected, and unfailingly altruistic and compassionate. Indeed, she was said to have a better bedside manner than <a href="/wiki/Poppy_Pomfrey" title="Poppy Pomfrey">Poppy Pomfrey</a>, the matron of the <a href="/wiki/Hospital_Wing" title="Hospital Wing">Hospital Wing</a>, whom she had a good relationship with. Admitting that <a href="/wiki/Remus_Lupin" title="Remus Lupin">Remus Lupin</a> made her realise just how many innocent werewolves were suffering on a monthly basis, Lobosca's ambition was to discover a less expensive and painful means than the <a href="/wiki/Wolfsbane_Potion" title="Wolfsbane Potion">Wolfsbane Potion</a> for werewolves around the <a href="/wiki/Wizarding_world" title="Wizarding world">wizarding world</a>.
</p><p>However, her werewolf status made her extremely insecure and timid, and very mistrustful and testy. She was initially sceptical and reserved about <a href="/wiki/Jacob%27s_sibling" title="Jacob&#39;s sibling">Jacob's sibling</a>, though she eventually accepted them as a friend, making her comparable to <a href="/wiki/Talbott_Winger" title="Talbott Winger">Talbott Winger</a>, another isolationist who struggled to open up.
</p><p>Chiara was also quite bookish and often found solace in reading. As a side effect of managing her affliction, she had a good understanding of the moon phases and its implications, and had an interest in Astronomy by extension. She was also the only student in her class to take Divination seriously, and was always eager to learn.
</p><p>In her spare time, Chiara enjoyed baking, listening to <a target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener" class="external text" href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Punk_rock">punk music</a>, and looking after the <a href="/wiki/Wolf" title="Wolf">wolf</a> <a href="/wiki/Borf" title="Borf">Borf</a>, who she had an affectionate relationship with.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Magical_abilities_and_skills">Magical abilities and skills</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Chiara_Lobosca?veaction=edit&amp;section=12" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Chiara_Lobosca?action=edit&amp;section=12" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li><b><a href="/wiki/Charm" title="Charm">Charms</a></b>: Chiara was capable of casting an effective <a href="/wiki/Shield_Charm" title="Shield Charm">Shield Charm</a> against some <a href="/wiki/Ever-Bashing_Boomerang" title="Ever-Bashing Boomerang">Ever-Bashing Boomerangs</a>, showing her to have been quite skilful in this branch of <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magic</a>.</li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Healing_magic" title="Healing magic">Healing magic</a></b>: Chiara was shown to be quite good at healing magic, being able to heal <a href="/wiki/Jacob%27s_sibling" title="Jacob&#39;s sibling">Jacob's sibling</a> after they had been attacked by the <a href="/wiki/Wizard_in_white_robes" title="Wizard in white robes">White-robed wizard</a>.</li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Divination" title="Divination">Divination</a></b>: Chiara was proficient in the art of divination, as she was able to achieve an <a href="/wiki/Ordinary_Wizarding_Level" title="Ordinary Wizarding Level">O.W.L.</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Divination_(class)" title="Divination (class)">subject</a> and study it further at <a href="/wiki/Nastily_Exhausting_Wizarding_Test" title="Nastily Exhausting Wizarding Test">N.E.W.T.</a>-level, thus showing her skill in the magical field.</li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Astronomy_(class)" title="Astronomy (class)">Astronomy</a></b>: Chiara was proficient in Astronomy, as she was able to achieve an <a href="/wiki/Ordinary_Wizarding_Level" title="Ordinary Wizarding Level">O.W.L.</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Divination_(class)" title="Divination (class)">subject</a> and study it further at <a href="/wiki/Nastily_Exhausting_Wizarding_Test" title="Nastily Exhausting Wizarding Test">N.E.W.T.</a>-level, thus showing her skill in this field.</li></ul>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Chiara_Lobosca?veaction=edit&amp;section=13" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Chiara_Lobosca?action=edit&amp;section=13" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>Chiara (Pronunciation: <i>kee-AHR-a</i>) is a name of Italian origin, meaning "light, clear".<sup id="cite_ref-22" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-22">&#91;22&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li>Lobosca seems to be derived from "lobo", a word that derives from the Latin <i>lupus</i>, and that means "wolf" in Spanish and Portuguese.</li></ul>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Chiara_Lobosca?veaction=edit&amp;section=14" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Chiara_Lobosca?action=edit&amp;section=14" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>Chiara is the first female werewolf named in canon.</li>
<li>It is unknown where Chiara stayed during her werewolf transformations in her Hogwarts years.</li>
<li>While her human form is much smaller than adult humans, her werewolf form is roughly the same size as other adult werewolves, although this might be modelling constraints.</li>
<li>Her quests, "Lone Wolf" and "Howling Halloween" may occur in either order. Though the overall outcome is not affected by the order, dialogue will change to reference previous events and Chiara will tell the player about her lycanthropy during the first quest.</li>
<li>Chiara becomes available as a romance option for the player with the release of the "Festival Fun" side quest.
<ul><li>During the event (taking place in year 5, in <a href="/wiki/1988" title="1988">1988</a> or <a href="/wiki/1989" title="1989">1989</a>), Chiara and some other students can be see doing the "Jump on it" dance from episode 8 of <a target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener" class="external text" href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/The_Fresh_Prince_of_Bel-Air_(season_6)">season 6 of <i>The Fresh Prince of Bel-Air</i></a>, which aired in 1995.</li></ul></li>
<li>While their normal attitude towards Divination is the opposite, Chiara's thoughts on <a href="/wiki/Dream_interpretation" title="Dream interpretation">Dream interpretation</a> mirrors that of <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>'s. They both expressed that they already knew what their nightmares meant: Chiara's about being a werewolf, and Harry's about the murder of <a href="/wiki/Cedric_Diggory" title="Cedric Diggory">Cedric Diggory</a>.</li></ul>
