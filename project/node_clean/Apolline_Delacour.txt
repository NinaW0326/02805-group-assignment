<p><b><a href="/wiki/Madam" title="Madam">Madame</a> Apolline Delacour</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Half-Veela" title="Half-Veela">half-Veela</a> <a href="/wiki/France" title="France">French</a> <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch</a>, married to <a href="/wiki/Delacour_sisters%27_father" title="Delacour sisters&#39; father">Monsieur Delacour</a>. She was described as beautiful and blonde, like her daughters, <a href="/wiki/Fleur_Delacour" title="Fleur Delacour">Fleur</a> and <a href="/wiki/Gabrielle_Delacour" title="Gabrielle Delacour">Gabrielle</a>; these traits were inherited from Apolline's <a href="/wiki/Veela" title="Veela">Veela</a> mother.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Apolline_Delacour?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Apolline_Delacour?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>In <a href="/wiki/1995" title="1995">1995</a>, Apolline and her husband visited Fleur before the Third Task of the <a href="/wiki/Triwizard_Tournament" title="Triwizard Tournament">Triwizard Tournament</a> at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>In the summer of <a href="/wiki/1997" title="1997">1997</a>, the Delacours came to visit the <a href="/wiki/Weasley_family" title="Weasley family">Weasleys</a> at <a href="/wiki/The_Burrow" title="The Burrow">the Burrow</a> to prepare for Fleur's <a href="/wiki/Wedding_of_William_Weasley_and_Fleur_Delacour" title="Wedding of William Weasley and Fleur Delacour">wedding</a> to <a href="/wiki/William_Weasley" title="William Weasley">Bill Weasley</a>. While there, Apolline enjoyed <a href="/wiki/Molly_Weasley" title="Molly Weasley">Molly Weasley</a>'s cooking and hospitality and helped with the preparations, as she was noted to be very accomplished with <a href="/wiki/Household_magic" title="Household magic">household spells</a>. During their visit, Apolline and her husband were shown to be very helpful, pleasant guests. She was seen listening to <a href="/wiki/Kingsley_Shacklebolt" title="Kingsley Shacklebolt">Kingsley Shacklebolt</a>'s corporeal <a href="/wiki/Lynx" title="Lynx">Lynx</a> <a href="/wiki/Patronus_Charm" title="Patronus Charm">Patronus</a>, which warned the Wedding guests that the <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">Ministry</a> had <a href="/wiki/Fall_of_the_Ministry_of_Magic" title="Fall of the Ministry of Magic">fallen</a> and that <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a> would soon be arriving. She, her husband and Gabrielle then safely evacuated the wedding, and returned to <a href="/wiki/France" title="France">France</a>, with Bill and Fleur retreating to <a href="/wiki/Shell_Cottage" title="Shell Cottage">Shell Cottage</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Through her daughter <a href="/wiki/Fleur_Delacour" title="Fleur Delacour">Fleur</a> and son-in-law <a href="/wiki/William_Weasley" title="William Weasley">Bill</a>, Apolline was the maternal grandmother of <a href="/wiki/Victoire_Weasley" title="Victoire Weasley">Victoire</a>, <a href="/wiki/Dominique_Weasley" title="Dominique Weasley">Dominique</a> and <a href="/wiki/Louis_Weasley" title="Louis Weasley">Louis Weasley</a>.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Physical_appearance">Physical appearance</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Apolline_Delacour?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Physical appearance">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Apolline_Delacour?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Physical appearance"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Apolline, due to her <a href="/wiki/Veela" title="Veela">Veela</a> heritage, was described as a beautiful woman with blonde hair, who wore long, green leafy robes to her <a href="/wiki/Fleur_Delacour" title="Fleur Delacour">daughter's</a> <a href="/wiki/Wedding_of_William_Weasley_and_Fleur_Delacour" title="Wedding of William Weasley and Fleur Delacour">wedding</a>.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Personality_and_traits">Personality and traits</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Apolline_Delacour?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Personality and traits">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Apolline_Delacour?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Personality and traits"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Apolline was shown to be a very gracious and sophisticated woman, as she and her husband both happily helped the <a href="/wiki/Weasley_family" title="Weasley family">Weasley family</a> prepare for the wedding of her daughter and son-in-law, and other wedding guests noted to her to have been pleasant and polite. She was also very grateful of Molly Weasley's cooking and hospitality whilst she stayed at her <a href="/wiki/The_Burrow" title="The Burrow">house</a>. Furthermore, she was shown to be a good mother who loved and cared for her family, as she visited her daughter <a href="/wiki/Fleur_Delacour" title="Fleur Delacour">Fleur</a> before she took part in the Third Task of the <a href="/wiki/Triwizard_Tournament" title="Triwizard Tournament">Triwizard Tournament</a>, showing her to have been very supportive and proud of her daughter's achievements.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Apolline_Delacour?veaction=edit&amp;section=4" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Apolline_Delacour?action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li><i>Apolline</i> is the French version of the name <i>Apollonia</i>, derived from Apollo, the Greek god of the sun, prophecy, music and beauty.</li>
<li><i>De la cour</i> is French for "of the court" (royal).</li></ul>
