<b>Elladora Guffy</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch</a> who lived next door to <a href="/wiki/Ethelbard_Mordaunt" title="Ethelbard Mordaunt">Ethelbard Mordaunt</a>. Madam Guffy was fond of practical jokes and had been known on occasion to enchant Mordaunt's garden furniture<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>. This infuriated Mordaunt, which he once expressed to the <i><a href="/wiki/Daily_Prophet" title="Daily Prophet">Daily Prophet</a></i> in a letter<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>.
