This <b>wizard</b> was appointed <a href="/wiki/Headmaster" title="Headmaster">Headmaster</a> of <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts</a> at some unknown time. After his death,<sup id="cite_ref-DOD_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DOD-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> his <a href="/wiki/Portrait" title="Portrait">portrait</a> was hung in the <a href="/wiki/Headmaster%27s_office" class="mw-redirect" title="Headmaster&#39;s office">Headmaster's office</a>, in order to give advice to the current head. He was depicted sleeping in an chair with an <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Astrolabe" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Astrolabe">astrolabe</a> nearby.
