<b>Leonie Tremblay</b> (née&#160;<b>Lestrange</b>) was a&#160;<a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch</a>&#160;and a member of the&#160;<a href="/wiki/Tremblay_family" title="Tremblay family">Tremblay family</a>. She was the daughter of <a href="/wiki/Falco_Lestrange" title="Falco Lestrange">Falco Lestrange</a> and <a href="/wiki/Salom%C3%A9_Lestrange" title="Salomé Lestrange">Salomé Lestrange</a>. She had a sister <a href="/wiki/Eglantine_Lestrange" title="Eglantine Lestrange">Eglantine</a>. She married <a href="/wiki/Bernard_Tremblay" title="Bernard Tremblay">Bernard Tremblay</a> and had two children with him, daughter <a href="/wiki/Clarisse_Lestrange" title="Clarisse Lestrange">Clarisse</a> and son <a href="/wiki/Falco_Tremblay" title="Falco Tremblay">Falco</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
