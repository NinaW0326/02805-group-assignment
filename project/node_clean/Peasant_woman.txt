<p>The <b>peasant woman</b> was a character in the tale <i><a href="/wiki/The_Wizard_and_the_Hopping_Pot" title="The Wizard and the Hopping Pot">The Wizard and the Hopping Pot</a></i> by <a href="/wiki/Beedle_the_Bard" title="Beedle the Bard">Beedle the Bard</a>. She was described as "old" and had a granddaughter afflicted by a crop of warts. A <a href="/wiki/Wizard%27s_father_(The_Wizard_and_the_Hopping_Pot)" class="mw-redirect" title="Wizard&#39;s father (The Wizard and the Hopping Pot)">kindly old wizard</a> used to make a <a href="/wiki/Poultice_for_warts" title="Poultice for warts">special poultice</a> for her but when he died, <a href="/wiki/Wizard_(The_Wizard_and_the_Hopping_Pot)" title="Wizard (The Wizard and the Hopping Pot)">his son</a> rudely refused to give her the potion.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
