<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Potions_Club_girl?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Potions_Club_girl?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_life">Early life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Potions_Club_girl?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Early life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Potions_Club_girl?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Early life"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>The girl came to be educated at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> and was <a href="/wiki/Sorting_ceremony" title="Sorting ceremony">sorted</a> into <a href="/wiki/Hufflepuff" title="Hufflepuff">Hufflepuff House</a>, with <a href="/wiki/Pomona_Sprout" title="Pomona Sprout">Pomona Sprout</a>, the <a href="/wiki/Herbology" title="Herbology">Herbology</a> <a href="/wiki/Professor" title="Professor">Professor</a>, as her <a href="/wiki/Head_of_House" title="Head of House">Head of House</a>. She shared a dormitory with other girls in the <a href="/wiki/Hufflepuff_Basement" title="Hufflepuff Basement">Hufflepuff Basement</a>, located below ground level and near the <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_kitchens" title="Hogwarts kitchens">Hogwarts kitchens</a>. At some point, she set up the <a href="/wiki/Potions_Club" title="Potions Club">Potions Club</a> in <a href="/wiki/Professor" title="Professor">Professor</a> <a href="/wiki/Pomona_Sprout" title="Pomona Sprout">Sprout</a>'s greenhouses, probably after asking permission, in order to help others get their <a href="/wiki/Potions_(class)" title="Potions (class)">Potions</a> homework done, as well as to help others brew potions.<sup id="cite_ref-HBPG_3-10" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBPG-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="1996-1997">1996-1997</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Potions_Club_girl?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: 1996-1997">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Potions_Club_girl?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: 1996-1997"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>In <a href="/wiki/1996" title="1996">1996</a>, she continued as the leader of <a href="/wiki/Potions_Club" title="Potions Club">Potions Club</a> with the new <a href="/wiki/Potions_Master" title="Potions Master">Potions Master</a> being <a href="/wiki/Professor" title="Professor">Professor</a> <a href="/wiki/Horace_Slughorn" title="Horace Slughorn">Horace E.F. Slughorn</a>. This year, <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> came to the <a href="/wiki/Hufflepuff" title="Hufflepuff">Hufflepuff</a> girl and asked if he could join the club in order to brew a <a href="/wiki/Shrinking_Solution" title="Shrinking Solution">Shrinking Solution</a> to help him get <a href="/wiki/Ginevra_Weasley" title="Ginevra Weasley">Ginny Weasley</a> to go with him to <a href="/wiki/Hogsmeade" title="Hogsmeade">Hogsmeade</a>. She told him that he must first prove that he is a good <a href="/wiki/Potioneer" title="Potioneer">Potioneer</a> and asked him to brew a <a href="/wiki/Volubilis_Potion" title="Volubilis Potion">Volubilis Potion</a>, which was also her favourite potion and could be used to change the drinker's voice, which she considered a waste. After he successfully made the potion, she accepted him into the <a href="/wiki/Potions_Club" title="Potions Club">Club</a> and allowed him to brew a <a href="/wiki/Shrinking_Solution" title="Shrinking Solution">Shrinking Solution</a> as well as the <a href="/wiki/Wiggenweld_Potion" title="Wiggenweld Potion">Wiggenweld potion</a> to help him on his trip with <a href="/wiki/Albus_Dumbledore" title="Albus Dumbledore">Dumbledore</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-HBPG_3-11" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBPG-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Physical_appearance">Physical appearance</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Potions_Club_girl?veaction=edit&amp;section=4" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Physical appearance">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Potions_Club_girl?action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Physical appearance"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>This girl had straight long brown hair, with white pale skin and brown eyes.<sup id="cite_ref-HBPG_3-12" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBPG-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Personality_and_traits">Personality and traits</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Potions_Club_girl?veaction=edit&amp;section=5" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Personality and traits">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Potions_Club_girl?action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Personality and traits"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>This girl appeared to be a very pleasant and decent individual, as she gladly allowed Harry Potter to join the Potions club and use their equipment and ingredients to brew potions. She was very congratulatory towards Harry, after she was impressed with the high quality of his <a href="/wiki/Volubilis_Potion" title="Volubilis Potion">Volubilis Potion</a>, meaning she was likely very friendly and humble.<sup id="cite_ref-HBPG_3-13" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBPG-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> Having been sorted into Hufflepuff, she most likely also hard-working, fair, tolerant, loyal, patient and kind-hearted.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Magical_abilities_and_skills">Magical abilities and skills</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Potions_Club_girl?veaction=edit&amp;section=6" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Potions_Club_girl?action=edit&amp;section=6" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li><b><a href="/wiki/Potion" title="Potion">Potions</a></b>: Being a member of the Hogwarts <a href="/wiki/Potions_Club" title="Potions Club">Potions Club</a>, she was very interested in and therefore proficient with the <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magical</a> art and <a href="/wiki/Science" title="Science">science</a> of potion-making. She presumably excelled in <a href="/wiki/Potions_(class)" title="Potions (class)">Potions class</a> whilst at Hogwarts, given her enthusiasm for her club, meaning she could likely brew a large variety of different brews and concoctions.<sup id="cite_ref-HBPG_3-14" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBPG-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup></li></ul>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Potions_Club_girl?veaction=edit&amp;section=7" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Potions_Club_girl?action=edit&amp;section=7" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>The identity of this girl is unknown but it is possible that she is <a href="/wiki/Megan_Jones" title="Megan Jones">Megan Jones</a>, a <a href="/wiki/Hufflepuff" title="Hufflepuff">Hufflepuff</a> girl who was in the same year as <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> during their time at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts</a>.</li>
<li>Several other girls appear in <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Half-Blood_Prince_(video_game)" title="Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince (video game)">Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince (video game)</a></i> with identical facial features and hair to this girl, but they are in one of the other three houses.</li></ul>
