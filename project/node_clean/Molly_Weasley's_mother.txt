<b>Mrs Prewett</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Pure-blood" title="Pure-blood">pure-blood</a> witch, the wife of <a href="/wiki/Molly_Weasley%27s_father" title="Molly Weasley&#39;s father">Mr Prewett</a>, and the mother of <a href="/wiki/Gideon_Prewett" title="Gideon Prewett">Gideon</a>, <a href="/wiki/Fabian_Prewett" title="Fabian Prewett">Fabian</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Molly_Weasley" title="Molly Weasley">Molly Prewett</a> (later <a href="/wiki/Weasley_family" title="Weasley family">Weasley</a>). She was the maternal grandmother of the <a href="/wiki/Weasley_family" title="Weasley family">Weasley</a> children and great grandmother of <a href="/wiki/Albus_Potter" title="Albus Potter">Albus</a>, <a href="/wiki/James_Potter_II" title="James Potter II">James</a> and <a href="/wiki/Lily_Potter" title="Lily Potter">Lily Potter</a>, <a href="/wiki/Victoire_Weasley" title="Victoire Weasley">Victoire</a>, <a href="/wiki/Dominique_Weasley" title="Dominique Weasley">Dominique</a>, <a href="/wiki/Louis_Weasley" title="Louis Weasley">Louis</a>, <a href="/wiki/Molly_Weasley_II" title="Molly Weasley II">Molly</a>, <a href="/wiki/Lucy_Weasley" title="Lucy Weasley">Lucy</a>, <a href="/wiki/Roxanne_Weasley" title="Roxanne Weasley">Roxanne</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Fred_Weasley_II" title="Fred Weasley II">Fred</a> Weasley, as well as <a href="/wiki/Rose_Granger-Weasley" title="Rose Granger-Weasley">Rose</a> and <a href="/wiki/Hugo_Weasley" class="mw-redirect" title="Hugo Weasley">Hugo Granger-Weasley</a>. She was also the mother-in-law of <a href="/wiki/Arthur_Weasley" title="Arthur Weasley">Arthur Weasley</a>. She could also be the niece of <a href="/wiki/Muriel" title="Muriel">Muriel</a>.
</p><p>She may or may not have had a big nose.<sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
