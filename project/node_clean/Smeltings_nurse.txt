<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Smeltings_nurse?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Smeltings_nurse?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Sometime in or before <a href="/wiki/1976" title="1976">1976</a><sup id="cite_ref-AGE_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-AGE-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> this woman was sired to a <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggle</a> family somewhere in the <a href="/wiki/Orrery" title="Orrery">world</a>.<br />She eventually was hired by the <a href="/wiki/Smeltings_Academy" title="Smeltings Academy">Smeltings Academy</a> of <a href="/wiki/Great_Britain" title="Great Britain">Great Britain</a> as a nurse there, and during her time in the career sent a letter, in <a href="/wiki/1994" title="1994">1994</a> to be precise, to the <a href="/wiki/Dursley_family" title="Dursley family">Dursley family</a> to inform them that the school no longer carried <a href="/wiki/British_terms#K" title="British terms">knickerbockers</a> large enough to fit <a href="/wiki/Dudley_Dursley" title="Dudley Dursley">their son</a> anymore. She also included a diet sheet comprised mostly of fruits and vegetables.<sup id="cite_ref-GOF3_2-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF3-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Personality_and_traits">Personality and traits</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Smeltings_nurse?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Personality and traits">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Smeltings_nurse?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Personality and traits"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Little is known of this woman's personality; however, it seemed that she was a fairly caring person as she not only was hired by a school, but was troubled enough by the weight of <a href="/wiki/Dudley_Dursley" title="Dudley Dursley">Dudley Dursley</a> that she sent a letter and a diet sheet to the <a href="/wiki/4_Privet_Drive" title="4 Privet Drive">Dursley home</a> to try and get the family to help Dudley lose weight.<sup id="cite_ref-GOF3_2-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF3-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
