<p>A <b>hopping cauldron</b> is a type of sentient <a href="/wiki/Cauldron" title="Cauldron">cauldron</a>, so named because it has a single leg underneath the base of the cauldron allowing for locomotion. Hopping cauldrons have a certain degree of personality; while they are faithful to most <a href="/wiki/Potioneer" title="Potioneer">potioneers</a>, they will lose their faith if unpleasant concoctions are mixed in them.<sup id="cite_ref-BOP_1-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-BOP-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="History">History</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Hopping_cauldron?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: History">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Hopping_cauldron?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: History"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>During his lifetime, <a href="/wiki/Zygmunt_Budge" title="Zygmunt Budge">Zygmunt Budge</a> owned a hopping cauldron which he used in his <a href="/wiki/Potion" title="Potion">potion</a> experiments. It was faithful to him at first, though it became "less cooperative" after an attempt by Budge to mix <a href="/wiki/Fluxweed" title="Fluxweed">fluxweed</a> and <a href="/wiki/Exploding_Fluid" title="Exploding Fluid">Exploding Fluid</a> in it. A hopping cauldron, perhaps Budge's own, was included in his masterwork <i><a href="/wiki/Book_of_Potions" title="Book of Potions">Book of Potions</a></i>.<sup id="cite_ref-BOP_1-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-BOP-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p><a href="/wiki/Beedle_the_Bard" title="Beedle the Bard">Beedle the Bard</a> included a hopping cauldron, known as <a href="/wiki/The_Hopping_Pot" title="The Hopping Pot">the Hopping Pot</a>, in his story <i><a href="/wiki/The_Wizard_and_the_Hopping_Pot" title="The Wizard and the Hopping Pot">The Wizard and the Hopping Pot</a></i>.<sup id="cite_ref-TBBR_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-TBBR-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
