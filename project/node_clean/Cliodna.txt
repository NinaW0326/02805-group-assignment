<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Cliodna?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Cliodna?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>An <a href="/wiki/Animagus" title="Animagus">Animagus</a> with the ability to turn into a sea <a href="/wiki/Bird" title="Bird">bird</a>, she was aided by <a href="/wiki/Cliodna%27s_birds" title="Cliodna&#39;s birds">three magical birds</a> that <a href="/wiki/Healing_magic" title="Healing magic">cured</a> the sick by singing them to sleep. It was also said that she could turn into a wave. She also discovered the properties of <a href="/wiki/Moondew" title="Moondew">Moondew</a>. Cliodne appeared on a <a href="/wiki/Chocolate_Frog_Card" title="Chocolate Frog Card">Chocolate Frog Card</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>In September of <a href="/wiki/1991" title="1991">1991</a>, <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> picked up a Chocolate Frog Card with a picture of Cliodna, who was scratching her nose.<sup id="cite_ref-PS6_4-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PS6-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Magical_abilities_and_skills">Magical abilities and skills</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Cliodna?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Cliodna?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li><b><a href="/wiki/Potion" title="Potion">Potions</a></b>: Cliodna was highly proficient with potions, as she discovered a new ingredient, <a href="/wiki/Moondew" title="Moondew">Moondew</a> and its properties in potion-making. Furthermore, she would have needed to have brewed the <a href="/wiki/Animagus_potion" title="Animagus potion">Animagus potion</a> in order for her to gain the ability to fully transform, a potion known for being highly difficult, complex and time-consuming, showing her very impressive talent in the art of potion-making.</li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Transfiguration" title="Transfiguration">Transfiguration</a></b>: She was also a mistress of <a href="/wiki/Transfiguration" title="Transfiguration">transfiguration</a>, being able to turn herself into a sea <a href="/wiki/Bird" title="Bird">bird</a> at will. Given how <a href="/wiki/Human_Transfiguration" title="Human Transfiguration">Human Transfiguration</a> is an incredibly complex and even dangerous branch of transfiguration, this was a remarkable <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magical</a> feat.</li></ul>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Cliodna?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Cliodna?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>In Irish mythology, <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Cliodna" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Cliodna">Cliodna</a> was a goddess of <a href="/wiki/Love" title="Love">love</a> and beauty, said to have three brightly coloured birds whose song healed the sick. She left Tir Tairngire to be with her mortal lover, Ciabh√°n, but drowned as she slept in Glandore harbour in County Cork.</li>
<li>She was also rumoured to be the "Queen of the <a href="/wiki/Banshee" title="Banshee">Banshees</a>".</li></ul>
