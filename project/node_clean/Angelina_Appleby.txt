<b>Angelina Appleby</b> (b. <a href="/wiki/1680s" title="1680s">1680s</a>) was a <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor" title="Gryffindor">Gryffindor</a> <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">student</a> at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> in the <a href="/wiki/1690s" title="1690s">1690s</a> and <a href="/wiki/1700s" title="1700s">1700s</a>. She was once <a href="/wiki/Quidditch_Captain" title="Quidditch Captain">Captain</a> of the <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor_Quidditch_team" title="Gryffindor Quidditch team">Gryffindor Quidditch team</a> from <a href="/wiki/1696" class="mw-redirect" title="1696">1696</a> to <a href="/wiki/1700" class="mw-redirect" title="1700">1700</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
