<b>Mrs Dursley</b> was the wife of <a href="/wiki/Dudley_Dursley" title="Dudley Dursley">Dudley Dursley</a>: the couple had two<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/Dudley_Dursley%27s_children" title="Dudley Dursley&#39;s children">children</a>. When her husband's cousin <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> visited them with his <a href="/wiki/Potter_family" title="Potter family">family</a>, their children would play while Dudley and Harry sat together quietly.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>She is related to the <a href="/wiki/Dursley_family" title="Dursley family">Dursley family</a>, and the <a href="/wiki/Evans_family" title="Evans family">Evans family</a>, who are the maternal family of her mother-in-law, <a href="/wiki/Petunia_Dursley" title="Petunia Dursley">Petunia</a>. She is related to the <a href="/wiki/Potter_family" title="Potter family">Potter family</a>, due to her marriage to <a href="/wiki/Dudley_Dursley" title="Dudley Dursley">Dudley Dursley</a>, whose cousin is <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>, and is related to her own maternal and paternal family.
</p><p>It is unknown as to whether she knows about her husband's cousin being part of the wizarding world.
</p>
