<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Waitress_at_Luchino_Caffe?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Waitress_at_Luchino_Caffe?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<figure class="thumb tright show-info-icon" style="width: 261px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/0/08/2017-07-24_%282%29.png/revision/latest?cb=20170803011546" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/0/08/2017-07-24_%282%29.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/259?cb=20170803011546" decoding="async" width="259" height="109" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="2017-07-24 (2).png" data-image-key="2017-07-24_(2).png" /></a> 	 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:2017-07-24_(2).png" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption">The Waitress working at the cafe</p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure>
<p>At the all night <a href="/wiki/Luchino_Caffe" title="Luchino Caffe">Luchino Caffe</a> on <a href="/wiki/Tottenham_Court_Road" title="Tottenham Court Road">Tottenham Court Road</a> in <a href="/wiki/London" title="London">London</a>, she served two cappuccinos to <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione Granger</a> and <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ron Weasley</a> on the evening of <a href="/wiki/1_August" title="1 August">1 August</a>, <a href="/wiki/1997" title="1997">1997</a>. <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> was also with them, but under his <a href="/wiki/Cloak_of_Invisibility" title="Cloak of Invisibility">Invisibility Cloak</a>. The waitress chewed gum as she worked and shot Ron a nasty look after overhearing him remark that her <a href="/wiki/Coffee" title="Coffee">coffee</a> was "revolting". She was then affronted when two more customers rudely waved her away when she shuffled over to take their order.<sup id="cite_ref-DH9_1-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH9-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>These customers were, in fact, the <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a> <a href="/wiki/Thorfinn_Rowle" title="Thorfinn Rowle">Thorfinn Rowle</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Antonin_Dolohov" title="Antonin Dolohov">Antonin Dolohov</a>; they had tracked Harry, Ron, and Hermione down using the <a href="/wiki/Taboo" title="Taboo">Taboo curse</a>. A <a href="/wiki/Luchino_Caffe_Duel" title="Luchino Caffe Duel">skirmish</a> broke out in the caf√©, and a <a href="/wiki/Stunning_Spell" title="Stunning Spell">Stunning Spell</a> Harry was aiming at Dolohov struck the waitress, making her collapse near the door as she was attempting to flee in terror. Afterwards, Hermione <a href="/wiki/Memory_Charm" title="Memory Charm">wiped her memory</a> of the event, and the the effects of the Stunning Spell wore off once the trio had <a href="/wiki/Apparition" title="Apparition">Disapparated</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-DH9_1-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH9-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Waitress_at_Luchino_Caffe?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Waitress_at_Luchino_Caffe?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>In <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Deathly_Hallows:_Part_1" title="Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 1">Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 1</a></i>, the waitress is portrayed by <a href="/wiki/Great_Britain" title="Great Britain">British</a> actress <a href="/wiki/Eva_Alexander" title="Eva Alexander">Eva Alexander</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-IMDb_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-IMDb-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li>In the film, <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> does not use his <a href="/wiki/Cloak_of_Invisibility" title="Cloak of Invisibility">Invisibility Cloak</a> and as such the waitress also serves Harry a cappuccino. When the trio is attacked by Rowle and Dolohov, the waitress is not present and does not notice anything amiss as she is listening to music on a <a href="/wiki/Headset" title="Headset">headset</a> in the back room. When she comes out, she is shocked by the devastation, and is told by Hermione to "leave." The Memory Charm is not used on her.</li></ul>
