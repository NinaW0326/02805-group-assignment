<b>MacDuff</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> who lived during the early <a href="/wiki/20th_century" title="20th century">20th century</a>. He was a member of <a href="/wiki/Gellert_Grindelwald" title="Gellert Grindelwald">Gellert Grindelwald</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Alliance" title="Alliance">Alliance</a>, and was among the <a href="/wiki/Dark_wizard" class="mw-redirect" title="Dark wizard">dark wizard</a>'s foremost acolytes.<sup id="cite_ref-COG23_1-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COG23-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
