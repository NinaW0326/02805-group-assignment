<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Gaspard_Shingleton?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Gaspard_Shingleton?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_life">Early life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Gaspard_Shingleton?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Early life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Gaspard_Shingleton?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Early life"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Gaspard was born in 1959.<sup id="cite_ref-COSG_2-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COSG-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-JKRS_1-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-JKRS-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Adulthood">Adulthood</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Gaspard_Shingleton?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Adulthood">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Gaspard_Shingleton?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Adulthood"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>At some point, he invented the <a href="/wiki/Self-Stirring_Cauldron" title="Self-Stirring Cauldron">Self-Stirring Cauldron</a> and went down in history while still alive,<sup id="cite_ref-COSG_2-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COSG-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-JKRS_1-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-JKRS-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> being covered in first year <a href="/wiki/History_of_Magic" title="History of Magic">History of Magic</a> classes at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts</a>. The <a href="/wiki/History_of_Magic" title="History of Magic">History of Magic</a> <a href="/wiki/First_year_exams" title="First year exams">exam</a> featured him.<sup id="cite_ref-PS16_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PS16-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>He was also immortalised on a silver <a href="/wiki/Chocolate_Frog_Card" title="Chocolate Frog Card">Chocolate Frog Card</a>, as part of the <a href="/wiki/Famous_Witches_and_Wizards_Cards" title="Famous Witches and Wizards Cards">Famous Witches and Wizards Cards</a> series.<sup id="cite_ref-COSG_2-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COSG-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Gaspard_Shingleton?veaction=edit&amp;section=4" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Gaspard_Shingleton?action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>It is weird that Gaspard Shingleton's achievements are covered so prominently in the <a href="/wiki/1992" title="1992">1992</a> <a href="/wiki/History_of_Magic" title="History of Magic">History of Magic</a> <a href="/wiki/First_year_exams" title="First year exams">first year exam</a>, when it is revealed in <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Deathly_Hallows" title="Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows">Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows</a></i> that "<i>Professor Bagshot doesn't cover anything later than the end of the nineteenth century</i>"<sup id="cite_ref-DH16_4-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH16-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> in her <a href="/wiki/A_History_of_Magic" title="A History of Magic">work</a>, which is used as the textbook for the subject.
<ul><li>Similarly, the inventor of the Self-Stirring Cauldron is described as a "batty old <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a>" in <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Philosopher%27s_Stone" title="Harry Potter and the Philosopher&#39;s Stone">Harry Potter and the Philosopher's Stone</a></i>, yet he would only be in his early thirties at the time.</li></ul></li></ul>
