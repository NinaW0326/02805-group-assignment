<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Magorian?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Magorian?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>On Saturday in the last weekend in May of <a href="/wiki/1996" title="1996">1996</a>, Magorian reminded <a href="/wiki/Rubeus_Hagrid" title="Rubeus Hagrid">Rubeus Hagrid</a> that he was no longer welcome in the <a href="/wiki/Forbidden_Forest" title="Forbidden Forest">Forbidden Forest</a> for his helping to protect <a href="/wiki/Firenze" title="Firenze">Firenze</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP30_1-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP30-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<figure class="thumb tleft show-info-icon" style="width: 182px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/1/12/Magorian.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20100104154236" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/1/12/Magorian.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/180?cb=20100104154236" decoding="async" width="180" height="160" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="Magorian.jpg" data-image-key="Magorian.jpg" /></a> 	 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:Magorian.jpg" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption">Magorian being strangled by Umbridge's ropes</p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure> 
<p>On <a href="/wiki/18_June" title="18 June">18 June</a> <a href="/wiki/1996" title="1996">1996</a>, when <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> and <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione Granger</a> led <a href="/wiki/Dolores_Umbridge" title="Dolores Umbridge">Dolores Umbridge</a> into the Forbidden Forest, he was among the centaurs who were greatly insulted by Umbridge's remarks about their race being "filthy <a href="/wiki/Half-breed" title="Half-breed">half-breeds</a>". During this incident, <a href="/wiki/Skirmish_in_the_Forbidden_Forest" title="Skirmish in the Forbidden Forest">a violent skirmish occurred</a>, during which Umbridge assaulted Magorian with the <a href="/wiki/Incarcerous_Spell" title="Incarcerous Spell">Incarcerous Spell</a>, which caused thick ropes to bind and strangle the centaur. The centaurs then attacked and carried Umbridge away.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP33_3-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP33-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>He led the charge of the centaurs from the Forbidden Forest during the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a>, breaking through the <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eater</a> ranks into the <a href="/wiki/Great_Hall" title="Great Hall">Great Hall</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-DH36_4-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH36-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Physical_appearance">Physical appearance</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Magorian?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Physical appearance">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Magorian?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Physical appearance"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Magorian had long, dark hair and a high-cheekboned face with a proud look.<sup id="cite_ref-appearance_2-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-appearance-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Personality_and_traits">Personality and traits</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Magorian?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Personality and traits">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Magorian?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Personality and traits"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>He was more of a traditional centaur and hated "traitors" such as <a href="/wiki/Firenze" title="Firenze">Firenze</a> for going to teach at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a>. He intensely disliked <a href="/wiki/Human" title="Human">humans</a>, however, centaurs don't usually kill the young and the innocent and during the period where the centaurs had turned against Hagrid he allowed Hagrid to pass unscathed solely due to him being accompanied by their young. He was shown to be in favour of killing any human if they wandered into the centaurs' midst and insulted them.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP30_1-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP30-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> However, his views of humanity might have changed after witnessing Harry's selfless actions and welcoming Firenze back into the herd.<sup id="cite_ref-DH36_4-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH36-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Magorian?veaction=edit&amp;section=4" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Magorian?action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>The name "Magorian" means "son of Magor", an Armenian form of Mark.</li></ul>
