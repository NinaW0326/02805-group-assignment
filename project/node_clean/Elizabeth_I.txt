<b>Elizabeth I</b> (<a href="/wiki/7_September" title="7 September">7 September</a> <a href="/wiki/1533" class="mw-redirect" title="1533">1533</a> - <a href="/wiki/24_March" title="24 March">24 March</a> <a href="/wiki/1603" class="mw-redirect" title="1603">1603</a>) was a <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggle</a> who reigned as <a href="/wiki/Queen" title="Queen">Queen</a> of <a href="/wiki/England" title="England">England</a> and <a href="/wiki/Ireland" title="Ireland">Ireland</a> from <a href="/wiki/1558" class="mw-redirect" title="1558">1558</a> until her <a href="/wiki/Death" title="Death">death</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-wikipedia_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-wikipedia-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> She was the daughter of <a href="/wiki/Henry_VIII" title="Henry VIII">King Henry VIII</a> by his second wife, <a href="/wiki/Anne_Boleyn" title="Anne Boleyn">Anne Boleyn</a>, and the half-sister of <a href="/wiki/Mary_I" title="Mary I">Mary I</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-wikipedia_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-wikipedia-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Queen Elizabeth never <a href="/wiki/Marriage" title="Marriage">married</a> during her lifetime.<sup id="cite_ref-wikipedia_1-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-wikipedia-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> However, there was "ample" evidence suggesting that the first <a href="/wiki/Lucius_Malfoy_I" title="Lucius Malfoy I">Lucius Malfoy</a> sought her hand in marriage, but was rebuffed by her.<sup id="cite_ref-pm-malfoys_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-pm-malfoys-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> Some <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizarding</a> historians believed that her later opposition to marriage was the result of a <a href="/wiki/Jinx_against_marriage" title="Jinx against marriage">jinx</a> that Malfoy placed on her in revenge for her spurning him.<sup id="cite_ref-pm-malfoys_2-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-pm-malfoys-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> This account was denied by future generations of the <a href="/wiki/Malfoy_family" title="Malfoy family">Malfoy family</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-pm-malfoys_2-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-pm-malfoys-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>During her rule, she was seen as the one the greatest ever rulers of England, with her reign being called the "Golden Age".<sup id="cite_ref-wikipedia_1-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-wikipedia-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Elizabeth_I?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Elizabeth_I?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The name "Elizabeth" comes from Ελισαβετ (Elisabet), the Greek form of the Hebrew name אֱלִישֶׁבַע ('Elisheva') meaning "my God is an oath" or perhaps "my God is abundance".
</p>
