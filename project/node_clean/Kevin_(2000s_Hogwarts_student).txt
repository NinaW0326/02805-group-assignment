<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Kevin_(2000s_Hogwarts_student)?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Kevin_(2000s_Hogwarts_student)?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Kevin began attending <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> at the age of eleven. He spoke to <a href="/wiki/Ivy_Warrington" title="Ivy Warrington">Ivy Warrington</a> in a <a href="/wiki/First_year" title="First year">first-year</a> boat on the way up to the school, and thought she was nice. He told <a href="/wiki/Minerva_McGonagall" title="Minerva McGonagall">Professor McGonagall</a> at the <a href="/wiki/Sorting_ceremony" title="Sorting ceremony">Sorting ceremony</a> how he saw and spoke to her when they found out she had vanished. Kevin was sorted into <a href="/wiki/Hufflepuff" title="Hufflepuff">Hufflepuff</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-MA_3-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-MA-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Kevin later helped a student and fellow Hufflepuff, <a href="/wiki/Robyn_Thistlethwaite" title="Robyn Thistlethwaite">Robyn Thistlethwaite</a> search for Ivy. He thought it was very easy to get lost in the school, and they had to try not to get lost too. He asked a <a href="/wiki/Portrait" title="Portrait">portrait</a> if he had seen Ivy pass through the <a href="/wiki/Grand_Staircase" title="Grand Staircase">Grand Staircase</a>, but he hadn't and warned Kevin he would tell <a href="/wiki/Argus_Filch" title="Argus Filch">Mr Filch</a> he saw the group.<sup id="cite_ref-MA_3-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-MA-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
