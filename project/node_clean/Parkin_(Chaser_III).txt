<b>Parkin</b> (fl. <a href="/wiki/1422" class="mw-redirect" title="1422">1422</a>) was a <a href="/wiki/Scotland" title="Scotland">Scottish</a> <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch or wizard</a> who played as a <a href="/wiki/Chaser" title="Chaser">Chaser</a> for the original <a href="/wiki/Wigtown_Wanderers" title="Wigtown Wanderers">Wigtown Wanderers</a> team during the <a href="/wiki/15th_century" title="15th century">15th century</a> and was a child of Wanderers founder <a href="/wiki/Walter_Parkin" title="Walter Parkin">Walter Parkin</a>. They were a formidable team during this time who won most of their matches, perhaps partly because Walter himself used to attend every game holding his wand in one hand and a meat cleaver in the other. The original Chasers invented their own signature move, known as the <a href="/wiki/Parkin%27s_Pincer" title="Parkin&#39;s Pincer">Parkin's Pincer</a>, where three Chasers fly from different directions at an opposing Chaser.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
