<b>Emma Vane</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggle</a> fashion designer. Clothing designed by her was featured in the <a href="/wiki/Costume_Materialization" title="Costume Materialization">Costume Materialization</a> collection opening that was held on <a href="/wiki/28_September" title="28 September">28 September</a>, <a href="/wiki/1997" title="1997">1997</a>, along with the work of fellow fashion designers <a href="/wiki/Julia_Dehoff" title="Julia Dehoff">Julia Dehoff</a> and <a href="/wiki/Mauricio_Carneiro" title="Mauricio Carneiro">Mauricio Carneiro</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-HPLE_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HPLE-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
