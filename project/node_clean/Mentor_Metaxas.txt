<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Mentor_Metaxas?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Mentor_Metaxas?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>He attended the final of the <a href="/wiki/2014_Quidditch_World_Cup" title="2014 Quidditch World Cup">427th Quidditch World Cup</a>, on <a href="/wiki/11_July" title="11 July">11 July</a>, <a href="/wiki/2014" title="2014">2014</a>. Shortly before the match started, he was spotted by <i><a href="/wiki/Daily_Prophet" title="Daily Prophet">Daily Prophet</a></i> reporter <a href="/wiki/Rita_Skeeter" title="Rita Skeeter">Rita Skeeter</a> chatting to the President of the <a href="/wiki/Argentinian_Council_of_Magic" title="Argentinian Council of Magic">Argentinian Council of Magic</a>, <a href="/wiki/Valentina_V%C3%A1zquez" title="Valentina Vázquez">Valentina Vázquez</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-the-qwc2014-final_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-the-qwc2014-final-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Mentor_Metaxas?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Mentor_Metaxas?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>If this individual's name is indication of anything, he is probably <a href="/wiki/Greece" title="Greece">Greek</a> or of Greek descent — the name "Mentor" is an Ancient Greek name that features in Greek Mythology,<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> and the surname Metaxas is also Greek: Greek archicted and marksman <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Anastasios_Metaxas" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Anastasios Metaxas">Anastasios Metaxas</a> (1862-1937), Greek politician <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Andreas_Metaxas" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Andreas Metaxas">Andreas Metaxas</a> (1790-1860), and Greek general and dictator <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ioannis_Metaxas" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Ioannis Metaxas">Ioannis Metaxas</a> (1871–1941) are some notable people who bore this surname.</li></ul>
