<p><b>Lucy Evans</b> (born c. <a href="/wiki/1939" title="1939">1939</a>) was a <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggle</a> housewife, and a witness of the <a href="/wiki/Millennium_Bridge" title="Millennium Bridge">Millennium Bridge</a> disaster in <a href="/wiki/1996" title="1996">1996</a>, aged 57. She was standing on the riverbank directly beneath the bridge when the collapse took place. She later asserted to <i><a href="/wiki/The_Guardian" title="The Guardian">The Guardian</a></i> that those responsible were, without a doubt, foreigners.<sup id="cite_ref-HPLE_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HPLE-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<ul><li>Lucy Evans is presumably named after <a class="new" title="Lucy Evans (real-world) (page does not exist)" rel="nofollow" data-uncrawlable-url="L3dpa2kvTHVjeV9FdmFuc18ocmVhbC13b3JsZCk/YWN0aW9uPWVkaXQmcmVkbGluaz0x">Lucy Evans</a>, who worked as an assistant music supervisor on <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Goblet_of_Fire_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire (film)">Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire</a></i>, and as an assistant to <a href="/wiki/David_Barron" title="David Barron">David Barron</a> on <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Half-Blood_Prince_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince (film)">Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince</a></i> and <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Deathly_Hallows:_Part_1" title="Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 1">Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 1</a></i>.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li>Much like <a href="/wiki/Mark_Evans" title="Mark Evans">Mark Evans</a>, Lucy is most likely not related to <a href="/wiki/Lily_J._Potter" title="Lily J. Potter">Lily Evans</a> despite sharing a surname.</li></ul>
