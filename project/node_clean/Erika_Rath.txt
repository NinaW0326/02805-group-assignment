<p><b>Erika Rath</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch</a> who played as the <a href="/wiki/Beater" title="Beater">Beater</a> on a <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts</a> <a href="/wiki/Quidditch_team" title="Quidditch team">Quidditch team</a> in the <a href="/wiki/1980s" title="1980s">1980s</a>. Possessed of terrible sportsmanship, she was known to deliberately send <a href="/wiki/Bludger" title="Bludger">Bludgers</a> at the crowds during games.<sup id="cite_ref-HM_3-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HM-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Physical_appearance">Physical appearance</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Erika_Rath?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Physical appearance">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Erika_Rath?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Physical appearance"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Erika had dark blue eyes, white skin and long, pale blonde hair. She was notable for having a substantial build with very muscular arms, as befitting a female <a href="/wiki/Beater" title="Beater">Beater</a>.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Magical_abilities_and_skills">Magical abilities and skills</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Erika_Rath?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Erika_Rath?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li><b><a href="/wiki/Flight" title="Flight">Flying</a></b>: Erika was a fairly accomplished <a href="/wiki/Broomstick" title="Broomstick">broomstick</a> flyer, playing as <a href="/wiki/Beater" title="Beater">Beater</a> for an unknown Hogwarts <a href="/wiki/Quidditch" title="Quidditch">Quidditch</a> team, although she possessed very poor sportsmanship, often resorting to foul play.</li></ul>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Possessions">Possessions</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Erika_Rath?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Possessions">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Erika_Rath?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Possessions"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li><b><a href="/wiki/Quidditch_Through_the_Ages" title="Quidditch Through the Ages">Quidditch Through the Ages</a></b>: Erika's dad gave her copy this book for her birthday.<sup id="cite_ref-HM_3-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HM-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup></li></ul>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Erika_Rath?veaction=edit&amp;section=4" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Erika_Rath?action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>Which house she is in depends on that of <a href="/wiki/Jacob%27s_sibling" title="Jacob&#39;s sibling">Jacob's sibling</a> (chosen by the player). If Jacob's sibling is in <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor" title="Gryffindor">Gryffindor</a>, <a href="/wiki/Hufflepuff" title="Hufflepuff">Hufflepuff</a> or <a href="/wiki/Slytherin" title="Slytherin">Slytherin</a>, Rath is in <a href="/wiki/Ravenclaw" title="Ravenclaw">Ravenclaw</a>. If <a href="/wiki/Jacob%27s_sibling" title="Jacob&#39;s sibling">Jacob's sibling</a> is in Ravenclaw, Rath is in Slytherin.</li>
<li>In official material posted by the creators of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter:_Hogwarts_Mystery" title="Harry Potter: Hogwarts Mystery">Harry Potter: Hogwarts Mystery</a></i>, including the loading screen from <a href="/wiki/4_September" title="4 September">4 September</a> to <a href="/wiki/10_October" title="10 October">10 October</a> <a href="/wiki/2019" title="2019">2019</a>, she is usually shown as a <a href="/wiki/Slytherin" title="Slytherin">Slytherin</a>.
<ul><li>She is often shown as a Slytherin alongside a Gryffindor <a href="/wiki/Skye_Parkin" title="Skye Parkin">Skye Parkin</a>. However, this is impossible in-game, as Rath is in Ravenclaw if Skye is in Gryffindor.</li></ul></li></ul>
