<p><b>Mrs Zabini</b> (fl. <a href="/wiki/1980" title="1980">1980</a>)&#160;was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch</a> and the mother of <a href="/wiki/Blaise_Zabini" title="Blaise Zabini">Blaise Zabini</a>. She was famously beautiful, and married <a href="/wiki/Blaise_Zabini%27s_mother%27s_seven_husbands" title="Blaise Zabini&#39;s mother&#39;s seven husbands">seven wizards</a> who each died in mysterious circumstances, leaving her with a large amount of gold from each.<sup id="cite_ref-Money_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Money-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> Her fame was known to <a href="/wiki/Horace_Slughorn" title="Horace Slughorn">Horace Slughorn</a>, who invited her son to his compartment on the <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Express" title="Hogwarts Express">Hogwarts Express</a> for that reason.<sup id="cite_ref-Reason_4-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Reason-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
