<p><b>Colin Creevey</b> (<a href="/wiki/1981" title="1981">1981</a> – <a href="/wiki/2_May" title="2 May">2 May</a>, <a href="/wiki/1998" title="1998">1998</a>) was a <a href="/wiki/Muggle-born" title="Muggle-born">Muggle-born</a> <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a>, son of <a href="/wiki/Colin_Creevey%27s_father" class="mw-redirect" title="Colin Creevey&#39;s father">Mr Creevey</a> and Mrs Creevey, and older brother of <a href="/wiki/Dennis_Creevey" title="Dennis Creevey">Dennis</a>. He was a big fan of the famous <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>, who was a year ahead of him in school. 
</p><p>During his <a href="/wiki/First_year" title="First year">first year</a> at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a>, he was <a href="/wiki/Sorting_ceremony" title="Sorting ceremony">sorted</a> into <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor" title="Gryffindor">Gryffindor House</a>. He was known for taking several pictures of Harry and other students. During his fourth year, he and Dennis became members of <a href="/wiki/Dumbledore%27s_Army" title="Dumbledore&#39;s Army">Dumbledore's Army</a>, an organisation taught and led by Harry Potter. Colin was expelled from Hogwarts in his sixth year, due to <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Voldemort</a>'s policy of not allowing Muggle-borns to attend the school. On <a href="/wiki/2_May" title="2 May">2 May</a>, <a href="/wiki/1998" title="1998">1998</a>, he snuck into the <a href="/wiki/Room_of_Requirement" title="Room of Requirement">Room of Requirement</a> with the rest of Dumbledore's Army and fought in the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a>, during which he was killed by the <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a>. 
</p>

<h3><span id="Early_life_(1981-1992)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_life_.281981-1992.29">Early life <span class="template-c">(1981-1992)</span></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Colin_Creevey?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Early life (1981-1992)">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Colin_Creevey?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Early life (1981-1992)"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Colin Creevey grew up in an ordinary <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggle</a> family. He was the son of a milkman and was amazed to find out he was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> who could attend Hogwarts. Colin was continually awestruck at everything to do with the <a href="/wiki/Wizarding_world" title="Wizarding world">wizarding world</a>. Colin had a younger brother named Dennis, who was also a wizard, an unusual thing to occur for two magical offspring to come from Muggle parents.
</p>

<p>Colin started attending <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts</a> in <a href="/wiki/1992" title="1992">1992</a>. He was sorted into <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor" title="Gryffindor">Gryffindor</a>. He was very frequently seen in Hogwarts with a <a href="/wiki/Colin_Creevey%27s_camera" title="Colin Creevey&#39;s camera">camera</a>, worshipping the ground that Harry Potter walked on. At one point he asked for an autographed photo of Harry, prompting <a href="/wiki/Draco_Malfoy" title="Draco Malfoy">Draco Malfoy</a> to tell everyone Harry was giving out signed photos. Colin responded by accusing Malfoy of being jealous. He was also amazed that he could take moving pictures.<sup id="cite_ref-COS6_7-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COS6-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>He got his photo with the celebrity <a href="/wiki/Defence_Against_the_Dark_Arts" title="Defence Against the Dark Arts">Defence Against the Dark Arts</a> teacher <a href="/wiki/Gilderoy_Lockhart" title="Gilderoy Lockhart">Gilderoy Lockhart</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-COS6_7-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COS6-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup> However, the photographic Harry had refused to appear on the photo, but Lockhart just about managed to drag him into view.<sup id="cite_ref-COS6_7-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COS6-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-COS7_8-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COS7-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup> When he went to Harry to have the photo signed, Harry was in a rush to get to <a href="/wiki/Quidditch" title="Quidditch">Quidditch</a> practise which prompted Colin to say he would go with him, as he had never watched a game before. Harry tried to stop him by claiming it was "boring" but Colin went along nevertheless.<sup id="cite_ref-COS7_8-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COS7-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<figure class="thumb tright show-info-icon" style="width: 252px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/9/92/Colin_bludger.gif/revision/latest?cb=20180116171314" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/9/92/Colin_bludger.gif/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/250?cb=20180116171314" decoding="async" width="250" height="100" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="Colin bludger.gif" data-image-key="Colin_bludger.gif" /></a> 	 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:Colin_bludger.gif" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption">Colin attempting to take pictures during a Quidditch match and almost gets hit by a <a href="/wiki/Bludger" title="Bludger">Bludger</a></p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure>
<p>As they walked to the pitch, Colin kept asking Harry questions about Quidditch. He asked Harry about flying, whether the <a href="/wiki/Nimbus_2000" title="Nimbus 2000">Nimbus 2000</a> was <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter%27s_broomsticks" title="Harry Potter&#39;s broomsticks">Harry's broomstick</a>, whether it was the best <a href="/wiki/Broomstick" title="Broomstick">broom</a> there was (in reality at the time it was the <a href="/wiki/Nimbus_2001" title="Nimbus 2001">Nimbus 2001</a>, but would later be replaced by the <a href="/wiki/Firebolt" title="Firebolt">Firebolt</a>) and finally, the rules of <a href="/wiki/Quidditch" title="Quidditch">Quidditch</a> which Harry resigned to explaining. Colin kept asking more questions the whole way, until Harry managed to shake him off at the changing rooms.<sup id="cite_ref-COS7_8-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COS7-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Colin sat in the stands taking pictures of the training session, asking Harry at one point to look at him while he took a photo. <div class="quote">
<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire">"<i>Harry, guess what? Guess what, Harry? My brother's starting! My brother Dennis!... He's really excited! I just hope he's in Gryffindor! Keep your fingers crossed, eh, Harry?</i>"</span></dd>

<p>During the <a href="/wiki/Sorting_ceremony" title="Sorting ceremony">Sorting ceremony</a> of <a href="/wiki/1994" title="1994">1994</a>, Colin, in an overly excited manner, told <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> to cross his fingers because Colin wanted his younger brother, <a href="/wiki/Dennis_Creevey" title="Dennis Creevey">Dennis</a>, to be sorted into the <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor" title="Gryffindor">Gryffindor House</a> along with him. Dennis looked at Colin as he walked up to the chair, gave him two thumbs up and whispered that he had fallen from the boat on the way across the lake. When Dennis told him something pulled him from the lake, Colin responded that it was probably the giant squid. Colin was delighted when Dennis was sorted into the same house.<sup id="cite_ref-GOF12_13-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF12-13">&#91;13&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Before the <a href="/wiki/Wand_weighing_ceremony" title="Wand weighing ceremony">Wand weighing ceremony</a>, Colin was sent to collect Harry from his <a href="/wiki/Potions_(class)" title="Potions (class)">Potions class</a> with <a href="/wiki/Professor" title="Professor">Professor</a> <a href="/wiki/Severus_Snape" title="Severus Snape">Snape</a>. Snape was annoyed with Colin interrupting his class, but he begrudgingly let Harry leave and take his belonging with him after Colin informed him Harry's attendance was compulsory for all Triwizard champions. Before the ceremony, Colin wished Harry good luck and told him that photographs would be taken of him for the <i><a href="/wiki/Daily_Prophet" title="Daily Prophet">Daily Prophet</a></i>.<sup id="cite_ref-GOF18_14-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF18-14">&#91;14&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Before the First Task of the <a href="/wiki/Triwizard_Tournament" title="Triwizard Tournament">Triwizard Tournament</a>, he and his younger brother Dennis got hold of a stack of "<i>Support <a href="/wiki/Cedric_Diggory" title="Cedric Diggory">Cedric Diggory</a>!</i>" <a href="/wiki/Potter_Stinks_badge" title="Potter Stinks badge">badges</a> and attempted to <a href="/wiki/Charm" title="Charm">bewitch</a> them to say "<i>Support Harry Potter!</i>" instead, all they managed to do was get them stuck on "<i>POTTER STINKS</i>", and later "<i>POTTER REALLY STINKS</i>". He was likely manipulated by <a href="/wiki/Rita_Skeeter" title="Rita Skeeter">Rita Skeeter</a> to give info on Harry's love life, as he is quoted saying that Harry was hardly ever seen out of the company of Hermione, a quote with which Colin may have unintentionally started Rita's theory that Hermione was Harry's "girlfriend".<sup id="cite_ref-GOF19_15-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF19-15">&#91;15&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>At the end of the year, Cedric Diggory was murdered on <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a>'s orders by <a href="/wiki/Peter_Pettigrew" title="Peter Pettigrew">Peter Pettigrew</a> and it was announced that <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Tom Riddle</a> had returned. This would have caused significant anxiety for Colin and his brother Dennis, as Voldemort loathed muggle-borns and with rumours of his rise to power upcoming would have almost certainly scared him.
</p>

<p>Colin's <a href="/wiki/Fourth_year" title="Fourth year">fourth year</a> would be a year of rebellion amongst the students. The previous school year ended with the start of the <a href="/wiki/Second_Wizarding_War" title="Second Wizarding War">Second Wizarding War</a> after <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a> returned and <a href="/wiki/Killing_Curse" title="Killing Curse">murdered</a> <a href="/wiki/Cedric_Diggory" title="Cedric Diggory">Cedric Diggory</a>. However Harry Potter's claims that Voldemort had returned were slated as 'lies' and it wasn't officially announced to be true. Due to the events that took place in Colin's third year, in <a href="/wiki/1995" title="1995">1995</a>, <a href="/wiki/Dolores_Umbridge" title="Dolores Umbridge">Dolores Umbridge</a> was appointed as the eyes of the <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">Ministry</a> to watch over Hogwarts. However, Umbridge was far from what the students had in mind as 'watching over' them, she was a cruel and incompetent teacher who refused to teach practical defensive spells to any students.
</p>

<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Fifth_year">Fifth year</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Colin_Creevey?veaction=edit&amp;section=8" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Fifth year">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Colin_Creevey?action=edit&amp;section=8" title="Edit section: Fifth year"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<p>Not a lot is known about what happened to Colin during his fifth year. Colin and his brother Dennis applauded Harry Potter after the <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor_Quidditch_team" title="Gryffindor Quidditch team">Gryffindor Quidditch team</a> had won the first match of the season, with them wanting a detailed blow by blow analysis.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP14_19-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP14-19">&#91;19&#93;</a></sup> 
</p><p>When <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a> entered Hogwarts and the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_the_Astronomy_Tower" title="Battle of the Astronomy Tower">Battle of the Astronomy Tower</a> struck, <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione Granger</a> called all D.A. members via their coins for reinforcements and Colin was not one of the few members to actually show for battle. Whether he intentionally stayed out of battle or if it was because he no longer carried his coin is unknown. It is likely that Colin stayed for the funeral of <a href="/wiki/Albus_Dumbledore" title="Albus Dumbledore">Albus Dumbledore</a>, showing his respects to the late headmaster as there was no mention of Colin attempting to take photographs during the procession like he normally would in a situation of some sort.
</p>
<h4><span id="Pulled_out_of_Education_(1997-1998)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="Pulled_out_of_Education_.281997-1998.29">Pulled out of Education <span class="template-c">(1997-1998)</span></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Colin_Creevey?veaction=edit&amp;section=9" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Pulled out of Education (1997-1998)">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Colin_Creevey?action=edit&amp;section=9" title="Edit section: Pulled out of Education (1997-1998)"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<p>In 1997, Hogwarts was taken over by <a href="/wiki/Severus_Snape" title="Severus Snape">Severus Snape</a> and <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a>. Due to Voldemort's policy of not allowing Muggle-borns to attend the school, Colin and his brother Dennis were unable to attend Hogwarts for their <a href="/wiki/Sixth_year" title="Sixth year">sixth year</a> and <a href="/wiki/Fourth_year" title="Fourth year">fourth year</a> of education respectively. It is also likely that the Creeveys went into hiding as there was a <a href="/wiki/Muggle-Born_Registration_Commission" title="Muggle-Born Registration Commission">Muggle-Born Registration Commission</a> running where any Muggle-born wizards or witches were to be put on trial regarding how they came to possess a wand.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Personality_and_traits">Personality and traits</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Colin_Creevey?veaction=edit&amp;section=12" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Personality and traits">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Colin_Creevey?action=edit&amp;section=12" title="Edit section: Personality and traits"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>

<p>Colin was a person who was very easily excited. When he first came into Hogwarts, he was excited about everything. He was also a fan of some wizarding "celebrities", such as <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> and <a href="/wiki/Gilderoy_Lockhart" title="Gilderoy Lockhart">Gilderoy Lockhart</a>. It is possible he had a bit of hero worship for Harry, as he was constantly in awe of anything Harry said or did in their first year together.<sup id="cite_ref-COS6_7-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COS6-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup> The night he was attacked by the basilisk he was presumed to be trying to sneak into the hospital wing to visit Harry.<sup id="cite_ref-COS10_10-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COS10-10">&#91;10&#93;</a></sup> During the Triwizard Tournament he and his brother tried to bewitch a number of "Potter Stinks" badges to support Harry, which backfired as they only made the badges say "Potter Really Stinks".<sup id="cite_ref-GOF19_15-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF19-15">&#91;15&#93;</a></sup> This hero worship may have reached its peak when he returned to Hogwarts despite not being a student anymore to help defend it against the forces of Lord Voldemort.<sup id="cite_ref-DH32_20-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH32-20">&#91;20&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>He always took pictures in his first year, which could lead to him being often annoying. This was partly due to him taking pictures at inopportune moments, such as when Ron was <a href="/wiki/Slug-vomiting_Charm" title="Slug-vomiting Charm">belching slugs</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-COS7_8-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COS7-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup> and Harry lost the bones in his arm.<sup id="cite_ref-COS10_10-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COS10-10">&#91;10&#93;</a></sup> It was due to this behaviour that some assumed he had been chosen for an attack by the monster in the Chamber of Secrets. 
</p><p>Colin was also brave, as he fought at the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a> against <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a> and his <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a>, even he was outright not allowed by Professor McGonagall, which he defied by sneaking back in, and was killed during the battle.<sup id="cite_ref-DH32_20-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH32-20">&#91;20&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Magical_abilities_and_skills">Magical abilities and skills</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Colin_Creevey?veaction=edit&amp;section=13" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Colin_Creevey?action=edit&amp;section=13" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li><b><a href="/wiki/Defence_Against_the_Dark_Arts" title="Defence Against the Dark Arts">Defence Against the Dark Arts</a></b>: Colin had at least some skill in Defence Against the Dark Arts, as he learned how to cast various defensive and offensive spells as a committed member of <a href="/wiki/Dumbledore%27s_Army" title="Dumbledore&#39;s Army">Dumbledore's Army</a>. He mastered both the <a href="/wiki/Impediment_Jinx" title="Impediment Jinx">Impediment Jinx</a><sup id="cite_ref-OOTP18_17-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP18-17">&#91;17&#93;</a></sup> and the <a href="/wiki/Full_Body-Bind_Curse" title="Full Body-Bind Curse">Full Body-Bind Curse</a><sup id="cite_ref-OOTPG_18-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTPG-18">&#91;18&#93;</a></sup> while in the D.A., but had trouble <a href="/wiki/Disarming_Charm" title="Disarming Charm">disarming</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP18_17-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP18-17">&#91;17&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Duelling" title="Duelling">Duelling</a></b>: Colin had some skill in duelling and in <a href="/wiki/Martial_magic" title="Martial magic">martial magic</a>, as he fought bravely against the Death Eaters in the Battle of Hogwarts, but he was not strong enough to survive, as he was killed in battle.<sup id="cite_ref-DH32_20-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH32-20">&#91;20&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Charm" title="Charm">Charms</a></b>: Colin was skilled with charms to an extent, as he could successfully cast simple charms, such as the <a href="/wiki/Levitation_Charm" title="Levitation Charm">Levitation Charm</a>, <a href="/wiki/Fire-Making_Spell" title="Fire-Making Spell">Fire-Making Spell</a> and <a href="/wiki/Summoning_Charm" title="Summoning Charm">Summoning Charm</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTPG_18-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTPG-18">&#91;18&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li><b>Photography</b>: Colin was a highly skilful photographer. Despite coming from a Muggle family, he quickly adapted to the wizarding method of producing photographs, with him successfully creating moving photographs in his first year.<sup id="cite_ref-COS6_7-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COS6-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup></li></ul>

<ul><li><b><a href="/wiki/Wand" title="Wand">Wand</a></b>: Colin owned a wand, which was of an unknown length, <a href="/wiki/Wand_wood" title="Wand wood">wand wood</a> and <a href="/wiki/Wand_core" title="Wand core">core</a> material. Like most British wizards, he most likely bought his wand at <a href="/wiki/Ollivanders" title="Ollivanders">Ollivanders</a> in <a href="/wiki/Diagon_Alley" title="Diagon Alley">Diagon Alley</a>, <a href="/wiki/London" title="London">London</a>, before beginning the <a href="/wiki/First_year" title="First year">first year</a> of his <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magical</a> education at Hogwarts in <a href="/wiki/1992" title="1992">1992</a>.</li>

He often loved to take pictures of <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> using his camera, along with images of other important people or happenings.<sup id="cite_ref-COS6_7-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COS6-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-COS7_8-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COS7-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup></li>

<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Family">Family</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Colin_Creevey?veaction=edit&amp;section=16" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Family">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Colin_Creevey?action=edit&amp;section=16" title="Edit section: Family"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Colin most likely had a good relationship with his family. His <a href="/wiki/Colin_Creevey%27s_father" class="mw-redirect" title="Colin Creevey&#39;s father">father</a> was a milkman. Colin stated that his parents were very excited that their son was a wizard, and Colin wanted to take some photos of Hogwarts, in order to show to his father.<sup id="cite_ref-COS6_7-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COS6-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup> Colin was also very close with his brother, <a href="/wiki/Dennis_Creevey" title="Dennis Creevey">Dennis</a>, and was also excited when Dennis came to Hogwarts, too, and was sorted into <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor" title="Gryffindor">Gryffindor</a> house.<sup id="cite_ref-GOF12_13-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF12-13">&#91;13&#93;</a></sup> When Colin was killed during the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a>, his family was likely devastated.
</p>

<p>Colin became a member of <a href="/wiki/Dumbledore%27s_Army" title="Dumbledore&#39;s Army">Dumbledore's Army</a> during his fourth year. It was an organisation founded and led by <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>, in order to teach the students <a href="/wiki/Defence_Against_the_Dark_Arts" title="Defence Against the Dark Arts">Defence Against the Dark Arts</a> and oppose <a href="/wiki/Dolores_Umbridge" title="Dolores Umbridge">Dolores Umbridge</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP16_16-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP16-16">&#91;16&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-OOTP18_17-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP18-17">&#91;17&#93;</a></sup> Colin had a good relationship with the most of them, including <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ron Weasley</a>, <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione Granger</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Ginevra_Weasley" title="Ginevra Weasley">Ginny Weasley</a>, who was also in his year.
</p><p>Other members of the army were <a href="/wiki/Fred_and_George_Weasley" title="Fred and George Weasley">Fred and George Weasley</a>, <a href="/wiki/Neville_Longbottom" title="Neville Longbottom">Neville Longbottom</a>, <a href="/wiki/Lee_Jordan" title="Lee Jordan">Lee Jordan</a>, <a href="/wiki/Angelina_Johnson" title="Angelina Johnson">Angelina Johnson</a>, <a href="/wiki/Katie_Bell" title="Katie Bell">Katie Bell</a> and <a href="/wiki/Alicia_Spinnet" title="Alicia Spinnet">Alicia Spinnet</a> from <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor" title="Gryffindor">Gryffindor</a>, <a href="/wiki/Ernest_Macmillan" title="Ernest Macmillan">Ernie Macmillan</a>, <a href="/wiki/Susan_Bones" title="Susan Bones">Susan Bones</a>, <a href="/wiki/Hannah_Abbott" title="Hannah Abbott">Hannah Abbott</a>, <a href="/wiki/Justin_Finch-Fletchley" title="Justin Finch-Fletchley">Justin Finch-Fletchley</a> and <a href="/wiki/Zacharias_Smith" title="Zacharias Smith">Zacharias Smith</a> from <a href="/wiki/Hufflepuff" title="Hufflepuff">Hufflepuff</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Terry_Boot" title="Terry Boot">Terry Boot</a>, <a href="/wiki/Anthony_Goldstein" title="Anthony Goldstein">Anthony Goldstein</a>, <a href="/wiki/Michael_Corner" title="Michael Corner">Michael Corner</a>, <a href="/wiki/Luna_Lovegood" title="Luna Lovegood">Luna Lovegood</a>, <a href="/wiki/Cho_Chang" title="Cho Chang">Cho Chang</a> and <a href="/wiki/Marietta_Edgecombe" title="Marietta Edgecombe">Marietta Edgecombe</a> from <a href="/wiki/Ravenclaw" title="Ravenclaw">Ravenclaw</a>. All of them (except Zacharias and Marietta) fought at the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a>. Colin's death during the battle was yet another loss that day that Harry felt keenly. Neville helped <a href="/wiki/Oliver_Wood" title="Oliver Wood">Oliver Wood</a> carry Colin's body inside the <a href="/wiki/Great_Hall" title="Great Hall">Great Hall</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-DH32_20-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH32-20">&#91;20&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Colin_Creevey?veaction=edit&amp;section=19" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Colin_Creevey?action=edit&amp;section=19" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li><i>Colin</i> is an anglicised form of the Gaelic name <i>Cailean</i> or <i>Coileain</i>, meaning "whelp," "pup" or "young cub".<sup id="cite_ref-23" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-23">&#91;23&#93;</a></sup> It can also be derived from the name <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Nicholas_(name)" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Nicholas (name)">Nicholas</a>, meaning "victorious people."</li>
<li><i>Creevey</i> is an English surname of Celtic origin, and is a contraction of <i>MacCreevey</i>, meaning "son of Reevey."<sup id="cite_ref-24" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-24">&#91;24&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li>It could also be a surname of Irish origin with variant spellings Creevy and Creavagh, is an Anglicised form of the Gaelic "O'Craoibhe" meaning "descendant of Craobhach", a byname meaning "curly (-headed)" or "prolific" (from "craobh" meaning "branch, bough").<sup id="cite_ref-25" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-25">&#91;25&#93;</a></sup></li></ul>

<ul><li><a href="/wiki/Hugh_Mitchell" title="Hugh Mitchell">Hugh Mitchell</a> played Colin in the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Chamber_of_Secrets_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets (film)">film adaptation</a> of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Chamber_of_Secrets" title="Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets">Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets</a></i>. He also voiced and provided the likeness for the character in the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Order_of_the_Phoenix_(video_game)" title="Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix (video game)">video game adaptation</a> of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Order_of_the_Phoenix" title="Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix">Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix</a></i>.</li>
<li>Hugh Mitchell did not reprise his role in the later films. An added character, <a href="/wiki/Nigel_Wolpert" title="Nigel Wolpert">Nigel Wolpert</a>, served as a replacement for him in the later films. Hugh has said that he is satisfied that Colin died fighting in <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Deathly_Hallows" title="Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows">Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows</a></i>, and that he is indifferent to the Nigel character.<sup id="cite_ref-26" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-26">&#91;26&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li>In the cut scene in which Colin is revealed to have been petrified by <a href="/wiki/Salazar_Slytherin%27s_basilisk" class="mw-redirect" title="Salazar Slytherin&#39;s basilisk">Slytherin's Monster</a>, in the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Chamber_of_Secrets_(video_game)" title="Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets (video game)">PS1 version</a> of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Chamber_of_Secrets" title="Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets">Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets</a></i>, he is wearing <a href="/wiki/Ravenclaw" title="Ravenclaw">Ravenclaw</a> robes. This is both odd and incorrect considering that in gameplay mode he is correctly depicted as a <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor" title="Gryffindor">Gryffindor</a>. This is most likely a mistake made by the game's developers.</li>
<li><i><a href="/wiki/Pottermore" title="Pottermore">Pottermore</a></i> shows a full moon when Professor <a href="/wiki/Albus_Dumbledore" title="Albus Dumbledore">Albus Dumbledore</a> visits Colin in the <a href="/wiki/Hospital_Wing" title="Hospital Wing">Hospital Wing</a> after the <a href="/wiki/Basilisk" title="Basilisk">Basilisk</a>'s attack. In <a href="/wiki/1992" title="1992">1992</a>, however, the full moon happened on November 10, two days after Colin was petrified.</li></ul>
