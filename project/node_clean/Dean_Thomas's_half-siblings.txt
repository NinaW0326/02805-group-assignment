<p>When Dean was forced to go on the run after the <a href="/wiki/Muggle-Born_Registration_Commission" title="Muggle-Born Registration Commission">Muggle-Born Registration Commission</a> was created in <a href="/wiki/1997" title="1997">1997</a>, his parents and sisters were very worried about him. <i><a href="/wiki/Potterwatch" title="Potterwatch">Potterwatch</a></i> asked its listeners to pass on any news they might have about him.<sup id="cite_ref-DH22_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH22-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Dean_Thomas%27s_half-siblings?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Dean_Thomas%27s_half-siblings?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>It is unknown how many half-siblings Dean had or any of their names. <a href="/wiki/J._K._Rowling" title="J. K. Rowling">J. K. Rowling</a> has only said that he has <i>"a number of half-brothers and sisters."</i><sup id="cite_ref-JKR_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-JKR-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li>Contrary to what J.K says, in the Deathly Hallows Lee Jordan says on <i>Potterwatch</i> that "his parents and sisters are desperate for news" meaning he could only have female half-sibling or that Lee knew only of his sisters and not his brothers. Or that his half-brothers do not know he's missing due their age.</li></ul>
