<b>Chief Burdock Muldoon</b> (<a href="/wiki/1429" class="mw-redirect" title="1429">1429</a> - <a href="/wiki/1490" class="mw-redirect" title="1490">1490</a>) was a <a href="/wiki/Great_Britain" title="Great Britain">British</a> <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> and the Chief of the <a href="/wiki/Wizards%27_Council" title="Wizards&#39; Council">Wizards' Council</a> either in the fourteenth<sup id="cite_ref-FBaWtFT_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-FBaWtFT-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> or fifteenth century.<sup id="cite_ref-FWC_1-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-FWC-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Burdock_Muldoon?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Burdock_Muldoon?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_life">Early life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Burdock_Muldoon?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Early life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Burdock_Muldoon?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Early life"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>It is debated whether Muldoon was born in <a href="/wiki/1429" class="mw-redirect" title="1429">1429</a> or about a century earlier. He presumably attended <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> in his youth, like so many other <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizards</a>.
</p>
<h3><span id="As_Chief_of_the_Wizards'_Council"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="As_Chief_of_the_Wizards.27_Council">As Chief of the Wizards' Council</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Burdock_Muldoon?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: As Chief of the Wizards&#039; Council">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Burdock_Muldoon?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: As Chief of the Wizards&#039; Council"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Muldoon attempted to define all <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magical</a> creatures as either a <a href="/wiki/Being" title="Being">Being</a> or a <a href="/wiki/Beast" title="Beast">Beast</a>, on the simple premise that any member of the magical community that walked on two legs were Beings, and all others were Beasts. However, when he called the first meeting of all Beings to discuss new magical laws, the summit was disrupted by <a href="/wiki/Diricawl" title="Diricawl">Diricawls</a>, <a href="/wiki/Augurey" title="Augurey">Augureys</a>, <a href="/wiki/Fwooper" title="Fwooper">Fwoopers</a>, <a href="/wiki/Pixie" title="Pixie">Pixies</a> and <a href="/wiki/Fairy" title="Fairy">Fairies</a> who could not understand what was going on, <a href="/wiki/Troll" title="Troll">Trolls</a> who proceeded to smash apart the meeting chamber with their clubs and <a href="/wiki/Hag" title="Hag">hags</a> who glided around the place in search of children to eat. Muldoon, as Chief of the Council, stood up to open the meeting, but slipped on <a href="/wiki/Porlock" title="Porlock">Porlock</a> dung and ran cursing from the hall.<sup id="cite_ref-FBaWtFT_3-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-FBaWtFT-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Embittered with the incident, Muldoon forswore any further attempts to integrate non-wizard members of the magical community into the <a href="/wiki/Wizards%27_Council" title="Wizards&#39; Council">Wizards' Council</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-FBaWtFT_3-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-FBaWtFT-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Burdock_Muldoon?veaction=edit&amp;section=4" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Burdock_Muldoon?action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li><a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Muldoon" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Muldoon">Muldoon</a> is an Irish family name. It is an anglicisation of the Irish <i>Ó Maoldúin</i>, "descended from Maoldúin," which in turn was a legendary first name. This name means "king or chieftain of the fort". They were said to be of royal birth and styled themselves as the Kings of Lurg, which is now in <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/County_Fermanagh" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:County Fermanagh">County Fermanagh</a>. Famous politicians with that surname include <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Robert_Muldoon" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Robert Muldoon">Sir Robert Muldoon</a> (1921–1992), a Prime Minister of New Zealand; and <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bristow_Muldoon" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Bristow Muldoon">Bristow Muldoon</a> (b. 1964), a former Scottish Labour politician.</li></ul>
