<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="History">History</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Greengrocer?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: History">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Greengrocer?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: History"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p><a href="/wiki/John_Kent" title="John Kent">John Kent</a> was a greengrocer who owned his own <a href="/wiki/John_Kent_Fruiterers" title="John Kent Fruiterers">store</a> as of <a href="/wiki/1991" title="1991">1991</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-PSF_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PSF-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>There was also a greengrocer that <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> and <a href="/wiki/Rubeus_Hagrid" title="Rubeus Hagrid">Rubeus Hagrid</a> walked past on their way to the <a href="/wiki/Leaky_Cauldron" title="Leaky Cauldron">Leaky Cauldron</a> in 1991.<sup id="cite_ref-PSF_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PSF-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>The <a href="/wiki/Muggle-born" title="Muggle-born">Muggle-born</a> <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch</a> <a href="/wiki/Mary_Cattermole" title="Mary Cattermole">Mary Cattermole</a>'s parents were greengrocers. <a href="/wiki/Dolores_Umbridge" title="Dolores Umbridge">Dolores Umbridge</a> taunted her with this fact during her trial before the <a href="/wiki/Muggle-Born_Registration_Commission" title="Muggle-Born Registration Commission">Muggle-Born Registration Commission</a> in <a href="/wiki/1997" title="1997">1997</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-DH13_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH13-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
