</p><p><b>Minister Basil Flack</b> was <a href="/wiki/Minister_for_Magic" title="Minister for Magic">Minister for Magic</a> in <a href="/wiki/1752" class="mw-redirect" title="1752">1752</a>.  He may have been appointed a temporary acting Minister instead of being elected by the <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">Wizarding</a> population due to the crisis of the <a href="/wiki/Goblin_Rebellions" title="Goblin Rebellions">goblin rebellions</a>.
</p><p>He was the Minister for Magic with the shortest term as of <a href="/wiki/2014" title="2014">2014</a>, only holding the office for two months. He resigned the post after the <a href="/wiki/Goblin" title="Goblin">goblins</a> joined forces with the <a href="/wiki/Werewolf" title="Werewolf">werewolves</a>, presumably as part of the same <a href="/wiki/18th_century_goblin_rebellion" title="18th century goblin rebellion">rebellion</a> that sparked his predecessor <a href="/wiki/Albert_Boot" title="Albert Boot">Albert Boot</a>'s resignation.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Basil_Flack?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Basil_Flack?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p><a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Basil_(name)" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Basil (name)">Basil</a> is a given name derived from the Greek <i>βασιλεύς</i>, meaning "king". <i>Flack</i> is an alternate spelling of the word <i>flak</i>, which is English slang for "adverse criticism". 
</p>
