<p>This <b>group of students</b> were four boys and a girl and were <a href="/wiki/Cedric_Diggory" title="Cedric Diggory">Cedric Diggory</a>'s friends, and was often seen with him.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Cedric_Diggory%27s_friends?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Cedric_Diggory%27s_friends?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>During the <a href="/wiki/1994%E2%80%931995_school_year" title="1994–1995 school year">1994–1995 school year</a>, at some point, they were going to <a href="/wiki/Charms_(class)" title="Charms (class)">Charms class</a>, when Cedric's bag split open, due to <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> casting the <a href="/wiki/Severing_Charm" title="Severing Charm">Severing Charm</a> on it, in order for him to speak to him privately. They tried to pick his things up, but Cedric told them to go ahead and tell <a href="/wiki/Filius_Flitwick" title="Filius Flitwick">Professor Flitwick</a> that he would be late.<sup id="cite_ref-GOF20_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF20-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> They, understandably, resented Harry Potter for being the <i>second</i> Hogwarts champion in the <a href="/wiki/Triwizard_Tournament" title="Triwizard Tournament">Triwizard Tournament</a>, and so stealing their friend's limelight, in their eyes.
</p>
