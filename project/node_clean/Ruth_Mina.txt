<b>Ruth Mina</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch</a> and a <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor" title="Gryffindor">Gryffindor</a> student at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> in the <a href="/wiki/1990s" title="1990s">1990s</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-list_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-list-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Sometime in the <a href="/wiki/1990s" title="1990s">1990s</a>, the <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Library" title="Hogwarts Library">library</a> books she took out were overdue, and thus her name was included on a list of students with overdue books that was put up on the bulletin board in <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor_Tower" title="Gryffindor Tower">Gryffindor Tower</a> by <a href="/wiki/Irma_Pince" title="Irma Pince">Madam Pince</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-list_2-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-list-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
