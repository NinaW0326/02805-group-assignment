<p><b>Mulciber</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Dark_wizard" class="mw-redirect" title="Dark wizard">Dark&#160;wizard</a>&#160;and one of the earliest <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a> of <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a>. He was possibly the father of <a href="/wiki/Mulciber_(Marauder-era)" class="mw-redirect" title="Mulciber (Marauder-era)">Mulciber</a>. He fought in the <a href="/wiki/First_Wizarding_War" title="First Wizarding War">First Wizarding War</a>, and travelled to <a href="/wiki/Hogsmeade" title="Hogsmeade">Hogsmeade</a> in order to "wish good luck" to Tom Riddle in trying to get a teacher position at Hogwarts. It is possible that Mulciber was deceased before the start of the <a href="/wiki/Second_Wizarding_War" title="Second Wizarding War">Second Wizarding War</a>, but, if he was not, he possibly fought at the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a> and was presumably killed or imprisoned in <a href="/wiki/Azkaban" title="Azkaban">Azkaban</a> after Voldemort's final defeat.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Mulciber_I?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Mulciber_I?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_life">Early life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Mulciber_I?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Early life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Mulciber_I?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Early life"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Mulciber attended <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> in his youth, and upon graduating he became one of the first <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a> under <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a>.
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="First_Wizarding_War">First Wizarding War</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Mulciber_I?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: First Wizarding War">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Mulciber_I?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: First Wizarding War"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Mulciber fought in the <a href="/wiki/First_Wizarding_War" title="First Wizarding War">First Wizarding War</a>, along with many other <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a>. He was also one of the group who travelled to Hogsmeade to "say <i>good luck</i>" to Voldemort, during his attempt to ask for work at Hogwarts, along with fellow Death Eaters <a href="/wiki/Nott" title="Nott">Nott</a>, <a href="/wiki/Rosier" title="Rosier">Rosier</a> and <a href="/wiki/Antonin_Dolohov" title="Antonin Dolohov">Antonin Dolohov</a>. He presumably fought in many battles during the First Wizarding War. It's unknown if he was still alive by the time of his master's downfall, or whether or not he was sent to Azkaban.
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Later_life">Later life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Mulciber_I?veaction=edit&amp;section=4" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Later life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Mulciber_I?action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Later life"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>It's possible that Mulciber also took part in the <a href="/wiki/Second_Wizarding_War" title="Second Wizarding War">Second Wizarding War</a> and fought in many battles, including the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a>. If this is the case he would've most likely been killed or imprisoned in Azkaban for his crimes.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Mulciber_I?veaction=edit&amp;section=5" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Mulciber_I?action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>May refer to <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mulciber" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Mulciber">Mulciber</a> (Hephaestus), meaning "smelter", is an alternate name for the gentle son of Zeus and Hera who had a club foot and was a craftsman and blacksmith in Greek mythology. In Roman mythology he is the god of fire and metallurgy, Vulcan.
</p><p>Mulciber is also a character in John Milton's <i><a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Paradise_Lost" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Paradise Lost">Paradise Lost</a></i>, a story based on mankind falling into sin and the struggle of heaven and hell. Mulciber was a fallen angel who is the architect of Pandemonium, the capital of Hell and home to the demons' council.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Mulciber_I?veaction=edit&amp;section=6" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Mulciber_I?action=edit&amp;section=6" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>During his trial, <a href="/wiki/Igor_Karkaroff" title="Igor Karkaroff">Igor Karkaroff</a> accused a Mulciber of being a Death Eater. It is not known whether he was referring to the older or younger Mulciber.</li>
<li>It is possible that Mulciber may have been one of the unidentified boys seen with <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Tom Riddle</a> in <a href="/wiki/Horace_Slughorn" title="Horace Slughorn">Horace Slughorn</a>'s memory, although this has not been confirmed.</li>
<li>It is unclear if he fought in the <a href="/wiki/Second_Wizarding_War" title="Second Wizarding War">Second Wizarding War</a>, as he would've been an elderly man if he was still alive.</li></ul>
