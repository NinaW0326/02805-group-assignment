<ul><li><a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Curd" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Curd">Curd</a> is a semisolid substance formed when milk sours. It is used as the base in making some <a href="/wiki/Cheese" title="Cheese">cheeses</a>.</li></ul>
