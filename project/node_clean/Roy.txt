<b>Roy</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> and <a href="/wiki/Slytherin" title="Slytherin">Slytherin</a> student at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School</a> during the <a href="/wiki/1990s" title="1990s">1990s</a>. In <a href="/wiki/1992" title="1992">1992</a> he became a member of the <a href="/wiki/Duelling_Club" title="Duelling Club">Duelling Club</a> and was ranked seventh on the club's top-ten <a href="/wiki/Duelling" title="Duelling">duellists</a> list.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Roy?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Roy?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The name "Roy" is the anglicised form of Ruadh. A notable bearer was the Scottish outlaw and folk hero Rob Roy (1671-1734). It is often associated with French roi "king".
</p><p>Roy is also the pet form of Roibeard (the Gaelic form of <a href="/wiki/Robert_(disambiguation)" title="Robert (disambiguation)">Robert</a>), the short form of Royal (meaning "king" or "kingly") or the Anglicised version of the name Ruadh (meaning "red") <a target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener" class="external autonumber" href="http://www.20000-names.com/male_r_names_3.htm">[1]</a> <a target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener" class="external autonumber" href="http://webspace.webring.com/people/ge/edgarbook/names/frames.html">[2]</a>. It is also possible that Roy is a shortened form of another name in the Potter universe such as Royden (see <a href="/wiki/Royden_Poke" title="Royden Poke">Royden Poke</a>) or Royston (see <a href="/wiki/Royston_Idlewind" title="Royston Idlewind">Royston Idlewind</a>).
</p>
