<b>Alex Sykes</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> who began attending <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> between <a href="/wiki/1987" title="1987">1987</a> and <a href="/wiki/1989" title="1989">1989</a>. By the <a href="/wiki/1993-1994_school_year" class="mw-redirect" title="1993-1994 school year">1993-1994 school year</a>, he had been made a <a href="/wiki/Prefect" title="Prefect">Prefect</a>, and was tasked with patrolling areas of the school off limits to students.<sup id="cite_ref-POAG_4-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-POAG-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
