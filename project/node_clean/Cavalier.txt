<p>A <b>cavalier</b> was the name given to the Royalist supporters of <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Charles_I_of_England" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Charles I of England">King Charles I</a> and his son <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Charles_II_of_England" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Charles II of England">Charles II</a> during the <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/English_Civil_War" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:English Civil War">English Civil War</a>, the Interregnum, and the Restoration (<a href="/wiki/1642" class="mw-redirect" title="1642">1642</a> â€“ c. <a href="/wiki/1679" class="mw-redirect" title="1679">1679</a>).<sup id="cite_ref-Wikipedia_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Wikipedia-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>There were many cavalier <a href="/wiki/Suit_of_armour" title="Suit of armour">suits of armour</a> at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Castle" title="Hogwarts Castle">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a>, under <a href="/wiki/Spirit" title="Spirit">spectral</a> possession. They could be aggressive to passers-by, but were particularly vulnerable to the <i><a href="/wiki/Fire-Making_Spell" title="Fire-Making Spell">Incendio</a></i> and <i><a href="/wiki/Freezing_Spell" title="Freezing Spell">Glacius</a></i> spells.<sup id="cite_ref-PAgba_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PAgba-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
