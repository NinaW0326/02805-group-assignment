<p><b>Flavius Belby</b> (<a href="/wiki/1715" class="mw-redirect" title="1715">1715</a> - <a href="/wiki/1791" class="mw-redirect" title="1791">1791</a>) was the only known <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> to survive a <a href="/wiki/Lethifold" title="Lethifold">Lethifold</a> attack. Through his quick thinking and bravery, he discovered the <a href="/wiki/Patronus_Charm" title="Patronus Charm">one way</a> to successfully drive off a Lethifold in <a href="/wiki/Papua_New_Guinea" title="Papua New Guinea">Papua New Guinea</a> in <a href="/wiki/1782" class="mw-redirect" title="1782">1782</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-FBWFT_2-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-FBWFT-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> and was subsequently immortalised on a <a href="/wiki/Chocolate_Frog_Card" title="Chocolate Frog Card">Chocolate Frog Card</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-COSG_1-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COSG-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Flavius_Belby?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Flavius_Belby?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_life">Early life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Flavius_Belby?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Early life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Flavius_Belby?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Early life"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Flavius was born in <a href="/wiki/1715" class="mw-redirect" title="1715">1715</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-COSG_1-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COSG-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> At some point in his life he was elected the President of his local <a href="/wiki/Gobstones" title="Gobstones">Gobstones Club</a>, a moment that he would always remember to be one of the happiest of his life.<sup id="cite_ref-FBWFT_2-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-FBWFT-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Lethifold_attack">Lethifold attack</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Flavius_Belby?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Lethifold attack">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Flavius_Belby?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Lethifold attack"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<div class="floatleft"><a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/8/81/Belby_-_Lethifold_attack_-_FB2017.jpeg/revision/latest?cb=20170502181839" class="image"><img alt="Belby - Lethifold attack - FB2017.jpeg" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/8/81/Belby_-_Lethifold_attack_-_FB2017.jpeg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/250?cb=20170502181839" decoding="async" width="250" height="96" data-image-name="Belby - Lethifold attack - FB2017.jpeg" data-image-key="Belby_-_Lethifold_attack_-_FB2017.jpeg" /></a></div>
<p>In <a href="/wiki/1782" class="mw-redirect" title="1782">1782</a>, aged 66 or 67, Belby was on holiday in <a href="/wiki/Papua_New_Guinea" title="Papua New Guinea">Papua New Guinea</a>. One night, when he was lying awake on his bed, a <a href="/wiki/Lethifold" title="Lethifold">Lethifold</a> entered his bedroom, sliding through the little space underneath the door. Belby was awake (which is unusual for a Lethifold attack), and as such, had an opportunity to defend himself. As the creature attempted to smother him, he grabbed his wand and attempted against the beast, but neither the <a href="/wiki/Stunning_Spell" title="Stunning Spell">Stunning Spell</a> nor the <a href="/wiki/Impediment_Jinx" title="Impediment Jinx">Impediment Jinx</a> had any effects on it. Belby struggled but the creature continued strangling him. Incapable of breathing, Belby was next to unconsciousness when, using his last ounce of strength, he recalled the memory of the day that he was elected president of his local <a href="/wiki/Gobstones" title="Gobstones">Gobstones Club</a>, and produced a corporeal <a href="/wiki/Patronus_Charm" title="Patronus Charm">Patronus</a> that drove the creature off.<sup id="cite_ref-FBWFT_2-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-FBWFT-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Belby would later write about this near-death experience:<sup id="cite_ref-FBWFT_2-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-FBWFT-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<div class="quote">
<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;Fantastic Beasts and Where to Find Them (real)">"<i>To my horror, the shadow began to creep up the bed, and I felt its slight weight upon me. It resembled nothing so much as a rippling black cape, the edges fluttering slightly as it slithered up the bed towards me. Paralysed with fear, I felt its clammy touch upon my chin...Desperately I mustered up my last reserve of energy. Pointing the wand away from myself into the deadly folds of the creature, summoning the memory of the day I had been voted President of the local Gobstones Club, I performed the Patronus Charm. Almost at once I felt fresh air upon my face. I looked up to see that deathly shadow being thrown into the air upon the horns of my Patronus. It flew across the room and slithered swiftly out of sight.</i>"</span></dd>
<dd>&#8212;Belby's account of the Lethifold Attack<sup class="noprint"><a href="/wiki/Fantastic_Beasts_and_Where_to_Find_Them_(real)" class="mw-redirect" title="Fantastic Beasts and Where to Find Them (real)">[src]</a></sup></dd>
<dd></dd></dl></div>
<p>Belby died less than a decade later, in <a href="/wiki/1791" class="mw-redirect" title="1791">1791</a>, but the attack and his miraculous survival was remembered for centuries after.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Legacy">Legacy</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Flavius_Belby?veaction=edit&amp;section=4" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Legacy">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Flavius_Belby?action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Legacy"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Belby's account of the attack was recorded in the book <i><a href="/wiki/Fantastic_Beasts_and_Where_to_Find_Them" title="Fantastic Beasts and Where to Find Them">Fantastic Beasts and Where to Find Them</a></i> under the entry "Dealing with the <a href="/wiki/Lethifold" title="Lethifold">Lethifold</a>".
</p><p>For being the only known wizard to survive a <a href="/wiki/Lethifold" title="Lethifold">Lethifold</a> attack though a combination of not being completely asleep and the use of a <a href="/wiki/Patronus_Charm" title="Patronus Charm">Patronus Charm</a>, he appeared on a <a href="/wiki/Chocolate_Frog_Card" title="Chocolate Frog Card">Chocolate Frog Card</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-COSG_1-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COSG-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Magical_skills_and_abilities">Magical skills and abilities</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Flavius_Belby?veaction=edit&amp;section=5" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Magical skills and abilities">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Flavius_Belby?action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Magical skills and abilities"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li><b><a href="/wiki/Charm" title="Charm">Charms</a></b>: Belby was exceptionally skilled in charms to the point where he was able to cast a corporeal <a href="/wiki/Patronus_Charm" title="Patronus Charm">Patronus Charm</a>, which is immensely advanced <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magic</a> on its own, but even more impressively, he was able to cast it flawlessly while being attacked and suffocated under a <a href="/wiki/Lethifold" title="Lethifold">Lethifold</a>, and succeeded in becoming the first person to survive an attack from one.<sup id="cite_ref-FBWFT_2-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-FBWFT-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Defence_Against_the_Dark_Arts" title="Defence Against the Dark Arts">Defence Against the Dark Arts</a></b>: Belby was able to successfully and resourcefully cast some defensive spells, such as the <a href="/wiki/Stunning_Spell" title="Stunning Spell">Stunning Spell</a> and the <a href="/wiki/Impediment_Jinx" title="Impediment Jinx">Impediment Jinx</a>, meaning he was evidently competent with defensive magic.</li></ul>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Flavius_Belby?veaction=edit&amp;section=6" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Flavius_Belby?action=edit&amp;section=6" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>"Flavius" is a Roman family name which meant "golden" or "yellow-haired" from Latin <i>flavus</i> "yellow, golden". Flavius was the family name of the 1st-century Roman emperors Vespasian, Titus and Domitian.
</p>
