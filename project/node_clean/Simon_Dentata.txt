<b>Simon Dentata</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> and the author of <i><a href="/wiki/The_Owl_Airforce" title="The Owl Airforce">The Owl Airforce: True Life Tales of War in Europe</a></i>. The book, published sometime prior to <a href="/wiki/December" title="December">December</a> <a href="/wiki/1926" title="1926">1926</a>, explored real tales about magic involvement in the <a href="/wiki/First_World_War" title="First World War">First World War</a>, one instance being the actions of an <a href="/wiki/Owl" title="Owl">owl</a> airforce.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
