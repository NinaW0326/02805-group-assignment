<b>Jane Court</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch</a> who began attending <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> in <a href="/wiki/1980" title="1980">1980</a><sup id="cite_ref-age_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-age-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-graduation_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-graduation-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> and was Sorted into <a href="/wiki/Hufflepuff" title="Hufflepuff">Hufflepuff</a>. In <a href="/wiki/1984" title="1984">1984</a>, she had been made a <a href="/wiki/Prefect" title="Prefect">Prefect</a>, and was responsible for watching over the <a href="/wiki/First_year" title="First year">first years</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> At some point in her life, she served time in <a href="/wiki/Azkaban" title="Azkaban">Azkaban</a> for an unknown crime, and was released by <a href="/wiki/September" title="September">September</a> <a href="/wiki/1988" title="1988">1988</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-Y5SQ_5-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Y5SQ-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Jane_Court?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Jane_Court?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span id="Hogwarts_years_(1980-1987)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="Hogwarts_years_.281980-1987.29">Hogwarts years <span class="template-c">(1980-1987)</span></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Jane_Court?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Hogwarts years (1980-1987)">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Jane_Court?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Hogwarts years (1980-1987)"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>She started going to Hogwarts in 1980, where she became a good duelist and met <a href="/wiki/Jacob%27s_sibling" title="Jacob&#39;s sibling">Jacob's sibling</a> in 1984.
</p><p>During the <a href="/wiki/Start-of-Term_Feast" title="Start-of-Term Feast">Start-of-Term Feast</a> of the <a href="/wiki/1985%E2%80%931986_school_year" title="1985–1986 school year">1985–1986 school year</a>, Jane told <a href="/wiki/Jacob%27s_sibling" title="Jacob&#39;s sibling">Jacob's sibling</a> she thought she saw the missing <a href="/wiki/Ben_Copper" title="Ben Copper">Ben Copper</a> studying <a href="/wiki/Transfiguration" title="Transfiguration">Transfiguration</a> by the <a href="/wiki/Clock_Tower" title="Clock Tower">Clock Tower</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Jane was still at Hogwarts the following two years and graduated in <a href="/wiki/1987" title="1987">1987</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-graduation_3-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-graduation-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span id="Later_life_(1987-)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="Later_life_.281987-.29">Later life <span class="template-c">(1987-)</span></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Jane_Court?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Later life (1987-)">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Jane_Court?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Later life (1987-)"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>After Hogwarts, Jane was sentenced to serve time in <a href="/wiki/Azkaban" title="Azkaban">Azkaban</a> for an unknown crime. She was released by September <a href="/wiki/1988" title="1988">1988</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-Y5SQ_5-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Y5SQ-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>In September of 1988 she joined other previous prefects to meet with new prefects in the <a href="/wiki/Three_Broomsticks_Inn" title="Three Broomsticks Inn">Three Broomsticks Inn</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-Y5SQ_5-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Y5SQ-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Later this year, she met <a href="/wiki/Jacob%27s_sibling" title="Jacob&#39;s sibling">Jacob's sibling</a> in <a href="/wiki/Knockturn_Alley" title="Knockturn Alley">Knockturn Alley</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
