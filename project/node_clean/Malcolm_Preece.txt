<b>Malcolm Preece</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> and student of <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a>, <a href="/wiki/Sorting_ceremony" title="Sorting ceremony">sorted</a> into <a href="/wiki/Hufflepuff" title="Hufflepuff">Hufflepuff</a> who was a <a href="/wiki/Chaser" title="Chaser">Chaser</a> on the <a href="/wiki/Hufflepuff_Quidditch_team" title="Hufflepuff Quidditch team">Hufflepuff Quidditch team</a> when it was captained by <a href="/wiki/Cedric_Diggory" title="Cedric Diggory">Cedric Diggory</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-QWC_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-QWC-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Malcolm_Preece?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Malcolm_Preece?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The name "Malcolm" is from Scottish MÃ¡el Coluim which means "disciple of Saint Columba". This was the name of four kings of Scotland starting in the 10th century, including Malcolm III, who became king after killing Macbeth, the usurper who had murdered his father. The character Malcolm in Shakespeare's tragedy <i>Macbeth</i> (1606) is based on him.
</p>
