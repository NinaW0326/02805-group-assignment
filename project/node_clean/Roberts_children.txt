<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Roberts_children?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Roberts_children?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The two children' of <a href="/wiki/Roberts" title="Roberts">Mr Roberts</a> and <a href="/wiki/Roberts%27s_wife" title="Roberts&#39;s wife">Mrs Roberts</a> were attacked by <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a> the day after the <a href="/wiki/1994_Quidditch_World_Cup" title="1994 Quidditch World Cup">1994 Quidditch World Cup</a>. The youngest, a boy, was spun like a top sixty feet up in the air, while his mother, father, and older sibling floated nearby.<sup id="cite_ref-GOF9_1-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF9-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>After the incident, their memories would have been <a href="/wiki/Memory_Charm" title="Memory Charm">altered</a> to forget what had happened along with their parents.<sup id="cite_ref-GOF10_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF10-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
