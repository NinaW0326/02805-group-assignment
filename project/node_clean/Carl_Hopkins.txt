<b>Carl Hopkins</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> and <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor" title="Gryffindor">Gryffindor</a> student who most likely began attending <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> in the <a href="/wiki/1990s" title="1990s">1990s</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-HBPG_3-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBPG-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-age_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-age-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>During the <a href="/wiki/1996%E2%80%931997_school_year" title="1996–1997 school year">1996–1997 school year</a>, he took <a href="/wiki/Nastily_Exhausting_Wizarding_Test" title="Nastily Exhausting Wizarding Test">N.E.W.T.-level</a> <a href="/wiki/Potions_(class)" title="Potions (class)">Potions</a>, as he someday hoped to be an <a href="/wiki/Auror" title="Auror">Auror</a>. He confessed to <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>, in the year <a href="/wiki/1997" title="1997">1996-1997</a>, that he would like a new <a href="/wiki/Broomstick" title="Broomstick">broom</a>. He also admits it is a disaster in <a href="/wiki/Potion" title="Potion">Potions</a>. At year end it can be seen walking in the <a href="/wiki/Viaduct_Courtyard" title="Viaduct Courtyard">Entrance Courtyard</a>, near <a href="/wiki/Scott_Ancrum" title="Scott Ancrum">Scott Ancrum</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-HBPG_3-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBPG-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
