This <b><a href="/wiki/Dark_Wizard" title="Dark Wizard">Dark Wizard</a></b>&#160;was a <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eater</a> who fought during the <a href="/wiki/Second_Wizarding_War" title="Second Wizarding War">Second Wizarding War</a>.

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_male_Death_Eater_killed_on_the_Hogwarts_Seventh_Floor?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_male_Death_Eater_killed_on_the_Hogwarts_Seventh_Floor?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>This wizard was presumably born in <a href="/wiki/Great_Britain" title="Great Britain">Great Britain</a> or <a href="/wiki/Ireland" title="Ireland">Ireland</a> some time during the <a href="/wiki/20th_century" title="20th century">20th century</a>. At some point in his adult life, he became well-versed in the <a href="/wiki/Dark_Arts" title="Dark Arts">Dark Arts</a> and joined the <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a>, becoming a loyal servant of <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a>. He presumably fought during the <a href="/wiki/First_Wizarding_War" title="First Wizarding War">First Wizarding War</a> with his fellow Death Eaters for Voldemort's <a href="/wiki/Pure-blood_supremacy" title="Pure-blood supremacy">cause</a>. 
</p><p>On <a href="/wiki/2_May" title="2 May">2 May</a> <a href="/wiki/1998" title="1998">1998</a>, This Death Eater was called by his master to fight in the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a>, which he responded to and fought with his fellow Death Eaters against the <a href="/wiki/Hogwartian" title="Hogwartian">defenders</a> of <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts</a>. During the battle, he was killed by a student or member of staff on the <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Castle#Seventh_Floor" title="Hogwarts Castle">Seventh Floor</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
