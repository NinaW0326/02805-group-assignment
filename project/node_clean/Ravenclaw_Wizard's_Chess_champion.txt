<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Ravenclaw_Wizard%27s_Chess_champion?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Ravenclaw_Wizard%27s_Chess_champion?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>This girl came to be educated at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> and was <a href="/wiki/Sorting_ceremony" title="Sorting ceremony">sorted</a> into <a href="/wiki/Ravenclaw" title="Ravenclaw">Ravenclaw House</a>. Her <a href="/wiki/Head_of_House" title="Head of House">Head of House</a> was <a href="/wiki/Professor" title="Professor">Professor</a> <a href="/wiki/Filius_Flitwick" title="Filius Flitwick">Filius Flitwick</a>, the <a href="/wiki/Charms_(class)" title="Charms (class)">Charms</a> <a href="/wiki/Charms_Master" title="Charms Master">Master</a>, and she shared a dormitory with four other girls in her year in the <a href="/wiki/Ravenclaw_Tower" title="Ravenclaw Tower">Ravenclaw Tower</a>. She was skilled in <a href="/wiki/Wizard%27s_Chess" title="Wizard&#39;s Chess">Wizard's Chess</a> and could be usually found playing in the <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Library" title="Hogwarts Library">Library</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTPG_2-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTPG-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
