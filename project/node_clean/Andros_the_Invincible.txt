<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Andros_the_Invincible?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Andros_the_Invincible?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Lifetime">Lifetime</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Andros_the_Invincible?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Lifetime">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Andros_the_Invincible?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Lifetime"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Andros was born in <a href="/wiki/Greece" title="Greece">Greece</a>, in the <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Classical_antiquity" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Classical antiquity">Classical antiquity</a>. At some point, he mastered <a href="/wiki/Wandless_magic" title="Wandless magic">wandless spellcasting</a> as well as the <a href="/wiki/Patronus_Charm" title="Patronus Charm">Patronus Charm</a>. His corporeal Patronus was claimed to be the size of a <a href="/wiki/Giant" title="Giant">Giant</a>, although whether its form was in fact a Giant or some other gigantic form is unclear. In recognition of his skill in wizardry, he earned the epithet 'the Invincible'.
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Post-mortem">Post-mortem</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Andros_the_Invincible?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Post-mortem">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Andros_the_Invincible?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Post-mortem"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>By the <a href="/wiki/1990s" title="1990s">1990s</a>, Andros was featured on a <a href="/wiki/Chocolate_Frog_Card" title="Chocolate Frog Card">Chocolate Frog Card</a>, which recorded his wizarding skills and depicted him conjuring his famous Patronus <a href="/wiki/Wandless_magic" title="Wandless magic">wandlessly</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-:0_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:0-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Magical_abilities_and_skills">Magical abilities and skills</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Andros_the_Invincible?veaction=edit&amp;section=4" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Andros_the_Invincible?action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li><b><a href="/wiki/Charm" title="Charm">Charms</a>: </b>Andros was exceptionally proficient and accomplished in charm-work, as he was able to produce a corporeal <a href="/wiki/Patronus_Charm" title="Patronus Charm">Patronus</a> to the size of a <a href="/wiki/Giant" title="Giant">giant</a>. Given the immense difficulty of casting such a powerful and advanced protective charm, this shows the true incredible extent of his <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magical</a> power as he was able to cast the spell to such a wondrous extent.</li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Wandless_magic" title="Wandless magic">Wandless magic</a></b>: Andros was also capable of producing his incredibly large Patronus without using a <a href="/wiki/Wand" title="Wand">wand</a>, showing him to him to have been a remarkably powerful and talented <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> of astonishing magical ability, given the high level of difficulty required in casting <a href="/wiki/Spell" title="Spell">spells</a> wandlessly and being able to do so with such a complicated charm.</li></ul>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Andros_the_Invincible?veaction=edit&amp;section=5" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Andros_the_Invincible?action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>Although Andros was featured on a famous wizard card, it is known that the size and corporeal form of a Patronus has no influence upon its power. In fact, another famous Patronus, belonging to <a href="/wiki/Illyius" title="Illyius">Illyius</a>, took the form of a little <a href="/wiki/Mouse" title="Mouse">mouse</a>, but was still of immense power.<sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup></li></ul>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Andros_the_Invincible?veaction=edit&amp;section=6" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Andros_the_Invincible?action=edit&amp;section=6" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>The word <i>AnÄ“r</i>, of which <i>Andros</i> is the genitive form, is Ancient Greek for "man". The word is currently used as a prefix in many non-Greek words. It is also the name of a Greek island (Andros), an Atlantic island, and several fictional characters in franchises such as <i>Power Rangers</i>, <i>Sandman</i> and <i>Marvel Comics</i>.</li></ul>
