<p>This <b>individual</b> served as the President of a "far-distant" <a href="/wiki/Foreign_country" title="Foreign country">country</a> in <a href="/wiki/1996" title="1996">1996</a>. In the summer of that year, the President was to telephone the <a href="/wiki/Prime_Minister" title="Prime Minister">Prime Minister</a> of the <a href="/wiki/Great_Britain" title="Great Britain">United Kingdom</a> but due to the visit of the recently sacked <a href="/wiki/Minister_for_Magic" title="Minister for Magic">Minister for Magic</a> <a href="/wiki/Cornelius_Fudge" title="Cornelius Fudge">Cornelius Fudge</a> and his successor <a href="/wiki/Rufus_Scrimgeour" title="Rufus Scrimgeour">Rufus Scrimgeour</a> he was made to forget to call, and telephone the next night instead.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
