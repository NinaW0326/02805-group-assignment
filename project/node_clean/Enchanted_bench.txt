<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Description">Description</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Enchanted_bench?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Description">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Enchanted_bench?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Description"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Enchanted benches are, when used properly, safe, though improper use of them such as reckless racing can cause them to catch <a href="/wiki/Fire" title="Fire">fire</a>. Though they are safe to fly, the experience can be disconcerting, as enchanted benches have a mind of their own, capable of flying at unpredictable angles, sharply ascending with little warning, and suddenly stopping. Therefore, they are not recommended to be flown by those with back or neck problems, those susceptible to <a href="/wiki/Motion_sickness" title="Motion sickness">motion sickness</a>, or <a href="/wiki/Pregnancy" title="Pregnancy">pregnant</a> women.<sup id="cite_ref-WWHP_1-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-WWHP-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>For added safety, enchanted benches are built with a back pouch meant to store small objects which may fall during the ride or injure others, such as <a href="/wiki/Glasses" title="Glasses">glasses</a> and <a href="/wiki/Wand" title="Wand">wands</a>. <a href="/wiki/Smart_Locker" title="Smart Locker">Smart Lockers</a> are supplied for riders with possessions larger than what fit inside these pouches, so that they may store their possessions beforehand.<sup id="cite_ref-WWHP_1-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-WWHP-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>In his lifetime, the subject depicted in a certain <a href="/wiki/Portrait_of_a_Quidditch_player" title="Portrait of a Quidditch player">portrait of a Quidditch player</a> hanging in <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Castle" title="Hogwarts Castle">Hogwarts Castle</a> test-flew enchanted benches. He apparently flew them frequently, taking them on such journeys as a trip from the <a href="/wiki/South_Pole" title="South Pole">South Pole</a> to the <a href="/wiki/North_Pole" title="North Pole">North Pole</a> and a flight over the <a href="/wiki/Himalayas" title="Himalayas">Himalayas</a>. The latter trip was also taken by a party of young ladies, who fell out during the course of the ride and thus prevented the Quidditch player from returning them.<sup id="cite_ref-WWHP_1-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-WWHP-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
