<b>Cantankerus Nott</b> (fl. <a href="/wiki/1930s" title="1930s">1930s</a>) was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> and a member of the <a href="/wiki/Pure-blood" title="Pure-blood">pure-blood</a> <a href="/wiki/Nott_family" title="Nott family">Nott family</a>. He was widely believed to have been the one who penned the <i><a href="/wiki/Pure-Blood_Directory" title="Pure-Blood Directory">Pure-Blood Directory</a></i>. This book listed the "<a href="/wiki/Sacred_Twenty-Eight" title="Sacred Twenty-Eight">twenty-eight truly pure-blood families</a>", with "<i>the aim of helping such families maintain the purity of their bloodlines</i>". The book was also published anonymously in Britain in the early <a href="/wiki/1930s" title="1930s">1930s</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-PM_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PM-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Cantankerus_Nott?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Cantankerus_Nott?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>"Cantankerous" is a word meaning ill-tempered, surly, argumentative, and uncooperative.</li></ul>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Cantankerus_Nott?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Cantankerus_Nott?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>He was most likely related to <a href="/wiki/Theodore_Nott" title="Theodore Nott">Theodore Nott</a> as well as his <a href="/wiki/Nott" title="Nott">father</a>. It is also possible that he was Theodore's father.</li>
<li>Some of the families presented in his apparent directory weren't as pure-blooded as he claimed, such as the <a href="/wiki/Ollivander_family" title="Ollivander family">Ollivander family</a>. Also many pure-blooded families such as the <a href="/wiki/Potter_family" title="Potter family">Potters</a> were not listed, in fact many pure-blood supremacists were angered by their lack of inclusion. Due to this, Cantankerus Nott seems to have been a highly biased individual.</li></ul>
