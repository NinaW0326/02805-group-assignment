<b>Troy "Tank" Duvall</b> was an <a href="/wiki/United_States_of_America" title="United States of America">American</a> <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> who played as a <a href="/wiki/Beater" title="Beater">Beater</a> on the <a href="/wiki/American_National_Quidditch_team" title="American National Quidditch team">American National Quidditch team</a>, and also served as the team's <a href="/wiki/Captain" title="Captain">Captain</a> at the <a href="/wiki/1994" title="1994">1994</a> <a href="/wiki/Quidditch_World_Cup" title="Quidditch World Cup">Quidditch World Cup</a>. He wore a helmet with a brown <a href="/wiki/Pumpkin" title="Pumpkin">pumpkin</a> design on it.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>By <a href="/wiki/1996" title="1996">1996</a>, Troy was included on a <a href="/wiki/Chocolate_Frog_Card" title="Chocolate Frog Card">Chocolate Frog Card</a> for his achievements in <a href="/wiki/Quidditch" title="Quidditch">Quidditch</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Troy_Duvall?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Troy_Duvall?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The name "Troy" is from a surname that originally denoted a person from the city of Troyes in <a href="/wiki/France" title="France">France</a>. This was also the name of the ancient city that was besieged by the Greeks in Homer's <i>Iliad</i>.
</p>
