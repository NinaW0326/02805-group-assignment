<b>Poderick Crookshank</b><sup id="cite_ref-HPLE_1-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HPLE-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> (born c. <a href="/wiki/1532" class="mw-redirect" title="1532">1532</a>) was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> whose <a href="/wiki/Portrait" title="Portrait">portrait</a>  hung at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-TMOHP_2-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-TMOHP-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>His portrait was inscribed "<i>Ano Dm. <a href="/wiki/1568" class="mw-redirect" title="1568">1568</a></i>", indicating the year in which it was painted, and "<i>Ætatis Suæ 36</i>", indicating his age at the time the portrait was made, as <i>anno etatis suæ</i> is <a href="/wiki/Latin" title="Latin">Latin</a> for "<i>in the year of his age</i>"<i>.</i><sup id="cite_ref-HPLE_1-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HPLE-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-TMOHP_2-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-TMOHP-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Poderick_Crookshank?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Poderick_Crookshank?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>Poderick Crookshank's portrait seems to have been based on a <a target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener" class="external text" href="http://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/0/03/Anthonis_Mor_024.jpg">1568 portrait</a> of <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Henry_Lee_of_Ditchley" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Henry Lee of Ditchley">Henry Lee of Ditchley</a> by <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Antonis_Mor" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Antonis Mor">Antonis Mor</a>.</li>
<li>At <i><a href="/wiki/The_Making_of_Harry_Potter" title="The Making of Harry Potter">The Making of Harry Potter</a></i>, the portrait of Poderick Crookshank was erroneously put in a frame labelled "<a href="/wiki/Google_Flange" title="Google Flange">Sir Google Flange</a>," but Crookshank was correctly identified in <i><a href="/wiki/The_Paintings_of_Hogwarts:_Masterpieces_from_the_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry_Sets" title="The Paintings of Hogwarts: Masterpieces from the School of Witchcraft and Wizardry Sets">The Paintings of Hogwarts: Masterpieces from the School of Witchcraft and Wizardry Sets</a>.</i></li>
<li>Within the in-universe continuity, this individual may have been <a href="/wiki/Crookshanks" title="Crookshanks">Crookshanks</a>'s namesake, just like a <a href="/wiki/Hedwig_(wizard)" title="Hedwig (wizard)">name from <i>A History of Magic</i></a> was picked by <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> as <a href="/wiki/Hedwig" title="Hedwig">his owl</a>'s name.</li></ul>
