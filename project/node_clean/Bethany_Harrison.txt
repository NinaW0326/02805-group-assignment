<p><b>Bethany Harrison</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Great_Britain" title="Great Britain">British</a> <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggle</a> child, the daughter of <a href="/wiki/Don_Harrison" title="Don Harrison">Don Harrison</a>, a construction foreman.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Bethany_Harrison?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Bethany_Harrison?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Bethany made a <a href="/wiki/Bethany_Harrison%27s_sketch" class="mw-redirect" title="Bethany Harrison&#39;s sketch">sketch</a> of a <a href="/wiki/Winifred_Whittle" title="Winifred Whittle">witch</a> that provided a clue for <a href="/wiki/Mathilda_Grimblehawk" title="Mathilda Grimblehawk">Mathilda Grimblehawk</a> and her partner investigating the <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magical</a> <a href="/wiki/Beast" title="Beast">beast</a> attack at the construction site.
</p><p>Soon after, Bethany went missing, although the flowers in her <a href="/wiki/Bethany_Harrison%27s_toybox" title="Bethany Harrison&#39;s toybox">toybox</a> provided further clues as to her possible whereabouts. Her <a href="/wiki/Bethany_Harrison%27s_fairy_necklace" title="Bethany Harrison&#39;s fairy necklace">fairy necklace</a> was later found in <a href="/wiki/Winifred_Whittle" title="Winifred Whittle">Winifred Whittle</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Winifred_Whittle%27s_cottage" title="Winifred Whittle&#39;s cottage">cottage</a> and she was ultimately escorted out of the forest by <a href="/wiki/Magnus" title="Magnus">Magnus</a>, a <a href="/wiki/Centaur" title="Centaur">centaur</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Her memory was later <a href="/wiki/Memory_Charm" title="Memory Charm">Obliviated</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
