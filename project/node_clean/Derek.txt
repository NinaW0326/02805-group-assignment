<b>Derek</b> (born c. <a href="/wiki/1982" title="1982">1982</a>) was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">student</a> who began attending <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts</a> in <a href="/wiki/1993" title="1993">1993</a>. During his <a href="/wiki/First_year" title="First year">first year</a>, he stayed at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">school</a> over the <a href="/wiki/Christmas_holidays" title="Christmas holidays">Christmas holidays</a>, and was surprised when <a href="/wiki/Albus_Dumbledore" title="Albus Dumbledore">Albus Dumbledore</a> asked him whether he had had any of the <a href="/wiki/Chipolata" title="Chipolata">Chipolatas</a> at the <a href="/wiki/Christmas" title="Christmas">Christmas</a> <a href="/wiki/Christmas_Feast" title="Christmas Feast">feast</a>. He blushed deeply at being personally addressed by the <a href="/wiki/Headmaster" title="Headmaster">Headmaster</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-PA11_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PA11-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Derek?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Derek?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The name "Derek" is from a Low German form of <i>Theodoric</i>. It was imported to England from the Low Countries in the 15th century.
</p>
