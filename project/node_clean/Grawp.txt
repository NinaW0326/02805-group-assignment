<p><b>Grawp</b> (b. post <a href="/wiki/1931" title="1931">1931</a>) was a <a href="/wiki/Giant" title="Giant">giant</a> that was known, despite standing a full sixteen feet tall, for his small stature and short height.<sup id="cite_ref-OP30_2-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OP30-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> He was the son of <a href="/wiki/Fridwulfa" title="Fridwulfa">Fridwulfa</a> and an <a href="/wiki/Grawp%27s_father" title="Grawp&#39;s father">unnamed giant</a> and, therefore, the maternal half-brother of <a href="/wiki/Rubeus_Hagrid" title="Rubeus Hagrid">Rubeus Hagrid</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-OP30_2-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OP30-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Grawp?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Grawp?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_life">Early life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Grawp?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Early life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Grawp?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Early life"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Around <a href="/wiki/1931" title="1931">1931</a>, the <a href="/wiki/Giant" title="Giant">giantess</a> <a href="/wiki/Fridwulfa" title="Fridwulfa">Fridwulfa</a> abandoned her human husband <a href="/wiki/Rubeus_Hagrid%27s_father" title="Rubeus Hagrid&#39;s father">Mr Hagrid</a> and their infant child <a href="/wiki/Rubeus_Hagrid" title="Rubeus Hagrid">Rubeus Hagrid</a>, a <a href="/wiki/Half-giant" title="Half-giant">half-giant</a>, who was smaller in stature than she wanted.<sup id="cite_ref-OP30_2-10" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OP30-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> She consequently rejoined her own kind in the mountains, and there she found a new mate.<sup id="cite_ref-OP30_2-11" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OP30-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> The two gave birth to the giant Grawp who, despite being a full-blooded giant, was still particularly small, and due to this he, too, was abandoned.<sup id="cite_ref-OP30_2-12" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OP30-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> He was frequently bullied by the other giants thereafter due to his small size.<sup id="cite_ref-OP30_2-13" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OP30-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="1995">1995</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Grawp?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: 1995">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Grawp?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: 1995"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<div class="quote">
<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix">"<i>Well, tha's why it took so long, see. Could on'y travel by nigh' an' through wild country an' stuff. Course, he covers the ground pretty well when he wants ter, but he kep' wantin' ter go back.</i>"</span></dd>
<dd>&#8212;Hagrid and Grawp's journey to Hogwarts<sup class="noprint"><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Order_of_the_Phoenix" title="Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix">[src]</a></sup></dd>
<dd></dd></dl></div>
<p><a href="/wiki/Rubeus_Hagrid" title="Rubeus Hagrid">Rubeus Hagrid</a>, along with his newly-found friend <a href="/wiki/Olympe_Maxime" title="Olympe Maxime">Olympe Maxime</a>, paid a visit to a <a href="/wiki/Giant_colony" title="Giant colony">tribe of giants</a> in June of 1995, on the orders of headmaster <a href="/wiki/Albus_Dumbledore" title="Albus Dumbledore">Albus Dumbledore</a> of Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry in an attempt to bring the giants over as allies of the <a href="/wiki/Order_of_the_Phoenix" title="Order of the Phoenix">Order of the Phoenix</a> in the war against <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a> and his <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-OP20_14-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OP20-14">&#91;14&#93;</a></sup>
After three days at the giant camp, Hagrid discovered his half-brother Grawp. He later dragged Grawp back to <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Castle" title="Hogwarts Castle">Hogwarts</a> with him due to the bullying he knew Grawp had received, and presumably to protect Grawp from Voldemort's enslavement of the giants.<sup id="cite_ref-OP30_2-14" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OP30-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> Despite his previous abuse, Grawp continuously attempted to return to the camp, but Hagrid forced him through <a href="/wiki/England" title="England">England</a> against his will.<sup id="cite_ref-OP30_2-15" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OP30-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Hagrid kept Grawp in the <a href="/wiki/Forbidden_Forest" title="Forbidden Forest">Forbidden Forest</a> for months, teaching him manners and pieces of English as they went.<sup id="cite_ref-OP30_2-16" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OP30-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> The other inhabitants of the forest, namely the <a href="/wiki/Centaur" title="Centaur">centaurs</a>, noticed these attempts and tried to warn Hagrid that his trial was futile.<sup id="cite_ref-OP27_15-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OP27-15">&#91;15&#93;</a></sup> 
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="1996">1996</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Grawp?veaction=edit&amp;section=4" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: 1996">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Grawp?action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: 1996"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>

<p>During a <a href="/wiki/Quidditch" title="Quidditch">Quidditch</a> Match in 1995, in which Ron played as <a href="/wiki/Keeper" title="Keeper">Keeper</a>, Hagrid secretely revealed Grawp's presence to <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> and <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione Granger</a>, asking them to look after Grawp in case he got sacked by <a href="/wiki/Dolores_Umbridge" title="Dolores Umbridge">Dolores Umbridge</a>. Despite their disapproval of Grawp's presence, they agreed in the end.<sup id="cite_ref-OP30_2-17" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OP30-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> After Hagrid was later temporarily sacked by Umbridge, Grawp became upset about his absense and after chasing the <a href="/wiki/Forbidden_Forest_Centaur_colony" title="Forbidden Forest Centaur colony">centaurs</a> away from Harry and Hermione before they could attack them, demanded about Hagrid's presence from Hermione, who responded that she didn't know where he was.<sup id="cite_ref-OP33_16-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OP33-16">&#91;16&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>After <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Voldemort</a>'s return and Umbridge's sacking from Hogwarts, Grawp was later reunited with Hagrid and was later sent by Dumbledore and Hagrid to live in the mountains near <a href="/wiki/Hogsmeade" title="Hogsmeade">Hogsmeade</a>, presumably in the <a href="/wiki/Cave_near_Hogsmeade" title="Cave near Hogsmeade">cave</a> where Hagrid hid after his sacking by Umbridge, with Hagrid commenting he was far happier there than in the Forest. Hagrid additionally considered getting a female giant for Grawp to socialise with, something Harry usually would have protested against but was unable to do so due to his grief over <a href="/wiki/Sirius_Black" title="Sirius Black">Sirius Black</a>'s death.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP38_17-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP38-17">&#91;17&#93;</a></sup>
Grawp also proved to be an indirect factor to Harry, Ron and Hermione discontinuing <a href="/wiki/Care_of_Magical_Creatures" title="Care of Magical Creatures">Care of Magical Creatures</a> once they began their sixth year as they disapproved of Hagrid's idea to train Grawp to be his teaching assistant alongside Hagrid's tendancy to teach dangerous creatures over more amicable ones.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP9_18-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP9-18">&#91;18&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<p>Grawp was aggressive and violent, both common traits for a true giant and was known to attack <a href="/wiki/Rubeus_Hagrid" title="Rubeus Hagrid">his brother</a> when in a foul mood.<sup id="cite_ref-OP30_2-19" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OP30-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> Despite this, Grawp <a href="/wiki/Love" title="Love">loved</a> his brother dearly and was badly upset when left alone in June of 1996.<sup id="cite_ref-OP33_16-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OP33-16">&#91;16&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<p>Grawp initially had great difficulties learning to trust Hagrid, however, as it took nearly a year to gain his trust. The language barrier may have been a factor in this since Hagrid could only speak English and, after nearly a year, Grawp could barely speak comprehensible English.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP30_19-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP30-19">&#91;19&#93;</a></sup> After nearly three years, he could still only just talk properly, and could not pronounce long or difficult words such as "<a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione</a>", which he said as "Hermy", and "<a href="/wiki/Rubeus_Hagrid" title="Rubeus Hagrid">Hagrid</a>", which he pronounced as "Hagger".<sup id="cite_ref-DH36_13-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH36-13">&#91;13&#93;</a></sup>

<p>However, while his language skills were still barely comprehensible, Grawp's personality on the other hand changed immensely under Hagrid's influence, despite doubts that Hagrid's efforts were completely futile. By the time he attended the funeral of <a href="/wiki/Albus_Dumbledore" title="Albus Dumbledore">Albus Dumbledore</a>, Grawp became more kind, gentle, and compassionate. He showed sorrow during the funeral and attempted to comfort Hagrid, who was more devastated by Dumbledore's death, by patting and embracing him (though causing Hagrid's chair to sink into the ground). Grawp also showed happiness and joy after <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Voldemort</a>'s final defeat, demonstrating that he had developed an understanding of human emotions. However, while most of his violent side was lost, he still retained some of it when he fought against the other giants during the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a>.
</p>
