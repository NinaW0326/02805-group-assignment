<b>Felix Rosier</b> (born c. 1969) was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> who began attending <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> in <a href="/wiki/1980" title="1980">1980</a> and was Sorted into <a href="/wiki/Slytherin" title="Slytherin">Slytherin</a>. In <a href="/wiki/1984" title="1984">1984</a>, he had been made a <a href="/wiki/Prefect" title="Prefect">Prefect</a>, and was responsible for watching over the <a href="/wiki/First_year" title="First year">first years</a>. He graduated from Hogwarts in <a href="/wiki/1987" title="1987">1987</a>.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Felix_Rosier?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Felix_Rosier?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span id="Early_life_(1968/69-1980)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_life_.281968.2F69-1980.29">Early life <span class="template-c">(1968/69-1980)</span></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Felix_Rosier?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Early life (1968/69-1980)">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Felix_Rosier?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Early life (1968/69-1980)"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Felix was born in the late <a href="/wiki/1960s" title="1960s">1960s</a>. His father may have been a top <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eater</a> killed or imprisoned during the <a href="/wiki/First_Wizarding_War" title="First Wizarding War">First Wizarding War</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-Death_Eater_father_4-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Death_Eater_father-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span id="Hogwarts_years_(1980-1987)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="Hogwarts_years_.281980-1987.29">Hogwarts years <span class="template-c">(1980-1987)</span></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Felix_Rosier?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Hogwarts years (1980-1987)">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Felix_Rosier?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Hogwarts years (1980-1987)"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Felix started his education in <a href="/wiki/1980" title="1980">1980</a> and was sorted into <a href="/wiki/Slytherin" title="Slytherin">Slytherin</a>. He cared about the <a href="/wiki/House_Cup" title="House Cup">House Cup</a> and having Slytherin in the lead. Sometimes this meant breaking the rules, such as using the <a href="/wiki/Freezing_Charm" title="Freezing Charm">Freezing Charm</a> on <a href="/wiki/Slytherin_Quidditch_team" title="Slytherin Quidditch team">Slytherin Quidditch team</a>'s opponents to help Slytherin win the <a href="/wiki/Inter-House_Quidditch_Cup" title="Inter-House Quidditch Cup">Quidditch Cup</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-Y3PrefectSQ_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Y3PrefectSQ-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>In his <a href="/wiki/Fifth_year" title="Fifth year">Fifth year</a>, Felix became the Slytherin male <a href="/wiki/Prefect" title="Prefect">Prefect</a>.
</p>
<h3><span id="After_Hogwarts_(1987-)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="After_Hogwarts_.281987-.29">After Hogwarts <span class="template-c">(1987-)</span></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Felix_Rosier?veaction=edit&amp;section=4" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: After Hogwarts (1987-)">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Felix_Rosier?action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: After Hogwarts (1987-)"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Felix later went on to become a <a href="/wiki/Dragonologist" title="Dragonologist">dragonologist</a> after graduating from Hogwarts and specialised in <a href="/wiki/Peruvian_Vipertooth" title="Peruvian Vipertooth">Peruvian Vipertooths</a>.
</p>

<p>At some point, Bill Weasley had contacted Felix and the other prefects for a get-together at the <a href="/wiki/Three_Broomsticks_Inn" title="Three Broomsticks Inn">Three Broomsticks</a> pub in Hogsmeade.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Magical_abilities_and_skills">Magical abilities and skills</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Felix_Rosier?veaction=edit&amp;section=5" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Felix_Rosier?action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li><b><a href="/wiki/Care_of_Magical_Creatures" title="Care of Magical Creatures">Care of Magical Creatures</a></b>: Felix was knowledgeable and skilled in the field of <a href="/wiki/Magizoology" title="Magizoology">Magizoology</a>, the study of <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magical</a> <a href="/wiki/Creature" title="Creature">creatures</a>. After graduating from Hogwarts, Felix became a <a href="/wiki/Dragonologist" title="Dragonologist">Dragonologist</a> and specialised in <a href="/wiki/Peruvian_Vipertooth" title="Peruvian Vipertooth">Peruvian Vipertooths</a>.</li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Charm" title="Charm">Charms</a></b>: Felix possessed a degree of talent in charm-work, being able to successfully use the <a href="/wiki/Freezing_Charm" title="Freezing Charm">Freezing Charm</a> to immobilise <a href="/wiki/Quidditch" title="Quidditch">Quidditch</a> opponents of the <a href="/wiki/Slytherin_Quidditch_team" title="Slytherin Quidditch team">Slytherin Quidditch team</a> surreptitiously.<sup id="cite_ref-Y3PrefectSQ_1-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Y3PrefectSQ-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> He was also skilled with the <a href="/wiki/Knockback_Jinx" title="Knockback Jinx">Knockback Jinx</a>.</li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Potion" title="Potion">Potions</a></b>: Felix was proficient at potion-making. He was capable of brewing the <a href="/wiki/Babbling_Beverage" title="Babbling Beverage">Babbling Beverage</a>.</li></ul>

<ul><li><b><a href="/wiki/Wand" title="Wand">Wand</a></b>: Felix likely purchased his wand at the age of eleven before attending <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a>.</li>
<li><b>Slytherin <a href="/wiki/Prefect_badge" title="Prefect badge">Prefect badge</a></b>: After Felix became the Slytherin Prefect, he obtained this Prefect badge which he wore on his uniform.</li></ul>
