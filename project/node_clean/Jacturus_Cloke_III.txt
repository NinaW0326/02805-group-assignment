<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Jacturus_Cloke_III?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Jacturus_Cloke_III?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>He lived in the <a href="/wiki/Cloke_Mansion" title="Cloke Mansion">Cloke Mansion</a> near <a href="/wiki/Upper_Flagley" title="Upper Flagley">Upper Flagley</a> along with the <a href="/wiki/Ghost" title="Ghost">ghost</a> of his ancestor <a href="/wiki/Horatio_Cloke" title="Horatio Cloke">Horatio Cloke</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-FBC9-1_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-FBC9-1-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>He attacked <a href="/wiki/Megan_Scott" title="Megan Scott">Megan Scott</a> and <a href="/wiki/Incarcerous_Spell" title="Incarcerous Spell">tied her up</a> when she was wandering in a nearby graveyard, but was driven off by a protective <a href="/wiki/Unicorn" title="Unicorn">unicorn</a>. He had to be <a href="/wiki/Stunning_Spell" title="Stunning Spell">Stupified</a> by <a href="/wiki/Mathilda_Grimblehawk" title="Mathilda Grimblehawk">Mathilda Grimblehawk</a> when she and <a href="/wiki/Mathilda_Grimblehawk%27s_partner" title="Mathilda Grimblehawk&#39;s partner">her partner</a> came to investigate.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> He later revealed that he was trying to gather valuable <a href="/wiki/Unicorn_hair" title="Unicorn hair">unicorn hair</a>, <a href="/wiki/Unicorn_horn" title="Unicorn horn">horn</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Unicorn_blood" title="Unicorn blood">blood</a> to restore the lost family fortune.<sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Magical_abilities_and_skills">Magical abilities and skills</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Jacturus_Cloke_III?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Jacturus_Cloke_III?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li><b><a href="/wiki/Transfiguration" title="Transfiguration">Transfiguration</a></b>: Cloke was capable of performing <a href="/wiki/Conjuration" title="Conjuration">conjuration</a>, a highly advanced and difficult branch of transfiguration. He effectively conjured ropes with the <a href="/wiki/Incarcerous_Spell" title="Incarcerous Spell">Incarcerous Spell</a> to bind <a href="/wiki/Megan_Scott" title="Megan Scott">Megan Scott</a> in an <a href="/wiki/Upper_Flagley" title="Upper Flagley">Upper Flagley</a> graveyard.<sup id="cite_ref-FBC9-1_1-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-FBC9-1-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup></li></ul>
