<b>Charis Crouch</b> (née <b>Black</b>) (<a href="/wiki/1919" title="1919">1919</a> – <a href="/wiki/1973" title="1973">1973</a>) was a <a href="/wiki/Pure-blood" title="Pure-blood">pure-blood</a> witch, the third daughter of <a href="/wiki/Arcturus_Black_II" title="Arcturus Black II">Arcturus Black</a> and <a href="/wiki/Lysandra_Yaxley" title="Lysandra Yaxley">Lysandra Yaxley</a> and sister of <a href="/wiki/Callidora_Black" title="Callidora Black">Callidora</a> and <a href="/wiki/Cedrella_Black" title="Cedrella Black">Cedrella</a>. She was married to <a href="/wiki/Caspar_Crouch" title="Caspar Crouch">Caspar Crouch</a>, and produced three children: a <a href="/wiki/Caspar_Crouch%27s_son" title="Caspar Crouch&#39;s son">son</a> and <a href="/wiki/Caspar_Crouch%27s_daughters" title="Caspar Crouch&#39;s daughters">two daughters</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-BFT_1-20" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-BFT-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Charis may be the mother of <a href="/wiki/Bartemius_Crouch_Senior" title="Bartemius Crouch Senior">Barty Crouch Snr</a>, although this has not been confirmed. Through her disowned sister <a href="/wiki/Cedrella_Black" title="Cedrella Black">Cedrella</a>, she is a maternal aunt of <a href="/wiki/Arthur_Weasley" title="Arthur Weasley">Arthur Weasley</a><sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> and a relative of the <a href="/wiki/Weasley_family" title="Weasley family">Weasley family</a>, and through her other sister <a href="/wiki/Callidora_Black" title="Callidora Black">Callidora</a>, she is related to the <a href="/wiki/Longbottom_family" title="Longbottom family">Longbottom family</a>.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Charis_Black?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Charis_Black?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Charis is named after the <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Charites" title="wikipedia:Charites"><img alt="WP favicon.PNG" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/7/7c/WP_favicon.PNG/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/18?cb=20110518111852" decoding="async" width="18" height="14" data-image-name="WP favicon.PNG" data-image-key="WP_favicon.PNG" /></a> <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Charites" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Charites">Charites</a> on  <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Wikipedia" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Wikipedia">Wikipedia</a> (singular <i>Charis</i>), the Greek goddesses of charm and beauty. The Greek <i>charis</i> means "grace, kindness."
</p><p>Like her mother <a href="/wiki/Lysandra_Yaxley" title="Lysandra Yaxley">Lysandra</a> and her sister <a href="/wiki/Callidora_Black" title="Callidora Black">Callidora</a>, Charis is named after a type of butterfly or moth, specifically a butterfly genus of the Riodinini tribe.
</p><p>There is an asteroid called <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/627_Charis" title="wikipedia:627 Charis"><img alt="WP favicon.PNG" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/7/7c/WP_favicon.PNG/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/18?cb=20110518111852" decoding="async" width="18" height="14" data-image-name="WP favicon.PNG" data-image-key="WP_favicon.PNG" /></a> <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/627_Charis" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:627 Charis">627 Charis</a> on  <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Wikipedia" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Wikipedia">Wikipedia</a> which was discovered on 4 March 1907 (twelve years before Charis's birth); this connects her to the Black family's history of astrological names.
</p>
