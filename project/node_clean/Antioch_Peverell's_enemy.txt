<p>This <b>man</b> (fl. <a href="/wiki/13th_century" title="13th century">13th century</a>) was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> with whom <a href="/wiki/Antioch_Peverell" title="Antioch Peverell">Antioch Peverell</a> had a quarrel.<sup id="cite_ref-TBB_1-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-TBB-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>

<p>When Antioch obtained his <a href="/wiki/Elder_Wand" title="Elder Wand">unbeatable wand</a>, he killed this wizard in a <a href="/wiki/Duel_between_Antioch_Peverell_and_his_enemy" title="Duel between Antioch Peverell and his enemy">duel</a> and later went into an inn, where he boasted about his powerful wand. <a href="/wiki/Antioch_Peverell" title="Antioch Peverell">Peverell</a>, while sleeping "wine-sodden", had his wand stolen and his throat slit by a <a href="/wiki/Antioch_Peverell%27s_killer" title="Antioch Peverell&#39;s killer">thief</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-TBB_1-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-TBB-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
