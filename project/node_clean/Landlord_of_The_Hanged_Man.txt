<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="The_Riddle_murders">The Riddle murders</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Landlord_of_The_Hanged_Man?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: The Riddle murders">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Landlord_of_The_Hanged_Man?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: The Riddle murders"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>The night the <a href="/wiki/Riddle_family" title="Riddle family">Riddles</a> were murdered, in the summer of <a href="/wiki/1943" title="1943">1943</a>, his pub was filled with villagers who wanted to gossip about the murders. After the <a href="/wiki/Riddles%27_cook" title="Riddles&#39; cook">Riddles' cook</a> entered the pub, bringing the news of the arrest of <a href="/wiki/Frank_Bryce" title="Frank Bryce">Frank Bryce</a>, the landlord commented that the war turned Bryce funny, perhaps hinting that he believed the gardener was guilty of the murders of his employers.<sup id="cite_ref-GoF_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GoF-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
