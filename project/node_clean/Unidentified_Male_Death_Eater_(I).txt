The <b>Unidentified Male Death Eater</b>&#160;was a <a href="/wiki/Dark_Wizard" title="Dark Wizard">Dark Wizard</a>&#160;and a servant of <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a>.&#160;He was present&#160;at the meeting at&#160;<a href="/wiki/Malfoy_Manor" title="Malfoy Manor">Malfoy Manor</a>&#160;in <a href="/wiki/July" title="July">July</a>&#160;<a href="/wiki/1997" title="1997">1997</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-DHF1_2-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DHF1-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> and also participated in the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a>&#160;during the <a href="/wiki/Second_Wizarding_War" title="Second Wizarding War">Second Wizarding War</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-DHF2_4-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DHF2-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_life">Early life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_Male_Death_Eater_(I)?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Early life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_Male_Death_Eater_(I)?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Early life"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>It is possible that he attended <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> around the <a href="/wiki/1970s" title="1970s">1970s</a>, and was subsequently <a href="/wiki/Sorting_ceremony" title="Sorting ceremony">sorted</a>&#160;into <a href="/wiki/Slytherin" title="Slytherin">Slytherin</a> house, along with most other <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a>. He would later graduate and become a Death Eater, swearing allegiance to <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-DHF1_2-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DHF1-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

He listened to <a href="/wiki/Severus_Snape" title="Severus Snape">Severus Snape</a> and Voldemort speak about the <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">Ministry of Magic</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-DHF1_2-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DHF1-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>

He was&#160;later seen standing in the destroyed courtyard after Voldemort boasted&#160;about Harry's supposed death. <p>After Voldemort was&#160;killed by Harry, this man was possibly killed during the final stages of the battle, or was imprisoned in <a href="/wiki/Azkaban" title="Azkaban">Azkaban</a>&#160;for his crimes as a Death Eater. It is also possible that he went on the run as an outlaw.&#160;
