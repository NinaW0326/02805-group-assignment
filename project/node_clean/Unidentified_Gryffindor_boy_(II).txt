<p>He was born during the <a href="/wiki/1980s" title="1980s">1980s</a> and attended <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a>, where he was sorted into <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor" title="Gryffindor">Gryffindor</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTPF_2-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTPF-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Fifth_year">Fifth year</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_Gryffindor_boy_(II)?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Fifth year">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_Gryffindor_boy_(II)?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Fifth year"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>In <a href="/wiki/1995" title="1995">1995</a> he and his friend were walking in the corridors when <a href="/wiki/Dolores_Umbridge" title="Dolores Umbridge">Dolores Umbridge</a> came by. She used a <a href="/wiki/Dolores_Umbridge%27s_neatness_charm" title="Dolores Umbridge&#39;s neatness charm">spell</a> to tidy their ties and pull their shirts into their trousers.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTPF_2-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTPF-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
