This <b>girl</b> was a small, dark-haired <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch</a> who began attending <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> in <a href="/wiki/1995" title="1995">1995</a>, and was <a href="/wiki/Sorting_ceremony" title="Sorting ceremony">Sorted</a> into <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor" title="Gryffindor">Gryffindor</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-house_2-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-house-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> In September 1995, <a href="/wiki/Fred_and_George_Weasley" title="Fred and George Weasley">Fred and George Weasley</a> had her test out their <a href="/wiki/Fainting_Fancies" title="Fainting Fancies">Fainting Fancies</a>, along with several other <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor" title="Gryffindor">Gryffindor</a> <a href="/wiki/First_year" title="First year">first years</a>. <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione Granger</a>, unable to stand around and watch Fred and George test their products on innocent first years, intervened. When the girl came to after passing out from the sweet she was given, George asked her if she felt okay, to which she replied, <i>"I think so."</i>
