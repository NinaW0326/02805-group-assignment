<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_life">Early life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_boy_who_attended_Charm_School?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Early life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_boy_who_attended_Charm_School?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Early life"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<div class="quote">
<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;Harry Potter and the Philosopher&#39;s Stone (video game)">"<i>I hope I get into Hufflepuff. That was my dad's house.</i>"</span></dd>
<dd>&#8212;The boy<sup class="noprint"><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Philosopher%27s_Stone_(video_game)" title="Harry Potter and the Philosopher&#39;s Stone (video game)">[src]</a></sup></dd>
<dd></dd></dl></div>
<p>This individual was born somewhere in the British Isles in around <a href="/wiki/1979" title="1979">1979</a> or <a href="/wiki/1980" title="1980">1980</a>, to a <a href="/wiki/Unidentified_boy_who_attended_Charm_School%27s_father" title="Unidentified boy who attended Charm School&#39;s father">former Hufflepuff student at Hogwarts</a>. He was apparently born into a <a href="/wiki/Half-blood" title="Half-blood">half-blood</a> family, as he appeared to have some contact with the <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggle</a> society.<sup id="cite_ref-bloodstatus_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-bloodstatus-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> Sometime in his childhood, he attended <a href="/wiki/Charm_School" title="Charm School">Charm School</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-PSgbc_3-10" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PSgbc-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Hogwarts_years">Hogwarts years</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_boy_who_attended_Charm_School?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Hogwarts years">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_boy_who_attended_Charm_School?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Hogwarts years"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<div class="quote">
<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;Harry Potter and the Philosopher&#39;s Stone (video game)">"<i>Are they going to show a film?</i>"</span></dd>
<dd>&#8212;The boy, while <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Express" title="Hogwarts Express">en route</a> to <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Castle" title="Hogwarts Castle">Hogwarts Castle</a>, on <a href="/wiki/1_September" title="1 September">1 September</a>, <a href="/wiki/1991" title="1991">1991</a><sup class="noprint"><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Philosopher%27s_Stone_(video_game)" title="Harry Potter and the Philosopher&#39;s Stone (video game)">[src]</a></sup></dd>
<dd></dd></dl></div>
<p>He started at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> in <a href="/wiki/1991" title="1991">1991</a>, and hoped to be <a href="/wiki/Sorting_ceremony" title="Sorting ceremony">Sorted</a> into his <a href="/wiki/Hufflepuff" title="Hufflepuff">father's old house</a>, but was Sorted in <a href="/wiki/Slytherin" title="Slytherin">Slytherin</a> instead. In his first week of classes, he commented that his old <a href="/wiki/Charm_School" title="Charm School">Charm School</a> was nothing like <a href="/wiki/Filius_Flitwick" title="Filius Flitwick">Professor Flitwick</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Charms_(class)" title="Charms (class)">Charms</a> classes.<sup id="cite_ref-PSgbc_3-11" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PSgbc-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
