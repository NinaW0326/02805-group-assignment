These devices were <b><a href="/wiki/Dark_Detector" title="Dark Detector">Dark Detectors</a></b> originally used by among others <a href="/wiki/Auror" title="Auror">Aurors</a> over the course of <a href="/wiki/Auror_Office" title="Auror Office">their duties</a> to detect <a href="/wiki/Dark_Arts" title="Dark Arts">Dark magical activity</a> in an area. In the late <a href="/wiki/2010s" title="2010s">2010s</a>, these were modified by then <a href="/wiki/Minister_for_Magic" title="Minister for Magic">Minister for Magic</a>, <a href="/wiki/Kingsley_Shacklebolt" title="Kingsley Shacklebolt">Kingsley Shacklebolt</a>, to be attuned specifically to the <a href="/wiki/Aura" title="Aura">traces</a> of <a href="/wiki/Foundable" title="Foundable">magical activity</a> unleashed by the <a href="/wiki/Calamity" title="Calamity">Calamity</a>. These were then made accessible to members of the <a href="/wiki/Statute_of_Secrecy_Task_Force" title="Statute of Secrecy Task Force">Statute of Secrecy Task Force</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-WU_1-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-WU-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="History">History</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Repurposed_Dark_Detectors?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: History">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Repurposed_Dark_Detectors?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: History"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="20th_century"><a href="/wiki/20th_century" title="20th century">20th century</a></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Repurposed_Dark_Detectors?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: 20th century">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Repurposed_Dark_Detectors?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: 20th century"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>These detectors were invented and distributed at some point prior to <a href="/wiki/1994" title="1994">1994</a>, at which point the retired <a href="/wiki/Auror" title="Auror">Auror</a> <a href="/wiki/Alastor_Moody" title="Alastor Moody">Alastor Moody</a> kept at least one such device in his collection of dark detectors.<sup id="cite_ref-GOFF_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOFF-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> After he retired from active duty, he kept it in his <a href="/wiki/Alastor_Moody%27s_house" title="Alastor Moody&#39;s house">house</a>, but it was stolen by <a href="/wiki/Bartemius_Crouch_Junior" title="Bartemius Crouch Junior">Bartemius Crouch Jr</a>, after he subdued and locked him in his own trunk so he could <a href="/wiki/Polyjuice_Potion" title="Polyjuice Potion">impersonate</a> him with the intent of facilitating the <a href="/wiki/Return_of_Lord_Voldemort" title="Return of Lord Voldemort">Return of Lord Voldemort</a> under guise of the <a href="/wiki/Defence_Against_the_Dark_Arts" title="Defence Against the Dark Arts">Defence Against the Dark Arts</a> teacher during the <a href="/wiki/1994-1995_school_year" class="mw-redirect" title="1994-1995 school year">1994-1995 school year</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-GOF_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>When <a href="/wiki/Dumbledore%27s_Army" title="Dumbledore&#39;s Army">Dumbledore's Army</a> reacquired a place to hold their meetings, the <a href="/wiki/Room_of_Requirement" title="Room of Requirement">Room of Requirement</a> provided a suitable room for them, in which there was a collection of broken dark detectors that <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> recognised as the ones in "Moody's" <a href="/wiki/Defence_Against_the_Dark_Arts" title="Defence Against the Dark Arts">office</a> the year before.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP_4-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="21st_century"><a href="/wiki/21st_century" title="21st century">21st century</a></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Repurposed_Dark_Detectors?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: 21st century">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Repurposed_Dark_Detectors?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: 21st century"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>When <a href="/wiki/Constance_Pickering" title="Constance Pickering">Constance Pickering</a> was charged with setting up the <a href="/wiki/Statute_of_Secrecy_Task_Force" title="Statute of Secrecy Task Force">Statute of Secrecy Task Force</a>, witches and wizards that volunteered to serve as <a href="/wiki/Calamity_Investigator" title="Calamity Investigator">Calamity Investigators</a> had access to these devices, which they could put down in areas to detect <a href="/wiki/Foundable" title="Foundable">traces</a> of the <a href="/wiki/Calamity" title="Calamity">Calamity</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-WU_1-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-WU-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
