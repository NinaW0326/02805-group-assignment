A <b>charmed skeleton</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Human" title="Human">human</a> skeleton that has been <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magically</a> <a href="/wiki/Necromancy" title="Necromancy">reanimated</a> by a <a href="/wiki/Dark_Wizard" title="Dark Wizard">Dark Wizard</a>, similar to <a href="/wiki/Inferius" title="Inferius">Inferi</a>. They were found in the <a href="/wiki/Shrieking_Shack" title="Shrieking Shack">Shrieking Shack</a> and attacked anyone they came into contact with by throwing bones at them.<sup id="cite_ref-POAG_2-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-POAG-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a> also summoned and used them in the <a href="/wiki/Duel_in_Little_Hangleton" title="Duel in Little Hangleton">Duel in Little Hangleton</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-GOFG_1-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOFG-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h3><span id="At_a_Hallowe'en_Feast"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="At_a_Hallowe.27en_Feast">At a Hallowe'en Feast</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Charmed_skeleton?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: At a Hallowe&#039;en Feast">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Charmed_skeleton?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: At a Hallowe&#039;en Feast"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>During a <a href="/wiki/Hallowe%27en_feast" class="mw-redirect" title="Hallowe&#39;en feast">Hallowe'en feast</a> in the <a href="/wiki/1980s" title="1980s">1980s</a> at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a>, <a href="/wiki/Albus_Dumbledore" title="Albus Dumbledore">Albus Dumbledore</a> was known to have <a href="/wiki/Charm" title="Charm">charmed</a> skeletons to dance for the students' entertainment. it is unclear if Dumbledore used necromancy to do so, or a movement charm similar to <i><a href="/wiki/Piertotum_Locomotor" title="Piertotum Locomotor">Piertotum Locomotor</a></i>.<sup id="cite_ref-HM_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HM-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Guarding_the_Shrieking_Shack">Guarding the Shrieking Shack</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Charmed_skeleton?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Guarding the Shrieking Shack">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Charmed_skeleton?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Guarding the Shrieking Shack"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<figure class="thumb tright show-info-icon" style="width: 182px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/9/99/Skelet.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20100619185708" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/9/99/Skelet.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/180?cb=20100619185708" decoding="async" width="180" height="159" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="Skelet.jpg" data-image-key="Skelet.jpg" /></a> 	 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:Skelet.jpg" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption">A Skeleton attempting to attack <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> and  <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione Granger</a> inside the <a href="/wiki/Shrieking_Shack" title="Shrieking Shack">Shrieking Shack</a></p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure>
<p>Charmed Skeletons guarded the tunnel from the <a href="/wiki/Whomping_Willow" title="Whomping Willow">Whomping Willow</a> to the <a href="/wiki/Shrieking_Shack" title="Shrieking Shack">Shrieking Shack</a>. In <a href="/wiki/1994" title="1994">1994</a>, when <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> and <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione Granger</a> entered the tunnel to save <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ron Weasley</a> from <a href="/wiki/Sirius_Black" title="Sirius Black">Sirius Black</a>, these skeletons attacked them. Harry and Hermione needed to work together to defeat the skeletons using the <a href="/wiki/Tickling_Charm" title="Tickling Charm">Tickling Charm</a>, as they only lost an arm if Harry or Hermione attacked them alone.<sup id="cite_ref-POAG_2-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-POAG-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Duel_in_Little_Hangleton">Duel in Little Hangleton</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Charmed_skeleton?veaction=edit&amp;section=4" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Duel in Little Hangleton">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Charmed_skeleton?action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Duel in Little Hangleton"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>After <a href="/wiki/Return_of_Lord_Voldemort" title="Return of Lord Voldemort">his resurrection</a> in <a href="/wiki/1995" title="1995">1995</a>, <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a> made some Charmed Skeletons from bones in the <a href="/wiki/Little_Hangleton_graveyard" title="Little Hangleton graveyard">Little Hangleton graveyard</a>, and had them attack <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>. Harry used simple bombarding <a href="/wiki/Jinx" title="Jinx">jinxes</a> to defeat them.<sup id="cite_ref-GOFG_1-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOFG-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Charmed_skeleton?veaction=edit&amp;section=5" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Charmed_skeleton?action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<figure class="thumb tright show-info-icon" style="width: 182px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/8/86/Skeleton_LEGOY2.png/revision/latest?cb=20201106115103" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/8/86/Skeleton_LEGOY2.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/180?cb=20201106115103" decoding="async" width="180" height="249" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="Skeleton LEGOY2.png" data-image-key="Skeleton_LEGOY2.png" /></a> 	 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:Skeleton_LEGOY2.png" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption">A charmed skeleton in <i><a href="/wiki/LEGO_Harry_Potter:_Years_5-7" title="LEGO Harry Potter: Years 5-7">LEGO Harry Potter: Years 5-7</a></i></p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure>
<ul><li>They appear in certain levels of <i><a href="/wiki/LEGO_Harry_Potter:_Years_1-4" title="LEGO Harry Potter: Years 1-4">LEGO Harry Potter: Years 1-4</a></i> and <i><a href="/wiki/LEGO_Harry_Potter:_Years_5-7" title="LEGO Harry Potter: Years 5-7">LEGO Harry Potter: Years 5-7</a></i>. They appear as a pile of <a href="/wiki/Bone" title="Bone">bones</a>. When you use the <a href="/wiki/Levitation_Charm" title="Levitation Charm">Levitation Charm</a> to assemble their bones together, they form a skeleton wearing a top hat, who will start to tap-dance. When this happens the game plays a piece of music. They will not attack the player and can be broken again with the casting of any spell, and can be rebuilt again.<sup id="cite_ref-LEGOHP1-4_4-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-LEGOHP1-4-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-LEGOHP5-7_5-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-LEGOHP5-7-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li>In theory, Charmed Skeletons do not differ from <a href="/wiki/Inferius" title="Inferius">Inferi</a>, with the only visible difference being that skeletons have further decayed than the corpses Inferi are made of. It is possible that the charmed skeletons were actually Inferi, mistaken for a different creature because of the differing appearance. However, when looking at the overall differences between Charmed Skeletons and Inferi are that Charmed Skeletons are not confirmed to be afraid of heat and light, while Inferi are confirmed to be afraid of both heat and light.</li></ul>
