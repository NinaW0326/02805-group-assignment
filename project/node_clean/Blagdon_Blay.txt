</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Blagdon_Blay?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Blagdon_Blay?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>When thief <a href="/wiki/Eldon_Elsrickle" title="Eldon Elsrickle">Eldon Elsrickle</a> introduced the <a href="/wiki/Unlocking_Charm" title="Unlocking Charm">Unlocking Spell</a> (which he had learnt from an <a href="/wiki/Ancient_African_sorcerer" title="Ancient African sorcerer">ancient African sorcerer</a>) to London, there was an unprecedented series of recurring burglaries and break-ins by unscrupulous <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizards</a>, upon the homes of <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggles</a> and <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">fellow wizards</a> alike.<sup id="cite_ref-WBoS_1-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-WBoS-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Blagdon Blay had his house broken into nineteen times in the course of just two weeks. He managed to <a href="/wiki/Spell_creation" title="Spell creation">develop</a> an <a href="/wiki/Anti-Alohomora_Charm" title="Anti-Alohomora Charm">Anti-Alohomora Charm</a> to secure his house, and soon all the London wizards sealed their houses shut at night, and special wizard task-forces protected those of Muggles.<sup id="cite_ref-WBoS_1-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-WBoS-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Magical_abilities_and_skills">Magical abilities and skills</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Blagdon_Blay?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Blagdon_Blay?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li><b><a href="/wiki/Charm" title="Charm">Charms</a></b>: Blay was highly accomplished with charm-work, having successfully invented the <a href="/wiki/Anti-Alohomora_Charm" title="Anti-Alohomora Charm">Anti-Alohomora Charm</a>, which he ingeniously used to prevent locks being broken with the <a href="/wiki/Unlocking_Charm" title="Unlocking Charm">Unlocking Charm</a> and preventing theft, to great success.</li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Spell_creation" title="Spell creation">Spell creation</a></b>: Blay succeeded in the highly difficult <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magical</a> art of inventing new <a href="/wiki/Spell" title="Spell">spells</a>, having invented the immensely useful <a href="/wiki/Anti-Alohomora_Charm" title="Anti-Alohomora Charm">Anti-Alohomora Charm</a>, an excellent magical achievement given the success of the charm.</li></ul>
