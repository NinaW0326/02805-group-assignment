<b>Dehoff</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard or witch</a> who invented <a href="/wiki/Bimetal_alchemical_thread" title="Bimetal alchemical thread">bimetal alchemical thread</a>. This <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magical</a> fibre, which Dehoff patented, was invisible to <a href="/wiki/Dark_Wizard" title="Dark Wizard">Dark Wizards</a>, and as such was used to make the protective product <a href="/wiki/Hex-a-Gone" title="Hex-a-Gone">Hex-a-Gone</a>. However, Hex-a-Gone had "extremely complicated and laborious instructions" that had to be carefully followed, and could result in unintended home "hexidents" if used improperly. Dehoff would not be held legally responsible for such "hexidents."<sup id="cite_ref-HPLE_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HPLE-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
