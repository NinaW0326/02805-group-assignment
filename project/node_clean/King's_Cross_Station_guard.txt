<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="1991">1991</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/King%27s_Cross_Station_guard?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: 1991">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/King%27s_Cross_Station_guard?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: 1991"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<div class="quote">
<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;Harry Potter and the Philosopher&#39;s Stone (film)"><b>Harry Potter</b>: "<i>Excuse me sir. Can you tell me where I might find Platform 9他?</i>"</span></dd>
<dd><b>Station guard</b>: "<i>9他? Think you're being funny do you?</i>"</dd>
<dd>&#8212; Harry, searching for Platform 9他<sup class="noprint"><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Philosopher%27s_Stone_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Philosopher&#39;s Stone (film)">[src]</a></sup></dd></dl>
</div>
<p><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> first met the station guard in <a href="/wiki/1991" title="1991">1991</a>, while trying to find his way to <a href="/wiki/King%27s_Cross_Station#Platform_9.C2.BE" title="King&#39;s Cross Station">Platform 9他</a>. When Harry mentioned the name of the platform, the guard thought Harry was mocking him, because, not being a wizard, he did not know it existed.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="1992">1992</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/King%27s_Cross_Station_guard?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: 1992">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/King%27s_Cross_Station_guard?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: 1992"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<div class="quote">
<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets (film)"><b>Station guard</b>: "<i>What d'you two think you're doing?</i>"</span></dd>
<dd><b>Harry Potter</b>: "<i>Sorry. Lost... lost control of the trolley</i>"</dd>
<dd>&#8212; The guard's reaction to <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry</a> and <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ron</a>'s collision with a wall<sup class="noprint"><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Chamber_of_Secrets_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets (film)">[src]</a></sup></dd></dl>
</div>
<p>In <a href="/wiki/1992" title="1992">1992</a>, Harry and <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ron Weasley</a> tried entering Platform 9他, but were prevented from doing so as the entrance had been magically blocked by the <a href="/wiki/House-elf" title="House-elf">House-elf</a> <a href="/wiki/Dobby" title="Dobby">Dobby</a>. The station guard saw the two boys running into the wall and asked them what they were doing. Harry got away, telling him that the two lost control of their trolleys.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/King%27s_Cross_Station_guard?veaction=edit&amp;section=5" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/King%27s_Cross_Station_guard?action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>The station guard was portrayed by <a href="/wiki/Harry_Taylor" title="Harry Taylor">Harry Taylor</a> in <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Philosopher%27s_Stone_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Philosopher&#39;s Stone (film)">Harry Potter and the Philosopher's Stone</a></i>, <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Chamber_of_Secrets_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets (film)">Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets</a></i> and <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Deathly_Hallows:_Part_2" title="Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 2">Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 2</a></i>.</li>
<li>He originally appeared in the Epilogue scene at King's Cross station, but he was cut from the second shoot of the scene.</li>
<li>The guard is also a playable character in the consoles versions of the LEGO video games.</li>
<li>In <i><a href="/wiki/LEGO_Harry_Potter:_Years_5-7" title="LEGO Harry Potter: Years 5-7">LEGO Harry Potter: Years 5-7</a></i>, despite being a <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggle</a> he appears in <a href="/wiki/Platform_Nine_and_Three-Quarters" title="Platform Nine and Three-Quarters">Platform Nine and Three-Quarters</a> and in the <a href="/wiki/Hogsmeade_station" title="Hogsmeade station">Hogsmeade station</a>.</li></ul>
