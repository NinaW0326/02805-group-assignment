<b>Henry "Harry" Potter</b> was either a <a href="/wiki/Pure-blood" title="Pure-blood">pure-blood</a> or <a href="/wiki/Half-Blood" class="mw-redirect" title="Half-Blood">half-blood</a> <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> who served on the <a href="/wiki/Wizengamot" title="Wizengamot">Wizengamot</a> from <a href="/wiki/1913" title="1913">1913</a> to <a href="/wiki/1921" title="1921">1921</a>. He was the father of <a href="/wiki/Fleamont_Potter" title="Fleamont Potter">Fleamont Potter</a>, the paternal grandfather of <a href="/wiki/James_Potter_I" title="James Potter I">James Potter</a>, the great-grandfather of the famous <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>, and an ancestor of <a href="/wiki/James_Potter_II" title="James Potter II">James Sirius</a>, <a href="/wiki/Albus_Potter" title="Albus Potter">Albus Severus</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Lily_Potter_(disambiguation)" title="Lily Potter (disambiguation)">Lily Luna Potter</a>.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Henry_Potter?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Henry_Potter?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Henry was born somewhere in England in or prior to <a href="/wiki/1892" class="mw-redirect" title="1892">1892</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-YOB_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-YOB-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> He was a direct descendant of <a href="/wiki/Hardwin_Potter" title="Hardwin Potter">Hardwin</a> and <a href="/wiki/Iolanthe_Peverell" title="Iolanthe Peverell">Iolanthe Potter</a> on his father's side.<sup id="cite_ref-Pottermore_4-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Pottermore-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/Henry_Potter%27s_mother" title="Henry Potter&#39;s mother">His mother</a> was a member of the <a href="/wiki/Fleamont_family" title="Fleamont family">Fleamont family</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-Pottermore_4-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Pottermore-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> It was her dying wish that Henry preserve her maiden name, which would otherwise die with her, and thus he named his son <a href="/wiki/Fleamont_Potter" title="Fleamont Potter">Fleamont</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-Pottermore_4-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Pottermore-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Henry served as a member of the <a href="/wiki/Wizengamot" title="Wizengamot">Wizengamot</a> from <a href="/wiki/1913" title="1913">1913</a> to <a href="/wiki/1921" title="1921">1921</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-Pottermore_4-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Pottermore-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> He caused a minor controversy by publicly condemning the then-<a href="/wiki/Minister_for_Magic" title="Minister for Magic">Minister for Magic</a> <a href="/wiki/Archer_Evermonde" title="Archer Evermonde">Archer Evermonde</a>'s decision to forbid the wizarding community from aiding <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggles</a> during the <a href="/wiki/First_World_War" title="First World War">First World War</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-Pottermore_4-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Pottermore-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> Henry's outspoken pro-Muggle views were one of the chief reasons for the <a href="/wiki/Potter_family" title="Potter family">Potter family</a>'s exclusion from the <a href="/wiki/Sacred_Twenty-Eight" title="Sacred Twenty-Eight">Sacred Twenty-Eight</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-Pottermore_4-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Pottermore-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Henry_Potter?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Henry_Potter?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>It is possible that <a href="/wiki/James_Potter_I" title="James Potter I">James</a> and <a href="/wiki/Lily_J._Potter" title="Lily J. Potter">Lily Potter</a> named their son, <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>, after him.</li>
<li>In the American film <i><a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/It%27s_a_Wonderful_Life" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:It&#39;s a Wonderful Life">It's a Wonderful Life</a></i> (director <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Frank_Capra" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Frank Capra">Frank Capra</a>, 1946), "<a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mr_Potter" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Mr Potter">Henry F. Potter</a>" is the name of the antagonist, whose mannerisms are much akin to <a href="/wiki/Lucius_Malfoy" title="Lucius Malfoy">Lucius Malfoy</a>.</li></ul>
