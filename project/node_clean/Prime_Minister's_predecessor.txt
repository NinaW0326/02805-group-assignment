This <b>man</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Great_Britain" title="Great Britain">British</a> <a href="/wiki/Prime_Minister_(title)" title="Prime Minister (title)">Prime Minister</a> who left office sometime before <a href="/wiki/1992" title="1992">1992</a>. He met with <a href="/wiki/Cornelius_Fudge" title="Cornelius Fudge">Cornelius Fudge</a>, who revealed the existence of the <a href="/wiki/Wizarding_world" title="Wizarding world">wizarding world</a> to him. Believing the meeting to be a hoax perpetrated by the opposing political party, he tried to throw Fudge out of the window of his <a href="/wiki/Prime_Minister%27s_office" title="Prime Minister&#39;s office">office</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP1_1-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP1-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
