<p><b>Professor Silvanus Kettleburn</b> (b. <a href="/wiki/22_November" title="22 November">22 November</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-shortstories_3-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-shortstories-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> (pre- <a href="/wiki/1927" title="1927">1927</a>) was a <a href="/wiki/Great_Britain" title="Great Britain">British</a> <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> who, after graduating from <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a>, served as <a href="/wiki/Professor" title="Professor">professor</a> of <a href="/wiki/Care_of_Magical_Creatures" title="Care of Magical Creatures">Care of Magical Creatures</a>. His recklessness and the nature of his subject resulted in him losing all his limbs but one arm and half of one leg. He retired in <a href="/wiki/1993" title="1993">1993</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-POA5_6-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-POA5-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup> but still made visits to <a href="/wiki/Dragon" title="Dragon">dragon</a> sanctuaries.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Silvanus_Kettleburn?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Silvanus_Kettleburn?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_life">Early life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Silvanus_Kettleburn?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Early life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Silvanus_Kettleburn?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Early life"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Silvanus Kettleburn was born to <a href="/wiki/Silvanus_Kettleburn%27s_father" title="Silvanus Kettleburn&#39;s father">a wizard</a> and <a href="/wiki/Silvanus_Kettleburn%27s_mother" title="Silvanus Kettleburn&#39;s mother">a witch</a> on <a href="/wiki/22_November" title="22 November">22 November</a>. He received magical instruction at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a>, in which he was Sorted into <a href="/wiki/Hufflepuff" title="Hufflepuff">Hufflepuff</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-Pottermore_1-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Pottermore-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> and presumably achieved high marks in <a href="/wiki/Care_of_Magical_Creatures" title="Care of Magical Creatures">Care of Magical Creatures</a>, later leading him to become a teacher in that subject.
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Professor_of_Care_of_Magical_Creatures">Professor of Care of Magical Creatures</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Silvanus_Kettleburn?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Professor of Care of Magical Creatures">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Silvanus_Kettleburn?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Professor of Care of Magical Creatures"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Under_Headmaster_Armando_Dippet">Under Headmaster Armando Dippet</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Silvanus_Kettleburn?veaction=edit&amp;section=4" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Under Headmaster Armando Dippet">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Silvanus_Kettleburn?action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Under Headmaster Armando Dippet"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<div class="quote">
<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;The Tales of Beedle the Bard (real)">"<i>...it was several months before the Great Hall lost its pungent aroma of wood smoke, and even longer before Professor Beery's head reassumed its normal proportions, and Professor Kettleburn was taken off probation...</i>"</span></dd>
<dd>&#8212;<i>The Fountain of Fair Fortune</i> incident<sup class="noprint"><a href="/wiki/The_Tales_of_Beedle_the_Bard_(real)" title="The Tales of Beedle the Bard (real)">[src]</a></sup></dd>
<dd></dd></dl></div>
<p>At some point during the early to mid-<a href="/wiki/1920s" title="1920s">1920s</a> or earlier, Silvanus met and befriended the famous <a href="/wiki/Magizoologist" title="Magizoologist">Magizoologist</a> <a href="/wiki/Newton_Scamander" title="Newton Scamander">Newton Scamander</a>, whom he helped with his best-selling book <i><a href="/wiki/Fantastic_Beasts_and_Where_to_Find_Them" title="Fantastic Beasts and Where to Find Them">Fantastic Beasts and Where to Find Them</a></i> by providing some of the information it contained about <a href="/wiki/Chimaera" title="Chimaera">Chimaeras</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-HM_5-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HM-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>At some point in his life, Kettleburn became the <a href="/wiki/Professor" title="Professor">Professor</a> of <a href="/wiki/Care_of_Magical_Creatures" title="Care of Magical Creatures">Care of Magical Creatures</a> at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School</a> under <a href="/wiki/Headmaster" title="Headmaster">Headmaster</a> <a href="/wiki/Armando_Dippet" title="Armando Dippet">Armando Dippet</a>. During his time as a professor, Kettleburn amassed sixty-two periods of probations.<sup id="cite_ref-TBB_7-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-TBB-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup> One of which was when a worm he provided for the play of <i><a href="/wiki/The_Fountain_of_Fair_Fortune" title="The Fountain of Fair Fortune">The Fountain of Fair Fortune</a></i> (which was in fact, an <a href="/wiki/Engorgement_Charm" title="Engorgement Charm">Engorged</a> <a href="/wiki/Ashwinder" title="Ashwinder">Ashwinder</a>) exploded and started a <a href="/wiki/Fire" title="Fire">fire</a>. This started a <a href="/wiki/Duelling" title="Duelling">duel</a> between the girls playing <a href="/wiki/Amata" title="Amata">Amata</a> and <a href="/wiki/Asha" title="Asha">Asha</a>, in which <a href="/wiki/Herbert_Beery" title="Herbert Beery">Professor Herbert Beery</a> was caught in the crossfire. Beery's head was reported to have been disfigured. It took him a long time for it to return to normal, which led to his retirement as a <a href="/wiki/Professor" title="Professor">professor</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-TBB_7-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-TBB-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>At some point in or after the 1969-1970 school year, a student by the name of <a href="/wiki/Patricia_Rakepick" title="Patricia Rakepick">Patricia Rakepick</a> approached him for information about how to enter the <a href="/wiki/Forbidden_Forest" title="Forbidden Forest">Forbidden Forest</a> undetected. Seen as how she was his most gifted pupil at the time of her inquiry, he questioned her as to why she wanted to go into the forest, to which she replied she merely wanted to explore it and to study the <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magical</a> <a href="/wiki/Creature" title="Creature">creatures</a> that dwelled within it, and told him she meant to become a <a href="/wiki/Magizoologist" title="Magizoologist">Magizoologist</a> after she left school. Accepting this explanation, he advised her to use a <a href="/wiki/Broomstick" title="Broomstick">broomstick</a>.
</p>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Under_Headmaster_Albus_Dumbledore">Under Headmaster Albus Dumbledore</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Silvanus_Kettleburn?veaction=edit&amp;section=5" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Under Headmaster Albus Dumbledore">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Silvanus_Kettleburn?action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Under Headmaster Albus Dumbledore"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<div class="quote">
<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;The Tales of Beedle the Bard (real)">"<i>By the time I became Headmaster, however, Professor Kettleburn had mellowed considerably, although there were always those who took the cynical view that with only one and a half of his original limbs remaining to him, he was forced to take life at a quieter pace.</i>"</span></dd>
<dd>&#8212;Kettleburn mellowing due to his extensive injuries<sup class="noprint"><a href="/wiki/The_Tales_of_Beedle_the_Bard_(real)" title="The Tales of Beedle the Bard (real)">[src]</a></sup></dd>
<dd></dd></dl></div>
<p>By the time <a href="/wiki/Albus_Dumbledore" title="Albus Dumbledore">Albus Dumbledore</a>, the <a href="/wiki/Transfiguration_(class)" title="Transfiguration (class)">Transfiguration</a> <a href="/wiki/Professor" title="Professor">Professor</a>, had taken over from Professor Dippet as <a href="/wiki/Headmaster" title="Headmaster">Headmaster</a>, Professor Kettleburn had calmed down a considerable amount, though this was likely because he only had one and a half of his original limbs left:<sup id="cite_ref-TBB_7-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-TBB-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup> one arm and half a leg.<sup id="cite_ref-Pottermore_1-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Pottermore-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>By the <a href="/wiki/1980s" title="1980s">1980s</a>, Professor Kettleburn had long become one of the regulars at the <a href="/wiki/Hospital_Wing" title="Hospital Wing">Hospital Wing</a> at the school, where he had been treated by <a href="/wiki/Poppy_Pomfrey" title="Poppy Pomfrey">Poppy Pomfrey</a> for and subsequently recuperated from various creature-induced injuries at the <a href="/wiki/Hospital_Wing" title="Hospital Wing">Hospital Wing</a> no less than nineteen times, to the point where he eventually had a bed reserved just for him. Even so, he apparently took this with a surprising amount of good humour, almost affectionately referring to the Hospital Wing as his 'home away from home' during his twentieth time visiting. On that particular occasion, Professor Kettleburn and <a href="/wiki/Rubeus_Hagrid" title="Rubeus Hagrid">Rubeus Hagrid</a> had been transporting a large number of magical creatures to the school for teaching purposes for the <a href="/wiki/Care_of_Magical_Creatures" title="Care of Magical Creatures">Care of Magical Creatures</a>, when they accidentally lost control over them and they escaped and overran <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Castle" title="Hogwarts Castle">Hogwarts Castle</a>, and got injured while in the process of rounding them up.<sup id="cite_ref-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>During the mid- to late portions of the same decade, he also spent a considerable time asking for students help in locating a <a href="/wiki/Chimaera" title="Chimaera">Chimaera</a> loose some where on the grounds of Hogwarts.<sup id="cite_ref-HM_5-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HM-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>During the <a href="/wiki/1987%E2%80%931988_school_year" title="1987â1988 school year">1987â1988 school year</a>, he taught <a href="/wiki/Fourth_year" title="Fourth year">Fourth years</a>, such as <a href="/wiki/Jacob%27s_sibling" title="Jacob&#39;s sibling">Jacob's sibling</a>,  classes on several magical creatures including <a href="/wiki/Bowtruckle" title="Bowtruckle">bowtruckles</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/Niffler" title="Niffler">nifflers</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-10" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-10">&#91;10&#93;</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/Streeler" title="Streeler">streelers</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-11" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-11">&#91;11&#93;</a></sup> and <a href="/wiki/Kneazle" title="Kneazle">kneazles</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-12" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-12">&#91;12&#93;</a></sup> He also bragged that he had won <a href="/wiki/Hogsmeade%27s_Most_Maimed_Award" title="Hogsmeade&#39;s Most Maimed Award">Hogsmeade's Most Maimed Award</a> for ten years running.<sup id="cite_ref-13" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-13">&#91;13&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>During the <a href="/wiki/1992-1993_school_year" class="mw-redirect" title="1992-1993 school year">1992-1993 school year</a>, his final year at Hogwarts, <a href="/wiki/Fred_Weasley" title="Fred Weasley">Fred</a> and <a href="/wiki/George_Weasley" title="George Weasley">George Weasley</a> stole a <a href="/wiki/Fire_Dwelling_Salamander" title="Fire Dwelling Salamander">salamander</a> from his class and fed it fireworks.<sup id="cite_ref-COS8_14-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COS8-14">&#91;14&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>The Hogwarts student <a href="/wiki/Jacob%27s_sibling" title="Jacob&#39;s sibling">Jacob's sibling</a> described his scent as the streets of <a href="/wiki/Edinburgh" title="Edinburgh">Edinburgh</a> after a storm.<sup id="cite_ref-HM_5-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HM-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Retirement">Retirement</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Silvanus_Kettleburn?veaction=edit&amp;section=6" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Retirement">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Silvanus_Kettleburn?action=edit&amp;section=6" title="Edit section: Retirement"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<div class="quote">
<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban">"<i>As to our second new appointment: well, I am sorry to tell you that Professor Kettleburn, our Care of Magical Creatures teacher, retired at the end of last year in order to enjoy more time with his remaining limbs. However, I am delighted to say that his place will be filled by none other than Rubeus Hagrid, who has agreed to take on this teaching job in addition to his gamekeeping duties.</i>"</span></dd>
<dd>&#8212;Announcement of Kettleburn's retirement<sup class="noprint"><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Prisoner_of_Azkaban" title="Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban">[src]</a></sup></dd>
<dd></dd></dl></div>
<p>Kettleburn remained in his position as Professor of <a href="/wiki/Care_of_Magical_Creatures" title="Care of Magical Creatures">Care of Magical Creatures</a> until <a href="/wiki/1993" title="1993">1993</a>, when he retired to spend more time with his remaining limbs. According to <a href="/wiki/Rubeus_Hagrid" title="Rubeus Hagrid">Rubeus Hagrid</a>, who succeeded him, Professor Dumbledore came straight to <a href="/wiki/Rubeus_Hagrid%27s_cabin" class="mw-redirect" title="Rubeus Hagrid&#39;s cabin">his hut</a> after Kettleburn said that he had had enough.<sup id="cite_ref-POA5_6-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-POA5-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup> As a retirement present, Albus Dumbledore presented Kettleburn with a set of enchanted wooden prosthetics, which needed constant replacement due to his habit of visiting dragon sanctuaries.<sup id="cite_ref-Pottermore_1-10" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Pottermore-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>During his retirement, Professor Kettleburn moved to <a href="/wiki/Hogsmeade" title="Hogsmeade">Hogsmeade</a>. During the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a>, his physical infirmities prevented him from fighting directly but, determined to play his part, he clambered into his attic and threw his entire stock of <a href="/wiki/Flobberworm" title="Flobberworm">Flobberworms</a> out of the skylight at passing <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a>. While this had little to no effect on the outcome of the battle, it was generally felt to show the right spirit.<sup id="cite_ref-shortstories_3-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-shortstories-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>He never married and had no children,<sup id="cite_ref-Pottermore_1-11" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Pottermore-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> and died sometime prior to <a href="/wiki/22_November" title="22 November">22 November</a>, <a href="/wiki/2014" title="2014">2014</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-PMInsider_4-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PMInsider-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Personality_and_traits">Personality and traits</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Silvanus_Kettleburn?veaction=edit&amp;section=7" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Personality and traits">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Silvanus_Kettleburn?action=edit&amp;section=7" title="Edit section: Personality and traits"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<div class="quote">
<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;Harry Potter: Hogwarts Mystery"><b>Rita Skeeter</b>: "<i>You regularly bring children face to face with creatures that could kill them in the blink of an eye.</i>"</span></dd>
<dd><b>Silvanus Kettleburn</b>: "<i>And I teach them to understand and respect those creatures so no one gets hurt.</i>"</dd>
<dd>&#8212; Profssor Kettleburn refuting sensationalist reporter Rita Skeeter's claim that he did not take his job seriously.<sup class="noprint"><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter:_Hogwarts_Mystery" title="Harry Potter: Hogwarts Mystery">[src]</a></sup></dd></dl>
</div><p>Silvanus Kettleburn had an extreme, professorial enthusiasm for the subject of <a href="/wiki/Magizoology" title="Magizoology">Magizoology</a> and great fondness for magical creatures of all kinds, especially the dangerous ones. Like <a href="/wiki/Newton_Scamander" title="Newton Scamander">Newton Scamander</a>, he advocated for the protection and preservation of magical creatures, informing his class that he would risk life and limb, and indeed had done so, to protect the magical creatures in his care. He was also quite dutiful in regards to his profession, brushing off <a href="/wiki/Rita_Skeeter" title="Rita Skeeter">Rita Skeeter</a>'s accusation that he was regularly endangering his students by saying he was more careful with his students than he was with himself.
</p><p>Despite this, Kettleburn was considered reckless by many people, including his former employer <a href="/wiki/Armando_Dippet" title="Armando Dippet">Armando Dippet</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-TBB_7-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-TBB-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup> and not without good reason. Indeed: True though it might be that the numerous injuries he had suffered or the limbs he had lost throughout his life, by, in his eagerness, recklessly neglecting to take the proper safety precautions when dealing with beasts, Silvanus Kettleburn did not mind or regret any of them, as he regarded his mangled body as a testament to his dedication to his work.
</p><p>In the lesson about Nifflers (with <a href="/wiki/Patricia_Rakepick" title="Patricia Rakepick">Rakepick's</a> <a href="/wiki/Sickleworth" title="Sickleworth">pet</a>), Kettleburn confided in her that he owned twelve of them, and still wanted twelve more, even when they had destroyed his boat, house and his favourite prosthetic leg.<sup id="cite_ref-HM_5-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HM-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>One notable example of Kettleburn's recklessness was the <i><a href="/wiki/The_Fountain_of_Fair_Fortune" title="The Fountain of Fair Fortune">The Fountain of Fair Fortune</a></i> incident in the <a href="/wiki/Great_Hall" title="Great Hall">Great Hall</a>. This incident resulted in a packed <a href="/wiki/Hospital_Wing" title="Hospital Wing">Hospital Wing</a>, a Great Hall smelling of burned wood, and <a href="/wiki/Herbert_Beery" title="Herbert Beery">Herbert Beery</a>'s head growing out of proportion. Kettleburn had mellowed considerably by the time Dumbledore took over,<sup id="cite_ref-TBB_7-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-TBB-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup> although he was regarded as a "<i>lovable if eccentric man</i>".<sup id="cite_ref-Pottermore_1-12" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Pottermore-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Magical_abilities_and_skills">Magical abilities and skills</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Silvanus_Kettleburn?veaction=edit&amp;section=8" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Silvanus_Kettleburn?action=edit&amp;section=8" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<div class="quote">
<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;Harry Potter: Hogwarts Mystery">"<i>Feel free to approach <a href="/wiki/Hieronymus" title="Hieronymus">him</a>, but watch out for his posterior, as it shoots flames capable of scorching your skin off. Don't you just love magical creatures? Let's begin!</i>"</span></dd>
<dd>&#8212;Silvanus Kettleburn's enthusiasm for magical beasts, such as <a href="/wiki/Fire_Crab" title="Fire Crab">Fire Crabs</a><sup class="noprint"><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter:_Hogwarts_Mystery" title="Harry Potter: Hogwarts Mystery">[src]</a></sup></dd>
<dd></dd></dl></div>
<ul><li><b><a href="/wiki/Care_of_Magical_Creatures" title="Care of Magical Creatures">Care of Magical Creatures</a></b>: As the Professor of Care of Magical Creatures, Kettleburn was highly knowledgeable and proficient in the field of <a href="/wiki/Magizoology" title="Magizoology">Magizoology</a>, the study of <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magical</a> <a href="/wiki/Creature" title="Creature">creatures</a>. He was particularly fond of dealing with dangerous <a href="/wiki/Beast" title="Beast">beasts</a> like his <a href="/wiki/Rubeus_Hagrid" title="Rubeus Hagrid">successor</a>, such as <a href="/wiki/Chimaera" title="Chimaera">Chimaeras</a>, but was more than capable of handling and teaching his students about non-violent beasts as well.</li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Charm" title="Charm">Charms</a></b>: Kettleburn appeared to be proficient with charms, given how he was able to successfully cast an <a href="/wiki/Engorgement_Charm" title="Engorgement Charm">Engorgement Charm</a> on an <a href="/wiki/Ashwinder" title="Ashwinder">Ashwinder</a> <a href="/wiki/Snake" title="Snake">snake</a> to significantly enlarge its size, and timely cast the <a href="/wiki/Sleeping_Charm" title="Sleeping Charm">Sleeping Charm</a> to subdue a <a href="/wiki/Ukrainian_Ironbelly" title="Ukrainian Ironbelly">Ukrainian Ironbelly</a>.</li></ul>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Possessions">Possessions</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Silvanus_Kettleburn?veaction=edit&amp;section=9" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Possessions">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Silvanus_Kettleburn?action=edit&amp;section=9" title="Edit section: Possessions"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li><b>House</b>: Kettleburn owned a small house in the village of <a href="/wiki/Hogsmeade" title="Hogsmeade">Hogsmeade</a> from where he would walk up to Hogwarts for work every day, and to which he retired to after leaving his profession.</li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Silvanus_Kettleburn%27s_wand" title="Silvanus Kettleburn&#39;s wand">Wand</a></b>: Like most wizards and witches, Professor Kettleburn owned a <a href="/wiki/Wand" title="Wand">wand</a>, which was 11Â½ inches long, made of <a href="/wiki/Chestnut_(tree)" title="Chestnut (tree)">chestnut</a> <a href="/wiki/Wand_wood" title="Wand wood">wood</a> and had a <a href="/wiki/Phoenix_feather" title="Phoenix feather">phoenix feather</a> <a href="/wiki/Wand_core" title="Wand core">core</a>, which was described as being "whippy".</li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Niffler" title="Niffler">Nifflers</a></b>: At some point, Kettleburn owned twelve Nifflers that destroyed his house, boat, and favourite replacement leg; he could hardly wait to get twelve more.<sup id="cite_ref-15" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-15">&#91;15&#93;</a></sup></li></ul>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Silvanus_Kettleburn?veaction=edit&amp;section=10" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Silvanus_Kettleburn?action=edit&amp;section=10" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>"Silvanus" is a Roman name derived from Latin <i>silva</i>, meaning "wood, forest". Silvanus was the Roman god of forests.</li></ul>
