<p>The <b>Captain</b> of the <a href="/wiki/Brigade_of_Witch-Hunters" title="Brigade of Witch-Hunters">Brigade of Witch-Hunters</a> was a character in popular wizarding tale <i><a href="/wiki/Babbitty_Rabbitty_and_her_Cackling_Stump" title="Babbitty Rabbitty and her Cackling Stump">Babbitty Rabbitty and her Cackling Stump</a></i>, by <a href="/wiki/Beedle_the_Bard" title="Beedle the Bard">Beedle the Bard</a>.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="History">History</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Captain_of_the_Brigade_of_Witch-Hunters?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: History">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Captain_of_the_Brigade_of_Witch-Hunters?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: History"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<div class="quote">
<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;The Tales of Beedle the Bard (real)">"<i> </i>[The King]<i> commanded the head of his army to form a Brigade of Witch-Hunters, and issued them with a pack of ferocious black hounds</i>"</span></dd>
<dd>&#8212;<a href="/wiki/Beedle_the_Bard" title="Beedle the Bard">Beedle the Bard</a>, <i><a href="/wiki/Babbitty_Rabbitty_and_her_Cackling_Stump" title="Babbitty Rabbitty and her Cackling Stump">Babbitty Rabbitty and her Cackling Stump</a></i>.<sup class="noprint"><a href="/wiki/The_Tales_of_Beedle_the_Bard_(real)" title="The Tales of Beedle the Bard (real)">[src]</a></sup></dd>
<dd></dd></dl></div>
<p>According to the tale, the <a href="/wiki/Brigade_of_Witch-Hunters" title="Brigade of Witch-Hunters">Brigade of Witch-Hunters</a> was created a long time ago by a <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggle</a> <a href="/wiki/King_(Babbitty_Rabbitty_and_her_Cackling_Stump)" title="King (Babbitty Rabbitty and her Cackling Stump)">King</a> of a very far away land who wanted to keep all the magic in the world for himself. In order to do so, he needed to gather all the <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizards and witches</a> in the world, so he formed the Brigade and ordered this man its captain.<sup id="cite_ref-Babbitty_Rabbitty_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Babbitty_Rabbitty-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Later in the tale, the Captain requested the King to bring <a href="/wiki/Sabre" title="Sabre">Sabre</a>, a hound from the Brigade, back to life with his "wand" (actually a twig with no magical powers whatsoever sold to him by a <a href="/wiki/Charlatan" title="Charlatan">Charlatan</a>). When the King's court mocked him for having no magical powers, he ordered the charlatan to be killed, however, the charlatan told him that <a href="/wiki/Babbitty_Rabbity" title="Babbitty Rabbity">Babbitty</a>, a witch and the King's washerwoman, was blocking his spells. When Babbitty denounced the charlatan for the fraud he was, she proposed the Captain to cut him in half, a deed the Captain was eager to do.<sup id="cite_ref-Babbitty_Rabbitty_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Babbitty_Rabbitty-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
