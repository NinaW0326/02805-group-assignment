<p>This <b>individual</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch</a> who lived in the <a href="/wiki/Great_Britain" title="Great Britain">British Isles</a>. She later got married to <a href="/wiki/Boy_who_owned_a_magic_hamster%27s_father" class="mw-redirect" title="Boy who owned a magic hamster&#39;s father">a wizard</a> and in c. <a href="/wiki/1980" title="1980">1980</a> she had <a href="/wiki/Boy_who_owned_a_magic_hamster" title="Boy who owned a magic hamster">a son</a>. They were presumably very poor as they saved on the <a href="/wiki/Electricity" title="Electricity">light bill</a> by using the <a href="/wiki/Wand-Lighting_Charm" title="Wand-Lighting Charm">Wand-Lighting Charm</a>, <i>Lumos</i>, to save <a href="/wiki/Galleon" title="Galleon">Galleons</a> to be able to afford their son's trip to <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-PSG_1-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PSG-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
