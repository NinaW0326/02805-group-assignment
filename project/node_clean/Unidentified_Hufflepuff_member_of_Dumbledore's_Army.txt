<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_Hufflepuff_member_of_Dumbledore%27s_Army?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_Hufflepuff_member_of_Dumbledore%27s_Army?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Hogwarts_years">Hogwarts years</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_Hufflepuff_member_of_Dumbledore%27s_Army?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Hogwarts years">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_Hufflepuff_member_of_Dumbledore%27s_Army?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Hogwarts years"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<div class="quote">
<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix (film)">"<i>Every great <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> in history has started out as nothing more than what we are now: students. If they can do it, why not us?</i>"</span></dd>
<dd>&#8212;<a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> inspiring the members of Dumbledore's Army<sup class="noprint"><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Order_of_the_Phoenix_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix (film)">[src]</a></sup></dd>
<dd></dd></dl></div><p>She started attending <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> in <a href="/wiki/1991" title="1991">1991</a> and was Sorted into <a href="/wiki/Hufflepuff" title="Hufflepuff">Hufflepuff</a>. Her <a href="/wiki/Unidentified_Gryffindor_member_of_Dumbledore%27s_Army" title="Unidentified Gryffindor member of Dumbledore&#39;s Army">twin sister</a>, however, was Sorted into <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor" title="Gryffindor">Gryffindor</a>.
</p><p>In <a href="/wiki/1994" title="1994">1994</a>, Hogwarts held the <a href="/wiki/Triwizard_Tournament" title="Triwizard Tournament">Triwizard Tournament</a>. She supported <a href="/wiki/Cedric_Diggory" title="Cedric Diggory">Cedric Diggory</a> in the Tournament and initially wore a <a href="/wiki/Potter_Stinks_badge" title="Potter Stinks badge">Potter Stinks badge</a>. She attended <a href="/wiki/Defence_Against_the_Dark_Arts" title="Defence Against the Dark Arts">Defence Against the Dark Arts</a> classes with students from the other houses this year and was taught by <a href="/wiki/Bartemius_Crouch_Junior" title="Bartemius Crouch Junior">Professor Alastor Moody</a>, who demonstrated the three <a href="/wiki/Unforgivable_Curses" title="Unforgivable Curses">Unforgivable Curses</a> in front of the class. At the end of the year, <a href="/wiki/Cedric_Diggory" title="Cedric Diggory">Cedric Diggory</a>, a fellow Hufflepuff, was murdered by <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a> and Hogwarts held a <a href="/wiki/Memorial_feast_to_Cedric_Diggory" title="Memorial feast to Cedric Diggory">Memorial feast to Cedric Diggory</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-GOFF_4-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOFF-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<figure class="thumb tleft show-info-icon" style="width: 232px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/3/32/Hufflepuff_harry.PNG/revision/latest?cb=20090818170320" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/3/32/Hufflepuff_harry.PNG/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/230?cb=20090818170320" decoding="async" width="230" height="159" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="Hufflepuff harry.PNG" data-image-key="Hufflepuff_harry.PNG" /></a> 	 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:Hufflepuff_harry.PNG" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption">Harry helps her during a D.A. meeting</p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure>
<p>In <a href="/wiki/1995" title="1995">1995</a>, her fifth year, <a href="/wiki/Dolores_Umbridge" title="Dolores Umbridge">Dolores Umbridge</a> was appointed as <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_High_Inquisitor" title="Hogwarts High Inquisitor">Hogwarts High Inquisitor</a> and <a href="/wiki/Defence_Against_the_Dark_Arts" title="Defence Against the Dark Arts">Defence Against the Dark Arts</a> teacher, so that the <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">Ministry of Magic</a> could keep watch on <a href="/wiki/Albus_Dumbledore" title="Albus Dumbledore">Albus Dumbledore</a> after he and <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> claimed that <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a> had returned. Umbridge refused to teach students any form of practical defence, and, given the circumstances, <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione Granger</a> felt that the student body needed a teacher who would, so then an alliance called <a href="/wiki/Dumbledore%27s_Army" title="Dumbledore&#39;s Army">Dumbledore's Army</a> was formed. The Hufflepuff girl joined the D.A. and practised at several meetings, learning spells such as the <a href="/wiki/Impediment_Jinx" title="Impediment Jinx">Impediment Jinx</a>, <a href="/wiki/Disarming_Charm" title="Disarming Charm">Disarming Charm</a> and the <a href="/wiki/Patronus_Charm" title="Patronus Charm">Patronus Charm</a>. During one lesson, Harry was giving a speech and whilst doing so he was checking on students. He checked on this student and raised her arm to give her better aim, and after doing so she managed to perform a <a href="/wiki/Nonverbal_spell" title="Nonverbal spell">nonverbal</a> version of the <a href="/wiki/Stunning_Spell" title="Stunning Spell">Stunning Spell</a>, to which she looked pleased and slightly embarrassed for Harry touching her. This was quite advanced <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magic</a> as other students were finding it difficult to perform disarming spells verbally.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTPF_5-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTPF-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_Hufflepuff_member_of_Dumbledore%27s_Army?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_Hufflepuff_member_of_Dumbledore%27s_Army?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>She is portrayed by actress <a href="/wiki/Siobhan_Ellen_Williams" title="Siobhan Ellen Williams">Siobhan Ellen Williams</a> in the films <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Goblet_of_Fire_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire (film)">Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire</a></i> and <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Order_of_the_Phoenix_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix (film)">Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix</a></i>. This actress also plays a <a href="/wiki/Unidentified_Gryffindor_member_of_Dumbledore%27s_Army" title="Unidentified Gryffindor member of Dumbledore&#39;s Army">Gryffindor student</a> in the same film.</li>
<li>As she is a notable character in the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Order_of_the_Phoenix_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix (film)">film adaptation</a> of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Order_of_the_Phoenix" title="Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix">Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix</a></i> she may be <a href="/wiki/Alice_Tolipan" title="Alice Tolipan">Alice Tolipan</a> (a named students on the Dumbledore's Army parchment that don't appear in the books).</li>
<li>She may also be <a href="/wiki/Megan_Jones" title="Megan Jones">Megan Jones</a>.</li>
<li>Another possible identity could be <a href="/wiki/Susan_Bones" title="Susan Bones">Susan Bones</a>.</li>
<li>In the DS version of <i><a href="/wiki/LEGO_Harry_Potter:_Years_5-7" title="LEGO Harry Potter: Years 5-7">LEGO Harry Potter: Years 5-7</a></i>, she is referred to as "<a href="/wiki/Hufflepuff" title="Hufflepuff">Hufflepuff</a> <a href="/wiki/Prefect" title="Prefect">Prefect</a>". This is not canon, as <a href="/wiki/Hannah_Abbott" title="Hannah Abbott">Hannah Abbott</a> was the female prefect for Hufflepuff in Harry Potter's year.</li></ul>
