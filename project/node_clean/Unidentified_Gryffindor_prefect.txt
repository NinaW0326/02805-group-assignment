This <b>prefect</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch</a> who attended <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> in the early <a href="/wiki/1990s" title="1990s">1990s</a>, and was a member of <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor" title="Gryffindor">Gryffindor</a> house. She served alongside <a href="/wiki/Percy_Weasley" title="Percy Weasley">Percy Weasley</a> from <a href="/wiki/1991" title="1991">1991</a> as <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor" title="Gryffindor">Gryffindor</a> <a href="/wiki/Prefect" title="Prefect">prefect</a> until she left the school in <a href="/wiki/1994" title="1994">1994</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-PSV_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PSV-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
