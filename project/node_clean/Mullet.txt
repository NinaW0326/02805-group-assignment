<b>Mullet</b> was an Irish <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch</a> who played as the <a href="/wiki/Chaser" title="Chaser">Chaser</a> for the <a href="/wiki/Irish_National_Quidditch_team" title="Irish National Quidditch team">Irish National Quidditch team</a> during the <a href="/wiki/1994_Quidditch_World_Cup" title="1994 Quidditch World Cup">1994 Quidditch World Cup</a>. She played during the <a href="/wiki/1994_Quidditch_World_Cup" title="1994 Quidditch World Cup">1994 Quidditch World Cup</a> final against <a href="/wiki/Bulgarian_National_Quidditch_team" title="Bulgarian National Quidditch team">Bulgaria</a>, which the Irish team won. She won her side a penalty when Bulgarian <a href="/wiki/Keeper" title="Keeper">Keeper</a>, <a href="/wiki/Lev_Zograf" title="Lev Zograf">Lev Zograf</a> fouled her by <a href="/wiki/Cobbing" title="Cobbing">cobbing</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
