<p>A <b>squire</b> was a medieval title for a teenage <a href="/wiki/Knight" title="Knight">Knight</a>-in-training. Squires were the shield bearers or armour bearers of a knight, having many duties such as protecting the knight if needed, accompanying the knight to tournaments, and carrying the knight's flag.<sup id="cite_ref-Wikipedia_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Wikipedia-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>There were many squire <a href="/wiki/Suit_of_armour" title="Suit of armour">suits of armour</a> at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Castle" title="Hogwarts Castle">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> under <a href="/wiki/Spirit" title="Spirit">spectral</a> possession. They could be aggressive to passers-by, but were particularly vulnerable to the <i><a href="/wiki/Fire-Making_Spell" title="Fire-Making Spell">Incendio</a></i> and <i><a href="/wiki/Freezing_Spell" title="Freezing Spell">Glacius</a></i> spells.<sup id="cite_ref-PAgba_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PAgba-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
