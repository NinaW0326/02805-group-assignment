<p><b>Basil</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> and <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">Ministry of Magic</a> employee. In the <a href="/wiki/1990s" title="1990s">1990s</a>, Basil worked for the <a href="/wiki/Department_of_Magical_Transportation" title="Department of Magical Transportation">Department of Magical Transportation</a>, most likely in the <a href="/wiki/Portkey_Office" title="Portkey Office">Portkey Office</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-DMT_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DMT-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>At the <a href="/wiki/Quidditch_World_Cup" title="Quidditch World Cup">Quidditch World Cup</a> final in <a href="/wiki/1994" title="1994">1994</a>, he was one of the <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizards</a> staffing the <a href="/wiki/Portkey" title="Portkey">Portkey</a> terminus, and had been there for most of the morning with a large party that was arriving from the <a href="/wiki/Black_Forest" title="Black Forest">Black Forest</a> in <a href="/wiki/Germany" title="Germany">Germany</a>. He was wearing a poncho and a kilt in an attempt to look like a <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggle</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-GF7_4-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GF7-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
