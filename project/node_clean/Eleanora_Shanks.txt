</p><p><b>Eleanora Shanks</b>, also known by the nickname <b>Crazy Lamb</b>, was an American <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizarding</a> criminal who operated in the <a href="/wiki/1920s" title="1920s">1920s</a>.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Eleanora_Shanks?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Eleanora_Shanks?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>In or around <a href="/wiki/1926" title="1926">1926</a>, the <a href="/wiki/Magical_Congress_of_the_United_States_of_America" title="Magical Congress of the United States of America">Magical Congress of the United States of America</a> issued a wanted poster for Shanks stating that she had escaped from the <a href="/wiki/New_York_Witches%27_Penitentiary" title="New York Witches&#39; Penitentiary">New York Witches' Penitentiary</a>, and was wanted for <a href="/wiki/Muggle" class="mw-redirect" title="Muggle">No-Maj</a> murder. It offered a reward of 1500 <a href="/wiki/Dragot" title="Dragot">Dragots</a> for information leading to her capture.
</p><p>When Shanks was booked and photographed, she was assigned the MACUSA Reg No 8153, but individuals were informed that she may appear much older as the picture was take some time ago.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
