<p><b>Rowena Ravenclaw</b> (fl. c. <a href="/wiki/First_millennium_A.D." title="First millennium A.D.">993</a>) was a <a href="/wiki/Scotland" title="Scotland">Scottish</a> <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch</a> and one of the <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_founders" title="Hogwarts founders">four founders</a> of <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a>. She was noted for her intelligence and creativity.
</p><p>Ravenclaw died sometime in the <a href="/wiki/11th_century" title="11th century">eleventh century</a>, after she fell fatally ill. Legend had it that a broken heart is the cause — because her daughter <a href="/wiki/Helena_Ravenclaw" title="Helena Ravenclaw">Helena</a> ran away with her <a href="/wiki/Ravenclaw%27s_Diadem" title="Ravenclaw&#39;s Diadem">diadem</a>. Rowena's portrait remains at Hogwarts.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Rowena_Ravenclaw?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Rowena_Ravenclaw?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_life">Early life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Rowena_Ravenclaw?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Early life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Rowena_Ravenclaw?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Early life"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<div class="quote">
<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire">"<i>Fair Ravenclaw, from glen….</i>"</span></dd>
<dd>&#8212;The <a href="/wiki/Sorting_Hat" title="Sorting Hat">Sorting Hat</a> on Ravenclaw's birthplace<sup class="noprint"><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Goblet_of_Fire" title="Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire">[src]</a></sup></dd>
<dd></dd></dl></div>
<p>Rowena Ravenclaw was born sometime in the <a href="/wiki/First_millennium_A.D." title="First millennium A.D.">tenth century</a>. She was from the glens of <a href="/wiki/Scotland" title="Scotland">Scotland</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-scotland_2-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-scotland-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> and was either born or married into the <a href="/wiki/Ravenclaw_family" title="Ravenclaw family">Ravenclaw family</a>. Rowena was a close friend of <a href="/wiki/Helga_Hufflepuff" title="Helga Hufflepuff">Helga Hufflepuff</a>, from the <a href="/wiki/Wales" title="Wales">Welsh valleys</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-OP11_9-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OP11-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>At an unknown time and by unknown means, Rowena acquired a <a href="/wiki/Wand" title="Wand">wand</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-FWC_10-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-FWC-10">&#91;10&#93;</a></sup> It is possible she purchased it from the <a href="/wiki/Ollivander_family" title="Ollivander family">Ollivander family</a>, who had been in the <a href="/wiki/Wand" title="Wand">wand</a>-making business since <a href="/wiki/B.C._era" title="B.C. era">382 B.C.</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-PS5_11-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PS5-11">&#91;11&#93;</a></sup> or made it herself.
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Hogwarts_founder">Hogwarts founder</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Rowena_Ravenclaw?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Hogwarts founder">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Rowena_Ravenclaw?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Hogwarts founder"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<div class="quote">
<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets">"<i>You all know, of course, that Hogwarts was founded over a thousand years ago — the precise date is uncertain — by the four greatest witches and wizards of the age. The four school Houses are named after them: Godric Gryffindor, Helga Hufflepuff, Rowena Ravenclaw, and Salazar Slytherin.</i>"</span></dd>
<dd>&#8212;<a href="/wiki/Cuthbert_Binns" title="Cuthbert Binns">Cuthbert Binns</a> teaching Hogwarts' history<sup class="noprint"><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Chamber_of_Secrets" title="Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets">[src]</a></sup></dd>
<dd></dd></dl></div><p>United by the common goal of creating the world's best magic school,<sup id="cite_ref-OP11_9-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OP11-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup> Rowena Ravenclaw and her good friends <a href="/wiki/Helga_Hufflepuff" title="Helga Hufflepuff">Helga Hufflepuff</a>, <a href="/wiki/Godric_Gryffindor" title="Godric Gryffindor">Godric Gryffindor</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Salazar_Slytherin" title="Salazar Slytherin">Salazar Slytherin</a> built <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Castle" title="Hogwarts Castle">Hogwarts Castle</a> together and established <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a><sup id="cite_ref-CS9_5-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-CS9-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> sometime around <a href="/wiki/First_millennium_A.D." title="First millennium A.D.">993</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-timeline_12-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-timeline-12">&#91;12&#93;</a></sup> It is a popular historical theory that the location and name of <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts</a> were both chosen by Rowena, who dreamt that a warty hog was leading her to a cliff by the lake.<sup id="cite_ref-WOMBAT_13-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-WOMBAT-13">&#91;13&#93;</a></sup> She also came up with the ever-changing floor plan.<sup id="cite_ref-HPMTC_14-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HPMTC-14">&#91;14&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Rowena selected her students according to intelligence and wisdom.<sup id="cite_ref-OP11_9-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OP11-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/Ravenclaw" title="Ravenclaw">Ravenclaw House</a> therefore values a sharp mind, wisdom, creativity, and cleverness in its members.<sup id="cite_ref-PS7_15-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PS7-15">&#91;15&#93;</a></sup> At some point, Rowena bore a daughter, <a href="/wiki/Helena_Ravenclaw" title="Helena Ravenclaw">Helena</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-DH31_7-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH31-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup> who was taught by Rowena herself at Hogwarts.<sup id="cite_ref-helenahouse_16-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-helenahouse-16">&#91;16&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>After a time in which the school enjoyed great prosperity, Rowena's fellow founder Salazar Slytherin proposed a controversial action in which <a href="/wiki/Muggle-born" title="Muggle-born">Muggle-born</a> students should not be admitted to Hogwarts based on their heritage. Ironically, a large percentage of Ravenclaw's own house were of Muggle descent. She and the rest of the founders refused Slytherin's plan and the problem escalated to such degrees that Salazar ultimately left the school permanently.
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Later_life_and_death">Later life and death</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Rowena_Ravenclaw?veaction=edit&amp;section=4" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Later life and death">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Rowena_Ravenclaw?action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Later life and death"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<div class="quote">
<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows">"<i>I stole the <a href="/wiki/Rowena_Ravenclaw%27s_Diadem" class="mw-redirect" title="Rowena Ravenclaw&#39;s Diadem">diadem</a>. I sought to make myself cleverer, more important than my mother. I ran away with it. My mother, they say, never admitted that the diadem was gone, but pretended that she had it still. She concealed her loss, my dreadful betrayal, even from the other founders of <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts</a>. Then my mother fell ill — fatally ill. In spite of my perfidy, she was desperate to see me one more time. She sent a man who had long loved me, though I spurned his advances, to find me. She knew that he would not rest until he had done so.</i>"</span></dd>
<dd>&#8212;<a href="/wiki/Helena_Ravenclaw" title="Helena Ravenclaw">Helena Ravenclaw</a>'s ghost reminiscing on her past<sup class="noprint"><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Deathly_Hallows" title="Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows">[src]</a></sup></dd>
<dd></dd></dl></div>
<p>At some point after her graduation from <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School</a>, her daughter Helena stole <a href="/wiki/Ravenclaw%27s_diadem" class="mw-redirect" title="Ravenclaw&#39;s diadem">Ravenclaw's diadem</a>, which was said to enhance the wearer's wisdom. Helena was jealous of her mother's famous intellect and took the diadem with her to <a href="/wiki/Albania" title="Albania">Albania</a>, far away from her mother's reach, in hopes of surpassing her. Ashamed of her daughter's betrayal, Rowena opted to keep her daughter's treachery a secret from others and denied it even from her fellow founders.<sup id="cite_ref-DH31_7-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH31-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Shortly afterwards, Rowena fell fatally ill. Disregarding her treason, she asked the <a href="/wiki/Bloody_Baron" title="Bloody Baron">Baron</a>, who was in love with Helena, to go and find her daughter, merely wishing to see her one last time. He eventually tracked her to a forest in <a href="/wiki/Albania" title="Albania">Albania</a> but when she refused to return with him, the Baron flew into a fit of rage, furious at her refusal and envious of her freedom, and stabbed her. Immediately overcome with guilt, he then took his own life. The two would later become, respectively, <a href="/wiki/Ravenclaw" title="Ravenclaw">Ravenclaw</a> and <a href="/wiki/Slytherin" title="Slytherin">Slytherin</a> house <a href="/wiki/Ghost" title="Ghost">ghosts</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-DH31_7-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH31-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup> Thus, Rowena apparently didn't get the chance to reconcile with her daughter before their untimely deaths, unless her daughter's ghost returned to the castle before Rowena's own death. Legend had it that a "<i>broken heart</i>" added to her early demise.<sup id="cite_ref-WotM_17-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-WotM-17">&#91;17&#93;</a></sup> This story may have had origin in her estrangement with her daughter. Rowena was the first of the founders to die<sup id="cite_ref-18" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-18">&#91;18&#93;</a></sup>.
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Legacy">Legacy</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Rowena_Ravenclaw?veaction=edit&amp;section=5" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Legacy">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Rowena_Ravenclaw?action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Legacy"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Rowena's stolen diadem would remain hidden in its lonely locale for centuries until the mid <a href="/wiki/1900s" title="1900s">1900s</a>, where her daughter's ghost was charmed by Sytherin student <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Tom Riddle</a> into revealing its location. Acting on this information, Riddle took the diadem from its hiding place and defiled both it and the memory of Ravenclaw by turning it into a <a href="/wiki/Horcrux" title="Horcrux">Horcrux</a>. The defiled artefact was then hidden within the castle Rowena had helped build for years more until it was <a href="/wiki/Crabbe" title="Crabbe">indirectly</a> <a href="/wiki/Fiendfyre" title="Fiendfyre">destroyed</a> by <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> during the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a> in <a href="/wiki/1998" title="1998">1998</a>.
</p><p>According to the questionable publication <i><a href="/wiki/The_Quibbler" title="The Quibbler">The Quibbler</a></i>, Rowena also developed a strategy for traversing the tricky stairs of the <a href="/wiki/Grand_Staircase" title="Grand Staircase">Grand Staircase</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-PAS_19-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PAS-19">&#91;19&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Physical_appearance">Physical appearance</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Rowena_Ravenclaw?veaction=edit&amp;section=6" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Physical appearance">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Rowena_Ravenclaw?action=edit&amp;section=6" title="Edit section: Physical appearance"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>From her statue in <a href="/wiki/Ravenclaw_Tower" title="Ravenclaw Tower">Ravenclaw Tower</a> and <a href="/wiki/Xenophilius_Lovegood" title="Xenophilius Lovegood">Xenophilius Lovegood</a>'s bust of her, Rowena was a beautiful yet austere-looking and slightly intimidating woman.<sup id="cite_ref-DH20_20-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH20-20">&#91;20&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-DH29_21-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH29-21">&#91;21&#93;</a></sup> She was tall,<sup id="cite_ref-DH31_7-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH31-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup> had pale skin, dark eyes, and long black hair.<sup id="cite_ref-WotM_17-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-WotM-17">&#91;17&#93;</a></sup> Rowena spoke in a pronounced <a href="/wiki/Scotland" title="Scotland">Scottish</a> accent.<sup id="cite_ref-WWHP_22-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-WWHP-22">&#91;22&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Personality_and_traits">Personality and traits</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Rowena_Ravenclaw?veaction=edit&amp;section=7" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Personality and traits">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Rowena_Ravenclaw?action=edit&amp;section=7" title="Edit section: Personality and traits"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Ravenclaw's most notable traits are her intelligence and creativity. She valued the same characteristics in her students.<sup id="cite_ref-PS7_15-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PS7-15">&#91;15&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-OP11_9-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OP11-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup> Rowena seemed strict and intimidating,<sup id="cite_ref-DH29_21-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH29-21">&#91;21&#93;</a></sup> but despite this she seemed to be forgiving and loving. This shown by the fact that she was willing to disregard <a href="/wiki/Helena_Ravenclaw" title="Helena Ravenclaw">her daughter's</a> betrayal just to see her one last time on her deathbed.<sup id="cite_ref-DH31_7-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH31-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Ravenclaw was very proud, not admitting that her daughter had run away with <a href="/wiki/Rowena_Ravenclaw%27s_Diadem" class="mw-redirect" title="Rowena Ravenclaw&#39;s Diadem">her diadem</a> not even to her close friends, the other <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_founders" title="Hogwarts founders">founders</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-DH29_21-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH29-21">&#91;21&#93;</a></sup> However, she wasn't prejudiced towards those of <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggle</a> parentage or inheritance, as she disagreed with <a href="/wiki/Salazar_Slytherin" title="Salazar Slytherin">Slytherin's</a> views on admittance to Hogwarts and a large percentage of her house were of Muggle descent.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Magical_abilities_and_skills">Magical abilities and skills</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Rowena_Ravenclaw?veaction=edit&amp;section=8" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Rowena_Ravenclaw?action=edit&amp;section=8" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<figure class="thumb tright show-info-icon" style="width: 152px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/3/3e/RavenclawCard.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20110729003916" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/3/3e/RavenclawCard.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/150?cb=20110729003916" decoding="async" width="150" height="225" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="RavenclawCard.jpg" data-image-key="RavenclawCard.jpg" /></a> 	 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:RavenclawCard.jpg" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption">Ravenclaw drawing up Hogwarts's ever-changing floor plan</p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure>
<p>Ravenclaw was described as the most brilliant <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch</a> of her time. Her capacity to cast a <a href="/wiki/Patronus_Charm" title="Patronus Charm">Patronus</a> further showed her superior <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magical</a> abilities.
</p>
<ul><li><b><a href="/wiki/Charm" title="Charm">Charms</a></b>: Rowena had a considerable level of skill and proficiency in charms. Along with the other founders, she bewitched the <a href="/wiki/Sorting_Hat" title="Sorting Hat">Sorting Hat</a> into a sentient <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magical</a> artefact with personality to <a href="/wiki/Sorting_ceremony" title="Sorting ceremony">sort</a> students well after their lifetime. She presumably enchanted her <a href="/wiki/Rowena_Ravenclaw%27s_diadem" class="mw-redirect" title="Rowena Ravenclaw&#39;s diadem">diadem</a> to enhance the wearer's wisdom. Furthermore, Rowena was capable of conjuring a non-corporeal <a href="/wiki/Patronus_Charm" title="Patronus Charm">Patronus</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-LEGOY2_8-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-LEGOY2-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup> despite its immense difficulty.</li>
<li><b>Magical architecture</b>: It is thought that Rowena herself devised the ever-changing floor plan of <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Castle" title="Hogwarts Castle">Hogwarts Castle</a>.</li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Divination" title="Divination">Divination</a></b>: According to legend, Rowena dreamt about a warty hog leading her to a cliff by the lake where she and her companions would build Hogwarts.<sup id="cite_ref-WOMBAT_13-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-WOMBAT-13">&#91;13&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li><b>Invention</b>: Rowena presumably crafted and enchanted <a href="/wiki/Ravenclaw%27s_diadem" class="mw-redirect" title="Ravenclaw&#39;s diadem">her diadem</a> personally. No other witch or wizard has been able to replicate its effects.</li></ul>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Rowena_Ravenclaw?veaction=edit&amp;section=11" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Rowena_Ravenclaw?action=edit&amp;section=11" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>In <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Welsh_poetry" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Welsh poetry">Welsh poetry</a>, Rowena is named "mother of the nation", which could link in to her being a founder of Hogwarts. She also is another character whose name surfaces in the great epic <i><a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ivanhoe" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Ivanhoe">Ivanhoe</a></i>. Rowena is a Latinised form of an old Germanic name: "Fame" (cf. the Anglo-Saxon hrōd, or hrōð), and "Joy", (cf. the Anglo-Saxon wyn(n)), or "Friend", (cf. the Anglo-Saxon wine). Rowena, Queen of Britain, is a figure in Britain's traditional history; she was supposedly the daughter of Hengest and the wife of Vortigern.</li>
<li>Other character of the same is Lady Rowena Trevanion from the short story "Ligeia" by Edgar Allan Poe (who also wrote "The Raven") whom the narrator marries after the death of his first wife with raven-colored hair. When Lady Rowena dies, he watches as she comes back to life in the form of his first wife.</li>
<li>Ravenclaw is simply 'raven' + 'claw'. A raven is a large black bird that belongs to the crow family. However, Ravenclaw's symbol is an eagle. Ravens and other birds of the corvid family are known to be smart birds and quick learners.</li></ul>

<li>It is possible that Ravenclaw is not Rowena's birth name, but her married name, although this has never been confirmed by <a href="/wiki/J._K._Rowling" title="J. K. Rowling">J.K Rowling</a>. Also even though the word "Raven" is in Ravenclaw's name, the crest of her house depicts an eagle. However, given that she was a proud woman, she could simply have declined to take the surname of her possible husband (father of Helena) upon marriage and kept her maiden name. It is also possible that she was never married.
<ul><li>It is interesting to note that Albania is sometimes considered the "Land of Eagles" in the 16th century and that Rowena's daughter fled to Albania, so it is therefore possible that Rowena's family or her possible husband's came from Albania before settling into Scotland.</li></ul></li>
<li>Rowena Ravenclaw's <a href="/wiki/Chocolate_Frog_Card" title="Chocolate Frog Card">Chocolate Frog Card</a>, as featured in the PC video games of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Philosopher%27s_Stone_(video_game)" title="Harry Potter and the Philosopher&#39;s Stone (video game)">Harry Potter and the Philosopher's Stone</a></i> and <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Chamber_of_Secrets_(video_game)" title="Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets (video game)">Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets</a></i>, Rowena is somewhat anachronistically depicted wearing sixteenth/seventeenth-century garments, while Ravenclaw lived in the tenth century.</li></ul>
