<b>Cedrella Weasley</b> (née <b>Black</b>) was a <a href="/wiki/Pure-blood" title="Pure-blood">pure-blood</a> witch, the second eldest daughter of <a href="/wiki/Arcturus_Black_II" title="Arcturus Black II">Arcturus Black II</a> and <a href="/wiki/Lysandra_Yaxley" title="Lysandra Yaxley">Lysandra Black</a>, and sister of <a href="/wiki/Charis_Black" title="Charis Black">Charis</a> and <a href="/wiki/Callidora_Black" title="Callidora Black">Callidora</a>.
</p><p>Cedrella married <a href="/wiki/Septimus_Weasley" title="Septimus Weasley">Septimus Weasley</a>. The <a href="/wiki/Weasley_family" title="Weasley family">Weasley family</a> had been considered <a href="/wiki/Blood_traitor" title="Blood traitor">blood traitors</a> ever since they protested their inclusion in the <a href="/wiki/Sacred_Twenty-Eight" title="Sacred Twenty-Eight">Sacred Twenty-Eight</a>, and so, due to her marriage, Cedrella was disowned and burned off the <a href="/wiki/Black_Family_Tapestry" title="Black Family Tapestry">Black family tree tapestry</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-BFT_2-16" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-BFT-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Cedrella and Septimus had three sons, one of whom is <a href="/wiki/Arthur_Weasley" title="Arthur Weasley">Arthur Weasley</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-arthur_4-10" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-arthur-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> Through Cedrella the <a href="/wiki/Weasley_family" title="Weasley family">Weasley family</a> is related not only to the Black family, but also to the <a href="/wiki/Crouch_family" title="Crouch family">Crouch</a> and <a href="/wiki/Longbottom_family" title="Longbottom family">Longbottom</a> families.<sup id="cite_ref-BFT_2-17" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-BFT-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> Through <a href="/wiki/Arthur_Weasley" title="Arthur Weasley">Arthur</a>, she has seven grandchildren.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Cedrella_Black?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Cedrella_Black?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p><i>Cedrella</i> is a rare feminine variant of "Cedric", which in turn is derived from the Old Celtic name <i>Caratacos</i>, which means "<a href="/wiki/Love" title="Love">love</a>".<sup id="cite_ref-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p><i><a target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener" class="external text" href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Cedrela">Cedrela</a> </i>is also a genus in the mahogany family, native to the New World. <i>Cedrela odorata</i> is a timber wood often sold under the name "Spanish-cedar" even though it is neither Spanish nor a cedar. It is used in the making of cigar boxes and guitars as well as general construction work.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Cedrella_Black?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Cedrella_Black?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li><a href="/wiki/Horace_Slughorn" title="Horace Slughorn">Horace Slughorn</a> claimed that the Blacks were almost exclusively sorted into <a href="/wiki/Slytherin" title="Slytherin">Slytherin House</a><sup id="cite_ref-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup>, thus Cedrella might be considered an example of a "good Slytherin" for her non-biased views about <a href="/wiki/Blood_status" title="Blood status">blood purity</a>.</li>
<li>Cedrella’s older sister Callidora was born in 1915, and her younger sister Charis was born in 1919, so Cedrella must have been born sometime between late 1915 and early 1919.</li>
<li>Cedrella had three sons. One of them was <a href="/wiki/Arthur_Weasley" title="Arthur Weasley">Arthur</a>, and is possible that another one was <a href="/wiki/Bilius" title="Bilius">Bilius</a>.</li>
<li>On the family tree, Cedrella is depicted next to her sisters. She looks similar to her great-aunt <a href="/wiki/Iola_Black" title="Iola Black">Iola</a>, who was likewise disowned due to her marriage.</li>
<li>Cedrella is similar to <a href="/wiki/Andromeda_Tonks" title="Andromeda Tonks">Andromeda Tonks</a>, who, like Cedrella, was the middle of three daughters and married against her family's wishes while her elder and younger sisters made "respectable" <a href="/wiki/Pure-blood" title="Pure-blood">pureblood</a> <a href="/wiki/Marriage" title="Marriage">marriages</a>.</li></ul>
