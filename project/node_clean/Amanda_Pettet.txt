<b>Amanda Pettet</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggle</a> reporter who co-wrote a front-page article with <a href="/wiki/Robert_Bliss" title="Robert Bliss">Robert Bliss</a> about the heat wave that swept over <a href="/wiki/Great_Britain" title="Great Britain">Great Britain</a> for the <i><a href="/wiki/Daily_Express" title="Daily Express">Daily Express</a></i> in the summer of <a href="/wiki/1995" title="1995">1995</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-HPLE_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HPLE-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Amanda_Pettet?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Amanda_Pettet?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>This character was presumably named after Amanda Pettett, who worked as a production assistant on the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Philosopher%27s_Stone_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Philosopher&#39;s Stone (film)">film adaptation</a> of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Philosopher%27s_Stone" title="Harry Potter and the Philosopher&#39;s Stone">Harry Potter and the Philosopher's Stone</a></i>, and as a construction coordinator on all seven <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_films" class="mw-redirect" title="Harry Potter films"><i>Harry Potter</i> films</a> released thereafter.<sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> Whether the spelling of her surname was altered intentionally or as a result of a typographical error is unknown.</li></ul>
