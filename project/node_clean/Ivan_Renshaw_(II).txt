<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Ivan_Renshaw_(II)?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Ivan_Renshaw_(II)?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The name "Ivan" is the newer form of the old Slavic name Іѡаннъ (Ioannu), which was derived from Greek <i>Ioannes</i>.It refers back to <i>John</i>,which means "God (Herbrew) is gracious".
</p><p><i>Renshaw </i>is a surname from Prestbury,Cheshire in England.The suffix <i>shaw </i>means "wood",so the whole name means "Ren-wood".
</p>
