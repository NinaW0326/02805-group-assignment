<b>Perpetua Fancourt</b> (<a href="/wiki/1900" title="1900">1900</a>-<a href="/wiki/1991" title="1991">1991</a>) was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch</a> who is credited with the invention of the <a href="/wiki/Lunascope" title="Lunascope">lunascope</a>, an astronomical device used to analyse the phases of the <a href="/wiki/Moon" title="Moon">moon</a>.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Perpetua_Fancourt?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Perpetua_Fancourt?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_life">Early life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Perpetua_Fancourt?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Early life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Perpetua_Fancourt?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Early life"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<div class="quote">
<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;Pottermore">"<i>Ravenclaw house has an illustrious history. Most of the greatest wizarding inventors and innovators were in our house, including Perpetua Fancourt, the inventor of the lunascope </i>[â€¦]<i>.</i>"</span></dd>
<dd>&#8212;<a href="/wiki/Robert_Hilliard" title="Robert Hilliard">Robert Hilliard</a>.<sup class="noprint"><a href="/wiki/Pottermore" title="Pottermore">[src]</a></sup></dd>
<dd></dd></dl></div><p>Perpetua Fancourt was born in <a href="/wiki/1900" title="1900">1900</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-PSg_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PSg-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> somewhere in the <a href="/wiki/Great_Britain" title="Great Britain">British Isles</a>.
</p><p>In her youth, she attended <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> from around <a href="/wiki/1911" title="1911">1911</a> to <a href="/wiki/1918" title="1918">1918</a>, where she was <a href="/wiki/Sorting_ceremony" title="Sorting ceremony">sorted</a> into <a href="/wiki/Ravenclaw" title="Ravenclaw">Ravenclaw</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-pottermore_2-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-pottermore-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Adulthood">Adulthood</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Perpetua_Fancourt?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Adulthood">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Perpetua_Fancourt?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Adulthood"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<figure class="thumb tleft show-info-icon" style="width: 202px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/0/04/Perpetua_FancourtGBC.png/revision/latest?cb=20110905212351" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/0/04/Perpetua_FancourtGBC.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/200?cb=20110905212351" decoding="async" width="200" height="233" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="Perpetua FancourtGBC.png" data-image-key="Perpetua_FancourtGBC.png" /></a> 	 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:Perpetua_FancourtGBC.png" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption">A younger Perpetua Fancourt</p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure>
<div class="quote">
<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban">"<i>It's a lunascope, old boy â€” no more messing around with moon charts, see?</i>"</span></dd>
<dd>&#8212;A diner at the <a href="/wiki/Leaky_Cauldron" title="Leaky Cauldron">Leaky Cauldron</a> in <a href="/wiki/1993" title="1993">1993</a>.<sup class="noprint"><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Prisoner_of_Azkaban" title="Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban">[src]</a></sup></dd>
<dd></dd></dl></div>
<p>After her graduation from <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School</a>, Ms. Fancourt studied further into <a href="/wiki/Astronomy_(class)" title="Astronomy (class)">Astronomy</a>, becoming an accomplished and celebrated Astronomer. Perhaps in an attempt to further the study of her area of expertise, she invented an optical device to counter the difficulty of working with moon charts, which she named <a href="/wiki/Lunascope" title="Lunascope">lunascope</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-PSg_3-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PSg-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> Ms. Fancourt must have created it at a fairly young age, as by <a href="/wiki/1926" title="1926">1926</a> they were available to own.
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Death_and_post-mortem">Death and post-mortem</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Perpetua_Fancourt?veaction=edit&amp;section=4" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Death and post-mortem">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Perpetua_Fancourt?action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Death and post-mortem"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Ms. Fancourt died in <a href="/wiki/1991" title="1991">1991</a>, aged around 91. In her honour, her achievement, the invention of the <a href="/wiki/Lunascope" title="Lunascope">lunascope</a>, was recorded on a <a href="/wiki/Chocolate_Frog_Card" title="Chocolate Frog Card">Chocolate Frog Card</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-PSg_3-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PSg-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>On <a href="/wiki/1_September" title="1 September">1 September</a>, <a href="/wiki/1991" title="1991">1991</a>, <a href="/wiki/Prefect" title="Prefect">Prefect</a> <a href="/wiki/Robert_Hilliard" title="Robert Hilliard">Robert Hilliard</a> welcomed the <a href="/wiki/Ravenclaw" title="Ravenclaw">Ravenclaw</a> <a href="/wiki/First_year" title="First year">first years</a> to <a href="/wiki/Ravenclaw_Tower" title="Ravenclaw Tower">Ravenclaw Tower</a> with a short speech in which he mentioned inventor Perpetua Fancourt as one of the many notable Hogwarts students who had been <a href="/wiki/Sorting_ceremony" title="Sorting ceremony">sorted</a>, like them, into Ravenclaw.<sup id="cite_ref-pottermore_2-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-pottermore-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Magical_abilities_and_skills">Magical abilities and skills</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Perpetua_Fancourt?veaction=edit&amp;section=5" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Perpetua_Fancourt?action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li><b><a href="/wiki/Astronomy_(class)" title="Astronomy (class)">Astronomy</a></b>: Fancourt was a celebrated and highly accomplished professional <a href="/wiki/Astronomer" title="Astronomer">Astronomer</a> with much knowledge of this branch of both <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magic</a> and <a href="/wiki/Science" title="Science">science</a>. She specialised in the study of <a href="/wiki/Moon" title="Moon">moons</a>, and even invented her own Astronomical device, the <a href="/wiki/Lunascope" title="Lunascope">Lunascope</a>, to help overcome the difficulty of <a href="/wiki/Moon_chart" title="Moon chart">Moon charts</a>, a very impressive achievement in the field which gained her recognition on a <a href="/wiki/Chocolate_Frog_Card" title="Chocolate Frog Card">Chocolate Frog Card</a>.</li></ul>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Perpetua_Fancourt?veaction=edit&amp;section=6" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Perpetua_Fancourt?action=edit&amp;section=6" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The name "Perpetua" is derived from Latin perpetuus meaning "continuous".
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Perpetua_Fancourt?veaction=edit&amp;section=7" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Perpetua_Fancourt?action=edit&amp;section=7" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>Based on her year of birth, she would have been three or four years below Newt Scamander, who owned one of her Lunascopes, during their school years at Hogwarts, though it is unknown if they knew each other.</li></ul>
