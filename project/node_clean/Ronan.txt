<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Ronan?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Ronan?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>On the night of <a href="/wiki/26_May" title="26 May">26 May</a> <a href="/wiki/1992" title="1992">1992</a>, Ronan along with fellow centaur <a href="/wiki/Bane" title="Bane">Bane</a> met <a href="/wiki/Rubeus_Hagrid" title="Rubeus Hagrid">Rubeus Hagrid</a> in the Forbidden Forest, who was investigating that night why <a href="/wiki/Unicorn" title="Unicorn">unicorns</a> in the forest were being slaughtered, although the two centaurs could not explain why and thus were unable to help him.<sup id="cite_ref-PS15_1-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PS15-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>He was distrustful of <a href="/wiki/Human" title="Human">humans</a>, although he tried to protect <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> and <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione Granger</a> from the other centaurs in <a href="/wiki/1996" title="1996">1996</a>, pointing out that they were "foals", during the <a href="/wiki/Skirmish_in_the_Forbidden_Forest" title="Skirmish in the Forbidden Forest">Skirmish in the Forbidden Forest</a> involving <a href="/wiki/Dolores_Umbridge" title="Dolores Umbridge">Dolores Umbridge</a>. He was described as speaking with a doleful tone, and seemed rather peaceful compared to other centaurs.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP33_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP33-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>He led the charge of the centaurs from the <a href="/wiki/Forbidden_Forest" title="Forbidden Forest">Forbidden Forest</a> during the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a>, breaking through the <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eater</a> ranks into the <a href="/wiki/Great_Hall" title="Great Hall">Great Hall</a>. It is likely that he witnessed the final battle between <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a> and Harry Potter.<sup id="cite_ref-DH36_2-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH36-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Physical_description">Physical description</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Ronan?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Physical description">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Ronan?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Physical description"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>He was described as having red hair, and a red beard. Below the waist, he had a <a href="/wiki/Horse" title="Horse">horse</a>'s gleaming chestnut body with a long and reddish tail.<sup id="cite_ref-PS15_1-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PS15-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Ronan?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Ronan?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p><b>R칩n치n</b> (anglicized <b>Ronan</b>) is an Irish male given name meaning "little seal", <i>R칩n</i> meaning "seal", and -<i>치n</i> being a diminutive suffix in Irish Gaelic).
</p>
