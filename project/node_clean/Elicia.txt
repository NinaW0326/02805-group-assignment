<b>Elicia</b> was a popular <a href="/wiki/Great_Britain" title="Great Britain">Britain</a> <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggle</a> <a href="/wiki/Television" title="Television">television</a> actress during the <a href="/wiki/1990s" title="1990s">1990s</a>. She gave an interview in an issue of the <a href="/wiki/Great_Britain" title="Great Britain">British</a> periodical <i><a href="/wiki/Radio_Times" title="Radio Times">Radio Times</a></i> that was published sometime during the summer of <a href="/wiki/1997" title="1997">1997</a>. The interview explored how she achieved such great success in her career.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
