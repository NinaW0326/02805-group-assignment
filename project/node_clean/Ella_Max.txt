<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Ella_Max?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Ella_Max?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li><i>Ella</i> means "all" and is short form of a name Alia.</li>
<li><i>Max</i> is a short form of a name Maximilian. The name means "greatest".</li>
<li>Therefore <i>Ella Max</i> can be translated as "the greatest of all".</li>
<li>Ella's possible daughter Elladora was most likely named after her, and her name etymology is symbolic: Elladora was Ella's gift, "Dora".</li></ul>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Ella_Max?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Ella_Max?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>This character's name appears only on the version of the <a href="/wiki/House_of_Black" title="House of Black">Black family tapestry</a> shown in the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Order_of_the_Phoenix_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix (film)">film adaptation</a> of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Order_of_the_Phoenix" title="Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix">Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix</a></i>. She was not included on the version of the tree which <a href="/wiki/J._K._Rowling" title="J. K. Rowling">J. K. Rowling</a> gave to a charity auction in <a href="/wiki/2006" title="2006">2006</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> However, according to director <a href="/wiki/David_Yates" title="David Yates">David Yates</a>, Rowling provided the filmmakers with a copy of the Black family tree going back eight generations.<sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> The filmmakers presumably received a more complete version of the tree than the one auctioned off, which only goes back six generations.</li>
<li>Given her placement on the tree in the film, it is possible that this character was the mother of <a href="/wiki/Sirius_Black_I" title="Sirius Black I">Sirius</a>, <a href="/wiki/Phineas_Nigellus_Black" title="Phineas Nigellus Black">Phineas Nigellus</a>, <a href="/wiki/Elladora_Black" title="Elladora Black">Elladora</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Iola_Black" title="Iola Black">Iola</a> (the third of whom may have been named in her honour). Cygnus would be their father, and Arcturus an uncle.</li>
<li>The names Ella, Ella-, -ella and -ella- appears many times in the Black family</li></ul>
<ol><li><b>Ella</b> Black née Max</li>
<li><b>Ella</b>dora Black</li>
<li>Cedr<b>ella</b> Weasley née Black</li>
<li>Dru<b>ella</b> Black née Rosier</li>
<li>B<b>ella</b>trix Lestrange née Black</li></ol>
