<figure class="thumb tleft show-info-icon" style="width: 182px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/f/f7/Baby_Troll_WU.png/revision/latest?cb=20201105083506" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/f/f7/Baby_Troll_WU.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/180?cb=20201105083506" decoding="async" width="180" height="180" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="Baby Troll WU.png" data-image-key="Baby_Troll_WU.png" /></a> 	 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:Baby_Troll_WU.png" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption">Baby Troll</p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure><p>They are the largest breed, weighing over a tonne,<sup><sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup></sup> and the most vicious kind of their species. They are often bald <a href="/wiki/Beast" title="Beast">beasts</a> with thick, dark grey skin. They sometimes use <a href="/wiki/Graphorn" title="Graphorn">Graphorns</a> as mounts, which results in them being scared due to Graphorns heavily disliking such travel.<sup id="cite_ref-FBR_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-FBR-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>

<p>A band of mountain trolls were wreaking havoc in <a href="/wiki/Liechtenstein" title="Liechtenstein">Liechtenstein</a>, which led to <a href="/wiki/Pierre_Bonaccord" title="Pierre Bonaccord">Pierre Bonaccord</a>'s position as <a href="/wiki/Supreme_Mugwump" title="Supreme Mugwump">Supreme Mugwump</a> of the <a href="/wiki/International_Confederation_of_Wizards" title="International Confederation of Wizards">International Confederation of Wizards</a> being challenged by the local wizarding community, as he tried to ban troll-hunting and grant them rights at the time.
</p><p><a href="/wiki/Quirinus_Quirrell%27s_first_mountain_troll" title="Quirinus Quirrell&#39;s first mountain troll">A male mountain troll</a> was used as a diversion so that <a href="/wiki/Professor" title="Professor">Professor</a> <a href="/wiki/Quirinus_Quirrell" title="Quirinus Quirrell">Quirinus Quirrell</a> could attempt to get past <a href="/wiki/Fluffy" title="Fluffy">Fluffy</a> to capture the <a href="/wiki/Philosopher%27s_Stone" title="Philosopher&#39;s Stone">Philosopher's Stone</a> for his master, <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-PS10_4-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PS10-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Late in August <a href="/wiki/2020" title="2020">2020</a>, Mountain trolls rode <a href="/wiki/Graphorn" title="Graphorn">graphorns</a> through <a href="/wiki/Hungary" title="Hungary">Hungary</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-CC_5-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-CC-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>.
</p>
