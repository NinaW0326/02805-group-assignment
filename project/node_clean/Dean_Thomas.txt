<div class="quote">
<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows">"<i>We're fighting, aren't we? The message said Harry was back, and we were going to fight! I'll have to get a wand, though....</i>"</span></dd>
<dd>&#8212;Dean's determination to fight in the Battle of Hogwarts<sup class="noprint"><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Deathly_Hallows" title="Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows">[src]</a></sup></dd>

<p><b>Dean Thomas</b> (b. <a href="/wiki/1979" title="1979">1979</a>/<a href="/wiki/1980" title="1980">1980</a>) was an <a href="/wiki/England" title="England">English</a> <a href="/wiki/Half-blood" title="Half-blood">half-blood</a> <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a>, the son of a <a href="/wiki/Dean_Thomas%27s_father" title="Dean Thomas&#39;s father">wizard</a> and a <a href="/wiki/Dean_Thomas%27s_mother" title="Dean Thomas&#39;s mother">Muggle mother</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-rowling1_2-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-rowling1-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> When Dean Thomas was very young, his father died and his mother married <a href="/wiki/Dean_Thomas%27s_stepfather" title="Dean Thomas&#39;s stepfather">another man</a>, giving Dean <a href="/wiki/Dean_Thomas%27s_half-siblings" title="Dean Thomas&#39;s half-siblings">several half-siblings</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-rowling1_2-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-rowling1-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> Since Dean's father never told his mother that he was a wizard and was eventually murdered by <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a> when he refused to join them, Dean grew up thinking he was <a href="/wiki/Muggle-born" title="Muggle-born">Muggle-born</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-rowling1_2-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-rowling1-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Dean was a <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor" title="Gryffindor">Gryffindor</a> student at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a>. He was in the same year as the famous <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> and played as a <a href="/wiki/Chaser" title="Chaser">Chaser</a> for his house's <a href="/wiki/Quidditch" title="Quidditch">Quidditch</a> <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor_Quidditch_team" title="Gryffindor Quidditch team">team</a> for a time. In his fifth year, he joined <a href="/wiki/Dumbledore%27s_Army" title="Dumbledore&#39;s Army">Dumbledore's Army</a>, an organisation taught and led by <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>. Dean (although a half-blood) could not prove that he had any wizarding heritage and was therefore in danger when <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a> took control of the <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">Ministry of Magic</a> in <a href="/wiki/1997" title="1997">1997</a>. 
</p><p>Dean was forced to go on the run to avoid arrest and imprisonment by the <a href="/wiki/Muggle-Born_Registration_Commission" title="Muggle-Born Registration Commission">Muggle-Born Registration Commission</a>, missing his seventh year of schooling. He was eventually captured by <a href="/wiki/Snatchers" title="Snatchers">Snatchers</a>, but <a href="/wiki/Skirmish_at_Malfoy_Manor" title="Skirmish at Malfoy Manor">escaped</a> <a href="/wiki/Malfoy_Manor" title="Malfoy Manor">Malfoy Manor</a> along with several other prisoners and soon returned to <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> to participate in the Battle of Hogwarts.
</p><p>Dean survived the <a href="/wiki/Second_Wizarding_War" title="Second Wizarding War">Second Wizarding War</a> and was seen after the final battle sitting next to <a href="/wiki/Seamus_Finnigan" title="Seamus Finnigan">Seamus Finnigan</a> and <a href="/wiki/Aberforth_Dumbledore" title="Aberforth Dumbledore">Aberforth Dumbledore</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Great_Hall" title="Great Hall">Great Hall</a>. His later life however, remains unknown.
</p>

<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_life">Early life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Dean_Thomas?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Early life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Dean_Thomas?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Early life"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<div class="quote">
<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows"><b>Dirk Cresswell</b>: "<i>Muggle-born, eh?</i>"</span></dd>
<dd><b>Dean</b>: "<i>Not sure. My dad left my mum when I was a kid. I’ve got no proof he was a wizard, though.</i>"</dd>
<dd>&#8212; Dean regarding his heritage<sup class="noprint"><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Deathly_Hallows" title="Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows">[src]</a></sup></dd></dl>
</div>
<p>Dean is the son of a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> <a href="/wiki/Dean_Thomas%27s_father" title="Dean Thomas&#39;s father">father</a> and a <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggle</a> <a href="/wiki/Dean_Thomas%27s_mother" title="Dean Thomas&#39;s mother">mother</a>, though he believed he was <a href="/wiki/Muggle-born" title="Muggle-born">Muggle-born</a> when he went to Hogwarts.<sup id="cite_ref-rowling1_2-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-rowling1-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> His father left when Dean was very young, never telling his wife that he was a wizard, out of the belief that not knowing the truth would protect her and their son from the potential dangers of the <a href="/wiki/First_Wizarding_War" title="First Wizarding War">First Wizarding War</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-rowling1_2-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-rowling1-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> Dean's father was later murdered by a group of <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a> when he refused to join their ranks.<sup id="cite_ref-rowling1_2-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-rowling1-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Dean's mother later remarried <a href="/wiki/Dean_Thomas%27s_stepfather" title="Dean Thomas&#39;s stepfather">a Muggle</a>, who raised Dean as his own son.<sup id="cite_ref-rowling1_2-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-rowling1-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> Dean lived in <a href="/wiki/London" title="London">London</a> with several <a href="/wiki/Dean_Thomas%27_half-siblings" class="mw-redirect" title="Dean Thomas&#39; half-siblings">half-siblings</a>, who were implied to all be female.<sup id="cite_ref-rowling1_2-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-rowling1-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-dh22_7-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-dh22-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup> He had a "very happy home life."<sup id="cite_ref-rowling1_2-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-rowling1-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> He was a supporter of the <a href="https://harrypotter.fandom.com/wiki/West_Ham_United_Football_Club" class="extiw" title="en:West Ham United Football Club">West Ham United Football Club</a>, and was very artistic and showed off his talent throughout his school career. All of Dean's half-siblings were <a href="/wiki/Muggle" class="mw-redirect" title="Muggle">non-magical</a>.
</p>

<p>Dean shared a dormitory with Seamus, who soon became his best friend, as well as with <a href="/wiki/Neville_Longbottom" title="Neville Longbottom">Neville Longbottom</a>, <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ron Weasley</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>, with whom he later became good friends. It is unknown, though, about who Dean attended the dance with, as his best friend <a href="/wiki/Seamus_Finnigan" title="Seamus Finnigan">Seamus Finnigan</a> was going with <a href="/wiki/Lavender_Brown" title="Lavender Brown">Lavender Brown</a> and <a href="/wiki/Neville_Longbottom" title="Neville Longbottom">Neville</a> was going with <a href="/wiki/Ginevra_Weasley" title="Ginevra Weasley">Ginny Weasley</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-GOF_10-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF-10">&#91;10&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Later on in the tournament, after Ron Weasley had forgiven Harry, Dean also seemed to be more supportive. After the third and final task, Harry returned to Hogwarts with <a href="/wiki/Cedric_Diggory" title="Cedric Diggory">Cedric Diggory</a>'s dead body, and he said that <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a> was at large again. Albus Dumbledore took this information and at the end of the year he gathered students for a <a href="/wiki/Memorial_feast_to_Cedric_Diggory" title="Memorial feast to Cedric Diggory">Memorial feast to Cedric Diggory</a> in which he told them about the events that took place during the third task.<sup id="cite_ref-GOF_10-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF-10">&#91;10&#93;</a></sup>

She called <a href="/wiki/Remus_Lupin" title="Remus Lupin">Remus Lupin</a> a "<i>dangerous half-breed</i>," which caused Dean to jump in to his defence, claiming that he was the best teacher he ever had, and commenting in response to Umbridge's comments that her <a href="/wiki/Alastor_Moody" title="Alastor Moody">predecessor</a> had actually performed curses on them that the man in question had "<i>turned out to be a <a href="/wiki/Bartemius_Crouch_Junior" title="Bartemius Crouch Junior">maniac</a></i>", although he noted that they had still learned a great deal. Additionally, he and Seamus argued when Dean believed Harry about Voldemort returning and Seamus initially did not.<sup id="cite_ref-OTP_11-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OTP-11">&#91;11&#93;</a></sup>

<p>Umbridge soon caused an uproar amongst most students due to her ineffective teaching and cruelty, and when it came time to rebel, Dean was one of the first to arrive for the inaugural meeting of the group that became <a href="/wiki/Dumbledore%27s_Army" title="Dumbledore&#39;s Army">Dumbledore's Army</a> in <a href="/wiki/1995" title="1995">1995</a>, at the <a href="/wiki/Hog%27s_Head_Inn" title="Hog&#39;s Head Inn">Hog's Head Inn</a>. Dean was an enthusiastic member of the D.A. and learned how to cast numerous defensive spells under Harry's skilful teaching, in the <a href="/wiki/Room_of_Requirement" title="Room of Requirement">Room of Requirement</a> where the group secretly held their classes.<sup id="cite_ref-OTP_11-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OTP-11">&#91;11&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Also that year, Dean accidentally insulted his new <a href="/wiki/Divination_(class)" title="Divination (class)">Divination</a> teacher, <a href="/wiki/Firenze" title="Firenze">Firenze</a>, after Umbridge had sacked <a href="/wiki/Sybill_Trelawney" title="Sybill Trelawney">Sybill Trelawney</a>. Firenze was telling Dean that he had been banished by his herd living in the <a href="/wiki/Forbidden_Forest" title="Forbidden Forest">Forbidden Forest</a>, and Dean was excited to discover there were more centaurs around the school grounds and asked him if Hagrid was breeding them. Firenze told Dean that centaurs were their own race, had little to do with humans and did not require their help to breed, which caused Dean to become extremely embarrassed.<sup id="cite_ref-OTP_11-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OTP-11">&#91;11&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>During his fifth year, Dean took his <a href="/wiki/Ordinary_Wizarding_Level" title="Ordinary Wizarding Level">O.W.L.</a> exams, and after he finished, he was pleased to hear that the results would not arrive until sometime in <a href="/wiki/July" title="July">July</a>, meaning he did not have to worry about them until the middle of the school holidays. Later in the year, Umbridge and her <a href="/wiki/Inquisitorial_Squad" title="Inquisitorial Squad">Inquisitorial Squad</a> would discover the D.A. and the meeting would end for the year. Some time around the end of his fifth year, Dean became romantically involved with <a href="/wiki/Ginevra_Weasley" title="Ginevra Weasley">Ginny Weasley</a><sup id="cite_ref-OTP_11-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OTP-11">&#91;11&#93;</a></sup> and the two continued dating well into his sixth year.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP_3-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>

<p>In his <a href="/wiki/1996%E2%80%931997_school_year" title="1996–1997 school year">sixth year</a> Dean replaced <a href="/wiki/Katie_Bell" title="Katie Bell">Katie Bell</a> as the <a href="/wiki/Chaser" title="Chaser">Chaser</a> for the <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor_Quidditch_team" title="Gryffindor Quidditch team">Gryffindor Quidditch team</a>. They dated in spite of her older <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">brother</a> Ron's disapproval. As a result, Dean became overly solicitous with her, trying not to provoke another argument, to keep their relationship going, which increasingly annoyed Ginny.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP_3-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>

He went on the run after the Ministry <a href="/wiki/Fall_of_the_Ministry_of_Magic" title="Fall of the Ministry of Magic">fell</a> under the control of <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a> and began rounding up Muggle-born wizards and witches. The Ministry also imprisoned them in <a href="/wiki/Azkaban" title="Azkaban">Azkaban</a> for <a href="/wiki/Muggle-Born_Registration_Commission" title="Muggle-Born Registration Commission">allegedly stealing magic</a>. This also meant he could not return to study his final year of education at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts</a>, as they now had a strict no-Muggle-born policy under the influence of new headmaster, <a href="/wiki/Severus_Snape" title="Severus Snape">Severus Snape</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-DH_12-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH-12">&#91;12&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<p>At some point whilst on the run Dean met <a href="/wiki/Edward_Tonks" title="Edward Tonks">Ted Tonks</a>, father of Order of the Phoenix member <a href="/wiki/Nymphadora_Tonks" title="Nymphadora Tonks">Nymphadora Tonks</a>, and travelled with him, later joining a group of fellow runaways — Muggle-born wizard <a href="/wiki/Dirk_Cresswell" title="Dirk Cresswell">Dirk Cresswell</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Goblin" title="Goblin">goblins</a> Griphook and <a href="/wiki/Gornuk" title="Gornuk">Gornuk</a>. The group stuck together and camped in different areas in an attempt to mislead any trails they may have left. At one point whilst on the run, Dean travelled alone with Griphook, most likely after the others were killed. Dean and Griphook often shared conversations about <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>, Dean fully supporting Harry in his quest and Griphook somewhat doubtful and occasionally finding the mentioning of Harry's name tedious. One evening they were overheard by Harry, Hermione, and Ron who were also in hiding; Dean expressed his worry at hearing that his former girlfriend, <a href="/wiki/Ginevra_Weasley" title="Ginevra Weasley">Ginny Weasley</a>, had gotten into trouble back at Hogwarts, as well as the belief that Harry was fighting Voldemort.<sup id="cite_ref-DH_12-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH-12">&#91;12&#93;</a></sup>

<p>Later, the group of fugitives were found by a group of <a href="/wiki/Snatchers" title="Snatchers">Snatchers</a> that was led by <a href="/wiki/Fenrir_Greyback" title="Fenrir Greyback">Fenrir Greyback</a>. Dean and Griphook were the only survivors of the violent capture. Around the same time, Harry Potter triggered the <a href="/wiki/Taboo" title="Taboo">Taboo curse</a> on Voldemort's name, alerting the Snatchers to his location. Held down in the basement of Malfoy Manor, the group had to listen helplessly as they overheard Hermione being tortured for information on the levels above.<sup id="cite_ref-DH_12-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH-12">&#91;12&#93;</a></sup>

<p>However, the group was soon rescued by <a href="/wiki/Dobby" title="Dobby">Dobby</a> the <a href="/wiki/House-elf" title="House-elf">house-elf</a>, who was fatally injured shortly after successfully transporting them to the home of <a href="/wiki/William_Weasley" title="William Weasley">Bill Weasley</a> and <a href="/wiki/Fleur_Delacour" title="Fleur Delacour">Fleur Delacour</a>, <a href="/wiki/Shell_Cottage" title="Shell Cottage">Shell Cottage</a>; Harry had had Dobby take Luna, Dean, and Mr Ollivander first before returning to collect the trio, with Bellatrix reacting with a well-timed knife throw to catch Dobby mid-warp on the second shift. <p>Dean was an open-minded and tolerant individual. When <a href="/wiki/Dolores_Umbridge" title="Dolores Umbridge">Dolores Umbridge</a> referred to <a href="/wiki/Remus_Lupin" title="Remus Lupin">Remus Lupin</a> as a "dangerous half-breed", he jumped to his former teacher's defence. On two occasions, he told her and <a href="/wiki/Severus_Snape" title="Severus Snape">Professor Snape</a> that Lupin was the best Defence Against the Dark Arts teacher that his class group ever had.<sup id="cite_ref-OTP_11-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OTP-11">&#91;11&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Dean was generally an easy-going and good-natured person, able to get along well with many people. He never displayed any anger towards <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> during their sixth year, even when his ex-girlfriend <a href="/wiki/Ginevra_Weasley" title="Ginevra Weasley">Ginny Weasley</a> started dating Harry shortly after he broke up with her. This was because he was aware of Ginny's childhood crush on the Boy Who Lived since she came to Hogwarts.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP_3-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> He also easily established a friendship with <a href="/wiki/Luna_Lovegood" title="Luna Lovegood">Luna Lovegood</a>, whom many would consider bizarre.<sup id="cite_ref-DH_12-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH-12">&#91;12&#93;</a></sup> 
</p><p>Dean was brave and loyal. He was eager to participate in the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">defence of his school</a> against <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a>, despite not even having a <a href="/wiki/Wand" title="Wand">wand</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-DH_12-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH-12">&#91;12&#93;</a></sup> and never lost his faith in Harry Potter. He believed Harry's claim that <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a> had returned<sup id="cite_ref-OTP_11-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OTP-11">&#91;11&#93;</a></sup> and had faith that Harry would eventually defeat the Dark Lord.
</p><p>Dean was apparently willing to violate school rules, if doing so would help a friend. In his third year, when Harry was not allowed to go to <a href="/wiki/Hogsmeade" title="Hogsmeade">Hogsmeade</a> because <a href="/wiki/Vernon_Dursley" title="Vernon Dursley">his uncle</a> had not signed his permission slip, Dean offered to forge the signature (unfortunately Harry could not accept, as he had already mentioned to <a href="/wiki/Minerva_McGonagall" title="Minerva McGonagall">Professor McGonagall</a> that his form was not signed).<sup id="cite_ref-POA_5-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-POA-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Dean was a skilled artist and also enjoyed reading the comic book <i><a href="/wiki/Loony_Nonby_v.s._Cornish_Pixie" title="Loony Nonby v.s. Cornish Pixie">Loony Nonby v.s. Cornish Pixie</a></i>, showing an interest in art.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Magical_abilities_and_skills">Magical abilities and skills</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Dean_Thomas?veaction=edit&amp;section=18" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Dean_Thomas?action=edit&amp;section=18" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li><b><a href="/wiki/Duelling" title="Duelling">Duelling</a></b>: Dean proved himself to be a highly proficient and fierce duellist as he and <a href="/wiki/Parvati_Patil" title="Parvati Patil">Parvati Patil</a> were able hold their own against <a href="/wiki/Antonin_Dolohov" title="Antonin Dolohov">Antonin Dolohov</a>, <a href="/wiki/Travers" title="Travers">Travers</a>, and a Masked <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eater</a> during the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-DH_12-10" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH-12">&#91;12&#93;</a></sup> Given the very strong duelling skills of these Death Eaters, this would have meant he was particularly gifted with <a href="/wiki/Martial_magic" title="Martial magic">martial magic</a>.</li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Defence_Against_the_Dark_Arts" title="Defence Against the Dark Arts">Defence Against the Dark Arts</a></b>: As a committed and loyal member of <a href="/wiki/Dumbledore%27s_Army" title="Dumbledore&#39;s Army">Dumbledore's Army</a>, it can be assumed that Dean was skilled in defensive magic, as he would have learned how to cast numerous offensive and defensive spells under <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>'s excellent teaching. He achieved a high <a href="/wiki/Ordinary_Wizarding_Level" title="Ordinary Wizarding Level">O.W.L.</a> score in the subject, seeing as he attended the <a href="/wiki/Nastily_Exhausting_Wizarding_Test" title="Nastily Exhausting Wizarding Test">N.E.W.T.</a>-level class during his <a href="/wiki/Sixth_year" title="Sixth year">sixth year</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP_3-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> He was also able to cast the <a href="/wiki/Boggart-Banishing_Spell" title="Boggart-Banishing Spell">Boggart-Banishing Spell</a> effectively, having turned a <a href="/wiki/Boggart" title="Boggart">Boggart</a> in the form of a <a href="/wiki/Loose_Hand" title="Loose Hand">Loose Hand</a> into being trapped in a mouse-trap in his <a href="/wiki/Third_year" title="Third year">third year</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-POA_5-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-POA-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Flight" title="Flight">Flying</a></b>: Dean was also a very skilful <a href="/wiki/Chaser" title="Chaser">Chaser</a>, as he was Harry's first choice to replace first <a href="/wiki/Katie_Bell" title="Katie Bell">Katie Bell</a> (who had been <a href="/wiki/Curse" title="Curse">cursed</a>) in the match against <a href="/wiki/Slytherin_Quidditch_team" title="Slytherin Quidditch team">Slytherin</a> for the <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor_Quidditch_team" title="Gryffindor Quidditch team">Gryffindor Quidditch team</a>, and then <a href="/wiki/Ginevra_Weasley" title="Ginevra Weasley">Ginny Weasley</a> (who was substituting as <a href="/wiki/Seeker" title="Seeker">Seeker</a>) in the final match against <a href="/wiki/Ravenclaw_Quidditch_team" title="Ravenclaw Quidditch team">Ravenclaw</a>, showing him to be a talented and confident <a href="/wiki/Quidditch" title="Quidditch">Quidditch</a> player and <a href="/wiki/Broomstick" title="Broomstick">broomstick</a> flyer.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP_3-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Transfiguration" title="Transfiguration">Transfiguration</a></b>: Dean was able to advance to N.E.W.T.-level <a href="/wiki/Transfiguration_(class)" title="Transfiguration (class)">Transfiguration</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP_3-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> He must have achieved either an '<a href="/wiki/Outstanding" title="Outstanding">Outstanding</a>' or an '<a href="/wiki/Exceeds_Expectations" title="Exceeds Expectations">Exceeds Expectations</a>' on his O.W.L. exam in this subject, showing his proficiency in this very difficult field of magic.</li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Charm" title="Charm">Charms</a></b>: Dean appeared to be talented in charm-work, as he was capable of conjuring a non-corporeal <a href="/wiki/Patronus_Charm" title="Patronus Charm">Patronus</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-LEGOY2_6-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-LEGOY2-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup> which is a mark of superior <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magical</a> ability and skill, given the immense difficulty of casting such an advanced and powerful protective charm. He also had no problem casting more simpler charms, such as the <a href="/wiki/Levitation_Charm" title="Levitation Charm">Levitation Charm</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTPG_16-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTPG-16">&#91;16&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li><b>Artist</b>: Dean appeared to be very artistic and was said to be "good with a quill". In his first year, he drew a "large <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor" title="Gryffindor">Gryffindor</a> <a href="/wiki/Lion" title="Lion">lion</a>" for the Gryffindor's first Quidditch match of the year, and on a "Potter for President" banner he had crafted with the help of <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione</a>, <a href="/wiki/Neville_Longbottom" title="Neville Longbottom">Neville</a>, <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ron</a> and <a href="/wiki/Seamus_Finnigan" title="Seamus Finnigan">Seamus</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-PS_4-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PS-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> In their <a href="/wiki/Fourth_year" title="Fourth year">fourth year</a>, after Harry completed the <a href="/wiki/Triwizard_Tournament" title="Triwizard Tournament">Triwizard Tournament</a>'s first task, he drew several banners in commemoration, depicting Harry evading the <a href="/wiki/Unidentified_female_Hungarian_Horntail" title="Unidentified female Hungarian Horntail">Hungarian Horntail</a>. Some showed <a href="/wiki/Cedric_Diggory" title="Cedric Diggory">Cedric Diggory</a> with his head on <a href="/wiki/Fire" title="Fire">fire</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-GOF_10-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF-10">&#91;10&#93;</a></sup> showing his talent in visual art.</li></ul>

<p>Little is known about Dean's relationship with his family. <a href="/wiki/Dean_Thomas%27s_father" title="Dean Thomas&#39;s father">His biological father</a> was a wizard who walked out on his Muggle wife and infant son in order to protect them from the Death Eaters. He never told his wife that he was a wizard, and was soon after killed by the Death Eaters when he refused to join their cause. <a href="/wiki/Dean_Thomas%27s_mother" title="Dean Thomas&#39;s mother">Dean's mother</a> married again <a href="/wiki/Dean_Thomas%27s_stepfather" title="Dean Thomas&#39;s stepfather">a Muggle</a>, who raised Dean as if he was his own son. Dean also had several <a href="/wiki/Dean_Thomas%27s_half-siblings" title="Dean Thomas&#39;s half-siblings">half-siblings</a>, through his stepfather.

<p>Dean grew close to Seamus Finnigan, one of his dorm-mates, during their first year at Hogwarts, and the two remained best friends throughout their school years. The two were often seen together at school, and Dean attended the <a href="/wiki/Quidditch_World_Cup" title="Quidditch World Cup">Quidditch World Cup</a> with Seamus and <a href="/wiki/Seamus_Finnigan%27s_mother" title="Seamus Finnigan&#39;s mother">his mother</a> in <a href="/wiki/1994" title="1994">1994</a>. Seamus initially believed the smear campaign the <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">Ministry of Magic</a> and the <i><a href="/wiki/Daily_Prophet" title="Daily Prophet">Daily Prophet</a></i> ran against <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> when he claimed that Voldemort had returned, but this did not appear to have affected his friendship with Dean, who believed Harry and who eventually managed to get Seamus to attend the final D.A. meeting of the year.<sup id="cite_ref-OTP_11-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OTP-11">&#91;11&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Seamus was also displeased when Dean was chosen as the replacement <a href="/wiki/Chaser" title="Chaser">Chaser</a> for the <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor_Quidditch_team" title="Gryffindor Quidditch team">Gryffindor Quidditch team</a> over him when <a href="/wiki/Katie_Bell" title="Katie Bell">Katie Bell</a> was injured, but again, the two still remained best friends.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP_3-10" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> Since Dean could not return to Hogwarts for his final year, the two did not see each other for some time, and they missed each other greatly. When Dean eventually did return to Hogwarts in <a href="/wiki/1998" title="1998">1998</a> to fight in the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">final battle</a>, Seamus was ecstatic to see him, and ran to hug him.<sup id="cite_ref-DH_12-13" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH-12">&#91;12&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<p>Dean started dating Ginny Weasley around the end of his fifth year.<sup id="cite_ref-OTP_11-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OTP-11">&#91;11&#93;</a></sup> Ginny's brother and Dean's roommate <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ron</a> disapproved of the romance, as demonstrated by his furious reaction to stumbling upon the couple kissing passionately in a deserted hallway. They appeared to be very close at first, but, according to Ginny's friend <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione Granger</a>, things began to get rocky as Ginny became displeased with Dean for what she perceived as over-protectiveness, as Dean constantly tried to assist her in everything. Ginny's enthusiasm about the relationship seemed to ebb by <a href="/wiki/Christmas" title="Christmas">Christmas</a> of 1996, as she did not seem terribly eager to see Dean upon her return to <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts</a>. The couple also began to row over little things, like Dean's constant attempts to help Ginny through the portrait hole into the Gryffindor common room, and they fought after Dean and Seamus laughed when Harry suffered a fractured skull courtesy of <a href="/wiki/Cormac_McLaggen" title="Cormac McLaggen">Cormac McLaggen</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP_3-11" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<p>Harry contributed to their ultimate break-up by bumping up against Ginny when she and Dean were going through the portrait hole while under his <a href="/wiki/Cloak_of_Invisibility" title="Cloak of Invisibility">Invisibility Cloak</a> and after having taken <a href="/wiki/Felix_Felicis" title="Felix Felicis">Felix Felicis</a>. Thus, luck was on the side of Harry, who had developed an interest in Ginny, and she thought Dean was trying to push her through the portrait. This row led to their split. <sup id="cite_ref-HBP_3-12" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Ginny clearly got over Dean soon after they broke up, as she began dating Harry soon after and admitted to him that she had never really given up on Harry. Dean, however, seemed astonished and hurt when he saw Harry and Ginny kissing, and went as far as smashing his glass, indicating that he took their split harder.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP_3-13" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> Later, while Dean was on the run and heard news about Ginny, Harry noted that Dean was concerned for her welfare.<sup id="cite_ref-DH_12-14" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH-12">&#91;12&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<p>Dean became a member of Dumbledore's Army in <a href="/wiki/1995" title="1995">1995</a>. He was friendly with most of the members.<sup id="cite_ref-OTP_11-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OTP-11">&#91;11&#93;</a></sup> He and <a href="/wiki/Luna_Lovegood" title="Luna Lovegood">Luna Lovegood</a>, in particular, developed a friendship in <a href="/wiki/1998" title="1998">1998</a>, as they were together during their imprisonment and <a href="/wiki/Skirmish_at_Malfoy_Manor" title="Skirmish at Malfoy Manor">rescue from Malfoy Manor</a> and later seemed to grow close while staying at <a href="/wiki/Shell_Cottage" title="Shell Cottage">Shell Cottage</a>. Dean was occasionally rather bemused at some of the odd things Luna said, but never ridiculed her, and was seen offering her his hand and helping her in the <a href="/wiki/Room_of_Requirement" title="Room of Requirement">Room of Requirement</a> just before the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-DH_12-15" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH-12">&#91;12&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Dean was also relatively close to his dorm-mates <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>, <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ron Weasley</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Neville_Longbottom" title="Neville Longbottom">Neville Longbottom</a>, though not to the same extent as with Seamus. Ron spent more time with Dean and Seamus during their <a href="/wiki/1994" title="1994">fourth year</a>, when he initially believed that Harry had cheated in order to get his name into the <a href="/wiki/Goblet_of_Fire" title="Goblet of Fire">Goblet of Fire</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-GOF_10-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF-10">&#91;10&#93;</a></sup> Ron very much disapproved when Dean started dating his <a href="/wiki/Ginevra_Weasley" title="Ginevra Weasley">younger sister</a>, though the two remained friendly.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP_3-14" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> He was also on friendly terms with <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione Granger</a>, another Gryffindor in his year.
</p><p>Dean made a banner for Harry during his first <a href="/wiki/Quidditch" title="Quidditch">Quidditch</a> match, and believed him about the return of <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a> in <a href="/wiki/1995" title="1995">1995</a>, unlike Seamus, eagerly joining Dumbledore's Army. Harry resented Dean slightly during their sixth year out of jealousy over Dean's romance with <a href="/wiki/Ginevra_Weasley" title="Ginevra Weasley">Ginny Weasley</a>, on whom he had developed a crush. When Dean and Ginny split up, Harry's enmity lifted, though Dean then became upset when Harry kissed his ex-girlfriend soon after their break-up.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP_3-15" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> In spite of this, Dean never seemed to carry any ill will towards them, as he was aware of Ginny's crush on Harry since her first year at Hogwarts. In fact, during his rescue from Malfoy Manor, he was reluctant to escape with <a href="/wiki/Dobby" title="Dobby">Dobby</a> and leave Harry behind.<sup id="cite_ref-DH_12-16" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH-12">&#91;12&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Dean was also good friends with both <a href="/wiki/Fred_and_George_Weasley" title="Fred and George Weasley">Fred and George Weasley</a>, older <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor" title="Gryffindor">Gryffindor</a> students. He helped them in their efforts to sabotage <a href="/wiki/Dolores_Umbridge" title="Dolores Umbridge">Dolores Umbridge</a> and helped them test <a href="/wiki/Skiving_Snackbox" title="Skiving Snackbox">Skiving Snackboxes</a> during his fifth year.<sup id="cite_ref-OTP_11-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OTP-11">&#91;11&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Other members of Dumbledore's Army were: <a href="/wiki/Lee_Jordan" title="Lee Jordan">Lee Jordan</a>, <a href="/wiki/Colin_and_Dennis_Creevey" title="Colin and Dennis Creevey">Colin and Dennis Creevey</a>, <a href="/wiki/Angelina_Johnson" title="Angelina Johnson">Angelina Johnson</a>, <a href="/wiki/Alicia_Spinnet" title="Alicia Spinnet">Alicia Spinnet</a>, <a href="/wiki/Katie_Bell" title="Katie Bell">Katie Bell</a>, <a href="/wiki/Lavender_Brown" title="Lavender Brown">Lavender Brown</a>, <a href="/wiki/Padma_and_Parvati_Patil" title="Padma and Parvati Patil">Parvati and Padma Patil</a>, <a href="/wiki/Ernest_Macmillan" title="Ernest Macmillan">Ernie Macmillan</a>, <a href="/wiki/Justin_Finch-Fletchley" title="Justin Finch-Fletchley">Justin Finch-Fletchley</a>, <a href="/wiki/Susan_Bones" title="Susan Bones">Susan Bones</a>, <a href="/wiki/Hannah_Abbott" title="Hannah Abbott">Hannah Abbott</a>, <a href="/wiki/Zacharias_Smith" title="Zacharias Smith">Zacharias Smith</a>, <a href="/wiki/Cho_Chang" title="Cho Chang">Cho Chang</a>, <a href="/wiki/Anthony_Goldstein" title="Anthony Goldstein">Anthony Goldstein</a>, <a href="/wiki/Michael_Corner" title="Michael Corner">Michael Corner</a>, <a href="/wiki/Terry_Boot" title="Terry Boot">Terry Boot</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Marietta_Edgecombe" title="Marietta Edgecombe">Marietta Edgecombe</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-OTP_11-10" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OTP-11">&#91;11&#93;</a></sup> Dean's relationship with the most of them is unknown.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Dean_Thomas?veaction=edit&amp;section=25" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Dean_Thomas?action=edit&amp;section=25" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Dean means "head" or "leader" and is often the title of a department chief at a college or university. Thomas means "twin".
</p>

<li>A drawing at one time posted on J.K. Rowling's website (and also mentioned on  <i><a href="/wiki/Pottermore" title="Pottermore">Pottermore</a></i>) revealed that Dean's first name was "Gary" in early drafts of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Philosopher%27s_Stone" title="Harry Potter and the Philosopher&#39;s Stone">Philosopher's Stone</a></i>.<sup id="cite_ref-17" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-17">&#91;17&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-pottermore_18-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-pottermore-18">&#91;18&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li>Rowling included information on Dean's background in an early draft of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Chamber_of_Secrets" title="Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets">Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets</a></i>, but ultimately cut it out, as she felt it was an "unnecessary digression." She stated that she had to a certain extent traded Dean's "voyage of discovery" for <a href="/wiki/Neville_Longbottom" title="Neville Longbottom">Neville Longbottom</a>'s because the latter character's discoveries about his past were more relevant to the main story.<sup id="cite_ref-rowling1_2-11" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-rowling1-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li>Rowling said that when it came time for casting the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Philosopher%27s_Stone_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Philosopher&#39;s Stone (film)">film adaptation</a> of <i>Philosopher's Stone,</i> she told director <a href="/wiki/Chris_Columbus" title="Chris Columbus">Chris Columbus</a> about Dean, and that she thought he was "slightly taken aback" by how much she had developed the background of such a minor character.<sup id="cite_ref-rowling1_2-12" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-rowling1-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li>In the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Order_of_the_Phoenix_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix (film)">film adaptation</a> of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Order_of_the_Phoenix" title="Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix">Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix</a></i>, Dean seemed to take <a href="/wiki/Lee_Jordan" title="Lee Jordan">Lee Jordan</a>'s place, such as helping Fred and George with their <a href="/wiki/Skiving_Snackbox" title="Skiving Snackbox">Skiving Snackbox</a> and saying Lee's line in the <a href="/wiki/Hog%27s_Head" class="mw-redirect" title="Hog&#39;s Head">Hog's Head</a>.</li>
<li>Dean is also the tallest out of all the boys in Harry's dorm.</li></ul>

<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Character_reduction">Character reduction</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Dean_Thomas?veaction=edit&amp;section=27" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Character reduction">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Dean_Thomas?action=edit&amp;section=27" title="Edit section: Character reduction"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<figure class="thumb tleft show-info-icon" style="width: 242px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/b/b1/Deanthomasoriginal.PNG/revision/latest?cb=20090811232538" class="image"><img alt="" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw%3D%3D" decoding="async" width="240" height="165" class="thumbimage lazyload" data-image-name="Deanthomasoriginal.PNG" data-image-key="Deanthomasoriginal.PNG" data-src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/b/b1/Deanthomasoriginal.PNG/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/240?cb=20090811232538" /></a> 	<noscript><a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/b/b1/Deanthomasoriginal.PNG/revision/latest?cb=20090811232538" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/b/b1/Deanthomasoriginal.PNG/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/240?cb=20090811232538" decoding="async" width="240" height="165" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="Deanthomasoriginal.PNG" data-image-key="Deanthomasoriginal.PNG" data-src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/b/b1/Deanthomasoriginal.PNG/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/240?cb=20090811232538" /></a></noscript> 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:Deanthomasoriginal.PNG" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption">Dean Thomas was originally a prominent character, and was better friends with the main trio. This is an early sketch drawn by JKR of how she originally wrote the scene "Midnight Duel" which originally included Dean Thomas, however in publishing he was cut</p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure>
<p>Dean Thomas was originally going to be a more prominent character in the novels, like <a href="/wiki/Neville_Longbottom" title="Neville Longbottom">Neville Longbottom</a>, <a href="/wiki/Ginevra_Weasley" title="Ginevra Weasley">Ginny Weasley</a> and <a href="/wiki/Luna_Lovegood" title="Luna Lovegood">Luna Lovegood</a>, but his role was initially cut down. Originally, Dean was called "Gary", and in <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Philosopher%27s_Stone" title="Harry Potter and the Philosopher&#39;s Stone">Harry Potter and the Philosopher's Stone</a></i>, Dean Thomas was going to be first introduced during the "Sorting Hat" chapter. His character was cut from the UK copies due to needing to shorten the chapter, however he still is in some US copies. He also was going to be featured in the chapter "Midnight Duel" going along with Harry, Ron, Hermione, and Neville to confront <a href="/wiki/Draco_Malfoy" title="Draco Malfoy">Draco Malfoy</a>, but he was cut out of this scene entirely. After having cut Dean out so much from the books, Jo did not want him to go unnoticed in the films and when the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Philosopher%27s_Stone_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Philosopher&#39;s Stone (film)">film adaptation</a> of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Philosopher%27s_Stone" title="Harry Potter and the Philosopher&#39;s Stone">Harry Potter and the Philosopher's Stone</a></i> was in production she blew away director <a href="/wiki/Chris_Columbus" title="Chris Columbus">Chris Columbus</a> with the amount of information she had on such a minor character. In <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Chamber_of_Secrets" title="Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets">Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets</a></i> J.K. Rowling had originally included Dean Thomas' background story, which explained about his "Muggle-born" blood status and his father being a wizard, but it was cut out again as it was "unnecessary content." Jo has said that she believes she swapped developing Dean Thomas to develop the character <a href="/wiki/Neville_Longbottom" title="Neville Longbottom">Neville Longbottom</a> instead, as he was more relevant to the overall plot. Dean later plays a somewhat larger role in <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Half-Blood_Prince" title="Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince">Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince</a></i>, where he becomes Ginny's boyfriend and a member of the Gryffindor Quidditch team, and <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Deathly_Hallows" title="Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows">Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows</a></i>, where he is on the run, kidnapped, and rescued.
</p>
