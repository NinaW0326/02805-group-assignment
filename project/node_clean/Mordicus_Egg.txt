<b>Professor Mordicus Egg</b> (fl. <a href="/wiki/1963" title="1963">1963</a>) was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> and an expert on <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggles</a>. He authored <i><a href="/wiki/The_Philosophy_of_the_Mundane:_Why_the_Muggles_Prefer_Not_to_Know" title="The Philosophy of the Mundane: Why the Muggles Prefer Not to Know">The Philosophy of the Mundane: Why the Muggles Prefer Not to Know</a></i><sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> and <i><a href="/wiki/Cooking_the_Muggle_Way" title="Cooking the Muggle Way">Cooking the Muggle Way</a></i>.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> He was at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> in the winter of <a href="/wiki/1993" title="1993">1993</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> and his name was carved into the wall of the <a href="/wiki/Divination_Classroom" title="Divination Classroom">Divination Classroom</a> in <a href="/wiki/North_Tower" title="North Tower">North Tower</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-HPLE_4-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HPLE-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>. He may also have been appointed Headmaster of Hogwarts at some unknown point in time.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Mordicus_Egg?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Mordicus_Egg?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>It is possible that he was the <a href="/wiki/Unidentified_male_Muggle_Studies_teacher" title="Unidentified male Muggle Studies teacher">male Muggle Studies teacher</a> replaced by <a href="/wiki/Charity_Burbage" title="Charity Burbage">Charity Burbage</a>, thus explaining both his knowledge of Muggles and his presence at Hogwarts. Another possibility is that he gained his title through his work at the <a href="/wiki/Institute_of_Muggle_Studies" title="Institute of Muggle Studies">Institute of Muggle Studies</a>.</li>
<li>The portrait of an older Mordicus Egg appears in the Headmaster's Office indicating that he served at Headmaster of Hogwarts at some point. Alternatively, this portrait may be of a possible relative that shares the same name as the author Mordicus Egg.</li></ul>
