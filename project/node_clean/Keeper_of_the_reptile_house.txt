The <b>keeper of the reptile house</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggle</a> who worked in the <a href="/wiki/London_Zoo_reptile_house" title="London Zoo reptile house">reptile house</a> of the <a href="/wiki/Surrey" title="Surrey">Surrey</a> <a href="/wiki/Zoo" title="Zoo">Zoo</a> in the summer of <a href="/wiki/1991" title="1991">1991</a>. When a <a href="/wiki/Boa_constrictor_at_the_Zoo" title="Boa constrictor at the Zoo">boa constrictor</a> escaped after the glass in front of its cage <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magically</a> <a href="/wiki/Vanishment" title="Vanishment">vanished</a>, the keeper watched in shock as it slithered away.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
