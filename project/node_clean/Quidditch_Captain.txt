<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Hogwarts_Quidditch_Captains">Hogwarts Quidditch Captains</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Quidditch_Captain?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Hogwarts Quidditch Captains">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Quidditch_Captain?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Hogwarts Quidditch Captains"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Quidditch Captains at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> are students and members of a <a href="/wiki/Quidditch_team#Hogwarts_Quidditch_teams" title="Quidditch team">House Quidditch team</a> appointed to be in charge of their House team. <a href="/wiki/Head_of_House" title="Head of House">Heads of House</a> appoint one member of their House Quidditch team to be the captain. The student would presumably continue to be captain until he or she graduates, barring severe injury or misconduct. Only one Quidditch Captain exists in a House at a time.
</p><p>Unlike <a href="/wiki/Prefect" title="Prefect">prefects</a> being firmly chosen from only the <a href="/wiki/Fifth_year" title="Fifth year">fifth year</a> students and above of each house, the exact requirement and restriction for choosing team captains is unknown, seeing as captains of the <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor_Quidditch_team" title="Gryffindor Quidditch team">Gryffindor Quidditch team</a>, <a href="/wiki/Oliver_Wood" title="Oliver Wood">Oliver Wood</a> was appointed in either his <a href="/wiki/Fourth_year" title="Fourth year">fourth year</a><sup id="cite_ref-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> or <a href="/wiki/Fifth_year" title="Fifth year">fifth year</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/Angelina_Johnson" title="Angelina Johnson">Angelina Johnson</a> in her <a href="/wiki/Seventh_year" title="Seventh year">seventh year</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> in his <a href="/wiki/Sixth_year" title="Sixth year">sixth year</a>, thus an age requirement might not have existed. It is however proven that Quidditch captaincy is not a simple matter of seniority on the team, as shown in the case of Harry Potter being made captain, while a member of his team, <a href="/wiki/Katie_Bell" title="Katie Bell">Katie Bell</a>, was a year older than him.
</p>

<p>When a Quidditch team member is appointed as Captain, they will retain their previous position (either <a href="/wiki/Beater" title="Beater">Beater</a>, <a href="/wiki/Chaser" title="Chaser">Chaser</a>, <a href="/wiki/Keeper" title="Keeper">Keeper</a> or <a href="/wiki/Seeker" title="Seeker">Seeker</a>) as well as assuming their additional responsibilities: Being required to hold Quidditch trials, plan training sessions and organise their House teams, and also responsible for almost anything regarding their House team activities and members.
</p><p>Captains are given status similarly equal to that of <a href="/wiki/Prefect" title="Prefect">prefects</a>, being given a silver <a href="/wiki/Captain_badge" title="Captain badge">badge</a> to wear on their <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_uniform" title="Hogwarts uniform">school uniform</a> or on their Quidditch robes during matches or practising, and are allowed to use the <a href="/wiki/Prefects%27_Bathroom" title="Prefects&#39; Bathroom">Prefects' Bathroom</a>. However, unlike prefects, captains are not allowed to dock <a href="/wiki/House_points" title="House points">House points</a>.
</p>
