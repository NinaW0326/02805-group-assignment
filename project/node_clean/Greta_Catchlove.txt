<b>Greta Catchlove</b> (b. <a href="/wiki/1960" title="1960">1960</a>) was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch</a>, who wrote the original edition of <i><a href="/wiki/Charm_Your_Own_Cheese" title="Charm Your Own Cheese">Charm Your Own Cheese</a></i>.<sup id="cite_ref-FWC_1-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-FWC-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> There was a portrait of Catchlove hung at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Castle" title="Hogwarts Castle">Hogwarts Castle</a>, guarding a secret shortcut between the second-floor landing of the <a href="/wiki/Grand_Staircase" title="Grand Staircase">Grand Staircase</a> and the <a href="/wiki/Entrance_Hall" title="Entrance Hall">Entrance Hall</a> side room. The password required to gain access to said shortcut was "<i>Cheese Charmer</i>".<sup id="cite_ref-POAG_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-POAG-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Greta_Catchlove?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Greta_Catchlove?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>An author named <a href="/wiki/Gerda_Curd" title="Gerda Curd">Gerda Curd</a> is credited with having produced a revised edition of <i>Charm Your Own Cheese</i>.<sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> It is possible that "Gerda Curd" is simply Catchlove's pen name.</li>
<li>If she attended <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts</a>, Catchlove might have been in the same year as the <a href="/wiki/Marauders" title="Marauders">Marauders</a>, <a href="/wiki/Lily_J._Potter" title="Lily J. Potter">Lily Evans</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Severus_Snape" title="Severus Snape">Severus Snape</a>. If she was not, she was in the year behind them.</li></ul>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Greta_Catchlove?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Greta_Catchlove?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>"Greta" is a short form of <i>Margareta</i>. A famous bearer of this name was Swedish actress Greta Garbo (1905-1990).
</p>
