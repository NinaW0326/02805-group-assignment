<b>Scarlett</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggle</a> friend of <a href="/wiki/Penny_Haywood" title="Penny Haywood">Penny Haywood</a>, who was killed by a <a href="/wiki/Werewolf" title="Werewolf">werewolf</a> in the summer of <a href="/wiki/1985" title="1985">1985</a>.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Scarlett?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Scarlett?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Scarlett was presumably born around the same time <a href="/wiki/Penny_Haywood" title="Penny Haywood">Penny Haywood</a> was born, in the late <a href="/wiki/1960s" title="1960s">1960s</a>&#160;or&#160;early <a href="/wiki/1970s" title="1970s">1970s</a>, and was raised in the <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggle</a> world. Every summer, Scarlett and her family would spend the holiday in the countryside, where they befriended Penny and her family.<sup id="cite_ref-HM_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HM-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>In the summer of <a href="/wiki/1985" title="1985">1985</a>, Scarlett and Penny snuck out of their house at night during the full-moon. They found a <a href="/wiki/Werewolf" title="Werewolf">werewolf</a> which fascinated Scarlett. Scarlett followed it while Penny followed them with her <a href="/wiki/Wand" title="Wand">wand</a> ready. Eventually, the werewolf noticed it was being followed and turned around. It killed Scarlett before Penny had time to react.<sup id="cite_ref-HM_1-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HM-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Penny later blamed herself for Scarlett's death and decided to brew a&#160;<a href="/wiki/Forgetfulness_Potion" title="Forgetfulness Potion">Forgetfulness Potion</a>. From then on, Penny's <a href="/wiki/Boggart" title="Boggart">Boggart</a> became the werewolf.<sup id="cite_ref-HM_1-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HM-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
