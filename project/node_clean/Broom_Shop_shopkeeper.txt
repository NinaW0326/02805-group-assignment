<p>This <b>individual</b> owned the <a href="/wiki/Broom_Shop" title="Broom Shop">Broom Shop</a> in <a href="/wiki/Diagon_Alley" title="Diagon Alley">Diagon Alley</a> by <a href="/wiki/1991" title="1991">1991</a>. By <a href="/wiki/August" title="August">August</a> <a href="/wiki/1992" title="1992">1992</a>, he'd been replaced as the shopkeeper by someone else. He was a blond man with blue eyes and a moustache, and often wore blue robes. He was a polite man, as he would let <a href="/wiki/First_year" title="First year">first year</a> students browse around his shop, even though he refused to sell them anything, as <a href="/wiki/Broomstick" title="Broomstick">broomsticks</a> are contraband for first years at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
