<p><b>Rosier</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Dark_Wizard" title="Dark Wizard">Dark Wizard</a> and one of the earliest <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a> of <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a>. He fought in the <a href="/wiki/First_Wizarding_War" title="First Wizarding War">First Wizarding War</a>, and travelled to <a href="/wiki/Hogsmeade" title="Hogsmeade">Hogsmeade</a> in order to "wish good luck" to Tom Riddle in trying to get a teacher position at Hogwarts.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP20_4-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP20-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> It is possible that Rosier was deceased before the start of the <a href="/wiki/Second_Wizarding_War" title="Second Wizarding War">Second Wizarding War</a>; if he was not, he possibly fought at the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a> and was presumably killed or imprisoned in <a href="/wiki/Azkaban" title="Azkaban">Azkaban</a> after Voldemort's final defeat.
</p>

<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_life">Early life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Rosier_(male)?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Early life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Rosier_(male)?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Early life"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Nothing is known of Rosier's early life.
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="First_Wizarding_War">First Wizarding War</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Rosier_(male)?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: First Wizarding War">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Rosier_(male)?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: First Wizarding War"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Rosier fought in the <a href="/wiki/First_Wizarding_War" title="First Wizarding War">First Wizarding War</a> along with many other Death Eaters. He was also one of the <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a> who travelled to <a href="/wiki/Hogsmeade" title="Hogsmeade">Hogsmeade</a>, to say "good luck"<i> </i>to Voldemort, during his attempt to ask for work at Hogwarts, along with <a href="/wiki/Nott" title="Nott">Nott</a>, <a href="/wiki/Mulciber_(Riddle-era)" class="mw-redirect" title="Mulciber (Riddle-era)">Mulciber</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Antonin_Dolohov" title="Antonin Dolohov">Dolohov</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP20_4-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP20-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> It is possible that he was the father of <a href="/wiki/Evan_Rosier" title="Evan Rosier">Evan Rosier</a>, who attended Hogwarts with <a href="/wiki/Severus_Snape" title="Severus Snape">Severus Snape</a> in the <a href="/wiki/1970s" title="1970s">1970s</a>, and who was killed by <a href="/wiki/Auror" title="Auror">Aurors</a> in the end of the <a href="/wiki/First_Wizarding_War" title="First Wizarding War">First Wizarding War</a>. After Voldemort's first downfall, Rosier was presumably killed, imprisoned, or defected. If he survived, it is most likely that he was one of the <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a> who did not search for Voldemort.
</p><p>It is unknown if Rosier was still alive during the <a href="/wiki/Second_Wizarding_War" title="Second Wizarding War">Second Wizarding War</a>.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Rosier_(male)?veaction=edit&amp;section=4" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Rosier_(male)?action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>It's possible that he is related to <a href="/wiki/Druella_Black" title="Druella Black">Druella Rosier</a>, wife of <a href="/wiki/Cygnus_Black_III" title="Cygnus Black III">Cygnus Black</a>, given the timeline it's likely that the two are brother and sister or possibly cousins.</li>
<li>He was possibly the father of <a href="/wiki/Evan_Rosier" title="Evan Rosier">Evan Rosier</a> or <a href="/wiki/Felix_Rosier" title="Felix Rosier">Felix Rosier</a>. The former was a known Death Eater while the latter was said to have a Death Eater father.<sup id="cite_ref-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li>Rosier is not mentioned in the "Guide to Lord Voldemort's Death Eaters" on  <i><a href="/wiki/Pottermore" title="Pottermore">Pottermore</a></i>.</li>
<li>It's possible that he was one of the boys seen with Tom Riddle in <a href="/wiki/Horace_Slughorn" title="Horace Slughorn">Horace Slughorn</a>'s memory, though it is unconfirmed.</li></ul>
