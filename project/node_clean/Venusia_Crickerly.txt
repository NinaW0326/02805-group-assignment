<p>A former <a href="/wiki/Auror" title="Auror">Auror</a>, Minister Crickerly was considered both competent and likeable. She died in office, however, following a <a href="/wiki/Mandrake" title="Mandrake">Mandrake</a>-related freak gardening accident.<sup id="cite_ref-WW_1-11" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-WW-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-HM36_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HM36-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
