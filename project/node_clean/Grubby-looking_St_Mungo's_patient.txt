This <b>grubby-looking warlock</b> came into <a href="/wiki/St_Mungo%27s_Hospital_for_Magical_Maladies_and_Injuries" title="St Mungo&#39;s Hospital for Magical Maladies and Injuries">St Mungo's</a> in <a href="/wiki/1995" title="1995">1995</a> because he clanged like a bell whenever he moved. With each of the clangs, he vibrated so much that he had to grab hold of his ears to keep his head steady. He was sat in the corner of the reception area when <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>, <a href="/wiki/Mad-Eye_Moody" class="mw-redirect" title="Mad-Eye Moody">Mad-Eye Moody</a>, <a href="/wiki/Nymphadora_Tonks" title="Nymphadora Tonks">Nymphadora Tonks</a> and the <a href="/wiki/Weasley_family" title="Weasley family">Weasley family</a> went to see <a href="/wiki/Arthur_Weasley" title="Arthur Weasley">Arthur Weasley</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
