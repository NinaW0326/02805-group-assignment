<p><b>Minister Pius Thicknesse</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Great_Britain" title="Great Britain">British</a> <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> and a <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">Ministry of Magic</a> official in the mid to late <a href="/wiki/1990s" title="1990s">1990s</a>. During the <a href="/wiki/Second_Wizarding_War" title="Second Wizarding War">Second Wizarding War</a>, he became Head of the <a href="/wiki/Department_of_Magical_Law_Enforcement" title="Department of Magical Law Enforcement">Department of Magical Law Enforcement</a> as well as, later in the War, <a href="/wiki/Minister_for_Magic" title="Minister for Magic">Minister for Magic</a> under <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort's</a> puppet <a href="/wiki/Fall_of_the_Ministry_of_Magic" title="Fall of the Ministry of Magic">regime</a> while under the <a href="/wiki/Imperius_Curse" title="Imperius Curse">Imperius Curse</a> and thus essentially Voldemort's puppet himself. He replaced <a href="/wiki/Rufus_Scrimgeour" title="Rufus Scrimgeour">Rufus Scrimgeour</a> as Minister of Magic, who had been murdered by Voldemort. Thicknesse took part in the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">final conflict of the War</a>, where he fought alongside the <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a>, still under the <a href="/wiki/Curse" title="Curse">curse</a>.
</p><p>He was ousted from power after <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Voldemort</a>'s defeat and was succeeded by <a href="/wiki/Kingsley_Shacklebolt" title="Kingsley Shacklebolt">Kingsley Shacklebolt</a> in his post of Minister for Magic.
</p>

<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_life">Early life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Pius_Thicknesse?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Early life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Pius_Thicknesse?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Early life"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Thicknesse was born to the likely <a href="/wiki/Pure-blood" title="Pure-blood">Pure-blood</a> or <a href="/wiki/Half-blood" title="Half-blood">Half-blood</a> Thicknesse family.<sup id="cite_ref-blood_2-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-blood-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> He most likely attended <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> in his youth, where he would have received his <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magical</a> education. He ultimately joined the <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">Ministry of Magic</a>, at some point in his adult life.<sup id="cite_ref-DH1_4-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH1-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span id="Head_of_the_Department_of_Magical_Law_Enforcement_(1996-1997)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="Head_of_the_Department_of_Magical_Law_Enforcement_.281996-1997.29">Head of the Department of Magical Law Enforcement <span class="template-c">(1996-1997)</span></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Pius_Thicknesse?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Head of the Department of Magical Law Enforcement (1996-1997)">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Pius_Thicknesse?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Head of the Department of Magical Law Enforcement (1996-1997)"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<div class="quote">
<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows">"<i>&#8203;</i>[…]<i> as Head of the Department of Magical Law Enforcement, Thicknesse has regular contact not only with the Minister himself, but also with the Heads of all the other Ministry departments.</i>"</span></dd>
<dd>&#8212;<a href="/wiki/Corban_Yaxley" title="Corban Yaxley">Yaxley</a>, concerning Thicknesse's post as Head of the <a href="/wiki/Department_of_Magical_Law_Enforcement" title="Department of Magical Law Enforcement">Department of Magical Law Enforcement</a><sup class="noprint"><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Deathly_Hallows" title="Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows">[src]</a></sup></dd>
<dd></dd></dl></div>
<p>When <a href="/wiki/Amelia_Bones" title="Amelia Bones">Amelia Bones</a>, Head of the Department of Magical Law Enforcement, was murdered by <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Voldemort</a> in the summer of <a href="/wiki/1996" title="1996">1996</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-HBP1_8-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP1-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup> Thicknesse was appointed her successor, presumably by then-<a href="/wiki/Minister_for_Magic" title="Minister for Magic">Minister for Magic</a> <a href="/wiki/Rufus_Scrimgeour" title="Rufus Scrimgeour">Rufus Scrimgeour</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-DH1_4-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH1-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>When one takes into consideration that the Department of Magical Law Enforcement is the largest Ministry department (to which the remaining six departments are all, in some respect, answerable with the possible exception of the <a href="/wiki/Department_of_Mysteries" title="Department of Mysteries">Department of Mysteries</a>),<sup id="cite_ref-FB_9-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-FB-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup> it seems to be safe to presume Thicknesse was high up at the Ministry at the time of his appointment in <a href="/wiki/1996" title="1996">1996</a>.
</p>
<h3><span id="Under_the_Imperius_Curse_(1997-1998)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="Under_the_Imperius_Curse_.281997-1998.29">Under the Imperius Curse <span class="template-c">(1997-1998)</span></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Pius_Thicknesse?veaction=edit&amp;section=4" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Under the Imperius Curse (1997-1998)">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Pius_Thicknesse?action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Under the Imperius Curse (1997-1998)"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<figure class="thumb tleft show-info-icon" style="width: 182px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/0/0a/ThicknesseMalfoyManor.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20180205164052" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/0/0a/ThicknesseMalfoyManor.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/180?cb=20180205164052" decoding="async" width="180" height="210" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="ThicknesseMalfoyManor.jpg" data-image-key="ThicknesseMalfoyManor.jpg" /></a> 	 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:ThicknesseMalfoyManor.jpg" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption">Thicknesse while Head of the <a href="/wiki/Department_of_Magical_Law_Enforcement" title="Department of Magical Law Enforcement">Department of Magical Law Enforcement</a></p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure>
<div class="quote">
<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows"><b>Lord Voldemort</b>: "<i>Well, Yaxley? </i>Will<i> the Ministry have fallen by next Saturday?</i>"</span></dd>
<dd><b>Yaxley</b>: "<i>My Lord, I have good news on that score. I have — with difficulty, and after great effort — succeeded in placing an Imperius Curse upon Pius Thicknesse.</i>"</dd>
<dd><b>Lord Voldemort</b>: "<i>It is a start. But Thicknesse is only one man. Scrimgeour must be surrounded by our people before I act. One failed attempt on the Minister's life will set me back a long way.</i>"</dd>
<dd>&#8212; <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a> and <a href="/wiki/Corban_Yaxley" title="Corban Yaxley">Yaxley</a> during the <a href="/wiki/Meeting_at_Malfoy_Manor" title="Meeting at Malfoy Manor">Meeting at Malfoy Manor</a><sup class="noprint"><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Deathly_Hallows" title="Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows">[src]</a></sup></dd></dl>

<p>In the summer of <a href="/wiki/1997" title="1997">1997</a>, as part of Voldemort's plan to take over the Ministry, <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eater</a> <a href="/wiki/Corban_Yaxley" title="Corban Yaxley">Yaxley</a> placed the Imperius Curse on Thicknesse. He mentioned that this was more difficult than he had expected and made him very nearly late for an important Death Eater meeting at <a href="/wiki/Malfoy_Manor" title="Malfoy Manor">Malfoy Manor</a> (as Thicknesse's nature allowed him to resist). Many of the other Death Eaters were impressed with Yaxley's work, <a href="/wiki/Antonin_Dolohov" title="Antonin Dolohov">Antonin Dolohov</a> clapping him on the back in congratulations, but Voldemort pointed out that Thicknesse was only one man, as well as that he needed Minister <a href="/wiki/Rufus_Scrimgeour" title="Rufus Scrimgeour">Rufus Scrimgeour</a> surrounded by his agents before he acted.<sup id="cite_ref-DH1_4-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH1-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Thicknesse proved useful, however. As head of the Department of Magical Law Enforcement, Thicknesse had regular contact not only with the Minister himself, but also with the Heads of all the other Ministry departments, making it easier to subjugate the others. Thicknesse proceeded to do just that, remaining undiscovered and using his position to infiltrate the senior ranks of the Ministry, slowly working together to bring Scrimgeour down.<sup id="cite_ref-DH1_4-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH1-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Thicknesse also aided Voldemort in trapping <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> at <a href="/wiki/4_Privet_Drive" title="4 Privet Drive">4 Privet Drive</a>, mainly by making it an imprisonable offence to connect the house to the <a href="/wiki/Floo_Network" title="Floo Network">Floo Network</a>, to place a <a href="/wiki/Portkey" title="Portkey">Portkey</a> there, or to <a href="/wiki/Apparition" title="Apparition">Apparate</a> in or out. Officially, all of this was done for Harry's protection, but in reality, it was pointless, as Harry was protected by a <a href="/wiki/Charm" title="Charm">charm</a> from <a href="/wiki/Lily_J._Potter" title="Lily J. Potter">his mother</a>'s blood while living with his <a href="/wiki/Dursley_family" title="Dursley family">relatives</a>. <a href="/wiki/Trace" title="Trace">The Trace</a> was also in effect, so if Harry or anyone around him cast a <a href="/wiki/Spell" title="Spell">spell</a> to get him out of there, Thicknesse would know about it and so would the Death Eaters.<sup id="cite_ref-DH4_10-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH4-10">&#91;10&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>The <a href="/wiki/Order_of_the_Phoenix" title="Order of the Phoenix">Order of the Phoenix</a>, as a result, believed Thicknesse had "gone over", which made it a big problem with the plan to move Harry. They abandoned the original plan, as they were unable to wait for the Trace to break: the moment Harry turned seventeen, all the protection he received from his mother would be lost. In short, Thicknesse thought he had Harry cornered good and proper, to which Harry could not help but agree.<sup id="cite_ref-DH4_10-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH4-10">&#91;10&#93;</a></sup> As such, the Order was reduced to using the only means of magical transport left to them: <a href="/wiki/Broomstick" title="Broomstick">broomsticks</a>, <a href="/wiki/Thestral" title="Thestral">Thestrals</a> and <a href="/wiki/Sirius_Black%27s_motorbike" class="mw-redirect" title="Sirius Black&#39;s motorbike">Sirius Black's motorbike</a>, setting the stage for the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_the_Seven_Potters" title="Battle of the Seven Potters">flight from Little Whinging</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-DH4_10-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH4-10">&#91;10&#93;</a></sup> and the murder of <a href="/wiki/Alastor_Moody" title="Alastor Moody">Alastor Moody</a> by Voldemort.<sup id="cite_ref-DH5_11-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH5-11">&#91;11&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<p>Eventually, Thicknesse and the other subverted high-ranking Ministry officials, launched a <a href="/wiki/Fall_of_the_Ministry_of_Magic" title="Fall of the Ministry of Magic">"smooth and virtually silent" coup</a> alongside the <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-DH11_3-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH11-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> and on <a href="/wiki/1_August" title="1 August">1 August</a>, <a href="/wiki/1997" title="1997">1997</a>, the Ministry fell.<sup id="cite_ref-DH8_12-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH8-12">&#91;12&#93;</a></sup> The <i><a href="/wiki/Daily_Prophet" title="Daily Prophet">Daily Prophet</a></i> was also taken over, the official version of Scrimgeour's murder was that he resigned, as well as Thicknesse replaced him. Only the Order seemed to be aware that he was under the Imperius Curse. As a result, Thicknesse was nothing but a puppet, taking care of everyday business while Voldemort, effectively the true Minister, was free to extend his power beyond the Ministry.<sup id="cite_ref-DH11_3-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH11-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Thicknesse, at Voldemort's direction, installed a new regime at the Ministry that was more in line with <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eater</a> ideology.<sup id="cite_ref-DH11_3-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH11-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> His actions included having a <a href="/wiki/Magic_is_Might_statue" title="Magic is Might statue">statue</a> depicting a witch and wizard on top of a throne made from the bodies of crude-looking <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggles</a>, inscribed with the motto "<i><a href="/wiki/Magic_is_Might" title="Magic is Might">Magic is Might</a></i>" set up in the <a href="/wiki/Atrium" title="Atrium">Ministry Atrium</a>, replacing the <a href="/wiki/Fountain_of_Magical_Brethren" title="Fountain of Magical Brethren">Fountain of Magical Brethren</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-DH12_13-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH12-13">&#91;13&#93;</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/Corban_Yaxley" title="Corban Yaxley">Yaxley</a> succeeded Thicknesse as head of the <a href="/wiki/Department_of_Magical_Law_Enforcement" title="Department of Magical Law Enforcement">Department of Magical Law Enforcement</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-DH12_13-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH12-13">&#91;13&#93;</a></sup> and several other Death Eaters, such as <a href="/wiki/Travers" title="Travers">Travers</a><sup id="cite_ref-DH13_5-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH13-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> and <a href="/wiki/Selwyn" title="Selwyn">Selwyn</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-DH21_14-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH21-14">&#91;14&#93;</a></sup> were appointed to other powerful positions within the Ministry.
</p><p>Meanwhile at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School</a>, the <a href="/wiki/Dark_Arts" title="Dark Arts">Dark Arts</a> and <a href="/wiki/Muggle_Studies#1997" title="Muggle Studies">prejudiced depictions of Muggles</a> were taught by professors <a href="/wiki/Amycus_Carrow" title="Amycus Carrow">Amycus</a> and <a href="/wiki/Alecto_Carrow" title="Alecto Carrow">Alecto Carrow</a> respectively under the new <a href="/wiki/Headmaster" title="Headmaster">Headmaster</a>, <a href="/wiki/Severus_Snape" title="Severus Snape">Severus Snape</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-DH12_13-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH12-13">&#91;13&#93;</a></sup> Hogwarts attendance became mandatory, as well as students must prove their blood-status, with Muggle-borns removed.<sup id="cite_ref-DH11_3-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH11-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> was declared "Undesirable No. 1", wanted for questioning about the death of <a href="/wiki/Albus_Dumbledore" title="Albus Dumbledore">Albus Dumbledore</a><sup id="cite_ref-DH13_5-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH13-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> with a ten-thousand-<a href="/wiki/Galleon" title="Galleon">Galleon</a> reward on his head<sup id="cite_ref-DH12_13-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH12-13">&#91;13&#93;</a></sup> (which was by <a href="/wiki/March" title="March">March</a>, <a href="/wiki/1998" title="1998">1998</a> increased to two-hundred-thousand when attempts to find him all over the country continued to fail).<sup id="cite_ref-DH23_15-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH23-15">&#91;15&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Using falsified research done in the <a href="/wiki/Department_of_Mysteries" title="Department of Mysteries">Department of Mysteries</a>, the Ministry claimed that <a href="/wiki/Muggle-born" title="Muggle-born">Muggle-borns</a> "stole" their magic from "real" witches and wizards. As a result, the <a href="/wiki/Muggle-Born_Registration_Commission" title="Muggle-Born Registration Commission">Muggle-Born Registration Commission</a> was set up to persecute and imprison Muggle-borns,<sup id="cite_ref-DH11_3-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH11-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> with <a href="/wiki/Dolores_Umbridge" title="Dolores Umbridge">Dolores Umbridge</a> as its head.<sup id="cite_ref-DH13_5-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH13-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> To round up any Muggle-borns or <a href="/wiki/Blood_traitor" title="Blood traitor">blood traitors</a> on the run, the <a href="/wiki/Snatchers" title="Snatchers">Snatchers</a> organisation was formed.<sup id="cite_ref-DH19_16-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH19-16">&#91;16&#93;</a></sup> Also people with known connections to the <a href="/wiki/Order_of_the_Phoenix" title="Order of the Phoenix">Order of the Phoenix</a> or sympathies for Muggles, such as <a href="/wiki/Arthur_Weasley" title="Arthur Weasley">Arthur Weasley</a>, were put under surveillance.<sup id="cite_ref-DH13_5-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH13-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<p>During the Battle of Hogwarts, Thicknesse was stationed within the ranks of the <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a> and fought with them against the castle's defenders. He was amongst a group of masked and hooded Death Eaters who successfully penetrated Hogwarts and duelled with <a href="/wiki/Percy_Weasley" title="Percy Weasley">Percy Weasley</a>. His hood slipped off as he tried to avoid being cursed and Percy mocked his former boss before using a <a href="/wiki/Sea_urchin_jinx" class="mw-redirect" title="Sea urchin jinx">sea urchin jinx</a>, causing Thicknesse to sprout sea urchin-like spikes all over his body. The corridor they were in was then damaged in an explosion that killed <a href="/wiki/Fred_Weasley" title="Fred Weasley">Fred Weasley</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-DH31_17-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH31-17">&#91;17&#93;</a></sup> Thicknesse and his comrades later retreated at Voldemort's command.<sup id="cite_ref-DH33_18-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH33-18">&#91;18&#93;</a></sup>

<p>It is uncertain if Thicknesse was killed during the battle, or just <a href="/wiki/Stunning_Spell" title="Stunning Spell">stunned</a>. The latter seems more likely, as <a href="/wiki/Arthur_Weasley" title="Arthur Weasley">Arthur Weasley</a>, like the rest of the <a href="/wiki/Order_of_the_Phoenix" title="Order of the Phoenix">Order of the Phoenix</a>, knew Thicknesse was under the influence of the <a href="/wiki/Imperius_Curse" title="Imperius Curse">Imperius Curse</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-DH4_10-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH4-10">&#91;10&#93;</a></sup> After <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Voldemort</a>'s death, all the people across the country who had been under the <a href="/wiki/Imperius_Curse" title="Imperius Curse">Imperius Curse</a> came back to themselves. If he survived the battle, this would include Thicknesse. Right after the battle, <a href="/wiki/Kingsley_Shacklebolt" title="Kingsley Shacklebolt">Kingsley Shacklebolt</a> was named temporary <a href="/wiki/Minister_for_Magic" title="Minister for Magic">Minister for Magic</a>, which later became a permanent appointment.<sup id="cite_ref-DH36_19-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH36-19">&#91;19&#93;</a></sup> If Thicknesse did survive the battle, it is unclear if he rejoined the Ministry or was incarcerated. The latter possibility is unlikely, as he was acting under the Imperius Curse and had no control over his actions. 
</p><p>In spite of effectively having served as the 34th British <a href="/wiki/Minister_for_Magic" title="Minister for Magic">Minister for Magic</a> from <a href="/wiki/1997" title="1997">1997</a> to <a href="/wiki/1998" title="1998">1998</a>, Thicknesse was omitted from most official records about the <a href="/wiki/Fall_of_the_Ministry_of_Magic" title="Fall of the Ministry of Magic">Death Eater regime</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-WW_20-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-WW-20">&#91;20&#93;</a></sup> as his unconsciousness eschews any responsibility for his actions at the time. 
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Physical_appearance">Physical appearance</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Pius_Thicknesse?veaction=edit&amp;section=9" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Physical appearance">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Pius_Thicknesse?action=edit&amp;section=9" title="Edit section: Physical appearance"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Thicknesse was described as having long black hair and a beard streaked with silver, as well as "a great overhanging forehead" that shadowed his glinting eyes.<sup id="cite_ref-DH13_5-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH13-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> thought he resembled a <a href="/wiki/Crab" title="Crab">crab</a> looking out from beneath a rock.<sup id="cite_ref-DH13_5-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH13-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Personality_and_traits">Personality and traits</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Pius_Thicknesse?veaction=edit&amp;section=10" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Personality and traits">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Pius_Thicknesse?action=edit&amp;section=10" title="Edit section: Personality and traits"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>

<p>It is unknown what Thicknesse was like before the Imperius Curse was placed upon him, or after it was removed when Voldemort was defeated. While under the curse however, his air of lofty authority mixed with his appearance made him generally intimidating and a little too happy with the way things were being run at the Ministry under him and the <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a> (most prominently <a href="/wiki/Corban_Yaxley" title="Corban Yaxley">Yaxley</a>), casually espousing the view that <a href="/wiki/Blood_traitor" title="Blood traitor">blood traitors</a> were as bad as <a href="/wiki/Mudblood" title="Mudblood">Mudbloods</a> to <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>, who was <a href="/wiki/Polyjuice_Potion" title="Polyjuice Potion">disguised</a> as <a href="/wiki/Albert_Runcorn" title="Albert Runcorn">Albert Runcorn</a> at the time.<sup id="cite_ref-DH13_5-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH13-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>It would be unfair to judge Thicknesse solely on his activities during his <a href="/wiki/1997%E2%80%931998_school_year" title="1997–1998 school year">tenure as Minister for Magic</a> however, as he was being controlled by the Death Eater <a href="/wiki/Corban_Yaxley" title="Corban Yaxley">Yaxley</a> at the time and was not responsible for his actions. However, since the Death Eaters had to resort to the Imperius Curse to get Thicknesse on their side, it is safe to say that he was not a sympathiser of their ideals. As Head of the <a href="/wiki/Department_of_Magical_Law_Enforcement" title="Department of Magical Law Enforcement">Department of Magical Law Enforcement</a>, the largest department in the Ministry, one can presume he was a fairly capable <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> and as a law enforcement official. It is implied that he was a strong-willed individual, as he tried very hard to resist the effects of the <a href="/wiki/Imperius_Curse" title="Imperius Curse">Imperius Curse</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-DH1_4-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH1-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<ul><li><b>Leadership skills</b>: Thicknesse obviously had the necessary skills of leadership when then-Minister for Magic <a href="/wiki/Rufus_Scrimgeour" title="Rufus Scrimgeour">Rufus Scrimgeour</a> appointed Thicknesse to replace <a href="/wiki/Amelia_Bones" title="Amelia Bones">Amelia Bones</a> as <a href="/wiki/Department_of_Magical_Law_Enforcement" title="Department of Magical Law Enforcement">Head of the Department of Magical Law Enforcement</a> during the <a href="/wiki/Second_Wizarding_War" title="Second Wizarding War">Second Wizarding War</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-DH1_4-10" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH1-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Charm" title="Charm">Charms</a></b>: Thicknesse was capable of conjuring a non-corporeal <a href="/wiki/Patronus_Charm" title="Patronus Charm">Patronus</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-LEGOY2_7-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-LEGOY2-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup> a highly advanced and powerful piece of <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magic</a>, as well as a mark of superior magical skill.</li></ul>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Pius_Thicknesse?veaction=edit&amp;section=14" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Pius_Thicknesse?action=edit&amp;section=14" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>The name <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Pius" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Pius">Pius</a> is derived from the Latin word <i>pius</i>, which despite being similar to the English word "pious", has a different shade of meaning: <i>that is, it refers to one who has reverence for law, government, as well as order.</i> Thus it is particularly appropriate for a head of the Department of Magical Law Enforcement and Minister of Magic. It has also been the regal name of twelve popes. Thus, the name of the man who created an organisation to oppress Muggle-borns may allude to the <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Inquisition" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Inquisition">Inquisition</a>, which persecuted heretics as well as, ironically, witches.</li>
<li>Thicknesse, meanwhile, may mean "thick" and the Proto-Germanic word <i>nessye</i> (or <i>nessieh</i>), meaning "tongue", probably alluding to the anti-Muggle-born propaganda passed during his tenure as Minister.</li>
<li>In Swedish, Thicknesse was renamed "Pius Korpulens", the word "<i>korpulens</i>" meaning "<i>girth</i>". In Serbian, Thicknesse was renamed "Pije Glupsije", the word "<i>glupsije</i>" being derived from the word "<i>glup</i>" which means "<i>dumb</i>". In Italian, Thicknesse was renamed "Pius O'tusoe", that sound like the word "<i>ottuso</i>", an Italian word for "<i>dumb</i>". These translations may have been inspired by the fact that "thick" is also British slang for "not too bright".</li></ul>

<ul><li>In <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Deathly_Hallows:_Part_2" title="Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 2">Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 2</a></i>, he is often seen standing quite close to Voldemort, suggesting that he is indeed a Death Eater of high rank. It is possible that he was given this status as a result of his role as a puppet-Minister.</li></ul></li></ul>

<ul><li>In the Harry Potter Pronunciation Guide at the Scholastic official website, Thicknesse's name is spelt phonetically as "PY-us THIK-nuss" (as in "<i>thick<b>ness</b></i>").<sup id="cite_ref-22" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-22">&#91;22&#93;</a></sup> However, Rowling pronounces it as "PY-us thik-NESS" (as in "<i>Loch <b>Ness</b></i>").</li>
<li>Pius Thicknesse has the distinction of being the Minister for Magic with the shortest term in office, out of the Ministers featured in the books: 274 days (9 months and 1 day).  <i><a href="/wiki/Pottermore" title="Pottermore">Pottermore</a></i>, however, established that <a href="/wiki/Basil_Flack" title="Basil Flack">Basil Flack</a> was in office for only two months in <a href="/wiki/1752" class="mw-redirect" title="1752">1752</a> before resigning due to a mismanaged <a href="/wiki/18th_century_goblin_rebellion" title="18th century goblin rebellion">goblin rebellion</a>.</li>
<li>In <i><a href="/wiki/LEGO_Harry_Potter:_Years_5-7" title="LEGO Harry Potter: Years 5-7">LEGO Harry Potter: Years 5-7</a></i>, Thicknesse does not appear in the main storyline at any point, but is an unlockable character. Somewhat inaccurately, Thicknesse is depicted as a <a href="/wiki/Dark_Wizard" title="Dark Wizard">Dark Wizard</a>, with the ability to cast the <a href="/wiki/Unforgivable_Curses" title="Unforgivable Curses">Unforgivable Curses</a> and manipulate <a href="/wiki/Curse" title="Curse">cursed</a> objects. His character can be found wandering around <a href="/wiki/Knockturn_Alley" title="Knockturn Alley">Knockturn Alley</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-LEGOY2_7-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-LEGOY2-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup></li></ul>
