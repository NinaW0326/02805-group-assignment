<b>Red</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Goblin" title="Goblin">goblin</a> bellboy who worked for the <a href="/wiki/Magical_Congress_of_the_United_States_of_America" title="Magical Congress of the United States of America">Magical Congress of the United States of America</a> during the <a href="/wiki/1920s" title="1920s">1920s</a>. He helped operate the lifts which transported passengers to any of the hundreds of floors within the <a href="/wiki/Woolworth_Building" title="Woolworth Building">MACUSA headquarters</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
