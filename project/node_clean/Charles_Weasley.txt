<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;Harry Potter and the Philosopher&#39;s Stone">"<i>How's <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">yer</a> brother Charlie? I liked him a lot — great with animals.</i>"</span></dd>
<dd>&#8212;<a href="/wiki/Rubeus_Hagrid" title="Rubeus Hagrid">Rubeus Hagrid</a>'s opinion of Charlie<sup class="noprint"><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Philosopher%27s_Stone" title="Harry Potter and the Philosopher&#39;s Stone">[src]</a></sup></dd>
<dd></dd></dl></div>
<p><b>Charles</b><sup id="cite_ref-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup> <b>"Charlie" Weasley</b> (b. <a href="/wiki/12_December" title="12 December">12 December</a><sup id="cite_ref-mugglenet_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-mugglenet-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>, <a href="/wiki/1972" title="1972">1972</a><sup id="cite_ref-year_2-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-year-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-HM-year_3-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HM-year-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>) was an <a href="/wiki/England" title="England">English</a> <a href="/wiki/Pure-blood" title="Pure-blood">pure-blood</a> <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a>, the second eldest son of <a href="/wiki/Arthur_Weasley" title="Arthur Weasley">Arthur</a> and <a href="/wiki/Molly_Weasley" title="Molly Weasley">Molly Weasley</a> <span class="template-c">(née Prewett)</span>, younger brother of <a href="/wiki/William_Weasley" title="William Weasley">Bill Weasley</a> and the elder brother of <a href="/wiki/Percy_Weasley" title="Percy Weasley">Percy</a>, <a href="/wiki/George_Weasley" title="George Weasley">George</a>, <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ron</a>, <a href="/wiki/Ginevra_Weasley" title="Ginevra Weasley">Ginny</a>, and the late <a href="/wiki/Fred_Weasley" title="Fred Weasley">Fred Weasley</a>. 
</p><p>He attended <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> from <a href="/wiki/1984" title="1984">1984</a>-<a href="/wiki/1991" title="1991">1991</a> and was sorted into <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor" title="Gryffindor">Gryffindor House</a>. He was both a <a href="/wiki/Prefect" title="Prefect">Prefect</a> and <a href="/wiki/Quidditch" title="Quidditch">Quidditch</a> <a href="/wiki/Quidditch_Captain#Hogwarts_Quidditch_Captains" title="Quidditch Captain">Captain</a> during his time at school, after which he went to study <a href="/wiki/Dragon" title="Dragon">dragons</a> in <a href="/wiki/Romania" title="Romania">Romania</a>.
</p><p>During the <a href="/wiki/Second_Wizarding_War" title="Second Wizarding War">Second Wizarding War</a>, Charlie was a member of the second <a href="/wiki/Order_of_the_Phoenix" title="Order of the Phoenix">Order of the Phoenix</a> and fought in the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a> in <a href="/wiki/1998" title="1998">1998</a>, arriving with reinforcements during the final portion of the battle. He was also present when <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> defeated <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a> once and for all.
</p><p>Charlie survived the Second Wizarding War and continued his work with <a href="/wiki/Dragon" title="Dragon">dragons</a>. He never married and had no children,<sup id="cite_ref-10" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-10">&#91;10&#93;</a></sup> but through his siblings and their respective marriages, he had many nieces and nephews.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Charles_Weasley?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Charles_Weasley?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span id="Early_life_(1972-1983)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_life_.281972-1983.29">Early life <span class="template-c">(1972-1983)</span></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Charles_Weasley?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Early life (1972-1983)">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Charles_Weasley?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Early life (1972-1983)"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<div class="quote">
<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets">"<i>My brother Charlie was always more of an outdoor type, so he went for Care of Magical Creatures...</i>"</span></dd>
<dd>&#8212;<a href="/wiki/Percy_Weasley" title="Percy Weasley">Percy Weasley</a> regarding his older brother<sup class="noprint"><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Chamber_of_Secrets" title="Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets">[src]</a></sup></dd>
<dd></dd></dl></div>
<p>Charles Weasley was the second of seven children born to <a href="/wiki/Arthur_Weasley" title="Arthur Weasley">Arthur</a> and <a href="/wiki/Molly_Weasley" title="Molly Weasley">Molly Weasley</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-COS4_11-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COS4-11">&#91;11&#93;</a></sup> He was a child during the height of the <a href="/wiki/First_Wizarding_War" title="First Wizarding War">First Wizarding War</a>. In the fighting, his maternal uncles, <a href="/wiki/Fabian_Prewett" title="Fabian Prewett">Fabian</a> and <a href="/wiki/Gideon_Prewett" title="Gideon Prewett">Gideon Prewett</a>, both members of the <a href="/wiki/Order_of_the_Phoenix" title="Order of the Phoenix">Order of the Phoenix</a>, were murdered fighting <a href="/wiki/Antonin_Dolohov" title="Antonin Dolohov">Antonin Dolohov</a> and four other <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP9_12-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP9-12">&#91;12&#93;</a></sup> While he was still a child, the wizarding world became peaceful again (for a time) after <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a>'s first defeat on <a href="/wiki/31_October" title="31 October">31 October</a>, <a href="/wiki/1981" title="1981">1981</a>.
</p>
<figure class="thumb tright show-info-icon" style="width: 237px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/5/59/The_Burrow.png/revision/latest?cb=20150828154152" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/5/59/The_Burrow.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/235?cb=20150828154152" decoding="async" width="235" height="158" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="The Burrow.png" data-image-key="The_Burrow.png" /></a> 	 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:The_Burrow.png" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption"><a href="/wiki/The_Burrow" title="The Burrow">The Burrow</a>, where Charlie grew up with his family</p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure>
<p>Charlie and his five brothers — Bill, Percy, Fred, George, and Ron — as well as his younger sister, Ginny,<sup id="cite_ref-PS6_13-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PS6-13">&#91;13&#93;</a></sup> grew up in the Burrow on the outskirts of <a href="/wiki/Ottery_St_Catchpole" title="Ottery St Catchpole">Ottery St Catchpole</a> in <a href="/wiki/Devon" title="Devon">Devon</a>, <a href="/wiki/England" title="England">England</a>. Like all his siblings, he was home educated in reading, writing and simple maths by his mother. The Weasley family was not wealthy compared to other wizarding families. Many other <a href="/wiki/Pure-blood" title="Pure-blood">pure-blood</a> families, particularly the <a href="/wiki/Malfoy_family" title="Malfoy family">Malfoys</a>, disdained them for their "<i>blood traitor</i>", pro-Muggle beliefs and their lack of wealth.<sup id="cite_ref-COS7_14-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COS7-14">&#91;14&#93;</a></sup> They tried their best to make up for this with their <a href="/wiki/Love" title="Love">love</a>. It can be assumed that Charlie had a fairly happy childhood, as all the Weasley siblings did.
</p><p>Charlie showed a keen interest in <a href="/wiki/Dragonologist" title="Dragonologist">Dragonology</a> from a young age. During a game of truth-or-dare with friends sometime during in <a href="/wiki/1989%E2%80%931990_school_year" title="1989–1990 school year">1989–1990</a>, <a href="/wiki/William_Weasley" title="William Weasley">Bill Weasley</a> revealed that, in his youth, Charlie used to try and turn the family pets into dragons. Since this was before he started at Hogwarts and knew any spells, he would settle on sticking paper wings on <a href="/wiki/Peter_Pettigrew#Scabbers" title="Peter Pettigrew">Scabbers</a> using <a href="/wiki/Spellotape" title="Spellotape">Spellotape</a> and on making Errol fly around with a dragon mask, and then following them around while jotting down his "observations of dragon behaviour".<sup id="cite_ref-HM6_15-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HM6-15">&#91;15&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span id="Hogwarts_years_(1984-1991)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="Hogwarts_years_.281984-1991.29">Hogwarts years <span class="template-c">(1984-1991)</span></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Charles_Weasley?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Hogwarts years (1984-1991)">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Charles_Weasley?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Hogwarts years (1984-1991)"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>On <a href="/wiki/1_September" title="1 September">1 September</a> <a href="/wiki/1984" title="1984">1984</a>, Charlie started his first term at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> and was sorted into <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor" title="Gryffindor">Gryffindor House</a> like the rest of his family, and was in the same year as <a href="/wiki/Hufflepuff" title="Hufflepuff">Hufflepuff</a> <a href="/wiki/Nymphadora_Tonks" title="Nymphadora Tonks">Nymphadora Tonks</a>. By the end of his second year, Charlie became the <a href="/wiki/Seeker" title="Seeker">Seeker</a> of the <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor_Quidditch_team" title="Gryffindor Quidditch team">Gryffindor Quidditch team</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-16" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-16">&#91;16&#93;</a></sup> He greatly excelled in the position.
</p>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Third_and_fourth_years">Third and fourth years</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Charles_Weasley?veaction=edit&amp;section=4" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Third and fourth years">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Charles_Weasley?action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Third and fourth years"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<p>In his <a href="/wiki/Third_year" title="Third year">third year</a>, Charlie elected to take the <a href="/wiki/Care_of_Magical_Creatures" title="Care of Magical Creatures">Care of Magical Creatures</a> class since he liked working outdoors.<sup id="cite_ref-COS14_17-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COS14-17">&#91;17&#93;</a></sup> It was around this time that he snuck into the <a href="/wiki/Forbidden_Forest" title="Forbidden Forest">Forbidden Forest</a>, having become convinced that he could catch a dragon in there. Well aware of the dangerous creatures within, Bill reluctantly accompanied him so his little brother would not have to face such dangers on his own.<sup id="cite_ref-y4ch4_18-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-y4ch4-18">&#91;18&#93;</a></sup> He would later admit to a <a href="/wiki/Jacob%27s_sibling" title="Jacob&#39;s sibling">classmate of his</a> how he had been sneaking into the Forbidden Forest more often than Bill or the teachers knew about. In the forest he had met and befriended a centaur by the name of <a href="/wiki/Torvus" title="Torvus">Torvus</a>, though he seldom talked about it because he did not want to brag or know where he sometimes went off to.
</p><p>In his <a href="/wiki/Fourth_year" title="Fourth year">fourth year</a>, Charlie was involved in Jacob's sibling's search for the <a href="/wiki/Forest_Vault" title="Forest Vault">Forest Vault</a>, and joined them many times in searches in the Forbidden Forest.<sup id="cite_ref-19" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-19">&#91;19&#93;</a></sup>. He entered the Forest Vault along with Jacob's sibling, <a href="/wiki/Rubeus_Hagrid" title="Rubeus Hagrid">Rubeus Hagrid</a>, Torvus and another student (either <a href="/wiki/Rowan_Khanna" title="Rowan Khanna">Rowan Khanna</a>, <a href="/wiki/Penny_Haywood" title="Penny Haywood">Penny Haywood</a> or Bill Weasley). In the vault, he was excited to see a dragon portrait.<sup id="cite_ref-20" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-20">&#91;20&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Fifth_year">Fifth year</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Charles_Weasley?veaction=edit&amp;section=5" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Fifth year">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Charles_Weasley?action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Fifth year"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>

<p>By his <a href="/wiki/Fifth_year" title="Fifth year">fifth year</a>, Charlie was appointed as a prefect.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP9_12-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP9-12">&#91;12&#93;</a></sup> Near the beginning of his fifth year, he joined a study session with his brothers Bill and Percy, as well as Jacob's sibling.<sup id="cite_ref-21" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-21">&#91;21&#93;</a></sup> That year, he and Jacob's sibling also sold items and worked to get money to buy a <a href="/wiki/Dragon_egg" title="Dragon egg">dragon egg</a> from the suspicious <a href="/wiki/Alistair_Fidgen" title="Alistair Fidgen">Alistair Fidgen</a>, although this egg later turned out to be an <a href="/wiki/Acromantula" title="Acromantula">Acromantula</a> egg. However, as Charlie got a piece of a dragon, he was nonetheless satisfied.<sup id="cite_ref-22" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-22">&#91;22&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Later that year, Charlie, Bill, Percy and Jacob's sibling asked <a href="/wiki/Nicholas_de_Mimsy-Porpington" title="Nicholas de Mimsy-Porpington">Nearly Headless Nick</a> questions about the Marauder's Map. Charlie got excited when Sir Nicholas mentioned that <a href="/wiki/Jacob_(Hogwarts_student)" title="Jacob (Hogwarts student)">Jacob</a> had believed there had been a dragon in the castle. Later, Charlie and Bill went to the <a href="/wiki/Prefects%27_Bathroom" title="Prefects&#39; Bathroom">Prefects' Bathroom</a> to look for <a href="/wiki/Duncan_Ashe" title="Duncan Ashe">Duncan Ashe</a>. Charlie was disappointed when Duncan left before he had a chance to ask about the dragon.<sup id="cite_ref-23" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-23">&#91;23&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Sixth_and_seventh_years">Sixth and seventh years</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Charles_Weasley?veaction=edit&amp;section=6" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Sixth and seventh years">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Charles_Weasley?action=edit&amp;section=6" title="Edit section: Sixth and seventh years"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<p>Charlie eventually became <a href="/wiki/Quidditch_Captain#Hogwarts_Quidditch_Captains" title="Quidditch Captain">Captain</a> of the Gryffindor Quidditch team<sup id="cite_ref-PS9_24-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PS9-24">&#91;24&#93;</a></sup> at some point in his fifth year or later.<sup id="cite_ref-25" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-25">&#91;25&#93;</a></sup> After he left Hogwarts, the Seeker Position for the Gryffindor Quidditch team was taken by <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> and the Quidditch Captain position was taken by <a href="/wiki/Keeper" title="Keeper">Keeper</a> <a href="/wiki/Oliver_Wood" title="Oliver Wood">Oliver Wood</a>.
</p><p>Early in his <a href="/wiki/Sixth_year" title="Sixth year">sixth year</a>, Charlie considered dropping out of Hogwarts due to what had happened in the Cursed Vault in the <a href="/wiki/1988%E2%80%931989_school_year" title="1988–1989 school year">previous school year</a>. However, Jacob's sibling convinced him to finish his education.<sup id="cite_ref-26" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-26">&#91;26&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Charlie took his <a href="/wiki/Apparition" title="Apparition">Apparition</a> test twice. He failed on the first attempt, materialising five miles south of his target location on top of an old <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggle</a> woman doing her shopping. He passed on the second attempt. His brothers George and Fred made fun of this, but not to his face.<sup id="cite_ref-GOF6_27-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF6-27">&#91;27&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Sometime during the year, Charlie became a founding member of the <a href="/wiki/Circle_of_Khanna" title="Circle of Khanna">Circle of Khanna</a>, a group devoted to avenging <a href="/wiki/Rowan_Khanna" title="Rowan Khanna">Rowan Khanna</a> and locating the final <a href="/wiki/Cursed_Vaults" title="Cursed Vaults">cursed vault</a>. It was at the groups first meeting in the <a href="/wiki/Hog%27s_Head_Inn" title="Hog&#39;s Head Inn">Hog's Head Inn</a> that he learned of the malevolent organisation "<a href="/wiki/R" title="R">R</a>" and it's <a href="/wiki/Plot_to_find_the_Cursed_Vaults" title="Plot to find the Cursed Vaults">motives</a> to locate the final Cursed Vault before them.<sup id="cite_ref-hm20_28-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-hm20-28">&#91;28&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Prior to his graduation, Charlie was given a Quidditch Trophy.<sup id="cite_ref-29" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-29">&#91;29&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span id="Studying_dragons_(1991-1995)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="Studying_dragons_.281991-1995.29">Studying dragons <span class="template-c">(1991-1995)</span></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Charles_Weasley?veaction=edit&amp;section=7" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Studying dragons (1991-1995)">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Charles_Weasley?action=edit&amp;section=7" title="Edit section: Studying dragons (1991-1995)"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<div class="quote">
<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire">"<i>Keep back there, Hagrid! They can shoot fire at a range of twenty feet, you know! I've seen this Horntail do forty!</i>"</span></dd>

<dd></dd></dl></div>
<p>After leaving school, Charlie went to <a href="/wiki/Romania" title="Romania">Romania</a> to study <a href="/wiki/Dragon" title="Dragon">dragons</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-COS4_11-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COS4-11">&#91;11&#93;</a></sup> At some point, he acquired a new wand, and his old wand was passed down to his youngest brother, <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ron</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-PS6_13-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PS6-13">&#91;13&#93;</a></sup> However, many of his peers believed that, had he chosen to, he could have played Quidditch professionally and would have been good enough to play for the English national side.<sup id="cite_ref-PS10_30-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PS10-30">&#91;30&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>At <a href="/wiki/Christmas" title="Christmas">Christmas</a> in <a href="/wiki/1991" title="1991">1991</a>, Charlie was visited by his parents and his sister, <a href="/wiki/Ginevra_Weasley" title="Ginevra Weasley">Ginny</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-PS12_31-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PS12-31">&#91;31&#93;</a></sup> During the early part of <a href="/wiki/1992" title="1992">1992</a>, Charlie received a letter from Ron, asking for help in getting rid of a baby dragon Rubeus Hagrid acquired off a man in a pub. The dragon, a <a href="/wiki/Norwegian_Ridgeback" title="Norwegian Ridgeback">Norwegian Ridgeback</a> named <a href="/wiki/Norberta" title="Norberta">Norbert</a>, was being illegally kept, but Ron — along with his friends <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> and <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione Granger</a> — did not want their friend to get into trouble. Charlie, who had always got on well with Hagrid because of their shared love of dragons, agreed to help and arranged for <a href="/wiki/Charles_Weasley%27s_colleagues" title="Charles Weasley&#39;s colleagues">some of his friends</a> to pick up Norbert and deliver him to the dragon sanctuary in Romania where he worked.<sup id="cite_ref-PS14_32-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PS14-32">&#91;32&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<p>In <a href="/wiki/1993" title="1993">1993</a>, Charlie joined the rest of his family for a holiday in <a href="/wiki/Egypt" title="Egypt">Egypt</a> after his father won seven hundred galleons in the annual <i><a href="/wiki/Daily_Prophet" title="Daily Prophet">Daily Prophet</a></i> Grand Prize Galleon Draw.<sup id="cite_ref-POA1_33-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-POA1-33">&#91;33&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>During the summer of <a href="/wiki/1994" title="1994">1994</a>, Charlie returned to <a href="/wiki/The_Burrow" title="The Burrow">the Burrow</a> to attend the <a href="/wiki/Quidditch_World_Cup" title="Quidditch World Cup">Quidditch World Cup</a> with the rest of his family. <h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Later_life">Later life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Charles_Weasley?veaction=edit&amp;section=12" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Later life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Charles_Weasley?action=edit&amp;section=12" title="Edit section: Later life"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Following the war, Charlie did not marry or have children, as he was more interested in studying dragons than in forming a romantic attachment. However, he had many nieces and nephews through all of his living siblings. 
</p><p>As of the summer of <a href="/wiki/2014" title="2014">2014</a>, he was still unmarried, which prompted the nosy journalist <a href="/wiki/Rita_Skeeter" title="Rita Skeeter">Rita Skeeter</a> to wonder what were the reasons behind his prolonged celibacy.<sup id="cite_ref-DP08jul14_46-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DP08jul14-46">&#91;46&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>In <a href="/wiki/2019" title="2019">2019</a>, <a href="/wiki/Magizoologist" title="Magizoologist">at least one</a> member of the <a href="/wiki/Statute_of_Secrecy_Task_Force" title="Statute of Secrecy Task Force">Statute of Secrecy Task Force</a> visited the <a href="/wiki/Romanian_Dragon_Sanctuary" title="Romanian Dragon Sanctuary">Romanian Dragon Sanctuary</a> to study dragons, where they ended up being grouped together with Charlie. While there, Charlie held a <a href="/wiki/Nowhere_to_Hide" title="Nowhere to Hide">course</a> that would allow them to spot weaknesses in dragon's hide, which would later help them <a href="/wiki/Calamity" title="Calamity">in the field</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-WU_1_47-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-WU_1-47">&#91;47&#93;</a></sup> along with aiding them in their study of the creatures to increase the amount of damage they can deal. In addition, he was also joined with said member in the grand opening of <a href="/wiki/Chinese_Dragon_Sanctuary" title="Chinese Dragon Sanctuary">Chinese Dragon Sanctuary</a>, where from witnessing the precision and power of a <a href="/wiki/Chinese_Fireball" title="Chinese Fireball">Chinese Fireball</a>, they increased their damaging power
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Physical_appearance">Physical appearance</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Charles_Weasley?veaction=edit&amp;section=13" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Physical appearance">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Charles_Weasley?action=edit&amp;section=13" title="Edit section: Physical appearance"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Charlie was short and stocky, and he had muscled arms. His face was weather beaten from working outdoors and was so freckled that he looked tanned. His fingers were usually covered in calluses and blisters, and, on one occasion during the summer of 1994, he had a large shiny burn on one of his arms.<sup id="cite_ref-GOF5_34-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF5-34">&#91;34&#93;</a></sup> Like all the <a href="/wiki/Weasley_family" title="Weasley family">Weasley family</a>, Charlie had red hair. It is possible he had long(ish) hair, as in 1997, on the day of Harry's seventeenth birthday, <a href="/wiki/Molly_Weasley" title="Molly Weasley">Molly</a> forcefully cut his hair "brutally short."<sup id="cite_ref-DH7_42-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH7-42">&#91;42&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Personality_and_traits">Personality and traits</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Charles_Weasley?veaction=edit&amp;section=14" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Personality and traits">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Charles_Weasley?action=edit&amp;section=14" title="Edit section: Personality and traits"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<div class="quote">
<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;Harry Potter: Hogwarts Mystery">"<i>Dragons are all I care about. Who has time for dating when there are so many dragons to study?</i>"</span></dd>

<dd></dd></dl></div>
<p>Charlie was described by his brother Percy as being "<i>always more of an outdoor type</i>." During his years at Hogwarts, Charlie liked animals and magical creatures; he later went to <a href="/wiki/Romania" title="Romania">Romania</a> in order to study about dragons. He also appeared to be friendly with his siblings, <a href="/wiki/Rubeus_Hagrid" title="Rubeus Hagrid">Rubeus Hagrid</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>, but he did not like the fact that his mother used to cut his hair short.<sup id="cite_ref-DH7_42-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH7-42">&#91;42&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Charlie was also brave, as he was sorted into <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor" title="Gryffindor">Gryffindor</a> house and fought in the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a> against Voldemort.<sup id="cite_ref-DH36_45-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH36-45">&#91;45&#93;</a></sup> After the war, Charlie did not marry or have his own family.<sup id="cite_ref-48" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-48">&#91;48&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Magical_abilities_and_skills">Magical abilities and skills</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Charles_Weasley?veaction=edit&amp;section=15" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Charles_Weasley?action=edit&amp;section=15" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li><b><a href="/wiki/Flight" title="Flight">Flying</a></b>: During his Hogwarts years, Charlie was the <a href="/wiki/Quidditch_Captain#Hogwarts_Quidditch_Captains" title="Quidditch Captain">Captain</a> as well as <a href="/wiki/Seeker" title="Seeker">Seeker</a> of the <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor_Quidditch_team" title="Gryffindor Quidditch team">Gryffindor Quidditch team</a>. He was an excellent <a href="/wiki/Quidditch" title="Quidditch">Quidditch</a> player, having helped Gryffindor win the Quidditch Cup many times. <a href="/wiki/Oliver_Wood" title="Oliver Wood">Oliver Wood</a> also claimed that Charlie could have played for <a href="/wiki/England" title="England">England</a>'s national team if he wanted to, showing him to have been a highly talent and accomplished <a href="/wiki/Broomstick" title="Broomstick">broomstick</a> flyer.<sup id="cite_ref-PS10_30-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PS10-30">&#91;30&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Care_of_Magical_Creatures" title="Care of Magical Creatures">Care of Magical Creatures</a></b>: Charlie was revealed to have taken the subject of Care of Magical Creatures during his Hogwarts years. It appeared that he was particularly talented in the subject, for even <a href="/wiki/Rubeus_Hagrid" title="Rubeus Hagrid">Rubeus Hagrid</a> (who himself had remarkable skills in handling magical creatures) commented that Charlie was <i>"great with animals"</i>. After graduating from Hogwarts, Charlie went to <a href="/wiki/Romania" title="Romania">Romania</a>, studying <a href="/wiki/Dragon" title="Dragon">dragons</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-PS8_7-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PS8-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup> which is proof of his talent in <a href="/wiki/Magizoology" title="Magizoology">Magizoology</a>, given how dragons are incredibly powerful and dangerous <a href="/wiki/Beast" title="Beast">beasts</a> to handle. He was skilled enough with dragon handling, that he helped to handle them during the <a href="/wiki/Triwizard_Tournament" title="Triwizard Tournament">Triwizard Tournament</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-GOF19_37-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF19-37">&#91;37&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-GOF20_38-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF20-38">&#91;38&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Duelling" title="Duelling">Duelling</a></b>: Charlie was shown to have considerable skill as a duellist, given how he participated in and survived the final fight in the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a>, showing him to have had much proficiency and skill in <a href="/wiki/Martial_magic" title="Martial magic">martial magic</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-DH36_45-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH36-45">&#91;45&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Apparition" title="Apparition">Apparition</a></b>: Like most adult wizards, Charlie could apparate at will. He learned how to apparate successfully after passing his Apparition test for the second time.<sup id="cite_ref-GOF6_27-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF6-27">&#91;27&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Charm" title="Charm">Charms</a></b>: Charlie was competent with the casting of charms, such as with the <a href="/wiki/Levitation_Charm" title="Levitation Charm">Levitation Charm</a> and <a href="/wiki/Exploding_Charm" title="Exploding Charm">Exploding Charm</a>. He was also able to produce a non-corporeal <a href="/wiki/Patronus_Charm" title="Patronus Charm">Patronus</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-LEGOY2_6-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-LEGOY2-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup> a mark of superior <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magical</a> ability given the immense difficulty of casting the spell.</li>
<li><b>Tracking skills</b>: Charlie had a talent for tracking the movements of magical creatures. At some point during his student years, he 'got it in his head' that he would be able to catch a <a href="/wiki/Dragon" title="Dragon">dragon</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Forbidden_Forest" title="Forbidden Forest">Forbidden Forest</a>. Bill attributed the fact that they neither starved or were attacked by any of the dangerous creatures dwelling in the woods due to Charlie's tracking skills.<sup id="cite_ref-y4ch4_18-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-y4ch4-18">&#91;18&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li><b>Social networking</b>: Charlie had very strong connections with foreign wizards and <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magical</a> <a href="/wiki/Creature" title="Creature">creatures</a>, along with the ones he had with wizards and magical creatures in <a href="/wiki/Great_Britain" title="Great Britain">Britain</a>. This was first evident when Charlie had some of his friends bring <a href="/wiki/Norberta" title="Norberta">Norbert</a> to him from <a href="/wiki/Rubeus_Hagrid" title="Rubeus Hagrid">Hagrid</a>, <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry</a>, <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ron</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione</a>, despite the risks involved with it.<sup id="cite_ref-PS14_32-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PS14-32">&#91;32&#93;</a></sup> Due to these skills, <a href="/wiki/Albus_Dumbledore" title="Albus Dumbledore">Dumbledore</a> gave Charlie the task of recruiting foreign wizards into the <a href="/wiki/Order_of_the_Phoenix" title="Order of the Phoenix">Order of the Phoenix</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP4_40-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP4-40">&#91;40&#93;</a></sup> Charlie's social networking skills later allowed him to gather reinforcements to fight against <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Voldemort</a>'s forces, an act which would later prove to be vital for Voldemort's <a href="/wiki/Voldemort%27s_Last_Stand" title="Voldemort&#39;s Last Stand">final defeat</a> and ending the <a href="/wiki/Second_Wizarding_War" title="Second Wizarding War">war</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-DH36_45-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH36-45">&#91;45&#93;</a></sup></li></ul>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Possessions">Possessions</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Charles_Weasley?veaction=edit&amp;section=16" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Possessions">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Charles_Weasley?action=edit&amp;section=16" title="Edit section: Possessions"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li><b><a href="/wiki/Charles_Weasley%27s_wand" title="Charles Weasley&#39;s wand">First wand</a></b>: Charlie owned an Ash wand with a unicorn hair core. His younger brother Ron used this wand from 1991 to 1993.</li>
<li><b>Messenger bag</b>: Charlie owned a messenger bag which he used at school. The messenger bag had a silver dragon head on the strap.</li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Prefect_badge" title="Prefect badge">Prefect badge</a></b>: Having been made prefect in his <a href="/wiki/Fifth_year" title="Fifth year">fifth year</a> at Hogwarts, Charlie owned a Gryffindor prefect badge, which he wore during his formal prefect duties.</li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Hebridean_Black" title="Hebridean Black">Hebridean Black</a> scale</b>: At some point before their <a href="/wiki/Sixth_year" title="Sixth year">sixth year</a>, Jacob's sibling gave him a Hebridean Black scale.</li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Crup" title="Crup">Cruppy</a></b>: Charlie owned and raised a Cruppy he named Dragon.</li>
<li><b>Second wand</b>: Charlie owned another wand from at least 1991.</li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Fireproof_balaclava" title="Fireproof balaclava">Fireproof balaclava</a></b>: Charlie had a fireproof balaclava which he used at work.</li></ul>

<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Weasley_family">Weasley family</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Charles_Weasley?veaction=edit&amp;section=19" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Weasley family">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Charles_Weasley?action=edit&amp;section=19" title="Edit section: Weasley family"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>

<p>Charlie had a good relationship with his family. <a href="/wiki/Arthur_Weasley" title="Arthur Weasley">Arthur</a> and <a href="/wiki/Molly_Weasley" title="Molly Weasley">Molly Weasley</a> both were <a href="/wiki/Pure-blood" title="Pure-blood">pure-blood</a> wizards who loved their children very much and cared about them. However, they were economically poor and were considered by many as "blood-traitors," because they were not prejudiced against <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggles</a> and <a href="/wiki/Muggle-born" title="Muggle-born">Muggle-borns</a>. Charlie's childhood might have been happy and friendly with his parents.
</p><p>He also had a good relationship with his siblings. He appeared to be closest with <a href="/wiki/William_Weasley" title="William Weasley">Bill</a>, because they were close in age. Bill was a good student, like Charlie; after <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts</a>, Bill worked for <a href="/wiki/Gringotts_Wizarding_Bank" title="Gringotts Wizarding Bank">Gringotts Wizarding Bank</a> in <a href="/wiki/Egypt" title="Egypt">Egypt</a>, and later married <a href="/wiki/Fleur_Delacour" title="Fleur Delacour">Fleur Delacour</a>. Charlie was the best man at <a href="/wiki/Wedding_of_William_Weasley_and_Fleur_Delacour" title="Wedding of William Weasley and Fleur Delacour">their wedding</a>. It is possible that, after the war, they were as close as they were during their early years.
</p><p>Charlie appeared to have a good relationship with his younger siblings, too. <a href="/wiki/Percy_Weasley" title="Percy Weasley">Percy</a> believed that Charlie was "more of an outdoor type"; the reason that he liked studying dragons. Percy seemed to be proud that his brother was a good student, but they were not on good terms during the <a href="/wiki/Second_Wizarding_War" title="Second Wizarding War">Second Wizarding War</a>; Percy believed that his family was wrong for supporting Dumbledore. After the war, Percy changed his mind and his relationship with his siblings became better.
</p><p><a href="/wiki/Fred_and_George_Weasley" title="Fred and George Weasley">Fred and George</a>, <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ron</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Ginevra_Weasley" title="Ginevra Weasley">Ginny</a> seemed to like Charlie; they tended to look up to Charlie as much as Bill. During their early years, Charlie might have been close with them, but he left the country, in order to study dragons. Charlie met them again in <a href="/wiki/1994" title="1994">1994</a> and in <a href="/wiki/1997" title="1997">1997</a>. They also fought together at the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a> and survived the war; except for Fred, who was killed in an explosion. Although Charlie was not present when Fred was killed during the first half of the battle, it is very likely that Charlie was devastated by his death, like the other members of the family.
</p>

<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Nieces_and_nephews">Nieces and nephews</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Charles_Weasley?veaction=edit&amp;section=21" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Nieces and nephews">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Charles_Weasley?action=edit&amp;section=21" title="Edit section: Nieces and nephews"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<p>After the <a href="/wiki/Second_Wizarding_War" title="Second Wizarding War">Second Wizarding War</a>, Charlie had many nieces and nephews, through the <a href="/wiki/Weasley_family" title="Weasley family">Weasley family</a>; Bill and Fleur's children: <a href="/wiki/Victoire_Weasley" title="Victoire Weasley">Victoire</a>, <a href="/wiki/Dominique_Weasley" title="Dominique Weasley">Dominique,</a> and <a href="/wiki/Louis_Weasley" title="Louis Weasley">Louis</a>; Percy and Audrey's children: <a href="/wiki/Molly_Weasley_II" title="Molly Weasley II">Molly</a> and <a href="/wiki/Lucy_Weasley" title="Lucy Weasley">Lucy</a>; George and Angelina's children: <a href="/wiki/Fred_Weasley_II" title="Fred Weasley II">Fred</a> and <a href="/wiki/Roxanne_Weasley" title="Roxanne Weasley">Roxanne</a>; Ron and Hermione's children: <a href="/wiki/Rose_Granger-Weasley" title="Rose Granger-Weasley">Rose</a> and <a href="/wiki/Hugo_Granger-Weasley" title="Hugo Granger-Weasley">Hugo</a>; and Ginny and Harry's children: <a href="/wiki/James_Potter_II" title="James Potter II">James</a>, <a href="/wiki/Albus_Potter" title="Albus Potter">Albus</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Lily_Potter" title="Lily Potter">Lily</a>. It is very likely that Charlie had a good relationship with all of them.
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Rubeus_Hagrid">Rubeus Hagrid</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Charles_Weasley?veaction=edit&amp;section=22" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Rubeus Hagrid">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Charles_Weasley?action=edit&amp;section=22" title="Edit section: Rubeus Hagrid"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<figure class="thumb tright show-info-icon" style="width: 177px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/c/cd/Rubeus_Hagrid.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20160121163026" class="image"><img alt="" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw%3D%3D" decoding="async" width="175" height="240" class="thumbimage lazyload" data-image-name="Rubeus Hagrid.jpg" data-image-key="Rubeus_Hagrid.jpg" data-src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/c/cd/Rubeus_Hagrid.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/175?cb=20160121163026" /></a> 	<noscript><a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/c/cd/Rubeus_Hagrid.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20160121163026" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/c/cd/Rubeus_Hagrid.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/175?cb=20160121163026" decoding="async" width="175" height="240" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="Rubeus Hagrid.jpg" data-image-key="Rubeus_Hagrid.jpg" data-src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/c/cd/Rubeus_Hagrid.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/175?cb=20160121163026" /></a></noscript> 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:Rubeus_Hagrid.jpg" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption"><a href="/wiki/Rubeus_Hagrid" title="Rubeus Hagrid">Rubeus Hagrid</a>, his friend</p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure>
<p>Charlie was a friend of Rubeus Hagrid. Rubeus Hagrid was the <a href="/wiki/Keeper_of_Keys_and_Grounds" title="Keeper of Keys and Grounds">gamekeeper</a> and later <a href="/wiki/Care_of_Magical_Creatures" title="Care of Magical Creatures">Care of Magical Creatures</a> professor at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a>. He described Charlie as being great with animals and stated that he had missed Charlie, during the time he studied dragons. They both liked magical creatures, and they met each other several times after Charlie left Hogwarts. Charlie invited Hagrid to watch some dragons he and his friends were breeding, during the <a href="/wiki/Triwizard_Tournament" title="Triwizard Tournament">Triwizard Tournament</a>. 
</p><p>In <a href="/wiki/1997" title="1997">1997</a>, Charlie stayed at <a href="/wiki/The_Burrow" title="The Burrow">the Burrow</a>; both he and Hagrid attended <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter%27s_seventeenth_birthday_party" class="mw-redirect" title="Harry Potter&#39;s seventeenth birthday party">Harry Potter's seventeenth birthday party</a> and Bill and Fleur's wedding. Charlie, Hagrid, and another wizard sang along a song about <a href="/wiki/Odo" title="Odo">Odo</a> the hero. They also fought together at the final battle of the <a href="/wiki/Second_Wizarding_War" title="Second Wizarding War">Second Wizarding War</a> and survived the war.
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Order_of_the_Phoenix">Order of the Phoenix</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Charles_Weasley?veaction=edit&amp;section=23" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Order of the Phoenix">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Charles_Weasley?action=edit&amp;section=23" title="Edit section: Order of the Phoenix"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<figure class="thumb tleft show-info-icon" style="width: 252px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/5/58/Order_of_the_Phoenix.png/revision/latest?cb=20140511035621" class="image"><img alt="" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw%3D%3D" decoding="async" width="250" height="105" class="thumbimage lazyload" data-image-name="Order of the Phoenix.png" data-image-key="Order_of_the_Phoenix.png" data-src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/5/58/Order_of_the_Phoenix.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/250?cb=20140511035621" /></a> 	<noscript><a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/5/58/Order_of_the_Phoenix.png/revision/latest?cb=20140511035621" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/5/58/Order_of_the_Phoenix.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/250?cb=20140511035621" decoding="async" width="250" height="105" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="Order of the Phoenix.png" data-image-key="Order_of_the_Phoenix.png" data-src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/5/58/Order_of_the_Phoenix.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/250?cb=20140511035621" /></a></noscript> 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:Order_of_the_Phoenix.png" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption">The original <a href="/wiki/Order_of_the_Phoenix" title="Order of the Phoenix">Order of the Phoenix</a></p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure>
<p>Charlie became a member of the Order of the Phoenix during the <a href="/wiki/Second_Wizarding_War" title="Second Wizarding War">Second Wizarding War</a>. However, it is possible that he had not met all the members of the order, as he lived in <a href="/wiki/Romania" title="Romania">Romania</a>, leading reinforcements for the order. It is possible that he knew some members, such as <a href="/wiki/Remus_Lupin" title="Remus Lupin">Remus Lupin</a>, <a href="/wiki/Alastor_Moody" title="Alastor Moody">Alastor Moody</a>, <a href="/wiki/Nymphadora_Tonks" title="Nymphadora Tonks">Nymphadora Tonks</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Kingsley_Shacklebolt" title="Kingsley Shacklebolt">Kingsley Shacklebolt</a>, as Lupin and Tonks attended Bill and Fleur's wedding with Charlie, and Charlie's father always spoke highly of Moody. Charlie had also been in the same year as Tonks at Hogwarts, and may have known her prior to joining the Order.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Charles_Weasley?veaction=edit&amp;section=24" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Charles_Weasley?action=edit&amp;section=24" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The name "Charles" comes from the Germanic name Karl, which was derived from a Germanic word which meant "man". However, an alternative theory states that it is derived from the common Germanic element hari meaning "army, warrior".
</p>

<ul><li>In <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Deathly_Hallows:_Part_1" title="Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 1">Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 1</a></i>, a young man with red hair is seen standing beside <a href="/wiki/Nymphadora_Tonks" title="Nymphadora Tonks">Nymphadora Tonks</a> at the wedding. It is likely that he is Charlie, due to his haircut (similar to the one described in the book), the fact that he is the same age as Tonks and also Tonks' alleged friendship with Charlie.</li>
<li>In <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Philosopher%27s_Stone" title="Harry Potter and the Philosopher&#39;s Stone">Harry Potter and the Philosopher's Stone</a></i>, Gryffindors reminisce about his era as a distant golden age, since which the Gryffindor team has known years of frustration, yet from what <a href="/wiki/J._K._Rowling" title="J. K. Rowling">J. K. Rowling</a> tells us of his age, he would only have left <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts</a> months before. However, in <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Prisoner_of_Azkaban" title="Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban">Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban</a></i>, <a href="/wiki/Minerva_McGonagall" title="Minerva McGonagall">Professor McGonagall</a> reveals that they had not been in the running for the Hogwarts <a href="/wiki/Inter-House_Quidditch_Cup" title="Inter-House Quidditch Cup">Quidditch Cup</a> for seven years. This means that Charlie would've been in his second or third year when they last won it. This may indicate a slight continuity error on J.K.'s part, perhaps indicating she originally envisioned Charlie as being older than he became. It is also possible that, like Harry, Charlie was an excellent Quidditch player, but still only won the Cup on one or two occasions due to other circumstances.</li>
<li>At Hogwarts, Charlie was in the same year as <a href="/wiki/Nymphadora_Tonks" title="Nymphadora Tonks">Nymphadora Tonks</a>.</li>
<li>In <i><a href="/wiki/J.K._Rowling:_A_Year_in_the_Life" title="J.K. Rowling: A Year in the Life">J.K. Rowling: A Year in the Life</a></i>, <a href="/wiki/J._K._Rowling" title="J. K. Rowling">J. K. Rowling</a> was asked if Charlie was <a href="/wiki/Sexual_orientation" title="Sexual orientation">gay</a>, to which she jokingly responded that he is not, just, "He's more interested in dragons than women." This has prompted some to think he is an <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Affectional_orientation" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Affectional orientation">aromantic</a> <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Asexuality" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Asexuality">asexual</a> but this has been neither confirmed nor denied by J.K. Rowling.</li>
<li>It is possible that in <i><a href="/wiki/LEGO_Harry_Potter:_Years_1-4" title="LEGO Harry Potter: Years 1-4">LEGO Harry Potter: Years 1-4</a></i>, the <a href="/wiki/Dragon_keeper" title="Dragon keeper">Dragon keeper</a>, found in the Year 4 level, <a href="/wiki/Dragon" title="Dragon">Dragons</a>, is, in fact, Charlie.</li>
<li>Since the subplot of his job of recruiting foreign wizards for the Order of the Phoenix was scrapped from the films, it was never acknowledged if Charlie was a member of the Order of the Phoenix in the film continuity due to his frequent omissions from the film adaptions.</li>
<li>The <a href="/wiki/Narrator" title="Narrator">narrator</a>'s dialogue on the PC version of the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Philosopher%27s_Stone_(video_game)" title="Harry Potter and the Philosopher&#39;s Stone (video game)">video game adaptation</a> of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Philosopher%27s_Stone" title="Harry Potter and the Philosopher&#39;s Stone">Harry Potter and the Philosopher's Stone</a></i> implies that Charlie himself came to Hogwarts to collect Norbert rather than sending his associates. Despite this, at least two figures on <a href="/wiki/Broomstick" title="Broomstick">broomsticks</a> are seen flying around the <a href="/wiki/Astronomy_Tower" title="Astronomy Tower">Astronomy Tower</a>, and the scene is too shadowy to distinguish any features of the people in question.</li>
<li>Charlie only makes a major appearance in person in <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Goblet_of_Fire" title="Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire">Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire</a></i> during the beginning of the book.</li>
<li>In <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter:_Hogwarts_Mystery" title="Harry Potter: Hogwarts Mystery">Harry Potter: Hogwarts Mystery</a></i>, <a href="/wiki/Jacob%27s_sibling" title="Jacob&#39;s sibling">Jacob's sibling</a> describes his scent as grass and honeysuckle.</li>
<li>In <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter:_Hogwarts_Mystery" title="Harry Potter: Hogwarts Mystery">Harry Potter: Hogwarts Mystery</a></i>, Charlie is depicted with brown eyes rather than the blue of his  <i><a href="/wiki/Pottermore" title="Pottermore">Pottermore</a></i> profile.</li>
<li>According to a drawing seen in <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter:_Hogwarts_Mystery" title="Harry Potter: Hogwarts Mystery">Harry Potter: Hogwarts Mystery</a></i>, Charlie's favourite dragon is the <a href="/wiki/Hebridean_Black" title="Hebridean Black">Hebridean Black</a>.</li></ul>
