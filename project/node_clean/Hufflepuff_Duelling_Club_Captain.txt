<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Hufflepuff_Duelling_Club_Captain?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Hufflepuff_Duelling_Club_Captain?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_life">Early life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Hufflepuff_Duelling_Club_Captain?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Early life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Hufflepuff_Duelling_Club_Captain?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Early life"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>This boy attended <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> via the <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Express" title="Hogwarts Express">Hogwarts Express</a> in the <a href="/wiki/1990s" title="1990s">1990s</a> and was <a href="/wiki/Sorting_ceremony" title="Sorting ceremony">Sorted</a> into <a href="/wiki/Hufflepuff" title="Hufflepuff">Hufflepuff House</a> during the <a href="/wiki/Sorting_ceremony" title="Sorting ceremony">Sorting ceremony</a>. His <a href="/wiki/Head_of_House" title="Head of House">Head of House</a> was <a href="/wiki/Pomona_Sprout" title="Pomona Sprout">Pomona Sprout</a>, the <a href="/wiki/Herbology" title="Herbology">Herbology</a> <a href="/wiki/Professor" title="Professor">Professor</a>, and he shared a dormitory with four boys in his year in the <a href="/wiki/Hufflepuff_Basement" title="Hufflepuff Basement">Hufflepuff Basement</a>, which is located below ground level and near the <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_kitchens" title="Hogwarts kitchens">Hogwarts kitchens</a>. He apparently excelled at <a href="/wiki/Defence_Against_the_Dark_Arts" title="Defence Against the Dark Arts">Defence Against the Dark Arts</a> and was chosen to represent <a href="/wiki/Hufflepuff" title="Hufflepuff">Hufflepuff</a> as the Hufflepuff Duelling Club Captain, possibly at <a href="/wiki/1992" title="1992">1992</a> when <a href="/wiki/Gilderoy_Lockhart" title="Gilderoy Lockhart">Gilderoy Lockhart</a> set up the club.<sup id="cite_ref-HBPG_3-11" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBPG-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span id="1996–1997_school_year"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="1996.E2.80.931997_school_year"><a href="/wiki/1996%E2%80%931997_school_year" title="1996–1997 school year">1996–1997 school year</a></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Hufflepuff_Duelling_Club_Captain?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: 1996–1997 school year">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Hufflepuff_Duelling_Club_Captain?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: 1996–1997 school year"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>In <a href="/wiki/1996" title="1996">1996</a>, when <a href="/wiki/Defence_Against_the_Dark_Arts" title="Defence Against the Dark Arts">Defence Against the Dark Arts</a> was taught by <a href="/wiki/Professor" title="Professor">Professor</a> <a href="/wiki/Severus_Snape" title="Severus Snape">Severus Snape</a>, the Hufflepuff boy witnessed <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> duelling <a href="/wiki/Vincent_Crabbe" title="Vincent Crabbe">Vincent Crabbe</a>, who tried to attack when he was in a hurry. He defeated Crabbe effortlessly and was approached by the boy, who was impressed by his remarkable duelling skills asked him to show the rest of the <a href="/wiki/Duelling_Club" title="Duelling Club">Duelling Club</a> how to cast the <a href="/wiki/Shield_Charm" title="Shield Charm">Shield Charm</a>, which he did easily.<sup id="cite_ref-HBPG_3-12" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBPG-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
