<b>Laurence Wakefield </b> was a <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggle</a> documentary filmmaker. He made several documentary <a href="/wiki/Television" title="Television">television</a> series, including <i><a href="/wiki/The_Australia_Series" title="The Australia Series">The Australia Series</a></i>, which was shown on British television in <a href="/wiki/1997" title="1997">1997</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
