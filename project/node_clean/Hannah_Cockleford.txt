<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Hannah_Cockleford?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Hannah_Cockleford?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>In <a href="/wiki/1484" class="mw-redirect" title="1484">1484</a>, the <a href="/wiki/Earl_of_Paunchley" title="Earl of Paunchley">Earl of Paunchley</a>, the tyrannical noble who lorded over the village of <a href="/wiki/Poppleton" title="Poppleton">Poppleton</a>, <a href="/wiki/England" title="England">England</a>, decided to hold a <a href="/wiki/Jousting_tournament" title="Jousting tournament">Jousting Tournament</a> on the grounds of his castle. Shortly before the match was set to begin, he ordered <a href="/wiki/Edmund_Gaddlegate" title="Edmund Gaddlegate">Edmund Gaddlegate</a>, a young boy, to put up pennants and banners. In the process, Edmund accidentally fell out of a tree, breaking his leg. His <a href="/wiki/Edmund_Gaddlegate%27s_mother" title="Edmund Gaddlegate&#39;s mother">mother</a> sent word to the Earl that her son would be unable to attend the match, which he had mandated all residents of Poppleton attend, whether they liked it or not.<sup id="cite_ref-WBoS_1-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-WBoS-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>In punishment, the Earl decided that Edmund would be tied to a <a href="/wiki/Horse" title="Horse">horse</a> and forced to joust against him in the Jousting Tournament, as well as against all the <a href="/wiki/Knight" title="Knight">knights</a> who had come to take part in the competition. When the knights learned that the Earl expected them to joust against an injured boy, however, they left in disgust, and thus the Earl announced that he alone would challenge Edmund.<sup id="cite_ref-WBoS_1-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-WBoS-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>As the Earl charged toward Edmund, Hannah, who was among the crowd of spectators watching the match, cast the strongest <a href="/wiki/Shield_Charm" title="Shield Charm">Shield Charm</a> of her life between them. Hitting the spell, the Earl was knocked back, his horse toppling over onto him. Though he survived, for the rest of his life, he believed he was a <a href="/wiki/Donkey" title="Donkey">donkey</a> named Hairy Cyril.<sup id="cite_ref-WBoS_1-10" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-WBoS-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Magical_abilities_and_skills">Magical abilities and skills</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Hannah_Cockleford?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Hannah_Cockleford?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li><b><a href="/wiki/Charm" title="Charm">Charms</a></b>: Hannah was very good with charms, given how she cast an extremely powerful <a href="/wiki/Shield_Charm" title="Shield Charm">Shield Charm</a> to prevent the <a href="/wiki/Earl_of_Paunchley" title="Earl of Paunchley">Earl of Paunchley</a> from jousting <a href="/wiki/Edmund_Gaddlegate" title="Edmund Gaddlegate">Edmund Gaddlegate</a>, a particularly impressive feat given how effective the charm worked and even rendered the Earl insane as a result.<sup id="cite_ref-WBoS_1-11" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-WBoS-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup></li></ul>
