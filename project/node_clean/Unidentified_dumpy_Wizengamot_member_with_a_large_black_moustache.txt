<p>This <b>individual</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> and member of the <a href="/wiki/Wizengamot" title="Wizengamot">Wizengamot</a> in the mid-<a href="/wiki/1990s" title="1990s">1990s</a>. He was dumpy and heavily-moustached. On <a href="/wiki/12_August" title="12 August">12 August</a>, he was present at <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Disciplinary_hearing_of_Harry_Potter" title="Disciplinary hearing of Harry Potter">disciplinary hearing</a>, appearing highly sceptical of <a href="/wiki/Arabella_Figg" title="Arabella Figg">Arabella Figg</a>'s testimony, whispering about it to <a href="/wiki/Unidentified_frizzy-haired_Wizengamot_member" title="Unidentified frizzy-haired Wizengamot member">the witch sitting next to him</a>. He and his neighbour later voted in favour of <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry</a>'s conviction.<sup id="cite_ref-OP8_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OP8-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
