<p><b>Mr Hagrid</b> (d. <a href="/wiki/1941" title="1941">1941</a>/<a href="/wiki/1942" title="1942">1942</a>) was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> who was <a href="/wiki/Rubeus_Hagrid" title="Rubeus Hagrid">Rubeus Hagrid</a>'s father and the first husband of <a href="/wiki/Fridwulfa" title="Fridwulfa">Fridwulfa</a>. He was short, not only in comparison to his giantess <a href="/wiki/Fridwulfa" title="Fridwulfa">wife</a> and <a href="/wiki/Half-giant" title="Half-giant">half-giant</a> <a href="/wiki/Rubeus_Hagrid" title="Rubeus Hagrid">son</a>, but by human standards as well.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Rubeus_Hagrid%27s_father?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Rubeus_Hagrid%27s_father?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Nothing is known of Mr Hagrid's life before he married the <a href="/wiki/Giant" title="Giant">giantess</a> <a href="/wiki/Fridwulfa" title="Fridwulfa">Fridwulfa</a>, who gave birth to their son <a href="/wiki/Rubeus_Hagrid" title="Rubeus Hagrid">Rubeus</a> (a <a href="/wiki/Half-giant" title="Half-giant">half-giant</a>) on <a href="/wiki/6_December" title="6 December">6 December</a> <a href="/wiki/1928" title="1928">1928</a>. 
</p><p>Fridwulfa was disappointed with their son's small stature and left the family when Rubeus was three years old, leaving Mr Hagrid heartbroken.<sup id="cite_ref-GOF23_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF23-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Mr Hagrid raised his son alone. By the time Rubeus was six, he was large and strong enough to lift his father up onto a dresser if Mr Hagrid annoyed him.<sup id="cite_ref-GOF23_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF23-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>
Mr Hagrid was at first unsure whether Rubeus could be a wizard, considering his giant blood, but he was delighted when Rubeus received his <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_acceptance_letter" title="Hogwarts acceptance letter">Hogwarts acceptance letter</a> in <a href="/wiki/1940" title="1940">1940</a>. By that point, Rubeus could carry his father on his shoulders, and Mr Hagrid was described as "beaming" in a photograph taken of them together.<sup id="cite_ref-:0_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:0-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup></p><figure class="thumb tright show-info-icon" style="width: 122px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/9/9e/GF_24.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20110802072202" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/9/9e/GF_24.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/120?cb=20110802072202" decoding="async" width="120" height="120" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="GF 24.jpg" data-image-key="GF_24.jpg" /></a> 	 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:GF_24.jpg" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption"><a href="/wiki/Rubeus_Hagrid" title="Rubeus Hagrid">Rubeus</a> and his father</p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure><p>Mr Hagrid died when Rubeus was in his second year at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts</a>, and therefore he never knew about his son's expulsion from <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> the <a href="/wiki/1942" title="1942">next year</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-:0_2-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:0-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
