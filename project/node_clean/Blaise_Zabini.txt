<p><b>Blaise Zabini</b> (b. <a href="/wiki/1979" title="1979">1979</a>/<a href="/wiki/1980" title="1980">1980</a>) was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a><sup id="cite_ref-HBP7_6-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP7-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup> and a student in <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>'s year at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a>. He was <a href="/wiki/Sorting_ceremony" title="Sorting ceremony">sorted</a> into the <a href="/wiki/Slytherin" title="Slytherin">Slytherin</a> house,<sup id="cite_ref-PS7_5-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PS7-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> and was on friendly terms with fellow Slytherins, <a href="/wiki/Draco_Malfoy" title="Draco Malfoy">Draco Malfoy</a> and his <a href="/wiki/Draco_Malfoy%27s_gang" title="Draco Malfoy&#39;s gang">gang</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Pansy_Parkinson" title="Pansy Parkinson">Pansy Parkinson</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP7_6-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP7-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Blaise_Zabini?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Blaise_Zabini?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_life">Early life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Blaise_Zabini?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Early life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Blaise_Zabini?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Early life"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Blaise Zabini was the son of a <a href="/wiki/Blaise_Zabini%27s_mother" title="Blaise Zabini&#39;s mother">famously beautiful witch</a> who had been widowed <a href="/wiki/Seven" title="Seven">seven</a> times over by <a href="/wiki/1996" title="1996">1996</a>. Each husband's <a href="/wiki/Death" title="Death">death</a> occurred both under suspicious circumstances and left the already wealthy Ms. Zabini and her son with more money. Blaise was incredibly vain and did not befriend <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggles</a> or <a href="/wiki/Blood_traitor" title="Blood traitor">Blood traitors</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP7_6-10" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP7-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span id="Hogwarts_years_(1991-1998)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="Hogwarts_years_.281991-1998.29">Hogwarts years <span class="template-c">(1991-1998)</span></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Blaise_Zabini?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Hogwarts years (1991-1998)">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Blaise_Zabini?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Hogwarts years (1991-1998)"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_years">Early years</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Blaise_Zabini?veaction=edit&amp;section=4" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Early years">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Blaise_Zabini?action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Early years"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<p>In <a href="/wiki/1991" title="1991">1991</a>, Blaise started his <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magical</a> education at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a>. He was the last student to be <a href="/wiki/Sorting_Hat" title="Sorting Hat">Sorted</a> in his <a href="/wiki/First_year" title="First year">first year</a> and was Sorted into <a href="/wiki/Slytherin" title="Slytherin">Slytherin House</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-PS7_5-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PS7-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> Due to Blaise's reserved nature, he kept to himself most of the time and did not really make friends with other <a href="/wiki/Slytherin" title="Slytherin">Slytherins</a>, nor other students in his year — though this may have been because he was prejudiced against <a href="/wiki/Muggle-born" title="Muggle-born">Muggle-borns</a> and "<a href="/wiki/Blood_traitor" title="Blood traitor">blood traitors</a>." Blaise seemed to get along with <a href="/wiki/Draco_Malfoy" title="Draco Malfoy">Draco Malfoy</a>, <a href="/wiki/Vincent_Crabbe" title="Vincent Crabbe">Vincent Crabbe</a>, <a href="/wiki/Gregory_Goyle" title="Gregory Goyle">Gregory Goyle</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Pansy_Parkinson" title="Pansy Parkinson">Pansy Parkinson</a> — he was willing to sit with them on the train journey to <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts</a> — but other than that, he was not mentioned to be hanging around that group.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP7_6-11" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP7-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Sixth_year">Sixth year</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Blaise_Zabini?veaction=edit&amp;section=5" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Sixth year">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Blaise_Zabini?action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Sixth year"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>

<p>Blaise was one of the students who received an invitation from Professor <a href="/wiki/Horace_Slughorn" title="Horace Slughorn">Horace Slughorn</a> for lunch on the <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Express" title="Hogwarts Express">Hogwarts Express</a> journey, which might imply that none of Blaise's relatives were Death Eaters, as Slughorn wanted nothing to do with them (it was the reason why he chose not to invite <a href="/wiki/Theodore_Nott" title="Theodore Nott">Theodore Nott</a> to join the Club). Slughorn was sizing up the student body for potential new members of his famous <a href="/wiki/Slug_Club" title="Slug Club">Slug Club</a>, which he started when he last taught at Hogwarts.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP7_6-12" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP7-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>During the meeting Blaise seemed quiet as usual and did not join in the discussions. However, he did give a little cough of "amused scepticism" when <a href="/wiki/Horace_Slughorn" title="Horace Slughorn">Slughorn</a> told the group that he thought <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> had powers beyond the ordinary. This caused him to provoke Ginny Weasley to snap at him, and Slughorn jokingly warned Blaise not to annoy Ginny, remembering how well she could cast a <a href="/wiki/Bat-Bogey_Hex" title="Bat-Bogey Hex">Bat-Bogey Hex</a>. When questioned on his family, Blaise talked about his mother and the line of husbands she had who had all left her large amounts of <a href="/wiki/Galleon" title="Galleon">galleons</a> in their wills after their suspicious deaths.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP7_6-13" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP7-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<p>After the lunch meeting ended, Blaise retired to his compartment on the Hogwarts Express in which <a href="/wiki/Draco_Malfoy" title="Draco Malfoy">Draco Malfoy</a>, <a href="/wiki/Vincent_Crabbe" title="Vincent Crabbe">Vincent Crabbe</a>, <a href="/wiki/Gregory_Goyle" title="Gregory Goyle">Gregory Goyle</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Pansy_Parkinson" title="Pansy Parkinson">Pansy Parkinson</a> were also sitting. Harry Potter followed Blaise back to this compartment under his <a href="/wiki/Cloak_of_Invisibility" title="Cloak of Invisibility">Invisibility cloak</a> in an attempt to hear Draco's conversation, and give him some perspective as to what Draco had been planning over the summer.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP7_6-14" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP7-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup> Once they arrived at Hogwarts, Blaise apparently passed the litmus test that Slughorn had set, as he continued to receive invitations to Slughorn's parties after the school year began.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP11_9-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP11-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup> He did not seem to have a high opinion of his fellow members, if his comments to <a href="/wiki/Draco_Malfoy" title="Draco Malfoy">Draco Malfoy</a> on the train were any indication, but, in spite of this, he continued to accept the invitations. </p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Physical_appearance">Physical appearance</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Blaise_Zabini?veaction=edit&amp;section=7" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Physical appearance">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Blaise_Zabini?action=edit&amp;section=7" title="Edit section: Physical appearance"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Blaise was a tall, dark skinned boy with high cheekbones and long, slanting eyes. He was described as being vain about his appearance.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP7_6-16" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP7-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Personality_and_traits">Personality and traits</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Blaise_Zabini?veaction=edit&amp;section=8" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Personality and traits">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Blaise_Zabini?action=edit&amp;section=8" title="Edit section: Personality and traits"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<div class="quote">
<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince">"<i>Zabini gave a tiny little cough that was clearly supposed to indicate amused scepticism.</i>"</span></dd>
<dd>&#8212;Blaise regarding <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a><sup class="noprint"><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Half-Blood_Prince" title="Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince">[src]</a></sup></dd>
<dd></dd></dl></div>
<p>Blaise seemed to be a highly arrogant individual, who looked down on nearly everyone and everything. He rarely expressed his disdainful opinions in a confrontational manner. He reacted with scornful amusement when <a href="/wiki/Horace_Slughorn" title="Horace Slughorn">Professor Slughorn</a> praised Harry Potter's power. When warned not to anger <a href="/wiki/Ginevra_Weasley" title="Ginevra Weasley">Ginny Weasley</a> (who insulted Blaise's vanity) he reacted with contempt.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP7_6-17" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP7-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup> He also insulted most of his fellow <a href="/wiki/Slug_Club" title="Slug Club">Slug Club</a> members and the parties themselves, though he continued to attend the meetings. He also did not seem to respect <a href="/wiki/Draco_Malfoy" title="Draco Malfoy">Draco Malfoy</a> very much.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP7_6-18" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP7-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Blaise was prejudiced against <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggles</a>, <a href="/wiki/Muggle-born" title="Muggle-born">Muggle-borns</a>, and those who were accepting of them. This was made clear when <a href="/wiki/Pansy_Parkinson" title="Pansy Parkinson">Pansy Parkinson</a> accused him of finding Ginny attractive. Blaise in response said he would never touch "<i>a filthy <a href="/wiki/Blood_traitor" title="Blood traitor">blood traitor</a> like her</i>". However, he also did not seem to hold a high opinion of followers of Voldemort, he taunted Draco on his father being outed as a <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eater</a>. It seemed he was so arrogant that he was more or less indifferent and dismissive of everyone, regardless of status.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP7_6-19" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP7-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Blaise_Zabini?veaction=edit&amp;section=10" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Blaise_Zabini?action=edit&amp;section=10" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>Blaise is a French name derived from the Latin <i>blaesus</i>, "lisping".<sup id="cite_ref-15" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-15">&#91;15&#93;</a></sup> In mythology Blaise was the tutor and, later, biographer, of Merlin - which makes this an appropriate name for a wizard. Another famous bearer was <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Blaise_Pascal" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Blaise Pascal">Blaise Pascal</a>, a French mathematician and philosopher. Also, <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Blaise_Castle" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Blaise Castle">Blaise Castle</a> is an eighteenth-century mansion house and estate in Bristol, England called "the finest place in England" in Jane Austen's <i>Northanger Abbey</i>.</li>
<li>The surname Zabini is Italian and Portuguese. Blaise may be of Italian or African descent, given that the East African country of Mozambique, whose official language remains Portuguese, was under Portuguese colonial rule from the fifteenth century until 1975.</li></ul>

<li>Because the name Blaise is gender-neutral, Blaise's gender and nature were hotly debated in fan forums for many years, and both male and female versions of the character were heavily featured in fanfiction. This was mostly due to the indeterminate nature of Blaise's character in canon and lack of further explanation on <a href="/wiki/J._K._Rowling" title="J. K. Rowling">J. K. Rowling</a>'s part until it was revealed in <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Half-Blood_Prince" title="Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince">Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince</a></i> that he was male.<sup id="cite_ref-16" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-16">&#91;16&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li>The Dutch edition of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Philosopher%27s_Stone" title="Harry Potter and the Philosopher&#39;s Stone">Harry Potter and the Philosopher's Stone</a></i> actually turned Blaise into a female character, by translating his name into Bella when he appears at the sorting ceremony. When the Dutch translation of <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Half_Blood_Prince" class="mw-redirect" title="Harry Potter and the Half Blood Prince"><i>Half-Blood Prince</i></a> was released, the character was renamed to the masculine Benno, correcting this mistake.
<ul><li>However, the Hebrew translation described Blaise as a female in the 1st book, and then as male in the 6th book, making a contradiction.</li></ul></li>
<li>In the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Half-Blood_Prince_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince (film)">film adaptation</a> of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Half-Blood_Prince" title="Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince">Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince</a></i>, Blaise plays as a <a href="/wiki/Chaser" title="Chaser">Chaser</a> on the <a href="/wiki/Slytherin_Quidditch_team" title="Slytherin Quidditch team">Slytherin Quidditch team</a>. It doesn't happen in the book.</li>
<li>In <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Deathly_Hallows:_Part_2" title="Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 2">Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 2</a></i>, Blaise is present in the <a href="/wiki/Skirmish_at_the_Room_of_Requirement" title="Skirmish at the Room of Requirement">fight</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Room_of_Requirement" title="Room of Requirement">Room of Requirement</a> in place of <a href="/wiki/Vincent_Crabbe" title="Vincent Crabbe">Vincent Crabbe</a>, whose <a href="/wiki/Jamie_Waylett" title="Jamie Waylett">actor</a> was dropped from the films following his conviction for possession of cannabis. Zabini essentially takes Goyle's place in the film while Goyle takes Crabbe's place and is the one to be killed by Fiendfyre.</li>
<li>Given his surname and his ethnicity, it is likely that Zabini is of (at least partial) Italian and African descent.</li></ul>
