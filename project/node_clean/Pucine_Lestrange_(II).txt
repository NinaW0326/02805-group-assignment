<b>Pucine Lestrange</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch</a> and a member of the <a href="/wiki/Pure-blood" title="Pure-blood">pure-blood</a> <a href="/wiki/Lestrange_family" title="Lestrange family">Lestrange family</a>. She's the daughter of <a href="/wiki/Fulcran_Lestrange_(II)" title="Fulcran Lestrange (II)">Fulcran Lestrange</a> and <a href="/wiki/Sot%C3%A9re_Lestrange" class="mw-redirect" title="Sotére Lestrange">Sotére Lestrange</a> and sister of <a href="/wiki/Balbine_Lestrange_(II)" title="Balbine Lestrange (II)">Balbine Lestrange (II)</a> and <a href="/wiki/Fulcran_Lestrange_(III)" title="Fulcran Lestrange (III)">Fulcran Lestrange</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> She was named after her grandmother, <a href="/wiki/Pucine_Lestrange_(I)" title="Pucine Lestrange (I)">Pucine Leroy</a>.
