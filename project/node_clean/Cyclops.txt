<b>Cyclops</b> was a one-eyed <a href="/wiki/Giant" title="Giant">Giant</a> from Ancient <a href="/wiki/Greece" title="Greece">Greece</a>. He lived in a cave at the foot of <a href="/wiki/Mount_Etna" title="Mount Etna">Mount Etna</a>. He was slain by the Greek hero, <a href="/wiki/Odysseus" title="Odysseus">Odysseus</a>, who used several <a href="/wiki/Sheep" title="Sheep">sheep</a> to help him do this task.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Cyclops?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Cyclops?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>Cyclops is based on <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Polyphemus" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Polyphemus">Polyphemus</a>, a cyclops that appears in Greek mythology, most prominently in <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Homer" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Homer">Homer</a>'s <i><a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Odyssey" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Odyssey">Odyssey</a></i>. In the <i>Odyssey</i>, Polyphemus was not slain by Odysseus, but merely blinded; and cyclopses are a separate species from giants.</li></ul>
