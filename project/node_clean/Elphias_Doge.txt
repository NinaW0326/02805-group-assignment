<p><b>Elphias Doge</b> (b. between <a href="/wiki/1_September" title="1 September">1 September</a> <a href="/wiki/1880" class="mw-redirect" title="1880">1880</a> and <a href="/wiki/31_August" title="31 August">31 August</a> <a href="/wiki/1881" class="mw-redirect" title="1881">1881</a>)<sup id="cite_ref-birth_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-birth-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> was a <a href="/wiki/Great_Britain" title="Great Britain">British</a> <a href="/wiki/Pure-blood" title="Pure-blood">pure-blood</a> <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a>, a <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">Ministry of Magic</a> jurist, a member of the <a href="/wiki/Order_of_the_Phoenix" title="Order of the Phoenix">Order of the Phoenix</a> in both the <a href="/wiki/First_Wizarding_War" title="First Wizarding War">First</a> and <a href="/wiki/Second_Wizarding_War" title="Second Wizarding War">Second Wizarding Wars</a>, and also a close friend of <a href="/wiki/Albus_Dumbledore" title="Albus Dumbledore">Albus Dumbledore</a>. 
</p><p>In the summer of <a href="/wiki/1995" title="1995">1995</a>, Doge was part of the <a href="/wiki/Advance_Guard" title="Advance Guard">Advance Guard</a>, in which he met <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>. After Dumbledore's death in <a href="/wiki/June" title="June">June</a>, <a href="/wiki/1997" title="1997">1997</a>, Mr Doge wrote him an obituary for the <i><a href="/wiki/Daily_Prophet" title="Daily Prophet">Daily Prophet</a></i>. Later that same summer, he attended the <a href="/wiki/Wedding_of_William_Weasley_and_Fleur_Delacour" title="Wedding of William Weasley and Fleur Delacour">wedding</a> of <a href="/wiki/Fleur_Delacour" title="Fleur Delacour">Fleur</a> and <a href="/wiki/William_Weasley" title="William Weasley">Bill Weasley</a> at <a href="/wiki/The_Burrow" title="The Burrow">The Burrow</a>, and managed to <a href="/wiki/Apparition" title="Apparition">Disapparate</a> from there seconds before the <a href="/wiki/Attack_at_the_Burrow" title="Attack at the Burrow">Attack at the Burrow</a> that ensued when <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a> arrived after the <a href="/wiki/Fall_of_the_Ministry_of_Magic" title="Fall of the Ministry of Magic">fall of the Ministry</a>.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Elphias_Doge?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Elphias_Doge?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span id="Early_life_(1881-1899)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_life_.281881-1899.29">Early life <span class="template-c">(1881-1899)</span></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Elphias_Doge?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Early life (1881-1899)">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Elphias_Doge?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Early life (1881-1899)"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<div class="quote">
<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows">"<i>I had contracted dragon pox shortly before arriving at school, and while I was no longer contagious, my pock-marked visage and greenish hue did not encourage many to approach me.</i>"</span></dd>
<dd>&#8212;<i><a href="/wiki/Daily_Prophet" title="Daily Prophet">Daily Prophet</a></i>, Summer <a href="/wiki/1997" title="1997">1997</a>; <i>Albus Dumbledore Remembered</i> by Elphias Doge<sup class="noprint"><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Deathly_Hallows" title="Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows">[src]</a></sup></dd>
<dd></dd></dl></div>
<p>Elphias Doge was born around <a href="/wiki/1881" class="mw-redirect" title="1881">1881</a>, to the <a href="/wiki/Pure-blood" title="Pure-blood">pure-blood</a> <a href="/wiki/Doge_family" title="Doge family">Doge family</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-HPLE_2-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HPLE-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> Sometime in or prior to <a href="/wiki/1892" class="mw-redirect" title="1892">1892</a>, Doge contracted <a href="/wiki/Dragon_Pox" title="Dragon Pox">Dragon Pox</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-DH2_4-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH2-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>On <a href="/wiki/1_September" title="1 September">1 September</a>, <a href="/wiki/1892" class="mw-redirect" title="1892">1892</a>, at age eleven, Elphias Doge started at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a>. No longer contagious of his <a href="/wiki/Dragon_Pox" title="Dragon Pox">Dragon Pox</a>, but still with a "<i>pock-marked visage and greenish hue</i>", not many approached Doge. <a href="/wiki/Albus_Dumbledore" title="Albus Dumbledore">Albus Dumbledore</a>, however, looked past that and befriended him during their first day at Hogwarts. Doge believed that their mutual attraction was due to the fact that they both felt that they were outsiders: he had his greenish skin tone and Dumbledore was primarily known to others as the son of <a href="/wiki/Percival_Dumbledore" title="Percival Dumbledore">Percival Dumbledore</a>, who scarcely a year previously had been convicted of a savage and well-publicized attack upon three young <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggles</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-DH2_4-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH2-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> Their peers at Hogwarts would often think of Doge as Dumbledore's "<i>dim-witted but devoted sidekick</i>".<sup id="cite_ref-DH18_5-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH18-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>When Doge and Dumbledore left Hogwarts in <a href="/wiki/1899" class="mw-redirect" title="1899">1899</a>, they planned to take a <a href="/wiki/Grand_Tour" title="Grand Tour">Grand Tour</a> of the world together. However, when they were staying at the <a href="/wiki/Leaky_Cauldron" title="Leaky Cauldron">Leaky Cauldron</a> and preparing to leave for <a href="/wiki/Greece" title="Greece">Greece</a>, they got news that Dumbledore's mother <a href="/wiki/Kendra_Dumbledore" title="Kendra Dumbledore">Kendra</a> had died.<sup id="cite_ref-DH18_5-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH18-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> Doge attended Kendra's funeral and departed for the Grand Tour without Dumbledore. He wrote letters to Dumbledore about his "<i>narrow escapes from <a href="/wiki/Chimaera" title="Chimaera">Chimaeras</a> in Greece to the experiments of the <a href="/wiki/Egypt" title="Egypt">Egyptian</a> <a href="/wiki/Alchemy" title="Alchemy">alchemists</a></i>".<sup id="cite_ref-DH2_4-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH2-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> He came back home shortly after the death of Dumbledore's sister <a href="/wiki/Ariana_Dumbledore" title="Ariana Dumbledore">Ariana</a>, whose funeral he also attended.<sup id="cite_ref-DH8_8-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH8-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<p>During the <a href="/wiki/First_Wizarding_War" title="First Wizarding War">First Wizarding War</a> in the <a href="/wiki/1970s" title="1970s">1970s</a>, Elphias Doge became a member of the <a href="/wiki/Order_of_the_Phoenix" title="Order of the Phoenix">Order of the Phoenix</a>, an organisation led and founded by his friend <a href="/wiki/Albus_Dumbledore" title="Albus Dumbledore">Albus Dumbledore</a>, in order to oppose <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a> and his <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a>. During that time, he would usually wear a silly hat.<sup id="cite_ref-OP9_9-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OP9-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup> Given his role within the Order, it is more than likely that Doge fought in several battles of the <a href="/wiki/First_Wizarding_War" title="First Wizarding War">First Wizarding War</a>.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Personality_and_traits">Personality and traits</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Elphias_Doge?veaction=edit&amp;section=10" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Personality and traits">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Elphias_Doge?action=edit&amp;section=10" title="Edit section: Personality and traits"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>In his youth, Doge suffered from <a href="/wiki/Dragon_Pox" title="Dragon Pox">Dragon Pox</a>, and everybody at Hogwarts avoided him, except <a href="/wiki/Albus_Dumbledore" title="Albus Dumbledore">Albus Dumbledore</a>. This made Elphias friendly towards Dumbledore. Doge has appeared as being a very loyal friend.<sup id="cite_ref-DH2_4-10" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH2-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> He also disliked unfairness, as he got angry when <a href="/wiki/Muriel" title="Muriel">Muriel</a> falsely claimed that <a href="/wiki/Ariana_Dumbledore" title="Ariana Dumbledore">Ariana Dumbledore</a> was a <a href="/wiki/Squib" title="Squib">Squib</a> and that her family had imprisoned her.<sup id="cite_ref-DH8_8-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH8-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup> Doge also appeared as being a friend of the most members of the <a href="/wiki/Order_of_the_Phoenix" title="Order of the Phoenix">Order of the Phoenix</a>, such as <a href="/wiki/James_Potter_II" title="James Potter II">James</a> and <a href="/wiki/Lily_J._Potter" title="Lily J. <p>Elphias was close friends with <a href="/wiki/Albus_Dumbledore" title="Albus Dumbledore">Albus Dumbledore</a>, meeting him the first time at Hogwarts. They were both outcasts, as he contracted <a href="/wiki/Dragon_Pox" title="Dragon Pox">Dragon Pox</a> shortly before he began schooling.
</p><p>Dumbledore, on the other hand, was an outcast because of <a href="/wiki/Percival_Dumbledore" title="Percival Dumbledore">his Muggle-hating father</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-DH2_4-13" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH2-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> After graduating from school, they had planned to tour the world. However, when they were packing to go to <a href="/wiki/Greece" title="Greece">Greece</a> at the <a href="/wiki/Leaky_Cauldron" title="Leaky Cauldron">Leaky Cauldron</a>, Dumbledore got an <a href="/wiki/Owl" title="Owl">owl</a> informing him that his <a href="/wiki/Kendra_Dumbledore" title="Kendra Dumbledore">mother</a> had died and that he had to go home instead.<sup id="cite_ref-DH18_5-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH18-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>While Elphias was travelling to Greece, Dumbledore had to take care of his siblings. At the time Elphias was ending his travel, Ariana was killed.<sup id="cite_ref-DH2_4-14" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH2-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> Elphias learned that she was accidentally killed in a duel between Albus, <a href="/wiki/Aberforth_Dumbledore" title="Aberforth Dumbledore">Aberforth</a> and <a href="/wiki/Gellert_Grindelwald" title="Gellert Grindelwald">Gellert Grindelwald</a>. No one ever learned who killed her.
</p><p>In their later life, Dumbledore and Elphias remained close friends. Elphias had a high opinion of Albus; he thought that he could see the good inside all the people, however apparently insignificant or wretched. After Dumbledore's death, Elphias wrote a heartfelt obituary for the <i><a href="/wiki/Daily_Prophet" title="Daily Prophet">Daily Prophet</a></i>. At Bill and Fleur's wedding, Elphias stated to Harry Potter that he "<i>certainly knew Albus the longest</i>", if you didn't count his own brother, Aberforth.<sup id="cite_ref-DH2_4-15" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH2-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Muriel">Muriel</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Elphias_Doge?veaction=edit&amp;section=16" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Muriel">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Elphias_Doge?action=edit&amp;section=16" title="Edit section: Muriel"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p><a href="/wiki/Muriel" title="Muriel">Muriel</a> was the loud, ill-mannered, opinionated, 107 year-old aunt of Molly Weasley. She enjoyed gossip very much and <a href="/wiki/Rita_Skeeter" title="Rita Skeeter">Rita Skeeter</a>, and thus was incredibly pleased that Skeeter was to be writing a new book, known as <i><a href="/wiki/The_Life_and_Lies_of_Albus_Dumbledore" title="The Life and Lies of Albus Dumbledore">The Life and Lies of Albus Dumbledore</a></i>, that would slander Dumbledore. Muriel was delighted in revealing the dark parts of Dumbledore's history to Doge, who idolised and admired him and refused to hear anything bad about him. Being close to the Dumbledores, Doge was very emotional about the tragedy of <a href="/wiki/Ariana_Dumbledore" title="Ariana Dumbledore">Ariana</a>, so to hear Muriel so rudely cackling about it made him furious and feebly wheeze about how untrue it was. Muriel brought Doge to the verge of tears as Muriel made him feel wretched and miserable as he heard slanderous lies being told about Albus. While it is true that some of the information in Skeeter's book was true and Doge did skip over the parts that didn't make Dumbledore look very good, Doge still disliked Muriel for horrifying him with the terrible things Skeeter was going to write and the way Muriel took pleasure in hearing them.<sup id="cite_ref-DH8_8-12" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH8-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Elphias_Doge?veaction=edit&amp;section=18" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Elphias_Doge?action=edit&amp;section=18" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li><i>Doge</i> was the title held by the elected leaders of Italian maritime republics such as Venice, Genoa and Padua from the 7th to 19th century. It shared the same root as the title duke, in the Latin dux and Greek doux, both simply meaning leader.</li></ul>

<ul><li>Doge is portrayed by <a href="/wiki/Peter_Cartwright" title="Peter Cartwright">Peter Cartwright</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Order_of_the_Phoenix_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix (film)">film adaptation</a> of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Order_of_the_Phoenix" title="Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix">Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix</a></i> and <a href="/wiki/David_Ryall" title="David Ryall">David Ryall</a> in <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Deathly_Hallows:_Part_1" title="Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 1">Deathly Hallows: Part 1</a></i>.<sup id="cite_ref-14" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-14">&#91;14&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li>In <i><a href="/wiki/LEGO_Harry_Potter:_Years_5-7" title="LEGO Harry Potter: Years 5-7">LEGO Harry Potter: Years 5-7</a></i>, his talk with Harry about Dumbledore's childhood during the <a href="/wiki/Wedding_of_William_Weasley_and_Fleur_Delacour" title="Wedding of William Weasley and Fleur Delacour">Wedding of William Weasley and Fleur Delacour</a> is omitted.</li>
<li>In <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Deathly_Hallows:_Part_1" title="Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 1">Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 1</a></i>, when the wedding is interrupted, and before Elphias Disapparates, he says "Nice meeting you, Mr Potter". This causes a mistake in the film since in the fifth movie they had already seen each other when Harry was being rescued from the Dursleys. Although, they may not have actually spoken to each other upon Harry's rescue, and therefore did not officially "meet" each other until the seventh film.</li></ul>
