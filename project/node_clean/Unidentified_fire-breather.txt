<figure class="thumb tleft show-info-icon" style="width: 277px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/c/cd/Circus_Arcanus_Fire-breather.gif/revision/latest?cb=20200713123404" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/c/cd/Circus_Arcanus_Fire-breather.gif/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/275?cb=20200713123404" decoding="async" width="275" height="115" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="Circus Arcanus Fire-breather.gif" data-image-key="Circus_Arcanus_Fire-breather.gif" /></a> 	 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:Circus_Arcanus_Fire-breather.gif" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption">The Fire-breather with the Circus</p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure>
<p>He was part of the <a href="/wiki/Circus_Arcanus" title="Circus Arcanus">Circus Arcanus</a>, a travelling freak show.<sup id="cite_ref-WU_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-WU-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>During the <a href="/wiki/2020s" title="2020s">2020s</a>, he appeared as a <a href="/wiki/Foundable" title="Foundable">Foundable</a> during the <a href="/wiki/Calamity" title="Calamity">Calamity</a>. Volunteer members of the <a href="/wiki/Statute_of_Secrecy_Task_Force" title="Statute of Secrecy Task Force">Statute of Secrecy Task Force</a> were able to free him, allowing he to return to his original time.<sup id="cite_ref-WU_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-WU-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
