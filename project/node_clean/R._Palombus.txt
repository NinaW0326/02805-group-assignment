<b>R. Palombus</b> was an <a href="/wiki/United_States_of_America" title="United States of America">American</a> <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard or witch</a> and reporter for <i><a href="/wiki/The_New_York_Ghost" title="The New York Ghost">The New York Ghost</a></i> in the <a href="/wiki/1920s" title="1920s">1920s</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-nyghost-dec6_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-nyghost-dec6-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-nyghost-dec7_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-nyghost-dec7-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="History">History</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/R._Palombus?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: History">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/R._Palombus?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: History"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Palombus wrote an article about the questioning of "suspicious <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizards</a>" by <a href="/wiki/Magical_Congress_of_the_United_States_of_America" title="Magical Congress of the United States of America">MACUSA</a> that was published in the <a href="/wiki/6_December" title="6 December">6 December</a> <a href="/wiki/1926" title="1926">1926</a> sunset issue of the newspaper.<sup id="cite_ref-nyghost-dec6_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-nyghost-dec6-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> This was followed by a report by Palombus in <a href="/wiki/7_December" title="7 December">the next day's</a> sunset issue about how MACUSA was cooperating with the <a href="/wiki/International_Confederation_of_Wizards" title="International Confederation of Wizards">International Confederation of Wizards</a> to crack down on "illegal wizards."<sup id="cite_ref-nyghost-dec7_2-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-nyghost-dec7-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
