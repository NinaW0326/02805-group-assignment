<b>Bertie Bott</b> (b. <a href="/wiki/1935" title="1935">1935</a>) was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizarding</a> confectioner, who accidentally created <a href="/wiki/Bertie_Bott%27s_Every_Flavour_Beans" title="Bertie Bott&#39;s Every Flavour Beans">Bertie Bott's Every Flavour Beans</a>. For this, he was featured on a <a href="/wiki/Chocolate_Frog_Card" title="Chocolate Frog Card">Chocolate Frog Card</a>.

<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_life">Early life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Bertie_Bott?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Early life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Bertie_Bott?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Early life"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Bott was born in the year <a href="/wiki/1935" title="1935">1935</a>, somewhere in <a href="/wiki/Great_Britain" title="Great Britain">Great Britain</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-PSG_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PSG-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-COSG_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COSG-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Invention_of_Every_Flavour_Beans">Invention of Every Flavour Beans</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Bertie_Bott?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Invention of Every Flavour Beans">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Bertie_Bott?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Invention of Every Flavour Beans"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<figure class="thumb tleft show-info-icon" style="width: 172px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/5/5b/Bertie_Bott_USA_Card.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20091112000614" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/5/5b/Bertie_Bott_USA_Card.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/170?cb=20091112000614" decoding="async" width="170" height="197" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="Bertie Bott USA Card.jpg" data-image-key="Bertie_Bott_USA_Card.jpg" /></a> 	 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:Bertie_Bott_USA_Card.jpg" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption">A younger Bott, creating the <a href="/wiki/Bertie_Bott%27s_Every_Flavour_Beans" title="Bertie Bott&#39;s Every Flavour Beans">Every Flavour Beans</a></p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure>
<p>During years of his life, Bott dedicated himself to confectionary and sweets production. One day, in the mid-<a href="/wiki/20th_century" title="20th century">twentieth century</a>, Bott made a mistake that would make him famous: while trying to create tasty sweets from food, a pair of dirty socks were accidentally included in the experiment. Recognising the sales potential of sweets that presented "<i>a risk with every mouthful</i>", Bott started commercialising them under the name <a href="/wiki/Bertie_Bott%27s_Every_Flavour_Beans" title="Bertie Bott&#39;s Every Flavour Beans">Bertie Bott's Every Flavour Beans</a>. For the invention of these famous sweets, Bott is featured on a <a href="/wiki/Chocolate_Frog_Card" title="Chocolate Frog Card">Chocolate Frog Card</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-PSG_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PSG-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-COSG_2-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COSG-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>In <a href="/wiki/1993%E2%80%931994_school_year" title="1993â€“1994 school year">1993-1994</a> at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a>, a <a href="/wiki/Portrait" title="Portrait">Portrait</a> of him guarded a secret room, the password to enter was "<i>Bibble</i>".<sup id="cite_ref-POAG_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-POAG-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
