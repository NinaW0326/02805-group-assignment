This <b><a href="/wiki/Potions_(class)" title="Potions (class)">Potions</a> <a href="/wiki/Professor" title="Professor">Professor</a></b> (fl. <a href="/wiki/16th_century" title="16th century">16th century</a>) was a <a href="/wiki/Great_Britain" title="Great Britain">British</a> <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard or witch</a> who taught at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> during <a href="/wiki/Zygmunt_Budge" title="Zygmunt Budge">Zygmunt Budge</a>'s school days.<sup id="cite_ref-BOP_2-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-BOP-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/16th_century_Potions_Professor?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/16th_century_Potions_Professor?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>They had apparently been teaching at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> for a long time, as Budge described this Professor as "ancient".<sup id="cite_ref-BOP_2-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-BOP-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>When needing wild, uncontrollable laughter for his <a href="/wiki/Laughing_Potion" title="Laughing Potion">Laughing Potion</a>, Budge often thought of an incident where a rival student of his spilled <a href="/wiki/Love_Potion" class="mw-redirect" title="Love Potion">Love Potion</a> onto himself, thus becoming attracted to this Professor. In Budge's opinion, this was the most humorous occurrence he had ever witnessed.<sup id="cite_ref-BOP_2-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-BOP-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>By the age of fourteen, Budge was able to correct this Professor on the proper usage of <a href="/wiki/Mackled_Malaclaw" title="Mackled Malaclaw">Mackled Malaclaw</a> <a href="/wiki/Mackled_Malaclaw_tail" title="Mackled Malaclaw tail">tails</a> in <a href="/wiki/Potion" title="Potion">Potion-making</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-BOP_2-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-BOP-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
