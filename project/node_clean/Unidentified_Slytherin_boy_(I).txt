This <b>male Slytherin student</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> who attended <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> and was <a href="/wiki/Sorting_ceremony" title="Sorting ceremony">sorted</a> into <a href="/wiki/Slytherin" title="Slytherin">Slytherin House</a>. He was among the stundents who attended the first <a href="/wiki/Care_of_Magical_Creatures" title="Care of Magical Creatures">Care of Magical Creatures</a> class taught by <a href="/wiki/Rubeus_Hagrid" title="Rubeus Hagrid">Rubeus Hagrid</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-POAF_2-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-POAF-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_Slytherin_boy_(I)?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_Slytherin_boy_(I)?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>This boy started his <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magical</a> education at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> in <a href="/wiki/1991" title="1991">1991</a> and was <a href="/wiki/Sorting_ceremony" title="Sorting ceremony">Sorted</a> into <a href="/wiki/Slytherin" title="Slytherin">Slytherin House</a>. He attended the first <a href="/wiki/Care_of_Magical_Creatures" title="Care of Magical Creatures">Care of Magical Creatures</a> class taught by <a href="/wiki/Rubeus_Hagrid" title="Rubeus Hagrid">Rubeus Hagrid</a>. Once <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> returned from his flight upon <a href="/wiki/Buckbeak" title="Buckbeak">Buckbeak</a>, the boy was pushed out of the way by the jealous and bitter <a href="/wiki/Draco_Malfoy" title="Draco Malfoy">Draco Malfoy</a> as he approached the <a href="/wiki/Hippogriff" title="Hippogriff">Hippogriff</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-POAF_2-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-POAF-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
