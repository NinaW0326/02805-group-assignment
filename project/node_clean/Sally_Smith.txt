<b>Sally Smith</b> (c. <a href="/wiki/1979" title="1979">1979</a>-<a href="/wiki/1980" title="1980">1980</a>) was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch</a> who began attending <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> in <a href="/wiki/1991" title="1991">1991</a>. She was in the same year as <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> and was sorted into either&#160;<a href="/wiki/Gryffindor" title="Gryffindor">Gryffindor</a>, <a href="/wiki/Hufflepuff" title="Hufflepuff">Hufflepuff</a>, or&#160;<a href="/wiki/Slytherin" title="Slytherin">Slytherin</a>&#160;house.
</p>

<ul><li>Smith appeared on the student list shown by <a href="/wiki/J._K._Rowling" title="J. K. Rowling">J. K. Rowling</a> in <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_Me" title="Harry Potter and Me">Harry Potter and Me</a></i>, but her given name was only partially visible, appearing to begin with "Ge-". On  <i><a href="/wiki/Pottermore" title="Pottermore">Pottermore</a></i>, Rowling revealed that the character's given name was originally "Georgina," but that this was crossed out and replaced with "Sally."<sup id="cite_ref-pottermore_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-pottermore-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li>It is possible Sally is one of the many unidentified Hufflepuff girls.</li></ul>
