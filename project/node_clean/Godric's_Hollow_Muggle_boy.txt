<div class="quote">
<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows">"<i>Nice costume, Mister!</i>"</span></dd>
<dd>&#8212;<a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggle</a> boy residing in <a href="/wiki/Godric%27s_Hollow" title="Godric&#39;s Hollow">Godric's Hollow</a> to <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a><sup class="noprint"><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Deathly_Hallows" title="Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows">[src]</a></sup></dd>
<dd></dd></dl></div>
<p>The boy was presumably born in the <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggle</a> part of <a href="/wiki/Godric%27s_Hollow" title="Godric&#39;s Hollow">Godric's Hollow</a>. On <a href="/wiki/Hallowe%27en" title="Hallowe&#39;en">Hallowe'en</a> in <a href="/wiki/1981" title="1981">1981</a>, the boy came across <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a>. Thinking that the Dark Lord was a <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggle</a> in a <a href="/wiki/Hallowe%27en" title="Hallowe&#39;en">Hallowe'en</a> disguise, he made a cheerful comment. Upon seeing Voldemort's face, however, he fled in terror. Voldemort considered killing him, but decided it was "unnecessary", as he had <a href="/wiki/Attack_at_Godric%27s_Hollow_(1981)" title="Attack at Godric&#39;s Hollow (1981)">more important matters</a> to attend to.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
