This <b><a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a></b> sold <a href="/wiki/Pumpkin_Juice" title="Pumpkin Juice">pumpkin juice</a> at the <a href="/wiki/1994" title="1994">1994</a> <a href="/wiki/Quidditch_World_Cup" title="Quidditch World Cup">Quidditch World Cup</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-LEGOY1_1-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-LEGOY1-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>He was quite capable of using the <a href="/wiki/Levitation_Charm" title="Levitation Charm">Levitation Charm</a>, doing so to move the glasses of pumpkin juice over the counter to his customers.<sup id="cite_ref-LEGOY1_1-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-LEGOY1-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>He wore a pointed green wizard's hat and matching green robes. He smiled broadly, chatting happily to his customers as he <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magically</a> poured the drinks and brought them over the counter to them.
</p>
