He had <a href="/wiki/Charms_(class)" title="Charms (class)">Charms</a><sup id="cite_ref-Charms_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Charms-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>, <a href="/wiki/Flying_(class)" title="Flying (class)">Flying</a><sup id="cite_ref-Flying_4-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Flying-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> and <a href="/wiki/History_of_Magic" title="History of Magic">History of Magic</a><sup id="cite_ref-HoM_2-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HoM-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> classes with <a href="/wiki/Jacob%27s_sibling" title="Jacob&#39;s sibling">Jacob's sibling</a>.

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Gryffindor_boy_who_struggled_with_the_Levitation_Charm?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Gryffindor_boy_who_struggled_with_the_Levitation_Charm?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<div class="quote">
<dl><dd><span>"<i>That's funny, he's a Gryffindor in our year but I never learned his name...</i>"</span></dd>
<dd>&#8212;<a href="/wiki/Ben_Copper" title="Ben Copper">Ben Copper</a> about this boy</dd>
<dd></dd></dl></div><p>This individual started attending Hogwarts on <a href="/wiki/1_September" title="1 September">1 September</a> <a href="/wiki/1984" title="1984">1984</a> and was <a href="/wiki/Sorting_ceremony" title="Sorting ceremony">sorted</a> into <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor" title="Gryffindor">Gryffindor</a> <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Houses" title="Hogwarts Houses">House</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-Charms_1-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Charms-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-HoM_2-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HoM-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>The individual frequently struggled with the <a href="/wiki/Levitation_Charm" title="Levitation Charm">Levitation Charm</a> in <a href="/wiki/Charms_(class)" title="Charms (class)">Charms classes</a>, taught by <a href="/wiki/Filius_Flitwick" title="Filius Flitwick">Professor Flitwick</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-Charms_1-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Charms-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>In his first year, he bit someone. Jacob's sibling suspected that he was the <a href="/wiki/Werewolf" title="Werewolf">werewolf</a> whom <a href="/wiki/Cecil_Lee" title="Cecil Lee">Cecil Lee</a> was looking for. <a href="/wiki/Rowan_Khanna" title="Rowan Khanna">Rowan Khanna</a> doubted it.<sup id="cite_ref-31oct_5-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-31oct-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>By their <a href="/wiki/Fifth_year" title="Fifth year">fifth year</a>, Jacob's sibling and their friends did not remember the boy's name; Jacob's sibling had completely forgotten the boy was in their year by that point. <a href="/wiki/Ben_Copper" title="Ben Copper">Ben Copper</a> considered it too rude to ask him his name.<sup id="cite_ref-HoM_2-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HoM-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<figure class="thumb tleft show-info-icon" style="width: 182px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/6/65/LeviosaKid_O.W.L.png/revision/latest?cb=20190425200751" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/6/65/LeviosaKid_O.W.L.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/180?cb=20190425200751" decoding="async" width="180" height="180" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="LeviosaKid O.W.L.png" data-image-key="LeviosaKid_O.W.L.png" /></a> 	 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:LeviosaKid_O.W.L.png" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption">The Gryffindor receives his <a href="/wiki/Ordinary_Wizarding_Level" title="Ordinary Wizarding Level">O.W.L.</a> results</p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure>
<p>Shortly before <a href="/wiki/Hallowe%27en" title="Hallowe&#39;en">Hallowe'en</a> <a href="/wiki/1988" title="1988">1988</a><sup id="cite_ref-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup>, the boy volunteered to help <a href="/wiki/Albus_Dumbledore" title="Albus Dumbledore">Albus Dumbledore</a> decorate the <a href="/wiki/Great_Hall" title="Great Hall">Great Hall</a> for the <a href="/wiki/Hallowe%27en_Feast" title="Hallowe&#39;en Feast">Hallowe'en Feast</a>. He carved <a href="/wiki/Pumpkin" title="Pumpkin">pumpkins</a> along with Jacob's sibling, <a href="/wiki/Rowan_Khanna" title="Rowan Khanna">Rowan Khanna</a>, <a href="/wiki/Penny_Haywood" title="Penny Haywood">Penny Haywood</a> and <a href="/wiki/William_Weasley" title="William Weasley">Bill Weasley</a>. Jacob's sibling and Rowan did not remember his name, and the latter wondered whether he'd be offended if they asked him for his name. They hoped he didn't try to levitate the pumpkin he was working on, and noticed that he appeared to be carving a feather in to it. He also helped the two make decorations.<sup id="cite_ref-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>At the end of the school year, he took the <a href="/wiki/Ordinary_Wizarding_Level" title="Ordinary Wizarding Level">Ordinary Wizarding Levels</a> and got at least three <a href="/wiki/Acceptable" title="Acceptable">Acceptable</a> grades.<sup id="cite_ref-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup> Despite struggling with the subject, he managed to get a high enough grade in Charms to continue taking the subject at <a href="/wiki/Nastily_Exhausting_Wizarding_Test" title="Nastily Exhausting Wizarding Test">N.E.W.T.</a> level.<sup id="cite_ref-Charms_1-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Charms-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> He also continued taking History of Magic.<sup id="cite_ref-HoM_2-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HoM-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>In <a href="/wiki/1989%E2%80%931990_school_year" title="1989–1990 school year">1989–1990 school year</a>, after <a href="/wiki/Rowan_Khanna" title="Rowan Khanna">Rowan Khanna</a>'s death, his dad wanted to pull him out, because he thought Hogwarts wasn't safe.<sup id="cite_ref-Charms_1-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Charms-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Gryffindor_boy_who_struggled_with_the_Levitation_Charm?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Gryffindor_boy_who_struggled_with_the_Levitation_Charm?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>When Jacob's sibling casts a powerful Levitation Charm to levitate themselves, they mention they have been "practising it over and over with a struggling Gryffindor kid in Charms class".<sup id="cite_ref-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup> Although unconfirmed, they were likely referring to this individual.</li>
<li>This individual is the only student in Jacob's sibling's <a href="/wiki/History_of_Magic" title="History of Magic">History of Magic</a> class whose name is unknown, as well as the only one in the class who is not in their <a href="/wiki/Defence_Against_the_Dark_Arts" title="Defence Against the Dark Arts">Defence Against the Dark Arts</a> class.</li>
<li>It is possible that he is <a href="/wiki/Thorin" title="Thorin">Thorin</a>.</li>
<li>He is commonly known as the "Leviosa Kid" amongst fans.</li></ul>
