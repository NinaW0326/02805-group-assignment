<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix">"<i>Personally I think this is really important, possibly more important than anything else we'll do this year, even with our <a href="/wiki/Ordinary_Wizarding_Level" title="Ordinary Wizarding Level">O.W.L.s</a> coming up. Obviously, they are in denial about the return of <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">You-Know-Who</a>.</i>"</span></dd>
<dd>&#8212; Ernie Macmillan's high opinion of <a href="/wiki/Dumbledore%27s_Army" title="Dumbledore&#39;s Army">Dumbledore's Army</a> <sup class="noprint"><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Order_of_the_Phoenix" title="Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix">[src]</a></sup></dd>

<p><b>Ernest "Ernie" Macmillan</b> (b. <a href="/wiki/1980" title="1980">1980</a>) was a <a href="/wiki/Pure-blood" title="Pure-blood">pure-blood</a><sup id="cite_ref-COS11_2-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COS11-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> who attended <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> from <a href="/wiki/1991" title="1991">1991</a>-<a href="/wiki/1998" title="1998">1998</a>. He was in the same year as the famous <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> and was sorted into <a href="/wiki/Hufflepuff" title="Hufflepuff">Hufflepuff</a> <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Houses" title="Hogwarts Houses">House</a>. He was also born into the Macmillan family, one of the <a href="/wiki/Sacred_Twenty-Eight" title="Sacred Twenty-Eight">Sacred Twenty-Eight</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-COS11_2-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COS11-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Ernie was an academically strong student and good friends with <a href="/wiki/Hannah_Abbott" title="Hannah Abbott">Hannah Abbott</a> and <a href="/wiki/Justin_Finch-Fletchley" title="Justin Finch-Fletchley">Justin Finch-Fletchley</a>. In <a href="/wiki/1992" title="1992">1992</a>, Ernie initially suspected that <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> was the <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Heir of Slytherin</a> during the opening of the <a href="/wiki/Chamber_of_Secrets" title="Chamber of Secrets">Chamber of Secrets</a> in his Second year.<sup id="cite_ref-COS11_2-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COS11-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> However after <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione Granger</a> was petrified he told Harry that he knew Harry would never do that to her. After he apologised to Harry for his suspicions and accusations, the two became good friends.<sup id="cite_ref-COS15_8-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COS15-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>In Ernie's fourth year their friendship temporarily wavered after Harry's name came out of the cup after Hufflepuff's <a href="/wiki/Cedric_Diggory" title="Cedric Diggory">Cedric Diggory</a>. He thought Harry cheated and proceeded to wear Potter stinks badges, courtesy of <a href="/wiki/Draco_Malfoy" title="Draco Malfoy">Draco Malfoy</a>. When Harry proclaimed that Voldemort had returned at the end of the year Ernie was one of the few who believed him.<sup id="cite_ref-OP_4-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OP-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>In his fifth year, Ernie became a member of <a href="/wiki/Dumbledore%27s_Army" title="Dumbledore&#39;s Army">Dumbledore's Army</a>, an organisation taught and led by Harry for the purposes of learning practical defensive magic.<sup id="cite_ref-OP_4-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OP-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> He joined the D.A. again when it was reinstated during his seventh year, opposing <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eater</a> control of Hogwarts and fighting in the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a> on the <a href="/wiki/2_May" title="2 May">2 May</a>, <a href="/wiki/1998" title="1998">1998</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-DH_9-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>It remains unknown what happened to Ernie after the <a href="/wiki/Second_Wizarding_War" title="Second Wizarding War">Second Wizarding War</a>, but he likely survived as his name wasn't listed among the casualties. What Ernie did after the war, if he survived, is unknown.
</p>

<div class="quote">
<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets">"<i>In case you’re getting ideas, I might tell you that you can trace my family back through nine generations of witches and warlocks and my blood’s as pure as anyone’s.</i>"</span></dd>
<dd>&#8212;Ernie believing Harry Potter to be the Heir of Slytherin<sup class="noprint"><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Chamber_of_Secrets" title="Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets">[src]</a></sup></dd>

<p>Ernie was born into a <a href="/wiki/Pure-blood" title="Pure-blood">pure-blood</a> <a href="/wiki/Macmillan_family" title="Macmillan family">family</a> that can trace its lineage back nine generations; he may be related to <a href="/wiki/Melania_Macmillan" title="Melania Macmillan">Melania Black</a> <span class="template-c">(née <a href="/wiki/Macmillan_family" title="Macmillan family">Macmillan</a>)</span>, <a href="/wiki/Sirius_Black" title="Sirius Black">Sirius Black</a>'s paternal grandmother, a pure-blood family, which references to both his surname and blood heritage. Although he was proud of his blood status, Ernie was not prejudiced against those of other <a href="/wiki/Blood_status" title="Blood status">blood statuses</a>, befriending and looking out for <a href="/wiki/Justin_Finch-Fletchley" title="Justin Finch-Fletchley">Justin Finch-Fletchley</a>, a <a href="/wiki/Muggle-born" title="Muggle-born">Muggle-born</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-COS11_2-10" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COS11-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>

<p>Ernie began attending <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> on <a href="/wiki/1_September" title="1 September">1 September</a>, <a href="/wiki/1991" title="1991">1991</a>. He was <a href="/wiki/Sorting_Hat" title="Sorting Hat">sorted</a> into <a href="/wiki/Hufflepuff" title="Hufflepuff">Hufflepuff House</a>, which valued characteristics such as loyalty and friendship, and readiness to work. During the <a href="/wiki/Welcoming_Feast" title="Welcoming Feast">Welcoming Feast</a> he soon became friends with fellow Hufflepuff students <a href="/wiki/Hannah_Abbott" title="Hannah Abbott">Hannah Abbott</a>, <a href="/wiki/Susan_Bones" title="Susan Bones">Susan Bones</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Justin_Finch-Fletchley" title="Justin Finch-Fletchley">Justin Finch-Fletchley</a> and stayed friends with them through out the entire school year.<sup id="cite_ref-PS7_10-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PS7-10">&#91;10&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-COS11_2-11" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COS11-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<p>During his <a href="/wiki/1992%E2%80%931993_school_year" title="1992–1993 school year">second year</a>, Ernie came to believe that <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> was the <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Heir of Slytherin</a>, after he spoke <a href="/wiki/Parseltongue" title="Parseltongue">Parseltongue</a> in front of the entire <a href="/wiki/Duelling_Club" title="Duelling Club">Duelling Club</a>. In actuality Harry was trying to stop the <a href="/wiki/Snake" title="Snake">snake</a> <a href="/wiki/Snake_Summons_Spell" title="Snake Summons Spell">conjured</a> by <a href="/wiki/Draco_Malfoy" title="Draco Malfoy">Draco Malfoy</a> from attacking <a href="/wiki/Justin_Finch-Fletchley" title="Justin Finch-Fletchley">Justin Finch-Fletchley</a>. However, Ernie perceived it differently. He believed that Harry was in fact urging the snake to attack instead. He showed bravery when he stepped in to protect his friend, suggesting that he should hide in their common room and stay there until the coast was clear.<sup id="cite_ref-COS11_2-12" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COS11-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<p>Ernie went into discussion with the other Hufflepuffs regarding if Harry was the heir of <a href="/wiki/Slytherin" title="Slytherin">Slytherin</a>, considering the coincidences with the victims. He voiced that being a Parselmouth was the very symbol of a Dark wizard and was probably the reason why <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Voldemort</a> wanted to kill Harry, Voldemort did not want another Dark Lord competing against him. Harry, who was attempting to explain to Justin what really happened, was eavesdropping on their conversation and attempted to convince Ernie that the altercation at the <a href="/wiki/Duelling_Club" title="Duelling Club">Duelling Club</a> was not what it seemed. Ernie stated that Harry should not try anything, as he himself was <a href="/wiki/Pure-blood" title="Pure-blood">pure-blood</a> and also brought up the fact that Harry hated his <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggle</a> relatives. When it seemed that their argument was going nowhere, Harry eventually gave up.<sup id="cite_ref-COS11_2-13" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COS11-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<p>Soon enough, Ernie's fears worsened when Justin was <a href="/wiki/Petrification" title="Petrification">petrified</a>. He discussed the matter with their mutual friend Hannah, who was initially sceptical because she thought Harry was nice, but was eventually persuaded to Ernie's point of view.<sup id="cite_ref-COS11_2-14" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COS11-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>However, <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione Granger</a>, one of Harry's best friends, was later petrified as well. This made Ernie realise that Harry could not be the Heir of Slytherin, as he knew Harry would never harm Hermione, and he gave Harry a public apology. He believed that Malfoy could be the culprit, but Harry gave an emotionless no, making Ernie and Hannah stare.<sup id="cite_ref-COS15_8-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COS15-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup>

<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Third_year">Third year</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Ernest_Macmillan?veaction=edit&amp;section=6" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Third year">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Ernest_Macmillan?action=edit&amp;section=6" title="Edit section: Third year"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<p>In his <a href="/wiki/1993-1994_school_year" class="mw-redirect" title="1993-1994 school year">third year</a>, Ernie took many subjects similar to Hermione. He once told <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ron Weasley</a> that she had never missed a <a href="/wiki/Muggle_Studies" title="Muggle Studies">Muggle Studies</a> lesson, despite having another class at exactly the same time that she also attended. This was due to Hermione's use of a <a href="/wiki/Time-Turner" title="Time-Turner">Time-Turner</a> to attend all her lessons.<sup id="cite_ref-POA12_11-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-POA12-11">&#91;11&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<p>In the summer of <a href="/wiki/1994" title="1994">1994</a>, Ernie and his family attended the <a href="/wiki/Quidditch_World_Cup" title="Quidditch World Cup">Quidditch World Cup</a> final between <a href="/wiki/Irish_National_Quidditch_team" title="Irish National Quidditch team">Ireland</a> and <a href="/wiki/Bulgarian_National_Quidditch_team" title="Bulgarian National Quidditch team">Bulgaria</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-GOF7_12-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF7-12">&#91;12&#93;</a></sup> Whilst there a <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eater</a> <a href="/wiki/Reappearance_of_the_Dark_Mark" title="Reappearance of the Dark Mark">raid</a> ensued, resulting in chaos amongst those attending.
</p><p>Ernie's fourth year held place to the <a href="/wiki/Triwizard_Tournament" title="Triwizard Tournament">Triwizard Tournament</a>, which was hosted at Hogwarts that year. He supported fellow Hufflepuff <a href="/wiki/Cedric_Diggory" title="Cedric Diggory">Cedric Diggory</a> when he qualified and again stopped speaking to Harry Potter, who also qualified, but underage, as he was one of many students who initially believed that Harry had entered himself into the tournament in a bid for further glory. He tried to convince friends in Hufflepuff to agree with him, but they needed not much convincing.<sup id="cite_ref-GF_13-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GF-13">&#91;13&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<p>He also wore a <i><a href="/wiki/Potter_Stinks_badge" title="Potter Stinks badge">Support Cedric Diggory/Potter stinks</a></i> badge. Ernie and other Hufflepuffs frequently harassed Harry Potter with their badges, even though Cedric asked them not to. However, he likely wore off wearing this badge after Cedric requested his housemates to stop wearing them after the first task. After the tournament came to a close, Cedric Diggory was <a href="/wiki/Killing_Curse" title="Killing Curse">murdered</a> by <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a>, coming as a shock to all students and staff members. He attended Diggory's memorial feast as did his friends. Cedric Diggory was a member of <a href="/wiki/Hufflepuff" title="Hufflepuff">Hufflepuff</a> and since Harry was with him when he died, Ernie appeared to have forgotten any ill wishes he thought of Harry and believed his claims to Voldemort returning whilst the Ministry did not. Ernie was among the very few students that did believe Harry's account of Voldemort's <a href="/wiki/Return_of_Lord_Voldemort" title="Return of Lord Voldemort">return</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-GF_13-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GF-13">&#91;13&#93;</a></sup>

<dd></dd></dl></div><p>In <a href="/wiki/1995" title="1995">1995</a>, Ernie became a Hufflepuff <a href="/wiki/Prefect" title="Prefect">prefect</a>, alongside <a href="/wiki/Hannah_Abbott" title="Hannah Abbott">Hannah Abbott</a>. Unlike previous years, during which he had thought ill of Harry for certain acts he believed him to have committed, Ernie was one of the few Hogwarts students who believed Harry's story that <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a> had returned. He even went as far as to make an openly public declaration of support on Harry's behalf.<sup id="cite_ref-OP_4-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OP-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>

<p>Shortly after the beginning of term, Ernie joined Dumbledore's Army because of his belief in Harry and because of his dismay with Dolores Umbridge, a <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">Ministry of Magic</a> employee who became <a href="/wiki/Defence_Against_the_Dark_Arts" title="Defence Against the Dark Arts">Defence Against the Dark Arts</a> professor but refused to teach students any practical defence. Ernie believed Dumbledore's Army to be one of the most important things they would be doing that year—apart from <a href="/wiki/Ordinary_Wizarding_Level" title="Ordinary Wizarding Level">O.W.L.s</a>. In the run-up to his examinations, he would study between eight and ten hours a day. He developed the irritating habit of boasting about this fact, then inquiring about other students' study habits.<sup id="cite_ref-OP_4-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OP-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>When Headmaster Albus Dumbledore was forced to flee the school after the D.A. was discovered, <a href="/wiki/Umbridge_family" title="Umbridge family">Umbridge</a> took his place as Head of the school. However, <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts</a> itself refused to recognise her authority and sealed the <a href="/wiki/Headmaster%27s_Tower" title="Headmaster&#39;s Tower">Headmaster's Tower</a> against her, prompting her to throw what was referred to as a "right little tantrum". This act was witnessed by the <a href="/wiki/Fat_Friar" title="Fat Friar">Fat Friar</a>, Hufflepuff's house ghost, who told Ernie about it and who in turn relayed it to Harry, Ron and Hermione. Unfortunately, this happened to be overheard by Draco Malfoy and several other <a href="/wiki/Slytherin" title="Slytherin">Slytherins</a> who had joined Umbridge's newly-formed Inquisitorial Squad.<sup id="cite_ref-OP_4-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OP-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>When Malfoy stated that he would have to dock points from <a href="/wiki/Hufflepuff" title="Hufflepuff">Hufflepuff</a> and Gryffindor, Ernie immediately contradicted him by stating that fellow Prefects had no authority to do so, but was overridden by the Squad's new powers and his contradiction was used as an excuse to dock five points from Hufflepuff, much to Ernie's mingled shock and fury. When <a href="/wiki/Fred_Weasley" title="Fred Weasley">Fred</a> and <a href="/wiki/George_Weasley" title="George Weasley">George Weasley</a> implied that they were going to cause some trouble in the school, Ernie looked uncomfortable and made an excuse about some unfinished <a href="/wiki/Transfiguration_(class)" title="Transfiguration (class)">Transfiguration</a> homework so he wouldn't be associated.<sup id="cite_ref-OP_4-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OP-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>After term had ended, Ernie was one of the members of the D.A. who helped foil an ambush on Harry by <a href="/wiki/Draco_Malfoy" title="Draco Malfoy">Draco Malfoy</a>, <a href="/wiki/Vincent_Crabbe" title="Vincent Crabbe">Vincent Crabbe</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Gregory_Goyle" title="Gregory Goyle">Gregory Goyle</a> on the <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Express" title="Hogwarts Express">Hogwarts Express</a>, cursing them so vociferously that they were reduced to three gigantic <a href="/wiki/Slug" title="Slug">slugs</a> in Hogwarts robes. Ernie and Justin then helped hoist them into the luggage rack to ooze out the journey, taking particular pleasure in doing so as revenge for the points Malfoy had unfairly docked from Hufflepuff.<sup id="cite_ref-OP_4-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OP-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<p>Ernie's hard work and determination from the previous year paid off. He was the only Hufflepuff student in his year who reached the required level to advance to <a href="/wiki/Nastily_Exhausting_Wizarding_Test" title="Nastily Exhausting Wizarding Test">N.E.W.T</a> Potions. Ernie earned at least an '<a href="/wiki/Exceeds_Expectations" title="Exceeds Expectations">Exceeds Expectations</a>' on his O.W.L. exam, something <a href="/wiki/Severus_Snape" title="Severus Snape">Severus Snape</a> claimed was very hard to achieve. Ernie had also qualified for N.E.W.T-level <a href="/wiki/Defence_Against_the_Dark_Arts" title="Defence Against the Dark Arts">Defence Against the Dark Arts</a>, most likely because of Harry Potter's teaching the previous year. Ernie also attended <a href="/wiki/Apparition_(class)" title="Apparition (class)">Apparition lessons</a> with his fellow students and during the first lesson he did a leap into his hoop. An action that had both <a href="/wiki/Dean_Thomas" title="Dean Thomas">Dean Thomas</a> and <a href="/wiki/Seamus_Finnigan" title="Seamus Finnigan">Seamus Finnigan</a> roaring with laughter at his expense.<sup id="cite_ref-HP_5-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HP-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<p>Later in the year, Death Eaters invaded Hogwarts, Dumbledore's Army was called to battle and the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_the_Astronomy_Tower" title="Battle of the Astronomy Tower">Battle of the Astronomy Tower</a> broke out. It was likely Ernie never received his D.A. signal, as he had left the <a href="/wiki/Hufflepuff_Basement" title="Hufflepuff Basement">Hufflepuff Common Room</a> to investigate a disturbance he had heard when he saw <a href="/wiki/Draco_Malfoy" title="Draco Malfoy">Draco Malfoy</a>, <a href="/wiki/Severus_Snape" title="Severus Snape">Severus Snape</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> running down a corridor. He asked Harry what was going on, but got no response save for two of his housemates getting knocked aside. He would later discover that <a href="/wiki/Albus_Dumbledore" title="Albus Dumbledore">Albus Dumbledore</a> had been killed that night by Snape, and then presumably attended his funeral several days later to pay his respects.<sup id="cite_ref-HP_5-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HP-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Seventh_year">Seventh year</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Ernest_Macmillan?veaction=edit&amp;section=10" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Seventh year">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Ernest_Macmillan?action=edit&amp;section=10" title="Edit section: Seventh year"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<div class="quote">
<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows"><b>Professor McGonagall</b>: "<i>Prefects, when I give the word, you will organise your House and take your charges in an orderly fashion to the evacuation point.</i>"</span></dd>
<dd><b>Ernie</b>: "<i>And what if we want to stay and fight?</i>"</dd>
<dd>&#8212; Ernie's willingness to fight in the Battle of Hogwarts<sup class="noprint"><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Deathly_Hallows" title="Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows">[src]</a></sup></dd></dl>
</div><p>As a <a href="/wiki/Pure-blood" title="Pure-blood">pure-blood</a>, Ernie was able to return to Hogwarts for the 1997-1998 school year after the <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">Ministry of Magic</a> fell under <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a>'s control; indeed, Hogwarts attendance became mandatory. Presumably, Ernie's <a href="/wiki/Muggle-born" title="Muggle-born">Muggle-born</a> friend <a href="/wiki/Justin_Finch-Fletchley" title="Justin Finch-Fletchley">Justin Finch-Fletchley</a> was not able to come back to school, though <a href="/wiki/Hannah_Abbott" title="Hannah Abbott">Hannah Abbott</a> returned.
</p><p>Ernie and Hannah joined the revived <a href="/wiki/Dumbledore%27s_Army" title="Dumbledore&#39;s Army">Dumbledore's Army</a>, which was led by <a href="/wiki/Neville_Longbottom" title="Neville Longbottom">Neville Longbottom</a>, <a href="/wiki/Ginevra_Weasley" title="Ginevra Weasley">Ginny Weasley</a>, and Luna Lovegood in the absence of <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>, <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione Granger</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ron Weasley</a>, who were off hunting down Voldemort's <a href="/wiki/Horcrux" title="Horcrux">Horcruxes</a>. The D.A. members opposed the anti-<a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggle</a> propaganda and <a href="/wiki/Dark_Arts" title="Dark Arts">Dark Arts</a> taught by Death Eater professors <a href="/wiki/Alecto_Carrow" title="Alecto Carrow">Alecto</a> and <a href="/wiki/Amycus_Carrow" title="Amycus Carrow">Amycus Carrow</a>. They painted rebellious phrases on the walls and freed students from detention, where they faced beatings and even the <a href="/wiki/Cruciatus_Curse" title="Cruciatus Curse">Cruciatus Curse</a>.
</p>

<p>On <a href="/wiki/2_May" title="2 May">2 May</a>, <a href="/wiki/1998" title="1998">1998</a>, Harry, Ron and Hermione returned to Hogwarts as Voldemort approached with his army. A meeting was held in the <a href="/wiki/Great_Hall" title="Great Hall">Great Hall</a>, and when <a href="/wiki/Minerva_McGonagall" title="Minerva McGonagall">Professor McGonagall</a> laid out the plans to evacuate the younger students from the school, Ernie indicated his willingness to fight against the <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a> by directly asking McGonagall about those who wished to remain behind. His question was applauded by a number of students who held similar feelings.
</p><p>During the subsequent battle, Ernie, along with <a href="/wiki/Luna_Lovegood" title="Luna Lovegood">Luna Lovegood</a> and <a href="/wiki/Seamus_Finnigan" title="Seamus Finnigan">Seamus Finnigan</a>, came to the aid of Harry, Hermione, and Ron by using their <a href="/wiki/Patronus_Charm" title="Patronus Charm">Patronuses</a> to drive off a group of <a href="/wiki/Dementor" title="Dementor">Dementors</a>. Ernie's took the form of a <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Boar" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Boar">boar</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-DH32_7-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH32-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span id="Later_life_(1998-?)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="Later_life_.281998-.3F.29">Later life <span class="template-c">(1998-?)</span></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Ernest_Macmillan?veaction=edit&amp;section=12" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Later life (1998-?)">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Ernest_Macmillan?action=edit&amp;section=12" title="Edit section: Later life (1998-?)"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Ernie's current life is unknown. It can be assumed that he survived the final battle of the <a href="/wiki/Second_Wizarding_War" title="Second Wizarding War">Second Wizarding War</a> as his name wasn't mentioned amongst the casualties.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Physical_appearance">Physical appearance</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Ernest_Macmillan?veaction=edit&amp;section=13" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Physical appearance">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Ernest_Macmillan?action=edit&amp;section=13" title="Edit section: Physical appearance"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Ernie Macmillan was described to be a stout-looking boy with blondish hair.<sup id="cite_ref-COS11_2-15" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COS11-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> When concentrating, such as during <a href="/wiki/Apparition_(class)" title="Apparition (class)">Apparition lessons</a>, his face would go a deep shade of pink.<sup id="cite_ref-HP_5-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HP-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> Being stout implied that he was on the chubbier side, or was, at least, heavier than the average student.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Personality_and_traits">Personality and traits</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Ernest_Macmillan?veaction=edit&amp;section=14" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Personality and traits">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Ernest_Macmillan?action=edit&amp;section=14" title="Edit section: Personality and traits"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<div class="quote">
<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix">"<i>I must say, I'm looking forward to seeing <a href="/wiki/Narcissa_Malfoy" title="Narcissa Malfoy">Malfoy's mother</a>'s face when he gets off the train.</i>"</span></dd>
<dd>&#8212;Ernie shortly after helping Harry Potter hex <a href="/wiki/Draco_Malfoy" title="Draco Malfoy">Draco Malfoy</a><sup class="noprint"><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Order_of_the_Phoenix" title="Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix">[src]</a></sup></dd>
<dd></dd></dl></div><p>Despite being somewhat pompous, Ernie was loyal to his friends and always honest, traits of a true <a href="/wiki/Hufflepuff" title="Hufflepuff">Hufflepuff</a>. He would usually shake Harry's hand in a portentous manner and speak in a somewhat ostentatious and presumptuous manner. He was never afraid to speak his mind, and could readily admit when he was wrong.<sup id="cite_ref-CS_14-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-CS-14">&#91;14&#93;</a></sup> As a student, he was hard-working and strived to get good marks, which was particularly evident in his fifth year, when he claimed to be revising for his <a href="/wiki/Ordinary_Wizarding_Level" title="Ordinary Wizarding Level">O.W.Ls</a> around nine hours a day.<sup id="cite_ref-OP_4-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OP-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> He also displayed great bravery and loyalty in his participation in the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-DH_9-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Ernie did however seem to have a habit of jumping to conclusions regarding <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>; in his second year, he believed and ultimately spread a rumour that Harry was the <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Heir of Slytherin</a>. During his fourth year, Ernie made his disapproval of Harry's participation in the <a href="/wiki/Triwizard_Tournament" title="Triwizard Tournament">Triwizard Tournament</a> as an illegal fourth champion clear, claiming Harry had done it to steal the spotlight from Hufflepuff.<sup id="cite_ref-GF_13-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GF-13">&#91;13&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Despite being a <a href="/wiki/Pure-blood" title="Pure-blood">pure-blood</a> and proud of it, Ernie never displayed any prejudice towards those of different <a href="/wiki/Blood_status" title="Blood status">blood status</a>, as many other pure-bloods did. In fact, his two closest friends at Hogwarts were a <a href="/wiki/Muggle-born" title="Muggle-born">Muggle-born</a>, <a href="/wiki/Justin_Finch-Fletchley" title="Justin Finch-Fletchley">Justin Finch-Fletchley</a>, and a <a href="/wiki/Half-blood" title="Half-blood">half-blood</a>, <a href="/wiki/Hannah_Abbott" title="Hannah Abbott">Hannah Abbott</a>, and he looked out for both of them throughout his studies.<sup id="cite_ref-COS11_2-16" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COS11-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> His friendship with both of them technically made him a blood traitor by association, which may contradict on how proud he was of his wizarding lineage, or he may simply not care who he associated with. Ernie was also one of the first to openly express his support of Harry and <a href="/wiki/Albus_Dumbledore" title="Albus Dumbledore">Albus Dumbledore</a> during the Ministry's <a href="/wiki/Campaign_to_discredit_Albus_Dumbledore_and_Harry_Potter" title="Campaign to discredit Albus Dumbledore and Harry Potter">smear campaign</a> against them.<sup id="cite_ref-OP_4-10" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OP-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Magical_abilities_and_skills">Magical abilities and skills</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Ernest_Macmillan?veaction=edit&amp;section=15" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Ernest_Macmillan?action=edit&amp;section=15" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<div class="quote">
<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince">"<i>Good lesson, I thought, but Shield Charms are old hat, of course, for us old D.A. lags...</i>"</span></dd>
<dd>&#8212;Ernie's skill with the <a href="/wiki/Shield_Charm" title="Shield Charm">Shield Charm</a><sup class="noprint"><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Half-Blood_Prince" title="Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince">[src]</a></sup></dd>
<dd></dd></dl></div>
<ul><li><b><a href="/wiki/Potion" title="Potion">Potions</a></b>: Ernie was proficient in the <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magical</a> art of potion-making. He achieved either an '<a href="/wiki/Outstanding" title="Outstanding">Outstanding</a>' or an '<a href="/wiki/Exceeds_Expectations" title="Exceeds Expectations">Exceeds Expectations</a>' on his O.W.L. exam, as he was only one of twelve students able to advance to N.E.W.T.-level <a href="/wiki/Potions_(class)" title="Potions (class)">Potions</a> in <a href="/wiki/1996" title="1996">1996</a>, and was also the only <a href="/wiki/Hufflepuff" title="Hufflepuff">Hufflepuff</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP9_15-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP9-15">&#91;15&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Charm" title="Charm">Charms</a></b>: Ernie was shown to be proficient with charms, as during the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a> in his seventh year he was able to conjure a corporeal <a href="/wiki/Patronus_Charm" title="Patronus Charm">Patronus</a> (in the form of a <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Boar" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Boar">Boar</a>),<sup id="cite_ref-DH32_7-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH32-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup> which is a very advanced piece of <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magic</a>, and proof that he possessed superior magical ability, given the immense difficulty required in casting such an advanced and powerful protective charm.<sup id="cite_ref-PM_16-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PM-16">&#91;16&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Defence_Against_the_Dark_Arts" title="Defence Against the Dark Arts">Defence Against the Dark Arts</a></b>: Ernie earned an O.W.L. in this subject because of his association with <a href="/wiki/Dumbledore%27s_Army" title="Dumbledore&#39;s Army">Dumbledore's Army</a> and advanced to <a href="/wiki/Nastily_Exhausting_Wizarding_Test" title="Nastily Exhausting Wizarding Test">N.E.W.T.</a>-level in this subject. He was able to cast the <a href="/wiki/Shield_Charm" title="Shield Charm">Shield Charm</a> during the DADA N.E.W.T.s lessons proficiently thanks to his experience in the DA.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP9_15-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP9-15">&#91;15&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Duelling" title="Duelling">Duelling</a></b>: Ernie demonstrated himself to have been a highly capable duellist. He was educated in <a href="/wiki/Martial_magic" title="Martial magic">Martial magic</a> in Dumbledore's Army, and was able to <a href="/wiki/Hex" title="Hex">hex</a> <a href="/wiki/Draco_Malfoy" title="Draco Malfoy">Draco Malfoy</a>, <a href="/wiki/Gregory_Goyle" title="Gregory Goyle">Gregory Goyle</a> and <a href="/wiki/Vincent_Crabbe" title="Vincent Crabbe">Vincent Crabbe</a> into resembling <a href="/wiki/Slug" title="Slug">slugs</a> at the end of his fifth year.<sup id="cite_ref-OP_4-11" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OP-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> In his seventh year, he bravely fought in the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a> against the <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-DH_9-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup> and most likely survived, as he was not mentioned as a casualty.</li></ul>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Possessions">Possessions</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Ernest_Macmillan?veaction=edit&amp;section=16" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Possessions">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Ernest_Macmillan?action=edit&amp;section=16" title="Edit section: Possessions"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li><b><a href="/wiki/Wand" title="Wand">Wand</a></b>: Ernie owned a wand, which was of an unknown length, <a href="/wiki/Wand_wood" title="Wand wood">wood</a> and <a href="/wiki/Wand_core" title="Wand core">core</a> material. Being a British wizard, he likely purchased it from <a href="/wiki/Ollivanders" title="Ollivanders">Ollivanders</a> in <a href="/wiki/Diagon_Alley" title="Diagon Alley">Diagon Alley</a>, <a href="/wiki/London" title="London">London</a>, prior to starting his <a href="/wiki/First_year" title="First year">first year</a> at Hogwarts in <a href="/wiki/1991" title="1991">1991</a>. Ernie wielded his wand throughout his school life, and was able to perform powerful <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magic</a> with it, and used to fight in the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a> in <a href="/wiki/1998" title="1998">1998</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-DH32_7-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH32-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Prefect_badge" title="Prefect badge">Prefect badge</a></b>: Becoming a Hufflepuff <a href="/wiki/Prefect" title="Prefect">Prefect</a> at the start of the <a href="/wiki/1995%E2%80%931996_school_year" title="1995–1996 school year">1995–1996 school year</a>, Ernie would have owned and wore a Hufflepuff Prefect badge.<sup id="cite_ref-OP_4-12" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OP-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Potter_Stinks_badge" title="Potter Stinks badge">Potter Stinks badge</a></b>: Ernie owned and wore one of these derogatory enchanted Badges in <a href="/wiki/1994" title="1994">1994</a>, to show his disdain towards Harry Potter for supposedly stealing <a href="/wiki/Cedric_Diggory" title="Cedric Diggory">Cedric Diggory</a>'s limelight during the <a href="/wiki/Triwizard_Tournament" title="Triwizard Tournament">Triwizard Tournament</a> that year. He stopped wearing his badge once he became a strong supporter of Harry during the Tournament.<sup id="cite_ref-GF_13-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GF-13">&#91;13&#93;</a></sup></li></ul>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Relationships">Relationships</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Ernest_Macmillan?veaction=edit&amp;section=17" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Relationships">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Ernest_Macmillan?action=edit&amp;section=17" title="Edit section: Relationships"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Hannah_Abbott">Hannah Abbott</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Ernest_Macmillan?veaction=edit&amp;section=18" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Hannah Abbott">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Ernest_Macmillan?action=edit&amp;section=18" title="Edit section: Hannah Abbott"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<figure class="thumb tleft show-info-icon" style="width: 142px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/3/33/Abbot_cropped.PNG/revision/latest?cb=20100103014122" class="image"><img alt="" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw%3D%3D" decoding="async" width="140" height="219" class="thumbimage lazyload" data-image-name="Abbot cropped.PNG" data-image-key="Abbot_cropped.PNG" data-src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/3/33/Abbot_cropped.PNG/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/140?cb=20100103014122" /></a> 	<noscript><a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/3/33/Abbot_cropped.PNG/revision/latest?cb=20100103014122" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/3/33/Abbot_cropped.PNG/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/140?cb=20100103014122" decoding="async" width="140" height="219" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="Abbot cropped.PNG" data-image-key="Abbot_cropped.PNG" data-src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/3/33/Abbot_cropped.PNG/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/140?cb=20100103014122" /></a></noscript> 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:Abbot_cropped.PNG" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption"><a href="/wiki/Hannah_Abbott" title="Hannah Abbott">Hannah Abbott</a>, his good friend</p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure>
<p>Ernie and Hannah Abbott were close friends during their time at Hogwarts, having both been sorted into the house of Hufflepuff. They discussed the possibility of Harry being the <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Heir of Slytherin</a> together during their second year, and Hannah appeared easily influenced by Ernie's opinion.<sup id="cite_ref-CS_14-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-CS-14">&#91;14&#93;</a></sup> Both Hannah and Ernie shared the hobby of collecting <a href="/wiki/Chocolate_Frog_Card" title="Chocolate Frog Card">Chocolate Frog Cards</a>, like most of the students at Hogwarts. They both also strongly supported <a href="/wiki/Cedric_Diggory" title="Cedric Diggory">Cedric Diggory</a> as the School Champion during the <a href="/wiki/Triwizard_Tournament" title="Triwizard Tournament">Triwizard Tournament</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-GF_13-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GF-13">&#91;13&#93;</a></sup> and in 1995 they both became the Hufflepuff <a href="/wiki/Prefect" title="Prefect">prefects</a> in their fifth year.<sup id="cite_ref-OP_4-13" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OP-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Later, in the 1995-1996 year, both Ernest and Hannah Abbott joined <a href="/wiki/Dumbledore%27s_Army" title="Dumbledore&#39;s Army">Dumbledore's Army</a> and were active members of the organisation.<sup id="cite_ref-OP_4-14" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OP-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> They also fought together in the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-DH_9-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup> it can be assumed that they remained friends after the <a href="/wiki/Second_Wizarding_War" title="Second Wizarding War">Second Wizarding War</a>.
</p>

<p>Ernie's other closest friend was Justin Finch-Fletchley. During the Duelling Club in <a href="/wiki/1992" title="1992">1992</a>, he moved to protect his friend when it seemed that <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> was encouraging a snake to attack him. Believing Harry to be the Heir of Slytherin, and that Justin was his next target, he told Justin to hide in the dormitories. When Justin later turned up <a href="/wiki/Petrification" title="Petrification">petrified</a>, Ernie was very upset and angry with Harry until convinced that he was not the culprit behind the attack on his friend.<sup id="cite_ref-CS_14-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-CS-14">&#91;14&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Later, in the 1995-1996 year, both Ernest and Justin Finch-Fletchley joined <a href="/wiki/Dumbledore%27s_Army" title="Dumbledore&#39;s Army">Dumbledore's Army</a> and were active members of the organisation.<sup id="cite_ref-OP_4-15" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OP-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<p>During the <a href="/wiki/Chamber_of_Secrets" title="Chamber of Secrets">Chamber of Secrets</a> affair, Ernie held the suspicion that Harry Potter was the Heir of Slytherin when he saw him speak <a href="/wiki/Parseltongue" title="Parseltongue">Parseltongue</a> to a snake during the Duelling Club, and believed he was encouraging it to attack <a href="/wiki/Justin_Finch-Fletchley" title="Justin Finch-Fletchley">Justin Finch-Fletchley</a>. His suspicions were raised when Justin became the next victim of "<a href="/wiki/Slytherin%27s_Basilisk" class="mw-redirect" title="Slytherin&#39;s Basilisk"><i>Slytherin's Monster</i></a>". However, when <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione Granger</a> suffered the same fate, Ernie realised that it could not be Harry since he would never harm one of his best friends. To make amends, Ernie gave Harry a public apology.<sup id="cite_ref-CS_14-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-CS-14">&#91;14&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Despite generally getting along well, Ernie did follow the lead of his fellow <a href="/wiki/Hufflepuff" title="Hufflepuff">Hufflepuff</a> housemates and give Harry the cold shoulder when he was selected as the fourth <a href="/wiki/Triwizard_Tournament" title="Triwizard Tournament">Triwizard Champion</a> in 1994. Since <a href="/wiki/Cedric_Diggory" title="Cedric Diggory">Cedric Diggory</a> had been the chosen <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts</a> Champion, he felt, like many others in the school, that Harry was taking away Hufflepuff's chance for honour and glory.<sup id="cite_ref-GF_13-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GF-13">&#91;13&#93;</a></sup> However, with the tragic events surrounding the Third Task, Ernie believed Harry's story that <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a> had returned and publicly proclaimed his support despite the <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">Ministry of Magic</a> and the <i><a href="/wiki/Daily_Prophet" title="Daily Prophet">Daily Prophet</a></i> calling him a liar.<sup id="cite_ref-OP_4-16" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OP-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> Ernie's support of Harry was so strong that he was a staunch supporter of <a href="/wiki/Dumbledore%27s_Army" title="Dumbledore&#39;s Army">Dumbledore's Army</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-OP_4-17" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OP-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> and led the call to arms during the meeting of students in the Great Hall prior to the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-DH_9-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<ul><li>Ernie was born between April 21st and September 1st, as like <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> and <a href="/wiki/Draco_Malfoy" title="Draco Malfoy">Draco Malfoy</a>, he was unable to take the <a href="/wiki/Apparition_(class)" title="Apparition (class)">Apparition test</a> with the rest of his <a href="/wiki/Sixth_year" title="Sixth year">sixth year</a> class due to not yet being of-age.</li>
<li>Ernie was played by <a href="/wiki/Louis_Doyle" title="Louis Doyle">Louis Doyle</a> in the 2002's film adaption of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Chamber_of_Secrets" title="Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets">Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets</a></i> and returned three years later for 2005's <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Goblet_of_Fire" title="Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire">Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire</a></i>. Louis Doyle also provided the voice for the character in the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Order_of_the_Phoenix_(video_game)" title="Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix (video game)">video game adaptation</a> of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Order_of_the_Phoenix" title="Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix">Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix</a></i>. In <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Deathly_Hallows:_Part_2" title="Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 2">Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 2</a></i>, he is portrayed by <a href="/wiki/Jamie_Marks" title="Jamie Marks">Jamie Marks</a>.</li>
<li>Ernie's last name was misspelt in the closing credits for <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Chamber_of_Secrets_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets (film)">Harry Potter and Chamber of Secrets</a></i> as "MacMillan", but was corrected for <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Goblet_of_Fire_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire (film)">Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire</a></i>.</li>
<li>Ernie Macmillan once borrowed <i><a href="/wiki/Quidditch_Through_the_Ages" title="Quidditch Through the Ages">Quidditch Through the Ages</a></i> from the <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Library" title="Hogwarts Library">Hogwarts Library</a>, and was due to return the book on <a href="/wiki/12_August" title="12 August">12 August</a>.</li>
<li>In <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Prisoner_of_Azkaban" title="Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban">Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban</a></i>, Ernie's last name is misspelt as "McMillan".</li>
<li>In <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Chamber_of_Secrets" title="Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets">Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets</a></i>, Ernie's role is diminished to a non-speaking part. The part where he and Hannah Abbott talk in the library about their theory of Harry wanting to attack Justin Finch-Fletchley was cut, but appears on the deleted scenes.</li>
<li>In the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Half-Blood_Prince_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince (film)">film adaptation</a> of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Half-Blood_Prince" title="Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince">Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince</a></i>, Ernie is omitted. However, one of his lines is spoken instead by <a href="/wiki/Katie_Bell" title="Katie Bell">Katie Bell</a>.</li>
<li>Ernie is likely full or part Scottish, as his surname of Macmillan is a fairly common Scottish surname.</li></ul>
