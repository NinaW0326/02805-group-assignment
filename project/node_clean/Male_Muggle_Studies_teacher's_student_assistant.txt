</p><p>He loved <a href="/wiki/Muggle_Studies" title="Muggle Studies">Muggle Studies</a> class and worked in the capacity of an assistant to <a href="/wiki/Unidentified_male_Muggle_Studies_teacher" title="Unidentified male Muggle Studies teacher">the Muggle Studies Professor</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-CS_GBC_4-12" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-CS_GBC-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>

<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="1992">1992</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Male_Muggle_Studies_teacher%27s_student_assistant?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: 1992">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Male_Muggle_Studies_teacher%27s_student_assistant?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: 1992"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>When <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> gained possession of a torch during his second year, he thought he should give it to <a href="/wiki/Unidentified_male_Muggle_Studies_teacher" title="Unidentified male Muggle Studies teacher">the Muggle Studies teacher</a>. Finding out he was absent from his <a href="/wiki/Muggle_Studies_Classroom" title="Muggle Studies Classroom">classroom</a>, he gave it to his assistant. In exchange for the torch, the assistant gave Harry a harmonica.<sup id="cite_ref-CS_GBC_4-13" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-CS_GBC-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
