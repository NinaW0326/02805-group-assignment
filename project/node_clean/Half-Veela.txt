A <b>half-Veela</b> is a cross-breed between a <a href="/wiki/Human" title="Human">human</a> (normally a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard or witch</a>) and a <a href="/wiki/Veela" title="Veela">Veela</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-PM_2-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PM-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> It is unknown if Veela can breed with <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggles</a>, and it is unlikely that a Muggle would ever learn of their existence, thus most or all half-Veela will inherit <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magical</a> ability from their human parent.
</p>

<figure class="thumb tright show-info-icon" style="width: 142px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/a/a6/Fleur_Dealcour_promo.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20131203090347" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/a/a6/Fleur_Dealcour_promo.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/140?cb=20131203090347" decoding="async" width="140" height="210" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="Fleur Dealcour promo.jpg" data-image-key="Fleur_Dealcour_promo.jpg" /></a> 	 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:Fleur_Dealcour_promo.jpg" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption"><a href="/wiki/Fleur_Delacour" title="Fleur Delacour">Fleur Delacour</a>, a part-Veela</p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure>
<p>From their Veela parent, they gain some Veela traits, such as breathtaking beauty and the ability to <a href="/wiki/Veela_Magic" title="Veela Magic">entrance</a> men <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magically</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-GOF15_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF15-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> It is unknown if half-Veela are able to shape-shift into <a href="/wiki/Bird" title="Bird">bird</a>-like creatures as full Veela can.<sup id="cite_ref-GOF8_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF8-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Prejudice">Prejudice</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Half-Veela?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Prejudice">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Half-Veela?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Prejudice"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Because they are <a href="/wiki/Half-breed" title="Half-breed">half-breeds</a>, half-Veela may experience the same prejudices that befall other half-breeds, though they are more difficult to detect than other half-breeds, such as <a href="/wiki/Half-giant" title="Half-giant">Half-giants</a>, because apart from their great physical beauty, they are otherwise identical to humans.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Gender">Gender</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Half-Veela?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Gender">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Half-Veela?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Gender"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>

</p>
