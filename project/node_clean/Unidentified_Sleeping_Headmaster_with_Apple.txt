This <b>wizard</b> was appointed <a href="/wiki/Headmaster" title="Headmaster">Headmaster</a> of <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts</a> before <a href="/wiki/Armando_Dippet" title="Armando Dippet">Professor Dippet</a>. After his death, his <a href="/wiki/Portrait" title="Portrait">portrait</a> was hung in the <a href="/wiki/Headmaster%27s_office" class="mw-redirect" title="Headmaster&#39;s office">Headmaster's office</a>, in order to give advice to the current head. He was portrayed to have been sleeping on an chair, and there is an <a href="/wiki/Apple_(fruit)" title="Apple (fruit)">apple</a> and a teapot on the table beside it.<sup id="cite_ref-COSF_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COSF-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-HBPF_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBPF-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-DHF2_4-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DHF2-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-MHP_5-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-MHP-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>
