<b>Sasabonsams</b> are vampiric, spindle-legged creatures. They are used as mascots for the <a href="/wiki/Nigerian_National_Quidditch_team" title="Nigerian National Quidditch team">Nigerian National Quidditch team</a>. During the mascot attacks at the <a href="/wiki/2014_Quidditch_World_Cup" title="2014 Quidditch World Cup">2014 Quidditch World Cup</a>, the Sasabonsams became crazed due to the amount of <a href="/wiki/Blood" title="Blood">blood</a> being spilled and started to attack as well.<sup id="cite_ref-PM_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PM-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="History">History</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Sasabonsam?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: History">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Sasabonsam?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: History"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Jamaican <a href="/wiki/Keeper" title="Keeper">Keeper</a> <a href="/wiki/Kquewanda_Bailey" title="Kquewanda Bailey">Kquewanda Bailey</a>'s sudden unconsciousness in the middle of the <a href="/wiki/Jamaican_National_Quidditch_team" title="Jamaican National Quidditch team">Jamaica</a> versus <a href="/wiki/American_National_Quidditch_team" title="American National Quidditch team">USA</a> match was, at first, thought to have been some sort of magical interference from someone in the crowd, but the <a href="/wiki/International_Confederation_of_Wizards%27_Quidditch_Committee" class="mw-redirect" title="International Confederation of Wizards&#39; Quidditch Committee">ICWQC</a> duly confirmed that Bailey was suffering from an infected Sasabonsam bite sustained during the opening ceremony, ruling out any foul play.<sup id="cite_ref-usa-v-jam_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-usa-v-jam-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-usa-v-jam2_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-usa-v-jam2-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>In <a href="/wiki/June" title="June">June</a> <a href="/wiki/2014" title="2014">2014</a>, notices were put up in the <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Houses" title="Hogwarts Houses">house</a> <a href="/wiki/Common_room" title="Common room">common rooms</a> at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> stating that the school nurse would not view kindly students who claimed to have infected Sasabonsam bites in order to be excused from classes<sup id="cite_ref-HC_4-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HC-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Sasabonsam?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Sasabonsam?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>The <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Asanbosam" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Asanbosam">Sasabonsam</a> is a creature from West African folkfore. Alternatively depicted as either a vampire or an <a href="/wiki/Ogre" title="Ogre">ogre</a>, the creature has iron teeth and lives in trees, perched on its spindly legs. They are known mostly in Ghana and surrounding territories. Their name comes from the Akan word "Bonsam", meaning "devil".</li>
<li>Sasabonsams are not mentioned in the fifty-second edition of <i><a href="/wiki/Fantastic_Beasts_and_Where_to_Find_Them" title="Fantastic Beasts and Where to Find Them">Fantastic Beasts and Where to Find Them</a></i>. It's possible that they weren't discovered until after 1991. Another possibility is that, like vampires, they are classified as beings.</li></ul>
