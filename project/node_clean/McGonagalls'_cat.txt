The <b>McGonagalls' cat</b> was a pet of the <a href="/wiki/McGonagall_family" title="McGonagall family">McGonagall family</a>. <a href="/wiki/Isobel_Ross" title="Isobel Ross">Isobel McGonagall</a> learned her daughter <a href="/wiki/Minerva_McGonagall" title="Minerva McGonagall">Minerva</a> was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch</a> when she started displaying small, but unmistakable, signs of magic, such as the cat doing the infant's bidding before she could even talk.<sup id="cite_ref-pottermore_1-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-pottermore-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>It is interesting to note that <a href="/wiki/Minerva_McGonagall" title="Minerva McGonagall">Minerva McGonagall</a> had an affinity with cats since she was an infant; in her adulthood, both Minerva's <a href="/wiki/Patronus_Charm" title="Patronus Charm">Patronus</a><sup id="cite_ref-DH30_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH30-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> and <a href="/wiki/Animagus" title="Animagus">Animagus</a> form<sup id="cite_ref-POA6_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-POA6-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> was that of a cat.
</p>
