This <b><a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch</a></b> was the grandmother of <a href="/wiki/Constance_Pickering" title="Constance Pickering">Constance Pickering</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-text_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-text-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>She attended <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> in her youth and was a member of <a href="/wiki/Hufflepuff" title="Hufflepuff">Hufflepuff</a> house.<sup id="cite_ref-text_2-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-text-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>When Constance was ten years old, her grandmother came over for <a href="/wiki/Christmas" title="Christmas">Christmas</a>. She had a <a href="/wiki/Badger" title="Badger">badger</a> brooch, that Constance pilfered to play with. In punishment, Constance spent the rest of Christmas locked in the attic.<sup id="cite_ref-text_2-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-text-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
