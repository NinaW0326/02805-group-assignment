<p><b>Sir Winogrand</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> and an <a href="/wiki/Herbologist" title="Herbologist">herbologist</a> who kept a wide collection of water plants in the <a href="/wiki/African_Lakes" title="African Lakes">African Lakes</a>. Sometime before <a href="/wiki/1964" title="1964">1964</a>, <a href="/wiki/Selina_Sapworthy" title="Selina Sapworthy">Selina Sapworthy</a> accessed his collection and based on its large magical and scientific potential, wrote a book called <i><a href="/wiki/Winogrand%27s_Wondrous_Water_Plants" title="Winogrand&#39;s Wondrous Water Plants">Winogrand's Wondrous Water Plants</a></i>. The book has been awarded the <a href="/wiki/Herbology_Book_of_the_Year" title="Herbology Book of the Year">Herbology Book of the Year</a> by the <a href="/wiki/Herbology_High_Commission" title="Herbology High Commission">Herbology High Commission</a>, and it has been part of the required reading at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> for at least twenty-five years.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
