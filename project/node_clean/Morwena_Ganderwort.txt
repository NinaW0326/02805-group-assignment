<b>Morwena Ganderwort</b>, also known as "The <a href="/wiki/Snake" title="Snake">Snake</a>", was an <a href="/wiki/United_States_of_America" title="United States of America">American</a> <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizarding</a> criminal who operated in the <a href="/wiki/1920s" title="1920s">1920s</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-empiremagvid_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-empiremagvid-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>The <a href="/wiki/Magical_Congress_of_the_United_States_of_America" title="Magical Congress of the United States of America">Magical Congress of the United States of America</a> issued a wanted poster for her in or around <a href="/wiki/1926" title="1926">1926</a> for the crimes of exploitation of minor <a href="/wiki/House-elf" title="House-elf">Elves</a> and <a href="/wiki/Muggle" class="mw-redirect" title="Muggle">No-Maj</a> murder and offered as reward of 3659 <a href="/wiki/Dragot" title="Dragot">dragots</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
