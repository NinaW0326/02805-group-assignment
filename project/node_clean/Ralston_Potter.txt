<b>Ralston Potter</b> was a member of the <a href="/wiki/Wizengamot" title="Wizengamot">Wizengamot</a> from <a href="/wiki/1612" class="mw-redirect" title="1612">1612</a> to <a href="/wiki/1652" class="mw-redirect" title="1652">1652</a>. He was a staunch advocate of the <a href="/wiki/International_Statute_of_Wizarding_Secrecy" title="International Statute of Wizarding Secrecy">International Statute of Wizarding Secrecy</a>, in contrast to his more militant peers, who wished to declare war against <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggles</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-Pottermore_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Pottermore-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
