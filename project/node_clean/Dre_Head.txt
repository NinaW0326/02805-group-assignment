<p><b>Dre Head</b><sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> was a <a href="/wiki/Shrunken_head" title="Shrunken head">Shrunken head</a>. He had a Jamaican accent, and long dreadlocks. Dre Head was used as an accessory on the <a href="/wiki/Knight_Bus" title="Knight Bus">Knight Bus</a> in <a href="/wiki/August" title="August">August</a> <a href="/wiki/1993" title="1993">1993</a>. He spoke with passengers and frequently warned the driver, <a href="/wiki/Ernest_Prang" title="Ernest Prang">Ernie Prang</a>, about obstacles, and hazards like <i>"Ernie! Little old lady at 12 o'clock!"</i><sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p class="mw-empty-elt"></p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Personality_and_traits">Personality and traits</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Dre_Head?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Personality and traits">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Dre_Head?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Personality and traits"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Dre Head was very observant. He liked to say jokes like <i>"Mind your head"</i> or <i>"Hey, guys? Guys? Why the <b>long faces</b>?"</i>. He messed up his counting sometimes, as he once said "5 ,4, 3, 3&#189;, 2".
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Dre_Head?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Dre_Head?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Dre_Head?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Dre_Head?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>Dre Head was voiced by <a href="/wiki/Lenny_Henry" title="Lenny Henry">Lenny Henry</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Prisoner_of_Azkaban_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban (film)">film adaptation</a> of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Prisoner_of_Azkaban" title="Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban">Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban</a></i>.</li>
<li>One of the special features on disc 2 of the <i>Prisoner of Azkaban</i> DVD was a set of cast interviews conducted by British broadcaster <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Johnny_Vaughn" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Johnny Vaughn">Johnny Vaughn</a> and Dre Head. In these interviews, Dre showed awareness that the <i>Harry Potter</i> films were works of fiction, asking on several occasions if he could play a major character in later films. Despite this, he was afraid of the actors playing the <a href="/wiki/Dursley_family" title="Dursley family">Dursley family</a>, seeming unwilling to accept that it was merely their actors rather than the actual Dursleys.</li>
<li>Dre Head counts "<i>10... 9... 8... 7... 6... 5... 4... 3... 3½... 2... 1¾... <b>YES!</b></i>" This is incorrect, as 3<i>½</i> would come before 3.</li>
<li>On the DVD main menu, Dre Head is entirely animated rather than just animating the mouth.</li>
<li>He bears a slight resemblance to one of the <a href="/wiki/Shrunken_heads_in_the_Three_Broomsticks" title="Shrunken heads in the Three Broomsticks">Shrunken heads in the Three Broomsticks</a>.</li></ul>
