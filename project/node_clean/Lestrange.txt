<p><b>Lestrange</b> (born c. <a href="/wiki/1926" title="1926">1926</a>) was a <a href="/wiki/Pure-blood" title="Pure-blood">pure-blood</a> <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> and a <a href="/wiki/Slytherin" title="Slytherin">Slytherin</a> student at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a>. He was a member of <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Tom Riddle</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle%27s_gang" title="Tom Riddle&#39;s gang">gang</a> when they attended Hogwarts, and a member of the <a href="/wiki/Slug_Club" title="Slug Club">Slug Club</a>.
</p>

<figure class="thumb tleft show-info-icon" style="width: 252px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/0/09/Young_Death_Eaters.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20180720142700" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/0/09/Young_Death_Eaters.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/250?cb=20180720142700" decoding="async" width="250" height="104" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="Young Death Eaters.jpg" data-image-key="Young_Death_Eaters.jpg" /></a> 	 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:Young_Death_Eaters.jpg" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption">Lestrange at a <a href="/wiki/Slug_Club" title="Slug Club">Slug Club</a> supper with his school friends <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Tom Riddle</a>, <a href="/wiki/Avery_(Riddle-era)" class="mw-redirect" title="Avery (Riddle-era)">Avery</a> and others</p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure>
<p>Lestrange attended <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a>, and was sorted into <a href="/wiki/Slytherin" title="Slytherin">Slytherin</a> house, eventually becoming a member of <a href="/wiki/Professor" title="Professor">Professor</a> <a href="/wiki/Horace_Slughorn" title="Horace Slughorn">Horace Slughorn</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Slug_Club" title="Slug Club">Slug Club</a>. He was also a member of <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Tom Riddle</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle%27s_gang" title="Tom Riddle&#39;s gang">gang</a> when they were classmates. During his time at school, Lestrange forgot to turn in a <a href="/wiki/Potions_(class)" title="Potions (class)">Potions</a> essay for <a href="/wiki/Horace_Slughorn" title="Horace Slughorn">Professor Slughorn</a> and was nearly put in detention.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP17_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP17-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-HBP23_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP23-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
