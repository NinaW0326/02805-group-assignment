<dd>&#8212;Cormac threatening the <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a><sup class="noprint"><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Deathly_Hallows:_Part_1" title="Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 1">[src]</a></sup></dd>

<p><b>Mr McLaggen</b> was the father of <a href="/wiki/Cormac_McLaggen" title="Cormac McLaggen">Cormac McLaggen</a>. On <a href="/wiki/1_September" title="1 September">1 September</a>, <a href="/wiki/1997" title="1997">1997</a>, when <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a> <a href="/wiki/Raid_on_the_Hogwarts_Express" title="Raid on the Hogwarts Express">stopped</a> the <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Express" title="Hogwarts Express">Hogwarts Express</a> en route to <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> and boarded it to search for <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>, Cormac threatened them that his father would hear about their actions. Death Eaters, however, completely ignored Cormac and continued their search.<sup id="cite_ref-DHF1_1-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DHF1-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
