<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Bertha_Jorkins%27s_aunt?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Bertha_Jorkins%27s_aunt?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Bertha planned to visit her on an Albanian vacation in <a href="/wiki/1994" title="1994">1994</a>, right after she saw a <a href="/wiki/Bertha_Jorkins%27_second_cousin" class="mw-redirect" title="Bertha Jorkins&#39; second cousin">second cousin</a>, who resided to the north.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> However, on the way to her aunt's, Bertha stopped at an inn. She ran into <a href="/wiki/Peter_Pettigrew" title="Peter Pettigrew">Peter Pettigrew</a> there, and, after being convinced to take a walk with him, she was kidnapped and taken to <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a>, who eventually murdered her.
</p>
