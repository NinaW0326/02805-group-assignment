<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Post-mortem">Post-mortem</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Limebert?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Post-mortem">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Limebert?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Post-mortem"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Professor Limebert died sometime in or before <a href="/wiki/1992" title="1992">1992</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-DOD_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DOD-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> A portrait of him was hung in the <a href="/wiki/Headmaster%27s_office" class="mw-redirect" title="Headmaster&#39;s office">Headmaster's office</a> at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> in order to give advice to the current Head.<sup id="cite_ref-HeadPortrait_2-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HeadPortrait-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
