<p><b>Silas Crump</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Great_Britain" title="Great Britain">British</a>&#160;<a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> and petty criminal. 
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Silas_Crump?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Silas_Crump?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>During his career as a criminal, he had been evading capture by Officer <a href="/wiki/Cerberus_Langarm" title="Cerberus Langarm">Cerberus Langarm</a> from the <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">British Ministry of Magic</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Department_of_Magical_Law_Enforcement" title="Department of Magical Law Enforcement">Department of Magical Law Enforcement</a>. This was due to the fact that he had never been caught red-handed.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Crump may have been in <a href="/wiki/Slytherin" title="Slytherin">Slytherin House</a> at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> as there was a Slytherin scarf in his rented room at the <a href="/wiki/Leaky_Cauldron" title="Leaky Cauldron">Leaky Cauldron</a>. His cunning and highly resourceful personality would also seem to support this. There was also a <a href="/wiki/Chudley_Cannons" title="Chudley Cannons">Chudley Cannons</a> flag in his room, so he was most&#160;likely a supporter of them.
</p><p>He owned a <a class="new" title="Silas Crump&#39;s locked belt (page does not exist)" rel="nofollow" data-uncrawlable-url="L3dpa2kvU2lsYXNfQ3J1bXAlMjdzX2xvY2tlZF9iZWx0P2FjdGlvbj1lZGl0JnJlZGxpbms9MQ==">locked belt</a> which was found in the <a href="/wiki/London_sewers" title="London sewers">London sewers</a>. He would use this to restrain himself during the full <a href="/wiki/Moon" title="Moon">moon</a> as he was an unregistered <a href="/wiki/Werewolf" title="Werewolf">Werewolf</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
