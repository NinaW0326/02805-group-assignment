This <b>unidentified</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> who was seen leading a group of students on the <a href="/wiki/First-floor_corridor" title="First-floor corridor">First-floor corridor</a> before the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a> along with a <a href="/wiki/Unidentified_woman_in_a_red_dress_during_the_Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Unidentified woman in a red dress during the Battle of Hogwarts">witch in a red dress</a> and a <a href="/wiki/Unidentified_man_in_a_grey_suit_during_the_Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Unidentified man in a grey suit during the Battle of Hogwarts">man in a grey suit</a>. It is unknown if he survived.<sup id="cite_ref-DHF2_1-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DHF2-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
