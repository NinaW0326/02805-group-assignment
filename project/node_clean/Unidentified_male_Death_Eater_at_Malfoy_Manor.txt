<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_male_Death_Eater_at_Malfoy_Manor?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_male_Death_Eater_at_Malfoy_Manor?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>He was one of the many Death Eaters present at the <a href="/wiki/Meeting_at_Malfoy_Manor" title="Meeting at Malfoy Manor">meeting</a> in <a href="/wiki/Malfoy_Manor" title="Malfoy Manor">Malfoy Manor</a>. He was sitting in front of <a href="/wiki/Amycus_Carrow" title="Amycus Carrow">Amycus Carrow</a> during the meeting. When <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a> asked one of his followers to hand over their <a href="/wiki/Wand" title="Wand">wand</a>, he remained silent in the deeply terrifying and malevolent presence of his Dark Lord.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>This Death Eater may have taken part in the Battle of Hogwarts on the <a href="/wiki/2_May" title="2 May">2 May</a>, <a href="/wiki/1998" title="1998">1998</a>, although this is unconfirmed, as he may have died beforehand.
</p>
