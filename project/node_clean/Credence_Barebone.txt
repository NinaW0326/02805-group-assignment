<p><b>Credence Barebone</b> (allegedly born <b>Aurelius Dumbledore</b>; c. <a href="/wiki/1901" title="1901">1901</a><sup id="cite_ref-birth_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-birth-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>) was an <a href="/wiki/United_States_of_America" title="United States of America">American</a> <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> who lived during the <a href="/wiki/20th_century" title="20th century">20th century</a>. He was adopted by <a href="/wiki/Mary_Lou_Barebone" title="Mary Lou Barebone">Mary Lou Barebone</a>, the leader of a <a href="/wiki/Muggle" class="mw-redirect" title="Muggle">No-Maj</a> anti-<a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">witchcraft</a> group called the <a href="/wiki/New_Salem_Philanthropic_Society" title="New Salem Philanthropic Society">New Salem Philanthropic Society</a>. Due to the repression of his <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magic</a>, Credence developed a&#160;<a href="/wiki/Dark_Arts" title="Dark Arts">dark</a> parasitic&#160;force known as an <a href="/wiki/Obscurus" title="Obscurus">Obscurus</a> and became an <a href="/wiki/Obscurial" title="Obscurial">Obscurial</a>. While most Obscurials die before their tenth birthday, Credence lived into adulthood, which was previously unheard of in the <a href="/wiki/Wizarding_world" title="Wizarding world">wizarding world</a>, and a testament of his incredible power. 
</p><p>Credence was sought after by <a href="/wiki/Gellert_Grindelwald" title="Gellert Grindelwald">Gellert Grindelwald</a>, who wanted to use his power to kill <a href="/wiki/Albus_Dumbledore" title="Albus Dumbledore">Albus Dumbledore</a>, who he saw as the greatest threat to his <a href="/wiki/Global_wizarding_war" title="Global wizarding war">revolutionary cause</a>. While in search of his true identity, Credence heeded Grindelwald's call and joined <a href="/wiki/Alliance" title="Alliance">his army</a>.
</p>

<figure class="thumb tleft show-info-icon" style="width: 222px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/2/29/20190102_151111.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20190102141324" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/2/29/20190102_151111.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/220?cb=20190102141324" decoding="async" width="220" height="90" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="20190102 151111.jpg" data-image-key="20190102_151111.jpg" /></a> 	 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:20190102_151111.jpg" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption">Young Leta Lestrange with infant Aurelius in her arms</p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure>
<p>Credence Barebone was born around <a href="/wiki/1901" title="1901">1901</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-birth_1-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-birth-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> allegedly to the <a href="/wiki/Dumbledore_family" title="Dumbledore family">Dumbledore family</a>, as Aurelius Dumbledore, according to <a href="/wiki/Gellert_Grindelwald" title="Gellert Grindelwald">Gellert Grindelwald</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>In <a href="/wiki/1901" title="1901">1901</a>, Credence was taken by <a href="/wiki/Credence_Barebone%27s_aunt" title="Credence Barebone&#39;s aunt">his aunt</a> on a ship heading to America. He was switched in the crib by a young <a href="/wiki/Leta_Lestrange" title="Leta Lestrange">Leta Lestrange</a> for her half-brother <a href="/wiki/Corvus_Lestrange_(V)" title="Corvus Lestrange (V)">Corvus</a> because he was crying.<sup id="cite_ref-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup> The ship later sank during a storm and Credence was taken by Leta and <a href="/wiki/Irma_Dugard" title="Irma Dugard">Irma Dugard</a> to a lifeboat. Their lifeboat sailed to safety, but the lifeboat his aunt and Corvus took capsized, drowning them both. Baby Credence was taken to <a href="/wiki/United_States_of_America" title="United States of America">America</a> and put up for adoption in Corvus's place, unbeknownst to anyone but Leta.<sup id="cite_ref-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span id="Adopted_(1905-1926)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="Adopted_.281905-1926.29">Adopted (1905-1926)</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Credence_Barebone?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Adopted (1905-1926)">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Credence_Barebone?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Adopted (1905-1926)"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<div class="quote">
<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;Fantastic Beasts and Where to Find Them">"<i>His name's Credence. His mother beats him. She beats all those kids she adopted, but she seems to hate him the most.</i>"</span></dd>
<dd>&#8212;<a href="/wiki/Porpentina_Goldstein" title="Porpentina Goldstein">Tina Goldstein</a> regarding Credence's adoptive family life<sup class="noprint"><a href="/wiki/Fantastic_Beasts_and_Where_to_Find_Them" title="Fantastic Beasts and Where to Find Them">[src]</a></sup></dd>
<dd></dd></dl></div><figure class="thumb tright show-info-icon" style="width: 252px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/e/eb/Barebone_family.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20161209172705" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/e/eb/Barebone_family.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/250?cb=20161209172705" decoding="async" width="250" height="323" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="Barebone family.jpg" data-image-key="Barebone_family.jpg" /></a> 	 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:Barebone_family.jpg" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption">Credence with his adopted family</p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure>
<p>On <a href="/wiki/21_October" title="21 October">21 October</a> <a href="/wiki/1905" title="1905">1905</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-birth_1-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-birth-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> he was adopted by <a href="/wiki/Mary_Lou_Barebone" title="Mary Lou Barebone">Mary Lou Barebone</a>, an American <a href="/wiki/Muggle" class="mw-redirect" title="Muggle">No-Maj</a> woman who led the <a href="/wiki/New_Salem_Philanthropic_Society" title="New Salem Philanthropic Society">New Salem Philanthropic Society</a>, a hardline anti-<a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">witchcraft</a> group.<sup id="cite_ref-FB88_9-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-FB88-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup> She renamed him Credence <a href="/wiki/Barebone_family" title="Barebone family">Barebone</a>, raising him in <a href="/wiki/New_York" title="New York">New York</a> alongside her two other adopted children, <a href="/wiki/Chastity_Barebone" title="Chastity Barebone">Chastity</a> and <a href="/wiki/Modesty_Barebone" title="Modesty Barebone">Modesty</a>. Credence was the eldest of the three.
</p><p>Whilst under Mary Lou's care, Credence was physically and mentally abused by her who attempted to suppress his magical abilities. She often used his own belt that left marks on his hands. The frequent abuse would cause him to become an <a href="/wiki/Obscurial" title="Obscurial">Obscurial</a>.
</p><p>Mary Lou considered Credence's birth mother to be a "<i>wicked, unnatural woman</i>", suggesting that she was aware of his heritage.<sup id="cite_ref-FB88_9-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-FB88-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>During one meeting, Mary Lou was physically abusing Credence but was magically assaulted by <a href="/wiki/Auror" title="Auror">Auror</a> <a href="/wiki/Porpentina_Goldstein" title="Porpentina Goldstein">Tina Goldstein</a>. The open use of magic in front of a group of <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">non-magic people</a> required extensive <a href="/wiki/Memory_Charm" title="Memory Charm">Obliviation</a> and was a major scandal resulting in Porpentina's demotion to the <a href="/wiki/Wand_Permit_Office" title="Wand Permit Office">Wand Permit Office</a>.
</p>

<p>Following this, Credence hid with Nagini at <a href="/wiki/18_Rue_Philippe_Lorand" title="18 Rue Philippe Lorand">18 Rue Philippe Lorand</a>, being followed by <a href="/wiki/Gunnar_Grimmson" title="Gunnar Grimmson">Gunnar Grimmson</a>, the bounty hunter. At that location Credence found&#160;<a href="/wiki/Irma_Dugard" title="Irma Dugard">Irma Dugard</a>, the <a href="/wiki/Irma_Dugard" title="Irma Dugard">half-elf</a>, whom he believed to be his mother, as her name was on his adoption papers. Dugard revealed that she did not want to give him up, and noted that she was merely a servant to the <a href="/wiki/Lestrange_family" title="Lestrange family">Lestrange</a> household. &#160;&#160;
</p><p>After a brief moment of tenderness, Grimmson trapped Nagini in the wall to <a href="/wiki/Killing_Curse" title="Killing Curse">kill</a> Irma on Grindelwald's orders to keep Credence's identity a secret from him. The Obscurus manifested and Credence attempted to kill Grimmson, but failed to do so.&#160;&#160;

Credence, ready to learn the truth, accepted the map to the <a href="/wiki/Cimeti%C3%A8re_du_P%C3%A8re-Lachaise" title="Cimetière du Père-Lachaise">Pere Lachaise Cemetery</a> given by the Dark Wizard.

Wanting to relieve herself of her screaming brother, Leta swapped him with another baby on the ship, that baby being Credence.&#160;The ship sank before Leta could switch the babies&#160;back, and the real Corvus Lestrange drowned.

<p>He has black hair, brown eyes, and pale skin.<sup id="cite_ref-11" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-11">&#91;11&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-12" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-12">&#91;12&#93;</a></sup> Credence Barebone often appeared to be troubled, nervous, or embarrassed.<sup id="cite_ref-13" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-13">&#91;13&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-14" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-14">&#91;14&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-15" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-15">&#91;15&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-16" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-16">&#91;16&#93;</a></sup> He tends to stand with his head bowed.
</p>

</p><p>After being nearly killed by over 20 <a href="/wiki/Magical_Congress_of_the_United_States_of_America" title="Magical Congress of the United States of America">MACUSA</a> Aurors on the orders of <a href="/wiki/Seraphina_Picquery" title="Seraphina Picquery">President Picquery</a>, Credence became embittered towards most of the magical community, and, desperate to survive, and learn his true heritage, he joined the <a href="/wiki/Circus_Arcanus" title="Circus Arcanus">Circus Arcanus</a>, and befriended fellow outcast <a href="/wiki/Nagini" title="Nagini">Nagini</a>. However, Grindelwald was once again able to manipulate Credence - taking advantage of the youth's desire to learn of his true heritage, Grindelwald convinced Credence to join <a href="/wiki/Gellert_Grindelwald%27s_acolytes" title="Gellert Grindelwald&#39;s acolytes">his acolytes</a>, convincing Credence so effectively that even Nagini was unable to dissuade him. After finally being given <a href="/wiki/Credence_Barebone%27s_wand" title="Credence Barebone&#39;s wand">a wand</a> by his new master at <a href="/wiki/Nurmengard_Castle" title="Nurmengard Castle">Nurmengard Castle</a>, Credence accepted his true heritage as Aurelius Dumbledore, and seemingly agreed to help Grindelwald bypass the blood pact and kill <a href="/wiki/Albus_Dumbledore" title="Albus Dumbledore">Albus Dumbledore</a>.
</p>

<ul><li><b><a href="/wiki/Obscurus" title="Obscurus">Obscurus</a> <a href="/wiki/Transformation" title="Transformation">Transformation</a></b>: Credence was an Obscurial, prone to unleashing or embodying an Obscurus due to years of being forced to suppress his magic. He was immensely more powerful than other known Obscurials, living longer than any previously known, and requiring over 20 <a href="/wiki/Magical_Congress_of_the_United_States_of_America" title="Magical Congress of the United States of America">MACUSA</a> Aurors unleashing destructive spells to nearly destroy Credence, though he still managed to survive. Ever since then, Credence had acquired complete control over his Obscurus, being far more precise with his Obcurus attacks than before while fighting <a href="/wiki/Gunnar_Grimmson" title="Gunnar Grimmson">Gunnar Grimmson</a>, with the <a href="/wiki/Bounty_hunter" title="Bounty hunter">bounty hunter</a> being forced to constantly hold up a <a href="/wiki/Shield_Charm" title="Shield Charm">Shield Charm</a>, and Apparate away to safety. <a href="/wiki/Gellert_Grindelwald" title="Gellert Grindelwald">Gellert Grindelwald</a> went so far as to claim to <a href="/wiki/Gellert_Grindelwald%27s_acolytes" title="Gellert Grindelwald&#39;s acolytes">his acolytes</a> that Credence's Obscurus was the only being apart from himself powerful enough to defeat and kill <a href="/wiki/Albus_Dumbledore" title="Albus Dumbledore">Albus Dumbledore</a>, the so-called greatest wizard of all time, marking him the most powerful Obscurial to have ever existed.</li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">Magical</a> aptitude</b>: While Credence had not initially developed his magical abilities due to <a href="/wiki/Mary_Lou_Barebone" title="Mary Lou Barebone">Mary Lou Barebone</a>, his latent magical powers were incredible, which allowed him to survive long past the age of 10, despite having an <a href="/wiki/Obscurus" title="Obscurus">Obscurus</a> inside him. This was possibly due to Credence being a member of the <a href="/wiki/Dumbledore_family" title="Dumbledore family">Dumbledore family</a>. After being given a wand for the first time, Credence instinctively cast a spell that fired a bolt of fiery energy so powerful that it instantly destroyed a mountainside.</li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Wand" title="Wand">Wand</a> versatility</b>: Credence was able to effectively use the wand gifted to him by Grindelwald, without having been officially chosen by said wand, and Credence was even able to instinctively cast an extremely powerful spell from that wand, pulverising a mountainside.</li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Apparition" title="Apparition">Apparition</a></b>: Although where he learned how to Apparate was unknown (though possibly during the travels that led to him joining the <a href="/wiki/Circus_Arcanus" title="Circus Arcanus">Circus Arcanus</a>), Credence was able to Apparate away from Grindelwald's rally in <a href="/wiki/Paris" title="Paris">Paris</a>.</li></ul>

<p>Credence feared his adoptive mother due to years of emotional and physical abuse. She beat him with the intent of teaching him to suppress and be ashamed of his magical traits. By suppressing his <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magic</a>, he developed an Obscurus. It was noted by <a href="/wiki/Porpentina_Goldstein" title="Porpentina Goldstein">Tina Goldstein</a> that out of all Mary Lou's adopted children, Credence was the most hated.
</p><p>Credence learned over the years to willingly surrender his belt for a beating when he knew he did something wrong. He tended to be passive when it came to the beatings and he learned to take them submissively. Despite living in an environment where magic was shunned, Credence was drawn toward the ideas of wizardry and even entertained the thought of becoming part of the <a href="/wiki/Wizarding_world" title="Wizarding world">wizarding world</a>. Mary Lou seemed to be aware of Credence's interest in magic and did not hesitate to beat him for it. When Mary Lou was about to attack Credence, he transformed into his Obscurus form and killed her. <br clear="all" />
</p>

<p>Modesty was kind to her older foster brother and he was close to her as a result. One of the reasons Credence shared a close relationship with Modesty, was because she did not possess the same zealous attitude <a href="/wiki/Chastity_Barebone" title="Chastity Barebone">Chastity</a> did in regards to their adoptive mother's anti-witchcraft stance.
</p><p>Modesty was young and curious, she shared Credence's secret interest in <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magic</a>. Because of this shared interest, they trusted each other. When <a href="/wiki/Henry_Shaw_Junior" title="Henry Shaw Junior">Henry Shaw Junior</a> meanly insulted Credence, she took his hand to reassure him. Modesty often witnessed the beatings he got from their mother and comforted him.
</p><p>Modesty occasionally confided in Credence that she missed the nine brothers and sisters she had before she was adopted. When Credence found the toy wand under Modesty's bed, <a href="/wiki/Mary_Lou_Barebone" title="Mary Lou Barebone">Mary Lou</a> believed it to be his and beat him, despite Modesty's attempt at convincing her it was hers. This shows that she defended him when he was about to be whipped by their foster mother. After the Obscurus took control of Credence and killed their adopted family, she became fearful of him. Despite her immense fear of him, he&#160;showed no anger towards her and did not try to harm her, as she had been one of the few people in the world who had ever showed him any kindness, which he clearly respected her for.&#160;
</p>

<p>During his time at Circus Arcanus, Credence befriended fellow outcast Nagini. These two broken souls quickly developed a close relationship, due to their similarly tragic pasts. Nagini is Credence's only friend. After they escaped the Circus together, and wishing to stay together, Nagini assisted Credence on his quest to find out his true identity. At the time, Nagini became very caring and protective of Credence. An example of this is when she comforted him with a hug after they witnessed Irma Dugard's death, and refused to step aside when Yusuf Kama was at the point of killing Credence. Despite his friend begging him to stay with her, Credence joined Grindelwald's army in the end, leaving Nagini devastated and heartbroken afterwards. However, Nagini's devastation over losing Credence induced her to officially join the <a href="/wiki/Global_Wizarding_War" class="mw-redirect" title="Global Wizarding War">Global Wizarding War</a> against Grindelwald.
</p><p><a href="/wiki/Ezra_Miller" title="Ezra Miller">Ezra Miller</a> later <a href="/wiki/Lights,_Camera,_Magic!:_The_Making_of_Fantastic_Beasts:_The_Crimes_of_Grindelwald" title="Lights, Camera, Magic!: The Making of Fantastic Beasts: The Crimes of Grindelwald">says</a> that there is <a href="/wiki/Love" title="Love">love</a> between Credence and Nagini.
</p>

<p>Grindelwald disguised himself as <a href="/wiki/Percival_Graves" title="Percival Graves">Percival Graves</a>, and used Credence to try and locate the <a href="/wiki/Obscurial" title="Obscurial">Obscurial</a> terrorising <a href="/wiki/New_York" title="New York">New York</a>, whom he believed was <a href="/wiki/Modesty_Barebone" title="Modesty Barebone">Modesty Barebone</a>, promising him entrance into the wizarding world and training to perform magic. MACUSA's Aurors chased and attacked him, and when Newt and Tina attempted to calm him and protect him, Grindelwald interrupted them to provoke Credence to cause more destruction in the hopes of achieving a war between magical and non-magical people. Despite how he had earlier coldly dismissed Credence, it appeared that after Credence's magical heritage and power was revealed, Grindelwald considered him to have been unjustly treated by his mother and later the whole MACUSA, as he showed genuine emotions of rage when MACUSA destroyed Credence and later showed a level of pity when referring to how Mary Lou Barebone had nearly destroyed Credence.

</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Credence_Barebone?veaction=edit&amp;section=24" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Credence_Barebone?action=edit&amp;section=24" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>Credence is derived from the Latin <i>credere</i>, meaning to trust and to believe, and from <i>credentia, </i>meaning belief.<sup id="cite_ref-20" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-20">&#91;20&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li>Barebone is an Anglo-Saxon surname, thought to be taken from the name of an English village, Barbon or Barbourne.<sup id="cite_ref-21" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-21">&#91;21&#93;</a></sup>
<ul><li>It is also the surname of two notable Puritans, Praise-God Barebone<sup id="cite_ref-22" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-22">&#91;22&#93;</a></sup> and his son Nicholas Barbon.<sup id="cite_ref-23" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-23">&#91;23&#93;</a></sup> The former gave his name to the Barebone's Parliament, predecessor of Oliver Cromwell's Protectorate, while the latter was one of the first proponents of a free market and, after the Great Fire of London, helped to pioneer fire insurance was a leading player of the following reconstruction work.</li>
<li>Something described as "barebones" is "reduced to or comprising only the basic or essential elements of something."</li></ul></li></ul>
<ul><li>On the other hand, Credence's alleged birth name Aurelius is derived from Latin <i>aureus</i>, meaning golden and gilded. <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Marcus_Aurelius" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Marcus Aurelius">Marcus Aurelius</a> was a 2nd-century philosophical writer and Roman emperor. Aurelius was too the name of several early saints, such as <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Saint_Aurelius" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Saint Aurelius">Saint Aurelius</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-24" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-24">&#91;24&#93;</a></sup></li></ul>
