<b>Minister Eugenia Jenkins</b> was <a href="/wiki/Minister_for_Magic" title="Minister for Magic">Minister for Magic</a> between <a href="/wiki/1968" title="1968">1968</a> and <a href="/wiki/1975" title="1975">1975</a>. She dealt competently with the <a href="/wiki/Pure-blood" title="Pure-blood">pure-blood</a> riots during the <a href="/wiki/Squib_Rights_marches" title="Squib Rights marches">Squib Rights marches</a> in the late <a href="/wiki/1960s" title="1960s">sixties</a>. However, when confronted with the first rise of <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a>, she was ousted from office, being deemed inadequate to the challenge.<sup id="cite_ref-WW_1-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-WW-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
