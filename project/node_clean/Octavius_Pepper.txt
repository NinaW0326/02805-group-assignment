<b>Octavius Pepper</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> who was reported to have disappeared in the early spring of <a href="/wiki/1997" title="1997">1997</a> by the <i><a href="/wiki/Daily_Prophet" title="Daily Prophet">Daily Prophet</a></i>. His disappearance was believed to be connected to the <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Octavius_Pepper?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Octavius_Pepper?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The name "Octavius" is a Roman family name meaning "eighth" from Latin octavus. This was the original family name of the emperor Augustus (born Gaius Octavius).
</p>
