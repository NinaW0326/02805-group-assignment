<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Arcturus_Black_III?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Arcturus_Black_III?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>He was born in <a href="/wiki/1901" title="1901">1901</a> as the eldest son of <a href="/wiki/Sirius_Black_II" title="Sirius Black II">Sirius Black</a> and his wife <a href="/wiki/Hesper_Gamp" title="Hesper Gamp">Hesper Gamp</a>. His younger siblings were <a href="/wiki/Lycoris_Black" title="Lycoris Black">Lycoris</a> and <a href="/wiki/Regulus_Black_I" title="Regulus Black I">Regulus</a>.
</p><p>He married <a href="/wiki/Melania_Macmillan" title="Melania Macmillan">Melania Macmillan</a>, who bore him a daughter, <a href="/wiki/Lucretia_Black" title="Lucretia Black">Lucretia</a>, and a son, <a href="/wiki/Orion_Black" title="Orion Black">Orion</a>. Arcturus became a grandfather after Orion married their cousin <a href="/wiki/Walburga_Black" title="Walburga Black">Walburga</a> and had two sons, <a href="/wiki/Sirius_Black" title="Sirius Black">Sirius</a> and <a href="/wiki/Regulus_Black" title="Regulus Black">Regulus</a>. Lucretia had no children from her marriage to <a href="/wiki/Ignatius_Prewett" title="Ignatius Prewett">Ignatius Prewett</a>.
</p><p>At some point, Arcturus<sup id="cite_ref-PMordermerlin_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PMordermerlin-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> was awarded the <a href="/wiki/Order_of_Merlin" title="Order of Merlin">Order of Merlin, First Class</a>, for "<i>Services to the Ministry</i>".<sup id="cite_ref-OP6_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OP6-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> His grandson Sirius believed that he received the honour because he had given the Ministry a large amount of gold.<sup id="cite_ref-PMordermerlin_2-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PMordermerlin-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-OP6_3-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OP6-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> The medal was left at <a href="/wiki/12_Grimmauld_Place" title="12 Grimmauld Place">12 Grimmauld Place</a> after his death; in <a href="/wiki/1995" title="1995">1995</a>, Sirius found the medal while cleaning and threw it into a rubbish sack with contempt.<sup id="cite_ref-OP6_3-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OP6-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Arcturus died in <a href="/wiki/1991" title="1991">1991</a>. He had outlived both his siblings, his son, his daughter-in-law, and his younger grandson. He was survived by his daughter and his elder grandson.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Arcturus_Black_III?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Arcturus_Black_III?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>Based on a statement by <a href="/wiki/Horace_Slughorn" title="Horace Slughorn">Horace Slughorn</a>, Arcturus may have been a member of the <a href="/wiki/Slytherin" title="Slytherin">Slytherin House</a>. However, he attended Hogwarts c. <a href="/wiki/1912" title="1912">1912</a>-<a href="/wiki/1919" title="1919">1919</a>, and Slughorn did not become the Potions master until approximately <a href="/wiki/1931" title="1931">1931</a>.</li>
<li>Arcturus' second grandson was named <a href="/wiki/Regulus_Black" title="Regulus Black">Regulus Arcturus Black</a>. Regulus was probably named in honour of his grandfather and his great-uncle, Arcturus's brother.</li>
<li>On the Black family tree shown on the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Order_of_the_Phoenix_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix (film)">film adaptation</a> of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Order_of_the_Phoenix" title="Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix">Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix</a></i>, Arcturus's name is misspelt as "Arctorus".</li></ul>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Arcturus_Black_III?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Arcturus_Black_III?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p><a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Arcturus" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Arcturus">Arcturus</a> is the name of the brightest star in the constellation Boötes. Its name derives from Ancient Greek <i>Αρκτοῦρος</i>, meaning "bear guard"; this refers to the story in Greek mythology that Zeus placed Arcturus in the sky to look after Arcas and Callisto, who were turned into <a href="/wiki/Bear" title="Bear">bears</a> and then into the constellations Ursa Major and Ursa Minor to protect them from Hera's wrath.
</p>
