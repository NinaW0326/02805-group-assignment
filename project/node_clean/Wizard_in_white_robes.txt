<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Wizard_in_white_robes?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Wizard_in_white_robes?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>This wizard was once a student at <a href="/wiki/Mahoutokoro_School_of_Magic" title="Mahoutokoro School of Magic">Mahoutokoro School of Magic</a> who was expelled, presumably for practising the <a href="/wiki/Dark_Arts" title="Dark Arts">Dark Arts</a>.
</p><p>At some point, he travelled from his native <a href="/wiki/Japan" title="Japan">Japan</a> to <a href="/wiki/England" title="England">England</a> and successfully made a name for himself in the figurative underworld of wizarding <a href="/wiki/Great_Britain" title="Great Britain">Britain</a>, and eventually even joined the dangerous organisation known as the <a href="/wiki/R" title="R">Cabal</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Known to visit the street of shops and vendors specialising in the darker forms of magic in <a href="/wiki/London" title="London">London</a> known as <a href="/wiki/Knockturn_Alley" title="Knockturn Alley">Knockturn Alley</a> on occasion, at some point in <a href="/wiki/1989" title="1989">1989</a>, said dodgy place became the stage for a <a href="/wiki/Duelling" title="Duelling">duel</a> between the wizard in question and fellow criminal <a href="/wiki/Mundungus_Fletcher" title="Mundungus Fletcher">Mundungus Fletcher</a>, whom he defeated.<sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>The wizard was eventually caught by <a href="/wiki/Auror" title="Auror">Aurors</a>, but before he could be sent to <a href="/wiki/Azkaban" title="Azkaban">Azkaban</a>, he managed to escape custody. After escaping, he pursued <a href="/wiki/Jacob%27s_sibling" title="Jacob&#39;s sibling">Jacob's sibling</a> and <a href="/wiki/Merula_Snyde" title="Merula Snyde">Merula Snyde</a> at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> with the initial intention of murdering them.<sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Some time later, this wizard was tasked by <a href="/wiki/R" title="R">R</a> to capture Jacob's sibling and bring them to the hideout of their organisation in an effort to recruit them into their ranks. Having successfully gained access to the school grounds, and was able to corner them and another student near the <a href="/wiki/Great_Lake" title="Great Lake">Great Lake</a>, along with one of their fellow students, <a href="/wiki/Beatrice_Haywood" title="Beatrice Haywood">Beatrice Haywood</a>. Without hesitation, he cast the <a href="/wiki/Knockback_Jinx" title="Knockback Jinx">Knockback Jinx</a> on Jacob's sibling,<sup id="cite_ref-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> but before he could do anything else, their older brother and known adversary of 'R', <a href="/wiki/Jacob_(Hogwarts_student)" title="Jacob (Hogwarts student)">Jacob</a>, arrived and chased him off.<sup id="cite_ref-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup> Following this incident, <a href="/wiki/Auror" title="Auror">Aurors</a> from the <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">Ministry of Magic</a> was dispatch to apprehend him, only for him to evade capture. He fled the Hogwarts grounds following the <a href="/wiki/Duel_in_the_Forest_Grove" title="Duel in the Forest Grove">duel in the Forest Grove</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Personality_and_traits">Personality and traits</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Wizard_in_white_robes?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Personality and traits">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Wizard_in_white_robes?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Personality and traits"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>This individual appeared to be a cold-blooded and ruthless assassin. It was initially believed he intended to kill <a href="/wiki/Jacob%27s_sibling" title="Jacob&#39;s sibling">Jacob's sibling</a>, but claimed he was going to take them to <a href="/wiki/R" title="R">R</a> and they will become their next leader someday.<sup id="cite_ref-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Magical_abilities_and_skills">Magical abilities and skills</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Wizard_in_white_robes?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Wizard_in_white_robes?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li><b><a href="/wiki/Dark_Arts" title="Dark Arts">Dark Arts</a></b>: As a <a href="/wiki/Dark_Wizard" title="Dark Wizard">Dark Wizard</a> who worked as a assassin, he was highly proficient in dark magic, which, given his profession, presumably included the <a href="/wiki/Unforgivable_Curses" title="Unforgivable Curses">Unforgivable Curses</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Martial_magic" title="Martial magic"><b>Martial magic</b></a>: An accomplished duelist, this warlock at one point defeated <a href="/wiki/Mundungus_Fletcher" title="Mundungus Fletcher">Mundungus Fletcher</a> in a duel, who attributed his victory partially to the man's unusual <a href="/wiki/Accepted_combative_position" title="Accepted combative position">stance</a> and strange <a href="/wiki/Incantation" title="Incantation">incantations</a>. Later, he was notably able to neutralise the entire guard that was supposed to bring him to Azkaban, among them some of the best <a href="/wiki/Auror" title="Auror">Aurors</a> under <a href="/wiki/Alastor_Moody" title="Alastor Moody">Alastor Moody</a>'s command. Upon finally encountering his target at Hogwarts, he proved quick enough to be able to incapacitate <a href="/wiki/Jacob%27s_sibling" title="Jacob&#39;s sibling">Jacob's sibling</a> with a single spell before they got a chance to protect themselves. He was, however, unable to beat the famed <a href="/wiki/Alastor_Moody" title="Alastor Moody">Alastor Moody</a>, who successfully was able to disarm and capture him by means of the <a href="/wiki/Incarcerous_Spell" title="Incarcerous Spell">Incarcerous Spell,</a> binding him with robes.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Nonverbal_magic" class="mw-redirect" title="Nonverbal magic"><b>Nonverbal magic</b></a>: This wizard could cast spells without having to say them out loud, as demonstrated when he floored Jacob's sibling with a silent <a href="/wiki/Knockback_Jinx" title="Knockback Jinx">Knockback Jinx</a> upon finally cornering them, and later in his duel with <a href="/wiki/Alastor_Moody" title="Alastor Moody">Alastor Moody</a>.</li></ul>

<ul><li><b><a href="/wiki/Wand" title="Wand">Wand</a></b>: He was in ownership of a wand, which was of unknown length, <a href="/wiki/Wand_wood" title="Wand wood">wood</a> and <a href="/wiki/Wand_core" title="Wand core">core</a>.</li></ul>
