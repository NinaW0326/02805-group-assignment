<dd>&#8212;Sprout's view on Hogwarts and education<sup class="noprint"><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Half-Blood_Prince" title="Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince">[src]</a></sup></dd>

<p><b>Professor Pomona Sprout</b> (b. <a href="/wiki/15_May" title="15 May">15 May</a>)<sup id="cite_ref-birthday_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-birthday-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch</a> who worked as <a href="/wiki/Head_of_Hufflepuff_House" title="Head of Hufflepuff House">Head of Hufflepuff House</a> and <a href="/wiki/Professor" title="Professor">Head</a> of the <a href="/wiki/Herbology" title="Herbology">Herbology</a> <a href="/wiki/Herbology_department" title="Herbology department">department</a> at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a>. She attended the school in her youth, where she was <a href="/wiki/Sorting_ceremony" title="Sorting ceremony">Sorted</a> into <a href="/wiki/Hufflepuff" title="Hufflepuff">Hufflepuff</a> and excelled at <a href="/wiki/Herbology" title="Herbology">Herbology</a>. Some time after her <a href="/wiki/Seventh_year" title="Seventh year">graduation</a>, Sprout returned to Hogwarts to teach.
</p><p>Professor Sprout opposed <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a> during the <a href="/wiki/Second_Wizarding_War" title="Second Wizarding War">Second Wizarding War</a>. In <a href="/wiki/1993" title="1993">1993</a>, she grew the <a href="/wiki/Mandrake" title="Mandrake">Mandrakes</a> that would later be used in the <a href="/wiki/Mandrake_Restorative_Draught" title="Mandrake Restorative Draught">Mandrake Restorative Draught</a> to cure those who were petrified by <a href="/wiki/Salazar_Slytherin%27s_Basilisk" class="mw-redirect" title="Salazar Slytherin&#39;s Basilisk">Slytherin's Monster</a>. She also opposed <a href="/wiki/Dolores_Umbridge" title="Dolores Umbridge">Dolores Umbridge</a> and her tyranny over the school in the <a href="/wiki/1995-1996_school_year" class="mw-redirect" title="1995-1996 school year">1995-1996 school year</a>, which the rest of the staff were happy to join her in doing, voted for the school to remain open after the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_the_Astronomy_Tower" title="Battle of the Astronomy Tower">Battle of the Astronomy Tower</a>, and assisted in <a href="/wiki/Ousting_of_Severus_Snape" title="Ousting of Severus Snape">ousting Severus Snape</a> in <a href="/wiki/1998" title="1998">1998</a>. Sprout took a group of students to the battlements during the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a> and threw <a href="/wiki/Devil%27s_Snare" title="Devil&#39;s Snare">Devil's Snare</a> and various other dangerous plants onto the invading <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a>.
</p><p>She ultimately survived the War. By the early <a href="/wiki/21st_century" title="21st century">21st century</a>, <a href="/wiki/Neville_Longbottom" title="Neville Longbottom">Neville Longbottom</a> taught Herbology, alongside her.<sup id="cite_ref-MA_15-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-MA-15">&#91;15&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<p>Pomona Sprout was born into a wizarding family of unknown degree<sup id="cite_ref-Blood_2-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Blood-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> on <a href="/wiki/15_May" title="15 May">15 May</a><sup id="cite_ref-birthday_1-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-birthday-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> in <a href="/wiki/Great_Britain" title="Great Britain">Britain</a> or <a href="/wiki/Ireland" title="Ireland">Ireland</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-POB_14-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-POB-14">&#91;14&#93;</a></sup> She presumably purchased her wand from <a href="/wiki/Diagon_Alley" title="Diagon Alley">Diagon Alley</a> at the age of eleven, prior to attending <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a>.
</p><p>She was <a href="/wiki/Sorting_ceremony" title="Sorting ceremony">Sorted</a> into <a href="/wiki/Hufflepuff" title="Hufflepuff">Hufflepuff House</a><sup id="cite_ref-House_13-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-House-13">&#91;13&#93;</a></sup> upon her arrival at Hogwarts and excelled at <a href="/wiki/Herbology" title="Herbology">Herbology</a>, which may have been taught by <a href="/wiki/Herbert_Beery" title="Herbert Beery">Professor Herbert Beery</a> at the time,<sup id="cite_ref-Prof.Beery_16-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Prof.Beery-16">&#91;16&#93;</a></sup> and likely achieved a high-graded <a href="/wiki/Ordinary_Wizarding_Level" title="Ordinary Wizarding Level">O.W.L.</a> and <a href="/wiki/Nastily_Exhausting_Wizarding_Test" title="Nastily Exhausting Wizarding Test">N.E.W.T.</a> in the subject.
</p><p>Pomona and <a href="/wiki/Minerva_McGonagall" title="Minerva McGonagall">Minerva McGonagall</a>'s student time at Hogwarts overlapped by two years. The two students enjoyed an excellent relationship both then and in later years.<sup id="cite_ref-PM-mcgonagall_17-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PM-mcgonagall-17">&#91;17&#93;</a></sup>She felt homesick until her friend Minerva helped her getting used to the school. They also helped each other learning the subjects they evtually taught.<sup id="cite_ref-HM2_18-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HM2-18">&#91;18&#93;</a></sup> During her time at Hogwarts, she liked attending Quidditch matches.<sup id="cite_ref-19" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-19">&#91;19&#93;</a></sup> 
</p>

<p>At some point after her graduation, Sprout returned to Hogwarts as a member of the <a href="/wiki/Herbology_department" title="Herbology department">Herbology department</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-PS8_4-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PS8-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> She taught her classes in the <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_greenhouses" title="Hogwarts greenhouses">greenhouses</a> at the back of the castle<sup id="cite_ref-PS8_4-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PS8-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> and used one of the greenhouses as office.<sup id="cite_ref-PS.2Fgbc_20-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PS.2Fgbc-20">&#91;20&#93;</a></sup> At an unknown time, Professor Sprout became Head of Department and <a href="/wiki/Head_of_Hufflepuff_House" title="Head of Hufflepuff House">Head of Hufflepuff House</a>.
</p><p>Professor Sprout used to bring the most interesting specimens (some of which could dance and talk) from the <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_greenhouses" title="Hogwarts greenhouses">Herbology greenhouses</a> to decorate <a href="/wiki/Hufflepuff_Basement" title="Hufflepuff Basement">Hufflepuff Basement</a>. This was one of the reasons why Hufflepuffs were often very good in her subject.<sup id="cite_ref-pottermorehufflepuffs_21-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-pottermorehufflepuffs-21">&#91;21&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Professor Sprout was <a href="/wiki/Nymphadora_Tonks" title="Nymphadora Tonks">Nymphadora Tonks</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Head_of_House" title="Head of House">Head of House</a><sup id="cite_ref-Tonks.27s_house_22-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Tonks.27s_house-22">&#91;22&#93;</a></sup> in the late <a href="/wiki/1980s" title="1980s">1980s</a> and early <a href="/wiki/1990s" title="1990s">1990s</a>. Sprout did not make her a prefect in her fifth year due to her inability to behave herself.<sup id="cite_ref-OP9_23-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OP9-23">&#91;23&#93;</a></sup> However, Tonks presumably surprised Sprout after she earned at least five <a href="/wiki/Nastily_Exhausting_Wizarding_Test" title="Nastily Exhausting Wizarding Test">N.E.W.Ts</a> in her seventh year.
</p><p>Professor Sprout spent the summer holidays of <a href="/wiki/1986" title="1986">1986</a> experimenting with <a href="/wiki/Dragon_dung_fertiliser" class="mw-redirect" title="Dragon dung fertiliser">dragon dung fertiliser</a>, which she told <a href="/wiki/Jacob%27s_sibling" title="Jacob&#39;s sibling">Jacob's sibling</a> about during the first Herbology lesson of term. She then set the class to work on tending to the <a href="/wiki/Valerian" title="Valerian">Valerian</a> Sprig plants, which were a basis for many potions. Towards the end of class Professor Sprout asked <a href="/wiki/Penny_Haywood" title="Penny Haywood">Penny Haywood</a> to fetch her a plant pot, only for the girl to scream in terror when a <a href="/wiki/Werewolf" title="Werewolf">werewolf</a> suddenly burst out of the stack. The rest of the class began to panic as well, but Professor Sprout stepped forward and cast the <a href="/wiki/Boggart-Banishing_Spell" title="Boggart-Banishing Spell">Boggart-Banishing Spell</a> with her wand, revealing that the supposed werewolf was actually a <a href="/wiki/Boggart" title="Boggart">Boggart</a>. Professor Sprout explained to the class what a Boggart was before dismissing them to go and speak to Professor Dumbledore, as a Boggart had never appeared in the Herbology Greenhouses before.<sup id="cite_ref-HMy3ch1_24-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HMy3ch1-24">&#91;24&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Sometime in between <a href="/wiki/1987" title="1987">1987</a> and <a href="/wiki/1990" title="1990">1990</a>, <a href="/wiki/Gabriel_Truman" title="Gabriel Truman">Gabriel Truman</a>, a <a href="/wiki/Third_year" title="Third year">third year</a> at the time, got into a <a href="/wiki/Duelling" title="Duelling">duel</a> with a <a href="/wiki/Ravenclaw" title="Ravenclaw">Ravenclaw</a> <a href="/wiki/Prefect" title="Prefect">Prefect</a> who argued that the famous <a href="/wiki/Arithmancer" title="Arithmancer">Arithmancer</a> <a href="/wiki/Bridget_Wenlock" title="Bridget Wenlock">Bridget Wenlock</a> must have been in his own house, not Hufflepuff, as she was in fact. Gabriel felt he deserved a week's worth of detentions for his own actions, but Professor Sprout went easy on him, sending him off with a warning and a box of <a href="/wiki/Pink_Coconut_Ice" title="Pink Coconut Ice">coconut ice</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-pottermorehufflepuffs_21-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-pottermorehufflepuffs-21">&#91;21&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<p>Professor Sprout was still teaching at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts</a> by the <a href="/wiki/1991-1992_school_year" class="mw-redirect" title="1991-1992 school year">1991-1992 school year</a>. Just before the start of the new school year, <a href="/wiki/Albus_Dumbledore" title="Albus Dumbledore">Albus Dumbledore</a> trusted her to contribute to the defences of the <a href="/wiki/Philosopher%27s_Stone_Chambers" class="mw-redirect" title="Philosopher&#39;s Stone Chambers">Philosopher's Stone Chambers</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-PS14_25-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PS14-25">&#91;25&#93;</a></sup> Sprout, being the <a href="/wiki/Herbology" title="Herbology">Herbology</a> <a href="/wiki/Professor" title="Professor">professor</a>, possessed considerable knowledge of plants and thus planted a large amount of <a href="/wiki/Devil%27s_Snare" title="Devil&#39;s Snare">Devil's Snare</a> in her Chamber. The Devil's Snare in the chamber broke the fall of the one trying to get the Stone, but also tried to kill them. As the dangerous plant liked to wrap itself around anyone within the vicinity and strangle them, the trick was to use <a href="/wiki/Fire" title="Fire">fire</a><sup id="cite_ref-PS16_26-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PS16-26">&#91;26&#93;</a></sup> or sunlight<sup id="cite_ref-PSf27_27-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PSf27-27">&#91;27&#93;</a></sup> to force the Snare into retreat. If the victim struggled, however, the plant would tighten its grip until they were dead.
</p>

<p>That year, she taught her first years three times a week in <a href="/wiki/Greenhouse_One" title="Greenhouse One">Greenhouse One</a> and taught them how to care for various herbs and fungi.<sup id="cite_ref-PS8_4-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PS8-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> The first lesson focused in recognising plants found in the Hogwarts grounds, using a copy of the <i><a href="/wiki/Ingredient_Encyclopedia" title="Ingredient Encyclopedia">Ingredient Encyclopedia</a></i> as a reference.<sup id="cite_ref-PS.2Fgbc_20-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PS.2Fgbc-20">&#91;20&#93;</a></sup> Other lessons covered the <a href="/wiki/Severing_Charm" title="Severing Charm">Severing Charm</a><sup id="cite_ref-PSconsole_28-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PSconsole-28">&#91;28&#93;</a></sup> and the <a href="/wiki/Fire-Making_Spell" title="Fire-Making Spell">Incendio Charm</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-PS.2Fps1_29-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PS.2Fps1-29">&#91;29&#93;</a></sup> Sprout also lectured her students about specific plants, such as Devil's Snare.<sup id="cite_ref-PS16_26-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PS16-26">&#91;26&#93;</a></sup> She also attended Quidditch matches.
</p><p>That year, Sprout met the famous <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> who was among the first-years she taught.<sup id="cite_ref-PS8_4-10" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PS8-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> She was also present at the End-of-Term Feast where <a href="/wiki/Slytherin" title="Slytherin">Slytherin</a> lost the <a href="/wiki/House_Cup" title="House Cup">House Cup</a> to <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor" title="Gryffindor">Gryffindor</a> ending their seven year winning streak, which likely pleased her immensely along with the rest of her House.
</p>

<p>As the <a href="/wiki/Herbology" title="Herbology">Herbology</a> teacher, Professor Sprout was in charge of doctoring the damaged <a href="/wiki/Whomping_Willow" title="Whomping Willow">Whomping Willow</a> by wrapping bandages around the broken branches after <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> and <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ron Weasley</a> drove <a href="/wiki/Flying_Ford_Anglia" title="Flying Ford Anglia">a bewitched car</a> into it.<sup id="cite_ref-CS6_30-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-CS6-30">&#91;30&#93;</a></sup> On the first day of term, <a href="/wiki/Gilderoy_Lockhart" title="Gilderoy Lockhart">Gilderoy Lockhart</a>, the new <a href="/wiki/Defence_Against_the_Dark_Arts" title="Defence Against the Dark Arts">Defence Against the Dark Arts</a> <a href="/wiki/Professor" title="Professor">teacher</a>, gave her useless pointers and arrogantly made out that he was a better <a href="/wiki/Herbologist" title="Herbologist">Herbologist</a> than her, which made her look distinctly cross and not at all her usual cheerful self. She was further irritated when Lockhart took Harry out of the lesson to speak to him, rudely closing the door in her face before she could make her opinion known. That day she taught her second years how to re-pot a new growth of Mandrake, whilst also warning them about the teething <a href="/wiki/Venomous_Tentacula" title="Venomous Tentacula">Venomous Tentacula</a>, giving the plant in question a sharp slap to make it withdraw the long feelers it had been inching over her shoulder.<sup id="cite_ref-CS6_30-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-CS6-30">&#91;30&#93;</a></sup> This lesson and the Mandrakes would later prove to be very useful.<sup id="cite_ref-CS9_31-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-CS9-31">&#91;31&#93;</a></sup>

<p>Sprout and her colleagues were understandably unnerved by the <a href="/wiki/Plot_to_open_the_Chamber_of_Secrets" title="Plot to open the Chamber of Secrets">opening</a> of the <a href="/wiki/Chamber_of_Secrets" title="Chamber of Secrets">Chamber of Secrets</a>. After <a href="/wiki/Mrs_Norris" title="Mrs Norris">Mrs Norris</a>, the <a href="/wiki/Cat" title="Cat">cat</a> belonging to <a href="/wiki/Argus_Filch" title="Argus Filch">Argus Filch</a>, was found <a href="/wiki/Petrification" title="Petrification">petrified</a>, Professor Dumbledore decided that they were to use a <a href="/wiki/Mandrake_Restorative_Draught" title="Mandrake Restorative Draught">Mandrake Restorative Draught</a> to cure her.<sup id="cite_ref-CS9_31-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-CS9-31">&#91;31&#93;</a></sup> Professor Sprout was put in charge of maturing her Mandrakes until they could be chopped up, <a href="/wiki/Stewed_Mandrake" title="Stewed Mandrake">stewed</a>, and used by <a href="/wiki/Potions_Master" title="Potions Master">Potions Master</a> <a href="/wiki/Severus_Snape" title="Severus Snape">Severus Snape</a> for the draught.<sup id="cite_ref-CS9_31-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-CS9-31">&#91;31&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Sprout attended the Quidditch match between the <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor_Quidditch_team" title="Gryffindor Quidditch team">Gryffindor</a> and <a href="/wiki/Slytherin_Quidditch_team" title="Slytherin Quidditch team">Slytherin teams</a>, sat high up in the stands with <a href="/wiki/Potions_Master" title="Potions Master">Potions Master</a> Severus Snape, <a href="/wiki/Charms_Master" title="Charms Master">Charms Master</a> <a href="/wiki/Filius_Flitwick" title="Filius Flitwick">Filius Flitwick</a>, <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Library" title="Hogwarts Library">librarian</a> <a href="/wiki/Irma_Pince" title="Irma Pince">Irma Pince</a> and <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Board_of_Governors" title="Hogwarts Board of Governors">Governor</a> <a href="/wiki/Lucius_Malfoy" title="Lucius Malfoy">Lucius Malfoy</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-CSf15_32-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-CSf15-32">&#91;32&#93;</a></sup>

<p>By the end of the year the Mandrakes had reached maturity and the Mandrake Restorative Draught was completed and administered to those who were petrified: <a href="/wiki/Mrs_Norris" title="Mrs Norris">Mrs Norris</a>, <a href="/wiki/Justin_Finch-Fletchley" title="Justin Finch-Fletchley">Justin Finch-Fletchley</a>, <a href="/wiki/Colin_Creevey" title="Colin Creevey">Colin Creevey</a>, <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione Granger</a>, <a href="/wiki/Penelope_Clearwater" title="Penelope Clearwater">Penelope Clearwater</a>, and the ghost of <a href="/wiki/Nicholas_de_Mimsy-Porpington" title="Nicholas de Mimsy-Porpington">Sir Nicholas de Mimsy Porpington</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-CS18_33-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-CS18-33">&#91;33&#93;</a></sup> At the <a href="/wiki/End-of-Term_Feast" title="End-of-Term Feast">End-of-Term Feast</a>, Professor Dumbledore and the rest of the school applauded Sprout for her contribution in the brewing of the Mandrake Draught.<sup id="cite_ref-CSf36_34-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-CSf36-34">&#91;34&#93;</a></sup> During the End-the-Term Feast, Gryffindor also won the House Cup for a second year in a row and Hagrid returned from his confinement in <a href="/wiki/Azkaban" title="Azkaban">Azkaban</a>.

<p>The next school year was relatively uneventful at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts</a>. On <a href="/wiki/16_October" title="16 October">16 October</a>, the <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor" title="Gryffindor">Gryffindors</a> and <a href="/wiki/Hufflepuff" title="Hufflepuff">Hufflepuffs</a> had <a href="/wiki/Herbology" title="Herbology">Herbology</a> class together, and Sprout taught them how to harvest <a href="/wiki/Puffapod" title="Puffapod">Puffapod</a> beans.<sup id="cite_ref-PA8_35-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PA8-35">&#91;35&#93;</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ron Weasley</a> accidentally dropped some of these beans onto the floor of the <a href="/wiki/Greenhouse_Three" title="Greenhouse Three">greenhouse</a> they were working in and they instantly bloomed, earning him Professor Sprout's disapproval. During the same year, <a href="/wiki/Sirius_Black" title="Sirius Black">Sirius Black</a>, a "<i>dangerous mass-murderer</i>",<sup id="cite_ref-POA3_36-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-POA3-36">&#91;36&#93;</a></sup> escaped from <a href="/wiki/Azkaban" title="Azkaban">Azkaban</a> and tried to get into <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts</a> on two occasions.<sup id="cite_ref-PA8_35-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PA8-35">&#91;35&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-POA13_37-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-POA13-37">&#91;37&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>On Christmas, Pomona Sprout was one of the thirteen Christmas Feast attendants. During the Christmas feast she was privy to the tense conversation between McGonagall and <a href="/wiki/Sybill_Trelawney" title="Sybill Trelawney">Trelawney</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-PA11_38-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PA11-38">&#91;38&#93;</a></sup>

<p>That school year the <a href="/wiki/Triwizard_Tournament" title="Triwizard Tournament">Triwizard Tournament</a> was held at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Castle" title="Hogwarts Castle">Hogwarts</a> and one of the Triwizard Champions was sixth year <a href="/wiki/Hufflepuff" title="Hufflepuff">Hufflepuff</a> <a href="/wiki/Cedric_Diggory" title="Cedric Diggory">Cedric Diggory</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-GF16_42-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GF16-42">&#91;42&#93;</a></sup> whom Sprout knew very well.<sup id="cite_ref-GF36_6-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GF36-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup> Cedric was not the only Hogwarts Champion; <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> had mysteriously been entered into the Tournament.<sup id="cite_ref-GF16_42-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GF16-42">&#91;42&#93;</a></sup> As such, Professor Sprout seemed to act somewhat distant towards Harry.<sup id="cite_ref-GF18_5-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GF18-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> On Christmas Day, Professor Sprout attended the Yule Ball where she danced with <a href="/wiki/Albus_Dumbledore" title="Albus Dumbledore">Professor Dumbledore</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-GF23_43-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GF23-43">&#91;43&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>At the end of the year Cedric Diggory was murdered during the third task of the Triwizard Tournament on <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a>'s orders.<sup id="cite_ref-GF32_44-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GF32-44">&#91;44&#93;</a></sup> Harry Potter at the risk of his own life brought Cedric's body back to the school grounds. Sprout spoke with <a href="/wiki/Amos_Diggory" title="Amos Diggory">Amos Diggory</a> and his wife, since she knew their son best as <a href="/wiki/Head_of_Hufflepuff_House" title="Head of Hufflepuff House">Head of his House</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-GF36_6-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GF36-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup> She attended the <a href="/wiki/Memorial_feast_to_Cedric_Diggory" title="Memorial feast to Cedric Diggory">Memorial Feast</a> in Cedric's memory<sup id="cite_ref-GF37_45-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GF37-45">&#91;45&#93;</a></sup> and believed Harry and Dumbledore's claims that Lord Voldemort had returned.<sup id="cite_ref-OP27_46-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OP27-46">&#91;46&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<p>The following school year, <a href="/wiki/Dolores_Umbridge" title="Dolores Umbridge">Dolores Umbridge</a> was made Professor of <a href="/wiki/Defence_Against_the_Dark_Arts" title="Defence Against the Dark Arts">Defence Against the Dark Arts</a> by the <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">Ministry of Magic</a> in accordance to the <a href="/wiki/Educational_Decree_Number_Twenty-Two" title="Educational Decree Number Twenty-Two">Educational Decree Number Twenty-Two</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-OP15_47-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OP15-47">&#91;47&#93;</a></sup> Umbridge was despised by the majority of the staff, including Sprout.<sup id="cite_ref-OP27_46-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OP27-46">&#91;46&#93;</a></sup>

<p>The <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor" title="Gryffindor">Gryffindor</a> <a href="/wiki/Ordinary_Wizarding_Level" title="Ordinary Wizarding Level">O.W.L.</a>-level class was lectured by Professor Sprout on the importance of the examinations during their first Herbology class.<sup id="cite_ref-OP13_48-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OP13-48">&#91;48&#93;</a></sup> Sprout also had the students make a practical exercise which involved using dragon dung fertiliser. She also set them a long essay for the following lesson.<sup id="cite_ref-OP13_48-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OP13-48">&#91;48&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Sometime this year, Sprout held career consultations with her <a href="/wiki/Hufflepuff" title="Hufflepuff">Hufflepuff</a> fifth years.<sup id="cite_ref-OP29_49-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OP29-49">&#91;49&#93;</a></sup> During this school year, Professor Sprout also held mock O.W.L.s on the Herbology Greenhouses for those wanting to revise for the exams.<sup id="cite_ref-OPg_50-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OPg-50">&#91;50&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>After Harry Potter took part in an interview for <i>The Quibbler</i> about the events at <a href="/wiki/Little_Hangleton" title="Little Hangleton">Little Hangleton</a> the previous June, he was put in detention by <a href="/wiki/Dolores_Umbridge" title="Dolores Umbridge">Professor Umbridge</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-OP26_51-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OP26-51">&#91;51&#93;</a></sup> The rest of the teaching staff, however, found the interview an act of bravery on Harry's behalf and, being forbidden of discussing it per the <a href="/wiki/Educational_Decree_Number_Twenty-Six" title="Educational Decree Number Twenty-Six">Educational Decree Number Twenty-Six</a>, found other ways to express their feelings; Pomona awarded Harry a large amount of house points for passing her a watering can.<sup id="cite_ref-OP26_51-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OP26-51">&#91;51&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<p>The teachers' capability of teaching was examined by Umbridge, as part of her duties as High Inquisitor. <a href="/wiki/Sybill_Trelawney" title="Sybill Trelawney">Sybill Trelawney</a>, the <a href="/wiki/Divination_(class)" title="Divination (class)">Divination</a> Professor, was sacked and almost dragged out of the castle by Umbridge. <a href="/wiki/Albus_Dumbledore" title="Albus Dumbledore">Albus Dumbledore</a>, finding a loophole in one of the <a href="/wiki/Educational_Decree" title="Educational Decree">Educational Decrees</a>, allowed the sacked Trelawney to remain occupying her premises on the North Tower. Trelawney was escorted back to the Castle by Sprout, <a href="/wiki/Minerva_McGonagall" title="Minerva McGonagall">Minerva McGonagall</a> and <a href="/wiki/Filius_Flitwick" title="Filius Flitwick">Filius Flitwick</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-OP26_51-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OP26-51">&#91;51&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Sprout managed to show Professor Umbridge her teaching capability and avoided being sacked herself.<sup id="cite_ref-OP26_51-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OP26-51">&#91;51&#93;</a></sup> She was later relieved to have Umbridge removed from the school and welcomed Professor Dumbledore back as Headmaster.

She also permitted them to swear loudly if the <a href="/wiki/Venomous_Tentacula" title="Venomous Tentacula">Venomous Tentacula</a> bit them or seized them unexpectedly from behind.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP11_53-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP11-53">&#91;53&#93;</a></sup>

The trio's first pod bounced free while they were trying to open it, and rebounded off the glass roof of the greenhouse, knocking Professor Sprout's battered hat off.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP14_54-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP14-54">&#91;54&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>In late April of that school year, Sprout helped the new <a href="/wiki/Potions_Master" title="Potions Master">Potions Master</a>, Horace Slughorn in picking some potion herbs. She left towards her <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_greenhouses" title="Hogwarts greenhouses">Greenhouses</a> after she had collected enough for Slughorn's third year class, with some to spare.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP22_55-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP22-55">&#91;55&#93;</a></sup> Slughorn also proceeded to steal some Venomous Tentacula leaves while Sprout was not around.<sup id="cite_ref-HBPf22_56-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBPf22-56">&#91;56&#93;</a></sup>

<p>At the end of the year, the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_the_Astronomy_Tower" title="Battle of the Astronomy Tower">Battle of the Astronomy Tower</a> took place, with the <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a> invading <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts</a> and fighting against the students, teachers, <a href="/wiki/Auror" title="Auror">Aurors</a> and the <a href="/wiki/Order_of_the_Phoenix" title="Order of the Phoenix">Order of the Phoenix</a>. Professor Sprout likely participated, as did <a href="/wiki/Minerva_McGonagall" title="Minerva McGonagall">Minerva McGonagall</a>, <a href="/wiki/Filius_Flitwick" title="Filius Flitwick">Filius Flitwick</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Rubeus_Hagrid" title="Rubeus Hagrid">Rubeus Hagrid</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP28_57-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP28-57">&#91;57&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>After Professor Dumbledore's death, Sprout met with fellow staff members Minerva McGonagall, Filius Flitwick, Horace Slughorn, and Rubeus Hagrid to discuss the future of <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts</a>. She was a staunch advocate of keeping the school open, stating that Dumbledore would have wanted it so. She also supported the suggestion that Dumbledore should be laid to rest at Hogwarts, though no Headmaster has ever received a burial there before.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP29_58-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP29-58">&#91;58&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Sprout attended Dumbledore's funeral and led the Hufflepuffs down to the grounds where the ceremony was held, looking cleaner than she ever had been before; without a single patch on her usually battered hat.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP30_59-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP30-59">&#91;59&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h3><span id="Later_life_(1998-2014)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="Later_life_.281998-2014.29">Later life <span class="template-c">(1998-2014)</span></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Pomona_Sprout?veaction=edit&amp;section=14" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Later life (1998-2014)">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Pomona_Sprout?action=edit&amp;section=14" title="Edit section: Later life (1998-2014)"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Sprout remained teaching at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> as <a href="/wiki/Herbology" title="Herbology">Herbology</a> <a href="/wiki/Professor" title="Professor">professor</a>. By <a href="/wiki/2014" title="2014">2014</a>; Neville Longbottom had joined the <a href="/wiki/Herbology_department" title="Herbology department">Herbology department</a> and taught the subject alongside her.<sup id="cite_ref-Epilogue_12-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Epilogue-12">&#91;12&#93;</a></sup> During the <a href="/wiki/Calamity" title="Calamity">Calamity</a>, she was able to give twenty-three six-hours classes regarding everything she knew about Herbology and herbal remedies to members of the <a href="/wiki/Statute_of_Secrecy_Task_Force" title="Statute of Secrecy Task Force">Statute of Secrecy Task Force</a> for increased recovery,<sup id="cite_ref-WU1_67-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-WU1-67">&#91;67&#93;</a></sup> and co-teach along with Professor Flitwick on how different magical approaches – when combined with teamwork – achieve devastating effects to enhance the efficiency of enchantments cast on a teammate.<sup id="cite_ref-WU2_68-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-WU2-68">&#91;68&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Personality_and_traits">Personality and traits</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Pomona_Sprout?veaction=edit&amp;section=16" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Personality and traits">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Pomona_Sprout?action=edit&amp;section=16" title="Edit section: Personality and traits"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<div class="quote">

<dd>&#8212;Professor Sprout's no-nonsense attitude as a teacher<sup class="noprint"><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Half-Blood_Prince" title="Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince">[src]</a></sup></dd>

<p>Professor Sprout was noted to be very cheerful, positive, and loyal,<sup id="cite_ref-CS6_30-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-CS6-30">&#91;30&#93;</a></sup> but was also very professional and just and would chide her students if necessary.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP14_54-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP14-54">&#91;54&#93;</a></sup> Thus, Sprout possessed all of the virtues and aspects belonging to <a href="/wiki/Hufflepuff" title="Hufflepuff">Hufflepuff House</a>. She was kind and nurturing to the students, and surely encouraged <a href="/wiki/Neville_Longbottom" title="Neville Longbottom">Neville Longbottom</a>'s penchant for <a href="/wiki/Herbology" title="Herbology">Herbology</a> because he was anxious to show her his <a href="/wiki/Mimbulus_mimbletonia" title="Mimbulus mimbletonia">Mimbulus mimbletonia</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-OP10_69-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OP10-69">&#91;69&#93;</a></sup> Sprout was also the most maternal of all the <a href="/wiki/Head_of_House" title="Head of House">Heads of House</a>, an example being when she calmed and got all of the students back to bed after the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_the_Astronomy_Tower" title="Battle of the Astronomy Tower">Battle of the Astronomy Tower</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP29_58-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP29-58">&#91;58&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>She also seemed horrified when she learnt that <a href="/wiki/Ginevra_Weasley" title="Ginevra Weasley">Ginny Weasley</a> had been kidnapped by <a href="/wiki/Salazar_Slytherin" title="Salazar Slytherin">Salazar Slytherin</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Salazar_Slytherin%27s_Basilisk" class="mw-redirect" title="Salazar Slytherin&#39;s Basilisk">Basilisk</a> and taken into the <a href="/wiki/Chamber_of_Secrets" title="Chamber of Secrets">Chamber of Secrets</a>, clasping her shaking hand over her mouth in shock.<sup id="cite_ref-CS16_70-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-CS16-70">&#91;70&#93;</a></sup> Professor Sprout was also very brave, always ready to fight for her side. During the Battle of Hogwarts, she did not question McGonagall's decision to defend Hogwarts and immediately sought to provide whatever assistance she could, collecting various dangerous plants to fight Voldemort and his Death Eaters,<sup id="cite_ref-DH30_62-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH30-62">&#91;62&#93;</a></sup> showing her utmost loyalty and dedication for defending her school and standing up for what is right.
</p>

<p>As the <a href="/wiki/Head_of_Herbology" title="Head of Herbology">Head of Herbology</a> at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts</a>, <a href="/wiki/Professor" title="Professor">Professor</a> Sprout was an accomplished and skilful <a href="/wiki/Herbologist" title="Herbologist">Herbologist</a>, as well as a powerful witch in her own right. Testaments to her considerable abilities were her proficiency in the subject she taught, and her participation of and survival of the legendary <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a>.

<ul><li><b><a href="/wiki/Herbology" title="Herbology">Herbology</a></b>: As the <a href="/wiki/Professor" title="Professor">Professor</a> of <a href="/wiki/Herbology" title="Herbology">Herbology</a> at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a>, Sprout was an incredibly skilful <a href="/wiki/Herbologist" title="Herbologist">Herbologist</a>. Her talents ranged from taking care of harmless plants, such as <a href="/wiki/Umbrella_Flower" title="Umbrella Flower">Umbrella Flowers</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-CS6_30-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-CS6-30">&#91;30&#93;</a></sup> and <a href="/wiki/Puffapod" title="Puffapod">Puffapods</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-PA8_35-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PA8-35">&#91;35&#93;</a></sup> to more dangerous ones, such as <a href="/wiki/Whomping_Willow" title="Whomping Willow">Whomping Willows</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-CS6_30-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-CS6-30">&#91;30&#93;</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/Mandrake" title="Mandrake">Mandrakes</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-CS6_30-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-CS6-30">&#91;30&#93;</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/Snargaluff" title="Snargaluff">Snargaluffs</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-HBP14_54-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP14-54">&#91;54&#93;</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/Venomous_Tentacula" title="Venomous Tentacula">Venomous Tentaculas</a> and even the highly lethal <a href="/wiki/Devil%27s_Snare" title="Devil&#39;s Snare">Devil's Snare</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-DH30_62-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH30-62">&#91;62&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Charm" title="Charm">Charms</a></b>: As the Professor of Herbology, Sprout was well versed in <a href="/wiki/Herbology" title="Herbology">Herbology</a>-related charms, such as the <a href="/wiki/Fire-Making_Spell" title="Fire-Making Spell">Fire-Making Spell</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-PSpc_71-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PSpc-71">&#91;71&#93;</a></sup> the <a href="/wiki/Incendio_Duo_Spell" title="Incendio Duo Spell">Incendio Duo Spell</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-CSps1_72-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-CSps1-72">&#91;72&#93;</a></sup> the <a href="/wiki/Lumos_Solem_Spell" title="Lumos Solem Spell">Lumos Solem Spell</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-PSf27_27-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PSf27-27">&#91;27&#93;</a></sup>, the <a href="/wiki/Herbivicus_Charm" title="Herbivicus Charm">Herbivicus Charm</a><sup id="cite_ref-GOFVG_73-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOFVG-73">&#91;73&#93;</a></sup> and even the <a href="/wiki/Severing_Charm" title="Severing Charm">Severing Charm</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-CSpc_74-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-CSpc-74">&#91;74&#93;</a></sup> She was also able to conjure a non-corporeal <a href="/wiki/Patronus_Charm" title="Patronus Charm">Patronus</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-LEGOY1_9-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-LEGOY1-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-LEGOY2_11-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-LEGOY2-11">&#91;11&#93;</a></sup> which is an incredibly advanced and powerful piece of protective <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magic</a> and concrete evidence of her possessing superior magical ability.</li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Potion" title="Potion">Potions</a></b>: As the Professor of Herbology, Sprout was also very skilled in potion-making, as she was highly familiar and accustomed to the various ingredients required for certain potions.<sup id="cite_ref-PS.2Fgbc_20-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PS.2Fgbc-20">&#91;20&#93;</a></sup> She also experimented with new ways of making potions, including <a href="/wiki/Dragon_dung_Fertiliser" title="Dragon dung Fertiliser">Dragon dung Fertiliser</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-HMy3ch1_24-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HMy3ch1-24">&#91;24&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Duelling" title="Duelling">Duelling</a></b>: Sprout was shown to have a considerable level of skill in <a href="/wiki/Duelling" title="Duelling">duelling</a>, given how she fought in the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a> and survived. She helped her co-workers, <a href="/wiki/Minerva_McGonagall" title="Minerva McGonagall">McGonagall</a> and <a href="/wiki/Filius_Flitwick" title="Filius Flitwick">Flitwick</a>, to chase <a href="/wiki/Severus_Snape" title="Severus Snape">Snape</a> out of Hogwarts.<sup id="cite_ref-DH30_62-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH30-62">&#91;62&#93;</a></sup> Later on, during the final battle, she took a troop of students up to the <a href="/wiki/Astronomy_Tower" title="Astronomy Tower">Astronomy Tower</a>, which was noted to be a great place to work spells.<sup id="cite_ref-DH31_64-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH31-64">&#91;64&#93;</a></sup> She eventually survived the battle, showing her excellent skills in <a href="/wiki/Martial_magic" title="Martial magic">martial magic</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-DH36_66-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH36-66">&#91;66&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Defence_Against_the_Dark_Arts" title="Defence Against the Dark Arts">Defence Against the Dark Arts</a></b>: Sprout was highly capable of fighting the <a href="/wiki/Dark_Arts" title="Dark Arts">Dark Forces</a>, as she demonstrated when she bravely and successfully fought in the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a> against the <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a>, highly dangerous <a href="/wiki/Dark_Wizard" title="Dark Wizard">Dark Wizards</a>, emerging herself victorious. She was also skilled at dealing with dark creatures, as she successfully repelled a <a href="/wiki/Boggart" title="Boggart">Boggart</a> when it appeared in one the Herbology greenhouses, with the <a href="/wiki/Boggart-Banishing_Spell" title="Boggart-Banishing Spell">Boggart-Banishing Spell</a> for <a href="/wiki/Penny_Haywood" title="Penny Haywood">Penny Haywood</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-HMy3ch1_24-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HMy3ch1-24">&#91;24&#93;</a></sup></li></ul>

<p>Pomona was usually kind to Harry Potter (as well as to his friends, <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione Granger</a> and <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ron Weasley</a>).
</p><p>During the <a href="/wiki/Triwizard_Tournament" title="Triwizard Tournament">Triwizard Tournament</a>, however, Sprout became somewhat distant towards Harry as she thought he was stealing <a href="/wiki/Cedric_Diggory" title="Cedric Diggory">Cedric Diggory</a>'s protagonism as Hogwarts Champion.<sup id="cite_ref-GF18_5-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GF18-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> After Diggory's death, Sprout became friendlier to Harry once again.
</p><p>Professor Sprout rewarded twenty <a href="/wiki/House_points" title="House points">house points</a> to <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> for his bravery in speaking out shortly after the publication of his interview on <i><a href="/wiki/The_Quibbler" title="The Quibbler">The Quibbler</a></i> about the <a href="/wiki/Return_of_Lord_Voldemort" title="Return of Lord Voldemort">return</a> of <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a>, saying that she was giving them to him for passing her a watering can (defying the <a href="/wiki/Campaign_to_discredit_Albus_Dumbledore_and_Harry_Potter" title="Campaign to discredit Albus Dumbledore and Harry Potter">position</a> of the <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">Ministry of Magic</a> and <a href="/wiki/Dolores_Umbridge" title="Dolores Umbridge">Dolores Umbridge</a>, then-<a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_High_Inquisitor" title="Hogwarts High Inquisitor">High Inquisitor</a> of <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts</a>)<sup id="cite_ref-OP29_49-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OP29-49">&#91;49&#93;</a></sup> In May, <a href="/wiki/1998" title="1998">1998</a>, Sprout helped defend <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts</a> to give time for <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry</a> to find <a href="/wiki/Rowena_Ravenclaw" title="Rowena Ravenclaw">Rowena Ravenclaw</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Rowena_Ravenclaw%27s_Diadem" class="mw-redirect" title="Rowena Ravenclaw&#39;s Diadem">Diadem</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-DH30_62-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH30-62">&#91;62&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-DH31_64-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH31-64">&#91;64&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Hogwarts_staff">Hogwarts staff</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Pomona_Sprout?veaction=edit&amp;section=24" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Hogwarts staff">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Pomona_Sprout?action=edit&amp;section=24" title="Edit section: Hogwarts staff"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Professor Sprout enjoyed a very good and professional relationship with the most of Hogwarts staff. Despite her respective strong friendship with <a href="/wiki/Minerva_McGonagall" title="Minerva McGonagall">Minerva McGonagall</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-DH30_62-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH30-62">&#91;62&#93;</a></sup> Sprout also very much respected <a href="/wiki/Albus_Dumbledore" title="Albus Dumbledore">Albus Dumbledore</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP30_59-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP30-59">&#91;59&#93;</a></sup> After the Headmaster's death, Sprout was distraught.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP30_59-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP30-59">&#91;59&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>She was always friendly with <a href="/wiki/Filius_Flitwick" title="Filius Flitwick">Filius Flitwick</a>, the Head of <a href="/wiki/Ravenclaw" title="Ravenclaw">Ravenclaw</a> house and <a href="/wiki/Charm" title="Charm">Charms</a> professor. Both Sprout and Flitwick supported <a href="/wiki/Albus_Dumbledore" title="Albus Dumbledore">Albus Dumbledore</a> and the <a href="/wiki/Order_of_the_Phoenix" title="Order of the Phoenix">Order of the Phoenix</a>, although they were not official members. After the death of Dumbledore, Flitwick and Sprout (along with others) sat and discussed about it and about <a href="/wiki/Severus_Snape" title="Severus Snape">Severus Snape</a>'s disloyalty.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP30_59-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP30-59">&#91;59&#93;</a></sup> The next year, they both tried to protect the students from <a href="/wiki/Amycus_Carrow" title="Amycus Carrow">Amycus</a> and <a href="/wiki/Alecto_Carrow" title="Alecto Carrow">Alecto Carrow</a> and fought at the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a> and survived.
</p><p>Sprout also appeared to be friendly with <a href="/wiki/Horace_Slughorn" title="Horace Slughorn">Horace Slughorn</a>, the <a href="/wiki/Potions_Master" title="Potions Master">Potions Master</a>. In 1997, Sprout helped Slughorn pick some herbs for <a href="/wiki/Potion" title="Potion">potions</a>. She left towards her Greenhouses after she had collected enough for Slughorn's third year class, with some to spare.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP22_55-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP22-55">&#91;55&#93;</a></sup> Slughorn also proceeded to steal some Venomous Tentacula leaves while Sprout was not around.<sup id="cite_ref-HBPf22_56-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBPf22-56">&#91;56&#93;</a></sup> However, he fought at the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a> with Sprout.<sup id="cite_ref-DHpt2_65-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DHpt2-65">&#91;65&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Other Hogwarts staff that she had a good relationship with were: <a href="/wiki/Poppy_Pomfrey" title="Poppy Pomfrey">Poppy Pomfrey</a>, the <a href="/wiki/Matron" title="Matron">Matron</a>, <a href="/wiki/Argus_Filch" title="Argus Filch">Argus Filch</a>, the caretaker, <a href="/wiki/Rubeus_Hagrid" title="Rubeus Hagrid">Rubeus Hagrid</a>, the <a href="/wiki/Care_of_Magical_Creatures" title="Care of Magical Creatures">Care of Magical Creatures</a> professor.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Pomona_Sprout?veaction=edit&amp;section=25" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Pomona_Sprout?action=edit&amp;section=25" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>In Roman mythology, <i>Pomona</i> was the goddess of fruit trees, gardens and orchards.<sup id="cite_ref-Mythology_76-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Mythology-76">&#91;76&#93;</a></sup> <i>Sprout</i> is a verb meaning "<i>to begin to grow, shoot forth, as a plant from a seed</i>".<sup id="cite_ref-SproutWiktionary_77-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-SproutWiktionary-77">&#91;77&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<ul><li>Sprout is portrayed by <a href="/wiki/Great_Britain" title="Great Britain">British</a> actress <a href="/wiki/Miriam_Margolyes" title="Miriam Margolyes">Miriam Margolyes</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_(film_series)" title="Harry Potter (film series)">film adaptations</a> of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Chamber_of_Secrets_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets (film)">Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets</a></i> and <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Deathly_Hallows:_Part_2" title="Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 2">Deathly Hallows: Part 2</a></i>.</li>
<li>The first physical appearance of Professor Sprout in media was in the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Philosopher%27s_Stone_(video_game)" title="Harry Potter and the Philosopher&#39;s Stone (video game)">PC/PS1 video game</a> of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Philosopher%27s_Stone" title="Harry Potter and the Philosopher&#39;s Stone">Harry Potter and the Philosopher's Stone</a></i>. This was long before any actress had been cast as Sprout, so her appearance was completely different from the later films and games. On the PC version, Sprout appeared as a short, not-really-overweight witch with straight black hair pulled back in a bun, a cheery face and emerald green robes. On the PS1 version, she was featured as a short witch (just a bit taller than the eleven-year old Harry Potter) wearing her red hair in a long plait down her back. Regardless of how she looked, she was voiced by <a href="/wiki/Eve_Karpf" title="Eve Karpf">Eve Karpf</a>, who also voiced McGonagall, Hooch and the Fat Lady in the same game.</li>
<li>During most of the first six books, Sprout was the only Head of House whose name was not an <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Alliteration" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Alliteration">alliteration</a>. This was changed when Horace Slughorn replaced Severus Snape as Head of Slytherin House in <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Half-Blood_Prince" title="Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince">Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince</a></i>. Up until the release of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Deathly_Hallows:_Part_1" title="Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 1">Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows, Part 1</a></i>, she was also the only Head of House (with the exception of Slughorn, who was first introduced in the sixth instalment) who had not appeared in all the films.</li>
<li>In the books, Professor Sprout is described as having grey, flyaway hair, while in the film versions of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Chamber_of_Secrets" title="Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets">Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets</a></i> and <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Deathly_Hallows" title="Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows">Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows</a></i>, she is depicted as having short, curly hair.</li>
<li>In <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Deathly_Hallows:_Part_2" title="Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 2">Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 2</a></i>, during the time between the first part and the second part of the Battle, Pomona was seen helping <a href="/wiki/Poppy_Pomfrey" title="Poppy Pomfrey">Madam Pomfrey</a> taking care of some <a href="/wiki/Students" class="mw-redirect" title="Students">students</a> near the tables.</li>
<li>In <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Deathly_Hallows:_Part_2" title="Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 2">Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 2</a></i>, plants are seen attacking <a href="/wiki/Giant" title="Giant">giants</a> at the beginning of the battle. This could be a part of Professor Sprout's protection.</li>
<li>In <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Deathly_Hallows:_Part_2" title="Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 2">Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 2</a></i>, when <a href="/wiki/Filius_Flitwick" title="Filius Flitwick">Filius Flitwick</a>, <a href="/wiki/Horace_Slughorn" title="Horace Slughorn">Horace Slughorn</a> and <a href="/wiki/Molly_Weasley" title="Molly Weasley">Molly Weasley</a> are placing <a href="/wiki/Protective_enchantment" title="Protective enchantment">Protective enchantments</a> around <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Castle" title="Hogwarts Castle">Hogwarts Castle</a>, a chain of spells can be seen coming from the Herbology greenhouses. This could be Professor Sprout casting the spell <i><a href="/wiki/Protego_Maxima" title="Protego Maxima">Protego Maxima</a></i> to help protect the school quickly as <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Voldemort</a> and his Death Eaters are coming to the castle.</li>
<li>In early German, Italian and Portuguese translations of the first book, Professor Sprout was accidentally referred to as a male, even though chapter 8 refers to her as a "<i>dumpy little witch</i>".</li>
<li>As Professor Sprout's name is related to plants, it is possible that a <a href="/wiki/Naming_Seer" title="Naming Seer">Naming Seer</a> was consulted when she was born.</li></ul>
