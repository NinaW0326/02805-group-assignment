This <b>individual</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggle</a> postman, whose route in <a href="/wiki/Little_Whinging" title="Little Whinging">Little Whinging</a> included <a href="/wiki/Privet_Drive" title="Privet Drive">Privet Drive</a>. In the summer of <a href="/wiki/1994" title="1994">1994</a>, the postman delivered a <a href="/wiki/Molly_Weasley%27s_letter_to_Vernon_and_Petunia_Dursley_(1994)" class="mw-redirect" title="Molly Weasley&#39;s letter to Vernon and Petunia Dursley (1994)">letter</a> to the <a href="/wiki/Dursley_family" title="Dursley family">Dursley family</a>, although he chose to ring the doorbell and deliver the letter personally instead of leaving it in the mailbox. The letter in question had been sent by <a href="/wiki/Molly_Weasley" title="Molly Weasley">Molly Weasley</a>, who chose to use <a href="/wiki/Muggle_Mail" title="Muggle Mail">Muggle post</a> instead of an <a href="/wiki/Owl" title="Owl">owl</a>, and was absolutely covered in postage stamps save for a small space where she had written their address. The postman had found this as rather amusing, although <a href="/wiki/Vernon_Dursley" title="Vernon Dursley">Vernon Dursley</a> did not.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
