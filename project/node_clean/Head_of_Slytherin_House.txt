<p>The <b>Head of Slytherin House</b> is a <a href="/wiki/Professor" title="Professor">Professor</a> at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> who is in charge of looking after and disciplining the students of their <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Houses" title="Hogwarts Houses">House</a>. The Head of House also serves as a Professor of a subject. Most, if not all, of the heads of <a href="/wiki/Slytherin" title="Slytherin">Slytherin</a> house attended Hogwarts and were sorted into Slytherin. It is unknown how a professor would be elected for the post, but it's possible that the headmaster chooses the professor who was sorted into Slytherin and embodies the qualities that house founder <a href="/wiki/Salazar_Slytherin" title="Salazar Slytherin">Salazar Slytherin</a> possessed such as determination, resourcefulness, and cunning.<sup id="cite_ref-PS7_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PS7-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-OOTP11_4-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP11-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<ul><li><figure class="thumb tright show-info-icon" style="width: 182px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/8/8b/Severus_Snape_Profile.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20081103135333" class="image"><img alt="" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw%3D%3D" decoding="async" width="180" height="254" class="thumbimage lazyload" data-image-name="Severus Snape Profile.jpg" data-image-key="Severus_Snape_Profile.jpg" data-src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/8/8b/Severus_Snape_Profile.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/180?cb=20081103135333" /></a> 	<noscript><a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/8/8b/Severus_Snape_Profile.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20081103135333" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/8/8b/Severus_Snape_Profile.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/180?cb=20081103135333" decoding="async" width="180" height="254" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="Severus Snape Profile.jpg" data-image-key="Severus_Snape_Profile.jpg" data-src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/8/8b/Severus_Snape_Profile.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/180?cb=20081103135333" /></a></noscript> 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:Severus_Snape_Profile.jpg" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption"><a href="/wiki/Severus_Snape" title="Severus Snape">Severus Snape</a>, Head of Slytherin House from <a href="/wiki/1981" title="1981">1981</a> to <a href="/wiki/1997" title="1997">1997</a> and later Headmaster</p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure><a href="/wiki/Salazar_Slytherin" title="Salazar Slytherin">Salazar Slytherin</a> (c. <a href="/wiki/11th_century" title="11th century">1000</a>) Salazar Slytherin established the Slytherin House. He had a very strong belief that only pure-blood witches and wizards should be trained at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a>. Because of his convictions, he often had disagreements with the other founders to the school, particularly <a href="/wiki/Godric_Gryffindor" title="Godric Gryffindor">Godric Gryffindor</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-COS9_2-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COS9-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup><br />&#160;</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Horace_Slughorn" title="Horace Slughorn">Horace Slughorn</a> (between the <a href="/wiki/1920s" title="1920s">1920s</a> and <a href="/wiki/1981" title="1981">1981</a>) Horace was the Potions master and was well-known for his "<a href="/wiki/Slug_Club" title="Slug Club">Slug Club</a>," a group of talented, well-connected, or famous students he favoured.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP4_5-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP4-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-HBP7_6-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP7-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup> Partly through the Slug Club, Horace developed a very close relationship with <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Tom Riddle</a>. He told Tom about <a href="/wiki/Horcrux" title="Horcrux">Horcruxes</a>, believing the information he gave out would purely be used for educational purposes.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP23_7-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP23-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup> When later incidents proved otherwise, Horace retired from his position, presumably because he felt ashamed of himself for the mistake he had made.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP22_8-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP22-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Severus_Snape" title="Severus Snape">Severus Snape</a> (between <a href="/wiki/1981" title="1981">1981</a> and <a href="/wiki/1990" title="1990">1990</a> - <a href="/wiki/1997" title="1997">1997</a>) Severus was the Potions master at Hogwarts,<sup id="cite_ref-PS8_9-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PS8-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup> and served as a <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eater</a> for a while. When Snape learned of <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Voldemort</a>'s plan to kill the <a href="/wiki/Potter_family" title="Potter family">Potter family</a>, likely including <a href="/wiki/Lily_J._Potter" title="Lily J. Potter">Lily Potter</a> whom Severus had romantic feelings for, Snape served as a double agent, secretly working behind Voldemort's back for <a href="/wiki/Albus_Dumbledore" title="Albus Dumbledore">Dumbledore</a> even after his death.<sup id="cite_ref-DH33_10-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH33-10">&#91;10&#93;</a></sup> His career ended when <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Voldemort</a> killed him in the hopes of gaining the full allegiance of the <a href="/wiki/Elder_Wand" title="Elder Wand">Elder Wand</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-DH32_11-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH32-11">&#91;11&#93;</a></sup> Snape was a difficult teacher, and was disliked by many students outside of Slytherin House.<sup id="cite_ref-PS8_9-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PS8-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-COS5_12-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COS5-12">&#91;12&#93;</a></sup> He had a special dislike for <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> because of a grudge against Harry's father.<sup id="cite_ref-POA14_13-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-POA14-13">&#91;13&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-OOTP28_14-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP28-14">&#91;14&#93;</a></sup> It was also well-known that Snape deeply desired the <a href="/wiki/Defence_Against_the_Dark_Arts" title="Defence Against the Dark Arts">Defence Against the Dark Arts</a> position, but he never got his chance to teach that class until <a href="/wiki/1996" title="1996">1996</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP8_15-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP8-15">&#91;15&#93;</a></sup><br />&#160;</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Horace_Slughorn" title="Horace Slughorn">Horace Slughorn</a> (<a href="/wiki/1997" title="1997">1997</a> - Pre <a href="/wiki/2016" title="2016">2016</a>)<sup id="cite_ref-shortstories_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-shortstories-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> Horace, after being convinced by <a href="/wiki/Albus_Dumbledore" title="Albus Dumbledore">Dumbledore</a> and <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>, returned to Hogwarts as the Potions master.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP4_5-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP4-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> After Severus killed Dumbledore and became headmaster of Hogwarts, it was decided that Horace was to become the Head of Slytherin House once again.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP29_16-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP29-16">&#91;16&#93;</a></sup> Following his second retirement, a portrait of Slughorn was placed in the common room.<sup id="cite_ref-shortstories_1-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-shortstories-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup></li></ul>
