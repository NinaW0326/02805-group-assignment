He was a member of the <a href="/wiki/Slytherin_Quidditch_team" title="Slytherin Quidditch team">Slytherin Quidditch team</a>, serving as their <a href="/wiki/Keeper" title="Keeper">Keeper</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-PS11_2-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PS11-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="History">History</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Miles_Bletchley?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: History">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Miles_Bletchley?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: History"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span id="1991–1992_school_year"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="1991.E2.80.931992_school_year"><a href="/wiki/1991%E2%80%931992_school_year" title="1991–1992 school year">1991–1992 school year</a></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Miles_Bletchley?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: 1991–1992 school year">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Miles_Bletchley?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: 1991–1992 school year"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Bletchley played in <a href="/wiki/Slytherin" title="Slytherin">Slytherin</a>'s match against <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor" title="Gryffindor">Gryffindor</a> this year. He was unable to block <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor" title="Gryffindor">Gryffindor</a>'s only goal attempt during that match, from <a href="/wiki/Angelina_Johnson" title="Angelina Johnson">Angelina Johnson</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-PS11_2-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PS11-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="1992-1993_school_year"><a href="/wiki/1992-1993_school_year" class="mw-redirect" title="1992-1993 school year">1992-1993 school year</a></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Miles_Bletchley?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: 1992-1993 school year">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Miles_Bletchley?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: 1992-1993 school year"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Bletchley, along with the rest of the team, received a new <a href="/wiki/Nimbus_2001" title="Nimbus 2001">Nimbus 2001</a> this year from <a href="/wiki/Lucius_Malfoy" title="Lucius Malfoy">Lucius Malfoy</a> as an incentive to allow his son <a href="/wiki/Draco_Malfoy" title="Draco Malfoy">Draco</a> onto the team as <a href="/wiki/Seeker" title="Seeker">Seeker</a>. He and the rest of the team flaunted these new <a href="/wiki/Broomstick" title="Broomstick">broomsticks</a> to the <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor_Quidditch_team" title="Gryffindor Quidditch team">Gryffindor Quidditch team</a>. He laughed along with the other members of the team at <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ronald Weasley</a> when his <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley%27s_first_wand" class="mw-redirect" title="Ronald Weasley&#39;s first wand">broken wand</a> redirected an attempted <a href="/wiki/Slug-Vomiting_Charm" class="mw-redirect" title="Slug-Vomiting Charm">Slug-Vomiting Charm</a> back at him.<sup id="cite_ref-COSF_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COSF-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span id="1995–1996_school_year"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="1995.E2.80.931996_school_year"><a href="/wiki/1995%E2%80%931996_school_year" title="1995–1996 school year">1995–1996 school year</a></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Miles_Bletchley?veaction=edit&amp;section=4" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: 1995–1996 school year">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Miles_Bletchley?action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: 1995–1996 school year"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>During this year, Bletchley took part in a small plot by the Slytherin Quidditch team to <a href="/wiki/Jinx" title="Jinx">jinx</a> various members of the Gryffindor team prior to their first match. He hit <a href="/wiki/Alicia_Spinnet" title="Alicia Spinnet">Alicia Spinnet</a> with a jinx that made her eyebrows grow excessively large. Despite doing this in front of fourteen witnesses, <a href="/wiki/Severus_Snape" title="Severus Snape">Severus Snape</a>, who seemed to support this action, merely chose to assume she had used a poorly applied <a href="/wiki/Hair-Thickening_Charm" class="mw-redirect" title="Hair-Thickening Charm">Hair-Thickening Charm</a> on herself, and Bletchley was not punished.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP19_4-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP19-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Later, during the match itself, Bletchley managed to save at least one attempted goal by Angelina Johnson. He, along with the rest of his team and the Slytherin supporters, sang "<a href="/wiki/Weasley_is_Our_King" title="Weasley is Our King">Weasley is Our King</a>" throughout the match in a successful attempt to unnerve Ron Weasley, Gryffindor's new Keeper.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP19_4-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP19-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Personality_and_traits">Personality and traits</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Miles_Bletchley?veaction=edit&amp;section=5" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Personality and traits">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Miles_Bletchley?action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Personality and traits"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Miles was a mean-spirited individual who lacked good sportsmanship. He took enjoyment in mocking others,<sup id="cite_ref-COSF_3-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COSF-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> and evidently saw no problem with foul play against members of the Gryffindor Quidditch team.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP19_4-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP19-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Magical_abilities_and_skills">Magical abilities and skills</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Miles_Bletchley?veaction=edit&amp;section=6" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Miles_Bletchley?action=edit&amp;section=6" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li><b><a href="/wiki/Flight" title="Flight">Flying</a></b>: Miles played as <a href="/wiki/Keeper" title="Keeper">Keeper</a> for the <a href="/wiki/Slytherin_Quidditch_team" title="Slytherin Quidditch team">Slytherin Quidditch team</a> at Hogwarts, meaning he was a fairly accomplished and talented <a href="/wiki/Broomstick" title="Broomstick">broomstick</a> flyer and <a href="/wiki/Quidditch" title="Quidditch">Quidditch</a> player.<sup id="cite_ref-PS11_2-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PS11-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Dark_Arts" title="Dark Arts">Dark Arts</a></b>: Miles <a href="/wiki/Jinx" title="Jinx">jinxed</a> <a href="/wiki/Alicia_Spinnet" title="Alicia Spinnet">Alicia Spinnet</a> with an unpleasant jinx that caused her eyebrows to grow rapidly, meaning he had some skill with <a href="/wiki/Dark_charm" title="Dark charm">Dark charms</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP19_4-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP19-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup></li></ul>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Miles_Bletchley?veaction=edit&amp;section=7" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Miles_Bletchley?action=edit&amp;section=7" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>In the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Philosopher%27s_Stone_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Philosopher&#39;s Stone (film)">film adaptation</a> of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Philosopher%27s_Stone" title="Harry Potter and the Philosopher&#39;s Stone">Harry Potter and the Philosopher's Stone</a></i>, the Slytherin <a href="/wiki/Keeper" title="Keeper">Keeper</a> was played by <a href="/wiki/Amy_Domenica" title="Amy Domenica">Amy Puglia</a>, despite there never having been any girls on the Slytherin squad in the books. In <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Chamber_of_Secrets_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets (film)">Chamber of Secrets</a>,</i> he instead was depicted as a redhead and was played by <a href="/wiki/David_Churchyard" title="David Churchyard">David Churchyard</a>.</li>
<li>In the video game <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter:_Quidditch_World_Cup" title="Harry Potter: Quidditch World Cup">Harry Potter: Quidditch World Cup</a></i>, the Slytherin Keeper is named "<a href="/wiki/Kevin_Bletchley" title="Kevin Bletchley">Kevin Bletchley</a>". It is possible this was intended to be Miles.</li></ul>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Miles_Bletchley?veaction=edit&amp;section=8" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Miles_Bletchley?action=edit&amp;section=8" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>"Miles" is from the Germanic name <i>Milo</i>, introduced by the Normans to England in the form <i>Miles</i>. The meaning is not known for certain. It is possibly connected to the Slavic name element <i>mil</i> meaning "gracious". From an early date it was associated with Latin <i>miles</i> "soldier".
</p>
