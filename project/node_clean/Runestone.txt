A <b>runestone</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magical</a> stone engraved with a <a href="/wiki/Rune" title="Rune">rune</a>. In the <a href="/wiki/21st_century" title="21st century">21st century</a>, the <a href="/wiki/Statute_of_Secrecy_Task_Force" title="Statute of Secrecy Task Force">Statute of Secrecy Task Force</a> used them to access various challenges that were related to the <a href="/wiki/Calamity" title="Calamity">Calamity</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-WU_1-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-WU-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="History">History</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Runestone?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: History">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Runestone?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: History"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>During the <a href="/wiki/Calamity" title="Calamity">Calamity</a> which occurred throughout the <a href="/wiki/2010s" title="2010s">2010s</a>, these mysterious <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magical</a> stones started appearing throughout the <a href="/wiki/Wizarding_world" title="Wizarding world">wizarding world</a>. They allowed volunteer members of the <a href="/wiki/Statute_of_Secrecy_Task_Force" title="Statute of Secrecy Task Force">Statute of Secrecy Task Force</a> to participate in magical challenges in <a href="/wiki/Fortress" title="Fortress">Fortresses</a>, such as <a href="/wiki/Duelling" title="Duelling">duelling</a> other <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizards</a> or fighting aggressive and dangerous <a href="/wiki/Creature" title="Creature">creatures</a>, such as <a href="/wiki/Acromantula" title="Acromantula">Acromantulas</a> and <a href="/wiki/Werewolf" title="Werewolf">Werewolves</a>. Many different runestones existed which allowed individuals to enter all sorts of challenges in Fortresses.<sup id="cite_ref-WU_1-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-WU-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
