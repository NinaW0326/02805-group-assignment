<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Google_Flange?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Google_Flange?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Google Flange lived during the early <a href="/wiki/17th_century" title="17th century">17th century</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-wwhp_2-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-wwhp-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> At some point in his lifetime, he was <a href="/wiki/Knight" title="Knight">knighted</a>, thus earning the title "Sir."<sup id="cite_ref-wwhp_2-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-wwhp-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>He died in or before <a href="/wiki/1991" title="1991">1991</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-DOD_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DOD-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> A <a href="/wiki/Portrait" title="Portrait">portrait</a> of him, dated <a href="/wiki/1600" class="mw-redirect" title="1600">1600</a> and featuring the word "abracadabra" written in the shape of triangle, was hung at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Castle" title="Hogwarts Castle">Hogwarts Castle</a> sometime after his death.<sup id="cite_ref-wwhp_2-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-wwhp-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-PSf16_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PSf16-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> In the <a href="/wiki/1990s" title="1990s">1990s</a>, it was hanging in the <a href="/wiki/Grand_Staircase" title="Grand Staircase">Grand Staircase</a>, near the <a href="/wiki/Third-floor_corridor" title="Third-floor corridor">Third-floor corridor</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-PSf16_3-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PSf16-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Google_Flange?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Google_Flange?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>The portrait of Google Flange was based on a <a target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener" class="external text" href="http://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/5/52/King_Charles_I_by_Gerrit_van_Honthorst_sm.jpg">1628 portrait</a> of <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Charles_I_of_England" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Charles I of England">Charles I of England</a> by <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gerard_van_Honthorst" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Gerard van Honthorst">Gerard van Honthorst</a>.</li>
<li>Writing the word "abracadabra" repeatedly to form a triangle, as it appears in the portrait, is an ancient mystical practise.<sup id="cite_ref-abrawiki_4-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-abrawiki-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Quintus_Serenus_Sammonicus" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Quintus Serenus Sammonicus">Quintus Serenus Sammonicus</a>, a <a href="/wiki/First_millennium_A.D." title="First millennium A.D.">third century A.D.</a> <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ancient_Rome" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Ancient Rome">Roman</a> physician, advised people afflicted with <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/malaria" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:malaria">malaria</a> to wear amulets bearing such an inscription, claiming it warded off disease.<sup id="cite_ref-abrawiki_4-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-abrawiki-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li>At <i><a href="/wiki/The_Making_of_Harry_Potter" title="The Making of Harry Potter">The Making of Harry Potter</a></i>, the portrait of <a href="/wiki/Poderick_Crookshank" title="Poderick Crookshank">Poderick Crookshank</a> was erroneously put in a frame inscribed "Sir Google Flange," but Crookshank was correctly identified in <i><a href="/wiki/The_Paintings_of_Hogwarts:_Masterpieces_from_the_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry_Sets" title="The Paintings of Hogwarts: Masterpieces from the School of Witchcraft and Wizardry Sets">The Paintings of Hogwarts: Masterpieces from the School of Witchcraft and Wizardry Sets</a>.</i></li></ul>
