<p><b>Expelliarmus Gargoyles</b>&#160;are sentient, malevolent&#160;<a href="/wiki/Gargoyle" title="Gargoyle">gargoyles</a> that have been <a href="/wiki/Piertotum_Locomotor" title="Piertotum Locomotor">animated</a> to attack individuals. They appear at the end of many <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts</a> <a href="/wiki/Spell" title="Spell">Spell</a> challenges, except the <i><a href="/wiki/Fire-Making_Spell" title="Fire-Making Spell">Incendio</a></i> challenge where three must be fought. It will offensively fire bolts of yellow <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magical</a> energy in order to harm an individual. Its only weakness is the <a href="/wiki/Disarming_Charm" title="Disarming Charm">Disarming Charm</a>, in which its spells can be deflected with; furthermore, the Gargoyle can deflect spells simply by using its wings as shields. Several deflected spells are needed to strike and harm the Gargoyle, which is needed to destroy it and reduce it into rubble.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Expelliarmus_Gargoyle?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Expelliarmus_Gargoyle?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>These Gargoyles only appear as recurring bosses in the XBOX, PlayStation 2 and Game Cube versions of the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Chamber_of_Secrets_(video_game)" title="Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets (video game)">video game adaptation</a>&#160;of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Chamber_of_Secrets" title="Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets">Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets</a></i>. Thus, these Gargoyles cannot be considered <a href="/wiki/Canon" title="Canon">canonical</a>, as they only appear in non-canonical <a href="/wiki/Spell" title="Spell">spell</a> challenges; furthermore, the <a href="/wiki/Disarming_Charm" title="Disarming Charm">Disarming Charm</a> does not deflect spells, rendering their usage fully void.</li></ul>
