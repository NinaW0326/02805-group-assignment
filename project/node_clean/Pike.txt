<b>Pike</b><sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> (b. <a href="/wiki/1979" title="1979">1979</a>-<a href="/wiki/1980" title="1980">1980</a>) was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> who attended <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts</a> in the <a href="/wiki/1990s" title="1990s">1990s</a> and was <a href="/wiki/Sorting_Hat" title="Sorting Hat">Sorted</a> into <a href="/wiki/Slytherin" title="Slytherin">Slytherin</a> <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Houses" title="Hogwarts Houses">house</a>. He was commonly seen between <a href="/wiki/1993" title="1993">1993</a> and <a href="/wiki/1994" title="1994">1994</a> with <a href="/wiki/Draco_Malfoy" title="Draco Malfoy">Draco Malfoy</a>, <a href="/wiki/Vincent_Crabbe" title="Vincent Crabbe">Vincent Crabbe</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Gregory_Goyle" title="Gregory Goyle">Gregory Goyle</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-POAF_2-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-POAF-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Pike?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Pike?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<figure class="thumb tleft show-info-icon" style="width: 182px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/8/8a/SlytherinBoy.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20090322174413" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/8/8a/SlytherinBoy.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/180?cb=20090322174413" decoding="async" width="180" height="135" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="SlytherinBoy.jpg" data-image-key="SlytherinBoy.jpg" /></a> 	 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:SlytherinBoy.jpg" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption">Pike in <a href="/wiki/Hogsmeade" title="Hogsmeade">Hogsmeade</a> near the Shrieking Shack</p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure>
<p>Pike began his education at Hogwarts at some point, and was <a href="/wiki/Sorting_ceremony" title="Sorting ceremony">sorted</a> into <a href="/wiki/Slytherin" title="Slytherin">Slytherin</a> <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Houses" title="Hogwarts Houses">house</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-POAF_2-10" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-POAF-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>He was part of <a href="/wiki/Draco_Malfoy" title="Draco Malfoy">Draco Malfoy</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Draco_Malfoy%27s_gang" title="Draco Malfoy&#39;s gang">gang</a> during the <a href="/wiki/1993%E2%80%931994_school_year" title="1993–1994 school year">1993–1994 school year</a> and was one of the Slytherins that cruelly imitated a <a href="/wiki/Dementor" title="Dementor">Dementor</a> in an effort to scare <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> in the first <a href="/wiki/Care_of_Magical_Creatures" title="Care of Magical Creatures">Care of Magical Creatures</a> class of the year. He was also with Malfoy in the <a href="/wiki/Stone_Circle" title="Stone Circle">Stone Circle</a> when Malfoy was <a href="/wiki/Muggle_duelling" title="Muggle duelling">punched</a> by <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione Granger</a> in <a href="/wiki/1994" title="1994">1994</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-POAF_2-11" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-POAF-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Pike?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Pike?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>Pike was portrayed by <a href="/wiki/Bronson_Webb" title="Bronson Webb">Bronson Webb</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Prisoner_of_Azkaban_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban (film)">film adaptation</a> of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Prisoner_of_Azkaban" title="Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban">Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban</a></i><sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li>Before his name was revealed many fans assumed this individual was <a href="/wiki/Theodore_Nott" title="Theodore Nott">Theodore Nott</a>. Pike is somewhat similar in appearance to the way Nott is described in the novels. The difference however is that Pike is shown to be a member of Draco's gang while Nott is only loosely associated with the gang.</li>
<li>It is possible that Pike was created specifically to replace <a href="/wiki/Gregory_Goyle" title="Gregory Goyle">Gregory Goyle</a> in certain action-oriented scenes as <a href="/wiki/Josh_Herdman" title="Josh Herdman">Josh Herdman</a> had injured his arm and would have been unable to perform them. For instance, Pike replaces Goyle in the scene where Malfoy and his gang harass Ron and Hermione outside the <a href="/wiki/Shrieking_Shack" title="Shrieking Shack">Shrieking Shack</a>.</li></ul>
