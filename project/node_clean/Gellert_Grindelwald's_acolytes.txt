<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="History">History</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Gellert_Grindelwald%27s_acolytes?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: History">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Gellert_Grindelwald%27s_acolytes?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: History"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<figure class="thumb tleft show-info-icon" style="width: 222px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/9/9d/Grindelwald_Vinda_Abernathy.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20190325020928" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/9/9d/Grindelwald_Vinda_Abernathy.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/220?cb=20190325020928" decoding="async" width="220" height="91" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="Grindelwald Vinda Abernathy.jpg" data-image-key="Grindelwald_Vinda_Abernathy.jpg" /></a> 	 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:Grindelwald_Vinda_Abernathy.jpg" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption"><a href="/wiki/Gellert_Grindelwald" title="Gellert Grindelwald">Grindelwald</a> with his acolytes, <a href="/wiki/Vinda_Rosier" title="Vinda Rosier">Vinda Rosier</a> and <a href="/wiki/Abernathy" title="Abernathy">Abernathy</a></p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure>
<p>In 1927, they accompanied Grindelwald to <a href="/wiki/Paris" title="Paris">Paris</a> to assist with his plans in recruiting <a href="/wiki/Credence_Barebone" title="Credence Barebone">Credence Barebone</a>. They killed the unknown couple and their small child in order to take over their apartment as a temporary headquarters. When Grindelwald, during a <a href="/wiki/Gellert_Grindelwald%27s_Paris_rally" title="Gellert Grindelwald&#39;s Paris rally">rally</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Lestrange_Mausoleum" title="Lestrange Mausoleum">Lestrange family Mausoleum</a>, conjured a <a href="/wiki/Protego_Diabolica" title="Protego Diabolica">circle of protective fire</a> around himself, and added an enchantment designed to test the loyalty of his followers: those who entered with complete fidelity and believed in his cause would survive; those who did not, would be engulfed and perish. Krafft, Nagel, MacDuff, Carrow, Abernathy, Vinda Rosier, Credence Barebone, Queenie Goldstein and several <a href="/wiki/Auror" title="Auror">Aurors</a> who had initially been under the command of <a href="/wiki/Theseus_Scamander" title="Theseus Scamander">Theseus Scamander</a> passed through unharmed, but Krall, who had had second thoughts, died in fire.<sup id="cite_ref-COGS_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COGS-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
