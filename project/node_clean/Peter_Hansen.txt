<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Peter_Hansen?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Peter_Hansen?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>He played as the <a href="/wiki/Chaser" title="Chaser">Chaser</a> on the <a href="/wiki/Nordic_National_Quidditch_team" title="Nordic National Quidditch team">Nordic National Quidditch team</a> during the four-hundred and twenty-second <a href="/wiki/Quidditch_World_Cup" title="Quidditch World Cup">Quidditch World Cup</a>, in <a href="/wiki/1994" title="1994">1994</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-QWC_1-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-QWC-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> He was possibly <a href="/wiki/Denmark" title="Denmark">Danish</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>During the <a href="/wiki/1996%E2%80%931997_school_year" title="1996–1997 school year">1996–1997 school year</a>, Hansen's <a href="/wiki/Chocolate_Frog_Card" title="Chocolate Frog Card">Chocolate Frog Card</a> was able to be won by beating <a href="/wiki/Helen_Dawlish" title="Helen Dawlish">Helen Dawlish</a> at a <a href="/wiki/Duelling" title="Duelling">duelling</a> challenge.<sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<ul><li><b><a href="/wiki/Flight" title="Flight">Flying</a></b>: As a <a href="/wiki/Chaser" title="Chaser">Chaser</a> of the <a href="/wiki/Nordic_National_Quidditch_team" title="Nordic National Quidditch team">Nordic National Quidditch team</a>, he was a highly proficient and talented <a href="/wiki/Broomstick" title="Broomstick">broomstick</a> flyer and <a href="/wiki/Quidditch" title="Quidditch">Quidditch</a>, with his skill and experience in the <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magical</a> sport clearly being demonstrated on the pitch and with his esteemed position.</li></ul>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Peter_Hansen?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Peter_Hansen?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<figure class="thumb tright show-info-icon" style="width: 252px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/e/e5/Peter_Hansen.jpeg/revision/latest?cb=20121021025014" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/e/e5/Peter_Hansen.jpeg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/250?cb=20121021025014" decoding="async" width="250" height="188" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="Peter Hansen.jpeg" data-image-key="Peter_Hansen.jpeg" /></a> 	 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:Peter_Hansen.jpeg" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption">Peter Hansen with Quaffle</p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure>
<p>The name "Peter" is derived from the Greek Πετρος (<i>Petros</i>) meaning "stone". This is a translation used in most versions of the New Testament of the name <i>Cephas</i>, meaning "stone" in Aramaic, which was given to the apostle Simon by Jesus'. Simon Peter was the most prominent of the apostles during Jesus's ministry and is often considered the first pope.
</p>
