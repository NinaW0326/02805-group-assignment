<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Dexter_Fortescue?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Dexter_Fortescue?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="As_Headmaster_of_Hogwarts">As Headmaster of Hogwarts</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Dexter_Fortescue?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: As Headmaster of Hogwarts">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Dexter_Fortescue?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: As Headmaster of Hogwarts"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Dexter Fortescue was made <a href="/wiki/Headmaster" title="Headmaster">Headmaster</a> of <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> in an unknown time period, certainly sometime after the early <a href="/wiki/18th_century" title="18th century">18th century</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-Ministry_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Ministry-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> After his death, his portrait was hung at the <a href="/wiki/Headmaster%27s_office" class="mw-redirect" title="Headmaster&#39;s office">Headmaster's office</a>, in order to offer advice to the current Head.<sup id="cite_ref-OP27_4-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OP27-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Post-mortem">Post-mortem</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Dexter_Fortescue?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Post-mortem">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Dexter_Fortescue?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Post-mortem"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>In his lifetime, Professor Fortescue had at least one child. His surname lived on to the <a href="/wiki/1990s" title="1990s">1990s</a>, in the person of <a href="/wiki/Florean_Fortescue" title="Florean Fortescue">Florean Fortescue</a>, his direct descendant, who ran <a href="/wiki/Florean_Fortescue%27s_Ice_Cream_Parlour" title="Florean Fortescue&#39;s Ice Cream Parlour">Florean Fortescue's Ice Cream Parlour</a> in <a href="/wiki/Diagon_Alley" title="Diagon Alley">Diagon Alley</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-PM_5-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PM-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="1995">1995</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Dexter_Fortescue?veaction=edit&amp;section=4" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: 1995">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Dexter_Fortescue?action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: 1995"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<div class="quote">
<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix">"<i>Insubordination, sir! Dereliction of duty!</i>"</span></dd>
<dd>&#8212;Fortescue to <a href="/wiki/Phineas_Nigellus_Black" title="Phineas Nigellus Black">Phineas Nigellus</a><sup class="noprint"><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Order_of_the_Phoenix" title="Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix">[src]</a></sup></dd>
<dd></dd></dl></div><p>Shortly after <a href="/wiki/Arthur_Weasley" title="Arthur Weasley">Arthur Weasley</a> was attacked by <a href="/wiki/Nagini" title="Nagini">Nagini</a> at the <a href="/wiki/Department_of_Mysteries" title="Department of Mysteries">Department of Mysteries</a>, in <a href="/wiki/1995" title="1995">1995</a>, Professor <a href="/wiki/Albus_Dumbledore" title="Albus Dumbledore">Albus Dumbledore</a> attempted to wake up the portrait of <a href="/wiki/Phineas_Nigellus_Black" title="Phineas Nigellus Black">Phineas Nigellus Black</a>, to tell <a href="/wiki/Sirius_Black" title="Sirius Black">Sirius Black</a> that <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> and the <a href="/wiki/Weasley_family" title="Weasley family">Weasley</a> children were going to <a href="/wiki/12_Grimmauld_Place" title="12 Grimmauld Place">12 Grimmauld Place</a>. As Phineas expressed unwillingness to help, the portrait of Dexter Fortescue was particularly vocal, as was <a href="/wiki/Armando_Dippet" title="Armando Dippet">Armando Dippet</a> and a portrait of an <a href="/wiki/Gimlet-eyed_Hogwarts_Headmistress" title="Gimlet-eyed Hogwarts Headmistress">unidentified female Head</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-OP22_6-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OP22-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup>
</p><div class="quote">
<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix">"<i>Blatant corruption! The Ministry did not cut deals with petty criminals in my day, no sir, they did not!</i>"</span></dd>
<dd>&#8212;Fortescue to <a href="/wiki/Cornelius_Fudge" title="Cornelius Fudge">Cornelius Fudge</a> and <a href="/wiki/Dolores_Umbridge" title="Dolores Umbridge">Dolores Umbridge</a><sup class="noprint"><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Order_of_the_Phoenix" title="Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix">[src]</a></sup></dd>
<dd></dd></dl></div>
<p>In <a href="/wiki/1995" title="1995">1995</a>, he roared his disapproval at <a href="/wiki/Cornelius_Fudge" title="Cornelius Fudge">Cornelius Fudge</a> and <a href="/wiki/Dolores_Umbridge" title="Dolores Umbridge">Dolores Umbridge</a> when the latter explained to the former how she had learned of the first meeting of <a href="/wiki/Dumbledore%27s_Army" title="Dumbledore&#39;s Army">Dumbledore's Army</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Hog%27s_Head" class="mw-redirect" title="Hog&#39;s Head">Hog's Head</a>. She explained that <a href="/wiki/Willy_Widdershins" title="Willy Widdershins">Willy Widdershins</a> had been there and had reported back. <a href="/wiki/Minerva_McGonagall" title="Minerva McGonagall">Minerva McGonagall</a>, also present, commented that she had wondered how Willy had gotten off so lightly after all those <a href="/wiki/Regurgitating_Toilet" title="Regurgitating Toilet">regurgitating toilets</a>, and Fortescue erupted, saying that they did not cut deals with petty criminals in his day.<sup id="cite_ref-OP27_4-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OP27-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="1998">1998</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Dexter_Fortescue?veaction=edit&amp;section=5" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: 1998">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Dexter_Fortescue?action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: 1998"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<p>When <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> went to the <a href="/wiki/Headmaster%27s_office" class="mw-redirect" title="Headmaster&#39;s office">Headmaster's office</a>, shortly after <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Voldemort%27s_Last_Stand" title="Voldemort&#39;s Last Stand">defeat</a> and the end of the <a href="/wiki/Second_Wizarding_War" title="Second Wizarding War">Second Wizarding War</a>, Fortescue joined the ovation made by the previous <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts</a> <a href="/wiki/Headmaster_portraits" title="Headmaster portraits">Headmaster</a>, waving him his ear trumpet.<sup id="cite_ref-DH36_7-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH36-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Personality_and_traits">Personality and traits</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Dexter_Fortescue?veaction=edit&amp;section=6" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Personality and traits">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Dexter_Fortescue?action=edit&amp;section=6" title="Edit section: Personality and traits"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Fortescue believed in honour and duty, reprimanding Phineas Nigellus for "dereliction of duty" and bellowing at Cornelius Fudge, Dolores Umbridge, <a href="/wiki/John_Dawlish" title="John Dawlish">John Dawlish</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Kingsley_Shacklebolt" title="Kingsley Shacklebolt">Kingsley Shacklebolt</a> for cutting deals with petty criminals.<sup id="cite_ref-OP27_4-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OP27-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>In life, he was hard of hearing, necessitating the use of an ear trumpet before his death, a trait passed along to his portrait self who is seen using it to listen to private conversations in the Headmaster's office and speaking loudly when addressing observers or other portraits.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Dexter_Fortescue?veaction=edit&amp;section=7" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Dexter_Fortescue?action=edit&amp;section=7" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li><a href="/wiki/J._K._Rowling" title="J. K. Rowling">J. K. Rowling</a> has confirmed on  <i><a href="/wiki/Pottermore" title="Pottermore">Pottermore</a></i> that <a href="/wiki/Florean_Fortescue" title="Florean Fortescue">Florean Fortescue</a>, who ran an <a href="/wiki/Florean_Fortescue%27s_Ice_Cream_Parlour" title="Florean Fortescue&#39;s Ice Cream Parlour">ice cream parlour</a> in <a href="/wiki/Diagon_Alley" title="Diagon Alley">Diagon Alley</a>, is one of his descendants.<sup id="cite_ref-PM_5-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PM-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup></li></ul>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Dexter_Fortescue?veaction=edit&amp;section=8" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Dexter_Fortescue?action=edit&amp;section=8" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The name "Dexter" comes from an occupational surname meaning "one who dyes" in Old English. It also coincides with the Latin word <i>dexter</i> meaning "right-handed, skilled".
</p>
