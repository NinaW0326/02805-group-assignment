<b>Corvus Lestrange</b> (b. pre <a href="/wiki/1879" class="mw-redirect" title="1879">1879</a> - d. pre <a href="/wiki/1927" title="1927">1927</a>) was a <a href="/wiki/France" title="France">French</a> <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> and a member of the <a href="/wiki/Pure-blood" title="Pure-blood">pure-blood</a><sup id="cite_ref-Blood_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Blood-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/Lestrange_family" title="Lestrange family">Lestrange family</a>. He was the son of <a href="/wiki/Corvus_Lestrange_(III)" title="Corvus Lestrange (III)">Corvus Lestrange</a> and <a href="/wiki/Eglantine_Lestrange" title="Eglantine Lestrange">Eglantine Lestrange</a>. He married the already married <a href="/wiki/Laurena_Kama" title="Laurena Kama">Laurena Kama</a> and had a daughter with her, <a href="/wiki/Leta_Lestrange" title="Leta Lestrange">Leta Lestrange</a>. When Laurena died, he married <a href="/wiki/Clarisse_Lestrange" title="Clarisse Lestrange">Clarisse Tremblay</a> and had a son with her, <a href="/wiki/Corvus_Lestrange_(V)" title="Corvus Lestrange (V)">Corvus</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-COGS_2-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COGS-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Corvus_Lestrange_(IV)?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Corvus_Lestrange_(IV)?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Corvus were acquainted with the Kama Family. One night, Corvus Lestrange put <a href="/wiki/Laurena_Kama" title="Laurena Kama">Laurena Kama</a> under the <a href="/wiki/Imperius_Curse" title="Imperius Curse">Imperius Curse</a> and kidnapped and married her. He had a daughter with her, <a href="/wiki/Leta_Lestrange" title="Leta Lestrange">Leta Lestrange</a>. Laurena died shortly after. Corvus never loved Leta, the same as with Laurena. Shortly after he married <a href="/wiki/Clarisse_Lestrange" title="Clarisse Lestrange">Clarisse Tremblay</a>. He did not love her either, but he had a son with her, <a href="/wiki/Corvus_Lestrange_(V)" title="Corvus Lestrange (V)">Corvus</a>. When Clarisse died, Corvus was the light in his eyes.<sup id="cite_ref-COGS_2-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COGS-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p><a href="/wiki/Yusuf_Kama" title="Yusuf Kama">Yusuf Kama</a>, son of Laurena Kama and <a href="/wiki/Mustafa_Kama" title="Mustafa Kama">Mustafa Kama</a>, swore an <a href="/wiki/Unbreakable_Vow" title="Unbreakable Vow">Unbreakable Vow</a> to kill young Corvus and end the Lestrange lineage as revenge for the abduction of his mother. In an attempt to protect his son, Lestrange sent both him and his daughter Leta to America along with his servant Irma Dugard. Sometime afterwards he passed away, and a family tree he owed ended at the French ministry.<sup id="cite_ref-COGS_2-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COGS-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Yusuf mistakenly believed <a href="/wiki/Aurelius_Dumbledore" class="mw-redirect" title="Aurelius Dumbledore">Credence Barebone</a> to be Corvus Lestrange (along with much of the rest of the wizarding world), as he was unaware that a young Leta had switched Corvus in his crib with <a href="/wiki/Aurelius_Dumbledore" class="mw-redirect" title="Aurelius Dumbledore">Aurelius Dumbledore</a>, and attempted to resolve his vow by killing Credence.<sup id="cite_ref-COGS_2-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COGS-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>After his death, Corvus Lestrange's remains were interred at the <a href="/wiki/Lestrange_Mausoleum" title="Lestrange Mausoleum">Lestrange Mausoleum</a>, at the <a href="/wiki/Cimeti%C3%A8re_du_P%C3%A8re-Lachaise" title="Cimetière du Père-Lachaise">Cimetière du Père-Lachaise</a>, in <a href="/wiki/Paris" title="Paris">Paris</a>, his grand marble tomb dominating the many sarcophagi there.<sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Personality_and_traits">Personality and traits</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Corvus_Lestrange_(IV)?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Personality and traits">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Corvus_Lestrange_(IV)?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Personality and traits"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>From what is known of his history, Corvus (IV) seemed to be a cold-hearted man who was not above using dark magic or taking another man's wife. Furthermore, he did not care about the consequences of his actions, showing him to be rather ruthless, selfish and egotistical. He did not love his daughter Leta or his second wife, but loved his son. It is possible that his love for his son was because Corvus V's birth meant the future survival of his family line.<sup id="cite_ref-COGS_2-10" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COGS-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Magical_abilities_and_skills">Magical abilities and skills</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Corvus_Lestrange_(IV)?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Corvus_Lestrange_(IV)?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li><b><a href="/wiki/Dark_Arts" title="Dark Arts">Dark Arts</a></b>: Corvus was able to successfully cast the <a href="/wiki/Imperius_Curse" title="Imperius Curse">Imperius Curse</a> on <a href="/wiki/Laurena_Kama" title="Laurena Kama">Laurena Kama</a>, allowing him to marry her against her will. His proficiency in casting this <a href="/wiki/Unforgivable_Curses" title="Unforgivable Curses">Unforgivable Curse</a> implies he was a skilled <a href="/wiki/Dark_Wizard" title="Dark Wizard">Dark Wizard</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-COGS_2-11" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COGS-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup></li></ul>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Corvus_Lestrange_(IV)?veaction=edit&amp;section=4" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Corvus_Lestrange_(IV)?action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>"<a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Corvus" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Corvus">Corvus</a>" is Latin for "<a href="/wiki/Raven" title="Raven">raven</a>", probably from the Greek κόραξ (<i>korax</i>, "<a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/raven" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:raven">raven</a>"). <i>Corvus</i> is the family name of medium to large black birds including crows, ravens, jackdaws and the like. This is thus an allusion to the Dark nature of the Lestrange family, as such birds (particularly ravens) have often been harbingers of doom in folklore.
</p>
