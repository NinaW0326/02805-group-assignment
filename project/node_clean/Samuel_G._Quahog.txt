<p><b>President Samuel G. Quahog</b> was an <a href="/wiki/United_States_of_America" title="United States of America">American</a> wizard and, as <a href="/wiki/President_of_the_Magical_Congress_of_the_United_States_of_America" title="President of the Magical Congress of the United States of America">President</a> of the <a href="/wiki/Magical_Congress_of_the_United_States_of_America" title="Magical Congress of the United States of America">Magical Congress of the United States of America</a>, the head of the US wizarding government.<sup id="cite_ref-PM9June_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PM9June-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Samuel_G._Quahog?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Samuel_G._Quahog?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Following the American victory over <a href="/wiki/Liechtenstein_National_Quidditch_team" title="Liechtenstein National Quidditch team">Liechtenstein</a> on the quarter-finals of the <a href="/wiki/2014_Quidditch_World_Cup" title="2014 Quidditch World Cup">2014 Quidditch World Cup</a>, the Liechtenstein mascot, a large and gloomy <a href="/wiki/Augurey" title="Augurey">Augurey</a> called <a href="/wiki/Hans" title="Hans">Hans</a>, was kidnapped by high-spirited American fans celebrating their team's historic triumph.<sup id="cite_ref-PM8June_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PM8June-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> Hans was put back in his customised pen the following day, <a href="/wiki/9_June" title="9 June">9 June</a> <a href="/wiki/2014" title="2014">2014</a>, but not before negotiations for his return reached the highest levels: the Liechtenstein Minister for Magic <a href="/wiki/Otto_Obermeier" title="Otto Obermeier">Otto Obermeier</a> and President Samuel G. Quahog had to exchange terse owls so that the bird was returned home. Even though President Quahog termed it was done in a "<i>friendly and cooperative fashion</i>", Minister Obermeier insisted that if any ill effects were discovered on <a href="/wiki/Hans" title="Hans">Hans</a>, Liechtenstein would "<i>of course lobby the <a href="/wiki/International_Confederation_of_Wizards%27_Quidditch_Committee" class="mw-redirect" title="International Confederation of Wizards&#39; Quidditch Committee">ICWQC</a> for the USA's immediate disqualification from the World Cup</i>".<sup id="cite_ref-PM9June_2-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PM9June-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
