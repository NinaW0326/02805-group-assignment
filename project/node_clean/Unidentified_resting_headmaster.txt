<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_resting_headmaster?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_resting_headmaster?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>This wizard became the <a href="/wiki/Headmaster" title="Headmaster">Headmaster</a> of <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> at an unknown point in history, although it is known his tenure was before that of <a href="/wiki/Armando_Dippet" title="Armando Dippet">Armando Dippet</a>. After his <a href="/wiki/Death" title="Death">death</a>, his <a href="/wiki/Portrait" title="Portrait">portrait</a> was hung in the <a href="/wiki/Headmaster%27s_office" class="mw-redirect" title="Headmaster&#39;s office">Headmaster's office</a>, in order to give advice to the current head.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> At the top of his golden frame is printed <i>"Seraphini Cherubini Throni"</i> which translates roughly as <i>"angelic cherub throne"</i>.<sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>A portrait also hung in the <a href="/wiki/Lower_Chambers_Portrait_Corridor" title="Lower Chambers Portrait Corridor">Lower Chambers Portrait Corridor</a> next to a <a href="/wiki/Portrait_of_a_bearded_man_holding_a_book" title="Portrait of a bearded man holding a book">Portrait of a bearded man holding a book</a> in <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Castle" title="Hogwarts Castle">Hogwarts Castle</a> during the <a href="/wiki/1993%E2%80%931994_school_year" title="1993–1994 school year">1993–1994 school year</a>. He was portrayed to have been resting in an armchair.<sup id="cite_ref-POAF_4-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-POAF-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
