An <b>unknown man</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Muggle" class="mw-redirect" title="Muggle">Muggle</a> and the father of two daughters, <a href="/wiki/Ivy_Warrington" title="Ivy Warrington">Ivy</a> and <a href="/wiki/Winifred_Warrington" title="Winifred Warrington">Winifred Warrington</a>. At least one daughter, Ivy was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-MA_1-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-MA-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>During her childhood, Ivy accidentally caused Winifred to vanish, and their grandmother put a <a href="/wiki/Memory_Charm" title="Memory Charm">Memory Charm</a> on her so she would forget what she did to her, but it caused her to completely forget Winifred. Ivy later started attending <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-MA_1-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-MA-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
