<p><b>Joscelind Wadcock</b> (b. <a href="/wiki/1911" title="1911">1911</a>) was a <a href="/wiki/Great_Britain" title="Great Britain">British</a> <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch</a> who played as a <a href="/wiki/Chaser" title="Chaser">Chaser</a> for the <a href="/wiki/Puddlemere_United" title="Puddlemere United">Puddlemere United</a> <a href="/wiki/Quidditch" title="Quidditch">Quidditch</a> <a href="/wiki/Quidditch_team" title="Quidditch team">team</a>. She holds the record for highest number of <a href="/wiki/Quidditch" title="Quidditch">Quidditch</a> goals in the British League during the <a href="/wiki/20th_century" title="20th century">20th century</a>, achieved during a <a href="/wiki/1931" title="1931">1931</a> match against the <a href="/wiki/Ballycastle_Bats" title="Ballycastle Bats">Ballycastle Bats</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-PSVG2_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PSVG2-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
