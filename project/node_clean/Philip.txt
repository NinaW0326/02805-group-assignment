<b>Philip</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> who attended <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> and was <a href="/wiki/Sorting_ceremony" title="Sorting ceremony">sorted</a> into <a href="/wiki/Hufflepuff" title="Hufflepuff">Hufflepuff</a>. In the <a href="/wiki/1985%E2%80%931986_school_year" title="1985–1986 school year">1985–1986 school year</a>, <a href="/wiki/Chauncy" title="Chauncy">Chauncy</a> helped him preparing for a <a href="/wiki/Charms_(class)" title="Charms (class)">Charms</a> exam, and he wanted to give him some sweets, but did not have any. His favourite sweets were <a href="/wiki/Cauldron_Cake" title="Cauldron Cake">Cauldron Cakes</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-y2sq_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-y2sq-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
