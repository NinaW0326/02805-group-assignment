<p><b>Morag</b> (c. <a href="/wiki/1981" title="1981">1981</a>) was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch</a> and a <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts</a> student in her youth. She was <a href="/wiki/Sorting_ceremony" title="Sorting ceremony">sorted</a> into either <a href="/wiki/Ravenclaw" title="Ravenclaw">Ravenclaw</a>, <a href="/wiki/Slytherin" title="Slytherin">Slytherin</a> or <a href="/wiki/Hufflepuff" title="Hufflepuff">Hufflepuff</a> on <a href="/wiki/1_September" title="1 September">1 September</a>, <a href="/wiki/1992" title="1992">1992</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-CSpc_4-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-CSpc-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Sometime in late <a href="/wiki/1992" title="1992">1992</a>, during a <a href="/wiki/Transfiguration" title="Transfiguration">Transfiguration</a> class about <a href="/wiki/Match_to_needle_spell" title="Match to needle spell">Transfiguring a matchstick</a> Morag tried to <a href="/wiki/Fire-Making_Spell" title="Fire-Making Spell">light</a> hers, and was scolded for it by <a href="/wiki/Minerva_McGonagall" title="Minerva McGonagall">Professor McGonagall</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-CSpc_4-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-CSpc-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
