<p><b>Harper</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> who began attending <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> in <a href="/wiki/1992" title="1992">1992</a>, and was sorted into <a href="/wiki/Slytherin" title="Slytherin">Slytherin</a>. He was described by <a href="/wiki/Ginevra_Weasley" title="Ginevra Weasley">Ginevra Weasley</a> as <i>"an idiot"</i>. During the <a href="/wiki/1996%E2%80%931997_school_year" title="1996–1997 school year">1996–1997 school year</a>, he was the reserve <a href="/wiki/Seeker" title="Seeker">Seeker</a> for the <a href="/wiki/Slytherin_Quidditch_team" title="Slytherin Quidditch team">Slytherin Quidditch team</a>, and played against <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor" title="Gryffindor">Gryffindor</a> when <a href="/wiki/Draco_Malfoy" title="Draco Malfoy">Draco Malfoy</a> pulled out of the squad.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP14_1-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP14-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Harper?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Harper?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Harper was born in the early <a href="/wiki/1980s" title="1980s">1980s</a>.
</p><p>In the early <a href="/wiki/1990s" title="1990s">1990s</a>, Harper began attending <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> and was <a href="/wiki/Sorting_ceremony" title="Sorting ceremony">Sorted</a> into <a href="/wiki/Slytherin" title="Slytherin">Slytherin</a> by the <a href="/wiki/Sorting_Hat" title="Sorting Hat">Sorting Hat</a>, and was shown to share the beliefs of his peers.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP14_1-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP14-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>In <a href="/wiki/1996" title="1996">1996</a>, he became a <a href="/wiki/Seeker" title="Seeker">Seeker</a> for the <a href="/wiki/Slytherin_Quidditch_team" title="Slytherin Quidditch team">Slytherin Quidditch team</a>. He was a dirty player, deliberately ramming the opposing Seeker <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> during the first <a href="/wiki/Quidditch" title="Quidditch">Quidditch</a> match of <a href="/wiki/1996" title="1996">1996</a> and making snide comments about <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ron Weasley</a>. He quickly spotted the <a href="/wiki/Golden_Snitch" title="Golden Snitch">Golden Snitch</a> before Harry did, possibly because Harry was furious about <a href="/wiki/Zacharias_Smith" title="Zacharias Smith">Zacharias Smith</a> and he was distracted. However, when it seemed that he was about to catch it, he was put off by Harry's desperate taunt, implying that <a href="/wiki/Draco_Malfoy" title="Draco Malfoy">Draco Malfoy</a> had paid Harper to play instead. Harper, in his distraction, fumbled the Snitch, costing his team the game.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP14_1-10" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP14-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Personality_and_traits">Personality and traits</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Harper?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Personality and traits">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Harper?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Personality and traits"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Harper appeared to share the <a href="/wiki/Pure-blood_supremacy" title="Pure-blood supremacy">pure-blood supremacist</a> beliefs of fellow <a href="/wiki/Slytherin" title="Slytherin">Slytherins</a>, as shown by his snide comments about <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ron Weasley</a> being a <a href="/wiki/Blood_traitor" title="Blood traitor">blood traitor</a>. He also lacked good sportsmanship and took part in foul play, deliberately ramming Harry Potter during the first <a href="/wiki/Quidditch" title="Quidditch">Quidditch</a> match of <a href="/wiki/1996" title="1996">1996</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP14_1-11" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP14-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Magical_skills_and_abilities">Magical skills and abilities</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Harper?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Magical skills and abilities">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Harper?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Magical skills and abilities"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li><b><a href="/wiki/Flight" title="Flight">Flying</a></b>: As a <a href="/wiki/Quidditch" title="Quidditch">Quidditch</a> player, Harper was a reasonably proficient <a href="/wiki/Broomstick" title="Broomstick">broomstick</a> flyer. Harper appeared to have a keen eyesight, spotting the Snitch before even <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> did, but being unable to catch it first.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP14_1-12" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP14-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup></li></ul>
