<b>Valmai Morgan</b> was a Welsh <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch</a> who played as the Chaser for the <a href="/wiki/Wales" title="Wales">Welsh</a> all-female <a href="/wiki/Quidditch" title="Quidditch">Quidditch</a> <a href="/wiki/Quidditch_team" title="Quidditch team">team</a>, the <a href="/wiki/Holyhead_Harpies" title="Holyhead Harpies">Holyhead Harpies</a><sup id="cite_ref-:0_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:0-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>.
</p><p>She was a <a href="/wiki/Chaser" title="Chaser">Chaser</a> for the team who replaced <a href="/wiki/Wilda_Griffiths" title="Wilda Griffiths">Wilda Griffiths</a> on the team, after the latter left for a 1000 <a href="/wiki/Galleon" title="Galleon">Galleon</a> fee to join <a href="/wiki/Puddlemere_United" title="Puddlemere United">Puddlemere United</a><sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>.
</p><p>Valmai started on a high note, scoring ten goals on her first game<sup id="cite_ref-:0_2-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:0-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>.
</p>
