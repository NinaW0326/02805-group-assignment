<b>John Buchanan</b><sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> (c. <a href="/wiki/1804" class="mw-redirect" title="1804">1804</a> - <a href="/wiki/1_July" title="1 July">1 July</a>, <a href="/wiki/1861" class="mw-redirect" title="1861">1861</a>)<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> was a <a href="/wiki/Scotland" title="Scotland">Scottish</a> <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> who lived in the <a href="/wiki/19th_century" title="19th century">19th century</a>. He was the husband of <a href="/wiki/Angus_Buchanan%27s_mother" class="mw-redirect" title="Angus Buchanan&#39;s mother">Mrs Buchanan</a> and the father of eleven children, including <a href="/wiki/Hamish_Buchanan" title="Hamish Buchanan">Hamish</a>, <a href="/wiki/Flora_Buchanan" title="Flora Buchanan">Flora</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Angus_Buchanan" title="Angus Buchanan">Angus</a>.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/John_Buchanan?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/John_Buchanan?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The <a href="/wiki/Buchanan_family" title="Buchanan family">Buchanans</a> were a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizarding</a> family with a long history. By the mid-<a href="/wiki/19th_century" title="19th century">19th century</a>, they had resided in a village in the <a href="/wiki/Scottish_Borders" title="Scottish Borders">Scottish Borders</a> for many generations and were shunned by their <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggle</a> neighbours, who did not know that they were <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magical</a>. They had a reputation for being extremely large, aggressive, and drinking excessively, which lead the Muggle villagers to conclude that Buchanan children were sent to correctional facilities or institutionalised when they <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">mysteriously disappeared at eleven</a>.
</p><p>Mr Buchanan and <a href="/wiki/Angus_Buchanan%27s_mother" class="mw-redirect" title="Angus Buchanan&#39;s mother">his wife</a> had a noisy, chaotic home filled with eleven children. Described as a "fierce" and "proud" man, he held strong anti-<a href="/wiki/Squib" title="Squib">Squib</a> views, boasting that no Squib had ever been born into the Buchanan line, and asserting that the birth of a Squib meant that a wizarding family was "in decline" and deserved to be "winnowed out." However, he and his wife did not notice when their third-born son, <a href="/wiki/Angus_Buchanan" title="Angus Buchanan">Angus</a>, failed to display signs of magical ability like all of his other siblings.
</p><p>Angus, being the kindest and largest of the Buchanan children, was well-liked by his brothers and sisters. Mr Buchanan's children thus went to elaborate lengths to conceal Angus's Squib status from him and his wife. His daughter <a href="/wiki/Flora_Buchanan" title="Flora Buchanan">Flora</a> forged a <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts</a> <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_acceptance_letter" title="Hogwarts acceptance letter">acceptance letter</a> for her younger brother, and, on <a href="/wiki/1_September" title="1 September">1 September</a>, <a href="/wiki/1858" class="mw-redirect" title="1858">1858</a>, the day that eleven-year-old Angus was due to begin his <a href="/wiki/First_year" title="First year">first year</a> at Hogwarts, his son <a href="/wiki/Hamish_Buchanan" title="Hamish Buchanan">Hamish</a> carried his little brother to <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Castle" title="Hogwarts Castle">Hogwarts Castle</a> on his <a href="/wiki/Hamish_Buchanan%27s_broomstick" title="Hamish Buchanan&#39;s broomstick">broomstick</a>.
</p><p>The ruse was revealed when the <a href="/wiki/Sorting_Hat" title="Sorting Hat">Sorting Hat</a> declined to sort Angus. News soon reached Mr Buchanan and his wife by <a href="/wiki/Owl" title="Owl">owl</a>. Humiliated, he refused to allow his son into the house when he finally arrived home on foot, telling him never to show his face again and chasing him away with <a href="/wiki/Curse" title="Curse">curses</a>.
</p><p>After his disownment, Angus made his own way in the Muggle world, eventually coming to play for the <a href="/wiki/Scottish_rugby_team" title="Scottish rugby team">Scottish rugby team</a>. Mr Buchanan forbade his other children from ever seeing Angus again, but, despite this, all ten of them turned out to support their brother at the Scottish team's first international match.<sup id="cite_ref-Pottermore_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Pottermore-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
