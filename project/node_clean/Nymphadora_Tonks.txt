<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix">"<i>Don't call me Nymphadora, Remus. It's <a href="/wiki/Tonks_family" title="Tonks family">Tonks</a>.</i>"</span></dd>
<dd>&#8212;Tonks' intense dislike of her first name<sup class="noprint"><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Order_of_the_Phoenix" title="Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix">[src]</a></sup></dd>

<p><b>Nymphadora Tonks</b>  (c. <a href="/wiki/1973" title="1973">1973</a><sup id="cite_ref-DOB_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DOB-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> – <a href="/wiki/2_May" title="2 May">2 May</a>, <a href="/wiki/1998" title="1998">1998</a>) more commonly known as <b>Tonks</b>, and occasionally <b>Dora</b>, was a <a href="/wiki/Great_Britain" title="Great Britain">British</a> <a href="/wiki/Half-blood" title="Half-blood">half-blood</a> <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch</a> and a <a href="/wiki/Metamorphmagus" title="Metamorphmagus">Metamorphmagus</a>. She was the only child of <a href="/wiki/Edward_Tonks" title="Edward Tonks">Ted</a> and <a href="/wiki/Andromeda_Tonks" title="Andromeda Tonks">Andromeda Tonks</a> <span class="template-c">(née Black)</span>. Tonks attended <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> from <a href="/wiki/1984" title="1984">1984</a>-<a href="/wiki/1991" title="1991">1991</a> and was Sorted into <a href="/wiki/Hufflepuff" title="Hufflepuff">Hufflepuff House</a>. She was in the same year at Hogwarts as <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor" title="Gryffindor">Gryffindor</a> <a href="/wiki/Charles_Weasley" title="Charles Weasley">Charlie Weasley</a>. 
</p><p>After leaving Hogwarts, Tonks joined the <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">Ministry of Magic</a> and trained to become an <a href="/wiki/Auror" title="Auror">Auror</a> under the watchful eye of <a href="/wiki/Alastor_Moody" title="Alastor Moody">Alastor Moody</a>, qualifying in <a href="/wiki/1994" title="1994">1994</a>. In <a href="/wiki/1995" title="1995">1995</a>, she joined the second <a href="/wiki/Order_of_the_Phoenix" title="Order of the Phoenix">Order of the Phoenix</a>, working undercover in the Ministry and helping to guard the <a href="/wiki/Department_of_Mysteries" title="Department of Mysteries">Department of Mysteries</a>. In June of 1998, she fought in the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_the_Department_of_Mysteries" title="Battle of the Department of Mysteries">Battle of the Department of Mysteries</a>. At some point, Tonks fell in love with <a href="/wiki/Remus_Lupin" title="Remus Lupin">Remus Lupin</a>, her fellow Order member and a <a href="/wiki/Lycanthropy" title="Lycanthropy">werewolf</a> who felt he was too poor, old, and dangerous for her and thus rejected her feelings, although he felt the same way. In the wake of the return of <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a>, Tonks was assigned to <a href="/wiki/Hogsmeade" title="Hogsmeade">Hogsmeade</a> as part of the Auror task force sent to guard Hogwarts. She later fought in the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_the_Astronomy_Tower" title="Battle of the Astronomy Tower">Battle of the Astronomy Tower</a>. After seeing the love <a href="/wiki/Fleur_Delacour" title="Fleur Delacour">Fleur Delacour</a> held for <a href="/wiki/William_Weasley" title="William Weasley">Bill Weasley</a> despite his injuries, she publicly declared her love for Remus.
</p><p>Tonks and Remus were <a href="/wiki/Wedding_of_Remus_Lupin_and_Nymphadora_Tonks" title="Wedding of Remus Lupin and Nymphadora Tonks">married</a> in the summer of <a href="/wiki/1997" title="1997">1997</a>. She formed part of the <a href="/wiki/Advance_Guard" title="Advance Guard">Advance Guard</a> and bravely participated in the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_the_Seven_Potters" title="Battle of the Seven Potters">Battle of the Seven Potters</a>. Tonks became pregnant by this time and Remus began to worry that he had passed on his werewolf condition to their son, though this proved not to be the case as the child had inherited only Tonks' metamorphic abilities. Remus, in response to this, attempted to leave Tonks, but was convinced by their friend <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> to return to her. She gave birth to a son, <a href="/wiki/Edward_Lupin" title="Edward Lupin">Teddy Remus Lupin</a> (named after her late father Edward "Teddy") shortly after Easter in <a href="/wiki/1998" title="1998">1998</a> and the couple named Harry the godfather. On 2 May, 1998 Tonks fought in the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a> and was finally murdered by <a href="/wiki/Bellatrix_Lestrange" title="Bellatrix Lestrange">Bellatrix Lestrange</a>, her maternal aunt and a <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eater</a>, a devoted servant of <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort.</a> Her murder was avenged by her order colleague and friend <a href="/wiki/Molly_Weasley" title="Molly Weasley">Molly Weasley</a>. After her death, her son was raised by her mother and Harry. 
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Nymphadora_Tonks?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Nymphadora_Tonks?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span id="Early_life_(1973-1984)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_life_.281973-1984.29">Early life <span class="template-c">(1973-1984)</span></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Nymphadora_Tonks?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Early life (1973-1984)">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Nymphadora_Tonks?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Early life (1973-1984)"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Nymphadora Tonks was born in late <a href="/wiki/1972" title="1972">1972</a> to <a href="/wiki/1973" title="1973">1973</a><sup id="cite_ref-DOB_1-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DOB-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> to <a href="/wiki/Edward_Tonks" title="Edward Tonks">Edward Tonks</a>, a <a href="/wiki/Muggle-born" title="Muggle-born">Muggle-born</a> <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> and <a href="/wiki/Andromeda_Tonks" title="Andromeda Tonks">Andromeda Tonks</a> <span class="template-c">(née Black)</span>, a <a href="/wiki/Pure-blood" title="Pure-blood">pure-blood</a> <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch</a> and former member of the noble <a href="/wiki/House_of_Black" title="House of Black">House of Black</a> although she was burned off the family tree after marrying a muggle-born, which was considered a betrayal to the family. Her mother noticed that Tonks changed hair colour on the day of her birth, meaning she was a Metamorphmagus.
</p>
<h3><span id="Hogwarts_years_(1984-1991)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="Hogwarts_years_.281984-1991.29">Hogwarts years <span class="template-c">(1984-1991)</span></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Nymphadora_Tonks?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Hogwarts years (1984-1991)">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Nymphadora_Tonks?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Hogwarts years (1984-1991)"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<figure class="thumb tleft show-info-icon" style="width: 252px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/b/be/1984_First_Years.png/revision/latest?cb=20180128212546" class="image"><img alt="" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw%3D%3D" decoding="async" width="250" height="131" class="thumbimage lazyload" data-image-name="1984 First Years.png" data-image-key="1984_First_Years.png" data-src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/b/be/1984_First_Years.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/250?cb=20180128212546" /></a> 	<noscript><a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/b/be/1984_First_Years.png/revision/latest?cb=20180128212546" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/b/be/1984_First_Years.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/250?cb=20180128212546" decoding="async" width="250" height="131" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="1984 First Years.png" data-image-key="1984_First_Years.png" data-src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/b/be/1984_First_Years.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/250?cb=20180128212546" /></a></noscript> 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:1984_First_Years.png" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption">The <a href="/wiki/1984" title="1984">1984</a> <a href="/wiki/Sorting_ceremony" title="Sorting ceremony">Sorting ceremony</a>, with a pink-haired Tonks in the front</p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure>
<p>Tonks began her <a href="/wiki/First_year" title="First year">first year</a> at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> in <a href="/wiki/1984" title="1984">1984</a> and was sorted into <a href="/wiki/Hufflepuff" title="Hufflepuff">Hufflepuff House</a> at the <a href="/wiki/Sorting_ceremony" title="Sorting ceremony">Sorting ceremony</a>. Being the daughter of the ostracised Andromeda Tonks, she was rejected by the <a href="/wiki/Pure-blood_supremacy" title="Pure-blood supremacy">pure-blood supremacists</a> of the <a href="/wiki/House_of_Black" title="House of Black">House of Black</a>.
</p>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Third_year">Third year</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Nymphadora_Tonks?veaction=edit&amp;section=4" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Third year">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Nymphadora_Tonks?action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Third year"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<p>This was the first year in which students were able to take at least two elective classes, one of them being <a href="/wiki/Divination_(class)" title="Divination (class)">Divination</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-13" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-13">&#91;13&#93;</a></sup> Also in the <a href="/wiki/1986%E2%80%931987_school_year" title="1986–1987 school year">1986–1987 school year</a>, at the start of the <a href="/wiki/Herbology" title="Herbology">Herbology</a> lesson on <a href="/wiki/Valerian" title="Valerian">Valerian</a> Sprigs, Tonks warned people to watch out, for she spilled some soil on the floor. When the Herbology Professor, also her <a href="/wiki/Head_of_House" title="Head of House">Head of House</a>, <a href="/wiki/Pomona_Sprout" title="Pomona Sprout">Pomona Sprout</a>, asked fellow Housemate <a href="/wiki/Penny_Haywood" title="Penny Haywood">Penny Haywood</a> to fetch a pail for the watering demonstration, Tonks took the time to briefly wonder aloud about which plant would be most suitable for bothering a certain "irritable, birdlike" <a href="/wiki/Irma_Pince" title="Irma Pince">librarian</a>. The musing was cut short by Penny's scream, and the class realised in shock that a supposed <a href="/wiki/Werewolf" title="Werewolf">werewolf</a> had burst out of a pot. Professor Sprout took care of the situation by casting the <a href="/wiki/Boggart-Banishing_Spell" title="Boggart-Banishing Spell">Boggart-Banishing Spell</a>, and introduced the class to what a <a href="/wiki/Boggart" title="Boggart">Boggart</a> was. As Professor Sprout left to report the incident to <a href="/wiki/Headmaster" title="Headmaster">Headmaster</a> <a href="/wiki/Albus_Dumbledore" title="Albus Dumbledore">Albus Dumbledore</a>, Tonks asked <a href="/wiki/Jacob%27s_sibling" title="Jacob&#39;s sibling">Jacob's sibling</a> in puzzlement about <a href="/wiki/Penny_Haywood" title="Penny Haywood">Penny's</a> fear, implying she didn't understand why Penny's worst fear would be werewolves.<sup id="cite_ref-14" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-14">&#91;14&#93;</a></sup>  
</p>
<figure class="thumb tright show-info-icon" style="width: 252px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/f/fe/Tumblr_p8uscgsYCZ1uvaytto1_1280.png/revision/latest?cb=20180906125456" class="image"><img alt="" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw%3D%3D" decoding="async" width="250" height="106" class="thumbimage lazyload" data-image-name="Tumblr p8uscgsYCZ1uvaytto1 1280.png" data-image-key="Tumblr_p8uscgsYCZ1uvaytto1_1280.png" data-src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/f/fe/Tumblr_p8uscgsYCZ1uvaytto1_1280.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/250?cb=20180906125456" /></a> 	<noscript><a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/f/fe/Tumblr_p8uscgsYCZ1uvaytto1_1280.png/revision/latest?cb=20180906125456" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/f/fe/Tumblr_p8uscgsYCZ1uvaytto1_1280.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/250?cb=20180906125456" decoding="async" width="250" height="106" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="Tumblr p8uscgsYCZ1uvaytto1 1280.png" data-image-key="Tumblr_p8uscgsYCZ1uvaytto1_1280.png" data-src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/f/fe/Tumblr_p8uscgsYCZ1uvaytto1_1280.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/250?cb=20180906125456" /></a></noscript> 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:Tumblr_p8uscgsYCZ1uvaytto1_1280.png" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption">Tonks coming to the conclusion that she should stop <a href="/wiki/Metamorphmagus" title="Metamorphmagus">Metamorphosing</a> into teachers</p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure>
<p>Before the start of the lesson on "rolling" in the <a href="/wiki/Flying_(class)" title="Flying (class)">Flying class</a>, Tonks chatted excitedly with <a href="/wiki/Rowan_Khanna" title="Rowan Khanna">Rowan Khanna</a> and Jacob's sibling, for as <a href="/wiki/Third_year" title="Third year">third years</a>, they would finally get to go to <a href="/wiki/Hogsmeade" title="Hogsmeade">Hogsmeade</a>. She was especially looking forward to visiting <a href="/wiki/Zonko%27s_Joke_Shop" title="Zonko&#39;s Joke Shop">Zonko's Joke Shop</a> and <a href="/wiki/Honeydukes" title="Honeydukes">Honeydukes</a>, along with having <a href="/wiki/Butterbeer" title="Butterbeer">Butterbeer</a> at the <a href="/wiki/Three_Broomsticks_Inn" title="Three Broomsticks Inn">Three Broomsticks Inn</a>. After the lesson, she revealed she had crashed into the <a href="/wiki/Owlery" title="Owlery">Owlery</a>, the <a href="/wiki/Astronomy_Tower" title="Astronomy Tower">Astronomy Tower</a>, the <a href="/wiki/Whomping_Willow" title="Whomping Willow">Whomping Willow</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Rubeus_Hagrid%27s_garden" title="Rubeus Hagrid&#39;s garden">Rubeus Hagrid's garden</a>, but that apparently was a few crashes fewer than her average. Tonks later revealed that she had often gotten detentions for turning into teachers using her Metamorphmagus abilities.<sup id="cite_ref-15" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-15">&#91;15&#93;</a></sup> 
</p><p>When revisiting an old lesson in Herbology, re-potting baby <a href="/wiki/Mandrake" title="Mandrake">Mandrakes</a>, Tonks forgot her <a href="/wiki/Earmuffs" title="Earmuffs">earmuffs</a> and resorted to rolling up some <a href="/wiki/Jelly_Slug" title="Jelly Slug">Jelly Slugs</a> and sticking them in her ears, which proved to be sub-optimal, for though she was not affected by the screaming Mandrakes; after class, she also could not properly converse with Jacob's sibling, due to being unable to hear.<sup id="cite_ref-16" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-16">&#91;16&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Fourth_year">Fourth year</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Nymphadora_Tonks?veaction=edit&amp;section=5" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Fourth year">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Nymphadora_Tonks?action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Fourth year"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<p>At some point, Tonks taught herself the highly advanced Patronus Charm. She later saved <a href="/wiki/Jacob%27s_sibling" title="Jacob&#39;s sibling">Jacob's sibling</a> from a Dementor, after <a href="/wiki/Penny_Haywood" title="Penny Haywood">Penny</a> was knocked out by one. She was then able to teach Jacob's sibling and <a href="/wiki/Merula_Snyde" title="Merula Snyde">Merula Snyde</a> the same spell.<sup id="cite_ref-Patronus_17-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Patronus-17">&#91;17&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>One night in her fourth year,<sup id="cite_ref-18" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-18">&#91;18&#93;</a></sup> Tonks took the biggest <a href="/wiki/Venomous_Tentacula" title="Venomous Tentacula">Venomous Tentacula</a> from Sprout's greenhouse. She attempted to put it under Filch's desk, but fell on her face on the way, accidentally tossing it into the Potions Classroom. The plant ended up grabbing Professor <a href="/wiki/Severus_Snape" title="Severus Snape">Severus Snape</a>. He and Sprout agreed to give Tonks daily detention until the end of her school days, but Sprout gave her the chance to not get detention if she managed to pass a special Herbology test.<sup id="cite_ref-Y4SQ_19-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Y4SQ-19">&#91;19&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Tonks studied for the test in the <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Library" title="Hogwarts Library">Hogwarts Library</a>, where she came across Jacob's sibling. Tonks told them what had happened and the two agreed to study together.<sup id="cite_ref-Y4SQ_19-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Y4SQ-19">&#91;19&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Fifth_year">Fifth year</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Nymphadora_Tonks?veaction=edit&amp;section=6" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Fifth year">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Nymphadora_Tonks?action=edit&amp;section=6" title="Edit section: Fifth year"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<div class="quote">
<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix"><b>Tonks</b>: "<i>I was never a <a href="/wiki/Prefect" title="Prefect">prefect</a> myself. My <a href="/wiki/Pomona_Sprout" title="Pomona Sprout">Head of House</a> said I lacked certain necessary qualities.</i>"</span></dd>
<dd><b>Ginny Weasley</b>: "<i>Like what?</i>"</dd>

</div>
<p>Professor Sprout didn't make her one of Hufflepuff's prefects in her fifth year because she lacked the ability to behave properly.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP9_20-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP9-20">&#91;20&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Despite her behaviour, Tonks achieved good marks on her exams and qualified for <a href="/wiki/Auror" title="Auror">Auror</a> training when she left school in 1991. This means that she took <a href="/wiki/Defence_Against_the_Dark_Arts" title="Defence Against the Dark Arts">Defence Against the Dark Arts</a>, <a href="/wiki/Transfiguration_(class)" title="Transfiguration (class)">Transfiguration</a>, <a href="/wiki/Charms_(class)" title="Charms (class)">Charms</a>, <a href="/wiki/Potions_(class)" title="Potions (class)">Potions</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Herbology" title="Herbology">Herbology</a> at the least in her sixth and seventh year. She earned '<a href="/wiki/Outstanding" title="Outstanding">Outstanding</a>' or '<a href="/wiki/Exceeds_Expectations" title="Exceeds Expectations">Exceeds Expectations</a>' on all of her <a href="/wiki/Nastily_Exhausting_Wizarding_Test" title="Nastily Exhausting Wizarding Test">N.E.W.T.s</a>.
</p>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Sixth_year">Sixth year</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Nymphadora_Tonks?veaction=edit&amp;section=7" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Sixth year">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Nymphadora_Tonks?action=edit&amp;section=7" title="Edit section: Sixth year"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<p>During her sixth year, Tonks proceeded to take N.E.W.T. level classes on <a href="/wiki/Defence_Against_the_Dark_Arts" title="Defence Against the Dark Arts">Defence Against the Dark Arts</a>, <a href="/wiki/Transfiguration_(class)" title="Transfiguration (class)">Transfiguration</a>, <a href="/wiki/Charms_(class)" title="Charms (class)">Charms</a>, <a href="/wiki/Potions_(class)" title="Potions (class)">Potions</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Herbology" title="Herbology">Herbology</a>. Sometime that year, she became a founding member of the <a href="/wiki/Circle_of_Khanna" title="Circle of Khanna">Circle of Khanna</a>, a group devoted to avenging <a href="/wiki/Rowan_Khanna" title="Rowan Khanna">Rowan Khanna</a> and locating the final <a href="/wiki/Cursed_Vaults" title="Cursed Vaults">cursed vault</a>. It was at the group's first meeting in the <a href="/wiki/Hog%27s_Head_Inn" title="Hog&#39;s Head Inn">Hog's Head Inn</a> that she learned of the malevolent organisation "<a href="/wiki/R" title="R">R</a>" and its <a href="/wiki/Plot_to_find_the_Cursed_Vaults" title="Plot to find the Cursed Vaults">motives</a> to locate the final Cursed Vault before them.<sup id="cite_ref-hm20_21-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-hm20-21">&#91;21&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>She joined <a href="/wiki/Jacob%27s_sibling" title="Jacob&#39;s sibling">Jacob's sibling</a> on his mission to infiltrate "<a href="/wiki/R" title="R">R</a>"s meeting, using her <a href="/wiki/Metamorphmagus" title="Metamorphmagus">Metamorphmagus</a> abilities to turn into a dark witch. She kept an eye on the meeting, inducing the suspicion of the <a href="/wiki/Wizard_in_white_robes" title="Wizard in white robes">Wizard in white robes</a>. However she managed to get through the meeting, until <a href="/wiki/Patricia_Rakepick" title="Patricia Rakepick">Patricia Rakepick</a> apparated into the scene and attacked <a href="/wiki/Jacob%27s_sibling" title="Jacob&#39;s sibling">Jacob's sibling</a>. She duelled the <a href="/wiki/Wizard_in_white_robes" title="Wizard in white robes">Wizard in white robes</a> and an unidentified dark witch alone, and managed to hold her ground, even managing to disarm and bind the <a href="/wiki/Wizard_in_white_robes" title="Wizard in white robes">Wizard in white robes</a> in ropes. 
</p>
<h3><span id="Career_as_an_Auror_(1992-1995)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="Career_as_an_Auror_.281992-1995.29">Career as an Auror <span class="template-c">(1992-1995)</span></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Nymphadora_Tonks?veaction=edit&amp;section=8" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Career as an Auror (1992-1995)">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Nymphadora_Tonks?action=edit&amp;section=8" title="Edit section: Career as an Auror (1992-1995)"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<div class="quote">
<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix">"<i>Nearly failed on stealth and tracking. I'm dead clumsy! Did you hear me break that plate downstairs?</i>"</span></dd>
<dd>&#8212;Tonks talking to Harry Potter about Auror training<sup class="noprint"><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Order_of_the_Phoenix" title="Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix">[src]</a></sup></dd>

<p>After passing a series of character tests that examine practical skills and how well candidates react under pressure, Tonks underwent three years of <a href="/wiki/Auror_training" title="Auror training">Auror training</a> under the tutelage of <a href="/wiki/Alastor_Moody" title="Alastor Moody">Alastor Moody</a>. Her natural <a href="/wiki/Metamorphmagus" title="Metamorphmagus">metamorphic abilities</a> allowed her to easily pass the Concealment and Disguise portion of the training without doing any work or study at all. However, Tonks' clumsiness gave her trouble with the Stealth and Tracking portion of the course. She admitted to <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> that she almost failed, as she had a habit of breaking items.  
</p><p>Tonks qualified fully as an Auror in 1994, she was one of the last ones taken on.<sup id="cite_ref-ootp3_22-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-ootp3-22">&#91;22&#93;</a></sup> According to her father, she had been through worse experiences than many of her next battles. 
</p>

<p>With the return of <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a> in 1995, Tonks was one of the few Ministry employees to be convinced of the truth, rather than accepting the Ministry lie that He-Who-Must-Not-Be-Named hadn't returned. She joined the reconstituted <a href="/wiki/Order_of_the_Phoenix" title="Order of the Phoenix">Order of the Phoenix</a>, and, alongside <a href="/wiki/Kingsley_Shacklebolt" title="Kingsley Shacklebolt">Kingsley Shacklebolt</a>, worked as a covert operative inside the Ministry, feeding the Order information. Tonks had to be careful around the Ministry, though, as indiscreet actions would have seen her lose her position as an Auror. She also took shifts guarding <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Sybill_Trelawney%27s_first_prophecy" title="Sybill Trelawney&#39;s first prophecy">prophecy</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Department_of_Mysteries" title="Department of Mysteries">Department of Mysteries</a>, along with other members of the Order. She was considered a considerable asset to the Order.

<p>In the summer of 1995, Tonks was part of the Order's <a href="/wiki/Advance_Guard" title="Advance Guard">Advance Guard</a> sent to escort Harry from <a href="/wiki/4_Privet_Drive" title="4 Privet Drive">4 Privet Drive</a> to <a href="/wiki/12_Grimmauld_Place" title="12 Grimmauld Place">12 Grimmauld Place</a>, after his encounter with <a href="/wiki/Dementor" title="Dementor">Dementors</a> in <a href="/wiki/Little_Whinging" title="Little Whinging">Little Whinging</a>. In order to lure the <a href="/wiki/Dursley_family" title="Dursley family">Dursleys</a> from the house so they could retrieve <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>, she sent a letter via the <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggle</a> post, informing them that they had been short-listed in the "<i>All-England Best Kept Suburban Lawn Competition.</i>" With the Dursleys out of the way, the Advance Guard arrived to protect Harry during the journey to London. Tonks befriended the young wizard immediately and helped him pack his trunk, although she was not very good with household spells, something at which her mother excelled. She was impressed by his <a href="/wiki/Firebolt" title="Firebolt">Firebolt</a> <a href="/wiki/Broomstick" title="Broomstick">broomstick</a>. During the broomstick flight to Grimmauld Place, Tonks flew in the lead of the formation and convinced Moody to land immediately instead of doubling back.<sup id="cite_ref-ootp3_22-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-ootp3-22">&#91;22&#93;</a></sup> 
</p>

<p>After rescuing Harry from the Dursleys, Tonks was extremely busy at the Ministry, and the situation wasn't helped by <a href="/wiki/Rufus_Scrimgeour" title="Rufus Scrimgeour">Rufus Scrimgeour</a> asking awkward questions, possibly due to her association with Mad-Eye Moody. Still, she found the time to attend the party at Grimmauld Place for <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ron Weasley</a> and <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione Granger</a> to congratulate them on becoming <a href="/wiki/Prefect" title="Prefect">prefects</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP9_20-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP9-20">&#91;20&#93;</a></sup> She was later part of the Order task force, disguised as an old woman, that escorted Harry, Hermione, Ron, <a href="/wiki/Ginevra_Weasley" title="Ginevra Weasley">Ginny</a>, <a href="/wiki/Fred_Weasley" title="Fred Weasley">Fred</a>, and <a href="/wiki/George_Weasley" title="George Weasley">George</a> to King's Cross Station, so they could catch the <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Express" title="Hogwarts Express">Hogwarts Express</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP10_23-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP10-23">&#91;23&#93;</a></sup>

<p>Tonks continued to serve the <a href="/wiki/Order_of_the_Phoenix" title="Order of the Phoenix">Order of the Phoenix</a>, but she had become withdrawn and depressed. The change in personality — and her seeming difficulties in controlling her metamorphic abilities — was noted by those around her. Her physical changes included her hair becoming mousy brown and lank, while her body became thinner. Some, including <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione</a> and Harry, thought the change in her mood and appearance was caused by the death of her cousin, Sirius, since she had been fighting his murderer, Bellatrix, and had failed to stop her, and thus might be feeling guilty.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP5_29-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP5-29">&#91;29&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>However, there was more than one reason for it and yet another cause of her depression was her <a href="/wiki/Love" title="Love">love</a> for Remus Lupin, who became her close friend over a year.<sup id="cite_ref-Remus_30-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Remus-30">&#91;30&#93;</a></sup> One night when they lay in hiding outside a known Death Eater's house, she revealed her feelings. However, he felt a relationship with her would be disastrous for her, as he was a <a href="/wiki/Werewolf" title="Werewolf">werewolf</a>, too old for her, and poor, so he started to volunteer in dangerous, long-term missions in order to keep distance between them. Tonks confided her feelings to <a href="/wiki/Molly_Weasley" title="Molly Weasley">Molly Weasley</a>, and was often at the Burrow for "<i>tea and sympathy</i>". Her feelings even caused her Patronus to change its form to that of a wolf, although its large, shaggy intermediate form only reinforced Harry's "Sirius" theory.
</p>

<p>With the news that <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a> had returned being confirmed by the Ministry, Hogwarts received a team of <a href="/wiki/Auror" title="Auror">Aurors</a> to help protect the castle from any threats. Tonks was part of the task force and was stationed in Hogsmeade. On 1 September, she rescued Harry from being sent back to London in one of the carriages on the <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Express" title="Hogwarts Express">Hogwarts Express</a>, where he had been incapacitated by <a href="/wiki/Draco_Malfoy" title="Draco Malfoy">Draco Malfoy</a>, then covered with his <a href="/wiki/Cloak_of_Invisibility" title="Cloak of Invisibility">Invisibility cloak</a>, and escorted him to the school gates. She could do this because she had been watching the students exit the train and immediately went to investigate when Harry had not gotten off too.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP8_10-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP8-10">&#91;10&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Tonks often patrolled the corridors of Hogwarts at the request of <a href="/wiki/Albus_Dumbledore" title="Albus Dumbledore">Albus Dumbledore</a>, when he knew he would be away from the school searching for information regarding Voldemort's <a href="/wiki/Horcrux" title="Horcrux">Horcruxes</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP21_31-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP21-31">&#91;31&#93;</a></sup> She also patrolled Hogsmeade and met Harry shortly after he had confronted <a href="/wiki/Mundungus_Fletcher" title="Mundungus Fletcher">Mundungus Fletcher</a>, when the latter was trying to sell items from Grimmauld Place to <a href="/wiki/Aberforth_Dumbledore" title="Aberforth Dumbledore">Aberforth Dumbledore</a>. She apparently had abandoned her guard post in <a href="/wiki/Hogsmeade" title="Hogsmeade">Hogsmeade</a> and "<i>come bursting in to see Dumbledore, if that's really why she was here</i>". This may have been linked to a werewolf attack on a five year old boy.
</p>

<p>Tonks and Remus married in a quiet ceremony in July, 1997, shortly before the Order operation to spirit Harry away from the <a href="/wiki/Dursley_family" title="Dursley family">Dursleys</a> ahead of his seventeenth birthday. Both Remus and Tonks were unhappy that Harry was unable to attend their small wedding, as he couldn't be allowed to leave the protection of his relatives' home, since it was <a href="/wiki/Bond_of_blood_charm" title="Bond of blood charm">linked</a> to the power of his mother's <a href="/wiki/Sacrificial_protection" title="Sacrificial protection">loving sacrifice</a> when she willingly died for him. 
</p><p><a href="/wiki/Pius_Thicknesse" title="Pius Thicknesse">Pius Thicknesse</a>, <a href="/wiki/Head_of_the_Department_of_Magical_Law_Enforcement%27s_office" title="Head of the Department of Magical Law Enforcement&#39;s office">Head</a> of the <a href="/wiki/Department_of_Magical_Law_Enforcement" title="Department of Magical Law Enforcement">Department of Magical Law Enforcement</a>, had made it an imprisonable offence to create a <a href="/wiki/Portkey" title="Portkey">Portkey</a> near Harry's home, and as he was underage he could be Traced if he <a href="/wiki/Apparition" title="Apparition">Apparated</a>, and hence could not leave to attend without endangering his life.<sup id="cite_ref-DH4_34-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH4-34">&#91;34&#93;</a></sup> This did not leave Harry nor the Order a lot of options.
</p><p>Tonks had unexpectedly become pregnant sometime after the wedding and presumably tried to tell Harry before the Battle of the Seven Potters, but was interrupted by "<a href="/wiki/Mad-Eye_Moody" class="mw-redirect" title="Mad-Eye Moody">Mad-Eye" Moody</a>.
</p>

<p>The Order ultimately decided that they would break the charm around Harry several days before his birthday on 31 July, so that they would have the element of surprise; as Voldemort apparently expected them to wait until he was seventeen to attempt to whisk him away. Tonks, along with a dozen members of the Order and Harry's friends, travelled to <a href="/wiki/4_Privet_Drive" title="4 Privet Drive">4 Privet Drive</a>, where they were to escort Harry to safety. Tonks took the role of a protector, flying on a broom with <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ron Weasley</a> (disguised as Harry). During the flight from Little Whinging, Tonks and Ron were chased by Bellatrix as they headed to Ron's <a href="/wiki/Muriel" title="Muriel">Great-Aunt Muriel</a>'s home. Remus was grateful and relieved that she was alive, but "<i>sounded almost angry at Tonks</i>" when he demanded what kept her so long and what had happened to her. As she told what happened; "<i>A muscle was jumping in Lupin's jaw. He nodded, but seemed unable to say anything else.</i>" Mr and Mrs Weasley ran outside when they got the news of Tonks and Ron's arrival, and thanked Lupin and Tonks repeatedly for bringing back their sons alive. The moment was sobered by <a href="/wiki/William_Weasley" title="William Weasley">Bill Weasley</a>'s revelation that her mentor, <a href="/wiki/Mad-Eye_Moody" class="mw-redirect" title="Mad-Eye Moody">Mad-Eye Moody</a>, had been murdered by <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a>. Tonks openly cried in front of her friends at the news that her beloved mentor had been murdered, as she was Mad-Eye's favourite and protégée at the Ministry of Magic.<sup id="cite_ref-DH5_35-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH5-35">&#91;35&#93;</a></sup>

<div class="quote">

<p>Shortly after the wedding, Tonks and Remus split up. Remus feared for the child Tonks was carrying, worried that the child would be a <a href="/wiki/Werewolf" title="Werewolf">werewolf</a> like him. All of his old fears over his relationship with Tonks resurfaced, and he withdrew from her rather than hurt her. Tonks moved back in with her parents. However, their split didn't last very long. Remus, after arguing with Harry at <a href="/wiki/12_Grimmauld_Place" title="12 Grimmauld Place">12 Grimmauld Place</a>, finally saw sense and reunited with Tonks sometime before Christmas.
</p><p>Before Easter in <a href="/wiki/1998" title="1998">1998</a>, Tonks learnt that her father had been murdered by <a href="/wiki/Snatchers" title="Snatchers">Snatchers</a>. She ultimately gave birth to a son, <a href="/wiki/Edward_Lupin" title="Edward Lupin">Teddy Remus Lupin</a>, just after Easter and named him in honour of her late father. Tonks and Lupin both felt that Harry should become Teddy's godfather, and he accepted. To the relief of Remus, Teddy inherited Tonks's <a href="/wiki/Metamorphmagus" title="Metamorphmagus">metamorphmagi</a> abilities, which he demonstrated mere hours after his birth, and showed no signs of <a href="/wiki/Lycanthropy" title="Lycanthropy">lycanthropy</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-37" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-37">&#91;37&#93;</a></sup>

<p>Tonks', Remus', <a href="/wiki/Fred_Weasley" title="Fred Weasley">Fred Weasley</a>'s and <a href="/wiki/Colin_Creevey" title="Colin Creevey">Colin Creevey</a>'s deaths caused Harry great pain before and after his ultimate defeat of Lord Voldemort. <p>Tonks was a young witch with a pale heart-shaped face and dark twinkling eyes.<sup id="cite_ref-ootp3_22-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-ootp3-22">&#91;22&#93;</a></sup> As a <a href="/wiki/Metamorphmagus" title="Metamorphmagus">Metamorphmagus</a>, she could change her appearance at will. She typically kept her hair short and spiky, and often bubblegum-pink.  
</p><p>She has also worn short and spiky violet hair, curly white-blonde hair, and waist length tomato-red hair. Her natural hair colour might have been mousy brown since it was stuck on that colour during the year she had difficulty morphing.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP5_29-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP5-29">&#91;29&#93;</a></sup>

<div class="quote">
<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince">"<i>Last year she had been inquisitive (to the point of being a little annoying at times), she had laughed easily, she had made jokes.</i>"</span></dd>

<dd></dd></dl></div>
<p>Tonks was enthusiastic, bubbly, and easily able to make friends with people she had just met. She was eager and would often want to help <a href="/wiki/Molly_Weasley" title="Molly Weasley">Molly Weasley</a> in the preparation of dinner at <a href="/wiki/Grimmauld_Place" title="Grimmauld Place">Grimmauld Place</a>, something that Molly was not eager about. As a <a href="/wiki/Metamorphmagus" title="Metamorphmagus">Metamorphmagus</a>, Tonks was known for using her gift for lighter purposes, such as experimenting with her appearance for entertainment. Despite this, she had difficulty controlling her magical abilities when she was unhappy, such as when her love was rejected by <a href="/wiki/Remus_Lupin" title="Remus Lupin">Remus Lupin</a> or when she was very angry.
</p>

<p>Tonks was notoriously clumsy and curious. For example, she smashed a plate in the Dursley's kitchen during their rescue of Harry Potter and knocked over an umbrella stand in Grimmauld Place. In her youth, Tonks tried to solve her mistakes in unorthodox ways, though they were not always effective.
</p><p>Despite her young age, Tonks possessed an incredible will. This is evident when she cast a non-verbal Patronus Charm, which requires a powerfully happy memory, despite being highly miserable at the time. Tonks was a staunchly loyal supporter of <a href="/wiki/Albus_Dumbledore" title="Albus Dumbledore">Albus Dumbledore</a>. In her work for the <a href="/wiki/Order_of_the_Phoenix" title="Order of the Phoenix">Order of the Phoenix</a>, she was brave, dependable, and selfless. She repeatedly put her life on the line for the sake of following through with Dumbledore's plans.
</p><p>Tonks rather disliked her given name and considered her mother as a fool for naming her as such, thus insisting to be called "Tonks" instead. She didn't express any rejection when others referred to her as "Dora", a shortened form of the given name she detested so much. This may have been because her father and her husband both called her it.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Magical_abilities_and_skills">Magical abilities and skills</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Nymphadora_Tonks?veaction=edit&amp;section=22" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Nymphadora_Tonks?action=edit&amp;section=22" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>

<dd></dd></dl></div><p>Having been trained in the use of both defensive and offensive <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magic</a> by <a href="/wiki/Alastor_Moody" title="Alastor Moody">Alastor Moody</a>, Tonks was a highly accomplished and skilful <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch</a>. She was a proficient duellist and a <a href="/wiki/Metamorphmagus" title="Metamorphmagus">Metamorphmagus</a>. Her skills allowed her to hold her own against and even defeat several experienced <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a>.

<ul><li><b><a href="/wiki/Nonverbal_spell" title="Nonverbal spell">Non-verbal magic</a></b>: Tonks was capable of performing non-verbal magic, including advanced charms. For instance, she was able to duel several skilled Death Eaters without saying a single word. This testifies to her being a skilful powerful witch, as non-verbal magic is very difficult.</li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Auror_training" title="Auror training">Auror</a> skills</b>: Tonks was rather young for an Auror and the last person to become a fully fledged Auror before Harry Potter was accepted, which spoke for her considerable magical skills. However, her clumsiness gave her trouble with the Stealth and Tracking portion of the course, as she almost failed for this. Her work position required proficiency in the practical application of magic and exceptional marks at school, highlighting her abilities as a witch. Besides this, <a href="/wiki/Edward_Tonks" title="Edward Tonks">her father</a> noted that she had been in several sticky situations in her time as an Auror and had always found a way to emerge from them unscathed.</li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Duelling" title="Duelling">Duelling</a></b>: As an official <a href="/wiki/Dark_Wizard" title="Dark Wizard">Dark Wizard</a> catcher, Tonks' duelling talents were naturally considerable and her reflexes were very fast despite her clumsiness, which sometimes got her into trouble at work. She fired a <a href="/wiki/Stunning_Spell" title="Stunning Spell">Stunning Spell</a> at top Death Eater <a href="/wiki/Lucius_Malfoy" title="Lucius Malfoy">Lucius Malfoy</a>, though it should be noted that he was caught off guard and it is unknown if it actually hit him. She was capable of duelling <a href="/wiki/Thorfinn_Rowle" title="Thorfinn Rowle">Thorfinn Rowle</a> to a stalemate despite him rapidly firing spells at her, before Harry Potter hit him with a hex. Tonks survived her first two encounters with the most powerful lieutenant of <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a>, her aunt <a href="/wiki/Bellatrix_Lestrange" title="Bellatrix Lestrange">Bellatrix Lestrange</a>. Despite being defeated by her aunt during the first encounter, she was able to duel her for a significant period of time. During her second encounter with her aunt, she was able to duel her aunt to a standstill in a mid-air battle between the two and injured Bellatrix's husband, <a href="/wiki/Rodolphus_Lestrange" title="Rodolphus Lestrange">Rodolphus Lestrange</a>, at the same time, albeit with help from <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ron Weasley</a>. However, Tonks was ultimately murdered by Bellatrix during the Battle of Hogwarts, though she just had recovered from giving birth and it is unknown what the nature of this encounter was. In her youth, she was even able to hold her ground against an <a href="/wiki/Unidentified_Dark_Witch" title="Unidentified Dark Witch">Unidentified Dark Witch</a> and the <a href="/wiki/Wizard_in_white_robes" title="Wizard in white robes">Wizard in white robes</a> alone, and even managed to disarm and bind the <a href="/wiki/Wizard_in_white_robes" title="Wizard in white robes">Wizard in white robes</a> in ropes, while she was only a sixth year and her enemies were adults.</li></ul>

<ul><li><b><a href="/wiki/Metamorphmagus" title="Metamorphmagus">Metamorphmagus</a></b>: Unlike most other wizards and witches, Tonks possessed the ability to change her physical appearance at will, without the need for a potion or a spell to assist. With simply a thought, she was capable of changing her facial features, nose shape, the colour of her hair and eyes, or even her entire form. Tonks used this ability both to entertain people and to stop people from recognising her, as evidenced by her ability to take the form of <a href="/wiki/Cuthbert_Binns" title="Cuthbert Binns">Professor Binns</a>, a <a href="/wiki/Ghost" title="Ghost">ghost</a>. Tonks' rare skill was invaluable for espionage, as she worked as a covert operative inside the Ministry to feed the Order information and disguised herself as an old woman to escort Harry to the Hogwarts Express. She also passed the Disguise and Concealment portion of her Auror training without any studying.</li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Transfiguration" title="Transfiguration">Transfiguration</a></b>: As an Auror, Tonks would have achieved a N.E.W.T. in this <a href="/wiki/Transfiguration_(class)" title="Transfiguration (class)">subject</a> as well, since Aurors frequently have to transfigure and <a href="/wiki/Untransfiguration" title="Untransfiguration">un-transfigure</a> things in their line of work. She achieved either an "Exceeds Expectations" or "Outstanding" in this subject.<sup id="cite_ref-OotP29_41-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OotP29-41">&#91;41&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Charm" title="Charm">Charms</a></b>: Tonks demonstrated considerable talent in charm-work. In <a href="/wiki/1995" title="1995">1995</a>, she effectively cast a <a href="/wiki/Scouring_Charm" title="Scouring Charm">Scouring Charm</a> to clean <a href="/wiki/Hedwig" title="Hedwig">Hedwig</a>'s cage, another charm to pack Harry's belongings before leaving <a href="/wiki/Privet_Drive" title="Privet Drive">Privet Drive</a>, and the <a href="/wiki/Stunning_Spell" title="Stunning Spell">Stunning Spell</a> against Lucius Malfoy. She was also able to effectively cast the <a href="/wiki/Shield_Charm" title="Shield Charm">Shield Charm</a> as a means of protection. Furthermore, Tonks could cast a corporeal <a href="/wiki/Patronus_Charm" title="Patronus Charm">Patronus</a>, which is an incredibly advanced piece of <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magic</a>, by the time she was in her <a href="/wiki/Fourth_year" title="Fourth year">fourth year</a> in Hogwarts.<sup id="cite_ref-42" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-42">&#91;42&#93;</a></sup> Besides this, she was one of only a handful of people who knew how to use a Patronus as a means of communication.</li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Defence_Against_the_Dark_Arts" title="Defence Against the Dark Arts">Defence Against the Dark Arts</a></b>: A natural performer in defensive magic, Tonks proved her skill in this field as an Auror and Order of the Phoenix member, including <a href="/wiki/Counter-curse" title="Counter-curse">counter-curses</a> as she could remove a <a href="/wiki/Full_Body-Bind_Curse" title="Full Body-Bind Curse">Full Body-Bind Curse</a>. She knew how to check if a door had an <a href="/wiki/Imperturbable_Charm" title="Imperturbable Charm">Imperturbable Charm</a> on it, by flicking objects at the door in question, teaching this skill to Ginny Weasley. Seeing as how a N.E.W.T. in the subject is a mandatory requirement for Auror training, she likely achieved excellent results at both O.W.L. and N.E.W.T. level.</li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Flight" title="Flight">Flying</a></b>: Tonks was a great <a href="/wiki/Broomstick" title="Broomstick">broomstick</a> flyer, as demonstrated by her escorting Harry from the Dursleys' house along with the Order. She was even able to emerge unscathed from a duel fought on a mid-air broomstick against her aunt, who was also proficient in flying a broomstick.</li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Apparition" title="Apparition">Apparition</a></b>: Like most adult wizards and witches, Tonks was capable of Apparating successfully at will. In <a href="/wiki/1996" title="1996">1996</a>, after visiting Molly Weasley, she left by Disapparating.</li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Healing_magic" title="Healing magic">Healing magic</a></b>: In <a href="/wiki/1996" title="1996">1996</a>, Tonks effectively used the <a href="/wiki/Healing_spell" title="Healing spell">healing spell</a> <i><a href="/wiki/Episkey" title="Episkey">Episkey</a></i> to fix <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry</a>'s nose when it was broken, showing she was skilled with medical magic.</li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Potion" title="Potion">Potions</a></b>: Tonks had achieved a <a href="/wiki/Nastily_Exhausting_Wizarding_Test" title="Nastily Exhausting Wizarding Test">N.E.W.T.</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Potions_(class)" title="Potions (class)">subject</a>, considering how knowledge of <a href="/wiki/Poison" title="Poison">poisons</a> and <a href="/wiki/Antidote" title="Antidote">antidotes</a> are essential studies for Aurors.<sup id="cite_ref-OotP29_41-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OotP29-41">&#91;41&#93;</a></sup> She must have earned an "Outstanding" in her O.W.L. exam, since she was taught by <a href="/wiki/Severus_Snape" title="Severus Snape">Severus Snape</a>, and likely had earned either an 'Outstanding' or an 'Exceeds Expectations' on her N.E.W.T. exam.</li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Herbology" title="Herbology">Herbology</a></b>: As an Auror, Tonks would have had to have achieved either an "Outstanding" or "Exceeds Expectations" in her O.W.L. and N.E.W.T. in this subject, considering how Aurors often have to handle dangerous magical plants in their work.<sup id="cite_ref-OotP29_41-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OotP29-41">&#91;41&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Divination" title="Divination">Divination</a></b>: Tonks possessed considerable proficiency and knowledge in the field of Divination, as she earned either an "Exceeds Expectations" or "Outstanding" in the <a href="/wiki/Divination_(class)" title="Divination (class)">subject</a> at O.W.L. level that allowed her to study it further at N.E.W.T. level.</li>
<li><b>Detective skills</b>: As an Auror, Tonks was an effective detective, intelligent and able to make logical deductions. For example, she quickly found Harry <a href="/wiki/Full_Body-Bind_Curse" title="Full Body-Bind Curse">immobilised</a> on the <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Express" title="Hogwarts Express">Hogwarts Express</a> based on subtle clues. Additionally, she was able to carefully fulfil her duties to the Order without jeopardising her career at the Ministry.</li></ul>

<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="The_House_of_Black">The House of Black</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Nymphadora_Tonks?veaction=edit&amp;section=26" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: The House of Black">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Nymphadora_Tonks?action=edit&amp;section=26" title="Edit section: The House of Black"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<p>Tonks maintained a very good relationship with her parents, although she utterly detested the name Nymphadora, which was bestowed upon her by <a href="/wiki/Andromeda_Tonks" title="Andromeda Tonks">her mother</a>. She took after <a href="/wiki/Edward_Tonks" title="Edward Tonks">her father</a>, preferring a little disorder and mess to fanatical cleanliness which mother ironically excelled at, referring to the <a href="/wiki/Dursley_family" title="Dursley family">Dursleys</a>' immaculate home as she rescued Harry from the house. She was affectionately called Dora by her father, which she didn't seem to mind even though it was technically a shortened version of her first name. Shortly before her death, Tonks learned that her own father died and decided to name her son after him.
</p>

<p>Tonks's extended family was populated by members of the <a href="/wiki/House_of_Black" title="House of Black">House of Black</a>, although she wasn't considered a member of the House since her mother had married a <a href="/wiki/Muggle-born" title="Muggle-born">Muggle-born</a> and had been struck off the family tree, much like her first cousin once removed, <a href="/wiki/Sirius_Black" title="Sirius Black">Sirius Black</a>. He mentioned that Tonks' mother was his favourite cousin while growing up. Since Sirius spent most of Tonks' life in <a href="/wiki/Azkaban" title="Azkaban">Azkaban</a>, the two never got a chance to really know each other. On occasion Tonks mentioned that she thought Sirius was still handsome even after the time he spent in Azkaban.
</p><p>
Despite the fact that they barely knew each other, it can be assumed that she was upset by his death, most likely because he was family and an ally to the <a href="/wiki/Order_of_the_Phoenix" title="Order of the Phoenix">Order of the Phoenix</a>. Prior to Sirius' death, Tonks was duelling Bellatrix (Sirius' murderer) and was defeated by her. Hermione stated that she might feel guilty because of this, but it was only speculation.</p><div class="quote">

<p>Her aunts included Bellatrix Lestrange and <a href="/wiki/Narcissa_Malfoy" title="Narcissa Malfoy">Narcissa Malfoy</a>, which meant <a href="/wiki/Draco_Malfoy" title="Draco Malfoy">Draco Malfoy</a> was among her many cousins. Both Bellatrix and Narcissa refused to acknowledge Nymphadora as their niece, as they had already severed all bonds with Andromeda when she married a Muggle-born, and even felt humiliated when Voldemort made fun of them about Tonks' marriage with a werewolf. Bellatrix seemed particularly eager to kill her niece whenever they encountered one another, especially after Voldemort instructed her to "<i>prune</i>" her family tree. The pair had several duels, and Tonks noted that Bellatrix seemed as keen to murder her as she was to rid the Dark Lord of Harry. She eventually succeeded during the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a> on 2 May, 1998. 
</p><p>It can be assumed that Narcissa didn't consider <a href="/wiki/Edward_Lupin" title="Edward Lupin">Teddy Lupin</a>, Nymphadora's son, to be a member of the Black family either, as he had known Muggle ancestry and a werewolf for a father, thus making his blood impure. Since her mother was disowned after marrying her father, Tonks' name wasn't on the Black's family tree, so the family house-elf, <a href="/wiki/Kreacher" title="Kreacher">Kreacher</a>, who was supposed to listen to anyone in the family, refused to take orders from Tonks. Instead, he kept muttering insults about her <a href="/wiki/Half-blood" title="Half-blood">half-blood</a> status.

<p>At some point during her time with the <a href="/wiki/Order_of_the_Phoenix" title="Order of the Phoenix">Order of the Phoenix</a>, Tonks met and fell in <a href="/wiki/Love" title="Love">love</a> with fellow member Remus Lupin, who found her amusing, then impressing, and eventually reciprocated her feelings, although he didn't notice them at first because he was used to considering himself unclean and unworthy. After a year of increasingly warm friendship, Tonks made an idle remark about one of their fellow Order members during a mission they had together ("He’s still handsome, isn't he, even after Azkaban?"). Before he could stop himself, Remus had replied bitterly that he supposed she had fallen for his old friend ("He always got the women."). At this, Tonks replied: "You’d know perfectly well who I’ve fallen for, if you weren’t too busy feeling sorry for yourself to notice." 
</p><p>Despite being extremely happy, Remus felt that any relationship between the two of them was too dangerous because he was a <a href="/wiki/Werewolf" title="Werewolf">werewolf</a>. He also felt he was too old for her — being thirteen years older than she was — and too poor due to his difficulties in maintaining a job because of his condition. She tried on several occasions to convince him otherwise, but he constantly refused her pleas and even volunteered for long-term, lethal missions to keep distance between them. Tonks became depressed over this and confided in <a href="/wiki/Molly_Weasley" title="Molly Weasley">Molly Weasley</a>. Her emotional state caused her <a href="/wiki/Metamorphmagus" title="Metamorphmagus">metamorphic</a> abilities to suffer, and her hair became mousy brown rather than its usual pink (although it is possible that this is just due to her either wanting to look more like her husband or her misery at him not loving her appearing deliberately). Her <a href="/wiki/Patronus_Charm" title="Patronus Charm">Patronus</a> also changed, resembling a <a href="/wiki/Wolf" title="Wolf">wolf</a> that showed her love for Remus. While on Hogsmeade duty, Tonks was informed that a werewolf had been killed by <a href="/wiki/Fenrir_Greyback" title="Fenrir Greyback">Fenir Greyback</a>. She showed great concern by immediately contacting Dumbledore, to confirm whether or not it had been Lupin who was killed.
</p>

<p>In <a href="/wiki/1997" title="1997">1997</a>, following the death of <a href="/wiki/Albus_Dumbledore" title="Albus Dumbledore">Albus Dumbledore</a> and the scarring of <a href="/wiki/William_Weasley" title="William Weasley">Bill Weasley</a>, Tonks openly declared her love for Remus, citing that <a href="/wiki/Fleur_Delacour" title="Fleur Delacour">Fleur Delacour</a> still loved Bill despite the nature of his injuries and the possibility of werewolf contamination. Remus protested that the situations were not the same, but eventually relented and the two began a relationship.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP29_43-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP29-43">&#91;43&#93;</a></sup> They were <a href="/wiki/Wedding_of_Remus_Lupin_and_Nymphadora_Tonks" title="Wedding of Remus Lupin and Nymphadora Tonks">married</a> quickly in a quiet ceremony in the summer of 1997, shortly before the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_the_Seven_Potters" title="Battle of the Seven Potters">Battle of the Seven Potters</a>. Tonks became pregnant by this time, so Remus, fearing that his child would be a <a href="/wiki/Werewolf" title="Werewolf">werewolf</a>, was initially horrified at the prospect, believing he had put both Tonks and his unborn child in great jeopardy. They split up just after Bill and Fleur's wedding, and Tonks went to stay with her parents. However, they reconciled sometime before Christmas, after <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry</a> and Remus had argued over his responsibilities as a husband and father at Grimmauld Place.
</p><p>After this on the radio station <i>Potterwatch</i>, Remus said that if he could talk to Harry then, he would have told him to follow his instincts because they are almost always right, it can be assumed that he was talking about returning to Tonks, with whom he passed almost all her pregnancy. The couple's son, <a href="/wiki/Edward_Lupin" title="Edward Lupin">Teddy Remus Lupin</a>, was born just after Easter, <a href="/wiki/1998" title="1998">1998</a>, named after Tonks' father who had been killed a few weeks earlier by <a href="/wiki/Snatchers" title="Snatchers">Snatchers</a>, and his father Remus. Sadly, the family would not have long together. During the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a>, both Tonks and Remus were killed.<sup id="cite_ref-DH33_2-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH33-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<p>Tonks got pregnant in <a href="/wiki/1997" title="1997">1997</a> and was excited about her newborn son despite the fact that his birth would be around the time of a war. He was born in April and the couple choose to name him Teddy after Tonks' murdered father. It was obvious that Tonks loved her son very much. He was a <a href="/wiki/Metamorphmagus" title="Metamorphmagus">Metamorphmagus</a>, like his mother. Nymphadora wanted <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> to be his godfather, as she and Remus decided that there was no one better than their young friend and wanted him to raise their son if they died.
</p><p>Unfortunately Tonks was murdered by her aunt, <a href="/wiki/Bellatrix_Lestrange" title="Bellatrix Lestrange">Bellatrix Lestrange</a> in the Battle of Hogwarts and never got to see her son grow up. Teddy was raised by his grandmother, <a href="/wiki/Andromeda_Tonks" title="Andromeda Tonks">Andromeda</a>. He also spent much of his time with Harry, Ginny, and their children. Teddy visited the Potter home four nights and week for dinner and was over so much that Harry thought he might as well move in with them. It can be assumed that Harry acted as a parental figure towards Teddy, honouring Tonks and Remus.

<p>Alastor "Mad-Eye" Moody was Tonks' mentor during her time training as an <a href="/wiki/Auror" title="Auror">Auror</a>, and later whilst they were both in the <a href="/wiki/Order_of_the_Phoenix" title="Order of the Phoenix">Order of the Phoenix</a>. Despite Moody's surly attitude and behaviour, Tonks was always at ease with him, making funny comments at his opinions and lectures, such as the one he gave <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> on elementary wand-safety the night the <a href="/wiki/Advance_Guard" title="Advance Guard">Advance Guard</a> escorted him to Grimmauld Place.<sup id="cite_ref-ootp3_22-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-ootp3-22">&#91;22&#93;</a></sup> Other witches and wizards found him too intimidating and weren't brave enough to tease him openly, but because the pair were quite close, Tonks didn't mind laughing at him.
</p><p>For his part, Moody was openly fond of Tonks. Harry once noted that she had been his favourite during their time together at the Ministry. When Tonks was injured during her duel with Bellatrix Lestrange in the <a href="/wiki/Department_of_Mysteries" title="Department of Mysteries">Department of Mysteries</a>, Mad-Eye crawled his way to her side in an effort to help her regain consciousness, despite having been injured himself by <a href="/wiki/Antonin_Dolohov" title="Antonin Dolohov">Antonin Dolohov</a>. Tonks was devastated by Moody's death in 1997, openly crying in front of the <a href="/wiki/Weasley_family" title="Weasley family">Weasley family</a> and rest of the Order of the Phoenix members.<sup id="cite_ref-DH5_35-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH5-35">&#91;35&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<p>Nymphadora Tonks and Harry Potter met in 1995 when, as a member of the <a href="/wiki/Advance_Guard" title="Advance Guard">Advance Guard</a>, she was a part of the Order's operation to transfer Harry from <a href="/wiki/4_Privet_Drive" title="4 Privet Drive">4 Privet Drive</a> to <a href="/wiki/12_Grimmauld_Place" title="12 Grimmauld Place">12 Grimmauld Place</a>. At the time of their first meeting, Harry was intimidated by the rest of the Advance Guard, but not Tonks who was clumsy and funny. The pair immediately took a liking to each other, and Harry was particularly impressed by Tonks's position as an Auror and extraordinary <a href="/wiki/Metamorphmagus" title="Metamorphmagus">Metamorphmagus</a> powers. Tonks was impressed with Harry's Firebolt and tried to comfort him during his trying times with the Ministry of Magic. Adopting a big sister role for him, Tonks would greet him by saying "<i>Wotcher, Harry</i>". Later, during the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_the_Department_of_Mysteries" title="Battle of the Department of Mysteries">Battle of the Department of Mysteries</a>, Tonks was among the Order members that came to the rescue of Harry and his friends. Harry was later relieved to hear that she would recover from the injuries she suffered at the hands of <a href="/wiki/Bellatrix_Lestrange" title="Bellatrix Lestrange">Bellatrix Lestrange</a>.
</p><p>Tonks' friendship with Harry grew for the next two and a half years. In 1996, Harry hoped Tonks would be among the Aurors sent to escort him to the <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Express" title="Hogwarts Express">Hogwarts Express</a>, though she sadly was not. Shortly after, Tonks rescued Harry on the Hogwarts Express; she performed the <a href="/wiki/Counter-curse" title="Counter-curse">counter-curse</a> to remove the <a href="/wiki/Full_Body-Bind_Curse" title="Full Body-Bind Curse">Full Body-Bind Curse</a> placed on him by <a href="/wiki/Draco_Malfoy" title="Draco Malfoy">Draco Malfoy</a>, healed his broken nose and ensured he reached safety within Hogwarts. She could do this because she had been watching the students exit the train and immediately went to investigate when Harry had not gotten off too, indicating her protectiveness towards him despite her heartache. Harry was disappointed by how gloomy Tonks had become, missing his friend's bubbly nature.
</p><p>When she married Remus Lupin in <a href="/wiki/1997" title="1997">1997</a>, a happier Tonks was sorry that Harry couldn't attend the wedding due to him being required to remain with his <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggle</a> relatives to ensure the protective charm on him held. Later, when Lupin briefly left Tonks, due to fears that the child she carried would be infected with <a href="/wiki/Lycanthropy" title="Lycanthropy">lycanthropy</a> like himself, Harry was furious with him for leaving his family and wanting to leave his friend to raise a baby without its dad. It was largely because of Harry's words that Lupin returned to Tonks. In <a href="/wiki/1998" title="1998">1998</a>, when she gave birth to a son named <a href="/wiki/Edward_Lupin" title="Edward Lupin">Teddy Lupin</a>, both Tonks and Lupin agreed that Harry would be the godfather. Harry was devastated when he learned that Nymphadora was murdered by her aunt, Bellatrix Lestrange. He noted that the grief he felt over the death of Tonks was akin to a physical wound.
</p><p>Even after defeating Voldemort, the pain of losing Tonks still affected him and he likely never stopped missing her. In memory of both Tonks and Lupin, Harry acted as a father figure to their orphaned son Teddy. Teddy despite being raised by <a href="/wiki/Andromeda_Tonks" title="Andromeda Tonks">Tonks' mother</a>, would spend three or four nights a week dining with the Potter and Weasley families.
</p>

<p>Tonks and fellow Order member Molly Weasley had a rather close relationship. Though there was a significant difference in age between the two, the pair often confided in one another. Molly often invited Tonks to dine with the <a href="/wiki/Weasley_family" title="Weasley family">Weasley family</a>, and she was always a welcome guest at the events held by the Weasley matriarch. Indeed, Mrs Weasley appeared to be Tonks' closest adult friend. Molly appeared to be the only person that Tonks confided her love of <a href="/wiki/Remus_Lupin" title="Remus Lupin">Remus Lupin</a> to, and was noted by Harry as acting somewhat frosty to Lupin during the Christmas holidays, though he misinterpreted the reason why. 
</p><p>Several people suspected that Molly hoped her son <a href="/wiki/William_Weasley" title="William Weasley">Bill Weasley</a> would fall in love with Tonks, rather than marry <a href="/wiki/Fleur_Delacour" title="Fleur Delacour">Fleur Delacour</a>. For her part, Molly reacted heatedly to disparaging remarks made by Fleur in regards to Tonks, which is indicative of the high opinion she held of Tonks.
</p><p>At the conclusion of the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a>, it was Molly who duelled and killed Tonks's murderer, <a href="/wiki/Bellatrix_Lestrange" title="Bellatrix Lestrange">Bellatrix Lestrange</a>. It is likely that Molly knew <a href="/wiki/Edward_Lupin" title="Edward Lupin">Teddy Lupin</a>, Nymphadora's son, because Teddy was dating <a href="/wiki/Victoire_Weasley" title="Victoire Weasley">Victoire Weasley</a>, Molly's granddaughter. In addition to this, as Harry Potter's godson, he frequented Harry and Ginny's house.
</p>

She immediately struck up a friendship with them, acting somewhat like an older sister to the teenagers during their time at Grimmauld Place. Ginny appreciated Tonks since, although she was an adult, she was interested in having fun and taught her how to check if a door had an <a href="/wiki/Imperturbable_Charm" title="Imperturbable Charm">Imperturbable Charm</a> on it, by flicking objects at the door in question. Hermione liked the fact that Tonks was proof that women could also be successful and get good positions after school. Both liked the fact that she could change her appearance at will, as she entertained them both over dinner by changing her nose into various shapes.<sup id="cite_ref-ootp5_44-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-ootp5-44">&#91;44&#93;</a></sup>

<p>In <a href="/wiki/1996" title="1996">1996</a>, Ginny revealed that she would prefer Tonks rather than Fleur to marry her brother <a href="/wiki/William_Weasley" title="William Weasley">Bill</a> and be part of their family. Ironically, Tonks was, albeit distantly, related to Ginny anyway. Hermione and Ginny felt worried about Tonks being depressed lately, such that it even affected her magical abilities. The two, however, became glad about her marriage with <a href="/wiki/Remus_Lupin" title="Remus Lupin">Remus Lupin</a> in mid-1997.
</p><p>During the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a>, Tonks and Ginny fought against some Death Eaters. Later, the two girls were very devastated when they found out during the one-hour armistice that Tonks had been murdered, and mourned over her body together along with Remus', <a href="/wiki/Fred_Weasley" title="Fred Weasley">Fred</a>'s, and over fifty others that had fallen during the battle. It is also possible that Hermione and Ginny provided a surrogate aunt and mother figure, respectively, during the childhood of Tonks' son Teddy, considering that Ginny's <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">husband</a> was Teddy's godfather and served as a father figure to him.

<p><a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ron Weasley</a> and Tonks became good friends in 1995, when they were both staying at the <a href="/wiki/12_Grimmauld_Place" title="12 Grimmauld Place">12 Grimmauld Place</a>, headquarters of the <a href="/wiki/Order_of_the_Phoenix" title="Order of the Phoenix">Order of the Phoenix</a>. Ron liked Tonks and thought she was a nice girl, but less beautiful than Fleur Delacour. However, he acknowledged her funniness and friendliness. Tonks and Ron both fought during the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_the_Department_of_Mysteries" title="Battle of the Department of Mysteries">Battle of the Department of Mysteries</a>, the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_the_Astronomy_Tower" title="Battle of the Astronomy Tower">Battle of the Astronomy Tower</a>, the Battle of the Seven Potters (where they were paired together and barely escaped from <a href="/wiki/Bellatrix_Lestrange" title="Bellatrix Lestrange">Bellatrix Lestrange</a>), in the Attack at the Burrow, and in the Battle of Hogwarts, where Tonks was murdered by Bellatrix Lestrange. Ron was saddened by her death, and after the war he became a friend of Tonks' son, Teddy.
</p><p>Tonks had a good relationship with the other members of the Weasley family, too. She seemed to be friendly with the twins <a href="/wiki/Fred_and_George_Weasley" title="Fred and George Weasley">Fred and George</a>, as they all were funny and enjoyed having fun. Fred was killed during the final battle, like Tonks was. Their bodies were put near to each other at the <a href="/wiki/Great_Hall" title="Great Hall">Great Hall</a>, as well as the body of <a href="/wiki/Remus_Lupin" title="Remus Lupin">Remus Lupin</a>.
</p>

After the Battle of the Seven Potters was over, Arthur thanked Tonks and Lupin, because they saved Ron and George. <p>Nymphadora Tonks was a good friend of <a href="/wiki/Kingsley_Shacklebolt" title="Kingsley Shacklebolt">Kingsley Shacklebolt</a>, an auror like her and also one of the most recent members of the Order. They fought together in many battles for the <a href="/wiki/Second_Wizarding_War" title="Second Wizarding War">Second Wizarding War</a>. After Tonks was murdered, Kingsley was very sad.
</p><p>Tonks might have been a friend of <a href="/wiki/Sturgis_Podmore" title="Sturgis Podmore">Sturgis Podmore</a>, <a href="/wiki/Elphias_Doge" title="Elphias Doge">Elphias Doge</a>, <a href="/wiki/Emmeline_Vance" title="Emmeline Vance">Emmeline Vance</a>, <a href="/wiki/Arabella_Figg" title="Arabella Figg">Arabella Figg</a>, <a href="/wiki/Hestia_Jones" title="Hestia Jones">Hestia Jones</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Mundungus_Fletcher" title="Mundungus Fletcher">Mundungus Fletcher</a>, as they had contact through the order. She shared the fate of being one of many members of the Order killed in various battles during the wars. Tonks also knew <a href="/wiki/Aberforth_Dumbledore" title="Aberforth Dumbledore">Aberforth Dumbledore</a>. 
</p><p>Nymphadora greatly respected Professor <a href="/wiki/Albus_Dumbledore" title="Albus Dumbledore">Albus Dumbledore</a>, founder and leader of the Order. She also had a good relationship with <a href="/wiki/Minerva_McGonagall" title="Minerva McGonagall">Minerva McGonagall</a>, as they sat together grieving (along with others) after Dumbledore's death. When Tonks publicly declared her love for Lupin, McGonagall thought that Tonks should marry him, and that Dumbledore would be happy if he knew that there was a little more love in the world.
</p><p>Severus Snape was Tonks' Potions professor and would therefore have contributed to her becoming an Auror, as a high <a href="/wiki/Nastily_Exhausting_Wizarding_Test" title="Nastily Exhausting Wizarding Test">N.E.W.T.</a> score is required for all major magical subjects. During her years as a Hogwarts student, however, Tonks almost killed Snape by inadvertently throwing a <a href="/wiki/Venomous_Tentacula" title="Venomous Tentacula">Venemous Tentacula</a> at him. This mishap served to infuriate Snape to such a degree that Tonks deemed him capable of "killing [her]", but, under <a href="/wiki/Pomona_Sprout" title="Pomona Sprout">Pomona Sprout</a>'s prompting, he relented to give her permanent detention instead.<sup id="cite_ref-45" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-45">&#91;45&#93;</a></sup>  In her adulthood, Tonks seemed to have no particular opinion of Snape regarding his loyalty, though it can be assumed that she trusted him because Dumbledore did but did not view him as a friend. In <a href="/wiki/1996" title="1996">1996</a>, Snape insulted her wolf Patronus, which by extension was an insult to her love for Lupin. After Snape's murder of Dumbledore, Tonks came to view the former as an enemy. Tonks died before learning of Snape's true loyalty or that he had tried to save her husband during the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_the_Seven_Potters" title="Battle of the Seven Potters">Battle of the Seven Potters</a>.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Nymphadora_Tonks?veaction=edit&amp;section=37" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Nymphadora_Tonks?action=edit&amp;section=37" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li><i>Nymphadora</i> literally means "Gift of the Nymphs." Nymphs are nature spirits and minor deities in Greek mythology who rule trees, sacred groves, streams and the ocean. During the Renaissance, northern Europeans equated the classical nymphs with their own legends of elves. If we therefore read "gift of the nymphs" as "gift of the elves", this echoes the ancient myth of babies stolen by elves and replaced by hideous monsters called "changelings". 'Nymph' is also the name of the "immature stage of a dragonfly or damselfly before it moults, before metamorphosing into a winged adult". Both meanings could be references to her metamorphosing skills.</li>
<li>A 'Tonk' literally means, 'a fool or idiot', 'a powerful hit or strike'. This could be related to Tonks's clumsiness and ability. The name <i>Tonks</i> may have been taken from notable British painter, art teacher and surgeon <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Henry_Tonks" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Henry Tonks">Henry Tonks</a>, who combined his artistic and surgical background to record exact images of the various types of facial injuries that occurred among soldiers during World War I. His emphasis on recording, drawing and painting facial features may have led Rowling to chose this surname and more specifically used it to refer to a specific individual who had the ability to change her facial features at will.</li>
<li>Her husband's surname, Lupin, is quite fitting for Tonks: As a Metamorphmagus, she was noted for her ability to change her appearance to adapt or blend in in her work as an Auror, as wolves are one of the most adaptive mammals on the planet. It is a fitting name for someone whose occupation is capturing dark wizards/witches, as wolves are known to serve the needs of their pack above all else, making Tonks somewhat wolf-like herself, not to mention her rebellious personality and that wolves are extremely wild animals. Further similarities between her and wolves include her having been sorted into Hufflepuff (whose dorms and common room are subterranean) much like the dens of wolves, and that she is a relative of Sirius Black (whose Animagus form is that of a dog, a distant cousin of the wolf).</li></ul>

<ul><li>Tonks was the first confirmed Metamorphmagus introduced in canon. Her son Teddy is the second.</li>
<li>In <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter:_Hogwarts_Mystery" title="Harry Potter: Hogwarts Mystery">Harry Potter: Hogwarts Mystery</a></i>, if selecting Tonks as the distraction for Madam <a href="/wiki/Irma_Pince" title="Irma Pince">Irma Pince</a>, and as the person who later accompanied <a href="/wiki/Jacob%27s_sibling" title="Jacob&#39;s sibling">Jacob's sibling</a>, <a href="/wiki/Tulip_Karasu" title="Tulip Karasu">Tulip Karasu</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Barnaby_Lee" title="Barnaby Lee">Barnaby Lee</a> into the <a href="/wiki/Vault_of_Fear" title="Vault of Fear">Vault of Fear</a>, when meeting up at the <a href="/wiki/Training_Grounds" title="Training Grounds">Training Grounds</a>, Tonks  brought a pot of baby mandrake for the distraction. She later would reveal that all of the occurring threats caused by Dark Magic had her considering to become an <a href="/wiki/Auror" title="Auror">Auror</a>. After practising together, Jacob's sibling told Tonks she could really be an Auror someday, given her being so powerful. Tonks thanked her friend for the compliment, and stated that she started to take things more seriously after their talk.<sup id="cite_ref-46" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-46">&#91;46&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li>Considering the birth of <a href="/wiki/Delphini" title="Delphini">Delphini</a>, daughter of Voldemort and Bellatrix, before <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a>, it is possible that the reason Voldemort told Bellatrix to prune her family tree and get rid of Nymphadora was Voldemort's pride, because he didn't want his offspring to have such unworthy relatives, as Tonks would be Delphini's first cousin, half-blood and married to werewolf. Interestingly, it seems that Voldemort never paid any special attention to Bellatrix's niece before, though Tonks was the child from the scandalous marriage between Bellatrix's sister from the noble House of Black and a Muggle-born, as well as being a skilled Auror who fought against his minions. This instruction from the Dark Lord made Bellatrix more eager in deciding that killing Nymphadora was her priority. Delphini was born at the <a href="/wiki/Malfoy_Manor" title="Malfoy Manor">Malfoy Manor</a>, so it is possible that at the moment of the <a href="/wiki/Meeting_at_Malfoy_Manor" title="Meeting at Malfoy Manor">Meeting at Malfoy Manor</a> she already was born or that Bellatrix was in early stages of pregnancy</li></ul>

<ul><li><a href="/wiki/J._K._Rowling" title="J. K. Rowling">J. K. Rowling</a> originally intended for Tonks to survive the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a>, but chose to have her and Remus die in reparation for giving <a href="/wiki/Arthur_Weasley" title="Arthur Weasley">Arthur Weasley</a> a reprieve in the fifth book.</li>
<li>Though Tonks and <a href="/wiki/Charles_Weasley" title="Charles Weasley">Charlie Weasley</a> were in the same year at school, there was no reason to believe they knew each other well before their appearances in <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter:_Hogwarts_Mystery" title="Harry Potter: Hogwarts Mystery">Hogwarts Mystery</a> (in which <a href="/wiki/Jacob%27s_sibling" title="Jacob&#39;s sibling">Jacob's sibling</a> is also in their year). <ul><li>Tonks is portrayed by <a href="/wiki/Natalia_Tena" title="Natalia Tena">Natalia Tena</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_(film_series)" title="Harry Potter (film series)">film adaptations</a> of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Order_of_the_Phoenix" title="Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix">Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix</a></i>, <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Half-Blood_Prince_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince (film)">Half-Blood Prince</a></i> and <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Deathly_Hallows:_Part_1" title="Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 1">Deathly Hallows: Part 1</a></i> and <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Deathly_Hallows:_Part_2" title="Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 2">Part 2</a>.</i> Coincidentally, Tena shares her character's initials.
<ul><li>During Natalia's audition for the role of Tonks, she tripped over a table and chair giving the impression that she's clumsy (like Tonks). Wherein the <a href="/wiki/David_Yates" title="David Yates">director</a> found her energy and humour as the very thing he's looking for, for the role of the young <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch</a>.</li></ul></li>
<li>English actress <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Anna_Friel" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Anna Friel">Anna Friel</a>, who was <a href="/wiki/David_Thewlis" title="David Thewlis">David Thewlis</a>' common law wife at the time, auditioned for the part of Tonks since the character is romantically linked to Remus Lupin. However, she did not get the role.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Kelly_Macdonald" title="Kelly Macdonald">Kelly Macdonald</a> was originally asked to play Tonks<sup id="cite_ref-47" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-47">&#91;47&#93;</a></sup>. She was later cast as <a href="/wiki/Helena_Ravenclaw" title="Helena Ravenclaw">Helena Ravenclaw</a>.</li>
<li>In the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Order_of_the_Phoenix_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix (film)">film adaptation</a> of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Order_of_the_Phoenix" title="Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix">Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix</a></i>, to get Tonks's look, the front part of Natalie Tena's hair was dyed, and the back part was a dyed wig.<sup id="cite_ref-48" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-48">&#91;48&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li>In the books, Nymphadora's hair is described as "short and spiky", but in the films it is usually shoulder-length. It is also purple as opposed to pink as the film-makers had associated the colour pink with <a href="/wiki/Dolores_Umbridge" title="Dolores Umbridge">Professor Dolores Umbridge</a>. It was portrayed as short and brown in the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Half-Blood_Prince_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince (film)">film adaptation</a> of <i>Half-Blood Prince</i> however, as it had been in the book of the same name. It is possible she may be depressed over the death of her cousin Sirius and is having trouble with her <a href="/wiki/Metamorphmagus" title="Metamorphmagus">morphing</a> like in the book.</li>
<li>Tonks has very little to do in any of the Harry Potter films in which she appears. She has four lines in <i>Order of the Phoenix</i>, four lines in <i>Half-Blood Prince</i>, five lines in <i>DH Part 1</i> and no lines in <i>DH Part 2</i> (except in a deleted scene where she has one line). Her romance with Remus, her pregnancy and her Metamorphmagi were not given much light, only being briefly mentioned due to pacing in the films. She is a character that is 'squeezed in,' but loved nevertheless, by her fans.</li>
<li>In the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Order_of_the_Phoenix_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix (film)">film adaptation</a> of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Order_of_the_Phoenix" title="Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix">Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix</a></i>, Tonks isn't seen changing her hair colour the way she does in the books. Instead, it only changes once involuntarily (from purple to red) when she angrily tells <a href="/wiki/Alastor_Moody" title="Alastor Moody">Mad-Eye Moody</a> not to call her "Nymphadora." It does briefly change to white when she apparates in during the fight in the <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">Ministry</a>.</li>
<li>In the last film it appears that Tonks is shown in the Quad Battlements with Kingsley, Mr Weasley and her husband Remus, whereas in the books Tonks most likely ran to the grounds to find Remus because that is where <a href="/wiki/Aberforth_Dumbledore" title="Aberforth Dumbledore">Aberforth Dumbledore</a> told her Remus was.</li>
<li>Her shortest appearance to date was <i>Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 2</i>, in which she appeared only in two brief scenes and had no lines. However, a deleted scene shows her being reunited with Remus on <a href="/wiki/The_Quad" title="The Quad">The Quad</a>. He tells her that she should be with Teddy, and she responds: "He'll sleep 'till dawn, and snore like his father. It's you that needs me tonight."</li>
<li>Several publicity photos show Tena wearing a blonde wig as Tonks, but she does not do so in any of her four film appearances to date.</li>
<li>On the second disc of the Order of the Phoenix DVD, there is a special feature in which Natalia Tena, in make up, costume and character, gives everyone a tour of the Harry Potter studios and sets. She chats with cast and crew members and plays with props from both the fifth and previous films, practises her duelling and demonstrates her talents as a musician. She does more in this feature than in any of the films.</li></ul>
