<p>She wrote a <a href="/wiki/Georgina_Smyth%27s_letter_to_Tilden_Toots" title="Georgina Smyth&#39;s letter to Tilden Toots">letter</a> to the radio programme <i><a href="/wiki/Toots,_Shoots_%27n%27_Roots" title="Toots, Shoots &#39;n&#39; Roots">Toots, Shoots 'n' Roots</a></i> asking how to treat a <a href="/wiki/Flitterbloom" title="Flitterbloom">Flitterbloom</a> that she had not <a href="/wiki/Water" title="Water">watered</a> in months. The Flitterbloom was needed for a <a href="/wiki/Potion" title="Potion">potion</a> that Smyth planned to brew, but, even after she threatened the plant with <a href="/wiki/Curse" title="Curse">curses</a>, it would not revive.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
