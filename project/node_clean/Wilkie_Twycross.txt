<p><b>Wilkie Twycross</b> (b. before <a href="/wiki/1979" title="1979">1979</a>) was a <a href="/wiki/Great_Britain" title="Great Britain">British</a> <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> and employee of the <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">British Ministry of Magic</a>, who was the <a href="/wiki/Apparition_Instructor" title="Apparition Instructor">Apparition Instructor</a> at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> to students who were age seventeen or approaching age seventeen and wish to obtain a licence to <a href="/wiki/Apparition" title="Apparition">Apparate</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP18_1-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP18-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>  
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Wilkie_Twycross?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Wilkie_Twycross?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<div class="quote">
<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince">"<i>The important things to remember when <a href="/wiki/Apparition" title="Apparition">Apparating</a> are the three D's.</i>"</span></dd>
<dd>&#8212;Wilkie Twycross<sup class="noprint"><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Half-Blood_Prince" title="Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince">[src]</a></sup></dd>
<dd></dd></dl></div><p>Twycross was described as "<i>oddly colourless, with transparent eyelashes, wispy hair, and an insubstantial air, as though a single gust of wind might blow him away</i>." <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>, upon meeting him, wondered whether this was because of the large amount of Apparating and Disapparating he did.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP18_1-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP18-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Many of the <a href="/wiki/Sixth_year" title="Sixth year">sixth year</a> students in Twycross's <a href="/wiki/1997" title="1997">1997</a> <a href="/wiki/Apparition_(class)" title="Apparition (class)">Apparition class</a> disliked and called him various insults, inspired by the "Three D's of Apparition" that he tried to teach them. The most moderate of these were "<a href="/wiki/Dog" title="Dog">dog</a>-breath" and "<a href="/wiki/Faeces" title="Faeces">dung</a>-head." Perhaps because of this, Twycross seemed very fond of his few good students, such as <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione Granger</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP18_1-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP18-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Magical_abilities_and_skills">Magical abilities and skills</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Wilkie_Twycross?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Wilkie_Twycross?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li><b><a href="/wiki/Apparition" title="Apparition">Apparition</a></b>: As the <a href="/wiki/Apparition_Instructor" title="Apparition Instructor">Apparition Instructor</a> for <a href="/wiki/Sixth_year" title="Sixth year">sixth year</a> Hogwarts students, Twycross would have been highly accomplished in apparition as a form of <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magical</a> transportation. He had a very sophisticated understanding of the art of apparition, teaching his students the specifics needed to apparate successfully and the <a href="/wiki/Splinching" title="Splinching">dangers</a> of it, showing his proficiency in this field.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP18_1-10" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP18-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup></li></ul>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Wilkie_Twycross?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Wilkie_Twycross?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p><i><a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Wilkie" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Wilkie">Wilkie</a></i> is a surname used in <a href="/wiki/Scotland" title="Scotland">Scotland</a> and <a href="/wiki/England" title="England">England</a> possibly a diminutive of William. <i><a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Twycross" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Twycross">Twycross</a></i> is a small village and civil parish in Leicestershire, England. Also <i>twy-</i> is an archaic form of <i>two</i> so his surname could literally mean <i>two-cross</i>, or one who crosses between two places; hence, one who Apparates.
</p>
