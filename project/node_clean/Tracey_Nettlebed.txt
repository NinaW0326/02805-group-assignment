<b>Tracey Nettlebed</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Slytherin" title="Slytherin">Slytherin</a> <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">student</a> at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a>. Several students stated that she was willing to <i>"dish the dirt on anyone"</i> if what she gained in return was to her satisfaction. In keeping with this assessment, in <a href="/wiki/1996" title="1996">1996</a> she agreed to tell <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> what she knew about <a href="/wiki/Draco_Malfoy" title="Draco Malfoy">Draco Malfoy</a>'s activities in the <a href="/wiki/Seventh-floor_corridor" title="Seventh-floor corridor">Seventh-floor corridor</a> in exchange for a <a href="/wiki/Skiving_Snackbox" title="Skiving Snackbox">Skiving Snackbox</a>, which she planned to use to skip <a href="/wiki/Transfiguration_(class)" title="Transfiguration (class)">Transfiguration</a> (a subject she described as <i>"stupid"</i>). She warned Harry not to tell anyone about what she told him.
