<dd>&#8212;<a href="/wiki/Lee_Jordan" title="Lee Jordan">Lee Jordan</a> appeals to listeners on <a href="/wiki/Potterwatch" title="Potterwatch">Potterwatch</a><sup class="noprint"><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Deathly_Hallows" title="Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows">[src]</a></sup></dd>

<p><b>Mrs Thomas</b> was the mother of <a href="/wiki/Dean_Thomas" title="Dean Thomas">Dean Thomas</a>. Though she was a <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggle</a>, her <a href="/wiki/Dean_Thomas%27s_father" title="Dean Thomas&#39;s father">first husband</a> was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a>, but he never told her so, believing that not knowing the truth would keep her safe. He deserted Mrs Thomas when their son was still a small boy and was eventually murdered by <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-jkr_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-jkr-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Mrs Thomas remarried a <a href="/wiki/Dean_Thomas%27_stepfather" class="mw-redirect" title="Dean Thomas&#39; stepfather">Muggle man</a> and had <a href="/wiki/Dean_Thomas%27_half-siblings" class="mw-redirect" title="Dean Thomas&#39; half-siblings">several children</a> with him. The family lived in <a href="/wiki/London" title="London">London</a> and were described as having "a very happy home life".<sup id="cite_ref-jkr_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-jkr-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>When a <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_acceptance_letter" title="Hogwarts acceptance letter">letter</a> arrived for Dean from <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts</a> in <a href="/wiki/1991" title="1991">1991</a>, Mrs Thomas considered the possibility that her first husband might have been a wizard, but, like her son, was never able to confirm this suspicion.<sup id="cite_ref-jkr_1-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-jkr-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>In <a href="/wiki/1995" title="1995">1995</a>, Dean opted not to let his parents know about the murder of <a href="/wiki/Cedric_Diggory" title="Cedric Diggory">Cedric Diggory</a>, presumably because he worried they would withdraw him from Hogwarts.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Dean had to go into hiding in <a href="/wiki/1997" title="1997">1997</a> because he was unable to prove he was a <a href="/wiki/Half-blood" title="Half-blood">half-blood</a> to the <a href="/wiki/Muggle-Born_Registration_Commission" title="Muggle-Born Registration Commission">Muggle-Born Registration Commission</a>. Mrs Thomas and her second husband were not sure where their son had gone, and were deeply concerned, so the radio programme <i><a href="/wiki/Potterwatch" title="Potterwatch">Potterwatch</a></i> asked its listeners to contact the Thomases if they knew anything about Dean.<sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
