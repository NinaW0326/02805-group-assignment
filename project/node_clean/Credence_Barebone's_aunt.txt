<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Credence_Barebone%27s_aunt?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Credence_Barebone%27s_aunt?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>This woman was caring for her nephew <a href="/wiki/Credence_Barebone" title="Credence Barebone">Aurelius Dumbledore</a>. At some point during a ship voyage to <a href="/wiki/United_States_of_America" title="United States of America">America</a> in <a href="/wiki/1901" title="1901">1901</a>, the ship she and Aurelius went on sank during a storm. Unbeknownst to her, her nephew was switched in the crib with another <a href="/wiki/Corvus_Lestrange_(V)" title="Corvus Lestrange (V)">baby</a> by a young <a href="/wiki/Leta_Lestrange" title="Leta Lestrange">Leta Lestrange</a>. The life boat she went on capsized, drowning her when she dives into the water trying to save Corvus whom she believed was her nephew.
</p><p>Aurelius survived and ended up being raised by an <a href="/wiki/Mary_Lou_Barebone" title="Mary Lou Barebone">unrelated woman</a> in <a href="/wiki/New_York" title="New York">New York</a>, who renamed him Credence Barebone.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Physical_description">Physical description</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Credence_Barebone%27s_aunt?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Physical description">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Credence_Barebone%27s_aunt?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Physical description"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The aunt of Credence Barebone was described being a dark-haired young woman. She wears a nightdress and life jacket minutes prior before her death.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Personality">Personality</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Credence_Barebone%27s_aunt?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Personality">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Credence_Barebone%27s_aunt?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Personality"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Judging on her actions the aunt is showed to have taken her responsibility as her nephew's caretaker very serious to the point that she willingly risk her own life to save the child she believed to be him.
</p>
