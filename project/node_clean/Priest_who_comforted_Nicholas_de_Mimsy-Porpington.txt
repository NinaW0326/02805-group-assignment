<p>This <b>individual</b> (fl. <a href="/wiki/1492" class="mw-redirect" title="1492">1492</a>) was a priest and the man who comforted <a href="/wiki/Nicholas_de_Mimsy-Porpington" title="Nicholas de Mimsy-Porpington">Sir Nicholas de Mimsy-Porpington</a> just before his execution on the dawn of <a href="/wiki/1492" class="mw-redirect" title="1492">1492</a>. Upon denying Sir Nicholas of wearing a hat (extravagant plumed hats were somewhat of a passion of his), the <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> knew his end was nigh.<sup id="cite_ref-background_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-background-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
