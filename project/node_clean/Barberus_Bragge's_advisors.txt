Chief <a href="/wiki/Barberus_Bragge" title="Barberus Bragge">Barberus Bragge</a> had a group of <b>advisors</b>. 
</p><p>During a <a href="/wiki/Quidditch" title="Quidditch">Cuaditch</a> game in <a href="/wiki/1269" class="mw-redirect" title="1269">1269</a>, Chief Bragge released a <a href="/wiki/Golden_Snidget" title="Golden Snidget">Golden Snidget</a> onto the field, and offered the player who captured it one-hundred and fifty <a href="/wiki/Wizarding_currency" title="Wizarding currency">Galleons</a>. In protest, <a href="/wiki/Modesty_Rabnott" title="Modesty Rabnott">Modesty Rabnott</a> summoned the Snidget to her and fled the game. After she was caught, Bragge had to be calmed down by his advisers to prevent him to turn <a href="/wiki/Modesty_Rabnott" title="Modesty Rabnott">Modesty</a> into a horned toad, or worse. Rabnott was only then fined ten <a href="/wiki/Wizarding_currency" title="Wizarding currency">Galleons</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
