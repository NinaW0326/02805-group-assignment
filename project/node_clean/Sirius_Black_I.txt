<b>Sirius Black I</b> (<a href="/wiki/1845" class="mw-redirect" title="1845">1845</a> – <a href="/wiki/1853" class="mw-redirect" title="1853">1853</a>) was a <a href="/wiki/Pure-blood" title="Pure-blood">pure-blood</a> <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a>, possibly the eldest son of <a href="/wiki/Cygnus_Black_I" title="Cygnus Black I">Cygnus Black</a> and <a href="/wiki/Ella_Max" title="Ella Max">Ella Max</a>, and the elder brother of <a href="/wiki/Phineas_Nigellus_Black" title="Phineas Nigellus Black">Phineas Nigellus</a>, <a href="/wiki/Elladora_Black" title="Elladora Black">Elladora</a> and <a href="/wiki/Iola_Black" title="Iola Black">Iola</a>. Sirius died when he was only eight years old, for unknown reasons.<sup id="cite_ref-BFT_1-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-BFT-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Sirius_Black_I?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Sirius_Black_I?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Sirius is a name of the brightest star in the night sky. In the <a href="/wiki/House_of_Black" title="House of Black">Black family</a> there were also <a href="/wiki/Sirius_Black_II" title="Sirius Black II">Sirius Black II</a> (a nephew of Sirius I, son of Phineas Nigellus) and <a href="/wiki/Sirius_Black" title="Sirius Black">Sirius III</a>, <a href="/wiki/Walburga_Black" title="Walburga Black">Walburga</a>'s and <a href="/wiki/Orion_Black" title="Orion Black">Orion's</a> son. If Cygnus was his father, <a href="/wiki/Arcturus_Black_I" title="Arcturus Black I">Arcturus Black I</a> was his uncle.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Sirius_Black_I?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Sirius_Black_I?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>The reason why Sirius died so young, aged 7 or 8, is currently unknown. However, it is interesting to note that he died during the <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Third_cholera_pandemic_(1852%E2%80%9360)" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Third cholera pandemic (1852–60)">third cholera pandemic (1852–60)</a> that claimed 23,000 lives in Great Britain, 10,000 lives in <a href="/wiki/London" title="London">London</a> alone. Even though <a href="/wiki/J._K._Rowling" title="J. K. Rowling">J. K. Rowling</a> has said that "<i>a wizard could catch anything a <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggle</a> might catch, but he could cure all of it</i>",<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> it could be the case that Sirius had not yet manifested his magical powers at the time due to his young age (<a href="/wiki/Seven" title="Seven">seven</a> is the age by which most experts believe that <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magic</a> will reveal itself in a wizard or witch<sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>). This, however, would not explain why an adult wizard failed to heal him, though it's possible Rowling's comment referred to the workings of a wizard's magical innate immune system and not to the existence of a wizarding cure for every non-magical malady.</li>
<li>Sirius was maybe a son of <a href="/wiki/Cygnus_Black_I" title="Cygnus Black I">Cygnus Black I</a> and his wife Ella Max, but if this is true, that means Cygnus was 16 years old when he had Sirius. This is possibly a mathematical mistake. If Cygnus was his father, <a href="/wiki/Arcturus_Black_I" title="Arcturus Black I">Arcturus Black I</a> was his uncle. His aunt may have been <a href="/wiki/Misapinoa_Black" title="Misapinoa Black">Misapinoa Blishwick (née Black)</a>.</li></ul>
