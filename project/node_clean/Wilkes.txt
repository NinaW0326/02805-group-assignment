<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Wilkes?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Wilkes?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Wilkes attended <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> in the <a href="/wiki/1970s" title="1970s">1970s</a> and was <a href="/wiki/Sorting_ceremony" title="Sorting ceremony">sorted</a> into <a href="/wiki/Slytherin" title="Slytherin">Slytherin</a> house.<sup id="cite_ref-GOF27_3-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF27-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>According to <a href="/wiki/Sirius_Black" title="Sirius Black">Sirius Black</a>, Wilkes was a member of a <a href="/wiki/Gang_of_Slytherins" title="Gang of Slytherins">gang of Slytherins</a> and aspiring <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a> that included <a href="/wiki/Severus_Snape" title="Severus Snape">Severus Snape</a>, <a href="/wiki/Avery_(Marauder-era)" class="mw-redirect" title="Avery (Marauder-era)">Avery</a>, <a href="/wiki/Mulciber_(Marauder-era)" class="mw-redirect" title="Mulciber (Marauder-era)">Mulciber</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Evan_Rosier" title="Evan Rosier">Evan Rosier</a>. They all entered the service of <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a> during the height of the <a href="/wiki/First_Wizarding_War" title="First Wizarding War">First Wizarding War</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-GOF27_3-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF27-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Wilkes fought in several battles of the war. In <a href="/wiki/1980" title="1980">1980</a>, a year before <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a>'s downfall, Wilkes was caught by <a href="/wiki/Auror" title="Auror">Aurors</a> and was killed, while attempting to avoid being imprisoned in <a href="/wiki/Azkaban" title="Azkaban">Azkaban</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-GOF27_3-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF27-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Magical_abilities_and_skills">Magical abilities and skills</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Wilkes?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Wilkes?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li><b><a href="/wiki/Dark_Arts" title="Dark Arts">Dark Arts</a>: </b>Being a <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eater</a>, Wilkes would have been highly proficient and accomplished in dark magic. More than likely, they would have been able to perform the <a href="/wiki/Unforgivable_Curses" title="Unforgivable Curses">Unforgivable Curses</a>, along with various other <a href="/wiki/Dark_charm" title="Dark charm">dark charms</a> and <a href="/wiki/Spell" title="Spell">spells</a>.</li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Duelling" title="Duelling">Duelling</a></b>: Being a Death Eater who participated and fought in the <a href="/wiki/First_Wizarding_War" title="First Wizarding War">First Wizarding War</a>, Wilkes likely at least a fairly competent duellist with some skill in <a href="/wiki/Martial_magic" title="Martial magic">martial magic</a>. Despite this, they were not able to avoid being killed by <a href="/wiki/Auror" title="Auror">Aurors</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-GOF27_3-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF27-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup></li></ul>
