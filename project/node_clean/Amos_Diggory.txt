<p><b>Amos Diggory</b><sup id="cite_ref-GoF21_2-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GoF21-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-GoF6_3-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GoF6-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-GoF9_5-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GoF9-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> was a <a href="/wiki/Great_Britain" title="Great Britain">British</a> <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> who worked for the <a href="/wiki/Department_for_the_Regulation_and_Control_of_Magical_Creatures" title="Department for the Regulation and Control of Magical Creatures">Department for the Regulation and Control of Magical Creatures</a> in the <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">British Ministry of Magic</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-GoF6_3-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GoF6-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> He and <a href="/wiki/Cedric_Diggory%27s_mother" title="Cedric Diggory&#39;s mother">his wife</a> lost their son <a href="/wiki/Cedric_Diggory" title="Cedric Diggory">Cedric</a> in <a href="/wiki/June" title="June">June</a> of <a href="/wiki/1995" title="1995">1995</a> during the last and third task in the <a href="/wiki/Triwizard_Tournament" title="Triwizard Tournament">Triwizard Tournament</a>, when he was murdered by <a href="/wiki/Peter_Pettigrew" title="Peter Pettigrew">Peter Pettigrew</a> on <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a>'s orders.<sup id="cite_ref-GoF32_6-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GoF32-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>After the events of the <a href="/wiki/Second_Wizarding_War" title="Second Wizarding War">Second Wizarding War</a>, Amos still had not made peace with the death of his son. In <a href="/wiki/2020" title="2020">2020</a>, he sought a meeting with <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>, in the hopes of saving his deceased son via a new <a href="/wiki/Time-turner" class="mw-redirect" title="Time-turner">time-turner</a> that was discovered.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Amos_Diggory?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Amos_Diggory?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_life">Early life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Amos_Diggory?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Early life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Amos_Diggory?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Early life"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Amos was born into the wizarding <a href="/wiki/Diggory_family" title="Diggory family">Diggory family</a> and was the only child of his parents.<sup id="cite_ref-Act3Scene18_7-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Act3Scene18-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup> He presumably attended <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardy</a> in his youth, where he would have received his <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magical</a> education. He ended up working in the <a href="/wiki/Ministry_of_Magic" title="Ministry of Magic">Ministry of Magic</a> and by the <a href="/wiki/1980s" title="1980s">1980s</a> he worked for the <a href="/wiki/Department_for_the_Regulation_and_Control_of_Magical_Creatures" title="Department for the Regulation and Control of Magical Creatures">Department for the Regulation and Control of Magical Creatures</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-HM_8-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HM-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Venture_to_Hogwarts">Venture to Hogwarts</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Amos_Diggory?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Venture to Hogwarts">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Amos_Diggory?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Venture to Hogwarts"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>At some point between <a href="/wiki/1984" title="1984">1984</a> and <a href="/wiki/1985" title="1985">1985</a>, Amos came to Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry to celebrate <a href="/wiki/Rubeus_Hagrid" title="Rubeus Hagrid">Rubeus Hagrid</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Rubeus_Hagrid%27s_birthday_celebration" title="Rubeus Hagrid&#39;s birthday celebration">birthday</a>. Shortly before the celebration, he allowed <a href="/wiki/Jacob%27s_sibling" title="Jacob&#39;s sibling">Jacob's sibling</a> to choose between a <a href="/wiki/Fire_crab" class="mw-redirect" title="Fire crab">fire crab</a>, a <a href="/wiki/Murtlap" title="Murtlap">Murtlap</a> and a <a href="/wiki/Porlock" title="Porlock">Porlock</a> as a birthday present for Hagrid.<sup id="cite_ref-HM_8-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HM-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Second_Wizarding_War">Second Wizarding War</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Amos_Diggory?veaction=edit&amp;section=5" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Second Wizarding War">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Amos_Diggory?action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Second Wizarding War"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Amos subsequently had little to do in the <a href="/wiki/Second_Wizarding_War" title="Second Wizarding War">Second Wizarding War</a> waged by the <a href="/wiki/Order_of_the_Phoenix" title="Order of the Phoenix">Order of the Phoenix</a> against Lord Voldemort; his son was ultimately avenged when Voldemort was defeated.
</p>
<h3><span id="Life_at_St_Oswald's_Home_and_the_timeturner"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="Life_at_St_Oswald.27s_Home_and_the_timeturner">Life at St Oswald's Home and the timeturner</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Amos_Diggory?veaction=edit&amp;section=6" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Life at St Oswald&#039;s Home and the timeturner">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Amos_Diggory?action=edit&amp;section=6" title="Edit section: Life at St Oswald&#039;s Home and the timeturner"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>By <a href="/wiki/2020" title="2020">2020</a>, Amos was living at <a href="/wiki/St_Oswald%27s_Home_for_Old_Witches_and_Wizards" title="St Oswald&#39;s Home for Old Witches and Wizards">St Oswald’s Home for Old Witches and Wizards</a>. One of his carers was <a href="/wiki/Delphini" title="Delphini">Delphini</a>, who affectionately called him uncle. One day, Amos heard about how <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> may have found a <a href="/wiki/Time-turner" class="mw-redirect" title="Time-turner">time-turner</a>. He decided to schedule a meeting with him. However, each meeting kept getting changed to a later date at the last minute. Several months later, Amos decided to visit Harry at his home in the middle of the night. Delphini helped Amos and he apprehended Harry when he returned home.
</p><p>Amos begged Harry to use the time-turner to go back in time and save Cedric. Harry politely declined, stating he couldn’t meddle with time. Amos thought Harry was partly responsible for Cedric's death, because Voldemort never wanted Cedric. He was devastated to hear that Voldemort considered Cedric to be “the spare”. Harry lied to Amos and told him the story about a new time-turner was untrue. Amos thought Harry to be cold.<sup id="cite_ref-13" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-13">&#91;13&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Unbeknownst to Amos, the conversation was overheard by <a href="/wiki/Albus_Potter" title="Albus Potter">Albus Potter</a>. Albus decided to visit him and tell him that he and his friend <a href="/wiki/Scorpius_Malfoy" title="Scorpius Malfoy">Scorpius Malfoy</a> would be happy to go back in time and save Cedric. Amos did not take them seriously at first considering they were only children, but he was convinced they could when Delphini persuaded him and made him see even children could change the future by reminding him of the great things that happened in the <a href="/wiki/Second_Wizarding_War" title="Second Wizarding War">Second Wizarding War</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-14" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-14">&#91;14&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>The <a href="/wiki/Plot_to_restore_Lord_Voldemort" title="Plot to restore Lord Voldemort">plan</a> to save Cedric inevitably changed the future, but not to the better one Amos had hoped for. It is unknown what happened to him in the changed timelines, but they were eventually undone. This left Amos without a son again. It is not known how he felt after this, but he still lamented about Cedric and would have no choice but to accept Cedric was gone.<sup id="cite_ref-CC_15-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-CC-15">&#91;15&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Amos was later visited by Harry and <a href="/wiki/Draco_Malfoy" title="Draco Malfoy">Draco Malfoy</a>, who believed that he was responsible for their sons going missing again. They found out that Amos had sent them owls, which Amos denied and said he had no idea about their whereabouts. They told Amos that the children were last sighted with his niece Delphini, which surprised Amos, since he told them that he does not have a niece.<sup id="cite_ref-16" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-16">&#91;16&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<p>Amos was described as being "tall"; he had a ruddy face and a "scrubby" brown beard.<sup id="cite_ref-GoF6_3-11" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GoF6-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<p>Amos was extremely proud of his son, Cedric, to the point of boasting about his accomplishments at every given opportunity.<sup id="cite_ref-GoF6_3-12" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GoF6-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> He became quite angry and disrespectful when Cedric was not even given a mention in the <i><a href="/wiki/Daily_Prophet" title="Daily Prophet">Daily Prophet</a></i> story about the <a href="/wiki/Triwizard_Tournament" title="Triwizard Tournament">Triwizard Tournament</a>, whereas <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry</a> got a whole page to himself, though this was understandable, given that Cedric was meant to be the only <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts</a> champion.<sup id="cite_ref-GoF21_2-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GoF21-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Amos was quite genial and friendly when walking through the woods with the Weasleys, Harry, and Hermione Granger. He was rather paternal, but came off as somewhat haughty and rude due to his loud voice and boastfulness of his son's accomplishments.
</p><p>He was also quite emotional, roaring with joy when discussing Cedric winning a <a href="/wiki/Quidditch" title="Quidditch">Quidditch</a> game<sup id="cite_ref-GoF6_3-13" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GoF6-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> and roaring with anger at <a href="/wiki/Winky" title="Winky">Winky</a> the <a href="/wiki/House-elf" title="House-elf">house-elf</a> when interrogating her.<sup id="cite_ref-GoF9_5-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GoF9-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> He sobbed openly when he saw Cedric had died and when he was interviewed about his son's death, but at least didn't blame Harry for it, and contrary, believed Harry's claim of Voldemort's return and thanked him for returning Cedric's body.<sup id="cite_ref-GoF37_12-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GoF37-12">&#91;12&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>By the time Amos was an old man, with him stating to be dying, he was still incredibly grief-stricken to even greater levels than before. He lamented deeply about his "beautiful" son and no longer respected or cared for the memorization of Cedric to the whole wizarding world as a man who died bravely, only wanting to see him at least one last time, and showed no hesitation in attempting to meddle with time to bring him back. He displayed a level of impatience but coupled with strong determination to the point after months of relentlessly sending owls to Harry turned out to be useless, he resolved firmly to go round to his house without caring Harry had just arrived home and was about to prepare his children for a new school year, showing he had became somewhat apathetic to the needs of others in his bitterness. Even though he once said he didn't think Harry was responsible for Cedric's death and thanked him for bringing Cedric's body back, he changed his mind by the time he found out about a new time-turner. He thought Harry to be partly responsible since Voldemort wanted to kill him really, not Cedric. Amos would angrily declare Harry to be a stone-cold Ministry man when he would not go back in time. He was also considered a "miserable old sod" by his carers at his home.<sup id="cite_ref-CC_15-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-CC-15">&#91;15&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Magical_abilities_and_skills">Magical abilities and skills</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Amos_Diggory?veaction=edit&amp;section=9" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Amos_Diggory?action=edit&amp;section=9" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li><b><a href="/wiki/Magizoology" title="Magizoology">Magizoology</a></b>: As a member of the <a href="/wiki/Department_for_the_Regulation_and_Control_of_Magical_Creatures" title="Department for the Regulation and Control of Magical Creatures">Department for the Regulation and Control of Magical Creatures</a>, Amos Diggory had a high level of knowledge regarding <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magical</a> <a href="/wiki/Creature" title="Creature">creatures</a>. He knew how to <a href="/wiki/Ministry_of_Magic_Classification" title="Ministry of Magic Classification">classify</a> them and the various laws regulating the ownership of such creatures, such as <a href="/wiki/Ban_on_Experimental_Breeding" title="Ban on Experimental Breeding">Ban on Experimental Breeding</a>.</li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Charm" title="Charm">Charms</a></b>: Amos could produce a non-corporeal <a href="/wiki/Patronus_Charm" title="Patronus Charm">Patronus Charm</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-LEGO_4-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-LEGO-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> a mark of superior magical ability and talent given the immense difficulty of performing the charm. He could also successfully cast other charms, such as the <a href="/wiki/Eradication_Spell" title="Eradication Spell">Eradication Spell</a>, which he used to eradicate the <a href="/wiki/Dark_Mark" title="Dark Mark">Dark Mark</a> in the sky after it was conjured after the <a href="/wiki/1994_Quidditch_World_Cup" title="1994 Quidditch World Cup">1994 Quidditch World Cup</a>, along with both <a href="/wiki/Stunning_Spell" title="Stunning Spell">Stunning Spell</a>, and its <a href="/wiki/Counter-charm" title="Counter-charm">Counter-charm</a> the <a href="/wiki/Reviving_Spell" title="Reviving Spell">Reviving Spell</a>. He could also perform the <a href="/wiki/Reverse_Spell" title="Reverse Spell">Reverse Spell</a>, which he used to reveal that the last spell cast with <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter%27s_wand" title="Harry Potter&#39;s wand">wand</a> during the <a href="/wiki/Reappearance_of_the_Dark_Mark" title="Reappearance of the Dark Mark">Reappearance of the Dark Mark</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-GoF9_5-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GoF9-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup></li></ul>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Relationships">Relationships</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Amos_Diggory?veaction=edit&amp;section=10" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Relationships">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Amos_Diggory?action=edit&amp;section=10" title="Edit section: Relationships"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Wife">Wife</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Amos_Diggory?veaction=edit&amp;section=11" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Wife">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Amos_Diggory?action=edit&amp;section=11" title="Edit section: Wife"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Amos and his <a href="/wiki/Cedric_Diggory%27s_mother" title="Cedric Diggory&#39;s mother">wife</a> were obviously a happily married couple, but she could get displeased with his often arrogant and boastful behaviour.
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Cedric_Diggory">Cedric Diggory</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Amos_Diggory?veaction=edit&amp;section=12" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Cedric Diggory">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Amos_Diggory?action=edit&amp;section=12" title="Edit section: Cedric Diggory"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p><a href="/wiki/Cedric_Diggory" title="Cedric Diggory">Cedric Diggory</a> was Amos's son. He was extremely proud of Cedric and his accomplishments, often boasting about them, making Cedric embarrassed, and was pleased by him being chosen to be in the Triwizard Tournament. Amos was devastated by his son's death and in 2020, asked Harry Potter to go back in time and bring him back, but then had to accept that Cedric was gone.
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Weasley_family">Weasley family</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Amos_Diggory?veaction=edit&amp;section=13" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Weasley family">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Amos_Diggory?action=edit&amp;section=13" title="Edit section: Weasley family"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<div class="quote">
<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire (film)">"<i>Parting of the ways, I think, old chap? See you at the match.</i>"</span></dd>
<dd>&#8212;Amos Diggory's farewell to <a href="/wiki/Arthur_Weasley" title="Arthur Weasley">Arthur Weasley</a> at the <a href="/wiki/Quidditch_World_Cup" title="Quidditch World Cup">Quidditch World Cup</a><sup class="noprint"><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Goblet_of_Fire_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire (film)">[src]</a></sup></dd>
<dd></dd></dl></div>
<p><a href="/wiki/Arthur_Weasley" title="Arthur Weasley">Arthur Weasley</a> knew Amos from the Ministry of Magic. They were quite friendly, but could be at odds, especially during Winky's interrogation.
</p><p>Amos's relationship with the other Weasleys is unknown, but almost had a public confrontation with <a href="/wiki/Molly_Weasley" title="Molly Weasley">Molly Weasley</a> about Harry being in the Tournamemt.
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Harry_Potter">Harry Potter</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Amos_Diggory?veaction=edit&amp;section=14" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Harry Potter">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Amos_Diggory?action=edit&amp;section=14" title="Edit section: Harry Potter"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>'s relationship with Amos was rather mixed. Amos was sometimes rude to Harry, mostly when boasting about how <a href="/wiki/Cedric" title="Cedric">Cedric</a> beat him at Quidditch. Before the Third Task, Amos was not pleased about Cedric not being mentioned and Harry getting a whole page to himself. Despite this, Amos thanked Harry for bringing Cedric's body. However, by <a href="/wiki/2020" title="2020">2020</a>, Amos's increased grief made him blame Harry partially for Cedric's death and outright demanded Harry to bring him back through <a href="/wiki/Time" title="Time">time</a> travel and was angry and bitter when Harry refused to, declaring him a "stone-cold" Ministry man.
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Bartemius_Crouch_Senior">Bartemius Crouch Senior</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Amos_Diggory?veaction=edit&amp;section=15" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Bartemius Crouch Senior">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Amos_Diggory?action=edit&amp;section=15" title="Edit section: Bartemius Crouch Senior"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Amos's relationship with <a href="/wiki/Bartemius_Crouch_Senior" title="Bartemius Crouch Senior">Barty Crouch Snr</a> happened to be a rather strict one. When Amos thought Crouch's house-elf <a href="/wiki/Winky" title="Winky">Winky</a> had conjured the <a href="/wiki/Dark_Mark" title="Dark Mark">Dark Mark</a>, Crouch was offended by this and reprimanded Amos, especially since Crouch had proved and demonstrated throughout his long career that he detested and despised the <a href="/wiki/Dark_Arts" title="Dark Arts">Dark Arts</a> and those who practise them.
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Ludo_Bagman">Ludo Bagman</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Amos_Diggory?veaction=edit&amp;section=16" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Ludo Bagman">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Amos_Diggory?action=edit&amp;section=16" title="Edit section: Ludo Bagman"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Amos's relationship with <a href="/wiki/Ludovic_Bagman" title="Ludovic Bagman">Ludo Bagman</a> is mostly unknown, since they were only seen together after the conjuring of the Dark Mark. It is possible that their relationship was positive.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Amos_Diggory?veaction=edit&amp;section=17" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Amos_Diggory?action=edit&amp;section=17" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>"Amos" is one of the twelve minor prophets of the <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Old_Testament" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Old Testament">Old Testament</a>; it means "carried" in Hebrew.<sup id="cite_ref-BTN_17-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-BTN-17">&#91;17&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Amos_Diggory?veaction=edit&amp;section=18" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Amos_Diggory?action=edit&amp;section=18" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>He is portrayed by <a href="/wiki/Jeff_Rawle" title="Jeff Rawle">Jeff Rawle</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Goblet_of_Fire_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire (film)">fourth film</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-IMDB_18-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-IMDB-18">&#91;18&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li>Rawle also provided the voice for a wizard in both video game instalments of the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Deathly_Hallows" title="Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows">seventh book</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-IMDB_18-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-IMDB-18">&#91;18&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li>In the fourth film, Amos is depicted as being quieter and more amicable than his book counterpart is shown to be.</li>
<li>In the film he is also clean-shaven and wears oval-shaped glasses, as opposed to his book counterpart. Also, he isn't very tall, unlike the book.</li>
<li>In <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Cursed_Child_(play)" title="Harry Potter and the Cursed Child (play)">Cursed Child</a> he is portrayed by <a href="/wiki/Barry_McCarthy" title="Barry McCarthy">Barry McCarthy</a>.</li>
<li>According to <a href="/wiki/Badeea_Ali" title="Badeea Ali">Badeea Ali</a>'s art quiz in <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter:_Hogwarts_Mystery" title="Harry Potter: Hogwarts Mystery">Harry Potter: Hogwarts Mystery</a></i>, Amos Diggory was a famous painter, although whether it refers him or another individual with the same name is unknown as there are no other sources connecting Amos to painting.</li></ul>
