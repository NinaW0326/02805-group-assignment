<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Franciscus_Fieldwake?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Franciscus_Fieldwake?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>He wrote the book <i><a href="/wiki/Protection_Charm_Your_Mind:_A_Practical_Guide_to_Counter_Legilimensy" title="Protection Charm Your Mind: A Practical Guide to Counter Legilimensy">Protection Charm Your Mind: A Practical Guide to Counter Legilimensy</a></i>.<sup id="cite_ref-book_1-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-book-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p><i><a href="/wiki/The_New_York_Ghost" title="The New York Ghost">The New York Ghost</a></i> published a report by him about a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard or witch</a> from <a href="/wiki/Idaho" title="Idaho">Idaho</a> caught drunk <a href="/wiki/Broomstick" title="Broomstick">broom</a>-riding after winning a <a href="/wiki/Foxtrot" title="Foxtrot">foxtrot</a> competition in its <a href="/wiki/6_December" title="6 December">6 December</a> <a href="/wiki/1926" title="1926">1926</a> morning issue.<sup id="cite_ref-dec6sunrise_2-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-dec6sunrise-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
