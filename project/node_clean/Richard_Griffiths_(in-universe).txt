<b>Richard Griffiths</b> (fl. <a href="/wiki/1997" title="1997">1997</a>) was a <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggle</a> actor who had a large part in <i><a href="/wiki/Equus" title="Equus">Equus</a></i>, a play that was staged sometime in the late <a href="/wiki/1990s" title="1990s">1990s</a>. There was a poster mentioning him at <a href="/wiki/Luchino_Caffe" title="Luchino Caffe">Luchino Caffe</a> in the summer of <a href="/wiki/1997" title="1997">1997</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-DHpt1_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DHpt1-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
