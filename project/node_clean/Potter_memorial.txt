The <b>Potter memorial</b> is a statue at the centre of the village square in <a href="/wiki/Godric%27s_Hollow" title="Godric&#39;s Hollow">Godric's Hollow</a>. From a distance, it appears as an obelisk, carved with the names of people who died in a war (presumably <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggle</a> casualties of World War I or II). While Muggles can see only this obelisk, a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch or wizard</a> can see that the memorial changes into a statue of a <a href="/wiki/Potter_family" title="Potter family">family</a> as he or she approaches: <a href="/wiki/James_and_Lily_Potter" title="James and Lily Potter">James, Lily</a>, and an infant <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-DH16_1-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH16-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="History">History</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Potter_memorial?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: History">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Potter_memorial?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: History"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Harry Potter and <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione Granger</a> visited Godric's Hollow during Christmas of <a href="/wiki/1997" title="1997">1997</a> while they <a href="/wiki/Horcrux_hunt" class="mw-redirect" title="Horcrux hunt">hunted Voldemort's Horcruxes</a> in order for Harry to see where he had been born (and lost his parents) as well as possibly where Dumbledore left <a href="/wiki/Godric_Gryffindor%27s_sword" class="mw-redirect" title="Godric Gryffindor&#39;s sword">Godric Gryffindor's sword</a> for them. They visited the memorial, as well as Harry's parents graves.<sup id="cite_ref-DH16_1-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH16-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
