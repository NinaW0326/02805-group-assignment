<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/British_Prime_Minister_in_1996?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/British_Prime_Minister_in_1996?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span id="Meeting_with_the_'Other_Minister'"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="Meeting_with_the_.27Other_Minister.27">Meeting with the 'Other Minister'</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/British_Prime_Minister_in_1996?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Meeting with the &#039;Other Minister&#039;">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/British_Prime_Minister_in_1996?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Meeting with the &#039;Other Minister&#039;"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<div class="quote">
<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince"><b>Cornelius Fudge</b>: "<i>The Minister of Magic only reveals him--or herself to the Muggle Prime Minister of the day. We find it the best way to maintain secrecy.</i>"</span></dd>
<dd><b>Prime Minister</b>: "<i>But then, why hasn't a former Prime Minister warned me?</i>"</dd>
<dd><b>Cornelius Fudge</b>: "<i>My dear Prime Minister, are you ever going to tell anybody?</i>"</dd>
<dd>&#8212; The Prime Minister and <a href="/wiki/Cornelius_Fudge" title="Cornelius Fudge">Cornelius Fudge</a><sup class="noprint"><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Half-Blood_Prince" title="Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince">[src]</a></sup></dd></dl>
</div>
<p>Before becoming Prime Minister, this man had a very hard and gruelling campaign in the 1980s. On the day he won the election in the early <a href="/wiki/1990s" title="1990s">1990s</a>, he was greeted by the currently incumbent <a href="/wiki/Minister_for_Magic" title="Minister for Magic">Minister for Magic</a>, <a href="/wiki/Cornelius_Fudge" title="Cornelius Fudge">Cornelius Fudge</a>, who told him about the <a href="/wiki/Wizarding_world" title="Wizarding world">world of magic</a>. The Muggle Minister was actually better at accepting this than his <a href="/wiki/Prime_Minister%27s_predecessor" title="Prime Minister&#39;s predecessor">predecessor</a>, who thought Fudge was an attempt to sabotage the Prime Minister's career, and tried to throw the wizard out the window. The Prime Minister then tried to pretend none of this had happened. Despite Fudge's claim that they would probably never meet again after the first introduction, circumstances, essentially all the <a href="/wiki/Second_Wizarding_War" title="Second Wizarding War">Dark activities of the wizarding world</a>, colluded to render this statement invalid, much to the Prime Minister's discomfort.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP1_1-11" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP1-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>In <a href="/wiki/1993" title="1993">1993</a>, Fudge met with the Prime Minister to inform him that <a href="/wiki/Sirius_Black" title="Sirius Black">Sirius Black</a> <a href="/wiki/Azkaban_escapes" title="Azkaban escapes">escaped</a> from <a href="/wiki/Azkaban" title="Azkaban">Azkaban</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP1_1-12" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP1-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> Since Black was convicted of murdering <a href="/wiki/Peter_Pettigrew" title="Peter Pettigrew">Peter Pettigrew</a> and <a href="/wiki/Peter_Pettigrew%27s_unidentified_victims" title="Peter Pettigrew&#39;s unidentified victims">twelve Muggle bystanders</a> on a London street, and was thus a severe threat to both communities, Fudge felt that it was important that the Muggle Prime Minister be informed.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP1_1-13" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP1-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> As such, the event of his meeting was recorded in the <a href="/wiki/Daily_Prophet" title="Daily Prophet">Daily Prophet</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-PA3_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PA3-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> Subsequently, the Prime Minister put out a warning to the Muggle communities that Black was a known serial killer, but not specifically a wizard.<sup id="cite_ref-POA2_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-POA2-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-HBP1_1-14" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP1-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>In <a href="/wiki/1994" title="1994">1994</a>, Fudge met with the Prime Minister to discuss the events of the <a href="/wiki/Quidditch_World_Cup" title="Quidditch World Cup">Quidditch World Cup</a>, where several <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggles</a> had been tortured by <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a>, but declared that everything was fine and was being dealt with (i.e. covered up). He also informed the Prime Minister that they would be bringing three <a href="/wiki/Dragon" title="Dragon">dragons</a> and a <a href="/wiki/Sphinx" title="Sphinx">sphinx</a> into the country, and as they were classified as highly dangerous creatures, it was in the rules that the Muggle Prime Minister had to be informed.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP1_1-15" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP1-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Second_Wizarding_War">Second Wizarding War</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/British_Prime_Minister_in_1996?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Second Wizarding War">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/British_Prime_Minister_in_1996?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Second Wizarding War"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<figure class="thumb tright show-info-icon" style="width: 302px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/c/cb/Daily_Prophet_Prime_Minister.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20110415130121" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/c/cb/Daily_Prophet_Prime_Minister.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/300?cb=20110415130121" decoding="async" width="300" height="199" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="Daily Prophet Prime Minister.jpg" data-image-key="Daily_Prophet_Prime_Minister.jpg" /></a> 	 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:Daily_Prophet_Prime_Minister.jpg" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption">The <a href="/wiki/Daily_Prophet" title="Daily Prophet">Daily Prophet</a> reports <a href="/wiki/Rufus_Scrimgeour" title="Rufus Scrimgeour">Scrimgeour's</a> meeting with the Prime Minister</p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure>
<p>Early in <a href="/wiki/1996" title="1996">1996</a>, Fudge met with the Minister to inform him of the mass breakout from Azkaban. Then, later in <a href="/wiki/1996" title="1996">1996</a>, the Prime Minister was under pressure after the collapse of the <a href="/wiki/Brockdale_Bridge" title="Brockdale Bridge">Brockdale Bridge</a>, an alleged hurricane that had devastated parts of the South-West of <a href="/wiki/England" title="England">England</a>, and two highly publicised murders one of which had taken place around the corner from <a href="/wiki/Prime_Minister%27s_office" title="Prime Minister&#39;s office">10 Downing Street</a>. He was informed by the former <a href="/wiki/Minister_for_Magic" title="Minister for Magic">Minister for Magic</a> <a href="/wiki/Cornelius_Fudge" title="Cornelius Fudge">Cornelius Fudge</a> that <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a> and his <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a> were responsible for these events, and introduced him to his own replacement as Minister for Magic, <a href="/wiki/Rufus_Scrimgeour" title="Rufus Scrimgeour">Rufus Scrimgeour</a>. Scrimgeour told him that his Junior Minister, <a href="/wiki/Herbert_Chorley" title="Herbert Chorley">Herbert Chorley</a>, had been put under a poorly cast <a href="/wiki/Imperius_Curse" title="Imperius Curse">Imperius Curse</a>, which was the real reason he had been acting like a duck, and that he was being transferred to St Mungo's. Scrimgeour also informed him that his new secretary, <a href="/wiki/Kingsley_Shacklebolt" title="Kingsley Shacklebolt">Kingsley Shacklebolt</a>, was actually a highly skilled <a href="/wiki/Auror" title="Auror">Auror</a>, placed there for his security.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP1_1-16" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP1-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Early in <a href="/wiki/1997" title="1997">1997</a>, Scrimgeour met with the Prime Minister to inform him about the terror and all the deaths, including a <a href="/wiki/Unidentified_Muggle_family" title="Unidentified Muggle family">Muggle family</a>; this meeting was briefly mentioned in the <i><a href="/wiki/Daily_Prophet" title="Daily Prophet">Daily Prophet</a></i> as an 'exclusive' story.<sup id="cite_ref-DHF1_4-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DHF1-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>His fate following the <a href="/wiki/Second_Wizarding_War" title="Second Wizarding War">Second Wizarding War</a> is unknown.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/British_Prime_Minister_in_1996?veaction=edit&amp;section=4" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/British_Prime_Minister_in_1996?action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>The Prime Minister is never named in the series, and may be intended to be a fictional character. Since the Harry Potter series is set mostly in the 1990s the most likely real-life candidate would be <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/John_Major" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:John Major">Sir John Major</a>, the Prime Minister of Britain from 1990 to 1997. However during the last half of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Half-Blood_Prince" title="Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince">Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince</a></i> and <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Deathly_Hallows" title="Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows">Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows</a></i>, there was a change of government in Britain and <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Tony_Blair" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Tony Blair">Tony Blair</a> became Prime Minister, serving from 1997 to 2007. Additionally, Fudge refers to his predecessor as "he", when in real life, it would have been <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Margaret_Thatcher" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Margaret Thatcher">Margaret Thatcher</a>.</li>
<li>Winston Churchill was the <a href="/wiki/Great_Britain" title="Great Britain">British</a> Prime Minister during <a href="/wiki/Gellert_Grindelwald" title="Gellert Grindelwald">Grindelwald</a>'s reign of terror in the <a href="/wiki/1940s" title="1940s">1940s</a>, and would have had to cope with that while presiding over the largest Muggle war in history.</li>
<li>It is unknown if he ever met <a href="/wiki/Pius_Thicknesse" title="Pius Thicknesse">Pius Thicknesse</a>, who briefly became Minister for Magic in 1997. It is highly unlikely, since Thicknesse was under the control of Lord Voldemort and likely would have not collaborated with the Prime Minister, let alone the fact that he was in control of the Muggle communities.</li>
<li>Given that the Harry Potter series is fictional, it is entirely possible that this Prime Minister, and thus the previous Prime Minister as well, are none of the real life Prime Ministers of the United Kingdom, but entirely fictional ones with different names and identities. As Britain had, up until that date, had but one female Prime Minister, the pronoun "she" would be rather conclusive as to identity in the real-world.</li></ul>
