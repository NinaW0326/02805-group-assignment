During his life, he lived in <a href="/wiki/Godric%27s_Hollow" title="Godric&#39;s Hollow">Godric's Hollow</a>, and had <a href="/wiki/H._Knighton%27s_wife" title="H. Knighton&#39;s wife">a wife</a> and <a href="/wiki/H._Knighton%27s_offspring" title="H. Knighton&#39;s offspring">at least one child</a>.
