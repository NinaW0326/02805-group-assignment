<p><b>George Weasley</b> (b. <a href="/wiki/1_April" title="1 April">1 April</a>, <a href="/wiki/1978" title="1978">1978</a>) was an <a href="/wiki/England" title="England">English</a> <a href="/wiki/Pure-blood" title="Pure-blood">pure-blood</a> <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a>, the fifth son and the less dominant among the twins of <a href="/wiki/Arthur_Weasley" title="Arthur Weasley">Arthur Weasley</a> and <a href="/wiki/Molly_Weasley" title="Molly Weasley">Molly Weasley</a> <span class="template-c">(née Prewett)</span>, younger brother of <a href="/wiki/William_Weasley" title="William Weasley">Bill</a>, <a href="/wiki/Charles_Weasley" title="Charles Weasley">Charlie</a> and  <a href="/wiki/Percy_Weasley" title="Percy Weasley">Percy</a> and older brother to <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ron</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Ginevra_Weasley" title="Ginevra Weasley">Ginny</a>, and younger twin brother and best friend of the late <a href="/wiki/Fred_Weasley" title="Fred Weasley">Fred Weasley</a>. George's first few years were marked by the height of the <a href="/wiki/First_Wizarding_War" title="First Wizarding War">First Wizarding War</a> and <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a>'s first fall. He attended <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> from <a href="/wiki/1989" title="1989">1989</a>-<a href="/wiki/1996" title="1996">1996</a> with Fred, and was <a href="/wiki/Sorting_ceremony" title="Sorting ceremony">Sorted</a> into <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor" title="Gryffindor">Gryffindor House</a>. He and Fred were popular students, becoming <a href="/wiki/Beater" title="Beater">Beaters</a> for the Gryffindor <a href="/wiki/Quidditch" title="Quidditch">Quidditch</a> <a href="/wiki/Quidditch_team" title="Quidditch team">team</a> in their second year, and members of <a href="/wiki/Dumbledore%27s_Army" title="Dumbledore&#39;s Army">Dumbledore's Army</a> in their unfinished seventh year. 
A born prankster and inventor, George left Hogwarts prior to graduation to become a successful entrepreneur, alongside his twin brother, and opened the joke shop <a href="/wiki/Weasleys%27_Wizard_Wheezes" title="Weasleys&#39; Wizard Wheezes">Weasleys' Wizard Wheezes</a> at <a href="/wiki/93_Diagon_Alley" title="93 Diagon Alley">93 Diagon Alley</a>. He was a member of the <a href="/wiki/Order_of_the_Phoenix" title="Order of the Phoenix">Order of the Phoenix</a> and fought in the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_the_Seven_Potters" title="Battle of the Seven Potters">Battle of the Seven Potters</a> and the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a>; in the latter, Fred died, leaving George devastated.
</p><p>Sometime after the end of the <a href="/wiki/Second_Wizarding_War" title="Second Wizarding War">Second Wizarding</a> war, George married <a href="/wiki/Angelina_Johnson" title="Angelina Johnson">Angelina Johnson</a>, with whom he had a son named <a href="/wiki/Fred_Weasley_II" title="Fred Weasley II">Fred</a>, in honour of his late twin, and a daughter named <a href="/wiki/Roxanne_Weasley" title="Roxanne Weasley">Roxanne</a>. He and his brother Ron also turned Weasleys' Wizard Wheezes into a huge money spinner.
</p>

<p>George Weasley was appropriately born <a href="/wiki/1_April" title="1 April">1 April</a>, <a href="/wiki/1978" title="1978">1978</a>, April Fool's Day, as he and his brother <a href="/wiki/Fred_Weasley" title="Fred Weasley">Fred</a> were mischievous pranksters. Fred was born first.<sup id="cite_ref-bornfirst_15-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-bornfirst-15">&#91;15&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-bornfirst2_16-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-bornfirst2-16">&#91;16&#93;</a></sup> George was raised at his <a href="/wiki/Weasley_family" title="Weasley family">family</a> home of <a href="/wiki/The_Burrow" title="The Burrow">the Burrow</a>, near <a href="/wiki/Ottery_St_Catchpole" title="Ottery St Catchpole">Ottery St Catchpole</a> in <a href="/wiki/Devon" title="Devon">Devon</a>, <a href="/wiki/England" title="England">England</a>. He and Fred were younger than siblings <a href="/wiki/William_Weasley" title="William Weasley">Bill</a>, <a href="/wiki/Charles_Weasley" title="Charles Weasley">Charlie</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Percy_Weasley" title="Percy Weasley">Percy</a>, and older than siblings <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ron</a> and <a href="/wiki/Ginevra_Weasley" title="Ginevra Weasley">Ginny</a>. Although the family was poor, they were loving and happy.
</p><p>George and Fred were troublemakers from a young age, delighting in playing clever pranks and in teasing their siblings (especially Percy), and it was a challenge for their mother to try to keep them under control. It was not uncommon for explosions to be heard coming from the bedroom they shared.<sup id="cite_ref-GOF_17-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF-17">&#91;17&#93;</a></sup> Some of the stunts they pulled as children include setting a <a href="/wiki/Dungbomb" title="Dungbomb">Dungbomb</a> off under their great-aunt <a href="/wiki/Muriel" title="Muriel">Muriel</a>'s chair during a <a href="/wiki/Christmas" title="Christmas">Christmas</a> dinner,<sup id="cite_ref-DH_11-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH-11">&#91;11&#93;</a></sup> turning Ron's teddy bear into a giant spider after Ron accidentally broke Fred's toy broomstick,<sup id="cite_ref-COS_8-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COS-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup> giving Ron an <a href="/wiki/Acid_Pops" title="Acid Pops">Acid Pop</a> which burnt a hole in his tongue,<sup id="cite_ref-HBP_14-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP-14">&#91;14&#93;</a></sup> and nearly tricking Ron into taking an <a href="/wiki/Unbreakable_Vow" title="Unbreakable Vow">Unbreakable Vow</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP_14-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP-14">&#91;14&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<p>George was educated at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> from <a href="/wiki/1989" title="1989">1989</a>-<a href="/wiki/1996" title="1996">1996</a>, and was Sorted into <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor" title="Gryffindor">Gryffindor House</a>. He and his twin brother made very good friends with fellow Gryffindors <a href="/wiki/Lee_Jordan" title="Lee Jordan">Lee Jordan</a>, <a href="/wiki/Angelina_Johnson" title="Angelina Johnson">Angelina Johnson</a>, <a href="/wiki/Alicia_Spinnet" title="Alicia Spinnet">Alicia Spinnet</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Oliver_Wood" title="Oliver Wood">Oliver Wood</a>, and became well known for their senses of humour. During their first year at Hogwarts, George and Fred swiped the <a href="/wiki/Marauder%27s_Map" title="Marauder&#39;s Map">Marauder's Map</a> from <a href="/wiki/Argus_Filch" title="Argus Filch">Argus Filch</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Caretaker%27s_Filing_Cabinet" class="mw-redirect" title="Caretaker&#39;s Filing Cabinet">office</a>, which further aided in their mischief.<sup id="cite_ref-POA_10-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-POA-10">&#91;10&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<p>In his second year, George gained a position on the <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor_Quidditch_team" title="Gryffindor Quidditch team">Gryffindor Quidditch team</a> as a <a href="/wiki/Beater" title="Beater">Beater</a>, as did Fred. They were both extremely skilled players, described by Oliver Wood as being like "human <a href="/wiki/Bludger" title="Bludger">Bludgers</a>".<sup id="cite_ref-PS_2-14" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PS-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>It was said to <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> by their youngest brother, <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ron</a>, that Fred and George achieved "<i>really good marks</i>", at least in their early years of schooling.<sup id="cite_ref-PS_2-15" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PS-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<p>George started his third year in 1991, when his younger brother <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ron</a> began his first year, along with <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>, who soon became his best friend. The twins' jumpers had their initials on them, prompting George to quip that they knew they were "Gred and Forge".<sup id="cite_ref-PS_2-16" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PS-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> George also insisted that his older brother <a href="/wiki/Percy_Weasley" title="Percy Weasley">Percy</a> wear his jumper and sit with his brothers instead of the other <a href="/wiki/Prefect" title="Prefect">Prefects</a>, reminding him that the Christmas holidays were a time for family.<sup id="cite_ref-PS_2-17" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PS-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>

While Harry was unconscious in the hospital wing, George and Fred attempted to send Harry a toilet seat, showing their friendly side toward him.<sup id="cite_ref-PS_2-18" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PS-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>

<p>In the summer of 1992, Fred, George, and Ron rescued Harry from his <a href="/wiki/Dursley_family" title="Dursley family">relatives'</a> <a href="/wiki/4_Privet_Drive" title="4 Privet Drive">home</a> after not hearing from him during the previous summer month. They pulled the bars off his window and flew him to the Burrow in their father's <a href="/wiki/Flying_Ford_Anglia" title="Flying Ford Anglia">Ford Anglia</a>. While at the Burrow George and Fred came to know Harry better as they spent time flying with him. The twins soon began their fourth year at Hogwarts, and took to teasing their younger sister Ginny as she began her first year. They were trying to cheer her up, unaware that her emotional state was fragile due to the influence of <a href="/wiki/T._M._Riddle%27s_Diary" title="T. M. Riddle&#39;s Diary">Tom Riddle's Diary</a>.

<p>That year, the Chamber of Secrets was opened and frightening messages were written on the castle walls. Rumours were circulating that Harry was responsible for it, since he spoke <a href="/wiki/Parseltongue" title="Parseltongue">Parseltongue</a> and thus might be the <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Heir of Slytherin</a>. Neither George nor Fred took these rumours seriously, they found the whole thing laughable and frequently joked about it. They also came to <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione Granger</a>'s defence when <a href="/wiki/Draco_Malfoy" title="Draco Malfoy">Draco Malfoy</a> called her a "<i>filthy little <a href="/wiki/Mudblood" title="Mudblood">Mudblood</a></i>", lunging at him, only to be blocked by Captain <a href="/wiki/Marcus_Flint" title="Marcus Flint">Marcus Flint</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-COS_8-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COS-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup> 
</p><p><span style="font-size:14px;font-weight:normal;line-height:22px;">George helped protect Harry form a rogue Bludger during Gryffindor's opening match against Slytherin.</span><span style="font-size:14px;font-weight:normal;line-height:22px;"> </span>After Gryffindor won the Quidditch match, George went to the hospital wing with the rest of the Gryffindor Quidditch team to see Harry, because <a href="/wiki/Gilderoy_Lockhart" title="Gilderoy Lockhart">Gilderoy Lockhart</a> had removed all his bones, and congratulated him for his unbelievable flying, but not before seeing Marcus Flint yelling at Draco Malfoy for "<i>having the <a href="/wiki/Golden_Snitch" title="Golden Snitch">Snitch</a> on top of his head and not noticing</i>", of which he informed Harry.<sup id="cite_ref-COS_8-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COS-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup> At the end of the school year, George was relieved when his younger sister was saved — and delighted when she revealed that Percy had a secret girlfriend, as this was another opportunity to take the mickey out of him.<sup id="cite_ref-COS_8-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COS-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<p>Arthur Weasley had won a large amount of gold from a Ministry contest, something that was both a surprise and delight for the family. The Weasleys used the money won that summer, to take a holiday to <a href="/wiki/Egypt" title="Egypt">Egypt</a>, where the oldest son <a href="/wiki/William_Weasley" title="William Weasley">Bill</a> was working as a <a href="/wiki/Curse-Breaker" title="Curse-Breaker">Curse Breaker</a>. While there, Fred and George tried to push Percy, who had been acting even more arrogant and pompous than usual because he learned he had been appointed <a href="/wiki/Head_Boy_or_Girl" title="Head Boy or Girl">Head Boy</a>, into a pyramid. Their mother stopped them and scolded them severely. They and their brothers went in all the pyramids. They also annoyed <a href="/wiki/Ginevra_Weasley" title="Ginevra Weasley">Ginny</a> when she couldn't go in the last one, most likely because her their mother thought her too young.<sup id="cite_ref-POA_10-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-POA-10">&#91;10&#93;</a></sup>

<p>In 1993, George began his fifth year at Hogwarts. Around Christmas, he and Fred decided to give the Marauder's Map to <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> in order to help him get into <a href="/wiki/Hogsmeade" title="Hogsmeade">Hogsmeade</a> without permission, as they had already memorised it. They gambled on the outcome, and won a great deal of money because of it. However, <a href="/wiki/Ludovic_Bagman" title="Ludovic Bagman">Ludo Bagman</a> defaulted on the bet. Fred suggested threatening to inform the <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">Ministry of Magic</a> of Bagman's activities, but George was against it, arguing that it was blackmail. That year, George and Fred also started trying out the prank devices they invented, starting with the <a href="/wiki/Ton-Tongue_Toffee" title="Ton-Tongue Toffee">Ton-Tongue Toffee</a> on <a href="/wiki/Dudley_Dursley" title="Dudley Dursley">Dudley Dursley</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-POA_10-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-POA-10">&#91;10&#93;</a></sup>

<p>Once at school for their sixth year (before their sixth year they attended the Quidditch World Cup with their family), George and Fred began selling, as well as experimenting with, their inventions. That year, Hogwarts also hosted the <a href="/wiki/Triwizard_Tournament" title="Triwizard Tournament">Triwizard Tournament</a>, which Fred and George both wanted to enter, despite being underage. <p>George spent the summer of 1995 in <a href="/wiki/12_Grimmauld_Place" title="12 Grimmauld Place">12 Grimmauld Place</a>, the headquarters of the newly reconstituted <a href="/wiki/Order_of_the_Phoenix" title="Order of the Phoenix">Order of the Phoenix</a>, along with most of his family. He and Fred pocketed <a href="/wiki/Doxy" title="Doxy">Doxies</a> while cleaning up in order to use in their experiments, and spied on Order meetings using their <a href="/wiki/Extendable_Ears" title="Extendable Ears">Extendable Ears</a> invention. They had also passed their <a href="/wiki/Apparition" title="Apparition">Apparition</a> <a href="/wiki/Apparition_(class)" title="Apparition (class)">tests</a> "with distinction", and delighted in annoying other inhabitants of the house by frequently Apparating and Disapparating around the house.<sup id="cite_ref-OOP_7-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOP-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup> When <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ron</a> and <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione</a> received news that they were <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor" title="Gryffindor">Gryffindor</a>'s new <a href="/wiki/Prefect" title="Prefect">Prefects</a>, George was slightly disgruntled by their mother's overjoyed reaction, and he and Fred both taunted Ron over his accomplishment.<sup id="cite_ref-OOP_7-10" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOP-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup>

<p>Although neither George nor Fred were very concerned about completing their educations, they returned to Hogwarts for their seventh year, and continued to develop and sell their joke products. Hermione managed to stop them from testing out their products on younger students after threatening to tell their mother that they had been tricking first-years into trying out treats from the <a href="/wiki/Skiving_Snackbox" title="Skiving Snackbox">Skiving Snackboxes</a>, a threat George considered "below the belt".
</p><p>George and Fred also joined <a href="/wiki/Dumbledore%27s_Army" title="Dumbledore&#39;s Army">Dumbledore's Army</a>. They greatly disliked the new Ministry appointed <a href="/wiki/Defence_Against_the_Dark_Arts" title="Defence Against the Dark Arts">Defence Against the Dark Arts</a> professor and High Inquisitor Dolores Umbridge and wanted to oppose <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">Ministry</a> control at Hogwarts, as well as prepare to fight <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a> and his <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-OOP_7-11" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOP-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Later in the year, Umbridge kicked George, Fred, and Harry off the <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor" title="Gryffindor">Gryffindor</a> <a href="/wiki/Quidditch" title="Quidditch">Quidditch</a> <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor_Quidditch_team" title="Gryffindor Quidditch team">team</a> and gave them all lifetime bans from <a href="/wiki/Quidditch" title="Quidditch">Quidditch</a> after George and Harry got into a fight with <a href="/wiki/Draco_Malfoy" title="Draco Malfoy">Draco Malfoy</a> (although their bans were lifted immediately when Umbridge was removed from Hogwarts).<sup id="cite_ref-OOP_7-12" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOP-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup> The <a href="/wiki/Slytherin" title="Slytherin">Slytherins</a> had written the lyrics to the demeaning song <i><a href="/wiki/Weasley_is_Our_King" title="Weasley is Our King">Weasley is Our King</a></i>, intended to humiliate Ron and undermine the Gryffindor team, and then insulted George's parents and Harry's late mother. Things would get worse for George and his family around Christmas, when their father was attacked by <a href="/wiki/Nagini" title="Nagini">Nagini</a>, Voldemort's snake. The Weasleys split their Christmas holidays between Order headquarters and <a href="/wiki/St_Mungo%27s_Hospital_for_Magical_Maladies_and_Injuries" title="St Mungo&#39;s Hospital for Magical Maladies and Injuries">St Mungo's</a>, although <a href="/wiki/Percy_Weasley" title="Percy Weasley">Percy</a> did not visit and sent the jumper his mother sent him back unopened, continuing his estrangement.<sup id="cite_ref-OOP_7-13" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOP-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<p>None of this might have been possible without the funding <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> had given them in 1995.<sup id="cite_ref-GOF_17-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF-17">&#91;17&#93;</a></sup> For Harry's generosity, the twins allowed him to take any merchandise free of charge. In the summer of 1996, Harry, <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ron</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione Granger</a> visited the shop and Fred showed Harry around. He also offered Hermione a <a href="/wiki/Patented_Daydream_Charm" title="Patented Daydream Charm">Patented Daydream Charm</a> for free after she called them "extraordinary magic", but still charged Ron for his purchases. Fred and George also questioned <a href="/wiki/Ginevra_Weasley" title="Ginevra Weasley">Ginny</a> on her love life, much to her irritation.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP_14-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP-14">&#91;14&#93;</a></sup> That Christmas, the twins stayed at the<a href="/wiki/The_Burrow" title="The Burrow"> Burrow</a> for the holidays. When <a href="/wiki/Percy_Weasley" title="Percy Weasley">Percy</a> arrived with <a href="/wiki/Minister_for_Magic" title="Minister for Magic">Minister</a> <a href="/wiki/Rufus_Scrimgeour" title="Rufus Scrimgeour">Rufus Scrimgeour</a> and it became clear that the two had only come so that the Minister could question Harry. George threw mashed parsnips at his older brother, along with Fred and Ginny, causing Percy to leave.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP_14-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP-14">&#91;14&#93;</a></sup>

Fred and George had also created a <a href="/wiki/Defence_Against_the_Dark_Arts" title="Defence Against the Dark Arts">Defence Against the Dark Arts</a> line of products, such as "Shield Hats" and "Shield Cloaks" that were widely purchased by the Ministry for its staff.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP_14-10" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP-14">&#91;14&#93;</a></sup> Throughout the school year of 1996 to 1997, the twins were able to disguise products to be sent into Hogwarts by mail order (even though all their merchandise was banned by <a href="/wiki/Argus_Filch" title="Argus Filch">Argus Filch</a>) to the students. <p>As supporters of Albus Dumbledore, George and Fred both joined the <a href="/wiki/Order_of_the_Phoenix" title="Order of the Phoenix">Order of the Phoenix</a> once they were of age. They participated in the Battle of the Seven Potters, along with their father and their younger brother Ron. Fred and George took <a href="/wiki/Polyjuice_Potion" title="Polyjuice Potion">Polyjuice Potion</a> to become one of the "seven Potters". While in Privet Drive they also tried numerous times to lighten the mood. 
</p><p>Once in the air a large group of Death Eaters attacked the Order members and their charges, this led to a massive sky battle. George flew with <a href="/wiki/Remus_Lupin" title="Remus Lupin">Remus Lupin</a> on a broomstick and lost an ear due to an off-target <i><a href="/wiki/Sectumsempra" title="Sectumsempra">Sectumsempra</a></i> spell fired by <a href="/wiki/Severus_Snape" title="Severus Snape">Severus Snape</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-DH_11-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH-11">&#91;11&#93;</a></sup> There was nothing that could be done about George's ear, as it had been cursed off with Dark magic. However, he took this optimistically, and turned it into a joke, calling himself "holey" and also saying his mother would now be able to tell the difference between them.<sup id="cite_ref-DH_11-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH-11">&#91;11&#93;</a></sup> 
</p>

Fred also reminisced about his late uncle <a href="/wiki/Bilius" title="Bilius">Bilius</a>, to the entertainment of George, Ron, Harry, and Hermione.<sup id="cite_ref-DH2_18-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH2-18">&#91;18&#93;</a></sup> Discussing how Billius used to down a whole bottle of <a href="/wiki/Firewhisky" title="Firewhisky">Firewhiskey</a>, go out on to the dance floor left up his robes and pull out bunches of flowers. <p>George never truly got over Fred's death. He eventually married <a href="/wiki/Angelina_Johnson" title="Angelina Johnson">Angelina Johnson</a>, and named his first child <a href="/wiki/Fred_Weasley_II" title="Fred Weasley II">Fred</a> in loving memory of the twin brother he lost during the final battle.<sup id="cite_ref-Unwritten_9-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Unwritten-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup> The couple also had a daughter named <a href="/wiki/Roxanne_Weasley" title="Roxanne Weasley">Roxanne</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-Unwritten_9-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Unwritten-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup> George continued to run <a href="/wiki/Weasleys%27_Wizard_Wheezes" title="Weasleys&#39; Wizard Wheezes">Weasleys' Wizard Wheezes</a> to great success; his younger brother <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ron</a> retired from being an Auror after two years and helped him to turn the shop into a real money-spinner.<sup id="cite_ref-Unwritten_9-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Unwritten-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>During the <a href="/wiki/Calamity" title="Calamity">Calamity</a>, George would meet a member of the responding <a href="/wiki/Statute_of_Secrecy_Task_Force" title="Statute of Secrecy Task Force">Statute of Secrecy Task Force</a> in the Leaky Cauldron and discussed his affinity for <a href="/wiki/Pyrotechnics" title="Pyrotechnics">Pyrotechnics</a> with them, resulting in their power increasing when having cast a critical blow.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Physical_appearance">Physical appearance</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/George_Weasley?veaction=edit&amp;section=18" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Physical appearance">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/George_Weasley?action=edit&amp;section=18" title="Edit section: Physical appearance"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>George, like the other members of the <a href="/wiki/Weasley_family" title="Weasley family">Weasley family</a>, sported flaming-red hair and freckles, and was shorter than his brothers <a href="/wiki/William_Weasley" title="William Weasley">Bill</a>, <a href="/wiki/Percy_Weasley" title="Percy Weasley">Percy</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ron</a>, and somewhat stocky, like his brothers <a href="/wiki/Fred_Weasley" title="Fred Weasley">Fred</a> and <a href="/wiki/Charles_Weasley" title="Charles Weasley">Charlie</a>. However, he was still fairly tall, as he is mentioned as being taller than <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-19" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-19">&#91;19&#93;</a></sup> As a result of an injury from <a href="/wiki/Severus_Snape" title="Severus Snape">Severus Snape</a>'s <i><a href="/wiki/Sectumsempra" title="Sectumsempra">Sectumsempra</a></i> during the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_the_Seven_Potters" title="Battle of the Seven Potters">Battle of the Seven Potters</a>, George has lost one of his ears, briefly distinguishing him from his identical twin until Fred's death.<sup id="cite_ref-DH_11-10" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH-11">&#91;11&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Personality_and_traits">Personality and traits</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/George_Weasley?veaction=edit&amp;section=19" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Personality and traits">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/George_Weasley?action=edit&amp;section=19" title="Edit section: Personality and traits"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<div class="quote">

<p>George was the quieter, gentler of the twins,<sup id="cite_ref-20" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-20">&#91;20&#93;</a></sup> George's sense of humour was not harsh, and he was inclined to make fun of himself on occasion as well — even when he was injured and lost an ear.<sup id="cite_ref-DH_11-11" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH-11">&#91;11&#93;</a></sup> He also appeared to be slightly more moral, as he was against blackmailing <a href="/wiki/Ludovic_Bagman" title="Ludovic Bagman">Ludo Bagman</a> when the former <a href="/wiki/Quidditch" title="Quidditch">Quidditch</a> player defaulted on a bet at the <a href="/wiki/Quidditch_World_Cup" title="Quidditch World Cup">Quidditch World Cup</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-GOF_17-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF-17">&#91;17&#93;</a></sup> However, he still displayed a slight vindictive streak when it came to dealing with people he loathed; for example, he and his brother <a href="/wiki/Fred_Weasley" title="Fred Weasley">Fred</a> had absolutely no compunction in forcing <a href="/wiki/Graham_Montague" title="Graham Montague">Graham Montague</a> into a <a href="/wiki/Vanishing_Cabinet" title="Vanishing Cabinet">Vanishing Cabinet</a>, an act which nearly killed him, according to <a href="/wiki/Draco_Malfoy" title="Draco Malfoy">Draco Malfoy</a>. When Draco Malfoy insulted his parents and Harry's mother due to his team losing the Quidditch match, both he and Harry gave him a severe beating and was not at all remorseful of doing it.
</p>

<p>George's prankster nature was enjoyed by nearly everyone in his family, aside from his brother <a href="/wiki/Percy_Weasley" title="Percy Weasley">Percy</a> and his mother, who both considered jokes to be immature and childish. However, his pranks targeted at Percy were usually good-natured, and the only thing that got hurt was Percy's pride. He and Fred particularly enjoyed teasing Percy and their younger brother <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ron</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-COS_8-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COS-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup> while they were especially close to their sister <a href="/wiki/Ginevra_Weasley" title="Ginevra Weasley">Ginny</a>, who was similar to them in temperament.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP_14-12" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP-14">&#91;14&#93;</a></sup> However, they were protective of their siblings, and eventually extended this familial protectiveness towards Ron's friends <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> and <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione Granger</a> (who later became their in-laws).
</p><p>Both he and Fred were renowned for their senses of humour; they were able to make people laugh and relax even in very trying times. Neither George nor Fred took school very seriously, but they were both very talented wizards, and demonstrated their ingenuity in their inventions and their business sense in running a highly lucrative shop. They were also staunchly loyal to their loved ones and very brave, (as evidenced by their been sorted into <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor" title="Gryffindor">Gryffindor</a>) and unafraid to stand up against what they thought was wrong, whether it was <a href="/wiki/Dolores_Umbridge" title="Dolores Umbridge">Dolores Umbridge</a>'s tyranny at Hogwarts or <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a>.
</p>

<p>Although not a high achiever academically, George was a very clever, talented, and creative wizard. In fact, he and Fred once attempted, before being of age to go to Hogwarts, to cast an advanced spell like the <a href="/wiki/Unbreakable_Vow" title="Unbreakable Vow">Unbreakable Vow</a> with their younger sibling Ron (and would have succeeded if their father had not discovered the attempt). <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> once expressed bewilderment as to how he and Fred received only three <a href="/wiki/Ordinary_Wizarding_Level" title="Ordinary Wizarding Level">O.W.L.s</a> each, remarking that they "really know their stuff". When the twins turned seventeen, they were able to use <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magic</a> outside of Hogwarts, and took full advantage of this. They did everything by <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magic</a> and frequently <a href="/wiki/Apparition" title="Apparition">Apparated</a> around <a href="/wiki/12_Grimmauld_Place" title="12 Grimmauld Place">12 Grimmauld Place</a> and <a href="/wiki/The_Burrow" title="The Burrow">the Burrow</a>, much to their <a href="/wiki/Molly_Weasley" title="Molly Weasley">mother</a>'s irritation.<sup id="cite_ref-OOP_7-18" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOP-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

After the twins left Hogwarts, Harry lamented that he was never able to find replacements as talented as they were for the team.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP_14-13" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP-14">&#91;14&#93;</a></sup></li></ul>

<ul><li><b><a href="/wiki/Spell_creation" title="Spell creation">Spell creation</a></b>: George was capable of inventing <a href="/wiki/Spell" title="Spell">spells</a>, <a href="/wiki/Potion" title="Potion">potions</a>, and other magical products, a highly advanced and rare skill. Along with Fred, George invented a wide variety of items for their shop. Some notable items include <a href="/wiki/Extendable_Ears" title="Extendable Ears">Extendable Ears</a>, which can be used to spy on others, a series of <a href="/wiki/Skiving_Snackbox" title="Skiving Snackbox">sickness-inducing sweets</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-OOP_7-20" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOP-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup> and <a href="/wiki/Patented_Daydream_Charm" title="Patented Daydream Charm">Patented Daydream Charms</a>, which <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione Granger</a> considered to be "extraordinary magic". The twins also invented items for <a href="/wiki/Defence_Against_the_Dark_Arts" title="Defence Against the Dark Arts">Defence Against the Dark Arts</a> rather than pranks, such as <a href="/wiki/Decoy_Detonator" title="Decoy Detonator">Decoy Detonators</a> and <a href="/wiki/Shield_Hat" title="Shield Hat">Shield Hats</a>; the latter were purchased by the <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">Ministry of Magic</a> itself for its employees as a protection method during the <a href="/wiki/Second_Wizarding_War" title="Second Wizarding War">Second Wizarding War</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP_14-14" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP-14">&#91;14&#93;</a></sup></li></ul>

After Fred's death, George continued to operate the business alongside his younger brother Ron, turning it into a bigger "money-spinner".<sup id="cite_ref-HBP_14-18" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP-14">&#91;14&#93;</a></sup></li></ul>

<p><a href="/wiki/Fred_Weasley" title="Fred Weasley">Fred Weasley</a> was George’s twin, best friend, and partner in crime.They were as close as two brothers could ever be. The two would always agree with one another, and never get into any fights or arguments, (at least not real ones.) The two did just about everything together. They often finished each other’s sentences and spoke in a back and forth way that could confuse others at times. They were trouble makers, often pranking their siblings, particularly <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ron</a> and <a href="/wiki/Percy_Weasley" title="Percy Weasley">Percy</a>, and even sold pranking charms and spells in the boys bathroom. Together, Fred and George designed many ingenious pranks and shared the ambition to found a <a href="/wiki/Weasleys%27_Wizard_Wheezes" title="Weasleys&#39; Wizard Wheezes">joke shop</a> together. They were also both <a href="/wiki/Beater" title="Beater">Beaters</a> for the <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor_Quidditch_team" title="Gryffindor Quidditch team">Gryffindor Quidditch team</a> and close friends with <a href="/wiki/Lee_Jordan" title="Lee Jordan">Lee Jordan</a>.
</p><p>George was devastated by the death of his twin in the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a>, and would never truly get over this loss. He eventually named his <a href="/wiki/Fred_Weasley_II" title="Fred Weasley II">son</a> after Fred. 
</p>

<p>Like his younger siblings, George tended to look up to his older brothers <a href="/wiki/William_Weasley" title="William Weasley">Bill</a> and <a href="/wiki/Charles_Weasley" title="Charles Weasley">Charlie</a>. However, he and Fred delighted in teasing <a href="/wiki/Percy_Weasley" title="Percy Weasley">Percy</a>, who was closer to their age and rather stuffy.<sup id="cite_ref-COS_8-10" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COS-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup> George was furious with Percy when he fell out with the family over their <a href="/wiki/Arthur_Weasley" title="Arthur Weasley">father</a>’s support of <a href="/wiki/Albus_Dumbledore" title="Albus Dumbledore">Albus Dumbledore</a> and <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> instead of the <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">Ministry</a>, but the family reconciled just before the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a> began when Percy apologised for his mistakes. 
</p><p>George and Fred also enjoyed teasing their only younger brother, <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ron</a>. Ron’s phobia of spiders derives from a prank the twins played on him when he was young.In Ron's first year, George teased his younger brother by teaching him a fabricated spell, which in turn led Ron to get embarrassed in front of his friends Harry Potter and Hermione Granger.
</p>
<dl><dd>"<i>Sunshine, daisies,<br />butter mellow,<br />turn this stupid,<br />fat rat yellow."</i></dd>
<dd><i></i>—Ron Weasley casting the fake spell, taught to him by George Weasley</dd></dl>
<p>They also teased him over things such as his embarrassing crush on <a href="/wiki/Fleur_Delacour" title="Fleur Delacour">Fleur Delacour</a>. After Fred's death, Ron helped George run the joke shop for a time. George was quite close to his sister <a href="/wiki/Ginevra_Weasley" title="Ginevra Weasley">Ginny</a>, who was similar to the twins in temperament and also liked to tease Ron. They were protective of her, as well, often disapproving of her love life.
Like most members of the Weasley family, George got on well with his parents.
</p><p>Mrs Weasley was often unhappy with the twins' lack of seriousness and commitment to his learning. She was unhappy with both their earning of only three O.W.L.s and desire to own a joke shop. Mrs Weasley was very upset when she first saw George's cut ear, and thanked <a href="/wiki/Remus_Lupin" title="Remus Lupin">Remus Lupin</a>, who saved George from Death Eaters.
</p><p>Mr Weasley was more laid back than his wife. While not overly supportive of George and Fred's wrongdoing, he seemed to encourage the trying of new things. Mr Weasley was excited when informed of George's adventure rescuing Harry Potter from the Dursleys and only pretended to be strict. He was upset by George's injuries after the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_the_Seven_Potters" title="Battle of the Seven Potters">Battle of the Seven Potters</a>, but also very glad that George survived the battle.
</p><p>After the war, <a href="/wiki/Arthur_Weasley" title="Arthur Weasley">Mr</a> and <a href="/wiki/Molly_Weasley" title="Molly Weasley">Mrs Weasley</a> had two grandchildren through George and his wife, <a href="/wiki/Angelina_Johnson" title="Angelina Johnson">Angelina Johnson</a>.
</p>

<p>George first met Harry Potter in his <a href="/wiki/1991" title="1991">third year</a> at Hogwarts, when Harry was beginning his first year and became friends with <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ron</a>. The twins became friendly with him when he joined the <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor_Quidditch_team" title="Gryffindor Quidditch team">Gryffindor Quidditch team</a> as their <a href="/wiki/Seeker" title="Seeker">Seeker</a>, giving them hope to win the <a href="/wiki/House_Cup" title="House Cup">House Cup</a>.  The <a href="/wiki/1992" title="1992">following year</a>, George and Fred cheered Harry up when many of the students believed that he might be the <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Heir of Slytherin</a>, which they scoffed at, and the year after that, they gave him the Marauder's Map to help him sneak into <a href="/wiki/Hogsmeade" title="Hogsmeade">Hogsmeade</a>.
</p><p>The twins also stood up for Harry when others doubted his claim that <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a> had returned or challenged his authority in <a href="/wiki/Dumbledore%27s_Army" title="Dumbledore&#39;s Army">Dumbledore's Army</a>, and assisted him in contacting <a href="/wiki/Sirius_Black" title="Sirius Black">Sirius Black</a> when he told them and <a href="/wiki/Ginevra_Weasley" title="Ginevra Weasley">Ginny</a> of his desire to speak to his godfather. Both twins risked their lives in the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_the_Seven_Potters" title="Battle of the Seven Potters">operation</a> to remove Harry from his <a href="/wiki/4_Privet_Drive" title="4 Privet Drive">relatives’ home</a> in <a href="/wiki/1997" title="1997">1997</a>. They seemed to regard Harry almost as another younger sibling, and were inclined to both be both protective and teasing of him. Harry was very upset by George's injuries after the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_the_Seven_Potters" title="Battle of the Seven Potters">Battle of the Seven Potters</a>. He eventually became George's brother-in-law through his marriage to <a href="/wiki/Ginevra_Weasley" title="Ginevra Weasley">Ginny</a>.
</p>

<p>George became friendly with <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione Granger</a> after she became a close friend to his younger brother <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ron</a>. Although Hermione disapproved of some of the twins’ antics, since she was a <a href="/wiki/Prefect" title="Prefect">Prefect</a>, she generally got on fairly well with them. They seem to regard Hermione as somewhat of a sister due to her close relationship with Ron and the fact that she stayed at the Weasley's mutliple times during the holidays. They were outraged when <a href="/wiki/Draco_Malfoy" title="Draco Malfoy">Draco Malfoy</a> called her a “<a href="/wiki/Mudblood" title="Mudblood">Mudblood</a>” in <a href="/wiki/1992" title="1992">1992</a>, to the point of attempting to physically attack him. Hermione also warned Fred and his twin brother that their <a href="/wiki/Ageing_Potion" title="Ageing Potion">Ageing Potion</a> wouldn't trick the age line protecting the <a href="/wiki/Goblet_of_Fire" title="Goblet of Fire">Goblet of Fire</a> due to the fact that Dumbledore would have thought of a foolish ageing potion in advance. They found out she was right when they were blown backwards and grew long, white beards.
</p><p>George and Fred also tried to get Hermione to relax, joking around with her about things such as their late uncle <a href="/wiki/Bilius" title="Bilius">Bilius</a>’s penchant for making mischief at weddings. Hermione also admired the twins’ cleverness and creativity in their spell work, once remarking that they could do some "extraordinary magic", though she did not agree with how they put it to use. George also admired Hermione's magical talents and even bet on her beating Ron in a dual during training for Dumbledore's Army. Hermione was also one of the few people willing to stand up to the twins when she disagreed with them. For example, when she caught them testing sweets from the <a href="/wiki/Skiving_Snackbox" title="Skiving Snackbox">Skiving Snackbox</a> on first-year students with <a href="/wiki/Lee_Jordan" title="Lee Jordan">Lee Jordan</a>, she was furious, and told them off for what she considered taking advantage of innocent children. George and Fred initially scoffed at her concerns and at her ability to make them stop, but backed down when she threatened to tell their <a href="/wiki/Molly_Weasley" title="Molly Weasley">mother</a>. However, the twins did not seem to hold a grudge, as they continued to be friendly. Hermione would eventually become George's sister-in-law through her marriage to <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ron</a>.
</p>

<p>George was friendly with <a href="/wiki/Angelina_Johnson" title="Angelina Johnson">Angelina Johnson</a>, a <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor" title="Gryffindor">Gryffindor</a> in his year and fellow <a href="/wiki/Quidditch" title="Quidditch">Quidditch</a> player, during their school years. She attended the <a href="/wiki/Yule_Ball" title="Yule Ball">Yule Ball</a> with his twin brother Fred in <a href="/wiki/1994" title="1994">1994</a>. After Fred’s death and the end of the <a href="/wiki/Second_Wizarding_War" title="Second Wizarding War">Second Wizarding War</a>, she and George evidently grew closer, and eventually married and had two children together. One of the children was named after George's late twin.
</p>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Children">Children</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/George_Weasley?veaction=edit&amp;section=28" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Children">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/George_Weasley?action=edit&amp;section=28" title="Edit section: Children"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<p>After the <a href="/wiki/Second_Wizarding_War" title="Second Wizarding War">Second Wizarding War</a>, George married Angelina Johnson and together they had two children; <a href="/wiki/Fred_Weasley_II" title="Fred Weasley II">Fred</a>, who was named after his <a href="/wiki/Fred_Weasley" title="Fred Weasley">late twin brother</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Roxanne_Weasley" title="Roxanne Weasley">Roxanne</a>. George possibly had a good relationship with his children, and joked in front of them, as Ron did to his children.
</p>

<p>George had also several nieces and nephews through his siblings: <a href="/wiki/Victoire_Weasley" title="Victoire Weasley">Victoire</a>, <a href="/wiki/Dominique_Weasley" title="Dominique Weasley">Dominique</a> and <a href="/wiki/Louis_Weasley" title="Louis Weasley">Louis</a>, Bill and Fleur's children; <a href="/wiki/Molly_Weasley_II" title="Molly Weasley II">Molly</a> and <a href="/wiki/Lucy_Weasley" title="Lucy Weasley">Lucy</a>, Percy and his wife, Audrey's children; <a href="/wiki/Rose_Granger-Weasley" title="Rose Granger-Weasley">Rose</a> and <a href="/wiki/Hugo_Granger-Weasley" title="Hugo Granger-Weasley">Hugo</a>, Ron and Hermione's children; <a href="/wiki/James_Potter_II" title="James Potter II">James</a>, <a href="/wiki/Albus_Potter" title="Albus Potter">Albus</a> and <a href="/wiki/Lily_Potter" title="Lily Potter">Lily</a>, Ginny and Harry's children. It is supposed that George had a good relationship with his nieces and nephews, and that they liked him. James also appeared to act like George, because they both enjoyed pulling pranks and teasing their siblings.  
</p>

<p><a href="/wiki/Minerva_McGonagall" title="Minerva McGonagall">Minerva McGonagall</a> was the <a href="/wiki/Transfiguration_(class)" title="Transfiguration (class)">Transfiguration</a> teacher during George and his brother, Fred's years at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts</a>. Although McGonagall was always well known for being strict and stern, she was never shown punishing or even telling off Fred and George for their wild behaviour and many pranks. It was known however that they were good <a href="/wiki/Quidditch" title="Quidditch">Quidditch</a> players from a good family, and she was visibly displeased with having the twins and Harry kicked off the <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor" title="Gryffindor">Gryffindor</a> <a href="/wiki/Quidditch" title="Quidditch">Quidditch</a> <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor_Quidditch_team" title="Gryffindor Quidditch team">team</a>. McGonagall often perceived the twins as naughty but seemed to share their dislike of <a href="/wiki/Dolores_Umbridge" title="Dolores Umbridge">Dolores Umbridge</a>, who was the <a href="/wiki/Defence_Against_the_Dark_Arts" title="Defence Against the Dark Arts">Defence Against the Dark Arts</a> teacher for a small time. McGonagall did not attempt to remove the fireworks that surfaced around the school causing great disruptions. When George made his legendary escape in <a href="/wiki/1996" title="1996">1996</a>, McGonagall assisted <a href="/wiki/Peeves" title="Peeves">Peeves</a> to unscrew an overhead chandelier, in an obvious homage to Fred and George.
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Lee_Jordan">Lee Jordan</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/George_Weasley?veaction=edit&amp;section=31" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Lee Jordan">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/George_Weasley?action=edit&amp;section=31" title="Edit section: Lee Jordan"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>

<p><a href="/wiki/Lee_Jordan" title="Lee Jordan">Lee Jordan</a> was best friends with Fred and George during his time at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts</a>, and often got into trouble along with them. In <a href="/wiki/1995" title="1995">1995</a> he joined <a href="/wiki/Dumbledore%27s_Army" title="Dumbledore&#39;s Army">Dumbledore's Army</a> and helped the twins harass <a href="/wiki/Dolores_Umbridge" title="Dolores Umbridge">Dolores Umbridge</a>, one incident of which resulted in him being punished by "writing lines", scarring his hand. Lee Jordan also attended Fleur and Bill Weasley's wedding, and danced with <a href="/wiki/Ginevra_Weasley" title="Ginevra Weasley">Ginny Weasley</a> in 1997. On <a href="/wiki/2_May" title="2 May">2 May</a>, <a href="/wiki/1998" title="1998">1998</a>, Lee returned with Fred and George to fight in the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a>. After Fred's death, George and Lee both defeated the <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eater</a> <a href="/wiki/Corban_Yaxley" title="Corban Yaxley">Yaxley</a>.
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Hogwarts_staff">Hogwarts staff</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/George_Weasley?veaction=edit&amp;section=32" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Hogwarts staff">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/George_Weasley?action=edit&amp;section=32" title="Edit section: Hogwarts staff"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>

<p>Fred and George had a good relationship with most <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts</a> professors. Professor <a href="/wiki/Filius_Flitwick" title="Filius Flitwick">Filius Flitwick</a>, the <a href="/wiki/Charms_(class)" title="Charms (class)">Charms</a> professor, seemed to like them; he was very happy by their spell work. He also thought that they were very talented wizards, and started to support them, becoming against <a href="/wiki/Dolores_Umbridge" title="Dolores Umbridge">Dolores Umbridge</a> in <a href="/wiki/1995" title="1995">1995</a>.  When Fred and George set off fireworks around the ground she spent a day running around putting them out. After she had done Flitwick's classroom, he told her that he could have put them all out easily but was concerned he didn't have the authority to do so. After Easter break, the twins used one of their inventions, a <a href="/wiki/Portable_Swamp" title="Portable Swamp">Portable Swamp</a> to change a corridor into a swamp and then shortly afterwards they departed from school to go into the world of business. Flitwick was seen cheering and supporting them at their departure after they ran rings around Umbridge and Filch.  Flitwick was asked to remove the swamp they created, which he did but left a small patch in the corner as he believed it to be really good magic.
</p>

<p>Fred and George were friends with <a href="/wiki/Rubeus_Hagrid" title="Rubeus Hagrid">Rubeus Hagrid</a>, the <a href="/wiki/Care_of_Magical_Creatures" title="Care of Magical Creatures">Care of Magical Creatures</a> professor, during their time at Hogwarts. Hagrid seemed to be one of their favourite members of the staff, as he was not too serious, unlike many other professors. They were also among the few people who believed that Hagrid was a great person even after they learned that he was a <a href="/wiki/Half-giant" title="Half-giant">half-giant</a>. Hagrid also thought that Fred and George were trouble-makers, but very funny. He once compared them with Harry Potter's deceased father <a href="/wiki/James_Potter_I" title="James Potter I">James</a> and his best friend <a href="/wiki/Sirius_Black" title="Sirius Black">Sirius Black</a>. Furthermore, they were all among Harry Potter's closest friends. Fred and George fought in the Second Wizarding War along with Hagrid. However, Fred was killed in the Battle of Hogwarts, during an explosion. It is possible that Hagrid was very sad when he learned about that.
</p>

<p>Both Fred and George appeared to like the Headmaster <a href="/wiki/Albus_Dumbledore" title="Albus Dumbledore">Albus Dumbledore</a> and thought that he was the greatest wizard of all time. He seemed to like them, too, despite that they were trouble-makers, and he knew they were good persons. Along with his twin, George joined the <a href="/wiki/Order_of_the_Phoenix" title="Order of the Phoenix">Order of the Phoenix</a>, an organisation founded by Dumbledore, in order to oppose <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a> and his <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a> as well as <a href="/wiki/Dumbledore%27s_Army" title="Dumbledore&#39;s Army">Dumbledore's Army</a> to oppose Dolores Umbridge and the Ministry of Magic. Fred and George both showed loyalty to Dumbledore by punishing Umbridge after Umbridge became headmistress.
</p>

<p>Fred and George disliked <a href="/wiki/Severus_Snape" title="Severus Snape">Severus Snape</a> the potions master. Snape disliked them, too. This could be because of the fact that the Weasley twins were trouble-makers, like Snape's schoolboy rivals the <a href="/wiki/Marauders" title="Marauders">Marauders</a>, especially <a href="/wiki/James_Potter_I" title="James Potter I">James Potter I</a> and <a href="/wiki/Sirius_Black" title="Sirius Black">Sirius Black</a>. Snape was eventually charged with being <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eater</a>; however, he was a double agent and working for the <a href="/wiki/Order_of_the_Phoenix" title="Order of the Phoenix">Order of the Phoenix</a>. He accidentally hit George with a <a href="/wiki/Sectumsempra" title="Sectumsempra">Sectumsempra</a>, while aiming at a <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eater</a> during the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_the_Seven_Potters" title="Battle of the Seven Potters">Battle of the Seven Potters</a>. George possibly learned that Snape was on their side during the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a>.

Fred and George also "nicked" the <a href="/wiki/Marauder%27s_Map" title="Marauder&#39;s Map">Marauder's Map</a> from Filch, while they were in his office. Filch also had a great knowledge of all Hogwarts' secret passages, only less than Fred and George's. When <a href="/wiki/Dolores_Umbridge" title="Dolores Umbridge">Dolores Umbridge</a> was appointed at Hogwarts, Filch supported her, and tried to punish the twins again.
</p>
<figure class="thumb tleft show-info-icon" style="width: 147px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/6/68/Dolores_Umbridge_Deathly_Hallows_promotional_image.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20101127041001" class="image"><img alt="" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw%3D%3D" decoding="async" width="145" height="191" class="thumbimage lazyload" data-image-name="Dolores Umbridge Deathly Hallows promotional image.jpg" data-image-key="Dolores_Umbridge_Deathly_Hallows_promotional_image.jpg" data-src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/6/68/Dolores_Umbridge_Deathly_Hallows_promotional_image.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/145?cb=20101127041001" /></a> 	<noscript><a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/6/68/Dolores_Umbridge_Deathly_Hallows_promotional_image.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20101127041001" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/6/68/Dolores_Umbridge_Deathly_Hallows_promotional_image.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/145?cb=20101127041001" decoding="async" width="145" height="191" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="Dolores Umbridge Deathly Hallows promotional image.jpg" data-image-key="Dolores_Umbridge_Deathly_Hallows_promotional_image.jpg" data-src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/6/68/Dolores_Umbridge_Deathly_Hallows_promotional_image.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/145?cb=20101127041001" /></a></noscript> 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:Dolores_Umbridge_Deathly_Hallows_promotional_image.jpg" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption">Dolores Umbridge, his previous DADA professor and enemy</p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure>
<p>Fred and George hated <a href="/wiki/Dolores_Umbridge" title="Dolores Umbridge">Dolores Umbridge</a>, who was appointed by the <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">Ministry of Magic</a> as the <a href="/wiki/Defence_Against_the_Dark_Arts" title="Defence Against the Dark Arts">Defence Against the Dark Arts</a> professor in <a href="/wiki/1995" title="1995">1995</a>. Umbridge was a sadistic woman who enjoyed punishing and torturing students. Almost every resident of Hogwarts at the time hated Umbridge, but she particularly hated <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> and Fred and George. When George and Harry attacked <a href="/wiki/Draco_Malfoy" title="Draco Malfoy">Draco Malfoy</a>, Umbridge kicked them off the team, along with Fred (although their bans were lifted immediately following Umbridge's removal from the school). Fred and George were the ones who opposed Umbridge. They also created a <a href="/wiki/Portable_Swamp" title="Portable Swamp">Portable Swamp</a>, in order to anger Umbridge. When she attempted to punish them, Fred and George insulted her in front of the whole school and then fled with their broomsticks. After the war, Umbridge was imprisoned in <a href="/wiki/Azkaban" title="Azkaban">Azkaban</a>, for her crimes against <a href="/wiki/Muggle-born" title="Muggle-born">muggle-borns</a>.
</p>
<h3><span id="Dumbledore's_Army"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="Dumbledore.27s_Army">Dumbledore's Army</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/George_Weasley?veaction=edit&amp;section=33" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Dumbledore&#039;s Army">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/George_Weasley?action=edit&amp;section=33" title="Edit section: Dumbledore&#039;s Army"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<figure class="thumb tright show-info-icon" style="width: 235px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/9/96/Dumbledore%27s_Army.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20161123060553" class="image"><img alt="" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw%3D%3D" decoding="async" width="233" height="155" class="thumbimage lazyload" data-image-name="Dumbledore&#39;s Army.jpg" data-image-key="Dumbledore&#39;s_Army.jpg" data-src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/9/96/Dumbledore%27s_Army.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/233?cb=20161123060553" /></a> 	<noscript><a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/9/96/Dumbledore%27s_Army.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20161123060553" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/9/96/Dumbledore%27s_Army.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/233?cb=20161123060553" decoding="async" width="233" height="155" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="Dumbledore&#39;s Army.jpg" data-image-key="Dumbledore&#39;s_Army.jpg" data-src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/9/96/Dumbledore%27s_Army.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/233?cb=20161123060553" /></a></noscript> 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:Dumbledore%27s_Army.jpg" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption"><a href="/wiki/Dumbledore%27s_Army" title="Dumbledore&#39;s Army">Dumbledore's Army</a>, his alias</p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure>
<p>In <a href="/wiki/1995" title="1995">1995</a>, George and Fred joined <a href="/wiki/Dumbledore%27s_Army" title="Dumbledore&#39;s Army">Dumbledore's Army</a>, an organisation founded and led by <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>, in order to oppose <a href="/wiki/Dolores_Umbridge" title="Dolores Umbridge">Dolores Umbridge</a> and teach <a href="/wiki/Defence_Against_the_Dark_Arts" title="Defence Against the Dark Arts">Defence Against the Dark Arts</a> to the other students. The twins knew most of them, and they had a good relationship with most members of the army, including Harry Potter, Hermione Granger, Ron Weasley, Ginny Weasley, Lee Jordan and Angelina Johnson.
</p><p>Some members of the army were also members of the <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor_Quidditch_team" title="Gryffindor Quidditch team">Gryffindor Quidditch team</a>, including <a href="/wiki/Alicia_Spinnet" title="Alicia Spinnet">Alicia Spinnet</a> and <a href="/wiki/Katie_Bell" title="Katie Bell">Katie Bell</a>. The girls appeared to be close with George, whom they possibly knew since their first year, as well as his brother, Fred. Both of them were very upset when Umbridge kicked Harry and the twins off the team. George met them again when they fought at the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a> in <a href="/wiki/1998" title="1998">1998</a> and they possibly survived the war.
</p><p>Other members of the army were <a href="/wiki/Neville_Longbottom" title="Neville Longbottom">Neville Longbottom</a>, <a href="/wiki/Luna_Lovegood" title="Luna Lovegood">Luna Lovegood</a>, <a href="/wiki/Dean_Thomas" title="Dean Thomas">Dean Thomas</a>, <a href="/wiki/Seamus_Finnigan" title="Seamus Finnigan">Seamus Finnigan</a>, <a href="/wiki/Lavender_Brown" title="Lavender Brown">Lavender Brown</a>, <a href="/wiki/Padma_and_Parvati_Patil" title="Padma and Parvati Patil">Parvati and Padma Patil</a>, <a href="/wiki/Colin_and_Dennis_Creevey" title="Colin and Dennis Creevey">Colin and Dennis Creevey</a>, <a href="/wiki/Ernest_Macmillan" title="Ernest Macmillan">Ernie Macmillan</a>, <a href="/wiki/Hannah_Abbott" title="Hannah Abbott">Hannah Abbott</a>, <a href="/wiki/Justin_Finch-Fletchley" title="Justin Finch-Fletchley">Justin Finch-Fletchley</a>, <a href="/wiki/Susan_Bones" title="Susan Bones">Susan Bones</a>, <a href="/wiki/Cho_Chang" title="Cho Chang">Cho Chang</a>, <a href="/wiki/Anthony_Goldstein" title="Anthony Goldstein">Anthony Goldstein</a>, <a href="/wiki/Michael_Corner" title="Michael Corner">Michael Corner</a>, <a href="/wiki/Terry_Boot" title="Terry Boot">Terry Boot</a>, <a href="/wiki/Marietta_Edgecombe" title="Marietta Edgecombe">Marietta Edgecombe</a> and <a href="/wiki/Zacharias_Smith" title="Zacharias Smith">Zacharias Smith</a>. George was friendly with them, although not very close; however, he disliked Zacharias Smith, who didn't believe that Voldemort had returned, and did not fight at the Battle of Hogwarts, as did Marietta Edgecombe, who betrayed the army to Umbridge. However, the rest of them fought for Dumbledore's Army, and at least one of them, Colin Creevey, was killed by Death Eaters.
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="The_Marauders">The Marauders</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/George_Weasley?veaction=edit&amp;section=34" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: The Marauders">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/George_Weasley?action=edit&amp;section=34" title="Edit section: The Marauders"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<div class="quote">

<dd></dd></dl></div>
<figure class="thumb tright show-info-icon" style="width: 222px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/c/cc/Marauders_1997.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20080414050923" class="image"><img alt="" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw%3D%3D" decoding="async" width="220" height="174" class="thumbimage lazyload" data-image-name="Marauders 1997.jpg" data-image-key="Marauders_1997.jpg" data-src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/c/cc/Marauders_1997.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/220?cb=20080414050923" /></a> 	<noscript><a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/c/cc/Marauders_1997.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20080414050923" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/c/cc/Marauders_1997.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/220?cb=20080414050923" decoding="async" width="220" height="174" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="Marauders 1997.jpg" data-image-key="Marauders_1997.jpg" data-src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/c/cc/Marauders_1997.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/220?cb=20080414050923" /></a></noscript> 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:Marauders_1997.jpg" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption">James Potter, Sirius Black, Remus Lupin, and Peter Pettigrew</p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure>
<p>George and his brother <a href="/wiki/Fred_Weasley" title="Fred Weasley">Fred</a> regarded the <a href="/wiki/Marauders" title="Marauders">Marauders</a> with a kind of idolatry and they believed that they owed a lot to them, as their invention, the <a href="/wiki/Marauder%27s_Map" title="Marauder&#39;s Map">Marauder's Map</a>, helped them to achieve a lot of their aims. Although they owned the map for many years, they did not refer to them as the Marauders, but instead they called them by their nicknames: <a href="/wiki/Remus_Lupin" title="Remus Lupin">Moony</a>, <a href="/wiki/Peter_Pettigrew" title="Peter Pettigrew">Wormtail</a>, <a href="/wiki/Sirius_Black" title="Sirius Black">Padfoot</a>, and <a href="/wiki/James_Potter_I" title="James Potter I">Prongs</a>. 
</p><p>While they were in possession of the map, the twins did not, however, work out that two of the Marauders were two of their friends in the <a href="/wiki/Order_of_the_Phoenix" title="Order of the Phoenix">Order of the Phoenix</a>: <a href="/wiki/Remus_Lupin" title="Remus Lupin">Remus Lupin</a> and <a href="/wiki/Sirius_Black" title="Sirius Black">Sirius Black</a>. Neither were they aware that Prongs was in fact <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry</a>'s father, <a href="/wiki/James_Potter_I" title="James Potter I">James</a>, and that Wormtail was actually <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eater</a> <a href="/wiki/Peter_Pettigrew" title="Peter Pettigrew">Peter Pettigrew</a>.
</p><p>He and Fred were both friends of Sirius Black after they both learnt of his innocence. Sirius was friendly with them, but he argued with the twins (for example: when he told them to wait at home while their father was in danger). Sirius was killed by <a href="/wiki/Bellatrix_Lestrange" title="Bellatrix Lestrange">Bellatrix Lestrange</a> during the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_the_Department_of_Mysteries" title="Battle of the Department of Mysteries">Battle of the Department of Mysteries</a> in <a href="/wiki/1996" title="1996">1996</a>. The twins were saddened by his death, but tried not to remind Harry about it.
</p><p>They were also friends of Remus Lupin, whom they first met in <a href="/wiki/1993" title="1993">1993</a>, when he was <a href="/wiki/Defence_Against_the_Dark_Arts" title="Defence Against the Dark Arts">Defence Against the Dark Arts</a> professor. They considered him among the best teachers of this lesson they had ever had. Lupin fought with the twins in many battles of the Second War, and was killed in the final battle, along with Fred. Their bodies were put near each other's in the <a href="/wiki/Great_Hall" title="Great Hall">Great Hall</a>.
</p><p>It is unknown if the twins ever learnt the true identities of the Marauders, although it is possible they did eventually learn the truth after Voldemort's return considering that Sirius mentioned Pettigrew's nickname Wormtail in front of them while explaining Voldemort's intentions to them without them questioning that. It is also unknown if Lupin and Sirius ever learnt that the twins both had the Marauders Map in their possession during their time in Hogwarts.
</p>

<p>George also became a member of the Order of the Phoenix during the <a href="/wiki/Second_Wizarding_War" title="Second Wizarding War">Second Wizarding War</a>. The Order was an organisation led by <a href="/wiki/Albus_Dumbledore" title="Albus Dumbledore">Albus Dumbledore</a> in order to fight <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a> and his <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a>. George had a good relationship with most members of the order. Some notable relationships include Remus Lupin, Sirius Black, his other family members (excluding Percy who had fallen out with the other Weasleys, and Ginny who was underage) and some of the Hogwarts professors.
</p><p>Another member of the Order that they knew was <a href="/wiki/Nymphadora_Tonks" title="Nymphadora Tonks">Nymphadora Tonks</a>. Tonks and the twins were close in age, and they were friendly. They fought together in the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_the_Seven_Potters" title="Battle of the Seven Potters">Battle of the Seven Potters</a> and the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a>.Tonks was killed during the final battle, too. Her body was put near Fred's, as well as Lupin's, after their death.
</p><p>The twins also knew <a href="/wiki/Alastor_Moody" title="Alastor Moody">Alastor Moody</a> and <a href="/wiki/Kingsley_Shacklebolt" title="Kingsley Shacklebolt">Kingsley Shacklebolt</a>. They appeared to have a good relationship, although they were not very close. When Moody was killed by Voldemort, everyone was sad. Before the twins learned about it, they were joking with each other. They immediately stopped talking once they heard Moody died, and their expressions immediately switched to sadness. Kingsley survived the war and became Minister for Magic.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/George_Weasley?veaction=edit&amp;section=37" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/George_Weasley?action=edit&amp;section=37" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p><i><a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/George_(given_name)" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:George (given name)">George</a></i> is a popular male given name derived from the Greek word γεωργος (<i>georgos</i>), meaning "farmer" or "earthworker". <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Saint_George" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Saint George">Saint George</a> was a third-century martyr who became a patron saint of England after the Crusades. Six kings of Britain have borne the name George. Other famous bearers include the composer George Frideric Händel, first American president George Washington, two kings of Greece, and explorer George Vancouver. It was also the pen name of English writers George Eliot and George Orwell.<sup id="cite_ref-25" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-25">&#91;25&#93;</a></sup>  <a href="/wiki/J._K._Rowling" title="J. K. Rowling">J. K. Rowling</a> has stated that she chose the name for liking and and finding it fitting with the "old-fashioned" theme of the other Weasley brothers.<sup id="cite_ref-26" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-26">&#91;26&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<ul><li>George is portrayed by <a href="/wiki/Great_Britain" title="Great Britain">British</a> actor <a href="/wiki/James_and_Oliver_Phelps" title="James and Oliver Phelps">Oliver Phelps</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_(film_series)" title="Harry Potter (film series)">film adaptations</a> of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Philosopher%27s_Stone_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Philosopher&#39;s Stone (film)">Harry Potter and the Philosopher's Stone</a></i>, <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Chamber_of_Secrets_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets (film)">Chamber of Secrets</a></i>, <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Prisoner_of_Azkaban_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban (film)">Prisoner of Azkaban</a></i>, <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Goblet_of_Fire_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire (film)">Goblet of Fire</a></i>, <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Order_of_the_Phoenix_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix (film)">Order of the Phoenix</a></i>, <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Half-Blood_Prince_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince (film)">Half-Blood Prince</a></i> and <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Deathly_Hallows:_Part_1" title="Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 1">Deathly Hallows: Part 1</a></i> and <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Deathly_Hallows:_Part_2" title="Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 2">Part 2</a></i> and so is one of the 13 characters to appear in all 8 film adaptations.</li>
<li>There has been some suggestion that George and Fred are named after their maternal uncles, <a href="/wiki/Fabian_Prewett" title="Fabian Prewett">Fabian</a> and <a href="/wiki/Gideon_Prewett" title="Gideon Prewett">Gideon Prewett</a>, who were killed in the <a href="/wiki/First_Wizarding_War" title="First Wizarding War">First Wizarding War</a>, and who share George and Fred's first initials.</li>
<li>It is possible that he and Fred were the basis for Kaoru and Hikaru Hitachiin, a pair of mischievous, red-haired twins from the shojo manga, "Ouran Highschool Host Club."</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Daniel_Radcliffe" title="Daniel Radcliffe">Daniel Radcliffe</a> plays George in the "Seven Potters" scene.</li>
<li>George and <a href="/wiki/Fred_Weasley" title="Fred Weasley">Fred</a> earned fewer <a href="/wiki/Ordinary_Wizarding_Level" title="Ordinary Wizarding Level">O.W.Ls</a> combined than each of the rest of their brothers separately.</li>
<li>George and Fred were the only sons of Arthur and Molly Weasley who did not become <a href="/wiki/Prefect" title="Prefect">prefects</a> during their education at Hogwarts.</li>
<li>In the novels, <a href="/wiki/J._K._Rowling" title="J. K. Rowling">J. K. Rowling</a> described the twins as "shorter and stockier" and Percy and Ron as "long and lanky", but in the film, Fred and George were a lot taller and lankier than Percy and Ron.</li>
<li>George Weasley's O.W.L. results have not been specifically stated by name; he had mentioned in <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Order_of_the_Phoenix" title="Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix">Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix</a></i> that he had an inspected <a href="/wiki/Charms_(class)" title="Charms (class)">Charms</a> lesson, and that he and his brother Fred were going to sell extendable ears before <a href="/wiki/Herbology" title="Herbology">Herbology</a>. <a href="/wiki/Lee_Jordan" title="Lee Jordan">Lee Jordan</a> also mentioned that they played Exploding Snap in <a href="/wiki/Defence_Against_the_Dark_Arts" title="Defence Against the Dark Arts">Defence Against the Dark Arts</a>. Since they only passed three subjects, these are most likely the subjects they passed (although it is also possible that the twins chose different subjects to take beyond OWL level and purposely failed other courses to free up their schedules).</li>
<li>Rowling has said that George and Fred are among her favourite characters.<sup id="cite_ref-27" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-27">&#91;27&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li>Unlike <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ron</a>, his younger brother, George never got mad at <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> for dating and romancing with George's sister, <a href="/wiki/Ginevra_Weasley" title="Ginevra Weasley">Ginny</a>. However, George was protective of Ginny.</li>
<li>The Weasley twins were born April Fools' Day - April 1. It's traditional to play a prank on someone on this day, which seems to be a particular hobby of Fred and George's.</li>
<li>In <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Deathly_Hallows:_Part_1" title="Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 1">Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 1</a></i> it is George who walks in on Harry and Ginny kissing in the kitchen on Harry's birthday, instead of Ron bursting in on them in Ginny's bedroom.</li>
<li>It is interesting to note that the only times Fred or George have been injured were when they were apart. They were split up at the time of Fred's death and when George lost his ear.</li>
<li>Unsurprisingly, losing his ear barely affected George at all, joking only minutes after having it cut off. By the morning he's even using his ear as a place to hold things.</li></ul>
