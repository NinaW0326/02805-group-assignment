<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Known_action_figures">Known action figures</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Action_figure?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Known action figures">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Action_figure?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Known action figures"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li><a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ronald Weasley</a> bought an <a href="/wiki/Miniature_figurine_of_Viktor_Krum" title="Miniature figurine of Viktor Krum">action figure</a> of <a href="/wiki/Viktor_Krum" title="Viktor Krum">Viktor Krum</a>, the famous <a href="/wiki/Bulgaria" title="Bulgaria">Bulgarian</a> <a href="/wiki/Seeker" title="Seeker">Seeker</a>, during the <a href="/wiki/1994" title="1994">1994</a> <a href="/wiki/Quidditch_World_Cup" title="Quidditch World Cup">Quidditch World Cup</a>. It walked around and scowled and could even do Viktor's real posture and way of walking. After Viktor Krum took <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione</a> to the <a href="/wiki/Yule_Ball" title="Yule Ball">Yule Ball</a>, Harry found the arm of the model lying on the floor of the boys' dormitory. Ron had probably broken it, because he was jealous of Hermione going to the Yule Ball with Viktor as his dance partner, so he broke it out of frustration and anger towards Krum.<sup id="cite_ref-GoF_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GoF-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li>The <a href="/wiki/Triwizard_Tournament" title="Triwizard Tournament">Triwizard</a> champions drew four <a href="/wiki/Model_dragon" title="Model dragon">miniature models</a> of <a href="/wiki/Dragon" title="Dragon">dragons</a> to determine which real dragons each of them would face in the First Task.<sup id="cite_ref-GoF_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GoF-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li>In <a href="/wiki/1996" title="1996">1996</a> <a href="/wiki/Molly_Weasley" title="Molly Weasley">Molly Weasley</a> made a <a href="/wiki/Christmas_cake" title="Christmas cake">Christmas cake</a> which had a moving <a href="/wiki/Penguin" title="Penguin">penguin</a> on it.<sup id="cite_ref-OotP_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OotP-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li>In 1996 during <a href="/wiki/Christmas" title="Christmas">Christmas</a> at <a href="/wiki/12_Grimmauld_Place" title="12 Grimmauld Place">12 Grimmauld Place</a> a <a href="/wiki/Father_Christmas" title="Father Christmas">Father Christmas</a> figure was flying on a <a href="/wiki/Broomstick" title="Broomstick">broomstick</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-ootpf_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-ootpf-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li>In 1996, a small <a href="/wiki/Model_dragon" title="Model dragon">model</a> <a href="/wiki/Dragon" title="Dragon">dragon</a> was seen <a href="/wiki/Fire" title="Fire">roasting</a> nuts in the <a href="/wiki/Weasleys%27_Dragon_Roasted_Nuts" title="Weasleys&#39; Dragon Roasted Nuts">roast chestnuts sale</a> machine in <a href="/wiki/Diagon_Alley" title="Diagon Alley">Diagon Alley</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li>In <a href="/wiki/1997" title="1997">1997</a>, <a href="/wiki/William_Weasley" title="William Weasley">Bill Weasley</a> and <a href="/wiki/Fleur_Delacour" title="Fleur Delacour">Fleur Delacour</a>'s wedding cake was topped with figures of <a href="/wiki/Phoenix" title="Phoenix">phoenixes</a> that took flight when the cake was cut.<sup id="cite_ref-DH_5-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Xenophilius_Lovegood" title="Xenophilius Lovegood">Xenophilius Lovegood</a> and <a href="/wiki/Luna_Lovegood" title="Luna Lovegood">Luna Lovegood</a> made models of <a href="/wiki/Creature" title="Creature">creatures</a> with flapping wings and snapping jaws. These figures were hung from the ceiling of their <a href="/wiki/Lovegood_House" title="Lovegood House">home</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-DH_5-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup></li></ul>
