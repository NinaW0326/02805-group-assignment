<b>Dave Monk</b> was a reporter for <i><a href="/wiki/Metro" title="Metro">Metro</a></i>, a <a href="/wiki/Great_Britain" title="Great Britain">British</a> <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggle</a> newspaper. He wrote the front-page article of the <a href="/wiki/12_August" title="12 August">12 August</a>, <a href="/wiki/1995" title="1995">1995</a> issue, about the birth and recovery of his two sons, <a href="/wiki/Tom_Monk" title="Tom Monk">Tom</a> and <a href="/wiki/Jack_Monk" title="Jack Monk">Jack</a>, who were delivered prematurely.<sup id="cite_ref-HPLE_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HPLE-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Dave_Monk?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Dave_Monk?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>David Monk is the deputy editor of the British newspaper <i><a href="/wiki/Metro" title="Metro">Metro</a></i> in real life.<sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> He wrote a blog post explaining that newspapers are sometimes asked to create mock front pages to be used in film and television productions. A preliminary front-page mock-up featuring a fake article was given to <i>Metro</i> by the <i>Harry Potter</i> production team. Monk tweaked the design so that it looked more like a genuine issue of <i>Metro</i>, and added his own name to the mock article's byline, as the original author had not included one. He does not have a wife named Tilly or twin sons named Jack and Tom.<sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup></li></ul>
