<div class="mw-parser-output"><p>The <b>Hogwarts Express driver</b> was the individual who drives the <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Express" title="Hogwarts Express">Hogwarts Express</a> from <a href="/wiki/Platform_Nine_and_Three-Quarters" title="Platform Nine and Three-Quarters">Platform Nine and Three-Quarters</a> to the <a href="/wiki/Hogsmeade_station" title="Hogsmeade station">Hogsmeade station</a>.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="History">History</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Express_driver?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: History">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Express_driver?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: History"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>In <a href="/wiki/1991" title="1991">1991</a>, <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione Granger</a> once asked the Hogwarts Express driver when they would be arriving at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts</a>. The driver claimed that they were nearly there.<sup id="cite_ref-PS6_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PS6-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>In <a href="/wiki/1993" title="1993">1993</a>, the Hogwarts Express <a href="/wiki/Trolley_witch" title="Trolley witch">trolley witch</a> assured the <a href="/wiki/Trio" title="Trio">trio</a> that she would be up with the Hogwarts Express driver if <a href="/wiki/Remus_Lupin" title="Remus Lupin">Remus Lupin</a> woke and was hungry. On the same day, Hermione went to ask the driver why the train had stopped when <a href="/wiki/Dementor" title="Dementor">dementors</a> were searching the train for <a href="/wiki/Sirius_Black" title="Sirius Black">Sirius Black</a>, and Remus Lupin also went to speak to the same individual.<sup id="cite_ref-POA5_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-POA5-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
