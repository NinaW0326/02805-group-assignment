<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Poliakoff?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Poliakoff?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>In <a href="/wiki/1994" title="1994">1994</a>, he was a member of the Durmstrang delegation that travelled to <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> to compete in the <a href="/wiki/Triwizard_Tournament" title="Triwizard Tournament">Triwizard Tournament</a>. He entered his name into the <a href="/wiki/Goblet_of_Fire" title="Goblet of Fire">Goblet of Fire</a> to compete, but <a href="/wiki/Viktor_Krum" title="Viktor Krum">Viktor Krum</a> was selected as the Champion instead. He is shown to be a sloppy eater as <a href="/wiki/Igor_Karkaroff" title="Igor Karkaroff">Igor Karkaroff</a> commented on the food stains on his robes after the welcoming feast in 1994. He hinted to Igor that he wanted <a href="/wiki/Wine" title="Wine">wine</a> once at the feast, but Karkaroff refused, as he had offered it to Viktor, not to Poliakoff.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> Karkaroff was not kind to Poliakoff, snapping at his sloppy manners and interrupting for the wine. Upon seeing Harry Potter for the first time, Poliakoff was dumbfounded, pointing openly at Harry's forehead to a <a href="/wiki/Durmstrang_girl" title="Durmstrang girl">girl</a> next to him.
</p><p>He left at the end of the year, in which he, along with his schoolmates, showed little care for Karkaroff's cowardly departure from the <a href="/wiki/Return_of_Lord_Voldemort" title="Return of Lord Voldemort">return of Lord Voldemort</a>, as Karkaroff did force his students to steer the <a href="/wiki/Durmstrang_Ship" title="Durmstrang Ship">Durmstrang Ship</a> while he remained in the cabin on their journey to Hogwarts.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Personality_and_traits">Personality and traits</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Poliakoff?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Personality and traits">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Poliakoff?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Personality and traits"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Poliakoff possesses poor manners, being a sloppy eater who dribbled food down his robes despite being in the company of two foreign schools, while gluttonously intervened Karkaroff's offer of wine for Krum. When noticing the famous Harry Potter, Poliakoff rather tactlessly pointed at his forehead to a girl next to him.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Relationships">Relationships</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Poliakoff?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Relationships">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Poliakoff?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Relationships"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Igor_Karkaroff">Igor Karkaroff</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Poliakoff?veaction=edit&amp;section=4" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Igor Karkaroff">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Poliakoff?action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Igor Karkaroff"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Poliakoff appeared not to have a very good relationship with his headmaster, as he was called a <i>"disgusting boy"</i> because of his messy uniform and was declined when he requested wine from Karkaroff.
</p><p>As with the rest of the students who visited Hogwarts, Poliakoff did not miss his headmaster too much when he fled after the <a href="/wiki/Dark_Mark" title="Dark Mark">Dark Mark</a> signalled the <a href="/wiki/Return_of_Lord_Voldemort" title="Return of Lord Voldemort">return of Lord Voldemort</a>, as Igor treated them like servants during their journey.
</p>
