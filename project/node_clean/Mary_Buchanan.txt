<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Mary_Buchanan?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Mary_Buchanan?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Mrs Buchanan married into the <a href="/wiki/Buchanan_family" title="Buchanan family">Buchanan family</a>, an old wizarding clan who lived in a village in the <a href="/wiki/Scottish_Borders" title="Scottish Borders">Scottish Borders</a> and had a reputation for being large, aggressive, and alcoholic. She and <a href="/wiki/Angus_Buchanan%27s_father" class="mw-redirect" title="Angus Buchanan&#39;s father">her husband</a> had eleven children. As a result, their home was noisy and chaotic, and Mrs Buchanan often had her hands full trying to keep things in order.
</p><p>Despite the fact that her husband held strong anti-<a href="/wiki/Squib" title="Squib">Squib</a> views, neither Mrs Buchanan nor her husband noticed when their third-born son, <a href="/wiki/Angus_Buchanan" title="Angus Buchanan">Angus</a>, failed to show evidence of magical ability like all of his brothers and sisters. Angus, being the kindest and largest of the Buchanan children, was beloved by his siblings. The children thus conspired to hide the fact that Angus was a Squib from their parents. <a href="/wiki/Flora_Buchanan" title="Flora Buchanan">Flora</a> forged a <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts</a> <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_acceptance_letter" title="Hogwarts acceptance letter">acceptance letter</a> for her younger brother, and, on <a href="/wiki/1_September" title="1 September">1 September</a>, <a href="/wiki/1858" class="mw-redirect" title="1858">1858</a>, the day that eleven-year-old Angus was due to begin his <a href="/wiki/First_year" title="First year">first year</a> at Hogwarts, his older brother <a href="/wiki/Hamish_Buchanan" title="Hamish Buchanan">Hamish</a> flew him to <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Castle" title="Hogwarts Castle">Hogwarts Castle</a> on his <a href="/wiki/Hamish_Buchanan%27s_broomstick" title="Hamish Buchanan&#39;s broomstick">broomstick</a>.
</p><p>The deception was revealed when the <a href="/wiki/Sorting_Hat" title="Sorting Hat">Sorting Hat</a> politely declined to sort Angus. News soon reached Mrs Buchanan and her husband by <a href="/wiki/Owl" title="Owl">owl</a>. Humiliated, Mr Buchanan disowned the boy, chasing him away with <a href="/wiki/Curse" title="Curse">curses</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-Pottermore_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Pottermore-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
