<p>This <b>student</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch or wizard</a> who attended <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> between <a href="/wiki/1984" title="1984">1984</a> and <a href="/wiki/1991" title="1991">1991</a>. Their brother <a href="/wiki/Jacob_(Hogwarts_student)" title="Jacob (Hogwarts student)">Jacob</a>'s expulsion from Hogwarts for endangering fellow students in his quest to find the <a href="/wiki/Cursed_Vaults" title="Cursed Vaults">Cursed Vaults</a> had an impact on his younger sibling's <a href="/wiki/First_year" title="First year">first year</a> at Hogwarts when many other students whispered about them for their elder sibling's breaking of school rules which had disgraced their shared schoolhouse. The incident was well-covered in the <i><a href="/wiki/Daily_Prophet" title="Daily Prophet">Daily Prophet</a></i>, as was Jacob's running away from home, which had a profound effect on the younger sibling.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Jacob%27s_sibling?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Jacob%27s_sibling?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span id="Early_life_(Before_1983)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_life_.28Before_1983.29">Early life <span class="template-c">(Before <a href="/wiki/1983" title="1983">1983</a>)</span></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Jacob%27s_sibling?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Early life (Before 1983)">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Jacob%27s_sibling?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Early life (Before 1983)"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>They were born in <a href="/wiki/1972" title="1972">1972</a> or <a href="/wiki/1973" title="1973">1973</a> to <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">magical parents</a>. They had at least one older sibling, <a href="/wiki/Jacob_(Hogwarts_student)" title="Jacob (Hogwarts student)">Jacob</a>.
</p><p>They apparently lived in close proximity to <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggle</a> dwellings,<sup id="cite_ref-14" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-14">&#91;14&#93;</a></sup>  but they showed little knowledge of muggle life as an adolescent and must have had limited interaction with their neighbours.<sup id="cite_ref-15" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-15">&#91;15&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>When they were five or six years, they and Jacob pretended to duel with <a href="/wiki/Liquorice_Wand" title="Liquorice Wand">Liquorice Wands</a>. At one point, Jacob convinced his sibling to steal <a href="/wiki/Sickle" title="Sickle">Sickles</a> from their mother's coat pockets.
</p><p>During their childhood, their parents worked together to teach Jacob and his sibling that they could not go around flaunting their <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magic</a>. In particular, their <a href="/wiki/Jacob%27s_mother" title="Jacob&#39;s mother">mother</a> was strict about the use of magic outside of school, refusing both of her children to ride a <a href="/wiki/Broomstick" title="Broomstick">broomstick</a> before they began attending school.
</p><p>Jacob began attending Hogwarts during their childhood. Tragedy struck the family when they received an <a href="/wiki/Owl" title="Owl">owl</a> from <a href="/wiki/Professor" title="Professor">Professor</a> <a href="/wiki/Albus_Dumbledore" title="Albus Dumbledore">Albus Dumbledore</a>, informing them that Jacob had been <a href="/wiki/Discipline_at_Hogwarts#Expulsion" title="Discipline at Hogwarts">expelled</a> for endangering his fellow students in his attempt to prove that the fabled <a href="/wiki/Cursed_Vaults" title="Cursed Vaults">Cursed Vaults</a> really existed.
</p><p>Shortly after being sent home, Jacob ran away and went missing; many believed that he went on to serve <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a>, whereas other rumours said he had returned to Hogwarts after his expulsion to resume his search. His younger sibling would, years later, reject the former possibility. When his sibling and mother heard he went missing, they were upset over the news and cried.
</p>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Meeting_Rowan_Khanna">Meeting Rowan Khanna</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Jacob%27s_sibling?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Meeting Rowan Khanna">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Jacob%27s_sibling?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Meeting Rowan Khanna"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<p>Shortly after receiving the <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_acceptance_letter" title="Hogwarts acceptance letter">Hogwarts acceptance letter</a>, Jacob's sibling travelled to <a href="/wiki/Diagon_Alley" title="Diagon Alley">Diagon Alley</a> to buy school supplies, and met and befriended future fellow student and housemate <a href="/wiki/Rowan_Khanna" title="Rowan Khanna">Rowan Khanna</a>, who came from a wizarding family of wood farmers. Rowan suggested to start at <a href="/wiki/Flourish_and_Blotts" title="Flourish and Blotts">Flourish and Blotts</a>. While picking up <i><a href="/wiki/The_Standard_Book_of_Spells" title="The Standard Book of Spells">The Standard Book of Spells</a></i>, Jacob's sibling excitedly remarked how they looked forward to learning all the <a href="/wiki/Charm" title="Charm">Charms</a>; upon picking up <i><a href="/wiki/Fantastic_Beasts_and_Where_to_Find_Them" title="Fantastic Beasts and Where to Find Them">Fantastic Beasts and Where to Find Them</a></i>, they said they were familiar with it and thought it to be brilliant. Afterwards, they journeyed to <a href="/wiki/Ollivanders" title="Ollivanders">Ollivanders Wand Shop</a> to purchase their <a href="/wiki/Wand_of_Jacob%27s_sibling" class="mw-redirect" title="Wand of Jacob&#39;s sibling">first wand</a>: <a href="/wiki/Garrick_Ollivander" title="Garrick Ollivander">Garrick Ollivander</a> expressed both concern and curiosity about what sort of effect their brother's choices had made on this individual, but still looked forward to seeing what kind of life they would lead. When the shopping concluded, Jacob's sibling and Rowan promised to see each other again on the train.<sup id="cite_ref-16" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-16">&#91;16&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span id="Hogwarts_years_(1984-1991)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="Hogwarts_years_.281984-1991.29">Hogwarts years <span class="template-c">(<a href="/wiki/1984" title="1984">1984</a>-<a href="/wiki/1991" title="1991">1991</a>)</span></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Jacob%27s_sibling?veaction=edit&amp;section=4" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Hogwarts years (1984-1991)">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Jacob%27s_sibling?action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Hogwarts years (1984-1991)"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="First_year">First year</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Jacob%27s_sibling?veaction=edit&amp;section=5" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: First year">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Jacob%27s_sibling?action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: First year"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<h5><span class="mw-headline" id="Sorting_ceremony">Sorting ceremony</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Jacob%27s_sibling?veaction=edit&amp;section=6" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Sorting ceremony">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Jacob%27s_sibling?action=edit&amp;section=6" title="Edit section: Sorting ceremony"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h5>
<figure class="thumb tright show-info-icon" style="width: 318px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/1/1e/1984_sorting.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20200403034410" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/1/1e/1984_sorting.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/316?cb=20200403034410" decoding="async" width="316" height="178" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="1984 sorting.jpg" data-image-key="1984_sorting.jpg" /></a> 	 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:1984_sorting.jpg" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption"><a href="/wiki/First_year" title="First year">First years</a> being <a href="/wiki/Sorting_ceremony" title="Sorting ceremony">Sorted</a> in <a href="/wiki/1984" title="1984">1984</a></p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure>
<p>On <a href="/wiki/1_September" title="1 September">1 September</a> <a href="/wiki/1984" title="1984">1984</a>, Jacob's sibling boarded the <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Express" title="Hogwarts Express">Hogwarts Express</a> on <a href="/wiki/Platform_Nine_and_Three-Quarters" title="Platform Nine and Three-Quarters">Platform Nine and Three-Quarters</a>, through the magical barrier at <a href="/wiki/King%27s_Cross_Station" title="King&#39;s Cross Station">King's Cross Station</a>. Upon arrival, they were greeted at the <a href="/wiki/Great_Hall" title="Great Hall">Great Hall</a> by the rest of the students and the staff. <a href="/wiki/Minerva_McGonagall" title="Minerva McGonagall">Professor McGonagall</a> proceeded to provide them with a short explanation of the four <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Houses" title="Hogwarts Houses">Hogwarts Houses</a> — <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor" title="Gryffindor">Gryffindor</a>, the brave and chivalrous, <a href="/wiki/Hufflepuff" title="Hufflepuff">Hufflepuff</a>, the kind and diligent, <a href="/wiki/Ravenclaw" title="Ravenclaw">Ravenclaw</a>, the witty and wise, and <a href="/wiki/Slytherin" title="Slytherin">Slytherin</a>, the cunning and ambitious — voicing her hope that all of the newcomers would make fine additions to the ranks of great witches and wizards that each House had produced in the past. When Jacob's sibling was called to be <a href="/wiki/Sorting_ceremony" title="Sorting ceremony">Sorted</a>, the <a href="/wiki/Sorting_Hat" title="Sorting Hat">Sorting Hat</a> remarked that "[it hadn't] <i>seen one like this in a long time</i>", and took their choice of House into account — which was the same as their brother Jacob's. Later, they found that Rowan had been Sorted into the same House as them.<sup id="cite_ref-y1ch2_17-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-y1ch2-17">&#91;17&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>When the sorting was done and all <a href="/wiki/First_year" title="First year">first years</a> have found places to sit among their new housemates, <a href="/wiki/Albus_Dumbledore" title="Albus Dumbledore">Professor Dumbledore</a> rose and stood behind the <a href="/wiki/Owl_Lecturn" title="Owl Lecturn">Owl Lecturn</a> in front of the <a href="/wiki/High_Table" title="High Table">High Table</a>. He gave a brief speech of welcomes, educated the new arrivals on the rules of the <a href="/wiki/House_Cup" title="House Cup">House Cup</a> and on what a great honour it would be to win it. At the end of the <a href="/wiki/Start-of-Term_Feast" title="Start-of-Term Feast">Start-of-Term Feast</a>, Jacob's sibling's <a href="/wiki/Head_of_House" title="Head of House">Head of House</a> moved to stand beside the <a href="/wiki/Headmaster" title="Headmaster">Headmaster</a>, announcing to the students of their house that it was time for them to move to their <a href="/wiki/Common_room" title="Common room">common room</a>. Once there, Jacob's sibling reunited with Rowan, and the two briefly discussed their shared excitement over finally attending school and their new House before agreeing to go to bed, as they would have to be up bright and early for <a href="/wiki/Charms_(class)" title="Charms (class)">Charms</a> first thing in the morning.<sup id="cite_ref-y1ch2_17-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-y1ch2-17">&#91;17&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h5><span class="mw-headline" id="Charms_class">Charms class</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Jacob%27s_sibling?veaction=edit&amp;section=7" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Charms class">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Jacob%27s_sibling?action=edit&amp;section=7" title="Edit section: Charms class"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h5>
<p>As Charms was one of the subjects Jacob's sibling had most looked forward to, when arriving at the <a href="/wiki/Charms_Classroom" title="Charms Classroom">Charms Classroom</a>, they courteously greeted the <a href="/wiki/Charms_Master" title="Charms Master">Charms Master</a>, <a href="/wiki/Filius_Flitwick" title="Filius Flitwick">Professor Flitwick</a>. Recognising the individual as Jacob's sibling, Filius Flitwick answered in kind, claiming that he was certain the student would do just fine, as their older brother had been one of his most skilled students at Hogwarts prior to his expulsion. Even so, he subtly warned them against trouble-making, adding that Jacob had been one of his most rebellious students as well.<sup id="cite_ref-18" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-18">&#91;18&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h5><span class="mw-headline" id="Meeting_Merula_Snyde">Meeting Merula Snyde</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Jacob%27s_sibling?veaction=edit&amp;section=8" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Meeting Merula Snyde">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Jacob%27s_sibling?action=edit&amp;section=8" title="Edit section: Meeting Merula Snyde"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h5>
<p>Later on, Jacob's sibling encountered <a href="/wiki/Merula_Snyde" title="Merula Snyde">Merula Snyde</a>, an imperious and cruel <a href="/wiki/Slytherin" title="Slytherin">Slytherin</a> student, who tried to bully Rowan into admitting her as the most powerful witch to ever attend Hogwarts. Jacob's sibling intervened on Rowan's behalf and stood up for them against Merula, but before their argument went any further, <a href="/wiki/Severus_Snape" title="Severus Snape">Professor Snape</a> ordered them to get into the <a href="/wiki/Potions_Classroom" title="Potions Classroom">Potions Classroom</a> for the <a href="/wiki/Potions_(class)" title="Potions (class)">subsequent class</a>. The animosity formed between Merula and Jacob's sibling grew in that lesson, for at the end of the class, Merula sabotaged the <a href="/wiki/Cure_for_Boils" title="Cure for Boils">Cure for Boils</a> Jacob's sibling brewed with <a href="/wiki/Bulbadox_powder" title="Bulbadox powder">Bulbadox powder</a>, which caused the potion to explode, resulting in them and <a href="/wiki/Rowan_Khanna" title="Rowan Khanna">Rowan Khanna</a> being punished unfairly by Snape for their presumed clumsiness.<sup id="cite_ref-19" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-19">&#91;19&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Not long after the incident, Jacob's sibling was introduced to <a href="/wiki/Ben_Copper" title="Ben Copper">Ben Copper</a> and the House <a href="/wiki/Prefect" title="Prefect">Prefect</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-20" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-20">&#91;20&#93;</a></sup> The mutual dislike between Jacob's sibling and Merula Snyde led to a <a href="/wiki/Duelling" title="Duelling">duel</a>, which was interrupted by the arrival of Professors Snape and <a href="/wiki/Filius_Flitwick" title="Filius Flitwick">Flitwick</a>. Snape instructed both Merula and Jacob's sibling to arrive in the <a href="/wiki/West_Tower" title="West Tower">West Tower</a> to receive their punishment. On their way, they encountered <a href="/wiki/Penny_Haywood" title="Penny Haywood">Penny Haywood</a>, the most popular girl in their year. She thanked them for having the courage to stand up to Merula, the school bully, and offered them her help in whatever they needed, which Jacob's sibling appreciated. Once they met Snape, Jacob's sibling lost house points for their house, while Merula did not.<sup id="cite_ref-21" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-21">&#91;21&#93;</a></sup> Before Snape could dispense punishment, however, he was interrupted by the school caretaker, <a href="/wiki/Argus_Filch" title="Argus Filch">Argus Filch</a>, who required him and so Snape was forced to depart from the two students although he unknowingly was followed by Jacob's sibling and Merula Snyde who subsequently overheard Snape and Filch talking about the mysterious <a href="/wiki/Cursed_Vaults" title="Cursed Vaults">Cursed Vaults</a>, resulting in Jacob's sibling deciding to try to find them.<sup id="cite_ref-22" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-22">&#91;22&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h5><span class="mw-headline" id="Encountering_Fenrir_Greyback">Encountering Fenrir Greyback</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Jacob%27s_sibling?veaction=edit&amp;section=9" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Encountering Fenrir Greyback">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Jacob%27s_sibling?action=edit&amp;section=9" title="Edit section: Encountering Fenrir Greyback"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h5>
<p>On <a href="/wiki/Hallowe%27en" title="Hallowe&#39;en">Hallowe'en</a>, <a href="/wiki/Fenrir_Greyback" title="Fenrir Greyback">Fenrir Greyback</a> and his <a href="/wiki/Werewolf_army" title="Werewolf army">army</a> <a href="/wiki/Fenrir_Greyback%27s_Attack_on_Hogwarts" class="mw-redirect" title="Fenrir Greyback&#39;s Attack on Hogwarts">attacked Hogwarts</a>. <a href="/wiki/Chiara_Lobosca" title="Chiara Lobosca">Chiara Lobosca</a>, a werewolf student (in human form at the time), spotted Greyback and two werewolves emerging from the <a href="/wiki/Forbidden_Forest" title="Forbidden Forest">Forbidden Forest</a> and tried to attack them, but was easily defeated. Shortly afterwards, Greyback and his two werewolf companions encountered Jacob's sibling, who defiantly stood up to them and briefly held off one of the two werewolves with their spells. Before Greyback could better coordinate the assault on and hence overwhelm the young pupil, however, he caught the scent of <a href="/wiki/Albus_Dumbledore" title="Albus Dumbledore">Albus Dumbledore</a> and made a hasty retreat, who arrived just too late to capture him. The headmaster briefly examined the injured student on the ground and instructed the other one to return to their dormitory while he attended to Chiara, explaining that he would contact the Ministry of Magic and ask them to send an expert from the <a href="/wiki/Werewolf_Capture_Unit" title="Werewolf Capture Unit">Werewolf Capture Unit</a> to help to stop Grayback.
</p>

<p>The next full moon, Greyback and his army attacked Hogwarts again. This time, they encountered Jacob's sibling and <a href="/wiki/Cecil_Lee" title="Cecil Lee">Cecil Lee</a>, whom the Ministry had sent on Dumbledore's behest. During the confrontation, Cecil tried to use the <a href="/wiki/Homorphus_Charm" title="Homorphus Charm">Homorphus Charm</a> on Greyback's werewolf companions, but, as he was out of range, it failed. While Cecil was surrounded by the two other werewolves, Greyback transformed. He was attacked by Chiara in her werewolf form, but swiftly and brutally put her out of action by knocking her unconscious. After this, Jacob's sibling successfully held him at bay by some swift-spell-casting. Having fended off the two werewolves attacking him, who had retreated to Greyback's side, Cecil swiftly stepped forward and conjured ropes from the end of his wand to restrain the pack leader. Having captured the three werewolves, Cecil agreed not to capture Chiara due to her heroic actions.

Noticing that <a href="/wiki/Mrs_Norris" title="Mrs Norris">Mrs Norris</a> was guarding the door, they brew a <a href="/wiki/Sleeping_Draught" title="Sleeping Draught">Sleeping Draught</a> and used it on her to get past the door. <h5><span id="Ben_Copper's_disappearance"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="Ben_Copper.27s_disappearance">Ben Copper's disappearance</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Jacob%27s_sibling?veaction=edit&amp;section=12" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Ben Copper&#039;s disappearance">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Jacob%27s_sibling?action=edit&amp;section=12" title="Edit section: Ben Copper&#039;s disappearance"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h5>
<p>During the <a href="/wiki/1985%E2%80%931986_school_year" title="1985–1986 school year">1985–1986 school year</a>, Ben Copper went missing shortly after the start of their <a href="/wiki/Second_year" title="Second year">second year</a>, and Jacob's sibling tried to search the castle for him. While doing so, they found a note in the <a href="/wiki/Artefact_Room" title="Artefact Room">Artefact Room</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-25" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-25">&#91;25&#93;</a></sup> With help from Rowan, the note was deciphered, revealing that it was signed by someone using the initial "<a href="/wiki/R" title="R">R</a>" and contained instructions to locate a black quill hidden in a common room different from their own, which would turn into a note with additional information when <a href="/wiki/Untransfiguration" title="Untransfiguration">untransfigured</a>. These instructions were allegedly meant for Ben to follow, and the note was speculated to have a connection to his disappearance. Jacob's sibling was puzzled, but decided to do as the note said in hopes of finding Ben. They learned the <a href="/wiki/Shrinking_Charm" title="Shrinking Charm">Shrinking Charm</a> and <a href="/wiki/Engorgement_Charm" title="Engorgement Charm">Engorgement Charm</a> in order to enter the other house's common room by using them on themselves and sneaking in undetected when the common room was empty, during <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor_vs_Slytherin_Quidditch_match_(1985)" title="Gryffindor vs Slytherin Quidditch match (1985)">a game</a> of <a href="/wiki/Quidditch" title="Quidditch">Quidditch</a> between Gryffindor and Slytherin. In preparation for this, Jacob's sibling questioned Flitwick about the ramifications of using such <a href="/wiki/Spell" title="Spell">spells</a> on living things, but Flitwick became suspicious and strictly forbade it.<sup id="cite_ref-26" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-26">&#91;26&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h5><span class="mw-headline" id="Preparing_for_the_Deathday_Party">Preparing for the Deathday Party</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Jacob%27s_sibling?veaction=edit&amp;section=13" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Preparing for the Deathday Party">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Jacob%27s_sibling?action=edit&amp;section=13" title="Edit section: Preparing for the Deathday Party"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h5>

Jacob's sibling then helped Sir Nicholas prepare for his Deathday Party by making and sending invitation <a href="/wiki/Howler" title="Howler">Howlers</a> and sabotage Erling's party by preserving the food that was intended to be spoiled. However, this act was discovered by <a href="/wiki/Pomona_Sprout" title="Pomona Sprout">Professor Sprout</a>, so Sir Nicholas gave his own food to Erling. On Hallowe'en, the two parties were eventually combined due to neither of them being satisfying, and Jacob's sibling danced with <a href="/wiki/Myrtle_Warren" title="Myrtle Warren">Myrtle Warren</a> there.<sup id="cite_ref-27" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-27">&#91;27&#93;</a></sup>

<p>Despite <a href="/wiki/Filius_Flitwick" title="Filius Flitwick">Flitwick</a>'s objection, Jacob's sibling and <a href="/wiki/Rowan_Khanna" title="Rowan Khanna">Rowan</a> sneaked into the <a href="/wiki/Common_room" title="Common room">common room</a> as planned and got the black quill, but while exiting they were caught by the common room's corresponding <a href="/wiki/Head_of_House" title="Head of House">Head of House</a>, who asked them to explain their behaviour. Shortly after this, they untransfigured the quill using <a href="/wiki/Reparifarge" title="Reparifarge">Reparifarge</a>, and it transformed into another note from <a href="/wiki/R" title="R">R</a>, instructing the reader to go to the farthest <a href="/wiki/Icy_Corridor" title="Icy Corridor">corridor</a> at the end of the fifth floor, re-transfigure the scroll into a black quill, and return it to the common room in which it had been hidden. They also found <a href="/wiki/Ben_Copper" title="Ben Copper">Ben</a> frozen in the <a href="/wiki/Cursed_ice" title="Cursed ice">cursed ice</a>, but the professors saved him by casting the <a href="/wiki/Fire-Making_Spell" title="Fire-Making Spell">Fire-Making Spell</a>, explaining that the cursed ice appeared to have developed resistance to the <a href="/wiki/Knockback_Jinx" title="Knockback Jinx">Knockback Jinx</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-28" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-28">&#91;28&#93;</a></sup>

After learning, they returned to the corridor and used it to find a hidden door covered with ice. They tried opening it but Rowan got hurt by some flying ice, so Jacob's sibling sent Rowan to the <a href="/wiki/Hospital_Wing" title="Hospital Wing">Hospital Wing</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-29" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-29">&#91;29&#93;</a></sup> Rowan later recovered and suggested they get help from <a href="/wiki/William_Weasley" title="William Weasley">Bill Weasley</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-30" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-30">&#91;30&#93;</a></sup>

<p>Rumours spread around <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts</a> that a white werewolf had attacked a student. Jacob's sibling started investigating and asked <a href="/wiki/Chiara_Lobosca" title="Chiara Lobosca">Chiara Lobosca</a> for help, who was reluctant at first but eventually agreed. She revealed to them that she was a werewolf and was afraid that the attacker might have been herself. They duelled Chiara in her werewolf form, which drove her off into the <a href="/wiki/Forbidden_Forest" title="Forbidden Forest">Forbidden Forest</a> to spend the rest of the night safely.<sup id="cite_ref-31" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-31">&#91;31&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-32" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-32">&#91;32&#93;</a></sup>

<h6><span class="mw-headline" id="Meeting_the_Malfoys">Meeting the Malfoys</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Jacob%27s_sibling?veaction=edit&amp;section=21" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Meeting the Malfoys">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Jacob%27s_sibling?action=edit&amp;section=21" title="Edit section: Meeting the Malfoys"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h6>
<p><a href="/wiki/Lucius_Malfoy" title="Lucius Malfoy">Lucius Malfoy</a> came to <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts</a> to inspect as a <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Board_of_Governors" title="Hogwarts Board of Governors">governor</a>, during which he questioned Jacob's sibling and <a href="/wiki/Ben_Copper" title="Ben Copper">Ben Copper</a>. Jacob's sibling and <a href="/wiki/Merula_Snyde" title="Merula Snyde">Merula Snyde</a> were asked to babysit his son <a href="/wiki/Draco_Malfoy" title="Draco Malfoy">Draco</a>. During the inspection, <a href="/wiki/Lucius_Malfoy%27s_wand" title="Lucius Malfoy&#39;s wand">Lucius' wand</a> got stolen. Lucius planned to blame his <a href="/wiki/House-elf" title="House-elf">house-elf</a> <a href="/wiki/Dobby" title="Dobby">Dobby</a> for the theft as he knew where the wand had been kept, but Dobby claimed to Jacob's sibling that he did not do it. Later, they found out that it was Draco who had stolen the wand and they brought the wand back to Lucius.<sup id="cite_ref-38" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-38">&#91;38&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h6><span class="mw-headline" id="Celebrating_Hogwarts_teachers">Celebrating Hogwarts teachers</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Jacob%27s_sibling?veaction=edit&amp;section=22" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Celebrating Hogwarts teachers">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Jacob%27s_sibling?action=edit&amp;section=22" title="Edit section: Celebrating Hogwarts teachers"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h6>
<p>Jacob's sibling participated in the <a href="/wiki/Teacher_Appreciation_Celebration" title="Teacher Appreciation Celebration">Teacher Appreciation Celebration</a>. They interviewed <a href="/wiki/Minerva_McGonagall" title="Minerva McGonagall">Professor McGonagall</a>, this year's <a href="/wiki/Guest_of_Honour" title="Guest of Honour">Guest of Honour</a>, and did a presentation about her on the final celebration feast.<sup id="cite_ref-39" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-39">&#91;39&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Third_year">Third year</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Jacob%27s_sibling?veaction=edit&amp;section=23" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Third year">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Jacob%27s_sibling?action=edit&amp;section=23" title="Edit section: Third year"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<p>Jacob's sibling took <a href="/wiki/Care_of_Magical_Creatures" title="Care of Magical Creatures">Care of Magical Creatures</a> and <a href="/wiki/Divination_(class)" title="Divination (class)">Divination</a> as electives.<sup id="cite_ref-40" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-40">&#91;40&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>During the year, they also met with their <a href="/wiki/Prefect" title="Prefect">Prefect</a> who was about to graduate, and they taught Jacob's sibling to cast the <a href="/wiki/Freezing_Charm" title="Freezing Charm">Freezing Charm</a>, to brew the <a href="/wiki/Babbling_Beverage" title="Babbling Beverage">Babbling Beverage</a> and to tend <a href="/wiki/Snargaluff" title="Snargaluff">Snargaluff</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-41" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-41">&#91;41&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h5><span class="mw-headline" id="Becoming_an_Animagus">Becoming an Animagus</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Jacob%27s_sibling?veaction=edit&amp;section=24" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Becoming an Animagus">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Jacob%27s_sibling?action=edit&amp;section=24" title="Edit section: Becoming an Animagus"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h5>
<p>In the beginning of their <a href="/wiki/Third_year" title="Third year">third year</a>, at the end of a <a href="/wiki/Herbology" title="Herbology">Herbology</a> class, a <a href="/wiki/Werewolf" title="Werewolf">werewolf</a> appeared in the <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_greenhouses" title="Hogwarts greenhouses">Greenhouse</a>, which later turned out to be a <a href="/wiki/Boggart" title="Boggart">Boggart</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-HM31_42-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HM31-42">&#91;42&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>A visit to <a href="/wiki/Hogsmeade" title="Hogsmeade">Hogsmeade</a> was due since it was Jacob's sibling's third year, but they were initially not allowed to go because they have broken a lot of school rules. Their <a href="/wiki/Head_of_House" title="Head of House">Head of House</a> offered to let them go if they studied hard, which they did, eventually gaining permission.<sup id="cite_ref-HM31_42-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HM31-42">&#91;42&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Afterwards, Jacob's sibling became an <a href="/wiki/Animagus" title="Animagus">Animagus</a> with the help of <a href="/wiki/Penny_Haywood" title="Penny Haywood">Penny Haywood</a> and <a href="/wiki/Talbott_Winger" title="Talbott Winger">Talbott Winger</a> (who was an unregistered Animagus already).<sup id="cite_ref-Animagus_9-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Animagus-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-43" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-43">&#91;43&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h5><span class="mw-headline" id="Hogsmeade">Hogsmeade</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Jacob%27s_sibling?veaction=edit&amp;section=25" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Hogsmeade">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Jacob%27s_sibling?action=edit&amp;section=25" title="Edit section: Hogsmeade"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h5>
<p>Arriving at <a href="/wiki/Hogsmeade" title="Hogsmeade">Hogsmeade</a>, Jacob's sibling went to the <a href="/wiki/Three_Broomsticks_Inn" title="Three Broomsticks Inn">Three Broomsticks</a> and asked <a href="/wiki/Rosmerta" title="Rosmerta">Madam Rosmerta</a> about Jacob. She gave them a black quill that belonged to Jacob. Using <a href="/wiki/Reparifarge" title="Reparifarge">Reparifarge</a>, Jacob's sibling <a href="/wiki/Transformation" title="Transformation">transformed</a> it back into <a href="/wiki/Jacob%27s_notebook" class="mw-redirect" title="Jacob&#39;s notebook">a notebook</a> and told Rosmerta what it was. But <a href="/wiki/Argus_Filch" title="Argus Filch">Filch</a>, who overheard, seized the notebook and took it to his office, as ordered by <a href="/wiki/Severus_Snape" title="Severus Snape">Professor Snape</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-44" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-44">&#91;44&#93;</a></sup> With the help of <a href="/wiki/Nymphadora_Tonks" title="Nymphadora Tonks">Nymphadora Tonks</a>, Jacob's sibling tricked Filch and got the notebook back. To hide in his office again, they used the <a href="/wiki/Shrinking_Charm" title="Shrinking Charm">Shrinking Charm</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-45" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-45">&#91;45&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h5><span class="mw-headline" id="Frog_Choir_audition">Frog Choir audition</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Jacob%27s_sibling?veaction=edit&amp;section=26" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Frog Choir audition">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Jacob%27s_sibling?action=edit&amp;section=26" title="Edit section: Frog Choir audition"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h5>
<p>Jacob's sibling heard <a href="/wiki/Filius_Flitwick" title="Filius Flitwick">Professor Flitwick</a> announce in the <a href="/wiki/Great_Hall" title="Great Hall">Great Hall</a> that an audition for the <a href="/wiki/Frog_Choir" title="Frog Choir">Frog Choir</a> would soon be held. They met <a href="/wiki/Tulip_Karasu" title="Tulip Karasu">Tulip Karasu</a>, a <a href="/wiki/Ravenclaw" title="Ravenclaw">Ravenclaw</a> girl, and accepted to take her <a href="/wiki/Toad" title="Toad">toad</a>, <a href="/wiki/Dennis_(toad)" title="Dennis (toad)">Dennis</a>, with them to the Frog Choir audition. Jacob's sibling and Dennis did a few things to bond, one of which was playing tricks on <a href="/wiki/Irma_Pince" title="Irma Pince">Madam Pince</a>. They then met with the <a href="/wiki/Weird_Sisters" title="Weird Sisters">Weird Sisters</a> and practised with them. They sang in front of <a href="/wiki/Severus_Snape" title="Severus Snape">Professor Snape</a> to test their nerves. They went to the audition, and so did <a href="/wiki/Merula_Snyde" title="Merula Snyde">Merula Snyde</a>. Merula duelled Jacob's sibling later, and she told them that she had been wanting the place in the choir because of her memories with <a href="/wiki/Merula_Snyde%27s_mother" title="Merula Snyde&#39;s mother">her mom</a> before she got arrested. Jacob's sibling won the audition, but it is unknown whether they gave the place to Merula.<sup id="cite_ref-FC_11-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-FC-11">&#91;11&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-46" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-46">&#91;46&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h5><span id="Retrieving_Merula's_key"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="Retrieving_Merula.27s_key">Retrieving Merula's key</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Jacob%27s_sibling?veaction=edit&amp;section=27" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Retrieving Merula&#039;s key">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Jacob%27s_sibling?action=edit&amp;section=27" title="Edit section: Retrieving Merula&#039;s key"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h5>
<p><a href="/wiki/Rowan_Khanna" title="Rowan Khanna">Rowan Khanna</a> deciphered the notebook, and they learn about <a href="/wiki/Jacob%27s_room" title="Jacob&#39;s room">a room</a> where Jacob researched the Cursed Vaults. The room was closed with a double lock, and no spell worked against it. Jacob's sibling and another student read that the lock was the property of <a href="/wiki/Tulip_Karasu" title="Tulip Karasu">Tulip Karasu</a> and asked her for help. After testing their character, Tulip told that she had one of the keys from that lock, and <a href="/wiki/Merula_Snyde" title="Merula Snyde">Merula Snyde</a> had the other one.<sup id="cite_ref-47" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-47">&#91;47&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Tulip and Jacob's sibling went to the <a href="/wiki/Courtyard" title="Courtyard">courtyard</a> to ask Merula for the key. She agreed after Jacob's sibling defeated her in a duel again, but she tells them she had already taken what she needed from there and warned them not to trust Tulip.<sup id="cite_ref-48" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-48">&#91;48&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h5><span id="Entering_Jacob's_room"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="Entering_Jacob.27s_room">Entering Jacob's room</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Jacob%27s_sibling?veaction=edit&amp;section=28" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Entering Jacob&#039;s room">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Jacob%27s_sibling?action=edit&amp;section=28" title="Edit section: Entering Jacob&#039;s room"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h5>
<p>After getting the key, <a href="/wiki/Tulip_Karasu" title="Tulip Karasu">Tulip</a> and Jacob's sibling went into the room and encountered a <a href="/wiki/Boggart" title="Boggart">Boggart</a>, which took the form of <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Voldemort</a>. Remembering <a href="/wiki/Pomona_Sprout" title="Pomona Sprout">Professor Sprout</a> had used a <a href="/wiki/Boggart-Banishing_Spell" title="Boggart-Banishing Spell">spell</a> against a Boggart earlier in <a href="/wiki/Herbology" title="Herbology">Herbology</a> class, they went to the <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Library" title="Hogwarts Library">Library</a> to find a book about it and to the <a href="/wiki/Artefact_Room" title="Artefact Room">Artefact Room</a> to practise it on a Boggart there. The Boggart took the form of a blood-sodden <a href="/wiki/Merula_Snyde" title="Merula Snyde">Merula Snyde</a> scolding Tulip for betraying her and Jacob's sibling transformed it to a ridiculously-dressed Merula using the spell. Subsequently, Tulip confessed that her greed and naivety had driven her to double-cross Merula, though Jacob's sibling urged her not to self-deprecate and gave her the benefit of the doubt when she swore she would never repeat the same mistake.<sup id="cite_ref-y3ch7_10-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-y3ch7-10">&#91;10&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>They went back to Jacob's room and defeated the Boggart there in a similar manner. After searching, they found a note indicating the entrance to the next <a href="/wiki/Cursed_Vaults" title="Cursed Vaults">Cursed Vault</a> might be in the Library because there had been more Boggarts than anywhere when someone had tampered with the vault before.<sup id="cite_ref-y3ch7_10-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-y3ch7-10">&#91;10&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h5><span class="mw-headline" id="Convincing_Barnaby_Lee">Convincing Barnaby Lee</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Jacob%27s_sibling?veaction=edit&amp;section=29" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Convincing Barnaby Lee">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Jacob%27s_sibling?action=edit&amp;section=29" title="Edit section: Convincing Barnaby Lee"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h5>
<p>Jacob's sibling went to the <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Library" title="Hogwarts Library">Library</a> with some friends while <a href="/wiki/Tulip_Karasu" title="Tulip Karasu">Tulip</a> continued to search <a href="/wiki/Jacob%27s_room" title="Jacob&#39;s room">Jacob's room</a>. Jacob's sibling found a note that suggested the <a href="/wiki/Restricted_Section" title="Restricted Section">Restricted Section</a> and decided to meet up with Tulip, but encountered <a href="/wiki/Merula_Snyde" title="Merula Snyde">Merula</a> along the way. Tulip suggested Jacob's sibling team up with <a href="/wiki/Barnaby_Lee" title="Barnaby Lee">Barnaby Lee</a> to deal with Merula. They met with Barnaby and through their persuasion, Barnaby realised he would like to work with them more than with Merula, his current affiliation. He challenged them to a duel and drank <a href="/wiki/Butterbeer" title="Butterbeer">Butterbeer</a> with them.<sup id="cite_ref-49" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-49">&#91;49&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h5><span class="mw-headline" id="Bonding_with_Talbott_Winger">Bonding with Talbott Winger</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Jacob%27s_sibling?veaction=edit&amp;section=30" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Bonding with Talbott Winger">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Jacob%27s_sibling?action=edit&amp;section=30" title="Edit section: Bonding with Talbott Winger"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h5>
<p><a href="/wiki/Penny_Haywood" title="Penny Haywood">Penny Haywood</a> told Jacob's sibling that her friend <a href="/wiki/Talbott_Winger" title="Talbott Winger">Talbott Winger</a> had been spending time alone and missing classes, so Jacob's sibling went to check on him. He was about to tell Jacob's sibling what he had been up to when they discovered <a href="/wiki/Merula_Snyde" title="Merula Snyde">Merula Snyde</a> had been spying on them invisibly, possibly under the <a href="/wiki/Disillusionment_Charm" title="Disillusionment Charm">Disillusionment Charm</a>. They suspected Merula had heard the part about Talbott being an unregistered <a href="/wiki/Animagus" title="Animagus">Animagus</a>, so Jacob's sibling went to confront Merula, who didn't show an interest in reporting them but didn't promise to not do so either. They then studied <a href="/wiki/Memory_Charm" title="Memory Charm">Obliviate</a> and may have used it on Merula so she didn't remember anymore.<sup id="cite_ref-BOAF_50-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-BOAF-50">&#91;50&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-51" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-51">&#91;51&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Talbott revealed that his <a href="/wiki/Talbott_Winger%27s_swan_necklace" title="Talbott Winger&#39;s swan necklace">necklace</a> was missing, whose feather had been extracted from <a href="/wiki/Talbott_Winger%27s_mother" title="Talbott Winger&#39;s mother">his mother</a>'s Animagus form, a <a href="/wiki/White_Swan" title="White Swan">white swan</a>. Jacob's sibling helped find the necklace. They made use of their advantage of transforming into animals and eventually decided it was in <a href="/wiki/Argus_Filch" title="Argus Filch">Filch</a>'s office or being played with by <a href="/wiki/Mrs_Norris" title="Mrs Norris">Mrs Norris</a>. They saw Mrs Norris with the necklace, and Jacob's sibling transformed into their Animagus form to distract her while Talbott retrieved the necklace. Talbott then admitted it was the anniversary of their parents' murder, which, together with the missing necklace, were what had been letting him down of late. Talbott befriended Jacob's sibling after this experience.<sup id="cite_ref-BOAF_50-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-BOAF-50">&#91;50&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h5><span class="mw-headline" id="Entering_the_Vault_of_Fear">Entering the Vault of Fear</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Jacob%27s_sibling?veaction=edit&amp;section=31" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Entering the Vault of Fear">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Jacob%27s_sibling?action=edit&amp;section=31" title="Edit section: Entering the Vault of Fear"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h5>
<p>Jacob's sibling invited a friend (possibly <a href="/wiki/Rowan_Khanna" title="Rowan Khanna">Rowan</a>, <a href="/wiki/William_Weasley" title="William Weasley">Bill</a> or <a href="/wiki/Nymphadora_Tonks" title="Nymphadora Tonks">Tonks</a>) along with <a href="/wiki/Tulip_Karasu" title="Tulip Karasu">Tulip</a> and <a href="/wiki/Barnaby_Lee" title="Barnaby Lee">Barnaby</a> to <a href="/wiki/Infiltration_of_the_Vault_of_Fear" title="Infiltration of the Vault of Fear">enter</a> the <a href="/wiki/Vault_of_Fear" title="Vault of Fear">Vault of Fear</a>. They went to the <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Library" title="Hogwarts Library">Library</a> and the friend distracted <a href="/wiki/Irma_Pince" title="Irma Pince">Madam Pince</a> so they could enter the <a href="/wiki/Restricted_Section" title="Restricted Section">Restricted Section</a>. Before entering, <a href="/wiki/Merula_Snyde" title="Merula Snyde">Merula</a> and <a href="/wiki/Ismelda_Murk" title="Ismelda Murk">Ismelda</a> showed up. Jacob's sibling won a duel with Merula and Barnaby stayed back to hold them off while the rest went on. They searched the Restricted Section and found a way to enter the Vault. Barnaby rejoined the team and they went in.<sup id="cite_ref-HM39_52-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HM39-52">&#91;52&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>The Vault became covered in mist and they found three <a href="/wiki/Boggart" title="Boggart">Boggarts</a>, all taking the form of <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Voldemort</a> and kidnapping one of Jacob's sibling's friends. Jacob's sibling used <a href="/wiki/Boggart-Banishing_Spell" title="Boggart-Banishing Spell">Riddikulus</a> to defeat them all, before a mysterious voice said it was too late and made their friends vanish. Jacob's sibling faced another Boggart Voldemort and won a duel. The mist cleared up and Jacob's sibling used <a href="/wiki/Jacob%27s_wand" title="Jacob&#39;s wand">Jacob's broken wand</a> as an "ultimate sacrifice" to open the core of the Vault, inside which were a map of the <a href="/wiki/Forbidden_Forest" title="Forbidden Forest">Forbidden Forest</a> and an <a href="/wiki/Arrow" title="Arrow">arrow</a>. They took them and left the Vault.<sup id="cite_ref-HM39_52-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HM39-52">&#91;52&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Jacob's sibling and their friends went to <a href="/wiki/Three_Broomsticks_Inn" title="Three Broomsticks Inn">Three Broomsticks</a> to drink <a href="/wiki/Butterbeer" title="Butterbeer">Butterbeer</a> and celebrate their success. <a href="/wiki/Albus_Dumbledore" title="Albus Dumbledore">Professor Dumbledore</a> visited and called Jacob's sibling back to his office. He listened to Jacob's sibling's vault-breaking story and explained that during his absence, he went to invite a professional <a href="/wiki/Curse-Breaker" title="Curse-Breaker">Curse-Breaker</a> to <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts</a>. He later awarded Jacob's sibling one hundred points.<sup id="cite_ref-53" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-53">&#91;53&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Fourth_year">Fourth year</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Jacob%27s_sibling?veaction=edit&amp;section=32" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Fourth year">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Jacob%27s_sibling?action=edit&amp;section=32" title="Edit section: Fourth year"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<h5><span class="mw-headline" id="Meeting_Patricia_Rakepick">Meeting Patricia Rakepick</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Jacob%27s_sibling?veaction=edit&amp;section=33" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Meeting Patricia Rakepick">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Jacob%27s_sibling?action=edit&amp;section=33" title="Edit section: Meeting Patricia Rakepick"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h5>
<p>At the start of <a href="/wiki/1987%E2%80%931988_school_year" title="1987–1988 school year">1987–1988 school year</a>, <a href="/wiki/Albus_Dumbledore" title="Albus Dumbledore">Professor Dumbledore</a> introduced Madam <a href="/wiki/Patricia_Rakepick" title="Patricia Rakepick">Patricia Rakepick</a>, a <a href="/wiki/Curse-Breaker" title="Curse-Breaker">Curse-Breaker</a> invited to the school, at the <a href="/wiki/Start-of-Term_Feast" title="Start-of-Term Feast">Start-of-Term Feast</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-HM41_54-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HM41-54">&#91;54&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>A sleepwalking epidemic spread throughout the school due to the opening of a third, unknown <a href="/wiki/Cursed_Vaults" title="Cursed Vaults">Cursed Vault</a>, with victims apparently heading to the <a href="/wiki/Forbidden_Forest" title="Forbidden Forest">Forbidden Forest</a>. One of the first victims was <a href="/wiki/Tulip_Karasu" title="Tulip Karasu">Tulip Karasu</a>, who was sent to the <a href="/wiki/Hospital_Wing" title="Hospital Wing">Hospital Wing</a>. She and Jacob's sibling talked about the incident and concluded that the third vault was likely located in the forest. Jacob's sibling attracted the attention of Madam Rakepick and she taught them the <a href="/wiki/Shield_Charm" title="Shield Charm">Shield Charm</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-HM41_54-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HM41-54">&#91;54&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h5><span class="mw-headline" id="Learning_the_Patronus_Charm">Learning the Patronus Charm</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Jacob%27s_sibling?veaction=edit&amp;section=34" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Learning the Patronus Charm">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Jacob%27s_sibling?action=edit&amp;section=34" title="Edit section: Learning the Patronus Charm"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h5>
<p>Jacob's sibling met with <a href="/wiki/Ben_Copper" title="Ben Copper">Ben Copper</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Artefact_Room" title="Artefact Room">Artefact Room</a>. They found a black quill and <a href="/wiki/Untransfiguration" title="Untransfiguration">untransfigured</a> it to reveal another note from <a href="/wiki/R" title="R">R</a> saying something would be unleashed. <a href="/wiki/Penny_Haywood" title="Penny Haywood">Penny Haywood</a> was later found in the <a href="/wiki/Hospital_Wing" title="Hospital Wing">Hospital Wing</a>, having been attacked by a mysterious creature. Jacob's sibling and <a href="/wiki/Nymphadora_Tonks" title="Nymphadora Tonks">Tonks</a> searched the <a href="/wiki/Training_Grounds" title="Training Grounds">Training Grounds</a>, where Jacob's sibling was faced with a <a href="/wiki/Dementor" title="Dementor">Dementor</a>. Tonks arrived in time and cast the <a href="/wiki/Patronus_Charm" title="Patronus Charm">Patronus Charm</a> to drive the Dememtor away.<sup id="cite_ref-Patronus_55-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Patronus-55">&#91;55&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-56" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-56">&#91;56&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Jacob's sibling asked Tonks to teach them the Patronus Charm, and Tonks brought them to <a href="/wiki/Hogsmeade" title="Hogsmeade">Hogsmeade</a> to play a prank to enlighten their mood. However, they found another note from R there, saying the Dementors were unleashed but nothing more. While preparing for the study, <a href="/wiki/Merula_Snyde" title="Merula Snyde">Merula Snyde</a> joined them. Although their first attempts were unsuccessful, they learned more from <a href="/wiki/Minerva_McGonagall" title="Minerva McGonagall">Professor McGonagall</a>. They found the Dementor again on the Training Grounds, and Jacob's sibling successfully conjured a corporeal Patronus which chased the Dementor out of the school.<sup id="cite_ref-Patronus_55-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Patronus-55">&#91;55&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h5><span class="mw-headline" id="Working_with_Patricia_Rakepick">Working with Patricia Rakepick</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Jacob%27s_sibling?veaction=edit&amp;section=35" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Working with Patricia Rakepick">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Jacob%27s_sibling?action=edit&amp;section=35" title="Edit section: Working with Patricia Rakepick"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h5>
<p>Jacob's sibling decided to investigate Rakepick and asked the professors their opinions about her, which were mostly negative. Rakepick found out about that and invited Jacob's sibling to a private conversation, in which she told them to stop doing so, while continuing with her plan of using their assistance.<sup id="cite_ref-57" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-57">&#91;57&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>At a certain point, Rakepick repeatedly invited Jacob's sibling, their friends, as well as their enemies <a href="/wiki/Merula_Snyde" title="Merula Snyde">Merula</a> and <a href="/wiki/Ismelda_Murk" title="Ismelda Murk">Ismelda</a>, to meet at the <a href="/wiki/Training_Grounds" title="Training Grounds">Training Grounds</a> for a little competition to elect an assistant to her.
</p>
<h5><span class="mw-headline" id="Venturing_into_the_Forbidden_Forest">Venturing into the Forbidden Forest</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Jacob%27s_sibling?veaction=edit&amp;section=36" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Venturing into the Forbidden Forest">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Jacob%27s_sibling?action=edit&amp;section=36" title="Edit section: Venturing into the Forbidden Forest"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h5>
<p>Jacob's sibling asked Professor <a href="/wiki/Silvanus_Kettleburn" title="Silvanus Kettleburn">Kettleburn</a> what is the best way to go to the Forbidden Forest, and he agreed to answer that question if they studied enough about <a href="/wiki/Bowtruckle" title="Bowtruckle">bowtruckles</a>. At some point, they received a forged letter supposedly signed by Kettleburn, which was actually a bait to lure them to the <a href="/wiki/Courtyard" title="Courtyard">Courtyard</a>, where a <a href="/wiki/R%27s_messenger" class="mw-redirect" title="R&#39;s messenger">cloaked figure</a> attacked them with the <a href="/wiki/Full_Body-Bind_Curse" title="Full Body-Bind Curse">Full Body-Bind Curse</a> and told them to stay away from the Cursed Vaults, the Forbidden Forest and Rakepick, further adding that "death is coming to Hogwarts" and may come to them as well if they disobey. The figure told that someone would eventually remove the binding curse, but it is unknown who did so.<sup id="cite_ref-58" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-58">&#91;58&#93;</a></sup> After freed, Jacob's sibling only told about this incident to a few friends.
</p><p>Kettleburn, after satisfied with Jacob's sibling's study on bowtruckles, answered their question. He said that by using a broom they could get to the forest mostly undetected. They then proceeded to try to get a broom, first by asking <a href="/wiki/Rolanda_Hooch" title="Rolanda Hooch">Madam Hooch</a> to borrow one, which she refused due to the <a href="/wiki/Black_Lake_incident" title="Black Lake incident">Black Lake incident</a> and not trusting them, and later by attempting to steal one from Filch's office, where all brooms they found were broken. (They incidentally found, however, another message transfigured into a black quill, which was a threat from <a href="/wiki/R" title="R">R</a> to Rakepick telling her to leave Hogwarts.) As a last resort, they asked another student to borrow their broom; the student was reluctant at first, but Jacob's sibling managed to convince them to borrow the broom by talking to them at <a href="/wiki/Three_Broomsticks_Inn" title="Three Broomsticks Inn">Three Broomsticks</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-59" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-59">&#91;59&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Jacob's sibling later went to the Forbidden Forest by <a href="/wiki/Broomstick" title="Broomstick">broomstick</a> together with <a href="/wiki/Charles_Weasley" title="Charles Weasley">Charles Weasley</a> and another friend.<sup id="cite_ref-60" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-60">&#91;60&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Fifth_year">Fifth year</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Jacob%27s_sibling?veaction=edit&amp;section=37" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Fifth year">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Jacob%27s_sibling?action=edit&amp;section=37" title="Edit section: Fifth year"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<p>At the start of the <a href="/wiki/1988%E2%80%931989_school_year" title="1988–1989 school year">1988–1989 school year</a>, their <a href="/wiki/Fifth_year" title="Fifth year">fifth year</a>, Jacob's sibling met Penny's younger sister <a href="/wiki/Beatrice_Haywood" title="Beatrice Haywood">Beatrice</a>, who gave them a hand-made <a href="/wiki/Puffskein" title="Puffskein">Puffskein</a>. They later went to the Library to speak with Bill Weasley who was studying for his <a href="/wiki/Nastily_Exhausting_Wizarding_Test" title="Nastily Exhausting Wizarding Test">N.E.W.T.</a> Exams. After this they met Madam Rakepick on the Training Grounds who along with Bill and Merula had been chosen as Rakepick's apprentice curse-breakers in their pursuit of the fourth Cursed Vault. She taught them how to cast the <a href="/wiki/Incarcerous_Spell" title="Incarcerous Spell">Incarcerous Spell</a> and had Jacob's sibling demonstrate it on Merula much to her chagrin. That night, Jacob's sibling went to meet Ben Copper in the Courtyard where they discussed Ben attacking the former last year. Before they could finish their conversation, both were confronted and attacked by <a href="/wiki/Unidentified_Dark_Wizard" title="Unidentified Dark Wizard">another figure in a red cloak</a>. The figure subdued both with a <a href="/wiki/Full_Body-Bind_Curse" title="Full Body-Bind Curse">Full Body-Bind Curse</a> before repeating their previous warning that death was coming to Hogwarts and telling them that they needed them alive and that before the school term ended, one of their friends would die.<sup id="cite_ref-61" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-61">&#91;61&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Jacob's sibling was later invited to the Three Broomsticks by Merula Snyde along with Bill Weasley. The three talked about how they should start co-operating. Soon, Penny Haywood came to inform that Beatrice was missing. The four decided to split into pairs. Jacob's sibling and one of the three searched the <a href="/wiki/Red_Cap" title="Red Cap">Red Cap</a>'s Hole and <a href="/wiki/Spider%27s_Lair" class="mw-redirect" title="Spider&#39;s Lair">Spider's Lair</a>, but could not find Beatrice. In the latter location, Jacob's sibling fought the same <a href="/wiki/Acromantula" title="Acromantula">Acromantula</a> who was guarding the Forest Vault the previous school year.<sup id="cite_ref-62" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-62">&#91;62&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Jacob's sibling got <a href="/wiki/Ordinary_Wizarding_Level" title="Ordinary Wizarding Level">O.W L.s</a> in <a href="/wiki/Potions_(class)" title="Potions (class)">Potions</a>, <a href="/wiki/Charms_(class)" title="Charms (class)">Charms</a>, <a href="/wiki/Transfiguration_(class)" title="Transfiguration (class)">Transfiguration</a>, <a href="/wiki/Herbology" title="Herbology">Herbology</a>, <a href="/wiki/Defence_Against_the_Dark_Arts" title="Defence Against the Dark Arts">Defence Against the Dark Arts</a>, <a href="/wiki/History_of_Magic" title="History of Magic">History of Magic</a> and <a href="/wiki/Care_of_Magical_Creatures" title="Care of Magical Creatures">Care of Magical Creatures</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-63" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-63">&#91;63&#93;</a></sup> They most likely also got O.W.L.s in <a href="/wiki/Divination" title="Divination">Divination</a> and <a href="/wiki/Astronomy_(class)" title="Astronomy (class)">Astronomy</a>, two subjects which they continued to take in their sixth year.<sup id="cite_ref-64" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-64">&#91;64&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Sixth_year">Sixth year</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Jacob%27s_sibling?veaction=edit&amp;section=38" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Sixth year">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Jacob%27s_sibling?action=edit&amp;section=38" title="Edit section: Sixth year"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<p>During the <a href="/wiki/1989%E2%80%931990_school_year" title="1989–1990 school year">1989–1990 school year</a>, their <a href="/wiki/Sixth_year" title="Sixth year">sixth year</a>, Jacob's sibling proceeded to take <a href="/wiki/Nastily_Exhausting_Wizarding_Test" title="Nastily Exhausting Wizarding Test">N.E.W.T.</a>-level <a href="/wiki/Transfiguration_(class)" title="Transfiguration (class)">Transfiguration</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-HM61t_65-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HM61t-65">&#91;65&#93;</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/Charms_(class)" title="Charms (class)">Charms</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-HM61c_66-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HM61c-66">&#91;66&#93;</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/Divination_(class)" title="Divination (class)">Divination</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-HM62_67-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HM62-67">&#91;67&#93;</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/Herbology" title="Herbology">Herbology</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-HM62h_68-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HM62h-68">&#91;68&#93;</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/History_of_Magic" title="History of Magic">History of Magic</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-HM62hm_69-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HM62hm-69">&#91;69&#93;</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/Potions_(class)" title="Potions (class)">Potions</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-HM63p_70-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HM63p-70">&#91;70&#93;</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/Care_of_Magical_Creatures" title="Care of Magical Creatures">Care of Magical Creatures</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-HM63cmc_71-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HM63cmc-71">&#91;71&#93;</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/Defence_Against_the_Dark_Arts" title="Defence Against the Dark Arts">Defence Against the Dark Arts</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-:0_72-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:0-72">&#91;72&#93;</a></sup> and <a href="/wiki/Astronomy_(class)" title="Astronomy (class)">Astronomy</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-HM625_73-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HM625-73">&#91;73&#93;</a></sup>

<p>Sometime during the school year, <a href="/wiki/Talbott_Winger" title="Talbott Winger">Talbott Winger</a> informed Jacob's sibling about a white <a href="/wiki/Owl" title="Owl">owl</a> that he discovered in the <a href="/wiki/Owlery" title="Owlery">Owlery</a>, which dropped many letters. One of the letters was written in <a href="/wiki/Japanese" title="Japanese">Japanese</a>, which they translated to a message informing them of a white quill by the shore of the <a href="/wiki/Great_Lake" title="Great Lake">Great Lake</a>. After a search of the lake shore, they found a white quill, which they <a href="/wiki/Reparifarge" title="Reparifarge">untransfigured</a> to reveal a message sent by the organisation <a href="/wiki/R" title="R">R</a> addressed to <a href="/wiki/Jacob_(Hogwarts_student)" title="Jacob (Hogwarts student)">Jacob</a>, asking him to meet the sender in the <a href="/wiki/Forest_Grove" title="Forest Grove">Forbidden Forest Grove</a>. Jacob's sibling then joined up with <a href="/wiki/Ben_Copper" title="Ben Copper">Ben Copper</a> to go together into the Forest Grove to intercept the meeting. Ben then recruited <a href="/wiki/Merula_Snyde" title="Merula Snyde">Merula Snyde</a> to join them, which was at first not accepted by Jacob's sibling, however he was eventually convinced to take her.
</p>
<h5><span class="mw-headline" id="Entering_the_Forest_and_searching">Entering the Forest and searching</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Jacob%27s_sibling?veaction=edit&amp;section=40" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Entering the Forest and searching">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Jacob%27s_sibling?action=edit&amp;section=40" title="Edit section: Entering the Forest and searching"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h5>
<p>The three then went into the <a href="/wiki/Forbidden_Forest" title="Forbidden Forest">Forbidden Forest</a>, heading to the <a href="/wiki/Forest_Grove" title="Forest Grove">Forest Grove</a>. Unbeknownst to them however, they were being followed from a close distance behind them by their friend, <a href="/wiki/Rowan_Khanna" title="Rowan Khanna">Rowan Khanna</a>. Eventually they reached the Forest Grove, and began to search for clues. 
The search led them to the discovery of an <a href="/wiki/Opal_Necklace" title="Opal Necklace">Opal Necklace</a> belonging to the <a href="/wiki/Dark_Wizard" title="Dark Wizard">dark witch</a> <a href="/wiki/Patricia_Rakepick" title="Patricia Rakepick">Patricia Rakepick</a>. As they were discussing the discovery, a swarm of <a href="/wiki/Dementor" title="Dementor">Dementors</a> showed up and circled the group. The group then began casting the <a href="/wiki/Patronus_Charm" title="Patronus Charm">Patronus Charm</a> to protect themselves, however they were unsuccessful as they were overwhelmed with the sheer number of present Dementors. 
</p>
<h5><span id="Duelling_with_Rakepick_and_Rowan's_death"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="Duelling_with_Rakepick_and_Rowan.27s_death">Duelling with Rakepick and Rowan's death</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Jacob%27s_sibling?veaction=edit&amp;section=41" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Duelling with Rakepick and Rowan&#039;s death">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Jacob%27s_sibling?action=edit&amp;section=41" title="Edit section: Duelling with Rakepick and Rowan&#039;s death"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h5>

<h5><span id="Rowan's_memory"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="Rowan.27s_memory">Rowan's memory</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Jacob%27s_sibling?veaction=edit&amp;section=42" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Rowan&#039;s memory">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Jacob%27s_sibling?action=edit&amp;section=42" title="Edit section: Rowan&#039;s memory"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h5>

<p>Rowan's death struck the entirety of Hogwarts as an extremely painful event and their death was mourned by all of their friends, who found it difficult to come to terms with their tragic loss. In particular to suffer were Jacob's sibling, <a href="/wiki/Ben_Copper" title="Ben Copper">Ben Copper</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Merula_Snyde" title="Merula Snyde">Merula Snyde</a>, all of whom had watched them die. A memorial service took place in the Great Hall, where Professor Dumbledore gave a brief eulogy of Rowan's life, aspirations and sacrifice before leading the students and staff in raising their wands as a tribute to Rowan.<sup id="cite_ref-death_75-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-death-75">&#91;75&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h5><span class="mw-headline" id="Forming_the_Circle_of_Khanna">Forming the <a href="/wiki/Circle_of_Khanna" title="Circle of Khanna">Circle of Khanna</a></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Jacob%27s_sibling?veaction=edit&amp;section=43" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Forming the Circle of Khanna">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Jacob%27s_sibling?action=edit&amp;section=43" title="Edit section: Forming the Circle of Khanna"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h5>
<p>Rowan's murder prompted a vengeful Jacob's sibling, Merula and Ben to form their own secret organisation to combat the malevolent "R" and get revenge on Rakepick for what she had done.<sup id="cite_ref-death_75-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-death-75">&#91;75&#93;</a></sup> The organisation was named <a href="/wiki/Circle_of_Khanna" title="Circle of Khanna">Circle of Khanna</a> in memory of Rowan with Jacob's sibling taking up the role of leader.<sup id="cite_ref-hm620_13-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-hm620-13">&#91;13&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h5><span id="The_group's_first_tasks"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="The_group.27s_first_tasks">The group's first tasks</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Jacob%27s_sibling?veaction=edit&amp;section=44" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: The group&#039;s first tasks">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Jacob%27s_sibling?action=edit&amp;section=44" title="Edit section: The group&#039;s first tasks"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h5>
<p>After the forming of the group, all members with the exception of Jacob's sibling and <a href="/wiki/Penny_Haywood" title="Penny Haywood">Penny Haywood</a> split into smaller groups based on their abilities and interests. Each group went into a different location in or near <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Castle" title="Hogwarts Castle">Hogwarts Castle</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-hm620_13-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-hm620-13">&#91;13&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>One such group consisted of Merula Snyde and <a href="/wiki/Ismelda_Murk" title="Ismelda Murk">Ismelda Murk</a>, who went to the <a href="/wiki/Classroom_3C" title="Classroom 3C">Defence Against the Dark Arts classroom</a> along with <a href="/wiki/Beatrice_Haywood" title="Beatrice Haywood">Beatrice Haywood</a>, who found about the Circle of Khanna from the two and showed interest in joining. The three studied <a href="/wiki/Curse" title="Curse">curses</a> and dark artefacts, as this knowledge could be useful for entering a <a href="/wiki/Cursed_Vaults" title="Cursed Vaults">Cursed Vault</a>. Some time later, Jacob's sibling came to the classroom to see how the group was doing and was surprised to find Beatrice, as the group was meant to be secret. Not long after, Penny came to the classroom, as she heard from <a href="/wiki/Cedric_Diggory" title="Cedric Diggory">Cedric Diggory</a> that Beatrice had gone there. Penny and Beatrice made up after their feud which had started in the beginning of the school year.<sup id="cite_ref-hm620_13-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-hm620-13">&#91;13&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Another group gathered in the <a href="/wiki/Classroom_1B" title="Classroom 1B">Transfiguration classroom</a>. This group consisted of Jae Kim, <a href="/wiki/Nymphadora_Tonks" title="Nymphadora Tonks">Nymphadora Tonks</a>, Tulip Karasu, and <a href="/wiki/Andre_Egwu" title="Andre Egwu">Andre Egwu</a>, and focused on disguises and stealth. When Jae went to the Transfiguration classroom, he was followed by <a href="/wiki/Fred_Weasley" title="Fred Weasley">Fred</a> and <a href="/wiki/George_Weasley" title="George Weasley">George Weasley</a>, who were interested in joining the Circle of Khanna. Jae went to the Defence Against the Dark Arts classroom to ask if the twins could join the Circle of Khanna as well. As he entered the classroom, he saw Beatrice accepted as a member, and came to the conclusion that Fred and George could join as well as they were also younger students. Jacob's sibling followed Jae to the Transfiguration classroom, where the other students explained their plans to Jacob's sibling.<sup id="cite_ref-hm620_13-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-hm620-13">&#91;13&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p><a href="/wiki/Charles_Weasley" title="Charles Weasley">Charles Weasley</a>, <a href="/wiki/Liz_Tuttle" title="Liz Tuttle">Liz Tuttle</a>, and Barnaby Lee gathered on the school grounds where <a href="/wiki/Care_of_Magical_Creatures" title="Care of Magical Creatures">Care of Magical Creatures</a> classes were held. The three decided to train marine creatures, specifically <a href="/wiki/Plimpy" title="Plimpy">Plimpies</a> and <a href="/wiki/Murtlap" title="Murtlap">Murtlaps</a>, as they could be helpful for finding the final Cursed Vault. However, as one of the Murtlaps, Hester, went missing, Charlie went to look for Jacob's sibling for help, and found them in the Transfiguration classroom. The two went back to the school grounds where they looked for Hester until they were able to find her.<sup id="cite_ref-hm620_13-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-hm620-13">&#91;13&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p><a href="/wiki/Talbott_Winger" title="Talbott Winger">Talbott Winger</a> and <a href="/wiki/Badeea_Ali" title="Badeea Ali">Badeea Ali</a> teamed up to make a <a href="/wiki/Map_of_the_Great_Lake" title="Map of the Great Lake">Map of the Great Lake</a>, which could be helpful in figuring out the location of the final Cursed Vault. Talbott scoured the area for any landmarks and Badeea painted the lake. The two went to the <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Library" title="Hogwarts Library">Hogwarts Library</a> in order to work on their painting. Some time later, Jacob's sibling came to the Library and saw the two. Badeea and Talbott explained their plan to Jacob's sibling. However, librarian <a href="/wiki/Irma_Pince" title="Irma Pince">Irma Pince</a> did not approve of the students talking and painting in the library, and as such, they decided to continue on the <a href="/wiki/Training_Grounds" title="Training Grounds">Training Grounds</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-hm620_13-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-hm620-13">&#91;13&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Ben Copper decided to invite his friend Cedric Diggory to the Circle of Khanna, seeing that his good <a href="/wiki/Duelling" title="Duelling">duelling</a> skills could help the group. The two, along with <a href="/wiki/Diego_Caplan" title="Diego Caplan">Diego Caplan</a>, went to the Training Grounds in order to train duelling spells. After the three had trained for hours, Jacob's sibling, who had agreed to meet Talbott and Badeea in the area, spotted the three students.<sup id="cite_ref-hm620_13-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-hm620-13">&#91;13&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Physical_description">Physical description</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Jacob%27s_sibling?veaction=edit&amp;section=45" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Physical description">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Jacob%27s_sibling?action=edit&amp;section=45" title="Edit section: Physical description"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Jacob's sibling's physical appearance is mostly a mystery. However, it is known that they are slender and of average height. They usually wore the traditional school robes.<sup id="cite_ref-76" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-76">&#91;76&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Personality_and_traits">Personality and traits</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Jacob%27s_sibling?veaction=edit&amp;section=46" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Personality and traits">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Jacob%27s_sibling?action=edit&amp;section=46" title="Edit section: Personality and traits"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Jacob's sibling was extremely compassionate and, while not as perceptive as their best friend <a href="/wiki/Rowan_Khanna" title="Rowan Khanna">Rowan Khanna</a>, still possessed a keen intellect. They were very talented and academically competent, being skilled in a number of magical fields, receiving much praise from the majority of their teachers, often responding to such praise by grinning. They were also skilled at coming up with things quickly and lying effectively, though they never lied if they could avoid doing so.
</p><p>Thanks to the actions of their brother <a href="/wiki/Jacob_(Hogwarts_student)" title="Jacob (Hogwarts student)">Jacob</a>, they were often the subject of dislike and apprehension and had difficulty making friends. They were unfazed by this, and instead chose to be nonjudgmental and accepting of those around them. Jacob's sibling was very loyal to their friends. They were always eager to help them, such as when they helped <a href="/wiki/Ben_Copper" title="Ben Copper">Ben Copper</a> overcome his fear of <a href="/wiki/Flight" title="Flight">riding</a> a <a href="/wiki/Broomstick" title="Broomstick">broomstick</a> and convinced him to stop isolating in a closet, and when they acted like they could see through <a href="/wiki/Talbott_Winger" title="Talbott Winger">Talbott Winger</a>'s apparent lack of desire for friendship in general. Jacob's sibling was also very empathetic, as best shown by how they treated Talbott Winger, <a href="/wiki/Barnaby_Lee" title="Barnaby Lee">Barnaby Lee</a>, <a href="/wiki/Ismelda_Murk" title="Ismelda Murk">Ismelda Murk</a> and <a href="/wiki/Merula_Snyde" title="Merula Snyde">Merula Snyde</a>.
</p><p>Jacob's sibling was very courageous, as they stood up to <a href="/wiki/Merula_Snyde" title="Merula Snyde">Merula Snyde</a>, <a href="/wiki/Severus_Snape" title="Severus Snape">Severus Snape</a>, and even <a href="/wiki/Fenrir_Greyback" title="Fenrir Greyback">Fenrir Greyback</a>, and sought out the <a href="/wiki/Cursed_Vaults" title="Cursed Vaults">Cursed Vaults</a> relentlessly in spite of the teachers warning them not to. Jacob's sibling believed that in finding the vaults, they would gain new knowledge and treasure, but most of their drive came from wanting to find their brother, whom they loved unconditionally.
</p><p>Though never a defining trait, Jacob's sibling had a cheeky and occasionally sarcastic sense of humour, such as when they encouraged Ben to stop isolating and added <i>"&#8230;though the skulls might miss you"</i>. In their first year, Merula told them they were unwanted to which they replied <i>"You're the one everyone wishes would just go away"</i>. When <a href="/wiki/Chiara_Lobosca" title="Chiara Lobosca">Chiara Lobosca</a> accused them of having ulterior motives for chatting, they replied, <i>"It's possible my ulterior motive might be trying to get to know you better."</i>
</p><p>In their <a href="/wiki/Second_year" title="Second year">second year</a>, Jacob's sibling developed a passion for <a href="/wiki/Quidditch" title="Quidditch">Quidditch</a> as well as possessing a natural talent for it, which caused them to bond with <a href="/wiki/Skye_Parkin" title="Skye Parkin">Skye Parkin</a>. Their skills were best served as being a <a href="/wiki/Chaser" title="Chaser">Chaser</a>.
</p><p>For Jacob's sibling, their years at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts</a> have been a journey of growth and renewal. Despite being a lonely child who frequently watched their brother closely with immense concern and kept to themselves, Jacob's sibling has found a family at school that can relate to them and give them new purpose and meaning. Their social skills improved phenomenally during their magical education. Indeed, they tend to form the closest relationships (positive and negative) with students who bring out their own strengths: being around <a href="/wiki/Rowan_Khanna" title="Rowan Khanna">Rowan Khanna</a> has made them more intellectual, being around <a href="/wiki/Ben_Copper" title="Ben Copper">Ben Copper</a> has made them more compassionate, being around <a href="/wiki/Penny_Haywood" title="Penny Haywood">Penny Haywood</a> has made them more extroverted, being around <a href="/wiki/Nymphadora_Tonks" title="Nymphadora Tonks">Nymphadora Tonks</a> has made them more unafraid of being themselves, being around <a href="/wiki/Skye_Parkin" title="Skye Parkin">Skye Parkin</a> has made them more sports-minded, and being around <a href="/wiki/Merula_Snyde" title="Merula Snyde">Merula Snyde</a> has made them more empathetic and less vulnerable. Jacob's sibling tends to have some of those effects on their friends in turn, as people like Haywood and <a href="/wiki/Talbott_Winger" title="Talbott Winger">Talbott Winger</a> have pointed out, they are both a safe place and dependable source when their friends are vulnerable, endangered, conflicted, or all of the above.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Magical_abilities_and_skills">Magical abilities and skills</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Jacob%27s_sibling?veaction=edit&amp;section=47" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Jacob%27s_sibling?action=edit&amp;section=47" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>

<ul><li><b><a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">Magical</a> aptitude</b>: An academically strong student, Jacob's sibling quickly achieved such good results in their schoolwork that they received praise from most of their teachers, with <a href="/wiki/Albus_Dumbledore" title="Albus Dumbledore">Albus Dumbledore</a>, arguably the greatest wizard of all time, openly acknowledging their magical prowess to be exemplary. They demonstrated tremendous ability and proved a cleverer student than most of their classmates, quickly mastering and becoming the best in most of the fields of magic taught at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts</a>. Indeed, their formidable magical power allowed them to achieve <a href="/wiki/Ordinary_Wizarding_Level" title="Ordinary Wizarding Level">O.W.L.s</a> in all their subjects, to apprehend the immensely savage <a href="/wiki/Fenrir_Greyback" title="Fenrir Greyback">Fenrir Greyback</a> alongside the <a href="/wiki/Werewolf_Capture_Unit" title="Werewolf Capture Unit">Werewolf Capture Unit</a>, to assist <a href="/wiki/Auror" title="Auror">Auror</a> <a href="/wiki/Alastor_Moody" title="Alastor Moody">Alastor Moody</a> in tracking down the malevolent organisation '<a href="/wiki/R" title="R">R</a>', to come out as the triumphant winner of the prestigious <a href="/wiki/All-Wizard_Tournament" title="All-Wizard Tournament">All-Wizard Tournament</a>, to master the extremely powerful and difficult <a href="/wiki/Patronus_Charm" title="Patronus Charm">Patronus Charm</a> by only their fourth year, and to save Hogwarts from the menacing threat of the <a href="/wiki/Cursed_Vaults" title="Cursed Vaults">Cursed Vaults</a> on numerous occasions.</li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Love" title="Love">Love</a></b>: Jacob's sibling loved <a href="/wiki/Jacob_(Hogwarts_student)" title="Jacob (Hogwarts student)">their older brother</a> unconditionally, being devastated when he went missing from home and shedding tears over his sudden disappearance. Their love for him spurred them to take action and rescue him by obsessively searching for the <a href="/wiki/Cursed_Vaults" title="Cursed Vaults">Cursed Vaults</a> for many years, despite their teachers explicitly forbidding them to do so under the threat of expulsion from <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts</a> and despite the extreme danger in which Jacob's sibling repeatedly placed themselves under while seeking out each of the Cursed Vaults. Although their love was at its strongest when directed towards their brother, as he was the driving force that kept them persisting until they found him, their love also extended towards their friends, whom they were always willing to help or assist in whatever way they could whenever their friends were vulnerable, endangered or conflicted, which, in turn, resulted in Jacob's sibling developing an exceedingly compassionate nature. Their compassion even enabled them to sympathise with those whom they were initially antagonistic with, such as <a href="/wiki/Merula_Snyde" title="Merula Snyde">Merula Snyde</a>, <a href="/wiki/Barnaby_Lee" title="Barnaby Lee">Barnaby Lee</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Ismelda_Murk" title="Ismelda Murk">Ismelda Murk</a>.</li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Wandless_magic" title="Wandless magic">Wandless</a> and <a href="/wiki/Nonverbal_spell" title="Nonverbal spell">nonverbal magic</a></b>: Jacob's sibling was apparently quite capable in casting nonverbal spells, as they subdued a <a href="/wiki/Hungarian_Horntail" title="Hungarian Horntail">Hungarian Horntail</a> using the <a href="/wiki/Sleeping_Charm" title="Sleeping Charm">Sleeping Charm</a>, duelled against <a href="/wiki/Patricia_Rakepick" title="Patricia Rakepick">Patricia Rakepick</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Buried_Vault" title="Buried Vault">Portrait Vault</a> and later again in the <a href="/wiki/Forest_Grove" title="Forest Grove">Forest Grove</a>, all without uttering a word. They also demonstrated impressive wandless magic when using the <a href="/wiki/Butterfly-conjuring_spell" title="Butterfly-conjuring spell">Butterfly-conjuring spell</a> during their imitation of the <a href="/wiki/Beauxbatons_Academy_of_Magic" title="Beauxbatons Academy of Magic">Beauxbatons</a> dance routine, a performance that <a href="/wiki/Olympe_Maxime" title="Olympe Maxime">Madame Maxime</a> ultimately deemed magnificent.</li></ul>

<ul><li><b><a href="/wiki/Potion" title="Potion">Potions</a></b>: Jacob's sibling was superbly talented in potion-making, as they achieved an '<a href="/wiki/Outstanding" title="Outstanding">Outstanding</a>' on their O.W.L. exam<sup id="cite_ref-77" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-77">&#91;77&#93;</a></sup> to be able to advance to the N.E.W.T.-level <a href="/wiki/Potions_(class)" title="Potions (class)">Potions</a> class taught by Severus Snape,<sup id="cite_ref-HM63p_70-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HM63p-70">&#91;70&#93;</a></sup> thus displaying their great proficiency for potion-making. They were able to brew everything from basic to moderately difficult concoctions to the desired effect, notably brewing a <a href="/wiki/Fire-Breathing_Potion" title="Fire-Breathing Potion">Fire-Breathing Potion</a> flawlessly after only seeing the <a href="/wiki/Potions_Master" title="Potions Master">Potions Master</a> do it once. Indeed, despite his personal dislike of the student, <a href="/wiki/Severus_Snape" title="Severus Snape">Severus Snape</a> himself admitted that this individual had an undeniable gift for potions and <a href="/wiki/Penny_Haywood" title="Penny Haywood">Penny Haywood</a>, arguably the best student of the subject, praised Jacob's sibling's talent in potion-making, going so far as to suggest that they consider a career as a <a href="/wiki/Potioneer" title="Potioneer">Potioneer</a>.</li></ul>

<ul><li><b><a href="/wiki/Charm" title="Charm">Charms</a></b>: Jacob's sibling was known to be exceptionally skilled in charm-work, as they achieved either an '<a href="/wiki/Exceeds_Expectations" title="Exceeds Expectations">Exceeds Expectations</a>' or '<a href="/wiki/Outstanding" title="Outstanding">Outstanding</a>' on their O.W.L. exam to be able to advance to N.E.W.T.-level Charms.<sup id="cite_ref-HM61c_66-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HM61c-66">&#91;66&#93;</a></sup> In their very first lesson, they cast the best <a href="/wiki/Wand-Lighting_Charm" title="Wand-Lighting Charm">Wand-Lighting Charm</a> that Professor Flitwick had seen from a first-year in a long time. They were usually the first to master any new spell Professor Flitwick taught (such as the <a href="/wiki/Disarming_Charm" title="Disarming Charm">Disarming Charm</a>, the <a href="/wiki/Levitation_Charm" title="Levitation Charm">Levitation Charm</a>, and the <a href="/wiki/Unlocking_Charm" title="Unlocking Charm">Unlocking Charm</a>) with little difficulty, and by their second year, they were capable of casting the <a href="/wiki/Shrinking_Charm" title="Shrinking Charm">Shrinking Charm</a> to such an effect that they became the size of an insect and thus, virtually invisible to the naked eye. They could also just as easily reverse the effect with an <a href="/wiki/Engorgement_Charm" title="Engorgement Charm">Engorgement Charm</a> without experiencing any side effects. During class, Professor Flitwick would often commend them for their 'fine technique' and even request that they assist him in demonstrating how to cast spells properly for their fellow classmates. By their fourth year, they produced a <a href="/wiki/Shield_Charm" title="Shield Charm">Shield Charm</a> that was strong enough to deflect a <a href="/wiki/Blasting_Curse" title="Blasting Curse">Blasting Curse</a> from the much older and more experienced Patricia Rakepick, visibly impressing her. They even performed an advanced <a href="/wiki/Memory_Charm" title="Memory Charm">Memory Charm</a> to erase the <a href="/wiki/Memory" title="Memory">memory</a> of Merula Snyde when she overhead important information about <a href="/wiki/Talbott_Winger" title="Talbott Winger">Talbott Winger</a>. Furthermore, they learned how to conjure a fully-corporeal <a href="/wiki/Patronus_Charm" title="Patronus Charm">Patronus Charm</a> by their fourth year, an outstanding feat for a Hogwarts student. Indeed, the <a href="/wiki/Charms_Master" title="Charms Master">Charms Master</a> himself openly acknowledged Jacob's sibling to be one of his most talented students.</li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Defence_Against_the_Dark_Arts" title="Defence Against the Dark Arts">Defence Against the Dark Arts</a></b>: When they were tutored, alongside the rest of their year, by the famous <a href="/wiki/Patricia_Rakepick" title="Patricia Rakepick">Patricia Rakepick</a>, in Defence Against the Dark Arts, Jacob's sibling soon became the best student of the subject, seamlessly mastering <a href="/wiki/Salvio_hexia" title="Salvio hexia">Hex Deflection</a> and proving quite adept in repelling the dark creatures of the lessons. Rakepick herself praised their abilities in this field, with them having achieved either an '<a href="/wiki/Exceeds_Expectations" title="Exceeds Expectations">Exceeds Expectations</a> or '<a href="/wiki/Outstanding" title="Outstanding">Outstanding</a>' on their <a href="/wiki/Ordinary_Wizarding_Level" title="Ordinary Wizarding Level">O.W.L.</a> exam to be able to study the subject further at <a href="/wiki/Nastily_Exhausting_Wizarding_Test" title="Nastily Exhausting Wizarding Test">N.E.W.T.</a>-level.<sup id="cite_ref-:0_72-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:0-72">&#91;72&#93;</a></sup> They also successfully learned many defensive and offensive spells during their DADA education, including spells taught by their classmates. For an example of their defensive skills, in their <a href="/wiki/Third_year" title="Third year">Third year</a>, they were successfully able to subdue a <a href="/wiki/Boggart" title="Boggart">Boggart</a> in the form of <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a> with the <a href="/wiki/Boggart-Banishing_Spell" title="Boggart-Banishing Spell">Boggart-Banishing Spell</a>. Indeed, <a href="/wiki/Alastor_Moody" title="Alastor Moody">Alastor Moody</a>, arguably the greatest Auror of all time, expressed his pride in Jacob's sibling, praising them for their Auror-like mindset while <a href="/wiki/Nymphadora_Tonks" title="Nymphadora Tonks">Nymphadora Tonks</a>, who would later grow up to become a successful <a href="/wiki/Auror" title="Auror">Auror</a>, suggested that Jacob's sibling consider the career, given that they had experience hunting down <a href="/wiki/Dark_wizard" class="mw-redirect" title="Dark wizard">Dark wizards</a> and clearly displayed familiarity with the job and training.</li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Dark_Arts" title="Dark Arts">Dark Arts</a></b>: Though seldom a practitioner of the Dark Arts, Jacob's sibling was nonetheless surprisingly adept in its usage, being able to successfully cast a vast array of <a href="/wiki/Dark_charm" title="Dark charm">dark charms</a> such as the <a href="/wiki/Hex" title="Hex">hex</a> <i><a href="/wiki/Densaugeo" title="Densaugeo">Densaugeo</a></i>, the <a href="/wiki/Sea_Urchin_Jinx" title="Sea Urchin Jinx">Sea Urchin Jinx</a>, the <a href="/wiki/Knockback_Jinx" title="Knockback Jinx">Knockback Jinx</a>, the <a href="/wiki/Curse_of_the_Bogies" title="Curse of the Bogies">Curse of the Bogies</a>, the <a href="/wiki/Ear-Shrivelling_Curse" title="Ear-Shrivelling Curse">Ear-Shrivelling Curse</a>, the <a href="/wiki/Jelly-Legs_Curse" title="Jelly-Legs Curse">Jelly-Legs Curse</a>, the <a href="/wiki/Conjunctivitis_Curse" title="Conjunctivitis Curse">Conjunctivitis Curse</a>, the <a href="/wiki/Reductor_Curse" title="Reductor Curse">Reductor Curse</a> and the <a href="/wiki/Blasting_Curse" title="Blasting Curse">Blasting Curse</a>. They were, however, horrified when Patricia Rakepick used the <a href="/wiki/Cruciatus_Curse" title="Cruciatus Curse">Cruciatus Curse</a> on a Dark Wizard in <a href="/wiki/Knockturn_Alley" title="Knockturn Alley">Knockturn Alley</a>, rejecting her offer to teach the curse to them and adamantly refusing to ever use it on anyone.</li></ul>

<ul><li><b><a href="/wiki/Duelling" title="Duelling">Duelling</a></b>: Jacob's sibling quickly became a formidable duelist for their age, outclassing the likes of the more experienced Merula Snyde and even <a href="/wiki/William_Weasley" title="William Weasley">Bill Weasley</a>, both of whom were of one of the most skilled students at Hogwarts, with the latter having two years of education over them. Though usually coming out triumphant in most duels against their peers, they eres unable to beat <a href="/wiki/Ben_Copper" title="Ben Copper">Ben Copper</a> in a duel to the death, who trounced them but was quickly defeated when Patricia Rakepick came for the rescue, striking him with a <a href="/wiki/Blasting_Curse" title="Blasting Curse">Blasting Curse</a>. This individual's skills were already so great by their first year that they could even hold off <a href="/wiki/Fenrir_Greyback" title="Fenrir Greyback">Fenrir Greyback</a> while he was in his werewolf form, with some swift spell-casting. By their fifth year, they were able to out-duel a dark witch in the Knockturn Alley, despite being there for the first time and defeated an adult wizard named <a href="/wiki/Alistair_Fidgen" title="Alistair Fidgen">Alistair Fidgen</a>, when he threatened their friend <a href="/wiki/Jae_Kim" title="Jae Kim">Jae Kim</a>. However, they were no match for Patricia Rakepick, as she easily subdued them before they could even cast a spell in their fifth year and again when they attempted to attack her in their sixth year. They also acknowledged the vast gap between them and <a href="/wiki/Albus_Dumbledore" title="Albus Dumbledore">Dumbledore</a>, noting that it would be suicide for them to try to infiltrate his office. This individual was able to out-duel the more experienced <a href="/wiki/Peter_Pettigrew" title="Peter Pettigrew">Peter Pettigrew</a> after he revealed himself to them. Their duelling aptitude was also clearly staggering in that they were capable of facing and subduing some of the most dangerous beasts in the world, notably being capable enough to overpower the <a href="/wiki/Forest_Vault_Acromantula" title="Forest Vault Acromantula">Forest Vault Acromantula</a> (with even the formidable <a href="/wiki/Rubeus_Hagrid" title="Rubeus Hagrid">Rubeus Hagrid</a> being seemingly unwilling to contend with the beast), to single-handedly defeat a <a href="/wiki/Hungarian_Horntail" title="Hungarian Horntail">Hungarian Horntail</a> (with even the incredibly powerful <a href="/wiki/Patricia_Rakepick" title="Patricia Rakepick">Patricia Rakepick</a> being incapable of performing such a feat during her youth, despite having received assistance from accomplices) and to hold off the incredibly savage <a href="/wiki/Chimaera" title="Chimaera">Chimaera</a> long enough for others to come to their rescue on more than one occasion. By their sixth year, they were even able to contend with <a href="/wiki/Alastor_Moody" title="Alastor Moody">Alastor Moody</a>, who was considered to be the most famous <a href="/wiki/Auror" title="Auror">Auror</a> of all time and an extremely skilled duelist, in a duel training with him. Although they themselves acknowledged that Moody wasn't fighting seriously, which Moody himself admitted as he was only trying to assess their skills, Moody still acknowledged their duelling prowess, even going so far as to say that they could give some of his Aurors difficulty in besting them. Their ability to fight both skilled duellists and dark wizards showed them to have considerable proficiency in <a href="/wiki/Martial_magic" title="Martial magic">martial magic</a>.</li></ul>

<ul><li><b>Detective skills</b>: Jacob's sibling had tremendous skills in tracking and detection, since they tracked down their brother <a href="/wiki/Jacob_(Hogwarts_student)" title="Jacob (Hogwarts student)">Jacob</a> to the <a href="/wiki/Buried_Vault" title="Buried Vault">Portrait Vault</a> after years of skilfully employing both inductive and deductive reasoning in their dogged pursuit of the <a href="/wiki/Cursed_Vaults" title="Cursed Vaults">Cursed Vaults</a>.</li>
<li><b>Teaching skills</b>: Jacob's sibling was an excellent teacher of Charms, as they often tutored struggling students. This was even acknowledged by Professor Flitwick and Professor Tofty.</li>
<li><b>Leadership skills</b>: Jacob's sibling was shown to be a very accomplished and talented leader, as they were able to found and effectively lead the <a href="/wiki/Circle_of_Khanna" title="Circle of Khanna">Circle of Khanna</a> against the malevolent organisation '<a href="/wiki/R" title="R">R</a>', productively coordinating the group's efforts and inspiring fierce loyalty from them all.</li>
<li><b>Indomitable willpower</b>: The will of Jacob's sibling was extremely strong, and while not above self-doubt, they have never been known to give up on anything or anyone, tirelessly seeking the <a href="/wiki/Cursed_Vaults" title="Cursed Vaults">Cursed Vaults</a> for the sake of <a href="/wiki/Jacob_(Hogwarts_student)" title="Jacob (Hogwarts student)">their brother</a>. Their willpower gave them great courage, enough to stand in the face of <a href="/wiki/Merula_Snyde" title="Merula Snyde">Merula</a>'s antics, <a href="/wiki/Severus_Snape" title="Severus Snape">Snape</a>'s cruelty, and <a href="/wiki/Fenrir_Greyback" title="Fenrir Greyback">Greyback</a>'s murderous intent.</li></ul>

<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Family">Family</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Jacob%27s_sibling?veaction=edit&amp;section=50" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Family">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Jacob%27s_sibling?action=edit&amp;section=50" title="Edit section: Family"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<div class="quote">
<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;Harry Potter: Hogwarts Mystery"><b>Jacob</b>: "<i>Thank you for not giving up on me.</i>"</span></dd>
<dd><b>Jacob's sibling</b>: "<i>I could have never given up on you. You're my older brother.</i>"</dd>
<dd>&#8212; <a href="/wiki/Jacob_(Hogwarts_student)" title="Jacob (Hogwarts student)">Jacob</a> and his younger sibling, after reuniting.<sup class="noprint"><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter:_Hogwarts_Mystery" title="Harry Potter: Hogwarts Mystery">[src]</a></sup></dd></dl>
</div>
<p><a href="/wiki/Jacob_(Hogwarts_student)" title="Jacob (Hogwarts student)">Jacob</a> was this individual's older brother. It is implied that they had a rather close and affectionate relationship with him during their early childhood, as they pretended to duel with <a href="/wiki/Liquorice_Wand" title="Liquorice Wand">Liquorice Wands</a> with Jacob when they were younger and even stole <a href="/wiki/Sickle" title="Sickle">Sickles</a> from their mother's coat pockets at his behest. When Jacob came home after his <a href="/wiki/First_year" title="First year">first year</a> of <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts</a>, he tried to teach them all the magic he learned at Hogwarts, of which they were unable to perform any of it, but it remained one of the three possibilities of their happiest memory used to cast the <a href="/wiki/Patronus_Charm" title="Patronus Charm">Patronus Charm</a>. After Jacob went mysteriously missing following his expulsion from Hogwarts, his younger sibling was left devastated and mourned his disappearance with their mother. When assaulted by a <a href="/wiki/Dementor" title="Dementor">Dementor</a>, they are forced to relive their worst memory: their mother sobbing uncontrollably upon learning Jacob's disappearance.
</p><p>Jacob's disappearance also had major repercussions on his sibling outside of <a href="/wiki/Jacob%27s_family" title="Jacob&#39;s family">family</a> tragedy. Due to his morbid obsession with the <a href="/wiki/Cursed_Vaults" title="Cursed Vaults">Cursed Vaults</a>, subsequent expulsion and apparent initiation into the <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a>, Jacob inadvertently established a rather infamous reputation and was perceived as insane by the wizarding world. Being of his kin, his younger sibling, too, was largely viewed by suspicion, distrust and even hate (with <a href="/wiki/Severus_Snape" title="Severus Snape">Snape</a> and <a href="/wiki/Merula_Snyde" title="Merula Snyde">Merula</a> being among their biggest detractors), which thus affected their early years of schooling and ability to make a first good impression on people. Despite all this, however, Jacob's sibling never felt bitter or resentful towards the negative publicity that they received, caused by their brother, and continued to feel nothing but unconditional love for Jacob. Their early years at Hogwarts was marked by agony over the mysterious whereabouts of their brother, thus leading them to resolve to find and rescue him no matter what.
</p><p>Indeed, this strong, unconditional love for their brother became their primary point of obsession with seeking the <a href="/wiki/Cursed_Vaults" title="Cursed Vaults">Cursed Vaults</a>, inciting them to sometimes behave recklessly and enact dangerous schemes in their goal to save Jacob, such as igniting the cloak of a member of faculty and fighting a troll in the Hogwarts grounds. They would also defend Jacob's honour against accusations and mockery when necessary, refusing to believe the perpetuated rumour that he was insane and had joined the <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a>.
</p><p>After finally rescuing and reuniting their brother in <a href="/wiki/1989" title="1989">1989</a>, Jacob's sibling was shown to be incredibly relieved and happy at seeing their brother safe and sound.
</p>

<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Rowan_Khanna">Rowan Khanna</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Jacob%27s_sibling?veaction=edit&amp;section=55" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Rowan Khanna">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Jacob%27s_sibling?action=edit&amp;section=55" title="Edit section: Rowan Khanna"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<p>Jacob's sibling met <a href="/wiki/Rowan_Khanna" title="Rowan Khanna">Rowan Khanna</a> at Diagon Alley. The two became best friends at school. They both bonded over the fact that both of them did not truly have anyone else going into Hogwarts &#8212; Jacob's sibling was regarded as insane thanks to their brother's actions, and Rowan was socially inept and mostly talked about trees. They continuously helped each other get through both everyday and life-threatening situations. When Rowan died, they were devastated, and it was hard for them to get over the loss.
</p>

They cared deeply for his well-being, as they became very worried when he went missing, and showed their loyalty to him by infiltrating <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor_Tower" title="Gryffindor Tower">Gryffindor Tower</a> merely because it may or may not lead to him. They also helped Ben to get over his fear of flying.
</p>

<p>Jacob's sibling admired <a href="/wiki/Penny_Haywood" title="Penny Haywood">Penny Haywood</a> for her popularity, kindness, and overall competence. She treated them cordially, but they got her attention by standing up to Merula Snyde. They brewed a potion together to neutralise Mrs Norris. Penny was grateful for Jacob's sibling putting her in a life-threatening situation, as she loved excitement more than they did. She always seemed to know what was going on at Hogwarts, which made her very useful as well. They were surprised when she was close to <a href="/wiki/Talbott_Winger" title="Talbott Winger">Talbott Winger</a>.
</p>

<p><a href="/wiki/Merula_Snyde" title="Merula Snyde">Merula Snyde</a> became the archrival of Jacob's sibling almost immediately upon arriving at Hogwarts. Merula came from a supremacist family and desired to surpass all others, thus she considered herself superior to virtually everyone. She bullied Jacob's sibling for allegedly being cursed, though they did not truly hate Merula and merely wished she would leave them alone. They first met when Merula tried to force <a href="/wiki/Rowan_Khanna" title="Rowan Khanna">Rowan Khanna</a> into claiming she was the most powerful witch in the <a href="/wiki/Wizarding_world" title="Wizarding world">wizarding world</a>, and she baffled them by already knowing who they were. Jacob's sibling learned she was born of two <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a>, and while it made them realise why she was a bully, it did not soften their mutual enmity at all.
</p><p>They were forced to resort to violence when Merula antagonised them and again when she antagonised <a href="/wiki/Ben_Copper" title="Ben Copper">Ben Copper</a>. During their first round, Merula bested them with a <a href="/wiki/Trip_Jinx" title="Trip Jinx">Trip Jinx</a>, but they bested her the second time via the <a href="/wiki/Disarming_Charm" title="Disarming Charm">Disarming Charm</a>. They were forced to challenge her a third time in their second year, and while Merula agreed to no more duelling, she refused to stay off his tail. They were also enemies because they both sought to find the <a href="/wiki/Cursed_Vaults" title="Cursed Vaults">Cursed Vaults</a>, albeit for different reasons. When Jacob's sibling arrived with <a href="/wiki/Rowan_Khanna" title="Rowan Khanna">Rowan Khanna</a> and another friend, she used the Trip Jinx against all three of them, confusing Jacob's sibling who did not understand why she wanted to solve the mystery as much as he did. She believed they were going to meet a sticky end someday.
</p><p>Despite being enemies, they began getting along better when <a href="/wiki/Patricia_Rakepick" title="Patricia Rakepick">Patricia Rakepick</a> recruited the both of them as apprentices.
</p>

<p>Jacob's sibling generally had a positive relationship with the staff at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts</a>, who were all impressed by their academic prowess. However, they were also looked at with apprehension because of Jacob, and was looked at with particular apprehension for their wrongdoings. They generally got along with <a href="/wiki/Filius_Flitwick" title="Filius Flitwick">Filius Flitwick</a>, though he hesitated teaching them the <a href="/wiki/Trip_Jinx" title="Trip Jinx">Trip Jinx</a>. They also had a good relationship with <a href="/wiki/Rubeus_Hagrid" title="Rubeus Hagrid">Rubeus Hagrid</a> and frequently came to his rescue. Their worst relationship, inevitably, was with <a href="/wiki/Severus_Snape" title="Severus Snape">Severus Snape</a>, who always treated them with contempt and frequently looked for reasons to take House Points from them. Even though Dumbledore could get stern and firm with Jacob's sibling, it was for their own good and apart from punishing them to constant detentions with Pitts, he had defended them from practically all of the staff's criticism.

<p>Presumably well aware of the events that lead to the <a href="/wiki/Discipline_at_Hogwarts#Expulsion" title="Discipline at Hogwarts">expulsion</a> of former Hogwarts student <a href="/wiki/Jacob_(Hogwarts_student)" title="Jacob (Hogwarts student)">Jacob</a> long before she ever met his younger sibling, given her own interest in the vaults, <a href="/wiki/Patricia_Rakepick" title="Patricia Rakepick">Patricia Rakepick</a> took interest in the latter individual for their first-hand experience with the Cursed Vaults, and is known to have kept an eye on them, possibly even before she was invited back to Hogwarts by the Headmaster of the day, Albus Dumbledore, to help put the threat of the Cursed Vaults to rest. Though she did not waste any time soliciting the help of said student and their group of friends to make her own, sanctioned search for the vaults easier, and even assured Jacob's sibling she meant them no harm and even were investigating the identity of the <a href="/wiki/R%27s_messenger" class="mw-redirect" title="R&#39;s messenger">mysterious wizard</a> that had attacked them in the <a href="/wiki/Clock_Tower_Courtyard" title="Clock Tower Courtyard">Clock Tower Courtyard</a> after curfew by forging a letter with the handwriting of Silvanus Kettleburn. Severus Snape, however, the Potions Master and former schoolmate of Rakepick's, did not trust her at all, going so far as to saying she had ulterior motives for being at Hogwarts and that she was after the Cursed Vaults for her own gain, and quite possibly meant to kill both them and anyone else who stood in her way. Rakepick continued, however, to request the assistance of Jacob's sibling, teaching them the <a href="/wiki/Shield_Charm" title="Shield Charm">Shield Charm</a> to test their abilities and, getting impressive results, continued to offer guidance, such as lending them <a href="/wiki/Sickleworth" title="Sickleworth">her own</a> <a href="/wiki/Niffler" title="Niffler">Niffler</a> to help in the search for clues and eventually teaching them the <a href="/wiki/Spider_repelling_spell" title="Spider repelling spell">Spider repelling spell</a> in order to prepare them for a potential fight with Acromantulas. 
</p><p>In the end, however, Snape's suspicions were proven partially correct when she fooled Jacob's sibling into breaking into the <a href="/wiki/Argus_Filch%27s_office" class="mw-redirect" title="Argus Filch&#39;s office">office</a> of <a href="/wiki/Argus_Filch" title="Argus Filch">Argus Filch</a> for a wild goose chase, only to swoop in and retrieve something she herself had been looking for. This eventually left Jacob's sibling uncertain if Rakepick can be trusted or if they are playing right into the hand of their potential enemy. Rakepick did redeem herself for her indiscretions, however, when she saved the life of Jacob's sibling by coming to their rescue just as their attacker was about to deliver the <i>coup de grace</i>, casting a <a href="/wiki/Blasting_Curse" title="Blasting Curse">Blasting Curse</a> so powerful that the attacker was blasted backwards, across almost the whole length of the <a href="/wiki/Clock_Tower_Courtyard" title="Clock Tower Courtyard">Clocktower Courtyard</a> and into a wall, knocking them out cold. Upon undoing the <a href="/wiki/Disillusionment_Charm" title="Disillusionment Charm">Disillusionment Charm</a> the attacker was under, they discovered that the person in question was <a href="/wiki/Ben_Copper" title="Ben Copper">Ben Copper</a>, whom Rakepick transported back into the castle to prevent him from hurting himself or others while Jacob's sibling went after the Forest Vault.

<ul><li>This individual is the protagonist of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter:_Hogwarts_Mystery" title="Harry Potter: Hogwarts Mystery">Harry Potter: Hogwarts Mystery</a></i>. Name, surname, gender, House and physical appearance are chosen by the player. The wand material also varies based on the player's goals and personality traits. Jacob's sibling's relationships, personality and choices may vary significantly depending on the player's decisions.
<ul><li>Even though the House is one of the factors chosen by the player, rivalry with <a href="/wiki/Merula_Snyde" title="Merula Snyde">Merula Snyde</a> still occurs even if the individual is picked to be with Slytherin.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Rowan_Khanna" title="Rowan Khanna">Rowan Khanna</a>'s gender and house is always the same as Jacob's sibling's.</li></ul></li>
<li>According to <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Philosopher%27s_Stone" title="Harry Potter and the Philosopher&#39;s Stone">the first book</a>, Slytherin had won the <a href="/wiki/House_Cup" title="House Cup">House Cup</a> the six years before <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor" title="Gryffindor">Gryffindor</a>'s win in <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>'s first year, <a href="/wiki/1992" title="1992">1992</a> (in <a href="/wiki/1986" title="1986">1986</a> through <a href="/wiki/1991" title="1991">1991</a>). The fact that this individual's house is player-determined and that they are able to help their house win the <a href="/wiki/House_Cup" title="House Cup">House Cup</a> can potentially cause a contradiction to the established <a href="/wiki/Canon" title="Canon">canon</a> of the books, by having another house win the Cup in those school years.</li>
<li>After the "Meet the Malfoys" side quest, Jacob's sibling can befriend <a href="/wiki/Dobby" title="Dobby">Dobby</a> which potentially contradicts canon where Dobby said no wizard had treated him as an equal, although technically Jacob's sibling may have been a witch and indeed is one in almost all promotional content.</li>
<li>As Jacob's sibling, you can also become an <a href="/wiki/Animagus" title="Animagus">Animagus</a>; depending on how you answer Penny's question, you can become a <a href="/wiki/Cat" title="Cat">cat</a>, a <a href="/wiki/Dog" title="Dog">dog</a>, or a <a href="/wiki/Bird" title="Bird">bird</a>. This can be done through a time-limited Animagus side quest, during which you can learn the ability and convince <a href="/wiki/Talbott_Winger" title="Talbott Winger">Talbott Winger</a> to register with the <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">Ministry</a>. However, in the Flying Solo side quest, they are both unregistered as of Year 3, both have concerns about being found by <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a> and Death Eaters, even if Jacob's sibling can decide to voluntarily register at a later date.</li>
<li>In the <a target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener" class="external text" href="https://youtu.be/27OiVEaAaoM?t=41s">official launch trailer</a> of the game, <a href="/wiki/Penny_Haywood" title="Penny Haywood">Penny Haywood</a> says, "Thanks, <b>Maya!</b> I know this plan is going to work." From this, we can assume that the original name of this character is Maya.<figure class="thumb tright show-info-icon" style="width: 252px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/9/96/Penny_mentioning_Maya.png/revision/latest?cb=20180829072115" class="image"><img alt="" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw%3D%3D" decoding="async" width="250" height="141" class="thumbimage lazyload" data-image-name="Penny mentioning Maya.png" data-image-key="Penny_mentioning_Maya.png" data-src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/9/96/Penny_mentioning_Maya.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/250?cb=20180829072115" /></a> 	<noscript><a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/9/96/Penny_mentioning_Maya.png/revision/latest?cb=20180829072115" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/9/96/Penny_mentioning_Maya.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/250?cb=20180829072115" decoding="async" width="250" height="141" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="Penny mentioning Maya.png" data-image-key="Penny_mentioning_Maya.png" data-src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/9/96/Penny_mentioning_Maya.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/250?cb=20180829072115" /></a></noscript> 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:Penny_mentioning_Maya.png" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption"><a href="/wiki/Penny_Haywood" title="Penny Haywood">Penny</a> mentions the name <b>Maya</b> in <a target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener" class="external text" href="https://youtu.be/27OiVEaAaoM">official launch trailer</a> of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter:_Hogwarts_Mystery" title="Harry Potter: Hogwarts Mystery">Harry Potter: Hogwarts Mystery</a></i></p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure></li>
<li>In the <a href="/wiki/1984%E2%80%931985_school_year" title="1984–1985 school year">1984–1985 school year</a>, Jacob's sibling had visions showing Hogwarts covered in ice and an animated suit of armour. Their best friend, <a href="/wiki/Rowan_Khanna" title="Rowan Khanna">Rowan Khanna</a>, said while discussing the vision with them later that "it appeared to have been some kind of prophecy", suggesting Jacob's sibling might be a <a href="/wiki/Seer" title="Seer">Seer</a>. Although, since Rowan was only a first-year, they might have been wrong. However, the incident with the <a href="/wiki/Cursed_ice" title="Cursed ice">Cursed ice</a> later on hints that Jacob's sibling could indeed be a Seer. Jacob's sibling's later had another vision of two unknown rooms and <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a>, suggesting that this individual were a Seer. They later also hears brief spoken messages from Jacob in their mind, both of which were later revealed to be a result of how both they and Jacob had in common a natural gift in <a href="/wiki/Legilimency" title="Legilimency">Legilimency</a>, suggesting that Jacob might have used said ability to send them <a href="/wiki/Vibe" title="Vibe">vibes</a>, which is a similar but distinct process.
<ul><li>Jacob's sibling may also occasionally receive precognitions of what the opponent's next spell would be in duelling.</li></ul></li>
<li>In the Beta mode of the game, there was a character named <a href="/wiki/William_(Hogwarts_student)" title="William (Hogwarts student)">William</a>. William was meant to be the name of the player's older brother. However, every scene where the name William was mentioned was replaced by the name Jacob in the final cut of the game. It's unknown whether William was intended to be the original name of Jacob or whether he was another character cut from the game.</li></ul>

<ul><li>When playing <a href="/wiki/Gobstones" title="Gobstones">Gobstones</a> against Tonks, Jacob's sibling jokes that they might also be a <a href="/wiki/Metamorphmagus" title="Metamorphmagus">Metamorphmagus</a>, a tongue-in-cheek reference to the fact that the player can change the physical features of the character at any time they want.</li>
<li>In the fifth year, you can choose to sit <a href="/wiki/Ordinary_Wizarding_Level" title="Ordinary Wizarding Level">O.W.L.</a> through a side quest. You gain a minimum of seven <a href="/wiki/Acceptable" title="Acceptable">Acceptable</a>'s, even if you fail to answer any question or perform any tasks. The maximum number of <a href="/wiki/Outstanding" title="Outstanding">Outstanding</a> grades is in the following seven subjects:
<ul><li><a href="/wiki/Charms_(class)" title="Charms (class)">Charms</a></li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Potions_(class)" title="Potions (class)">Potions</a></li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Transfiguration_(class)" title="Transfiguration (class)">Transfiguration</a></li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Care_of_Magical_Creatures" title="Care of Magical Creatures">Care of Magical Creatures</a></li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Herbology" title="Herbology">Herbology</a></li>
<li><a href="/wiki/History_of_Magic" title="History of Magic">History of Magic</a></li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Defence_Against_the_Dark_Arts" title="Defence Against the Dark Arts">Defence Against the Dark Arts</a></li>
<li>Divination and Astronomy are never acknowledged in the quest despite the fact that Jacob's sibling most likely got O.W.L.s in those subjects as well as they were able to continue them in their sixth year.</li></ul></li>
<li>Jacob's sibling might be bisexual since (regardless of selected gender) they have the option of fancying and/or dating male and female characters.</li></ul>
