<p><b>Garrick Ollivander</b> (b. <a href="/wiki/25_September" title="25 September">25 September</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-pottermore_1-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-pottermore-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> pre <a href="/wiki/1908" title="1908">1908</a>)<sup id="cite_ref-yearage_2-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-yearage-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>) was a <a href="/wiki/Half-blood" title="Half-blood">half-blood</a><sup id="cite_ref-pottermore_1-10" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-pottermore-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> who was the proprietor of <a href="/wiki/Ollivanders" title="Ollivanders">Ollivanders</a> in <a href="/wiki/Diagon_Alley" title="Diagon Alley">Diagon Alley</a> during most of the <a href="/wiki/20th_century" title="20th century">20th century</a>. Ollivander was widely considered the best <a href="/wiki/Wandmaker" title="Wandmaker">wandmaker</a> in the world, and many <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizards and witches</a> bought their <a href="/wiki/Wand" title="Wand">wands</a> from him.
</p><p>In <a href="/wiki/1996" title="1996">1996</a>, Ollivander was captured and <a href="/wiki/Cruciatus_Curse" title="Cruciatus Curse">tortured</a> by <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a>. He was rescued in <a href="/wiki/1998" title="1998">1998</a> by <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>, <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ron Weasley</a>, <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione Granger</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Dobby" title="Dobby">Dobby</a>. He imparted important information regarding <a href="/wiki/Wandlore" title="Wandlore">wandlore</a> to Harry, it helped him in his understanding of the <a href="/wiki/Deathly_Hallows" title="Deathly Hallows">Deathly Hallows</a>.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Garrick_Ollivander?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Garrick_Ollivander?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Family_heritage">Family heritage</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Garrick_Ollivander?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Family heritage">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Garrick_Ollivander?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Family heritage"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<dl><dd><div class="noprint"><i>Main article&#58; <a href="/wiki/Ollivander_family" title="Ollivander family">Ollivander family</a></i></div></dd></dl>
<p>Ollivander's family had long been associated with the profession of <a href="/wiki/Wandlore" title="Wandlore">wandcraft</a>. The surname means 'he who owns the olive wand' which is of <a href="/wiki/Mediterranean_Sea" title="Mediterranean Sea">Mediterranean</a> origin, suggesting that the family emigrated to Britain from elsewhere. Mr Ollivander believes that his ancestors arrived in Britain with the Romans and set up a stall and eventually a shop, to sell newer and improved wands to British wizards who had poorly constructed wands that were unreliable.<sup id="cite_ref-pm_10-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-pm-10">&#91;10&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_life">Early life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Garrick_Ollivander?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Early life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Garrick_Ollivander?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Early life"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Garrick Ollivander was born to <a href="/wiki/Gervaise_Ollivander" title="Gervaise Ollivander">Gervaise Ollivander</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-wandwoods_11-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-wandwoods-11">&#91;11&#93;</a></sup> a wizard, and his <a href="/wiki/Garrick_Ollivander%27s_mother" title="Garrick Ollivander&#39;s mother">wife</a>, a <a href="/wiki/Muggle-born" title="Muggle-born">Muggle-born</a> witch,<sup id="cite_ref-pottermore_1-11" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-pottermore-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> on the <a href="/wiki/25_September" title="25 September">25 September</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-pottermore_1-12" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-pottermore-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> in or before the year <a href="/wiki/1907" title="1907">1907</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-yearage_2-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-yearage-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>He attended <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> in his youth, where he was Sorted into <a href="/wiki/Ravenclaw" title="Ravenclaw">Ravenclaw House</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-pottermore_1-13" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-pottermore-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Career_as_a_wandmaker">Career as a wandmaker</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Garrick_Ollivander?veaction=edit&amp;section=4" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Career as a wandmaker">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Garrick_Ollivander?action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Career as a wandmaker"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<div class="quote">
<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;Pottermore">"<i>Renowned wandmaker Mr Ollivander owns the wand shop Ollivanders: Makers of Fine Wands Since 382 BC in Diagon Alley. His shop is widely considered the best place to purchase a wand.</i>"</span></dd>
<dd>&#8212;Description<sup class="noprint"><a href="/wiki/Pottermore" title="Pottermore">[src]</a></sup></dd>
<dd></dd></dl></div>
<figure class="thumb tleft show-info-icon" style="width: 252px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/e/e5/Harry-potter1-ollivander.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20170709044320" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/e/e5/Harry-potter1-ollivander.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/250?cb=20170709044320" decoding="async" width="250" height="104" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="Harry-potter1-ollivander.jpg" data-image-key="Harry-potter1-ollivander.jpg" /></a> 	 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:Harry-potter1-ollivander.jpg" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption">Ollivander holding one of the many wands inside his shop</p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure>
<p>Ollivander grew up in the family business and showed precocious talent in <a href="/wiki/Wandlore" title="Wandlore">wandmaking</a>. Soon, he developed an ambition to improve upon the cores and wand woods he made and developed a determination to pursue the ideal wand.<sup id="cite_ref-pottermore_1-14" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-pottermore-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Before Garrick Ollivander's proprietorship of <a href="/wiki/Ollivanders" title="Ollivanders">Ollivanders</a>, wizards used a wide variety of wand cores; the customer would often present their <a href="/wiki/Wandmaker" title="Wandmaker">wandmaker</a> with a magical substance to which they were attached, and he would turn it into their wand core.<sup id="cite_ref-pottermore_1-15" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-pottermore-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> Mr Ollivander believed that doing so altered the wand's temperament,<sup id="cite_ref-GOF18_12-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF18-12">&#91;12&#93;</a></sup> and he revolutionised the way <a href="/wiki/Wand" title="Wand">wands</a> were crafted by having cores of immensely powerful magical substances expertly enclosed in specially selected and complementary <a href="/wiki/Wand_wood" title="Wand wood">wandwoods</a>, with the result being that the wand would be matched to an owner that the wand itself felt an affinity for.<sup id="cite_ref-pottermore_1-16" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-pottermore-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<figure class="thumb tright show-info-icon" style="width: 236px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/e/e3/Sorcerers-stone-ollivander.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20180523031707" class="image"><img alt="" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw%3D%3D" decoding="async" width="234" height="124" class="thumbimage lazyload" data-image-name="Sorcerers-stone-ollivander.jpg" data-image-key="Sorcerers-stone-ollivander.jpg" data-src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/e/e3/Sorcerers-stone-ollivander.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/234?cb=20180523031707" /></a> 	<noscript><a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/e/e3/Sorcerers-stone-ollivander.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20180523031707" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/e/e3/Sorcerers-stone-ollivander.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/234?cb=20180523031707" decoding="async" width="234" height="124" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="Sorcerers-stone-ollivander.jpg" data-image-key="Sorcerers-stone-ollivander.jpg" data-src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/e/e3/Sorcerers-stone-ollivander.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/234?cb=20180523031707" /></a></noscript> 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:Sorcerers-stone-ollivander.jpg" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption">Ollivander talking with a customer</p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure>
<p>While there was initially substantial resistance to this new way wands were made, it soon became clear that wands made by Ollivander were far superior to any others. His methods of locating wand woods and core substances, marrying them together, and matching them to ideal owners were all jealously guarded secrets that were coveted by rival wandmakers.<sup id="cite_ref-pottermore_1-17" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-pottermore-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Sometime into his career, in the summer of <a href="/wiki/1938" title="1938">1938</a>, Ollivander sold <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Tom Riddle</a> his <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle%27s_wand" title="Tom Riddle&#39;s wand">wand</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-PS5_5-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PS5-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Shortly before the <a href="/wiki/1984%E2%80%931985_school_year" title="1984–1985 school year">1984–1985 school year</a>, Ollivander sold a wand to a <a href="/wiki/Jacob%27s_sibling" title="Jacob&#39;s sibling">soon-to-be student</a> of <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a>, just as he did with <a href="/wiki/Jacob_(Hogwarts_student)" title="Jacob (Hogwarts student)">their brother</a> before them.<sup id="cite_ref-13" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-13">&#91;13&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>As with other wandmakers, Ollivander made it his business to study the mystifying <a href="/wiki/Elder_Wand" title="Elder Wand">Elder Wand</a> in depth, such as its history and distinctive characteristics. Unlike <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione Granger</a>, he believed that the Elder Wand was not a myth or tale, and showed great enthusiasm with the powerful object despite the fact that it was dangerous and has had a bloody history. When held captive by Lord Voldemort, he was forced to relate all information he had known about the powerful wand. He also revealed that the last person rumoured to own the wand was <a href="/wiki/Mykew_Gregorovitch" title="Mykew Gregorovitch">Gregorovitch</a>, a foreign wandmaker, though he did not believe the rumour and thought that Gregorovitch spread it to boost his business.<sup id="cite_ref-DH24_14-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH24-14">&#91;14&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<p>Ollivander supplied <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> with a <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter%27s_wand" title="Harry Potter&#39;s wand">wand</a>, and as he did so, he told Harry a few facts about wands. He explained that a wand has a <a href="/wiki/Wand_core" title="Wand core">core</a>, in Harry's case a <a href="/wiki/Phoenix" title="Phoenix">phoenix</a> <a href="/wiki/Phoenix_feather" title="Phoenix feather">feather</a>. He told Harry that the body of the wand is made from a choice of different <a href="/wiki/Wand_wood" title="Wand wood">woods</a> with different magical properties. Ollivander said he made a similar wand using another feather from the <a href="/wiki/Fawkes" title="Fawkes">same phoenix</a>, but using a different wood. Harry's wand is <a href="/wiki/Holly" title="Holly">holly</a>, which symbolises protection, rather than Voldemort's <a href="/wiki/Yew" title="Yew">yew</a>, which is symbolic of death. He also explained that the wand chooses the wizard, rather than the other way around.<sup id="cite_ref-PS5_5-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PS5-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> Immediately after Harry left the shop, Ollivander sent a <a href="/wiki/Garrick_Ollivander%27s_letter_to_Albus_Dumbledore" title="Garrick Ollivander&#39;s letter to Albus Dumbledore">letter</a> to <a href="/wiki/Albus_Dumbledore" title="Albus Dumbledore">Albus Dumbledore</a> informing him of the wand's sale.<sup id="cite_ref-GOF36_15-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF36-15">&#91;15&#93;</a></sup>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Physical_appearance">Physical appearance</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Garrick_Ollivander?veaction=edit&amp;section=12" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Physical appearance">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Garrick_Ollivander?action=edit&amp;section=12" title="Edit section: Physical appearance"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<div class="quote">
<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;Harry Potter and the Philosopher&#39;s Stone">"<i>An old man was standing before them, his wide, pale eyes shining like moons through the gloom of the shop.</i>"</span></dd>
<dd>&#8212;Harry Potter meeting Ollivander<sup class="noprint"><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Philosopher%27s_Stone" title="Harry Potter and the Philosopher&#39;s Stone">[src]</a></sup></dd>

<p>When first meeting him, Harry Potter described Garrick Ollivander as an old man with pale silvery eyes and white skin.<sup id="cite_ref-eyes_6-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-eyes-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-skin_7-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-skin-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup> However, after he was kidnapped by Voldemort's Death Eaters, Ollivander's appearance changed for the worse as a result of the torture and abuse he suffered at the hands of Voldemort. After he was rescued, he was described to be emaciated, with yellow skin, his facial bones sticking out, skeleton-like hands, and vast, sunken eyes.<sup id="cite_ref-DH24_14-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH24-14">&#91;14&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Personality_and_traits">Personality and traits</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Garrick_Ollivander?veaction=edit&amp;section=13" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Personality and traits">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Garrick_Ollivander?action=edit&amp;section=13" title="Edit section: Personality and traits"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<div class="quote">
<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;Harry Potter and the Philosopher&#39;s Stone">"<i>I remember every wand I’ve ever sold, Mr Potter. Every single wand. It so happens that the phoenix whose tail feather is in your wand, gave another feather — just one other. It is very curious indeed that you should be destined for this wand when its brother — why, its brother gave you that scar.</i>"</span></dd>
<dd>&#8212;Ollivander's excellent memory and deep knowledge of wands<sup class="noprint"><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Philosopher%27s_Stone" title="Harry Potter and the Philosopher&#39;s Stone">[src]</a></sup></dd>
<dd></dd></dl></div>
<figure class="thumb tright show-info-icon" style="width: 231px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/2/23/Olivander.png/revision/latest?cb=20131025185213" class="image"><img alt="" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw%3D%3D" decoding="async" width="229" height="157" class="thumbimage lazyload" data-image-name="Olivander.png" data-image-key="Olivander.png" data-src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/2/23/Olivander.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/229?cb=20131025185213" /></a> 	<noscript><a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/2/23/Olivander.png/revision/latest?cb=20131025185213" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/2/23/Olivander.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/229?cb=20131025185213" decoding="async" width="229" height="157" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="Olivander.png" data-image-key="Olivander.png" data-src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/2/23/Olivander.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/229?cb=20131025185213" /></a></noscript> 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:Olivander.png" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption">Ollivander and his many wands</p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure>
<p>Ollivander was an intelligent man, with a perfect memory when it came to his work; he remembered the details of every wand he ever sold. He seemed to become happier the more difficult the challenge to find exactly the right wand for a difficult customer was. He used a magical <a href="/wiki/Garrick_Ollivander%27s_tape_measure" class="mw-redirect" title="Garrick Ollivander&#39;s tape measure">tape measure</a> to help assess customers for their wand.<sup id="cite_ref-PS5_5-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PS5-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Ollivander had a professorial enthusiasm for wandlore and was immensely learned in it. A less savoury aspect of his personality was that his fascination with wandlore sometimes eclipsed his sense of right and wrong; he was so fascinated with what a powerful wizard can do when matched with the right wand that he became detached from the good or evil nature of their actions. A prime example of this would be <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Tom Riddle</a>, who became Lord Voldemort. Even after the <a href="/wiki/First_Wizarding_War" title="First Wizarding War">First Wizarding War</a>, Ollivander remembered him as having done "great things" with his wand — "<i>terrible, yes, but great</i>".<sup id="cite_ref-PS5_5-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PS5-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> An even more extreme example was during the <a href="/wiki/Second_Wizarding_War" title="Second Wizarding War">Second Wizarding War</a>, when Ollivander, having just been rescued from a year of imprisonment and torture at Voldemort's hands, was briefly enthralled by the thought of what Voldemort might do when matched with the <a href="/wiki/Elder_Wand" title="Elder Wand">Elder Wand</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-DH24_14-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH24-14">&#91;14&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>However, Ollivander still valued his own life and well-being, and divulged information to Voldemort whenever threatened with <a href="/wiki/Death" title="Death">death</a> and tortured by the Cruciatus Curse.<sup id="cite_ref-DH24_14-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH24-14">&#91;14&#93;</a></sup> The wandmaker feared the Dark Lord's wrath and begged for mercy when informed that his (Ollivander's) advice about using another wand was incorrect.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Magical_abilities_and_skills">Magical abilities and skills</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Garrick_Ollivander?veaction=edit&amp;section=14" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Garrick_Ollivander?action=edit&amp;section=14" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<div class="quote">
<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire">"<i>"Avis!" The <a href="/wiki/Viktor_Krum%27s_wand" title="Viktor Krum&#39;s wand">hornbeam wand</a> let off a blast like a gun, and a number of small, twittering <a href="/wiki/Bird" title="Bird">birds</a> flew out of the end and through the open window into the watery sunlight.</i>"</span></dd>
<dd>&#8212;Ollivander performing the <a href="/wiki/Bird-Conjuring_Charm" title="Bird-Conjuring Charm">Bird-Conjuring Charm</a><sup class="noprint"><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Goblet_of_Fire" title="Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire">[src]</a></sup></dd>
<dd></dd></dl></div>
<ul><li><b><a href="/wiki/Wandmaker" title="Wandmaker">Wandmaking</a>/<a href="/wiki/Wandlore" title="Wandlore">Wandlore</a></b>: Garrick Ollivander, just as other Ollivander family members, was considered as the best British wand-maker, with an immense knowledge of wandlore. He was very knowledgeable of the different magical properties possessed by different wands, in particular relation to their <a href="/wiki/Wand_wood" title="Wand wood">woods</a> and <a href="/wiki/Wand_core" title="Wand core">cores</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-PS5_5-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PS5-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-GOF18_12-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF18-12">&#91;12&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-DH24_14-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH24-14">&#91;14&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Non-verbal_magic" class="mw-redirect" title="Non-verbal magic">Non-verbal magic</a></b>: Ollivander had the ability to perform non-verbal spells, as no incantation was spoken by him when he conjured <a href="/wiki/Smokescreen_Spell" title="Smokescreen Spell">smoke rings</a> in 1994. Given the high level of difficulty required in the casting of spells non-verbally, this shows he was a wizard of very impressive magical skills.<sup id="cite_ref-GOF18_12-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF18-12">&#91;12&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Apparition" title="Apparition">Apparition</a></b>: Like most accomplished adult wizards, he could Apparate, but failed in 1998 at Malfoy Manor due to <a href="/wiki/Anti-Disapparition_Jinx" title="Anti-Disapparition Jinx">enchantments</a> placed on the cellar.<sup id="cite_ref-DH23_21-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH23-21">&#91;21&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Transfiguration" title="Transfiguration">Transfiguration</a></b>: He was skilled with <a href="/wiki/Conjuration" title="Conjuration">conjuration</a>, a highly difficult and complex branch of transfiguration, conjuring <a href="/wiki/Orchideous" title="Orchideous">flowers</a>, <a href="/wiki/Bird-Conjuring_Charm" title="Bird-Conjuring Charm">birds</a>, smoke rings, and <a href="/wiki/Fountain_of_wine" title="Fountain of wine">wine</a> in 1994.<sup id="cite_ref-GOF18_12-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF18-12">&#91;12&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Charm" title="Charm">Charms</a></b>: Ollivander was able to produce a non-corporeal <a href="/wiki/Patronus_Charm" title="Patronus Charm">Patronus Charm</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-LEGOY1_8-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-LEGOY1-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-LEGOY2_9-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-LEGOY2-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup> a mark of superior <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magical</a> ability given the immense difficulty required in the casting of the spell. He was also proficient with the <a href="/wiki/Knockback_Jinx" title="Knockback Jinx">Knockback Jinx</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-PSG_25-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PSG-25">&#91;25&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Wand" title="Wand">Wand</a> versatility</b>: In <a href="/wiki/1994" title="1994">1994</a>, Ollivander used Cedric's, Fleur's, Viktor's and Harry's wands, each with successful effect.<sup id="cite_ref-GOF18_12-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF18-12">&#91;12&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li><b>Eidetic memory</b>: Ollivander was able to recite the precise details of every wand he ever crafted or sold. This eidetic — or photographic — memory of these details borders on perfect recall, a very rare ability.<sup id="cite_ref-PS5_5-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PS5-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li><b>Business</b>: Ollivander also possessed excellent business skills, being highly able to successfully operate and manage his own wand shop for at least sixty years.<sup id="cite_ref-PS5_5-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PS5-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup></li></ul>

<p>Like most of the <a href="/wiki/Wizarding_world" title="Wizarding world">wizarding world</a>, Ollivander heard of Harry Potter and his <a href="/wiki/Attack_at_Godric%27s_Hollow_(1981)" title="Attack at Godric&#39;s Hollow (1981)">defeat</a> of <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a> in 1981. He first met Harry in 1991 and sold him his <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter%27s_wand" title="Harry Potter&#39;s wand">wand</a>. The <a href="/wiki/Phoenix_feather" title="Phoenix feather">phoenix feather</a> in <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter%27s_wand" title="Harry Potter&#39;s wand">Harry's</a> and <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle%27s_wand" title="Tom Riddle&#39;s wand">Riddle's</a> wands came from the same phoenix, <a href="/wiki/Fawkes" title="Fawkes">Fawkes</a>, something that Ollivander considered "curious". However, Harry was unsure whether he liked Ollivander due to the wandmaker's fascination by what Voldemort did with his wand, despite the terrible things he did with it.<sup id="cite_ref-PS5_5-10" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PS5-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Ollivander's relationship with <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> grew after the <a href="/wiki/Skirmish_at_Malfoy_Manor" title="Skirmish at Malfoy Manor">Skirmish at Malfoy Manor</a> when Harry rescued him from imprisonment and during their subsequent stay at the <a href="/wiki/Shell_Cottage" title="Shell Cottage">Shell Cottage</a>. Harry would go on to obtain vital information about the <a href="/wiki/Elder_Wand" title="Elder Wand">Elder Wand</a> from Ollivander, which would later prove instrumental for bringing about the final defeat of Lord Voldemort.<sup id="cite_ref-DH24_14-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH24-14">&#91;14&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Garrick_Ollivander?veaction=edit&amp;section=21" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Garrick_Ollivander?action=edit&amp;section=21" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Garrick is an Old English name meaning "one who governs with a spear".<sup id="cite_ref-27" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-27">&#91;27&#93;</a></sup> Ollivander is of Mediterranean origin meaning "he who owns an olive wand".<sup id="cite_ref-pm_10-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-pm-10">&#91;10&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<ul><li>Garrick Ollivander is played by the late <a href="/wiki/John_Hurt" title="John Hurt">John Hurt</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Philosopher%27s_Stone_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Philosopher&#39;s Stone (film)">film adaptation</a> of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Philosopher%27s_Stone" title="Harry Potter and the Philosopher&#39;s Stone">Harry Potter and the Philosopher's Stone</a></i>, the first part of the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Deathly_Hallows:_Part_1" title="Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 1">film adaptation</a> of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Deathly_Hallows" title="Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows">Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows</a></i> and the second part of the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Deathly_Hallows:_Part_1" title="Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 1">film adaptation</a> of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Deathly_Hallows" title="Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows">Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows</a></i>. He was originally set to appear in the the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Goblet_of_Fire_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire (film)">film adaptation</a> of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Goblet_of_Fire" title="Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire">Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire</a></i><sup id="cite_ref-28" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-28">&#91;28&#93;</a></sup> but the character's scenes were cut from the final film and have not been released.</li>
<li>Ollivander is one of the only people before <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Philosopher%27s_Stone" title="Harry Potter and the Philosopher&#39;s Stone">Harry Potter and the Philosopher's Stone</a></i> who knew Voldemort's real name. He said in the first book he knows that Voldemort's wand caused Harry's scar. While this is not stated in any explicit way, the very fact that Voldemort has only ever had one wand (except for his use of the wand of <a href="/wiki/Lucius_Malfoy" title="Lucius Malfoy">Lucius Malfoy</a> and the <a href="/wiki/Elder_Wand" title="Elder Wand">Elder Wand</a> very late in life) would ensure that Ollivander would have sold him a wand at age 11, when he still used his former name. It is possible that he somehow deduced the secret sometime before July 1991, be it through mere deduction or that Dumbledore himself told him, since Dumbledore did say that Ollivander contacted him to tell him that Harry had bought the only other wand in existence crafted with one of Fawkes's feathers. He might simply have recognised the wand in the hands of Lord Voldemort.</li></ul>
