<b>Owle Bullock</b><sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> was a <a href="/wiki/Dark_Wizard" title="Dark Wizard">Dark Wizard</a> or witch who authored <i><a href="/wiki/Secrets_of_the_Darkest_Art" title="Secrets of the Darkest Art">Secrets of the Darkest Art</a></i>, a book that was "<i>full of <a href="/wiki/Dark_Arts" title="Dark Arts">evil magic</a></i>", including detailed instructions on how to create a <a href="/wiki/Horcrux" title="Horcrux">Horcrux</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
