<b>Eduardus Limus</b>, also known by the nickname <b>Pretty Face</b>, was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizarding</a> criminal who operated in the <a href="/wiki/1920s" title="1920s">1920s</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-cob_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-cob-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Eduardus_Limus_(criminal)?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Eduardus_Limus_(criminal)?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>In <a href="/wiki/1926" title="1926">1926</a>, the <a href="/wiki/International_Wizarding_Police" title="International Wizarding Police">International Wizarding Police</a> issued a wanted poster for Limus, stating that he was wanted for "counterfeiting <a href="/wiki/Wand" title="Wand">wands</a>" and for committing "<a href="/wiki/Muggle" class="mw-redirect" title="Muggle">No-Maj</a> murder" in <a href="/wiki/Asia" title="Asia">Asia</a> and <a href="/wiki/Europe" title="Europe">Europe</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-cob_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-cob-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> It described him as a "powerful wizard" who was "<a href="/wiki/Wand" title="Wand">wanded</a> and dangerous."<sup id="cite_ref-cob_1-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-cob-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> A reward of 5000 <a href="/wiki/Dragot" title="Dragot">Dragots</a> was offered for information leading to his capture.<sup id="cite_ref-cob_1-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-cob-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
