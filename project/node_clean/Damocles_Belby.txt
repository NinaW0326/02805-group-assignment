<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Damocles_Belby?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Damocles_Belby?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_life">Early life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Damocles_Belby?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Early life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Damocles_Belby?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Early life"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>While being educated at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a>, Damocles Belby was a favourite student of <a href="/wiki/Horace_Slughorn" title="Horace Slughorn">Horace Slughorn</a>, the <a href="/wiki/Potions_Master" title="Potions Master">Potions Master</a>, who remarked that he was an "outstanding" <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP7_2-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP7-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Later_life">Later life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Damocles_Belby?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Later life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Damocles_Belby?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Later life"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>After graduating from <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a>, Damocles Belby went on to become a <a href="/wiki/Potioneer" title="Potioneer">Potioneer</a> and invented the <a href="/wiki/Wolfsbane_Potion" title="Wolfsbane Potion">Wolfsbane Potion</a> sometime after the mid-<a href="/wiki/1970s" title="1970s">1970s</a>. This potion, made up mostly of <a href="/wiki/Aconite" title="Aconite">Aconite</a>, was able to relieve (but not cure) the effects of <a href="/wiki/Werewolf" title="Werewolf">Lycanthropy</a> by allowing the werewolf to retain his or her human intelligence while transformed. The potion's development led to werewolves gaining a better chance to integrate themselves into wizarding society, which presumably resulted in his Order of Merlin award. Damocles had a nephew, <a href="/wiki/Marcus_Belby" title="Marcus Belby">Marcus Belby</a>, who stated that his uncle and <a href="/wiki/Marcus_Belby%27s_father" title="Marcus Belby&#39;s father">father</a> did not get along. As such, Damocles had little contact with his relatives, and was not very well known to his nephew Marcus.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP7_2-10" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP7-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> This is probably why Marcus failed to gain much recognition in the <a href="/wiki/Slug_Club" title="Slug Club">Slug Club</a> from Slughorn.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Magical_skills_and_abilities">Magical skills and abilities</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Damocles_Belby?veaction=edit&amp;section=4" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Magical skills and abilities">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Damocles_Belby?action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Magical skills and abilities"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p><a href="/wiki/Horace_Slughorn" title="Horace Slughorn">Horace Slughorn</a> claimed that Damocles Belby was an "outstanding wizard" and that he well deserved his <a href="/wiki/Order_of_Merlin" title="Order of Merlin">Order of Merlin</a> award,<sup id="cite_ref-HBP7_2-11" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP7-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> meaning he was likely highly proficient in many branches of <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magic</a>.
</p>
<ul><li><b><a href="/wiki/Potion" title="Potion">Potions</a></b>: Damocles Belby was extremely adept in the art of potion-making. He was one of Horace Slughorn's finest students, and after graduating Hogwarts, he developed an accomplished career as a <a href="/wiki/Potioneer" title="Potioneer">Potioneer</a>. He was also so skilled that he managed to invent the <a href="/wiki/Wolfsbane_Potion" title="Wolfsbane Potion">Wolfsbane Potion</a>, a potion "particularly complex" according to <a href="/wiki/Remus_Lupin" title="Remus Lupin">Remus Lupin</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-POA8_4-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-POA8-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup></li></ul>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Damocles_Belby?veaction=edit&amp;section=5" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Damocles_Belby?action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Latinized form of the Greek name Δαμοκλῆς (<i>Damokles</i>), which was derived from δᾶμος (<i>damos</i>) "the people" (a Doric Greek variant of δῆμος (<i>demos</i>)) and κλέος (<i>kleos</i>) "glory". In Greek legend Damocles was a member of the court of Dionysius the Elder, the king of Syracuse. Damocles expressed envy of the king's station so Dionysius offered to switch roles with him for a day. To illustrate to Damocles the peril of a man in his position he suspended a sword over the throne.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Damocles_Belby?veaction=edit&amp;section=6" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Damocles_Belby?action=edit&amp;section=6" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>The text of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Half-Blood_Prince" title="Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince">Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince</a></i> only refers to Damocles as Marcus's uncle, but does not make it clear if he is a paternal or maternal uncle. As such, this individual's surname was uncertain. He was later referred to as "Damocles Belby" in <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter:_Hogwarts_Mystery" title="Harry Potter: Hogwarts Mystery">Harry Potter: Hogwarts Mystery</a></i>.</li></ul>
