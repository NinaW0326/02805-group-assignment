<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/African_prince?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/African_prince?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Although <a href="/wiki/Quirinus_Quirrell" title="Quirinus Quirrell">Quirinus Quirrell</a> claimed to have received his turban from this individual,<sup id="cite_ref-PS8_2-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PS8-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> Quirrell used the turban to hide the face of <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a> on the back of his head.<sup id="cite_ref-PS17_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PS17-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> Therefore, it is possible that this individual does not exist, particularly as there are no <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizarding</a> princes,<sup id="cite_ref-HBP16_4-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP16-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> and it would be odd if a <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggle</a> prince knew about a zombie. Additionally, zombies are&#160;known to be&#160;native to the <a href="/wiki/United_States_of_America" title="United States of America">United States of America</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-PM_5-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PM-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> as opposed to the continent of <a href="/wiki/Africa" title="Africa">Africa</a>.
</p>
