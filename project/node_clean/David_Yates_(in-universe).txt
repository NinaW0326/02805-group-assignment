<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Millennium_Bridge_disaster">Millennium Bridge disaster</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/David_Yates_(in-universe)?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Millennium Bridge disaster">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/David_Yates_(in-universe)?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Millennium Bridge disaster"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<div class="quote">
<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince (film)">"<i>The police are continuing with the investigation into the cause of the <a href="/wiki/Millennium_Bridge" title="Millennium Bridge">Millennium Bridge</a> disaster. River traffic has been halted as police search for survivors. The surrounding area remains closed. The Mayor has urged Londoners to remain calm...</i>"</span></dd>
<dd>&#8212;A <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggle</a> radio broadcast<sup class="noprint"><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Half-Blood_Prince_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince (film)">[src]</a></sup></dd>
<dd></dd></dl></div>
<p>After the collapse of the <a href="/wiki/Millennium_Bridge" title="Millennium Bridge">Millennium Bridge</a> at the hands of <a href="/wiki/Fenrir_Greyback" title="Fenrir Greyback">Fenrir Greyback</a> and a group of <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a>, the Mayor urged <a href="/wiki/London" title="London">Londoners</a> to remain calm, for this was a most unusual event.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> He also addressed <i><a href="/wiki/The_Guardian" title="The Guardian">The Guardian</a></i>, where he described Londoners as having been left "<i>scared, confused and desperate for answers</i>" and pledging to uncover the truth behind the disaster (widely speculated among <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggles</a> to have been caused by a fundamentalist political group).<sup id="cite_ref-HPLE_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HPLE-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/David_Yates_(in-universe)?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/David_Yates_(in-universe)?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>The existence of this individual constitutes an anachronism as the office was created by the <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Greater_London_Authority_Act_1999" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Greater London Authority Act 1999">Greater London Authority Act 1999</a> and the first Mayor of London, <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ken_Livingstone" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Ken Livingstone">Ken Livingstone</a>, was elected in <a href="/wiki/2000" title="2000">2000</a>.</li>
<li>The Mayor's name is an obvious reference to <a href="/wiki/David_Yates" title="David Yates">David Yates</a>, the man who directed the last four <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_films" class="mw-redirect" title="Harry Potter films"><i>Harry Potter</i> films</a>, including <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Half-Blood_Prince_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince (film)">Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince</a></i>.</li></ul>
