<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Mahoutokoro_School_of_Magic">Mahoutokoro School of Magic</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Mahoutokoro_potions_champion?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Mahoutokoro School of Magic">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Mahoutokoro_potions_champion?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Mahoutokoro School of Magic"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>The Japanese wizard attended <a href="/wiki/Mahoutokoro_School_of_Magic" title="Mahoutokoro School of Magic">Mahoutokoro School of Magic</a> for his education.<sup id="cite_ref-WBP_1-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-WBP-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Wizarding_Schools_Potions_Championship">Wizarding Schools Potions Championship</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Mahoutokoro_potions_champion?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Wizarding Schools Potions Championship">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Mahoutokoro_potions_champion?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Wizarding Schools Potions Championship"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<figure class="thumb tright show-info-icon" style="width: 182px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/7/7f/River_troll_bridge.png/revision/latest?cb=20140823185734" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/7/7f/River_troll_bridge.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/180?cb=20140823185734" decoding="async" width="180" height="122" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="River troll bridge.png" data-image-key="River_troll_bridge.png" /></a> 	 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:River_troll_bridge.png" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption">The wizard running past the <a href="/wiki/River_Troll" class="mw-redirect" title="River Troll">River Troll</a> underneath the bridge</p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure>
<p>The wizard became a skilled <a href="/wiki/Potioneer" title="Potioneer">potioneer</a> and at age seventeen or above competed in the <a href="/wiki/Wizarding_Schools_Potions_Championship" title="Wizarding Schools Potions Championship">Wizarding Schools Potions Championship</a> for the golden cauldron, where he represented his school, Mahoutokoro. At the start of the championship in the <a href="/wiki/Enchanted_garden_(Book_of_Potions)" title="Enchanted garden (Book of Potions)">enchanted garden</a>, he headed towards the bridge, hoping to get an early lead. However, while crossing the bridge, he awakened a <a href="/wiki/River_Troll" class="mw-redirect" title="River Troll">River Troll</a>. Fortunately, the <a href="/wiki/Troll" title="Troll">Troll</a> was incapacitated by the <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_potions_champion" title="Hogwarts potions champion">Hogwarts potions champion</a>, who used a <a href="/wiki/Shrinking_Solution" title="Shrinking Solution">Shrinking Solution</a> on it.<sup id="cite_ref-WBP_1-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-WBP-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
