<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Magical_abilities_and_skills">Magical abilities and skills</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_2000s_Hogwarts_student?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_2000s_Hogwarts_student?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li><b><a href="/wiki/Charm" title="Charm">Charms</a></b>: This individual was proficient in the casting of various charms. They successfully used the <a href="/wiki/Fire-Making_Spell" title="Fire-Making Spell">Fire-Making Spell</a>, <a href="/wiki/Freezing_Spell" title="Freezing Spell">Freezing Spell</a> and <a href="/wiki/Stunning_Spell" title="Stunning Spell">Stunning Spell</a> whilst duelling.</li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Duelling" title="Duelling">Duelling</a></b>: This individual was an accomplished duellist, as they were an active member of the Hogwarts <a href="/wiki/Duelling_Club" title="Duelling Club">Duelling Club</a>, and were skilled with <a href="/wiki/Martial_magic" title="Martial magic">martial magic</a>.</li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Dark_Arts" title="Dark Arts">Dark Arts</a></b>: This individual was capable of casting some <a href="/wiki/Dark_charm" title="Dark charm">Dark charms</a> successfully, ranging from the <a href="/wiki/Oppugno_Jinx" title="Oppugno Jinx">Oppugno Jinx</a> to the highly dangerous <a href="/wiki/Curse" title="Curse">curse</a> <i><a href="/wiki/Sectumsempra" title="Sectumsempra">Sectumsempra</a></i>.</li></ul>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Possessions">Possessions</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_2000s_Hogwarts_student?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Possessions">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_2000s_Hogwarts_student?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Possessions"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li><b><a href="/wiki/Wand" title="Wand">Wand</a></b>: They purchased their wand from <a href="/wiki/Ollivanders" title="Ollivanders">Ollivanders</a> before their first year.</li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Gossamer" title="Gossamer">Gossamer</a> portrait</b>: They kept the Gossamer portrait in their dormitory to keep them from causing trouble around the <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Castle" title="Hogwarts Castle">castle</a>.</li></ul>
