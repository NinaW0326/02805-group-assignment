<p><b>Regina Rowle</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Great_Britain" title="Great Britain">British</a> <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch</a> who worked at the <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">British Ministry of Magic</a> in the <a href="/wiki/2010s" title="2010s">2010s</a>, in the capacity of Junior <a href="/wiki/Unspeakable" title="Unspeakable">Unspeakable</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-WUtrailer_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-WUtrailer-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>At some point in her career, she entered the <a href="/wiki/Love_Chamber" title="Love Chamber">Love Chamber</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Department_of_Mysteries" title="Department of Mysteries">Department of Mysteries</a>; the log documenting the time of her entry and exit was brought to light during the investigation over the <a href="/wiki/Calamity" title="Calamity">Calamity</a>, as it also showed <a href="/wiki/Grim_Fawley" title="Grim Fawley">Grim Fawley</a> was frequently in the room at odd times.<sup id="cite_ref-WUtrailer_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-WUtrailer-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
