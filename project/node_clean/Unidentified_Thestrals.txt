<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_Thestrals?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_Thestrals?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The Thestrals were either introduced or born into the <a href="/wiki/Forbidden_Forest" title="Forbidden Forest">Forbidden Forest</a> and the herd of <a href="/wiki/Thestral" title="Thestral">Thestrals</a> already existing therein. As they were adults, it is likely that they had lived in the Forest for some time.
</p><p>On <a href="/wiki/17_June" title="17 June">17 June</a>, <a href="/wiki/1996" title="1996">1996</a>, <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> had a dream about <a href="/wiki/Sirius_Black" title="Sirius Black">Sirius Black</a> being tortured by Lord Voldemort in the <a href="/wiki/Department_of_Mysteries" title="Department of Mysteries">Department of Mysteries</a>;<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP31_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP31-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/Dolores_Umbridge" title="Dolores Umbridge">Dolores Umbridge</a> caught him and <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione Granger</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Fire" title="Fire">fire</a> talking to <a href="/wiki/Kreacher" title="Kreacher">Kreacher</a> at <a href="/wiki/12_Grimmauld_Place" title="12 Grimmauld Place">12 Grimmauld Place</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP32_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP32-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> but Hermione led the pair into the Forest, where the <a href="/wiki/Centaur" title="Centaur">centaur</a> colony drew <a href="/wiki/Dolores_Umbridge" title="Dolores Umbridge">Dolores Umbridge</a> away and, when <a href="/wiki/Grawp" title="Grawp">Grawp</a> the <a href="/wiki/Giant" title="Giant">giant</a> assaulted them, they fired arrows at him, peppering the ground and those on it with <a href="/wiki/Blood" title="Blood">blood</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP33_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP33-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p><a href="/wiki/Luna_Lovegood" title="Luna Lovegood">Luna Lovegood</a>, <a href="/wiki/Ginevra_Weasley" title="Ginevra Weasley">Ginny Weasley</a>, <a href="/wiki/Neville_Longbottom" title="Neville Longbottom">Neville Longbottom</a> and <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ron Weasley</a>, who had been captured by Umbridge's <a href="/wiki/Inquisitorial_Squad" title="Inquisitorial Squad">Inquisitorial Squad</a>, met them in the Forest. The Thestrals, drawn to them by the giant <a href="/wiki/Blood" title="Blood">blood</a> soaking Harry and Hermione, offered a method of transport on which they could get to <a href="/wiki/London" title="London">London</a> and thus to the Ministry.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP33_3-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP33-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>After several hours riding, the Thestrals finally reached their destination, and began scavenging for food in the rubbish bins outside the Ministry.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP34_4-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP34-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> It can be presumed that they returned to <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts</a>.
</p>
