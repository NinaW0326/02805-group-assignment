This <b>individual</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Dark_Wizard" title="Dark Wizard">Dark Wizard</a>&#160;and&#160;<a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eater</a> who served <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a> during the <a href="/wiki/Second_Wizarding_War" title="Second Wizarding War">Second Wizarding War</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-DHF2_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DHF2-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_male_Death_Eater_killed_by_a_grey_haired_wizard?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_male_Death_Eater_killed_by_a_grey_haired_wizard?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>He became a member of the <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eater</a> sometime prior to or during the <a href="/wiki/Second_Wizarding_War" title="Second Wizarding War">Second Wizarding War</a>, presumably participating in numerous battles with his fellow Death Eaters, such as the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_the_Department_of_Mysteries" title="Battle of the Department of Mysteries">Battle of the Department of Mysteries</a>, <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_the_Astronomy_Tower" title="Battle of the Astronomy Tower">Battle of the Astronomy Tower</a> and <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_the_Seven_Potters" title="Battle of the Seven Potters">Battle of the Seven Potters</a>. On <a href="/wiki/2_May" title="2 May">2 May</a>, <a href="/wiki/1998" title="1998">1998</a>, he fought at the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a> as a member of <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a>'s army of Death Eaters, <a href="/wiki/Snatchers" title="Snatchers">Snatchers</a> and <a href="/wiki/Dark_Arts" title="Dark Arts">Dark</a> <a href="/wiki/Creature" title="Creature">creatures</a>. During the final stage of the battle, he was killed during the <a href="/wiki/Skirmish_at_the_Great_Hall" title="Skirmish at the Great Hall">Skirmish at the Great Hall</a> by a <a href="/wiki/Unidentified_grey_haired_man_during_the_Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Unidentified grey haired man during the Battle of Hogwarts">grey haired wizard</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-DHF2_1-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DHF2-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
