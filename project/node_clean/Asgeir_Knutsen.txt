<b>Asgeir Knutsen</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch</a> who played as a <a href="/wiki/Beater" title="Beater">Beater</a> on the <a href="/wiki/Nordic_National_Quidditch_team" title="Nordic National Quidditch team">Nordic National Quidditch team</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>During the <a href="/wiki/1996%E2%80%931997_school_year" title="1996–1997 school year">1996–1997 school year</a>, Knutsens <a href="/wiki/Chocolate_Frog_Card" title="Chocolate Frog Card">Chocolate Frog Card</a> could be won by beating <a href="/wiki/Alison_Denshaw" title="Alison Denshaw">Alison Denshaw</a> at an <a href="/wiki/Exploding_Snap" title="Exploding Snap">Exploding Snap</a> challenge.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Asgeir_Knutsen?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Asgeir_Knutsen?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The name "Asgeir" is from the Old Norse name Ásgeirr (or possibly the modern Icelandic name Ásgeir), derived from the elements áss meaning "god" and geirr meaning "spear". It is, however, a masculine name.
</p>
