<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Magical_abilities_and_skills">Magical abilities and skills</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Felix_Summerbee?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Felix_Summerbee?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li><b><a href="/wiki/Spell_creation" title="Spell creation">Spell creation</a></b>: Summerbee mastered the highly complicated and advanced <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magical</a> art of inventing new <a href="/wiki/Spell" title="Spell">spells</a>, having successfully created the <a href="/wiki/Cheering_Charm" title="Cheering Charm">Cheering Charm</a> in his lifetime, a highly impressive magical achievement which gained him recognition on a silver <a href="/wiki/Chocolate_Frog_Card" title="Chocolate Frog Card">Chocolate Frog Card</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-COSG_1-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COSG-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Charm" title="Charm">Charms</a></b>: Summerbee was highly talented with charm-work, given how he successfully invented the <a href="/wiki/Cheering_Charm" title="Cheering Charm">Cheering Charm</a>, a charm which makes the target feel elated and and cheerful, by its namesake.</li></ul>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Felix_Summerbee?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Felix_Summerbee?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>Felix Summerbee was the first <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> featured on <a href="/wiki/J.K.Rowling_Official_Site" class="mw-redirect" title="J.K.Rowling Official Site">J. K. Rowling's official website</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Wizard_of_the_Month" title="Wizard of the Month">Wizard of the Month</a> feature in May, 2004. He was featured again in May, 2005, and was the only character to have been featured twice.</li></ul>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Felix_Summerbee?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Felix_Summerbee?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The name "Felix" comes from a Roman cognomen, meaning "lucky", "successful" in Latin. It was acquired as an agnomen, or nickname, by the 1st-century BC Roman general Sulla.
</p>
