<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_French_policeman?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_French_policeman?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>In <a href="/wiki/1927" title="1927">1927</a>, he was guarding near <a href="/wiki/Unidentified_Magical_Statue" title="Unidentified Magical Statue">the entrance</a> to <a href="/wiki/Place_Cach%C3%A9e" title="Place Cachée">Place Cachée</a>. When <a href="/wiki/Newton_Scamander" title="Newton Scamander">Newt Scamander</a> and <a href="/wiki/Jacob_Kowalski" title="Jacob Kowalski">Jacob Kowalski</a> arrived at <a href="/wiki/Paris" title="Paris">Paris</a> to investigate, Newt used the <a href="/wiki/Confundus_Charm" title="Confundus Charm">Confundus Charm</a> on the Policeman to distract him for a moment.<sup id="cite_ref-COG48_2-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COG48-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
