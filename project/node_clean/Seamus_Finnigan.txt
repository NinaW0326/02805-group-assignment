<div class="quote">
<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;Harry Potter and the Philosopher&#39;s Stone">"<i>I’m half-and-half. Me dad’s a Muggle. Mum didn’t tell him she was a witch ’til after they were married. Bit of a nasty shock for him.</i>"</span></dd>
<dd>&#8212;Seamus regarding his heritage<sup class="noprint"><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Philosopher%27s_Stone" title="Harry Potter and the Philosopher&#39;s Stone">[src]</a></sup></dd>
<dd></dd></dl></div>
<p><b>Seamus Finnigan</b> (b. <a href="/wiki/1979" title="1979">1979</a>/<a href="/wiki/1980" title="1980">1980</a>) was an <a href="/wiki/Ireland" title="Ireland">Irish</a> <a href="/wiki/Half-blood" title="Half-blood">half-blood</a><sup id="cite_ref-blood_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-blood-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a>, son of <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggle</a> <a href="/wiki/Seamus_Finnigan%27s_father" title="Seamus Finnigan&#39;s father">Mr Finnigan</a> and <a href="/wiki/Ireland" title="Ireland">Irish</a> witch <a href="/wiki/Seamus_Finnigan%27s_mother" title="Seamus Finnigan&#39;s mother">Mrs Finnigan</a>. Mr Finnigan did not learn this until after they were married and it gave him a "nasty shock" when he first learned it.<sup id="cite_ref-PS7_5-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PS7-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Seamus was a <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor" title="Gryffindor">Gryffindor</a> student at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a>. He was also the best friend of <a href="/wiki/Dean_Thomas" title="Dean Thomas">Dean Thomas</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-PS7_5-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PS7-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> Seamus originally doubted <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>'s claim that <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a> had returned in <a href="/wiki/1995" title="1995">1995</a> (as his mother doubted it).<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP11_15-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP11-15">&#91;15&#93;</a></sup> However, late in 1996 he eventually joined <a href="/wiki/Dumbledore%27s_Army" title="Dumbledore&#39;s Army">Dumbledore's Army</a>. The D.A. was an organisation taught and led by Harry Potter. Seamus joined after apologising to Harry.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP_13-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP-13">&#91;13&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>In <a href="/wiki/1997" title="1997">1997</a> he continued his <a href="/wiki/Seventh_year" title="Seventh year">seventh year</a> of education at Hogwarts, although the school was under the control of Voldemort. Seamus remained in the D.A. and fought bravely in the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-DH_14-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH-14">&#91;14&#93;</a></sup> It is currently unknown what he did after the <a href="/wiki/Second_Wizarding_War" title="Second Wizarding War">Second Wizarding War</a>.
</p>

<h3><span id="Early_life_(1980-1991)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_life_.281980-1991.29">Early life <span class="template-c">(1980-1991)</span></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Seamus_Finnigan?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Early life (1980-1991)">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Seamus_Finnigan?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Early life (1980-1991)"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<div class="quote">
<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;Harry Potter and the Philosopher&#39;s Stone">"<i>...the way Seamus Finnigan told it, he'd spent most of his childhood zooming around the countryside on his broomstick.</i>"</span></dd>
<dd>&#8212;Seamus' supposed childhood<sup class="noprint"><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Philosopher%27s_Stone" title="Harry Potter and the Philosopher&#39;s Stone">[src]</a></sup></dd>
<dd></dd></dl></div>
<p>Seamus was born to Muggle <a href="/wiki/Seamus_Finnigan%27s_father" title="Seamus Finnigan&#39;s father">Mr Finnigan</a> and his witch <a href="/wiki/Seamus_Finnigan%27s_mother" title="Seamus Finnigan&#39;s mother">wife</a><sup id="cite_ref-PS7_5-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PS7-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> at some point between the September of <a href="/wiki/1979" title="1979">1979</a> and the August of <a href="/wiki/1980" title="1980">1980</a>. It was mentioned that Seamus' father had no idea that his wife was a witch before they married and it "came as a nasty shock to him when he found out", but nevertheless accepted it.<sup id="cite_ref-PS7_5-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PS7-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Seamus came from an <a href="/wiki/Ireland" title="Ireland">Irish</a> family (as suggested by his name) and as shown in the Quidditch World Cup he attended just before his fourth year. It is possible that Seamus was from <a href="/wiki/Kenmare" title="Kenmare">Kenmare</a>, County Kerry due to his support of the <a href="/wiki/Kenmare_Kestrels" title="Kenmare Kestrels">local Quidditch team</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP11_15-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP11-15">&#91;15&#93;</a></sup> and him and his mother support of the <a href="/wiki/Irish_National_Quidditch_team" title="Irish National Quidditch team">Irish National Quidditch team</a> in the <a href="/wiki/1994_Quidditch_World_Cup" title="1994 Quidditch World Cup">1994 Quidditch World Cup</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-GOF7_2-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF7-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> He had an older cousin named <a href="/wiki/Fergus" title="Fergus">Fergus</a>, who would frequently <a href="/wiki/Apparition" title="Apparition">Apparate</a> just to annoy him. Seamus had plans to annoy Fergus back, once he passed his apparition test.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP_16-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP-16">&#91;16&#93;</a></sup>

When Harry and <a href="/wiki/Draco_Malfoy" title="Draco Malfoy">Draco Malfoy</a> were chosen to demonstrate blocking spells, Seamus and the rest of the class learned that Harry could <a href="/wiki/Parseltongue" title="Parseltongue">speak with snakes</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-COS11_24-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COS11-24">&#91;24&#93;</a></sup>

<p>In his first <a href="/wiki/Divination_(class)" title="Divination (class)">Divination</a> lesson with <a href="/wiki/Sybill_Trelawney" title="Sybill Trelawney">Sybill Trelawney</a>, the class debated whether or not she had seen a <a href="/wiki/Grim" title="Grim">Grim</a> in Harry's teacup. Tilting his head from side to side, Seamus expressed his opinion that, tilted right, the tea leaves did resemble a Grim, but tilted left, it looked more like a donkey.<sup id="cite_ref-POA6_26-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-POA6-26">&#91;26&#93;</a></sup> In his first <a href="/wiki/Potions_(class)" title="Potions (class)">Potions</a> lesson, Seamus told Harry and <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ron</a> that Sirius Black, an escaped <a href="/wiki/Azkaban" title="Azkaban">Azkaban</a> convict, had been spotted near the school. Looking excited, he informed them that a <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggle</a> woman had spotted Black and called the special hot line set up by the <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">Ministry of Magic</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-POA7_9-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-POA7-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>After lunch, Seamus had his first <a href="/wiki/Defence_Against_the_Dark_Arts" title="Defence Against the Dark Arts">Defence Against the Dark Arts</a> lesson with <a href="/wiki/Remus_Lupin" title="Remus Lupin">Remus Lupin</a>. When Seamus' turn came to face a <a href="/wiki/Boggart" title="Boggart">Boggart</a>, the <a href="/wiki/Non-being" class="mw-redirect" title="Non-being">non-being</a> turned into a <a href="/wiki/Banshee" title="Banshee">banshee</a> with floor-length black hair and a skeletal green face, representing his greatest inner fear. When the banshee began to shriek, Seamus used the <i><a href="/wiki/Boggart-Banishing_Spell" title="Boggart-Banishing Spell">Riddikulus</a></i> <a href="/wiki/Charm" title="Charm">charm</a> to take her voice away; for his participation, Seamus earned five <a href="/wiki/House_points" title="House points">House points</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-POA7_9-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-POA7-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup>

<p>On <a href="/wiki/Hallowe%27en" title="Hallowe&#39;en">Hallowe'en</a>, Seamus paid his first visit to <a href="/wiki/Hogsmeade" title="Hogsmeade">Hogsmeade</a> with the rest of the third years. It can be assumed that he largely spend this trip with his best friend Dean. When the <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor" title="Gryffindor">Gryffindors</a> learned that Sirius Black had attacked the <a href="/wiki/Fat_Lady" title="Fat Lady">Fat Lady</a>, they were all sent back to the <a href="/wiki/Great_Hall" title="Great Hall">Great Hall</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-POA8_27-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-POA8-27">&#91;27&#93;</a></sup> Once in the hall they were joined by the other Houses, in an attempt to keep the students safe. With the rest of Gryffindor, Seamus was aggravated by the choice of <a href="/wiki/Cadogan" title="Cadogan">Sir Cadogan</a> to guard the House's portrait hole calling him a "lunatic". When Seamus complained to <a href="/wiki/Head_Boy_or_Girl" title="Head Boy or Girl">Head Boy</a> <a href="/wiki/Percy_Weasley" title="Percy Weasley">Percy Weasley</a>, Percy replied that Sir Cadogan was the only one who willingly volunteered for the job, as he was unbalanced.<sup id="cite_ref-POA9_28-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-POA9-28">&#91;28&#93;</a></sup>

<p>Along with most of the school, Seamus went home for the Christmas holidays. When he returned he was impressed that Harry had received a <a href="/wiki/Firebolt" title="Firebolt">Firebolt</a> for Christmas.<sup id="cite_ref-POA12_29-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-POA12-29">&#91;29&#93;</a></sup> When Harry went down to breakfast the morning of his first Quidditch match with the broom, Seamus served as a "guard of honour" to protect the broom. After the <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor_Quidditch_team" title="Gryffindor Quidditch team">Gryffindor Quidditch team</a> defeated <a href="/wiki/Ravenclaw" title="Ravenclaw">Ravenclaw</a>, Seamus was very happy and cheered Harry, along with the other Gryffindors. Seamus was also awoken in the boys' dormitory when Ron announced that Sirius Black had <a href="/wiki/Sirius_Black%27s_break-in_of_Hogwarts_Castle_(1994)" title="Sirius Black&#39;s break-in of Hogwarts Castle (1994)">broken into</a> the dormitory with a <a href="/wiki/Sirius_Black%27s_knife" title="Sirius Black&#39;s knife">knife</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-POA13_30-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-POA13-30">&#91;30&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>When Professor Lupin resigned at the end of the year, after being exposed as a <a href="/wiki/Werewolf" title="Werewolf">werewolf</a>, Seamus was among the students who were sorry to see him go, along with the other Gryffindor students.<sup id="cite_ref-POA22_31-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-POA22-31">&#91;31&#93;</a></sup>

<p>Over the summer of <a href="/wiki/1994" title="1994">1994</a>, Seamus and his mother, along with Dean, attended the <a href="/wiki/Quidditch_World_Cup" title="Quidditch World Cup">Quidditch World Cup</a> between <a href="/wiki/Bulgaria" title="Bulgaria">Bulgaria</a> and <a href="/wiki/Ireland" title="Ireland">Ireland</a>. Being Irish, the pair obviously supported the latter; their tent was covered with shamrocks. When Seamus ran into <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry</a>, <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ron</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione</a>, they assured him that they would be supporting Ireland, as well. Presumably Seamus was delighted when the <a href="/wiki/Irish_National_Quidditch_team" title="Irish National Quidditch team">Irish National Quidditch team</a> won.<sup id="cite_ref-GOF7_2-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF7-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>By the start of term, Seamus was still wearing his Ireland rosette; it still called out the names of the Irish players, albeit feebly and exhaustedly.<sup id="cite_ref-GOF11_32-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF11-32">&#91;32&#93;</a></sup> At the Welcoming Feast, Seamus learned that the <a href="/wiki/Triwizard_Tournament" title="Triwizard Tournament">Triwizard Tournament</a> would be held at Hogwarts that year.<sup id="cite_ref-GOF12_33-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF12-33">&#91;33&#93;</a></sup> Seamus' first day of classes did not sit well with him. In his first class, <a href="/wiki/Herbology" title="Herbology">Herbology</a>, he was required to collect bubotuber pus for <a href="/wiki/Pomona_Sprout" title="Pomona Sprout">Professor Sprout</a>. In his next class, <a href="/wiki/Care_of_Magical_Creatures" title="Care of Magical Creatures">Care of Magical Creatures</a>, he was required to help look after Hagrid's newly bred <a href="/wiki/Blast-Ended_Skrewt" title="Blast-Ended Skrewt">Blast-Ended Skrewts</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-GOF13_34-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF13-34">&#91;34&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>When Harry's name mysteriously came out of the <a href="/wiki/Goblet_of_Fire" title="Goblet of Fire">Goblet of Fire</a>, Seamus was among the students who believed Harry had tricked the Goblet into letting him compete. On <a href="/wiki/25_December" title="25 December">Christmas Day</a>, Seamus attended the <a href="/wiki/Yule_Ball" title="Yule Ball">Yule Ball</a> with <a href="/wiki/Lavender_Brown" title="Lavender Brown">Lavender Brown</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-GOF22_35-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF22-35">&#91;35&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<p>On his first night back at school, Seamus got into an argument with Harry Potter about the latter's claim of Lord Voldemort's return. A devout reader of the <i><a href="/wiki/Daily_Prophet" title="Daily Prophet">Daily Prophet</a></i>, Seamus's mother believed what the papers were saying about Harry and <a href="/wiki/Albus_Dumbledore" title="Albus Dumbledore">Albus Dumbledore</a>, and had been very reluctant to allow Seamus to return to school. When Seamus asked Harry what really happened that night, Harry snapped at him to read the <i>Prophet</i> like his mother. When <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ron Weasley</a> arrived to break the argument up, Seamus thought that, for believing Harry, Ron was as insane as Harry was.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP11_15-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP11-15">&#91;15&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>After Harry's interview with <a href="/wiki/Rita_Skeeter" title="Rita Skeeter">Rita Skeeter</a> appeared in <i><a href="/wiki/The_Quibbler" title="The Quibbler">The Quibbler</a></i>, Seamus apologised for his behaviour. He told Harry that he now believed Voldemort was back and had sent a copy of Harry's interview to his mother. Seamus and Harry were soon friends again.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP_13-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP-13">&#91;13&#93;</a></sup>

He greatly missed his best friend <a href="/wiki/Dean_Thomas" title="Dean Thomas">Dean Thomas</a>, who was forced to go on the run because of his presumed <a href="/wiki/Muggle-born" title="Muggle-born">Muggle-born</a> status. Seamus was part of the restored Dumbledore's Army, who ended up staying in the <a href="/wiki/Room_of_Requirement" title="Room of Requirement">Room of Requirement</a> to escape the <a href="/wiki/Alecto_and_Amycus_Carrow" title="Alecto and Amycus Carrow">Carrows</a>. The school year was incredibly difficult for Dumbledore's Army. He, along with many others, started to show the cost of standing up to the new regime at Hogwarts: Seamus' face was so bruised that when Harry returned to the school in May of <a href="/wiki/1998" title="1998">1998</a>, Seamus was unrecognisable until he spoke.<sup id="cite_ref-DH_14-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH-14">&#91;14&#93;</a></sup>

<h3><span id="Later_life_(post_1998)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="Later_life_.28post_1998.29">Later life <span class="template-c">(post 1998)</span></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Seamus_Finnigan?veaction=edit&amp;section=11" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Later life (post 1998)">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Seamus_Finnigan?action=edit&amp;section=11" title="Edit section: Later life (post 1998)"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>He survived the Second Wizarding War and was seen sitting with Dean Thomas and <a href="/wiki/Aberforth_Dumbledore" title="Aberforth Dumbledore">Aberforth Dumbledore</a> once the battle had ended. It is unknown what happened to him after the war. It can be assumed that he attended the <a href="/wiki/2014" title="2014">2014</a> reunion of Dumbledore's Army.
</p><p>During the <a href="/wiki/Calamity" title="Calamity">Calamity</a> that affected the Wizarding World in the 2010s, Seamus appeared as a <a href="/wiki/Foundable" title="Foundable">Foundable</a>, clad in robes for the <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor_Quidditch_team" title="Gryffindor Quidditch team">Gryffindor Quidditch team</a> tryouts. He was pursued by a <a href="/wiki/Fire-breathing_chicken" title="Fire-breathing chicken">Fire-breathing chicken</a> <a href="/wiki/Confoundable" title="Confoundable">Confoundable</a>, necessitating the usage of the <a href="/wiki/Ebublio_Jinx" title="Ebublio Jinx">Ebublio Jinx</a> by volunteer members of the <a href="/wiki/Statute_of_Secrecy_Task_Force" title="Statute of Secrecy Task Force">Statute of Secrecy Task Force</a> to repel the fowls and return his foundable back to its original place.<sup id="cite_ref-WU_37-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-WU-37">&#91;37&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Physical_appearance">Physical appearance</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Seamus_Finnigan?veaction=edit&amp;section=12" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Physical appearance">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Seamus_Finnigan?action=edit&amp;section=12" title="Edit section: Physical appearance"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Being a Gryffindor, Seamus usually wore a red-and-gold tie and a black jumper along with his black Hogwarts robes. His uniform was always scruffy and untidy, making him look like a slob. During Seamus's final year at Hogwarts, his face had been battered and bruised by the <a href="/wiki/Alecto_and_Amycus_Carrow" title="Alecto and Amycus Carrow">Carrows</a> to an extent that neither Harry nor Dean Thomas could recognise him, until he spoke with his distinct Irish accent.<sup id="cite_ref-DH_14-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH-14">&#91;14&#93;</a></sup> Seamus had sandy coloured hair.<sup id="cite_ref-PS7_5-10" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PS7-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<p>Through his school years, Seamus generally appeared good-natured and easy-going. He was also rather <a href="/wiki/Bangs-and-smells_magic" title="Bangs-and-smells magic">clumsy, as he set fire to a feather</a> he was supposed to be levitating in his first year. He seems to display traits of more than one Hogwarts house, since the <a href="/wiki/Sorting_Hat" title="Sorting Hat">Sorting Hat</a> had to think for almost a minute before deciding to sort him into Gryffindor.<sup id="cite_ref-PS7_5-11" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PS7-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> Seamus was also rather curious, asking <a href="/wiki/Quirinus_Quirrell" title="Quirinus Quirrell">Professor Quirrell</a> where he got his <a href="/wiki/Quirinus_Quirrell%27s_turban" title="Quirinus Quirrell&#39;s turban">turban</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-PS8_17-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PS8-17">&#91;17&#93;</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/Nicholas_de_Mimsy-Porpington" title="Nicholas de Mimsy-Porpington">Nicholas de Mimsy-Porpington</a> how he could be "nearly headless",<sup id="cite_ref-PS7_5-12" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PS7-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> and <a href="/wiki/Severus_Snape" title="Severus Snape">Professor Snape</a> about <a href="/wiki/Inferius" title="Inferius">Inferi</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP_16-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP-16">&#91;16&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Of all the boys in Harry's dormitory, Seamus was the least enthusiastic supporter of <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> and <a href="/wiki/Albus_Dumbledore" title="Albus Dumbledore">Albus Dumbledore</a>, often demonstrating scepticism of Harry's claims. However, after reading Rita Skeeter's interview with Harry in the fifth year, he apologised to Harry and sent a copy to his "mam", as she did not believe that Voldemort was back. He did join <a href="/wiki/Dumbledore%27s_Army" title="Dumbledore&#39;s Army">Dumbledore's Army</a> sometime in <a href="/wiki/1996" title="1996">1996</a>, although he did not sign the actual membership list of the group.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP_13-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP-13">&#91;13&#93;</a></sup> After Dumbledore's death the <a href="/wiki/1997" title="1997">following year</a>, Seamus insisted on remaining at Hogwarts in order to attend the <a href="/wiki/Funeral_of_Albus_Dumbledore" title="Funeral of Albus Dumbledore">funeral</a> for the fallen headmaster, despite his mother's wish to remove him immediately from the perceived dangers at the school, demonstrating his allegiance to Dumbledore and Harry.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP_16-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP-16">&#91;16&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Seamus also demonstrated the characteristic bravery and loyalty of his house by opposing the <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a> at great personal risk during his final year at Hogwarts and in the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">final battle</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-DH_14-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH-14">&#91;14&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<p>Seamus was able to conjure a corporeal fox Patronus. <ul><li><b><a href="/wiki/Charm" title="Charm">Charms</a></b>: Seamus achieved a high O.W.L. score in this <a href="/wiki/Charms_(class)" title="Charms (class)">subject</a>, which enabled him to attend the N.E.W.T.-level class. <li><b><a href="/wiki/Transfiguration" title="Transfiguration">Transfiguration</a></b>: Seamus achieved a high mark on his O.W.L. exam, proving his proficiency in the <a href="/wiki/Transfiguration_(class)" title="Transfiguration (class)">subject</a>. He advanced to <a href="/wiki/Nastily_Exhausting_Wizarding_Test" title="Nastily Exhausting Wizarding Test">N.E.W.T.</a>-level.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP_16-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP-16">&#91;16&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Defence_Against_the_Dark_Arts" title="Defence Against the Dark Arts">Defence Against the Dark Arts</a></b>: Seamus achieved a high <a href="/wiki/Ordinary_Wizarding_Level" title="Ordinary Wizarding Level">O.W.L.</a> score in this subject,<sup id="cite_ref-HBP_16-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP-16">&#91;16&#93;</a></sup> likely because of his association with Dumbledore's Army, where he would have learnt many defensive and offensive <a href="/wiki/Spell" title="Spell">spells</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP_13-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP-13">&#91;13&#93;</a></sup> He advanced to the <a href="/wiki/Nastily_Exhausting_Wizarding_Test" title="Nastily Exhausting Wizarding Test">N.E.W.T.</a> class in 1996.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP9_39-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP9-39">&#91;39&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Pyrotechnics" title="Pyrotechnics">Pyrotechnics</a></b>: Whilst not a specific branch of magic, Seamus was very skilled in his ability to conjure or create <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magic</a> that would have a pyrotechnic effect, stemming from his <a href="/wiki/Bangs-and-smells_magic" title="Bangs-and-smells magic">various incendiary mishaps</a> in his first year.<sup id="cite_ref-PS10_10-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PS10-10">&#91;10&#93;</a></sup> This was demonstrated usefully during the Battle of Hogwarts when Professor McGonagall instructed him and <a href="/wiki/Neville_Longbottom" title="Neville Longbottom">Neville Longbottom</a> to explode the bridge in the <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Castle#The_Grounds" title="Hogwarts Castle">Hogwarts grounds</a> using <a href="/wiki/Charge" title="Charge">charges</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-DHF2_36-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DHF2-36">&#91;36&#93;</a></sup></li></ul>

<p>Seamus's best friend was <a href="/wiki/Dean_Thomas" title="Dean Thomas">Dean Thomas</a>, one of his dormmates, during their first year at Hogwarts, and the two remained best friends throughout their school years. The two were often seen together at school, and Dean attended the Quidditch World Cup with Seamus and his mother in 1994.<sup id="cite_ref-GOF_41-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF-41">&#91;41&#93;</a></sup> Seamus initially believed the smear campaign the Ministry of Magic and the <i>Daily Prophet</i> ran against Harry Potter when he claimed that Voldemort had returned, but this does not appear to have affected his friendship with Dean, who did believe Harry and who eventually managed to get Seamus to attend the final D.A. meeting of the year.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP_13-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP-13">&#91;13&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Seamus was also displeased when Dean was chosen as the replacement Chaser for the Gryffindor Quidditch team over him when <a href="/wiki/Katie_Bell" title="Katie Bell">Katie Bell</a> was injured, but again, the two still remained close friends. Since Dean could not<sup id="cite_ref-DH_14-11" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH-14">&#91;14&#93;</a></sup> return to Hogwarts for his final year, the two did not see each other for some time. When Dean eventually did return to Hogwarts in 1998 to fight in the final battle, Seamus was ecstatic to see him, and ran to hug him.
</p>

<p>Seamus was also friends with his other roommates, <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>, <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ron Weasley</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Neville_Longbottom" title="Neville Longbottom">Neville Longbottom</a>, though not to the same extent as with Dean Thomas. He and Harry had small fallings out over Harry being chosen as a <a href="/wiki/Triwizard_Tournament" title="Triwizard Tournament">Triwizard Champion</a><sup id="cite_ref-GOF_41-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF-41">&#91;41&#93;</a></sup> and Seamus's doubt over Harry's claim that <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a> had returned,<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP_13-10" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP-13">&#91;13&#93;</a></sup> but managed to patch things up. When Ron had a row with Harry himself about the <a href="/wiki/Goblet_of_Fire" title="Goblet of Fire">Goblet of Fire</a> choosing Harry as a champion, he spent a lot of time with Seamus and Dean. However, when Seamus expressed his disbelief about Harry's statements that <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a> was back, Ron supported Harry and got very angry with Seamus. However, they became friends again.
</p><p>Seamus also appeared to be friendly with <a href="/wiki/Lavender_Brown" title="Lavender Brown">Lavender Brown</a>, whom he took to the <a href="/wiki/Yule_Ball" title="Yule Ball">Yule Ball</a> in <a href="/wiki/1994" title="1994">1994</a><sup id="cite_ref-GOF_41-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF-41">&#91;41&#93;</a></sup> They may have been romantically involved around that time, though they may have just gone together as friends. Later on, Ron would get romantically involved with Lavender and there were no signs of bitterness between him and Seamus, which may indicate that Seamus and Lavender were never a couple.
</p><p>Seamus also seemed to have been friendly with classmates and fellow <a href="/wiki/Dumbledore%27s_Army" title="Dumbledore&#39;s Army">D.A.</a> members <a href="/wiki/Hannah_Abbott" title="Hannah Abbott">Hannah Abbott</a>, <a href="/wiki/Ernest_Macmillan" title="Ernest Macmillan">Ernie Macmillan</a> and <a href="/wiki/Susan_Bones" title="Susan Bones">Susan Bones</a>; during the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a>, he was frequently seen fighting alongside them, even though his best friend Dean Thomas had returned to Hogwarts. His friendships with Hannah, Susan and Ernie may have became closer when Dean was on the run and he had to make new friends, both Harry and Ron were also absent from school leaving Seamus with only Neville as a friend.<sup id="cite_ref-DH_14-12" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH-14">&#91;14&#93;</a></sup> Seamus had also met other members of the army, such as <a href="/wiki/Ginevra_Weasley" title="Ginevra Weasley">Ginny Weasley</a>, <a href="/wiki/Angelina_Johnson" title="Angelina Johnson">Angelina Johnson</a>, <a href="/wiki/Alicia_Spinnet" title="Alicia Spinnet">Alicia Spinnet</a>, <a href="/wiki/Katie_Bell" title="Katie Bell">Katie Bell</a>, <a href="/wiki/Lee_Jordan" title="Lee Jordan">Lee Jordan</a>, <a href="/wiki/Fred_and_George_Weasley" title="Fred and George Weasley">Fred and George Weasley</a>, <a href="/wiki/Colin_Creevey" title="Colin Creevey">Colin</a> and <a href="/wiki/Dennis_Creevey" title="Dennis Creevey">Dennis Creevey</a>, <a href="/wiki/Michael_Corner" title="Michael Corner">Michael Corner</a>, <a href="/wiki/Anthony_Goldstein" title="Anthony Goldstein">Anthony Goldstein</a>, <a href="/wiki/Terry_Boot" title="Terry Boot">Terry Boot</a>, <a href="/wiki/Luna_Lovegood" title="Luna Lovegood">Luna Lovegood</a>, <a href="/wiki/Cho_Chang" title="Cho Chang">Cho Chang</a>, <a href="/wiki/Marietta_Edgecombe" title="Marietta Edgecombe">Marietta Edgecombe</a>, <a href="/wiki/Justin_Finch-Fletchley" title="Justin Finch-Fletchley">Justin Finch-Fletchley</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Zacharias_Smith" title="Zacharias Smith">Zacharias Smith</a>, although their relationship with Seamus is unknown.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Seamus_Finnigan?veaction=edit&amp;section=20" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Seamus_Finnigan?action=edit&amp;section=20" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Seamus is an Irish version of the name James, which means "supplanter" (one who takes the place of another, usually by force). Finnigan is an Irish name that includes finn (white, fair) and means "fair-haired one."
</p>

<ul><li>Seamus has been played by Irish actor <a href="/wiki/Devon_Murray" title="Devon Murray">Devon Murray</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_(film_series)" title="Harry Potter (film series)">film adaptations</a> of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Philosopher%27s_Stone_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Philosopher&#39;s Stone (film)">Philosopher's Stone</a></i>, <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Chamber_of_Secrets_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets (film)">Chamber of Secrets</a></i>, <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Prisoner_of_Azkaban_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban (film)">Prisoner of Azkaban</a></i>, <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Goblet_of_Fire_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire (film)">Goblet of Fire</a></i>, <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Order_of_the_Phoenix_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix (film)">Order of the Phoenix</a></i>, <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Half-Blood_Prince_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince (film)">Half-Blood Prince</a></i>, and both parts of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Deathly_Hallows_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows (film)">Deathly Hallows</a></i>, and so is one of the 14 characters to appear in all 8 film adaptations.</li>
<li>In the Philosopher's Stone video game, Seamus (whose name is only mentioned in the dialogue) appears to be a Ravenclaw. Even more curiously he is mostly always seen next to a boy whose appearance matches the movie - Dean Thomas - and who is a Hufflepuff.</li>
<li>In the first three <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_(book_series)" title="Harry Potter (book series)">Harry Potter</a></i> <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_(film_series)" title="Harry Potter (film series)">film adaptations</a>, Seamus is credited as Seamus Finnegan.</li>
<li>Devon Murray does not fit the physical description given of Seamus Finnigan, but he does have the Irish accent needed for the role.</li>
<li>A running gag in the films is that Seamus keeps causing things to be set on fire and (sometimes) burning his eyebrows off. In the Philosopher's Stone, he blows up a feather in an attempt to make it levitate. In the Half-Blood Prince, he causes an explosion in his cauldron. This was shown prominently in <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Deathly_Hallows:_Part_2" title="Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 2">Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 2</a></i>, as he set up charges on the bridge so they could blow it up. Professor McGonagall states because of his incidents, that he has a penchant for it.</li>
<li>By the events of <i>Deathly Hallows: Part 2</i>, it appears that he is second in command to <a href="/wiki/Neville_Longbottom" title="Neville Longbottom">Neville Longbottom</a> in Dumbledore's Army. This is shown when Neville and Seamus lead a group of fighters to the <a href="/wiki/Wooden_Bridge" title="Wooden Bridge">Wooden Bridge</a> and when he advises Neville to stand down from Voldemort.</li>
<li>Devon Murray has the record for breaking the most fake wands on the Harry Potter set.</li>
<li>In the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Philosopher%27s_Stone_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Philosopher&#39;s Stone (film)">film adaptation</a> of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Philosopher%27s_Stone" title="Harry Potter and the Philosopher&#39;s Stone">Harry Potter and the Philosopher's Stone</a></i>, it is Hermione, not Seamus, who asks Sir <a href="/wiki/Nicholas_de_Mimsy-Porpington" title="Nicholas de Mimsy-Porpington">Nicholas de Mimsy-Porpington</a> about his nickname, "Nearly Headless Nick".</li>
<li>In <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Half-Blood_Prince_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince (film)">Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince (film)</a></i>, Seamus tries out for Beater not a Chaser.</li>
<li>In the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Goblet_of_Fire_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire (film)">film adaptation</a> of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Goblet_of_Fire" title="Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire">Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire</a></i>, it is a Beauxbaton student, (played by <a href="/wiki/Arielle_Brown" title="Arielle Brown">Arielle Brown</a>) not Lavender, with whom Seamus went to the <a href="/wiki/Yule_Ball" title="Yule Ball">Yule Ball</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-42" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-42">&#91;42&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-43" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-43">&#91;43&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li>In the Nickelodeon hit show <i>Zoey 101</i>, there is an Irish character in the Season 4 episode "Alone at PCA" named after Seamus Finnigan.</li>
<li>Seamus is a playable character in <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Deathly_Hallows:_Part_2_(video_game)" title="Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 2 (video game)">Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 2 (video game)</a></i>.<sup id="cite_ref-DHG2_44-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DHG2-44">&#91;44&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li>Alfie Enoch and Devon Murray have both expressed that they like the fan theory of Dean and Seamus ending up together.<sup id="cite_ref-45" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-45">&#91;45&#93;</a></sup></li></ul>
