<p><b>Mrs Granger</b> (temporarily known as <b>Monica Wilkins</b>)<sup id="cite_ref-DH_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> was an <a href="/wiki/England" title="England">English</a> <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggle</a> woman, the wife of <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger%27s_father" title="Hermione Granger&#39;s father">Mr Granger</a>, and mother of <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione Jean Granger</a>. Working as a <a href="/wiki/Dentist" title="Dentist">dentist</a> alongside her husband, she and her family lived in a <a href="/wiki/Grangers%27_house" class="mw-redirect" title="Grangers&#39; house">house</a> located in <a href="/wiki/England" title="England">England</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-DHPO_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DHPO-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>She and her husband were proud and accepting of their daughter's identity as a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch</a>, which explained some strange things which happened around her when she was a child. They entered the magical world in 1992 to buy Hermione's school supplies, and were assisted by <a href="/wiki/Arthur_Weasley" title="Arthur Weasley">Arthur Weasley</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-COS_4-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COS-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> Several years later, however, Hermione was forced to alter her parents' memories and give them new identities as Wendell and Monica Wilkins, to protect them from the <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-DH_2-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>After the <a href="/wiki/Second_Wizarding_War" title="Second Wizarding War">Second Wizarding War</a> ended, Hermione found Mrs Granger and her husband in <a href="/wiki/Australia" title="Australia">Australia</a> and returned their memories.<sup class="noprint">&#91;<a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Wikipedia:Citing_sources" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Wikipedia:Citing sources"><i><span title="The material in the vicinity of this tag needs references to reliable sources." style="white-space: nowrap;">citation needed</span></i></a>&#93;</sup> They gained grandchildren through Hermione and <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ron Weasley</a>'s marriage, <a href="/wiki/Rose_Granger-Weasley" title="Rose Granger-Weasley">Rose</a> and <a href="/wiki/Hugo_Weasley" class="mw-redirect" title="Hugo Weasley">Hugo Granger-Weasley</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-DH_2-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-A1_1-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-A1-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_life">Early life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger%27s_mother?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Early life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger%27s_mother?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Early life"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>

<p>Mrs Granger was born somewhere in Great Britain to <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggle</a> parents, at least one of whom may have been descended from a <a href="/wiki/Squib" title="Squib">Squib</a>.
</p><p>She studied for a career in <a href="/wiki/Dentist" title="Dentist">dentistry</a>. This was presumably how she met <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger%27s_father" title="Hermione Granger&#39;s father">Mr Granger</a>, who was in the same profession.
</p><p>She and Mr Granger both achieved a Licentiate in Dental Surgery, earning the prefix of "doctor", and married, living in a <a href="/wiki/Grangers%27_house" class="mw-redirect" title="Grangers&#39; house">house</a> on a street somewhere in England.
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Motherhood">Motherhood</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger%27s_mother?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Motherhood">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger%27s_mother?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Motherhood"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<div class="quote">
<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows">"<i>The Forest of Dean. I came camping here once, with my mum and dad.</i>"</span></dd>
<dd>&#8212;<a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione</a> mentioning her parents during the Horcrux hunt<sup class="noprint"><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Deathly_Hallows" title="Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows">[src]</a></sup></dd>

<p>Mrs Granger later became pregnant with the couple's child around 1978, and gave birth to a baby girl on <a href="/wiki/19_September" title="19 September">19 September</a>, <a href="/wiki/1979" title="1979">1979</a>. They named her Hermione Jean Granger,<sup id="cite_ref-DH_2-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> choosing "a nice, unusual" first name because they wanted to establish how educated and intelligent they were.<sup id="cite_ref-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>The couple gave Hermione a <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger%27s_bedroom" title="Hermione Granger&#39;s bedroom">bedroom</a> on the first-floor,<sup id="cite_ref-DHPO_3-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DHPO-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> and they lived happily for many years.
</p><p>At some point, Mr and Mrs Granger took Hermione camping in the <a href="/wiki/Forest_of_Dean" title="Forest of Dean">Forest of Dean</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-DH_2-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> They also went to a theatre on <a href="/wiki/Shaftesbury_Avenue" title="Shaftesbury Avenue">Shaftesbury Avenue</a> in London,<sup id="cite_ref-DHPO_3-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DHPO-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> and at least once went to <a href="/wiki/Tottenham_Court_Road" title="Tottenham Court Road">Tottenham Court Road</a> with her.<sup id="cite_ref-DH_2-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="1992-1993">1992-1993</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger%27s_mother?veaction=edit&amp;section=6" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: 1992-1993">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger%27s_mother?action=edit&amp;section=6" title="Edit section: 1992-1993"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<div class="quote">
<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets">"<i> </i>[...]<i> [Arthur Weasley] was distracted almost at once by the sight of Hermione's parents, who were standing nervously at the counter that ran all along the great marble hall, waiting for Hermione to introduce them.</i>"</span></dd>
<dd>&#8212;Description<sup class="noprint"><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Chamber_of_Secrets" title="Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets">[src]</a></sup></dd>
<dd></dd></dl></div><p>For the 1992–1993 school year, Mr and Mrs Granger had to purchase more school equipment for their daughter. Firstly, in order to buy the supplies, they went to <a href="/wiki/Gringotts_Wizarding_Bank" title="Gringotts Wizarding Bank">Gringotts Wizarding Bank</a> to exchange <a href="/wiki/Muggle_money" title="Muggle money">Muggle money</a> with the <a href="/wiki/Wizarding_currency" title="Wizarding currency">wizarding currency</a>.
</p><p>There, they met <a href="/wiki/Arthur_Weasley" title="Arthur Weasley">Arthur Weasley</a>, a wizard obsessed with Muggles, and his family, who were friends of their daughter. Arthur was fascinated by the ten-pound notes that Mr Granger had in his hand, and asked them all about their money.
</p>

<p>The Grangers went with the <a href="/wiki/Weasley_family" title="Weasley family">Weasleys</a> to Flourish and Blotts to purchase spellbooks with their new money. <a href="/wiki/Gilderoy_Lockhart" title="Gilderoy Lockhart">Gilderoy Lockhart</a> was there doing book signings, for which Mr and Mrs Granger queued up with the Weasleys. After <a href="/wiki/Lucius_Malfoy" title="Lucius Malfoy">Lucius Malfoy</a> approached Arthur and began arguing, Mrs Granger and her husband watched apprehensively. Being a pure-blood supremacist, Malfoy scorned Arthur for being in the Grangers' company, glancing at them. The two wizards proceeded to fight each other, before <a href="/wiki/Rubeus_Hagrid" title="Rubeus Hagrid">Rubeus Hagrid</a> stopped them.
</p><p>The Grangers left with the Weasleys, shocked and frightened by what they had just witnessed. They went to the <a href="/wiki/Leaky_Cauldron" title="Leaky Cauldron">Leaky Cauldron</a> Inn, sitting by the fireplace, before saying farewell to the Weasleys and <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> and leaving with their daughter for the Muggle world.
</p><p>After the school term ended, Mr and Mrs Granger took Hermione on holiday to France during the summer holidays. There, Hermione ate <a href="/wiki/Bouillabaisse" title="Bouillabaisse">bouillabaisse</a>, which she liked the taste of.<sup id="cite_ref-GOF_7-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="1995-1996">1995-1996</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger%27s_mother?veaction=edit&amp;section=7" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: 1995-1996">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger%27s_mother?action=edit&amp;section=7" title="Edit section: 1995-1996"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<div class="quote">
<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix"><b>Harry</b>: "<i>What are you doing here? I thought you were skiing with your mum and dad.</i>"</span></dd>
<dd><b>Hermione</b>: "<i>Well, to tell the truth, skiing's not really my thing. So I've come for Christmas... <dd>&#8212; <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione</a> speaking with <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a><sup class="noprint"><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Order_of_the_Phoenix" title="Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix">[src]</a></sup></dd></dl>
</div><p>During the 1995–1996 school year, Mr and Mrs Granger arranged a skiing trip over the Christmas holidays, possibly in France, where they had gone once before.
</p><p>However, Hermione told them at the last minute that she wanted to stay at Hogwarts in order to study hard for her fifth year exams, the <a href="/wiki/Ordinary_Wizarding_Level" title="Ordinary Wizarding Level">Ordinary Wizarding Levels</a>, which are roughly equivalent to <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggle</a> O-Levels. Mrs Granger and her husband were both disappointed, but, because they wanted their daughter to achieve good grades, they understood.
</p>

<p>One afternoon, Mrs Granger called upstairs to Hermione's bedroom, telling her that she had cooked her dinner. She and her husband were sitting in the living room looking at a house in <a href="/wiki/Australia" title="Australia">Australia</a> on an unnamed television programme.<sup id="cite_ref-DHPO_3-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DHPO-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Hermione came downstairs with her wand and secretly cast a <a href="/wiki/False_memory_charm" class="mw-redirect" title="False memory charm">False memory charm</a> on both of her parents. She made them believe that they were called Wendell and Monica Wilkins, a couple with a desire to go to Australia and live there. She did this in order to keep them safe should the <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a> come after them.
</p><p>"Monica" and "Wendell" proceeded to act on their apparent ambition to move, and quickly packed up their belongings and left their house.
</p><p>It is possible that the "Wilkinses" moved into a house along Australia's coast which they had been admiring before their daughter bewitched them. Otherwise, it is unknown exactly where they went or what they did there.
</p><p>Immediately after the conclusion of the <a href="/wiki/Second_Wizarding_War" title="Second Wizarding War">Second Wizarding War</a>, Hermione sought out her parents and returned their memories. They presumably then returned to England.
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Later_life">Later life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger%27s_mother?veaction=edit&amp;section=9" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Later life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger%27s_mother?action=edit&amp;section=9" title="Edit section: Later life"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Mrs Granger and her husband gained a son-in-law when Hermione married <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ron Weasley</a>. The two of them proceeded to have two children, <a href="/wiki/Rose_Granger-Weasley" title="Rose Granger-Weasley">Rose</a> and <a href="/wiki/Hugo_Granger-Weasley" title="Hugo Granger-Weasley">Hugo</a>.
</p><p>The Grangers were presumably close to their grandchildren, as Ron referred to his father as "Granddad Weasley", implying that Mr Granger was "Granddad Granger".
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Physical_description">Physical description</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger%27s_mother?veaction=edit&amp;section=10" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Physical description">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger%27s_mother?action=edit&amp;section=10" title="Edit section: Physical description"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Mrs Granger had dark brown hair, green eyes, and pale skin. She was only slightly shorter than her husband and appeared to favour dark clothing, such as black and blue, especially when she was younger.<sup id="cite_ref-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<p>Mr and Mrs Granger first met the <a href="/wiki/Weasley_family" title="Weasley family">Weasley family</a> in <a href="/wiki/Gringotts_Wizarding_Bank" title="Gringotts Wizarding Bank">Gringotts Wizarding Bank</a>, where they stood nervously waiting for their daughter to introduce them.
</p><p>The couple came to trust the Weasleys very much, allowing their daughter to stay at their house over the holidays, prior to the school term, and to go to the <a href="/wiki/Quidditch_World_Cup" title="Quidditch World Cup">Quidditch World Cup</a> in <a href="/wiki/1994" title="1994">1994</a>.
</p><p>The two families were brought even closer when Hermione married <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ronald Weasley</a> and had <a href="/wiki/Rose_Granger-Weasley" title="Rose Granger-Weasley">Rose</a> and <a href="/wiki/Hugo_Granger-Weasley" title="Hugo Granger-Weasley">Hugo</a>.
</p>

<ul><li>Mrs Granger was portrayed by <a href="/wiki/Heather_Bleasdale" title="Heather Bleasdale">Heather Bleasdale</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Chamber_of_Secrets_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets (film)">film adaptation</a> of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Chamber_of_Secrets" title="Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets">Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets</a></i>. She was replaced by <a href="/wiki/Michelle_Fairley" title="Michelle Fairley">Michelle Fairley</a> for <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Deathly_Hallows:_Part_1" title="Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 1">Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 1</a></i>.<sup id="cite_ref-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li>In a deleted scene from <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Deathly_Hallows:_Part_1" title="Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 1">Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 1</a></i>, <a href="/wiki/Corban_Yaxley" title="Corban Yaxley">Corban Yaxley</a> comes searching for the Grangers at their home, but only finds it empty after their memory modification and relocation to Australia.<sup id="cite_ref-10" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-10">&#91;10&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li>It is possible that Mrs Granger's name is Jean Granger, as <a href="/wiki/J._K._Rowling" title="J. K. Rowling">J. K. Rowling</a> repeatedly gives the firstborn son or daughter their parent of the same sex's given name as a middle name. Notable examples include <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry James</a>, <a href="/wiki/William_Weasley" title="William Weasley">William Arthur</a>, <a href="/wiki/Ginevra_Weasley" title="Ginevra Weasley">Ginevra Molly</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Albus_Dumbledore" title="Albus Dumbledore">Albus Percival</a>.</li>
<li>In the earliest draft of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Philosopher%27s_Stone" title="Harry Potter and the Philosopher&#39;s Stone">Harry Potter and the Philosopher's Stone</a></i> the Potters lived on an island, and the Grangers lived on the shore nearby. When Voldemort killed James and Lily, Mr Granger noticed an explosion. He got on his boat and rowed out to the island to see what had happened, and found Harry.<sup id="cite_ref-11" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-11">&#91;11&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li>Mrs Granger was originally intended to have another <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger%27s_sister" title="Hermione Granger&#39;s sister">daughter</a>, but she was cut because, by <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Order_of_the_Phoenix" title="Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix">Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix</a></i>, she still had not made an appearance and <a href="/wiki/J._K._Rowling" title="J. K. Rowling">J. K. Rowling</a> felt that it was too late to introduce her.</li>
<li>"<a href="/wiki/Wilkins_family" title="Wilkins family">Wilkins</a>" is the surname of a magical family.</li></ul>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Name_etymology">Name etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger%27s_mother?veaction=edit&amp;section=16" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Name etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger%27s_mother?action=edit&amp;section=16" title="Edit section: Name etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>"Granger" is derived from <i>grange</i>, a barn or farm with buildings that store grain, thus "granger" is a term for a farm bailiff. Also, "Granger" is a member of the Grange Movement of the United States, which involved agricultural activism in the nineteenth century.
</p><p>The name "Monica" is of unknown meaning, most likely of North African or Phoenician origin. In the 4th century this name was borne by the North African saint Monica of Hippo, the mother of Saint Augustine, whom she converted to Christianity. Since the Middle Ages it has been associated with Latin moneo "advisor" and Greek monos "one". As an English name, Monica has been in general use since the 18th century.
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="J._K._Rowling">J. K. Rowling</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger%27s_mother?veaction=edit&amp;section=17" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: J. K. Rowling">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger%27s_mother?action=edit&amp;section=17" title="Edit section: J. K. Rowling"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>On Mr and Mrs Granger, <a href="/wiki/J._K._Rowling" title="J. K. Rowling">J. K. Rowling</a> said "<i>I have deliberately kept Hermione's family in the background. You see so much of Ron’s family so I thought that I would keep Hermione's family, by contrast, quite ordinary. They are dentists, as you know. They are a bit bemused by their odd daughter but quite proud of her all the same</i>"<sup id="cite_ref-HPL_12-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HPL-12">&#91;12&#93;</a></sup>.
</p><p>After a <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_series" title="Harry Potter series">Harry Potter</a></i> fan asked if the Grangers would appear again, Rowling said, "<i>Well we've seen them very briefly, but they're dentists so they're not that interesting</i>"<sup id="cite_ref-HPL_12-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HPL-12">&#91;12&#93;</a></sup>.
</p>
