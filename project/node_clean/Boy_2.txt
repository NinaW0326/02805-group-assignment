This <b>individual</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> and a student at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a>, and was <a href="/wiki/Sorting_Hat" title="Sorting Hat">sorted</a> into <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor" title="Gryffindor">Gryffindor</a>. He attended <a href="/wiki/Care_of_Magical_Creatures" title="Care of Magical Creatures">Care of Magical Creatures</a> class during the <a href="/wiki/1993%E2%80%931994_school_year" title="1993–1994 school year">1993–1994 school year</a>, and was present when <a href="/wiki/Draco_Malfoy" title="Draco Malfoy">Draco Malfoy</a> was provocatively attacked by <a href="/wiki/Buckbeak" title="Buckbeak">Buckbeak</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-POAF_3-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-POAF-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
