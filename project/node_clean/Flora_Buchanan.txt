<b>Flora Buchanan</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Scotland" title="Scotland">Scottish</a> <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch</a> who lived in the <a href="/wiki/19th_century" title="19th century">19th century</a>. She was the daughter of <a href="/wiki/Angus_Buchanan%27s_father" class="mw-redirect" title="Angus Buchanan&#39;s father">Mr</a> and <a href="/wiki/Angus_Buchanan%27s_mother" class="mw-redirect" title="Angus Buchanan&#39;s mother">Mrs Buchanan</a> and the older sister of <a href="/wiki/Angus_Buchanan" title="Angus Buchanan">Angus Buchanan</a>.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Flora_Buchanan?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Flora_Buchanan?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Born into the <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizarding</a> <a href="/wiki/Buchanan_family" title="Buchanan family">Buchanan family</a> sometime in the mid-<a href="/wiki/19th_century" title="19th century">19th century</a>, Flora grew up in a village in the <a href="/wiki/Scottish_Borders" title="Scottish Borders">Scottish Borders</a> with ten siblings, including <a href="/wiki/Hamish_Buchanan" title="Hamish Buchanan">Hamish</a> and <a href="/wiki/Angus_Buchanan" title="Angus Buchanan">Angus</a> (the latter of whom, at least, was younger than her). The Buchanan children had a reputation for being rowdy and large. Flora and her sisters won the village <a href="/wiki/Tug-of-war" title="Tug-of-war">tug-of-war</a> competition every year.
</p><p>When Flora turned eleven, she received a <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_acceptance_letter" title="Hogwarts acceptance letter">Hogwarts acceptance letter</a>, and began attending <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a>. The local <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggle</a> villagers, who were unaware of the Buchanan family's <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magical</a> heritage, assumed that she had been sent to a corrective facility or mental institution. It is unknown which <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Houses" title="Hogwarts Houses">House</a> she was <a href="/wiki/Sorting_ceremony" title="Sorting ceremony">sorted</a> into whilst she attended Hogwarts.
</p><p>Flora and her other siblings were especially fond of their brother Angus. Since <a href="/wiki/Angus_Buchanan%27s_father" class="mw-redirect" title="Angus Buchanan&#39;s father">their father</a> was highly prejudiced against <a href="/wiki/Squib" title="Squib">Squibs</a>, Flora and her siblings covered for Angus when, unlike them, he displayed no signs of <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magical</a> ability. After no Hogwarts acceptance letter arrived for Angus, Flora forged one for him, thus keeping their parents in the dark for a few more weeks.<sup id="cite_ref-Pottermore_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Pottermore-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
