His presence was noted on the <a href="/wiki/Marauder%27s_Map" title="Marauder&#39;s Map">Marauder's Map</a> during the <a href="/wiki/1993%E2%80%931994_school_year" title="1993–1994 school year">1993–1994 school year</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
