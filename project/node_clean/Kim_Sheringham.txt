<b>Kim Sheringham</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch</a> and <a href="/wiki/Ravenclaw" title="Ravenclaw">Ravenclaw</a> student at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> in the <a href="/wiki/1990s" title="1990s">1990s</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-HBPG_3-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBPG-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> 
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Kim_Sheringham?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Kim_Sheringham?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>In <a href="/wiki/1996" title="1996">1996</a>, she took <a href="/wiki/Luna_Lovegood%27s_dress_robes" title="Luna Lovegood&#39;s dress robes">a silver dress</a> belonging to <a href="/wiki/Luna_Lovegood" title="Luna Lovegood">Luna Lovegood</a>, which she described as "<i>horrid</i>" and thought needed to be hidden "<i>somewhere dark.</i>" Luna wanted to wear this dress to <a href="/wiki/Horace_Slughorn" title="Horace Slughorn">Professor Slughorn</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Slug_Club_Christmas_party" title="Slug Club Christmas party">Christmas party</a>. However, although she said she thought it ought to be kept out of sight, Kim wore the dress herself in public, allowing <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> to find her in the <a href="/wiki/Clock_Tower_Courtyard" title="Clock Tower Courtyard">Clock Tower Courtyard</a>. Harry had to give her a bottle of <a href="/wiki/Butterbeer" title="Butterbeer">Butterbeer</a> in order to get her to give the dress back.<sup id="cite_ref-HBPG_3-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBPG-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
