<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Sam_(Obliviator)?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Sam_(Obliviator)?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>In <a href="/wiki/1926" title="1926">1926</a>, Sam was tasked with <a href="/wiki/Memory_Charm" title="Memory Charm">wiping the memory</a> of the <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">No-Maj</a> <a href="/wiki/Jacob_Kowalski" title="Jacob Kowalski">Jacob Kowalski</a> after he entered the MACUSA headquarters, but was intercepted by <a href="/wiki/Queenie_Goldstein" title="Queenie Goldstein">Queenie Goldstein</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-fb68_1-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-fb68-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> Queenie, a <a href="/wiki/Legilimency" title="Legilimency">Legilimens</a>, was able to read Sam's mind and see that he was in relationships with two women at the same time, fellow MACUSA employees <a href="/wiki/Ruby_(witch)" title="Ruby (witch)">Ruby</a> and <a href="/wiki/Cecily" title="Cecily">Cecily</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-fb68_1-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-fb68-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> Queenie threatened to reveal his infidelity and was thus able to convince him not to Obliviate Jacob.<sup id="cite_ref-fb68_1-10" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-fb68-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Physical_Appearance">Physical Appearance</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Sam_(Obliviator)?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Physical Appearance">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Sam_(Obliviator)?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Physical Appearance"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
