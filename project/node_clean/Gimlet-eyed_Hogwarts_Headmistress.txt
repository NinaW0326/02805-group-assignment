<p>This <b>unidentified female Head</b> was a gimlet-eyed <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch</a> who, at some point, became <a href="/wiki/Headmaster" title="Headmaster">Headmistress</a> of <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a>. In <a href="/wiki/1995" title="1995">1995</a>, she threatened fellow Hogwarts Headmaster <a href="/wiki/Phineas_Nigellus_Black" title="Phineas Nigellus Black">Phineas Nigellus Black</a> with her unusually thick wand when he told the current Hogwarts Headmaster <a href="/wiki/Albus_Dumbledore" title="Albus Dumbledore">Albus Dumbledore</a> he didn't feel like passing a message onto his great-great grandson, <a href="/wiki/Sirius_Black" title="Sirius Black">Sirius Black</a>. Phineas Black apparently took her seriously enough to rapidly revise his position.
</p><p>In <a href="/wiki/1998" title="1998">1998</a>, she, along with the other portraits of former Heads, gave <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> a standing ovation after he <a href="/wiki/Voldemort%27s_Last_Stand" title="Voldemort&#39;s Last Stand">killed</a> the <a href="/wiki/Dark_Arts" title="Dark Arts">Dark</a> <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a>.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Personality_and_traits">Personality and traits</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Gimlet-eyed_Hogwarts_Headmistress?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Personality and traits">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Gimlet-eyed_Hogwarts_Headmistress?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Personality and traits"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>This witch seemed to have a forceful personality as she was quick to threaten Phineas Nigellus Black with her wand when he ignored Dumbledore's request. Apparently she was rather intimidating as she was taken seriously by Black. Due to this she may have been a skilled duelist when she was alive.
</p><p>Her portrait appeared to be extremely loyal towards Albus Dumbledore, and presumably towards whoever was the current headmaster/headmistress.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Gimlet-eyed_Hogwarts_Headmistress?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Gimlet-eyed_Hogwarts_Headmistress?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>It is possible that this <a href="/wiki/Headmaster" title="Headmaster">Headmistress</a> was the <a href="/wiki/Headteacher_of_Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry_during_1792" title="Headteacher of Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry during 1792">Headteacher of Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry during 1792</a>.</li>
<li>At one point she threatened Phineas Black with her wand. It's unknown if portraits can use magic within their portraits, though Phineas's reaction indicates that she could potentially harm him. It's also possible that she simply would've used her wand as a club.</li></ul>
