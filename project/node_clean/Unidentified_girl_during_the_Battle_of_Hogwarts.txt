This <b>girl</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch</a> who fought in the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a>. She was injured in an unknown manner, probably from a <a href="/wiki/Spell" title="Spell">spell</a>. During the hour long break in which the <a href="/wiki/Dark_Wizard" title="Dark Wizard">Dark Wizard</a> <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a> ordered his <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a> to stop fighting, the girl was comforted by <a href="/wiki/Ginevra_Weasley" title="Ginevra Weasley">Ginny Weasley</a>. She was scared and wanted to go home.<sup id="cite_ref-DH32_2-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH32-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_girl_during_the_Battle_of_Hogwarts?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_girl_during_the_Battle_of_Hogwarts?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>As the girl is not known to be a member of <a href="/wiki/Dumbledore%27s_Army" title="Dumbledore&#39;s Army">Dumbledore's Army</a> or the <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor_Quidditch_team" title="Gryffindor Quidditch team">Gryffindor Quidditch team</a>, it can be assumed that she was at school prior to the event.</li>
<li>In the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Deathly_Hallows:_Part_2_(video_game)" title="Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 2 (video game)">second video game adaptation</a> of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Deathly_Hallows" title="Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows">Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows</a></i> during <i>Surrender</i>, <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> came across some students while walking through the <a href="/wiki/Viaduct_Courtyard" title="Viaduct Courtyard">Viaduct Courtyard</a>. One of them was a blonde-haired <a href="/wiki/Hufflepuff" title="Hufflepuff">Hufflepuff</a> girl with brown skin who says the same as this individual said. It is unknown if this is her, as she was not comforted by Ginny.</li></ul>
