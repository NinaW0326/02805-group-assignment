<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Boy_who_kept_a_bezoar_in_his_pocket?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Boy_who_kept_a_bezoar_in_his_pocket?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<div class="quote">
<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;Harry Potter and the Philosopher&#39;s Stone (video game)">"<i>McGonagall is the head of Gryffindor, but she'll still deduct House points from them without mercy.</i>"</span></dd>
<dd>&#8212;This boy speaking to <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> on the first day of term<sup class="noprint"><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Philosopher%27s_Stone_(video_game)" title="Harry Potter and the Philosopher&#39;s Stone (video game)">[src]</a></sup></dd>
<dd></dd></dl></div><p>This boy started attending <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> in the <a href="/wiki/1991-1992_school_year" class="mw-redirect" title="1991-1992 school year">1991-1992 school year</a>, and was <a href="/wiki/Sorting_ceremony" title="Sorting ceremony">Sorted</a> into <a href="/wiki/Ravenclaw" title="Ravenclaw">Ravenclaw</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-PSgba_2-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PSgba-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>On the first day of term, he was pacing by the <a href="/wiki/First-floor_corridor" title="First-floor corridor">First-floor corridor</a>, shortly before <a href="/wiki/Quirinus_Quirrell" title="Quirinus Quirrell">Quirinus Quirrell</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Defence_Against_the_Dark_Arts" title="Defence Against the Dark Arts">Defence Against the Dark Arts</a> class. Shortly before his first <a href="/wiki/Potions_(class)" title="Potions (class)">Potions</a> class, he commented on <a href="/wiki/Severus_Snape" title="Severus Snape">Professor Snape</a>'s unpleasant demeanour: "<i>Snape always looks like he smells something bad</i>". He did not complete Snape's assignment of gathering all the ingredients for the <a href="/wiki/Wiggenweld_Potion" title="Wiggenweld Potion">Wiggenweld Potion</a> (only <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry</a>, <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ron</a> and <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione</a> managed to procure <a href="/wiki/Wiggentree_bark" title="Wiggentree bark">Wiggentree bark</a>) â€” at one point, he expressed his wish that someone made an Instant Wiggenweld Potion mix, given the difficulty of gathering all the required ingredients.<sup id="cite_ref-PSgba_2-10" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PSgba-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<div class="quote">
<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;Harry Potter and the Philosopher&#39;s Stone (video game)">"<i>My brother told me if you take off fast enough, you can look back and see the top of your head.</i>"</span></dd>
<dd>&#8212;Shortly before his first <a href="/wiki/Flying_(class)" title="Flying (class)">Flying</a> lesson.<sup class="noprint"><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Philosopher%27s_Stone_(video_game)" title="Harry Potter and the Philosopher&#39;s Stone (video game)">[src]</a></sup></dd>
<dd></dd></dl></div>
<p>Before the <a href="/wiki/First_year" title="First year">first-years'</a> first <a href="/wiki/Flying_(class)" title="Flying (class)">Flying</a> lesson, he spent some time chatting about it in the <a href="/wiki/Dungeons" title="Dungeons">Dungeons</a>. According to <a href="/wiki/Boy_who_kept_a_bezoar_in_his_pocket%27s_brother" title="Boy who kept a bezoar in his pocket&#39;s brother">his brother</a>, it was possible for one to take off with such speed that one could look back and see the top of one's head.<sup id="cite_ref-PSgba_2-11" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PSgba-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Shortly after the <a href="/wiki/Hallowe%27en_feast" class="mw-redirect" title="Hallowe&#39;en feast">Hallowe'en feast</a>, he mockingly told <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> "<i>I thought trolls lived under bridges, not in restrooms</i>".<sup id="cite_ref-PSgba_2-12" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PSgba-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<div class="quote">
<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;Harry Potter and the Philosopher&#39;s Stone (video game)">"<i>I am not going to get levitation sickness, am I?</i>"</span></dd>
<dd>&#8212;Shortly before his first <a href="/wiki/Charms_(class)" title="Charms (class)">Charms</a> lesson.<sup class="noprint"><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Philosopher%27s_Stone_(video_game)" title="Harry Potter and the Philosopher&#39;s Stone (video game)">[src]</a></sup></dd>
<dd></dd></dl></div>
<p>Shortly before <a href="/wiki/Charms_(class)" title="Charms (class)">Charms</a> class, he wondered if practising the <a href="/wiki/Levitation_Charm" title="Levitation Charm">Levitation Charm</a> would provoke <a href="/wiki/Levitation_sickness" title="Levitation sickness">levitation sickness</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-PSgba_2-13" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PSgba-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> He also made fun of <a href="/wiki/Quirinus_Quirrell" title="Quirinus Quirrell">Quirinus Quirrell</a> before one of his <a href="/wiki/Defence_Against_the_Dark_Arts" title="Defence Against the Dark Arts">Defence Against the Dark Arts</a> class concerning the <a href="/wiki/Wand-Lighting_Charm" title="Wand-Lighting Charm">Wand-Lighting Charm</a>, by saying "<i>I wonder if Quirrell is as scared of the light as he is of the dark?</i>".<sup id="cite_ref-PSgba_2-14" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PSgba-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<div class="quote">
<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;Harry Potter and the Philosopher&#39;s Stone (video game)">"<i>It's always funny until someone gets hurt or <a href="/wiki/Avifors_Spell" title="Avifors Spell">turns into a bird</a>.</i>"</span></dd>
<dd>&#8212;This student is hit by a spell.<sup class="noprint"><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Philosopher%27s_Stone_(video_game)" title="Harry Potter and the Philosopher&#39;s Stone (video game)">[src]</a></sup></dd>
<dd></dd></dl></div>
<p>He did not seem to engage in the rumours the rest of the student body conveyed: about the <a href="/wiki/Third-floor_corridor" title="Third-floor corridor">Forbidden Corridor</a>, he said "<i>I don't think there's anything in the forbidden corridor. And I see no reason to prove that theory</i>"; about the <a href="/wiki/Restricted_Section" title="Restricted Section">Restricted Section</a> he said "<i>People say a lot of rubbish about the Restricted Section</i>".<sup id="cite_ref-PSgba_2-15" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PSgba-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> He also thought Common Rooms were improperly named: "<i>Why are they called Common rooms when only people from a certain house can go into them? Doesn't that make them uncommon rooms?</i>".<sup id="cite_ref-PSgba_2-16" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PSgba-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<div class="quote">
<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;Harry Potter and the Philosopher&#39;s Stone (video game)">"<i>Sometimes Hufflepuff players apologise when they score. That's so nice of them.</i>"</span></dd>
<dd>&#8212;The boy.<sup class="noprint"><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Philosopher%27s_Stone_(video_game)" title="Harry Potter and the Philosopher&#39;s Stone (video game)">[src]</a></sup></dd>
<dd></dd></dl></div>
<p>Throghout the school year, he was confident that <a href="/wiki/Ravenclaw_Quidditch_team" title="Ravenclaw Quidditch team">Ravenclaw Quidditch team</a> would win the <a href="/wiki/Inter-House_Quidditch_Cup" title="Inter-House Quidditch Cup">Quidditch Cup</a> ("<i>The Quidditch Cup is as good as in our hands. Maybe we should wash 'em</i>"), and seemed to be rather competitive about it (<i>It isn't whether you win or lose... wait a minute, yes it is!</i>).<sup id="cite_ref-PSgba_2-17" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PSgba-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
