<b>Aristotle Twelvetrees</b> was an <a href="/wiki/United_States_of_America" title="United States of America">American</a> <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-pottermore-rappaport_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-pottermore-rappaport-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> He served as <a href="/wiki/Keeper_of_Treasure_and_Dragots" title="Keeper of Treasure and Dragots">Keeper of Treasure and Dragots</a> during the <a href="/wiki/President_of_the_Magical_Congress_of_the_United_States_of_America" title="President of the Magical Congress of the United States of America">presidency</a> of <a href="/wiki/Emily_Rappaport" title="Emily Rappaport">Emily Rappaport</a> in the late <a href="/wiki/18th_century" title="18th century">18th century</a>, and was apparently good at his job, being described as a "competent man."<sup id="cite_ref-pottermore-rappaport_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-pottermore-rappaport-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Aristotle_Twelvetrees?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Aristotle_Twelvetrees?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>He had a daughter, <a href="/wiki/Dorcus_Twelvetrees" title="Dorcus Twelvetrees">Dorcus</a>, who did not take after him.<sup id="cite_ref-pottermore-rappaport_1-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-pottermore-rappaport-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> She did poorly at <a href="/wiki/Ilvermorny_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Ilvermorny School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Ilvermorny</a> and was still living with him at the time he took office.<sup id="cite_ref-pottermore-rappaport_1-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-pottermore-rappaport-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> Her fraternisation with the <a href="/wiki/Muggle" class="mw-redirect" title="Muggle">No-Maj</a> <a href="/wiki/Bartholomew_Barebone" title="Bartholomew Barebone">Bartholomew Barebone</a> lead to one of the greatest breaches of the <a href="/wiki/International_Statute_of_Wizarding_Secrecy" title="International Statute of Wizarding Secrecy">International Statute of Secrecy</a> in history and prompted the passage of <a href="/wiki/Rappaport%27s_Law" title="Rappaport&#39;s Law">Rappaport's Law</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-pottermore-rappaport_1-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-pottermore-rappaport-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
