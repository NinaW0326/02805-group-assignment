The <b>Prime Minister of the United Kingdom of Great Britain and Northern Ireland</b>, informally shortened to <b>Prime Minister</b>, is an appointed <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggle</a> individual to serve as head of government of the <a href="/wiki/Great_Britain" title="Great Britain">United Kingdom</a>.
</p><p>The British Prime Minister was traditionally informed of the existence of the <a href="/wiki/Wizarding_world" title="Wizarding world">wizarding world</a> on the night of their appointment and was occasionally visited by their wizard counterpart, the <a href="/wiki/Minister_for_Magic" title="Minister for Magic">Minister for Magic</a>, over affairs that would have an impact on the Muggle world, such as the escape of <a href="/wiki/Sirius_Black" title="Sirius Black">Sirius Black</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-PA3_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PA3-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> The Prime Minster was also later informed of the mass breakout from <a href="/wiki/Azkaban" title="Azkaban">Azkaban</a> and the return of <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a>. The secrecy of the wizarding world is maintained in that, in the event that the Prime Minister were to tell anyone of their meetings with the wizards, it is hardly likely that they would be believed.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
