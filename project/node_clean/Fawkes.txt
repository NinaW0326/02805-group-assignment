<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets">"<i>About time, too. He's been looking dreadful for days; I've been telling him to get a move on.</i>"</span></dd>
<dd>&#8212;After Fawkes burst into flames<sup class="noprint"><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Chamber_of_Secrets" title="Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets">[src]</a></sup></dd>

<p><b>Fawkes</b> was a highly intelligent male <a href="/wiki/Phoenix" title="Phoenix">phoenix</a> and <a href="/wiki/Albus_Dumbledore" title="Albus Dumbledore">Albus Dumbledore</a>'s animal companion and defender. It is unknown how long Fawkes had been in Dumbledore's service. He had been loyal to Dumbledore for many years prior to the Headmaster's death in <a href="/wiki/1997" title="1997">1997</a>.
</p><p>Fawkes was instrumental in helping <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> defeat <a href="/wiki/Salazar_Slytherin%27s_basilisk" class="mw-redirect" title="Salazar Slytherin&#39;s basilisk">Salazar Slytherin's basilisk</a> in <a href="/wiki/1993" title="1993">1993</a>. His <a href="/wiki/Phoenix_tears" title="Phoenix tears">tears</a> (which possessed <a href="/wiki/Healing_magic" title="Healing magic">healing</a> properties, as with his species) saved Harry's life after his arm was punctured by the basilisk's fang and its <a href="/wiki/Basilisk_venom" title="Basilisk venom">venom</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-COS17_5-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COS17-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> In <a href="/wiki/1996" title="1996">1996</a>, Fawkes came to Dumbledore's aid in fighting <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a> during the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_the_Department_of_Mysteries" title="Battle of the Department of Mysteries">Battle of the Department of Mysteries</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP36_3-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP36-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Following Dumbledore's death, Fawkes sang his <a href="/wiki/Phoenix_Lament" title="Phoenix Lament">Lament</a> over the grounds of <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a>, and then left and flew away, never to be seen again.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP29_8-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP29-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup> His tail feathers were the cores of the two twin <a href="/wiki/Wand" title="Wand">wands</a> which were held by <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a> and <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-GOF36_9-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF36-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Fawkes?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Fawkes?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_life">Early life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Fawkes?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Early life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Fawkes?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Early life"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Much of Fawkes' origins are unknown, but at some point in or prior to <a href="/wiki/1938" title="1938">1938</a>, two of his tail-feathers were procured by the wand maker <a href="/wiki/Garrick_Ollivander" title="Garrick Ollivander">Garrick Ollivander</a>, and fashioned into the <a href="/wiki/Wand_core" title="Wand core">cores</a> of two wands (a rare event, as should the two meet, it would initiate <a href="/wiki/Reverse_Spell" title="Reverse Spell">Priori Incantatem</a>), made from <a href="/wiki/Yew" title="Yew">yew</a> and <a href="/wiki/Holly" title="Holly">holly</a>. The <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle%27s_wand" title="Tom Riddle&#39;s wand">yew wand</a> went to <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Tom Riddle</a> and the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter%27s_wand" title="Harry Potter&#39;s wand">holly wand</a> went to <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-GOF36_9-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF36-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Fawkes had not been owned by another wizard prior to <a href="/wiki/Albus_Dumbledore" title="Albus Dumbledore">Albus Dumbledore</a>, and thus spent his early life as a wild phoenix.<sup id="cite_ref-accio_10-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-accio-10">&#91;10&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<p>Dumbledore and Fawkes met sometime before 1938,<sup id="cite_ref-age_1_11-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-age_1-11">&#91;11&#93;</a></sup> and Fawkes lived with and faithfully served the famed wizard for at least 59 years.<sup id="cite_ref-age_2_12-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-age_2-12">&#91;12&#93;</a></sup> As part of his efforts to help Harry in his quest to defeat and kill the <a href="/wiki/Dark_Wizard" title="Dark Wizard">Dark Wizard</a> Voldemort and his followers, Dumbledore called Fawkes into battle whenever needed.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP36_3-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP36-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> Throughout the years, there were several instances where Fawkes was extremely helpful.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Physical_appearance">Physical appearance</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Fawkes?veaction=edit&amp;section=10" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Physical appearance">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Fawkes?action=edit&amp;section=10" title="Edit section: Physical appearance"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Fawkes had crimson feathers on his body and a golden tail as long as a peacock's. He was roughly the size of a <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/swan" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:swan">swan</a>. His claws and beak were gleaming gold — the latter was "long" — and his eyes were black. The scarlet body feathers glowed faintly in darkness, while the golden tail feathers were hot to the touch, though it is unknown if they were actually capable of burning someone as neither Harry — when Fawkes carried him from the Chamber of Secrets in 1993<sup id="cite_ref-COS17_5-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COS17-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> — nor Dumbledore — when he escaped the Ministry officials with Fawkes's help in 1996<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP27_14-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP27-14">&#91;14&#93;</a></sup> — were burned by his tail feathers, however it is possible that Fawkes could control the temperature of his tail feathers and chosen not to burn them.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Personality_and_traits">Personality and traits</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Fawkes?veaction=edit&amp;section=11" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Personality and traits">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Fawkes?action=edit&amp;section=11" title="Edit section: Personality and traits"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>

<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets">"<i>Fawkes. You were fantastic, Fawkes...</i>"</span></dd>
<dd>&#8212;Harry congratulating Fawkes's bravery in the Chamber of Secrets<sup class="noprint"><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Chamber_of_Secrets" title="Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets">[src]</a></sup></dd>
<dd></dd></dl></div>
<p>Fawkes served as <a href="/wiki/Albus_Dumbledore" title="Albus Dumbledore">Albus Dumbledore</a>'s messenger and his constant companion, forming a close bond with the old wizard. Fawkes was also affiliated with <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> and the members of the <a href="/wiki/Order_of_the_Phoenix" title="Order of the Phoenix">Order of the Phoenix</a> and was the inspiration for its name.
</p><p>Fawkes was extremely loyal and courageous, as displayed by his attack on <a href="/wiki/Salazar_Slytherin%27s_basilisk" class="mw-redirect" title="Salazar Slytherin&#39;s basilisk">Salazar Slytherin's basilisk</a><sup id="cite_ref-COS17_5-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COS17-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> and his defence of Dumbledore during his <a href="/wiki/Duel_in_the_Ministry_Atrium" title="Duel in the Ministry Atrium">duel with Voldemort</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP36_3-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP36-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> Considering he came back to life every time he died he had no reason to fear death and was therefore willing to sacrifice himself for people who won his loyalty.
</p><p>He was also shown to be highly intelligent, being capable of understanding Dumbledore and bringing <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry the</a> <a href="/wiki/Sorting_Hat" title="Sorting Hat">Sorting Hat</a> and the <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle%27s_Diary" class="mw-redirect" title="Tom Riddle&#39;s Diary">diary</a> when he needed them.<sup id="cite_ref-COS17_5-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COS17-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>After Dumbledore's death, he returned to the wild with no ties to another <a href="/wiki/Human" title="Human">human</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-accio_10-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-accio-10">&#91;10&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<ul><li><b><a href="/wiki/Immortality" title="Immortality">Immortality</a></b>: Like all phoenixes, Fawkes appeared to be immortal. He periodically died by bursting into flames (an event called a "Burning Day"), and was then reborn from the ashes.<sup id="cite_ref-COS12_2-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COS12-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> He was also immune to the gaze of a basilisk, making phoenixes the only known creatures to be unaffected by the deadly serpent's gaze.<sup id="cite_ref-COS17_5-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COS17-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> Additionally Fawkes was able to swallow Lord Voldemort's Killing Curse and was quickly reborn.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP36_3-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP36-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Healing_magic" title="Healing magic">Healing magic</a></b>: Fawkes was also able to heal most injuries simply by crying on them. <a href="/wiki/Phoenix_tears" title="Phoenix tears">Phoenix tears</a> are the only known cure for <a href="/wiki/Basilisk_venom" title="Basilisk venom">basilisk venom</a>, which of course he used to heal <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>'s infected wound after the <a href="/wiki/Skirmish_in_the_Chamber_of_Secrets" title="Skirmish in the Chamber of Secrets">Skirmish in the Chamber of Secrets</a> in <a href="/wiki/1993" title="1993">1993</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-COS17_5-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COS17-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> In <a href="/wiki/1995" title="1995">1995</a>, he used his healing tears to cure Harry again of the effects of <a href="/wiki/Acromantula_venom" title="Acromantula venom">Acromantula venom</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-GOF36_9-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF36-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup></li></ul>

<ul><li><b><a href="/wiki/Apparition" title="Apparition">Apparition</a></b>: Being a Phoenix, Fawkes had the natural ability to apparate at will, disappearing and reappearing in a flash of <a href="/wiki/Fire" title="Fire">fire</a>. Much like the <a href="/wiki/House-elves" class="mw-redirect" title="House-elves">house-elves</a>, he could do so regardless of the anti-Apparition enchantments surrounding Hogwarts. This may be because the Hogwarts enchantments apply only to humans. Fawkes could also take people along with him, as well as remotely teleport one of his feathers and send it to a distant location as a form of signal or warning.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP27_14-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP27-14">&#91;14&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Phoenix_Lament" title="Phoenix Lament">Phoenix song</a></b>: Fawkes also possessed remarkable and soul-touching singing talents.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP29_8-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP29-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup> A single note of his song was said to have effects similar to swallowing a drop of warming liquid, and instilling courage and calm. Phoenix song is reputed to increase the courage of the pure of heart and strike fear into the heart of the impure.</li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Superhuman_strength" title="Superhuman strength">Super strength</a></b>: As a phoenix, Fawkes can carry immensely heavy loads. For example, he carried the combined weight of <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>, <a href="/wiki/Ginevra_Weasley" title="Ginevra Weasley">Ginny Weasley</a>, <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ron Weasley</a> and <a href="/wiki/Gilderoy_Lockhart" title="Gilderoy Lockhart">Gilderoy Lockhart</a> out of the <a href="/wiki/Chamber_of_Secrets" title="Chamber of Secrets">Chamber of Secrets</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-COS17_5-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COS17-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li><b>Physical Combat</b>: Although Phoenixes are gentle and are not known for fighting, Fawkes was able to blind the <a href="/wiki/Serpent_of_Slytherin" title="Serpent of Slytherin">Serpent of Slytherin</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Chamber_of_Secrets" title="Chamber of Secrets">Chamber of Secrets</a> by gouging out its eyes, showing he had clear ability in physically wounding his opponents.<sup id="cite_ref-COS17_5-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COS17-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup></li></ul>

<p>Fawkes had a very close relationship with <a href="/wiki/Albus_Dumbledore" title="Albus Dumbledore">Albus Dumbledore</a>. Dumbledore cared for Fawkes after each of his rebirths, and in return he was very faithful to the elderly wizard. Fawkes protected Dumbledore in his <a href="/wiki/Duel_in_the_Ministry_Atrium" title="Duel in the Ministry Atrium">duel</a> with <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a> in the ministry atrium in 1996 by swallowing a <a href="/wiki/Killing_Curse" title="Killing Curse">Killing Curse</a> aimed at him,<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP36_3-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP36-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> and teleported Dumbledore away from the school when the <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">Ministry</a> attempted to arrest him in <a href="/wiki/1996" title="1996">1996</a>, for supposedly building an army (Dumbledore's Army) in what the minister <a href="/wiki/Cornelius_Fudge" title="Cornelius Fudge">Cornelius Fudge</a> believed to be an attempt to overthrow the Ministry of Magic.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP27_14-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP27-14">&#91;14&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>When Dumbledore died in 1997, Fawkes sang a <a href="/wiki/Phoenix_Lament" title="Phoenix Lament">lament</a> for his now deceased master that was described as both sad and beautiful, then left the school forever.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP29_8-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP29-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup> Dumbledore also named the Order of the Phoenix after his beloved friend and champion.
</p>

<p>Fawkes aided those who were loyal to Dumbledore, such as <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>. In <a href="/wiki/1993" title="1993">1993</a>, he brought the <a href="/wiki/Sorting_Hat" title="Sorting Hat">Sorting Hat</a> to Harry in the <a href="/wiki/Chamber_of_Secrets" title="Chamber of Secrets">Chamber of Secrets</a>, and defended him from <a href="/wiki/Salazar_Slytherin%27s_Basilisk" class="mw-redirect" title="Salazar Slytherin&#39;s Basilisk">Salazar Slytherin's Basilisk</a> by pecking out its eyes. When a fang pierced Harry's arm and poisoned him with its <a href="/wiki/Basilisk_venom" title="Basilisk venom">venom</a>, Fawkes used his <a href="/wiki/Phoenix_tears" title="Phoenix tears">tears</a> to heal the wound, saving Harry's life. He then brought the <a href="/wiki/Horcrux" title="Horcrux">Horcrux</a> <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle%27s_diary" class="mw-redirect" title="Tom Riddle&#39;s diary">diary</a> to Harry so he could destroy it.<sup id="cite_ref-COS17_5-10" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COS17-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> Harry frequently greeted Fawkes when he visited Dumbledore's office and Fawkes also healed his <a href="/wiki/Acromantula_venom" title="Acromantula venom">Acromantula venom</a> wounded arm after he escaped Lord Voldemort during his fourth year<sup id="cite_ref-GOF36_9-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF36-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup>. Harry was deeply mournful when Fawkes left Hogwarts, given his immense loyalty and service to him over the years.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP29_8-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP29-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>It should be noted that Harry's wand happened to be made with one of Fawkes's tail feathers. Whether this had anything to do with his bond with Harry or whether it was simply a coincidence is unknown. Lord Voldemort was the only other known wizard who had a wand made from one of Fawkes's feathers.<sup id="cite_ref-GOF36_9-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF36-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Fawkes?veaction=edit&amp;section=16" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Fawkes?action=edit&amp;section=16" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Fawkes is named after <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Guy_Fawkes" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Guy Fawkes">Guy Fawkes</a>, since he periodically explodes (in order to be reborn from the ashes, like all phoenixes), a joke on the author's part.

<ul><li>Fawkes was made by a mechanical machine. When Richard Harris, who played Dumbledore at that time, saw the robot, he said: "They sure do train those things well." He was thinking that it was a real bird. (As seen in <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Prisoner_of_Azkaban_(film)#Home_Video_Release_Dates" title="Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban (film)">Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban: Ultimate Edition</a></i>)</li>
<li>Pottermore has a Feature on bringing Fawkes to life.</li>
<li>The fact that Fawkes, who sacrificed to save Dumbledore's life at the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_the_Department_of_Mysteries" title="Battle of the Department of Mysteries">Battle of the Department of Mysteries</a>, didn't come to his aid at the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_the_Astronomy_Tower" title="Battle of the Astronomy Tower">Battle of the Astronomy Tower</a> hints to the true nature of the headmaster's death, meaning he was killed by his own orders.</li></ul>
