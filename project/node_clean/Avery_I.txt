<p><b>Avery I</b><sup id="cite_ref-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> (born c. <a href="/wiki/1920s" title="1920s">1920s</a>) was a <a href="/wiki/Pure-blood" title="Pure-blood">pure-blood</a> or <a href="/wiki/Half-blood" title="Half-blood">half-blood</a><sup id="cite_ref-blood_2-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-blood-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> who attended <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> and was sorted into <a href="/wiki/Slytherin" title="Slytherin">Slytherin House</a>. <figure class="thumb tleft show-info-icon" style="width: 252px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/0/09/Young_Death_Eaters.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20180720142700" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/0/09/Young_Death_Eaters.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/250?cb=20180720142700" decoding="async" width="250" height="104" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="Young Death Eaters.jpg" data-image-key="Young_Death_Eaters.jpg" /></a> 	 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:Young_Death_Eaters.jpg" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption">Avery at a <a href="/wiki/Slug_Club" title="Slug Club">Slug Club</a> supper with his school friends Tom Riddle, Lestrange, and others</p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure>
<p>Avery attended Hogwarts at the same time as <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Tom Riddle</a>, and was <a href="/wiki/Sorting_ceremony" title="Sorting ceremony">sorted</a> into <a href="/wiki/Slytherin" title="Slytherin">Slytherin House</a>. He became a member of <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle%27s_gang" title="Tom Riddle&#39;s gang">Riddle's gang</a>, and was a favourite student of <a href="/wiki/Horace_Slughorn" title="Horace Slughorn">Horace Slughorn</a>. Avery was present at the same Slug Club meeting in <a href="/wiki/1944" title="1944">1944</a> or <a href="/wiki/1945" title="1945">1945</a> as <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Tom Riddle</a> and was reprimanded by the Potions Master for failing to hand in a particular essay.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP17_6-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP17-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-HBP23_7-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP23-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Avery_I?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Avery_I?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p><i>Avery</i> is an English surname derived from an Old French version of the name Alfred. The meaning of the name is derived from the Old English words <i>aelf</i>, meaning elf, and <i>raed</i>, meaning counsel.
</p>
