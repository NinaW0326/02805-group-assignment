<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Selwyn?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Selwyn?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Second_Wizarding_War">Second Wizarding War</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Selwyn?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Second Wizarding War">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Selwyn?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Second Wizarding War"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Selwyn was one of the many <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a> who fought in the <a href="/wiki/Second_Wizarding_War" title="Second Wizarding War">Second Wizarding War</a> upon pledging his loyalty to Tom Riddle.
</p>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Battle_of_the_Seven_Potters">Battle of the Seven Potters</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Selwyn?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Battle of the Seven Potters">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Selwyn?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Battle of the Seven Potters"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<div class="quote">
<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows">"<i>Your wand, Selwyn! Give me your wand!</i>"</span></dd>
<dd>&#8212;<a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a> to Selwyn, during the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_the_Seven_Potters" title="Battle of the Seven Potters">Battle of the Seven Potters</a><sup class="noprint"><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Deathly_Hallows" title="Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows">[src]</a></sup></dd>
<dd></dd></dl></div><p>Selwyn participated in the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_the_Seven_Potters" title="Battle of the Seven Potters">Battle of the Seven Potters</a>, and chased <a href="/wiki/Rubeus_Hagrid" title="Rubeus Hagrid">Rubeus Hagrid</a> and the real <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>. Towards the end of the chase, he was ordered by <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a> to surrender his <a href="/wiki/Wand" title="Wand">wand</a> when <a href="/wiki/Lucius_Malfoy" title="Lucius Malfoy">Lucius Malfoy</a>'s was destroyed by Harry's <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter%27s_wand" title="Harry Potter&#39;s wand">wand</a>. Harry and Hagrid however were able to escape, likely sparing Selwyn's wand from being destroyed.<sup id="cite_ref-DH4_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH4-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p><h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Ambush_at_the_Lovegood_House">Ambush at the Lovegood House</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Selwyn?veaction=edit&amp;section=4" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Ambush at the Lovegood House">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Selwyn?action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Ambush at the Lovegood House"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<p>In early <a href="/wiki/1998" title="1998">1998</a>, Selwyn travelled with <a href="/wiki/Travers" title="Travers">Travers</a> to <a href="/wiki/Xenophilius_Lovegood" title="Xenophilius Lovegood">Xenophilius Lovegood</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Lovegood_House" title="Lovegood House">house</a>. Lovegood had been printing articles in <i><a href="/wiki/The_Quibbler" title="The Quibbler">The Quibbler</a></i> urging the public to support Harry Potter, and his daughter <a href="/wiki/Luna_Lovegood" title="Luna Lovegood">Luna</a> was taken to control his behaviour. Lovegood <a href="/wiki/Ambush_at_the_Lovegood_House" title="Ambush at the Lovegood House">alerted the Death Eaters</a> when <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry</a>, <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ron Weasley</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione Granger</a> came to his home, hoping that they would return his daughter, if he handed over Harry. However, as Selwyn and Travers arrived, the <a href="/wiki/Erumpent" title="Erumpent">Erumpent</a> horn in the residence blew up, giving Harry, Hermione, and Ron a chance to hide. Selwyn beat up and tortured Lovegood, suspicious that the wizard had been lying, but Hermione then engineered the trio's escape so that the Death Eaters saw Harry before they <a href="/wiki/Apparition" title="Apparition">disapparated</a>. Afterwards, Selwyn and Travers arrested Lovegood.<sup id="cite_ref-DH21_4-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH21-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Physical_appearance">Physical appearance</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Selwyn?veaction=edit&amp;section=5" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Physical appearance">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Selwyn?action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Physical appearance"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Little is known about Selwyn's appearance; it is, however, known that he had a rough voice. As a Death Eater, he bore the Dark Mark on his left inner forearm, which had faded to a mere scar upon Voldemort's death.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Personality_and_traits">Personality and traits</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Selwyn?veaction=edit&amp;section=6" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Personality and traits">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Selwyn?action=edit&amp;section=6" title="Edit section: Personality and traits"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Being a Death Eater, Selwyn was a cruel character; he assaulted <a href="/wiki/Xenophilius_Lovegood" title="Xenophilius Lovegood">Xenophilius Lovegood</a> when he called him and <a href="/wiki/Travers" title="Travers">Travers</a>, thinking that he wanted to entrap them. He also threatened Xenophilius that he would kill or mutilate his daughter, <a href="/wiki/Luna_Lovegood" title="Luna Lovegood">Luna</a>, if he did not obey their commands. He was also quite suspicious, as he believed that Xenophilius wanted to entrap and kill him and Travers.<sup id="cite_ref-DH21_4-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH21-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Magical_abilities_and_skills">Magical abilities and skills</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Selwyn?veaction=edit&amp;section=7" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Selwyn?action=edit&amp;section=7" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li><b><a href="/wiki/Dark_Arts" title="Dark Arts">Dark Arts</a></b>: As a loyal Death Eater and servant of the Dark Lord Voldemort, Selwyn would have been highly proficient in dark magic and an accomplished <a href="/wiki/Dark_Wizard" title="Dark Wizard">Dark Wizard</a>. He was probably talented in the casting of various <a href="/wiki/Dark_charm" title="Dark charm">dark charms</a>, as he likely used dark magic on <a href="/wiki/Xenophilius_Lovegood" title="Xenophilius Lovegood">Xenophilius Lovegood</a> when he thought the man had tried to lure himself and Travers into a trap.</li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Duelling" title="Duelling">Duelling</a></b>: Being a Death Eater, Selwyn would have been accomplished in <a href="/wiki/Martial_magic" title="Martial magic">martial magic</a> and combat. He fought in the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_the_Seven_Potters" title="Battle of the Seven Potters">Battle of the Seven Potters</a>, for which he chased down <a href="/wiki/Rubeus_Hagrid" title="Rubeus Hagrid">Rubeus Hagrid</a> and <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> on a broomstick,<sup id="cite_ref-DH4_3-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH4-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> meaning he would have been a fairly competent duellist.</li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Flight" title="Flight">Flying</a></b>: Selwyn was an accomplished <a href="/wiki/Broomstick" title="Broomstick">broomstick</a> flyer, as he participated in the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_the_Seven_Potters" title="Battle of the Seven Potters">Battle of the Seven Potters</a> in <a href="/wiki/1997" title="1997">1997</a>, where he was able to successfully keep up with <a href="/wiki/Sirius_Black%27s_motorcycle" class="mw-redirect" title="Sirius Black&#39;s motorcycle">Sirius Black's flying motorbike</a> and dodge everything <a href="/wiki/Rubeus_Hagrid" title="Rubeus Hagrid">Rubeus Hagrid</a> and <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> sent at him.<sup id="cite_ref-DH4_3-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH4-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Martial_magic" title="Martial magic">Martial magic</a>: Selwyn was an experienced user of Martial Magic being able duel Harry Potter and Rubeus Hagrid and succeed and doing copious amounts of damage. Selwyn could also cast a Killing Curse which means he is extremely accomplished at Illegal Magic. He could also fly a broom and duel at the same time making him a strong duellist.</li></ul>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Possessions">Possessions</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Selwyn?veaction=edit&amp;section=8" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Possessions">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Selwyn?action=edit&amp;section=8" title="Edit section: Possessions"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li><b><a href="/wiki/Selwyn%27s_wand" title="Selwyn&#39;s wand">Wand</a></b>: Selwyn owned a <a href="/wiki/Wand" title="Wand">wand</a>, which was of an unknown length, <a href="/wiki/Wand_wood" title="Wand wood">wand wood</a> and <a href="/wiki/Wand_core" title="Wand core">wand core</a>. He used his wand whilst fighting during the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_the_Seven_Potters" title="Battle of the Seven Potters">Battle of the Seven Potters</a>, during which <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a> demanded he take his wand to <a href="/wiki/Curse" title="Curse">curse</a> <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>, before he escaped.<sup id="cite_ref-DH4_3-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH4-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> However, as Harry escaped, this would have saved his wand from imminent destruction. He possibly purchased it from <a href="/wiki/Ollivanders" title="Ollivanders">Ollivanders</a> in <a href="/wiki/Diagon_Alley" title="Diagon Alley">Diagon Alley</a>, <a href="/wiki/London" title="London">London</a>, along with most wizards in <a href="/wiki/Great_Britain" title="Great Britain">Great Britain</a>.</li>
<li>Death Eater Mask: Most Death Eaters including Selwyn had there own Death Eater mask, though this was never mentioned in the books for Selwyn.</li></ul>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Selwyn?veaction=edit&amp;section=9" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Selwyn?action=edit&amp;section=9" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>When Harry, Ron, and Hermione <a href="/wiki/Infiltration_of_the_British_Ministry_of_Magic_(1997)" title="Infiltration of the British Ministry of Magic (1997)">infiltrated</a> the <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">Ministry of Magic</a> in <a href="/wiki/1997" title="1997">1997</a>, <a href="/wiki/Dolores_Umbridge" title="Dolores Umbridge">Dolores Umbridge</a> mentioned that the <a href="/wiki/Selwyn_family" title="Selwyn family">Selwyns</a> were an old <a href="/wiki/Pure-blood" title="Pure-blood">pure-blood</a> family, and claimed to be related to them; however, this was almost certainly a lie since her father was <a href="/wiki/Orford_Umbridge" title="Orford Umbridge">Orford Umbridge</a>, a janitor whom she found too embarrassing for him to be related the <a href="/wiki/Sacred_Twenty-Eight" title="Sacred Twenty-Eight">Sacred Twenty-Eight</a>, and her mother was the muggle <a href="/wiki/Ellen_Cracknell" title="Ellen Cracknell">Ellen Cracknell</a>.</li>
<li>Selwyn may have been <a href="/wiki/Hedwig%27s_killer" title="Hedwig&#39;s killer">Hedwig's killer</a>, as he was one of the Death Eaters pursuing Harry and Hagrid at the time.<sup id="cite_ref-DH13_1-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH13-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup></li></ul>
