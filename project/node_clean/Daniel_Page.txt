<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Daniel_Page?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Daniel_Page?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_life">Early life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Daniel_Page?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Early life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Daniel_Page?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Early life"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<figure class="thumb tleft show-info-icon" style="width: 222px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/d/de/The_Bloody_Pages.png/revision/latest?cb=20200604125037" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/d/de/The_Bloody_Pages.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/220?cb=20200604125037" decoding="async" width="220" height="101" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="The Bloody Pages.png" data-image-key="The_Bloody_Pages.png" /></a> 	 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:The_Bloody_Pages.png" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption">A Muggle newspaper reporting the arrest of Daniel's family</p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure>
<p>Daniel was born and raised somewhere in the <a href="/wiki/Great_Britain" title="Great Britain">British Isles</a> between <a href="/wiki/1988" title="1988">1988</a> and <a href="/wiki/2004" title="2004">2004</a>. He was the third child and youngest son of a <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggle</a> father and a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch</a> mother, and was raised alongside an older brother and sister. The only one of the two boys to possess magical ability, Daniel nevertheless attended a Muggle primary school prior to receiving his <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_acceptance_letter" title="Hogwarts acceptance letter">letter of acceptance</a> from Hogwarts. While apparently loving, Daniel's childhood were still marred by controversy from an early age, marked by the illicit activities of their father and, in due time, also Daniel's older brother Reggie, who was a <a href="/wiki/Squib" title="Squib">Squib</a>. At some point, they were eventually cornered by the Muggle <a href="/wiki/Police" title="Police">police</a> and arrested.<sup id="cite_ref-MA_2-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-MA-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<figure class="thumb tright show-info-icon" style="width: 243px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/a/a0/Daniel_Page_and_his_mother.png/revision/latest?cb=20200804162248" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/a/a0/Daniel_Page_and_his_mother.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/241?cb=20200804162248" decoding="async" width="241" height="91" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="Daniel Page and his mother.png" data-image-key="Daniel_Page_and_his_mother.png" /></a> 	 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:Daniel_Page_and_his_mother.png" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption">Daniel Page visiting his mother in prison</p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure>
<p>Having "tried to help" using magic but only ended up "scaring a bunch of Muggles", Daniel's mother was also apprehended. While her two apparent accomplices were both tried and sentenced to a Muggle prison, her violation of the <a href="/wiki/International_Statute_of_Wizarding_Secrecy" title="International Statute of Wizarding Secrecy">International Statute of Wizarding Secrecy</a> came to the attention of the <a href="/wiki/Ministry_of_Magic" title="Ministry of Magic">Ministry of Magic</a>, which, presumably through the efforts of the <a href="/wiki/Muggle_Liaison_Office" title="Muggle Liaison Office">Muggle Liaison Office</a>, successfully claimed jurisdiction and had her released into the custody of the <a href="/wiki/Department_of_Magical_Law_Enforcement" title="Department of Magical Law Enforcement">Department of Magical Law Enforcement</a>. Put on trail before a wizarding court of law, she was accused and found guilty of being a member of the <a href="/wiki/Nocturnal_Order_of_Tricks_and_Exhibitions" title="Nocturnal Order of Tricks and Exhibitions">Nocturnal Order of Tricks and Exhibitions</a>, and sentenced to <a href="/wiki/Azkaban" title="Azkaban">Azkaban</a>. The family's arrest was covered by a Muggle newspaper, dubbing them "The Bloody Pages", after which Daniel, who was now left in the care of his older sister, found school to be anything but fun.<sup id="cite_ref-MA_2-10" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-MA-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Education">Education</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Daniel_Page?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Education">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Daniel_Page?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Education"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Daniel finally arrived at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> when he was eleven. He did so with a sigh of relief; as few witches and wizards read Muggle newspaper and hence, nobody knew him, taking off a lot of the pressure he had been under after all that happened.<sup id="cite_ref-MA_2-11" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-MA-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Personality_and_traits">Personality and traits</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Daniel_Page?veaction=edit&amp;section=4" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Personality and traits">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Daniel_Page?action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Personality and traits"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Upon arriving at school, Daniel came off as a solitary, studious young wizard that often preferred focusing on his academics over interacting with other people. When he befriended <a href="/wiki/Unidentified_2000s_Hogwarts_student" title="Unidentified 2000s Hogwarts student">among others</a> <a href="/wiki/Robyn_Thistlethwaite" title="Robyn Thistlethwaite">Robyn Thistlethwaite</a> and <a href="/wiki/Ivy_Warrington" title="Ivy Warrington">Ivy Warrington</a>, he became significantly more open and sociable. He also kept well-informed on the goings on in the wizarding community, to the point that he was very familiar with the notorious group known as <a href="/wiki/Nocturnal_Order_of_Tricks_and_Exhibitions" title="Nocturnal Order of Tricks and Exhibitions">NOTME</a>. Following his mother's imprisonment after her violation of the <a href="/wiki/International_Statute_of_Wizarding_Secrecy" title="International Statute of Wizarding Secrecy">International Statute of Wizarding Secrecy</a>, he grew to view it as a "stupid law", and expressed the belief that while they should not necessarily go around flaunting their abilities to the masses, he nevertheless believed that keeping magic from the <a href="/wiki/Muggle_world" title="Muggle world">Muggle world</a> entirely as a selfish thing to do, apparently blind to the extent to which permitting magic around non-wizards could be exploited for nefarious purposes.<sup id="cite_ref-MA_2-12" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-MA-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
