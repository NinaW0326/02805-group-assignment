<b>Badeea Ali</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch</a> who started attending <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> in <a href="/wiki/1984" title="1984">1984</a> and was sorted into <a href="/wiki/Ravenclaw" title="Ravenclaw">Ravenclaw</a>. A creatively ingenious <a href="/wiki/Soul" title="Soul">soul</a>, she took an instant interest in the <a href="/wiki/Portrait" title="Portrait">portraits</a> that hung all throughout the castle from the moment she set foot in the castle and proceeded to spend most of her time outside of her lessons experimenting with the creation of <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magical</a> paintings of people and places, and the necessary <a href="/wiki/Spell" title="Spell">spellwork</a> required to make them come to life.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Badeea_Ali?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Badeea_Ali?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Badeea was born in <a href="/wiki/1972" title="1972">1972</a> or <a href="/wiki/1973" title="1973">1973</a>. Her <a href="/wiki/Badeea_Ali%27s_mother" title="Badeea Ali&#39;s mother">mother</a> was a <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggle</a> engineer, making Badeea either <a href="/wiki/Muggle-born" title="Muggle-born">Muggle-born</a> or <a href="/wiki/Half-blood" title="Half-blood">half-blood</a>.
</p><p>She owned a pet <a href="/wiki/Cat" title="Cat">cat</a> called <a href="/wiki/Laith" title="Laith">Laith</a>. She loved painting the cat and said she could never have enough cat portraits.<sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> She also enjoyed experimenting to create new <a href="/wiki/Defensive_Charm" title="Defensive Charm">defensive spells</a>.
</p><p>She may have been Muslim, given that she wore a <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/hijab" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:hijab">hijab</a>, a head covering traditionally worn by women of the <a href="/wiki/Islam" title="Islam">Islamic</a> faith, along with her Arabic name (as most Muslims have them).
</p>
<h3><span id="Hogwarts_years_(1984-1991)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="Hogwarts_years_.281984-1991.29">Hogwarts years <span class="template-c">(1984-1991)</span></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Badeea_Ali?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Hogwarts years (1984-1991)">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Badeea_Ali?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Hogwarts years (1984-1991)"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Badeea started attending <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> on <a href="/wiki/1_September" title="1 September">1 September</a> and was <a href="/wiki/Sorting_ceremony" title="Sorting ceremony">sorted</a> into <a href="/wiki/Ravenclaw" title="Ravenclaw">Ravenclaw</a> house. Due to being a female Ravenclaw of the same year, she may have shared a <a href="/wiki/Ravenclaw_Girls%27_Dormitory" title="Ravenclaw Girls&#39; Dormitory">dorm room</a> with <a href="/wiki/Tulip_Karasu" title="Tulip Karasu">Tulip Karasu</a>.
</p><p>She spent her afternoons in the <a href="/wiki/Clock_Tower_Courtyard" title="Clock Tower Courtyard">Clock Tower Courtyard</a>, where she created magical portraits. She did not pay attention to the events going on at Hogwarts.<sup id="cite_ref-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>She successfully convinced another student, <a href="/wiki/Jae_Kim" title="Jae Kim">Jae Kim</a>, that she was able to <a href="/wiki/Apparition" title="Apparition">apparate</a>, but in reality she used an <a href="/wiki/Invisibility_cloak" title="Invisibility cloak">invisibility cloak</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>In her third year, Badeea took <a href="/wiki/Divination_(class)" title="Divination (class)">Divination</a> as an elective.<sup id="cite_ref-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Fifth_year">Fifth year</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Badeea_Ali?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Fifth year">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Badeea_Ali?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Fifth year"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<p>In the <a href="/wiki/1988%E2%80%931989_school_year" title="1988–1989 school year">1988–1989 school year</a>, she had <a href="/wiki/Defence_Against_the_Dark_Arts" title="Defence Against the Dark Arts">Defence Against the Dark Arts</a> and <a href="/wiki/History_of_Magic" title="History of Magic">History of Magic</a><sup id="cite_ref-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup> classes with, among others, <a href="/wiki/Jacob%27s_sibling" title="Jacob&#39;s sibling">Jacob's sibling</a>.
</p><p>She and Jacob's sibling met in the Clock Tower Courtyard when Badeea Ali was creating a magical portrait of <a href="/wiki/Hogsmeade" title="Hogsmeade">Hogsmeade</a>. Jacob's sibling needed her help to rescue <a href="/wiki/Beatrice_Haywood" title="Beatrice Haywood">Beatrice Haywood</a>, who was stuck in a painting, after <a href="/wiki/Nicholas_de_Mimsy-Porpington" title="Nicholas de Mimsy-Porpington">Nicholas de Mimsy-Porpington</a> had told them about Badeea. Badeea explained that she had been attempting to recreate the portrait with Beatrice. She advised them to try something they would never normally do find a solution; taking this to heart, Jacob's sibling put on a cheery, complimentary attitude towards <a href="/wiki/Pitts" title="Pitts">Pitts</a> in detention, <a href="/wiki/Argus_Filch" title="Argus Filch">Argus Filch</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Merula_Snyde" title="Merula Snyde">Merula Snyde</a>, and obtained surprising results.
</p><p>Later on, Jacob's sibling met up with Badeea in Defence Against the Dark Arts classroom when the former was looking for <a href="/wiki/Patricia_Rakepick" title="Patricia Rakepick">Patricia Rakepick</a>. Jacob's sibling asked Badeea to teach them one ability.
</p><p>Badeea and Jacob's sibling later invented the <a href="/wiki/Star_Shower_Spell" title="Star Shower Spell">Star Shower Spell</a> as part of the latter's chaos event in an attempt to get the <a href="/wiki/Vault_Portrait" title="Vault Portrait">Vault Portrait</a> from <a href="/wiki/Peeves" title="Peeves">Peeves</a>.
</p>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Sixth_year">Sixth year</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Badeea_Ali?veaction=edit&amp;section=4" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Sixth year">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Badeea_Ali?action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Sixth year"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<p>Sometime during the year, Badeea became a founding member of the <a href="/wiki/Circle_of_Khanna" title="Circle of Khanna">Circle of Khanna</a>, a group devoted to avenging <a href="/wiki/Rowan_Khanna" title="Rowan Khanna">Rowan Khanna</a> and locating the final <a href="/wiki/Cursed_Vaults" title="Cursed Vaults">cursed vault</a>. It was at the groups first meeting in the <a href="/wiki/Hog%27s_Head_Inn" title="Hog&#39;s Head Inn">Hog's Head Inn</a> that she learned of the malevolent organisation "<a href="/wiki/R" title="R">R</a>" and it's <a href="/wiki/Plot_to_find_the_Cursed_Vaults" title="Plot to find the Cursed Vaults">motives</a> to locate the final Cursed Vault before them. As their first task, Badeea and <a href="/wiki/Talbott_Winger" title="Talbott Winger">Talbott</a> made a <a href="/wiki/Map_of_the_Great_Lake" title="Map of the Great Lake">Map of the Great Lake</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-hm20_3-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-hm20-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Physical_Appearance">Physical Appearance</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Badeea_Ali?veaction=edit&amp;section=5" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Physical Appearance">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Badeea_Ali?action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Physical Appearance"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Badeea wore a blue sparkled hijab, and had brown eyes and pale skin.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Personality_and_traits">Personality and traits</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Badeea_Ali?veaction=edit&amp;section=6" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Personality and traits">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Badeea_Ali?action=edit&amp;section=6" title="Edit section: Personality and traits"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Being a member of <a href="/wiki/Ravenclaw" title="Ravenclaw">Ravenclaw</a> house, Badeea was a highly intelligent and creative young witch with an impressive talent for painting and the experimentation with new forms of <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magic</a> and <a href="/wiki/Spell" title="Spell">spells</a>. She was academically-minded, who took great interest in her schoolwork and thus often enjoyed discussing academic topics with other students. She was also friendly and good-natured, as she was willing to assist <a href="/wiki/Jacob%27s_sibling" title="Jacob&#39;s sibling">Jacob's sibling</a> when they needed her help. Indeed, she was so devoted to her hobby as an artist that she did not pay much attention to many of the events that went on at Hogwarts, showing her to be a fiercely dedicated individualist who preferred living in her own imagination and world, rather than being too obsessed with reality. She feared anything with which she could not interact through reason, which included birds.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Magical_abilities_and_skills">Magical abilities and skills</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Badeea_Ali?veaction=edit&amp;section=7" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Badeea_Ali?action=edit&amp;section=7" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li><b><a href="/wiki/Spell_creation" title="Spell creation">Spell creation</a></b>: Badeea invented the <a href="/wiki/Star_Shower_Spell" title="Star Shower Spell">Star Shower Spell</a> with the help from <a href="/wiki/Jacob%27s_sibling" title="Jacob&#39;s sibling">Jacob's sibling</a> and <a href="/wiki/Severus_Snape" title="Severus Snape">Severus Snape</a>, along with the <a href="/wiki/Jinx" title="Jinx">jinx</a> <i><a href="/wiki/Flipendo_Maxima" title="Flipendo Maxima">Flipendo Maxima</a></i>, a highly powerful version of the <a href="/wiki/Knockback_Jinx" title="Knockback Jinx">Knockback Jinx</a>, both whilst she attended Hogwarts. These were incredibly impressive achievements for a Hogwarts student, given how the art of spell creation is both highly complex and even dangerous, showing her to have been a highly accomplished and inventive witch in this respect.</li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Charm" title="Charm">Charms</a></b>: Badeea was proficient in charm-work, as she successfully created the <a href="/wiki/Star_Shower_Spell" title="Star Shower Spell">Star Shower Spell</a>, an <a href="/wiki/Atmospheric_Charm" title="Atmospheric Charm">Atmospheric Charm</a> and was able to place the necessary charms on the <a href="/wiki/Portrait" title="Portrait">portraits</a> she painted to give them personality. She also enjoyed experimenting with new <a href="/wiki/Defensive_Charm" title="Defensive Charm">Defensive Charms</a> in her free time.</li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Astronomy_(class)" title="Astronomy (class)">Astronomy</a></b>: Badeea was skilled in astronomy given how she had earned a <a href="/wiki/Ordinary_Wizarding_Level" title="Ordinary Wizarding Level">O.W.L.</a> in the subject at Hogwarts, allowing her to study it at <a href="/wiki/Nastily_Exhausting_Wizarding_Test" title="Nastily Exhausting Wizarding Test">N.E.W.T.</a> level.</li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Divination" title="Divination">Divination</a></b>: Badeea was skilled in the art of divination, given how she had earned a <a href="/wiki/Ordinary_Wizarding_Level" title="Ordinary Wizarding Level">O.W.L.</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Divination_(class)" title="Divination (class)">subject</a> at Hogwarts, allowing her to study it at <a href="/wiki/Nastily_Exhausting_Wizarding_Test" title="Nastily Exhausting Wizarding Test">N.E.W.T.</a> level.</li>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Badeea_Ali?veaction=edit&amp;section=8" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Badeea_Ali?action=edit&amp;section=8" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>Based on her name, it is possible that she was of Arabic descent. Her wearing of a hijab further supports the idea that she is of Arabic Muslim background. However, even non-Arab Muslims usually have names with these roots (i.e. ones from Pakistan or India, more common in the UK and Ireland).
<ul><li>Her being of Islamic background does not seem to have caused any complications for her, despite the religion traditionally having a strongly hostile attitude towards the practising of any kind of magic. However, it is possible that her family do not practice Islam very strictly, have found a way to reconcile their faith with it or simply have excused her case, as preventing Badeea from mastering her magic or forcing her to suppress it could result in her becoming an <a href="/wiki/Obscurial" title="Obscurial">Obscurial</a>.</li></ul></li></ul>
