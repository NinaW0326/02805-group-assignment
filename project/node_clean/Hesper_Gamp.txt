<b>Hesper Black (née Gamp)</b> (fl. 1901-1906) was a <a href="/wiki/Pure-blood" title="Pure-blood">pure-blood</a> <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch</a> who married <a href="/wiki/Sirius_Black_II" title="Sirius Black II">Sirius Black II</a>. The couple had three children together: <a href="/wiki/Arcturus_Black_III" title="Arcturus Black III">Arcturus</a>, <a href="/wiki/Lycoris_Black" title="Lycoris Black">Lycoris</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Regulus_Black_I" title="Regulus Black I">Regulus</a>.
</p><p>She was a grandmother of <a href="/wiki/Orion_Black" title="Orion Black">Orion Black</a> and <a href="/wiki/Lucretia_Black" title="Lucretia Black">Lucretia Black</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-BFT_2-12" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-BFT-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Hesper_Gamp?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Hesper_Gamp?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<figure class="thumb tleft show-info-icon" style="width: 142px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/5/59/Regulus_Black_I.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20120411184646" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/5/59/Regulus_Black_I.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/140?cb=20120411184646" decoding="async" width="140" height="165" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="Regulus Black I.jpg" data-image-key="Regulus_Black_I.jpg" /></a> 	 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:Regulus_Black_I.jpg" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption">Hesper’s son <a href="/wiki/Regulus_Black_I" title="Regulus Black I">Regulus</a> on the family tree</p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure>
<p><i>Hesper</i> is an English variation of the name <i>Esther</i><sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>, which is believed to be derived from the Persian word for "star," possibly an allusion to the naming pattern of the <a href="/wiki/House_of_Black" title="House of Black">family</a> she married into.
</p><p>The <i>Book of Esther</i> in the <i>Old Testament</i> tells the story of Queen <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Esther" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Esther">Esther</a>, the Jewish wife of the king of Persia who saved her people from extermination.<sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>It's also possible that her name derives from <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hesperus" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Hesperus">Hesperus</a>, the "Evening Star" from Greek mythology (Vesper for the Romans). This was a name assigned to the planet Venus, visible during the evening, whose morning counterpart ("Morning Star") would become called "Lucifer" in Latin.<sup id="cite_ref-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
