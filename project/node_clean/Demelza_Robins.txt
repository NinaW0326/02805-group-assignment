<p>Upon her arrival at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> Demelza was sorted into the house of <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor" title="Gryffindor">Gryffindor</a>. Demelza had a talent for the sport <a href="/wiki/Quidditch" title="Quidditch">Quidditch</a> but had not tried out for the Quidditch team yet as almost every year they had full capacity.
</p>

<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="1997-1998_school_year">1997-1998 school year</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Demelza_Robins?veaction=edit&amp;section=5" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: 1997-1998 school year">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Demelza_Robins?action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: 1997-1998 school year"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<p>Not being in <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>'s year and the <a href="/wiki/1997%E2%80%931998_school_year" title="1997–1998 school year">1997–1998 school year</a> being Harry's final year of education meant that Demelza would also be returning to continue her education at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts</a> in <a href="/wiki/1997" title="1997">1997</a> after the death of former Headmaster Albus Dumbledore. With Dumbledore dead the school was to be taken over by <a href="/wiki/Minerva_McGonagall" title="Minerva McGonagall">Minerva McGonagall</a> but with <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a>'s rise to power <a href="/wiki/Severus_Snape" title="Severus Snape">Severus Snape</a> took to the position.
</p>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Battle_of_Hogwarts"><a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Demelza_Robins?veaction=edit&amp;section=6" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Battle of Hogwarts">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Demelza_Robins?action=edit&amp;section=6" title="Edit section: Battle of Hogwarts"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<p>In last few months of the school year the <a href="/wiki/Second_Wizarding_War" title="Second Wizarding War">Second Wizarding War</a> came to a close with the Battle of Hogwarts. Lord Voldemort and his <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eater</a> army invaded the castle and a battle broke out to defend it and protect Harry Potter. The castle was almost completely destroyed and there were many casualties. It is unknown if Demelza was present for the battle or if she was too young to stay in combat.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Magical_abilities_and_skills">Magical abilities and skills</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Demelza_Robins?veaction=edit&amp;section=7" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Demelza_Robins?action=edit&amp;section=7" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li><b><a href="/wiki/Flight" title="Flight">Flying</a></b>: Demelza was an accomplished <a href="/wiki/Broomstick" title="Broomstick">broomstick</a> flyer and <a href="/wiki/Quidditch" title="Quidditch">Quidditch</a> player, as she was considered a good enough player to earn a spot on the Gryffindor team as a Chaser. <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> also remarked that she was particularly talented at dodging <a href="/wiki/Bludger" title="Bludger">Bludgers</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP11_2-10" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP11-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup></li></ul>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Demelza_Robins?veaction=edit&amp;section=8" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Demelza_Robins?action=edit&amp;section=8" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Demelza as a name is most commonly found in the Caucasus region and the areas near the Caspian Sea, and is likely from some of the North Caucasian dialects.  Outside of the Caucasus region, Demelza began as a Cornish place name that meant either "fort of Maeldaf" or "thy sweetness" ("de" meaning thy and "melza" meaning honey or sweet in Cornish).<sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> It only came into use as a given name in the <a href="/wiki/1950s" title="1950s">1950s</a>, after the novelist <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Winston_Graham" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Winston Graham">Winston Graham</a> chose it for the title character of his <a href="/wiki/1946" title="1946">1946</a> novel, <i>Demelza</i>, which was the second instalment in his <i><a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/The_Poldark_Novels" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:The Poldark Novels">Poldark</a></i> series.<sup id="cite_ref-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup><a href="/wiki/J._K._Rowling" title="J. K. Rowling">J. K. Rowling</a> may have chosen it as a tribute to <a target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener" class="external text" href="http://www.demelza.org.uk/">Demelza Hospice Care for Children</a>, a charity supported by <a href="/wiki/Daniel_Radcliffe" title="Daniel Radcliffe">Daniel Radcliffe</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
