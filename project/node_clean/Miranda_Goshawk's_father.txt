<b>Mr Goshawk</b> (fl. <a href="/wiki/1915" title="1915">1915</a>-<a href="/wiki/1920" title="1920">1920</a>) was the husband of <a href="/wiki/Miranda_Goshawk%27s_mother" title="Miranda Goshawk&#39;s mother">Mrs Goshawk</a>, and the father of the <a href="/wiki/Goshawk_sisters" title="Goshawk sisters">nine Goshawk sisters</a>, including <a href="/wiki/Diadema_Goshawk" title="Diadema Goshawk">Diadema</a>, <a href="/wiki/Romilda_Goshawk" title="Romilda Goshawk">Romilda</a>, <a href="/wiki/Tangwystl_Goshawk" title="Tangwystl Goshawk">Tangwystl</a> and <a href="/wiki/Miranda_Goshawk" title="Miranda Goshawk">Miranda Goshawk</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-WBoS_3-11" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-WBoS-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>This individual had several <a href="/wiki/Miranda_Goshawk%27s_aunts_and_uncles" title="Miranda Goshawk&#39;s aunts and uncles">siblings</a> and, through their marriages, many <a href="/wiki/Miranda_Goshawk%27s_cousins" title="Miranda Goshawk&#39;s cousins">nieces and nephews</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-WBoS_3-12" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-WBoS-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
