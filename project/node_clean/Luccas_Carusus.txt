<b>Luccas Carusus</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> presumably associated in some way with the sport of <a href="/wiki/Quidditch" title="Quidditch">Quidditch</a>, possibly a member of the <a href="/wiki/Quidditch_Union_for_the_Administration_and_Betterment_of_the_British_League_and_its_Endeavours" title="Quidditch Union for the Administration and Betterment of the British League and its Endeavours">Quidditch Union for the Administration and Betterment of the British League and its Endeavours</a>. They wrote the foreword for the revised edition of <i><a href="/wiki/Quidditch_Teams_of_Britain_and_Ireland" title="Quidditch Teams of Britain and Ireland">Quidditch Teams of England and Ireland</a></i>.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
