<p>The <b>werewolf army</b>, led by <a href="/wiki/Fenrir_Greyback" title="Fenrir Greyback">Fenrir Greyback</a>, was a group of werewolves who intended to infect as many people as possible with <a href="/wiki/Werewolf" title="Werewolf">lycanthropy</a> in the hope that one day the werewolf community would be large enough to take over the wizarding community. 
</p><p>The werewolves, particularly Greyback, specialised in attacking children. This way, they presumably hoped that being infected young would cause them to integrate into the werewolf society sooner.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="History">History</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Werewolf_army?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: History">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Werewolf_army?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: History"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<div class="quote">
<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince"><b>Remus Lupin</b>: "<i>I've been living among my fellows, my equals. Werewolves. ...I'm not complaining, it is necessary work and who better to do it than I?</i>"</span></dd>
<dd><b>Harry Potter</b>: "<i>How come they like Voldemort?</i>"</dd>
<dd><b>Remus Lupin</b>: "<i>They think that, under his rule, they will have a better life. </i>"</dd>
<dd>&#8212; Werewolves' involvement in  the <a href="/wiki/Second_Wizarding_War" title="Second Wizarding War">Second Wizarding War</a><sup class="noprint"><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Half-Blood_Prince" title="Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince">[src]</a></sup></dd></dl>
</div>
<p>During the Second Wizarding War, the werewolves allied themselves with the dark side, though they were not considered <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a>. This is probably because Voldemort didn't trust their <a href="/wiki/Half-breed" title="Half-breed">half-breed</a> status. Greyback, although he was not branded with the <a href="/wiki/Dark_Mark" title="Dark Mark">Dark Mark</a>, became the most integrated into the Death Eaters out of the werewolf community. He thought that by becoming affiliated with Voldemort it would be easier for him and his fellow werewolves to get access to people to attack. He also believed that under his rule, the werewolf race would have a higher place in society than under the <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">Ministry of Magic</a>. Greyback, though permitted to wear Death Eater robes, was treated with very little respect among the true Death Eaters, who would not hesitate to attack him if he steps out of his bounds, a fact he was painfully aware of.
</p><p>Because of his membership of the <a href="/wiki/Order_of_the_Phoenix" title="Order of the Phoenix">Order of the Phoenix</a>, Remus Lupin was sent under <a href="/wiki/Albus_Dumbledore" title="Albus Dumbledore">Albus Dumbledore</a>'s orders to integrate himself into the underground werewolf community to serve as a spy for the Order.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> However, his attempt to live among the magical community made it difficult for him to gain the army's trust.
</p><p>Upon Voldemort successfully <a href="/wiki/Fall_of_the_Ministry_of_Magic" title="Fall of the Ministry of Magic">conquering the Ministry</a>, the werewolf army became more active, becoming <a href="/wiki/Snatchers" title="Snatchers">Snatchers</a> to capture Muggle-borns and insurgents for reward money. Greyback in particular led a platoon of Snatchers for such purposes. Upon <a href="/wiki/Voldemort%27s_Last_Stand" title="Voldemort&#39;s Last Stand">Voldemort's defeat</a>, Greyback was defeated and either captured or killed, thus leaving the army's status unknown.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Werewolf_army?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Werewolf_army?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Werewolves would have been indistinguishable from other witches and wizards during the&#160;Battle Of Hogwarts as it was not a full moon, something we know because Lupin and Greyback&#160;did not transform. Before the Covered Bridge is blown out you can clearly hear wolves, which could have only been real wolves and not transformed werewolves.
</p>
