<p>This <b><a href="/wiki/Dark_Wizard" title="Dark Wizard">Dark Wizard</a></b> was a <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eater</a> and one of <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a>'s servants during the <a href="/wiki/Second_Wizarding_War" title="Second Wizarding War">Second Wizarding War</a>. He fought during the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a> in <a href="/wiki/1998" title="1998">1998</a>, although it is unknown if he was killed during the battle, abandoned the battle or was defeated, arrested and sentenced to imprisonment in <a href="/wiki/Azkaban" title="Azkaban">Azkaban</a> for his crimes as as a Death Eater.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
