<b>Davey Gudgeon</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> and student at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> in the <a href="/wiki/1970s" title="1970s">1970s</a>. He got his face scarred and almost lost his eye trying to get close enough to the <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Whomping_Willow" title="Hogwarts Whomping Willow">Whomping Willow</a> to touch its trunk. For their own safety (and possibly the Willow's as well), students were forbidden to go near the tree since that event occurred.<sup id="cite_ref-POA10_2-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-POA10-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
