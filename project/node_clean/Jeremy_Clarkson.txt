<b>Jeremy Clarkson</b> is a <a href="/wiki/Great_Britain" title="Great Britain">British</a> broadcaster and journalist best known as the co-presenter of the <a href="/wiki/Television" title="Television">television</a> programme <i><a href="/wiki/Top_Gear" title="Top Gear">Top Gear</a></i>. In <a href="/wiki/2015" title="2015">2015</a>, the <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/BBC" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:BBC">BBC</a> announced it was cancelling Clarkson's contract after he verbally and physically attacked one of the show's producers, thus leading to an <a href="/wiki/Internet" title="Internet">online</a> petition to reinstate him.<sup id="cite_ref-twitter_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-twitter-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Among those who signed the petition was <a href="/wiki/Vernon_Dursley" title="Vernon Dursley">Vernon Dursley</a>, who stated that <i>Top Gear</i> was the only reason he paid his <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/television_licence" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:television licence">television licence</a>, which all British households are required to pay yearly in order to legally view television broadcasts.<sup id="cite_ref-twitter_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-twitter-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
