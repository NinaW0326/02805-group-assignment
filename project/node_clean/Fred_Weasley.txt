<p><b>Fred Weasley</b> (<a href="/wiki/1_April" title="1 April">1 April</a>, <a href="/wiki/1978" title="1978">1978</a> – <a href="/wiki/2_May" title="2 May">2 May</a>, <a href="/wiki/1998" title="1998">1998</a>)<sup id="cite_ref-DH32_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH32-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> was an <a href="/wiki/England" title="England">English</a> <a href="/wiki/Pure-blood" title="Pure-blood">pure-blood</a> <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a>, was the fourth son and the middle child the most daring and dominant among the twins of <a href="/wiki/Arthur_Weasley" title="Arthur Weasley">Arthur Weasley</a> and <a href="/wiki/Molly_Weasley" title="Molly Weasley">Molly Weasley</a> <span class="template-c">(née Prewett)</span>, younger brother to <a href="/wiki/William_Weasley" title="William Weasley">Bill</a>, <a href="/wiki/Charles_Weasley" title="Charles Weasley">Charlie</a> and  <a href="/wiki/Percy_Weasley" title="Percy Weasley">Percy</a>, and older brother of <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ron</a> and  <a href="/wiki/Ginevra_Weasley" title="Ginevra Weasley">Ginny Weasley</a>, and older twin brother and best friend to <a href="/wiki/George_Weasley" title="George Weasley">George Weasley</a>. Fred's first few years were marked by the height of the <a href="/wiki/First_Wizarding_War" title="First Wizarding War">First Wizarding War</a> and when <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a> fell for the first time. 
</p><p>He attended <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> from <a href="/wiki/1989" title="1989">1989</a>-<a href="/wiki/1996" title="1996">1996</a> and was sorted into <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor" title="Gryffindor">Gryffindor House</a>. Both he and his twin brother were popular students, known for their sense of humour, pranks, and were also <a href="/wiki/Beater" title="Beater">Beaters</a> for the <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor_Quidditch_team" title="Gryffindor Quidditch team">Gryffindor Quidditch team</a>. In their seventh year, they joined <a href="/wiki/Dumbledore%27s_Army" title="Dumbledore&#39;s Army">Dumbledore's Army</a>, an organisation taught and led by <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>.
</p><p>A born prankster and inventor, Fred left Hogwarts shortly prior to graduation to become a successful entrepreneur, alongside his twin brother, George, and opened the joke shop <a href="/wiki/Weasleys%27_Wizard_Wheezes" title="Weasleys&#39; Wizard Wheezes">Weasleys' Wizard Wheezes</a> at <a href="/wiki/93_Diagon_Alley" title="93 Diagon Alley">93 Diagon Alley</a>. Fred was also a member of the <a href="/wiki/Order_of_the_Phoenix" title="Order of the Phoenix">Order of the Phoenix</a>; he fought in the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_the_Seven_Potters" title="Battle of the Seven Potters">Battle of the Seven Potters</a> (<a href="/wiki/1997" title="1997">1997</a>) and was a commentator for <i><a href="/wiki/Potterwatch" title="Potterwatch">Potterwatch</a></i> during the height of the <a href="/wiki/Second_Wizarding_War" title="Second Wizarding War">Second Wizarding War</a>. Fred participated in the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a> (<a href="/wiki/1998" title="1998">1998</a>), defending the castle's passageways with his twin. While fighting alongside his brother, <a href="/wiki/Percy_Weasley" title="Percy Weasley">Percy</a>, he was killed in an explosion caused by <a href="/wiki/Augustus_Rookwood" title="Augustus Rookwood">Augustus Rookwood</a>.
</p>

<h3><span id="Early_life_(1978-1989)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_life_.281978-1989.29">Early life <span class="template-c">(1978-1989)</span></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Fred_Weasley?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Early life (1978-1989)">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Fred_Weasley?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Early life (1978-1989)"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>

<p>Appropriately enough, Fred Weasley, along with his identical twin <a href="/wiki/George_Weasley" title="George Weasley">George</a>, was born on <a href="/wiki/1_April" title="1 April">1 April</a>, <a href="/wiki/1978" title="1978">1978</a>; April Fool's Day to <a href="/wiki/Arthur_Weasley" title="Arthur Weasley">Arthur</a> and <a href="/wiki/Molly_Weasley" title="Molly Weasley">Molly Weasley</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-11" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-11">&#91;11&#93;</a></sup> Fred was born first.<sup id="cite_ref-bornfirst_12-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-bornfirst-12">&#91;12&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-bornfirst2_13-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-bornfirst2-13">&#91;13&#93;</a></sup> He was raised at his family home of <a href="/wiki/The_Burrow" title="The Burrow">The Burrow</a>, near <a href="/wiki/Ottery_St_Catchpole" title="Ottery St Catchpole">Ottery St Catchpole</a> in <a href="/wiki/Devon" title="Devon">Devon</a>, <a href="/wiki/England" title="England">England</a>. In addition to his twin brother, Fred had three older brothers, <a href="/wiki/William_Weasley" title="William Weasley">Bill</a>, <a href="/wiki/Charles_Weasley" title="Charles Weasley">Charlie</a> and <a href="/wiki/Percy_Weasley" title="Percy Weasley">Percy</a>, and two younger siblings, Ron and <a href="/wiki/Ginevra_Weasley" title="Ginevra Weasley">Ginny</a>. The <a href="/wiki/Weasley_family" title="Weasley family">Weasley family</a> was poor, but loving.
</p><p>Fred and George enjoyed making mischief from a young age, and were quite a handful for their parents. It was not uncommon for explosions to be heard coming from the bedroom the two shared. They also greatly enjoyed teasing their brothers Percy and Ron. At one point, they even used their family's pet <a href="/wiki/Weasleys%27_Puffskein" title="Weasleys&#39; Puffskein">Puffskein</a> for <a href="/wiki/Bludger" title="Bludger">Bludger</a> practice.<sup id="cite_ref-FB.7CR.7CAn_A-Z_of_Fantastic_Beasts.7CPuffskein_14-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-FB.7CR.7CAn_A-Z_of_Fantastic_Beasts.7CPuffskein-14">&#91;14&#93;</a></sup> At the age of five, Fred <a href="/wiki/Underage_magic" title="Underage magic">transformed</a> Ron's <a href="/wiki/Teddy_bear" title="Teddy bear">teddy bear</a> into a giant spider, sparking his arachnophobia, after Ron broke his <a href="/wiki/Toy_broomstick" title="Toy broomstick">toy broomstick</a>. Also around that age, he gave Ron an <a href="/wiki/Acid_Pops" title="Acid Pops">Acid Pop</a>, which burnt a hole through his tongue.<sup id="cite_ref-COS_15-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COS-15">&#91;15&#93;</a></sup> When they were seven years old, Fred and George nearly succeeded in getting Ron to make an <a href="/wiki/Unbreakable_Vow" title="Unbreakable Vow">Unbreakable Vow</a> until their father found them; he was so angry that Fred later said "<i>his </i>(Fred's)<i> left buttock has never been the same since.</i>"<sup id="cite_ref-HBP_9-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup> One <a href="/wiki/Christmas" title="Christmas">Christmas</a>, Fred and George set off a <a href="/wiki/Dungbomb" title="Dungbomb">Dungbomb</a> under the chair of their great-aunt <a href="/wiki/Muriel" title="Muriel">Muriel</a> during a family dinner. The elderly witch most likely wrote the twins out of her will for this stunt, and stopped attending Christmas celebrations at the Burrow, for which all the Weasley children were thankful.<sup id="cite_ref-DH_16-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH-16">&#91;16&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<p>Fred was educated at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> from <a href="/wiki/1989" title="1989">1989</a>-<a href="/wiki/1996" title="1996">1996</a>, and was Sorted into <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor" title="Gryffindor">Gryffindor</a> <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Houses" title="Hogwarts Houses">House</a>. He was very popular among the Gryffindors in school, and he and George were well known for their senses of humour and pranks. The twins even set up their own joke shop in the disused bathroom on the seventh floor. Among Fred's many friends were: <a href="/wiki/Lee_Jordan" title="Lee Jordan">Lee Jordan</a>, <a href="/wiki/Angelina_Johnson" title="Angelina Johnson">Angelina Johnson</a>, <a href="/wiki/Alicia_Spinnet" title="Alicia Spinnet">Alicia Spinnet</a>, <a href="/wiki/Oliver_Wood" title="Oliver Wood">Oliver Wood</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Katie_Bell" title="Katie Bell">Katie Bell</a>. During their first year at Hogwarts, Fred and George swiped the <a href="/wiki/Marauder%27s_Map" title="Marauder&#39;s Map">Marauder's Map</a> from <a href="/wiki/Argus_Filch" title="Argus Filch">Argus Filch</a>'s office.<sup id="cite_ref-POA_17-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-POA-17">&#91;17&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Second_year">Second year</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Fred_Weasley?veaction=edit&amp;section=5" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Second year">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Fred_Weasley?action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Second year"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<p>In their second year, Fred and George became <a href="/wiki/Beater" title="Beater">Beaters</a> for the <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor_Quidditch_team" title="Gryffindor Quidditch team">Gryffindor Quidditch team</a>. They both used <a href="/wiki/Cleansweep_Five" title="Cleansweep Five">Cleansweep Five</a> broomsticks and were so skilled at <a href="/wiki/Quidditch" title="Quidditch">Quidditch</a> that they were both described as being like "Human <a href="/wiki/Bludger" title="Bludger">Bludgers</a>".<sup id="cite_ref-PS_5-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PS-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>It was noted by Ron Weasley on the Hogwarts Express that Fred and George achieved "really good marks", at least in their early years.<sup id="cite_ref-PS_5-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PS-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<p>Fred also became friends with <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> in his third year, when Harry palled around with his younger brother <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ron</a> and was made the Quidditch team's <a href="/wiki/Seeker" title="Seeker">Seeker</a>. Fred and the other players believed that the addition of Harry would be a real shot in the arm to their chances of winning the <a href="/wiki/Inter-House_Quidditch_Cup" title="Inter-House Quidditch Cup">Quidditch Cup</a>. Around the <a href="/wiki/Christmas" title="Christmas">Christmas</a> holidays, Fred and George got in trouble for bewitching several <a href="/wiki/Snowball" title="Snowball">snowballs</a> to follow <a href="/wiki/Quirinus_Quirrell" title="Quirinus Quirrell">Professor Quirrell</a> around, and hit him in the <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle#Search_for_the_Philosopher.27s_Stone" title="Tom Riddle">back of the head</a> (they were, of course, unaware that they were throwing snowballs in Voldemort's face).
</p><p>They stayed in the castle for the holidays along with their brothers and Harry, all of whom received <a href="/wiki/British_terms#J" title="British terms">jumpers</a> from <a href="/wiki/Molly_Weasley" title="Molly Weasley">Mrs Weasley</a>. The jumpers she sent to Fred and <a href="/wiki/George_Weasley" title="George Weasley">George</a> didn't have their initials on them as Harry's and Ron's did, prompting them to call themselves "Gred and Forge". The twins also dragged <a href="/wiki/Percy_Weasley" title="Percy Weasley">Percy</a> away from his fellow <a href="/wiki/Prefect" title="Prefect">prefects</a>, insisting that Christmas was time for family, and stole his prefect badge, much to his irritation.<sup id="cite_ref-PS_5-10" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PS-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<p>In the summer of 1992, Fred, George, and Ron rescued Harry from his relatives' home after not hearing from him during the previous summer month. They pulled the bars off his window and flew him to the<a href="/wiki/The_Burrow" title="The Burrow"> Burrow</a> in their father's <a href="/wiki/Flying_Ford_Anglia" title="Flying Ford Anglia">Ford Anglia</a>. While at the Burrow Fred and George came to know Harry better as they spent time flying with him. The twins soon began their fourth year at Hogwarts, and took to teasing their younger sister <a href="/wiki/Ginevra_Weasley" title="Ginevra Weasley">Ginny</a> as she began her first year. They were trying to cheer her up, unaware that her emotional state was fragile due to the influence of <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle%27s_Diary" class="mw-redirect" title="Tom Riddle&#39;s Diary">Tom Riddle's Diary</a>.
</p>

<p>That year, the Chamber of Secrets was opened and frighting messages were written on the castle walls. Rumours were circulating that Harry was responsible for it, since he spoke <a href="/wiki/Parseltongue" title="Parseltongue">Parseltongue</a> and thus might be the <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Heir of Slytherin</a>. Neither Fred nor George took these rumours seriously, they found the whole thing laughable and frequently joked about it (apparently taking Harry though crowded corridors shouting, "Make way!"). They also came to <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione Granger</a>'s defence when <a href="/wiki/Draco_Malfoy" title="Draco Malfoy">Draco Malfoy</a> called her a "<i>filthy little <a href="/wiki/Mudblood" title="Mudblood">Mudblood</a></i>", by lunging at him, only to be blocked by <a href="/wiki/Slytherin_Quidditch_team" title="Slytherin Quidditch team">Slytherin Quidditch</a> <a href="/wiki/Quidditch_Captain" title="Quidditch Captain">Captain</a> <a href="/wiki/Marcus_Flint" title="Marcus Flint">Marcus Flint</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-COS_15-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COS-15">&#91;15&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Fred helped protect Harry form a rogue <a href="/wiki/Bludger" title="Bludger">Bludger</a> during Gryffindor's opening match against Slytherin. After Gryffindor won the Quidditch match, Fred went to the hospital wing with the rest of the Gryffindor Quidditch team to see Harry because <a href="/wiki/Gilderoy_Lockhart" title="Gilderoy Lockhart">Gilderoy Lockhart</a> had removed all the bones in his arm, and congratulated him for his unbelievable flying, but not before seeing Marcus Flint yelling at Draco Malfoy for "<i>having the <a href="/wiki/Golden_Snitch" title="Golden Snitch">Snitch</a> on top of his head and not noticing</i>", of which he informed Harry.<sup id="cite_ref-COS_15-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COS-15">&#91;15&#93;</a></sup> At the end of the school year, Fred was relieved when his younger sister was saved — and delighted when she revealed that Percy had a secret girlfriend, as this was another opportunity to take the mickey out of him.<sup id="cite_ref-COS_15-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COS-15">&#91;15&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<p><a href="/wiki/Arthur_Weasley" title="Arthur Weasley">Arthur Weasley</a> had won a large amount of gold from a Ministry contest, something that was both a surprise and delight for the family. The Weasleys used the money won that summer to take a holiday to <a href="/wiki/Egypt" title="Egypt">Egypt</a>, where the oldest son <a href="/wiki/William_Weasley" title="William Weasley">Bill</a> was working as a <a href="/wiki/Curse-Breaker" title="Curse-Breaker">Curse Breaker</a>. While there, Fred and George tried to push Percy, who had been acting even more arrogant and pompous than usual, because he learned he had been appointed <a href="/wiki/Head_Boy_or_Girl" title="Head Boy or Girl">Head Boy</a>, into a pyramid. Their mother stopped them and scolded them severely. They and their brothers went in all the pyramids. They also annoyed <a href="/wiki/Ginevra_Weasley" title="Ginevra Weasley">Ginny</a> when she couldn't go in the last one, most likely because her their mother thought her too young.<sup id="cite_ref-POA_17-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-POA-17">&#91;17&#93;</a></sup>

<p>In Harry's third year and their fifth, Fred and George gave Harry the <a href="/wiki/Marauder%27s_Map" title="Marauder&#39;s Map">Marauder's Map</a>, as they already knew it by heart and Harry needed it in order to sneak into <a href="/wiki/Hogsmeade" title="Hogsmeade">Hogsmeade</a>. They also tried to cheer him up after he fainted near the <a href="/wiki/Dementor" title="Dementor">Dementors</a> that were guarding the school that year, due to <a href="/wiki/Sirius_Black" title="Sirius Black">Sirius Black</a> <a href="/wiki/Azkaban_escapes#1993" title="Azkaban escapes">escaping</a> from <a href="/wiki/Azkaban" title="Azkaban">Azkaban</a>. Their attempt to cheer up Ron after his pet rat <a href="/wiki/Peter_Pettigrew" title="Peter Pettigrew">Scabbers</a> went missing was not as successful, as Fred and George reminded him that Scabbers was boring.<sup id="cite_ref-POA_17-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-POA-17">&#91;17&#93;</a></sup> That year, they also won the Quidditch Cup along with Harry.
</p><p>Fred and George began inventing magical prank items the summer of 1994, starting with the <a href="/wiki/Ton-Tongue_Toffee" title="Ton-Tongue Toffee">Ton-Tongue Toffee</a>, which Fred "dropped" near Harry's cousin <a href="/wiki/Dudley_Dursley" title="Dudley Dursley">Dudley Dursley</a> in order to test it out, which proved to be  very successful. Fred also attended the <a href="/wiki/Quidditch_World_Cup" title="Quidditch World Cup">Quidditch World Cup</a> with his parents, siblings, <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione Granger</a>, and gambled all of his money on the outcome, which their father told them not to mention to their mother. When <a href="/wiki/Ludovic_Bagman" title="Ludovic Bagman">Ludo Bagman</a> defaulted on his bet, Fred was furious, and suggested that it was "time to play dirty" like Bagman by informing the <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">Ministry of Magic</a> of his activities. George was against the idea, arguing that it was blackmail.

<p>Once at school for their sixth year (before their sixth year they attended the Quidditch World Cup with their family), George and Fred began selling, as well as experimenting with, their inventions. That year, Hogwarts also hosted the <a href="/wiki/Triwizard_Tournament" title="Triwizard Tournament">Triwizard Tournament</a>, which Fred and George both wanted to enter, despite being underage. They attempted to fool the <a href="/wiki/Age_Line" title="Age Line">Age Line</a> spell that <a href="/wiki/Albus_Dumbledore" title="Albus Dumbledore">Albus Dumbledore</a> put around the <a href="/wiki/Goblet_of_Fire" title="Goblet of Fire">Goblet of Fire</a> using an Ageing Potion, but the attempt was unsuccessful, though they both thought their beards were uproariously funny.

At the end of the school year, <a href="/wiki/Cedric_Diggory" title="Cedric Diggory">Cedric Diggory</a>, one of the champions, was murdered by <a href="/wiki/Peter_Pettigrew" title="Peter Pettigrew">Peter Pettigrew</a> on the orders of the newly revived <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a>, who was reborn.<sup id="cite_ref-GOF_18-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF-18">&#91;18&#93;</a></sup> Fred and George were surprised and very grateful when Harry gave them the thousand Galleons he won in the Triwizard Tournament in order for them to start <a href="/wiki/Weasleys%27_Wizard_Wheezes" title="Weasleys&#39; Wizard Wheezes">their joke shop.</a><sup id="cite_ref-GOF_18-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF-18">&#91;18&#93;</a></sup>

<p>In the summer of 1995, before their final year at Hogwarts, Fred, and George passed their <a href="/wiki/Apparition_(class)" title="Apparition (class)">Apparition</a> tests "with distinction," and took to <a href="/wiki/Apparition" title="Apparition">Apparating</a> and <a href="/wiki/Apparition" title="Apparition">Disapparating</a> around <a href="/wiki/12_Grimmauld_Place" title="12 Grimmauld Place">12 Grimmauld Place</a> to surprise people, much to the displeasure of their mother. They helped clean up the headquarters of the <a href="/wiki/Order_of_the_Phoenix" title="Order of the Phoenix">Order of the Phoenix</a>, but they also pocketed <a href="/wiki/Doxy" title="Doxy">Doxies</a> from behind the curtains (and possibly <a href="/wiki/Wartcap_powder" title="Wartcap powder">Wartcap powder</a>) for their experiments, and spied on the Order's meetings using the <a href="/wiki/Extendable_Ears" title="Extendable Ears">Extendable Ears</a> they invented (although most of the time the Ears proved useless as the door was often <a href="/wiki/Imperturbable_Charm" title="Imperturbable Charm">Imperturbed</a>).

<p>Fred and George resumed testing and selling their products at Hogwarts in their seventh year, though <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione Granger</a> managed to prevent them from testing out <a href="/wiki/Skiving_Snackbox" title="Skiving Snackbox">Skiving Snackboxes</a> on unsuspecting first years by threatening to tell their mother.The twins also joined Dumbledore's Army to oppose the dictatorial running of Hogwarts under High Inquisitor <a href="/wiki/Dolores_Umbridge" title="Dolores Umbridge">Dolores Umbridge</a>, and to prepare to fight <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a> and his <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a>.
</p><p>Umbridge kicked them both off the <a href="/wiki/Quidditch_team" title="Quidditch team">Quidditch team</a> that year after George and Harry got into a fight with <a href="/wiki/Draco_Malfoy" title="Draco Malfoy">Draco Malfoy</a>, who had penned the demeaning song <i><a href="/wiki/Weasley_is_Our_King" title="Weasley is Our King">Weasley is Our King</a></i> to humiliate <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ron</a>, and then proceeded to insult the Weasley parents and the late <a href="/wiki/Lily_J._Potter" title="Lily J. Potter">Lily Potter</a>. Fred would have participated in the fight, but was held back by three of his team-mates. The Weasleys suffered a near-devastating blow that Christmas when <a href="/wiki/Arthur_Weasley" title="Arthur Weasley">Arthur Weasley</a> was bitten by <a href="/wiki/Nagini" title="Nagini">Nagini</a>, Lord Voldemort's snake pet. Fred was greatly upset by his father's injury, especially since <a href="/wiki/Percy_Weasley" title="Percy Weasley">Percy</a> did not join his family at <a href="/wiki/St_Mungo%27s_Hospital_for_Magical_Maladies_and_Injuries" title="St Mungo&#39;s Hospital for Magical Maladies and Injuries">St Mungo's</a>, deepening his estrangement.
</p><p>When the D.A. was discovered later in the school year, <a href="/wiki/Albus_Dumbledore" title="Albus Dumbledore">Albus Dumbledore</a> took the blame and departed Hogwarts, leaving Umbridge as Headmistress. Fred and George decided that they no longer cared about getting into trouble, and that they were going to cause as much mayhem for Umbridge as possible. When the <a href="/wiki/Inquisitorial_Squad" title="Inquisitorial Squad">Inquisitorial Squad</a> was formed and one member, <a href="/wiki/Graham_Montague" title="Graham Montague">Graham Montague</a>, attempted to dock House Points from them, Fred and George shoved him head-first into a <a href="/wiki/Vanishing_Cabinet" title="Vanishing Cabinet">Vanishing Cabinet</a> before he could finish speaking.
</p>

<p>Initially, Fred and George's ambitions to open a joke shop had been something of a disappointment to their parents, in particular their mother, who had hoped they would seek employment with the <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">Ministry of Magic</a> like their father.<sup id="cite_ref-OOP_10-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOP-10">&#91;10&#93;</a></sup> However, the spectacular success of the twins' business, <a href="/wiki/Weasleys%27_Wizard_Wheezes" title="Weasleys&#39; Wizard Wheezes">Weasleys' Wizard Wheezes</a>, located at 93 <a href="/wiki/Diagon_Alley" title="Diagon Alley">Diagon Alley</a>, immensely impressed them both and became a source of much joy and pride in their sons accomplishment.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP_9-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup> What had started out as a mail-order experiment in entrepreneurship skyrocketed into a very lucrative undertaking. <h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Post-mortem">Post-mortem</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Fred_Weasley?veaction=edit&amp;section=18" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Post-mortem">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Fred_Weasley?action=edit&amp;section=18" title="Edit section: Post-mortem"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>His whole family, including extended family and friends such as Harry and Hermione, were devastated when Fred died. Ron and Harry came very close to forgetting their primary goal of hunting down Voldemort's remaining <a href="/wiki/Horcrux" title="Horcrux">Horcruxes</a> in the wake of Fred's death, being so enraged that they wished to hunt down Death Eaters to kill, with only Hermione's persuasiveness were they able to restrain themselves. Percy became extremely furious that he chased down Rookwood in an attempt at revenge, and Molly was utterly murderous when <a href="/wiki/Bellatrix_Lestrange" title="Bellatrix Lestrange">Bellatrix Lestrange</a> mocked her of Fred's death, to the point of killing her with astounding duelling skills. The rest of the Weasley family survived to see the downfall of Voldemort and the end of the <a href="/wiki/Second_Wizarding_War" title="Second Wizarding War">war</a>. His twin brother George later had <a href="/wiki/Fred_Weasley_II" title="Fred Weasley II">a son</a> whom he named Fred in honour of his lost brother.
</p><p>In the 2010s, Fred's past self appeared as a <a href="/wiki/Foundable" title="Foundable">Foundable</a> in two different instances during the <a href="/wiki/Calamity" title="Calamity">Calamity</a>. In the first, he appeared wearing a <a href="/wiki/Weasley_jumper" title="Weasley jumper">Weasley jumper</a> with the letter F for his first name on the front, hounded by a pair of <a href="/wiki/Howler" title="Howler">Howler</a> <a href="/wiki/Confoundable" title="Confoundable">Confoundables</a>. Volunteer members of the <a href="/wiki/Statute_of_Secrecy_Task_Force" title="Statute of Secrecy Task Force">Statute of Secrecy Task Force</a> had to cast the <a href="/wiki/Fire-Making_Spell" title="Fire-Making Spell">Fire-Making Spell</a> in order to disperse them. Fred also appeared alongside his Yule Ball date, Angelina, trapped by a snowstorm Confoundable, which required the usage of <a href="/wiki/Meteolojinx_Recanto" title="Meteolojinx Recanto">Meteolojinx Recanto</a> in order to dispel it and return the pair to their own time.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Physical_description">Physical description</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Fred_Weasley?veaction=edit&amp;section=19" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Physical description">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Fred_Weasley?action=edit&amp;section=19" title="Edit section: Physical description"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>

<p>Fred, similar to all the <a href="/wiki/Weasley_family" title="Weasley family">Weasley</a> children, had a head of flaming-red hair and abundant freckles. He had a shorter and stockier build, like his brothers <a href="/wiki/George_Weasley" title="George Weasley">George</a> and <a href="/wiki/Charles_Weasley" title="Charles Weasley">Charlie</a>, rather than a tall, thin build like his brothers <a href="/wiki/William_Weasley" title="William Weasley">Bill</a>, <a href="/wiki/Percy_Weasley" title="Percy Weasley">Percy</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ron</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-PS_5-11" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PS-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> However, Fred was taller than Harry Potter, and thus was still above average height.<sup id="cite_ref-19" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-19">&#91;19&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>He also has a big nose, like Molly.<sup id="cite_ref-20" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-20">&#91;20&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Personality_and_traits">Personality and traits</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Fred_Weasley?veaction=edit&amp;section=20" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Personality and traits">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Fred_Weasley?action=edit&amp;section=20" title="Edit section: Personality and traits"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>

<p>Fred Weasley was the more outgoing, daring, and sarcastic of the twins. During the rescue from the <a href="/wiki/Dursley_family" title="Dursley family">Dursleys</a> in <a href="/wiki/1992" title="1992">1992</a>, <a href="/wiki/George_Weasley" title="George Weasley">George</a> <a href="/wiki/Lock_picking" title="Lock picking">picked</a> the lock on Harry's bedroom door with a hairpin, while Fred insisted <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggle</a> tricks were important to know, even if it was "a bit slow"<sup id="cite_ref-COS_15-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COS-15">&#91;15&#93;</a></sup>, and "accidentally dropped" a <a href="/wiki/Ton-Tongue_Toffee" title="Ton-Tongue Toffee">Ton-Tongue Toffee</a> for <a href="/wiki/Dudley_Dursley" title="Dudley Dursley">Dudley Dursley</a> to find and eat. Fred concocted the scheme of trying to fool <a href="/wiki/Albus_Dumbledore" title="Albus Dumbledore">Albus Dumbledore</a>'s Age Line to get into the <a href="/wiki/Triwizard_Tournament" title="Triwizard Tournament">Triwizard Tournament</a>, though it was unsuccessful, and was in favour of blackmailing <a href="/wiki/Ludovic_Bagman" title="Ludovic Bagman">Ludo Bagman</a> after he defaulted on a bet, while George had reservations.<sup id="cite_ref-GOF_18-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF-18">&#91;18&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Fred tended to take the ringleader role in their pranks, but was also crueller in his humour or in dealing with those he disliked<sup id="cite_ref-msnbc_21-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-msnbc-21">&#91;21&#93;</a></sup>; for example, he and George had absolutely no compunction in forcing <a href="/wiki/Graham_Montague" title="Graham Montague">Montague</a> into a <a href="/wiki/Vanishing_Cabinet" title="Vanishing Cabinet">Vanishing Cabinet</a>, an act which, according to <a href="/wiki/Draco_Malfoy" title="Draco Malfoy">Malfoy</a>, nearly killed him. However, it is possible that he was unaware that the cabinet was broken and thought Montague would have stumbled out of another cabinet, too confused to remember to dock points from them, as he initially was planning to do. Fred could be quite callous in dealing with the victims of his pranks, particularly his older brother <a href="/wiki/Percy_Weasley" title="Percy Weasley">Percy</a>, but was more compassionate when he did not feel that the victim deserved it; he immediately provided <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione Granger</a> with <a href="/wiki/Bruise_removal_paste" title="Bruise removal paste">bruise removal paste</a> after she was accidentally given a black eye by the twins' <a href="/wiki/Boxing_telescope" title="Boxing telescope">boxing telescope</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP_9-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup> Fred was also the first to forgive Percy when he reconciled with the family.<sup id="cite_ref-DH_16-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH-16">&#91;16&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Both he and George were renowned for their senses of humour; they were able to make people laugh and relax even in very difficult times. Neither Fred nor George took school very seriously, but they were both very talented wizards, and demonstrated their ingenuity in their inventions and their business sense in running a highly lucrative shop. They were also staunchly loyal to their loved ones and very brave,(as evidenced by their been sorted into <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor" title="Gryffindor">Gryffindor</a>) and unafraid to stand up against what they thought was wrong, whether it was <a href="/wiki/Dolores_Umbridge" title="Dolores Umbridge">Dolores Umbridge</a>'s tyranny at Hogwarts or <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a>.
</p>

<p>Although not a high achiever academically, Fred was a very gifted and creative <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a>. He and <a href="/wiki/George_Weasley" title="George Weasley">his brother</a> were extremely skilled at charming and bewitching items. <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> once expressed bewilderment as to how he and George received only three <a href="/wiki/Ordinary_Wizarding_Level" title="Ordinary Wizarding Level">O.W.L.s</a> each, remarking that they "<i>really know their stuff</i>".<sup id="cite_ref-OOP_10-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOP-10">&#91;10&#93;</a></sup> When the twins turned seventeen, they were able to use <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magic</a> outside of Hogwarts and took full advantage of this. They did everything by [magic] and frequently <a href="/wiki/Apparition" title="Apparition">Apparated</a> around <a href="/wiki/12_Grimmauld_Place" title="12 Grimmauld Place">12 Grimmauld Place</a> and the <a href="/wiki/The_Burrow" title="The Burrow">Burrow</a>.

<li><b><a href="/wiki/Transfiguration" title="Transfiguration">Transfiguration</a></b>: Fred was proficient it transfiguration; it was one of the few <a href="/wiki/Ordinary_Wizarding_Level" title="Ordinary Wizarding Level">O.W.L.s</a> Fred ever tried to achieve.<sup id="cite_ref-23" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-23">&#91;23&#93;</a></sup> He was shown to be talented with <a href="/wiki/Transformation" title="Transformation">transformation</a>, given how he successfully <a href="/wiki/Teddy_bear_to_spider" title="Teddy bear to spider">turned</a> a <a href="/wiki/Teddy_bear" title="Teddy bear">teddy bear</a> into a <a href="/wiki/Spider" title="Spider">spider</a> when he was a young boy,<sup id="cite_ref-COS9_24-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COS9-24">&#91;24&#93;</a></sup> and also effortlessly <a href="/wiki/Transforming_Spells" title="Transforming Spells">transformed</a> a <a href="/wiki/Knife_to_paper_aeroplane_spell" title="Knife to paper aeroplane spell">knife into a paper aeroplane</a> with one lazy flick of his wand, showing his talent in this field.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP_9-10" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Potion" title="Potion">Potions</a></b>: As part-owner of <a href="/wiki/Weasley%27s_Wizard_Wheezes" class="mw-redirect" title="Weasley&#39;s Wizard Wheezes">Weasley's Wizard Wheezes</a>, Fred was quite skilled in creating new <a href="/wiki/Potion" title="Potion">potions</a>, most of the time with positive, humorous effects. He and Fred invented several <a href="/wiki/Love_Potion" class="mw-redirect" title="Love Potion">love potions</a> as part of their <a href="/wiki/WonderWitch" title="WonderWitch">WonderWitch</a> line of feminine products, showing his proficiency in potion-making, as love potions are known to be advanced brews.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP_9-11" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Defence_Against_the_Dark_Arts" title="Defence Against the Dark Arts">Defence Against the Dark Arts</a></b>: Fred was skilled in Defence Against the Dark Arts; he achieved an <a href="/wiki/Ordinary_Wizarding_Level" title="Ordinary Wizarding Level">O.W.L.</a> in it and further improved his skills in <a href="/wiki/Dumbledore%27s_Army" title="Dumbledore&#39;s Army">Dumbledore's Army</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-25" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-25">&#91;25&#93;</a></sup> Fred's talent at defence would contribute to his ability to invent Defence Against the Dark Arts items with George such as <a href="/wiki/Decoy_Detonator" title="Decoy Detonator">Decoy Detonators</a>, and to obtain rare and useful defence items such as <a href="/wiki/Peruvian_Instant_Darkness_Powder" title="Peruvian Instant Darkness Powder">Peruvian Instant Darkness Powder</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP_9-12" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup> Fred also joined the <a href="/wiki/Order_of_the_Phoenix" title="Order of the Phoenix">Order of the Phoenix</a> at a young age, and participated in a number of its operations. He fought in the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_the_Seven_Potters" title="Battle of the Seven Potters">Battle of the Seven Potters</a> and came out unscathed, and in the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a>, during which he defeated a <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eater</a> opponent and was in charge of guarding the secret school passages, along with George.<sup id="cite_ref-DH_16-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH-16">&#91;16&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Duelling" title="Duelling">Duelling</a></b>: Fred was a skilled duelist even though he only fought in the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_the_Seven_Potters" title="Battle of the Seven Potters">Battle of the Seven Potters</a> and the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a>. He survived the Battle of the Seven Potters uninjured, and defeated many <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a> prior to his death in the Battle of Hogwarts, showing his excellent skills in <a href="/wiki/Martial_magic" title="Martial magic">martial magic</a> to have participated in such violent and dangerous conflicts.<sup id="cite_ref-DH_16-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH-16">&#91;16&#93;</a></sup></li></ul>

<p>George Weasley was Fred's twin, best friend, and partner in crime. They were about as close as two brothers can be. They did just about everything together. They often finished each other's sentences and spoke in a back and forth way that could confuse others at times. Together, Fred and George designed many ingenious pranks and shared the ambition to found a <a href="/wiki/Weasleys%27_Wizard_Wheezes" title="Weasleys&#39; Wizard Wheezes">joke shop</a> together.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP_9-15" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup> They were also both <a href="/wiki/Beater" title="Beater">Beaters</a> for the <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor_Quidditch_team" title="Gryffindor Quidditch team">Gryffindor Quidditch team</a> and close friends with <a href="/wiki/Lee_Jordan" title="Lee Jordan">Lee Jordan</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-OOP_10-13" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOP-10">&#91;10&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>George was devastated by the death of his twin in the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a>, and would never truly get over this loss. He eventually named his firstborn <a href="/wiki/Fred_Weasley_II" title="Fred Weasley II">son</a> after Fred.<sup id="cite_ref-DH_16-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH-16">&#91;16&#93;</a></sup>

<p>Like his younger siblings, Fred tended to look up to his older brothers <a href="/wiki/William_Weasley" title="William Weasley">Bill</a> and <a href="/wiki/Charles_Weasley" title="Charles Weasley">Charlie</a>. However, he and George delighted in teasing <a href="/wiki/Percy_Weasley" title="Percy Weasley">Percy</a>, who was closer to their age and very different from them in personality. Their <a href="/wiki/Molly_Weasley" title="Molly Weasley">mother</a> was always encouraging the twins to be more like Percy, who was responsible and academically motivated, but Fred and George thought him to be stuffy and arrogant. Thus, they frequently mocked any behaviour they saw as arrogant on his part, such as his pride in his position as a school <a href="/wiki/Prefect" title="Prefect">prefect</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-COS_15-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COS-15">&#91;15&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>The twins were the angriest with Percy when he became estranged with the rest of the family over their support of <a href="/wiki/Albus_Dumbledore" title="Albus Dumbledore">Albus Dumbledore</a> and <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> instead of the <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">Ministry</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-OOP_10-14" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOP-10">&#91;10&#93;</a></sup> but also seemed the most inclined to ask about him during his estrangement, as Fred asked their mother if Percy was coming home for the <a href="/wiki/Christmas_holidays" title="Christmas holidays">Christmas holidays</a> in <a href="/wiki/1996" title="1996">1996</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP_9-16" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup> Fred was the first to accept Percy's apology during the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a>, and he and George subsequently acted as if the split had never happened. During the battle, Fred and Percy fought side by side, and Percy was devastated over his brother's death, fiercely protecting his body and furiously pursuing <a href="/wiki/Augustus_Rookwood" title="Augustus Rookwood">Augustus Rookwood</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-DH_16-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH-16">&#91;16&#93;</a></sup>

<p>The twins also enjoyed teasing their only younger brother, Ron. Notably, Fred transfigured his <a href="/wiki/Teddy_bear" title="Teddy bear">teddy bear</a> into a <a href="/wiki/Spider" title="Spider">spider</a> after Ron broke his <a href="/wiki/Toy_broomstick" title="Toy broomstick">toy broomstick</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-COS_15-10" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COS-15">&#91;15&#93;</a></sup> once nearly manipulated him into taking an <a href="/wiki/Unbreakable_Vow" title="Unbreakable Vow">Unbreakable Vow</a> with him and George,<sup id="cite_ref-HBP_9-17" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup> used his <a href="/wiki/Weasleys%27_Puffskein" title="Weasleys&#39; Puffskein">puffskein</a> for bludger practice<sup id="cite_ref-FB.7CR.7CA-Z.7CPuffskein_26-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-FB.7CR.7CA-Z.7CPuffskein-26">&#91;26&#93;</a></sup>, and teased him over his embarrassing crush on <a href="/wiki/Fleur_Delacour" title="Fleur Delacour">Fleur Delacour</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-GOF_18-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF-18">&#91;18&#93;</a></sup> Fred and George sometimes pestered Ron to the point of infuriating him, such as when they mocked him for becoming a <a href="/wiki/Prefect" title="Prefect">prefect</a>, a position Ron never tried to use against them.<sup id="cite_ref-OOP_10-15" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOP-10">&#91;10&#93;</a></sup>

They were protective of her, as well, and disapproved of her love life, it is unknown to Fred that Harry and Ginny were going out during her fifth and his sixth year and then dated after the battle and then married.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP_9-18" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Following his death, Fred gained seven nieces and five nephews.

<p>Like most members of the <a href="/wiki/Weasley_family" title="Weasley family">Weasley family</a>, Fred got on well with his parents. The atmosphere was generally light, relaxed and casual.
</p><p>Molly Weasley was often described as the only one Fred and George were afraid of, and was often unhappy with Fred's lack of seriousness and commitment to his learning. She was unhappy with both his earning of only three <a href="/wiki/Ordinary_Wizarding_Level" title="Ordinary Wizarding Level">O.W.L.s</a> and desire to own a joke shop. However, Mrs Weasley loved him very much and was devastated by her son's death during the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a>, on <a href="/wiki/2_May" title="2 May">2 May</a> <a href="/wiki/1998" title="1998">1998</a> and was seen crying over his dead body.
</p><p>Arthur Weasley was more laid back than his wife. While not overly supportive of Fred's wrongdoing, he seemed to encourage the trying of new things. Mr Weasley was excited when informed of Fred's adventure rescuing <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> from the <a href="/wiki/Dursley_family" title="Dursley family">Dursleys</a> and only pretended to be strict. However, he was cranky when Fred slipped <a href="/wiki/Dudley_Dursley" title="Dudley Dursley">Dudley Dursley</a> an enchanted toffee. Mr Weasley was immensely saddened by Fred's death, and was seen with his wife near his dead body.
</p>

<p>Fred first met Harry Potter in his <a href="/wiki/1991" title="1991">third year</a> at Hogwarts, when Harry was beginning his first year and needed help on the train; then Harry became friends with <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ron</a>. The twins became friendly with him when he joined the <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor_Quidditch_team" title="Gryffindor Quidditch team">Gryffindor Quidditch team</a> as their <a href="/wiki/Seeker" title="Seeker">Seeker</a>, giving them hope to win the <a href="/wiki/House_Cup" title="House Cup">House Cup</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-PS_5-16" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PS-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> After Harry Potter's <a href="/wiki/Skirmish_in_the_Underground_Chambers" title="Skirmish in the Underground Chambers">encounter with Quirinus Quirrell</a>, the twins were responsible for trying to send him a toilet seat to amuse him, but it was confiscated by Madam Pomfrey because it wasn't very hygienic.
</p><p>The <a href="/wiki/1992" title="1992">following year</a>, Fred and George cheered Harry up when many of the students believed that he might be the <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Heir of Slytherin</a>, which they scoffed at,<sup id="cite_ref-COS_15-11" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COS-15">&#91;15&#93;</a></sup> and the year after that, they gave him the <a href="/wiki/Marauder%27s_Map" title="Marauder&#39;s Map">Marauder's Map</a> to help him sneak into <a href="/wiki/Hogsmeade" title="Hogsmeade">Hogsmeade</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-POA_17-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-POA-17">&#91;17&#93;</a></sup> They also stood up for Harry when others doubted his claim that <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a> had returned or challenged his authority in <a href="/wiki/Dumbledore%27s_Army" title="Dumbledore&#39;s Army">Dumbledore's Army</a>, and assisted him in contacting <a href="/wiki/Sirius_Black" title="Sirius Black">Sirius Black</a> when he told them and <a href="/wiki/Ginevra_Weasley" title="Ginevra Weasley">Ginny</a> of his desire to speak to his godfather.<sup id="cite_ref-OOP_10-16" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOP-10">&#91;10&#93;</a></sup> Both twins risked their lives in the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_the_Seven_Potters" title="Battle of the Seven Potters">operation</a> to remove Harry from his <a href="/wiki/4_Privet_Drive" title="4 Privet Drive">relatives' home</a> in <a href="/wiki/1997" title="1997">1997</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-DH_16-11" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH-16">&#91;16&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>They seemed to regard Harry almost as another younger sibling, and were inclined both to be protective of him and teasing of him. Fred even stated that it was time for Harry to "play with the big boys" in Harry's third year when he was excluded from the Hogsmeade visit and Fred as well as George stayed behind with him. Harry was devastated by Fred's death during the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a> to the point where he felt like the world had ended, though he could not bring himself to join the Weasleys' small group around his body.
</p><p>He eventually became Fred's brother-in-law through his marriage to <a href="/wiki/Ginevra_Weasley" title="Ginevra Weasley">Ginny</a>.
</p>

<p>Fred became friendly with Hermione Granger after she became a close friend to his younger brother <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ron</a>. Although Hermione disapproved of some of the twins' antics, since she was a <a href="/wiki/Prefect" title="Prefect">Prefect</a>, she generally got on fairly well with them. They seemed to regard Hermione like a sister, due to her relationship with Ron and as she is always trying to keep them on track, despite being almost two years younger than them.<sup id="cite_ref-OOP_10-17" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOP-10">&#91;10&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>They were outraged when <a href="/wiki/Draco_Malfoy" title="Draco Malfoy">Draco Malfoy</a> called her a "<a href="/wiki/Mudblood" title="Mudblood">Mudblood</a>" in <a href="/wiki/1992" title="1992">1992</a>, to the point of attempting to physically attack him.<sup id="cite_ref-COS_15-12" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COS-15">&#91;15&#93;</a></sup> Hermione also warned Fred and his twin brother that their <a href="/wiki/Ageing_Potion" title="Ageing Potion">Ageing Potion</a> would not trick the age line protecting the <a href="/wiki/Goblet_of_Fire" title="Goblet of Fire">Goblet of Fire</a> due to the fact that Dumbledore would have thought of a foolish ageing potion in advance. They found out she was right when they were blown backwards and grew long, white beards. Fred and George also tried to get Hermione to relax, joking around with her about things such as their late uncle <a href="/wiki/Bilius" title="Bilius">Bilius</a>'s penchant for making mischief at weddings.<sup id="cite_ref-DH_16-12" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH-16">&#91;16&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Hermione also admired the twins' cleverness and creativity in their spell work, once remarking that they could do some "extraordinary magic,"<sup id="cite_ref-HBP_9-19" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup> though she did not agree with how they put it to use. Hermione was also one of the few people willing to stand up to Fred and George when she disagreed with them. For example, when she caught them testing sweets from the <a href="/wiki/Skiving_Snackbox" title="Skiving Snackbox">Skiving Snackbox</a> on first-year students with <a href="/wiki/Lee_Jordan" title="Lee Jordan">Lee Jordan</a>, she was furious, and told them off for what she considered taking advantage of innocent children. Fred and George initially scoffed at her concerns and at her ability to make them stop, but backed down when she threatened to tell their <a href="/wiki/Molly_Weasley" title="Molly Weasley">mother</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-OOP_10-18" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOP-10">&#91;10&#93;</a></sup> However, the twins did not seem to hold a grudge, as they continued to be friendly; Fred even later gave Hermione <a href="/wiki/Patented_Daydream_Charm" title="Patented Daydream Charm">material</a> from the <a href="/wiki/Weasleys%27_Wizard_Wheezes" title="Weasleys&#39; Wizard Wheezes">joke shop</a> free of charge.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP_9-20" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup> She was saddened by the death of Fred, comforting a swollen-eyed Ginny.
</p><p>Hermione would eventually become Fred's sister-in-law through her marriage to <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ron</a>.
</p>

<p>Minerva McGonagall was the <a href="/wiki/Transfiguration_(class)" title="Transfiguration (class)">Transfiguration</a> teacher during Fred and his brother, George's years at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts</a>. Although McGonagall was always well known for being strict and stern, but she was never shown punishing or even telling off Fred and George for their wild behaviour and many pranks. It was known however that they were good <a href="/wiki/Quidditch" title="Quidditch">Quidditch</a> players from a good family, and she was visibly displeased with having Fred, George, and Harry kicked off the <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor_Quidditch_team" title="Gryffindor Quidditch team">Gryffindor Quidditch team</a>.
</p><p>McGonagall often perceived the twins as troublemakers but seemed to share their dislike of <a href="/wiki/Dolores_Umbridge" title="Dolores Umbridge">Dolores Umbridge</a>, who was the <a href="/wiki/Defence_Against_the_Dark_Arts" title="Defence Against the Dark Arts">Defence Against the Dark Arts</a> teacher for a small time. McGonagall did not attempt to remove the fireworks that surfaced around the school causing great disruptions. When Fred made his legendary escape in <a href="/wiki/1996" title="1996">1996</a>, McGonagall assisted <a href="/wiki/Peeves" title="Peeves">Peeves</a> to unscrew an overhead chandelier. It was Fred's desire that such mayhem existed.
</p>

<p>Fred and George had a good relationship with many <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts</a> professors. Professor <a href="/wiki/Filius_Flitwick" title="Filius Flitwick">Filius Flitwick</a>, the <a href="/wiki/Charms_(class)" title="Charms (class)">Charms</a> professor and <a href="/wiki/Head_of_Ravenclaw_House" title="Head of Ravenclaw House">Head of Ravenclaw House</a>, seemed to like them and he was very happy with their spell work. He also thought that they were very talented wizards and supported them in rebelling against <a href="/wiki/Dolores_Umbridge" title="Dolores Umbridge">Dolores Umbridge</a> in <a href="/wiki/1995" title="1995">1995</a>. When Fred and George set off fireworks around the grounds she spent a day running around putting them out. After she had finished inspecting Flitwick's classroom, he told her that he could have put them all out easily, but was concerned he didn't have the authority to do so.
</p><p>After Easter break, the twins used one of their inventions, a <a href="/wiki/Portable_Swamp" title="Portable Swamp">Portable Swamp</a> to change a corridor and several courtyards into swamps, shortly afterwards they departed from school to go into the world of business. Flitwick was seen cheering and supporting them at their departure after they circled around Umbridge and Filch. Flitwick was asked to remove the swamp they created, which he did but left a small patch in the corner as he believed it to be really good magic.
</p>

Hagrid seemed to be one of their favourite members of the staff, as he was not too serious and very tolerant, as many other professors were viewed as. They were also among the few people who still believed that Hagrid was a great person, even after they learned that he was a <a href="/wiki/Half-giant" title="Half-giant">half-giant</a>. Hagrid thought that Fred and George were trouble-makers, but still very funny.
</p><p>He once compared them with <a href="/wiki/James_Potter_I" title="James Potter I">James Potter</a> and <a href="/wiki/Sirius_Black" title="Sirius Black">Sirius Black</a>. Furthermore, they were all among Harry Potter's closest friends. Fred and George fought in the <a href="/wiki/Second_Wizarding_War" title="Second Wizarding War">Second Wizarding War</a> along with Hagrid. However, during the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a>, he, <a href="/wiki/George_Weasley" title="George Weasley">George</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Rubeus_Hagrid" title="Rubeus Hagrid">Hagrid</a> split up. However, Fred was killed in the explosion soon afterward.
</p>

<p>Fred and George hated <a href="/wiki/Dolores_Umbridge" title="Dolores Umbridge">Dolores Umbridge</a> with a passion, who was appointed by the <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">Ministry of Magic</a> as the <a href="/wiki/Defence_Against_the_Dark_Arts" title="Defence Against the Dark Arts">Defence Against the Dark Arts</a> professor in <a href="/wiki/1995" title="1995">1995</a>. <p>In <a href="/wiki/1995" title="1995">1995</a>, Fred and George joined <a href="/wiki/Dumbledore%27s_Army" title="Dumbledore&#39;s Army">Dumbledore's Army</a>, an organisation founded and led by <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>, in order to oppose <a href="/wiki/Dolores_Umbridge" title="Dolores Umbridge">Dolores Umbridge</a> and teach <a href="/wiki/Defence_Against_the_Dark_Arts" title="Defence Against the Dark Arts">Defence Against the Dark Arts</a> to the other students. The twins knew most of them, and they had a good relationship with most members of the army, including Harry, Hermione, Ron, Ginny, Lee and Angelina.
</p><p>Some members of the army were also members of the <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor_Quidditch_team" title="Gryffindor Quidditch team">Gryffindor Quidditch team</a>, including <a href="/wiki/Alicia_Spinnet" title="Alicia Spinnet">Alicia Spinnet</a> and <a href="/wiki/Katie_Bell" title="Katie Bell">Katie Bell</a>. The girls appeared to be close with Fred, whom they possibly knew since their first year, as well as George. Both of them were very upset when Umbridge kicked Harry and the twins off the team. Fred met them again when they fought at the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a> in <a href="/wiki/1998" title="1998">1998</a>.
</p><p>Other members of the army were <a href="/wiki/Neville_Longbottom" title="Neville Longbottom">Neville Longbottom</a>, <a href="/wiki/Luna_Lovegood" title="Luna Lovegood">Luna Lovegood</a>, <a href="/wiki/Dean_Thomas" title="Dean Thomas">Dean Thomas</a>, <a href="/wiki/Seamus_Finnigan" title="Seamus Finnigan">Seamus Finnigan</a>, <a href="/wiki/Lavender_Brown" title="Lavender Brown">Lavender Brown</a>, <a href="/wiki/Padma_and_Parvati_Patil" title="Padma and Parvati Patil">Parvati and Padma Patil</a>, <a href="/wiki/Colin_and_Dennis_Creevey" title="Colin and Dennis Creevey">Colin and Dennis Creevey</a>, <a href="/wiki/Ernest_Macmillan" title="Ernest Macmillan">Ernie Macmillan</a>, <a href="/wiki/Hannah_Abbott" title="Hannah Abbott">Hannah Abbott</a>, <a href="/wiki/Justin_Finch-Fletchley" title="Justin Finch-Fletchley">Justin Finch-Fletchley</a>, <a href="/wiki/Susan_Bones" title="Susan Bones">Susan Bones</a>, <a href="/wiki/Cho_Chang" title="Cho Chang">Cho Chang</a>, <a href="/wiki/Anthony_Goldstein" title="Anthony Goldstein">Anthony Goldstein</a>, <a href="/wiki/Michael_Corner" title="Michael Corner">Michael Corner</a>, <a href="/wiki/Terry_Boot" title="Terry Boot">Terry Boot</a>, <a href="/wiki/Marietta_Edgecombe" title="Marietta Edgecombe">Marietta Edgecombe</a> and <a href="/wiki/Zacharias_Smith" title="Zacharias Smith">Zacharias Smith</a>. Fred was friendly with them, although not very close; however, he disliked Zacharias Smith, who didn't believe that Voldemort had returned, and did not fight at the Battle of Hogwarts, as did Marietta Edgecombe, who betrayed the army to Umbridge. However, the rest of them fought for Dumbledore's Army, and at least two of them, Colin Creevey and Lavender Brown were killed by Death Eaters.
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="The_Marauders">The Marauders</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Fred_Weasley?veaction=edit&amp;section=38" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: The Marauders">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Fred_Weasley?action=edit&amp;section=38" title="Edit section: The Marauders"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<div class="quote">
<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban (film)">"<i>We owe them so much.</i>"</span></dd>
<dd>&#8212;Fred's brother George on the Marauders<sup class="noprint"><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Prisoner_of_Azkaban_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban (film)">[src]</a></sup></dd>
<dd></dd></dl></div>
<figure class="thumb tleft show-info-icon" style="width: 252px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/c/cc/Marauders_1997.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20080414050923" class="image"><img alt="" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw%3D%3D" decoding="async" width="250" height="198" class="thumbimage lazyload" data-image-name="Marauders 1997.jpg" data-image-key="Marauders_1997.jpg" data-src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/c/cc/Marauders_1997.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/250?cb=20080414050923" /></a> 	<noscript><a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/c/cc/Marauders_1997.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20080414050923" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/c/cc/Marauders_1997.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/250?cb=20080414050923" decoding="async" width="250" height="198" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="Marauders 1997.jpg" data-image-key="Marauders_1997.jpg" data-src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/c/cc/Marauders_1997.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/250?cb=20080414050923" /></a></noscript> 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:Marauders_1997.jpg" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption">James Potter, Sirius Black, Remus Lupin, and Peter Pettigrew</p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure>
<p>Fred and his brother <a href="/wiki/George_Weasley" title="George Weasley">George</a> regarded the <a href="/wiki/Marauders" title="Marauders">Marauders</a> with a kind of idolatry and they believed that they owed a lot to them, as their invention, the <a href="/wiki/Marauder%27s_Map" title="Marauder&#39;s Map">Marauder's Map</a>, helped them to achieve a lot of their aims. Although they owned the map for many years, they did not refer to them as the Marauders, but instead they called them by their nicknames: <a href="/wiki/Remus_Lupin" title="Remus Lupin">Moony</a>, <a href="/wiki/Peter_Pettigrew" title="Peter Pettigrew">Wormtail</a>, <a href="/wiki/Sirius_Black" title="Sirius Black">Padfoot</a>, and <a href="/wiki/James_Potter_I" title="James Potter I">Prongs</a>.
</p><p>While they were in possession of the map, the twins did not, however, work out that two of the Marauders were two of their friends in the <a href="/wiki/Order_of_the_Phoenix" title="Order of the Phoenix">Order of the Phoenix</a>: <a href="/wiki/Remus_Lupin" title="Remus Lupin">Remus Lupin</a> and <a href="/wiki/Sirius_Black" title="Sirius Black">Sirius Black</a>. Neither were they aware that Prongs was in fact <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry</a>'s father, <a href="/wiki/James_Potter_I" title="James Potter I">James</a>, and that Wormtail was actually <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eater</a> <a href="/wiki/Peter_Pettigrew" title="Peter Pettigrew">Peter Pettigrew</a>.
</p><p>He and George were both friends of Sirius Black. Sirius was friendly with them, but he argued with the twins (for example: when he told them to wait at home while their father was in danger). Sirius was killed by <a href="/wiki/Bellatrix_Lestrange" title="Bellatrix Lestrange">Bellatrix Lestrange</a> during the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_the_Department_of_Mysteries" title="Battle of the Department of Mysteries">Battle of the Department of Mysteries</a> in <a href="/wiki/1996" title="1996">1996</a>. The twins were saddened by his death, but tried not to remind Harry about it.
</p><p>They were also friends of Remus Lupin, whom they first met in <a href="/wiki/1993" title="1993">1993</a>, when he was <a href="/wiki/Defence_Against_the_Dark_Arts" title="Defence Against the Dark Arts">Defence Against the Dark Arts</a> professor. They considered him among the best teachers of this lesson they had ever had. Lupin fought with the twins in many battles of the Second War, and was killed in the final battle, along with Fred. Their bodies were put near each other's in the <a href="/wiki/Great_Hall" title="Great Hall">Great Hall</a>.
</p><p>It is unknown if the twins ever learnt the true identities of the Marauders, although it is possible they did eventually learn the truth after Voldemort's return considering that Sirius mentioned Pettigrew's nickname Wormtail in front of them while explaining Voldemort's intentions to them without them questioning that. It is also unknown if Lupin and Sirius ever learnt that the twins both had the Marauders Map in their possession during their time in Hogwarts.
</p>

<p>Fred also became a member of the <a href="/wiki/Order_of_the_Phoenix" title="Order of the Phoenix">Order of the Phoenix</a> during the <a href="/wiki/Second_Wizarding_War" title="Second Wizarding War">Second Wizarding War</a>. The Order was an organisation led by <a href="/wiki/Albus_Dumbledore" title="Albus Dumbledore">Albus Dumbledore</a> in order to fight <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a> and his <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a>. Fred had a good relationship with most members of the order. Some notable relationships include Remus Lupin, Sirius Black, his other family members (excluding Percy who had fallen out with the other Weasleys, and Ginny who was underage) and some of the Hogwarts professors.
</p><p>Another member of the Order that they knew was <a href="/wiki/Nymphadora_Tonks" title="Nymphadora Tonks">Nymphadora Tonks</a>. Tonks and the twins were close in age, and they were friendly. They fought together in the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_the_Seven_Potters" title="Battle of the Seven Potters">Battle of the Seven Potters</a> and the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a>. Tonks was killed during the battle, too. Her body was put near Fred's, as well as Lupin's, after their death.
</p><p>The twins also knew <a href="/wiki/Alastor_Moody" title="Alastor Moody">Alastor Moody</a> and <a href="/wiki/Kingsley_Shacklebolt" title="Kingsley Shacklebolt">Kingsley Shacklebolt</a>. They appeared to have a good relationship, although they were not very close. When Moody was killed by Voldemort, everyone was sad. When the twins learned about it, they were joking with each other. They immediately stopped talking, and their expressions immediately switched to sadness. Kingsley survived the war and became Minister for Magic.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Fred_Weasley?veaction=edit&amp;section=41" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Fred_Weasley?action=edit&amp;section=41" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The name <i>Fred</i> is generally considered a short form of the name <i>Frederick</i>.<sup id="cite_ref-27" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-27">&#91;27&#93;</a></sup> <i>Alfred</i>, and <i>Wilfred</i>, along with now being a name in its own right<a target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener" class="external autonumber" href="http://webspace.webring.com/people/ge/edgarbook/names/frames.html">[1]</a>. <i>Frederick</i> is the English form of a Germanic name meaning "peaceful ruler," derived from <i>frid</i>, "peace," and <i>ric</i>, "ruler, power." It has been a very common name for rulers of the Holy Roman Empire, Germany, Austria, and Scandinavia, such as <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Frederick_the_Great" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Frederick the Great">Frederick the Great</a> of Prussia.<sup id="cite_ref-28" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-28">&#91;28&#93;</a></sup> Fred is also sometimes used as a diminutive of <i><a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Alfred_(name)" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Alfred (name)">Alfred</a></i>, derived from the Old English <i>Ælfræd</i>, meaning "elf counsel," or <i>Alfrid</i>, meaning "old peace." <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Alfred_the_Great" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Alfred the Great">Alfred the Great</a> was a ninth-century king of Wessex who battled the Danes and translated many Latin books into Old English.<sup id="cite_ref-29" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-29">&#91;29&#93;</a></sup> In Norwegian, Danish, and Swedish, <i>Fred</i> means "peace."
</p><p>There has been some suggestion that Fred and George are named after their maternal uncles, <a href="/wiki/Fabian_Prewett" title="Fabian Prewett">Fabian</a> and <a href="/wiki/Gideon_Prewett" title="Gideon Prewett">Gideon Prewett</a>, who were killed in the <a href="/wiki/First_Wizarding_War" title="First Wizarding War">First Wizarding War</a>, who may have been twins, and who share Fred and George's first initials.
</p><p>Another possible significance of the twins' names is that George III became king because of the early death of Frederick, Prince of Wales. George III was also deaf in one ear. <a href="/wiki/J._K._Rowling" title="J. K. Rowling">J. K. Rowling</a> has stated that she chose the name for liking and and finding it fitting  with the "old-fashioned" theme of the other Weasley brothers.<sup id="cite_ref-30" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-30">&#91;30&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<ul><li>Fred is portrayed by <a href="/wiki/Great_Britain" title="Great Britain">British</a> actor <a href="/wiki/James_and_Oliver_Phelps" title="James and Oliver Phelps">James Phelps</a> in all eight <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_(film_series)" title="Harry Potter (film series)">Harry Potter film adaptations</a> and so is one of the 14 characters to appear in all of the films.</li>
<li>In the films, Fred and George are nearly always seen side-by-side. For the viewer to distinguish between Fred and George, actor James Phelps(Fred) is always placed on the left side of the screen and Oliver Phelps(George) is always placed on the right side of the screen, similar to how they are called "Fred and George".</li>
<li>He and George may have been the basis for Kaoru and Hikaru Hitachiin from the shoujo manga, "Ouran High School Host Club". They are both a pair of red-haired, mischievous twins.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Daniel_Radcliffe" title="Daniel Radcliffe">Daniel Radcliffe</a> plays Fred in the "Seven Potters" scene in his capacity as a decoy Harry.</li>
<li>Fred's last words were: "You actually are joking, Perce — I don't think I've heard you joke since you were —"</li>
<li>In the games, they used to own a shop in a deserted bathroom in Hogwarts, where they sold Wizard Cards, Chocolate Frogs, Cauldron Cakes and other magical items.</li>
<li>Fred and George each earned fewer <a href="/wiki/Ordinary_Wizarding_Level" title="Ordinary Wizarding Level">O.W.L.s</a> than the rest of their brothers.</li>
<li>Fred and George were the only sons of Arthur and Molly Weasley who did not become <a href="/wiki/Prefect" title="Prefect">prefects</a> during their education at Hogwarts.</li>
<li>When <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> and his friends lost 150 <a href="/wiki/House_points" title="House points">House points</a> in 1992, he lost favour with the Gryffindor Quidditch team. While understandable, it seems odd that Fred and George turned on him.</li>
<li>In the novels, the twins are described as being "shorter and stockier" and Percy and Ron as "long and lanky," but in the films, Fred and George are taller and leaner than both Percy and Ron.</li>
<li>In her drawing of the <a href="/wiki/Weasley_family#Family_Tree" title="Weasley family">Weasley family tree</a>, <a href="/wiki/J._K._Rowling" title="J. K. Rowling">J. K. Rowling</a> put the year of Fred's death down as 1997.<sup id="cite_ref-31" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-31">&#91;31&#93;</a></sup> In canon, he actually died in May 1998.</li>
<li>His name was <a href="/wiki/Bellatrix_Lestrange" title="Bellatrix Lestrange">Bellatrix Lestrange</a>'s last word, in the variation of "Freddie." In the last film this line was cut out and instead it is George who calls Fred "Freddie" when asking if he was ready for battle. <div class="quote"></div></li></ul>
<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 2"><b>George</b>: "<i>You okay, Freddie?</i>"</span></dd>
<dd><b>Fred</b>: "<i>Yeah</i>"</dd>

George addresses his brother as 'Freddie'<sup class="noprint"><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Deathly_Hallows:_Part_2" title="Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 2">[src]</a></sup></dd></dl>

<ul><li>Despite the fact that they only earned three O.W.L.s each, in <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Goblet_of_Fire" title="Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire">Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire</a></i>, Fred mentioned that he was transfiguring a raccoon in <a href="/wiki/Transfiguration_(class)" title="Transfiguration (class)">Transfiguration</a>. In <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Order_of_the_Phoenix" title="Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix">Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix</a></i>, George said that he might be able to sell a "few Extendable ears before <a href="/wiki/Herbology" title="Herbology">Herbology</a>" and <a href="/wiki/Lee_Jordan" title="Lee Jordan">Lee Jordan</a> said that Fred and George played <a href="/wiki/Exploding_Snap" title="Exploding Snap">Exploding Snap</a> during <a href="/wiki/Defence_Against_the_Dark_Arts" title="Defence Against the Dark Arts">Defence Against the Dark Arts</a>, and that they had an inspected <a href="/wiki/Charms_(class)" title="Charms (class)">Charms</a> lesson.</li>
<li>Fittingly, Fred and George are born on April Fools' Day.</li>
<li>Fred and George might be named in honour of their mother's brothers Fabian and Gideon, as Fred and Fabian begin with the letter "F", and George and Gideon begin with the letter "G".</li>
<li>The only times Fred or George have been injured were when they were apart. They were split up at the time of Fred's death and when George lost his ear.</li>
<li>In <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Deathly_Hallows:_Part_2" title="Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 2">Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 2</a></i>, it appears as though Fred is killed by a <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eater</a> as the last moment seen of Fred is him scrambling on the floor with a Death Eater standing over him just as both start to cast a spell. However, due to the brevity of the scene, it escapes most people that the twin in this scene is George, not Fred. The twin in this scene is wearing a purple jacket, which is what George wears during the Battle of Hogwarts; Fred's jacket is green. Furthermore, the ear is also a clue, as it is mangled and has no earlobe. Fred's death is not seen on-screen much to the dismay of fans. It is not on the DVD version, or in the deleted scenes.</li>
<li>The only time Fred saw George as old men, was when they put their names into the <a href="/wiki/Goblet_of_Fire" title="Goblet of Fire">Goblet of Fire</a>.</li>
<li>Oliver Phelps, who plays George, stated that it was quite upsetting for him to watch his real-life twin brother, James, act as if he were dead as Fred in Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 2.</li>
<li>James Phelps admitted that when he was playing a dead Fred, he was actually asleep and the entire cast and crew went off to lunch without him, leaving him sleeping in the Great Hall set.</li></ul>
<h3><span id="Author's_comments"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="Author.27s_comments">Author's comments</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Fred_Weasley?veaction=edit&amp;section=43" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Author&#039;s comments">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Fred_Weasley?action=edit&amp;section=43" title="Edit section: Author&#039;s comments"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p><a href="/wiki/J._K._Rowling" title="J. K. Rowling">J. K. Rowling</a> has commented that she always knew Fred was going to be killed in the series, though she guessed that readers would have expected George to die first, since he was the "gentler" of the two: <i>"Fred is normally the funnier but also the crueller of the two. So they might have thought that George would be the more vulnerable one and, therefore, the one to die... Either one of them would have been terrible to kill. It was awful killing Fred. I hated that."</i><sup id="cite_ref-msnbc_21-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-msnbc-21">&#91;21&#93;</a></sup> She also described Fred and George as <i>"the risk-takers in the family... who just take a totally different life path and were prepared to risk everything"</i>.<sup id="cite_ref-32" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-32">&#91;32&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Rowling has also stated that Fred and George are among her favourite characters.<sup id="cite_ref-33" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-33">&#91;33&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
