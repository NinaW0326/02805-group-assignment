<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_1910s_Gryffindor_Girl_(I)?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_1910s_Gryffindor_Girl_(I)?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Sometime in the <a href="/wiki/1910s" title="1910s">1910s</a>, whilst attending Hogwarts, she and her <a href="/wiki/Unidentified_Gryffindor_girl_2_(1910%27s)" class="mw-redirect" title="Unidentified Gryffindor girl 2 (1910&#39;s)">friend</a> spitefully gossiped about <a href="/wiki/Leta_Lestrange" title="Leta Lestrange">Leta Lestrange</a> about being an unwanted child and for the <a href="/wiki/Death" title="Death">death</a> of her <a href="/wiki/Corvus_Lestrange_(V)" title="Corvus Lestrange (V)">half-brother</a>, before her friend hit with the <a href="/wiki/Dark_charm" title="Dark charm">dark charm</a> <i><a href="/wiki/Oscausi" title="Oscausi">Oscausi</a></i> by Leta out of retaliation, sealing her mouth shut.<sup id="cite_ref-COGS_2-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COGS-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
