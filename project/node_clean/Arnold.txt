<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Arnold?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Arnold?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Arnold was purchased from Ginny's brothers <a href="/wiki/Fred_and_George_Weasley" title="Fred and George Weasley">Fred and George Weasley</a> at <a href="/wiki/Weasley%27s_Wizard_Wheezes" class="mw-redirect" title="Weasley&#39;s Wizard Wheezes">their shop</a> in August of <a href="/wiki/1996" title="1996">1996</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP6_2-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP6-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> Arnold was transported in a cage during the car ride from <a href="/wiki/The_Burrow" title="The Burrow">The Burrow</a> to <a href="/wiki/King%27s_Cross_Station" title="King&#39;s Cross Station">King's Cross Station</a> on <a href="/wiki/1_September" title="1 September">1 September</a> 1996.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP7_1-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP7-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>In the <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor_Tower" title="Gryffindor Tower">Gryffindor common room</a> in <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts</a> he was seen playing with Ginny<sup id="cite_ref-HBP11_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP11-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> or riding her shoulder.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP14_4-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP14-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> It is likely that he was taken with the Weasleys when the family went into hiding in the spring of <a href="/wiki/1998" title="1998">1998</a>.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Arnold?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Arnold?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Ginny's father, <a href="/wiki/Arthur_Weasley" title="Arthur Weasley">Arthur Weasley</a>, was mistakenly called "Arnold Weasley" in a <a href="/wiki/FURTHER_MISTAKES_AT_THE_MINISTRY_OF_MAGIC" title="FURTHER MISTAKES AT THE MINISTRY OF MAGIC">1994 <i>Daily Prophet</i> article</a> written by <a href="/wiki/Rita_Skeeter" title="Rita Skeeter">Rita Skeeter</a>. "Arnold" is also an anagram of "Ronald", a possible reference to <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ginny's older brother</a>.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Arnold?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Arnold?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>In the novel he is purple, but in the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Half-Blood_Prince_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince (film)">film adaptation</a> of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Half-Blood_Prince" title="Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince">Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince</a></i>, he is pink.</li>
<li>In <i><a href="/wiki/LEGO_Harry_Potter:_Years_5-7" title="LEGO Harry Potter: Years 5-7">LEGO Harry Potter: Years 5-7</a></i> Ginny has Arnold already in <a href="/wiki/1995" title="1995">1995</a> and the player can use him in the levels.</li>
<li>According to the Hogwarts <a href="/wiki/Shopping_List" title="Shopping List">supply list</a>, students are allowed to take an <a href="/wiki/Owl" title="Owl">owl</a>, a <a href="/wiki/Cat" title="Cat">cat</a>, or a <a href="/wiki/Toad" title="Toad">toad</a> to Hogwarts. However, it seems that Ginny was allowed to take her pygmy puff like her <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">brother</a> was able to bring his <a href="/wiki/Rat" title="Rat">rat</a>.</li>
