<b>Daphne Greengrass</b> (b. <a href="/wiki/1979" title="1979">1979</a>/<a href="/wiki/1980" title="1980">1980</a>)<sup id="cite_ref-birth_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-birth-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> was a <a href="/wiki/Pure-blood" title="Pure-blood">pure-blood</a> <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch</a> and a member of the <a href="/wiki/Greengrass_family" title="Greengrass family">Greengrass family</a>, an old <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizarding</a> family and one of the <a href="/wiki/Sacred_Twenty-Eight" title="Sacred Twenty-Eight">Sacred Twenty-Eight</a><i>.</i> She began attending <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> in <a href="/wiki/1991" title="1991">1991</a> and was <a href="/wiki/Sorting_ceremony" title="Sorting ceremony">sorted</a> into <a href="/wiki/Slytherin" title="Slytherin">Slytherin House</a>. She was in the same year as the famous <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-birth_1-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-birth-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Daphne_Greengrass?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Daphne_Greengrass?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_life">Early life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Daphne_Greengrass?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Early life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Daphne_Greengrass?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Early life"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Daphne Greengrass was born to <a href="/wiki/Pure-blood" title="Pure-blood">pure-blood</a> <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizarding</a><sup id="cite_ref-blood-dh_2-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-blood-dh-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-hpm_3-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-hpm-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> parents in <a href="/wiki/1979" title="1979">1979</a> or <a href="/wiki/1980" title="1980">1980</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-birth_1-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-birth-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> She had at least one sibling, a sister, <a href="/wiki/Astoria_Malfoy" title="Astoria Malfoy">Astoria</a>, who was two years younger than her.<sup id="cite_ref-leaky131_6-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-leaky131-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>It is unknown if the Greengrass sisters were raised to believe in the ideals of <a href="/wiki/Pure-blood_supremacy" title="Pure-blood supremacy">pure-blood supremacy</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup> Their <a href="/wiki/Greengrass_family" title="Greengrass family">family</a> was one of the oldest, "truest" pure-blood families in <a href="/wiki/Great_Britain" title="Great Britain">Britain</a>, and were counted among the <a href="/wiki/Sacred_Twenty-Eight" title="Sacred Twenty-Eight">Sacred Twenty-Eight</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-pure_blood_8-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-pure_blood-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup> One of the sisters' distant <a href="/wiki/Astoria_Malfoy%27s_ancestor" title="Astoria Malfoy&#39;s ancestor">ancestors</a> was <a href="/wiki/Curse" title="Curse">cursed</a> with a <a href="/wiki/Blood_malediction" title="Blood malediction">blood malediction</a>, which resurfaced in Astoria.<sup id="cite_ref-cc-4-4_9-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-cc-4-4-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup> It is unknown whether Daphne suffered from the same condition as her younger sister.
</p>

<p>On <a href="/wiki/1_September" title="1 September">1 September</a> <a href="/wiki/1991" title="1991">1991</a>, Daphne began her education at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> and was <a href="/wiki/Sorting_ceremony" title="Sorting ceremony">sorted</a> into <a href="/wiki/Slytherin" title="Slytherin">Slytherin</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-hpm_3-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-hpm-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-10thingsaboutHarry_5-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-10thingsaboutHarry-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>During her school years, it is unknown if she was a member of <a href="/wiki/Pansy_Parkinson" title="Pansy Parkinson">Pansy Parkinson</a>'s "<a href="/wiki/Pansy_Parkinson%27s_gang" title="Pansy Parkinson&#39;s gang">gang</a>" of Slytherin girls.<sup id="cite_ref-10thingsaboutHarry_5-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-10thingsaboutHarry-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>She took her <a href="/wiki/Ordinary_Wizarding_Level" title="Ordinary Wizarding Level">O.W.L.s</a> in <a href="/wiki/1996" title="1996">1996</a> at the same time as <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione Granger</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-ootp31_10-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-ootp31-10">&#91;10&#93;</a></sup> The <a href="/wiki/1996%E2%80%931997_school_year" title="1996–1997 school year">following school year</a>, she was not one of the selected students who advanced to <a href="/wiki/Nastily_Exhausting_Wizarding_Test" title="Nastily Exhausting Wizarding Test">N.E.W.T.</a>-level <a href="/wiki/Potions_(class)" title="Potions (class)">Potions</a>, and thus she evidently failed to achieve the required grade or did not find continuing on to N.E.W.T. Potions worthwhile.
</p><p>It is unknown if she was present during the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a> on <a href="/wiki/2_May" title="2 May">2 May</a> <a href="/wiki/1998" title="1998">1998</a> and led from the <a href="/wiki/Great_Hall" title="Great Hall">Great Hall</a> by <a href="/wiki/Argus_Filch" title="Argus Filch">Filch</a>, along with her fellow Slytherins.
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Later_life">Later life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Daphne_Greengrass?veaction=edit&amp;section=4" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Later life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Daphne_Greengrass?action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Later life"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Daphne's younger sister Astoria eventually married <a href="/wiki/Draco_Malfoy" title="Draco Malfoy">Draco Malfoy</a>, a fellow Slytherin in the same year as Daphne at Hogwarts.<sup id="cite_ref-10thingsaboutHarry_5-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-10thingsaboutHarry-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> She had a nephew, <a href="/wiki/Scorpius_Malfoy" title="Scorpius Malfoy">Scorpius</a>, by this marriage.<sup id="cite_ref-epilogue_11-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-epilogue-11">&#91;11&#93;</a></sup> It is currently unknown whether Daphne herself ever married or had any children of her own.
</p><p>In the summer of <a href="/wiki/2019" title="2019">2019</a>, Daphne lost Astoria to the blood curse.<sup id="cite_ref-cc-4-4_9-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-cc-4-4-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-cc-1-4_12-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-cc-1-4-12">&#91;12&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Daphne_Greengrass?veaction=edit&amp;section=5" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Daphne_Greengrass?action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The name Daphne means "Laurel" in Greek. It is derived from the nymph <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Daphne" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Daphne">Daphne</a> in Greek mythology, who was pursued by the god Apollo. She prayed to her father, the river god Peneus, to change her body in order to escape the love-struck Apollo and Peneus then transformed her into the laurel tree, which Apollo then claimed as one of his sacred symbols. In Ancient Greece, a wreath of laurels was given as a prize and considered a symbol of prosperity, fame, and victory.<sup id="cite_ref-13" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-13">&#91;13&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-14" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-14">&#91;14&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p><a target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener" class="external text" href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Daphne_(plant)">Daphne</a> is also the name of a plant noted for its fragrant flower, but for also being wholly <a href="/wiki/Poison" title="Poison">poisonous</a>.
</p>
