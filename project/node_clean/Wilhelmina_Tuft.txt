<b>Minister Wilhelmina Tuft</b> was <a href="/wiki/Minister_for_Magic" title="Minister for Magic">Minister for Magic</a> from <a href="/wiki/1948" title="1948">1948</a> to <a href="/wiki/1959" title="1959">1959</a>, being elected at least twice. Tuft was a cheery witch who presided over a period of welcome peace and prosperity. She died in office after discovering, too late, her allergy to <a href="/wiki/Alihotsy" title="Alihotsy">Alihotsy</a>-flavoured fudge, and was succeeded by her son <a href="/wiki/Ignatius_Tuft" title="Ignatius Tuft">Ignatius Tuft</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
