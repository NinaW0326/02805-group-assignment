<b>Charles Epanel</b> (<a href="/wiki/1936" title="1936">1936</a>-<a href="/wiki/1972" title="1972">1972</a>) was a <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggle</a> who lived in <a href="/wiki/Little_Hangleton" title="Little Hangleton">Little Hangleton</a>. He died for an unknown reason at the age of 36. He was buried in the <a href="/wiki/Little_Hangleton_graveyard" title="Little Hangleton graveyard">Little Hangleton graveyard</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-GOFG_1-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOFG-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
