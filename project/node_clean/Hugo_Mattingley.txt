<b>Hugo Mattingley</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> and <a href="/wiki/Ravenclaw" title="Ravenclaw">Ravenclaw</a> student at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> in the <a href="/wiki/1990s" title="1990s">1990s</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-POAG_4-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-POAG-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> He was usually seen spending time in the <a href="/wiki/Middle_Courtyard" title="Middle Courtyard">Transfiguration Courtyard</a> during the <a href="/wiki/1996%E2%80%931997_school_year" title="1996–1997 school year">1996–1997 school year</a>. He was a fan of <a href="/wiki/Gilderoy_Lockhart" title="Gilderoy Lockhart">Gilderoy Lockhart</a>. Unlike most people who thought that the <a href="/wiki/Potions_Master" title="Potions Master">Potions Master</a> <a href="/wiki/Horace_Slughorn" title="Horace Slughorn">Horace Slughorn</a> was a stakeholder, Trevor thought that he was a good teacher.<sup id="cite_ref-HBPG_3-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBPG-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
