<p><b>Quintia McQuoid</b> (died c. <a href="/wiki/1407" class="mw-redirect" title="1407">1407</a>) was a notable <a href="/wiki/Middle_Ages" title="Middle Ages">Medieval</a> <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch</a> and <a href="/wiki/Potioneer" title="Potioneer">Potioneer</a>, active in, at least, the early <a href="/wiki/15th_century" title="15th century">15th century</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-WBoP_1-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-WBoP-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Quintia_McQuoid?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Quintia_McQuoid?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>She died at a young age, after consuming a <a href="/wiki/Cure_for_ague" title="Cure for ague">cure for ague</a> that had been improperly brewed. To honour her memory, the <a href="/wiki/Wizarding_Schools_Potions_Championship" title="Wizarding Schools Potions Championship">Wizarding Schools Potions Championship</a> was first held, in an <a href="/wiki/Enchanted_garden_(Book_of_Potions)" title="Enchanted garden (Book of Potions)">enchanted garden</a>, in <a href="/wiki/1407" class="mw-redirect" title="1407">1407</a>, and has been held every seven years since, gathering <a href="/wiki/Potions_(class)" title="Potions (class)">Potions</a> students from schools like <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts</a>, <a href="/wiki/Mahoutokoro_School_of_Magic" title="Mahoutokoro School of Magic">Mahoutokoro</a>, <a href="/wiki/Uagadou_School_of_Magic" title="Uagadou School of Magic">Uagadou</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Koldovstoretz_school" title="Koldovstoretz school">Koldovstoretz</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-WBoP_1-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-WBoP-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
