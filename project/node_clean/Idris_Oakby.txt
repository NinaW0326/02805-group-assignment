<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Idris_Oakby?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Idris_Oakby?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_Life">Early Life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Idris_Oakby?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Early Life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Idris_Oakby?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Early Life"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Idris Oakby was born in <a href="/wiki/1872" class="mw-redirect" title="1872">1872</a> to parents of unknown <a href="/wiki/Blood_status" title="Blood status">blood status</a>, in an unknown country, therefore, nothing is known of her upbringing or education. In her adult life, she set up a charitable organisation called <a href="/wiki/Society_for_the_Support_of_Squibs" title="Society for the Support of Squibs">Society for the Support of Squibs</a> which supported Squibs and helped them live in the <a href="/wiki/Wizarding_world" title="Wizarding world">Wizarding world</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-JKRS_1-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-JKRS-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> She may have been frowned upon by other wizards and witches for her charitable work, considering how poorly Squibs are generally viewed in the magical world.
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Death_and_Post-Mortem">Death and Post-Mortem</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Idris_Oakby?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Death and Post-Mortem">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Idris_Oakby?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Death and Post-Mortem"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Oakby <a href="/wiki/Death" title="Death">died</a> in <a href="/wiki/1985" title="1985">1985</a> at the age of 113. She was awarded '<a href="/wiki/Wizard_of_the_Month" title="Wizard of the Month">Wizard of the Month</a>' in <a href="/wiki/2007" title="2007">2007</a>, for recognition for her admirable philanthropic efforts she performed in her lifetime.<sup id="cite_ref-JKRS_1-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-JKRS-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Idris_Oakby?veaction=edit&amp;section=4" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Idris_Oakby?action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The name "Idris" means "ardent lord" from Welsh <i>udd</i>, "lord, prince" combined with <i>ris</i>, "ardent", "enthusiastic", "impulsive".
</p>
