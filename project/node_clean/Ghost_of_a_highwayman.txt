This <b><a href="/wiki/Ghost" title="Ghost">ghost</a> of a highwayman</b> resided at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a>. In <a href="/wiki/1993" title="1993">1993</a>, he jumped through a window in the <a href="/wiki/Entrance_Hall" title="Entrance Hall">Entrance Hall</a> on his ghost horse, sending ghostly debris flying without doing any actual damage. He then rode into the <a href="/wiki/Great_Hall" title="Great Hall">Great Hall</a>, holding a severed head in the air by its hair, a <a href="/wiki/Unidentified_ghost_(VII)" title="Unidentified ghost (VII)">ghost knight</a> mounted on another <a href="/wiki/Unidentified_ghost_horse_(I)" title="Unidentified ghost horse (I)">ghost horse</a> was chasing after him.<sup id="cite_ref-POAF_1-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-POAF-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Ghost_of_a_highwayman?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Ghost_of_a_highwayman?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>Connecting the title of a highwayman and that he stole the head of another ghost, this could be the ghost of <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Dick_Turpin" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Dick Turpin">Richard "Dick" Turpin</a>, an English man known as a burglar, horse thief and robber. If that is true <a href="/wiki/Unidentified_ghost_horse_(I)" title="Unidentified ghost horse (I)">the horse</a> he is riding could be Black Bess, Turpin's black horse.</li></ul>
