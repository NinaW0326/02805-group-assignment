<div class="quote">
<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire">"<i>There was Antonin Dolohov. I — I saw him torture countless <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggles</a> and — and non-supporters of the Dark Lord.</i>"</span></dd>
<dd>&#8212;Dolohov's sadistic nature<sup class="noprint"><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Goblet_of_Fire" title="Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire">[src]</a></sup></dd>
<dd></dd></dl></div>
<p><b>Antonin Dolohov</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Dark_Wizard" title="Dark Wizard">Dark Wizard</a> and one of the <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a> of <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a>. Dolohov was a powerful <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a>, and among all the Death Eaters, only <a href="/wiki/Bellatrix_Lestrange" title="Bellatrix Lestrange">Bellatrix Lestrange</a> and <a href="/wiki/Severus_Snape" title="Severus Snape">Severus Snape</a> could equal him in magical ability. He fought in the <a href="/wiki/First_Wizarding_War" title="First Wizarding War">First Wizarding War</a>, during which he tortured many <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggles</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizards and witches</a> who were not supporters of the Dark Lord. Along with four other Death Eaters, he participated in the murders of <a href="/wiki/Fabian_Prewett" title="Fabian Prewett">Fabian</a> and <a href="/wiki/Gideon_Prewett" title="Gideon Prewett">Gideon Prewett</a>. Dolohov was caught, convicted of these crimes, and sentenced to life imprisonment in <a href="/wiki/Azkaban" title="Azkaban">Azkaban</a>.
</p><p>He escaped from prison after Voldemort's <a href="/wiki/Return_of_Lord_Voldemort" title="Return of Lord Voldemort">return</a> during the <a href="/wiki/Azkaban_escapes#1996" title="Azkaban escapes">mass break-out</a> of <a href="/wiki/1996" title="1996">1996</a>, and participated in the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_the_Department_of_Mysteries" title="Battle of the Department of Mysteries">Battle of the Department of Mysteries</a>, where he was re-captured and incarcerated. By the summer of <a href="/wiki/1997" title="1997">1997</a>, Dolohov was at large again. He and <a href="/wiki/Thorfinn_Rowle" title="Thorfinn Rowle">Thorfinn Rowle</a> attacked <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>, <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione Granger</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ron Weasley</a> at <a href="/wiki/Tottenham_Court_Road" title="Tottenham Court Road">Tottenham Court Road</a>, but they were defeated and his memory was wiped. In <a href="/wiki/1998" title="1998">1998</a>, Dolohov fought in the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a>, during which he murdered <a href="/wiki/Remus_Lupin" title="Remus Lupin">Remus Lupin</a> and possibly some others. At the climax of the battle, he engaged <a href="/wiki/Filius_Flitwick" title="Filius Flitwick">Filius Flitwick</a> in a duel and was defeated.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Antonin_Dolohov?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Antonin_Dolohov?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span id="First_Wizarding_War_(1970s-1981)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="First_Wizarding_War_.281970s-1981.29">First Wizarding War <span class="template-c">(1970s-1981)</span></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Antonin_Dolohov?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: First Wizarding War (1970s-1981)">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Antonin_Dolohov?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: First Wizarding War (1970s-1981)"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>In <a href="/wiki/1970" title="1970">1970</a>, Dolohov's master began open hostilities against the rest of the <a href="/wiki/Wizarding_world" title="Wizarding world">wizarding world</a>, beginning the <a href="/wiki/First_Wizarding_War" title="First Wizarding War">First Wizarding War</a>.
</p><p>According to <a href="/wiki/Igor_Karkaroff" title="Igor Karkaroff">Igor Karkaroff</a>, Dolohov was among the most important Death Eaters and tortured many <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggles</a> and non-supporters of <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a> during the <a href="/wiki/First_Wizarding_War" title="First Wizarding War">First Wizarding War</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-GF30_7-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GF30-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup> He murdered <a href="/wiki/Fabian_Prewett" title="Fabian Prewett">Fabian</a> and <a href="/wiki/Gideon_Prewett" title="Gideon Prewett">Gideon Prewett</a>, the brothers of <a href="/wiki/Molly_Weasley" title="Molly Weasley">Molly Weasley</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-OP25_8-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OP25-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup> He was not the only participant in the act, though, since "<a href="/wiki/Alastor_Moody" title="Alastor Moody">Mad-Eye" Moody</a> informed <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> years later that it took five Death Eaters to kill the two wizards,<sup id="cite_ref-OP9_9-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OP9-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup> whose murders were described as "brutal."<sup id="cite_ref-OP25_8-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OP25-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup> Karkaroff also stated that Dolohov was among Voldemort's most important followers.<sup id="cite_ref-GF30_7-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GF30-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<p>In the year before the fall of Voldemort on <a href="/wiki/31_October" title="31 October">31 October</a> <a href="/wiki/1981" title="1981">1981</a>, Dolohov was captured and sent to <a href="/wiki/Azkaban" title="Azkaban">Azkaban</a> prison for his crimes.<sup id="cite_ref-10" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-10">&#91;10&#93;</a></sup> His name was the first one that <a href="/wiki/Igor_Karkaroff" title="Igor Karkaroff">Igor Karkaroff</a> revealed to the <a href="/wiki/Council_of_Magical_Law" title="Council of Magical Law">Council of Magical Law</a> in an attempt to get himself freed from Azkaban, only to be informed that Dolohov had been captured around the same time as him.<sup id="cite_ref-GF30_7-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GF30-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup> Dolohov then spent the next 14 years in his maximum security cell surrounded by Dementors. His cell was close to that of Bellatrix Lestrange's.
</p>
<h3><span id="Second_Wizarding_War_(1995-1998)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="Second_Wizarding_War_.281995-1998.29">Second Wizarding War <span class="template-c">(1995-1998)</span></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Antonin_Dolohov?veaction=edit&amp;section=5" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Second Wizarding War (1995-1998)">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Antonin_Dolohov?action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Second Wizarding War (1995-1998)"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Early in <a href="/wiki/1996" title="1996">1996</a>, Dolohov, along with the <a href="/wiki/Lestrange_family" title="Lestrange family">Lestranges</a> and several other <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eater</a> convicts, <a href="/wiki/Azkaban_escapes#1996" title="Azkaban escapes">escaped Azkaban</a> and rejoined Voldemort. It was speculated in the <i><a href="/wiki/Daily_Prophet" title="Daily Prophet">Daily Prophet</a></i> that the escape was aided by <a href="/wiki/Sirius_Black" title="Sirius Black">Sirius Black</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-OP25_8-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OP25-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup> but that was refuted in the aftermath of the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_the_Department_of_Mysteries" title="Battle of the Department of Mysteries">Battle of the Department of Mysteries</a>.
</p>

<p>On <a href="/wiki/18_June" title="18 June">18 June</a> <a href="/wiki/1996" title="1996">1996</a> <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a> lured Harry Potter to the <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">Ministry of Magic</a> precipitating the Battle of the Department of Mysteries. During the battle, Dolohov was paired with <a href="/wiki/Jugson" title="Jugson">Jugson</a> as the <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a> searched for the five students who had come to the Ministry with Harry Potter. They tracked <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>, <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione Granger</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Neville_Longbottom" title="Neville Longbottom">Neville Longbottom</a> to a room off the <a href="/wiki/Time_Room" title="Time Room">Time Room</a>, hitting the three of them with <a href="/wiki/Impediment_Jinx" title="Impediment Jinx">Impediment Jinxes</a>. When Dolohov began to alert the other Death Eaters as to their location, Hermione used <a href="/wiki/Silencing_Charm" title="Silencing Charm">Silencing Charm</a> on him to prevent it. He retaliated with an <a href="/wiki/Antonin_Dolohov%27s_curse" title="Antonin Dolohov&#39;s curse">unnamed curse</a> which, despite being weaker than normal, because it was cast <a href="/wiki/Nonverbal_spell" title="Nonverbal spell">non-verbally</a>, severely injured Hermione. With a kick, Dolohov broke Neville's wand and nose and then cornered Harry, wordlessly threatening him. However, he was momentarily distracted, giving Harry the chance to <a href="/wiki/Full_Body-Bind_Curse" title="Full Body-Bind Curse">Body-Bind</a> him.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP35_11-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP35-11">&#91;11&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Before several <a href="/wiki/Order_of_the_Phoenix" title="Order of the Phoenix">Order of the Phoenix</a> members arrived, Dolohov recovered, and he was able to defeat <a href="/wiki/Alastor_Moody" title="Alastor Moody">Alastor Moody</a> in a duel. He placed <a href="/wiki/Neville_Longbottom" title="Neville Longbottom">Neville Longbottom</a> under <a href="/wiki/Dancing_Feet_Spell" title="Dancing Feet Spell">Dancing Feet Spell</a> and attacked Harry with the same spell he used on Hermione, but most of the harm was blocked by Harry's <a href="/wiki/Shield_Charm" title="Shield Charm">Shield Charm</a>. Then he tried used <a href="/wiki/Summoning_Charm" title="Summoning Charm">Summoning Charm</a> on the <a href="/wiki/Prophecy" title="Prophecy">prophecy</a> in Harry's hand, but he was pushed by <a href="/wiki/Sirius_Black" title="Sirius Black">Sirius Black</a>. He duelled Black where he seemed to get the upper hand but was again incapacitated by Harry with Body-Bind before he could hit Sirius with his signature spell.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP35_11-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP35-11">&#91;11&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Presumably, Dolohov was captured in the aftermath of the battle by <a href="/wiki/Auror" title="Auror">Aurors</a> and returned to <a href="/wiki/Azkaban" title="Azkaban">Azkaban</a>.
</p>

<p>Dolohov was described as having a "<i>long, pale, twisted face</i>" and as "burly," although not as large as the "huge" <a href="/wiki/Thorfinn_Rowle" title="Thorfinn Rowle">Thorfinn Rowle</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-DH9_13-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH9-13">&#91;13&#93;</a></sup> He had also dark hair.<sup id="cite_ref-hair_2-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-hair-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> As a Death Eater, he bore the <a href="/wiki/Dark_Mark" title="Dark Mark">Dark Mark</a> on his left inner forearm.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Personality_and_traits">Personality and traits</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Antonin_Dolohov?veaction=edit&amp;section=11" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Personality and traits">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Antonin_Dolohov?action=edit&amp;section=11" title="Edit section: Personality and traits"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Dolohov was extremely loyal to <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a> and was a merciless Death Eater willing to commit various atrocities without hesitation or regret. He showed no remorse about wounding sixteen-year-old Hermione Granger; in fact, he was grinning about it as he tried to persuade Harry Potter to hand over the prophecy they were fighting over. Dolohov was very proud when he defeated <a href="/wiki/Alastor_Moody" title="Alastor Moody">Alastor Moody</a> in a duel, as Moody was a highly skilled wizard.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP35_11-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP35-11">&#91;11&#93;</a></sup> Dolohov also displayed a highly arrogant and self-confident attitude and had developed a habit of engaging more than one opponent at a time or challenging powerful wizards such as the Prewett brothers, Moody, <a href="/wiki/Sirius_Black" title="Sirius Black">Sirius Black</a>, <a href="/wiki/Remus_Lupin" title="Remus Lupin">Remus Lupin</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Filius_Flitwick" title="Filius Flitwick">Filius Flitwick</a>.
</p><p>Dolohov was on friendly terms with his fellow Death Eaters, as he seemed to consider <a href="/wiki/Corban_Yaxley" title="Corban Yaxley">Yaxley</a> and <a href="/wiki/Thorfinn_Rowle" title="Thorfinn Rowle">Thorfinn Rowle</a> as close friends. When Voldemort spoke of how <a href="/wiki/Charity_Burbage" title="Charity Burbage">Charity Burbage</a> supported <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggle</a> rights and would permit breeding with them, Dolohov smiled in a confused fashion as if he literally thought that the idea of doing such a thing was disgusting, showing that he, like other Death Eaters, hated Muggles, <a href="/wiki/Muggle-born" title="Muggle-born">Muggle-borns</a> and most likely people who were <a href="/wiki/Half-blood" title="Half-blood">half-bloods</a> and <a href="/wiki/Blood_traitor" title="Blood traitor">blood traitors</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-DH1_12-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH1-12">&#91;12&#93;</a></sup> Ironically, however, his master was in fact half-blood. It is not mentioned if Dolohov knew or not about Voldemort`s true <a href="/wiki/Blood_status" title="Blood status">blood status</a>.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Magical_abilities_and_skills">Magical abilities and skills</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Antonin_Dolohov?veaction=edit&amp;section=12" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Antonin_Dolohov?action=edit&amp;section=12" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>

<p>Dolohov was a highly powerful and dangerous <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eater</a>. His immense <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magical</a> skills allowed him to go up against and defeat many powerful wizards and witches, including many <a href="/wiki/Auror" title="Auror">Aurors</a> and <a href="/wiki/Order_of_the_Phoenix" title="Order of the Phoenix">Order of the Phoenix</a> members. It is likely that he was one of the most powerful Death Eaters.
</p>

<ul><li><b><a href="/wiki/Duelling" title="Duelling">Duelling</a></b>: Antonin Dolohov was an extremely skilful duellist, highly proficient in <a href="/wiki/Martial_magic" title="Martial magic">martial magic</a> and was one of the most dangerous and feared <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a>. He participated in the murders of <a href="/wiki/Gideon_Prewett" title="Gideon Prewett">Gideon</a> and <a href="/wiki/Fabian_Prewett" title="Fabian Prewett">Fabian Prewett</a>, both highly accomplished wizards, during the <a href="/wiki/First_Wizarding_War" title="First Wizarding War">First Wizarding War</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-OP25_8-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OP25-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup> During the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_the_Department_of_Mysteries" title="Battle of the Department of Mysteries">Battle of the Department of Mysteries</a>, he duelled <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>, <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione Granger</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Neville_Longbottom" title="Neville Longbottom">Neville Longbottom</a> single-handedly, despite a <a href="/wiki/Silencing_Charm" title="Silencing Charm">Silencing Charm</a> forcing him to use all <a href="/wiki/Nonverbal_spell" title="Nonverbal spell">nonverbal magic</a> to do so. He incapacitated Hermione and broke Neville's nose, and Harry only managed to defeat him because he was momentarily distracted. Shortly afterwards, he defeated and injured <a href="/wiki/Alastor_Moody" title="Alastor Moody">Alastor "Mad-Eye" Moody</a>, widely considered to be one of the best <a href="/wiki/Auror" title="Auror">Aurors</a> in recent times (and still highly respected even though he had retired), in a <a href="/wiki/Duelling" title="Duelling">duel</a>, although Moody had grown rusty due to his retirement. He then went on to duel <a href="/wiki/Sirius_Black" title="Sirius Black">Sirius Black</a>, with Sirius being unable to defeat Dolohov until Harry knocked him out from behind.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP35_11-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP35-11">&#91;11&#93;</a></sup> When he and fellow Death Eater <a href="/wiki/Thorfinn_Rowle" title="Thorfinn Rowle">Thorfinn Rowle</a> ambushed Harry, Ron and Hermione in 1997, Dolohov lasted longer than his partner, defeating Harry and Ron in the process, and was only defeated by Hermione Granger due to a sneak attack.<sup id="cite_ref-DH9_13-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH9-13">&#91;13&#93;</a></sup> In the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a>, Dolohov duelled and killed <a href="/wiki/Remus_Lupin" title="Remus Lupin">Remus Lupin</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-JKR_16-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-JKR-16">&#91;16&#93;</a></sup> a powerful wizard who was an expert in <a href="/wiki/Defence_Against_the_Dark_Arts" title="Defence Against the Dark Arts">Defence Against the Dark Arts</a> and capable of <a href="/wiki/Wandless_magic" title="Wandless magic">wandless magic</a>, although it must be noted that Remus was not at the height of his duelling skills as he had spent two months not duelling due to having to constantly watch over his pregnant wife and thus his reflexes had been dulled, which contributed to Dolohov's ability to defeat and kill him. However, although he put up a good fight, Dolohov proved to be unable to defeat the former duelling champion <a href="/wiki/Filius_Flitwick" title="Filius Flitwick">Filius Flitwick</a> and was defeated and even possibly killed by the superior duellist.<sup id="cite_ref-DH36_19-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH36-19">&#91;19&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Dark_Arts" title="Dark Arts">Dark Arts</a></b>: Being a sadistic Death Eater, Dolohov was highly proficient and talented in dark magic and the casting of <a href="/wiki/Dark_charm" title="Dark charm">dark charms</a>, making him a formidable <a href="/wiki/Dark_Wizard" title="Dark Wizard">Dark Wizard</a>. Dolohov could cast the <a href="/wiki/Unforgivable_Curses" title="Unforgivable Curses">Unforgivable Curses</a> (which require ability and genuine malice to perform) as he attempted to cast the <a href="/wiki/Killing_Curse" title="Killing Curse">Killing Curse</a> on Hermione before he was stopped by Harry Potter.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP35_11-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP35-11">&#91;11&#93;</a></sup> He was arrested for torturing Muggles and other enemies so it can be assumed that he was very competent with the <a href="/wiki/Cruciatus_Curse" title="Cruciatus Curse">Cruciatus Curse</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-GF30_7-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GF30-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup> He was also highly skilful with other <a href="/wiki/Curse" title="Curse">curses</a>, most notably the <a href="/wiki/Antonin_Dolohov%27s_curse" title="Antonin Dolohov&#39;s curse">purple curse</a> that he favoured on multiple occasions,<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP35_11-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP35-11">&#91;11&#93;</a></sup> as well as the <a href="/wiki/Expulso_Curse" title="Expulso Curse">Expulso Curse</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-DH9_13-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH9-13">&#91;13&#93;</a></sup> He could also cast <a href="/wiki/Jinx" title="Jinx">jinxes</a> such as the <a href="/wiki/Impediment_Jinx" title="Impediment Jinx">Impediment Jinx</a> and the <a href="/wiki/Dancing_Feet_Spell" title="Dancing Feet Spell">Dancing Feet Spell</a>, which he cast during the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_the_Department_of_Mysteries" title="Battle of the Department of Mysteries">Battle of the Department of Mysteries</a> with <a href="/wiki/Jugson" title="Jugson">Jugson</a> at <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> and <a href="/wiki/Neville_Longbottom" title="Neville Longbottom">Neville Longbottom</a> successfully.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP35_11-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP35-11">&#91;11&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Transfiguration" title="Transfiguration">Transfiguration</a></b>: Dolohov was able to <a href="/wiki/Incarcerous_Spell" title="Incarcerous Spell">conjure ropes</a> from his wand,<sup id="cite_ref-DH9_13-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH9-13">&#91;13&#93;</a></sup> which implied he was highly proficient in this notably complicated branch of <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magic</a>, as <a href="/wiki/Conjuration" title="Conjuration">conjuration</a> is an advanced form of transfiguration.</li></ul>

<ul><li><b><a href="/wiki/Nonverbal_spell" title="Nonverbal spell">Non-verbal magic</a></b>: Dolohov was highly skilled at nonverbal magic as shown during the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_the_Department_of_Mysteries" title="Battle of the Department of Mysteries">Battle of the Department of Mysteries</a> when he was hit with a Silencing Charm cast by Hermione Granger. Despite being unable to speak, he fought Hermione, Harry, and Neville all at once, incapacitating Hermione with <a href="/wiki/Antonin_Dolohov%27s_curse" title="Antonin Dolohov&#39;s curse">his signature spell</a> (although its effects were not as powerful). He was also able to cast the <a href="/wiki/Incarcerous_Spell" title="Incarcerous Spell">Incarcerous Spell</a> non-verbally. Furthermore, he attempted to kill Hermione with a non-verbal <a href="/wiki/Killing_Curse" title="Killing Curse">Killing Curse</a> which is said to be highly difficult.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP35_11-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP35-11">&#91;11&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Charm" title="Charm">Charms</a></b>: Dolohov appeared to be skilled with charms, as he was able to cast the <a href="/wiki/Summoning_Charm" title="Summoning Charm">Summoning Charm</a> in a failed attempt to get the prophecy from Harry Potter in 1996.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP35_11-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP35-11">&#91;11&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Spell_creation" title="Spell creation">Spell creation</a> (possibly)</b>: <a href="/wiki/Antonin_Dolohov%27s_Curse" class="mw-redirect" title="Antonin Dolohov&#39;s Curse">Dolohov's signature spell</a> involved using a slashing movement with his wand which would shoot purple <a href="/wiki/Fire" title="Fire">fire</a> at his victim. He used this spell several times during the battle of the Department of Mysteries (<a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione</a> was the only one to be struck).<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP35_11-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP35-11">&#91;11&#93;</a></sup> Dolohov was the only known practitioner of this unidentified spell and may have even invented it himself.</li></ul>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Antonin_Dolohov?veaction=edit&amp;section=14" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Antonin_Dolohov?action=edit&amp;section=14" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>Dolohov is a surname of Russian origin. It is the name of a character in <i><a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/War_and_Peace" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:War and Peace">War and Peace</a></i>, by Leo Tolstoy. This Dolohov is brave, but cruel and vindictive, though he is caring towards his mother and sister. <a href="/wiki/J._K._Rowling" title="J. K. Rowling">J. K. Rowling</a> has indirectly stated that she got the surname from this book.<sup id="cite_ref-20" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-20">&#91;20&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li>Antonin is the French or Czech version of the name "Anthony". The name is derived from the Latin <i><a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Antonius" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Antonius">Antonius</a></i> and <i><a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Antoninus" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Antoninus">Antoninus</a></i>. Antonius was the name of a prominent Roman family whose members included soldier and politician Marcus Antonius, more commonly known as <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mark_Antony" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Mark Antony">Mark Antony</a>. Antoninus was the name of another prominent Roman family whose members included Emperors <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Antoninus_Pius" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Antoninus Pius">Antoninus Pius</a> and <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Marcus_Aurelius" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Marcus Aurelius">Marcus Aurelius Antoninus</a>.</li></ul>

<ul><li>In <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Half-Blood_Prince" title="Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince">Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince</a></i>, it is mentioned that "Dolohov" was among <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Tom Marvolo Riddle</a>'s followers when he asked <a href="/wiki/Albus_Dumbledore" title="Albus Dumbledore">Albus Dumbledore</a> for the <a href="/wiki/Defence_Against_the_Dark_Arts" title="Defence Against the Dark Arts">Defence Against the Dark Arts</a> position sometime prior to the <a href="/wiki/First_Wizarding_War" title="First Wizarding War">First Wizarding War</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP20_21-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP20-21">&#91;21&#93;</a></sup> Since this individual's given name is not stated, it is not known whether he is Antonin Dolohov or a relative.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Arben_Bajraktaraj" title="Arben Bajraktaraj">Arben Bajraktaraj</a> portrayed Antonin Dolohov in the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Order_of_the_Phoenix_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix (film)">film adaptation</a> of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Order_of_the_Phoenix" title="Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix">Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix</a></i> (although he is listed in the credits as "Azkaban Death Eater") and reprises the role in the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Deathly_Hallows:_Part_1" title="Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 1">first instalment</a> of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Deathly_Hallows" title="Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows">Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows</a></i>.<sup id="cite_ref-22" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-22">&#91;22&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li>In the book, <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Deathly_Hallows:_Part_1" title="Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 1">Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 1</a></i>, <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ron Weasley</a> suggests killing Dolohov and <a href="/wiki/Thorfinn_Rowle" title="Thorfinn Rowle">Thorfinn Rowle</a> after evading their attack on <a href="/wiki/Tottenham_Court_Road" title="Tottenham Court Road">Tottenham Court Road</a>, but reluctantly, and is relieved when Harry decides it is better to <a href="/wiki/Memory_Charm" title="Memory Charm">obliviate</a> them. In the film, however, Ron appears eager to kill Dolohov, partly out of anger for <a href="/wiki/Luchino_Caffe_Duel" title="Luchino Caffe Duel">their recent attack</a>, and possibly due to Dolohov's hand in the deaths of his uncles, <a href="/wiki/Fabian_Prewett" title="Fabian Prewett">Fabian</a> and <a href="/wiki/Gideon_Prewett" title="Gideon Prewett">Gideon Prewett</a>. Hermione is appalled by the suggestion, but Ron accedes to Harry's order to use the Memory Charm on him instead.</li>
<li>Dolohov tends to be subject to the <a href="/wiki/Full_Body-Bind_Curse" title="Full Body-Bind Curse">Full Body-Bind Curse</a>, being hit by it twice during the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_the_Department_of_Mysteries" title="Battle of the Department of Mysteries">Battle of the Department of Mysteries</a>, once during the <a href="/wiki/Luchino_Caffe_Duel" title="Luchino Caffe Duel">Luchino Caffe Duel</a> and once during the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a>.</li>
<li>Dolohov has no lines in the films aside from "<i><a href="/wiki/Expulso_Curse" title="Expulso Curse">Expulso</a></i>" but he can be heard grunting in the seventh movie.</li>
<li>In the film adaptation of <i>Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix</i>, Dolohov participated in the Battle of the Department of Mysteries, during which he is seen holding <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione Granger</a> hostage, which did not happen in the book.</li>
<li>Dolohov appeared in the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Deathly_Hallows:_Part_2" title="Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 2">second film adaptation</a> of <i>Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows</i>, during which he is seen standing alongside all the Death Eaters when Voldemort announces that Harry Potter is “dead”.</li></ul>
