<b>Lucas Picquery</b><sup id="cite_ref-UVB_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-UVB-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> was an American <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> who played as a <a href="/wiki/Beater" title="Beater">Beater</a> for the <a href="/wiki/2014" title="2014">2014</a> <a href="/wiki/American_National_Quidditch_team" title="American National Quidditch team">American National Quidditch team</a>. According to <a href="/wiki/Ludovic_Bagman" title="Ludovic Bagman">Ludovic Bagman</a> in a report to the <i><a href="/wiki/Daily_Prophet" title="Daily Prophet">Daily Prophet</a></i>, Beaters Picquery and <a href="/wiki/Pringle" title="Pringle">Pringle</a> would have to "<i>find better form</i>" if they were to best the <a href="/wiki/Brazilian_National_Quidditch_team" title="Brazilian National Quidditch team">Brazilian</a> Beaters <a href="/wiki/Rafael_Santos" title="Rafael Santos">Santos</a> and <a href="/wiki/Carlos_Clodoaldo" title="Carlos Clodoaldo">Clodoaldo</a> in their semi-final match on <a href="/wiki/4_July" title="4 July">4 July</a>, <a href="/wiki/2014" title="2014">2014</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-PM_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PM-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Lucas_Picquery?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Lucas_Picquery?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Bagman, however, had spoken too soon.<sup id="cite_ref-UVB_1-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-UVB-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> On the first day of the match on <a href="/wiki/4_July" title="4 July">4 July</a>, Picquery sent a <a href="/wiki/Bludger" title="Bludger">Bludger</a> right into the face of the American <a href="/wiki/Keeper" title="Keeper">Keeper</a> <a href="/wiki/Susan_Blancheflower" title="Susan Blancheflower">Susan Blancheflower</a>, prompting her to leap onto his broomstick to reprimand him, leaving the goal wide open and allowing <a href="/wiki/Alejandra_Alonso" title="Alejandra Alonso">Alejandra Alonso</a> to score a goal.<sup id="cite_ref-UVB_1-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-UVB-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>During the playoffs for third place against <a href="/wiki/Japan" title="Japan">Japan</a> on <a href="/wiki/9_July" title="9 July">9 July</a>, Picquery mostly kept his head down until the end when he stopped his own <a href="/wiki/Seeker" title="Seeker">Seeker</a> for competing against <a href="/wiki/Noriko_Sato" title="Noriko Sato">Noriko Sato</a> for the <a href="/wiki/Golden_Snitch" title="Golden Snitch">Snitch</a>. Him and <a href="/wiki/Darius_Smackhammer" title="Darius Smackhammer">Darius Smackhammer</a> were caught in a broom-lock, although they embraced the other team upon landing.
</p>
