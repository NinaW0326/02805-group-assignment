<p>The <b>Knight Bus conductor</b> is the <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> who keeps the <a href="/wiki/Knight_Bus" title="Knight Bus">Knight Bus</a> on time.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>The conductor's job includes greeting passengers and handling baggage. The conductor also offers drinks: thirteen <a href="/wiki/Sickle" title="Sickle">Sickles</a> buys a cup of hot chocolate, while fifteen gets a hot <a href="/wiki/Water" title="Water">water</a> bottle and a toothbrush in any colour.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
The only known Knight Bus conductor is <a href="/wiki/Stanley_Shunpike" title="Stanley Shunpike">Stanley Shunpike</a>. However, as the Knight Bus travels both at day time and night time, it is unlikely that there would be only one conductor. Also, when Stan was sent to <a href="/wiki/Azkaban" title="Azkaban">Azkaban</a> in <a href="/wiki/1996" title="1996">1996</a>, it is assumed that another person took his shift.<sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
