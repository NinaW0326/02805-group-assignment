<b>Mrs Ross</b> was a Scottish <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch</a> and she and <a href="/wiki/Isobel_Ross%27s_father" title="Isobel Ross&#39;s father">her husband</a> had at least one child, <a href="/wiki/Isobel_Ross" title="Isobel Ross">Isobel</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-pottermore_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-pottermore-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>When Isobel became romantically involved with a <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggle</a> neighbour, <a href="/wiki/Robert_McGonagall_Senior" title="Robert McGonagall Senior">Robert McGonagall</a>, she knew her parents would not approve, and thus she chose to keep the relationship a secret. Eventually, the young couple eloped, provoking the Ross' anger to the extent that they completely cut off ties with their daughter.<sup id="cite_ref-pottermore_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-pottermore-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
