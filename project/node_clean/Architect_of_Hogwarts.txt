<div class="quote">
<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;The Wizarding World of Harry Potter">"<i>The front hall also boasts statues of the one-eyed Witch and the Architect of Hogwarts.</i>"</span></dd>
<dd>&#8212;Description<sup class="noprint"><a href="/wiki/The_Wizarding_World_of_Harry_Potter" title="The Wizarding World of Harry Potter">[src]</a></sup></dd>
<dd></dd></dl></div>
<p>This <b>individual</b> (fl. <i>circa</i> <a href="/wiki/First_millennium_A.D." title="First millennium A.D.">993</a>) was the architect responsible for planning and designing <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Castle" title="Hogwarts Castle">Hogwarts Castle</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> along with <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_founders" title="Hogwarts founders">Founder</a> <a href="/wiki/Rowena_Ravenclaw" title="Rowena Ravenclaw">Rowena Ravenclaw</a>, who devised the ever-changing floorplan.
</p><p>There is a golden statue of this individual at Hogwarts <a href="/wiki/Entrance_Hall" title="Entrance Hall">Entrance Hall</a>, in which he is shown among the four house mascots holding a model of <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts</a> on one of his hands, and charts and a set square in the other.<sup id="cite_ref-CSf20_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-CSf20-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> The statue guards a hidden passageway to the <a href="/wiki/Founders%27_Tower" title="Founders&#39; Tower">Founders' Tower</a>.
</p>
