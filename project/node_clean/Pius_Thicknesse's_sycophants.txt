These <b>nineteen individuals</b> (fl. <a href="/wiki/1997" title="1997">1997</a>) were <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizards</a> who worked at the <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">Ministry of Magic</a> under its <a href="/wiki/Fall_of_the_Ministry_of_Magic" title="Fall of the Ministry of Magic">Death Eater rule</a>. They adulated <a href="/wiki/Minister_for_Magic" title="Minister for Magic">Minister for Magic</a> <a href="/wiki/Pius_Thicknesse" title="Pius Thicknesse">Pius Thicknesse</a> excessively. On the morning of <a href="/wiki/2_September" title="2 September">2 September</a>, <a href="/wiki/1997" title="1997">1997</a>, these individuals followed Minister Thicknesse around Level One of the Ministry.<sup id="cite_ref-DHF1_2-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DHF1-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
