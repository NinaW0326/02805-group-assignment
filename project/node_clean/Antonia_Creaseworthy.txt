<b>Dame Antonia Creaseworthy</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch</a> who lived in the <a href="/wiki/17th_century" title="17th century">17th century</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-HPLE_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HPLE-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>. There were two <a href="/wiki/Portrait" title="Portrait">portraits</a> of her at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-HPLE_2-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HPLE-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-mhop_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-mhop-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> She served as <a href="/wiki/Headmaster" title="Headmaster">Headmistress</a> of Hogwarts at an unknown point in time.<sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Antonia_Creaseworthy?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Antonia_Creaseworthy?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Creaseworthy lived during the <a href="/wiki/17th_century" title="17th century">17th century</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-HPLE_2-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HPLE-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> During her life, she earned the title "Dame,"<sup id="cite_ref-mhop_3-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-mhop-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> the female equivalent of the honour of <a href="/wiki/Knight" title="Knight">knighthood</a> in <a href="/wiki/Great_Britain" title="Great Britain">Great Britain</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-damewiki_6-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-damewiki-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Her name was carved into a wood panel on the wall of <a href="/wiki/Sybill_Trelawney" title="Sybill Trelawney">Professor Trelawney's</a> <a href="/wiki/Divination_(class)" title="Divination (class)">Divination</a> <a href="/wiki/Divination_Classroom" title="Divination Classroom">classroom</a> at some point.<sup id="cite_ref-HPLE2_7-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HPLE2-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>In the winter of <a href="/wiki/1993" title="1993">1993</a>, Creaseworthy's name appeared on the <a href="/wiki/Marauder%27s_Map" title="Marauder&#39;s Map">Marauder's Map</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-poamap_8-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-poamap-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup> possibly indicating that she was still alive at this point, or lingering at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts</a> as a <a href="/wiki/Ghost" title="Ghost">ghost</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Two <a href="/wiki/Portrait" title="Portrait">portraits</a> of Creaseworthy were hung within <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Castle" title="Hogwarts Castle">Hogwarts Castle</a> sometime after her <a href="/wiki/Death" title="Death">death</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-death_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-death-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-HPLE_2-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HPLE-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-mhop_3-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-mhop-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> One was a full-body portrait painted in <a href="/wiki/1624" class="mw-redirect" title="1624">1624</a>, in which Creaseworthy was depicted as wearing an orange robe and leaning on a chair.<sup id="cite_ref-HPLE_2-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HPLE-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> The other was a small portrait in which Creaseworthy was depicted as wearing a blue dress, green shawl/robe, and feathered headdress.<sup id="cite_ref-mhop_3-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-mhop-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
