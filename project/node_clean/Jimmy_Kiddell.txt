<b>Jimmy Kiddell</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Great_Britain" title="Great Britain">British</a> <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> and <a href="/wiki/Wandmaker" title="Wandmaker">wandmaker</a> who owned the shop <a href="/wiki/Jimmy_Kiddell%27s_Wonderful_Wands" title="Jimmy Kiddell&#39;s Wonderful Wands">Jimmy Kiddell's Wonderful Wands</a> in <a href="/wiki/Diagon_Alley" title="Diagon Alley">Diagon Alley</a>. He is presumably an inferior wandmaker to <a href="/wiki/Garrick_Ollivander" title="Garrick Ollivander">Garrick Ollivander</a> as most wizard children get their wands at <a href="/wiki/Ollivanders" title="Ollivanders">Ollivanders</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-PSF_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PSF-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
