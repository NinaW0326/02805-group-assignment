This unidentified <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magical</a> <b><a href="/wiki/Creature" title="Creature">creature</a></b> was owned by <a href="/wiki/Rubeus_Hagrid" title="Rubeus Hagrid">Rubeus Hagrid</a> and lived in his <a href="/wiki/Rubeus_Hagrid%27s_cabin" class="mw-redirect" title="Rubeus Hagrid&#39;s cabin">hut</a>. It was a plant-like creature with an appearance similar to the <a href="/wiki/Mandrake" title="Mandrake">Mandrake</a> plant.<sup id="cite_ref-POAF_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-POAF-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="History">History</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_creature?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: History">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_creature?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: History"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>It was implied to be an illegal creature, when Hagrid hid it under a tea towel when the <a href="/wiki/Committee_for_the_Disposal_of_Dangerous_Creatures" title="Committee for the Disposal of Dangerous Creatures">Committee for the Disposal of Dangerous Creatures</a>, including <a href="/wiki/Albus_Dumbledore" title="Albus Dumbledore">Albus Dumbledore</a>, <a href="/wiki/Cornelius_Fudge" title="Cornelius Fudge">Cornelius Fudge</a> and <a href="/wiki/Walden_Macnair" title="Walden Macnair">Walden Macnair</a> the Executioner, arrived for the disposal of <a href="/wiki/Buckbeak" title="Buckbeak">Buckbeak</a> the <a href="/wiki/Hippogriff" title="Hippogriff">Hippogriff</a> some days after Hagrid's hearing with the <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Board_of_Governors" title="Hogwarts Board of Governors">Hogwarts Board of Governors</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-POAF_1-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-POAF-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
