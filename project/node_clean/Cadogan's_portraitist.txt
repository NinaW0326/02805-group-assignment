This <b>portraitist</b> (fl. Middle Ages) was responsible for creating the <a href="/wiki/Portrait" title="Portrait">portrait</a> of <a href="/wiki/Cadogan" title="Cadogan">Sir Cadogan</a> that would eventually be hung in <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Castle" title="Hogwarts Castle">Hogwarts Castle</a>. The portrait was composed of both Cadogan and <a href="/wiki/Cadogan%27s_pony" title="Cadogan&#39;s pony">his pony</a> on a vibrant landscape. As the portraitist interpreted Cadogan as a fairly unbalanced <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a>, constantly looking for a fight and falling from his <a href="/wiki/Horse" title="Horse">horse</a>, this was how the portrait acted upon its completion.<sup id="cite_ref-PM_1-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PM-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
