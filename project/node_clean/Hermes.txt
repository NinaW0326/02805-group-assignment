<p><b>Hermes</b> (fl. 1991-1995) was a <a href="/wiki/Screech_owl" title="Screech owl">screech owl</a> belonging to <a href="/wiki/Percy_Weasley" title="Percy Weasley">Percy Weasley</a>. Hermes was purchased for Percy by <a href="/wiki/Arthur_Weasley" title="Arthur Weasley">his father</a> as a reward for becoming a <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor" title="Gryffindor">Gryffindor</a> <a href="/wiki/Prefect" title="Prefect">prefect</a> in his fifth year at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a>.
</p><p>In <a href="/wiki/1993" title="1993">1993</a>, Hermes was stacked along with <a href="/wiki/Hedwig" title="Hedwig">Hedwig</a> and other belongings at the <a href="/wiki/Leaky_Cauldron" title="Leaky Cauldron">Leaky Cauldron</a> before the departure to <a href="/wiki/King%27s_Cross_Station" title="King&#39;s Cross Station">King's Cross Station</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-POA5_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-POA5-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>In <a href="/wiki/1995" title="1995">1995</a>, Percy used Hermes to deliver <a href="/wiki/Percy_Weasley%27s_letter_to_Ronald_Weasley_(1995)_II" title="Percy Weasley&#39;s letter to Ronald Weasley (1995) II">a letter</a> to his brother <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ronald</a>, advising him not to trust <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> any longer and side with <a href="/wiki/Dolores_Umbridge" title="Dolores Umbridge">Dolores Umbridge</a> and the <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">British Ministry of Magic</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Hermes?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Hermes?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li><a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hermes" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Hermes">Hermes</a> (Ἑρμής) was the messenger of the gods in Greek mythology. Coincidentally, this is also the origin of the name "Hermione". This is very fitting for Percy, as he and Hermione always got along well at school, and given Percy's pompous nature, he would prefer to name his owl after a divine messenger.</li></ul>
