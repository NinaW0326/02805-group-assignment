<p><b>Broderick Bode</b> (<a href="/wiki/1946" title="1946">1946</a><sup id="cite_ref-birth_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-birth-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> â€“ <a href="/wiki/January" title="January">January</a>, <a href="/wiki/1996" title="1996">1996</a><sup id="cite_ref-death_2-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-death-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>) was a <a href="/wiki/Great_Britain" title="Great Britain">British</a> <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> and <a href="/wiki/Unspeakable" title="Unspeakable">Unspeakable</a> working in the <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">British Ministry of Magic</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Department_of_Mysteries" title="Department of Mysteries">Department of Mysteries</a>, partnered with <a href="/wiki/Professor" title="Professor">Professor</a> <a href="/wiki/Saul_Croaker" title="Saul Croaker">Saul Croaker</a> and a friend of <a href="/wiki/Arthur_Weasley" title="Arthur Weasley">Arthur Weasley</a>. Bode was described as being "a sallow-skinned wizard with a very mournful face", and due to his strange nature spoke in a "sepulchral" type of voice and had an unfaltering gaze that made anyone he looked at uncomfortable.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP7_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP7-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Broderick_Bode?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Broderick_Bode?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Summers_of_1994_and_1995">Summers of 1994 and 1995</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Broderick_Bode?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Summers of 1994 and 1995">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Broderick_Bode?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Summers of 1994 and 1995"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>In <a href="/wiki/1994" title="1994">1994</a>, Bode attended the <a href="/wiki/Quidditch_World_Cup" title="Quidditch World Cup">Quidditch World Cup</a> with fellow Unspeakable <a href="/wiki/Saul_Croaker" title="Saul Croaker">Saul Croaker</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-GOF7_4-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF7-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> The <a href="/wiki/1995" title="1995">following summer</a>, he ran into colleague <a href="/wiki/Arthur_Weasley" title="Arthur Weasley">Arthur Weasley</a>, who was in a rush to reach Courtroom Ten with <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>, who had a hearing with the <a href="/wiki/Wizengamot" title="Wizengamot">Wizengamot</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP7_3-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP7-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>That same year, Bode became a target of the <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a>, as <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a> was seeking a <a href="/wiki/Sybill_Trelawney%27s_first_prophecy" title="Sybill Trelawney&#39;s first prophecy">prophecy</a> pertaining to himself and Harry, a record of which was stored in the <a href="/wiki/Hall_of_Prophecy" title="Hall of Prophecy">Hall of Prophecies</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Department_of_Mysteries" title="Department of Mysteries">Department of Mysteries</a>. Death Eater <a href="/wiki/Avery_(Marauder-era)" class="mw-redirect" title="Avery (Marauder-era)">Avery</a> informed the Dark Lord that an Unspeakable would be able to fetch the prophecy for him, after an attempt to force <a href="/wiki/Sturgis_Podmore" title="Sturgis Podmore">Sturgis Podmore</a> to steal it while under the <a href="/wiki/Imperius_Curse" title="Imperius Curse">Imperius Curse</a> failed.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP26_5-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP26-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Attempt_at_theft_and_injury">Attempt at theft and injury</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Broderick_Bode?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Attempt at theft and injury">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Broderick_Bode?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Attempt at theft and injury"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p><a href="/wiki/Lucius_Malfoy" title="Lucius Malfoy">Lucius Malfoy</a>, one of Voldemort's spies at the <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">Ministry</a>, placed Bode under the Imperius Curse to force him to attempt the same theft. <a href="/wiki/Lucius_Malfoy" title="Lucius Malfoy">Lucius Malfoy</a> noted that Bode showed unusual <a href="/wiki/Spell_resistance" title="Spell resistance">resistance</a> to the Imperius curse, which <a href="/wiki/Augustus_Rookwood" title="Augustus Rookwood">Augustus Rookwood</a> suggested may have been because Bode, being an Unspeakable, knew what would happen if he tried to remove one of the Prophecies. The moment he touched the orb, the defensive spells around it were triggered, as prophecies can only be obtained by those about whom they are made. Bode suffered <a href="/wiki/Spell" title="Spell">spell</a> damage that affected his mind, causing him to believe he was a teapot. However, the shock of it lifted the <a href="/wiki/Imperius_Curse" title="Imperius Curse">Imperius Curse</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP35_6-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP35-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Believing Bode had simply been injured in a workplace accident, he was taken to <a href="/wiki/St_Mungo%27s_Hospital_for_Magical_Maladies_and_Injuries" title="St Mungo&#39;s Hospital for Magical Maladies and Injuries">St Mungo's Hospital for Magical Maladies and Injuries</a> for treatment, but his injuries seemed irreparable, and as such was moved to the Spell Damage ward, left lying in his hospital bed, comatose most of the time, and those times he was awake, he was mumbling and staring at the ceiling.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP23_7-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP23-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Murder">Murder</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Broderick_Bode?veaction=edit&amp;section=4" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Murder">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Broderick_Bode?action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Murder"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<figure class="thumb tleft show-info-icon" style="width: 232px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/6/67/Broderick_Bode_St_Mungo%27s.png/revision/latest?cb=20161210211558" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/6/67/Broderick_Bode_St_Mungo%27s.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/230?cb=20161210211558" decoding="async" width="230" height="182" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="Broderick Bode St Mungo&#39;s.png" data-image-key="Broderick_Bode_St_Mungo&#39;s.png" /></a> 	 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:Broderick_Bode_St_Mungo%27s.png" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption">Broderick Bode in convalescence at St Mungo's, on Christmas 1995 (Note the "<i>rather ugly plant with long, swaying tentacles</i>" on his bedside table)</p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure>
<p>Eventually, Bode's condition took an upturn, and his health began to steadily improve. Upon learning of this, the <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a>, unable to risk the no-longer-Imperiused Bode getting better and exposing their plan, sent him an anonymous "<a href="/wiki/Christmas" title="Christmas">Christmas</a> present" â€” a potted plant and a calendar. The <a href="/wiki/Healer" title="Healer">Healer</a> in charge of the ward at the time, <a href="/wiki/Miriam_Strout" title="Miriam Strout">Miriam Strout</a>, was busy over the Christmas period, delivering other presents, looking after patients, seeing to visitations from family and friends etc. and as such overlooked the plant's dangers, in direct contradiction of <a href="/wiki/St_Mungo%27s_Hospital_for_Magical_Maladies_and_Injuries" title="St Mungo&#39;s Hospital for Magical Maladies and Injuries">St Mungo</a>'s strict guidelines on decorations permitted in wards, and left it on Bode's bedside cabinet table. Over the next few weeks during the Christmas holidays, <a href="/wiki/Miriam_Strout" title="Miriam Strout">Healer Strout</a>, noticing Bode's improving speech and mobility, encouraged Bode to look after the plant himself, unaware that that the "<i>rather ugly plant with long, swaying tentacles</i>" Strout had mistook for a <a href="/wiki/Flitterbloom" title="Flitterbloom">Flitterbloom</a>, was instead a disguised cutting of <a href="/wiki/Devil%27s_Snare" title="Devil&#39;s Snare">Devil's Snare</a>. One night, the plant, touched by the convalescent Bode, throttled him instantly, causing Bode to asphyxiate.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP23_7-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP23-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-OOTP25_8-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP25-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Legacy">Legacy</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Broderick_Bode?veaction=edit&amp;section=5" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Legacy">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Broderick_Bode?action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Legacy"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Broderick Bode was discovered dead in his bed the next morning, and <a href="/wiki/Healer" title="Healer">Healers</a> were immediately called to the scene, though were unable to revive Bode. St Mungo's, as a result, promised a full inquiry, though they were unable to account for the presence of the plant on the ward, and Healer Strout herself was suspended on full pay.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP25_8-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP25-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Voldemort also severely punished Avery for his bad advice, especially after hearing a true account of what would have happened from former <a href="/wiki/Unspeakable" title="Unspeakable">Unspeakable</a> and spy <a href="/wiki/Augustus_Rookwood" title="Augustus Rookwood">Augustus Rookwood</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP26_5-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP26-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>The death of Bode was ruled as a "tragic accident" and the <i><a href="/wiki/Daily_Prophet" title="Daily Prophet">Daily Prophet</a></i> reported it as "Tragic Demise of Ministry of Magic Worker," though Harry realised the truth during the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_the_Department_of_Mysteries" title="Battle of the Department of Mysteries">Battle of the Department of Mysteries</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP35_6-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP35-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup> Aside from Harry and his friends, there seemed to be nobody in <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts</a> who knew or cared about Bode's death.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP25_8-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP25-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Broderick_Bode?veaction=edit&amp;section=6" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Broderick_Bode?action=edit&amp;section=6" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The meaning of the word "bode" is to be an omen. Also, a stop or delay.
</p>
