<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Fat_Lady?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Fat_Lady?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Lifetime">Lifetime</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Fat_Lady?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Lifetime">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Fat_Lady?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Lifetime"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Next to nothing is known about the Fat Lady's early life or what her name was when she was alive. She presumably was educated at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> and was Sorted into <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor" title="Gryffindor">Gryffindor</a> house.
</p><p>It is unknown when or how she died, but it was by some time in the 1960s at the very latest (since her portrait already guarded the entrance to <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor_Tower" title="Gryffindor Tower">Gryffindor Tower</a> by then).
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="As_a_portrait">As a portrait</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Fat_Lady?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: As a portrait">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Fat_Lady?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: As a portrait"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<figure class="thumb tright show-info-icon" style="width: 252px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/1/17/Fat_lady.JPG/revision/latest?cb=20180607053257" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/1/17/Fat_lady.JPG/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/250?cb=20180607053257" decoding="async" width="250" height="224" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="Fat lady.JPG" data-image-key="Fat_lady.JPG" /></a> 	 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:Fat_lady.JPG" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption">The Fat Lady's portrait</p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure>
<p>After the Fat Lady died, a painting depicting her was hung at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Castle" title="Hogwarts Castle">Hogwarts Castle</a>, guarding the <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor_Tower" title="Gryffindor Tower">Gryffindor Common Room</a>. It is also unknown who or what, if anything, guarded the common room before her. The Fat Lady was a dutiful guardian to the Gryffindor Tower, only granting entrance to those who produce the correct password.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP11_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP11-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> She had, however, been known for reprimanding students when they returned late, annoyed that they woke her up from her sleep.<sup id="cite_ref-PS9_4-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PS9-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-GOF31_5-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF31-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> She had been known to move about occasionally and had a friend, <a href="/wiki/Violet" title="Violet">Violet</a>, who visited her, especially during such holidays as Christmas break. The two drank together frequently, once consuming several vats of wine that were in a picture of some drunk monks.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP17_6-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP17-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="1960s"><a href="/wiki/1960s" title="1960s">1960s</a></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Fat_Lady?veaction=edit&amp;section=4" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: 1960s">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Fat_Lady?action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: 1960s"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<p>When <a href="/wiki/Molly_Weasley" title="Molly Weasley">Molly Prewett</a> was a student, she returned to Gryffindor Tower at four o'clock in the morning after taking a late night stroll with her then boyfriend (now husband), <a href="/wiki/Arthur_Weasley" title="Arthur Weasley">Arthur Weasley</a>, and they both got a telling-off from the Fat Lady.<sup id="cite_ref-GOF31_5-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF31-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="1975"><a href="/wiki/1975" title="1975">1975</a></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Fat_Lady?veaction=edit&amp;section=5" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: 1975">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Fat_Lady?action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: 1975"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<p>After the end of their friendship, Severus Snape remained outside the Gryffindor Common Room until Lily came out to talk to him.<sup id="cite_ref-DH33_7-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH33-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<div class="quote">
<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;Harry Potter and the Philosopher&#39;s Stone">"<i>Where on earth have you all been?</i>"</span></dd>
<dd>&#8212;The Fat Lady scolding first year Gryffindors for being out late<sup class="noprint"><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Philosopher%27s_Stone" title="Harry Potter and the Philosopher&#39;s Stone">[src]</a></sup></dd>

<p>In <a href="/wiki/1991" title="1991">1991</a>, the Fat Lady allowed <a href="/wiki/First_year" title="First year">first year</a> Gryffindors into Gryffindor tower with the correct password.<sup id="cite_ref-PS7_2-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PS7-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> When <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> and <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ronald Weasley</a> had left the dormitory late one night to <a href="/wiki/Duelling" title="Duelling">duel</a> <a href="/wiki/Draco_Malfoy" title="Draco Malfoy">Draco Malfoy</a> at the third floor, she went off for a nightime visit, therefore meaning they, <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione Granger</a> (who had tried to stop them leaving) and <a href="/wiki/Neville_Longbottom" title="Neville Longbottom">Neville Longbottom</a> (who had forgotten the password), could not enter, resulting in all four of them heading down to the third floor. By the time she had returned to her portrait and the four of them had returned back to the dormitory, she questioned them on where they had been.<sup id="cite_ref-PS9_4-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PS9-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> Later in December <a href="/wiki/1991" title="1991">1991</a>, when Harry Potter left the dormitory at night under his <a href="/wiki/Cloak_of_Invisibility" title="Cloak of Invisibility">Invisbility Cloak</a>, she questioned who was there when Harry came through her portrait, as she could not see him due to him being fully <a href="/wiki/Invisibility" title="Invisibility">invisible</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-PS12_8-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PS12-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h4><span id="1992–1993_school_year"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="1992.E2.80.931993_school_year"><a href="/wiki/1992%E2%80%931993_school_year" title="1992–1993 school year">1992–1993 school year</a></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Fat_Lady?veaction=edit&amp;section=7" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: 1992–1993 school year">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Fat_Lady?action=edit&amp;section=7" title="Edit section: 1992–1993 school year"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<p>At the start of the school year when <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> and <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ronald Weasley</a> arrived late to the castle, due to missing the <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Express" title="Hogwarts Express">Hogwarts Express</a> and being forced to drive the <a href="/wiki/Flying_Ford_Anglia" title="Flying Ford Anglia">Flying Ford Anglia</a> to school (and crashing it into the <a href="/wiki/Whomping_Willow" title="Whomping Willow">Whomping Willow</a>), the two initially did not know the password to enter. However, their friend <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione Granger</a> soon arrived to tell them the password.<sup id="cite_ref-COS5_9-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COS5-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h4><span id="1994–1995_school_year"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="1994.E2.80.931995_school_year"><a href="/wiki/1994%E2%80%931995_school_year" title="1994–1995 school year">1994–1995 school year</a></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Fat_Lady?veaction=edit&amp;section=9" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: 1994–1995 school year">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Fat_Lady?action=edit&amp;section=9" title="Edit section: 1994–1995 school year"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<div class="quote">
<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire">"<i>Well, well, well, Violet’s just told me everything. Who’s just been chosen as school champion, then?</i>"</span></dd>
<dd>&#8212;The Fat Lady congratulating Harry Potter<sup class="noprint"><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Goblet_of_Fire" title="Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire">[src]</a></sup></dd>
<dd></dd></dl></div>
<p>In <a href="/wiki/1994" title="1994">1994</a>, when <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> had been chosen as the fourth <a href="/wiki/Triwizard_Tournament" title="Triwizard Tournament">Triwizard</a> champion, the Fat Lady was informed of this by her friend and fellow portrait <a href="/wiki/Violet" title="Violet">Violet</a>, and she seemed very eager to meet Harry when he went back to the common room, even bringing her friends from portraits with her.<sup id="cite_ref-GOF17_13-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF17-13">&#91;13&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>When Harry Potter and <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ron Weasley</a> were called by <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione Granger</a> to go to the <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_kitchens" title="Hogwarts kitchens">kitchen</a>, the Fat Lady was annoyed that she had to open the door just after they planned to not use it.<sup id="cite_ref-GOF21_14-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF21-14">&#91;14&#93;</a></sup> Later that year, after the <a href="/wiki/Yule_Ball" title="Yule Ball">Yule Ball</a> ended, the Fat Lady and Violet were both drunk, and Harry Potter had some trouble getting her to open the door. Again, when Harry tried to enter the tower, the Fat Lady was asleep and he had to yell the password to wake her up, much to her irritation.<sup id="cite_ref-GOF23_15-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF23-15">&#91;15&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h4><span id="1995–1996_school_year"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="1995.E2.80.931996_school_year"><a href="/wiki/1995%E2%80%931996_school_year" title="1995–1996 school year">1995–1996 school year</a></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Fat_Lady?veaction=edit&amp;section=10" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: 1995–1996 school year">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Fat_Lady?action=edit&amp;section=10" title="Edit section: 1995–1996 school year"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<div class="quote">
<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix">"<i>Are you going to give me the password or will I have to stay awake all night waiting for you to finish your conversation?</i>"</span></dd>

<dd></dd></dl></div>
<p>At the start of the school year, the Fat Lady initally refused to allow Harry to enter as he did know the password, but <a href="/wiki/Neville_Longbottom" title="Neville Longbottom">Neville Longbottom</a> arrived and gave the correct password, <i><a href="/wiki/Mimbulus_mimbletonia" title="Mimbulus mimbletonia">Mimbulus mimbletonia</a></i>, and she allowed them entry. When Harry, Ron and Hermione were discussing <a href="/wiki/Dolores_Umbridge" title="Dolores Umbridge">Dolores Umbridge</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Black_Quill" title="Black Quill">draconian</a> detentions for Harry outside the common room, the Fat Lady was annoyed at them for waking her up with their talking.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP13_16-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP13-16">&#91;16&#93;</a></sup> At the end of the school year, when Harry Potter sprinted out of the common room, she called upon him not to miss the <a href="/wiki/End-of-Term_Feast" title="End-of-Term Feast">End-of-Term Feast</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP38_17-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP38-17">&#91;17&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<p>After she and her friend Violet had overindulged on an entire vat of five-hundred year old <a href="/wiki/Wine" title="Wine">wine</a> over <a href="/wiki/Christmas" title="Christmas">Christmas</a> <a href="/wiki/1996" title="1996">1996</a>, meaning she was rather hungover and pale looking when Harry and Ron returned to Hogwarts in the castle a few days after new year in <a href="/wiki/1997" title="1997">1997</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP17_6-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP17-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Once, when Harry Potter returned late and woke her up, she refused to let him in, claiming that the password changed overnight. When Harry ran off to see Dumbledore regarding Slughorn's memory, she called after him and admitted that she was just kidding, though Harry proceeded to Dumbledore's office anyway.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP23_18-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP23-18">&#91;18&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Later that year, when Dumbledore was seemingly <a href="/wiki/Killing_Curse" title="Killing Curse">murdered</a> by <a href="/wiki/Severus_Snape" title="Severus Snape">Severus Snape</a>, the Fat Lady was in sorrow and she allowed Harry to enter the Tower without the password, while she cried.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP29_19-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP29-19">&#91;19&#93;</a></sup> She presumably continued to carry out her same role after the <a href="/wiki/Second_Wizarding_War" title="Second Wizarding War">war</a>.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Physical_Appearance">Physical Appearance</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Fat_Lady?veaction=edit&amp;section=13" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Physical Appearance">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Fat_Lady?action=edit&amp;section=13" title="Edit section: Physical Appearance"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The Fat Lady was, as suggested by her nickname, a "very fat woman" who was painted wearing a pink silk dress.<sup id="cite_ref-PS7_2-10" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PS7-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> On one occasion, her dress was said to be "satin,"<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP11_3-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP11-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> which is either a mistake or evidence that the Fat Lady was capable of changing her clothes. 
</p><p>She looked "rather paler than usual" while suffering the effects of a hangover,<sup id="cite_ref-HBP17_6-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP17-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup> which might suggest that she had a pale complexion under normal circumstances.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Personality_and_traits">Personality and traits</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Fat_Lady?veaction=edit&amp;section=14" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Personality and traits">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Fat_Lady?action=edit&amp;section=14" title="Edit section: Personality and traits"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<div class="quote">
<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix">"<i>No password, no entrance.</i>"</span></dd>
<dd>&#8212;The Fat Lady's strict attitude<sup class="noprint"><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Order_of_the_Phoenix" title="Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix">[src]</a></sup></dd>
<dd></dd></dl></div>
<p>The Fat Lady was well known during her life for her indulgence in good food and drink, as well as a passion for "tip-top security," traits that her portrait would later possess.<sup id="cite_ref-PM_30-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PM-30">&#91;30&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>As the guardian of the <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor_Tower" title="Gryffindor Tower">Gryffindor Tower</a>'s entrance, she was very dutiful, refusing entry to students who did not know the correct password.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP11_3-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP11-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> She was often irritated by those who would return late, waking her from her sleep.<sup id="cite_ref-PS9_4-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PS9-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> At such times, she would not hesitate to even lie to such students that the password has changed overnight, making them believe they have to sleep outside for the night.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP23_18-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP23-18">&#91;18&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Despite her dutiful and strict nature, she did have a sensitive side. After <a href="/wiki/Sirius_Black" title="Sirius Black">Sirius Black</a> slashed her portrait, the Fat Lady was too scared to continue her duties,<sup id="cite_ref-POA8_10-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-POA8-10">&#91;10&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-POA9_11-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-POA9-11">&#91;11&#93;</a></sup> until <a href="/wiki/Albus_Dumbledore" title="Albus Dumbledore">Dumbledore</a> provided her with additional security.<sup id="cite_ref-POA14_12-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-POA14-12">&#91;12&#93;</a></sup> After Dumbledore's death, she was so distraught that she allowed <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> entry without the password and she began to cry.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP29_19-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP29-19">&#91;19&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

In the first film, given the Fat Lady's dress and background, she seems to have lived in the sixteenth century Europe.</li>
<li>The Spriggs version of the Fat Lady's portrait is also used in the video game adaptations of <i>Philosopher's Stone</i> and <i>Chamber of Secrets</i>.</li></ul></li>
<li>In the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Prisoner_of_Azkaban_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban (film)">film adaptation</a> of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Prisoner_of_Azkaban" title="Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban">Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban</a></i>, she is played by <a href="/wiki/Dawn_French" title="Dawn French">Dawn French</a>, who portrays the character with a more humorous side, such as giving her a bad singing voice (possibly from the saying "the fat lady sings"). This iteration of her is larger than the previous one. She is much louder and more flamboyant and would be obvious to any passing members of other houses, who would also be able to hear Gryffindor students as they gave her the password, which this version of the portrait is reluctant to listen to. By this time, her portrait has been moved to the <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Castle" title="Hogwarts Castle">seventh floor</a> of the <a href="/wiki/Marble_Staircase_Tower" title="Marble Staircase Tower">Grand Staircase Tower</a>. The portrait is shown to swing backwards rather than forwards in this film as well. In the third film, given the Fat Lady's dress and background, she seems to have lived during the <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Classical_antiquity" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Classical antiquity">Classical period</a>. When the Gryffindor students aren't impressed with her singing, she mutters "Plebs" as they pass her. She also wears a white dress instead of a pink one.
<ul><li>The reason why the location of the Fat Lady's portrait was changed in the third film was because the filmmakers opted not to return to Gloucester Cathedral, where the scenes for the Fat Lady in the first film were shot.</li></ul></li>
<li>In the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Goblet_of_Fire_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire (film)">film adaptation</a> of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Goblet_of_Fire" title="Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire">Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire</a></i>, it is suggested that the Fat Lady's portrait may actually be a hidden passageway or shortcut. This is because the Gryffindor Boys' Dormitory is shown to be located in a small tower at the corner of the <a href="/wiki/The_Quad" title="The Quad">Paved Courtyard</a> close to the <a href="/wiki/Clock_Tower" title="Clock Tower">Clock Tower</a> and it is presumed the Fat Lady's portrait remains located in the <a href="/wiki/Grand_Staircase" title="Grand Staircase">Grand Staircase</a>.</li>
<li>In <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Chamber_of_Secrets_(video_game)" title="Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets (video game)">Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets (video game)</a></i>, she is instead named <b>The Pink Lady</b>.</li>
<li>In the video game versions of the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Order_of_the_Phoenix_(video_game)" title="Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix (video game)">fifth</a> and <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Half-Blood_Prince_(video_game)" title="Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince (video game)">sixth</a> films, characters do not give the Fat Lady a password, as she simply opens to admit them upon recognising them.</li>
<li>It can also be noted that although the location of the portrait changes in the films, the size and shape of the actual <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor_Tower" title="Gryffindor Tower">Gryffindor Common Room</a> remains the same.</li>
<li>The Fat Lady is a playable character in <i><a href="/wiki/LEGO_Harry_Potter:_Years_5-7" title="LEGO Harry Potter: Years 5-7">LEGO Harry Potter: Years 5-7</a></i>, in which she, despite being opaque and not floating like ghosts, only has <a href="/wiki/Ghost" title="Ghost">ghostly</a> abilities (like scaring other characters).</li>
<li>In <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter:_Hogwarts_Mystery" title="Harry Potter: Hogwarts Mystery">Harry Potter: Hogwarts Mystery</a></i>, the entrance to the Gryffindor Common Room appears to be a normal door, though it is possible that it leads to the Fat Lady's corridor.</li></ul>
