<b>Glenda Chittock</b> (b. <a href="/wiki/1964" title="1964">1964</a>) was a <a href="/wiki/Great_Britain" title="Great Britain">British</a> <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch</a> who was host of the <a href="/wiki/Wizarding_Wireless_Network" title="Wizarding Wireless Network">Wizarding Wireless Network</a> programme, <i><a href="/wiki/Witching_Hour" title="Witching Hour">Witching Hour</a></i>. Being host of this enormously famous radio programme, she is featured on a <a href="/wiki/Chocolate_Frog_Card" title="Chocolate Frog Card">Chocolate Frog Card</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
