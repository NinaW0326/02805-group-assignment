<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Dolores_Umbridge%27s_brother?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Dolores_Umbridge%27s_brother?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Umbridge was born in <a href="/wiki/1973" title="1973">1973</a> or earlier to the <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> <a href="/wiki/Orford_Umbridge" title="Orford Umbridge">Orford Umbridge</a> and <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggle</a> <a href="/wiki/Ellen_Cracknell" title="Ellen Cracknell">Ellen Cracknell</a>, and had an older sister named <a href="/wiki/Dolores_Umbridge" title="Dolores Umbridge">Dolores</a>. His status as a <a href="/wiki/Squib" title="Squib">Squib</a> would often lead his <a href="/wiki/Orford_Umbridge" title="Orford Umbridge">father</a> and sister to blame his <a href="/wiki/Ellen_Cracknell" title="Ellen Cracknell">mother</a> for being the cause of it, which led the <a href="/wiki/Umbridge_family" title="Umbridge family">family</a> to be fraught with tensions. When Dolores was fifteen years old, his parents separated and he would return to the <a href="/wiki/Muggle_world" title="Muggle world">Muggle world</a> with his mother, while his sister would stay with his father in the <a href="/wiki/Wizarding_world" title="Wizarding world">wizarding world</a> and would never speak of him again.<sup id="cite_ref-PM_2-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PM-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
