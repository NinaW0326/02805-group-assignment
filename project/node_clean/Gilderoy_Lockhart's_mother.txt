<b>Mrs Lockhart</b> was the <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch</a> mother of <a href="/wiki/Gilderoy_Lockhart" title="Gilderoy Lockhart">Gilderoy Lockhart</a>, former celebrity and <a href="/wiki/Defence_Against_the_Dark_Arts" title="Defence Against the Dark Arts">Defence Against the Dark Arts</a> <a href="/wiki/Professor" title="Professor">Professor</a> at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Gilderoy_Lockhart%27s_sisters" title="Gilderoy Lockhart&#39;s sisters">two older Squib daughters</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-PM_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PM-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Gilderoy_Lockhart%27s_mother?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Gilderoy_Lockhart%27s_mother?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Mrs Lockhart was very ambitious for <a href="/wiki/Gilderoy_Lockhart" title="Gilderoy Lockhart">her son</a>, and encouraged him in the belief that he was a remarkable person â€” something that showed in her rather flashy choice of name for her son.<sup id="cite_ref-celebrationhp_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-celebrationhp-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> Her overly pampering nature for her son, especially after discovering that he is her only child to have <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magical</a> powers, caused his vanity to grow like a tenacious weed, which led to his obnoxious, attention-seeking nature that branched off into ignorance, laziness, skill deterioration, fraudulence and eventually self-induced amnesia.<sup id="cite_ref-PM_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PM-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>The <a href="/wiki/Healer" title="Healer">Healer</a> who looked after <a href="/wiki/Gilderoy_Lockhart" title="Gilderoy Lockhart">Lockhart</a>, <a href="/wiki/Miriam_Strout" title="Miriam Strout">Miriam Strout</a>, stated he never got visitors in <a href="/wiki/December" title="December">December</a> <a href="/wiki/1995" title="1995">1995</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP23_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP23-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> This suggests that his mother was deceased by then, considering the doting affection she showed for her son, or that she came to despise his obnoxiousness and vanity. However, it is unlikely that she and the rest of the family ever knew about how far Lockhart went in his desire for attention, as Dumbledore never outright publicised it and the fact that Dumbledore would reveal it to Lockhart's family of all people would be uncharacteristically cruel for him.
</p>
