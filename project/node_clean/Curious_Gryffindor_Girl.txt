<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Sorting_and_early_years">Sorting and early years</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Curious_Gryffindor_Girl?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Sorting and early years">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Curious_Gryffindor_Girl?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Sorting and early years"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>She attended <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a>, likely beginning in <a href="/wiki/1992" title="1992">1992</a>, and was <a href="/wiki/Sorting_ceremony" title="Sorting ceremony">Sorted</a> into <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor" title="Gryffindor">Gryffindor</a> <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Houses" title="Hogwarts Houses">House</a>.
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Fourth_year">Fourth year</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Curious_Gryffindor_Girl?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Fourth year">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Curious_Gryffindor_Girl?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Fourth year"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>In her <a href="/wiki/Fourth_year" title="Fourth year">fourth year</a> at Hogwarts, she was awoken one morning a day or two after the first <a href="/wiki/Hogsmeade_weekend_trips" title="Hogsmeade weekend trips">trip to Hogsmeade</a> of the <a href="/wiki/1995-1996_school_year" class="mw-redirect" title="1995-1996 school year">1995-1996 school year</a> by <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ron Weasley</a> attempting to enter the <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor_Girls%27_Dormitory" title="Gryffindor Girls&#39; Dormitory">Gryffindor Girls' Dormitory</a>. However, because the staircase had been bewitched to make a loud noise when a boy tried to climb them, it alerted her and her friend to his presence.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP17_3-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP17-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
