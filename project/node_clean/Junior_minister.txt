<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Known_junior_ministers">Known junior ministers</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Junior_minister?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Known junior ministers">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Junior_minister?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Known junior ministers"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li><a href="/wiki/Herbert_Chorley" title="Herbert Chorley">Herbert Chorley</a>, a junior minister for the Muggle government. In <a href="/wiki/1996" title="1996">1996</a>, he fell under a botched <a href="/wiki/Imperius_Curse" title="Imperius Curse">Imperius Curse</a> and began acting like a <a href="/wiki/Duck" title="Duck">duck</a>. He was sent to <a href="/wiki/St_Mungo%27s_Hospital_for_Magical_Maladies_and_Injuries" title="St Mungo&#39;s Hospital for Magical Maladies and Injuries">St Mungo's Hospital for Magical Maladies and Injuries</a> for recovery.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Cornelius_Fudge" title="Cornelius Fudge">Cornelius Fudge</a>, who was a junior minister for the <a href="/wiki/Department_of_Magical_Accidents_and_Catastrophes" title="Department of Magical Accidents and Catastrophes">Department of Magical Accidents and Catastrophes</a> in <a href="/wiki/1981" title="1981">1981</a>.</li></ul>
