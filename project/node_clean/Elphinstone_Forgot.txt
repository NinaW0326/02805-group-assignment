<b>Elphinstone Forgot</b> was an admiral in an unknown navy. His name was written on the label of a bottle of <a href="/wiki/Nux_Myristica" title="Nux Myristica">Nux Myristica</a>, presumably indicating that he gathered it, or that it once belonged to him.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
