<b>Martha</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggle</a> girl who worked at <a href="/wiki/Wool%27s_Orphanage" title="Wool&#39;s Orphanage">Wool's Orphanage</a> in the <a href="/wiki/1930s" title="1930s">1930s</a>. She worked at the orphanage during <a href="/wiki/Albus_Dumbledore" title="Albus Dumbledore">Albus Dumbledore</a>'s visit to <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Tom Riddle</a>, when the latter was eleven, by which time she was looking after children who had <a href="/wiki/Chicken_pox" title="Chicken pox">chicken pox</a>. Martha worked at the orphanage from a time before Tom Riddle was born to sometime after Tom Riddle went to <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> at the age of eleven.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP13_1-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP13-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
