<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_Aurors?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_Aurors?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>They were both waiting at <a href="/wiki/King%27s_Cross_Station" title="King&#39;s Cross Station">King's Cross Station</a> on <a href="/wiki/1_September" title="1 September">1 September</a>, <a href="/wiki/1996" title="1996">1996</a> as a security escort for <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>. Both were described as grim-faced and dressed as <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggles</a> in dark suits. Upon the arrival of Harry and the <a href="/wiki/Weasley_family" title="Weasley family">Weasleys</a>, the Aurors quickly escorted them into the station without speaking, although Harry found his escort's over-protectiveness somewhat irritating.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP7_1-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP7-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
