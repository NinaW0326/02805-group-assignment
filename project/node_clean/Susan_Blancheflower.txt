<b>Susan Blancheflower</b> was an <a href="/wiki/United_States_of_America" title="United States of America">American</a> <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch</a> and the <a href="/wiki/Keeper" title="Keeper">Keeper</a> for the <a href="/wiki/American_National_Quidditch_team" title="American National Quidditch team">American National Quidditch team</a> in the <a href="/wiki/2014_Quidditch_World_Cup" title="2014 Quidditch World Cup">2014 Quidditch World Cup</a><sup id="cite_ref-PMI_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PMI-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Susan_Blancheflower?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Susan_Blancheflower?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Following the America-<a href="/wiki/Jamaica" title="Jamaica">Jamaica</a> match in the quarter-finals, <a href="/wiki/Ludovic_Bagman" title="Ludovic Bagman">Ludovic Bagman</a> criticised Blancheflower for letting in so many Jamaican goals in a report made to the <i><a href="/wiki/Daily_Prophet" title="Daily Prophet">Daily Prophet</a></i><sup id="cite_ref-PMI_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PMI-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>. Partly because of this, he gave her team low odds on winning the semifinals<sup id="cite_ref-PMI_1-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PMI-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>.
</p><p>During the America-<a href="/wiki/Brazil" title="Brazil">Brazil</a> semifinal match, she was hit in the face with a <a href="/wiki/Bludger" title="Bludger">bludger</a> sent there by one of her own <a href="/wiki/Beater" title="Beater">Beaters</a>, <a href="/wiki/Lucas_Picquery" title="Lucas Picquery">Lucas Picquery</a>. She then attempted to jump onto his broom, and was cautioned by the referee. Her game did not improve, however, and she made a simple mistake and let <a href="/wiki/Alejandra_Alonso" title="Alejandra Alonso">Alejandra Alonso</a> score, putting Brazil ten points ahead.&#160;
</p><p>During the playoffs for third place against <a href="/wiki/Japan" title="Japan">Japan</a>, the Japanese <a href="/wiki/Chaser" title="Chaser">Chasers</a> got eighteen goals past her. At the end of the game, though, the two teams embraced.
</p>
