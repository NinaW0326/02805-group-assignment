<p><b>Ragnok </b>(fl. <a href="/wiki/1995" title="1995">1995</a>) was a <a href="/wiki/Goblin" title="Goblin">goblin</a> employed at <a href="/wiki/Gringotts_Wizarding_Bank" title="Gringotts Wizarding Bank">Gringotts Wizarding Bank</a>.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Ragnok?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Ragnok?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<figure class="thumb tright show-info-icon" style="width: 252px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/3/34/Ragnok%27sOffice.png/revision/latest?cb=20140620231328" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/3/34/Ragnok%27sOffice.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/250?cb=20140620231328" decoding="async" width="250" height="377" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="Ragnok&#39;sOffice.png" data-image-key="Ragnok&#39;sOffice.png" /></a> 	 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:Ragnok%27sOffice.png" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption">Ragnok's office at <a href="/wiki/Gringotts_Wizarding_Bank" title="Gringotts Wizarding Bank">Gringotts Wizarding Bank</a></p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure>
<p>Ragnok worked at <a href="/wiki/Gringotts_Wizarding_Bank" title="Gringotts Wizarding Bank">Gringotts Wizarding Bank</a> in the 1990's , and had an office next to <a href="/wiki/Griphook" title="Griphook">Griphook</a> in the main offices at <a href="/wiki/Diagon_Alley" title="Diagon Alley">Diagon Alley</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-WWoHP_1-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-WWoHP-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> Ragnok was acquainted with <a href="/wiki/William_Weasley" title="William Weasley">Bill Weasley</a>, and seemingly known to <a href="/wiki/Remus_Lupin" title="Remus Lupin">Professor Remus Lupin</a> as well.
</p><p>It seems that Ragnok's leanings were such that Professor Lupin thought he might be amenable to helping the <a href="/wiki/Order_of_the_Phoenix" title="Order of the Phoenix">Order of the Phoenix</a>, though when Bill tried to convince him to do so in <a href="/wiki/1995" title="1995">1995</a>, Ragnok declined, as he was feeling a lot of hostility toward <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizards</a> at the time. <a href="/wiki/Ludovic_Bagman" title="Ludovic Bagman">Ludo Bagman</a> had recently failed to pay some goblins winnings to which they felt they were entitled, and Ragnok believed that the <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">Ministry of Magic</a> had conspired to hide evidence of this. He expressed his thoughts on the matter very vehemently.<sup id="cite_ref-OP5_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OP5-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
