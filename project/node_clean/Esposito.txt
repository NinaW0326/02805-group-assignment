<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Esposito?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Esposito?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Mrs Esposito lived on the main floor of the <a href="/wiki/679_West_24th_Street" title="679 West 24th Street">679</a> <a href="/wiki/West_24th_Street" title="West 24th Street">West 24th Street</a> brownstone house. She rented out an apartment on the second floor to two sisters, <a href="/wiki/Porpentina_Goldstein" title="Porpentina Goldstein">Tina</a> and <a href="/wiki/Queenie_Goldstein" title="Queenie Goldstein">Queenie Goldstein</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-FB40_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-FB40-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> She did not allow them to bring home male guests. However, on the night of <a href="/wiki/6_December" title="6 December">6 December</a> Tina sneaked <a href="/wiki/Newton_Scamander" title="Newton Scamander">Newt Scamander</a> and <a href="/wiki/Jacob_Kowalski" title="Jacob Kowalski">Jacob Kowalski</a> upstairs. Newt and Jacob needed a place to hide from the <a href="/wiki/Magical_Congress_of_the_United_States_of_America" title="Magical Congress of the United States of America">MACUSA</a> authorities pursuing them.<sup id="cite_ref-FB40_1-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-FB40-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-FB39_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-FB39-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> Hearing noise on the stairs, Mrs Esposito called out, asking if Tina was alone. Tina untruthfully replied that she was always alone.<sup id="cite_ref-FB40_1-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-FB40-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
