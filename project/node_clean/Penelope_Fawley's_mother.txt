<p><b>Mrs Padgett</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Great_Britain" title="Great Britain">British</a> <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch</a>, the wife of <a href="/wiki/Penelope_Fawley%27s_father" title="Penelope Fawley&#39;s father">Mr Padgett</a> and the mother of <a href="/wiki/Penelope_Fawley" title="Penelope Fawley">Penelope Fawley (née Padgett)</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-WU_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-WU-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Penelope_Fawley%27s_mother?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Penelope_Fawley%27s_mother?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>During the <a href="/wiki/Second_Wizarding_War" title="Second Wizarding War">Second Wizarding War</a>, Mr and Mrs Padgett were both outspokenly supportive of <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a> and his <a href="/wiki/Magic_is_Might" title="Magic is Might">Magic is Might</a> Campaign; following the war, the latter was imprisoned in <a href="/wiki/Azkaban" title="Azkaban">Azkaban</a> for involving herself in the conflict.<sup id="cite_ref-WU_2-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-WU-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> Before she was imprisoned, the last thing she gave her daughter was a <a href="/wiki/Comet_290" title="Comet 290">Comet 290</a> <a href="/wiki/Broomstick" title="Broomstick">broomstick</a>, which was regarded as the only thing she was left with to remember her by.<sup id="cite_ref-WU_2-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-WU-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>When <a href="/wiki/Penelope_Fawley" title="Penelope Fawley">Penelope</a> came to work in the <a href="/wiki/Auror_Office" title="Auror Office">Auror Office</a>, <a href="/wiki/Gethsemane_Prickle" title="Gethsemane Prickle">Gethsemane Prickle</a> was suspicious of her due to her parents' ties to the <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-WU_2-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-WU-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
