<b>Fulbert the Fearful</b> (<a href="/wiki/1014" class="mw-redirect" title="1014">1014</a> -<a href="/wiki/1097" class="mw-redirect" title="1097">1097</a>) was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> who was famous for being so cowardly that he never ventured out of his house. In a twist of irony, he died when a <a href="/wiki/Defensive_Charm" title="Defensive Charm">Defensive Charm</a> backfired and his roof caved in. For this reason, he was immortalised on a <a href="/wiki/Chocolate_Frog_Card" title="Chocolate Frog Card">Chocolate Frog Card</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Fulbert_the_Fearful?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Fulbert_the_Fearful?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The name "Fulbert" is from the Germanic elements <i>folk</i> "people" and <i>beraht</i> "bright". Saint Fulbert was an 11th-century bishop of Chartres.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Fulbert_the_Fearful?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Fulbert_the_Fearful?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<figure class="thumb tright show-info-icon" style="width: 125px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/8/81/Fulbert_the_Fearful.png/revision/latest?cb=20130809214034" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/8/81/Fulbert_the_Fearful.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/123?cb=20130809214034" decoding="async" width="123" height="123" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="Fulbert the Fearful.png" data-image-key="Fulbert_the_Fearful.png" /></a> 	 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:Fulbert_the_Fearful.png" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption">A younger Fulbert</p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure>
<div class="quote">
<dl><dd><span>"<i>It is impossible to live without failing at something, unless you live so cautiously that you might as well not have lived at all â€“ in which case, you fail by default.</i>"</span></dd>
<dd>&#8212;<a href="/wiki/J._K._Rowling" title="J. K. Rowling">J. K. Rowling</a>'s Harvard Commencement Speech in 2008<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup></dd>
<dd></dd></dl></div>
<p>It appears that Fulbert the Fearful embodies J.K. Rowling's personal belief that living an excessively cautious life is hardly a life at all, and doing so will just makes you lose. She expressed this during her Commencement Speech at Harvard University in 2008.
</p>
