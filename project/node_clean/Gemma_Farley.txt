<b>Gemma Farley</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch</a> who began attending <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> between <a href="/wiki/1985" title="1985">1985</a> and <a href="/wiki/1987" title="1987">1987</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-PM_3-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PM-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Gemma_Farley?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Gemma_Farley?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p><a href="/wiki/Sorting_ceremony" title="Sorting ceremony">Sorted</a> into <a href="/wiki/Slytherin" title="Slytherin">Slytherin</a>, she was made a <a href="/wiki/Prefect" title="Prefect">prefect</a> by <a href="/wiki/1991" title="1991">1991</a>, and welcomed <a href="/wiki/First_year" title="First year">first years</a> when they initially arrived in her <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Houses" title="Hogwarts Houses">House</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-PM_3-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PM-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> Among them would have been boys <a href="/wiki/Draco_Malfoy" title="Draco Malfoy">Draco Malfoy</a>, <a href="/wiki/Gregory_Goyle" title="Gregory Goyle">Gregory Goyle</a>, <a href="/wiki/Vincent_Crabbe" title="Vincent Crabbe">Vincent Crabbe</a>, <a href="/wiki/Blaise_Zabini" title="Blaise Zabini">Blaise Zabini</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Theodore_Nott" title="Theodore Nott">Theodore Nott</a> and girls <a href="/wiki/Pansy_Parkinson" title="Pansy Parkinson">Pansy Parkinson</a>, <a href="/wiki/Millicent_Bulstrode" title="Millicent Bulstrode">Millicent Bulstrode</a>, <a href="/wiki/Daphne_Greengrass" title="Daphne Greengrass">Daphne Greengrass</a> and <a href="/wiki/Tracey_Davis" title="Tracey Davis">Tracey Davis</a>.
</p>
