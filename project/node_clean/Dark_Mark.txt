<div class="quote">

<p>The Dark Mark appears as a glittering green skull with a snake protruding from its mouth.<sup id="cite_ref-GOF9_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF9-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> This might have been inspired by the hiding place of the <a href="/wiki/Salazar_Slytherin%27s_Basilisk" class="mw-redirect" title="Salazar Slytherin&#39;s Basilisk">Basilisk</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Chamber_of_Secrets" title="Chamber of Secrets">Chamber of Secrets</a>. When summoned, the basilisk would emerge from the mouth of <a href="/wiki/Salazar_Slytherin" title="Salazar Slytherin">Salazar Slytherin</a>. The serpent is also the symbol of <a href="/wiki/Slytherin" title="Slytherin">Slytherin</a> house, and thus appropriate to be used by the <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Heir of Slytherin</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-COS17_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COS17-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>The symbol might also be related to <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a> being a <a href="/wiki/Parseltongue" title="Parseltongue">Parselmouth</a>, a gift he thought made him unlike the rest of the <a href="/wiki/Wizarding_world" title="Wizarding world">wizarding world</a>. The Dark Mark on the forearm is black, while the Dark Mark conjured by <i><a href="/wiki/Morsmordre" title="Morsmordre">Morsmordre</a></i> is green, this is most likely for more contrast with each respective background as a black Dark Mark would be hard to see in the night sky. It is also said that the Dark Mark represents immortality and power.<sup id="cite_ref-GOF9_1-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF9-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>The Dark Mark in the sky and on the Death Eaters' arms are different in appearance. The one in the sky is plainly a skull with a snake coming out straight out of its mouth,<sup id="cite_ref-GOF9_1-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF9-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> while the brand on a Death Eater's arm is much more intricate and detailed, the snake being much longer and winding.<sup id="cite_ref-GOF33_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF33-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<p>An inactive Dark Mark appears as a faint mark on the inner part of the left forearm, similar to that of "a vivid red <a href="/wiki/Tattoo" title="Tattoo">tattoo</a>"; an active one is jet black.<sup id="cite_ref-GOF33_3-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF33-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p><a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a> placed the Dark Mark <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/branding" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:branding">brand</a> on his followers' inner left forearms both as a sign of their loyalty to him and as a method of summoning them to him when he desired.<sup id="cite_ref-GOF9_1-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF9-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> This was done by pressing his finger to any <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eater's</a> Mark, which turns it jet black and delivers a burning sensation to any person with the Dark Mark. Feeling the burn, the Death Eaters then <a href="/wiki/Apparition" title="Apparition">Apparate</a> to Voldemort's side.<sup id="cite_ref-GOF33_3-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF33-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> It is unknown whether all Death Eaters will always feel the burning, or if Voldemort can choose to signal a specific person or people. Traitors such as <a href="/wiki/Igor_Karkaroff" title="Igor Karkaroff">Igor Karkaroff</a> could somehow avoid being summoned by Voldemort.<sup id="cite_ref-GOF23_4-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF23-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-GOF27_5-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF27-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<figure class="thumb tright show-info-icon" style="width: 146px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/1/13/DarkMark_DracoMalfoyArm.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20120818125613" class="image"><img alt="" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw%3D%3D" decoding="async" width="144" height="146" class="thumbimage lazyload" data-image-name="DarkMark DracoMalfoyArm.jpg" data-image-key="DarkMark_DracoMalfoyArm.jpg" data-src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/1/13/DarkMark_DracoMalfoyArm.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/144?cb=20120818125613" /></a> 	<noscript><a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/1/13/DarkMark_DracoMalfoyArm.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20120818125613" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/1/13/DarkMark_DracoMalfoyArm.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/144?cb=20120818125613" decoding="async" width="144" height="146" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="DarkMark DracoMalfoyArm.jpg" data-image-key="DarkMark_DracoMalfoyArm.jpg" data-src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/1/13/DarkMark_DracoMalfoyArm.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/144?cb=20120818125613" /></a></noscript> 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:DarkMark_DracoMalfoyArm.jpg" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption">Draco showing his Dark Mark</p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure>
<p>The deepness of the Mark's colour is connected to Voldemort's physical health. When Voldemort suffered his first defeat, the Marks on the Death Eaters faded, but as he began regaining his strength, the faded marks became clearer, and burnt slightly, a sign of Voldemort's return to his Death Eaters; the burn may be sudden and stings rather sharply, as Snape suddenly gripped where his mark was during the night <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> was solving the <a href="/wiki/Golden_egg" title="Golden egg">Golden egg</a> riddle.<sup id="cite_ref-GOF25_6-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF25-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup> Death Eaters could also use it to signal Voldemort by pressing on their Dark Marks. Bearing this mark also allows the Death Eaters to bypass a <a href="/wiki/Death_Eater%27s_barrier_curse" class="mw-redirect" title="Death Eater&#39;s barrier curse">cursed barrier</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP27_7-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP27-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup> During the height of the <a href="/wiki/Second_Wizarding_War" title="Second Wizarding War">Second Wizarding War</a>, they were under orders to only signal <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Voldemort</a> if they had successfully captured <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>, or face severe penalties. After Voldemort was defeated for the final time, the Dark Marks faded to a scar, similar to Harry's lightning-bolt scar. They would no longer burn, but would remain nevertheless. There is no possible way of removing the Dark Mark.
</p>
<figure class="thumb tleft show-info-icon" style="width: 252px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/a/a9/Markdolohov.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20100710170414" class="image"><img alt="" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw%3D%3D" decoding="async" width="250" height="123" class="thumbimage lazyload" data-image-name="Markdolohov.jpg" data-image-key="Markdolohov.jpg" data-src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/a/a9/Markdolohov.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/250?cb=20100710170414" /></a> 	<noscript><a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/a/a9/Markdolohov.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20100710170414" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/a/a9/Markdolohov.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/250?cb=20100710170414" decoding="async" width="250" height="123" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="Markdolohov.jpg" data-image-key="Markdolohov.jpg" data-src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/a/a9/Markdolohov.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/250?cb=20100710170414" /></a></noscript> 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:Markdolohov.jpg" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption">The Dark Mark on <a href="/wiki/Antonin_Dolohov" title="Antonin Dolohov">Dolohov's</a> arm</p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure>
<p>Only Voldemort's inner circle were branded with the Dark Mark, which was considered a great honour among his followers. Thus, people like the <a href="/wiki/Snatchers" title="Snatchers">Snatchers</a> never received one.<sup id="cite_ref-DH23_8-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH23-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p><a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione Granger</a> used the <a href="/wiki/Protean_Charm" title="Protean Charm">Protean Charm</a> to create <a href="/wiki/Dumbledore%27s_Army_coin" title="Dumbledore&#39;s Army coin">special coins</a> for <a href="/wiki/Dumbledore%27s_Army" title="Dumbledore&#39;s Army">Dumbledore's Army</a>, which would heat up when signalling a meeting, and she claimed that she indeed got the idea from the Dark Mark.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP16_9-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP16-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p><a href="/wiki/Peter_Pettigrew" title="Peter Pettigrew">Peter Pettigrew</a>, despite being a low-ranking Death Eater for his cowardice and lacking skills, was branded with a Dark Mark. However, he was not given the same respect as the other Death Eaters would have received, and was instead treated as a lowly household servant by both his master and his colleagues. Voldemort used this particular Dark Mark brand shortly after his resurrection to summon his other Death Eaters.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="In_the_sky">In the sky</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Dark_Mark?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: In the sky">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Dark_Mark?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: In the sky"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<dl><dd><div class="noprint"><i>Main article&#58; <a href="/wiki/Morsmordre" title="Morsmordre">Morsmordre</a></i></div><figure class="thumb tright show-info-icon" style="width: 238px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/f/f6/Dark_Mark_Over_Hogwarts.JPG/revision/latest?cb=20161206211841" class="image"><img alt="" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw%3D%3D" decoding="async" width="236" height="142" class="thumbimage lazyload" data-image-name="Dark Mark Over Hogwarts.JPG" data-image-key="Dark_Mark_Over_Hogwarts.JPG" data-src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/f/f6/Dark_Mark_Over_Hogwarts.JPG/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/236?cb=20161206211841" /></a> 	<noscript><a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/f/f6/Dark_Mark_Over_Hogwarts.JPG/revision/latest?cb=20161206211841" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/f/f6/Dark_Mark_Over_Hogwarts.JPG/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/236?cb=20161206211841" decoding="async" width="236" height="142" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="Dark Mark Over Hogwarts.JPG" data-image-key="Dark_Mark_Over_Hogwarts.JPG" data-src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/f/f6/Dark_Mark_Over_Hogwarts.JPG/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/236?cb=20161206211841" /></a></noscript> 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:Dark_Mark_Over_Hogwarts.JPG" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption">The Dark Mark above the <a href="/wiki/Astronomy_Tower" title="Astronomy Tower">Astronomy Tower</a></p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure></dd></dl>
<p>As a signature of their work, the <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a> can also use a spell to create an image of the Dark Mark in the sky. The incantation of this spell is <i>Morsmordre</i>. The appearance of the Mark in the sky is basically the same as the Mark on the forearm, but green.<sup id="cite_ref-GOF9_1-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF9-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Notable uses include in the woods during the <a href="/wiki/1994" title="1994">1994</a> <a href="/wiki/Quidditch_World_Cup" title="Quidditch World Cup">Quidditch World Cup</a> Final, when it was cast by <a href="/wiki/Bartemius_Crouch_Junior" title="Bartemius Crouch Junior">Barty Crouch Jnr</a>, and over the <a href="/wiki/Astronomy_Tower" title="Astronomy Tower">Astronomy Tower</a> of <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> by <a href="/wiki/Gibbon" title="Gibbon">Gibbon</a> in 1997. It normally appears where there has been a murder by the hands of Death Eaters or <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a>. Due to the terror Voldemort and his Death Eaters had brought to the world in the First Wizarding War, the Dark Mark being cast over a location brings deep fear to the community, even after Voldemort's first downfall.<sup id="cite_ref-GOF9_1-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF9-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>The other form of the Dark Mark has been seen when Voldemort touched the Dark Mark branded on an arm, a skull forms in the sky above and the alerted Death Eaters <a href="/wiki/Apparition" title="Apparition">Apparate</a> out of its mouth instead of a snake.<sup id="cite_ref-GOF33_3-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF33-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Dark_Mark_similarities">Dark Mark similarities</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Dark_Mark?veaction=edit&amp;section=4" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Dark Mark similarities">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Dark_Mark?action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Dark Mark similarities"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>During his studentship at Hogwarts, <a href="/wiki/Gilderoy_Lockhart" title="Gilderoy Lockhart">Gilderoy Lockhart</a> shot a holographic image of his own face like the Dark Mark into the air, in an attempt to garner attention.<sup id="cite_ref-pottermorebio_10-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-pottermorebio-10">&#91;10&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p><a href="/wiki/Fred_and_George_Weasley" title="Fred and George Weasley">Fred and George Weasley</a> created <a href="/wiki/Edible_Dark_Mark" title="Edible Dark Mark">Edible Dark Marks</a> for their joke shop, <a href="/wiki/Weasleys%27_Wizard_Wheezes" title="Weasleys&#39; Wizard Wheezes">Weasleys' Wizard Wheezes</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-HBPF_11-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBPF-11">&#91;11&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>When Harry mentioned that the <a href="/wiki/Dumbledore%27s_Army_coin" title="Dumbledore&#39;s Army coin">Dumbledore's Army coin</a> reminded him of the Death Eaters' brands, he incorrectly referred to the brand as a scar.
</p><p>Voldemort summoning his Death Eaters by touching their Dark Marks and vice versa seems to be the same, or at least a similar, magic to <a href="/wiki/Credence_Barebone" title="Credence Barebone">Credence Barebone</a> summoning <a href="/wiki/Gellert_Grindelwald" title="Gellert Grindelwald">Gellert Grindelwald</a> by touching the Deathly Hallows necklace Grindelwald gave him.
</p>

<ul><li>As the spells used to brand and conjure the Dark Mark were invented by <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a>, he may have based the spell on the <a href="/wiki/Protean_Charm" title="Protean Charm">Protean Charm</a>, as <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione Granger</a> mentioned in <a href="/wiki/1995" title="1995">1995</a> that the Dark Mark inspired her to use the <a href="/wiki/Protean_Charm" title="Protean Charm">Protean Charm</a> to create the <a href="/wiki/Dumbledore%27s_Army_coin" title="Dumbledore&#39;s Army coin">method of communication</a> used by <a href="/wiki/Dumbledore%27s_Army" title="Dumbledore&#39;s Army">Dumbledore's Army</a>.</li>
<li>In <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Half-Blood_Prince" title="Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince">Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince</a></i>, the Dark Mark is cast by <a href="/wiki/Gibbon" title="Gibbon">Gibbon</a> in order to lure Dumbledore to the <a href="/wiki/Astronomy_Tower" title="Astronomy Tower">Astronomy Tower</a>. In the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Half-Blood_Prince_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince (film)">film version</a>, the Mark is cast by <a href="/wiki/Bellatrix_Lestrange" title="Bellatrix Lestrange">Bellatrix Lestrange</a> in celebration after Dumbledore's death.</li>
<li>In the beginning of the fourth film, the skull and snake appear as a green glowing light, while in three later instances, the skull is moulded from the clouds.</li>
<li>In every film with the Dark Mark in the sky, Death Eaters are shown able to <a href="/wiki/Apparition" title="Apparition">Apparate</a> out of it in clouds of smoke.</li>
<li>At the near end of the fourth film, when Barty Jnr held Harry inside his office, he kept rubbing Harry's wound where Pettigrew cut him, using the blood to form a crude form of the Dark Mark.</li></ul>

<ul><li>In the books and in the <a href="/wiki/File:Backcover.jpg" title="File:Backcover.jpg">back cover of the Scholastic edition</a> of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Half-Blood_Prince" title="Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince">Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince</a></i>, the Dark Mark is depicted by <a href="/wiki/Mary_GrandPr%C3%A9" title="Mary GrandPré">Mary GrandPré</a> as being a group of green glowing stars, like a monstrous constellation. Otherwise in the film adaptations, they are either shaped from the clouds or made of glittering green light.</li>
<li>In the the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Half-Blood_Prince_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince (film)">film adaptation</a> of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Half-Blood_Prince" title="Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince">Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince</a></i>, Draco Malfoy openly displays his Dark Mark to Albus Dumbledore. In the novel, it is not verified that Draco received the Mark, though it was highly speculated by Harry Potter that it was what Draco showed <a href="/wiki/Borgin" title="Borgin">Borgin</a> as a form of intimidation.</li>
<li>In <i><a href="/wiki/LEGO_Harry_Potter:_Years_1-4" title="LEGO Harry Potter: Years 1-4">LEGO Harry Potter: Years 1-4</a></i>, during the Quidditch World Cup, you can see the shadow of Barty Crouch Jnr when the ring of fire comes, Death Eaters stay and attack; however, in the book and film they flee.</li>
<li>The Dark Mark has been used on both the <a href="/wiki/J._K._Rowling%27s_official_site" title="J. K. Rowling&#39;s official site">J. K. Rowling's official site</a> and  <i><a href="/wiki/Pottermore" title="Pottermore">Pottermore</a></i> to indicate content of a spoiler-inducing nature.</li>
<li>During the Second World War, members of the Nazis paramilitary organisation the Waffen-SS had their blood group tattooed on their inner left arm.  During the immediate aftermath of the war, this was used to identify fugitive SS men. This may be the inspiration for the Dark Mark.
<ul><li>When <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione Granger</a> remarked that once the Ministry started hunting down Death Eaters, <a href="/wiki/Sirius_Black" title="Sirius Black">Sirius Black</a>'s lack of a Dark Mark would be evidence that he is not a Death Eater, which meant that bearing a Dark Mark is sufficient evidence.</li></ul></li>
<li>Another potential inspiration is the ancient idea that when a man or woman made a deal with a incubus or succubus to become a witch, they gained a visible mark or blemish on their skin to mark them as one who had made a deal with a demon.</li></ul>
