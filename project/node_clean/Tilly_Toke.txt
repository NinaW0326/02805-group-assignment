<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Tilly_Toke?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Tilly_Toke?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Holidaying_in_Ilfracombe">Holidaying in Ilfracombe</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Tilly_Toke?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Holidaying in Ilfracombe">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Tilly_Toke?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Holidaying in Ilfracombe"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Tilly had been holidaying with her family in <a href="/wiki/Ilfracombe" title="Ilfracombe">Ilfracombe</a>, in <a href="/wiki/Devon" title="Devon">Devon</a> in <a href="/wiki/1932" title="1932">1932</a>. One day, a rogue <a href="/wiki/Common_Welsh_Green" title="Common Welsh Green">Common Welsh Green</a> <a href="/wiki/Dragon" title="Dragon">dragon</a> swooped down upon the beach, full of sunbathing <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggles</a>. Along with her family, Tilly fended off the attack, thus preventing any fatalities. The family then proceeded to perform "the largest batch of <a href="/wiki/Memory_Charm" title="Memory Charm">Memory Charms</a> this century" on the inhabitants of Ilfracombe.<sup id="cite_ref-FBaWtFT_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-FBaWtFT-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>The Toke family were all awarded <a href="/wiki/Order_of_Merlin" title="Order of Merlin">Order of Merlin</a>, First Class, for their heroic actions in Ilfracombe.<sup id="cite_ref-FBaWtFT_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-FBaWtFT-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Postmortem">Postmortem</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Tilly_Toke?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Postmortem">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Tilly_Toke?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Postmortem"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>In <a href="/wiki/1993" title="1993">1993</a>, a Portrait of Tilly was hung at the first-floor corridor of Hogwarts Castle, protecting a secret area. A password was needed to access it. The password to said portrait was <i>Dirigible</i>.<sup id="cite_ref-POAG_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-POAG-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Magical_abilities_and_skills">Magical abilities and skills</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Tilly_Toke?veaction=edit&amp;section=4" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Tilly_Toke?action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li><b><a href="/wiki/Charm" title="Charm">Charms</a></b>: Toke was shown to be highly proficient with charm-work, in particular with the advanced <a href="/wiki/Memory_Charm" title="Memory Charm">Memory Charm</a>, for which she used along with her family to erase all the memories of all the residents of <a href="/wiki/Ilfracombe" title="Ilfracombe">Ilfracombe</a> after the <a href="/wiki/Ilfracombe_Incident" title="Ilfracombe Incident">Ilfracombe Incident</a>, a remarkable <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magical</a> achievement which gained her recognition on a <a href="/wiki/Chocolate_Frog_Card" title="Chocolate Frog Card">Chocolate Frog Card</a> and gained her an <a href="/wiki/Order_of_Merlin" title="Order of Merlin">Order of Merlin</a> first class award, for upholding the <a href="/wiki/International_Statute_of_Wizarding_Secrecy" title="International Statute of Wizarding Secrecy">International Statute of Wizarding Secrecy</a>.</li></ul>
