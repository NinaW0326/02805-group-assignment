<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Adrian_Tutley?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Adrian_Tutley?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>In recent times, Tutley attended the <a href="/wiki/International_Symposium_of_Animagi" title="International Symposium of Animagi">International Symposium of Animagi</a>, and, considering himself one of the far more experienced attendees, was outraged when a team of fourteen-year old students from <a href="/wiki/Uagadou_School_of_Magic" title="Uagadou School of Magic">Uagadou School of Magic</a> led an exhibition of synchronised transforming into animals such as <a href="/wiki/Elephant" title="Elephant">elephants</a> and <a href="/wiki/Cheetah" title="Cheetah">cheetahs</a>. Tutley even went on to lodge a formal complaint with the <a href="/wiki/International_Confederation_of_Wizards" title="International Confederation of Wizards">International Confederation of Wizards</a> because of this.<sup id="cite_ref-PM_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PM-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Magical_abilities_and_skills">Magical abilities and skills</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Adrian_Tutley?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Adrian_Tutley?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li><b><a href="/wiki/Transfiguration" title="Transfiguration">Transfiguration</a></b>: Tutley was exceptionally proficient with the art of <a href="/wiki/Human_Transfiguration" title="Human Transfiguration">Human Transfiguration</a>, that he learned how to become an <a href="/wiki/Animagus" title="Animagus">Animagus</a>, allowing him to transform into a <a href="/wiki/Gerbil" title="Gerbil">gerbil</a> at will.</li></ul>
