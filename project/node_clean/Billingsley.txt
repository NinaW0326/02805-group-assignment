<b>Billingsley</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> who attended <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> during <a href="/wiki/Jacob%27s_sibling" title="Jacob&#39;s sibling">Jacob's sibling</a>'s time at school. He appeared to be a very popular student who was proficient in both <a href="/wiki/Transfiguration_(class)" title="Transfiguration (class)">Transfiguration</a> and <a href="/wiki/Charms_(class)" title="Charms (class)">Charms</a> classes, which many other students spoke about often.

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Billingsley?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Billingsley?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Billingsley started attending Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry in <a href="/wiki/1983" title="1983">1983</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-age_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-age-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> He became very well known for the many ways he <a href="/wiki/Transfiguration" title="Transfiguration">transfigured</a> objects, which was spoken about and often admired in <a href="/wiki/Transfiguration_(class)" title="Transfiguration (class)">Transfiguration</a> class by <a href="/wiki/Jacob%27s_sibling" title="Jacob&#39;s sibling">Jacob's sibling</a>'s year.
</p><p>Billingsley's many achievements include turning a satchel to a <a href="/wiki/Goat" title="Goat">goat</a>, the <a href="/wiki/Minerva_McGonagall" title="Minerva McGonagall">teacher</a>'s desk to a ball of yarn, and himself to a surfboard which made McGonagall very upset with him. In an unknown way, Billingsley's head was once trapped in an umbrella flower.<sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> He also <a href="/wiki/Levitation_Charm" title="Levitation Charm">levitated</a> himself and the staff was unable to get him down for some time.<sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>He was less skilled with charms-work, however. In his <a href="/wiki/First_year" title="First year">first year</a>, he recited an <a href="/wiki/Incantation" title="Incantation">incantation</a> incorrectly (presumably for the <a href="/wiki/Fire-Making_Spell" title="Fire-Making Spell">Fire-Making Spell</a>), and accidentally set <a href="/wiki/Fire" title="Fire">fire</a> to the <a href="/wiki/Boathouse" title="Boathouse">Boathouse</a>. He also once spilled some <a href="/wiki/Swelling_Solution" title="Swelling Solution">Swelling Solution</a> on the hands of <a href="/wiki/Potions_Master" title="Potions Master">Potions Master</a> <a href="/wiki/Severus_Snape" title="Severus Snape">Severus Snape</a>.
</p><p>In the <a href="/wiki/1988%E2%80%931989_school_year" title="1988–1989 school year">1988–1989 school year</a>, Billingsley accidentally <a href="/wiki/Transfiguration" title="Transfiguration">Transfigured</a> a <a href="/wiki/Ferret" title="Ferret">ferret</a> into a fountain instead of a feather duster. During the same year, <a href="/wiki/Andre_Egwu" title="Andre Egwu">Andre Egwu</a> noted that animals seemed to hate Billingsley.<sup id="cite_ref-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> He also performed the <a href="/wiki/Exploding_Charm" title="Exploding Charm">Exploding Charm</a> during breakfast, splattering <a href="/wiki/Egg" title="Egg">eggs</a> and <a href="/wiki/Bacon" title="Bacon">bacon</a> everywhere.<sup id="cite_ref-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Accoding <a href="/wiki/Charles_Weasley" title="Charles Weasley">Charlie Weasley</a>, sometime during <a href="/wiki/1989%E2%80%931990_school_year" title="1989–1990 school year">1989–1990 school year</a> Billingsley showed up to class in his jim jams.<sup id="cite_ref-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Magical_abilities_and_skills">Magical abilities and skills</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Billingsley?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Billingsley?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li><b><a href="/wiki/Charm" title="Charm">Charms</a></b>: Billingsley was proficient with charm-work, with his skills in this field of <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magic</a> often being discussed by other Hogwarts students. He was a high academic achiever in <a href="/wiki/Charms_(class)" title="Charms (class)">Charms class</a>, and was able to perform many charms successfully, albeit sometimes recklessly, such as the <a href="/wiki/Exploding_Charm" title="Exploding Charm">Exploding Charm</a>.</li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Transfiguration" title="Transfiguration">Transfiguration</a></b>: This highly advanced and difficult branch of <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magic</a> was Billingsley's main area of expertise, being reputed for his excellent skills in this field. He was capable of <a href="/wiki/Transforming_Spells" title="Transforming Spells">transforming</a> many objects, including numerous animals as well. He was known to be a top academic achiever in <a href="/wiki/Transfiguration_(class)" title="Transfiguration (class)">Transfiguration</a> class, testament to his talent.</li></ul>
