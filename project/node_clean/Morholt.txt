<b>Morholt</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Giant" title="Giant">Giant</a> from <a href="/wiki/Ireland" title="Ireland">Ireland</a>. He was the brother of the <a href="/wiki/Morholt%27s_brother" title="Morholt&#39;s brother">King of Ireland</a>. He wounded the Celtic hero, <a href="/wiki/Tristan" title="Tristan">Tristan</a> with a <a href="/wiki/Poison" title="Poison">poisoned</a> sword after Tristan tried to kill him.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Morholt?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Morholt?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>Morholt was based on the <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Morholt" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Morholt">figure in Celtic legend of the same name</a>.</li>
<li>Although Morholt's brother was the King of Ireland, Morholt would not have been a prince nor royalty as the High King was elected, as opposed to inheriting the position from a family member.</li></ul>
