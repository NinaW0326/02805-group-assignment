<b>Violet Tillyman</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch</a> who lived in the <a href="/wiki/19th_century" title="19th century">19th century</a>. In <a href="/wiki/1855" class="mw-redirect" title="1855">1855</a>, she was deemed to be missing after she left her home via the <a href="/wiki/Floo_Network" title="Floo Network">Floo Network</a>, but failed to reach her intended destination. She resurfaced twenty years later in what came to be known as "the most notorious case of accidental misdirection" in the history of the Floo Network.<sup id="cite_ref-PM_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PM-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Violet_Tillyman?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Violet_Tillyman?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Violet was married to a bullying wizard named <a href="/wiki/Albert_Tillyman" title="Albert Tillyman">Albert Tillyman</a>. One day in <a href="/wiki/1855" class="mw-redirect" title="1855">1855</a>, she had a terrible argument with him and afterward she <a href="/wiki/Floo_Network" title="Floo Network">leapt into the fireplace</a> in the living room of the home they shared. Tearfully, she declared that she wanted to go to <a href="/wiki/Violet_Tillyman%27s_mother" title="Violet Tillyman&#39;s mother">her mother's</a> <a href="/wiki/Violet_Tillyman%27s_mother%27s_house" title="Violet Tillyman&#39;s mother&#39;s house">house</a>, but the words did not come out clearly between her sobs and hiccoughs. Thus, she accidentally ended up in the home of <a href="/wiki/Myron_Otherhaus" title="Myron Otherhaus">Myron Otherhaus</a> in <a href="/wiki/Bury_St_Edmunds" title="Bury St Edmunds">Bury St Edmunds</a>, <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Suffolk" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Suffolk">Suffolk</a>. She was covered in ash and her face was red and tear-streaked, but nonetheless she and Myron instantly fell in love with each other.<sup id="cite_ref-PM_2-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PM-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Two weeks later, <a href="/wiki/Albert_Tillyman" title="Albert Tillyman">Albert</a> found that the house was getting dirty and thus decided it was time for Violet to come home. He took the <a href="/wiki/Floo_Network" title="Floo Network">Floo Network</a> to his mother-in-law's home. She claimed that her daughter had never arrived. Albert did not believe her at first, but after he raged and searched the house, he accepted that she was telling the truth.<sup id="cite_ref-PM_2-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PM-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>In an effort to find Violet, posters were put up and a series of articles were run in the <i><a href="/wiki/Daily_Prophet" title="Daily Prophet">Daily Prophet</a></i>, but to no avail. Violet remained missing. No one admitted to seeing her exit another fireplace or knowing of her whereabouts.<sup id="cite_ref-PM_2-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PM-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>For several months following the mysterious disappearance of Violet, the <a href="/wiki/Great_Britain" title="Great Britain">British</a> wizarding population feared using the <a href="/wiki/Floo_Network" title="Floo Network">Floo Network</a>, believing they might vanish into thin air as she apparently had. But as time passed and nobody else disappeared, people forgot about Violet's disappearance and resumed using the Floo Network. Albert did not forget however and never again travelled by Floo. He also learned <a href="/wiki/Cleaning_spell" title="Cleaning spell">cleaning spells</a> and <a href="/wiki/Darning_spell" title="Darning spell">darning spells</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-PM_2-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PM-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Over the next twenty years, Violet lived in seclusion with Myron in Bury St Edmunds, giving birth to <a href="/wiki/Violet_Tillyman%27s_children" title="Violet Tillyman&#39;s children">seven children</a>. Finally, after Albert's death in <a href="/wiki/1875" class="mw-redirect" title="1875">1875</a>, she chose to resurface, revealing her story to the public.<sup id="cite_ref-PM_2-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PM-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Violet_Tillyman?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Violet_Tillyman?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>Wizarding laws pertaining to <a href="/wiki/Marriage" title="Marriage">marriage</a> and divorce are unknown. It is possible that wizarding marriages can be voluntarily dissolved by one party without the knowledge of the other or the involvement of an official agency, like the <a href="/wiki/Ministry_of_Magic" title="Ministry of Magic">Ministry of Magic</a> and thus that Violet was able to secretly divorce Albert on her own and wed Myron. In addition, it is possible that Violet was <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Declared_death_in_absentia" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Declared death in absentia">declared dead <i>in absentia</i></a> at some point after her disappearance, thus dissolving her marriage to Albert and allowing her to marry Myron. However, it is also possible that Myron and Violet lived together without ever marrying.</li></ul>
