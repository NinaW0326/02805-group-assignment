<b>Loxias</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Dark_wizard" class="mw-redirect" title="Dark wizard">Dark wizard</a> who became Master of the <a href="/wiki/Elder_Wand" title="Elder Wand">Elder Wand</a> after he murdered <a href="/wiki/Barnabas_Deverill" title="Barnabas Deverill">Barnabas Deverill</a>. Loxias gave it the name of <i>"<a href="/wiki/Elder_Wand" title="Elder Wand">the Deathstick</a>"</i>, and used it to vanquish his enemies. It is unknown who killed him, as—according to Dumbledore—many claimed to have finished him off, including <a href="/wiki/Loxias%27s_mother" title="Loxias&#39;s mother">his own mother</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-TBB_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-TBB-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> but from <a href="/wiki/Xenophilius_Lovegood" title="Xenophilius Lovegood">Xenophilius Lovegood</a>'s understanding of history, either <a href="/wiki/Arcus" title="Arcus">Arcus</a> or <a href="/wiki/Livius" title="Livius">Livius</a> seems to have done it, and thus became the next master of the Elder Wand.<sup id="cite_ref-DH21_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH21-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
