<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_Muggle_driver?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_Muggle_driver?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>In <a href="/wiki/1992" title="1992">1992</a>, he witnessed <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> and <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ronald Weasley</a> leave <a href="/wiki/King%27s_Cross_Station" title="King&#39;s Cross Station">King's Cross Station</a> in the flying <a href="/wiki/Flying_Ford_Anglia" title="Flying Ford Anglia">Ford Anglia</a> on <a href="/wiki/1_September" title="1 September">1 September</a>, <a href="/wiki/1992" title="1992">1992</a>. He was sitting in his cab, and was stunned by what he saw, leaning out his window to get a better look.<sup id="cite_ref-COSF_1-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COSF-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>The <a href="/wiki/Evening_Prophet" title="Evening Prophet">Evening Prophet</a> later reported on the <a href="/wiki/FLYING_FORD_ANGLIA_MYSTIFIES_MUGGLES" title="FLYING FORD ANGLIA MYSTIFIES MUGGLES">incident</a>, stating that the flying car had been seen by <i>"no less than seven Muggles."</i><sup id="cite_ref-COSF_1-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COSF-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_Muggle_driver?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_Muggle_driver?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>This individual, along with <a href="/wiki/Angus_Fleet" title="Angus Fleet">Angus Fleet</a>, was presumably one of the <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggles</a> that <a href="/wiki/Molly_Weasley" title="Molly Weasley">Molly Weasley</a> mentioned to her son <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ron</a>, saying that he caused <a href="/wiki/Arthur_Weasley" title="Arthur Weasley">Arthur Weasley</a> to face an inquiry at <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">work</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-CS6_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-CS6-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup></li></ul>
