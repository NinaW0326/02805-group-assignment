<b>Leticia Somnolens</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Middle_Ages" title="Middle Ages">Medieval</a> <a href="/wiki/Hag" title="Hag">hag</a> known for conspiring against a <a href="/wiki/Unidentified_Princess" title="Unidentified Princess">princess</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-POAG_1-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-POAG-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> and apparently served as a source of inspiration for the <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggle</a> fairy tale, <a href="/wiki/Sleeping_Beauty" title="Sleeping Beauty">Sleeping Beauty</a>.

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Leticia_Somnolens?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Leticia_Somnolens?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>A spiteful and vicious creature, Somnolens was jealous of <a href="/wiki/Unidentified_Princess" title="Unidentified Princess">the daughter</a> of <a href="/wiki/Unidentified_King" title="Unidentified King">the local King</a> and tainted a spindle with the <a href="/wiki/Draught_of_Living_Death" title="Draught of Living Death">Draught of Living Death</a>. She then tricked the young princess into pricking her finger on it, sending her into a deep sleep. Her plan was foiled, however, by a <a href="/wiki/Unidentified_young_wizard" title="Unidentified young wizard">young wizard</a> who smeared his lips with <a href="/wiki/Wiggenweld_Potion" title="Wiggenweld Potion">Wiggenweld Potion</a>, and then kissed the princess, curing her and bringing her out of the sleep.<sup id="cite_ref-POAG_1-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-POAG-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
