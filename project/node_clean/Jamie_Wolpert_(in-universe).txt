<b>Jamie Wolpert</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggle</a> journalist who, in the summer of <a href="/wiki/1996" title="1996">1996</a>, wrote the front-page article about the collapse of the <a href="/wiki/Millennium_Bridge" title="Millennium Bridge">Millennium Bridge</a> for <i><a href="/wiki/The_Guardian" title="The Guardian">The Guardian</a></i>.<sup id="cite_ref-HPLE_2-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HPLE-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>

<ul><li>This character was named after <a href="/wiki/Jamie_Wolpert" title="Jamie Wolpert">Jamie Wolpert</a>, who worked behind-the-scenes as an assistant to <a href="/wiki/David_Yates" title="David Yates">David Yates</a> on <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Order_of_the_Phoenix_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix (film)">Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix</a></i> and <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Half-Blood_Prince_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince (film)">Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince</a></i>, and as a book consultant on <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Deathly_Hallows:_Part_1" title="Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 1">Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 1</a></i> and <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Deathly_Hallows:_Part_2" title="Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 2">Part 2</a></i>. He also had a cameo as a <a href="/wiki/Unidentified_newspaper_vendor" title="Unidentified newspaper vendor"><i>Daily Prophet</i> newspaper vendor</a> at the <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">Ministry of Magic</a> in <i>Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix</i>.<sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup></li></ul>
