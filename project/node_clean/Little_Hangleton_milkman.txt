<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Little_Hangleton_milkman?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Little_Hangleton_milkman?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>This individual replaces the role of&#160;<a href="/wiki/Frank_Bryce" title="Frank Bryce">Frank Bryce</a> in <i><a href="/wiki/LEGO_Harry_Potter:_Years_1-4" title="LEGO Harry Potter: Years 1-4">LEGO Harry Potter: Years 1-4</a></i>.<sup id="cite_ref-LEGOY1_1-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-LEGOY1-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> Despite this, it is highly likely that he still existed <a href="/wiki/Canon" title="Canon">canonically</a> as the milkman of Little Hangleton.</li>
<li>Instead of noticing the lights on on the second floor, the milkman notices empty <a href="/wiki/Milk" title="Milk">milk</a> bottles on the front steps of the <a href="/wiki/Riddle_House" title="Riddle House">Riddle House</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-LEGOY1_1-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-LEGOY1-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li>His death at the hands of <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a> cannot be considered canonical, as no echo of him emerges from Voldemort's wand during the <a href="/wiki/Duel_in_Little_Hangleton" title="Duel in Little Hangleton">Duel in Little Hangleton</a>, and further in canon, it was Frank Bryce who was murdered during&#160;that incident.<sup id="cite_ref-LEGOY1_1-10" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-LEGOY1-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>&#160;</li></ul>
