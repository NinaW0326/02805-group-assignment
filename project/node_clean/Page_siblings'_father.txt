<b>Mr Page</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggle</a> who married <a href="/wiki/Page_siblings%27_mother" title="Page siblings&#39; mother">a witch</a> and had three children. They had a <a href="/wiki/Daniel_Page%27s_sister" title="Daniel Page&#39;s sister">a daughter</a> and two sons, <a href="/wiki/Squib" title="Squib">squib</a> <a href="/wiki/Reggie_Page" title="Reggie Page">Reggie</a> and <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> <a href="/wiki/Daniel_Page" title="Daniel Page">Daniel</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-MA_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-MA-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
Mr Page was involved in illicit situations, and he involved their elder son in such activities. They were eventually caught by Muggle police, and the wife tried to use her magic to help the two escape. She frightened the police and violated the <a href="/wiki/International_Statute_of_Wizarding_Secrecy" title="International Statute of Wizarding Secrecy">International Statute of Wizarding Secrecy</a> in doing so.<sup id="cite_ref-MA_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-MA-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
Her efforts didn't help, and she was sentenced to <a href="/wiki/Azkaban" title="Azkaban">Azkaban</a> while Mr Page and their son were taken to a Muggle prison. The incident was put in a Muggle newspaper under the heading, "The Bloody Pages". Their younger son, Daniel was put under the guardianship of their daughter.<sup id="cite_ref-MA_1-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-MA-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
