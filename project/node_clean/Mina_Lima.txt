<b>Mina Lima</b> was presumably a famous <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch</a>. <i><a href="/wiki/The_Quibbler" title="The Quibbler">The Quibbler</a></i> ran an article in <a href="/wiki/1996" title="1996">1996</a> which claimed that she was the same person as <a href="/wiki/Stubby_Boardman" title="Stubby Boardman">Stubby Boardman</a>. However, the veracity of this claim is doubtful, given the fact that the magazine published the exact same claim regarding <a href="/wiki/Sirius_Black" title="Sirius Black">Sirius Black</a> the <a href="/wiki/1995" title="1995">year</a> before.<sup id="cite_ref-HBPF_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBPF-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Mina_Lima?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Mina_Lima?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>This character's name is presumably a tribute to the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_films" class="mw-redirect" title="Harry Potter films"><i>Harry Potter</i> film</a> graphic artists <a href="/wiki/Miraphora_Mina" title="Miraphora Mina">Miraphora Mina</a> and <a href="/wiki/Eduardo_Lima" title="Eduardo Lima">Eduardo Lima</a>. Their company <a href="/wiki/MinaLima_Design" title="MinaLima Design">MinaLima Design</a> has continued to make beautiful design work and their blog features all of their inspirations and design ideas.</li></ul>
