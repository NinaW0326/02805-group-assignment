<p><b>Travers</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Great_Britain" title="Great Britain">British</a> <a href="/wiki/Dark_Wizard" title="Dark Wizard">Dark Wizard</a> and a <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eater</a>. He fought in the <a href="/wiki/First_Wizarding_War" title="First Wizarding War">First Wizarding War</a>, during which he helped murdered <a href="/wiki/Marlene_McKinnon" title="Marlene McKinnon">Marlene McKinnon</a> and her <a href="/wiki/McKinnon_family" title="McKinnon family">family</a>, and was imprisoned for committing this crime.<sup id="cite_ref-GOF30_4-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF30-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> Travers escaped from <a href="/wiki/Azkaban" title="Azkaban">Azkaban</a> after <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Return_of_Lord_Voldemort" title="Return of Lord Voldemort">return</a> in <a href="/wiki/1995" title="1995">1995</a> and fought in several battles of the <a href="/wiki/Second_Wizarding_War" title="Second Wizarding War">Second Wizarding War</a>, including the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_the_Seven_Potters" title="Battle of the Seven Potters">Battle of the Seven Potters</a>, the <a href="/wiki/Ambush_at_the_Lovegood_House" title="Ambush at the Lovegood House">Ambush at the Lovegood House</a> and the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a>. His ultimate fate is uncertain.
</p>

<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_life">Early life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Travers?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Early life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Travers?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Early life"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Travers was born into the <a href="/wiki/Travers_family" title="Travers family">Travers family</a>, one of the <a href="/wiki/Sacred_Twenty-Eight" title="Sacred Twenty-Eight">Sacred Twenty-Eight</a> families published in the <i><a href="/wiki/Pure-Blood_Directory" title="Pure-Blood Directory">Pure-Blood Directory</a></i> in the <a href="/wiki/1930s" title="1930s">1930s</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="First_Wizarding_War">First Wizarding War</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Travers?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: First Wizarding War">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Travers?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: First Wizarding War"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>During the First Wizarding War, Travers murdered the family of <a href="/wiki/Marlene_McKinnon" title="Marlene McKinnon">Marlene McKinnon</a>, according to <a href="/wiki/Igor_Karkaroff" title="Igor Karkaroff">Igor Karkaroff</a>. According to Karkaroff, Travers was among Lord Voldemort's most important supporters.<sup id="cite_ref-GOF30_4-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF30-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> He could not escape imprisonment and was sentenced to <a href="/wiki/Azkaban" title="Azkaban">Azkaban</a> sometime in, or prior to, <a href="/wiki/1981" title="1981">1981</a> for this crime.
</p>

</p><p>He was apparently arrested at some point afterwards, though he escaped again in the <a href="/wiki/Azkaban_escapes" title="Azkaban escapes">1997 mass break-out</a> with <a href="/wiki/Stanley_Shunpike" title="Stanley Shunpike">Stan Shunpike</a> that was hushed up by the <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">British Ministry of Magic</a> as <a href="/wiki/Kingsley_Shacklebolt" title="Kingsley Shacklebolt">Kingsley Shacklebolt</a> said that Travers should be in Azkaban.<sup id="cite_ref-DH5_6-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH5-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup> On <a href="/wiki/27_July" title="27 July">27 July</a>, 1997 Travers participated in the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_the_Seven_Potters" title="Battle of the Seven Potters">Battle of the Seven Potters</a>, pursuing Hermione Granger, who was disguised as <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Kingsley_Shacklebolt" title="Kingsley Shacklebolt">Kingsley Shacklebolt</a>, who <a href="/wiki/Curse" title="Curse">cursed</a> him, causing his hood to fall back revealing to Shacklebolt that he had broken out of prison.<sup id="cite_ref-DH5_6-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH5-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Before the beginning of August of the same year, Travers was convicted again for his <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eater</a> activities.<sup id="cite_ref-DHvg_pt1_7-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DHvg_pt1-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<div class="quote">

<p>On <a href="/wiki/1_August" title="1 August">1 August</a>, 1997, the <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">British Ministry of Magic</a> was <a href="/wiki/Fall_of_the_Ministry_of_Magic" title="Fall of the Ministry of Magic">taken over</a> by the <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a> and <a href="/wiki/Pius_Thicknesse" title="Pius Thicknesse">Pius Thicknesse</a> (under the influence of the <a href="/wiki/Imperius_Curse" title="Imperius Curse">Imperius Curse</a>) was made Minister for Magic.<sup id="cite_ref-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Travers was then released from <a href="/wiki/Azkaban" title="Azkaban">Azkaban</a> when the Death Eater-led Ministry hosted up a mass breakout.<sup id="cite_ref-DHvg_pt1_7-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DHvg_pt1-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Now falsely cleared, Travers went on to work at the Ministry of Magic (at the <a href="/wiki/Department_of_Magical_Law_Enforcement" title="Department of Magical Law Enforcement">Department of Magical Law Enforcement</a>), as a superior to <a href="/wiki/Mafalda_Hopkirk" title="Mafalda Hopkirk">Mafalda Hopkirk</a>. He also seemed to have some involvement with the recently created <a href="/wiki/Muggle-Born_Registration_Commission" title="Muggle-Born Registration Commission">Muggle-Born Registration Commission</a>, as he got to choose which Ministry employee could act as a stenographer for the Muggle-born trials.<sup id="cite_ref-DH13_9-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH13-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Battle_of_Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Travers?veaction=edit&amp;section=9" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Battle of Hogwarts">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Travers?action=edit&amp;section=9" title="Edit section: Battle of Hogwarts"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<p>On <a href="/wiki/2_May" title="2 May">2 May</a>, <a href="/wiki/1998" title="1998">1998</a> Travers took part in the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a>. During the conflict, Travers fought <a href="/wiki/Parvati_Patil" title="Parvati Patil">Parvati Patil</a> and <a href="/wiki/Dean_Thomas" title="Dean Thomas">Dean Thomas</a> with the help of <a href="/wiki/Antonin_Dolohov" title="Antonin Dolohov">Dolohov</a> and another Death Eater.<sup id="cite_ref-DH32_12-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH32-12">&#91;12&#93;</a></sup> His ultimate fate is uncertain though it's likely that he was either killed in battle, imprisoned in <a href="/wiki/Azkaban" title="Azkaban">Azkaban</a> for his crimes, or on the run from the law, following the <a href="/wiki/Voldemort%27s_Last_Stand" title="Voldemort&#39;s Last Stand">defeat</a> of Voldemort and the Death Eaters.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Physical_appearance">Physical appearance</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Travers?veaction=edit&amp;section=10" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Physical appearance">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Travers?action=edit&amp;section=10" title="Edit section: Physical appearance"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Travers was described as a tall, thin <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> with a crown of bushy grey hair, a long sharp nose, and a cool voice.<sup id="cite_ref-hair_2-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-hair-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> As a Death Eater, he bore the <a href="/wiki/Dark_Mark" title="Dark Mark">Dark Mark</a> on his left inner forearm, which had faded to a mere scar since Voldemort's death.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Personality_and_traits">Personality and traits</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Travers?veaction=edit&amp;section=11" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Personality and traits">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Travers?action=edit&amp;section=11" title="Edit section: Personality and traits"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Travers was a <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eater</a>, obsessed with <a href="/wiki/Pure-blood_supremacy" title="Pure-blood supremacy">blood purity</a>. In <a href="/wiki/1998" title="1998">1998</a>, he expressed his disdain towards <a href="/wiki/Muggle-born" title="Muggle-born">Muggle-born</a> <a href="/wiki/Wandless" title="Wandless">beggars</a>, whom he laughed at (going as far as calling them "it"), as well as <a href="/wiki/Goblin" title="Goblin">goblins</a>, whom he didn't like to associate with. He was also something of a xenophobe: when he was met with an apparently foreign wizard, Dragomir Despard (in truth <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ron Weasley</a>), Travers didn't shake hands with him with his entire hand, but instead just used two of his fingers, acting as though he feared he would be soiled.<sup id="cite_ref-DH26_11-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH26-11">&#91;11&#93;</a></sup> His hatred towards Muggles lead him to hate <a href="/wiki/Blood_traitor" title="Blood traitor">blood traitors</a> as well, as he brutally murdered a member of the Order of the Phoenix and her entire family. He seemed quite calm and composed as an individual, especially when compared to more mentally unstable Death Eaters such as <a href="/wiki/Bellatrix_Lestrange" title="Bellatrix Lestrange">Bellatrix Lestrange</a> or <a href="/wiki/Selwyn" title="Selwyn">Selwyn</a>. He was willing to be social with other Death Eaters shown when he greeted Bellatrix Lestrange (actually a disguised <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione Granger</a>) and spoke with her on their way to <a href="/wiki/Gringotts_Wizarding_Bank" title="Gringotts Wizarding Bank">Gringotts</a>, though he was also suspicious of her presence and continued to watch her. He was also offended when Bellatrix (again Hermione) suggested that he did not have as much of Lord Voldemort's favour as she, showing that he desired his master's approval.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Magical_abilities_and_skills">Magical abilities and skills</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Travers?veaction=edit&amp;section=12" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Travers?action=edit&amp;section=12" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li><b><a href="/wiki/Dark_Arts" title="Dark Arts">Dark Arts</a></b>: Being a <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eater</a>, Travers was highly talented and well-versed in dark magic, being an accomplished <a href="/wiki/Dark_Wizard" title="Dark Wizard">Dark Wizard</a>. He would have been very capable of casting multiple <a href="/wiki/Dark_charm" title="Dark charm">dark charms</a> and most likely the <a href="/wiki/Unforgivable_Curses" title="Unforgivable Curses">Unforgivable Curses</a>.</li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Duelling" title="Duelling">Duelling</a></b>: Being a high ranking Death Eater, Travers was a competent duellist. During the <a href="/wiki/First_Wizarding_War" title="First Wizarding War">First Wizarding War</a> he took part in the murder of the <a href="/wiki/McKinnon_family" title="McKinnon family">McKinnon family</a>, who <a href="/wiki/Rubeus_Hagrid" title="Rubeus Hagrid">Rubeus Hagrid</a> described as some of the best wizards and witches of their time. He was however unable to overcome <a href="/wiki/Kingsley_Shacklebolt" title="Kingsley Shacklebolt">Kingsley Shacklebolt</a> while flying during the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_the_Seven_Potters" title="Battle of the Seven Potters">Battle of the Seven Potters</a> and was injured in the process. He also took part in the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a> where he was briefly seen duelling against <a href="/wiki/Parvati_Patil" title="Parvati Patil">Parvati Patil</a>.</li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Charm" title="Charm">Charms</a></b>: Travers was shown to be proficient in charm-work, as he capable of casting the rather advanced <a href="/wiki/Human-presence-revealing_Spell" title="Human-presence-revealing Spell">Human-presence-revealing Spell</a> in order to reveal the presence of <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> during the <a href="/wiki/Ambush_at_the_Lovegood_House" title="Ambush at the Lovegood House">Ambush at the Lovegood House</a> in <a href="/wiki/1997" title="1997">1997</a>.</li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Flight" title="Flight">Flying</a></b>: Travers was furthermore an accomplished <a href="/wiki/Broomstick" title="Broomstick">broomstick</a> flyer, as he took part in the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_the_Seven_Potters" title="Battle of the Seven Potters">Battle of the Seven Potters</a>, in which he fought members of the <a href="/wiki/Order_of_the_Phoenix" title="Order of the Phoenix">Order of the Phoenix</a> whilst flying.</li></ul>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Possessions">Possessions</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Travers?veaction=edit&amp;section=13" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Possessions">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Travers?action=edit&amp;section=13" title="Edit section: Possessions"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li><b><a href="/wiki/Wand" title="Wand">Wand</a></b>: Travers's wand was of unknown length, <a href="/wiki/Wand_wood" title="Wand wood">wood</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Wand_core" title="Wand core">core</a> material. He used it to cast the <i><a href="/wiki/Human-presence-revealing_spell" class="mw-redirect" title="Human-presence-revealing spell">Human-presence-revealing spell</a></i> to detect the presence of <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> during the <a href="/wiki/Ambush_at_the_Lovegood_House" title="Ambush at the Lovegood House">ambush at the Lovegood House</a> in 1998.<sup id="cite_ref-DH21_10-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH21-10">&#91;10&#93;</a></sup> A <a href="/wiki/Wandless" title="Wandless">convicted Muggle-born witch</a> pleaded Travers to lend her his wand to prove she was a witch, but he refused and cruelly mocked her.<sup id="cite_ref-DH26_11-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH26-11">&#91;11&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Gringotts_Wizarding_Bank" title="Gringotts Wizarding Bank">Gringotts</a> vault</b>: It is likely that Travers had a vault at <a href="/wiki/Gringotts_Wizarding_Bank" title="Gringotts Wizarding Bank">Gringotts Wizarding Bank</a>, because that is where he was going when he met "<a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Bellatrix Lestrange</a>", actually Hermione, "<a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Dragomir Despard</a>", actually Ron (both in <a href="/wiki/Polyjuice_Potion" title="Polyjuice Potion">disguise</a>), and Harry and <a href="/wiki/Griphook" title="Griphook">Griphook</a> under the <a href="/wiki/Cloak_of_Invisibility" title="Cloak of Invisibility">Invisibility Cloak</a>, in <a href="/wiki/Diagon_Alley" title="Diagon Alley">Diagon Alley</a>, during <a href="/wiki/1998" title="1998">1998</a>. He was also seen holding a key. It is unknown if this is his family vault or his own personal one, though as he comes from an old Pure-Blood family the former is most likely.</li></ul>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Travers?veaction=edit&amp;section=14" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Travers?action=edit&amp;section=14" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p><i>Travers</i> is an English and French surname that described a man who lived near a bridge or ford, or occasionally as an occupational name for the collector of tolls at such a location. It is derived from the Old French verb <i>traverser</i>, meaning "to cross"<sup id="cite_ref-13" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-13">&#91;13&#93;</a></sup>.
</p><p>Travers also shares a surname with the writer <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/P._L._Travers" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:P. L. Travers">P. L. Travers</a>, author of the <i><a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mary_Poppins" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Mary Poppins">Mary Poppins</a></i> books: J. K. Rowling is an admirer of P. L. Travers and followed Travers in having her books published under her first two initials and surname rather than her full name.<sup id="cite_ref-14" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-14">&#91;14&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
