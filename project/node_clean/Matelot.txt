<div class="mw-parser-output"><p><i>The Marvellous Matelot'</i> was a performer who had a show on at the <a href="/wiki/Brobeck_Theatre" title="Brobeck Theatre">Brobeck Theatre</a> in <a href="/wiki/1927" title="1927">1927</a>. The two and a half hour show was on each day from Monday to Thursday with performances in the afternoon and evening, with all seats reserved.<sup id="cite_ref-AM_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-AM-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
