The <b>Educational Office</b> is a division of the <a href="/wiki/International_Confederation_of_Wizards" title="International Confederation of Wizards">International Confederation of Wizards</a> responsible for overseeing <a href="/wiki/Wizarding_school" title="Wizarding school">wizarding schools</a> around the world.<sup id="cite_ref-PM_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PM-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> The eleven long-established and prestigious wizarding schools worldwide are registered in the Office. Those wishing to know if there was an approved <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magical</a> school in their region could send an <a href="/wiki/Owl" title="Owl">owl</a> to the Educational Office.<sup id="cite_ref-PM_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PM-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
