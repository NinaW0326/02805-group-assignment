This <b>ghost</b> resided in <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a>. In <a href="/wiki/1992" title="1992">1992</a> during the <a href="/wiki/Hallowe%27en" title="Hallowe&#39;en">Hallowe'en</a> <a href="/wiki/Hallowe%27en_feast" class="mw-redirect" title="Hallowe&#39;en feast">feast</a> the <a href="/wiki/Ghost" title="Ghost">ghosts</a> had a reenactment of <a href="/wiki/Honorific" title="Honorific">Sir</a> <a href="/wiki/Nicholas_de_Mimsy-Porpington" title="Nicholas de Mimsy-Porpington">Nicholas de Mimsy-Porpington</a>'s death in <a href="/wiki/1492" class="mw-redirect" title="1492">1492</a>, and this particular ghost had the honour of playing <a href="/wiki/Nicholas_de_Mimsy-Porpington%27s_executioner" title="Nicholas de Mimsy-Porpington&#39;s executioner">the executioner</a> of Sir Nicholas.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
