<p><b>Zacharias Smith</b> (b. c. <a href="/wiki/1979" title="1979">1979</a>/<a href="/wiki/1981" title="1981">1981</a>) was a <a href="/wiki/Great_Britain" title="Great Britain">British</a> <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> who attended <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a>, who was <a href="/wiki/Sorting_ceremony" title="Sorting ceremony">sorted</a> into and a member of <a href="/wiki/Hufflepuff" title="Hufflepuff">Hufflepuff</a> <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Houses" title="Hogwarts Houses">House</a>. He was a <a href="/wiki/Chaser" title="Chaser">Chaser</a> on the <a href="/wiki/Hufflepuff_Quidditch_team" title="Hufflepuff Quidditch team">Hufflepuff Quidditch team</a> and a member of <a href="/wiki/Dumbledore%27s_Army" title="Dumbledore&#39;s Army">Dumbledore's Army</a>, an organisation taught and led by <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP16_9-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP16-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup> In <a href="/wiki/1998" title="1998">1998</a> he abandoned the D.A. instead of fighting with them during the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-DH31_8-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH31-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p><a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ronald Weasley</a> detested him as soon as they met, mostly because of his insolent and cynical attitude while talking to the <a href="/wiki/Trio" title="Trio">trio</a> and his disloyalty towards <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> and Hogwarts.
</p>

<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_life">Early life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Zacharias_Smith?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Early life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Zacharias_Smith?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Early life"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Zacharias was born into the <a href="/wiki/Smith_family" title="Smith family">Smith family</a>, most likely between 1979 and 1981 in Great Britain or Ireland.
</p>
<h3><span id="Education_at_Hogwarts_(1990s)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="Education_at_Hogwarts_.281990s.29">Education at Hogwarts <span class="template-c">(1990s)</span></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Zacharias_Smith?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Education at Hogwarts (1990s)">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Zacharias_Smith?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Education at Hogwarts (1990s)"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_years">Early years</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Zacharias_Smith?veaction=edit&amp;section=4" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Early years">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Zacharias_Smith?action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Early years"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<p>Zacharias probably started his education at Hogwarts in either <a href="/wiki/1991" title="1991">1991</a> or <a href="/wiki/1992" title="1992">1992</a>. During the <a href="/wiki/Welcoming_Feast" title="Welcoming Feast">Welcoming Feast</a>, Zacharias was <a href="/wiki/Sorting_ceremony" title="Sorting ceremony">sorted</a> into the house of <a href="/wiki/Hufflepuff" title="Hufflepuff">Hufflepuff</a> and it is possible he shared a dormitory with <a href="/wiki/Ernest_Macmillan" title="Ernest Macmillan">Ernie Macmillan</a>, <a href="/wiki/Justin_Finch-Fletchley" title="Justin Finch-Fletchley">Justin Finch-Fletchley</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Wayne_Hopkins" title="Wayne Hopkins">Wayne Hopkins</a>. Zacharias did not fit the normal Hufflepuff sorting, as he showed selfishness, disloyalty, and cowardice, while most Hufflepuffs are  loyal and kind.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Physical_appearance">Physical appearance</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Zacharias_Smith?veaction=edit&amp;section=8" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Physical appearance">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Zacharias_Smith?action=edit&amp;section=8" title="Edit section: Physical appearance"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Zacharias Smith was described as a tall, skinny blond boy with an upturned nose.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP16_9-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP16-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Personality_and_traits">Personality and traits</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Zacharias_Smith?veaction=edit&amp;section=9" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Personality and traits">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Zacharias_Smith?action=edit&amp;section=9" title="Edit section: Personality and traits"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<div class="quote">
<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix">"<i>That Zacharias bloke's a wart.</i>"</span></dd>
<dd>&#8212;Zacharias's unpleasant personality<sup class="noprint"><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Order_of_the_Phoenix" title="Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix">[src]</a></sup></dd>
<dd></dd></dl></div>
<p>Zacharias Smith seemed to generally be a rather unpleasant person and was seen as overly critical of <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Dumbledore%27s_Army" title="Dumbledore&#39;s Army">D.A.</a><sup id="cite_ref-OOTP16_9-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP16-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup> He was also pushy and insensitive in questioning <a href="/wiki/Ginevra_Weasley" title="Ginevra Weasley">Ginny Weasley</a> and rude and biased in his <a href="/wiki/Quidditch" title="Quidditch">Quidditch</a> commentary.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP14_14-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP14-14">&#91;14&#93;</a></sup> He was obnoxious, condescending, pessimistic, and a hypocritical ingrate. He failed to see flaws in himself and was constantly openly insulting people or pointing out exaggerated facts or opinions about said people. When he was part of <a href="/wiki/Dumbledore%27s_Army" title="Dumbledore&#39;s Army">Dumbledore's Army</a>, he obnoxiously criticised and challenged Harry's competence as a teacher as well as a wizard — he didn't seem to realise that he himself was doing terribly in general, as seen by his struggling with the <a href="/wiki/Disarming_Charm" title="Disarming Charm">Disarming Charm</a> (though this might be due to the Weasley Twins' intervention).<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP18_6-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP18-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup> The main reason he attended the D.A. recruitment meeting was to hear details regarding <a href="/wiki/Cedric_Diggory" title="Cedric Diggory">Cedric Diggory</a>'s death; even though Harry made it clear the meeting was not about such topics and that he has no intention of speaking of it, Smith pressed on the matter until Harry bluntly told him to stay down or leave.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP16_9-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP16-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Aside from this lack of fair play, Smith also showed cowardice and a lack of the loyalty uncharacteristic of <a href="/wiki/Hufflepuff" title="Hufflepuff">Hufflepuff</a> house in fleeing, instead of fighting for his school and fellow students in their time of greatest need. He was so selfish that he went as far as to forcefully rush through <a href="/wiki/First_year" title="First year">first years</a> in his great haste to escape.<sup id="cite_ref-DH31_8-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH31-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup> He did not stay for the late headmaster <a href="/wiki/Funeral_of_Albus_Dumbledore" title="Funeral of Albus Dumbledore">Dumbledore's funeral</a>, instead leaving the moment his "haughty-looking" father came to pick him up.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP30_17-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP30-17">&#91;17&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Magical_abilities_and_skills">Magical abilities and skills</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Zacharias_Smith?veaction=edit&amp;section=10" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Zacharias_Smith?action=edit&amp;section=10" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li><b><a href="/wiki/Flight" title="Flight">Flying</a></b>: Zacharias joined the <a href="/wiki/Hufflepuff_Quidditch_team" title="Hufflepuff Quidditch team">Hufflepuff Quidditch team</a>, playing as a <a href="/wiki/Chaser" title="Chaser">Chaser</a>, implying he was a reasonably skilled and talented <a href="/wiki/Broomstick" title="Broomstick">broomstick</a> flyer and <a href="/wiki/Quidditch" title="Quidditch">Quidditch</a> player.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP26_12-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP26-12">&#91;12&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Defence_Against_the_Dark_Arts" title="Defence Against the Dark Arts">Defence Against the Dark Arts</a></b>: In <a href="/wiki/1995" title="1995">1995</a>, he reluctantly and cynically joined <a href="/wiki/Dumbledore%27s_Army" title="Dumbledore&#39;s Army">Dumbledore's Army</a> and therefore would have learned to cast various defensive and offensive <a href="/wiki/Spell" title="Spell">spells</a> and how to <a href="/wiki/Duelling" title="Duelling">duel</a> with <a href="/wiki/Martial_magic" title="Martial magic">martial magic</a>, under the expertise teaching of <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>. However, his overall performance during the lessons was far from exemplary, as he had difficulty casting the <a href="/wiki/Disarming_Charm" title="Disarming Charm">Disarming Charm</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP18_6-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP18-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup> although he did try much harder after it was announced several <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a> had <a href="/wiki/Azkaban_escapes" title="Azkaban escapes">escaped</a> from <a href="/wiki/Azkaban" title="Azkaban">Azkaban</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP26_12-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP26-12">&#91;12&#93;</a></sup> meaning that he likely improved his skills.</li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Charm" title="Charm">Charms</a></b>: Zacharias was capable of producing a non-corporeal <a href="/wiki/Patronus_Charm" title="Patronus Charm">Patronus</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-LEGOY2_7-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-LEGOY2-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup> a highly advanced and difficult piece of <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magic</a>, which he likely learnt how to cast in Dumbledore's Army.</li></ul>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Zacharias_Smith?veaction=edit&amp;section=11" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Zacharias_Smith?action=edit&amp;section=11" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The name Zacharias is a variant of <i><a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Zechariah_(given_name)" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Zechariah (given name)">Zechariah</a></i>, a Hebrew name meaning "God has remembered" (this was probably a misnomer for Zacharias). This was the name of the father of John the Baptist. He and his wife were very old when they had John, and Zechariah was initially disbelieving when an angel informed them they would have a son; as punishment for his doubt, he was struck mute. This may allude to Zacharias Smith's scepticism.
</p>

<ul><li>In the closing credits of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Order_of_the_Phoenix_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix (film)">Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix</a></i>, <a href="/wiki/Nick_Shirm" title="Nick Shirm">Nick Shirm</a> is identified as "Somewhat Doubtful Boy", rather than by name. Shirm also voiced the character in the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Order_of_the_Phoenix_(video_game)" title="Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix (video game)">video game</a>, where he is explicitly identified as Zacharias.</li>
<li>Zacharias Smith seems to be more pleasant in the movies than in the novels, like for example in the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Order_of_the_Phoenix_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix (film)">film adaptation</a> of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Order_of_the_Phoenix" title="Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix">Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix</a></i>, before the Christmas holidays began, he applauded happily about Harry's Dumbledore's Army lessons.</li>
<li>Zacharias Smith may be <a href="/wiki/Smith_family" title="Smith family">related</a> to <a href="/wiki/Hepzibah_Smith" title="Hepzibah Smith">Hepzibah Smith</a>, and therefore possibly a descendant of <a href="/wiki/Helga_Hufflepuff" title="Helga Hufflepuff">Helga Hufflepuff</a>, founder of his <a href="/wiki/Hufflepuff" title="Hufflepuff">house</a> at Hogwarts. Should this be true, Zacharias would be an odd irony compared to his ancestor Hufflepuff, lacking loyalty and fair play - this would explain, though, why someone that did not carry many Hufflepuff traits would still be sorted into that house, as he could have requested to be put in Hufflepuff.</li>
<li>It could also be that, since Helga Hufflepuff would take students that were not picked by the other founders, the Sorting Hat sensed that Smith didn't possess any of the qualities needed for the other houses and put him in Hufflepuff by default.</li>
<li>In the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Deathly_Hallows:_Part_2" title="Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 2">second film adaptation</a> of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Deathly_Hallows" title="Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows">Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows</a></i>, he is possibly seen standing in the courtyard, witnessing <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Voldemort</a> announcing Harry's apparent <a href="/wiki/Death" title="Death">death</a>, suggesting that, unlike in the novel, he <i>did</i> fight in the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a> in the film.</li>
<li>Zacharias Smith may be named for another fantasy figure, <a target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener" class="external text" href="https://villains.fandom.com/wiki/Doctor_Zachary_Smith">Doctor Zachary Smith</a> of Lost In Space. Doctor Smith was also known for cowardice, selfishness, and being quick to cast aspersions.</li></ul>
