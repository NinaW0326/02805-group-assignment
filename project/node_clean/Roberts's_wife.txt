<b>Mrs Roberts</b> was the wife of the <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggle</a> <a href="/wiki/Roberts" title="Roberts">man</a> who managed the campground near to where the <a href="/wiki/Quidditch_World_Cup" title="Quidditch World Cup">Quidditch World Cup</a> was held in <a href="/wiki/1994" title="1994">1994</a>.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Roberts%27s_wife?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Roberts%27s_wife?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>She had <a href="/wiki/Roberts_children" title="Roberts children">two children</a> with him, including at least one son, who was her youngest child. The family lived together in a stone cottage at the front of the campsite.<sup id="cite_ref-GOF7_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF7-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>The Roberts family were attacked by <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a> the night after the <a href="/wiki/Quidditch" title="Quidditch">Quidditch</a> match. One of the <a href="/wiki/Dark_Wizard" title="Dark Wizard">Dark Wizards</a> flipped Mrs Roberts upside down in mid-air, making her nightdress fall down, and revealing her "voluminous" drawers to everyone. The other <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a> laughed as she struggled to cover herself. She was not seriously injured during the incident.<sup id="cite_ref-GOF9_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF9-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Mrs Roberts' memory was <a href="/wiki/Memory_Charm" title="Memory Charm">altered</a> to forget the incident like her husband.<sup id="cite_ref-GOF10_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF10-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
