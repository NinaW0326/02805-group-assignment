<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire">"<i>Bill had a pen-friend at a school in Brazil once. He wanted Bill to go on a student exchange trip, but Mum and Dad couldn't afford it. So the pen-friend got all offended and sent him a cursed hat, it made his ears shrivel up.</i>"</span></dd>
<dd>&#8212;<a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ron Weasley</a><sup class="noprint"><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Goblet_of_Fire" title="Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire">[src]</a></sup></dd>
<dd></dd></dl></div>
<p>This <b>individual</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a>, who was a student that attended the <a href="/wiki/Wizarding_school" title="Wizarding school">wizarding school</a> of <a href="/wiki/Castelobruxo" title="Castelobruxo">Castelobruxo</a> in <a href="/wiki/Brazil" title="Brazil">Brazil</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-GOF7_1-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF7-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>At some point, he became pen-friends with <a href="/wiki/William_Weasley" title="William Weasley">Bill Weasley</a>, and their two families were invited to participate in a <a href="/wiki/Student_exchange_programme" title="Student exchange programme">student exchange programme</a>. Unfortunately, due to the <a href="/wiki/Weasley_family" title="Weasley family">Weasley family</a>'s poor financial situation, Bill was unable to go. His pen-friend was extremely offended and sent Bill a <a href="/wiki/Curse" title="Curse">cursed</a> hat, which made his <a href="/wiki/Ear-shrivelling_Curse" class="mw-redirect" title="Ear-shrivelling Curse">ears shrivel up</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-GOF7_1-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF7-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
