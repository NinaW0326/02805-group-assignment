<b>Robert Louis Balfour Stevenson</b> (<a href="/wiki/13_November" title="13 November">13 November</a> <a href="/wiki/1850" class="mw-redirect" title="1850">1850</a> - <a href="/wiki/3_December" title="3 December">3 December</a> <a href="/wiki/1894" class="mw-redirect" title="1894">1894</a>) was a <a href="/wiki/Scotland" title="Scotland">Scottish</a> writer who wrote thirteen novels, as well as a number of collections of poems, essays, and short stories. Stevenson was a literary celebrity during his lifetime.<sup id="cite_ref-wikipedia_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-wikipedia-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
