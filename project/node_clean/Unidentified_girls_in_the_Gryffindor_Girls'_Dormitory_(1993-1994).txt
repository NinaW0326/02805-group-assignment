<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_girls_in_the_Gryffindor_Girls%27_Dormitory_(1993-1994)?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_girls_in_the_Gryffindor_Girls%27_Dormitory_(1993-1994)?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_lifes">Early lifes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_girls_in_the_Gryffindor_Girls%27_Dormitory_(1993-1994)?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Early lifes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_girls_in_the_Gryffindor_Girls%27_Dormitory_(1993-1994)?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Early lifes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>These two girls started their <a href="/wiki/First_year" title="First year">first year</a> of <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magical</a> education at Hogwarts in <a href="/wiki/1991" title="1991">1991</a>, and were both <a href="/wiki/Sorting_ceremony" title="Sorting ceremony">Sorted</a> into <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor" title="Gryffindor">Gryffindor</a> house. They shared a <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor_Girls%27_Dormitory" title="Gryffindor Girls&#39; Dormitory">dormitory</a> with <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione Granger</a>, <a href="/wiki/Parvati_Patil" title="Parvati Patil">Parvati Patil</a> and <a href="/wiki/Lavender_Brown" title="Lavender Brown">Lavender Brown</a>, who were also Gryffindor girls in their year.<sup id="cite_ref-POAG_2-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-POAG-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Third_year">Third year</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_girls_in_the_Gryffindor_Girls%27_Dormitory_(1993-1994)?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Third year">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_girls_in_the_Gryffindor_Girls%27_Dormitory_(1993-1994)?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Third year"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<div class="quote">
<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban (video game)">"<i>I can't believe they've brought Dementors in to guard Hogwarts. I'm never going near the forest again, I'll tell you that!</i>"</span></dd>
<dd>&#8212;The brown-haired girl to <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione Granger</a> regarding the <a href="/wiki/Dementor" title="Dementor">Dementors</a> placed around the school<sup class="noprint"><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Prisoner_of_Azkaban_(video_game)" title="Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban (video game)">[src]</a></sup></dd>
<dd></dd></dl></div>
<p>Throughout the <a href="/wiki/1993%E2%80%931994_school_year" title="1993–1994 school year">1993–1994 school year</a> at Hogwarts, their <a href="/wiki/Third_year" title="Third year">third year</a>, these two girls often hung around in the <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor_Girls%27_Dormitory" title="Gryffindor Girls&#39; Dormitory">Gryffindor Girls' Dormitory</a> in <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor_Tower" title="Gryffindor Tower">Gryffindor Tower</a>, and talked amongst each other.<sup id="cite_ref-POAG_2-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-POAG-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<div class="quote">
<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban (video game)">"<i>I'm so excited about <a href="/wiki/Care_of_Magical_Creatures" title="Care of Magical Creatures">Care of Magical Creatures</a>. Ugh, I only wish <a href="/wiki/Vincent_Crabbe" title="Vincent Crabbe">Crabbe</a> and <a href="/wiki/Gregory_Goyle" title="Gregory Goyle">Goyle</a> weren't going to be there.</i>"</span></dd>
<dd>&#8212;The blonde-haired girl to the dark-haired girl in the dormitory<sup class="noprint"><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Prisoner_of_Azkaban_(video_game)" title="Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban (video game)">[src]</a></sup></dd>
<dd></dd></dl></div>
<p>This year at Hogwarts, the time Dementors were standing guard at every entrance. Neither one of these girls liked the Dementors. After the brown haired-girl heard that she was doing the same subjects as <a href="/wiki/Draco_Malfoy" title="Draco Malfoy">Draco Malfoy</a>, she was highly displeased, and wished she did not have any classes with him. Her friend felt the same about having <a href="/wiki/Care_of_Magical_Creatures" title="Care of Magical Creatures">Care of Magical Creatures</a> with <a href="/wiki/Gregory_Goyle" title="Gregory Goyle">Gregory Goyle</a> and <a href="/wiki/Vincent_Crabbe" title="Vincent Crabbe">Vincent Crabbe</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-POAG_2-10" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-POAG-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<div class="quote">
<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban (video game)">"<i>Hello Hermione! I'm going to try out for the Quidditch team next term!</i>"</span></dd>
<dd>&#8212;The brown-haired girl speaking to <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione Granger</a> in the girls' dormitory<sup class="noprint"><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Prisoner_of_Azkaban_(video_game)" title="Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban (video game)">[src]</a></sup></dd>
<dd></dd></dl></div>
<p>The brown-haired planned on trying out for the <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor_Quidditch_team" title="Gryffindor Quidditch team">Gryffindor Quidditch team</a>. As she had a <a href="/wiki/Beater%27s_bat" title="Beater&#39;s bat">Beater's bat</a> by her bed, she presumably liked playing <a href="/wiki/Beater" title="Beater">Beater</a>, but the team already had two; <a href="/wiki/Fred_Weasley" title="Fred Weasley">Fred</a> and <a href="/wiki/George_Weasley" title="George Weasley">George Weasley</a>. However, it is possible that she hoped she could replace one of them, or was simply confused.<sup id="cite_ref-POAG_2-11" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-POAG-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>The two girls, as well as Parvati and Lavender, were up and ready for their first day before Hermione was, though Parvati and Lavender had already left. While Hermione rushed to her class, the two friends remained in the dormitory, talking for a while.<sup id="cite_ref-POAG_2-12" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-POAG-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> In the lesson, they were taught about <a href="/wiki/Boggart" title="Boggart">Boggarts</a> by <a href="/wiki/Remus_Lupin" title="Remus Lupin">Remus Lupin</a>, the new teacher as of <a href="/wiki/Gilderoy_Lockhart" title="Gilderoy Lockhart">Professor Lockhart</a>'s departure, and had a practical lesson on how to <a href="/wiki/Boggart-Banishing_Spell" title="Boggart-Banishing Spell">banish</a> them. The girls faced the <a href="/wiki/Boggart_in_the_Wardrobe" title="Boggart in the Wardrobe">Boggart</a>, and it turned into either a <a href="/wiki/Rat" title="Rat">rat</a>, <a href="/wiki/Rattlesnake" title="Rattlesnake">rattlesnake</a> or bloody eyeball for them.<sup id="cite_ref-POA7_3-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-POA7-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>After <a href="/wiki/Sirius_Black" title="Sirius Black">Sirius Black</a> broke onto <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor_Tower" title="Gryffindor Tower">Gryffindor Tower</a>, the girls slept with the rest of the school in the <a href="/wiki/Great_Hall" title="Great Hall">Great Hall</a> in squashy purple sleeping bags that <a href="/wiki/Albus_Dumbledore" title="Albus Dumbledore">Professor Dumbledore</a> <a href="/wiki/Sleeping_bag_conjuring_spell" title="Sleeping bag conjuring spell">conjured</a>. However, she returned to her normal bed the day after, and spent most of her time talking with her friend in their dormitory.<sup id="cite_ref-POAG_2-13" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-POAG-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Personality_and_traits">Personality and traits</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_girls_in_the_Gryffindor_Girls%27_Dormitory_(1993-1994)?veaction=edit&amp;section=4" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Personality and traits">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_girls_in_the_Gryffindor_Girls%27_Dormitory_(1993-1994)?action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Personality and traits"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<div class="quote">
<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban (video game)">"<i>I'm really looking forward to the summer holidays!</i>"</span></dd>
<dd>&#8212;The Gryffindor girl speaking with Hermione.<sup class="noprint"><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Prisoner_of_Azkaban_(video_game)" title="Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban (video game)">[src]</a></sup></dd>
<dd></dd></dl></div>
<p>The brown-haired girl was more outgoing than her blonde-haired friend, openly shouting "<i>go away!</i>" one morning when Hermione rushed passed her, while her friend just exclaimed "<i>hey!</i>". She was the only girl in her dormitory who liked <a href="/wiki/Quidditch" title="Quidditch">Quidditch</a>, and was unashamed at saying that <a href="/wiki/Cedric_Diggory" title="Cedric Diggory">Cedric Diggory</a> was the most handsome boy in the school. She was also rather lazy at times, staying in the dormitory while everyone else ran off for <a href="/wiki/Defence_Against_the_Dark_Arts" title="Defence Against the Dark Arts">Defence Against the Dark Arts</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-POAG_2-14" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-POAG-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
