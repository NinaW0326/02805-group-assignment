<p><b>Adrian</b> (b. <a href="/wiki/1959" title="1959">1959</a> or <a href="/wiki/1960" title="1960">1960</a>) was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> who worked as a photographer. During his school years at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts</a>, he was a member of the <a href="/wiki/Slug_Club" title="Slug Club">Slug Club</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-script_3-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-script-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Adrian?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Adrian?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_life">Early life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Adrian?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Early life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Adrian?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Early life"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Adrian was born in either <a href="/wiki/1959" title="1959">1959</a> or <a href="/wiki/1960" title="1960">1960</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-graduation_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-graduation-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> He began attending <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> in <a href="/wiki/1971" title="1971">1971</a>, and, at some point, was made a member of the <a href="/wiki/Slug_Club" title="Slug Club">Slug Club</a> by his <a href="/wiki/Potions_Master" title="Potions Master">Potions Master</a>, <a href="/wiki/Horace_Slughorn" title="Horace Slughorn">Horace Slughorn</a>. Slughorn had high expectations on the future of this student. Adrian eventually <a href="/wiki/Graduation_ceremony" title="Graduation ceremony">graduated</a> in <a href="/wiki/1978" title="1978">1978</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-script_3-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-script-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Adulthood">Adulthood</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Adrian?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Adulthood">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Adrian?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Adulthood"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Adrian grew up to be a photographer, to <a href="/wiki/Horace_Slughorn" title="Horace Slughorn">Slughorn</a>'s disappointment, as he hoped more of him. The professor still maintained, however, a good relationship with him.<sup id="cite_ref-script_3-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-script-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<figure class="thumb tleft show-info-icon" style="width: 202px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/7/7d/Dean_Thomas_Slug_Club_Christmas_Party.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20100612205321" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/7/7d/Dean_Thomas_Slug_Club_Christmas_Party.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/200?cb=20100612205321" decoding="async" width="200" height="249" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="Dean Thomas Slug Club Christmas Party.jpg" data-image-key="Dean_Thomas_Slug_Club_Christmas_Party.jpg" /></a> 	 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:Dean_Thomas_Slug_Club_Christmas_Party.jpg" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption">Adrian and Dean Thomas</p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure>
<p>On <a href="/wiki/20_December" title="20 December">20 December</a>, <a href="/wiki/1996" title="1996">1996</a>, Adrian attended a <a href="/wiki/Slug_Club_Christmas_party" title="Slug Club Christmas party">Christmas party</a> at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Castle" title="Hogwarts Castle">Hogwarts Castle</a>, held by his former professor, <a href="/wiki/Horace_Slughorn" title="Horace Slughorn">Horace Slughorn</a>. Slughorn introduced <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> to him, and he took a picture of Harry and Slughorn together. By <a href="/wiki/March" title="March">March</a> of the following year, Slughorn kept this picture on the front and centre of "the shelf."<sup id="cite_ref-HBP.2Ff_4-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP.2Ff-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Adrian?veaction=edit&amp;section=4" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Adrian?action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Several saints and six popes have borne this name, including the only English pope, Adrian IV, and the only Dutch pope, Adrian VI. As an English name, it has been in use since the Middle Ages, though it was not popular until modern times.<sup id="cite_ref-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
