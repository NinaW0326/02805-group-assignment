This <b>gigantic serpent</b> guarded a hill in the <a href="/wiki/Enchanted_garden_(Book_of_Potions)" title="Enchanted garden (Book of Potions)">enchanted garden</a> that the <a href="/wiki/Wizarding_Schools_Potions_Championship" title="Wizarding Schools Potions Championship">Wizarding Schools Potions Championship</a> took place in. The <a href="/wiki/Snake" title="Snake">snake</a> was of a massive size, had six eyes, and possessed the <a href="/wiki/Dragon_hide" title="Dragon hide">thick, spell-repellent hide</a> of a <a href="/wiki/Dragon" title="Dragon">dragon</a> and the <a href="/wiki/Basilisk_venom" title="Basilisk venom">venomous fangs</a> of the <a href="/wiki/Basilisk" title="Basilisk">basilisk</a>. The hill the serpent guards was placed at a key point in the garden; all the possible paths converge on the hill, meaning the serpent must be faced by any champion in the tournament.<sup id="cite_ref-BOP_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-BOP-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="History">History</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Gigantic_serpent?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: History">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Gigantic_serpent?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: History"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_potions_champion" title="Hogwarts potions champion">Hogwarts potions champion</a> eluded the serpent by first attempting to gain its attention by starting a <a href="/wiki/Fire" title="Fire">fire</a> in the nearby forest with a cartful of dry hay and the <a href="/wiki/Fire-Making_Spell" title="Fire-Making Spell">Fire-Making Spell</a>, and, when this did not work, destroying an abandoned tower on the hill by disintegrating its supports with the <a href="/wiki/Reductor_Curse" title="Reductor Curse">Reductor Curse</a>. With the snake's attention drawn, the student attracted it by smashing a <a href="/wiki/Pungous_Onion" title="Pungous Onion">Pungous Onion</a> provided by <i><a href="/wiki/Book_of_Potions" title="Book of Potions">Book of Potions</a></i> and then poured <a href="/wiki/Sleeping_Potion" title="Sleeping Potion">Sleeping Potion</a> onto its outstretched tongue while it was investigating the scent. This put the creature to sleep long enough for the champion to pass the hill.<sup id="cite_ref-BOP_2-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-BOP-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
