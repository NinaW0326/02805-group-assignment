<dd>&#8212;Andromeda's refusal to live up to her families' expectations<sup class="noprint"><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Order_of_the_Phoenix" title="Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix">[src]</a></sup></dd>

<p><b>Andromeda Tonks</b> (née <b>Black</b>) (b. <a href="/wiki/1951" title="1951">1951</a>-<a href="/wiki/1955" title="1955">1955</a><sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>), also known as <b>Dromeda</b>, was an <a href="/wiki/England" title="England">English</a> <a href="/wiki/Pure-blood" title="Pure-blood">pure-blood</a> <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch</a> and the middle daughter of <a href="/wiki/Cygnus_Black_III" title="Cygnus Black III">Cygnus</a> and <a href="/wiki/Druella_Black" title="Druella Black">Druella Black</a> <span class="template-c">(née Rosier)</span>, as well as the sister of <a href="/wiki/Bellatrix_Lestrange" title="Bellatrix Lestrange">Bellatrix</a> and <a href="/wiki/Narcissa_Malfoy" title="Narcissa Malfoy">Narcissa</a>. She attended <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> in the <a href="/wiki/1960s" title="1960s">1960s</a> and was sorted into <a href="/wiki/Slytherin" title="Slytherin">Slytherin House</a>.
</p><p>After Hogwarts, she married a <a href="/wiki/Muggle-born" title="Muggle-born">Muggle-born</a>, <a href="/wiki/Edward_Tonks" title="Edward Tonks">Ted Tonks</a>, and was disowned by her prejudiced <a href="/wiki/Pure-blood_supremacy" title="Pure-blood supremacy">pure-blood supremacist</a> family. From that point onward she and her relatives had no further contact. In <a href="/wiki/1973" title="1973">1973</a> she became the mother of <a href="/wiki/Nymphadora_Tonks" title="Nymphadora Tonks">Nymphadora Tonks</a>. Her daughter later became an <a href="/wiki/Auror" title="Auror">Auror</a> in <a href="/wiki/1994" title="1994">1994</a> under the watchful eyes of <a href="/wiki/Alastor_Moody" title="Alastor Moody">Alastor Moody</a>.
</p><p>During the <a href="/wiki/Second_Wizarding_War" title="Second Wizarding War">Second Wizarding War</a>, Andromeda and her husband were allies of the <a href="/wiki/Order_of_the_Phoenix" title="Order of the Phoenix">Order of the Phoenix</a>, as their daughter was a member. Nymphadora married <a href="/wiki/Remus_Lupin" title="Remus Lupin">Remus Lupin</a> in <a href="/wiki/1997" title="1997">1997</a> and had a son, <a href="/wiki/Edward_Lupin" title="Edward Lupin">Edward Remus Lupin</a>. In <a href="/wiki/1998" title="1998">1998</a> during the height of the war Andromeda's husband was murdered by <a href="/wiki/Snatchers" title="Snatchers">Snatchers</a>. Remus was murdered during the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a> and Nymphadora was also murdered by Andromeda's older sister, <a href="/wiki/Bellatrix_Lestrange" title="Bellatrix Lestrange">Bellatrix Lestrange</a>.
</p><p>After the <a href="/wiki/Second_Wizarding_War" title="Second Wizarding War">Second Wizarding War</a>, Andromeda raised her grandson with the help of his godfather <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>, which meant that Teddy also spent time with the <a href="/wiki/Potter_family" title="Potter family">Potter</a> and <a href="/wiki/Weasley_family" title="Weasley family">Weasley</a> families.
</p>

<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_life">Early life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Andromeda_Tonks?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Early life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Andromeda_Tonks?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Early life"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<figure class="thumb tleft show-info-icon" style="width: 176px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/5/50/Narcissa%2C_Andromeda%2C_and_Bellatrix.jpeg/revision/latest?cb=20180502172712" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/5/50/Narcissa%2C_Andromeda%2C_and_Bellatrix.jpeg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/174?cb=20180502172712" decoding="async" width="174" height="234" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="Narcissa, Andromeda, and Bellatrix.jpeg" data-image-key="Narcissa,_Andromeda,_and_Bellatrix.jpeg" /></a> 	 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:Narcissa,_Andromeda,_and_Bellatrix.jpeg" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption">A young Andromeda (right) with her older sister <a href="/wiki/Bellatrix_Lestrange" title="Bellatrix Lestrange">Bellatrix</a> and her younger sister <a href="/wiki/Narcissa_Malfoy" title="Narcissa Malfoy">Narcissa</a></p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure>
<p>Andromeda was born in the early <a href="/wiki/1950s" title="1950s">1950s</a>. She was the second daughter of <a href="/wiki/Cygnus_Black_III" title="Cygnus Black III">Cygnus</a> and <a href="/wiki/Druella_Black" title="Druella Black">Druella Black</a> (née <a href="/wiki/Rosier_family" title="Rosier family">Rosier</a>), and younger sister of Bellatrix Lestrange and older sister of <a href="/wiki/Narcissa_Malfoy" title="Narcissa Malfoy">Narcissa Malfoy</a>. <a href="/wiki/Walburga_Black" title="Walburga Black">Walburga</a> and <a href="/wiki/Orion_Black" title="Orion Black">Orion Black</a> were her aunt and uncle on her father's side, and <a href="/wiki/Sirius_Black" title="Sirius Black">Sirius</a> and <a href="/wiki/Regulus_Black" title="Regulus Black">Regulus Black</a> her cousins. <a href="/wiki/Evan_Rosier" title="Evan Rosier">Evan Rosier</a> may be a relative on her mother's side. <a href="/wiki/Alphard_Black" title="Alphard Black">Alphard Black</a> was also Andromeda's uncle.<sup id="cite_ref-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>She was Sirius Black's favourite cousin, but he never met her married family before his imprisonment in <a href="/wiki/Azkaban" title="Azkaban">Azkaban</a> in <a href="/wiki/1981" title="1981">1981</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-OTP_6-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OTP-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Hogwarts_years">Hogwarts years</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Andromeda_Tonks?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Hogwarts years">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Andromeda_Tonks?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Hogwarts years"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<div class="quote">
<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows">"<i>She is no niece of ours, my Lord. We — Narcissa and I — have never set eyes on our sister since she married the <a href="/wiki/Edward_Tonks" title="Edward Tonks">Mudblood</a>. This brat has nothing to do with either of us, nor any beast she marries.</i>"</span></dd>
<dd>&#8212;The recent marriage of Nymphadora Tonks<sup class="noprint"><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Deathly_Hallows" title="Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows">[src]</a></sup></dd>
<dd></dd></dl></div><p>Andromeda attended <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> from the mid-1960s to the early <a href="/wiki/1970s" title="1970s">1970s</a> and was <a href="/wiki/Sorting_Hat" title="Sorting Hat">sorted</a> into <a href="/wiki/Slytherin" title="Slytherin">Slytherin</a> <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Houses" title="Hogwarts Houses">House</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-slytherin_3-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-slytherin-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> She may have been very skilled in <a href="/wiki/Charms_(class)" title="Charms (class)">Charms</a> class, given her proficiency with casting various household charms. It is also possible that she was talented in <a href="/wiki/Defence_Against_the_Dark_Arts" title="Defence Against the Dark Arts">Defence Against the Dark Arts</a>, because in her adult life she managed to resist torture without revealing any vital information or losing her sanity.
</p><p>At some point during or after her education at Hogwarts, Andromeda met and subsequently fell in love with <a href="/wiki/Muggle-born" title="Muggle-born">Muggle-born</a> wizard <a href="/wiki/Edward_Tonks" title="Edward Tonks">Ted Tonks</a>, despite her family's steadfast belief in the importance of <a href="/wiki/Blood_status" title="Blood status">blood purity</a>.
</p>

Statements by her sisters indicate that both of them cut off contact with Andromeda because they considered her a <a href="/wiki/Blood_traitor" title="Blood traitor">blood traitor</a> for her marriage.<sup id="cite_ref-DH_7-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>In <a href="/wiki/1973" title="1973">1973</a>, around the age of twenty, Andromeda and Ted had one child, <a href="/wiki/Nymphadora_Tonks" title="Nymphadora Tonks">Nymphadora Tonks</a>. Andromeda selected the name, which her daughter strongly disliked, preferring to be known simply as "Tonks" by her friends. Ted, Remus Lupin, and Andromeda called her Dora for short.
</p><p>Andromeda had a talent for cleanliness that her husband and daughter did not share.<sup id="cite_ref-OTP_6-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OTP-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup>

Nymphdora graduated from Hogwarts in <a href="/wiki/1991" title="1991">1991</a> and went on to train and later work as an <a href="/wiki/Auror" title="Auror">Auror</a> for the <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">British Ministry of Magic</a>. Nymphadora worked as an Auror between the wars and during the <a href="/wiki/Second_Wizarding_War" title="Second Wizarding War">Second Wizarding War</a>. Andromeda frequently worried about her daughter's safety.

<h3><span id="Second_Wizarding_War_(1995-1998)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="Second_Wizarding_War_.281995-1998.29">Second Wizarding War <span class="template-c">(1995-1998)</span></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Andromeda_Tonks?veaction=edit&amp;section=5" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Second Wizarding War (1995-1998)">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Andromeda_Tonks?action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Second Wizarding War (1995-1998)"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<div class="quote">
<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows"><b>Andromeda</b>: "<i>What happened to our daughter? Hagrid said you were ambushed; where is Nymphadora?</i>"</span></dd>

<dd>&#8212; Andromeda asks Harry about her daughter's safety<sup class="noprint"><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Deathly_Hallows" title="Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows">[src]</a></sup></dd></dl>
</div><p>Although Andromeda and Ted did not appear to be official members of the <a href="/wiki/Order_of_the_Phoenix" title="Order of the Phoenix">Order of the Phoenix</a>, they allowed their <a href="/wiki/Tonks_house" title="Tonks house">home</a> to be one of the Order's safe-houses and were supportive of the organisation to which their daughter belonged. Nymphadora married <a href="/wiki/Remus_Lupin" title="Remus Lupin">Remus Lupin</a> in the summer of <a href="/wiki/1997" title="1997">1997</a>; he suspected that her parents did not approve because he was a <a href="/wiki/Werewolf" title="Werewolf">werewolf</a>, though it is unknown if this was true; given the fact that Andromeda was not a pure-blood supremacist.
</p><p>After the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_the_Seven_Potters" title="Battle of the Seven Potters">Battle of the Seven Potters</a>, they were instrumental in Harry Potter's moving from <a href="/wiki/Privet_Drive" title="Privet Drive">Privet Drive</a> to <a href="/wiki/The_Burrow" title="The Burrow">The Burrow</a>: <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> and <a href="/wiki/Rubeus_Hagrid" title="Rubeus Hagrid">Rubeus Hagrid</a> took temporary refuge in Tonks' home, where Andromeda took care of Hagrid's injuries. She seemed somewhat offended when Harry first reacted to her as if she were her sister Bellatrix because of their resemblance. She and Ted were both very worried about their daughter when they learned that the Order was ambushed by <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a> when transporting Harry, though Ted tried to reassure his wife by pointing out that their daughter had been through worse in the course of her work as an <a href="/wiki/Auror" title="Auror">Auror</a>.
</p><p>When the <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">Ministry of Magic</a> fell under <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a>'s control in August of 1997, the magical protections around the Order safe-houses were broken. Andromeda and Ted were interrogated and <a href="/wiki/Cruciatus_Curse" title="Cruciatus Curse">tortured</a> by Death Eaters for information pertaining to Harry Potter and the Order. Their son-in-law reported that they were "<i>shaken, obviously, but otherwise okay</i>".<sup id="cite_ref-DH_7-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup> Soon after this, their daughter discovered that she was pregnant and came to stay with Andromeda and Ted, as her husband temporarily left her out of fear that he would pass on lycanthropy to their child.
</p><p>The Ministry soon began persecuting <a href="/wiki/Muggle-born" title="Muggle-born">Muggle-borns</a> by forming the <a href="/wiki/Muggle-Born_Registration_Commission" title="Muggle-Born Registration Commission">Muggle-Born Registration Commission</a>, which charged Muggle-borns with having stolen their magic and their wands from "real" witches and wizards. Ted refused to register on principle and was forced to go on the run; Andromeda, as a <a href="/wiki/Pure-blood" title="Pure-blood">pure-blood</a>, was safe and stayed with her pregnant daughter. Some time before the birth of her grandson <a href="/wiki/Edward_Lupin" title="Edward Lupin">Teddy</a>, Andromeda was left a widow, as her husband was murdered by <a href="/wiki/Snatchers" title="Snatchers">Snatchers</a>.
</p><p>Andromeda's daughter and son-in-law were killed in action during the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a> by <a href="/wiki/Bellatrix_Lestrange" title="Bellatrix Lestrange">Bellatrix Lestrange</a>, Andromeda's sister, and <a href="/wiki/Antonin_Dolohov" title="Antonin Dolohov">Antonin Dolohov</a>, respectively. Bellatrix was later killed herself by <a href="/wiki/Molly_Weasley" title="Molly Weasley">Molly Weasley</a>.
</p>

<p>Andromeda was left with custody of her grandson, though Teddy also spent a lot of time with his godfather, <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>, and the <a href="/wiki/Weasley_family" title="Weasley family">Weasley family</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-DH_7-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Physical_appearance">Physical appearance</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Andromeda_Tonks?veaction=edit&amp;section=7" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Physical appearance">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Andromeda_Tonks?action=edit&amp;section=7" title="Edit section: Physical appearance"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<div class="quote">
<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows">"<i>As she moved forward into the room, Mrs Tonks's resemblance to her sister Bellatrix became much less pronounced: Her hair was a light soft brown and her eyes were wider and kinder.</i>"</span></dd>
<dd>&#8212;Description<sup class="noprint"><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Deathly_Hallows" title="Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows">[src]</a></sup></dd>
<dd></dd></dl></div><p>Andromeda was described to have an uncanny physical resemblance to her older sister, <a href="/wiki/Bellatrix_Lestrange" title="Bellatrix Lestrange">Bellatrix</a>, to the extent where Harry almost attacked her during their first meeting. Hence, she most likely had the patrician beauty and aristocratic bearing that were the typical characteristics of the <a href="/wiki/House_of_Black" title="House of Black">House of Black</a>, the only differences being that, while Bellatrix was dark-haired and had heavily-lidded malicious eyes, Andromeda had light brown hair, and her eyes were wider and kinder.<sup id="cite_ref-hair_2-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-hair-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p><h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Personality_and_traits">Personality and traits</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Andromeda_Tonks?veaction=edit&amp;section=8" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Personality and traits">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Andromeda_Tonks?action=edit&amp;section=8" title="Edit section: Personality and traits"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>It is safe to assume, given her marriage to a <a href="/wiki/Muggle-born" title="Muggle-born">Muggle-born</a>, that Andromeda was very different from both of her sisters: while her sisters married into "respectable" pure-blood families, she married for love despite her family's opposition, leading to her family's hatred of her.
</p><p>She was clearly a good mother, as seen by her showing her concern about her daughter's whereabouts after the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_the_Seven_Potters" title="Battle of the Seven Potters">Battle of the Seven Potters</a> and taking care of her during her pregnancy. She was also a good grandmother, as she raised her daughter's son after her death.
</p><p>Apart from her husband and daughter, her cousin <a href="/wiki/Sirius_Black" title="Sirius Black">Sirius Black</a> considered her to be his only true family, demonstrating that Andromeda always treated Sirius with respect and kindness. In addition to being a good wife, mother, and grandmother, she was a good friend because, despite being under torture, she did not reveal anything that could harm <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> or any of her friends. Like <a href="/wiki/Molly_Weasley" title="Molly Weasley">Molly Weasley</a>, Andromeda was a woman dedicated to her family and friends. Thus, in many ways, Andromeda's personality contradicted the "evil Slytherin" stereotype held by many students of the other Houses. Instead, she appeared to embody the Slytherin principle of fraternity, as seen by her standing by her friends, family, and allies.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Magical_abilities_and_skills">Magical abilities and skills</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Andromeda_Tonks?veaction=edit&amp;section=9" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Andromeda_Tonks?action=edit&amp;section=9" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li><b><a href="/wiki/Healing_magic" title="Healing magic">Healing magic</a></b>: After the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_the_Seven_Potters" title="Battle of the Seven Potters">Battle of the Seven Potters</a>, Harry and Hagrid took refuge in her house, where she successfully healed Hagrid's injuries. Given Hagrid's half-giant nature and concomitant natural magical resistance, this does imply she was a talented Healer.<sup id="cite_ref-DH_7-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Household_magic" title="Household magic">Household magic</a></b>: Andromeda had a talent for cleanliness and household spells, a talent that her husband and her daughter did not share. According to <a href="/wiki/Nymphadora_Tonks" title="Nymphadora Tonks">Nymphadora Tonks</a>, Andromeda mastered highly the <a href="/wiki/Pack_Charm" title="Pack Charm">Pack Charm</a>, getting stuff to fit itself in neatly and even getting socks to fold themselves.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP3_8-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP3-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup></li></ul>

<p>Andromeda's relationship with her parents was likely a bad one. Because she married a <a href="/wiki/Muggle-born" title="Muggle-born">Muggle-born</a> against her family's ideals of blood purity she was disowned and not considered a Black after that. It is safe to assume that her parents did not consider her as their daughter anymore and, like Bellatrix and Narcissa, cut off all contact with her. It is also possible that Andromeda's parents never met their granddaughter.
</p><p>It is unknown what kind of relationship Andromeda had with her older sister, Bellatrix Lestrange, and younger sister, Narcissa Malfoy, when they were younger. However, both her sisters believed in <a href="/wiki/Pure-blood" title="Pure-blood">pure-blood</a> supremacy, whereas Andromeda obviously did not, given her marriage.
</p>

<p>After she married the Muggle-born Ted, Andromeda was disowned and both her sisters cut off all contact with her.<sup id="cite_ref-DH_7-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup> Andromeda's relationship with Bellatrix was likely particularly hateful, given that Bellatrix murdered Andromeda's own daughter (though, given the circumstances, she likely didn't learn of the latter until after the battle and her eldest sister's death). It can be assumed that Bellatrix would not have hesitated to kill Andromeda herself if she ever encountered her again. Andromeda also seemed to be offended when <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>, upon first seeing her, reacted as if she were her older sister.<sup id="cite_ref-DH_7-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup> It can also be presumed that Andromeda showed no remorse for her older sister's death at the hands of <a href="/wiki/Molly_Weasley" title="Molly Weasley">Molly Weasley</a> given Bellatrix's cruelty towards her and her murder of her child and Sirius. Andromeda's relationship with Narcissa, however, is unknown, though it was most likely similar to that of her relationship to Bellatrix and thus extremely hateful. It is doubtful the two ever met again even following Voldemort's defeat as Narcissa still strongly valued pure-blood supremacy.
</p><p>Andromeda's younger cousin Sirius Black told Harry Potter in <a href="/wiki/1995" title="1995">1995</a> that Andromeda was his favourite cousin.<sup id="cite_ref-OTP_6-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OTP-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup> Like her, Sirius was considered a "<a href="/wiki/Blood_traitor" title="Blood traitor">blood traitor</a>" by their family, and was disowned for his beliefs. Thus, the two were likely close in their younger years. Sirius considered Andromeda and Tonks his only and true family.
</p>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Ted_Tonks">Ted Tonks</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Andromeda_Tonks?veaction=edit&amp;section=13" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Ted Tonks">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Andromeda_Tonks?action=edit&amp;section=13" title="Edit section: Ted Tonks"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<p>Andromeda married <a href="/wiki/Edward_Tonks" title="Edward Tonks">Ted Tonks</a> in spite of her family's hatred of <a href="/wiki/Muggle-born" title="Muggle-born">Muggle-borns</a>, which led to her being disowned<sup id="cite_ref-OTP_6-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OTP-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup> and shunned by her own sisters. Thus, it can be assumed that the two loved each other greatly. Ted comforted his wife when they were unsure what happened to their daughter after the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_the_Seven_Potters" title="Battle of the Seven Potters">Battle of the Seven Potters</a>, reassuring her that, as an <a href="/wiki/Auror" title="Auror">Auror</a>, Tonks had been through worse.<sup id="cite_ref-DH_7-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Order_of_the_Phoenix">Order of the Phoenix</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Andromeda_Tonks?veaction=edit&amp;section=16" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Order of the Phoenix">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Andromeda_Tonks?action=edit&amp;section=16" title="Edit section: Order of the Phoenix"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Andromeda was not an official member of the <a href="/wiki/Order_of_the_Phoenix" title="Order of the Phoenix">Order of the Phoenix</a>, but nevertheless, she supported the Order's ideals (given her marriage to a <a href="/wiki/Ted_Tonks" class="mw-redirect" title="Ted Tonks">Muggle-born</a>). As her daughter and son-in-law were members, she would allow the Order use of her house as a safe house. It is likely that she has a good relation with all of the members, and because she was at Hogwarts at the same time as <a href="/wiki/Arthur_Weasley" title="Arthur Weasley">Arthur and</a> <a href="/wiki/Molly_Weasley" title="Molly Weasley">Molly Weasley</a>, a friendship might have started there. The degree and nature of her involvement during the <a href="/wiki/First_Wizarding_War" title="First Wizarding War">First Wizarding War</a> is unknown, but it is reasonable to assume that she helped them in some way. Andromeda proved her loyalty in 1998 when she was tortured by Death Eater in her home and did not reveal anything of importance regarding Harry or the Order, despite the fact that she was under torture.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Andromeda_Tonks?veaction=edit&amp;section=17" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Andromeda_Tonks?action=edit&amp;section=17" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Many members of the <a href="/wiki/House_of_Black" title="House of Black">House of Black</a> — such as <a href="/wiki/Sirius_Black" title="Sirius Black">Sirius</a>, <a href="/wiki/Bellatrix_Lestrange" title="Bellatrix Lestrange">Bellatrix</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Cygnus_Black_(disambiguation)" title="Cygnus Black (disambiguation)">Cygnus</a> — appear to be named after stars or constellations, and the name Andromeda follows this pattern. It is a northern <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Andromeda_(constellation)" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Andromeda (constellation)">constellation</a> that contains the Andromeda Galaxy and was named after the character from Greek mythology: a <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Andromeda_(mythology)" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Andromeda (mythology)">princess</a> who was chained to a rock to be sacrificed to a sea monster, as divine punishment for her mother's (Cassiopeia) boasting, but was saved by the hero Perseus, whom she then married. This may allude to Andromeda Tonks' estrangement from her aristocratic, but cruel extended family.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Andromeda_Tonks?veaction=edit&amp;section=18" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Andromeda_Tonks?action=edit&amp;section=18" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>Out of the three Black sisters, Andromeda was the only sister that has not appeared in any of the films.</li>
<li>Andromeda is also the only one of the five Black cousins who has not appeared in the films, as her two sisters appeared in the <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Half-Blood_Prince_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince (film)">Half-Blood Prince</a></i> through to the <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Deathly_Hallows:_Part_2" title="Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 2">Deathly Hallows: Part 2</a></i>, Bellatrix also appearing in the <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Order_of_the_Phoenix_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix (film)">Order of the Phoenix.</a> </i><a href="/wiki/Sirius_Black" title="Sirius Black">Sirius Black</a> appeared in <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Prisoner_of_Azkaban" title="Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban">Prisoner of Azkaban</a></i> through <i>Order of the Phoenix</i> and in <i>Deathly Hallows: Part 2,</i> and a younger <a href="/wiki/Regulus_Black" title="Regulus Black">Regulus Black</a> appeared in one of <a href="/wiki/Horace_Slughorn" title="Horace Slughorn">Horace Slughorn</a>'s photographs in the <i>Half-Blood Prince</i>.</li>
<li>Due to the similarities in appearance shared by Andromeda and her sister, <a href="/wiki/Bellatrix_Lestrange" title="Bellatrix Lestrange">Bellatrix Lestrange</a>, fans of the Harry Potter series set up an online petition to have <a href="/wiki/Helena_Bonham_Carter" title="Helena Bonham Carter">Helena Bonham Carter</a>, who plays Bellatrix, play Andromeda in <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Deathly_Hallows:_Part_1" title="Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 1">Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 1</a></i>.<sup id="cite_ref-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup> However, as Harry and Hagrid escape to <a href="/wiki/The_Burrow" title="The Burrow">the Burrow</a> rather than the Tonks house, Andromeda and Ted were both cut from the film.</li></ul>
