<p><b>Evan Rosier</b> (Between <a href="/wiki/1953" title="1953">1953</a> &amp; <a href="/wiki/1966" title="1966">1966</a><sup id="cite_ref-birthschool_1-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-birthschool-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> – c. <a href="/wiki/1981" title="1981">1981</a><sup id="cite_ref-died_2-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-died-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>) was a <a href="/wiki/Pure-blood" title="Pure-blood">pure-blood</a> <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a>, possibly son of <a href="/wiki/Rosier" title="Rosier">Rosier</a>, and a <a href="/wiki/Slytherin" title="Slytherin">Slytherin</a> student at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> during the <a href="/wiki/1970s" title="1970s">1970s</a>. After graduating from Hogwarts, he became a <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eater</a> of <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a> and supported him in the <a href="/wiki/First_Wizarding_War" title="First Wizarding War">First Wizarding War</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-PM_8-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PM-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup> Towards the end of the war he was pursued by <a href="/wiki/Auror" title="Auror">Aurors</a> and was killed by <a href="/wiki/Alastor_Moody" title="Alastor Moody">Alastor Moody</a> while trying to resist arrest.<sup id="cite_ref-GoF30_5-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GoF30-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Evan_Rosier?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Evan_Rosier?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_life">Early life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Evan_Rosier?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Early life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Evan_Rosier?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Early life"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<div class="quote">
<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire">"<i>Snape knew more curses when he arrived at school than half the kids in seventh year, and he was part of a gang of Slytherins who nearly all turned out to be Death Eaters.</i>"</span></dd>
<dd>&#8212;<a href="/wiki/Sirius_Black" title="Sirius Black">Sirius Black</a> talks about the Slytherin gang that Rosier was a part of<sup class="noprint"><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Goblet_of_Fire" title="Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire">[src]</a></sup></dd>
<dd></dd></dl></div><p>Possibly related to <a href="/wiki/Rosier" title="Rosier">Rosier</a> or <a href="/wiki/Druella_Black" title="Druella Black">Druella</a>, Evan attended <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> in the <a href="/wiki/1970s" title="1970s">1970s</a> and was <a href="/wiki/Sorting_ceremony" title="Sorting ceremony">Sorted</a> into <a href="/wiki/Slytherin" title="Slytherin">Slytherin</a> house. According to <a href="/wiki/Sirius_Black" title="Sirius Black">Sirius Black</a>, he was part of a <a href="/wiki/Gang_of_Slytherins" title="Gang of Slytherins">group of Slytherins</a> and aspiring <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a> which included <a href="/wiki/Severus_Snape" title="Severus Snape">Severus Snape</a>, as well as <a href="/wiki/Wilkes" title="Wilkes">Wilkes</a>, <a href="/wiki/Avery_(Marauder-era)" class="mw-redirect" title="Avery (Marauder-era)">Avery</a><sup id="cite_ref-GoF27_9-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GoF27-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup> and <a href="/wiki/Mulciber_(Marauder-era)" class="mw-redirect" title="Mulciber (Marauder-era)">Mulciber</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-DH33_10-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH33-10">&#91;10&#93;</a></sup> They all entered the service of <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a> during the height of the <a href="/wiki/First_Wizarding_War" title="First Wizarding War">First Wizarding War</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-GoF30_5-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GoF30-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>
</p><h3><span class="mw-headline" id="First_Wizarding_War_and_death">First Wizarding War and death</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Evan_Rosier?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: First Wizarding War and death">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Evan_Rosier?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: First Wizarding War and death"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<div class="quote">
<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire">"<i>Took a bit of me with him, though.</i>"</span></dd>
<dd>&#8212;<a href="/wiki/Alastor_Moody" title="Alastor Moody">Alastor Moody</a> regarding Rosier's death<sup class="noprint"><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Goblet_of_Fire" title="Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire">[src]</a></sup></dd>
<dd></dd></dl></div><p>During the <a href="/wiki/First_Wizarding_War" title="First Wizarding War">First Wizarding War</a>, the Death Eaters were fiercely pursued by <a href="/wiki/Auror" title="Auror">Aurors</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-GoF30_5-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GoF30-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> Evan was eventually caught by famous Auror <a href="/wiki/Alastor_Moody" title="Alastor Moody">Alastor Moody</a> around the same time as one of his comrades, <a href="/wiki/Wilkes" title="Wilkes">Wilkes</a>. The two resisted arrest, Rosier even blasting a significant part of Moody's nose off during their <a href="/wiki/Duel_between_Alastor_Moody_and_Evan_Rosier" title="Duel between Alastor Moody and Evan Rosier">duel</a>. Moody defeated and killed Evan.<sup id="cite_ref-GoF30_5-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GoF30-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>A year later, <a href="/wiki/Igor_Karkaroff" title="Igor Karkaroff">Igor Karkaroff</a>, while selling out his comrades to the <a href="/wiki/Council_of_Magical_Law" title="Council of Magical Law">Council of Magical Law</a> in exchange for his own freedom, named Evan, but was informed of his death, making his naming of Rosier worthless.<sup id="cite_ref-GoF30_5-10" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GoF30-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> According to Karkaroff, Evan was among Voldemort's important supporters.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Personality_and_traits">Personality and traits</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Evan_Rosier?veaction=edit&amp;section=4" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Personality and traits">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Evan_Rosier?action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Personality and traits"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Little is known about Evan Rosier's personality, except the fact that he chose to resist and try to avoid imprisonment.<sup id="cite_ref-GoF30_5-11" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GoF30-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> Rosier was courageous enough to resist capture from the legendary auror Alastor Moody, rather than flee to save his own neck, something that <a href="/wiki/Slytherin" title="Slytherin">Slytherins</a> (such as <a href="/wiki/Phineas_Nigellus_Black" title="Phineas Nigellus Black">Phineas Nigellus Black</a>) called "bravery".<sup id="cite_ref-OP23_11-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OP23-11">&#91;11&#93;</a></sup> This also shows that Evan was willing to fight to the death if necessary. Furthermore, Rosier likely had a <a href="/wiki/Pure-blood" title="Pure-blood">pure-blood</a> <a href="/wiki/Pure-blood_supremacy" title="Pure-blood supremacy">obsession</a> and a passion for the <a href="/wiki/Dark_Arts" title="Dark Arts">Dark Arts</a>, like most of his fellow Death Eater friends at Hogwarts.<sup id="cite_ref-DH33_10-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH33-10">&#91;10&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Magical_abilities_and_skills">Magical abilities and skills</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Evan_Rosier?veaction=edit&amp;section=5" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Evan_Rosier?action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li><b><a href="/wiki/Martial_magic" title="Martial magic">Martial magic</a></b>: Evan Rosier was a reasonably skilled <a href="/wiki/Duelling" title="Duelling">duellist</a>, as he was capable of duelling with <a href="/wiki/Alastor_Moody" title="Alastor Moody">Alastor Moody</a>, a powerful <a href="/wiki/Auror" title="Auror">Auror</a>, and badly injuring him, blasting a part of his nose. However, he was killed by Moody during the same duel.<sup id="cite_ref-GoF30_5-12" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GoF30-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> Still, the fact that Moody generally tried to avoid killing his enemies when feasible means that Evan likely fought hard enough that he was simply too difficult to capture alive.</li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Dark_Arts" title="Dark Arts">Dark Arts</a></b>: As a Death Eater, Rosier would have been accomplished with dark magic, including the <a href="/wiki/Unforgivable_Curses" title="Unforgivable Curses">Unforgivable Curses</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-GoF30_5-13" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GoF30-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> Given that Alastor Moody was unable to heal the wounds sustained to his nose,<sup id="cite_ref-GoF30_5-14" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GoF30-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> it's likely that Evan hit him with a <a href="/wiki/Dark_charm" title="Dark charm">Dark charm</a>, as the injuries caused by those spells were generally difficult to heal.</li></ul>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Evan_Rosier?veaction=edit&amp;section=6" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Evan_Rosier?action=edit&amp;section=6" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li><i>Evan</i> is a Greek name meaning "of noble birth",<sup id="cite_ref-12" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-12">&#91;12&#93;</a></sup> possibly a reference to the belief that <a href="/wiki/Pure-blood" title="Pure-blood">pure-bloods</a> are the wizarding world's aristocracy. It can also be a <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Evan" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Evan">Welsh name</a> meaning "young warrior", which Evan Rosier was.</li>
<li>Rosier is the name of a lesser demon in folklore.</li></ul>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Evan_Rosier?veaction=edit&amp;section=7" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Evan_Rosier?action=edit&amp;section=7" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>It's likely that he is the son of <a href="/wiki/Rosier_(male)" title="Rosier (male)">Rosier</a>, one of Lord Voldemort's earliest Death Eaters.</li>
<li>Evan also may have been related to <a href="/wiki/Druella_Black" title="Druella Black">Druella Black</a> née Rosier, and thus to her daughters <a href="/wiki/Bellatrix_Lestrange" title="Bellatrix Lestrange">Bellatrix</a>, <a href="/wiki/Andromeda_Tonks" title="Andromeda Tonks">Andromeda</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Narcissa_Malfoy" title="Narcissa Malfoy">Narcissa</a>, though this is unconfirmed. Given the fact that Evan was born in either the 1950s or the 1960s, Druella would be the right age to be his aunt, so it is likely that Evan is the cousin of Bellatrix, Andromeda and Narcissa. Also, unlike the muggle world where people with the same surname can be totally unrelated, wizards with the same name are, usually, related in someway or another.</li>
<li>In the book, Moody remarks on how Rosier had taken a part of his nose with him when they were fighting. However, in the film, Moody points to his eye since his nose was still intact.</li></ul>
