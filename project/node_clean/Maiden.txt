<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="The_Warlock">The Warlock</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Maiden?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: The Warlock">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Maiden?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: The Warlock"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<div class="quote">
<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;The Tales of Beedle the Bard (real)">"<i>It might have taken the warlock fifty years to find such a woman, yet it so happened that the very day after he decided to seek her, a maiden answer his every wish arrived in the neighbourhood to visit her kinsfolk</i>"</span></dd>
<dd>&#8212;The Warlock meets the maiden<sup class="noprint"><a href="/wiki/The_Tales_of_Beedle_the_Bard_(real)" title="The Tales of Beedle the Bard (real)">[src]</a></sup></dd>

<p>The <a href="/wiki/Warlock_(The_Warlock%27s_Hairy_Heart)" title="Warlock (The Warlock&#39;s Hairy Heart)">Warlock</a> decided to find an astoundingly beautiful, magically talented, and wealthy wife, so that he is envied by all, and it just so happened that he found a maiden who met every expectation the very next day. The maiden was fascinated as well as repelled by the Warlock's cold and reclusive personality, but agreed to attend a feast at his castle. The <a href="/wiki/Warlock_(The_Warlock%27s_Hairy_Heart)" title="Warlock (The Warlock&#39;s Hairy Heart)">Warlock</a> attempted to flatter the maiden, but she confronted him saying that she would only believe these lovely words if she thought he had a heart. The Warlock thus took her down to the dungeon of the castle and showed her an enchanted crystal casket, within which lay his own beating heart.<sup id="cite_ref-TBB_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-TBB-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<div class="quote">
<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;The Tales of Beedle the Bard (real)">"<i>You speak well, Warlock, and I would be delighted by your attentions, if only I thought you had a heart!</i>"</span></dd>
<dd>&#8212;The maiden confronting the <a href="/wiki/Warlock_(The_Warlock%27s_Hairy_Heart)" title="Warlock (The Warlock&#39;s Hairy Heart)">Warlock</a><sup class="noprint"><a href="/wiki/The_Tales_of_Beedle_the_Bard_(real)" title="The Tales of Beedle the Bard (real)">[src]</a></sup></dd>
<dd></dd></dl></div>
<p>When she met the <a href="/wiki/Warlock_(The_Warlock%27s_Hairy_Heart)" title="Warlock (The Warlock&#39;s Hairy Heart)">warlock</a>, she was fascinated as well as repelled by him, but agrees to attend a feast at his castle. The <a href="/wiki/Warlock_(The_Warlock%27s_Hairy_Heart)" title="Warlock (The Warlock&#39;s Hairy Heart)">Warlock</a> attempted to flatter the maiden, but she confronts him saying that she would only believe these lovely words if she thought he had a heart. The warlock then took her to the deepest dungeon to show his most valuable treasure, his own beating heart. The maiden was repelled by the beating heart and made the <a href="/wiki/Warlock_(The_Warlock%27s_Hairy_Heart)" title="Warlock (The Warlock&#39;s Hairy Heart)">Warlock</a> put it back in his chest. Once he had done so, the smell of her hair as well as the beauty of her skin pierced his newly awakened heart. Soon he kills the maiden in an attempt to exchange his own heart for hers. Having always disregarded love as a sign of weakness, coupled with his newfound inhumanity, the warlock felt absolutely no sadness nor regret in killing the maiden.<sup id="cite_ref-TBB_1-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-TBB-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>

</p><h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Personality_and_traits">Personality and traits</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Maiden?veaction=edit&amp;section=8" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Personality and traits">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Maiden?action=edit&amp;section=8" title="Edit section: Personality and traits"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<div class="quote">
<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;The Tales of Beedle the Bard (real)">"<i>... the unnamed maiden of </i><a href="/wiki/The_Warlock%27s_Hairy_Heart" title="The Warlock&#39;s Hairy Heart">The Warlock's Hairy Heart</a><i> - acts more like <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">our</a> idea of a story book princess</i>"</span></dd>

<dd></dd></dl></div><p>The unnamed maiden acts more like the idea of a <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggle</a> storybook princess, than the rest of the heroines in <i><a href="/wiki/The_Tales_of_Beedle_the_Bard_(real)" title="The Tales of Beedle the Bard (real)">The Tales of Beedle the Bard</a></i>. Her main strength was probably her perception: she was able to sense the coldness that lay behind the warmth of the Warlock's flattery, where others failed to see it. She was also described as a prodigiously skilled, very wealthy witch.<sup id="cite_ref-TBB_1-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-TBB-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Magical_abilities_and_skills">Magical abilities and skills</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Maiden?veaction=edit&amp;section=9" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Maiden?action=edit&amp;section=9" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li><b><a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">Magical</a> Mastery</b>: The maiden was described as a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch</a> of prodigious skill, meaning she was a very powerful and talented witch who was highly proficient in many different fields and branches of <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magic</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-TBB_1-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-TBB-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li><b>Magical Empathy</b>: The maiden appears to have some version of magical empathy, as she is able to sense the true feelings and emotions of one, as she did with the <a href="/wiki/Warlock_(The_Warlock%27s_Hairy_Heart)" title="Warlock (The Warlock&#39;s Hairy Heart)">warlock</a>, possibly <a href="/wiki/Legilimency" title="Legilimency">Legilimency</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-TBB_1-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-TBB-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup></li></ul>
