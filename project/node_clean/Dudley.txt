<p><b>Mr Dudley</b> may have been a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> who worked in the <a href="/wiki/Department_of_Magical_Accidents_and_Catastrophes" title="Department of Magical Accidents and Catastrophes">Department of Magical Accidents and Catastrophes</a>. When <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>, <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ron Weasley</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione Granger</a> were caught by <a href="/wiki/Snatchers" title="Snatchers">Snatchers</a> in <a href="/wiki/1998" title="1998">1998</a>, Harry quickly invented the pseudonym Vernon Dudley to conceal his identity, combining the first names of his <a href="/wiki/Vernon_Dursley" title="Vernon Dursley">uncle</a> and <a href="/wiki/Dudley_Dursley" title="Dudley Dursley">cousin</a>. Pressed for the identity of his father by <a href="/wiki/Scabior" title="Scabior">Scabior</a>, one of the Snatchers, Harry claimed to be the son of a wizard who worked in the Department of Magical Accidents and Catastrophes. Scabior responded to this by stating that he believed someone named Dudley actually worked in that department.<sup id="cite_ref-DH23_1-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH23-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
