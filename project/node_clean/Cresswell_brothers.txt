<p>The <b>Cresswell brothers</b> were the sons of <a href="/wiki/Dirk_Cresswell" title="Dirk Cresswell">Dirk Cresswell</a> and his <a href="/wiki/Dirk_Cresswell%27s_wife" title="Dirk Cresswell&#39;s wife">wife</a>. In <a href="/wiki/1997" title="1997">1997</a>, their father was forced to go on the run after <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a> <a href="/wiki/Fall_of_the_Ministry_of_Magic" title="Fall of the Ministry of Magic">took over</a> the <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">Ministry of Magic</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-DH13_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH13-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> In <a href="/wiki/1998" title="1998">1998</a>, he was caught and killed by <a href="/wiki/Snatchers" title="Snatchers">Snatchers</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-DH22_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH22-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> It is unknown what happened to them after <a href="/wiki/1998" title="1998">1998</a>.
</p>
