<b>Mirabella Plunkett</b> (b. <a href="/wiki/1839" class="mw-redirect" title="1839">1839</a>) was a <a href="/wiki/Great_Britain" title="Great Britain">British</a> <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch</a> famous for falling in love with a <a href="/wiki/Unidentified_Merman" title="Unidentified Merman">merman</a> in <a href="/wiki/Loch_Lomond" title="Loch Lomond">Loch Lomond</a> while on holiday. After her parents forbade her to marry him, she <a href="/wiki/Transfiguration" title="Transfiguration">transfigured</a> herself into a <a href="/wiki/Haddock" title="Haddock">haddock</a> and was never seen again. She was featured on a <a href="/wiki/Chocolate_Frog_Card" title="Chocolate Frog Card">Chocolate Frog Card</a> and on a <a href="/wiki/Portrait" title="Portrait">portrait</a> hung at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Castle" title="Hogwarts Castle">Hogwarts Castle</a>.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Mirabella_Plunkett?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Mirabella_Plunkett?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_life">Early life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Mirabella_Plunkett?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Early life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Mirabella_Plunkett?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Early life"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Mirabella Plunkett was born in <a href="/wiki/1839" class="mw-redirect" title="1839">1839</a> to <a href="/wiki/Mirabella_Plunkett%27s_father" title="Mirabella Plunkett&#39;s father">Mr</a> and <a href="/wiki/Mirabella_Plunkett%27s_mother" title="Mirabella Plunkett&#39;s mother">Mrs Plunkett</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-HPPAVG_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HPPAVG-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> Nothing about her background or education is known.
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Finding_love">Finding love</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Mirabella_Plunkett?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Finding love">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Mirabella_Plunkett?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Finding love"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>At some point in her life, Mirabella travelled to <a href="/wiki/Loch_Lomond" title="Loch Lomond">Loch Lomond</a> on holiday, where she met and fell in <a href="/wiki/Love" title="Love">love</a> with a <a href="/wiki/Unidentified_Merman" title="Unidentified Merman">merman</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-HPPAVG_2-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HPPAVG-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Mirabella and the merman decided to <a href="/wiki/Marriage" title="Marriage">marry</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-HPPAVG_2-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HPPAVG-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> However, Mirabella's parents objected strongly, and forbade her to marry him.<sup id="cite_ref-HPPAVG_2-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HPPAVG-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> In response, Mirabella <a href="/wiki/Transfiguration" title="Transfiguration">transfigured</a> herself into a <a href="/wiki/Haddock" title="Haddock">haddock</a>, never to be seen again.<sup id="cite_ref-HPPAVG_2-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HPPAVG-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> It is unknown if she ever returned to her <a href="/wiki/Merpeople" title="Merpeople">merman</a> lover once she became a haddock.
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Legacy">Legacy</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Mirabella_Plunkett?veaction=edit&amp;section=4" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Legacy">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Mirabella_Plunkett?action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Legacy"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>After her disappearance, Mirabella was not forgotten. She was eventually immortalised on a <a href="/wiki/Chocolate_Frog_Card" title="Chocolate Frog Card">Chocolate Frog Card</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-HPPAVG_2-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HPPAVG-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> There was also a <a href="/wiki/Portrait" title="Portrait">portrait</a> of her hung at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Castle" title="Hogwarts Castle">Hogwarts Castle</a>, which guarded a secret shortcut between the first-floor landing of the <a href="/wiki/Grand_Staircase" title="Grand Staircase">Grand Staircase</a> and the <a href="/wiki/Entrance_Hall" title="Entrance Hall">Entrance Hall</a> side room.<sup id="cite_ref-HPPAVGPC_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HPPAVGPC-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> The password required to gain access to said shortcut was <i>haddock</i>.<sup id="cite_ref-HPPAVGPC_3-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HPPAVGPC-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Magical_abilities_and_skills">Magical abilities and skills</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Mirabella_Plunkett?veaction=edit&amp;section=5" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Mirabella_Plunkett?action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li><b><a href="/wiki/Transfiguration" title="Transfiguration">Transfiguration</a></b>: Mirabella was highly proficient in the art of transfiguration, given how she successfully transfigured herself into a <a href="/wiki/Haddock" title="Haddock">haddock</a> fish; a famous achievement in the field which gained her recognition on a <a href="/wiki/Chocolate_Frog_Card" title="Chocolate Frog Card">Chocolate Frog Card</a>. Given how both <a href="/wiki/Human_Transfiguration" title="Human Transfiguration">Human Transfiguration</a> and <a href="/wiki/Trans-Species_Transformation" title="Trans-Species Transformation">Trans-Species Transformation</a> are incredibly complex and advanced branches of transfiguration, this shows her remarkable talent she possessed in this notably very difficult branch of <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magic</a>.</li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Love" title="Love">Love</a></b>: Mirabella was an incredibly loving <a href="/wiki/Soul" title="Soul">soul</a>, who famously fell in love with a <a href="/wiki/Unidentified_Merman" title="Unidentified Merman">Merman</a> and even went to the drastic measures of transfiguring herself into a haddock, just so she could be with her lover in the <a href="/wiki/Water" title="Water">water</a> together as fish, showing her love to have been very strong and powerful indeed.</li></ul>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Mirabella_Plunkett?veaction=edit&amp;section=6" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Mirabella_Plunkett?action=edit&amp;section=6" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The name "Mirabella" is derived from Latin mirabilis, "wonderful".
</p>
