This <b>individual</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Snatchers" title="Snatchers">Snatcher</a>, in <a href="/wiki/1998" title="1998">1998</a>. He was among the group of Snatchers, led by <a href="/wiki/Fenrir_Greyback" title="Fenrir Greyback">Fenrir Greyback</a>, who captured <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>, <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ron Weasley</a> and <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione Granger</a> and brought them to <a href="/wiki/Malfoy_Manor" title="Malfoy Manor">Malfoy Manor</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-DH23_2-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH23-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> He later participated in the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a> on <a href="/wiki/2_May" title="2 May">2 May</a> <a href="/wiki/1998" title="1998">1998</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-DHF2_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DHF2-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
