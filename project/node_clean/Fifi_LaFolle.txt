<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Fifi_LaFolle?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Fifi_LaFolle?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_Life">Early Life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Fifi_LaFolle?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Early Life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Fifi_LaFolle?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Early Life"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Fifi LaFolle was born in <a href="/wiki/1888" class="mw-redirect" title="1888">1888</a> to parents of unknown blood status. She either was home schooled by her parents or attended <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a>, which she would have attended from 1899 to 1906.
</p><p>In her adult life, she began writing her famous book collection, titled the <i><a href="/wiki/Enchanted_Encounters" title="Enchanted Encounters">Enchanted Encounters</a></i>.<sup id="cite_ref-JKRS_1-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-JKRS-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Death_and_Post-Mortem">Death and Post-Mortem</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Fifi_LaFolle?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Death and Post-Mortem">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Fifi_LaFolle?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Death and Post-Mortem"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>LaFolle died in <a href="/wiki/1971" title="1971">1971</a> at the age of 83. She was on a <a href="/wiki/Chocolate_Frog_Card" title="Chocolate Frog Card">Chocolate Frog Card</a> after her death and she was awarded 'Wizard of the Month' in <a href="/wiki/2005" title="2005">2005</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-JKRS_1-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-JKRS-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Fifi_LaFolle?veaction=edit&amp;section=4" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Fifi_LaFolle?action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>Fifi LaFolle was Wizard of the Month for <a href="/wiki/October" title="October">October</a> <a href="/wiki/2005" title="2005">2005</a>.</li>
<li>The image accompanying her entry on the website appears to be based on <a href="/wiki/Great_Britain" title="Great Britain">British</a> romance writer <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Barbara_Cartland" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Barbara Cartland">Barbara Cartland</a>.</li>
<li>In French, 'La Folle' means 'the mad woman.'</li></ul>
