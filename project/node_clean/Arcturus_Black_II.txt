<b>Arcturus Black</b> (<a href="/wiki/1884" class="mw-redirect" title="1884">1884</a>–<a href="/wiki/1959" title="1959">1959</a>) was a <a href="/wiki/Pure-blood" title="Pure-blood">pure-blood</a> <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> and the third son of <a href="/wiki/Phineas_Nigellus_Black" title="Phineas Nigellus Black">Phineas Nigellus Black</a> and <a href="/wiki/Ursula_Flint" title="Ursula Flint">Ursula Flint</a>. He was also brother of <a href="/wiki/Sirius_Black_II" title="Sirius Black II">Sirius</a>, <a href="/wiki/Phineas_Black" title="Phineas Black">Phineas</a>, <a href="/wiki/Cygnus_Black_II" title="Cygnus Black II">Cygnus</a> and <a href="/wiki/Belvina_Black" title="Belvina Black">Belvina</a>. He married <a href="/wiki/Lysandra_Yaxley" title="Lysandra Yaxley">Lysandra Yaxley</a>, and the couple had three children: <a href="/wiki/Callidora_Black" title="Callidora Black">Callidora</a>, <a href="/wiki/Cedrella_Black" title="Cedrella Black">Cedrella</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Charis_Black" title="Charis Black">Charis</a>. Through his daughter Cedrella, who was disowned for marrying "<a href="/wiki/Blood_traitor" title="Blood traitor">blood traitor</a>" <a href="/wiki/Septimus_Weasley" title="Septimus Weasley">Septimus Weasley</a>, Arcturus is the maternal grandfather of <a href="/wiki/Arthur_Weasley" title="Arthur Weasley">Arthur Weasley</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-BFT_1-23" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-BFT-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Arcturus_Black_II?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Arcturus_Black_II?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p><a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Arcturus" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Arcturus">Arcturus</a> is the name of the brightest star in the constellation Boötes. Its name derives from Ancient Greek Αρκτοῦρος, meaning "bear guard"; this refers to the story in Greek mythology that Zeus placed Arcturus in the sky to look after Arcas and Callisto, who were turned into bears and then into the constellations Ursa Major and Ursa Minor to protect them from Hera's wrath.
</p>

<ul><li>Based on a statement by <a href="/wiki/Horace_Slughorn" title="Horace Slughorn">Horace Slughorn</a>, Arcturus may have been a member of the <a href="/wiki/Slytherin" title="Slytherin">Slytherin House</a>. However, he attended Hogwarts c. <a href="/wiki/1895" class="mw-redirect" title="1895">1895</a> - <a href="/wiki/1902" title="1902">1902</a>, when Slughorn wasn't Potions teacher.</li>
<li>Arcturus was named after <a href="/wiki/Arcturus_Black_I" title="Arcturus Black I">his great-uncle</a>. After that, <a href="/wiki/Arcturus_Black_III" title="Arcturus Black III">his nephew</a> was named Arcturus.</li></ul>
