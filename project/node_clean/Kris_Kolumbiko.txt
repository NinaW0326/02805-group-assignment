<p><b>Kris Kolumbiko</b> (b. <a href="/wiki/1730s" title="1730s">1730s</a>/<a href="/wiki/1740s" title="1740s">1740s</a>) was a <a href="/wiki/Slytherin" title="Slytherin">Slytherin</a> <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">student</a> at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> in the <a href="/wiki/1750s" title="1750s">1750s</a>. He was <a href="/wiki/Quidditch_Captain#Hogwarts_Quidditch_Captains" title="Quidditch Captain">Captain</a> of the <a href="/wiki/Slytherin" title="Slytherin">Slytherin</a> <a href="/wiki/Quidditch" title="Quidditch">Quidditch</a> <a href="/wiki/Slytherin_Quidditch_team" title="Slytherin Quidditch team">team</a> from <a href="/wiki/1752" class="mw-redirect" title="1752">1752</a> to <a href="/wiki/1756" class="mw-redirect" title="1756">1756</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup></p><figure class="thumb tleft show-info-icon" style="width: 170px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/a/ae/Slytherin_Captains_Plaque_2.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20111029034854" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/a/ae/Slytherin_Captains_Plaque_2.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/168?cb=20111029034854" decoding="async" width="168" height="236" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="Slytherin Captains Plaque 2.jpg" data-image-key="Slytherin_Captains_Plaque_2.jpg" /></a> 	 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:Slytherin_Captains_Plaque_2.jpg" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption">Kris Kolumbiko's name appearing on a plaque</p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure>
