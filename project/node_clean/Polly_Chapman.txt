<b>Polly Chapman</b> (born <a href="/wiki/2006" title="2006">2005</a> or <a href="/wiki/2007" title="2007">2006</a>) was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch</a> and <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor" title="Gryffindor">Gryffindor</a> student in <a href="/wiki/Albus_Potter" title="Albus Potter">Albus Potter</a>'s year at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a>.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Polly_Chapman?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Polly_Chapman?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_life">Early life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Polly_Chapman?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Early life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Polly_Chapman?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Early life"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Polly Chapman was born somewhere in the <a href="/wiki/Great_Britain" title="Great Britain">United Kingdom</a> between <a href="/wiki/1_September" title="1 September">1 September</a> <a href="/wiki/2006" title="2006">2005</a> and <a href="/wiki/31_August" title="31 August">31 August</a> <a href="/wiki/2007" title="2007">2006</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-act1scene4_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-act1scene4-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="First_year">First year</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Polly_Chapman?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: First year">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Polly_Chapman?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: First year"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Polly began attending <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> on 1 September <a href="/wiki/2017" title="2017">2017</a> and was sorted into <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor" title="Gryffindor">Gryffindor</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-act1scene4_3-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-act1scene4-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-act2scene11_4-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-act2scene11-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> During the <a href="/wiki/Sorting_ceremony" title="Sorting ceremony">sorting ceremony</a>, she remarked that it was not surprising that <a href="/wiki/Scorpius_Malfoy" title="Scorpius Malfoy">Scorpius Malfoy</a> was sorted into <a href="/wiki/Slytherin" title="Slytherin">Slytherin</a>, but expressed shock when <a href="/wiki/Albus_Potter" title="Albus Potter">Albus Potter</a> was placed in the same house.<sup id="cite_ref-act1scene4_3-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-act1scene4-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>When Albus Potter proved unable to get his <a href="/wiki/Broomstick" title="Broomstick">broomstick</a> to rise off the ground during their first <a href="/wiki/Flying_(class)" title="Flying (class)">flying</a> lesson with <a href="/wiki/Rolanda_Hooch" title="Rolanda Hooch">Madam Hooch</a>, Polly disparaged him, stating that he wasn't "like his father at all."<sup id="cite_ref-act1scene4_3-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-act1scene4-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Second_year">Second year</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Polly_Chapman?veaction=edit&amp;section=4" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Second year">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Polly_Chapman?action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Second year"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>During a <a href="/wiki/Potions_(class)" title="Potions (class)">Potions</a> lesson in their second year, Polly once again teased Albus, calling him an "irrelevance" and suggesting that even the school's <a href="/wiki/Portrait" title="Portrait">portraits</a> tried to avoid him when he walked past.<sup id="cite_ref-act1scene4_3-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-act1scene4-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Fourth_year">Fourth year</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Polly_Chapman?veaction=edit&amp;section=5" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Fourth year">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Polly_Chapman?action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Fourth year"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Polly's life was briefly changed in <a href="/wiki/2020" title="2020">2020</a> when Albus and Scorpius <a href="/wiki/Time-Turner" title="Time-Turner">altered the past.</a> In an alternate reality, Albus was in Gryffindor as well. However, in their <a href="/wiki/Defence_Against_the_Dark_Arts" title="Defence Against the Dark Arts">Defence Against the Dark Arts</a> lesson taught by <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione Granger</a>, Albus questioned Hermione about the changed circumstances which prompted her to deduct points from Gryffindor.<sup id="cite_ref-act2scene11_4-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-act2scene11-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> Polly angrily accused Albus of losing points on purpose because he "hate[d] Gryffindor and everyone [knew] it."<sup id="cite_ref-act2scene11_4-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-act2scene11-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> Professor Granger told Polly to sit down before she was forced to dock even more points.<sup id="cite_ref-act2scene11_4-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-act2scene11-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> Later, when Albus and Scorpius were discussing how their actions had changed history in the <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Library" title="Hogwarts Library">Hogwarts Library</a>, Polly shushed them.<sup id="cite_ref-act2scene16_5-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-act2scene16-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Her life was changed again when Albus and Scorpius meddled with the past a second time. Polly still attended Hogwarts in a world where Voldemort won and Albus was never born. Polly asked Scorpius whether he intended to invite her to the <a href="/wiki/Blood_Ball" title="Blood Ball">Blood Ball</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-act3scene2_6-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-act3scene2-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Personality_and_traits">Personality and traits</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Polly_Chapman?veaction=edit&amp;section=6" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Personality and traits">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Polly_Chapman?action=edit&amp;section=6" title="Edit section: Personality and traits"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Polly Chapman was a very confident and opinionated young witch. She tended to speak her mind and was unafraid of asking for something she wanted - such as asking Scorpius to the <a href="/wiki/Blood_Ball" title="Blood Ball">Blood Ball</a> in an alternate universe. Her wit was as sharp as her tongue, but could sometimes be harsh and cruel, particularly towards the shy and unsure Albus Potter, whom she often spitefully disparaged and mocked due to his perceived lack of abilities.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Polly_Chapman?veaction=edit&amp;section=7" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Polly_Chapman?action=edit&amp;section=7" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>In the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Cursed_Child_(play)#Original_West_End_production" title="Harry Potter and the Cursed Child (play)">original West End production</a> of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Cursed_Child" title="Harry Potter and the Cursed Child">Harry Potter and the Cursed Child</a></i>, Polly is played by <a href="/wiki/Claudia_Grant" title="Claudia Grant">Claudia Grant</a>.</li>
<li>In an alternative universe, Polly used Harry Potter's name as a form of insult or slang similar to "Oh Merlin". It is possible that it was considered normal to insult Potter from the grave.</li></ul>
