<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Antioch_Peverell%27s_killer?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Antioch_Peverell%27s_killer?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>In the 13th century, this wizard was at an inn when <a href="/wiki/Antioch_Peverell" title="Antioch Peverell">Antioch Peverell</a> came in after killing a wizard he had once quarrelled with. Peverell began to drink wine and brag about his his new <a href="/wiki/Elder_Wand" title="Elder Wand">indestructible wand</a> had granted him victory over his enemy, which the unknown wizard overheard.
</p><p>Envious of the wand's power the wizard waited until Peverell, drunk with wine, went to sleep. Slipping into his room, the wizard stole the wand and slit Peverell's throat to make sure he wasn't followed. The wizard then slipped into the night with his new powerful wand. It's unknown what happened to this wizard afterwards, though he likely suffered the same fate as Antioch and was killed by another for the possession of the <a href="/wiki/Elder_Wand" title="Elder Wand">Elder Wand</a>.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Antioch_Peverell%27s_killer?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Antioch_Peverell%27s_killer?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>The identity of this wizard is unknown, as is the wizard who killed him.</li>
<li>In the consoles versions of the video game adaption of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Deathly_Hallows:_Part_1_(video_game)" title="Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 1 (video game)">Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 1</a></i>, the killer used the <a href="/wiki/Killing_Curse" title="Killing Curse">Killing Curse</a> instead of a knife to murder Antioch.</li>
<li>In <i><a href="/wiki/LEGO_Harry_Potter:_Years_5-7" title="LEGO Harry Potter: Years 5-7">LEGO Harry Potter: Years 5-7</a></i>, he is omitted. Instead, his victim <a href="/wiki/Antioch_Peverell" title="Antioch Peverell">Antioch Peverell</a> got grabbed by <a href="/wiki/Death" title="Death">Death</a> but what happened to the <a href="/wiki/Elder_Wand" title="Elder Wand">Elder Wand</a> or who owned it before <a href="/wiki/Mykew_Gregorovitch" title="Mykew Gregorovitch">Gregorovitch</a> is unknown.</li></ul>
