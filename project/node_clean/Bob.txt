<p><b>Bob</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Great_Britain" title="Great Britain">British</a> <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> who worked for the <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">British Ministry of Magic</a>, under the <a href="/wiki/Department_for_the_Regulation_and_Control_of_Magical_Creatures" title="Department for the Regulation and Control of Magical Creatures">Department for the Regulation and Control of Magical Creatures</a>. He was described as big and bearded.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP7_1-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP7-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Bob?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Bob?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p><a href="/wiki/Arthur_Weasley" title="Arthur Weasley">Arthur Weasley</a> and <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> ran into Bob while waiting for a lift in the Ministry lobby on the <a href="/wiki/12_August" title="12 August">12 August</a> <a href="/wiki/1995" title="1995">1995</a>, the day of Harry's <a href="/wiki/Disciplinary_hearing_of_Harry_Potter" title="Disciplinary hearing of Harry Potter">disciplinary</a> hearing. He was carrying a large cardboard box that contained a <a href="/wiki/Fire-breathing_chicken" title="Fire-breathing chicken">Fire-breathing chicken</a> that he strongly suspected was a serious violation of the <a href="/wiki/Ban_on_Experimental_Breeding" title="Ban on Experimental Breeding">Ban on Experimental Breeding</a>. When the lift arrived, he got on with Arthur and Harry, and exited at the floor of the <a href="/wiki/Department_for_the_Regulation_and_Control_of_Magical_Creatures" title="Department for the Regulation and Control of Magical Creatures">Department for the Regulation and Control of Magical Creatures</a>, the department in which he worked for.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP7_1-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP7-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Magical_abilities_and_skills">Magical abilities and skills</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Bob?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Bob?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li><b><a href="/wiki/Magizoology" title="Magizoology">Magizoology</a></b>: As an employee for the <a href="/wiki/Department_for_the_Regulation_and_Control_of_Magical_Creatures" title="Department for the Regulation and Control of Magical Creatures">Department for the Regulation and Control of Magical Creatures</a> under the <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">British Ministry of Magic</a>, Bob would have been knowledgeable and experienced in the study of <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magical</a> <a href="/wiki/Creature" title="Creature">creatures</a>, such as with the handling and classification of them. He was able to quickly detect that a <a href="/wiki/Fire-breathing_chicken" title="Fire-breathing chicken">Fire-breathing chicken</a> was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a>-bred creature, showing his knowledge in this field.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP7_1-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP7-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup></li></ul>
