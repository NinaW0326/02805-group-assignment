<p><b>William Sayre</b> (d. <a href="/wiki/1608" class="mw-redirect" title="1608">1608</a>) was an <a href="/wiki/Ireland" title="Ireland">Irish</a> <a href="/wiki/Pure-blood" title="Pure-blood">pure-blood</a> <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a>. He was the husband of <a href="/wiki/Rionach_Sayre" title="Rionach Sayre">Rionach Sayre</a> and the father of <a href="/wiki/Isolt_Sayre" title="Isolt Sayre">Isolt Sayre</a>. Isolt was one of the founders of <a href="/wiki/Ilvermorny_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Ilvermorny School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Ilvermorny School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a>. William showed sympathy towards his <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggle</a> neighbours and because of that was murdered by his sister-in-law <a href="/wiki/Gormlaith_Gaunt" title="Gormlaith Gaunt">Gormlaith Gaunt</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-Pottermore_1-16" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Pottermore-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/William_Sayre?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/William_Sayre?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>William was a descendant of <a href="/wiki/Morrigan" title="Morrigan">Morrigan</a>, an <a href="/wiki/Animagus" title="Animagus">Animagus</a> whose animal form was a <a href="/wiki/Crow" title="Crow">crow</a>. By <a href="/wiki/1603" class="mw-redirect" title="1603">1603</a>, he had married a <a href="/wiki/Pure-blood" title="Pure-blood">pure-blood</a> <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch</a>, <a href="/wiki/Rionach_Sayre" title="Rionach Sayre">Rionach Gaunt</a> and was living at <a href="/wiki/Ilvermorny_Cottage" title="Ilvermorny Cottage">Ilvermorny Cottage</a> in <a href="/wiki/Coomloughra" title="Coomloughra">Coomloughra</a>, <a href="/wiki/County_Kerry" title="County Kerry">County Kerry</a>, <a href="/wiki/Ireland" title="Ireland">Ireland</a>. Their daughter, <a href="/wiki/Isolt_Sayre" title="Isolt Sayre">Isolt</a>, was born that year.<sup id="cite_ref-Pottermore_1-17" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Pottermore-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>William nicknamed his daughter ‚ÄòMorrigan‚Äô for her affinity for all natural things when she was young. He and his wife <a href="/wiki/Love" title="Love">loved</a> their daughter very much and were quietly helpful to their&#160;<a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggle</a>&#160;neighbours, producing <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magical</a> cures for <a href="/wiki/Human" title="Human">humans</a> and livestock alike.<sup id="cite_ref-Pottermore_1-18" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Pottermore-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>However, when Isolt was five, William and Rionach were killed in a fire started by his estranged sister-in-law, <a href="/wiki/Gormlaith_Gaunt" title="Gormlaith Gaunt">Gormlaith Gaunt</a>, who "saved" Isolt with the intention of raising her with a belief in <a href="/wiki/Pure-blood_supremacy" title="Pure-blood supremacy">pure-blood superiority</a>. Isolt eventually fled Gormlaith and moved to the <a href="/wiki/United_States_of_America" title="United States of America">New World</a>, where she befriended a <a href="/wiki/Pukwudgie" title="Pukwudgie">Pukwudgie</a>, who she named William in honour of her father. William had two granddaughters through Isolt's marriage to <a href="/wiki/James_Steward" title="James Steward">James Steward</a>, <a href="/wiki/Martha_Steward_II" title="Martha Steward II">Martha</a> and <a href="/wiki/Rionach_Steward" title="Rionach Steward">Rionach Steward</a>, as well as two grandsons by adoption, <a href="/wiki/Chadwick_Boot" title="Chadwick Boot">Chadwick</a> and <a href="/wiki/Webster_Boot" title="Webster Boot">Webster Boot</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-Pottermore_1-19" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Pottermore-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Personality_and_traits">Personality and traits</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/William_Sayre?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Personality and traits">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/William_Sayre?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Personality and traits"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>William, much like his wife, was a very kind-hearted and generous man and father, who <a href="/wiki/Love" title="Love">loved</a> his wife and daughter dearly and cared much for the well-being and happiness of others. Along with his life, he held very tolerant attitudes towards his <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggle</a> neighbours, helping them with <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magic</a>, meaning despite being a pure-blood, he viewed them as equal to <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">Wizardkind</a>, and was not a <a href="/wiki/Pure-blood_supremacy" title="Pure-blood supremacy">pure-blood supremacist</a> like many other pure-bloods.<sup id="cite_ref-Pottermore_1-20" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Pottermore-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Magical_abilities_and_skills">Magical abilities and skills</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/William_Sayre?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/William_Sayre?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li><b><a href="/wiki/Healing_magic" title="Healing magic">Healing magic</a></b>: William was proficient in the art of medical magic, as he was able to find <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magical</a> cures for his Muggle neighbour's illnesses and livestock alike, meaning he would have used <a href="/wiki/Healing_spell" title="Healing spell">healing spells</a>, <a href="/wiki/Potion" title="Potion">potions</a> and other magical remedies.<sup id="cite_ref-Pottermore_1-21" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Pottermore-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup></li></ul>
