<p><b>Nigel Wolpert</b><sup id="cite_ref-surname_8-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-surname-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup> (c.<a href="/wiki/1983" title="1983">1983</a>)<sup id="cite_ref-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup> was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> and a student who attended <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a>, during the <a href="/wiki/1990s" title="1990s">1990s</a>. He was <a href="/wiki/Sorting_ceremony" title="Sorting ceremony">sorted</a> into <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor" title="Gryffindor">Gryffindor</a> <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Houses" title="Hogwarts Houses">House</a> in 1994.<sup id="cite_ref-GOF_4-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> Nigel was an avid fan and friend of the famous <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> and was a member of <a href="/wiki/Dumbledore%27s_Army" title="Dumbledore&#39;s Army">Dumbledore's Army</a>, an organisation taught and led by Harry.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTPF_6-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTPF-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Nigel fought in the 1998 <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a>, despite being underage at the time.<sup id="cite_ref-DHF2_7-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DHF2-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup> He fought alongside his friends and presumably survived to see Harry defeat <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a> once and for all.<sup id="cite_ref-lives_10-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-lives-10">&#91;10&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<p>Nigel Wolpert began attending <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> in 1994. He started when famous student Harry Potter was in his fourth year of schooling and Nigel was <a href="/wiki/Sorting_ceremony" title="Sorting ceremony">sorted</a> into Gryffindor house. Nigel also appeared to have a great deal of respect for the school headmaster. During <a href="/wiki/Albus_Dumbledore" title="Albus Dumbledore">Dumbledore</a>'s welcoming speech, Nigel seemed completely captivated. This was in contrast with the other students who were getting distracted by friends, floating candles, and the Great Hall's enchanted ceiling.<sup id="cite_ref-GOF_4-10" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<p>Nigel had also shown an interest in <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> (who was dubbed the 'Boy Who Lived') and had asked <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ron Weasley</a> if he could get him Harry's autograph. Ron agreed to get him Harry's autograph in exchange for Nigel bringing Ron his mail that day, which Nigel did. It is unknown if he ever got Harry's autograph. Ron dismissed him after getting his mail and said they would talk about it later. Nigel then left looking very disappointed.<sup id="cite_ref-GOF_4-11" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Nigel was seen supporting champion Harry Potter throughout the <a href="/wiki/Triwizard_Tournament" title="Triwizard Tournament">Triwizard Tournament</a>. For instance, when Nigel was cheering for Harry after he emerged from the lake, <a href="/wiki/Draco_Malfoy" title="Draco Malfoy">Draco Malfoy</a> hit Nigel over the head and pulled his hat down over his face in irritation.<sup id="cite_ref-GOF_4-12" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Also, despite being only a first year Nigel attended the <a href="/wiki/Yule_Ball" title="Yule Ball">Yule Ball</a>. This means he must have been asked by someone much older than him, as third year <a href="/wiki/Ginevra_Weasley" title="Ginevra Weasley">Ginny Weasley</a> accepted <a href="/wiki/Neville_Longbottom" title="Neville Longbottom">Neville Longbottom</a>'s offer. Ginny doubted she had be asked by anyone else and being so young she would not have been allowed to attend otherwise. Only fourth year or older could attend the ball.<sup id="cite_ref-GOF_4-13" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<p>The following year, <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts</a> would be put under the watchful eyes of <a href="/wiki/Dolores_Umbridge" title="Dolores Umbridge">Dolores Umbridge</a>, the newly Ministry-appointed <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_High_Inquisitor" title="Hogwarts High Inquisitor">Hogwarts High Inquisitor</a> and <a href="/wiki/Defence_Against_the_Dark_Arts" title="Defence Against the Dark Arts">Defence Against the Dark Arts</a> teacher. Umbridge was making the students' lives a misery and was refusing to teach them any practical forms of Defence Against the Dark Arts. When <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> and <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione Granger</a> set up an organisation against this Nigel joined and became a part of Dumbledore's Army.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTPF_6-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTPF-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup>

<p>When attending the <a href="/wiki/Dumbledore%27s_Army" title="Dumbledore&#39;s Army">D.A.</a>'s meetings Nigel would often stay close to Harry, whom he looked up to. With Nigel's enthusiasm in lessons Harry would often use him as a 'test dummy', such as to demonstrate the Stunning Spell. He also assisted Harry by acting as a look-out as the students left the <a href="/wiki/Room_of_Requirement" title="Room of Requirement">Room of Requirement</a> at the end of the meetings. When Dolores Umbridge and her <a href="/wiki/Inquisitorial_Squad" title="Inquisitorial Squad">Inquisitorial Squad</a> discovered the D.A. meetings, Nigel was at the front of the class along with Harry. He was among the students to serve one of <a href="/wiki/Dolores_Umbridge" title="Dolores Umbridge">Umbridge</a>'s 'medieval' detentions as punishment for being a member of <a href="/wiki/Dumbledore%27s_Army" title="Dumbledore&#39;s Army">Dumbledore's Army</a>. Towards the end of the year <a href="/wiki/Fred_and_George_Weasley" title="Fred and George Weasley">Fred and George Weasley</a> put their plans of escape into action and Nigel was amongst the crowd of students and staff to witness their departure and cheered them off after they turned a school corridor into a swamp.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTPF_6-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTPF-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Magical_abilities_and_skills">Magical abilities and skills</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Nigel_Wolpert?veaction=edit&amp;section=9" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Nigel_Wolpert?action=edit&amp;section=9" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li><b><a href="/wiki/Defence_Against_the_Dark_Arts" title="Defence Against the Dark Arts">Defence Against the Dark Arts</a></b>: Since Nigel trained with <a href="/wiki/Dumbledore%27s_Army" title="Dumbledore&#39;s Army">Dumbledore's Army</a> under the instruction of <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>, he would have been skilled with defensive and offensive spells. When he was only twelve (in his second year), Nigel successfully used the <a href="/wiki/Stunning_Spell" title="Stunning Spell">Stunning Spell</a> on Harry.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTPF_6-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTPF-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup> He was also skilled and confident enough in this field to successfully duel against many <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a> alongside with his friends during the Battle of Hogwarts.<sup id="cite_ref-DHF2_7-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DHF2-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Duelling" title="Duelling">Duelling</a></b>: Despite his young age, Nigel was a surprisingly confident duellist who fought bravely in the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a> against Voldemort's dangerous army of Death Eaters.<sup id="cite_ref-DHF2_7-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DHF2-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup> He likely survived the battle, as his name was not mentioned among the <a href="/wiki/Fallen_Fifty" title="Fallen Fifty">Fallen Fifty</a>.</li></ul>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Nigel_Wolpert?veaction=edit&amp;section=11" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Nigel_Wolpert?action=edit&amp;section=11" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>"<i>Nigel</i>" is derived from the Latin "<i>Nigellus</i>"<i>.</i></li>
<li>The name Nigel also has Scottish origin in which it means "<i>Champion</i>".</li>
<li>The name Wolpert means "<i>descendent of Waldobert</i>".<sup id="cite_ref-11" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-11">&#91;11&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li>"<i>Waldobert</i>" is a Germanic name composed of the elements "<i>wald</i>", meaning "<i>rule</i>", and "<i>beraht</i>", meaning "<i>bright</i>". It was the name of a 7th-century French saint.<sup id="cite_ref-12" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-12">&#91;12&#93;</a></sup></li></ul>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Nigel_Wolpert?veaction=edit&amp;section=12" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Nigel_Wolpert?action=edit&amp;section=12" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>Nigel Wolpert is portrayed by <a href="/wiki/William_Melling" title="William Melling">William Melling</a> in <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Goblet_of_Fire_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire (film)">Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire</a></i>, <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Order_of_the_Phoenix_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix (film)">Order of the Phoenix</a></i>, <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Half-Blood_Prince_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince (film)">Half-Blood Prince</a></i> and in <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Deathly_Hallows:_Part_1" title="Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 1">Deathly Hallows: Part 1</a></i> and <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Deathly_Hallows:_Part_2" title="Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 2">2</a></i>. He has only ever appeared in the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_(film_series)" title="Harry Potter (film series)">films</a>, never in any of the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_(book_series)" title="Harry Potter (book series)">novels</a>. Nigel seems to fill a role as composite replacement for both <a href="/wiki/Colin_Creevey" title="Colin Creevey">Colin</a> and <a href="/wiki/Dennis_Creevey" title="Dennis Creevey">Dennis Creevey</a>.</li>
<li>While the script called for Nigel to die during the Battle of Hogwarts and be carried into the Great Hall (like Colin was in the book),<sup id="cite_ref-SK_13-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-SK-13">&#91;13&#93;</a></sup> William Melling noted at LeakyCon 2012 that he had grown so much that he was no longer able to fit the description and his death was not portrayed.<sup id="cite_ref-lives_10-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-lives-10">&#91;10&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li>In the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Order_of_the_Phoenix_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix (film)">film adaptation</a> of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Order_of_the_Phoenix" title="Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix">Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix</a></i>, Nigel attends the first meeting of what would become Dumbledore's Army in the Hog's Head, despite the fact that he would have only been a second year at the time, meaning he would not have been allowed to go to Hogsmeade.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/William_Melling" title="William Melling">William Melling</a> has a non-speaking role in the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Half-Blood_Prince_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince (film)">sixth film</a> and he is shown several times in the background. He originally was present in the bathroom during Draco Malfoy and Harry Potter's duel, but he was later cut from this scene.<sup id="cite_ref-14" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-14">&#91;14&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li>Nigel appears in both parts of the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Deathly_Hallows_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows (film)">film adaptation</a> of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Deathly_Hallows" title="Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows">Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows</a></i>: in <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Deathly_Hallows:_Part_1" title="Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 1">Part 1</a></i> he appears on the Hogwarts Express as a non-speaking character, in <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Deathly_Hallows:_Part_2" title="Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 2">Part 2</a></i> he appears in the <a href="/wiki/Room_of_Requirement" title="Room of Requirement">Room of Requirement</a> with the rest of the <a href="/wiki/Dumbledore%27s_Army" title="Dumbledore&#39;s Army">Dumbledore's Army</a>. His only line is: "Lightning has struck, I repeat, lightning has struck!", as he announces Harry's arrival. He says this when he warns the other students and the Order of the Phoenix of Harry Potter's arrival.
<ul><li>It should be noted that Nigel's role in <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Deathly_Hallows:_Part_2" title="Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 2">Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 2</a></i> seems to fill the role <a href="/wiki/Lee_Jordan" title="Lee Jordan">Lee Jordan</a> had in the novel.</li></ul></li>
<li>Storyboards for the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Order_of_the_Phoenix_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix (film)">film adaptation</a> of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Order_of_the_Phoenix" title="Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix">Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix</a></i> drawn by Nick Pelham consistently refer to Nigel as "Philip". Whether a different character was originally intended for this scene, Nigel was going to be renamed, or this was simply an error on the artist's part is unclear.<sup id="cite_ref-15" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-15">&#91;15&#93;</a></sup></li></ul>
