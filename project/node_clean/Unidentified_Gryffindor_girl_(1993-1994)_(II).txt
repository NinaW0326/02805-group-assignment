This <b><a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch</a></b> (b. <a href="/wiki/1979" title="1979">1979</a>-<a href="/wiki/1980" title="1980">1980</a>) attended <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> as a <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor" title="Gryffindor">Gryffindor</a> from <a href="/wiki/1991" title="1991">1991</a> onwards, and was a classmate of <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-POAF_2-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-POAF-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>In her <a href="/wiki/Third_year" title="Third year">third year</a>, she took <a href="/wiki/Divination_(class)" title="Divination (class)">Divination</a> and <a href="/wiki/Care_of_Magical_Creatures" title="Care of Magical Creatures">Care of Magical Creatures</a> as her electives.<sup id="cite_ref-POAF_2-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-POAF-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> Providing she was not a <a href="/wiki/Muggle-born" title="Muggle-born">Muggle-born</a>, she presumably graduated in <a href="/wiki/1998" title="1998">1998</a>.
</p>

<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_life">Early life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_Gryffindor_girl_(1993-1994)_(II)?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Early life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_Gryffindor_girl_(1993-1994)_(II)?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Early life"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>The red-haired witch was born in <a href="/wiki/1979" title="1979">1979</a> or <a href="/wiki/1980" title="1980">1980</a>, to presumably a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizarding</a> family.
</p><p>She received her <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_acceptance_letter" title="Hogwarts acceptance letter">Hogwarts acceptance letter</a> in <a href="/wiki/1991" title="1991">1991</a>, and began her first year at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> on <a href="/wiki/1_September" title="1 September">1 September</a>, that same year.
</p>

<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Third_year">Third year</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_Gryffindor_girl_(1993-1994)_(II)?veaction=edit&amp;section=4" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Third year">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_Gryffindor_girl_(1993-1994)_(II)?action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Third year"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>

<p>After the summer holidays, the Gryffindor witch returned to Hogwarts, though this time <a href="/wiki/Dementor" title="Dementor">Dementors</a> were standing guard at every entrance. She said that she could not believe that the <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">Ministry of Magic</a> had stationed them around the school. She took <a href="/wiki/Divination_(class)" title="Divination (class)">Divination</a> and <a href="/wiki/Care_of_Magical_Creatures" title="Care of Magical Creatures">Care of Magical Creatures</a> as her elective subjects in her <a href="/wiki/Third_year" title="Third year">third year</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-POAF_2-10" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-POAF-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>In the lesson, they were taught about <a href="/wiki/Boggart" title="Boggart">Boggarts</a> by <a href="/wiki/Remus_Lupin" title="Remus Lupin">Remus Lupin</a>, the new teacher as of <a href="/wiki/Gilderoy_Lockhart" title="Gilderoy Lockhart">Professor Lockhart</a>'s departure, and had a practical lesson on how to <a href="/wiki/Boggart-Banishing_Spell" title="Boggart-Banishing Spell">banish</a> them. The girl faced the Boggart, and it frightened her by turning into either a <a href="/wiki/Rat" title="Rat">rat</a>, <a href="/wiki/Rattlesnake" title="Rattlesnake">rattlesnake</a> or eyeball.<sup id="cite_ref-POA7_3-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-POA7-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<p>During the first lesson, they were told that their copies of <i><a href="/wiki/The_Monster_Book_of_Monsters" title="The Monster Book of Monsters">The Monster Book of Monsters</a></i>, which had bitten them, had to be stroked in order to open. When Professor Hagrid introduced the <a href="/wiki/Hippogriff" title="Hippogriff">Hippogriff</a> <a href="/wiki/Buckbeak" title="Buckbeak">Buckbeak</a>, she, along with the rest of the class, did not seem to be able to believe her eyes. After Hagrid asked for a volunteer, the Gryffindor girl was afraid and stepped back, leaving <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> to interact with Buckbeak instead. She watched apprehensively alongside Hermione and <a href="/wiki/Neville_Longbottom" title="Neville Longbottom">Neville Longbottom</a>, and applauded happily after Harry flew the Hippogriff. Unfortunately for him, Malfoy insulted Buckbeak, who proceeded to attack him. The Gryffindor girl ran and hid behind a rock with her best-friend and Parvati. The class was dismissed while Hagrid took Malfoy to <a href="/wiki/Poppy_Pomfrey" title="Poppy Pomfrey">Madam Pomfrey</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Hospital_Wing" title="Hospital Wing">Hospital Wing</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-POAF_2-11" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-POAF-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<p>The Gryffindor girl was later present during the lesson that Hermione, after being insulted by Professor Trelawney, stormed out of the class. At the time, she was crystal-gazing with her friend, and looked up in surprise after Hermione left. She returned to her work straight away, and was later the last person out of the classroom. On the <a href="/wiki/Divination_Stairwell" title="Divination Stairwell">Divination staircase</a>, she walked with a boy, rather than with her friend, who was ahead of her with another girl. Harry, who was returning to the classroom, accidentally walked into the girl, and said sorry.<sup id="cite_ref-POAF_2-12" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-POAF-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>The girl watched Harry Potter fly on his <a href="/wiki/Firebolt" title="Firebolt">Firebolt</a> and cheered happily.<sup id="cite_ref-POAF_2-13" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-POAF-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<p>This girl had red hair, which, during her Hogwarts years, she often wore as plaits. She had blue eyes and white skin, and wore her school uniform very neatly. Unlike her friend, during her magical creatures class in her third year, she did not take off her robe, but she did take off her jumper and tie it around her waist. She was considerably shorter than fellow Gryffindor <a href="/wiki/Neville_Longbottom" title="Neville Longbottom">Neville Longbottom</a>, and was slightly shorter than <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione Granger</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-POAF_2-14" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-POAF-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_Gryffindor_girl_(1993-1994)_(II)?veaction=edit&amp;section=6" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_Gryffindor_girl_(1993-1994)_(II)?action=edit&amp;section=6" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li><a href="/wiki/J._K._Rowling" title="J. K. Rowling">J. K. Rowling</a> has been asked about the two unidentified girls in three interviews. She said their names were in her notebook, and promised twice to post them on her <a href="/wiki/J._K._Rowling%27s_official_site" title="J. K. Rowling&#39;s official site">website</a>, but has not as of yet.<sup id="cite_ref-wbd_4-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-wbd-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-leaky_5-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-leaky-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-pottercast_6-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-pottercast-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup> Even if she does, it will be impossible to say which name belongs to which girl.</li>
<li>The girls' names may have already appeared partially on the class list shown on <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_Me" title="Harry Potter and Me">Harry Potter and Me</a></i>.</li>
<li>It is highly likely that the girl was either <a href="/wiki/Alice_Tolipan" title="Alice Tolipan">Alice Tolipan</a> or <a href="/wiki/Luca_Caruso_(wizard)" title="Luca Caruso (wizard)">Luca Caruso</a>, the two unknown individuals who joined <a href="/wiki/Dumbledore%27s_Army" title="Dumbledore&#39;s Army">Dumbledore's Army</a>. If not, then she and her friend were the only Gryffindors in their year not to join the group.</li>
<li>Other possible identities include <a href="/wiki/Sally-Anne_Perks" title="Sally-Anne Perks">Sally-Anne Perks</a>, <a href="/wiki/Lily_Moon" title="Lily Moon">Lily Moon</a>, <a href="/wiki/Sophie_Roper" title="Sophie Roper">Sophie Roper</a>, <a href="/wiki/Sally_Smith" title="Sally Smith">Sally Smith</a>, or <a href="/wiki/Runcorn" title="Runcorn">Runcorn</a>.</li>
<li>She is portrayed by <a href="/wiki/Danielle_Downey" title="Danielle Downey">Danielle Downey</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Prisoner_of_Azkaban_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban (film)">film adaptation</a> of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Prisoner_of_Azkaban" title="Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban">Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban</a></i>.</li></ul>
