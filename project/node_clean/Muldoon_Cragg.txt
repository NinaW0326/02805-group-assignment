<b>Muldoon Cragg</b><sup id="cite_ref-name_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-name-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> that was depicted in a large painting that hung in <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor_Tower" title="Gryffindor Tower">Gryffindor Tower</a>, at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Castle" title="Hogwarts Castle">Hogwarts Castle</a>. In his lifetime, he was <a href="/wiki/Head_of_Gryffindor_House" title="Head of Gryffindor House">Head of Gryffindor House</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-MHP_2-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-MHP-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>In the painting, Cragg is depicted as standing against a windowsill in the background, absorbed in reading while, in the foreground, <a href="/wiki/Tobias_Stump" title="Tobias Stump">Tobias Stump</a> watches over a game of <a href="/wiki/Wizard%27s_Chess" title="Wizard&#39;s Chess">Wizard's Chess</a> between <a href="/wiki/Podric_Piles" title="Podric Piles">Podric Piles</a> and <a href="/wiki/Uric_Oapley" title="Uric Oapley">Uric Oapley</a>. Cragg is depicted in black robes and matching pointy hat, with a voluminous powdered wig, and reading a book.
</p>
