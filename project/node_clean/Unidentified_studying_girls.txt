<p>These <b>three girls</b> were <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witches</a> and students at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a>. They were seen studying and poring over books while they&#160;stood by the statue of the <a href="/wiki/First_Headmaster_of_Hogwarts" title="First Headmaster of Hogwarts">first Headmaster of Hogwarts</a>. <a href="/wiki/Nicholas_de_Mimsy-Porpington" title="Nicholas de Mimsy-Porpington">Sir Nicholas de Mimsy-Porpington</a> then passed them, and they looked up briefly before returning to their reading. <a href="/wiki/Percy_Weasley" title="Percy Weasley">Percy Weasley</a> and <a href="/wiki/Penelope_Clearwater" title="Penelope Clearwater">Penelope Clearwater</a> then passed them as well.
</p>
