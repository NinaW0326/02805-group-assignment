<b>Terry Skeres</b><sup id="cite_ref-ccp_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-ccp-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> and <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor" title="Gryffindor">Gryffindor</a> student at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> during the <a href="/wiki/1990s" title="1990s">1990s</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-dhp2_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-dhp2-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-photo1_4-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-photo1-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-photo2_5-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-photo2-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> On <a href="/wiki/2_May" title="2 May">May 2</a> <a href="/wiki/1998" title="1998">1998</a>, he participated in the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a>, helping defend the school against <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-dhp2_3-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-dhp2-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-photo1_4-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-photo1-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-photo2_5-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-photo2-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> It is unknown if he survived the battle, but he possibly did, as his name was not mentioned among the <a href="/wiki/Fallen_Fifty" title="Fallen Fifty">Fallen Fifty</a>. 
</p>
