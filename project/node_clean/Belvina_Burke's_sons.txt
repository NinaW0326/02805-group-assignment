<a href="/wiki/Belvina_Black" title="Belvina Black">Belvina Black</a> married <a href="/wiki/Herbert_Burke" title="Herbert Burke">Herbert Burke</a>, and they had three children: <b>two sons</b> and <a href="/wiki/Belvina_Burke%27s_daughter" title="Belvina Burke&#39;s daughter">one daughter</a>. They were all <a href="/wiki/Pure-blood" title="Pure-blood">pure-blood</a> and related through Belvina to the <a href="/wiki/House_of_Black" title="House of Black">Black family</a>.
</p><p>These two <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizards</a> were grandsons of famous <a href="/wiki/Phineas_Nigellus_Black" title="Phineas Nigellus Black">Phineas Nigellus Black</a>, who was a <a href="/wiki/Headmaster" title="Headmaster">Headmaster</a> of <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a>. They likely attended the school.
</p><p>They had a <a href="/wiki/Belvina_Burke%27s_daughter" title="Belvina Burke&#39;s daughter">sister</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-BFT_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-BFT-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
