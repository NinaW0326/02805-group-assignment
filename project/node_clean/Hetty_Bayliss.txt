The <i><a href="/wiki/Daily_Prophet" title="Daily Prophet">Daily Prophet</a></i> reported how she saw the <a href="/wiki/Flying_Ford_Anglia" title="Flying Ford Anglia">Flying Ford Anglia</a> in the sky on <a href="/wiki/1_September" title="1 September">1 September</a>, <a href="/wiki/1992" title="1992">1992</a> while hanging her washing.<sup id="cite_ref-COS5_1-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COS5-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
