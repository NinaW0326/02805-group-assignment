<b>Arcturus Black</b> (<a href="/wiki/1835" class="mw-redirect" title="1835">1835</a> – <a href="/wiki/1893" class="mw-redirect" title="1893">1893</a>) was a <a href="/wiki/Pure-blood" title="Pure-blood">pure-blood</a> <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a>. He was an early member of the <a href="/wiki/House_of_Black" title="House of Black">House of Black</a>, and it is possible that he was the brother of <a href="/wiki/Cygnus_Black_I" title="Cygnus Black I">Cygnus</a> and <a href="/wiki/Misapinoa_Black" title="Misapinoa Black">Misapinoa</a>, and uncle of <a href="/wiki/Phineas_Nigellus_Black" title="Phineas Nigellus Black">Phineas Nigellus</a> (and his siblings <a href="/wiki/Sirius_Black_I" title="Sirius Black I">Sirius</a>, <a href="/wiki/Elladora_Black" title="Elladora Black">Elladora</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Iola_Black" title="Iola Black">Iola</a>).<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Arcturus_Black_I?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Arcturus_Black_I?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p><i>Arcturus</i> is a name of the fourth brightest <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Arcturus" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Arcturus">star</a> in the sky and the brightest star in the constellation Boötes, it is approximately 36 light-years from Earth. There were other <a href="/wiki/House_of_Black" title="House of Black">Blacks</a> with the name <a href="/wiki/Arcturus_Black_(disambiguation)" title="Arcturus Black (disambiguation)">Arcturus</a>, <a href="/wiki/Arcturus_Black_II" title="Arcturus Black II">Arcturus Black II</a> and <a href="/wiki/Arcturus_Black_III" title="Arcturus Black III">Arcturus Black III</a>.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Arcturus_Black_I?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Arcturus_Black_I?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>Based on a statement by <a href="/wiki/Horace_Slughorn" title="Horace Slughorn">Horace Slughorn</a>, Arcturus may have graduated at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts</a> and was sorted into <a href="/wiki/Slytherin" title="Slytherin">Slytherin</a> house. However, he attended Hogwarts c. 1846 - 1853, when Slughorn wasn't even born.</li>
<li>This character appears only on the version of the Black family tree shown in the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Order_of_the_Phoenix_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix (film)">film adaptation</a> of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Order_of_the_Phoenix" title="Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix">Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix</a></i>. He was not included on the version of the tree which <a href="/wiki/J._K._Rowling" title="J. K. Rowling">J. K. Rowling</a> gave to a charity auction in <a href="/wiki/2006" title="2006">2006</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> However, according to director <a href="/wiki/David_Yates" title="David Yates">David Yates</a>, Rowling provided the filmmakers with a copy of the Black family tree going back eight generations.<sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> The filmmakers presumably received a more complete version of the tree than the one auctioned off, which only goes back six generations.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Cygnus_Black_I" title="Cygnus Black I">Cygnus Black I</a> was possibly his older brother. It's possible that <a href="/wiki/Arcturus_Black_II" title="Arcturus Black II">Arcturus' great nephew</a> is named after him.</li></ul>
