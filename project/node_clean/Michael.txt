<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Michael?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Michael?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<figure class="thumb tleft show-info-icon" style="width: 202px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/6/66/Fred_George_Michael_OOTP_1.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20091031212618" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/6/66/Fred_George_Michael_OOTP_1.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/200?cb=20091031212618" decoding="async" width="200" height="135" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="Fred George Michael OOTP 1.jpg" data-image-key="Fred_George_Michael_OOTP_1.jpg" /></a> 	 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:Fred_George_Michael_OOTP_1.jpg" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption">Fred and George comfort Michael in 1996</p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure>
<p>Michael started his education at Hogwarts in the mid-<a href="/wiki/1990s" title="1990s">1990s</a>. Upon arriving at Hogwarts, Michael was <a href="/wiki/Sorting_ceremony" title="Sorting ceremony">sorted</a> into <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor" title="Gryffindor">Gryffindor</a> <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Houses" title="Hogwarts Houses">house</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTPF_3-11" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTPF-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>During the <a href="/wiki/1995%E2%80%931996_school_year" title="1995â1996 school year">1995â1996 school year</a>, <a href="/wiki/Fred_and_George_Weasley" title="Fred and George Weasley">Fred and George Weasley</a> comforted him after <a href="/wiki/Dolores_Umbridge" title="Dolores Umbridge">Dolores Umbridge</a> made him use a <a href="/wiki/Black_Quill" title="Black Quill">blood quill</a> to write lines after he was caught pulling a rebellious prank on Umbridge, telling him that his wounds were already healing, and that the pain would eventually go away. Shortly after this, he was joined by <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> in the courtyard when Umbridge turned up, because it was then that the Weasley twins decided to put their <a href="/wiki/Fred_and_George_Weasley%27s_departure_from_Hogwarts" title="Fred and George Weasley&#39;s departure from Hogwarts">plan of escape</a> into action, a plan that Michael witnessed and supported.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTPF_3-12" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTPF-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Michael?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Michael?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The name Michael comes from the Hebrew Mikha-el, meaning "Who is like God?"<sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Michael?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Michael?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>Michael has only appeared in the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Order_of_the_Phoenix_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix (film)">film adaptation</a> of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Order_of_the_Phoenix" title="Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix">Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix</a></i>. There was no mention about him in the book.</li>
<li>Michael may have been a member of Dumbledore's Army to have served detention using the Blood Quill in the film adaptation, as all members listed on Dumbledore's Army's parchment were punished as a group. There were, however, multiple other reasons to serve detention, seeing as in the film adaptation Umbridge and the Ministry passed at least one-hundred and thirty-six <a href="/wiki/Educational_Decree" title="Educational Decree">Educational Decrees</a>.</li></ul>
