<b>Krafft</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> who lived during the early <a href="/wiki/20th_century" title="20th century">20th century</a>. He was a member of <a href="/wiki/Gellert_Grindelwald" title="Gellert Grindelwald">Gellert Grindelwald</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Alliance" title="Alliance">Alliance</a>, and was among the <a href="/wiki/Dark_wizard" class="mw-redirect" title="Dark wizard">dark wizard</a>'s foremost acolytes.<sup id="cite_ref-COGS_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COGS-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Krafft?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Krafft?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Krafft joined <a href="/wiki/Gellert_Grindelwald" title="Gellert Grindelwald">Gellert Grindelwald</a> at some point prior to <a href="/wiki/1927" title="1927">1927</a> and became one of his <a href="/wiki/Gellert_Grindelwald%27s_army" class="mw-redirect" title="Gellert Grindelwald&#39;s army">acolytes</a>. He travelled to <a href="/wiki/Paris" title="Paris">Paris</a> to help Grindelwald's plans in recruiting <a href="/wiki/Credence_Barebone" title="Credence Barebone">Credence Barebone</a> and attended his rally at the <a href="/wiki/Cimeti%C3%A8re_du_P%C3%A8re-Lachaise" title="Cimetière du Père-Lachaise">Cimetière du Père-Lachaise</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Lestrange_Mausoleum" title="Lestrange Mausoleum">Lestrange Mausoleum</a>. Krafft <a href="/wiki/Apparition" title="Apparition">disapparated</a> away after crossing the <a href="/wiki/Protego_Diabolica" title="Protego Diabolica">enchanted fire</a> that tested one's loyalty following the entrance of <a href="/wiki/Auror" title="Auror">Aurors</a> and did not participate in the subsequent <a href="/wiki/Gellert_Grindelwald%27s_Paris_rally" title="Gellert Grindelwald&#39;s Paris rally">battle</a> after that.<sup id="cite_ref-COGS_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COGS-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Physical_appearance">Physical appearance</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Krafft?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Physical appearance">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Krafft?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Physical appearance"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Krafft wore a militaristic jacket.<sup id="cite_ref-Archive_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Archive-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Magical_abilities_and_skills">Magical abilities and skills</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Krafft?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Krafft?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li><b><a href="/wiki/Apparition" title="Apparition">Apparition</a></b>: Kraft was capable of apparition like most other accomplished adult wizards. He successfully disapparated away from the <a href="/wiki/Lestrange_Mausoleum" title="Lestrange Mausoleum">Lestrange Mausoleum</a> in <a href="/wiki/1927" title="1927">1927</a> to escape the battle there and spread the word of his master.<sup id="cite_ref-COGS_1-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COGS-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup></li></ul>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Krafft?veaction=edit&amp;section=4" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Krafft?action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>Krafft may be derived from the word "kraft", which in Swedish, Norwegian and Danish can be used to mean "power" or "force" depending on the context, or the German counterpart, in which it means just the former.</li></ul>
