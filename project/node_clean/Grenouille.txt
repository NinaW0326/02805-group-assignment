<dd>&#8212; Extract from <i>Hélas, Je me suis Transfiguré Les Pieds</i><sup class="noprint"><a href="/wiki/Quidditch_Through_the_Ages_(real)" title="Quidditch Through the Ages (real)">[src]</a></sup></dd></dl>

<p><b>Grenouille</b> is a character in the <a href="/wiki/15th_century" title="15th century">15th century</a> play <i><a href="/wiki/H%C3%A9las,_Je_me_suis_Transfigur%C3%A9_Les_Pieds" title="Hélas, Je me suis Transfiguré Les Pieds">Hélas, Je me suis Transfiguré Les Pieds</a></i> ("Alas, I have Transfigured My Feet") by the French writer, <a href="/wiki/Malecrit" title="Malecrit">Malecrit</a>.
</p><p>In the play, Grenouille has to refuse help to <a href="/wiki/Crapaud" title="Crapaud">Crapaud</a> since he is to play as a <a href="/wiki/Keeper" title="Keeper">Keeper</a> in a <a href="/wiki/Quidditch" title="Quidditch">Quidditch</a> match.<sup id="cite_ref-QA8_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-QA8-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
