<b>Licorus Black</b><sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> (<a href="/wiki/1808" class="mw-redirect" title="1808">1808</a> â€” <a href="/wiki/1872" class="mw-redirect" title="1872">1872</a><sup id="cite_ref-hpts_1-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-hpts-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>) was a <a href="/wiki/Pure-blood" title="Pure-blood">pure-blood</a> <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a>, as well as the earliest known member of the <a href="/wiki/Pure-blood" title="Pure-blood">pure-blood</a> <a href="/wiki/House_of_Black" title="House of Black">House of Black</a>. He married a pure-blood <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch</a>, <a href="/wiki/Magenta_Tripe" title="Magenta Tripe">Magenta Tripe</a>, and is the earliest known ancestor of the Black family, being depicted on the <a href="/wiki/Black_Family_Tapestry" title="Black Family Tapestry">Black family tree tapestry</a> that hangs in the ancestral home of <a href="/wiki/12_Grimmauld_Place" title="12 Grimmauld Place">12 Grimmauld Place</a><sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>. It is possible that he was the brother of <a href="/wiki/Hesper_Black" title="Hesper Black">Hesper</a>, <a href="/wiki/Alexia_Walkin_Black" title="Alexia Walkin Black">Alexia Walkin</a>, <a href="/wiki/Phoebe_Black" title="Phoebe Black">Phoebe</a> and <a href="/wiki/Eduardus_Limette_Black" title="Eduardus Limette Black">Eduardus Limette Black</a>.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Licorus_Black?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Licorus_Black?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Licorus was born to the <a href="/wiki/House_of_Black" title="House of Black">House of Black</a> in <a href="/wiki/1808" class="mw-redirect" title="1808">1808</a>, to unknown pure-blood parents. He possibly seems to have had four siblings: <a href="/wiki/Alexia_Walkin_Black" title="Alexia Walkin Black">Alexia Walkin</a>, <a href="/wiki/Phoebe_Black" title="Phoebe Black">Phoebe</a>, <a href="/wiki/Hesper_Black" title="Hesper Black">Hesper</a> and <a href="/wiki/Eduardus_Limette_Black" title="Eduardus Limette Black">Eduardus Limette Black</a>.
</p><p>Mr Black was raised in a household that strongly believed in the importance of blood purity, and as such likely carried a grudge against <a href="/wiki/Muggle-born" title="Muggle-born">Muggle-borns</a>, whom most of the family called <a href="/wiki/Mudblood" title="Mudblood">Mudbloods</a>. His brother Eduardus was disowned for unknown reasons, which likely had an effect on him.
</p><p>At the age of eleven, Black presumably began his schooling at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a>, where he may have been <a href="/wiki/Sorting_ceremony" title="Sorting ceremony">Sorted</a> into <a href="/wiki/Slytherin" title="Slytherin">Slytherin House</a>.
</p><p>Sometime after completing his schooling, Black married a pure-blood witch, with whom he seems to have had several children: a daughter, <a href="/wiki/Misapinoa_Black" title="Misapinoa Black">Misapinoa</a>, who married into the <a href="/wiki/Blishwick_family" title="Blishwick family">Blishwick family</a> by being wed to <a href="/wiki/Jimbo_Blishwick" title="Jimbo Blishwick">Jimbo Blishwick</a>, and two sons, <a href="/wiki/Arcturus_Black_I" title="Arcturus Black I">Arcturus</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Cygnus_Black_I" title="Cygnus Black I">Cygnus Black</a>. It is likely, after Cygnus's death, that Licorus and Magenta helped Ella Max raise Cygnus's children: Sirius, Phineas Nigellus, Iola and Elladora.
</p><p>Licorus&#160;died in <a href="/wiki/1872" class="mw-redirect" title="1872">1872</a>.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Licorus_Black?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Licorus_Black?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p><i>Licorus</i> is a form of the name <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Lycorus" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Lycorus">Lycorus</a>.
</p><p>In Greek mythology, Lycorus was a son of <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Apollo" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Apollo">the god Apollo</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> After him a city was named Lycoreia.<sup id="cite_ref-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Licorus_Black?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Licorus_Black?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>Based on a statement by <a href="/wiki/Horace_Slughorn" title="Horace Slughorn">Horace Slughorn</a>, Licorus may have been a member of Slytherin house. However, Black attended Hogwarts in the <a href="/wiki/1810s" title="1810s">1810s</a>, long before Slughorn was even born.<sup id="cite_ref-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li>This character appears only on the version of the Black family tree shown in the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Order_of_the_Phoenix_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix (film)">film adaptation</a> of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Order_of_the_Phoenix" title="Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix">Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix</a></i>. He was not included on the version of the tree which <a href="/wiki/J._K._Rowling" title="J. K. Rowling">J. K. Rowling</a> gave to a charity auction in 2006<sup id="cite_ref-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup>. However, according to director <a href="/wiki/David_Yates" title="David Yates">David Yates</a>, Rowling provided the filmmakers with a copy of the Black family tree going back eight generations<sup id="cite_ref-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup>. The filmmakers presumably received a more complete version of the tree than the one auctioned off, which only goes back six generations.</li>
<li>Except for the colour of his hat, this character's picture on the family tree bears a striking resemblance to that of <a href="/wiki/Pollux_Black" title="Pollux Black">Pollux Black</a>. This was likely done by the film-makers to save time, as other characters, such as <a href="/wiki/Elladora_Black" title="Elladora Black">Elladora</a> and <a href="/wiki/Callidora_Black" title="Callidora Black">Callidora Black</a> also have images that are remarkably similar<sup id="cite_ref-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup>.</li>
<li>His name is similar to the name of his relative, <a href="/wiki/Lycoris_Black" title="Lycoris Black">Lycoris Black</a>.</li>
<li>He is the first known member of the&#160;<a href="/wiki/House_of_Black" title="House of Black">Black family</a>.</li></ul>
