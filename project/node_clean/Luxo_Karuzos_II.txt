<b>Luxo Karuzos II</b> was the editor-in-chief of the <i><a href="/wiki/The_Wizard%27s_Voice" title="The Wizard&#39;s Voice">The Wizard's Voice</a></i> in <a href="/wiki/1926" title="1926">1926</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-newspaper_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-newspaper-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> He wrote an editorial entitled "Rare Spells Exercised to Control the Truth" in the <a href="/wiki/6_December" title="6 December">6 December</a> 1926 issue of the newspaper.<sup id="cite_ref-newspaper_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-newspaper-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
