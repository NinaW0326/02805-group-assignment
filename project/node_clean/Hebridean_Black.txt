<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Description_and_traits">Description and traits</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Hebridean_Black?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Description and traits">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Hebridean_Black?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Description and traits"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>It has dark rough scales, ridges along its back, and a tail tipped with an arrow-shaped spike. The Hebridean Black has brilliant purple eyes, bat-like wings and can grow to be up to thirty feet long. Its favourite food is <a href="/wiki/Deer" title="Deer">deer</a>, although it has been known to carry off cows.<sup id="cite_ref-FBR_2-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-FBR-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>The Hebridean Black is more aggressive than the other dragon native to the British Isles, the <a href="/wiki/Common_Welsh_Green" title="Common Welsh Green">Common Welsh Green</a>, and thus requires a territory of as much as one hundred square miles each. The <a href="/wiki/MacFusty_clan" title="MacFusty clan">MacFusty clan</a> take responsibility for the dragons, and have so done for centuries.<sup id="cite_ref-FBR_2-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-FBR-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>They are also the team mascot for the <a href="/wiki/Banchory_Bangers" title="Banchory Bangers">Banchory Bangers</a> <a href="/wiki/Quidditch" title="Quidditch">Quidditch</a> team from <a href="/wiki/Scotland" title="Scotland">Scotland</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-QTA5_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-QTA5-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
