<b><a href="/wiki/Crispin_Cronk" title="Crispin Cronk">Crispin Cronk</a>'s sphinxes</b> were several pet <a href="/wiki/Sphinx" title="Sphinx">Sphinxes</a> that <a href="/wiki/Crispin_Cronk" title="Crispin Cronk">Crispin Cronk</a> insisted on keeping in his backyard.<sup id="cite_ref-PSG_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PSG-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-COSG_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COSG-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-POAG_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-POAG-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="History">History</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Crispin_Cronk%27s_sphinxes?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: History">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Crispin_Cronk%27s_sphinxes?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: History"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>During the <a href="/wiki/19th_century" title="19th century">19th century</a>, the domestication and private ownership of <a href="/wiki/Sphinx" title="Sphinx">Sphinxes</a> was illegal and, after Cronk refused to hand his sphinxes over to the <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">British Ministry of Magic</a>, the Ministry was forced to convict Cronk and send him to <a href="/wiki/Azkaban" title="Azkaban">Azkaban</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-PSG_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PSG-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-COSG_2-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COSG-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-POAG_3-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-POAG-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>It is unknown what happened to Cronk's sphinxes after this point, however, it is presumed that they were taken into the care of the <a href="/wiki/Beast_Division" title="Beast Division">Beast Division</a> of the <a href="/wiki/Department_for_the_Regulation_and_Control_of_Magical_Creatures" title="Department for the Regulation and Control of Magical Creatures">Department for the Regulation and Control of Magical Creatures</a> and returned to their native land of <a href="/wiki/Egypt" title="Egypt">Egypt</a>.
</p>
