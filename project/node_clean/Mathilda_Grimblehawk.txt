<p><b>Mathilda Grimblehawk</b> (fl. <a href="/wiki/1999" title="1999">1999</a>) was a <a href="/wiki/Great_Britain" title="Great Britain">British</a> <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch</a> employed at the <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">British Ministry of Magic</a> as an investigator of <a href="/wiki/Creature" title="Creature">creature</a>-related incidents. During her years at the Ministry, she dealt competently with <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizards</a>, <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggles</a> and even <a href="/wiki/Centaur" title="Centaur">Centaurs</a> alike.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Mathilda_Grimblehawk?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Mathilda_Grimblehawk?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_life">Early life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Mathilda_Grimblehawk?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Early life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Mathilda_Grimblehawk?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Early life"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Mathilda Grimblehawk was born somewhere in the <a href="/wiki/Great_Britain" title="Great Britain">British Isles</a>, into the wizarding <a href="/wiki/Grimblehawk_family" title="Grimblehawk family">Grimblehawk family</a>. During her formative years, she attended <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a>, where she was known to have taken <a href="/wiki/Potions_(class)" title="Potions (class)">Potions</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> She presumably also took and excelled in <a href="/wiki/Care_of_Magical_Creatures" title="Care of Magical Creatures">Care of Magical Creatures</a>, given her future career as a <a href="/wiki/Magizoologist" title="Magizoologist">Magizoologist</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-WU_4-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-WU-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Career">Career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Mathilda_Grimblehawk?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Mathilda_Grimblehawk?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Career"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>After she left school, she eventually joined the Ministry of Magic, where she became an investigator for the <a href="/wiki/Beast_Division" title="Beast Division">Beast Division</a>.
</p><p>Along with <a href="/wiki/Mathilda_Grimblehawk%27s_partner" title="Mathilda Grimblehawk&#39;s partner">her partner</a> (the new intern) she helped investigate several cases involving <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magical</a> <a href="/wiki/Beast" title="Beast">beasts</a>. Their first case together involved an attack on <a href="/wiki/Gordon_Horton" title="Gordon Horton">Gordon Horton</a>, <a href="/wiki/Quidditch" title="Quidditch">Quidditch</a> <a href="/wiki/Keeper" title="Keeper">keeper</a> for the <a href="/wiki/Chudley_Cannons" title="Chudley Cannons">Chudley Cannons</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> She believed the Cannons to be the best <a href="/wiki/Quidditch_team" title="Quidditch team">Quidditch team</a> in the league, despite their performance last season.<sup id="cite_ref-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Other cases on which she worked included the disruption at the <a href="/wiki/Leaky_Cauldron" title="Leaky Cauldron">Leaky Cauldron</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup> the attack on <a href="/wiki/Don_Harrison" title="Don Harrison">Don Harrison</a>'s construction crew,<sup id="cite_ref-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup> the story from the <a href="/wiki/Siren%27s_Rest%27s_landlord" class="mw-redirect" title="Siren&#39;s Rest&#39;s landlord">Siren's Rest's landlord</a> of a <a href="/wiki/Wales" title="Wales">Welsh</a> lake monster,<sup id="cite_ref-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup> the disappearance of <a href="/wiki/Bilius_Finbok" title="Bilius Finbok">Bilius Finbok</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-10" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-10">&#91;10&#93;</a></sup> the investigation in the sewers of <a href="/wiki/London" title="London">London</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-11" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-11">&#91;11&#93;</a></sup> and the fire at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-12" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-12">&#91;12&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>According to <a href="/wiki/Gethsemane_Prickle" title="Gethsemane Prickle">Gethsemane Prickle</a>, she once, during the course of her duties, decided she would taste a <a href="/wiki/Draught_of_Living_Death" title="Draught of Living Death">Draught of Living Death</a> before administering it to a magical creature. Mathilda begrudgingly admitted to it, saying that it had "<i>only happened</i> once".<sup id="cite_ref-FBC2.4_13-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-FBC2.4-13">&#91;13&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>By <a href="/wiki/2018" title="2018">2018</a>, Mathilda was working with the newly-created <a href="/wiki/Statute_of_Secrecy_Task_Force" title="Statute of Secrecy Task Force">Statute of Secrecy Task Force</a> to aid them in dealing with the results of the <a href="/wiki/Calamity" title="Calamity">Calamity</a>. As a <a href="/wiki/Magizoologist" title="Magizoologist">Magizoologist</a>, she ministered the <a href="/wiki/Ministry_Magizoology_Orientation" title="Ministry Magizoology Orientation">several</a> <a href="/wiki/Safety_Orientation" title="Safety Orientation">courses</a> for its members interested in pursuing a career as Magizoologists, including an intensive, week-long one centred on how to ride on a <a href="/wiki/Kelpie" title="Kelpie">Kelpie</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-WU_4-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-WU-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> As a bat-expert, she also helped to improve members' insight into the <a href="/wiki/Bat-Bogey_Hex" title="Bat-Bogey Hex">Bat-Bogey Hex</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-14" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-14">&#91;14&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Personality_and_traits">Personality and traits</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Mathilda_Grimblehawk?veaction=edit&amp;section=4" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Personality and traits">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Mathilda_Grimblehawk?action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Personality and traits"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Mathilda Grimblehawk possessed a great enthusiasm for the subject of&#160;<a href="/wiki/Magizoology" title="Magizoology">Magizoology</a>&#160;and great fondness for <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magical</a> <a href="/wiki/Creature" title="Creature">creatures</a> of all kinds. She was also very friendly and sociable, as she delighted in the chance to travel and meet new people as part of her work, and apparently greatly enjoyed <a href="/wiki/Mathilda_Grimblehawk%27s_partner" title="Mathilda Grimblehawk&#39;s partner">her partner's</a> company.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Magical_abilities_and_skills">Magical abilities and skills</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Mathilda_Grimblehawk?veaction=edit&amp;section=5" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Mathilda_Grimblehawk?action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li><b><a href="/wiki/Magizoology" title="Magizoology">Magizoology</a></b>: As a member of the&#160;<a href="/wiki/Department_for_the_Regulation_and_Control_of_Magical_Creatures" title="Department for the Regulation and Control of Magical Creatures">Department for the Regulation and Control of Magical Creatures</a>, Mathidla Grimblehawk was very familiar with a wide variety of <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magical</a> <a href="/wiki/Creature" title="Creature">creatures</a>, and knew how to&#160;<a href="/wiki/Ministry_of_Magic_Classification" title="Ministry of Magic Classification">classify</a>&#160;them and the various laws regulating said creatures, as well as how to adeptly handle and understand them.</li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Martial_magic" title="Martial magic">Martial magic</a></b>: As a field member of the <a href="/wiki/Department_for_the_Regulation_and_Control_of_Magical_Creatures" title="Department for the Regulation and Control of Magical Creatures">Department for the Regulation and Control of Magical Creatures</a>, Mathilda would have become a fairly accomplished <a href="/wiki/Duelling" title="Duelling">duellist</a>, at least by the <a href="/wiki/2010s" title="2010s">2010s</a>, as she would have received formal training in combative magic under the tutelage of <a href="/wiki/Mordecai_Berrycloth" title="Mordecai Berrycloth">Mordecai Berrycloth</a> from the <a href="/wiki/Auror_Office" title="Auror Office">Auror Office</a> as part of her preparations before joining the Statute of Secrecy Task Force.<sup id="cite_ref-WU_4-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-WU-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Potion" title="Potion">Potions</a></b>: As a Hogwarts student, Mathilda was known to have taken <a href="/wiki/Potions_(class)" title="Potions (class)">Potions</a>, a subject which she succeeded in, showing her to have been skilled in the art of potion-making.</li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Charm" title="Charm">Charms</a></b>: Mathilda was capable with charm-work, given how she often performed various charms successfully during her career investigations.</li></ul>
