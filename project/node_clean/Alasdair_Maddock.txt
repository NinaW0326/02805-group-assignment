<b>Alasdair Maddock</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> and former <a href="/wiki/Chaser" title="Chaser">Chaser</a> for the <a href="/wiki/Montrose_Magpies" title="Montrose Magpies">Montrose Magpies</a><sup id="cite_ref-:0_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:0-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> who had what some people believed to have been a rather unsettling obsession with <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggle</a> sports. He once tried to play football "for a laugh" and tried to incorporate it into <a href="/wiki/Quidditch" title="Quidditch">Quidditch</a>. Once, he tried to dribble the <a href="/wiki/Quaffle" title="Quaffle">Quaffle</a> like a <a href="/wiki/Basketball" title="Basketball">basketball</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-:0_2-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:0-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> and on another occasion, tried to hit the <a href="/wiki/Quaffle" title="Quaffle">Quaffle</a> with his head like a <a href="/wiki/Football" title="Football">football</a> player. He was later fired by the manager, <a href="/wiki/Cormack_McLeod" title="Cormack McLeod">Cormack McLeod</a>, for playing <a href="/wiki/Golf" title="Golf">golf</a> in the summer of <a href="/wiki/1999" title="1999">1999</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
