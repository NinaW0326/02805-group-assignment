The <b>Ice Knight</b> was an <a href="/wiki/Piertotum_Locomotor" title="Piertotum Locomotor">animated</a> <a href="/wiki/Suit_of_armour" title="Suit of armour">suit of armour</a> that guarded the <a href="/wiki/Cursed_Vaults" title="Cursed Vaults">Cursed Vaults</a> on the fifth floor of <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Castle" title="Hogwarts Castle">Hogwarts Castle</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-HM_1-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HM-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="History">History</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Ice_Knight?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: History">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Ice_Knight?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: History"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>In the <a href="/wiki/1984%E2%80%931985_school_year" title="1984–1985 school year">1984–1985 school year</a>, <a href="/wiki/Jacob%27s_sibling" title="Jacob&#39;s sibling">Jacob's sibling</a> had a vision of the Ice Knights. <a href="/wiki/Rowan_Khanna" title="Rowan Khanna">Rowan Khanna</a> researched it in the summer holidays, but was not able to find any information.<sup id="cite_ref-HM_1-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HM-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>When Jacob's sibling, <a href="/wiki/William_Weasley" title="William Weasley">Bill Weasley</a> and another student eventually found and opened the vault, the Ice Knight immediately attacked them, freezing two of the students. Jacob's sibling was eventually able to defeat it using offensive <a href="/wiki/Spell" title="Spell">spells</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-HM_1-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HM-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Four years later, Jacob's sibling returned with Bill when they were completing <a href="/wiki/Cadogan" title="Cadogan">Sir Cadogan</a>'s agenda, initiating a second duel with the Ice Knight, who lost a second time.<sup id="cite_ref-HM_1-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HM-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
