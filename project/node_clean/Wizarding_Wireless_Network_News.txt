<div class="mw-parser-output"><p>The <b>Wizarding Wireless Network News</b> was a news programme broadcasted on the <a href="/wiki/Wizarding_Wireless_Network" title="Wizarding Wireless Network">Wizarding Wireless Network</a>. During <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a>'s control of the <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">Ministry of Magic</a>, this news broadcast stopped reporting murders by the <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a> and <a href="/wiki/Snatchers" title="Snatchers">Snatchers</a>, despite their occurrence.<sup id="cite_ref-DH22_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH22-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Wizarding_Wireless_Network_News?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Wizarding_Wireless_Network_News?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>In the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Order_of_the_Phoenix_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix (film)">film adaptation</a> of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Order_of_the_Phoenix" title="Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix">Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix</a></i>, <a href="/wiki/Cornelius_Fudge" title="Cornelius Fudge">Cornelius Fudge</a> is heard on the <a href="/wiki/Wizarding_Wireless_Network" title="Wizarding Wireless Network">Wizarding Wireless Network</a> giving an interview on the recent disappearances.<sup id="cite_ref-OPf14_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OPf14-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> This broadcast may be the Wizarding Wireless Network News.</li>
<li>In the Portuguese edition of the <i>Harry Potter</i> novels, "Wizarding Wireless Network News" was translated as "<i>Agência Noticiosa da Feitiçaria</i>", meaning "Wizarding News Agency". The sense that it was a news segment of the <a href="/wiki/Wizarding_Wireless_Network" title="Wizarding Wireless Network">WWN</a> was lost altogether, making it seem like a Ministry of Magic-controlled <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/News_agency" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:News agency">newswire</a> instead.</li>
<li>In the Norwegian edition of the Harry Potter novels, &#160;"Wizarding Wireless Network News" was translated as "Magisk rikskringkasting", or "MRK", which is a play on NRK, the Norsk rikskringkasting - generally expressed in English as the Norwegian Broadcasting Corporation, changing the letters N with the letter M and thus changing the word Norsk/Norwegian with the word Magisk/Magical.</li></ul>
