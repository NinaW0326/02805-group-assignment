<b>Sofie</b> was a female <a href="/wiki/Great_Britain" title="Great Britain">British</a> <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggle</a> politician. She worked for <a href="/wiki/David_Yates_(in-universe)" title="David Yates (in-universe)">David Yates</a> at the <a href="/wiki/London_City_Hall" title="London City Hall">City Hall</a> at <a href="/wiki/London" title="London">London</a> and her office had a view of the River Thames out-stretch.<sup id="cite_ref-HBPF_1-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBPF-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Sofie?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Sofie?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>In the summer of <a href="/wiki/1996" title="1996">1996</a>, while at work, she and her co-workers witnessed the <a href="/wiki/Dark_Mark" title="Dark Mark">Dark Mark</a> appearing in the sky towering over the City of <a href="/wiki/London" title="London">London</a> and the <a href="/wiki/Millennium_Bridge" title="Millennium Bridge">Millennium Bridge</a> collapsing. This attack was led by <a href="/wiki/Fenrir_Greyback" title="Fenrir Greyback">Fenrir Greyback</a> and a rally of <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a> who were celebrating capturing and kidnapping <a href="/wiki/Garrick_Ollivander" title="Garrick Ollivander">Garrick Ollivander</a> from his shop in <a href="/wiki/Diagon_Alley" title="Diagon Alley">Diagon Alley</a> for their leader, <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-HBPF_1-10" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBPF-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Sofie?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Sofie?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>Sofie (or Sophie) was played by British actress <a href="/wiki/Teresa_Mahoney" title="Teresa Mahoney">Teresa Mahoney</a>, she was the only Muggle to be identified and credited in the film and she has no spoken lines.</li>
<li>The characters spelling is uncertain, <i>IMDb</i> lists it as "<i>Sophie</i>" whereas Teresa Mahoney's agency profile lists it as "<i>Sofie</i>". Her agency page also lists her character's job as a politician.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup></li></ul>
