<b>Wandkeepers</b> are individuals employed by <a href="/wiki/Garrick_Ollivander" title="Garrick Ollivander">Garrick Ollivander</a><sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> to run the various branches of <a href="/wiki/Ollivanders" title="Ollivanders">Ollivanders</a> located throughout wizarding <a href="/wiki/Great_Britain" title="Great Britain">Britain</a>, especially <a href="/wiki/Hogsmeade" title="Hogsmeade">Hogsmeade</a>, and to a further extent, the main store in <a href="/wiki/Diagon_Alley" title="Diagon Alley">Diagon Alley</a>, while the <a href="/wiki/Wandmaker" title="Wandmaker">wandmaker</a> is away.<sup id="cite_ref-WWHP_3-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-WWHP-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Magical_abilities_and_skills">Magical abilities and skills</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Wandkeeper?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Wandkeeper?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>In order to be eligible for the job title of Wandkeeper, the person in question must have an expertise in the following:
</p>
<ul><li><b><a href="/wiki/Charm" title="Charm">Charms</a></b>: Must be able to cast many charms such as the <a href="/wiki/Mending_Charm" title="Mending Charm">Mending Charm</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-cody_5-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-cody-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/Meteolojinx_Recanto" title="Meteolojinx Recanto">weather-related charms</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-cody_5-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-cody-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> the <a href="/wiki/Freezing_Charm" title="Freezing Charm">Freezing Charm</a><sup id="cite_ref-immobulus_6-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-immobulus-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup> and the <a href="/wiki/Herbivicus_Charm" title="Herbivicus Charm">Herbivicus Charm</a>;<sup id="cite_ref-hayley_7-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-hayley-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup> as well as the <a href="/wiki/Levitation_Charm" title="Levitation Charm">Levitation Charm</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-cody_5-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-cody-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> the <a href="/wiki/Water-Making_Spell" title="Water-Making Spell">Water-Making Spell</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-hayley_7-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-hayley-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup> the <a href="/wiki/Wand-Lighting_Charm" title="Wand-Lighting Charm">Wand-Lighting Charm</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-cody_5-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-cody-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> the <i><a href="/wiki/Descendo" title="Descendo">Descendo</a></i> charm<sup id="cite_ref-hayley_7-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-hayley-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup> and the <a href="/wiki/Summoning_Charm" title="Summoning Charm">Summoning Charm</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-Katie_8-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Katie-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Divination" title="Divination">Divination</a></b>: Must be able to foresee costumers arriving in the shop.<sup id="cite_ref-cody_5-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-cody-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Nonverbal_spell" title="Nonverbal spell">Nonverbal magic</a></b>: Must be able to produce nonverbal spells smoothly, such as the <a href="/wiki/Herbivicus_Charm" title="Herbivicus Charm">Herbivicus Charm</a><sup id="cite_ref-hayley_7-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-hayley-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup> and the <a href="/wiki/Freezing_Charm" title="Freezing Charm">Freezing Charm</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-Katie_8-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Katie-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Wandlore" title="Wandlore">Wandlore</a></b>: Must be knowledgeable in the complex science and <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magic</a> of wandlore.<sup id="cite_ref-wandlore_9-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-wandlore-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup></li></ul>
