<b>Jeremiah Garnet</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Great_Britain" title="Great Britain">British</a> <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> and radio reporter for the <a href="/wiki/Wizarding_Wireless_Network" title="Wizarding Wireless Network">Wizarding Wireless Network</a>.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Jeremiah_Garnet?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Jeremiah_Garnet?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>He was investigating a <a href="/wiki/Fire" title="Fire">fire</a> at the <a href="/wiki/Boathouse" title="Boathouse">Boathouse</a> of <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> and believed it to be caused by a <a href="/wiki/Dragon" title="Dragon">dragon</a>. His zealousness to develop this story led him to fake a <a class="new" title="Jeremiah Garnet&#39;s photograph (page does not exist)" rel="nofollow" data-uncrawlable-url="L3dpa2kvSmVyZW1pYWhfR2FybmV0JTI3c19waG90b2dyYXBoP2FjdGlvbj1lZGl0JnJlZGxpbms9MQ==">photograph</a> of a dragon flying over Hogwarts. This forgery was discovered by <a href="/wiki/Mathilda_Grimblehawk" title="Mathilda Grimblehawk">Mathilda Grimblehawk</a> and <a href="/wiki/Mathilda_Grimblehawk%27s_partner" title="Mathilda Grimblehawk&#39;s partner">her partner</a> during their investigation of the fire.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Garnet managed to capture the beast responsible for the fire and sent it to the <a href="/wiki/Wizarding_Wireless_Network" title="Wizarding Wireless Network">Wizarding Wireless Network</a> for an exclusive report, but Mathilda and her partner managed to rescue the beast and relocate it.
In addition to reporting, Garnet was also working on <i><a class="new" title="Hogwarts Castle: A History of Cultivated Fungus (page does not exist)" rel="nofollow" data-uncrawlable-url="L3dpa2kvSG9nd2FydHNfQ2FzdGxlOl9BX0hpc3Rvcnlfb2ZfQ3VsdGl2YXRlZF9GdW5ndXM/YWN0aW9uPWVkaXQmcmVkbGluaz0x">Hogwarts Castle: A History of Cultivated Fungus</a></i>.
</p>
