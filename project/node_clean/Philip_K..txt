<b>Philip K.</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggle</a> man who was buried in the <a href="/wiki/Little_Hangleton" title="Little Hangleton">graveyard</a> in the village of <a href="/wiki/Little_Hangleton" title="Little Hangleton">Little Hangleton</a>. His gravemarker was damaged by a <a href="/wiki/Killing_Curse" title="Killing Curse">Killing Curse</a> cast by <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a>, during the <a href="/wiki/Duel_in_Little_Hangleton" title="Duel in Little Hangleton">Duel in Little Hangleton</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
