<b>Irma Black</b> (née<b> Crabbe</b>) (fl. <a href="/wiki/1925" title="1925">1925</a>-<a href="/wiki/1938" title="1938">1938</a>) was an <a href="/wiki/England" title="England">English</a> <a href="/wiki/Pure-blood" title="Pure-blood">pure-blood</a> <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch</a> who was the wife of <a href="/wiki/Pollux_Black" title="Pollux Black">Pollux Black</a>, the mother of <a href="/wiki/Walburga_Black" title="Walburga Black">Walburga Black</a>, <a href="/wiki/Alphard_Black" title="Alphard Black">Alphard Black</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Cygnus_Black_III" title="Cygnus Black III">Cygnus Black III</a>, (through <a href="/wiki/Walburga_Black" title="Walburga Black">her first child</a>) the maternal grandmother of <a href="/wiki/Sirius_Black" title="Sirius Black">Sirius Black</a> and <a href="/wiki/Regulus_Black" title="Regulus Black">Regulus Black</a>, and (through <a href="/wiki/Cygnus_Black_III" title="Cygnus Black III">her third child</a>) the paternal grandmother of <a href="/wiki/Bellatrix_Lestrange" title="Bellatrix Lestrange">Bellatrix Lestrange</a>, <a href="/wiki/Andromeda_Tonks" title="Andromeda Tonks">Andromeda Tonks</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Narcissa_Malfoy" title="Narcissa Malfoy">Narcissa Malfoy</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Irma may also be a relative of <a href="/wiki/Vincent_Crabbe" title="Vincent Crabbe">Vincent Crabbe</a> and <a href="/wiki/Crabbe" title="Crabbe">his father</a>.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Irma_Crabbe?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Irma_Crabbe?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>Irma was possibly a member of <a href="/wiki/Slytherin" title="Slytherin">Slytherin</a> house as a <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts</a> student, like the rest of <a href="/wiki/Crabbe_family" title="Crabbe family">her family</a>.</li>
<li>It is possible that Irma had a niece: <a href="/wiki/Araminta_Meliflua_Black" title="Araminta Meliflua Black">Araminta Meliflua</a>. This is presumed as <a href="/wiki/Walburga_Black" title="Walburga Black">Walburga Black</a> (Irma’s daughter) was first cousin of Araminta. <a href="/wiki/Pollux_Black" title="Pollux Black">Pollux Black</a> (Irma’s husband) had three siblings, only <a href="/wiki/Dorea_Black" title="Dorea Black">one</a> is known to have married and had <a href="/wiki/Charlus_Potter%27s_son" title="Charlus Potter&#39;s son">a son</a>.</li></ul>
