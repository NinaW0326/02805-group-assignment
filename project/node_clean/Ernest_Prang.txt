<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Ernest_Prang?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Ernest_Prang?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_life">Early life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Ernest_Prang?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Early life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Ernest_Prang?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Early life"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Ernest Prang's origin is unknown. He attended <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> in his youth and was a member of <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor" title="Gryffindor">Gryffindor</a> house.<sup id="cite_ref-House_7-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-House-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup> Eventually, he began driving the <a href="/wiki/Knight_Bus" title="Knight Bus">Knight Bus</a>. He worked with <a href="/wiki/Stanley_Shunpike" title="Stanley Shunpike">Stan Shunpike</a>, the conductor of the Knight Bus, who sometimes called Ernie by his nickname, Ern.<sup id="cite_ref-PA3_3-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PA3-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> 
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="1990s"><a href="/wiki/1990s" title="1990s">1990s</a></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Ernest_Prang?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: 1990s">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Ernest_Prang?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: 1990s"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<div class="quote">
<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban">"<i>Best go wake up <a href="/wiki/Madam" title="Madam">Madam</a> <a href="/wiki/Marsh" title="Marsh">Marsh</a>, Stan, We’ll be in <a href="/wiki/Abergavenny" title="Abergavenny">Abergavenny</a> in a minute</i>"</span></dd>
<dd>&#8212;Ernie whilst driving the Knight Bus<sup class="noprint"><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Prisoner_of_Azkaban" title="Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban">[src]</a></sup></dd>
<dd></dd></dl></div>
<p><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> first encountered Prang in <a href="/wiki/1993" title="1993">1993</a>, when he was picked up by the <a href="/wiki/Knight_Bus" title="Knight Bus">Knight Bus</a> after leaving his <a href="/wiki/Petunia_Dursley" title="Petunia Dursley">Aunt</a> and <a href="/wiki/Vernon_Dursley" title="Vernon Dursley">Uncle</a>'s <a href="/wiki/4_Privet_Drive" title="4 Privet Drive">house</a>. When Harry was taken to the <a href="/wiki/Leaky_Cauldron" title="Leaky Cauldron">Leaky Cauldron</a> Ernie followed him in and was forced to leave by <a href="/wiki/Cornelius_Fudge" title="Cornelius Fudge">Cornelius Fudge</a>, the then-Minister of Magic.<sup id="cite_ref-PA3_3-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PA3-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Ernie was also present at <a href="/wiki/Albus_Dumbledore" title="Albus Dumbledore">Albus Dumbledore</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Funeral_of_Albus_Dumbledore" title="Funeral of Albus Dumbledore">funeral</a> in <a href="/wiki/1997" title="1997">1997</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-HP30_8-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HP30-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Physical_appearance">Physical appearance</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Ernest_Prang?veaction=edit&amp;section=4" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Physical appearance">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Ernest_Prang?action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Physical appearance"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Prang was described as an elderly grey-haired wizard with very thick glasses, giving him an "<a href="/wiki/Owl" title="Owl">owlish</a>" appearance.<sup id="cite_ref-PA3_3-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PA3-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Personality_and_traits">Personality and traits</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Ernest_Prang?veaction=edit&amp;section=5" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Personality and traits">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Ernest_Prang?action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Personality and traits"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Prang was very clearly committed to his driving, and seldom talked much to passengers on the Knight Bus. (leaving that to Stan Shunpike, preferring to concentrate on his driving).<sup id="cite_ref-PA3_3-10" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PA3-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> He also was clearly a supporter of <a href="/wiki/Albus_Dumbledore" title="Albus Dumbledore">Albus Dumbledore</a>, following his decision to attend his funeral, implying he also supported the <a href="/wiki/Order_of_the_Phoenix" title="Order of the Phoenix">Order of the Phoenix</a> and its goals as well.<sup id="cite_ref-HP30_8-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HP30-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Prang's driving skills left much to be desired and were in dire need of improvement, as he frequently drove off the road and into objects, such as post boxes, lamp posts and trees. This may have been at least partly due to the bus' extreme speed. Fortunately, due to the bus' magical nature, the objects got out of the way.<sup id="cite_ref-PA3_3-11" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PA3-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Ernest_Prang?veaction=edit&amp;section=6" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Ernest_Prang?action=edit&amp;section=6" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Ernie Prang and <a href="/wiki/Stanley_Shunpike" title="Stanley Shunpike">Stan Shunpike</a>, the driver and conductor of the <a href="/wiki/Knight_Bus" title="Knight Bus">Knight Bus</a>, were named after <a href="/wiki/J._K._Rowling" title="J. K. Rowling">J. K. Rowling</a>'s grandfathers, Ernest Arthur Rowling and Stanley George Volant, respectively.<sup id="cite_ref-Pottermore_9-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Pottermore-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup> Ernie's last name is a play on words, as "prang" is English slang meaning "to wreck an automobile".
</p>
