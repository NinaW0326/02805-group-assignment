<b>Oliver</b><sup id="cite_ref-pottermore_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-pottermore-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> <b>Rivers</b> (c. <a href="/wiki/1979" title="1979">1979</a>-<a href="/wiki/1980" title="1980">1980</a>) was a wizard who began attending <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> in <a href="/wiki/1991" title="1991">1991</a>. He was in the same year as <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> and was Sorted into either <a href="/wiki/Hufflepuff" title="Hufflepuff">Hufflepuff</a>&#160;or&#160;<a href="/wiki/Ravenclaw" title="Ravenclaw">Ravenclaw</a> house.<sup id="cite_ref-house_2-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-house-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Oliver_Rivers?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Oliver_Rivers?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>Oliver Rivers can be deduced to have been listed as a <a href="/wiki/Ravenclaw" title="Ravenclaw">Ravenclaw</a> on the list of students in a page of <a href="/wiki/J._K._Rowling" title="J. K. Rowling">J. K. Rowling</a>'s notes shown on <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_Me" title="Harry Potter and Me">Harry Potter and Me</a></i>. However, as two listed Hufflepuffs (<a href="/wiki/Anthony_Goldstein" title="Anthony Goldstein">Anthony Goldstein</a> and <a href="/wiki/Michael_Corner" title="Michael Corner">Michael Corner</a>) had their houses changed to Ravenclaw in the novels as published, two of those listed as Ravenclaws must have been changed to Hufflepuff. Rivers may be one of those whose house was changed.</li>
<li>It is likely he was one of the students, whose house wasn't changed, so was a Ravenclaw and shared a <a href="/wiki/Ravenclaw_Boys%27_Dormitory" title="Ravenclaw Boys&#39; Dormitory">dormitory</a> with <a href="/wiki/Terry_Boot" title="Terry Boot">Terry Boot</a>, <a href="/wiki/Michael_Corner" title="Michael Corner">Michael Corner</a>&#160;and&#160;<a href="/wiki/Anthony_Goldstein" title="Anthony Goldstein">Anthony Goldstein</a>,&#160;because his original surname was Quirrel and <a href="/wiki/Quirinus_Quirrell" title="Quirinus Quirrell">Quirinus Quirrell</a> was a Ravenclaw.</li></ul>
