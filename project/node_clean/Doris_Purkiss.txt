<b>Doris Purkiss</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch</a> who lived at <a href="/wiki/18_Acanthia_Way" title="18 Acanthia Way">number 18</a> <a href="/wiki/Acanthia_Way" title="Acanthia Way">Acanthia Way</a>, <a href="/wiki/Little_Norton" title="Little Norton">Little Norton</a>. In <a href="/wiki/1995" title="1995">1995</a>, she gave an interview to <i><a href="/wiki/The_Quibbler" title="The Quibbler">The Quibbler</a></i> claiming that <a href="/wiki/Sirius_Black" title="Sirius Black">Sirius Black</a> was actually <a href="/wiki/Stubby_Boardman" title="Stubby Boardman">Stubby Boardman</a>. She also stated that he had been enjoying a romantic dinner with her on the night of the murders of <a href="/wiki/Peter_Pettigrew" title="Peter Pettigrew">Peter Pettigrew</a> and twelve <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggles</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP10_1-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP10-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
