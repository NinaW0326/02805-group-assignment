<b>Anne Boleyn</b> (c. <a href="/wiki/1501" class="mw-redirect" title="1501">1501</a> â€“ <a href="/wiki/19_May" title="19 May">19 May</a> <a href="/wiki/1536" class="mw-redirect" title="1536">1536</a>) was <a href="/wiki/Queen" title="Queen">Queen</a> consort of <a href="/wiki/England" title="England">England</a> between <a href="/wiki/1533" class="mw-redirect" title="1533">1533</a> and <a href="/wiki/1536" class="mw-redirect" title="1536">1536</a>, as the second wife of the notorious <a href="/wiki/Henry_VIII" title="Henry VIII">King Henry VIII</a>, and mother to <a href="/wiki/Elizabeth_I" title="Elizabeth I">Queen Elizabeth I</a> of England.<sup id="cite_ref-wikipedia_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-wikipedia-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Boleyn was accused by <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggles</a> of being a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-wombat_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-wombat-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> There may have been a popular <a href="/wiki/Wizarding_world" title="Wizarding world">wizarding</a> theory that she was instead a <a href="/wiki/Squib" title="Squib">Squib</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-wombat_2-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-wombat-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> However, Anne's true blood status is unknown.
</p><p>If Boleyn was indeed a Squib, it suggests her siblings could be magical. Notable direct descendants of Anne's sister Mary are Queen Elizabeth II and Diana, Princess of Wales. This could imply that they have magical blood along with descendants including Prince William, Duke of Cambridge and Prince Harry, Duke of Sussex.
</p><p>A portrait of a woman resembling Anne hung in the <a href="/wiki/Grand_Staircase" title="Grand Staircase">Grand Staircase</a>, near the second-floor landing, at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-PSF_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PSF-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
