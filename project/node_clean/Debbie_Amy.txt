<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Debbie_Amy?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Debbie_Amy?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>In the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Philosopher%27s_Stone_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Philosopher&#39;s Stone (film)">film adaptation</a> of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Philosopher%27s_Stone" title="Harry Potter and the Philosopher&#39;s Stone">Harry Potter and the Philosopher's Stone</a></i>, the <a href="/wiki/Shrieking_book" title="Shrieking book">book in which this individual is mentioned</a> appears to be a copy of <i><a target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener" class="external text" href="http://books.google.ca/books?id=tSs3AAAAMAAJ&amp;printsec=frontcover#v=onepage&amp;q=&amp;f=false">The Works of the Most Reverend Dr John Tillotson</a></i>. As an Easter Egg, it seems someone in the special effects department digitally replaced some of the book's original text with names: "<i><a href="/wiki/Laura" title="Laura">Laura</a> and <a href="/wiki/Chas" title="Chas">Chas</a></i>" (left page, bottom paragraph), "<i><a href="/wiki/Terry_Chloe" title="Terry Chloe">Terry Michelle Chloe</a> and <a href="/wiki/Aimee" title="Aimee">Aimee</a></i>" (middle right page), "<i>Debbie Amy and <a href="/wiki/Leon" title="Leon">Leon</a></i>" (right page, bottom paragraph).</li></ul>
