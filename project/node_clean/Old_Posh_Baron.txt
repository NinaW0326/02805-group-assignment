<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="History">History</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Old_Posh_Baron?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: History">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Old_Posh_Baron?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: History"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span id="1991–1992_school_year"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="1991.E2.80.931992_school_year"><a href="/wiki/1991%E2%80%931992_school_year" title="1991–1992 school year">1991–1992 school year</a></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Old_Posh_Baron?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: 1991–1992 school year">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Old_Posh_Baron?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: 1991–1992 school year"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>In the <a href="/wiki/1991%E2%80%931992_school_year" title="1991–1992 school year">1991–1992 school year</a>, it hung in a <a href="/wiki/Portrait_Room" title="Portrait Room">Portrait Room</a> next to the <a href="/wiki/Great_Hall" title="Great Hall">Great Hall</a>, guarding a secret chamber where a <a href="/wiki/Nimbus_2000" title="Nimbus 2000">Nimbus 2000</a> was kept. The <a href="/wiki/Password" title="Password">password</a> to this room, "<i>Fizzy Pop</i>", was traded to <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> for fifty earwax flavour <a href="/wiki/Bertie_Bott%27s_Every_Flavour_Beans" title="Bertie Bott&#39;s Every Flavour Beans">Bertie Bott's Every Flavour Beans</a> by <a href="/wiki/Fred_and_George_Weasley" title="Fred and George Weasley">Fred and George Weasley</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span id="1992–1993_school_year"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="1992.E2.80.931993_school_year"><a href="/wiki/1992%E2%80%931993_school_year" title="1992–1993 school year">1992–1993 school year</a></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Old_Posh_Baron?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: 1992–1993 school year">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Old_Posh_Baron?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: 1992–1993 school year"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>In the <a href="/wiki/1992%E2%80%931993_school_year" title="1992–1993 school year">1992–1993 school year</a>, the Portrait Room, where the Baron's portrait hung became an out-of-bounds area, so the portrait was moved to a secret room behind a bookcase, guarding a small chamber with a lever.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Old_Posh_Baron?veaction=edit&amp;section=4" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Old_Posh_Baron?action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>This individual died sometime prior to <a href="/wiki/1991" title="1991">1991</a>, as the portraits hung at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Castle" title="Hogwarts Castle">Hogwarts Castle</a> depict deceased individuals.<sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li>All of the special portraits in the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Philosopher%27s_Stone_(video_game)" title="Harry Potter and the Philosopher&#39;s Stone (video game)">PS1 version</a> of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Philosopher%27s_Stone" title="Harry Potter and the Philosopher&#39;s Stone">Harry Potter and the Philosopher's Stone</a></i> return in the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Chamber_of_Secrets_(video_game)" title="Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets (video game)">next game</a> as guards to the Founders' Duels Arenas. As this portrait does not guard any arena in <i>Chamber of Secrets</i>, it is likely this individual is a <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor" title="Gryffindor">Gryffindor</a>.</li></ul>
