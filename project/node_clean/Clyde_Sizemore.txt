<b>Clyde Sizemore</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggle</a> theatre director in the early <a href="/wiki/20th_century" title="20th century">20th century</a>. He directed a production of <i><a href="/wiki/Oh,_Rosana!" title="Oh, Rosana!">Oh, Rosana!</a></i> that was slated to open at the <a href="/wiki/Brockman_Theatre" title="Brockman Theatre">Brockman Theatre</a> in <a href="/wiki/New_York" title="New York">New York</a> on <a href="/wiki/4_January" title="4 January">4 January</a> <a href="/wiki/1927" title="1927">1927</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-rosana_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-rosana-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
