<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Gringotts_Head_Goblin?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Gringotts_Head_Goblin?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>It is unknown if he was still employed at Gringotts, because he was not seen among the other Goblins when Harry and his friends broke into the bank on <a href="/wiki/1_May" title="1 May">1 May</a>, <a href="/wiki/1998" title="1998">1998</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-DH26_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH26-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li>The Head Goblin was portrayed by <a href="/wiki/Warwick_Davis" title="Warwick Davis">Warwick Davis</a>, who also portrayed <a href="/wiki/Professor" title="Professor">Professor</a> <a href="/wiki/Filius_Flitwick" title="Filius Flitwick">Filius Flitwick</a>. Davis went on to play <a href="/wiki/Griphook" title="Griphook">Griphook</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Deathly_Hallows_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows (film)">film adaptation</a> of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Deathly_Hallows" title="Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows">Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows</a></i>.</li>
<li>This goblin's role in the story is replaced by <a href="/wiki/Griphook" title="Griphook">Griphook</a> in most <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Philosopher%27s_Stone_(video_game)" title="Harry Potter and the Philosopher&#39;s Stone (video game)">video game adaptations</a>.</li></ul>
