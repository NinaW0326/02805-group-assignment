<b>Derwent Shimpling</b> (b. <a href="/wiki/1912" title="1912">1912</a>) was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizarding</a> comedian. He once ate an entire <a href="/wiki/Venomous_Tentacula" title="Venomous Tentacula">Venomous Tentacula</a> on a bet, and, though he survived, his skin was left purple.<sup id="cite_ref-PSG_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PSG-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-COSG_4-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COSG-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Derwent_Shimpling?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Derwent_Shimpling?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_life">Early life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Derwent_Shimpling?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Early life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Derwent_Shimpling?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Early life"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Shimpling was born in the year <a href="/wiki/1912" title="1912">1912</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-PSG_3-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PSG-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-COSG_4-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COSG-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="As_a_comedian">As a comedian</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Derwent_Shimpling?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: As a comedian">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Derwent_Shimpling?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: As a comedian"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Shimpling became a famed comedian, very popular for his daring sense of humour.<sup id="cite_ref-WoM_2-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-WoM-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> Shimpling was also famous for making bets lightly. Once, he ate an entire <a href="/wiki/Venomous_Tentacula" title="Venomous Tentacula">Venomous Tentacula</a> on a bet, and, though he survived, his skin was left permanently purple.<sup id="cite_ref-PSG_3-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PSG-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-COSG_4-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COSG-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Derwent_Shimpling?veaction=edit&amp;section=5" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Derwent_Shimpling?action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p><i>Derwent </i>means "valley thick with oaks". It is a village in Derbyshire that has been drowned by the Ladybower reservoir. Oaks usually symboloze strength and endurance,as Derwent had to bear through as he ate a Venomous Tentacula.
</p><p>Shimpling is a village and civil parish in South Suffolk, England. Located around seven miles from Bury St Edmunds, it is a part of the Baghbert district.
</p>
