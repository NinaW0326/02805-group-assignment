<p><b>Mrs Crouch</b> (d.<a href="/wiki/1980s" title="1980s">1980s</a>) was the wife of <a href="/wiki/Bartemius_Crouch_Senior" title="Bartemius Crouch Senior">Bartemius Crouch Snr</a>. She participated in one of the few <a href="/wiki/Azkaban_escapes#1982" title="Azkaban escapes">escapes</a> from <a href="/wiki/Azkaban" title="Azkaban">Azkaban</a> in the prison's history, taking the place of her son, <a href="/wiki/Bartemius_Crouch_Junior" title="Bartemius Crouch Junior">Bartemius Crouch Junior</a>.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Bartemius_Crouch_Junior%27s_mother?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Bartemius_Crouch_Junior%27s_mother?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_life">Early life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Bartemius_Crouch_Junior%27s_mother?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Early life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Bartemius_Crouch_Junior%27s_mother?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Early life"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Mrs Crouch was born into a <a href="/wiki/Pure-blood" title="Pure-blood">pure-blood</a> family.<sup id="cite_ref-Blood_3-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Blood-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> She married <a href="/wiki/Bartemius_Crouch_Senior" title="Bartemius Crouch Senior">Bartemius Crouch</a> and had one son, also named <a href="/wiki/Bartemius_Crouch_Junior" title="Bartemius Crouch Junior">Bartemius</a>. Mr Crouch was so focused on his career within the <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">Ministry of Magic</a> that he gave little devotion to his duties as a husband and father. As such, Mrs Crouch was namely responsible for home and raising their child.<sup id="cite_ref-GOF27_4-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF27-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="First_Wizarding_War_and_aftermath">First Wizarding War and aftermath</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Bartemius_Crouch_Junior%27s_mother?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: First Wizarding War and aftermath">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Bartemius_Crouch_Junior%27s_mother?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: First Wizarding War and aftermath"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<figure class="thumb tleft show-info-icon" style="width: 262px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/a/ae/B4C30M1_Wizengamot_trial_Pensieve_memory.png/revision/latest?cb=20190313000102" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/a/ae/B4C30M1_Wizengamot_trial_Pensieve_memory.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/260?cb=20190313000102" decoding="async" width="260" height="104" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="B4C30M1 Wizengamot trial Pensieve memory.png" data-image-key="B4C30M1_Wizengamot_trial_Pensieve_memory.png" /></a> 	 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:B4C30M1_Wizengamot_trial_Pensieve_memory.png" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption">Mrs Crouch witnessing her son on trial</p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure>
<p>Shortly after <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort's</a> first downfall, in <a href="/wiki/1981" title="1981">1981</a>, Barty Jnr was caught amongst a group of <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a> who were convicted of <a href="/wiki/Cruciatus_Curse" title="Cruciatus Curse">torturing</a> <a href="/wiki/Auror" title="Auror">Aurors</a> <a href="/wiki/Alice_Longbottom" title="Alice Longbottom">Alice</a> and <a href="/wiki/Frank_Longbottom" title="Frank Longbottom">Frank Longbottom</a> into <a href="/wiki/Insanity" title="Insanity">insanity</a>. Mrs Crouch was present for her son's trial in <a href="/wiki/Courtroom_Ten" title="Courtroom Ten">Courtroom Ten</a>, sitting next to her husband, who presided as the judge, sobbing. She suffered the terrible and sad experience of watching her husband renounce their nineteen-year-old son and condemn him to life in <a href="/wiki/Azkaban" title="Azkaban">Azkaban</a> without listening to boy's crying pleas and explanations. After the sentence was passed, she collapsed in shock and grief of what had happened.<sup id="cite_ref-GOF30_5-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF30-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Rescuing_her_son_and_death">Rescuing her son and death</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Bartemius_Crouch_Junior%27s_mother?veaction=edit&amp;section=4" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Rescuing her son and death">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Bartemius_Crouch_Junior%27s_mother?action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Rescuing her son and death"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<div class="quote">
<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire">"<i>My mother saved me. She knew she was dying. She persuaded my father to rescue me as a last favour to her.</i>"</span></dd>
<dd>&#8212;Barty Jnr describing his mother's last favour.<sup class="noprint"><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Goblet_of_Fire" title="Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire">[src]</a></sup></dd>
<dd></dd></dl></div><p>About one year after their son's imprisonment, Mrs Crouch's health deteriorated drastically from the stress. In the last few weeks of her life, Mrs Crouch convinced her husband to allow her to trade places with her son in the wizard prison during their deathbed visit of their son that was permitted due to her husband's high status in the Ministry. Both mother and son drank <a href="/wiki/Polyjuice_Potion" title="Polyjuice Potion">Polyjuice Potion</a> to take each other's appearance, and she went to Azkaban while Barty Jnr went to live in hiding with the help of their <a href="/wiki/House-elf" title="House-elf">House-elf</a> <a href="/wiki/Winky" title="Winky">Winky</a>. Mrs Crouch drank the remaining potion slowly and sparingly, to ensure herself not to be exposed for the other prisoners to see, and died shortly thereafter in Azkaban. She was buried by the <a href="/wiki/Dementor" title="Dementor">Dementors</a> outside the walls under her son's name and appearance,<sup id="cite_ref-GOF35_6-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF35-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup> as <a href="/wiki/Sirius_Black" title="Sirius Black">Sirius Black</a> witnessed.<sup id="cite_ref-GOF27_4-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF27-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p><h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Postmortem">Postmortem</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Bartemius_Crouch_Junior%27s_mother?veaction=edit&amp;section=5" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Postmortem">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Bartemius_Crouch_Junior%27s_mother?action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Postmortem"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Since Barty Snr could not have retrieved his wife's body from Azkaban without exposing what they had done, he staged her death, and then held a quiet, private funeral for her, with an empty grave.<sup id="cite_ref-GOF35_6-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF35-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Winky would use Mrs Crouch's dying wish for her son's freedom to persuade the elder Barty to allow his son attend the 422nd <a href="/wiki/Quidditch_World_Cup" title="Quidditch World Cup">Quidditch World Cup</a>. Though Crouch eventually relented, this led to a downward spiral of negative events that led to both father and son to be destroyed along with Voldemort's return to full power, leaving Mrs Crouch's efforts for her son to be tragically wasted.<sup id="cite_ref-GOF35_6-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF35-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Physical_appearance">Physical appearance</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Bartemius_Crouch_Junior%27s_mother?veaction=edit&amp;section=6" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Physical appearance">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Bartemius_Crouch_Junior%27s_mother?action=edit&amp;section=6" title="Edit section: Physical appearance"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Mrs Crouch was a short, frail and wispy-looking witch.<sup id="cite_ref-GOF30_5-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF30-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> When she swapped places with her son in prison, she took on his 20-years old appearance for the remainder of her life, and died bearing his form and name.<sup id="cite_ref-GOF35_6-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF35-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Personality_and_traits">Personality and traits</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Bartemius_Crouch_Junior%27s_mother?veaction=edit&amp;section=7" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Personality and traits">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Bartemius_Crouch_Junior%27s_mother?action=edit&amp;section=7" title="Edit section: Personality and traits"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Mrs Crouch was a frail and emotional woman, as she was sobbing when her son was accused of crimes punishable by life sentence, to the point of fainting when her husband ruthlessly carried out the sentence and denouncing their son.<sup id="cite_ref-GOF30_5-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF30-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> She was a very <a href="/wiki/Love" title="Love">loving</a> mother, as she swapped places with her son to allow him to escape prison while suffering in his place and desiring him to live out his new life for the better. According to Winky, Mrs Crouch would have wanted her son to enjoy some fresh air and freedom, not to be imprisoned by the <a href="/wiki/Imperius_Curse" title="Imperius Curse">Imperius Curse</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-GOF35_6-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF35-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup> However, despite her love for her son, she had a poor judge of character, being oblivious or in denial to her son's true nature, with her act in rescuing him from Azkaban ultimately resulting in Voldemort's return and the downfalls of both both her son and husband.
</p>

<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Barty_Crouch_Snr">Barty Crouch Snr</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Bartemius_Crouch_Junior%27s_mother?veaction=edit&amp;section=9" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Barty Crouch Snr">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Bartemius_Crouch_Junior%27s_mother?action=edit&amp;section=9" title="Edit section: Barty Crouch Snr"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>

<p>Mrs Crouch's husband, <a href="/wiki/Bartemius_Crouch_Senior" title="Bartemius Crouch Senior">Barty Crouch Snr</a>, was very neglectful of his family life due to his devotion in the <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">Ministry of Magic</a>'s eradication of the <a href="/wiki/Dark_Arts" title="Dark Arts">Dark Forces</a> such as the <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-GOF27_4-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF27-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> She was crushed when he dismissed their only son and sentenced him to Azkaban for life.<sup id="cite_ref-GOF30_5-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF30-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> However, they clearly loved each other deeply, no matter how the public views Mr Crouch's ruthlessness, as he agreed to his wife's dying wish to save their son in exchange for the last few weeks of her terminally-ill life. As Barty could not retrieve his wife's corpse without fearing exposure of the jailbreak, he had to stage her death and hold a quiet, private funeral for her with an empty grave. Barty would be persuaded to let his son attend the Quidditch World Cup when his wife's wishes were brought up by Winky, showing he still love her despite thirteen years have passed.<sup id="cite_ref-GOF35_6-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF35-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>During his <a href="/wiki/Insanity" title="Insanity">delusive state</a> from barely escaping <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort's</a> <a href="/wiki/Imperius_Curse" title="Imperius Curse">Imperius Curse</a>, Barty was muttering to a tree, talking as though his wife is still alive.<sup id="cite_ref-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Barty_Crouch_Jnr">Barty Crouch Jnr</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Bartemius_Crouch_Junior%27s_mother?veaction=edit&amp;section=10" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Barty Crouch Jnr">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Bartemius_Crouch_Junior%27s_mother?action=edit&amp;section=10" title="Edit section: Barty Crouch Jnr"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<div class="quote">
<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire">"<i>She said my mother would have wanted it. She told my father that my mother had died to give me freedom. She had not saved me for a life of imprisonment.</i>"</span></dd>
<dd>&#8212;Barty Jnr reiterating what Winky claimed Mrs Crouch would have wanted<sup class="noprint"><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Goblet_of_Fire" title="Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire">[src]</a></sup></dd>
<dd></dd></dl></div><p>Mrs Crouch loved her only son, <a href="/wiki/Bartemius_Crouch_Junior" title="Bartemius Crouch Junior">Barty Crouch Jnr</a>, very much. She was sobbing uncontrollably when he was convicted of a crime, and collapsed in shock and grief when her husband sentenced their child to life in prison.<sup id="cite_ref-GOF30_5-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF30-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> Mrs Crouch's love was what led to her health to deteriorate, and made her sacrifice her own life to rescue her son, while hoping that he would be able to live a second chance of freedom and a new leaf. She died bearing his appearance and name.<sup id="cite_ref-GOF35_6-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF35-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Originally, Barty Jnr seemed to have loved his mother in return, as he was crying for her help during his sentencing,<sup id="cite_ref-GOF30_5-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF30-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> and was screaming for her for the first week in his cell.<sup id="cite_ref-GOF35_6-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF35-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup> However, as time passed, with the combination of the <a href="/wiki/Dementor" title="Dementor">Dementors</a>' negative effects and his father's cruel and ruthless disinheriting, this caused Crouch Jnr to suffer a severe mental decline, changing him into an insane and psychopathic murderer who was fanatically loyal to Lord Voldemort, thus blinding him from those who truly loved him, namely his mother and the house-elf Winky. This renewed hatred led Barty to forget his mother and commit more crimes, such as patricide and reentering Voldemort's services, and then receiving the worse-than-<a href="/wiki/Death" title="Death">death</a> <a href="/wiki/Dementor%27s_Kiss" title="Dementor&#39;s Kiss">Dementor's Kiss</a>. Conclusively, Mrs Crouch's love, sacrifices and good intentions were tragically wasted.
</p>
