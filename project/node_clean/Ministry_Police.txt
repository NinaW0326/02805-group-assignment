The <b>Ministry Police</b> were a body in charge of law enforcement and providing security services within the headquarters of the <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">Ministry of Magic</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-poster_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-poster-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>After the <a href="/wiki/Fall_of_the_Ministry_of_Magic" title="Fall of the Ministry of Magic">fall of the Ministry of Magic</a>, the Ministry Police came under the control of Death Eaters and issued the <a href="/wiki/Undesirable" title="Undesirable">Undesirable</a> No. 1 posters for information on the whereabouts of <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-poster_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-poster-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> During the <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eater</a> regime, members of the Ministry Police were stationed at the <a href="/wiki/Atrium" title="Atrium">Atrium</a> and arrested <a href="/wiki/Muggle-born" title="Muggle-born">Muggle-borns</a> who arrived at the <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">Ministry</a>. They were also in charge of transporting convicted <a href="/wiki/Muggle-born" title="Muggle-born">Muggle-borns</a> from the courtrooms. Each member wore a dark red strip of fabric around his or her left arm, signifying their position. These armbands were also worn by Snatchers, suggesting a possible connection between the two groups.<sup id="cite_ref-DHpt1_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DHpt1-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Ministry_Police?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Ministry_Police?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>While it is likely that the Ministry of Magic indeed have some sort of unit of security wizards tasked with providing security services, the existence of a Ministry <i>Police</i> can not be considered canon, due to the fact that <a href="/wiki/Amos_Diggory" title="Amos Diggory">Amos Diggory</a> was unfamiliar with the word "policemen", something that would not have been the case if the Ministry, where he worked, had their own inter-ministerial 'police' force.</li>
<li>They may in actuality be <a href="/wiki/Watchwizard" title="Watchwizard">watchwizards and/or watchwitches</a>, like <a href="/wiki/Eric_Munch" title="Eric Munch">Eric Munch</a>.</li></ul>
