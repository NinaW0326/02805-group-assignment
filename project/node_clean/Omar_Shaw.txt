<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Omar_Shaw?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Omar_Shaw?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>He was fond of <a href="/wiki/Weasleys%27_Wizard_Wheezes" title="Weasleys&#39; Wizard Wheezes">Weasleys' Wizard Wheezes</a>, and, while at school during the <a href="/wiki/1996-1997_school_year" class="mw-redirect" title="1996-1997 school year">1996-1997 school year</a>, he traded <a href="/wiki/Stink_Pellets" title="Stink Pellets">Stink Pellets</a>, <a href="/wiki/Fanged_Frisbee" title="Fanged Frisbee">Fanged Frisbees</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Ever-Bashing_Boomerang" title="Ever-Bashing Boomerang">Ever-Bashing Boomerangs</a> he had purchased at the store for various objects (<a href="/wiki/Gobstones" title="Gobstones">Gobstones</a>, <a href="/wiki/Chocolate_Frog_Card" title="Chocolate Frog Card">Chocolate Frog Cards</a>, <a href="/wiki/Wizard%27s_Chess" title="Wizard&#39;s Chess">chess</a> pieces, and <a href="/wiki/Butterbeer" title="Butterbeer">Butterbeer</a>). He could usually be seen spending time in the <a href="/wiki/The_Quad" title="The Quad">Paved Courtyard</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-HBPG_4-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBPG-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Omar_Shaw?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Omar_Shaw?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The name "Omar" means "eloquent speaker" in Hebrew and "long-lived" in Arabic.
</p>
