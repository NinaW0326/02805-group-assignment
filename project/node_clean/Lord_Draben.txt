<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Lord_Draben?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Lord_Draben?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>In his lifetime, Lord Draben was a <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Cavalier" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Cavalier">Cavalier</a>: a Royalist supporter of <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Charles_I_of_England" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Charles I of England">King Charles I</a> and his son <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Charles_II_of_England" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Charles II of England">Charles II</a> during the English Civil War, the Interregnum, and the Restoration in the mid-<a href="/wiki/17th_century" title="17th century">seventeenth century</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-PSF_2-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PSF-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>When Lord Draben died, he became a <a href="/wiki/Ghost" title="Ghost">ghost</a>, taking residence at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Castle" title="Hogwarts Castle">Hogwarts Castle</a>. He attended the <a href="/wiki/Start-of-Term_Feast" title="Start-of-Term Feast">Start-of-Term Feast</a> in <a href="/wiki/1991" title="1991">1991</a>, and later joined a group of <a href="/wiki/Ghost_carollers" title="Ghost carollers">ghost carollers</a> singing <i><a href="/wiki/Christmas_at_Hogwarts" title="Christmas at Hogwarts">Christmas at Hogwarts</a></i> later that year.<sup id="cite_ref-PSF_2-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PSF-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
