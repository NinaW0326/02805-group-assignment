<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Bowman_Wright?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Bowman_Wright?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Born to a witch <a href="/wiki/Bowman_Wright%27s_mother" title="Bowman Wright&#39;s mother">mother</a> and a <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggle</a> <a href="/wiki/Bowman_Wright%27s_father" title="Bowman Wright&#39;s father">father</a>, he invented the <a href="/wiki/Golden_Snitch" title="Golden Snitch">Golden Snitch</a> as a replacement for the endangered <a href="/wiki/Golden_Snidget" title="Golden Snidget">Golden Snidget</a>, combining his interest in <a href="/wiki/Science" title="Science">science</a>, <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magic</a>, and sports.<sup id="cite_ref-PSG_2-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PSG-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-COSG_3-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COSG-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-JKRS_4-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-JKRS-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-QTAR_5-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-QTAR-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Magical_abilities_and_skills">Magical abilities and skills</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Bowman_Wright?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Bowman_Wright?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li><b><a href="/wiki/Charm" title="Charm">Charms</a></b>: Being a professional <a href="/wiki/Metal_charmer" title="Metal charmer">Metal charmer</a>, Bowman was highly gifted and skilful with charm-work, as he was the inventor of the famous <a href="/wiki/Golden_Snitch" title="Golden Snitch">Golden Snitch</a>, a <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magical</a> <a href="/wiki/Levitation" title="Levitation">flying</a> object used in <a href="/wiki/Quidditch" title="Quidditch">Quidditch</a>, casting the necessary charms needed to both craft and enchant the Snitch, which continued to last long after his death, showing his superb proficiency in this field of <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magic</a>.</li></ul>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Bowman_Wright?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Bowman_Wright?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The English "wright" is a word referring to a skilled craftsman.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Bowman_Wright?veaction=edit&amp;section=4" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Bowman_Wright?action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>The dates given for Bowman's birth and death on his Famous Wizard Card (1492-1560) do not agree with the timeframe laid down in <i><a href="/wiki/Quidditch_Through_the_Ages_(real)" title="Quidditch Through the Ages (real)">Quidditch Through the Ages</a></i>, which would indicate that he was alive towards the end of the <a href="/wiki/1300s" title="1300s">1300s</a>.</li>
<li>Bowman shares the same last name as <a href="/wiki/Bonnie_Wright" title="Bonnie Wright">Bonnie Wright</a>, who portrays <a href="/wiki/Ginevra_Weasley" title="Ginevra Weasley">Ginny Weasley</a> in the <i>Harry Potter</i> films. Incidentally, Bonnie Wright's parents, Gary Wright and Sheila Teague, are both jewellers.</li></ul>
