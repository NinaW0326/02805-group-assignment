<p><b>Stephen "Steve" McClaren</b> was the manager of the <a href="/wiki/England_national_football_team" title="England national football team">England national football team</a> by <a href="/wiki/July" title="July">July</a> of <a href="/wiki/1996" title="1996">1996</a>. When the team's captain <a href="/wiki/John_Terry" title="John Terry">John Terry</a> could not complete a practise due to a reoccurrence of a knee injury, McClaren made the decision to replace him with <a href="/wiki/Sol_Campbell" title="Sol Campbell">Sol Campbell</a> for the upcoming group E fixture against the <a href="/wiki/Russia_national_football_team" title="Russia national football team">Russia national football team</a> in <a href="/wiki/Moscow" title="Moscow">Moscow</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

In 1996, McClaren was working as the assistant manager of <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Derby_County_F.C." class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Derby County F.C.">Derby County F.C.</a></li></ul>
