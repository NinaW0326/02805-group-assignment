<b>Denison Frisby</b> was an <a href="/wiki/England" title="England">English</a> <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> who played as <a href="/wiki/Keeper" title="Keeper">Keeper</a> on the <a href="/wiki/English_National_Quidditch_team" title="English National Quidditch team">English National Quidditch team</a> during the four-hundred and twenty-second <a href="/wiki/Quidditch_World_Cup" title="Quidditch World Cup">Quidditch World Cup</a>, in <a href="/wiki/1994" title="1994">1994</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-QWC_1-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-QWC-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>By <a href="/wiki/1996" title="1996">1996</a>, Frisby was included on a <a href="/wiki/Chocolate_Frog_Card" title="Chocolate Frog Card">Chocolate Frog Card</a> for his achievements in the sport of <a href="/wiki/Quidditch" title="Quidditch">Quidditch</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-HBPG_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBPG-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
