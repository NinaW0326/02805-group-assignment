</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Brutus_Scrimgeour?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Brutus_Scrimgeour?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Life_and_career">Life and career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Brutus_Scrimgeour?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Life and career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Brutus_Scrimgeour?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Life and career"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>During his life, Scrimgeour taught at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-QoSC_2-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-QoSC-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> and was eventually appointed <a href="/wiki/Headmaster" title="Headmaster">headmaster</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-hp-kinect_3-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-hp-kinect-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-mohp_4-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-mohp-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Scrimgeour was a fan of Quidditch and may have been a <a href="/wiki/Beater" title="Beater">Beater</a> at some point.<sup id="cite_ref-qta_5-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-qta-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> He wrote a guide for Beaters, <i><a href="/wiki/The_Beaters%27_Bible" title="The Beaters&#39; Bible">The Beaters' Bible</a></i>, and gave a positive review of the book <i><a href="/wiki/Quidditch_Through_the_Ages" title="Quidditch Through the Ages">Quidditch Through the Ages</a></i> by <a href="/wiki/Kennilworthy_Whisp" title="Kennilworthy Whisp">Kennilworthy Whisp</a>, saying that it was "<i>the definitive work on the origins and history of Quidditch</i>" and that he "<i>highly recommended</i>" it.<sup id="cite_ref-qta_5-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-qta-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Legacy">Legacy</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Brutus_Scrimgeour?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Legacy">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Brutus_Scrimgeour?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Legacy"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Scrimgeour died sometime in or before the year <a href="/wiki/1994" title="1994">1994</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-DOD_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DOD-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> Multiple <a href="/wiki/Portrait" title="Portrait">portraits</a> of him were thereafter hung within <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Castle" title="Hogwarts Castle">Hogwarts Castle</a>.
</p><p>One portrait, hung in the <a href="/wiki/Trophy_Room" title="Trophy Room">Trophy Room</a>, hid a secret passage to the fourth floor.<sup id="cite_ref-ootp-gba_6-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-ootp-gba-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup> During the <a href="/wiki/1994-1995_school_year" class="mw-redirect" title="1994-1995 school year">1994-1995 school year</a>, the <a href="/wiki/Password" title="Password">password</a> to open this passage was initially "<i>Beati pacifici</i>,"<sup id="cite_ref-ootp-gba_6-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-ootp-gba-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup> meaning "blessed are the peacemakers" in <a href="/wiki/Latin" title="Latin">Latin</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup> This was changed to "<i>Iunctis viribus</i>" ("by united efforts" in Latin)<sup id="cite_ref-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup> later in the same school year.<sup id="cite_ref-ootp-gba_6-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-ootp-gba-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Another portrait of Scrimgeour was hung in the <a href="/wiki/Headmaster%27s_office" class="mw-redirect" title="Headmaster&#39;s office">Headmaster's office</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-mohp_4-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-mohp-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<figure class="thumb tright show-info-icon" style="width: 252px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/d/d8/Brutus_Scrimgeour.png/revision/latest?cb=20151024191356" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/d/d8/Brutus_Scrimgeour.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/250?cb=20151024191356" decoding="async" width="250" height="216" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="Brutus Scrimgeour.png" data-image-key="Brutus_Scrimgeour.png" /></a> 	 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:Brutus_Scrimgeour.png" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption">Brutus's portrait.</p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure><p>The name "Brutus" is a Roman cognomen meaning "heavy" in Latin. Famous bearers include Lucius Junius Brutus, the traditional founder of the Roman Republic, and Marcus Junius Brutus, the statesman who conspired to assassinate Julius Caesar.
</p><h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Brutus_Scrimgeour?veaction=edit&amp;section=5" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Brutus_Scrimgeour?action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>Scrimgeour may have been related to <a href="/wiki/Rufus_Scrimgeour" title="Rufus Scrimgeour">Rufus Scrimgeour</a>, a <a href="/wiki/Minister_for_Magic" title="Minister for Magic">Minister for Magic</a> killed by <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a>.</li>
<li>Scrimgeour's portrait was painted by artist <a class="new" title="Steve Mitchell (page does not exist)" rel="nofollow" data-uncrawlable-url="L3dpa2kvU3RldmVfTWl0Y2hlbGw/YWN0aW9uPWVkaXQmcmVkbGluaz0x">Steve Mitchell</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup> Mitchell created several portraits for the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_films" class="mw-redirect" title="Harry Potter films"><i>Harry Potter</i> films</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-10" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-10">&#91;10&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li>On "The Quest of Sir Cadogan" feature on Disc 2 of the <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Prisoner_of_Azkaban_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban (film)">Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban</a></i> DVD, Scrimgeour's portrait appears to be inverted horizontally.</li>
<li>The phrase <i>beati pacifici</i>, <a href="/wiki/Latin" title="Latin">Latin</a> for "blessed are the peacemakers", is a portion of <a href="/wiki/Bible" title="Bible">Matthew 5:9</a>. The full verse reads <i>beati pacifici quoniam filii Dei vocabuntur</i> ("Blessed are the peacemakers: for they shall be called the children of <a href="/wiki/God" title="God">God</a>").</li></ul>
