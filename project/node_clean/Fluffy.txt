<p><b>Fluffy</b> was a gigantic, monstrous male <a href="/wiki/Three-headed_dog" title="Three-headed dog">three-headed dog</a> who was once cared for by <a href="/wiki/Rubeus_Hagrid" title="Rubeus Hagrid">Rubeus Hagrid</a>. Fluffy's greatest weakness was the inability to resist falling asleep to the sound of music.<sup id="cite_ref-PS16_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PS16-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Fluffy?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Fluffy?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span id="Guarding_the_Philosopher's_Stone"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="Guarding_the_Philosopher.27s_Stone">Guarding the Philosopher's Stone</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Fluffy?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Guarding the Philosopher&#039;s Stone">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Fluffy?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Guarding the Philosopher&#039;s Stone"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p><a href="/wiki/Rubeus_Hagrid" title="Rubeus Hagrid">Rubeus Hagrid</a> originally purchased Fluffy from a "<a href="/wiki/Greek_man" title="Greek man">Greek chappie</a>" at <a href="/wiki/Leaky_Cauldron" title="Leaky Cauldron">The Leaky Cauldron</a>. Hagrid loaned Fluffy to the headmaster, Albus Dumbledore, to aid in guarding the <a href="/wiki/Philosopher%27s_Stone" title="Philosopher&#39;s Stone">Philosopher's Stone</a>, during the <a href="/wiki/1991%E2%80%931992_school_year" title="1991–1992 school year">1991–1992 school year</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-PS11_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PS11-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> In addition, as further insurance against intruders, the door leading to the room Fluffy was in was locked <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magically</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-PS9_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PS9-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<figure class="thumb tleft show-info-icon" style="width: 252px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/1/1b/Fluffy2.png/revision/latest?cb=20170925204853" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/1/1b/Fluffy2.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/250?cb=20170925204853" decoding="async" width="250" height="104" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="Fluffy2.png" data-image-key="Fluffy2.png" /></a> 	 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:Fluffy2.png" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption">Fluffy sleeping before the trio enter his room</p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure>
<p><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>, <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione Granger</a> and <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ron Weasley</a>, along with <a href="/wiki/Neville_Longbottom" title="Neville Longbottom">Neville Longbottom</a>, first encountered Fluffy when <a href="/wiki/Draco_Malfoy" title="Draco Malfoy">Draco Malfoy</a> challenged Harry to a <a href="/wiki/Duelling" title="Duelling">duel</a>, which he accepted. When Hermione tried to stop Harry and Ron from sneaking out later that night, she found herself locked out of the <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor_Tower" title="Gryffindor Tower">Gryffindor Tower</a>, as the <a href="/wiki/Fat_Lady" title="Fat Lady">Fat Lady</a> had left sometime earlier, and ended up going along with them. Neville Longbottom, who had forgotten the password to the common room and was sleeping on the floor outside, joined them as well. When they arrived at the <a href="/wiki/Trophy_Room" title="Trophy Room">Trophy Room</a>, the intended site of the duel, Malfoy was nowhere to be found. To avoid being caught by <a href="/wiki/Argus_Filch" title="Argus Filch">Argus Filch</a>, they ran away and ended up in the forbidden area on the third floor, staring at the massive three-headed dog. Despite being furious about having broken countless school rules, Hermione had noticed that the dog was standing on a trap door, and clearly guarding something.<sup id="cite_ref-PS9_3-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PS9-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> They later deduced, from hints about <a href="/wiki/Nicolas_Flamel" title="Nicolas Flamel">Nicolas Flamel</a> dropped unintentionally by Hagrid, that this "something" was the <a href="/wiki/Philosopher%27s_Stone" title="Philosopher&#39;s Stone">Philosopher's Stone</a>.
</p>
<figure class="thumb tright show-info-icon" style="width: 252px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/c/c6/Fluffy_HP1.png/revision/latest?cb=20170925204810" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/c/c6/Fluffy_HP1.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/250?cb=20170925204810" decoding="async" width="250" height="104" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="Fluffy HP1.png" data-image-key="Fluffy_HP1.png" /></a> 	 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:Fluffy_HP1.png" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption">Fluffy angry-looking after the trio disturbed him</p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure>
<p>Later that year, Hagrid happily told the trio that he had a won a <a href="/wiki/Norberta" title="Norberta">dragon egg</a> in a card game at the <a href="/wiki/Hog%27s_Head" class="mw-redirect" title="Hog&#39;s Head">Hog's Head</a>. Thinking that it was too much of a coincidence that, as Hagrid had always dreamed of owning a <a href="/wiki/Dragon" title="Dragon">dragon</a>, someone just happened to show up at the local pub with a dragon egg, the trio questioned Hagrid about the supposed <a href="/wiki/Dragon_dealer" title="Dragon dealer">dragon dealer</a>. Upon finding out that Hagrid had never even seen the man's face, as he wore a hood, yet Hagrid had drunkenly told him how to get past Fluffy, they came to the conclusion that the hooded man must have been <a href="/wiki/Severus_Snape" title="Severus Snape">Severus Snape</a>, when it was really <a href="/wiki/Quirinus_Quirrell" title="Quirinus Quirrell">Quirinus Quirrell</a>. Before Hagrid told Quirrell, though he didn't know it was Quirrell, Hagrid and Dumbledore were the only ones who knew how to get past Fluffy.<sup id="cite_ref-PS14_4-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PS14-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>As that meant the stone was no longer safe, they decided to go after Snape themselves, and Harry brought along the <a href="/wiki/Flute" title="Flute">flute</a> that Hagrid had given him for <a href="/wiki/Christmas" title="Christmas">Christmas</a> to use in lulling Fluffy to sleep. Arriving at the room, they noticed an enchanted <a href="/wiki/Harp" title="Harp">harp</a> at a sleeping Fluffy's feet, and believed that Professor Snape was already on his way to retrieving the stone. Fluffy soon awoke, as the enchantment on the harp wore off, and it stopped playing. As Harry played the flute, which almost immediately put Fluffy back to sleep, Ron crept over to the trap door and opened it, but was unable to see anything below. Hermione took over playing the flute, while Harry jumped in first, followed by Ron. After they called up to Hermione that it was safe, she stopped playing and jumped down the trap door just in time, as Fluffy woke up as soon as the music stopped.<sup id="cite_ref-PS16_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PS16-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Return_to_Greece">Return to Greece</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Fluffy?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Return to Greece">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Fluffy?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Return to Greece"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>After Fluffy's duties were done, and the Stone was destroyed, Hagrid set Fluffy free temporarily in the <a href="/wiki/Forbidden_Forest" title="Forbidden Forest">Forbidden Forest</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-bluepeter_5-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-bluepeter-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> Shortly thereafter, he was sent back to his native <a href="/wiki/Greece" title="Greece">Greece</a> by Dumbledore.<sup id="cite_ref-jkr-twitter-fluffy_6-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-jkr-twitter-fluffy-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>In <a href="/wiki/2019" title="2019">2019</a>, Fluffy was suspected to have been involved in an incident with a taco truck.<sup id="cite_ref-WU_7-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-WU-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Personality_and_traits">Personality and traits</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Fluffy?veaction=edit&amp;section=4" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Personality and traits">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Fluffy?action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Personality and traits"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Fluffy was generally vicious, fierce, and extremely strong, though he possibly acted somewhat more docile around certain people, like <a href="/wiki/Rubeus_Hagrid" title="Rubeus Hagrid">Rubeus Hagrid</a>, who was able to get Fluffy into the third floor corridor. One known way to calm Fluffy was to play music, which made him fall asleep.<sup id="cite_ref-PS16_1-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PS16-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/Three-headed_dog" title="Three-headed dog">Three-headed dogs</a> were very rare creatures, and it is unknown if, like the <a href="/wiki/Runespoor" title="Runespoor">Runespoor</a>, each head served a different purpose.
</p>

<ul><li>In the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Philosopher%27s_Stone_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Philosopher&#39;s Stone (film)">film adaptation</a> of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Philosopher%27s_Stone" title="Harry Potter and the Philosopher&#39;s Stone">Harry Potter and the Philosopher's Stone</a></i>, Hagrid says he bought Fluffy from "an <a href="/wiki/Ireland" title="Ireland">Irish</a> feller" rather than "a <a href="/wiki/Greece" title="Greece">Greek</a> chappie".</li>
<li>In the film adaptation Neville was not present when the <a href="/wiki/Trio" title="Trio">trio</a> first met Fluffy.</li>
<li>In the book Quirrell and the trio use different musical instruments to put Fluffy to sleep, a harp and a flute. In the film adaptation the harp is still playing when they enter the room and only stops just before they jump down into the trap door just in the nick of time. In <i><a href="/wiki/LEGO_Harry_Potter:_Years_1-4" title="LEGO Harry Potter: Years 1-4">LEGO Harry Potter: Years 1-4</a></i> the harp is broken when they first enter the room and they have to distract Fluffy with a <a href="/wiki/Rubber_duck" title="Rubber duck">rubber duck</a> so that they can fix the harp.</li>
<li>Three-headed dogs are prominent in Greek and Roman mythology and are also known as <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/hellhound" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:hellhound">hellhounds</a>, the chief of all being <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Cerberus" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Cerberus">Cerberus</a>, Guardian to the Underworld. It is odd that the film adaptation would rather have had Hagrid purchase Fluffy from an Irish as opposed to Greek man, as Cerberus played a major role in Greek mythology.</li>
<li>The <a href="/wiki/Noble_Collection" title="Noble Collection">Noble Collection</a> sells a pewter metal version of Fluffy.</li>
<li>In <a href="/wiki/The_Wizarding_World_of_Harry_Potter" title="The Wizarding World of Harry Potter">The Wizarding World of Harry Potter</a>, one can purchase a plush version of Fluffy. Additionally, a massive animatronic of Fluffy eating a bone in a cave in the Forbidden Forest can be found in the <a href="/wiki/Hagrid%27s_Magical_Creatures_Motorbike_Adventure" title="Hagrid&#39;s Magical Creatures Motorbike Adventure">Hagrid's Magical Creatures Motorbike Adventure</a> attraction.</li>
<li>The film version of Fluffy appears to be physically based on a Staffordshire Bull Terrier.<sup class="noprint">&#91;<a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Wikipedia:Citing_sources" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Wikipedia:Citing sources"><i><span title="The material in the vicinity of this tag needs references to reliable sources." style="white-space: nowrap;">citation needed</span></i></a>&#93;</sup></li>
<li>To make Fluffy appear more realistic, his heads were made to move independently and to each have their own personality. One was "smart", one was "alert", and one was "sleepy".<sup id="cite_ref-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup></li></ul>
