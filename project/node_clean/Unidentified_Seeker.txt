<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_Seeker?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_Seeker?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<div class="quote">
<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;Harry Potter: Hogwarts Mystery">"<i>I've only ever played Seeker. Am I unbalanced?</i>"</span></dd>
<dd>&#8212;This girl to <a href="/wiki/Orion_Amari" title="Orion Amari">Orion Amari</a><sup class="noprint"><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter:_Hogwarts_Mystery" title="Harry Potter: Hogwarts Mystery">[src]</a></sup></dd>
<dd></dd></dl></div><p>During her time at Hogwarts, she was <a href="/wiki/Sorting_ceremony" title="Sorting ceremony">Sorted</a> into the same house as <a href="/wiki/Jacob%27s_sibling" title="Jacob&#39;s sibling">Jacob's sibling</a>. At some point by the <a href="/wiki/1985%E2%80%931986_school_year" title="1985–1986 school year">1985–1986 school year</a>, she became the <a href="/wiki/Seeker" title="Seeker">Seeker</a> of her house's Quidditch team.<sup id="cite_ref-HM_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HM-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>She continued playing on the team the following year.<sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>She was also afraid of <a href="/wiki/Gnome" title="Gnome">Gnomes</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>She apparently was not skilled at <a href="/wiki/Divination_(class)" title="Divination (class)">Divination</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
