<b>Vicky Bishopper</b> (b. <a href="/wiki/1710s" title="1710s">1710s</a>) was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch</a> and <a href="/wiki/Slytherin" title="Slytherin">Slytherin</a> student at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> in the <a href="/wiki/1720s" title="1720s">1720s</a> and <a href="/wiki/1730s" title="1730s">1730s</a>. She was <a href="/wiki/Quidditch_Captain#Hogwarts_Quidditch_Captains" title="Quidditch Captain">Captain</a> of the <a href="/wiki/Slytherin" title="Slytherin">Slytherin</a> <a href="/wiki/Quidditch" title="Quidditch">Quidditch</a> <a href="/wiki/Slytherin_Quidditch_team" title="Slytherin Quidditch team">team</a> from <a href="/wiki/1728" class="mw-redirect" title="1728">1728</a> to <a href="/wiki/1732" class="mw-redirect" title="1732">1732</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
