</p><p><b>Linfred of Stinchcombe</b> (also known by the nickname <b>The Potterer</b>) was a <a href="/wiki/12th_century" title="12th century">twelfth-century</a> <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> and pioneering <a href="/wiki/Potioneer" title="Potioneer">potioneer</a>, credited with inventing many medicinal <a href="/wiki/Potion" title="Potion">potions</a>. He was the founding patriarch of the <a href="/wiki/Potter_family" title="Potter family">Potter family</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-PM_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PM-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Linfred_of_Stinchcombe?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Linfred_of_Stinchcombe?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>In the <a href="/wiki/12th_century" title="12th century">12th century</a>, Linfred lived in the village of <a href="/wiki/Stinchcombe" title="Stinchcombe">Stinchcombe</a>, <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gloucestershire" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Gloucestershire">Gloucestershire</a>, where he had a reputation as an eccentric, absent-minded man.<sup id="cite_ref-PM_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PM-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> Nonetheless, his helpful nature and affability made him universally liked and his <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggle</a> neighbours often came to him seeking remedies for their ailments. He was always able to brew these&#160;<a href="/wiki/Potion" title="Potion">potions</a> for them, using ingredients readily-available from his garden.<sup id="cite_ref-PM_1-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PM-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p> The Muggles remained unaware that Linfred's remedies were <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magical</a> and thus continued thinking of him as just a strange, lovable man who enjoyed "pottering about in his garden with all his funny plants."<sup id="cite_ref-PM_1-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PM-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> This lead to him being given the nickname "the Potterer", which over the years was corrupted into simply "Potter."<sup id="cite_ref-PM_1-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PM-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>As his neighbours did not know he was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a>, Linfred was able to conduct experiments in peace, inventing a number of medicinal potions (including ones which later developed into <a href="/wiki/Skele-Gro" title="Skele-Gro">Skele-Gro</a> and <a href="/wiki/Pepperup_Potion" title="Pepperup Potion">Pepperup Potion</a>).<sup id="cite_ref-PM_1-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PM-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> He sold these potions to fellow witches and wizards, amassing great wealth in the process.<sup id="cite_ref-PM_1-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PM-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Linfred fathered seven children. He left each of them a significant amount of <a href="/wiki/Galleon" title="Galleon">gold</a> upon his death. His eldest son, <a href="/wiki/Hardwin_Potter" title="Hardwin Potter">Hardwin</a>, married <a href="/wiki/Iolanthe_Peverell" title="Iolanthe Peverell">Iolanthe Peverell</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-PM_1-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PM-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Magical_abilities_and_skills">Magical abilities and skills</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Linfred_of_Stinchcombe?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Linfred_of_Stinchcombe?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li><a href="/wiki/Potion" title="Potion"><b>Potions</b></a>: Linfred was a master <a href="/wiki/Potioneer" title="Potioneer">Potioneer</a> and inventor in the twelfth century. He invented a number of medicinal potions that are still useful to this day.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Herbology" title="Herbology"><b>Herbology</b></a>: He possessed considerable skills in Herbology, as he was able to grow the plants in his garden needed for ingredients in brewing potions.</li></ul>
