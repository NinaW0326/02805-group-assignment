<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Cook_for_the_Riddles">Cook for the Riddles</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Riddles%27_cook?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Cook for the Riddles">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Riddles%27_cook?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Cook for the Riddles"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>This woman served the <a href="/wiki/Riddle_family" title="Riddle family">Riddle family</a> in the capacity of a cook. She probably lived in the <a href="/wiki/Riddle_House" title="Riddle House">Riddle House</a> along with other employees. She tried many times to get acquainted with fellow employee <a href="/wiki/Frank_Bryce" title="Frank Bryce">Frank Bryce</a>, but the man turned her down, preferring solitary work.<sup id="cite_ref-GOF1_1-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF1-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="The_Riddle_Murders">The Riddle Murders</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Riddles%27_cook?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: The Riddle Murders">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Riddles%27_cook?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: The Riddle Murders"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Many town residents gathered at <a href="/wiki/The_Hanged_Man" title="The Hanged Man">The Hanged Man</a> to gossip on the night the <a href="/wiki/Riddle_family" title="Riddle family">Riddles</a> were murdered in <a href="/wiki/1943" title="1943">1943</a>. It was her who brought the news about the arrest of the Riddles' gardener, <a href="/wiki/Frank_Bryce" title="Frank Bryce">Frank Bryce</a>, after quite a dramatic entrance, and was subsequently bought more and more <a href="/wiki/Sherry" title="Sherry">sherries</a> by the pub patrons, eager to hear more. She suspected Bryce did it, pointing out how easily he could have done it, since he had a set of spare keys to the <a href="/wiki/Riddle_House" title="Riddle House">house</a>, and there was no evidence of a forceful entry.<sup id="cite_ref-GOF1_1-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF1-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
