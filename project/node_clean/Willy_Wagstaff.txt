<b>"Honest" Willy Wagstaff</b> was a street vendor who worked in <a href="/wiki/Diagon_Alley" title="Diagon Alley">Diagon Alley</a><sup id="cite_ref-:0_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:0-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>. The <a href="/wiki/Department_of_Magical_Equipment_Control" title="Department of Magical Equipment Control">Department of Magical Equipment Control</a> issued a warning about him in the <i><a href="/wiki/Daily_Prophet" title="Daily Prophet">Daily Prophet</a></i>, alleging that he sold defective merchandise, including loose-bottomed <a href="/wiki/Cauldron" title="Cauldron">cauldrons</a> and <a href="/wiki/Wand" title="Wand">wands</a> that had to be recalled after several people were burned by them<sup id="cite_ref-:0_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:0-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>.
