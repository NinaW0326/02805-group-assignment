<p><b>Gideon Flatworthy</b> (d. <a href="/wiki/18_September" title="18 September">18 September</a>, <a href="/wiki/1743" class="mw-redirect" title="1743">1743</a>) was a <a href="/wiki/Great_Britain" title="Great Britain">British</a> <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> and an anti-<a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggle</a> extremist. </p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Gideon_Flatworthy?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Gideon_Flatworthy?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_life">Early life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Gideon_Flatworthy?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Early life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Gideon_Flatworthy?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Early life"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Little is known of his early life. He was born in <a href="/wiki/Europe" title="Europe">Europe</a>, as it is known that he had an account in <a href="/wiki/Gringotts_Wizarding_Bank" title="Gringotts Wizarding Bank">Gringotts Wizarding Bank</a> but did not use it as he was too lazy.<sup id="cite_ref-WB_1-10" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-WB-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Later_life">Later life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Gideon_Flatworthy?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Later life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Gideon_Flatworthy?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Later life"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>After learning the <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magical</a> arts, he formed the <a href="/wiki/Accionites" title="Accionites">Accionites</a>, a gang of anti-muggle activists who mostly just sat around and did nothing. When they ran out of <a href="/wiki/Gold" title="Gold">gold</a>, instead of going to <a href="/wiki/Gringotts_Wizarding_Bank" title="Gringotts Wizarding Bank">Gringotts</a> to get more he instead summoned several valuable <a href="/wiki/Goblin" title="Goblin">goblin</a>-made artefacts. The <a href="/wiki/Goblin" title="Goblin">goblins</a> who had created them came to the <a href="/wiki/Accionites" title="Accionites">Accionites</a>' headquarters, brandishing swords angrily and "baying for the Accionites' blood".<sup id="cite_ref-WB_1-11" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-WB-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Death_and_post-mortem">Death and post-mortem</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Gideon_Flatworthy?veaction=edit&amp;section=4" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Death and post-mortem">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Gideon_Flatworthy?action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Death and post-mortem"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>After disapparating twice to avoid the goblins and being abandoned by his gang, he tried to <a href="/wiki/Summoning_Charm" title="Summoning Charm">summon</a> a farm, some livestock, and a well-stocked larder. He was crushed to death by the bales of hay and cattle that landed on top of him. Experts are uncertain why he did this (they still debate about it) although the most common theory is that he became delirious from lack of food.<sup id="cite_ref-WB_1-12" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-WB-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> Sometime after he died, <a href="/wiki/Miranda_Goshawk" title="Miranda Goshawk">Miranda Goshawk</a> published his story in her <i><a href="/wiki/Book_of_Spells" title="Book of Spells">Book of Spells</a></i>, in a wizarding play about the <a href="/wiki/Summoning_Charm" title="Summoning Charm">Summoning Charm</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-WB_1-13" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-WB-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Physical_appearance">Physical appearance</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Gideon_Flatworthy?veaction=edit&amp;section=5" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Physical appearance">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Gideon_Flatworthy?action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Physical appearance"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>He was extremely overweight from having lain on a pile of cushions most of his adult life, and wore a golden crown with blue robes. Flatworthy also had an extremely thin moustache.<sup id="cite_ref-WB_1-14" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-WB-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Personality_and_traits">Personality and traits</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Gideon_Flatworthy?veaction=edit&amp;section=6" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Personality and traits">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Gideon_Flatworthy?action=edit&amp;section=6" title="Edit section: Personality and traits"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Gideon was a <a href="/wiki/Wizarding_supremacy" title="Wizarding supremacy">wizarding supremacist</a>, having formed the <a href="/wiki/Accionites" title="Accionites">Accionites</a> group to prove wizarding superiority. However, he was very lazy, and his act of despising muggles was solely believing that being able to lift and carry objects magically was what made him and his group superior to them. His laziness was exemplified by his constant usage of the Summoning Charm to bring objects to him, to the point of taking gold and treasures from <a href="/wiki/Gringotts_Wizarding_Bank" title="Gringotts Wizarding Bank">Gringotts Wizarding Bank</a> rather than withdrawing some from his own vault. He was arrogant, believing that sitting on a smelly cushion and did nothing but summoning objects to him was something for his peers to admire.<sup id="cite_ref-WB_1-15" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-WB-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Magical_abilities_and_skills">Magical abilities and skills</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Gideon_Flatworthy?veaction=edit&amp;section=7" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Gideon_Flatworthy?action=edit&amp;section=7" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li><b><a href="/wiki/Apparition" title="Apparition">Apparition</a></b>: Flatworthy disapparated at least twice in his life, with great success, whilst being attacked by a horde of angry <a href="/wiki/Goblin" title="Goblin">goblins</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-WB_1-16" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-WB-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Charm" title="Charm">Charms</a></b>: Flatworthy was an expert at performing the <a href="/wiki/Summoning_Charm" title="Summoning Charm">Summoning Charm</a>, having used it successfully at multiple times in his life (this was because he believed himself to be above lifting and carrying things non-<a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magically</a>). This came at the cost of his life eventually, as when he tried to lift cattle and hay with the charm, he was crushed and killed by it all when it fell upon him.<sup id="cite_ref-WB_1-17" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-WB-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup></li></ul>
