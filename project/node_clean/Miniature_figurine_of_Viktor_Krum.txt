A <b>miniature figurine</b> of <a href="/wiki/Viktor_Krum" title="Viktor Krum">Viktor Krum</a>, the <a href="/wiki/Bulgaria" title="Bulgaria">Bulgarian</a> <a href="/wiki/Seeker" title="Seeker">Seeker</a>, along with figures of other famous players, was one of many merchandising products sold at the <a href="/wiki/Quidditch_World_Cup" title="Quidditch World Cup">Quidditch World Cup</a> in <a href="/wiki/1994" title="1994">1994</a>. It was animated and could walk. <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ron Weasley</a>, a big fan of Krum, bought one.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> During the <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Express" title="Hogwarts Express">Hogwarts Express</a> ride that year, he showed it to <a href="/wiki/Neville_Longbottom" title="Neville Longbottom">Neville Longbottom</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> He later destroyed it when the real <a href="/wiki/Viktor_Krum" title="Viktor Krum">Viktor</a> went to the <a href="/wiki/Yule_Ball" title="Yule Ball">Yule Ball</a> with <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione Granger</a>, for whom he was developing intense feelings.<sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
