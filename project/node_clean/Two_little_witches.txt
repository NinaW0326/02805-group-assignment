The <b>two little <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witches</a></b> were toddler-age sisters who attended the <a href="/wiki/1994_Quidditch_World_Cup" title="1994 Quidditch World Cup">1994 Quidditch World Cup</a> with their parents.<sup id="cite_ref-GOF7_1-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF7-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Two_little_witches?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Two_little_witches?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>During the <a href="/wiki/1994_Quidditch_World_Cup" title="1994 Quidditch World Cup">1994 Quidditch World Cup</a>, <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> spotted them. They looked a little older than <a href="/wiki/Kevin" title="Kevin">Kevin</a>, who was about two, and were both flying around on <a href="/wiki/Toy_broomstick" title="Toy broomstick">toy broomsticks</a> that rose only about two feet off the ground. A <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">Ministry of Magic</a> official caught sight of them and went to warn their parents — who he assumed were still in bed — against letting children display <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magical</a> abilities in plain view. It is also possible that the girls' parents were simply unaware of the presence of <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggles</a> nearby.<sup id="cite_ref-GOF7_1-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF7-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
