The <b>wand</b> of <a href="/wiki/Susan_Bones" title="Susan Bones">Susan Bones</a>&#160;was&#160;of an unknown length, <a href="/wiki/Wand_wood" title="Wand wood">wood</a>&#160;and <a href="/wiki/Wand_core" title="Wand core">core</a> materials.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTPG_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTPG-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="History">History</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Susan_Bones%27s_wand?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: History">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Susan_Bones%27s_wand?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: History"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Like most <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witches and wizards</a>&#160;in <a href="/wiki/Great_Britain" title="Great Britain">Great Britain</a>, Susan presumably purchased her <a href="/wiki/Wand" title="Wand">wand</a>&#160;from <a href="/wiki/Ollivanders" title="Ollivanders">Ollivanders</a> in <a href="/wiki/Diagon_Alley" title="Diagon Alley">Diagon Alley</a> at the age of eleven before starting her <a href="/wiki/First_year" title="First year">first year</a> at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a>.
</p><p>She wielded her wand throughout her school life and as a member of <a href="/wiki/Dumbledore%27s_Army" title="Dumbledore&#39;s Army">Dumbledore's Army</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-OOTPG_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTPG-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> as well as during her <a href="/wiki/Ordinary_Wizarding_Level" title="Ordinary Wizarding Level">O.W.L.</a> examinations.
</p><p>In <a href="/wiki/1995" title="1995">1995</a>, Susan was confronted by two <a href="/wiki/Slytherin" title="Slytherin">Slytherin</a> bullies, <a href="/wiki/Vincent_Crabbe" title="Vincent Crabbe">Vincent Crabbe</a> and <a href="/wiki/Gregory_Goyle" title="Gregory Goyle">Gregory Goyle</a>, who insulted her family and even had the audacity to take her wand from her, rendering her unable to fight back. She was saved by the timely intervention of <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>, <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ron Weasley</a> and <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione Granger</a>. The <a href="/wiki/Trio" title="Trio">trio</a>&#160;then&#160;<a href="/wiki/Duelling" title="Duelling">duelled</a>&#160;the two and defeated them, returning Susan's wand to her.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTPG_1-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTPG-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> She also presumably wielded it in the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a> in <a href="/wiki/1998" title="1998">1998</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-DHF2_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DHF2-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
