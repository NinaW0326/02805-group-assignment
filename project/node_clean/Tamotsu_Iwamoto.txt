<b>Tamotsu Iwamoto</b> was a Japanese <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> who played as <a href="/wiki/Keeper" title="Keeper">Keeper</a> for the <a href="/wiki/Japanese_National_Quidditch_team" title="Japanese National Quidditch team">Japanese National Quidditch team</a> during the four-hundred and twenty-second <a href="/wiki/Quidditch_World_Cup" title="Quidditch World Cup">Quidditch World Cup</a>, in the year <a href="/wiki/1994" title="1994">1994</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-QWC_1-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-QWC-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>During the <a href="/wiki/1996%E2%80%931997_school_year" title="1996–1997 school year">1996–1997 school year</a>, Iwamoto's <a href="/wiki/Chocolate_Frog_Card" title="Chocolate Frog Card">Chocolate Frog Card</a> could be won by beating <a href="/wiki/Felix_Brunt_(II)" title="Felix Brunt (II)">Felix Brunt</a> at a <a href="/wiki/Gobstones" title="Gobstones">Gobstones</a> challenge.<sup id="cite_ref-HBPG_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBPG-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
