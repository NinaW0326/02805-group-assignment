<b>Fay Dunbar</b> (b. c. <a href="/wiki/1978" title="1978">1978</a>/<a href="/wiki/1979" title="1979">1979</a>) was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch</a> and <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor" title="Gryffindor">Gryffindor</a> student who aspired to become a <a href="/wiki/Auror" title="Auror">member</a> of the <a href="/wiki/Auror_Office" title="Auror Office">Auror Office</a>. She attended <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> in the <a href="/wiki/1990s" title="1990s">1990s</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-HBPG_4-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBPG-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Fay hoped to become an <a href="/wiki/Auror" title="Auror">Auror</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-HBPG_4-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBPG-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> It is unknown if she fulfilled her dream after graduating from Hogwarts.
</p>

<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_life">Early life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Fay_Dunbar?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Early life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Fay_Dunbar?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Early life"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Fay Dunbar was born in late <a href="/wiki/1979" title="1979">1979</a> or early-to-mid <a href="/wiki/1980" title="1980">1980</a>, presumably into a <a href="/wiki/Pure-blood" title="Pure-blood">pure-blood</a> or <a href="/wiki/Half-blood" title="Half-blood">half-blood</a> family, as she seemed unfamiliar with a <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggle</a> hairdryer.<sup id="cite_ref-Blood_2-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Blood-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> She was also a fan of <a href="/wiki/Gobstones" title="Gobstones">Gobstones</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-HBPG_4-10" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBPG-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>At the age of eleven, Fay was accepted into <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> in <a href="/wiki/Scotland" title="Scotland">Scotland</a>.
</p>

<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Fifth_year">Fifth year</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Fay_Dunbar?veaction=edit&amp;section=4" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Fifth year">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Fay_Dunbar?action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Fifth year"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<p>In her <a href="/wiki/Fifth_year" title="Fifth year">fifth year</a>, Fay sat her <a href="/wiki/Ordinary_Wizarding_Level" title="Ordinary Wizarding Level">Ordinary Wizarding Levels</a>. By this time, she had decided she wished to be a <a href="/wiki/Auror" title="Auror">member</a> of the <a href="/wiki/Auror_Office" title="Auror Office">Auror Office</a> and be a <a href="/wiki/Dark_Wizard" title="Dark Wizard">Dark Wizard</a>-catcher. She achieved an <a href="/wiki/Exceeds_Expectations" title="Exceeds Expectations">Exceeds Expectations</a> or <a href="/wiki/Outstanding" title="Outstanding">Outstanding</a> in <a href="/wiki/Transfiguration_(class)" title="Transfiguration (class)">Transfiguration</a>, good marks in <a href="/wiki/Herbology" title="Herbology">Herbology</a>, <a href="/wiki/Defence_Against_the_Dark_Arts" title="Defence Against the Dark Arts">Defence Against the Dark Arts</a> and <a href="/wiki/Charms_(class)" title="Charms (class)">Charms</a> and an <a href="/wiki/Outstanding" title="Outstanding">Outstanding</a> in <a href="/wiki/Potions_(class)" title="Potions (class)">Potions</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-HBPG_4-11" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBPG-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<p>In her <a href="/wiki/Sixth_year" title="Sixth year">sixth year</a> or <a href="/wiki/Seventh_year" title="Seventh year">seventh year</a> in <a href="/wiki/1996" title="1996">1996</a>, Fay studied hard for her <a href="/wiki/Nastily_Exhausting_Wizarding_Test" title="Nastily Exhausting Wizarding Test">Nastily Exhausting Wizarding Tests</a>, still wanting to become an Auror. This year, she could often be found in the <a href="/wiki/Middle_Courtyard" title="Middle Courtyard">Middle Courtyard</a>, near the <a href="/wiki/Transfiguration_department" title="Transfiguration department">Transfiguration department</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-HBPG_4-12" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBPG-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Fay_Dunbar?veaction=edit&amp;section=8" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Fay_Dunbar?action=edit&amp;section=8" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Derived from Middle English faie meaning "<a href="/wiki/Fairy" title="Fairy">fairy</a>". It appears in Geoffrey of Monmouth's Arthurian legends in the name of <a href="/wiki/Morgan_le_Fay" title="Morgan le Fay">Morgan le Fay</a>. It has been used as a given name since the 19th century. In some cases it may be used as a short form of Faith,<sup id="cite_ref-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> or another spelling for the mythological Irish creature; the Fae.
</p><p>Dunbar means "castle headland" and comes from the old barony of Dunbar, now in East Lothian in Scotland. The place name Dunbar itself comes from the Gaelic dun meaning "fort" and barr meaning "summit". Locality from the town of Dunbar, at the mouth of the Frith of Forth, Scotland, so called from its situation on the rock which at this place projects into the sea.<sup id="cite_ref-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Fay_Dunbar?veaction=edit&amp;section=9" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Fay_Dunbar?action=edit&amp;section=9" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>Fay only appears in the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Prisoner_of_Azkaban_(video_game)" title="Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban (video game)">third</a> and <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Half-Blood_Prince_(video_game)" title="Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince (video game)">sixth</a> video games. She does not appear in either the books or films.</li>
<li>If she survived the Battle of Hogwarts, it is likely that she would, despite her academical shortcomings in potions, have gone on to join the Ministry of Magic alongside her peers, Harry Potter, Ron Weasley and Neville Longbottom, seen as fighting in and surviving such a battle would have meant she met the requirements to join the <a href="/wiki/Auror_Office" title="Auror Office">Auror Office</a> and students who did were offered a place there as a means of replenishing its numbers, as most of the Ministry's Aurors were killed during the Second Wizarding War.</li></ul>
