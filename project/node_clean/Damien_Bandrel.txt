</p><p><b>Damien Bandrel</b>, also known by the nickname <b>Black Scorpion</b>, was an <a href="/wiki/United_States_of_America" title="United States of America">American</a> <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizarding</a> criminal who operated in the <a href="/wiki/1920s" title="1920s">1920s</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-globalfaneventvid_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-globalfaneventvid-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>In or around <a href="/wiki/1926" title="1926">1926</a>, the <a href="/wiki/Magical_Congress_of_the_United_States_of_America" title="Magical Congress of the United States of America">Magical Congress of the United States of America</a> issued a wanted poster for Bandrel, which stated that he was wanted for forging <a href="/wiki/Potion" title="Potion">potions</a> and <a href="/wiki/Spell" title="Spell">spells</a> and offered a 2500 <a href="/wiki/Dragot" title="Dragot">dragot</a> reward for his arrest.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
