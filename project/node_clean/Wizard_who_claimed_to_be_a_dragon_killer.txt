<p>This <b>individual</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> who attended the <a href="/wiki/1994" title="1994">1994</a> <a href="/wiki/Quidditch_World_Cup" title="Quidditch World Cup">Quidditch World Cup</a> between <a href="/wiki/Bulgaria" title="Bulgaria">Bulgaria</a> and <a href="/wiki/Ireland" title="Ireland">Ireland</a>. He claimed that he was a <a href="/wiki/Dragon_killer" title="Dragon killer">dragon killer</a> in an attempt to impress a group of <a href="/wiki/Veela" title="Veela">Veela</a>, but <a href="/wiki/Wizard_who_claimed_to_be_a_Vampire_Hunter" title="Wizard who claimed to be a Vampire Hunter">his friend</a> stated that he was actually a dishwasher at the <a href="/wiki/Leaky_Cauldron" title="Leaky Cauldron">Leaky Cauldron</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-GOF9_1-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF9-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> It is extremely unlikely that the "dragon killer"'s claim was true.
</p>
