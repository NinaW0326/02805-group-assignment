<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Montgomery?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Montgomery?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Born in c. <a href="/wiki/1992" title="1992">1992</a> to <a href="/wiki/Montgomery_sisters%27_mother" class="mw-redirect" title="Montgomery sisters&#39; mother">a pure-blood or half-blood witch</a> somewhere in <a href="/wiki/Ireland" title="Ireland">Ireland</a> or <a href="/wiki/Great_Britain" title="Great Britain">Great Britain</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-HOME_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HOME-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>, Montgomery had at least <a href="/wiki/Montgomery_sisters" title="Montgomery sisters">two sisters</a> who began attending <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> sometime from <a href="/wiki/1990" title="1990">1990</a> to <a href="/wiki/1996" title="1996">1996</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-SISTERS_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-SISTERS-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>When he was five years old, his <a href="/wiki/Montgomery_sisters%27_mother" class="mw-redirect" title="Montgomery sisters&#39; mother">mother</a> supposedly refused to aid the <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a> in their <a href="/wiki/Pure-blood_supremacy" title="Pure-blood supremacy">cause</a> and hence they ordered the <a href="/wiki/Werewolf" title="Werewolf">werewolf</a> <a href="/wiki/Fenrir_Greyback" title="Fenrir Greyback">Fenrir Greyback</a> to attack the little boy.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP22_4-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP22-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> Although he was taken to <a href="/wiki/St_Mungo%27s_Hospital_for_Magical_Maladies_and_Injuries" title="St Mungo&#39;s Hospital for Magical Maladies and Injuries">St Mungo's Hospital for Magical Maladies and Injuries</a>, the <a href="/wiki/Healer" title="Healer">healers</a> there could not save him and he <a href="/wiki/Death" title="Death">died</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP22_4-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP22-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
