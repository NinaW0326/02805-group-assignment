<b>Mr Trelawney</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-PM_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PM-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> He was the great-grandson of the famous <a href="/wiki/Seer" title="Seer">Seer</a> <a href="/wiki/Cassandra_Trelawney" title="Cassandra Trelawney">Cassandra Trelawney</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-PM_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PM-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> However, along with the two generations of <a href="/wiki/Trelawney_family" title="Trelawney family">Trelawneys</a> preceding him, he did not possess the gift of <a href="/wiki/Divination" title="Divination">Second Sight</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-ootp15_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-ootp15-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> He married a <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggle</a> <a href="/wiki/Sybill_Trelawney%27s_mother" title="Sybill Trelawney&#39;s mother">woman</a>, and together they had a daughter, <a href="/wiki/Sybill_Trelawney" title="Sybill Trelawney">Sybill</a>, who turned out to have limited Sight.<sup id="cite_ref-PM_1-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PM-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
