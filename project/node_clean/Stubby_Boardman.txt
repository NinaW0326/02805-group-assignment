<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Stubby_Boardman?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Stubby_Boardman?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>He retired in <a href="/wiki/1980" title="1980">1980</a> after having a turnip thrown at his ear during a concert in <a href="/wiki/Little_Norton" title="Little Norton">Little Norton</a> at the <a href="/wiki/Little_Norton_Church_Hall" title="Little Norton Church Hall">Church Hall</a>. He and <a href="/wiki/Doris_Purkiss" title="Doris Purkiss">Doris Purkiss</a> appeared to be in a relationship.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP10_1-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP10-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>It was said by <i><a href="/wiki/The_Quibbler" title="The Quibbler">The Quibbler</a></i> in <a href="/wiki/1995" title="1995">1995</a> that he was in fact <a href="/wiki/Sirius_Black" title="Sirius Black">Sirius Black</a> in disguise, based on the word of Purkiss, who claimed that she was having a romantic dinner with him on the night he supposedly <a href="/wiki/Massacre_in_London" title="Massacre in London">murdered</a> <a href="/wiki/Peter_Pettigrew" title="Peter Pettigrew">Peter Pettigrew</a> and twelve <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggles</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP10_1-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP10-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> In <a href="/wiki/1996" title="1996">1996</a>, <i><a href="/wiki/The_Quibbler" title="The Quibbler">The Quibbler</a></i> claimed this person was <a href="/wiki/Mina_Lima" title="Mina Lima">Mina Lima</a> in disguise.<sup id="cite_ref-HBPF_2-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBPF-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> Both claims were false.
</p>
