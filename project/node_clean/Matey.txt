<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="History">History</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Matey?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: History">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Matey?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: History"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>On <a href="/wiki/5_October" title="5 October">5 October</a>, <a href="/wiki/1995" title="1995">1995</a>, when <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>, <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ron Weasley</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione Granger</a> walked through the front door of Aberforth's pub in <a href="/wiki/Hogsmeade" title="Hogsmeade">Hogsmeade</a>, the <a href="/wiki/Hog%27s_Head" class="mw-redirect" title="Hog&#39;s Head">Hog's Head</a>, where they had scheduled the first meeting of what was to become <a href="/wiki/Dumbledore%27s_Army" title="Dumbledore&#39;s Army">Dumbledore's Army</a>, Matey darted out from behind the bar counter suddenly. Aberforth followed Matey into another room, calling out, "<i>Matey, come back here.</i>".<sup id="cite_ref-OOTPF_1-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTPF-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
