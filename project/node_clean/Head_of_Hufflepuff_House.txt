The <b>Head of Hufflepuff</b> house is a professor at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> with the special obligation of looking after the students of their <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Houses" title="Hogwarts Houses">house</a>: <a href="/wiki/Hufflepuff" title="Hufflepuff">Hufflepuff</a>. It is unknown how a professor would be elected for the post, but it's most likely that the headmaster chooses the professor whom embodies all the qualities <a href="/wiki/Helga_Hufflepuff" title="Helga Hufflepuff">Helga Hufflepuff</a> herself had (loyalty, kindness, generosity, etc.) and probably was in Hufflepuff House when he/she was in Hogwarts.<sup id="cite_ref-PS7_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PS7-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-OOTP11_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP11-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<p>The following are special duties that were performed by the Head of Hufflepuff in the past.
</p>
<ul><li><a href="/wiki/1992%E2%80%931993_school_year" title="1992–1993 school year">1992–1993 school year</a>: to inform the <a href="/wiki/Hufflepuff" title="Hufflepuff">Hufflepuff</a> students of new rules regarding the opening of the <a href="/wiki/Chamber_of_Secrets" title="Chamber of Secrets">Chamber of Secrets</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-COS14_5-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COS14-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1993%E2%80%931994_school_year" title="1993–1994 school year">1993–1994 school year</a>: to notify their students of the rules in regards to protecting them from <a href="/wiki/Azkaban" title="Azkaban">Azkaban</a> escapee, <a href="/wiki/Sirius_Black" title="Sirius Black">Sirius Black</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-POA14_6-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-POA14-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1997%E2%80%931998_school_year" title="1997–1998 school year">1997–1998 school year</a>: to round up their students prior to the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-DH30_7-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH30-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup></li></ul>
