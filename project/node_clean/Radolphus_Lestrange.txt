<b>Minister Radolphus Lestrange</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> who, from <a href="/wiki/1835" class="mw-redirect" title="1835">1835</a> to <a href="/wiki/1841" class="mw-redirect" title="1841">1841</a>, served as <a href="/wiki/Minister_for_Magic" title="Minister for Magic">Minister for Magic</a> of <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">Britain</a> and <a href="/wiki/Ireland" title="Ireland">Ireland</a>, being elected at least once.<sup id="cite_ref-WW_1-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-WW-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Radolphus_Lestrange?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Radolphus_Lestrange?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>He unsuccessfully attempted to close down the <a href="/wiki/Department_of_Mysteries" title="Department of Mysteries">Department of Mysteries</a>, and eventually resigned due to ill health, which was rumoured to be an inability to cope with stress of the office.<sup id="cite_ref-WW_1-10" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-WW-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Radolphus_Lestrange?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Radolphus_Lestrange?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>The name Radolphus seems to be a variation of "Radulf", which is itself derived from the Norse elements ráð meaning "counsel" and úlfr meaning "wolf".</li></ul>
