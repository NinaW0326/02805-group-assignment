<b>Falco Aesalon</b> was an Ancient <a href="/wiki/Greece" title="Greece">Greek</a> <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a>. Being able to transform into a <a href="/wiki/Falcon" title="Falcon">falcon</a> at will, he was the first recorded <a href="/wiki/Animagus" title="Animagus">Animagus</a>. For this spectacular <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magical</a> feat, he was placed on a <a href="/wiki/Chocolate_Frog_Card" title="Chocolate Frog Card">Chocolate Frog Card</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Magical_abilities_and_skills">Magical abilities and skills</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Falco_Aesalon?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Falco_Aesalon?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li><b><a href="/wiki/Transfiguration" title="Transfiguration">Transfiguration</a></b>: Falco mastered the art of <a href="/wiki/Human_Transfiguration" title="Human Transfiguration">Self-Transfiguration</a> and became an <a href="/wiki/Animagus" title="Animagus">Animagus</a>, showing him to have been exceptionally gifted in this highly difficult and complex branch of <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magic</a>, given the immense skill and dedication required to successfully become an Animagus.</li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Potion" title="Potion">Potions</a></b>: Having become an Animagus, he would have needed to have successfully brewed the <a href="/wiki/Animagus_potion" title="Animagus potion">Animagus potion</a>, a potion known for being highly advanced, complex and time-consuming, showing his great level of skill in potion-making.</li></ul>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Falco_Aesalon?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Falco_Aesalon?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p><i>Falco Aesalon</i> is the former scientific name of the <a href="/wiki/Europe" title="Europe">European</a> Merlin, before it and the American Merlin were reclassified as the same species.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Falco_Aesalon?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Falco_Aesalon?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>Its unknown whether Falco was the first wizard to discover the process of becoming an Animagus, or if other Animagi that were not recorded in magical history did it before him.</li></ul>
