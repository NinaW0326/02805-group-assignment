The <b>Leprechaun</b>, sometimes known as the <b>Clauricorn</b>, is a mischievous <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magical</a> <a href="/wiki/Beast" title="Beast">beast</a>, although it is not malicious. They have a reputation as pranksters, but have never inflicted lasting damage on a <a href="/wiki/Human" title="Human">Human</a>. Native only to <a href="/wiki/Ireland" title="Ireland">Ireland</a>, they are fully sentient and capable of speech but have never requested the <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">British Ministry of Magic</a> to reclassify them as <a href="/wiki/Being" title="Being">Beings</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-FBR_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-FBR-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Physical_appearance">Physical appearance</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Leprechaun?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Physical appearance">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Leprechaun?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Physical appearance"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Green in colour, the Leprechaun reaches a height of around six inches, and normally wear clothing made out of leaves. Leprechauns main diet consists of vegetation, primarily leaves.<sup id="cite_ref-FBR_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-FBR-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Although they are humanoid-like creatures, it is unlikely that they are related to <a href="/wiki/Human" title="Human">humans</a> or any other human-related <a href="/wiki/Being" title="Being">beings</a>, such as <a href="/wiki/Goblin" title="Goblin">goblins</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-FBR_1-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-FBR-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Habitat_and_traits">Habitat and traits</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Leprechaun?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Habitat and traits">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Leprechaun?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Habitat and traits"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Leprechauns live in forests and wooded areas, and they enjoy attracting <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggle</a> attention to themselves. This tendency has led to them being heavily integrated into Muggle children's stories and legends. Leprechauns have the ability to create a gold-like substance that disappears, much to their amusement, after a few hours. Much of this <a href="/wiki/Leprechaun_gold" title="Leprechaun gold">Leprechaun gold</a> is created in the form of <a href="/wiki/Wizarding_currency" title="Wizarding currency">Galleon</a> coins.<sup id="cite_ref-FBR_1-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-FBR-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Leprechaun?veaction=edit&amp;section=4" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Leprechaun?action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>The <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/leprechaun" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:leprechaun">leprechaun</a> is a <a href="/wiki/Fairy" title="Fairy">fairy</a>-like <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magical</a> <a href="/wiki/Creature" title="Creature">creature</a> from <a href="/wiki/Ireland" title="Ireland">Irish</a> mythology. They are generally depicted as tiny old men with red hair and beards, clad in green, who make shoes, hide coins in pots of gold hidden at the ends of rainbows, and cause mischief. "Clauricorn" is presumably derived from <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/clurichaun" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:clurichaun">clurichaun</a>, an evil variety of leprechaun associated with drunkards.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Warwick_Davis" title="Warwick Davis">Warwick Davis</a>, the actor who portrayed Professor <a href="/wiki/Filius_Flitwick" title="Filius Flitwick">Filius Flitwick</a> and <a href="/wiki/Griphook" title="Griphook">Griphook</a> the <a href="/wiki/Goblin" title="Goblin">goblin</a>, has also starred as an evil leprechaun in the <i>Leprechaun</i> comedy horror film series.</li></ul>
