<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Puddifoot?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Puddifoot?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<figure class="thumb tleft show-info-icon" style="width: 182px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/d/d8/MadamPuddifoot.png/revision/latest?cb=20151229180943" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/d/d8/MadamPuddifoot.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/180?cb=20151229180943" decoding="async" width="180" height="298" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="MadamPuddifoot.png" data-image-key="MadamPuddifoot.png" /></a> 	 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:MadamPuddifoot.png" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption">Madam Puddifoot working in her shop</p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure>
<p>In the <a href="/wiki/1980s" title="1980s">1980s</a>, Puddifoot was introduced to <a href="/wiki/Jacob%27s_sibling" title="Jacob&#39;s sibling">Jacob's sibling</a> by <a href="/wiki/William_Weasley" title="William Weasley">Bill Weasley</a> and <a href="/wiki/Nymphadora_Tonks" title="Nymphadora Tonks">Nymphadora Tonks</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-HM_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HM-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>In 1996, <a href="/wiki/Valentine%27s_day" class="mw-redirect" title="Valentine&#39;s day">Valentine's day</a>, Puddifoot decorated her shop by placing several golden <a href="/wiki/Cherub" title="Cherub">cherubs</a> around and had them throw confetti over visiting couples every so often. She served coffee to the couple <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> and <a href="/wiki/Cho_Chang" title="Cho Chang">Cho Chang</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP25_1-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP25-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>By at least the <a href="/wiki/2010s" title="2010s">2010s</a>, during the <a href="/wiki/Calamity" title="Calamity">Calamity</a>, volunteer members of the <a href="/wiki/Statute_of_Secrecy_Task_Force" title="Statute of Secrecy Task Force">Statute of Secrecy Task Force</a> met with Madam Puddifoot to try to convince her to join the SOS Task Force officially. She enthusiastically agreed and informed all staff to do whatever necessary to help support the <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">Ministry</a>'s efforts.<sup id="cite_ref-WU_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-WU-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Physical_appearance">Physical appearance</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Puddifoot?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Physical appearance">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Puddifoot?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Physical appearance"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Puddifoot was a very stout woman with brown eyes and a shiny black bun.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP25_1-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP25-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Puddifoot?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Puddifoot?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>In <i><a href="/wiki/LEGO_Harry_Potter:_Years_5-7" title="LEGO Harry Potter: Years 5-7">LEGO Harry Potter: Years 5-7</a></i>, Madam Puddifoot is depicted with blonde hair rather than black. She is not playable under normal circumstances, but may be temporarily controlled with the <a href="/wiki/Imperius_Curse" title="Imperius Curse">Imperius Curse</a> in the level "Dumbledore's Army" in order to collect the <a href="/wiki/Slytherin" title="Slytherin">Slytherin House</a> Crest for that level.<sup id="cite_ref-LEGOy2_4-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-LEGOy2-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li>It is possible she, alongside her fellow <a href="/wiki/Hogsmeade" title="Hogsmeade">Hogsmeade</a> residents, participated in the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a>, as <a href="/wiki/Horace_Slughorn" title="Horace Slughorn">Horace Slughorn</a> was recruiting them and <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a> declared a final stand for the entire <a href="/wiki/Second_Wizarding_War" title="Second Wizarding War">Second Wizarding War</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-DH36_5-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH36-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup></li></ul>
