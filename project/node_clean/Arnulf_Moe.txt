</p><p><b>Arnulf Moe</b> was a Norwegian wizard and the manager of the <a href="/wiki/Norwegian_National_Quidditch_team" title="Norwegian National Quidditch team">Norwegian National Quidditch team</a> in <a href="/wiki/2014" title="2014">2014</a>. It was his decision to change the team mascot from performing <a href="/wiki/Troll" title="Troll">trolls</a> to the <a href="/wiki/Selma" title="Selma">Selma</a>. When questioned about the change after conflict the Selma had with the <a href="/wiki/Fiji" title="Fiji">Fijian</a> <a href="/wiki/Dukuwaqa" title="Dukuwaqa">Dukuwaqa</a>, Moe defended his choice, claiming that the Selma represented the "steely determination and ferocity of the Norwegian players".<sup id="cite_ref-PM_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PM-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
