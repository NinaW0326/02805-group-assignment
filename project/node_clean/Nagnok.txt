<b>Nagnok</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Gringotts_Wizarding_Bank" title="Gringotts Wizarding Bank">Gringotts</a> <a href="/wiki/Goblin" title="Goblin">goblin</a> who was accidentally killed by an untrained <a href="/wiki/Security_Troll" class="mw-redirect" title="Security Troll">Security Troll</a> sent by the <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">Ministry of Magic</a>, sometime in or before the <a href="/wiki/18th_century" title="18th century">18th century</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-WOMBAT_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-WOMBAT-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> His accidental death was a catalyst to one of many <a href="/wiki/Goblin_Rebellions" title="Goblin Rebellions">Goblin Rebellions</a> in the <a href="/wiki/17th_century" title="17th century">17th</a> and <a href="/wiki/18th_century" title="18th century">18th centuries</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
