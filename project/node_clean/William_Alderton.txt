<p><b>William Alderton</b><sup id="cite_ref-DHpt1_script_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DHpt1_script-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> was allegedly the father of <a href="/wiki/William_Alderton%27s_alleged_son" class="mw-redirect" title="William Alderton&#39;s alleged son">Alderton</a>, a wizard who was tried for being a Muggle-born by the <a href="/wiki/Muggle-Born_Registration_Commission" title="Muggle-Born Registration Commission">Muggle-Born Registration Commission</a> during the height of the <a href="/wiki/Second_Wizarding_War" title="Second Wizarding War">Second Wizarding War</a>. Because the commission found Alderton guilty of this, it is likely that he was a <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggle</a>. Despite this, his son claimed on leaving his trial that his father was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a>. He also claimed that he once worked for the <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">British Ministry of Magic</a> for thirty years. Apparently, William Alderton always wore his jacket inside-out, while working for the Ministry of Magic<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
