<p><b>Anthony Goldstein</b> (b. c. <a href="/wiki/1980" title="1980">1980</a>) was a <a href="/wiki/Half-blood" title="Half-blood">half-blood</a><sup id="cite_ref-blood_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-blood-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> who began attending <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> in <a href="/wiki/1991" title="1991">1991</a>. He was Sorted into <a href="/wiki/Ravenclaw" title="Ravenclaw">Ravenclaw</a> house. In his fifth year he was selected as one of his House's <a href="/wiki/Prefect" title="Prefect">prefects</a><sup id="cite_ref-Prefect_3-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Prefect-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> and joined <a href="/wiki/Dumbledore%27s_Army" title="Dumbledore&#39;s Army">Dumbledore's Army</a>, an organisation taught and lead by <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-DA_9-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DA-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup> Anthony later fought beside fellow D.A. members in the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a> the last battle of the <a href="/wiki/Second_Wizarding_War" title="Second Wizarding War">Second Wizarding War</a>.
</p>

<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_life">Early life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Anthony_Goldstein?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Early life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Anthony_Goldstein?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Early life"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Anthony was born into the <a href="/wiki/Goldstein_family" title="Goldstein family">Goldstein family</a>. He had at least one magical parent<sup id="cite_ref-blood_1-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-blood-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> and was distantly related to <a href="/wiki/Porpentina_Goldstein" title="Porpentina Goldstein">Porpentina</a> and <a href="/wiki/Queenie_Goldstein" title="Queenie Goldstein">Queenie Goldstein</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-JKR-twitter-goldstein_5-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-JKR-twitter-goldstein-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> He was also <a href="/wiki/Judaism" title="Judaism">Jewish</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-Jew_10-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Jew-10">&#91;10&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Hogwarts_years">Hogwarts years</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Anthony_Goldstein?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Hogwarts years">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Anthony_Goldstein?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Hogwarts years"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>

<p>In <a href="/wiki/1991" title="1991">1991</a>, Anthony received his <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_acceptance_letter" title="Hogwarts acceptance letter">acceptance letter</a> and began attending <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a>, being <a href="/wiki/Sorting_ceremony" title="Sorting ceremony">Sorted</a> into <a href="/wiki/Ravenclaw" title="Ravenclaw">Ravenclaw</a>. He befriended dorm-mates <a href="/wiki/Terry_Boot" title="Terry Boot">Terry Boot</a> and <a href="/wiki/Michael_Corner" title="Michael Corner">Michael Corner</a>, and was also on speaking terms with <a href="/wiki/Ernest_Macmillan" title="Ernest Macmillan">Ernest Macmillan</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-OPg_11-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OPg-11">&#91;11&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<p>In his fifth year, Anthony was made one of <a href="/wiki/Ravenclaw" title="Ravenclaw">Ravenclaw</a>'s new <a href="/wiki/Prefect" title="Prefect">prefects</a>, alongside <a href="/wiki/Padma_Patil" title="Padma Patil">Padma Patil</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-Prefect_3-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Prefect-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> He, Michael, and Terry attended the meeting in the <a href="/wiki/Hog%27s_Head" class="mw-redirect" title="Hog&#39;s Head">Hog's Head</a> to discuss learning defensive and offensive magic from <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>. Anthony vocally agreed with Hermione Granger that <a href="/wiki/Dolores_Umbridge" title="Dolores Umbridge">Dolores Umbridge</a>'s Defence Against the Dark Arts classes were useless, as they were only being taught the theory, which is not enough.<sup id="cite_ref-OP16_4-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OP16-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> He signed the <a href="/wiki/Dumbledore%27s_Army" title="Dumbledore&#39;s Army">Dumbledore's Army</a> parchment and joined the group.<sup id="cite_ref-OP18_12-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OP18-12">&#91;12&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<p>Hermione, Harry Potter, and <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ron Weasley</a> met Anthony outside of the hospital wing and told him of the location of the D.A. meetings — in the <a href="/wiki/Room_of_Requirement" title="Room of Requirement">Room of Requirement</a> — and how to access it.<sup id="cite_ref-OPg_11-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OPg-11">&#91;11&#93;</a></sup> He was paired with <a href="/wiki/Zacharias_Smith" title="Zacharias Smith">Zacharias Smith</a> in one such meeting to practise the <a href="/wiki/Disarming_Charm" title="Disarming Charm">Disarming Charm</a>. However, whenever Zacharias opened his mouth to cast the spell and disarm Anthony, <a href="/wiki/Fred_Weasley" title="Fred Weasley">Fred</a> and <a href="/wiki/George_Weasley" title="George Weasley">George Weasley</a> would slyly disarm him.<sup id="cite_ref-OP18_12-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OP18-12">&#91;12&#93;</a></sup>

</p><p>On the <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Express" title="Hogwarts Express">Hogwarts Express</a> after the school year's conclusion, Anthony sat in a compartment with Terry, Michael, <a href="/wiki/Hannah_Abbott" title="Hannah Abbott">Hannah Abbott</a>, <a href="/wiki/Susan_Bones" title="Susan Bones">Susan Bones</a>, <a href="/wiki/Justin_Finch-Fletchley" title="Justin Finch-Fletchley">Justin Finch-Fletchley</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Ernest_Macmillan" title="Ernest Macmillan">Ernie Macmillan</a>. <p>Anthony was part of the revived DA during the <a href="/wiki/1997-1998_school_year" class="mw-redirect" title="1997-1998 school year">1997-1998 school year</a>, hiding in the <a href="/wiki/Room_of_Requirement" title="Room of Requirement">Room of Requirement</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-DH29_15-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH29-15">&#91;15&#93;</a></sup> He bravely fought in the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a>. <h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Personality_and_traits">Personality and traits</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Anthony_Goldstein?veaction=edit&amp;section=6" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Personality and traits">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Anthony_Goldstein?action=edit&amp;section=6" title="Edit section: Personality and traits"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Anthony was a very friendly and good-natured individual,<sup id="cite_ref-OPg_11-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OPg-11">&#91;11&#93;</a></sup> who was willing to disobey and risk being punished by <a href="/wiki/Dolores_Umbridge" title="Dolores Umbridge">Dolores Umbridge</a> in opposition to her tyranny over <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts</a>, as a devoted member of <a href="/wiki/Dumbledore%27s_Army" title="Dumbledore&#39;s Army">Dumbledore's Army</a>. He was also very courageous and determined, as he bravely fought against the <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a> along with most of his friends and fellow students to defend his school.<sup id="cite_ref-DH29_15-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH29-15">&#91;15&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Magical_skills_and_abilities">Magical skills and abilities</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Anthony_Goldstein?veaction=edit&amp;section=7" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Magical skills and abilities">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Anthony_Goldstein?action=edit&amp;section=7" title="Edit section: Magical skills and abilities"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li><b><a href="/wiki/Defence_Against_the_Dark_Arts" title="Defence Against the Dark Arts">Defence Against the Dark Arts</a></b>: As a committed and loyal member of <a href="/wiki/Dumbledore%27s_Army" title="Dumbledore&#39;s Army">Dumbledore's Army</a>, Anthony would have learnt how to cast multiple highly practical defensive and offensive <a href="/wiki/Spell" title="Spell">spells</a>, under the masterful teaching of <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-OP18_12-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OP18-12">&#91;12&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-OP38_14-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OP38-14">&#91;14&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Duelling" title="Duelling">Duelling</a></b>: Anthony was shown to be a skilled duellist with a high level talent in <a href="/wiki/Martial_magic" title="Martial magic">martial magic</a>. Anthony and his friends at the end of the <a href="/wiki/1995-1996_school_year" class="mw-redirect" title="1995-1996 school year">1995-1996 school year</a> were able to successfully duel and defeat <a href="/wiki/Draco_Malfoy" title="Draco Malfoy">Draco Malfoy</a>, <a href="/wiki/Vincent_Crabbe" title="Vincent Crabbe">Crabbe</a> and <a href="/wiki/Gregory_Goyle" title="Gregory Goyle">Goyle</a> after they tried to attack Harry Potter on the <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Express" title="Hogwarts Express">Hogwarts Express</a>. They were able to successfully cast various unpleasant offensive spells on them, that they at the end of the duel resembled <a href="/wiki/Slug" title="Slug">slugs</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-OP38_14-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OP38-14">&#91;14&#93;</a></sup> Furthermore, Anthony bravely fought in the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a> in <a href="/wiki/1998" title="1998">1998</a>, along his D.A. comrades against the <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a> and presumably survived.</li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Charm" title="Charm">Charms</a></b>: He was capable of producing a non-corporeal <a href="/wiki/Patronus_Charm" title="Patronus Charm">Patronus</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-LEGOY2_7-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-LEGOY2-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup> a mark of superior <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magical</a> ability and talent, given the immense difficulty of casting such an advanced and powerful protective charm. He presumably learnt how to cast this charm with the help of Dumbledore's Army's Patronus lesson.</li></ul>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Anthony_Goldstein?veaction=edit&amp;section=8" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Anthony_Goldstein?action=edit&amp;section=8" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Anthony was an <a href="/wiki/Egypt" title="Egypt">Egyptian</a> ascetic monk considered to be the founder of Christian monasticism. Goldstein is a relatively common Jewish surname, which is a part of his Jewish heritage.<sup id="cite_ref-Jew_10-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Jew-10">&#91;10&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Anthony_Goldstein?veaction=edit&amp;section=9" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Anthony_Goldstein?action=edit&amp;section=9" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>Anthony was voiced by <a href="/wiki/Tom_Wheatley" title="Tom Wheatley">Tom Wheatley</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Order_of_the_Phoenix_(video_game)" title="Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix (video game)">video game adaptation</a> of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Order_of_the_Phoenix" title="Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix">Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix</a></i>.</li>
<li>In <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Order_of_the_Phoenix_(video_game)" title="Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix (video game)">Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix (video game)</a></i>, Anthony was portrayed as light skinned with blond hair and blue eyes. He was slightly shorter than <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-OPg_11-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OPg-11">&#91;11&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li>From the student list seen in <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_Me" title="Harry Potter and Me">Harry Potter and Me</a></i>, it can be gleaned that Anthony was originally intended to be a <a href="/wiki/Hufflepuff" title="Hufflepuff">Hufflepuff</a>.</li>
<li>Anthony was the first character to be identified as Jewish, the information being revealed by <a href="/wiki/J._K._Rowling" title="J. K. Rowling">J. K. Rowling</a> on Twitter as an example of a Jewish person attending <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-Jew_10-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Jew-10">&#91;10&#93;</a></sup></li></ul>
