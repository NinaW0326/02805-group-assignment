<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Violetta_Bulstrode?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Violetta_Bulstrode?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p><i><a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Violetta" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Violetta">Violetta</a></i> is a version of the name Violet, derived from the flower. It was the stage name of a German-born circus performer born without legs or arms who was well-known during the 1920s. The name Violetta could also be meant to mean violent or violence.
</p><p><i>Bulstrode </i>is an English surname that can refer to the the medival village of Bulstrode in Berkshire. It means either "the bull's marsh", or "the fort on the marsh". It could refer to a violent attitude.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Violetta_Bulstrode?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Violetta_Bulstrode?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>Violetta is the great-grandmother of <a href="/wiki/Narcissa_Malfoy" title="Narcissa Malfoy">Narcissa Malfoy</a>, <a href="/wiki/Bellatrix_Lestrange" title="Bellatrix Lestrange">Bellatrix Lestrange</a>, <a href="/wiki/Sirius_Black" title="Sirius Black">Sirius Black</a>, <a href="/wiki/Andromeda_Tonks" title="Andromeda Tonks">Andromeda Tonks</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Regulus_Black" title="Regulus Black">Regulus Black</a>, the great-great-grandmother of <a href="/wiki/Nymphadora_Tonks" title="Nymphadora Tonks">Nymphadora Lupin</a> and <a href="/wiki/Draco_Malfoy" title="Draco Malfoy">Draco Malfoy</a>, and the great-great-great-grandmother of <a href="/wiki/Edward_Lupin" title="Edward Lupin">Teddy Lupin</a> and <a href="/wiki/Scorpius_Malfoy" title="Scorpius Malfoy">Scorpius Malfoy</a>.</li>
<li>Before the reveal that <a href="/wiki/Fleamont_Potter" title="Fleamont Potter">Fleamont</a> and <a href="/wiki/Euphemia_Potter" title="Euphemia Potter">Euphemia Potter</a> were the parents of <a href="/wiki/James_Potter_I" title="James Potter I">James</a>, it was speculated that Violetta's daughter <a href="/wiki/Dorea_Black" title="Dorea Black">Dorea</a> and her husband <a href="/wiki/Charlus_Potter" title="Charlus Potter">Charlus</a> were his parents and thus the grandparents of <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>. Therefore, Violetta was the presumed grandmother and great-grandmother of James and Harry, respectively.</li></ul>
