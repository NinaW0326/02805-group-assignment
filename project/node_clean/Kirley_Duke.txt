<b>Kirley Duke McCormack</b> (b. <a href="/wiki/1971" title="1971">1971</a>) was a <a href="/wiki/Great_Britain" title="Great Britain">British</a> <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> and the lead guitarist for the popular band <a href="/wiki/Weird_Sisters" title="Weird Sisters">The Weird Sisters</a>, and one of its first three members.<sup id="cite_ref-HM_5-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HM-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> Kirley was the son of <a href="/wiki/Quidditch" title="Quidditch">Quidditch</a> player <a href="/wiki/Catriona_McCormack" title="Catriona McCormack">Catriona McCormack</a> and the brother of <a href="/wiki/Meaghan_McCormack" title="Meaghan McCormack">Meaghan McCormack</a>. Both women played for the <a href="/wiki/Pride_of_Portree" title="Pride of Portree">Pride of Portree</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-QTA7_3-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-QTA7-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> He attended <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a><sup id="cite_ref-HM_5-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HM-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> from <a href="/wiki/1983" title="1983">1983</a><sup id="cite_ref-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup>.
</p>
