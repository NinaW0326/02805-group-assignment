<p>He was working as the manager of <a href="/wiki/Gambol_and_Japes_Wizarding_Joke_Shop" title="Gambol and Japes Wizarding Joke Shop">Gambol and Japes Wizarding Joke Shop</a> in <a href="/wiki/Diagon_Alley" title="Diagon Alley">Diagon Alley</a> sometime before <a href="/wiki/August" title="August">August</a> <a href="/wiki/1992" title="1992">1992</a>. When <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> entered his shop that year whilst shopping in Diagon Alley, he brought some packets of <a href="/wiki/Stink_Pellets" title="Stink Pellets">Stink Pellets</a> from him for four <a href="/wiki/Galleon" title="Galleon">galleons</a> each, along with some additional <a href="/wiki/Non-explodable,_luminous_balloons" title="Non-explodable, luminous balloons">Non-explodable, luminous balloons</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-COSG_1-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COSG-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
