<p>This individual would have began Hogwarts between <a href="/wiki/1_September" title="1 September">1 September</a> <a href="/wiki/1983" title="1983">1983</a> and 1 September <a href="/wiki/1989" title="1989">1989</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-age_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-age-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>  
</p><p>In the <a href="/wiki/1989%E2%80%931990_school_year" title="1989–1990 school year">1989–1990 school year</a>, he was found <a href="/wiki/Petrification" title="Petrification">petrified</a>. He was later brought to the <a href="/wiki/Hospital_Wing" title="Hospital Wing">Hospital Wing</a> along with other petrified students.<sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> 
</p>
