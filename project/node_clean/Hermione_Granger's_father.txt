<p><b>Mr Granger</b> (temporarily known as&#160;<b>Wendell Wilkins</b>)<sup id="cite_ref-DH_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> was a <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggle</a>, the husband of <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger%27s_mother" title="Hermione Granger&#39;s mother">Mrs Granger</a>, and father of <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione Jean Granger</a>. Working as a <a href="/wiki/Dentist" title="Dentist">dentist</a> alongside his wife, he and his family lived in a <a href="/wiki/Grangers%27_house" class="mw-redirect" title="Grangers&#39; house">house</a> located on the outskirts of <a href="/wiki/London" title="London">London</a>.
</p><p>He and his wife were proud and accepting of their daughter's identity as a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch</a>, which explained some strange things which happened around her when she was a child. They entered the magical world in <a href="/wiki/1992" title="1992">1992</a> to buy Hermione's school supplies, and were assisted by <a href="/wiki/Arthur_Weasley" title="Arthur Weasley">Arthur Weasley</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-COS_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COS-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> Several years later, however, Hermione was forced to alter her parents' memories and give them new identities as Wendell and Monica Wilkins, to protect them from the <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-DH_2-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>After the <a href="/wiki/Second_Wizarding_War" title="Second Wizarding War">Second Wizarding War</a> ended, Hermione found Mr Granger and his wife in <a href="/wiki/Australia" title="Australia">Australia</a> and returned their memories.<sup class="noprint">&#91;<a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Wikipedia:Citing_sources" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Wikipedia:Citing sources"><i><span title="The material in the vicinity of this tag needs references to reliable sources." style="white-space: nowrap;">citation needed</span></i></a>&#93;</sup> They gained grandchildren through Hermione and <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ron Weasley</a>'s marriage, <a href="/wiki/Rose_Granger-Weasley" title="Rose Granger-Weasley">Rose</a> and <a href="/wiki/Hugo_Weasley" class="mw-redirect" title="Hugo Weasley">Hugo Granger-Weasley</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-DH_2-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-A1_1-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-A1-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_life">Early life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger%27s_father?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Early life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger%27s_father?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Early life"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>

<p>Mr Granger was born to <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggle</a> parents, at least one of whom may have been descended from a Squib.
</p><p>Mr Granger was a well-qualified individual who held a career in <a href="/wiki/Dentist" title="Dentist">dentistry</a>. This was presumably how he met <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger%27s_mother" title="Hermione Granger&#39;s mother">his wife</a>, who was in the same profession.
</p><p>He and his wife both achieved a Licentiate in Dental Surgery, earning the prefix of "doctor" and married, living in a <a href="/wiki/Grangers%27_house" class="mw-redirect" title="Grangers&#39; house">house</a> on a street somewhere in England.
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Fatherhood">Fatherhood</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger%27s_father?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Fatherhood">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger%27s_father?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Fatherhood"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<div class="quote">
<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows">"<i>The Forest of Dean. I came camping here once, with my mum and dad.</i>"</span></dd>
<dd>&#8212;Hermione mentioning her parents during the Horcrux hunt<sup class="noprint"><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Deathly_Hallows" title="Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows">[src]</a></sup></dd>
<dd></dd></dl></div><figure class="thumb tright show-info-icon" style="width: 157px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/d/d6/Harry-potter-deathly-hallows1-mr_granger.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20160712203855" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/d/d6/Harry-potter-deathly-hallows1-mr_granger.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/155?cb=20160712203855" decoding="async" width="155" height="231" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="Harry-potter-deathly-hallows1-mr granger.jpg" data-image-key="Harry-potter-deathly-hallows1-mr_granger.jpg" /></a> 	 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:Harry-potter-deathly-hallows1-mr_granger.jpg" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption">Mr Granger standing next to his daughter</p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure>
<p>On <a href="/wiki/19_September" title="19 September">19 September</a>, <a href="/wiki/1979" title="1979">1979</a> they had a daughter, <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione Jean Granger</a>. The couple chose a "nice, unusual" first name that others had difficulty pronouncing because they "<i>liked to prove how clever they were</i>."<sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>The couple gave Hermione a <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger%27s_bedroom" title="Hermione Granger&#39;s bedroom">bedroom</a> on the first floor of their house,<sup id="cite_ref-DHPO_5-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DHPO-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> and they lived happily for many years.
</p><p>At some point, Mr and Mrs Granger took Hermione camping in the <a href="/wiki/Forest_of_Dean" title="Forest of Dean">Forest of Dean</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-DH_2-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> They also went to a theatre on <a href="/wiki/Shaftesbury_Avenue" title="Shaftesbury Avenue">Shaftesbury Avenue</a> in <a href="/wiki/London" title="London">London</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-DHPO_5-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DHPO-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> and at least once went to <a href="/wiki/Tottenham_Court_Road" title="Tottenham Court Road">Tottenham Court Road</a> with her.<sup id="cite_ref-DH_2-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Hermione attended a <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggle</a> school and was rather bright for her age. Hermione tells Horace Slughorn that one time whilst at work, her father was bitten by a <a href="/wiki/Robbie_Fenwick" title="Robbie Fenwick">boy</a> and had to be taken to hospital to receive several stitches.
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Discovering_the_wizarding_world">Discovering the wizarding world</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger%27s_father?veaction=edit&amp;section=4" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Discovering the wizarding world">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger%27s_father?action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Discovering the wizarding world"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<div class="quote">
<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;Harry Potter and the Philosopher&#39;s Stone">"<i>I've tried a few simple spells myself and they've all worked for me. Nobody in my family's magic at all, it was ever such a surprise when I got my letter, but I was ever so pleased, of course, it's the best school of witchcraft there is I've heard-- I've learned all the course books by heart, of course.</i>"</span></dd>
<dd>&#8212;Hermione regarding her Hogwarts acceptance letter<sup class="noprint"><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Philosopher%27s_Stone" title="Harry Potter and the Philosopher&#39;s Stone">[src]</a></sup></dd>
<dd></dd></dl></div><p>At some point prior to <a href="/wiki/1_September" title="1 September">1 September</a> <a href="/wiki/1991" title="1991">1991</a>, Mr Granger and his wife were visited by a staff member from <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a>, who gave them the letter inviting their daughter to the school. This visit revealed their daughter, Hermione, to be a <a href="/wiki/Muggle-born" title="Muggle-born">Muggle-born</a> <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch</a>, something that apparently came as quite a surprise. Hermione, at least, was shocked, but very pleased, and her parents were supportive of her.<sup id="cite_ref-PS_6-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PS-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup>
</p><h4><span class="mw-headline" id="1991-1992">1991-1992</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger%27s_father?veaction=edit&amp;section=5" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: 1991-1992">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger%27s_father?action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: 1991-1992"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>

<p>In <a href="/wiki/1991" title="1991">1991</a>, Mr Granger and his wife accompanied their daughter to <a href="/wiki/Diagon_Alley" title="Diagon Alley">Diagon Alley</a> to purchase her school supplies, including her wand from <a href="/wiki/Ollivanders" title="Ollivanders">Ollivanders</a>.
</p><p>Mrs and Mr Granger were separated from their daughter after she took the <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Express" title="Hogwarts Express">Hogwarts Express</a> to her new school. They were bemused by Hermione's new identity, but were proud of her, as she had memorised all of her school books before leaving.<sup id="cite_ref-PS_6-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PS-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup>

</p>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="1992-1993">1992-1993</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger%27s_father?veaction=edit&amp;section=6" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: 1992-1993">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger%27s_father?action=edit&amp;section=6" title="Edit section: 1992-1993"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<div class="quote">
<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets">"<i>[Arthur Weasley] was distracted almost at once by the sight of Hermione's parents, who were standing nervously at the counter that ran all along the great marble hall, waiting for Hermione to introduce them.</i>"</span></dd>

<dd></dd></dl></div><p>For the <a href="/wiki/1992%E2%80%931993_school_year" title="1992–1993 school year">1992–1993 school year</a>, Mr and Mrs Granger had to purchase more school equipment for their daughter. Firstly, in order to buy the supplies, they went to <a href="/wiki/Gringotts_Wizarding_Bank" title="Gringotts Wizarding Bank">Gringotts Wizarding Bank</a> to exchange <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggle</a> <a href="/wiki/Muggle_money" title="Muggle money">money</a> for <a href="/wiki/Wizarding_currency" title="Wizarding currency">wizarding currency</a>.
</p><p>There, they met her friends Harry Potter and <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ron Weasley</a>, as well as Ron's family. Ron's father, <a href="/wiki/Arthur_Weasley" title="Arthur Weasley">Arthur Weasley</a>, was obsessed with Muggles. Arthur was fascinated by the ten-pound notes that Mr Granger had in his hand, asked them all about their money, and took them out for a drink.<sup id="cite_ref-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<p>The Grangers went with the <a href="/wiki/Weasley_family" title="Weasley family">Weasleys</a> to Flourish and Blotts to purchase spellbooks with their new money. <a href="/wiki/Gilderoy_Lockhart" title="Gilderoy Lockhart">Gilderoy Lockhart</a> was there doing book signings, which Mr and Mrs Granger queued up for with the Weasleys. When <a href="/wiki/Lucius_Malfoy" title="Lucius Malfoy">Lucius Malfoy</a> approached Arthur and began arguing, Mr Granger and his wife watched apprehensively. Being a pure-blood supremacist, Malfoy scorned Arthur for being in the Grangers' company, glancing at them. The two wizards proceeded to argue with each other, before <a href="/wiki/Rubeus_Hagrid" title="Rubeus Hagrid">Rubeus Hagrid</a> stopped them.
</p><p>The Grangers left with the Weasleys, shocked and frightened by what they had just witnessed. They went to the <a href="/wiki/Leaky_Cauldron" title="Leaky Cauldron">Leaky Cauldron</a> Inn, sitting by the fireplace, before saying farewell to the Weasleys and <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> and leaving with their daughter for the Muggle world. After the school term ended, Mr and Mrs Granger took Hermione on holiday to <a href="/wiki/France" title="France">France</a> during the summer holidays. There, Hermione ate <a href="/wiki/Bouillabaisse" title="Bouillabaisse">bouillabaisse</a>, which she liked the taste of.<sup id="cite_ref-GOF_10-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF-10">&#91;10&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="1995-1996">1995-1996</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger%27s_father?veaction=edit&amp;section=7" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: 1995-1996">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger%27s_father?action=edit&amp;section=7" title="Edit section: 1995-1996"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<div class="quote">

</div><p>During the <a href="/wiki/1995%E2%80%931996_school_year" title="1995–1996 school year">1995–1996 school year</a>, Mr and Mrs Granger arranged a skiing trip over the <a href="/wiki/Christmas_holidays" title="Christmas holidays">Christmas holidays</a>. However, Hermione told them at the last minute that she wanted to stay at Hogwarts in order to study hard for her fifth year exams, the Ordinary Wizarding Levels, which are roughly equivalent to <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggle</a> O-Levels. Mr Granger and his wife were both disappointed, but, because they wanted their daughter to achieve good grades, they understood. Hermione instead ended up going to <a href="/wiki/12_Grimmauld_Place" title="12 Grimmauld Place">12 Grimmauld Place</a> to cheer up Harry, who had been refusing to speak to any of the house's occupants<sup id="cite_ref-OTP_7-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OTP-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup> and spent Christmas with the <a href="/wiki/Weasley_family" title="Weasley family">Weasley family</a>.

This was to protect her parents from <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a> during the worst of the <a href="/wiki/Second_Wizarding_War" title="Second Wizarding War">Second Wizarding War</a>, as their daughter was very close friends with <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> and she was a Muggle-born.<sup id="cite_ref-11" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-11">&#91;11&#93;</a></sup>

<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Later_life">Later life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger%27s_father?veaction=edit&amp;section=9" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Later life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger%27s_father?action=edit&amp;section=9" title="Edit section: Later life"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Sometime after the war ended in 1998, Hermione found him and restored his memories back to their original state.<sup id="cite_ref-BLC_12-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-BLC-12">&#91;12&#93;</a></sup> Later in his life he would become a grandfather to <a href="/wiki/Rose_Granger-Weasley" title="Rose Granger-Weasley">Rose</a> and <a href="/wiki/Hugo_Granger-Weasley" title="Hugo Granger-Weasley">Hugo Weasley</a> and the father-in-law of <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ron Weasley</a>.
</p>

<p>Mr and Mrs Granger first met the <a href="/wiki/Weasley_family" title="Weasley family">Weasley family</a> in <a href="/wiki/Gringotts_Wizarding_Bank" title="Gringotts Wizarding Bank">Gringotts Wizarding Bank</a>, where they stood nervously waiting for their daughter to introduce them.
</p><p>The couple came to trust the Weasleys very much, allowing their daughter to stay at their house over the holidays, prior to the school term, and to go to the <a href="/wiki/Quidditch_World_Cup" title="Quidditch World Cup">Quidditch World Cup</a> in <a href="/wiki/1994" title="1994">1994</a>.
</p><p>The two families were brought even closer when Hermione married <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ronald Weasley</a> and had <a href="/wiki/Rose_Granger-Weasley" title="Rose Granger-Weasley">Rose</a> and <a href="/wiki/Hugo_Granger-Weasley" title="Hugo Granger-Weasley">Hugo</a>.
</p>

<ul><li>Mr Granger was portrayed by <a href="/wiki/Tom_Knight" title="Tom Knight">Tom Knight</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Chamber_of_Secrets_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets (film)">film adaptation</a> of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Chamber_of_Secrets" title="Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets">Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets</a></i>, but he was replaced by <a href="/wiki/Ian_Kelly" title="Ian Kelly">Ian Kelly</a> for <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Deathly_Hallows:_Part_1" title="Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 1">Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 1</a></i><sup id="cite_ref-13" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-13">&#91;13&#93;</a></sup>.</li>
<li>In the earliest draft of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Philosopher%27s_Stone" title="Harry Potter and the Philosopher&#39;s Stone">Harry Potter and the Philosopher's Stone</a></i> the Potters lived on an island, and the Grangers lived on the shore nearby. When Voldemort killed James and Lily, Mr Granger noticed an explosion. He got on his boat and rowed out to the island to see what had happened, and found Harry.<sup id="cite_ref-14" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-14">&#91;14&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li>In the first part of the film adaptation of the final book, Hermione vanishes from all the photographs kept in the Granger house after she cast the <a href="/wiki/Memory_Charm" title="Memory Charm">Memory Charm</a> on Mr and Mrs Granger. She was upset by what she had done to her parents, and cried several times afterwards. The Death Eater <a href="/wiki/Corban_Yaxley" title="Corban Yaxley">Yaxley</a> can be seen in a deleted scene entering the empty home in search of Hermione.</li>
<li>It is possible his "Wendell Wilkins" alias may be a reference to <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Wendell_Wilkie" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Wendell Wilkie">Wendell Wilkie</a>, an American politician who ran unsuccessfully for president against Franklin Delano Roosevelt in 1940.</li></ul>
