<b>Merula Snyde's father</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Dark_Wizard" title="Dark Wizard">Dark</a> <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> of <a href="/wiki/Great_Britain" title="Great Britain">British</a> origin. </p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Merula_Snyde%27s_father?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Merula_Snyde%27s_father?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>In his youth, Merula's father attended <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a>, and was <a href="/wiki/Sorting_ceremony" title="Sorting ceremony">sorted</a> into <a href="/wiki/Slytherin" title="Slytherin">Slytherin</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup> During his Hogwarts years, he played <a href="/wiki/Quidditch" title="Quidditch">Quidditch</a> for the <a href="/wiki/Slytherin_Quidditch_team" title="Slytherin Quidditch team">Slytherin Quidditch team</a> in an unknown position.<sup id="cite_ref-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup> Later is his life, he married his <a href="/wiki/Merula_Snyde%27s_mother" title="Merula Snyde&#39;s mother">wife</a>, and they had one daughter together, <a href="/wiki/Merula_Snyde" title="Merula Snyde">Merula</a>.
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="First_Wizarding_War">First Wizarding War</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Merula_Snyde%27s_father?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: First Wizarding War">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Merula_Snyde%27s_father?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: First Wizarding War"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>He and his wife both became <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a>, loyal and devoted servants of the <a href="/wiki/Dark_Wizard" title="Dark Wizard">Dark Lord</a> <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Voldemort</a>. Both he and his wife participated in the <a href="/wiki/First_Wizarding_War" title="First Wizarding War">First Wizarding War</a>, they likely fought against many <a href="/wiki/Auror" title="Auror">Aurors</a> and members of the <a href="/wiki/Order_of_the_Phoenix" title="Order of the Phoenix">Order of the Phoenix</a>.
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="After_the_war">After the war</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Merula_Snyde%27s_father?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: After the war">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Merula_Snyde%27s_father?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: After the war"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>On <a href="/wiki/Merula_Snyde%27s_tenth_birthday" title="Merula Snyde&#39;s tenth birthday">Merula's tenth birthday</a>, he and his wife gave Merula a broom as a present. They took turns taking Merula on the broom. Less than a year later, he and his wife were incarcerated in <a href="/wiki/Azkaban" title="Azkaban">Azkaban</a> for their crimes committed as Death Eaters.<sup id="cite_ref-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Magical_abilities_and_skills">Magical abilities and skills</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Merula_Snyde%27s_father?veaction=edit&amp;section=4" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Merula_Snyde%27s_father?action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li><b><a href="/wiki/Dark_Arts" title="Dark Arts">Dark Arts</a></b>: Being a Death Eater, Merula's father would have been accomplished in dark magic, including the casting of <a href="/wiki/Dark_charm" title="Dark charm">Dark charms</a> and most likely the <a href="/wiki/Unforgivable_Curses" title="Unforgivable Curses">Unforgivable Curses</a>.</li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Duelling" title="Duelling">Duelling</a></b>: Considering how Death Eaters are proficient in <a href="/wiki/Martial_magic" title="Martial magic">martial magic</a>, Merula's father would have been a competent duellist who fought against <a href="/wiki/Auror" title="Auror">Aurors</a> and members of the <a href="/wiki/Order_of_the_Phoenix" title="Order of the Phoenix">Order of the Phoenix</a> during the <a href="/wiki/First_Wizarding_War" title="First Wizarding War">First Wizarding War</a>.</li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Flight" title="Flight">Flying</a></b>: Whilst attending Hogwarts in his youth, Merula's father played <a href="/wiki/Quidditch" title="Quidditch">Quidditch</a> for the <a href="/wiki/Slytherin_Quidditch_team" title="Slytherin Quidditch team">Slytherin Quidditch team</a>, showing he was talented with <a href="/wiki/Broomstick" title="Broomstick">broomstick</a> flying.</li></ul>
