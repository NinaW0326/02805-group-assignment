The <b>son</b> of <a href="/wiki/Celestina_Warbeck" title="Celestina Warbeck">Celestina Warbeck</a> was the product of her second marriage to a <a href="/wiki/Celestina_Warbeck%27s_second_husband" title="Celestina Warbeck&#39;s second husband">wizard who worked as her manager</a>. His parents split after ten years of marriage when Celestina took up with <a href="/wiki/Irving_Warble" title="Irving Warble">Irving Warble</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-Pottermore_2-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Pottermore-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
