<p><b>Jacob Grimm</b> (<a href="/wiki/1785" class="mw-redirect" title="1785">1785</a>–<a href="/wiki/1863" class="mw-redirect" title="1863">1863</a>) and <b>Wilhelm Grimm</b> (<a href="/wiki/1786" class="mw-redirect" title="1786">1786</a>–<a href="/wiki/1859" class="mw-redirect" title="1859">1859</a>), commonly referred to collectively as the <b>Brothers Grimm</b>, were <a href="/wiki/Germany" title="Germany">German</a> philologists and cultural historians who specialised in folklore.<sup id="cite_ref-wikipedia_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-wikipedia-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="History">History</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Brothers_Grimm?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: History">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Brothers_Grimm?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: History"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>In <a href="/wiki/1812" class="mw-redirect" title="1812">1812</a>, they published <i><a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Grimms%27_Fairy_Tales" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Grimms&#39; Fairy Tales">Children's and Household Tales</a></i>, a collection of German fairy tales they had gathered.<sup id="cite_ref-wikipedia_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-wikipedia-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> Among the stories it contained were "<a href="/wiki/Cinderella" title="Cinderella">Cinderella</a>" and "<a href="/wiki/Snow_White_and_the_Seven_Dwarves" title="Snow White and the Seven Dwarves">Snow White and the Seven Dwarves</a>".<sup id="cite_ref-wikipedia_1-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-wikipedia-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> These became favourites of <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggle</a> children but remained relatively unknown among <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizarding</a> families.<sup id="cite_ref-dh7_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-dh7-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> However, the <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts</a> <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Library" title="Hogwarts Library">library</a> kept at least one copy of <i><a href="/wiki/Household_Stories" title="Household Stories">Household Stories</a></i>, an English translation of the tales by <a href="/wiki/Lucy_Crane" title="Lucy Crane">Lucy Crane</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-hple_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-hple-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
