<p><b>Mrs Evans</b> (died between <a href="/wiki/1_September" title="1 September">1 September</a> <a href="/wiki/1971" title="1971">1971</a> and <a href="/wiki/31_October" title="31 October">31 October</a> <a href="/wiki/1981" title="1981">1981</a><sup id="cite_ref-wbd2004_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-wbd2004-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-2005interview_2-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2005interview-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-dodexplanation_3-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-dodexplanation-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>) was a <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggle</a> woman and the mother of <a href="/wiki/Petunia_Dursley" title="Petunia Dursley">Petunia</a> and <a href="/wiki/Lily_J._Potter" title="Lily J. Potter">Lily Evans</a>. Through Petunia, she was the maternal grandmother of <a href="/wiki/Dudley_Dursley" title="Dudley Dursley">Dudley Dursley</a>, and through Lily, the maternal grandmother of <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> and great-grandmother of <a href="/wiki/Lily_L._Potter" title="Lily L. Potter">Lily Luna</a>, <a href="/wiki/Albus_Potter" title="Albus Potter">Albus Severus</a> and <a href="/wiki/James_Potter_II" title="James Potter II">James Sirius Potter</a>.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Evans_sisters%27_mother?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Evans_sisters%27_mother?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Mrs Evans and <a href="/wiki/Evans_sisters%27_father" title="Evans sisters&#39; father">her husband</a> lived in <a href="/wiki/Cokeworth" title="Cokeworth">Cokeworth</a>, <a href="/wiki/England" title="England">England</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-cokeworth_4-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-cokeworth-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> The couple had two daughters: <a href="/wiki/Petunia_Dursley" title="Petunia Dursley">Petunia</a>, born in the late <a href="/wiki/1950s" title="1950s">1950s</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Lily_J._Potter" title="Lily J. Potter">Lily</a>, born <a href="/wiki/30_January" title="30 January">30 January</a> <a href="/wiki/1960" title="1960">1960</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-dh16_5-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-dh16-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-dh33_6-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-dh33-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>While Petunia was a <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggle</a> like her parents, Lily turned out to be a <a href="/wiki/Muggle-born" title="Muggle-born">Muggle-born</a> <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch</a>, something that made Mr and Mrs Evans proud.<sup id="cite_ref-ps4_7-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-ps4-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup> Petunia, on the other hand, was jealous, considering her sister a "freak."<sup id="cite_ref-ps4_7-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-ps4-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup> According to Petunia, Mr and Mrs Evans favoured Lily, talking about her constantly.<sup id="cite_ref-ps4_7-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-ps4-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>On <a href="/wiki/1_September" title="1 September">1 September</a> <a href="/wiki/1971" title="1971">1971</a>, Mrs Evans and her husband took Lily and Petunia to <a href="/wiki/Platform_Nine_and_Three-Quarters" title="Platform Nine and Three-Quarters">Platform Nine and Three-Quarters</a>, in order to see Lily off to her first year at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-dh33_6-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-dh33-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Death_and_legacy">Death and legacy</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Evans_sisters%27_mother?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Death and legacy">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Evans_sisters%27_mother?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Death and legacy"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Mrs Evans and her husband died "normal Muggle death[s]" sometime before the murder of their daughter Lily and son-in-law <a href="/wiki/James_Potter_I" title="James Potter I">James Potter</a>, on the night of <a href="/wiki/31_October" title="31 October">31 October</a>, <a href="/wiki/1981" title="1981">1981</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-wbd2004_1-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-wbd2004-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-2005interview_2-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2005interview-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-dodexplanation_3-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-dodexplanation-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> This meant that their grandson by Lily, <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> was left in the care of Petunia, his only surviving blood relation.<sup id="cite_ref-ootp37_8-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-ootp37-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<p>When Harry looked into the Mirror of Erised in <a href="/wiki/1991" title="1991">1991</a>, he saw both his maternal and paternal grandparents, among a number of other relatives he had never met.<sup id="cite_ref-PS12_9-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PS12-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
