<p><b>Percy Ignatius Weasley</b> (b. <a href="/wiki/22_August" title="22 August">22 August</a>, <a href="/wiki/1976" title="1976">1976</a>) was an <a href="/wiki/England" title="England">English</a> <a href="/wiki/Pure-blood" title="Pure-blood">pure-blood</a> <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a>, the third child of <a href="/wiki/Arthur_Weasley" title="Arthur Weasley">Arthur</a> and <a href="/wiki/Molly_Weasley" title="Molly Weasley">Molly Weasley</a> <span class="template-c">(née Prewett)</span>. He was the younger brother of <a href="/wiki/William_Weasley" title="William Weasley">Bill</a> and <a href="/wiki/Charles_Weasley" title="Charles Weasley">Charlie</a> and the older brother of <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ron</a>, <a href="/wiki/Ginevra_Weasley" title="Ginevra Weasley">Ginny</a>, <a href="/wiki/George_Weasley" title="George Weasley">George</a>, and the late <a href="/wiki/Fred_Weasley" title="Fred Weasley">Fred Weasley</a>. He attended <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> from <a href="/wiki/1987" title="1987">1987</a>-<a href="/wiki/1994" title="1994">1994</a>, and was both a <a href="/wiki/Prefect" title="Prefect">prefect</a> and <a href="/wiki/Head_Boy_or_Girl" title="Head Boy or Girl">Head Boy</a>.
</p><p>He took the rules very seriously, and did not believe that <a href="/wiki/Fred_and_George_Weasley" title="Fred and George Weasley">Fred and George Weasley</a> would get very far with their constant pranks. It was also once stated by his brother Ron that "<i>Percy wouldn't know a joke if it danced naked in front of him wearing <a href="/wiki/Dobby" title="Dobby">Dobby</a>'s tea cosy</i>". A high achiever, he went to work at the <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">Ministry of Magic</a> after leaving school, working in the <a href="/wiki/Department_of_International_Magical_Cooperation" title="Department of International Magical Cooperation">Department of International Magical Cooperation</a> under <a href="/wiki/Bartemius_Crouch_Senior" title="Bartemius Crouch Senior">Barty Crouch Snr</a> and later as <a href="/wiki/Junior_Assistant_to_the_Minister_for_Magic" title="Junior Assistant to the Minister for Magic">Junior Assistant to the Minister for Magic</a> himself.
</p><p>However, Percy's rise through the Ministry caused a rift between him and his family following the <a href="/wiki/Return_of_Lord_Voldemort" title="Return of Lord Voldemort">return</a> of <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a>. His father thought that his promotion to <a href="/wiki/Minister_for_Magic" title="Minister for Magic">Minister for Magic</a> <a href="/wiki/Cornelius_Fudge" title="Cornelius Fudge">Cornelius Fudge</a>'s office was a plot by the Minister to keep tabs on the Weasleys, and through them, <a href="/wiki/Albus_Dumbledore" title="Albus Dumbledore">Albus Dumbledore</a>. Percy denied this and remained steadfastly loyal to the Ministry's line that Voldemort was not back, estranging himself from his family and furthermore condemning them for their support of and affiliation with <a href="/wiki/Albus_Dumbledore" title="Albus Dumbledore">Albus Dumbledore</a> and the <a href="/wiki/Order_of_the_Phoenix" title="Order of the Phoenix">Order of the Phoenix</a>. Even though the Ministry did accept that the Dark Lord had indeed returned following the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_the_Department_of_Mysteries" title="Battle of the Department of Mysteries">Battle of the Department of Mysteries</a>, Percy's shame over his behaviour stopped him from reconciling with his family. 
</p><p>After the <a href="/wiki/Fall_of_the_Ministry_of_Magic" title="Fall of the Ministry of Magic">fall of the Ministry</a> to the <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a> in <a href="/wiki/1997" title="1997">1997</a>, Percy finally realised just how corrupt the Ministry was and fully accepted that he had been wrong the whole time and abandoned the Ministry. Percy was tipped off by <a href="/wiki/Aberforth_Dumbledore" title="Aberforth Dumbledore">Aberforth Dumbledore</a> about the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a> in <a href="/wiki/1998" title="1998">1998</a> and finally made up with his family. He survived the battle but was grief-stricken over the death of his brother Fred, whom he was fighting alongside and was determined to avenge.
</p><p>Following the downfall of Voldemort, Percy returned to work for the Ministry and became Head of the <a href="/wiki/Department_of_Magical_Transportation" title="Department of Magical Transportation">Department of Magical Transportation</a><sup id="cite_ref-PMRS1_7-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PMRS1-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup> under the new Minister for Magic, <a href="/wiki/Kingsley_Shacklebolt" title="Kingsley Shacklebolt">Kingsley Shacklebolt</a>. He married a woman named <a href="/wiki/Audrey_Weasley" title="Audrey Weasley">Audrey</a> and had two daughters, <a href="/wiki/Molly_Weasley_II" title="Molly Weasley II">Molly</a>, named after her paternal grandmother, and <a href="/wiki/Lucy_Weasley" title="Lucy Weasley">Lucy</a>.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Percy_Weasley?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Percy_Weasley?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_life">Early life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Percy_Weasley?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Early life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Percy_Weasley?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Early life"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Percy Ignatius Weasley was the third of seven children born to <a href="/wiki/Arthur_Weasley" title="Arthur Weasley">Arthur</a> and <a href="/wiki/Molly_Weasley" title="Molly Weasley">Molly Weasley</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-PS_12-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PS-12">&#91;12&#93;</a></sup> He was a small child during the height of the <a href="/wiki/First_Wizarding_War" title="First Wizarding War">First Wizarding War</a>. In the fighting, his maternal uncles, <a href="/wiki/Fabian_Prewett" title="Fabian Prewett">Fabian</a> and <a href="/wiki/Gideon_Prewett" title="Gideon Prewett">Gideon Prewett</a>, both members of the <a href="/wiki/Order_of_the_Phoenix" title="Order of the Phoenix">Order of the Phoenix</a>, were murdered fighting <a href="/wiki/Antonin_Dolohov" title="Antonin Dolohov">Antonin Dolohov</a> and four other <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-OP_13-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OP-13">&#91;13&#93;</a></sup> While he was still a child, the wizarding world became peaceful again (for a time) after <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a>'s first defeat on <a href="/wiki/31_October" title="31 October">31 October</a>, <a href="/wiki/1981" title="1981">1981</a>.
</p>
<figure class="thumb tleft show-info-icon" style="width: 237px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/5/59/The_Burrow.png/revision/latest?cb=20150828154152" class="image"><img alt="" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw%3D%3D" decoding="async" width="235" height="158" class="thumbimage lazyload" data-image-name="The Burrow.png" data-image-key="The_Burrow.png" data-src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/5/59/The_Burrow.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/235?cb=20150828154152" /></a> 	<noscript><a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/5/59/The_Burrow.png/revision/latest?cb=20150828154152" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/5/59/The_Burrow.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/235?cb=20150828154152" decoding="async" width="235" height="158" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="The Burrow.png" data-image-key="The_Burrow.png" data-src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/5/59/The_Burrow.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/235?cb=20150828154152" /></a></noscript> 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:The_Burrow.png" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption"><a href="/wiki/The_Burrow" title="The Burrow">The Burrow</a>, where Percy grew up along with his family</p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure>
<p>Percy and his five brothers — <a href="/wiki/William_Weasley" title="William Weasley">Bill</a>, <a href="/wiki/Charles_Weasley" title="Charles Weasley">Charlie</a>, <a href="/wiki/Fred_Weasley" title="Fred Weasley">Fred</a>, <a href="/wiki/George_Weasley" title="George Weasley">George</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ron</a> — as well as his younger sister, <a href="/wiki/Ginevra_Weasley" title="Ginevra Weasley">Ginny</a>, grew up in the Burrow on the outskirts of <a href="/wiki/Ottery_St_Catchpole" title="Ottery St Catchpole">Ottery St Catchpole</a> in <a href="/wiki/Devon" title="Devon">Devon</a>, England. Like all his siblings, he was home educated in reading, writing and mathematics by his mother. 
</p><p>The Weasley family was not wealthy at all, compared to other wizarding families. Many other <a href="/wiki/Pure-blood" title="Pure-blood">pure-blood</a> families, particularly the Malfoys, disdained them for their "<i><a href="/wiki/Blood_traitor" title="Blood traitor">blood traitor</a></i>" beliefs and lack of wealth. However, this did not bother the Weasleys and they continued to stand by their beliefs. Also Molly and Arthur tried their best to make up for this with their <a href="/wiki/Love" title="Love">love</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-CS_14-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-CS-14">&#91;14&#93;</a></sup> When Percy was seven his five-year-old brother Fred <a href="/wiki/Teddy_bear_to_spider" title="Teddy bear to spider">transformed</a> Ron's teddy bear into a giant <a href="/wiki/Spider" title="Spider">spider</a>, which caused Ron's arachnophobia. This was in retaliation for Ron breaking Fred's <a href="/wiki/Toy_broomstick" title="Toy broomstick">toy broomstick</a>. It can be assumed that Percy had a fairly happy childhood, as all the Weasley siblings did. 
</p>

<p>He started at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> in September of 1987 and was sorted into <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor" title="Gryffindor">Gryffindor House</a>, where he shared a dormitory with <a href="/wiki/Oliver_Wood" title="Oliver Wood">Oliver Wood</a>. He took a pet rat named <a href="/wiki/Peter_Pettigrew" title="Peter Pettigrew">Scabbers</a> with him.

<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Second_year">Second year</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Percy_Weasley?veaction=edit&amp;section=4" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Second year">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Percy_Weasley?action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Second year"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<p>Early in his second year, Percy joined his two older brothers and <a href="/wiki/Jacob%27s_sibling" title="Jacob&#39;s sibling">a friend of theirs</a> in a study session in the <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Library" title="Hogwarts Library">Hogwarts Library</a>. Percy introduced himself and his pet rat to this individual.<sup id="cite_ref-15" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-15">&#91;15&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>He and his two brothers came to <a href="/wiki/Jacob%27s_room" title="Jacob&#39;s room">Jacob's room</a> with Jacob's sibling in order to talk to <a href="/wiki/Nicholas_de_Mimsy-Porpington" title="Nicholas de Mimsy-Porpington">Nicholas de Mimsy-Porpington</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-16" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-16">&#91;16&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>At some point during the year, Scabbers went missing. During the first Gryffindor vs. Ravenclaw <a href="/wiki/Quidditch" title="Quidditch">Quidditch</a> match, he caught Jacob's sibling in the <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor_Common_Room" title="Gryffindor Common Room">Gryffindor Common Room</a> with Jae Kim using magic outside of class. Percy planned to report them but after Jacob's sibling managed to convince him not to report them by agreeing to find Scabbers.<sup id="cite_ref-17" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-17">&#91;17&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Third_year">Third year</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Percy_Weasley?veaction=edit&amp;section=5" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Third year">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Percy_Weasley?action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Third year"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<p>In his third year, Percy likely chose <a href="/wiki/Divination_(class)" title="Divination (class)">Divination</a> and <a href="/wiki/Muggle_Studies" title="Muggle Studies">Muggle Studies</a> as electives, as in the <a href="/wiki/1992-1993_school_year" class="mw-redirect" title="1992-1993 school year">1992-1993 school year</a> he suggested <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry</a> take these classes for them being very useful in his opinion, but he did not go to <a href="/wiki/Care_of_Magical_Creatures" title="Care of Magical Creatures">Care of Magical Creatures</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-COS14_18-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COS14-18">&#91;18&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>As a third year, Percy was allowed to visit <a href="/wiki/Hogsmeade" title="Hogsmeade">Hogsmeade</a>. When he visited the village for the first time, he went to the <a href="/wiki/Three_Broomsticks_Inn" title="Three Broomsticks Inn">Three Broomsticks Inn</a> with Charlie.<sup id="cite_ref-19" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-19">&#91;19&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Fifth_year">Fifth year</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Percy_Weasley?veaction=edit&amp;section=6" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Fifth year">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Percy_Weasley?action=edit&amp;section=6" title="Edit section: Fifth year"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<div class="quote">

<dd></dd></dl></div><p>An academically inclined, ambitious, and hard working student. Percy was made <a href="/wiki/Prefect" title="Prefect">prefect</a> in his fifth year. As a reward for this, Percy was given a Screech Owl — whom he named <a href="/wiki/Hermes" title="Hermes">Hermes</a>, and new robes by his parents. Scabbers was handed down to his younger brother, <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ron</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-PS_12-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PS-12">&#91;12&#93;</a></sup>

<p>In his new duties as a prefect, Percy was responsible for the new first year Gryffindors, including bringing them to their dormitories on the first day, and to lead them to safety during <a href="/wiki/Hallowe%27en" title="Hallowe&#39;en">Hallowe'en</a> in 1991, following the arrival of a troll in the dungeons. He remained at Hogwarts during the Christmas period in 1991, and sat his <a href="/wiki/Ordinary_Wizarding_Level" title="Ordinary Wizarding Level">O.W.Ls</a> at the end of the year.<sup id="cite_ref-PS_12-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PS-12">&#91;12&#93;</a></sup> At some point in his fifth or sixth year, Percy began dating <a href="/wiki/Penelope_Clearwater" title="Penelope Clearwater">Penelope Clearwater</a>, a <a href="/wiki/Ravenclaw" title="Ravenclaw">Ravenclaw</a> prefect, though he kept it a secret from his family, most likely to prevent his twin brothers Fred and George from mercilessly teasing him (which they did most of the time).<sup id="cite_ref-CS_14-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-CS-14">&#91;14&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<p>Due to the attacking of students with following the opening of the <a href="/wiki/Chamber_of_Secrets" title="Chamber of Secrets">Chamber of Secrets</a>, Percy, like the other prefects, was required to patrol the castle to maintain order and report anything suspicious. He would also sneak into empty classrooms to be with Penelope, so as to be away from the prying eyes of his siblings, but was caught on one occasion by his youngest sister <a href="/wiki/Ginevra_Weasley" title="Ginevra Weasley">Ginny</a>. He swore her to secrecy, and in doing so, was inadvertently responsible for the events at the end of that school year, as he prevented Ginny from telling <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ron</a> and Harry about her involvement with the Chamber of Secrets, thinking that she was telling them about him and Penelope.<sup id="cite_ref-CS_14-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-CS-14">&#91;14&#93;</a></sup>

He was reunited with Penelope after <a href="/wiki/Pomona_Sprout" title="Pomona Sprout">Professor Sprout</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Mandrake" title="Mandrake">Mandrake</a> potion restored those petrified to their normal state, and Ginny, who was saved from <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Tom Marvolo Riddle</a> and the Chamber of Secrets by Harry. <p>After leaving school, Percy went to work at the <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">Ministry of Magic</a>, just as he had planned to, and was assigned to the <a href="/wiki/Department_of_International_Magical_Cooperation" title="Department of International Magical Cooperation">Department of International Magical Cooperation</a> under Bartemius Crouch Snr. During the summer of 1994, Percy worked diligently under Crouch, a man whom he admired and respected for his strict adherence to rules and his perfectionist attitude towards his work. He was responsible for authoring a dull report on standardising cauldron thickness. He also learned about the plans for the resurrection of the <a href="/wiki/Triwizard_Tournament" title="Triwizard Tournament">Triwizard Tournament</a>, which he took great delight with in hinting about to his younger siblings. He had no patience for any noise when working, and even popped his head out of the window to tell his family to be quiet. Two weeks before the Quidditch World Cup, Percy passed his <a href="/wiki/Apparition" title="Apparition">Apparition</a> test. He took to Apparating downstairs to breakfast every morning just to prove he could in fact do it.<sup id="cite_ref-GF_21-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GF-21">&#91;21&#93;</a></sup>

<p>When <a href="/wiki/Cornelius_Fudge" title="Cornelius Fudge">Cornelius Fudge</a> did not believe Harry Potter's claim that <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a> was back, he thought that Dumbledore was using the threat of his return as a springboard, spreading rumours so as to destabilise his administration. Surprisingly, despite the trouble he was previously in, Percy was offered the position of <a href="/wiki/Junior_Assistant_to_the_Minister_for_Magic" title="Junior Assistant to the Minister for Magic">Junior Assistant to Fudge</a> himself. He expected his family to be pleased with him, but his father, who supported Harry and Dumbledore, made it clear that he thought that Percy had been offered the post so that Fudge could keep track of the family, <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry</a>, and Dumbledore. Following this, Percy and his father had a huge row, with Percy telling his father that he had to struggle against his lousy reputation since he had started at the Ministry, that he had no ambition, and that was the reason the family was so poor. He also told his father that he was an idiot for believing <a href="/wiki/Albus_Dumbledore" title="Albus Dumbledore">Dumbledore</a> and his loyalty remained with the <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">Ministry</a>. He also rubbished Harry's claim that Lord Voldemort had returned, saying it was not good enough.<sup id="cite_ref-OP_13-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OP-13">&#91;13&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Percy severed all ties with his family, wanting everyone to know that he had nothing to do with them. He packed his bags the same night and left. He moved to <a href="/wiki/London" title="London">London</a> where he was able to get himself a <a href="/wiki/Percy_Weasley%27s_London_residence" title="Percy Weasley&#39;s London residence">flat</a>, and continued with the position in Fudge's office. His mother went to London to try to talk with him, but Percy slammed the door in her face. As such, Percy is the only of-age member of the Weasley family who did not join the revived <a href="/wiki/Order_of_the_Phoenix" title="Order of the Phoenix">Order of the Phoenix</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-OP_13-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OP-13">&#91;13&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<p>In the summer of 1995, Percy acted as Court Scribe during <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Disciplinary_hearing_of_Harry_Potter" title="Disciplinary hearing of Harry Potter">hearing</a> before the <a href="/wiki/Wizengamot" title="Wizengamot">Wizengamot</a> for using magic in front of a <a href="/wiki/Dudley_Dursley" title="Dudley Dursley">Muggle</a>. To Harry's surprise and dismay, Percy refused to look at him, and even agreed with one of Fudge's biased comments against Harry. Following the hearing, despite Harry being cleared of all charges, Percy believed that Harry only got off due to a technicality, and he also ignored his father, who was waiting outside for Harry. Percy's estrangement from his family continued, although he did <a href="/wiki/Percy_Weasley%27s_letter_to_Ronald_Weasley_(1995)_II" title="Percy Weasley&#39;s letter to Ronald Weasley (1995) II">write</a> a letter to <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ron</a>, congratulating him on becoming a <a href="/wiki/Prefect" title="Prefect">prefect</a> and urging him to stop associating with Harry (who he felt was a bad influence) as well as believing that his family would one day realise their mistake for believing Dumbledore and apologise to him. Finding the letter extremely offensive, Ron tore it up and threw it into the fire. This letter also greatly hurt Harry's feelings. On the same day, Percy gave an interview to the <i><a href="/wiki/Daily_Prophet" title="Daily Prophet">Daily Prophet</a></i> concerning <a href="/wiki/Dolores_Umbridge" title="Dolores Umbridge">Dolores Umbridge</a>'s appointment as <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_High_Inquisitor" title="Hogwarts High Inquisitor">Hogwarts High Inquisitor</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-OP_13-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OP-13">&#91;13&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Later that year during Christmas, Percy returned his traditional Christmas jumper to his mother unopened without a note, much to her distress, and neither visited his father in <a href="/wiki/St_Mungo%27s_Hospital_for_Magical_Maladies_and_Injuries" title="St Mungo&#39;s Hospital for Magical Maladies and Injuries">St Mungo's Hospital for Magical Maladies and Injuries</a> nor at least asked about him following his wounding by <a href="/wiki/Nagini" title="Nagini">Nagini</a>, much to Fred and George's fury.<sup id="cite_ref-OP_13-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OP-13">&#91;13&#93;</a></sup>

Percy kept a firm grip on Harry's shoulder to hold him firmly in place and once again acted as a scribe for Fudge. He left after being ordered to make a copy of his notes for the <i>Daily Prophet</i>, thus avoiding the fate that awaited the remaining officials. Dumbledore, with no intentions of being taken to Azkaban then proceeded to knock out Umbridge, Fudge, Kingsley, and <a href="/wiki/John_Dawlish" title="John Dawlish">Dawlish</a> and made his escape.<sup id="cite_ref-OP_13-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OP-13">&#91;13&#93;</a></sup>

<p>Following the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_the_Department_of_Mysteries" title="Battle of the Department of Mysteries">Battle of the Department of Mysteries</a>, Percy now knew the truth about <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a>'s return, but continued to side with the Ministry because he couldn't bring himself to face his family after seeing that they were right all along when siding with Harry and Dumbledore and he was wrong for siding with the Ministry, feeling shame over it. When Fudge was succeeded by <a href="/wiki/Rufus_Scrimgeour" title="Rufus Scrimgeour">Rufus Scrimgeour</a> as Minister, Percy continued in his role as Junior Assistant. His mother masked this defection by claiming that Percy was too busy in the Ministry to return for the holidays, when in reality, the last thing Percy wanted to do was be around his estranged family.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP_22-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP-22">&#91;22&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>During Christmas in <a href="/wiki/1996" title="1996">1996</a>, Percy was pressured by Rufus Scrimgeour to go to <a href="/wiki/The_Burrow" title="The Burrow">The Burrow</a> with him. Scrimgeour was using the visit as a pretence to have a talk with Harry, who was spending Christmas there. Scrimgeour exploited Percy's family connections to get close to Harry, and Percy was very uncomfortable with seeing his family again, as he had not planned to see them for the holidays. His mother was overjoyed to see him and immediately ran to hug him, while his father and siblings simply stared at him coldly. Percy only spoke to his mother (and rather briefly and reluctantly, at that), and the visit ended badly with Percy storming out after <a href="/wiki/Ginevra_Weasley" title="Ginevra Weasley">Ginny</a>, <a href="/wiki/Fred_and_George_Weasley" title="Fred and George Weasley">Fred, and George</a> flung mashed parsnips at him. In June 1997, he attended the <a href="/wiki/Funeral_of_Albus_Dumbledore" title="Funeral of Albus Dumbledore">funeral of Albus Dumbledore</a> with other Ministry officials, including Dolores Umbridge.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP_22-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP-22">&#91;22&#93;</a></sup>

<p>On 1 August, 1997, Percy was the only member of his family who did not attend his brother <a href="/wiki/William_Weasley" title="William Weasley">Bill</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Wedding_of_William_Weasley_and_Fleur_Delacour" title="Wedding of William Weasley and Fleur Delacour">wedding</a> to <a href="/wiki/Fleur_Delacour" title="Fleur Delacour">Fleur Delacour</a>, despite Molly's hopes that he would, which led her to cry again. On the same day, the <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">Ministry of Magic</a> was overthrown by <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a>, and Rufus Scrimgeour was killed and replaced by <a href="/wiki/Pius_Thicknesse" title="Pius Thicknesse">Pius Thicknesse</a>, a puppet of Voldemort's. Percy finally began to see what was happening around him, but could do nothing since he was being watched closely. He continued to work at the Ministry, presumably under the new Minister. Eventually, though, he managed to make contact with <a href="/wiki/Aberforth_Dumbledore" title="Aberforth Dumbledore">Aberforth Dumbledore</a>, Albus Dumbledore's estranged younger brother, who had connections with the <a href="/wiki/Order_of_the_Phoenix" title="Order of the Phoenix">Order of the Phoenix</a> and <a href="/wiki/Dumbledore%27s_Army" title="Dumbledore&#39;s Army">Dumbledore's Army</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-DH_23-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH-23">&#91;23&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>On 1 May, 1998, Aberforth tipped Percy off to the fact that <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts</a> was taking a stand against Voldemort. Percy <a href="/wiki/Apparition" title="Apparition">Apparated</a> to the <a href="/wiki/Hog%27s_Head" class="mw-redirect" title="Hog&#39;s Head">Hog's Head</a>, and travelled down the secret tunnel to the <a href="/wiki/Room_of_Requirement" title="Room of Requirement">Room of Requirement</a>, hoping he hadn't missed the battle. In the room, he found the majority of his family trying to restrain <a href="/wiki/Ginevra_Weasley" title="Ginevra Weasley">Ginny</a> from joining the battle. In a tense moment, Percy apologised profusely to his family, and his brother <a href="/wiki/Fred_Weasley" title="Fred Weasley">Fred</a>, who, along with <a href="/wiki/George_Weasley" title="George Weasley">George</a>, were the most vocal in their anger towards Percy, was the first to forgive him. Reunited with his family, he stood alongside with the defenders of Hogwarts against the Death Eaters.<sup id="cite_ref-DH_23-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH-23">&#91;23&#93;</a></sup>

<p>During the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a>, Percy and Fred fought alongside each other. Percy was duelling the then Minister <a href="/wiki/Pius_Thicknesse" title="Pius Thicknesse">Pius Thicknesse</a> and in the middle of the battle, he jokingly told the Minister he had resigned. Fred was surprised with his brother for making a joke after so long — but the joyous mood was shattered when an explosion destroyed part of the corridor they were in and killed Fred. Percy, grief-stricken, refused to leave his brother's body until Harry, who was quite angry at him before, and Ron helped him store it safely in a wall niche. Full of anger and sorrow, Percy stormed back into battle, charging after Death Eater <a href="/wiki/Augustus_Rookwood" title="Augustus Rookwood">Augustus Rookwood</a>, who was attacking a group of students further down the corridor.<sup id="cite_ref-DH_23-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH-23">&#91;23&#93;</a></sup>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Physical_description">Physical description</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Percy_Weasley?veaction=edit&amp;section=17" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Physical description">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Percy_Weasley?action=edit&amp;section=17" title="Edit section: Physical description"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Percy was tall and thin, like his <a href="/wiki/Arthur_Weasley" title="Arthur Weasley">father</a> and brothers <a href="/wiki/William_Weasley" title="William Weasley">Bill</a> and <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ron</a>, and with the vivid red hair and freckles characteristic of the <a href="/wiki/Weasley_family" title="Weasley family">Weasley family</a>. He wore horn-rimmed glasses, and also tried to carry himself in a dignified manner, although he usually came off looking pretentious and silly.
</p><p>By <a href="/wiki/2014" title="2014">2014</a>, <a href="/wiki/Rita_Skeeter" title="Rita Skeeter">Rita Skeeter</a> noticed Percy had "<i>aged considerably since the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a></i>" and was "<i>greying and balding</i>".<sup id="cite_ref-PMRS1_7-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PMRS1-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Personality_and_traits">Personality and traits</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Percy_Weasley?veaction=edit&amp;section=18" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Personality and traits">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Percy_Weasley?action=edit&amp;section=18" title="Edit section: Personality and traits"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<div class="quote">
<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire">"<i>Percy wouldn't recognise a joke if it danced naked in front of him wearing <a href="/wiki/Dobby" title="Dobby">Dobby</a>'s tea-cosy.</i>"</span></dd>
<dd>&#8212;<a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ron Weasley</a> on Percy's lack of humour<sup class="noprint"><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Goblet_of_Fire" title="Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire">[src]</a></sup></dd>
<dd></dd></dl></div><figure class="thumb tright show-info-icon" style="width: 222px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/1/1f/Percy.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20150828155322" class="image"><img alt="" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw%3D%3D" decoding="async" width="220" height="167" class="thumbimage lazyload" data-image-name="Percy.jpg" data-image-key="Percy.jpg" data-src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/1/1f/Percy.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/220?cb=20150828155322" /></a> 	<noscript><a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/1/1f/Percy.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20150828155322" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/1/1f/Percy.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/220?cb=20150828155322" decoding="async" width="220" height="167" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="Percy.jpg" data-image-key="Percy.jpg" data-src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/1/1f/Percy.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/220?cb=20150828155322" /></a></noscript> 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:Percy.jpg" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption">Percy Weasley as a <a href="/wiki/Prefect" title="Prefect">prefect</a> in his sixth year</p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure>
<p>Percy was extremely ambitious and dedicated to his goals, which was apparently to become <a href="/wiki/Minister_for_Magic" title="Minister for Magic">Minister for Magic</a>, though it is possible that it was just Fred and George joking.<sup id="cite_ref-CS_14-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-CS-14">&#91;14&#93;</a></sup> A stickler for rules and regulations, he respected authority and, when he attained it himself, expected respect in return. However, Percy usually just seemed affected and self-important since he constantly tried to look and sound dignified, including calling his parents "Mother" and "Father" rather than "Mum" and "Dad" like the rest of his siblings.<sup id="cite_ref-PA_20-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PA-20">&#91;20&#93;</a></sup> He had a sophisticated way of speaking, thinking it made him sound flamboyant, but he simply came off as pompous and grandiose.
</p><p>Percy was also highly opinionated, and made loud comments regarding the <a href="/wiki/Sirius_Black" title="Sirius Black">Sirius Black</a> situation and the measures he would suggest when he made it into the <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">Ministry of Magic</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-PA_20-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PA-20">&#91;20&#93;</a></sup> He was also very dismissive of <a href="/wiki/Ludovic_Bagman" title="Ludovic Bagman">Ludo Bagman</a>'s skills in running the <a href="/wiki/Department_of_Magical_Games_and_Sports" title="Department of Magical Games and Sports">Department of Magical Games and Sports</a> when compared to Mr Crouch, but that didn't stop him attempting to make a good impression in front of him when he met him at the Quidditch World Cup.<sup id="cite_ref-GF_21-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GF-21">&#91;21&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Percy was extremely pompous, arrogant, and stiff-necked; he took himself very seriously and was often the butt of pranks and jokes played on him by his fun-loving siblings. 
</p><p>However, he showed rare glimmerings of a sense of humour when joking with <a href="/wiki/Penelope_Clearwater" title="Penelope Clearwater">Penelope Clearwater</a> over sabotaging Harry's broomstick,<sup id="cite_ref-GF_21-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GF-21">&#91;21&#93;</a></sup> and when <a href="/wiki/Duelling" title="Duelling">duelling</a> Pius Thicknesse during the Battle of Hogwarts.<sup id="cite_ref-DH_23-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH-23">&#91;23&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Percy could be snobbish and bossy at times, as Fred and George have said, but he also cared about his family at the end, although sometimes when it was too late. He was also very pompous and when he wanted to get something, he sometimes only cared about himself rather than his family. He cared immensely about his own self-image and always tried to appear sophisticated, intelligent and upper-class, but only succeeded at becoming narcissistic, coarse and rather insensitive.  
</p><p>Percy proved himself to be very much of a social climber. He wanted to achieve prominence at the Ministry, choosing to dedicate himself to the institution and abandoning his family when they chose to rebel against the Ministry. He was a sychophant, constantly trying to suck up to Barty Crouch Snr or Fudge, despite the former not knowing his actual name. His love of order and rules, and obedience to authorities blinded him to the truth about Lord Voldemort's return and he felt ashamed after learning the truth and couldn't bring himself to see his family. It even made him miss out on three important events of his family: the joining of the <a href="/wiki/Order_of_the_Phoenix" title="Order of the Phoenix">Order of the Phoenix</a>, a visit to his <a href="/wiki/Attack_on_Arthur_Weasley" title="Attack on Arthur Weasley">injured</a> <a href="/wiki/Arthur_Weasley" title="Arthur Weasley">father</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-OP_13-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OP-13">&#91;13&#93;</a></sup> and his <a href="/wiki/William_Weasley" title="William Weasley">eldest brother</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Wedding_of_William_Weasley_and_Fleur_Delacour" title="Wedding of William Weasley and Fleur Delacour">wedding</a>. Despite this, however, Percy is not entirely without reason, as after the Ministry fell and was taken over by the Death Eaters, Percy finally realised his wrongs and admitted to them and apologised sincerely to his family, reconciling with them.<sup id="cite_ref-DH_23-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH-23">&#91;23&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Despite his normal determination to maintain proper behaviour, Percy was an avid fan of <a href="/wiki/Quidditch" title="Quidditch">Quidditch</a> and could sometimes forget decorum in demonstrating his enthusiasm for it. In the <a href="/wiki/1993-1994_school_year" class="mw-redirect" title="1993-1994 school year">1993-1994 school year</a>, he made a 10 Galleon bet with Penelope Clearwater that Gryffindor would win the Gryffindor vs. Ravenclaw Quidditch match, despite the fact that he didn't have ten Galleons at the time.<sup id="cite_ref-PA13_27-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PA13-27">&#91;27&#93;</a></sup> Later that year, when Gryffindor won the final match of the season, vs. <a href="/wiki/Slytherin" title="Slytherin">Slytherin</a>, Percy jumped "<i>up and down like a maniac, all dignity forgotten.</i>"<sup id="cite_ref-PA15_28-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PA15-28">&#91;28&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Magical_abilities_and_skills">Magical abilities and skills</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Percy_Weasley?veaction=edit&amp;section=19" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Percy_Weasley?action=edit&amp;section=19" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>

<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Weasley_family">Weasley family</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Percy_Weasley?veaction=edit&amp;section=23" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Weasley family">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Percy_Weasley?action=edit&amp;section=23" title="Edit section: Weasley family"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>

<p>Percy was quite different in temperament from his family members, which impacted his relationships with them. His academic studies and responsible nature earned him praise and adoration in the eyes of his mother and father, although even <a href="/wiki/Arthur_Weasley" title="Arthur Weasley">Mr Weasley</a> was not above having a small laugh at the expense of his son's stuffiness.<sup id="cite_ref-PA_20-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PA-20">&#91;20&#93;</a></sup> However, his behaviour alienated him from his younger siblings, especially <a href="/wiki/Fred_Weasley" title="Fred Weasley">Fred</a> and <a href="/wiki/George_Weasley" title="George Weasley">George</a>, who were the exact opposite of him. Because of this, Percy was a constant butt of their jokes and pranks, including bewitching his prefect and Head Boy badges to read <i>Pinhead</i><sup id="cite_ref-CS_14-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-CS-14">&#91;14&#93;</a></sup> and <i>Bighead Boy</i><sup id="cite_ref-PA_20-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PA-20">&#91;20&#93;</a></sup> respectively, and sending <a href="/wiki/Dragon" title="Dragon">dragon</a> dung to his office at the Ministry.<sup id="cite_ref-GF_21-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GF-21">&#91;21&#93;</a></sup> However, the twins' pranks were generally good-natured ribbing at his expense — during Christmas in <a href="/wiki/1991" title="1991">1991</a>, the twins forbade him from sitting with the other prefects, noting that Christmas was a time for family.<sup id="cite_ref-PS_12-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PS-12">&#91;12&#93;</a></sup> He tried to be a role-model for <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ron</a> and <a href="/wiki/Ginevra_Weasley" title="Ginevra Weasley">Ginny</a>, but they took after the fun-loving twins.
</p><p>In <a href="/wiki/1995" title="1995">1995</a>, he broke off all ties with his family following an argument with his father over his promotion to Junior Assistant to Fudge. Proud and ambitious, he could not conceive that Fudge would be using him to spy on Dumbledore. He could also not see that both the Ministry and the <i><a href="/wiki/Daily_Prophet" title="Daily Prophet">Daily Prophet</a></i> were not telling the public the truth over the return of <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a>. When the truth was revealed, he felt ashamed about his actions and guiltily shunned his family until the moment that he might have lost them during the Battle of Hogwarts, even though he had been wanting to apologise to them for some time, at which Percy sincerely apologised to them and was forgiven by them all.
</p><p>Curiously enough, while it was the twins who were the most angry at him within the family, constantly telling the others that he was no good and to forget about him, it was also Fred and George who were the first to forgive him and welcome him back to the family. Fred was the first to forgive him, while George seemed to carry on as if the split had never occurred, and made a small joke about Percy having been a prefect.<sup id="cite_ref-DH_23-10" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH-23">&#91;23&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<p>Over the years, Percy was particularly proud and protective of his youngest brother, Ron. During Ron's first year, Percy was extremely proud that he had beaten <a href="/wiki/Minerva_McGonagall" title="Minerva McGonagall">Professor McGonagall</a>'s enchanted chess set that guarded the <a href="/wiki/Philosopher%27s_Stone" title="Philosopher&#39;s Stone">Philosopher's Stone</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-PS_12-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PS-12">&#91;12&#93;</a></sup> During the Second Task of the <a href="/wiki/Triwizard_Tournament" title="Triwizard Tournament">Triwizard Tournament</a>, he rushed to help him from the lake after he had been rescued by Harry Potter.<sup id="cite_ref-GF_21-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GF-21">&#91;21&#93;</a></sup> After severing ties with his family, Ron was the only one he contacted, congratulating him on becoming a prefect, and urging him to cut his ties with Harry Potter so that he would not be dragged down with him, although this only disgusted Ron even further.<sup id="cite_ref-OP_13-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OP-13">&#91;13&#93;</a></sup> Finally, after reconciling with his family, in the brief ceasefire allowed to them by <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a> during the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a>, Percy comforted Ron following Fred's death, which also devastated him. Through the marriage of his brother <a href="/wiki/William_Weasley" title="William Weasley">Bill Weasley</a>, Percy became brother-in-law to <a href="/wiki/Fleur_Delacour" title="Fleur Delacour">Fleur Delacour</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-DH_23-11" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH-23">&#91;23&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<p>Harry Potter first met Percy Weasley at <a href="/wiki/King%27s_Cross_Station" title="King&#39;s Cross Station">King's Cross Station</a> when he saw him go through the barrier to <a href="/wiki/Platform_Nine_and_Three-Quarters" title="Platform Nine and Three-Quarters">platform 9¾</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-PS_12-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PS-12">&#91;12&#93;</a></sup> In Harry's second year, Percy offered Harry some advice regarding his choice of subjects that he would take in the third year.<sup id="cite_ref-PA_20-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PA-20">&#91;20&#93;</a></sup> For the most part, Percy liked Harry, and awarded him full marks during the <a href="/wiki/Triwizard_Tournament" title="Triwizard Tournament">Triwizard Tournament</a> when he stood in for <a href="/wiki/Bartemius_Crouch_Senior" title="Bartemius Crouch Senior">Bartemius Crouch Snr</a> during the Second Task.<sup id="cite_ref-GF_21-10" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GF-21">&#91;21&#93;</a></sup> While Harry was also generally friendly towards Percy — although he frequently laughed at Fred and George's pranks — he also felt that he had always liked Percy the least of all the Weasleys after he abandoned his family, possibly due to his somewhat pompous nature.<sup id="cite_ref-OP_13-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OP-13">&#91;13&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>However, in Harry's fifth year, when the Ministry began publishing articles about Harry being "<i>a liar and very violent</i>", Percy bought into it, wanting to be obedient to <a href="/wiki/Cornelius_Fudge" title="Cornelius Fudge">Cornelius Fudge</a>. He considered Harry a bad influence for Ron and sent him <a href="/wiki/Percy_Weasley%27s_letter_to_Ronald_Weasley_(1995)_II" title="Percy Weasley&#39;s letter to Ronald Weasley (1995) II">a letter</a> encouraging him to sever ties with Harry and to stay on Umbridge's good side so that he could become eligible for <a href="/wiki/Head_Boy_or_Girl" title="Head Boy or Girl">Head Boyship</a> in a few years. Although Harry attempted to make light of the letter's content, Ron promptly tore it to pieces and threw it in the fire, calling Percy "<i>the world's biggest git</i>". At that point, Harry hated Percy for not believing in him, for not giving him recognition in the <a href="/wiki/Disciplinary_hearing_of_Harry_Potter" title="Disciplinary hearing of Harry Potter">disciplinary hearing</a>, and for accepting Fudge's mockeries.<sup id="cite_ref-OP_13-10" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OP-13">&#91;13&#93;</a></sup> Percy finally came to his senses when the Ministry fell to Voldemort, and he fought alongside Harry in the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a>. This appeared to have caused Harry to forgive Percy and consider him an ally again, as he went to some lengths as to try to make him move to avoid the oncoming attacks from Death Eaters despite the risk on himself. At this point, Percy seemed to have lost what enmity he had towards Harry, looking visibly sombre and saddened as Harry's dead body was brought before Hogwarts. After Voldemort's death, he is among those who hugged Harry for his victory over Voldemort, Harry's lack of protest showing his better views of Percy.
</p><p>They later became brothers-in-law through Harry's marriage to Ginny. However, their relationship remained distant as while seeing both of his sons go to Hogwarts, Harry thought he overheard Percy talking loudly about <a href="/wiki/Broomstick_regulations" title="Broomstick regulations">broomstick regulations</a>, and was glad to have the excuse not to stop and say hello but while he apparently preferred to not interact with Percy, he no longer hated Percy and had forgiven him and apparently decided to leave how badly Percy treated him once in the past.
</p>

<figure class="thumb tright show-info-icon" style="width: 166px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/9/94/Hermione_Granger_Half_Blood_Prince.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20140628051648" class="image"><img alt="" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw%3D%3D" decoding="async" width="164" height="225" class="thumbimage lazyload" data-image-name="Hermione Granger Half Blood Prince.jpg" data-image-key="Hermione_Granger_Half_Blood_Prince.jpg" data-src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/9/94/Hermione_Granger_Half_Blood_Prince.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/164?cb=20140628051648" /></a> 	<noscript><a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/9/94/Hermione_Granger_Half_Blood_Prince.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20140628051648" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/9/94/Hermione_Granger_Half_Blood_Prince.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/164?cb=20140628051648" decoding="async" width="164" height="225" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="Hermione Granger Half Blood Prince.jpg" data-image-key="Hermione_Granger_Half_Blood_Prince.jpg" data-src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/9/94/Hermione_Granger_Half_Blood_Prince.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/164?cb=20140628051648" /></a></noscript> 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:Hermione_Granger_Half_Blood_Prince.jpg" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption"><a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione Granger</a>, his future sister-in-law</p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure>
<p>From 1991-1994, Percy was on very good terms with Hermione Granger, due to their academic brilliance and strong work ethic. Just after her <a href="/wiki/Sorting_Hat" title="Sorting Hat">Sorting</a>, Hermione talked to Percy about her enthusiasm about learning magic, particularly <a href="/wiki/Transfiguration" title="Transfiguration">Transfiguration</a>, and it is likely that she admired him for being a Prefect, and being as academically brilliant as her. It is mentioned that Hermione got on with Percy better than the rest of the Weasleys. Their bond was broken after the <a href="/wiki/1994_Quidditch_World_Cup" title="1994 Quidditch World Cup">1994 Quidditch World Cup</a>, when Percy condemned his boss's house-elf <a href="/wiki/Winky" title="Winky">Winky</a>, and Hermione was angry at his unfairness, having recently realised how badly <a href="/wiki/House-elves" class="mw-redirect" title="House-elves">house-elves</a> were treated. They were never again as close, and when Percy broke ties with his family, this generally included Hermione as she was closely associated with the Weasleys and Harry. Hermione eventually became Percy's sister-in-law, and the relationship between them is unknown.
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Penelope_Clearwater">Penelope Clearwater</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Percy_Weasley?veaction=edit&amp;section=27" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Penelope Clearwater">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Percy_Weasley?action=edit&amp;section=27" title="Edit section: Penelope Clearwater"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>

<p>Percy began dating <a href="/wiki/Penelope_Clearwater" title="Penelope Clearwater">Penelope Clearwater</a>, a fellow <a href="/wiki/Prefect" title="Prefect">prefect</a>, sometime during his fifth year. She boosted his morale by a tonne, and he flaunted himself even more, though he kept his relationship secret from his family, not wanted to be laughed at by anyone else. They would meet in deserted classrooms to kiss, but were caught once by Percy's sister, Ginny. Percy was distraught when Penelope was <a href="/wiki/Petrification" title="Petrification">petrified</a> in <a href="/wiki/1993" title="1993">1993</a>, and immensely relieved when she was cured by Professor Sprout's Mandrake potion. The secrecy was broken when Ginny revealed the truth to her brothers, asking them not to tease Percy over the fact, which they did anyway.<sup id="cite_ref-CS_14-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-CS-14">&#91;14&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>With the secret out, Percy and Penelope appeared in public together, and he kept a photo of her. During his stay at the <a href="/wiki/Leaky_Cauldron" title="Leaky Cauldron">Leaky Cauldron</a> before the start of his seventh year, he accused Ron of dripping tea on it.<sup id="cite_ref-PA_20-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PA-20">&#91;20&#93;</a></sup> He met her on platform 9¾, showing off his Head Boy badge, and, later in the year, wagered ten <a href="/wiki/Galleon" title="Galleon">Galleons</a> on the outcome of the <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor" title="Gryffindor">Gryffindor</a> vs. <a href="/wiki/Ravenclaw" title="Ravenclaw">Ravenclaw</a> match, despite not actually having ten galleons. He playfully remarked to her about sabotaging Harry's <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter%27s_Firebolt" title="Harry Potter&#39;s Firebolt">Firebolt</a> when she asked if she could touch it.<sup id="cite_ref-PA_20-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PA-20">&#91;20&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>They presumably broke up later, as Percy married <a href="/wiki/Audrey_Weasley" title="Audrey Weasley">another woman</a>.
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Barty_Crouch">Barty Crouch</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Percy_Weasley?veaction=edit&amp;section=28" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Barty Crouch">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Percy_Weasley?action=edit&amp;section=28" title="Edit section: Barty Crouch"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<div class="quote">
<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire">"<i>They'll be announcing their engagement any day soon...</i>"</span></dd>
<dd>&#8212;<a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ron Weasley</a> on Percy and Crouch<sup class="noprint"><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Goblet_of_Fire" title="Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire">[src]</a></sup></dd>
<dd></dd></dl></div>
<p>Percy Weasley worked under Crouch for thirteen months and was absolutely obsessed with him: indeed, Percy's brother Ronald joked in August of 1994 that he should be expecting their engagement any day.<sup id="cite_ref-GOF5_4-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF5-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> Crouch, however, cannot remember Percy's name: as a running gag, he referred to his protege as "Weatherby". Percy's admiration of Crouch was so strong that he defended Crouch when his brothers mocked him and when Crouch sacked <a href="/wiki/Winky" title="Winky">Winky</a>, putting his friendship with <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione Granger</a> under strain when she condemned Crouch's seemingly unreasonable act.
</p><p>After Voldemort subjugated Crouch and forced him to write letters to declare sickness, Weasley obeyed the orders via these letters unquestioningly. This eventually got Percy into trouble, as when Crouch escaped and was seen delirious on Hogwarts ground, the Ministry accused Percy of being irresponsible and unperceptive, that he should have seen the written orders as potential forgeries and that he should have informed a superior of Crouch's questionable status.
</p><p>It is unknown what Percy thought of Crouch after the man had been found insane in the <a href="/wiki/Forbidden_Forest" title="Forbidden Forest">Forbidden Forest</a> and participated in breaking out his criminal son from Azkaban, and it is unknown how Percy reacted when learning of his death.
</p><p>Crouch also indirectly proved to be a major factor for Percy's huge loyalty to the Ministry and his temporary estrangement from his own family, especially as his twin brothers Fred and George Weasley started teasing him more and his younger brother Ron stopped trusting him after learning of Crouch's son. Additionally, the trouble Percy got into over Crouch's disappearance only made him more ambitious to the point that he accepted the post of Junior Assistant to the Minister for Magic under Fudge without question, and put his Ministry career before his own family when Voldemort returned to full power, just as Crouch had done.
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Cornelius_Fudge">Cornelius Fudge</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Percy_Weasley?veaction=edit&amp;section=29" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Cornelius Fudge">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Percy_Weasley?action=edit&amp;section=29" title="Edit section: Cornelius Fudge"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<figure class="thumb tright show-info-icon" style="width: 182px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/6/6d/Corneliushogwarts.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20100312184011" class="image"><img alt="" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw%3D%3D" decoding="async" width="180" height="240" class="thumbimage lazyload" data-image-name="Corneliushogwarts.jpg" data-image-key="Corneliushogwarts.jpg" data-src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/6/6d/Corneliushogwarts.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/180?cb=20100312184011" /></a> 	<noscript><a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/6/6d/Corneliushogwarts.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20100312184011" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/6/6d/Corneliushogwarts.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/180?cb=20100312184011" decoding="async" width="180" height="240" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="Corneliushogwarts.jpg" data-image-key="Corneliushogwarts.jpg" data-src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/6/6d/Corneliushogwarts.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/180?cb=20100312184011" /></a></noscript> 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:Corneliushogwarts.jpg" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption"><a href="/wiki/Cornelius_Fudge" title="Cornelius Fudge">Cornelius Fudge</a>, his former Ministry superior</p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure>
<p>Cornelius Fudge originally looked down on Percy's <a href="/wiki/Weasley_family" title="Weasley family">family</a> due to their affiliation with Muggles; however, in 1995 as an attempt to spy on the Weasley family and through them, Albus Dumbledore, Fudge promoted Percy to become his Junior Minister. Ironically, this led to Percy's defection from his family. Percy commented in his <a href="/wiki/Percy_Weasley%27s_letter_to_Ronald_Weasley_(1995)_II" title="Percy Weasley&#39;s letter to Ronald Weasley (1995) II">letter</a> to his younger brother <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ron</a> that the Minister would not be more gracious to him for this defection. He agreed with Fudge on that Harry was violent and disturbed, as that Dumbledore was plotting to overthrow Fudge when the Ministry began publishing articles.
</p><p>Over the final year of Fudge's term as Minister, he used Percy as the court scribe on at least two occasions: <a href="/wiki/Disciplinary_hearing_of_Harry_Potter" title="Disciplinary hearing of Harry Potter">Harry's disciplinary hearing</a> and the meeting in the Headmaster's Office following the <a href="/wiki/Dumbledore%27s_Army#Discovery" title="Dumbledore&#39;s Army">discovery of Dumbledore's Army</a>. Percy, during both moments, heartily agreed with Fudge's decisions, and laughed when Fudge made insulting mockeries at Harry and Dumbledore. When Fudge's folly had been exposed and he was forced to resign as Minister in disgrace, Percy remained loyal to the Ministry; it is unknown how he reacted to Fudge's dismissal.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Percy_Weasley?veaction=edit&amp;section=30" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Percy_Weasley?action=edit&amp;section=30" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p><a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Percy_(given_name)" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Percy (given name)">Percy</a> is a short form of the name <i>Percival</i>, which is derived from Old French, meaning "pierce the veil". It was the name of one of the Knights of the Round Table in Arthurian legend. This may be a pattern in names in the <a href="/wiki/Weasley_family" title="Weasley family">Weasley family</a>, as Percy's <a href="/wiki/Arthur_Weasley" title="Arthur Weasley">father</a> is named <i><a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/King_Arthur" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:King Arthur">Arthur</a></i> and his <a href="/wiki/Ginevra_Weasley" title="Ginevra Weasley">sister</a> is named <i>Ginevra</i>, a version of <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Guinevere" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Guinevere">Guinevere</a>. Percival is also one of <a href="/wiki/Albus_Dumbledore" title="Albus Dumbledore">Albus Dumbledore</a>'s middle names, and Albus formerly had reddish (auburn) hair, suggesting a possible link between the two families. It can also be short for Perseus, which is derived from Ancient Greek, meaning "destroyer." It could possibly be clues for him destroying his relationship with his family.
</p><p>The namesake for Percy's middle name, Ignatius, was likely his <a href="/wiki/Molly_Weasley" title="Molly Weasley">mother</a>'s uncle <a href="/wiki/Ignatius_Prewett" title="Ignatius Prewett">Ignatius Prewett</a><sup id="cite_ref-31" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-31">&#91;31&#93;</a></sup>. Ignatius is a Roman name altered from its original Etruscan to resemble the Latin <i>ignis</i>, meaning "fire". It was the name of several saints, including the founder of the Jesuits.<sup id="cite_ref-32" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-32">&#91;32&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

However, unlike Fudge and Crouch Snr, Percy was able to break out of this mould after seeing the error of his ways. Additionally Percy never had to go through an incident that would have destroyed his reputation at the Ministry.</li>
<li>While the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Order_of_the_Phoenix_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix (film)">film adaptation</a> of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Order_of_the_Phoenix" title="Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix">Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix</a></i> did show Percy as one of Fudge's supporters, it omitted the details of Percy becoming Fudge's Junior Assistant or any mention of him defecting from his family, when he decided to remain obedient to the Ministry and accept their story that Voldemort had not returned.</li>
<li>Due to Percy being reduced to a background character in <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Deathly_Hallows:_Part_2" title="Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 2">Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 2</a></i>, the films never acknowledged Percy's estrangement from his family or the apology he made to them during the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a>.</li>
<li>Percy is omitted from the film adaptations of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Goblet_of_Fire_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire (film)">Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire</a></i> and <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Half-Blood_Prince_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince (film)">Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince</a></i>.</li>
<li>Out of the six films he was used in, Percy only had a speaking appearance in the first three films, while he has no dialogue in the fifth, seventh, and eighth films.</li></ul>
