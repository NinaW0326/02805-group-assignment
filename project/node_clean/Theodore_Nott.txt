<b>Theodore Nott</b> (b. <a href="/wiki/1979" title="1979">1979</a>/<a href="/wiki/1980" title="1980">1980</a>) was a <a href="/wiki/Pure-blood" title="Pure-blood">pure-blood</a><sup id="cite_ref-rowling_2-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-rowling-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-blood_3-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-blood-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> who was a <a href="/wiki/Slytherin" title="Slytherin">Slytherin</a> student in the same year as <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a>. He came from a <a href="/wiki/Nott_family" title="Nott family">family</a> of <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a> and supporters of <a href="/wiki/Pure-blood_supremacy" title="Pure-blood supremacy">Pure-blood supremacy</a>.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Theodore_Nott?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Theodore_Nott?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span id="Early_life_(1980-1991)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_life_.281980-1991.29">Early life <span class="template-c">(1980-1991)</span></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Theodore_Nott?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Early life (1980-1991)">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Theodore_Nott?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Early life (1980-1991)"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Theodore Nott was born to an elderly <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a>, <a href="/wiki/Nott" title="Nott">Mr Nott</a> and <a href="/wiki/Theodore_Nott%27s_mother" title="Theodore Nott&#39;s mother">his wife</a>. After the death of his mother, Theodore was raised by his father, who was a <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eater</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-rowling_2-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-rowling-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span id="Hogwarts_years_(1991-1998)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="Hogwarts_years_.281991-1998.29">Hogwarts years <span class="template-c">(1991-1998)</span></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Theodore_Nott?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Hogwarts years (1991-1998)">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Theodore_Nott?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Hogwarts years (1991-1998)"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Theodore started at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> on <a href="/wiki/1_September" title="1 September">1 September</a> <a href="/wiki/1991" title="1991">1991</a> and was sorted into <a href="/wiki/Slytherin" title="Slytherin">Slytherin</a> house.<sup id="cite_ref-PS7_5-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PS7-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>During a <a href="/wiki/Care_of_Magical_Creatures" title="Care of Magical Creatures">Care of Magical Creatures</a> lesson in his <a href="/wiki/Fifth_year" title="Fifth year">fifth year</a>, he was one of only three students present who could see <a href="/wiki/Thestral" title="Thestral">Thestrals</a>. This suggests that he may have witnessed his mother's <a href="/wiki/Death" title="Death">death</a> firsthand.<sup id="cite_ref-ootp21_6-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-ootp21-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-rowling2_7-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-rowling2-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup> He found the <a href="/wiki/Creature" title="Creature">creatures</a> rather distasteful and unpleasant as he watched one eating.<sup id="cite_ref-ootp21_6-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-ootp21-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Fifth-year">Fifth-year</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Theodore_Nott?veaction=edit&amp;section=4" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Fifth-year">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Theodore_Nott?action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Fifth-year"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<p><a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione Granger</a> pointed out Theodore's name to <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> for the first time in <a href="/wiki/1996" title="1996">1996</a>. She did this when Theodore was gathered in the school library with fellow Slytherins: <a href="/wiki/Vincent_Crabbe" title="Vincent Crabbe">Vincent Crabbe</a>, <a href="/wiki/Gregory_Goyle" title="Gregory Goyle">Gregory Goyle</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Draco_Malfoy" title="Draco Malfoy">Draco Malfoy</a>. All four boys had fathers who had recently been outed by Harry as Death Eaters in an article published in <i><a href="/wiki/The_Quibbler" title="The Quibbler">The Quibbler</a></i>. Theodore was the only member of the group whose reaction to seeing Harry was not specifically noted as being either negative or threatening, as it was not noticeable at all.<sup id="cite_ref-ootp26_8-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-ootp26-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<p>While on the <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Express" title="Hogwarts Express">Hogwarts Express</a> in <a href="/wiki/1996" title="1996">1996</a>, <a href="/wiki/Horace_Slughorn" title="Horace Slughorn">Horace Slughorn</a> waylaid <a href="/wiki/Blaise_Zabini" title="Blaise Zabini">Blaise Zabini</a> and inquired after Theodore's father. Blaise replied that <a href="/wiki/Nott" title="Nott">Mr Nott</a> was a Death Eater imprisoned in <a href="/wiki/Azkaban" title="Azkaban">Azkaban</a> for breaking into the <a href="/wiki/Department_of_Mysteries" title="Department of Mysteries">Department of Mysteries</a> the previous June. Slughorn, not wanting any association with Death Eaters, was not pleased and thus invited Blaise, not Theodore, to join the <a href="/wiki/Slug_Club" title="Slug Club">Slug Club</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP7_9-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP7-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Theodore was one of four Slytherin students who progressed to the <a href="/wiki/Nastily_Exhausting_Wizarding_Test" title="Nastily Exhausting Wizarding Test">N.E.W.T.</a>-level in <a href="/wiki/Potions_(class)" title="Potions (class)">Potions</a>. During his first lesson with Professor Slughorn, he sat next to Draco, and they both sniggered when Hermione revealed that she was a <a href="/wiki/Muggle-born" title="Muggle-born">Muggle-born</a>. They also were sceptical about the effects of <a href="/wiki/Amortentia" title="Amortentia">Amortentia</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP9_10-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP9-10">&#91;10&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Seventh_year"><a href="/wiki/Seventh_year" title="Seventh year">Seventh year</a></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Theodore_Nott?veaction=edit&amp;section=6" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Seventh year">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Theodore_Nott?action=edit&amp;section=6" title="Edit section: Seventh year"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<p>Theodore attended his seventh year at Hogwarts. Due to being a <a href="/wiki/Pure-blood" title="Pure-blood">pure-blood</a> <a href="/wiki/Slytherin" title="Slytherin">Slytherin</a>, as well as the son of a Death Eater, he was likely treated better than most. Shortly before the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a>, students were being evacuated from the school including the Slytherins. It is unknown if he was one of the few students to join <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a>, or if he simply joined those who evacuated.
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Later_life">Later life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Theodore_Nott?veaction=edit&amp;section=7" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Later life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Theodore_Nott?action=edit&amp;section=7" title="Edit section: Later life"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>In <a href="/wiki/2020" title="2020">2020</a> Nott duelled <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> (the Head of the <a href="/wiki/Department_of_Magical_Law_Enforcement" title="Department of Magical Law Enforcement">Department of Magical Law Enforcement</a>), before being defeated and arrested by him for the illegal possession of an unregistered and advanced <a href="/wiki/Time-Turner" title="Time-Turner">Time-Turner</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-CC_11-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-CC-11">&#91;11&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Nott had created at least two Time-Turners for <a href="/wiki/Lucius_Malfoy" title="Lucius Malfoy">Lucius Malfoy</a>. The prototype, which was restricted by a boundary of five minutes, was seized by Potter and used by his <a href="/wiki/Albus_Potter" title="Albus Potter">son</a> to travel to the past. The improved Time-Turner, which gleamed gold and allowed the traveller to stay in the past for an unlimited time, was kept by Lucius who never used it. Draco Malfoy then used it to save his <a href="/wiki/Scorpius_Malfoy" title="Scorpius Malfoy">son</a> in the past.<sup id="cite_ref-CC_11-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-CC-11">&#91;11&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Physical_description">Physical description</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Theodore_Nott?veaction=edit&amp;section=8" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Physical description">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Theodore_Nott?action=edit&amp;section=8" title="Edit section: Physical description"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Theodore was "weedy" and "stringy," indicating that he was tall, thin, and perhaps weakly built.<sup id="cite_ref-ootp21_6-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-ootp21-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-ootp26_8-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-ootp26-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup> He was also considered to be "<a href="/wiki/Rabbit" title="Rabbit">rabbity</a>" in appearance by some.<sup id="cite_ref-rowling2_7-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-rowling2-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Personality_and_traits">Personality and traits</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Theodore_Nott?veaction=edit&amp;section=9" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Personality and traits">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Theodore_Nott?action=edit&amp;section=9" title="Edit section: Personality and traits"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<div class="quote">
<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince">"<i>Harry saw Malfoy lean close to Nott and whisper something; both of them snickered.</i>"</span></dd>
<dd>&#8212;Nott and <a href="/wiki/Draco_Malfoy" title="Draco Malfoy">Draco Malfoy</a> insulting Hermione's <a href="/wiki/Muggle-born" title="Muggle-born">Muggle-born</a> status<sup class="noprint"><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Half-Blood_Prince" title="Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince">[src]</a></sup></dd>
<dd></dd></dl></div><p>Theodore was a solitary boy who never felt compelled to join "gangs," such as the <a href="/wiki/Draco_Malfoy%27s_gang" title="Draco Malfoy&#39;s gang">one</a> headed by Draco Malfoy.<sup id="cite_ref-rowling_2-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-rowling-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> He was seen as a loner who preferred to do things by himself, although he did join in when Malfoy laughed at <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione Granger</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Blood_status" title="Blood status">status</a> as a <a href="/wiki/Muggle-born" title="Muggle-born">Muggle-born</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-HBP9_10-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP9-10">&#91;10&#93;</a></sup> which suggests he was a <a href="/wiki/Pure-blood_supremacy" title="Pure-blood supremacy">pure-blood supremacist</a> like Malfoy and their fathers.
</p><p>Theodore was particularly clever and inventive, best demonstrated by his cunning and secretive creation of two illegal <a href="/wiki/Time-Turner" title="Time-Turner">Time-Turners</a>, a spectacular feat not many others were capable of doing.<sup id="cite_ref-CC_11-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-CC-11">&#91;11&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Magical_abilities_and_skills">Magical abilities and skills</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Theodore_Nott?veaction=edit&amp;section=10" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Theodore_Nott?action=edit&amp;section=10" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li><b><a href="/wiki/Potion" title="Potion">Potions</a></b>: Theodore was evidently skilled at potion-making, as he was only one of twelve students able to advance to <a href="/wiki/Nastily_Exhausting_Wizarding_Test" title="Nastily Exhausting Wizarding Test">N.E.W.T.</a>-level <a href="/wiki/Potions_(class)" title="Potions (class)">Potions</a> in <a href="/wiki/1996" title="1996">1996</a>. He either achieved an '<a href="/wiki/Exceeds_Expectations" title="Exceeds Expectations">Exceeds Expectations</a>' or an '<a href="/wiki/Outstanding" title="Outstanding">Outstanding</a>' on his <a href="/wiki/Ordinary_Wizarding_Level" title="Ordinary Wizarding Level">O.W.L.</a> exam, showing his proficiency in the subject.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP9_10-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP9-10">&#91;10&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">Magical</a> device invention</b>: Theodore was notably very talented in the complex art of creating powerful magical devices. He successfully created two <a href="/wiki/Time-Turner" title="Time-Turner">Time-Turners</a> that allow the user to travel far back into time, surpassing the <a href="/wiki/Hour-Reversal_Charm" title="Hour-Reversal Charm">Hour-Reversal Charm</a> on the Ministry-issued devices.<sup id="cite_ref-CC_11-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-CC-11">&#91;11&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Duelling" title="Duelling">Duelling</a></b>: Theodore had some degree of skill in duelling other wizards and in <a href="/wiki/Martial_magic" title="Martial magic">martial magic</a>, as he duelled <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> in <a href="/wiki/2020" title="2020">2020</a>, although he was defeated by the far superior duellist and arrested, meaning his combative abilities were hardly exceptional.<sup id="cite_ref-CC_11-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-CC-11">&#91;11&#93;</a></sup></li></ul>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Possessions">Possessions</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Theodore_Nott?veaction=edit&amp;section=11" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Possessions">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Theodore_Nott?action=edit&amp;section=11" title="Edit section: Possessions"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li><b>Two <a href="/wiki/Time-Turner#True_Time-Turners" title="Time-Turner">Time-Turners</a></b>: Theodore created two Time-Turners while working for <a href="/wiki/Lucius_Malfoy" title="Lucius Malfoy">Lucius Malfoy</a>, that had not been registered nor approved by the <a href="/wiki/Ministry_of_Magic" title="Ministry of Magic">Ministry</a>. Nott kept the prototype, which was made of inexpensive metal. The time-turner allowed the traveller to travel as far back as they wanted in <a href="/wiki/Time" title="Time">time</a>, but stay in the past for five-minutes before returning to the present.<sup id="cite_ref-CC_11-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-CC-11">&#91;11&#93;</a></sup></li></ul>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Theodore_Nott?veaction=edit&amp;section=12" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Theodore_Nott?action=edit&amp;section=12" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p><a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Theodore_(given_name)" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Theodore (given name)">Theodore</a> is an English name that comes from the Greek name <i>Theodros</i>, which is derived from the words <i>theos</i>, meaning "God", and <i>dōron</i>, meaning "gift". Thus, it is often considered to mean "gift of God". It was the name of many early Christian saints. The Welsh version of the name, <i>Tewdwr</i>, was anglicised to <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Tudor" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Tudor">Tudor</a>, the name of a royal dynasty of England that ended with <a href="/wiki/Elizabeth_I" title="Elizabeth I">Elizabeth I</a>. It could imply that Theodore was "God's gift" to his parents, if the version of his mother being an older woman are true and she was already out of age to get pregnant.
</p><p>In Norse mythology, <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/N%C3%B3tt" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Nótt">Nótt</a> is the personification of night. Also, <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Josiah_C._Nott" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Josiah C. Nott">Josiah C. Nott</a> was a nineteenth century American physician who advocated and popularised polygenist theories about the inherent superiority of Caucasians.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Theodore_Nott?veaction=edit&amp;section=13" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Theodore_Nott?action=edit&amp;section=13" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>Rowling created a subplot which was eventually cut from the series storyline, where Theodore and <a href="/wiki/Draco_Malfoy" title="Draco Malfoy">Draco Malfoy</a> were talking in the garden of <a href="/wiki/Malfoy_Manor" title="Malfoy Manor">Malfoy Manor</a>, which would have been one of the few times that Draco would be seen conversing with someone he regarded as an equal, as Rowling said that Theodore was just as <a href="/wiki/Pure-blood" title="Pure-blood">pure-blooded</a> as him. Rowling stated that she liked the scene so much that she tried to use it twice: first in <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Chamber_of_Secrets" title="Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets">Chamber of Secrets</a></i> and second in <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Goblet_of_Fire" title="Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire">Goblet of Fire</a></i>.<sup id="cite_ref-rowling_2-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-rowling-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li>Rowling states that she knows much more about Nott than what is revealed in the books, and expresses a similar sentiment in regards to <a href="/wiki/Dean_Thomas" title="Dean Thomas">Dean Thomas</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-rowling_2-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-rowling-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li>It is possible that Theodore Nott was portrayed by Elliot Francis, who played an unidentified boy at Slughorn's dinner party. Like the Matthew Lewis (Neville Longbottom) and Robert Knox (Marcus Belby) he was not seen as a guest at the christmas party again. In the book, all three of them (Neville, Marcus and Theodore) were not invited by Slughorn for Christmas.</li></ul>
