<b>Mr Krum</b> (fl. <a href="/wiki/1977" title="1977">1977</a>-<a href="/wiki/1995" title="1995">1995</a>) was a <a href="/wiki/Bulgarian" title="Bulgarian">Bulgarian</a> <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a>, who was the father of <a href="/wiki/Viktor_Krum" title="Viktor Krum">Viktor Krum</a>. He was described as having dark hair and a hooked nose like his son.<sup id="cite_ref-GOF31_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF31-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Mr Krum travelled to <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> with his <a href="/wiki/Viktor_Krum%27s_mother" title="Viktor Krum&#39;s mother">wife</a> to watch Viktor face the final task of the <a href="/wiki/Triwizard_Tournament" title="Triwizard Tournament">Triwizard Tournament</a> in <a href="/wiki/1995" title="1995">1995</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-GOF31_2-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF31-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Viktor told <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> that his <a href="/wiki/Viktor_Krum%27s_grandfather" title="Viktor Krum&#39;s grandfather">grandfather</a> was killed by <a href="/wiki/Gellert_Grindelwald" title="Gellert Grindelwald">Gellert Grindelwald</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-DH8_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH8-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> This man could have been either Mr Krum's father or his father-in-law.
</p>
