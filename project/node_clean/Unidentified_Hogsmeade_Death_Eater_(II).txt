This <b><a href="/wiki/Dark_Wizard" title="Dark Wizard">Dark Wizard</a></b> (d. <a href="/wiki/2_May" title="2 May">2 May</a> <a href="/wiki/1998" title="1998">1998</a>) was a <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eater</a> of <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a> who fought in the <a href="/wiki/Second_Wizarding_War" title="Second Wizarding War">Second Wizarding War</a>. He guarded <a href="/wiki/Hogsmeade" title="Hogsmeade">Hogsmeade</a> village with <a href="/wiki/Unidentified_Hogsmeade_Death_Eater_(I)" title="Unidentified Hogsmeade Death Eater (I)">another Death Eater</a> on <a href="/wiki/1_May" title="1 May">1</a> and <a href="/wiki/2_May" title="2 May">2 May</a> <a href="/wiki/1998" title="1998">1998</a>. He also fought during the Battle of Hogwarts, and manged to survive until the second part of the Battle, where he stood by <a href="/wiki/Antonin_Dolohov" title="Antonin Dolohov">Antonin Dolohov</a> watching the Hogwarts Defenders as they believed <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> was "dead". He was killed during the battle.<sup id="cite_ref-DHF2_1-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DHF2-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
