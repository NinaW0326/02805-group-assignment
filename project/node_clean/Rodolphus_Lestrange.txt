<p><b>Rodolphus Lestrange</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Pure-blood" title="Pure-blood">pure-blood</a> <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> who, with his brother <a href="/wiki/Rabastan_Lestrange" title="Rabastan Lestrange">Rabastan</a>, joined the <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a> and fought in the <a href="/wiki/First_Wizarding_War" title="First Wizarding War">First Wizarding War</a>. The <a href="/wiki/Lestrange_family" title="Lestrange family">Lestranges</a> were considered to be among <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a>'s most loyal and trusted servants and were the only ones along with Barty Crouch Jnr who searched for him after his first downfall.
</p><p>After Voldemort was defeated by Harry Potter when Harry was only a baby, Rodolphus and his wife <a href="/wiki/Bellatrix_Lestrange" title="Bellatrix Lestrange">Bellatrix</a>, along with Rabastan and <a href="/wiki/Bartemius_Crouch_Junior" title="Bartemius Crouch Junior">Barty Crouch Jnr</a> participated in the <a href="/wiki/Attack_on_Frank_and_Alice_Longbottom" title="Attack on Frank and Alice Longbottom">torture and permanent incapacitation</a> of <a href="/wiki/Alice_Longbottom" title="Alice Longbottom">Alice</a> and <a href="/wiki/Frank_Longbottom" title="Frank Longbottom">Frank Longbottom</a> and was sentenced to life imprisonment in <a href="/wiki/Azkaban" title="Azkaban">Azkaban</a>. About fourteen years later, Rodolphus escaped with a group of other Death Eaters and fought in several battles of the <a href="/wiki/Second_Wizarding_War" title="Second Wizarding War">Second Wizarding War</a>, including the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_the_Department_of_Mysteries" title="Battle of the Department of Mysteries">Battle of the Department of Mysteries</a> and the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_the_Seven_Potters" title="Battle of the Seven Potters">Battle of the Seven Potters</a>. After Lord Voldemort's final defeat, Rodolphus was imprisoned in Azkaban once more.
</p><p>In <a href="/wiki/2020" title="2020">2020</a>, he escaped from Azkaban once again, and set to look for Voldemort and Bellatrix's illegitimate daughter, <a href="/wiki/Delphini" title="Delphini">Delphini</a>, informing her of her destiny and heritage.
</p>

<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_life">Early life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Rodolphus_Lestrange?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Early life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Rodolphus_Lestrange?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Early life"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Rudolphus Lestrange was born to the <a href="/wiki/Pure-blood" title="Pure-blood">Pure-blood</a> <a href="/wiki/Lestrange_Family" class="mw-redirect" title="Lestrange Family">Lestrange Family</a>, in <a href="/wiki/Great_Britain" title="Great Britain">Great Britain</a>, prior to <a href="/wiki/1964" title="1964">1964</a>.
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Hogwarts_Years">Hogwarts Years</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Rodolphus_Lestrange?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Hogwarts Years">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Rodolphus_Lestrange?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Hogwarts Years"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>When Rodolphus attended <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a>, in the <a href="/wiki/1970s" title="1970s">1970s</a>, he was sorted into <a href="/wiki/Slytherin" title="Slytherin">Slytherin</a> house. He became friends with many other pure-blood students and, according to <a href="/wiki/Sirius_Black" title="Sirius Black">Sirius Black</a> joined a <a href="/wiki/Gang_of_Slytherins" title="Gang of Slytherins">group of friends</a> that included many future <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a> like; <a href="/wiki/Bellatrix_Lestrange" title="Bellatrix Lestrange">Bellatrix Black</a>, <a href="/wiki/Lucius_Malfoy" title="Lucius Malfoy">Lucius Malfoy</a>, <a href="/wiki/Evan_Rosier" title="Evan Rosier">Evan Rosier</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Severus_Snape" title="Severus Snape">Severus Snape</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-OTP_4-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OTP-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="First_Wizarding_War">First Wizarding War</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Rodolphus_Lestrange?veaction=edit&amp;section=4" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: First Wizarding War">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Rodolphus_Lestrange?action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: First Wizarding War"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>

<p>He and Bellatrix eventually married sometime before 1971<sup id="cite_ref-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> and both took the <a href="/wiki/Dark_Mark" title="Dark Mark">Dark Mark</a>, fighting in the <a href="/wiki/First_Wizarding_War" title="First Wizarding War">First Wizarding War</a>. She married him only to uphold <a href="/wiki/House_of_Black" title="House of Black">her family</a>'s tradition of marrying respectable <a href="/wiki/Pure-blood" title="Pure-blood">pure-bloods</a> as she was obsessively in love with <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a>. It is unknown how Rodolphus felt about her. However, the two did seem to work together effectively as Death Eaters.
</p><p>In <a href="/wiki/1981" title="1981">1981</a>, when Lord Voldemort was defeated the first time, Rodolphus and his wife refused to accept it. Along with Rodolphus' brother <a href="/wiki/Rabastan_Lestrange" title="Rabastan Lestrange">Rabastan</a> and <a href="/wiki/Bartemius_Crouch_Junior" title="Bartemius Crouch Junior">Barty Crouch Jnr</a>, they attacked <a href="/wiki/Auror" title="Auror">Aurors</a> and <a href="/wiki/Order_of_the_Phoenix" title="Order of the Phoenix">Order of the Phoenix</a> members <a href="/wiki/Alice_Longbottom" title="Alice Longbottom">Alice</a> and <a href="/wiki/Frank_Longbottom" title="Frank Longbottom">Frank Longbottom</a>. They tortured the couple with the <a href="/wiki/Cruciatus_Curse" title="Cruciatus Curse">Cruciatus Curse</a> to the point of insanity, and were subsequently captured and imprisoned in <a href="/wiki/Azkaban" title="Azkaban">Azkaban</a> prison for this crime. Upon their trial, the <a href="/wiki/Lestrange_family" title="Lestrange family">Lestranges</a> proudly declared their continuing devotion and awaited their master's return.<sup id="cite_ref-GOF_6-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Second_Wizarding_War">Second Wizarding War</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Rodolphus_Lestrange?veaction=edit&amp;section=5" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Second Wizarding War">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Rodolphus_Lestrange?action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Second Wizarding War"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>In <a href="/wiki/1996" title="1996">1996</a>, the <a href="/wiki/Dementor" title="Dementor">Dementors</a> of Azkaban revolted, and Rodolphus and Bellatrix <a href="/wiki/Azkaban_escapes#1996" title="Azkaban escapes">escaped</a> prison along with eight other Death Eaters, including his brother Rabastan to rejoin Voldemort, who had regained his powers and was stronger than before thanks to Wormtail and Barty Crouch Jnr.
</p>

</p><h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Battle_of_the_Seven_Potters">Battle of the Seven Potters</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Rodolphus_Lestrange?veaction=edit&amp;section=7" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Battle of the Seven Potters">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Rodolphus_Lestrange?action=edit&amp;section=7" title="Edit section: Battle of the Seven Potters"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<p>By <a href="/wiki/1997" title="1997">1997</a>, Rodolphus was again at large, and participated in the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_the_Seven_Potters" title="Battle of the Seven Potters">Battle of the Seven Potters</a>, pursuing <a href="/wiki/Nymphadora_Tonks" title="Nymphadora Tonks">Nymphadora Tonks</a> and <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ron Weasley</a>, who was disguised as <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> with <a href="/wiki/Polyjuice_Potion" title="Polyjuice Potion">Polyjuice Potion</a>, with his wife and other Death Eaters. Tonks injured Rodolphus at some point during the pursuit, and she and Ron escaped.<sup id="cite_ref-DH_7-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>During the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a> the <a href="/wiki/1998" title="1998">next year</a>, Rodolphus lost his <a href="/wiki/Bellatrix_Lestrange" title="Bellatrix Lestrange">wife</a>, who was killed by <a href="/wiki/Molly_Weasley" title="Molly Weasley">Molly Weasley</a>. What happened to Rodolphus remained unknown during the battle but at some point he is imprisoned again for his crimes.
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Post_War">Post War</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Rodolphus_Lestrange?veaction=edit&amp;section=8" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Post War">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Rodolphus_Lestrange?action=edit&amp;section=8" title="Edit section: Post War"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Sometime in between the war's end and <a href="/wiki/2020" title="2020">2020</a>, Rodolphus escaped from Azkaban for the third time and sought out the daughter of his former master, <a href="/wiki/Delphini" title="Delphini">Delphini</a>. Rodolphus revealed Delphi's heritage to her and it is currently unknown what has become of him, as he didn't appear to be involved with Delphini's plan to use <a href="/wiki/Albus_Potter" title="Albus Potter">Albus Potter</a> to bring back Lord Voldemort.<sup class="noprint">&#91;<a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Wikipedia:Citing_sources" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Wikipedia:Citing sources"><i><span title="The material in the vicinity of this tag needs references to reliable sources." style="white-space: nowrap;">citation needed</span></i></a>&#93;</sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Physical_appearance">Physical appearance</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Rodolphus_Lestrange?veaction=edit&amp;section=9" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Physical appearance">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Rodolphus_Lestrange?action=edit&amp;section=9" title="Edit section: Physical appearance"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Rodolphus was a tall, thickset man with very dark hair, and after being in Azkaban, he appeared thin and his looks might have diminished. As a Death Eater, he bears the Dark Mark on his left inner forearm, which has since faded to a scar upon Voldemort's death.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Personality_and_traits">Personality and traits</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Rodolphus_Lestrange?veaction=edit&amp;section=10" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Personality and traits">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Rodolphus_Lestrange?action=edit&amp;section=10" title="Edit section: Personality and traits"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Rodolphus was one of Voldemort's most loyal <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a> and therefore can be considered to be sadistic and a <a href="/wiki/Pure-blood" title="Pure-blood">pure-blood</a> supremacist. His crime against the Longbottoms confirms his willingness to use the <a href="/wiki/Dark_Arts" title="Dark Arts">Dark Arts</a> to an unspeakable extent. He might not have been concerned at all about being sent to Azkaban as he "stared blankly" throughout the course of his trial, possibly sharing Bellatrix's belief that Voldemort would return and free them. He did not show any resent towards his wife for having an affair with Voldemort, and even went onto notifying their daughter, Delphini, on her heritage and destiny. He didn't talk very often and, unlike his wife, he seemed to be an extremely serious person who barely even smiled.
</p><p>Rodolphus stood to be a reasonably skilled wizard, as he managed to use the Cruciatus Curse to devastating effects, driving Frank and Alice to insanity. He also managed to escape from Azkaban three times before <a href="/wiki/2020" title="2020">2020</a>, an unprecedented feat with apparently no outside help in the last case.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Magical_abilities_and_skills">Magical abilities and skills</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Rodolphus_Lestrange?veaction=edit&amp;section=11" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Rodolphus_Lestrange?action=edit&amp;section=11" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li><b><a href="/wiki/Dark_Arts" title="Dark Arts">Dark Arts</a>:</b> Rodolphus was able to cast an effective <a href="/wiki/Cruciatus_Curse" title="Cruciatus Curse">Cruciatus Curse</a> (which takes power, nerve, and ability to perform). He participated in driving Frank and Alice Longbottom into insanity.</li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Martial_magic" title="Martial magic">Martial magic</a>:</b> As one of Voldemort's top Death Eaters he likely took part in many battles during both wars against trained <a href="/wiki/Auror" title="Auror">aurors</a> and <a href="/wiki/Order_of_the_Phoenix" title="Order of the Phoenix">Order of the Phoenix</a> members. He was one of the Death Eaters to capture Frank and Alice Longbottom (both being immensely skilled). He participated in the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_the_Department_of_Mysteries" title="Battle of the Department of Mysteries">Battle of the Department of Mysteries</a> where he fought against the Order members who arrived, though he was defeated and captured following the battle. He also fought in the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_the_Seven_Potters" title="Battle of the Seven Potters">Battle of the Seven Potters</a> but was defeated and injured by his niece <a href="/wiki/Nymphadora_Tonks" title="Nymphadora Tonks">Nymphadora Tonks</a>. It is unknown if he was present during the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a> but if he was it is highly likely he was defeated long before his wife.</li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Flight" title="Flight">Flying</a>:</b> In the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_the_Seven_Potters" title="Battle of the Seven Potters">Battle of the Seven Potters</a>, he duelled his <a href="/wiki/Nymphadora_Tonks" title="Nymphadora Tonks">niece</a> during flight in a losing effort.</li></ul>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Relationships">Relationships</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Rodolphus_Lestrange?veaction=edit&amp;section=12" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Relationships">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Rodolphus_Lestrange?action=edit&amp;section=12" title="Edit section: Relationships"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Wife">Wife</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Rodolphus_Lestrange?veaction=edit&amp;section=13" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Wife">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Rodolphus_Lestrange?action=edit&amp;section=13" title="Edit section: Wife"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>

<p>In marrying Bellatrix Black, Rodolphus made a "<i>respectable <a href="/wiki/Pure-blood" title="Pure-blood">pure-blood</a> marriage</i>",<sup id="cite_ref-OTP_4-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OTP-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> as was expected of him. There is no indication that their relationship went beyond this. She never expressed any love or concern for him. It was Voldemort to whom she spoke in a romantic manner instead. However, she and Rodolphus did seem to work effectively together as Death Eaters. Rodolphus <a href="/wiki/Cruciatus_Curse" title="Cruciatus Curse">tortured</a> <a href="/wiki/Alice_Longbottom" title="Alice Longbottom">Alice</a> and <a href="/wiki/Frank_Longbottom" title="Frank Longbottom">Frank Longbottom</a> alongside his wife, <a href="/wiki/Rabastan_Lestrange" title="Rabastan Lestrange">brother</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Bartemius_Crouch_Junior" title="Bartemius Crouch Junior">Barty Crouch Jnr</a> in <a href="/wiki/1981" title="1981">1981</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-GOF_6-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup> He fought with Bellatrix during the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_the_Department_of_Mysteries" title="Battle of the Department of Mysteries">Battle of the Department of Mysteries</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-OTP_4-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OTP-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> He was also with her during the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_the_Seven_Potters" title="Battle of the Seven Potters">Battle of the Seven Potters</a>, as <a href="/wiki/Nymphadora_Tonks" title="Nymphadora Tonks">Nymphadora Tonks</a> mentioned cursing him and being pursued by Bellatrix.<sup id="cite_ref-DH_7-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup> Sirius Black described them as part of the same "gang of Slytherins" at Hogwarts, which also included <a href="/wiki/Lucius_Malfoy" title="Lucius Malfoy">Lucius Malfoy</a>, <a href="/wiki/Evan_Rosier" title="Evan Rosier">Evan Rosier</a> and <a href="/wiki/Severus_Snape" title="Severus Snape">Severus Snape</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-OTP_4-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OTP-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Bellatrix once told Narcissa that if she had sons, she would "<i>be glad to give them up to the service of the Dark Lord</i>".<sup id="cite_ref-HBP_8-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>No children appear beneath Bellatrix and Rodolphus' names on the <a href="/wiki/House_of_Black" title="House of Black">Black family tapestry</a>, indicating that she had no daughters with Rodolphus either. However, Roldolphus did allow Bellatrix to have access to his <a href="/wiki/Lestrange_Vault" title="Lestrange Vault">family's vault</a> at <a href="/wiki/Gringotts_Wizarding_Bank" title="Gringotts Wizarding Bank">Gringotts Wizarding Bank</a> upon marriage, as Bellatrix referred it as "[my] vault".
</p><p>It was later revealed that Bellatrix had a daughter named <a href="/wiki/Delphini" title="Delphini">Delphini</a> with <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a>. This indicates that Bellatrix didn't have much marital loyalty to Rodolphus at this time. It is entirely possible that Rodolphus approved his wife's relationship with their master, as the Lestranges worshipped Voldemort, furthering that their relationship as a couple is strictly professional.
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Stepdaughter"><a href="/wiki/Delphini" title="Delphini">Stepdaughter</a></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Rodolphus_Lestrange?veaction=edit&amp;section=14" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Stepdaughter">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Rodolphus_Lestrange?action=edit&amp;section=14" title="Edit section: Stepdaughter"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Delphi claimed that Rodolphus found her after many years of him being in prison. It is unknown what sort of relationship he had with his wife's daughter but it is possible that as Delphi was the daughter of Lord Voldemort, he may of had some level of respect for her. It is also possible that he taught her the dark arts and how to duel.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Rodolphus_Lestrange?veaction=edit&amp;section=15" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Rodolphus_Lestrange?action=edit&amp;section=15" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p><a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Rodolphus" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Rodolphus">Rodolphus</a> is the Latin form of the name 'Rudolf', which is derived from the Germanic name <i>Hrodwulf</i>, meaning "famous wolf".<sup id="cite_ref-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup> This may allude to prominent Nazi <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Rudolf_Hess" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Rudolf Hess">Rudolf Hess</a>, as the <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eater</a> ideology has similarities to those of Nazism, according to <a href="/wiki/J._K._Rowling" title="J. K. Rowling">J. K. Rowling</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-10" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-10">&#91;10&#93;</a></sup> Removing the l, Rodophus is Greek for 'Rosy-cheeked'.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Rodolphus_Lestrange?veaction=edit&amp;section=16" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Rodolphus_Lestrange?action=edit&amp;section=16" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>J.K. Rowling has revealed that <a href="/wiki/Bellatrix_Lestrange" title="Bellatrix Lestrange">Bellatrix Lestrange</a>'s true love was <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Voldemort</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-11" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-11">&#91;11&#93;</a></sup> suggesting that the Lestranges' marriage was not affectionate, at least on the part of Bellatrix, who merely made "a respectable pure-blood marriage", as was expected of her. Rodolphus' feelings are not known.</li>
<li>In <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Half-Blood_Prince" title="Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince">Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince</a></i> someone by the surname "Lestrange" is shown having gone to school with <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Tom Riddle</a>. It is unknown if this person is Rodolphus' father or another relative.</li>
<li>It's unknown if Rodolphus was aware of Voldemort giving <a href="/wiki/Helga_Hufflepuff%27s_Cup" class="mw-redirect" title="Helga Hufflepuff&#39;s Cup">Helga Hufflepuff's Cup</a> to Bellatrix, or if he was aware of its presence in his <a href="/wiki/Lestrange_Vault" title="Lestrange Vault">family vault</a>.</li>
