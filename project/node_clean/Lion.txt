<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Lions_in_the_wizarding_world">Lions in the wizarding world</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Lion?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Lions in the wizarding world">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Lion?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Lions in the wizarding world"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>It is the mascot of <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor" title="Gryffindor">Gryffindor</a> <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Houses" title="Hogwarts Houses">House</a> at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-PM_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PM-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> as it represents courage and chivalry, the two most pronounced traits of a Gryffindor. In addition, <a href="/wiki/Luna_Lovegood" title="Luna Lovegood">Luna Lovegood</a> made <a href="/wiki/Luna_Lovegood%27s_lion_hat" class="mw-redirect" title="Luna Lovegood&#39;s lion hat">a hat in the shape of a lion's head</a>, and would wear it at all <a href="/wiki/Quidditch" title="Quidditch">Quidditch</a> games between Gryffindor and <a href="/wiki/Slytherin" title="Slytherin">Slytherin Houses</a>, to show her support for Gryffindor, over Slytherin.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP19_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP19-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Many <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magical</a> <a href="/wiki/Beast" title="Beast">beasts</a> have the physical features of lions. <a href="/wiki/Sphinx" title="Sphinx">Sphinxes</a> and <a href="/wiki/Manticore" title="Manticore">Manticores</a> are magical beasts that have the body of a lion and the head of a <a href="/wiki/Human" title="Human">human</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-GF31_4-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GF31-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/Chimaera" title="Chimaera">Chimaeras</a> have the head of a lion, the body of a <a href="/wiki/Goat" title="Goat">goat</a>, and the tail of a <a href="/wiki/Dragon" title="Dragon">dragon</a>. <a href="/wiki/Griffin" title="Griffin">Griffins</a> have bodies that are part lion and part <a href="/wiki/Eagle" title="Eagle">eagle</a>. <a href="/wiki/Kneazle" title="Kneazle">Kneazles</a> have a lightly plumed tail that resembles a lion's.<sup id="cite_ref-FBR_5-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-FBR-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/Zouwu" title="Zouwu">Zouwus</a> have a mane that resembles a lion's.<sup id="cite_ref-COGS_6-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COGS-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>It is a possible corporeal form of the <a href="/wiki/Patronus_Charm" title="Patronus Charm">Patronus Charm</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/Patricia_Rakepick" title="Patricia Rakepick">Patricia Rakepick</a>'s Patronus took the form of a lioness.<sup id="cite_ref-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="History">History</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Lion?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: History">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Lion?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: History"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<figure class="thumb tright show-info-icon" style="width: 182px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/7/79/Lion_-_Fantastic_Beasts.png/revision/latest?cb=20200416060009" class="image"><img alt="" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw%3D%3D" decoding="async" width="180" height="179" class="thumbimage lazyload" data-image-name="Lion - Fantastic Beasts.png" data-image-key="Lion_-_Fantastic_Beasts.png" data-src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/7/79/Lion_-_Fantastic_Beasts.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/180?cb=20200416060009" /></a> 	<noscript><a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/7/79/Lion_-_Fantastic_Beasts.png/revision/latest?cb=20200416060009" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/7/79/Lion_-_Fantastic_Beasts.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/180?cb=20200416060009" decoding="async" width="180" height="179" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="Lion - Fantastic Beasts.png" data-image-key="Lion_-_Fantastic_Beasts.png" data-src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/7/79/Lion_-_Fantastic_Beasts.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/180?cb=20200416060009" /></a></noscript> 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:Lion_-_Fantastic_Beasts.png" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption">An escaped Lion from Central Park Zoo</p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure>
<p>On <a href="/wiki/6_December" title="6 December">6 December</a> <a href="/wiki/1926" title="1926">1926</a>, a lion escaped from the <a href="/wiki/Central_Park_Zoo" title="Central Park Zoo">Central Park Zoo</a> in <a href="/wiki/New_York" title="New York">New York</a> as a results of damages done by the <a href="/wiki/Erumpent" title="Erumpent">Erumpent</a> set loose from <a href="/wiki/Newton_Scamander%27s_suitcase" title="Newton Scamander&#39;s suitcase">Newton Scamander's suitcase</a>. It was seen later wandering the streets of the <a href="/wiki/Diamond_District" title="Diamond District">Diamond District</a> by <a href="/wiki/Newton_Scamander" title="Newton Scamander">Newton Scamander</a>, <a href="/wiki/Jacob_Kowalski" title="Jacob Kowalski">Jacob Kowalski</a>, and the <a href="/wiki/New_York_Police_Department" title="New York Police Department">New York Police Department</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-10" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-10">&#91;10&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>At the <a href="/wiki/1994_Quidditch_World_Cup" title="1994 Quidditch World Cup">1994 Quidditch World Cup</a>, the scarves for those supporting <a href="/wiki/Bulgarian_National_Quidditch_team" title="Bulgarian National Quidditch team">Bulgaria</a> were adorned with lions that really roared.<sup id="cite_ref-GF7_11-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GF7-11">&#91;11&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Later <a href="/wiki/1994" title="1994">that year</a>, while introducing a dance lesson for the <a href="/wiki/Yule_Ball" title="Yule Ball">Yule Ball</a>, <a href="/wiki/Professor" title="Professor">Professor</a> <a href="/wiki/Minerva_McGonagall" title="Minerva McGonagall">McGonagall</a> stated that inside every boy was a lordly lion prepared to prance.<sup id="cite_ref-GOFfilm_12-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOFfilm-12">&#91;12&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>In the summer of <a href="/wiki/1996" title="1996">1996</a>, when observing him for the first time, the <a href="/wiki/Prime_Minister" title="Prime Minister">Prime Minister</a> thought that <a href="/wiki/Rufus_Scrimgeour" title="Rufus Scrimgeour">Rufus Scrimgeour</a> looked rather like an old lion.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP1_13-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP1-13">&#91;13&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
