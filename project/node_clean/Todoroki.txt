</p><p><b>Todoroki</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Japan" title="Japan">Japanese</a> <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> who played as the <a href="/wiki/Keeper" title="Keeper">Keeper</a> for the <a href="/wiki/Japanese_National_Quidditch_team" title="Japanese National Quidditch team">Japanese National Quidditch team</a> during the <a href="/wiki/2014_Quidditch_World_Cup" title="2014 Quidditch World Cup">2014 Quidditch World Cup</a>.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Todoroki?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Todoroki?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>During the match between Japan and the <a href="/wiki/American_National_Quidditch_team" title="American National Quidditch team">American National Quidditch team</a>, the Americans only managed to get twelve goals past Todoroki, leading <i><a href="/wiki/Daily_Prophet" title="Daily Prophet">Daily Prophet</a></i> sportswriter <a href="/wiki/Ginevra_Weasley" title="Ginevra Weasley">Ginny Potter</a> to declare Todoroki an "undersung hero of the Japanese side who was on superb form" in her report of the match.<sup id="cite_ref-PM_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PM-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
