<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Stunned_Death_Eater?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Stunned_Death_Eater?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Born in or before the <a href="/wiki/1980s" title="1980s">1980s</a>, most likely to a <a href="/wiki/Pure-blood" title="Pure-blood">pure</a> or <a href="/wiki/Half-blood" title="Half-blood">half-blood</a> family, he procured a <a href="/wiki/Wand" title="Wand">wand</a> and was trained in the <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magical</a> arts, possibly at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a>.
</p><p>He met <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Tom Riddle</a> sometime before <a href="/wiki/May" title="May">May</a> , and formally became a <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eater</a> and one of his loyal servants.
</p><p>On the <a href="/wiki/1_May" title="1 May">1 May</a> in <a href="/wiki/1998" title="1998">1998</a>, this Death Eater was called upon to fight in the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a> in <a href="/wiki/Scotland" title="Scotland">Scotland</a>.
</p><p>Sometime throughout the battle, he was <a href="/wiki/Stunning_Spell" title="Stunning Spell">stunned</a> by <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Viaduct_Courtyard" title="Viaduct Courtyard">Viaduct Courtyard</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>His fate is unclear, though it is probable that he was killed in battle, abandoned the battle or else captured and imprisoned in <a href="/wiki/Azkaban" title="Azkaban">Azkaban</a> for his crimes as a Death Eater.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Physical_appearance">Physical appearance</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Stunned_Death_Eater?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Physical appearance">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Stunned_Death_Eater?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Physical appearance"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>This man had a small black moustache, short black hair, and narrow eyes. He was of an average height and build, with Caucasian skin flushed from the battle. He wore a pair of black boots and a black cape, as well as a shirt that, right before he was Stunned, shone brightly.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Personality_and_traits">Personality and traits</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Stunned_Death_Eater?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Personality and traits">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Stunned_Death_Eater?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Personality and traits"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>If his membership as a Death Eater reveals his personality, he was a cruel, sadistic individual and a <a href="/wiki/Pure-blood_supremacy" title="Pure-blood supremacy">pure-blood supremacist</a>, with him also being cunning, yet not entirely as intelligent as other Death Eaters, as he was easily stunned by Harry Potter off-guard, implying he was perhaps somewhat inattentive.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Magical_abilities_and_skills">Magical abilities and skills</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Stunned_Death_Eater?veaction=edit&amp;section=4" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Stunned_Death_Eater?action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li><b><a href="/wiki/Dark_Arts" title="Dark Arts">Dark Arts</a></b>: Being a Death Eater, he would have been an accomplished <a href="/wiki/Dark_Wizard" title="Dark Wizard">Dark Wizard</a> who was highly proficient in dark magic and with the casting of <a href="/wiki/Dark_charm" title="Dark charm">dark charms</a>, most likely including the <a href="/wiki/Unforgivable_Curses" title="Unforgivable Curses">Unforgivable Curses</a>.</li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Duelling" title="Duelling">Duelling</a></b>: As this Death Eater fought in the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a> against the defenders of <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts</a>, he would have been a fairly competent duellist with some skill in <a href="/wiki/Martial_magic" title="Martial magic">martial magic</a>, but he was not skilled enough to avoid being <a href="/wiki/Stunning_Spell" title="Stunning Spell">stunned</a> off-guard by Harry Potter, a far superior duellist.</li></ul>
