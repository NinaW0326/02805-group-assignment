<b>Agnes Monkleigh</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch</a> and <a href="/wiki/Slytherin" title="Slytherin">Slytherin</a> student at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> in the <a href="/wiki/1990s" title="1990s">1990s</a>. During the <a href="/wiki/1996%E2%80%931997_school_year" title="1996–1997 school year">1996–1997 school year</a>, she blocked one of the exits in the <a href="/wiki/Herbology_Store" title="Herbology Store">Herbology Store</a>, forcing <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> to beat her in a <a href="/wiki/Duelling" title="Duelling">duel</a> in order to get past.<sup id="cite_ref-HBPG_3-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBPG-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<p>The name "Agnes" is the Latinized form of the Greek name ‘Αγνη (Hagne), derived from Greek ‘αγνος (hagnos) meaning "chaste".
