<b>Aidan Kiely</b> was an <a href="/wiki/Ireland" title="Ireland">Irish</a> <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> who played as the <a href="/wiki/Seeker" title="Seeker">Seeker</a> for the <a href="/wiki/Kenmare_Kestrels" title="Kenmare Kestrels">Kenmare Kestrels</a> <a href="/wiki/Quidditch" title="Quidditch">Quidditch</a> <a href="/wiki/Quidditch_team" title="Quidditch team">team</a> during the <a href="/wiki/1990s" title="1990s">1990s</a><sup id="cite_ref-:0_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:0-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>. In a game against the <a href="/wiki/Montrose_Magpies" title="Montrose Magpies">Montrose Magpies</a>, Aidan managed to catch the Snitch against opposing seeker <a href="/wiki/Alasdair_Maddock" title="Alasdair Maddock">Alasdair Maddock</a> when the former attempted to use Muggle Football techniques during the game<sup id="cite_ref-:0_2-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:0-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>.
</p>
