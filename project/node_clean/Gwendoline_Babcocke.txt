<b>Gwendoline Babcocke</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Scotland" title="Scotland">Scottish</a> <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch</a> that worked as a shop assistant at <a href="/wiki/The_Magic_Neep" title="The Magic Neep">The Magic Neep</a> in <a href="/wiki/Hogsmeade" title="Hogsmeade">Hogsmeade</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-FBC7_1-10" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-FBC7-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Gwendoline_Babcocke?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Gwendoline_Babcocke?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>She received several notes and gifts such as chocolates and lavender perfume from an anonymous admirer, including an night-time invitation to the <a href="/wiki/Boathouse" title="Boathouse">Boathouse</a> at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> which caught <a href="/wiki/Fire" title="Fire">fire</a> just as she arrived.<sup id="cite_ref-FBC7_1-11" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-FBC7-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Enamoured with <a href="/wiki/Dragon" title="Dragon">dragons</a>, she owned a copy of <i><a href="/wiki/Dragon_Breeding_for_Pleasure_and_Profit" title="Dragon Breeding for Pleasure and Profit">Dragon Breeding for Pleasure and Profit</a></i> but would never leave Hogsmeade to work with them as it would break <a href="/wiki/Gwendoline_Babcocke%27s_mother" title="Gwendoline Babcocke&#39;s mother">her mum</a>'s heart. In addition to her interest in <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magical</a> <a href="/wiki/Beast" title="Beast">beasts</a>, she said she was a natural at the <a href="/wiki/Stinging_Jinx" title="Stinging Jinx">Stinging Jinx</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-FBC7_1-12" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-FBC7-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
