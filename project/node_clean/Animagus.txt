<p>An <b>Animagus</b> (pl. <i>Animagi</i>) was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch or wizard</a> who could <a href="/wiki/Transformation" title="Transformation">transform</a> themselves into an animal and back again at will. It was a learned skill, unlike the hereditary skill of a <a href="/wiki/Metamorphmagus" title="Metamorphmagus">Metamorphmagus</a>. Information on Animagi was taught to <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_students" class="mw-redirect" title="Hogwarts students">Hogwarts students</a> during their <a href="/wiki/Third_year" title="Third year">third year</a> <a href="/wiki/Transfiguration_(class)" title="Transfiguration (class)">Transfiguration class</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-POA6_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-POA6-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> An <a href="/wiki/Animagus_Registry" title="Animagus Registry">Animagus Registry</a> existed to keep track of those that had learned to transform in this way.<sup id="cite_ref-POA18_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-POA18-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Acquiring_the_skill">Acquiring the skill</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Animagus?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Acquiring the skill">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Animagus?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Acquiring the skill"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>It took skill, practice, and patience for wizards and witches to become Animagi. The process of becoming an Animagus was long and arduous, and had the potential to backfire and cause the transformation to go horribly wrong. Many witches and wizards simply felt that their time might better be employed in other ways, as the skill was of limited practical use unless one had a great need of disguise or concealment.<sup id="cite_ref-TBB_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-TBB-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Part of the process by which one became an Animagus was holding the <a href="/wiki/Mandrake_leaf" title="Mandrake leaf">leaf</a> of a <a href="/wiki/Mandrake" title="Mandrake">mandrake</a> in their mouth for an entire month, using the leaf for the creation of a potion, reciting an incantation (<i><a href="/wiki/Animagus_Spell" title="Animagus Spell">Amato Animo Animato Animagus</a></i>) on a daily basis,<sup id="cite_ref-PM_4-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PM-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-PPHA_5-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PPHA-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> and drinking the <a href="/wiki/Animagus_potion" title="Animagus potion">Animagus potion</a> during a lightning storm. Once the initial training was over, an Animagus could then change at will, with or without the use of a <a href="/wiki/Wand" title="Wand">wand</a>. Animagi who took on the form of species capable of <a href="/wiki/Flight" title="Flight">Flight</a> could enjoy the sensation without the need of <a href="/wiki/Broomstick" title="Broomstick">broomsticks</a>, <a href="/wiki/Flying_carpet" title="Flying carpet">flying carpet</a>, or riding on flying animals.<sup id="cite_ref-Q_6-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Q-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup> It was debated (but generally believed possible) for one's Animagus form to be a <a href="/wiki/Beast" title="Beast">magical creature</a>, and also debated (but generally believed impossible) for an Animagus to be a <a href="/wiki/Dementor" title="Dementor">dementor</a>.
</p>
<figure class="thumb tleft show-info-icon" style="width: 252px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/0/00/Padfoot.png/revision/latest?cb=20131205235724" class="image"><img alt="" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw%3D%3D" decoding="async" width="250" height="140" class="thumbimage lazyload" data-image-name="Padfoot.png" data-image-key="Padfoot.png" data-src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/0/00/Padfoot.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/250?cb=20131205235724" /></a> 	<noscript><a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/0/00/Padfoot.png/revision/latest?cb=20131205235724" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/0/00/Padfoot.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/250?cb=20131205235724" decoding="async" width="250" height="140" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="Padfoot.png" data-image-key="Padfoot.png" data-src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/0/00/Padfoot.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/250?cb=20131205235724" /></a></noscript> 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:Padfoot.png" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption"><a href="/wiki/Sirius_Black" title="Sirius Black">Sirius Black</a> as "Padfoot," one of the four known (modern) unregistered Animagi</p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure>
<p>The process was extremely difficult and could result in disaster (such as permanent half-human, half-animal mutations) if done incorrectly. A witch or wizard had to keep a single mandrake leaf in their mouth for an entire month (from full moon to full moon). If the leaf was removed or swallowed, the witch or wizard would have to start over again. At the next visible full moon (if the night happened to be cloudy, one would have to start over) the wizard had to spit the leaf in a phial within range of the moon's pure rays. To the moon-struck phial, the wizard or witch must add one of their own hairs, a silver teaspoon of dew that had not seen sunlight or been touched by human feet for seven days, and the chrysalis of a <a href="/wiki/Death%27s-head_Hawk_Moth" title="Death&#39;s-head Hawk Moth">Death's-head Hawk Moth</a>. The mixture had to then be put in a quiet, dark place, and could not be in any way disturbed.<sup id="cite_ref-PP_7-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PP-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>The next thing that had to happen was for the wizard to wait for an electrical storm, whenever that might be. During this waiting period the wizard would have to, at sunrise and sundown without fail, chant the incantation <i><a href="/wiki/Animagus_Spell" title="Animagus Spell">Amato Animo Animato Animagus</a></i> with the tip of their wand placed over the heart. When, at last, there was a lightning storm, the wizard had to move immediately to a large and secure place, recite the incantation one final time, and then drink the potion.<sup id="cite_ref-PP1_8-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PP1-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Teaching">Teaching</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Animagus?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Teaching">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Animagus?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Teaching"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>At <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a>, the theory of Animagus transfiguration was taught about in <a href="/wiki/Third_year" title="Third year">third year</a>. When <a href="/wiki/Minerva_McGonagall" title="Minerva McGonagall">Minerva McGonagall</a>, an Animagus herself, was the teacher, it also included a practical demonstration. Talented and determined students could acquire the skill during their school years if they wished to. Minerva had become an Animagus by the age of seventeen, being taught by <a href="/wiki/Albus_Dumbledore" title="Albus Dumbledore">Professor Dumbledore</a>, who made a note within <i><a href="/wiki/The_Tales_of_Beedle_the_Bard_(real)" title="The Tales of Beedle the Bard (real)">The Tales of Beedle the Bard</a></i> to explain for her that, while Animagi were usually people who saught to avoid punishment when they broke the law, she merely became one to further her studies in Transfiguration. She learned to become an Animagus under Dumbledore's tutelage during her time as a student.<sup id="cite_ref-TBB_3-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-TBB-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>At <a href="/wiki/Uagadou_School_of_Magic" title="Uagadou School of Magic">Uagadou School of Magic</a>, a school known for its focus on self-transfiguration, students could become Animagi by the age of fourteen.<sup id="cite_ref-pottermore-uagadou_9-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-pottermore-uagadou-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Details">Details</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Animagus?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Details">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Animagus?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Details"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<figure class="thumb tright show-info-icon" style="width: 205px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/0/0c/Scabbers.JPG/revision/latest?cb=20080813001357" class="image"><img alt="" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw%3D%3D" decoding="async" width="203" height="149" class="thumbimage lazyload" data-image-name="Scabbers.JPG" data-image-key="Scabbers.JPG" data-src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/0/0c/Scabbers.JPG/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/203?cb=20080813001357" /></a> 	<noscript><a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/0/0c/Scabbers.JPG/revision/latest?cb=20080813001357" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/0/0c/Scabbers.JPG/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/203?cb=20080813001357" decoding="async" width="203" height="149" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="Scabbers.JPG" data-image-key="Scabbers.JPG" data-src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/0/0c/Scabbers.JPG/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/203?cb=20080813001357" /></a></noscript> 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:Scabbers.JPG" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption"><a href="/wiki/Peter_Pettigrew" title="Peter Pettigrew">Peter Pettigrew</a> as "Wormtail", one of the four known (modern) unregistered Animagi, known also as Scabbers in his rat form</p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure>
<p>An Animagus could not choose their animal form. Rather, one became the animal which suited them best.<sup id="cite_ref-accio_10-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-accio-10">&#91;10&#93;</a></sup> It may be that a wizard's Animagus form was always the same as their full-bodied Patronus; <a href="/wiki/Minerva_McGonagall" title="Minerva McGonagall">Minerva McGonagall</a> was one such case, both being a known <a href="/wiki/Tabby_Cat" class="mw-redirect" title="Tabby Cat">cat</a> Animagus and having a cat Patronus.<sup id="cite_ref-DH30_11-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH30-11">&#91;11&#93;</a></sup> Apparently, the form one's Patronus took may have been determined by the same inner-traits as the Animagus form.
</p><p>An Animagus in animal form was not restrained by the usual lifespan of the animal they were. Animagus <a href="/wiki/Peter_Pettigrew" title="Peter Pettigrew">Peter Pettigrew</a> lived as a <a href="/wiki/Rat" title="Rat">rat</a> for at least twelve years, despite rodents normally living at most a few years.<sup id="cite_ref-POA19_12-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-POA19-12">&#91;12&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Each Animagus bore an identifying mark on their animal form that was caused by some visible trait on their human body. This could be physical, like their dental structure, or acquired, like glasses. Both Minerva McGonagall and <a href="/wiki/Rita_Skeeter" title="Rita Skeeter">Rita Skeeter</a> had markings on their Animagus forms caused by their spectacles. If an Animagus underwent a major physical change, such as the loss of a limb, their animal form would reflect that, as in the case of Peter Pettigrew missing a finger, and his rat form missing a toe.<sup id="cite_ref-POA19_12-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-POA19-12">&#91;12&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>An Animagus could consciously choose if they also wanted to transfigure their jewellery and clothing with them.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Nature">Nature</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Animagus?veaction=edit&amp;section=4" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Nature">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Animagus?action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Nature"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The difference between <a href="/wiki/Transfiguration" title="Transfiguration">Transfiguration</a> and the Animagus transformation ability was that an Animagus could change into an animal whenever they want, without a wand or an incantation. Being an Animagus was an ability, and Transfiguring required a spell. An Animagus would still think as a human does when they were in their animal form, seeing as Rita Skeeter was able to eavesdrop on others' conversations when she was in the form of a <a href="/wiki/Beetle" title="Beetle">beetle</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-GOF37_13-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF37-13">&#91;13&#93;</a></sup> However, an Animagus' feelings were not as complex when they were in their animal form. A <a href="/wiki/Dementor" title="Dementor">Dementor</a>'s influence on an Animagus in its animal form was weaker than on its human form.<sup id="cite_ref-POA21_14-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-POA21-14">&#91;14&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<figure class="thumb tright show-info-icon" style="width: 252px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/6/63/Padfoot_to_Sirius_Black.gif/revision/latest?cb=20180601011616" class="image"><img alt="" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw%3D%3D" decoding="async" width="250" height="90" class="thumbimage lazyload" data-image-name="Padfoot to Sirius Black.gif" data-image-key="Padfoot_to_Sirius_Black.gif" data-src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/6/63/Padfoot_to_Sirius_Black.gif/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/250?cb=20180601011616" /></a> 	<noscript><a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/6/63/Padfoot_to_Sirius_Black.gif/revision/latest?cb=20180601011616" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/6/63/Padfoot_to_Sirius_Black.gif/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/250?cb=20180601011616" decoding="async" width="250" height="90" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="Padfoot to Sirius Black.gif" data-image-key="Padfoot_to_Sirius_Black.gif" data-src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/6/63/Padfoot_to_Sirius_Black.gif/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/250?cb=20180601011616" /></a></noscript> 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:Padfoot_to_Sirius_Black.gif" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption"><a href="/wiki/Sirius_Black" title="Sirius Black">Sirius Black</a> transforms back to his human form</p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure>
<p>Animagi also had the ability to communicate with normal animals. There seemed to be a clear understanding of want and need in this ability, but the full extent and quality is unknown. <a href="/wiki/Sirius_Black" title="Sirius Black">Sirius Black</a>'s Animagus form, a black <a href="/wiki/Dog" title="Dog">dog</a> named Padfoot or Snuffles, was able to communicate on a rudimentary level with <a href="/wiki/Crookshanks" title="Crookshanks">Crookshanks</a>, <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione Granger</a>'s part-<a href="/wiki/Kneazle" title="Kneazle">kneazle</a> cat. As well, <a href="/wiki/Peter_Pettigrew" title="Peter Pettigrew">Peter Pettigrew</a>'s Animagus form, a grey rat named Wormtail or Scabbers, was able to communicate on the same level with passing rats that gave him hints and directions as he travelled around Albania in search of <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Voldemort</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-POA19_12-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-POA19-12">&#91;12&#93;</a></sup> The precise form of communication is yet unknown, though it was probably not telepathy since Sirius did not know Crookshanks' name until Hermione told him.
</p>
<figure class="thumb tleft show-info-icon" style="width: 252px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/1/15/Animagus_reversal_spell_GIF.gif/revision/latest?cb=20180607225736" class="image"><img alt="" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw%3D%3D" decoding="async" width="250" height="103" class="thumbimage lazyload" data-image-name="Animagus reversal spell GIF.gif" data-image-key="Animagus_reversal_spell_GIF.gif" data-src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/1/15/Animagus_reversal_spell_GIF.gif/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/250?cb=20180607225736" /></a> 	<noscript><a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/1/15/Animagus_reversal_spell_GIF.gif/revision/latest?cb=20180607225736" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/1/15/Animagus_reversal_spell_GIF.gif/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/250?cb=20180607225736" decoding="async" width="250" height="103" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="Animagus reversal spell GIF.gif" data-image-key="Animagus_reversal_spell_GIF.gif" data-src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/1/15/Animagus_reversal_spell_GIF.gif/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/250?cb=20180607225736" /></a></noscript> 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:Animagus_reversal_spell_GIF.gif" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption"><a href="/wiki/Peter_Pettigrew" title="Peter Pettigrew">Peter Pettigrew</a> is transformed back into his human form</p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure>
<p>There was a <a href="/wiki/Animagus_reversal_spell" title="Animagus reversal spell">reversal spell</a> which created a blue and white flash that would force an Animagus out of its animal form. <a href="/wiki/Remus_Lupin" title="Remus Lupin">Remus Lupin</a> and Sirius Black once used it to force Peter Pettigrew out of his rat form.<sup id="cite_ref-POA19_12-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-POA19-12">&#91;12&#93;</a></sup> This spell could possibly be the <a href="/wiki/Homorphus_Charm" title="Homorphus Charm">Homorphus Charm</a> that <a href="/wiki/Gilderoy_Lockhart" title="Gilderoy Lockhart">Gilderoy Lockhart</a> claimed he used on the <a href="/wiki/Wagga_Wagga_Werewolf" title="Wagga Wagga Werewolf">Wagga Wagga Werewolf</a> to force him into his human form.<sup id="cite_ref-COS10_15-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COS10-15">&#91;15&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>It was also important to note that <a href="/wiki/Werewolf" title="Werewolf">werewolves</a> were fundamentally different from Animagi. <a href="/wiki/Lycanthropy" title="Lycanthropy">Lycanthropy</a> was caused by a blood infection transmitted through being bitten by another werewolf, whereas the Animagus skill was learned. The werewolves could not keep their minds after a transformation (unless they took the <a href="/wiki/Wolfsbane_Potion" title="Wolfsbane Potion">Wolfsbane Potion</a>), nor could they control themselves when they transformed, while Animagi could do both. However, as werewolves only harmed humans, Animagi, while in their animal forms, were safe to be in the company of werewolves. They could actually help calm them down and make the transformations easier, as demonstrated with the <a href="/wiki/Marauders" title="Marauders">Marauders:</a> three of them transformed to help the fourth, who was inflicted with such a curse.<sup id="cite_ref-POA18_2-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-POA18-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Wizarding_law">Wizarding law</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Animagus?veaction=edit&amp;section=5" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Wizarding law">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Animagus?action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Wizarding law"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<div class="quote">
<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire">"<i>You know that I can disguise myself most effectively.</i>"</span></dd>
<dd>&#8212;<a href="/wiki/Peter_Pettigrew" title="Peter Pettigrew">Peter Pettigrew</a> speaking about his advantage of being unregistered<sup class="noprint"><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Goblet_of_Fire" title="Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire">[src]</a></sup></dd>
<dd></dd></dl></div>
<p>Every Animagus was required to register with the <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">Ministry of Magic</a> at the <a href="/wiki/Animagus_Registry" title="Animagus Registry">Animagus Registry</a>. This registration included revealing one's animal form and one's distinguishing markings. The register was available to the public. The primary reason for registration was to ensure the Animagi did not abuse their abilities, as the Ministry would be able to keep track of them.<sup id="cite_ref-POA18_2-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-POA18-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> A prime example was that <a href="/wiki/Rita_Skeeter" title="Rita Skeeter">Rita Skeeter</a> was able to get into <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts</a> undetected (despite being banned from Hogwarts grounds by Dumbledore) by transforming into a beetle.<sup id="cite_ref-GOF37_13-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF37-13">&#91;13&#93;</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/Sirius_Black" title="Sirius Black">Sirius Black</a> was able to escape from <a href="/wiki/Azkaban" title="Azkaban">Azkaban</a> and infiltrate <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Castle" title="Hogwarts Castle">Hogwarts Castle</a> in his Animagus form, as the Ministry did not take this into account when imprisoning him. Peter Pettigrew managed to fake his death and hide from the rest of the world in his Animagus form, as the Ministry did not take this into account either since it would help them consider the possibility that he was alive;<sup id="cite_ref-POA19_12-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-POA19-12">&#91;12&#93;</a></sup> Pettigrew regarded this advantage as an effective disguise to the <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Dark Lord</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-GOF1_16-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF1-16">&#91;16&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>The penalty for failing to register as an Animagus was a sentence in <a href="/wiki/Azkaban" title="Azkaban">Azkaban</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-arrest_17-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-arrest-17">&#91;17&#93;</a></sup> although the length of time is unknown. Rita Skeeter gave up an entire year of writing articles, her main means of livelihood, so that <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione Granger</a> would not turn her in as unregistered, and consented to writing an article about Harry's account of the night <a href="/wiki/Lord" title="Lord">Lord</a> <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Voldemort</a> was resurrected, free of charge at Hermione's request.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP25_18-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP25-18">&#91;18&#93;</a></sup> That suggests a rather lengthy sentence, even though being sent to Azkaban for any length of time was a considerably bad punishment to begin with.
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Animagus_Registry">Animagus Registry</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Animagus?veaction=edit&amp;section=6" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Animagus Registry">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Animagus?action=edit&amp;section=6" title="Edit section: Animagus Registry"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Due to the complexity of the magic involved, the time it took, and the limited use (for those who were registered) of the ability, Animagi eventually became very rare. There was a total of seven known registered Animagi in the twentieth century, among who there was <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts</a>' <a href="/wiki/Transfiguration" title="Transfiguration">Transiguration</a> <a href="/wiki/Professor" title="Professor">Professor</a> <a href="/wiki/Minerva_McGonagall" title="Minerva McGonagall">Minerva McGonagall</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-POA6_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-POA6-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> According to Remus Lupin, it was also partially because of this complexity that Animagi were required to register themselves.<sup id="cite_ref-POA18_2-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-POA18-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Known_unregistered_Animagi">Known unregistered Animagi</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Animagus?veaction=edit&amp;section=7" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Known unregistered Animagi">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Animagus?action=edit&amp;section=7" title="Edit section: Known unregistered Animagi"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>On the night of June 6th, 1994, Remus Lupin stated that the Ministry "<i>never knew that there used to be three unregistered Animagi running around Hogwarts</i>", the three being his schoolmates Sirius Black, Peter Pettigrew and the late James Potter;<sup id="cite_ref-POA18_2-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-POA18-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> Black's, Pettigrew's and Potter's Animagi nature was revealed by Remus Lupin to Harry Potter, Hermione Granger and Ron Weasley in the <a href="/wiki/Shrieking_Shack" title="Shrieking Shack">Shrieking Shank</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-POA18_2-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-POA18-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> and by Sirius Black himself to Albus Dumbledore in <a href="/wiki/Filius_Flitwick%27s_office" title="Filius Flitwick&#39;s office">Professor Flitwick's office</a> on the same night.<sup id="cite_ref-POA22_19-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-POA22-19">&#91;19&#93;</a></sup> The existence of a fourth unregistered Animagus, <a href="/wiki/Rita_Skeeter" title="Rita Skeeter">Rita Skeeter</a>, was spotted by <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione Granger</a> about a year later,<sup id="cite_ref-GOF37_13-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF37-13">&#91;13&#93;</a></sup> and revealed to <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> and <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ron Weasley</a> in June 1995 (on the <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Express" title="Hogwarts Express">Hogwarts Express</a> returning to London) and, indirectly, to <a href="/wiki/Luna_Lovegood" title="Luna Lovegood">Luna Lovegood</a> on February 14th, 1996 (at the <a href="/wiki/Three_Broomsticks_Inn" title="Three Broomsticks Inn">Three Broomsticks Inn</a>).<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP25_18-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP25-18">&#91;18&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>The 2579th edition of <i><a href="/wiki/Transfiguration_Today" title="Transfiguration Today">Transfiguration Today</a></i> contained a guide to identifying unregistered Animagi.<sup id="cite_ref-FBF_20-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-FBF-20">&#91;20&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Animagus?veaction=edit&amp;section=12" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Animagus?action=edit&amp;section=12" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p><i>Animagus</i> is a combination of the word "animal" and the Latin word <i>magus</i>, meaning "animal <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a>."
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Animagus?veaction=edit&amp;section=13" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Animagus?action=edit&amp;section=13" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>According to <a href="/wiki/Wizards%27_Ordinary_Magic_and_Basic_Aptitude_Test" title="Wizards&#39; Ordinary Magic and Basic Aptitude Test">W.O.M.B.A.T.</a>, an Animagus transformation is two of the following: a kind of Self-<a href="/wiki/Transfiguration" title="Transfiguration">Transfiguration</a>, a kind of Self-<a href="/wiki/Charm" title="Charm">Charm</a>, or a kind of magic that is neither Transfiguration nor Charm. You are asked to pick the statement that is incorrect. This would mean Animagus transformation is either a kind of Self-Transfiguration but not actually a Transfiguration or Charm, a kind of Self-Charm but not actually a Transfiguration or Charm, or a kind of Self-Transfiguration and Charms/
<ul><li>Applying college-level logic on this, it would be possible for the Animagus transformation to be both a Self-Charm and a Self-Transfiguration because if either the Self-Transfiguration or the Self-Charm statements were wrong, you would have been left with two conflicting statements, one that affirms the status of this and one that specifically denies the affirmation.</li></ul></li>
<li>An Animagus does not know what his or her animal form will be prior to the transformation.<sup id="cite_ref-cubreporter_28-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-cubreporter-28">&#91;28&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li>Albus Dumbledore indirectly stated that the Marauders never revealed to anyone, the <a href="/wiki/Order_of_the_Phoenix" title="Order of the Phoenix">Order of the Phoenix</a> included, that three of them became Animagi until mid-1994, when Sirius Black told the entire story to him.<sup id="cite_ref-POA22_19-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-POA22-19">&#91;19&#93;</a></sup> It is unknown if <a href="/wiki/Lily_J._Potter" title="Lily J. Potter">Lily Evans</a> knew it, but as she married James Potter, she supposedly did.
<ul><li>It is worth noting that James Potter saved Severus Snape from Remus Lupin <i>without</i> transforming himself, because Snape told to Dumbledore about Lupin being a werewolf, but never mentioned Potter being an Animagus, as can be deduced by the fact that Dumbledore, as stated above, didn't know it.</li></ul></li>
<li>It is not known if the registration system is used worldwide, or just by some countries.
<ul><li>Interestingly, the first four known Animagi prior to the registration system all took the form of birds (Falco Aesalon, Morrigan, Morgan le Fay and Cliodna). The two known registered Animagi both become cats (Minerva McGonagall and an unknown witch).</li></ul></li>
<li>Given an Animagus form is determined by the wizard's personality, it's unknown if the culture the wizard lives in has any influence. Some animals are attributed with different traits in various cultures. For example, Mongolian culture has historically revered wolves, attributing them as the ultimate hunters who can only die by human hand if the wolf consents to death as wolves are considered to be inherently superior to mankind. In other cultures, wolves are vilified and treated as cowardly, blood thirsty, and loathsome beasts.</li>
<li>A teacher who teaches the skill does not need to be an Animagus him/herself, as shown when Professor Dumbledore, who is not an Animagus, taught Minerva McGonagall.<sup id="cite_ref-TBB_3-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-TBB-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li>In Latin, the female form of Magus is Maga. However, in canon, female Animagi are never referred to as "Animaga" or "Animagae", which would be the female plural.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/J._K._Rowling" title="J. K. Rowling">J. K. Rowling</a> has stated that she would fancy an otter  her favourite animal  as her Animagus form, though if she actually transformed, she suspects she might be "a guinea pig or something, which would be quite embarrassing."<sup id="cite_ref-cubreporter_28-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-cubreporter-28">&#91;28&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li>J. K. Rowling once joked that <a href="/wiki/Godric_Gryffindor" title="Godric Gryffindor">Godric Gryffindor</a> was actually the <a href="/wiki/Giant_Squid" title="Giant Squid">Giant Squid</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Great_Lake" title="Great Lake">Hogwarts lake</a>.</li>
<li>In the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Prisoner_of_Azkaban_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban (film)">film adaptation</a> of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Prisoner_of_Azkaban" title="Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban">Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban</a></i>:
<ul><li>Peter Pettigrew is fully clothed when he transforms to his human form in the Shrieking Shack, but later, when he transforms to a rat, he leaves his clothes behind, demonstrating that a wizard is able to consciously choose to transform what they are wearing along with them.</li>
<li>Sirius's Animagus form is a large, mangy and tough looking dog but in the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Order_of_the_Phoenix_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix (film)">film adaptation</a> of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Order_of_the_Phoenix" title="Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix">Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix</a></i>, he looks more like a kind harmless dog. It could be that he fixed his appearances in his human form, as well as the fact that he was potentially recovering from being mentally and emotionally broken due to the loss of James and Lily, the betrayal of Pettigrew, his framing, and his years of imprisonment in <a href="/wiki/Azkaban" title="Azkaban">Azkaban</a>.</li></ul></li>
<li>In <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter:_Hogwarts_Mystery" title="Harry Potter: Hogwarts Mystery">Harry Potter: Hogwarts Mystery</a></i>, Jacob's sibling has the option to become an <a href="/wiki/Eagle" title="Eagle">Eagle</a>, <a href="/wiki/Black_And_White_Cat" class="mw-redirect" title="Black And White Cat">Black And White Cat</a>, or brown dog Animagus in a time-limited, optional sidequest, meaning they do not necessarily become one. Those that do the side quest, has to convince Talbott Winger to register, despite fears of him suffering the same fate as his parents, as an Animagus. His parents were identified and killed by Death Eaters.</li>
<li>During the <a href="/wiki/1994%E2%80%931995_school_year" title="19941995 school year">1994-1995</a> <a href="/wiki/Triwizard_Tournament" title="Triwizard Tournament">Triwizard Tournament</a>, when <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> was searching for methods for the second task, he wished that he could become an Animagus so he could transform into a fish and survive in the <a href="/wiki/Great_Lake" title="Great Lake">Great Lake</a> for an hour, not realising that his form is predetermined.<sup id="cite_ref-GOF26_29-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF26-29">&#91;29&#93;</a></sup></li></ul>
