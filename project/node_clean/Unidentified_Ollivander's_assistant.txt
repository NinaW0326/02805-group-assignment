<p><b>Ollivander's assistant</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch</a> who helped <a href="/wiki/Garrick_Ollivander" title="Garrick Ollivander">Garrick Ollivander</a> at his <a href="/wiki/Ollivanders" title="Ollivanders">shop</a>. She was known to ask the customer whether they wanted to buy the <a href="/wiki/Wand" title="Wand">wand</a> they were given and told them how it much would cost.<sup id="cite_ref-WWHP_1-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-WWHP-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
