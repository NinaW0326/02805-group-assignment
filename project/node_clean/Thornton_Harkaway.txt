<b>Thornton Harkaway</b> was an <a href="/wiki/United_States_of_America" title="United States of America">American</a> <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> and the <a href="/wiki/President_of_the_Magical_Congress_of_the_United_States_of_America" title="President of the Magical Congress of the United States of America">President of the Magical Congress of the United States of America</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-PM_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PM-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Thornton_Harkaway?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Thornton_Harkaway?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>He opted to have the headquarters for the <a href="/wiki/Magical_Congress_of_the_United_States_of_America" title="Magical Congress of the United States of America">Magical Congress of the United States of America</a> moved to his hometown of <a href="/wiki/Williamsburg" title="Williamsburg">Williamsburg</a>, <a href="/wiki/Virginia" title="Virginia">Virginia</a> in <a href="/wiki/1760" class="mw-redirect" title="1760">1760</a>. He was described as flamboyant. He bred <a href="/wiki/Crup" title="Crup">Crups</a> and unfortunately his pack savagely attacked several <a href="/wiki/Muggle" class="mw-redirect" title="Muggle">No-Majs</a>, who afterwards were only able to bark for a period of 48 hours. This breach of the <a href="/wiki/International_Statute_of_Wizarding_Secrecy" title="International Statute of Wizarding Secrecy">Statute of Secrecy</a> led to him leaving office in disgrace.<sup id="cite_ref-PM_1-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PM-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>It may not be a coincidence that <a href="/wiki/Public_Hospital_for_Persons_of_Insane_and_Disordered_Minds" title="Public Hospital for Persons of Insane and Disordered Minds">the first dedicated psychiatric hospital</a> was established in Williamsburg as odd sightings the Harkaway residence might account for many No-Majs who were admitted, but who were actually sane.<sup id="cite_ref-PM_1-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PM-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
