This <b>individual</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> who attended <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> and was sorted into <a href="/wiki/Hufflepuff" title="Hufflepuff">Hufflepuff House</a>. During his school years, he performed in the <a href="/wiki/Frog_Choir" title="Frog Choir">Frog Choir</a> in the 1990s.<sup id="cite_ref-POAF_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-POAF-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_Choir_Member_(I)?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_Choir_Member_(I)?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>In <a href="/wiki/1993" title="1993">1993</a>, after the sorting, he appeared with other members singing <a href="/wiki/Double_Trouble" title="Double Trouble">Something Wicked This Way Comes</a> before Dumbledore spoke.<sup id="cite_ref-POAF_2-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-POAF-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>In <a href="/wiki/1997" title="1997">1997</a>, he was part of the group who sang <a href="/wiki/In_Noctem" title="In Noctem">In Noctem</a> before Headmaster <a href="/wiki/Albus_Dumbledore" title="Albus Dumbledore">Albus Dumbledore</a> was <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_the_Astronomy_Tower" title="Battle of the Astronomy Tower">killed</a> by Professor <a href="/wiki/Severus_Snape" title="Severus Snape">Severus Snape</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-HBPF_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBPF-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>It is unknown if he survived the <a href="/wiki/Second_Wizarding_War" title="Second Wizarding War">Second Wizarding War</a>.
</p>
