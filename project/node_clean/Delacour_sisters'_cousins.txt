<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Delacour_sisters%27_cousins?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Delacour_sisters%27_cousins?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>When they attended the <a href="/wiki/Wedding_of_William_Weasley_and_Fleur_Delacour" title="Wedding of William Weasley and Fleur Delacour">wedding</a>, the three Veela had exotic flowers and bewitched birds decorating their hats. Their beauty quickly attracted the attention of <a href="/wiki/Fred_and_George_Weasley" title="Fred and George Weasley">Fred and George Weasley</a>, two of the groom's younger brothers. Fred escorted them inside <a href="/wiki/The_Burrow" title="The Burrow">the Burrow</a> while George was forced to assist a "gaggle of middle-aged witches".
</p><p>Later that evening, during the reception, <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> noticed that Fred and George "disappeared into the darkness" with Fleur's cousins.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Delacour_sisters%27_cousins?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Delacour_sisters%27_cousins?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>The Veela were portrayed by <a href="/wiki/Alison_Cain" title="Alison Cain">Alison Cain</a> and an unknown actress in <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Deathly_Hallows:_Part_1" title="Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 1">Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 1</a></i>.</li>
<li>These two girls must be more distant cousins to Fleur and Gabrielle Delacour than first cousins, as they are full-blooded Veela, whereas the Delacours are <a href="/wiki/Half-Veela" title="Half-Veela">quarter-Veela</a>. This indicates that they could not have had the same grandparents, since Fleur and Gabrielle's grandfather was a wizard. They are presumably related to the <a href="/wiki/Delacour_family" title="Delacour family">Delacours</a> through their <a href="/wiki/Apolline_Delacour%27s_mother" title="Apolline Delacour&#39;s mother">maternal grandmother</a>, who was a Veela. Alternatively George could simply be using the term 'Veela cousins' to mean that they&#160;have visible Veela ancestry.&#160;It is not directly stated that they are full blooded Veela and may simply be&#160;<a href="/wiki/Half-Veela" title="Half-Veela">part-Veela</a>&#160;like Fleur and Gabrielle.</li></ul>
