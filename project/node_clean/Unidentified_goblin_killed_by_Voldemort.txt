<dl><dd><span><b>Goblin</b>: "<i>M-my Lord… we t-tried t-to st-stop them… Im-impostors, my Lord… broke — broke into the — into the Lestranges' v-vault…</i>"</span></dd>
<dd><b>Voldemort</b>: "<i>Impostors? What impostors? I thought Gringotts had ways of revealing impostors? Who were they?</i>"</dd>
<dd><b>Goblin</b>: "<i>It was… it was… the P-Potter b-boy and t-two accomplices…</i>"</dd>
<dd><b>Voldemort</b>: "<i>And they took? Tell me! What did they take?</i>"</dd>
<dd><b>Goblin</b>: "<i>A… a s-small golden c-cup, m-my Lord…</i>"</dd>
<dd>&#8212; The goblin's last words<sup class="noprint"><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Deathly_Hallows" title="Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows">[src]</a></sup></dd></dl>
</div>
<p>This <b><a href="/wiki/Goblin" title="Goblin">goblin</a></b> was given the unenviable task of informing <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a> that a <a href="/wiki/Break-in_of_Gringotts_Wizarding_Bank_(1998)" title="Break-in of Gringotts Wizarding Bank (1998)">break-in</a> had occurred at <a href="/wiki/Gringotts_Wizarding_Bank" title="Gringotts Wizarding Bank">Gringotts Wizarding Bank</a>, his current place of occupation, and that an item had been stolen from the <a href="/wiki/Lestrange_family" title="Lestrange family">Lestrange</a> <a href="/wiki/Lestrange_Vault" title="Lestrange Vault">Vault</a>, in May 1998.
</p>
<figure class="thumb tright show-info-icon" style="width: 252px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/5/53/Slaughter_at_Malfoy_Manor.png/revision/latest?cb=20171015153245" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/5/53/Slaughter_at_Malfoy_Manor.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/250?cb=20171015153245" decoding="async" width="250" height="128" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="Slaughter at Malfoy Manor.png" data-image-key="Slaughter_at_Malfoy_Manor.png" /></a> 	 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:Slaughter_at_Malfoy_Manor.png" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption">The goblin is killed, along with all others that came to bring Voldemort the bad news</p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure>
<p>When Voldemort demanded to know what had been taken, the goblin hesitantly replied that the stolen object was a <a href="/wiki/Helga_Hufflepuff%27s_Cup" class="mw-redirect" title="Helga Hufflepuff&#39;s Cup">small golden cup</a>. Enraged at the loss of one of his <a href="/wiki/Horcrux" title="Horcrux">Horcruxes</a>, Voldemort immediately <a href="/wiki/Massacre_at_Malfoy_Manor" title="Massacre at Malfoy Manor">killed</a> the goblin with the <a href="/wiki/Killing_Curse" title="Killing Curse">Killing Curse</a>, as well as several <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizards</a> present at the time.
</p><p>Voldemort kicked the goblin's corpse aside as he paced the room and planned his next move.<sup id="cite_ref-DH27_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH27-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
