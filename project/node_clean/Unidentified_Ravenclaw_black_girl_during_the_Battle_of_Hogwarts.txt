This <b><a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch</a></b> was a student at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> and was <a href="/wiki/Sorting_ceremony" title="Sorting ceremony">sorted</a> into <a href="/wiki/Ravenclaw" title="Ravenclaw">Ravenclaw</a>. She participated during the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a> and was seen running out of the <a href="/wiki/Great_Hall" title="Great Hall">Great Hall</a> and into the <a href="/wiki/Viaduct_Courtyard" title="Viaduct Courtyard">Viaduct Courtyard</a> while the <a href="/wiki/Protective_enchantment" title="Protective enchantment">Protective Enchantments</a> were set up.<sup id="cite_ref-DHF2_3-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DHF2-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
