<b>Emily Tyler</b> (born between <a href="/wiki/1_September" title="1 September">1 September</a> <a href="/wiki/1970" title="1970">1970</a> and <a href="/wiki/31_August" title="31 August">31 August</a> <a href="/wiki/1971" title="1971">1971</a>) was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch</a> who attended <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a>.
</p><p>In the <a href="/wiki/1986%E2%80%931987_school_year" title="1986–1987 school year">1986–1987 school year</a>, <a href="/wiki/William_Weasley" title="William Weasley">Bill Weasley</a> fell in <a href="/wiki/Love" title="Love">love</a> with her. He asked <a href="/wiki/Jacob%27s_sibling" title="Jacob&#39;s sibling">Jacob's sibling</a> for advice on how to pursue her, but she was not interested in dating him.<sup id="cite_ref-Y3sq_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Y3sq-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Emily_Tyler?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Emily_Tyler?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span id="Hogwarts_years_(1982–1989)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="Hogwarts_years_.281982.E2.80.931989.29">Hogwarts years <span class="template-c">(1982–1989)</span></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Emily_Tyler?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Hogwarts years (1982–1989)">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Emily_Tyler?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Hogwarts years (1982–1989)"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Emily began attending <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts</a> on <a href="/wiki/1_September" title="1 September">1 September</a> <a href="/wiki/1982" title="1982">1982</a>. She was sorted into <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor" title="Gryffindor">Gryffindor House</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-Y3sq_3-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Y3sq-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Fifth_year">Fifth year</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Emily_Tyler?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Fifth year">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Emily_Tyler?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Fifth year"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<p><a href="/wiki/William_Weasley" title="William Weasley">Bill Weasley</a> thought she was very beautiful, and she impressed him in their <a href="/wiki/Defence_Against_the_Dark_Arts" title="Defence Against the Dark Arts">Defence Against the Dark Arts</a> class when she single-handedly used the <a href="/wiki/Stunning_Spell" title="Stunning Spell">Stunning Spell</a> and defeated a group of <a href="/wiki/Vampire_bat" title="Vampire bat">vampire bats</a>. Bill asked <a href="/wiki/Jacob%27s_sibling" title="Jacob&#39;s sibling">Jacob's sibling</a> for advice, but was very nervous about approaching Emily because he had never been in love before.<sup id="cite_ref-Y3sq_3-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Y3sq-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Jacob's sibling eventually agreed to speak to Emily for him, and she was very surprised by the news. Emily wanted to hear everything, and when she found out that he was very shy, she called him "a prat" and put down the <a href="/wiki/Weasley_family" title="Weasley family">Weasley family</a> by wondering who would ever date one, even if they are good-looking. Jacob's sibling was very angry at her rudeness and defended Bill. When Bill found out about her true personality, he eventually gave up on those feelings for her.<sup id="cite_ref-Y3sq_3-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Y3sq-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Sixth_year">Sixth year</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Emily_Tyler?veaction=edit&amp;section=4" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Sixth year">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Emily_Tyler?action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Sixth year"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<p>In the <a href="/wiki/1987%E2%80%931988_school_year" title="1987–1988 school year">1987–1988 school year</a>, Emily wanted to run the <a href="/wiki/Celestial_Ball" title="Celestial Ball">Celestial Ball</a> decorating committee, and supposedly spread false rumours about her fellow competitor, <a href="/wiki/Penny_Haywood" title="Penny Haywood">Penny Haywood</a>, in the attempts of hurting their chance of winning.<sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Seventh_year">Seventh year</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Emily_Tyler?veaction=edit&amp;section=5" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Seventh year">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Emily_Tyler?action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Seventh year"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<p>In the <a href="/wiki/1988%E2%80%931989_school_year" title="1988–1989 school year">1988–1989 school year</a>, when <a href="/wiki/Ismelda_Murk" title="Ismelda Murk">Ismelda Murk</a> bumped into Emily and refused to apologise, Emily got hold of <a class="new" title="Ismelda Murk&#39;s diary (page does not exist)" rel="nofollow" data-uncrawlable-url="L3dpa2kvSXNtZWxkYV9NdXJrJTI3c19kaWFyeT9hY3Rpb249ZWRpdCZyZWRsaW5rPTE=">Ismelda Murk's diary</a> and proceeded to read it aloud, mocking Ismelda for having a crush on <a href="/wiki/Barnaby_Lee" title="Barnaby Lee">Barnaby Lee</a> and publicly humiliating her in front of many others.<sup id="cite_ref-Y5sq_5-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Y5sq-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>She was present in the <a href="/wiki/Great_Hall" title="Great Hall">Great Hall</a> when Jacob's sibling, <a href="/wiki/Nymphadora_Tonks" title="Nymphadora Tonks">Nymphadora Tonks</a>, <a href="/wiki/Tulip_Karasu" title="Tulip Karasu">Tulip Karasu</a> and <a href="/wiki/Jae_Kim" title="Jae Kim">Jae Kim</a> unleashed <a href="/wiki/Jinx" title="Jinx">jinxed</a> <a href="/wiki/Ever-Bashing_Boomerang" title="Ever-Bashing Boomerang">Ever-Bashing Boomerangs</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Personality_and_traits">Personality and traits</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Emily_Tyler?veaction=edit&amp;section=6" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Personality and traits">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Emily_Tyler?action=edit&amp;section=6" title="Edit section: Personality and traits"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Emily was a very haughty, narcissistic, and contemptuous young woman with clearly a very high opinion of herself, who seemed to exemplify the more negative stereotypes of <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor" title="Gryffindor">Gryffindors</a>. She scornfully looked down upon <a href="/wiki/William_Weasley" title="William Weasley">Bill Weasley</a> and <a href="/wiki/Weasley_Family" class="mw-redirect" title="Weasley Family">his family</a> due to their low socio-economic status and was displeased with the idea of someone she perceived as below her being attracted to her. It can therefore be implied that she preferred to associate herself with individuals of high social status in the <a href="/wiki/Wizarding_world" title="Wizarding world">wizarding world</a>, to satisfy her snobbish and elitist nature.
</p><p>She was also very opinionated and could be rather acerbic when it came to voicing her opinions of others, such as when she derided and humiliated Ismelda Murk, showing her to be a bully towards others she disliked, as well as having a capacity for disproportionate cruelty in the face of even the slightest infraction. She could also be rather competitive and spiteful, as she supposedly spread false and malicious rumours about Penny Haywood in order to beat her over running the <a href="/wiki/Celestial_Ball" title="Celestial Ball">Celestial Ball</a> decorating committee, showing her to be very petty with a penchant for gossip. Therefore, her friends only seemed to mock Ismelda with her only out of fear that she would start teasing them as well, showing her to be a stereotypical mean-girl with an overly high opinion of herself with a selfish disregard for other's feelings.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Magical_abilities_and_skills">Magical abilities and skills</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Emily_Tyler?veaction=edit&amp;section=7" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Emily_Tyler?action=edit&amp;section=7" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li><b><a href="/wiki/Defence_Against_the_Dark_Arts" title="Defence Against the Dark Arts">Defence Against the Dark Arts</a></b>: Emily was clearly proficient in this subject, as she was very able to single-handedly and successfully defeat a group of <a href="/wiki/Vampire_bat" title="Vampire bat">vampire bats</a> with the <a href="/wiki/Stunning_Spell" title="Stunning Spell">Stunning Spell</a> during a lesson, showing she was competent with the field of defensive <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magic</a>.</li></ul>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Relationships">Relationships</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Emily_Tyler?veaction=edit&amp;section=8" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Relationships">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Emily_Tyler?action=edit&amp;section=8" title="Edit section: Relationships"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Emily's popularity in <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> was apparent in the form of having amount of girls following her lead; however, whether she had genuine friendships with her followers was debatable, as shown during the observation of <a href="/wiki/Jacob%27s_sibling" title="Jacob&#39;s sibling">Jacob's sibling</a> and <a href="/wiki/Penny_Haywood" title="Penny Haywood">Penny Haywood</a>, investigating the cause of a commotion. During the confrontation between Emily and Ismelda, a surrounding <a href="/wiki/Sixth_year" title="Sixth year">sixth year</a> <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor" title="Gryffindor">Gryffindor</a> girl admitted to not really wanting to bully Ismelda, but <i>"if it's either her or me..."</i>, whereas another fellow Gryffindor sixth year outright stated <i>"I don't even like Emily. I'm only friends with her so she doesn't tease me."</i><sup id="cite_ref-Y5sq_5-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Y5sq-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Bill_Weasley">Bill Weasley</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Emily_Tyler?veaction=edit&amp;section=9" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Bill Weasley">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Emily_Tyler?action=edit&amp;section=9" title="Edit section: Bill Weasley"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Emily thought she was superior to <a href="/wiki/William_Weasley" title="William Weasley">Bill Weasley</a> and <a href="/wiki/Weasley_family" title="Weasley family">his family</a>, and considered him "ill-mannered" and "completely delusional" if he ever thought he had a chance with dating her. He was in <a href="/wiki/Love" title="Love">love</a> with her, but she considered the news "a good laugh".<sup id="cite_ref-Y3sq_3-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Y3sq-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Ismelda_Murk">Ismelda Murk</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Emily_Tyler?veaction=edit&amp;section=10" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Ismelda Murk">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Emily_Tyler?action=edit&amp;section=10" title="Edit section: Ismelda Murk"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Emily and <a href="/wiki/Ismelda_Murk" title="Ismelda Murk">Ismelda Murk</a> had no connection to one another prior to the latter accidentally bumping into her. In retaliation, Emily read the contents of the latter's diary out loud, humiliating Ismelda.

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Emily_Tyler?veaction=edit&amp;section=11" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Emily_Tyler?action=edit&amp;section=11" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>During <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter:_Hogwarts_Mystery" title="Harry Potter: Hogwarts Mystery">Harry Potter: Hogwarts Mystery</a></i>, Year 5, Side Quest "Crushed", a <a href="/wiki/Third_year" title="Third year">third year</a> Gryffindor girl shared that she heard <a href="/wiki/Rita_Skeeter" title="Rita Skeeter">Rita Skeeter</a> promising Emily Tyler an internship after graduation. Given that both women thrive on malicious gossip, this is fitting.</li>
<li>During <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter:_Hogwarts_Mystery" title="Harry Potter: Hogwarts Mystery">Harry Potter: Hogwarts Mystery</a></i>, Year 3, Side Quest "Bill's Secret", after Emily talked down about Bill, Jacob's sibling can choose to let him down gently, or be brutally honest to share her remarks, then comfort him again.</li>
<li>Despite being a Gryffindor, the character from the main series Emily could most be said to resemble is <a href="/wiki/Pansy_Parkinson" title="Pansy Parkinson">Pansy Parkinson</a>; both are the chief female bully of their generation of students, and both antagonise both the protagonist and their friends during their time at Hogwarts.</li></ul>
