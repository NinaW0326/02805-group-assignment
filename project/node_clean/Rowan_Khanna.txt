<b>Rowan Khanna</b> (<a href="/wiki/December" title="December">December</a><sup id="cite_ref-xmas_1-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-xmas-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/1972" title="1972">1972</a> – <a href="/wiki/1990" title="1990">1990</a>)<sup id="cite_ref-death_2-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-death-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard or witch</a> who began attending <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> in <a href="/wiki/1984" title="1984">1984</a>. Their family owned <a href="/wiki/Khanna_Farms" title="Khanna Farms">a farm</a> that supplied high-quality wood for the manufacture of <a href="/wiki/Wand" title="Wand">wands</a> and <a href="/wiki/Broomstick" title="Broomstick">broomsticks</a>.
</p><p>Rowan aspired to become the youngest professor in Hogwarts history, but this dream was never realised as a result of their tragic murder at the hands of the <a href="/wiki/Dark_Wizard" title="Dark Wizard">Dark Witch</a> <a href="/wiki/Patricia_Rakepick" title="Patricia Rakepick">Patricia Rakepick</a> in 1990.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Rowan_Khanna?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Rowan_Khanna?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_life">Early life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Rowan_Khanna?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Early life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Rowan_Khanna?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Early life"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Rowan was born into a British-Indian <a href="/wiki/Khanna_family" title="Khanna family">wizarding family</a> in <a href="/wiki/Great_Britain" title="Great Britain">Great Britain</a> in December <a href="/wiki/1972" title="1972">1972</a>. Their family owned a <a href="/wiki/Khanna_Farms" title="Khanna Farms">farm</a> that supplied high-quality wood for the manufacture of <a href="/wiki/Wand" title="Wand">wands</a> and <a href="/wiki/Broomstick" title="Broomstick">broomsticks</a>.
</p><p>Rowan was interested in reading from a young age. They were considered nerdy and weird by the age of eleven and had never made any friends.
</p><p>While shopping for school supplies in <a href="/wiki/Diagon_Alley" title="Diagon Alley">Diagon Alley</a>, Rowan met and befriended future fellow student and housemate, <a href="/wiki/Jacob%27s_sibling" title="Jacob&#39;s sibling">Jacob's sibling</a>, to whom Rowan advised starting with <a href="/wiki/Flourish_and_Blotts" title="Flourish and Blotts">Flourish and Blotts</a>. When the shopping concluded, Rowan and Jacob's sibling promised to see each other again on the train.<sup id="cite_ref-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span id="Hogwarts_years_(1984-1990)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="Hogwarts_years_.281984-1990.29">Hogwarts years <span class="template-c">(1984-1990)</span></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Rowan_Khanna?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Hogwarts years (1984-1990)">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Rowan_Khanna?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Hogwarts years (1984-1990)"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="First_year">First year</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Rowan_Khanna?veaction=edit&amp;section=4" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: First year">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Rowan_Khanna?action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: First year"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<p>Rowan began attending <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> in <a href="/wiki/1984" title="1984">1984</a> and was sorted into the same <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Houses" title="Hogwarts Houses">House</a> as <a href="/wiki/Jacob_(Hogwarts_student)" title="Jacob (Hogwarts student)">Jacob's</a> <a href="/wiki/Jacob%27s_sibling" title="Jacob&#39;s sibling">sibling</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-y1ch2_8-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-y1ch2-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Before the start of their first <a href="/wiki/Potions_(class)" title="Potions (class)">Potions class</a>, Jacob's sibling found <a href="/wiki/Merula_Snyde" title="Merula Snyde">Merula Snyde</a>, an imperious and cruel <a href="/wiki/Slytherin" title="Slytherin">Slytherin</a> witch, trying to bully Rowan into admitting her as the most powerful witch to in Hogwarts. Rowan tried to reason with Merula by listing out other factors, but Merula wouldn't budge, which prompted Jacob's sibling to intervene on Rowan's behalf and stand up for their friend. Professor <a href="/wiki/Severus_Snape" title="Severus Snape">Severus Snape</a> ordered them to get into the classroom before the argument could escalate further. Rowan later thanked Jacob's sibling for standing up to Merula for them.<sup id="cite_ref-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Later on in the year, Rowan accompanied Jacob's sibling to find the first <a href="/wiki/Cursed_Vaults" title="Cursed Vaults">cursed vault</a>.
</p>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Second_year">Second year</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Rowan_Khanna?veaction=edit&amp;section=5" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Second year">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Rowan_Khanna?action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Second year"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<p>Upon realising <a href="/wiki/Ben_Copper" title="Ben Copper">Ben Copper</a>'s disappearance during the <a href="/wiki/Start-of-Term_Feast" title="Start-of-Term Feast">Start-of-Term Feast</a> of the <a href="/wiki/1985%E2%80%931986_school_year" title="1985–1986 school year">1985–1986 school year</a>, Rowan and Jacob's sibling proceeded to search for their friend.<sup id="cite_ref-10" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-10">&#91;10&#93;</a></sup> When finding a clue that only led to another, one claiming to be found in another <a href="/wiki/Common_room" title="Common room">common room</a>, they devised a plan for Jacob's sibling to sneak into the said common room during the <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor_vs_Slytherin_Quidditch_match_(1985)" title="Gryffindor vs Slytherin Quidditch match (1985)">Quidditch match between Gryffindor and Slytherin</a>, with Rowan keeping watch.<sup id="cite_ref-11" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-11">&#91;11&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Jacob's sibling felt the need to return to the <a href="/wiki/Icy_Corridor" title="Icy Corridor">Icy Corridor</a> after witnessing Professors <a href="/wiki/Minerva_McGonagall" title="Minerva McGonagall">McGonagall</a> and <a href="/wiki/Severus_Snape" title="Severus Snape">Snape</a> rescuing Ben. Accompanied by Rowan, with the use of <i><a href="/wiki/Revelio_Charm" title="Revelio Charm">Revelio</a></i>, they discovered the <a href="/wiki/Vanished_Stairs" title="Vanished Stairs">Vanished Stairs</a>, and the door to the <a href="/wiki/Vault_of_Ice" title="Vault of Ice">Vault of Ice</a>, which attacked them, subsequently injuring Rowan.<sup id="cite_ref-12" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-12">&#91;12&#93;</a></sup> When visiting Rowan at the <a href="/wiki/Hospital_Wing" title="Hospital Wing">Hospital Wing</a>, Jacob's sibling brought a book about <a href="/wiki/Wendelin_the_Weird" title="Wendelin the Weird">Wendelin the Weird</a>, much to Rowan's delight. Rowan confessed being quite shaken up by the incident in the Icy Corridor and suggested Jacob's sibling seeking help from someone more powerful, namely <a href="/wiki/William_Weasley" title="William Weasley">Bill Weasley</a>, who Rowan held in great admiration.<sup id="cite_ref-13" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-13">&#91;13&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Sixth_year_and_Death">Sixth year and Death</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Rowan_Khanna?veaction=edit&amp;section=6" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Sixth year and Death">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Rowan_Khanna?action=edit&amp;section=6" title="Edit section: Sixth year and Death"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>

<p>Sometime during the school year, <a href="/wiki/Jacob%27s_sibling" title="Jacob&#39;s sibling">Jacob's sibling</a>, <a href="/wiki/Merula_Snyde" title="Merula Snyde">Merula Snyde</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Ben_Copper" title="Ben Copper">Ben Copper</a> went into the <a href="/wiki/Forest_Grove" title="Forest Grove">Forest Grove</a> to intercept a possible meeting between <a href="/wiki/Jacob_(Hogwarts_student)" title="Jacob (Hogwarts student)">Jacob</a> and <a href="/wiki/R" title="R">R</a>, unknowingly being followed from a close distance behind them by Rowan. During the search, a swarm of <a href="/wiki/Dementor" title="Dementor">dementors</a> showed up, and circled the group. However, the <a href="/wiki/Dark_Wizard" title="Dark Wizard">Dark Witch</a> <a href="/wiki/Patricia_Rakepick" title="Patricia Rakepick">Patricia Rakepick</a> <a href="/wiki/Apparition" title="Apparition">apparated</a> into the vicinity and cast a powerful <a href="/wiki/Patronus_Charm" title="Patronus Charm">Patronus Charm</a>, which warded off all of the dementors. She then engaged in a <a href="/wiki/Duel_in_the_Forest_Grove" title="Duel in the Forest Grove">duel</a> with the students, which was watched by Rowan. At some point in the duel, she cast a <a href="/wiki/Killing_Curse" title="Killing Curse">Killing Curse</a> at Ben, intending to kill him. However, Rowan sacrificed their life by jumping in front of Ben, ending their life and saving that of their friends.<sup id="cite_ref-HM18_20-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HM18-20">&#91;20&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Legacy">Legacy</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Rowan_Khanna?veaction=edit&amp;section=7" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Legacy">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Rowan_Khanna?action=edit&amp;section=7" title="Edit section: Legacy"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<figure class="thumb tleft show-info-icon" style="width: 252px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/1/11/Rowan_Tribute.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20200221080810" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/1/11/Rowan_Tribute.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/250?cb=20200221080810" decoding="async" width="250" height="120" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="Rowan Tribute.jpg" data-image-key="Rowan_Tribute.jpg" /></a> 	 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:Rowan_Tribute.jpg" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption">Hogwarts Students and staff raise their wand as a tribute to Rowan</p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure>
<p>Rowan's death struck the entirety of Hogwarts as an extremely painful event and their death was mourned by all of their friends, who found it difficult to come to terms with their tragic loss. In particular to suffer were <a href="/wiki/Jacob%27s_sibling" title="Jacob&#39;s sibling">Jacob's sibling</a>, <a href="/wiki/Ben_Copper" title="Ben Copper">Ben Copper</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Merula_Snyde" title="Merula Snyde">Merula Snyde</a>, all of whom had watched them die. A memorial service took place in the Great Hall, where Professor Dumbledore gave a brief eulogy of Rowan's life, aspirations and sacrifice before leading the students and staff in raising their wands as a tribute to Rowan.
</p><p>Rowan's murder prompted a vengeful Jacob's sibling, Merula and Ben to form their own secret organisation to combat the malevolent "R" and get revenge on Rakepick for what she had done.<sup id="cite_ref-death_2-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-death-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> The organisation was named <a href="/wiki/Circle_of_Khanna" title="Circle of Khanna">Circle of Khanna</a> in memory of Rowan.<sup id="cite_ref-21" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-21">&#91;21&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Physical_description">Physical description</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Rowan_Khanna?veaction=edit&amp;section=8" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Physical description">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Rowan_Khanna?action=edit&amp;section=8" title="Edit section: Physical description"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Rowan had black hair, brown skin, and wore glasses. They tended to wear regular <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_uniform" title="Hogwarts uniform">Hogwarts uniform</a> robes most of the time, although they wore blue <a href="/wiki/Dress_robes" title="Dress robes">dress robes</a> to the <a href="/wiki/Celestial_Ball" title="Celestial Ball">Celestial Ball</a>. They were known to snore heavily.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Personality_and_traits">Personality and traits</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Rowan_Khanna?veaction=edit&amp;section=9" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Personality and traits">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Rowan_Khanna?action=edit&amp;section=9" title="Edit section: Personality and traits"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Rowan Khanna was a voracious reader and aspired to be the youngest professor at Hogwarts. They once said the only thing they enjoy more than reading is planning. Their favourite professor was <a href="/wiki/Albus_Dumbledore" title="Albus Dumbledore">Albus Dumbledore</a> and their favourite game was <a href="/wiki/Gobstones" title="Gobstones">Gobstones</a>. Their favourite sweet was Sugar Quills.
</p><p>Rowan was an incredibly brave, loyal, and selfless individual, shown by their loyalty towards their friends, mostly their best friend, <a href="/wiki/Jacob%27s_sibling" title="Jacob&#39;s sibling">Jacob's sibling</a>. Ultimately, Rowan sacrificed their own life to protect <a href="/wiki/Ben_Copper" title="Ben Copper">Ben Copper</a> from the <a href="/wiki/Killing_Curse" title="Killing Curse">Killing Curse</a> cast by <a href="/wiki/Patricia_Rakepick" title="Patricia Rakepick">Patricia Rakepick</a> at the <a href="/wiki/Forbidden_Forest" title="Forbidden Forest">Forbidden Forest</a> during their sixth year.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Magical_abilities_and_skills">Magical abilities and skills</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Rowan_Khanna?veaction=edit&amp;section=10" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Rowan_Khanna?action=edit&amp;section=10" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<div class="quote">
<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;Harry Potter: Hogwarts Mystery">"<i>Rowan Khanna is exceptionally bright.</i>"</span></dd>
<dd>&#8212;<a href="/wiki/Albus_Dumbledore" title="Albus Dumbledore">Albus Dumbledore</a> regarding Rowan's intelligence<sup class="noprint"><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter:_Hogwarts_Mystery" title="Harry Potter: Hogwarts Mystery">[src]</a></sup></dd>
<dd></dd></dl></div>
<ul><li><b>Intellectual giftedness</b>: Rowan Khanna was exceptionally intelligent, perceptive and well-read, having been an excellent academic achiever through their Hogwarts years, with <a href="/wiki/Albus_Dumbledore" title="Albus Dumbledore">Albus Dumbledore</a> himself acknowledging their high intelligence. As such, Rowan was able to consistently aid their best friend <a href="/wiki/Jacob%27s_sibling" title="Jacob&#39;s sibling">Jacob's sibling</a> with useful insights or observations, making them an invaluable member in Jacob's sibling's band of misfits.</li></ul>
<figure class="thumb tright show-info-icon" style="width: 152px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/7/73/Rowan_Khanna_teaching.jpeg/revision/latest?cb=20200628121439" class="image"><img alt="" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw%3D%3D" decoding="async" width="150" height="150" class="thumbimage lazyload" data-image-name="Rowan Khanna teaching.jpeg" data-image-key="Rowan_Khanna_teaching.jpeg" data-src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/7/73/Rowan_Khanna_teaching.jpeg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/150?cb=20200628121439" /></a> 	<noscript><a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/7/73/Rowan_Khanna_teaching.jpeg/revision/latest?cb=20200628121439" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/7/73/Rowan_Khanna_teaching.jpeg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/150?cb=20200628121439" decoding="async" width="150" height="150" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="Rowan Khanna teaching.jpeg" data-image-key="Rowan_Khanna_teaching.jpeg" data-src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/7/73/Rowan_Khanna_teaching.jpeg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/150?cb=20200628121439" /></a></noscript> 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:Rowan_Khanna_teaching.jpeg" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption">Rowan teaching History of Magic in class</p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure>
<ul><li><b>Teaching skills</b>: Rowan was an excellent teacher of <a href="/wiki/History_of_Magic" title="History of Magic">History of Magic</a>, as they often intervened to teach the class whenever <a href="/wiki/Professor" title="Professor">Professor</a> <a href="/wiki/Cuthbert_Binns" title="Cuthbert Binns">Cuthbert Binns</a> became distracted. Under their tutelage, the entire class was not only attentive to Rowan's lessons (despite the subject's infamy for being extremely dull) but also able to pass their History of Magic O.W.L. and study the subject further at N.E.W.T.-level, which is a testament to Rowan's superb skills at teaching others.</li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/History_of_Magic" title="History of Magic">History of Magic</a></b>: Arguably their most favourite subject out of all the magical fields taught at Hogwarts, Rowan was considerably knowledgeable and well-read in magical history, having read <i><a href="/wiki/Hogwarts:_A_History" title="Hogwarts: A History">Hogwarts: A History</a></i> in anticipation of their first year to such detail that they considered themselves an expert on Hogwarts. They possessed an almost manic enthusiasm for the subject and often taught their History of Magic class with such passion that, despite the subject's notoriety for being extremely dull, their lectures appealed to the entire class, with their best friend <a href="/wiki/Jacob%27s_sibling" title="Jacob&#39;s sibling">Jacob's sibling</a> even noting that Rowan's zeal was infectious. They would go on to achieve an O.W.L. on the subject and study it further at N.E.W.T.-level. Indeed, <a href="/wiki/Professor" title="Professor">Professor</a> <a href="/wiki/Cuthbert_Binns" title="Cuthbert Binns">Cuthbert Binns</a> would mournfully acknowledge Rowan to be a brilliant student following their death, and conducted a lesson on the similarly life-changing event of the dissolving of the <a href="/wiki/Wizards%27_Council" title="Wizards&#39; Council">Wizards' Council</a> in honour of them.</li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Potion" title="Potion">Potions</a></b>: While not as proficient as their best friend <a href="/wiki/Jacob%27s_sibling" title="Jacob&#39;s sibling">Jacob's sibling</a> in the art of potion-making, Rowan was still shown to be particularly knowledgeable in the nature and effects of various concoctions. During their very first-ever Potions lesson, Rowan was able to swiftly identify the substance that Jacob's sibling's cauldron was coated with to be <a href="/wiki/Bulbadox_powder" title="Bulbadox powder">Bulbadox powder</a>, succinctly describing its explosive effects to Jacob's sibling. They later achieved an '<a href="/wiki/Outstanding" title="Outstanding">Outstanding</a>' on their <a href="/wiki/Ordinary_Wizarding_Level" title="Ordinary Wizarding Level">O.W.L.</a> exam to be able to advance to the <a href="/wiki/Nastily_Exhausting_Wizarding_Test" title="Nastily Exhausting Wizarding Test">N.E.W.T.</a>-level Potions class taught by <a href="/wiki/Severus_Snape" title="Severus Snape">Severus Snape</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-22" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-22">&#91;22&#93;</a></sup> thus proving themselves adept in the subject.</li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Charm" title="Charm">Charms</a></b>: Although Charms was never one of their strong suits and they often required tutoring from Jacob's sibling, Rowan was still able to achieve either an ' <a href="/wiki/Exceeds_Expectations" title="Exceeds Expectations">Exceeds Expectations</a>' or '<a href="/wiki/Outstanding" title="Outstanding">Outstanding</a>' on their O.W.L. exam to be able to study the subject further at N.E.W.T.-level, thus showcasing their skill in the magical subject.</li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Transfiguration" title="Transfiguration">Transfiguration</a></b>: Rowan was proficient in Transfiguration, as they earned either an ' <a href="/wiki/Exceeds_Expectations" title="Exceeds Expectations">Exceeds Expectations</a>' or '<a href="/wiki/Outstanding" title="Outstanding">Outstanding</a>' on their O.W.L. exam to be able to study the subject further at N.E.W.T.-level, thus showcasing their skill in the magical subject.</li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Herbology" title="Herbology">Herbology</a></b>: Rowan was proficient in Herbology, as they were able to achieve an O.W.L. on the subject and study it further at N.E.W.T.-level, thus showcasing their skill in the magical field.</li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Care_of_Magical_Creatures" title="Care of Magical Creatures">Care of Magical Creatures</a></b>: Rowan was proficient at caring for magical creatures, being able to achieve an O.W.L. on the subject and study it further at N.E.W.T.-level, thus showcasing their skill in the subject.</li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Wandlore" title="Wandlore">Wandlore</a></b>: Having grown up on a wand-tree farm, Rowan had an impressive knowledge of <a href="/wiki/Wand_wood" title="Wand wood">wand woods</a>, being able to recognise them at first sight.</li></ul>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Rowan_Khanna?veaction=edit&amp;section=12" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Rowan_Khanna?action=edit&amp;section=12" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The word "<i>Rowan</i>" comes from the Irish word "<i>Ruadháin</i>", which means "<i>red-haired</i>" or in this instance might be a British derivative for 'Rohan' - a common Indian first name. "Rowan" is also an epicene name which fits either gender. "<i>Khanna</i>" is a surname of members or descendants of members of the <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Khatri" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Khatri">Khatri clan</a> from the Indian Dhai Ghar community.
</p><p><a href="/wiki/Rowan" title="Rowan">Rowan</a> is also a type of <a href="/wiki/Rowan" title="Rowan">wood</a> used for wands.
</p>
