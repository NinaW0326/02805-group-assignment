<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Ivy_Warrington?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Ivy_Warrington?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_life">Early life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Ivy_Warrington?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Early life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Ivy_Warrington?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Early life"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Ivy was born to a <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggle</a> <a href="/wiki/Warrington_sisters%27_father" title="Warrington sisters&#39; father">father</a>. She had a <a href="/wiki/Warrington_sisters%27_grandmother" title="Warrington sisters&#39; grandmother">grandmother</a> who was a witch, and one sister, <a href="/wiki/Winifred_Warrington" title="Winifred Warrington">Winifred Warrington</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-MA_4-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-MA-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>At one point during her childhood, Ivy accidentally <a href="/wiki/Vanishment" title="Vanishment">vanished</a> Winifred. Their grandmother tried to help her forget what she did by using a <a href="/wiki/Memory_Charm" title="Memory Charm">Memory Charm</a>. However, it caused her to wholly forget about Winifred. Ivy had an ability to vanish, and would do so even when she didn't intend to.<sup id="cite_ref-MA_4-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-MA-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Education">Education</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Ivy_Warrington?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Education">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Ivy_Warrington?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Education"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>At the age of eleven, Ivy began attending <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a>. When she arrived at school, she vanished by accident. During her <a href="/wiki/Sorting_ceremony" title="Sorting ceremony">Sorting ceremony</a>, Ivy was missing. She was lost in the school, so <a href="/wiki/Minerva_McGonagall" title="Minerva McGonagall">Professor McGonagall</a> asked <a href="/wiki/Rubeus_Hagrid" title="Rubeus Hagrid">Rubeus Hagrid</a> to set up a search party.<sup id="cite_ref-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Magical_abilities_and_skills">Magical abilities and skills</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Ivy_Warrington?veaction=edit&amp;section=4" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Ivy_Warrington?action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li><b><a href="/wiki/Charm" title="Charm">Charms</a></b>: Ivy was proficient in the casting of charms. She could successfully cast the <a href="/wiki/Levitation_Charm" title="Levitation Charm">Levitation Charm</a>, the <a href="/wiki/Disarming_Charm" title="Disarming Charm">Disarming Charm</a> and the <a href="/wiki/Locking_Spell" title="Locking Spell">Locking Spell</a>, for example.</li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Transfiguration" title="Transfiguration">Transfiguration</a></b>: She could successfully cast the <a href="/wiki/Vanishing_Spell" title="Vanishing Spell">Vanishing Spell</a>, to the point that she was able to vanish large objects, like luggage, adult-sized suits of armors and even people.</li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Healing_magic" title="Healing magic">Healing magic</a></b>: She successfully cast the <a href="/wiki/Healing_spell" title="Healing spell">Healing spell</a> <i><a href="/wiki/Episkey" title="Episkey">Episkey</a></i> to heal <a href="/wiki/Daniel_Page" title="Daniel Page">Daniel</a>'s nose.<sup id="cite_ref-MA_4-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-MA-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup></li></ul>
