<b>Model dragons</b><sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> are <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magical</a>, realistic models of various types of <a href="/wiki/Dragon" title="Dragon">dragons</a> such as; <a href="/wiki/Hungarian_Horntail" title="Hungarian Horntail">Hungarian Horntails</a>, <a href="/wiki/Chinese_Fireball" title="Chinese Fireball">Chinese Fireballs</a>, <a href="/wiki/Swedish_Short-Snout" title="Swedish Short-Snout">Swedish Short-Snouts</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Common_Welsh_Green" title="Common Welsh Green">Common Welsh Greens</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-GoF20_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GoF20-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Uses">Uses</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Model_dragon?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Uses">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Model_dragon?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Uses"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>These models have various usages, such as <a href="/wiki/Weasleys%27_Dragon_Roasted_Nuts" title="Weasleys&#39; Dragon Roasted Nuts">roasting chestnuts</a> and, presumably other foods.<sup id="cite_ref-HBPF_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBPF-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Most notably, they were used by the <a href="/wiki/Triwizard_Tournament" title="Triwizard Tournament">Triwizard Tournament</a> champions who each drew small model dragons out of a silk bag prior to the <a href="/wiki/Triwizard_Tournament#The_First_Task" title="Triwizard Tournament">First Task</a>. The type of model indicated which dragon they had to face. A long time after the Second Task, the <a href="/wiki/Miniature_figurine_of_Viktor_Krum" title="Miniature figurine of Viktor Krum">miniature figurine</a> of <a href="/wiki/Viktor_Krum" title="Viktor Krum">Viktor Krum</a> was seen trying to fight <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>'s dragon model at the <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor_Boys%27_Dormitory" title="Gryffindor Boys&#39; Dormitory">Gryffindor Boys' Dormitory</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-GoF20_2-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GoF20-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>In <a href="/wiki/Diagon_Alley" title="Diagon Alley">Diagon Alley</a> in <a href="/wiki/1996" title="1996">1996</a>, there was a dragon model in the <a href="/wiki/Weasleys%27_Dragon_Roasted_Nuts" title="Weasleys&#39; Dragon Roasted Nuts">roast chestnuts sale</a> machine.<sup id="cite_ref-HBPF_3-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBPF-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
