<b>Edmund Grubb</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Ghost" title="Ghost">ghost</a> at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a>.
</p><p>He lived in the <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Victorian_era" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Victorian era">Victorian era</a>, and died in the doorway of the <a href="/wiki/Great_Hall" title="Great Hall">Great Hall</a> at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Castle" title="Hogwarts Castle">Hogwarts Castle</a> after ingesting <a href="/wiki/Poison" title="Poison">poisonous</a> berries. After his death, he returned as a ghost to haunt the Castle, sometimes stopping people from getting into the Great Hall out of spite.
</p>
