<dd>&#8212; The maid of the <a href="/wiki/Leaky_Cauldron" title="Leaky Cauldron">Leaky Cauldron</a><sup class="noprint"><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Prisoner_of_Azkaban_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban (film)">[src]</a></sup></dd></dl>

<p>This <b>young <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch</a> maid</b> worked for <a href="/wiki/Tom" title="Tom">Tom</a>, the then-landlord of the <a href="/wiki/Leaky_Cauldron" title="Leaky Cauldron">Leaky Cauldron</a>. In <a href="/wiki/1993" title="1993">1993</a>, she knocked on the door of a room and announced herself as housekeeping. The room's resident responded with a bellowing roar and slammed the door in her face. Completely unperturbed, she remarked that she would come back later and walked away. She pulled a waggon with cleaning supplies and had a magical broom that swept by itself.<sup id="cite_ref-POAF_2-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-POAF-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
