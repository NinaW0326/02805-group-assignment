<b>Corvus Lestrange</b> was a <a href="/wiki/French" title="French">French</a> <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> and a member of the <a href="/wiki/Pure-blood" title="Pure-blood">pure-blood</a> <a href="/wiki/Lestrange_family" title="Lestrange family">Lestrange family</a>. He was the son of <a href="/wiki/Corvus_Lestrange_(I)" title="Corvus Lestrange (I)">Corvus Lestrange</a> and <a href="/wiki/Heloise_Lestrange" title="Heloise Lestrange">Heloise Beaufort</a>. He had a brother <a href="/wiki/Falco_Lestrange" title="Falco Lestrange">Falco</a>. He married <a href="/wiki/Minette_Lestrange" title="Minette Lestrange">Minette Rosier</a> and had three children with her: two daughters, <a href="/wiki/Manon_Perrot" title="Manon Perrot">Manon</a> and <a href="/wiki/Josette_Gage" title="Josette Gage">Josette</a>, and a son <a href="/wiki/Corvus_Lestrange_(III)" title="Corvus Lestrange (III)">Corvus</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
