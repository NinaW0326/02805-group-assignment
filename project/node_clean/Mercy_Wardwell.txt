<b>Mercy Wardwell</b><sup id="cite_ref-Insider_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Insider-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>&#160;was an American <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch</a> who played as the <a href="/wiki/Chaser" title="Chaser">Chaser</a> for the <a href="/wiki/American_National_Quidditch_team" title="American National Quidditch team">American National Quidditch team</a>, and competed in the <a href="/wiki/2014_Quidditch_World_Cup" title="2014 Quidditch World Cup">2014 Quidditch World Cup</a>.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Mercy_Wardwell?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Mercy_Wardwell?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>During the <a href="/wiki/United_States_of_America" title="United States of America">America</a>-<a href="/wiki/Brazil" title="Brazil">Brazil</a>&#160;semi-finals&#160;taking place on <a href="/wiki/4_July" title="4 July">4 July</a> and <a href="/wiki/5_July" title="5 July">5 July</a>, the nerves got to Wardwell and after her fifth fumble with the <a href="/wiki/Quaffle" title="Quaffle">Quaffle</a>, she began to repeatedly hit her head against her broom handle before being stopped by fellow teammate and <a href="/wiki/Seeker" title="Seeker">Seeker</a>, <a href="/wiki/Darius_Smackhammer" title="Darius Smackhammer">Darius Smackhammer</a>.
</p><p>During the play-offs for third place against <a href="/wiki/Japan" title="Japan">Japan</a> on <a href="/wiki/9_July" title="9 July">9 July</a>, she and her fellow American Chasers, <a href="/wiki/Arsenia_Gonzales" title="Arsenia Gonzales">Arsenia Gonzales</a> and <a href="/wiki/Quentin_Kowalski" title="Quentin Kowalski">Quentin Kowalski</a>, scored twelve goals. The Japanese Seeker, <a href="/wiki/Noriko_Sato" title="Noriko Sato">Noriko Sato</a>, went into a spectatular dive and grabbed the <a href="/wiki/Golden_Snitch" title="Golden Snitch">Snitch</a> from under Wardwell's heel, allowing Japan to win with a score of 330-120.
</p>
