<p>The <b>Fat Friar</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a>, who attended <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> in his youth, and was <a href="/wiki/Sorting_ceremony" title="Sorting ceremony">sorted</a> into <a href="/wiki/Hufflepuff" title="Hufflepuff">Hufflepuff</a>. He was a jolly man and very forgiving. He devoted his life to religion and, after his death, returned to the school as the resident ghost of <a href="/wiki/Hufflepuff" title="Hufflepuff">Hufflepuff House</a>.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Fat_Friar?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Fat_Friar?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Life">Life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Fat_Friar?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Fat_Friar?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Life"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<div class="quote">
<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;Harry Potter and the Philosopher&#39;s Stone">"<i>Hope to see you in Hufflepuff! My old house, you know.</i>"</span></dd>
<dd>&#8212;The Fat Friar to the first-years before the <a href="/wiki/Sorting_ceremony" title="Sorting ceremony">Sorting ceremony</a><sup class="noprint"><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Philosopher%27s_Stone" title="Harry Potter and the Philosopher&#39;s Stone">[src]</a></sup></dd>
<dd></dd></dl></div><p>The Friar was born no earlier than <a href="/wiki/First_millennium_A.D." title="First millennium A.D.">982</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-birthdate_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-birthdate-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> somewhere in the <a href="/wiki/Great_Britain" title="Great Britain">British Isles</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-PS7_4-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PS7-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>At the age of eleven, the Friar became a student at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> in <a href="/wiki/Scotland" title="Scotland">Scotland</a>. He was <a href="/wiki/Sorting_ceremony" title="Sorting ceremony">Sorted</a> into <a href="/wiki/Hufflepuff" title="Hufflepuff">Hufflepuff House</a> and was taught by <a href="/wiki/Helga_Hufflepuff" title="Helga Hufflepuff">Helga Hufflepuff</a> in the magical arts the seven years that followed.<sup id="cite_ref-house_6-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-house-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>At some point in his life, he joined the clergy as part of a mendicant religious order, meaning that he spent his life begging in the name of charity. A noble life for a noble man. Apparently, given his physically large appearance, he indulged in the pleasures of food<sup id="cite_ref-PS7_4-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PS7-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> and drink.<sup id="cite_ref-PSf13_5-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PSf13-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>The Fat Friar's kindness was to be his undoing. He was executed because senior churchmen grew suspicious of his ability to cure the <a href="/wiki/Pox" title="Pox">pox</a> merely by poking peasants with a stick, and his ill-advised habit of pulling <a href="/wiki/Rabbit" title="Rabbit">rabbits</a> out of the <a href="/wiki/Communion" title="Communion">communion</a> cup.<sup id="cite_ref-POT_7-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-POT-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Post-mortem">Post-mortem</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Fat_Friar?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Post-mortem">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Fat_Friar?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Post-mortem"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<figure class="thumb tleft show-info-icon" style="width: 252px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/4/4b/Friar_Feast.png/revision/latest?cb=20180607053942" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/4/4b/Friar_Feast.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/250?cb=20180607053942" decoding="async" width="250" height="104" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="Friar Feast.png" data-image-key="Friar_Feast.png" /></a> 	 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:Friar_Feast.png" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption">The Fat Friar after his death</p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure>
<p>The Friar returned from the dead as a <a href="/wiki/Ghost" title="Ghost">ghost</a>, and returned to <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Castle" title="Hogwarts Castle">Hogwarts Castle</a>, where he became the house ghost of his former House, <a href="/wiki/Hufflepuff" title="Hufflepuff">Hufflepuff</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-PS7_4-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PS7-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> He has thus far spent the majority of his afterlife within the castle walls along with the other Hogwarts ghosts.
</p><p>After his death, the Fat Friar retained his kind and joyful demeanour. He eagerly welcomed new students to the school and offered assistance whenever needed.
</p>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="1985-1986_school_year">1985-1986 school year</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Fat_Friar?veaction=edit&amp;section=4" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: 1985-1986 school year">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Fat_Friar?action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: 1985-1986 school year"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<p>The Fat Friar participated in planning <a href="/wiki/Nicholas_de_Mimsy-Porpington%27s_four-hundred_and_ninety-third_Deathday_Party" title="Nicholas de Mimsy-Porpington&#39;s four-hundred and ninety-third Deathday Party">Sir Nicholas' 493rd deathday party</a> when <a href="/wiki/Erling" class="mw-redirect" title="Erling">Erling</a>, a friend of his, visited Hogwarts. When he knew that Sir Nicholas and Erling had the same deathday, he suggested eagerly for the two to hold a party together, but it was rejected by Erling. Nevertheless, the Friar guided Erling through a tour in Hogwarts, including the <a href="/wiki/Charms_Classroom" title="Charms Classroom">Charms classroom</a> and the <a href="/wiki/Dungeons" title="Dungeons">dungeons</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>During their parties, when he saw neither of them was going on well, he gathered them together and asked again for a collective party, which they agreed. He attended the party and a dance.<sup id="cite_ref-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<p>Shortly before the <a href="/wiki/Start-of-Term_Feast" title="Start-of-Term Feast">Start-of-Term Feast</a> on <a href="/wiki/1_September" title="1 September">1 September</a>, <a href="/wiki/1991" title="1991">1991</a>, the ghosts came across the first years waiting for <a href="/wiki/Minerva_McGonagall" title="Minerva McGonagall">Professor McGonagall</a>, while discussing whether <a href="/wiki/Peeves" title="Peeves">Peeves</a> should be allowed to attend the Feast (for he had given the house elves quite a bit of trouble, throwing food and breaking dishes). The Friar was excited to see the new students, and smiled around at them. He later attended the Feast, and waved merrily at students being <a href="/wiki/Sorting_ceremony" title="Sorting ceremony">Sorted</a> into <a href="/wiki/Hufflepuff" title="Hufflepuff">Hufflepuff</a>, notably <a href="/wiki/Hannah_Abbott" title="Hannah Abbott">Hannah Abbott</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-PS7_4-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PS7-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>On <a href="/wiki/2_September" title="2 September">2 September</a>, the Friar haunted the <a href="/wiki/Charms_corridor" title="Charms corridor">Charms Corridor</a>, chanting aloud as he hovered around.<sup id="cite_ref-PSps1_10-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PSps1-10">&#91;10&#93;</a></sup>

<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;Harry Potter and the Philosopher&#39;s Stone">"<i>The table on the right cheered and clapped as Hannah went to sit down at the Hufflepuff table. Harry saw the ghost of the Fat Friar waving merrily at her.</i>"</span></dd>
<dd>&#8212;Description of the Friar's cheerful deposition <sup class="noprint"><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Philosopher%27s_Stone" title="Harry Potter and the Philosopher&#39;s Stone">[src]</a></sup></dd>
<dd></dd></dl></div>
<p>The Friar was very cheerful and welcoming, and happily greeted new students at Hogwarts.<sup id="cite_ref-PS7_4-12" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PS7-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> He also tended to find something to value in anyone and look past their faults, as he always insisted with the other ghosts to let <a href="/wiki/Peeves" title="Peeves">Peeves</a> attend the <a href="/wiki/Start-of-Term_Feast" title="Start-of-Term Feast">Start-of-Term Feasts</a> despite his many wrongdoings.<sup id="cite_ref-PS7_4-13" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PS7-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-GF12_13-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GF12-13">&#91;13&#93;</a></sup> He also appeared to be quite sociable, meeting and engaging in conversation with fellow ghosts during <a href="/wiki/Nicholas_de_Mimsy-Porpington" title="Nicholas de Mimsy-Porpington">Nicholas de Mimsy-Porpington</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Nicholas_de_Mimsy-Porpington%27s_five-hundredth_Deathday_Party" title="Nicholas de Mimsy-Porpington&#39;s five-hundredth Deathday Party">five-hundredth Deathday Party</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-CS8_11-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-CS8-11">&#91;11&#93;</a></sup> He also seems to have some House pride, as he happily reminisced about his "old House" when welcoming <a href="/wiki/First_year" title="First year">first-years</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-PS7_4-14" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PS7-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> Despite this, he was always resentful of the fact that he was never made a <a href="/wiki/Cardinal" title="Cardinal">cardinal</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-POT_7-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-POT-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup> His overly kindness has proven to be his undoing, as he would heal the pox simply by poking with a stick, and pull rabbits out of the communion cup, showing recklessness in terms of concealing his wizardry.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Magical_abilities_and_skills">Magical abilities and skills</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Fat_Friar?veaction=edit&amp;section=13" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Fat_Friar?action=edit&amp;section=13" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li><b><a href="/wiki/Healing_magic" title="Healing magic">Healing magic</a></b>: The Fat Friar was a talented <a href="/wiki/Healer" title="Healer">Healer</a>, as he could cure sick individuals <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magically</a> with the touch of his <a href="/wiki/Wand" title="Wand">wand</a>. This meant he was remarkably proficient with the casting of <a href="/wiki/Healing_spell" title="Healing spell">Healing spells</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-POT_7-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-POT-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Transfiguration" title="Transfiguration">Transfiguration</a></b>: The Fat Friar was proficient with this very advanced branch of magic, as he was able to conjure <a href="/wiki/Rabbit" title="Rabbit">rabbits</a> from within the communion cup.<sup id="cite_ref-POT_7-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-POT-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/Conjuration" title="Conjuration">Conjuration</a> is a particularly difficult form of transfiguration, which is proof of his skill in this field.</li></ul>

<ul><li>The Friar was portrayed by <a href="/wiki/Simon_Fisher-Becker" title="Simon Fisher-Becker">Simon Fisher-Becker</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Philosopher%27s_Stone_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Philosopher&#39;s Stone (film)">film adaptation</a> of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Philosopher%27s_Stone" title="Harry Potter and the Philosopher&#39;s Stone">Harry Potter and the Philosopher's Stone</a></i>, the only film he has appeared in.</li>
<li>The Friar is the only House ghost whose death was not discussed in the books. The circumstances of his death were finally revealed via  <i><a href="/wiki/Pottermore" title="Pottermore">Pottermore</a></i>.</li>
<li>The Fat Friar's name is probably a pun on the term "Fat Fryer"; equipment used to deep fry food, such as chips. It is associated with food, as is the Fat Friar and <a href="/wiki/Hufflepuff" title="Hufflepuff">Hufflepuff</a> in general.</li>
<li>Alternatively, the Fat Friar's name could be alliterative and a nod to the character Friar Tuck, as well as the historical <a target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener" class="external text" href="http://www.lib.rochester.edu/camelot/rh/ftchar.htm">Robert Stafford</a>, a real-life chaplain linked to the name Friar Tuck in 1429.</li>
<li>The term "Friar" refers to a man who is a member of a religious order which relies on charity, such as in Catholic Christianity. This ties in with the Friar's religious affiliation during his life.</li></ul>
