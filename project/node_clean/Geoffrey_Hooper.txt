<p><b>Geoffrey Hooper</b> is a student who attended <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> and ended up in the <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor" title="Gryffindor">Gryffindor House</a>. He tried out for the <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor" title="Gryffindor">Gryffindor</a> <a href="/wiki/Keeper" title="Keeper">Keeper</a> position in <a href="/wiki/1995" title="1995">1995</a>. He performed better during his trial than <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ron Weasley</a>, but <a href="/wiki/Angelina_Johnson" title="Angelina Johnson">Angelina Johnson</a> felt that Geoffrey was a whiner and complainer and thus Ron was selected for the position instead.<sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Geoffrey_Hooper?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Geoffrey_Hooper?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The name "Geoffrey" is from a Norman French form of a Germanic name. The second element is Germanic frid "peace", but the first element may be either gawia "territory", walah "stranger" or gisil "hostage".
</p>
