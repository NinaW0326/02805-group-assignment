This <b>individual</b> was the older sister of <a href="/wiki/Ismelda_Murk" title="Ismelda Murk">Ismelda Murk</a>. She attended <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> and was <a href="/wiki/Sorting_Ceremony" class="mw-redirect" title="Sorting Ceremony">sorted</a> into <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor" title="Gryffindor">Gryffindor</a>. She was made a <a href="/wiki/Prefect" title="Prefect">Prefect</a> and, at some point after graduation, joined the <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">British Ministry of Magic</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-HMC_3-11" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HMC-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Her parents were very proud of her achievements, causing them to neglect their younger daughter.<sup id="cite_ref-HMC_3-12" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HMC-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Sometime during <a href="/wiki/1989%E2%80%931990_school_year" title="1989–1990 school year">1989–1990 school year</a> she wanted to visit Ismelda at Hogwarts.<sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
