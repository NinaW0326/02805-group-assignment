This "<b>magic goblin</b>" worked at <a href="/wiki/Gringotts_Wizarding_Bank" title="Gringotts Wizarding Bank">Gringotts Wizarding Bank</a> during the <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard management</a> in <a href="/wiki/1997" title="1997">1997</a>-<a href="/wiki/1998" title="1998">1998</a>, after the <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">Ministry of Magic</a> <a href="/wiki/Fall_of_the_Ministry_of_Magic" title="Fall of the Ministry of Magic">fell</a> to the <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-DHpt2_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DHpt2-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>He was present at the bank when <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>, <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione Granger</a>, <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ronald Weasley</a> and <a href="/wiki/Griphook" title="Griphook">Griphook</a> hosted a <a href="/wiki/Break-in_of_Gringotts_Wizarding_Bank_(1998)" title="Break-in of Gringotts Wizarding Bank (1998)">break-in</a> in order to retrieve one of <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort's</a> <a href="/wiki/Horcrux" title="Horcrux">Horcruxes</a>, <a href="/wiki/Helga_Hufflepuff%27s_Cup" class="mw-redirect" title="Helga Hufflepuff&#39;s Cup">Helga Hufflepuff's Cup</a>, from the <a href="/wiki/Lestrange_Vault" title="Lestrange Vault">Lestrange Vault</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-DHpt2_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DHpt2-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Magic_goblin?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Magic_goblin?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>This goblin was played by <a href="/wiki/Harrison_Davis" title="Harrison Davis">Harrison Davis</a> in <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Deathly_Hallows:_Part_2" title="Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 2">Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 2</a></i>. Despite not being credited, the character is identified as "Magic Goblin" in a Behind the Scenes feature. in DVD2.<sup id="cite_ref-DHpt2_1-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DHpt2-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li>It's unknown why this character is called Magic goblin, as all goblins have magical abilities. Presumably, he was particularly proficient at magic compared to the average Gringotts goblin, and his job in Gringotts may have revolved around this skill.</li></ul>
