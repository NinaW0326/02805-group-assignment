<p>This <b>individual</b> worked as the <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Station_master" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Station master">station master</a> at <a href="/wiki/Aviemore_train_station" title="Aviemore train station">Aviemore train station</a> in <a href="/wiki/1981" title="1981">1981</a>. He speaks in very strong <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Scots_language" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Scots language">Scots</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-Act4Scene2_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Act4Scene2-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>When they inadvertently were brought back in time to <a href="/wiki/1981" title="1981">1981</a> by <a href="/wiki/Delphini" title="Delphini">Delphini</a>, wandless and broomless <a href="/wiki/Albus_Potter" title="Albus Potter">Albus Potter</a> and <a href="/wiki/Scorpius_Malfoy" title="Scorpius Malfoy">Scorpius Malfoy</a> walked from the Hogwarts grounds to Aviemore train station. There, they learned from the oblivious station master that it was the day before <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a> <a href="/wiki/Attack_at_Godric%27s_Hollow_(1981)" title="Attack at Godric&#39;s Hollow (1981)">killed</a> <a href="/wiki/James_Potter_I" title="James Potter I">James</a> and <a href="/wiki/Lily_J._Potter" title="Lily J. Potter">Lily Potter</a>, and the two boys journeyed to <a href="/wiki/Godric%27s_Hollow" title="Godric&#39;s Hollow">Godric's Hollow</a> in the <a href="/wiki/West_Country" title="West Country">West Country</a> to prevent Delphini from changing history.<sup id="cite_ref-Act4Scene2_3-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Act4Scene2-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
