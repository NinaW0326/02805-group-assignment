Representing his or her school, the young potioneer, under the guidance of <a href="/wiki/Zygmunt_Budge" title="Zygmunt Budge">Zygmunt Budge</a>, won the <a href="/wiki/Wizarding_Schools_Potions_Championship" title="Wizarding Schools Potions Championship">Wizarding Schools Potions Championship</a> and invented the <a href="/wiki/Potion_of_All_Potential" title="Potion of All Potential">Potion of All Potential</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="History">History</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_potions_champion?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: History">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_potions_champion?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: History"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_potions_champion?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_potions_champion?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>The British or Irish wizard or witch received their <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magical</a> education from <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a>. During their years at the school, the individual found the <a href="/wiki/Book_of_Potions" title="Book of Potions">Book of Potions</a> in the library, classified as dangerous by the librarian. In the book, the individual found the lingering spirit of <a href="/wiki/16th_century" title="16th century">16th century</a> potioneer <a href="/wiki/Zygmunt_Budge" title="Zygmunt Budge">Zygmunt Budge</a>. Zygmunt Budge, still angry centuries later at being unable to enter the <a href="/wiki/Wizarding_Schools_Potions_Championship" title="Wizarding Schools Potions Championship">Wizarding Schools Potions Championship</a> (because he was 14 at the time, three years younger than the minimum age), revealed that he wished to guide the wizard to winning the championship using the  <a href="/wiki/Book_of_Potions" title="Book of Potions">Book of Potions</a>. Under his assistance, the individual first produced a <a href="/wiki/Cure_for_Boils" title="Cure for Boils">Cure for Boils</a> and cured a <a href="/wiki/House-elf" title="House-elf">House-elf</a>.
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Wizarding_Schools_Potions_Championship">Wizarding Schools Potions Championship</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_potions_champion?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Wizarding Schools Potions Championship">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_potions_champion?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Wizarding Schools Potions Championship"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Using a <a href="/wiki/Portkey" title="Portkey">Portkey</a> disguised as a boot, the individual, aged 17 or above, entered the <a href="/wiki/Wizarding_Schools_Potions_Championship" title="Wizarding Schools Potions Championship">Wizarding Schools Potions Championship</a> against a <a href="/wiki/Koldovstoretz_potions_champion" title="Koldovstoretz potions champion">Koldovstoretz student</a>, a <a href="/wiki/Mahoutokoro_potions_champion" title="Mahoutokoro potions champion">Mahoutokoro student</a>, and an <a href="/wiki/Uagadou_potions_champion" title="Uagadou potions champion">Uagadou student</a>. The individual arrived late, creating a highly unorthodox situation. However, they still were able to participate due to a magical binding contract requiring him to compete. 
</p><p>The <a href="/wiki/Mahoutokoro_potions_champion" title="Mahoutokoro potions champion">Mahoutokoro potions champion</a> first went across the bridge, awakening a <a href="/wiki/River_Troll" class="mw-redirect" title="River Troll">River Troll</a>. In order to pass, the Hogwarts potions champion produced a <a href="/wiki/Shrinking_Solution" title="Shrinking Solution">Shrinking Solution</a> and used it to incapacitate the troll.
</p><p>Soon after, the <a href="/wiki/Koldovstoretz_potions_champion" title="Koldovstoretz potions champion">Koldovstoretz potions champion</a> arrived at the battlefield but was knocked out by a <a href="/wiki/Red_Cap" title="Red Cap">Red Cap</a> he had disturbed while crossing it. In order to keep up and have any chance at winning, the Hogwarts potions champion had to safely get through the field. To do this, he or she brewed a <a href="/wiki/Beautification_Potion" title="Beautification Potion">Beautification Potion</a> and used it on the Red Caps to scare them away, and then safely crossed the field.
</p>
<figure class="thumb tright show-info-icon" style="width: 302px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/d/d8/Serpenthill.png/revision/latest?cb=20140823193536" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/d/d8/Serpenthill.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/300?cb=20140823193536" decoding="async" width="300" height="237" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="Serpenthill.png" data-image-key="Serpenthill.png" /></a> 	 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:Serpenthill.png" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption">The <a href="/wiki/Snake" title="Snake">Serpent</a> surrounding the hill</p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure>
<p>The individual then encountered a giant thick-hided venomous <a href="/wiki/Snake" title="Snake">Serpent</a> surrounding the hill. He or she then produced a <a href="/wiki/Sleeping_Potion" title="Sleeping Potion">Sleeping Potion</a> to make the serpent fall asleep. The potioneer continued on, now able to cross the hill without the danger of the serpent.
</p><p>Thus the Hogwarts potions champion completed the first stage of the championship and moved on to begin the second stage at third place, behind the <a href="/wiki/Mahoutokoro_potions_champion" title="Mahoutokoro potions champion">Mahoutokoro potions champion</a> at first and the <a href="/wiki/Uagadou_potions_champion" title="Uagadou potions champion">Uagadou potions champion</a> at second.
</p><p>In the forest, the individual encountered the next challenge, a <a href="/wiki/Banshee" title="Banshee">Banshee</a> in the forest. Wearing ear plugs, the potions champion avoided the Banshee's cry and poured a <a href="/wiki/Laughing_Potion" title="Laughing Potion">Laughing Potion</a> down its throat, reducing the Banshee to harmless laughter.
</p><p>The individual continued along a path to the Golden Cauldron. However, the path, which led through a cave, was blocked by a swarm of <a href="/wiki/Doxy" title="Doxy">Doxys</a>. The potioneer used an Atomiser to spray <a href="/wiki/Doxycide" title="Doxycide">Doxycide</a> and open a way through the swarm.
</p><figure class="thumb tleft show-info-icon" style="width: 120px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/a/a5/PotionOfAllPotential.png/revision/latest?cb=20151105004259" class="image"><img alt="PotionOfAllPotential.png" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/a/a5/PotionOfAllPotential.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/118?cb=20151105004259" decoding="async" width="118" height="250" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="PotionOfAllPotential.png" data-image-key="PotionOfAllPotential.png" /></a> 	 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:PotionOfAllPotential.png" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 	</figcaption> </figure><p>
The Hogwarts potions champion arrived first at the Golden Cauldron, the prize of the Championship. The cauldron was caged and surrounded by a multitude of locks. To obtain the cauldron, a potioneer had to choose choose the correct lock and open it with a key on first try. The Hogwarts potions champion brewed and drank a batch of <a href="/wiki/Felix_Felicis" title="Felix Felicis">Felix Felicis</a>, and used its luck to choose the correct lock and unlock the cauldron.
</p><p class="mw-empty-elt"></p>
<figure class="thumb tright show-info-icon" style="width: 222px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/5/50/Newpotion.png/revision/latest?cb=20160601233551" class="image"><img alt="" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw%3D%3D" decoding="async" width="220" height="149" class="thumbimage lazyload" data-image-name="Newpotion.png" data-image-key="Newpotion.png" data-src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/5/50/Newpotion.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/220?cb=20160601233551" /></a> 	<noscript><a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/5/50/Newpotion.png/revision/latest?cb=20160601233551" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/5/50/Newpotion.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/220?cb=20160601233551" decoding="async" width="220" height="149" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="Newpotion.png" data-image-key="Newpotion.png" data-src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/5/50/Newpotion.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/220?cb=20160601233551" /></a></noscript> 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:Newpotion.png" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption">The effects of the potion invented by the champion on a toad (left), as compared to a normal one (right)</p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure>
<p>However, the final test before winning the Championship was for the potioneer to use the Golden Cauldron and brew a potion of their own invention. The Hogwarts potion champion brewed the <a href="/wiki/Potion_of_All_Potential" title="Potion of All Potential">Potion of All Potential</a>, that could enhance the drinker and realise all that is best of them. With this legendary potion, the Hogwarts potioneer won the <a href="/wiki/Wizarding_Schools_Potions_Championship" title="Wizarding Schools Potions Championship">Wizarding Schools Potions Championship</a>.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Magical_abilities_and_skills">Magical abilities and skills</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_potions_champion?veaction=edit&amp;section=4" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_potions_champion?action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li><b><a href="/wiki/Potion" title="Potion">Potions</a></b>: This individual was a remarkably gifted <a href="/wiki/Potioneer" title="Potioneer">potioneer</a>, who was incredibly talented in the brewing of a wide variety of different <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magical</a> solutions and concoctions. They were so proficient in this field of magic that they qualified as the Hogwarts Potions Champion for the <a href="/wiki/Wizarding_Schools_Potions_Championship" title="Wizarding Schools Potions Championship">Wizarding Schools Potions Championship</a>, which they applied their exceptional skills and knowledge of potions and their ingredients to brew various different potions to overcome several highly difficult tasks. They were even able to brew the legendary <a href="/wiki/Potion_of_All_Potential" title="Potion of All Potential">Potion of All Potential</a> and win the competition, showing their skills as a young potioneer to have been truly outstanding.</li></ul>
