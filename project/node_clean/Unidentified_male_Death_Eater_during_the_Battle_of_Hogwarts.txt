<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_male_Death_Eater_during_the_Battle_of_Hogwarts?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_male_Death_Eater_during_the_Battle_of_Hogwarts?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>This Death Eater took part at the <a href="/wiki/Meeting_at_Malfoy_Manor" title="Meeting at Malfoy Manor">Meeting at Malfoy Manor</a>. He sat directly across from <a href="/wiki/Severus_Snape" title="Severus Snape">Severus Snape</a>, quite close to the seat of Voldemort.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>
When <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> went to the <a href="/wiki/Forbidden_Forest" title="Forbidden Forest">Forbidden Forest</a>, this man was one of the Death Eaters who stood guard and reported that Harry wasn't anywhere to be seen. He was among the Death Eaters who watched Voldemort cast the <a href="/wiki/Killing_Curse" title="Killing Curse">Killing Curse</a> on Harry. He later was present to witness Harry jumping from <a href="/wiki/Rubeus_Hagrid" title="Rubeus Hagrid">Hagrid</a>'s arms in the <a href="/wiki/Viaduct_Courtyard" title="Viaduct Courtyard">Viaduct Courtyard</a>. Following this, he flees from the battle once Harry was revealed to be alive. He presumably was arrested following Lord Voldemort's defeat or otherwise is on the run.<sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup></p><figure class="thumb tleft show-info-icon" style="width: 182px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/6/60/TonyKirwood.png/revision/latest?cb=20111002100034" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/6/60/TonyKirwood.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/180?cb=20111002100034" decoding="async" width="180" height="101" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="TonyKirwood.png" data-image-key="TonyKirwood.png" /></a> 	 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:TonyKirwood.png" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption">The Death Eater sitting directly across from <a href="/wiki/Severus_Snape" title="Severus Snape">Severus Snape</a> during the <a href="/wiki/Meeting_at_Malfoy_Manor" title="Meeting at Malfoy Manor">Meeting at Malfoy Manor</a></p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_male_Death_Eater_during_the_Battle_of_Hogwarts?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_male_Death_Eater_during_the_Battle_of_Hogwarts?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>This individual is portrayed by <a href="/wiki/Peter_G._Reed" title="Peter G. Reed">Peter G. Reed</a> in <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Deathly_Hallows:_Part_1" title="Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 1">Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 1</a></i> and <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Deathly_Hallows:_Part_2" title="Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 2">Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 2</a></i>.</li>
<li>In the book, <a href="/wiki/Antonin_Dolohov" title="Antonin Dolohov">Antonin Dolohov</a> is the <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eater</a> to say "No sign of him, my Lord." However in the scene of the <a href="/wiki/Forbidden_Forest" title="Forbidden Forest">Forbidden Forest</a> in the movie, this unidentified Death Eater is the man to say this line.</li>
<li>In the book, <a href="/wiki/Thorfinn_Rowle" title="Thorfinn Rowle">Thorfinn Rowle</a> is the Death Eater to say "QUIET!" to silence Hagrid in the Forbidden Forest, whilst in the movie, this Death Eater says it instead.</li>
<li>In a Behind the Scenes clip, this man is seen <a href="/wiki/Apparition" title="Apparition">Disapparating</a>, this probably means he fled the castle as the battle continued, meaning he was not one of the most faithful Death Eaters.</li></ul>
