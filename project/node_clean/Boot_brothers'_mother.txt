<b>Mrs Boot</b> (d. mid-<a href="/wiki/1620s" title="1620s">1620s</a> to early <a href="/wiki/1630s" title="1630s">1630s</a>) was an <a href="/wiki/Ireland" title="Ireland">Irish</a> <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch</a> who travelled to the <a href="/wiki/United_States_of_America" title="United States of America">New World</a> with the <a href="/wiki/Plymouth_settlement" title="Plymouth settlement">Plymouth settlement</a> in the <a href="/wiki/17th_century" title="17th century">17th century</a>. She was the mother of <a href="/wiki/Chadwick_Boot" title="Chadwick Boot">Chadwick</a> and <a href="/wiki/Webster_Boot" title="Webster Boot">Webster Boot</a>, two founders of <a href="/wiki/Ilvermorny_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Ilvermorny School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Ilvermorny School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-Pottermore_1-12" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Pottermore-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Boot_brothers%27_mother?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Boot_brothers%27_mother?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Mrs Boot and <a href="/wiki/Boot_brothers%27_father" title="Boot brothers&#39; father">her husband</a> were originally from <a href="/wiki/Ireland" title="Ireland">Ireland</a>. In the <a href="/wiki/1620s" title="1620s">1620s</a>, they decided to move to the <a href="/wiki/United_States_of_America" title="United States of America">New World</a> with their young sons, <a href="/wiki/Chadwick_Boot" title="Chadwick Boot">Chadwick</a> and <a href="/wiki/Webster_Boot" title="Webster Boot">Webster</a>, in search of an adventure. They travelled with the <a href="/wiki/Plymouth_settlement" title="Plymouth settlement">Plymouth settlement</a>, befriending <a href="/wiki/James_Steward" title="James Steward">James Steward</a>, an <a href="/wiki/England" title="England">English</a> <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggle</a>, on their journey across the <a href="/wiki/Atlantic_Ocean" title="Atlantic Ocean">Atlantic Ocean</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-Pottermore_1-13" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Pottermore-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>However, when the family left the settlement to venture into the nearby woods for their adventure, they were attacked by a <a href="/wiki/Hidebehind" title="Hidebehind">Hidebehind</a>. Mrs Boot and her husband were killed, but their sons were saved by <a href="/wiki/Isolt_Sayre" title="Isolt Sayre">Isolt Sayre</a> and <a href="/wiki/William_(Pukwudgie)" title="William (Pukwudgie)">William the Pukwudgie</a>, and subsequently nursed back to health.<sup id="cite_ref-Pottermore_1-14" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Pottermore-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Isolt returned to the site of the attack a two weeks later, intending to give the brothers' parents a decent burial, only to find James Steward in the process of digging graves. James had become concerned when his friends went missing, and thus went looking for them in the woods.<sup id="cite_ref-Pottermore_1-15" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Pottermore-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Mrs Boot's sons were adopted by Isolt and James. Isolt took her <a href="/wiki/Wand" title="Wand">wand</a>, which had a <a href="/wiki/Unicorn" title="Unicorn">unicorn</a> <a href="/wiki/Unicorn_hair" title="Unicorn hair">tail hair</a> <a href="/wiki/Wand_core" title="Wand core">core</a>, along with her husband's wand. However, both wands were broken and their cores eventually died, meaning they could not be passed on to the couple's sons. Nonetheless, Isolt was able to study the couple's wands, and this helped her to craft new wands for her adopted sons.<sup id="cite_ref-Pottermore_1-16" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Pottermore-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Mrs Boot's sons would go on to help found <a href="/wiki/Ilvermorny_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Ilvermorny School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Ilvermorny School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a>. Through her son Chadwick, she become an ancestor of <a href="/wiki/Calderon-Boot_family" title="Calderon-Boot family">Calderon-Boot family</a>, and through her son Webster, an ancestor of later British Boots (including <a href="/wiki/Terry_Boot" title="Terry Boot">Terry Boot</a>).<sup id="cite_ref-Pottermore_1-17" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Pottermore-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
