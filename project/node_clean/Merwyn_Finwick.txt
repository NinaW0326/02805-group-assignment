<strong class="mw-selflink selflink">Merwyn Finwick</strong> was a <a href="/wiki/Great_Britain" title="Great Britain">British</a> <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> and the <a href="/wiki/Keeper" title="Keeper">Keeper</a> for the <a href="/wiki/Tutshill_Tornados" title="Tutshill Tornados">Tutshill Tornados</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/Brevis_Birch" title="Brevis Birch">Brevis Birch</a>, the team's <a href="/wiki/Quidditch_Captain#Hogwarts_Quidditch_Captains" title="Quidditch Captain">captain</a>, blamed their unexpected loss in a match against the <a href="/wiki/Ballycastle_Bats" title="Ballycastle Bats">Ballycastle Bats</a> on a bout of <a href="/wiki/Narcolepsy" title="Narcolepsy">narcolepsy</a> suffered by Merwyn.<sup id="cite_ref-wizardsb_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-wizardsb-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> The Tornados had been at the top of the <a href="/wiki/British_and_Irish_Quidditch_League" title="British and Irish Quidditch League">league</a> before this defeat.<sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
