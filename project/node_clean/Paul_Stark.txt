</p><p><a href="/wiki/Remus_Lupin" title="Remus Lupin">Remus Lupin</a> owned a trunk produced by Paul Stark.<sup id="cite_ref-trunk_1-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-trunk-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
