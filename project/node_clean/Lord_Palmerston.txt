<b>Henry John Temple, 3rd Viscount Palmerston</b> (<a href="/wiki/20_October" title="20 October">20 October</a>, <a href="/wiki/1784" class="mw-redirect" title="1784">1784</a> â€“ <a href="/wiki/18_October" title="18 October">18 October</a>, <a href="/wiki/1865" class="mw-redirect" title="1865">1865</a>) was the Muggle <a href="/wiki/Prime_Minister_(title)" title="Prime Minister (title)">Prime Minister</a> from <a href="/wiki/6_February" title="6 February">6 February</a>, <a href="/wiki/1855" class="mw-redirect" title="1855">1855</a> to <a href="/wiki/19_February" title="19 February">19 February</a>, <a href="/wiki/1858" class="mw-redirect" title="1858">1858</a> and again from <a href="/wiki/12_June" title="12 June">12 June</a>, <a href="/wiki/1859" class="mw-redirect" title="1859">1859</a> to <a href="/wiki/18_October" title="18 October">18 October</a>, <a href="/wiki/1865" class="mw-redirect" title="1865">1865</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-Wikipedia_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Wikipedia-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>The <a href="/wiki/Minister_for_Magic" title="Minister for Magic">Minister for Magic</a>, <a href="/wiki/Priscilla_Dupont" title="Priscilla Dupont">Priscilla Dupont</a>, developed an irrational loathing of him. This attained such proportions that it ended up in her being forced to step down on <a href="/wiki/17_February" title="17 February">17 February</a>, <a href="/wiki/1858" class="mw-redirect" title="1858">1858</a>, after such incidents as coins turning to frogspawn in his coat pockets.<sup id="cite_ref-PMministers_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PMministers-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Ironically, Palmerston was forced to resign by the Muggles two days later on <a href="/wiki/19_February" title="19 February">19 February</a>, <a href="/wiki/1858" class="mw-redirect" title="1858">1858</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-PMministers_2-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PMministers-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> He was later reelected on <a href="/wiki/12_June" title="12 June">12 June</a>, <a href="/wiki/1859" class="mw-redirect" title="1859">1859</a> and died in office.<sup id="cite_ref-Wikipedia_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Wikipedia-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
