The <a href="/wiki/Ravenclaw" title="Ravenclaw">Ravenclaw</a> student <a href="/wiki/Raymond" title="Raymond">Raymond</a> owned a <b>kitten</b> when he attended <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-PSG_1-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PSG-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="History">History</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Raymond%27s_kitten?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: History">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Raymond%27s_kitten?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: History"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>She fell down one of the <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Castle" title="Hogwarts Castle">Castle</a>'s wells in <a href="/wiki/1991" title="1991">1991</a>. Raymond was distraught because of this, and tried to reach her, but the well was too deep. <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> found her when he was lost in the <a href="/wiki/Dungeons" title="Dungeons">Dungeons</a>, inside an <a href="/wiki/Potions_Classroom_adjoining_room" title="Potions Classroom adjoining room">adjoining room</a>, which linked to the <a href="/wiki/Bottom_of_the_Well" title="Bottom of the Well">Bottom of the Well</a>, as well as other rooms. Raymond was very pleased to have her back. Raymond later allowed <a href="/wiki/Nicholas_de_Mimsy-Porpington" title="Nicholas de Mimsy-Porpington">Nearly Headless Nick</a> to use her for an experiment in the <i>Upper Castle</i> in which students had to chase her around on unstable rocky platforms while she dropped purple <a href="/wiki/Bertie_Bott%27s_Every_Flavour_Beans" title="Bertie Bott&#39;s Every Flavour Beans">Bertie Bott's Every Flavour Beans</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-PSG_1-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PSG-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
