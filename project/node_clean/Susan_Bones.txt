<p><b>Susan Bones</b> (b. <a href="/wiki/1979" title="1979">1979</a>/<a href="/wiki/1980" title="1980">1980</a>) was a <a href="/wiki/Half-blood" title="Half-blood">half-blood</a> <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch</a><sup id="cite_ref-HPM_2-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HPM-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> and a <a href="/wiki/Hufflepuff" title="Hufflepuff">Hufflepuff</a> student at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> from <a href="/wiki/1991" title="1991">1991</a>-<a href="/wiki/1998" title="1998">1998</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-OP16_5-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OP16-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> She was a member of the prominent <a href="/wiki/Bones_family" title="Bones family">Bones family</a>, a family with strong connections to the <a href="/wiki/Ministry_of_Magic" title="Ministry of Magic">Ministry of Magic</a>.
</p><p>Susan's entire <a href="/wiki/Bones_family" title="Bones family">family</a>, including her grandparents, <a href="/wiki/Edgar_Bones" title="Edgar Bones">uncle</a>, <a href="/wiki/Edgar_Bones%27s_wife" class="mw-redirect" title="Edgar Bones&#39;s wife">aunt</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Edgar_Bones%27s_children" class="mw-redirect" title="Edgar Bones&#39;s children">cousins</a> were all murdered personally by <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a> during the <a href="/wiki/First_Wizarding_War" title="First Wizarding War">First Wizarding War</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-OP25_8-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OP25-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup> and her other aunt <a href="/wiki/Amelia_Bones" title="Amelia Bones">Amelia Bones</a> was <a href="/wiki/Killing_Curse" title="Killing Curse">murdered</a> by Voldemort in <a href="/wiki/July" title="July">July</a> of <a href="/wiki/1996" title="1996">1996</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP1_9-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP1-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup> In her fifth year, Susan became a member of <a href="/wiki/Dumbledore%27s_Army" title="Dumbledore&#39;s Army">Dumbledore's Army</a>, an organisation taught and led by <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-OP16_5-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OP16-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> Susan fought along side her fellow D.A. members in the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a>.
</p><p>It can be assumed that Susan survived the <a href="/wiki/Second_Wizarding_War" title="Second Wizarding War">Second Wizarding War</a>, as her name was not listed amongst the casualties of the fallen deceased. What happened to Susan afterwards is unknown.
</p>

<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_life">Early life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Susan_Bones?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Early life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Susan_Bones?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Early life"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Little is known about Susan's early life, other than the fact that she was born to at least one wizard parent, and presumably named after her aunt <a href="/wiki/Amelia_Bones" title="Amelia Bones">Amelia</a>'s middle name. Susan came from a largely magical family background, and also a rather powerful and respected family.
</p><p>Due to her family's position, they were sought after during the <a href="/wiki/First_Wizarding_War" title="First Wizarding War">First Wizarding War</a> by <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a> and a large majority were killed. Susan was born in or around <a href="/wiki/1980" title="1980">1980</a>, a year before the war ended.
</p>

<p>Susan Bones started her education at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> in <a href="/wiki/1991" title="1991">1991</a> the same year as the famous <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>. Susan shared a <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_boats" title="Hogwarts boats">boat</a> with <a href="/wiki/Seamus_Finnigan" title="Seamus Finnigan">Seamus Finnigan</a> and <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione Granger</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-PSF_10-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PSF-10">&#91;10&#93;</a></sup> She was the third student to be called upon by <a href="/wiki/Minerva_McGonagall" title="Minerva McGonagall">Minerva McGonagall</a> for the <a href="/wiki/Sorting_ceremony" title="Sorting ceremony">Sorting ceremony</a> and was Sorted into <a href="/wiki/Hufflepuff" title="Hufflepuff">Hufflepuff House</a><sup id="cite_ref-PS7_4-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PS7-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> the Head of Hufflepuff House at the time was <a href="/wiki/Pomona_Sprout" title="Pomona Sprout">Pomona Sprout</a>, the <a href="/wiki/Herbology" title="Herbology">Herbology</a> <a href="/wiki/Professor" title="Professor">Professor</a>. She was sorted into this house along with <a href="/wiki/Hannah_Abbott" title="Hannah Abbott">Hannah Abbott</a>, <a href="/wiki/Justin_Finch-Fletchley" title="Justin Finch-Fletchley">Justin Finch-Fletchley</a>, <a href="/wiki/Wayne_Hopkins" title="Wayne Hopkins">Wayne Hopkins</a>, <a href="/wiki/Megan_Jones" title="Megan Jones">Megan Jones</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Ernest_Macmillan" title="Ernest Macmillan">Ernie Macmillan</a> all of whom she presumably became friends with. It is also assumed that Hufflepuff house is the house that many of her relatives were Sorted into as well.
</p><p>During her first year, Susan would learn all about the basics of <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magic</a> and <a href="/wiki/Spell" title="Spell">spell</a> performance, like her other classmates. She was also present at the <a href="/wiki/End-of-Term_Feast" title="End-of-Term Feast">End-of-Term Feast</a> during which Gryffindor won the <a href="/wiki/House_Cup" title="House Cup">House Cup</a> breaking Slytherin's seven year winning streak.
</p>

<p>During her <a href="/wiki/1993-1994_school_year" class="mw-redirect" title="1993-1994 school year">third year</a>, she would have to select what subjects she wished to take. The school would also be in believed danger from Azkaban escapee, <a href="/wiki/Sirius_Black" title="Sirius Black">Sirius Black</a>, who was suspected to be looking for Harry Potter.
</p>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Fourth_year">Fourth year</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Susan_Bones?veaction=edit&amp;section=7" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Fourth year">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Susan_Bones?action=edit&amp;section=7" title="Edit section: Fourth year"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<p>During Susan's <a href="/wiki/1994%E2%80%931995_school_year" title="1994–1995 school year">fourth year</a> Hogwarts held the <a href="/wiki/Triwizard_Tournament" title="Triwizard Tournament">Triwizard Tournament</a> and Hufflepuff's <a href="/wiki/Cedric_Diggory" title="Cedric Diggory">Cedric Diggory</a> was one of the Triwizard champions. It is likely that Susan Bones supported Cedric Diggory in favour of Harry Potter, as he was in her house and both her best friends <a href="/wiki/Hannah_Abbott" title="Hannah Abbott">Hannah Abbott</a> and <a href="/wiki/Ernest_Macmillan" title="Ernest Macmillan">Ernie Macmillan</a> were strong supporters of Diggory.
</p>

<p>Cedric Diggory was technically the joint-winner with Harry Potter after he and Harry both reached the <a href="/wiki/Triwizard_Cup" title="Triwizard Cup">Triwizard Cup</a>, thus making them the champions. Sadly Diggory lost his life to <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a> after being <a href="/wiki/Portkey" title="Portkey">taken</a> to <a href="/wiki/Little_Hangleton" title="Little Hangleton">Little Hangleton</a> and the <a href="/wiki/Second_Wizarding_War" title="Second Wizarding War">Second Wizarding War</a> began. The students were horrified to learn of Cedric's death, mainly students of <a href="/wiki/Hufflepuff" title="Hufflepuff">Hufflepuff</a>, who were closest to him, but the relationship of Susan with Cedric is unknown. It is more than likely that Susan attended the <a href="/wiki/Memorial_feast_to_Cedric_Diggory" title="Memorial feast to Cedric Diggory">Memorial feast to Cedric Diggory</a> at the end of the school year.
</p>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Fifth_year">Fifth year</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Susan_Bones?veaction=edit&amp;section=8" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Fifth year">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Susan_Bones?action=edit&amp;section=8" title="Edit section: Fifth year"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>

<p>In <a href="/wiki/1995" title="1995">1995</a>, Hogwarts was to be under the watchful eye of the Minister and his personally appointed <a href="/wiki/Defence_Against_the_Dark_Arts" title="Defence Against the Dark Arts">Defence Against the Dark Arts</a> teacher <a href="/wiki/Dolores_Umbridge" title="Dolores Umbridge">Dolores Umbridge</a>. Umbridge worked with Susan's aunt in the Ministry, and both served on the court for Harry Potter's trial. When Umbridge came to Hogwarts, she ensured that no practical defensive spells would be taught in her lessons, despite the claims that Lord Voldemort had returned. Thus she did not teach anything worth learning.
</p>

<p>With the students looking for a teacher, and Harry with the best knowledge of defensive and offensive spells, Hermione Granger pledged the idea of forming an alliance to learn spells and rebel against Umbridge. This would be called <a href="/wiki/Dumbledore%27s_Army" title="Dumbledore&#39;s Army">Dumbledore's Army</a>, which Susan would become a member of. During the first meeting, Susan was curious to know whether Harry could produce a corporeal <a href="/wiki/Patronus_Charm" title="Patronus Charm">Patronus</a>, a fact which she had been told by her aunt; when Harry asked if she knew Madam Bones, Susan confirmed the familial relationship.<sup id="cite_ref-OP16_5-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OP16-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Harry later gave lessons on Patronuses, but it is unknown whether Susan produced one or what form it took. Later on in the year, the <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a> who murdered her uncle <a href="/wiki/Edgar_Bones" title="Edgar Bones">Edgar</a> and his family escaped from <a href="/wiki/Azkaban" title="Azkaban">Azkaban</a>, the wizarding prison, and many students questioned Susan for information about this. After the Death Eaters' escape, Susan told Harry that she understood how he felt, knowing that the person who had murdered his family was at large and constantly being asked questions about it. Harry finally had someone who could almost relate to his state of mind. Susan was pestered throughout the year by students asking about her family and its murdered members, which she found distressing.<sup id="cite_ref-OP25_8-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OP25-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<p>On top of dealing with the murders of her family members, this year was also her <a href="/wiki/Ordinary_Wizarding_Level" title="Ordinary Wizarding Level">O.W.L.</a> year at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts</a>, which left her in even greater stress as she attempted to keep it all together and do well in her exams. Also, on at least one occasion Vincent Crabbe and Gregory Goyle took it upon themselves to bully Susan, making fun of her aunt and complaining that she was interfering with the <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">Ministry of Magic</a>'s agenda. They went so far as to take her wand, and <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry</a>, <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ron</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione</a> had to come to her aid. The trio duelled the two Slytherins, defeating them and forcing them to leave Susan alone. Susan got her wand back and learned about the D.A. meeting in the <a href="/wiki/Room_of_Requirement" title="Room of Requirement">Room of Requirement</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTPG_7-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTPG-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>At the end of the year, Susan was among the D.A. members who came to Harry's aid when he was attacked on the <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Express" title="Hogwarts Express">Hogwarts Express</a> by Malfoy, Crabbe and Goyle, her own erstwhile bullies. Susan and her friends all unleashed a volley of curses which reduced the three to gigantic slugs.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP38_12-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP38-12">&#91;12&#93;</a></sup>

<p>During the summer holidays before her <a href="/wiki/1997-1998_school_year" class="mw-redirect" title="1997-1998 school year">sixth year</a> at Hogwarts, one of Susan's last living relatives and one of the greatest witches of her time, <a href="/wiki/Amelia_Bones" title="Amelia Bones">Amelia Bones</a>, was personally murdered by Lord Voldemort in her own home, though to her credit she did not go down without a fight.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP1_9-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP1-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup> This caused Susan some distress and worry, as most of her family had been killed during the <a href="/wiki/First_Wizarding_War" title="First Wizarding War">First Wizarding War</a> and now that the <a href="/wiki/Second_Wizarding_War" title="Second Wizarding War">Second Wizarding War</a> had been declared official it was only a matter of time before <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a> sought out after the remaining members of her family. When Susan returned to Hogwarts, she was learning how to <a href="/wiki/Apparition" title="Apparition">Apparate</a> and on one occasion <a href="/wiki/Splinching" title="Splinching">Splinched</a> herself during one of the lessons, losing one of her legs. Her leg was successfully reattached, but the incident appeared to leave Susan somewhat shaken.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP18_13-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP18-13">&#91;13&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Later on in the school year, Hogwarts fell <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_the_Astronomy_Tower" title="Battle of the Astronomy Tower">under attack</a> by Death Eaters. The D.A. members were called to arms via <a href="/wiki/Dumbledore%27s_Army_coin" title="Dumbledore&#39;s Army coin">their coins</a>, and despite the murder of her aunt, Susan did not show up for battle, and possibly try and avenge her, it is possible that since the D.A. meetings were no longer held that Susan no longer carried her coin around with her and did not notice the call to arms message.
</p>

<p>Not much is known about the relationship of Susan with her family. Susan was a member of the <a href="/wiki/Bones_family" title="Bones family">Bones family</a>, a family of many great witches and wizards. The Bones family supported the <a href="/wiki/Order_of_the_Phoenix" title="Order of the Phoenix">Order of the Phoenix</a> during the <a href="/wiki/First_Wizarding_War" title="First Wizarding War">First Wizarding War</a>. <p>Dumbledore's Army was an organisation founded by <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>, <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ron Weasley</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione Granger</a>, and led by Harry. Susan became a member during her fifth year, in order to be taught <a href="/wiki/Defence_Against_the_Dark_Arts" title="Defence Against the Dark Arts">Defence Against the Dark Arts</a> by Harry, as <a href="/wiki/Dolores_Umbridge" title="Dolores Umbridge">Dolores Umbridge</a>, the new D.A.D.A. professor, didn't teach them how to properly defend themselves, thinking they would oppose the <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">Ministry of Magic</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-OP16_5-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OP16-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Susan became a member, along with her fellow <a href="/wiki/Hufflepuff" title="Hufflepuff">Hufflepuffs</a> <a href="/wiki/Hannah_Abbott" title="Hannah Abbott">Hannah Abbott</a>, <a href="/wiki/Ernest_Macmillan" title="Ernest Macmillan">Ernie Macmillan</a>, <a href="/wiki/Justin_Finch-Fletchley" title="Justin Finch-Fletchley">Justin Finch-Fletchley</a> and <a href="/wiki/Zacharias_Smith" title="Zacharias Smith">Zacharias Smith</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-OP16_5-10" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OP16-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> Susan shared a dormitory with <a href="/wiki/Hannah_Abbott" title="Hannah Abbott">Hannah Abbott</a> (a fellow Hufflepuff) and was most probably invited to join <a href="/wiki/Dumbledore%27s_Army" title="Dumbledore&#39;s Army">Dumbledore's Army</a> by her. She may have been also a close friend of Ernie and Justin. It is unknown if she was a friend of Zacharias, too. Susan and her fellow housemates (except Zacharias) fought for <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts</a> at the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a>.
</p><p>She was very impressed by learning that Harry could cast a Patronus, and she also told him how she felt about her relatives' deaths during the <a href="/wiki/First_Wizarding_War" title="First Wizarding War">First Wizarding War</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-OP16_5-11" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OP16-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>She was also seemed to be a friend of <a href="/wiki/Seamus_Finnigan" title="Seamus Finnigan">Seamus Finnigan</a>, whom she shared a boat with on her first year and was often seen talking to him in lessons and in <a href="/wiki/Dumbledore%27s_Army" title="Dumbledore&#39;s Army">Dumbledore's Army</a> meetings.<sup id="cite_ref-PSF_10-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PSF-10">&#91;10&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Other members of the army she possibly knew are <a href="/wiki/Ginevra_Weasley" title="Ginevra Weasley">Ginny Weasley</a>, <a href="/wiki/Parvati_Patil" title="Parvati Patil">Parvati Patil</a>, <a href="/wiki/Lavender_Brown" title="Lavender Brown">Lavender Brown</a>, <a href="/wiki/Dean_Thomas" title="Dean Thomas">Dean Thomas</a>, <a href="/wiki/Padma_Patil" title="Padma Patil">Padma Patil</a>, <a href="/wiki/Luna_Lovegood" title="Luna Lovegood">Luna Lovegood</a>, <a href="/wiki/Neville_Longbottom" title="Neville Longbottom">Neville Longbottom</a>, <a href="/wiki/Alicia_Spinnet" title="Alicia Spinnet">Alicia Spinnet</a>, <a href="/wiki/Angelina_Johnson" title="Angelina Johnson">Angelina Johnson</a>, <a href="/wiki/Katie_Bell" title="Katie Bell">Katie Bell</a>, <a href="/wiki/Fred_and_George_Weasley" title="Fred and George Weasley">Fred and George Weasley</a>, <a href="/wiki/Colin_Creevey" title="Colin Creevey">Colin</a> and <a href="/wiki/Dennis_Creevey" title="Dennis Creevey">Dennis Creevey</a>, <a href="/wiki/Terry_Boot" title="Terry Boot">Terry Boot</a>, <a href="/wiki/Anthony_Goldstein" title="Anthony Goldstein">Anthony Goldstein</a>, <a href="/wiki/Michael_Corner" title="Michael Corner">Michael Corner</a>, <a href="/wiki/Cho_Chang" title="Cho Chang">Cho Chang</a> and <a href="/wiki/Marietta_Edgecombe" title="Marietta Edgecombe">Marietta Edgecombe</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-OP16_5-12" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OP16-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> The majority of them fought at the Battle of Hogwarts and their fate is ambiguous.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Susan_Bones?veaction=edit&amp;section=17" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Susan_Bones?action=edit&amp;section=17" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>"Susan" is the Anglicised form of Susanna, meaning Lily or Rose.<sup id="cite_ref-15" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-15">&#91;15&#93;</a></sup> The lily is often a symbol of purity. It is noted that Susan is most likely named after her Aunt <a href="/wiki/Amelia_Bones" title="Amelia Bones">Amelia Bones</a>, whose middle name is Susan.</li>
<li>"Bones" is the word for the skeletal form of a being, many members of Susan's family are murdered or have been killed during the books and this may be in reference to this.</li></ul>

<ul><li>In the first two films, she was played by <a href="/wiki/Eleanor_Columbus" title="Eleanor Columbus">Eleanor Columbus</a>, the daughter of director <a href="/wiki/Chris_Columbus" title="Chris Columbus">Chris Columbus</a>, making her one of the few <a href="/wiki/United_States_of_America" title="United States of America">American</a> actresses to appear in the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_(film_series)" title="Harry Potter (film series)">films</a> as one of the students. However, she had no dialogue. She was portrayed by <a href="/wiki/Emma_Jayne-Corboz" class="mw-redirect" title="Emma Jayne-Corboz">Emma Jayne-Corboz</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Deathly_Hallows_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows (film)">film adaptations</a> of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Deathly_Hallows" title="Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows">Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows</a></i>.</li>
<li>She is one of the Original Forty students listed by <a href="/wiki/J._K._Rowling" title="J. K. Rowling">J. K. Rowling</a> along with <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>, <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione Granger</a> and <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ronald Weasley</a>.</li>
<li>In the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Order_of_the_Phoenix_(video_game)" title="Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix (video game)">video game adaptation</a> of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Order_of_the_Phoenix" title="Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix">Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix</a></i>, Susan was voiced by <a href="/wiki/Alex_Tregear" title="Alex Tregear">Alex Tregear</a>.</li>
<li>Susan wears her hair in a long plait down her back, however in the films and video games she is shown to leave her hair down.</li>
<li>The middle name of Amelia Bones is Susan, which could imply that Susan was named after her aunt, or that the name Susan runs in the Bones family.</li>
<li>In the <a target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener" class="external text" href="http://atrossbooks.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/03/Harry-Potter-and-the-Order-of-the-Phoenix-2nd-draft.pdf">earliest draft</a> of the fifth film, it is Susan who says "<i>That is most remarkable, Mr Potter</i>".</li>
<li>In the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Order_of_the_Phoenix_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix (film)">film adaptation</a> of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Order_of_the_Phoenix" title="Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix">Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix</a></i>, Susan is omitted and one of Susan's lines is spoken instead by <a href="/wiki/Luna_Lovegood" title="Luna Lovegood">Luna Lovegood</a>.</li>
<li>In the books, the sorting is done in alphabetical order, but in the films, it is not, and in 1991, Susan is the third sorted. She shows relief when put into Hufflepuff, suggesting that her family may all be in Hufflepuff. Susan is the only non-major character in the series whose Sorting is shown onscreen (the others being Harry, Ron, Hermione, Draco, and Lily, though the latter was in another year and shown in a memory.)</li></ul>
