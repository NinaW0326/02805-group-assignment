These <b>individuals</b> were the <a href="/wiki/Muggle" class="mw-redirect" title="Muggle">Muggle</a> parents of a <a href="/wiki/Unidentified_2000s_Hogwarts_student" title="Unidentified 2000s Hogwarts student">child</a> who was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch or wizard</a>. They lived in the early <a href="/wiki/21st_century" title="21st century">21st century</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-Video_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Video-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_2000s_Hogwarts_student%27s_parents?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_2000s_Hogwarts_student%27s_parents?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>At one point in between <a href="/wiki/1998" title="1998">1998</a> and <a href="/wiki/2004" title="2004">2004</a>, they had a <a href="/wiki/Unidentified_2000s_Hogwarts_student" title="Unidentified 2000s Hogwarts student">child</a>. When their child was young, they were taken on holidays. Their child soon began to cause strange things to happen, but when they told their parents, they laughed and thought they had to be seeing things.<sup id="cite_ref-Video_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Video-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>At the age of eleven, their child found out about their magical heritage by <a href="/wiki/Rubeus_Hagrid" title="Rubeus Hagrid">Rubeus Hagrid</a>, and began attending <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-Video_1-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Video-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
