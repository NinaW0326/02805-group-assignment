<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_goblin_stockbroker?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_goblin_stockbroker?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="1991">1991</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_goblin_stockbroker?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: 1991">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_goblin_stockbroker?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: 1991"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>By the end of the summer of <a href="/wiki/1991" title="1991">1991</a>, this goblin worked at <a href="/wiki/Gringotts_Wizarding_Bank" title="Gringotts Wizarding Bank">Gringotts Wizarding Bank</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-Rusty_Goffe_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Rusty_Goffe-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="1993">1993</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_goblin_stockbroker?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: 1993">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_goblin_stockbroker?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: 1993"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>During the <a href="/wiki/Christmas_holidays" title="Christmas holidays">Christmas holidays</a> in <a href="/wiki/1993" title="1993">1993</a>, this goblin was among a group of carollers in <a href="/wiki/Hogsmeade" title="Hogsmeade">Hogsmeade Village</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-Rusty_Goffe_1-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Rusty_Goffe-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> He was rudely pushed aside by an angry and <a href="/wiki/Cloak_of_Invisibility" title="Cloak of Invisibility">Invisibility Cloaked</a>-<a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-POA_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-POA-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="1995">1995</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_goblin_stockbroker?veaction=edit&amp;section=4" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: 1995">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_goblin_stockbroker?action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: 1995"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>On <a href="/wiki/11_August" title="11 August">11 August</a> <a href="/wiki/1995" title="1995">1995</a>, this goblin lost a large amount of gold trading on the <a href="/wiki/Potions_market" title="Potions market">potions market</a>. The <a href="/wiki/12_August" title="12 August">following day</a>, he went to the <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">Ministry of Magic</a> Headquarters, in <a href="/wiki/London" title="London">London</a>, to take care of business, where he told a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> the "terrible day" he had the day before.<sup id="cite_ref-OP_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OP-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="1996">1996</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_goblin_stockbroker?veaction=edit&amp;section=5" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: 1996">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_goblin_stockbroker?action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: 1996"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>By <a href="/wiki/1996" title="1996">1996</a>, this goblin still worked at <a href="/wiki/Gringotts_Wizarding_Bank" title="Gringotts Wizarding Bank">Gringotts</a>, on the then-almost deserted <a href="/wiki/Diagon_Alley" title="Diagon Alley">Diagon Alley</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-Rusty_Goffe_1-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Rusty_Goffe-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="1998">1998</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_goblin_stockbroker?veaction=edit&amp;section=6" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: 1998">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_goblin_stockbroker?action=edit&amp;section=6" title="Edit section: 1998"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>It is unknown what happened to him after when <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a> took over the <a href="/wiki/Ministry_of_Magic" title="Ministry of Magic">Ministry of Magic</a> and took control over <a href="/wiki/Gringotts_Wizarding_Bank" title="Gringotts Wizarding Bank">Gringotts</a>.
</p>
