<p><b>Marilyn</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch</a> and eccentric <a href="/wiki/Hufflepuff" title="Hufflepuff">Hufflepuff</a> student at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts</a> during the <a href="/wiki/1990s" title="1990s">1990s</a>. She collected <a href="/wiki/Beetle" title="Beetle">beetle</a> pincers in her spare time as a hobby.<sup id="cite_ref-PSG_2-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PSG-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> She had a younger brother named <a href="/wiki/Arthur_(Hufflepuff_student)" title="Arthur (Hufflepuff student)">Arthur</a>, who were both described by other students as "being a bit off", meaning others found them somewhat strange.<sup id="cite_ref-COSG_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COSG-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
