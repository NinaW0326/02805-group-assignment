<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Paper_shop_girl?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Paper_shop_girl?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>During the <a href="/wiki/Christmas" title="Christmas">Christmas</a> holidays of <a href="/wiki/1996" title="1996">1996</a>, she was visited by <a href="/wiki/Fred_Weasley" title="Fred Weasley">Fred</a> and <a href="/wiki/George_Weasley" title="George Weasley">George Weasley</a>; the latter showed her card tricks that she found extremely impressive, unaware that George was using real <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magic</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP16_1-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP16-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>The Weasley twins mentioned her to <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> and their younger brother <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ron</a> when they announced that they were leaving <a href="/wiki/The_Burrow" title="The Burrow">the Burrow</a> for the village, and refused to assist Harry and Ron in peeling sprouts. This was partly because Ron had thrown a knife at Fred, who taunted him about his romance with <a href="/wiki/Lavender_Brown" title="Lavender Brown">Lavender Brown</a>, suggesting that she must have suffered "extensive brain damage" to be dating him.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP16_1-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP16-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
