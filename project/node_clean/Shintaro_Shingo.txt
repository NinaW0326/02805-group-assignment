<b>Shintaro Shingo</b><sup id="cite_ref-LB_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-LB-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> was a Japanese wizard who played as a <a href="/wiki/Beater" title="Beater">Beater</a> for the <a href="/wiki/2014" title="2014">2014</a> <a href="/wiki/Japanese_National_Quidditch_team" title="Japanese National Quidditch team">Japanese National Quidditch team</a>. During the seventh round match of the <a href="/wiki/2014_Quidditch_World_Cup" title="2014 Quidditch World Cup">427th Quidditch World Cup</a>, against <a href="/wiki/Polish_National_Quidditch_team" title="Polish National Quidditch team">Poland</a>, Shingo, along with fellow Beater <a href="/wiki/Masaki_Hongo" title="Masaki Hongo">Hongo</a>, was largely responsible for Japan's decisive victory by preventing Poland's <a href="/wiki/Seeker" title="Seeker">Seeker</a>, Wladyslaw Wolfke from catching the Snitch.<sup id="cite_ref-PM_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PM-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
