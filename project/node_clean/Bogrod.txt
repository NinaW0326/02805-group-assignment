<p><b>Bogrod</b> (d. <a href="/wiki/1_May" title="1 May">1 May</a>, <a href="/wiki/1998" title="1998">1998</a>) was a <a href="/wiki/Goblin" title="Goblin">Goblin</a> and a bank teller at <a href="/wiki/Gringotts_Wizarding_Bank" title="Gringotts Wizarding Bank">Gringotts Wizarding Bank</a> in the late <a href="/wiki/1990s" title="1990s">1990s</a>. In <a href="/wiki/1998" title="1998">1998</a>, during their <a href="/wiki/Break-in_of_Gringotts_Wizarding_Bank_(1998)" title="Break-in of Gringotts Wizarding Bank (1998)">break-in</a> of <a href="/wiki/Gringotts_Wizarding_Bank" title="Gringotts Wizarding Bank">Gringotts Wizarding Bank</a>, <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>, <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ronald Weasley</a> and <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione Granger</a> put Bogrod under the <a href="/wiki/Imperius_Curse" title="Imperius Curse">Imperius Curse</a> in order to enter <a href="/wiki/Lestrange_Vault" title="Lestrange Vault">Lestrange Vault</a> undetected. Whilst the trio tried to escape, Bogrod was <a href="/wiki/Fire" title="Fire">burnt</a> to <a href="/wiki/Death" title="Death">death</a> by the <a href="/wiki/Gringott%27s_Dragon" title="Gringott&#39;s Dragon">security dragon</a>.
</p>

<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="The_Calamity">The Calamity</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Bogrod?veaction=edit&amp;section=4" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: The Calamity">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Bogrod?action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: The Calamity"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>During the <a href="/wiki/Calamity" title="Calamity">Calamity</a> which affected the <a href="/wiki/Wizarding_world" title="Wizarding world">Wizarding world</a> in the <a href="/wiki/2010s" title="2010s">2010s</a>, a <a href="/wiki/Foundable" title="Foundable">Foundable</a> <a href="/wiki/Memory" title="Memory">memory</a> of Bogrod could be found trapped in a <a href="/wiki/Atmospheric_Charm" title="Atmospheric Charm">storm</a> <a href="/wiki/Confoundable" title="Confoundable">Confoundable</a>. Members of the <a href="/wiki/Statute_of_Secrecy_Task_Force" title="Statute of Secrecy Task Force">Statute of Secrecy Task Force</a> had to free him with <i><a href="/wiki/Meteolojinx_Recanto" title="Meteolojinx Recanto">Meteolojinx Recanto</a></i>, which eradicated the storm cloud and sent the Foundable back to its original place.<sup id="cite_ref-WU_4-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-WU-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> 
</p>

<ul><li>Bogrod was portrayed by <a href="/wiki/Jon_Key" title="Jon Key">Jon Key</a> in <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Deathly_Hallows:_Part_2" title="Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 2">Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 2</a></i>.<sup id="cite_ref-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li>Despite this line was cut from the final film, the original <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Deathly_Hallows:_Part_2" title="Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 2">Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 2</a></i> screenplay included a line in which an <a href="/wiki/Imperius_Curse" title="Imperius Curse">Imperiused</a> Bogrod intimidates another Goblin to let "Madam Lestrange" into her vault by saying "<i>I've run this bank for forty-five years, long before anyone had even heard of a Death Eater. The day may come that they feel they can run this place better than me, but that day has not yet arrived. Am I understood?</i>"<sup id="cite_ref-script_6-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-script-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup> The novel implies, however, that even though Bogrod enjoys some seniority (being assisted by a younger goblin on command), he does not run the bank: another goblin reminds him that they are to follow higher instructions regarding <a href="/wiki/Bellatrix_Lestrange" title="Bellatrix Lestrange">Bellatrix Lestrange</a>.</li>
<li>In the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Deathly_Hallows:_Part_2" title="Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 2">film adaptation</a>&#160;of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Deathly_Hallows" title="Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows">Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows</a></i>, Bogrod fails to escape with <a href="/wiki/Griphook" title="Griphook">Griphook</a> and is scorched to death by the dragon. In the book, what happened to Bogrod is unspecified, and we last hear of him when Ron and Hermione struggle to keep him from falling under the tide of multiplying gold at the <a href="/wiki/Lestrange_Vault" title="Lestrange Vault">Lestrange Vault</a>. His remains were never shown except in <i><a href="/wiki/LEGO_Harry_Potter:_Years_5-7" title="LEGO Harry Potter: Years 5-7">LEGO Harry Potter: Years 5-7</a></i> where his skeleton appears after getting burned.</li></ul>
