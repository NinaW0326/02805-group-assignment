<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Bozo?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Bozo?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="1992">1992</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Bozo?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: 1992">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Bozo?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: 1992"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<div class="quote">
<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets">"<i>Out of the way, there. This is for the </i>Daily Prophet<i>.</i>"</span></dd>
<dd>&#8212;Bozo treats Ron Weasley inconsiderately in pursuit of the perfect shot<sup class="noprint"><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Chamber_of_Secrets" title="Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets">[src]</a></sup></dd>
<dd></dd></dl></div><p>Bozo<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> covered <a href="/wiki/Gilderoy_Lockhart" title="Gilderoy Lockhart">Gilderoy Lockhart</a>'s book-signing at <a href="/wiki/Flourish_and_Blotts" title="Flourish and Blotts">Flourish and Blotts</a> in <a href="/wiki/1992" title="1992">1992</a>, and was so single-mindedly focused on getting a good shot at one point that he backed into <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ronald Weasley</a>, stepping on his foot as he snapped at him to get out of the way. When Lockhart saw that <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> was in the crowd, he pulled Harry to the front and shook his hand, deliberately giving the photographer an opportunity to take pictures he felt would be "worth the front page."<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p><h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Triwizard_Tournament">Triwizard Tournament</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Bozo?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Triwizard Tournament">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Bozo?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Triwizard Tournament"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Bozo was present at the <a href="/wiki/Wand_weighing_ceremony" title="Wand weighing ceremony">wand weighing ceremony</a> preceding the 1994 <a href="/wiki/Triwizard_Tournament" title="Triwizard Tournament">Triwizard Tournament</a>, accompanying Rita Skeeter to do a piece on the tournament for the <i>Prophet</i>. He appeared to be entranced by <a href="/wiki/Fleur_Delacour" title="Fleur Delacour">Fleur Delacour</a>, as he attempted to bring her into greater prominence of the group shots he was taking, and was watching her out of the corner of his eye during the ceremony.<sup id="cite_ref-GOF18_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF18-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Later that school year, Bozo accompanied Rita on a visit to the <a href="/wiki/Three_Broomsticks_Inn" title="Three Broomsticks Inn">Three Broomsticks Inn</a>. While there, Rita conferred with Bozo on the possibility of spinning a story out of <a href="/wiki/Ludovic_Bagman" title="Ludovic Bagman">Ludovic Bagman</a> being pursued by a group of <a href="/wiki/Goblin" title="Goblin">goblins</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Bozo?veaction=edit&amp;section=5" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Bozo?action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>A bozo is a foolish or incompetent person.<sup id="cite_ref-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li>The Tagalog word <i>boso</i> which means 'to maliciously watch/peek at somebody through a hole or slit.'<sup id="cite_ref-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li><a target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener" class="external text" href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bozo_the_Clown">Bozo</a> is also the name of an iconic American clown, and is at times considered a generic term for a clown.</li></ul>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Bozo?veaction=edit&amp;section=6" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Bozo?action=edit&amp;section=6" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>Bozo was portrayed by <a href="/wiki/Peter_O%27Farrell" title="Peter O&#39;Farrell">Peter O'Farrell</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Chamber_of_Secrets_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets (film)">film adaptation</a> of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Chamber_of_Secrets" title="Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets">Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets</a></i> and <a href="/wiki/Robert_Wilfort" title="Robert Wilfort">Robert Wilfort</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Goblet_of_Fire_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire (film)">film adaptation</a> of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Goblet_of_Fire" title="Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire">Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire</a></i>.</li>
<li>The photographer from <i>Chamber of Secrets</i> is not identified as the same wizard as Bozo in the books.  <i><a href="/wiki/Pottermore" title="Pottermore">Pottermore</a></i>, however, confirms them to be the same character by giving them a singular entry that describes them being the same person.</li>
<li>In the second novel as well as the GBA version of the video game, Bozo was shown to have a rude personality, but in the film adaptation, he appears to have good manners.</li></ul>
