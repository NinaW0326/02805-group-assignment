<p><b>Pablo Diego José Francisco de Paula Juan Nepomuceno María de los Remedios Cipriano de la Santísima Trinidad Clito Ruiz y Picasso</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggle</a> artist. His works are known in both the <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggle</a> and the <a href="/wiki/Wizarding_world" title="Wizarding world">Wizarding world</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> However, it is also possible that <a href="/wiki/Severus_Snape" title="Severus Snape">Severus Snape</a> only knew about him because he was a <a href="/wiki/Half-blood" title="Half-blood">half-blood</a>, and therefore had more knowledge of the <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggle</a> world than the average wizard.
</p>
