This <b>wizard</b> was appointed <a href="/wiki/Headmaster" title="Headmaster">Headmaster</a> of <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts</a> at some unknown time. After his death, his <a href="/wiki/Portrait" title="Portrait">portrait</a> was hung in the <a href="/wiki/Headmaster%27s_office" class="mw-redirect" title="Headmaster&#39;s office">Headmaster's office</a>, in order to give advice to the current head. This wizard's hair is in the style of portraits painted during the reign of King Charles II of England (<a href="/wiki/1630" class="mw-redirect" title="1630">1630</a> - <a href="/wiki/1685" class="mw-redirect" title="1685">1685</a>).<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> He was portrayed sleeping on an chair with a book in hand.
</p>
