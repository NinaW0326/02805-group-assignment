<p><b>Cormac McLaggen</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> and member of the <a href="/wiki/McLaggen_family" title="McLaggen family">McLaggen family</a>, a family known for being "<i>big in the <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">Ministry</a></i>". He attended <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> from <a href="/wiki/1990" title="1990">1990</a> to <a href="/wiki/1997" title="1997">1997</a>. He was Sorted into <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor" title="Gryffindor">Gryffindor</a> house and was one year above the famous <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>.
</p><p>McLaggen lost the opportunity to try out for his house's <a href="/wiki/Quidditch" title="Quidditch">Quidditch</a> <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor_Quidditch_team" title="Gryffindor Quidditch team">team</a> in his sixth year after he ate <a href="/wiki/Poison" title="Poison">poisonous</a> <a href="/wiki/Doxy_egg" title="Doxy egg">Doxy eggs</a> on a bet, but became the reserve <a href="/wiki/Keeper" title="Keeper">Keeper</a> in his <a href="/wiki/1996" title="1996">seventh year</a>. During the course of his last year at Hogwarts, he also became a member of the <a href="/wiki/Slug_Club" title="Slug Club">Slug Club</a>, escorted <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione Granger</a> to the club's <a href="/wiki/Slug_Club_Christmas_party" title="Slug Club Christmas party">Christmas party</a>, and was primarily responsible for the Gryffindor Quidditch team's embarrassing loss to <a href="/wiki/Hufflepuff" title="Hufflepuff">Hufflepuff</a> by hitting Harry Potter (seeker at the time) over the head with a beater's bat.
</p><p>Like most <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor" title="Gryffindor">Gryffindor</a> <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">students</a>, McLaggen participated in the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">final battle</a> of the <a href="/wiki/Second_Wizarding_War" title="Second Wizarding War">Second Wizarding War</a> and fought alongside the defenders of Hogwarts. McLaggen survived the battle, it is currently unknown what happened to McLaggen after the war.
</p>

<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_life">Early life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Cormac_McLaggen?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Early life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Cormac_McLaggen?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Early life"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>

<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince"><b>Horace Slughorn</b>: "<i>Now, you, Cormac, I happen to know you see a lot of your Uncle Tiberius, because he has a rather splendid picture of the two of you hunting nogtails in, I think, Norfolk?</i>"</span></dd>

<p>Little is known of McLaggen's early life, though it appears that he was born into a relatively influential wizarding family, as his uncle <a href="/wiki/Tiberius" title="Tiberius">Tiberius</a> was an important official in the <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">Ministry of Magic</a> and was friendly with the head of the <a href="/wiki/Auror" title="Auror">Auror</a> Office and later <a href="/wiki/Minister_for_Magic" title="Minister for Magic">Minister for Magic</a> <a href="/wiki/Rufus_Scrimgeour" title="Rufus Scrimgeour">Rufus Scrimgeour</a>. Tiberius took his nephew hunting <a href="/wiki/Nogtail" title="Nogtail">Nogtails</a> on at least one occasion, with Scrimgeour and <a href="/wiki/Bertie_Higgs" title="Bertie Higgs">Bertie Higgs</a> in <a href="/wiki/Norfolk" title="Norfolk">Norfolk</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP7_5-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP7-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_years">Early years</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Cormac_McLaggen?veaction=edit&amp;section=4" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Early years">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Cormac_McLaggen?action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Early years"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<p>Cormac McLaggen started his education at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> in <a href="/wiki/1990" title="1990">1990</a> and was Sorted into <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor" title="Gryffindor">Gryffindor</a>, along with <a href="/wiki/Katie_Bell" title="Katie Bell">Katie Bell</a> who also was a Sorted Gryffindor that year. Cormac was a skilled flyer on a broom and <a href="/wiki/Quidditch" title="Quidditch">Quidditch</a> player, specifically playing the position of <a href="/wiki/Keeper" title="Keeper">Keeper</a>.
</p>

<p>In <a href="/wiki/1995%E2%80%931996_school_year" title="1995–1996 school year">1995–1996 school year</a>, McLaggen's <a href="/wiki/Sixth_year" title="Sixth year">sixth year</a> at Hogwarts, he ate a pound of <a href="/wiki/Poison" title="Poison">poisonous</a> <a href="/wiki/Doxy_egg" title="Doxy egg">Doxy eggs</a> on a bet to prove his bravery. This foolhardy decision kept him from joining the <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor_Quidditch_team" title="Gryffindor Quidditch team">Gryffindor Quidditch team</a>, something he cared a great deal about. With <a href="/wiki/Oliver_Wood" title="Oliver Wood">Oliver Wood</a>'s graduation at the end of the <a href="/wiki/1993%E2%80%931994_school_year" title="1993–1994 school year">1993–1994 school year</a> and the <a href="/wiki/Triwizard_Tournament" title="Triwizard Tournament">Triwizard Tournament</a> cancelling all <a href="/wiki/Quidditch" title="Quidditch">Quidditch</a> matches the following year, the Gryffindor team was in need of a <a href="/wiki/Keeper" title="Keeper">Keeper</a> at the beginning of the <a href="/wiki/1995%E2%80%931996_school_year" title="1995–1996 school year">1995–1996 school year</a>. Unfortunately for McLaggen, he was in the <a href="/wiki/Hospital_Wing" title="Hospital Wing">Hospital Wing</a> being treated for doxy egg poisoning at the time of the trials, and <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ron Weasley</a> was made <a href="/wiki/Keeper" title="Keeper">Keeper</a> instead.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP11_8-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP11-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<p>The following year, McLaggen's last, he met Professor <a href="/wiki/Horace_Slughorn" title="Horace Slughorn">Horace Slughorn</a> on the <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Express" title="Hogwarts Express">Hogwarts Express</a>. Because of his well-connected uncle <a href="/wiki/Tiberius" title="Tiberius">Tiberius</a>, McLaggen was invited to be a member of the Slug Club.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP7_5-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP7-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<p>Soon after his seventh year began, McLaggen tried out for the <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor_Quidditch_team" title="Gryffindor Quidditch team">Gryffindor Quidditch team</a>, but he managed to make only four out of five saves. This was because Hermione Granger, who overheard McLaggen making rude remarks about <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ron</a> and <a href="/wiki/Ginevra_Weasley" title="Ginevra Weasley">Ginny Weasley</a>, had dishonestly placed a <a href="/wiki/Confundus_Charm" title="Confundus Charm">Confundus Charm</a> on him. McLaggen angrily asked team captain <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> for another try, but Harry refused, and Ron was given the position of <a href="/wiki/Keeper" title="Keeper">Keeper</a> again, as he beat McLaggen in saves.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP11_8-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP11-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>In December, McLaggen attended Professor Slughorn's <a href="/wiki/Slug_Club_Christmas_party" title="Slug Club Christmas party">Christmas party</a> with Hermione, who was also a member of the Slug Club. The evening did not go well for him, however, as Hermione only asked him out to get back at Ron dating <a href="/wiki/Lavender_Brown" title="Lavender Brown">Lavender Brown</a> to spite her. McLaggen ended up driving her crazy by talking about himself excessively, boasting about his Quidditch abilities, and being too forward romantically. She avoided him for most of the night to talk to Harry and <a href="/wiki/Luna_Lovegood" title="Luna Lovegood">Luna Lovegood</a>. McLaggen asked Harry if he had seen her later, which Harry denied, and was irritated to find that she had left. After eating a foul tasting <a href="/wiki/Dragon_tartare" title="Dragon tartare">dragon tartare</a>, McLaggen vomited on <a href="/wiki/Severus_Snape" title="Severus Snape">Severus Snape</a>'s shoes, earning himself <a href="/wiki/Discipline_at_Hogwarts" title="Discipline at Hogwarts">detention</a> for the next month.<sup id="cite_ref-HBPf_10-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBPf-10">&#91;10&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<p>When Ron was hospitalised in <a href="/wiki/March" title="March">March</a>, before Gryffindor's second game against <a href="/wiki/Hufflepuff" title="Hufflepuff">Hufflepuff</a>, Harry Potter was forced to play McLaggen as his reserve Keeper. In the week leading up to the match, Harry had his doubts about his new team-mate. Although he was skilled, McLaggen was also bossy and repeatedly tried to advise Harry on game strategy and insulted the other players' abilities. The match was a no-holds-barred disaster, as McLaggen spent much more time trying to tell the rest of the team how to play rather than watching the rings, resulting in several goals for the opponent. This culminated in him wrestling a <a href="/wiki/Beater%27s_bat" title="Beater&#39;s bat">Beater's bat</a> away from <a href="/wiki/Jimmy_Peakes" title="Jimmy Peakes">Jimmy Peakes</a> and attempting to show him how to hit a <a href="/wiki/Bludger" title="Bludger">Bludger</a>, only to knock Harry off his broomstick and crack his skull.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP19_4-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP19-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>The fiasco was possibly Gryffindor's worst and most embarrassing loss ever (a final score of 320-60), and Ron told Harry in the <a href="/wiki/Hospital_Wing" title="Hospital Wing">Hospital Wing</a> that he thought McLaggen's enraged teammates <i>"might've dealt with him before you get out of here..."</i><sup id="cite_ref-HBP19_4-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP19-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> When Ron recovered, he resumed the <a href="/wiki/Keeper" title="Keeper">Keeper</a> position while Cormac was permanently removed from the team.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Physical_appearance">Physical appearance</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Cormac_McLaggen?veaction=edit&amp;section=8" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Physical appearance">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Cormac_McLaggen?action=edit&amp;section=8" title="Edit section: Physical appearance"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>McLaggen was described as a large, wire-haired youth.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP7_5-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP7-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> His face went red whenever he became angry. At the <a href="/wiki/Keeper" title="Keeper">Keeper</a> tryouts in <a href="/wiki/1996" title="1996">1996</a>, <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> thought that McLaggen was large enough that he could block all three goal hoops without even moving.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP11_8-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP11-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Personality_and_traits">Personality and traits</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Cormac_McLaggen?veaction=edit&amp;section=9" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Personality and traits">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Cormac_McLaggen?action=edit&amp;section=9" title="Edit section: Personality and traits"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<div class="quote">
<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince">"<i>You should have heard the way he was talking about Ron and Ginny! He's got a nasty temper. You wouldn't want someone like that on the team.</i>"</span></dd>
<dd>&#8212;McLaggen's foul and rude behaviour<sup class="noprint"><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Half-Blood_Prince" title="Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince">[src]</a></sup></dd>

<figure class="thumb tleft show-info-icon" style="width: 252px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/9/95/Ron_cormac.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20090724060942" class="image"><img alt="" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw%3D%3D" decoding="async" width="250" height="142" class="thumbimage lazyload" data-image-name="Ron cormac.jpg" data-image-key="Ron_cormac.jpg" data-src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/9/95/Ron_cormac.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/250?cb=20090724060942" /></a> 	<noscript><a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/9/95/Ron_cormac.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20090724060942" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/9/95/Ron_cormac.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/250?cb=20090724060942" decoding="async" width="250" height="142" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="Ron cormac.jpg" data-image-key="Ron_cormac.jpg" data-src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/9/95/Ron_cormac.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/250?cb=20090724060942" /></a></noscript> 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:Ron_cormac.jpg" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption">Cormac attempting to intimidate Ron Weasley</p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure>
<p>Aggressive, arrogant, and self-righteous, McLaggen epitomised the most negative aspects of the stereotypical <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor" title="Gryffindor">Gryffindor</a> characteristics. Though brave, he was foolhardy rather than self-sacrificing. He was also arrogant and pushy and felt a strong sense of entitlement.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP19_4-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP19-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> According to <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione Granger</a>, McLaggen was rude, insensitive, and over-assertive when making romantic advances. All he did was talk about himself and lacked manners and consideration of others. It was due to this that Hermione remarked that <a href="/wiki/Grawp" title="Grawp">Grawp</a> (Hagrid's <a href="/wiki/Giant" title="Giant">giant</a> half-brother) was more mannered than Cormac.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP15_7-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP15-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup> 
</p><p>McLaggen also had a bad temper; for example, <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> thought he might <a href="/wiki/Muggle_duelling" title="Muggle duelling">punch</a> him for refusing his demand to give him another <a href="/wiki/Quidditch" title="Quidditch">Quidditch</a> tryout.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP11_8-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP11-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup> He also blamed others, doing so with <a href="/wiki/Ginevra_Weasley" title="Ginevra Weasley">Ginny Weasley</a> for not properly shooting at her brother Ron during <a href="/wiki/Keeper" title="Keeper">Keeper</a> tryouts. When Harry was forced to put McLaggen on the team as a substitute Keeper, McLaggen proved to be a poor team player and a poor Keeper overall. He spent much more time in the match offering unwanted observations of others rather than concentrating on what he was meant to be doing in the game.
</p><p>Regardless of his negative traits, Cormac proved himself quite brave and righteous, willingly joining his allies in the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a> despite having no obligation to do so, and actually acting as one of the leaders of <a href="/wiki/Dumbledore%27s_Army" title="Dumbledore&#39;s Army">Dumbledore's Army</a> during <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort's</a> reign over <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts</a>, alongside <a href="/wiki/Neville_Longbottom" title="Neville Longbottom">Neville Longbottom</a>, <a href="/wiki/Ginevra_Weasley" title="Ginevra Weasley">Ginny Weasley</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Luna_Lovegood" title="Luna Lovegood">Luna Lovegood</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-DHF2_9-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DHF2-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<p>In his seventh year, McLaggen became familiar with <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione Granger</a> when they were both members of the <a href="/wiki/Slug_Club" title="Slug Club">Slug Club</a>. Hermione seemed to think that McLaggen was arrogant and unpleasant, and when she overheard him insult <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ron</a> and <a href="/wiki/Ginevra_Weasley" title="Ginevra Weasley">Ginny Weasley</a> during <a href="/wiki/Quidditch" title="Quidditch">Quidditch</a> try-outs, she hit him with a <a href="/wiki/Confundus_Charm" title="Confundus Charm">Confundus Charm</a>, unbeknownst to him.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP7_5-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP7-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>However, when Hermione and Ron later had a row over Ron’s romance with <a href="/wiki/Lavender_Brown" title="Lavender Brown">Lavender Brown</a>, she tried to make him jealous by asking McLaggen to attend <a href="/wiki/Horace_Slughorn" title="Horace Slughorn">Professor Slughorn</a>’s <a href="/wiki/Slug_Club_Christmas_party" title="Slug Club Christmas party">Christmas party</a> with her, choosing the person she thought would annoy Ron the most. He accepted, but spent most of the evening boasting about himself, to her irritation. He was very forward with her under the mistletoe, prompting Hermione to abandon him to seek out <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> and <a href="/wiki/Luna_Lovegood" title="Luna Lovegood">Luna Lovegood</a> and to avoid him for the rest of the party. He was very annoyed when he was unable to find her.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP15_7-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP15-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup> This indicates that while McLaggen was romantically interested in Hermione, she found him to be a rude braggart.
</p>

<p>Although they were in the same House, Harry strongly disliked McLaggen. The two first met in <a href="/wiki/1996" title="1996">1996</a> when they were both questioned by <a href="/wiki/Horace_Slughorn" title="Horace Slughorn">Professor Slughorn</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP7_5-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP7-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> They met again when McLaggen tried out for the <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor_Quidditch_team" title="Gryffindor Quidditch team">Gryffindor Quidditch team</a>, of which Harry was the <a href="/wiki/Quidditch_Captain#Hogwarts_Quidditch_Captains" title="Quidditch Captain">Captain</a>. It seemed that McLaggen wrongly expected preferential treatment from Harry because the two were both in the <a href="/wiki/Slug_Club" title="Slug Club">Slug Club</a>.
</p><p>When <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ron Weasley</a> beat McLaggen in saves, he accused <a href="/wiki/Ginevra_Weasley" title="Ginevra Weasley">Ginny Weasley</a> of purposely giving her brother an advantage. He angrily confronted Harry about it, who coldly refuted the accusations. Harry thought that McLaggen might punch him for a moment.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP11_8-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP11-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup> Later in the year, when McLaggen temporarily replaced Ron, he annoyed Harry immensely by repeatedly offering advice on game strategy, and spent so much time ordering around his team-mates and knocking Harry out instead of blocking goals that <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor" title="Gryffindor">Gryffindor</a> ultimately lost the match.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP19_4-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP19-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Cormac also disliked Harry because he was best friends with <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione Granger</a> (whom Cormac fancied). Cormac and Harry also seemed to both represent different aspects of Gryffindor: with Cormac personifying the negative traits such as arrogance and self-righteousness, while Harry embodied the positive traits of chivalry, courage, and having a self-sacrificing nature. However, they both seemed to have nasty tempers.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Cormac_McLaggen?veaction=edit&amp;section=15" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Cormac_McLaggen?action=edit&amp;section=15" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The name <i>Cormac</i> is borne by a number of figures from Irish legend and history, including <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Cormac_mac_Airt" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Cormac mac Airt">Cormac mac Airt</a>, a warrior king of <a href="/wiki/Ireland" title="Ireland">Ireland</a>, and <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Cormac_Cond_Longas" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Cormac Cond Longas">Cormac Cond Longas</a>, an exiled prince of Ulster. The name is most commonly translated as "charioteer", but can be also translated into "raven"- a bird strongly associated with warfare in Celtic mythology. This may allude to McLaggen's aggressive personality. The name can also be translated to mean "son of defilement", from the Gaelic <i>corb</i>, "defilement", and <i>mac</i>, "son".<sup id="cite_ref-12" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-12">&#91;12&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
