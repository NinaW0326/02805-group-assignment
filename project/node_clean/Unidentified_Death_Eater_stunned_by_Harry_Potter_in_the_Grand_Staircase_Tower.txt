This <b>individual</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Dark_Wizard" title="Dark Wizard">Dark Wizard</a> and <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eater</a> who participated in the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a> during the <a href="/wiki/Second_Wizarding_War" title="Second Wizarding War">Second Wizarding War</a>.
</p><p>This man was one of the first Death Eaters to break into <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> after the <a href="/wiki/Protective_enchantment" title="Protective enchantment">Protective Enchantments</a> were broken. He was seen flying through the <a href="/wiki/Marble_Staircase_Tower" title="Marble Staircase Tower">tower</a> of the <a href="/wiki/Grand_Staircase" title="Grand Staircase">Grand Staircase</a>, but was Stunned by <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>. The Death Eater subsequently collided with the pillars, possibly knocking himself out. It is unknown if he participated in the rest of the battle or if he survived. If he did, he would have likely been put in Azkaban for aiding Voldemort.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
