<p><b>Edgar Bones</b> (d. <a href="/wiki/1981" title="1981">1981</a>) was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a>, son of <a href="/wiki/Amelia_Bones%27_father" title="Amelia Bones&#39; father">Mr</a> and <a href="/wiki/Amelia_Bones%27_mother" title="Amelia Bones&#39; mother">Mrs Bones</a>, brother of <a href="/wiki/Amelia_Bones" title="Amelia Bones">Amelia</a> and at least one other person. He attended <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> and was possibly sorted into <a href="/wiki/Hufflepuff" title="Hufflepuff">Hufflepuff House</a>.
</p><p>Edgar became a member of the original <a href="/wiki/Order_of_the_Phoenix" title="Order of the Phoenix">Order of the Phoenix</a> and fought in the <a href="/wiki/First_Wizarding_War" title="First Wizarding War">First Wizarding War</a>. At some point he married a <a href="/wiki/Edgar_Bones%27s_wife" class="mw-redirect" title="Edgar Bones&#39;s wife">woman</a> and had several <a href="/wiki/Edgar_Bones%27s_children" class="mw-redirect" title="Edgar Bones&#39;s children">children</a>. According to <a href="/wiki/Alastor_Moody" title="Alastor Moody">Alastor Moody</a> Edgar was a "great wizard". He and his family were murdered by <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a> before the war's end.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP9_3-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP9-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Edgar_Bones?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Edgar_Bones?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Edgar was born into a <a href="/wiki/Bones_family" title="Bones family">wizarding family</a> and had a sister, <a href="/wiki/Amelia_Bones" title="Amelia Bones">Amelia</a>. He attended <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a>, where he was possibly sorted into <a href="/wiki/Hufflepuff" title="Hufflepuff">Hufflepuff</a> house. Edgar and Amelia also had at least one other sibling, the parent of their niece <a href="/wiki/Susan_Bones" title="Susan Bones">Susan</a>.
</p><p>During the <a href="/wiki/First_Wizarding_War" title="First Wizarding War">First Wizarding War</a>, Edgar Bones became member of the <a href="/wiki/Order_of_the_Phoenix" title="Order of the Phoenix">Order of the Phoenix</a>, an organisation led and founded by <a href="/wiki/Albus_Dumbledore" title="Albus Dumbledore">Albus Dumbledore</a>, in order to oppose the <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Dark Lord Voldemort</a> and his <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a>. Edgar also married a <a href="/wiki/Edgar_Bones%27s_wife" class="mw-redirect" title="Edgar Bones&#39;s wife">woman</a> and had <a href="/wiki/Edgar_Bones%27s_children" class="mw-redirect" title="Edgar Bones&#39;s children">more than one child</a> with her. Edgar was also described as being a great wizard by <a href="/wiki/Alastor_Moody" title="Alastor Moody">Alastor Moody</a>. <a href="/wiki/Rubeus_Hagrid" title="Rubeus Hagrid">Rubeus Hagrid</a> also told <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> that the <a href="/wiki/Bones_family" title="Bones family">Bones family</a> was considered among the greatest wizards of all time.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP9_3-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP9-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Edgar was murdered, along with his <a href="/wiki/Edgar_Bones%27s_wife" class="mw-redirect" title="Edgar Bones&#39;s wife">wife</a> and <a href="/wiki/Edgar_Bones%27s_children" class="mw-redirect" title="Edgar Bones&#39;s children">children</a>, by <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a> sometime in the later half of 1981.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP25_5-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP25-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> His <a href="/wiki/Amelia_Bones%27_mother" title="Amelia Bones&#39; mother">mother</a> and <a href="/wiki/Amelia_Bones%27_father" title="Amelia Bones&#39; father">father</a> were also murdered by <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Voldemort</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Edgar_Bones?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Edgar_Bones?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The name "Edgar" is derived from the Old English elements <i>ead</i> "rich, blessed" and <i>gar</i> "spear". This was the name of a 10th-century English king who is regarded as a saint. The name did not survive long after the Norman conquest, but it was revived in the 18th century, in part due to a character by this name in Sir Walter Scott's novel 'The Bride of Lammermoor' (1819).
</p>
