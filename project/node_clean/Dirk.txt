<p><b>"Dodgy" Dirk</b> (fl. <a href="/wiki/1932" title="1932">1932</a> â€” <a href="/wiki/1972" title="1972">1972</a>) was an <a href="/wiki/England" title="England">English</a> <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggle</a> who lived in <a href="/wiki/Ilfracombe_Incident" title="Ilfracombe Incident">Ilfracombe</a>, notable for eluding the mass <a href="/wiki/Memory_Charm" title="Memory Charm">Memory Charms</a> after witnessing the <a href="/wiki/Ilfracombe_Incident" title="Ilfracombe Incident">Ilfracombe Incident</a> of <a href="/wiki/1932" title="1932">1932</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-FBR_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-FBR-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Dirk?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Dirk?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>In <a href="/wiki/1932" title="1932">1932</a>, a <a href="/wiki/Common_Welsh_Green" title="Common Welsh Green">Common Welsh Green</a> <a href="/wiki/Dragon" title="Dragon">dragon</a> descended upon a crowded beach in <a href="/wiki/Ilfracombe" title="Ilfracombe">Ilfracombe</a>, attacking a group of sunbathers, among which was Dirk, who had his lilo pierced by the <a href="/wiki/Beast" title="Beast">beast</a>. By sheer chance, the <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizarding</a> <a href="/wiki/Tilly_Toke" title="Tilly Toke">Toke</a> family was on the beach during the attack, and managed to protect the beach-goers, and proceeding to perform a multitude of <a href="/wiki/Memory_Charm" title="Memory Charm">Memory Charms</a> in order to make the <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">non-magical folk</a> forget about the attack. However, Dirk somehow managed to evade the <a href="/wiki/Memory_Charm" title="Memory Charm">Memory Charms</a>, and went on for at least forty years afterwards frequenting bars along the south coast of <a href="/wiki/England" title="England">England</a>, talking about the day a <i>"dirty great flying <a href="/wiki/Lizard" title="Lizard">lizard</a>"</i> poked a hole in his <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Air_mattress" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Air mattress">lilo</a>, and gaining the nickname "Dodgy" Dirk. <a href="/wiki/Blenheim_Stalk" title="Blenheim Stalk">Blenheim Stalk</a> mentioned this in his <a href="/wiki/1972" title="1972">1972</a> book <i><a href="/wiki/Muggles_Who_Notice" title="Muggles Who Notice">Muggles Who Notice</a></i>.<sup id="cite_ref-FBR_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-FBR-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
