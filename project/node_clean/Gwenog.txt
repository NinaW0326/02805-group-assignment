<b>Gwenog</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Great_Britain" title="Great Britain">British</a> <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch</a> who lived near <a href="/wiki/Queerditch_Marsh" title="Queerditch Marsh">Queerditch Marsh</a> during the <a href="/wiki/11th_century" title="11th century">11th century</a>. She was friends with <a href="/wiki/Gertie_Keddle" title="Gertie Keddle">Gertie Keddle</a>, and often played the game that would become <a href="/wiki/Quidditch" title="Quidditch">Quidditch</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
