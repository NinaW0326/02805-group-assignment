This <b>individual </b> was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch or wizard</a> who became the <a href="/wiki/Professor" title="Professor">professor</a> of <a href="/wiki/Defence_Against_the_Dark_Arts" title="Defence Against the Dark Arts">Defence Against the Dark Arts</a> at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a>, for the <a href="/wiki/1989%E2%80%931990_school_year" title="1989–1990 school year">1989–1990 school year</a>.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_1989-1990_Defence_Against_the_Dark_Arts_teacher?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_1989-1990_Defence_Against_the_Dark_Arts_teacher?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Many considered this teacher to be useless. <a href="/wiki/Charles_Weasley" title="Charles Weasley">Charles Weasley</a> talked about this to his older brother <a href="/wiki/William_Weasley" title="William Weasley">William Weasley</a>, who had graduated Hogwarts the <a href="/wiki/1988%E2%80%931989_school_year" title="1988–1989 school year">previous year</a> and had now been working at <a href="/wiki/Gringotts_Wizarding_Bank" title="Gringotts Wizarding Bank">Gringotts</a> as <a href="/wiki/Curse-Breaker" title="Curse-Breaker">Curse-Breaker</a>. As such, Bill had asked <a href="/wiki/Albus_Dumbledore" title="Albus Dumbledore">Albus Dumbledore</a> permission to use <a href="/wiki/Classroom_3C" title="Classroom 3C">Classroom 3C</a> for tutoring students after daily classes were done.<sup id="cite_ref-HM65_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HM65-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>By the end of the year, this professor needed to be replaced because of <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Jinx_on_the_post_of_Defence_Against_the_Dark_Arts_teacher" title="Jinx on the post of Defence Against the Dark Arts teacher">jinx</a> on the post.
</p>
