The <b>Giant Squid</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magical</a> <a href="/wiki/Beast" title="Beast">beast</a> that lived in the <a href="/wiki/Great_Lake" title="Great Lake">Great Lake</a>, on the grounds of <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Castle" title="Hogwarts Castle">Hogwarts Castle</a>. Despite the generalised fear of it and a <a href="/wiki/Chocolate_Frog_Card" title="Chocolate Frog Card">Chocolate Frog Card</a> describing it as "the bane" of Hogwarts students, the Giant Squid was actually semi-domesticated.<sup id="cite_ref-PM_1-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PM-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Giant_Squid?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Giant_Squid?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<figure class="thumb tright show-info-icon" style="width: 202px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/5/5b/Slytherin_Squid.png/revision/latest?cb=20130826041044" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/5/5b/Slytherin_Squid.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/200?cb=20130826041044" decoding="async" width="200" height="118" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="Slytherin Squid.png" data-image-key="Slytherin_Squid.png" /></a> 	 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:Slytherin_Squid.png" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption">The Squid passes by the <a href="/wiki/Slytherin_Dungeon" title="Slytherin Dungeon">Slytherin Common Room</a></p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure>
<p>It was very docile and helpful to the students of Hogwarts, such as when <a href="/wiki/Dennis_Creevey" title="Dennis Creevey">Dennis Creevey</a> fell into the Lake, and he was immediately pushed safely back into the boat.<sup id="cite_ref-GOF12_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF12-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> The squid was also known to have played with students, such as when <a href="/wiki/Fred_Weasley" title="Fred Weasley">Fred Weasley</a>, <a href="/wiki/George_Weasley" title="George Weasley">George Weasley</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Lee_Jordan" title="Lee Jordan">Lee Jordan</a> tickled its tentacles as it was lying part way out of the lake,<sup id="cite_ref-PS16_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PS16-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> and to have accepted food from students, such as when it received toast from <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-GOF18_4-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF18-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Early on in his time at Hogwarts, <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ronald Weasley</a> thought <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Tom Riddle</a> may have received his <a href="/wiki/Special_Award_for_Services_to_the_School" title="Special Award for Services to the School">Special Award for Services to the School</a> for saving a teacher from the squid,<sup id="cite_ref-COS13_5-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COS13-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> and in his fourth year, half-jokingly expressed his hope that there was some chance the squid would harm <a href="/wiki/Viktor_Krum" title="Viktor Krum">Viktor Krum</a>, who was bathing in the lake.<sup id="cite_ref-GOF24_6-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF24-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup> Needless to say, Riddle's award had nothing to do with Hogwarts' Giant Squid,<sup id="cite_ref-COS13_5-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COS13-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> and Viktor was unharmed,<sup id="cite_ref-GOF24_6-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF24-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup> as the squid was demonstrably not a threat to anyone.
</p><p>The squid had been seen as being either red<sup id="cite_ref-POAG_7-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-POAG-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup> or purple in colour.<sup id="cite_ref-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup> However since squid in the wild can change their colour using cells called chromatophores that lay just below the surface of the skin, it is entirely possible the Giant Squid was also able to change colours and even patterns at will.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Giant_Squid?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Giant_Squid?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li><a href="/wiki/J._K._Rowling" title="J. K. Rowling">J. K. Rowling</a> once joked that the Squid is <a href="/wiki/Godric_Gryffindor" title="Godric Gryffindor">Godric Gryffindor</a>, one of the four founders of <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts</a>. This would make it the world's largest <a href="/wiki/Animagus" title="Animagus">Animagus</a>. On the eleventh hour of every evening, the Squid would rise from the lake and transform into Gryffindor, who would wander the school grounds before returning at sunrise. She was unexpectedly taken seriously by many fans, before she was forced to clarify this was actually a joke.</li>
<li>After the release of <i><a href="/wiki/Fantastic_Beasts_and_Where_to_Find_Them_(film)" title="Fantastic Beasts and Where to Find Them (film)">Fantastic Beasts and Where to Find Them</a></i>, fans speculated the Giant Squid may be the "dangerous creature" <a href="/wiki/Newton_Scamander" title="Newton Scamander">Newt Scamender</a> released before being thrown out of Hogwarts. However, promotional material clarified that the creature in question was a <a href="/wiki/Jarvey" title="Jarvey">Jarvey</a>, shooting down the theory.
<ul><li>Before the details of Newt's life were released, others fans had also theorised the Squid had been smuggled into Hogwarts by the <a href="/wiki/Marauders" title="Marauders">Marauders</a> as part of some prank. Unlike the previous one, this theory has not been debunked by official sources, though it may be in the future.</li></ul></li></ul>
<figure class="thumb tright show-info-icon" style="width: 202px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/6/65/LEGO_Giant_Squid.png/revision/latest?cb=20130928061253" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/6/65/LEGO_Giant_Squid.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/200?cb=20130928061253" decoding="async" width="200" height="137" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="LEGO Giant Squid.png" data-image-key="LEGO_Giant_Squid.png" /></a> 	 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:LEGO_Giant_Squid.png" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption">The Giant Squid in <i><a href="/wiki/LEGO_Harry_Potter:_Years_1-4" title="LEGO Harry Potter: Years 1-4">LEGO Harry Potter: Years 1-4</a></i></p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure>
<ul><li>The Giant Squid has not appeared in any of the Harry Potter films. However, it was originally set to make a brief appearance in <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Prisoner_of_Azkaban_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban (film)">Prisoner of Azkaban</a></i> during the scene where Harry rides <a href="/wiki/Buckbeak" title="Buckbeak">Buckbeak</a> over Black Lake, as evidenced by an early script.<sup id="cite_ref-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup></li></ul>
<figure class="thumb tright show-info-icon" style="width: 202px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/d/dd/Giant_squid.gif/revision/latest?cb=20091004093357" class="image"><img alt="" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw%3D%3D" decoding="async" width="200" height="191" class="thumbimage lazyload" data-image-name="Giant squid.gif" data-image-key="Giant_squid.gif" data-src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/d/dd/Giant_squid.gif/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/200?cb=20091004093357" /></a> 	<noscript><a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/d/dd/Giant_squid.gif/revision/latest?cb=20091004093357" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/d/dd/Giant_squid.gif/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/200?cb=20091004093357" decoding="async" width="200" height="191" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="Giant squid.gif" data-image-key="Giant_squid.gif" data-src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/d/dd/Giant_squid.gif/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/200?cb=20091004093357" /></a></noscript> 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:Giant_squid.gif" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption">The <a href="/wiki/Giant_Squid" title="Giant Squid">Giant Squid</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Chocolate_Frog_Card" title="Chocolate Frog Card">Chocolate Frog Card</a></p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure>
<ul><li>The fact that the Giant Squid is magical may be how it is able to survive in the Black Lake as a normal giant squid is a deep-sea-dwelling creature and cannot live in fresh water such as found in inland lochs in Scotland, as the sunlight, pressure and lack of salinity, space, and food would be fatal to it - bread and toast would also be unable to be digested by it, and sticks would break its skin if they tickled it. However, as the Durmstrang ship arrived underwater and it is implied that wizards can travel to other places underwater, the giant squid may be able to do the same as the Ship and leave the loch and receive the required levels of space, salt, depth, and food whenever it needs. Its magic may also account for its lifespan: the same squid lived at least since Lily (Harry's mother) was at school, and either it, or its ancestor, appears on a petroglyph attributed to the lake's merpeople.</li></ul>
