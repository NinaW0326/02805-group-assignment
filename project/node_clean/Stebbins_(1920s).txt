<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Stebbins_(1920s)?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Stebbins_(1920s)?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>In <a href="/wiki/1927" title="1927">1927</a>, he was tasked with the job of spying on <a href="/wiki/Newton_Scamander" title="Newton Scamander">Newton Scamander</a>, but was thwarted when Newt cast the <a href="/wiki/Ventus_Jinx" title="Ventus Jinx">Ventus Jinx</a> creating a forceful wind that delayed him long enough for Newt to <a href="/wiki/Apparition" title="Apparition">disapparate</a>. Stebbins accompanied <a href="/wiki/Torquil_Travers" title="Torquil Travers">Torquil Travers</a> to <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts</a> with the group of <a href="/wiki/Auror" title="Auror">Aurors</a> trying to force <a href="/wiki/Albus_Dumbledore" title="Albus Dumbledore">Albus Dumbledore</a> to fight <a href="/wiki/Gellert_Grindelwald" title="Gellert Grindelwald">Gellert Grindelwald</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-COGS_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COGS-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
