<p><b>Mr Belby</b> (fl. <a href="/wiki/1996" title="1996">1996</a>) was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> and the father of <a href="/wiki/Marcus_Belby" title="Marcus Belby">Marcus Belby</a>. He did not get along with Marcus's uncle, <a href="/wiki/Damocles_Belby" title="Damocles Belby">Damocles</a>, as he did not believe brewing <a href="/wiki/Potion" title="Potion">potions</a> a worthwhile pursuit,<sup id="cite_ref-Potions_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Potions-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> and, as such, the two men had little contact.
</p>
