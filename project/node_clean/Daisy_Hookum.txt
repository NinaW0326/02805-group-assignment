<b>Daisy Hookum</b> (b. <a href="/wiki/1962" title="1962">1962</a>) was a <a href="/wiki/Great_Britain" title="Great Britain">British</a> <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch</a>, who wrote a best-selling book, <i><a href="/wiki/My_Life_as_a_Muggle" title="My Life as a Muggle">My Life as a Muggle</a></i>, after voluntarily giving up using <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magic</a> for a year. She was married to celebrity <a href="/wiki/Herbologist" title="Herbologist">Herbologist</a>, <a href="/wiki/Potioneer" title="Potioneer">Potioneer</a> and radio presenter <a href="/wiki/Tilden_Toots" title="Tilden Toots">Tilden Toots</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-JKRS_1-10" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-JKRS-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
