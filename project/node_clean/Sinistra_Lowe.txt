<b>Sinistra Lowe</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch</a> and an advocate of the <a href="/wiki/Pure-blood_supremacy" title="Pure-blood supremacy">supremacy</a> of <a href="/wiki/Pure-blood" title="Pure-blood">pure-blood</a> wizards and witches, having authored at least one book on the subject, <i><a href="/wiki/The_Muggle_Conspiracy" title="The Muggle Conspiracy">The Muggle Conspiracy</a></i>.<sup id="cite_ref-dhscreen_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-dhscreen-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/Dolores_Umbridge" title="Dolores Umbridge">Dolores Umbridge</a> had a copy of this work in her desk at the <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">Ministry of Magic</a>, while on the post of <a href="/wiki/Senior_Undersecretary_to_the_Minister_for_Magic" title="Senior Undersecretary to the Minister for Magic">Senior Undersecretary to the Minister for Magic</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-dhscreen_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-dhscreen-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
