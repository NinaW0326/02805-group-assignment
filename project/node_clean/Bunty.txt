</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Bunty?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Bunty?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_life">Early life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Bunty?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Early life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Bunty?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Early life"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>In her youth, Bunty attended <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a>, where she would have received her <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magical</a> education. She presumably studied and was academically successful in <a href="/wiki/Care_of_Magical_Creatures" title="Care of Magical Creatures">Care of Magical Creatures</a> class.
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Career">Career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Bunty?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Bunty?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Career"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Bunty attended the book signing of <i><a href="/wiki/Fantastic_Beasts_and_Where_to_Find_Them" title="Fantastic Beasts and Where to Find Them">Fantastic Beasts and Where to Find Them</a></i> in early <a href="/wiki/1927" title="1927">1927</a>, having been obsessed with author <a href="/wiki/Newton_Scamander" title="Newton Scamander">Newt Scamander</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-backstory_5-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-backstory-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> The two of them developed a friendship, and by September of that year Bunty had gotten a job as assistant of Newt.<sup id="cite_ref-COGS_6-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COGS-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>In June 1927, Bunty was fully established as Newt's assistant, and helped him find some baby <a href="/wiki/Niffler" title="Niffler">Nifflers</a> who had escaped in his house. Upon Newt's decision to travel to <a href="/wiki/Paris" title="Paris">Paris</a> in search of <a href="/wiki/Credence_Barebone" title="Credence Barebone">Credence Barebone</a>, he left Bunty a note informing her of his travels, and asking her to look after his creatures in his absence.<sup id="cite_ref-COGS_6-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COGS-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Personality_and_traits">Personality and traits</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Bunty?veaction=edit&amp;section=4" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Personality and traits">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Bunty?action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Personality and traits"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Bunty was clearly a compassionate young witch with a keen interest and passion in caring for <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magical</a> <a href="/wiki/Creature" title="Creature">creatures</a> as Newt Scamander's assistant. Newt Scamander regarded her to be a trustworthy and reliable individual, so much so he granted her permission to access his home to care for his many creatures whilst on his travels. Due to her enthusiasm and effort as Newt's assistant along with her passionate caring for his various <a href="/wiki/Beast" title="Beast">beasts</a>, it can be presumed she also held his passion and interest in <a href="/wiki/Magizoology" title="Magizoology">Magizoology</a> very highly.<sup id="cite_ref-COGS_6-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COGS-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup> 
&#160;
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Magical_abilities_and_skills">Magical abilities and skills</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Bunty?veaction=edit&amp;section=5" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Bunty?action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li><b><a href="/wiki/Care_of_Magical_Creatures" title="Care of Magical Creatures">Care of Magical Creatures</a></b>: Bunty was very skilled in the caring and handling of various magical creatures, being very able to care for Newt's creatures as his assistant. In particular, she was shown handling and caring for several baby <a href="/wiki/Niffler" title="Niffler">Nifflers</a> very competently and carefully and was trusted by Newt to care for his larger and more dangerous beasts, showing she was accomplished in this field.<sup id="cite_ref-COGS_6-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COGS-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup> &#160;</li></ul>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Bunty?veaction=edit&amp;section=7" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Bunty?action=edit&amp;section=7" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>The name "Bunty" has multiple origins and uses, mostly animal-related, including:</li>
<li>As a term of endearment that stems from a dialect name for lamb that means "to butt."
<ul><li>Bunting is a form of animal behaviour, often found in cats, in which the animal butts or rubs their head against other things, including people. It is generally considered to be a form of territorial&#160;scent-marking&#160;behaviour, where the cat rubs the&#160;scent glands&#160;on their cheeks and forehead on the object being marked.</li></ul></li>
<li>Stemming from the English and Scottish word <i>buntin</i>, meaning "plump."</li>
<li>The name Bunting is given to a group of Eurasian and African passerine birds of the family Emberizidae.</li></ul>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Bunty?veaction=edit&amp;section=8" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Bunty?action=edit&amp;section=8" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>Bunty is played by <a href="/wiki/Victoria_Yeates" title="Victoria Yeates">Victoria Yeates</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Fantastic_Beasts:_The_Crimes_of_Grindelwald" title="Fantastic Beasts: The Crimes of Grindelwald">second instalment</a> of the <i><a href="/wiki/Fantastic_Beasts_(film_series)" title="Fantastic Beasts (film series)">Fantastic Beasts</a></i> film series.<sup id="cite_ref-pm_3-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-pm-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li>Though Bunty's role in the film was minimal, <a href="/wiki/J._K._Rowling" title="J. K. Rowling">J. K. Rowling</a> devised a "fully fleshed" backstory for her (in a similar sentiment of what she disclosed about minor characters of the <i>Harry Potter</i> book series, such as <a href="/wiki/Dean_Thomas" title="Dean Thomas">Dean Thomas</a>, <a href="/wiki/Theodore_Nott" title="Theodore Nott">Theodore Nott</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Filius_Flitwick" title="Filius Flitwick">Filius Flitwick</a>). Rowling provided <a href="/wiki/Eddie_Redmayne" title="Eddie Redmayne">Eddie Redmayne</a> and Victoria Yeates three pages of original text about the origin of Bunty's relationship with Newt.<sup id="cite_ref-backstory_5-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-backstory-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup></li></ul>
