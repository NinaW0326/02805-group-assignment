<b>Minerva</b> was a powerful <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch</a>. She was the grandmother of <a href="/wiki/Isobel_Ross" title="Isobel Ross">Isobel McGonagall</a> (n√©e <a href="/wiki/Ross_family" title="Ross family">Ross</a>) and the great-grandmother of: <a href="/wiki/Minerva_McGonagall" title="Minerva McGonagall">Minerva</a>, <a href="/wiki/Malcolm_McGonagall" title="Malcolm McGonagall">Malcolm</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Robert_McGonagall_Jr" class="mw-redirect" title="Robert McGonagall Jr">Robert McGonagall Jr</a>.
</p><p>Isobel named her firstborn child in her grandmother's honour. She did this because she missed her family and the magical community, which she had to leave behind when she married a <a href="/wiki/Robert_McGonagall_Senior" title="Robert McGonagall Senior">Muggle man</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-pottermore_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-pottermore-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Magical_abilities_and_skills">Magical abilities and skills</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Minerva?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Minerva?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Minerva was an extremely powerful <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch</a>, just like her great-granddaughter would grow up to become. Not much is known about the true extent of her magical abilities, however. 
</p>
<ul><li><a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic"><b>Magical</b></a><b> Mastery</b>: Although it is not known exactly how powerful Minerva was, she was widely believed to be extremely powerful and her talents were renown to her granddaughter <a href="/wiki/Isobel_Ross" title="Isobel Ross">Isobel</a>.</li></ul>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Minerva?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Minerva?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Minerva is the Roman goddess of wisdom and craftsmanship. Her Greek deity is Athena, goddess of wisdom, strategy, warfare, and craftsmanship. Minerva is less honoured than Athena and the warfare is left all to Mars, god of war. 
</p>
