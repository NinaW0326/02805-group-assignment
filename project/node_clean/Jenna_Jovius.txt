<div class="mw-parser-output"><p><b>Jenna Jovius</b> was a individual who lived at 34 Lippincot road, in the borough of Chelsea, <a href="/wiki/London" title="London">London</a> in <a href="/wiki/1927" title="1927">1927</a>. In that year the <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">British Ministry of Magic</a> wrote a letter addressed to Jovius which was validated by ministry official <a href="/wiki/D._Dussal" title="D. Dussal">D. Dussal</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-Archive_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Archive-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
