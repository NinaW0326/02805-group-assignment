<b>Cyrille Lestrange</b> was a French <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> and a member of the <a href="/wiki/Pure-blood" title="Pure-blood">pure-blood</a> <a href="/wiki/Lestrange_family" title="Lestrange family">Lestrange family</a>. Cyrille had a brother <a href="/wiki/Corvus_Lestrange_(I)" title="Corvus Lestrange (I)">Corvus</a>. He married <a href="/wiki/Darenne_Lestrange" title="Darenne Lestrange">Darenne Moreau</a>, and had two sons with her, <a href="/wiki/Cyrille_Lestrange_(II)" title="Cyrille Lestrange (II)">Cyrille</a> and <a href="/wiki/Fulcran_Lestrange_(I)" title="Fulcran Lestrange (I)">Fulcran</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
