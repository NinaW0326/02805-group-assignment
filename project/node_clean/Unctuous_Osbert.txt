<b>Minister Unctuous Osbert</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Great_Britain" title="Great Britain">British</a> <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> who was <a href="/wiki/Minister_for_Magic" title="Minister for Magic">Minister for Magic</a> from <a href="/wiki/1789" class="mw-redirect" title="1789">1789</a> to <a href="/wiki/1798" class="mw-redirect" title="1798">1798</a>. His term in office was somewhat controversial, as he was accused of having been a puppet in the hands of <a href="/wiki/Septimus_Malfoy" title="Septimus Malfoy">Septimus Malfoy</a>.  Osbert was elected at least twice.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Unctuous_Osbert?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Unctuous_Osbert?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_life">Early life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Unctuous_Osbert?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Early life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Unctuous_Osbert?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Early life"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Unctuous was born a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> into the <a href="/wiki/Pure-blood" title="Pure-blood">Pure-blood</a> or <a href="/wiki/Half-blood" title="Half-blood">Half-blood</a> Osbert family,<sup id="cite_ref-blood_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-blood-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> somewhere in the <a href="/wiki/Great_Britain" title="Great Britain">British Isles</a>. It is likely he attended <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> in his youth.
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Minister_for_Magic">Minister for Magic</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Unctuous_Osbert?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Minister for Magic">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Unctuous_Osbert?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Minister for Magic"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Unctuous Osbert was elected <a href="/wiki/Minister_for_Magic" title="Minister for Magic">Minister for Magic</a> of <a href="/wiki/Great_Britain" title="Great Britain">Great Britain</a> sometime into the late <a href="/wiki/18th_century" title="18th century">eighteenth century</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-Pottermore_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Pottermore-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p><a href="/wiki/Septimus_Malfoy" title="Septimus Malfoy">Septimus Malfoy</a>, a member of the powerful and wealthy <a href="/wiki/Malfoy_family" title="Malfoy family">Malfoy family</a>, was an enormously influential <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> at the <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">Ministry</a> in the day and Minister Osbert was, during the course of his tenure, influenced to some degree by Malfoy's interests. Many claimed that Minister Osbert was little more than a puppet of Malfoy's, who would have been the <i>de facto</i> head of the Ministry.<sup id="cite_ref-Pottermore_2-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Pottermore-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Osbert left the office in <a href="/wiki/1798" class="mw-redirect" title="1798">1798</a>, to be succeeded by <a href="/wiki/Artemisia_Lufkin" title="Artemisia Lufkin">Artemisia Lufkin</a>.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Unctuous_Osbert?veaction=edit&amp;section=4" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Unctuous_Osbert?action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p><i>Unctuous</i> is an English word deriving from the Latin <i>ung≈ç</i>, meaning "I anoint with oil". The English word means "to be insincerely polite and earnest". 
</p>
