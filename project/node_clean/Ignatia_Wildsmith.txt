<b>Ignatia Wildsmith</b> (<a href="/wiki/1227" class="mw-redirect" title="1227">1227</a>—<a href="/wiki/1320" class="mw-redirect" title="1320">1320</a>) was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch</a> and wizarding inventor, who was famously the creator of <a href="/wiki/Floo_Powder" title="Floo Powder">Floo Powder</a>. During her childhood, she attended <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a>, and was <a href="/wiki/Sorting_ceremony" title="Sorting ceremony">sorted</a> into <a href="/wiki/Ravenclaw" title="Ravenclaw">Ravenclaw</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-pottermore_2-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-pottermore-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Ignatia_Wildsmith?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Ignatia_Wildsmith?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_life">Early life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Ignatia_Wildsmith?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Early life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Ignatia_Wildsmith?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Early life"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<div class="quote">
<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;Pottermore">"<i>Ravenclaw house has an illustrious history. Most of the greatest wizarding inventors and innovators were in our house, including </i>[…]<i> Ignatia Wildsmith, the inventor of Floo powder.</i>"</span></dd>
<dd>&#8212;<a href="/wiki/Robert_Hilliard" title="Robert Hilliard">Robert Hilliard</a>.<sup class="noprint"><a href="/wiki/Pottermore" title="Pottermore">[src]</a></sup></dd>
<dd></dd></dl></div>
<p>Ignatia Wildsmith was born in <a href="/wiki/1227" class="mw-redirect" title="1227">1227</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-PSg_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PSg-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> somewhere in the <a href="/wiki/Great_Britain" title="Great Britain">British Isles</a>.
</p><p>In her youth, she attended <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> from around <a href="/wiki/1238" class="mw-redirect" title="1238">1238</a> to <a href="/wiki/1245" class="mw-redirect" title="1245">1245</a>, where she was <a href="/wiki/Sorting_ceremony" title="Sorting ceremony">sorted</a> into <a href="/wiki/Ravenclaw" title="Ravenclaw">Ravenclaw</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-pottermore_2-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-pottermore-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Adulthood">Adulthood</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Ignatia_Wildsmith?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Adulthood">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Ignatia_Wildsmith?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Adulthood"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<figure class="thumb tleft show-info-icon" style="width: 202px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/6/6d/Ignatia_WildsmithGBC.png/revision/latest?cb=20110906185152" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/6/6d/Ignatia_WildsmithGBC.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/200?cb=20110906185152" decoding="async" width="200" height="235" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="Ignatia WildsmithGBC.png" data-image-key="Ignatia_WildsmithGBC.png" /></a> 	 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:Ignatia_WildsmithGBC.png" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption">A younger Ignatia Wildsmith</p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure>
<p>After her graduation from <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School</a>, Ms. Wildsmith presumably studied further into <a href="/wiki/Herbology" title="Herbology">Herbology</a>, and discovered the <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magical</a> properties of the <a href="/wiki/Floo" title="Floo">Floo</a> plant. From it, she devised a magical powder, <a href="/wiki/Floo_Powder" title="Floo Powder">Floo Powder</a>, which, when thrown into a <a href="/wiki/Fire" title="Fire">fire</a>, could transport <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizards</a> from one fireplace to another via a <a href="/wiki/Floo_Network" title="Floo Network">network of fireplaces</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-PSg_3-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PSg-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Death_and_post-mortem">Death and post-mortem</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Ignatia_Wildsmith?veaction=edit&amp;section=4" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Death and post-mortem">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Ignatia_Wildsmith?action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Death and post-mortem"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Ms. Wildsmith died in <a href="/wiki/1320" class="mw-redirect" title="1320">1320</a>, aged around 93. In her honour, her achievement, the invention of the <a href="/wiki/Floo_Powder" title="Floo Powder">Floo Powder</a>, was recorded on a <a href="/wiki/Chocolate_Frog_Card" title="Chocolate Frog Card">Chocolate Frog Card</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-PSg_3-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PSg-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>On <a href="/wiki/1_September" title="1 September">1 September</a>, <a href="/wiki/1991" title="1991">1991</a>, <a href="/wiki/Prefect" title="Prefect">Prefect</a> <a href="/wiki/Robert_Hilliard" title="Robert Hilliard">Robert Hilliard</a> welcomed the <a href="/wiki/Ravenclaw" title="Ravenclaw">Ravenclaw</a> <a href="/wiki/First_year" title="First year">first years</a> to <a href="/wiki/Ravenclaw_Tower" title="Ravenclaw Tower">Ravenclaw Tower</a> with a short speech in which he mentioned inventor Ignatia Wildsmith as one of the many notable Hogwarts students who had been <a href="/wiki/Sorting_ceremony" title="Sorting ceremony">Sorted</a>, like them, into <a href="/wiki/Ravenclaw" title="Ravenclaw">Ravenclaw</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-pottermore_2-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-pottermore-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Ignatia_Wildsmith?veaction=edit&amp;section=6" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Ignatia_Wildsmith?action=edit&amp;section=6" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>On her <a href="/wiki/Chocolate_Frog_Card" title="Chocolate Frog Card">Famous Wizard Card</a>, Ignatia's outfit bears a resemblance to <a href="https://disney.fandom.com/wiki/Maleficent" class="extiw" title="w:c:disney:Maleficent">Maleficent</a> from Disney's <i><a href="https://disney.fandom.com/wiki/Sleeping_Beauty" class="extiw" title="w:c:disney:Sleeping Beauty">Sleeping Beauty</a></i>. Throughout the film, Maleficent is associated with green flames, even using them to teleport herself. She also has <a href="https://disney.fandom.com/wiki/Diablo" class="extiw" title="w:c:disney:Diablo">a raven companion</a>, though this may be coincidental, as Ignatia was not revealed to be a Ravenclaw at the time the card was designed.</li></ul>
