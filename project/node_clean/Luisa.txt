<b>Luisa</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch</a> who began attending <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> in the late <a href="/wiki/1970s" title="1970s">1970s</a> or <a href="/wiki/1980s" title="1980s">1980s</a>. She was friends with a <a href="/wiki/Hufflepuff" title="Hufflepuff">Hufflepuff</a> student sorted in <a href="/wiki/1984" title="1984">1984</a>. During the <a href="/wiki/1986%E2%80%931987_school_year" title="1986–1987 school year">1986–1987 school year</a>, she was attacked by a <a href="/wiki/Boggart" title="Boggart">Boggart</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Quidditch_Changing_Rooms" title="Quidditch Changing Rooms">Quidditch Changing Rooms</a>, and was sent to the <a href="/wiki/Hospital_Wing" title="Hospital Wing">Hospital Wing</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-HM34h_3-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HM34h-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
