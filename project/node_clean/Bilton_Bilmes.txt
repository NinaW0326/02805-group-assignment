<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Bilton_Bilmes?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Bilton_Bilmes?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_life">Early life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Bilton_Bilmes?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Early life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Bilton_Bilmes?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Early life"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Bilton Bilmes was born somewhere in the <a href="/wiki/Great_Britain" title="Great Britain">British Isles</a> into a wizarding family of unknown&#160;<a href="/wiki/Blood_status" title="Blood status">blood status</a>, and attended <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a>. During his school career, if his later profession is any indication, he was well known for his sense of humour. Sometime either during his schooling or after he graduated from Hogwarts, he poured <a href="/wiki/Bulbadox_powder" title="Bulbadox powder">Bulbadox powder</a> in <a href="/wiki/Horace_Slughorn" title="Horace Slughorn">Horace Slughorn</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Mead" title="Mead">mead</a> as a prank.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> It is also unknown what <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Houses" title="Hogwarts Houses">House</a> he was <a href="/wiki/Sorting_ceremony" title="Sorting ceremony">sorted</a> into whilst at Hogwarts.
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Later_life">Later life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Bilton_Bilmes?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Later life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Bilton_Bilmes?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Later life"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>He once accidentally set off some <a href="/wiki/Stink_Pellets" title="Stink Pellets">Stink Pellets</a> in the closet in his home and, unable to get the smell out, resolved to burn every piece of clothing he owned and buy new ones, only to accidentally burn his home to the ground in the process. This mistake would haunt him for many years to come. By the <a href="/wiki/1980s" title="1980s">1980s</a>, Bilton had settled down in <a href="/wiki/Hogsmeade" title="Hogsmeade">Hogsmeade Village</a> and took up a job at the local joke shop. Loud and affable, and with a childlike fondness and enthusiasm for pranks and mischief, Bilton was always happy to accommodate the young scoundrels of <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Castle" title="Hogwarts Castle">Hogwarts Castle</a> with the latest within classical trouble-making whenever they came to his shop looking for prank devices, and actively encouraged their practical jokes.
</p>
