<b>Chauncey Oldridge</b> (<a href="/wiki/1342" class="mw-redirect" title="1342">1342</a> â€“ <a href="/wiki/1379" class="mw-redirect" title="1379">1379</a>) was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> and the first known victim of <a href="/wiki/Dragon_Pox" title="Dragon Pox">Dragon Pox</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-PSG_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PSG-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-COSG_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COSG-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> Oldridge was born in <a href="/wiki/1342" class="mw-redirect" title="1342">1342</a>, eventually contracting <a href="/wiki/Dragon_Pox" title="Dragon Pox">Dragon Pox</a> at some point in his life. As a result, his skin turned green and became covered with blemishes. While others before him have contracted Dragon Pox,<sup id="cite_ref-QTAR_4-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-QTAR-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> he was the first to succumb to it in <a href="/wiki/1379" class="mw-redirect" title="1379">1379</a>, with no <a href="/wiki/Dragon_pox_cure" title="Dragon pox cure">cure</a> yet available. Becoming famous for being the first person to fall victim to <a href="/wiki/Dragon_pox" class="mw-redirect" title="Dragon pox">Dragon pox</a>, Chauncey Oldridge became immortalised on a <a href="/wiki/Chocolate_Frog_Card" title="Chocolate Frog Card">Chocolate Frog Card</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-PSG_2-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PSG-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-COSG_3-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COSG-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Chauncey_Oldridge?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Chauncey_Oldridge?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The name "Chauncey" comes from a Norman surname of unknown meaning. It was used as a given name in American in honour of Harvard president Charles Chauncey (1592-1672).
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Chauncey_Oldridge?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Chauncey_Oldridge?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>Before the publication of <i><a href="/wiki/Quidditch_Through_the_Ages_(real)" title="Quidditch Through the Ages (real)">Quidditch Through the Ages</a></i>, it appeared that Oldridge was the first person ever to contract <a href="/wiki/Dragon_Pox" title="Dragon Pox">Dragon Pox</a> in history. However, the book clarifies it was already known to <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">Wizardkind</a> at least a century before Oldridge, so in this case, the word "victim" is assumed to mean he was the first person to have died from it.</li></ul>
