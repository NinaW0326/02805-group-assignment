<b>Craig Bowker Junior</b> (d. <a href="/wiki/30_October" title="30 October">30 October</a>, <a href="/wiki/2020" title="2020">2020</a>) was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> and student at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> in the late <a href="/wiki/2010s" title="2010s">2010s</a> and early <a href="/wiki/2020s" title="2020s">2020s</a>.

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Craig_Bowker_Junior?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Craig_Bowker_Junior?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Craig Bowker Junior was born somewhere in the <a href="/wiki/Great_Britain" title="Great Britain">United Kingdom</a> or <a href="/wiki/Ireland" title="Ireland">Ireland</a> in the mid-<a href="/wiki/2000s" title="2000s">2000s</a>. He attended <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> and was sorted in <a href="/wiki/Slytherin" title="Slytherin">Slytherin</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-act1scene4_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-act1scene4-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-act3scene15_4-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-act3scene15-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> On <a href="/wiki/1_September" title="1 September">1 September</a> <a href="/wiki/2017" title="2017">2017</a>, he was present for the year's <a href="/wiki/Sorting_ceremony" title="Sorting ceremony">sorting ceremony</a>, and expressed his disbelief when <a href="/wiki/Albus_Potter" title="Albus Potter">Albus Potter</a> was placed in his house.<sup id="cite_ref-act1scene4_3-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-act1scene4-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Upon returning to <a href="/wiki/2020" title="2020">2020</a> from altering the past, <a href="/wiki/Scorpius_Malfoy" title="Scorpius Malfoy">Scorpius Malfoy</a> found himself in an alternate reality in which <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a> won, and went to the <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Library" title="Hogwarts Library">Hogwarts Library</a> to research how this could have happened.<sup id="cite_ref-act3scene4_5-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-act3scene4-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> That universe's Craig encountered Scorpius in the library and, frightened, asked him what he was doing there. He then revealed that he was still working on the homework that his universe's Scorpius had demanded he do for him.<sup id="cite_ref-act3scene4_5-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-act3scene4-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Meanwhile, in the main timeline, <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> arrived at Hogwarts with <a href="/wiki/Ginevra_Weasley" title="Ginevra Weasley">Ginny</a> to confront his son Albus about his continuing association with Scorpius, and upon entering the <a href="/wiki/Slytherin_Dungeon" title="Slytherin Dungeon">Slytherin dormitories</a> Craig objected, noting that it was against school rules for parents and professors to enter house dormitories, but <a href="/wiki/Minerva_McGonagall" title="Minerva McGonagall">Headmistress McGonagall</a> shushed him. They found the boys' beds empty.<sup id="cite_ref-act3scene15_4-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-act3scene15-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>A short time later, Craig located Albus and Scorpius on the <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Quidditch_pitch" title="Hogwarts Quidditch pitch">Quidditch pitch</a> and informed them that the entire school was looking for them. <a href="/wiki/Delphini" title="Delphini">Delphi</a> then fired a <a href="/wiki/Killing_Curse" title="Killing Curse">Killing Curse</a> at him, killing him instantly.<sup id="cite_ref-act3scene19_6-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-act3scene19-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p><a href="/wiki/Delphini" title="Delphini">Delphini</a> was later sent to <a href="/wiki/Azkaban" title="Azkaban">Azkaban</a> for this crime.<sup id="cite_ref-act4scene11_7-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-act4scene11-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
