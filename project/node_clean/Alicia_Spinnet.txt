<p><b>Alicia Spinnet</b> (b. <a href="/wiki/1977" title="1977">1977</a>/<a href="/wiki/1978" title="1978">1978</a>) was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch</a> and <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor" title="Gryffindor">Gryffindor</a> student at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> from <a href="/wiki/1989" title="1989">1989</a>-<a href="/wiki/1996" title="1996">1996</a>. She played as a reserve Chaser and later <a href="/wiki/Chaser" title="Chaser">Chaser</a> on the <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor" title="Gryffindor">Gryffindor</a> <a href="/wiki/Quidditch" title="Quidditch">Quidditch</a> <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor_Quidditch_team" title="Gryffindor Quidditch team">team</a>. During her Hogwarts years, she became close friends with <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione Granger</a>, <a href="/wiki/Cho_Chang" title="Cho Chang">Cho Chang</a>, <a href="/wiki/Katie_Bell" title="Katie Bell">Katie Bell</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Angelina_Johnson" title="Angelina Johnson">Angelina Johnson</a>. In her seventh year, she joined <a href="/wiki/Dumbledore%27s_Army" title="Dumbledore&#39;s Army">Dumbledore's Army</a>, an organisation taught and led by <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>. In 1998, she returned at Hogwarts in order to fight in the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a> against <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a> and his <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-DH30_4-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH30-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_life">Early life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Alicia_Spinnet?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Early life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Alicia_Spinnet?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Early life"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Alicia was born in <a href="/wiki/1977" title="1977">1977</a> or <a href="/wiki/1978" title="1978">1978</a> in <a href="/wiki/Great_Britain" title="Great Britain">Great Britain</a> or <a href="/wiki/Ireland" title="Ireland">Ireland</a>.
</p>
<h3><span id="Hogwarts_years_(1989-1996)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="Hogwarts_years_.281989-1996.29">Hogwarts years <span class="template-c">(1989-1996)</span></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Alicia_Spinnet?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Hogwarts years (1989-1996)">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Alicia_Spinnet?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Hogwarts years (1989-1996)"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_years">Early years</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Alicia_Spinnet?veaction=edit&amp;section=4" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Early years">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Alicia_Spinnet?action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Early years"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<p>Alicia Spinnet attended <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> from <a href="/wiki/1989" title="1989">1989</a> to <a href="/wiki/1996" title="1996">1996</a>, and was sorted into <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor" title="Gryffindor">Gryffindor House</a>. There, she became best friends with fellow Gryffindors <a href="/wiki/Katie_Bell" title="Katie Bell">Katie Bell</a>, <a href="/wiki/Angelina_Johnson" title="Angelina Johnson">Angelina Johnson</a>, <a href="/wiki/Lee_Jordan" title="Lee Jordan">Lee Jordan</a>, <a href="/wiki/Oliver_Wood" title="Oliver Wood">Oliver Wood</a>, and the twins <a href="/wiki/Fred_and_George_Weasley" title="Fred and George Weasley">Fred and George Weasley</a> who all shared an interest in <a href="/wiki/Quidditch" title="Quidditch">Quidditch</a>.
</p><p>Alicia tried out for the <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor_Quidditch_team" title="Gryffindor Quidditch team">Gryffindor Quidditch team</a> and made reserve position; according to a comment by <a href="/wiki/Lee_Jordan" title="Lee Jordan">Lee Jordan</a>, she was a good pick by Oliver, meaning she had an impressive skill at Quidditch but still only made reserve.
</p>

<p>Alicia was only a reserve on the <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor" title="Gryffindor">Gryffindor</a> <a href="/wiki/Quidditch" title="Quidditch">Quidditch</a> <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor_Quidditch_team" title="Gryffindor Quidditch team">team</a> until 1991, her third year, when she would become a Chaser. Alicia was most often the <a href="/wiki/Chaser" title="Chaser">Chaser</a> who attempts penalty shots for <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor" title="Gryffindor">Gryffindor</a> when the other team commits a foul. In 1991, a new <a href="/wiki/Seeker" title="Seeker">Seeker</a>, <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>, joined the team. Alicia became friendly with Harry, too. The Gryffindor Quidditch team won two times during this year, but was defeated by Ravenclaw, costing them their chance at the title. However, <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor" title="Gryffindor">Gryffindor House</a> would have had the most points by the end of the year, due to Harry Potter, <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ron Weasley</a>, <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione Granger</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Neville_Longbottom" title="Neville Longbottom">Neville Longbottom</a> because of their actions the previous night.<sup id="cite_ref-PS_5-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PS-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

During practise, Wood commented on his chasers playing "superbly". Gryffindor had originally planned to play <a href="/wiki/Slytherin" title="Slytherin">Slytherin</a> first, but due to Draco's injury, they faced the <a href="/wiki/Hufflepuff_Quidditch_team" title="Hufflepuff Quidditch team">Hufflepuff team</a> instead, captained by <a href="/wiki/Cedric_Diggory" title="Cedric Diggory">Cedric Diggory</a>. Alicia was also present the night that <a href="/wiki/Sirius_Black" title="Sirius Black">Sirius Black</a> successfully broke into Gryffindor Tower.<sup id="cite_ref-POA_9-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-POA-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup>

<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Sixth_year">Sixth year</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Alicia_Spinnet?veaction=edit&amp;section=8" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Sixth year">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Alicia_Spinnet?action=edit&amp;section=8" title="Edit section: Sixth year"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<p>During her sixth year, the Quidditch matches were cancelled again, due to the <a href="/wiki/Triwizard_Tournament" title="Triwizard Tournament">Triwizard Tournament</a>. Despite being in the sixth year, it is unknown whether Alicia was old enough to enter her name into the Goblet of Fire. However, the year ended with the death of champion <a href="/wiki/Cedric_Diggory" title="Cedric Diggory">Cedric Diggory</a> and the return of <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-GOF_10-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF-10">&#91;10&#93;</a></sup>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Personality_and_traits">Personality and traits</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Alicia_Spinnet?veaction=edit&amp;section=12" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Personality and traits">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Alicia_Spinnet?action=edit&amp;section=12" title="Edit section: Personality and traits"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Alicia was a good-natured and friendly individual who was very interested in <a href="/wiki/Quidditch" title="Quidditch">Quidditch</a>. She did also care about her friends, as she was afraid for <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>'s life while a <a href="/wiki/Bludger" title="Bludger">bludger</a> was chasing him. Furthermore, she strongly disliked those with <a href="/wiki/Pure-blood_supremacy" title="Pure-blood supremacy">pure-blood supremacist</a> views, such as <a href="/wiki/Draco_Malfoy" title="Draco Malfoy">Draco Malfoy</a>, and believed that no differences exist between pure-bloods and muggle-borns, as she screamed to Malfoy <i>"How dare you!"</i>, when he called <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione Granger</a> a <i>"<a href="/wiki/Mudblood" title="Mudblood">mudblood</a>"</i>. She was also brave, as she was sorted at <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor" title="Gryffindor">Gryffindor</a> house, and was a member of <a href="/wiki/Dumbledore%27s_Army" title="Dumbledore&#39;s Army">Dumbledore's Army</a> in order to oppose <a href="/wiki/Dolores_Umbridge" title="Dolores Umbridge">Dolores Umbridge</a>. Furthermore, she fought at the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a> in order to help defeat the Death Eaters and win the final battle, showing her to be very courageous and determined indeed. 
</p>

<p>Alicia was a member of the <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor_Quidditch_team" title="Gryffindor Quidditch team">Gryffindor Quidditch team</a>, during her years at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts</a>. There, she became best friends with fellow members <a href="/wiki/Angelina_Johnson" title="Angelina Johnson">Angelina Johnson</a>, with whom she possibly shared a dormitory, and <a href="/wiki/Katie_Bell" title="Katie Bell">Katie Bell</a>, who was a year younger than her. Alicia, Angelina and Katie were also the only girls at the team.
</p><p>She was also a good friend of the twins <a href="/wiki/Fred_and_George_Weasley" title="Fred and George Weasley">Fred and George Weasley</a>. Fred and George liked doing pranks and teasing the others, but defended their friends. When Fred and George were kicked off from the team by <a href="/wiki/Dolores_Umbridge" title="Dolores Umbridge">Dolores Umbridge</a>, Alicia was very angry with them, as their loss at the team would cost them the Quidditch cup.
</p><p>Another friend of hers was <a href="/wiki/Lee_Jordan" title="Lee Jordan">Lee Jordan</a>. Although he wasn't a member of the team, he was the Quidditch matches commentator, and strongly supported the Gryffindor Quidditch team. Lee liked doing pranks, too.
</p><p>Alicia was also a close friend of <a href="/wiki/Oliver_Wood" title="Oliver Wood">Oliver Wood</a>, the Captain of the team. However, they argued several times, as in <a href="/wiki/1992" title="1992">1992</a>; Alicia shouted at Oliver, when he wanted Harry to keep on playing, while a Bludger was chasing him. Oliver had been very proud of the team by the end of the year; the year that they eventually won the Quidditch cup.
</p><p>Other members of the team were <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> and <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ron Weasley</a>. Harry, the <a href="/wiki/Seeker" title="Seeker">Seeker</a>, was extremely good, but Ron had a lack of confidence, due to the <a href="/wiki/Slytherin" title="Slytherin">Slytherins</a> making fun of him. Eventually, Ron was proved to be very good, as the members of the team, including Alicia, supported him and advised him not to be bothered by the Slytherins.
</p><p>Alicia seemed to care about Harry, as he was her friend, and she thought he shouldn't keep on playing in <a href="/wiki/1992" title="1992">1992</a>, while a Bludger was chasing him. She also went as far as to kiss Harry after he won them a match against Ravenclaw (along with Angelina and Katie). Alicia also became a member of <a href="/wiki/Dumbledore%27s_Army" title="Dumbledore&#39;s Army">Dumbledore's Army</a>, in order to be taught <a href="/wiki/Defence_Against_the_Dark_Arts" title="Defence Against the Dark Arts">Defence Against the Dark Arts</a> by Harry, and she later fought at the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a>, alongside Harry and the other members of the team.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Alicia_Spinnet?veaction=edit&amp;section=17" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Alicia_Spinnet?action=edit&amp;section=17" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Alicia is related to Alice and ultimately the German Adalheid, meaning "noble type (of person) also kind and beauty." The last name really exists and is often spelled Spinnett. A spinet a small upright piano. Alicia's name also may be a variation of spinner or spinney, a small grove of trees.
</p>

<ul><li>Alicia Spinnet's ethnicity is unknown as it was never clearly described or stated in the books.</li>
<li>Alicia Spinnet was portrayed by <a href="/wiki/Leilah_Sutherland" title="Leilah Sutherland">Leilah Sutherland</a> in <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Philosopher%27s_Stone_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Philosopher&#39;s Stone (film)">Harry Potter and the Philosopher's Stone</a></i> and by <a href="/wiki/Rochelle_Douglas" title="Rochelle Douglas">Rochelle Douglas</a> for <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Chamber_of_Secrets_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets (film)">Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets</a></i>. If Alicia appeared in latter films, none of the actresses or extras were credited.</li>
<li>None of the actresses who previously portrayed Alicia have been asked to reprise the role for any of the other <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_(film_series)" title="Harry Potter (film series)">films</a>,<sup class="noprint">&#91;<a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Wikipedia:Citing_sources" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Wikipedia:Citing sources"><i><span title="The material in the vicinity of this tag needs references to reliable sources." style="white-space: nowrap;">citation needed</span></i></a>&#93;</sup> nor have any new ones been recast. She did not appear in the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a> scene in the <i>Deathly Hallows</i> film.</li>
<li>Out of the three Chasers who played for Gryffindor during Harry's first five years at Hogwarts, Alicia is the only one to not be injured during a Quidditch match in any of the films (Angelina crashed into the stands in <i>Philosopher's Stone</i> and Katie's broom caught fire in <i>Prisoner of Azkaban</i>). She is also the only member of the original Gryffindor team to never have a speaking role in any of the films.</li></ul>
