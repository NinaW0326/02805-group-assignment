<b>Alguff the Awful</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Goblin" title="Goblin">goblin</a> who was known for being particularly foul-smelling. He was well-known throughout the goblin community for attempting to sell vials of his own sweat to a <a href="/wiki/Dungbomb" title="Dungbomb">Dungbomb</a> maker. For this, he was featured on a <a href="/wiki/Chocolate_Frog_Card" title="Chocolate Frog Card">Chocolate Frog Card</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
