<p><b>Hooky</b> was a famous <a href="/wiki/House-elf" title="House-elf">house-elf</a>, though what he achieved or was well-known for is unknown.
</p><p>A small statue of Hooky was located in the <a href="/wiki/Dungeons" title="Dungeons">dungeons</a> of <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Castle" title="Hogwarts Castle">Hogwarts Castle</a>, at the bottom of a corridor full of <a href="/wiki/Wiggenweld_Potion" title="Wiggenweld Potion">Wiggenweld Potions</a>, <a href="/wiki/Potions_(class)" title="Potions (class)">Potions</a> equipment, and ingredients. However, it is unlikely that he worked there at the <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">school</a> due to <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione Granger</a>'s absent knowledge of his kind in the castle.
</p><p>Hooky's statue was one of those required by Hermione in order to complete a challenge set by <a href="/wiki/Fred_Weasley" title="Fred Weasley">Fred</a> and <a href="/wiki/George_Weasley" title="George Weasley">George Weasley</a> during the <a href="/wiki/1993%E2%80%931994_school_year" title="1993–1994 school year">1993–1994 school year</a>. Every time she found a set of three statues, she was allowed to read a chapter of <i><a href="/wiki/The_Dark_Forces:_A_Guide_to_Self-Protection" title="The Dark Forces: A Guide to Self-Protection">The Dark Forces: A Guide to Self-Protection</a></i>.
</p>
