<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_Gryffindor_man_(III)?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_Gryffindor_man_(III)?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_life">Early life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_Gryffindor_man_(III)?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Early life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_Gryffindor_man_(III)?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Early life"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>This individual was born in either <a href="/wiki/Great_Britain" title="Great Britain">Great Britain</a> or <a href="/wiki/Ireland" title="Ireland">Ireland</a>, sometime before <a href="/wiki/1974" title="1974">1974</a>. He attended <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> in his youth, and was <a href="/wiki/Sorting_ceremony" title="Sorting ceremony">sorted</a> into <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor" title="Gryffindor">Gryffindor House</a>.
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Later_life">Later life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_Gryffindor_man_(III)?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Later life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_Gryffindor_man_(III)?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Later life"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Sometime after <a href="/wiki/Graduation_ceremony" title="Graduation ceremony">his graduation</a>, he returned to Hogwarts and became the <a href="/wiki/Head_of_House" title="Head of House">Head</a> of <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor" title="Gryffindor">Gryffindor House</a>. He had a portrait of himself hung in <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor_Common_Room" title="Gryffindor Common Room">his house's common room</a>, depicting him wearing a navy-blue coat and standing against a red background, with the Hogwarts crest printed beside his head. He had, unfortunately, died by <a href="/wiki/1991" title="1991">1991</a>.
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Post-mortem">Post-mortem</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_Gryffindor_man_(III)?veaction=edit&amp;section=4" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Post-mortem">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_Gryffindor_man_(III)?action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Post-mortem"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>The wizard's portrait remained, and in <a href="/wiki/1994" title="1994">1994</a>, he was terrified by <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> opening his <a href="/wiki/Golden_egg" title="Golden egg">wailing gold egg</a>, holding his hands over his ears in agony. 
The next year, he wondered what <a href="/wiki/Godric_Gryffindor" title="Godric Gryffindor">Godric Gryffindor</a> would say if he were still alive, perhaps indicating a dislike for that generation.<sup id="cite_ref-GOFG_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOFG-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Physical_appearance">Physical appearance</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_Gryffindor_man_(III)?veaction=edit&amp;section=5" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Physical appearance">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_Gryffindor_man_(III)?action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Physical appearance"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>This wizard was tall and had a serious face; it was quite tan. He had dark, narrow eyes similar to <a href="/wiki/Phineas_Nigellus_Black" title="Phineas Nigellus Black">Phineas Nigellus Black</a>'s, and a long black beard with matching hair, both having silver streaks in them.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Personality_and_traits">Personality and traits</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_Gryffindor_man_(III)?veaction=edit&amp;section=6" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Personality and traits">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_Gryffindor_man_(III)?action=edit&amp;section=6" title="Edit section: Personality and traits"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>He was, if his house indicated his personality, a brave man, who was very loyal, nervous, and chivalrous. He had plenty courage and daring, and obviously was deemed to be worth following, given his appointment as Head of House. However, he was also slightly snide and critical, telling sarcastic jokes about the <a href="/wiki/Fat_Lady" title="Fat Lady">Fat Lady</a>, even though she did not like this and asked him not to.
</p>
