<p>This <b>man</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> and a <a href="/wiki/Magical_Maintenance_Department" title="Magical Maintenance Department">Magical Maintenance Department</a> worker at the <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">Ministry of Magic</a> in the late-<a href="/wiki/1990s" title="1990s">1990s</a>. On the morning of <a href="/wiki/2_September" title="2 September">2 September</a>, <a href="/wiki/1997" title="1997">1997</a>, this wizard greeted "<a href="/wiki/Reginald_Cattermole" title="Reginald Cattermole">Reginald Cattermole</a>" and complained of the <a href="/wiki/Whitehall_underground_public_toilets" title="Whitehall underground public toilets">workers' entrance to the Ministry</a>, without knowing he was actually talking to <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ronald Weasley</a> in <a href="/wiki/Polyjuice_Potion" title="Polyjuice Potion">disguise</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-DH12_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH12-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
