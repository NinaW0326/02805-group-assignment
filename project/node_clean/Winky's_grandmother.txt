<p><b>Winky's grandmother</b> was a <a href="/wiki/House-elf" title="House-elf">house-elf</a> that faithfully served the <a href="/wiki/Crouch_family" title="Crouch family">Crouch family</a> her entire life. After <a href="/wiki/Winky" title="Winky">Winky</a> was dismissed by her master, <a href="/wiki/Bartemius_Crouch_Senior" title="Bartemius Crouch Senior">Barty Crouch Snr</a>, in <a href="/wiki/August" title="August">August</a> <a href="/wiki/1994" title="1994">1994</a>, Winky despaired at what her grandmother would say if she knew.<sup id="cite_ref-GOF21_1-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF21-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
