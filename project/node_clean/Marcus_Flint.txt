<p><b>Marcus Flint</b> (b. <a href="/wiki/1975" title="1975">1975</a>/<a href="/wiki/1976" title="1976">1976</a>)<sup id="cite_ref-DOB_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DOB-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> who attended <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> from <a href="/wiki/1987" title="1987">1987</a> to <a href="/wiki/1994" title="1994">1994</a> and was sorted into <a href="/wiki/Slytherin" title="Slytherin">Slytherin</a> house. He was a <a href="/wiki/Chaser" title="Chaser">Chaser</a> on the Slytherin <a href="/wiki/Quidditch" title="Quidditch">Quidditch</a> <a href="/wiki/Slytherin_Quidditch_team" title="Slytherin Quidditch team">team</a>, as well as its Captain from at least <a href="/wiki/1991" title="1991">1991</a> to <a href="/wiki/1994" title="1994">1994</a>.

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Marcus_Flint?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Marcus_Flint?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_life">Early life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Marcus_Flint?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Early life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Marcus_Flint?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Early life"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Marcus was born in September <a href="/wiki/1975" title="1975">1975</a> to August <a href="/wiki/1976" title="1976">1976</a><sup id="cite_ref-DOB_1-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DOB-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> in <a href="/wiki/Great_Britain" title="Great Britain">Great Britain</a>. He was a descendant of the <a href="/wiki/Pure-blood" title="Pure-blood">pure-blood</a> <a href="/wiki/Flint_family" title="Flint family">Flint family</a> and perhaps related in some way to former <a href="/wiki/Minister_for_Magic" title="Minister for Magic">Minister for Magic</a>, <a href="/wiki/Josephina_Flint" title="Josephina Flint">Josephina Flint</a> and <a href="/wiki/Ursula_Flint" title="Ursula Flint">Ursula Black</a> (née Flint). It is unknown if Marcus himself was a pure-blood.
</p>

<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_years">Early years</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Marcus_Flint?veaction=edit&amp;section=4" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Early years">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Marcus_Flint?action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Early years"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<figure class="thumb tright show-info-icon" style="width: 252px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/3/3b/Slytherin_team.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20160730192804" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/3/3b/Slytherin_team.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/250?cb=20160730192804" decoding="async" width="250" height="108" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="Slytherin team.jpg" data-image-key="Slytherin_team.jpg" /></a> 	 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:Slytherin_team.jpg" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption">Marcus and other Slytherins playing a game of Quidditch</p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure>
<p>Flint began attending <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> in <a href="/wiki/1987" title="1987">1987</a><sup id="cite_ref-DOB_1-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DOB-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> and was <a href="/wiki/Sorting_ceremony" title="Sorting ceremony">Sorted</a> into <a href="/wiki/Slytherin" title="Slytherin">Slytherin</a> <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Houses" title="Hogwarts Houses">House</a>. He lived in a dormitory in the <a href="/wiki/Slytherin_Dungeon" title="Slytherin Dungeon">Slytherin Dungeon</a>, and his <a href="/wiki/Head_of_House" title="Head of House">Head of House</a> was the <a href="/wiki/Potions_Master" title="Potions Master">Potions Master</a>, <a href="/wiki/Professor" title="Professor">Professor</a> <a href="/wiki/Severus_Snape" title="Severus Snape">Severus Snape</a>.
</p><p>At some point, he joined the <a href="/wiki/Slytherin_Quidditch_team" title="Slytherin Quidditch team">Slytherin Quidditch team</a> as a <a href="/wiki/Chaser" title="Chaser">Chaser</a> and became its <a href="/wiki/Quidditch_Captain#Hogwarts_Quidditch_Captains" title="Quidditch Captain">captain</a> by his <a href="/wiki/Fifth_year" title="Fifth year">fifth year</a>. Flint was known for being very rough and often brutal in his Quidditch-playing; for example, his handshakes with <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor" title="Gryffindor">Gryffindor</a> Captain and rival <a href="/wiki/Oliver_Wood" title="Oliver Wood">Oliver Wood</a> often turned into attempts to break one another's fingers. He was also not above committing fouls, blocking <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> in a <a href="/wiki/1991" title="1991">1991</a> Quidditch match.<sup id="cite_ref-PS11_6-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PS11-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<p>In <a href="/wiki/1992" title="1992">1992</a>, Flint was pleased when his team received <a href="/wiki/Nimbus_2001" title="Nimbus 2001">new broomsticks</a>, courtesy of <a href="/wiki/Lucius_Malfoy" title="Lucius Malfoy">Lucius Malfoy</a>, when his son <a href="/wiki/Draco_Malfoy" title="Draco Malfoy">Draco</a> became the team's new <a href="/wiki/Seeker" title="Seeker">Seeker</a>. They bragged to the <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor_Quidditch_team" title="Gryffindor Quidditch team">Gryffindor Quidditch team</a> about this and mocked their rivals' older brooms. This prompted <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione Granger</a> to insult Draco, remarking that "<i>at least no one on the Gryffindor team had to </i>buy<i> their way in</i>". Draco retaliated by calling Hermione a "<i>filthy little <a href="/wiki/Mudblood" title="Mudblood">Mudblood</a></i>" to the outrage of the Gryffindors. Flint had to stand in front of Draco to prevent <a href="/wiki/Fred_Weasley" title="Fred Weasley">Fred</a> and <a href="/wiki/George_Weasley" title="George Weasley">George Weasley</a> from attacking and injuring his Seeker. <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ron Weasley</a> then attempted to <a href="/wiki/Slug-Vomiting_Charm" class="mw-redirect" title="Slug-Vomiting Charm">curse</a> Draco, but it backfired, and the entire Slytherin team laughed uproariously as Ron vomited <a href="/wiki/Slug" title="Slug">slugs</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-CS7_7-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-CS7-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup> 
</p><p>Flint was angered by Draco's stupidity during a match against Gryffindor. He reprimanded him after the game because Draco failed to notice the <a href="/wiki/Golden_Snitch" title="Golden Snitch">Golden Snitch</a> next to him, causing Slytherin to lose the match. This was witnessed by George Weasley.<sup id="cite_ref-COS10_8-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COS10-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup>

<p>During Flint's final year, he participated in a scheme to scare Harry Potter with Draco, <a href="/wiki/Vincent_Crabbe" title="Vincent Crabbe">Vincent Crabbe</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Gregory_Goyle" title="Gregory Goyle">Gregory Goyle</a>. The four Slytherins dressed up as <a href="/wiki/Dementor" title="Dementor">Dementors</a>, which were guarding Hogwarts that year due to <a href="/wiki/Sirius_Black" title="Sirius Black">Sirius Black</a>'s escape from <a href="/wiki/Azkaban" title="Azkaban">Azkaban</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-POA13_9-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-POA13-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup> Following their cruel prank, <a href="/wiki/Professor" title="Professor">Professor</a> <a href="/wiki/Minerva_McGonagall" title="Minerva McGonagall">Minerva McGonagall</a> took fifty <a href="/wiki/House_points" title="House points">House points</a> from <a href="/wiki/Slytherin" title="Slytherin">Slytherin</a> and sentenced all four of them to detention as punishment. During the Quidditch final between Gryffindor and Slytherin <a href="/wiki/1993%E2%80%931994_school_year" title="1993–1994 school year">that year</a>, Flint played unscrupulously yet again against the rival team, smashing into <a href="/wiki/Angelina_Johnson" title="Angelina Johnson">Angelina Johnson</a> with his broom, which resulted in <a href="/wiki/Fred_Weasley" title="Fred Weasley">Fred Weasley</a> throwing his <a href="/wiki/Beater%27s_bat" title="Beater&#39;s bat">Beater's bat</a> at his head, smashing his nose against his broom handle and causing it to bleed. Flint later managed during the game to score a goal against Gryffindor, but Harry caught the <a href="/wiki/Golden_Snitch" title="Golden Snitch">Golden Snitch</a>, allowing Gryffindor to win the game and to gain the <a href="/wiki/Inter-House_Quidditch_Cup" title="Inter-House Quidditch Cup">Quidditch Cup</a> at last.<sup id="cite_ref-POA15_10-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-POA15-10">&#91;10&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Later_Life">Later Life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Marcus_Flint?veaction=edit&amp;section=7" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Later Life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Marcus_Flint?action=edit&amp;section=7" title="Edit section: Later Life"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>It is unknown what happened to Flint after he graduated Hogwarts and what his status was during the <a href="/wiki/Second_Wizarding_War" title="Second Wizarding War">Second Wizarding War</a>.
</p><p>During the 2010s, Flint's younger self appeared as a <a href="/wiki/Foundable" title="Foundable">Foundable</a> during the <a href="/wiki/Calamity" title="Calamity">Calamity</a>, clad in his Slytherin Quidditch uniform and entrapped by a magical <a href="/wiki/Fire" title="Fire">fire</a> <a href="/wiki/Confoundable" title="Confoundable">Confoundable</a>. Volunteer members of the <a href="/wiki/Statute_of_Secrecy_Task_Force" title="Statute of Secrecy Task Force">Statute of Secrecy Task Force</a> were able to free him by casting the <a href="/wiki/Water-Making_Spell" title="Water-Making Spell">Water-Making Spell</a>, allowing Flint to return to his original time.<sup id="cite_ref-WU_11-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-WU-11">&#91;11&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Personality_and_traits">Personality and traits</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Marcus_Flint?veaction=edit&amp;section=9" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Personality and traits">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Marcus_Flint?action=edit&amp;section=9" title="Edit section: Personality and traits"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<div class="quote">
<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;Harry Potter and the Philosopher&#39;s Stone">"'<i>Now, I want a nice fair game, all of you', <a href="/wiki/Rolanda_Hooch" title="Rolanda Hooch">she</a> said, once they were all gathered around her. <dd>&#8212;Rolanda Hooch's observation of Flint's cheating.<sup class="noprint"><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Philosopher%27s_Stone" title="Harry Potter and the Philosopher&#39;s Stone">[src]</a></sup></dd>

<p>Flint did not follow good sportsmanship when playing <a href="/wiki/Quidditch" title="Quidditch">Quidditch</a>, despite his position as <a href="/wiki/Quidditch_Captain#Hogwarts_Quidditch_Captains" title="Quidditch Captain">Captain</a> of his <a href="/wiki/Slytherin_Quidditch_team" title="Slytherin Quidditch team">team</a>. He allowed <a href="/wiki/Draco_Malfoy" title="Draco Malfoy">Draco Malfoy</a> to join the team when bribed with the new <a href="/wiki/Nimbus_2001" title="Nimbus 2001">Nimbus 2001</a> <a such as blocking <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> in <a href="/wiki/1991" title="1991">1991</a><sup id="cite_ref-PS11_6-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PS11-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup> and trying to scare him by disguising as a <a href="/wiki/Dementor" title="Dementor">Dementor</a> in <a href="/wiki/1993" title="1993">1993</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-POA13_9-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-POA13-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup> He seemed to find the misfortunes of others amusing as he laughed when Ron's <a href="/wiki/Slug-vomiting_Charm" title="Slug-vomiting Charm">Slug-vomiting Charm</a> backfired on himself.<sup id="cite_ref-CS7_7-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-CS7-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup> When Draco failed to notice the <a href="/wiki/Golden_Snitch" title="Golden Snitch">Snitch</a> floating above his head, leading the team to lose, Flint yelled at the new <a href="/wiki/Seeker" title="Seeker">Seeker</a> rather harshly for his foolishness.<sup id="cite_ref-COS10_8-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COS10-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup> It was unknown how he viewed the importance of <a href="/wiki/Blood_status" title="Blood status">blood status</a>, though he seemed to approve of when Draco called <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione</a> a <a href="/wiki/Mudblood" title="Mudblood">mudblood</a> and even defended him from harm afterwards, implying he may have held some prejudice towards <a href="/wiki/Muggle-born" title="Muggle-born">Muggle-borns</a> or that he was a <a href="/wiki/Pure-blood_supremacy" title="Pure-blood supremacy">pure-blood supremacist</a>, similar to many other Slytherin students.<sup id="cite_ref-CS7_7-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-CS7-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<ul><li><b><a href="/wiki/Flight" title="Flight">Flying</a></b>: Marcus played as a <a href="/wiki/Chaser" title="Chaser">Chaser</a> on the <a href="/wiki/Slytherin_Quidditch_team" title="Slytherin Quidditch team">Slytherin Quidditch team</a>. He was the <a href="/wiki/Captain" title="Captain">Captain</a> from at least his fifth year to his seventh year, demonstrating he was an accomplished and skilled <a href="/wiki/Broomstick" title="Broomstick">broomstick</a> flyer and <a href="/wiki/Quidditch" title="Quidditch">Quidditch</a> player, despite his frequent cheating and foul play on the Quidditch pitch.<sup id="cite_ref-PS11_6-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PS11-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-POA15_10-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-POA15-10">&#91;10&#93;</a></sup></li></ul>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Marcus_Flint?veaction=edit&amp;section=11" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Marcus_Flint?action=edit&amp;section=11" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>The name Marcus is derived from Mars, the god of war in Roman mythology. This may allude to Flint's aggression. It is interestingly the opposite of <a href="/wiki/Oliver_Wood" title="Oliver Wood">Oliver Wood</a>'s name, which means "olive tree", ultimately symbolising Wood's peaceful nature.</li>
<li>A flint is a stone used for lighting a <a href="/wiki/Fire" title="Fire">fire</a>. Once again, this alludes to his relationship with Oliver Wood; flint is kindling fire, and wood burns when in contact with fire.</li></ul>

<ul><li>Flint was originally stated to be a <a href="/wiki/Sixth_year" title="Sixth year">sixth-year</a> in <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Philosopher%27s_Stone" title="Harry Potter and the Philosopher&#39;s Stone">Harry Potter and the Philosopher's Stone</a></i>, yet he was still at the school in <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Prisoner_of_Azkaban" title="Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban">Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban</a></i> (two years after his debut). <a href="/wiki/J._K._Rowling" title="J. K. Rowling">J. K. Yeates reprised the role and was credited correctly in the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Chamber_of_Secrets_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets (film)">second</a> film. He wore false teeth in both films.</li>
<li>Flint once borrowed <i><a href="/wiki/Quidditch_Through_the_Ages_(real)" title="Quidditch Through the Ages (real)">Quidditch Through the Ages</a></i> from the <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Library" title="Hogwarts Library">Hogwarts Library</a> and was due to return the book on <a href="/wiki/22_June" title="22 June">22 June</a>.</li>
<li>If <a href="/wiki/Stanley_Shunpike" title="Stanley Shunpike">Stan Shunpike</a> attended Hogwarts, he would have been in the same year as Marcus Flint.<sup class="noprint">&#91;<a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Wikipedia:Citing_sources" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Wikipedia:Citing sources"><i><span title="The material in the vicinity of this tag needs references to reliable sources." style="white-space: nowrap;">citation needed</span></i></a>&#93;</sup></li>
<li>In <i><a href="/wiki/LEGO_Harry_Potter:_Years_1-4" title="LEGO Harry Potter: Years 1-4">LEGO Harry Potter: Years 1-4</a></i>, Marcus Flint is the <a href="/wiki/Slytherin_Quidditch_team" title="Slytherin Quidditch team">Slytherin team</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Seeker" title="Seeker">Seeker</a>.</li>
<li>He was referred to as "Captain Flint" because he was captain of the Slytherin Quidditch Team.
