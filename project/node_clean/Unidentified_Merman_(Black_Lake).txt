<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_Merman_(Black_Lake)?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_Merman_(Black_Lake)?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>During the Second Task, <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> tried to borrow his spear to cut the ropes binding the hostages, but the merman refused, laughing as he yanked the spear back from Harry.<sup id="cite_ref-GOF26_1-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF26-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> It is unknown if he was present at <a href="/wiki/Funeral_of_Albus_Dumbledore" title="Funeral of Albus Dumbledore">Dumbledore's funeral</a> in <a href="/wiki/1997" title="1997">1997</a>, along with the rest of his colony.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP30_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP30-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Physical_appearance">Physical appearance</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_Merman_(Black_Lake)?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Physical appearance">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_Merman_(Black_Lake)?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Physical appearance"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<div class="quote">
<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire">"<i>...he swam swiftly toward a seven-foot tall merman with a long green beard and a choker of shark fangs...</i>"</span></dd>
<dd>&#8212;<a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>'s description on the merman<sup class="noprint"><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Goblet_of_Fire" title="Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire">[src]</a></sup></dd>
<dd></dd></dl></div>
<p>The merman was large, rising to almost seven feet tall. He bore a choker of <a href="/wiki/Shark_fang" title="Shark fang">shark fangs</a> around his neck and had a long green beard.<sup id="cite_ref-GOF26_1-10" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF26-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Personality_and_traits">Personality and traits</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_Merman_(Black_Lake)?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Personality and traits">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_Merman_(Black_Lake)?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Personality and traits"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Despite he was unhelpful to <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> when he tried to have access to his spear, he was loyal, obeying the orders to not aid any of the champions during the task.<sup id="cite_ref-GOF26_1-11" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF26-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
