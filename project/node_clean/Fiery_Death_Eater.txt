<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Fiery_Death_Eater?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Fiery_Death_Eater?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>This <a href="/wiki/Dark_Wizard" title="Dark Wizard">Dark Wizard</a> was born sometime in or before 1981, into either a <a href="/wiki/Pure-blood" title="Pure-blood">pure</a> or <a href="/wiki/Half-blood" title="Half-blood">half-blood</a> family, presumably in <a href="/wiki/Great_Britain" title="Great Britain">Great Britain</a>.
</p><p>He was trained in the <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magical</a> arts sometime before <a href="/wiki/1998" title="1998">1998</a>, most likely at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts</a>,&#160;given that he fought in a battle that year and had became a follower and servant of&#160;<a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a>, an immensely powerful <a href="/wiki/Dark_Wizard" title="Dark Wizard">Dark Wizard</a> sometime before then. Being a Death Eater, he would have been highly accomplished in the <a href="/wiki/Dark_Arts" title="Dark Arts">Dark Arts</a>.&#160;
</p><p>This person, toward the end of the battle, was <a href="/wiki/Fire-Making_Spell" title="Fire-Making Spell">set ablaze</a> and <a href="/wiki/Everte_Statum" title="Everte Statum">thrown</a> across the length of the <a href="/wiki/Great_Hall" title="Great Hall">Great Hall</a>&#160;to the floor; he very likely died of his injuries.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
