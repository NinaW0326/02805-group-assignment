<p><b>Marietta Edgecombe</b> (born c. <a href="/wiki/1978" title="1978">1978</a> – <a href="/wiki/1982" title="1982">1982</a>) was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch</a> who was a student at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a>, starting sometime between <a href="/wiki/1990" title="1990">1990</a> and <a href="/wiki/1993" title="1993">1993</a>. She was <a href="/wiki/Sorting_ceremony" title="Sorting ceremony">Sorted</a> into <a href="/wiki/Ravenclaw" title="Ravenclaw">Ravenclaw</a> <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Houses" title="Hogwarts Houses">House</a>. In the <a href="/wiki/1995%E2%80%931996_school_year" title="1995–1996 school year">1995–1996 school year</a>, Marietta joined <a href="/wiki/Dumbledore%27s_Army" title="Dumbledore&#39;s Army">Dumbledore's Army</a> at the urging of her best friend <a href="/wiki/Cho_Chang" title="Cho Chang">Cho Chang</a>, an organisation taught and led by <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>, despite her being reluctant to get on <a href="/wiki/Dolores_Umbridge" title="Dolores Umbridge">Dolores Umbridge</a>'s bad side. 
</p><p>When she became afraid that her <a href="/wiki/Edgecombe" title="Edgecombe">mother's</a> job at the <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">Ministry of Magic</a> might be threatened by her membership in the organisation, Marietta betrayed the D.A. to Umbridge. This activated a <a href="/wiki/Dumbledore%27s_Army_parchment_jinx" title="Dumbledore&#39;s Army parchment jinx">jinx that caused boils to erupt on Marietta's face</a>, and a <a href="/wiki/Memory_Charm" title="Memory Charm">Memory Charm</a> was later placed on her by <a href="/wiki/Kingsley_Shacklebolt" title="Kingsley Shacklebolt">Kingsley Shacklebolt</a> to prevent her from fully incriminating her fellow D.A. members.
</p>

<h3><span id="Early_life_(Birth_-_early_1990s)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_life_.28Birth_-_early_1990s.29">Early life (Birth - early 1990s)</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Marietta_Edgecombe?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Early life (Birth - early 1990s)">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Marietta_Edgecombe?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Early life (Birth - early 1990s)"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Little is known of Marietta's life before she began attending <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts</a>. She was born into a wizarding family, so she likely received her <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_acceptance_letter" title="Hogwarts acceptance letter">Hogwarts acceptance letter</a> by an owl.
</p>
<h3><span id="Hogwarts_years_(1990s)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="Hogwarts_years_.281990s.29">Hogwarts years (1990s)</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Marietta_Edgecombe?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Hogwarts years (1990s)">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Marietta_Edgecombe?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Hogwarts years (1990s)"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_years">Early years</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Marietta_Edgecombe?veaction=edit&amp;section=4" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Early years">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Marietta_Edgecombe?action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Early years"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<p>Marietta began attending <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> as early as <a href="/wiki/1990" title="1990">1990</a> and as late as <a href="/wiki/1993" title="1993">1993</a>. She was sorted into <a href="/wiki/Ravenclaw" title="Ravenclaw">Ravenclaw</a> house.<sup id="cite_ref-OTP_3-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OTP-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> At some point, she met Cho Chang, and became a friend of hers.
</p><p>When <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry</a> asked Cho to the <a href="/wiki/Yule_Ball" title="Yule Ball">Yule Ball</a>, Marietta was amongst the group of friends to giggle at the boy, much to Harry's discomfort. When Cho fell into a somewhat emotional wreck due to <a href="/wiki/Cedric_Diggory" title="Cedric Diggory">Cedric Diggory</a>'s death, Marietta was the only one of her many friends who remained with her. Cho considered her a true friend because of this loyalty. However, Marietta was forbidden by her parents to go against the Ministry during the summer of that year, as the Ministry had denied Harry and <a href="/wiki/Albus_Dumbledore" title="Albus Dumbledore">Albus Dumbledore</a>'s claims of Voldemort's return; this would later put her at odds with Cho, who believed Harry.<sup id="cite_ref-OTP_3-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OTP-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<div class="quote">
<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix">"<i>She doesn't really want to be here I made her come with me. Her parents have forbidden her from doing anything that might upset Umbridge — her mum works for the Ministry.</i>"</span></dd>
<dd>&#8212;Cho regarding Marietta<sup class="noprint"><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Order_of_the_Phoenix" title="Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix">[src]</a></sup></dd>
<dd></dd></dl></div>
<p>During the 1995-1996 school year, Marietta was persuaded to attend the first meeting of what would become Dumbledore's Army in the <a href="/wiki/Hog%27s_Head" class="mw-redirect" title="Hog&#39;s Head">Hog's Head</a> pub in <a href="/wiki/Hogsmeade" title="Hogsmeade">Hogsmeade</a> by Cho, despite her obvious suspicion of Harry's intentions as well as his claims that <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a> had <a href="/wiki/Return_of_Lord_Voldemort" title="Return of Lord Voldemort">returned</a>. She gave Harry a thoroughly distrustful look to tell him that, given her way, she would not be there and slopped <a href="/wiki/Butterbeer" title="Butterbeer">Butterbeer</a> down herself with a shriek when the name "Voldemort" was said. She also gave Cho a disapproving look for signing the <a href="/wiki/Dumbledore%27s_Army_parchment" title="Dumbledore&#39;s Army parchment">piece of parchment</a> drawn up by <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione Granger</a> for all members to sign and only added her name reluctantly.<sup id="cite_ref-OTP_3-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OTP-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<p>Despite Harry's extremely effective teaching methods, Marietta did not seem to enjoy the lessons, especially during the first lesson when Cho accidentally set her sleeve on fire, and continued to resent Harry, seeing both him and Dumbledore as liars and nutters. She acted disrespectfully to Harry, behaviour which Cho told him to ignore. Her membership in the D.A. became particularly difficult as it increasingly came into conflict with <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_High_Inquisitor" title="Hogwarts High Inquisitor">Hogwarts High Inquisitor</a> Dolores Umbridge, who was at Hogwarts as the representative of the <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">Ministry of Magic</a> where Marietta's mother worked. Even after the <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a>' <a href="/wiki/Azkaban_escapes#1996" title="Azkaban escapes">mass breakout</a> of <a href="/wiki/Azkaban" title="Azkaban">Azkaban</a> and Harry's subsequent interview was published in <i><a href="/wiki/The_Quibbler" title="The Quibbler">The Quibbler</a></i>, Marietta continued to distrust Harry.<sup id="cite_ref-OTP_3-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OTP-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>After six months of the meetings, and from the pressure regarding her mother's career, Marietta finally betrayed Dumbledore's Army to the Ministry. After dinner, while the rest of Dumbledore's Army was learning how to cast a <a href="/wiki/Patronus_Charm" title="Patronus Charm">Patronus</a>, Marietta instead went to Umbridge's <a href="/wiki/Defence_Against_the_Dark_Arts_professor%27s_office" class="mw-redirect" title="Defence Against the Dark Arts professor&#39;s office">office</a> and gave her an advantageous hint about the <a href="/wiki/Room_of_Requirement" title="Room of Requirement">Room of Requirement</a>. With a little further questioning from Umbridge, Marietta confessed that a meeting was taking place there. Right after that, Marietta came to regret her decision, as the parchment she and the other members of the D.A. had signed at the first meeting was <a href="/wiki/Dumbledore%27s_Army_parchment_jinx" title="Dumbledore&#39;s Army parchment jinx">jinxed</a>, making anyone who betrayed the organisation break out in pimples that spelt the word "SNEAK" across their face. Upon catching her face in Umbridge's mirror, Marietta covered her face with shame.<sup id="cite_ref-OTP_3-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OTP-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>As Marietta could not be persuaded to uncover her face (which muffled her voice), she was unable to verbally confess everything to <a href="/wiki/Cornelius_Fudge" title="Cornelius Fudge">Cornelius Fudge</a>. As such, Umbridge simply asked her and told her to nod. However, just before she was to confess all to Umbridge, <a href="/wiki/Kingsley_Shacklebolt" title="Kingsley Shacklebolt">Kingsley Shacklebolt</a>, an <a href="/wiki/Auror" title="Auror">Auror</a> and a secret member of the <a href="/wiki/Order_of_the_Phoenix" title="Order of the Phoenix">Order of the Phoenix</a>, took advantage of Marietta's silent shame to perform a <a href="/wiki/Memory_Charm" title="Memory Charm">Memory Charm</a> on her so that she could not implicate Albus Dumbledore and Harry. The sudden change in her testimony angered Umbridge so much that the professor began shaking her violently and only stopped when Dumbledore pointed <a href="/wiki/Elder_Wand" title="Elder Wand">his wand</a> at her in anger and caused Edgecombe to <a href="/wiki/Defensive_charge" title="Defensive charge">emit a forcible charge</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-OTP_3-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OTP-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<p>After Dumbledore took the blame of the organisation's formation, Marietta was one of the few people who witnessed the fierce battle between Fudge's brigade and Dumbledore in the <a href="/wiki/Headmaster%27s_office" class="mw-redirect" title="Headmaster&#39;s office">Headmaster's office</a>. However, as she went to the <a href="/wiki/Hospital_Wing" title="Hospital Wing">Hospital Wing</a> to clear up the blemishes, she was unable to give anyone a full account. Throughout her stay there, <a href="/wiki/Poppy_Pomfrey" title="Poppy Pomfrey">Madam Pomfrey</a> was unable to make the slightest change in the jinx. Cho was upset for her friend and defended her to Harry, saying Marietta merely made a mistake and called Hermione's jinx a <i>"horrible trick"</i> whilst trying to persuade Harry to forgive Marietta. Harry, however, considered Marietta a traitor and her informing the ruining of the D.A., to which he replied that the jinx was a <i>"brilliant idea"</i>. After this row, their relationship fell apart.<sup id="cite_ref-OTP_3-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OTP-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>At the end of the year, Marietta was seen wearing a balaclava to cover her disfigured face, accompanying Cho on the <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Express" title="Hogwarts Express">train</a>, evading Harry.<sup id="cite_ref-OTP_3-10" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OTP-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Marietta still had blemishes on her face from the jinx the <a href="/wiki/1996-1997_school_year" class="mw-redirect" title="1996-1997 school year">following school year</a>, and took to wearing thick make-up to try to cover them up, with limited success. She sat with <a href="/wiki/Cho_Chang" title="Cho Chang">Cho</a> on the <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Express" title="Hogwarts Express">train</a> to Hogwarts; they saw Harry during this journey, but did not speak to him and looked away when he walked past them on the train.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP_6-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup> The formation of the pimples on her face would eventually fade, leaving a few scars behind.<sup id="cite_ref-JKR_7-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-JKR-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Physical_appearance">Physical appearance</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Marietta_Edgecombe?veaction=edit&amp;section=5" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Physical appearance">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Marietta_Edgecombe?action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Physical appearance"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Marietta was described as having curly, reddish-blonde hair.<sup id="cite_ref-OTP_3-11" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OTP-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> After she was struck with the parchment jinx for betraying the D.A., she bore pimples spelling "SNEAK" across her face. During this time, Marietta covered them up first with a balaclava and then thick makeup, the latter of which did not completely hide them. They faded with time, but Marietta was left with a few scars.<sup id="cite_ref-JKR_7-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-JKR-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Personality_and_traits">Personality and traits</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Marietta_Edgecombe?veaction=edit&amp;section=6" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Personality and traits">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Marietta_Edgecombe?action=edit&amp;section=6" title="Edit section: Personality and traits"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<div class="quote">
<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix">"<i>She's a lovely person really. She just made a mistake...</i>"</span></dd>
<dd>&#8212;<a href="/wiki/Cho_Chang" title="Cho Chang">Cho Chang</a> defending Marietta<sup class="noprint"><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Order_of_the_Phoenix" title="Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix">[src]</a></sup></dd>
<dd></dd></dl></div>
<p>When Cho became depressed over Cedric's death, Marietta was presumably comforting and supportive, being the only one to remain with her when the other friends left irresponsibly. Cho claimed Marietta was a lovely person, an attribute that was mostly unseen in her behaviour to others.
</p><p>However, Marietta was one of the many people who were loyal to the Ministry of Magic and was sceptical of Harry Potter's statement of Lord Voldemort <a href="/wiki/Return_of_Lord_Voldemort" title="Return of Lord Voldemort">returning</a>. She thought that he and Albus Dumbledore were liars and remained cynical towards him throughout her time in the D.A., and would not be swayed by the genuine article within <i><a href="/wiki/The_Quibbler" title="The Quibbler">The Quibbler</a> </i>about Voldemort's return; she only joined because Cho wanted her to. As with the majority of the <a href="/wiki/Wizarding_world" title="Wizarding world">magical community</a>, Marietta feared to hear the name "Voldemort", as she shrieked aloud and slopped Butterbeer down her front.
</p><p>Marietta's mother was a Ministry employee and as such, Marietta felt strong pressure to be on Dolores Umbridge's good side. Her apparent faithfulness to Cho Chang diminished when Cho forced her to join an illegal organisation, and she eventually betrayed Dumbledore's Army meaning her classmates (including Cho) who were in the organisation were almost expelled from school. Even if it meant that she would forsake a chance to learn more practical defence, especially the <a href="/wiki/Patronus_Charm" title="Patronus Charm">Patronus Charm</a> that others were so eager to learn, Marietta chose her family's loyalty first. While Cho insisted that Marietta simply made a mistake, Harry Potter felt that Marietta was disgraceful and an unforgivable traitor and Hermione commented that she was "stupid".<sup id="cite_ref-HBP_6-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>When her face was horribly disfigured for her betrayal against Dumbledore's Army, she became extremely embarrassed and refused to uncover her face to testify verbally, leading to some setbacks for Umbridge and Fudge that ultimately gave Kingsley a chance to modify her memory. Because of the jinx scarring her face, she took great measures to cover her disfigured face, and even became anxious in the presence of Harry Potter afterwards, implying she may have felt some guilt over her treacherous behaviours, but lacked the courage to apologise to him.  
</p><p>She was shown to be a poor judge of character, as she heavily didn't believe Harry Potter and Albus Dumbledore's story, instead believing the Ministry's <a href="/wiki/Campaign_to_discredit_Albus_Dumbledore_and_Harry_Potter" title="Campaign to discredit Albus Dumbledore and Harry Potter">smear campaign against them</a>. She was also very critical and insolent towards Harry during their DA meetings, and even laughed at him the year before when he tried asking Cho out the year before, showing her as a rather unpleasant girl with a somewhat sour and spiteful attitude towards others. 
</p>

<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Family">Family</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Marietta_Edgecombe?veaction=edit&amp;section=9" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Family">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Marietta_Edgecombe?action=edit&amp;section=9" title="Edit section: Family"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Marietta's mother worked for the Ministry, and therefore, Marietta herself was loyal to it; her parents forbade her from getting onto the Ministry's bad side during her school year. She feared that any bad impressions left on Dolores Umbridge, a Ministry representative at Hogwarts, would injure her mother's reputation at the office, and therefore refused to get on Umbridge's bad side.
</p>

<p>Marietta is one of <a href="/wiki/Cho_Chang" title="Cho Chang">Cho Chang</a>'s gang of giggling friends and accompanies with her to almost everywhere. After the rest of the friends left Cho due to her emotional breakdown from her late boyfriend <a href="/wiki/Cedric_Diggory" title="Cedric Diggory">Cedric Diggory</a>'s death, Marietta is the only one who remained with her. This means that while the others only followed Cho for her popularity, Marietta is indeed loyal and true. However, when Cho brought Marietta to Dumbledore's Army against her will, Marietta's loyalty for Cho faded rapidly, and eventually betrayed her and the entire group, and was willing to have even her best friend expelled for being part of <a href="/wiki/Educational_Decree_Number_Twenty-Four" title="Educational Decree Number Twenty-Four">an illegal organisation</a>.

It is unknown how their friendship was during the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a>, but Marietta did not fight alongside Cho, showing her loyalty to friends was wavered. 
</p>

<p>When <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> first asked Cho to the <a href="/wiki/Yule_Ball" title="Yule Ball">Yule Ball</a>, Marietta was one of the many girls that giggled at him, something that irritated Harry. The following year, when the Ministry had a smear campaign in the <i><a href="/wiki/Daily_Prophet" title="Daily Prophet">Daily Prophet</a></i> against Harry and his claims that Lord Voldemort had returned, Marietta believed it due to her family's connections with the Ministry. As such, when she was forced to join Dumbledore's Army, which Harry was the leader, she was very distrustful and disrespectful to his words and actions.
</p><p>Despite Harry's extremely effective methods of teaching and <i><a href="/wiki/The_Quibbler" title="The Quibbler">The Quibbler</a></i> printing out Harry's version of the story, Marietta continued to believe that Harry was an unstable lunatic, and distrusted him, an attitude that Harry ignored at Cho's behest. After Marietta betrayed the organisation, Harry felt no sympathy for her despite her mother's position, and he insulted her, leading to the ultimate breakup between him and Cho when she defended Marietta. When hearing that Marietta could not remove the pustules inflicted by her treason, Harry saw it as just desserts, and reflected that Hermione was brilliant to have jinxed the list, later sneering at Marietta's limited success in hiding her marks the following year. She refused to face Harry on the Hogwarts Express journey home at the end of her final year, implying she felt some guilt for betraying him. 
</p>
<h3><span id="Dumbledore's_Army"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="Dumbledore.27s_Army">Dumbledore's Army</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Marietta_Edgecombe?veaction=edit&amp;section=12" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Dumbledore&#039;s Army">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Marietta_Edgecombe?action=edit&amp;section=12" title="Edit section: Dumbledore&#039;s Army"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<figure class="thumb tleft show-info-icon" style="width: 240px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/9/96/Dumbledore%27s_Army.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20161123060553" class="image"><img alt="" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw%3D%3D" decoding="async" width="238" height="158" class="thumbimage lazyload" data-image-name="Dumbledore&#39;s Army.jpg" data-image-key="Dumbledore&#39;s_Army.jpg" data-src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/9/96/Dumbledore%27s_Army.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/238?cb=20161123060553" /></a> 	<noscript><a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/9/96/Dumbledore%27s_Army.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20161123060553" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/9/96/Dumbledore%27s_Army.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/238?cb=20161123060553" decoding="async" width="238" height="158" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="Dumbledore&#39;s Army.jpg" data-image-key="Dumbledore&#39;s_Army.jpg" data-src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/9/96/Dumbledore%27s_Army.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/238?cb=20161123060553" /></a></noscript> 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:Dumbledore%27s_Army.jpg" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption">Dumbledore's Army, the organisation Marietta betrayed</p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure>
<p>Cho forced Marietta to attend the illegal organisation, Dumbledore's Army, against her will, despite it being a great potential of ruining her mother's reputation at the Ministry. None of the members seemed to care too much about Marietta's cynical behaviour against their formation, until she betrayed them to Umbridge, nearly expelling them all. Once that happened, they considered her a traitor and felt that many of the following events were her fault; Hermione referred Marietta as "stupid" while Ron thought up of a large number of insults when Harry brought up the subject. Also, this betrayal led to the split between Harry and Cho, Cho being the only member to stand up for Marietta.
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Dolores_Umbridge">Dolores Umbridge</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Marietta_Edgecombe?veaction=edit&amp;section=13" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Dolores Umbridge">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Marietta_Edgecombe?action=edit&amp;section=13" title="Edit section: Dolores Umbridge"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<figure class="thumb tright show-info-icon" style="width: 162px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/6/68/Dolores_Umbridge_Deathly_Hallows_promotional_image.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20101127041001" class="image"><img alt="" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw%3D%3D" decoding="async" width="160" height="211" class="thumbimage lazyload" data-image-name="Dolores Umbridge Deathly Hallows promotional image.jpg" data-image-key="Dolores_Umbridge_Deathly_Hallows_promotional_image.jpg" data-src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/6/68/Dolores_Umbridge_Deathly_Hallows_promotional_image.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/160?cb=20101127041001" /></a> 	<noscript><a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/6/68/Dolores_Umbridge_Deathly_Hallows_promotional_image.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20101127041001" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/6/68/Dolores_Umbridge_Deathly_Hallows_promotional_image.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/160?cb=20101127041001" decoding="async" width="160" height="211" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="Dolores Umbridge Deathly Hallows promotional image.jpg" data-image-key="Dolores_Umbridge_Deathly_Hallows_promotional_image.jpg" data-src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/6/68/Dolores_Umbridge_Deathly_Hallows_promotional_image.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/160?cb=20101127041001" /></a></noscript> 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:Dolores_Umbridge_Deathly_Hallows_promotional_image.jpg" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption">Dolores Umbridge, her DADA professor</p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure> 
<p>Marietta, being the daughter of a Ministry employee, was adamant in not doing anything that may go against Umbridge, who was a high-ranking Ministry official assigned to Hogwarts as a teacher and inspector, fearing that doing anything that seemed rebellious may damage her mother's reputation and standing within the government. As such, Marietta was very reluctant in joining the illegal Dumbledore's Army.
</p><p>Eventually, her loyalty to her mother and Umbridge overwhelmed that to Cho, and Marietta betrayed DA's whereabouts to the High Inquisitor. Umbridge, desperate in getting more information in expelling Potter, put up a falsified sweet side to coax Marietta, who was too ashamed to show her face when Hermione's jinx disfigured it, to reveal more to Cornelius Fudge. When Kingsley Shacklebolt secretly modified Marietta's memories to contradict what Umbridge was made to believe earlier, Umbridge lost her temper at this change of testimony, and even violently shook the girl until Dumbledore threatened her. It was then that Umbridge deemed Marietta untrustworthy. 
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Albus_Dumbledore">Albus Dumbledore</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Marietta_Edgecombe?veaction=edit&amp;section=14" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Albus Dumbledore">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Marietta_Edgecombe?action=edit&amp;section=14" title="Edit section: Albus Dumbledore"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<figure class="thumb tleft show-info-icon" style="width: 161px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/0/09/600full-Albus-Dumbledore-the-prisoner-of-azkaban-photo.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20141122220412" class="image"><img alt="" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw%3D%3D" decoding="async" width="159" height="216" class="thumbimage lazyload" data-image-name="600full-Albus-Dumbledore-the-prisoner-of-azkaban-photo.jpg" data-image-key="600full-Albus-Dumbledore-the-prisoner-of-azkaban-photo.jpg" data-src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/0/09/600full-Albus-Dumbledore-the-prisoner-of-azkaban-photo.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/159?cb=20141122220412" /></a> 	<noscript><a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/0/09/600full-Albus-Dumbledore-the-prisoner-of-azkaban-photo.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20141122220412" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/0/09/600full-Albus-Dumbledore-the-prisoner-of-azkaban-photo.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/159?cb=20141122220412" decoding="async" width="159" height="216" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="600full-Albus-Dumbledore-the-prisoner-of-azkaban-photo.jpg" data-image-key="600full-Albus-Dumbledore-the-prisoner-of-azkaban-photo.jpg" data-src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/0/09/600full-Albus-Dumbledore-the-prisoner-of-azkaban-photo.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/159?cb=20141122220412" /></a></noscript> 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:600full-Albus-Dumbledore-the-prisoner-of-azkaban-photo.jpg" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption">Albus Dumbledore, her headmaster</p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure>
<p>For the first few years of her schooling, Marietta had no problem with the headmaster, <a href="/wiki/Albus_Dumbledore" title="Albus Dumbledore">Albus Dumbledore</a>, who was also highly revered within the wizarding community. However, when the Ministry campaigned against Dumbledore and Potter, Marietta, out of loyalty for her family, believed the Ministry over Dumbledore. Even after Harry's interview in the Quibbler, Marietta refused to believe either of them and continued to think of them as nutters and liars, and ultimately betrayed Harry and Dumbledore's Army. Despite this distrust and betrayal, Dumbledore seemed to hold no grudge against Marietta and even defended her from Umbridge's assault, meaning he likely sympathised with her. 
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Marietta_Edgecombe?veaction=edit&amp;section=15" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Marietta_Edgecombe?action=edit&amp;section=15" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li><i>Marietta</i> is a female given name, an <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Italian_language" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Italian language">Italian</a> diminutive of <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Maria" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Maria">Maria</a>. It means "little bitter".</li>
<li><i>Edgecombe</i> is a misspelling of Edgcumbe, an English surname. The name Edgecombe was also used for a <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/County" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:County">county</a> located in the <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/U.S._state" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:U.S. state">U.S. state</a> of <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/North_Carolina" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:North Carolina">North Carolina</a>, named after <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Richard_Edgcumbe,_1st_Baron_Edgcumbe" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Richard Edgcumbe, 1st Baron Edgcumbe">Richard Edgcumbe</a>, a <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Member_of_Parliament" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Member of Parliament">Member of Parliament</a> from <a href="/wiki/1701" class="mw-redirect" title="1701">1701</a> to <a href="/wiki/1742" class="mw-redirect" title="1742">1742</a> and a <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/List_of_Commissioners_of_the_Treasury#Commissioners_of_the_Treasury_of_Great_Britain_.281714-1800.29" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:List of Commissioners of the Treasury">Lord of the Treasury</a>, who became 1st Baron Edgcumbe in 1742.</li></ul>

<figure class="thumb tright show-info-icon" style="width: 152px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/1/17/Marietta_Edgecombe_lego.png/revision/latest?cb=20111210014601" class="image"><img alt="" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw%3D%3D" decoding="async" width="150" height="215" class="thumbimage lazyload" data-image-name="Marietta Edgecombe lego.png" data-image-key="Marietta_Edgecombe_lego.png" data-src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/1/17/Marietta_Edgecombe_lego.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/150?cb=20111210014601" /></a> 	<noscript><a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/1/17/Marietta_Edgecombe_lego.png/revision/latest?cb=20111210014601" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/1/17/Marietta_Edgecombe_lego.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/150?cb=20111210014601" decoding="async" width="150" height="215" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="Marietta Edgecombe lego.png" data-image-key="Marietta_Edgecombe_lego.png" data-src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/1/17/Marietta_Edgecombe_lego.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/150?cb=20111210014601" /></a></noscript> 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:Marietta_Edgecombe_lego.png" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption">Marietta Edgecombe in <i><a href="/wiki/LEGO_Harry_Potter:_Years_5-7" title="LEGO Harry Potter: Years 5-7">LEGO Harry Potter: Years 5-7</a></i></p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure>
<ul><li>As Marietta performed non-verbal spells during her time in Dumbledore's Army, it suggests she was doing her <a href="/wiki/Nastily_Exhausting_Wizarding_Test" title="Nastily Exhausting Wizarding Test">N.E.W.T.s</a>, which is consistent with her being in the same school year as Cho, as this is when Harry and his friends started to practice non-verbal magic.</li>
<li>In the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Order_of_the_Phoenix_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix (film)">film adaptation</a> of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Order_of_the_Phoenix" title="Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix">Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix</a></i>, Marietta is absent, and <a href="/wiki/Cho_Chang" title="Cho Chang">Cho Chang</a> is the one who "betrayed" the <a href="/wiki/Dumbledore%27s_Army" title="Dumbledore&#39;s Army">D.A.</a> whilst under the effects of <a href="/wiki/Veritaserum" title="Veritaserum">Veritaserum</a>, though Marietta was mentioned in the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Order_of_the_Phoenix_(video_game)" title="Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix (video game)">game</a> when Harry asked Cho to bring her to the DA meeting when trying to ask her for a date.</li>
<li>In the books, Marietta has no distinguishable dialogue, though other characters have discussed and made references about her.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/J._K._Rowling" title="J. K. Rowling">J. K. Rowling</a> confirmed that Marietta's pimples faded but left a few scars, commenting <i>"I loathe a traitor!"</i>.<sup id="cite_ref-JKR_7-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-JKR-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li>It is unknown if Marietta returned to Hogwarts and participated in the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a> alongside Cho, nor if she survived the reign of Lord Voldemort, but given her loyalty issues and that she was considered a traitor by all other members of the D.A., she was likely not accepted back into Dumbledore's Army.</li></ul>
