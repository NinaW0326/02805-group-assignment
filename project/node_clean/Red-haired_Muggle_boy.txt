<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_life">Early life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Red-haired_Muggle_boy?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Early life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Red-haired_Muggle_boy?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Early life"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>This boy was presumably born in the <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggle</a> part of <a href="/wiki/Ottery_St_Catchpole" title="Ottery St Catchpole">Ottery St Catchpole</a>, a village in <a href="/wiki/Devon" title="Devon">Devon</a>, <a href="/wiki/England" title="England">England</a>, and still lived there in <a href="/wiki/1997" title="1997">1997</a>.
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Wedding_of_Bill_Weasley_and_Fleur_Delacour">Wedding of Bill Weasley and Fleur Delacour</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Red-haired_Muggle_boy?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Wedding of Bill Weasley and Fleur Delacour">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Red-haired_Muggle_boy?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Wedding of Bill Weasley and Fleur Delacour"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>In order to disguise <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> during the <a href="/wiki/Wedding_of_William_Weasley_and_Fleur_Delacour" title="Wedding of William Weasley and Fleur Delacour">wedding</a> of <a href="/wiki/William_Weasley" title="William Weasley">Bill Weasley</a> and <a href="/wiki/Fleur_Delacour" title="Fleur Delacour">Fleur Delacour</a> on <a href="/wiki/1_August" title="1 August">1 August</a>, <a href="/wiki/1997" title="1997">1997</a>, <a href="/wiki/Fred_Weasley" title="Fred Weasley">Fred Weasley</a> stole a few hairs from the head of this boy. It was used to make <a href="/wiki/Polyjuice_Potion" title="Polyjuice Potion">Polyjuice Potion</a> for Harry to take on his form. This boy's hair was red in colour, making it possible for Harry to pass as a <a href="/wiki/Weasley_family" title="Weasley family">Weasley</a> cousin. He was also slightly plump, as Harry mentioned being uncomfortable in his dress robes, for the boy he impersonated was slightly fatter than him. However, it should be noted that Harry was&#160;rather thin.<sup id="cite_ref-DH8_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH8-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
