<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Express_conductor?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Express_conductor?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_life">Early life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Express_conductor?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Early life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Express_conductor?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Early life"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<div class="quote">
<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;The Wizarding World of Harry Potter">"<i>Ya' know, I tried one of those </i>[Muggle recording cameras]<i>, and it does not taste like an apple.</i>"</span></dd>
<dd>&#8212;The conductor relays information on his past experiences with "Muggle contraptions"<sup class="noprint"><a href="/wiki/The_Wizarding_World_of_Harry_Potter" title="The Wizarding World of Harry Potter">[src]</a></sup></dd>
<dd></dd></dl></div>
<p>This individual was born at some point in or before <a href="/wiki/1960" title="1960">1960</a>, probably somewhere in the <a href="/wiki/Great_Britain" title="Great Britain">British Isles</a>, given his British accent. He does not seem to have been raised around <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggles</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-blood_2-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-blood-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> In his youth, he attended <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a>, and admitted to have gone into the <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_kitchens" title="Hogwarts kitchens">kitchens</a> on at least one occasion.<sup id="cite_ref-kitchens_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-kitchens-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> After his education in the <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magical</a> arts, he joined the <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Railways" title="Hogwarts Railways">Hogwarts Railways</a> and started working as the conductor of the <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Express" title="Hogwarts Express">Hogwarts Express</a> sometime before <a href="/wiki/1978" title="1978">1978</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-startedworking_4-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-startedworking-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>At some point in his life, he tried to eat a <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggle</a> <a href="/wiki/Cine-camera" title="Cine-camera">cine-camera</a> expecting it to taste like an <a href="/wiki/Apple_(fruit)" title="Apple (fruit)">apple</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-camera_5-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-camera-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> As such, when he recalled this incident, he remarked he was "not a fan of eating cameras".<sup id="cite_ref-camera2_6-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-camera2-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Conductor_of_the_Hogwarts_Express">Conductor of the Hogwarts Express</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Express_conductor?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Conductor of the Hogwarts Express">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Express_conductor?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Conductor of the Hogwarts Express"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>

<div class="quote">
<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;Harry Potter and the Philosopher&#39;s Stone (video game)">"<i>Fine looking group of students this time. It'll be time to get underway soon.</i>"</span></dd>
<dd>&#8212;The conductor to <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> on <a href="/wiki/1_September" title="1 September">1 September</a>, <a href="/wiki/1991" title="1991">1991</a><sup class="noprint"><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Philosopher%27s_Stone_(video_game)" title="Harry Potter and the Philosopher&#39;s Stone (video game)">[src]</a></sup></dd>

<p>Sometime in the <a href="/wiki/1970s" title="1970s">1970s</a>, the conductor met <a href="/wiki/Sirius_Black" title="Sirius Black">Sirius Black</a>, then a student at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School</a>. He recalled him as a "bright lad, very good-natured".<sup id="cite_ref-Sirius_7-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Sirius-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>By the early <a href="/wiki/1990s" title="1990s">1990s</a>, the conductor was reported by some students to smell "funny".<sup id="cite_ref-PSgbc_8-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PSgbc-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>In the summer of <a href="/wiki/1993" title="1993">1993</a>, <a href="/wiki/Sirius_Black" title="Sirius Black">Sirius Black</a> <a href="/wiki/Azkaban_escapes#1993" title="Azkaban escapes">escaped</a> from <a href="/wiki/Azkaban" title="Azkaban">Azkaban</a>, where he was imprisoned on false charges. Having met the man as a student years prior, the conductor did not believe he was, as the <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">Ministry of Magic</a> had declared, an <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">ally</a> of the <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Dark Lord</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-Sirius_7-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Sirius-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup> On <a href="/wiki/1_September" title="1 September">1 September</a> <a href="/wiki/1993" title="1993">1993</a>, the train was stopped on its way to Hogwarts for inspection by <a href="/wiki/Dementor" title="Dementor">Dementors</a> searching for Black. During the stop, <a href="/wiki/Remus_Lupin" title="Remus Lupin">Remus Lupin</a> went to have a word with the driver.<sup id="cite_ref-PA5_9-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PA5-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup> The Hogwarts Express non-stop journey from <a href="/wiki/King%27s_Cross_Station" title="King&#39;s Cross Station">King's Cross</a> to <a href="/wiki/Hogsmeade_station" title="Hogsmeade station">Hogsmeade</a> was interrupted briefly, but the conductor still managed to assist it in arriving at its destination on time.<sup id="cite_ref-PA5_9-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PA5-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<div class="quote">
<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 2">"<i>All aboard.</i>"</span></dd>
<dd>&#8212;The conductor<sup class="noprint"><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Deathly_Hallows:_Part_2" title="Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 2">[src]</a></sup></dd>
<dd></dd></dl></div>
<p>As of <a href="/wiki/2017" title="2017">2017</a> he still worked as conductor for the <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Express" title="Hogwarts Express">Hogwarts Express</a>, and is still making it from <a href="/wiki/Platform_Nine_and_Three-Quarters" title="Platform Nine and Three-Quarters">the platform</a> to <a href="/wiki/Hogsmeade_station" title="Hogsmeade station">Hogsmeade station</a> on time.
</p><p>In the August of <a href="/wiki/2019" title="2019">2019</a>, he appeared as a <a href="/wiki/Foundable" title="Foundable">Foundable</a> during the <a href="/wiki/Calamity" title="Calamity">Calamity</a>. Volunteer members of the <a href="/wiki/Statute_of_Secrecy_Task_Force" title="Statute of Secrecy Task Force">Statute of Secrecy Task Force</a> were able to free him.<sup id="cite_ref-WU_10-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-WU-10">&#91;10&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Express_conductor?veaction=edit&amp;section=4" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Express_conductor?action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>It is unknown if the Conductor underwent the same sort of progressive "mental takeover" by the Express as experienced by the <a href="/wiki/Trolley_witch" title="Trolley witch">Trolley Witch</a>, and by the same token whether his life was similarly extended.</li>
<li>As a wizard with strong connections to Hogwarts, he, along with the <a href="/wiki/Trolley_witch" title="Trolley witch">Honeydukes Express lady</a>, may have been called upon to participate in the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a>, however this is unknown.</li></ul>
<figure class="thumb tright show-info-icon" style="width: 252px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/0/07/Conductor.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20110521041217" class="image"><img alt="" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw%3D%3D" decoding="async" width="250" height="217" class="thumbimage lazyload" data-image-name="Conductor.jpg" data-image-key="Conductor.jpg" data-src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/0/07/Conductor.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/250?cb=20110521041217" /></a> 	<noscript><a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/0/07/Conductor.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20110521041217" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/0/07/Conductor.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/250?cb=20110521041217" decoding="async" width="250" height="217" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="Conductor.jpg" data-image-key="Conductor.jpg" data-src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/0/07/Conductor.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/250?cb=20110521041217" /></a></noscript> 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:Conductor.jpg" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption">The conductor at <a href="/wiki/The_Wizarding_World_of_Harry_Potter" title="The Wizarding World of Harry Potter">The Wizarding World of Harry Potter</a></p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure>
<ul><li>The conductor is included in <a href="/wiki/The_Wizarding_World_of_Harry_Potter" title="The Wizarding World of Harry Potter">The Wizarding World of Harry Potter</a>. The conductor of the Hogwarts Express knows anything and everything about the village of Hogsmeade. He maintains a keen knowledge of what can be purchased where throughout the area, as well as knowing the history of the village and all the locations within. Visitors to the town may stop by the station to ask him any number of questions, as he enjoys answering any query delivered his way. The conductor carries with him at all times a <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Express_conductor%27s_pocket_watch" title="Hogwarts Express conductor&#39;s pocket watch">gold enchanted pocket watch</a> that he uses to be sure everything in his charge is always on time. The Hogwarts Express engine head plaque is engraved on the top of the watch cover along with the engine number of the Hogwarts Express, 5972. On the reverse side is the Hogwarts school crest and motto. The face of the watch is enchanted but may appear to show the words "Hogsmeade Station" on the top of the face, "King's Cross" on the bottom, with the left hand side reading: "Late" and the right hand side reading: "On time".</li>
<li>Through the film series a lot of people can be seen with clothes identical to his own, so it is possible that he appears in the films too, despite never identified.</li></ul>
