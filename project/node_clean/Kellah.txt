<b>Kellah</b> (born c. <a href="/wiki/1980" title="1980">1980</a>)<sup id="cite_ref-age_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-age-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch</a> who attended <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> from <a href="/wiki/1991" title="1991">1991</a> to <a href="/wiki/1998" title="1998">1998</a> and was <a href="/wiki/Sorting_ceremony" title="Sorting ceremony">Sorted</a> into <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor" title="Gryffindor">Gryffindor</a> <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Houses" title="Hogwarts Houses">House</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-POAF_3-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-POAF-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Kellah shared a dormitory in <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor_Tower" title="Gryffindor Tower">Gryffindor Tower</a> with <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione Granger</a>, <a href="/wiki/Lavender_Brown" title="Lavender Brown">Lavender Brown</a>, <a href="/wiki/Parvati_Patil" title="Parvati Patil">Parvati Patil</a>, <a href="/wiki/Fay_Dunbar" title="Fay Dunbar">Fay Dunbar</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Fay_Dunbar%27s_friend" class="mw-redirect" title="Fay Dunbar&#39;s friend">another girl</a> whom she stayed with for six years. In her <a href="/wiki/Third_year" title="Third year">third year</a>, she chose <a href="/wiki/Care_of_Magical_Creatures" title="Care of Magical Creatures">Care of Magical Creatures</a> as an elective subject, with <a href="/wiki/Rubeus_Hagrid" title="Rubeus Hagrid">Rubeus Hagrid</a> as her <a href="/wiki/Professor" title="Professor">professor</a>. Kellah also visited <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Hospital_Wing" title="Hospital Wing">Hospital Wing</a> after he was attacked by the <a href="/wiki/Dementor" title="Dementor">Dementors</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-POAF_3-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-POAF-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_life">Early life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Kellah?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Early life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Kellah?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Early life"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Kellah was born in either <a href="/wiki/1979" title="1979">1979</a> or <a href="/wiki/1980" title="1980">1980</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-age_1-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-age-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> likely in either <a href="/wiki/Great_Britain" title="Great Britain">Great Britain</a> or <a href="/wiki/Ireland" title="Ireland">Ireland</a>.
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Hogwarts_years">Hogwarts years</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Kellah?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Hogwarts years">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Kellah?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Hogwarts years"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_years">Early years</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Kellah?veaction=edit&amp;section=4" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Early years">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Kellah?action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Early years"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<p>In <a href="/wiki/1991" title="1991">1991</a>, Kellah began her magical studies at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> and was <a href="/wiki/Sorting_ceremony" title="Sorting ceremony">Sorted</a> into <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor" title="Gryffindor">Gryffindor House</a>, home to the brave and daring, during the <a href="/wiki/Sorting_ceremony" title="Sorting ceremony">Sorting ceremony</a>. Along with the other Gryffindors, Kellah was led by <a href="/wiki/Percy_Weasley" title="Percy Weasley">Percy Weasley</a> to the <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor_Tower" title="Gryffindor Tower">Gryffindor Common Room</a> and through the <a href="/wiki/Fat_Lady" title="Fat Lady">Fat Lady</a>'s portrait. She shared a dormitory with <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione Granger</a>, <a href="/wiki/Lavender_Brown" title="Lavender Brown">Lavender Brown</a>, <a href="/wiki/Parvati_Patil" title="Parvati Patil">Parvati Patil</a> and <a href="/wiki/Fay_Dunbar" title="Fay Dunbar">Fay Dunbar</a>. Her <a href="/wiki/Head_of_House" title="Head of House">Head of House</a> was <a href="/wiki/Minerva_McGonagall" title="Minerva McGonagall">Minerva McGonagall</a>, the <a href="/wiki/Professor" title="Professor">Professor</a> of <a href="/wiki/Transfiguration_(class)" title="Transfiguration (class)">Transfiguration</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-POAF_3-10" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-POAF-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Third_year">Third year</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Kellah?veaction=edit&amp;section=5" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Third year">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Kellah?action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Third year"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>

<p>In <a href="/wiki/1993" title="1993">1993</a>, Kellah did clap after <a href="/wiki/Rubeus_Hagrid" title="Rubeus Hagrid">Rubeus Hagrid</a> was announced as <a href="/wiki/Silvanus_Kettleburn" title="Silvanus Kettleburn">Silvanus Kettleburn</a>'s successor as the <a href="/wiki/Care_of_Magical_Creatures" title="Care of Magical Creatures">Care of Magical Creatures</a> <a href="/wiki/Professor" title="Professor">professor</a>, although very unenthused, unlike her fellow enthusiastic Gryffindors, implying she may not have been massively fond of him.<sup id="cite_ref-POAF_3-11" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-POAF-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>

<p><a href="/wiki/Remus_Lupin" title="Remus Lupin">Remus Lupin</a> was appointed as the <a href="/wiki/Defence_Against_the_Dark_Arts" title="Defence Against the Dark Arts">Defence Against the Dark Arts</a> <a href="/wiki/Professor" title="Professor">professor</a> that school year. Kellah attended her first Defence Against the Dark Arts class when she presented a <a href="/wiki/Boggart" title="Boggart">Boggart</a> to the class.<sup id="cite_ref-POAF_3-12" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-POAF-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> One by one, each student had to confront the Boggart, which would take the form of their greatest fear. Kellah's turned into either a <a href="/wiki/Rat" title="Rat">rat</a>, <a href="/wiki/Rattlesnake" title="Rattlesnake">rattlesnake</a> or a bloody eyeball.<sup id="cite_ref-POA7_4-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-POA7-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<p>This year, she chose <a href="/wiki/Care_of_Magical_Creatures" title="Care of Magical Creatures">Care of Magical Creatures</a> as an elective subject and was present when <a href="/wiki/Professor" title="Professor">Professor</a> <a href="/wiki/Rubeus_Hagrid" title="Rubeus Hagrid">Rubeus Hagrid</a>, <a href="/wiki/Professor" title="Professor">Professor</a> <a href="/wiki/Silvanus_Kettleburn" title="Silvanus Kettleburn">Silvanus Kettleburn</a>'s successor, presented a <a href="/wiki/Hippogriff" title="Hippogriff">Hippogriff</a> named <a href="/wiki/Buckbeak" title="Buckbeak">Buckbeak</a> to the class. Kellah also was around Harry Potter's bed in the hospital wing and presented his broken broomstick to him with <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ron Weasley</a>. Kellah appeared to be close with <a href="/wiki/Seamus_Finnigan" title="Seamus Finnigan">Seamus Finnigan</a>, opting to sit with him during lessons and meal times as opposed to her friends <a href="/wiki/Lavender_Brown" title="Lavender Brown">Lavender Brown</a> and <a href="/wiki/Parvati_Patil" title="Parvati Patil">Parvati Patil</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-POAF_3-13" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-POAF-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Physical_appearance">Physical appearance</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Kellah?veaction=edit&amp;section=6" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Physical appearance">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Kellah?action=edit&amp;section=6" title="Edit section: Physical appearance"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>She was a black girl with black/brown hair worn in braids. She wore a single stud earring on each ear.<sup id="cite_ref-POAF_3-14" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-POAF-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Kellah?veaction=edit&amp;section=7" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Kellah?action=edit&amp;section=7" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>Kellah appears only in the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Prisoner_of_Azkaban_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban (film)">film adaptation</a> of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Prisoner_of_Azkaban" title="Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban">Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban</a></i>. Portrayed by <a href="/wiki/Kandice_Morris" title="Kandice Morris">Kandice Morris</a>, this character is listed in the film's credits as simply "Girl 1," but on the actress' <a target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener" class="external text" href="http://www.yptc.co.uk/agency/kandicemorris.php">casting agency profile</a>, her name is given.</li>
<li>Kellah is mistakenly listed as <a href="/wiki/Lavender_Brown" title="Lavender Brown">Lavender Brown</a> in an article in the August 3, 2007 issue of <i>Entertainment Weekly.</i></li></ul>
