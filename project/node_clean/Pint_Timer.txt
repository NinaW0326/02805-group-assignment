<b>Pint Timers</b> were <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magical</a> devices created to ensure that <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizards and witches</a> belonging to the <a href="/wiki/Statute_of_Secrecy_Task_Force" title="Statute of Secrecy Task Force">Statute of Secrecy Task Force</a> did not linger in pubs for too long. If any wizard stayed inside a pub for more than two hours, the pint timer would immediately eject them from the pub.<sup id="cite_ref-WU_1-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-WU-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="History">History</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Pint_Timer?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: History">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Pint_Timer?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: History"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Upon noticing that many members of the Statute of Secrecy Task Force would mysteriously linger in pubs for hours after eating, <a href="/wiki/Constance_Pickering" title="Constance Pickering">Constance Pickering</a> asked a <a href="/wiki/Calamity_Investigator" title="Calamity Investigator">Calamity Investigator</a> in the task force to help come up with a way to force them to return to work. The Calamity Investigator ended up creating the pint timer, which was soon put into effect and ensured that the errant task force members would return to their jobs immediately after finishing their meals.<sup id="cite_ref-WU_1-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-WU-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
