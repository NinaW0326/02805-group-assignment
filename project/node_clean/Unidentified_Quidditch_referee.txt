<p>This <b><a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a></b> was a <a href="/wiki/Quidditch" title="Quidditch">Quidditch</a> <a href="/wiki/Quidditch_referee" title="Quidditch referee">referee</a> sometime between <a href="/wiki/1357" class="mw-redirect" title="1357">1357</a> and the <a href="/wiki/1990s" title="1990s">1990s</a>. During one of the matches he refereed, he found himself transported to the <a href="/wiki/Sahara_Desert" title="Sahara Desert">Sahara Desert</a>, as someone from the crowd of spectators <a href="/wiki/Portus" title="Portus">transformed</a> his <a href="/wiki/Broomstick" title="Broomstick">broomstick</a> into a <a href="/wiki/Portkey" title="Portkey">Portkey</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-QTAR6_1-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-QTAR6-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
