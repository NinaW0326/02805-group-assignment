<b>Eliana</b> was a young <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch</a> who featured in the ancient story of <a href="/wiki/Illyius" title="Illyius">Illyius</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-WBoS_1-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-WBoS-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Eliana?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Eliana?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>According to legend, Eliana was a young, beautiful <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch</a> who long ago lived in <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizarding</a> <a href="/wiki/Illyius%27_village" class="mw-redirect" title="Illyius&#39; village">village in the mountains</a>. In the dense forest that surrounded the hamlet, there resided a <a href="/wiki/Dark_Wizard" title="Dark Wizard">Dark wizard</a>, <a href="/wiki/Raczidian" title="Raczidian">Raczidian</a>. The villagers and Raczidian managed to co-exist peacefully for many years; he left them alone, and, in return, they stayed away from the part of the woods where his fortess resided.<sup id="cite_ref-WBoS_1-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-WBoS-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>One day, however, Eliana caught the special attention of Raczdian while she was out gathering <a href="/wiki/Berry" title="Berry">berries</a> in the forest. Taken by her, he decided she would make him the perfect wife, and he thus sent a missive to her <a href="/wiki/Eliana%27s_parents" title="Eliana&#39;s parents">parents</a> demanding her hand in marriage. They refused. Raczidian then threatened to send his Dementors to destroy the village unless they delivered Eliana to him.<sup id="cite_ref-WBoS_1-10" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-WBoS-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>The village elders conferred with each other and agreed to resist. Eliana was sent to the shack of <a href="/wiki/Illyius" title="Illyius">Illyius</a>, a shy, poor orphan who had been ordered to sit out the battle and keep Eliana company because his <a href="/wiki/Mouse" title="Mouse">mouse</a> <a href="/wiki/Patronus_Charm" title="Patronus Charm">Patronus</a> was perceived as being too feeble to be of any assistance in the battle.<sup id="cite_ref-WBoS_1-11" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-WBoS-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>At first, the villagers were able to stave off the hoard of Dementors with the collective force of their Patronuses, but gradually the sheer number of foes began to overwhelm them. Eliana begged Illyius to do something. He cast the Patronus Charm, and despite its size, the mouse shone brilliantly, bringing the Dementors to a halt. Raczidian then decided to enter the fray himself. He attempted to summon a Patronus to ward off Illyius's mouse, but because he was not pure of heart, <a href="/wiki/Maggot" title="Maggot">maggots</a> shot from his <a href="/wiki/Raczidian%27s_wand" title="Raczidian&#39;s wand">wand</a> instead and devoured him.<sup id="cite_ref-WBoS_1-12" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-WBoS-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Eliana went on to marry Illyius, who was hailed as a hero, and lived a long, happy life.<sup id="cite_ref-WBoS_1-13" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-WBoS-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
