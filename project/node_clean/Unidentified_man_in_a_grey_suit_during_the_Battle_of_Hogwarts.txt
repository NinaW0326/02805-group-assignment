This <b>individual</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> who fought at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> in <a href="/wiki/1998" title="1998">1998</a> during the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a>, and was present during <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort's</a> "victory" speech. He survived the battle.<sup id="cite_ref-DHF2_1-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DHF2-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
