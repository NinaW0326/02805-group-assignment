This <b>woman</b> (fl. <a href="/wiki/1991" title="1991">1991</a>) was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch</a> and one of the seven registered <a href="/wiki/Animagus" title="Animagus">Animagi</a> of the <a href="/wiki/20th_century" title="20th century">twentieth century</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> taking the form of a black <a href="/wiki/Cat" title="Cat">cat</a>. This witch was a regular patron of the <a href="/wiki/Leaky_Cauldron" title="Leaky Cauldron">Leaky Cauldron</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-LEGOHP_1-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-LEGOHP-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_registered_Animagus?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_registered_Animagus?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<figure class="thumb tleft show-info-icon" style="width: 182px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/2/2b/Cat_Animagus.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20100713111133" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/2/2b/Cat_Animagus.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/180?cb=20100713111133" decoding="async" width="180" height="128" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="Cat Animagus.jpg" data-image-key="Cat_Animagus.jpg" /></a> 	 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:Cat_Animagus.jpg" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption">Her Animagus form</p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure>
<ul><li>Notably, the way this witch morphs into her <a href="/wiki/Animagus" title="Animagus">Animagus</a> form differs noticeably from the other Animagi in the game (<a href="/wiki/Sirius_Black" title="Sirius Black">Sirius Black</a>, <a href="/wiki/Rita_Skeeter" title="Rita Skeeter">Rita Skeeter</a>, <a href="/wiki/Peter_Pettigrew" title="Peter Pettigrew">Peter Pettigrew</a> and <a href="/wiki/Minerva_McGonagall" title="Minerva McGonagall">Minerva McGonagall</a>). While the latter first shrink in size and then transform into their respective Animagus form, the unidentified witch transforms in a puff of blue smoke.</li>
<li>A witch sharing the same minifigure model can be seen in many other places throughout both <i><a href="/wiki/LEGO_Harry_Potter:_Years_1-4" title="LEGO Harry Potter: Years 1-4">LEGO Harry Potter: Years 1-4</a></i> and <a href="/wiki/LEGO_Harry_Potter:_Years_5-7" title="LEGO Harry Potter: Years 5-7">its sequel</a>, most notably as the playable character Witch (White). Whether every instance of this model appearing is meant to be the same character is uncertain, though the playable character cannot be this character, as she lacks the ability to turn into an Animagus form (an ability in the game).</li></ul>
