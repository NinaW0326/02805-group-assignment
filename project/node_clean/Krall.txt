<p><b>Krall</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> who lived during the early <a href="/wiki/20th_century" title="20th century">20th century</a>. He was a member of <a href="/wiki/Gellert_Grindelwald" title="Gellert Grindelwald">Gellert Grindelwald</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Alliance" title="Alliance">Alliance</a>, and was among the dark wizard's foremost acolytes.
</p><p>In <a href="/wiki/1927" title="1927">1927</a>, he accompanied his master to <a href="/wiki/Paris" title="Paris">Paris</a> and aided his efforts to recruit <a href="/wiki/Credence_Barebone" title="Credence Barebone">Credence Barebone</a>, but perished there when his dedication to Grindelwald's <a href="/wiki/For_the_Greater_Good" title="For the Greater Good">cause</a> proved inadequate.<sup id="cite_ref-COG114_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COG114-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Krall?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Krall?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Krall became a supporter of <a href="/wiki/Gellert_Grindelwald" title="Gellert Grindelwald">Gellert Grindelwald</a> at some point prior to <a href="/wiki/1927" title="1927">1927</a> and became one of his <a href="/wiki/Gellert_Grindelwald%27s_acolytes" title="Gellert Grindelwald&#39;s acolytes">acolytes</a>. He travelled to <a href="/wiki/Paris" title="Paris">Paris</a> to help further Grindelwald's plans, where he expressed doubt that <a href="/wiki/Credence_Barebone" title="Credence Barebone">Credence Barebone</a> was a match for <a href="/wiki/Albus_Dumbledore" title="Albus Dumbledore">Albus Dumbledore</a>. Grindelwald bluntly asked him if he would stand by his side, and while Krall hastily confirmed he would, Grindelwald looked unconvinced of his honesty.
</p>
<figure class="thumb tleft show-info-icon" style="width: 222px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/3/39/Krall%27s_death.gif/revision/latest?cb=20190326101805" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/3/39/Krall%27s_death.gif/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/220?cb=20190326101805" decoding="async" width="220" height="92" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="Krall&#39;s death.gif" data-image-key="Krall&#39;s_death.gif" /></a> 	 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:Krall%27s_death.gif" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption">Krall dies in the flames</p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure>
<p>Krall attended Grindelwald's <a href="/wiki/Gellert_Grindelwald%27s_Paris_rally" title="Gellert Grindelwald&#39;s Paris rally">rally</a> at the <a href="/wiki/Lestrange_Mausoleum" title="Lestrange Mausoleum">Lestrange family Mausoleum</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Cimeti%C3%A8re_du_P%C3%A8re-Lachaise" title="Cimetière du Père-Lachaise">Cimetière du Père-Lachaise</a>. He was indirectly killed by his own master in <a href="/wiki/1927" title="1927">1927</a> when he failed to cross <a href="/wiki/Protego_Diabolica" title="Protego Diabolica">enchanted fire</a> that tested one's loyalty.<sup id="cite_ref-COG114_1-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COG114-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Personality_and_traits">Personality and traits</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Krall?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Personality and traits">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Krall?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Personality and traits"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Krall was described as being <i>"sulky"</i> and <i>"ambitious"</i>.<sup id="cite_ref-deadline_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-deadline-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> Despite siding with Grindelwald when he rose to power throughout the <a href="/wiki/Wizarding_world" title="Wizarding world">wizarding world</a>, Krall was conflicted and disillusioned with his decision, to an extent. His moral struggle did not go unnoticed by his master. When push came to shove, Krall's morals outweighed his lust for power, leading to his death in Grindelwald's fire.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Relationships">Relationships</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Krall?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Relationships">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Krall?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Relationships"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Gellert_Grindelwald">Gellert Grindelwald</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Krall?veaction=edit&amp;section=4" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Gellert Grindelwald">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Krall?action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Gellert Grindelwald"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<figure class="thumb tright show-info-icon" style="width: 182px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/c/ce/Grindelwald_Headshot_COG.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20190313141118" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/c/ce/Grindelwald_Headshot_COG.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/180?cb=20190313141118" decoding="async" width="180" height="248" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="Grindelwald Headshot COG.jpg" data-image-key="Grindelwald_Headshot_COG.jpg" /></a> 	 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:Grindelwald_Headshot_COG.jpg" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption"><a href="/wiki/Gellert_Grindelwald" title="Gellert Grindelwald">Gellert Grindelwald</a>, his former master and killer</p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure>
<p>Krall was initially loyal to Gellert Grindelwald, who once trusted him enough to include him among one of his acolytes. However, at some point, his loyalty had began to waver. Sensing this, Grindelwald had no qualms confronting him over it, bluntly asking whether he would stand by him as he collected Credence to use against Albus Dumbledore and not looking convinced when Krall tried to assure him. Ultimately, Krall died when crossing Grindelwald's <i><a href="/wiki/Protego_Diabolica" title="Protego Diabolica">Protego Diabolica</a></i> due to his inadequate loyalty.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Krall?veaction=edit&amp;section=5" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Krall?action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>In a few Slavic languages, <i>kral</i> means "king", derived from the given name "Karl" (itself derived from Old Norse <i>karl</i>, "free man"), <i>i. e.</i> Charlemagne.
<ul><li>In Westrobothnian, it means "insects" or "that which crawls"</li></ul></li>
<li><i>Kraal</i> is an Afrikaans and Dutch word for an enclosure for cattle or other livestock surrounded by a fence of thorn-bush branches, a palisade, mud wall, or other fencing, roughly circular in form. It stems from the Portuguese <i>curral</i>, found in English as <i>corral</i>.</li>
<li>"Kralle" is German and means "claw" or "talon", possibly alluding to Krall's role as a henchman.</li></ul>
