<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="History">History</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Barking_Mad_Male_Gargoyle?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: History">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Barking_Mad_Male_Gargoyle?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: History"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>It was located on a <a href="/wiki/Fourth-floor_balcony" title="Fourth-floor balcony">balcony</a> on the fourth floor of the <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Castle" title="Hogwarts Castle">Hogwarts Castle</a>. It would randomly spout nonsense, or repeatedly insist that it could not speak.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTPG_1-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTPG-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>During the <a href="/wiki/1995%E2%80%931996_school_year" title="1995–1996 school year">1995–1996 school year</a>, <a href="/wiki/Cuthbert_Binns" title="Cuthbert Binns">Professor Binns</a> assigned his <a href="/wiki/Fifth_year" title="Fifth year">Fifth year</a> students the task of listing five talking gargoyles as a homework assignment. <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> located the gargoyles as a favour to <a href="/wiki/Dean_Thomas" title="Dean Thomas">Dean Thomas</a> so that he could attend the first meeting of <a href="/wiki/Dumbledore%27s_Army" title="Dumbledore&#39;s Army">Dumbledore's Army</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Room_of_Requirement" title="Room of Requirement">Room of Requirement</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTPG_1-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTPG-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Like the others, casting the <a href="/wiki/Fire-Making_Spell" title="Fire-Making Spell">Fire-Making Spell</a> on the gargoyle would cause it to breathe <a href="/wiki/Fire" title="Fire">fire</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTPG_1-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTPG-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Barking_Mad_Male_Gargoyle?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Barking_Mad_Male_Gargoyle?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>The gargoyle was voiced by <a href="/wiki/Jonathan_Kydd" title="Jonathan Kydd">Jonathan Kydd</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Order_of_the_Phoenix_(video_game)" title="Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix (video game)">video game adaptation</a> of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Order_of_the_Phoenix" title="Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix">Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix</a></i>.</li>
<li>On the Norwegian dubbed version of the game, this Gargoyle was voiced by voice actor Yngvar Numme, who had the voice of the 1994's Lion King as the warthog Pumba, which was the cause of much amusement among Norwegian Harry Potter fans, seeing as the gargoyle resembled the character.</li>
<li>The gargoyle's name was identified during the game's credits.</li>
