This <b><a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a></b> was one of the habitual clients of the <a href="/wiki/Leaky_Cauldron" title="Leaky Cauldron">Leaky Cauldron</a>. In <a href="/wiki/1991" title="1991">1991</a> he commented with <a href="/wiki/Dedalus_Diggle" title="Dedalus Diggle">Dedalus Diggle</a> on the return of <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> as he was accompanied by <a href="/wiki/Rubeus_Hagrid" title="Rubeus Hagrid">Hagrid</a> to access <a href="/wiki/Diagon_Alley" title="Diagon Alley">Diagon Alley</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-PSF_1-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PSF-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
