<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Sam%27s_owner?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Sam%27s_owner?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>This individual was crossing the bridge with his pet puggle <a href="/wiki/Sam_(dog)" title="Sam (dog)">Sam</a> when the collapse took place. When both were thrown down into <a href="/wiki/River_Thames" title="River Thames">River Thames</a>, this individual was left unconscious. Sam, then dragged him as he swam half a mile down the river, to safety. <i><a href="/wiki/The_Guardian" title="The Guardian">The Guardian</a></i> later went on to report that Sam was to become the first dog ever to be honoured with a medal for gallantry.<sup id="cite_ref-HPLE_1-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HPLE-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
