He was made a <a href="/wiki/Prefect" title="Prefect">prefect</a> in his <a href="/wiki/Fifth_year" title="Fifth year">fifth year</a> and, at least during the <a href="/wiki/1993%E2%80%931994_school_year" title="1993–1994 school year">1993–1994 school year</a>, often patrolled the <a href="/wiki/Dungeon_Corridor" title="Dungeon Corridor">Dungeon Corridor</a> with <a href="/wiki/Dungeon_Corridor_Slytherin_prefect_(I)" title="Dungeon Corridor Slytherin prefect (I)">a fellow Slytherin prefect</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
