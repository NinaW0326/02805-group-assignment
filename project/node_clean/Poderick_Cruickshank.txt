<b>Poderick Cruickshank</b> (born c. <a href="/wiki/1492" class="mw-redirect" title="1492">1492</a>) was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> whose <a href="/wiki/Portrait" title="Portrait">portrait</a> hung at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-MHP_1-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-MHP-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>His portrait was inscribed "<i>Anno MDXXVII</i>", indicating that it was painted in <a href="/wiki/1527" class="mw-redirect" title="1527">1527</a>, and "<i>Ætatis Suæ XXXV</i>", indicating he was 35 at the time the portrait was made, as <i>ættatis suæ</i> is <a href="/wiki/Latin" title="Latin">Latin</a> for "<i>of his age</i>"<i>.</i><sup id="cite_ref-MHP_1-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-MHP-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
