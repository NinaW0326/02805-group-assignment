<p>The <b>Vaults Guardian</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Giant_Fire_Crab" title="Giant Fire Crab">Giant Fire Crab</a> placed in <a href="/wiki/Spellbook_vault" title="Spellbook vault">one of the vaults</a> of <a href="/wiki/Gringotts_Wizarding_Bank" title="Gringotts Wizarding Bank">Gringotts Wizarding Bank</a> to guard the <a href="/wiki/Incendio_spellbook" title="Incendio spellbook">spellbook</a> for the <a href="/wiki/Fire-Making_Spell" title="Fire-Making Spell">Fire-Making Spell</a> contained within. Gaining access to the vault required a <a href="/wiki/Spellbook_vault_key" title="Spellbook vault key">special key made of five different coloured gemstones</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
