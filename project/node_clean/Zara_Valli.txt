<b>Zara Valli</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Hufflepuff" title="Hufflepuff">Hufflepuff</a> student at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> in the <a href="/wiki/1990s" title="1990s">1990s</a>. On the <a href="/wiki/2_September" title="2 September">first</a> day of the <a href="/wiki/1996%E2%80%931997_school_year" title="1996–1997 school year">1996–1997 school year</a>, she had to go to the <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Library" title="Hogwarts Library">library</a>, in order to do research for a fifteen-inch essay she had to write for <a href="/wiki/Charms_(class)" title="Charms (class)">Charms</a>. She agreed to give <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> some <a href="/wiki/Moondew" title="Moondew">Moondew</a> in exchange for two <a href="/wiki/Gobstones" title="Gobstones">Gobstones</a> while there. Later in the year, she could be found in the <a href="/wiki/Viaduct" title="Viaduct">Viaduct</a>, where she traded Gobstones for <a href="/wiki/Potion" title="Potion">potions</a>.
</p>
