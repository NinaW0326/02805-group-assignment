<p><b>Lily Luna Potter</b><sup id="cite_ref-PM_6-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PM-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup> (b. c. <a href="/wiki/2008" title="2008">2008</a>) was an <a href="/wiki/England" title="England">English</a> <a href="/wiki/Half-blood" title="Half-blood">half-blood</a> <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch</a>, the youngest child and only daughter of <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry</a> and <a href="/wiki/Ginevra_Weasley" title="Ginevra Weasley">Ginevra Potter</a> <span class="template-c">(née <a href="/wiki/Weasley_family" title="Weasley family">Weasley</a>)</span>. Lily has two older brothers, <a href="/wiki/James_Potter_II" title="James Potter II">James Sirius</a> and <a href="/wiki/Albus_Potter" title="Albus Potter">Albus Severus Potter</a>. She was named in honour of her late paternal grandmother <a href="/wiki/Lily_J._Potter" title="Lily J. Potter">Lily Potter</a> and her middle name comes from her parents' good friend <a href="/wiki/Luna_Lovegood" title="Luna Lovegood">Luna Lovegood</a>. It is also possible that Luna is Lily's godmother.
</p><p>Lily began her education at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> in 2019 along with her cousin, <a href="/wiki/Hugo_Granger-Weasley" title="Hugo Granger-Weasley">Hugo Granger-Weasley</a> and was sorted into <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor" title="Gryffindor">Gryffindor House</a>.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Lily_L._Potter?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Lily_L._Potter?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Lily Luna Potter was born to Harry and Ginevra Potter, as their youngest child and only daughter. She has two older brothers: <a href="/wiki/James_Potter_II" title="James Potter II">James Sirius</a> and <a href="/wiki/Albus_Potter" title="Albus Potter">Albus Severus Potter</a>. Lily is around two years younger than Albus, and four years younger than James.
</p><p>In 2014, Lily, along with her family, attended the <a href="/wiki/2014_Quidditch_World_Cup_final" title="2014 Quidditch World Cup final">2014 Quidditch World Cup final</a> in the Patagonian Desert. Lily was sporting Bulgarian red, along with the majority of her family. It should also be noted that she was very well behaved, as <a href="/wiki/Rita_Skeeter" title="Rita Skeeter">Rita Skeeter</a> did not make any scathing comments regarding her behaviour.
</p>
<figure class="thumb tleft show-info-icon" style="width: 249px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/6/69/Harry_Potter_Epilogue.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20180811150508" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/6/69/Harry_Potter_Epilogue.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/247?cb=20180811150508" decoding="async" width="247" height="103" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="Harry Potter Epilogue.jpg" data-image-key="Harry_Potter_Epilogue.jpg" /></a> 	 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:Harry_Potter_Epilogue.jpg" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption">Lily with her family at Platform 9¾ in 2017</p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure>
<p>In 2017, she went to see her brothers and her cousin, <a href="/wiki/Rose_Granger-Weasley" title="Rose Granger-Weasley">Rose Granger-Weasley</a>, off at the <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Express" title="Hogwarts Express">Hogwarts Express</a>, but was not herself old enough to attend <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a>. She also acted as excited and enthusiastic as her mother Ginny had acted when Harry and Ron went to their first year of Hogwarts. She and her cousin, <a href="/wiki/Hugo_Granger-Weasley" title="Hugo Granger-Weasley">Hugo Granger-Weasley</a>, are around the same age. Hugo and Lily talked excitedly about which house they wanted to be in at Hogwarts.
</p><p>In <a href="/wiki/2019" title="2019">2019</a>, Lily began her education at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> and was <a href="/wiki/Sorting_ceremony" title="Sorting ceremony">sorted</a> into <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor" title="Gryffindor">Gryffindor</a> <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Houses" title="Hogwarts Houses">House</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-CC_7-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-CC-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup> In <a href="/wiki/2020" title="2020">2020</a> she was seen on <a href="/wiki/Platform_Nine_and_Three-Quarters" title="Platform Nine and Three-Quarters">Platform 9¾</a> along with the rest of her family, sitting on her older brother's trolley.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Physical_appearance">Physical appearance</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Lily_L._Potter?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Physical appearance">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Lily_L._Potter?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Physical appearance"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Lily inherited the red hair of her mother's <a href="/wiki/Weasley_Family" class="mw-redirect" title="Weasley Family">family</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-DH-E_3-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH-E-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> and had inherited most of the Weasleys' physical traits, including her hair colour. Her characteristic trait was her cheery-looking smile, which was possibly inherited from <a href="/wiki/Lily_Potter" title="Lily Potter">Lily Potter</a>, her late grandmother.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Personality_and_traits">Personality and traits</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Lily_L._Potter?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Personality and traits">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Lily_L._Potter?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Personality and traits"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Lily seemed to have a personality similar to her mother. She was very jealous of her two older brothers when they were leaving for <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts</a>, quite similar to the eagerness and envy Ginny displayed when the last of her brothers went away to school in <a href="/wiki/1991" title="1991">1991</a>.
</p><p>Lily also seemed to enjoy talking to her family. She and her cousin Hugo, had an "animated discussion" about what house they would be in, suggesting they were very close.
</p>

<p>Lily was around the same age as <a href="/wiki/Hugo" title="Hugo">Hugo</a>, and excitedly discussed Hogwarts with him in 2017, suggesting they may be close. Lily was also very excited when she found out her cousin <a href="/wiki/Victoire_Weasley" title="Victoire Weasley">Victoire Weasley</a> was dating <a href="/wiki/Edward_Lupin" title="Edward Lupin">Teddy Lupin</a>.
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Teddy_Lupin">Teddy Lupin</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Lily_L._Potter?veaction=edit&amp;section=8" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Teddy Lupin">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Lily_L._Potter?action=edit&amp;section=8" title="Edit section: Teddy Lupin"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p><a href="/wiki/Edward_Lupin" title="Edward Lupin">Teddy Lupin</a> was Lily's father's godson. He often spent time at the Potters' home, and Lily was very excited at the news that he was dating her cousin Victoire. She hoped that they might marry so that Teddy would become an official member of the family. This is very similar to Ginny's longing to have <a href="/wiki/Nymphadora_Tonks" title="Nymphadora Tonks">Nymphadora Tonks</a> (Teddy's late mother) in her family, deepening the connection between Lily and her mother.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Lily_L._Potter?veaction=edit&amp;section=9" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Lily_L._Potter?action=edit&amp;section=9" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>The given name <i>Lily</i> comes from the name of the flower, which is a symbol of purity. Specifically, the lily is often associated with the Virgin Mary; one type of flower is known as the Madonna lily. Greek mythology also links lilies to the Muses and to the goddess Hera, and the flowers continue to be representative of purity and love in Europe. In Chinese, the lily is a play on words for "forever in love", and the flowers were thought to be capable of averting evil.</li>
<li><i>Luna</i> means "<a href="/wiki/Moon" title="Moon">Moon</a>" in Latin. It is also the name of the Ancient Roman goddess of the moon who was the mother of the Menae, fifty goddesses who embodied the phases of the moon used by the Ancient Greeks to keep time. In some myths, she was also the mother of the Nemean Lion.</li>
<li><i>Potter</i> is a very common surname of English origin. It traditionally refers to the occupation of making pottery. Beatrix Potter was a well-known English children's author. Potter's Field is the name for a burial ground for the unknown or unclaimed dead, particularly soldiers and orphans; the Potter family had many members that fit that description. The Potter Box is a model for making ethical decisions developed at Harvard University.</li>
<li>She was named after important people in her father and mother's life. Lily – after his mother and her paternal grandmother, <a href="/wiki/Lily_J._Potter" title="Lily J. Potter">Lily Evans</a>, who died to protect Harry from <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Voldemort</a> when he was just a baby. Luna – after <a href="/wiki/Luna_Lovegood" title="Luna Lovegood">Luna Lovegood</a>, Ginny's good friend at Hogwarts. Many people believe this name was from <a href="/wiki/Remus_Lupin" title="Remus Lupin">Remus Lupin</a>, but this was disproven by <a href="/wiki/J._K._Rowling" title="J. K. Rowling">J. K. Rowling</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup></li></ul>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Lily_L._Potter?veaction=edit&amp;section=10" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Lily_L._Potter?action=edit&amp;section=10" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li><a href="/wiki/Daphne_de_Beistegui" title="Daphne de Beistegui">Daphne de Beistegui</a> portrays Lily Potter in <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Deathly_Hallows:_Part_2" title="Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 2">Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 2</a></i>.</li>
<li>Lily is portrayed by British actresses Christiana Hutchings, Zoe Brough and Cristina Fray alternating the role in the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Cursed_Child_(play)#Original_West_End_production" title="Harry Potter and the Cursed Child (play)">original West End production</a> of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Cursed_Child" title="Harry Potter and the Cursed Child">Harry Potter and the Cursed Child</a></i>.<sup id="cite_ref-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-10" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-10">&#91;10&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li>Lily was the first of Harry's children to be introduced in the epilogue, where she was shown holding her father's hand, much as her <a href="/wiki/Ginevra_Weasley" title="Ginevra Weasley">mother</a> was introduced in <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Philosopher%27s_Stone" title="Harry Potter and the Philosopher&#39;s Stone">Philosopher's Stone</a></i>.</li>
<li>She is the only child of <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry</a> and Ginny who was named after a person still living at the end of the series: <a href="/wiki/Luna_Lovegood" title="Luna Lovegood">Luna</a> still lived while <a href="/wiki/Lily_J._Potter" title="Lily J. Potter">Lily</a>, <a href="/wiki/James_Potter_I" title="James Potter I">James</a>, <a href="/wiki/Sirius_Black" title="Sirius Black">Sirius</a>, <a href="/wiki/Albus_Dumbledore" title="Albus Dumbledore">Albus</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Severus_Snape" title="Severus Snape">Severus</a> all had died.</li>
<li>In the film version, Lily is seen standing with James, even sitting on top of his trunk when he was taking it into King's Cross.</li>
<li>In the novel, Lily protests about her desire to go to Hogwarts, despite not being able to go for another two years. This is very similar to the desire her mother, Ginny, expressed in <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Philosopher%27s_Stone" title="Harry Potter and the Philosopher&#39;s Stone">Philosopher's Stone</a>.</i></li>
<li>She is related to the <a href="/wiki/House_of_Black" title="House of Black">House of Black</a> because her maternal great-grandmother was <a href="/wiki/Cedrella_Black" title="Cedrella Black">Cedrella Black</a>. Her maternal great-grand aunt was <a href="/wiki/Lucretia_Black" title="Lucretia Black">Lucretia Black</a>. Some sort of relative on her paternal side was <a href="/wiki/Dorea_Black" title="Dorea Black">Dorea Black</a>.</li>
<li>She is also related to <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a> and the Gaunts through her paternal ancestor <a href="/wiki/Ignotus_Peverell" title="Ignotus Peverell">Ignotus Peverell</a>. Ignotus Peverell's brother was <a href="/wiki/Cadmus_Peverell" title="Cadmus Peverell">Cadmus Peverell</a> who is Voldemort's ancestor.</li></ul>
