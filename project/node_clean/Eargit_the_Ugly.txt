<b>Eargit the Ugly</b> (fl. <a href="/wiki/14th_century" title="14th century">14th century</a>) was the <a href="/wiki/Goblin" title="Goblin">goblin</a> representative at the <a href="/wiki/14th_century" title="14th century">14th century</a> summit of the <a href="/wiki/Wizards%27_Council" title="Wizards&#39; Council">Wizards' Council</a>. He was featured on a rare <a href="/wiki/Chocolate_Frog_Card" title="Chocolate Frog Card">Chocolate Frog Card</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
