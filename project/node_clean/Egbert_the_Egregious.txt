<b>Egbert the Egregious</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> who lived in the <a href="/wiki/Middle_Ages" title="Middle Ages">Middle Ages</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-TBBE_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-TBBE-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>He was once the Master of the <a href="/wiki/Elder_Wand" title="Elder Wand">Elder Wand</a> after killing <a href="/wiki/Emeric_the_Evil" title="Emeric the Evil">Emeric the Evil</a> in a <a href="/wiki/Duel_Between_Emeric_the_Evil_and_Egbert_the_Egregious" class="mw-redirect" title="Duel Between Emeric the Evil and Egbert the Egregious">ferocious duel</a> (Emeric was "slaughtered"). 
What became of Egbert after mastering the <a href="/wiki/Elder_Wand" title="Elder Wand">Elder Wand</a> is unknown, but Dumbledore suggests Egbert did not last long. Therefore, it is possible that another wizard killed him in order to gain ownership of the legendary <a href="/wiki/Wand" title="Wand">wand</a>. A century later, <a href="/wiki/Godelot" title="Godelot">Godelot</a> is recorded to have obtained mastery of the wand.<sup id="cite_ref-TBBE_2-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-TBBE-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Magical_abilities_and_skills">Magical abilities and skills</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Egbert_the_Egregious?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Egbert_the_Egregious?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li><b><a href="/wiki/Martial_magic" title="Martial magic">Martial magic</a></b>: Egbert was notable for being the only known wizard, apart from <a href="/wiki/Albus_Dumbledore" title="Albus Dumbledore">Albus Dumbledore</a>, to defeat the master of the <a href="/wiki/Elder_Wand" title="Elder Wand">Elder Wand</a> in a <a href="/wiki/Duel_between_Emeric_the_Evil_and_Egbert_the_Egregious" title="Duel between Emeric the Evil and Egbert the Egregious">duel</a>, managing to slaughter <a href="/wiki/Emeric_the_Evil" title="Emeric the Evil">Emeric the Evil</a> in a battle for the wand, showing his exceptional proficiency and power as a duellist. Despite this, Dumbledore suspects that he may have lost the Elder Wand in a duel himself.<sup id="cite_ref-TBBE_2-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-TBBE-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup></li></ul>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Egbert_the_Egregious?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Egbert_the_Egregious?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The name "Egbert" means "bright edge" from the Old English elements <i>ecg</i> "edge of a sword" and <i>beorht</i> "bright". This was the name of kings of Kent and Wessex as well as two English saints. The name was rarely used after the Norman conquest but was revived in the 19th century.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Egbert_the_Egregious?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Egbert_the_Egregious?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>Judging by his title <i>egregious</i> which is defined as outstandingly bad, Egbert was presumably a notorious <a href="/wiki/Dark_wizard" class="mw-redirect" title="Dark wizard">dark wizard</a> himself. His activities are unknown, other than his battle for possession of the <a href="/wiki/Elder_Wand" title="Elder Wand">Elder Wand</a>.</li></ul>
