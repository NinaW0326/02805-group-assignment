<p><b>Edward "Ted" Tonks</b><sup id="cite_ref-Pottermore_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Pottermore-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> (d. <a href="/wiki/March" title="March">March</a>, <a href="/wiki/1998" title="1998">1998</a>) was a <a href="/wiki/Muggle-born" title="Muggle-born">Muggle-born</a> <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a>, husband of <a href="/wiki/Andromeda_Tonks" title="Andromeda Tonks">Andromeda Tonks</a> <span class="template-c">(née <a href="/wiki/House_of_Black" title="House of Black">Black</a>)</span>, father of <a href="/wiki/Nymphadora_Tonks" title="Nymphadora Tonks">Nymphadora</a> and grandfather of <a href="/wiki/Edward_Lupin" title="Edward Lupin">Teddy Lupin</a>.
</p><p>When Ted married Andromeda, who was born into a prejudiced <a href="/wiki/Pure-blood" title="Pure-blood">pure-blood</a> family, her family singled her out for scorn. The couple had one child, Nymphadora, who became a member of the <a href="/wiki/Order_of_the_Phoenix" title="Order of the Phoenix">Order of the Phoenix</a> during the <a href="/wiki/Second_Wizarding_War" title="Second Wizarding War">Second Wizarding War</a>. Ted and Andromeda were not members of the Order but assisted them occasionally, allowing the Order to use their home as a safe-house during the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_the_Seven_Potters" title="Battle of the Seven Potters">Battle of the Seven Potters</a>.
</p><p>When <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a> succeeded in taking over the <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">Ministry of Magic</a> in <a href="/wiki/1997" title="1997">1997</a> and the <a href="/wiki/Muggle-Born_Registration_Commission" title="Muggle-Born Registration Commission">Muggle-Born Registration Commission</a> was established, Ted went on the run. He was eventually caught and murdered by a group of <a href="/wiki/Snatchers" title="Snatchers">Snatchers</a> in <a href="/wiki/March" title="March">March</a> of <a href="/wiki/1998" title="1998">1998</a>.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Edward_Tonks?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Edward_Tonks?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_life">Early life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Edward_Tonks?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Early life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Edward_Tonks?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Early life"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<div class="quote">
<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix">"<i>Andromeda married a Muggle-born, Ted Tonks, so —</i>"</span></dd>
<dd>&#8212;<a href="/wiki/Sirius_Black" title="Sirius Black">Sirius Black</a> regarding the Tonks family<sup class="noprint"><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Order_of_the_Phoenix" title="Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix">[src]</a></sup></dd>
<dd></dd></dl></div><figure class="thumb tright show-info-icon" style="width: 232px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/6/6d/Andromeda_and_Ted.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20130301161113" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/6/6d/Andromeda_and_Ted.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/230?cb=20130301161113" decoding="async" width="230" height="161" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="Andromeda and Ted.jpg" data-image-key="Andromeda_and_Ted.jpg" /></a> 	 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:Andromeda_and_Ted.jpg" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption">Ted and Andromeda on the Black family tree</p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure>
<p>Ted was born into the <a href="/wiki/Tonks_family" title="Tonks family">Tonks family</a> but, unlike his <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggle</a> relatives, was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a>. He married <a href="/wiki/Andromeda_Tonks" title="Andromeda Tonks">Andromeda Black</a>, who he affectionately called "Dromeda", in spite of her <a href="/wiki/House_of_Black" title="House of Black">family</a>'s belief in <a href="/wiki/Blood_status" title="Blood status">blood purity</a>. She was disowned and burned off the <a href="/wiki/House_of_Black#Family_Tree" title="House of Black">family tree</a> tapestry in the house of Black for not making a "<i>respectable pure-blood marriage</i>", unlike her sisters <a href="/wiki/Narcissa_Malfoy" title="Narcissa Malfoy">Narcissa</a> and <a href="/wiki/Bellatrix_Lestrange" title="Bellatrix Lestrange">Bellatrix</a>. Ted and his wife had one child, for whom Andromeda selected the name Nymphadora.<sup id="cite_ref-OTP_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OTP-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Ted and his daughter, whom he fondly called "Dora", lacked the talent for magical cleanliness that Andromeda had. He was aware of <a href="/wiki/Arthur_Weasley" title="Arthur Weasley">Arthur Weasley</a> and his fascination with <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggle</a> items.<sup id="cite_ref-DH_4-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span id="Second_Wizarding_War_(1995-1998)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="Second_Wizarding_War_.281995-1998.29">Second Wizarding War <span class="template-c">(1995-1998)</span></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Edward_Tonks?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Second Wizarding War (1995-1998)">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Edward_Tonks?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Second Wizarding War (1995-1998)"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<div class="quote">
<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows">"<i>Hagrid's fine, son. The wife's seeing to him now. How are you feeling? Anything else broken? I've fixed your ribs, your tooth, and your arm. I'm Ted, by the way, Ted Tonks —  Dora's father.</i>"</span></dd>
<dd>&#8212;Ted meets Harry Potter after the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_the_Seven_Potters" title="Battle of the Seven Potters">Battle of the Seven Potters</a><sup class="noprint"><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Deathly_Hallows" title="Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows">[src]</a></sup></dd>
<dd></dd></dl></div><p>When <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a> returned to power, Ted's daughter joined the second <a href="/wiki/Order_of_the_Phoenix" title="Order of the Phoenix">Order of the Phoenix</a> to fight against the growing threat.<sup id="cite_ref-OTP_3-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OTP-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> Neither Ted nor Andromeda were official members, but nonetheless supported their daughter and allowed their home to be used as a safe-house. In <a href="/wiki/1997" title="1997">1997</a>, Tonks married <a href="/wiki/Remus_Lupin" title="Remus Lupin">Remus Lupin</a>, who suspected that Ted and Andromeda disapproved because of his <a href="/wiki/Werewolf" title="Werewolf">werewolf</a> status.
</p><p>After the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_the_Seven_Potters" title="Battle of the Seven Potters">Battle of the Seven Potters</a>, <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> and <a href="/wiki/Rubeus_Hagrid" title="Rubeus Hagrid">Rubeus Hagrid</a> crashed in the garden of the Tonks home and were both injured. Ted brought the two inside, where he proceeded to fix Harry's broken ribs, tooth and arm. He and Andromeda were very worried for their daughter's safety when Harry informed them that <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a> ambushed the group. Ted attempted to calm Andromeda down by pointing out that Dora had faced worse during the course of her career as an <a href="/wiki/Auror" title="Auror">Auror</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-DH5_5-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH5-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> Sometime after Harry and Hagrid took the <a href="/wiki/Portkey" title="Portkey">Portkey</a> to <a href="/wiki/The_Burrow" title="The Burrow">the Burrow</a>, Ted sent the pieces of <a href="/wiki/Sirius_Black" title="Sirius Black">Sirius Black</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Sirius_Black%27s_motorcycle" class="mw-redirect" title="Sirius Black&#39;s motorcycle">flying motorbike</a> to Arthur Weasley, where they were kept in the Burrow's shed for the remainder of the war.
</p>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="On_the_run_and_death">On the run and death</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Edward_Tonks?veaction=edit&amp;section=4" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: On the run and death">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Edward_Tonks?action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: On the run and death"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<div class="quote">
<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows"><b>Dirk Cresswell</b>: "<i>You believe that story, then, do you, Ted? You believe <a href="/wiki/Severus_Snape" title="Severus Snape">Snape</a> killed <a href="/wiki/Albus_Dumbledore" title="Albus Dumbledore">Dumbledore</a>?</i>"</span></dd>
<dd><b>Ted</b>: "<i>Course I do. You're not going to sit there and tell me you think Potter had anything to do with it?</i>"</dd>
<dd>&#8212; A discussion of Dumbledore's death while on the run<sup class="noprint"><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Deathly_Hallows" title="Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows">[src]</a></sup></dd></dl>
</div><p>When the <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">Ministry of Magic</a> fell under <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a>'s control in August <a href="/wiki/1997" title="1997">1997</a>, all known safe-houses of the Order lost their protective enchantments and were raided by mixtures of Ministry employees and <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a>. Ted and Andromeda were <a href="/wiki/Cruciatus_Curse" title="Cruciatus Curse">tortured</a> for information about Harry, like many other families with connections to Order members; Remus later reported that they were "<i>shaken, obviously, but otherwise okay</i>". Some time after this, their daughter came to stay with them upon discovering that she was pregnant. Remus temporarily left her side, worried about passing on <a href="/wiki/Werewolf" title="Werewolf">lycanthropy</a> to their child.
</p><p>When the Ministry began its persecution of <a href="/wiki/Muggle-born" title="Muggle-born">Muggle-borns</a>, Ted, on a matter of principle, refused to register with the <a href="/wiki/Muggle-Born_Registration_Commission" title="Muggle-Born Registration Commission">Muggle-Born Registration Commission</a>. He was forced to leave to avoid Death Eaters who were operating near his home. Andromeda did not accompany him, as her <a href="/wiki/Pure-blood" title="Pure-blood">pure-blood</a> status kept her safe and she had to care for their pregnant daughter.
</p><p>Ted then went on the run, eventually meeting up with fellow fugitives <a href="/wiki/Dirk_Cresswell" title="Dirk Cresswell">Dirk Cresswell</a> and <a href="/wiki/Dean_Thomas" title="Dean Thomas">Dean Thomas</a>, as well as goblins <a href="/wiki/Griphook" title="Griphook">Griphook</a> and <a href="/wiki/Gornuk" title="Gornuk">Gornuk</a>, and they proceeded to stay on the move throughout the countryside. At one point, the group discussed <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> — unaware that Harry was listening in, along with <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ron Weasley</a> and <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione Granger</a>. Ted dismissed the <i><a href="/wiki/Daily_Prophet" title="Daily Prophet">Daily Prophet</a>'</i>s claims that Harry was involved in Albus Dumbledore's death and stated his belief that <i><a href="/wiki/The_Quibbler" title="The Quibbler">The Quibbler</a></i> was the only place left to get reliable news. Eventually, the group was caught by a gang of <a href="/wiki/Snatchers" title="Snatchers">Snatchers</a> led by <a href="/wiki/Fenrir_Greyback" title="Fenrir Greyback">Fenrir Greyback</a>. Ted, Dirk, and Gornuk were murdered resisting capture.<sup id="cite_ref-DH_4-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Post-mortem">Post-mortem</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Edward_Tonks?veaction=edit&amp;section=5" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Post-mortem">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Edward_Tonks?action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Post-mortem"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<div class="quote">
<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows">"<i>Let's take a moment to report those deaths that the </i><a href="/wiki/Wizarding_Wireless_Network_News" title="Wizarding Wireless Network News">Wizarding Wireless Network News</a><i> and </i>Daily Prophet<i> don't think important enough to mention. <dd>&#8212;<a href="/wiki/Lee_Jordan" title="Lee Jordan">Lee Jordan</a> announcing the deaths on <i>Potterwatch</i><sup class="noprint"><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Deathly_Hallows" title="Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows">[src]</a></sup></dd>
<dd></dd></dl></div><p>The death of Ted Tonks and his comrades was announced on <i><a href="/wiki/Potterwatch" title="Potterwatch">Potterwatch</a></i>, as the <i><a href="/wiki/Daily_Prophet" title="Daily Prophet">Daily Prophet</a></i> had ceased reporting deaths caused by Voldemort's regime. When Nymphadora gave birth to her son, she and Remus decided to name him <a href="/wiki/Edward_Lupin" title="Edward Lupin">Edward</a>, or "Teddy", in her father's memory.<sup id="cite_ref-DH_4-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p><h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Physical_appearance">Physical appearance</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Edward_Tonks?veaction=edit&amp;section=6" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Physical appearance">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Edward_Tonks?action=edit&amp;section=6" title="Edit section: Physical appearance"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Ted was a "<i>fair-haired, big-bellied</i>" man, with a mellow and pleasant voice.<sup id="cite_ref-desc_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-desc-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Magical_abilities_and_skills">Magical abilities and skills</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Edward_Tonks?veaction=edit&amp;section=7" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Edward_Tonks?action=edit&amp;section=7" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li><b><a href="/wiki/Healing_magic" title="Healing magic">Healing magic</a></b>: Ted was shown to be proficient in the <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magical</a> art of healing, as he was able to repair <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>'s broken tooth after the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_the_Seven_Potters" title="Battle of the Seven Potters">Battle of the Seven Potters</a>, showing he was skilful with the casting of <a href="/wiki/Healing_spell" title="Healing spell">Healing spells</a>.</li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Charm" title="Charm">Charms</a></b>: Ted was shown to be competent with charm-work, as shown when he swiftly and successfully used the <a href="/wiki/Summoning_Charm" title="Summoning Charm">Summoning Charm</a> to summon some <a href="/wiki/Salmon" title="Salmon">salmon</a> out of <a href="/wiki/Water" title="Water">water</a> to eat whilst living as an outlaw in the English countryside.</li></ul>

<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Family">Family</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Edward_Tonks?veaction=edit&amp;section=9" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Family">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Edward_Tonks?action=edit&amp;section=9" title="Edit section: Family"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Andromeda_Black">Andromeda Black</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Edward_Tonks?veaction=edit&amp;section=10" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Andromeda Black">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Edward_Tonks?action=edit&amp;section=10" title="Edit section: Andromeda Black"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<p>Andromeda most likely married Ted (a <a href="/wiki/Muggle-born" title="Muggle-born">Muggle-born</a>) in secret, before telling her family<sup id="cite_ref-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup>; she was disowned from her family and burned off the <a href="/wiki/House_of_Black#Black_family_tree" title="House of Black">Black Family Tree</a>. Considering that Andromeda gave up her entire family for Ted, they definitely had a strong and affectionate relationship. Ted called his wife by the nickname "Dromeda".
</p><p>In <a href="/wiki/1973" title="1973">1973</a>, the couple's only <a href="/wiki/Nymphadora_Tonks" title="Nymphadora Tonks">child</a> was born and Andromeda named her Nymphadora, but Ted regularly called her "Dora" a name that, unlike Nymphadora, she did not seem to mind.
</p><p>Andromeda was devastated when her husband was murdered by <a href="/wiki/Snatchers" title="Snatchers">Snatchers</a>.
</p>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Nymphadora_Tonks">Nymphadora Tonks</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Edward_Tonks?veaction=edit&amp;section=11" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Nymphadora Tonks">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Edward_Tonks?action=edit&amp;section=11" title="Edit section: Nymphadora Tonks"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>

<p>Ted and <a href="/wiki/Andromeda_Tonks" title="Andromeda Tonks">Andromeda</a>'s only daughter, who was born sometime in <a href="/wiki/1973" title="1973">1973</a>. Her name was Nymphadora, but she was nicknamed "Dora" by her father. Ted and his daughter most likely had a close relationship, considering Dora named her son after him. Both Ted and his daughter had a tendency to be untidy and disorganised. Tonks was likely devastated by her father's death.
</p>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Remus_Lupin">Remus Lupin</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Edward_Tonks?veaction=edit&amp;section=12" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Remus Lupin">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Edward_Tonks?action=edit&amp;section=12" title="Edit section: Remus Lupin"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>

<p>Remus married Ted's daughter, Nymphadora; neither Ted nor Andromeda attended <a href="/wiki/Wedding_of_Remus_Lupin_and_Nymphadora_Tonks" title="Wedding of Remus Lupin and Nymphadora Tonks">the wedding</a>, but only because they did not know about it<sup id="cite_ref-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup>. Remus's <a href="/wiki/Werewolf" title="Werewolf">werewolf</a> status was most likely never a problem for Ted, given the open-minded nature of his few recorded statements.
</p><p>However the opposite may also be true, as Remus had stated that his wife's parents were disgusted by their marriage when he asked Harry "<i>What parents would want their only daughter to marry a werewolf?</i>" However this is unknown if that was the genuine attitude of Ted as he never made such a statement. It could have been possible Remus merely imagined his future in-laws would disapprove of him, as his distressed mind was considering other scenarios as to why he should not get married, such as being an older man than Nymphadora.
</p>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Teddy_Lupin">Teddy Lupin</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Edward_Tonks?veaction=edit&amp;section=13" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Teddy Lupin">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Edward_Tonks?action=edit&amp;section=13" title="Edit section: Teddy Lupin"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<p><a href="/wiki/Edward_Lupin" title="Edward Lupin">Teddy</a> is Ted's grandson, son of his daughter. Ted was killed before his grandson was born, but was probably aware of the pregnancy. Teddy's parents named him after his grandfather.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Edward_Tonks?veaction=edit&amp;section=14" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Edward_Tonks?action=edit&amp;section=14" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p><a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Edward" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Edward">Edward</a> is derived from the Old English words <i>ead</i>, meaning "blessed", and <i>weard</i>, meaning "guard", and has been the name of many members of the English nobility and royalty.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Edward_Tonks?veaction=edit&amp;section=15" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Edward_Tonks?action=edit&amp;section=15" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>Ted Tonks is rumoured to be the newsreader <a href="/wiki/Ted" title="Ted">Ted</a> who is mentioned in the first chapter of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Philosopher%27s_Stone" title="Harry Potter and the Philosopher&#39;s Stone">Harry Potter and the Philosopher's Stone</a></i>.</li>
<li>Ted Tonks and his wife Andromeda were both cut from the <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Deathly_Hallows:_Part_1" title="Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 1">Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 1</a></i>, meaning that Harry and Hagrid escape to <a href="/wiki/The_Burrow" title="The Burrow">the Burrow</a> rather than the <a href="/wiki/Tonks_house" title="Tonks house">Tonks house</a>. The trio also do not encounter Ted nor any other members of his group while on the run. However, he, Dirk Cresswell, and Gornuk are mentioned on radio as having been killed.</li>
<li>It is possible that Ted Tonks was a <a href="/wiki/Metamorphmagus" title="Metamorphmagus">Metamorphmagus</a>, a magical trait he could have passed onto his daughter.</li></ul>
