<p><b>Willy Widdershins</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Great_Britain" title="Great Britain">British</a> <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> who repeatedly engaged in <a href="/wiki/Muggle-baiting" title="Muggle-baiting">Muggle-baiting</a> pranks. He was arrested by the <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">Ministry of Magic</a> for enchanting a series of <a href="/wiki/Regurgitating_Toilet" title="Regurgitating Toilet">regurgitating toilets</a> in <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggle</a> areas, but he made a deal with the Ministry, that if he spied on <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> and his friends, they would let him go. He carried out his assignment, and the Ministry kept their end of the bargain and released him. He was later caught selling <a href="/wiki/Biting_doorknob" title="Biting doorknob">biting doorknobs</a> and was subsequently given a prison sentence that he could not avoid.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Willy_Widdershins?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Willy_Widdershins?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Willy Widdershins eventually discovered he was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> and procured a <a href="/wiki/Wand" title="Wand">wand</a>.
</p><p>In adulthood, he repeatedly engaged in <a href="/wiki/Muggle-baiting" title="Muggle-baiting">Muggle-baiting</a> pranks. He was arrested by the <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">British Ministry of Magic</a> for enchanting a series of <a href="/wiki/Regurgitating_Toilet" title="Regurgitating Toilet">regurgitating toilets</a> in <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggle</a> areas such as <a href="/wiki/Bethnal_Green" title="Bethnal Green">Bethnal Green</a>, one of which backfired against him during its creation. He was badly injured in his capture and was wrapped heavily in bandages.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP22_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP22-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> He made a deal with the Ministry, that if he spied on <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> and his friends, they would let him go. He did so, and reported to <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_High_Inquisitor" title="Hogwarts High Inquisitor">Hogwarts High Inquisitor</a> <a href="/wiki/Dolores_Umbridge" title="Dolores Umbridge">Dolores Umbridge</a> and <a href="/wiki/Cornelius_Fudge" title="Cornelius Fudge">Cornelius Fudge</a> that they were planning on making a <a href="/wiki/Dumbledore%27s_Army" title="Dumbledore&#39;s Army">secret Defence Against the Dark Arts group</a> while they were in a meeting at the <a href="/wiki/Hog%27s_Head" class="mw-redirect" title="Hog&#39;s Head">Hog's Head</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP16_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP16-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> By doing so he was able to avoid prosecution.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP27_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP27-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Widdershins was later caught selling <a href="/wiki/Biting_doorknob" title="Biting doorknob">biting doorknobs</a>, which bit off the fingers of two <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggles</a>, and was subsequently given a prison sentence that he could not avoid.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP22_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP22-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Willy_Widdershins?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Willy_Widdershins?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p><i>Widdershins</i> (sometimes <i>withershins</i>, <i>widershins</i> or <i>widderschynnes</i>) means to take a course opposite the apparent motion of the sun (in the Northern hemisphere), going anti-clockwise, lefthandwise, or to circle an object, by always keeping it on the left.<sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
