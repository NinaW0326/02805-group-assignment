<b>Susie Sooth</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Great_Britain" title="Great Britain">British</a> <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch</a> and <a href="/wiki/Seer" title="Seer">Seer</a> who did readings for a living. She was certified by the <a href="/wiki/Divinus_Haruspex_Association_of_Clairvoyants" title="Divinus Haruspex Association of Clairvoyants">Divinus Haruspex Association of Clairvoyants</a>. An advertisement promoting her services appeared inside the <a href="/wiki/Knight_Bus" title="Knight Bus">Knight Bus</a> in <a href="/wiki/1993" title="1993">1993</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-image1_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-image1-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Susie_Sooth?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Susie_Sooth?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>The name of this character was presumably intended as a combination of the word <i>soothsayer</i>, meaning "one who predicts the future,"<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> and the name of <a href="/wiki/England" title="England">English</a> musician <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Siouxsie_Sioux" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Siouxsie Sioux">Siouxsie Sioux</a> (pronounced "SOO-zee soo"), who is best known as the lead singer of the band <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Siouxsie_and_the_Banshees" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Siouxsie and the Banshees">Siouxsie and the Banshees</a>.</li></ul>
