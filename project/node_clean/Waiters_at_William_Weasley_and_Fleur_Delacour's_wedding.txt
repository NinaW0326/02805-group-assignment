A host of <b>waiters</b> served food at the <a href="/wiki/Wedding_of_William_Weasley_and_Fleur_Delacour" title="Wedding of William Weasley and Fleur Delacour">wedding</a> of <a href="/wiki/William_Weasley" title="William Weasley">Bill Weasley</a> and <a href="/wiki/Fleur_Delacour" title="Fleur Delacour">Fleur Delacour</a> on <a href="/wiki/1_August" title="1 August">1 August</a>, <a href="/wiki/1997" title="1997">1997</a> at <a href="/wiki/The_Burrow" title="The Burrow">the Burrow</a>.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="History">History</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Waiters_at_William_Weasley_and_Fleur_Delacour%27s_wedding?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: History">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Waiters_at_William_Weasley_and_Fleur_Delacour%27s_wedding?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: History"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>They wore white robes and arrived at two o'clock; while guests were arriving, they sat with the <a href="/wiki/Band_at_Bill_Weasley_and_Fleur_Delacour%27s_wedding" class="mw-redirect" title="Band at Bill Weasley and Fleur Delacour&#39;s wedding">band</a> under a tree close to the main tent, smoking pipes. After the vows were completed and the reception began, the waiters filled the tent and carried silver trays of <a href="/wiki/Pumpkin_Juice" title="Pumpkin Juice">Pumpkin juice</a>, <a href="/wiki/Butterbeer" title="Butterbeer">Butterbeer</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Firewhisky" title="Firewhisky">Firewhisky</a>, others tottering piles of tarts and sandwiches.<sup id="cite_ref-DH8_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH8-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>When the reception was <a href="/wiki/Attack_at_the_Burrow" title="Attack at the Burrow">attacked</a> by <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a> that evening following the <a href="/wiki/Fall_of_the_Ministry_of_Magic" title="Fall of the Ministry of Magic">fall</a> of the <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">Ministry of Magic</a>, the waiters presumably fled, along with most of the guests.<sup id="cite_ref-DH9_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH9-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
