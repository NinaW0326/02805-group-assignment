<p><b>Euphemia Rowle</b> was the <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch</a> who raised her following the end of the <a href="/wiki/Second_Wizarding_War" title="Second Wizarding War">Second Wizarding War</a>. According to <a href="/wiki/Delphini" title="Delphini">Delphini</a>, she did not care much for her ward, only taking her in for the gold. Rowle kept an <a href="/wiki/Augurey" title="Augurey">augurey</a> in a cage, and said that the creature kept crying because it could see Delphini was going to come to a "sticky end."<sup id="cite_ref-Act3Scene16_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Act3Scene16-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Euphemia_Rowle?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Euphemia_Rowle?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>Delphini mentions Euphemia Rowle only took her in for the gold. While it is unclear who would pay Rowle to raise her, it may have been <a href="/wiki/Rodolphus_Lestrange" title="Rodolphus Lestrange">Rodolphus Lestrange</a>, Delphini's stepfather, who is known to have contacted her at least on one occasion.<sup id="cite_ref-Act4Scene11_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Act4Scene11-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li>Euphemia Rowle shares the same first name with <a href="/wiki/Euphemia_Potter" title="Euphemia Potter">Euphemia Potter</a>, Harry Potter's grandmother.</li></ul>
