<div class="mw-parser-output"><p><b>Flurry</b> was an imaginary friend that <a href="/wiki/Scorpius_Malfoy" title="Scorpius Malfoy">Scorpius Malfoy</a> invented to keep himself company as a child.<sup id="cite_ref-act2scene14_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-act2scene14-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> His friendship with Flurry reportedly ended when they had a disagreement over the correct rules of <a href="/wiki/Gobstones" title="Gobstones">Gobstones</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-act2scene14_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-act2scene14-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> Scorpius recounted this story to <a href="/wiki/Delphini" title="Delphini">Delphi</a> many years later when she discussed her own lonely childhood.<sup id="cite_ref-act2scene14_1-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-act2scene14-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
