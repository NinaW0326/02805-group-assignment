<p><b>Buckley Cooper</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> who wrote to the <i><a href="/wiki/Daily_Prophet" title="Daily Prophet">Daily Prophet</a></i> advice page.<sup id="cite_ref-DP_1-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DP-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> He had a long-running feud with his <a href="/wiki/Buckley_Cooper%27s_brother" title="Buckley Cooper&#39;s brother">brother</a> and wanted to know if the <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">Ministry of Magic</a> would ignore it if he got revenge for the <a href="/wiki/Curse" title="Curse">cursed</a> <a href="/wiki/Broomstick" title="Broomstick">broomstick</a> he had been sent by abducting his brother's children and turning them into <a href="/wiki/Vampire" title="Vampire">vampire</a> <a href="/wiki/Ferret" title="Ferret">ferrets</a>. In his reply, <a href="/wiki/Dempster_Wiggleswade" title="Dempster Wiggleswade">Dempster Wiggleswade</a> warned Cooper not to go through with these actions.<sup id="cite_ref-DP_1-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DP-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
