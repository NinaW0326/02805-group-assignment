<p><b>Myron Wagtail</b> (b. <a href="/wiki/1970" title="1970">1970</a>) was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> and the lead singer of the <a href="/wiki/Wizarding_world" title="Wizarding world">wizarding world</a>'s popular band <a href="/wiki/Weird_Sisters" title="Weird Sisters">The Weird Sisters</a>.

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Myron_Wagtail?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Myron_Wagtail?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Wagtail was born some time in the year <a href="/wiki/1970" title="1970">1970</a>, in <a href="/wiki/Great_Britain" title="Great Britain">Great Britain</a> or <a href="/wiki/Ireland" title="Ireland">Ireland</a>. Attending <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> from the year <a href="/wiki/1981" title="1981">1981</a> or <a href="/wiki/1982" title="1982">1982</a>, he was a self-proclaimed fan of <a href="/wiki/Jacob%27s_sibling" title="Jacob&#39;s sibling">Jacob's Sibling</a> and invited them to perform with his band without the knowledge of the Hogwarts staff. The band and Jacob's sibling used <a href="/wiki/Ismelda_Murk" title="Ismelda Murk">Ismelda Murk</a>, <a href="/wiki/Ben_Copper" title="Ben Copper">Ben Copper</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Severus_Snape" title="Severus Snape">Severus Snape</a> as inspiration for their concert. At this time in the 1980s, the band consisted of Wagtail, <a href="/wiki/Kirley_Duke" title="Kirley Duke">Kirley Duke</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Orsino_Thruston" title="Orsino Thruston">Orsino Thruston</a>. Myron and <a href="/wiki/Rowan_Khanna" title="Rowan Khanna">Rowan Khanna</a> shared an interest in postmodern existentialist fiction.<sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Wagtail, along with his band, performed at the <a href="/wiki/Yule_Ball" title="Yule Ball">Yule Ball</a> of <a href="/wiki/1994" title="1994">1994</a> held at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts</a> in honour of the <a href="/wiki/Triwizard_Tournament" title="Triwizard Tournament">Triwizard Tournament</a>, by which time the band had at least eight members.<sup id="cite_ref-GOF23_4-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF23-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Magical_abilities_and_skills">Magical abilities and skills</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Myron_Wagtail?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Myron_Wagtail?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li><b><a href="/wiki/Charm" title="Charm">Charms</a></b>: Wagtail appeared to be highly proficient with charm-work. He was able to use the <a href="/wiki/Music_spell" title="Music spell">Music spell</a> to enchant instruments to play on their own accord, and could use the <a href="/wiki/Amplifying_Charm" title="Amplifying Charm">Amplifying Charm</a> to amplify the sound of his voice whilst performing. He was even capable of conjuring a non-corporeal <a href="/wiki/Patronus_Charm" title="Patronus Charm">Patronus</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-LEGOHP_2-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-LEGOHP-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> a clear mark of superior <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magical</a> skill and talent given the immense difficulty of casting such a powerful protective charm.</li>
<li><b>Singing</b>: Being the lead vocalist for the very popular wizarding band the <a href="/wiki/Weird_Sisters" title="Weird Sisters">Weird Sisters</a>, Wagtail was a very talented and capable singer and live music performer.</li></ul>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Myron_Wagtail?veaction=edit&amp;section=4" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Myron_Wagtail?action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The name "Myron" is derived from Greek μυρον (myron) meaning "sweet oil, perfume". Myron was the name of a 5th-century BC Greek sculptor.
</p>
