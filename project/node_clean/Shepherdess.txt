<p>The <i><b>Shepherdess</b></i> was a portrait hung at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Castle" title="Hogwarts Castle">Hogwarts Castle</a>, on the <a href="/wiki/Staircase_between_the_ground-floor_corridor_and_second-floor_corridor" title="Staircase between the ground-floor corridor and second-floor corridor">Charms Staircase</a>. She was depicted in a white dress, with her staff and with her flock behind her, in the background. She spoke in a strong West country accent.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTPG_2-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTPG-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Shepherdess?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Shepherdess?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>The Shepherdess is quite similar to a <a href="/wiki/Girl_with_Flowers" title="Girl with Flowers">portrait of a girl with flowers</a> featured in the film adaptations of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Philosopher%27s_Stone_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Philosopher&#39;s Stone (film)">Philosopher's Stone</a></i>, <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Chamber_of_Secrets_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets (film)">Chamber of Secrets</a></i> and <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Prisoner_of_Azkaban_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban (film)">Prisoner of Azkaban</a></i> (portrayed by <a href="/wiki/Violet_Columbus" title="Violet Columbus">Violet Columbus</a>).</li>
<li>This individual may have died sometime prior to <a href="/wiki/1995" title="1995">1995</a>, as many portraits hung at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Castle" title="Hogwarts Castle">Hogwarts Castle</a> depict deceased individuals.</li>
<li>She is voiced by <a href="/wiki/Eleanor_Hans" title="Eleanor Hans">Eleanor Hans</a>.</li></ul>
