<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_five_Muggles?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_five_Muggles?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>They were murdered by <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a> or their supporters in early <a href="/wiki/1998" title="1998">1998</a>, presumably for amusement or sport. <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggle</a> authorities wrongly attributed the deaths to a gas leak. Their deaths were reported on <i><a href="/wiki/Potterwatch" title="Potterwatch">Potterwatch</a></i> in March of <a href="/wiki/1998" title="1998">1998</a>, with <a href="/wiki/Order_of_the_Phoenix" title="Order of the Phoenix">Order of the Phoenix</a> members informing the broadcaster <a href="/wiki/Lee_Jordan" title="Lee Jordan">Lee Jordan</a> their deaths were in fact caused by the <a href="/wiki/Killing_Curse" title="Killing Curse">Killing Curse</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-DH22_1-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH22-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
