<p><b>Griphook</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Goblin" title="Goblin">goblin</a> who worked at <a href="/wiki/Gringotts_Wizarding_Bank" title="Gringotts Wizarding Bank">Gringotts Wizarding Bank</a> in <a href="/wiki/Diagon_Alley" title="Diagon Alley">Diagon Alley</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-PS5_2-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PS5-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> He would later go on the run during the height of the <a href="/wiki/Second_Wizarding_War" title="Second Wizarding War">Second Wizarding War</a> following the fall of the Ministry of Magic and the raise of <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a>'s new regime.<sup id="cite_ref-DH15_5-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH15-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> He would later assist <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> in his break-in of Gringotts in order to obtain <a href="/wiki/Helga_Hufflepuff%27s_Cup" class="mw-redirect" title="Helga Hufflepuff&#39;s Cup">Helga Hufflepuff's Cup</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-DH26_6-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH26-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>However, despite being rescued by them, Griphook's general mistrust of wizardkind resulted in him betraying Harry Potter, <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ron Weasley</a> and <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione Granger</a> when he believed that they would not hand him the <a href="/wiki/Sword_of_Gryffindor" title="Sword of Gryffindor">Sword of Gryffindor</a> after destroying the Cup and other <a href="/wiki/Horcrux" title="Horcrux">Horcruxes</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-DH26_6-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH26-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup> This eventually resulted in his own downfall when the Goblins reported the Cup's theft to Voldemort, who subsequently slaughtered all of them, including Griphook, in a fit of rage.<sup id="cite_ref-DHF2_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DHF2-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Work_for_Gringotts">Work for Gringotts</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Griphook?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Work for Gringotts">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Griphook?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Work for Gringotts"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<div class="quote">
<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;Harry Potter and the Philosopher&#39;s Stone"><b>Griphook</b>: "<i>Stand back. If anyone but a Gringotts goblin tried that, they’d be sucked through the door and trapped in there.</i>"</span></dd>
<dd><b>Harry Potter</b>: "<i>How often do you check to see if anyone’s inside?</i>"</dd>
<dd><b>Griphook</b>: "<i>About once every ten years.</i>"</dd>
<dd>&#8212; Griphook to Harry Potter in 1991<sup class="noprint"><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Philosopher%27s_Stone" title="Harry Potter and the Philosopher&#39;s Stone">[src]</a></sup></dd></dl>
</div><p>Griphook worked for <a href="/wiki/Gringotts_Wizarding_Bank" title="Gringotts Wizarding Bank">Gringotts Wizarding Bank</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-PS5_2-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PS5-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>In <a href="/wiki/1990" title="1990">1990</a>, Griphook tasked his subordinate, the <a href="/wiki/Curse-Breaker" title="Curse-Breaker">Curse-Breaker</a> <a href="/wiki/William_Weasley" title="William Weasley">Bill Weasley</a> with retrieving a <a href="/wiki/Gold" title="Gold">golden</a> <a href="/wiki/Dragon_egg" title="Dragon egg">dragon egg</a> forged by the infamous <a href="/wiki/Goblin" title="Goblin">goblin</a> blacksmith <a href="/wiki/Urgruff_the_Unwary" title="Urgruff the Unwary">Urgruff the Unwary</a>, as it was residing with a mother dragon who had purloined it from him after she mistook it for one of her own eggs. After the Curse-Breaker managed to successfully procure it with help from <a href="/wiki/Charles_Weasley" title="Charles Weasley">his brother</a> and friends, Griphook requested that they regale him with the story of their adventure, and, after learning that one of Bill's accomplices, <a href="/wiki/Jacob%27s_sibling" title="Jacob&#39;s sibling">Jacob's sibling</a>, had single-handedly subdued a <a href="/wiki/Hungarian_Horntail" title="Hungarian Horntail">Hungarian Horntail</a>, proposed that they join Gringotts as a Curse-Breaker after graduating from Hogwarts.<sup id="cite_ref-HM6_7-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HM6-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>In <a href="/wiki/1991" title="1991">1991</a>, he took <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> and <a href="/wiki/Rubeus_Hagrid" title="Rubeus Hagrid">Rubeus Hagrid</a> to vaults <a href="/wiki/Vault_687" title="Vault 687">687</a> and <a href="/wiki/Vault_713" title="Vault 713">713</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-PS5_2-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PS5-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Second_Wizarding_War">Second Wizarding War</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Griphook?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Second Wizarding War">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Griphook?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Second Wizarding War"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>In <a href="/wiki/1997" title="1997">1997</a>, Griphook went on the run after the fall of the <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">Ministry of Magic</a> to <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a>, because the Ministry of Magic placed wizards in control of the goblins and he refused to acknowledge any <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> as his master.<sup id="cite_ref-DH15_5-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH15-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> However, he had a last laugh when he was assigned to put <a href="/wiki/Godric_Gryffindor%27s_Sword" class="mw-redirect" title="Godric Gryffindor&#39;s Sword">Godric Gryffindor's Sword</a> in <a href="/wiki/Bellatrix_Lestrange" title="Bellatrix Lestrange">Bellatrix Lestrange</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Lestrange_Vault" title="Lestrange Vault">vault</a>. He noticed that the sword was a fake, but decided not to reveal that information to Bellatrix, as not to help the Death Eaters.<sup id="cite_ref-DH23_8-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH23-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="On_the_run">On the run</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Griphook?veaction=edit&amp;section=4" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: On the run">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Griphook?action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: On the run"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<figure class="thumb tleft show-info-icon" style="width: 238px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/5/51/Dean_griphook_3.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20170914212633" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/5/51/Dean_griphook_3.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/236?cb=20170914212633" decoding="async" width="236" height="133" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="Dean griphook 3.jpg" data-image-key="Dean_griphook_3.jpg" /></a> 	 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:Dean_griphook_3.jpg" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption"><a href="/wiki/Dean_Thomas" title="Dean Thomas">Dean Thomas</a> and Griphook on the run from Snatchers</p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure>
<p>Griphook soon met up with <a href="/wiki/Dirk_Cresswell" title="Dirk Cresswell">Dirk Cresswell</a> and <a href="/wiki/Gornuk" title="Gornuk">Gornuk</a>, and finally <a href="/wiki/Edward_Tonks" title="Edward Tonks">Ted Tonks</a> and <a href="/wiki/Dean_Thomas" title="Dean Thomas">Dean Thomas</a>, and they spent several months on the run. The group was then eventually caught by a gang of <a href="/wiki/Snatchers" title="Snatchers">Snatchers</a> led by the dangerously fierce werewolf, <a href="/wiki/Fenrir_Greyback" title="Fenrir Greyback">Fenrir Greyback</a>. Ted, Dirk and Gornuk were killed, but Griphook and Dean were taken prisoner.<sup id="cite_ref-DH23_8-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH23-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Shortly after, Greyback's team was alerted to the true location of <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>, <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione Granger</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ron Weasley</a> after they broke the <a href="/wiki/Taboo" title="Taboo">Taboo</a> on Lord Voldemort's name. Griphook, together with the others captured, was taken to <a href="/wiki/Malfoy_Manor" title="Malfoy Manor">Malfoy Manor</a> and then imprisoned in the cellar. However, the trio were in possession of the real <a href="/wiki/Sword_of_Gryffindor" title="Sword of Gryffindor">Sword of Gryffindor</a>. Bellatrix, believing that the sword was taken from her vault, tortured Hermione with the <a href="/wiki/Cruciatus_Curse" title="Cruciatus Curse">Cruciatus Curse</a> to find out how the trio came into possession of it. She came up with a lie that it was a fake, and Harry then persuaded Griphook to go along with her story.<sup id="cite_ref-DH23_8-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH23-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<figure class="thumb tright show-info-icon" style="width: 246px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/f/f5/Goblin_Griphook_with_Ron_and_Hermione_in_the_bckgrnd_02.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20091206014000" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/f/f5/Goblin_Griphook_with_Ron_and_Hermione_in_the_bckgrnd_02.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/244?cb=20091206014000" decoding="async" width="244" height="116" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="Goblin Griphook with Ron and Hermione in the bckgrnd 02.jpg" data-image-key="Goblin_Griphook_with_Ron_and_Hermione_in_the_bckgrnd_02.jpg" /></a> 	 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:Goblin_Griphook_with_Ron_and_Hermione_in_the_bckgrnd_02.jpg" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption">Griphook talking with Harry, Ron, and Hermione at Shell Cottage whilst resting</p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure>
<p>The <a href="/wiki/House-elf" title="House-elf">house-elf</a> <a href="/wiki/Dobby" title="Dobby">Dobby</a> rescued all the prisoners from the Manor, and they took refuge at <a href="/wiki/Shell_Cottage" title="Shell Cottage">Shell Cottage</a>. Griphook's legs were injured during the escape, thus <a href="/wiki/Fleur_Delacour" title="Fleur Delacour">Fleur Delacour</a> administered <a href="/wiki/Skele-Gro" title="Skele-Gro">Skele-Gro</a> to heal him. He watched from the bedroom window as Harry buried Dobby's body, surprised by the great respect Harry paid Dobby and by Hermione Granger's later revelation that she wanted house-elves to be freed, as few wizards treat house-elves and other non-human beings with respect.<sup id="cite_ref-DH24_3-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH24-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

Because of his favourable impression of Harry, and Hermione pointing out that all magical beings were united in resisting Voldemort, Griphook agreed to help them — in exchange for the <a href="/wiki/Sword_of_Gryffindor" title="Sword of Gryffindor">Sword of Gryffindor</a>. Griphook considered it <a href="/wiki/Goblin" title="Goblin">goblin</a> property, claiming that <a href="/wiki/Godric_Gryffindor" title="Godric Gryffindor">Godric Gryffindor</a> had stolen it from <a href="/wiki/Ragnuk" title="Ragnuk">Ragnuk</a>, the maker of the sword. <p>When <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a> found out about the break-in, he summoned several goblins to <a href="/wiki/Malfoy_Manor" title="Malfoy Manor">Malfoy Manor</a>, including Griphook, who arrived clutching the Sword of Gryffindor. <h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Personality_and_traits">Personality and traits</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Griphook?veaction=edit&amp;section=7" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Personality and traits">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Griphook?action=edit&amp;section=7" title="Edit section: Personality and traits"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<div class="quote">
<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows">"<i>The longer they spent time together, the more Harry realised that he did not much like the goblin. Griphook was unexpectedly bloodthirsty, laughed at the idea of pain in lesser creatures, and seemed to relish the possibility that they might have to hurt other wizards to reach the <a href="/wiki/Lestrange_Vault" title="Lestrange Vault">Lestranges' vault</a>.</i>"</span></dd>
<dd>&#8212;Griphook personality, as described by Harry<sup class="noprint"><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Deathly_Hallows" title="Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows">[src]</a></sup></dd>
<dd></dd></dl></div><p>Being a goblin, Griphook severely distrusted <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizardkind</a> and hated wizarding arrogance, stemming from the fact that goblins had been treated brutally by wizards in the past. Griphook had a somewhat barbaric and savage personality as he enjoyed the idea of pain in lesser creatures,<sup id="cite_ref-DH25_9-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH25-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup> was eager to harm wizards and showed pleasure at the idea of checking once every ten years if anyone was trapped inside <a href="/wiki/Vault_713" title="Vault 713">Vault 713</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-PS5_2-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PS5-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> Like most goblins, Griphook also believed that any goblin-made artefacts should be returned to goblins once the original purchaser had died. Due to this, he stated that goblins were the only true owners of such artefacts and that any non-goblin purchasing or possessing them was little more than renting or thieving rather than owning them.<sup id="cite_ref-DH25_9-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH25-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Because of the prejudice Griphook had witnessed, he was amazed by the respect Harry Potter had shown to creatures such as elves and goblins. This was most apparent when Griphook observed that Harry buried Dobby, a house-elf, after he was killed by Bellatrix Lestrange, in a grave dug by hand.<sup id="cite_ref-DH24_3-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH24-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> Despite this, Griphook was unable to fully overcome his distrust and hatred for wizardkind, which resulted in him betraying Harry Potter, Ron Weasley and Hermione Granger when they intended to use the Sword of Gryffindor to destroy <a href="/wiki/Helga_Hufflepuff%27s_Cup" class="mw-redirect" title="Helga Hufflepuff&#39;s Cup">Helga Hufflepuff's Cup</a> and the other Horcruxes.<sup id="cite_ref-DH26_6-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH26-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup> This betrayal ultimately proved to be Griphook's own undoing, as he was murdered by Voldemort alongside the other Goblins who reported the Cup's theft to him and Griphook's death caused the Sword to vanish back into the Sorting Hat.<sup id="cite_ref-DHF2_1-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DHF2-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
