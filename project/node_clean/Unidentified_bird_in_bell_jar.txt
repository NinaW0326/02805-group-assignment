<div class="quote">
<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix">"<i>Drifting along in the sparkling current inside was a tiny, jewel-bright egg. As it rose in the jar it cracked open and a <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hummingbird" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Hummingbird">hummingbird</a> emerged, which was carried to the very top of the jar, but as it fell on the draft, its feathers became bedraggled and damp again, and by the time it had been borne back to the bottom of the jar, it had been enclosed once more in its egg.</i>"</span></dd>
<dd>&#8212;The bird in the bell jar<sup class="noprint"><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Order_of_the_Phoenix" title="Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix">[src]</a></sup></dd>
<dd></dd></dl></div>
<p>This <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hummingbird" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Hummingbird">hummingbird</a> was used in <a href="/wiki/Department_of_Mysteries" title="Department of Mysteries">Department of Mysteries</a> to research <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Life" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Life">life</a> and <a href="/wiki/Death" title="Death">death</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Time_Room" title="Time Room">Time Room</a>. It lived in a <a href="/wiki/Bell_jar" title="Bell jar">bell jar</a>, which allowed it to become an <a href="/wiki/Egg" title="Egg">egg</a>, a <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hatchling" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Hatchling">chick</a>, an adult, and then start life over again.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP34_1-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP34-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> During the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_the_Department_of_Mysteries" title="Battle of the Department of Mysteries">Battle of the Department of Mysteries</a>, the bird was released and an unidentified <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eater</a> replaced it.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP35_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP35-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>In <a href="/wiki/September" title="September">September</a> <a href="/wiki/2020" title="2020">2020</a>, during the <a href="/wiki/Calamity" title="Calamity">Calamity</a>, this hummingbird appeared as a <a href="/wiki/Foundable" title="Foundable">Foundable</a>. Volunteer members of the <a href="/wiki/Statute_of_Secrecy_Task_Force" title="Statute of Secrecy Task Force">Statute of Secrecy Task Force</a> were able to return to his original time.<sup id="cite_ref-WU_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-WU-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
