<p>An <b>Acromantula</b> was a giant <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magical</a> species of <a href="/wiki/Spider" title="Spider">spider</a>, native to the rainforests of <a href="/wiki/Far_East" title="Far East">Southeast Asia</a>, particularly <a href="/wiki/Borneo" title="Borneo">Borneo</a> where it inhabited dense jungles. Acromantulas were believed to be a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a>-bred species, designed to guard dwellings or treasure hoards, and were first created before the <a href="/wiki/Ban_on_Experimental_Breeding" title="Ban on Experimental Breeding">Ban on Experimental Breeding</a> in <a href="/wiki/1965" title="1965">1965</a>. These giant spiders with a taste for <a href="/wiki/Human" title="Human">human</a> <a href="/wiki/Flesh" title="Flesh">flesh</a> were first spotted in <a href="/wiki/1794" class="mw-redirect" title="1794">1794</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-FB_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-FB-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Physical_description">Physical description</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Acromantula?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Physical description">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Acromantula?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Physical description"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<figure class="thumb tleft show-info-icon" style="width: 222px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/d/db/Acromantula_PS.gif/revision/latest?cb=20180608001711" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/d/db/Acromantula_PS.gif/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/220?cb=20180608001711" decoding="async" width="220" height="93" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="Acromantula PS.gif" data-image-key="Acromantula_PS.gif" /></a> 	 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:Acromantula_PS.gif" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption">Acromantula colony in the Forbidden Forest</p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure>
<p>Acromantulas sported eight, black eyes (white if blind) and were typically covered in thick black hair, with a leg span that can reach up to fifteen feet.<sup id="cite_ref-FB_1-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-FB-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> On average, they were the size of a carthorse, though old specimens such as <a href="/wiki/Aragog" title="Aragog">Aragog</a> may grow to 15 feet in leg span.<sup id="cite_ref-COS15_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COS15-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>They possessed a set of giant pedipalps which they used to eat live prey or their own dead kin. Their pincers produced a clicking sound when they were agitated or excited.<sup id="cite_ref-FB_1-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-FB-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> They also secreted poison when excited.<sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> They are sentient, and capable of <a href="/wiki/Human" title="Human">human</a> speech.<sup id="cite_ref-COS15_2-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COS15-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-FB_1-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-FB-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>The fangs contained highly toxic <a href="/wiki/Acromantula_venom" title="Acromantula venom">venom</a>, valued at 100 <a href="/wiki/Wizarding_currency" title="Wizarding currency">Galleons</a> a pint in <a href="/wiki/1996" title="1996">1996</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-HP22_4-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HP22-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Reproduction">Reproduction</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Acromantula?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Reproduction">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Acromantula?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Reproduction"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The female was usually larger than the male, and can lay up to one-hundred <a href="/wiki/Acromantula_egg" title="Acromantula egg">soft, wide, beach ball-sized eggs</a> at a time. They usually hatched in six to eight weeks.<sup id="cite_ref-FB_1-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-FB-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> Acromantula eggs were a Class A <a href="/wiki/Non-Tradeable_Material" title="Non-Tradeable Material">Non-Tradeable Goods</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-FB_1-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-FB-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Social_Behaviour">Social Behaviour</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Acromantula?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Social Behaviour">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Acromantula?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Social Behaviour"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p class="mw-empty-elt"></p><figure class="thumb tleft show-info-icon" style="width: 131px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/3/37/Acromantula-0.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20170430024543" class="image"><img alt="Acromantula-0.jpg" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/3/37/Acromantula-0.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/129?cb=20170430024543" decoding="async" width="129" height="206" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="Acromantula-0.jpg" data-image-key="Acromantula-0.jpg" /></a> 	 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:Acromantula-0.jpg" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 	</figcaption> </figure>
<p class="mw-empty-elt"></p><p>In contrary to most non-magical spiders, Acromantulas were social animals, who lived in large colonies consisting of hundreds of their species. These colonies were led by the oldest male and female of said colonies, who were highly respected and met with absolute obedience. The Acromantula Aragog, for example, was able to keep his entire colony from eating Hagrid, despite their instincts telling them to attack humans, because he respected Hagrid as a friend.<sup id="cite_ref-COS15_2-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COS15-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Acromantulas were capable of forming emotional attachments to other sentient beings, but only if they were raised well from a very young age. A good example was the friendship between Aragog and Hagrid, with the latter having raised the giant spider from when he was still in his egg.<sup id="cite_ref-COS13_5-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COS13-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> However, this friendship did not automatically extend to people associated with whoever managed to win the respect of an Acromantula nor do other members of a colony automatically treated said person as friend, as they will first and foremost treat humans and other sentient beings like prey. This is demonstrated after Aragog died, the other spiders immediately tried to eat Hagrid, despite him being a good friend to their former leader.<sup id="cite_ref-HP22_4-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HP22-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Whenever a member of an Acromantula colony died, their corpse were eaten by the other spiders upon death. This included the leaders of a colony. It was not known if a dead Acromantula will be mourned by other members of its species so it cannot be said for certain if this was a funeral rite or simply a way to acquire food.<sup id="cite_ref-FB_1-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-FB-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-HP22_4-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HP22-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>The <a href="/wiki/Basilisk" title="Basilisk">basilisk</a> was the worst enemy of all spiders, including Acromantulas, feared so much that they did not even dare to speak of it, similar to how wizards of <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>'s world view <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a> and used a euphemism in place of his name. It was their greatest threat due to the basilisk's killing gaze and the fact that spiders had many eyes that grant extreme field of view, thus making them highly vulnerable targets.<sup id="cite_ref-COS15_2-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COS15-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Habitat">Habitat</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Acromantula?veaction=edit&amp;section=4" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Habitat">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Acromantula?action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Habitat"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Acromantulas tend to reside in thick jungles and heavily forested areas, and their webs are large and dome-shaped.<sup id="cite_ref-FB_1-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-FB-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-COS15_2-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COS15-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Acromantula?veaction=edit&amp;section=6" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Acromantula?action=edit&amp;section=6" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The name Acromantula has the Greek ακρος (acros/acro), meaning high or "peak," and -mantula, from the English "<a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/tarantula" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:tarantula">tarantula</a>." True to their name, Acromantulas are massive arachnids who tend to spin their expansive webs high in tree tops. Despite the names bearing a phonetic similarity to <a href="/wiki/Tarantula" title="Tarantula">Tarantulas</a>, they appear more like Wolf Spiders and are, in all depictions, <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Araneomorphae" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Araneomorphae">araneomorphae.</a>
</p><p>The eight eyes of the Acromantula are used to symbolise the number eight in the <a href="/wiki/Study_of_Ancient_Runes" title="Study of Ancient Runes">runic alphabet</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-JKRS_8-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-JKRS-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<ul><li>In the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Chamber_of_Secrets_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets (film)">film adaptation</a> of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Chamber_of_Secrets" title="Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets">Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets</a></i>, Harry Potter used the <a href="/wiki/Spider_repelling_spell" title="Spider repelling spell">Spider repelling spell</a> (<i>Arania Exumai</i>), a spell for defence use against spiders, while attempting to escape the <a href="/wiki/Forbidden_Forest_Acromantula_colony" title="Forbidden Forest Acromantula colony">Acromantula colony</a>. He learned the spell from <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Tom Riddle</a> upon visiting the latter's sixteen year old memory in his <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle%27s_Diary" class="mw-redirect" title="Tom Riddle&#39;s Diary">diary</a>. The spell was never mentioned or depicted in the book.<sup id="cite_ref-COSF_9-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COSF-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li>The Acromantulas in the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Chamber_of_Secrets_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets (film)">film</a> more closely resemble wolf spiders (family Lycosidae) than actual tarantulas (family Theraphosidae), but that may reflect upon the fact that wolf spiders were the first kind of spider to ever be referred to as "tarantulas".</li>
<li>In <i><a href="/wiki/Fantastic_Beasts_and_Where_to_Find_Them_(companion_book)" title="Fantastic Beasts and Where to Find Them (companion book)">Fantastic Beasts and Where to Find Them</a></i>, <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ron</a> pens an added nine "X"s to the Acromantula's <a href="/wiki/Ministry_of_Magic_Classification" title="Ministry of Magic Classification">Classification</a>. This is a reference to his fear of spiders. He and Harry also cross out the "unconfirmed" at the end of the sentence "Rumours of an Acromantula colony being established in Scotland are unconfirmed" and replace it with "Confirmed by Harry Potter and Ron Weasley."</li>
<li>In the films, Acromantulas have two big eyes, at the height of human eyes, with a line of four small eyes underneath and an eye on each side of the head, human temple height. This is a detail that is not specified in the books.</li>
<li>The <a href="/wiki/Boggart" title="Boggart">boggart</a> in the form of a giant spider that Ron faced off in the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Prisoner_of_Azkaban_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban (film)">film adaptation</a> of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Prisoner_of_Azkaban" title="Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban">Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban</a></i> closely resembles a <a href="/wiki/Black_widow" title="Black widow">Black widow</a>, with the exception of its size, which may have been the result of his encounter with the much larger sized Acromantulas.<sup id="cite_ref-POAF_10-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-POAF-10">&#91;10&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li>In <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Forbidden_Journey" title="Harry Potter and the Forbidden Journey">Harry Potter and the Forbidden Journey</a></i>, riders encounter Acromantulas in the <a href="/wiki/Forbidden_Forest" title="Forbidden Forest">Forbidden Forest</a> portion of the the ride.</li>
<li>The Acromantulas inhabiting the Forbidden Forest resemble, at certain point, the Great Spiders of Mirkwood from J. R. R. Tolkien's <i>The Hobbit</i>.</li></ul>
