<p>A <b>part-vampire</b> is a being with both <a href="/wiki/Human" title="Human">human</a> and <a href="/wiki/Vampire" title="Vampire">vampire</a> traits. Part-vampires are therefore a type of <a href="/wiki/Half-breed" title="Half-breed">half-breed</a>, with human and vampire ancestors.<sup id="cite_ref-JKRS_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-JKRS-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> They supposedly resemble a human but with subtle vampire traits such as mildly exaggerated fangs and pale skin. It is unknown whether they will inherit any vampire tenancies, (e.g. an aversion to sunlight). This has been confirmed because it is proved that vampires are related to humans since they are featured in the Ministry's <i><a href="/wiki/Guidelines_for_the_Treatment_of_Non-Wizard_Part-Humans" title="Guidelines for the Treatment of Non-Wizard Part-Humans">Guidelines for the Treatment of Non-Wizard Part-Humans</a></i>.<sup id="cite_ref-GOF10_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF10-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Singer <a href="/wiki/Lorcan_d%27Eath" title="Lorcan d&#39;Eath">Lorcan d'Eath</a> is a part-vampire.<sup id="cite_ref-JKRS_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-JKRS-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
