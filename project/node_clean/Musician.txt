<li><b><a href="/wiki/Coldplay" title="Coldplay">Coldplay</a></b>: A Muggle band.</li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Gorillaz" title="Gorillaz">Gorillaz</a></b>: A Muggle band.</li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Lorcan_d%27Eath" title="Lorcan d&#39;Eath">Lorcan d'Eath</a></b>: A very popular <a href="/wiki/Part-vampire" title="Part-vampire">part-vampire</a> singer.<sup id="cite_ref-JKRS_5-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-JKRS-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Bletchley_(musician)" title="Bletchley (musician)">Madame Bletchley</a></b>. A <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch</a> who worked as a professional musician, who in <a href="/wiki/1940" title="1940">1940</a> received an award.<sup id="cite_ref-award_6-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-award-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Spellbound" title="Spellbound">Spellbound</a></b>: An all-witch band; frequently hit by "tragic scandals".<sup id="cite_ref-POAF_7-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-POAF-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/The_Bent-Winged_Snitches" title="The Bent-Winged Snitches">The Bent-Winged Snitches</a></b>: A controversial American wizarding band.<sup id="cite_ref-PM_8-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PM-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/The_Hobgoblins" title="The Hobgoblins">The Hobgoblins</a></b>: Famous wizarding band fronted by <a href="/wiki/Stubby_Boardman" title="Stubby Boardman">Stubby Boardman</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP10_9-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP10-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Weird_Sisters" title="Weird Sisters">Weird Sisters</a></b>: A very popular wizarding band.<sup id="cite_ref-GOF23_10-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF23-10">&#91;10&#93;</a></sup></li></ul>
