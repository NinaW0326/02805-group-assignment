<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Libatius_Borage?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Libatius_Borage?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>In his youth, he attended the <a href="/wiki/Wizarding_school" title="Wizarding school">wizarding school</a> of <a href="/wiki/Castelobruxo" title="Castelobruxo">Castelobruxo</a>, in <a href="/wiki/Brazil" title="Brazil">Brazil</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-PM_3-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PM-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Among his many published works are <i><a href="/wiki/Advanced_Potion-Making" title="Advanced Potion-Making">Advanced Potion-Making</a></i>, <i><a href="/wiki/Asiatic_Anti-Venoms" title="Asiatic Anti-Venoms">Asiatic Anti-Venoms</a></i> and <i><a href="/wiki/Have_Yourself_a_Fiesta_in_a_Bottle!" title="Have Yourself a Fiesta in a Bottle!">Have Yourself a Fiesta in a Bottle!</a></i><sup id="cite_ref-PM_3-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PM-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> â€” the first and second of which are used at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a>, as a textbook for <a href="/wiki/Nastily_Exhausting_Wizarding_Test" title="Nastily Exhausting Wizarding Test">N.E.W.T.</a>-level classes,<sup id="cite_ref-HBP9_4-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP9-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> and as a reference text for <a href="/wiki/Ordinary_Wizarding_Level" title="Ordinary Wizarding Level">O.W.L.</a>-level classes,<sup id="cite_ref-OP16_5-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OP16-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> respectively.
</p><p>Despite Borage's accomplishments, <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts</a> <a href="/wiki/Potions_Master" title="Potions Master">Potions Master</a> <a href="/wiki/Severus_Snape" title="Severus Snape">Severus Snape</a> seemed to be more adept at <a href="/wiki/Potions_(class)" title="Potions (class)">potion-making</a> than even him, adding many footnotes to his own copy edition of Borage's book, significantly improving it.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP9_4-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP9-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>In the <a href="/wiki/1980s" title="1980s">1980s</a>, Borage's portrait hung at the <a href="/wiki/Hippogriff_Clubhouse" title="Hippogriff Clubhouse">Hippogriff Clubhouse</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-HM_2-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HM-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Magical_abilities_and_skills">Magical abilities and skills</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Libatius_Borage?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Libatius_Borage?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li><b><a href="/wiki/Potion" title="Potion">Potions</a></b>: Borage was a highly accomplished and skilled <a href="/wiki/Potioneer" title="Potioneer">potioneer</a> in his lifetime, having written several books on the subject and was considered to be one the most famous potioneers in the world.<sup id="cite_ref-PM_3-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PM-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-HBP9_4-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP9-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Alchemy" title="Alchemy">Alchemy</a></b>: Borage also had significant knowledge in the field of Alchemy, having written about the subject extensively in <i><a href="/wiki/Advanced_Potion-Making" title="Advanced Potion-Making">Advanced Potion-Making</a></i>.<sup id="cite_ref-HBPF_6-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBPF-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup></li></ul>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Libatius_Borage?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Libatius_Borage?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Libatius Borage's name probably derived from libation, a ritual offering of drink to the gods, and Borage, an edible herb believed to bestow excitement and courage in the hearts of those who consumed it.
</p>
