<p>The&#160;<b>Fates</b>&#160;(also known as <b>Moirai</b>) is a group of three weaving <a href="/wiki/Greece" title="Greece">greek</a> goddesses who assign individual destinies to&#160;mortals&#160;at birth. Their names are&#160;Clotho&#160;(the Spinner),&#160;Lachesis&#160;(the Alloter) and&#160;Atropos&#160;(the Inflexible). Professor Trelawney claimed have gotten information about the future <a href="/wiki/Divination_(class)" title="Divination (class)">Divination</a> examination from them.<sup><sup id="cite_ref-POA15_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-POA15-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup></sup>
</p>
