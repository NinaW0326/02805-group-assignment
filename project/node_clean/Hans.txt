<b>Hans</b> was a male&#160;<a href="/wiki/Augurey" title="Augurey">Augurey</a> and the team mascot for the <a href="/wiki/Liechtenstein_National_Quidditch_team" title="Liechtenstein National Quidditch team">Liechtenstein National Quidditch team</a>, who even had <a href="/wiki/Hans_Fan_Club" title="Hans Fan Club">his own fan club</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Hans?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Hans?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>During the celebrations following the quarter-final match of the <a href="/wiki/2014_Quidditch_World_Cup" title="2014 Quidditch World Cup">2014 Quidditch World Cup</a>, in which the <a href="/wiki/American_National_Quidditch_team" title="American National Quidditch team">USA</a> defeated <a href="/wiki/Liechtenstein_National_Quidditch_team" title="Liechtenstein National Quidditch team">Liechtenstein</a> on <a href="/wiki/8_June" title="8 June">8 June</a>, <a href="/wiki/2014" title="2014">2014</a>, Hans was reported to have been kidnapped by celebrating American fans. Liechtenstein coach and manager <a href="/wiki/Ferdinand_J%C3%A4gendorf" title="Ferdinand Jägendorf">Ferdinand Jägendorf</a> issued the following statement: "<i>Das finden wir nicht lustig</i>" ("we don't find that funny").<sup id="cite_ref-PM_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PM-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>He was returned the following day after an exchange of terse owls between <a href="/wiki/Liechtenstein" title="Liechtenstein">Liechtenstein</a> <a href="/wiki/Minister_for_Magic" title="Minister for Magic">Minister for Magic</a> <a href="/wiki/Otto_Obermeier" title="Otto Obermeier">Otto Obermeier</a> and <a href="/wiki/President_of_the_Magical_Congress_of_the_United_States_of_America" title="President of the Magical Congress of the United States of America">President</a> of <a href="/wiki/Magical_Congress_of_the_United_States_of_America" title="Magical Congress of the United States of America">MACUSA</a> <a href="/wiki/Samuel_G._Quahog" title="Samuel G. Quahog">Samuel G. Quahog</a>, although <a href="/wiki/Magizoologist" title="Magizoologist">magizoologists</a> had to keep him under close observation for ill effects.<sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
