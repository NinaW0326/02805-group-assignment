<p><b>Emmeline Vance</b> (d. early <a href="/wiki/July" title="July">July</a>, <a href="/wiki/1996" title="1996">1996</a>)<sup id="cite_ref-death_2-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-death-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch</a> and a member of the first and second <a href="/wiki/Order_of_the_Phoenix" title="Order of the Phoenix">Order of the Phoenix</a>. She most likely fought in several battles in both the <a href="/wiki/First_Wizarding_War" title="First Wizarding War">First</a> and <a href="/wiki/Second_Wizarding_War" title="Second Wizarding War">Second</a> Wizarding Wars with the Order, and was also a member of the <a href="/wiki/Advance_Guard" title="Advance Guard">Advance Guard</a>. Emmeline was murdered by <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a> in mid-<a href="/wiki/1996" title="1996">1996</a>. She was considered a highly talented and prodigious witch and her <a href="/wiki/Death" title="Death">death</a> was a significant and devastating loss to the Order.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Emmeline_Vance?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Emmeline_Vance?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<figure class="thumb tleft show-info-icon" style="width: 252px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/5/58/Order_of_the_Phoenix.png/revision/latest?cb=20140511035621" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/5/58/Order_of_the_Phoenix.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/250?cb=20140511035621" decoding="async" width="250" height="105" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="Order of the Phoenix.png" data-image-key="Order_of_the_Phoenix.png" /></a> 	 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:Order_of_the_Phoenix.png" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption">The first <a href="/wiki/Order_of_the_Phoenix" title="Order of the Phoenix">Order of the Phoenix</a></p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure>
<p>Emmeline served as a member of the original <a href="/wiki/Order_of_the_Phoenix" title="Order of the Phoenix">Order of the Phoenix</a>, an organisation constructed by <a href="/wiki/Albus_Dumbledore" title="Albus Dumbledore">Albus Dumbledore</a> in order to oppose <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a> and his <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a>. She fought in the <a href="/wiki/First_Wizarding_War" title="First Wizarding War">First Wizarding War</a> and unlike many others ultimately survived, living well past Voldemort's first fall.<sup id="cite_ref-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<figure class="thumb tright show-info-icon" style="width: 252px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/2/22/B5C3M1_Advance_Guard_at_Privet_Drive.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20180424050733" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/2/22/B5C3M1_Advance_Guard_at_Privet_Drive.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/250?cb=20180424050733" decoding="async" width="250" height="98" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="B5C3M1 Advance Guard at Privet Drive.jpg" data-image-key="B5C3M1_Advance_Guard_at_Privet_Drive.jpg" /></a> 	 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:B5C3M1_Advance_Guard_at_Privet_Drive.jpg" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption">The <a href="/wiki/Advance_Guard" title="Advance Guard">Advance Guard</a> inside <a href="/wiki/4_Privet_Drive" title="4 Privet Drive">4 Privet Drive</a>, preparing to escort <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> to safety</p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure>
<p>When Lord Voldemort returned to power in <a href="/wiki/1995" title="1995">1995</a>, Vance became a member of the reconstituted Order of the Phoenix. One of Vance's more important duties was to serve as a member of the <a href="/wiki/Advance_Guard" title="Advance Guard">Advance Guard</a> that acted as protectors of <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> after they extracted him from <a href="/wiki/4_Privet_Drive" title="4 Privet Drive">4 Privet Drive</a> and took him to the safety of the Order's headquarters at <a href="/wiki/12_Grimmauld_Place" title="12 Grimmauld Place">12 Grimmauld Place</a>. During the flight, Vance and the other Order members constantly changed their positions and Harry glimpsed Vance with her <a href="/wiki/Wand" title="Wand">wand</a> out, alert and waiting for signs of danger.<sup id="cite_ref-ootp3_3-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-ootp3-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Emmeline's long distinguished service to the Order ended however, when she was attacked and murdered by <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a> near the residence of the <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggle</a> <a href="/wiki/Prime_Minister" title="Prime Minister">Prime Minister</a> in the second week of <a href="/wiki/July" title="July">July</a>, <a href="/wiki/1996" title="1996">1996</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-death_2-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-death-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> Her death was considered a significant and devastating loss for the Order, and her murder was publicised in <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggle</a> newspapers, most likely because of the place of her murder being so close to the Prime Minister. <a href="/wiki/Severus_Snape" title="Severus Snape">Severus Snape</a> claimed to have given information to Voldemort that led to Vance's death, but considering he was in fact a double agent ultimately loyal to the Order, it is unknown if he actually did so.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP2_6-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP2-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Magical_abilities_and_skills">Magical abilities and skills</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Emmeline_Vance?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Emmeline_Vance?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li><b><a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">Magical</a> aptitude</b>: Being a highly respected member of the <a href="/wiki/Order_of_the_Phoenix" title="Order of the Phoenix">Order of the Phoenix</a>, Vance was considered by fellow members of the Order to be a highly talented and skilful witch, meaning that she was proficient in many areas and fields of magic. Much of the sorrow expressed after her murder was a further testament to the extent of her power whilst she was alive.</li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Charm" title="Charm">Charms</a></b>: Vance could produce a non-corporeal <a href="/wiki/Patronus_Charm" title="Patronus Charm">Patronus Charm</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-LEGOY2_4-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-LEGOY2-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> a mark of superior magical ability and talent given the immense difficulty of performing such a powerful and advanced protective charm.</li></ul>

<ul><li><b><a href="/wiki/Flight" title="Flight">Flying</a></b>: Vance was a highly skilled and accomplished <a href="/wiki/Broomstick" title="Broomstick">broomstick</a> flyer, being a member of the Advance Guard who successfully escorted Harry Potter to 12 Grimmauld Place via broomstick flight. She was also noted to have had the ability to been adept enough to simultaneously fly and brandish her wand at the same time by Tonks, something said to be extremely difficult to do, showing her exceptional flying skills.<sup id="cite_ref-ootp3_3-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-ootp3-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup></li></ul>

<ul><li><b><a href="/wiki/Emmeline_Vance%27s_wand" title="Emmeline Vance&#39;s wand">Wand</a></b>: Emmeline Vance owned a <a href="/wiki/Wand" title="Wand">wand</a>, which was of an unknown length, <a href="/wiki/Wand_wood" title="Wand wood">wand wood</a> and <a href="/wiki/Wand_core" title="Wand core">Core</a>. She wielded her wand throughout her membership of the <a href="/wiki/Order_of_the_Phoenix" title="Order of the Phoenix">Order of the Phoenix</a>, and the <a href="/wiki/Advance_Guard" title="Advance Guard">Advance Guard</a> in <a href="/wiki/1995" title="1995">1995</a>. What happened to her wand after her death and murder is unknown.</li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Broomstick" title="Broomstick">Broomstick</a></b>: Being a highly adept and accomplished flyer and a member of the <a href="/wiki/Advance_Guard" title="Advance Guard">Advance Guard</a>, Emmeline would have owned her own broom, which was of an unknown make and model.</li></ul>

<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Order_of_the_Phoenix">Order of the Phoenix</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Emmeline_Vance?veaction=edit&amp;section=6" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Order of the Phoenix">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Emmeline_Vance?action=edit&amp;section=6" title="Edit section: Order of the Phoenix"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Emmeline evidently had a highly positive relationship with most members of the <a href="/wiki/Order_of_the_Phoenix" title="Order of the Phoenix">Order of the Phoenix</a>, as they respected her immensely for her skill as a witch and her loyalty and dedication to the Order. Many of the Order's members were deeply devastated by her <a href="/wiki/Death" title="Death">death</a> and murder at the hands of the <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a>, and considered her a great loss to their cause. <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> also mentioned her in a discussion with <a href="/wiki/Albus_Dumbledore" title="Albus Dumbledore">Dumbledore</a> about life and death.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Emmeline_Vance?veaction=edit&amp;section=7" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Emmeline_Vance?action=edit&amp;section=7" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The name "Emmeline" comes from an Old French form of the Germanic name Amelina, originally a diminutive of Germanic names beginning with the element amal meaning "work". It was introduced to England by the Normans.
</p>
