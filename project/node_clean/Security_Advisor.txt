<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="History">History</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Security_Advisor?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: History">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Security_Advisor?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: History"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>In <a href="/wiki/1926" title="1926">1926</a>, the <i><a href="/wiki/The_New_York_Ghost" title="The New York Ghost">New York Ghost</a></i> reported that the International Confederation of Wizards' Security Advisors had called for an immediate conference in the city of <a href="/wiki/Antwerp" title="Antwerp">Antwerp</a>, in <a href="/wiki/Belgium" title="Belgium">Belgium</a>, in response to the rise of <a href="/wiki/Gellert_Grindelwald" title="Gellert Grindelwald">Gellert Grindelwald</a> and his <a href="/wiki/Global_wizarding_war" title="Global wizarding war">escalating campaign of violence</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-FB_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-FB-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Security_Advisor?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Security_Advisor?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>It is unknown if the Security Advisers are employed by the <a href="/wiki/International_Confederation_of_Wizards" title="International Confederation of Wizards">International Confederation of Wizards</a> to assist wizarding governing bodies, the Confederation itself or both.</li>
<li>Being the magical equivalent of the UN, it is unknown if there is an International Confederation of Wizard's Security Council, but if so, that would be where the Security Advisers work.</li>
<li>Given the name, the <a href="/wiki/International_Magical_Office_of_Law" title="International Magical Office of Law">International Magical Office of Law</a>, whose purpose has yet to be explained in the books, might fulfil the role equivalent to the UN's Security Council for the ICW. Regardless, if there is indeed is such a branch of the ICW, it presumably serves a similar purpose as its non-magical counterpart, namely the maintenance of international peace and security in the wizarding world, as well as accepting new members to the International Confederation of Wizards and approving any changes to its <a href="/wiki/International_Statute_of_Wizarding_Secrecy" title="International Statute of Wizarding Secrecy">International Statutes of Secrecy</a>. Its powers would presumably likewise include the establishment of peacekeeping operations, which would explain both why and how the Security Advisers would call for an immediate conference in response to the rise of Gellert Grindelwald - whose attacks in Europe constituted considerable violations to wizarding secrecy - as well as who or what was usually behind setting up <a href="/wiki/International_Task_Force" title="International Task Force">international task forces</a>, like the one stationed in <a href="/wiki/Tibet" title="Tibet">Tibet</a>.</li></ul>
