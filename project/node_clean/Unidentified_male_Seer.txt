<p>This <b>unidentified <a href="/wiki/Seer" title="Seer">Seer</a></b> was an elderly, bearded <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> who made a little known <a href="/wiki/Prophecy_at_the_Department_of_Mysteries_(I)" title="Prophecy at the Department of Mysteries (I)">prophecy</a> that was held in the <a href="/wiki/Hall_of_Prophecy" title="Hall of Prophecy">Hall of Prophecy</a>. His <a href="/wiki/Prophecy_Record" title="Prophecy Record">Prophecy Record</a> was broken during the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_the_Department_of_Mysteries" title="Battle of the Department of Mysteries">Battle of the Department of Mysteries</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP35_1-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP35-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_male_Seer?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_male_Seer?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>This seer speaks of something occurring on the <a href="/wiki/Solstice" title="Solstice">Solstice</a>. Both the Winter and Summer Solstice have been recognised and celebrated in many cultures around the world. Perhaps coincidentally, the release date of the final book was announced during Winter Solstice 2006.
</p>
