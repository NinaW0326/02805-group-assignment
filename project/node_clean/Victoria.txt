<b>Victoria</b> (full name <b>Alexandrina Victoria</b>; <a href="/wiki/24_May" title="24 May">24 May</a> <a href="/wiki/1819" class="mw-redirect" title="1819">1819</a> â€“ <a href="/wiki/22_January" title="22 January">22 January</a> <a href="/wiki/1901" title="1901">1901</a>) was a <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggle</a> who reigned as <a href="/wiki/Queen" title="Queen">Queen</a> of the United Kingdom of <a href="/wiki/Great_Britain" title="Great Britain">Great Britain</a> and <a href="/wiki/Ireland" title="Ireland">Ireland</a> from <a href="/wiki/1837" class="mw-redirect" title="1837">1837</a> to <a href="/wiki/1901" title="1901">1901</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-wikipedia_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-wikipedia-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p><a href="/wiki/Evangeline_Orpington" title="Evangeline Orpington">Evangeline Orpington</a>, the <a href="/wiki/Minister_for_Magic" title="Minister for Magic">Minister for Magic</a> from <a href="/wiki/1849" class="mw-redirect" title="1849">1849</a> to <a href="/wiki/1855" class="mw-redirect" title="1855">1855</a>, was a close friend of Queen Victoria.<sup id="cite_ref-pm-ministers_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-pm-ministers-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> The queen never suspected that Orpington was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-pm-ministers_2-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-pm-ministers-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Victoria was buried on <a href="/wiki/4_February" title="4 February">4 February</a> <a href="/wiki/1901" title="1901">1901</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-wikipedia_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-wikipedia-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> The Minister for Magic at the time, <a href="/wiki/Faris_Spavin" title="Faris Spavin">Faris Spavin</a>, attended her <a href="/wiki/Funeral_of_Victoria" title="Funeral of Victoria">funeral</a> in an admiral's hat and spats.<sup id="cite_ref-pm-ministers_2-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-pm-ministers-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> This was the final act that made the <a href="/wiki/Wizengamot" title="Wizengamot">Wizengamot</a> suggest that Spavin, then 147 years old, retire.<sup id="cite_ref-pm-ministers_2-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-pm-ministers-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>A <a href="/wiki/Portrait" title="Portrait">portrait</a> of Victoria hung in the abandoned <a href="/wiki/Old_Bell_Tower" title="Old Bell Tower">old bell tower</a> near the <a href="/wiki/Quidditch_pitch" title="Quidditch pitch">pitch</a> where the <a href="/wiki/Chudley_Cannons" title="Chudley Cannons">Chudley Cannons</a> practised.<sup id="cite_ref-fb-cases_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-fb-cases-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Victoria was a descendant of King <a href="/wiki/William_I" title="William I">William I</a> and a great-great-grandmother of Queen <a href="/wiki/Elizabeth_II" title="Elizabeth II">Elizabeth II</a>.
</p>
