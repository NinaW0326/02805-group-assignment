<b>Dame</b><sup id="cite_ref-dameportrait_4-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-dameportrait-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> <b>Phyllida Spore</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch</a> who lived during the <a href="/wiki/15th_century" title="15th century">15th century</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-1408portrait_6-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1408portrait-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-ps5_5-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-ps5-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> A celebrated <a href="/wiki/Herbologist" title="Herbologist">Herbologist</a>, she was the author of the textbook <i><a href="/wiki/One_Thousand_Magical_Herbs_and_Fungi" title="One Thousand Magical Herbs and Fungi">One Thousand Magical Herbs and Fungi</a></i><sup id="cite_ref-ps5_5-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-ps5-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> and also served as <a href="/wiki/Headmaster" title="Headmaster">Headmistress</a> of <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-GFf21_3-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GFf21-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Phyllida_Spore?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Phyllida_Spore?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<figure class="thumb tleft show-info-icon" style="width: 158px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/0/06/Phyllida_Spore.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20110517222221" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/0/06/Phyllida_Spore.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/156?cb=20110517222221" decoding="async" width="156" height="212" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="Phyllida Spore.jpg" data-image-key="Phyllida_Spore.jpg" /></a> 	 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:Phyllida_Spore.jpg" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption">Phyllida Spore</p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure>
<p>Phyllida Spore lived in the <a href="/wiki/15th_century" title="15th century">15th century</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-1408portrait_6-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1408portrait-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup> She studied <a href="/wiki/Herbology" title="Herbology">Herbology</a>, writing <i><a href="/wiki/One_Thousand_Magical_Herbs_and_Fungi" title="One Thousand Magical Herbs and Fungi">One Thousand Magical Herbs and Fungi</a></i>, a textbook that was still used at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> in the <a href="/wiki/1990s" title="1990s">1990s</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-ps5_5-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-ps5-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-hbp25_7-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-hbp25-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup> 
</p><p>Spore became the <a href="/wiki/Headmaster" title="Headmaster">Headmistress</a> of Hogwarts during her lifetime.<sup id="cite_ref-GFf21_3-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GFf21-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> At some point, she also earned the title "Dame,"<sup id="cite_ref-dameportrait_4-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-dameportrait-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> the female equivalent of the honour of <a href="/wiki/Knight" title="Knight">knighthood</a> in <a href="/wiki/Great_Britain" title="Great Britain">Great Britain</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-damewiki_8-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-damewiki-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Professor Spore died sometime in or before <a href="/wiki/1995" title="1995">1995</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-DOD_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DOD-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> Two <a href="/wiki/Portrait" title="Portrait">portraits</a> of her hung at Hogwarts: one painted in <a href="/wiki/1408" class="mw-redirect" title="1408">1408</a> depicted a middle aged woman,<sup id="cite_ref-1408portrait_6-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1408portrait-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup> and another, hung in the <a href="/wiki/Headmaster%27s_office" class="mw-redirect" title="Headmaster&#39;s office">Headmaster's office</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-GFf21_3-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GFf21-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> depicted her as a younger witch with long dark hair sitting in a chair with skull-shaped finials.<sup id="cite_ref-dameportrait_4-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-dameportrait-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Magical_abilities_and_skills">Magical abilities and skills</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Phyllida_Spore?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Phyllida_Spore?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li><b><a href="/wiki/Herbology" title="Herbology">Herbology</a></b>: Phyllida was a very skilled and knowledgeable <a href="/wiki/Herbologist" title="Herbologist">Herbologist</a>, having thorough knowledge of <i><a href="/wiki/One_Thousand_Magical_Herbs_and_Fungi" title="One Thousand Magical Herbs and Fungi">One Thousand Magical Herbs and Fungi</a></i> and having written several books about the subject.<sup id="cite_ref-ps5_5-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-ps5-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Healing_magic" title="Healing magic">Healing magic</a></b>: One of her books (<i><a href="/wiki/Healing_at_Home_with_Herbs" title="Healing at Home with Herbs">Healing at Home with Herbs</a></i>) specialised on the healing properties of such herbs, showing she also had good knowledge of healing <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magic</a>.</li></ul>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Phyllida_Spore?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Phyllida_Spore?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>Phyllida's portrait at the <a href="/wiki/Headmaster%27s_office" class="mw-redirect" title="Headmaster&#39;s office">Headmaster's office</a> appears only in the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Goblet_of_Fire_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire (film)">film adaptation</a> of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Goblet_of_Fire" title="Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire">Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire</a></i>. In the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Chamber_of_Secrets_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets (film)">film adaptation</a> of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Chamber_of_Secrets" title="Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets">Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets</a></i> a large painting of <a href="/wiki/Antonia_Creaseworthy" title="Antonia Creaseworthy">Antonia Creaseworthy</a> occupies that space, and in the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Order_of_the_Phoenix_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix (film)">film adaptation</a> of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Order_of_the_Phoenix" title="Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix">Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix</a></i> and the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Half-Blood_Prince_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince (film)">film adaptation</a> of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Half-Blood_Prince" title="Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince">Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince</a></i> the portrait of <a href="/wiki/Phineas_Nigellus_Black" title="Phineas Nigellus Black">Phineas Nigellus Black</a> is hung there.</li>
<li>The portrait of Spore that is featured in <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Goblet_of_Fire_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire (film)">Goblet of Fire</a></i> was based on an <a target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener" class="external text" href="http://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/1/1d/Elizabeth_Barrett_Browning_by_Michele_Gordigiani_1858.jpg">1858 portrait of poet Elizabeth Barrett Browning (1806–1861)</a>, by Michele Gordigiani.</li></ul>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Phyllida_Spore?veaction=edit&amp;section=4" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Phyllida_Spore?action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Her first name is from Greek for "leaf" or "plant." A spore is a body produced by fungi, algae and non-flowering plants that is very protective and resistant to drought.
</p>
