<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="History">History</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Lonely_Male_Gargoyle?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: History">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Lonely_Male_Gargoyle?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: History"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>It was located at one end of the <a href="/wiki/Viaduct" title="Viaduct">Viaduct</a> of <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Castle" title="Hogwarts Castle">the castle</a>, near the <a href="/wiki/Viaduct_Courtyard" title="Viaduct Courtyard">Entrance Courtyard</a>. Until <a href="/wiki/1994" title="1994">1994</a>, <a href="/wiki/Lonely_Male_Gargoyle%27s_friend" title="Lonely Male Gargoyle&#39;s friend">another gargoyle</a> sat on a plinth facing it, but it was knocked over the side of the wall by a <a href="/wiki/Hungarian_Horntail" title="Hungarian Horntail">Hungarian Horntail</a> during the <a href="/wiki/Triwizard_Tournament" title="Triwizard Tournament">First Task</a> of the <a href="/wiki/Triwizard_Tournament" title="Triwizard Tournament">Triwizard Tournament</a>. Ever since, the gargoyle has longed for some company.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTPG_1-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTPG-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>During the <a href="/wiki/1995%E2%80%931996_school_year" title="1995–1996 school year">1995–1996 school year</a>, <a href="/wiki/Cuthbert_Binns" title="Cuthbert Binns">Professor Binns</a> assigned his <a href="/wiki/Fifth_year" title="Fifth year">Fifth year</a> students the task of listing five talking gargoyles as a homework assignment. <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> located the gargoyles as a favour to <a href="/wiki/Dean_Thomas" title="Dean Thomas">Dean Thomas</a> so that he could attend the first meeting of <a href="/wiki/Dumbledore%27s_Army" title="Dumbledore&#39;s Army">Dumbledore's Army</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Room_of_Requirement" title="Room of Requirement">Room of Requirement</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTPG_1-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTPG-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Like the others, casting the <a href="/wiki/Fire-Making_Spell" title="Fire-Making Spell">Fire-Making Spell</a> on the gargoyle would cause it to breathe <a href="/wiki/Fire" title="Fire">fire</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTPG_1-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTPG-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
