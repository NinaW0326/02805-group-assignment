<p>This <b>ghost</b> attended <a href="/wiki/Nicholas_de_Mimsy-Porpington" title="Nicholas de Mimsy-Porpington">Nicholas de Mimsy-Porpington</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Nicholas_de_Mimsy-Porpington%27s_five-hundredth_Deathday_Party" title="Nicholas de Mimsy-Porpington&#39;s five-hundredth Deathday Party">five-hundredth Deathday party</a> on <a href="/wiki/31_October" title="31 October">31 October</a>, <a href="/wiki/1992" title="1992">1992</a>. He was described as portly. He floated through spoiled food and claimed that he could almost taste it.<sup id="cite_ref-CS8_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-CS8-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
