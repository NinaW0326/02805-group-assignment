<b>Ariel "Speedy" Singleton</b> was an <a href="/wiki/United_States_of_America" title="United States of America">American</a> <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch</a> who played as <a href="/wiki/Seeker" title="Seeker">Seeker</a> on the <a href="/wiki/American_National_Quidditch_team" title="American National Quidditch team">American National Quidditch team</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-QWC_1-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-QWC-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>During the <a href="/wiki/1996%E2%80%931997_school_year" title="1996–1997 school year">1996–1997 school year</a>, Singleton's <a href="/wiki/Chocolate_Frog_Card" title="Chocolate Frog Card">Chocolate Frog Card</a> could be won by beating <a href="/wiki/Luna_Lovegood" title="Luna Lovegood">Luna Lovegood</a> in a <a href="/wiki/Duelling" title="Duelling">duelling</a> challenge.<sup id="cite_ref-HBPG_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBPG-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Ariel_Singleton?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Ariel_Singleton?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<figure class="thumb tright show-info-icon" style="width: 182px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/c/cd/Seeker_-_Ariel_Speedy_Singleton_QWC.png/revision/latest?cb=20121021030725" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/c/cd/Seeker_-_Ariel_Speedy_Singleton_QWC.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/180?cb=20121021030725" decoding="async" width="180" height="123" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="Seeker - Ariel Speedy Singleton QWC.png" data-image-key="Seeker_-_Ariel_Speedy_Singleton_QWC.png" /></a> 	 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:Seeker_-_Ariel_Speedy_Singleton_QWC.png" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption">Ariel Singleton</p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure>
<p>The name "Ariel" means "lion of God" in Hebrew. In the Old Testament it is used as another name for the city of Jerusalem. Shakespeare used it as the name of a spirit in his play <i>The Tempest</i> (1611), and one of the moons of Uranus bears this name in his honour.
</p>
