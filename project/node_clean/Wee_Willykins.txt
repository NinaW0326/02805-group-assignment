<div class="mw-parser-output"><p><b>Wee Willykins</b> was a character in <a href="/wiki/Beatrix_Bloxam" title="Beatrix Bloxam">Beatrix Bloxam</a>'s heavily-reworked version of the <a href="/wiki/Beedle_the_Bard" title="Beedle the Bard">Beedle the Bard</a> tale <i><a href="/wiki/The_Wizard_and_the_Hopping_Pot" title="The Wizard and the Hopping Pot">The Wizard and the Hopping Pot</a></i>. In the story, Wee Willykins cured his dolls' stomachaches after initially being "an old grumpy-wumpkins" and refusing to do so, causing the golden hopping pot to dance delightedly on "its tiny rosy toes" and fill with sweets for Wee Willykins and the dolls.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
