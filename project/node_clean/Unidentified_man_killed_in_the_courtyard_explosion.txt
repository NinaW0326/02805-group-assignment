This <b>individual</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> who fought during the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a> in <a href="/wiki/1998" title="1998">1998</a>. He was one of the defenders who were stationed by the <a href="/wiki/Viaduct_Courtyard_Gatehouses" title="Viaduct Courtyard Gatehouses">checkpoint towers</a> entrance to the <a href="/wiki/Viaduct_Courtyard" title="Viaduct Courtyard">Viaduct Courtyard</a>. When&#160;the towers were attacked he ran into the courtyard but was killed by either an explosion or the heat from it.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
