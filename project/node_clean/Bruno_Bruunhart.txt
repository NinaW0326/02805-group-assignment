<b>Bruno Bruunhart</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Liechtenstein" title="Liechtenstein">Liechtenstein</a> <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> and the <a href="/wiki/Seeker" title="Seeker">Seeker</a>&#160;for the <a href="/wiki/2014" title="2014">2014</a>&#160;<a href="/wiki/Liechtenstein_National_Quidditch_team" title="Liechtenstein National Quidditch team">Liechtenstein National Quidditch team</a>. On the third day of the fifth match of the&#160;<a href="/wiki/2014_Quidditch_World_Cup" title="2014 Quidditch World Cup">2014 Quidditch World Cup</a>, he managed to catch the <a href="/wiki/Golden_Snitch" title="Golden Snitch">Snitch</a>, grabbing it within inches from the outstretched hand of&#160;<a href="/wiki/Chad" title="Chad">Chadian</a>&#160;Seeker&#160;<a href="/wiki/Jacques_Miskine" title="Jacques Miskine">Jacques Miskine</a>. This enabled Liechtenstein to win with a score of 470 to 330.<sup id="cite_ref-lie-v-tcd3_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-lie-v-tcd3-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
