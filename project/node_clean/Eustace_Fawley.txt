<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Eustace_Fawley?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Eustace_Fawley?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p><a href="/wiki/Sorting_ceremony" title="Sorting ceremony">Sorted</a> into <a href="/wiki/Hufflepuff" title="Hufflepuff">Hufflepuff House</a>, he was involved with a <a href="/wiki/Fire" title="Fire">fire</a> at the <a href="/wiki/Boathouse" title="Boathouse">Boathouse</a> during his <a href="/wiki/Third_year" title="Third year">third year</a>. He said he came down to practise <a href="/wiki/Gobstones" title="Gobstones">gobstones</a>, but ended up losing <a href="/wiki/Eustace_Fawley%27s_father" title="Eustace Fawley&#39;s father">his father</a>'s best set during the fire.<sup id="cite_ref-FBC7_3-11" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-FBC7-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>He was known to read <a href="/wiki/Martin_Miggs" title="Martin Miggs">Martin Miggs</a> comics and has <a class="new" title="Eustace Fawley&#39;s uncle (page does not exist)" rel="nofollow" data-uncrawlable-url="L3dpa2kvRXVzdGFjZV9GYXdsZXklMjdzX3VuY2xlP2FjdGlvbj1lZGl0JnJlZGxpbms9MQ==">an uncle</a> that lived in <a href="/wiki/Fiji" title="Fiji">Fiji</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-FBC7_3-12" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-FBC7-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Eustace_Fawley?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Eustace_Fawley?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Eustace is the English form of <i>Eustathios</i>, derived from the Greek word <i>eustathes </i>meaning "well-built, stable".
</p><p>Fawley is a name derived from the Old English word <i>fugelere</i> which means "hunter of wild birds, fowler".
</p>
