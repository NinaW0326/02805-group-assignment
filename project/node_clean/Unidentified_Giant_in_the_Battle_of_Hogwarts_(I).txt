<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_Giant_in_the_Battle_of_Hogwarts_(I)?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_Giant_in_the_Battle_of_Hogwarts_(I)?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Along with the rest of the <a href="/wiki/Giant_colony" title="Giant colony">Giant colony</a>, he fought in the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a> on <a href="/wiki/2_May" title="2 May">2 May</a>, <a href="/wiki/1998" title="1998">1998</a>. During the bloody and violent battle, he destroyed the <a href="/wiki/Suit_of_armour" title="Suit of armour">suits of armour</a> with one of the <a href="/wiki/Quidditch" title="Quidditch">Quidditch</a> goal hoops.<sup id="cite_ref-DHF2_1-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DHF2-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> His fate after the battle is unknown, although it is possible that he was killed during the battle. 
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_Giant_in_the_Battle_of_Hogwarts_(I)?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_Giant_in_the_Battle_of_Hogwarts_(I)?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>This giant is played by <a href="/wiki/Philip_Wright" title="Philip Wright">Philip Wright</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Deathly_Hallows:_Part_2" title="Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 2">film adaptation</a> of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Deathly_Hallows" title="Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows">Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows</a></i>.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li>In <i><a href="/wiki/LEGO_Harry_Potter:_Years_5-7" title="LEGO Harry Potter: Years 5-7">LEGO Harry Potter: Years 5-7</a></i> he also appears with a <a href="/wiki/Quidditch" title="Quidditch">Quidditch</a> goal hoop, but instead he stands in front of an entrance and blocks it. The trio uses a phonogragh to make him dance then kill him with a <a href="/wiki/Portable_Swamp" title="Portable Swamp">Portable Swamp</a> in a <a class="new" title="Weasley box (page does not exist)" rel="nofollow" data-uncrawlable-url="L3dpa2kvV2Vhc2xleV9ib3g/YWN0aW9uPWVkaXQmcmVkbGluaz0x">Weasley box</a>.</li></ul>
