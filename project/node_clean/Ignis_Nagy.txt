<p><b>Ignis Nagy</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Hungary" title="Hungary">Hungarian</a> <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch</a> who had dedicated her life to <a href="/wiki/Dragonologist" title="Dragonologist">the study of dragons</a>, having authored at least <a href="/wiki/From_Egg_to_Inferno:_a_Dragon-Keeper%27s_Guide" title="From Egg to Inferno: a Dragon-Keeper&#39;s Guide">one book</a> on the subject.<sup id="cite_ref-WUDragon_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-WUDragon-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> A controversial figure, as a scholar, she was noted for her keen interest in the elemental force of <a href="/wiki/Fire" title="Fire">fire</a>, often studying it and its magical qualities up close.<sup id="cite_ref-WUFire_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-WUFire-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Ignis_Nagy?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Ignis_Nagy?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>In the <a href="/wiki/2010s" title="2010s">2010s</a>, Ignis Nagy encountered and spoke with members of the <a href="/wiki/Statute_of_Secrecy_Task_Force" title="Statute of Secrecy Task Force">Statute of Secrecy Task Force</a>, who were pursuing the careers of <a href="/wiki/Auror" title="Auror">Auror</a> and <a href="/wiki/Magizoologist" title="Magizoologist">Magizoologist</a> respectively. With the former, she helped the individual in question to embrace the study of flames, and with the latter, she had a brief chat on the subject that inspired said person to re-read her work, and both of these meetings ended up being useful in their field work.<sup id="cite_ref-WUDragon_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-WUDragon-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-WUFire_2-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-WUFire-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Magical_abilities_and_skills">Magical abilities and skills</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Ignis_Nagy?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Ignis_Nagy?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li><b><a href="/wiki/Magizoology" title="Magizoology">Magizoology</a></b>: As a professional Dragonologist, she was highly knowledgeable about <a href="/wiki/Dragon" title="Dragon">dragons</a>. She was known to have authored <a href="/wiki/From_Egg_to_Inferno:_a_Dragon-Keeper%27s_Guide" title="From Egg to Inferno: a Dragon-Keeper&#39;s Guide">one book</a> on the subject, showing her expertise in her field.<sup id="cite_ref-WUDragon_1-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-WUDragon-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li><b>Knowledge of Elemental <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">Magic</a></b>: Nagy was an expert on the magical properties of <a href="/wiki/Fire" title="Fire">fire</a>, with her excellent knowledge of this field allowing her to teach <a href="/wiki/Auror" title="Auror">Auror</a> of the <a href="/wiki/Statute_of_Secrecy_Task_Force" title="Statute of Secrecy Task Force">Statute of Secrecy Task Force</a> how to withstand flames, and increase their stamina.<sup id="cite_ref-WUFire_2-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-WUFire-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup></li></ul>
