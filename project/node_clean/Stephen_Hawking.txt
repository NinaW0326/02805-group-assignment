</p><p><b>Stephen William Hawking</b> (<a href="/wiki/8_January" title="8 January">8 January</a> <a href="/wiki/1942" title="1942">1942</a> — <a href="/wiki/14_March" title="14 March">14 March</a> <a href="/wiki/2018" title="2018">2018</a>) was an <a href="/wiki/England" title="England">English</a> <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggle</a> theoretical physicist internationally considered the best since <a href="/wiki/Albert_Einstein" title="Albert Einstein">Albert Einstein</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-poa-film_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-poa-film-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> He died on the 14th of March 2018.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Stephen_Hawking?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Stephen_Hawking?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>When <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> arrived at <a href="/wiki/Leaky_Cauldron" title="Leaky Cauldron">The Leaky Cauldron</a> after fleeing the <a href="/wiki/Dursley_family" title="Dursley family">Dursley</a> <a href="/wiki/4_Privet_Drive" title="4 Privet Drive">house</a> in <a href="/wiki/1993" title="1993">1993</a>, there was a <a href="/wiki/Wizard_in_the_Leaky_Cauldron" title="Wizard in the Leaky Cauldron">wizard</a> sitting at a table reading <i><a href="/wiki/A_Brief_History_of_Time" title="A Brief History of Time">A Brief History of Time</a></i>, a best-selling popular science book written by Hawking.<sup id="cite_ref-poa-film_2-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-poa-film-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>At the time of this book's publication, Hawking had been living with <a href="/wiki/Lou_Gehrig%27s_disease" title="Lou Gehrig&#39;s disease">Lou Gehrig's disease</a> for over twenty years, requiring him to use a <a href="/wiki/Wheelchair" title="Wheelchair">wheelchair</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-poa-film_2-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-poa-film-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Coincidentally, Hawking died on the same date (14 March) as Albert Einstein was born, and at the same age (76) at which Einstein died.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Stephen_Hawking?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Stephen_Hawking?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The name "Stephen" comes from the Greek name Στεφανος (Stephanos) meaning "crown".
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Stephen_Hawking?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Stephen_Hawking?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>Stephen Hawking was portrayed by <a href="/wiki/Eddie_Redmayne" title="Eddie Redmayne">Eddie Redmayne</a>, who portrayed <a href="/wiki/Newton_Scamander" title="Newton Scamander">Newt Scamander</a> in the film <i><a href="/wiki/Fantastic_Beasts_and_Where_to_Find_Them_(film)" title="Fantastic Beasts and Where to Find Them (film)">Fantastic Beasts and Where to Find Them</a></i>, in the 2014 film <i>The Theory of Everything</i>. For his performance, Redmayne won the <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Academy_Award_for_Best_Actor" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Academy Award for Best Actor">Academy Award for Best Actor</a>.</li>
<li>Stephen Hawking was also played by <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Benedict_Cumberbatch" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Benedict Cumberbatch">Benedict Cumberbatch</a> in the 2004 BBC television film <i><a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hawking_(2004_film)" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Hawking (2004 film)">Hawking</a></i>. Cumberbatch voiced a spoof of <a href="/wiki/Alan_Rickman" title="Alan Rickman">Alan Rickman</a>'s portrayal of <a href="/wiki/Severus_Snape" title="Severus Snape">Severus Snape</a> in an episode of <i><a href="/wiki/The_Simpsons" title="The Simpsons">The Simpsons</a></i>.</li></ul>
