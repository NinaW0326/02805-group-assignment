This <b><a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch</a></b> was a <a href="/wiki/Snatchers" title="Snatchers">Snatcher</a> who fought in the <a href="/wiki/Second_Wizarding_War" title="Second Wizarding War">Second Wizarding War</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a> for <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a>. She eventually died at the <a href="/wiki/Skirmish_at_the_Wooden_Bridge" title="Skirmish at the Wooden Bridge">Skirmish at the Covered Bridge</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-DHF2_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DHF2-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_female_Snatcher_during_the_Battle_of_Hogwarts?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_female_Snatcher_during_the_Battle_of_Hogwarts?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_life">Early life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_female_Snatcher_during_the_Battle_of_Hogwarts?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Early life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_female_Snatcher_during_the_Battle_of_Hogwarts?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Early life"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>The woman was born sometime in or before <a href="/wiki/1980" title="1980">1980</a>, and was at least somewhat learned in the magical arts. Sometime from <a href="/wiki/1997-1998_school_year" class="mw-redirect" title="1997-1998 school year">1997-1998</a> she became a <a href="/wiki/Snatchers" title="Snatchers">Snatcher</a> for the <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">Voldemort-controlled Ministry of Magic</a>, capturing <a href="/wiki/Muggle-born" title="Muggle-born">Muggle-borns</a> for a reward.<sup id="cite_ref-DHF2_3-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DHF2-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Battle_and_death">Battle and death</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_female_Snatcher_during_the_Battle_of_Hogwarts?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Battle and death">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_female_Snatcher_during_the_Battle_of_Hogwarts?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Battle and death"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>On <a href="/wiki/2_May" title="2 May">2 May</a>, <a href="/wiki/1998" title="1998">1998</a>, the woman participated in the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a> with her fellow Snatchers, presumably casting an unidentified blue spell at the shield along with the others. She died at the <a href="/wiki/Skirmish_at_the_Wooden_Bridge" title="Skirmish at the Wooden Bridge">Skirmish at the Covered Bridge</a>, and hence was technically killed by <a href="/wiki/Neville_Longbottom" title="Neville Longbottom">Neville Longbottom</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-DHF2_3-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DHF2-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
