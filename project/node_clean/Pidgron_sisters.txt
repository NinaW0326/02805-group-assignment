The <b>Pidgron sisters</b> were three <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witches</a> who had "quite a bit of padding in the bottom department".<sup><sup id="cite_ref-EveningProphet_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-EveningProphet-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup></sup> They lived in Britain in the <a href="/wiki/1990s" title="1990s">1990s</a>.
</p>

<ul><li>On <i><a href="/wiki/Evening_Prophet" title="Evening Prophet">Evening Prophet</a></i> prop featured in the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Chamber_of_Secrets_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets (film)">film adaptation</a> of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Chamber_of_Secrets" title="Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets">Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets</a></i>, the Pidgron sisters are mentioned to have been involved in a broom accident caused by <a href="/wiki/Armando_Dippet" title="Armando Dippet">Armando Dippet</a> while <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> and <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ronald Weasley</a> flew a <a href="/wiki/Flying_Ford_Anglia" title="Flying Ford Anglia">Ford Anglia</a> to <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Castle" title="Hogwarts Castle">Hogwarts Castle</a> (a breach of the <a href="/wiki/International_Statute_of_Wizarding_Secrecy" title="International Statute of Wizarding Secrecy">International Statute of Wizarding Secrecy</a>): when elderly <a href="/wiki/Armando_Dippet" title="Armando Dippet">Armando Dippet</a> dropped some bread from his broomstick, he lost his focus and accidentally crashed into <a href="/wiki/Felicitania_Tugwood" title="Felicitania Tugwood">Felicitania Tugwood</a> who, in turn, managed to fall on the broomsticks of the Pidgron sisters, who were knocked off their brooms. While Tugwood fell into the <a href="/wiki/River_Mersey" title="River Mersey">Mersey</a>, the sisters fell on something soft and this, combined with the fact that had "quite a bit of padding in the bottom department", prevented them from having any injuries.<sup><sup id="cite_ref-EveningProphet_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-EveningProphet-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup></sup></li>
<li>This, however, cannot be considered <a href="/wiki/Canon" title="Canon">canon</a>, since Rowling's statements and books imply that Dippet has been dead since the <a href="/wiki/1950s" title="1950s">1950s</a>.</li></ul>
