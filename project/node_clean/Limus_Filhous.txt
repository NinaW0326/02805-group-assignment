<b>Limus Filhous</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> who worked as a reporter and columnist for the <i><a href="/wiki/Daily_Prophet" title="Daily Prophet">Daily Prophet</a></i>. In <a href="/wiki/1927" title="1927">1927</a>, Limus wrote an editorial entitled "<i>Grindewald Escape: No conclusions yet pledge the <a href="/wiki/International_Confederation_of_Wizards" title="International Confederation of Wizards">I.C.W.</a></i>".<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
