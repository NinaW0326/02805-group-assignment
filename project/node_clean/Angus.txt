<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Angus?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Angus?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>He was seen outside <a href="/wiki/Quality_Quidditch_Supplies" title="Quality Quidditch Supplies">Quality Quidditch Supplies</a> in <a href="/wiki/Diagon_Alley" title="Diagon Alley">Diagon Alley</a> on <a href="/wiki/31_July" title="31 July">31 July</a>, <a href="/wiki/1991" title="1991">1991</a>, commenting on a <a href="/wiki/Nimbus_2000" title="Nimbus 2000">Nimbus 2000</a> displayed in the window.<sup id="cite_ref-PS5_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PS5-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> The <a href="/wiki/1992" title="1992">following year</a>, he was seen at <a href="/wiki/Flourish_and_Blotts" title="Flourish and Blotts">Flourish and Blotts</a>, on the day that <a href="/wiki/Gilderoy_Lockhart" title="Gilderoy Lockhart">Gilderoy Lockhart</a> appeared there to sign copies of his new book, <i><a href="/wiki/Magical_Me" title="Magical Me">Magical Me</a></i>.<sup id="cite_ref-COSF_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COSF-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Angus?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Angus?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>Angus was portrayed by <a href="/wiki/Ben_Borowiecki" title="Ben Borowiecki">Ben Borowiecki</a> in the first two <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_films" class="mw-redirect" title="Harry Potter films"><i>Harry Potter</i> films</a>. He is credited as "Diagon Alley Boy" in <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Philosopher%27s_Stone_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Philosopher&#39;s Stone (film)">Philosopher's Stone</a></i> and "Angus (Diagon Boy)" in <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Chamber_of_Secrets_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets (film)">Chamber of Secrets</a></i>. Though the character appears in the novel <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Philosopher%27s_Stone" title="Harry Potter and the Philosopher&#39;s Stone">Harry Potter and the Philosopher's Stone</a></i>, his name is not given, and he is described as simply "one" of a group of "boys of about Harry's age."</li>
<li>In <i><a href="/wiki/LEGO_Harry_Potter:_Years_1-4" title="LEGO Harry Potter: Years 1-4">LEGO Harry Potter: Years 1-4</a></i>, he is absent but due to the character "Boy" appearing Angus may appear generic.</li></ul>
