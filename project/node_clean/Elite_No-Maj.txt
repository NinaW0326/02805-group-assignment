<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Elite_No-Maj?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Elite_No-Maj?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>This individual was one of the attendees of <a href="/wiki/Henry_Shaw_Junior" title="Henry Shaw Junior">Henry Shaw Junior</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Fundraising_Dinner_for_the_Re-election_of_Senator_Henry_Shaw" title="Fundraising Dinner for the Re-election of Senator Henry Shaw">Fundraising Dinner</a> on the <a href="/wiki/6_December" title="6 December">6 December</a>, <a href="/wiki/1926" title="1926">1926</a>. During the dinner, he witnessed the sudden and horrific death of Henry Shaw Junior at the hands of <a href="/wiki/Credence_Barebone" title="Credence Barebone">Credence Barebone</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Obscurial" title="Obscurial">Obscurial</a> form, in which he and all of the No-Majs present witnessed <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magic</a> with their own eyes.<sup id="cite_ref-FBF_1-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-FBF-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
