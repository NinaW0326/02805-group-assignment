<b>Tristan</b> was an ancient <a href="/wiki/Celts" title="Celts">Celtic</a> hero who tried to kill the <a href="/wiki/Giant" title="Giant">giant</a> <a href="/wiki/Morholt" title="Morholt">Morholt</a>. He was wounded with a <a href="/wiki/Poison" title="Poison">poisoned</a> sword by the giant in the process.
