<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Mopsus_(antiquity)?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Mopsus_(antiquity)?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Mopsus was born sometime in Ancient <a href="/wiki/Greece" title="Greece">Greece</a>. He was famous for having defeated fellow Seer <a href="/wiki/Calchas" title="Calchas">Calchas</a> in a competition of their powers. For his fame he was commemorated on a <a href="/wiki/Silver" title="Silver">silver</a> <a href="/wiki/Chocolate_Frog_Card" title="Chocolate Frog Card">Chocolate Frog Card</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-PSG_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PSG-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-POAG_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-POAG-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-COSG_1-10" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COSG-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>There was a <a href="/wiki/Portrait" title="Portrait">portrait</a> of Mopsus hung in the <a href="/wiki/Dungeons" title="Dungeons">Dungeons</a> of <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Castle" title="Hogwarts Castle">Hogwarts Castle</a>. It hid a secret chamber, and the <a href="/wiki/Password" title="Password">password</a> was "<i>Forsooth</i>".<sup id="cite_ref-POAG_3-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-POAG-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Magical_abilities_and_skills">Magical abilities and skills</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Mopsus_(antiquity)?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Mopsus_(antiquity)?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li><b><a href="/wiki/Divination" title="Divination">Divination</a></b>: Mopsus was a natural born <a href="/wiki/Seer" title="Seer">Seer</a>, and thus possessed the <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magical</a> ability to view the future in <a href="/wiki/Vision" title="Vision">visions</a>. His talents in Divination were shown to be extraordinary, given how he managed to defeat the rival Seer <a href="/wiki/Calchas" title="Calchas">Calchas</a> with his superior precognitive powers, which awarded him his legendary status in wizarding history.<sup id="cite_ref-COSG_1-11" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COSG-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup></li></ul>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Mopsus_(antiquity)?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Mopsus_(antiquity)?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>Mopsus in Greek mythology was a seer and diviner in the court of Amphilochus. He correctly predicted that an upcoming battle would end in great defeat, to the contrary of rival seer Calchas's prediction that it would end in great victory. He later died in a quarrel with Amphilochus over the right to the throne.<sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup></li></ul>
