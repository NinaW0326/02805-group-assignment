<b>Cordelia Misericordia</b> (<a href="/wiki/1298" class="mw-redirect" title="1298">1298</a>-<a href="/wiki/1401" class="mw-redirect" title="1401">1401</a>) was a <a href="/wiki/Great_Britain" title="Great Britain">British</a> <a href="/wiki/Hag" title="Hag">hag</a> who lived in the <a href="/wiki/Middle_Ages" title="Middle Ages">Middle Ages</a>. She represented the <a href="/wiki/Hag" title="Hag">hags</a> at a fourteenth century summit of the <a href="/wiki/Wizards%27_Council" title="Wizards&#39; Council">Wizards' Council</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-POAG_1-10" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-POAG-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
