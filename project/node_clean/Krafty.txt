<p><b>Krafty</b> was a <a href="/wiki/House-elf" title="House-elf">house-elf</a> that worked at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> in the <a href="/wiki/1980s" title="1980s">1980s</a>. They worked in the <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_kitchens" title="Hogwarts kitchens">kitchens</a> along with the other <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_house-elves" title="Hogwarts house-elves">Hogwarts house-elves</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-HM_1-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HM-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
