<p><b>Eldon Pembroke</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Hufflepuff" title="Hufflepuff">Hufflepuff</a> <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">student</a> at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> in the <a href="/wiki/1990s" title="1990s">1990s</a>. During the <a href="/wiki/1996-1997_school_year" class="mw-redirect" title="1996-1997 school year">1996-1997 school year</a>, he could usually be found in the <a href="/wiki/Charms_Classroom" title="Charms Classroom">Charms Classroom</a>, and enjoyed playing <a href="/wiki/Exploding_Snap" title="Exploding Snap">Exploding Snap</a> with other students.
</p>
