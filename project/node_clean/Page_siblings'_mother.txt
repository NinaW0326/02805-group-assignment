An <b>unknown woman</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch</a> who married <a href="/wiki/Page_siblings%27_father" title="Page siblings&#39; father">a Muggle</a> and had three children. They had a <a href="/wiki/Daniel_Page%27s_sister" title="Daniel Page&#39;s sister">daughter</a> and two sons, a <a href="/wiki/Squib" title="Squib">Squib</a>, <a href="/wiki/Reggie_Page" title="Reggie Page">Reggie</a> and a younger son and <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a>, <a href="/wiki/Daniel_Page" title="Daniel Page">Daniel</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-MA_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-MA-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Page_siblings%27_mother?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Page_siblings%27_mother?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Her husband was involved in illicit activities, and he involved their elder son in it. They were eventually caught by Muggle police, and she tried to use her magic to help the two escape. She frightened the police and violated the <a href="/wiki/International_Statute_of_Wizarding_Secrecy" title="International Statute of Wizarding Secrecy">International Statute of Secrecy</a> in doing so.<sup id="cite_ref-MA_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-MA-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Her efforts did not help, and Mr Page and their son were taken to a Muggle prison. Because she violated wizarding law, she was put in the custody of the <a href="/wiki/Department_of_Magical_Law_Enforcement" title="Department of Magical Law Enforcement">Department of Magical Law Enforcement</a>. At her trial in a wizarding court of law, she was accused and found guilty of being a member of the <a href="/wiki/Nocturnal_Order_of_Tricks_and_Exhibitions" title="Nocturnal Order of Tricks and Exhibitions">Nocturnal Order of Tricks and Exhibitions</a>, and sentenced to <a href="/wiki/Azkaban" title="Azkaban">Azkaban</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-MA_1-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-MA-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<figure class="thumb tright show-info-icon" style="width: 222px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/d/de/The_Bloody_Pages.png/revision/latest?cb=20200604125037" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/d/de/The_Bloody_Pages.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/220?cb=20200604125037" decoding="async" width="220" height="101" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="The Bloody Pages.png" data-image-key="The_Bloody_Pages.png" /></a> 	 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:The_Bloody_Pages.png" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption">A Muggle newspaper reporting the arrest of her family</p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure>
<p>It is unknown if she was involved in the organisation, because anyone who used magic openly was suspected of being so. The incident was put in a Muggle newspaper under the heading, "The Bloody Pages". Their younger son, Daniel was put under the guardianship of their daughter.<sup id="cite_ref-MA_1-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-MA-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
