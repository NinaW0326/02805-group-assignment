<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Laurena_Kama?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Laurena_Kama?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Hailing from a <a href="/wiki/Pure-blood" title="Pure-blood">pure-blood</a> family and happily married to the <a href="/wiki/Senegal" title="Senegal">Senegalese</a> pure-blood <a href="/wiki/Mustafa_Kama" title="Mustafa Kama">Mustafa Kama</a>, Laurena was put under the <a href="/wiki/Imperius_Curse" title="Imperius Curse">Imperius Curse</a> by <a href="/wiki/Corvus_Lestrange_(IV)" title="Corvus Lestrange (IV)">Corvus Lestrange</a> and forcibly had a daughter with him. Laurena died after giving birth to <a href="/wiki/Leta_Lestrange" title="Leta Lestrange">Leta Lestrange</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-s101_2-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-s101-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Laurena_Kama?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Laurena_Kama?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>Laurena Kama is played by <a href="/wiki/Isaura_Barb%C3%A9-Brown" title="Isaura Barbé-Brown">Isaura Barbé-Brown</a> in <i><a href="/wiki/Fantastic_Beasts:_The_Crimes_of_Grindelwald" title="Fantastic Beasts: The Crimes of Grindelwald">Fantastic Beasts: The Crimes of Grindelwald</a></i>.<sup id="cite_ref-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li>Though her marriage to Corvus was not directly stated, it is strongly implied by Yusuf's words that Corvus "remarried not three months after her death".<sup id="cite_ref-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li>Her second marriage can be seen as similar to that of <a href="/wiki/Merope_Riddle" title="Merope Riddle">Merope Riddle</a> and <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle_Senior" title="Tom Riddle Senior">Tom Riddle Snr</a>. Both Laurena and Riddle Senior were forced into marriage and did not rear the resultant children. Riddle Senior abandoned his son, <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Tom Riddle</a>, whereas Laurena died giving birth, similar to Merope.</li>
<li>It is also noteworthy that Corvus used the Imperius Curse as opposed to a love potion as to prevent any ill effects on his daughter.</li></ul>
