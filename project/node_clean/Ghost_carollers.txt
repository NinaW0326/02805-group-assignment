<p>The <b>ghost carollers</b> were a choir of <a href="/wiki/Ghost" title="Ghost">ghosts</a> at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> that sang Christmas carols at winter. Among their members was <a href="/wiki/Lord_Draben" title="Lord Draben">Lord Draben</a>. They sang <i><a href="/wiki/Christmas_at_Hogwarts" title="Christmas at Hogwarts">Christmas at Hogwarts</a></i> in the <a href="/wiki/Entrance_Hall" title="Entrance Hall">Entrance Hall</a> in <a href="/wiki/December" title="December">December</a>, <a href="/wiki/1991" title="1991">1991</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-PSF_1-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PSF-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
