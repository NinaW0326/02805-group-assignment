<b>Franziska Faust</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Germany" title="Germany">German</a> <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch</a> and the manager of the <a href="/wiki/German_National_Quidditch_team" title="German National Quidditch team">German National Quidditch team</a> in <a href="/wiki/2014" title="2014">2014</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-PM_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PM-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Franziska_Faust?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Franziska_Faust?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>On <a href="/wiki/22_May" title="22 May">22 May</a>, <a href="/wiki/2014" title="2014">2014</a>, during the final match of the first round of the <a href="/wiki/2014_Quidditch_World_Cup" title="2014 Quidditch World Cup">427th Quidditch World Cup</a> between <a href="/wiki/Welsh_National_Quidditch_team" title="Welsh National Quidditch team">Wales</a> and <a href="/wiki/German_National_Quidditch_team" title="German National Quidditch team">Germany</a>, German <a href="/wiki/Seeker" title="Seeker">Seeker</a> <a href="/wiki/Thorsten_Pfeffer" title="Thorsten Pfeffer">Thorsten Pfeffer</a> nearly died after failing an attempt at the life-threatening <a href="/wiki/Wronski_Feint" title="Wronski Feint">Wronski Feint</a>. Pfeffer collided with the ground at what onlookers estimated to be sixty miles per hour, and his death was only averted by the quick action of the <a href="/wiki/Healer" title="Healer">Healers</a> present. Neither players nor crowd were in a celebratory mood, even as Wales won, and only once manager Franziska Faust revealed that Pfeffer would be likely to recover did Welsh manager Gwenog Jones tell the press she was "<i>bloody delighted</i>".<sup id="cite_ref-PM_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PM-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
