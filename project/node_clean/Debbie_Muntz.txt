<b>Debbie Muntz</b> was an <a href="/wiki/United_States_of_America" title="United States of America">American</a> <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch</a> who played as a <a href="/wiki/Chaser" title="Chaser">Chaser</a> on the <a href="/wiki/American_National_Quidditch_team" title="American National Quidditch team">American National Quidditch team</a> during the four-hundred and twenty-second <a href="/wiki/Quidditch_World_Cup" title="Quidditch World Cup">Quidditch World Cup</a>, in <a href="/wiki/1994" title="1994">1994</a>.
</p><p>During the <a href="/wiki/1996%E2%80%931997_school_year" title="1996–1997 school year">1996–1997 school year</a>, Muntz's <a href="/wiki/Chocolate_Frog_Card" title="Chocolate Frog Card">Chocolate Frog Card</a> could be won by beating <a href="/wiki/Poppy_Caxton" title="Poppy Caxton">Poppy Caxton</a> at a <a href="/wiki/Gobstones" title="Gobstones">Gobstones</a> challenge.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
