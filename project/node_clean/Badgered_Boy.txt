<p>This <b>individual</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> who attended <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> in the <a href="/wiki/1990s" title="1990s">1990s</a>. In <a href="/wiki/1992" title="1992">1992</a>, he was <a href="/wiki/Transfiguration" title="Transfiguration">transfigured</a> by <a href="/wiki/Unidentified_boy_who_transfigured_his_friend" title="Unidentified boy who transfigured his friend">his friend</a> into a <a href="/wiki/Badger" title="Badger">badger</a>. He was later seen, still with black and white striped hair.<sup id="cite_ref-COS11_3-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COS11-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
