<p><b>Rabastan Lestrange</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Pure-blood" title="Pure-blood">pure-blood</a> <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a>, brother of <a href="/wiki/Rodolphus_Lestrange" title="Rodolphus Lestrange">Rodolphus</a>, brother-in-law of <a href="/wiki/Bellatrix_Lestrange" title="Bellatrix Lestrange">Bellatrix</a> and possibly son of <a href="/wiki/Lestrange" title="Lestrange">Lestrange</a>.
</p><p>He was a <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eater</a> who fought in the <a href="/wiki/First_Wizarding_War" title="First Wizarding War">First Wizarding War</a>. The <a href="/wiki/Lestrange_family" title="Lestrange family">Lestranges</a> were among <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a>'s most loyal and trusted servants, the only ones who searched for him after his first downfall. After Voldemort's first downfall, Rabastan, Rodolphus, his wife <a href="/wiki/Bellatrix_Lestrange" title="Bellatrix Lestrange">Bellatrix</a> and <a href="/wiki/Bartemius_Crouch_Junior" title="Bartemius Crouch Junior">Barty Crouch Jnr</a> participated in the <a href="/wiki/Attack_on_Frank_and_Alice_Longbottom" title="Attack on Frank and Alice Longbottom">torture and permanent incapacitation</a> of <a href="/wiki/Alice_Longbottom" title="Alice Longbottom">Alice</a> and <a href="/wiki/Frank_Longbottom" title="Frank Longbottom">Frank Longbottom</a> and were sentenced to life imprisonment in <a href="/wiki/Azkaban" title="Azkaban">Azkaban</a>. Rabastan later escaped with a group of <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a> and fought in several battles of the <a href="/wiki/Second_Wizarding_War" title="Second Wizarding War">Second Wizarding War</a>, including the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_the_Department_of_Mysteries" title="Battle of the Department of Mysteries">Battle of the Department of Mysteries</a>, and possibly the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a>.
</p><p>After Lord Voldemort's final defeat, Rabastan was presumably killed or imprisoned in <a href="/wiki/Azkaban" title="Azkaban">Azkaban</a> once more.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Rabastan_Lestrange?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Rabastan_Lestrange?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_life">Early life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Rabastan_Lestrange?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Early life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Rabastan_Lestrange?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Early life"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Rabastan possibly attended <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> and was sorted into <a href="/wiki/Slytherin" title="Slytherin">Slytherin</a> house, like his brother Rodolphus. It is possible that he didn't attend Hogwarts at the same time as Rodolphus, as he was not mentioned among a small group of Slytherins and future <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a>, as Rodolphus and <a href="/wiki/Bellatrix_Lestrange" title="Bellatrix Lestrange">Bellatrix</a> were.
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="First_Wizarding_War">First Wizarding War</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Rabastan_Lestrange?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: First Wizarding War">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Rabastan_Lestrange?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: First Wizarding War"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>

<p>Rabastan served as one of <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a> during the <a href="/wiki/First_Wizarding_War" title="First Wizarding War">First Wizarding War</a>. He, along with <a href="/wiki/Rodolphus_Lestrange" title="Rodolphus Lestrange">Rodolphus</a> and <a href="/wiki/Bellatrix_Lestrange" title="Bellatrix Lestrange">Bellatrix</a>, were among Voldemort's most loyal servants.
</p><p>Soon after the conflict ended in late <a href="/wiki/1981" title="1981">1981</a>, Rabastan was put on trial with his brother <a href="/wiki/Rodolphus_Lestrange" title="Rodolphus Lestrange">Rodolphus</a>, sister-in-law <a href="/wiki/Bellatrix_Lestrange" title="Bellatrix Lestrange">Bellatrix</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Bartemius_Crouch_Junior" title="Bartemius Crouch Junior">Barty Crouch Jnr</a> for being a Death Eater and for participating in the <a href="/wiki/Cruciatus_Curse" title="Cruciatus Curse">torture</a> of <a href="/wiki/Alice_Longbottom" title="Alice Longbottom">Alice</a> and <a href="/wiki/Frank_Longbottom" title="Frank Longbottom">Frank Longbottom</a>. They were all sentenced to life imprisonment in <a href="/wiki/Azkaban" title="Azkaban">Azkaban</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-GOF30_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF30-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Second_Wizarding_War">Second Wizarding War</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Rabastan_Lestrange?veaction=edit&amp;section=4" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Second Wizarding War">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Rabastan_Lestrange?action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Second Wizarding War"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Escape_from_Azkaban">Escape from Azkaban</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Rabastan_Lestrange?veaction=edit&amp;section=5" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Escape from Azkaban">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Rabastan_Lestrange?action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Escape from Azkaban"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<p>Rabastan <a href="/wiki/Azkaban_escapes#1996" title="Azkaban escapes">escaped</a> Azkaban in <a href="/wiki/1996" title="1996">1996</a>, along with his brother, sister-in-law and several other Death Eaters with the <a href="/wiki/Dementor" title="Dementor">Dementors</a>' defection to Voldemort, who had returned.
</p>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Battle_of_the_Department_of_Mysteries">Battle of the Department of Mysteries</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Rabastan_Lestrange?veaction=edit&amp;section=6" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Battle of the Department of Mysteries">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Rabastan_Lestrange?action=edit&amp;section=6" title="Edit section: Battle of the Department of Mysteries"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<div class="quote">
<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix">"<i>We'll split into pairs and search, and don't forget, be gentle with Potter until we've got the prophecy, you can kill the others if necessary...</i>"</span></dd>
<dd>&#8212;<a href="/wiki/Lucius_Malfoy" title="Lucius Malfoy">Lucius Malfoy</a> gives orders to the group of Death Eaters<sup class="noprint"><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Order_of_the_Phoenix" title="Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix">[src]</a></sup></dd>
<dd></dd></dl></div><p>He later participated in the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_the_Department_of_Mysteries" title="Battle of the Department of Mysteries">Battle of the Department of Mysteries</a>. He went with fellow Death Eater <a href="/wiki/Crabbe" title="Crabbe">Crabbe Snr</a> to find six D.A. members, and went to the Time Room, where Harry, Hermione, and Neville, were hiding. He may have been the Death Eater whose head fell into the <a href="/wiki/Bell_jar" title="Bell jar">bell jar</a> and turned into a baby's head.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP35_4-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP35-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> He was presumably returned to Azkaban afterwards, though he was at large once again by late <a href="/wiki/1997" title="1997">1997</a> after another <a href="/wiki/Azkaban_escapes#1997" title="Azkaban escapes">Azkaban break-out</a>.
</p><p>Presumably, Rabastan participated in other battles in the <a href="/wiki/Second_Wizarding_War" title="Second Wizarding War">Second Wizarding War</a>, including the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a>. It is unknown what happened to Rabastan after Voldemort's death. He was either killed or imprisoned again in Azkaban.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Physical_appearance">Physical appearance</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Rabastan_Lestrange?veaction=edit&amp;section=7" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Physical appearance">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Rabastan_Lestrange?action=edit&amp;section=7" title="Edit section: Physical appearance"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Rabastan was described as a quite thin man. It is unknown if <a href="/wiki/Azkaban" title="Azkaban">Azkaban</a> took a toll on his appearance. As a Death Eater, he bore the <a href="/wiki/Dark_Mark" title="Dark Mark">Dark Mark</a> on his left inner forearm, which had faded to a mere scar upon Voldemort's death.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Personality_and_traits">Personality and traits</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Rabastan_Lestrange?veaction=edit&amp;section=8" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Personality and traits">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Rabastan_Lestrange?action=edit&amp;section=8" title="Edit section: Personality and traits"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Rabastan was a very loyal <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eater</a>, willing to use the <a href="/wiki/Dark_Arts" title="Dark Arts">Dark Arts</a> to commit atrocities for the sake of his master. He was not as brave or as calm as his brother as he is described as "nervous-looking" during his trial in 1981 with his eyes "darting around the crowd".
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Magical_abilities_and_skills">Magical abilities and skills</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Rabastan_Lestrange?veaction=edit&amp;section=9" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Rabastan_Lestrange?action=edit&amp;section=9" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li><b><a href="/wiki/Dark_Arts" title="Dark Arts">Dark Arts</a></b>: Being a Death Eater, Rabastan was highly proficient with dark magic and <a href="/wiki/Dark_charm" title="Dark charm">dark charms</a>, being a powerful <a href="/wiki/Dark_Wizard" title="Dark Wizard">Dark Wizard</a>. Rabastan could cast the <a href="/wiki/Cruciatus_Curse" title="Cruciatus Curse">Cruciatus Curse</a>, an exceptionally dark <a href="/wiki/Curse" title="Curse">curse</a> which requires power, ability and malice to perform, to devastating effect on <a href="/wiki/Alice_Longbottom" title="Alice Longbottom">Alice</a> and <a href="/wiki/Frank_Longbottom" title="Frank Longbottom">Frank Longbottom</a>, rendering them both insane from the torture.<sup id="cite_ref-GOF30_3-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF30-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Duelling" title="Duelling">Duelling</a></b>: Rabastan participated in the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_the_Department_of_Mysteries" title="Battle of the Department of Mysteries">Battle of the Department of Mysteries</a>, showing he was a competent duellist with considerable talent in <a href="/wiki/Martial_magic" title="Martial magic">martial magic</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP35_4-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP35-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> He likely participated in the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_the_Seven_Potters" title="Battle of the Seven Potters">Battle of the Seven Potters</a> and helped his brother duel <a href="/wiki/Nymphadora_Tonks" title="Nymphadora Tonks">Nymphadora Tonks</a>. If Rabastan fought in the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a> he most likely fled when the reinforcements arrived or was overwhelmed by them.</li></ul>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Rabastan_Lestrange?veaction=edit&amp;section=10" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Rabastan_Lestrange?action=edit&amp;section=10" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The name Rabastan may be a play on <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Rastaban" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Rastaban">Rastaban</a>, the traditional name of one of the stars of the constellation Draco. It is derived from an Arabic phrase "Rās al-Thu`bān (رأس الثعبان)" meaning "head of the serpent"; the snake is a symbol strongly connected with <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a> and <a href="/wiki/Slytherin" title="Slytherin">Slytherin</a>. The star is also known as <i>Alwaid</i>, meaning "who is to be destroyed" in Arabic.
</p><p>His name might otherwise be derived from the Germanic elements <i>hraban </i>("raven") and <i>stein</i> ("stone"), the latter of which became <i>stan </i>in Old English. The Germanic origins of <a href="/wiki/Rodolphus_Lestrange" title="Rodolphus Lestrange">his brother's</a> name might support this theory.
</p><p>The name "Lestrange" echoes the French phrase <i>l'étrange</i>, literally "the strange one." In Old French, <i>estrange</i> means "foreigner."<sup id="cite_ref-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> The surname may allude to English journalist <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Roger_L%27Estrange" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Roger L&#39;Estrange">Roger L'Estrange</a>, who was known for opposing religious toleration and for being involved in plots against the government of William and Mary, similar to the Lestranges' intolerance about <a href="/wiki/Blood_status" title="Blood status">blood purity</a> and involvement in the <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a> organisation.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Rabastan_Lestrange?veaction=edit&amp;section=11" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Rabastan_Lestrange?action=edit&amp;section=11" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>During the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_the_Department_of_Mysteries" title="Battle of the Department of Mysteries">Battle of the Department of Mysteries</a>, Rabastan was paired with fellow <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eater</a> <a href="/wiki/Crabbe" title="Crabbe">Crabbe</a> during the pursuit of the <a href="/wiki/Dumbledore%27s_Army" title="Dumbledore&#39;s Army">Dumbledore's Army</a> members. He and Crabbe pursued <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>, <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione Granger</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Neville_Longbottom" title="Neville Longbottom">Neville Longbottom</a> into the <a href="/wiki/Department_of_Mysteries#Time_Chamber" title="Department of Mysteries">Time Chamber</a>. It is unknown whether Rabastan was the Death Eater whom Harry <a href="/wiki/Stunning_Spell" title="Stunning Spell">stunned</a>, or the one whom Hermione knocked into a <a href="/wiki/Bell_jar" title="Bell jar">bell jar</a>, causing the head to continuously age and de-age. It is likely to be the former, as Rabastan is said to be thin while the baby-headed Death Eater's arms are said to be thick, and Rabastan likely would have been recognised and referred to by name in the book (as Dolohov was) if it was he who took his mask off.<sup id="cite_ref-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li>Interestingly, <a href="/wiki/Sirius_Black" title="Sirius Black">Sirius Black</a> does not mention him as a member of the <a href="/wiki/Gang_of_Slytherins" title="Gang of Slytherins">Gang of Slytherins</a> during his time at Hogwarts, though Rabastan's <a href="/wiki/Rodolphus_Lestrange" title="Rodolphus Lestrange">brother</a> and <a href="/wiki/Bellatrix_Black" class="mw-redirect" title="Bellatrix Black">sister-in-law</a> were. It's possible that he did not attend Hogwarts at the same time as his brother, though it's also likely that Sirius simply did not name the entire group.</li></ul>
