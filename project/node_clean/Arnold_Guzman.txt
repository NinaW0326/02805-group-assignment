<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Arnold_Guzman?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Arnold_Guzman?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>In 1927, Guzman attended a hearing at the <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">British Ministry of Magic</a>, being a part of a committee comprised of himself, the <a href="/wiki/Department_of_Magical_Law_Enforcement" title="Department of Magical Law Enforcement">Head of the Department of Magical Law Enforcement</a>, <a href="/wiki/Torquil_Travers" title="Torquil Travers">Torquil Travers</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Rudolph_Spielman" title="Rudolph Spielman">Rudolph Spielman</a> of the International Confederation Wizards. The subject of the hearing being over the possible appeal of <a href="/wiki/Newton_Scamander" title="Newton Scamander">Newt Scamander</a>'s international travel ban. Scamander's appeal was ultimately denied by Guzman and the other committee members.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Personality_and_traits">Personality and traits</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Arnold_Guzman?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Personality and traits">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Arnold_Guzman?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Personality and traits"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Arnold was a calm, but prim and very determined person, fearing the <a href="/wiki/Global_Wizarding_War" class="mw-redirect" title="Global Wizarding War">Global Wizarding War</a> that <a href="/wiki/Gellert_Grindelwald" title="Gellert Grindelwald">Gellert Grindelwald</a> had begun, as Arnold valued peace and diplomacy.
</p>
