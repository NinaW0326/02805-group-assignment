<p>A <b>Minister</b> was a title, used in both <a href="/wiki/Wizarding_world" title="Wizarding world">magical</a> and <a href="/wiki/Muggle_world" title="Muggle world">non-magical</a> contexts, by individuals holding high-ranking positions in government.
</p><p>In the case of wizarding Britain, it was most commonly used to refer to the <a href="/wiki/Minister_for_Magic" title="Minister for Magic">Minister for Magic</a>, the most senior official within the magical community's government structure, the <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">Ministry of Magic</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-PS5_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PS5-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-WW_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-WW-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> Alternatively, some senior members within the Ministry's Departments also held the title of Minister: <a href="/wiki/Hector_Podmore" title="Hector Podmore">Hector Podmore</a>, for example, held the title while British envoy at the <a href="/wiki/International_Confederation_of_Wizards" title="International Confederation of Wizards">International Confederation of Wizards</a>; in <a href="/wiki/1996" title="1996">1996</a>, a new <a href="/wiki/Unidentified_Minister_of_Magical_Transportation" title="Unidentified Minister of Magical Transportation">Minister of Magical Transportation</a> was appointed and he attended the <a href="/wiki/Slug_Club_Christmas_party" title="Slug Club Christmas party">Slug Club Christmas party</a>, where he was greeted by his former <a href="/wiki/Potions_Master" title="Potions Master">Potions Master</a>, <a href="/wiki/Horace_Slughorn" title="Horace Slughorn">Horace Slughorn</a>;<sup id="cite_ref-HBP15_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP15-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> during the <a href="/wiki/Calamity" title="Calamity">Calamity</a>, <a href="/wiki/Gareth_Greengrass" title="Gareth Greengrass">Gareth Greengrass</a> was a Senior <a href="/wiki/Unspeakable" title="Unspeakable">Unspeakable</a> that also had that title.<sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>In the early <a href="/wiki/1980s" title="1980s">1980s</a>, <a href="/wiki/Cornelius_Fudge" title="Cornelius Fudge">Cornelius Fudge</a> worked as <a href="/wiki/Junior_minister" title="Junior minister">Junior Minister</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Department_of_Magical_Accidents_and_Catastrophes" title="Department of Magical Accidents and Catastrophes">Department of Magical Accidents and Catastrophes</a>. <sup id="cite_ref-POA10_5-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-POA10-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p><br />
</p>
