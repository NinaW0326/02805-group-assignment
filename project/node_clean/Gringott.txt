<b>Gringott</b> (fl. <a href="/wiki/1474" class="mw-redirect" title="1474">1474</a>)<sup id="cite_ref-WWoHP_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-WWoHP-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> was a celebrated <a href="/wiki/Goblin" title="Goblin">goblin</a> who founded <a href="/wiki/Gringotts_Wizarding_Bank" title="Gringotts Wizarding Bank">Gringotts Wizarding Bank</a> in <a href="/wiki/1474" class="mw-redirect" title="1474">1474</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-WWoHP_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-WWoHP-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> For the Bank's founding and presumable management, Gringott was featured on a <a href="/wiki/Chocolate_Frog_Card" title="Chocolate Frog Card">Chocolate Frog Card</a>.
