<b>Arthur</b> was an eccentric <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> and <a href="/wiki/Hufflepuff" title="Hufflepuff">Hufflepuff</a> student who attended <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> in <a href="/wiki/1992" title="1992">1992</a>. He was the younger brother of <a href="/wiki/Marilyn" title="Marilyn">Marilyn</a> and collected beetle pincers like his sister, although he raved that his collection was much better than hers. He also owned a "smashing" collection of non-magical cricket balls.<sup id="cite_ref-COSG_2-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COSG-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Arthur and his sister were both described by other students as "a bit off."<sup id="cite_ref-COSG_2-10" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COSG-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Arthur_(Hufflepuff_student)?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Arthur_(Hufflepuff_student)?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The meaning of the name <i>Arthur</i> is unknown. It could be derived from British <i>art</i> "bear" combined with <i>viros</i> "man", or it could be related to Irish <i>art</i> "stone". Alternatively it could be related to an obscure Roman family name <i>Artorius</i>. Arthur is the name of the central character in Arthurian legend, a 6th-century king of the Britons who presided over the knights of the Round Table.
</p>
