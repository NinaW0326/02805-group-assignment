He was known to have escaped during one of the <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eater</a>-led raids at the height of <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Voldemort</a>â€™s reign, but otherwise, he was historically inconsequential.<sup id="cite_ref-FightingForces_1-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-FightingForces-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
