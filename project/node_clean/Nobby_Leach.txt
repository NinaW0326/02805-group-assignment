<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Nobby_Leach?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Nobby_Leach?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_life">Early life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Nobby_Leach?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Early life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Nobby_Leach?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Early life"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Leach was born into a <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggle</a> family in <a href="/wiki/Great_Britain" title="Great Britain">Great Britain</a>, sometime in or prior to <a href="/wiki/1945" title="1945">1945</a>. He was somehow trained in the <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magical</a> arts and eventually managed to become the <a href="/wiki/Minister_for_Magic" title="Minister for Magic">Minister for Magic</a> for Britain and <a href="/wiki/Ireland" title="Ireland">Ireland</a>, being elected at least once.
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="As_Minister_for_Magic">As Minister for Magic</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Nobby_Leach?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: As Minister for Magic">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Nobby_Leach?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: As Minister for Magic"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>In <a href="/wiki/1962" title="1962">1962</a>, Leach was the first <a href="/wiki/Muggle-born" title="Muggle-born">Muggle-born</a> to be appointed to the post of <a href="/wiki/Minister_for_Magic" title="Minister for Magic">Minister for Magic</a>. Several of the oldest members of the <a href="/wiki/Wizengamot" title="Wizengamot">Wizengamot</a> resigned government posts in protest of his appointment.<sup id="cite_ref-PM_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PM-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> Though Leach was believed to have played a part in England's 1966 World Cup Win, he denied these claims, stating that he had no part whatsoever.<sup id="cite_ref-PM_2-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PM-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> He left his post in <a href="/wiki/1968" title="1968">1968</a> due to a shady plot, of which <a href="/wiki/Abraxas_Malfoy" title="Abraxas Malfoy">Abraxas Malfoy</a> was believed part<sup id="cite_ref-pottermore_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-pottermore-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>, that resulted in his contracting a mysterious illness.<sup id="cite_ref-PM_2-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PM-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
