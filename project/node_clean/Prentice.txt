<p><b>Mr Prentice</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggle</a> who lived in <a href="/wiki/Little_Whinging" title="Little Whinging">Little Whinging</a> in the <a href="/wiki/1990s" title="1990s">1990s</a>.
</p><p>In the evening of <a href="/wiki/2_August" title="2 August">2 August</a>, <a href="/wiki/1995" title="1995">1995</a>, after <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> and <a href="/wiki/Dudley_Dursley" title="Dudley Dursley">Dudley Dursley</a> were <a href="/wiki/Attack_in_Little_Whinging" title="Attack in Little Whinging">attacked</a> by <a href="/wiki/Dementor" title="Dementor">Dementors</a>, <a href="/wiki/Arabella_Figg" title="Arabella Figg">Arabella Figg</a> told Harry, a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> to keep his wand out in case of another attack, not minding the presence of <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggle</a> Mr Prentice as they crossed <a href="/wiki/Wisteria_Walk" title="Wisteria Walk">Wisteria Walk</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-OP1_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OP1-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Prentice?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Prentice?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>When <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Order_of_the_Phoenix" title="Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix">Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix</a></i> was released, many thought Mr Prentice was, perhaps, another member of the <a href="/wiki/Order_of_the_Phoenix" title="Order of the Phoenix">Order</a> who, like <a href="/wiki/Arabella_Figg" title="Arabella Figg">Mrs Figg</a> was assigned to watch over Harry during the summer, given her lack of preoccupation of him spotting Harry's wand. <a href="/wiki/J._K._Rowling" title="J. K. Rowling">J. K. Rowling</a>, however, dispelled these rumours on her <a href="/wiki/J._K._Rowling%27s_official_site" title="J. K. Rowling&#39;s official site">J. K. Rowling's official site</a> by saying "<i>Mark Evans is... nobody. He's nobody in the sense that Mr Prentice, Madam Marsh and Gordon-Dudley's-gang-member are nobodies, just background people who need names, but who have no role other than the walk-on parts assigned to them</i>". It is, thus, more than likely that the risk of Mr Prentice noticing the wand was outweighed by the risk of encountering Dementors without a wand at the ready by Mrs Figg.</li></ul>
