<b>Professor Regulus Moonshine</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> who developed a <a href="/wiki/Potion" title="Potion">potion</a> to suppress <a href="/wiki/Hag" title="Hag">hags</a>' normal appetite for <a href="/wiki/Human" title="Human">human</a> <a href="/wiki/Flesh" title="Flesh">flesh</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-DP_2-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DP-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> He lost several chunks of own body while conducting the clinical trials.
</p><p>An article in the <i><a href="/wiki/Daily_Prophet" title="Daily Prophet">Daily Prophet</a></i> called him "Professor",<sup id="cite_ref-DP_2-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DP-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> but it is not known if he ever taught at a <a href="/wiki/Wizarding_school" title="Wizarding school">wizarding school</a>, like <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a>.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Regulus_Moonshine?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Regulus_Moonshine?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The name "Regulus" is a Roman cognomen which meant "rule" in Latin. This was the name of several early saints. Moonshine, also known as white lightning, mountain dew, hooch or Tennessee white whiskey, is a high-proof distilled spirit, generally produced illicitly.
</p>
