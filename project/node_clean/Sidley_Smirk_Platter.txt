<b>Sir Sidley Smirk Platter</b><sup id="cite_ref-LE_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-LE-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>, <small><a href="/wiki/Grand_Sorcerer" title="Grand Sorcerer">Grand Sorc.</a></small>, (born c. <a href="/wiki/1503" class="mw-redirect" title="1503">1503</a>) was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> who was appointed <a href="/wiki/Supreme_Mugwump" title="Supreme Mugwump">Supreme Mugwump</a> of the <a href="/wiki/International_Confederation_of_Wizards" title="International Confederation of Wizards">International Confederation of Wizards</a> sometime after <a href="/wiki/Pierre_Bonaccord" title="Pierre Bonaccord">Pierre Bonaccord</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-portrait_1-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-portrait-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Smirk Platter was depicted in a <a href="/wiki/Portrait" title="Portrait">portrait</a> that hung somewhere at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Castle" title="Hogwarts Castle">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-portrait_1-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-portrait-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> This portrait was painted in <a href="/wiki/1542" class="mw-redirect" title="1542">1542</a>, when he was 39 years old, as indicated by the inscription "Anno Etatis Suae 39"<sup id="cite_ref-portrait_1-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-portrait-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> (<i>anno aetatis suae</i> is a Latin term meaning "in the year of his age"<sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>).
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Sidley_Smirk_Platter?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Sidley_Smirk_Platter?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>This portrait was made specifically for the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_films" class="mw-redirect" title="Harry Potter films"><i>Harry Potter</i> films</a>, and was modelled after <a class="new" title="Matt Cooke (page does not exist)" rel="nofollow" data-uncrawlable-url="L3dpa2kvTWF0dF9Db29rZT9hY3Rpb249ZWRpdCZyZWRsaW5rPTE=">Matt Cooke</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> a member of the dressing props team since <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Prisoner_of_Azkaban_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban (film)">Prisoner of Azkaban</a></i>.</li>
<li>Despite bearing Matt Cooke's likeness, this portrait seems to have been based on a <a target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener" class="external text" href="http://www.elizabethan-portraits.com/HenryWriothesley2.jpg">portrait of Henry Wriothesley, 3rd Earl of Southampton (c. 1600)</a>, by an unknown artist.</li>
<li>Smirk Platter may have passed away sometime prior to the <a href="/wiki/1990s" title="1990s">1990s</a> as many portraits hung at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Castle" title="Hogwarts Castle">Hogwarts Castle</a> depict deceased individuals.</li></ul>
