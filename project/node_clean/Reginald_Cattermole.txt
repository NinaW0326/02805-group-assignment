<p><b>Reginald "Reg" Cattermole</b> (b. sun in <a href="/wiki/Libra" title="Libra">Libra</a>, <a href="/wiki/Moon" title="Moon">moon</a> in <a href="/wiki/Virgo" title="Virgo">Virgo</a><sup id="cite_ref-HPLE_1-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HPLE-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>) was a <a href="/wiki/Great_Britain" title="Great Britain">British</a> <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> employed at the <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">Ministry of Magic</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Magical_Maintenance_Department" title="Magical Maintenance Department">Magical Maintenance Department</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-DH12_3-12" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH12-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> He was married to <a href="/wiki/Mary_Cattermole" title="Mary Cattermole">Mary</a>, and the couple had three children, <a href="/wiki/Maisie_Cattermole" title="Maisie Cattermole">Maisie</a>, <a href="/wiki/Ellie_Cattermole" title="Ellie Cattermole">Ellie</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Alfred_Cattermole" title="Alfred Cattermole">Alfred</a>. He lived at <a href="/wiki/27_Chislehurst_Gardens" title="27 Chislehurst Gardens">27</a> <a href="/wiki/Chislehurst_Gardens" title="Chislehurst Gardens">Chislehurst Gardens</a>, <a href="/wiki/Great_Tolling" title="Great Tolling">Great Tolling</a>, <a href="/wiki/Evesham" title="Evesham">Evesham</a> with his wife and children.
</p>

<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_Life">Early Life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Reginald_Cattermole?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Early Life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Reginald_Cattermole?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Early Life"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Not much is known about Reginald's early life except that he began to work at the <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">British Ministry of Magic</a> as part of the <a href="/wiki/Magical_Maintenance_Department" title="Magical Maintenance Department">Magical Maintenance Department</a> in his adulthood.
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Infiltration_of_the_Ministry">Infiltration of the Ministry</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Reginald_Cattermole?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Infiltration of the Ministry">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Reginald_Cattermole?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Infiltration of the Ministry"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>

<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows">"<i>You realise that I am on my way downstairs to interrogate your wife, Cattermole? <p>On <a href="/wiki/2_September" title="2 September">2 September</a>, <a href="/wiki/1997" title="1997">1997</a>, Reginald headed for the Ministry not just for work, but for his <a href="/wiki/Muggle-born" title="Muggle-born">Muggle-born</a> wife <a href="/wiki/Mary_Cattermole" title="Mary Cattermole">Mary Cattermole</a> who was to be tried by the <a href="/wiki/Muggle-Born_Registration_Commission" title="Muggle-Born Registration Commission">Muggle-Born Registration Commission</a>, as the Ministry had been <a href="/wiki/Fall_of_the_Ministry_of_Magic" title="Fall of the Ministry of Magic">taken over</a> by <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a> and Muggle-borns were being persecuted. Outside, Reginald met <a href="/wiki/Mafalda_Hopkirk" title="Mafalda Hopkirk">Mafalda Hopkirk</a>, who was actually <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione Granger</a> disguised by use of <a href="/wiki/Polyjuice_Potion" title="Polyjuice Potion">Polyjuice Potion</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-DH12_3-13" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH12-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>She gave him a <a href="/wiki/Puking_Pastille" title="Puking Pastille">Puking Pastille</a>, and when he started to vomit, she took some of his <a href="/wiki/Human_hair" title="Human hair">hair</a>. Reginald still tried hard to get into the Ministry, desperate to be by his wife's side, despite repeatedly vomiting and being unable to walk straight, but Hermione advised him to go to <a href="/wiki/St_Mungo%27s_Hospital_for_Magical_Maladies_and_Injuries" title="St Mungo&#39;s Hospital for Magical Maladies and Injuries">St Mungo's</a>. After being unable to stand any longer, he finally <a href="/wiki/Apparition" title="Apparition">Disapparated</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-DH12_3-14" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH12-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<p>This was all part of Hermione Granger, <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ron Weasley</a>'s plan to <a href="/wiki/Infiltration_of_the_Ministry_of_Magic" title="Infiltration of the Ministry of Magic">access the Ministry undercover</a>. Hermione transformed into Mafalda Hopkirk, Ron used Reginald's hair and Polyjuice Potion to take on his form, and after Harry took on the form of <a href="/wiki/Albert_Runcorn" title="Albert Runcorn">Albert Runcorn</a>, the three headed into the Ministry.<sup id="cite_ref-DH12_3-15" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH12-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Reginald returned to the Ministry, pale but no longer ill, just in time to witness Ron, Harry, and Hermione assisting in the escape of <a href="/wiki/Muggle-born" title="Muggle-born">Muggle-borns</a> who had been detained by the <a href="/wiki/Muggle-Born_Registration_Commission" title="Muggle-Born Registration Commission">Muggle-Born Registration Commission</a>, including his wife. Harry advised them to take their children and leave the country while <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a> was still in power.<sup id="cite_ref-DH13_4-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH13-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Later_life">Later life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Reginald_Cattermole?veaction=edit&amp;section=4" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Later life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Reginald_Cattermole?action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Later life"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>It is unknown if Reginald and Mary took Harry's advice, or what happened to them after the <a href="/wiki/Second_Wizarding_War" title="Second Wizarding War">Second Wizarding War</a> ended. However, Ron was dubious they escaped as he got the impression Reginald was not quick-witted when he impersonated him.<sup id="cite_ref-DH14_6-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH14-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Physical_description">Physical description</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Reginald_Cattermole?veaction=edit&amp;section=5" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Physical description">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Reginald_Cattermole?action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Physical description"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Reginald was described as being a small, "<a href="/wiki/Ferret" title="Ferret">ferretty</a>" individual.<sup id="cite_ref-DH12_3-16" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH12-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> 
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Magical_abilities_and_skills">Magical abilities and skills</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Reginald_Cattermole?veaction=edit&amp;section=6" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Reginald_Cattermole?action=edit&amp;section=6" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li><b><a href="/wiki/Apparition" title="Apparition">Apparition</a></b>: Like most accomplished adult wizards, Reginald was capable of apparating at will.<sup id="cite_ref-DH12_3-17" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH12-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Charm" title="Charm">Charms</a></b>: Reginald was capable of producing a non-corporeal <a href="/wiki/Patronus_Charm" title="Patronus Charm">Patronus</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-LEGOY2_5-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-LEGOY2-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> a highly advanced and powerful protective charm.</li></ul>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Reginald_Cattermole?veaction=edit&amp;section=8" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Reginald_Cattermole?action=edit&amp;section=8" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>"Reginald" is from <i>Reginaldus</i>, which is a Latinized form of <i>Reynold</i>. It is also a cognate with the Old Norse Rögnvaldr, from which the name Ronald is derived.
</p>
