<b>Kenneth Towler</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> who began attending <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> in <a href="/wiki/1989" title="1989">1989</a>, and was <a href="/wiki/Sorting_ceremony" title="Sorting ceremony">sorted</a> into <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor" title="Gryffindor">Gryffindor</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-PM_3-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PM-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> In his <a href="/wiki/Fifth_year" title="Fifth year">fifth year</a>, <a href="/wiki/Fred_Weasley" title="Fred Weasley">Fred Weasley</a> put <a href="/wiki/Bulbadox_powder" title="Bulbadox powder">Bulbadox powder</a> in his pyjamas, causing his skin to break out in boils.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP12_2-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP12-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
