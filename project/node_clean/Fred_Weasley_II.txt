<b>Fred Weasley</b> (b. <a href="/wiki/2000s" title="2000s">2000s</a>) was the oldest child of <a href="/wiki/George_Weasley" title="George Weasley">George</a> and <a href="/wiki/Angelina_Johnson" title="Angelina Johnson">Angelina Weasley</a> <span class="template-c">(née Johnson)</span>. He was named in memory of his uncle <a href="/wiki/Fred_Weasley" title="Fred Weasley">Fred Weasley</a>, who was killed during the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a>. He had a younger sister named&#160;<a href="/wiki/Roxanne_Weasley" title="Roxanne Weasley">Roxanne</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-story_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-story-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Fred attended the <a href="/wiki/2014_Quidditch_World_Cup" title="2014 Quidditch World Cup">2014 Quidditch World Cup</a> with his family.<sup id="cite_ref-PM_4-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PM-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
