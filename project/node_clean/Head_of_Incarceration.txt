<ul><li>Given the fact that the police do in fact hold prisoners following arrest prior to imprisonment, and are involved in the transportation of prisoners, it is possible that Spielman worked for the <a href="/wiki/International_Wizarding_Police" title="International Wizarding Police">International Wizarding Police</a>.
<ul><li>If so, that would confirm the theory that the I.W.P. is tied to the <a href="/wiki/International_Confederation_of_Wizards" title="International Confederation of Wizards">International Confederation of Wizards</a>, since that's who Spielman is said to be working for.</li></ul></li></ul>
