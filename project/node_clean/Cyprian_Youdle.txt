<b>Cyprian Youdle</b> (<a href="/wiki/1312" class="mw-redirect" title="1312">1312</a>â€“<a href="/wiki/1357" class="mw-redirect" title="1357">1357</a>) was an <a href="/wiki/England" title="England">English</a> <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> and <a href="/wiki/Quidditch_referee" title="Quidditch referee">Quidditch referee</a> from <a href="/wiki/Norfolk" title="Norfolk">Norfolk</a>, the only known referee to have been killed during a <a href="/wiki/Quidditch" title="Quidditch">Quidditch</a> match, during a friendly <a href="/wiki/Quidditch" title="Quidditch">Quidditch</a> match between local <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizards</a> in <a href="/wiki/1357" class="mw-redirect" title="1357">1357</a>. The <a href="/wiki/Cyprian_Youdle%27s_killer" title="Cyprian Youdle&#39;s killer">caster</a> of the <a href="/wiki/Curse" title="Curse">curse</a> was never caught, but was suspected to be one of the members in the crowd.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>By the 1990s, he was featured on a <a href="/wiki/Chocolate_Frog_Card" title="Chocolate Frog Card">Famous Wizard Card</a>.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Cyprian_Youdle?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Cyprian_Youdle?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The name "Cyprian" comes from the Roman family name Cyprianus which meant "from Cyprus" in Latin. Saint Cyprian was a 3rd-century bishop of Carthage and a martyr under the emperor Valerian.
</p>
