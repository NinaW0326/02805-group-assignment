<b>Misapinoa Blishwick</b> (née <b>Black</b>) (<a href="/wiki/1826" class="mw-redirect" title="1826">1826</a> – <a href="/wiki/1926" title="1926">1926</a>) was a <a href="/wiki/Pure-blood" title="Pure-blood">pure-blood</a> <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch</a>. She was an early member of the <a href="/wiki/House_of_Black" title="House of Black">House of Black</a>, but her precise relationship to the other known members of the Black family remains unknown. She married a pure-blood wizard named <a href="/wiki/Jimbo_Blishwick" title="Jimbo Blishwick">Jimbo Blishwick</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-ootpfilm_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-ootpfilm-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>It is possible that her parents were <a href="/wiki/Licorus_Black" title="Licorus Black">Licorus Black</a> and <a href="/wiki/Magenta_Tripe" title="Magenta Tripe">Magenta Tripe</a>. She was likely an aunt of <a href="/wiki/Phineas_Nigellus_Black" title="Phineas Nigellus Black">Phineas Nigellus Black</a>. It is unknown if she had any children.
</p><p>She is depicted on the <a href="/wiki/Black_Family_Tapestry" title="Black Family Tapestry">Black family tree tapestry</a> in <a href="/wiki/12_Grimmauld_Place" title="12 Grimmauld Place">12 Grimmauld Place</a>, <a href="/wiki/London" title="London">London</a>.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Misapinoa_Black?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Misapinoa_Black?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>This character appears only on the version of the <a href="/wiki/Black_Family_Tapestry" title="Black Family Tapestry">Black family tree tapestry</a> shown in the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Order_of_the_Phoenix_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix (film)">film adaptation</a> of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Order_of_the_Phoenix" title="Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix">Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix</a></i>. She was not included on the version of the tree which <a href="/wiki/J._K._Rowling" title="J. K. Rowling">J. K. Rowling</a> gave to a charity auction in <a href="/wiki/2006" title="2006">2006</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> According to director <a href="/wiki/David_Yates" title="David Yates">David Yates</a>, Rowling provided the filmmakers with a copy of the Black family tree going back eight generations.<sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> The filmmakers presumably received a more complete version of the tree than the one auctioned off, which only goes back six generations.</li>
<li>Misapinoa lived to 99- 100. This is unusual because members of the Black family often died young by wizarding standards.</li>
<li>The face used to depict Misapinoa on the Black family tree in the <i>Order of the Phoenix</i> film was also used for <a href="/wiki/Lucretia_Black" title="Lucretia Black">Lucretia Black</a>.</li>
<li>Based on a statement by <a href="/wiki/Horace_Slughorn" title="Horace Slughorn">Horace Slughorn</a>, Misapinoa may have been a member of Slytherin House. However, she attended <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts</a> c. <a href="/wiki/1837" class="mw-redirect" title="1837">1837</a>-<a href="/wiki/1844" class="mw-redirect" title="1844">1844</a>, long before Slughorn was even born.</li>
<li>Misapinoa was maybe a sister of <a href="/wiki/Arcturus_Black_I" title="Arcturus Black I">Arcturus Black I</a>.</li></ul>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Misapinoa_Black?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Misapinoa_Black?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Misapinoa's name may be from the word "<a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Messapian_language" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Messapian language">Messapian</a>", an extinct language that was once spoken in south eastern Italy, or it could be from the word "mishap", which ties along with how much older she lived to be than most members of the <a href="/wiki/House_of_Black" title="House of Black">House of Black</a>.
</p>
