<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_male_Death_Eater_during_the_Battle_of_Hogwarts_(III)?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_male_Death_Eater_during_the_Battle_of_Hogwarts_(III)?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>In <a href="/wiki/1997" title="1997">1997</a>, he was present during the <a href="/wiki/Meeting_at_Malfoy_Manor" title="Meeting at Malfoy Manor">Meeting</a> at <a href="/wiki/Malfoy_Manor" title="Malfoy Manor">Malfoy Manor</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-DHF1_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DHF1-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> In <a href="/wiki/1998" title="1998">1998</a>, he participated in the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a>, and was present in the <a href="/wiki/Forbidden_Forest" title="Forbidden Forest">Forbidden Forest</a> when <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Voldemort</a> <i>killed</i> <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>. He was later seen when the <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a> were fleeing from the battle.<sup id="cite_ref-DHF2_4-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DHF2-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> After Voldemort's defeat he was possibly killed or imprisoned in <a href="/wiki/Azkaban" title="Azkaban">Azkaban</a> afterwards.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Physical_Appearance">Physical Appearance</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_male_Death_Eater_during_the_Battle_of_Hogwarts_(III)?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Physical Appearance">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_male_Death_Eater_during_the_Battle_of_Hogwarts_(III)?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Physical Appearance"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>This Death Eater seemed to have fairly large ears, fairly short brown hair, and sunken eyes. He also had a large "Adam's Apple" around his neck area.
</p>
