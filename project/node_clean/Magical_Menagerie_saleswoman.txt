<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Magical_Menagerie_saleswoman?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Magical_Menagerie_saleswoman?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>In <a href="/wiki/1991" title="1991">1991</a>, she was willing to give <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> some <a href="/wiki/Giant_Purple_Toad_wart" title="Giant Purple Toad wart">warts</a> from a <a href="/wiki/Giant_Purple_Toad" title="Giant Purple Toad">Giant Purple Toad</a> if he could catch it.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> In <a href="/wiki/1992" title="1992">1992</a>, she was absent, possibly on vacation,&#160;and a <a href="/wiki/Magical_Menagerie_salesman" title="Magical Menagerie salesman">man</a> had to do her job.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> She advised a <a href="/wiki/Double-ended-newt_wizard" title="Double-ended-newt wizard">customer</a> on how to take care of a <a href="/wiki/Double-ended_Newt" class="mw-redirect" title="Double-ended Newt">double-ended newt</a>, sold <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ron Weasley</a>&#160;a bottle of <a href="/wiki/Rat_tonic" title="Rat tonic">rat tonic</a> for <a href="/wiki/Peter_Pettigrew" title="Peter Pettigrew">Scabbers</a> during the summer of <a href="/wiki/1993" title="1993">1993</a>, and sold <a href="/wiki/Crookshanks" title="Crookshanks">Crookshanks</a> to <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione Granger</a> at that time as well.<sup id="cite_ref-PA4_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PA4-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
