<p>A <b>security troll</b> was a specially-trained <a href="/wiki/Troll" title="Troll">troll</a> who guarded certain places and objects in <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizarding</a> society.<sup id="cite_ref-POA14_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-POA14-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> Some <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizards and witches</a> made their living out of training security trolls.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP29_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP29-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="History">History</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Security_troll?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: History">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Security_troll?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: History"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="1993-1994">1993-1994</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Security_troll?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: 1993-1994">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Security_troll?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: 1993-1994"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>In <a href="/wiki/1993" title="1993">1993</a>, <a href="/wiki/Albus_Dumbledore" title="Albus Dumbledore">Albus Dumbledore</a> hired security trolls to guard the <a href="/wiki/Fat_Lady" title="Fat Lady">Fat Lady</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Portrait" title="Portrait">portrait</a> after <a href="/wiki/Sirius_Black" title="Sirius Black">Sirius Black</a> attacked her in an attempt to get into <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor_Tower" title="Gryffindor Tower">Gryffindor Tower</a> and <a href="/wiki/Cadogan" title="Cadogan">Sir Cadogan</a> was fired for letting him in while he was covering for the Fat Lady. These Trolls were placed in the <a href="/wiki/North_Wing" title="North Wing">North Wing</a> and were evidently a different type of Troll than the <a href="/wiki/Mountain_troll" title="Mountain troll">mountain</a> variety, since they seemed considerably more intelligent and less smelly. They spent all their time pacing the corridor in front of the Fat Lady's portrait, giving dirty looks to everyone who happened by and comparing the sizes of their clubs.<sup id="cite_ref-POA14_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-POA14-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> They left at the end of the year, when Sirius led the Ministry into knowing that he fled out of the country.
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="1996">1996</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Security_troll?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: 1996">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Security_troll?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: 1996"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>In <a href="/wiki/1996" title="1996">1996</a>, <a href="/wiki/Dolores_Umbridge" title="Dolores Umbridge">Dolores Umbridge</a> apparently hired an armed security troll to guard <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter%27s_Firebolt" title="Harry Potter&#39;s Firebolt">Firebolt</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP30_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP30-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> This was after <a href="/wiki/Fred_and_George_Weasley" title="Fred and George Weasley">Fred and George Weasley</a> made their final departure from <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts</a> and had stolen their brooms back, Umbridge decided that it would be best to move Harry's broom to the <a href="/wiki/Dungeons" title="Dungeons">Dungeons</a> with the added security measure of security trolls.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP33_4-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP33-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> It was eventually dismissed when Umbridge left, and Harry got his Firebolt back.
</p><p>Security trolls were employed at <a href="/wiki/Gringotts_Wizarding_Bank" title="Gringotts Wizarding Bank">Gringotts Wizarding Bank</a>, helping to inspect lifts and carts.<sup id="cite_ref-WWHP_5-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-WWHP-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>In the <a href="/wiki/21st_century" title="21st century">21st century</a>, security trolls were unleashed as a <a href="/wiki/Confoundable" title="Confoundable">Confoundable</a> by the <a href="/wiki/Calamity" title="Calamity">Calamity</a> and had to be dealt with by the <a href="/wiki/Statute_of_Secrecy_Task_Force" title="Statute of Secrecy Task Force">Statute of Secrecy Task Force</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-WU_6-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-WU-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Security_troll?veaction=edit&amp;section=4" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Security_troll?action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>Despite not appearing in the films, the trolls are included in the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Prisoner_of_Azkaban_(video_game)" title="Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban (video game)">video game adaptation</a> of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Prisoner_of_Azkaban" title="Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban">Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban</a></i> as well as the first LEGO video game.</li>
<li>In <i><a href="/wiki/LEGO_Harry_Potter:_Years_1-4" title="LEGO Harry Potter: Years 1-4">LEGO Harry Potter: Years 1-4</a></i>, they appear wearing blue security hats. They would hit the ground causing giant waves blowing away anyone who hits a spell at them.</li></ul>
