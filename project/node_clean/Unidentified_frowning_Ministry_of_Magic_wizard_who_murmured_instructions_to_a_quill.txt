<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows">"<i> </i>[Harry]<i> therefore set off along the corridor again, passing nobody but a frowning wizard who was murmuring instructions to a quill that floated in front of him, scribbling on a trail of parchment.</i>"</span></dd>

<p>This <b>individual</b> was a <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">Ministry of Magic</a> Official. On <a href="/wiki/2_September" title="2 September">2 September</a>, <a href="/wiki/1997" title="1997">1997</a>, he passed by <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> (<a href="/wiki/Polyjuice_Potion" title="Polyjuice Potion">disguised</a> as <a href="/wiki/Albert_Runcorn" title="Albert Runcorn">Albert Runcorn</a>) while under his <a href="/wiki/Cloak_of_Invisibility" title="Cloak of Invisibility">Invisibility Cloak</a> on Level One of the Ministry of Magic. The wizard was murmuring instructions to a floating quill scribbling on a trail of parchment.<sup id="cite_ref-DH13_2-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH13-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
