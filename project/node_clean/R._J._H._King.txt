<b>R. J. H. King</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">student</a> at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> in the late <a href="/wiki/1960s" title="1960s">1960s</a>. Sorted into <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor" title="Gryffindor">Gryffindor</a>, King played on the <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor_Quidditch_team" title="Gryffindor Quidditch team">Gryffindor Quidditch team</a>, and won an award for it in <a href="/wiki/1969" title="1969">1969</a>. This plaque was placed by those belonging to fellow Gryffindors <a href="/wiki/M._G._McGonagall" title="M. G. McGonagall">M. G. McGonagall</a> and <a href="/wiki/James_Potter_I" title="James Potter I">James Potter</a>, who may have been on the Quidditch team at the same time as them.
</p>
