<p><b>Alderton</b><sup id="cite_ref-DHpt1_script_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DHpt1_script-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> living somewhere in the British Isles during the <a href="/wiki/Second_Wizarding_War" title="Second Wizarding War">Second Wizarding War</a>. Despite his claims to be <a href="/wiki/Half-blood" title="Half-blood">half-blood</a>, Alderton was 'interviewed' by the <a href="/wiki/Muggle-Born_Registration_Commission" title="Muggle-Born Registration Commission">Muggle-Born Registration Commission</a> on <a href="/wiki/2_September" title="2 September">2 September</a>, <a href="/wiki/1997" title="1997">1997</a>, and was either sent to <a href="/wiki/Azkaban" title="Azkaban">Azkaban</a> or <a href="/wiki/Wandless" title="Wandless">stripped of his wand and job</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-DHF1_1-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DHF1-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="History">History</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Alderton?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: History">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Alderton?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: History"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_life">Early life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Alderton?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Early life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Alderton?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Early life"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>According to Alderton, he was the son of <a href="/wiki/William_Alderton" title="William Alderton">William Alderton</a>, a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a>, making him a <a href="/wiki/Half-blood" title="Half-blood">half-blood</a>. The <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">Ministry of Magic</a>, however, determined he was a <a href="/wiki/Muggle-born" title="Muggle-born">Muggle-born</a> wizard.<sup id="cite_ref-DHF1_1-10" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DHF1-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>He most likely attended <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> in his youth.
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Second_Wizarding_War_and_Prosecution">Second Wizarding War and Prosecution</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Alderton?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Second Wizarding War and Prosecution">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Alderton?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Second Wizarding War and Prosecution"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>In <a href="/wiki/1997" title="1997">1997</a>, after the <a href="/wiki/Infiltration_of_the_Ministry_of_Magic" title="Infiltration of the Ministry of Magic">infiltration</a> of the <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">Ministry of Magic</a> by <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a>, he was singled out as being Muggle-born by the <a href="/wiki/Muggle-Born_Registration_Commission" title="Muggle-Born Registration Commission">Muggle-Born Registration Commission</a> and was tried. He was found guilty during the trial and was therefore led away by two Ministry guards afterwards, either to await imprisonment or to be stripped of his wand and job. As he left the courtroom, he desperately tried to convince the guards that he was a half-blood by claiming that his <a href="/wiki/William_Alderton" title="William Alderton">father</a> was a <a href="/wiki/Pure-blood" title="Pure-blood">pure-blood</a> wizard, but was unsuccessful. It is unknown what happened to him afterwards.<sup id="cite_ref-DHF1_1-11" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DHF1-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
