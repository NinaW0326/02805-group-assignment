<p><b>Nanette Desford</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch</a> and <a href="/wiki/Ravenclaw" title="Ravenclaw">Ravenclaw</a> student at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> in the <a href="/wiki/1990s" title="1990s">1990s</a>. She was usually found spending time by the <a href="/wiki/Clock_Tower" title="Clock Tower">Clock Tower</a> during the <a href="/wiki/1996%E2%80%931997_school_year" title="1996–1997 school year">1996–1997 school year</a>. She often played <a href="/wiki/Exploding_Snap" title="Exploding Snap">Exploding Snap</a>. She gave <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> <a href="/wiki/Chocolate_Frog_Card" title="Chocolate Frog Card">Chocolate Frog Cards</a> whenever he beat her.<sup id="cite_ref-HBPG_3-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBPG-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
