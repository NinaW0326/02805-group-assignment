<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Physical_appearance">Physical appearance</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Corrine_Cribb?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Physical appearance">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Corrine_Cribb?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Physical appearance"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>She looked like a middle-aged woman in the late <a href="/wiki/1980s" title="1980s">1980s</a>. She wore all black. She had dark coloured eyes and black hair with two strands of grey hair made into a bun.<sup id="cite_ref-HM_1-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HM-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Personality_and_traits">Personality and traits</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Corrine_Cribb?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Personality and traits">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Corrine_Cribb?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Personality and traits"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>According to Rubeus Hagrid, Corrine Cribb was a cruel and callous witch with an utter disregard for both people and animals, as shown with her sadistic desire to have a <a href="/wiki/Dragon" title="Dragon">dragon</a> skinned alive simply to make a coat from its skin. She also saw no problem with threatening other individuals.<sup id="cite_ref-HM_1-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HM-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
