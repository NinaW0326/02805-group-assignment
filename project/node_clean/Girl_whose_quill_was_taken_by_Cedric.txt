<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Girl_whose_quill_was_taken_by_Cedric?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Girl_whose_quill_was_taken_by_Cedric?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<div class="quote">
<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;Harry Potter and the Philosopher&#39;s Stone (video game)">"<i>Potter! I'm going to report this blatant disregard for a prefect's authority. 6 points from Gryffindor!</i>"</span></dd>
<dd>&#8212;This student is hit by a spell<sup class="noprint"><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Philosopher%27s_Stone_(video_game)" title="Harry Potter and the Philosopher&#39;s Stone (video game)">[src]</a></sup></dd>
<dd></dd></dl></div>
<p>She attended breakfast in the <a href="/wiki/Great_Hall" title="Great Hall">Great Hall</a> on the day of the <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor_Quidditch_team" title="Gryffindor Quidditch team">Gryffindor</a> vs. <a href="/wiki/Ravenclaw_Quidditch_team" title="Ravenclaw Quidditch team">Ravenclaw</a> <a href="/wiki/Quidditch" title="Quidditch">Quidditch</a> match, where she commented that <a href="/wiki/Cedric" title="Cedric">Cedric</a> had taken her <a href="/wiki/Quill" title="Quill">writing quill</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-PSgba_2-10" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PSgba-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<div class="quote">
<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;Harry Potter and the Philosopher&#39;s Stone (video game)">"<i>I just want you to know that you're no less of a hero to me just because I have to take 6 House points from you for being out at night.</i>"</span></dd>
<dd>&#8212;The prefect catches <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> breaking school rules.<sup class="noprint"><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Philosopher%27s_Stone_(video_game)" title="Harry Potter and the Philosopher&#39;s Stone (video game)">[src]</a></sup></dd>
<dd></dd></dl></div>
<p>She was patrolling the <a href="/wiki/Third-floor_corridor" title="Third-floor corridor">Third-floor corridor</a> on the night of <a href="/wiki/4_June" title="4 June">4 June</a>, <a href="/wiki/1992" title="1992">1992</a>. When <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>, <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ronald Weasley</a> and <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione Granger</a> passed by <a href="/wiki/Peeves" title="Peeves">Peeves</a>, she heard something and went to investigate.<sup id="cite_ref-PSgba_2-11" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PSgba-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<div class="quote">
<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;Harry Potter and the Philosopher&#39;s Stone (video game)">"<i>Wow, Harry Potter himself, violating the rules like any other student. You really have the common touch. 6 points from Gryffindor for being out at night.</i>"</span></dd>
<dd>&#8212;The prefect catches <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> breaking school rules<sup class="noprint"><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Philosopher%27s_Stone_(video_game)" title="Harry Potter and the Philosopher&#39;s Stone (video game)">[src]</a></sup></dd>
<dd></dd></dl></div>
<p>She admired <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> and, whenever she caught him sneaking around at night, she was surprised to find him there, breaking school rules just like "any other student" and even assuring him that the fact that she had to deduct him <a href="/wiki/House_points" title="House points">house points</a> did not change the fact that she looked up to him as a hero.<sup id="cite_ref-PSgba_2-12" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PSgba-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
