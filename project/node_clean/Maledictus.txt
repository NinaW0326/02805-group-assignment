<p>A <b>Maledictus</b> (plural <b>Maledictuses</b><sup id="cite_ref-description_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-description-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>) is a female individual who carries a <a href="/wiki/Blood_malediction" title="Blood malediction">blood curse</a> that eventually turns her into a <a href="/wiki/Beast" title="Beast">beast</a> permanently.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> The curse is carried from birth and passed down from mother to daughter.<sup id="cite_ref-description_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-description-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> The beast ultimately transformed into can vary based on the curse.<sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> Before their permanent transformation, they have the ability to change shape at will, which becomes gradually uncontrollable. A <a href="/wiki/Werewolf" title="Werewolf">Werewolf</a> is not considered a Maledictus.<sup id="cite_ref-description_1-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-description-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Maledictus?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Maledictus?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li><i>Maledictus</i> is a Latin word meaning "accursed [one]", from <i>maledicere</i> ("to speak ill of, slander, curse"). This is a masculine word; as all Maledictuses are female, it should logically be the female form, <i>Maledicta</i>.<sup class="noprint">&#91;<a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Wikipedia:Citing_sources" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Wikipedia:Citing sources"><i><span title="The material in the vicinity of this tag needs references to reliable sources." style="white-space: nowrap;">citation needed</span></i></a>&#93;</sup><figure class="thumb tright show-info-icon" style="width: 164px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/2/27/Circus_Arcanus_1.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20181120124717" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/2/27/Circus_Arcanus_1.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/162?cb=20181120124717" decoding="async" width="162" height="329" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="Circus Arcanus 1.jpg" data-image-key="Circus_Arcanus_1.jpg" /></a> 	 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:Circus_Arcanus_1.jpg" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption">The poster of Nagini, the maledictus, of <a href="https://harrypotter.fandom.com/wiki/Circus_Arcanu" class="extiw" title="en:Circus Arcanu">Circus Arcanus</a></p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure></li></ul>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Maledictus?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Maledictus?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>Maledictuses were very different from Animagi.<sup id="cite_ref-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li>Nagini's human form was the first time a Maledictus was mentioned in the wizarding world franchise, though blood-related curses were mentioned before with the case of <a href="/wiki/Astoria_Malfoy" title="Astoria Malfoy">Astoria Malfoy</a>.</li>
<li>It's unknown if a Muggle can carry the Maledictus curse. As yet we do not know if Nagini is magical or not, as it has not been revealed, and it is possible that Muggle women, as well as witches, may be afflicted.
<ul><li>Nagini is stated to not have a wand, however according to the script she did manage to use Parseltongue to cast a spell when transformed. It has also been stated in interviews that she has magic "unlike that of wizards" suggesting that she may be a non-magical human able to use limited magic due to the curse she is under.</li></ul></li>
<li>A Maledictus is not destined to be evil.<sup id="cite_ref-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li>It seems that a Maledictus' form is not limited by the lifespan of the animal they transform into, similar to Animagi. Nagini lived to be at least 71 years old, longer than a snake's normal life expectancy.</li></ul>
