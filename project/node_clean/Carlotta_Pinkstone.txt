<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Carlotta_Pinkstone?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Carlotta_Pinkstone?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_life">Early life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Carlotta_Pinkstone?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Early life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Carlotta_Pinkstone?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Early life"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Carlotta Pinkstone was born in <a href="/wiki/1922" title="1922">1922</a>, somewhere in the <a href="/wiki/Great_Britain" title="Great Britain">British Isles</a>. If she enrolled at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a>, she attended the school in around <a href="/wiki/1933" title="1933">1933</a> to <a href="/wiki/1940" title="1940">1940</a>, under <a href="/wiki/Headmaster" title="Headmaster">Headmaster</a> <a href="/wiki/Armando_Dippet" title="Armando Dippet">Armando Dippet</a>.
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Adulthood">Adulthood</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Carlotta_Pinkstone?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Adulthood">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Carlotta_Pinkstone?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Adulthood"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<div class="quote">
<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;Wizard of the Month">"<i>STOP SPELL SUPPRESSION!</i>"</span></dd>
<dd>&#8212;One of Carlotta's activism signs<sup class="noprint"><a href="/wiki/Wizard_of_the_Month" title="Wizard of the Month">[src]</a></sup></dd>
<dd></dd></dl></div><figure class="thumb tleft show-info-icon" style="width: 143px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/e/e7/CarlottaPinkstoneFWC2.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20120103192409" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/e/e7/CarlottaPinkstoneFWC2.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/141?cb=20120103192409" decoding="async" width="141" height="223" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="CarlottaPinkstoneFWC2.jpg" data-image-key="CarlottaPinkstoneFWC2.jpg" /></a> 	 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:CarlottaPinkstoneFWC2.jpg" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption">Carlotta in shackles</p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure>
<p>It was Ms. Pinkstone's belief that the annulment of the <a href="/wiki/International_Statute_of_Wizarding_Secrecy" title="International Statute of Wizarding Secrecy">International Statute of Wizarding Secrecy</a> (established by the <a href="/wiki/International_Confederation_of_Wizards" title="International Confederation of Wizards">International Confederation of Wizards</a> in <a href="/wiki/1692" class="mw-redirect" title="1692">1692</a>) and consequent mixing of the <a href="/wiki/Wizarding_world" title="Wizarding world">wizarding</a> and <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggle</a> societies was opportune and convenient in the <a href="/wiki/20th_century" title="20th century">20th century</a>. As such, she became a vigorous activist for that cause, and deliberately and unlawfully used magic indiscriminately in public places, in full sight of <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggles</a>, which led to a series of convictions and imprisonments in <a href="/wiki/Azkaban" title="Azkaban">Azkaban</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-PSG_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PSG-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-COSG_2-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COSG-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-POAG_3-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-POAG-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Pinkstone was still alive by the <a href="/wiki/1990s" title="1990s">1990s</a>, by which time she was featured on a <a href="/wiki/Chocolate_Frog_Card" title="Chocolate Frog Card">Chocolate Frog Card</a>, which pictured her symbolically in shackles.<sup id="cite_ref-PSG_1-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PSG-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-COSG_2-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COSG-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-POAG_3-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-POAG-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Carlotta_Pinkstone?veaction=edit&amp;section=5" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Carlotta_Pinkstone?action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>Carlotta Pinkstone was <i><a href="/wiki/Wizard_of_the_Month" title="Wizard of the Month">Wizard of the Month</a></i> on <i><a href="/wiki/J._K._Rowling_Official_Site" class="mw-redirect" title="J. K. Rowling Official Site">J.K. Rowling's Official Site</a></i>, in which she is shown with black hair despite having previously been pictured with red hair in all of her video game appearances.</li>
<li>Her name may be a reference to the famous British feminist <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Emmeline_Pankhurst" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Emmeline Pankhurst">Emmeline Pankhurst</a>.</li></ul>
