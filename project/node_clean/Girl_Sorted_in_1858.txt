This <b>girl</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> student<sup id="cite_ref-Pottermore_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Pottermore-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> from <a href="/wiki/1858" class="mw-redirect" title="1858">1858</a> to <a href="/wiki/1865" class="mw-redirect" title="1865">1865</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-Wikipedia_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Wikipedia-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>During her <a href="/wiki/Sorting_ceremony" title="Sorting ceremony">Sorting ceremony</a> on <a href="/wiki/1_September" title="1 September">1 September</a>, <a href="/wiki/1858" class="mw-redirect" title="1858">1858</a>, her name was called but <a href="/wiki/Angus_Buchanan" title="Angus Buchanan">Angus Buchanan</a>, a <a href="/wiki/Squib" title="Squib">Squib</a> pretending to actually be a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> who was among the crowd of <a href="/wiki/First_year" title="First year">first-years</a>, threw himself ahead of her in sheer desperation and placed the <a href="/wiki/Sorting_Hat" title="Sorting Hat">Sorting Hat</a> upon his head. The Hat announced kindly that he was "<i>a good-hearted chap, but no wizard</i>", and the boy left the hall in tears.<sup id="cite_ref-Pottermore_2-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Pottermore-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
