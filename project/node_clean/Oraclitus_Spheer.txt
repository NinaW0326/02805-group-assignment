<b>Oraclitus Spheer</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a>. He had a <a href="/wiki/Portrait" title="Portrait">Portrait</a> of himself hung in the <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor_Tower_reading_room" title="Gryffindor Tower reading room">Gryffindor Tower reading room</a>, hiding <a href="/wiki/Fred_Weasley" title="Fred Weasley">Fred</a> and <a href="/wiki/George_Weasley" title="George Weasley">George Weasley</a>'s convenience shop. His password was "<i>Flibbertigibbet</i>".<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
