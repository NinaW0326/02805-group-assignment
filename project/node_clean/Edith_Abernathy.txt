</p><p>During the <a href="/wiki/2010s" title="2010s">2010s</a>, her Niffler appeared as a <a href="/wiki/Foundable" title="Foundable">Foundable</a> during the <a href="/wiki/Calamity" title="Calamity">Calamity</a>. Volunteer members of the <a href="/wiki/Statute_of_Secrecy_Task_Force" title="Statute of Secrecy Task Force">Statute of Secrecy Task Force</a> were able to free him, allowing Niffler to return to Edith.<sup id="cite_ref-WU_1-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-WU-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
