He blocked access to areas of the school grounds that were off limits during the <a href="/wiki/1996%E2%80%931997_school_year" title="1996–1997 school year">1996–1997 school year</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-HBPG_3-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBPG-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
