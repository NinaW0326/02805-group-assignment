This <b>singer</b> was an <a href="/wiki/United_States_of_America" title="United States of America">American</a>&#160;female&#160;<a href="/wiki/Goblin" title="Goblin">goblin</a> who sung jazz at the wizarding speakeasy <a href="/wiki/The_Blind_Pig" title="The Blind Pig">The Blind Pig</a> during the <a href="/wiki/1920s" title="1920s">1920s</a>. She would use her <a href="/wiki/Wandless_magic" title="Wandless magic">wandless magic</a> to create smoky images to accompany her singing.<sup id="cite_ref-FBS82_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-FBS82-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Goblin_jazz_singer?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Goblin_jazz_singer?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>She started working at the <a href="/wiki/The_Blind_Pig" title="The Blind Pig">The Blind Pig</a>, sometime before <a href="/wiki/1926" title="1926">1926</a>. She was performing with a band of goblin musicians on <a href="/wiki/6_December" title="6 December">6 December</a> <a href="/wiki/1926" title="1926">1926</a> when <a href="/wiki/Newton_Scamander" title="Newton Scamander">Newton Scamander</a> and his companions came to obtain information from <a href="/wiki/Gnarlak" title="Gnarlak">Gnarlak</a> regarding Newt's escaped <a href="/wiki/Beast" title="Beast">beasts</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-FBS82_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-FBS82-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Magical_abilities_and_skills">Magical abilities and skills</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Goblin_jazz_singer?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Goblin_jazz_singer?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li><b><a href="/wiki/Wandless_magic" title="Wandless magic">Wandless magic</a></b>: Being a <a href="/wiki/Goblin" title="Goblin">Goblin</a>, she was shown to be proficient in wandless magic, being able to <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magically</a> create smoky images to visually assist her musical performances, to a very precise and beautiful effect.</li></ul>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Goblin_jazz_singer?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Goblin_jazz_singer?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>The goblin was portrayed by <a href="/wiki/Aretha_Ayeh" title="Aretha Ayeh">Aretha Ayeh</a> through motion reference and her singing voice was performed by <a href="/wiki/Emmi" title="Emmi">Emmi</a> in <i><a href="/wiki/Fantastic_Beasts_and_Where_to_Find_Them_(film)" title="Fantastic Beasts and Where to Find Them (film)">Fantastic Beasts and Where to Find Them</a></i>.</li>
<li>She is the first female goblin featured in any <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_universe" title="Harry Potter universe">Harry Potter media</a>.</li></ul>
