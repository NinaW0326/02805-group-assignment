<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Bertha_Jorkins?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Bertha_Jorkins?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_life">Early life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Bertha_Jorkins?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Early life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Bertha_Jorkins?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Early life"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Bertha Jorkins was born at some point between <a href="/wiki/1_September" title="1 September">1 September</a>, <a href="/wiki/1953" title="1953">1953</a>, and <a href="/wiki/31_August" title="31 August">31 August</a>, <a href="/wiki/1958" title="1958">1958</a>. Her parentage is unknown.
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Hogwarts_years">Hogwarts years</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Bertha_Jorkins?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Hogwarts years">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Bertha_Jorkins?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Hogwarts years"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Bertha Jorkins was a few years ahead of <a href="/wiki/James_Potter_I" title="James Potter I">James Potter</a>, <a href="/wiki/Sirius_Black" title="Sirius Black">Sirius Black</a>, <a href="/wiki/Remus_Lupin" title="Remus Lupin">Remus Lupin</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Peter_Pettigrew" title="Peter Pettigrew">Peter Pettigrew</a> at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a>. She was a gossipy girl and not very bright, which Sirius noted to not be a very good combination.<sup id="cite_ref-GOF19_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF19-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> During her school years she would regularly complain about mistreatment from other students and spread rumours, even once ending up in front of <a href="/wiki/Albus_Dumbledore" title="Albus Dumbledore">Dumbledore</a> following an incident. Bertha was described as being a slightly plump girl with a sulky expression, though her expression could just be due to the fact she was complaining to Dumbledore. She once complained about being <a href="/wiki/Hex" title="Hex">Hexed</a> by a <a href="/wiki/Florence%27s_boyfriend" title="Florence&#39;s boyfriend">boy</a> whom she teased because she had caught him kissing <a href="/wiki/Florence" title="Florence">Florence</a> behind the <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_greenhouses" title="Hogwarts greenhouses">Hogwarts greenhouses</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-GOF30_4-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF30-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Career_at_the_Ministry">Career at the Ministry</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Bertha_Jorkins?veaction=edit&amp;section=4" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Career at the Ministry">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Bertha_Jorkins?action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Career at the Ministry"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>After graduating from Hogwarts, Bertha joined the <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">Ministry of Magic</a> and floated between Departments. She finally ended up in the <a href="/wiki/Department_of_Magical_Games_and_Sports" title="Department of Magical Games and Sports">Department of Magical Games and Sports</a> under the head of <a href="/wiki/Ludovic_Bagman" title="Ludovic Bagman">Ludo Bagman</a>.
</p><p>Bertha had the misfortune of making a visit to the estate of <a href="/wiki/Bartemius_Crouch_Senior" title="Bartemius Crouch Senior">Barty Crouch Snr</a> with some papers for him to sign, where she discovered the Crouch family secret that they were keeping their convicted <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eater</a> son <a href="/wiki/Bartemius_Crouch_Junior" title="Bartemius Crouch Junior">Barty Crouch Jnr</a> trapped in their house under the <a href="/wiki/Imperius_Curse" title="Imperius Curse">Imperius Curse</a> and an <a href="/wiki/Invisibility_cloak" title="Invisibility cloak">invisibility cloak</a> when their <a href="/wiki/House-elf" title="House-elf">house-elf</a> <a href="/wiki/Winky" title="Winky">Winky</a> was talking to the criminal, of which Bertha heard enough to deduce who was under the cloak. When Bertha confronted Crouch Snr, he had to place a powerful Memory Charm on her to alter her memory and make her forget what she had found out. However, the Memory Charm was so powerful, in fact, that she suffered permanent brain damage, leading to the extreme forgetfulness she had since been known for.<sup id="cite_ref-GOF35_5-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF35-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Death">Death</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Bertha_Jorkins?veaction=edit&amp;section=5" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Death">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Bertha_Jorkins?action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Death"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Bertha Jorkins went on holiday to <a href="/wiki/Albania" title="Albania">Albania</a> in <a href="/wiki/1994" title="1994">1994</a>. One night in a wayside inn, she ran into <a href="/wiki/Peter_Pettigrew" title="Peter Pettigrew">Peter Pettigrew</a>, who the Ministry thought to be dead at the hands of <a href="/wiki/Sirius_Black" title="Sirius Black">Sirius Black</a>. Although she recognised him, he was able to persuade her to take a stroll with him into the woods, and he was able to overpower Bertha and bring her before his master, <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a>, who was hiding in Albania. Lord Voldemort was able to break through the memory charm placed by Crouch Snr on her through <a href="/wiki/Cruciatus_Curse" title="Cruciatus Curse">torture</a> and learned of the imprisonment of his follower Crouch Jnr, and also learned valuable information about the <a href="/wiki/Triwizard_Tournament" title="Triwizard Tournament">Triwizard Tournament</a> which he would use to try to kill <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-GOF1_6-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF1-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Breaking through the memory charm had left Bertha severely "damaged," by permanently damaging her mind and body beyond repair, and Lord Voldemort murdered her with the <a href="/wiki/Killing_Curse" title="Killing Curse">Killing Curse</a>, because he had no more use for her. Voldemort used her death to turn his <a href="/wiki/Snake" title="Snake">snake</a> <a href="/wiki/Nagini" title="Nagini">Nagini</a> into a <a href="/wiki/Horcrux" title="Horcrux">Horcrux</a>. Pettigrew noted this was a poor move, believing that the Ministry would notice her disappearance and that they could have simply modified her memory to cover their tracks, which Voldemort believed would be an insult to her <i>memory</i>.<sup id="cite_ref-GOF1_6-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF1-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Postmortem">Postmortem</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Bertha_Jorkins?veaction=edit&amp;section=6" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Postmortem">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Bertha_Jorkins?action=edit&amp;section=6" title="Edit section: Postmortem"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>As a result of her demise, Bertha did not return when she was supposed to. Her reputation of being forgetful and not very bright made many believe she had lost track of time and was perhaps just lost, so the truth was not discovered for a while.<sup id="cite_ref-GOF5_7-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF5-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Physical_Appearance">Physical Appearance</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Bertha_Jorkins?veaction=edit&amp;section=7" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Physical Appearance">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Bertha_Jorkins?action=edit&amp;section=7" title="Edit section: Physical Appearance"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Bertha was a slightly plump woman.<sup id="cite_ref-GOF30_4-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF30-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Personality_and_traits">Personality and traits</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Bertha_Jorkins?veaction=edit&amp;section=8" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Personality and traits">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Bertha_Jorkins?action=edit&amp;section=8" title="Edit section: Personality and traits"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Bertha was a very gossipy and nosy woman, though she was not very bright, which in <a href="/wiki/Sirius_Black" title="Sirius Black">Sirius Black</a>'s opinion, not a very good combination.<sup id="cite_ref-GOF19_3-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF19-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> She was displeased with the mistreatment she received from her schoolmates despite her nosiness being the cause of their motive.<sup id="cite_ref-GOF30_4-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF30-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> Even in adulthood, her intelligence did not improve much, as upon discovering Barty Crouch Jnr still alive and hidden from the world, she confronted Crouch Snr instead of leaving to report to the Ministry, thus letting to her being struck with a Memory Charm. This ultimately became her downfall, as upon seeing the still alive Peter Pettigrew, she easily fell into his trap (which Sirius stated was a high possibility due to her poor personality combination) by accepting his proposal to take a stroll instead of reporting him, thus leading to her capture and delivery to Voldemort's clutches.<sup id="cite_ref-GOF35_5-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF35-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>After being struck by Crouch's intensive Memory Charm, Bertha's short term memory was permanently compromised, leading others to see as a new trait of forgetfulness that nobody questioned about.<sup id="cite_ref-GOF5_7-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF5-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-GOF35_5-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF35-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> When Voldemort tortured her extensively to break the Memory Charm, it left her damaged beyond repair, physically and mentally,<sup id="cite_ref-GOF34_8-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF34-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup> though Pettigrew believed she was still fit enough to have her memories modified again and sent back to the Ministry to avoid suspicions.<sup id="cite_ref-GOF1_6-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF1-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Upon her brief return to the mortal realm, Bertha was supportive of Harry Potter, as she moved to haunt Voldemort in order to give Harry time to escape.<sup id="cite_ref-GOF34_8-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF34-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Bertha_Jorkins?veaction=edit&amp;section=9" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Bertha_Jorkins?action=edit&amp;section=9" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Her first name, Bertha, means "intelligent" or "shining" — an ironic first name, as she was gossipy, forgetful and not always wisely cautious about danger. Jorkins may be from Mr Jorkins, a character in Charles Dickens' <i>David Copperfield</i> and <i>A Christmas Carol</i>.
</p>
