<b>Panju Weasley</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Great_Britain" title="Great Britain">British</a>&#160;<a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a>&#160;and the son of <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ron Weasley</a> and <a href="/wiki/Padma_Patil" title="Padma Patil">Padma Patil</a> in an <a href="/wiki/Alternate_reality" title="Alternate reality">alternate reality</a> created by <a href="/wiki/Albus_Potter" title="Albus Potter">Albus Potter</a> and <a href="/wiki/Scorpius_Malfoy" title="Scorpius Malfoy">Scorpius Malfoy</a> when they <a href="/wiki/Time-Turner" title="Time-Turner">changed</a> the events of the first task in the <a href="/wiki/Triwizard_Tournament" title="Triwizard Tournament">Triwizard Tournament</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-p1-a2-s9_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-p1-a2-s9-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-p1-a2-s17_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-p1-a2-s17-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Panju_Weasley?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Panju_Weasley?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Panju Weasley was born to <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ron Weasley</a> and <a href="/wiki/Padma_Patil" title="Padma Patil">Padma Patil</a> sometime in the mid-<a href="/wiki/2000s" title="2000s">2000s</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-p1-a2-s9_2-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-p1-a2-s9-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-p1-a2-s17_3-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-p1-a2-s17-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> He existed only in the <a href="/wiki/Alternate_reality" title="Alternate reality">alternate reality</a> created by <a href="/wiki/Albus_Potter" title="Albus Potter">Albus Potter</a> and <a href="/wiki/Scorpius_Malfoy" title="Scorpius Malfoy">Scorpius Malfoy</a> <a href="/wiki/Time-Turner" title="Time-Turner">travelling back in time</a> and changing the outcome of the first task of the <a href="/wiki/Triwizard_Tournament" title="Triwizard Tournament">Triwizard Tournament</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-p1-a2-s9_2-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-p1-a2-s9-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-p1-a2-s17_3-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-p1-a2-s17-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> In this universe, Ron married Padma instead of <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione Granger</a>, since Hermione never dated <a href="/wiki/Viktor_Krum" title="Viktor Krum">Viktor Krum</a>, which in the main timeline made him jealous and prompted him to act on his feelings.<sup id="cite_ref-p1-a2-s9_2-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-p1-a2-s9-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-p1-a2-s17_3-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-p1-a2-s17-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-p1-a2-s16_4-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-p1-a2-s16-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Panju attended <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-p1-a2-s9_2-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-p1-a2-s9-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-p1-a2-s17_3-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-p1-a2-s17-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> He bet his universe's version of his cousin <a href="/wiki/Albus_Potter" title="Albus Potter">Albus Potter</a> that he couldn't get sorted into <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor" title="Gryffindor">Gryffindor</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-p1-a2-s9_2-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-p1-a2-s9-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> This prompted Albus to convince the <a href="/wiki/Sorting_Hat" title="Sorting Hat">Sorting Hat</a> to place him in Gryffindor to spite Panju.<sup id="cite_ref-p1-a2-s9_2-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-p1-a2-s9-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>He had a mischievous spirit and frequently got into trouble at Hogwarts.<sup id="cite_ref-p1-a2-s9_2-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-p1-a2-s9-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-p1-a2-s17_3-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-p1-a2-s17-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> According to his father, he usually laughed when confronted about his behaviour, and had a cocky smile that everyone wanted to wipe off his face.<sup id="cite_ref-p1-a2-s9_2-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-p1-a2-s9-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> When Panju landed in trouble at Hogwarts during the 2020-2021 school year, his father went to the school to talk to him in person instead of sending a <a href="/wiki/Howler" title="Howler">Howler</a>, since his mother thought this would be more effective.<sup id="cite_ref-p1-a2-s9_2-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-p1-a2-s9-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> In another incident later that same school year, Panju ended up with no eyebrows and a "rather large" moustache after a mishap in <a href="/wiki/Potions_(class)" title="Potions (class)">Potions</a> class, prompting his father to once again pay a visit to Hogwarts to talk to him.<sup id="cite_ref-p1-a2-s17_3-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-p1-a2-s17-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Ron's comments about Panju helped Albus and Scorpius realise that their actions had changed history and created an alternate reality.<sup id="cite_ref-p1-a2-s9_2-10" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-p1-a2-s9-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-p1-a2-s16_4-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-p1-a2-s16-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>He was <a href="/wiki/Un-born" title="Un-born">un-born</a> when the Timeline was changed yet again, erasing him permanently from existence.&#160;
</p>
