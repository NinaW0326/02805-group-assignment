<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Cho_Chang%27s_mother?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Cho_Chang%27s_mother?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>

<p>Her birthday was <a href="/wiki/7_September" title="7 September">7 September</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-born_1-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-born-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> Cho remembered this fact at the last minute in <a href="/wiki/1995" title="1995">1995</a> and rushed to the <a href="/wiki/Owlery" title="Owlery">Owlery</a> at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts</a> to send her mother a gift.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP14_4-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP14-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Madam Chang (like her <a href="/wiki/Cho_Chang%27s_father" title="Cho Chang&#39;s father">husband</a>) wanted her daughter to not get on <a href="/wiki/Dolores_Umbridge" title="Dolores Umbridge">Dolores Umbridge</a>'s bad side in her <a href="/wiki/Sixth_year" title="Sixth year">sixth year</a>. However, Cho went against their wishes and joined <a href="/wiki/Dumbledore%27s_Army" title="Dumbledore&#39;s Army">Dumbledore's Army</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP18_5-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP18-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> Umbridge later threatened to fire Madam Chang because of her daughter's involvement in the DA.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTPF_2-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTPF-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Cho_Chang%27s_mother?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Cho_Chang%27s_mother?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>In the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Order_of_the_Phoenix_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix (film)">film adaptation</a> of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Order_of_the_Phoenix" title="Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix">Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix</a></i>, <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione Granger</a> mentions that <a href="/wiki/Dolores_Umbridge" title="Dolores Umbridge">Dolores Umbridge</a> is threatening to fire <a href="/wiki/Cho_Chang" title="Cho Chang">Cho's</a> mother from her job at the <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">Ministry of Magic</a>, presumably because of Cho's involvement in <a href="/wiki/Dumbledore%27s_Army" title="Dumbledore&#39;s Army">Dumbledore's Army</a>. This addition to the plot was likely made because <a href="/wiki/Marietta_Edgecombe" title="Marietta Edgecombe">Marietta Edgecombe</a> and her <a href="/wiki/Marietta_Edgecombe%27s_mother" class="mw-redirect" title="Marietta Edgecombe&#39;s mother">mother</a>, a known Ministry employee, were cut from the script.</li></ul>
