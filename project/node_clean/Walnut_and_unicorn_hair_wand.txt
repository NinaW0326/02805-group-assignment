</p><p>This <b><a href="/wiki/Wand" title="Wand">wand</a></b> was nine and half inches long, made of <a href="/wiki/Walnut_(tree)" title="Walnut (tree)">walnut</a> with a <a href="/wiki/Unicorn_hair" title="Unicorn hair">unicorn hair</a> core. It was created by <a href="/wiki/Garrick_Ollivander" title="Garrick Ollivander">Garrick Ollivander</a> sometime prior to <a href="/wiki/31_July" title="31 July">31 July</a>, <a href="/wiki/1991" title="1991">1991</a>, who deemed it a "good, strong wand". He offered it to <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> when he was picking out his first wand, but the wand and the wizard were not compatible.<sup id="cite_ref-HPK_1-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HPK-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
