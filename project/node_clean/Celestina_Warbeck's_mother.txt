<b>Mrs Warbeck</b> was the <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggle</a> wife of <a href="/wiki/Celestina_Warbeck%27s_father" title="Celestina Warbeck&#39;s father">Mr Warbeck</a>, a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a>, and the mother of internationally famous singer <a href="/wiki/Celestina_Warbeck" title="Celestina Warbeck">Celestina Warbeck</a>.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Celestina_Warbeck%27s_mother?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Celestina_Warbeck%27s_mother?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Mrs Warbeck, a <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggle</a>, lived in <a href="/wiki/Wales" title="Wales">Wales</a> in the early <a href="/wiki/20th_century" title="20th century">20th century</a>. Before <a href="/wiki/Marriage" title="Marriage">marrying</a>, she pursued a career in acting, but was not successful. She met <a href="/wiki/Celestina_Warbeck%27s_father" title="Celestina Warbeck&#39;s father">Mr Warbeck</a>, a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> who worked in the <a href="/wiki/Muggle_Liaison_Office" title="Muggle Liaison Office">Muggle Liaison Office</a>, after she was attacked (presumably during a performance) by a <a href="/wiki/Lethifold" title="Lethifold">Lethifold</a> that had hidden itself by taking the form of a stage curtain.<sup id="cite_ref-Pottermore_1-13" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Pottermore-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> The couple married on an unknown date and had at least one child, <a href="/wiki/Celestina_Warbeck" title="Celestina Warbeck">Celestina</a>, who was born <a href="/wiki/18_August" title="18 August">18 August</a><sup id="cite_ref-Pottermore_1-14" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Pottermore-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/1917" title="1917">1917</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-chocolatefrog_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-chocolatefrog-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Celestina proved to be an exceptionally gifted singer at a young age.<sup id="cite_ref-Pottermore_1-15" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Pottermore-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> Mrs Warbeck wished to enrol Celestina in wizarding stage school, but upon learning there was no such thing, she agreed to allow her to attend <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-Pottermore_1-16" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Pottermore-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> Fiercely supportive of her daughter, Mrs Warbeck sent many letters to the school, requesting the creation of a <a href="/wiki/Frog_Choir" title="Frog Choir">choir</a>, drama club, and dancing class to allow young Celestina to showcase her talents.<sup id="cite_ref-Pottermore_1-17" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Pottermore-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
