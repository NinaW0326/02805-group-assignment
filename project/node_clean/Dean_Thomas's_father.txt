The <b>biological father of <a href="/wiki/Dean_Thomas" title="Dean Thomas">Dean Thomas</a></b> was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> who abandoned his <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggle</a> <a href="/wiki/Dean_Thomas%27s_mother" title="Dean Thomas&#39;s mother">wife</a> and infant son in a courageous effort to protect them from the <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a>. He was soon after murdered by the Death Eaters when he refused to join their cause. His widow, who was never told that her husband was a wizard, eventually remarried a <a href="/wiki/Dean_Thomas%27s_stepfather" title="Dean Thomas&#39;s stepfather">Muggle man</a>, who raised Dean as his own son.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>When Dean realised he was a wizard at age eleven, his mother suspected that her first husband might have also been a wizard; however, not knowing for certain, <a href="/wiki/Dean_Thomas%27s_mother" title="Dean Thomas&#39;s mother">Mrs Thomas</a> left Dean to grow up believing he may be <a href="/wiki/Muggle-born" title="Muggle-born">Muggle-born</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-DH15_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH15-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
