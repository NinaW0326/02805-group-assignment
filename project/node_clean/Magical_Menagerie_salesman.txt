<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Magical_Menagerie_salesman?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Magical_Menagerie_salesman?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<figure class="thumb tleft show-info-icon" style="width: 182px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/2/22/Christmasspell.gif/revision/latest?cb=20120715155316" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/2/22/Christmasspell.gif/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/180?cb=20120715155316" decoding="async" width="180" height="84" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="Christmasspell.gif" data-image-key="Christmasspell.gif" /></a> 	 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:Christmasspell.gif" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption">This wizard conjuring Christmas decorations</p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure>
<p>Sometime in <a href="/wiki/1992" title="1992">1992</a>, he was hired as a temporary salesman for the <a href="/wiki/Magical_Menagerie" title="Magical Menagerie">Magical Menagerie</a>, as the usual <a href="/wiki/Magical_Menagerie_saleswoman" title="Magical Menagerie saleswoman">saleswoman</a> was on holiday. He was very stingy during morning hours and did not allow people to enter the shop, however in the afternoon he was willing to give <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> some <a href="/wiki/Lacewing_fly" title="Lacewing fly">lacewing flies</a> if he could catch them. Harry was warned of this man's morning grumpiness by <a href="/wiki/Menagerie_Message" title="Menagerie Message">a note</a> from his friend <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ronald Weasley</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-COSG_1-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COSG-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>In <a href="/wiki/December" title="December">December</a> <a href="/wiki/1992" title="1992">1992</a>, he used a <a href="/wiki/Christmas_decorations_conjuring_spell" title="Christmas decorations conjuring spell">spell</a> to <a href="/wiki/Conjuration" title="Conjuration">conjure</a> decorations for <a href="/wiki/Christmas" title="Christmas">Christmas</a> in the shop.<sup id="cite_ref-COSG_1-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COSG-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Magical_Menagerie_salesman?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Magical_Menagerie_salesman?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>In the PS1 version of the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Chamber_of_Secrets_(video_game)" title="Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets (video game)">video game adaption</a> of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Chamber_of_Secrets" title="Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets">Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets</a></i> the individual is old and will not allow Harry to destroy the crates in the shop, however in the console version he is also very stingy and will not let people inside the Menagerie. In the GBA version <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ronald Weasley</a> describes him as miserable and old in a note to Harry that says he should be careful as the salesman would throw those out who sneak around.</li></ul>
