<b>Morag MacDougal</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch</a> who began attending <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> in <a href="/wiki/1991" title="1991">1991</a>. She was in the same year as <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>. She was the witch who <a href="/wiki/Neville_Longbottom" title="Neville Longbottom">Neville Longbottom</a> had to give the <a href="/wiki/Sorting_Hat" title="Sorting Hat">Sorting Hat</a> back to when he accidentally ran off with it after being sorted.<sup id="cite_ref-PS7_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PS7-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Morag_MacDougal?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Morag_MacDougal?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>As <a href="/wiki/Isobel_MacDougal" title="Isobel MacDougal">Isobel MacDougal</a> never appears in the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_books" class="mw-redirect" title="Harry Potter books"><i>Harry Potter</i> novels</a>, it is possible that she is the same character as Morag, having been renamed at some point without <a href="/wiki/J._K._Rowling" title="J. K. Rowling">J. K. Rowling</a> noting the change on her handwritten list of the forty original students in <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry's</a> year.</li>
<li>A student named <a href="/wiki/Morag" title="Morag">Morag</a> appearand in the PC version of Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets. She worked with matches in Professor McGonagall's transfiguration class. It is possible that this is Morag MacDougal, who may have repeated her first year. (Match to needle is a spell known to be covered in first year transfiguration classes.)</li></ul>
