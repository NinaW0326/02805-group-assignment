<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Mazoni?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Mazoni?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>In the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Cursed_Child_(play)#Original_West_End_production" title="Harry Potter and the Cursed Child (play)">original West End production</a> of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Cursed_Child" title="Harry Potter and the Cursed Child">Harry Potter and the Cursed Child</a></i>, Mazoni was played by <a href="/wiki/Nuno_Silva" title="Nuno Silva">Nuno Silva</a><sup id="cite_ref-castlist_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-castlist-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> who is listed as playing <a href="/wiki/Bane" title="Bane">Bane</a> and other roles in <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Cursed_Child" title="Harry Potter and the Cursed Child">Harry Potter and the Cursed Child</a></i>.</li>
<li>This character only appears in the play programme and not the published <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Cursed_Child" title="Harry Potter and the Cursed Child">Harry Potter and the Cursed Child</a></i> script book, so the role may have been added later than the development of the rehearsal script.</li></ul>
