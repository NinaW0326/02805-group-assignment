<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_wizard_killed_at_the_Quad_battlements?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_wizard_killed_at_the_Quad_battlements?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>This wizard participated in the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a>. After the <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a> managed to break the protective enchantments protecting <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Castle" title="Hogwarts Castle">Hogwarts Castle</a>, he was aiming spells at the oncoming forces at the <a href="/wiki/Viaduct" title="Viaduct">Viaduct</a> over at the <a href="/wiki/The_Quad_battlements" title="The Quad battlements">the battlements</a> around <a href="/wiki/The_Quad" title="The Quad">The Quad</a>. <a href="/wiki/Unidentified_Death_Eater_who_engaged_Arthur_Weasley" title="Unidentified Death Eater who engaged Arthur Weasley">A Death Eater</a> <a href="/wiki/Skirmish_at_the_Quad_battlements" title="Skirmish at the Quad battlements">blew up part of the battlements</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Apparition" title="Apparition">Apparated</a> into the <a href="/wiki/Catwalks_by_the_Quad_battlements" title="Catwalks by the Quad battlements">catwalks</a> within. There, he ruthlessly killed this wizard, who was caught off-guard, with a <a href="/wiki/Killing_Curse" title="Killing Curse">Killing Curse</a> before starting a duel with <a href="/wiki/Arthur_Weasley" title="Arthur Weasley">Arthur Weasley</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-DHpt2_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DHpt2-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<ul><li>It is unclear who this wizard was, as he only appeared for a split second in <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Deathly_Hallows:_Part_2" title="Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 2">Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 2</a></i>. As he was by Order members <a href="/wiki/Arthur_Weasley" title="Arthur Weasley">Arthur Weasley</a>, <a href="/wiki/Kingsley_Shacklebolt" title="Kingsley Shacklebolt">Kingsley Shacklebolt</a> and <a href="/wiki/Remus_Lupin" title="Remus Lupin">Remus Lupin</a>, there is the possibility he was a part of the <a href="/wiki/Order_of_the_Phoenix" title="Order of the Phoenix">Order of the Phoenix</a>, but it is more likely he was a member of the Hogwarts staff.</li></ul>
