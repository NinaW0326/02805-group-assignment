<p><b>Barry Wee Willie Winkle</b> (b. <a href="/wiki/14_August" title="14 August">14 August</a>, <a href="/wiki/1236" class="mw-redirect" title="1236">1236</a>) was the <a href="/wiki/List_of_oldest_wizards" title="List of oldest wizards">oldest</a> known <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> in the <a href="/wiki/1990s" title="1990s">1990s</a>, when he reached the age of 755. To celebrate the occasion, he held a massive party, inviting every wizard and witch he had ever known, a total of thirty million people. It was one of the biggest parties in wizarding history. The scale of this party made <i><a href="/wiki/Daily_Prophet" title="Daily Prophet">Daily Prophet</a></i> headlines in 1991.<sup id="cite_ref-PSf_1-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PSf-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Barry_Winkle?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Barry_Winkle?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>How Winkle attained such an advanced age (older than even&#160;<a href="/wiki/Nicolas_Flamel" title="Nicolas Flamel">Nicolas Flamel</a>, who had access to a&#160;<a href="/wiki/Philosopher%27s_Stone" title="Philosopher&#39;s Stone">Philosopher's Stone</a>) is unknown. He may have possessed a&#160;Philosopher's Stone&#160;of his own, created a Horcrux or otherwise staved off ageing through some other magical means, though this is pure speculation. It is of course possible that he was of unusually good health, even for a wizard, and reached this age naturally, though such a thing would be unheard of.</li>
<li>If every wizard and witch he has ever known attended his party, it means either that there were wizards older than him or that <a href="/wiki/Ghost" title="Ghost">ghosts</a> also attended it. It can also be assumed, however, that it included only wizards and witches who were still alive.</li>
<li>Giving the Daily Prophet's habit for exaggerating or fluffing up details or putting in their own biased assumptions on matters to make them more intriguing, it is unknown - perhaps even unlikely - whether or not the amount of individuals attending a simple party to have actually been in the millions. In addition, Barry Winkle's exact age may also be an exaggeration.</li></ul>
