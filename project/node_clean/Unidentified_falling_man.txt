<p>This <b>man</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> who attended the <a href="/wiki/1994_Quidditch_World_Cup" title="1994 Quidditch World Cup">1994 Quidditch World Cup</a> in <a href="/wiki/August" title="August">August</a> of <a href="/wiki/1994" title="1994">1994</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-GOFF_1-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOFF-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_falling_man?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_falling_man?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Born sometime prior to <a href="/wiki/1994" title="1994">1994</a>, he attended the <a href="/wiki/1994_Quidditch_World_Cup" title="1994 Quidditch World Cup">1994 Quidditch World Cup</a>, supporting <a href="/wiki/Bulgaria" title="Bulgaria">Bulgaria</a>. He attempted to flee when the <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a> came and began <a href="/wiki/Levicorpus" title="Levicorpus">levitating</a> people and <a href="/wiki/Fire-Making_Spell" title="Fire-Making Spell">burning</a> <a href="/wiki/Charmed_Tent" title="Charmed Tent">tents</a>, but was pushed back, falling to the ground and screaming. <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>, <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ron Weasley</a> and <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione Granger</a> witnessed the man's falling as they emerged from their <a href="/wiki/Perkins%27s_tent" title="Perkins&#39;s tent">tent</a>, but they did not try to help him. It is unknown what happened to him; he either managed to escape, was trampled by the crowd, or perhaps was <a href="/wiki/Killing_Curse" title="Killing Curse">killed</a> by the <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-GOFF_1-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOFF-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
