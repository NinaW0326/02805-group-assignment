<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Slytherin_Duelling_Club_Captain?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Slytherin_Duelling_Club_Captain?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_life">Early life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Slytherin_Duelling_Club_Captain?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Early life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Slytherin_Duelling_Club_Captain?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Early life"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>The boy came to be educated at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> and was <a href="/wiki/Sorting_ceremony" title="Sorting ceremony">Sorted</a> into <a href="/wiki/Slytherin" title="Slytherin">Slytherin House</a>. His <a href="/wiki/Head_of_House" title="Head of House">Head of House</a> was <a href="/wiki/Professor" title="Professor">Professor</a> <a href="/wiki/Severus_Snape" title="Severus Snape">Severus Snape</a>, the <a href="/wiki/Potions_Master" title="Potions Master">Potions Master</a> and later <a href="/wiki/Defence_Against_the_Dark_Arts" title="Defence Against the Dark Arts">Defence Against the Dark Arts</a> teacher, and he shared a dormitory with four other boys in his year in the <a href="/wiki/Slytherin_Dungeon" title="Slytherin Dungeon">Slytherin Dungeon</a>. He apparently excelled at <a href="/wiki/Defence_Against_the_Dark_Arts" title="Defence Against the Dark Arts">Defence Against the Dark Arts</a> and/or <a href="/wiki/Duelling" title="Duelling">Duelling</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-HBPG_2-10" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBPG-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span id="1996–1997_school_year"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="1996.E2.80.931997_school_year"><a href="/wiki/1996%E2%80%931997_school_year" title="1996–1997 school year">1996–1997 school year</a></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Slytherin_Duelling_Club_Captain?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: 1996–1997 school year">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Slytherin_Duelling_Club_Captain?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: 1996–1997 school year"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>The boy became the Slytherin Duelling Club Captain in <a href="/wiki/1996" title="1996">1996</a>, when the <a href="/wiki/Duelling_Club" title="Duelling Club">Duelling Club</a> was revived by <a href="/wiki/Albus_Dumbledore" title="Albus Dumbledore">Albus Dumbledore</a> to prepare the students in case the school was attacked by <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a>. He challenged <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> to a <a href="/wiki/Duelling" title="Duelling">Duel</a> and taunted him by saying that he did not know the <a href="/wiki/Full_Body-Bind_Curse" title="Full Body-Bind Curse">Full Body-Bind Curse</a> (<i>Petrificus Totalus</i>), which Harry proceeded to use to defeat him.<sup id="cite_ref-HBPG_2-11" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBPG-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Magical_abilities_and_skills">Magical abilities and skills</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Slytherin_Duelling_Club_Captain?veaction=edit&amp;section=4" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Slytherin_Duelling_Club_Captain?action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li><b><a href="/wiki/Duelling" title="Duelling">Duelling</a></b>: As the head of the <a href="/wiki/Slytherin" title="Slytherin">Slytherin</a> <a href="/wiki/Duelling_Club" title="Duelling Club">Duelling Club</a>, this student was a highly talented duellist, who was skilled with the casting of the <a href="/wiki/Stunning_Spell" title="Stunning Spell">Stunning Spell</a>, <a href="/wiki/Disarming_Charm" title="Disarming Charm">Disarming Charm</a>, <a href="/wiki/Shield_Charm" title="Shield Charm">Shield Charm</a>, <i><a href="/wiki/Levicorpus" title="Levicorpus">Levicorpus</a></i> and the <a href="/wiki/Full_Body-Bind_Curse" title="Full Body-Bind Curse">Full Body-Bind Curse</a> in combat, meaning he was very proficient with <a href="/wiki/Martial_magic" title="Martial magic">martial magic</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-HBPG_2-12" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBPG-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup></li></ul>
