<p>A <b>talking mirror</b> is a <a href="/wiki/Mirror" title="Mirror">mirror</a> which advises anybody who looks into it on how to improve their appearance. Both the <a href="/wiki/Weasley_family" title="Weasley family">Weasley family</a> and the <a href="/wiki/Leaky_Cauldron" title="Leaky Cauldron">Leaky Cauldron</a> own one.
</p><p>Talking mirrors also seem to have a personality and mind of their own, as the one in the <a href="/wiki/The_Burrow" title="The Burrow">Weasleys'</a> seemed to be brusque and rude, ordering people rather than advising.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> The one in the <a href="/wiki/Leaky_Cauldron" title="Leaky Cauldron">Leaky Cauldron</a>, which, when <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> tried flattening his hair, said "You're fighting a losing battle there, dear."<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p><a href="/wiki/Malodora_Grymm" title="Malodora Grymm">Malodora Grymm</a>, a <a href="/wiki/Hag" title="Hag">hag</a> who lived in the <a href="/wiki/Middle_Ages" title="Middle Ages">Middle Ages</a>, regularly consulted a talking mirror to compliment her on her looks, which she had <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magically</a> enhanced using a <a href="/wiki/Beautification_Potion" title="Beautification Potion">Beautification Potion</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> 
</p><p>Talking mirrors may be considered a subtype of <a href="/wiki/Enchanted_mirror" title="Enchanted mirror">enchanted mirrors</a>.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Talking_mirror?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Talking_mirror?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>The talking <a href="/wiki/Category:Mirrors" title="Category:Mirrors">mirror</a> is similar to the <a target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener" class="external text" href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Magic_Mirror_(Snow_White)">magic mirror</a> used in the fairy tale <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Snow_White" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Snow White">Snow White</a>, which, like the one in the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_universe" title="Harry Potter universe">Harry Potter universe</a>, comments on the user's appearance. On the other hand, talking mirrors are not all-seeing (an ability which is instead echoed in <a href="/wiki/Scrying_mirror" title="Scrying mirror">scrying mirrors</a>).</li></ul>
