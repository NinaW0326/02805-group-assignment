This <b><a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch</a></b> was a student at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> at least during the <a href="/wiki/1997-1998_school_year" class="mw-redirect" title="1997-1998 school year">1997-1998 school year</a>. She participated in the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a> and helped a <a href="/wiki/Unidentified_Hogwarts_employees#Black_Hogwarts_witch_at_the_Viaduct_Courtyard_before_the_Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Unidentified Hogwarts employees">teacher</a> to go to the <a href="/wiki/Viaduct_Courtyard" title="Viaduct Courtyard">Viaduct Courtyard</a>. It is unknown if she survived.<sup id="cite_ref-DHF2_2-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DHF2-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
