<b>Albert</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> and student at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a>, who was <a href="/wiki/Sorting_ceremony" title="Sorting ceremony">sorted</a> into <a href="/wiki/Hufflepuff" title="Hufflepuff">Hufflepuff</a> in the <a href="/wiki/1990s" title="1990s">1990s</a>. He said that <a href="/wiki/Argus_Filch" title="Argus Filch">Argus Filch</a> talked to his <a href="/wiki/Mrs_Norris" title="Mrs Norris">cat</a> suggesting that he was a character in the <a href="/wiki/Plot_to_open_the_Chamber_of_Secrets" title="Plot to open the Chamber of Secrets">plot</a> to open the <a href="/wiki/Chamber_of_Secrets" title="Chamber of Secrets">Chamber of Secrets</a>, and that Filch punished him for being out of bed at night time. He said that he wished there would be an Heir of Hufflepuff and asked <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> if he could say Albert in <a href="/wiki/Parseltongue" title="Parseltongue">Parseltongue</a>. He once got a bag of beetle pincers by <a href="/wiki/Owl" title="Owl">owl</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-COSG_3-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COSG-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
