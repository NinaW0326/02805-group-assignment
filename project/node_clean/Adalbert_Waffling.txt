<p><b>Adalbert Waffling</b> (d. <a href="/wiki/1981" title="1981">1981</a>) was a <a href="/wiki/Great_Britain" title="Great Britain">British</a> <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> who was a <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magical</a> theoretician, credited to have written "all about magic". Waffling is somewhat seen as the "father of magical theory", as it is a popular claim that every modern witch or wizard has learned from his writings.
</p><p>Waffling was the one who formulated the <a href="/wiki/Fundamental_Laws_of_Magic" title="Fundamental Laws of Magic">Fundamental Laws of Magic</a> and wrote <i><a href="/wiki/Magical_Theory" title="Magical Theory">Magical Theory</a></i>, a most popular textbook still being used today by the young witches and wizards at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a>.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Adalbert_Waffling?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Adalbert_Waffling?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_life">Early life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Adalbert_Waffling?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Early life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Adalbert_Waffling?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Early life"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Adalbert Waffling was born in or before the 19th-century. By the <a href="/wiki/1890s" title="1890s">1890s</a> he was already a most notable theoretician.<sup id="cite_ref-DH2_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH2-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> Waffling might have attended <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> in his youth, like so many other wizards before him.
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Later_life">Later life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Adalbert_Waffling?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Later life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Adalbert_Waffling?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Later life"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<figure class="thumb tleft show-info-icon" style="width: 167px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/6/64/Adalbert_Waffling_USA_Card.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20100206015908" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/6/64/Adalbert_Waffling_USA_Card.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/165?cb=20100206015908" decoding="async" width="165" height="206" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="Adalbert Waffling USA Card.jpg" data-image-key="Adalbert_Waffling_USA_Card.jpg" /></a> 	 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:Adalbert_Waffling_USA_Card.jpg" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption">Waffling studying <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">Magic</a></p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure><p>By the <a href="/wiki/1890s" title="1890s">1890s</a>, Waffling was already one of most notable wizards of the date,<sup id="cite_ref-DH2_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH2-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> so it is more than likely he published some of his writings before this point. Around this time <a href="/wiki/Albus_Dumbledore" title="Albus Dumbledore">Albus Dumbledore</a>, the "<i>most brilliant student ever seen [at Hogwarts]</i>", was in regular correspondence with Waffling in order to exchange ideas about magic.<sup id="cite_ref-DH2_1-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH2-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/Minerva_McGonagall" title="Minerva McGonagall">Minerva McGonagall</a> later shared these letters with members of the <a href="/wiki/Statute_of_Secrecy_Task_Force" title="Statute of Secrecy Task Force">Statute of Secrecy Task Force</a> who pursued a <a href="/wiki/Professor" title="Professor">Professorship</a> profession to help improve their skills in addressing the <a href="/wiki/Calamity" title="Calamity">Calamity</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Waffling died in <a href="/wiki/1981" title="1981">1981</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-PSG_4-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PSG-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Adalbert_Waffling?veaction=edit&amp;section=4" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Adalbert_Waffling?action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<figure class="thumb tright show-info-icon" style="width: 182px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/c/c9/Theory.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20090904115030" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/c/c9/Theory.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/180?cb=20090904115030" decoding="async" width="180" height="250" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="Theory.jpg" data-image-key="Theory.jpg" /></a> 	 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:Theory.jpg" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption"><i><a href="/wiki/Magical_Theory" title="Magical Theory">Magical Theory</a></i>, Waffling's most popular textbook</p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure>
<ul><li>In early editions of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Prisoner_of_Azkaban" title="Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban">Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban</a></i>, <i><a href="/wiki/A_History_of_Magic" title="A History of Magic">A History of Magic</a></i> is incorrectly said to be written by Adalbert Waffling, instead of <a href="/wiki/Bathilda_Bagshot" title="Bathilda Bagshot">Bathilda Bagshot</a>.</li>
<li>In <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Deathly_Hallows" title="Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows">Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows</a></i>, it is revealed that <a href="/wiki/Albus_Dumbledore" title="Albus Dumbledore">Albus Dumbledore</a> corresponded with Waffling during his time at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a>. However, the dates given for Waffling's birth on his <a href="/wiki/Chocolate_Frog_Card" title="Chocolate Frog Card">Chocolate Frog Card</a> say that he was born after Dumbledore graduated (in <a href="/wiki/1899" class="mw-redirect" title="1899">1899</a>).</li>
<li>Adalbert Waffling's death date coincides with the height of the <a href="/wiki/First_Wizarding_War" title="First Wizarding War">First Wizarding War</a> and the original <a href="/wiki/Attack_at_Godric%27s_Hollow_(1981)" title="Attack at Godric&#39;s Hollow (1981)">downfall of Lord Voldemort</a>. It is unknown if he died as a result of the war, or died of another reason.</li></ul>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Adalbert_Waffling?veaction=edit&amp;section=5" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Adalbert_Waffling?action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p><i>Adal</i> is Old High German for "<i>noble</i>" or "<i>aristocratic</i>"; <i>berta</i>, "<i>bright</i>". To waffle means unable to make a firm decision.
</p>
