<b>Sean Peakes</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Hufflepuff" title="Hufflepuff">Hufflepuff</a> <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">student</a> at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> in the <a href="/wiki/1990s" title="1990s">1990s</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-HBPG_3-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBPG-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Sean_Peakes?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Sean_Peakes?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>He bought the last <a href="/wiki/Reusable_Hangman" title="Reusable Hangman">Reusable Hangman</a> that <a href="/wiki/Weasleys%27_Wizard_Wheezes" title="Weasleys&#39; Wizard Wheezes">Weasleys' Wizard Wheezes</a> had in stock in <a href="/wiki/1996" title="1996">1996</a>. At Hogwarts later that year, a group of <a href="/wiki/Slytherin" title="Slytherin">Slytherins</a> tried to steal the game from him, so he hid from them in the <a href="/wiki/Clock_Tower" title="Clock Tower">Clock Tower</a>. <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>, who was looking for a Reusable Hangman for <a href="/wiki/Ginevra_Weasley" title="Ginevra Weasley">Ginny Weasley</a>, found him there, and he agreed to give the game to Harry in exchange for three <a href="/wiki/Stink_Pellets" title="Stink Pellets">Stink Pellets</a>. He planned to use these Stink Pellets in order to drive off the Slytherins if they gave him trouble again.<sup id="cite_ref-HBPG_3-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBPG-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>He may have been related to <a href="/wiki/Glanmore_Peakes" title="Glanmore Peakes">Glanmore Peakes</a> or to <a href="/wiki/Jimmy_Peakes" title="Jimmy Peakes">Jimmy Peakes</a>, who attended Hogwarts around the same time, but was in <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor" title="Gryffindor">Gryffindor</a>.
</p>
