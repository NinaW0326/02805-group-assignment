<b>Kenny Markham</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor" title="Gryffindor">Gryffindor</a> student at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> in the <a href="/wiki/1990s" title="1990s">1990s</a>.
</p><p>As a lover of <a href="/wiki/Wizard%27s_Chess" title="Wizard&#39;s Chess">wizard's chess</a>, he collected chess pieces, and traded his extra ones with other students for <a href="/wiki/Gobstones" title="Gobstones">Gobstones</a> or <a href="/wiki/Chocolate_Frog_Card" title="Chocolate Frog Card">Chocolate Frog Cards</a>. He could usually be found in the <a href="/wiki/Classroom_1B" title="Classroom 1B">Transfiguration Classroom</a> during the <a href="/wiki/1996%E2%80%931997_school_year" title="1996–1997 school year">1996–1997 school year</a>.
</p>
