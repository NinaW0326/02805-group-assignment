This <b>individual</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> who fought during the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a> in <a href="/wiki/1998" title="1998">1998</a>. He was hiding behind some stones at the <a href="/wiki/Viaduct_Courtyard" title="Viaduct Courtyard">Viaduct Courtyard</a> <a href="/wiki/Viaduct_Courtyard_Gatehouses" title="Viaduct Courtyard Gatehouses">checkpoint towers</a> when it exploded. It is possible that he was waiting for some Death Eater to pass by there, hoping to kill him from cover and without the risk of counter-attack. Another option is that he could be injured. It's unknown if he survived the battle.<sup id="cite_ref-DHF2_1-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DHF2-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
