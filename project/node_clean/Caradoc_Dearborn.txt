<p><b>Caradoc Dearborn</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Great_Britain" title="Great Britain">British</a> <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> and member of the original <a href="/wiki/Order_of_the_Phoenix" title="Order of the Phoenix">Order of the Phoenix</a>. He fought in the <a href="/wiki/First_Wizarding_War" title="First Wizarding War">First Wizarding War</a> and was presumably murdered by <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a> before the end of the war. His body was never found.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP9_1-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP9-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Caradoc_Dearborn?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Caradoc_Dearborn?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>During the <a href="/wiki/First_Wizarding_War" title="First Wizarding War">First Wizarding War</a>, Caradoc became a member of the <a href="/wiki/Order_of_the_Phoenix" title="Order of the Phoenix">Order of the Phoenix</a>, an organisation led and founded by <a href="/wiki/Albus_Dumbledore" title="Albus Dumbledore">Albus Dumbledore</a>, in order to oppose the <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Dark Lord Voldemort</a> and his <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP9_1-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP9-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>During the height of the conflict, Caradoc vanished and was never heard from afterwards. According to <a href="/wiki/Alastor_Moody" title="Alastor Moody">Alastor Moody</a>, he disappeared six months after the photograph of the original Order was taken. His body was never found and he was presumed dead.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP9_1-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP9-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<ul><li><b><a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">Magical</a> skills</b>: To be a member of the <a href="/wiki/Order_of_the_Phoenix" title="Order of the Phoenix">Order of the Phoenix</a>, it is highly likely that Caradoc was a talented wizard and most likely used his magical ability in a number of battles against the <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a> before his untimely death.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP9_1-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP9-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup></li></ul>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Caradoc_Dearborn?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Caradoc_Dearborn?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p><a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Caradoc" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Caradoc">Caradoc</a>, sometimes known as <i>Caradog</i>, was one of the Knights of the Round Table in Arthurian legend. He was also a semi-legendary ancestor of the mediaeval kings of the Welsh <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Kingdom_of_Gwent" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Kingdom of Gwent">Kingdom of Gwent</a>. <i>Caractacus</i> is the Latinized form of Caradoc.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
