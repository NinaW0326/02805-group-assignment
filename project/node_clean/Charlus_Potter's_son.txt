<b>Charlus Potter's son</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Pure-blood" title="Pure-blood">pure-blood</a> <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> and the son and only child of <a href="/wiki/Charlus_Potter" title="Charlus Potter">Charlus Potter</a> and <a href="/wiki/Dorea_Black" title="Dorea Black">Dorea Potter</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-BFT_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-BFT-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> and a first cousin of <a href="/wiki/Walburga_Black" title="Walburga Black">Walburga</a>, <a href="/wiki/Alphard_Black" title="Alphard Black">Alphard</a> and <a href="/wiki/Cygnus_Black_III" title="Cygnus Black III">Cygnus Black</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-BFT_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-BFT-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Charlus_Potter%27s_son?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Charlus_Potter%27s_son?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>It was once speculated that <a href="/wiki/James_Potter_I" title="James Potter I">James Potter</a> was in fact Charlus Potter's son. However, the actual names of James's parents, <a href="/wiki/Fleamont_Potter" title="Fleamont Potter">Fleamont</a> and <a href="/wiki/Euphemia_Potter" title="Euphemia Potter">Euphemia Potter</a>, were revealed via  <i><a href="/wiki/Pottermore" title="Pottermore">Pottermore</a></i>.<sup id="cite_ref-PotterFamilyPottermore_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PotterFamilyPottermore-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> In spite of this, it is still more than likely that Charlus might be a cousin of James Potter and his descendants.</li>
<li>Ironically, although it was not James, this man was related to many of those close to <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>, for instance, being a first cousin once removed of <a href="/wiki/Sirius_Black" title="Sirius Black">Sirius and</a> <a href="/wiki/Regulus_Black" title="Regulus Black">Regulus Black</a> and <a href="/wiki/Bellatrix_Lestrange" title="Bellatrix Lestrange">Bellatrix</a>, <a href="/wiki/Andromeda_Tonks" title="Andromeda Tonks">Andromeda</a> and <a href="/wiki/Narcissa_Malfoy" title="Narcissa Malfoy">Narcissa Black</a>, and so a cousin of <a href="/wiki/Nymphadora_Tonks" title="Nymphadora Tonks">Nymphadora and</a> <a href="/wiki/Edward_Lupin" title="Edward Lupin">Ted Lupin</a> and <a href="/wiki/Draco_Malfoy" title="Draco Malfoy">Draco and</a> <a href="/wiki/Scorpius_Malfoy" title="Scorpius Malfoy">Scorpius Malfoy</a> respectively, a second cousin of <a href="/wiki/Arthur_Weasley" title="Arthur Weasley">Arthur Weasley</a>, possibly a second cousin of <a href="/wiki/Bartemius_Crouch_Senior" title="Bartemius Crouch Senior">Bartemius Crouch Snr</a><sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> and <a href="/wiki/Bartemius_Crouch_Junior" title="Bartemius Crouch Junior">his son</a>, and a possible distant relative of <a href="/wiki/Neville_Longbottom" title="Neville Longbottom">Neville Longbottom</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li>If Harry really didn't have any living relatives other than the <a href="/wiki/Dursley_family" title="Dursley family">Dursleys</a> by&#160;<a href="/wiki/1981" title="1981">1981</a>, it can be assumed that Charlus, Dorea and their son were all dead by that date.</li>
<li>This individual was not featured on the Potter family tree on Pottermore.</li></ul>
