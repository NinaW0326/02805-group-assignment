<p><b>James Sirius</b><sup id="cite_ref-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-WFT_8-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-WFT-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup> <b>Potter</b> (b. c. <a href="/wiki/2004" title="2004">2004</a><sup id="cite_ref-JKR-twitter-James-Potter_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-JKR-twitter-James-Potter-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>) was an <a href="/wiki/England" title="England">English</a> <a href="/wiki/Half-blood" title="Half-blood">half-blood</a><sup id="cite_ref-blood_2-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-blood-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a>, the eldest child of <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry</a> and <a href="/wiki/Ginevra_Weasley" title="Ginevra Weasley">Ginevra Potter</a> <span class="template-c">(née Weasley)</span>. James was two years older than his brother <a href="/wiki/Albus_Potter" title="Albus Potter">Albus Severus</a> and four years older than his sister <a href="/wiki/Lily_L._Potter" title="Lily L. Potter">Lily Luna</a>. His godparents, as well as his uncle and aunt were <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ron Weasley</a> and <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione Granger</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup>  
</p><p>James was named in honour of his paternal grandfather and his father's godfather (<a href="/wiki/James_Potter_I" title="James Potter I">James Potter</a> and <a href="/wiki/Sirius_Black" title="Sirius Black">Sirius Black</a>). In <a href="/wiki/2015" title="2015">2015</a> he began attending <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> and was to no one's surprise sorted into <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor" title="Gryffindor">Gryffindor House</a>.
</p>

<h3><span id="Early_life_(2004-2015)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_life_.282004-2015.29">Early life <span class="template-c">(2004-2015)</span></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/James_Potter_II?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Early life (2004-2015)">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/James_Potter_II?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Early life (2004-2015)"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<div class="quote">
<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;Pottermore">"<i>The <a href="/wiki/Potter_family" title="Potter family">Potter family</a> —  minus Mother, Ginny Potter, who of course is here in the journalists’ enclosure with me —  have been given prime places in the front row.</i>"</span></dd>
<dd>&#8212;The 2014 Quidditch World Cup<sup class="noprint"><a href="/wiki/Pottermore" title="Pottermore">[src]</a></sup></dd>
<dd></dd></dl></div>
<p>James Sirius Potter was born in either 2003 or 2004 to <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry</a> and <a href="/wiki/Ginevra_Weasley" title="Ginevra Weasley">Ginny Potter</a>. He was named in honour of his paternal grandfather and his father's godfather. 
</p>
<figure class="thumb tleft show-info-icon" style="width: 237px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/f/ff/The_Potters_at_the_2014_Quidditch_World_Cup_final.png/revision/latest?cb=20180318021353" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/f/ff/The_Potters_at_the_2014_Quidditch_World_Cup_final.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/235?cb=20180318021353" decoding="async" width="235" height="166" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="The Potters at the 2014 Quidditch World Cup final.png" data-image-key="The_Potters_at_the_2014_Quidditch_World_Cup_final.png" /></a> 	 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:The_Potters_at_the_2014_Quidditch_World_Cup_final.png" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption">James attends the <a href="/wiki/2014_Quidditch_World_Cup" title="2014 Quidditch World Cup">2014 Quidditch World Cup</a> with his family</p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure>
<p>In 2014, James, along with his family, attended the finale of the Quidditch World Cup in the Patagonian Desert. A few days before the match, his father introduced him and his younger brother <a href="/wiki/Albus_Potter" title="Albus Potter">Albus</a> to the <a href="/wiki/Bulgarian_National_Quidditch_team" title="Bulgarian National Quidditch team">Bulgarian National Quidditch team</a> <a href="/wiki/Seeker" title="Seeker">Seeker</a>, and an old associate of his father, <a href="/wiki/Viktor_Krum" title="Viktor Krum">Viktor Krum</a>. James along with the rest of his family, with the exception of his mother (who was in the journalists' enclosure), sported Bulgarian red. 
</p><p>James laughed when Albus almost toppled out of the VIP box cheering when <a href="/wiki/Gon%C3%A7alo_Flores" title="Gonçalo Flores">Gonçalo</a> <a href="/wiki/Gon%C3%A7alo_Flores" title="Gonçalo Flores">Flores</a> scored the first goal of the game, but his uncle <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ron</a> grabbed the back of his robes and saved him. Due to James laughing, <i><a href="/wiki/Daily_Prophet" title="Daily Prophet">Daily Prophet</a></i> correspondent <a href="/wiki/Rita_Skeeter" title="Rita Skeeter">Rita Skeeter</a> (who was covering the match live with Ginny) suggested that he may have pushed Albus.<sup id="cite_ref-the-qwc2014-final_10-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-the-qwc2014-final-10">&#91;10&#93;</a></sup> James was seen applauding after Bulgaria won the tournament.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Personality_and_traits">Personality and traits</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/James_Potter_II?veaction=edit&amp;section=4" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Personality and traits">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/James_Potter_II?action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Personality and traits"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<div class="quote">
<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows">"<i>You interrupted them? You are </i>so<i> like Ron —</i>"</span></dd>
<dd>&#8212;Ginny regarding her son's personality<sup class="noprint"><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Deathly_Hallows" title="Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows">[src]</a></sup></dd>
<dd></dd></dl></div>
<p>James was much very like his uncle (<a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ron Weasley</a>), in terms of his poor timing and knack for interfering with other people's love lives. He also seemed to have inherited his good sense of humour and love of mischief from his two uncles, <a href="/wiki/Fred_Weasley" title="Fred Weasley">Fred</a> and <a href="/wiki/George_Weasley" title="George Weasley">George Weasley</a>. 
</p><p>He was also very much like his two namesakes, in his mischievous and trouble-making nature. He was shown to be very teasing, particularly towards his younger brother, often making comments to worry him about Hogwarts. Unlike his brother, James appeared to be rather independent and confident about himself. Harry remarked that, "<i>everything comes easy for James</i>", suggesting that he was easygoing and popular with others his own age.  
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/James_Potter_II?veaction=edit&amp;section=11" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/James_Potter_II?action=edit&amp;section=11" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li><i><b>James</b></i> is a Hebrew name from the name Jacob (יַעֲקֹב), meaning <i>"Heel"</i> as in Genesis narrative, Jacob was born grasping Esau's heel and later bought his birthright. This was the name of James's grandfather and the middle name of his father.</li>
<li><i><b>Sirius</b></i> is the meaning of <i>"Star"</i> as it is the brightest star seen from Earth, it is also the Dog Star from Ancient Greek Mythology as he was Orion's dog. This is the name of James's father's godfather.</li>
<li>Potter is a very common surname of English origin. It traditionally refers to the occupation of making pottery. Beatrix Potter was a well-known English children's author.</li>
<li>Potter's Field is the name for a burial ground for the unknown or unclaimed dead, particularly soldiers and orphans; the Potter family had many members that fit that description.</li>
<li>The Potter Box is a model for making ethical decisions developed at Harvard University.</li></ul>
