<p>This <b>individual</b> (born c. <a href="/wiki/1973" title="1973">1973</a>-<a href="/wiki/1975" title="1975">1975</a>) was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> who, in the early <a href="/wiki/1990s" title="1990s">1990s</a>, was one of the <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor" title="Gryffindor">Gryffindor</a> <a href="/wiki/Prefect" title="Prefect">Prefects</a> at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-PSgba_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PSgba-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>He was patrolling the <a href="/wiki/Seventh-floor_corridor" title="Seventh-floor corridor">Seventh-floor corridor</a> on the night of <a href="/wiki/12_September" title="12 September">12 September</a>, <a href="/wiki/1991" title="1991">1991</a>, the night when <a href="/wiki/Draco_Malfoy" title="Draco Malfoy">Draco Malfoy</a> challenged <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> for a <a href="/wiki/Duel" class="mw-redirect" title="Duel">wizards' duel</a> on the <a href="/wiki/Trophy_Room" title="Trophy Room">Trophy Room</a> at midnight. As Harry was making his way there, he found this Prefect pacing back and forth and blocking the shortest route to the stairs.<sup id="cite_ref-PSgba_3-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PSgba-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
