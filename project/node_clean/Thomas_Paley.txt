<b>Thomas Paley</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor" title="Gryffindor">Gryffindor</a> <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">student</a> at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> in the <a href="/wiki/1990s" title="1990s">1990s</a>. He was usually seen spending time by the <a href="/wiki/Viaduct" title="Viaduct">Viaduct</a> with <a href="/wiki/Terrence_Fogarty" title="Terrence Fogarty">Terrence Fogarty</a> during the <a href="/wiki/1996%E2%80%931997_school_year" title="1996–1997 school year">1996–1997 school year</a>. He confessed to <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> that he would like a new <a href="/wiki/Broomstick" title="Broomstick">broom</a> and admitted it was a disaster in <a href="/wiki/Potion" title="Potion">Potions</a>.
</p>
