<p><b>Madam Borboleta Candies Ltd.</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizarding</a> confectionary company.<sup id="cite_ref-butterfly_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-butterfly-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> It was established in <a href="/wiki/1913" title="1913">1913</a>, presumably by <a href="/wiki/Borboleta" title="Borboleta">Madam Borboleta</a>, and had offices in <a href="/wiki/Paris" title="Paris">Paris</a> and <a href="/wiki/London" title="London">London</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-butterfly_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-butterfly-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> One of the products this company manufactured was <a href="/wiki/Sugared_Butterfly_Wings" title="Sugared Butterfly Wings">Sugared Butterfly Wings</a>.
</p>

<ul><li>The dates "<a href="/wiki/1913" title="1913">1913</a>-<a href="/wiki/2003" title="2003">2003</a>" are printed on the <a href="/wiki/Sugared_Butterfly_Wings" title="Sugared Butterfly Wings">Sugared Butterfly Wings</a> tin.<sup id="cite_ref-butterfly_1-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-butterfly-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> This is an anachronism, as the film in which the tin appears, <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Prisoner_of_Azkaban_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban (film)">Prisoner of Azkaban</a></i>, is set in <a href="/wiki/1993%E2%80%931994_school_year" title="1993â€“1994 school year">1993-1994</a>.</li>
<li><i>Borboleta</i> is a Portuguese noun meaning "butterfly". This is most likely a reference to the company's only known product, Sugared Butterfly Wings.</li></ul>
