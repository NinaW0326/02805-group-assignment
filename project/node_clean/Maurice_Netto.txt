<b>Maurice Netto</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> who lived during the <a href="/wiki/20th_century" title="20th century">20th century</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-trophy_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-trophy-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Netto attended <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> in the <a href="/wiki/1940s" title="1940s">1940s</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-trophy_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-trophy-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> He received an award at the school in <a href="/wiki/1945" title="1945">1945</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-trophy_1-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-trophy-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>His name was carved into a wood panel on the wall of <a href="/wiki/Sybill_Trelawney" title="Sybill Trelawney">Professor Trelawney's</a> <a href="/wiki/Divination_(class)" title="Divination (class)">Divination</a> <a href="/wiki/Divination_Classroom" title="Divination Classroom">classroom</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
