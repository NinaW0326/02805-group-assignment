These <b>individuals</b> were students at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> in the <a href="/wiki/1990s" title="1990s">1990s</a>. They fought during the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a> in <a href="/wiki/1998" title="1998">1998</a> against the <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a>, but then died on the <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Castle#Seventh_Floor" title="Hogwarts Castle">Seventh Floor</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
