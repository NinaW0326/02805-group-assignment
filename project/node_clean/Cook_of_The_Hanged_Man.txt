<p>The <b>cook</b> working at <a href="/wiki/The_Hanged_Man" title="The Hanged Man">The Hanged Man</a> was a resident of <a href="/wiki/Little_Hangleton" title="Little Hangleton">Little Hangleton</a> in <a href="/wiki/1943" title="1943">1943</a>. When all three <a href="/wiki/Riddle_family" title="Riddle family">Riddles</a> living in the area were murdered in their own <a href="/wiki/Riddle_House" title="Riddle House">house</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Frank_Bryce" title="Frank Bryce">Frank Bryce</a> was framed for the murder (the real murderer being <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Voldemort</a>) the townsfolk gossiped about it all around town. That night, many residents converged at the Hanged Man, the local tavern, to voice their opinions, the cook being one of them. He, like the rest, believed Frank had committed the murder.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
