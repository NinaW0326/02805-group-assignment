<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Vinda_Rosier?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Vinda_Rosier?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Vinda Rosier became a loyal and devoted follower and acolyte of <a href="/wiki/Gellert_Grindelwald" title="Gellert Grindelwald">Gellert Grindelwald</a> at some point before <a href="/wiki/1927" title="1927">1927</a>. She appeared to be Grindelwald's lieutenant.<sup id="cite_ref-COGS_7-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COGS-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>When Rosier and her fellow acolytes <a href="/wiki/Killing_Curse" title="Killing Curse">killed</a> the owners of a <a href="/wiki/Unidentified_home_used_by_Gellert_Grindelwald" title="Unidentified home used by Gellert Grindelwald">house</a>, making it their hiding place, she spoke to Grindelwald about the world in which they would be able to kill every single <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">non-magic person</a>, but he told her that they would not kill every single one (that the beast of burden will always be necessary).<sup id="cite_ref-COGS_7-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COGS-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>In the <a href="/wiki/French_Ministry_of_Magic_Headquarters" title="French Ministry of Magic Headquarters">French Ministry of Magic</a> she enabled <a href="/wiki/Abernathy" title="Abernathy">Abernathy</a>, under the <a href="/wiki/Human_transfiguration" class="mw-redirect" title="Human transfiguration">disguise</a> of an elderly lady, to safely depart from the Ministry with a stolen <a href="/wiki/Lestrange_family" title="Lestrange family">Lestrange family</a> box from the <a href="/wiki/French_Ministry_of_Magic_Records_Room" title="French Ministry of Magic Records Room">Records Room</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-COGS_7-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COGS-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<figure class="thumb tleft show-info-icon" style="width: 222px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/9/97/Vinda_Queenie.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20190325020105" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/9/97/Vinda_Queenie.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/220?cb=20190325020105" decoding="async" width="220" height="91" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="Vinda Queenie.jpg" data-image-key="Vinda_Queenie.jpg" /></a> 	 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:Vinda_Queenie.jpg" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption">Vinda finds Queenie in one of the Paris' streets</p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure>
<p>Later on, Vinda located a distraught <a href="/wiki/Queenie_Goldstein" title="Queenie Goldstein">Queenie Goldstein</a> and brought her to the house being used by Grindelwald and his acolytes. At the headquarters she comforted her and provided her with <a href="/wiki/Tea" title="Tea">tea</a>. When Queenie informed her she intended to leave so she could see her sister <a href="/wiki/Porpentina_Goldstein" title="Porpentina Goldstein">Tina</a>, she confessed she was deeply committed to Grindelwald, their host, and he entered the room and implored Goldstein to join him.<sup id="cite_ref-COGS_7-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COGS-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<figure class="thumb tright show-info-icon" style="width: 222px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/2/28/Vinda_joining_Grindelwald.gif/revision/latest?cb=20190326102442" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/2/28/Vinda_joining_Grindelwald.gif/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/220?cb=20190326102442" decoding="async" width="220" height="92" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="Vinda joining Grindelwald.gif" data-image-key="Vinda_joining_Grindelwald.gif" /></a> 	 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:Vinda_joining_Grindelwald.gif" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption">Vinda in the circle of flames</p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure>
<p>Rosier attended the subsequent <a href="/wiki/Gellert_Grindelwald%27s_Paris_rally" title="Gellert Grindelwald&#39;s Paris rally">rally of Grindelwald's</a> at the <a href="/wiki/Lestrange_Mausoleum" title="Lestrange Mausoleum">Lestrange Mausoleum</a> as one of his acolytes. She went onstage to give Grindelwald a <a href="/wiki/Gellert_Grindelwald%27s_skull-hookah" title="Gellert Grindelwald&#39;s skull-hookah">skull hookah</a> to show everyone <a href="/wiki/Vision" title="Vision">visions</a> of the violence, warfare and bloodshed of the non-magical people, more specifically visions of the future <a href="/wiki/Second_World_War" title="Second World War">Second World War</a>. After a <a href="https://harrypotter.fandom.com/wiki/Red-haired_young_witch" class="extiw" title="en:Red-haired young witch">young witch with red hair</a> was killed by an Auror after she tried to attack him, Grindelwald in response to the Aurors' opposition to him, <a href="/wiki/Conjuration" title="Conjuration">conjured</a> <a href="/wiki/Protego_Diabolica" title="Protego Diabolica">a circle of protective fire around himself</a> to test the loyalty of his followers; she stepped into the black <a href="/wiki/Fire" title="Fire">flames</a> unaffected and unharmed, as she was truly loyal to him and his cause. 
Whilst still holding <a href="/wiki/Gellert_Grindelwald%27s_skull-hookah" title="Gellert Grindelwald&#39;s skull-hookah">skull hookah</a>, she was <a href="/wiki/Stunning_Spell" title="Stunning Spell">stunned</a> by <a href="/wiki/Leta_Lestrange" title="Leta Lestrange">Leta Lestrange</a>, causing the skull to be dropped and blasted into pieces. After that, she <a href="/wiki/Apparition" title="Apparition">disapparated</a> away intending to spread Grindelwald's words and message to the wider <a href="/wiki/Wizarding_world" title="Wizarding world">wizarding world</a>, as all of his followers had been commanded to do.<sup id="cite_ref-COGS_7-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COGS-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Personality_and_traits">Personality and traits</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Vinda_Rosier?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Personality and traits">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Vinda_Rosier?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Personality and traits"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<div class="quote">

</div><figure class="thumb tleft show-info-icon" style="width: 222px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/d/d5/Vinda_talking_with_Queenie.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20190325020255" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/d/d5/Vinda_talking_with_Queenie.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/220?cb=20190325020255" decoding="async" width="220" height="91" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="Vinda talking with Queenie.jpg" data-image-key="Vinda_talking_with_Queenie.jpg" /></a> 	 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:Vinda_talking_with_Queenie.jpg" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption">Vinda with Queenie Goldstein</p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure>
<p>Rosier was a mysteriously charming and ultimately malevolent witch, who was an avowed and loyal follower of the <a href="/wiki/Dark_Wizard" title="Dark Wizard">Dark Wizard</a> <a href="/wiki/Gellert_Grindelwald" title="Gellert Grindelwald">Gellert Grindelwald</a>, and supported his <a href="/wiki/Vision" title="Vision">vision</a> of <a href="/wiki/Wizarding_supremacy" title="Wizarding supremacy">wizarding supremacy</a> over <a href="/wiki/Muggles" class="mw-redirect" title="Muggles">non-magical humans</a> and their world. Vinda was outwardly courteous, sophisticated, and refined, and despite her deeply sinister ideology, could show kindness to those in need when it suited her to do so, as shown when she comforted and invited in a distraught <a href="/wiki/Queenie_Goldstein" title="Queenie Goldstein">Queenie Goldstein</a> off the raining streets of <a href="/wiki/Paris" title="Paris">Paris</a>, for <a href="/wiki/Tea" title="Tea">tea</a> in Grindelwald's abode. However, her motivation for doing this, would appear to be unknowingly place Queenie under Grindelwald's presence and influence, in the hope of recruiting new followers to his sinister cause.<sup id="cite_ref-COGS_7-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COGS-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Indeed, despite her seeming benevolence and good will, Vinda was in reality a very ill-intentioned witch, who desired to see wizards and witches rise up above non-magical peoples and rule them, or alternatively exterminate them. This lack of concern for the non-magical was made evident when she, <a href="/wiki/Carrow_(1920s)" title="Carrow (1920s)">Carrow</a>, and the other <a href="/wiki/Gellert_Grindelwald%27s_acolytes" title="Gellert Grindelwald&#39;s acolytes">Acolytes</a> murdered the <a href="/wiki/Muggle" class="mw-redirect" title="Muggle">Non-Magique</a> couple who owned the home, which they used as a temporary hideout. Ultimately, unlike most other followers of Grindelwald, Vinda was loyal to Grindelwald's true vision, but was more extreme, and proudly stated that she wanted the extermination of all non-magical individuals.<sup id="cite_ref-COGS_7-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COGS-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<p>Vinda was arguably <a href="/wiki/Gellert_Grindelwald" title="Gellert Grindelwald">Gellert Grindelwald</a>'s most trusted and loyal Acolyte, therefore being the Dark Wizard's anonymous lieutenant. Grindelwald trusted her with consoling Queenie Goldstein to soften her up for Grindelwald to convince her and also with keeping his <a href="/wiki/Gellert_Grindelwald%27s_skull-hookah" title="Gellert Grindelwald&#39;s skull-hookah">Skull-Hookah</a>, displaying his deep trust for Vinda. When Vinda casually and proudly stated that Grindelwald's new order would bring the destruction of all <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Non-magic people</a> and have them fleeing their cities in the millions, he told her they don't say such things out loud, but that they only want the freedom to be themselves and that they would not eliminate all muggles since the beast of burden would always be necessary.<sup id="cite_ref-COGS_7-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COGS-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Vinda_Rosier?veaction=edit&amp;section=6" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Vinda_Rosier?action=edit&amp;section=6" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>The name "Vinda" is a word of primarily Germanic/Norse origin that means "to wind; twist; wring."</li>
<li>Rosier is, most notable, the name of a fallen angel (a Dominion of the Second Sphere) and patron demon of tainted love and seduction.
<ul><li>In the Christian angelic hierarchy, Dominions regulate the duties of lower angels, and make themselves physically known to humans with extreme rarity.</li>
<li>Rosier is also a French name that means "rose bush" or "from the rose bush."</li></ul></li></ul>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Vinda_Rosier?veaction=edit&amp;section=7" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Vinda_Rosier?action=edit&amp;section=7" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>Vinda Rosier is portrayed by <a href="/wiki/Poppy_Corby-Tuech" title="Poppy Corby-Tuech">Poppy Corby-Tuech</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Fantastic_Beasts:_The_Crimes_of_Grindelwald" title="Fantastic Beasts: The Crimes of Grindelwald">second instalment</a> of the <i><a href="/wiki/Fantastic_Beasts_(film_series)" title="Fantastic Beasts (film series)">Fantastic Beasts</a></i> film series.<sup id="cite_ref-pm_3-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-pm-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li>Her name is given only as "Rosier (female)" in <i><a href="/wiki/Fantastic_Beasts:_The_Crimes_of_Grindelwald_-_The_Original_Screenplay" title="Fantastic Beasts: The Crimes of Grindelwald - The Original Screenplay">Fantastic Beasts: The Crimes of Grindelwald - The Original Screenplay</a></i>, but her first name was revealed on  <i><a href="/wiki/Pottermore" title="Pottermore">Pottermore</a></i>.</li>
<li>Vinda is similar to <a href="/wiki/Bellatrix_Lestrange" title="Bellatrix Lestrange">Bellatrix Lestrange</a> because they both served as deputy to dark wizards (Vinda to <a href="/wiki/Gellert_Grindelwald" title="Gellert Grindelwald">Grindelwald</a> and Bellatrix to <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Voldemort</a>). It is also possible that they're both related because the family name of Bellatrix's mother - <a href="/wiki/Druella_Black" title="Druella Black">Druella</a> before the marriage was a <a href="/wiki/Rosier_family" title="Rosier family">Rosier</a> like the family name of Vinda, and therefore Vinda could be a maternal relative of Bellatrix.</li></ul>
