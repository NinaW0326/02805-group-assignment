<b>Eglantine Lestrange </b>was a French <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch</a> and a member of the <a href="/wiki/Pure-blood" title="Pure-blood">pure-blood</a> <a href="/wiki/Lestrange_family" title="Lestrange family">Lestrange family</a>. She was the daughter of <a href="/wiki/Falco_Lestrange" title="Falco Lestrange">Falco Lestrange</a> and <a href="/wiki/Salom%C3%A9_Lestrange" title="Salomé Lestrange">Salomé Lestrange</a>. She married her cousin<sup id="cite_ref-family_tree_1-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-family_tree-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/Corvus_Lestrange_(III)" title="Corvus Lestrange (III)">Corvus Lestrange</a> and had a son with him, <a href="/wiki/Corvus_Lestrange_(IV)" title="Corvus Lestrange (IV)">Corvus</a>, through whom she was the grandmother of <a href="/wiki/Leta_Lestrange" title="Leta Lestrange">Leta Lestrange</a> and <a href="/wiki/Corvus_Lestrange_(V)" title="Corvus Lestrange (V)">Corvus Lestrange</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Eglantine_Lestrange?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Eglantine_Lestrange?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Eglantine's name originates from Old French and it means "spiny, needle-like", which is in reference to the thorns of the eglantine, which is a wild rose also known as sweet briar.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Eglantine_Lestrange?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Eglantine_Lestrange?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>Unlike all other spouses included in the family tree (<a href="/wiki/Heloise_Lestrange" title="Heloise Lestrange">Heloise Beaufort</a>, <a href="/wiki/Minette_Lestrange" title="Minette Lestrange">Minette Rosier</a>, <a href="/wiki/Laurena_Kama" title="Laurena Kama">Laurena Kama</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Clarisse_Lestrange" title="Clarisse Lestrange">Clarisse Tremblay</a>), Eglantine is depicted with the Lestrange surname. This might signify a case of <a href="/wiki/Inbreeding" title="Inbreeding">inbreeding</a>, since this was reported as a practise of the <a href="/wiki/Lestrange_family" title="Lestrange family">Lestrange family</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
<ul><li>A family tree released later reveals that Eglantine and Corvus (III) were first cousins, as their fathers (Falco and Corvus (II), respectively) were brothers.<sup id="cite_ref-family_tree_1-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-family_tree-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup></li></ul></li></ul>
