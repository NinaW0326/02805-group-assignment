<p>This <b>Ministry of Magic wizard</b> was an employee of the <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">British Ministry of Magic</a>. In <a href="/wiki/1994" title="1994">1994</a>, he was working at the <a href="/wiki/Quidditch_World_Cup" title="Quidditch World Cup">Quidditch World Cup</a>, helping to keep up security to prevent drawing attention from <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggles</a>. He was seen attempting to persuade a wizard named <a href="/wiki/Archie_Aymslowe" title="Archie Aymslowe">Archie Aymslowe</a> to change out of his flowery nightgown and into a pair of trousers.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
