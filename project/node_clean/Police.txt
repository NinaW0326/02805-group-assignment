</p><h2><span class="mw-headline" id="History">History</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Police?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: History">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Police?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: History"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<div class="quote">
<dl><dd><span>"<i><a href="/wiki/Arthur_Weasley" title="Arthur Weasley">Arnold Weasley</a>, who was charged with possession of a <a href="/wiki/Flying_Ford_Anglia" title="Flying Ford Anglia">flying car</a> two years ago, was yesterday involved in a tussle with several Muggle law-keepers ("policemen") over a number of highly aggressive dustbins.</i>"</span></dd>
<dd>&#8212;<a href="/wiki/Rita_Skeeter" title="Rita Skeeter">Rita Skeeter</a>, <i><a href="/wiki/FURTHER_MISTAKES_AT_THE_MINISTRY_OF_MAGIC" title="FURTHER MISTAKES AT THE MINISTRY OF MAGIC">FURTHER MISTAKES AT THE MINISTRY OF MAGIC</a></i>, the <i><a href="/wiki/Daily_Prophet" title="Daily Prophet">Daily Prophet</a></i></dd>
<dd></dd></dl></div><p>Three years before the birth of <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> two policemen named PC <a href="/wiki/Anderson" title="Anderson">Anderson</a> and Sergeant <a href="/wiki/Fisher" title="Fisher">Fisher</a> chased two young boys on a <a href="/wiki/Sirius_Black%27s_motorbike" class="mw-redirect" title="Sirius Black&#39;s motorbike">motorbike</a>. They caught the boys in a dead-end alley and asked for their names. The two boys first gave three random names: Wilberforce, Bathsheba and Elvendork. But soon after, they identified themselves as <a href="/wiki/James_Potter_I" title="James Potter I">James Potter</a> and <a href="/wiki/Sirius_Black" title="Sirius Black">Sirius Black</a>. The two policemen saw <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">three men</a> flying on <a href="/wiki/Broomstick" title="Broomstick">broomsticks</a>, and were shocked to see the young boys using <a href="/wiki/Wand" title="Wand">some sticks</a> to <a href="/wiki/Levitation_Charm" title="Levitation Charm">lift up</a> their <a href="/wiki/Police_car" title="Police car">police car</a> to fend off the men.
</p><p>In the summer of <a href="/wiki/1943" title="1943">1943</a>, the <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggle</a> <a href="/wiki/Frank_Bryce" title="Frank Bryce">Frank Bryce</a> was taken to a police station for questioning following the <a href="/wiki/Death" title="Death">deaths</a> of <a href="/wiki/Thomas_Riddle" title="Thomas Riddle">Thomas Riddle</a>, <a href="/wiki/Mary_Riddle" title="Mary Riddle">Mary Riddle</a> and <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle_Senior" title="Tom Riddle Senior">Tom Riddle Snr</a>. The police suspected Frank because there were no signs of forced entry and he was the only one with a key. Frank's only defence was that he saw a pale, dark-haired teenage boy near the house around the time of the murders but this was dismissed as a fabrication. However, the police were eventually forced to let him go after an autopsy report on the Riddles revealed no obvious cause of death. Though he was released without charge, the experience left him deeply mistrustful of police from that point forward. Despite this mistrust, he resolved to <a href="/wiki/Telephone" title="Telephone">call</a> the police upon hearing <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a> and <a href="/wiki/Peter_Pettigrew" title="Peter Pettigrew">Peter Pettigrew</a> plotting the capture and murder of Harry Potter. Before he could, however, his presence was discovered by <a href="/wiki/Nagini" title="Nagini">Nagini</a> and reported to Voldemort.
</p><p>A police officer was amongst <a href="/wiki/Peter_Pettigrew%27s_unidentified_victims" title="Peter Pettigrew&#39;s unidentified victims">the victims of Peter Pettigrew</a> in his <a href="/wiki/Blasting_Curse" title="Blasting Curse">attack</a> on <a href="/wiki/1_November" title="1 November">1 November</a>, <a href="/wiki/1981" title="1981">1981</a> that was blamed on a gas explosion.<sup id="cite_ref-Lego_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Lego-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>On <a href="/wiki/1_September" title="1 September">1 September</a>, <a href="/wiki/1992" title="1992">1992</a>, Mr <a href="/wiki/Angus_Fleet" title="Angus Fleet">Angus Fleet</a> of <a href="/wiki/Peebles" title="Peebles">Peebles</a> reported his sighting of the <a href="/wiki/Flying_Ford_Anglia" title="Flying Ford Anglia">Flying Ford Anglia</a> to the police.<sup id="cite_ref-CS5_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-CS5-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>On either <a href="/wiki/31_August" title="31 August">31 August</a> or 1 September, <a href="/wiki/1994" title="1994">1994</a>, <a href="/wiki/Alastor_Moody" title="Alastor Moody">Alastor Moody</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggle</a> neighbours called policemen to report a disturbance when his <a href="/wiki/Alastor_Moody%27s_dustbins" title="Alastor Moody&#39;s dustbins">dustbins</a> were set off by <a href="/wiki/Bartemius_Crouch_Junior" title="Bartemius Crouch Junior">Bartemius Crouch Jr</a>. This incident was later reported by <a href="/wiki/Rita_Skeeter" title="Rita Skeeter">Rita Skeeter</a> in an <a href="/wiki/FURTHER_MISTAKES_AT_THE_MINISTRY_OF_MAGIC" title="FURTHER MISTAKES AT THE MINISTRY OF MAGIC">article</a> in the <i><a href="/wiki/Daily_Prophet" title="Daily Prophet">Daily Prophet</a></i>.
</p><p>In <a href="/wiki/1995" title="1995">1995</a>, Harry Potter silently wished for someone, such as the police, to come as a <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eater</a> screamed while being tortured via the <a href="/wiki/Cruciatus_Curse" title="Cruciatus Curse">Cruciatus Curse</a> by Voldemort.
</p><p>Later that year, during the summer holidays, <a href="/wiki/Petunia_Dursley" title="Petunia Dursley">Aunt Petunia</a> screamed for <a href="/wiki/Vernon_Dursley" title="Vernon Dursley">Vernon</a> to call the police after <a href="/wiki/Dudley_Dursley" title="Dudley Dursley">Dudley Dursley</a> arrived home following being attacked by <a href="/wiki/Dementor" title="Dementor">Dementors</a> and she feared that he may have been mugged. The call was never made, however, as Dudley struggled to speak, indicating Harry Potter, and the <a href="/wiki/Dursley_family" title="Dursley family">Dursleys</a> then interrogated Harry regarding the matter.<sup id="cite_ref-OP2_4-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OP2-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
