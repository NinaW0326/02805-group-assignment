<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Rancorous_Carpe?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Rancorous_Carpe?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>He designed an elaborate <a href="/wiki/Rancorous_Carpe%27s_trap" title="Rancorous Carpe&#39;s trap">trap</a> in an attempt to capture and remove <a href="/wiki/Peeves" title="Peeves">Peeves</a> the poltergeist from Hogwarts; the trap included an assortment of weapons which the caretaker believed would be irresistible to Peeves, as well as a vast enchanted bell jar reinforced by various <a href="/wiki/Containment_Charm" title="Containment Charm">Containment Charms</a>. He believed it would work, but instead Peeves burst through the bell jar, scattering the corridor with broken glass and firing random weapons acquired from the trap. The castle was evacuated, and it wasn't until the <a href="/wiki/Headmaster" title="Headmaster">Headmistress</a>, <a href="/wiki/Eupraxia_Mole" title="Eupraxia Mole">Eupraxia Mole</a>, <a href="/wiki/Peeves%27_contract" title="Peeves&#39; contract">agreed</a> to let Peeves swim in the boys toilet once weekly, have first choice of stale bread from the <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_kitchens" title="Hogwarts kitchens">kitchen</a> for throwing purposes,&#160;and give him a new hat made by <a href="/wiki/Bonhabille" title="Bonhabille">Madame Bonhabille</a> of <a href="/wiki/Paris" title="Paris">Paris</a>, that the poltergeist agreed to stand down.<sup id="cite_ref-WW_2-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-WW-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Rancorous_Carpe?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Rancorous_Carpe?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li><i>Rancourous </i>is an English adjective which means "characterized by bitterness and resentment",which is a description that fits many caretakers at Hogwarts.</li>
<li><i>Carpe </i>may also be made to fit the exclamation <i>Carpe diem!</i>. It is used to urge someone to use what they have to succeed.This is how he planned to capture Peeves.</li></ul>
