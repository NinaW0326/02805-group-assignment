<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/R%27s_messengers?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/R%27s_messengers?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>This person sent <a href="/wiki/Jacob%27s_sibling" title="Jacob&#39;s sibling">Jacob's sibling</a> a fake invitation under the name of <a href="/wiki/Silvanus_Kettleburn" title="Silvanus Kettleburn">Silvanus Kettleburn</a> to meet at the castle's <a href="/wiki/Courtyard" title="Courtyard">Courtyard</a> at a certain time at night. Upon Jacob's sibling's arrival, the person appeared, covering their face with a red cloak. They told Jacob's sibling about the forgery, claimed to be a "messenger", and promptly attacked the student with the Full Body-Bind Curse. After the victim's paralysis, the messenger said there was still time to avoid the same fate of <a href="/wiki/Jacob_(Hogwarts_student)" title="Jacob (Hogwarts student)">Jacob</a> and warned to stay away from the <a href="/wiki/Forbidden_Forest" title="Forbidden Forest">Forbidden Forest</a>, the <a href="/wiki/Cursed_Vaults" title="Cursed Vaults">Cursed Vaults</a> and <a href="/wiki/Patricia_Rakepick" title="Patricia Rakepick">Patricia Rakepick</a>, saying that "death is coming to Hogwarts" and that it may come to Jacob's sibling too if they disobey. The messenger also said that, after their departure, someone would soon free Jacob's sibling from the binding curse, but it is unknown who did so.<sup id="cite_ref-HM_1-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HM-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
