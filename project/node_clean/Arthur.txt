<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Arthur?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Arthur?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Arthur ruled what is today <a href="/wiki/Great_Britain" title="Great Britain">Britain</a> during the <a href="/wiki/Middle_Ages" title="Middle Ages">Middle Ages</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-merlin_2-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-merlin-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> The <a href="/wiki/Dark_Wizard" title="Dark Wizard">Dark witch</a> <a href="/wiki/Morgan_le_Fay" title="Morgan le Fay">Morgan le Fay</a> was his half-sister,<sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> but his <a href="/wiki/Blood_status" title="Blood status">parentage</a> is unknown,<sup id="cite_ref-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> as is whether he was a <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggle</a> or not.
</p><p>The great <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> <a href="/wiki/Merlin" title="Merlin">Merlin</a> was a key member of <a href="/wiki/Court_of_King_Arthur" title="Court of King Arthur">the Court of King Arthur</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-merlin_2-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-merlin-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> The <a href="/wiki/Knights_of_the_Round_Table" title="Knights of the Round Table">Knights of the Round Table</a> were also an important part of the Court, and included <a href="/wiki/Lancelot_(knight)" title="Lancelot (knight)">Sir Lancelot</a>, <a href="/wiki/Bedivere" title="Bedivere">Sir Bedivere</a> and <a href="/wiki/Percivale" title="Percivale">Sir Percivale</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-pottermore-cadogan_3-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-pottermore-cadogan-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> Wizards and witches commonly hold that this order included a wizard, <a href="/wiki/Cadogan" title="Cadogan">Sir Cadogan</a>, who got the position by being a friend of Merlin.<sup id="cite_ref-pottermore-cadogan_3-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-pottermore-cadogan-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> Tellings of the story of King Arthur differed between the <a href="/wiki/Muggle_world" title="Muggle world">Muggle</a> and <a href="/wiki/Wizarding_world" title="Wizarding world">wizarding</a> worlds, in that Sir Cadogan was included in wizarding versions, but absent in Muggle ones.<sup id="cite_ref-pottermore-cadogan_3-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-pottermore-cadogan-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Arthur?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Arthur?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li><a href="/wiki/Richard_Harris" title="Richard Harris">Richard Harris</a>, who played <a href="/wiki/Albus_Dumbledore" title="Albus Dumbledore">Albus Dumbledore</a> in the <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Philosopher%27s_Stone_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Philosopher&#39;s Stone (film)">Harry Potter and the Philosopher's Stone</a></i> and <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Chamber_of_Secrets_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets (film)">Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets</a></i>, played Arthur in the <a href="/wiki/1967" title="1967">1967</a> film <i><a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Camelot_(film)" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Camelot (film)">Camelot</a></i>. Following his death in <a href="/wiki/2002" title="2002">2002</a> a statue of him in this role was erected in Bedford Row in the centre of his home town of <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Limerick" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Limerick">Limerick</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Jamie_Campbell_Bower" title="Jamie Campbell Bower">Jamie Campbell Bower</a>, who played the teenage <a href="/wiki/Gellert_Grindelwald" title="Gellert Grindelwald">Gellert Grindelwald</a> in <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Deathly_Hallows:_Part_1" title="Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 1">Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 1</a></i>, played Arthur in the TV series <i><a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Camelot_(TV_series)" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Camelot (TV series)">Camelot</a></i>. <a href="/wiki/Merlin" title="Merlin">Merlin</a> was played by <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Joseph_Fiennes" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Joseph Fiennes">Joseph Fiennes</a>, brother of <a href="/wiki/Ralph_Fiennes" title="Ralph Fiennes">Ralph Fiennes</a> (<a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a>), and <a href="/wiki/Morgan_le_Fay" title="Morgan le Fay">Morgan le Fay</a> was played by former Bond girl <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Eva_Green" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Eva Green">Eva Green</a>. ï»¿</li>
<li>King Arthur and <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> can be seen as similar; both held swords in fights (Arthur Excalibur and Harry Gryffindor's sword), both had mentors (<a href="/wiki/Merlin" title="Merlin">Merlin</a> and <a href="/wiki/Albus_Dumbledore" title="Albus Dumbledore">Dumbledore</a>) and both married women with a similar name (Guinevere and <a href="/wiki/Ginevra_Weasley" title="Ginevra Weasley">Ginevra</a>)</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/J._K._Rowling" title="J. K. Rowling">J. K. Rowling</a> said King Arthur from <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/T.H._White" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:T.H. White">T.H. White</a>'s <i>The Sword in The Stone</i> is <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>'s spiritual ancestor.</li>
<li>Arthur's time of existence in the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_universe" title="Harry Potter universe">Harry Potter universe</a> (like Merlin, Morgan le Fay etc) is paradoxical as the King Arthur of the Arthurian legends is said to have lived and reigned in the 5th or 6th century. As Merlin attended Hogwarts and was a part of King Arthur's Court and an advisor to him, then he, like the <i>legendary </i>version of Merlin predates Hogwarts by around 400/500 years.</li></ul>
