<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets">"<i>My dad's a milkman, he couldn't believe it either...</i>"</span></dd>
<dd>&#8212;<a href="/wiki/Colin_Creevey" title="Colin Creevey">Colin Creevey</a><sup class="noprint"><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Chamber_of_Secrets" title="Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets">[src]</a></sup></dd>

<p><b>Mr Creevey</b> was the father of <a href="/wiki/Colin_Creevey" title="Colin Creevey">Colin</a> and <a href="/wiki/Dennis_Creevey" title="Dennis Creevey">Dennis Creevey</a>. A <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggle</a>, Mr Creevey was employed as a <a href="/wiki/Milk" title="Milk">milkman</a>, and was astonished to learn that his elder son, Colin, was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a>. Colin sent plenty of photographs home to his father during his <a href="/wiki/First_year" title="First year">first year</a> at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> in <a href="/wiki/1992" title="1992">1992</a>, a high proportion, presumably, of <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>, who was an object of near-worship for young Colin.<sup id="cite_ref-COS6_1-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COS6-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
