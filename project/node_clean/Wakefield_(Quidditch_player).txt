<b>Wakefield</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Great_Britain" title="Great Britain">British</a>&#160;<a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard or witch</a> who played <a href="/wiki/Quidditch" title="Quidditch">Quidditch</a> professionally. Wakefield played for the <a href="/wiki/Appleby_Arrows" title="Appleby Arrows">Appleby Arrows</a> for an unknown amount of time until being traded to another team for <a href="/wiki/Galleon" title="Galleon">Galleons</a> in <a href="/wiki/1996" title="1996">1996</a>. This event was reported in the <i><a href="/wiki/Daily_Prophet" title="Daily Prophet">Daily Prophet</a></i>.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
