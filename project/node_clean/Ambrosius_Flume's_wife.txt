<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Ambrosius_Flume%27s_wife?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Ambrosius_Flume%27s_wife?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_life">Early life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Ambrosius_Flume%27s_wife?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Early life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Ambrosius_Flume%27s_wife?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Early life"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Mrs Flume was born a witch and possibly attended <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> as her husband did. At some point in her life, Mrs Flume married <a href="/wiki/Ambrosius_Flume" title="Ambrosius Flume">Ambrosius Flume</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP4_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP4-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Honeydukes">Honeydukes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Ambrosius_Flume%27s_wife?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Honeydukes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Ambrosius_Flume%27s_wife?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Honeydukes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>By <a href="/wiki/1993" title="1993">1993</a>, the couple lived in a flat above <a href="/wiki/Honeydukes" title="Honeydukes">Honeydukes Sweetshop</a> and ran together said shop. In <a href="/wiki/1993" title="1993">1993</a>, during one of the <a href="/wiki/Hogsmeade_weekend_trips" title="Hogsmeade weekend trips">weekend trips</a> to <a href="/wiki/Hogsmeade" title="Hogsmeade">Hogsmeade</a>, Mrs Flume asked <a href="/wiki/Ambrosius_Flume" title="Ambrosius Flume">her husband</a> to get some <a href="/wiki/Jelly_Slug" title="Jelly Slug">Jelly Slugs</a>, as the shop was filled with <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts</a> students.<sup id="cite_ref-PA10_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PA10-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Battle_of_Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Ambrosius_Flume%27s_wife?veaction=edit&amp;section=4" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Battle of Hogwarts">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Ambrosius_Flume%27s_wife?action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Battle of Hogwarts"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>It is possible that Mrs Flume participated in the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a> alongside her husband and other residents of <a href="/wiki/Hogsmeade" title="Hogsmeade">Hogsmeade</a> village, although this is unconfirmed.
</p>
