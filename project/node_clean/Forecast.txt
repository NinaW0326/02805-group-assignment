<p><i><b>Forecast</b></i> was a <i><a href="/wiki/Daily_Prophet" title="Daily Prophet">Daily Prophet</a></i> newspaper feature, including weather forecasts for all of <a href="/wiki/Great_Britain" title="Great Britain">Great Britain</a>. It was featured on page two of the periodical.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> It featured the day after's weather, the "day after tomorrow"'s, the "day after the day after tomorrow"'s, the "day after the day after the day after tomorrow"'s, the "day after the day after the day after the day after tomorrow"'s and the "day after that"'s.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
