<p>This <b><a href="/wiki/Portrait" title="Portrait">portrait</a> of a count</b> hangs next to the first-floor landing of the <a href="/wiki/Grand_Staircase" title="Grand Staircase">Grand Staircase</a> of <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Castle" title="Hogwarts Castle">Hogwarts Castle</a>. In <a href="/wiki/1992" title="1992">1992</a>, <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione Granger</a> told <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> that it was not a good sign to hear voices, even in the <a href="/wiki/Wizarding_world" title="Wizarding world">Wizarding world</a>, unaware that the voices he was hearing was really the <a href="/wiki/Parseltongue" title="Parseltongue">Parseltongue</a> of <a href="/wiki/Salazar_Slytherin%27s_Basilisk" class="mw-redirect" title="Salazar Slytherin&#39;s Basilisk">Salazar Slytherin's Basilisk</a> going through the school's pipes. The subject of this portrait agreed with her.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<ul><li>This individual was played by <a href="/wiki/David_Tysall" title="David Tysall">David Tysall</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Chamber_of_Secrets_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets (film)">film adaptation</a> of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Chamber_of_Secrets" title="Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets">Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets</a></i>.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup></li>
