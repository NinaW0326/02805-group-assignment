<b>Finbar Quigley</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Quidditch" title="Quidditch">Quidditch</a> player for the <a href="/wiki/Ballycastle_Bats" title="Ballycastle Bats">Ballycastle Bats</a><sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>. Finbar did not dare disagree with <a href="/wiki/Gwenog_Jones" title="Gwenog Jones">Gwenog Jones</a> when she commented on the ever-increasing number of fans of the <a href="/wiki/Holyhead_Harpies" title="Holyhead Harpies">Holyhead Harpies</a> for the <a href="/wiki/Daily_Prophet" title="Daily Prophet">Daily Prophet</a>, for he feared being <a href="/wiki/Transfiguration" title="Transfiguration">turned</a> into a <a href="/wiki/Wood_louse" title="Wood louse">Wood lice</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>

<ul><li>It is possible that Finbar Quigley is the <a href="/wiki/Quigley" title="Quigley">Beater</a> for the <a href="/wiki/Irish_National_Quidditch_team" title="Irish National Quidditch team">Irish National Quidditch team</a> as they share the same last name and Ballycastle could refer to either the small town in <a href="/wiki/Northern_Ireland" title="Northern Ireland">Northern Ireland</a> or the village in the Republic of <a href="/wiki/Ireland" title="Ireland">Ireland</a>.
