<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Violet_Tillyman%27s_children?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Violet_Tillyman%27s_children?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>In <a href="/wiki/1855" class="mw-redirect" title="1855">1855</a>, Violet had a nasty argument with her husband, <a href="/wiki/Albert_Tillyman" title="Albert Tillyman">Albert Tillyman</a>, and left her house through the <a href="/wiki/Floo_Network" title="Floo Network">Floo Network</a>. She tried to say, "<i>I want to go to <a href="/wiki/Violet_Tillyman%27s_mother" title="Violet Tillyman&#39;s mother">Mother's</a> house</i>", but in her distressed, sobbing state, this did not come out clearly, and thus she emerged from the fireplace of <a href="/wiki/Myron_Otherhaus" title="Myron Otherhaus">Myron Otherhaus</a>'s home in <a href="/wiki/Bury_St_Edmunds" title="Bury St Edmunds">Bury St Edmunds</a>. Myron and Violet fell in love, and, over the next twenty years, they had seven children. The children's existence apparently remained unknown to the world until Violet decided to come out of hiding following Albert's death in <a href="/wiki/1875" class="mw-redirect" title="1875">1875</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-PM_2-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PM-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Violet_Tillyman%27s_children?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Violet_Tillyman%27s_children?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>If Myron and Violet never married, as seems to be the case, this would make their seven children the only characters within <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_universe" title="Harry Potter universe"><i>Harry Potter</i> canon</a> known to have been born to non-married parents, until the addition of <a href="/wiki/Delphini" title="Delphini">Delphini</a> in <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Cursed_Child" title="Harry Potter and the Cursed Child">Harry Potter and the Cursed Child</a></i>.</li></ul>
