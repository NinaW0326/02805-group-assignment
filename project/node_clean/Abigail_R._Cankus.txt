<b>Abigail R. Cankus</b> was an <a href="/wiki/United_States_of_America" title="United States of America">American</a> <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch</a> who wrote <i><a href="/wiki/The_Flap_of_the_Cape" title="The Flap of the Cape">The Flap of the Cape</a></i>. This book was named the "Book of the Week" in the <a href="/wiki/6_December" title="6 December">6 December</a> <a href="/wiki/1926" title="1926">1926</a> issue of <i><a href="/wiki/The_New_York_Ghost" title="The New York Ghost">The New York Ghost</a></i>.<sup id="cite_ref-nyghost_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-nyghost-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
