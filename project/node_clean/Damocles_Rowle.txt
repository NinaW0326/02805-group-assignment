<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Damocles_Rowle?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Damocles_Rowle?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<figure class="thumb tleft show-info-icon" style="width: 247px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/3/3c/Azkaban-002.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20070811223314" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/3/3c/Azkaban-002.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/245?cb=20070811223314" decoding="async" width="245" height="104" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="Azkaban-002.jpg" data-image-key="Azkaban-002.jpg" /></a> 	 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:Azkaban-002.jpg" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption"><a href="/wiki/Azkaban" title="Azkaban">Azkaban</a> Prison, established by Damocles Rowle in <a href="/wiki/1718" class="mw-redirect" title="1718">1718</a></p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure>
<p>He was elected on a platform of being "tough on <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggles</a>". At the time, plans for a purpose-built wizarding prison, located on some <a href="/wiki/Hebrides" title="Hebrides">Hebridean</a> island, had been made. However, Rowle scrapped the plans and insisted on using <a href="/wiki/Azkaban" title="Azkaban">Azkaban</a> instead, which had previously been a <a href="/wiki/Dementor" title="Dementor">Dementor</a>-infested fortress used by the insane dark wizard <a href="/wiki/Ekrizdis" title="Ekrizdis">Ekrizdis</a> in the <a href="/wiki/15th_century" title="15th century">15th century</a> and was now abandoned. He claimed that the Dementors populating the island could be harnessed as guards, saving the <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">Ministry</a> time, expense and trouble, and thus being an advantage. Rowle carried out his plan, in spite of protests from experts on both Dementors and buildings with a dark history such as Azkaban. Many prisoners were placed there, and even those who had not been mad and dangerous when they came to Azkaban, swiftly became so. Yet none of them ever emerged while Rowle was the Minister.<sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>He was first elected as Minister in <a href="/wiki/1718" class="mw-redirect" title="1718">1718</a> and was re-elected at least once.
</p><p>His actions as Minister led to him being censured by the <a href="/wiki/International_Confederation_of_Wizards" title="International Confederation of Wizards">International Confederation of Wizards</a>, and he was therefore forced to step down, turning over the office to <a href="/wiki/Perseus_Parkinson" title="Perseus Parkinson">Perseus Parkinson</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-MfM_2-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-MfM-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> Despite Rowle being forced to step down however, the island still remained infested with Dementors, and the creatures were still used as guards until the outbreak of the <a href="/wiki/Second_Wizarding_War" title="Second Wizarding War">Second Wizarding War</a>.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Damocles_Rowle?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Damocles_Rowle?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p><a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Damocles" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Damocles">Damocles</a> is an anecdotal figure from Sicily. Supposedly a courier of <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Dionysius_II_of_Syracuse" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Dionysius II of Syracuse">Dionysius II of Syracuse</a>, Damocles commented on his king's fortunate position. Dionysius offered to switch positions with Damocles and offered to let him sit on the throne. Dionysius then arranged for a heavy sword to be suspended above the throne by only a single tail hair of a horse. This allegory is meant to illustrate that with great fortune comes great peril and anxiety. Damocles Rowle can be said to exemplify this allegory, as he was censured and forced to retire as Minister.
</p>
