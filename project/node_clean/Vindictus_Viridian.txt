<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Vindictus_Viridian?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Vindictus_Viridian?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Lifetime">Lifetime</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Vindictus_Viridian?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Lifetime">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Vindictus_Viridian?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Lifetime"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Based on his portrait, Viridian was a <a href="/wiki/Potioneer" title="Potioneer">potioneer</a>. At some point in his life, he was named <a href="/wiki/Headmaster" title="Headmaster">Headmaster</a> of <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-WWHP_4-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-WWHP-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> Also during his lifetime, he published a famous book on <a href="/wiki/Dark_charm" title="Dark charm">dark charms</a>: <i><a href="/wiki/Curses_and_Counter-Curses" title="Curses and Counter-Curses">Curses and Counter-Curses (Bewitch your Friends and Befuddle your Enemies with the Latest Revenges: Hair Loss, Jelly-Legs, Tongue-Tying and much, much more)</a></i>.<sup id="cite_ref-PS5_3-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PS5-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> He also published the book <i><a href="/wiki/How_to_Tame_Tigers" title="How to Tame Tigers">How to Tame Tigers</a></i>.<sup id="cite_ref-csf_5-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-csf-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Post-mortem">Post-mortem</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Vindictus_Viridian?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Post-mortem">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Vindictus_Viridian?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Post-mortem"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<figure class="thumb tleft show-info-icon" style="width: 202px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/1/1b/Dumbledore%27s_office_portraits.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20110709232116" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/1/1b/Dumbledore%27s_office_portraits.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/200?cb=20110709232116" decoding="async" width="200" height="144" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="Dumbledore&#39;s office portraits.jpg" data-image-key="Dumbledore&#39;s_office_portraits.jpg" /></a> 	 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:Dumbledore%27s_office_portraits.jpg" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption">Professor Viridian's portrait in the <a href="/wiki/Headmaster%27s_office" class="mw-redirect" title="Headmaster&#39;s office">Headmaster's office</a></p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure>
<p>Professor Viridian died sometime in or prior to <a href="/wiki/1994" title="1994">1994</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-DOD_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DOD-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> Two <a href="/wiki/Headmaster_portraits" title="Headmaster portraits">portraits</a> of him hung at Hogwarts: one was painted in <a href="/wiki/1703" class="mw-redirect" title="1703">1703</a><sup id="cite_ref-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup> and hung in the Headmaster's office along with the portraits of other Headmasters and Headmistresses,<sup id="cite_ref-WWHP_4-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-WWHP-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> and the other hung in the <a href="/wiki/Grand_Staircase" title="Grand Staircase">Grand Staircase</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-OPg_7-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OPg-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>In the <a href="/wiki/1995-1996_school_year" class="mw-redirect" title="1995-1996 school year">1995-1996 school year</a>, Viridian's portrait at the <a href="/wiki/Grand_Staircase" title="Grand Staircase">Grand Staircase</a> was used to conceal the entrance to the <a href="/wiki/Room_of_Rewards" title="Room of Rewards">Room of Rewards</a>. He was frequently annoyed by all the hustle and bustle of the students' school life. In order to gain entry to the Room of Rewards, <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione Granger</a> threatened to convince <a href="/wiki/Myrtle_Warren" title="Myrtle Warren">Myrtle Warren</a> to haunt the landing where the portrait hung. Disliking Myrtle, Viridian let <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> and his friends through.<sup id="cite_ref-OPg_7-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OPg-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<figure class="thumb tright show-info-icon" style="width: 152px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/a/ac/VindictusViridian3.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20100722204410" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/a/ac/VindictusViridian3.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/150?cb=20100722204410" decoding="async" width="150" height="269" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="VindictusViridian3.jpg" data-image-key="VindictusViridian3.jpg" /></a> 	 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:VindictusViridian3.jpg" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption">Portrait of an older Vindictus Viridian</p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure>
<ul><li><b><a href="/wiki/Dark_Arts" title="Dark Arts">Dark Arts</a></b>: Professor Viridian was highly proficient with <a href="/wiki/Curse" title="Curse">curses</a>, <a href="/wiki/Hex" title="Hex">hexes</a> and <a href="/wiki/Jinx" title="Jinx">jinxes</a>, and wrote <a href="/wiki/Curses_and_Counter-Curses" title="Curses and Counter-Curses">a book</a> which included such <a href="/wiki/Dark_charm" title="Dark charm">dark charms</a> as the: <a href="/wiki/Full_Body-Bind_Curse" title="Full Body-Bind Curse">Full Body-Bind Curse</a>, <a href="/wiki/Jelly-Legs_Curse" title="Jelly-Legs Curse">Jelly-Legs Curse</a>, <a href="/wiki/Leg-Locker_Curse" title="Leg-Locker Curse">Leg-Locker Curse</a>, <a href="/wiki/Pimple_Jinx" title="Pimple Jinx">Pimple Jinx</a>, <a href="/wiki/Stickfast_Hex" title="Stickfast Hex">Stickfast Hex</a>, <a href="/wiki/Tickling_Hex" title="Tickling Hex">Tickling Hex</a>, <a href="/wiki/Tongue-Tying_Curse" title="Tongue-Tying Curse">Tongue-Tying Curse</a> and <a href="/wiki/Hair_Loss_Curse" title="Hair Loss Curse">Hair Loss Curse</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-PS5_3-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PS5-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Potion" title="Potion">Potions</a></b>: Professor Viridian was a highly accomplished and skilful <a href="/wiki/Potioneer" title="Potioneer">Potioneer</a>, as evidenced by several portraits of him brewing various <a href="/wiki/Potion" title="Potion">potions</a>.</li></ul>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Vindictus_Viridian?veaction=edit&amp;section=5" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Vindictus_Viridian?action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The name Vindictus is derived from the Latin <i>vindicta</i> ("revenge") and the surname Viridian from the Latin <i>viridis</i> ("green").
</p>
