<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Tilden_Toots?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Tilden_Toots?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Tilden Toots was well-known for having three <a href="/wiki/Green_thumb" title="Green thumb">green thumbs</a>. He hosted the <i><a href="/wiki/Toots,_Shoots_%27n%27_Roots" title="Toots, Shoots &#39;n&#39; Roots">Toots, Shoots 'n' Roots</a></i> radio programme on the <a href="/wiki/Wizarding_Wireless_Network" title="Wizarding Wireless Network">Wizarding Wireless Network</a>, and he produced his own line of plant-related <a href="/wiki/Potion" title="Potion">potions</a>, such as <a href="/wiki/Rejuicing_Potion" title="Rejuicing Potion">Rejuicer</a> and <a href="/wiki/Regerminating_Potion" title="Regerminating Potion">Regerminator</a>. According to <a href="/wiki/Severus_Snape" title="Severus Snape">Severus Snape</a>, Toots built a fortune selling his potions.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>He was married to bestselling author <a href="/wiki/Daisy_Hookum" title="Daisy Hookum">Daisy Hookum</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-JKRS_1-13" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-JKRS-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Magical_abilities_and_skills">Magical abilities and skills</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Tilden_Toots?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Tilden_Toots?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li><b><a href="/wiki/Herbology" title="Herbology">Herbology</a></b>: Being a professional <a href="/wiki/Herbologist" title="Herbologist">Herbologist</a>, Toots was a natural expert in the study of <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magical</a> plants and fungi. Toots hosted his own radio show on the subject of magical gardening, called <i><a href="/wiki/Toots,_Shoots_%27n%27_Roots" title="Toots, Shoots &#39;n&#39; Roots">Toots, Shoots 'n' Roots</a></i>, in which he gave considerable knowledge and advice to other wizards and witches in this field.</li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Potion" title="Potion">Potions</a></b>: Toots was also a professional <a href="/wiki/Potioneer" title="Potioneer">Potioneer</a>, having invented at least two of his own gardening based potions, the <a href="/wiki/Rejuicing_Potion" title="Rejuicing Potion">Rejuicing Potion</a> and <a href="/wiki/Regerminating_Potion" title="Regerminating Potion">Regerminating Potion</a>, showing his excellent skills in this field of <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magic</a>.</li></ul>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Tilden_Toots?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Tilden_Toots?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>On <a href="/wiki/J._K._Rowling%27s_official_site" title="J. K. Rowling&#39;s official site">J. K. Rowling's official site</a>, Tilden Toots' radio show could sometimes be heard when one switched on the radio in Extra Stuff, prior to the site's redesign in <a href="/wiki/2012" title="2012">2012</a>.</li>
<li>If he attended <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts</a>, Toots might have been in the same year as the <a href="/wiki/Marauders" title="Marauders">Marauders</a>, <a href="/wiki/Lily_J._Potter" title="Lily J. Potter">Lily Evans</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Severus_Snape" title="Severus Snape">Severus Snape</a>. If he was not, he was in the year ahead of them.</li>
<li>He had a broad <a href="/wiki/West_Country" title="West Country">West Country</a> accent as an adult, which suggests he spent the better part of his youth in the West Country where there are several magical settlements.</li></ul>
