<p>A <b>student assistant</b> referred to any student at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> entrusted the task of assisting a member of the staff with the execution of their daily duties, presumably giving them extra credit for their trouble or simply allowing them to reap the benefits of the experience.<sup id="cite_ref-HM_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HM-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<ul><li><a href="/wiki/Jacob%27s_sibling" title="Jacob&#39;s sibling">Jacob's sibling</a> was a part-time student assistant of the <a href="/wiki/Matron" title="Matron">Matron</a> of the day, <a href="/wiki/Madam" title="Madam">Madam</a> <a href="/wiki/Poppy_Pomfrey" title="Poppy Pomfrey">Poppy Pomfrey</a>, and helped her in treating and looking after sick and injured students during the <a href="/wiki/1980s" title="1980s">1980s</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-HM_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HM-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li>The <a href="/wiki/Unidentified_1992_-_1993_Music_teacher" title="Unidentified 1992 - 1993 Music teacher">Music Professor</a> had to momentarily step out of his classroom during a lesson during the <a href="/wiki/1992-1993_school_year" class="mw-redirect" title="1992-1993 school year">1992-1993 school year</a>, leaving the task of monitoring the class to <a href="/wiki/Music_classroom_monitor" title="Music classroom monitor">a student assistant</a> from <a href="/wiki/Ravenclaw" title="Ravenclaw">Ravenclaw House</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-COSG_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COSG-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li>During the same year, the school's <a href="/wiki/Unidentified_male_Muggle_Studies_teacher" title="Unidentified male Muggle Studies teacher">Muggle Studies teacher</a> had also appointed a <a href="/wiki/Male_Muggle_Studies_teacher%27s_student_assistant" title="Male Muggle Studies teacher&#39;s student assistant">young man</a> in <a href="/wiki/Hufflepuff" title="Hufflepuff">Hufflepuff House</a> to be his student assistant.<sup id="cite_ref-COSG_2-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COSG-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup></li></ul>
