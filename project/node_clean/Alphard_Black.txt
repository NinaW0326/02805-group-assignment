<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Alphard_Black?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Alphard_Black?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Alphard was the middle child of <a href="/wiki/Pollux_Black" title="Pollux Black">Pollux Black</a> and <a href="/wiki/Irma_Crabbe" title="Irma Crabbe">Irma Crabbe</a>, and brother of <a href="/wiki/Walburga_Black" title="Walburga Black">Walburga</a> and <a href="/wiki/Cygnus_Black_III" title="Cygnus Black III">Cygnus</a>. He was born sometime after his sister and before his brother<sup id="cite_ref-BFT_3-15" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-BFT-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> and grew up in <a href="/wiki/12_Grimmauld_Place" title="12 Grimmauld Place">12 Grimmauld Place</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<figure class="thumb tleft show-info-icon" style="width: 182px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/b/b7/Alphard_2.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20130402032213" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/b/b7/Alphard_2.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/180?cb=20130402032213" decoding="async" width="180" height="157" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="Alphard 2.jpg" data-image-key="Alphard_2.jpg" /></a> 	 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:Alphard_2.jpg" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption">Alphard was burnt off from the <a href="/wiki/Black_Family_Tapestry" title="Black Family Tapestry">Black family tree tapestry</a> after his disownment</p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure>
<p>Alphard passed away from unknown causes sometime in <a href="/wiki/1976" title="1976">1976</a> or <a href="/wiki/1977" title="1977">1977</a>. In his will, he left a "decent amount of gold" to his nephew <a href="/wiki/Sirius_Black" title="Sirius Black">Sirius Black</a>, who was seventeen at the time.<sup id="cite_ref-Bloomsbury_2-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Bloomsbury-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> He was posthumously disowned and removed from the <a href="/wiki/Black_Family_Tapestry" title="Black Family Tapestry">Black family tree tapestry</a> by Sirius' mother, Walburga, and Sirius believed that this act of charity towards him was the reason why.<sup id="cite_ref-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Personality">Personality</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Alphard_Black?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Personality">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Alphard_Black?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Personality"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Judging from Alphard's action of helping his nephew when he ran away from home, it can be deduced that he was, at the very least, capable of love and fondness. It can therefore be inferred that he might have been kind and compassionate in his life, which made Alphard vastly different from his sister. He did not seem to fear being blasted off the tapestry, thus indicating that, like his nephew, he himself had a rebellious or brave bone.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Alphard_Black?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Alphard_Black?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Like many members of the <a href="/wiki/House_of_Black" title="House of Black">Black family</a>, Alphard is named after a star. <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Alphard" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Alphard">Alphard</a> is the brightest star in the constellation Hydra, and is known as the heart of the serpent. Its name is derived from the Arabic فرد|الفرد (<i>Al Fard</i>), meaning "the solitary one".
</p>
