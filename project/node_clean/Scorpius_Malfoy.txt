<p><b>Scorpius Hyperion Malfoy</b> (b. <a href="/wiki/2006" title="2006">2006</a>) was a <a href="/wiki/Great_Britain" title="Great Britain">British</a> <a href="/wiki/Pure-blood" title="Pure-blood">pure-blood</a><sup><sup id="cite_ref-blood_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-blood-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup></sup> <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> and the son of <a href="/wiki/Draco_Malfoy" title="Draco Malfoy">Draco</a> and <a href="/wiki/Astoria_Malfoy" title="Astoria Malfoy">Astoria Malfoy</a> <span class="template-c">(née <a href="/wiki/Greengrass_family" title="Greengrass family">Greengrass</a>)</span>.<sup><sup id="cite_ref-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup></sup> He attended <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> from <a href="/wiki/2017" title="2017">2017</a> onwards and was a classmate of <a href="/wiki/Rose_Granger-Weasley" title="Rose Granger-Weasley">Rose Granger-Weasley</a> and <a href="/wiki/Albus_Potter" title="Albus Potter">Albus Potter</a>. Scorpius quickly struck up a friendship with Albus, one which in time would grow to become very deep. He, however, struggled with a mistaken belief held by many against him that he was secretly the son of <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a> and not Draco Malfoy.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Scorpius_Malfoy?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Scorpius_Malfoy?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span id="Early_life_(2006-2017)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_life_.282006-2017.29">Early life <span class="template-c">(2006-2017)</span></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Scorpius_Malfoy?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Early life (2006-2017)">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Scorpius_Malfoy?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Early life (2006-2017)"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Scorpius was born circa <a href="/wiki/2006" title="2006">2006</a> to <a href="/wiki/Draco_Malfoy" title="Draco Malfoy">Draco Malfoy</a> and <a href="/wiki/Astoria_Malfoy" title="Astoria Malfoy">Astoria Greengrass</a>. Scorpius was raised in <a href="/wiki/Malfoy_Manor" title="Malfoy Manor">Malfoy Manor</a> and was said to have inherited his father's and <a href="/wiki/Lucius_Malfoy" title="Lucius Malfoy">grandfather</a>'s looks; blonde hair, grey eyes, and a pale pointed face. In contrast to his parents and grandparents, Scorpius was not raised with traditional <a href="/wiki/Pure-blood" title="Pure-blood">pure-blood</a> beliefs that taught him to regard <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggles</a> and non-pureblood wizards as inferior so he was much more tolerant of them.
</p><p>Childhood for Scorpius was one of isolation within Malfoy Manor. He had no interactions with children of his age before he began attending at Hogwarts. Scorpius read the Malfoy collection of books and became knowledgeable about the <a href="/wiki/Wizarding_world" title="Wizarding world">Wizarding world</a>, the <a href="/wiki/Second_Wizarding_War" title="Second Wizarding War">Second Wizarding War</a> and <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>.
</p>

<p>In <a href="/wiki/2017" title="2017">2017</a>, Scorpius was accompanied by his parents to <a href="/wiki/King%27s_Cross_Station" title="King&#39;s Cross Station">King's Cross Station</a> to go to his first year at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a>. Others in his year included <a href="/wiki/Albus_Potter" title="Albus Potter">Albus Potter</a> and <a href="/wiki/Rose_Granger-Weasley" title="Rose Granger-Weasley">Rose Granger-Weasley</a>; Rose was discouraged by her father from becoming "too friendly" with Scorpius and was encouraged to beat him in every test.<sup><sup id="cite_ref-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup></sup>
</p><p>Scorpius sat in an empty compartment on the <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Express" title="Hogwarts Express">Hogwarts Express</a> until he was later joined by Albus and Rose. He awkwardly introduced himself and said he had sweets they can share. Rose decided to leave but Albus stayed, joking that he only did for the sweets. They shared the sweets along with stories on their journey to Hogwarts, beginning their friendship.
</p><p>Scorpius was sorted into <a href="/wiki/Slytherin" title="Slytherin">Slytherin</a> and sat next to Albus at the <a href="/wiki/Start-of-Term_Feast" title="Start-of-Term Feast">Start of term Feast</a>. He had a hard time at Hogwarts since his fellow students made jokes about his parentage while they also mocked Albus for being a Potter in Slytherin. Regardless, he and Albus became great friends and Scorpius encouraged him to ignore what the students were saying about them. He also had a crush on Rose but she did not return his feelings.
</p>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Third_year">Third year</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Scorpius_Malfoy?veaction=edit&amp;section=5" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Third year">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Scorpius_Malfoy?action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Third year"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<p>Shortly before his third year at Hogwarts, in the summer of <a href="/wiki/2019" title="2019">2019</a>, Scorpius lost his mother to <a href="/wiki/Blood_malediction" title="Blood malediction">an illness</a> which left him and his father grief-stricken and heart-broken. Meeting Albus on Platform 9¾, he asked his friend to come to the funeral.
</p>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Fourth_year">Fourth year</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Scorpius_Malfoy?veaction=edit&amp;section=6" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Fourth year">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Scorpius_Malfoy?action=edit&amp;section=6" title="Edit section: Fourth year"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>

</p><p>On the Hogwarts Express, when Albus told him about his meeting with <a href="/wiki/Amos_Diggory" title="Amos Diggory">Amos Diggory</a> and the discovery of a <a href="/wiki/Time-Turner" title="Time-Turner">Time-Turner</a>, they made a plan to retrieve the Time-Turner and travel back in time to save <a href="/wiki/Cedric_Diggory" title="Cedric Diggory">Cedric Diggory</a>. <p>When they met her, Scorpius noticed that <a href="/wiki/Delphini" title="Delphini">Delphi</a> had an <a href="/wiki/Augurey" title="Augurey">Augurey</a> tattoo, which reminded him of a witch in the alternate reality where <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Voldemort</a> won. He realised that she was raised by the <a href="/wiki/Rowle_family" title="Rowle family">Rowles</a>, who were well known <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a>. She snapped their wands and killed <a href="/wiki/Craig_Bowker_Junior" title="Craig Bowker Junior">Craig Bowker Jnr</a> who was sent to find them, before she made Albus and Scorpius travel back in time with her to the third task of the <a href="/wiki/Triwizard_Tournament" title="Triwizard Tournament">Triwizard Tournament</a>.
</p><p>Albus and Scorpius, knowing the Time-Turner only kept them in the past for five minutes, tried to stall Delphi and ended up meeting Cedric Diggory in the maze. Cedric helped Albus and Scorpius and retrieved the Time-Turner, believing it to be part of the tournament. As he left, Albus and Scorpius told him that his father Amos loved him. However, Delphi managed to get the Time-Turner back and went back in time to <a href="/wiki/30_October" title="30 October">October 30</a>, <a href="/wiki/1981" title="1981">1981</a>, the day before <a href="/wiki/James_Potter_I" title="James Potter I">James</a> and <a href="/wiki/Lily_Potter" title="Lily Potter">Lily Potter</a> died. Delphi then destroyed the Time-Turner and left, abandoning Albus and Scorpius.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Physical_appearance">Physical appearance</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Scorpius_Malfoy?veaction=edit&amp;section=11" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Physical appearance">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Scorpius_Malfoy?action=edit&amp;section=11" title="Edit section: Physical appearance"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Scorpius inherited his father <a href="/wiki/Draco_Malfoy" title="Draco Malfoy">Draco</a>'s physical traits, meaning that he likely had blond hair, grey eyes, and a pale pointed face. He was also noted for looking almost the same as his father did at age eleven. He was said to resemble Draco as much as <a href="/wiki/Albus_Potter" title="Albus Potter">Albus Potter</a> resembled <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-DH-E_3-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH-E-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Personality_and_traits">Personality and traits</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Scorpius_Malfoy?veaction=edit&amp;section=12" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Personality and traits">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Scorpius_Malfoy?action=edit&amp;section=12" title="Edit section: Personality and traits"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<div class="quote">
<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;Harry Potter and the Cursed Child">"<i>The world changes and we change with it. I am better off in this world. But the world is not better. And I don't want that.</i>"</span></dd>
<dd>&#8212;Scorpius chooses to give up being "king" of a Hogwarts in a world ruled by Voldemort.<sup class="noprint"><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Cursed_Child" title="Harry Potter and the Cursed Child">[src]</a></sup></dd>
<dd></dd></dl></div><p>Scorpius was shown to be a brave, intelligent, and understanding individual. However, despite his traits he was shown to be incredibly lonely and insecure, who suffered from slight depression throughout his childhood and eventual years at Hogwarts. Scorpius was noted to be extremely kind, according to Albus.
</p><p>Scorpius was known to have a personality that Albus and himself describe as "nerdy" and "geeky," and was known to be something of a loner. A consummate social outcast, Scorpius relied on his romantic and amorous personality to lighten his burdens in life. A particular trait of his was his long-standing romantic interest in <a href="/wiki/Rose_Granger-Weasley" title="Rose Granger-Weasley">Rose Granger-Weasley</a>, his best friend's cousin and unreachable goal. Scorpius was rather persistent when he wanted something and vaguely shows his ambitious side in the fact that no other girl has ever caught his eye before, potentially referencing another part of his identity he, himself had yet to figure out. His overall neutral want to restore Rose's existence to the timeline shows that his interest in her was apparently more than just a simple schoolboy crush to the audience and can be implied that he actually <a href="/wiki/Love" title="Love">loves</a> her. However, this desperation to restore a timeline was sidelined by the overwhelming relationship between Scorpius and Albus as well as the confirmation from <a href="/wiki/Severus_Snape" title="Severus Snape">Snape</a> that Scorpius was generally "giving up his kingdom" by restoring the timelines all for Albus.<sup id="cite_ref-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Living in a timeline without Albus being his friend was shown to be more unbearable and saddening for Scorpius than living in a timeline without Rose's existence altogether.<sup id="cite_ref-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<div class="quote">
<dl><dd><span><b>Albus</b>: "<i>No. They’re not true. And I’ll tell you why. Because I don’t think Voldemort is capable of having a kind son — and you’re kind, Scorpius. To the depths of your belly, to the tips of your fingers. I truly believe Voldemort — Voldemort couldn’t have a child like you.</i>"</span></dd>
<dd><b>Scorpius</b>: "<i>That’s nice — that’s a nice thing to say.</i>"</dd>
<dd>&#8212; Albus reassuring Scorpius on his absolute kindness.</dd></dl>
</div>
<p>Scorpius was also known to be prone to feelings of jealousy. His jealousy was shown from when Scorpius once observed Albus showing interest in a girl, <a href="/wiki/Delphini" title="Delphini">Delphi</a>. In an alternate timeline when Albus was ordered by his father to stay away from Scorpius, Delphi admitted that in the letters Albus wrote to her so she can feel Scorpius' absence from his life. Scorpius retorted that it seemed like he has found another shoulder to cry on and inquired how many owls they've sent to one another. Through his backhanded comment, this jealousy can be inferred, but it was unknown what these complicated feelings really meant.
</p><p>In another alternate timeline where Albus was never born and Voldemort ruled, Scorpius was apparently more of a bully, as he allegedly forced <a href="/wiki/Craig_Bowker_Junior" title="Craig Bowker Junior">Craig Bowker Jnr</a> to do his homework for him. The Scorpius who inadvertently made this timeline shift was rather surprised and shocked at this revelation.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Relationships">Relationships</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Scorpius_Malfoy?veaction=edit&amp;section=15" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Relationships">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Scorpius_Malfoy?action=edit&amp;section=15" title="Edit section: Relationships"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Parents">Parents</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Scorpius_Malfoy?veaction=edit&amp;section=16" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Parents">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Scorpius_Malfoy?action=edit&amp;section=16" title="Edit section: Parents"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Scorpius had a close relationship with both his parents. He loved his <a href="/wiki/Astoria_Malfoy" title="Astoria Malfoy">mother</a> very much, and was very grief-stricken by her death. He believed she saw the best in him and his <a href="/wiki/Draco_Malfoy" title="Draco Malfoy">father</a>. His relationship with his father was slightly different, but he loved and admired him very much, believing he could depend on him to save him. He confided in his father who he relied on for comfort following his mother's death and had his approval for his friendship with Albus. Astoria and Draco raised Scorpius with the love and affection they did not have when they were children, and cared for him and his wellbeing. Unlike Draco himself, they did not spoil Scorpius, nor teach him their families' historical prejudice against Muggles. Indeed, when Scorpius and Albus were facing punishment for using the Time-Turner that caused a catastrophic change in reality (that he managed to reverse), Draco was prepared to accept any punishment that Professor McGonagall was about to hand down to the students.
</p>

</p><h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Scorpius_Malfoy?veaction=edit&amp;section=19" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Scorpius_Malfoy?action=edit&amp;section=19" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>The word <i>Scorpius</i> is Latin for "scorpion". Like his father (and to an extension of the <a href="/wiki/House_of_Black" title="House of Black">Black family</a>), Scorpius was named after a <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Scorpius" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Scorpius">constellation</a>, carrying on the tradition of giving astronomical names, started by the <a href="/wiki/House_of_Black" title="House of Black">family</a> of his paternal <a href="/wiki/Narcissa_Malfoy" title="Narcissa Malfoy">grandmother</a>. In Western astrology, it is the sign of <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Scorpio_(astrology)" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Scorpio (astrology)">Scorpio</a> and is linked in Greek mythology to <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hades" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Hades">Hades</a>, god of the underworld, as well as the scorpion that killed the hunter <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Orion" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Orion">Orion</a>.</li>
<li>In Greek mythology, <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hyperion_(mythology)" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Hyperion (mythology)">Hyperion</a> was the 'Titan of Light', the name means 'He who watches from above'. Hyperion was one of the twelve Titans who were eventually overthrown by the Olympians. He was also known for helping to overthrow his father, Ouranus. It is also the name of one of the moons of Saturn and of an <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hyperion_(poem)" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Hyperion (poem)">unfinished poem</a> by <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/John_Keats" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:John Keats">John Keats</a>.</li></ul>

<ul><li>Scorpius is currently portrayed by actor <a href="/wiki/Jonathan_Case" title="Jonathan Case">Jonathan Case</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Cursed_Child_(play)#Original_West_End_production" title="Harry Potter and the Cursed Child (play)">original West End production</a> of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Cursed_Child" title="Harry Potter and the Cursed Child">Harry Potter and the Cursed Child</a></i>. Past potrayals include Irish actor <a href="/wiki/Anthony_Boyle" title="Anthony Boyle">Anthony Boyle</a><sup id="cite_ref-11" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-11">&#91;11&#93;</a></sup> and <a href="/wiki/Samuel_Blenkin" title="Samuel Blenkin">Samuel Blenkin</a>. In 2019, Bubba Weiler took over the role from Anthony Boyle in the Broadway production.<sup id="cite_ref-12" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-12">&#91;12&#93;</a></sup> The original Melbourne cast featured Will McKenna as Scorpius Malfoy, who was nominated for a Helpmann Award for his performance.<sup id="cite_ref-13" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-13">&#91;13&#93;</a></sup> In the second year of the Melbourne production, Scorpius is played by Nyx Calder.<sup id="cite_ref-14" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-14">&#91;14&#93;</a></sup> As of 2020, Mathias Reiser portrays him in the Hamburg production.<sup id="cite_ref-15" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-15">&#91;15&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li>Scorpius was portrayed by <a href="/wiki/Bertie_Gilbert" title="Bertie Gilbert">Bertie Gilbert</a> in a brief scene of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Deathly_Hallows:_Part_2" title="Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 2">Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 2</a></i>. His face was never shown, and he only appears briefly while being hugged by his <a href="/wiki/Astoria_Malfoy" title="Astoria Malfoy">mother</a>. Bertie Gilbert stated on Twitter that he filmed a scene where Scorpius turns around and faces <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry</a>. This may appear as a deleted scene on Blu-Ray or the Ultimate Edition. It is not on the DVD.</li>
<li>Scorpius is a fourth cousin once removed of his classmates <a href="/wiki/Rose_Granger-Weasley" title="Rose Granger-Weasley">Rose Granger-Weasley</a> and <a href="/wiki/Albus_Potter" title="Albus Potter">Albus Potter</a>, and their siblings, <a href="/wiki/Hugo_Granger-Weasley" title="Hugo Granger-Weasley">Hugo Granger-Weasley</a>, <a href="/wiki/James_Potter_II" title="James Potter II">James Sirius Potter</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Lily_Potter" title="Lily Potter">Lily Luna Potter</a>, as all of them are descendants of <a href="/wiki/Phineas_Nigellus_Black" title="Phineas Nigellus Black">Phineas Nigellus Black</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-16" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-16">&#91;16&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li>In the Harry Potter Lego game, Scorpius is shown to wink at Rose Granger-Weasley while on the train, possibly hinting that he may be flirting with her. This is potentially corroborated by him asking her out on a date at school during the Cursed Child instalment of the series.</li>
<li>Scorpius is the second cousin of <a href="/wiki/Edward_Lupin" title="Edward Lupin">Teddy Lupin</a>.</li>
<li>Scorpius was the penultimate character introduced in the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_(book_series)" title="Harry Potter (book series)">Harry Potter books</a>.</li>
<li>Scorpius seems to have a hero worship towards Harry Potter, as he seems to have a high esteem of Harry. Scorpius has knowledge and an understanding of Harry Potter through studying of the wizard in his childhood. Furthermore, when coming out of the Black Lake, Scorpius was overjoyed in seeing Harry Potter.</li>
<li>Scorpius once had an imaginary friend called <a target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener" class="external text" href="https://harrypotter.fandom.com/wiki/Flurry">Flurry</a> they once fell out on the correct rules of <a href="/wiki/Gobstones" title="Gobstones">Gobstones</a>. Flurry's name was changed to Hector in the Melbourne production of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Cursed_Child" title="Harry Potter and the Cursed Child">Harry Potter and the Cursed Child</a></i>.</li></ul>
