<b>Astrix Alixan</b> (b. <a href="/wiki/1670s" title="1670s">1670s</a>) was a <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor" title="Gryffindor">Gryffindor</a> <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">student</a> at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> in the <a href="/wiki/1680s" title="1680s">1680s</a> and <a href="/wiki/1690s" title="1690s">1690s</a>. He was once <a href="/wiki/Quidditch_Captain#Hogwarts_Quidditch_Captains" title="Quidditch Captain">Captain</a> of the <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor_Quidditch_team" title="Gryffindor Quidditch team">Gryffindor Quidditch team</a> from <a href="/wiki/1688" class="mw-redirect" title="1688">1688</a> to <a href="/wiki/1692" class="mw-redirect" title="1692">1692</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Astrix_Alixan?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Astrix_Alixan?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>In <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Chamber_of_Secrets" title="Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets">Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets</a></i>, set during the <a href="/wiki/1992-1993_school_year" class="mw-redirect" title="1992-1993 school year">1992-1993 school year</a>, it is mentioned that the Gryffindor team had been one player short during the final match of the previous school year, against <a href="/wiki/Ravenclaw_Quidditch_team" title="Ravenclaw Quidditch team">Ravenclaw</a> (<a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> had been unconscious in the <a href="/wiki/Hospital_Wing" title="Hospital Wing">Hospital Wing</a>), and had for that reason suffered their worst defeat in three-hundred years.<sup id="cite_ref-COS7_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COS7-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> That would mean that their previous worst defeat ever had taken place in around the 1691-1692 school year, most likely during Astrix Alixan's captaincy.</li></ul>
