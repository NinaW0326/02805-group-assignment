<div class="mw-parser-output"><p><b>Cadium Featherstone</b> was an individual who lived at 6a Pelican Crescent in the borough of Chelsea, <a href="/wiki/London" title="London">London</a>, in <a href="/wiki/1927" title="1927">1927</a>. In that year, a letter addressed to Featherstone was written by the <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">British Ministry of Magic</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Wizengamot_Administration_Services" title="Wizengamot Administration Services">Wizengamot Administrative Department</a> and approved to be sent off.<sup id="cite_ref-Archive_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Archive-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
