The <b>Threat meter</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magical</a> device not unlike to the <a href="/wiki/Magical_Exposure_Threat_Level_Measurer" title="Magical Exposure Threat Level Measurer">Magical Exposure Threat</a> measurer at the <a href="/wiki/Woolworth_Building" title="Woolworth Building">headquarters</a> of the&#160;<a href="/wiki/Magical_Congress_of_the_United_States_of_America" title="Magical Congress of the United States of America">Magical Congress of the United States of America</a>, <a href="/wiki/New_York" title="New York">New York</a>, and was by the&#160;<a href="/wiki/Statute_of_Secrecy_Task_Force" title="Statute of Secrecy Task Force">Statute of Secrecy Task Force</a> in the <a href="/wiki/21st_century" title="21st century">21st century</a> to identify the danger a given <a href="/wiki/Foundable" title="Foundable">Foundable</a> posted to the <a href="/wiki/Statute_of_Secrecy" class="mw-redirect" title="Statute of Secrecy">Statute of Secrecy</a>. It was discovered that the more likely a Foundable is to break the International Statute of Secrecy, the higher the threat given by the system became, and the more difficult it was to return it to whence it came.<sup id="cite_ref-WU_1-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-WU-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>&#160;&#160;
