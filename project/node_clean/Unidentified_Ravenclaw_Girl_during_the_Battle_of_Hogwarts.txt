<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_Ravenclaw_Girl_during_the_Battle_of_Hogwarts?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_Ravenclaw_Girl_during_the_Battle_of_Hogwarts?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span id="1994–1995"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="1994.E2.80.931995">1994–1995</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_Ravenclaw_Girl_during_the_Battle_of_Hogwarts?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: 1994–1995">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_Ravenclaw_Girl_during_the_Battle_of_Hogwarts?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: 1994–1995"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>She was friends with <a href="/wiki/Cho_Chang" title="Cho Chang">Cho Chang</a> and often walked around Hogwarts with a group of fellow <a href="/wiki/Ravenclaw" title="Ravenclaw">Ravenclaw</a> girls during the <a href="/wiki/1994%E2%80%931995_school_year" title="1994–1995 school year">1994–1995 school year</a>. Sitting across from Cho, she was one of the two girls at the Ravenclaw table when Harry spilt his tea while attempting to smile at Cho.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>At the and of the school year, she attended the <a href="/wiki/Memorial_feast_to_Cedric_Diggory" title="Memorial feast to Cedric Diggory">memorial feast to Cedric Diggory</a>, and was seen sitting somewhere behind the <a href="/wiki/Trio" title="Trio">trio</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span id="1995–1996"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="1995.E2.80.931996">1995–1996</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_Ravenclaw_Girl_during_the_Battle_of_Hogwarts?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: 1995–1996">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_Ravenclaw_Girl_during_the_Battle_of_Hogwarts?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: 1995–1996"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<figure class="thumb tright show-info-icon" style="width: 252px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/0/07/Hogs_Head.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20150531084953" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/0/07/Hogs_Head.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/250?cb=20150531084953" decoding="async" width="250" height="104" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="Hogs Head.jpg" data-image-key="Hogs_Head.jpg" /></a> 	 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:Hogs_Head.jpg" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption">The girl sitting behind the Weasley twins</p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure>
<p>During the <a href="/wiki/1995%E2%80%931996_school_year" title="1995–1996 school year">1995–1996 school year</a>, she was seen attending the meeting gathered at the <a href="/wiki/Hog%27s_Head_Inn" title="Hog&#39;s Head Inn">Hog's Head Inn</a>, where the group, eventually to be called <a href="/wiki/Dumbledore%27s_Army" title="Dumbledore&#39;s Army">Dumbledore's Army</a>, was first proposed. She sat near <a href="/wiki/Padma_Patil" title="Padma Patil">Padma Patil</a> and behind <a href="/wiki/Fred_and_George_Weasley" title="Fred and George Weasley">Fred and George Weasley</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<figure class="thumb tright show-info-icon" style="width: 252px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/f/fb/Dumbledore-s-Army-ravenclaw-28262373-1024-768.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20150531084459" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/f/fb/Dumbledore-s-Army-ravenclaw-28262373-1024-768.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/250?cb=20150531084459" decoding="async" width="250" height="188" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="Dumbledore-s-Army-ravenclaw-28262373-1024-768.jpg" data-image-key="Dumbledore-s-Army-ravenclaw-28262373-1024-768.jpg" /></a> 	 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:Dumbledore-s-Army-ravenclaw-28262373-1024-768.jpg" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption">The girl standing next to Michael Corner after a DA lesson</p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure>
<p>She was then seen in Dumbledore's Army's lessons and practised defensive magic due to <a href="/wiki/Professor" title="Professor">Professor</a> <a href="/wiki/Dolores_Umbridge" title="Dolores Umbridge">Dolores Umbridge</a> refusing to teach practical <a href="/wiki/Defence_Against_the_Dark_Arts" title="Defence Against the Dark Arts">Defence Against the Dark Arts</a>. Heading to one meeting in the <a href="/wiki/Room_of_Requirement" title="Room of Requirement">Room of Requirement</a>, Cho and her, along with two other students, were seen followed by <a href="/wiki/Argus_Filch" title="Argus Filch">Argus Filch</a> and <a href="/wiki/Mrs_Norris" title="Mrs Norris">Mrs Norris</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>She attended a couple of meetings, including when the group learned the <a href="/wiki/Stunning_Spell" title="Stunning Spell">Stunning Spell</a>; she witnessed <a href="/wiki/Nigel_Wolpert" title="Nigel Wolpert">Nigel Wolpert</a> practising on <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>, as well as when <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione Granger</a> used it on <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ron Weasley</a>. She laughed at Ron when he was thrown across the room, and fussed over Hermione afterward.<sup id="cite_ref-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>She was often seen entering the Room of Requirement with other Ravenclaws including Cho and Luna. After Umbridge raided the Room of Requirement, as punishment she wrote lines in the Great Hall with a blood quill alongside fellow DA members, and awkwardly looked at Cho in entrance hall when finished, then scurried away with other Ravenclaws.<sup class="noprint">&#91;<a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Wikipedia:Citing_sources" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Wikipedia:Citing sources"><i><span title="The material in the vicinity of this tag needs references to reliable sources." style="white-space: nowrap;">citation needed</span></i></a>&#93;</sup>
</p>
<h3><span id="1997–1998"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="1997.E2.80.931998">1997–1998</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_Ravenclaw_Girl_during_the_Battle_of_Hogwarts?veaction=edit&amp;section=4" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: 1997–1998">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_Ravenclaw_Girl_during_the_Battle_of_Hogwarts?action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: 1997–1998"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>In 1998 she was part of the group of students who were hiding in the <a href="/wiki/Room_of_Requirement" title="Room of Requirement">Room of Requirement</a>. She participated in the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a>, and was present when the <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a> arrived at the <a href="/wiki/Viaduct_Courtyard" title="Viaduct Courtyard">Viaduct Courtyard</a> with <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>'s "corpse", and for <a href="/wiki/Ginevra_Weasley" title="Ginevra Weasley">Ginny Weasley</a>'s scream, which <a href="/wiki/Arthur_Weasley" title="Arthur Weasley">Arthur Weasley</a> stopped.<sup class="noprint">&#91;<a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Wikipedia:Citing_sources" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Wikipedia:Citing sources"><i><span title="The material in the vicinity of this tag needs references to reliable sources." style="white-space: nowrap;">citation needed</span></i></a>&#93;</sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Physical_description">Physical description</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_Ravenclaw_Girl_during_the_Battle_of_Hogwarts?veaction=edit&amp;section=5" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Physical description">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_Ravenclaw_Girl_during_the_Battle_of_Hogwarts?action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Physical description"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>She was skinny and taller than most girls. She had large ears and a large nose. Her hair was black curly and was usually tied in a tight pony, however in 1997-1998 she wore it straight.<sup id="cite_ref-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup> She also appeared to be of African descent or mixed race.
</p>
