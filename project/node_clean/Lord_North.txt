<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Lord_North?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Lord_North?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Lord North called <a href="/wiki/Minister_for_Magic" title="Minister for Magic">Minister for Magic</a> <a href="/wiki/Porteus_Knatchbull" title="Porteus Knatchbull">Porteus Knatchbull</a> in confidentially in <a href="/wiki/1782" class="mw-redirect" title="1782">1782</a>, to see whether he could help with <a href="/wiki/George_III" title="George III">King George III</a>'s emerging mental instability. Word leaked out that Lord North believed in wizards, and he was forced to resign after a motion of no confidence.<sup id="cite_ref-PMministers_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PMministers-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Lord_North?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Lord_North?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>In reality, Lord North resigned from Prime Minister after a vote of no confidence resulting from the <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Siege_of_Yorktown" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Siege of Yorktown">Siege of Yorktown</a>, which was the decisive battle against the <a href="/wiki/Great_Britain" title="Great Britain">British</a> in the <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/American_Revolutionary_War" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:American Revolutionary War">American Revolutionary War</a>.
<ul><li>Moreover, he subsequently served as Home Secretary and Leader of the House of Commons (jointly in the latter role with <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Charles_James_Fox" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Charles James Fox">Charles James Fox</a>) in a ministry nominally headed by <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/William_Cavendish-Bentinck,_3rd_Duke_of_Portland" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:William Cavendish-Bentinck, 3rd Duke of Portland">William Cavendish-Bentinck, 3rd Duke of Portland</a>.</li></ul></li></ul>
