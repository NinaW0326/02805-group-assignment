<b>Dunbar Oglethorpe</b> (b. <a href="/wiki/1968" title="1968">1968</a>) was a <a href="/wiki/Great_Britain" title="Great Britain">British</a> <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> who served as the Chief of the <a href="/wiki/Quidditch_Union_for_the_Administration_and_Betterment_of_the_British_League_and_its_Endeavours" title="Quidditch Union for the Administration and Betterment of the British League and its Endeavours">Quidditch Union for the Administration and Betterment of the British League and its Endeavours</a>. For this, he was featured on a <a href="/wiki/Chocolate_Frog_Card" title="Chocolate Frog Card">Chocolate Frog Card</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-PSG_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PSG-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-COSG_1-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COSG-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-POAG_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-POAG-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
