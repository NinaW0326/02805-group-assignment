<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="History">History</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Dentist?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: History">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Dentist?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: History"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p><a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger%27s_father" title="Hermione Granger&#39;s father">Mr</a> and <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger%27s_mother" title="Hermione Granger&#39;s mother">Mrs Granger</a>, parents of <a href="/wiki/Muggle-born" title="Muggle-born">Muggle-born</a> <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch</a> <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione Granger</a>, were both dentists.<sup id="cite_ref-PS12_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PS12-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> In the <a href="/wiki/1993%E2%80%931994_school_year" title="1993–1994 school year">1993–1994 school year</a>, she considered getting them some <a href="/wiki/Toothflossing_Stringmints" title="Toothflossing Stringmints">Toothflossing Stringmints</a> as a <a href="/wiki/Christmas" title="Christmas">Christmas</a> gift, believing they would enjoy them.<sup id="cite_ref-POA10_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-POA10-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p><i><a href="/wiki/My_Teeth" title="My Teeth">My Teeth</a></i> was a magazine of interest to those in this profession.<sup id="cite_ref-DHF1_4-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DHF1-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>When first observing <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle%27s_diary" class="mw-redirect" title="Tom Riddle&#39;s diary">Tom Riddle's diary</a>, <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> noted that there was nothing in it, "not even <i>Auntie Mabel's birthday</i> or <i>dentist, half-past three</i>."<sup id="cite_ref-CS13_5-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-CS13-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Dentist?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Dentist?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>In the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Half-Blood_Prince_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince (film)">film adaptation</a> of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Half-Blood_Prince" title="Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince">Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince</a></i>, it is implied that the profession does not exist in the <a href="/wiki/Wizarding_world" title="Wizarding world">wizarding world</a>, as Professor <a href="/wiki/Horace_Slughorn" title="Horace Slughorn">Horace Slughorn</a> did not know what they were.<sup id="cite_ref-HBPF_6-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBPF-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup> However, a <a href="/wiki/Surgical_and_Dental_operator" title="Surgical and Dental operator">surgical dental operator</a> stall can be seen in <a href="/wiki/Diagon_Alley" title="Diagon Alley">Diagon Alley</a> under <a href="/wiki/Mr_Mulpepper%27s_Apothecary" title="Mr Mulpepper&#39;s Apothecary">Mr Mulpepper's Apothecary</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_(film_series)" title="Harry Potter (film series)">films</a>, implying that the profession does in fact exist in the wizarding world. In addition, in the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Chamber_of_Secrets_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets (film)">film adaptation</a> of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Chamber_of_Secrets" title="Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets">Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets</a></i>, the clock in the <a href="/wiki/The_Burrow" title="The Burrow">Weasley house</a> showing that Fred, George, and Ron are home from rescuing Harry passes over "Quidditch", "Jail", and "Dentist", hinting that dentists are known in the wizarding world, or that the profession is just known by the family through Arthur's knowledge of <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggles</a>.</li></ul>
