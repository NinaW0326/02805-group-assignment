<p><b>Abbott</b> was buried in the cemetery at <a href="/wiki/Godric%27s_Hollow" title="Godric&#39;s Hollow">Godric's Hollow</a>. They were a possible ancestor of <a href="/wiki/Hannah_Abbott" title="Hannah Abbott">Hannah Abbott</a>, since wizarding families tended to live close together in certain villages after the <a href="/wiki/International_Statute_of_Wizarding_Secrecy" title="International Statute of Wizarding Secrecy">International Statute of Wizarding Secrecy</a> was effected in <a href="/wiki/1689" class="mw-redirect" title="1689">1689</a>. Harry noticed Abbott's tombstone while searching for <a href="/wiki/Potter_family" title="Potter family">his parents'</a> own in the Godric's Hollow graveyard on <a href="/wiki/24_December" title="24 December">Christmas Eve</a> of <a href="/wiki/1997" title="1997">1997</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Abbott was part of the Abbott family, which was one of the <a href="/wiki/Pure-blood" title="Pure-blood">pure-blood</a> families listed in the <a href="/wiki/Sacred_Twenty-Eight" title="Sacred Twenty-Eight">Sacred Twenty-Eight</a> directory in the <a href="/wiki/1930s" title="1930s">1930s</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-pottermore_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-pottermore-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> However, Abbott's actual blood-status was unknown and may have been a <a href="/wiki/Half-blood" title="Half-blood">half-blood</a> individual within the pure-blood family, like some Malfoys, or even a <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">muggle</a> or <a href="/wiki/Muggle-born" title="Muggle-born">muggle-born</a> that was not acknowledged or joined the family after the publication of the Sacred Twenty-Eight directory.<sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
