<b>Summerby</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> who attended <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> in the <a href="/wiki/1990s" title="1990s">1990s</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP26_2-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP26-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Summerby?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Summerby?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>He was a <a href="/wiki/Hufflepuff" title="Hufflepuff">Hufflepuff</a> student who replaced <a href="/wiki/Cedric_Diggory" title="Cedric Diggory">Cedric Diggory</a> as their <a href="/wiki/Quidditch" title="Quidditch">Quidditch</a> <a href="/wiki/Quidditch_team" title="Quidditch team">team</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Seeker" title="Seeker">Seeker</a> at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> after Diggory's tragic death.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP26_2-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP26-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>During a match against <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor" title="Gryffindor">Gryffindor</a> in <a href="/wiki/1996" title="1996">1996</a>, he was beaten to the <a href="/wiki/Golden_Snitch" title="Golden Snitch">Golden Snitch</a> by <a href="/wiki/Ginevra_Weasley" title="Ginevra Weasley">Ginny Weasley</a>, although Ginny said it was partly because he had a <a href="/wiki/Common_cold" title="Common cold">cold</a> and sneezed at the wrong time. Ginny Weasley replaced <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> following his life-long ban from Quidditch by <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts</a> <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_High_Inquisitor" title="Hogwarts High Inquisitor">High Inquisitor</a> <a href="/wiki/Dolores_Umbridge" title="Dolores Umbridge">Dolores Umbridge</a> (although this ban was immediately lifted when Umbridge was removed from Hogwarts).<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP26_2-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP26-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
