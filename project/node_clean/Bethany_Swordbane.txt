<b>Bethany Swordbane</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch</a> and a student at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a>. She was obsessed with <a href="/wiki/Goblin" title="Goblin">Goblins</a>. She would make <a href="/wiki/Constance_Pickering" title="Constance Pickering">Constance Pickering</a> go to <a href="/wiki/Gringotts_Wizarding_Bank" title="Gringotts Wizarding Bank">Gringotts</a> with her and they would stand in the lobby for hours while she ogled.<sup id="cite_ref-WU_1-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-WU-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
