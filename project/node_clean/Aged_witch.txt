<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Aged_witch?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Aged_witch?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>In the afternoon of <a href="/wiki/10_August" title="10 August">10 August</a>, <a href="/wiki/1992" title="1992">1992</a>, this witch was in <a href="/wiki/Knockturn_Alley" title="Knockturn Alley">Knockturn Alley</a>, carrying a tray of what appeared to be whole <a href="/wiki/Human" title="Human">human</a> fingernails; it is possible she planned to sell them.
</p><p>She witnessed <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> wandering <a href="/wiki/Knockturn_Alley" title="Knockturn Alley">Knockturn Alley</a>, staring anxiously around and clutching his broken <a href="/wiki/Glasses" title="Glasses">glasses</a> tightly to his face. Seeing his distress, she asked him if he was lost, only frightening him by her leer.
</p><p><a href="/wiki/Rubeus_Hagrid" title="Rubeus Hagrid">Rubeus Hagrid</a> soon arrived, having planned to buy some <a href="/wiki/Flesh-Eating_Slug_Repellent" title="Flesh-Eating Slug Repellent">Flesh-Eating Slug Repellent</a>, although his plans were forgotten when he saw Harry with the witch. His arrival, combined with his <a href="/wiki/Half-giant" title="Half-giant">prodigious size</a>, scared her and caused her to drop most of the fingernails, at which point she cursed.
</p><p>When Hagrid grabbed Harry and dragged him away, he accidentally knocked the tray out of the witch's hands; her terrified shrieks echoed against the cold stone walls, following the pair out of the alley.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Physical_appearance">Physical appearance</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Aged_witch?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Physical appearance">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Aged_witch?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Physical appearance"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch</a> had pitch-black hair, the tips of which were a light grey; her hair was dirty and fell down to her shoulders. Her wrinkled skin was pale, although when bathed in shadow it appeared quite tan. Her narrow eyes were a dark green colour, almost appearing blue or green in certain lights.<sup id="cite_ref-COSF_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COSF-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> She was rather elderly by <a href="/wiki/August" title="August">August</a> of <a href="/wiki/1992" title="1992">1992</a>, and had mossy teeth.<sup id="cite_ref-CoS4_2-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-CoS4-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Aged_witch?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Aged_witch?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li><a href="/wiki/Jenny_Tarren" title="Jenny Tarren">Jenny Tarren</a> portrayed this witch in the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Chamber_of_Secrets_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets (film)">film adaptation</a> of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Chamber_of_Secrets" title="Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets">Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets</a></i><sup id="cite_ref-imdb_4-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-imdb-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li>In the Game Boy Advance version of the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Chamber_of_Secrets_(video_game)" title="Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets (video game)"><i>Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets</i> video game</a>, this woman is referred to as a <a href="/wiki/Hag" title="Hag">hag</a> rather than a witch.</li></ul>
<ul><li>In the PS1 versions of the first two instalments, she is guarding the gate which serves as Knockturn Alley's entrance next to Gringotts. If Harry gets too close, she loudly warns him to go no further. She is handling her fingernail collection in both of these appearances. What might be a Knockturn Alley cat can be heard growling if the player approaches the gate but it could also be her.</li></ul>
