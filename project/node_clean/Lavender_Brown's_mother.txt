<b>Mrs Brown</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Pure-blood" title="Pure-blood">pure-blood</a><sup id="cite_ref-HPM_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HPM-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch</a>, wife of <a href="/wiki/Lavender_Brown%27s_father" title="Lavender Brown&#39;s father">Mr Brown</a> and mother of <a href="/wiki/Lavender_Brown" title="Lavender Brown">Lavender</a>. In <a href="/wiki/1993" title="1993">1993</a>, she and her husband sent a letter to Lavender, informing her that her pet <a href="/wiki/Rabbit" title="Rabbit">rabbit</a> <a href="/wiki/Binky" title="Binky">Binky</a> had been killed by a <a href="/wiki/Fox" title="Fox">fox</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-POA8_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-POA8-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Prior to that school year, either Mrs Brown or her husband signed a <a href="/wiki/Hogsmeade_permission_form" title="Hogsmeade permission form">Hogsmeade permission form</a> for Lavender.<sup id="cite_ref-POAF_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-POAF-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>On <a href="/wiki/2_May" title="2 May">2 May</a>, <a href="/wiki/1998" title="1998">1998</a>, her daughter died during the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a> when she was attacked by <a href="/wiki/Fenrir_Greyback" title="Fenrir Greyback">Fenrir Greyback</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-PageToScreen_4-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PageToScreen-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
