<b>Ragmar Dorkins</b> was the manager of the <a href="/wiki/Chudley_Cannons" title="Chudley Cannons">Chudley Cannons</a> <a href="/wiki/Quidditch" title="Quidditch">Quidditch</a> <a href="/wiki/Quidditch_team" title="Quidditch team">team</a>.

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Ragmar_Dorkins?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Ragmar_Dorkins?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>In <a href="/wiki/June" title="June">June</a> <a href="/wiki/1998" title="1998">1998</a>, Ragmar gave a statement to the <a href="/wiki/Daily_Prophet" title="Daily Prophet">Daily Prophet</a> pleading patience from Chudley Cannon fans after his team lost 350-0 in a game against the <a href="/wiki/Appleby_Arrows" title="Appleby Arrows">Appleby Arrows</a><sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>. 
</p><p>Later, in February 1999, the Daily Prophet took note of Ragmar's delight when the Chudley Cannons drew a match against the <a href="/wiki/Caerphilly_Catapults" title="Caerphilly Catapults">Caerphilly Catapults</a>, breaking their 16 game losing streak<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>. 
</p><p>In June 1999, the Chudley Cannons losing streak was broken when they won a game against the <a href="/wiki/Wigtown_Wanderers" title="Wigtown Wanderers">Wigtown Wanderers</a>, and Ragmar told the Daily Prophet that it was "the happiest day of my life"<sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>.
</p><p>In October 1999, the Daily Prophet noted Ragmar "collapsed in shock" after the Chudley Cannons won their second game in a row<sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>, against the <a href="/wiki/Falmouth_Falcons" title="Falmouth Falcons">Falmouth Falcons</a>.
</p>
