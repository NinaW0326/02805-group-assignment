<b>Alys</b><sup id="cite_ref-script_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-script-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> (born c. <a href="/wiki/1984" title="1984">1984</a>-<a href="/wiki/1985" title="1985">1985</a>) was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch</a> who began attending <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> in <a href="/wiki/1996" title="1996">1996</a><sup id="cite_ref-script_3-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-script-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> and was <a href="/wiki/Sorting_ceremony" title="Sorting ceremony">Sorted</a> into <a href="/wiki/Hufflepuff" title="Hufflepuff">Hufflepuff</a> <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Houses" title="Hogwarts Houses">House</a>. In her first year, she was taught <a href="/wiki/Potions_(class)" title="Potions (class)">Potions</a> by the <a href="/wiki/Potions_Master" title="Potions Master">Potions Master</a>, <a href="/wiki/Professor" title="Professor">Professor</a> <a href="/wiki/Horace_Slughorn" title="Horace Slughorn">Horace Slughorn</a>. As Alys was clearing up her things with the other students, Slughorn called to her, and reminded her to take the <a href="/wiki/Rat_tail" title="Rat tail">rat tail</a> she had left behind on the desk.<sup id="cite_ref-HBPF_2-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBPF-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Alys?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Alys?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The Welsh name "Alys" is probably directly rooted in the name <i>Alice</i>, which is rooted in the Old German name <i>Adelaide</i> (or, directly, <i>Adelheidis</i>), meaning, 'Nobility".
</p>
