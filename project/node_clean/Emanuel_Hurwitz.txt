<b>Emanuel Hurwitz</b> (<a href="/wiki/7_May" title="7 May">7 May</a>, <a href="/wiki/1919" title="1919">1919</a> â€” <a href="/wiki/19_November" title="19 November">19 November</a>, <a href="/wiki/2006" title="2006">2006</a>) was a <a href="/wiki/Great_Britain" title="Great Britain">British</a> <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggle</a> <a href="/wiki/Violin" title="Violin">violinist</a> who lead several orchestras and ensembles.<sup id="cite_ref-wikipedia_1-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-wikipedia-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>On Sunday, 7 May in a year prior to <a href="/wiki/1997" title="1997">1997</a>, at 7:30 in the evening, Emanuel and his wife, <a href="/wiki/Kay_Hurwitz" title="Kay Hurwitz">Kay</a>, a <a href="/wiki/Viola" title="Viola">violist</a>, gave a performance of <a href="/wiki/Wolfgang_Amadeus_Mozart" title="Wolfgang Amadeus Mozart">Mozart</a>'s <i><a href="/wiki/Mozart%27s_Sinfonia_Concertanti" class="mw-redirect" title="Mozart&#39;s Sinfonia Concertanti">Sinfonia Concertanti</a></i> at <a href="/wiki/St._Mary%27s_Church_Hall" title="St. Mary&#39;s Church Hall">St. Mary's Church Hall</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-hple_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-hple-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> The performance was directed by <a href="/wiki/Alec_Webb" title="Alec Webb">Alec Webb</a>, and the <a href="/wiki/Dollis_Chamber_Ensemble" title="Dollis Chamber Ensemble">Dollis Chamber Ensemble</a> accompanied the couple for the first time ever.<sup id="cite_ref-hple_3-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-hple-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione Granger</a> had a poster for this concert stuck up on the bulletin board in <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger%27s_bedroom" title="Hermione Granger&#39;s bedroom">her bedroom</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Granger_family" title="Granger family">Granger family</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Grangers%27_house" class="mw-redirect" title="Grangers&#39; house">house</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-hple_3-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-hple-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-dhp1_4-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-dhp1-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Emanuel_Hurwitz?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Emanuel_Hurwitz?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>In real life, Emanuel and Kay Hurwitz are, respectively, the stepgrandfather and grandmother of <a href="/wiki/Miraphora_Mina" title="Miraphora Mina">Miraphora Mina</a>, a graphic artist who worked on all eight <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_films" class="mw-redirect" title="Harry Potter films"><i>Harry Potter</i> films</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> Mina likely created the prop concert poster featured in <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Deathly_Hallows:_Part_1" title="Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 1">Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 1</a></i> as a tribute to them.</li></ul>
