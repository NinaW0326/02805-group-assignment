<p>The <b>owner</b> of the <a href="/wiki/Railview_Hotel" title="Railview Hotel">Railview Hotel</a> in <a href="/wiki/Cokeworth" title="Cokeworth">Cokeworth</a> was a <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggle</a> woman. On <a href="/wiki/30_July" title="30 July">30 July</a>, <a href="/wiki/1991" title="1991">1991</a>, she found about one hundred <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts</a> <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_acceptance_letter" title="Hogwarts acceptance letter">acceptance letters</a> for <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> at the front desk, as the boy was staying in <a href="/wiki/Railview_Hotel%27s_room_17" class="mw-redirect" title="Railview Hotel&#39;s room 17">room 17</a> with the <a href="/wiki/Dursley_family" title="Dursley family">Dursleys</a>. <a href="/wiki/Vernon_Dursley" title="Vernon Dursley">Vernon Dursley</a> took these letters from her and checked out of the hotel.<sup id="cite_ref-PS3_1-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PS3-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
