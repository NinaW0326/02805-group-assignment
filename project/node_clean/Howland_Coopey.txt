<b>Howland Coopey</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> who sent a letter to the <i><a href="/wiki/Daily_Prophet" title="Daily Prophet">Daily Prophet</a>'s</i> advice column<sup id="cite_ref-:0_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:0-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>. He was sick and wanted to know if the symptoms he was experiencing — a green-and-purple rash between his toes and sparks coming out of his nostrils when he sneezed — were cause for concern. In his reply, <a href="/wiki/Helbert_Spleen" title="Helbert Spleen">Professor Spleen</a> informed Coopey that he was suffering from a <i>"simple case"</i> of <a href="/wiki/Dragon_Pox" title="Dragon Pox">Dragon Pox</a><sup id="cite_ref-:0_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:0-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>. Oddly, despite Dragon Pox being potentially fatal, Professor Spleen does not recomned he go to St Mungo's, but rather to "immerse [his] nose continually in ice cold water<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>."
