A <b>Ministry Sleigh</b><sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> was a horse-drawn vehicle for travelling on surfaces of snow or ice, that was used by the <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">Ministry of Magic</a> for the transportation of Ministry officials. It was dark in colour, and had the Ministry logo embossed on the doors.
</p>

<p>On <a href="/wiki/18_December" title="18 December">18 December</a> of <a href="/wiki/1993" title="1993">1993</a>, <a href="/wiki/Cornelius_Fudge" title="Cornelius Fudge">Cornelius Fudge</a> used one of these sleighs to get to the <a href="/wiki/Three_Broomsticks_Inn" title="Three Broomsticks Inn">Three Broomsticks Inn</a>, in <a href="/wiki/Hogsmeade" title="Hogsmeade">Hogsmeade</a>. <a href="/wiki/Rubeus_Hagrid" title="Rubeus Hagrid">Rubeus Hagrid</a> met him outside the pub, and offered to hold the door of the sleigh open for him, but only achieved in tearing the small door from its hinges.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
