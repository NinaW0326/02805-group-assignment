This <b><a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a></b> was a <a href="/wiki/Snatchers" title="Snatchers">Snatcher</a> who fought during the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a>. He took part in the <a href="/wiki/Skirmish_at_the_Wooden_Bridge" title="Skirmish at the Wooden Bridge">skirmish</a> at the <a href="/wiki/Wooden_Bridge" title="Wooden Bridge">Wooden Bridge</a>, chasing after <a href="/wiki/Neville_Longbottom" title="Neville Longbottom">Neville Longbottom</a>. He died when the bridge collapsed.<sup id="cite_ref-DHF2_1-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DHF2-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
