<p>The <b>Senior Undersecretary to the Minister for Magic</b> was a high-ranking position in the bureaucracy of the <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">Ministry of Magic</a>, serving as the principal assistant to to the <a href="/wiki/Minister_for_Magic" title="Minister for Magic">Minister for Magic</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP8_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP8-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p><a href="/wiki/Dolores_Umbridge" title="Dolores Umbridge">Dolores Umbridge</a> was the only person known to have held the title, serving under <a href="/wiki/Cornelius_Fudge" title="Cornelius Fudge">Cornelius Fudge</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP8_2-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP8-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/Rufus_Scrimgeour" title="Rufus Scrimgeour">Rufus Scrimgeour</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-HBP16_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP16-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> as well as then <a href="/wiki/Pius_Thicknesse" title="Pius Thicknesse">Pius Thicknesse</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-DH12_4-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH12-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> until she was arrested and imprisoned in <a href="/wiki/Azkaban" title="Azkaban">Azkaban</a> for her crimes against <a href="/wiki/Muggle-born" title="Muggle-born">Muggle-borns</a> in <a href="/wiki/1998" title="1998">1998</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-imprisonment_5-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-imprisonment-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Senior_Undersecretary_to_the_Minister_for_Magic?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Senior_Undersecretary_to_the_Minister_for_Magic?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>During <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Careers_Advice" title="Careers Advice">Careers Advice</a> session, when <a href="/wiki/Minerva_McGonagall" title="Minerva McGonagall">Professor McGonagall</a> supported <a href="/wiki/Albus_Dumbledore" title="Albus Dumbledore">Albus Dumbledore</a>'s return to Hogwarts after his dismissal, Umbridge went into hysterics and proclaimed that Minerva had plans to make Dumbledore Minister for Magic and that she would become his Senior Undersecretary by usurping Umbridge's position, accusations that Minerva took as offensive.</li>
<li>In general, there are distinct differences between the organisation of the <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">British Ministry of Magic</a> and the British muggle government. One of the few Ministry of Magic positions that might be directly linked with a Muggle counterpart is that of <i>Senior Undersecretary to the Minister of Magic</i>. In the British Muggle government, under each&#160;<a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/minister_(government)" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:minister (government)">minister</a> is a civil servant who holds the title&#160;<a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Permanent_Under_Secretary_of_State" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Permanent Under Secretary of State">Permanent Under Secretary of State</a>. This person is the senior civil servant in that ministry and is responsible for both the day-to-day operations and routine administrative matters in that <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ministry_(government_department)" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Ministry (government department)">ministry</a>. If the two positions are indeed similar, it provides some insight into Dolores Umbridge and her time in the Ministry.&#160;As a&#160;<a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/civil_servant" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:civil servant">civil servant</a> she would presumably be beyond "political" matters, and would be expected to be loyal to the Ministry as an institution and not to a particular Minister of Magic. This would explain why she retained her position under several different Ministers of Magic. If there is indeed a&#160;<a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/civil_service" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:civil service">civil service</a> in the Ministry of Magic, it would also explain why she could not be easily dismissed from her position at the Ministry after her time at Hogwarts - until, that is, she was tried and convicted of criminal charges. This would also explain why&#160;<a href="/wiki/Arthur_Weasley" title="Arthur Weasley">Arthur Weasley</a>&#160;also managed to retain his position after&#160;<a href="/wiki/Pius_Thicknesse" title="Pius Thicknesse">Pius Thicknesse</a>&#160;became Minister of Magic.</li></ul>
