<b>Edward Rabnott</b><sup id="cite_ref-OOTPG_NDS_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTPG_NDS-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a>. There were portraits of him hung at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Castle" title="Hogwarts Castle">Hogwarts Castle</a>. He had at least three different personalities, who often talked and bickered amongst themselves.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTPG_1-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTPG-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Edward_Rabnott?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Edward_Rabnott?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span id="1995–1996_school_year"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="1995.E2.80.931996_school_year"><a href="/wiki/1995%E2%80%931996_school_year" title="1995–1996 school year">1995–1996 school year</a></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Edward_Rabnott?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: 1995–1996 school year">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Edward_Rabnott?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: 1995–1996 school year"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>In the <a href="/wiki/1995%E2%80%931996_school_year" title="1995–1996 school year">1995–1996 school year</a>, Edward's portraits hid a secret passageway from the <a href="/wiki/Seventh-floor_corridor" title="Seventh-floor corridor">Seventh-floor corridor</a> to the fourth-floor. The password to this shortcut was "<i>Three heads are better than one</i>." By <a href="/wiki/1995" title="1995">1995</a>, one of his portraits was covered in cobwebs and inaccessible. Edward had promised to give <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> the password to his secret passage, only if he could clear the cobwebs off the portrait. As Harry did so, the portrait kept his promise.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTPG_1-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTPG-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Edward_Rabnott?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Edward_Rabnott?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>Edward Rabnott's portrait seem to be a direct homage to <a target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener" class="external text" href="http://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/2/2d/Anthonis_van_Dyck_041.jpg"><i>Charles I, King of England, from Three Angles</i></a>, by <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Anthony_van_Dyck" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Anthony van Dyck">Anthony van Dyck</a>.</li>
<li>Edward may be a relative of <a href="/wiki/Modesty_Rabnott" title="Modesty Rabnott">Modesty Rabnott</a>.</li>
<li>Mr Rabnott may have died sometime prior to <a href="/wiki/1995" title="1995">1995</a>, as many portraits hung at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Castle" title="Hogwarts Castle">Hogwarts Castle</a> depict deceased individuals.</li>
<li>In the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Order_of_the_Phoenix_(video_game)" title="Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix (video game)">video game adaptation</a> of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Order_of_the_Phoenix" title="Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix">Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix</a></i>, Edward was voiced by <a href="/wiki/David_Coker" title="David Coker">David Coker</a>.</li></ul>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Edward_Rabnott?veaction=edit&amp;section=4" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Edward_Rabnott?action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The name "Edward" means "rich guard", derived from the Old English elements ead "rich, blessed" and weard "guard". Saint Edward the Confessor was the king of England shortly before the Norman conquest.
</p>
