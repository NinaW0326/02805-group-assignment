<p>The <b>Goblet of Fire</b> was the "impartial judge" for the <a href="/wiki/Triwizard_Tournament" title="Triwizard Tournament">Triwizard Tournament</a>, who chose which students will represent their respective schools during the upcoming tasks of the tournament.<sup id="cite_ref-GOF16_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF16-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>After the students have finished placing their name in Goblet, the Goblet then chose one student from each school by spitting out their name in a rush of red <a href="/wiki/Fire" title="Fire">flames</a>. Once chosen the participants were subject to a binding <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magical</a> contract in which they can not back out of the tournament.<sup id="cite_ref-GOF16_1-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF16-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Description">Description</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Goblet_of_Fire?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Description">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Goblet_of_Fire?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Description"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<figure class="thumb tright show-info-icon" style="width: 145px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/7/7c/Goblet_of_Fire_%28Pottermore%29.png/revision/latest?cb=20140118173833" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/7/7c/Goblet_of_Fire_%28Pottermore%29.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/143?cb=20140118173833" decoding="async" width="143" height="245" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="Goblet of Fire (Pottermore).png" data-image-key="Goblet_of_Fire_(Pottermore).png" /></a> 	 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:Goblet_of_Fire_(Pottermore).png" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption">The Goblet of Fire</p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure>
<p>The Goblet was described as being made of wood and — when active — has blue-white <a href="/wiki/Fire" title="Fire">flames</a> up to the edges, which turn red when someone puts their name in or when it spits the name of the chosen champions. When it was not in use, it was kept in a jewelled casket.<sup id="cite_ref-GOF16_1-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF16-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>It acted as an impartial judge. During the 1994 tournament, an age restriction was added and as such, an <a href="/wiki/Age_Line" title="Age Line">Age Line</a> was cast to ensure that no one under the minimum age could try to put their name into the goblet. Anyone who tried to do so with an <a href="/wiki/Ageing_Potion" title="Ageing Potion">Ageing Potion</a> would end up hit with its flames and forced them to age even further as old men, as demonstrated by what happened to <a href="/wiki/Fred_and_George_Weasley" title="Fred and George Weasley">Fred and George Weasley</a> when they tried to trick the goblet via that exact method.<sup id="cite_ref-GOF16_1-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF16-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Though it was an impartial judge, it can be <a href="/wiki/Charm" title="Charm">bewitched</a> to make decisions that transgress the rules. Since the goblet is a highly powerful magical artefact, it would require a <a href="/wiki/Confundus_Charm" title="Confundus Charm">Confundus Charm</a> of equal or greater power to accomplish. After the champions are selected, it will extinguish itself and will not reignite until the next tournament.<sup id="cite_ref-GOF17_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF17-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Creation">Creation</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Goblet_of_Fire?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Creation">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Goblet_of_Fire?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Creation"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>The Goblet of Fire was created at an unknown time and by an unknown person. As many who knew about the <a href="/wiki/Triwizard_Tournament" title="Triwizard Tournament">Triwizard Tournament</a> were unaware of its use in the competition in <a href="/wiki/1994" title="1994">1994</a>, it had either been created rather recently, or so long ago that it was long-forgotten by the time it was unveiled once again in 1994.
</p>

</p>

<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="The_Calamity">The Calamity</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Goblet_of_Fire?veaction=edit&amp;section=6" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: The Calamity">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Goblet_of_Fire?action=edit&amp;section=6" title="Edit section: The Calamity"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>During the <a href="/wiki/Calamity" title="Calamity">Calamity</a> which affected the <a href="/wiki/Wizarding_world" title="Wizarding world">Wizarding world</a> in the <a href="/wiki/2010s" title="2010s">2010s</a>, the Goblet of Fire was amongst the various <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magical</a> items which appeared across the world as one of many <a href="/wiki/Foundable" title="Foundable">Foundables</a>. These had to be overpowered by volunteer <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizards and witches</a> who worked for the <a href="/wiki/Statute_of_Secrecy_Task_Force" title="Statute of Secrecy Task Force">Statute of Secrecy Task Force</a>, to return these foundables to their original place.<sup id="cite_ref-WU_4-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-WU-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Goblet_of_Fire?veaction=edit&amp;section=7" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Goblet_of_Fire?action=edit&amp;section=7" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>In the films, the Goblet is larger than in the books and  <i><a href="/wiki/Pottermore" title="Pottermore">Pottermore</a></i>.</li>
<li>The Goblet may possess an unknown degree of sentience, similar to such objects as the <a href="/wiki/Sorting_Hat" title="Sorting Hat">Sorting Hat</a>. Beyond its mere ability to sort through applicants, this is further suggested by the fact that the <a href="/wiki/Confundus_Charm" title="Confundus Charm">Confundus Charm</a>, whose purpose is to confuse a mind, could be used against it, albeit only by a wizard of great skill.</li>
<li>What becomes of the Goblet afterwards is unknown, though it has been rendered effectively useless with the permanent discontinuation of the Triwizard Tournament.</li></ul>
