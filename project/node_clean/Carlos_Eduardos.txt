<b>Carlos Eduardos</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizarding</a> author. He edited the book <i><a href="/wiki/The_Scars_of_Salem:_Essays_on_the_Witch_Trials_of_1692" title="The Scars of Salem: Essays on the Witch Trials of 1692">The Scars of Salem: Essays on the Witch Trials of 1692</a></i>, which featured a collection of essays on the <a href="/wiki/Salem_Witch_Trials" title="Salem Witch Trials">Salem Witch Trials</a> and their lasting impact on the <a href="/wiki/United_States_of_America" title="United States of America">American</a> wizarding community.<sup id="cite_ref-minalima_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-minalima-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
