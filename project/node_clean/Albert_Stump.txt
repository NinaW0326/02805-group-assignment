<b>Albert Gibbering Stump</b>, <span class="template-c"><a href="/wiki/Order_of_Merlin" title="Order of Merlin">O.M. (unknown class)</a></span>, (b. <a href="/wiki/1662" class="mw-redirect" title="1662">1662</a>) was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> whose <a href="/wiki/Portrait" title="Portrait">portrait</a> (dated <a href="/wiki/1700" class="mw-redirect" title="1700">1700</a>, when Albert was 38) hung at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Castle" title="Hogwarts Castle">Hogwarts Castle</a> during the <a href="/wiki/1990s" title="1990s">1990s</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-HPLE_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HPLE-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Albert was part of the prominent wizarding <a href="/wiki/Stump_family" title="Stump family">Stump family</a> and, as such, was most likely related to <a href="/wiki/Grogan_Stump" title="Grogan Stump">Grogan Stump</a>, <a href="/wiki/Google_Stump" title="Google Stump">Google Stump</a> and <a href="/wiki/Tobias_Stump" title="Tobias Stump">Tobias Stump</a>.
</p>
