<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Alberta_Toothill?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Alberta_Toothill?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_life">Early life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Alberta_Toothill?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Early life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Alberta_Toothill?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Early life"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Alberta was born in <a href="/wiki/1391" class="mw-redirect" title="1391">1391</a>, somewhere in <a href="/wiki/England" title="England">England</a>. If she enrolled at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a>, she attended the school from around <a href="/wiki/1402" class="mw-redirect" title="1402">1402</a> to <a href="/wiki/1409" class="mw-redirect" title="1409">1409</a>, under <a href="/wiki/Headmaster" title="Headmaster">Headmistress</a> <a href="/wiki/Phyllida_Spore" title="Phyllida Spore">Phyllida Spore</a>.
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Adulthood">Adulthood</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Alberta_Toothill?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Adulthood">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Alberta_Toothill?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Adulthood"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<figure class="thumb tleft show-info-icon" style="width: 192px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/0/03/AlbertaToothill.png/revision/latest?cb=20120108020559" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/0/03/AlbertaToothill.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/190?cb=20120108020559" decoding="async" width="190" height="187" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="AlbertaToothill.png" data-image-key="AlbertaToothill.png" /></a> 	 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:AlbertaToothill.png" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption">Alberta Toothill cheering after winning the <a href="/wiki/All-England_Wizarding_Duelling_Competition" title="All-England Wizarding Duelling Competition">All-England Wizarding Duelling Competition</a></p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure>
<p>In <a href="/wiki/1430" class="mw-redirect" title="1430">1430</a>, aged thirty-eight or thirty-nine, Toothill took part in the <a href="/wiki/All-England_Wizarding_Duelling_Competition" title="All-England Wizarding Duelling Competition">All-England Wizarding Duelling Competition</a>. She made it to the finals, and proved to be an effective duellist when she, against all odds, defeated the expected winner, <a href="/wiki/Samson_Wiblin" title="Samson Wiblin">Samson Wiblin</a>, with a <a href="/wiki/Blasting_Curse" title="Blasting Curse">Blasting Curse</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-COSG_1-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COSG-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Alberta Toothill died in <a href="/wiki/1483" class="mw-redirect" title="1483">1483</a>, aged around ninety-two.<sup id="cite_ref-COSG_1-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COSG-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Post-mortem">Post-mortem</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Alberta_Toothill?veaction=edit&amp;section=4" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Post-mortem">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Alberta_Toothill?action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Post-mortem"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>By the <a href="/wiki/1990s" title="1990s">1990s</a>, Toothill was featured on a <a href="/wiki/Chocolate_Frog_Card" title="Chocolate Frog Card">Chocolate Frog Card</a> for her skill in duelling, which pictured her cheering and holding the All-England Wizarding Duelling trophy after her defeat of <a href="/wiki/Samson_Wiblin" title="Samson Wiblin">Samson Wiblin</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-COSG_1-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COSG-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Sometime in the <a href="/wiki/2010s" title="2010s">2010s</a>, a member of the <a href="/wiki/Statute_of_Secrecy_Task_Force" title="Statute of Secrecy Task Force">Statute of Secrecy Task Force</a> discovered a long-lost <a href="/wiki/Memory" title="Memory">memory</a> phial belonging to Alberta Toothill, featuring her 1430 <a href="/wiki/Duelling" title="Duelling">duelling</a> victory. The historical discovery brought new techniques to light — studying the duel came in handy to contain the threat of the <a href="/wiki/Calamity" title="Calamity">Calamity</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-WU_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-WU-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Magical_abilities_and_skills">Magical abilities and skills</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Alberta_Toothill?veaction=edit&amp;section=5" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Alberta_Toothill?action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li><b><a href="/wiki/Duelling" title="Duelling">Duelling</a></b>: Toothill was an incredibly accomplished and proficient duellist, having been the winner of the <a href="/wiki/All-England_Wizarding_Duelling_Competition" title="All-England Wizarding Duelling Competition">All-England Wizarding Duelling Competition</a> in <a href="/wiki/1430" class="mw-redirect" title="1430">1430</a>, defeating numerous skilled opponents during the tournament. She was also a mistress of <a href="/wiki/Martial_magic" title="Martial magic">martial magic</a>, having successfully cast a particularly powerful and destructive <a href="/wiki/Blasting_Curse" title="Blasting Curse">Blasting Curse</a> which defeated her opponent with much force. For her outstanding achievement in the sport of duelling, she was immortalised on a silver <a href="/wiki/Chocolate_Frog_Card" title="Chocolate Frog Card">Chocolate Frog Card</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-COSG_1-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COSG-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup></li></ul>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Alberta_Toothill?veaction=edit&amp;section=6" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Alberta_Toothill?action=edit&amp;section=6" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>The name <i>Alberta </i>is the feminine form of German name <i>Alberta </i>which means <i>noble and intelligent</i>.<i> </i></li></ul>
