<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/David_Heyman_(in-universe)?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/David_Heyman_(in-universe)?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>He signed a wanted poster issued for <a href="/wiki/Newton_Scamander" title="Newton Scamander">Newt Scamander</a> and <a href="/wiki/Jacob_Kowalski" title="Jacob Kowalski">Jacob Kowalski</a> on <a href="/wiki/6_December" title="6 December">6 December</a> 1926. It advised that, in the event of the pair's capture, he should be immediately contacted by <a href="/wiki/Telephone" title="Telephone">telephone</a> at 3100 Heyman.<sup id="cite_ref-itmfb_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-itmfb-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-poster2_2-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-poster2-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
