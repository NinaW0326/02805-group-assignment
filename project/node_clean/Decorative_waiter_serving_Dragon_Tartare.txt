<dd>&#8212; Waiter and Hermione Granger<sup class="noprint"><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Half-Blood_Prince_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince (film)">[src]</a></sup></dd></dl>

<p>This <b>waiter</b> served food and drinks at the <a href="/wiki/Slug_Club" title="Slug Club">Slug Club</a> <a href="/wiki/Christmas" title="Christmas">Christmas</a> <a href="/wiki/Slug_Club_Christmas_party" title="Slug Club Christmas party">party</a> in <a href="/wiki/1996" title="1996">1996</a> along with <a href="/wiki/Neville_Longbottom" title="Neville Longbottom">Neville Longbottom</a> and <a href="/wiki/Waiter_serving_Dragon_Tartare" title="Waiter serving Dragon Tartare">another waiter</a>. He was seen passing by when <a href="/wiki/Horace_Slughorn" title="Horace Slughorn">Horace Slughorn</a> and <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> were having their picture taken. He offered <a href="/wiki/Dragon_tartare" title="Dragon tartare">dragon tartare</a> to <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> and <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione Granger</a>. Both passed, and the waiter told them this choice was for the better, as the <i>hors d'oeuvre</i> gave one <i>"terribly bad breath."</i> However, upon hearing this, Hermione took the entire plate, hoping to repel the unwelcome attentions of her date, <a href="/wiki/Cormac_McLaggen" title="Cormac McLaggen">Cormac McLaggen</a>. He was later seen decorating a plate of food.<sup id="cite_ref-HBPF_1-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBPF-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
