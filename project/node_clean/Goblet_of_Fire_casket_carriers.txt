This was a <b>group</b> of four <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizards</a> that worked for the <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">Ministry of Magic</a>. In <a href="/wiki/1994" title="1994">1994</a> they delivered the <a href="/wiki/Goblet_of_Fire" title="Goblet of Fire">Goblet of Fire</a> to <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> during the <a href="/wiki/Triwizard_Tournament" title="Triwizard Tournament">Triwizard Tournament</a>, and were overseen by <a href="/wiki/Argus_Filch" title="Argus Filch">Argus Filch</a>, <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Caretaker" title="Hogwarts Caretaker">Hogwarts Caretaker</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-GOFF_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOFF-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
