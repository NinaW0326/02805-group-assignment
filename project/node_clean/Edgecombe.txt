<b><a href="/wiki/Madam" title="Madam">Madam</a> Edgecombe</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Great_Britain" title="Great Britain">British</a> <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch</a> and the wife of <a href="/wiki/Marietta_Edgecombe%27s_father" title="Marietta Edgecombe&#39;s father">Mr Edgecombe</a> and the mother of <a href="/wiki/Marietta_Edgecombe" title="Marietta Edgecombe">Marietta</a>. She worked in the <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">British Ministry of Magic</a> as a <a href="/wiki/Floo_Network_Regulator" title="Floo Network Regulator">Floo Network Regulator</a> and assisted <a href="/wiki/Dolores_Umbridge" title="Dolores Umbridge">Dolores Umbridge</a> in monitoring the incoming and outgoing communications of the <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Castle" title="Hogwarts Castle">Hogwarts Castle</a> fireplaces in <a href="/wiki/1995" title="1995">1995</a>. She was a supporter of the then-Minister <a href="/wiki/Cornelius_Fudge" title="Cornelius Fudge">Cornelius Fudge</a>'s claim that <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a> was dead. Due to her career, she forbade her daughter from doing anything that may get on Umbridge's bad side.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP18_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP18-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
