This <b>robot</b> was an <a href="/wiki/Charm" title="Charm">enchanted</a> toy robot that could be found in the <a href="/wiki/Muggle_Studies_Classroom" title="Muggle Studies Classroom">Muggle Studies Classroom</a>, and was presumably owned by the then <a href="/wiki/Muggle_Studies" title="Muggle Studies">Muggle Studies</a> <a href="/wiki/Professor" title="Professor">Professor</a> <a href="/wiki/Charity_Burbage" title="Charity Burbage">Charity Burbage</a> for academic purposes.<sup id="cite_ref-POAG_1-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-POAG-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="History">History</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Robot_in_the_Muggle_Studies_Classroom?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: History">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Robot_in_the_Muggle_Studies_Classroom?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: History"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>During the <a href="/wiki/1993%E2%80%931994_school_year" title="1993–1994 school year">1993–1994 school year</a> at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts</a>, when a <a href="/wiki/Ghoul_in_the_Muggle_Studies_Classroom" title="Ghoul in the Muggle Studies Classroom">Ghoul</a> was attacking <a href="/wiki/Neville_Longbottom" title="Neville Longbottom">Neville Longbottom</a>&#160;in the Muggle Studies Classroom, it also attacked <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ron Weasley</a> who was&#160;trying to rescue Neville&#160;from the Ghoul with the help of <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>. When Ron encountered the robot,&#160;it attacked him by firing rockets from its chest, but Ron managed to destroy it by using the <a href="/wiki/Knockback_Jinx" title="Knockback Jinx">Knockback Jinx</a>. It is possible that the robot was used&#160;or studied as part of&#160;<a href="/wiki/Muggle_Studies" title="Muggle Studies">Muggle Studies</a> lessons.<sup id="cite_ref-POAG_1-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-POAG-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
