<b>Chester Davies</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> who began attending <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> in <a href="/wiki/1980" title="1980">1980</a><sup id="cite_ref-age_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-age-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-graduation_5-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-graduation-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> and was <a href="/wiki/Sorting_ceremony" title="Sorting ceremony">sorted</a> into <a href="/wiki/Ravenclaw" title="Ravenclaw">Ravenclaw</a>. He had been made a <a href="/wiki/Prefect" title="Prefect">Prefect</a> in <a href="/wiki/1984" title="1984">1984</a> and was responsible for watching over the <a href="/wiki/First_year" title="First year">first year</a> students in Ravenclaw.<sup id="cite_ref-HM13_6-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HM13-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup> Chester was still at Hogwarts the following two years and graduated in <a href="/wiki/1987" title="1987">1987</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-graduation_5-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-graduation-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Chester_Davies?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Chester_Davies?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Chester Davies was born between <a href="/wiki/1_September" title="1 September">1 September</a> <a href="/wiki/1968" title="1968">1968</a> and <a href="/wiki/31_August" title="31 August">31 August</a> <a href="/wiki/1969" title="1969">1969</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-age_1-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-age-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> He had a younger brother, <a href="/wiki/Roger_Davies" title="Roger Davies">Roger Davies</a>, born about nine years later.<sup id="cite_ref-brother_3-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-brother-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span id="Hogwarts_years_(1980-1987)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="Hogwarts_years_.281980-1987.29">Hogwarts years <span class="template-c">(1980-1987)</span></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Chester_Davies?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Hogwarts years (1980-1987)">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Chester_Davies?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Hogwarts years (1980-1987)"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>He began attending <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> in <a href="/wiki/September" title="September">September</a> <a href="/wiki/1980" title="1980">1980</a> and was placed in <a href="/wiki/Ravenclaw" title="Ravenclaw">Ravenclaw House</a> by the <a href="/wiki/Sorting_Hat" title="Sorting Hat">Sorting Hat</a>. He became a <a href="/wiki/Prefect" title="Prefect">Prefect</a> in <a href="/wiki/1984" title="1984">1984</a>.
</p><p>During the <a href="/wiki/1986%E2%80%931987_school_year" title="1986–1987 school year">1986–1987 school year</a>, Chester was caught off guard by a <a href="/wiki/Full_Body-Bind_Curse" title="Full Body-Bind Curse">Full Body-Bind Curse</a> when he was in front of the <a href="/wiki/Potions_Classroom" title="Potions Classroom">Potions Classroom</a>. It was unknown if he was aware that the <a href="/wiki/Curse" title="Curse">curse</a> was cast by <a href="/wiki/Jacob%27s_sibling" title="Jacob&#39;s sibling">Jacob's sibling</a> with the coercion of <a href="/wiki/Penny_Haywood" title="Penny Haywood">Penny Haywood</a>, or who had revived him afterwards.<sup id="cite_ref-HM3P_7-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HM3P-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup> Later in the school year, when <a href="/wiki/Rita_Skeeter" title="Rita Skeeter">a guest speaker</a> was to arrive at Hogwarts, Chester believed the person to be <a href="/wiki/Ambrosius_Flume" title="Ambrosius Flume">Ambrosius Flume</a>, the owner of <a href="/wiki/Honeydukes" title="Honeydukes">Honeydukes</a> Sweet Shoppe.<sup id="cite_ref-HM3W_8-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HM3W-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Chester graduated from Hogwarts in <a href="/wiki/1987" title="1987">1987</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-age_1-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-age-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span id="Later_life_(1987-)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="Later_life_.281987-.29">Later life <span class="template-c">(1987-)</span></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Chester_Davies?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Later life (1987-)">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Chester_Davies?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Later life (1987-)"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<figure class="thumb tleft show-info-icon" style="width: 182px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/9/9b/Chester_Davies_Ministry_employee.png/revision/latest?cb=20191102173003" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/9/9b/Chester_Davies_Ministry_employee.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/180?cb=20191102173003" decoding="async" width="180" height="188" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="Chester Davies Ministry employee.png" data-image-key="Chester_Davies_Ministry_employee.png" /></a> 	 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:Chester_Davies_Ministry_employee.png" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption">Chester Davies as a Ministry employee, during the <a href="/wiki/1989%E2%80%931990_school_year" title="1989–1990 school year">1989–1990 school year</a></p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure>
<p>By <a href="/wiki/1988" title="1988">1988</a>, Chester started working in the <a href="/wiki/Improper_Use_of_Magic_Office" title="Improper Use of Magic Office">Improper Use of Magic Office</a> at the <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">British Ministry of Magic</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-Y5SQ_9-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Y5SQ-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>In <a href="/wiki/September" title="September">September</a> 1988, he met up in the <a href="/wiki/Three_Broomsticks_Inn" title="Three Broomsticks Inn">Three Broomsticks Inn</a> with other former Prefects <a href="/wiki/Angelica_Cole" title="Angelica Cole">Angelica Cole</a>, <a href="/wiki/Jane_Court" title="Jane Court">Jane Court</a> and <a href="/wiki/Felix_Rosier" title="Felix Rosier">Felix Rosier</a>, as well as Prefects at the time <a href="/wiki/William_Weasley" title="William Weasley">William Weasley</a>, <a href="/wiki/Charles_Weasley" title="Charles Weasley">Charles Weasley</a> and Jacob's sibling.<sup id="cite_ref-Y5SQ_9-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Y5SQ-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>In the <a href="/wiki/1989%E2%80%931990_school_year" title="1989–1990 school year">1989–1990 school year</a>, he met up with <a href="/wiki/Jacob%27s_sibling" title="Jacob&#39;s sibling">Jacob's sibling</a> in <a href="/wiki/Flourish_and_Blotts" title="Flourish and Blotts">Flourish and Blotts</a>, where he gave information which helped the student get into the <a href="/wiki/Ministry_of_Magic" title="Ministry of Magic">Ministry of Magic</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-HM610_10-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HM610-10">&#91;10&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Magical_abilities_and_skills">Magical abilities and skills</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Chester_Davies?veaction=edit&amp;section=4" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Chester_Davies?action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li><b><a href="/wiki/Charm" title="Charm">Charms</a></b>: Chester was proficient with charm-work, as he could cast the <a href="/wiki/Freezing_Charm" title="Freezing Charm">Freezing Charm</a> successfully.</li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Potion" title="Potion">Potions</a></b>: Chester was capable of brewing <a href="/wiki/Babbling_Beverage" title="Babbling Beverage">Babbling Beverage</a>.</li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Herbology" title="Herbology">Herbology</a></b>: Chester was knowledgeable about magical plants.</li></ul>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Chester_Davies?veaction=edit&amp;section=5" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Chester_Davies?action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>In <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter:_Hogwarts_Mystery" title="Harry Potter: Hogwarts Mystery">Harry Potter: Hogwarts Mystery</a></i>, Chester's role is prominent to players in Ravenclaw, including telling the player to focus on their schoolwork to build up <a href="/wiki/House_points" title="House points">House points</a>, searching for the missing <a href="/wiki/Ben_Copper" title="Ben Copper">Ben Copper</a>, and encouraging and helping the player learn new spells. However, his existence and interaction are not limited to Ravenclaw players, for in several scenes in the <a href="/wiki/Great_Hall" title="Great Hall">Great Hall</a>, he provides comments upon socialising regardless of the player's House, most notably helping Jacob's sibling and Dennis become members of <a href="/wiki/Frog_Choir" title="Frog Choir">Frog Choir</a>.</li></ul>
