<p><b>Jacob Kowalski</b> (born c. <a href="/wiki/1900" title="1900">1900</a>) was a <a href="/wiki/Poland" title="Poland">Polish</a>-born<sup id="cite_ref-poland_2-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-poland-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/United_States_of_America" title="United States of America">American</a> <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">No-Maj</a>, who lived at <a href="/wiki/435_Rivington_Street" title="435 Rivington Street">435</a> <a href="/wiki/Rivington_Street" title="Rivington Street">Rivington Street</a><sup id="cite_ref-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> in <a href="/wiki/New_York" title="New York">New York</a> during the <a href="/wiki/1920s" title="1920s">1920s</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-entertainmentweekly1_6-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-entertainmentweekly1-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-pottermore-everything_7-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-pottermore-everything-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup> Jacob fought in the <a href="/wiki/First_World_War" title="First World War">First World War</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-pottermorevideo_8-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-pottermorevideo-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup> Returning home to the United States he found work in a <a href="/wiki/Moreton_Dale_Canning_Factory" title="Moreton Dale Canning Factory">cannery</a>, but dreamt of becoming a baker.<sup id="cite_ref-entertainmentweekly1_6-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-entertainmentweekly1-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-pottermore-everything_7-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-pottermore-everything-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-ewposters_4-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-ewposters-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> On <a href="/wiki/5_December" title="5 December">5 December</a> <a href="/wiki/1926" title="1926">1926</a>, he went to <a href="/wiki/Steen_National_Bank" title="Steen National Bank">Steen National Bank</a> to apply for a loan of $2300,<sup id="cite_ref-bankloan_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-bankloan-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> but was turned down. 
</p><p>While at the bank he met <a href="/wiki/Newton_Scamander" title="Newton Scamander">Newt Scamander</a>, a <a href="/wiki/Great_Britain" title="Great Britain">British</a> <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> who stopped in <a href="/wiki/New_York" title="New York">New York City</a> on his travels to find and document <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magical</a> <a href="/wiki/Beast" title="Beast">beasts</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-entertainmentweekly1_6-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-entertainmentweekly1-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-pottermore-everything_7-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-pottermore-everything-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-snitchseeker1_9-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-snitchseeker1-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-boston_10-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-boston-10">&#91;10&#93;</a></sup> Through Newt he was exposed to the magical world, but had his memories wiped as was required by wizarding law.
</p><p>Jacob would later go on to own a very successful bakery subconsciously inspired by his experiences with Newt Scamander's beasts and financed through silver from <a href="/wiki/Occamy_egg" title="Occamy egg">Occamy eggs</a> provided by Scamander.
</p><p>It was later discovered that the memory modification largely didn't take and Jacob ended up back together with Queenie. Though the two were happy together, Queenie disagreed with Jacob's stance on his not wanting her to take a chance on him by breaking the law in marrying him. She bewitched him to make him want to be engaged to her, but the truth was revealed to him when they paid a visit to Newt Scamander. Following an argument with Queenie, he left with Scamander on another adventure, ultimately reuniting with Queenie, who had fallen under the influence of the philosophy of Gellert Grindelwald. While Queenie felt that Grindelwald's aims would allow the two of them to be together, Jacob was repulsed by Grindelwald, and watched sadly as Queenie joined with the Dark Wizard, leaving him behind.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Jacob_Kowalski?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Jacob_Kowalski?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Family_and_early_life">Family and early life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Jacob_Kowalski?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Family and early life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Jacob_Kowalski?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Family and early life"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Jacob was born around the year <a href="/wiki/1900" title="1900">1900</a> in <a href="/wiki/Poland" title="Poland">Poland</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-bankloan_1-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-bankloan-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-poland_2-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-poland-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> He immigrated to the <a href="/wiki/United_States_of_America" title="United States of America">United States</a> when he was young.<sup id="cite_ref-poland_2-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-poland-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>He had at least <a href="/wiki/Jacob_Kowalski%27s_brother" title="Jacob Kowalski&#39;s brother">one brother</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-fb-brother_11-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-fb-brother-11">&#91;11&#93;</a></sup> He also had a <a href="/wiki/Jacob_Kowalski%27s_grandfather" title="Jacob Kowalski&#39;s grandfather">grandfather</a> who kept <a href="/wiki/Pigeon" title="Pigeon">pigeons</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-FB80_12-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-FB80-12">&#91;12&#93;</a></sup> and a <a href="/wiki/Jacob_Kowalski%27s_grandmother" title="Jacob Kowalski&#39;s grandmother">grandmother</a> who passed on her recipe for <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/P%C4%85czki" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Pączki">pączki</a> to him and instilled in him a love of baking.<sup id="cite_ref-13" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-13">&#91;13&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Military_service_and_post-war_life">Military service and post-war life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Jacob_Kowalski?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Military service and post-war life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Jacob_Kowalski?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Military service and post-war life"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Jacob fought in the <a href="/wiki/First_World_War" title="First World War">First World War</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-FB50_14-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-FB50-14">&#91;14&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-FB9_15-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-FB9-15">&#91;15&#93;</a></sup> He served in <a href="/wiki/France" title="France">France</a> as part of the American <a href="/wiki/Expeditionary_Forces" title="Expeditionary Forces">Expeditionary Forces</a>, and remained stationed there until well after the conclusion of the war (presumably as part of a peace-keeping effort), returning to the United States in <a href="/wiki/1924" title="1924">1924</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-FB9_15-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-FB9-15">&#91;15&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-cb-france_16-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-cb-france-16">&#91;16&#93;</a></sup> His brother also fought in the war, but was killed at some point.<sup id="cite_ref-fb-brother_11-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-fb-brother-11">&#91;11&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>The extended length of his military service left him able to find only a menial job at the <a href="/wiki/Moreton_Dale_Canning_Factory" title="Moreton Dale Canning Factory">Moreton Dale Canning Factory</a> in his post-war life.<sup id="cite_ref-bankloan_1-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-bankloan-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-FB50_14-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-FB50-14">&#91;14&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-FB9_15-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-FB9-15">&#91;15&#93;</a></sup> He thus applied for a loan on <a href="/wiki/5_December" title="5 December">5 December</a> <a href="/wiki/1926" title="1926">1926</a> in the hope of getting the money he needed to realise his dream of opening a bakery.<sup id="cite_ref-bankloan_1-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-bankloan-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-FB9_15-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-FB9-15">&#91;15&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>By late <a href="/wiki/1926" title="1926">1926</a>, he was engaged to <a href="/wiki/Mildred" title="Mildred">Mildred</a>, but the engagement ended.<sup id="cite_ref-casebook-mildred_17-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-casebook-mildred-17">&#91;17&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<p>While visiting the <a href="/wiki/Steen_National_Bank" title="Steen National Bank">Steen National Bank</a> to secure a loan to set up his own bakery, Jacob meets <a href="/wiki/Newton_Scamander" title="Newton Scamander">Newt Scamander</a> when he arrived to find the <a href="/wiki/Niffler" title="Niffler">Niffler</a> that had escaped <a href="/wiki/Newton_Scamander%27s_suitcase" title="Newton Scamander&#39;s suitcase">his suitcase</a>. As Newt left suddenly to chase his Niffler, Jacob noticed that he accidentally left his <a href="/wiki/Occamy_egg" title="Occamy egg">Occamy egg</a> behind. Before he could return it, Jacob was summoned to banker <a href="/wiki/Gilbert_Bingley" title="Gilbert Bingley">Gilbert Bingley</a>'s office. Jacob presented Bingley with his suitcase full of pastries, but Bingley questioned how Jacob would return the loan and pointed out that his proposed bakery would not produce the same revenue as a factory and thus rejected <a href="/wiki/Jacob_Kowalski%27s_loan_application" title="Jacob Kowalski&#39;s loan application">his application</a>.
</p>

<p>As he left the office, Jacob noticed that the egg was about to hatch and called out to Newt, who pulled the egg, and Jacob accidentally, with a <a href="/wiki/Summoning_Charm" title="Summoning Charm">Summoning Charm</a> and <a href="/wiki/Apparition" title="Apparition">Disapparated</a> them onto the stairwell near the bank vault. Just as Newt recaptured his Niffler, Bingley arrived at the vault and, seeing the pilfered deposit boxes, mistakenly assumed that Jacob was trying to rob the bank, and sounded the alarm. Newt quickly placed a <a href="/wiki/Full_Body-Bind_Curse" title="Full Body-Bind Curse">Full Body-Bind Curse</a> on Bingley and Disapparated himself and Jacob to the outside of the bank just as the guards arrived. Subsequently, Newt tried to <a href="/wiki/Memory_Charm" title="Memory Charm">Obliviate</a> Jacob, but the No-Maj unknowingly grabbed Newt's briefcase, hit him with it, and fled.
</p><p>When Jacob returned to <a href="/wiki/Jacob_Kowalski%27s_apartment" title="Jacob Kowalski&#39;s apartment">his apartment</a> without a bank loan, his fiance Mildred left him.<sup id="cite_ref-casebook-mildred_17-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-casebook-mildred-17">&#91;17&#93;</a></sup> Shortly afterwards, Jacob placed the case down on his bed only to hear something protesting inside. As Jacob approached it, the lid opened and several magical creatures escaped, including a <a href="/wiki/Murtlap" title="Murtlap">murtlap</a> that attacked and bit Jacob. Following escaped <a href="/wiki/Billywig" title="Billywig">billywigs</a>, Newt and <a href="/wiki/Porpentina_Goldstein" title="Porpentina Goldstein">Tina Goldstein</a> arrived at Jacob's apartment, finding it half destroyed and Jacob unwell due to a reaction to the Murtlap bite. Newt <a href="/wiki/Mending_Charm" title="Mending Charm">repaired</a> Jacob's apartment to its original state, and Tina agreed to take Jacob with them to <a href="/wiki/Goldstein_sisters%27_apartment" title="Goldstein sisters&#39; apartment">her place</a>.

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Physical_appearance">Physical appearance</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Jacob_Kowalski?veaction=edit&amp;section=10" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Physical appearance">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Jacob_Kowalski?action=edit&amp;section=10" title="Edit section: Physical appearance"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>When he was a man in his mid to late twenties, he was of average height for a man of his time and had a stocky build. He had dark brown curly hair that is usually kept brushed and a matching moustache. He had dark brown eyes.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Personality_and_traits">Personality and traits</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Jacob_Kowalski?veaction=edit&amp;section=11" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Personality and traits">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Jacob_Kowalski?action=edit&amp;section=11" title="Edit section: Personality and traits"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Jacob was extremely warm, welcoming, compassionate, and friendly, with <a href="/wiki/Newton_Scamander" title="Newton Scamander">Newt Scamander</a> noting that people tend to like Jacob, and with Jacob claiming that he wishes to become a baker to make people happy, despising having to work in a canning factory, due to that job being joyless. Hence, Jacob managed to swiftly befriend <a href="/wiki/Queenie_Goldstein" title="Queenie Goldstein">Queenie Goldstein</a>, the somewhat socially awkward Newt Scamander, the somewhat prim <a href="/wiki/Porpentina_Goldstein" title="Porpentina Goldstein">Tina Goldstein</a>, and later even the most famous alchemist in history, <a href="/wiki/Nicolas_Flamel" title="Nicolas Flamel">Nicolas Flamel</a>. 
</p><p>While initially disturbed by magic, Jacob, unlike most <a href="/wiki/Muggle" class="mw-redirect" title="Muggle">No-Majes</a>, quickly came to admire and love wizardry, and was excited to learn more about the <a href="/wiki/Wizarding_world" title="Wizarding world">Wizarding World</a> during his adventures with Newt, Tina, and Queenie. Jacob seemed to adjust to extreme situations relatively quickly, as he wasn't mentally disturbed after almost getting killed by an <a href="/wiki/Erumpent" title="Erumpent">Erumpent</a> or contending with an <a href="/wiki/Occamy" title="Occamy">Occamy</a>, and quickly overcame his fear of <a href="/wiki/Nicolas_Flamel" title="Nicolas Flamel">Nicolas Flamel</a>'s gaunt appearance, politely claiming that the ancient alchemist looked young for his age. 
</p><p>Jacob seemed to have poor self-esteem, viewing himself as unintelligent, and thinking that there "are loads [like him]," but Queenie assured Jacob that he is unique, before their first kiss. Jacob's unique ability to accept other people is a testament to this, as the likes of <a href="/wiki/Henry_Shaw_Junior" title="Henry Shaw Junior">Henry Shaw Jr</a> were openly hateful of those lesser than themselves, while Jacob was pleasant and friendly to his fellow men. Moreover, Jacob had a good sense of humour, remarking that <a href="/wiki/Gnarlak" title="Gnarlak">Gnarlak</a> reminded him of his foreman after <a href="/wiki/Muggle_duelling" title="Muggle duelling">punching</a> the goblin in the face. 
</p><p>Jacob was also very selfless, with a strong sense of morality, and an incorruptible spirit, shown when he willingly stepped out into the rain for Obliviation, when he refused to endanger his beloved Queenie by marrying her, and when he wasn't swayed by <a href="/wiki/Gellert_Grindelwald" title="Gellert Grindelwald">Gellert Grindelwald</a>'s visions of a future <a href="/wiki/Second_World_War" title="Second World War">Second World War</a> (despite being horrified by them, as a veteran of the <a href="/wiki/First_World_War" title="First World War">First World War</a>), and tearfully refused to join his beloved Queenie among Grindelwald's forces. The incredible bravery and fortitude demonstrated by Jacob in <a href="/wiki/Paris" title="Paris">Paris</a>, as well as the loss Jacob suffered, seemingly earned him the admiration and sympathy of <a href="/wiki/Theseus_Scamander" title="Theseus Scamander">Theseus Scamander</a> and Nicolas Flamel, as well as the honour of being the first known No-Maj in history to visit <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a>.  
</p><p>While he initially appeared timid and shy, Jacob is in fact, exceptionally brave, having served in the <a href="/wiki/First_World_War" title="First World War">First World War</a>, willingly braving many adventures at Newt Scamander's side, and even refusing to join <a href="/wiki/Gellert_Grindelwald" title="Gellert Grindelwald">Gellert Grindelwald</a>'s forces, despite the fearsome <a href="/wiki/Protego_Diabolica" title="Protego Diabolica">black fire</a> threat as the alternative. Indeed, had Jacob been a wizard and attended <a href="/wiki/Ilvermorny_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Ilvermorny School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Ilvermorny School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a>, he would have been sorted into the house <a href="/wiki/Wampus_(House)" title="Wampus (House)">Wampus</a><sup id="cite_ref-jacobhouse_19-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-jacobhouse-19">&#91;19&#93;</a></sup>.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Abilities_and_skills">Abilities and skills</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Jacob_Kowalski?veaction=edit&amp;section=12" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Abilities and skills">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Jacob_Kowalski?action=edit&amp;section=12" title="Edit section: Abilities and skills"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li><b>Baking skills:</b> Jacob was an extremely skilled baker, taking inspiration from his family recipes, and confidently claiming that his goods surpassed those of any doughnut machine. This was due to baking being his passion, as Jacob wished to make people happy with his baked goods. His <a href="/wiki/Kowalski_Quality_Baked_Goods" title="Kowalski Quality Baked Goods">Kowalski Quality Baked Goods</a> quickly became extremely popular, with him baking various pastries and confections in the forms of fantastic creatures (unconsciously modelling them after the beasts he encountered on his adventures with <a href="/wiki/Newton_Scamander" title="Newton Scamander">Newt Scamander</a>).</li>
<li><b>Social networking:</b> Due to his uniquely friendly nature, Jacob was loved by most people, and was able to charm and get along with almost anyone, with his friendly nature contributing greatly to the success of his bakery, with Jacob incredibly quickly adjusting to meeting real <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizards</a>, to <a href="/wiki/Queenie_Goldstein" title="Queenie Goldstein">Queenie Goldstein</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Legilimency" title="Legilimency">Legilimency</a>, to meeting the 600-year-old <a href="/wiki/Nicolas_Flamel" title="Nicolas Flamel">Nicolas Flamel</a>, and to meeting <a href="/wiki/Albus_Dumbledore" title="Albus Dumbledore">Albus Dumbledore</a> at the usually <a href="/wiki/Unplottability" title="Unplottability">Unplottable</a> school of <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts</a>. Indeed, it seemed that Jacob's friendly nature and acceptance of magic was what most contributed to Queenie falling in love with him, and claiming that Jacob is unique. Later on, even the usually uptight <a href="/wiki/Newton_Scamander" title="Newton Scamander">Newt Scamander</a> trusted Jacob enough to ask his friend for advice on how to optimally flirt with his love interest <a href="/wiki/Porpentina_Goldstein" title="Porpentina Goldstein">Tina Goldstein</a>. As such, Jacob is incredibly well-connected to the <a href="/wiki/Wizarding_world" title="Wizarding world">Wizarding World</a> for a <a href="/wiki/Muggle" class="mw-redirect" title="Muggle">No-Maj</a>, being the first known No-Maj to ever visit <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a>, the other two being the parents of <a href="/wiki/Myrtle_Warren" title="Myrtle Warren">Myrtle Warren</a> two decades later.</li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Love" title="Love">Love</a></b>: Jacob was a very warm and loving person, evident in his desire to bring people happiness with his baked goods. It was also evident in his love for his girlfriend <a href="/wiki/Queenie_Goldstein" title="Queenie Goldstein">Queenie Goldstein</a>, since he loved her enough to refuse legally endangering her with a marriage, and he swiftly forgave Queenie for enchanting him, understanding her reasoning. As a result, Jacob was incredibly devastated after Queenie joined with <a href="/wiki/Gellert_Grindelwald" title="Gellert Grindelwald">Gellert Grindelwald</a>, as his morals made him unable to follow her there.</li>
<li><b>Indomitable willpower</b>: Despite occasionally appearing timid and shy, Jacob actually had immense willpower, enough to brave difficult or dangerous situations, and overcome strong emotional attachments when necessary. Hence, Jacob served in the <a href="/wiki/First_World_War" title="First World War">First World War</a>, he agreed to help <a href="/wiki/Newton_Scamander" title="Newton Scamander">Newt</a> accomplish the extremely dangerous task of re-capturing an <a href="/wiki/Erumpent" title="Erumpent">Erumpent</a>, he was not dissuaded when warned by <a href="/wiki/Queenie_Goldstein" title="Queenie Goldstein">Queenie Goldstein</a> of how dangerous <a href="/wiki/Credence_Barebone" title="Credence Barebone">Credence</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Obscurus" title="Obscurus">Obscurus</a> was, he wiped his own memories in an act of selfless bravery, he refused to endanger Queenie by marrying her despite his love for the beautiful witch, and most notably, while Jacob was horrified by <a href="/wiki/Gellert_Grindelwald" title="Gellert Grindelwald">Gellert Grindelwald</a>'s visions of a future <a href="/wiki/Second_World_War" title="Second World War">Second World War</a>, and while he still loved Queenie, Jacob showed his incorruptible spirit and morals by resisting Grindelwald's persuasive speech and tearfully refusing to join his beloved girlfriend among Grindelwald's forces.</li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Care_of_Magical_Creatures" title="Care of Magical Creatures">Care of Magical Creatures</a></b>: Despite being a <a href="/wiki/Muggle" class="mw-redirect" title="Muggle">No-Maj</a>, Jacob did an exceptional job at helping <a href="/wiki/Newton_Scamander" title="Newton Scamander">Newt Scamander</a> look after the many creatures inside his case.</li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Muggle_duelling" title="Muggle duelling">Muggle duelling</a></b>: As a veteran of the Great War, there is no doubt that Jacob had been taught self-defense and physical combat in the army. He demonstrated his physical prowess when he knocked out the goblin gangster <a href="/wiki/Gnarlak" title="Gnarlak">Gnarlak</a> with a single punch, and when he kicked down the door to <a href="/wiki/Percival_Graves%27_office" title="Percival Graves&#39; office">Percival Graves' office</a>.</li></ul>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Jacob_Kowalski?veaction=edit&amp;section=13" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Jacob_Kowalski?action=edit&amp;section=13" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li><i>Jacob</i> is derived from Late Latin Jacobus/Iacobus, from Greek Ἰάκωβος (<i>Iakobos</i>), from Hebrew יַעֲקֹב (<i>Yaʿqob/Yaʿaqov/Yaʿăqōḇ</i>). In the Abrahamic monotheistic religions (Judaism, Christianity, and Islam), Jacob, (in <a href="/wiki/Christianity" title="Christianity">Christianity</a> considered one of the Patriarchs) son of Isaac and Rebekah, was the third to have held a covenant with <a href="/wiki/God" title="God">God</a>, and was later named Israel (later the progenitor of the Twelve Tribes). His original name meant seizing by the/holder of the heel or supplanting/supplanter, which ties in with his pre-covenant life: in birth as having held his elder twin brother Esau's heel, as having persuaded Esau to give him the birthright of the eldest son, and as having his old almost-blind father bless him under the disguise of Esau. Jacob is also a cognate/reduced English form of the name James, the name of some of Jesus's disciples.</li>
<li>Jacob's role in Newt's life (from inadvertently switching cases to being Newt's friend/assistant in helping him catch the former's escaped beasts) is reflected from the name's original Hebrew root עקב (<i>ʿqb</i>) meaning to follow/be behind", but also to supplant/circumvent/assail/overreach or from the word for heel, עֲקֵב (<i>ʿaqeb</i>).</li>
<li><i>Kowalski</i> is the 2nd most common surname in Poland (as well as in the USA), meaning "blacksmith's son" (root <i>kowal</i> blacksmith). Jan Kowalski is the Polish equivalent to John Smith.
<ul><li>Additionally, <i>Smith</i> is the most popular surname in America. This could be seen as symbolic of Jacob being the everyman, a character in literature and drama that is ordinary and that the audience is meant to easily identify with, and are often put into extraordinary circumstances.</li></ul></li>
<li>Even though Jacob is no blacksmith, as his patronymic surname means, his <a href="/wiki/Kowalski_Quality_Baked_Goods" title="Kowalski Quality Baked Goods">preferred business</a> is still connected with intense fire the fashioning of pastry products through the usage of fire using an oven over the forge</li>
<li>Contrary to the movie's pronunciation of ko-<b>W</b>al-ski as per the name, the proper pronunciation is ko-<b>V</b>al-ski. In <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Polish_language" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Polish language">Polish</a> w is pronounced as in the <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Voiced_labiodental_fricative" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Voiced labiodental fricative">English v sound</a> (the <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Voiced_labio-velar_approximant" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Voiced labio-velar approximant">English w sound</a> corresponding to the letter <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/%C5%81" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Ł">ł</a>). The butchering of the name's native pronunciation was common during the Great Migration period in the United States. For example, German names such as Van Ortman were shortened to simply, Ortman. This was a process known as Americanization.</li>
<li>Kowalski means, Son of Smith, this could be an implication that he is a descendant of <a href="/wiki/Hepzibah_Smith" title="Hepzibah Smith">Hepzibah Smith</a>. Though Kowalski and Smith are very common surnames.</li></ul>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Jacob_Kowalski?veaction=edit&amp;section=14" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Jacob_Kowalski?action=edit&amp;section=14" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<figure class="thumb tright show-info-icon" style="width: 196px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/2/28/LegoJkowalski.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20170730211251" class="image"><img alt="" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw%3D%3D" decoding="async" width="194" height="300" class="thumbimage lazyload" data-image-name="LegoJkowalski.jpg" data-image-key="LegoJkowalski.jpg" data-src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/2/28/LegoJkowalski.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/194?cb=20170730211251" /></a> 	<noscript><a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/2/28/LegoJkowalski.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20170730211251" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/2/28/LegoJkowalski.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/194?cb=20170730211251" decoding="async" width="194" height="300" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="LegoJkowalski.jpg" data-image-key="LegoJkowalski.jpg" data-src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/2/28/LegoJkowalski.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/194?cb=20170730211251" /></a></noscript> 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:LegoJkowalski.jpg" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption">Jacob Kowalski as a <a href="/wiki/LEGO" title="LEGO">LEGO</a> character in <i><a href="/wiki/LEGO_Dimensions" title="LEGO Dimensions">LEGO Dimensions</a></i></p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure>
<ul><li>Jacob Kowalski is portrayed by <a href="/wiki/Dan_Fogler" title="Dan Fogler">Dan Fogler</a> in the <i><a href="/wiki/Fantastic_Beasts_(film_series)" title="Fantastic Beasts (film series)">Fantastic Beasts and Where to Find Them</a></i> film series.<sup id="cite_ref-boston_10-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-boston-10">&#91;10&#93;</a></sup> In an interview, Fogler stated his character speaks with a historical <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/New_York_City_English" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:New York City English">Brooklyn accent</a>, similar to that of early 20th-century comedian <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Lou_Costello" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Lou Costello">Lou Costello</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-20" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-20">&#91;20&#93;</a></sup> He has likened the relationship between Jacob and Newt as being akin to that of Holmes and Watson, or Don Quixote and Sancho Panza.<sup id="cite_ref-21" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-21">&#91;21&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li>Dan Fogler was around 14 years older than his character when he first played Jacob in Fantastic Beasts and where to find them.</li>
<li>Since Jacob is identified as having been born around 1900 in <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Partitions_of_Poland" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Partitions of Poland">(then-partitioned) Poland</a> (Poles being the largest Slavic ethnic group in the USA, immigration dating back to the Colonial era <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Roanoke_Colony" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Roanoke Colony">predating</a> the <a href="/wiki/Plymouth_settlement" title="Plymouth settlement">Pilgrims</a>), Jacob and/or his family must have immigrated&#160;in the latter&#160;years of&#160;1870-1914 (the largest immigration wave, which most present <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Polish_Americans" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Polish Americans">Polish Americans</a> are descended from), and as Poland never existed as an independent sovereign state (from 1795-<a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Second_Polish_Republic" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Second Polish Republic">1918</a>), the <a target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener" class="external text" href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Immigration_and_Naturalization_Service">U.S. Immigration and Naturalisation Service</a> classified Poles (including Jacob and/or family) into either <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Russian_Empire" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Russian Empire">Russian</a>, <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/German_Empire" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:German Empire">German</a>, or <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Austria-Hungary" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Austria-Hungary">Austrian</a>, depending on which partitioned territory the immigrant came from.</li>
<li>Jacob's connection with bread can be related to the 1870-1914 immigration wave, as immigrant Poles from this time period were called <i>za chlebem</i> (for bread) immigrants, because most were peasants in Poland who did not own land and lacked basic subsistence.</li>
<li>As Jacob mentioned participating in the <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/American_Expeditionary_Forces" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:American Expeditionary Forces">Expeditionary Forces (1917-1919) in World War I</a>, he would have been drafted through the amended <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Selective_Service_Act_of_1917" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Selective Service Act of 1917">Selective Service Act of 1917</a>. Under the original Act enacted May 28 1917, all men aged between 21-31 were required to register for military service, and after Congress amended the Act in August 1918, the age range was widened to 18-45. Due to his birth date around 1900 most probably before August, Jacob must've been drafted after the 1918 amendment the 3rd registration held on September 12 1918, as he would've been ineligible underage under the original 1917 Act.</li>
<li>It is worth noting that World War I motivated Polish Americans such as Jacob to vigorously contribute and/or support the War, with large numbers volunteering for or being drafted into the United States Army, working in war-related industries, and buying war bonds a common theme was to fight for America, defeat the Germans (who were one of the participants in the <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Partitions_of_Poland" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Partitions of Poland">partitions of Poland</a>, and for the restoration of Poland as a unified, independent nation.</li>
<li>His late return indicates that he would likely have been a member of the U.S. Third Army, which was created by General Pershing at the end of 1918 to perform occupation duties. Third Army was created from the Third and Fourth Army Corps which both took part in major combat operations at the end of the War from September to November of 1918. Despite his apparent desire to minimise his war experiences, it is most likely that Jacob was a combat veteran, having taken part in either the Battle of Saint-Mihiel or the bloody Meuse-Argonne offensive.</li>
<li>Although Jacob was born in Poland and emigrated to America, by the time he was in his mid-twenties he spoke with a heavy Brooklyn accent. As such, he likely immigrated when he was young child.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Quentin_Kowalski" title="Quentin Kowalski">Quentin Kowalski</a> may be a descendant of Jacob and <a href="/wiki/Queenie_Goldstein" title="Queenie Goldstein">Queenie Goldstein</a>, though this would breach <a href="/wiki/Rappaport%27s_Law" title="Rappaport&#39;s Law">Rappaport's Law</a>. Queenie and Jacob would need to have left the <a href="/wiki/United_States_of_America" title="United States of America">United States of America</a> to marry as to protect Queenie from prosecution - which may have happened when Tina and Newt married. It is possible they returned in 1965 when Rappaport's Law was repealed, or that their children or grandchildren travelled to the United States of their own accord.</li>
<li>If Jacob had been a wizard and attended Ilvermorny, he would have been Sorted into <a href="/wiki/Wampus_(House)" title="Wampus (House)">Wampus</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-jacobhouse_19-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-jacobhouse-19">&#91;19&#93;</a></sup></li></ul>
