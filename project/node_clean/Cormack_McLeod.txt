<p><b>Cormack McLeod</b> was the manager of the <a href="/wiki/Quidditch" title="Quidditch">Quidditch</a> <a href="/wiki/Quidditch_team" title="Quidditch team">team</a>, the <a href="/wiki/Montrose_Magpies" title="Montrose Magpies">Montrose Magpies</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-wizardsm_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-wizardsm-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> He fired one of the team's <a href="/wiki/Chaser" title="Chaser">Chasers</a>, <a href="/wiki/Alasdair_Maddock" title="Alasdair Maddock">Alasdair Maddock</a>, prior to a match against the <a href="/wiki/Caerphilly_Catapults" title="Caerphilly Catapults">Caerphilly Catapults</a> because he had been caught playing a <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggle</a> sport involving the use of "<i>peculiar metal sticks</i>" to knock "<i>non-flying balls into holes in the ground.</i>"<sup id="cite_ref-wizardsm_2-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-wizardsm-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-dp_4-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-dp-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> Maddock had previously upset Quidditch matches by trying out football and basketball tactics.<sup id="cite_ref-wizardsm_2-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-wizardsm-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
