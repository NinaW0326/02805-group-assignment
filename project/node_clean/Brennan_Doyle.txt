<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Brennan_Doyle?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Brennan_Doyle?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Brennan Doyle was born between <a href="/wiki/1_September" title="1 September">1 September</a> <a href="/wiki/1971" title="1971">1971</a> and <a href="/wiki/31_August" title="31 August">31 August</a> <a href="/wiki/1972" title="1972">1972</a>. 
In <a href="/wiki/1983" title="1983">1983</a>, he began attending <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> and was <a href="/wiki/Sorting_ceremony" title="Sorting ceremony">sorted</a> into <a href="/wiki/Ravenclaw" title="Ravenclaw">Ravenclaw</a>. 
</p><p>In the <a href="/wiki/1984%E2%80%931985_school_year" title="1984–1985 school year">1984–1985 school year</a>, Brennan joined the <a href="/wiki/Ravenclaw_Quidditch_team" title="Ravenclaw Quidditch team">Ravenclaw Quidditch team</a> as a <a href="/wiki/Keeper" title="Keeper">Keeper</a>, but got down to the substitute roster in the <a href="/wiki/1985%E2%80%931986_school_year" title="1985–1986 school year">1985–1986 school year</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-HM6_2-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HM6-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>In the <a href="/wiki/1989%E2%80%931990_school_year" title="1989–1990 school year">1989–1990 school year</a>, he attacked <a href="/wiki/Andre_Egwu" title="Andre Egwu">Andre Egwu</a> with the <a href="/wiki/Conjunctivitis_Curse" title="Conjunctivitis Curse">Conjunctivitis Curse</a> during Quidditch training, wanting to play on the team instead of Andre. This lead to a series of events which resulted in Brennan to be kicked out of the Ravenclaw Quidditch team for his foul play.<sup id="cite_ref-HM6_2-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HM6-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Magical_abilities_and_skills">Magical abilities and skills</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Brennan_Doyle?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Brennan_Doyle?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li><b><a href="/wiki/Flight" title="Flight">Flying</a></b>: Doyle was clearly an accomplished and talented <a href="/wiki/Broomstick" title="Broomstick">broomstick</a> flyer and <a href="/wiki/Quidditch" title="Quidditch">Quidditch</a> player, given how he played as <a href="/wiki/Keeper" title="Keeper">Keeper</a> for the <a href="/wiki/Ravenclaw_Quidditch_team" title="Ravenclaw Quidditch team">Ravenclaw Quidditch team</a>, which must demonstrate he had a high level of skill and experience in the <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magical</a> sport.<sup id="cite_ref-HM6_2-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HM6-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Dark_Arts" title="Dark Arts">Dark Arts</a></b>: Doyle had some skill with the casting of <a href="/wiki/Dark_charm" title="Dark charm">Dark charms</a>, given how he was able to successfully cast the <a href="/wiki/Conjunctivitis_Curse" title="Conjunctivitis Curse">Conjunctivitis Curse</a> on <a href="/wiki/Andre_Egwu" title="Andre Egwu">Andre Egwu</a> in <a href="/wiki/1989" title="1989">1989</a>.</li></ul>
