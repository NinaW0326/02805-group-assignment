<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Swoopstikes?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Swoopstikes?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Professor Swoopstikes became the <a href="/wiki/Potions_Master" title="Potions Master">Potions Master</a> at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> at an unknown point in the school's history. He was also an <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/entomologist" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:entomologist">entomologist</a>, with a vast knowledge of <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magical</a> insects. Professor Swoopstikes was also a fan of the <a href="/wiki/Tutshill_Tornados" title="Tutshill Tornados">Tutshill Tornados</a> Quidditch team.<sup id="cite_ref-YT_3-11" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-YT-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>A portrait of Professor Swoopstikes was hung in the <a href="/wiki/Tracery_Hall" title="Tracery Hall">Tracery Hall</a> at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Castle" title="Hogwarts Castle">Hogwarts Castle</a>, depicting him among his vast insect collection, holding a magnifying glass on one hand and a <a href="/wiki/Billywig" title="Billywig">Billywig</a> on the other.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Magical_abilities_and_skills">Magical abilities and skills</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Swoopstikes?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Swoopstikes?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li><b><a href="/wiki/Potion" title="Potion">Potions</a></b>: As a former <a href="/wiki/Potions_Master" title="Potions Master">Potions Master</a> who taught the <a href="/wiki/Potions_(class)" title="Potions (class)">subject</a>  at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a>, Swoopstikes would have been an expert in the <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magical</a> art and <a href="/wiki/Science" title="Science">science</a> of potion-making.</li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Magizoology" title="Magizoology">Magizoology</a></b>: Swoopstikes was also a <a href="/wiki/Magizoologist" title="Magizoologist">Magizoologist</a>, with an expansive knowledge of <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magical</a> insects, meaning he was highly knowledgeable of the study of <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magical</a> <a href="/wiki/Creature" title="Creature">creatures</a> and their habits.</li></ul>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Swoopstikes?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Swoopstikes?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>To "<i>swoop</i>" is to "<i>move rapidly downward through the air</i>", perhaps an indication towards <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/entomology" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:entomology">entomology</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-etymology2_4-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-etymology2-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li>A "<i>stike</i>" is a stanza, and therefore the plural form of stike, "<i>stikes</i>", refers to multiple stanzas.<sup id="cite_ref-etymology1_5-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-etymology1-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup></li></ul>
