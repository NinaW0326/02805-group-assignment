<b>Abraham Grimblehawk</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Great_Britain" title="Great Britain">British</a> <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> who worked for the <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">Ministry of Magic</a>. He was the uncle of <a href="/wiki/Mathilda_Grimblehawk" title="Mathilda Grimblehawk">Mathilda Grimblehawk</a>.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Abraham_Grimblehawk?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Abraham_Grimblehawk?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Abraham attended <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> and was placed in <a href="/wiki/Hufflepuff" title="Hufflepuff">Hufflepuff House</a>. He most likely studied <a href="/wiki/Herbology" title="Herbology">Herbology</a> and <a href="/wiki/Care_of_Magical_Creatures" title="Care of Magical Creatures">Care of Magical Creatures</a> at Hogwarts.<sup id="cite_ref-FBC_2-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-FBC-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Abraham worked in the <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">British Ministry of Magic</a>, but it is unknown in which department. It is most likely that he worked in the <a href="/wiki/Department_for_the_Regulation_and_Control_of_Magical_Creatures" title="Department for the Regulation and Control of Magical Creatures">Department for the Regulation and Control of Magical Creatures</a>, as he had extensive knowledge on the subject of <a href="/wiki/Magizoology" title="Magizoology">Magizoology</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-FBC_2-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-FBC-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Abraham and Mathilda were very good friends of <a href="/wiki/Gethsemane_Prickle" title="Gethsemane Prickle">Gethsemane Prickle</a><sup id="cite_ref-FBC_2-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-FBC-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>, a <a href="/wiki/Herbologist" title="Herbologist">Herbologist</a> and <a href="/wiki/Potioneer" title="Potioneer">Potioneer</a> who worked alongside the Department for the Regulation and Control of Magical Creatures.
</p><p>Abraham would often read <a href="/wiki/Newton_Scamander" title="Newton Scamander">Newton Scamander</a>'s <i><a href="/wiki/Fantastic_Beasts_and_Where_to_Find_Them" title="Fantastic Beasts and Where to Find Them">Fantastic Beasts and Where to Find Them</a></i> to Mathilda when she was younger.<sup id="cite_ref-FBC_2-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-FBC-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Abraham retired to <a href="/wiki/St_Oswald%27s_Home_for_Old_Witches_and_Wizards" title="St Oswald&#39;s Home for Old Witches and Wizards">St Oswald's Home for Old Witches and Wizards</a> in his later years.<sup id="cite_ref-FBC_2-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-FBC-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
