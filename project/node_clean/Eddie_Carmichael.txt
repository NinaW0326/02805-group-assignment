<b>Eddie Carmichael</b> (born c. <a href="/wiki/1978" title="1978">1978</a>-<a href="/wiki/1979" title="1979">1979</a>) was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> who began attending <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> in <a href="/wiki/1990" title="1990">1990</a>. He was a member of <a href="/wiki/Ravenclaw" title="Ravenclaw">Ravenclaw</a> house and claimed that he achieved nine "<a href="/wiki/Outstanding" title="Outstanding">Outstandings</a>" in his <a href="/wiki/Ordinary_Wizarding_Level" title="Ordinary Wizarding Level">O.W.L</a> exams. He claimed his success was down to <a href="/wiki/Baruffio%27s_Brain_Elixir" title="Baruffio&#39;s Brain Elixir">Baruffio's Brain Elixir</a>, and attempted to sell it to younger students. <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione Granger</a> stopped him and confiscated the potion.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP31_2-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP31-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
