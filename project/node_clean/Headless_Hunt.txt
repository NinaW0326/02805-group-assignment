<p>The <b>Headless Hunt</b> is an organisation of decapitated <a href="/wiki/Ghost" title="Ghost">ghosts</a> that play headless games. The organisation is headed by <a href="/wiki/Patrick_Delaney-Podmore" title="Patrick Delaney-Podmore">Sir Patrick Delaney-Podmore</a>. The organisation only accepts those whose heads have been completely severed from their bodies.<sup id="cite_ref-COS8_2-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COS8-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Activities">Activities</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Headless_Hunt?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Activities">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Headless_Hunt?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Activities"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The Headless Hunt participate in head-intensive events such as <a href="/wiki/Horseback_Head-Juggling" title="Horseback Head-Juggling">Horseback Head-Juggling</a>, <a href="/wiki/Headless_Bowling" title="Headless Bowling">Headless Bowling</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Head_Polo" title="Head Polo">Head Polo</a>. The <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor" title="Gryffindor">Gryffindor</a> ghost, <a href="/wiki/Nicholas_de_Mimsy-Porpington" title="Nicholas de Mimsy-Porpington">Nearly Headless Nick</a> is annually denied admission to the Headless Hunt despite only a half inch of skin holding his head to his neck.<sup id="cite_ref-COS8_2-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COS8-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="History">History</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Headless_Hunt?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: History">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Headless_Hunt?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: History"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The Headless Hunt attended <a href="/wiki/Nicholas_de_Mimsy-Porpington" title="Nicholas de Mimsy-Porpington">Nicholas de Mimsy-Porpington</a>'s five-hundredth <a href="/wiki/Deathday_Party" title="Deathday Party">Deathday Party</a>. Sir Patrick Delaney-Podmore, the leader of the&#160;Headless Hunt managed to offend Sir Nicholas by pointing out that he was not properly decapitated, but the other guests found him quite amusing. This left Sir Nicholas humiliated and embarrassed for the remainder of his party.<sup id="cite_ref-COS8_2-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COS8-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>The Headless Hunt also made an appearance in <a href="/wiki/1998" title="1998">1998</a> when they flew by <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>, just as he escaped the <a href="/wiki/Room_of_Requirement" title="Room of Requirement">Room of Requirement</a> with <a href="/wiki/Rowena_Ravenclaw%27s_Diadem" class="mw-redirect" title="Rowena Ravenclaw&#39;s Diadem">Rowena Ravenclaw's Diadem</a> during the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a>. How they assisted is not known as ghosts have little physical influence on the solid world.<sup id="cite_ref-DH31_4-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH31-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
