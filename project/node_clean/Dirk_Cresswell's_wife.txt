<p><b>Mrs Cresswell</b> was the wife of <a href="/wiki/Dirk_Cresswell" title="Dirk Cresswell">Dirk Cresswell</a>. She and Dirk had at least <a href="/wiki/Cresswell_brothers" title="Cresswell brothers">two sons</a> together.<sup id="cite_ref-DH13_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH13-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>In <a href="/wiki/1997" title="1997">1997</a>, Dirk, as a <a href="/wiki/Muggle-born" title="Muggle-born">Muggle-born</a> wizard, arranged to have his family tree falsified to avoid persecution by the <a href="/wiki/Muggle-Born_Registration_Commission" title="Muggle-Born Registration Commission">Muggle-Born Registration Commission</a>. Unfortunately, <a href="/wiki/Albert_Runcorn" title="Albert Runcorn">Albert Runcorn</a> uncovered the plan, and Dirk was arrested. <a href="/wiki/Arthur_Weasley" title="Arthur Weasley">Arthur Weasley</a> confronted Runcorn (who, at the time, was actually <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> under <a href="/wiki/Polyjuice_Potion" title="Polyjuice Potion">Polyjuice Potion</a>), suggesting that, if Dirk made it out of <a href="/wiki/Azkaban" title="Azkaban">Azkaban</a> alive, Runcorn would have to answer to Dirk's wife and their sons.<sup id="cite_ref-DH13_2-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH13-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Dirk managed to escape before being imprisoned, but, after a few months on the run, he was caught and murdered by <a href="/wiki/Snatchers" title="Snatchers">Snatchers</a>, leaving Mrs Cresswell a widow.<sup id="cite_ref-DH22_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH22-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
