<b>Wolfgang Amadeus Mozart</b> (<a href="/wiki/27_January" title="27 January">27 January</a>, <a href="/wiki/1756" class="mw-redirect" title="1756">1756</a> â€” <a href="/wiki/5_December" title="5 December">5 December</a>, <a href="/wiki/1791" class="mw-redirect" title="1791">1791</a>) was a prolific and influential Austrian composer of the Classical era.
</p><p>On Sunday, <a href="/wiki/7_May" title="7 May">7 May</a>, in a year prior to <a href="/wiki/1997" title="1997">1997</a>, <a href="/wiki/Emanuel_Hurwitz" title="Emanuel Hurwitz">Emanuel Hurwitz</a> and his wife, <a href="/wiki/Kay_Hurwitz" title="Kay Hurwitz">Kay</a>, a <a href="/wiki/Violin" title="Violin">violinist</a> and <a href="/wiki/Viola" title="Viola">violist</a> respectively, gave a performance of Mozart's <i><a href="/wiki/Mozart%27s_Sinfonia_Concertanti" class="mw-redirect" title="Mozart&#39;s Sinfonia Concertanti">Sinfonia Concertanti</a></i> at <a href="/wiki/St._Mary%27s_Church_Hall" title="St. Mary&#39;s Church Hall">St. Mary's Church Hall</a>. The couple were accompanied by the <a href="/wiki/Dollis_Chamber_Ensemble" title="Dollis Chamber Ensemble">Dollis Chamber Ensemble</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-dhp1_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-dhp1-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione Granger</a> had a poster for this concert stuck up on the bulletin board in <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger%27s_bedroom" title="Hermione Granger&#39;s bedroom">her bedroom</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Granger_family" title="Granger family">Granger family</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Grangers%27_house" class="mw-redirect" title="Grangers&#39; house">house</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-dhp1_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-dhp1-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
