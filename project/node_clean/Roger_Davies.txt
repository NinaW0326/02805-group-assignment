<p><b>Roger Davies</b> (c. <a href="/wiki/1977" title="1977">1977</a>/<a href="/wiki/1978" title="1978">1978</a>) was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> who was a <a href="/wiki/Ravenclaw" title="Ravenclaw">Ravenclaw</a> student at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> from <a href="/wiki/1989" title="1989">1989</a>-<a href="/wiki/1996" title="1996">1996</a>. He was the <a href="/wiki/Quidditch_Captain#Hogwarts_Quidditch_Captains" title="Quidditch Captain">Captain</a> of the <a href="/wiki/Ravenclaw_Quidditch_team" title="Ravenclaw Quidditch team">Ravenclaw Quidditch team</a> and played as a <a href="/wiki/Chaser" title="Chaser">Chaser</a>.
</p>

<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_life">Early life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Roger_Davies?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Early life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Roger_Davies?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Early life"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Roger was born between <a href="/wiki/1_September" title="1 September">1 September</a> <a href="/wiki/1977" title="1977">1977</a> and <a href="/wiki/31_August" title="31 August">31 August</a> <a href="/wiki/1978" title="1978">1978</a><sup id="cite_ref-age_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-age-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> in <a href="/wiki/Great_Britain" title="Great Britain">Great Britain</a> or <a href="/wiki/Ireland" title="Ireland">Ireland</a>. He had an older brother named <a href="/wiki/Chester_Davies" title="Chester Davies">Chester Davies</a> who was also in Ravenclaw.<sup id="cite_ref-brother_4-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-brother-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_years">Early years</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Roger_Davies?veaction=edit&amp;section=4" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Early years">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Roger_Davies?action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Early years"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<p>He began attending Hogwarts on <a href="/wiki/1_September" title="1 September">1 September</a> <a href="/wiki/1989" title="1989">1989</a><sup id="cite_ref-age_1-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-age-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> and was <a href="/wiki/Sorting_ceremony" title="Sorting ceremony">sorted</a> into <a href="/wiki/Ravenclaw" title="Ravenclaw">Ravenclaw</a> house. He joined the <a href="/wiki/Ravenclaw_Quidditch_team" title="Ravenclaw Quidditch team">Ravenclaw Quidditch team</a> as a <a href="/wiki/Chaser" title="Chaser">Chaser</a> and eventually became their Captain.<sup id="cite_ref-POA13_3-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-POA13-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<p>In <a href="/wiki/1994" title="1994">1994</a>, Roger was <a href="/wiki/Fleur_Delacour" title="Fleur Delacour">Fleur Delacour</a>'s date at the <a href="/wiki/Yule_Ball" title="Yule Ball">Yule Ball</a>. He was very taken with the <a href="/wiki/Half-Veela" title="Half-Veela">part</a>-<a href="/wiki/Veela" title="Veela">Veela</a> witch's beauty and charm. He was so mesmerised, in fact, that he was looking at her the whole time and did not notice what she was actually saying, as well as repeatedly missing his own mouth with his fork while trying to eat. After dancing for some time at the ball, Roger and Fleur left for the gardens, sneaking into the rose bushes for a romantic engagement.<sup id="cite_ref-GOF22_5-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF22-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>

<p>During <a href="/wiki/1994" title="1994">1994</a>, Davies attended the <a href="/wiki/Yule_Ball" title="Yule Ball">Yule Ball</a> as Fleur's partner. He was apparently too smitten by her <a href="/wiki/Part-Veela" class="mw-redirect" title="Part-Veela">part-Veela</a> <a href="/wiki/Veela_Magic" title="Veela Magic">magic</a> to actually take note of anything she said, most of which was criticism of Hogwarts, while constantly missing his own mouth with the fork during dinner. The two apparently enjoyed their time together, as they had a romantic night within the bushes during the later parts of the Ball. However, they did not keep in contact after the <a href="/wiki/Triwizard_Tournament" title="Triwizard Tournament">Triwizard Tournament</a> ended, as Fleur married <a href="/wiki/William_Weasley" title="William Weasley">Bill Weasley</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-GOF22_5-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF22-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<p>Sometime after <a href="/wiki/Cedric_Diggory" title="Cedric Diggory">Cedric Diggory</a>'s death, Roger asked Cho out, to which she refused, being interested in <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> at the time. Cho would later use this event to gauge Harry's feelings for her based on the jealousy he would produce, though he was only confused. Cho also watched Roger and his girlfriend holding hands and kissing over their <a href="/wiki/Sugar" title="Sugar">sugar</a> bowl in <a href="/wiki/Madam_Puddifoot%27s_Tea_Shop" title="Madam Puddifoot&#39;s Tea Shop">Madam Puddifoot's Tea Shop</a>, as though they were setting a standard that she expected Harry to live up to. Roger and his girlfriend stopped kissing to look at Harry in astonishment when Cho departed dramatically after an argument.<sup id="cite_ref-OTP25_6-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OTP25-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Roger_Davies?veaction=edit&amp;section=13" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Roger_Davies?action=edit&amp;section=13" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Means "famous spear" from the Germanic elements <i>hrod</i> "fame" and <i>ger</i> "spear". The Normans brought this name to England, where it replaced the Old English cognate Hroðgar (the name of the Danish king in the Anglo-Saxon epic 'Beowulf'). It was a common name in England during the Middle Ages.
</p>
