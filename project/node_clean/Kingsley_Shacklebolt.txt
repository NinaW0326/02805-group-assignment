<p><b>Minister Kingsley Shacklebolt</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Great_Britain" title="Great Britain">British</a> <a href="/wiki/Pure-blood" title="Pure-blood">pure-blood</a> <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a><sup id="cite_ref-HPLE_2-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HPLE-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> in the employment of the <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">Ministry of Magic</a>. He worked for the Ministry as an <a href="/wiki/Auror" title="Auror">Auror</a>, but joined the reconstituted <a href="/wiki/Order_of_the_Phoenix" title="Order of the Phoenix">Order of the Phoenix</a> in <a href="/wiki/1995" title="1995">1995</a> after being convinced of the return of <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a>. Working undercover within the Ministry, he fed the Order with information, and misdirected the Ministry's efforts to locate <a href="/wiki/Sirius_Black" title="Sirius Black">Sirius Black</a> by saying he was spotted in <a href="/wiki/Tibet" title="Tibet">Tibet</a>.  
</p><p>In <a href="/wiki/1996" title="1996">1996</a>, he fought in the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_the_Department_of_Mysteries" title="Battle of the Department of Mysteries">Battle of the Department of Mysteries</a>, and was later assigned to guard the <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggle</a> <a href="/wiki/Prime_Minister" title="Prime Minister">Prime Minister</a>, posing as his secretary. Forced into running away following the fall of the Ministry to Lord Voldemort in <a href="/wiki/1997" title="1997">1997</a>, Kingsley continued to oppose the new regime. In <a href="/wiki/1998" title="1998">1998</a>, he fought in the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a>, and was named Acting <a href="/wiki/Minister_for_Magic" title="Minister for Magic">Minister for Magic</a> in the aftermath. 
</p><p>When he was eventually officially confirmed in the position of Minister for Magic, he, along with <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>, <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ron Weasley</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione Granger</a>, worked hard to change the Ministry. They eventually succeeded in greatly reducing the corruption and pure-blood bias within the organisation. By <a href="/wiki/2019" title="2019">2019</a>, Kingsley was succeeded as Minister for Magic by Hermione.
</p>

<h3><span id="Early_life_(pre_1970s-1995)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_life_.28pre_1970s-1995.29">Early life <span class="template-c">(pre 1970s-1995)</span></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Kingsley_Shacklebolt?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Early life (pre 1970s-1995)">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Kingsley_Shacklebolt?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Early life (pre 1970s-1995)"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Not much has been revealed about Kingsley's life before becoming an Auror. All that is known is that he was born into a <a href="/wiki/Pure-blood" title="Pure-blood">pure-blood</a> wizarding family at a point in 1973 or earlier<sup id="cite_ref-Auror_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Auror-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> and that he attended <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> in his youth, although it is unknown into which <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Houses" title="Hogwarts Houses">House</a> he was sorted.<sup id="cite_ref-HPLE_2-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HPLE-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> He also apparently met with <a href="/wiki/James_Potter_I" title="James Potter I">James Potter</a>, as he saw the physical similarities between him and his son Harry.
</p>

<p>Kingsley worked as a high-ranking <a href="/wiki/Auror" title="Auror">Auror</a> in the <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">Ministry of Magic</a>, and was assigned to lead the task force for the recapture of the <a href="/wiki/Azkaban" title="Azkaban">Azkaban</a>-escapee <a href="/wiki/Sirius_Black" title="Sirius Black">Sirius Black</a>. When rumour of <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort's</a> return was denied by the Ministry, Kingsley was convinced of his return, and subsequently joined the <a href="/wiki/Order_of_the_Phoenix" title="Order of the Phoenix">Order of the Phoenix</a>. Along with <a href="/wiki/Nymphadora_Tonks" title="Nymphadora Tonks">Nymphadora Tonks</a>, he worked as a covert operative, keeping the Order supplied with information. In addition, since joining the reactivated Order, Kingsley was informed of Black's innocence as a fellow member of the Order, and so he sidetracked the investigation by feeding the Ministry disinformation that indicated Black was hiding in Tibet.<sup id="cite_ref-OP_5-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OP-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> In the August of 1995, after <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> was attacked by two dementors Kingsley volunteered to be a member of the Advance Guard. The Advance Guard were to escort Harry to the Order's Headquarters at <a href="/wiki/12_Grimmauld_Place" title="12 Grimmauld Place">12 Grimmauld Place</a> in London.

<p>With Dumbledore's death, and the <a href="/wiki/Second_Wizarding_War" title="Second Wizarding War">Second Wizarding War</a> in full swing, the Order's attention turned to protecting <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>. With the protection he had at the <a href="/wiki/4_Privet_Drive" title="4 Privet Drive">4 Privet Drive</a> due to expire on his seventeenth birthday, a plan was formed to break the protection early and escort him to safety at the Burrow. Although still assigned to protect the Muggle <a href="/wiki/Prime_Minister" title="Prime Minister">Prime Minister</a>, Kingsley was chosen to speak with the <a href="/wiki/Dursley_family" title="Dursley family">Dursleys</a>, as his ability to act and dress like a <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggle</a> would offend them less, and he informed them that they too would have to go into hiding for their protection.<sup id="cite_ref-DH_4-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>On <a href="/wiki/27_July" title="27 July">27 July</a>, Kingsley was part of the Order task force sent to escort Harry to safety. Acting as a protector, he rode a <a href="/wiki/Thestral" title="Thestral">Thestral</a> with <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione Granger</a> disguised as Harry by way of the <a href="/wiki/Polyjuice_Potion" title="Polyjuice Potion">Polyjuice Potion</a>. During the subsequent battle, Kingsley and Hermione were chased by five <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a>, injuring two and possibly killing one. They were also briefly chased by <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a> himself after he murdered <a href="/wiki/Alastor_Moody" title="Alastor Moody">Alastor Moody</a>, but he broke off the pursuit once the real Harry was located. His house was one of a dozen safe houses used to confuse the Death Eaters, so that they would be less likely to figure out the real destination to which Harry was being spirited away.<sup id="cite_ref-DH_4-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<p>Kingsley and Hermione finally made it back to the Burrow, and Kingsley believed that their plan had been betrayed, challenging <a href="/wiki/Remus_Lupin" title="Remus Lupin">Remus Lupin</a> with a personal question to ensure his identity. Satisfied, he relayed his news — including the fact that Lord Voldemort could fly unaided — to the rest of the <a href="/wiki/Order_of_the_Phoenix" title="Order of the Phoenix">Order</a> that had returned, and waited anxiously for the others. Eventually, though, Kingsley was forced to leave the Burrow and return to his duty guarding the Prime Minister.<sup id="cite_ref-DH_4-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> He was unable to help Remus and Bill search for Mad-Eye's body, as he needed to report back to the <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">Ministry</a> straight away.

<p>On 1 August, 1997, the <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">Ministry of Magic</a> fell to <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a> and his Death Eaters. He went into hiding, but remained active in the resistance effort, contributing to the <i><a href="/wiki/Potterwatch" title="Potterwatch">Potterwatch</a></i> radio broadcast under the alias of Royal, urging Wizards to help protect <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggle</a> neighbours and to keep faith during dark times.<sup id="cite_ref-DH_4-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>

<h3><span id="Later_life_(post_1998)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="Later_life_.28post_1998.29">Later life <span class="template-c">(post 1998)</span></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Kingsley_Shacklebolt?veaction=edit&amp;section=10" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Later life (post 1998)">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Kingsley_Shacklebolt?action=edit&amp;section=10" title="Edit section: Later life (post 1998)"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Kingsley was eventually made permanent Minister for Magic, and set about ridding the Ministry of corruption and discrimination. He banned the use of <a href="/wiki/Dementor" title="Dementor">Dementors</a> to guard <a href="/wiki/Azkaban" title="Azkaban">Azkaban</a>, and made sure they would not be used to torment the opponents of the Ministry. He appointed Harry Potter as the head of the <a href="/wiki/Auror" title="Auror">Auror</a> Department, and made <a href="/wiki/Percy_Weasley" title="Percy Weasley">Percy Weasley</a> a high-ranking official,<sup id="cite_ref-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup> reshuffling the Ministry thoroughly and becoming instrumental in the repairs of the wizarding world following Voldemort's reign of terror. He managed to revolutionise the way that the ministry worked, and with the assistance of <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione Granger</a>, eradicated the pro-pureblood laws. 
</p><p>Kingsley was still Minister in 2018, as Mathilda Grimblehawk wrote a letter to him.<sup id="cite_ref-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>When a sudden outbreak of a piece of chaotic magic cut through all of time, space and memory in <a href="/wiki/2019" title="2019">2019</a> and began whisking away artefacts, creatures, people, and even memories to mysteriously appear in the Muggle world, Kingsley, who was still Minister for Magic at the time, entered a partnership with the <a href="/wiki/International_Confederation_of_Wizards" title="International Confederation of Wizards">International Confederation of Wizards</a> and worked with said organisation to establish an <a href="/wiki/International_Task_Force" title="International Task Force">International Task Force</a> whose job it would be to counteract the surges of magical activity.<sup id="cite_ref-WU_9-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-WU-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup> He also set up a new <a href="/wiki/Calamity_Management_Team" title="Calamity Management Team">office</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Department_of_Magical_Accidents_and_Catastrophes" title="Department of Magical Accidents and Catastrophes">Department of Magical Accidents and Catastrophes</a>, whom he charged with the task of monitoring the <a href="/wiki/Calamity" title="Calamity">Calamity</a> and report any new surges to the <a href="/wiki/Statute_of_Secrecy_Task_Force" title="Statute of Secrecy Task Force">Statute of Secrecy Task Force</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-WU_9-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-WU-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup> Kingsley was also credited with modifying <a href="/wiki/Dark_Detector" title="Dark Detector">Dark Detectors</a> to help the Calamity Investigators in tracking <a href="/wiki/Aura" title="Aura">traces</a> of the <a href="/wiki/Calamity" title="Calamity">Calamity</a>. He also helped instruct members of the <a href="/wiki/Statute_of_Secrecy_Task_Force" title="Statute of Secrecy Task Force">Statute of Secrecy Task Force</a> who pursued an Auror career on focusing their mind, to help improve their skills in addressing said event.<sup id="cite_ref-10" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-10">&#91;10&#93;</a></sup> 
</p>

<p>Kingsley was a tall black wizard and was described as broad shouldered. He was bald, and wore a single gold hoop earring. He had a slow, deep voice that was said to be reassuring.<sup id="cite_ref-OP_5-12" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OP-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> In contrast with many other wizards, he was capable of dressing properly as a Muggle, to the point that the Muggle Prime Minister could not tell the difference.<sup id="cite_ref-HP_6-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HP-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Personality_and_traits">Personality and traits</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Kingsley_Shacklebolt?veaction=edit&amp;section=12" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Personality and traits">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Kingsley_Shacklebolt?action=edit&amp;section=12" title="Edit section: Personality and traits"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<div class="quote">
<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows"><b>Lee Jordan</b>: "<i>And what would you say, <a href="/wiki/Potterwatch" title="Potterwatch">Royal</a>, to those listeners who reply that in <a href="/wiki/Second_Wizarding_War" title="Second Wizarding War">these dangerous times</a>, it should be 'Wizards first'?</i>"</span></dd>
<dd><b>Kingsley Shacklebolt</b>: "<i>I'd say that it's one short step from 'Wizards first' to '<a href="/wiki/Pure-blood" title="Pure-blood">pure-bloods</a> first,' and then to '<a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a>'. <dd>&#8212; Kingsley Shacklebolt's belief in the equality of <a href="/wiki/Human" title="Human">humanity</a><sup class="noprint"><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Deathly_Hallows" title="Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows">[src]</a></sup></dd></dl>
</div>
<p>Kingsley was calm and controlled, with a deep reassuring voice and commanding stature. Unlike many wizards, Kingsley mastered the knack of dressing and acting like a <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggle</a>, leading to his assignment to guard the Muggle <a href="/wiki/Prime_Minister" title="Prime Minister">Prime Minister</a> during the <a href="/wiki/Second_Wizarding_War" title="Second Wizarding War">Second Wizarding War</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-DH_4-11" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-HP_6-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HP-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup> Kingsley was known to be level-headed, though his calm demeanour belied his incredible power and quiet fury, as seen after the Battle of the Seven Potters, he displayed his rarely witnessed temper when interrogating Remus Lupin in order to make sure it was him. This impressive example of his control over his very justifiable suspicions and anger were likely exacerbated by Mad Eye Moody's death at Voldemort's hands. This also showed Kingsley's respect of the legendary Auror.<sup id="cite_ref-DH_4-12" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Kingsley was also a good judge of character, and trusted individuals regardless of age, on the basis of his knowledge of their character and abilities. He trusted <a href="/wiki/Fred_Weasley" title="Fred Weasley">Fred</a> and <a href="/wiki/George_Weasley" title="George Weasley">George Weasley</a> with the defence of the secret passages during the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-DH_4-13" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> and allowed <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> into the <a href="/wiki/Auror" title="Auror">Auror</a> Department at the age of seventeen.<sup id="cite_ref-11" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-11">&#91;11&#93;</a></sup> When the corrupt Ministry was deeming Harry and Dumbledore as crackpots for claiming Voldemort's return, Kingsley instead sided with the Order of the Phoenix as he could see the sincerity in Harry's and Dumbledore's statements, and even believed that Sirius was innocent, to the point of misleading the hunting squad to a faraway location.<sup id="cite_ref-OP_5-13" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OP-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>The opposite of the <a href="/wiki/Pure-blood_supremacy" title="Pure-blood supremacy">pure-blood advocates</a>, Kingsley believed in equality and fairness for all wizards and <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggles</a>. He was also brave enough to call Voldemort by his name, though this resulted in him getting discovered by a group of Death Eaters.<sup id="cite_ref-DH_4-14" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Although a faithful and devoted individual, Kingsley was not a blindly loyal man, as he acted on his own judgement instead of waiting for orders (contrary to his colleague <a href="/wiki/John_Dawlish" title="John Dawlish">John Dawlish</a>), and was not afraid to speak his mind. He joined the Order of the Phoenix despite the Ministry's oppression, swiftly acting to modify <a href="/wiki/Marietta_Edgecombe" title="Marietta Edgecombe">Marietta Edgecombe</a>'s memories in secret simply by looking at the situation at hand, and stood forward to warn <a href="/wiki/Dolores_Umbridge" title="Dolores Umbridge">Dolores Umbridge</a> not to manhandle students. At the same time, he was skilled in maintaining a facade to the corrupt Ministry to hide his own agenda from them, such as giving the appearance of devoting himself to hunt down Sirius, or speaking rather condescendingly with <a href="/wiki/Arthur_Weasley" title="Arthur Weasley">Arthur Weasley</a> to appear they have no dubious allegations, an act that Arthur returned (though the two would quietly exchange friendly comments in between).<sup id="cite_ref-OP_5-14" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OP-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Magical_abilities_and_skills">Magical abilities and skills</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Kingsley_Shacklebolt?veaction=edit&amp;section=13" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Kingsley_Shacklebolt?action=edit&amp;section=13" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>As a high ranking <a href="/wiki/Auror" title="Auror">Auror</a> and eventual leader of the <a href="/wiki/Order_of_the_Phoenix" title="Order of the Phoenix">Order of the Phoenix</a>, Kingsley was a very powerful wizard with a well balanced set of abilities. His exceptional skills were looked highly upon by his fellow Order members, including fellow Auror <a href="/wiki/Nymphadora_Tonks" title="Nymphadora Tonks">Nymphadora Tonks</a>. Even <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a> himself seemed to regard Kingsley as a powerful wizard.
</p>
<ul><li><b><a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">Magical</a> mastery</b>: Kingsley Shacklebolt was an exceptionally powerful and formidable wizard, and, as a <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts</a> student, he excelled in his studies enough to meet the qualifications for <a href="/wiki/Auror_training" title="Auror training">Auror training</a> at the <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">British Ministry of Magic</a>, becoming an illustrious <a href="/wiki/Auror" title="Auror">Auror</a>, trusted with some of the most important missions. Kingsley was skilled enough to temporarily engage <a href="/wiki/Bellatrix_Lestrange" title="Bellatrix Lestrange">Bellatrix Lestrange</a> and survive, despite being defeated, and later even showed himself to be powerful enough to aid <a href="/wiki/Minerva_McGonagall" title="Minerva McGonagall">Minerva McGonagall</a> and <a href="/wiki/Horace_Slughorn" title="Horace Slughorn">Horace Slughorn</a> in duelling against <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a> himself, surviving the duel. It must be noted, however, that Kingsley and his two allies were swiftly defeated when Voldemort's rage had amplified the Dark Lord's magic.</li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Auror" title="Auror">Auror</a> skills</b>: Kingsley Shacklebolt was an exceptionally skilled Auror, more than likely completing the <a href="/wiki/Concealment_and_Disguise" title="Concealment and Disguise">Concealment and Disguise</a>, as well as the <a href="/wiki/Stealth_and_Tracking" title="Stealth and Tracking">Stealth and Tracking</a> portions of <a href="/wiki/Auror_training" title="Auror training">Auror training</a> with excellence. Indeed, <a href="/wiki/Nymphadora_Tonks" title="Nymphadora Tonks">Nymphadora Tonks</a> stated that Kingsley was a high-ranking <a href="/wiki/Auror" title="Auror">Auror</a>, and her claim was fully backed up by Kingsley's natural cunning, outstanding duelling ability, and unique talent in defensive magic. Kingsley was well-respected by his colleagues and superiors, since he was trusted by the <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">British Ministry of Magic</a> and the <a href="/wiki/Order_of_the_Phoenix" title="Order of the Phoenix">Order of the Phoenix</a> with many important missions, such as acting as the Muggle <a href="/wiki/Prime_Minister" title="Prime Minister">Prime Minister</a>'s undercover bodyguard (without said Prime Minister ever suspecting Kingsley being a wizard), leading the wizardhunt for <a href="/wiki/Sirius_Black" title="Sirius Black">Sirius Black</a> (secretly diverting the Ministry's efforts from capturing Sirius), assisting <a href="/wiki/Cornelius_Fudge" title="Cornelius Fudge">Minister for Magic Fudge</a> in the attempt to arrest <a href="/wiki/Albus_Dumbledore" title="Albus Dumbledore">Albus Dumbledore</a> (while secretly on Dumbledore's side), escorting <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> with the <a href="/wiki/Advance_Guard" title="Advance Guard">Advance Guard</a>, protecting a disguised <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione Granger</a> during the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_the_Seven_Potters" title="Battle of the Seven Potters">Battle of the Seven Potters</a>, and leading the Order members during the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a>. Kingsley also successfully pretended to be loyal to the corrupt administration of Cornelius Fudge during the <a href="/wiki/Campaign_to_discredit_Albus_Dumbledore_and_Harry_Potter" title="Campaign to discredit Albus Dumbledore and Harry Potter">Campaign to discredit Albus Dumbledore and Harry Potter</a>. Finally, Kingsley deduced the <a href="/wiki/Fall_of_the_Ministry_of_Magic" title="Fall of the Ministry of Magic">Fall of the Ministry of Magic</a> quickly enough to warn all of the attendees of <a href="/wiki/Wedding_of_William_Weasley_and_Fleur_Delacour" title="Wedding of William Weasley and Fleur Delacour">Bill and Fleur's wedding</a>, giving Harry, Ron, and Hermione enough time to escape.</li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Duelling" title="Duelling">Duelling</a></b>: Kingsley was an outstandingly skilled duellist, being able to defeat two <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a> at once, and to duel against the highly skilled <a href="/wiki/Augustus_Rookwood" title="Augustus Rookwood">Augustus Rookwood</a>. During the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_the_Seven_Potters" title="Battle of the Seven Potters">Battle of the Seven Potters</a>, Kingsley was chased by five <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a>, and even though he was on a <a href="/wiki/Thestral" title="Thestral">Thestral</a> and had to protect <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione Granger</a> at the same time, he stunned two of them (one of them being <a href="/wiki/Travers" title="Travers">Travers</a>), and possibly killed another. He was also able to hold off Lord Voldemort himself during the same battle, though Voldemort quickly lost interest in Kingsley and Hermione when the real Harry was found. Later, when cornered by several Death Eaters after triggering the <a href="/wiki/Taboo" title="Taboo">Taboo</a>, Kingsley single-handedly fought them off. While Kingsley was unable to defeat <a href="/wiki/Bellatrix_Lestrange" title="Bellatrix Lestrange">Bellatrix Lestrange</a> during the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_the_Department_of_Mysteries" title="Battle of the Department of Mysteries">Battle of the Department of Mysteries</a>, he did hold his ground for a time and survived, albeit injured. During the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a>, he was seen engaging a masked Death Eater in a fierce duel in which he most likely won due to his unscathed appearance later. During the second part of the same battle Kingsley engaged Lord Voldemort a second time in a prolonged duel, alongside two other masterful duellists: <a href="/wiki/Minerva_McGonagall" title="Minerva McGonagall">Minerva McGonagall</a> and <a href="/wiki/Horace_Slughorn" title="Horace Slughorn">Horace Slughorn</a>, and survived. Indeed, Kingsley and his two allies were only defeated when Voldemort's magic was briefly amplified by the rage he felt over Belatrix's death.<sup id="cite_ref-DH_4-15" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Defence_Against_the_Dark_Arts" title="Defence Against the Dark Arts">Defence Against the Dark Arts</a></b>: As an Auror, Kingsley received at least an <a href="/wiki/Exceeds_Expectations" title="Exceeds Expectations">Exceeds Expectations</a> in his <a href="/wiki/Ordinary_Wizarding_Level" title="Ordinary Wizarding Level">O.W.L.</a> and <a href="/wiki/Nastily_Exhausting_Wizarding_Test" title="Nastily Exhausting Wizarding Test">N.E.W.T.</a> for this subject,<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP29_12-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP29-12">&#91;12&#93;</a></sup> making him highly accomplished in defending against the <a href="/wiki/Dark_Arts" title="Dark Arts">Dark Arts</a>. He demonstrated his exceptional defensive abilities as both an Auror and a member of the Order of the Phoenix, with him being very accomplished in fighting the Dark Arts and <a href="/wiki/Dark_Wizard" title="Dark Wizard">Dark Wizards</a>.</li></ul>

<ul><li><b><a href="/wiki/Charm" title="Charm">Charms</a></b>: Kinglsey was highly capable in charms, since as an Auror, he received at least an Exceeds Expectations in his O.W.L. and N.E.W.T. for this <a href="/wiki/Charms_(class)" title="Charms (class)">subject</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP29_12-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP29-12">&#91;12&#93;</a></sup> given that he cast a powerful spell on a <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eater</a>, causing the latter fly backwards out of a window that had just been broken, but resealed itself. Kingsley was also very proficient with the <a href="/wiki/Memory_Charm" title="Memory Charm">Memory Charm</a>, shown when he altered <a href="/wiki/Marietta_Edgecombe" title="Marietta Edgecombe">Marietta Edgecombe</a>'s memory to prevent her from telling <a href="/wiki/Cornelius_Fudge" title="Cornelius Fudge">Cornelius Fudge</a>, <a href="/wiki/John_Dawlish" title="John Dawlish">John Dawlish</a> and <a href="/wiki/Dolores_Umbridge" title="Dolores Umbridge">Dolores Umbridge</a> that <a href="/wiki/Dumbledore%27s_Army" title="Dumbledore&#39;s Army">Dumbledore's Army</a> had been meeting in secret for several months. Moreover, Kingsley was able to conjure the extremely complex talking corporeal <a href="/wiki/Patronus_Charm" title="Patronus Charm">Patronus Charm</a> - a method of communication invented by <a href="/wiki/Albus_Dumbledore" title="Albus Dumbledore">Albus Dumbledore</a>, which took the form of a <a href="/wiki/Lynx" title="Lynx">lynx</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-DH_4-16" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> He was also the one who came up with the idea of re-purposing <a href="/wiki/Dark_Detector" title="Dark Detector">Dark Detectors</a> to assist the <a href="/wiki/Statute_of_Secrecy_Task_Force" title="Statute of Secrecy Task Force">Statute of Secrecy Task Force</a> by modifying and attuning them to find hidden <a href="/wiki/Aura" title="Aura">Traces</a> created by the <a href="/wiki/Calamity" title="Calamity">Calamity</a>; a feat he pulled off in only a couple of hours.<sup id="cite_ref-WU_9-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-WU-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Transfiguration" title="Transfiguration">Transfiguration</a></b>: As an Auror, Kingsley was skilled in Transfiguration, as he had to have received at least an Exceeds Expectations in his O.W.L. and N.E.W.T. for this subject.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP29_12-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP29-12">&#91;12&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Potion" title="Potion">Potions</a></b>: As an Auror, Kingsley was skilled in Potions, as he had to have received at least an Exceeds Expectations in his O.W.L. and N.E.W.T. for this subject.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP29_12-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP29-12">&#91;12&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Herbology" title="Herbology">Herbology</a></b>: As an Auror, Kingsley was skilled in Herbology, as he had to have received at least an Exceeds Expectations in his O.W.L. and N.E.W.T. for this subject.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP29_12-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP29-12">&#91;12&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Muggle_Studies" title="Muggle Studies">Muggle Studies</a></b>: Kingsley was extremely skilled in this subject, evident from his expertise in flawlessly blending in with <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggles</a>, to the point that the <a href="/wiki/Dursley_family" title="Dursley family">Dursley family</a> treated Kingsley better than most wizards, and with even the Muggle <a href="/wiki/Prime_Minister" title="Prime Minister">Prime Minister</a> being unaware of Kingsley being a wizard until <a href="/wiki/Cornelius_Fudge" title="Cornelius Fudge">Cornelius Fudge</a> revealed it.</li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Apparition" title="Apparition">Apparition</a></b>: Kingsley, like most accomplished adult wizards, was capable of apparating at will.<sup id="cite_ref-DH_4-17" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup></li></ul>

<ul><li><b><a href="/wiki/Flight" title="Flight">Flying</a></b>: Kingsley was a highly accomplished <a href="/wiki/Broomstick" title="Broomstick">broomstick</a> flyer, successfully escorting <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> to <a href="/wiki/12_Grimmauld_Place" title="12 Grimmauld Place">12 Grimmauld Place</a> on a broom alongside his other <a href="/wiki/Order_of_the_Phoenix" title="Order of the Phoenix">Order of the Phoenix</a> comrades.<sup id="cite_ref-OP_5-15" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OP-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Nonverbal_spell" title="Nonverbal spell">Nonverbal magic</a></b>: Kingsley was highly proficient in nonverbal spells, since in the battles of the <a href="/wiki/Department_of_Mysteries" title="Department of Mysteries">Department of Mysteries</a> and <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts</a>, Kingsley duelled many opponents, including <a href="/wiki/Bellatrix_Lestrange" title="Bellatrix Lestrange">Bellatrix Lestrange</a> and <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a> himself, without saying a word. Given the high level of difficulty required in the casting of <a href="/wiki/Spell" title="Spell">spells</a> non-verbally, this is further testament to his formidable magical skills as a wizard.</li>
<li><b>Leadership skills</b>: Kingsley was a highly effective leader, evident from his status as a very prominent <a href="/wiki/Auror" title="Auror">Auror</a> in the <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">British Ministry of Magic</a>, leading the Ministry wizard hunt for <a href="/wiki/Sirius_Black" title="Sirius Black">Sirius Black</a> astray (convincingly claiming that Sirius was hiding in <a href="/wiki/Tibet" title="Tibet">Tibet</a>), being the one to lead the <a href="/wiki/Order_of_the_Phoenix" title="Order of the Phoenix">Order of the Phoenix</a> following the deaths of <a href="/wiki/Albus_Dumbledore" title="Albus Dumbledore">Albus Dumbledore</a> and <a href="/wiki/Alastor_Moody" title="Alastor Moody">Alastor Moody</a>, and being an effective <a href="/wiki/Minister_for_Magic" title="Minister for Magic">Minister for Magic</a> following the <a href="/wiki/Second_Wizarding_War" title="Second Wizarding War">Second Wizarding War</a>, helping purge the Ministry of corruption, thereby making the British wizarding community a better, more tolerant and happier place for all wizards and witches.</li></ul>

<p>Kingsley had a good relationship with all the members of the Order of the Phoenix and their allies, including <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>, <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione Granger</a>, the <a href="/wiki/Weasley_family" title="Weasley family">Weasley family</a>, <a href="/wiki/Alastor_Moody" title="Alastor Moody">Alastor Moody</a>, <a href="/wiki/Nymphadora_Tonks" title="Nymphadora Tonks">Nymphadora Tonks</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Albus_Dumbledore" title="Albus Dumbledore">Albus Dumbledore</a>. Because of his beliefs, Kingsley was highly regarded by most members.
</p><p>He also seemed to be a friend of <a href="/wiki/Remus_Lupin" title="Remus Lupin">Remus Lupin</a> and <a href="/wiki/Sirius_Black" title="Sirius Black">Sirius Black</a>. After Sirius was killed by <a href="/wiki/Bellatrix_Lestrange" title="Bellatrix Lestrange">Bellatrix Lestrange</a>, Kingsley fought against Bellatrix, although he was defeated. He also appeared to be on good terms with <a href="/wiki/Elphias_Doge" title="Elphias Doge">Elphias Doge</a>, <a href="/wiki/Dedalus_Diggle" title="Dedalus Diggle">Dedalus Diggle</a>, <a href="/wiki/Emmeline_Vance" title="Emmeline Vance">Emmeline Vance</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Sturgis_Podmore" title="Sturgis Podmore">Sturgis Podmore</a>, as they were all members of the <a href="/wiki/Advance_Guard" title="Advance Guard">Advance Guard</a> in <a href="/wiki/1995" title="1995">1995</a>. He also supposedly knew <a href="/wiki/James_Potter_I" title="James Potter I">James Potter</a> personally, as he mentioned that his son (Harry) looked like James.
</p><p>Several members of the Order were killed during battles. It is unknown how Kingsley reacted when he learned about their deaths. After the <a href="/wiki/Second_Wizarding_War" title="Second Wizarding War">Second Wizarding War</a>, Kingsley and the remaining members of the Order likely remained in contact.
</p><p>As with the other members of the Order, he originally trusted <a href="/wiki/Severus_Snape" title="Severus Snape">Severus Snape</a>, who was a <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eater</a> turned to a double-agent on the Order's behalf. When Snape killed Dumbledore, under Dumbledore's secret request to die peacefully and not let <a href="/wiki/Draco_Malfoy" title="Draco Malfoy">Draco Malfoy</a> damage his <a href="/wiki/Soul" title="Soul">soul</a> by being the murderer, Kingsley lost his respect and trust in Snape. After Snape's true allegiance was revealed, this was most likely restored.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Kingsley_Shacklebolt?veaction=edit&amp;section=18" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Kingsley_Shacklebolt?action=edit&amp;section=18" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Both "shackle" and "bolt" refer to means of imprisonment. Specifically, a <i>shacklebolt</i> is the threaded pin of a shackle allowing the linking of multiple chains and metal cables. This also hints at the reason his patronus is a <i>lynx</i> (links).
</p><p>His first name, "Kingsley" suggests royalty, note the "King" in it. This may be the reason that in <i>Potterwatch</i> he was known as "Royal".
</p>

<ul><li>Kingsley was portrayed by <a href="/wiki/George_Harris" title="George Harris">George Harris</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Order_of_the_Phoenix_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix (film)">film adaptation</a> of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Order_of_the_Phoenix_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix (film)">Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix</a></i> and the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Deathly_Hallows_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows (film)">film adaptations</a> of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Deathly_Hallows" title="Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows">Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows</a></i>.</li>
<li>It is never mentioned if Kingsley was a member of the first Order of the Phoenix or not, as he is not in the original Order photograph. However, in <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Order_of_the_Phoenix" title="Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix">Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix</a></i>, he calls <a href="/wiki/James_Potter_I" title="James Potter I">James Potter</a> by his first name and compares Harry to him, indicating he knew him personally. This may indicate he was a member of the first Order, but this has never been officially stated. Since his age has not been declared officially, another possibility is that he may have also attended Hogwarts at the same time as James and his friends.</li>
<li>Kingsley also shows a good sense of humour in <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Order_of_the_Phoenix_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix (film)">Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix</a></i>. He is given <a href="/wiki/Phineas_Nigellus_Black" title="Phineas Nigellus Black">Phineas Nigellus Black</a>'s line concerning Dumbledore and how he has got style shortly after the Headmaster's escape from the <a href="/wiki/Minister_for_Magic" title="Minister for Magic">Minister for Magic</a> and several <a href="/wiki/Auror" title="Auror">Aurors</a>.</li>
<li>It is unknown which House Kingsley was <a href="/wiki/Sorting_ceremony" title="Sorting ceremony">Sorted</a> into during his time at Hogwarts, even to ministry records.</li>
<li>While not explicitly mentioned, it's possible that after <a href="/wiki/Dolores_Umbridge" title="Dolores Umbridge">Dolores Umbridge</a> was imprisoned in <a href="/wiki/Azkaban" title="Azkaban">Azkaban</a>, Kingsley repealed her <a href="/wiki/Anti-werewolf_legislation" title="Anti-werewolf legislation">anti-werewolf legislation</a>, given his friendship with the late <a href="/wiki/Remus_Lupin" title="Remus Lupin">Remus Lupin</a>.</li>
<li>Kingsley is the inspiration for the New York-based Wizard Rock band, Kingsley and the Shacklebolts.</li>
<li>Kingsley's role of becoming the next Minister for Magic is omitted from <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Deathly_Hallows:_Part_2" title="Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 2">Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 2</a></i>, but it can be assumed that he did become the next Minister for Magic.</li>
<li>From all the members of the <a href="/wiki/Order_of_the_Phoenix" title="Order of the Phoenix">Order of the Phoenix</a> who aided <a href="/wiki/Dumbledore%27s_Army" title="Dumbledore&#39;s Army">Dumbledore's Army</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_the_Department_of_Mysteries" title="Battle of the Department of Mysteries">Battle of the Department of Mysteries</a>, Kingsley is the only one to survive the <a href="/wiki/Second_Wizarding_War" title="Second Wizarding War">Second Wizarding War</a>.</li>
<li>Kingsley may have been of Nigerian descent, as he wore Nigerian robes in the films, described in <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter:_The_Character_Vault" title="Harry Potter: The Character Vault">Harry Potter: The Character Vault</a></i>.</li></ul>
