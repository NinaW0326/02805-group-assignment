<p><b><a target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener" class="external text" href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Count_Dracula">Count Dracula</a></b> is a fictional character created by <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggle</a> author <a href="/wiki/Bram_Stoker" title="Bram Stoker">Bram Stoker</a>, who appears as the title character and main antagonist in his 1897 Gothic horror novel, <i><a target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener" class="external text" href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Dracula">Dracula</a></i>. Dracula, a <a href="/wiki/Vampire" title="Vampire">vampire</a>, was, inspired by <a href="/wiki/Vlad_Drakul" title="Vlad Drakul">Vlad Drakul</a>, father of <a href="/wiki/Vlad_the_Impaler" title="Vlad the Impaler">Vlad the Impaler</a>.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Count_Dracula?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Count_Dracula?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>In an early version of the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Prisoner_of_Azkaban_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban (film)">film adaptation</a> of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Prisoner_of_Azkaban" title="Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban">Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban</a></i>, <a href="/wiki/Parvati_Patil" title="Parvati Patil">Parvati Patil</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Boggart" title="Boggart">boggart</a> would have taken the form of Dracula, who would have been forced into the clothing of <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Carmen_Miranda" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Carmen Miranda">Carmen Miranda</a> when <a href="/wiki/Boggart-Banishing_Spell" title="Boggart-Banishing Spell">Riddikulus</a> was used.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> The role was even cast, with <a class="new" title="Yana Yanezic (page does not exist)" rel="nofollow" data-uncrawlable-url="L3dpa2kvWWFuYV9ZYW5lemljP2FjdGlvbj1lZGl0JnJlZGxpbms9MQ==">Yana Yanezic</a> set to play the part, though it is unknown if it was ever filmed.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Sirius_Black" title="Sirius Black">Sirius Black</a> actor <a href="/wiki/Gary_Oldman" title="Gary Oldman">Gary Oldman</a> played Dracula in the <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bram_Stoker%27s_Dracula" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Bram Stoker&#39;s Dracula">1992 film</a> directed by <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Francis_Ford_Coppola" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Francis Ford Coppola">Francis Ford Coppola</a>.</li></ul>
