<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Emeric_Switch?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Emeric_Switch?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Switch was the author of <i><a href="/wiki/A_Beginner%27s_Guide_to_Transfiguration" title="A Beginner&#39;s Guide to Transfiguration">A Beginner's Guide to Transfiguration</a></i>.<sup id="cite_ref-PS5_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PS5-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-COS4_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COS4-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> This book was a required textbook for <a href="/wiki/First_year" title="First year">first-year</a> and <a href="/wiki/Second_year" title="Second year">second-year</a> <a href="/wiki/Transfiguration_(class)" title="Transfiguration (class)">Transfiguration</a> classes at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-PS5_2-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PS5-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-COS4_3-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COS4-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> He also contributed an article on the scientific aspects of <a href="/wiki/Human_Transfiguration" title="Human Transfiguration">Human Transfiguration</a> to <i><a href="/wiki/Transfiguration_Today" title="Transfiguration Today">Transfiguration Today</a></i> in <a href="/wiki/1926" title="1926">1926</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-fb-mag_4-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-fb-mag-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>A <a href="/wiki/Portrait" title="Portrait">portrait</a> of him hung at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Castle" title="Hogwarts Castle">Hogwarts Castle</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-Exhibition_1-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Exhibition-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Magical_abilities_and_skills">Magical abilities and skills</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Emeric_Switch?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Emeric_Switch?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li><b><a href="/wiki/Transfiguration" title="Transfiguration">Transfiguration</a></b>: Switch was an expert on this highly advanced, complicated and <a href="/wiki/Science" title="Science">scientific</a> branch of <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magic</a>, having authored the <a href="/wiki/Transfiguration_(class)" title="Transfiguration (class)">Transfiguration</a> textbook <i><a href="/wiki/A_Beginner%27s_Guide_to_Transfiguration" title="A Beginner&#39;s Guide to Transfiguration">A Beginner's Guide to Transfiguration</a></i>, as well as having contributed to the academic journal <i><a href="/wiki/Transfiguration_Today" title="Transfiguration Today">Transfiguration Today</a></i> about <a href="/wiki/Human_Transfiguration" title="Human Transfiguration">Human Transfiguration</a>, showing his proficiency and skill in this field.</li></ul>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Emeric_Switch?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Emeric_Switch?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p><i>Emeric </i>is a variant of <i>Emmerich</i>, which means "whole, universal". This could relate to him being able to do transfiguration, as he can wholly turn something into something universally else.
</p><p>To switch is to exchange one thing with another; could be extended to transfiguration, changing one form into another.
</p>
