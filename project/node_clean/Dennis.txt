<p><b>Dennis</b> (born c. <a href="/wiki/1980" title="1980">1980</a>) was a <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggle</a> and, in his youth, a member of <a href="/wiki/Dudley_Dursley" title="Dudley Dursley">Dudley Dursley</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Dudley_Dursley%27s_gang" title="Dudley Dursley&#39;s gang">gang</a>. Dennis, along with the rest of the group, was described as "big and stupid".
</p><p>During the summer months of <a href="/wiki/1991" title="1991">1991</a>, Dennis would go to <a href="/wiki/4_Privet_Drive" title="4 Privet Drive">Dudley's house</a> every day, along with his friends <a href="/wiki/Piers_Polkiss" title="Piers Polkiss">Piers Polkiss</a>, <a href="/wiki/Malcolm" title="Malcolm">Malcolm</a> and <a href="/wiki/Gordon" title="Gordon">Gordon</a>. This forced <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> to spend as much time as possible out of the house, as they were fond of <a href="/wiki/Harry_Hunting" title="Harry Hunting">bullying him</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-PS3_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PS3-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Dennis?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Dennis?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The name "Dennis" comes from Denys or Denis, the medieval French forms of Dionysius. Saint Denis was a 3rd-century missionary to Gaul who was beheaded in Paris. He is credited with converting the Gauls to Christianity and is considered the patron saint of France.
</p>
