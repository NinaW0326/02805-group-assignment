<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/German-speaking_woman?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/German-speaking_woman?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>She was described to be laughing and had much humour in her face when she opened the door to her home one day in <a href="/wiki/1997" title="1997">1997</a>. However, when she saw <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a> standing outside, her expression changed rapidly from happiness to terror.<sup id="cite_ref-DH12_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH12-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>When Voldemort came to her door in search of Gregorovitch on <a href="/wiki/1_September" title="1 September">1 September</a>, <a href="/wiki/1997" title="1997">1997</a>, she told him that the wandmaker did not live there, and that she did not know him. She tried to close the door on him, but Voldemort forced his way into the household. The woman tried to shield her two children, but Voldemort nonetheless murdered all of them with the <a href="/wiki/Killing_Curse" title="Killing Curse">Killing Curse</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-DH12_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH12-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> was forced to witness the woman's misfortune of meeting with Voldemort due to his connections with the Dark Lord, and felt deeply miserable for the family.<sup id="cite_ref-DH12_1-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH12-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/German-speaking_woman?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/German-speaking_woman?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>Although the woman's fluency in German and the architecture of her home strongly suggest she lived in <a href="/wiki/Germany" title="Germany">Germany</a>, she may have lived in another German-speaking region, such as <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Austria" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Austria">Austria</a>, <a href="/wiki/Switzerland" title="Switzerland">Switzerland</a>, or <a href="/wiki/Liechtenstein" title="Liechtenstein">Liechtenstein</a>. She might also have lived in a German speaking area or other German-speaking territory in the <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Czech_Republic" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Czech Republic">Czech Republic</a>, <a href="/wiki/Belgium" title="Belgium">Belgium</a>, <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Slovakia" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Slovakia">Slovakia</a>, <a href="/wiki/Hungary" title="Hungary">Hungary</a>, <a href="/wiki/Romania" title="Romania">Romania</a>, <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Lithuania" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Lithuania">Lithuania</a>, <a href="/wiki/Poland" title="Poland">Poland</a> or <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Yugoslavia" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Yugoslavia">former Yugoslavia</a>, <a href="/wiki/France" title="France">France</a> or <a href="/wiki/Italy" title="Italy">Italy</a>.</li>
<li>The way she tried to protect her children is reminiscent of how <a href="/wiki/Lily_J._Potter" title="Lily J. Potter">Lily Potter</a> tried to protect her son <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry</a>, although the woman was not given the choice to step aside and save herself.</li>
<li>It is unknown if this woman and her family were <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggles</a> or <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witches and wizards</a>.</li></ul>
