<p>This <b><a href="/wiki/House-elf" title="House-elf">house-elf</a></b> worked at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> in the <a href="/wiki/1980s" title="1980s">1980s</a>. They worked in the <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_kitchens" title="Hogwarts kitchens">kitchens</a> along with the other <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_house-elves" title="Hogwarts house-elves">Hogwarts house-elves</a>. They were regularly seen stealing naps, whilst leaning against a wall, in a corridor near the entrance to the kitchens, infuriating <a href="/wiki/Pitts" title="Pitts">Pitts</a>, the House Elf tasked with overseeing their actions as well as those of the other elves. When woken by <a href="/wiki/Jacob%27s_sibling" title="Jacob&#39;s sibling">Jacob's sibling</a>, the house-elf panicked, and quickly ran off. While Pitts was aware that one of his elves was taking regular naps in the corridors, he had no idea which elf it was, allowing this elf to get off scot-free.
</p>
