<b>Alexia Walkin Black</b><sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> was a <a href="/wiki/Pure-blood" title="Pure-blood">pure-blood</a> <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch</a>. She was an early member of the <a href="/wiki/House_of_Black" title="House of Black">House of Black</a>, predating <a href="/wiki/Arcturus_Black_I" title="Arcturus Black I">Arcturus Black I</a> and <a href="/wiki/Cygnus_Black_I" title="Cygnus Black I">Cygnus Black I</a>, but her precise relationship to other known members of the Black family is unknown. She never married, according to the <a href="/wiki/Black_Family_Tapestry" title="Black Family Tapestry">Black family tree tapestry</a>, and was childless.<sup id="cite_ref-ootpmovie_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-ootpmovie-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Alexia_Walkin_Black?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Alexia_Walkin_Black?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p><i>Alexia</i> is the feminine form of <i>Alexis</i>. The <a href="/wiki/Greece" title="Greece">Greek</a> name Αλεξις (Alexis), which meant "helper" or "defender", is derived from Greek αλεξω (<i>alexo</i>) "to defend, to help". This was the name of a 3rd-century BC Greek comic poet, and also of several <a href="/wiki/Saint" title="Saint">saints</a>. It is used somewhat interchangeably with the related name Αλεξιος or <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Emperor_Alexius" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Emperor Alexius">Alexius</a>, borne by five Byzantine emperors.<sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Black&#160;can be considered a colour, and is the result of the absence of, or complete absorption of, light.&#160;In Western culture, the colour black has typically been associated with evil, darkness, and witchcraft, but also with prestige and sophistication — appropriate for the House of Black. Black has also been associated with life and prosperity by other cultures. In ancient <a href="/wiki/China" title="China">China</a>, black was the symbol of the element of water, which also corresponds to&#160;Slytherin, the&#160;Hogwarts&#160;house of almost all members of the Black family. In English heraldry, black represents darkness and ignorance; a possible allusion to the prejudice common to members of the House of Black.
</p><p>However, the English word&#160;<i>black</i>&#160;is derived from either the Old English&#160;<i>blœc</i>, meaning "black", or&#160;<i>blac</i>, meaning "pale". Pallor&#160;is associated with sickness and the colour&#160;white, which is usually held to symbolise purity in Western and <a href="/wiki/India" title="India">Indian</a> culture, and death in East <a href="/wiki/Asia" title="Asia">Asian</a> cultures. This mirrors the typical colouring of members of the House of Black — dark hair and pale skin.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Alexia_Walkin_Black?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Alexia_Walkin_Black?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>This character appears only on the version of the Black family tree shown in the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Order_of_the_Phoenix_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix (film)">film adaptation</a> of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Order_of_the_Phoenix" title="Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix">Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix</a></i>. She was not included on the version of the tree which <a href="/wiki/J._K._Rowling" title="J. K. Rowling">J. K. Rowling</a> gave to a charity auction in <a href="/wiki/2006" title="2006">2006</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> She was presumably created by the production team of the films, with her name being selected in honour of <a target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener" class="external text" href="http://www.imdb.com/name/nm1023180/">Alexandra Walker</a>, an art director on <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Prisoner_of_Azkaban_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban (film)">Prisoner of Azkaban</a></i>, <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Goblet_of_Fire_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire (film)">Goblet of Fire</a></i>, and <i>Order of the Phoenix</i>.</li>
<li>Based on a statement of <a href="/wiki/Horace_Slughorn" title="Horace Slughorn">Horace Slughorn</a>, Alexia Walkin probably graduated at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts</a> and was sorted into <a href="/wiki/Slytherin" title="Slytherin">Slytherin</a> house. However, she attended Hogwarts in the 1820s, long before Slughorn was Potions teacher.</li>
<li>Her siblings are probably <a href="/wiki/Eduardus_Limette_Black" title="Eduardus Limette Black">Eduardus Limette</a>, <a href="/wiki/Phoebe_Black" title="Phoebe Black">Phoebe</a>, <a href="/wiki/Licorus_Black" title="Licorus Black">Licorus</a> and <a href="/wiki/Hesper_Black" title="Hesper Black">Hesper Black</a>.</li>
<li>On the scroll beneath Alexia’s image on the family tree, there are no dates.</li>
<li>It is possible that Walkin was a maiden name of Alexia’s mother.<sup id="cite_ref-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li>Based on Black Family Tree Tapestry, Alexia’s and <a href="/wiki/Andromeda_Black" class="mw-redirect" title="Andromeda Black">Andromeda&#160;Black</a>’s pictures are the same.</li></ul>
