<p>A <b>potioneer</b>, also known as a <b>potion-brewer</b> or <b>potion-maker</b>, is a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch or wizard</a> who makes <a href="/wiki/Potion" title="Potion">potions</a> for a living, meaning that their primary source of income comes from making potions or in someway related to potions in general. This could include working as a professional brewer, studying or inventing potions, or teaching potions as a subject at a <a href="/wiki/Wizarding_school" title="Wizarding school">wizarding school</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP9_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP9-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>A person who teaches potions is sometimes known as a <a href="/wiki/Potions_master" class="mw-redirect" title="Potions master">potions master</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-PS8_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PS8-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> one who has achieved a <a href="/wiki/Nastily_Exhausting_Wizarding_Test" title="Nastily Exhausting Wizarding Test">N.E.W.T.</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Potions_(class)" title="Potions (class)">subject</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP9_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP9-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>The <a href="/wiki/Most_Extraordinary_Society_of_Potioneers" title="Most Extraordinary Society of Potioneers">Most Extraordinary Society of Potioneers</a> was an organisation of potioneers led by <a href="/wiki/Hector_Dagworth-Granger" title="Hector Dagworth-Granger">Hector Dagworth-Granger</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP9_1-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP9-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
