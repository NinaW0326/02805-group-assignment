<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Dean_Thomas%27s_stepfather?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Dean_Thomas%27s_stepfather?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>He married <a href="/wiki/Dean_Thomas%27_mother" class="mw-redirect" title="Dean Thomas&#39; mother">Dean's mother</a> some time after she was abandoned by her <a href="/wiki/Dean_Thomas%27s_father" title="Dean Thomas&#39;s father">first husband</a>. They had several <a href="/wiki/Dean_Thomas%27s_half-siblings" title="Dean Thomas&#39;s half-siblings">children</a> together and lived happily in <a href="/wiki/London" title="London">London</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>After <a href="/wiki/Cedric_Diggory" title="Cedric Diggory">Cedric Diggory</a> was murdered by <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a> in <a href="/wiki/1995" title="1995">1995</a>, Dean consciously avoided telling his parents about the event, presumably because he did not want them to pull him out of <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts</a>, although it also seemed likely the same murderer would be coming after their son as well, hence he did not wish to fill his parents with unneeded dread.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP11_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP11-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>When Dean was forced to go into hiding during the <a href="/wiki/Second_Wizarding_War" title="Second Wizarding War">Second Wizarding War</a>, Mr Thomas and his wife did not know where he had gone, and feared the worst, so <i><a href="/wiki/Potterwatch" title="Potterwatch">Potterwatch</a></i> broadcasted a request urging anyone who was with Dean to order him to please contact his family.<sup id="cite_ref-DH22_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH22-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Dean_Thomas%27s_stepfather?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Dean_Thomas%27s_stepfather?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>It may have been possible that Dean Thomas' story could have been partially inspired by the song "Colour Him Father" by an American group The Winstons, which tells of a young man respecting his stepfather who cares for him and several siblings. The stepfather married a war widow, as the narrator says his true father "was killed in the war", akin to Dean Thomas' father being killed by Voldemort.</li></ul>
