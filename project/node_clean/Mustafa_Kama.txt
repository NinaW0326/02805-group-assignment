<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Mustafa_Kama?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Mustafa_Kama?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>In <a href="/wiki/1896" class="mw-redirect" title="1896">1896</a>, Mustafa's wife, Laurena, was <a href="/wiki/Imperius_Curse" title="Imperius Curse">Imperiused</a> by <a href="/wiki/Corvus_Lestrange_(IV)" title="Corvus Lestrange (IV)">Corvus Lestrange</a> and left him.<sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> When he learned of Laurena's death, he was driven insane out of grief. Shortly before his death, Mustafa made his son swear an <a href="/wiki/Unbreakable_Vow" title="Unbreakable Vow">Unbreakable Vow</a> to kill the person whom Corvus loved the most.<sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Personality_and_traits">Personality and traits</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Mustafa_Kama?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Personality and traits">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Mustafa_Kama?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Personality and traits"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>According to his son <a href="/wiki/Yusuf_Kama" title="Yusuf Kama">Yusuf</a>, Mustafa was very much in <a href="/wiki/Love" title="Love">love</a> with his wife, indicating that he was a devoted husband, but he went insane with grief after learning of her death. Indeed, Mustafa became so overwhelmed by his grief that he forced his son to give an <a href="/wiki/Unbreakable_Vow" title="Unbreakable Vow">Unbreakable Vow</a> to avenge his family' ruin by killing the one Corvus IV loved the most.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Mustafa_Kama?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Mustafa_Kama?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>His name means "the chosen one" in Arabic, an epithet of Muhammad.
</p>
