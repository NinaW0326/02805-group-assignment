<p>The <b>Vibes Twins</b> were <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizarding</a> twin sisters and musicians<sup id="cite_ref-MoHP_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-MoHP-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>, active in the <a href="/wiki/1990s" title="1990s">1990s</a>.
</p><p>In <a href="/wiki/August" title="August">August</a> of <a href="/wiki/1993" title="1993">1993</a>, the <i><a href="/wiki/Daily_Prophet" title="Daily Prophet">Daily Prophet</a></i> reported that the upcoming issue of the <i><a href="/wiki/Sunday_Prophet" title="Sunday Prophet">Sunday Prophet</a></i> would include an exposé of their past of fame, money, and shame.<sup id="cite_ref-LeakyCon2013_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-LeakyCon2013-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> In <a href="/wiki/September" title="September">September</a> of <a href="/wiki/1993" title="1993">1993</a>, the Vibes Twins were scheduled to feature in the <a href="/wiki/Spellbound" title="Spellbound">Spellbound</a> "<a href="/wiki/Witch_Way%3F" title="Witch Way?">Witch Way?</a>" musical tour, along with <a href="/wiki/DJ_Martha" title="DJ Martha">DJ Martha</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-MoHP_1-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-MoHP-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
