<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Elladora_Black?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Elladora_Black?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Elladora was born in <a href="/wiki/1850" class="mw-redirect" title="1850">1850</a>.
</p><p>She was possibly the daughter of <a href="/wiki/Cygnus_Black_I" title="Cygnus Black I">Cygnus Black I</a> and his wife <a href="/wiki/Ella_Max" title="Ella Max">Ella Max</a>. Her siblings were <a href="/wiki/Sirius_Black_I" title="Sirius Black I">Sirius Black I</a>, <a href="/wiki/Phineas_Nigellus_Black" title="Phineas Nigellus Black">Phineas Nigellus</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Iola_Black" title="Iola Black">Iola</a>. She never married or had children.<sup id="cite_ref-BFT_1-15" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-BFT-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Elladora instituted the policy of cutting off the heads of the family <a href="/wiki/House-elf" title="House-elf">house-elves</a> and mounting them on the wall once they became too old to carry a tea tray.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Elladora_Black?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Elladora_Black?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>Based on a statement by <a href="/wiki/Horace_Slughorn" title="Horace Slughorn">Horace Slughorn</a>, Elladora may have been a member of the <a href="/wiki/Slytherin" title="Slytherin">Slytherin House</a>. This is most likely true, since <a href="/wiki/Sirius_Black" title="Sirius Black">Sirius Black</a> said all of his <a href="/wiki/House_of_Black" title="House of Black">family</a> was in the Slytherin House. However, she attended Hogwarts c. <a href="/wiki/1861" class="mw-redirect" title="1861">1861</a> - <a href="/wiki/1868" class="mw-redirect" title="1868">1868</a>, when Slughorn wasn't even born.</li>
<li>Elladora and her siblings may have been children of <a href="/wiki/Cygnus_Black_I" title="Cygnus Black I">Cygnus Black I</a> and <a href="/wiki/Ella_Max" title="Ella Max">Ella Max</a>. In this case, their uncle would have been <a href="/wiki/Arcturus_Black_I" title="Arcturus Black I">Arcturus Black I</a>, Cygnus' brother, and aunt <a href="/wiki/Misapinoa_Black" title="Misapinoa Black">Misapinoa Blishwick née Black</a>.</li>
<li>If Elladora was the sister of Sirius, Phineas and Iola, and the daughter of Ella Max and the late Cygnus, this would mean that her father died when she was about one year old, and her brother died when she was three.</li>
<li>Elladora's and Callidora's pictures on the <a href="/wiki/Black_Family_Tapestry" title="Black Family Tapestry">Black family tree tapestry</a> are the same. Also, Callidora and Elladora have similar names.</li>
<li>Sirius once said to <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry,</a> "dear Aunt Elladora", but on the <a href="/wiki/House_of_Black" title="House of Black">Black family tree</a>, Elladora is Sirius' great-great-great aunt. Sirius may not have wanted to bore Harry with "great-great-great aunt", and just chosen to call her aunt, as it was easier.</li>
<li>Elladora was only three years old when her elder brother <a href="/wiki/Sirius_Black_I" title="Sirius Black I">Sirius</a> died.</li>
<li>Elladora could have possibly been a half-namesake for <a href="/wiki/Callidora_Black" title="Callidora Black">Callidora</a>, as both have names ending with Dora (which is the nickname of Elladora's <a href="/wiki/Nymphadora_Tonks" title="Nymphadora Tonks">four-greats-niece</a>, given to her by her father, though Tonks came from her <a href="/wiki/Andromeda_Tonks" title="Andromeda Tonks">disowned mother</a>).</li></ul>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Elladora_Black?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Elladora_Black?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>The name <i>Ella</i> is a Norman form of the Germanic <i>Alia</i>, meaning "other".<sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> It is also possibly related to <i><a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Aelle" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Aelle">Aella</a></i>, a Greek name meaning "whirlwind". Aella of mythology was an Amazon killed by Hercules. The name <i>Dora</i>, often considered a diminutive of <i>Theodora</i>, is derived from the Greek word <i>doron</i>, meaning "gift".<sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li>Elladora was probably named after her mother Ella.</li></ul>
