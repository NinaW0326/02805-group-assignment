<p>This <b>waitress</b> was a female <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggle</a> employee at <a href="/wiki/Treats" title="Treats">Treats Café</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-script_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-script-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> a café in <a href="/wiki/Surbiton_Station" title="Surbiton Station">Surbiton Station</a> in <a href="/wiki/1996" title="1996">1996</a>. She flirted with <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> during the summer of <a href="/wiki/1996" title="1996">1996</a> and almost came close to discovering the <a href="/wiki/Wizarding_world" title="Wizarding world">wizarding world</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-HBPF_1-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBPF-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<figure class="thumb tleft show-info-icon" style="width: 242px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/e/eb/Harry_caf%C3%A9_HBP_2.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20090509210832" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/e/eb/Harry_caf%C3%A9_HBP_2.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/240?cb=20090509210832" decoding="async" width="240" height="102" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="Harry café HBP 2.jpg" data-image-key="Harry_café_HBP_2.jpg" /></a> 	 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:Harry_caf%C3%A9_HBP_2.jpg" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption">Harry Potter reads the Daily Prophet in the café</p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure>
<p>She flirted with <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> in <a href="/wiki/Treats" title="Treats">Treats Café</a>, at <a href="/wiki/Surbiton_Station" title="Surbiton Station">Surbiton Station</a>, at some point in the summer of <a href="/wiki/1996" title="1996">1996</a>. Harry was a regular at the café in which she worked as he found taking trains kept his mind off things and would often spend nights there. She almost became aware of the existence of the <a href="/wiki/Wizarding_world" title="Wizarding world">Wizarding world</a> when Harry allowed her to see a copy of the <i><a href="/wiki/Daily_Prophet" title="Daily Prophet">Daily Prophet</a></i> with moving images, but she brushed off her suspicions due to working late.<sup id="cite_ref-HBPF_1-10" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBPF-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Waitress_at_Treats?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Waitress_at_Treats?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>The waitress was portrayed by <a href="/wiki/Elarica_Gallacher" title="Elarica Gallacher">Elarica Gallacher</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Half-Blood_Prince_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince (film)">film adaptation</a> of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Half-Blood_Prince" title="Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince">Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince</a></i>.</li>
<li>The waitress was not in the book, nor was the entire scene at Treats Café.</li>
<li>She was also included in <i><a href="/wiki/LEGO_Harry_Potter:_Years_5-7" title="LEGO Harry Potter: Years 5-7">LEGO Harry Potter: Years 5-7</a></i> although the restaraunt <a href="/wiki/Treats" title="Treats">Treats</a> does not appear, she appears sitting by Harry putting lipstick on her lips. She is a playable character only in the consoles versions.<sup id="cite_ref-LEGOY2_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-LEGOY2-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup></li></ul>
