Two <b>wizards</b> were in the <a href="/wiki/Hog%27s_Head_Inn" title="Hog&#39;s Head Inn">Hog's Head Inn</a> in <a href="/wiki/1995" title="1995">1995</a> on the day several <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts</a> students met there to discuss the formation of their own <a href="/wiki/Defence_Against_the_Dark_Arts" title="Defence Against the Dark Arts">Defence Against the Dark Arts</a> <a href="/wiki/Dumbledore%27s_Army" title="Dumbledore&#39;s Army">group</a>. They wore cloaks and sat in the corner. <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> would have thought they were <a href="/wiki/Dementor" title="Dementor">Dementors</a>, had they not been speaking in strong <a href="/wiki/Yorkshire" title="Yorkshire">Yorkshire</a> accents.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP16_1-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP16-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
