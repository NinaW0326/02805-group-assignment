The <i><b>Evening Prophet</b></i> was the name of the evening edition of the <a href="/wiki/Daily_Prophet" title="Daily Prophet">Daily Prophet</a>. Unlike the main <i>Prophet</i>, the <i>Evening Prophet</i> was only published when a particularly noteworthy event happened, in which case it is "rushed out".<sup id="cite_ref-PM_1-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PM-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>The only known times that this has happened are the illegal flight of a <a href="/wiki/Flying_Ford_Anglia" title="Flying Ford Anglia">Flying Ford Anglia</a> by <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> and <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ronald Weasley</a> across <a href="/wiki/Great_Britain" title="Great Britain">Great Britain</a> (<i>Flying Ford Anglia Mystifies Muggles</i>),<sup id="cite_ref-COS5_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COS5-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> and a search on <a href="/wiki/Malfoy_Manor" title="Malfoy Manor">Malfoy Manor</a> by the <a href="/wiki/Office_for_the_Detection_and_Confiscation_of_Counterfeit_Defensive_Spells_and_Protective_Objects" title="Office for the Detection and Confiscation of Counterfeit Defensive Spells and Protective Objects">Office for the Detection and Confiscation of Counterfeit Defensive Spells and Protective Objects</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP11_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP11-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Known_excerpts">Known excerpts</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Evening_Prophet?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Known excerpts">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Evening_Prophet?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Known excerpts"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>A known excerpt from the <i>Evening Prophet</i> was an article concerning <a href="/wiki/Arthur_Weasley" title="Arthur Weasley">Arthur Weasley</a>'s second search of Malfoy Manor. The text was the following:
</p><p><i>"This second search of the Death Eater's residence does not seem to have yielded any results. Arthur Weasley of the Office for the Detection and Confiscation of Counterfeit Defensive Spells and Protective Objects said that his team had been acting upon a confidential tip-off"</i><sup id="cite_ref-HBP11_3-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP11-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>This "confidential tip-off" was from <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>, who told Arthur Weasley at <a href="/wiki/King%27s_Cross" class="mw-redirect" title="King&#39;s Cross">King's Cross</a> about <a href="/wiki/Draco_Malfoy" title="Draco Malfoy">Draco Malfoy</a> and the thing that he was trying to get <a href="/wiki/Borgin" title="Borgin">Borgin</a> to fix when Harry, Ronald Weasley, and <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione Granger</a> eavesdropped on Malfoy in <a href="/wiki/Borgin_and_Burkes" title="Borgin and Burkes">Borgin and Burkes</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP11_3-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP11-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> This object turned out to be a <a href="/wiki/Vanishing_Cabinet" title="Vanishing Cabinet">Vanishing Cabinet</a>. The one in Borgin and Burkes had a twin in <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> and Malfoy was mending the two so that he could help the Death Eaters get into Hogwarts despite all the protective enchantments around it.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP27_4-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP27-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
