<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_Non-magique_toddler?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_Non-magique_toddler?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Together with his parents, the baby lived in an apartment block somewhere in Paris and was murdered by <a href="/wiki/Gellert_Grindelwald%27s_acolytes" title="Gellert Grindelwald&#39;s acolytes">Gellert Grindelwald's acolyte</a>&#160;<a href="/wiki/Carrow_(1920s)" title="Carrow (1920s)">Carrow</a> under the orders of Grindelwald himself. They later used the family's home as a temporary headquarters, from where he conducted a search for <a href="/wiki/Credence_Barebone" title="Credence Barebone">Credence Barebone</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-COGS_2-10" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COGS-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
