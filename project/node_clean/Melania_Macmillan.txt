<b>Melania Black</b> (née <b>Macmillan</b>) (fl. 1925-1929) was a <a href="/wiki/Pure-blood" title="Pure-blood">pure-blood</a> <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch</a> married to <a href="/wiki/Arcturus_Black_III" title="Arcturus Black III">Arcturus Black III</a>. The couple had two children, <a href="/wiki/Lucretia_Black" title="Lucretia Black">Lucretia</a> and <a href="/wiki/Orion_Black" title="Orion Black">Orion Black</a>. She was <a href="/wiki/Sirius_Black" title="Sirius Black">Sirius</a> and <a href="/wiki/Regulus_Black" title="Regulus Black">Regulus Black</a>'s paternal grandmother.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Melania_Macmillan?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Melania_Macmillan?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p><i>Melania</i> is the Italian, Spanish or Polish version of the name <i>Melanie</i>, which is derived from the Greek <i>melaina</i>, meaning "black, dark".<sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Melania_Macmillan?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Melania_Macmillan?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>Melania is almost certainly related to <a href="/wiki/Ernest_Macmillan" title="Ernest Macmillan">Ernest Macmillan</a>, a <a href="/wiki/Hufflepuff" title="Hufflepuff">Hufflepuff</a> student in <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>'s year who boasts that he can "trace [his] family back through nine generations of witches and warlocks." Considering the respective ages of Melania and Ernie, they are either cousins several times removed or Melania is the sister of Ernie's great-grandfather. If the latter, Melania could claim <i>six </i>generations of magical ancestors.</li>
<li>Likewise, if <a href="/wiki/Hufflepuff" title="Hufflepuff">Hufflepuff</a> is considered the <a href="/wiki/Macmillan_family" title="Macmillan family">Macmillan family</a>'s traditional house, she might have been <a href="/wiki/Sorting_ceremony" title="Sorting ceremony">Sorted</a> there unlike the majority of her <a href="/wiki/House_of_Black" title="House of Black">husband's family</a> and her descendants.</li>
<li>Melania is likely Scottish as Macmillan is a Scottish surname, though her family might have left the country before her birth.</li></ul>
