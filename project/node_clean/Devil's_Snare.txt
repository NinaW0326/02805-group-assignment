<p>It is composed of a mass of soft, springy tendrils and vines that possess some sense of touch, and resembles the <a href="/wiki/Flitterbloom" title="Flitterbloom">Flitterbloom</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP23_4-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP23-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> This plant uses its creepers and tendrils to ensnare anyone who touches it, binding their arms and legs and eventually choking them. The harder a person struggles against Devil's Snare, the faster and more tightly it binds them. If the victim is able to maintain their presence of mind and relaxes, the Snare will relax its grip on them. Struggling or resistance to Devil's Snare will cause the plant to exert a greater force of constriction. Devil's Snare prefers a dark, damp environment. It will stop its movement in the environment in front of bright light and will recoil away from the heat of <a href="/wiki/Fire" title="Fire">fire</a>, so a well-placed fire or light based spell such as the <a href="/wiki/Bluebell_Flames" title="Bluebell Flames">Bluebell Flames</a>, <a href="/wiki/Fire-Making_Spell" title="Fire-Making Spell">Fire-Making Spell</a>, <a href="/wiki/Wand-Lighting_Charm" title="Wand-Lighting Charm">Wand-Lighting Charm</a> or <a href="/wiki/Lumos_Solem_Spell" title="Lumos Solem Spell">Lumos Solem Spell</a> will effectively drive it away from its victims.<sup id="cite_ref-PS16_1-10" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PS16-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

Hermione Granger instantly identified it but initially struggled to remember how to kill the plant from she had learnt in <a href="/wiki/Herbology" title="Herbology">Herbology</a>, but thankfully, she remembered the plant's preference for damp and darkness, so she set the plant alight with the <a href="/wiki/Bluebell_Flames" title="Bluebell Flames">Bluebell Flames</a>, freeing both Harry and Ron from the deadly plant.<sup id="cite_ref-PS16_1-11" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PS16-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>

<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="1995-1996"><a href="/wiki/1995%E2%80%931996_school_year" title="1995–1996 school year">1995-1996</a></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Devil%27s_Snare?veaction=edit&amp;section=6" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: 1995-1996">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Devil%27s_Snare?action=edit&amp;section=6" title="Edit section: 1995-1996"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<div class="quote">
<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix">"<i>As his speech and mobility improved, Healer Strout encouraged Mr Bode to look after the plant himself, unaware that it was not an innocent Flitterbloom, but a cutting of Devil’s Snare, which, when touched by the convalescent Mr Bode, throttled him instantly.</i>"</span></dd>
<dd>&#8212;A spokeswizard for St Mungos regarding the death of Broderick Bode, reported in the <a href="/wiki/Daily_Prophet" title="Daily Prophet">Daily Prophet</a><sup class="noprint"><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Order_of_the_Phoenix" title="Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix">[src]</a></sup></dd>
<dd></dd></dl></div>
<p>In <a href="/wiki/1995" title="1995">1995</a>, <a href="/wiki/Broderick_Bode" title="Broderick Bode">Broderick Bode</a>, an <a href="/wiki/Unspeakable" title="Unspeakable">Unspeakable</a> working for the <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">British Ministry of Magic</a>, was a comatose patient at <a href="/wiki/St_Mungo%27s_Hospital_for_Magical_Maladies_and_Injuries" title="St Mungo&#39;s Hospital for Magical Maladies and Injuries">St Mungo's Hospital for Magical Maladies and Injuries</a>, after having been <a href="/wiki/Imperius_Curse" title="Imperius Curse">Imperiused</a> into attempting to steal <a href="/wiki/Sybill_Trelawney%27s_first_prophecy" title="Sybill Trelawney&#39;s first prophecy">a prophecy</a> from the <a href="/wiki/Department_of_Mysteries" title="Department of Mysteries">Department of Mysteries</a>. When Bode began to recover, a <a href="/wiki/Walden_Macnair" title="Walden Macnair">Death Eater</a> sent a potted Devil's Snare to the hospital in the disguise of a <a href="/wiki/Christmas" title="Christmas">Christmas</a> gift in order to silence him. <a href="/wiki/Healer" title="Healer">Healer</a> <a href="/wiki/Miriam_Strout" title="Miriam Strout">Miriam Strout</a> foolishly mistook the plant for a harmless <a href="/wiki/Flitterbloom" title="Flitterbloom">flitterbloom</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP23_4-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP23-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> and it subsequently asphyxiated Bode later that night.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP25_2-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP25-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Devil%27s_Snare?veaction=edit&amp;section=8" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Devil%27s_Snare?action=edit&amp;section=8" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>In the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Philosopher%27s_Stone_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Philosopher&#39;s Stone (film)">film adaptation</a> of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Philosopher%27s_Stone" title="Harry Potter and the Philosopher&#39;s Stone">Harry Potter and the Philosopher's Stone</a></i>, Hermione and Harry were able to escape the Devil's Snare by simply remaining calm. When Ron began to panic, Hermione saved him by casting <a href="/wiki/Lumos_Solem_Spell" title="Lumos Solem Spell">Lumos Solem</a>. The Snare also roared in its appearance when exposed to Hermione's spell.</li>
<li>In the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Goblet_of_Fire_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire (film)">film adaptation</a> of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Goblet_of_Fire" title="Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire">Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire</a></i>, the <a href="/wiki/Triwizard_Maze" title="Triwizard Maze">Hedge Maze</a> attacked the Champions during the third task of the <a href="/wiki/Triwizard_Tournament" title="Triwizard Tournament">Triwizard Tournament</a>. Some roots attacked Cedric, it may be related to Devil's Snare. Eventually, it kept strangling Cedric, then Harry freed him from it.</li>
<li>In <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Deathly_Hallows:_Part_2" title="Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 2">Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 2</a></i>, Devil's Snare can be seen inside the Room of Requirement growing out of a glass case at the bottom of a column and growing toward the ceiling.</li>
<li>In <i><a href="/wiki/LEGO_Harry_Potter:_Years_1-4" title="LEGO Harry Potter: Years 1-4">LEGO Harry Potter: Years 1-4</a></i>, the <a href="/wiki/First_year" title="First year">first year</a> <a href="/wiki/Herbology" title="Herbology">Herbology</a> students have a lesson on Devil's Snare. The plant gets out of control and captures Ron, as well as stealing <a href="/wiki/Pomona_Sprout%27s_wand" title="Pomona Sprout&#39;s wand">Pomona Sprout's wand</a>, requiring Harry and Hermione to destroy the plant with the <a href="/wiki/Lumos_Solem_Spell" title="Lumos Solem Spell">Lumos Solem Spell</a>.</li>
<li>In the PS2, Xbox and Gamecube versions of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Philosopher%27s_Stone_(video_game)" title="Harry Potter and the Philosopher&#39;s Stone (video game)">Harry Potter and the Philosopher's Stone</a></i>, the Devil Snare actually heavily resembles e a giant <a href="/wiki/Venomous_Tentacula" title="Venomous Tentacula">Venomous Tentacula</a> but coloured blue instead of green (and it is defeated the same way, using the <a href="/wiki/Severing_Charm" title="Severing Charm">Severing Charm</a> and the <a href="/wiki/Fire-Making_Spell" title="Fire-Making Spell">Fire-Making Spell</a> respectively).
<ul><li>This also applies to its portrayal in the GBA version, but it instead fires seeds continuously from its mouth if within a specific range and can only shoot them diagonally (unless Harry is very close to them then they'll shoot perpendicularly); unlike the Venomous Tentacula, it can be defeated by jinxing the nearby torch platters with <i>Flipendo</i> to make them tumble and burn the Devil's Snare.</li></ul></li>
<li>In the PS1 version, there is a circular chamber containing the Devil's Snare. Ron and Hermione run to a side and a part of the Devil's Snare forms a wall, blocking them from the rest of the circular chamber. There are several tree-like branches which are green that spring up when Harry gets to close and attempt to squash him. Harry must cast the <a href="/wiki/Knockback_Jinx" title="Knockback Jinx">Knockback Jinx</a> on the correct stalk (indicated in light green) in order to kill that stalk and bring the central stalk up, in which Harry must use a fully charged jinx at it to defeat part of its life. After defeating it each time and then setting the plant alight with the <a href="/wiki/Fire-Making_Spell" title="Fire-Making Spell">Fire-Making Spell</a> to kill it, the Devil's Snare blocking Ron and Hermione will wither away which allows them all to proceed.</li>
<li>Devil's snare is also another name for <i><a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Datura_stramonium" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Datura stramonium">datura stramonium</a></i>.</li></ul>
