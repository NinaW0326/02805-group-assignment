<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography&#160;</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Frank?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography ">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Frank?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography "><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p><a href="/wiki/Albus_Dumbledore" title="Albus Dumbledore">Albus Dumbledore</a> informed Newt that in Egypt was the Thunderbird who needed to be saved and taken home. Magizoologist found and rescued Frank from illegal trafficking and, though one of his legs bore the wounds of being chained, his gratitude to Newt was "evident"<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> and Scamander went to the <a href="/wiki/United_States_of_America" title="United States of America">United States of America</a> in <a href="/wiki/1926" title="1926">1926</a> in an attempt to return Frank to the arid climate of <a href="/wiki/Arizona" title="Arizona">Arizona</a>, the natural habitat of <a href="/wiki/Thunderbird" title="Thunderbird">Thunderbirds</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-EW2016-08-12_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-EW2016-08-12-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> Newt described him as a majestic, powerful, and regal creature, but a "wee-bit sensitive to strangers".<sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>After the battle against <a href="/wiki/Credence_Barebone" title="Credence Barebone">Credence Barebone</a> and <a href="/wiki/Gellert_Grindelwald" title="Gellert Grindelwald">Gellert Grindelwald</a>, Newt gave Frank a vial of <a href="/wiki/Swooping_Evil" title="Swooping Evil">Swooping Evil</a> venom, which the latter diffused through a storm he conjured to wipe <a href="/wiki/New_York" title="New York">New York</a>'s <a href="/wiki/No-Maj" class="mw-redirect" title="No-Maj">No-Maj</a> population of their memories of the devastation.
</p><p>Frank managed to return to Arizona after being released.<sup id="cite_ref-twitter1_4-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-twitter1-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Frank?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Frank?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>At the end of the <a href="/wiki/Fantastic_Beasts_and_Where_to_Find_Them_(film)" title="Fantastic Beasts and Where to Find Them (film)">film</a> inspired by <i><a href="/wiki/Fantastic_Beasts_and_Where_to_Find_Them_(companion_book)" title="Fantastic Beasts and Where to Find Them (companion book)">Fantastic Beasts and Where to Find Them</a></i>, Frank is seen leaving <a href="/wiki/New_York" title="New York">New York</a> flying towards the sun. As the sun was coming up at the time, this means Frank was flying eastwards, which is the wrong way to get to <a href="/wiki/Arizona" title="Arizona">Arizona</a>.
<ul><li>However, he could have made a U-turn.</li></ul></li></ul>
