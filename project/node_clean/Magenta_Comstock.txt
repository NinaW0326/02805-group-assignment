<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_Life">Early Life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Magenta_Comstock?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Early Life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Magenta_Comstock?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Early Life"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Magenta Comstock was born in 1895 unto parents of unknown blood status.
</p><p>She became an artist and began expertimenting with her artwork. Her portraits were known for their staring eyes which would often follow viewers home.
</p><p>She was on a <a href="/wiki/Chocolate_Frog_Card" title="Chocolate Frog Card">Chocolate Frog Card</a> by <a href="/wiki/1988" title="1988">1988</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Death_and_Post-Mortem">Death and Post-Mortem</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Magenta_Comstock?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Death and Post-Mortem">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Magenta_Comstock?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Death and Post-Mortem"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Comstock died in 1991 at the age of 96. She was awarded 'Wizard of the Month' in 2007.
</p>
