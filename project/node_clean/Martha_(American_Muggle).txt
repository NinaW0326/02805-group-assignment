<b>Martha</b> was an <a href="/wiki/United_States_of_America" title="United States of America">American</a> <a href="/wiki/Muggle" class="mw-redirect" title="Muggle">No-Maj</a> woman who lived in the early <a href="/wiki/20th_century" title="20th century">20th century</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-FB5_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-FB5-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>While at the scene of a <a href="/wiki/Obscurial" title="Obscurial">mysterious attack</a> in <a href="/wiki/New_York" title="New York">New York</a> in <a href="/wiki/1926" title="1926">1926</a>, a <a href="/wiki/Unidentified_reporter" title="Unidentified reporter">newspaper reporter</a> asked his <a href="/wiki/Unidentified_photographer_(II)" title="Unidentified photographer (II)">photographer colleague</a> if he felt like getting a drink, to which the photographer replied that he had promised Martha (presumably his wife or girlfriend) that he would abstain from consuming <a href="/wiki/Alcohol" title="Alcohol">alcohol</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-FB5_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-FB5-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
