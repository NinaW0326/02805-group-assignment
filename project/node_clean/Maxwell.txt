<b>Maxwell</b> was a worker at, and possible owner of, <a href="/wiki/Fleury%27s_Fancy_Fauna" title="Fleury&#39;s Fancy Fauna">Fleury's Fancy Fauna</a>, a shop in <a href="/wiki/New_York" title="New York">New York</a> dealing in <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magical</a> <a href="/wiki/Beast" title="Beast">beasts</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> It is unknown what became of him after the shop was closed down by <a href="/wiki/Magical_Congress_of_the_United_States_of_America" title="Magical Congress of the United States of America">MACUSA</a> officials for illegal breeding of magical creatures in <a href="/wiki/1925" title="1925">1925</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-fb19_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-fb19-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
