<p>An <b>executioner</b> was charged with the <a href="http://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/execution" class="extiw" title="wiktionary:execution">execution</a> of <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magical</a> <a href="/wiki/Creature" title="Creature">creatures</a> deemed to be a threat to others by the <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">Ministry of Magic</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Committee_for_the_Disposal_of_Dangerous_Creatures" title="Committee for the Disposal of Dangerous Creatures">Committee for the Disposal of Dangerous Creatures</a>.
</p><p>Executioners could also serve in executing <a href="/wiki/Human" title="Human">human</a> criminals who had been condemned by the law of committing capital crimes. <h2><span class="mw-headline" id="History">History</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Executioner?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: History">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Executioner?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: History"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>On <a href="/wiki/31_October" title="31 October">31 October</a>, <a href="/wiki/1492" class="mw-redirect" title="1492">1492</a>, an unidentified executioner had the task of carrying out <a href="/wiki/Nicholas_de_Mimsy-Porpington" title="Nicholas de Mimsy-Porpington">Nicholas de Mimsy-Porpington</a>'s (aka Nearly Headless Nick) death sentence.<sup id="cite_ref-JKRS_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-JKRS-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>In the <a href="/wiki/1920s" title="1920s">1920s</a>, <a href="/wiki/Bernadette" title="Bernadette">Bernadette</a> worked as an executioner for the <a href="/wiki/Magical_Congress_of_the_United_States_of_America" title="Magical Congress of the United States of America">Magical Congress of the United States of America</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-FBS_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-FBS-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p><a href="/wiki/Walden_Macnair" title="Walden Macnair">Walden Macnair</a> worked as an executioner for the Ministry of Magic, after having been acquitted for his activities as a <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eater</a> during the <a href="/wiki/First_Wizarding_War" title="First Wizarding War">First Wizarding War</a>. In 1993, he accompanied <a href="/wiki/Cornelius_Fudge" title="Cornelius Fudge">Fudge</a> to <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> to carry out the disposal of <a href="/wiki/Buckbeak" title="Buckbeak">Buckbeak</a>, the <a href="/wiki/Hippogriff" title="Hippogriff">Hippogriff</a> who had been sentenced to death for its attack on <a href="/wiki/Draco_Malfoy" title="Draco Malfoy">Draco Malfoy</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-POA16_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-POA16-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>In <a href="/wiki/1996" title="1996">1996</a>, Macnair was been caught red-handed in renewed Death Eater activities, and was arrested, losing his position as a Ministry-employed executioner and imprisoned to <a href="/wiki/Azkaban" title="Azkaban">Azkaban</a> for his crimes,<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP36_4-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP36-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> though the <a href="/wiki/Fall_of_the_Ministry_of_Magic" title="Fall of the Ministry of Magic">takeover</a> of the Ministry in the later year may have regained his position.
</p><p>Since the end of the <a href="/wiki/Second_Wizarding_War" title="Second Wizarding War">Second Wizarding War</a> it is unknown who was employed with fulfilling the role as executioner. 
</p>
