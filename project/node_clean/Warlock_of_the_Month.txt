<i><b>Warlock of the Month</b></i> was a <i><a href="/wiki/Daily_Prophet" title="Daily Prophet">Daily Prophet</a></i> newspaper segment that covered prestigious <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizards</a>. The segment was issued on a monthly basis. In <a href="/wiki/1926" title="1926">1926</a>, it covered <a href="/wiki/Fleamont_Potter" title="Fleamont Potter">Fleamont Potter</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Sleekeazy%27s_Hair_Potion" title="Sleekeazy&#39;s Hair Potion">Sleekeazy's Hair Potion</a>, which was said to 'woo' <a href="/wiki/United_States_of_America" title="United States of America">American</a> wizards; as well as <a href="/wiki/Griselda_Marchbanks" title="Griselda Marchbanks">Griselda Marchbanks</a> who had been interviewed by the <i>Prophet</i> and had claimed dissatisfaction with her <a href="/wiki/Charms_(class)" title="Charms (class)">Charms</a> tests results.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
