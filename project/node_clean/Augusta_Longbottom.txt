<p><b>Augusta Longbottom</b> (b. pre <a href="/wiki/1947" title="1947">1947</a>) was a <a href="/wiki/Pure-blood" title="Pure-blood">pure-blood</a> <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch</a> and member of the <a href="/wiki/Longbottom_family" title="Longbottom family">Longbottom family</a> through marriage. She was the mother of famous <a href="/wiki/Auror" title="Auror">Auror</a> <a href="/wiki/Frank_Longbottom" title="Frank Longbottom">Frank Longbottom</a>, mother-in-law to Auror <a href="/wiki/Alice_Longbottom" title="Alice Longbottom">Alice Longbottom</a>, and grandmother to <a href="/wiki/Neville_Longbottom" title="Neville Longbottom">Neville Longbottom</a>. Through Neville's marriage, she has a granddaughter-in-law, <a href="/wiki/Hannah_Abbott" title="Hannah Abbott">Hannah Longbottom</a>.
</p>

<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_life_and_marriage">Early life and marriage</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Augusta_Longbottom?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Early life and marriage">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Augusta_Longbottom?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Early life and marriage"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<div class="quote">
<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince">"<i>Take Charms and I shall drop Augusta a <a href="/wiki/Minerva_McGonagall%27s_letter_to_Augusta_Longbottom" title="Minerva McGonagall&#39;s letter to Augusta Longbottom">line</a> reminding her that just because she failed her Charms O.W.L., the subject is not necessarily worthless.</i>"</span></dd>

<p>Little is known about Augusta's early life except that she may have had at least one sibling.<sup id="cite_ref-Quill_10-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Quill-10">&#91;10&#93;</a></sup> In her school years she failed her <a href="/wiki/Charms_(class)" title="Charms (class)">Charms</a> <a href="/wiki/Ordinary_Wizarding_Level" title="Ordinary Wizarding Level">O.W.L.</a>, causing her to later label it a "soft option."<sup id="cite_ref-HBP9_9-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP9-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup> It's likely that Augusta went to school with Minerva McGonagall and attended under <a href="/wiki/Headmaster" title="Headmaster">Headmaster</a> <a href="/wiki/Armando_Dippet" title="Armando Dippet">Dippet</a>. It is also noted that she wanted her grandson to take <a href="/wiki/Transfiguration_(class)" title="Transfiguration (class)">Transfiguration</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-HBP9_9-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP9-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup> implying that while in school, she did well in that subject.
</p><p>At some point, Augusta married <a href="/wiki/Frank_Longbottom%27s_father" title="Frank Longbottom&#39;s father">Mr Longbottom</a>, a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-longbottomfamilytree_3-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-longbottomfamilytree-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> They had at least one child, a son, <a href="/wiki/Frank_Longbottom" title="Frank Longbottom">Frank</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-longbottomfamilytree_3-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-longbottomfamilytree-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>In <a href="/wiki/1947" title="1947">1947</a>, Neville's "granddad" (most likely Mr Longbottom) put a <a href="/wiki/Fanged_gerbil" title="Fanged gerbil">fanged gerbil</a> in Augusta's handbag as a prank, causing her to always keep a mousetrap inside it from that point onward.<sup id="cite_ref-QH_11-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-QH-11">&#91;11&#93;</a></sup> At some point Mr Longbottom died and his grandson was witness to it. 
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Raising_her_grandson">Raising her grandson</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Augusta_Longbottom?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Raising her grandson">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Augusta_Longbottom?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Raising her grandson"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<figure class="thumb tright show-info-icon" style="width: 232px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/a/a6/AugustaAndNeville1991.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20160408074805" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/a/a6/AugustaAndNeville1991.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/230?cb=20160408074805" decoding="async" width="230" height="193" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="AugustaAndNeville1991.jpg" data-image-key="AugustaAndNeville1991.jpg" /></a> 	 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:AugustaAndNeville1991.jpg" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption">Augusta takes her grandson to <a href="/wiki/Platform_Nine_and_Three-Quarters" title="Platform Nine and Three-Quarters">Platform 9¾</a> to start his magical education</p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure>
<p>During the <a href="/wiki/First_Wizarding_War" title="First Wizarding War">First Wizarding War</a>, Augusta's son <a href="/wiki/Frank_Longbottom" title="Frank Longbottom">Frank</a> was both an <a href="/wiki/Auror" title="Auror">Auror</a> and a member of the <a href="/wiki/Order_of_the_Phoenix" title="Order of the Phoenix">Order of the Phoenix</a>, as was his wife <a href="/wiki/Alice_Longbottom" title="Alice Longbottom">Alice</a>. The highly respected couple survived the war, but shortly after <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a>'s defeat in 1981, they were attacked by <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a> who tortured them into insanity with the <a href="/wiki/Cruciatus_Curse" title="Cruciatus Curse">Cruciatus Curse</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-GOF30_12-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF30-12">&#91;12&#93;</a></sup> The couple had to be institutionalised, and their infant son <a href="/wiki/Neville_Longbottom" title="Neville Longbottom">Neville</a> was placed into Augusta's care.
</p><p>Augusta was clearly strict with her grandson, who she initially feared was a <a href="/wiki/Squib" title="Squib">Squib</a>. Even after Neville proved to be a wizard, his grandmother was stern and demanding of him, sending him <a href="/wiki/Howler" title="Howler">Howlers</a> when his behaviour displeased her and pressuring him to uphold the "family honour." She often took Neville to visit his incapacitated parents at <a href="/wiki/St_Mungo%27s_Hospital_for_Magical_Maladies_and_Injuries" title="St Mungo&#39;s Hospital for Magical Maladies and Injuries">St Mungo's</a>, stating that he should be proud of their sacrifice.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP23_6-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP23-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Second_Wizarding_War">Second Wizarding War</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Augusta_Longbottom?veaction=edit&amp;section=4" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Second Wizarding War">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Augusta_Longbottom?action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Second Wizarding War"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>

<p>In <a href="/wiki/1995" title="1995">1995</a>, Augusta supported <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> and <a href="/wiki/Albus_Dumbledore" title="Albus Dumbledore">Albus Dumbledore</a> when they claimed that <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a> had returned to power, as did Neville. Her support even led her to stop subscribing to the <i><a href="/wiki/Daily_Prophet" title="Daily Prophet">Daily Prophet</a></i>, due to the newspaper's vicious smear campaign against Harry and Dumbledore.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP11_7-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP11-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup> When she found out that Neville has not told anyone of his parents' condition, she was furious at him for thinking he was ashamed and told Harry Potter, <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ronald Weasley</a>, <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione Granger</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Ginevra_Weasley" title="Ginevra Weasley">Ginny Weasley</a> about it.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP23_6-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP23-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup> Sternness soon turned to pride, as she finally became proud of Neville after his involvement in the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_the_Department_of_Mysteries" title="Battle of the Department of Mysteries">Battle of the Department of Mysteries</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP7_13-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP7-13">&#91;13&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<p>In <a href="/wiki/1997" title="1997">1997</a>, after <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a> had taken control of the <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">Ministry of Magic</a>, Neville was one of the leaders of the student rebellion against <a href="/wiki/Alecto_and_Amycus_Carrow" title="Alecto and Amycus Carrow">Alecto and Amycus Carrow</a> at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts</a>. In an attempt to force Neville to be more cooperative, <a href="/wiki/Auror" title="Auror">Auror</a> <a href="/wiki/John_Dawlish" title="John Dawlish">John Dawlish</a> was ordered to capture Augusta and take her hostage to ensure Neville's behaviour. Augusta, however, put up a fight, which Dawlish wasn't expecting; according to Neville, she put Dawlish in St Mungo's. After this, Augusta went on the run.<sup id="cite_ref-DH29_14-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH29-14">&#91;14&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>She later re-emerged at the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a> to assist Neville when he called everyone to arms against the <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eater</a> army. She was the last to come through the secret passageway between the <a href="/wiki/Hog%27s_Head" class="mw-redirect" title="Hog&#39;s Head">Hog's Head</a> and the <a href="/wiki/Room_of_Requirement" title="Room of Requirement">Room of Requirement</a>, so sealed it to prevent any <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a> from breaching the school, as <a href="/wiki/Aberforth_Dumbledore" title="Aberforth Dumbledore">Aberforth Dumbledore</a> was no longer protecting it. Augusta expressed great pride in her grandson for following in his parents' footsteps.<sup id="cite_ref-DH31_8-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH31-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup> Augusta fought during the battle and survived.<sup id="cite_ref-DHF2_15-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DHF2-15">&#91;15&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Little is known of Augusta's life after the war. Her grandson Neville eventually married <a href="/wiki/Hannah_Abbott" title="Hannah Abbott">Hannah Abbott</a><sup id="cite_ref-PM_16-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PM-16">&#91;16&#93;</a></sup> and took up a position as <a href="/wiki/Professor" title="Professor">Professor</a> of <a href="/wiki/Herbology" title="Herbology">Herbology</a> at Hogwarts.<sup id="cite_ref-DHE_17-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DHE-17">&#91;17&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Physical_appearance">Physical appearance</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Augusta_Longbottom?veaction=edit&amp;section=5" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Physical appearance">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Augusta_Longbottom?action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Physical appearance"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Augusta Longbottom was described as a formidable looking witch. She was tall, thin and bony. She commonly wore a hat with a stuffed vulture on it and carried a bright red handbag.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP23_6-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP23-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-POA7_18-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-POA7-18">&#91;18&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Personality_and_traits">Personality and traits</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Augusta_Longbottom?veaction=edit&amp;section=6" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Personality and traits">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Augusta_Longbottom?action=edit&amp;section=6" title="Edit section: Personality and traits"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<div class="quote">
<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince">"<i>That Harry Potter's got more backbone than the whole <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">Ministry of Magic</a> put together!</i>"</span></dd>
<dd>&#8212;Augusta's views on the Ministry<sup class="noprint"><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Half-Blood_Prince" title="Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince">[src]</a></sup></dd>
<dd></dd></dl></div><p>Augusta Longbottom was a severe woman who frequently claimed that her grandson, Neville, lacked the talents that his parents had possessed. She was angry when she realised Neville had never told anyone about why he lived with her, thinking that he was ashamed of his parents. Nevertheless, Augusta grew proud of her grandson after discovering how dedicated he was to defeating Lord Voldemort. She was civil towards <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>, <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ronald Weasley</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione Granger</a> when she met them in St Mungo's.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP23_6-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP23-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup> She might be somewhat biased towards what she is proud of, as she found her grandson's initial lack of talent as a failure of inheriting his father's prestige, and looked down on the idea of Neville studying <a href="/wiki/Charms_(class)" title="Charms (class)">charms class</a>, simply because she failed her <a href="/wiki/Ordinary_Wizarding_Level" title="Ordinary Wizarding Level">O.W.L.</a> in this subject herself, and instead favoured <a href="/wiki/Transfiguration_(class)" title="Transfiguration (class)">transfiguration</a>, most likely due to her own skill and success in it.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP9_9-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP9-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>She seemed to have a very strong judgement of morality, as she chose to believe Harry and <a href="/wiki/Albus_Dumbledore" title="Albus Dumbledore">Albus Dumbledore</a>'s honest claims that <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a> had <a href="/wiki/Return_of_Lord_Voldemort" title="Return of Lord Voldemort">returned</a> over the <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">Ministry's</a> incredibly biased and dishonest denial. She even ceased her subscription of the <i><a href="/wiki/Daily_Prophet" title="Daily Prophet">Daily Prophet</a></i> in angry protest for backing the Ministry's <a href="/wiki/Campaign_to_discredit_Albus_Dumbledore_and_Harry_Potter" title="Campaign to discredit Albus Dumbledore and Harry Potter">campaign to antagonise the truth</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP11_7-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP11-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Despite her elderly age (as Neville described her as a "little old witch, living alone"), she showed tremendous loyalty, bravery and logical thinking, as she easily and swiftly defeated the much younger <a href="/wiki/Auror" title="Auror">Auror</a> <a href="/wiki/John_Dawlish" title="John Dawlish">John Dawlish</a> who was holding her hostage and fled,<sup id="cite_ref-DH29_14-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH29-14">&#91;14&#93;</a></sup> and participated in the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a> without fear nor hesitancy.<sup id="cite_ref-DH31_8-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH31-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Magical_abilities_and_skills">Magical abilities and skills</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Augusta_Longbottom?veaction=edit&amp;section=7" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Augusta_Longbottom?action=edit&amp;section=7" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<div class="quote">
<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;J.K. Rowling PotterCast Interview">"<i>I really saw Mrs Longbottom as a powerful witch.</i>"</span></dd>
<dd>&#8212;<a href="/wiki/J._K._Rowling" title="J. K. Rowling">J. K. Rowling</a><sup class="noprint"><a href="/wiki/J.K._Rowling_PotterCast_Interview" title="J.K. Rowling PotterCast Interview">[src]</a></sup></dd>
<dd></dd></dl></div><p>Augusta Longbottom was considered a highly talented and powerful <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch</a>. Much of her skill lay in <a href="/wiki/Martial_magic" title="Martial magic">martial magic</a> and she was a highly formidable <a href="/wiki/Duelling" title="Duelling">duellist</a>, capable of overpowering <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a> and the <a href="/wiki/Auror" title="Auror">Auror</a> <a href="/wiki/John_Dawlish" title="John Dawlish">John Dawlish</a> who where much younger than her.<sup id="cite_ref-DH29_14-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH29-14">&#91;14&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-DH31_8-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH31-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup> Augusta's age did not interfere with her magical abilities, in a similar fashion with <a href="/wiki/Minerva_McGonagall" title="Minerva McGonagall">Minerva McGonagall</a>, and both witches survived the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a> when much younger witches and wizards perished. Despite the fact that she failed her <a href="/wiki/Charms_(class)" title="Charms (class)">Charms</a> <a href="/wiki/Ordinary_Wizarding_Level" title="Ordinary Wizarding Level">O.W.L</a>, Augusta must have been proficient enough in the various protective enchantments needed to survive whilst she was on the run. It is also probable that she was good with <a href="/wiki/Transfiguration" title="Transfiguration">Transfiguration</a>, as she was very insistent with her grandson taking the <a href="/wiki/Transfiguration_(class)" title="Transfiguration (class)">subject</a> at <a href="/wiki/Nastily_Exhausting_Wizarding_Test" title="Nastily Exhausting Wizarding Test">N.E.W.T.</a>-level.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP9_9-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP9-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup>
</p><ul><li><b><a href="/wiki/Duelling" title="Duelling">Duelling</a></b>: Despite her old age, Augusta Longbottom was an incredibly skilled duellist who the Death Eaters severely underestimated, as she easily defeated and injured <a href="/wiki/John_Dawlish" title="John Dawlish">John Dawlish</a>, a highly trained and skilled Auror, badly enough to result in him to be sent to <a href="/wiki/St_Mungo%27s_Hospital_for_Magical_Maladies_and_Injuries" title="St Mungo&#39;s Hospital for Magical Maladies and Injuries">St Mungo's Hospital for Magical Maladies and Injuries</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-DH29_14-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH29-14">&#91;14&#93;</a></sup> although Dawlish had not been fighting at full potency from having become "punch drunk" from being the favourite punching bag of the Order of the Phoenix. She also bravely participated in and survived the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-DH31_8-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH31-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-DHF2_15-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DHF2-15">&#91;15&#93;</a></sup> likely defeating many Death Eaters in process, showing her remarkable proficiency in <a href="/wiki/Martial_magic" title="Martial magic">martial magic</a>.</li></ul>

<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Neville_Longbottom">Neville Longbottom</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Augusta_Longbottom?veaction=edit&amp;section=9" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Neville Longbottom">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Augusta_Longbottom?action=edit&amp;section=9" title="Edit section: Neville Longbottom"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<div class="quote">

<p>Augusta was the guardian of Neville and raised him from a young age. During the early years of Neville's life, Augusta often thought that Neville was a <a href="/wiki/Squib" title="Squib">Squib</a> and was afraid he would not get into <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts</a>. She was extremely proud when he did but it was apparent that Augusta placed high expectations on Neville and was yet to be certain that he had substantial power.
</p><p>Augusta often reprimanded her grandson and sent Neville a <a href="/wiki/Howler" title="Howler">Howler</a> on at least two occasions.<sup id="cite_ref-COS5_19-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COS5-19">&#91;19&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-POA14_20-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-POA14-20">&#91;20&#93;</a></sup> She pushed Neville to be like his parents by doing well in school, especially in <a href="/wiki/Transfiguration" title="Transfiguration">Transfiguration</a> (a class Neville hated).<sup id="cite_ref-HBP9_9-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP9-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup> However, Neville loved Augusta and she cared a great deal for him, however stern she could be.
</p><p>In later years, she became proud of Neville as she realised how excellent a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> and moral person he was. She was shown commenting in the <i><a href="/wiki/Daily_Prophet" title="Daily Prophet">Daily Prophet</a></i> about his powers and during the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a>, she was proud to fight alongside him.<sup id="cite_ref-DH31_8-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH31-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup> After the end of the <a href="/wiki/Second_Wizarding_War" title="Second Wizarding War">Second Wizarding War</a> Neville married Hannah Abbott though it is unknown whether they had any children. Neville also replaced <a href="/wiki/Pomona_Sprout" title="Pomona Sprout">Professor Sprout</a> as the <a href="/wiki/Herbology" title="Herbology">Herbology</a> professor at Hogwarts, which would have truly made his grandmother proud.<sup id="cite_ref-DHE_17-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DHE-17">&#91;17&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<p>It is unknown what her relationship with Frank and Alice was before they were <a href="/wiki/Cruciatus_Curse" title="Cruciatus Curse">tortured</a>, but it must have been fairly close as Augusta was chosen to raise their infant son after they were no longer able to. After the <a href="/wiki/Attack_on_Frank_and_Alice_Longbottom" title="Attack on Frank and Alice Longbottom">attack</a>, Mrs Longbottom seemed to <a href="/wiki/Love" title="Love">love</a> her son and daughter-in-law, often taking Neville to visit them at <a href="/wiki/St_Mungo%27s" class="mw-redirect" title="St Mungo&#39;s">St Mungo's</a>. However, at times, she seemed to tire of providing for them, as she sounded weary when speaking to Alice and advised Neville to throw away a gum wrapper Alice gave him. However, it is assumed that she still cared for them as she obviously respected them and said numerous times that she wanted Neville to be more like them.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP23_6-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP23-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup>

<p>Augusta Longbottom was shown to highly respect <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> and demonstrated this by her support for his and <a href="/wiki/Albus_Dumbledore" title="Albus Dumbledore">Albus Dumbledore</a>'s honest claims that Lord Voldemort had indeed <a href="/wiki/Return_of_Lord_Voldemort" title="Return of Lord Voldemort">returned</a>. </p>

<p>Augusta Longbottom naturally respected Albus Dumbledore, considering that she allowed both her son and daughter-in-law to join the <a href="/wiki/Order_of_the_Phoenix" title="Order of the Phoenix">Order of the Phoenix</a> that Dumbledore led out of obvious trust of Dumbledore's leadership and despite Alicia and Frank becoming insane for their reputation as members of the Order, Augusta remained as respectful and loyal to Dumbledore as ever, showing she never blamed him for having failed to protect them. Augusta was utterly disgusted by then Ministry of Magic's blatantly dishonest slandering of Dumbledore and quickly cancelled her Daily Prophet subscription over her anger at them publishing Ministry propaganda. Augusta likely was devastated when Albus died and attended his funeral and would prove her loyalty to the great wizard by courageously fighting in the Battle of Hogwarts.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Augusta_Longbottom?veaction=edit&amp;section=14" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Augusta_Longbottom?action=edit&amp;section=14" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p><i>Augusta</i> is a Latin name and the feminine form of <i>Augustus</i>, meaning "great" or "venerable," derived from the Latin verb <i>augere</i>, "to increase."<sup id="cite_ref-22" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-22">&#91;22&#93;</a></sup> Augustus was the name given to the first emperor of Rome, and subsequently <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Augusta" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Augusta">Augusta</a> was sometimes used as a title for empresses of the Roman and Byzantine empires.
</p>
