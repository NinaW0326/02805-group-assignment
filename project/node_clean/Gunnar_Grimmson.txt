<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Gunnar_Grimmson?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Gunnar_Grimmson?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>After <a href="/wiki/Newton_Scamander" title="Newton Scamander">Newton Scamander</a> refused to assist the <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">British Ministry of Magic</a>, Grimmson was hired to travel to <a href="/wiki/Paris" title="Paris">Paris</a> in order to kill <a href="/wiki/Credence_Barebone" title="Credence Barebone">Credence Barebone</a>. Grimmson also, however, secretly received instructions from <a href="/wiki/Gellert_Grindelwald" title="Gellert Grindelwald">Gellert Grindelwald</a> to kill <a href="/wiki/Irma_Dugard" title="Irma Dugard">Irma Dugard</a>, to deprive Credence of the answers he was seeking and to push him into Grindelwald's arms. Grimmson accomplished his task during a swift <a href="/wiki/Attack_on_Irma_Dugard" title="Attack on Irma Dugard">attack</a>. Credence attempted to stop and slay Grimmson in revenge, but the latter was able to maintain a particularly strong protective <a href="/wiki/Shield_Charm" title="Shield Charm">Shield Charm</a> to protect himself from Credence's <a href="/wiki/Obscurial" title="Obscurial">Obscurial</a> form long enough to <a href="/wiki/Apparition" title="Apparition">Disapparate</a> and escape.<sup id="cite_ref-COGS_1-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COGS-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Personality_and_traits">Personality and traits</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Gunnar_Grimmson?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Personality and traits">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Gunnar_Grimmson?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Personality and traits"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Due to his status as a <a href="/wiki/Bounty_hunter" title="Bounty hunter">Bounty hunter</a> for the <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">British Ministry of Magic</a>, Gunnar Grimmson was an intensely malevolent and sinister individual, with a vocal and mutual dislike of <a href="/wiki/Newton_Scamander" title="Newton Scamander">Newt Scamander</a>, and seemingly only being loyal to <a href="/wiki/Gellert_Grindelwald" title="Gellert Grindelwald">Gellert Grindelwald</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-COGS_1-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COGS-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Indeed, Grimmson was the polar opposite of <a href="/wiki/Newton_Scamander" title="Newton Scamander">Newt Scamander</a> - Newt was a kindly caretaker of <a href="/wiki/Beast" title="Beast">beasts</a>, while Grimmson was a vicious beast hunter. He was also a cold-blooded and merciless killer, as he coldly and swiftly murdered <a href="/wiki/Irma_Dugard" title="Irma Dugard">Irma Dugard</a> with the <a href="/wiki/Killing_Curse" title="Killing Curse">Killing Curse</a> in order to fulfil Grindelwald's wishes, showing him to be a very dangerous <a href="/wiki/Dark_Wizard" title="Dark Wizard">Dark Wizard</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-COGS_1-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COGS-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Magical_abilities_and_skills">Magical abilities and skills</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Gunnar_Grimmson?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Gunnar_Grimmson?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li><b><a href="/wiki/Bounty_hunter" title="Bounty hunter">Bounty hunter</a> skills</b>: Gunnar Grimmson was a highly skilled bounty hunter, since the <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">British Ministry of Magic</a> trusted him with the important mission of tracking down and killing the extremely dangerous <a href="/wiki/Obscurial" title="Obscurial">Obscurial</a> <a href="/wiki/Credence_Barebone" title="Credence Barebone">Credence Barebone</a>, while <a href="/wiki/Gellert_Grindelwald" title="Gellert Grindelwald">Gellert Grindelwald</a> trusted Grimmson to kill <a href="/wiki/Irma_Dugard" title="Irma Dugard">Irma Dugard</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-COGS_1-10" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COGS-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup></li></ul>
<figure class="thumb tright show-info-icon" style="width: 182px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/e/e8/Grimmson_kills_Irma.gif/revision/latest?cb=20190820164605" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/e/e8/Grimmson_kills_Irma.gif/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/180?cb=20190820164605" decoding="async" width="180" height="75" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="Grimmson kills Irma.gif" data-image-key="Grimmson_kills_Irma.gif" /></a> 	 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:Grimmson_kills_Irma.gif" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption">Grimmson ruthlessly and swiftly killing Irma Durgard</p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure>
<ul><li><b><a href="/wiki/Dark_Arts" title="Dark Arts">Dark Arts</a></b>: Grimmson, as a bounty hunter, was highly proficient in the Dark Arts, swiftly casting the <a href="/wiki/Killing_Curse" title="Killing Curse">Killing Curse</a> which killed Irma Dugard. Casting the <a href="/wiki/Unforgivable_Curses" title="Unforgivable Curses">Unforgivable Curses</a> require considerable <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magical</a> skill and malice to perform correctly. Grimmson also used a <a href="/wiki/Spell" title="Spell">spell</a> or <a href="/wiki/Curse" title="Curse">curse</a> that made Nagini stick to the wall, with seemingly no way to escape until Credence destroyed the wall she was stuck in.<sup id="cite_ref-COGS_1-11" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COGS-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Charm" title="Charm">Charms</a></b>: Grimmson seemed to be very skilled with charms, as he was able to cast and maintain an extremely powerful <a href="/wiki/Shield_Charm" title="Shield Charm">Shield Charm</a>, enough to block the devastating attacks of Credence's <a href="/wiki/Obscurus" title="Obscurus">Obscurus</a> long enough to Disapparate away to safety.<sup id="cite_ref-COGS_1-12" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COGS-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Apparition" title="Apparition">Apparition</a></b>: Grimmson, like most adult wizards, was capable of Apparating at will, though he seemed more skilled than most, managing to Disapparate from Credence without relinquishing his Shield Charm until the very moment he was gone, without losing concentration.<sup id="cite_ref-COGS_1-13" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COGS-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup></li></ul>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Gunnar_Grimmson?veaction=edit&amp;section=4" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Gunnar_Grimmson?action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>Gunnar is a name of Nordic origin (Gunnarr in Old Norse), meaning &#160;fighter, soldier, and attacker, and stem from an old Viking saying which means "Brave and bold warrior". (G<i>unnr</i>&#160;"war" and&#160;<i>arr</i>&#160;"warrior").</li>
<li>Grimmson is a patronymic name of Icelandic origin meaning "son of Grímur," ultimately meaning "fierce", "to thunder," or (in the context of an alternate name for Odin), "mask."</li></ul>
