<b>Podric Batworthy</b>, <span class="template-c"><a href="/wiki/Order_of_Merlin" title="Order of Merlin">O.M. (First Class)</a></span>, <span class="template-c">(<a href="/wiki/Grand_Sorcerer" title="Grand Sorcerer">Grand Sorc.</a>)</span>, was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> whose <a href="/wiki/Portrait" title="Portrait">portrait</a> hung at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Castle" title="Hogwarts Castle">Hogwarts Castle</a> in the <a href="/wiki/1990s" title="1990s">1990s</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-HPLE_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HPLE-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> Batworthy lived in or after the <a href="/wiki/15th_century" title="15th century">15th century</a>, seeing as that was when the <a href="/wiki/Wizengamot" title="Wizengamot">Wizengamot</a> created the <a href="/wiki/Order_of_Merlin" title="Order of Merlin">Order of Merlin</a> award.<sup id="cite_ref-pottermore-ordermerlin_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-pottermore-ordermerlin-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Podric Batworthy is, in all likelihood, an ancestor of <a href="/wiki/Podric_Batworthy_XXIII" title="Podric Batworthy XXIII">Podric Batworthy XXIII</a>, who ran <a href="/wiki/Beard_Trimming" title="Beard Trimming">Beard Trimming</a> in <a href="/wiki/Diagon_Alley" title="Diagon Alley">Diagon Alley</a> in the <a href="/wiki/1990s" title="1990s">1990s</a>.
</p>
