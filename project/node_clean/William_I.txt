<b>William I</b> (c. <a href="/wiki/1028" class="mw-redirect" title="1028">1028</a> - <a href="/wiki/1087" class="mw-redirect" title="1087">1087</a>), also known as <b>William the Conqueror</b>, was the first <a href="/wiki/Normans" title="Normans">Norman</a> <a href="/wiki/King" title="King">King</a> of <a href="/wiki/England" title="England">England</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-wikipedia_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-wikipedia-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-malfoy_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-malfoy-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="History">History</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/William_I?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: History">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/William_I?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: History"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>He led the <a href="/wiki/Norman_conquest" title="Norman conquest">Norman conquest</a> of England in <a href="/wiki/1066" class="mw-redirect" title="1066">1066</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-wikipedia_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-wikipedia-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-malfoy_2-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-malfoy-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> The <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> <a href="/wiki/Armand_Malfoy" title="Armand Malfoy">Armand Malfoy</a> was among his invading army.<sup id="cite_ref-malfoy_2-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-malfoy-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> Sometime after being crowned king, William granted Malfoy a <a href="/wiki/Malfoy_Manor" title="Malfoy Manor">piece of land</a> in <a href="/wiki/Wiltshire" title="Wiltshire">Wiltshire</a>, in return for questionable services which Armand had rendered him.<sup id="cite_ref-malfoy_2-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-malfoy-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
