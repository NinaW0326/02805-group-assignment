<b>Percival Shacklehorn</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Great_Britain" title="Great Britain">British</a> <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> and self-professed adventurer. In actuality an unscrupulous <a href="/wiki/Wizard_poacher" title="Wizard poacher">poacher</a>, he was at one point fined by the <a href="/wiki/Ministry_of_Magic" title="Ministry of Magic">Ministry of Magic</a> for illegally hunting a <a href="/wiki/Graphorn" title="Graphorn">Graphorn</a>, a <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magical</a> <a href="/wiki/Creature" title="Creature">creature</a> that was endangered during the <a href="/wiki/1920s" title="1920s">1920s</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Percival_Shacklehorn?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Percival_Shacklehorn?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>He went to the <a href="/wiki/Austria" title="Austria">Austrian</a> <a href="/wiki/Alps" title="Alps">Alps</a> in the hopes of capturing a <a href="/wiki/Graphorn" title="Graphorn">Graphorn</a>. While there, a <a href="/wiki/Mountain_Troll" class="mw-redirect" title="Mountain Troll">Mountain Troll</a> found its way into his <a href="/wiki/Percival_Shacklehorn%27s_tent" title="Percival Shacklehorn&#39;s tent">magically-expanded tent</a>, knocking him unconscious in the process.
</p><p>The <a href="/wiki/Creature" title="Creature">creature</a> broke a bottle of <a href="/wiki/Invisibility_Potion" title="Invisibility Potion">Invisibility Potion</a> that Shacklehorn, a competent <a href="/wiki/Potioneer" title="Potioneer">potioneer</a>, had brewed to aid him in hunting the Graphorn. This rendered the beast invisible when <a href="/wiki/Laura_Thorn" title="Laura Thorn">Laura Thorn</a>, a <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggle</a> monster hunter investigating a <a href="/wiki/Yeti" title="Yeti">Yeti</a> sighting, chanced into Shacklehorn's tent (on which he had failed to place <a href="/wiki/Muggle-Repelling_Charm" title="Muggle-Repelling Charm">Muggle-Repelling Charms</a>). The tent was <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magically</a> transported back to the <a href="/wiki/Leaky_Cauldron" title="Leaky Cauldron">Leaky Cauldron</a> in <a href="/wiki/London" title="London">London</a>, taking its three, unlikely occupants with it.
</p><p>Shacklehorn initially claimed he was in the Alps to view Graphorns in their natural habitat when questioned by <a href="/wiki/Mathilda_Grimblehawk" title="Mathilda Grimblehawk">Mathilda Grimblehawk</a> and <a href="/wiki/Mathilda_Grimblehawk%27s_partner" title="Mathilda Grimblehawk&#39;s partner">her partner</a>. However, upon further interrogation, he admitted that he had been trying to catch one. He was fined by the <a href="/wiki/Ministry_of_Magic" title="Ministry of Magic">Ministry of Magic</a> for illegally hunting a magical creature. Grimblehawk and her partner were able to capture the <a href="/wiki/Troll" title="Troll">troll</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Shacklehorn was, apparently, a supporter of <a href="/wiki/Puddlemere_United" title="Puddlemere United">Puddlemere United</a>, as he kept a poster of that <a href="/wiki/Quidditch" title="Quidditch">Quidditch</a> <a href="/wiki/Quidditch_team" title="Quidditch team">team</a> in his tent.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Magical_abilities_and_skills">Magical abilities and skills</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Percival_Shacklehorn?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Percival_Shacklehorn?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li><b><a href="/wiki/Potion" title="Potion">Potions</a></b>: Shacklehorn was proficient in the <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magical</a> art and <a href="/wiki/Science" title="Science">science</a> of potion-making, having successfully brewed a batch of the <a href="/wiki/Invisibility_potion" class="mw-redirect" title="Invisibility potion">Invisibility potion</a>&#160;for his travels.&#160;</li></ul>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Possessions">Possessions</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Percival_Shacklehorn?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Possessions">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Percival_Shacklehorn?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Possessions"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li><b><a href="/wiki/Percival_Shacklehorn%27s_socks" title="Percival Shacklehorn&#39;s socks">Socks</a></b>: Shacklehorn owned a pair of socks which were said to have had an unpleasant and sweaty odour.</li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Percival_Shacklehorn%27s_suitcase" title="Percival Shacklehorn&#39;s suitcase">Suitcase</a></b>: Shacklehorn owned a suitcase, which he took with him on his travels to contain his possessions and clothing.</li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Percival_Shacklehorn%27s_tent" title="Percival Shacklehorn&#39;s tent">Tent</a></b>: Shacklehorn owned a tent, which was <a href="/wiki/Charm" title="Charm">charmed</a> to be much larger on the inside than it appeared to be on the outside. Its interior included columns, a staircase, multiple armchairs, wooden tables, and a <a href="/wiki/Grandfather_clock" title="Grandfather clock">Grandfather clock</a>.</li>
<li><b>Net</b>: Shacklehorn owned a net which he attempted to use to capture a <a href="/wiki/Graphorn" title="Graphorn">Graphorn</a>.</li></ul>
