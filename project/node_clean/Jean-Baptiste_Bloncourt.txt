<p><b>Jean-Baptiste Bloncourt</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Haiti" title="Haiti">Haitian</a> <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> who played as a <a href="/wiki/Beater" title="Beater">Beater</a> for the <a href="/wiki/2014" title="2014">2014</a> <a href="/wiki/Haitian_National_Quidditch_team" title="Haitian National Quidditch team">Haitian National Quidditch team</a>. During the third match of the <a href="/wiki/2014_Quidditch_World_Cup" title="2014 Quidditch World Cup">427th Quidditch World Cup</a>, Haiti versus <a href="/wiki/Brazilian_National_Quidditch_team" title="Brazilian National Quidditch team">Brazil</a>, Jean-Baptiste Bloncourt showed his ineptitude, and cost his team the championship.<sup id="cite_ref-PM_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PM-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Jean-Baptiste_Bloncourt?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Jean-Baptiste_Bloncourt?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>In the first hour of the match, Bloncourt mis-hit a ferocious <a href="/wiki/Bludger" title="Bludger">Bludger</a>, sending it hurtling toward his own teammate, <a href="/wiki/Keeper" title="Keeper">Keeper</a> <a href="/wiki/Lenelle_Paraison" title="Lenelle Paraison">Lenelle Paraison</a>. Paraison was left with a broken nose.<sup id="cite_ref-PM_2-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PM-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> Four hours into the match, the Haitian <a href="/wiki/Seeker" title="Seeker">Seeker</a> <a href="/wiki/Sylvian_Jolicoeur" title="Sylvian Jolicoeur">Sylvian Jolicoeur</a> came within inches of capturing the Snitch and securing the win, but another of Bloncourt's misaimed <a href="/wiki/Bludger" title="Bludger">Bludgers</a> knocked him out cold. To make matters worse, the <a href="/wiki/Golden_Snitch" title="Golden Snitch">Snitch</a> proceeded to get caught up in Bloncourt's sleeve, who incurred in <a href="/wiki/Snitchnip" title="Snitchnip">Snitchnip</a> when he wrestled it out of his undergarments and grabbed it. The team was immediately disqualified.<sup id="cite_ref-PM_2-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PM-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> Following the match, Bloncourt was reported to be hiding in an undisclosed location.<sup id="cite_ref-PM_2-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PM-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
