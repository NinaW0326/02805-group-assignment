<p><b>Alderton</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Muggle-born" title="Muggle-born">Muggle-born</a> or <a href="/wiki/Half-blood" title="Half-blood">half-blood</a> wizard who was sent to <a href="/wiki/Azkaban" title="Azkaban">Azkaban</a> for "stealing <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magic</a>" by the <a href="/wiki/Muggle-Born_Registration_Commission" title="Muggle-Born Registration Commission">Muggle-Born Registration Commission</a> in <a href="/wiki/1997" title="1997">1997</a>, while <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a> was in control of the <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">Ministry of Magic</a> during the height of the <a href="/wiki/Second_Wizarding_War" title="Second Wizarding War">Second Wizarding War</a>. He told <a href="/wiki/Dolores_Umbridge" title="Dolores Umbridge">Dolores Umbridge</a> and <a href="/wiki/Corban_Yaxley" title="Corban Yaxley">Yaxley</a> that he was the son of <a href="/wiki/Arkie_Alderton" title="Arkie Alderton">Arkie Alderton</a>, who was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> known for designing <a href="/wiki/Broomstick" title="Broomstick">broomsticks</a>, but he was not believed. Alderton only stopped screaming desperately when he was threatened with the <a href="/wiki/Dementor%27s_Kiss" title="Dementor&#39;s Kiss">Dementor's Kiss</a>, but his <i>"dry sobs echoed through the corridor"</i> as he was dragged away to prison.<sup id="cite_ref-DH13_1-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH13-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> He was likely released after <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort's</a> final defeat.<sup id="cite_ref-DH36_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH36-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
