<b>Pitts</b> was a <a href="/wiki/House-elf" title="House-elf">house-elf</a> employed at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a>. He was previously owned by another person, who treated him cruelly and gave him to the Hogwarts kitchens for next to nothing.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> According to <a href="/wiki/Rita_Skeeter" title="Rita Skeeter">Rita Skeeter</a>, <a href="/wiki/Jacob_(Hogwarts_student)" title="Jacob (Hogwarts student)">Jacob</a> spoke to him shortly before his expulsion.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> He was strict when it came to making sure other house-elves and students in detention did their work in the kitchens.
</p>
