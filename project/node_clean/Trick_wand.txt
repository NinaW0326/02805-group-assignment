<p><b>Trick Wands</b>, also known as <b>Fake Wands</b>,<sup id="cite_ref-PM_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PM-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> are joke objects that were created by <a href="/wiki/Fred_and_George_Weasley" title="Fred and George Weasley">Fred and George Weasley</a> and sold in the shop they founded, <a href="/wiki/Weasleys%27_Wizard_Wheezes" title="Weasleys&#39; Wizard Wheezes">Weasleys' Wizard Wheezes</a>. These wands appear normal in every way, but when used to cast a <a href="/wiki/Spell" title="Spell">spell</a>, they <a href="/wiki/Transformation" title="Transformation">turn</a> into random objects (e.g. rubber <a href="/wiki/Chicken_(animal)" title="Chicken (animal)">chickens</a>). A more expensive version beats the user around the head and neck.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="History">History</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Trick_wand?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: History">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Trick_wand?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: History"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>In 1994, <a href="/wiki/Molly_Weasley" title="Molly Weasley">Mrs Molly Weasley</a> got very frustrated when she picked a stray one of these up even though she had banned them from their <a href="/wiki/The_Burrow" title="The Burrow">house</a>, the Burrow.<sup id="cite_ref-GOF5_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF5-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>At the <a href="/wiki/Quidditch_World_Cup" title="Quidditch World Cup">Quidditch World Cup</a>, <a href="/wiki/Ludovic_Bagman" title="Ludovic Bagman">Ludo Bagman</a> found the wands very amusing and offered to pay <a href="/wiki/Fred_and_George_Weasley" title="Fred and George Weasley">Fred and George Weasley</a> five <a href="/wiki/Galleon" title="Galleon">Galleons</a> for one.<sup id="cite_ref-GOF7_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF7-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Just before <a href="/wiki/Minerva_McGonagall" title="Minerva McGonagall">Professor McGonagall</a> announced the <a href="/wiki/Yule_Ball" title="Yule Ball">Yule Ball</a> at Hogwarts in 1994, <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> and <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ron Weasley</a> were having a sword fight with a couple of trick wands in <a href="/wiki/Transfiguration_(class)" title="Transfiguration (class)">Transfiguration class</a> Ron's had turned into a tin <a href="/wiki/Parrot" title="Parrot">parrot</a>, with Harry's becoming a rubber <a href="/wiki/Haddock" title="Haddock">haddock</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-GOF22_4-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF22-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
