<b>Minister Priscilla Dupont</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Great_Britain" title="Great Britain">British</a> <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch</a> and the <a href="/wiki/Minister_for_Magic" title="Minister for Magic">Minister for Magic</a> from <a href="/wiki/1855" class="mw-redirect" title="1855">1855</a> to <a href="/wiki/17_February" title="17 February">17 February</a>, <a href="/wiki/1858" class="mw-redirect" title="1858">1858</a>.  She was elected Minister likely only once.<sup id="cite_ref-PM_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PM-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Priscilla_Dupont?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Priscilla_Dupont?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Dupont was elected as the <a href="/wiki/Minister_for_Magic" title="Minister for Magic">Minister for Magic</a> of <a href="/wiki/Great_Britain" title="Great Britain">Great Britain</a> in <a href="/wiki/1855" class="mw-redirect" title="1855">1855</a>. Upon her accession to the office, she developed an irrational loathing of the <a href="/wiki/Prime_Minister_(title)" title="Prime Minister (title)">Muggle Prime Minister</a>, <a href="/wiki/Lord_Palmerston" title="Lord Palmerston">Lord Palmerston</a>. This attained such proportions that it ended up in her being forced to step down on <a href="/wiki/17_February" title="17 February">17 February</a>, <a href="/wiki/1858" class="mw-redirect" title="1858">1858</a>, after such incidents as <a href="/wiki/Muggle_money" title="Muggle money">coins</a> turning to <a href="/wiki/Frogspawn" title="Frogspawn">frogspawn</a> in Lord Palmerston's coat pockets. Ironically, Palmerston was forced to resign by the Muggles two days later on <a href="/wiki/19_February" title="19 February">19 February</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-PM_2-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PM-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Magical_abilities_and_skills">Magical abilities and skills</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Priscilla_Dupont?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Priscilla_Dupont?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li><b><a href="/wiki/Transfiguration" title="Transfiguration">Transfiguration</a></b>: Dupont possessed a degree of proficiency in this branch of <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magic</a>, as she was capable of <a href="/wiki/Transforming_Spells" title="Transforming Spells">turning coins</a> into <a href="/wiki/Frogspawn" title="Frogspawn">Frogspawn</a> in the pockets of <a href="/wiki/Lord_Palmerston" title="Lord Palmerston">Lord Palmerston</a> successfully.</li></ul>
