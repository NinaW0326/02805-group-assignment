<b>Paul Males</b> (born c. <a href="/wiki/1970s" title="1970s">1970s</a>) was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> who was attending <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> during the <a href="/wiki/1991%E2%80%931992_school_year" title="1991–1992 school year">1991–1992 school year</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-MOHP_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-MOHP-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>In <a href="/wiki/1991" title="1991">1991</a>, he received a letter via <a href="/wiki/Owl_Postal_Service" title="Owl Postal Service">owl post</a>, incorrectly addressed to him at "<i>School Of Witchcraft &amp; Wizzardry</i>".<sup id="cite_ref-MOHP_2-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-MOHP-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
