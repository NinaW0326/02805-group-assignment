<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Andrew_Kirke?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Andrew_Kirke?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Andrew became a <a href="/wiki/Beater" title="Beater">Beater</a> on the <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor" title="Gryffindor">Gryffindor</a> <a href="/wiki/Quidditch" title="Quidditch">Quidditch</a> <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor_Quidditch_team" title="Gryffindor Quidditch team">team</a>, along with <a href="/wiki/Jack_Sloper" title="Jack Sloper">Jack Sloper</a>, in <a href="/wiki/1995" title="1995">1995</a>. They took over the positions after <a href="/wiki/Fred_and_George_Weasley" title="Fred and George Weasley">Fred and George Weasley</a>, who were given a lifelong ban from <a href="/wiki/Quidditch" title="Quidditch">Quidditch</a> by <a href="/wiki/Dolores_Umbridge" title="Dolores Umbridge">Dolores Umbridge</a> (their bans were lifted right after Umbridge was removed from Hogwarts). <a href="/wiki/Angelina_Johnson" title="Angelina Johnson">Angelina Johnson</a>, the team Captain, was unimpressed by Andrew and Jack's performances during try-outs, but considered them to be better than the others who had tried out. <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ron Weasley</a> considered them to be dreadful players, even worse than he was.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP19_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP19-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>During <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor" title="Gryffindor">Gryffindor</a>'s quick defeat to <a href="/wiki/Hufflepuff" title="Hufflepuff">Hufflepuff</a>, Andrew had a pitiful performance; when <a href="/wiki/Zacharias_Smith" title="Zacharias Smith">Zacharias Smith</a> flew in his direction carrying the Quaffle, Andrew shrieked and fell off his broomstick. <a href="/wiki/Ginevra_Weasley" title="Ginevra Weasley">Ginny Weasley</a> caught the <a href="/wiki/Golden_Snitch" title="Golden Snitch">Golden Snitch</a> twenty-two minutes into the match, causing Gryffindor to lose by only ten points.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP26_4-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP26-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> Later, after the match with Ravenclaw, he and his teammates victoriously carried Ron.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP30_5-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP30-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Andrew_Kirke?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Andrew_Kirke?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>Andrew Kirke's name is possibly derived from <i>The Chronicles of Narnia</i> book <i>The Magician's Nephew,</i> which features a main character named Digory Kirke with a maternal uncle named Andrew Ketterley.
<ul><li>This is the same namesake of fellow <a href="/wiki/Portrait_of_a_Quidditch_player" title="Portrait of a Quidditch player">Quidditch player</a> <a href="/wiki/Cedric_Diggory" title="Cedric Diggory">Cedric Diggory</a>.</li></ul></li></ul>
