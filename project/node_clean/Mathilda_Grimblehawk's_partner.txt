<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Mathilda_Grimblehawk%27s_partner?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Mathilda_Grimblehawk%27s_partner?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Sometime after attending <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a><sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> they joined the Ministry as a new recruit. On their first day, they helped investigate the attack on <a href="/wiki/Gordon_Horton" title="Gordon Horton">Gordon Horton</a>, <a href="/wiki/Quidditch" title="Quidditch">Quidditch</a> <a href="/wiki/Keeper" title="Keeper">keeper</a> for the <a href="/wiki/Chudley_Cannons" title="Chudley Cannons">Chudley Cannons</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Magical_abilities_and_skills">Magical abilities and skills</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Mathilda_Grimblehawk%27s_partner?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Mathilda_Grimblehawk%27s_partner?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li><b><a href="/wiki/Charm" title="Charm">Charms</a></b>: They were proficient with charm-work, being able to successfully cast many useful charms including the <a href="/wiki/Scouring_Charm" title="Scouring Charm">Scouring Charm</a>, <a href="/wiki/Mending_Charm" title="Mending Charm">Mending Charm</a>, <a href="/wiki/Unlocking_Charm" title="Unlocking Charm">Unlocking Charm</a>, <a href="/wiki/Memory_Charm" title="Memory Charm">Memory Charm</a>, <a href="/wiki/Revelio_Charm" title="Revelio Charm">Revelio Charm</a> and <i><a href="/wiki/Tergeo" title="Tergeo">Tergeo</a></i>.</li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Study_of_Ancient_Runes" title="Study of Ancient Runes">Study of Ancient Runes</a></b>: They were able to decipher the runes in <a href="/wiki/Winifred_Whittle" title="Winifred Whittle">Winifred Whittle</a>'s journal, showing they were skilled and knowledgeable in the field of ancient <a href="/wiki/Rune" title="Rune">runes</a>.</li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Healing_magic" title="Healing magic">Healing magic</a></b>: They had considerable talent in the field of healing magic, given how they were able to successfully heal a <a href="/wiki/Grindylow" title="Grindylow">Grindylow</a>.</li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Magizoology" title="Magizoology">Magizoology</a></b>: This individual was knowledgeable of <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magical</a> <a href="/wiki/Creature" title="Creature">creatures</a> and worked as an employee in the <a href="/wiki/Department_for_the_Regulation_and_Control_of_Magical_Creatures" title="Department for the Regulation and Control of Magical Creatures">Department for the Regulation and Control of Magical Creatures</a> under the <a href="/wiki/Beast_Division" title="Beast Division">Beast Division</a>, and frequently dealt with various different <a href="/wiki/Beast" title="Beast">beasts</a> during their career fieldwork.</li></ul>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Possessions">Possessions</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Mathilda_Grimblehawk%27s_partner?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Possessions">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Mathilda_Grimblehawk%27s_partner?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Possessions"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li><b><a href="/wiki/Mathilda_Grimblehawk%27s_partner%27s_wand" title="Mathilda Grimblehawk&#39;s partner&#39;s wand">Wand</a></b>: It is presumed they purchased this <a href="/wiki/Wand" title="Wand">wand</a> at age eleven before attending <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a>. Their wand seemed to have a brass inlay and a very polished handle.</li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Magnifying_glass" title="Magnifying glass">Magnifying glass</a></b>: They seemed to own a magnifying glass, which they presumably used during their investigations.</li></ul>
