This <b>individual</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> and a <a href="/wiki/Snatchers" title="Snatchers">Snatcher</a>. In <a href="/wiki/1997" title="1997">1997</a> he and some fellow Snatchers patrolled <a href="/wiki/Diagon_Alley" title="Diagon Alley">Diagon Alley</a> in their search for <a href="/wiki/Muggle-born" title="Muggle-born">Muggle-borns</a>. He and his fellow Snatchers died when the <a href="/wiki/Wooden_Bridge" title="Wooden Bridge">Covered Bridge</a> were blown up by <a href="/wiki/Neville_Longbottom" title="Neville Longbottom">Neville Longbottom</a>, <a href="/wiki/Seamus_Finnigan" title="Seamus Finnigan">Seamus Finnigan</a> and other students.<sup id="cite_ref-DHF2_1-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DHF2-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-LEGOY2_2-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-LEGOY2-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
