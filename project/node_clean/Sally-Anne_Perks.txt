<b>Sally-Anne Perks</b> (c. <a href="/wiki/1979" title="1979">1979</a>-<a href="/wiki/1980" title="1980">1980</a>) was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch</a> who began attending <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> in <a href="/wiki/1991" title="1991">1991</a>, though she may not have stayed for her <a href="/wiki/Ordinary_Wizarding_Level" title="Ordinary Wizarding Level">Ordinary Wizarding Level</a> year.<sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> She was <a href="/wiki/Sorting_ceremony" title="Sorting ceremony">sorted</a> right before <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> and was either sorted into <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor" title="Gryffindor">Gryffindor</a>, <a href="/wiki/Hufflepuff" title="Hufflepuff">Hufflepuff</a>, or <a href="/wiki/Slytherin" title="Slytherin">Slytherin</a> house.<sup id="cite_ref-PS7_2-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PS7-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Sally-Anne_Perks?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Sally-Anne_Perks?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>Although Sally-Anne Perks was in the same year as Harry in <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Philosopher%27s_Stone" title="Harry Potter and the Philosopher&#39;s Stone">Harry Potter and the Philosopher's Stone</a></i>, there is no other mention of her in the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_(book_series)" title="Harry Potter (book series)">series</a>.
<ul><li>However, she may be the <a href="/wiki/Bespectacled_Slytherin_girl" title="Bespectacled Slytherin girl">unidentified bespectacled girl</a> or one of the unnamed Gryffindor girls in Harry's year, in <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Philosopher%27s_Stone_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Philosopher&#39;s Stone (film)">Harry Potter and the Philosopher's Stone (film)</a></i>. It is also possible that she was one of the two un-named Hufflepuff girls in Harry's year, since only <a href="/wiki/Susan_Bones" title="Susan Bones">Susan Bones</a>, <a href="/wiki/Hannah_Abbott" title="Hannah Abbott">Hannah Abbott</a> and <a href="/wiki/Megan_Jones" title="Megan Jones">Megan Jones</a> were mentioned to be sorted into Hufflepuff.</li></ul></li>
<li>Sally-Anne Perks was not called to take her test for a practical examination in the <a href="/wiki/1996" title="1996">1996</a> <a href="/wiki/Ordinary_Wizarding_Level" title="Ordinary Wizarding Level">O.W.L.s</a>. Her turn should have come after the <a href="/wiki/Padma_and_Parvati_Patil" title="Padma and Parvati Patil">Patil twins</a> but before <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>. This could just be a mistake of <a href="/wiki/J._K._Rowling" title="J. K. Rowling">J. K. Rowling</a>, or Sally-Anne may have been unable to attend her test. She could also have repeated one or more years, which would make her a fourth or younger year while Harry was in his fifth year.</li>
<li>The option for Perks as a family name for the protagonist of <i><a href="/wiki/Fantastic_Beasts:_Cases_from_the_Wizarding_World" title="Fantastic Beasts: Cases from the Wizarding World">Fantastic Beasts: Cases from the Wizarding World</a></i> suggests Sally-Anne may have been a <a href="/wiki/Half-blood" title="Half-blood">half-blood</a> or a <a href="/wiki/Pure-blood" title="Pure-blood">pure-blood</a>.</li></ul>
