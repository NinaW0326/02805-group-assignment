<p>The <b>President of the United States</b> is the elected <a href="/wiki/Muggle" class="mw-redirect" title="Muggle">No-Maj</a> head of state and head of government of the <a href="/wiki/United_States_of_America" title="United States of America">United States of America</a>, as well as the commander-in-chief of the <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/United_States_Armed_Forces" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:United States Armed Forces">United States Armed Forces</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Before his accidental death at the hand of an <a href="/wiki/Obscurus" title="Obscurus">Obscurus</a> in <a href="/wiki/1926" title="1926">1926</a>, <a href="/wiki/Henry_Shaw_Junior" title="Henry Shaw Junior">Henry Shaw Junior</a>, then-<a href="/wiki/United_States_Senate" title="United States Senate">Senator</a> for <a href="/wiki/New_York" title="New York">New York</a>, was speculated to be likely to become the President of the United States in a near future.<sup id="cite_ref-FB55_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-FB55-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> Also in <a href="/wiki/1926" title="1926">1926</a>, an improperly <a href="/wiki/Memory_Charm" title="Memory Charm">Obliviated</a> <a href="/wiki/Muggle" class="mw-redirect" title="Muggle">No-Maj</a> remarked that he thought the President (at that point, <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Calvin_Coolidge" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Calvin Coolidge">Calvin Coolidge</a>), was a <a href="/wiki/Bear" title="Bear">bear</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>In <a href="/wiki/1996" title="1996">1996</a>, President <a href="/wiki/Bill_Clinton" title="Bill Clinton">Bill Clinton</a> toured <a href="/wiki/Europe" title="Europe">Europe</a>, granting an exclusive interview to the <a href="/wiki/Great_Britain" title="Great Britain">British</a> newspaper <i><a href="/wiki/The_Guardian" title="The Guardian">The Guardian</a></i>.<sup id="cite_ref-HPLE_4-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HPLE-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> Around the same time, President <a href="/wiki/George_Bush" class="mw-redirect" title="George Bush">George Bush</a> awarded the <a href="/wiki/Congressional_Gold_Medal_of_Honor" title="Congressional Gold Medal of Honor">Congressional Gold Medal of Honor</a> to the then-current <a href="/wiki/Dalai_Lama" title="Dalai Lama">Dalai Lama</a>, a move that angered <a href="/wiki/China" title="China">China</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-HPLE_4-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HPLE-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
