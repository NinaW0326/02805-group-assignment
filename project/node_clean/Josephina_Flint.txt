<b>Minister Josephina Flint</b> was <a href="/wiki/Minister_for_Magic" title="Minister for Magic">Minister for Magic</a> from <a href="/wiki/1819" class="mw-redirect" title="1819">1819</a> to <a href="/wiki/1827" class="mw-redirect" title="1827">1827</a>.  She was elected at least twice.<sup id="cite_ref-WW_2-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-WW-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>She revealed an unhealthy anti-<a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggle</a> bias in office and disliked new Muggle technology such as the <a href="/wiki/Telegraph" title="Telegraph">telegraph</a>, which she claimed interfered with proper <a href="/wiki/Wand" title="Wand">wand</a> function.<sup id="cite_ref-WW_2-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-WW-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
