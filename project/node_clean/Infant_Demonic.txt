<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Infant_Demonic?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Infant_Demonic?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>They were part of the <a href="/wiki/Circus_Arcanus" title="Circus Arcanus">Circus Arcanus</a>, a travelling freak show, which billed them as the "greatest freak of nature ever placed on exhibition." Based on a depiction of them on a poster, they were a congenitally deformed person with a <a href="/wiki/Devil" title="Devil">devil</a>-like tail and horns, and claw-like hands and feet.<sup id="cite_ref-poster_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-poster-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Infant_Demonic?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Infant_Demonic?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>The Infant Demonic resembles The Lobster Boy, <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Grady_Stiles" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Grady Stiles">Grady Stiles</a> .</li>
<li>Considering the <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magical</a> nature of the Circus Arcanus, it seems likely that this was a magical <a href="/wiki/Beast" title="Beast">beast</a> or <a href="/wiki/Being" title="Being">being</a>, possibly a kind of <a href="/wiki/Demon" title="Demon">Demon</a>, given its name.</li></ul>
