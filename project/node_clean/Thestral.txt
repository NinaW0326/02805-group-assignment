<p>The <b>Thestral</b> is a breed of <a href="/wiki/Winged_horse" title="Winged horse">winged horse</a> with a skeletal body, face with reptilian features, and wide, leathery wings that resemble a bat's. They are native to the <a href="/wiki/Great_Britain" title="Great Britain">British Isles</a> and <a href="/wiki/Ireland" title="Ireland">Ireland</a>, though they have been spotted in parts of <a href="/wiki/France" title="France">France</a> and the <a href="/wiki/Iberian_Peninsula" title="Iberian Peninsula">Iberian Peninsula</a>.They are very rare, and are considered dangerous by the <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">British Ministry of Magic</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-ootp21_5-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-ootp21-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> Thestrals are, undeservedly, known as omens of misfortune and aggression by many <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizards</a> because they are visible only to those who have witnessed <a href="/wiki/Death" title="Death">death</a> at least once or due to their somewhat grim, gaunt and <a href="/wiki/Ghost" title="Ghost">ghostly</a> appearance.<sup id="cite_ref-OP10_1-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OP10-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-OP17_6-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OP17-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-OOTP33_3-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP33-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-OP34_7-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OP34-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-FB_8-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-FB-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Due to Thestrals' classification as XXXX, only experienced wizards should try to handle Thestrals. Breeding as well as owning these beasts may be discouraged or even illegal without Ministry consent; in fact, wizards that live in areas not protected against Muggles are forced by law to perform <a href="/wiki/Disillusionment_Charm" title="Disillusionment Charm">Disillusionment Charms</a> on their Thestrals regularly.<sup id="cite_ref-FB_8-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-FB-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>A Thestral is also one possible corporeal form of the <a href="/wiki/Patronus_Charm" title="Patronus Charm">Patronus Charm</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-Patronus_9-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Patronus-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Physical_appearance">Physical appearance</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Thestral?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Physical appearance">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Thestral?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Physical appearance"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<div class="quote">
<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix">"<i>If he had had to give them a name, he supposed he would have called them horses, though there was something reptilian about them, too. Wings sprouted from each wither — vast, black leathery wings that looked as though they ought to belong to giant bats. <p>Thestrals have quite a disturbing appearance and the wizards who are capable of seeing them often only describe these creatures as being sinister and spooky. This is because they are seen as having big, bony figures and their dragon-like faces which bear white, glittering eyes that lack both expression and pupils. Additionally, they are lured by the scent of blood.
</p><p>Being a type of winged horse, most of their anatomy is identical to a horse, excluding their large wings that sprout from their back. Unlike the <a href="/wiki/Abraxan" title="Abraxan">Abraxan</a>, another breed of winged horses, Thestral's wings do not possess any feathers at all; they have vast, black and leathery wings that are more similar to those of bats.<sup id="cite_ref-FB_8-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-FB-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Their fleshless, lustrous bodies are covered with a translucent and glossy coat. This smooth and dark skin is a bit slippery and so thin that Thestral's bones are clearly defined through the entire extension of their sleek bodies. These eerie horses have long black manes, as well as a large tail, either with flowing black hair, like horses or ending in a tuft, like zebras.<sup id="cite_ref-JK_10-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-JK-10">&#91;10&#93;</a></sup> Another distinction is their sharp fangs used to seize and slash their prey.
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Behaviour">Behaviour</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Thestral?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behaviour">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Thestral?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Behaviour"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<div class="quote">
<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix">"<i>...they're dead clever an' useful!</i>"</span></dd>
<dd>&#8212;<a href="/wiki/Rubeus_Hagrid" title="Rubeus Hagrid">Hagrid</a> during a fifth year Care of Magical Creatures lesson<sup class="noprint"><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Order_of_the_Phoenix" title="Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix">[src]</a></sup></dd>
<dd></dd></dl></div>
<figure class="thumb tright show-info-icon" style="width: 252px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/8/87/Thestral_on_land.gif/revision/latest?cb=20090531135244" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/8/87/Thestral_on_land.gif/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/250?cb=20090531135244" decoding="async" width="250" height="106" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="Thestral on land.gif" data-image-key="Thestral_on_land.gif" /></a> 	 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:Thestral_on_land.gif" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption">A Thestral stretching its wings</p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure>
<p>Thestrals are social creatures who live in herds. <a href="/wiki/Professor" title="Professor">Professor</a> <a href="/wiki/Rubeus_Hagrid" title="Rubeus Hagrid">Rubeus Hagrid</a> stated that they are "dead clever".<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP21_2-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP21-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> In fact trained Thestrals are smart enough to understand their rider's words when they ask to travel to a specific location.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP33_3-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP33-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>These magical creatures can be found in dark environments and the forest is their natural habitat. They communicate with each other through a shrill and strange shriek that resembles some sort of monstrous bird.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP21_2-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP21-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>They appear to be loyal creatures, able to discern a friend from an enemy and offering help to humans in need of transportation. Thestrals would forcefully attack anyone or anything they see as a threat and in the unusual case of domesticated Thestrals, any enemy of its owners.<sup id="cite_ref-DH4_11-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH4-11">&#91;11&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-DHEpilogue_12-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DHEpilogue-12">&#91;12&#93;</a></sup> Thestrals are not marks of ill omen, nor (their spooky appearance notwithstanding) are they in any way threatening to humans, always allowing for the fright that the first sight of them tends to give the observer.<sup id="cite_ref-PM_13-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PM-13">&#91;13&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>In the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a>, Hogwarts' trained flock of Thestrals cooperated with <a href="/wiki/Buckbeak" title="Buckbeak">Buckbeak</a>, the <a href="/wiki/Hippogriff" title="Hippogriff">Hippogriff</a>, to attack the <a href="/wiki/Giant" title="Giant">Giants</a> fighting for Voldemort. It's unclear whether or not wild herds can similarly cooperate with other species.<sup id="cite_ref-DH36_4-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH36-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Diet">Diet</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Thestral?veaction=edit&amp;section=4" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Diet">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Thestral?action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Diet"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<figure class="thumb tleft show-info-icon" style="width: 252px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/2/2c/Order-of-the-phoenix-_Thestrals.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20160620215133" class="image"><img alt="" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw%3D%3D" decoding="async" width="250" height="100" class="thumbimage lazyload" data-image-name="Order-of-the-phoenix- Thestrals.jpg" data-image-key="Order-of-the-phoenix-_Thestrals.jpg" data-src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/2/2c/Order-of-the-phoenix-_Thestrals.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/250?cb=20160620215133" /></a> 	<noscript><a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/2/2c/Order-of-the-phoenix-_Thestrals.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20160620215133" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/2/2c/Order-of-the-phoenix-_Thestrals.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/250?cb=20160620215133" decoding="async" width="250" height="100" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="Order-of-the-phoenix- Thestrals.jpg" data-image-key="Order-of-the-phoenix-_Thestrals.jpg" data-src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/2/2c/Order-of-the-phoenix-_Thestrals.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/250?cb=20160620215133" /></a></noscript> 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:Order-of-the-phoenix-_Thestrals.jpg" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption">Thestral foal eating a piece of raw meat</p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure>
<p>Thestrals are carnivorous animals and are attracted to the smell of blood. <a href="/wiki/Professor" title="Professor">Professor</a> <a href="/wiki/Wilhelmina_Grubbly-Plank" title="Wilhelmina Grubbly-Plank">Wilhelmina Grubbly-Plank</a> mentions that they often attack birds. This indicates that, naturally, they hunt not only for food in the ground, but also to pursue flying prey.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP15_14-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP15-14">&#91;14&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>The Thestrals that live within the <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts</a> grounds, in the <a href="/wiki/Forbidden_Forest" title="Forbidden Forest">Forbidden Forest</a>, are properly fed and well trained. They don't attack other creatures or students unless seriously disturbed.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP15_14-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP15-14">&#91;14&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Abilities">Abilities</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Thestral?veaction=edit&amp;section=5" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Abilities">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Thestral?action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Abilities"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<div class="quote">
<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix">"<i>The horse crouched slowly, then rocketed upwards so fast and so steeply that Harry had to clench his arms and legs tightly around the horse to avoid sliding backwards over its bony rump. He closed his eyes and pressed his face down into the horses silky mane as they burst through the topmost branches of the trees and soared out into a blood-red sunset. Harry did not think he had ever moved so fast: the Thestral streaked over the castle, its wide wings hardly beating...</i>"</span></dd>
<dd>&#8212;A Thestral taking off and in mid flight<sup class="noprint"><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Order_of_the_Phoenix" title="Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix">[src]</a></sup></dd>
<dd></dd></dl></div>
<p>The <a href="/wiki/Thestral_tail_hair" title="Thestral tail hair">Thestral tail hair</a> is a powerful and tricky substance that can be mastered only by a witch or wizard capable of facing death. It should be noted that this substance can be used as a core in a wand's conception and it was used to create the most powerful wand known by <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizards</a>, the <a href="/wiki/Elder_Wand" title="Elder Wand">Elder Wand</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-JK_10-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-JK-10">&#91;10&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>The most well-known ability of these beasts is their <a href="/wiki/Invisibility" title="Invisibility">invisibility</a> to those who have not seen <a href="/wiki/Death" title="Death">death</a>. In other words, they are only visible to people who have seen someone dying and fully accepted, understood and internalised the concept.<sup id="cite_ref-OP10_1-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OP10-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Thestrals have an extraordinary sense of smell and will easily recognise the smell of <a href="/wiki/Blood" title="Blood">blood</a> and fresh flesh, even if the source of the scent is rather distanced.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP21_2-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP21-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>They also have quite a useful sense of direction. The Thestral can understand exactly where their riders need to go. If their riders have a certain destination in mind, they only need to say the destination and the creature will diligently carry them to the intended location — much like owls do with letters.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP33_3-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP33-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<figure class="thumb tright show-info-icon" style="width: 252px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/2/2f/Thestral_on_air.gif/revision/latest?cb=20090531135632" class="image"><img alt="" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw%3D%3D" decoding="async" width="250" height="106" class="thumbimage lazyload" data-image-name="Thestral on air.gif" data-image-key="Thestral_on_air.gif" data-src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/2/2f/Thestral_on_air.gif/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/250?cb=20090531135632" /></a> 	<noscript><a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/2/2f/Thestral_on_air.gif/revision/latest?cb=20090531135632" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/2/2f/Thestral_on_air.gif/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/250?cb=20090531135632" decoding="async" width="250" height="106" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="Thestral on air.gif" data-image-key="Thestral_on_air.gif" data-src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/2/2f/Thestral_on_air.gif/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/250?cb=20090531135632" /></a></noscript> 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:Thestral_on_air.gif" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption">Thestrals mid-flight, flying at very high-speeds</p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure>
<p>These gentle, winged beasts are very capable and fast fliers and can travel long distances hardly beating their large wings. For example, in 1996, six members of the Hogwarts herd (ridden by <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>, <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione Granger</a>, <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ron Weasley</a>, <a href="/wiki/Ginevra_Weasley" title="Ginevra Weasley">Ginny Weasley</a>, <a href="/wiki/Neville_Longbottom" title="Neville Longbottom">Neville Longbottom</a>, <a href="/wiki/Luna_Lovegood" title="Luna Lovegood">Luna Lovegood</a>) were capable of flying from Hogwarts (<a href="/wiki/Scotland" title="Scotland">Scotland</a>) to the <a href="/wiki/Ministry_of_Magic" title="Ministry of Magic">Ministry of Magic</a> (<a href="/wiki/London" title="London">London</a>) in a brief amount of time. It is possible that they were faster than even the fastest <a href="/wiki/Broomstick" title="Broomstick">broomstick</a>, as when Harry rode his, he did not think he had ever moved so fast, and he was the owner of a top-of-the-line broomstick, the <a href="/wiki/Firebolt" title="Firebolt">Firebolt</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP33_3-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP33-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Their powerful wings are capable of lifting, at least, the burden of two <a href="/wiki/Human" title="Human">humans</a> plus their own weight.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP33_3-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP33-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Interaction_with_humans">Interaction with humans</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Thestral?veaction=edit&amp;section=7" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Interaction with humans">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Thestral?action=edit&amp;section=7" title="Edit section: Interaction with humans"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Thestrals can be domesticated and mounted, so they are used as an alternative to <a href="/wiki/Broomstick" title="Broomstick">brooms</a>, <a href="/wiki/Apparition" title="Apparition">Apparition</a>, and other methods of transportation. Once trained, they are very diligent and will quickly carry their owners wherever they wish to go. However, travel by Thestral is technically illegal, as it is a breach of the <a href="/wiki/International_Statute_of_Wizarding_Secrecy" title="International Statute of Wizarding Secrecy">International Statute of Wizarding Secrecy</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-15" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-15">&#91;15&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<figure class="thumb tleft show-info-icon" style="width: 247px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/6/67/Thestral_flight.gif/revision/latest?cb=20140604022511" class="image"><img alt="" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw%3D%3D" decoding="async" width="245" height="123" class="thumbimage lazyload" data-image-name="Thestral flight.gif" data-image-key="Thestral_flight.gif" data-src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/6/67/Thestral_flight.gif/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/245?cb=20140604022511" /></a> 	<noscript><a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/6/67/Thestral_flight.gif/revision/latest?cb=20140604022511" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/6/67/Thestral_flight.gif/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/245?cb=20140604022511" decoding="async" width="245" height="123" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="Thestral flight.gif" data-image-key="Thestral_flight.gif" data-src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/6/67/Thestral_flight.gif/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/245?cb=20140604022511" /></a></noscript> 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:Thestral_flight.gif" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption">Thestrals transporting wizards</p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure>
<p>Even with all their useful abilities, Thestrals are rarely used as methods of transportation due to their reputation as omens of evil and their somewhat dreadful and even distasteful appearance.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP21_2-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP21-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>When riding a Thestral, the traveller usually holds the creature's mane to ensure balance. To aid the mounting, the wizards also place their legs behind the wing joints to provide safety. Flying on the back of a Thestral during a long journey is frequently an unpleasant experience, particularly to those who dare riding them without seeing the creature. The high speed flight on an invisible steed can be terrifying. The wind will, eventually, cause a temporary deafness and will force the riders to close their eyes. It is often difficult to keep balance on their slick backs.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP33_3-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP33-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>The Hogwarts herd is gentle towards humans, they react satisfactorily to caresses and avoid attacking owls. However, taking into consideration the Ministry of Magic classification as "dangerous",<sup id="cite_ref-ootp21_5-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-ootp21-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> this behaviour may be exclusive to well-trained Thestrals,<sup id="cite_ref-FB_8-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-FB-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup> or just mere prejudice from the Ministry.
</p>

To people who cannot see Thestrals, it appears that the carriages are autonomous.<sup id="cite_ref-OP10_1-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OP10-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> The herd at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts</a> started with a male and five females. </p><p>Rubeus Hagrid, the trainer and breeder of this specific herd, strongly suspected that this was the only trained large group of Thestrals in the whole of <a href="/wiki/Great_Britain" title="Great Britain">Great Britain</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP21_2-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP21-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>

<p><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> first saw the Thestrals at Hogwarts in <a href="/wiki/September" title="September">September</a> of <a href="/wiki/1995" title="1995">1995</a>, after having witnessed the murder of <a href="/wiki/Cedric_Diggory" title="Cedric Diggory">Cedric Diggory</a> in <a href="/wiki/June" title="June">June</a>. Harry could not see them that <a href="/wiki/June" title="June">June</a> because he had not yet dealt with what he had witnessed. At first, he wondered why the supposedly horseless carriages were suddenly pulled by such sinister creatures when they were able to move on their own. He pointed the Thestrals out to <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ron Weasley</a> , and realised that Ron could not see them. Sensing his desperation, <a href="/wiki/Luna_Lovegood" title="Luna Lovegood">Luna Lovegood</a> assured him that she had always been able to see the horses and that he was just as sane as she was. Given Luna's odd habits and beliefs, this statement did not completely reassure Harry.<sup id="cite_ref-OP10_1-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OP10-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>They were first identified by name by <a href="/wiki/Professor" title="Professor">Professor</a> <a href="/wiki/Wilhelmina_Grubbly-Plank" title="Wilhelmina Grubbly-Plank">Wilhelmina Grubbly-Plank</a>, after <a href="/wiki/Hedwig" title="Hedwig">Hedwig</a> was found injured, when she mentioned that Thestrals sometimes go after birds. They were next mentioned by Hagrid in a <a href="/wiki/Care_of_Magical_Creatures" title="Care of Magical Creatures">Care of Magical Creatures</a> class, where the students were told that they could be used as mounts, and introduced their navigation abilities.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP15_14-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP15-14">&#91;14&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>In June of 1996, Harry, <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione Granger</a>, <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ron</a> , <a href="/wiki/Ginevra_Weasley" title="Ginevra Weasley">Ginny Weasley</a>, <a href="/wiki/Luna_Lovegood" title="Luna Lovegood">Luna Lovegood</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Neville_Longbottom" title="Neville Longbottom">Neville Longbottom</a> flew to <a href="/wiki/London" title="London">London</a> atop the Thestrals in order to find <a href="/wiki/Sirius_Black" title="Sirius Black">Sirius Black</a>, whom Harry believed to be in danger in the <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">Ministry of Magic</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP33_3-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP33-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> (Hermione, Ron, and Ginny had trouble mounting their Thestrals for the ride since, at least to the three of them, the Thestrals were invisible).<sup id="cite_ref-OP34_7-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OP34-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<p>The <a href="/wiki/Order_of_the_Phoenix" title="Order of the Phoenix">Order of the Phoenix</a> made use of Thestrals in the summer of 1997 during the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_the_Seven_Potters" title="Battle of the Seven Potters">Battle of the Seven Potters</a>. Six of the Order's members took <a href="/wiki/Polyjuice_Potion" title="Polyjuice Potion">Polyjuice Potion</a> to disguise themselves as Harry, and then the "seven Potters" and their protectors fled <a href="/wiki/4_Privet_Drive" title="4 Privet Drive">4 Privet Drive</a> to safe houses of other members. Thestrals were used as transportation by the Order, seeing as they are incredibly fast and very clever.  <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione Granger</a> and <a href="/wiki/Kingsley_Shacklebolt" title="Kingsley Shacklebolt">Kingsley Shacklebolt</a> flew on a Thestral (Hermione was not confident on a broom), and so did Bill Weasley and <a href="/wiki/Fleur_Delacour" title="Fleur Delacour">Fleur Delacour</a> (Fleur did not like brooms).<sup id="cite_ref-DH4_11-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH4-11">&#91;11&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<p>During the second stage of the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a>, Thestrals were seen attacking <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Giant" title="Giant">Giant</a> soldiers from the air. They were lead by the <a href="/wiki/Hippogriff" title="Hippogriff">Hippogriff</a> <a href="/wiki/Buckbeak" title="Buckbeak">Buckbeak</a>. It can be assumed that after the battle Thestrals were treated with much more respect then previously and maybe they were not considered as unlucky as they were always believed to be.<sup id="cite_ref-DH36_4-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH36-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Nineteen years later, <a href="/wiki/James_Potter_II" title="James Potter II">James Potter II</a> teasingly warned his younger brother <a href="/wiki/Albus_Potter" title="Albus Potter">Albus</a> about the Thestrals before they left for Hogwarts. This teasing however was stopped by Harry Potter, by informing his son that Thestrals were not dangerous without reason to be.<sup id="cite_ref-DHEpilogue_12-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DHEpilogue-12">&#91;12&#93;</a></sup>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Thestral?veaction=edit&amp;section=13" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Thestral?action=edit&amp;section=13" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The name thestral comes from the archaic English word <i>thester</i>, meaning "dark" or "gloomy",<sup id="cite_ref-23" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-23">&#91;23&#93;</a></sup> and the suffix <i>-al</i>, meaning "of or pertaining to".<sup id="cite_ref-24" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-24">&#91;24&#93;</a></sup> Together, the name ultimately means "of/pertaining to the dark" or "to gloom."

<ul><li><a href="/wiki/J._K._Rowling" title="J. K. Rowling">J. K. Rowling</a> stated that the core of the <a href="/wiki/Elder_Wand" title="Elder Wand">Elder Wand</a> is a <a href="/wiki/Thestral_tail_hair" title="Thestral tail hair">Thestral tail hair</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-JK_10-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-JK-10">&#91;10&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li>J. K. Rowling also stated that the reason Harry was not able to see Thestrals until the beginning of his fifth year was that when he left Hogwarts in <a href="/wiki/June" title="June">June</a> he had not yet fully come to terms with <a href="/wiki/Cedric_Diggory" title="Cedric Diggory">Cedric</a>'s death.<sup id="cite_ref-EBF_20-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-EBF-20">&#91;20&#93;</a></sup>
<ul><li>As described in <i>Goblet of Fire</i>, when Harry and Cedric are transported into the graveyard, Harry's scar begins to hurt and then Wormtail kills Cedric. It mentions "A blast of green light blazed through Harry's eyelids, and he felt something heavy fall to the ground beside him." That meant that Harry had his eyes closed when Cedric died and did not know what fell next to him. Therefore, Harry did not actually witness Cedric's death and should not have been able to see Thestrals as a result of it. Despite this, it is canon that Harry can see Thestrals, as it was clearly stated that he could see them in <i>Phoenix</i>, and thus the way in which he witnessed Cedric's death can be described as a consistency error. If one considers it a mistake that Harry was able to see Thestrals, then he should not have been able to see them until either the death of Sirius Black, or Albus Dumbledore, depending on whether witnessing Black falling through the Veil would count. Thestrals, however, can be seen someone who has gained a sufficient understanding of what death means, and it is possible this was enough for Harry to see them, even though he had not witnessed Cedric's death directly.<sup id="cite_ref-PM_13-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PM-13">&#91;13&#93;</a></sup></li></ul></li>
<li>Harry also had the chance to witness at least two people die before the death of <a href="/wiki/Cedric_Diggory" title="Cedric Diggory">Cedric Diggory</a> in <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Goblet_of_Fire" title="Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire">Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire</a></i>. His mother was murdered by <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a> when he was one, and <a href="/wiki/Quirinus_Quirrell" title="Quirinus Quirrell">Professor Quirrell</a> in his first year. Despite this, <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry</a> is not able to see the Thestrals until the beginning of his <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Order_of_the_Phoenix" title="Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix">fifth year</a>. <a href="/wiki/J._K._Rowling" title="J. K. Rowling">J. K. Rowling</a> has explained this stating that he was in his crib when <a href="/wiki/Lily_J._Potter" title="Lily J. Potter">his mother</a> was murdered, and passed out before Quirrell died.<sup id="cite_ref-JK2_25-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-JK2-25">&#91;25&#93;</a></sup> It's also possible that when his parents died, Harry was too young to truly understand the concept of accepting someone's death. This creates a paradox in the films, since the first film appears to show Harry seeing Quirrell die.
<ul><li>This can be attributed to the fact that the <i>Order of the Phoenix</i> novel was published two years after the first film was released.</li></ul></li>
<li>It may be possible that <a href="/wiki/William_Weasley" title="William Weasley">Bill</a>, <a href="/wiki/Fleur_Delacour" title="Fleur Delacour">Fleur</a> and <a href="/wiki/Kingsley_Shacklebolt" title="Kingsley Shacklebolt">Kingsley Shacklebolt</a> all saw someone die as they all rode a Thestral during the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_the_Seven_Potters" title="Battle of the Seven Potters">Battle of the Seven Potters</a>. The explanation is that Kingsley Shacklebolt most likely saw a death during the <a href="/wiki/First_Wizarding_War" title="First Wizarding War">First Wizarding War</a>. However, who Fleur and Bill saw die is unknown. It is possible, that, like Hermione, Fleur could not see Thestrals, since she lived in <a href="/wiki/France" title="France">France</a> where the <a href="/wiki/First_Wizarding_War" title="First Wizarding War">First Wizarding War</a> wasn't as effective, and she was only four years old when Voldemort was defeated the first time; however, it is equally possible that she witnessed another death during her life.</li>
<li>In the films, Thestrals do not seem to have visible <a href="/wiki/Thestral_tail_hair" title="Thestral tail hair">hair</a>.</li>
<li>Thestrals also don’t seem to have fangs in the film, at least in the young foals. Instead they have a pointed beak which they use to grab food and then they swallow it, almost without chewing, much like birds eat worms.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Neville_Longbottom" title="Neville Longbottom">Neville Longbottom</a> does not seem to be able to see Thestrals in the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Order_of_the_Phoenix_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix (film)">film adaptation</a> of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Order_of_the_Phoenix" title="Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix">Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix</a></i>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Kazu_Kibuishi" title="Kazu Kibuishi">Kazu Kibuishi</a> illustrated the cover of the Scholastic special edition version of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Order_of_the_Phoenix" title="Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix">Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix</a></i> which features Harry riding a Thestral, and two other people (possibly Ron and Hermione) riding Thestrals in the background.</li>
<li>In an interview for the fifth film, <a href="/wiki/Daniel_Radcliffe" title="Daniel Radcliffe">Daniel Radcliffe</a> described the Thestrals as resembling something the Four Horsemen would ride, alluding to the Four Horsemen of the Apocalypse of the Christian Bible, among whom <a href="/wiki/Death" title="Death">Death</a> is a member.</li>
<li>After the <a href="/wiki/Second_Wizarding_War" title="Second Wizarding War">Second Wizarding War</a>, many individuals were likely able to see Thestrals as many witnessed the death of Voldemort.</li>
<li>It is possible that <a href="/wiki/Scorpius_Malfoy" title="Scorpius Malfoy">Scorpius Malfoy</a> was able to see thestrals after possibly witnessing the death of his mother Astoria in the summer of <a href="/wiki/2019" title="2019">2019</a>.</li>
<li>It is unknown whether <a href="/wiki/Albus_Potter" title="Albus Potter">Albus Potter</a> was able to see thestrals after witnessing the death of <a href="/wiki/Craig_Bowker_Junior" title="Craig Bowker Junior">Craig Bowker Jnr</a>.</li></ul>
