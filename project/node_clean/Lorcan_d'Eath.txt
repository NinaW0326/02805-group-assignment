He spent nineteen weeks at number one with his song "<a href="/wiki/Necks_to_You" title="Necks to You">Necks to You</a>."<sup id="cite_ref-JKRS_1-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-JKRS-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>

<ul><li>ï»¿Lorcan means "fierce one" in Gaelic, a common name for fictional vampires. The name d'Eath is an obvious reference to death.</li></ul>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Lorcan_d%27Eath?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Lorcan_d%27Eath?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>Lorcan shares his name with the <a href="/wiki/Lorcan" class="mw-redirect" title="Lorcan">son</a> of <a href="/wiki/Luna_Lovegood" title="Luna Lovegood">Luna Lovegood</a>.</li>
<li>If Lorcan attended <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts</a>, he may have been in the same year as <a href="/wiki/Glenda_Chittock" title="Glenda Chittock">Glenda Chittock</a>, during the <a href="/wiki/Marauders" title="Marauders">Marauders</a> fifth year.</li></ul>
