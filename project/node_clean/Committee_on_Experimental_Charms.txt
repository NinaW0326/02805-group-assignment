<p>The <b>Committee on Experimental Charms</b> was a committee of the <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">British Ministry of Magic</a> founded by <a href="/wiki/Balfour_Blane" title="Balfour Blane">Balfour Blane</a> in late <a href="/wiki/16th_century" title="16th century">16th</a> or early <a href="/wiki/17th_century" title="17th century">17th century</a> that oversaw the use and <a href="/wiki/Spell_creation" title="Spell creation">development</a> of experimental <a href="/wiki/Charm" title="Charm">charms</a>.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="History">History</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Committee_on_Experimental_Charms?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: History">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Committee_on_Experimental_Charms?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: History"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The Committee was founded by <a href="/wiki/Balfour_Blane" title="Balfour Blane">Balfour Blane</a> sometime before <a href="/wiki/1700" class="mw-redirect" title="1700">1700</a>. After thriving for nearly three centuries and integrating into the mainstream <a href="/wiki/Ministry_of_Magic" title="Ministry of Magic">Ministry of Magic</a> after its founding in <a href="/wiki/1707" class="mw-redirect" title="1707">1707</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-PMministers_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PMministers-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> the Committee had to deal with some extremely odd <a href="/wiki/Ferret" title="Ferret">ferrets</a> found in the possession of <a href="/wiki/Mortlake" title="Mortlake">Mortlake</a> in <a href="/wiki/1992" title="1992">1992</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-COS3_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COS3-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> Around this time, <a href="/wiki/Gilbert_Wimple" title="Gilbert Wimple">Gilbert Wimple</a> joined them.<sup id="cite_ref-gof_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-gof-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> The Committee once made a study on the percentage of wizards and witches that believed that <a href="/wiki/Atmospheric_Charm" title="Atmospheric Charm">Atmospheric Charms</a> should be regulated due to their effect upon the environment.<sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
