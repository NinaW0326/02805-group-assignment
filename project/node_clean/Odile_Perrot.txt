<b>Odile Perrot</b> was a French <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch</a> and a member of the <a href="/wiki/Perrot_family" title="Perrot family">Perrot family</a>. She was the daughter of <a href="/wiki/Manon_Perrot" title="Manon Perrot">Manon Perrot</a> and <a href="/wiki/Sebastien_Perrot" title="Sebastien Perrot">Sebastien Perrot</a>, and the sister of <a href="/wiki/Huguette_Perrot" title="Huguette Perrot">Huguette</a> and <a href="/wiki/Laurelle_Perrot" title="Laurelle Perrot">Laurelle Perrot</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
