<b>Lancaster Pyke</b>, also known as <b>Magic Joe</b><sup id="cite_ref-poster_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-poster-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>, was an <a href="/wiki/United_States_of_America" title="United States of America">American</a> <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizarding</a> criminal who operated in the <a href="/wiki/United_States_of_America" title="United States of America">United States of America</a> in the <a href="/wiki/1920s" title="1920s">1920s</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-CinemaBlend_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-CinemaBlend-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<p>In <a href="/wiki/1926" title="1926">1926</a>, the <a href="/wiki/Magical_Congress_of_the_United_States_of_America" title="Magical Congress of the United States of America">Magical Congress of the United States of America</a> issued a wanted poster for Pyke offering 4850 <a href="/wiki/Dragot" title="Dragot">dragots</a> for is arrest, which described him as "older" and stated that he was wanted for "embezzlement of <a href="/wiki/Spell" title="Spell">spells</a> and <a href="/wiki/Muggle" class="mw-redirect" title="Muggle">No-Maj</a> murder".<sup id="cite_ref-poster_1-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-poster-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
