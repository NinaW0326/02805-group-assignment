The <b>Gryffindor Knight Statue</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Suit_of_armour" title="Suit of armour">suit of armour</a> associated with the House of <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor" title="Gryffindor">Gryffindor</a> at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a>.
