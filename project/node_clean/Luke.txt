<b>Luke</b>, also known as <b>Luke the Evangelist</b>, was the traditional author of two books of the <a href="/wiki/Bible" title="Bible">Bible</a>, the <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gospel_of_Luke" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Gospel of Luke">Gospel of Luke</a> and the <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Acts_of_the_Apostles" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Acts of the Apostles">Acts of the Apostles</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-wikipedia_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-wikipedia-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> He was one of the four Evangelists.<sup id="cite_ref-wikipedia_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-wikipedia-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
