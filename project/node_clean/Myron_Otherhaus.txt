<b>Myron Otherhaus</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> who lived in the <a href="/wiki/19th_century" title="19th century">19th century</a>. Described as "very handsome," he was resided in <a href="/wiki/Bury_St_Edmunds" title="Bury St Edmunds">Bury St Edmunds</a>, <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Suffolk" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Suffolk">Suffolk</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-PM_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PM-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Myron_Otherhaus?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Myron_Otherhaus?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>One day in <a href="/wiki/1855" class="mw-redirect" title="1855">1855</a>, <a href="/wiki/Violet_Tillyman" title="Violet Tillyman">Violet Tillyman</a> unexpectedly tumbled out of Myron's fireplace via the <a href="/wiki/Floo_Network" title="Floo Network">Floo Network</a>, having left her home after a nasty argument with her bullying husband, <a href="/wiki/Albert_Tillyman" title="Albert Tillyman">Albert</a>. In a distressed state, she had tried to say, "<i>I want to go to <a href="/wiki/Violet_Tillyman%27s_mother" title="Violet Tillyman&#39;s mother">Mother's</a> house</i>", but the words had not come out clearly between her sobs and hiccoughs. However, despite Violet's red, tear-streaked face and ash-covered clothing, she and Myron immediately fell in love with each other.<sup id="cite_ref-PM_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PM-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>For the next twenty years, Myron and Violet lived together in seclusion in Bury St Edmunds, having <a href="/wiki/Violet_Tillyman%27s_seven_children" class="mw-redirect" title="Violet Tillyman&#39;s seven children">seven children</a>. After Albert's death in <a href="/wiki/1875" class="mw-redirect" title="1875">1875</a>, Violet finally chose to resurface publicly, and she and Myron lived together happily for the rest of their lives.<sup id="cite_ref-PM_1-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PM-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Myron_Otherhaus?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Myron_Otherhaus?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>If Myron and Violet never married, as seems to be the case, this would make their seven children the only characters within <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_universe" title="Harry Potter universe"><i>Harry Potter</i> canon</a> known to have been born to non-married parents, until the addition of <a href="/wiki/Delphini" title="Delphini">Delphini</a> in <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Cursed_Child" title="Harry Potter and the Cursed Child">Harry Potter and the Cursed Child</a></i>.</li></ul>
