<b>Chieftainess Elfrida Clagg</b> (<a href="/wiki/1612" class="mw-redirect" title="1612">1612</a> - <a href="/wiki/1687" class="mw-redirect" title="1687">1687</a>) was a <a href="/wiki/Great_Britain" title="Great Britain">British</a> <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch</a> and the Chieftainess of the <a href="/wiki/Wizards%27_Council" title="Wizards&#39; Council">Wizards' Council</a> either in the <a href="/wiki/14th_century" title="14th century">fourteenth</a><sup id="cite_ref-FBaWtFT_5-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-FBaWtFT-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> or <a href="/wiki/17th_century" title="17th century">seventeenth century</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-WoM_1-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-WoM-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> Either way, she was <a href="/wiki/Burdock_Muldoon" title="Burdock Muldoon">Burdock Muldoon</a>'s immediate successor of the post.<sup id="cite_ref-FBaWtFT_5-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-FBaWtFT-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Elfrida_Clagg?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Elfrida_Clagg?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_life">Early life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Elfrida_Clagg?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Early life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Elfrida_Clagg?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Early life"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>It is debated whether Clagg was born in <a href="/wiki/1612" class="mw-redirect" title="1612">1612</a> or about one or two centuries earlier. She presumably attended <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> in her youth, like so many other <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witches</a>.
</p>
<h3><span id="As_Chieftainess_of_the_Wizards'_Council"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="As_Chieftainess_of_the_Wizards.27_Council">As Chieftainess of the Wizards' Council</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Elfrida_Clagg?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: As Chieftainess of the Wizards&#039; Council">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Elfrida_Clagg?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: As Chieftainess of the Wizards&#039; Council"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<figure class="thumb tleft show-info-icon" style="width: 152px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/8/8b/Elfrida_Clagg_POA.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20090902230614" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/8/8b/Elfrida_Clagg_POA.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/150?cb=20090902230614" decoding="async" width="150" height="248" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="Elfrida Clagg POA.jpg" data-image-key="Elfrida_Clagg_POA.jpg" /></a> 	 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:Elfrida_Clagg_POA.jpg" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption">Elfrida Clagg, as depicted in her <a href="/wiki/Chocolate_Frog_Card" title="Chocolate Frog Card">Chocolate Frog Card</a></p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure>
<p>Clagg was <a href="/wiki/Burdock_Muldoon" title="Burdock Muldoon">Burdock Muldoon</a>'s successor on the post of Chief of the <a href="/wiki/Wizards%27_Council" title="Wizards&#39; Council">Wizards' Council</a>. Madame Clagg attempted to redefine the classification of <a href="/wiki/Being" title="Being">Beings</a> and <a href="/wiki/Beast" title="Beast">Beasts</a> by declaring that all those magical creatures that could speak the human tongue were Beings. However, this once more caused problems with the beings meeting as the <a href="/wiki/Troll" title="Troll">Trolls</a> were again classified as Beings and destroyed the meeting chamber, <a href="/wiki/Jarvey" title="Jarvey">Jarveys</a> were in attendance and tore as many Council members' ankles as they could, and the <a href="/wiki/Centaur" title="Centaur">Centaurs</a> boycotted the meeting as the <a href="/wiki/Merpeople" title="Merpeople">Merpeople</a> were not invited. A large delegation of <a href="/wiki/Ghost" title="Ghost">ghosts</a> also attended, but left in disgust of what they later called "the Council's unashamed emphasis on the needs of the living as opposed to the wishes of the dead".<sup id="cite_ref-FBaWtFT_5-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-FBaWtFT-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Also, due to the declining numbers of <a href="/wiki/Golden_Snidget" title="Golden Snidget">Golden Snidgets</a>, Clagg made them a protected species, and outlawed both their hunting, and their use in <a href="/wiki/Quidditch" title="Quidditch">Quidditch</a> matches.<sup id="cite_ref-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Elfrida_Clagg?veaction=edit&amp;section=4" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Elfrida_Clagg?action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>"Elfrida" is from the Old English name Ælfþryð <a target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener" class="external text" href="http://d-k-e.euro.ru/elfsaga.htm">meaning "elf strength"</a> from the element <i>ælf</i> combined with <i>þryð</i> "strength". Ælfþryð was a 10th-century queen of England. This name was rare after the Norman conquest, but it was revived in the 19th century.
</p>
