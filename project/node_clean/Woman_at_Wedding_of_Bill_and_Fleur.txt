She also helped fight the <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a> that <a href="/wiki/Attack_at_the_Burrow" title="Attack at the Burrow">attacked</a> after the fall of the <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">Ministry of Magic</a> was announced by <a href="/wiki/Kingsley_Shacklebolt" title="Kingsley Shacklebolt">Kingsley Shacklebolt</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Patronus_Charm" title="Patronus Charm">Patronus</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-DHF1_1-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DHF1-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
