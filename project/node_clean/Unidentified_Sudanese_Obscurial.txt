<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_Sudanese_Obscurial?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_Sudanese_Obscurial?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>This young <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch</a> was born in <a href="/wiki/Sudan" title="Sudan">Sudan</a> in <a href="/wiki/1918" title="1918">1918</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-fb61_1-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-fb61-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>While still a young child, her <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magical</a> powers were discovered by <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggles</a>, leading them to imprison her.<sup id="cite_ref-fb61_1-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-fb61-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-fb108_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-fb108-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> This treatment lead her to suppress her magic to the point that she became an <a href="/wiki/Obscurial" title="Obscurial">Obscurial</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-fb61_1-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-fb61-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-fb108_2-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-fb108-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>In <a href="/wiki/September" title="September">September</a> <a href="/wiki/1926" title="1926">1926</a>, when she was eight years old, the <a href="/wiki/Great_Britain" title="Great Britain">British</a> <a href="/wiki/Magizoologist" title="Magizoologist">magizoologist</a> <a href="/wiki/Newton_Scamander" title="Newton Scamander">Newt Scamander</a> came across her while in <a href="/wiki/Africa" title="Africa">Africa</a> studying magical <a href="/wiki/Beast" title="Beast">beasts</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-fb61_1-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-fb61-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> Newt tried to save the girl, managing to separate the Obscurus from her, though she died regardless.<sup id="cite_ref-fb61_1-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-fb61-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-fb65_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-fb65-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> Newt was able to preserve the Obscurus despite the death of its host and afterward kept it in a magical bubble in his <a href="/wiki/Newton_Scamander%27s_suitcase" title="Newton Scamander&#39;s suitcase">magical suitcase</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-fb65_3-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-fb65-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>The girl represented the first documented incidence of an Obscurial in centuries.<sup id="cite_ref-fb61_1-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-fb61-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
