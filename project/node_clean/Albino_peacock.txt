<p>An <b>albino peacock</b> is a male peafowl, distinctive for its pure white colour due to total or partial lack of <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Biological_pigment" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Biological pigment">biochromes</a>. 
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="History">History</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Albino_peacock?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: History">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Albino_peacock?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: History"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p><a href="/wiki/Lucius_Malfoy" title="Lucius Malfoy">Lucius Malfoy</a> kept at least one on the grounds of <a href="/wiki/Malfoy_Manor" title="Malfoy Manor">Malfoy Manor</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-dh1_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-dh1-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> It was on top of the hedge when <a href="/wiki/Severus_Snape" title="Severus Snape">Severus Snape</a> and <a href="/wiki/Corban_Yaxley" title="Corban Yaxley">Yaxley</a> arrived at the Manor for a <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eater</a> meeting in July <a href="/wiki/1997" title="1997">1997</a>. It rustled the bushes as it strutted, alarming Yaxley, who drew his <a href="/wiki/Yaxley%27s_wand" class="mw-redirect" title="Yaxley&#39;s wand">wand</a>, but put it away once he saw the noise's source.<sup id="cite_ref-dh1_1-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-dh1-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Albino_peacock?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Albino_peacock?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>Several colour varieties of <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Peafowl" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Peafowl">peafowl</a> have been bred, including white.<sup id="cite_ref-peafowlvarieties_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-peafowlvarieties-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> This differs from <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Albinism" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Albinism">albinism</a>, which would cause lack of pigmentation not just in the feathers, but in the skin and eyes as well.<sup id="cite_ref-peafowlvarieties_3-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-peafowlvarieties-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> However, in Chapter 23 of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Deathly_Hallows" title="Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows">Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows</a></i>, the peacock at Malfoy Manor is explicitly referred to as "albino".</li>
<li>Many have argued that this peacock was, in fact, <a href="/wiki/Lucius_Malfoy" title="Lucius Malfoy">Lucius Malfoy</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Patronus_Charm" title="Patronus Charm">Patronus</a>. It was thought that this proved Lucius was, in point of fact, not a true <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eater</a>, as Death Eaters cannot produce Patronuses. Despite this, <a href="/wiki/J._K._Rowling" title="J. K. Rowling">J. K. Rowling</a> confirmed in an interview that this was merely an albino peacock.</li>
<li>In <i><a href="/wiki/LEGO_Harry_Potter:_Years_5-7" title="LEGO Harry Potter: Years 5-7">LEGO Harry Potter: Years 5-7</a></i>, <a href="/wiki/Lucius_Malfoy" title="Lucius Malfoy">Lucius Malfoy</a> can control a pet peacock, although it is blue in colour.</li></ul>
