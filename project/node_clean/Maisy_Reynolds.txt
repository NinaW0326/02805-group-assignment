<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Maisy_Reynolds?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Maisy_Reynolds?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>She was studying at Hogwarts in the <a href="/wiki/1997-1998_school_year" class="mw-redirect" title="1997-1998 school year">1997-1998 school year</a>, when <a href="/wiki/Severus_Snape" title="Severus Snape">Professor Severus Snape</a> was <a href="/wiki/Headmaster" title="Headmaster">Headmaster</a>. At some point during the school year, she disobeyed <a href="/wiki/Alecto_Carrow" title="Alecto Carrow">Alecto Carrow</a>, which made the <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eater</a> "pay a visit" to <a href="/wiki/Maisy_Reynolds%27s_father" title="Maisy Reynolds&#39;s father">her father</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-script_4-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-script-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> She also became a member of <a href="/wiki/Dumbledore%27s_Army" title="Dumbledore&#39;s Army">Dumbledore's Army</a> this year.<sup id="cite_ref-DHF2_3-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DHF2-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>In the evening of <a href="/wiki/1_May" title="1 May">1 May</a>, after <a href="/wiki/Ousting_of_Severus_Snape" title="Ousting of Severus Snape">Snape's ousting</a>, when the school was gathered in the <a href="/wiki/Great_Hall" title="Great Hall">Great Hall</a> to discuss the evacuation of underage students from the Castle because of the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">battle that would soon ensue</a>, <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a>'s voice sounded through the walls to warn the school that all he wanted was <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>, and that if they did not give him to him, the battle was inevitable. Maisy, who stood in a corner of the Hall, started screaming in terror at his voice.<sup id="cite_ref-DHF2_3-10" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DHF2-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> It is unknown if Maisy stayed to participate in the battle or if she was evacuated from the school beforehand.
</p>
