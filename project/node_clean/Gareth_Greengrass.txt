<b>Minister</b><sup id="cite_ref-Minister_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Minister-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> <b>Gareth Greengrass</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Great_Britain" title="Great Britain">British</a> <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> who worked as a Senior <a href="/wiki/Unspeakable" title="Unspeakable">Unspeakable</a><sup id="cite_ref-WU_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-WU-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> in the <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">British Ministry of Magic</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Department_of_Mysteries" title="Department of Mysteries">Department of Mysteries</a>.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Gareth_Greengrass?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Gareth_Greengrass?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Born into the <a href="/wiki/Greengrass_family" title="Greengrass family">Greengrass family</a>, one of the <a href="/wiki/Sacred_Twenty-Eight" title="Sacred Twenty-Eight">oldest pure-blooded families</a> in <a href="/wiki/Great_Britain" title="Great Britain">Britain</a>, and like most magical children in the area, presumably attended <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> during his formative years.<sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> Upon completing his magical education, Gareth joined the <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">British Ministry of Magic</a>, where he successfully landed a job as a <a href="/wiki/Research_assistant" title="Research assistant">research assistant</a> and eventually was able to work his way up to the point where he earned a place as a <a href="/wiki/Unspeakable" title="Unspeakable">Unspeakable</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Department_of_Mysteries" title="Department of Mysteries">Department of Mysteries</a>. A gifted employee, he would dedicate most of his adult life to conducting confidential research into the mysteries of magic, and eventually rose to a place of seniority in the department, becoming a Senior Unspeakable.
</p><p>In the <a href="/wiki/2010s" title="2010s">2010s</a>, he joined a council that oversaw the investigation into the disappearance of <a href="/wiki/Grim_Fawley" title="Grim Fawley">Grim Fawley</a>'s wife <a href="/wiki/Penelope_(Grim_Fawley%27s_wife)" class="mw-redirect" title="Penelope (Grim Fawley&#39;s wife)">Penelope</a> and four other people known as the <a href="/wiki/London_Five" title="London Five">London Five</a>. He promised Grim that he would do everything in his power to find Penelope, and that no cost would be spared; however the council called off the investigation before she was found and ultimately put in a recommendation that Grim took time off of work to recover and care for <a href="/wiki/Grim_Fawley%27s_children" title="Grim Fawley&#39;s children">his children</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
