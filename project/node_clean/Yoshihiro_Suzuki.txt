<b>Yoshihiro Suzuki</b> was a Japanese <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> who played on the <a href="/wiki/Japanese_National_Quidditch_team" title="Japanese National Quidditch team">Japanese National Quidditch team</a> during the four-hundred and twenty-second <a href="/wiki/Quidditch_World_Cup" title="Quidditch World Cup">Quidditch World Cup</a>, in <a href="/wiki/1994" title="1994">1994</a>, a member of the <a href="/wiki/Suzuki_family" title="Suzuki family">Suzuki family</a>. He played as a <a href="/wiki/Chaser" title="Chaser">Chaser</a>. He wears a scarf around his head.
</p><p>During the <a href="/wiki/1996%E2%80%931997_school_year" title="1996–1997 school year">1996–1997 school year</a>, Suzuki's <a href="/wiki/Chocolate_Frog_Card" title="Chocolate Frog Card">Chocolate Frog Card</a> was able to be won by beating <a href="/wiki/Lucy_Hurst" title="Lucy Hurst">Lucy Hurst</a> at an <a href="/wiki/Exploding_Snap" title="Exploding Snap">Exploding Snap</a> challenge.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
