<b>Gunhilda Kneen</b> (fl. <a href="/wiki/12th_century" title="12th century">12th century</a>) was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch</a> who was married to <a href="/wiki/Goodwin_Kneen" title="Goodwin Kneen">Goodwin Kneen</a>. She played <i>Kwidditch</i> (<i><a href="/wiki/Quidditch" title="Quidditch">Quidditch</a></i>), and was a <i>Catcher</i> (<i><a href="/wiki/Chaser" title="Chaser">Chaser</a></i>). Prior to a match against a nearby team from Ilkley, Gunhilda contracted a touch of <a href="/wiki/Dragon_Pox" title="Dragon Pox">Dragon Pox</a>, and was unable to play, forcing the team to use <a href="/wiki/Radulf" title="Radulf">Radulf</a>. After the victory, Goodwin got drunk at the local inn, and Gunhilda was not best pleased - she <a href="/wiki/Finger-removing_jinx" title="Finger-removing jinx">jinxed</a> him, causing him to lose his fingers temporarily.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Gunhilda_Kneen?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Gunhilda_Kneen?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The name "Gunhilda" comes from the Old Norse name Gunnhildr, derived from the elements gunnr, "war" and hildr, "battle".
