<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Bean?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Bean?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>During her time at Hogwarts, she was <a href="/wiki/Sorting_ceremony" title="Sorting ceremony">Sorted</a> into an unknown house. At some point by the <a href="/wiki/1985%E2%80%931986_school_year" title="1985–1986 school year">1985–1986 school year</a>, she became a <a href="/wiki/Beater" title="Beater">Beater</a> of an unknown <a href="/wiki/Quidditch_team" title="Quidditch team">Quidditch team</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-q11_4-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-q11-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>She talked to her <a href="/wiki/Beater%27s_bat" title="Beater&#39;s bat">bat</a> after <a href="/wiki/Quidditch" title="Quidditch">Quidditch</a> matches as her lucky tradition.<sup id="cite_ref-qc_5-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-qc-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>She was extremely dedicated to her position as a Beater to the point where she neglected her studies and did not attend any lessons since her second house match in the <a href="/wiki/1985%E2%80%931986_school_year" title="1985–1986 school year">1985–1986 school year</a>. As a result, at the beginning of the <a href="/wiki/1986%E2%80%931987_school_year" title="1986–1987 school year">1986–1987 school year</a>, she was suspended from her Quidditch team.<sup id="cite_ref-q21_3-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-q21-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
