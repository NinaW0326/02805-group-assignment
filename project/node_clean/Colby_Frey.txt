<b>Colby Frey</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Great_Britain" title="Great Britain">British</a> <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> who attended <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> in the early <a href="/wiki/21st_century" title="21st century">21st century</a> and was a member of <a href="/wiki/Slytherin" title="Slytherin">Slytherin</a> house. He was the twin brother of <a href="/wiki/Fisher_Frey" title="Fisher Frey">Fisher Frey</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-MA_3-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-MA-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Colby_Frey?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Colby_Frey?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>At the age of eleven, he and Fisher began attending <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> in <a href="/wiki/Slytherin" title="Slytherin">Slytherin</a> house. They befriended <a href="/wiki/Cassandra_(Hogwarts_student)" title="Cassandra (Hogwarts student)">Cassandra</a>, and she led their group in bullying their fellow students including <a href="/wiki/Daniel_Page" title="Daniel Page">Daniel Page</a>. 
</p><p>They went to the <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Library" title="Hogwarts Library">Library</a> one night to find a newspaper article about Daniel. Daniel, <a href="/wiki/Ivy_Warrington" title="Ivy Warrington">Ivy Warrington</a> and their <a href="/wiki/Unidentified_2000s_Hogwarts_student" title="Unidentified 2000s Hogwarts student">friend</a> found out that they had broken curfew and went to find out why. They watched the group at a distance, until Colby noticed and told Cassandra. 
</p>
