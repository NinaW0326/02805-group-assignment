<b>Rubens Winikus</b> was presumably the founder of <a href="/wiki/Rubens_Winikus_and_Company_Inc." title="Rubens Winikus and Company Inc.">Rubens Winikus and Company Inc.</a>, a corporation which manufactured and sold <a href="/wiki/Potion" title="Potion">potions</a>, including <a href="/wiki/Skele-Gro" title="Skele-Gro">Skele-Gro</a>, of which they were the sole producer.<sup id="cite_ref-COSF_1-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COSF-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<ul><li>This character is mentioned only on the label of a bottle featured in the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Chamber_of_Secrets_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets (film)">film adaptation</a> of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Chamber_of_Secrets" title="Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets">Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets</a></i>. His name is presumably a tribute to <a href="/wiki/Ruth_Winick" title="Ruth Winick">Ruth Winick</a>, who worked as a graphic artist on <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Philosopher%27s_Stone_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Philosopher&#39;s Stone (film)">Philosopher's Stone</a></i> and <i>Chamber of Secrets</i>, and as a draughtsman on <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Prisoner_of_Azkaban_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban (film)">Prisoner of Azkaban</a></i> and <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Goblet_of_Fire_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire (film)">Goblet of Fire</a></i>.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup></li></ul>
