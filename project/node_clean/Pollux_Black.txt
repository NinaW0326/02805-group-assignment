<b>Pollux Black</b> (<a href="/wiki/1912" title="1912">1912</a> – <a href="/wiki/1990" title="1990">1990</a>) was a <a href="/wiki/Pure-blood" title="Pure-blood">pure-blood</a> <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a>, the eldest child of <a href="/wiki/Cygnus_Black_II" title="Cygnus Black II">Cygnus Black II</a> and <a href="/wiki/Violetta_Bulstrode" title="Violetta Bulstrode">Violetta Bulstrode</a>, the brother of <a href="/wiki/Cassiopeia_Black" title="Cassiopeia Black">Cassiopeia Black</a>, <a href="/wiki/Marius_Black" title="Marius Black">Marius Black</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Dorea_Black" title="Dorea Black">Dorea Black</a>, the husband of <a href="/wiki/Irma_Crabbe" title="Irma Crabbe">Irma Crabbe</a>, the father of <a href="/wiki/Walburga_Black" title="Walburga Black">Walburga Black</a>, <a href="/wiki/Alphard_Black" title="Alphard Black">Alphard Black</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Cygnus_Black_III" title="Cygnus Black III">Cygnus Black III</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-BFT_1-32" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-BFT-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> the grandfather of <a href="/wiki/Sirius_Black" title="Sirius Black">Sirius Black</a>, <a href="/wiki/Regulus_Black" title="Regulus Black">Regulus Black</a>, <a href="/wiki/Bellatrix_Lestrange" title="Bellatrix Lestrange">Bellatrix Lestrange</a>, <a href="/wiki/Andromeda_Tonks" title="Andromeda Tonks">Andromeda Tonks</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Narcissa_Malfoy" title="Narcissa Malfoy">Narcissa Malfoy</a>, the great-grandfather of <a href="/wiki/Draco_Malfoy" title="Draco Malfoy">Draco Malfoy</a>, <a href="/wiki/Nymphadora_Tonks" title="Nymphadora Tonks">Nymphadora Tonks</a> and <a href="/wiki/Delphini" title="Delphini">Delphini</a>, and the great-great-grandfather of <a href="/wiki/Edward_Lupin" title="Edward Lupin">Teddy Lupin</a> and <a href="/wiki/Scorpius_Malfoy" title="Scorpius Malfoy">Scorpius Malfoy</a>.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Pollux_Black?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Pollux_Black?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Like many members of the <a href="/wiki/House_of_Black" title="House of Black">Black family</a>, the name <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Pollux_(star)" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Pollux (star)">Pollux</a> comes from a star. It is the brightest star in the constellation Gemini. The name refers to Castor and Pollux, the twin sons of Zeus and Leda in Greek mythology. The name is traditionally thought to mean <i>"much wine"</i>, since astrologers associate Pollux with prosperity and celebration with wine. Pollux's sister Cassiopeia was named after a constellation.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Pollux_Black?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Pollux_Black?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>On the Black Family Tree, it states that Pollux and (possibly) Irma were thirteen years old when they had their first child, <a href="/wiki/Walburga_Black" title="Walburga Black">Walburga</a>. This is most likely one of the numerous mathematical mistakes made by <a href="/wiki/J._K._Rowling" title="J. K. Rowling">J. K. Rowling</a> (who frequently admits that maths is not her strong suit). Then again, it's possible Rowling is discreetly showing us that teenage pregnancy is as common in the Wizarding world as it is in the Muggle world.</li>
<li>Based on a statement by <a href="/wiki/Horace_Slughorn" title="Horace Slughorn">Horace Slughorn</a>, Pollux may have been a member of the <a href="/wiki/Slytherin" title="Slytherin">Slytherin House</a>. This is likely due to most of the <a href="/wiki/House_of_Black" title="House of Black">Black family</a> (with the exception of Sirius Black) being in the aforementioned House. However, he attended Hogwarts c. <a href="/wiki/1923" title="1923">1923</a> – <a href="/wiki/1930" title="1930">1930</a>, which slightly predates Slughorn's fifty-year tenure from around <a href="/wiki/1931" title="1931">1931</a> – <a href="/wiki/1981" title="1981">1981</a>.</li>
<li>Pollux named his last son (<a href="/wiki/Cygnus_Black_III" title="Cygnus Black III">Cygnus Black III</a>) in honour of his father (<a href="/wiki/Cygnus_Black_II" title="Cygnus Black II">Cygnus Black II</a>).</li>
<li>At the beginning of the tree <a href="/wiki/Licorus_Black" title="Licorus Black">Licorus Black</a> is depicted with a green hat, who is very similar to Pollux, but Pollux on the image is wearing a red hat.</li>
<li>It remains unknown what Pollux felt about the Death Eaters and the rise of Lord Voldemort within Britain. Two of his grandchildren would become Death Eaters and other members of his extended family did join or support them which implies that Pollux may have encouraged them to embrace their blood purity. When he died in 1990 his grandchildren Sirius and Bellatrix were both serving life sentences in Azkaban for crimes they were accused of committing on the behalf of the Death Eaters though Sirius would later be revealed to be innocent.</li></ul>
