<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Jason_Huntingdon?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Jason_Huntingdon?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>In <a href="/wiki/1996" title="1996">1996</a>, on the <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Express" title="Hogwarts Express">Hogwarts Express</a>, Jason stole a pair of <a href="/wiki/Luna_Lovegood" title="Luna Lovegood">Luna Lovegood</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Spectrespecs" title="Spectrespecs">Spectrespecs</a>, which he did not know how to use. <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> retrieved them giving him a periodical in return. Once at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts</a>, Jason was usually spending time at the <a href="/wiki/Great_Hall" title="Great Hall">Great Hall</a> and at the <a href="/wiki/Viaduct_Entrance" title="Viaduct Entrance">Viaduct Entrance</a>. Jason traded an <a href="/wiki/Armadillo_bile" title="Armadillo bile">Armadillo bile</a> with Harry Potter.<sup id="cite_ref-HBPG_3-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBPG-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Jason_Huntingdon?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Jason_Huntingdon?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The name "Jason" is from the Greek name Ιασων (Iason), which was derived from Greek ιασθαι (iasthai) "to heal". In Greek mythology Jason was the leader of the Argonauts. After his uncle Pelias overthrew his father as king of Iolcos, Jason went in search of the Golden Fleece in order to win back the throne. During his journeys he married the sorceress Medea, who helped him gain the fleece and kill his uncle, but who later turned against him when he fell in love with another woman.
</p>
