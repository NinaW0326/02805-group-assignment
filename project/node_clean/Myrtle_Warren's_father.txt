<p><b>Mr Warren</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggle</a><sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> and the father of <a href="/wiki/Muggle-born" title="Muggle-born">Muggle-born</a> <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch</a> <a href="/wiki/Myrtle_Warren" title="Myrtle Warren">Myrtle Elizabeth Warren</a>, who attended <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a>, and was killed by a <a href="/wiki/Salazar_Slytherin%27s_Basilisk" class="mw-redirect" title="Salazar Slytherin&#39;s Basilisk">Basilisk</a> there on <a href="/wiki/13_June" title="13 June">13 June</a>, <a href="/wiki/1943" title="1943">1943</a>. He planned to go to the school with his <a href="/wiki/Myrtle_Warren%27s_mother" title="Myrtle Warren&#39;s mother">wife</a> soon after this tragedy occurred.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>His daughter's <a href="/wiki/Ghost" title="Ghost">ghost</a>, known as Moaning Myrtle, resided in the <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Castle" title="Hogwarts Castle">girls' bathroom</a> at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts</a>.
</p>
