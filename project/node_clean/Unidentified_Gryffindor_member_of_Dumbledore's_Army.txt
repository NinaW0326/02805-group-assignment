<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_Gryffindor_member_of_Dumbledore%27s_Army?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_Gryffindor_member_of_Dumbledore%27s_Army?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Education_at_Hogwarts">Education at Hogwarts</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_Gryffindor_member_of_Dumbledore%27s_Army?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Education at Hogwarts">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_Gryffindor_member_of_Dumbledore%27s_Army?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Education at Hogwarts"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<figure class="thumb tright show-info-icon" style="width: 222px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/6/62/Unidentified_Dumbledores_armymember_n.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20161021144355" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/6/62/Unidentified_Dumbledores_armymember_n.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/220?cb=20161021144355" decoding="async" width="220" height="124" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="Unidentified Dumbledores armymember n.jpg" data-image-key="Unidentified_Dumbledores_armymember_n.jpg" /></a> 	 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:Unidentified_Dumbledores_armymember_n.jpg" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption">Unidentified Gryffindor Girl with Ginny Weasley and her twin sister in 1995</p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure>
<p>She began attending <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> with her twin sister in <a href="/wiki/1991" title="1991">1991</a> and was <a href="/wiki/Sorting_ceremony" title="Sorting ceremony">sorted</a> into <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor" title="Gryffindor">Gryffindor</a>. Her twin sister, however, was sorted into <a href="/wiki/Hufflepuff" title="Hufflepuff">Hufflepuff</a>.
</p>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Fourth_year">Fourth year</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_Gryffindor_member_of_Dumbledore%27s_Army?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Fourth year">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_Gryffindor_member_of_Dumbledore%27s_Army?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Fourth year"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<p>In <a href="/wiki/1994" title="1994">1994</a>, Hogwarts held the <a href="/wiki/Triwizard_Tournament" title="Triwizard Tournament">Triwizard Tournament</a>. Her twin sister supported <a href="/wiki/Cedric_Diggory" title="Cedric Diggory">Cedric Diggory</a> in the Tournament and wore a <a href="/wiki/Potter_Stinks_badge" title="Potter Stinks badge">Potter Stinks badge</a>. She shared <a href="/wiki/Defence_Against_the_Dark_Arts" title="Defence Against the Dark Arts">Defence Against the Dark Arts</a> with students from the other houses this year and was taught by <a href="/wiki/Professor" title="Professor">Professor</a> <a href="/wiki/Alastor_Moody" title="Alastor Moody">Alastor Moody</a>, who was actually escaped Death Eater <a href="/wiki/Bartemius_Crouch_Junior" title="Bartemius Crouch Junior">Barty Crouch Jnr</a> in disguise via <a href="/wiki/Polyjuice_Potion" title="Polyjuice Potion">Polyjuice Potion</a>. At the end of the year, Cedric Diggory was murdered under <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a>'s orders and Hogwarts held a <a href="/wiki/Memorial_feast_to_Cedric_Diggory" title="Memorial feast to Cedric Diggory">Memorial Feast</a> at which <a href="/wiki/Headmaster" title="Headmaster">Headmaster</a> <a href="/wiki/Albus_Dumbledore" title="Albus Dumbledore">Dumbledore</a> announced <a href="/wiki/Return_of_the_Dark_Lord" class="mw-redirect" title="Return of the Dark Lord">Voldemort's return</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-GOFF_4-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOFF-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Fifth_year">Fifth year</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_Gryffindor_member_of_Dumbledore%27s_Army?veaction=edit&amp;section=4" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Fifth year">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_Gryffindor_member_of_Dumbledore%27s_Army?action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Fifth year"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<p>In <a href="/wiki/1995" title="1995">1995</a>, <a href="/wiki/Dolores_Umbridge" title="Dolores Umbridge">Dolores Umbridge</a> was appointed as <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_High_Inquisitor" title="Hogwarts High Inquisitor">Hogwarts High Inquisitor</a> and <a href="/wiki/Defence_Against_the_Dark_Arts" title="Defence Against the Dark Arts">Defence Against the Dark Arts</a> teacher, so that the <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">Ministry of Magic</a> could watch over <a href="/wiki/Albus_Dumbledore" title="Albus Dumbledore">Albus Dumbledore</a> after he and <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> claimed <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a> had returned. Umbridge refused to teach students any form of practical defence, and, given the circumstances, <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione Granger</a> felt that the student body needed a teacher who would, so then an alliance called <a href="/wiki/Dumbledore%27s_Army" title="Dumbledore&#39;s Army">Dumbledore's Army</a> was formed. The Gryffindor girl's twin joined the D.A., and following in her sister's footsteps, she did so as well.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTPF_5-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTPF-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
