The <b>Head of the Muggle-Born Registration Commission</b> was a position of power within the <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">Ministry of Magic</a> for a short amount of time. It was created along with the <a href="/wiki/Muggle-Born_Registration_Commission" title="Muggle-Born Registration Commission">Muggle-Born Registration Commission</a> when <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a> was indirectly in control of the Ministry during the height of the <a href="/wiki/Second_Wizarding_War" title="Second Wizarding War">Second Wizarding War</a>, and is the leading power within this organisation. The only head of the Muggle-Born Registration Commission was <a href="/wiki/Dolores_Umbridge" title="Dolores Umbridge">Dolores Umbridge</a>, the founder of the commission. It was never intended to be a permanent committee; however, former members were recognised for being part of the Commission.

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="History">History</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Head_of_the_Muggle-Born_Registration_Commission?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: History">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Head_of_the_Muggle-Born_Registration_Commission?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: History"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<figure class="thumb tleft show-info-icon" style="width: 222px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/f/fd/Umbridge-2-.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20160721184141" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/f/fd/Umbridge-2-.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/220?cb=20160721184141" decoding="async" width="220" height="124" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="Umbridge-2-.jpg" data-image-key="Umbridge-2-.jpg" /></a> 	 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:Umbridge-2-.jpg" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption">Dolores Umbridge, the only Head of the Commission</p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure>
<p>On <a href="/wiki/1_August" title="1 August">1 August</a>, <a href="/wiki/1997" title="1997">1997</a>, <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a> and his <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a> took over the <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">Ministry of Magic</a>. <a href="/wiki/Minister_for_Magic" title="Minister for Magic">Minister for Magic</a> <a href="/wiki/Rufus_Scrimgeour" title="Rufus Scrimgeour">Rufus Scrimgeour</a> was tortured and then killed, the <a href="/wiki/Imperius_Curse" title="Imperius Curse">Imperius Cursed</a> <a href="/wiki/Pius_Thicknesse" title="Pius Thicknesse">Pius Thicknesse</a> was installed as a puppet in his place, and the Death Eaters began a campaign against their enemies, notably <a href="/wiki/Muggle-born" title="Muggle-born">Muggle-borns</a>. They were ordered to register with the <a href="/wiki/Muggle-Born_Registration_Commission" title="Muggle-Born Registration Commission">Muggle-Born Registration Commission</a>, which convicted them of stealing <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magic</a> from "real" <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizards and witches</a> (<a href="/wiki/Pure-blood" title="Pure-blood">pure-bloods</a>) and imprisoned them in <a href="/wiki/Azkaban" title="Azkaban">Azkaban</a>, though the entire trial was unfair and <a href="/wiki/Muggle-born" title="Muggle-born">Muggle-borns</a> were punished whether the evidence proved them guilty or not. Some were threatened with the <a href="/wiki/Dementor%27s_Kiss" title="Dementor&#39;s Kiss">Dementor's Kiss</a>, some were imprisoned, and some were stripped of their wands and jobs, and reduced to being <a href="/wiki/Wandless" title="Wandless">street beggars</a>. Those who refused to register, such as <a href="/wiki/Edward_Tonks" title="Edward Tonks">Ted Tonks</a>, were pursued by <a href="/wiki/Snatchers" title="Snatchers">Snatchers</a>, sometimes fatally. Dolores Umbridge, in her position as the Head of this department, enjoyed her newfound authority and sadistically tormented the many Muggle-borns; she assigned many of her subordinates to produce anti-Muggle <a href="/wiki/Mudbloods_and_the_Dangers_They_Pose_to_a_Peaceful_Pure-Blood_Society" title="Mudbloods and the Dangers They Pose to a Peaceful Pure-Blood Society">pamphlets</a> in an autonomous manner, and seemed rather unpopular due to her tyrannical and sadistic manner.
</p><p>The position was eliminated after Voldemort was killed by <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> and the Commission was disbanded in <a href="/wiki/May" title="May">May</a> of <a href="/wiki/1998" title="1998">1998</a>, its Head arrested, trialled and jailed in <a href="/wiki/Azkaban" title="Azkaban">Azkaban</a> for crimes against Muggle-borns, some of whom did not survive the ordeals of the prison themselves.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Duties">Duties</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Head_of_the_Muggle-Born_Registration_Commission?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Duties">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Head_of_the_Muggle-Born_Registration_Commission?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Duties"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<dl><dd><div class="noprint"><i>Main article&#58; <a href="/wiki/Muggle-Born_Registration_Commission" title="Muggle-Born Registration Commission">Muggle-Born Registration Commission</a></i></div></dd></dl>
<ul><li>The head of the Commission conducted the trials and directed the efforts to round up <a href="/wiki/Muggle-born" title="Muggle-born">Muggle-borns</a>, which was performed by <a href="/wiki/Snatchers" title="Snatchers">Snatchers</a>.</li>
<li>Oversaw the creation of pamphlets and similar propaganda to alert the magical community to the dangers of Muggle-borns. One such pamphlet showed a rose with a frown on its face, representing <a href="/wiki/Pure-blood" title="Pure-blood">pure-bloods</a> and a weed choking the flower, representing the Muggle-borns. The title of this particular pamphlet was <i><a href="/wiki/Mudbloods_and_the_Dangers_They_Pose_to_a_Peaceful_Pure-Blood_Society" title="Mudbloods and the Dangers They Pose to a Peaceful Pure-Blood Society">Mudbloods and the Dangers They Pose to a Peaceful Pure-Blood Society</a></i>.</li></ul>
