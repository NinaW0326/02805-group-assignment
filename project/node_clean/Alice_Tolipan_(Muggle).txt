<b>Alice Tolipan</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggle</a> musician. Along with <a href="/wiki/Luca_Caruso_(Muggle)" title="Luca Caruso (Muggle)">Luca Caruso</a>, she provided the music for the <a href="/wiki/Regency_Playhouse" title="Regency Playhouse">Regency Playhouse</a>'s adapted production of <i><a href="/wiki/Romeo_and_Juliet" title="Romeo and Juliet">Romeo and Juliet</a></i>, which was staged during the summer of <a href="/wiki/1997" title="1997">1997</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
