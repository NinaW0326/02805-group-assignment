<b>Musidora Barkwith</b> (<a href="/wiki/1520" class="mw-redirect" title="1520">1520</a>â€“<a href="/wiki/1666" class="mw-redirect" title="1666">1666</a>) was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch</a> and composer. Her unfinished work, the <i><a href="/wiki/Wizarding_Suite" title="Wizarding Suite">Wizarding Suite</a></i>, featured an <a href="/wiki/Exploding_Tuba" title="Exploding Tuba">Exploding Tuba</a>. It has been banned ever since a performance in <a href="/wiki/1902" title="1902">1902</a> blew the roof off of the <a href="/wiki/Ackerley_Town_Hall" title="Ackerley Town Hall">Town Hall</a> of <a href="/wiki/Ackerley" title="Ackerley">Ackerley</a>. For this event, she was immortalised on a bronze <a href="/wiki/Chocolate_Frog_Card" title="Chocolate Frog Card">Chocolate Frog Card</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-COSG_1-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COSG-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
