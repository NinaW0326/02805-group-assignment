<p>The <b>Trio</b>, sometimes known as the <b>Golden Trio</b>, was the popular name given to the gang of three best friends and former <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor" title="Gryffindor">Gryffindor</a> students at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a>, being <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>, <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ronald Weasley</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione Granger</a>. The trio was allied with the <a href="/wiki/Order_of_the_Phoenix" title="Order of the Phoenix">Order of the Phoenix</a>, <a href="/wiki/Dumbledore%27s_Army" title="Dumbledore&#39;s Army">Dumbledore's Army</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Albus_Dumbledore" title="Albus Dumbledore">Albus Dumbledore</a>, and in their adult lives and careers, the <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">British Ministry of Magic</a>.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Members">Members</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Trio?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Members">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Trio?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Members"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> was one of the most famous <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizards</a> of modern time, also known as the 'Boy Who Lived' and the 'Chosen One'. He is known for being the cause of Voldemort's first downfall and is responsible for the Dark Lord's final defeat. Harry was also the teacher/leader of <a href="/wiki/Dumbledore%27s_Army" title="Dumbledore&#39;s Army">Dumbledore's Army</a> and was the best in his year at <a href="/wiki/Defence_Against_the_Dark_Arts" title="Defence Against the Dark Arts">Defence Against the Dark Arts</a>. After the <a href="/wiki/Second_Wizarding_War" title="Second Wizarding War">Second Wizarding War</a> he became an <a href="/wiki/Auror" title="Auror">Auror</a> and rose to the position of Head Auror at the age of 26. By the summer of <a href="/wiki/2020" title="2020">2020</a> Harry was promoted to the Head of the <a href="/wiki/Department_of_Magical_Law_Enforcement" title="Department of Magical Law Enforcement">Department of Magical Law Enforcement</a>.
</p><p><a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ron Weasley</a> assisted Harry and Hermione in the hunt for Voldemort's <a href="/wiki/Horcrux" title="Horcrux">Horcruxes</a> and fought in the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a>. Ron was a member of Dumbledore's Army and demonstrated a talent for <a href="/wiki/Wizard%27s_Chess" title="Wizard&#39;s Chess">Wizard's Chess</a>. After the Second Wizarding War he became an Auror, alongside Harry. Ron was an Auror for two years before retiring and joining his brother <a href="/wiki/George_Weasley" title="George Weasley">George</a> at <a href="/wiki/Weasley%27s_Wizard_Wheezes" class="mw-redirect" title="Weasley&#39;s Wizard Wheezes">Weasley's Wizard Wheezes</a>.
</p><p><a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione Granger</a> assisted Harry and Ron in the hunt for Voldemort's Horcruxes and fought in the Battle of Hogwarts. Hermione was the founder of Dumbledore's army and was top of her year, while attending Hogwarts. After graduating Hogwarts she went to work for the <a href="/wiki/Ministry_of_Magic" title="Ministry of Magic">Ministry of Magic</a>, working in the <a href="/wiki/Department_for_the_Regulation_and_Control_of_Magical_Creatures" title="Department for the Regulation and Control of Magical Creatures">Department for the Regulation and Control of Magical Creatures</a> and the Department of Magical Law Enforcement. Hermione eventually became the <a href="/wiki/Minister_for_Magic" title="Minister for Magic">Minister for Magic</a>.
</p>

<ul><li><a href="/wiki/Steve_Kloves" title="Steve Kloves">Steve Kloves</a> often used "the trio" when writing the screenplays when referring to Harry, Ron, and Hermione while <a href="/wiki/J._K._Rowling" title="J. K. Rowling">J. K. Rowling</a> would use HRH for short in her writing.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li>The term has been widely adopted by fans and is used for the title of "<a href="/wiki/The_Trio_theme" title="The Trio theme">The Trio theme</a>" heard in the films of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Deathly_Hallows" title="Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows">Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows</a></i>. <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_(book_series)" title="Harry Potter (book series)"><i>Harry Potter</i></a> series author <a href="/wiki/J._K._Rowling" title="J. K. Rowling">J. K. Rowling</a> also once used the term in a <a href="/wiki/2007" title="2007">2007</a> interview.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li>Interestingly, the members of the Golden Trio have all three of the <a href="/wiki/Blood_status" title="Blood status">blood statuses</a> of the <a href="/wiki/Wizarding_world" title="Wizarding world">wizarding world</a> as Harry is a <a href="/wiki/Half-blood" title="Half-blood">half-blood</a>, Ron is a <a href="/wiki/Pure-blood" title="Pure-blood">pure-blood</a>, and Hermione is a <a href="/wiki/Muggle-born" title="Muggle-born">Muggle-born</a>.
<ul><li>Harry's a half-blood because his father James came from a family like Ron's whereas his mother Lily is a Muggle-born like Hermione.</li></ul></li>
<li>The Trio also differ from each other in appearance. They each have different <a href="/wiki/Eye" title="Eye">eye</a> colours. Harry has green eyes (although portrayed as blue in the films), Ron has blue eyes (although often portrayed as green or brown), and Hermione has brown eyes.
<ul><li>Harry and Ron flipping eye colours in the films can be attributed to a bad reaction Daniel Radcliffe (Harry) had to coloured contacts</li></ul></li>
<li>They not only have differing eye colours, but also various hair colours. Harry has black hair (although portrayed as dark brown in the films), Ron has red hair, and Hermione has brown hair (although portrayed as somewhere between light brown and dirty blonde in the films).</li>
<li>All three members are related or blood related to the <a href="/wiki/Weasley_family" title="Weasley family">Weasleys</a>, as Ron is a Weasley, Hermione married Ron making her a Weasley by marriage, and Harry married into the Weasley family through Ginny making him an in-law to the Weasley family, as well.</li>
<li>Each of their wand cores is a core of the wands made in <a href="/wiki/Ollivanders" title="Ollivanders">Ollivanders</a>: Hermione's wand's core is <a href="/wiki/Dragon_heartstring" title="Dragon heartstring">dragon heartstring</a>, Ron's first and second wand cores are <a href="/wiki/Unicorn_hair" title="Unicorn hair">unicorn hair</a>, and Harry's is <a href="/wiki/Phoenix_feather" title="Phoenix feather">phoenix tail feather</a>.</li>
<li>Interestingly, despite Harry being the leader of the Trio he is actually the youngest of the Trio. Whereas Hermione is the oldest and Ron is the second oldest.</li>
<li>The three bring different parts to the Trio, Harry brings leadership and power, Ron brings muscle and comedy, and Hermione brings intelligence and reason.</li>
<li>The three also come from different socioeconomic backgrounds: Harry comes from the quietly rich and wealthy Potter line, Ron is from the almost iconically poor Weasley family, and Hermione is not mentioned to come from any exceptional wealth or poverty, so it is assumed that her dentist parents are comfortably middle-class.</li>
<li>The Trio is an example of a <a target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener" class="external text" href="http://tvtropes.org/pmwiki/pmwiki.php/Main/FreudianTrio">Freudian Trio</a>.</li>
<li>In addition to referring to Harry, Ron, and Hermione as the "Golden Trio", some fandom members consider <a href="/wiki/Ginevra_Weasley" title="Ginevra Weasley">Ginny Weasley</a>, <a href="/wiki/Neville_Longbottom" title="Neville Longbottom">Neville Longbottom</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Luna_Lovegood" title="Luna Lovegood">Luna Lovegood</a> to comprise a second "Silver Trio".</li></ul>
