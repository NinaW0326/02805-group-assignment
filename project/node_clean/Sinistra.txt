<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Sinistra?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Sinistra?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The name "Sinistra" is the name of a <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sinistra" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Sinistra">star</a> in the constellation <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ophiuchus" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Ophiuchus">Ophiuchus</a>, which probably means that the family, like the <a href="/wiki/House_of_Black" title="House of Black">House of Black</a>, named their children after those of stars and constellations.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Sinistra?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Sinistra?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>Professor Sinistra was apparently considered for the role of <a href="/wiki/Defence_Against_the_Dark_Arts" title="Defence Against the Dark Arts">Defence Against the Dark Arts</a> <a href="/wiki/Professor" title="Professor">professor</a>, as the name was written next to those of <a href="/wiki/Enid_Pettigrew" title="Enid Pettigrew">Enid Pettigrew</a> and <a href="/wiki/Oakden_Hernshaw" title="Oakden Hernshaw">Oakden Hernshaw</a>. However, the name was not written with a year next to it, implying that the name may have just been considered.</li></ul>
