<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Ethan_Parkin?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Ethan_Parkin?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Ethan Parkin was born at some point in around the late <a href="/wiki/1940s" title="1940s">1940s</a> or early <a href="/wiki/1950s" title="1950s">1950s</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-age_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-age-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> He attended <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> in the <a href="/wiki/1960s" title="1960s">1960s</a>, and played on the <a href="/wiki/Quidditch_team" title="Quidditch team">Quidditch team</a> of his house. In the <a href="/wiki/1966" title="1966">1966</a>â€“<a href="/wiki/1967" title="1967">1967</a> school year, he played in the highest-scoring Quidditch match in Hogwarts history, and his team won the match.<sup id="cite_ref-TLSQ2_5-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-TLSQ2-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>His daughter, <a href="/wiki/Skye_Parkin" title="Skye Parkin">Skye</a>, played <a href="/wiki/Chaser" title="Chaser">Chaser</a> as a student at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a>, in the <a href="/wiki/1980s" title="1980s">1980s</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-HM_4-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HM-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>He was injured in a Quidditch match against the <a href="/wiki/Montrose_Magpies" title="Montrose Magpies">Montrose Magpies</a>, resulting in Skye temporarily leaving Hogwarts to check on her father and keep him company as he recovered.<sup id="cite_ref-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup> Fortunately, Ethan was recovering rather rapidly.<sup id="cite_ref-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>He came to watch Skye's first house match, and expected to see a <a href="/wiki/Parkin%27s_Pincer" title="Parkin&#39;s Pincer">Parkin's Pincer</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup> After the match, he gave his daughter a new <a href="/wiki/Comet_260" title="Comet 260">Comet 260</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-TLSQ_3-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-TLSQ-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>In the <a href="/wiki/1986%E2%80%931987_school_year" title="1986â€“1987 school year">1986â€“1987 school year</a>, Ethan Parkin came to Hogwarts during the final match of the <a href="/wiki/Inter-House_Quidditch_Cup" title="Inter-House Quidditch Cup">Quidditch Cup</a>. His arrival overshadowed the match.<sup id="cite_ref-TLSQ2_5-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-TLSQ2-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
