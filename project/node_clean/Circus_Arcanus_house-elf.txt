<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Circus_Arcanus_house-elf?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Circus_Arcanus_house-elf?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<figure class="thumb tleft show-info-icon" style="width: 182px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/4/49/Circus_Arcanus_House-elf.gif/revision/latest?cb=20200713123623" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/4/49/Circus_Arcanus_House-elf.gif/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/180?cb=20200713123623" decoding="async" width="180" height="75" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="Circus Arcanus House-elf.gif" data-image-key="Circus_Arcanus_House-elf.gif" /></a> 	 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:Circus_Arcanus_House-elf.gif" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption">The House-elf packs up the Circus and leaves with <a href="/wiki/Skender" title="Skender">Skender</a></p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure>
<p>This house elf was employed by the <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> <a href="/wiki/Skender" title="Skender">Skender</a> as a personal servant to help him with the running of the <a href="/wiki/Circus_Arcanus" title="Circus Arcanus">Circus Arcanus</a>. Following the riot which broke out in the circus in <a href="/wiki/1927" title="1927">1927</a> <a href="/wiki/Paris" title="Paris">Paris</a>, due to <a href="/wiki/Credence_Barebone" title="Credence Barebone">Credence Barebone</a> releasing some caged <a href="/wiki/Firedrake" title="Firedrake">Firedrakes</a> which subsequently <a href="/wiki/Fire" title="Fire">burned</a> down the circus, this House-elf used his <a href="/wiki/House-elf_magic" title="House-elf magic">magic</a> to assist in master in packing up the circus equipment in Skender's trunk and leaving the city by carriage.<sup id="cite_ref-COGS_3-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COGS-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>During the <a href="/wiki/2020s" title="2020s">2020s</a>, he appeared as a <a href="/wiki/Foundable" title="Foundable">Foundable</a> during the <a href="/wiki/Calamity" title="Calamity">Calamity</a>. Volunteer members of the <a href="/wiki/Statute_of_Secrecy_Task_Force" title="Statute of Secrecy Task Force">Statute of Secrecy Task Force</a> were able to free him, allowing him to return to his original time.<sup id="cite_ref-WU_1-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-WU-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Magical_abilities_and_skills">Magical abilities and skills</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Circus_Arcanus_house-elf?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Circus_Arcanus_house-elf?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li><b><a href="/wiki/House-elf_magic" title="House-elf magic">House-elf magic</a></b>: Being a house-elf, he could perform <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magic</a> <a href="/wiki/Wandless_magic" title="Wandless magic">without</a> a wand or <a href="/wiki/Incantation" title="Incantation">incantations</a>, which allowed him to effortlessly use magic to help pack up the Circus Arcanus equipment for his master.<sup id="cite_ref-COGS_3-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COGS-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Apparition" title="Apparition">Apparition</a></b>: This house-elf could also apparate at will.<sup id="cite_ref-COGS_3-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COGS-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup></li></ul>
