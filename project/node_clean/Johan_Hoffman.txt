<b>Johan Hoffman</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Seer" title="Seer">Seer</a> and a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> who made a <a href="/wiki/Prophecy" title="Prophecy">prophecy</a> in <a href="/wiki/January" title="January">January</a> of <a href="/wiki/1665" class="mw-redirect" title="1665">1665</a>. His or her prophecy was stored in the <a href="/wiki/Hall_of_Prophecy" title="Hall of Prophecy">Hall of Prophecy</a> in the <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">British Ministry of Magic</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-HPLE_1-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HPLE-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
