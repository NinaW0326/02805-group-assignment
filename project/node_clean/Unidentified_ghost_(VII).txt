This headless <b>ghost</b> resided at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a>. In <a href="/wiki/1991" title="1991">1991</a>, it was seen in the <a href="/wiki/Great_Hall" title="Great Hall">Great Hall</a>, just standing. He visited the <a href="/wiki/Helena_Ravenclaw" title="Helena Ravenclaw">Grey Lady</a> in the school's <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Library" title="Hogwarts Library">library</a>, riding around on a horse.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> In <a href="/wiki/1993" title="1993">1993</a>, he was seen chasing <a href="/wiki/Ghost_of_a_highwayman" title="Ghost of a highwayman">another ghost</a> to get his head back.
