<b>Josefina Calderon</b>&#160;was a <a href="/wiki/17th_century" title="17th century">17th-century</a> <a href="/wiki/Mexico" title="Mexico">Mexican</a> <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch</a> and <a href="/wiki/Healer" title="Healer">Healer</a> who became the wife of <a href="/wiki/Chadwick_Boot" title="Chadwick Boot">Chadwick Boot</a>, one of the founders of <a href="/wiki/Ilvermorny_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Ilvermorny School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Ilvermorny School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-Pottermore_1-12" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Pottermore-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>The couple established the <a href="/wiki/Calderon-Boot_family" title="Calderon-Boot family">Calderon-Boot family</a>, which remains one of America's most prominent wizarding families today.<sup id="cite_ref-Pottermore_1-13" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Pottermore-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
