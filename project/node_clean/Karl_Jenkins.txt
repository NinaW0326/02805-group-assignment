<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_life">Early life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Karl_Jenkins?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Early life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Karl_Jenkins?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Early life"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Karl Jenkins was born somewhere in the <a href="/wiki/Great_Britain" title="Great Britain">United Kingdom</a> between <a href="/wiki/1_September" title="1 September">1 September</a> <a href="/wiki/2006" title="2006">2005</a> and <a href="/wiki/31_August" title="31 August">31 August</a> <a href="/wiki/2007" title="2007">2006</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-act1scene4_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-act1scene4-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="First_year">First year</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Karl_Jenkins?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: First year">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Karl_Jenkins?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: First year"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Karl began attending <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> on 1 September <a href="/wiki/2017" title="2017">2017</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-act1scene4_3-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-act1scene4-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> As the <a href="/wiki/Sorting_ceremony" title="Sorting ceremony">sorting ceremony</a> was starting, he expressed wonder at the prospect of <a href="/wiki/Albus_Potter" title="Albus Potter">Albus Potter</a>, son of the famous <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>, being in his year.<sup id="cite_ref-act1scene4_3-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-act1scene4-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>When Albus Potter proved unable to get his <a href="/wiki/Broomstick" title="Broomstick">broomstick</a> to rise off the ground during their first <a href="/wiki/Flying_(class)" title="Flying (class)">flying</a> lesson with <a href="/wiki/Rolanda_Hooch" title="Rolanda Hooch">Madam Hooch</a>, Karl teased him, calling him the "<a href="/wiki/Slytherin" title="Slytherin">Slytherin</a> <a href="/wiki/Squib" title="Squib">Squib</a>."<sup id="cite_ref-act1scene4_3-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-act1scene4-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Second_year">Second year</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Karl_Jenkins?veaction=edit&amp;section=4" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Second year">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Karl_Jenkins?action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Second year"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>During a <a href="/wiki/Second_year" title="Second year">second-year</a> <a href="/wiki/Potions_(class)" title="Potions (class)">Potions</a> lesson, Albus and <a href="/wiki/Scorpius_Malfoy" title="Scorpius Malfoy">Scorpius Malfoy</a> had difficulty preparing their <a href="/wiki/Potion" title="Potion">potion</a>, prompting Karl to tell the other students in the class to "leave [Albus] and Voldemortâ€™s child to it."<sup id="cite_ref-act1scene4_3-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-act1scene4-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Fourth_year">Fourth year</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Karl_Jenkins?veaction=edit&amp;section=5" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Fourth year">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Karl_Jenkins?action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Fourth year"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Upon returning to <a href="/wiki/2020" title="2020">2020</a> after <a href="/wiki/Time-Turner" title="Time-Turner">altering the past</a>, <a href="/wiki/Scorpius_Malfoy" title="Scorpius Malfoy">Scorpius Malfoy</a> found himself in an <a href="/wiki/Alternate_reality" title="Alternate reality">alternate reality</a> in which <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a> won, and was greeted as the "Scorpion King" by Karl Jenkins on the Hogwarts grounds.<sup id="cite_ref-act3scene2_4-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-act3scene2-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/Yann_Fredericks" title="Yann Fredericks">Yann Fredericks</a> asked Scorpius if he was still on for an event the following night and Karl enthused they were both ready to "spill some proper <a href="/wiki/Muggle-born" title="Muggle-born">Mudblood</a> guts."<sup id="cite_ref-act3scene2_4-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-act3scene2-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
