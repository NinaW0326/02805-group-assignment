<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Dimitar_Draganov?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Dimitar_Draganov?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>Dimitar is a Bulgarian name of Greek origin, stemming from the name of the Greek fertility goddess Demeter.</li>
<li>Draganov is the patronymic-turned-surname derivative of Dragan, a Slavic name originating from <i>"драг" [drag]</i>, meaning "dear".</li></ul>
