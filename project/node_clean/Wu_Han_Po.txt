</p><p><b>Wu Han Po</b>, also known by the nickname <b>Silver Dragon</b>, was an <a href="/wiki/United_States_of_America" title="United States of America">American</a> <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizarding</a> criminal who operated in the <a href="/wiki/1920s" title="1920s">1920s</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-FB_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-FB-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>In <a href="/wiki/1926" title="1926">1926</a>, the <a href="/wiki/Magical_Congress_of_the_United_States_of_America" title="Magical Congress of the United States of America">Magical Congress of the United States of America</a> issued a wanted poster for Han Po, which stated that he was wanted for <a href="/wiki/Muggle" class="mw-redirect" title="Muggle">No-Maj</a> smuggling and murder, and described him as "bearing <a href="/wiki/Wand" title="Wand">wand</a> and extremely dangerous."<sup id="cite_ref-FB_1-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-FB-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> A reward of 4,500 <a href="/wiki/Dragot" title="Dragot">Dragots</a> was offered for his arrest.<sup id="cite_ref-FB_1-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-FB-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
