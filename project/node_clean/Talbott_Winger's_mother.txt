<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Talbott_Winger%27s_mother?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Talbott_Winger%27s_mother?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>At some point in her life, she trained to become <a href="/wiki/Healer" title="Healer">Healer</a>, and worked as one at <a href="/wiki/St_Mungo%27s_Hospital_for_Magical_Maladies_and_Injuries" title="St Mungo&#39;s Hospital for Magical Maladies and Injuries">St Mungo's Hospital for Magical Maladies and Injuries</a>, a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizarding</a> hospital in <a href="/wiki/London" title="London">London</a>. She was also an <a href="/wiki/Animagus" title="Animagus">Animagus</a>, and in preparation for an attack, taught her son how to become one.
</p><p>She and her husband were vehemently opposed to the <a href="/wiki/Dark_Wizard" title="Dark Wizard">Dark Wizard</a> <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a> and his <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a> reign of terror across <a href="/wiki/Great_Britain" title="Great Britain">Great Britain</a>, although she and her husband for some reason did not join the <a href="/wiki/Order_of_the_Phoenix" title="Order of the Phoenix">Order of the Phoenix</a>. One day, she and Talbott's father were murdered by <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a>, though their son managed to escape, due to Animagus ability to transform into an <a href="/wiki/Eagle" title="Eagle">Eagle</a> at will, allowing him to fly away to safety.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Personality_and_traits">Personality and traits</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Talbott_Winger%27s_mother?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Personality and traits">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Talbott_Winger%27s_mother?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Personality and traits"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>She was evidently a mothering and kind soul, as shown by her work as a <a href="/wiki/Healer" title="Healer">Healer</a>, and by nurturing upbringing of her son. She evidently <a href="/wiki/Love" title="Love">loved</a> and cared about her son Talbott immensely and his welfare, as she taught her son to become an Animagus, in order to help him escape danger should he have needed to, an ability which saved her son's life. She was also clearly a very powerful and accomplished witch in her own right, as shown by her career path and her very remarkable ability to have become an Animagus.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Magical_abilities_and_skills">Magical abilities and skills</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Talbott_Winger%27s_mother?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Talbott_Winger%27s_mother?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li><b><a href="/wiki/Healing_magic" title="Healing magic">Healing magic</a></b>: As a professional <a href="/wiki/Healer" title="Healer">Healer</a> employed at <a href="/wiki/St_Mungo%27s_Hospital_for_Magical_Maladies_and_Injuries" title="St Mungo&#39;s Hospital for Magical Maladies and Injuries">St Mungo's Hospital for Magical Maladies and Injuries</a>, she would have been an expert in the art of medical <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magic</a>, including with <a href="/wiki/Healing_spell" title="Healing spell">healing spells</a>, healing potions and other magical remedies.</li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Transfiguration" title="Transfiguration">Transfiguration</a></b>: Talbott's mother was remarkably skilled in Self-Transfiguration that she was able to become an <a href="/wiki/Animagus" title="Animagus">Animagus</a>. She could transform into a <a href="/wiki/White_Swan" title="White Swan">White Swan</a>&#160;at will. In addition, she would have received at least an "<a href="/wiki/Exceeds_Expectations" title="Exceeds Expectations">Exceeds Expectations</a>" or an "<a href="/wiki/Outstanding" title="Outstanding">Outstanding</a>" in this <a href="/wiki/Transfiguration_(class)" title="Transfiguration (class)">subject</a> at both <a href="/wiki/Ordinary_Wizarding_Level" title="Ordinary Wizarding Level">O.W.L.</a> and <a href="/wiki/Nastily_Exhausting_Wizarding_Test" title="Nastily Exhausting Wizarding Test">N.E.W.T.</a> level, as it is a compulsory requirement for Healer training.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP29_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP29-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Potion" title="Potion">Potions</a></b>: For her career, she would have been very skilled and knowledgeable in the brewing of various healing potions. She would have received either an "Exceeds Expectations" or "Outstanding" at both O.W.L. and N.E.W.T. level in the <a href="/wiki/Potions_(class)" title="Potions (class)">subject</a>, as is the requirement for all Healers.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP29_3-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP29-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> She would have also successfully brewed the <a href="/wiki/Animagus_potion" title="Animagus potion">Animagus potion</a> in order to complete her Animagus training, a potion known for being very time-consuming and complex, showing her to have been highly proficient in the art of potion-making.</li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Herbology" title="Herbology">Herbology</a></b>: As part of her job, she believed in using fresh herbal ingredients for healing, so she kept a garden in her home of magical and mundane plants. It also influenced Talbott to be interested in the subject at Hogwarts. She would have received either an "Exceeds Expectations" or "Outstanding in this subject at both N.E.W.T and O.W.L. level, as it is a compulsory requirement for Healer training.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP29_3-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP29-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Charm" title="Charm">Charms</a></b>: As a Healer, she would have achieved either an "Exceeds Expectations" or "Outstanding" at both O.W.L. and N.E.W.T. level in this <a href="/wiki/Charms_(class)" title="Charms (class)">subject</a>, as it is a compulsory requirement for Healer training.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP29_3-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP29-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Defence_Against_the_Dark_Arts" title="Defence Against the Dark Arts">Defence Against the Dark Arts</a></b>: She would received either an "Exceeds Expectations" or "Outstanding" at both O.W.L. and N.E.W.T. level in this subject, as it is a compulsory subject requirement for all Healers.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP29_3-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP29-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> She would have been proficient with medicating or curing health conditions and injuries caused by <a href="/wiki/Dark_Arts" title="Dark Arts">dark magic</a>, such as by using <a href="/wiki/Counter-curse" title="Counter-curse">counter-curses</a>.</li></ul>
