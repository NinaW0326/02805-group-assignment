<p><b>Professor Septima Vector</b> (fl. <a href="/wiki/1974" title="1974">1974</a> – <a href="/wiki/1996" title="1996">1996</a>) was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch</a> and <a href="/wiki/Professor" title="Professor">Professor</a> of <a href="/wiki/Arithmancy" title="Arithmancy">Arithmancy</a> at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> from at least the <a href="/wiki/1991%E2%80%931992_school_year" title="1991–1992 school year">1991–1992 school year</a>. She may have also been the <a href="/wiki/Advanced_Arithmancy_Studies" title="Advanced Arithmancy Studies">Advanced Arithmancy Studies</a> teacher.
</p><p>Professor Vector was known among students as a "really strict" teacher, so much so that some pupils were unsure whether to take her classes. She was also known for giving her pupils plenty of highly complicated and difficult homework. Her subject was <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione Granger</a>'s favourite subject while she was in school, but <a href="/wiki/Fay_Dunbar%27s_friend" class="mw-redirect" title="Fay Dunbar&#39;s friend">one of her roommates</a> did not like it because she found it too difficult.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Septima_Vector?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Septima_Vector?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_life">Early life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Septima_Vector?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Early life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Septima_Vector?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Early life"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Nothing is known about Vector's early life, though she likely attended a Wizarding School such as <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> and excelled in <a href="/wiki/Arithmancy" title="Arithmancy">Arithmancy</a>, and may even have taken <a href="/wiki/Advanced_Arithmancy_Studies" title="Advanced Arithmancy Studies">Advanced Arithmancy Studies</a>.
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Career_at_Hogwarts">Career at Hogwarts</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Septima_Vector?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Career at Hogwarts">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Septima_Vector?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Career at Hogwarts"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<div class="quote">
<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince">"<i>We are pleased to welcome a new member of staff this year</i>"</span></dd>
<dd>&#8212;The <a href="/wiki/Headmaster" title="Headmaster">Head</a> announcing a new teacher's arrival.<sup class="noprint"><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Half-Blood_Prince" title="Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince">[src]</a></sup></dd>
<dd></dd></dl></div><p>At an unknown point in her life, Vector accepted a teaching position at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School</a>. She became the <a href="/wiki/Professor" title="Professor">Professor</a> of <a href="/wiki/Arithmancy" title="Arithmancy">Arithmancy</a>, being given <a href="/wiki/Classroom_7A" title="Classroom 7A">Classroom 7A</a> on the seventh floor of <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Castle" title="Hogwarts Castle">Hogwarts Castle</a> to teach her lessons in, and presumably an office elsewhere on the school grounds. Her relationship with the rest of the staff is unknown, though she was considered very strict and old-fashioned by some of her students, so she may have been like <a href="/wiki/Minerva_McGonagall" title="Minerva McGonagall">Minerva McGonagall</a> in personality.
</p><figure class="thumb tleft show-info-icon" style="width: 252px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/a/a0/Harry-potter1-septima.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20170904141209" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/a/a0/Harry-potter1-septima.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/250?cb=20170904141209" decoding="async" width="250" height="104" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="Harry-potter1-septima.jpg" data-image-key="Harry-potter1-septima.jpg" /></a> 	 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:Harry-potter1-septima.jpg" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption">Vector sat by <a href="/wiki/Rubeus_Hagrid" title="Rubeus Hagrid">Rubeus Hagrid</a> during the Start-of-Term Feast</p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure>
<p>Another class, called <a href="/wiki/Advanced_Arithmancy_Studies" title="Advanced Arithmancy Studies">Advanced Arithmancy Studies</a>, was known to be taught at Hogwarts. It is likely that Professor Vector also took this class. The <a href="/wiki/Advanced_Arithmancy_Studies_classroom" title="Advanced Arithmancy Studies classroom">Advanced Arithmancy Studies classroom</a> was located on the first floor of <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Castle" title="Hogwarts Castle">Hogwarts Castle</a>, in the <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Turris_Magnus" title="Hogwarts Turris Magnus">Turris Magnus</a>.
</p>

She also witnessed as Harry Potter was <a href="/wiki/Sorting_ceremony" title="Sorting ceremony">sorted</a> into <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor" title="Gryffindor">Gryffindor House</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-PSF_4-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PSF-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>

<dd>&#8212;<a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ron Weasley</a> concerning <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione Granger</a>'s impossible timetable.<sup class="noprint"><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Prisoner_of_Azkaban" title="Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban">[src]</a></sup></dd>
<dd></dd></dl></div><p>In 1993, Hermione Granger began studying Arithmancy with Professor Vector and excelled in the class. After one lesson, they had a talk about their lesson the following day, which <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ronald Weasley</a> overheard, and was confused, as Hermione had <a href="/wiki/Care_of_Magical_Creatures" title="Care of Magical Creatures">Care of Magical Creatures</a> the day before, and so could not have been in Arithmancy. In reality, she had been using a <a href="/wiki/Time-Turner" title="Time-Turner">Time-Turner</a> to get to all her lessons.<sup id="cite_ref-POA12_5-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-POA12-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Also that year, <a href="/wiki/Fay_Dunbar%27s_friend" class="mw-redirect" title="Fay Dunbar&#39;s friend">a ginger girl</a>, roommate of Hermione and best friend of <a href="/wiki/Fay_Dunbar" title="Fay Dunbar">Fay Dunbar</a>, took her subject as well. However, unlike her, she found it hard, and confided in Hermione that it was too difficult for her.<sup id="cite_ref-POAVG_6-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-POAVG-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<div class="quote">
<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire"><b>Hermione</b>: "<i>Lots of homework? <a href="/wiki/Professor" title="Professor">Professor</a> Vector didn't give </i>us<i> any at all.</i>"</span></dd>
<dd><b>Ron</b>: "<i>Well, bully for Professor Vector.</i>"</dd>
<dd>&#8212; <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione Granger</a> and <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ron Weasley</a> in <a href="/wiki/1994" title="1994">1994</a>.<sup class="noprint"><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Goblet_of_Fire" title="Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire">[src]</a></sup></dd></dl>
</div><p>On September 1st, 1994, the first day of the <a href="/wiki/1994-1995_school_year" class="mw-redirect" title="1994-1995 school year">1994-1995 school year</a>, Professor Vector did not give her fourth year students any homework, an unusual occurrence, which <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione Granger</a> mentioned after <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ronald Weasley</a> told her that he had had a lot of homework.<sup id="cite_ref-GOF13_7-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF13-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup> She was also presumably present for the arrival of the Triwizard Tournament participants, the <a href="/wiki/Yule_Ball" title="Yule Ball">Yule Ball</a> and the memorial feast for Cedric Diggory at the end of the year.<sup id="cite_ref-GOF_8-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup>
</p><h4><span id="1995–1996_school_year"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="1995.E2.80.931996_school_year"><a href="/wiki/1995%E2%80%931996_school_year" title="1995–1996 school year">1995–1996 school year</a></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Septima_Vector?veaction=edit&amp;section=7" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: 1995–1996 school year">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Septima_Vector?action=edit&amp;section=7" title="Edit section: 1995–1996 school year"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<div class="quote">
<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix">"<i> </i>[Hagrid]<i> waved a hand and narrowly missed concussing a frightened-looking Professor Vector, who was passing.</i>"</span></dd>
<dd>&#8212;Professor Vector leaving the <a href="/wiki/Great_Hall" title="Great Hall">Great Hall</a> one morning.<sup class="noprint"><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Order_of_the_Phoenix" title="Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix">[src]</a></sup></dd>
<dd></dd></dl></div><p>Professor Vector was once again teaching at Hogwarts in 1995, where she was present at the Start-of-Term Feast. When the new DADA teacher, <a href="/wiki/Dolores_Umbridge" title="Dolores Umbridge">Dolores Umbridge</a>, rudely interrupted Dumbledore to give a speech, Professor Vector and her colleagues were stunned at her audacity. Like her student Hermione Granger, Professor Vector and the other teachers understood the true gravity of the speech (the Ministry was interfering at Hogwarts) and did not clap when Umbridge finished her speech. Professor Umbridge was later appointed High Inquisitor with powers to inspect and dismiss teachers she felt were not up to scratch (or rather, those that were loyal to Dumbledore). Professor Vector's classes were presumably inspected, but she likely proved to be a competent teacher as she was not dismissed or put on probation.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP_9-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>When Rubeus Hagrid returned from a mission given to him by Headmaster <a href="/wiki/Albus_Dumbledore" title="Albus Dumbledore">Dumbledore</a>, he had a conversation with <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>, <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ron Weasley</a>, and Hermione Granger, Professor Vector walked past them and was almost knocked off her feet by Hagrid. As she was passing them and they were entering the <a href="/wiki/Entrance_Hall" title="Entrance Hall">Entrance Hall</a>, she had presumably come downstairs to the <a href="/wiki/Great_Hall" title="Great Hall">Great Hall</a> to eat.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP20_10-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP20-10">&#91;10&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Also that year, one boy told someone that he was unsure if he wanted to do <a href="/wiki/Arithmancy_(class)" title="Arithmancy (class)">Arithmancy</a> because of Professor Vector's strict demeanour<sup id="cite_ref-OTPg_11-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OTPg-11">&#91;11&#93;</a></sup>.
</p><p>Later in the year, Umbridge became Headmistress after Dumbledore was ousted from Hogwarts, but Professor Vector and her fellow staff members did nothing to help her and instead found means of annoying or hindering her. Indeed, when fireworks went berserk all over the place and a corridor was turned into a swamp (both times courtesy of the Weasley twins), Professor Vector did not attempt to remove either obstacle and took great pleasure in having the Headmistress run all over the castle getting rid of escaped fireworks in the classrooms. When Hogwarts was restored to normalcy and Umbridge was ousted and pursued from the premises by Peeves, Professor Vector and the other teachers did nothing to halt the students from chasing after her.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTP_9-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTP-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="1996-1997_school_year">1996-1997 school year</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Septima_Vector?veaction=edit&amp;section=8" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: 1996-1997 school year">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Septima_Vector?action=edit&amp;section=8" title="Edit section: 1996-1997 school year"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<div class="quote">
<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince">"<i><a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione</a> </i>[...]<i> dashed off to see Professor Vector about a mistake she thought she might have made in her last <a href="/wiki/Arithmancy_essay" title="Arithmancy essay">Arithmancy essay</a>.</i>"</span></dd>
<dd>&#8212;Professor Vector being visited by <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione Granger</a> in May.<sup class="noprint"><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Half-Blood_Prince" title="Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince">[src]</a></sup></dd>
<dd></dd></dl></div><p>In 1996, Hermione Granger thought she may had made a mistake in her homework and visited Professor Vector to discuss it.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP24_12-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP24-12">&#91;12&#93;</a></sup>
</p><h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Later_life">Later life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Septima_Vector?veaction=edit&amp;section=9" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Later life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Septima_Vector?action=edit&amp;section=9" title="Edit section: Later life"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>It is likely that Professor Vector participated in the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a> in <a href="/wiki/May" title="May">May</a>, <a href="/wiki/1998" title="1998">1998</a>. Her later life is unknown. If she did participate in the Battle, she presumably survived, as her name was not given among the <a href="/wiki/Fallen_Fifty" title="Fallen Fifty">Fallen Fifty</a>.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Physical_appearance">Physical appearance</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Septima_Vector?veaction=edit&amp;section=10" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Physical appearance">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Septima_Vector?action=edit&amp;section=10" title="Edit section: Physical appearance"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Professor Vector was a witch with long, thin black hair with a fair skin colour. She often wore red robes with a matching pointed hat.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Personality_and_traits">Personality and traits</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Septima_Vector?veaction=edit&amp;section=11" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Personality and traits">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Septima_Vector?action=edit&amp;section=11" title="Edit section: Personality and traits"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Professor Vector was described as very strict by a student who was not sure if he should take her lessons.<sup id="cite_ref-OTPg_11-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OTPg-11">&#91;11&#93;</a></sup> She was also said to have given out masses of complicated homework to her students, who were all <a href="/wiki/Third_year" title="Third year">third years</a> or over, though appeared to be rather sociable with the other teachers. In addition to her strictness, she was shown to be a competent and skilled teacher, as her lessons passed inspection by Dolores Umbridge; furthermore, she taught Arithmancy for at least six years at Hogwarts, proving her competency and experience as a professor.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Magical_abilities_and_skills">Magical abilities and skills</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Septima_Vector?veaction=edit&amp;section=12" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Septima_Vector?action=edit&amp;section=12" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li><b><a href="/wiki/Arithmancy" title="Arithmancy">Arithmancy</a></b>: Vector was a highly accomplished, knowledgeable and talented <a href="/wiki/Arithmancer" title="Arithmancer">Arithmancer</a>, teaching the <a href="/wiki/Arithmancy_(class)" title="Arithmancy (class)">subject</a> for at least six years, showing her deep and rich knowledge and expertise in this very difficult and complicated branch of <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magic</a>.</li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Charm" title="Charm">Charms</a></b>: Professor Vector was capable of conjuring a non-corporeal <a href="/wiki/Patronus_Charm" title="Patronus Charm">Patronus</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-LEGO_3-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-LEGO-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> which is a mark of superior <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magical</a> ability and skill, meaning she would have been a skilful and powerful <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch</a> with considerable talent in charm-work, given the immense difficulty of casting the charm.</li></ul>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Septima_Vector?veaction=edit&amp;section=13" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Septima_Vector?action=edit&amp;section=13" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Septima most likely comes directly from Latin <i>Septima</i> which translates to "the seventh" (female form). As seven is the most powerful magical number, and Arithmancy deals with the numbers appearing in magic, this name suits the character well. A "vector" is a concept in physics and mathematics, specifying a quantity that has both magnitude and direction, and represented by an arrow.
</p>
