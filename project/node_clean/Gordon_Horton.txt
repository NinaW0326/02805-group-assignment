<b>Gordon Horton</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> who played as the <a href="/wiki/Keeper" title="Keeper">Keeper</a> for the <a href="/wiki/Quidditch" title="Quidditch">Quidditch</a> <a href="/wiki/Quidditch_team" title="Quidditch team">team</a> the <a href="/wiki/Chudley_Cannons" title="Chudley Cannons">Chudley Cannons</a>.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Gordon_Horton?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Gordon_Horton?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>He was attacked and injured by a <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magical</a> <a href="/wiki/Beast" title="Beast">beast</a> at the <a href="/wiki/Chudley_Cannons%27_training_grounds" class="mw-redirect" title="Chudley Cannons&#39; training grounds">Chudley Cannons' training grounds</a>. He was sent to <a href="/wiki/St_Mungo%27s_Hospital_for_Magical_Maladies_and_Injuries" title="St Mungo&#39;s Hospital for Magical Maladies and Injuries">St Mungo's Hospital for Magical Maladies and Injuries</a> to be treated by a <a href="/wiki/Healer" title="Healer">Healer</a>. <a href="/wiki/Omar_Abasi" title="Omar Abasi">Omar Abasi</a> concluded that he had a mild concussion from his fall.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>It was later discovered that he had been attacked by a <a href="/wiki/Hippogriff" title="Hippogriff">Hippogriff</a> nesting with its young in the nearby <a href="/wiki/Old_Bell_Tower" title="Old Bell Tower">Old Bell Tower</a>.
</p>
