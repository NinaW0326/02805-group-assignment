<b>Ethelred the Ever-Ready</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Middle_Ages" title="Middle Ages">medieval</a> <a href="/wiki/Dark_Wizard" title="Dark Wizard">Dark Wizard</a>, infamous for being offended for no reason and <a href="/wiki/Curse" title="Curse">cursing</a> innocent bystanders. Eventually he was imprisoned, and subsequently <a href="/wiki/Death" title="Death">died</a> in jail.<sup id="cite_ref-PSG_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PSG-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-COSG_1-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COSG-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-POAG_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-POAG-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Magical_abilities_and_skills">Magical abilities and skills</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Ethelred_the_Ever-Ready?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Ethelred_the_Ever-Ready?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li><b><a href="/wiki/Dark_Arts" title="Dark Arts">Dark Arts</a></b>: Ethelred was skilfull with the casting of unpleasant <a href="/wiki/Curse" title="Curse">curses</a> during his lifetime, which he often inflicted on innocent individuals unprovoked, gaining him his sinister and disturbed reputation.<sup id="cite_ref-COSG_1-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COSG-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup></li></ul>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Ethelred_the_Ever-Ready?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Ethelred_the_Ever-Ready?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>The name <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ethelred_the_Unready" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Ethelred the Unready">Ethelred</a> is composed from Old English elements æðele, meaning "noble", and ræd, meaning "counsel", being bombastic and common in the Royal House of Wessex.</li></ul>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Ethelred_the_Ever-Ready?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Ethelred_the_Ever-Ready?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>Ethelred the Every-Ready's name was likely inspired by King <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ethelred_the_Unready" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Ethelred the Unready">Ethelred II of England</a>, who was known as <i>the Unready</i> after his death (from Old English <i>Æþelræd Unræd).</i> In that time, "unready" was probably understood pejorative as "evil counsel" (opposite to the name) or "bad plan"; the king was considered ill-advised or indecisive in his conflict with the Danes.</li></ul>
