<p>When arriving at the battle he was violently grabbed by <a href="/wiki/Devil%27s_Snare" title="Devil&#39;s Snare">Devil's Snare</a> in the grounds of <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts</a>, which held him down and tried to strangle him. However, he managed to break free and continued to fight in the battle. He was later killed during the battle.<sup id="cite_ref-DHF2_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DHF2-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_Giant_in_the_Battle_of_Hogwarts_(II)?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_Giant_in_the_Battle_of_Hogwarts_(II)?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>This giant is played by <a href="/wiki/Gary_Sayer" title="Gary Sayer">Gary Sayer</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Deathly_Hallows:_Part_2" title="Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 2">film adaptation</a> of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Deathly_Hallows" title="Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows">Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows</a></i>.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li>In the film he had a bit of hair, but in the video game he was completely bald.</li>
<li>In <i><a href="/wiki/LEGO_Harry_Potter:_Years_5-7" title="LEGO Harry Potter: Years 5-7">LEGO Harry Potter: Years 5-7</a></i> he is seen standing in a courtyard in which he sees the trio. When he runs after them he collapses with the wall and falls to the ground. Some of the torches and a bit roof burries him. However he breaks free and is later seen close to the <a href="/wiki/Wooden_Bridge" title="Wooden Bridge">Wooden Bridge</a> fighting <a href="/wiki/Grawp" title="Grawp">Grawp</a>. He is then killed by two <a href="/wiki/Suit_of_armour" title="Suit of armour">suits of armour</a>.</li>
<li>This giant's reddish skin and the small, horn-like protrusions over his eyes give him a resemblance to an <a href="/wiki/Oni" title="Oni">Oni</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Ogre" title="Ogre">ogre</a>-like creature from <a href="/wiki/Japan" title="Japan">Japanese</a> mythology.</li></ul>
