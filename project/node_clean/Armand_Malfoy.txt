<b>Armand Malfoy</b> (fl. <a href="/wiki/1066" class="mw-redirect" title="1066">1066</a>) was a <a href="/wiki/France" title="France">French</a> born <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> who arrived in <a href="/wiki/Great_Britain" title="Great Britain">Great Britain</a> with the Norman invasion. He was the first <a href="/wiki/Malfoy_family" title="Malfoy family">Malfoy</a> in Britain, and the founder of <a href="/wiki/Malfoy_Manor" title="Malfoy Manor">Malfoy Manor</a> in <a href="/wiki/Wiltshire" title="Wiltshire">Wiltshire</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-pottermore_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-pottermore-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Armand_Malfoy?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Armand_Malfoy?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_life">Early life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Armand_Malfoy?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Early life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Armand_Malfoy?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Early life"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Armand was presumably born sometime in the late <a href="/wiki/First_millennium_A.D." title="First millennium A.D.">10th</a> or early <a href="/wiki/11th_century" title="11th century">11th century</a> in France. He came to Britain along with the Norman invasion, and presumably gained the friendship of <a href="/wiki/William_I" title="William I">William the Conqueror</a> sometime around this time.
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Life_in_Britain">Life in Britain</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Armand_Malfoy?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Life in Britain">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Armand_Malfoy?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Life in Britain"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Sometime after William I was made King of <a href="/wiki/England" title="England">England</a> in <a href="/wiki/1066" class="mw-redirect" title="1066">1066</a>, Armand provided various services to the king. Though the exact details of these services are unknown, they were believed to be shady and magical in nature. In exchange for these services, King William granted Armand a prime piece of land in Wiltshire, seizing the property from the former landholders.
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Legacy">Legacy</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Armand_Malfoy?veaction=edit&amp;section=4" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Legacy">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Armand_Malfoy?action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Legacy"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>This piece of property would go on to become Malfoy Manor, and would be the home of the Malfoy family for the next ten centuries. Later members of the family would expand the estate even further by annexing more property from the surrounding <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggles</a>.
</p>

<ul><li>Armand may have attended <a href="/wiki/Beauxbatons_Academy_of_Magic" title="Beauxbatons Academy of Magic">Beauxbatons Academy of Magic</a>, if the school already existed in his youth.</li></ul>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Armand_Malfoy?veaction=edit&amp;section=6" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Armand_Malfoy?action=edit&amp;section=6" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li><i>Armand </i>is a French name, derived from the Germanic name <i>Herman</i>, meaning "army man".</li>
<li><i>Malfoy </i>comes from the French <i>mal foi</i>, meaning "bad faith".</li>
<li>Therefore, the name <i>Armand Malfoy </i>could be taken to mean "man of bad faith," which could describe any (or rather, <i>almost&#160;</i>any)&#160;of Malfoy's descendants.</li></ul>
