<p><b>Niko Nenad</b> (d. <a href="/wiki/1809" class="mw-redirect" title="1809">1809</a>)<sup id="cite_ref-PM_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PM-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> was a <a href="/wiki/Romania" title="Romania">Romanian</a> <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> who played as a <a href="/wiki/Beater" title="Beater">Beater</a> for the <a href="/wiki/Romanian_National_Quidditch_team" title="Romanian National Quidditch team">Romanian National Quidditch team</a> in the early <a href="/wiki/19th_century" title="19th century">19th century</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-PM_1-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PM-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> He was described as an extremely volatile and unstable man, with a habit of injuring himself.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Niko_Nenad?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Niko_Nenad?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Nenad was an extremely volatile and unstable man. During the quarter- and semi-finals of the <a href="/wiki/1809_Quidditch_World_Cup" title="1809 Quidditch World Cup">1809 Quidditch World Cup</a>, the other players on the Romanian team witnessed him knock himself over the head with his own <a href="/wiki/Broomstick" title="Broomstick">broom</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Fire-Making_Spell" title="Fire-Making Spell">set</a> his feet on <a href="/wiki/Fire" title="Fire">fire</a> out of frustration. <a href="/wiki/Ivan_Popa" title="Ivan Popa">Ivan Popa</a> had to intervene twice when he tried to strangle two different <a href="/wiki/Quidditch_referee" title="Quidditch referee">referees</a>. His teammates became so concerned with his behaviour that they tried to convince the team's manager to replace him with another player for the final — which was to be played between Romania and <a href="/wiki/Mexico" title="Mexico">New Spain</a> on the <a href="/wiki/West_Siberian_Plain" title="West Siberian Plain">West Siberian Plain</a> — but he chose not to listen.<sup id="cite_ref-PM_1-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PM-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Prior to the final, Nenad <a href="/wiki/Tree_animating_jinx" title="Tree animating jinx">jinxed the whole of the forest</a> adjacent to the <a href="/wiki/Quidditch_pitch" title="Quidditch pitch">pitch</a>, handsomely paying local <a href="/wiki/Siberia" title="Siberia">Siberian</a> <a href="/wiki/Dark_Wizard" title="Dark Wizard">Dark Wizards</a> to help him carry out the job, and possibly also calling upon less-principled fans. Two hours into the game, when New Spain were ahead on points and Romania were exhausted, Nenad deliberately sent a <a href="/wiki/Bludger" title="Bludger">Bludger</a> flying into the forest. The trees instantly sprang to life and proceeded to attack the stadium, resulting in several injuries and deaths. Nenad was killed early in the carnage by a "particularly violent" <a href="/wiki/Spruce" title="Spruce">spruce</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-PM_1-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PM-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Magical_abilities_and_skills">Magical abilities and skills</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Niko_Nenad?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Niko_Nenad?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li><b><a href="/wiki/Flight" title="Flight">Flying</a></b>: Nenad played as <a href="/wiki/Beater" title="Beater">Beater</a> in his lifetime for the <a href="/wiki/Romanian_National_Quidditch_team" title="Romanian National Quidditch team">Romanian National Quidditch team</a>, showing he was an accomplished flyer and <a href="/wiki/Quidditch" title="Quidditch">Quidditch</a> player, despite his extremely violent behaviour displayed on the pitch.<sup id="cite_ref-PM_1-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PM-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Charm" title="Charm">Charms</a></b>: Nenad had some skills in the casting of charms, as he was able to set his feet <a href="/wiki/Fire" title="Fire">alight</a> in a furious temper with the <a href="/wiki/Fire-Making_Spell" title="Fire-Making Spell">Fire-Making Spell</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-PM_1-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PM-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Dark_Arts" title="Dark Arts">Dark Arts</a></b>: Nenad was capable of <a href="/wiki/Tree_animating_jinx" title="Tree animating jinx">animating</a> an entire forest with a <a href="/wiki/Jinx" title="Jinx">jinx</a>, with the help of Siberian <a href="/wiki/Dark_Wizard" title="Dark Wizard">Dark Wizards</a>, showing he was skilled with dark magic and with the casting of <a href="/wiki/Dark_charm" title="Dark charm">Dark charm</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-PM_1-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PM-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup></li></ul>
