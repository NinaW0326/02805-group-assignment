<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Romilda_Goshawk?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Romilda_Goshawk?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_life">Early life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Romilda_Goshawk?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Early life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Romilda_Goshawk?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Early life"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Romilda grew up in a <a href="/wiki/Goshawk_family" title="Goshawk family">wizarding family</a> with eight sisters, including <a href="/wiki/Diadema_Goshawk" title="Diadema Goshawk">Diadema</a>, <a href="/wiki/Tangwystl_Goshawk" title="Tangwystl Goshawk">Tangwystl</a>, and the youngest, <a href="/wiki/Miranda_Goshawk" title="Miranda Goshawk">Miranda</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-WBoS_1-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-WBoS-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Along with the other seven older sisters, Romilda used to try to give Miranda the wrong <a href="/wiki/Incantation" title="Incantation">incantation</a> for every <a href="/wiki/Spell" title="Spell">spell</a> she attempted to cast, thus hindering her learning of <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magic</a>. Miranda found learning spells complex enough as it was, given that the instructions that she found in the old-fashioned textbooks she was given during her school years were needlessly complicated.<sup id="cite_ref-WBoS_1-10" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-WBoS-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Miranda kept Romilda out of her room when she wanted some peace by casting the <a href="/wiki/Bat-Bogey_Hex" title="Bat-Bogey Hex">Bat-Bogey Hex</a> on her.<sup id="cite_ref-WBoS_1-11" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-WBoS-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Later_life">Later life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Romilda_Goshawk?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Later life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Romilda_Goshawk?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Later life"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<figure class="thumb tleft show-info-icon" style="width: 252px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/8/8e/Goshtail.png/revision/latest?cb=20130106023720" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/8/8e/Goshtail.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/250?cb=20130106023720" decoding="async" width="250" height="153" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="Goshtail.png" data-image-key="Goshtail.png" /></a> 	 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:Goshtail.png" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption">Romilda's tail</p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure>
<p>As a result of the difficulty she experienced learning spells as a child, when she grew up, Miranda became quite an accomplished spellbook author, wishing to make teaching and learning about spells an easier task. When she released <i><a href="/wiki/Book_of_Spells" title="Book of Spells">Book of Spells</a></i>, Miranda had a few "special editions" (actually editions containing deliberate misprints) sent to her sisters, and as a result, Romilda was left with a <a href="/wiki/Tail-growing_hex" title="Tail-growing hex">tail</a> that eventually vanished.<sup id="cite_ref-WBoS_1-12" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-WBoS-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
