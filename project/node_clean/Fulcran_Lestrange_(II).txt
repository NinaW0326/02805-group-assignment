</p><p><b>Fulcran Lestrange</b> was a wizard and a member of the <a href="/wiki/Pure-blood" title="Pure-blood">pure-blood</a> <a href="/wiki/Lestrange_family" title="Lestrange family">Lestrange family</a>. He was the son of <a href="/wiki/Fulcran_Lestrange_(I)" title="Fulcran Lestrange (I)">Fulcran Lestrange</a> and <a href="/wiki/Pucine_Lestrange_(I)" title="Pucine Lestrange (I)">Pucine Leroy</a>. He married <a href="/wiki/Sot%C3%A9re_Lestrange" class="mw-redirect" title="Sotére Lestrange">Sotére Petit</a> and had a three children with her, two daughters <a href="/wiki/Balbine_Lestrange_(II)" title="Balbine Lestrange (II)">Balbine</a> and <a href="/wiki/Pucine_Lestrange_(II)" title="Pucine Lestrange (II)">Pucine</a>, and a son <a href="/wiki/Fulcran_Lestrange_(III)" title="Fulcran Lestrange (III)">Fulcran</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
