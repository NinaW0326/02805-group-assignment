<p><b>Mary Elizabeth Cattermole</b> (fl. 1997) was a <a href="/wiki/Great_Britain" title="Great Britain">British</a> <a href="/wiki/Muggle-born" title="Muggle-born">Muggle-born</a> <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch</a>. She lived&#160;at <a href="/wiki/27_Chislehurst_Gardens" title="27 Chislehurst Gardens">27</a> <a href="/wiki/Chislehurst_Gardens" title="Chislehurst Gardens">Chislehurst Gardens</a>, <a href="/wiki/Great_Tolling" title="Great Tolling">Great Tolling</a>, <a href="/wiki/Evesham" title="Evesham">Evesham</a> with her husband and children. She was&#160;described as small with dark hair that she wore in a neat bun.<sup id="cite_ref-DH13_1-12" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH13-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Mary_Cattermole?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Mary_Cattermole?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>She was the daughter of a <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggle</a> <a href="/wiki/Mary_Cattermole%27s_father" title="Mary Cattermole&#39;s father">greengrocer</a> and <a href="/wiki/Mary_Cattermole%27s_mother" title="Mary Cattermole&#39;s mother">his wife</a>. She married <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">Ministry of Magic</a> employee <a href="/wiki/Reginald_Cattermole" title="Reginald Cattermole">Reginald Cattermole</a>, with whom she had two daughters, <a href="/wiki/Maisie_Cattermole" title="Maisie Cattermole">Maisie</a> and <a href="/wiki/Ellie_Cattermole" title="Ellie Cattermole">Ellie</a>, and one son, <a href="/wiki/Alfred_Cattermole" title="Alfred Cattermole">Alfred</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-DH13_1-13" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH13-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<figure class="thumb tleft show-info-icon" style="width: 253px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/b/b1/Trial1.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20150816122531" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/b/b1/Trial1.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/251?cb=20150816122531" decoding="async" width="251" height="105" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="Trial1.jpg" data-image-key="Trial1.jpg" /></a> 	 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:Trial1.jpg" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption">Dolores Umbridge interrogates Mary in the <i><a href="/wiki/Muggle-Born_Registration_Commission" title="Muggle-Born Registration Commission">Muggle-Born Registration Commission</a></i> court room</p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure><p> In <a href="/wiki/1997" title="1997">1997</a>, Mary was accused of stealing the <a href="/wiki/Mary_Cattermole%27s_wand" title="Mary Cattermole&#39;s wand">wand</a> of a <a href="/wiki/Pure-blood" title="Pure-blood">pure-blood</a> or <a href="/wiki/Half-blood" title="Half-blood">half-blood</a> witch or wizard, and was brought before a trial by the Muggle-Born Registration Commission. She was interrogated by <a href="/wiki/Dolores_Umbridge" title="Dolores Umbridge">Dolores Umbridge</a> and <a href="/wiki/Corban_Yaxley" title="Corban Yaxley">Yaxley</a>, and would have been convicted and sent to <a href="/wiki/Azkaban" title="Azkaban">Azkaban</a> if not for the help of Harry and Hermione (disguised as <a href="/wiki/Albert_Runcorn" title="Albert Runcorn">Albert Runcorn</a> and <a href="/wiki/Mafalda_Hopkirk" title="Mafalda Hopkirk">Mafalda Hopkirk</a> respectively). Harry, Hermione and Ron (disguised as her husband Reginald Cattermole) assisted her and a group of Muggle-borns who had been in for questioning escape the Ministry. Harry advised Mary and Reg to take their children and flee the country while <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Voldemort</a> was still in power.<sup id="cite_ref-DH13_1-14" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH13-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>It is unknown if Mary and Reginald managed to escape successfully, or what happened to them and their children after the war.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Mary_Cattermole?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Mary_Cattermole?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>"Mary" is the English form of Maria, which was the Latin form of the New Testament Greek names Μαριαμ (<i>Mariam</i>) and Μαρια (<i>Maria</i>) (the spellings are interchangeable) which in turn come from the Hebrew name מִרְיָם (<i>Miryam</i>). The meaning is not known for certain, but there are several theories including "sea of bitterness", "rebelliousness", and "wished for child". It is also possible that the source of the name lies in ancient Egyptian, and was derived from <i>mry</i> "beloved" or <i>mr</i> "love".
</p>
