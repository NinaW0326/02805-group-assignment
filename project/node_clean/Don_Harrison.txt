<p><b>Don Harrison</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Great_Britain" title="Great Britain">British</a> <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggle</a> construction foreman. 
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Don_Harrison?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Don_Harrison?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>When beginning construction project in a forest that was home to <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magical</a> <a href="/wiki/Beast" title="Beast">beasts</a>, his site and workers were attacked. Six of the workers walked off the job as a result.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Harrison's daughter <a href="/wiki/Bethany_Harrison" title="Bethany Harrison">Bethany</a> was at the site during the attack and made a <a href="/wiki/Bethany_Harrison%27s_drawing" title="Bethany Harrison&#39;s drawing">drawing</a> of the beast responsible.
</p><p>During the subsequent <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">Ministry of Magic</a> investigation (during which <a href="/wiki/Mathilda_Grimblehawk" title="Mathilda Grimblehawk">Mathilda Grimblehawk</a> and <a href="/wiki/Mathilda_Grimblehawk%27s_partner" title="Mathilda Grimblehawk&#39;s partner">Mathilda Grimblehawk's partner</a>, of the <a href="/wiki/Department_for_the_Regulation_and_Control_of_Magical_Creatures" title="Department for the Regulation and Control of Magical Creatures">Department for the Regulation and Control of Magical Creatures</a>, posed as Muggle inspectors from the local constabulary), his daughter <a href="/wiki/Bethany_Harrison" title="Bethany Harrison">Bethany</a> went missing. A distraught Don Harrison pledged to level the whole forest to find her, before being calmed down by Mathilda, who assured him they would find his daughter.<sup id="cite_ref-FBC3.2_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-FBC3.2-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
