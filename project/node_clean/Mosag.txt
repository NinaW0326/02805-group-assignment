<p><b>Mosag</b> was a female <a href="/wiki/Acromantula" title="Acromantula">Acromantula</a>, and the wife of <a href="/wiki/Aragog" title="Aragog">Aragog</a>. Sometime before <a href="/wiki/1993" title="1993">1993</a>, Mosag gave birth to hundreds of baby acromantulas and co-founded the <a href="/wiki/Forbidden_Forest_Acromantula_colony" title="Forbidden Forest Acromantula colony">acromantula colony</a> located in the <a href="/wiki/Forbidden_Forest" title="Forbidden Forest">Forbidden Forest</a> near <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a>. Her husband died later in <a href="/wiki/1997" title="1997">1997</a>. Their colony and descendants took part in the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a> in <a href="/wiki/1998" title="1998">1998</a>.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Mosag?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Mosag?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Family_life">Family life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Mosag?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Family life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Mosag?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Family life"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<figure class="thumb tleft show-info-icon" style="width: 152px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/3/33/Aragog.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20061209012125" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/3/33/Aragog.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/150?cb=20061209012125" decoding="async" width="150" height="98" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="Aragog.jpg" data-image-key="Aragog.jpg" /></a> 	 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:Aragog.jpg" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption">Aragog, Mosag's husband</p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure>
<div class="quote">
<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets">"<i>...and you see how our family has grown, all through Hagrid's goodness...</i>"</span></dd>
<dd>&#8212;Aragog<sup class="noprint"><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Chamber_of_Secrets" title="Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets">[src]</a></sup></dd>
<dd></dd></dl></div>
<p>Sometime before <a href="/wiki/1993" title="1993">1993</a>, Mosag was born and found by <a href="/wiki/Rubeus_Hagrid" title="Rubeus Hagrid">Rubeus Hagrid</a>. Hagrid already owned one acromantula, <a href="/wiki/Aragog" title="Aragog">Aragog</a>, who needed a wife to start a colony. Hagrid brought Mosag to the <a href="/wiki/Forbidden_Forest" title="Forbidden Forest">Forbidden Forest</a> near the <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts</a> school grounds and the two reproduced and began a large colony of Acromantulas. These acromantulas quickly grew to form a large colony that would attack any fresh meat. It is likely that she, like Aragog, liked Hagrid, and ordered her sons and daughters not to attack him.<sup id="cite_ref-COS15_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COS15-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<figure class="thumb tright show-info-icon" style="width: 152px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/e/e8/Spiders.JPG/revision/latest?cb=20090814104207" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/e/e8/Spiders.JPG/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/150?cb=20090814104207" decoding="async" width="150" height="103" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="Spiders.JPG" data-image-key="Spiders.JPG" /></a> 	 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:Spiders.JPG" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption">The colony of Aragog and Mosag</p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure>
<p>In <a href="/wiki/1997" title="1997">1997</a>, Aragog died.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP22_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP22-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> Later, when <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Voldemort</a> attacked Hogwarts castle during the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a> in 1998, Aragog and Mosag's many sons and daughters were forced by the <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a> to fight for their side. However, this fight was won by the <a href="/wiki/Order_of_the_Phoenix" title="Order of the Phoenix">Order of the Phoenix</a>, the side opposing the Death Eaters.<sup id="cite_ref-DH_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Mosag?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Mosag?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>Mosag is a Scottish Gaelic word which means "dirty female" or "filthy" according to <a href="/wiki/J._K._Rowling" title="J. K. Rowling">J. K. Rowling</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> <i>A Dictionary of the Gaelic Language</i> (Norman Macleod and Daniel Dewar, 1839) defines <i>mosag</i> as "a tawdry, drabbish woman; a worthless woman; a mean or avaricious woman."</li></ul>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Mosag?veaction=edit&amp;section=4" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Mosag?action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>Mosag was only mentioned passively in <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Chamber_of_Secrets" title="Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets">Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets</a></i>,<sup id="cite_ref-COS15_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COS15-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> and is not mentioned in the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Chamber_of_Secrets_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets (film)">film version</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li>It is unknown if Mosag fought in the Battle of Hogwarts. Because she never actually appears and is only briefly mentioned once, it is possible that she died sometime before Aragog. This seems likely, as she likely held the same respect for Hagrid as her husband, considering that Hagrid was responsible for her getting a mate to start a colony, and she likely would have stopped her children from attacking Hagrid if she were still around.</li></ul>
