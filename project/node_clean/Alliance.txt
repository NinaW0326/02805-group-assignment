<div class="quote">
<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;Fantastic Beasts: The Crimes of Grindelwald - The Original Screenplay">"<i>We only want freedom. The freedom to be ourselves.</i>"</span></dd>
<dd>&#8212;Grindelwald's seemingly noble mission statement, to mask his true sinister agenda<sup class="noprint"><a href="/wiki/Fantastic_Beasts:_The_Crimes_of_Grindelwald_-_The_Original_Screenplay" title="Fantastic Beasts: The Crimes of Grindelwald - The Original Screenplay">[src]</a></sup></dd>
<dd></dd></dl></div>
<p>The <b>Alliance</b><sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> was an association of seditious <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witches and wizards</a> who followed the <a href="/wiki/Dark_Wizard" title="Dark Wizard">Dark Wizard</a> <a href="/wiki/Gellert_Grindelwald" title="Gellert Grindelwald">Gellert Grindelwald</a> in the early <a href="/wiki/20th_century" title="20th century">twentieth century</a>. Fanatics dedicated to Grindelwald's ideology of <a href="/wiki/Wizarding_supremacy" title="Wizarding supremacy">Wizarding supremacy</a>, they referred to themselves as his "<b><a href="/wiki/Gellert_Grindelwald%27s_acolytes" title="Gellert Grindelwald&#39;s acolytes">acolytes</a></b>". Their main goal was to expose the <a href="/wiki/Wizarding_world" title="Wizarding world">world wizarding community</a> and provoke a war with <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Non-magic people</a>, with the intent overturning the <a href="/wiki/International_Statute_of_Wizarding_Secrecy" title="International Statute of Wizarding Secrecy">International Statute of Wizarding Secrecy</a> and eventually forcing the non-magical population into slavery under their motto: <i>"<a href="/wiki/For_the_Greater_Good" title="For the Greater Good">For the Greater Good</a>"</i>. While many of the acolytes were fervent believers in Grindelwaldâ€™s mantra of magical supremacy at any cost, most were simply idealistic young witches and wizards who were taken in by the dark wizard's charisma and manipulation.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="History">History</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Alliance?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: History">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Alliance?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: History"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Formation">Formation</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Alliance?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Formation">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Alliance?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Formation"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<div class="quote">
<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;Fantastic Beasts: The Crimes of Grindelwald">"<i>When we've won they'll flee cities in their millions. They've had their time.</i>"</span></dd>
<dd>&#8212;Vinda Rosier<sup class="noprint"><a href="/wiki/Fantastic_Beasts:_The_Crimes_of_Grindelwald" title="Fantastic Beasts: The Crimes of Grindelwald">[src]</a></sup></dd>
<dd></dd></dl></div><p>Between about <a href="/wiki/1900" title="1900">1900</a>, when Grindelwald fled Britain, and <a href="/wiki/1945" title="1945">1945</a>, the year of his downfall, <a href="/wiki/Dark_Wizard" title="Dark Wizard">Dark Wizard</a> <a href="/wiki/Gellert_Grindelwald" title="Gellert Grindelwald">Gellert Grindelwald</a> formed an army of witches and wizards. Grindelwald intended to unite the <a href="/wiki/Deathly_Hallows" title="Deathly Hallows">Deathly Hallows</a>, which he believed would make him and his followers invincible. The Deathly Hallows' symbol became the symbol of the Alliance. They used <a href="/wiki/Nurmengard_Castle" title="Nurmengard Castle">Nurmengard Castle</a> as a base. According to Grindelwald, <a href="/wiki/Auror" title="Auror">Aurors</a> ruthlessly fought against his acolytes "simply for believing".<sup id="cite_ref-COGS_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COGS-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p><h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Global_wizarding_war">Global wizarding war</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Alliance?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Global wizarding war">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Alliance?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Global wizarding war"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<div class="quote">
<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;Fantastic Beasts: The Crimes of Grindelwald">"<i>Your policies of violence and suppression are driving supporters into his arms</i>"</span></dd>
<dd>&#8212;Albus Dumbledore to <a href="/wiki/Torquil_Travers" title="Torquil Travers">Torquil Travers</a><sup class="noprint"><a href="/wiki/Fantastic_Beasts:_The_Crimes_of_Grindelwald" title="Fantastic Beasts: The Crimes of Grindelwald">[src]</a></sup></dd>
<dd></dd></dl></div><figure class="thumb tleft show-info-icon" style="width: 202px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/9/92/Alliance_pamphlet.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20190808082341" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/9/92/Alliance_pamphlet.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/200?cb=20190808082341" decoding="async" width="200" height="306" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="Alliance pamphlet.jpg" data-image-key="Alliance_pamphlet.jpg" /></a> 	 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:Alliance_pamphlet.jpg" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption">A pamphlet advertising Grindelwald's rally in Paris</p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure>
<p>In <a href="/wiki/1927" title="1927">1927</a> Grindelwald acquired the support of <a href="/wiki/Queenie_Goldstein" title="Queenie Goldstein">Queenie Goldstein</a> and <a href="/wiki/Gunnar_Grimmson" title="Gunnar Grimmson">Gunnar Grimmson</a>. Later, all of his followers attended a meeting in the <a href="/wiki/Lestrange_Mausoleum" title="Lestrange Mausoleum">Lestrange Mausoleum</a>. There, he gave a rousing speech, in which he shared his idealist perspective of <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">non-magic people</a> and told of the <a href="/wiki/Second_World_War" title="Second World War">Second World War</a>, implying it would come to pass if the army lost to the wizarding governments. Grindelwald, openly recognising the battalion of Aurors that had infiltrated the rally, told his followers to remain calm as he revealed their presence and invited them into the circle. They killed <a href="/wiki/Red-haired_young_witch" title="Red-haired young witch">a young witch</a>, making Grindelwald's followers furious. After making a martyr of the girl, Grindelwald commanded his followers to disapparate from the scene while he faced off against the Aurors.<sup id="cite_ref-COGS_2-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-COGS-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>After a number of years terrorising <a href="/wiki/Europe" title="Europe">Europe</a>, Grindelwald was <a href="/wiki/Duel_between_Albus_Dumbledore_and_Gellert_Grindelwald" title="Duel between Albus Dumbledore and Gellert Grindelwald">defeated</a> by <a href="/wiki/Albus_Dumbledore" title="Albus Dumbledore">Albus Dumbledore</a> in <a href="/wiki/1945" title="1945">1945</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> While the army's status at this time is unknown, it can be assumed that the remaining members were hunted down and arrested by wizarding authorities, killed for resisting, or possibly had dissolved into hiding.<sup class="noprint">&#91;<a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Wikipedia:Citing_sources" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Wikipedia:Citing sources"><i><span title="The material in the vicinity of this tag needs references to reliable sources." style="white-space: nowrap;">citation needed</span></i></a>&#93;</sup>
</p>
