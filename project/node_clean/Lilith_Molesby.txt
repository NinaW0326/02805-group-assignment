<b>Lilith Molesby</b> was a lovelorn <a href="/wiki/Great_Britain" title="Great Britain">British</a>&#160;<a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch</a> who wrote in to the <i><a href="/wiki/Daily_Prophet" title="Daily Prophet">Daily Prophet</a></i> problem page and had her letter published in the <a href="/wiki/1_June" title="1 June">1 June</a> <a href="/wiki/1999" title="1999">1999</a> issue.<sup id="cite_ref-dp3_1-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-dp3-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>In the letter, Lilith sought advice on how to get a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> to fall in <a href="/wiki/Love" title="Love">love</a> with her, reporting that this man had told her friends he would rather be "beaten to death by <a href="/wiki/Troll" title="Troll">trolls</a> than take her out for dinner." She asked whether giving the wizard a <a href="/wiki/Love_Potion" class="mw-redirect" title="Love Potion">Love Potion</a> could help resolve her dilemma. Her letter was answered by <a href="/wiki/Grizel_Hurtz" title="Grizel Hurtz">Grizel Hurtz</a>, who advised her to start using&#160;<a href="/wiki/Hate_Potion" title="Hate Potion">Hate Potions</a>&#160;instead, as this would be preferable to having to constantly "doctor" her beloved's drinks.<sup id="cite_ref-dp3_1-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-dp3-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
