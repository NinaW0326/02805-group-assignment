<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Bhavana_Patel?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Bhavana_Patel?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>She could usually be found walking around the <a href="/wiki/Greenhouse_Two" title="Greenhouse Two">second</a> <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_greenhouses" title="Hogwarts greenhouses">Herbology greenhouse</a> during the <a href="/wiki/1996%E2%80%931997_school_year" title="1996–1997 school year">1996–1997 school year</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-HBPG_3-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBPG-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Bhavana_Patel?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Bhavana_Patel?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Bhavana means <i>becoming, cultivating </i>or <i>producing. </i>Patel is an Indian surname, meaning <i>headman</i> or<i> village chief</i>.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Bhavana_Patel?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Bhavana_Patel?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>Her surname, Patel, was originally going to be given to the <a href="/wiki/Patil_family" title="Patil family">Patil twins</a>, <a href="/wiki/Parvati_Patil" title="Parvati Patil">Parvati</a> and <a href="/wiki/Padma_Patil" title="Padma Patil">Padma</a>.</li>
<li>Based on her name, she is presumably of Indian descent.</li></ul>
