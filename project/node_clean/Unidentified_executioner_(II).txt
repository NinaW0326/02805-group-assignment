<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_executioner_(II)?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_executioner_(II)?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Along with <a href="/wiki/Bernadette" title="Bernadette">Bernadette</a> and <a href="/wiki/Unidentified_executioner_(I)" title="Unidentified executioner (I)">another witch</a>, he assisted in the execution of <a href="/wiki/Newton_Scamander" title="Newton Scamander">Newton Scamander</a> and <a href="/wiki/Porpentina_Goldstein" title="Porpentina Goldstein">Porpentina Goldstein</a> after being sentenced to <a href="/wiki/Death" title="Death">Death</a> by <a href="/wiki/Gellert_Grindelwald" title="Gellert Grindelwald">Gellert Grindelwald</a> (disguised as <a href="/wiki/Percival_Graves" title="Percival Graves">Percival Graves</a>) for violating the <a href="/wiki/International_Statute_of_Wizarding_Secrecy" title="International Statute of Wizarding Secrecy">International Statute of Wizarding Secrecy</a>. The execution was stopped when Newt and Tina were rescued by a <a href="/wiki/Swooping_Evil" title="Swooping Evil">Swooping Evil</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-FBS_1-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-FBS-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>During the fight, Newt knocked him out by whacking him in the face with his (Newt's) handcuffs.<sup id="cite_ref-FBS_1-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-FBS-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
