<b>Cygnus Black</b> (<a href="/wiki/1829" class="mw-redirect" title="1829">1829</a>—<a href="/wiki/1851" class="mw-redirect" title="1851">1851</a>) was a <a href="/wiki/Pure-blood" title="Pure-blood">pure-blood</a> <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a>. He was an early member of the <a href="/wiki/House_of_Black" title="House of Black">House of Black</a>, and possibly the brother of <a href="/wiki/Arcturus_Black_I" title="Arcturus Black I">Arcturus</a> and <a href="/wiki/Misapinoa_Black" title="Misapinoa Black">Misapinoa</a> and father of <a href="/wiki/Phineas_Nigellus_Black" title="Phineas Nigellus Black">Phineas Nigellus</a> and his siblings (<a href="/wiki/Sirius_Black_I" title="Sirius Black I">Sirius</a>, <a href="/wiki/Elladora_Black" title="Elladora Black">Elladora</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Iola_Black" title="Iola Black">Iola</a>), but his precise relationship to other known members of the Black family is unknown. He married <a href="/wiki/Ella_Max" title="Ella Max">Ella Max</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Cygnus_Black_I?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Cygnus_Black_I?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>This character appears only on the version of the Black family tree shown in the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Order_of_the_Phoenix_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix (film)">film adaptation</a> of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Order_of_the_Phoenix" title="Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix">Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix</a></i>. He was not included on the version of the tree which <a href="/wiki/J._K._Rowling" title="J. K. Rowling">J. K. Rowling</a> gave to a charity auction in <a href="/wiki/2006" title="2006">2006</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> However, according to director <a href="/wiki/David_Yates" title="David Yates">David Yates</a>, Rowling provided the filmmakers with a copy of the Black family tree going back eight generations.<sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> The filmmakers presumably received a more complete version of the tree than the one auctioned off, which only goes back six generations.</li>
<li>Cygnus was probably the son of <a href="/wiki/Licorus_Black" title="Licorus Black">Licorus Black</a>.</li>
<li>Given his placement on the tree in the film, this character may have been the older brother of <a href="/wiki/Arcturus_Black_I" title="Arcturus Black I">Arcturus I</a>, and the father of <a href="/wiki/Sirius_Black_I" title="Sirius Black I">Sirius I</a>, <a href="/wiki/Phineas_Nigellus_Black" title="Phineas Nigellus Black">Phineas Nigellus</a>, <a href="/wiki/Elladora_Black" title="Elladora Black">Elladora</a> (who would have been named after her mother Ella), and <a href="/wiki/Iola_Black" title="Iola Black">Iola</a>.</li>
<li>If Iola was Cygnus' daughter, that means that he died before or after she was born, because she was born after 1850 and he died in 1851.</li>
<li>Based on a statement of <a href="/wiki/Horace_Slughorn" title="Horace Slughorn">Horace Slughorn</a>, Cygnus may have been a member of <a href="/wiki/Slytherin" title="Slytherin">Slytherin House</a>. However, he attended Hogwarts c. <a href="/wiki/1840" class="mw-redirect" title="1840">1840</a> - <a href="/wiki/1847" class="mw-redirect" title="1847">1847</a>, when Slughorn was not Potions teacher.</li>
<li>If it is proven that Cygnus is the father of <a href="/wiki/Phineas_Nigellus_Black" title="Phineas Nigellus Black">Phineas Nigellus Black</a>, then it can be said that the name Cygnus is, by tradition, skipped every other generation, or his descendants are named after him out of respect for him.</li>
<li>Cygnus would have been quite young to have become a father (starting at 16 with Sirius I, 18 with Phineas, 21 with Elladora, and, 22 for Iola). J.K. Rowling has stated that Math was not her strong suit.</li></ul>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Cygnus_Black_I?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Cygnus_Black_I?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Cygnus means "swan". A <a href="/wiki/Cygnus_Black_II" title="Cygnus Black II">possible grandson</a> of Cygnus I is named after him. Cygnus is also the name of a constellation in the Northern Hemisphere near Lacerta and Lyra, containing the star Deneb. Also called Northern Cross and Swan.
</p>
