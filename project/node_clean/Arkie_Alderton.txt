<p><b>Arkie Alderton</b> was a well-known <a href="/wiki/Broomstick" title="Broomstick">broomstick</a> designer<sup id="cite_ref-DH13_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH13-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> and the owner of <a href="/wiki/Arkie_Alderton%27s_Kwik-Repair_Shop" title="Arkie Alderton&#39;s Kwik-Repair Shop">Arkie Alderton's Kwik-Repair Shop</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-AQ.21_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-AQ.21-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>One of the <a href="/wiki/Muggle-born" title="Muggle-born">Muggle-borns</a> rounded up for the <a href="/wiki/Muggle-Born_Registration_Commission" title="Muggle-Born Registration Commission">Muggle-Born Registration Commission</a> in <a href="/wiki/1997" title="1997">1997</a>&#160;swore that he was a <a href="/wiki/Half-blood" title="Half-blood">half-blood</a> by <a href="/wiki/Arkie_Alderton%27s_alleged_son" title="Arkie Alderton&#39;s alleged son">claiming he was his son</a>, but he was not believed.<sup id="cite_ref-DH13_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DH13-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
