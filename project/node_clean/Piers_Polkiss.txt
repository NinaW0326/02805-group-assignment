<p><b>Piers Polkiss</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggle</a> and <a href="/wiki/Dudley_Dursley" title="Dudley Dursley">Dudley Dursley</a>'s best friend, when they were young.<sup id="cite_ref-PS2_1-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PS2-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> Despite being scrawny with a face like a <a href="/wiki/Rat" title="Rat">rat</a>, he was a member of <a href="/wiki/Dudley_Dursley" title="Dudley Dursley">Dudley Dursley</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Dudley_Dursley%27s_gang" title="Dudley Dursley&#39;s gang">gang</a> and was usually the one who held people's arms behind their backs so Dudley could hit them. Piers did not like <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>, because Dudley did not, and he always followed Dudley's lead.<sup id="cite_ref-PS2_1-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PS2-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> Piers would also join Dudley at <a href="/wiki/Smeltings_Academy" title="Smeltings Academy">Smeltings Academy</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-PS3_2-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PS3-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<p>On <a href="/wiki/23_June" title="23 June">23 June</a> <a href="/wiki/1991" title="1991">1991</a>, Dudley's eleventh birthday, Piers accompanied him, the <a href="/wiki/Dursley_family" title="Dursley family">Dursleys</a> and Harry to the <a href="/wiki/Zoo" title="Zoo">zoo</a>. During the trip, Piers saw Harry <a href="/wiki/Parseltongue" title="Parseltongue">talking</a> to a <a href="/wiki/Boa_constrictor_at_the_Zoo" title="Boa constrictor at the Zoo">snake</a> in the reptile house. When Harry was pushed out of the way, and then <a href="/wiki/Underage_magic" title="Underage magic">unintentionally</a> made the <a href="/wiki/Vanishing_Spell" title="Vanishing Spell">glass disappear</a>, Piers and Dudley jumped back in surprise. When Piers told the Dursleys that Harry had been talking to the snake, they gave Harry his longest ever punishment.<sup id="cite_ref-PS2_1-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PS2-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Piers_Polkiss?veaction=edit&amp;section=4" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Piers_Polkiss?action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Piers is of Anglo-Saxon heritage and means "rock." It is a variant of Peter, from the Greek for "rock." Polkiss may be a variation of Polk, which itself is a variation of the Scottish names Pogue, Pollack, or Pollock, meaning "from the pool or pit."
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Piers_Polkiss?veaction=edit&amp;section=5" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Piers_Polkiss?action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>Due to being Dudley's closest friend of the group, Piers was most likely the gang's second-in-command.</li>
<li>Piers Polkiss was portrayed by <a href="/wiki/Jason_Boyd" title="Jason Boyd">Jason Boyd</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Order_of_the_Phoenix_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix (film)">film adaptation</a> of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Order_of_the_Phoenix" title="Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix">Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix</a></i>.</li>
<li>Piers Polkiss was not mentioned nor did he appear in the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Philosopher%27s_Stone_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Philosopher&#39;s Stone (film)">film adaptation</a> of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Philosopher%27s_Stone" title="Harry Potter and the Philosopher&#39;s Stone">Harry Potter and the Philosopher's Stone</a></i>.</li>
<li>Piers Polkiss has not appeared in <i><a href="/wiki/LEGO_Harry_Potter:_Years_1-4" title="LEGO Harry Potter: Years 1-4">LEGO Harry Potter: Years 1-4</a></i> and <i><a href="/wiki/LEGO_Harry_Potter:_Years_5-7" title="LEGO Harry Potter: Years 5-7">LEGO Harry Potter: Years 5-7</a></i>.</li>
<li>It is mentioned several times in the books that all the members of Dudley's gang were big and stupid, but Piers was always described as being scrawny with a face like a rat. It is possible that Piers was scrawny when compared to the other members or that he gained a significant amount of weight after he went to the zoo with Dudley and Harry.</li></ul>
