<b>Tamsin Applebee</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch</a> who attended <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a>, and was a member of <a href="/wiki/Hufflepuff" title="Hufflepuff">Hufflepuff</a> house. She played as a  <a href="/wiki/Chaser" title="Chaser">Chaser</a> on the <a href="/wiki/Hufflepuff_Quidditch_team" title="Hufflepuff Quidditch team">Hufflepuff Quidditch team</a> when it was captained by <a href="/wiki/Cedric_Diggory" title="Cedric Diggory">Cedric Diggory</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
