<b>Grizel Hurtz</b> was an advice columnist who wrote for the <i><a href="/wiki/Daily_Prophet" title="Daily Prophet">Daily Prophet</a></i>. She dealt with queries of an emotional nature, while her co-columnists <a href="/wiki/Zamira_Gulch" title="Zamira Gulch">Zamira Gulch</a>, <a href="/wiki/Helbert_Spleen" title="Helbert Spleen">Helbert Spleen</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Dempster_Wiggleswade" title="Dempster Wiggleswade">Dempster Wiggleswade</a> addressed more straightforward issues. On one occasion, Hurtz responded to a letter from a <a href="/wiki/Lilith_Molesby" title="Lilith Molesby">lovelorn witch</a>, who was thinking about using a <a href="/wiki/Love_Potion" class="mw-redirect" title="Love Potion">Love Potion</a> to attract the man whom she loved. Grizel suggested she use a <a href="/wiki/Hate_Potion" title="Hate Potion">Hate potion</a> instead, to rid herself of her obsession.<sup id="cite_ref-DP_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DP-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Grizel_Hurtz?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Grizel_Hurtz?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>Hurtz is a homophone of the English word <i>hurts</i>. This may be a play on the term "agony aunt", which is a <a href="/wiki/Great_Britain" title="Great Britain">British</a> slang term for a advice columnist.</li></ul>
