<b>Wilda Griffiths</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">witch</a> and <a href="/wiki/Chaser" title="Chaser">Chaser</a> on the <a href="/wiki/Puddlemere_United" title="Puddlemere United">Puddlemere United</a> <a href="/wiki/Quidditch" title="Quidditch">Quidditch</a> <a href="/wiki/Quidditch_team" title="Quidditch team">team</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> Before that, she was a <a href="/wiki/Chaser" title="Chaser">Chaser</a> for the <a href="/wiki/Holyhead_Harpies" title="Holyhead Harpies">Holyhead Harpies</a>; her departure from the team was a sore spot for the Harpies, especially for <a href="/wiki/Quidditch_Captain#Hogwarts_Quidditch_Captains" title="Quidditch Captain">Captain</a> <a href="/wiki/Gwenog_Jones" title="Gwenog Jones">Gwenog Jones</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> When Puddlemere and the Harpies faced off for the first time after Griffiths' move, the game turned into a riot, during which Griffiths disappeared, and it is unknown what happened to her.<sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
