<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Pyotr_Vulchanov?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Pyotr_Vulchanov?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>He played as <a href="/wiki/Beater" title="Beater">Beater</a> for his team during the <a href="/wiki/1994_Quidditch_World_Cup" title="1994 Quidditch World Cup">1994 Quidditch World Cup</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-GOF8_1-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF8-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>By <a href="/wiki/1996" title="1996">1996</a>, he was included on a <a href="/wiki/Chocolate_Frog_Card" title="Chocolate Frog Card">Chocolate Frog Card</a> for his achievements in <a href="/wiki/Quidditch" title="Quidditch">Quidditch</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-HBPG_4-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBPG-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>His son <a href="/wiki/Boris_Vulchanov" title="Boris Vulchanov">Boris</a> participated in the <a href="/wiki/2014_Quidditch_World_Cup" title="2014 Quidditch World Cup">2014 Quidditch World Cup</a> as a <a href="/wiki/Beater" title="Beater">Beater</a> for <a href="/wiki/Bulgarian_National_Quidditch_team" title="Bulgarian National Quidditch team">Bulgaria</a> in the same way his father had, and the team made it to the finals, much the way Pyotr's had.<sup id="cite_ref-PM_3-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PM-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Pyotr_Vulchanov?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Pyotr_Vulchanov?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Etymology"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>The name "Pyotr" is a Russian form of the name Peter. The form used in Bulgaria is Petar.</li>
<li>Vulchanov possibly comes from the Bulgarian word for "wolf"/вълк/. Rangel Vulchanov (1928 - ) is a famous Bulgarian film director.</li></ul>
