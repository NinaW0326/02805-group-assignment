<p>This <b>individual</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Great_Britain" title="Great Britain">British</a>&#160;<a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> who sold <a href="/wiki/Amulet" title="Amulet">Amulets</a> in <a href="/wiki/1996" title="1996">1996</a> in <a href="/wiki/Diagon_Alley" title="Diagon Alley">Diagon Alley</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP6_1-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP6-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_Amulet-seller_in_Diagon_Alley?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_Amulet-seller_in_Diagon_Alley?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>In <a href="/wiki/1996" title="1996">1996</a>, he tried to sell one to <a href="/wiki/Molly_Weasley" title="Molly Weasley">Molly Weasley</a> to give to <a href="/wiki/Ginevra_Weasley" title="Ginevra Weasley">Ginny Weasley</a>, however <a href="/wiki/Arthur_Weasley" title="Arthur Weasley">Arthur Weasley</a> threatened him with arrest.<sup id="cite_ref-HBP6_1-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBP6-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> He was one of many preying, in various ways, on the renewed fear which the <a href="/wiki/Wizarding_world" title="Wizarding world">wizarding&#160;populace</a> felt due to the recently acknowledged <a href="/wiki/Return_of_Lord_Voldemort" title="Return of Lord Voldemort">return</a> of <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Voldemort</a>&#160;and his <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">followers</a>. It is most likely therefore that the supposedly protective amulets he was selling were counterfeit goods which possessed no actual <a href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magical</a> properties.&#160;
</p>
