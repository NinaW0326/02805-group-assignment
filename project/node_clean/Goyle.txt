<p><b>Mr Goyle</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Dark_Wizard" title="Dark Wizard">Dark Wizard</a>, <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eater</a>, husband of <a href="/wiki/Gregory_Goyle%27s_mother" title="Gregory Goyle&#39;s mother">Mrs Goyle</a> and the father of <a href="/wiki/Gregory_Goyle" title="Gregory Goyle">Gregory</a>. Goyle fought during the <a href="/wiki/First_Wizarding_War" title="First Wizarding War">First Wizarding War</a>. He didn't search for <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a> after his first downfall, but this was forgiven in <a href="/wiki/1995" title="1995">1995</a>, after Voldemort's <a href="/wiki/Return_of_Lord_Voldemort" title="Return of Lord Voldemort">return</a> at the <a href="/wiki/Little_Hangleton_graveyard" title="Little Hangleton graveyard">Little Hangleton graveyard</a>, in which Goyle was present.<sup id="cite_ref-GOF33_2-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF33-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> Goyle possibly fought at several battles of the Second Wizarding War, including the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a>. After the battle and Voldemort's final defeat, Goyle was presumably killed or imprisoned in <a href="/wiki/Azkaban" title="Azkaban">Azkaban</a>.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Goyle?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Goyle?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="First_Wizarding_War">First Wizarding War</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Goyle?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: First Wizarding War">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Goyle?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: First Wizarding War"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Goyle was a <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eater</a> during the <a href="/wiki/First_Wizarding_War" title="First Wizarding War">First Wizarding War</a> and was loyal to his master up until his downfall, when Goyle managed to avoid <a href="/wiki/Azkaban" title="Azkaban">Azkaban</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-GOF33_2-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF33-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> Around the downfall of Voldemort, Goyle and <a href="/wiki/Gregory_Goyle%27s_mother" title="Gregory Goyle&#39;s mother">his wife</a> had a baby boy, <a href="/wiki/Gregory_Goyle" title="Gregory Goyle">Gregory Goyle</a>, who would be educated at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts</a> from <a href="/wiki/1991" title="1991">1991</a> to <a href="/wiki/1998" title="1998">1998</a>, and be friends with <a href="/wiki/Vincent_Crabbe" title="Vincent Crabbe">Vincent Crabbe</a> and <a href="/wiki/Draco_Malfoy" title="Draco Malfoy">Draco Malfoy</a>.
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Second_Wizarding_War">Second Wizarding War</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Goyle?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Second Wizarding War">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Goyle?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Second Wizarding War"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<div class="quote">
<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix (video game)">"<i>My father reckons <a href="/wiki/Amelia_Bones" title="Amelia Bones">your aunt</a>'s a troublemaker, someone will teach her a lesson!</i>"</span></dd>
<dd>&#8212;<a href="/wiki/Gregory_Goyle" title="Gregory Goyle">Gregory Goyle</a> mentioning his father to <a href="/wiki/Susan_Bones" title="Susan Bones">Susan Bones</a><sup class="noprint"><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Order_of_the_Phoenix_(video_game)" title="Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix (video game)">[src]</a></sup></dd>
<dd></dd></dl></div><p>Goyle was one of the few Death Eaters who returned to <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a> when he rose to power again in <a href="/wiki/1995" title="1995">1995</a>. Voldemort was displeased when he learned that Goyle did not attempt to find him, but Goyle was forgiven.<sup id="cite_ref-GOF33_2-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF33-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>During the <a href="/wiki/1995-1996_school_year" class="mw-redirect" title="1995-1996 school year">1995-1996 school year</a>, Goyle's son, <a href="/wiki/Gregory_Goyle" title="Gregory Goyle">Gregory</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Vincent_Crabbe%27s_father" class="mw-redirect" title="Vincent Crabbe&#39;s father">Crabbe</a>'s son, <a href="/wiki/Vincent_Crabbe" title="Vincent Crabbe">Vincent</a>, both cornered <a href="/wiki/Susan_Bones" title="Susan Bones">Susan Bones</a>, and Gregory told her that his father thought that her aunt <a href="/wiki/Amelia_Bones" title="Amelia Bones">Amelia Bones</a> was a trouble-maker.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTPG_4-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTPG-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>His actions following his master's return are unknown. It is likely that he participated in many battles during the <a href="/wiki/Second_Wizarding_War" title="Second Wizarding War">Second Wizarding War</a>, including the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hogwarts" title="Battle of Hogwarts">Battle of Hogwarts</a>. He was presumably killed or imprisoned in Azkaban following Voldemort's defeat.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Physical_description">Physical description</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Goyle?veaction=edit&amp;section=4" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Physical description">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Goyle?action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Physical description"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Much like his son he was described as a large man, about as large as <a href="/wiki/Crabbe" title="Crabbe">Crabbe</a> and significantly more so than <a href="/wiki/Nott" title="Nott">Nott</a>. He was also known to have a dull sounding voice.<sup id="cite_ref-GOF33_2-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF33-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Personality_and_traits">Personality and traits</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Goyle?veaction=edit&amp;section=5" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Personality and traits">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Goyle?action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Personality and traits"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Not much is known about Goyle's individual personality, though he appeared to be somewhat unintelligent. He was however outspoken against certain <a href="/wiki/British_Ministry_of_Magic" title="British Ministry of Magic">Ministry of Magic</a> employees such as <a href="/wiki/Amelia_Bones" title="Amelia Bones">Amelia Bones</a>, possibly for being against the <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a> or for giving <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> a free <a href="/wiki/Disciplinary_hearing_of_Harry_Potter" title="Disciplinary hearing of Harry Potter">trial</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-OOTPG_4-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OOTPG-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> He apparently was not loyal enough to search for his <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">master</a> after his original downfall or otherwise believed him to be finished.<sup id="cite_ref-GOF33_2-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF33-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> Being a Death Eater, it can presumed he shared his master's ideals of <a href="/wiki/Pure-blood_supremacy" title="Pure-blood supremacy">pure-blood supremacy</a>, as he was one of the Death Eaters who returned to Voldemort's side once he had returned, showing his loyalty and commitment to his dark master and his cause.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Magical_abilities_and_skills">Magical abilities and skills</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Goyle?veaction=edit&amp;section=6" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Goyle?action=edit&amp;section=6" title="Edit section: Magical abilities and skills"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li><b><a href="/wiki/Dark_Arts" title="Dark Arts">Dark Arts</a></b>: Being a Death Eater, Goyle would have been accomplished with dark magic, likely including the <a href="/wiki/Unforgivable_Curses" title="Unforgivable Curses">Unforgivable Curses</a>.</li>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Apparition" title="Apparition">Apparition</a></b>: Like most adult wizards, Goyle was capable of apparating at will. He successfully apparated to the <a href="/wiki/Little_Hangleton_graveyard" title="Little Hangleton graveyard">Little Hangleton graveyard</a> in <a href="/wiki/1995" title="1995">1995</a> to witness the <a href="/wiki/Return_of_Lord_Voldemort" title="Return of Lord Voldemort">return</a> of his master.<sup id="cite_ref-GOF33_2-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GOF33-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup></li></ul>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Goyle?veaction=edit&amp;section=7" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Goyle?action=edit&amp;section=7" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>It is not specified if Goyle attended <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a>, though if he did he was likely in <a href="/wiki/Slytherin" title="Slytherin">Slytherin</a> like his son.</li>
<li>Goyle's only specified appearance is in <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Goblet_of_Fire" title="Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire">Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire</a></i>. It is unverified if he participated in any of the later events of the <a href="/wiki/Second_Wizarding_War" title="Second Wizarding War">Second Wizarding War</a>.</li>
<li>In the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Goblet_of_Fire_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire (film)">film adaptation</a> of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Goblet_of_Fire" title="Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire">Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire</a></i>, Goyle was played by <a href="/wiki/Alex_Palmer" title="Alex Palmer">Alex Palmer</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> but was only credited as "Death Eater".<sup id="cite_ref-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup></li></ul>
