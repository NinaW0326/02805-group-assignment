This <b>individual</b> was a witch or <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> who became the <a href="/wiki/Professor" title="Professor">professor</a> of <a href="/wiki/Defence_Against_the_Dark_Arts" title="Defence Against the Dark Arts">Defence Against the Dark Arts</a> at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a>, for the <a href="/wiki/1986%E2%80%931987_school_year" title="1986–1987 school year">1986–1987 school year</a>. 
</p><p>They were considered far from an improvement of the <a href="/wiki/Unidentified_1985-1986_Defence_Against_the_Dark_Arts_teacher" title="Unidentified 1985-1986 Defence Against the Dark Arts teacher">previous year's professor</a>, which made <a href="/wiki/Tulip_Karasu" title="Tulip Karasu">Tulip Karasu</a> wonder whether they would ever learn the <a href="/wiki/Boggart-Banishing_Spell" title="Boggart-Banishing Spell">Boggart-Banishing Spell</a> if they continued to teach the subject.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>The professor would probably never have continued to teach the subject for the next school year, because of <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Jinx_on_the_post_of_Defence_Against_the_Dark_Arts_teacher" title="Jinx on the post of Defence Against the Dark Arts teacher">jinx</a> which ensured the teacher of the subject would have needed to be replaced by the end of the year.
</p><p>According to <a href="/wiki/Jacob%27s_sibling" title="Jacob&#39;s sibling">Jacob's sibling</a>, this teacher was the worst out of their first five teachers.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
