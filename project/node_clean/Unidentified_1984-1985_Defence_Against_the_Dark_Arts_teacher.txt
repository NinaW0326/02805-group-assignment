This <b>individual</b> became the <a href="/wiki/Professor" title="Professor">professor</a> of <a href="/wiki/Defence_Against_the_Dark_Arts" title="Defence Against the Dark Arts">Defence Against the Dark Arts</a> at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> for the <a href="/wiki/1984%E2%80%931985_school_year" title="1984–1985 school year">1984–1985 school year</a>. However, because of <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Jinx_on_the_post_of_Defence_Against_the_Dark_Arts_teacher" title="Jinx on the post of Defence Against the Dark Arts teacher">jinx</a> on the post, this individual was "lost" by the end of the year and needed to be replaced.</p><figure class="thumb tright show-info-icon" style="width: 252px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/f/fb/HM_year_1_ch._8_Penny_mentioning_DADA_professor.png/revision/latest?cb=20180429154216" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/f/fb/HM_year_1_ch._8_Penny_mentioning_DADA_professor.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/250?cb=20180429154216" decoding="async" width="250" height="141" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="HM year 1 ch. 8 Penny mentioning DADA professor.png" data-image-key="HM_year_1_ch._8_Penny_mentioning_DADA_professor.png" /></a> 	 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:HM_year_1_ch._8_Penny_mentioning_DADA_professor.png" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption"><a href="/wiki/Penny_Haywood" title="Penny Haywood">Penny Haywood</a> mentioning the loss of this professor.</p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure><p> According to <a href="/wiki/Penny_Haywood" title="Penny Haywood">Penny Haywood</a>, <a href="/wiki/Severus_Snape" title="Severus Snape">Severus Snape</a> tried unsuccessfully to get the job.<sup id="cite_ref-professor_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-professor-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
