<p><b>Penelope Taylor</b> (born c. <a href="/wiki/1974" title="1974">1974</a>) was a <a href="/wiki/Non-magic_people" title="Non-magic people">Muggle</a> woman from <a href="/wiki/Hull" title="Hull">Hull</a>, and a survivor of the <a href="/wiki/Millennium_Bridge" title="Millennium Bridge">Millennium Bridge</a> disaster in <a href="/wiki/1996" title="1996">1996</a>, aged 22.
</p><p>She noticed "<i>these plumes of smoke in the sky</i>", just before the bridge collapsed, which were (although she did not know) a gang of <a href="/wiki/Death_Eaters" title="Death Eaters">Death Eaters</a> led by <a href="/wiki/Fenrir_Greyback" title="Fenrir Greyback">Fenrir Greyback</a>. Penelope Taylor attributed the collapse to what she believed were devils, punishing the Londoners for their "<i>decadent Western lifestyle</i>". She escaped the collapse with only a broken leg and pelvis, because she stopped to try and see the smoke, and later gave a statement to <i><a href="/wiki/The_Guardian" title="The Guardian">The Guardian</a></i>.<sup id="cite_ref-HPLE_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HPLE-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
