<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Angus_Matlock?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Angus_Matlock?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Angus Matlock was <a href="/wiki/Sorting_ceremony" title="Sorting ceremony">sorted</a> into <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor" title="Gryffindor">Gryffindor</a> when he arrived at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a>. He was in at least his <a href="/wiki/Third_year" title="Third year">third year</a> of education by the <a href="/wiki/1996-1997_school_year" class="mw-redirect" title="1996-1997 school year">1996-1997 school year</a>, referring on occasion to <a href="/wiki/First_year" title="First year">first</a> and <a href="/wiki/Second_year" title="Second year">second years</a> as younger students. Angus became captain for <a href="/wiki/Gryffindor" title="Gryffindor">Gryffindor</a> when the <a href="/wiki/Duelling_Club" title="Duelling Club">Duelling Club</a> was revived by <a href="/wiki/Albus_Dumbledore" title="Albus Dumbledore">Albus Dumbledore</a>. Angus convinced <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> to join the duelling club, and requested that he and <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ron Weasley</a> show a group of watching <a href="/wiki/First_year" title="First year">first years</a> how to cast the <a href="/wiki/Stunning_Spell" title="Stunning Spell">Stunning Spell</a> and <a href="/wiki/Disarming_Charm" title="Disarming Charm">Disarming Charm</a>, along with how to dodge <a href="/wiki/Spell" title="Spell">spells</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-HBPG_3-11" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBPG-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Angus had a rather controlling and loud personality, shouting at first years for not paying attention and demanding Ron and Harry duel; however he also was rather impressed by Harry Potter for being able to fight <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a>. Ron Weasley did not seem too happy to find out that Angus was the leader of the duelling club for Gryffindor when he and Harry first arrived.<sup id="cite_ref-HBPG_3-12" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HBPG-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
