<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="1993">1993</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Manager_of_Flourish_and_Blotts?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: 1993">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Manager_of_Flourish_and_Blotts?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: 1993"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<div class="quote">
<dl><dd><span title="Source:&#160;Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban">"<i>Stop it! Stop it!</i>"</span></dd>
<dd>&#8212;The manager trying to stop copies of the <a href="/wiki/The_Monster_Book_of_Monsters" title="The Monster Book of Monsters">The Monster Book of Monsters</a> from attacking each other<sup class="noprint"><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Prisoner_of_Azkaban" title="Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban">[src]</a></sup></dd>
<dd></dd></dl></div><p>In <a href="/wiki/1993" title="1993">1993</a>, he told <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry</a> that he had once lost a lot of money on <i><a href="/wiki/The_Invisible_Book_of_Invisibility" title="The Invisible Book of Invisibility">The Invisible Book of Invisibility</a>,</i> because they never found the books. When the <i><a href="/wiki/The_Monster_Book_of_Monsters" title="The Monster Book of Monsters">The Monster Book of Monsters</a></i> became one books required by students at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts</a>, he had to cope with the new additions to his book list attacking both each other and him; the manager spent much of that summer vowing never to stock that book again. Whenever he had to grab a copy for a customer, he would become rather unhappy due to the books' viciousness; while he was relieved that Harry already got a copy beforehand, he was on the verge of tears when <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ronald Weasley</a> and <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione Granger</a> requested two copies total. He also recommend Harry to not buy <i><a href="/wiki/Death_Omens:_What_to_Do_When_You_Know_the_Worst_is_Coming" title="Death Omens: What to Do When You Know the Worst is Coming">Death Omens: What to Do When You Know the Worst is Coming</a></i>, as it was enough to frighten anyone to <a href="/wiki/Death" title="Death">death</a>, and in general doesn't recommend reading books about death omens because one would start seeing them everywhere.<sup id="cite_ref-PA4_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PA4-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p><h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Manager_of_Flourish_and_Blotts?veaction=edit&amp;section=4" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Manager_of_Flourish_and_Blotts?action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>As the owner of <a href="/wiki/Flourish_and_Blotts" title="Flourish and Blotts">Flourish and Blotts</a>, it is possible that his name is either Flourish or Blotts.
