<b>Professor Harfang Munter</b> was the second <a href="/wiki/Headmaster" title="Headmaster">Headmaster</a> of <a href="/wiki/Durmstrang_Institute" title="Durmstrang Institute">Durmstrang Institute</a>. He took over the school shortly after the mysterious death of his predecessor, founder <a href="/wiki/Nerida_Vulchanova" title="Nerida Vulchanova">Nerida Vulchanova</a>, a great <a href="/wiki/Bulgaria" title="Bulgaria">Bulgarian</a> witch. He was a <a href="/wiki/Wizardkind" title="Wizardkind">wizard</a> of "dubious allegiance or nefarious intent", having stressed the importance of <a href="/wiki/Duelling" title="Duelling">duelling</a> and all forms of <a href="/wiki/Martial_magic" title="Martial magic">martial magic</a> — something that the school is reputed for, even today, having kept it as an important part of its curriculum.<sup id="cite_ref-PM_2-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PM-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Harfang_Munter?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Harfang_Munter?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>Harfang is a city of "civilized" giants in the Narnia universe of C. S. Lewis. <i>Harfång</i> is also a Swedish word, borrowed in other languages, such as French, used to refer to male snowy owls (literally: "hare-catcher"), the same species used for <a href="/wiki/Hedwig" title="Hedwig">Hedwig</a> in the movies.</li>
<li>Munter is the Danish, Swedish, German and Norwegian word for "cheerful", and while it is unlikely to be the cause of the name, it still provides irony in the fact that the Headmaster is described to be of villainous nature.</li>
<li>He shares a first name with Harfang Longbottom who married Callidora Black.</li></ul>
