<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="History">History</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Ricbert?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: History">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Ricbert?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: History"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Following <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a>, <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ronald Weasley</a> and <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione Granger</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Break-in_of_Gringotts_Wizarding_Bank_(1998)" title="Break-in of Gringotts Wizarding Bank (1998)">break-in</a> of the Bank in <a href="/wiki/1998" title="1998">1998</a>, Ricbert was in the main lobby as the <a href="/wiki/Gringott%27s_Dragon" title="Gringott&#39;s Dragon">security dragon</a> violently erupted through it.<sup id="cite_ref-DHpt2_2-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DHpt2-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> Despite being only a few feet from where the <a href="/wiki/Dragon" title="Dragon">dragon</a> erupted into the lobby, Ricbert managed to escape the bank, but was soon summoned, with other goblins and guards, to <a href="/wiki/Malfoy_Manor" title="Malfoy Manor">Malfoy Manor</a>, where he was <a href="/wiki/Killing_Curse" title="Killing Curse">killed</a> by <a href="/wiki/Tom_Riddle" title="Tom Riddle">Lord Voldemort</a> in an <a href="/wiki/Massacre_at_Malfoy_Manor" title="Massacre at Malfoy Manor">angry rage</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-script_1-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-script-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Ricbert?veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Ricbert?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>This goblin is not named in <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Deathly_Hallows:_Part_2" title="Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 2">Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 2</a></i>, however, the original screenplay had <a href="/wiki/Bogrod" title="Bogrod">Bogrod</a> referring to him as "Ricbert".<sup id="cite_ref-script_1-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-script-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Rusty_Goffe" title="Rusty Goffe">Rusty Goffe</a> portrays Ricbert in <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Deathly_Hallows:_Part_2" title="Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 2">Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 2</a></i>.<sup id="cite_ref-rustygoffe_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-rustygoffe-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li>In the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Deathly_Hallows:_Part_2" title="Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 2">second part of the film adaptation</a> of <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Deathly_Hallows" title="Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows">Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows</a></i>, this goblin is the one who <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione Granger</a> (disguised as <a href="/wiki/Bellatrix_Lestrange" title="Bellatrix Lestrange">Bellatrix Lestrange</a>) and <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Weasley" title="Ronald Weasley">Ronald Weasley</a> (disguised as "Dragomir Despard") address. He later calls for <a href="/wiki/Bogrod" title="Bogrod">Bogrod</a>. In the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Deathly_Hallows" title="Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows">novel</a>, <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Deathly_Hallows:_Part_2_(video_game)" title="Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 2 (video game)">video game</a> and <a href="/wiki/LEGO_Harry_Potter:_Years_5-7" title="LEGO Harry Potter: Years 5-7">LEGO game</a>, however, they approach Bogrod from the beginning.</li></ul>
