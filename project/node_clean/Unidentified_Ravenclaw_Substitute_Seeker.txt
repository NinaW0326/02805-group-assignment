This <b>individual</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Ravenclaw" title="Ravenclaw">Ravenclaw</a> student at <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_School_of_Witchcraft_and_Wizardry" title="Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a> and a substitute <a href="/wiki/Seeker" title="Seeker">Seeker</a> on their <a href="/wiki/Quidditch" title="Quidditch">Quidditch</a> <a href="/wiki/Ravenclaw_Quidditch_team" title="Ravenclaw Quidditch team">team</a> in <a href="/wiki/1991" title="1991">1991</a> to <a href="/wiki/1992" title="1992">1992</a>. He was filling in when <a href="/wiki/Unidentified_Ravenclaw_Seeker" title="Unidentified Ravenclaw Seeker">the actual Seeker</a> was unable to play.<sup id="cite_ref-PSG_2-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PSG-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
