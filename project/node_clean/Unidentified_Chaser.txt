<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_Chaser?veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_Chaser?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>During his time at Hogwarts, he was <a href="/wiki/Sorting_ceremony" title="Sorting ceremony">Sorted</a> into an unknown house. At some point during his time at school, he became a <a href="/wiki/Chaser" title="Chaser">Chaser</a> of an unknown Quidditch team.<sup id="cite_ref-q1_4-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-q1-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>In the <a href="/wiki/1985%E2%80%931986_school_year" title="1985–1986 school year">1985–1986 school year</a> during the first practise match of the term, <a href="/wiki/Skye_Parkin" title="Skye Parkin">Skye Parkin</a> saved Jacob's sibling from getting hit by a <a href="/wiki/Bludger" title="Bludger">Bludger</a> that was heading towards the stands. However, while she saved them, her play cost her team by knocking out this individual for the season.<sup id="cite_ref-q1_4-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-q1-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>He always ate <a href="/wiki/Kipper" title="Kipper">kippers</a> for breakfast before each Quidditch match. The one time he skipped was before the <a href="/wiki/Quidditch_friendly" title="Quidditch friendly">Quidditch friendly</a> against <a href="/wiki/Erika_Rath" title="Erika Rath">Erika Rath</a>'s team during which he was injured, and as such, he made sure to never skip eating kippers for breakfast again.<sup id="cite_ref-qc_3-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-qc-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Before the final match, Skye Parkin was injured and unable to play. As a result, this individual played on the team during the final match.<sup id="cite_ref-qc_3-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-qc-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>He continued playing on the team the <a href="/wiki/1986%E2%80%931987_school_year" title="1986–1987 school year">following year</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> He ate kippers for an entire week before the team's first House match that year.<sup id="cite_ref-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup> After this, <a href="/wiki/Poppy_Pomfrey" title="Poppy Pomfrey">Poppy Pomfrey</a> told him not to eat so many kippers.<sup id="cite_ref-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Behind_the_scenes">Behind the scenes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_Chaser?veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/wiki/Unidentified_Chaser?action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Behind the scenes"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny section-edit-pencil-icon"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg>edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<figure class="thumb tright show-info-icon" style="width: 182px"> 	<a href="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/f/ff/Unidentified_Chaser.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20191211141410" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/harrypotter/images/f/ff/Unidentified_Chaser.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/180?cb=20191211141410" decoding="async" width="180" height="164" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="Unidentified Chaser.jpg" data-image-key="Unidentified_Chaser.jpg" /></a> 	 	<figcaption class="thumbcaption"> 		 			<a href="/wiki/File:Unidentified_Chaser.jpg" class="info-icon"><svg><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-info-small"></use></svg></a> 		 		 		 			<p class="caption">This character's original appearance</p> 		 	</figcaption> </figure>
<ul><li>His house is the same as that of <a href="/wiki/Jacob%27s_sibling" title="Jacob&#39;s sibling">Jacob's sibling</a>, which is chosen by the player.</li>
<li>Originally, the Chaser was female and had copper hair and light skin. The character's appearance was later changed to a male with dark skin and black hair for the "Quidditch Cup" achievement quest. Later, in Quidditch Season 2 Chapter 1, his appearance was changed yet again, this time with light skin and a different haircut, although he still keeps his appearance with dark skin in other places such as the Clean Sweep event. However, lines said in the Quidditch chapters indicate these are all the same character.</li></ul>
